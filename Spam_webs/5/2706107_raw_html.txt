            <div class="entry-excerpt">
             <p>
              <strong>
               Orlando Event: Kaskade's Spark Run is coming, save $10 w/ promo code SPARKEDM!! Orlando EDM | Orlando Entertainment | Orlando Music Festivals | Orlando 5K
              </strong>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <a id="dd_start">
             </a>
             <p>
              <a href="http://fsedm.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Spark-Run-Logo_Orlando-EDM_Orlando-Events_Orlando-Entertainment_Orlando-5K_Orlando-Fun-Run_Orlando-Spark-Run-Tickets.png" rel="fancybox[7348]">

              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              Wanna see Kaskade in Orlando before the end of the year? Of course you do!! Could you use a spare $10? Thats a burrito and coupleCoronas, of course you could!!
              <span style="color: #0000ff;">
               <a href="http://www.thesparkrun.com/locations/Orlando" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thesparkrun.com/locations/Orlando', 'Kaskades Spark Run']);" style="color: #0000ff;" target="_blank">
                Kaskades Spark Run
               </a>
              </span>
              is coming to Orlando at The Central Florida Fairgrounds October 4th at 8pm and were about to save you some cash!! Get your crew together and use promo code
              <strong>
               SPARKEDM
              </strong>
              , youll save ten dollars per ticket!! If youd like to take a chance at winning some tickets,
              <span style="color: #0000ff;">
               <a href="http://fsedm.com/win-tickets-to-kaskades-spark-run-orlando/" style="color: #0000ff;" target="_blank">
                click this link
               </a>
              </span>
              for more info!!
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               <strong>
                Purchase here:
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="https://www.eventbrite.com/e/the-spark-run-orlando-october-4-2014-registration-12667939147" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.eventbrite.com/e/the-spark-run-orlando-october-4-2014-registration-12667939147', '']);" target="_blank">
               <img alt="Spark Run Orlando Tickets_Orlando EDM_Orlando Events_Orlando Entertainment" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7361" height="150" src="http://fsedm.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Spark-Run-Orlando-Tickets_Orlando-EDM_Orlando-Events_Orlando-Entertainment.jpg" width="300"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Youve probably seen the buzz around town already, but if not here are the details from TheSparkRun.com
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://fsedm.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Spark-Run-Course_Spark-Run-Orlando_Orlando-EDM_The-Spark-Run-Orlando-Tickets.jpg" rel="fancybox[7348]" target="_blank">
               <img alt="The Spark Run Course_Spark Run Orlando_Orlando EDM_The Spark Run Orlando Tickets" class="aligncenter wp-image-7362" height="314" src="http://fsedm.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Spark-Run-Course_Spark-Run-Orlando_Orlando-EDM_The-Spark-Run-Orlando-Tickets.jpg" width="621"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              (Click Map To Expand)
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/708117345910324" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/events/708117345910324', 'The Spark Run']);" target="_blank">
               The Spark Run
              </a>
              is the ultimate nighttime 5k run/walk. It is a high-energy family friendly event featuring an interactive environment full of light and sound providing a sensory voyage like no other. The map below illustrates just a few of the sensational zones each runner will experience as you make your way through the course.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               IGNITE THE NIGHT.
              </strong>
              The Spark Run celebrates originality and we highly encourage outrageous costumes, glow sticks and LEDs. To help you get started, all paid participants receive:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Premium t-shirt
              </li>
              <li>
               Race bib
              </li>
              <li>
               LED bracelet
              </li>
              <li>
               Temporary tattoo
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              <strong>
               FAMILY FUN.
              </strong>
              The Spark Run is all about fun! This is a 5k run/walk, it is not a timed event and we encourage bringing the whole family. Ages 12 and under are only $5 so decorate your baby joggers and bring the little ones.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               COME EARLY, STAY LATE.
              </strong>
              You will want to come early and enjoy the festivities at the pre-run plaza. There will be face painting, music and plenty of glow gear and merchandise available. Dont forget to stay for the ultimate after-party featuring amazing live performances.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               COMMUNITY.
              </strong>
              The Spark Run celebrates fun, health, originality and giving back to the community. We partner with a local charity at each of our events in an effort to bring awareness and donations to these great causes.
             </p>
