         <p>
          Washington state set the bar for funny messaging regarding its newly-legal marijuana when Seattle Police Department put up a public service website called
          <a href="http://spdblotter.seattle.gov/2012/11/09/marijwhatnow-a-guide-to-legal-marijuana-use-in-seattle/">
           mariwhatnow?
          </a>
          as a guide to the dos and donts of legally enjoying. Now Colorado is following suit with an ad campaign to discourage high driving.
         </p>
         <p>
          Called Drive High, Get a DUI, the campaign aims to shame stoners into staying off the roads while baked. And theyre actually pretty funny. The ads will run in Colorado from March 10 to April 14. Watch below.
