         <p>
          Antonio La Cava, a retired Italian schoolteacher, decided that after 42 years of teaching, he could do even more to spread the
          <a href="http://inhabitat.com/trippy-book-themed-cafe-beckons-bibliophiles-inside-for-espresso/">
           love of reading
          </a>
          to children. So in 2003, he bought a used Ape motorbike and modified it to create
          <a href="http://unconsumption.tumblr.com/post/77391318066/it-is-a-beautiful-italian-story-of-the-retired">
           a portable library
          </a>
          that houses 700 books. Hes been traveling in the Bibliomotocarro ever since.
         </p>
