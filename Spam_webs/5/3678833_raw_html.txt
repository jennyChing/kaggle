     <h1>
      Black Hat Multimedia Archives
     </h1>
     <p>
      This archive of computer security presentations is provided free of charge as a service to the world wide computer security community.
     </p>
     <p>
      Speaker presentations and materials are put on-line generally two weeks after the event. Audio and video are generally available 6-9 months after the conference. If a speaker is listed on the conference page, but their speech is not present here it generally means there is no available audio, video or materials.
     </p>
     <p>
      If you have questions or have found a broken link, please send a message to ping at blackhat dt com
     </p>
     <p>
      Many Black Hat talks are available in audio and video formats.  While we reorganize the site to include direct links, please peruse our
      <a href="feed://www.blackhat.com/BlackHatRSS.xml">
       RSS feed
      </a>
      for links to those talks currently online.
      <br/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Many of the presentations available on this site require
      <a href="http://www.adobe.com" title="Adobe Website">
       <strong>
        Acrobat Reader.
       </strong>
      </a>
      <a href="http://www.adobe.com" title="Adobe Website">
       <img alt="Get Adobe Acrobat" border="0" height="31" src="../images/getacrobat.gif" width="88"/>
      </a>
      Please use at least Acrobat 5.0 in order to view these files.
      <br/>
     </p>
     <p>
      If you want to purchase complete video or audio of a conference, or just a specific speaker please visit
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.tsok.net?_wsConference_Codedatarq=2001-BHB&amp;ac=BHB" title="The Sound of Knowledge">
        The Source of Knowledge
       </a>
      </strong>
      website. They have professionally recorded video and audio available for purchase.
     </p>
