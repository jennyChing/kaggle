        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         How Male Enhancement Pills Work
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-10T13:43:06+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 10, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.callenish.com/author/admin" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Bruce Atherton
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span style="float:right">
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://www.callenish.com/how-male-enhancement-pills-work.php#respond">
            Leave a Comment
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <img class="post-photo aligncenter" src="http://www.callenish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/pills.jpg"/>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         The average size of an American mans erect penis is 5.5 inches, a
         <a class="liexternal" href="http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/10.1111/(ISSN)1743-6109" target="_blank">
          Journal of Sex Medicine
         </a>
         study shows. At the same time, only 35 percent of all men are satisfied with their penis size. Only 17 percent of men have erect penis length exceeding seven inches, yet the majority of individuals believe that this size is the norm.
        </p>
        <p>
         Unrealistic expectations and the adult film industry are easily to blame for this lack of self-confidence and satisfaction with ones body. As a result, many men are looking for safe and efficient enhancement possibilities.
        </p>
        <p>
         Are you one of these men? If so, youre probably thinking about supplements that will help you increase the size of your penis. Do these pills work and if so, how many inches can you add to your length?
        </p>
        <h2>
         What Do They Contain?
        </h2>
        <p>
         The safest size increase pills rely on natural extracts that help accomplish several different goals:
        </p>
        <div class="su-list su-list-style-">
         <ul>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-check" style="color:green">
           </i>
           They improve the flow of blood to the penis
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-check" style="color:green">
           </i>
           They boost testosterone production
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-check" style="color:green">
           </i>
           They affect the libido
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-check" style="color:green">
           </i>
           They result in an energy boost
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <p>
         Some of the ingredients used commonly to accomplish these goals include:
        </p>
        <div class="su-list su-list-style-">
         <ul>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           <a class="liwikipedia" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epimedium" target="_blank">
            horny goat weed
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           deer antler velvet
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           <a class="liexternal" href="http://umm.edu/health/medical/altmed/herb/ginkgo-biloba" target="_blank">
            ginkgo biloba
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           ginseng
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           damiana
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           maca
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           tribulus
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           <a class="liwikipedia" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yohimbine" target="_blank">
            yohimbine
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           zinc
          </li>
          <li>
           <i class="fa fa-pagelines" style="color:green">
           </i>
           and L-arginine
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <p>
         They have been known as aphrodisiacs for centuries and many are still a part of traditional medicine. The plant extracts are used for the treatment of
         <a class="liexternal" href="http://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/infertility/" target="_blank">
          infertility
         </a>
         , premature ejaculation and erectile dysfunction.
        </p>
        <p>
         The best supplements feature aphrodisiacs, vasodilators and testosterone boosters. Such combinations of ingredients deliver the best results and as they accumulate in the body, the results become easier to obtain.
        </p>
        <h2>
         How Do Male Enhancement Supplements Work?
        </h2>
        <p>
         Its important to keep in mind that very few natural products are created for the sole purpose of increasing your penis size.
         <a class="liinternal" href="http://www.callenish.com" target="_blank">
          Most such products
         </a>
         deliver male enhancement by affecting the quality and the size of erections.
        </p>
        <p>
         Improved blood flow to the penis makes it easier to get an erection. The same applies to higher levels of testosterone. As a result, men taking such supplements experience harder and bigger erections.
        </p>
        <p>
         This function is the one that also contributes to a gradual increase in the penis size. Having more blood entering the penile tissue will result in mild stretching.
        </p>
        <p>
         As the penis is stretched time and time again, it starts gaining inches both in length and in girth. This is why numerous men using supplements over the long run report seeing an increase in their penis size.
        </p>
        <h2>
         A Few Words Of Caution
        </h2>
        <p>
         The male enhancement market features a wide range of supplements. All of these products come with lucrative marketing campaigns that promise spectacular results.
        </p>
        <p>
         Look beyond the marketing hype, in order to make sure that youre choosing the best product that the market has to offer. Researching both the ingredients and the reputation of the manufacturer will help you make up your mind.
        </p>
        <p>
         Try to find out more about each ingredient. Clinical studies have been performed about various natural extracts and aphrodisiacs. Many such substances deliver the desired results but some ingredients included in the formulas of supplements could be inefficient.
        </p>
        <p>
         Avoid all supplement options that refrain from disclosing all of the information about the ingredients. Items relying on a secret formula could potentially be hazardous. Reputable manufacturers give their clients sufficient amounts of information for informed selections to be made.
        </p>
        <p>
         Penis size increase pills are not created equal. Some products will go beyond expectations while others will be a complete disappointment. Refrain from opting for anything that promises instant results.
        </p>
        <p>
         Natural supplementation requires some time to start delivering benefits. Opting for quality, natural ingredients will help you see sustainable results without putting your health at risk.
        </p>
