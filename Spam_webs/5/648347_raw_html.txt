       India Fires Nuclear Capable Missile
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="entrytext">
      <header class="entry-header">
       <h3 class="entry-title">
        India test-fires nuclear capable strategic ballistic missile
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://andrewtheprophet.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Agni-11.jpg">
         <img alt="Agni-11" class="aligncenter wp-image-3951" data-wp-pid="3951" height="398" src="http://andrewtheprophet.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Agni-11.jpg" width="530"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <div class="entry-meta capital">
        <p>
         19 hours ago
         <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-11-09T13:48:53+00:00">
         </time>
         BY MONITORING DESK
        </p>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-thumbnail">
      </div>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.andrewtheprophet.com">
         India test-fired on Sunday a nuclear-capable strategic ballistic missile, Agni-II, according to Indian media reports.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.andrewtheprophet.com">
         The missile has a range of over 2,000 kilometres and has been inducted into the Indian armys strategic forces arsenal for nuclear deterrence.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        A report said the medium-range missile is equipped with an advanced high-accuracy navigation system and guided by a state-of-the-art command and control system which is powered by a solid rocket propellant system.
       </p>
       <p>
        The two-stage surface-to-surface missiles range could be increased to 3,000 kilometres by reducing its payload, the report said, adding that the missile could be fired from both rail and road mobile launchers.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.andrewtheprophet.com">
         The Agni-II ballistic missile would take only 15 minutes to be readied for firing. India has first successfully test-fired Agni-II prototype in 1999.
        </a>
       </p>
