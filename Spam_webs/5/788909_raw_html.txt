       <h1 class="heading">
        Holiday Gift Guide: For the Accessory-Obsessed Girl
       </h1>
       <p class="post-byline">
        By
        <span class="post-author">
         <a href="http://mblog.macys.com/author/jovel-phillips/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jovel Phillips">
          Jovel Phillips
         </a>
        </span>
        ,
        <time datetime="2014-Dec-MonT02:12:28EDT" pubdate="pubdate">
         December 1, 2014
        </time>
       </p>
      </header>
      <figure class="feature">
       <img alt="main" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" height="698" src="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/12/main.jpg" width="982"/>
       <figcaption>
       </figcaption>
      </figure>
      <p>
       If youre reading this, then congrats, youve survived another Black Friday/Cyber Monday shopping weekend! Before you celebrate, though, keep in mind that the holiday madness is just beginning. So, whether youre still buying presents for the besties or hunting for the perfect gift for bae, consider our team here at mBLOG your new personal shoppers. First up, lets take care of those ladies who have a soft spot for all things shiny, chic, and fab: the accessory lover.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/12/image1.jpg">
        <img alt="image1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10208" height="1147" src="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/12/image1.jpg" width="989"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        1. Plumed to Perfection
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Tell the shoe diva in your family to sit down before she unwraps this present!
       <strong>
        <a href="http://mcys.co/1vLcOK0" target="_blank">
         INC International Concepts
        </a>
       </strong>
       super elegant Shiah platform pumps are drop-dead gorgeous.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        2. On the Spot
       </strong>
       <br/>
       What glamour girl doesnt like a good animal print, right? Help her unleash her wild side with this fierce
       <a href="http://mcys.co/1z9EPvu" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         Betsey Johnson
        </strong>
       </a>
       timepiece. Plus, the rose gold face is so unique and on-trend just like her!
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        3. Shine Bright
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Finding something for the lady who loves to steal the spotlight? Thats an easy one: patent leather. In a beautiful, bright hue, like this
       <a href="http://mcys.co/1tEGrZm" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         Dooney &amp; Bourke
        </strong>
       </a>
       crossbody, shell always be the one to light up the room.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/12/image2.jpg">
        <img alt="image2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10209" height="1147" src="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/12/image2.jpg" width="954"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        4. Razzle Dazzle
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Sporty girls will get a kick out of these comfy
       <a href="http://mcys.co/1HSqF7Q" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         Steve Madden
        </strong>
       </a>
       slip-on sneakers. Casual but far from boring, the glittery rhinestone embellishments make them a holiday gifting win.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        5. Off the Chain
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Big bling is in!
       <a href="http://mcys.co/1tEGRip" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         Kenneth Cole New Yorks
        </strong>
       </a>
       multi-chain necklace has exactly what your fashionista needs to give her street cred a boost.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        6. What a Stud
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Good things come in small packages (or, in this case a pretty little gift box). Just take a glance at these Pansy studs from
       <a href="http://mcys.co/1zIZ3MD" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         kate spade new york
        </strong>
       </a>
       . The crystal centers and polished cream petals are simply to die for.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Need a bit more help? No problem. Our friends at
        <a href="http://mcys.co/1ya8HcI" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Macys By Appointment
         </strong>
        </a>
        can help you find just what youre looking for.
       </em>
      </p>
