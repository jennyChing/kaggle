        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Has self-doubt been walking away with your certainty?
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="author vcard">
          <i class="icon-author icon-metas" title="Author ">
          </i>
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://kriscahill.com/author/admin/" title="View all posts by Kris Cahill">
           Kris Cahill
          </a>
          <span class="bl_sep">
           
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="onDate date updated">
          <i class="icon-time icon-metas" title="Date">
          </i>
          <a href="http://kriscahill.com/has-self-doubt-been-walking-away-with-your-certainty/" rel="bookmark">
           25 January, 2014
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <i class="icon-folder-open icon-metas" title="Categories">
          </i>
          <a href="http://kriscahill.com/category/energy/" rel="tag">
           Energy Awareness
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://kriscahill.com/category/healing-yourself/" rel="tag">
           Healing Yourself
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <img alt="small.dramatic-sky-VeraKratochvil.PDP" class="alignleft wp-image-6694" height="295" src="http://kriscahill.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/small.dramatic-sky-VeraKratochvil.PDP_.jpg" width="400"/>
           Certainty is the energy of confidence.
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           You already know how good it feels to have confidence on your side. When you vibrate at the energy of confidence, youre golden. Confidence is an attractive energy; when you have this one working for you, youll find it easier to attract more of what you want, without effort.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <a href="http://kriscahill.com/attitude-certainty-will-get/">
            With certainty
           </a>
           , you dont have to prove anything to anyone else. When you feel sure of yourself, thats good enough for you to move ahead with your plans. Theres a kind of magic that happens, spiritually, when you do this. You will be rewarded for choosing certainty.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Its funny how things just seem to fall into place when you are certain.
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Certainty is a vibration of energy. When you are matched to this vibration, you attract more of what you want to yourself. If you are certain of yourself, you appear confident and capable. Other people are aware of this vibration and are more likely to trust you, hire you, believe you, and want to work with you. Think of the people you find yourself attracted to, whether you know them personally or not, who exude confidence and certainty. Men and women alike appear more attractive when they are at ease and look confident.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           On the other hand, you may find it difficult to believe or trust someone who looks nervous, uncertain, and doubts himself. Other people feel the same way when you do this. If you think youre not enough, or wont be up to the task at hand, it can be pretty tough to create what you want to have. Its hard to get people to sign up for your dreams, plans, and ideas, if youre mired in self-doubt. Self-doubt is also a vibration of energy.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           What tools help you to have more certainty for yourself?
          </span>
         </h4>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           First of all,
           <a href="http://kriscahill.com/how-to-have-fun-while-growing-brilliantly/">
            it helps to have faith in yourself
           </a>
           . You can develop faith in you by getting to know who you really are, what makes you tick, what you want, what you love. Learn to trust you. If this means that you need to stay away from negative people who cant validate this kind of healthy self love, then do so.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://kriscahill.com/heres-why-meditation-is-fun/">
           Meditation
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           , especially grounded meditation, helps you to feel safe, clear out the noise, and create a clear direct communication with yourself. Meditation is a powerful tool that will help you to build your confidence in yourself.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           If you have faith in your own spirit, faith in yourself, and trust that things will work out for you if you do the work youre here to do, you cant go wrong.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           What are the things you dont need in order to be certain?
          </span>
         </h4>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           You dont need anyone else to sign off on your certainty, or to approve of you. You dont need to prove yourself, or to convince anyone else youre right. You dont even have to BE right. You dont have to know how everything is going to work out, you just need to find your certainty and go for it. If you decide to have your certainty for yourself, you can do so.
          </span>
         </p>

          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Kris Cahill
