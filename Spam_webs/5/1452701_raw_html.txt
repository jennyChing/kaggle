        <p>
         <a href="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chocolate-Chip-Pumpkin-Bread-Allergy-Safe.jpg">
          <img alt="Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread Allergy Safe" class="wp-image-1136 aligncenter" height="491" src="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chocolate-Chip-Pumpkin-Bread-Allergy-Safe.jpg" width="360"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now that all of the Halloween decorations are up and the weather has been in the low to mid 80s here in Florida it finally feels like Fall is in full swing! What better way to celebrate the Fall season with some delicious allergy safe Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread! With two picky girls I have to find fun and tricky ways to get them to eat their veggies, even if it means adding chocolate to a recipe
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Pumpkin-Bread-Dry-Ingredients-Mixture.jpg">
          <img alt="Pumpkin Bread Dry Ingredients Mixture" class="wp-image-1140 aligncenter" height="287" src="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Pumpkin-Bread-Dry-Ingredients-Mixture.jpg" width="416"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         First Pre heat your oven to 360 degrees. Then we are going to combine most of the dry ingredients together. In a large bowl combine your flour, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Allergy-Safe-Pumpkin-Bread.jpg">
          <img alt="Allergy Safe Pumpkin Bread" class="wp-image-1139 aligncenter" height="291" src="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Allergy-Safe-Pumpkin-Bread.jpg" width="382"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         In a separate bowl beat your eggs, sugar, pumpkin, and canola oil until ingredients are combined.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chocolate-Chip-Pumpkin-Bread-Kid-Recipe.jpg">
          <img alt="Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread Kid Recipe" class="wp-image-1142 aligncenter" height="304" src="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chocolate-Chip-Pumpkin-Bread-Kid-Recipe.jpg" width="406"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Fold in your chocolate chips, or in our case we used the chunky chocolate pieces from Enjoy Life. You want to fold in the chocolate so its nicely mixed into the mixture. Pour into a greased bread pan and bake for about an hour and 10 minutes at 350 degrees, or until a toothpick comes out clean from the middle of the pumpkin bread. Let cool for a bit and then cut into slices and enjoy! Super easy, huh?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://abccreativelearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Allergy-Safe-Chocolate-Chip-Pumpkin-Bread-Recipe.jpg">

         </a>

            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">

             1 1/2 cup of sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/4 cup of Canned Pumpkin
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup of Canola Oil
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             Enjoy Life Chocolate Chips - as much as you want
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Mix together in a large bowl your flour, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In another bowl beat the eggs, sugar, pumpkin, and canola oil until ingredients are combined.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Fold in your chocolate chips
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour pumpkin bread mixture into a greased bread pan (this recipe makes 1 pumpkin bread)
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake for 60 to 70 minutes or until toothpick is inserted and comes out clean
            </li>
           </ol>
