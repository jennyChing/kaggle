  <h1 class="latest_tutorial">
   Puppy bow Hair Clip with mini Bowdabra
  </h1>
  <div class="subcat_date">
   April 21, 2015
  </div>
  <div class="blog_area">
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2955-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2955-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7414 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2955-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
   </p>
   <p>
    Who doesnt love dressing up their fur babies or precious baby girls in adorable hair bows? This puppy bow hair clip is so darn cute and the best part . . . its so easy and quick to make using your Mini Bowdabra!
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2941-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2941-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7402 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2941-001-276x300.jpg" width="276"/>
    </a>
   </p>
   <p>
    Materials:
   </p>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Mini Bowdabra
    </li>
    <li>
     7/8 Grosgrain Puppy Paw ribbon
    </li>
    <li>
     3/8 Grosgrain gray ribbon
    </li>
    <li>
     3/8 Grosgrain black ribbon
    </li>
    <li>
     Clear nail polish
    </li>
    <li>
     Hot glue
    </li>
    <li>
     Googly eyes
    </li>
    <li>
     Pink foam scrap or ribbon for tongue
    </li>
    <li>
     Hair clip or rubber band
    </li>
   </ul>
   <p>
   </p>
   <p>
    Well start by making a
    <strong>
     Fold and Twist Bowdabra bow
    </strong>
    thats used for one sided pattern ribbon:
    <br/>
    1. Cut a 12 piece of Bowdabra wire and fold in half and place into your Mini Bowdabra with a loop at one end and two loose wires at the other end, tucking both ends underneath the Bowdabra.
    <br/>
    2. Take a 12 piece of paw print ribbon and make a small loop 1 wide, with the right side of the pattern facing up, creasing the ribbon, and press into the Mini Bowdabra.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2948-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2948-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7409 aligncenter" height="253" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2948-001-300x253.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
    3. Twist the other side of the ribbon so that the pattern is facing up, and make a loop the same size as before. Scrunch down the ribbon with your Mini Bowdabra Wand.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2949-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2949-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7410 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2949-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
    4. Thread the two loose ends of the Bowdabra Bow Wire through the loop. Pull the wire tightly while the bow is still in the Mini Bowdabra. Pull the bow out and tie the wire around to the back and knot.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2950-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2950-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7411 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2950-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
    5. For dove-tail ends, fold the end ribbon in half and place scissors at corner edge and cut in a 45 degree angle to the fold.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2951-002.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2951-002" class=" size-medium wp-image-7412 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2951-002-277x300.jpg" width="277"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
    6. Finishing ribbon  Cut a small piece of ribbon approximately 4 long. Place a bead of hot glue around wire and attach ribbon along glue to the back of the bow, trimming off excess and sealing with clear polish. A clip or rubber band can be placed on the finishing ribbon before gluing.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2952-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2952-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7413 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2952-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    Ta Da!
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2956.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2956" class=" size-medium wp-image-7415 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2956-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
    <strong>
     To make the puppy head:
    </strong>
    <br/>
     Cut six four inch strips of grosgrain ribbon.
    <br/>
     Hot glue two ends in an inverted V pattern, as pictured.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2942-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2942-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7403 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2942-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
     Take opposite ends and loop under and glue.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2943-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2943-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7404 aligncenter" height="209" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2943-001-300x209.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
     Glue third strip over right loop.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2944-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2944-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7405 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2944-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
     Glue fourth strip over left loop.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2945-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2945-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7406 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2945-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
     Continue gluing the fifth and sixth strips on in a criss-cross pattern, as pictured.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2946-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2946-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7407 aligncenter" height="249" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2946-001-300x249.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
     Cut two 1 1/2 pieces of black 3/8 grosgrain ribbon, rounding off bottom edge.
    <br/>
     Glue to top side of ribbon.
    <br/>
     Glue on googly eyes.
    <br/>
     Cut out round nose from black grosgrain ribbon, seal with clear polish and glue on.
    <br/>
     Cut out tongue from scrap of pink foam sheet or pink ribbon, glue on.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2947-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2947-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7408 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2947-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    <br/>
     Glue Bowdabra Bow to top and voila . . .
    <br/>
    An adorable Puppy Bowdabra Bow Hair Clip!
   </p>
   <p>
    <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2955-001.jpg">
     <img alt="IMG_2955-001" class=" size-medium wp-image-7414 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://www.bowdabrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_2955-001-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
    </a>
    Have fun making Bowdabra Bow hair clips!
   </p>

function createCookie(name,value,days) {

  var nameEQ = name + "=";
