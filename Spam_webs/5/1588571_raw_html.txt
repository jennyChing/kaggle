       <h2>
        Window Seats
       </h2>
       <p>
        Maybe it's the warming rays of the sun. Or the view to the outside world from a sheltered and comfortable spot. Or maybe it's a combination of all. Whatever it is, there's something about window seats that makes them so idyllic. Just looking at these cozy, sunny spots makes you want to grab a book, curl up and watch as the world passes you by. Talk about relaxing!
       </p>
       <p>
        This list of 26 window seats offers something for everyone -- the classically minded through to the severe modernists. Check it out, and let us know which was your favourite window seat. We're putting our money on #13:
       </p>

       <p>
        This classic window seat in a bay window is warm and inviting. Its classic style will never go out of date.
       </p>

       <h2>
        18. Window Nook
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/07b6e-0c89b-ba65f-64b0e-8af3b.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.casasugar.com/Affordable-Framing-Resource-30777501">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        For a morning OJ and muffin, this is the perfect breakfast nook. Bathed in light and lifted further by the fun yellow fabric, this would make Monday mornings bearable (just).
       </p>
       <h2>
        19. Window Daybed
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/1399e-5032b-c9630-6194a-6e2ff.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.buzzfeed.com/peggy/things-that-definitely-belong-in-your-dream-home?sub=2085879_996692">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        More like a daybed than a window seat, this comfy spot comes complete with a TV in the wall.
       </p>
       <h2>
        20. Window Box Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/d71e0-fe783-d39c4-cea94-8b38a.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://media-cache-ak0.pinimg.com/originals/df/3d/5f/df3d5fce16ee28e09c51a67345d3f96c.jpg">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/41ebf-a5bf5-5d933-e061e-2c992.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.accentondesign.net/blog/">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        For the modernists among us, this window box has clean lines and a modern form. Nothing is getting between you, the sun and some much needed relaxation in this house.
       </p>
       <h2>
        21. Scandinavian Window Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/d177e-16f84-4ec80-07cfa-cb00e.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://media-cache-ak0.pinimg.com/originals/9f/35/8f/9f358fd7496f67cfbe0f4cb2866effee.jpg">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        When you're snowed in for the winter, don't panic. Light a warming fire, grab a great book, and snuggle up on this sleek window seat.
       </p>
       <h2>
        22. Modern Window Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/9f40c-05be2-44529-8aea6-c7b39.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://indulgy.com/sososimps/Fireplace">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Another example of the wonders that can be achieved with a bench, an animal fur and a cushion. Fresh flowers optional.
       </p>
       <h2>
        23. Contemporary Window Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/e9863-099f2-317e4-1b6f0-a3b29.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://lostintimepl.blogspot.com/">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The neutral palette, wall sconce and stack of books makes this window seat the perfect spot for relaxation.
       </p>
       <h2>
        24. Fun Window Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/9f0d1-d8130-0ceaa-9c594-7a63b.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.homeadore.com/2013/04/04/modern-guest-house-odell-construction/">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Fun colours, exposed timber and retro furniture give this window seat a fun, playful feel. Looks like some pretty special views over the water to boot.
       </p>
       <h2>
        25. Bathroom Window Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/14704-7f80f-79107-eddb9-42348.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.dwell.com/house-tours/slideshow/bellwether-belvedere?slide=8&amp;c=y&amp;paused=true#6">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Even the bathroom can benefit from a window seat. This one continues into the shower, creating the perfect spot to sit down and relax in the morning.
       </p>
       <h2>
        26. Floating Window Seat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="https://s3.servicecentral.com.au/art_img/a2382-adfb8-278f0-a5a7b-80bb1.jpg" style="cursor: default;"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.iconichouses.com/the-d-house-donovan-hill/">
         Image
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        This window seat looks like it's floating. It really brings the outdoors in, creating a light, bright, modern space.
       </p>
