        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Love Potion Science Experiment for Kids
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-20T05:00:54+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 20, 2015
         </time>
         By:
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Brittany
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/science-experiment-for-kids-love-potion/#respond">
           comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" data-pin-height="28" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.loveplayandlearn.com%2Fscience-experiment-for-kids-love-potion%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.loveplayandlearn.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F01%2FLove-Potion.jpg&amp;description=FUN%20Love%20Potion%20Experiment%20for%20Kids%21" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         We are obsessed with
         <strong>
          science experiments
         </strong>
         here in our house. Especially the fizzy and explosion kind! We thought it would super fun to make a love potion explosion. This is such a fun and educational science activity great for Valentines day but is equally fun anytime of the year. It was a huge hit!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Love-Potion.jpg">
          <img alt="Science Experiment Kids Love Potion" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3948" height="1000" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Love-Potion.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: verdana, geneva;">
          <strong>
           For this science experiment youll need:
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 Vases- I got my vases at the dollar store
         </li>
         <li>
          baking soda
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0019MMQXM/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0019MMQXM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=loplle-20&amp;linkId=52NOVCEZTFRPPNBQ" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0019MMQXM/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0019MMQXM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=loplle-20&amp;linkId=52NOVCEZTFRPPNBQ', 'Kool-aid packets']);">
           Kool-aid packets
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          vinegar
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003VS755Q/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B003VS755Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=loplle-20&amp;linkId=UKAHGMC4VGNAMOQZ" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003VS755Q/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B003VS755Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=loplle-20&amp;linkId=UKAHGMC4VGNAMOQZ', 'glitter']);">
           glitter
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline; font-family: verdana, geneva;">
          Love Potion Science
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step 1.
         </strong>
         Pour around 1-2 tablespoons of baking soda and half a packet of glitter in one vase and then fill the other vase up with vinegar and add a half packet of your color choice of Kool-aid and then add the rest of the glitter.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3966.jpg">
          <img alt="Science Experiment for kids" class="aligncenter wp-image-3939 size-large" height="1024" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3966-683x1024.jpg" width="683"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step 2.
         </strong>
         Put your vases in a baking pan to contain the explosion! Have your child add the liquid to the baking soda
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3974.jpg">
          <img alt="Science Experiment for kids" class="aligncenter wp-image-3940 size-large" height="678" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3974-1024x678.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step 3
         </strong>
         . Observe the love potion in action! So cool
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3984.jpg">
          <img alt="Science Experiment for kids" class="aligncenter wp-image-3942 size-large" height="959" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3984-1024x959.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The glitter made the coolest bubbles. We talked about the reaction of vinegar and baking soda
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3989.jpg">
          <img alt="Science Experiment for kids" class="aligncenter wp-image-3943 size-large" height="1024" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3989-683x1024.jpg" width="683"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then we decided to do another love potion and make a hypothesis about what was going to happen. This time she wanted to add more of the baking soda to the vase.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3921.jpg">
          <img alt="Love Potion Science Experiment" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3944" height="1024" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3921-792x1024.jpg" width="792"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         She loved the massive explosions. We talked about what observation meant and then talked about what we were observing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3935.jpg">
          <img alt="Love Potion Science Experiment" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3945" height="1024" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3935-683x1024.jpg" width="683"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thenwe talked about why this love potion was different than the first one. Because she had added so much baking soda there was different layers in her potion: the baking soda, theliquid, and the fizz. She thought this was really cool looking
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3940.jpg">
          <img alt="Science Experiment for kids" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3947" height="1024" src="http://www.loveplayandlearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_3940-683x1024.jpg" width="683"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This science experiment for kids was huge hit! So fun and so pretty.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
