    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C">
      The Challenge
     </span>
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      Youve had the leader briefing and the mechanics have familiarised you with the small
    Honda CT110. You load your bag into the truck and get on your bike. Ahead is 10-11
    days of riding covering 3000-4000 kms across the Australian landscape. No, this is
    not your average weekend ride and you will be challenged by a combination of road
    surfaces including bitumen, sand, gravel, stones, river crossings, bulldust and snow
    on your way to the destination.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      The Postie Bike Challenge is a truly unique motorcycle adventure. It is not a typical
    tour or a rally - you ride at your own pace but you are not alone. There will be
    50 other riders, several support vehicles with fuel, water, mechanics, satellite
    phones and first aid staff. All you need is to turn up with your riding gear - we
    even supply the duffle bag for your clothes and camp gear.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      Our routes take you through towns and landscapes that most would avoid on their own
    due to the vast distances, lack of mechanical support, inhospitable landscape and
    lack of fuel. At times you will be tested by the shear distance to be covered in
    a day. At other times it will be more technical riding where intense concentration
    is required.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      You will be taken out of your comfort zone on some days, but the sense of personal
    accomplishment and camaraderie that such an event inspires makes it all worthwhile.
    Whether you come alone or with a friend, you are sure to leave with many new friendships.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      Nothing will fill you with more pride and satisfaction than knowing that we are supporting
    local charities and community groups along the way and that the donation of the motorcycle
    and/or cash will go to worthy local community programs.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="Wp-Normal-P">
     <span class="Normal-C-C0">
      This is the event that will have you telling your friends stories ad nauseam!
     </span>
    </p>
