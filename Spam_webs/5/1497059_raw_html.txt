        <p>
         <a href="http://epigenie.com/ncbi-nih-tools-for-surfing-the-epigenome/" target="_blank">
          Tools
         </a>
         , where would mankind be without them? The EpiGenie team decided to search out and compile a list of the best tools and
         <a href="http://epigenie.com/databases-for-epigeneticists/">
          databases
         </a>
         (that require no coding knowledge) that epigenetics researchers cant live without.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here we go:
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Browsers:
        </h2>
        <p>
         Go ahead, get your feet wet.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.epigenomebrowser.org/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.epigenomebrowser.org/', 'Epigenome Browser']);" target="_blank">
           Epigenome Browser
          </a>
          : For the UCSC genome browser fans.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://vizhub.wustl.edu/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://vizhub.wustl.edu/', 'VizHub']);" target="_blank">
           VizHub
          </a>
          :How apple would do it.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://epigenomegateway.wustl.edu/browser/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://epigenomegateway.wustl.edu/browser/', 'WashU Epigenome Browser']);" target="_blank">
           WashU Epigenome Browser
          </a>
          : How samsung would do it.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/epigenomics" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/epigenomics', 'NCBI']);" target="_blank">
           NCBI
          </a>
          : Did you know they had and epigenomics section?
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://useast.ensembl.org/info/website/tutorials/encode.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://useast.ensembl.org/info/website/tutorials/encode.html', 'Ensembl']);">
           Ensembl
          </a>
          : Now featuring ENCODE.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://gralggen.lsi.upc.edu/recerca/genexp/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://gralggen.lsi.upc.edu/recerca/genexp/', 'GenExp']);" target="_blank">
           GenExp
          </a>
          : Aweb-based visualisation tool to interactively explore a genomic database.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         The Databases:
        </h2>
        <p>
         While youre a pioneer, youre certainly not the first one to tread these waters. Go have a look at lifetimes of work:
        </p>
        <h3>
         Chromatin
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.actrec.gov.in/histome/index.php" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.actrec.gov.in/histome/index.php', 'Histome']);" target="_blank">
           Histome
          </a>
          :a database dedicated to displaying information about human histone variants, sites of their post-translational modifications and about various histone modifying enzymes.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h3>
         DNA Methylation
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.neuroepigenomics.org/methylomedb/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.neuroepigenomics.org/methylomedb/', 'MethylomeDB']);" target="_blank">
           MethylomeDB
          </a>
          : TheBrain Methylome Database! This database includes genome-wide DNA methylation profiles for human and mouse brains.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://202.97.205.78/diseasemeth/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://202.97.205.78/diseasemeth/', 'DiseaseMeth']);" target="_blank">
           DiseaseMeth
          </a>
          : Aweb based resource focused on the aberrant methylomes of human diseases.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://bioinfo2.ugr.es/NGSmethDB/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://bioinfo2.ugr.es/NGSmethDB/', 'NGSmethDB']);" target="_blank">
           NGSmethDB
          </a>
          : Adedicated database for the storage, browsing and data mining of whole-genome, single-base-pair resolution methylomes. We collect NGS data from high-throughput sequencing together with bisulfite conversion of DNA from literature and public repositories, then generating high-quality chromosome methylation maps for many different tissues, pathological conditions and species.
         </li>
         <li>
