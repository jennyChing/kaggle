        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Teadora Nourishing Lip Butter Rainforest at Dawn
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-22T13:35:58+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 22, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://beautytidbits.com/author/jasmeetc/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Harshleen
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div id="getsocialmain">
         <p style="text-align: right;">
          <span class="bk-button-wrapper">
           <a class="bk-button default left rounded small" href="" target="_blank">
            Press sample
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Dry lips arent fun. The dryness, flaking and a feeling of tightness it brings on is far from comfortable. Lips dont produce oil like the rest of your face, which is why theyre so susceptible to dryness from being constantly exposed to the elements  the harsh winter winds or the sizzling summer rays. And if youre a lip licker, that doesnt help either.
         </p>
         <p>
          Hydration is the key to fighting chapped lips that need rich moisture in order to heal, especially if drying, matte long-wear lipsticks are part of your beauty routine. Another lip care must is exfoliation that removes dead, flaky skin and makes your lipstick go on smooth. Teadora beauty have you covered both ways with their new dual action lip butter that serves as a gentle exfoliant, as well as a natural moisturizer to keep your pout soft &amp; hydrated.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.beautytidbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Teadora-Lip-Butter-Rainforest-at-Dawn.jpg">
           <img alt="Teadora Lip Butter Rainforest at Dawn" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11810" height="467" src="http://cdn.beautytidbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Teadora-Lip-Butter-Rainforest-at-Dawn.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Teadora lip butters are made with Carnauba Wax-the plant-based alternative to beeswax-and other healing ingredients from the rainforests of Brazil that provide your lips with the hydration they crave. You can choose from two deliciously natural flavors
          <strong>
           Laranja
          </strong>
           orange in Portuguese  a delicious citrusy flavor or
          <strong>
           AaAu
          </strong>
           a berry-chocolate flavor  to exfoliate and nourish your lips! Heres the full ingredient list :
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Organic Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Organic Copernicia Cerifera (Carnauba) Wax, Astrocaryum Murumuru (Murumuru) Seed Butter, Mangifera Indica (Mango) Seed Butter, Theobroma Grandiflorum (Cupuau) Seed Butter, Organic Euterpe Oleracea Fruit (Aa) Oil, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis (Orange) Peel Wax, Organic Mauritia Flexuosa Fruit (Buriti) Oil, Bertholletia Excelsa Seed (Brazil Nut) Oil, Non-GMO Tocopherol (mixed Ester  Vitamin E), Carapa Guianensis (Andiroba) Nut Oil, Ascorbyl Palmitate (Vitamin C), Passiflora Incarnata (Passionfruit) Seed Oil, Organic Stevia Rebaudiana Bertoni, Natural Flavor.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.beautytidbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Teadora-Nourishing-Lip-Butter-Rainforest-at-Dawn.jpg">
           <img alt="Teadora Nourishing Lip Butter Rainforest at Dawn" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11811" height="883" src="http://cdn.beautytidbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Teadora-Nourishing-Lip-Butter-Rainforest-at-Dawn.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          I tried out the the
          <strong>
           Rainforest at Dawn
          </strong>
          Lip Butter. Packed with antioxidant and vitamin-rich Buriti, Brazil Nut, Andoriba, Maracuj (Passion Fruit), Aa oils, Cupuau, Murumuru, Mango butters plus Carnaba Wax, this lip butter has a delicious citrusy sweet, orange flavoryummy!
         </p>
         <p>
          To use this lip butter as a light exfoliating treatment before moisturizing, just scoop a larger portion of the butter with your finger and apply on your lips. The formula doesnt feel too gritty and works gently to exfoliate lips without any irritation or tingling. It really does make my lips feel soft and smooth. I also like using this lip butter before applying a not-so-creamy lipstick to prep and moisturize my lips plus it really helps the lippie to glide on smoothly (buh-bye chapped lips)!
         </p>
         <p>
          Teadora Nourishing Lip Butter ($15) is available for purchase at
          <a href="http://www.teadorabeauty.com/store/skin-care" target="_blank">
           teadorabeauty.com
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          100% vegan, Free of parabens, synthetic fragrance, silicone, gluten, phthalate, sulfates and other harsh chemicals.
         </p>
