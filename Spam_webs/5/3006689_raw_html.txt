          New Fashion Police Cast: Is Kathy Griffin the Wrong Choice?

         <div id="content-area">
          <p>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <strong>
             E!s
             <em>
              Fashion Police
             </em>
            </strong>
           </span>
           returned last night for its first episode since the passing of Joan Rivers with Kathy Griffin and Brad Goreski joining original castmates Kelly Osbourne and Giuliana Rancic to discuss the best, worst, and most questionable outfit choices from the previous nights
           <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/2015-golden-globes-red-hot-red-carpet/" target="_blank" title="2015 Golden Globes Red Hot Red Carpet">
            Golden Globes red carpet
           </a>
           . In addition to shaking up the panel, this edition of
           <em>
            Fashion Police
           </em>
           came with a new set design and somber attitudebut one thing is clear  with Kathy Griffin as the host
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <strong>
             the new format is notgoing to work.
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Kathy Griffin Fashion Police" class="aligncenter wp-image-6487" height="375" src="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Kathy-Griffin-Fashion-Police.jpg" width="600"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://livingfiesta.com/fashion-police-kathy-griffin/&amp;media=http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Kathy-Griffin-Fashion-Police.jpg&amp;description=New Fashion Police Cast: Is Kathy Griffin the Wrong Choice')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           As Joan Rivers replacement, many of us assumed Griffin would take over as the host, guiding the showfrom topic to topic but thats not whathappened.Rather than a group of experts weighing in on topics as presented by their leader,last nights
           <em>
            Fashion Police
           </em>
           conducted itself more like a panel of equalswith Rancic emerging asa natural but unnamed leader (a la Loni Love on FOXs
           <em>
            The Real)
           </em>
           .
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <strong>
             Rancic guided most of the discussion
            </strong>
           </span>
           even prompting each host to reveal theirBest and Worst Dressed in thefinal segment, but rather than ending with the hosts picks vetoing the rest of the panel  like they would have in Rivers day  Rancic turned to us, the viewers,forthe final weigh in. So its clear 
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <strong>
             Griffin isnot in charge of
             <em>
              Fashion Police
             </em>
             but is Rancic the leader or are we?
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Griffins failure to take the reigns doesntmake her
           <em>
            completely
           </em>
           wrong for the show  she does havesome redeeming qualities. Though less provocative than the late Rivers, Griffins jokesseem to flow more naturally than her predecessor. In the past some of Rivershumor came across blatantly scriptedbut she always providedviewers with memorable zingers.Griffin may make us chuckle, and I believe some jokes came to her on the spot,but none ofher jeersendure once the hour is up.
          </p>
          <p>
           Perhaps her lackluster clowning is a result of her lack of fashion knowledge. Griffin is quick to taunt starlets who dont get it right but
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #800080;">
             sheseemsto haveno commentary for those who have it together.
            </span>
           </strong>
           If a comedienne who has made several Worst Dressed listsis going to host a show about fashion a little research would be a huge help.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Brad-Goreski-Fashion-Police1.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Brad Goreski Fashion Police" class="aligncenter wp-image-6490" height="400" src="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Brad-Goreski-Fashion-Police1.jpg" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://livingfiesta.com/fashion-police-kathy-griffin/&amp;media=http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Brad-Goreski-Fashion-Police1.jpg&amp;description=New Fashion Police Cast: Is Kathy Griffin the Wrong Choice')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <strong>
             Brad Goreski on the other handis a great addition to the show!
            </strong>
           </span>
           A male stylist who isnt afraid to bring his honest opinion? It is as if George Kotsiopoulosnever left!Goreski is funny without trying and really knows his stuff perhaps he could have one-one-one sessions to catch Griffin up to speed. There are quite a few changes that need to be made for Fashion Police 2.0 to work but Goreski can stayas is.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           This first return back to television was rough. It is noweven more evident Joan Rivers was a vital part of the show and a lot of work needs to be done to make it work in her absence.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <em>
             <strong>
              Fashion Police
             </strong>
            </em>
           </span>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #800080;">
             returns next Monday at 9pm on E!
            </span>
           </strong>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            <span style="color: #333333;">
             Lets pray they use this week to get it together.
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
