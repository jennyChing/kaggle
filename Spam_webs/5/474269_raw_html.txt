             <h3 class="title">
              <a href="/entries/gadgets/are-the-germ-killing-devices-so-optimal">
               Are the germ-killing devices so optimal
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post_wrapper">
              <div class="date" title="1406718180000">
               July 30, 2014
              </div>
              <div class="content">
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span>
                 Since the
                </span>
                <strong>
                 fight against bacteria
                </strong>
                <span>
                 becomes top priority for people today, hundreds of companies today try to respond to this wish by providing their own
                </span>
                <strong>
                 germ-killing products
                </strong>
                <span>
                 . Today, there are lots of devices that are claimed to be extremely efficient in the overall protection of human health. Some of them fight the dust and the allergens, others specialise in speedy bacteria elimination of surfaces and clothes. Before buying such a device, though, ask yourself  are these
                </span>
                <strong>
                 germ-killing devices really so optimal
                </strong>
                <span>
                 ? We have tested and checked some of them, so here are the final results:
                 <!--more-->
                </span>
               </p>
               <ul style="text-align: justify;">
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <strong>
                   Zapper Throat Spray
                  </strong>
                  <span>
                    this is a medical spray that is created to boost your immune system. By spraying a particular doze in your month, you are supposed to be protected against all kinds of germs that may affect your body. Does it worth it? Clinical microbiology specialists claim that probably it is not as efficient as described. Why?  Because the germs are not in your throat only!
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <strong>
                   Vicks Germ Free Humidifier
                  </strong>
                  <span>
                    it is reported to eliminate up to 99.5 % from the germs. The device works thanks to ultraviolet rays that simply purify the entire air in a premise. Specialists claim that this product is really worth it to be bought and installed at home or in the office.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <strong>
                   Floor Steam Cleaner from Hammacher Schlemmer
                  </strong>
                  <span>
                   . Most likely, there is no a man on the planet, who has never heard of Hammacher Schlemmer. Also, personally I cannot think of his product that has no success and no efficiency. So, its cleanser for floors is worth it and it kills not only germs, but mould, viruses and pests!
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <strong>
                   Oreck Halo Vacuum Cleaner
                  </strong>
                  <span>
                   . It uses UV-C light and it is claimed to kill all the germs. Well, as any other vacuum cleaning device it might be quite efficient in sanitising and restoring the hygiene. Though, its price does not worth it since any other vacuum cleaner will do almost the same job.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span>
                 If you are a happy owner of some other germ-cleaning innovative device, feel free to share with us your observations, too!
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span>
                </span>
               </p>
