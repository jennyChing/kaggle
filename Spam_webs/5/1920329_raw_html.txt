           <u>
            <span itemprop="name">
             How to Make Your Journey as a Cancer Patient Fruitful
            </span>
           </u>
          </h2>
          <!-- &lt;h3&gt;Article Summary:&lt;span itemprop="description"&gt; After cancer diagnosis, you should learn how to make your life fruitful or pleasant.          </header>
         <p>
          Category:

         <p>
          <span itemprop="articleBody">
           Even if you have been diagnosed with cancer, you have to become aware that it's not the end of the world. You still have chance to make it through-or to live a little longer-with the various treatments available for you. You can make your experience as a cancer patient fruitful, if not successful, by considering these steps, which involve seeking alternative cancer treatments:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           1. Stay Positive
           <br/>
           <br/>
           It does not matter how things are pushing you to think in a negative way, you have to learn to view your situation in a positive light. This will help to make your burden a little lighter. You should not contemplate any feeling of disappoint and fear. The first thing that you can do to adjust your outlook is to go through inspirational books. You can also ask yourself: What are the wonderful things that happened after I was diagnosed of my condition? There are no more fights in the family. You feel the love and care of everyone surrounding you. Those and other things will help you stay positive.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           2. Live as Normal as Possible
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Depending on your situation, you should try to learn to live your life as normal as you can. If your oncologist says that you can still function, then work. Continue holding your family barbecue party every weekend. Go to the shopping mall with your kids, watch movies, and dine out. Actually, there are so many things that you can still do. All you have to do is to ask your doctor to know of your restrictions.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           3.  Surround Yourself with People who Love You
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Some cancer patients, specially those who are already in serious condition, do so due to the fact that they think that no one can help them in their situation. No one can change what is happening to them. They are wrong. Don't separate yourself from people. If you surround yourself with people who care for you, their positive spirits will lift you. Their stories will make you smile. You will view things in a positive note. More important, you will be more encouraged to seek all the necessary treatment options for you, including alternative cancer treatments.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           4. Participate with the Treatments
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Increased patient participation and trust is known to improve outcome. So it's best that you follow all the advice and instructions of your doctor. Know which cures are available for you and which ones are effective. Inquire about how each treatment is performed, what the side effects that you should anticipate are and what the measures of success are.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           To be able to trust your healthcare professional more, you (or your family member) ought to check on his background before you decide to get his service. You should investigate on his experiences, specially in cases like yours.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           You may also look into joining support groups. These will enable you to hear the stories of people who suffer from similar condition-how they reacted to the diagnosis, what their experiences during treatment are (specially if and when they sought alternative cancer treatments), and a lot more.
          </span>
