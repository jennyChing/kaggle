          Fisher-Price Jumperoo Review
         </h1>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          On the fence about whether or not the
          <strong>
           Fisher-Price Jumperoo is the right
           <a href="http://www.evababygear.com/baby-jumpers/" title="Baby Jumpers">
            baby jumper
           </a>
          </strong>
          for your little one? If so, then keep reading because this article provides an in-depth overview on this baby gear option, which should help you make an informed buying decision.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: left;">
          Jumperoo Features and Benefits
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          The
          <strong>
           <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="y" data-identifier="B000LXQVA4" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="evababygear-20" href="http://www.evababygear.com/product/B000LXQVA4/US/evababygear-20/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Fischer-Price Jumperoo
           </a>
           is one of those baby equipment
          </strong>
          selections that has all kinds of features and benefits, something that makes it a top quality baby gear option.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: left;">
          Its features include:
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="y" data-cloak="y" data-identifier="B0042D69V0" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="y" data-tag="evababygear-20" href="http://www.evababygear.com/product/B0042D69V0/US/evababygear-20/?cart=y" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img class="easyazon-image alignright" height="500" src="http://www.evababygear.com/wp-content/uploads/51l2H9gt1rL1.jpg" width="471"/>
          </a>
         </h3>
         <ul style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           Sturdy standalone set-up with a newly designed frame that pivots to a nesting position.
          </li>
          <li>
           5 pounds.
          </li>
          <li>
           A weight recommendation of up to 25 pounds.
          </li>
          <li>
           Three height adjustments.
          </li>
          <li>
           Made from materials that are durable and easy to clean.
          </li>
          <li>
           Suspension system that is covered with a soft, protective fabric.
          </li>
          <li>
           A plush seat that rotates.
          </li>
          <li>
           Colorful toys situation in five different locations along with hanging toys, bright lights, and exciting sounds.
          </li>
          <li>
           Motion sensor that encourages movement.
          </li>
          <li>
           Fun rainforest theme and attractive color scheme, featuring light green, red, white, and beige.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h3 style="text-align: left;">
          From all of the features a Jumperoo has comes a list of benefits. They are:
         </h3>
         <ul style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           Provides your little one with a safe place to play and burn off energy.
          </li>
          <li>
           Stimulates your son or daughter to get moving with its motion sensor that activates the jumpers lights and sounds any time he or she jumps, moves around, or spins.
          </li>
          <li>
           Can be taken anywhere since its lightweight.
          </li>
          <li>
           Also easy to store with its foldable, nesting design.
          </li>
          <li>
           The Jumperoo grows as your baby grows thanks to its three height adjustments.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h2 style="text-align: left;">
          Parent Reviews
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          After doing some far-reaching research online, we found that there were a lot of parents who were willing to share their opinions, both good and bad, about this
          <strong>
           Jumperoo from
           <a href="http://www.fisher-price.com/" target="_blank">
            Fischer-Price
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: left;">
          The common pros that parents talked about related to this baby gear option included:
         </h3>
         <ul style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           How it was very, very easy to set up and most noted that it took about 15 minutes or less.
          </li>
          <li>
           That its an extremely interactive jumper, which kept their sons or daughters happily occupied for longer periods of time.
          </li>
          <li>
           Happy that it could be taken anywhere and that it was adjustable, so, parents could still use it even if their son or daughter when through growth spurts.
          </li>
          <li>
           That it had a nice overall look and that it wasnt an eyesore like other kids toys tend to be.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h3 style="text-align: left;">
          And, the cons:
         </h3>
         <ul style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           Since the lights and sounds ran on AA batteries alone, parents became frustrated with having to constantly change them.
          </li>
          <li>
           Some parents though it was a bit pricy.
          </li>
          <li>
           There were a few parents who thought the jumper was too stimulating for their baby and chose to keep the batteries out of it so their son or daughter wouldnt experience sensory overload.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h2 style="text-align: left;">
          Buying Tips
         </h2>
