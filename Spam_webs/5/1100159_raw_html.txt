         <div id="triberr_endorsement">

         <p>
          J turned 3 this week and we celebrated today with a Pirate Party for him. It was glorious weather, sun shining and the temperatures VERY hot. So you saw the
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/planning-a-pirate-party" target="_blank" title="Planning a Pirate Party">
           party invites and masks
          </a>
          heres how the party went.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-party.jpg">
           <img alt="pirate party" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3078" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-party.jpg" title="pirate party" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          At 3 J and his friends arent ready for full party games yet  we set up some play stations around the garden 
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-island-small-world-play.jpg">
           <img alt="Water table pirate small world play" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3086" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-island-small-world-play.jpg" title="Pirate Island Small World Play" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          A pirate small play in our water table
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Decorating-Treasure-Chests.jpg">
           <img alt="decorating pirate treasure chests" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3087" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Decorating-Treasure-Chests.jpg" title="decorating pirate treasure chests" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Decorate your own Treasure Chest  yes the Dads were in charge of this.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/keeping-cool.jpg">
           <img alt="sun suits for toddlers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3085" height="800" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/keeping-cool.jpg" title="keeping cool" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Our own ocean with ball monsters and tug boats in our paddling pool plus all our normal garden games including the slide, playhouse and sit on cars. We knew it was going to be hot so an emergency text was sent in the week BRING SWIM WEAR so glad that we did as it helped everyone keep cool today.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/party-bags.jpg">
           <img alt="pirate party loot bags" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3084" height="800" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/party-bags.jpg" title="pirate party loot bags" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I would love to be really creative with our party bags but its been a bit rushed this week so keeping with the pirate theme we managed to find some Pirate party goods from Wilkos and in our party bag there was a whistle (for playing those sea shanties), a magic telescope, Pirate Activity Sheet and an eye patch. From
          <a href="http://www.yellowmoon.co.uk" target="_blank" title="Yellow Moon">
           Yellow Moon
          </a>
          we also includes a finger puppet to colour in  there was different designs and I was able to find a design to match each  T ended up with a cheeky monkey which is her to a T.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-treasure-map-cake.jpg">
           <img alt="pirate party cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3080" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-treasure-map-cake.jpg" title="pirate treasure map cake" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I spent the morning baking and decorating (cant tell you how hot it was in the kitchen). A pirate treasure map cake which I painted with food colouring and used Dolly Mixture for treasure to decorate it with.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-cup-cakes.jpg">
           <img alt="pirate faces cup cakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3082" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-cup-cakes.jpg" title="pirate faces cup cakes" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Using my favourite fairy cake recipe I made 12 cakes 6 of which I decorated as pirates (using writing pens) and the other 6 I made mini treasure maps on.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/mini-pirate-treasure-map-cakes.jpg">
           <img alt="pirate treasure map cupcakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3081" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/mini-pirate-treasure-map-cakes.jpg" title="pirate treasure map cupcakes" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          We also made some pirate gingerbread men decorating them using the writing pens as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gingerbread-pirates.jpg">
           <img alt="pirate gingerbread men" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3083" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gingerbread-pirates.jpg" title="pirate gingerbread men" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          For our party tea we took the picnic outside and under the trees in the shade where it was slightly cooler and the children sat and ate their pirate party picnics.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-party-picnic.jpg">
           <img alt="Pirate Party Picnic" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3079" height="450" src="http://rainydaymum.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/pirate-party-picnic.jpg" title="Pirate Party Picnic" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
