           <div class="post-section" id="content-area" itemprop="articleBody">
            <p>
             Some of the most popular child stars grow up to be wonderful people who look incredibly good with all the talent and success in their career.
            </p>
            <p>
             However, there are also those child stars that turned out to be quite different than you imagine. This statement doesnt necessarily have to be a bad thing though, because there are ugly duckling stars that turned out to be a beautiful swan when they grew up.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              10. Macaulay Culkin.
             </strong>
             Christmas wont be complete without seeing his face in
             <strong>
              <em>
               Home Alone
              </em>
             </strong>
             . Now he is one of the best example of a child star melt down.
             <img alt="2 10" class="aligncenter wp-image-14576 size-full" height="375" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-10.png" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              09. Elijah Wood.
             </strong>
             Before he was Frodo, he was a cute child star who was in
             <em>
              <strong>
               The Adventures of Huck Finn and the Good Son,
              </strong>
             </em>
             with Culkin.
             <img alt="2 9" class="aligncenter wp-image-14575" height="279" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-91.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              08. Taylor Momsen.
             </strong>
             She was 7 in
             <em>
              <strong>
               How the Grinch Stole Christmas
              </strong>
             </em>
             and now she looks very cool with her band
             <em>
              The Pretty Reckless
             </em>
             .
             <img alt="2 8" class="aligncenter wp-image-14574" height="308" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-81.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              07. Nate Richert.
             </strong>
             You had a crush on him in
             <em>
              <strong>
               Sabrina the Teenage Witch
              </strong>
             </em>
             . Now he is a musician with a moustache.
             <img alt="2 7" class="aligncenter wp-image-14573" height="355" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-71.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              06. Ariana Richards.
             </strong>
             She will forever be known as the little girl from
             <strong>
              <em>
               Jurassic Park
              </em>
             </strong>
             , now she is a painter.
             <img alt="2 6" class="aligncenter wp-image-14572" height="292" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-61.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              05. Peter Ostrum.
             </strong>
             He was Charlie Bucket in the 1971
             <em>
              <strong>
               Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.
              </strong>
             </em>
             Now he is almost unrecognizable because of his moustache.
             <img alt="2 5" class="aligncenter wp-image-14571 size-full" height="317" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-52.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              04. Alexa Vega
             </strong>
             . The girl from
             <em>
              <strong>
               Spy Kids
              </strong>
             </em>
             is now a beautiful stunner with upcoming films like
             <em>
              <strong>
               Sin City
              </strong>
             </em>
             .
             <img alt="2 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14570" height="246" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-4.png" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              03, Kristen Stewart.
             </strong>
             Bella used to be the girl from
             <em>
              <strong>
               Panic Room
              </strong>
             </em>
             .
             <img alt="2 3" class="aligncenter wp-image-14569" height="333" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-32.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              02. Jonathan Linicki.
             </strong>
             The
             <em>
              <strong>
               Little Vampire
              </strong>
             </em>
             is now a hot hunk.
             <img alt="2 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14568" height="360" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-23.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              01. Matthew Lewis.
             </strong>
             Neville Longbottom of
             <em>
              <strong>
               Harry Potter
              </strong>
             </em>
             magically transformed from a geeky little wizard to a certified hunk.
             <img alt="2 1" class="aligncenter wp-image-14567" height="206" src="https://www.glozine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-12.jpg" width="500"/>
            </p>
