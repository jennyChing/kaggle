             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               Its PIZZA! Its PASTA! This recipe turned out SO amazingly wonderful! But how could it not? Combining pizza and pasta could never be a bad thing!!!! This recipe is easily customized depending on what your familys favorite pizza toppings are.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <br/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <b>
               <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                Supreme Pizza Pasta Bake
               </span>
              </b>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <b>
               <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                Ingredients
               </span>
              </b>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               2 Tbsp bacon grease or oil
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1 lb Italian Sausage, casings removed and crumbled
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1 onion, diced (reserve a few for the topping)
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1 bell pepper, diced (reserve a few for the topping)
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               8 oz mushrooms, sliced
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               3 cloves garlic, minced
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               28 oz pizza sauce
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1/2 tsp salt
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1/4 tsp black pepper
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1/2 tsp italian seasoning
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1/2 cup bacon, cooked and crumbled
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               3.25 oz pepperonis, (reserve a few for the topping)
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               2.25 oz can of black olives (reserve some for topping)
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1 egg
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1 cup whole milk
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               8 oz rotini noodles, cooked 1 minute less than al dente
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               3 cups shredded Mozzarella Cheese
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <b>
               <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                Directions
               </span>
              </b>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               Add 2 Tbsp bacon drippings to a large skillet.  Add crumbled Italian sausage, onions, bell peppers and mushrooms. (Be sure to save a few onions and bell peppers out for the topping) Cook until sausage is cooked through. Drain. Add meat mixture back to pot over low heat and add the minced garlic. Stir constantly for 1 minute then add the pizza sauce, salt, black pepper, Italian seasoning, cooked bacon, pepperoni and black olives. (reserve a few black olives and pepperoni for the topping). Keep over low heat, stirring occasionally until warmed through.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               Prepare noodles buy cooking them 1 minute less than the al dente instructions. While noodles are boiling, combine the egg, milk and parmesan cheese. Drain noodles and toss with egg mixture.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               Spray a 139 pan with cooking spray. Place noodles in the bottom of the pan. Next, layer meat sauce over the top. Top this with mozzarella cheese. Lastly, place any reserved pizza toppings on top to give it a pizza look. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until warmed through and cheese is melted.
              </span>
             </div>
