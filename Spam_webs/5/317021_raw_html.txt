       <h1>
        Dog Health Care And Dog Food During Vomiting, Sickness, Diarrhea And Dehydration
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <div addthis:title="Dog Health Care And Dog Food During Vomiting, Sickness, Diarrhea And Dehydration " addthis:url="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/dog-health-care-dog-food-vomiting-sickness-diarrhea-dehydration/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">
         <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count">

        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Dog Care During Sickness Vomiting and Diarrhea " class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-2340" height="200" src="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Dog-Sick-300x200.jpg" title="Dog Care During Sickness Vomiting and Diarrhea " width="300"/>
         When your dog falls sick, the most unfortunate part of it is he cannot tell you what he is going through. He will not have any inclination towards eating and just how humans feel, he will too feel lazy, feeble and energy less. A pet lover feeds the best dog food to his pooch but in spite of this, there cannot be anything more disappointing when his pet falls sick.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         It is however natural for dogs to fall sick sometimes and some of the common ailments may include vomiting, diarrhea, and upset tummy; there are effective ways by which these medical situations can be healed. Here are some suggestions with relation to dog health as to what to feed a sick dog and how to feed it. Apart from following these suggestions, it is recommended that if the dog is suffering from upset tummy for more than a day with no sign of improvement then it must be rushed to a qualified vet.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span id="more-2337">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Dog Vomiting
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          When vomiting is to be considered as serious
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         When your dog is vomiting then before searching for natural remedies you must first try to find out if it is something that can be treated at home or it requires a special attention. Even after vomiting if the dog is alert then the vomiting has occurred as a result of over eating or wrong eating. However of the dog is feeling lethargic, acting in a drowsy manner or there is blood in its stool, then you must not delay taking the dog to the vet.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          When dog is vomiting, is it ok to give it regular dog food?
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Under normal circumstances when dog is vomiting then it is a sign that its immune system is expelling harmful substance so under these circumstances follow these instructions.
         <br/>
         Do not give any food to the dog at least for 24 hours after it has vomited. Its stomach requires rest and time for the stomach irritation to wane.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Your dogs tummy will require bland dog foods after 24 hours of last episode of vomiting. Such bland dog foods are available at a reputed store or you can get it from the vet. Give the dog the bland foods for at least two days till he recovers completely from upset stomach.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         After two days you can start giving normal diet to the dog, but not in full proportion. You must mix half bland foods and half dogs normal diet to resume its diet plan.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Make sure that you supply your dog with sufficient fresh water as vomiting leads to loss of water from the body, so the dog must not be dehydrated during this time. You must keep sufficient water in the dog bowl and keep a watch that your dog is drinking it. Even when your pooch has not undergone vomiting them to the dogs bowl must always have the right amount of water to prevent dehydration in dogs.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         To prevent vomiting ensure that you always give
         <strong>
          <a href="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/dog-foods-that-could-boost-a-balanced-growth-for-your-darling-pooch/" target="_blank" title="Balanced Dog Food For Your Pooch">
           balanced food to the dog to maintain dogs health
          </a>
         </strong>
         . Balanced food will keep the dog healthy and free from common health ailments. Even if you are buying food for your dog from an online pet store ensure that you always buy a reputed online store considering the proper appropriate quality of the dog food.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Feed dog food to the vomiting dog twice a day. If you have a small breed of dog then it will do well with just one meal in a day. In case of a large breed of dog, one meal in a day is not sufficient as that will cause nausea which will lead to further vomiting.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Along with this one must also avoid giving certain foods to the dog which is not meant to be given to them.
         <a href="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/food-choices-of-two-best-friends-varies-a-lot-avoid-commonly-mistaken-dog-foods/" target="_blank" title="Human Food Fatal For Dogs">
          Certain human food can be quite fatal for the dog
         </a>
         , click here to know more on this.
        </p>
        <h1>
         <strong>
          Diarrhea in Dogs
          <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-2341 alignright" height="199" src="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/8416056824_59b1b68bf1_b-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Diarrhea in dogs is a serious condition and may take a bad turn if not treated properly. There can be many causes of diarrhea; for instance bacterial infection, worm infestation, food allergies, unhealthy diet etc. if diarrhea is severe and persists then you must seek for an immediate medical help.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong style="font-size: 1.8em;">
          Symptoms of diarrhea in Dogs
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Vomiting
         <br/>
          Pain
         <br/>
          Fever
         <br/>
          Dehydration
         <br/>
          Weight loss
         <br/>
          Sign of illness
         <br/>
          Blood in diarrhea
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Dog is suffering from diarrhea- Immediate action
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Keep the dog without food but not without water for at least 24 hours
         <br/>
          Feed the dog balanced diet that will include 50% each of boiled chicken and boiled rice
         <br/>
          Feed it adequate water to refill the water loss from its body
         <br/>
          Feed it the right dog food so that it stays healthy and does not develop any possibility of diarrhea
         <br/>
          Ensure that you keep the right amount of water in the dog bowl, keep a check that it is drinking
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="" class="alignleft wp-image-2342" height="275" src="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/4883281674_8428f07e53_s.jpg" width="275"/>
          Sickness in Dogs
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Just like humans dogs can fall sick; the underlying reason can be minor sickness, fever or illness. The pet shows various symptoms and being a responsible pet owner; one must monitor the symptoms and take action at the earliest. The common symptoms are as follows
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The pooch is refusing to eat its favorite dog meaty treat that it use to like before
         <br/>
          The dog is coughing unreasonably
         <br/>
          The dog is urinating less or more
         <br/>
          Dogs stomach is sick
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Some tips to feed a sick dog
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         It is very common for the dog not to have an appetite after it has undergone episodes of vomiting and diarrhea. It is however not suggested to keep the dog away from food for a prolonged period of time, therefore under this circumstances, it is essential that you follow the below mentioned procedures to feed the dog.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
          You can feed
         <a href="http://www.indianpetstore.com/dog/dog-food/dry-dog-food.html" target="_blank" title="Dry Dog Food">
          dry dog food
         </a>
         to your pooch but preferably moisten it with warm water. Do not use milk during this time.
         <br/>
          You have to stimulate the dog to eat and for this you need to slightly stroke the back of its head and neck.
         <br/>
          Try to feed the dog with your hand while patting it gently. Let it get the taste of food first to develop its urge to eat.
         <br/>
          To feed the dog, you can use a tongue depressor. This will successfully feed the pooch.
         <br/>
          If your dog still resists eating then you must take it to the vet to syringe feed it properly.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          What food is good for a sick pet?
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          After your dog has suffered from episodes of diarrhea and vomiting, you must start feeding it slowly. Do not hurry or expect the dog to resume its normal diet plan. This is what you must do after your dog has returned to normal.
         </li>
         <li>
          Begin with feeding small amount rather than feeding large amount all of a sudden. Even if the dog has will to eat then too start slow.
         </li>
         <li>
          Increase its fluid intake, light chicken broth and that too homemade is ideal in this situation.
         </li>
         <li>
          After the dog has eaten chicken broth then you can give it boiled sweet potato and plain yogurt as well. Never add salt, sugar or any other spices in these things.
         </li>
         <li>
          If the pet tolerates mashed potato then these can be added to the chicken broth as well.
         </li>
         <li>
          The right dog food keeps the dog healthy
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         It may surprise a dog owner regarding what caused diarrhea to the dog all of a sudden. The main cause for diarrhea is unhealthy food. It is not advisable to buy cheap dog food as the quality of it is never up to the mark.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         A dog requires various types of dog foods for instance
         <strong>
          dog veg treats, dry dog food, dog biscuits, dog dental treats, canned dog food, dog meaty treats
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          supplements
         </strong>
         . It is very essential that you feed high quality of such dog foods which are available at reputed online pet stores. Purchasing cheap dog food will only increase the vets bill.
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-2343" height="225" src="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/6040058686_f28b6ef5d1_b-300x225.jpg" title="" width="300"/>
          Dehydration in Dogs
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Dehydration is a serious condition which occurs as a result of loss of fluid from the body. Dehydration must be treated with immediate action and if left untreated, it can lead to severe consequences.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
        </h2>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Common causes of dehydration in dogs
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Vomiting
         <br/>
          Diarrhea
         <br/>
          Fever
         <br/>
          Over exposure to heat
         <br/>
          Not enough water intake
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Common signs of dehydration in dogs
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Lethargic feeling
         <br/>
          Too little or too much urination
         <br/>
          Less skin elasticity
         <br/>
          Sunken eyes
         <br/>
          Sticky dry gum
         <br/>
          Delayed capillary refill time (when you press your finger against the dogs gum, it takes time for the gum to return to its normal color)
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          What to do to prevent dehydration in dogs
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <ol>
         <li>
          The foremost thing to prevent dehydration is to provide the dog with clean drinking water. Its bowl must be cleaned every day and supplied with fresh water.
         </li>
         <li>
          The dogs water intake must be monitored to ensure that it is drinking the adequate amount of water. It is very crucial to monitor the dogs water intake especially after it has suffered from vomiting or diarrhea. In case your dog is not drinking sufficient water then it is better to take the dog to the vet soon.
         </li>
         <li>
          Take care from your side that
          <a href="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/are-you-keeping-the-right-quantity-of-water-in-the-dog-bowl-find-out/" target="_blank" title="The Right Amount Of Water In The Dog Bowl">
           you must keep right amount of water in the dog bowl
          </a>
          so that the dog is not dehydrated at any cost.
         </li>
         <li>
          While buying a dog bowl make sure that you buy the right dog bowl especially one with a weighted bottom so that the dog does not knock over the bowl.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <h1 style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Some reasons why dogs health is unstable
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Intestinal parasite
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Intestinal parasites are one of the prime reasons for frequent vomiting in pets. Dogs as well as cats get infected by pests easily which carry tapeworms. . These tapeworms in return infect the intestine and thus make the dog prone to vomiting.
         <a href="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/dont-miss-out-tick-control-on-your-furry-baby/" target="_blank" title="Tick Control On Pets">
          Flea and tick control must be done on the pet religiously
         </a>
         and with
         <a href="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/grooming-and-cleaning-the-pooch-is-easy-and-economical-with-amazing-products/" target="_blank" title="Economical Dog Grooming Products">
          various economical dog grooming products
         </a>
         , grooming and cleaning the pooch is easy and economical.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         To prevent this properly, dog grooming with the correct dog grooming products is very crucial.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Stomach acids
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The over production of bile or acids in stomach surely results in stomach acids and hence vomiting.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Food tolerance
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Change in food habits may leads to vomiting, diarrhea or sickness. Ideally when the dog is at home, its diet is constant, but when the dog also moves out with his owner for vacation, any change in diet may result is bad consequence. Therefore always carry
         <a href="http://blog.indianpetstore.com/delight-and-happiness-for-your-furry-friend-with-varieties-of-dog-foods/" target="_blank" title="Dog Food And Treats ">
          good dog food and treats
         </a>
         from an online pet shop to avoid such circumstances.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Eating fast
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Ideally puppies and sometimes even adult dogs have the habit of eating very fast. Gulping food too fast may lead to vomiting shortly after eating. To avoid this always choose a
         <a href="http://www.indianpetstore.com/stainless-steel-bowl-for-puppy-small.html" target="_blank" title="Dog Bowl For The Puppy ">
          dog bowl
         </a>
         that is meant for puppies to feed the puppy and give small quantity of food at a time. Well the same bowl can be used to feed the adult dog as well.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Human food
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         There are certain human foods which can be extremely fatal for the pooch. List down the names of such food and then make sure that these foods are avoided at every cost.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Illness
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Some pets suffer from liver problem due to which they either frequently vomit or suffer from loss of appetite. It is better to check the pet from a reputed vet to find out the underlying reason. More than this there are certain herbal supplements which are 100% pure to be effective on the pooch and cures liver dysfunction without any side effects.
         <br/>
         Protect the dog from cold weather
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Although dogs have huge tolerance level, they are prone to cold and cough just like humans. This is more common in case of pet dogs. Therefore to prevent such health ailments as winter sets in, provide the dog with a comfortable bed that is cushioned enough and keeps the pooch warm. You will find good dog beds at reputed online pet store that sells dog accessories. After all when dogs stay with human make sure that they get the basic comfort.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         So folks these were few basic but essential tips and suggestions which will keep your darling pooch healthy, fit and away from the frequent veterinary doctors visits.
