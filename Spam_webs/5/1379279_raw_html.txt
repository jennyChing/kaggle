        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Omar Bakri Muhammad, radical Muslim hate preacher linked to Lee Rigby killers, justifies murder of women and children who oppose ISIS
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h2 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <img alt="Omar_Bakri_CBS_news_Media_6-300x225" class="alignright size-full wp-image-276218" height="225" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Omar_Bakri_CBS_news_Media_6-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         <span style="color: #797c3f;">
          An extremist Muslim preacher who is said to have played role in radicalizing the murderers of soldier Lee Rigby has attempted to justify the killing of those fighting jihadis in Syria and Iraq. Omar Bakri Muhammad, who was known as the Tottenham Ayatollah, has been openly using Facebook to say that it is sometimes necessary to kill women and children sheltering in schools and hospitals.
         </span>
        </h2>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <img alt="THIS-is-Islam" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-276219" height="245" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/THIS-is-Islam-300x245.jpg" width="300"/>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2854604/Extremist-preacher-linked-radicalisation-Lee-Rigby-killers-attempts-justify-murder-women-children-oppose-ISIS-chilling-Facebook-message.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2854604/Extremist-preacher-linked-radicalisation-Lee-Rigby-killers-attempts-justify-murder-women-children-oppose-ISIS-chilling-Facebook-message.html', 'UK Daily Mail']);">
            UK Daily Mail
           </a>
          </span>
          Bakri, who is banned from Britain and is facing terrorism charges in Lebanon, has been blamed for radicalizing several young extremists, including the killers of Lee Rigby, Michael Adebolajo and Michael Adebowale.His posts on the social media site come just days after several websites were criticized for not raising the alarm about extreme messages posted by one of the killers of British soldier Lee Rigby in the six months before his death.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="945555_376866439086812_942487082_n1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-276214" height="480" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/945555_376866439086812_942487082_n1.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          On his account, which has now been deleted from Facebook, he the made a post under the title killing women and children.According to the
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a class="" href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/terrorism-in-the-uk/11262996/Hate-cleric-Omar-Bakri-Muhammad-preaches-killing-of-women-and-children-on-Facebook.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/terrorism-in-the-uk/11262996/Hate-cleric-Omar-Bakri-Muhammad-preaches-killing-of-women-and-children-on-Facebook.html', 'Sunday Telegraph']);" rel="nofollow" style="color: #1c4587; text-decoration: underline;" target="_blank">
            Sunday Telegraph
           </a>
          </span>
          , he wrote that even though this was not usually permitted, he added: One must distinguish between killing women and children and the Mujahideen fighting the Kuffar (non-believers) wherever they find them, whether that be in a school or hospital or elsewhere.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          He also added that the Mujahideen must kill people who do not believe in the extreme version
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <em>
            (actually, it is the only version)
           </em>
          </span>
          of Islam, wherever they find them.A Facebook spokesman told the newspaper they dont comment on individual cases but added they do not permit terrorist material on their site.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <img alt="An-Interpretation-of-Islam-e1400918598208" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-276215" height="1020" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/An-Interpretation-of-Islam-e1400918598208.jpg" width="801"/>
        </p>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          Bakri, believed to be 54 now, was previously the London-based spiritual leader of the extremist Islamic group Al-Muhajiroun.He previously live in London and was under investigation by UK police after he called for young British Muslims to take up arms and join Al Qaeda.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          On the London bombings it was also reported that Bakri referred to the four suicide bombers who killed 56 people on July 7 as the fantastic four.He said the British people were to blame for the terror attacks on the capital because they did not make enough effort to stop its own government committing its own atrocities in Iraq and Afghanistan.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Omar_Bakri_CBS_news_Media_8_-e1401131738868" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-276212" height="538" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Omar_Bakri_CBS_news_Media_8_-e1401131738868.jpg" width="715"/>
        </p>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          In March he told The Daily Mail when speaking of the troubles in Tripoli only jihad salafi can save it and save Lebanon.He added: This country needs to be made an Islamic state, obviously.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font" id="ext-gen136">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          The radical Islamic cleric was under house arrest in Tripoli since Lebanese security forces released him from jail in 2010, after striking a deal with the Shia extremist group Hezbollah.Bakri was sentenced to life following a terrorism conviction, but was released on bail after witnesses recanted their testimony.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #797c3f;">
          Omar Bakri Muhammad said if his son, who was sitting on his fathers knee during the interview, grew up and killed a soldier he would be happy.
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
