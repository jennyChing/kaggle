         Who Does The Bible Say God Is?
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">

          January 28, 2015

         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <h2>
         Who Does the Bible Say God Is?
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="Who Does the Bible Say God Is?" class="aligncenter wp-image-6690" height="491" src="http://www.satisfactionthroughchrist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Who-Does-the-Bible-Say-God-Is.jpg" width="332"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         With
         <a href="http://www.theopedia.com/Pantheism" title="Pantheism definition">
          Pantheism
         </a>
         running rampant these days, Ive become increasingly aware that Gods name and character are being grossly misused and misrepresented due to a confusion of who He is. God
         <strong>
          IS
         </strong>
         <strong>
          NOT
         </strong>
         the universe. Yet, God
         <strong>
          IS
         </strong>
         the
         <em>
          <a href="https://www.bible.com/bible/111/gen.1.niv" title="Genesis, chapter 1">
           Creator
          </a>
         </em>
         of the universe.
         <em>
          For He spoke, and it came to be; He commanded, and it stood firm (Psalm 33:9).
         </em>
         Conversely, the universe
         <strong>
          IS
         </strong>
         <strong>
          NOT
         </strong>
         God. The universe with all its terrain, water sources, precipitation capabilities, solar energy, etc. works
         <strong>
          BECAUSE GOD ALLOWS IT TO
         </strong>
         <a href="https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Colossians+1:16-17" title="Colossians 1:16-17">
          (Colossians 1: 16-17)
         </a>
         . He ordains it. God is not in you and you in Him just because you believe it to be so. This type of thinking implies that humans are divine, omnipotent beings. This is idolatry. God does, however, live in you in the form of Christ
         <a href="https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=romans+8%3A10&amp;version=NIV" title="Romans 8:10">
          (Romans 8:10)
         </a>
         , if you have confessed Him as your Lord and Savior
         <a href="https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=romans+10%3A13&amp;version=NIV" title="Romans 10:13">
          (Romans 10:13)
         </a>
         . God is not whoever you want Him to be. Is the sky red because you believe it to be so?
        </p>
        <p>
         Be forewarned that Satan is the master deceiver and revels in your corrupted thinking.
         <em>
          Even Satan disguises himself as an angel of light (2 Corinthians 11:14).
         </em>
         If you have been operating under this deception, the good news is that the Bible has answers as to who God
         <strong>
          REALLY IS
         </strong>
         . Before you unknowingly blaspheme the name or character of God, read what
         <a href="http://www.gotquestions.org/who-is-God.html" title="Who is God">
          the Bible says
         </a>
         about Him in regards to your current beliefs. I implore you 
         <em>
          <span class="text 1John-4-1">
           friends, do not believe every spirit, but test the spirits to see whether they are from God, because many false prophets have gone out into the world.
          </span>
          <span class="text 1John-4-2" id="en-NIV-30606">
           This is how you can recognize the Spirit of God: Every spirit that acknowledges that Jesus Christ has come in the flesh is from God,
          </span>
         </em>
         <em>
          <span class="text 1John-4-3" id="en-NIV-30607">
