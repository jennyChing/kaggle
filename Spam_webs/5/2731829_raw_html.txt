           <b>
            Importance of The QR Codes
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           It simply stands for the Quick Response Code which are basically 2D codes whose original creation was to serve in the automotive sector for tracking parts during vehicle manufacturing. The same codes are applied worldwide and they offer storage of mobile numbers, website URLs, plain text, email addresses and so on. Of late they have become popular as many people are using them as compared to old days when they were first created; there is a forecast that they will be used even more in the days to come. The
           <a href="http://qrcodemaker11.blogspot.com/2011/10/reasons-for-using-qr-code.html" rel="nofollow">
            <b>
             qr code maker
            </b>
           </a>
           have designed them to be applied in different industries like commercial tracking, transport, product marketing, ticketing, entertainment and in-store product labeling. They are being considered to fall in the category of the most popular two-dimensional barcodes. There is a number of attributes which make them more famous and most preferred compared to the normal linear barcodes. One of these features is the ability to allow small printout size.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           This is achieved by encoding the same quantity of data tens of times smaller as compared to the space of a normal bar code because their capability to carry information horizontally as well as vertically. It does not only carry out the process of encoding JIS Level1 but also Level 2 kanji character set. Another attribute that boosts the popularity of it is the ability to can be alienated into several data areas and conversely, the information stored in multiple QR Code symbols can be recreated as a single data symbol. Besides, it has the capability of managing hundred times more data than that managed by the normal codes They have the capability to store a maximum of 4296 characters. It has the ability to correct errors as well as re-establish the data in case the symbol is to some extent tempered with or destroyed.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           There are several advantages of using them that increase their trend Among them is the ease to read them by means of mobile phones anytime and from any place; this is more convenience and also makes the encoding of easier. This is because they dont need any special scanner to scan them since the camera of the cell phones be used to scan as well as production of the information. Though they were initially created in Japan, the QR codes are being used in Europe as well as the United States. Also the codes have an advantage of being put into a number of uses. They help in the easy access and the one does not need to further type anything as the codes can help to see text and images, visit websites, send messages and emails, and also dial phone numbers.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Another advantage of these codes is the availability of numerous apps to read them. The QR Codes have many available apps to read them. There exist various apps that can help in reading the codes on the mobile phones. Most of these apps come at no cost as you can download them free of charge. The codes developed and linked by the QR Code makers to profiles on various social networks like twitter, Facebook and others, to help easy sharing of the identities. Besides, they can be printed on the credit cards. The prospective QR Codes aspects can be used in making advertisements in the many products such as the jewelry world, fashion and more.
