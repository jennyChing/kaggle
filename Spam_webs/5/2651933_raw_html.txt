        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         MCPE Seeds: Triple Biome Seed
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-02T09:14:24+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 2, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://mcpeseeds.net/author/thanhfpt/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            thanhfpt
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

          </a>

       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         The spawn is in front of a hole leading down to a complex cave system. Without having to walk far you will find loads of coal, iron and even some gold.On the right side of spawn is a mesa biome. As usually, the mesa biome isbig with loads of open caves which you can enter and explore.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_432" style="width: 700px">
         <a href="http://mcpeseeds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Biome.jpg">
          <img alt="Triple Biome" class="size-full wp-image-432" height="404" src="http://mcpeseeds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Biome.jpg" width="690"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Triple Biome
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Its a cool seedincluding three different biomes at spawn: a flat landscape, a mesa biome and a desert. There are loads of things to explore too, like the cave system and the lava pools.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_433" style="width: 700px">
         <a href="http://mcpeseeds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Biome1.jpg">
          <img alt="Triple Biome" class="size-full wp-image-433" height="485" src="http://mcpeseeds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Biome1.jpg" width="690"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Triple Biome
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         For some there will be a large pool of lava. It appears to be a random generation as the submitter of the seed says there was lava for him there while we couldnt find any. Either way if you continue following the mesa biome you will find an opening in one of the mountains where there are plenty of lava.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_434" style="width: 700px">
         <a href="http://mcpeseeds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Biome2.jpg">
          <img alt="Triple Biome" class="size-full wp-image-434" height="404" src="http://mcpeseeds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Biome2.jpg" width="690"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Triple Biome
         </p>
