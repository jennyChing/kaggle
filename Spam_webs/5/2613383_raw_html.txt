              <span class="picGalCaption">
               Charles L Joseph took this picture of mad March hares in their traditional spring courtship in Suffolk. "I have been working to get the right set of images for two years now and was recently able to get close enough without disturbing the animals," he said.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li id="picGalleryNoScript_5">
              <a href="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59245000/jpg/_59245607_cherry-blossom-coal-tit-kei.jpg">
               <img alt="Coal tit in cherry blossom (c) Keith Cochrane" height="81" src="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59288000/jpg/_59288203_cherry-blossom-coal-tit-kei.jpg" width="144"/>
              </a>
              <span class="picGalCaption">
               Keith Cochrane took this photo of a coal tit perched in cherry blossom in Blyth, Northumberland. "l thought if l was to get into my car and use it as a hide next to the tree l might get a lovely image of the birds and the pink blossoms," he said.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li id="picGalleryNoScript_6">
              <a href="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59257000/png/_59257079_jamaremcufrogspawn.png">
               <img alt="Frogspawn close up (c) Jan Emery" height="81" src="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59288000/jpg/_59288204_jamaremcufrogspawn.jpg" width="144"/>
              </a>
              <span class="picGalCaption">
               Jan Emery was inspired to photograph frogspawn found in her garden pond. "I took early retirement from work in April 2010 and at that time bought my first DSLR camera and have become well and truly hooked on photography since," she told BBC Nature.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li id="picGalleryNoScript_7">
              <a href="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59257000/png/_59257078_black-headed-gull.png">
               <img alt="Black headed gull (c) Geoff Dicker" height="81" src="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59288000/jpg/_59288205_black-headed-gull.jpg" width="144"/>
              </a>
              <span class="picGalCaption">
               Black-headed gulls adopt their characteristic black hoods in spring and Geoff Dicker was able to capture this image at Lepe Shore, Hampshire. "I took it against the light to try and show the translucent nature of their primary feathers," he explained.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li id="picGalleryNoScript_8">
              <a href="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59256000/jpg/_59256994_honey_bee_hazel.jpg">
               <img alt="Honeybee on hazel catkin (c) Vaughan Grantham" height="81" src="http://news.bbcimg.co.uk/media/images/59288000/jpg/_59288206_honey_bee_hazel.jpg" width="144"/>
              </a>
              <span class="picGalCaption">
               Vaughan Grantham said of his photo taken in East Yorkshire: "It was one of the first warm sunny spring days and I was really pleased to see the honey bee on the hazel catkin." To share your photos with the BBC Springwatch Flickr group follow the link below this gallery.
              </span>
             </li>
