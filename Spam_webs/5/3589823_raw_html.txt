            	&lt;/div&gt;&lt;p class=""&gt;Something about how life or God or who  ever  is
behind the curtain of&lt;br /&gt;
all things  pulling all our strings,&lt;br /&gt;
the strings that jerk us up out of the void and into the light and&lt;br /&gt;

And I'm so beautiful none of what has come before me matters a flea's&lt;br /&gt;

fingers or by big bright eyes and the way I wiggle my toes&lt;br /&gt;
at the cosmos because I'm alive and that's all that matters and if&lt;br /&gt;
there's anything&lt;br /&gt;
at all close to pure being it's me!"  &lt;/p&gt;
