        <p>
         Nothing compares to the uncompromising majesty of the view from any of these twenty-one luxury rentals private balconies. Youll overlook the entire resort against the backdrop of El Arco at Lands End and Medano Beach. This particular view, while impressive during the day, is positively dreamlike after the sunsets when the infinity pool and rock grottos are lit up.
        </p>
        <p>
         All of the villas with direct ocean view are 2 bedroom/3 bathroom floorplans; because of their location, both bedrooms and the living areas are flooded with amazing natural light all throughout the day. If you prefer to get your kicks in the evening and relax during the day, simply draw the curtains and enjoy the comforts of central air-conditioning and cable television and internet.
        </p>
