        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://fashionforpassion.org/romantic-vintage-outdoor-wedding/vintage-wedding-rentals/" rel="attachment wp-att-735">
           <img alt="Vintage Wedding Rentals" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-735" height="483" src="http://fashionforpassion.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Vintage-Wedding-Rentals.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          When you are getting married, you will face so many things for making it perfect. As the moment that will just happen once in your whole life you will find it easier to be remember when you choose the best thing for your wedding occasion. Therefore, although your wedding is so long further than now, you can start choosing the things you want to have for your wedding. The first things you have to prepare are the theme and concept about your wedding. When you want to have a romantic wedding for you to be remembered, you can choose vintage outdoor wedding for it.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Generally, there are two aspects you will need when you want to have a concept of vintage outdoor wedding. The first is about your wedding decoration. You have to be sure that you have chosen vintage things to decorate your party. You can choose vintage radio, photo frames and also vintage bicycle, in case for decorative things for your wedding. You can also hold your wedding in the night, providing romantic atmosphere for you. Choose to use small lamps for your cute decoration in your party. Considering have a certain style of party with a tree decorated with small lamps is also a great idea. The second thing you need for your vintage outdoor wedding is your performance. Choose the dress and suit that represent the spirit of vintage. You can start by choosing the design of your dress and suit. Then, you can also choose the colour of it. You can consider using dress with laces for having beautiful performance. Support it with vintage style makeup to make your face glow and dramatic in the night. The beautiful vintage hairdo can be adapted for your performance in the big day.
         </p>
