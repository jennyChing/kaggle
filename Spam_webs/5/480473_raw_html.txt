        <h2 class="single-title">
         10 Brilliant Batman Graffiti Artworks
        </h2>
        <div class="after-title">
         <script type="text/javascript">
          &lt;!--
google_ad_client = "ca-pub-2687663125856668";
/* NEWwalyouSEO336-afterpost */
google_ad_slot = "9807439098";
google_ad_width = 336;
google_ad_height = 280;
google_ad_region= "test";
//--&gt;
         </script>
         <script src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
         Street art, mostly referring to Graffiti, is awesome, as long as its awesome. Well, few things are better than Batman, the Joker and more related stuff decorating the streets of your town.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-159520">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Paging Dr. Batman
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159521" rel="attachment wp-att-159521">
          <img alt="Batman Graffiti" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159521" height="400" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Batman-Graffiti-e1360509599412.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Some cities are so bad, they need a constant Bat-sign shining in the sky all the time. Too bad he doesnt really exist,
         <a href="http://walyou.com/slovak-batman/" target="_blank">
          except for in Slovakia
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h4>
         Robin Standing Out
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159522" rel="attachment wp-att-159522">
          <img alt="Batmen Graffiti" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159522" height="463" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Batmen-Graffiti-e1360509703702.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not sure what kid prefers being Robin than Batman, but there are some that find the allure of Damian Wayne and those that have come before him a bit too much to resist.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Heads or Tails
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159523" rel="attachment wp-att-159523">
          <img alt="Stairway Two-Face" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159523" height="400" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Stairway-Two-Face-e1360509788996.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Too bad Christopher Nolan killed off Harvey in the second film of his Dark Knight trilogy, because they really nailed the character, at least physically, unlike the foolish depiction in the 90s.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Split City
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159524" rel="attachment wp-att-159524">
          <img alt="Split City" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159524" height="397" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Split-City-e1360509928704.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I think Batman stories, regardless of the medium, are at their best when they show that Batman and the Joker are two sides of the same coin, and that Bruce Wayne is just as insane as the villains he chases down.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Catwoman Has His Back
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159525" rel="attachment wp-att-159525">
          <img alt="Catwoman Has his Back" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159525" height="400" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Catwoman-Has-his-Back-e1360510185202.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I think a lot of people were worried about Catwoman being in the most recent Batman film, not to mention the castin. Not everyone agrees, but I think the character worked for most of the time.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Like a Batcave
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159526" rel="attachment wp-att-159526">
          <img alt="Like a Bat Cave" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159526" height="605" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Like-a-Bat-Cave-e1360510275425.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im pretty sure that every day you have kids doing somescavenging in the area near their house, looking for a place to create their very own Bat-Cave.
        </p>
        <h4>
         When Theyre Old
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159527" rel="attachment wp-att-159527">
          <img alt="When they're old" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159527" height="528" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/When-theyre-old-e1360510388134.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve already explored
         <a href="http://walyou.com/when-superheroes-grow-old/" target="_blank">
          Superhero life during their pension period of the lives
         </a>
         , but this graffiti is much sadder.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Pick a Card
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159528" rel="attachment wp-att-159528">
          <img alt="Pick a Card" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159528" height="655" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Pick-a-Card-e1360510493230.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Which ever card you do choose eventually, it wont turn out too well.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Batman From Your Nightmares
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159529" rel="attachment wp-att-159529">
          <img alt="Scary Batman" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159529" height="600" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Scary-Batman-e1360510562123.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im pretty sure a lot of stoners, getting high in the alley where this amazing piece of art is, have had nightmares about the caped crusader chasing them.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Low-Tech Graffiti
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/?attachment_id=159530" rel="attachment wp-att-159530">
          <img alt="Low-Tech Graffiti" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-159530" height="566" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/02/Low-Tech-Graffiti-e1360510679243.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes all you need is a pen and some sunlight. For more brilliant graffiti artworks of superheroes in the streets of the world,
         <a href="http://walyou.com/superhero-graffiti/" target="_blank">
          check this out.
         </a>
        </p>
