       </span>
       <h1>
        <a href="http://www.moblized.com/blog/12-awesome-marketing-tools">
         12 Awesome Marketing Tools You've Never Heard Of
        </a>
       </h1>
       <ul class="sticky-share-btns social-share-btns nav nav-pills">
        <li class="google-share" data-url="http://www.moblized.com/blog/12-awesome-marketing-tools">
        </li>
        <li class="facebook-share" data-url="http://www.moblized.com/blog/12-awesome-marketing-tools">
        </li>
        <li class="twitter-share" data-handle="moblized" data-url="http://www.moblized.com/blog/12-awesome-marketing-tools">
        </li>
        <li class="linkedin-share" data-url="http://www.moblized.com/blog/12-awesome-marketing-tools">
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="tab-content mb40" id="tabInfoContent">
        <div class="tab-pane fade active in" id="post">
         <div class="img-main-wrap">
          <img class="img-main" src="http://dgib7javztya4.cloudfront.net/uploads/blog/156d9b8f125d549de2e4ab5587249bf3_large.jpg"/>
         </div>
         <article class="blog-post">
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre reading this you have likely developed your product, website, or business and want to promote it and increase customer engagement. Whether you are at Series B funding or still cultivating your startups business plan, you can bank on the need for consumer marketing.
          </p>
          <p>
           Here is a list of 15 little-known (but awesome) marketing tools that businesses and entrepreneurs can employ to increase business intelligence, improve brand awareness, and streamline marketing campaigns.
          </p>
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <h3>
           1.
           <a href="http://www.moblized.com/apps/mailcharts" target="_blank">
            MailCharts
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <em>
            Track &amp; Understand How Your Competitors Use Email Marketing
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price: $29/month
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="MailCharts Software Review - Moblized" src="https://www.moblized.com/uploads/blog/2014-05-23/5BzsBjVIGZA4.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           An email analytics aggregator, MailCharts performs industry-specific analyses on your competitors and industry players. Currently offering only basic email insights, they will soon be offering content analysis and full business insights.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Primary value proposition:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Track Competitors and industry leader mailing habits and content
           </li>
           <li>
            Make data-driven decisions
           </li>
           <li>
            See trending creative copy, not just numerical data
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What you get for $29/month:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Add any company / competitor to be tracked
           </li>
           <li>
            Track email activity for past 30 days
           </li>
           <li>
            Review email subject and length
           </li>
           <li>
            Email send-time &amp; frequency reports
           </li>
           <li>
            Discovery marketing copy &amp; design
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <h3>
           2.
           <a href="http://www.moblized.com/apps/riffle" target="_blank">
            Riffle
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <em>
            Skip the small talk
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price: Free
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Riffle App Review - Moblized" src="https://www.moblized.com/uploads/blog/2014-05-23/MRbc13iAqUy5.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           A Google Chrome plug-in that aims to help you tweet more intelligently, Riffle is usable anywhere you go on the web. Find a Twitter handle, right click, and click Riffle this Account. Riffle then rifles its way through their account and presents you with the data!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Primary value proposition:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Find influencers in your community and research competitors
           </li>
           <li>
            Add a personalized touch to your tweets by knowing the user better
           </li>
           <li>
            Learn about users faster
           </li>
           <li>
            Get insights on leads, clients, and competitors
           </li>
           <li>
            Works with 15 Twitter platforms
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What information you can get:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            The users digital footprint (social accounts, Klout score, devices used)
           </li>
           <li>
            General account information such as Tweets, followers, location, &amp; website
           </li>
           <li>
            Number of Retweets and Favorites per Tweet + link to top Tweets on MyTopTweet.com
           </li>
           <li>
            Top #Hashtags, @Mentions, and URLs shared
           </li>
           <li>
            Breakdown of Posts, Retweets, &amp; Replies and through what apps (HootSuite, Twitter for iPhone, Twitter.com, Buffer, etc)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <h3>
           3.
           <a href="http://www.moblized.com/apps/sitealerts" target="_blank">
            SiteAlerts
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <em>
            Instant insights on any website
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price: Free - $10/month - $100/month
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="SiteAlerts Software Review - Moblized" src="https://www.moblized.com/uploads/blog/2014-05-23/zZnwOXad8aNV.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           SiteAlerts is another competitor research tool from Dharmesh Shah, Founder and CTO of HubSpot, an inbound marketing software platform. Type in any website to receive a graphical gallery of information on that site.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Primary value proposition:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Get all of the information you wanted about your competitor in one dashboard
           </li>
           <li>
            Everything has a time frame  understand when not just what has changed
           </li>
           <li>
            See where search, social, direct, and referral traffic comes from + keywords
           </li>
           <li>
            Analyze competitor web technologies
           </li>
           <li>
            See top social referrers  where is your competitor focusing efforts?
           </li>
           <li>
            View top status updates and posts
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What you get for Free:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Tracking for your site and your arch rival
           </li>
           <li>
            Marketing Stats
           </li>
           <li>
            Technologies used
           </li>
           <li>
            Organic Keywords
           </li>
           <li>
            Up to 5 paid keywords
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <h3>
           4.
           <a href="http://circular.io/" target="_blank">
            Circular
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <em>
            Circular, an open source Buffer app.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price: Free
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Circular Web App Review - Moblized" src="https://www.moblized.com/uploads/blog/2014-05-23/Y5DdwWIhpcgh.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           If their tag-line didnt explain it all, Circular is the open-source version of Buffer  the popular tweet scheduling service. Write out your tweet, post it to Circulars queue, and set a time for it to be delivered. Another great advantage that Circular has over Buffer (aside from the price) is that it is on Github (code repository) and is built on easy-to-learn and well-established web-coding technologies. You can modify, extend, and customize the coding to accomplish a limitless number of automatic features.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Primary value proposition:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            The best features of Buffer only
           </li>
           <li>
            Simply works  queues posts into a schedule and posts automatically
           </li>
           <li>
            Open-Source, meaning its free and customizable (with some work)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What you can do:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Bookmarklet to easily add content from anywhere
           </li>
           <li>
            Create a schedule that can post automatically, every day, at the same time
           </li>
           <li>
            Upload pictures to posts
           </li>
           <li>
            Get email reminders when your queue is empty
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <h3>
           5.
           <a href="http://www.moblized.com/apps/hubyard" target="_blank">
            Hubyard
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <em>
            Social Media back in your hands.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price: Free
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Hubyard Dashboard Review - Moblized" src="https://www.moblized.com/uploads/blog/2014-05-23/jVMU4JyhgHY0.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Hubyard allows you to aggregate your social media feeds into one dashboard. The primary function is to bring your Twitter, Facebook, Instragram, and Tumblr accounts to the same place. This makes browsing, sharing, liking, and commenting on relevant content possible in one fell swoop.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Primary value proposition:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            View all feeds in one responsive gallery
           </li>
           <li>
            Redirect content from one feed to another or share to all social media at once
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What you can do:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Like, Retweet, comment, and share content from all feeds in one place
           </li>
           <li>
            Share content to multiple social media at once (Facebook, Instagram, tumblr)
           </li>
           <li>
            Create custom dashboards for certain accounts / usernames
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="height:5px;">
          </p>
          <h3>
           6.
           <a href="http://www.threecentsapp.com/" target="_blank">
            Three Cents
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <em>
            Instant Opinions
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong style="background-color: initial;">
            Price: Free
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Three Cents App Review - Moblized" src="https://www.moblized.com/uploads/blog/2014-05-23/RDrY6k9fIUiw.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           If I had a nickel for every time I was asked for my two cents in an online survey (assuming I gave it to them) I would be left with Three Cents. While I doubt this necessarily how the name came about, it was surely a similar train of thought. This crowd-sourced survey app allows users to pose a question to be answered by peers, contacts via SMS and email, or users on Twitter and Facebook.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Primary value proposition:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Create shareable surveys and get opinions from anyone on any topic
           </li>
           <li>
            Anyone can vote without downloading the app
           </li>
           <li>
            Easy to generate surveys
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What you can do:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Get user opinions on a specific topic, trend, etc
           </li>
           <li>
            Create Twitter-card-based surveys for exceptional user experience on Twitter
           </li>
           <li>
            Make surveys private and visible only to those selected
           </li>
           <li>
            Send a survey to your email list
           </li>
           <li>
            Create engaging surveys that include audio, picture, and location media (all searchable in-app)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           I created a survey in Three Cent for this review and within 2 hours received 21 responses from random users.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Question:
            </strong>
