         <h1 class="art-postheader">
          Microsoft 365, Sharepoint or Microsoft Exchange  Ideal Platform for Business Hosting?
         </h1>
         <div class="art-postheadericons art-metadata-icons">
          <span class="art-postdateicon">
           <span class="date">
            Published
           </span>
           <span class="entry-date" title="1:35 pm">
            September 16, 2013
           </span>
          </span>
          |
          <span class="art-postauthoricon">
           <span class="author">
            By
           </span>
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.724hosting.com/blog/author/724netsolutions/" title="View all posts by 724netsolutions">
             724netsolutions
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="art-postcontent clearfix">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.724hosting.com/" target="_blank" title="Microsoft Sharepoint and Microsoft Exchange based hosting services">
            Microsoft Sharepoint and Microsoft Exchange based hosting services
           </a>
           have traditionally being used for business needs catering from organizational emails, online collaboration, file sharing and web publishing. The combination of these platforms have many successful and happy clients in its kitty and a wide range of world class service providers offer these services. Recently, Microsoft has launched its Microsoft 365 platform, which is a suite of online applications that can also be rented on yearly basis and can be used as an anywhere, anytime available solution which targets to replace the desktop MS Office applications. Due to their very nature, 365 tools have always been compared with the Sharepoint and Exchange cloud platform due to their fair share of similarities.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Sharepoint
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.724hosting.com/sharepoint/" target="_blank" title="Microsoft Sharepoint">
            Microsoft Sharepoint
           </a>
           offers tools and services for organizations to collaborate more effectively in a shared online corporate space. These services allows users in an organization to share files on the fly, publish contents such as web logs and corporate documents as well as to collaborate with each other via chat kind of services. Due to being immensely popular, Sharepoint services are available for quiet less fee.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Exchange
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Similar is the case with
           <a href="http://www.724hosting.com/MSEXCHANGE/" target="_blank" title="Microsoft Exchange Cloud hosting services">
            Microsoft Exchange Cloud hosting services
           </a>
           . Through these offerings, people in an organization can collaborate very effectively through emails, voice messaging, chats etc on a variety of devices like PCs, Laptops, Tablets, Mobile Phones etc. The email is always available and is available as a Push Service, where the email works just like SMS. Apart from that, there are additional features like Calender and Task sync available to users through this service.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The Sharepoint and Exchange services are also designed to work together in unison, from scratch. These provide great interoperability between there components and are much easier to work with for users. Combined with years of enhancements and a track record that speaks for itself, there is no better platform for business users to work and collaborate online.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Office 365
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The Office 365 applications platform is a relatively new kid on the block. It has just been launched and people are yet not aware of the teething problems that these services might have. And due to being an initial release, these services also fall short on features. Office 365 offerings are only available through Microsoft at a premium price tag and the minimum commitment period is 1 year. Compared to that, the sharepoint and exchange services packages are available through multiple third party vendors at verycompetitive price tags that further justifies the appeal of these services.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Hence if you are in market for purchasing an online collaboration platform, Microsoft Sharepoint and Exchange services looks like a more viable option for the time being compared to the rather naive office 365 platform.
          </p>
