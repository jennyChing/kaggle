   <div class="wrap">
    <h2>
     Sun and Salt Water
    </h2>
    <p>
     <img alt="" src="/assets/images/orig/0171-Matt-Rockhold.jpg"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     I shot this in Central California with pro surfer Matt Rockhold. We were lucky the waves were perfect for what we were trying to do.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" src="/assets/images/orig/0157-Big-Sur.jpg"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     Searching for waves in Big Sur, California. Shot with a Nikon D3X, NIKKOR 24-70mm and SanDisk Extreme Pro CF card. I had to stand in a patch of poison oak to get the shot.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" src="/assets/images/orig/0170-Big-Sur-Surfer-in-Black-and-White.jpg"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     I set up this portrait shot in Big Sur, CA. I used off-camera flash to add just a little pop to the surfer.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" src="/assets/images/orig/0169-Monterey-Bay.jpg"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     This was shot in the Monterey Bay area with a Nikon COOLPIX AW100 and a SanDisk Extreme Pro SD card. I've always wanted to get this shot, so once I got a waterproof point-and-shoot I gave it a go.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" src="/assets/images/orig/0161-Rio-de-Janeiro.jpg"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     I woke up to perfect conditions in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I arrived at the break just aftersunrise. There was no wind, and the waves were lining up. Theres something magical when it just all comes together.
    </p>
