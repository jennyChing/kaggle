&lt;p&gt;Remember watching the TV shows from the fifties and mom always looked good while in the kitchen.&#160; &lt;a href="http://www.flirtyaprons.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"&gt;Aprons&lt;/a&gt; were a must have item to keep her clothes clean, but it also looked great.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#160;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;While I have had a few in the kitchen, they are far more utilitarian than pretty.&#160; Until now, that is!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#160;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://daybydayinourworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Flirty-Aprons-Logo.png"&gt;&lt;img style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="Flirty Aprons Logo" border="0" alt="Flirty Aprons Logo" src="http://daybydayinourworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Flirty-Aprons-Logo_thumb.png" width="244" height="43" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;Flirty Aprons is bringing back the idea of having an apron that is feminine and looks great while providing protection for the clothes underneath.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;Some key features:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Long ties to allow for tying in the front of back.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Double layer thickness to protect from spills and other messes.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;50 different styles to help find one that fits your personality. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#160;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;What more could a gal want?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#160;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;When it was time to select which I wanted to review, I got my older boys in on the decision.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#160;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Here I am wearing the one they selected while making brunch after Mass yesterday.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://daybydayinourworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/DSC07201.jpg"&gt;&lt;img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="DSC07201" border="0" alt="DSC07201" src="http://daybydayinourworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/DSC07201_thumb.jpg" width="312" height="432" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://daybydayinourworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/DSC07198.jpg"&gt;&lt;img style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="DSC07198" border="0" alt="DSC07198" src="http://daybydayinourworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/DSC07198_thumb.jpg" width="359" height="375" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
