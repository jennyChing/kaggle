                         They have a nice range of 12 shades, and I can use both Creamy Ivory and Buff Beige. I have been committed to this foundation for years now. It's the only one I use!
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <i>
                          <span style="color: #674ea7;">
                           3. The Rocket Volum' Express Mascara
                          </span>
                         </i>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-FSult-YRtmg/UvUvUk3JjJI/AAAAAAAALzA/eNHvGdnXODQ/s1600/blog76.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="417" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-FSult-YRtmg/UvUvUk3JjJI/AAAAAAAALzA/eNHvGdnXODQ/s1600/blog76.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-1ELB5HyWrkg/UvUvUgTuXuI/AAAAAAAALy8/ABISXuO8tIM/s1600/blog78.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="236" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-1ELB5HyWrkg/UvUvUgTuXuI/AAAAAAAALy8/ABISXuO8tIM/s1600/blog78.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="color: #a64d79;">
                        Maybelline The Rocket Volum' Express Mascara
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         This mascara is one of the top drugstore mascaras out there. Beauty gurus everywhere are boasting nothing but incredible things about this stuff. It is said to make your lashes 8 times bigger.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         The Rocket Volum' Express Mascara is known for giving lots of volume and also lots of length. It fills your lashes in without weighing them down like some other volumizing mascaras. The brush is big, and it's described as a "supersonic" brush. The brush helps separate the product on your lashes, and the formula was created to glide on easily.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <i>
                          <span style="color: #674ea7;">
                           4. Face Studio Master Hi-Light Blush
                          </span>
                         </i>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-BplOhL09Jyc/UvUyf4Ig1AI/AAAAAAAALzU/LTgzEWPx5B8/s1600/blog80.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-BplOhL09Jyc/UvUyf4Ig1AI/AAAAAAAALzU/LTgzEWPx5B8/s1600/blog80.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="color: #a64d79;">
                        Maybelline Face Studio Master Hi-Light Blush
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Drqapbafu-s/UvUyf8hr-tI/AAAAAAAALzQ/53WwTlR0LuY/s1600/blog81.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Drqapbafu-s/UvUyf8hr-tI/AAAAAAAALzQ/53WwTlR0LuY/s1600/blog81.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="color: #a64d79;">
                        Light Bronze
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         The Face Studio Master Hi-Light Blush is new, and is already taking the makeup world by storm. Maybelline really overdid it with these ones. Everyone is just loving these new blushes. They are gorgeous! They are highlighting blushes, so they give your cheeks a nice wash of color, and also a pretty highlight, so your skin looks more glowing and healthy.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         Those who love luminous looks will just love these. Some of them are also considered bronzers. So if you don't mind a shimmery highlighting kind of bronzer, this will work for you. The bronzing ones can be used on just the cheeks (there are no rules!) or all over the outer planes of the face.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         The colors are just beautiful (there are 6 to choose from) and they are very pigmented!
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <i>
                          <span style="color: #674ea7;">
                           5. Eye Studio Master Drama Cream Pencil
                          </span>
                         </i>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-GvhiZVqkPF4/UvU1-A3gxkI/AAAAAAAALzo/L3F-ehafS8g/s1600/blog84.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-GvhiZVqkPF4/UvU1-A3gxkI/AAAAAAAALzo/L3F-ehafS8g/s1600/blog84.png"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ytOycbie8tg/UvU193BvB9I/AAAAAAAALzs/sYPMs1IQXUw/s1600/blog83.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ytOycbie8tg/UvU193BvB9I/AAAAAAAALzs/sYPMs1IQXUw/s1600/blog83.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                       <span style="color: #a64d79;">
                        Maybelline Eye Studio Master Drama Cream Pencils
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         These Master Drama Cream Pencils are seriously impressive. They are very intense and also creamy, meaning they glide on very nicely (no tugging here at all).
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         They are very easy to blend and smudge (just make sure you do so before it completely dries). They stay put hour after hour without budging. They are also oil-free so that the formulation can hold more pigment concentration, therefore creating a super rich line.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         These are excellent eye liners and there are 6 beautiful colors to choose from, from blue to black to purple.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         So those are my top five Maybelline products, and with so many great items to choose from, it wasn't easy to pick them, but I feel these are definitely my favorites! If some of these spark an interest, try them out and see what you think! I'm loving these products and I always look forward to the new things that Maybelline creates, while still loving the oldies.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="color: purple;">
                         TOP 5 will be a new series on Everything Beauty. Every so often I will post on my favorite five products from a variety of brands that I love. And there's lots..... so until next time, hope you have a fabulous day and hope you enjoyed the post!
                        </span>
