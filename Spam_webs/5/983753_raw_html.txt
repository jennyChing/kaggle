      We are looking for someone to join this welcoming home in a well appointed period property. Currently in the property there is myself and my 6 yr old daughter and this has been our family home for many years. Also living here are two male professionals (one of which will be living away for work from the end of Feb, but keeping his room on for some time so he can come back to Northampton whenever he chooses). We have 4 cats and would consider allowing someone to bring a small pet or pets along with them, but cannot take any more cats or a dog. The house has a decent sized shared bathroom with loo and also separate loo. Utility room with washing facilities. Living room and dining room both have entertainment equipment. Kitchen is well equipped with all mod cons. Storage space can be made available in the cellar and the garage or you could use the garage for a small car. The rent includes ALL bills inc. council tax, broadband internet, extensive contents insurance and cleaning of communal areas. We are ideally looking for someone clean, friendly and being a bit geeky helps, but is not essential. Were quite a sociable bunch, but also respectful of each others space. Please dont hesitate to ask anything about the room and the house and come round and have a look!  :)
