        <span class="ate1">
        </span>
        <figure>
         <img alt="3 DIY Face Masks" class="bigImage" height="824" itemprop="image" src="http://s.fopii.com/p/3/-/3-DIY-Face-Masks-2-1594-800x0.jpg" width="795"/>
         <figcaption>
          3 DIY Face Masks
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <div class="article-exam">
         The skin on the face needs purification, nutrition and hydration. To satisfy this needs you dont have to visit a salon or buy expensive cosmetics. All the necessary ingredients for perfect skin are in your fridge.
        </div>
        <div class="article-text">
         <span class="ate2">
         </span>
         <div class="atext">
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Nourishing Face Mask
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mix 6 teaspoons of grape seed oil, 2 drops of rose oil, 1 teaspoon of warmed honey and 5 teaspoons of chopped almonds. Apply the mask on your face and wash off after 20 minutes. The grape seeds are a storehouse of antioxidants and honey nourishes, tightens and refreshes the skin.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Deep Hydration Face Mask
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           This mask is great for all skin types. Mix half a cup of sour cream, half an avocado blended and 1 teaspoon of honey. Leave on the skin for 20-30 minutes and then wash off. The avocados contain vitamin B and perfectly moisturise the skin.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Intense Cleansing Face Scrub
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           The perfect recipe for cleansing scrub. It not only removes dirt, but also gives the skin a glow!
           <br/>
           Mix half a cup of heavy cream, half a cup of chopped oatmeal flakes and 1/4 a cup of fresh grapefruit juice. Put the scrub for a few hours in the refrigerator. Then apply on the skin and massage lightly. Rinse.
          </p>
