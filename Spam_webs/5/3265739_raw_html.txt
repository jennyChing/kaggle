       <div class="section" id="ichnaea">
        <h1>
         Ichnaea
         <a class="headerlink" href="#ichnaea" title="Permalink to this headline">
          
         </a>
        </h1>
        <p>
         <code class="docutils literal">
          <span class="pre">
           Ichnaea
          </span>
         </code>
         is a service to provide geolocation coordinates
from other sources of data (cell or WiFi networks, GeoIP, etc.).
        </p>
        <p>
         Mozilla hosts an instance of this service, called the
         <a class="reference external" href="https://wiki.mozilla.org/CloudServices/Location">
          Mozilla Location
Service or MLS
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         You can interact with the service in two ways:
        </p>
        <ul class="simple">
         <li>
          If you know where you are, submit information about the radio environment
to the service
         </li>
         <li>
          or locate yourself, based on the radio environment around you.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <div class="section" id="about-the-name">
         <h2>
          About the name
          <a class="headerlink" href="#about-the-name" title="Permalink to this headline">
           
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          In Greek mythology, Ichnaea (Iknaia) means the tracker.
         </p>
