            </a>
            for one month each after a policeman caught them wearing womens clothes and underwear at a Dubai Metro station.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            The policeman was said to have caught the 34-year-old Filipino designer, A.F., and his 44-year-old countryman tailor, W.L., cross-dressing at Al Rigga Metro Station in March.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            When the Filipinos defended themselves before the Dubai Misdemeanours Court, they said they had been accustomed to wearing womens clothes since childhood.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            The defendants will be deported following the completion of their jail terms.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="advert" id="article-ad">
           <p id="article-below">
            <span style="background-color: transparent;">
             A.F. and W.L. have already appealed the verdict seeking leniency and to have the deportation order removed.
            </span>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            When questioned by the police, A.F. admitted that he was walking side by side with W.L. beside the station while dressed in womens clothes and high heels.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            Ive been living in the UAE for seven years and work in a fashion design place. Police arrested me because I was dressed like a woman. I had jeans, a womans shirt and a bra I had make-up on as well. I have been dressing like a woman since I was eight, A.F. testified to police interrogators.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            W.L. said that he had been working in the UAE since 1999.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="articleBody">
           <p>
            Ive been used to wearing womens outfit since I was ten. I was busted wearing a dress, bra and high heels.
           </p>
          </div>

           <p>
            He told the court he had used hormones so he would look like a woman.
           </p>
          </div>

           <p>
            The defendants are subject to appear before the Appeal Court later this month.
