     <p>
      <b>
       Thatcher on Acid
      </b>
      are an
      <a href="/wiki/Anarcho-punk" title="Anarcho-punk">
       anarcho-punk
      </a>
      band from the
      <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">
       United Kingdom
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      They formed in
      <a href="/wiki/Somerset" title="Somerset">
       Somerset
      </a>
      during 1983.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Glasper_2-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-Glasper-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      Their name is a satirical reference to former U.K. prime minister,
      <a href="/wiki/Margaret_Thatcher" title="Margaret Thatcher">
       Margaret Thatcher
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      Ben Corrigan, Bob Butler and Andy Tuck also played in
      <a href="/wiki/Schwartzeneggar" title="Schwartzeneggar">
       Schwartzeneggar
      </a>
      with ex-
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crass_(band)" title="Crass (band)">
       Crass
      </a>
      member,
      <a href="/wiki/Steve_Ignorant" title="Steve Ignorant">
       Steve Ignorant
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
       <a href="#cite_note-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      The band opened the anarcho-punk band
      <a href="/wiki/Conflict_(band)" title="Conflict (band)">
       Conflict
      </a>
      's "Gathering of the 5000" show at
      <a href="/wiki/Brixton_Academy" title="Brixton Academy">
       Brixton Academy
      </a>
      ,
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Glasper_2-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-Glasper-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      which event resulted in many arrests and achieved a degree of infamy.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">
       <a href="#cite_note-5">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        5
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
