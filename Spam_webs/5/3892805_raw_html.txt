       <p>
        Wow! We were *blown away* to see how much you liked the
        <a href="http://blog.redstamp.com/recipe-meatballs/" target="_blank" title="Red Stamp | Meatballs Two Ways Recipe">

        recipe! Which is why we are excited that we have a couple more weeks of fun recipes leading up to the big game. Dont like football? No problem. These would be perfect for a
        <a href="http://blog.redstamp.com/host-fabulous-game-night/" target="_blank" title="Red Stamp Blog | Game Night">
         game night
        </a>
        or a milestone birthday party too!
       </p>
       <p>
        We picked two of our most favorite sandwiches and made them into dips! Because lets be honestwho doesnt love a good dip?! The crowds will go wild for these delicious {and oh-so-easy} recipes. Be prepared for lots of pats on the back + bear hugs.
       </p>
       <a href="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Dips-Recipe-Red-Stamp-Collage-copy.jpg">
        <img alt="Red Stamp | Party Dip Recipe " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-760" height="1800" src="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Dips-Recipe-Red-Stamp-Collage-copy.jpg" title="Red Stamp | Party Dip Recipe " width="1165"/>
       </a>
       <a href="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/BLT-dip-Red-Stamp.jpg">
        <img alt="Red Stamp | Party Dip Recipe " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-762" height="1800" src="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/BLT-dip-Red-Stamp.jpg" title="Red Stamp | Party Dip Recipe " width="1212"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Smoky Bleu BLT Dip
        </strong>
        <br/>
        {serves approx. 6 people}
       </p>
       <p>
        1 lb. Bacon
        <br/>
        1 cup Mayonnaise
        <br/>
        1 cup Sour Cream
        <br/>
        4 oz. Bleu Cheese crumbles
        <br/>
        1 Roma Tomato (seeded and diced)
        <br/>
        1/2 cup Poblano Pepper (diced)
        <br/>
        1 cup Iceberg Lettuce (shredded)
        <br/>
        1 pkg. mini Pretzel Buns
       </p>
       <p>
        Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain on paper towels.
       </p>
       <p>
        In a medium bowl, combine mayonnaise and sour cream. Crumble bacon and bleu cheese into the sour cream and mayonnaise mixture. Mix in tomatoes and poblano peppers.
       </p>
       <p>
        Using a serrated knife, cut off top of pretzel buns and scoop out middle, save extra bread for dipping. Scoop dip mixture into each bun. Top with lettuce.
       </p>
       <a href="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Smoky-Bleu-BLT-Dip_Recipe-Card-Red-Stamp.jpg">
        <img alt="Red Stamp | Smoky Bleu BLT Dip Recipe Card" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-761" height="976" src="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Smoky-Bleu-BLT-Dip_Recipe-Card-Red-Stamp.jpg" title="Red Stamp | Smoky Bleu BLT Dip Recipe Card" width="680"/>
       </a>
       <a href="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Reuben-dip-Red-Stamp3.jpg">
        <img alt="Red Stamp | Dip Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-763" height="1196" src="http://blog.redstamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Reuben-dip-Red-Stamp3.jpg" title="Red Stamp | Dip Recipe" width="1800"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Reuben Dip
        </strong>
        <br/>
        {makes 8 to 10 servings}
       </p>
       <p>
        1/2 cup Mayonnaise
        <br/>
        1/2 cup Thousand Island Dressing
        <br/>
        8 oz. Sauerkraut (rinsed and squeezed dry)
        <br/>
        6 oz. Corned Beef (shredded)
        <br/>
        8 oz. Swiss Cheese (shredded)
        <br/>
        1 pkg. cocktail Rye Bread
       </p>
       <p>
        Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
       </p>
       <p>
        In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise and dressing, sauerkraut, corned beef, and Swiss cheese.
       </p>
       <p>
        Bake in a casserole dish for 20 to 25 minutes, until cheese is melted and mixture is hot. Serve with rye bread slices.
       </p>
