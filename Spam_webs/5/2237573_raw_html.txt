     <h2>
      <a href="http://canadiangaragedoors.ca/garage-door-repair-service-canada/how-to-prevent-your-garage-door-from-breaking-down-diy-overhead-door-tips">
       How to Prevent your Garage Door from Breaking Down  DIY Overhead Door Tips
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="summary-meta">
      <b>
       March 16, 2015
      </b>
     </div>
    </div>
    <div class="content">
     <p>
      <img alt="DIY Overhead Door Tips" src="/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/garage.jpg" style="float:right;margin-left:15px;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Most of the residences have a garage door for securing their vehicle. And, people think overhead door is just a simple mechanism that their opening and closing on a series of arms or rollers as easy as using a TV remote. They believe that moving a door doesnt take much effort and it doesnt look like it could be very dangerous, but this is just a myth. So, it is important for every homeowner to know the potential of garage door, as they hold an enormous amount of energy, which can lead to injury or even death, if not released correctly.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">

       Replace the weather Seals:
      </b>
      You may know that weather seals are made of rubber or soft plastic material on the sides and bottom of the door. So make sure that you get a new seal as per your garage door.

       Lubricating doors Parts
      </b>
      : As the time passes, your door will become noisy, which lead to annoyance and irritation to you and your entire household and even neighbors too. So, lubricate the metal parts including rollers, chains and hinges with silicone or lithium spray. But never attempt to lubricate your door with WD-40, as it is slippery and not designed for long-term solution.
     </p>

       Require proper cleansing:
      </b>

So, you need to do regular cleaning of your garage door to avoid accumulating debris or dirt on the track. Use a damp cloth for the cleaning of these parts and you can also use a solvent metal cleaner but only for sticky residue.

       Re-aligning the overhead door sensors:
