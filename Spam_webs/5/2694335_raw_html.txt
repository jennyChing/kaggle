     <h2 itemprop="name">
      NAILED IT!
     </h2>
     <meta content="2012-08-08" itemprop="datePublished"/>
     <div class="entry">
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7294" style="width: 522px">
       <a href="http://thebeautydepartment.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/TBDallblackeverythingmani.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-7294" height="1144" src="http://97.74.65.162/wp-content/uploads-c/2012/08/TBDallblackeverythingmani.jpg" title="TBDallblackeverythingmani" width="512"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        photos/post/design: Kristin Ess
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       It seems pastels and pops of color are the norm around here, but today were changing it up! Sometimes you just want a super chic black mani. I happen to love black more than any other color so this ones for my girls who like to live on the dark side. Here we go:
       <span id="more-7293">
       </span>
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        Paint your entire nail with black polish. We used
        <strong>
         <a href="http://bit.ly/NF3HSj" target="_blank">
          THIS ONE
         </a>
        </strong>
        as it was the richest + darkest of all the ones we tried.
       </li>
       <li>
        Next paint a matte topcoat over all of the nails. We had the best luck with
        <strong>
         <a href="http://bit.ly/OS83HW" target="_blank">
          THIS ONE
         </a>
        </strong>
        . It went on super smooth and dried very matte, very quickly.
       </li>
       <li>
        Next, just for fun we did an accent nail with stripes. Cute 1/4 strips of tape and lay them across the nail.
       </li>
       <li>
        Use your original shiny black polish to coat over the strips of tape. Let that dry while you do the next step.
       </li>
       <li>
        Make a little puddle of the shiny black polish and use your
        <strong>
         <a href="http://thebeautydepartment.com/2012/03/mani-monday-7/">
          dotting tool
         </a>
        </strong>
        to make different sized dots on the other nails. Theyll kind of end up looking like rain drops.
       </li>
       <li>
        When youre finished with your dots, peel back the tape strips on your accent nail to reveal the matte stripes. Then clean up the edges.
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       To be totally honest, I think this would even be great without the accent nail. Loved the way the dots looked like water drops, but you choose. As always, dont forget to tweet us a picture @TBDofficial!
      </p>
