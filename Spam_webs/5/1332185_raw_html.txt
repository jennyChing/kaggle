     <h2>
      What is Wolfram|Alpha?
     </h2>
     <img alt="Wolfram|Alpha computes answers and provides knowledge." id="computeText" src="/images/tour/wa-computes-answers.png" title="Wolfram|Alpha computes answers and provides knowledge."/>
     <p class="right">
      Wolfram|Alpha is an engine for
      <b>
       computing answers
      </b>
      and
      <b>
       providing knowledge
      </b>
      .
     </p>
     <p class="right">
      It works by using its vast store of expert-level knowledge and algorithms to automatically answer questions, do analysis, and generate reports.
     </p>
     <img alt="Wolfram|Alpha's long-term goal is to make all the world's knowledge systematically computable and accessible." class="right" src="/images/tour/long-term-goal.png" title="Wolfram|Alpha's long-term goal is to make all systematic knowledge computable and broadly accessible."/>

     <img src="/images/tour/tour1-spikey.gif"/>
     <p>
      <span class="phead">
       WOLFRAM|ALPHA
      </span>
      uses
      <b>
       built-in
       <br/>
       knowledge
      </b>
      curated by
      <b>
       human
       <br/>
       experts
      </b>
      to compute on the fly a
      <br/>
      <b>
       specific answer
      </b>
      and analysis for
      <br/>
      every query.
     </p>
