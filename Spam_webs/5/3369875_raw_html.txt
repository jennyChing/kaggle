        <img alt="LIttle Arthur Hammond in 'Rambo' gear." src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.977401.1321321803!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/moviebaby15f-1-web.jpg"/>

      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Its never to early to get your child started on the classics.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       British mom and author Emily Cleaver has created a pint-size sensation by photographing her infant son Arthur Hammond in classic movie poses.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       The film buffs blog, 
       <a href="http://www.studioarthur.co.uk/">
        Arthur Creates Scenes From Classic Movies
       </a>
       , is attracting more than 50,000 hits a month, according to the
       <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2059544/Im-movies-baby-The-month-old-boy-dressed-film-fan-father-echo-classic-scenes.html">
        Daily Mail
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/movies/adorable-baby-arthur-spoofs-iconic-movies-gallery-1.976309" onclick="window.open(this.href, '', 'resizable=no,status=no,location=no,toolbar=no,menubar=no,fullscreen=no,scrollbars=no,dependent=no'); return false;">
        <strong>
         PHOTOS: BABY ARTHUR'S MOST ADORABLE MOVIE SPOOFS
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Cleaver regularly raids Arthurs toy chest for movie props in order to re-create scenes from her favorite films without the violence or sex.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Arthurs homage to the 1979 sci-fi horror film Alien is a clear nod to the terrifying moment when a vicious creature bursts from actor John Hurts torso.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       A small blue stuffed animal is substituted for the sharp-toothed alien.
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="MOVIEBABY15F_3_WEB" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.977400!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/landscape_485/image.jpg"/>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Paging John Hurt! A soft bunny emerges from Arthurs tummy in this homage to the sci-fi classic Alien. (Tim Stewart News/Rex USA)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Cleaver has posed Arthur in a headband  la Rambo, behind crib bars with a poster of Raquel Welch like Tim Robbins in The Shawshank Redemption, and covered with fluttering rose petals like Mena Suvari in American Beauty.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Fans of the blog write to Cleaver with requests.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Arthur may not understand the films hes referencing but hes enjoying his brief moment in the limelight.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Arthur enjoys all the attention when he is doing the scenes and likes it when I take his photo, said Cleaver. He is a very happy, smiley baby.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <img alt="MOVIEBABY15F_4_WEB" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.977399!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/landscape_485/image.jpg"/>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Arthur is covered in rose petals in this take on the classic fantasy sequence with Mena Suvari from American Beauty.(Tim Stewart News/Rex USA)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Future projects include the Billy Wilder classic Some Like It Hot and the Godfather trilogy.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       Cleaver wants to create an album of Arthurs greatest hits for when hes old enough to appreciate the actual films.
      </p>
      <p class="DNTEXT">
       I dont know whether he will think they are cool or be terribly embarrassed, she said.
      </p>
