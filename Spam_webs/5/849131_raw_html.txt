        </span>
        <span class="author vcard hidden">
         <span class="fn">
          Kesha of We Got Kidz
         </span>
        </span>

         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="popular-baby-names-2014-pin" class="aligncenter wp-image-16041 wp-image-esh" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.wegotkidz.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/popular-baby-names-2014-pin.jpg?resize=400%2C725" title="Popular Baby Names 2014"/>
          </p>
          <p>

         </center>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Choosing a baby name is serious business in America. Where in some countries you have to
          <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://www.nytimes.com/2004/10/08/world/europe/08iht-danes.html?_r=0" rel="external nofollow" title="Picked baby's name? Not so fast, in Denmark | NYTimes.com">
           select a name from a specific roster
          </a>
          , in the US, the sky is the limit. (Hm. Sky. I like that name.)
         </p>
         <p>
          With name suggestions coming from coworkers, neighbors, family, and friends it can be a daunting task to select the name that will be with your little one for a lifetime.
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre anything like I was, you want to find a name thats unique but not too eccentric, dignified but not too nerdy  a name that your little one will be proud to be called on the playground and content with hearing when in the office. Thats a tall order!
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Related Content:
           <a href="http://www.wegotkidz.com/10-must-haves-for-your-babys-1st-year/" target="_blank" title="10 Must Haves for Your Babys 1st Year | We Got Kidz">
            10 Must Haves for Your Babys 1st Year
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          We Got Kidz is here to help with a few popular baby name suggestions that will surely have you inspired.
          <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://www.babynameslog.com/popular-baby-names-2014/" rel="external nofollow" title="Popular Baby Names 2014: Best Predictions Based On Trends | BabyNamesLog.com">
           BabyNamesLog.com
          </a>
          has painstakingly analyzed name charts and data from the official US names registry in order to determine what the popular name trends will be in 2014. Ive taken a few of their name predictions, added the meanings and given you the perfect list to start your name selection journey.
         </p>
         <p>
          If youve got a bun in the oven and expecting a new addition to the family in 2014, use this list of popular baby names to get inspired. Vary the endings for a unique twist, or use a name as is to stay hip with the trends.Ultimately, this list is here to help you get ahead of the game and
          <a href="http://www.babycenter.com/0_how-to-pick-a-name-for-your-baby_1505.bc" target="_blank" title="How to pick a name for your baby | BabyCenter.com">
           pick a baby name
          </a>
          that you AND your future doctor, lawyer, or world changer will be happy with.
         </p>
