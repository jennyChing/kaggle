          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Maybe you surf, maybe you don't.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           It doesn't matter.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Everyone who's been to the beach in the last 50 years is influenced by surf culture.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           If you've ever listened to a Beach Boys record (most of the guys in the group didn't surf) or donned a pair of baggy shorts or looked wistfully out at the breakers and dreamed of an endless summer of waves and bonfires with the perfect surfer guy or girl on your arm, you can claim a connection to surf culture.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           That's why even if you're the type who sits on the beach with your toes planted firmly in the sand, the world's best surf spots still matter.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           To get you to them, we asked the editors of
           <a href="http://www.surfingmagazine.com/" target="_blank">
            Surfing Magazine
           </a>
           to help us compile picks for the world's greatest surf spots (our top 10 is heavily influenced by theirs), adding them to a mix of contributions from pro surfers and our own experiences.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            50. Eisbach, Munich
           </strong>
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="50. Wave to nowhere." class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="50. Wave to nowhere." class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625103535-50-surf-spots-eisbach-story-top.jpg"/>
           </noscript>
           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            50. Wave to nowhere.
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           The most unusual wave on the list is found on an artificial river next to Englischer Garten, Munich's main park. It's completely landlocked.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           This one meter standing wave (meaning you don't move along the river, but surf in the same spot) is created by water pumped fast against a large rock, forming a crest.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           As long as there's enough water the ride never stops ... until you fall.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            49. Baja Malibu, Baja Norte, Mexico
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Though water cleanliness is an issue (don't swallow any), this exposed break works when offshore winds blow in from the east.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           You can ride both right and left and fall onto a sandy base.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            48. Fulong Beach, Taiwan
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           In northeast Taiwan, Fulong Beach has great camping nearby and produces thrilling right- and left-peeling waves from a sandbar.
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="48. Not huge waves, but a mellow scene and good camping." class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="48. Not huge waves, but a mellow scene and good camping." class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625160912-50-inline-fulong-beach-story-top.jpg"/>
           </noscript>
           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            48. Not huge waves, but a mellow scene and good camping.
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Waves range from one to two meters.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            47. Surin Beach, Phuket, Thailand
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Though Thailand isn't a world-class spot, we like the location, the warm opaque waters, fresh pad Thai at street stalls and raucous nightlife.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            46. Arugam Bay, Sri Lanka
           </strong>
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="46. Great winter surfing." class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="46. Great winter surfing." class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185237-50-inline-arugam-story-top.jpg"/>
           </noscript>
           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            46. Great winter surfing.
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Not just a great surf spot with a right-hand peak, surfers here share the beach with wild monkeys and elephants that graze in the forest nearby.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            45. Surfer's Point, Barbados
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           On the southernmost tip of Barbados you'll find hollow and fast beginner-to-intermediate peaks scattered along the bay.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Waves face both left and right.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <a href="http://travel.cnn.com/explorations/play/natural-wonder-bucket-list-50-spectacular-places-see-639593">
            50 awesome, amazing natural wonders
           </a>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            44. The Bubble, Fuerteventura, Canary Islands
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           On the north shore off the coast of El Jablito, this expert right-hand tube works best when it's head-high to double overhead.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Similar to many of the waves in the Canaries, the only thing that hurts this reef break are aggressive locals who resent giving visiting surfers a turn on "their" wave. Visitors may find themselves in a paddling race to get a shot at riding this classic face.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            43. Black's Beach, San Diego, California
           </strong>
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="43. Strong surfing." class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="43. Strong surfing." class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115747-50-inline-blacks-beach-story-top.jpg"/>
           </noscript>
           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            43. Strong surfing.
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           One for seasoned professionals, Black's Beach is one of the most powerful breaks in California.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           San Diego's best surfers flock here; it can get crowded on weekends.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            42. Cape Hatteras Lighthouse, Buxton, North Carolin
            <strong>
            </strong>
            a
           </strong>
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="42. Cape Hatteras: historic ride." class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="42. Cape Hatteras: historic ride." class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130625115907-50-inline-cape-hatteras-story-top.jpg"/>
           </noscript>
           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            42. Cape Hatteras: historic ride.
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Cape Hatteras Lighthouse has been popular since the 1970s and renowned for good surf caused by deadly hurricanes, which have over the years caused numerous shipwrecks and given it the nickname "the Atlantic graveyard."
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            41. Surf pool, United Arab Emirate
            <strong>
            </strong>
            s
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           The only artificial surf site to make the list, the wave pool at
           <a href="http://www.wadiadventure.ae" target="_blank">
            Wadi Adventure
           </a>
           in the United Arab Emirates has converted its share of skeptics, inspiring some to proclaim it the future of surfing.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           The surf pool can be adjusted to create rights, lefts and even barrels.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           "It may not be the best wave in the world, but it's the best wave pool in the world," says Surfing Magazine editor in chief Taylor Paul.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            40. Biarritz, France
           </strong>
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="40. Year-round action. " class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="40. Year-round action. " class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130624185437-50-inline-biarritz-story-top.jpg"/>

           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            40. Year-round action.
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Surfers have been hitting this spot since the 1950s.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           It pumps all year around thanks to ferocious North Atlantic swells that make their way down the Gulf de Gascogne.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            39. Ponta Preta, Maio, Cape Verde
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           In Portuguese "Ponta Preta" means black point, and if this was a ski slope it would most definitely be a black run.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           This break over exposed reef offers thrilling rides in winter for expert surfers.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            38. Tofino, Vancouver Island, Canada
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           The surfing capital of Canada offers a picturesque break on the west coast of the island.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Unique feature: it's surrounded by a looming rainforest.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Suitable for all abilities, the only prerequisite is a thick wetsuit -- it gets cold out there.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <strong>
            37. Byron Bay, New South Wales, Australia
           </strong>
          </p>
