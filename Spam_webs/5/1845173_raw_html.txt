         <h1 class="headline">
          Best-selling business book web sites
         </h1>
         <!-- BEGIN .article -->
         <div class="article">
          <p>
           If youre writing a business book  and want it to be a best seller  then youre probably also working on a web site to accompany your book. If you take a look at the
           <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/best-sellers-books/2013-08-04/hardcover-business-books/list.html" target="_blank">
            top 15
            <em>
             NY Times
            </em>
            Best-Selling Hardcover Business Books for August 2013
           </a>
           , youll see that most of the books have their own site. Some authors provide tools and resources that accompany their book. Others provide an opportunity to join a cause or get more involved in communities.
          </p>
          <p>
           Creating a book web site is an essential part of your marketing strategy. And  even if theres no dedicated site  at a minimum youll need to create marketing materials for your own site, your publisher and retail sites like Amazon.com.
          </p>
          <p>
           For ideas and inspiration, heres links to the web sites for the top 15

            <em>

            s Best Business Books of 2012
           </a>
