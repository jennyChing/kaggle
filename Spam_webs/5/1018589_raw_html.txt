                     <p>
                      <font face="Verdana">
                       <b>
                        ~ Golden Glow ~
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Someone flicked the switch,
                       <br/>
                       And light dispelled the dark,
                       <br/>
                       As the glow of morning
                       <br/>
                       Over came the night.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       In you I saw the dawning
                       <br/>
                       As you let eternity inside,
                       <br/>
                       Just like a stained glass window
                       <br/>
                       Glows with purest light behind.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       To banish cold we are attracted
                       <br/>
                       To a fire glowing warm,
                       <br/>
                       So my heart knows where to go
                       <br/>
                       As to your joy I'm drawn.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       You opened up your heart
                       <br/>
                       And God's truth became your guide,
                       <br/>
                       So your life shines forth a beauty
                       <br/>
                       Of treasure to be mined.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Like a diamond cut to shape
                       <br/>
                       Reflects intrinsic light,
                       <br/>
                       So the promises of God in you
                       <br/>
                       Are such a wondrous sight.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       When you let the Lord invade your soul
                       <br/>
                       I thanked Him for His gift,
                       <br/>
                       Of your light and life and warmth
                       <br/>
                       From the tender love you give.
                      </font>
                     </p>

                        The path of the righteous
                        <br/>
                        is like the first gleam of dawn,
                        <br/>
                        shining ever brighter
                        <br/>
                        till the full light of day.
                        <br/>
                        Proverbs 4:18
                        <br/>
                        <br/>

                        <br/>

                        <br/>

                        <br/>

                        <br/>

                        <br/>

                        <br/>
