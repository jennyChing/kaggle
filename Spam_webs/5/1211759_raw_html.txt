              <h2 class="news-deck">
               Download the theme now for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, or PlayStation Vita
              </h2>
              <p>
               As part of its ongoing celebration of PlayStations 20th anniversary, Sony has released a free theme for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, and PlayStation Vita that adds Original PlayStation sounds.
              </p>
              <p>
               YouTube user Ray Palafox II (via Kotaku) captured and uploaded the footage you see below of the special start-up sounds and more. The theme comes bundled with the PS4s latest firmware update.
              </p>
              <p>
               The Original PlayStation was released in Japan on December 3, 1994. To mark the occasion, Sony also this week announced a super-rare PS4 system modeled after the Original PlayStation.
              </p>
