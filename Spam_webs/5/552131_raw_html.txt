               </p>
               <p>
                Donia - the game is a simple mouse controlled game. move you cursor onto the screen and it becomes isora the older sister. push her into stone monsters to destroy them. rocks will be hidden on the floor so destroy them too. your aim is to get the other sister muku home safely without letting any monsters touch her.
               </p>
               <p>
                Jin- I'm not sure whether in your country of origin whether you have a ground floor so the animal you want could be either the butterfly (the very first creature you should find but it's very small) or the turtle (which I believe is the 3rd creature and is right before a hole normally).
               </p>
               <p>
                alex - if you keep knocking muku in to the holes don't worry you're not alone. try to exaggerate your movements to jump OVER muku not go through her.
               </p>
               <p>
                maoman - you didn't really ask a question but when fighting the boss focus all you attention pretty much on him. his eyes gets smaller once then he closes his eye, when this happens focus on the stone monster going after muku. also push muku to the end right at the start before the battle really begins to give you the most time to finish. only focus on the invisible ones when they show up, don't waste time on them waiting for them to become solid.
               </p>
               <p>
                anonymous- if you manage to finish without any of the lifes having been saved OR used you return to an empty house with all the walls grey and stark and I don't think you get the 'congratulations either. also muku and isora will not be smiling and happy at the end.
               </p>
               <p>
                and for the holes try to move with the screen!
               </p>

                hope this helps everybody!
