        <p>
         Yesterday I came across the Lays
         <strong>
          <a href="https://www.dousaflavor.com/">
           Do Us A Flavor
          </a>
         </strong>
         contest: a marketing campaign which has become wildly popular across the internet over recent days.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Lays
         </strong>
          the equivalent of
         <strong>
          Walkers
         </strong>
         in the UK  run a contest like this in the USA almost every year, encouraging fans of the product to submit their own ideas for new flavours in exchange for a very decent reward. The winner gets to have their creation produced and sold all across the country, as well as one million dollars, so its no real surprise that this competition gets a lot of attention.
        </p>
        <p>
         So hows the competition going this year, then?
         <em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Well, its not going quite as planned.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Something tells me the internet isnt taking it quite as seriously this time around
        </p>
