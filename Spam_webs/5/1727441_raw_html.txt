         <h2 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
          <a href="/Medical-Spa-Boston-MA-Blog/dream-spa-medicals-clay-mask-facial-using-nature-to-achieve-soft-smooth-skin-canton-ma" title="Dream Spa Medicals Clay Mask Facial: Using nature to achieve soft smooth skin - Canton, MA">
           Dream Spa Medicals Clay Mask Facial: Using nature to achieve soft smooth skin - Canton, MA
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="post-details">
          Thursday, October 30, 2014
         </div>
         <div class="post-body">
          <img alt="" src="/images/mary-kelley.jpg" style="float: left; width: 80px; margin-right: 10px; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid;"/>
          <p>
           By: Mary Kelley
          </p>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <img alt="" src="/images/clay-mask.jpg" style="padding-left: 10px; float: right;"/>
          <p itemprop="description">
           One of my favorite masques from
           <a href="/products.htm">
            SkinCeuticals
           </a>
           is the
           <strong>
            Clarifying Clay Masque
           </strong>
           .  The namesake ingredient is a one-two punch of kaolin clay and bentonite clay. These two natural clays absorb excess oil and help remove impurities from clogged pores.
          </p>
          <p>
           The clays work synergistically with an amazing blend of hydroxy acids designed to exfoliate the skin, leaving your skin soft and clear. The addition of aloe and chamomile soothes the skin. Suitable for all skin types, this masque really benefits normal, oily, combination, and acne/congestion prone skin. I love this masque even for my dehydrated skin because my nose and forehead is so clean and clear after I rinse the masque off; and, as a bonus, my skin isnt dried out or flaky afterwards!
          </p>
          <p>
           This light masque is a great treatment for use at home either used as a weekly masque or as a spot treatment for stubborn breakouts. However, the real magic happens when we use the
           <strong>
            Clarifying Clay Masque
           </strong>
           in the treatment room with our Clay Masque Facial.  Clients who are concerned with clogged pores, oily skin, or acne love this treatment because the combination of clay and hydroxy acids leaves skin soft and pliable--perfectly prepping the skin for extractions.
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <a href="/medical-facials-and-peels.htm">
            Clay Masque Facial
           </a>
           is perfect for most skin types and conditions but is ideally suited for clients with acne, oily skin (visibly large pores), or pores that tend to become clogged. We use steam for clients who need it but the treatment is just as effective without steam, making it suitable for those who have broken capillaries on their cheeks or more sensitive skin.
          </p>
          <p>
           Afterwards, your skin is clear, clean, and glowing. This is the perfect facial to kick off the cooler months with. Youll be free of the oil and congestion that makes pores more apparent and youll have softer, smoother skin just in time for late fall/early winter events.
Book your Clay Masque Facial with
           <a href="/">
            Dream Spa Medical
           </a>
           today! Our bookings fill up quickly this time of year and wed hate for you to miss out on gorgeous skin.
          </p>
