         <h2 itemprop="name">
          Chabad Rabbi Attacked in Vienna
         </h2>
         <div class="Desc" itemprop="description">
          A Muslim man attacked a Chabad rabbi Saturday night during the annual public Chanukah menorah-lighting ceremony in Vienna, Austria.
         </div>
         <div class="Author">
          <!--&lt;script&gt;View.FontSize.Write()&lt;/script&gt;-->
          <script>
           AddthisRegularBox(0,134958,1).write();
          </script>
          By Hana Levi Julian
          <div class="clf">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="Date">
          First Publish: 12/13/2009, 2:48 PM / Last Update: 12/13/2009, 4:13 PM
         </div>
        </div>
        <br/>
        <div class="Content">
         <div class="ImageOut">
          <div class="Image">
           <img alt="" src="/static/Resizer.ashx/news/468/282/262680.jpg"/>
           <div>
            <div class="ImageDesc">
            </div>
            <div class="ImageCredit">
             courtesy of COLLive.com
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <!--INFOLINKS_ON-->
         <div id="ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_Body_Body_Body_ArticleBody">
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
           A Muslim man attacked a Chabad rabbi Saturday night as he was conducting the annual ceremony to light the public Chanukah menorah in
           <st1:street w:st="on">
            <st1:address w:st="on">
             Stefenfaltz Square
            </st1:address>
           </st1:street>
           in the city of
           <st1:city w:st="on">
            Vienna
           </st1:city>
           ,
           <st1:country-region w:st="on">
            <st1:place w:st="on">
             Austria
            </st1:place>
           </st1:country-region>
           .
           <o:p>
           </o:p>
          </span>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            The attacker hurled himself at Rabbi Dov Gruzman, principal of the citys Jewish school run by the Chabad-Lubavitch Chassidic movement, and began punching him, a local resident told
            <em>
             Arutz Sheva
            </em>
            .
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            As the rabbi tried to hold off his attacker, the Muslim suddenly bit his victim, severing part of his finger in the process. The Muslim was caught and arrested by police, and was held for questioning. The rabbi was evacuated to the hospital where doctors rushed to reattach his finger.
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            Gruzman told
            <em>
             Arutz Sheva
            </em>
            that the Muslim had raced towards the entrance at the beginning of the ceremony and began to curse the Jews who were there and the Jewish people in general. I tried to hold him off, to keep him away from the entrance and he bit me really hard, and thats how he injured me, he said.
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            The event itself did not discourage Rabbi Gruzman, and in fact strengthened his resolve. We are glad that such an event occurred, he said. Today, because of what happened, we are planning [a much larger event]. We increased the number of sufganiot [Chanukah jelly doughnuts] from 50 to 700  and this is our answer to the attack and to anti-Semitism.
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            However, the rabbi himself will be unable to participate in Sunday evenings ceremony due to the fact that he is still hospitalized, and being monitored for infection from the bite.
           </span>
           <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">
            Nevertheless, he emphasized, through the Chanukah lighting ceremony he sends his blessing to every Jew around the world.
            <o:p>
            </o:p>
           </span>
          </p>
