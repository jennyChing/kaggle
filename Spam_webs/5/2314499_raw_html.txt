              <p>
               Yesterday marked the last exam of my final semester in Engineering course.Engineering is easily the challenging thing Ive done but it feels amazing to accomplish. I havent flunked out on any subjects for the last threesemesters and successfully completed the finalsemester of Electronic &amp; Communication EngineeringCurrently upholding 68%  6th semester. Hopefully with the final semester results, I will be crossing 70% mark.
              </p>
              <h6>
               Bragging about Projects?
              </h6>
              <p>
               Handed in my fourth year dissertation last month! Im the one in charge of this project on Embedded Systems, but Im really happy with the way it turned out. It was a lot of fun, not as high a standard as I would have liked however feel I did well.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://ocw.mit.edu/" target="_blank">
                MIT OpenCourseWare
               </a>
               &amp;
               <a href="http://nptel.ac.in/" target="_blank">
                NPTEL
               </a>
               saved my life in Radio Communications, Analog &amp; Digital Communications and many more subjects in these 4 year course. Im thankful for that.
              </p>
              <p>
               Im broadening my horizons and learning to cooperate and work with people who are different than me, In other words I leave for Bhopal in two weeks for my Interview to get into Indian Army which has been my dreamsince I was a kid. Once I clear this Interview, training for solid 6 months &amp; then Ill be able to brag about its successful completion too.Im also applying for various positions in both private &amp; Govt sectors where Ill be exposed to different atmosphere &amp; work culture.
              </p>
              <p>
               Social life is better than ever on the other hand, feeling a lot more confident and doing waaaaaaay better with girls! So all things considered this semester has been a success!
              </p>
