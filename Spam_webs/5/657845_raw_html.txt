          World of Warcraft: Azeroth by the Numbers
         </span>
        </h2>

         <div class="detail" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           From the rise of Onyxia to the fight for Pandaria, more than 100 million of you across the globe have visited Azeroth since the first realms went live in 2004. Weve gathered some epic statistics to show what youve been up to all this time.
          </p>
          <p>
           Curious about the faction breakdown among the 500 million characters youve created? Wonder how many instances your fellow adventurers run every day, or which pet is the most popular? Take a look at World of Warcrafts first official infographic below for some fun and surprising never-before-seen facts about the game.
          </p>
          <p>
           We hope everyone enjoys this glimpse at the inner workings of Azerothfeel free to find and share it on
           <a href="http://www.facebook.com/Warcraft" target="_blank">
            Facebook
           </a>
           and
           <a href="https://twitter.com/Warcraft" target="_blank">
            Twitter
           </a>
           . Thanks for being a part of World of Warcrafts history, and heres to new adventures with you in Warlords of Draenor!
          </p>
          <p>
           If you'd like to view the infographic in a separate window,
           <a href="http://media.wow-europe.com/infographic/en/world-of-warcraft-infographic.html" target="_blank">
            click here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
