          Carnival of Cities for 21 May 2014

          <p>
           Welcome to the Carnival of Cities blog carnival, where we tour the world in a singlepost, via submissions from a variety of different blogs, all about any aspect of one, single city or fair-sized town.
          </p>
          <p>
           Weve shifted to publishing monthly, normally on the 3rd Wednesday of the month, and the next edition will be hosted on my personal blog
           <a href="http://www.sheilasguide.com/" target="_blank">
            Sheilas Guide
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           If you would like to host a future Carnival edition on your blog, please contact me as the Carnival organizer: Sheila at sheilascarborough dot com. You do need to register on the carnival administration website (its easy and free) to submit to this or any blog carnival.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="broken_link" href="http://www.sheilasguide.com/the-carnival-of-cities-blog-carnival/" target="_blank" title="Since 2007!">
            Heres more info and background about the Carnival of Cities blog carnival
           </a>
           , and now.
          </p>
          <p>
           Off we go!
          </p>
          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Cities in Europe
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <b>
            Falkirk, Scotland
           </b>
           Karen Bryanphotographs the giant
           <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2014/05/19/the-kelpies-at-the-helix-falkirk/">
            The Kelpies at the Helix, Falkirk
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog">
            Europe A La Carte Blog
           </a>
           , saying, The Kelpies sculptures in Falkirk, Scotland, depicting the mythical Scottish water horse, are the worlds biggest equine sculptures.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Amsterdam, the Netherlands
           </b>
           Adam Groffman has advice about
           <a href="http://www.eatingamsterdamtours.com/blog/hotels-amsterdam-jordaan/">
            Where to Stay in Amsterdams Jordaan Neighborhood
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.eatingamsterdamtours.com/">
            Eating Amsterdam
           </a>
           , saying, Amsterdams prettiest neighborhood (its where youll find some of the citys most scenic canals) is also a great place to stay. These boutique hotels will make your weekend in Amsterdam that much more memorable!
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Budapest, Hungary
           </b>
           Adelina Wongasks
           <a href="http://packmeto.com/visiting-thermal-bath-in-budapest/">
            So You Want to Visit the Thermal Baths in Budapest?
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://packmeto.com/">
            Pack Me To
           </a>
           , saying, Everyone tells you to visit a thermal bath when youre in Budapest, but the overall experience can be incredibly confusing and disorienting. Here are some tips to make your visit effortless so you dont have wander around lost and confused.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Rome, Italy
           </b>
           MummyT suggests
           <a href="http://www.escapeartistes.com/2014/05/07/10-things-you-should-do-in-rome-with-kids-or-without/">
            10 Things You Should Do in Rome (With Kids or Without)
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.escapeartistes.com/">
            EscapeArtistes
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Prague, Czech Republic
           </b>
           Jennifer Miner shows you how to see
           <a href="http://thevacationgals.com/prague-best-just-two-days-weekend/">
            The Best of Prague In Just Two Days
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://thevacationgals.com/">
            The Vacation Gals
           </a>
           , saying, The best of Prague can be seen and experienced in just two days, with the proper itinerary or a knowledgeable guided tour company.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            London, England
           </b>
           Adampresents
           <a href="http://mygaytravelguide.com/enjoy-london-things-to-do-2139">
            3 Great Ways to Enjoy London
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://mygaytravelguide.com/">
            My Gay Travel Guide
           </a>
           , saying, I share my top 3 tips on how to make the most out of a short trip to London  including where to eat, shop and stay.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Siena, Italy
           </b>
           Cacinda Maloney takes us
           <a href="http://www.pointsandtravel.com/deep-into-the-secret-lair-of-sienas-contrade-system/">
            Deep into the Secret Lair of Sienas Contrade System
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.pointsandtravel.com/">
            PointsandTravel.com
           </a>
           , saying, Siena seems, at first glance, like a typical Italian city, but with a deeper look, you can see there is nothing typical about it. I got lucky and was privy to one of the most unusual neighborhood groups (The Noble Contrada del Bruco) I have ever seen. Come along with me as I explore the contrade system in Siena, that has been going on since the Middle Ages.
          </p>
          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Cities in Asia
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <b>
            Sendai, Japan
           </b>
           Cassie Kifer has fun
           <a href="http://www.everintransit.com/sendai-date-busho-tai/">
            Drinking Tea With a Ninja and a Samurai
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.everintransit.com/">
            Ever In Transit
           </a>
           , saying, An article about the unique way Sendai, Japan welcomes tourists to their city.
          </p>
          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Cities in the Americas
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <b>
            Albuquerque, New Mexico, USA
           </b>
           Travis explores
           <a href="http://flashpackerhq.com/spotlight-albuquerque-new-mexico-usa/">
            Albuquerque, New Mexico, USA
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://flashpackerhq.com/">
            Flashpacker HQ
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Las Vegas, Nevada, USA
           </b>
           Julienne de Leon goes clubbing at the Cosmopolitans
           <a href="http://a-social-life.com/2014/02/03/rose-rabbit-lie/">
            Rose. Rabbit. Lie
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://a-social-life.com/">
            a Social Life.
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Montevideo, Uruguay
           </b>
           Justin covers
           <a href="http://rootofgood.com/argentina-uruguay-montevideo/">
            Argentina and Uruguay Trip Part 1  Montevideo
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://rootofgood.com/">
            Root of Good
           </a>
           , saying, An overview of our trip to Montevideo, the capital city of Uruguay. A good highlight of all the sites, bites, and sounds of Montevideo from the parilla grilled meats to watching a live performance of Macbeth at the Teatro Solis!
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Key Biscayne, Florida, USA
           </b>
           Meredith Lambert checks out the
           <a href="http://merevin.com/lighthouse-at-bill-baggs-florida-state-park/">
            Lighthouse at Bill Baggs Florida State Park
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://merevin.com/">
            Merevin
           </a>
           , saying, A beautiful escape; the busy city of Miami is only 15 minutes away.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
           </b>
           Laurence Norah details the
           <a href="http://www.findingtheuniverse.com/2014/05/highlights-of-visit-to-rio-de-janeiro.html">
            Highlights of a Visit to Rio de Janeiro
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.findingtheuniverse.com/search/label/Travel">
            Finding the universe
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Morro Bay, California, USA
           </b>
           Nancy Brown reveals all in
           <a href="http://www.nancydbrown.com/2014/05/09/morro-bay-things-to-do.html">
            Morro Bay: Things to Do
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.nancydbrown.com/">
            Nancy D Brown
           </a>
           , saying, Are you headed to Californias central coast? Travel writer Nancy D. Brown shares her insider tips to discovering Morro Bay.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Lake Placid, New York, USA
           </b>
           Kim Rielly goes across generations in
           <a href="http://www.lakeplacid.com/blog/2013/08/family-fun-3-generations-and-4-days">
            Family Fun: 3 generations and 4 days
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.lakeplacid.com/blog">
            Lake Placid, NY  Adirondacks blogs
           </a>
           , saying, An interview with a family who chose Lake Placid, Adirondacks USA as the perfect destination to celebrate a 50th wedding anniversary!
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Toledo, Ohio, USA
           </b>
           Dominique King says that
           <a href="http://www.midwestguest.com/2014/05/new-penguin-beach-and-flamingo-key-exhibits-debut-at-toledo-zoo-on-may-23.html">
            Penguin Beach and Flamingo Key exhibits debut at Toledo Zoo on May 23
           </a>
           posted at
           <a href="http://www.midwestguest.com/">
            Midwest Guest
           </a>
           , saying, Penguins and flamingos take center stage as the Toledo Zoo in northern Ohio opens two new exhibits this weekendand I don a hard hat to check out the ongoing renovation of the zoos historic Aquarium building.
          </p>

           That concludes this edition, and thanks very much for visiting and supporting the Carnival.
