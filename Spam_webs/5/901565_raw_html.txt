       <h1 class="entry-title">
        The Magic and Mystery of Secret Passageways
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.barefootfloor.com/blog/index.php/2010/09/the-magic-and-mystery-of-secret-passageways/" rel="bookmark" title="11:08 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          September 20, 2010
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.barefootfloor.com/blog/index.php/author/" title="View all posts by ">
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_558" style="width: 268px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/Secret-playroom-behind-wardrobe.jpg">
          <img alt="Narnia Wardrobe Playroom" class="size-medium wp-image-558" height="300" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/Secret-playroom-behind-wardrobe-258x300.jpg" width="258"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Narnia Wardrobe Playroom
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Children love exploring new places, and every little crevice they can fit inside is a special window to another world. Their powerful imaginations fill them with joy as they play pretend. When I was little, I would crawl into the back of my dads van and imagine myself as a stowaway on a pirate ship. Fans of The
         <em>
          Lion, The Witch and the Wardobe
         </em>
         or
         <em>
          The Secret Garden
         </em>
         surely remember the pleasures of discovering hidden places. The mystery of a secret room holds magic in it. Lets not forget 12 Grimmauld Place from Harry Potter. It conceals through an illusion of rundown houses an entire underground market only magic-users can perceive. A hidden room isnt just a great gift. To a child, its real magic.
        </p>
        <p>
         With
         <a href="http://www.hiddenpassageway.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Creative Home Engineering
         </a>
         , we can actually turn our homes into a gothic mansion from mystery novels with secret hallways and hidden rooms. The bookshelf door can be operated with either a book that you pull or a button on a remote.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_561" style="width: 481px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/hidden-fire.jpg">
          <img alt="Secret Fireplace" class="size-full wp-image-561" height="243" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/hidden-fire.jpg" width="471"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Secret Fireplace
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_564" style="width: 478px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/secret-doors.jpg">
          <img alt="Secret Doors by Creative Home Engineering" class="size-full wp-image-564" height="568" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/secret-doors.jpg" width="468"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Secret Doors by Creative Home Engineering
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_562" style="width: 498px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/secrebottshelf.jpg">
          <img alt="Secret Bookshelf door by Creative Home Engineering" class="size-full wp-image-562" height="350" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/secrebottshelf.jpg" width="488"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Secret Bookshelf door by Creative Home Engineering
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         On a practical note, secret passageways can protect our family members from home invaders. When the Palace of Versailles was stormed during the French Revolution, Marie Antoinette escaped a violent mob in her bedroom through a secret passage concealed behind the walls.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_560" style="width: 478px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/winerack.jpg">
          <img alt="A wine rack that is not what it seems" class="size-full wp-image-560" height="323" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/winerack.jpg" width="468"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          A wine rack that is not what it seems
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         We always see in movies people hiding beneath the floorboards of a rickety farmhouse. One is so much more protected behind something that looks like a heavy bookshelf. The family heirlooms can also be protected here. A small nook behind a bookshelf offers extra protection for your
         <a href="http://www.barefootfloor.com/shop/shopdisplayproducts.asp?Search=YES">
          safe
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_556" style="width: 504px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/31-494x320.jpg">
          <img alt="Door Beneath Stairs by Creative Home Engineering" class="size-full wp-image-556" height="320" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/31-494x320.jpg" width="494"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Door Beneath Stairs by Creative Home Engineering
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Live like a secret agent. Dont be afraid to embrace eccentricity. People might call you crazy, but who cares when youre having fun. Its so Max Smart. If youve got the money, you can install layers of these secret doors that delve into a high tech underground laboratory. I personally want to get a custom phone booth with a trap door and a telephone in my shoe, but the possibilities are endless. Think Batcave. Think V for Vendetta. Turn your home into
         <em>
          Northanger Abbey
         </em>
         . I envision a secret passage through a victorian mirror ala Phantom of the Opera, but whatever you can think of, Creative Home Engineering can build it. The thrilling addition of secret passageways has me so excited, I might start keeping my safe behind a picture frame to get started.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_557" style="width: 554px">
         <a href="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/hidden-safe.jpg">
          <img alt="Large Secret Door by Creative Home Engineering" class="size-full wp-image-557" height="287" src="http://blog-images.barefootfloor.com/images/2010/09/hidden-safe.jpg" width="544"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Large Secret Door by Creative Home Engineering
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
