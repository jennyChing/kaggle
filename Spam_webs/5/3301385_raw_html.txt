     <p>
      Oatmeal cookie shot in this post will give you how to make it in a great taste. But let me remind you that you dont drink it too many. With this recipe, you could make it for about one minute. I am sure you have a lot of time to do. Keep in mind that this recipe could be available for 1 serving.
     </p>
     <p>
      Ingredients:
     </p>
     <p>
      1/2 fluid ounce: butterscotch schnapps
      <br/>
      1/2 fluid ounce: Irish cream liqueur
      <br/>
      1/2 fluid ounce: jagermeister liqueur
      <br/>
      1/2 fluid ounce: cinnamon schnapps
     </p>
     <p>
      Step:
     </p>
     <p>
      Take a shot glass and then layer butterscotch schnapps, Jagermeister and Irish cream.
      <br/>
      On top, float the cinnamon schnapps.
     </p>
     <p>
      That is all. Very easy, isnt it? It is very possible for you to use the fireball cinnamon whiskey to substitute the schnapps and it works. It doesnt bring any regret at all for you who want to try it and sure you will love it. The cinnamon liquor could be use just like Goldschlager. What makes you will love this recipe is it is perfect for winter time. All that you need is to serve this over ice on cold or snowy night. So, we are totally agreed if you could make it even though there is no Jagermeister. Now, for your party you could make a big hit with regular cinnamon schnapps and also red hot or other varieties. Some people love adding gingersnaps. Another alternative you may try is ignoring the jagermeister and blend the rest of liquors with vanilla ice cream. Surely you will love it because its taste is so yummy.
     </p>
