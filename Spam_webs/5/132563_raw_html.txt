Treat your family and friends to this simple dinner that will be able to please everyone!

          this cookbook

         with enough chicken recipes for you to have a new way to cook chicken each day of the year!

        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 can drained whole kernel corn
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup cooked chicken, finely chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1, lightly beaten egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tablespoons melted butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon pepper
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1-3/4 cups all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Oil for deep-fat frying
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            GREEN CHILI SAUCE:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup cubed butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon pepper
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon garlic powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon ground cumin
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 can chopped green chilies
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Shredded cheddar cheese (optional)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Take a large bowl and place the corn inside. Lightly crush the corn with a potato masher.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir in the chicken, milk, egg, salt and pepper. Then pour in the flour and baking powder to combine, and stir the corn mixture until it is completely combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Take a deep-fat fryer or a skillet and heat 2 in. of the oil to 375F. Take the batter and drop by  cupfuls into the oil. Fry these for 3 minutes on each side. Make sure they are golden brown. Drain them on paper towels and keep warm.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Now take a large saucepan to melt you butter over medium-low heat. Take your flour and seasonings and stir them in until it's smooth. Add your chilies and gradually stir in your milk. Bring it to a broil, and cook and stir for two minutes, or until thickened.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Serve fritters and sprinkle with cheese (optional).
           </li>
          </ol>
