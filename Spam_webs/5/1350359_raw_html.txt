          Buon Appetito! 20 Yummy Homemade Pasta Sauces
         </h1>
         <h4 class="post-meta clearfix">
          <div class="avatar nopin">
           <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-300 photo legacy-userphoto nopin" height="300" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/userphoto/536.jpg" width="300"/>
          </div>
          <div class="posted-on-wrapper">
           <p>
            by
            <a href="//www.brit.co/author/srao/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Sonia Rao">
             <span itemprop="author">
              Sonia Rao
             </span>

            </time>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing" style="display: none">

         </h4>

        <div class="post-content inner">
         <p>
          Were pretty sure that
          <a href="//www.brit.co/oodles-of-noodles-12-healthy-pasta-dishes/" target="_blank">
           pasta
          </a>
          is one of those foods thats just universally loved by everyone. Its right up there with
          <a href="//www.brit.co/6-ways-to-trick-out-the-classic-chocolate-chip-cookie/" target="_blank">
           chocolate chip cookies
          </a>
          . Weve already shown you some
          <a href="//www.brit.co/homemade-pasta/" target="_blank">
           awesome ways to make pasta at home
          </a>
          , so why not go all out and make your own sauce, too? Make them today, and enjoy them whenever you want (just make sure to stick em in your fridge!). From tomatillo pesto to cauliflower sauce, here are 20 delectable homemade sauces thatll have you saying holy moly about your ravioli!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-630078" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Simple-Marinara-645x430.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://www.ourfamilyeats.com/2011/09/simple-red-sauce/" target="_blank">
           Simple Marinara Sauce
          </a>
          : Of course, we gotta start out with the classic. Marinara sauce will always be a favorite. (via

           Our Family Eats
          </a>
          )

         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-630104" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="493" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Roasted-Pepper-645x493.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          2.
          <a href="http://www.tablefortwoblog.com/2012/06/20/roasted-red-pepper-pesto/#.UxJrGuNdWSo" target="_blank">
           Roasted Red Pepper Pesto
          </a>
          : By the time you get to the end of this roundup, youll realize just how much we love variations on pesto. This one in particular is extra special because it has a really great roasted flavor to it. Delicious! (via
          <a href="http://www.tablefortwoblog.com/2012/06/20/roasted-red-pepper-pesto/#.UxJrGuNdWSo" target="_blank">
           Table for Two
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>

         </p>
         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.healthy-delicious.com/2010/03/linguini-with-white-clam-sauce/" target="_blank">
           White Clam Sauce
          </a>
          : Feelin fancy? Whip up some clam sauce for your pasta. Go ahead and add this to your bucket list of recipes. You wont regret it. (via
          <a href="http://www.healthy-delicious.com/2010/03/linguini-with-white-clam-sauce/" target="_blank">
           Healthy. Delicious.
          </a>
          )

         </p>

          <a href="//www.brit.co/winter-squash-recipes/" target="_blank">
           squash

           Tasty Kitchen

          <a href="http://sweetkarolineadventuresinfood.blogspot.com/2010/06/puttanesca-sauce-two-ways.html" target="_blank">

          <em>
           quite
          </em>
          a story behind it. Well go ahead and let you click the link for that. (via

           Sweet Karoline

           )
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>

         </p>
         <p>
          8.
          <a href="http://teaspooncomm.com/teaspoonofspice/2013/08/spinach-avocado-alfredo-sauce-the-recipe-redux/" target="_blank">
           Spinach Avocado Alfredo Sauce
          </a>
          : Since we cant all chug cans of spinach like Popeye, we think this delectable spinach and avocado Alfredo sauce will suffice. (via
          <a href="http://teaspooncomm.com/teaspoonofspice/2013/08/spinach-avocado-alfredo-sauce-the-recipe-redux/" target="_blank">
           Teaspoon of Spice
          </a>
          )
         </p>

          <a href="//www.brit.co/?s=kids" target="_blank">
           your kiddos
          </a>

          <a href="http://www.insockmonkeyslippers.com/pasta-with-a-sneaky-green-sauce" target="_blank">

          </a>
          )

           Shes Cookin
          </a>
          )

          <a href="http://www.hungrycouplenyc.com/2013/04/creamy-and-light-cauliflower-sauce.html" target="_blank">
           Hungry Couple
          </a>
          )

         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-630176" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Thai-Peanut-Sauce-645x430.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>

          <a href="http://shesimmers.com/2009/03/how-to-make-thai-peanut-sauce-my-moms.html" target="_blank">
           Thai Peanut Sauce
          </a>
          : So, this peanut sauce may not have been what you imagined when we first mentioned pasta. But just imagine how tasty it would be on noodles. (via
          <a href="http://shesimmers.com/2009/03/how-to-make-thai-peanut-sauce-my-moms.html" target="_blank">
           She Simmers
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-630177" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="440" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Carrot-Apple-645x440.jpg" width="645"/>

          : We always thought apples and carrots were only used together in baby food, but this beautiful sauce proved us wrong. Now we cant wait to try it out. (via
          <a href="http://www.vegalicious.org/2013/10/29/celery-root-gnocchi-with-apple-carrot-sauce/" target="_blank">
           Vegalicious
          </a>
          )

         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-630178" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="429" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Four-Cheese-645x429.jpg" width="645"/>

          <a href="http://www.christinascucina.com/2013/09/trader-joes-diner-mac-cheese-copycat.html" target="_blank">

          <a href="http://www.christinascucina.com/2013/09/trader-joes-diner-mac-cheese-copycat.html" target="_blank">

          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-630312" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="433" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Sweet-Potato-645x433.jpg" width="645"/>
