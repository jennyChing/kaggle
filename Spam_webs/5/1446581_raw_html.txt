           <img alt="Sir Krispy Love and Bongos" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-29182" height="615" src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/krispy-love-bongo.jpg" title="Sir Krispy Love and Bongos" width="550"/>
          </noscript>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Krispy Love speaks, accompanied on bongo by a friend.  Okay, it's not actually a bongo...it's an upside down Home Depot bucket.  But it worked, right?
          </p>
         </div>
        </center>
        <p>
         I look forward to getting the video and such posted so you too can appreciate the performance that just about broke me.  That is Alex, aka "Krispy Love," leading his trio of Poetry Slammers with a poem that was so good it made Wilford Brimley break down in tears.  (He wasn't in attendance that night, you understand, he can just feel sugary disturbances in The Force.)  At the end of the evening both he and the Queen made it to the Showcase Showdown, and the Queen won.  But she chose Krispy Love as her champion, knighted him, and sent Sir Krispy Love into battle with our collective foe:
         <em>
          The Final Pinata
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 560px">
          <img alt="Krispy Love vs. The Duck Dynasty Pinata" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignnone size-full wp-image-29183" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/krispy-love-duck-dynasty-pinata.jpg" height="353" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" title="Krispy Love vs. The Duck Dynasty Pinata" width="550"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Krispy Love vs. The Duck Dynasty Pinata" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-29183" height="353" src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/krispy-love-duck-dynasty-pinata.jpg" title="Krispy Love vs. The Duck Dynasty Pinata" width="550"/>
          </noscript>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           In the dictionary under the word 'glee,' you see this picture.
          </p>
         </div>
        </center>
        <p>
         The battle was joined...and was quickly over.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img alt="Queen of the Fun Fest + Dead Pinata" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignnone size-full wp-image-29184" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/queen-fun-fest-dead-pinata.jpg" height="550" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" title="Queen of the Fun Fest + Dead Pinata" width="550"/>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Queen of the Fun Fest + Dead Pinata" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-29184" height="550" src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/queen-fun-fest-dead-pinata.jpg" title="Queen of the Fun Fest + Dead Pinata" width="550"/>
         </noscript>
        </center>
        <p>
         After DragonCon, having found Sir Krispy Love on Twitter (and you can too--he's
         <a href="https://twitter.com/WattsUnfiltered" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         ), this exchange happened:
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <br/>
         <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
          <p>
           <a href="https://twitter.com/WattsUnfiltered">
            @WattsUnfiltered
           </a>
           : Well then. Your poetic mission, should you choose to accept it, is to take as your subject: the gulper eel.
           <a href="https://twitter.com/jackofools">
            @jackofools
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
            Widgett Walls (@Widgett)
           <a href="https://twitter.com/Widgett/status/507599394133139456">
            September 4, 2014
           </a>
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
         </p>
        </center>
        <br/>
        <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
        </script>
        <p>
         It was the first thing off the top of my head.  Not literally, however.  Gulper eels make terrible hats.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 470px">
          <a href="http://www.cafepress.com/+illustration_of_gulper_eel_or_pelican_trucker_hat,1104475369" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Gulper eel trucker hat" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignnone size-full wp-image-29185" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/gulper-eel-trucker-hat.jpg" height="390" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" title="Gulper eel trucker hat" width="460"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Gulper eel trucker hat" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-29185" height="390" src="http://needcoffee.cachefly.net/needcoffee/uploads/2014/09/gulper-eel-trucker-hat.jpg" title="Gulper eel trucker hat" width="460"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           This is not what I meant.
          </p>
         </div>
        </center>
        <p>
         But Krispy Love responded with something unexpected: a haiku.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <em>
           Gulper eel sounds like
           <br/>
           painful Kama Sutra.  Help!
           <br/>
           Wikipedia
          </em>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         And it was exactly what I needed.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sir Krispy Love, if you can read this, then your next mission--your next topic, should you choose to accept it: "How lasers improve everything."  (salutes)  Special thanks to Kim for snapping the majority of these pics.  The good ones.  The blurry ones are probably mine.
        </p>
