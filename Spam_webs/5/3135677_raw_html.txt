              </a>
              (yes, the Jeannie who hosted
              <a href="http://spinsuckspro.com/downloads/interactive-and-powerful-customer-service/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://spinsuckspro.com/downloads/interactive-and-powerful-customer-service/', 'our webinar');" target="_blank">
               our webinar
              </a>
              earlier this week).
             </p>
             <p>
              Id met a couple of her colleagues on Twitter and then in real life. Id heard her name from our mutual friend,
              <a href="http://www.anchoradvisors.com/pages/advisors/82.php" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anchoradvisors.com/pages/advisors/82.php', 'Brad Farris');" target="_blank">
               Brad Farris
              </a>
              , and wanted to know who was this person. When we finally met, we discovered I worked with her college roommate right after college.
             </p>
             <p>
              Talk about a small world!
             </p>
             <p>
              She quickly became one of my best friends and her sons are definitely my boyfriends.
             </p>
             <p>
              When we get together, I always take my notebook because I know there is going to be a conversation (or six) that I have to blog about. In fact, its gotten to the point where shell say, This isnt to be blogged if its meant to stay between the two of us.
             </p>
             <p>
              I asked her a hundred years ago to sit on the
              <a href="http://spinsucks.com/?s=inquisition&amp;x=0&amp;y=0" target="_blank">
               Spin Sucks Inquisition
              </a>
              hot seat. It just so happened I scheduled the same week as her webinar.
             </p>
             <p>
              Welcome to Jeannie Walters week!
             </p>
             <h3>
              What is the Biggest Mistake Youve Made in Your Career?
             </h3>
             <p>
              The biggest mistake I made was thinking everything would stay the same for a while.
             </p>
             <p>
              I was in a business that was caught up in the unexpected and very fast-moving financial crisis in 2008. We thought we had a stable situation and it changed drastically in one weekend, we were left wondering what to do.
             </p>
             <p>
              Ill never forget that lesson.
             </p>
             <h3>
              What is One Thing about Yourself that Would Surprise Most People?
             </h3>
             <p>
              Im not as organized or OCD as people think. Im hyper-organized about certain parts of my life (hello, color-coded calendar!), but I let other stuff get messy.
             </p>
             <p>
              My life is really complicated with a husband who travels often right before or after I do, two active kids, and lots of great business stuff.
             </p>
             <p>
              That means sometimes my breakfast dishes sit all day or I let the inbox overflow.
             </p>
             <p>
              Im not always ok with it, but its how it has to be.
             </p>
             <p>
              Oh, and people also might not know that Justin Timberlake is my boyfriend.
             </p>
             <h3>
              If You Could Achieve Everything You Ever Wanted in Life, but Had to Die 10 Years Sooner, Would You Make that Trade?
             </h3>
             <p>
              I dont think I would, but thats because I think goals change.
             </p>
             <p>
              When Im 80, Ill want to do some cool stuff when Im 90.
             </p>
             <p>
              Whos to say when weve truly achieved?
             </p>
             <h3>
              What Industry Advice or Practice Would You Most Like to Cry Foul On?
             </h3>
             <p>
              Customer communications that are purposefully deceptive or overly complicated.
             </p>
             <p>
              Company communicators think theyre clever and create processes that are built to confuse.
             </p>
             <p>
              Its just wrong. And mean. And annoying. I really, really dislike it.
             </p>
             <p>
              Insurance and medical bills come to mind. We would never talk to one another that way. Why send it in writing?
             </p>
             <h3>
              What is the Best Book Youve Read in the Past Six Months?
             </h3>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Spin-Sucks-Communication-Reputation-Management-ebook/dp/B00IJYFDQ0/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1401816565&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=spin+sucks" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Spin-Sucks-Communication-Reputation-Management-ebook/dp/B00IJYFDQ0/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1401816565&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=spin+sucks', 'Spin Sucks');" target="_blank">
               Spin Sucks
              </a>
              ? Is that the right answer? (It is great!)
             </p>
             <p>
              Im also reading Emanuel Rosens newest book,
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Absolute-Value-Influences-Customers-Information-ebook/dp/B00DB361CE/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1401816555&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=Absolute+Value" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Absolute-Value-Influences-Customers-Information-ebook/dp/B00DB361CE/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1401816555&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=Absolute+Value', 'Absolute Value');" target="_blank">
               Absolute Value
              </a>
              , which is really interesting.
             </p>
             <p>
              And Ive enjoyed re-reading
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/The-Trumpet-Swan-full-color/dp/0064410943/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1401816597&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=The+Trumpet+of+the+Swan" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/The-Trumpet-Swan-full-color/dp/0064410943/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1401816597&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=The+Trumpet+of+the+Swan', 'The Trumpet of the Swan');" target="_blank">
               The Trumpet of the Swan
              </a>
              with my son.
             </p>
             <p>
              Sorry. Thats three books. Im not always great at following directions.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Why Does Spin Suck?
             </h3>
             <p>
              Spin is deceptive. Its about assuming the people youre trying to spin are dumb, uniformed, or worse.
             </p>
             <p>
              We live in an age when anyone can access information which used to be reserved for the elite. Why not embrace that and welcome more people into the process? Thats where real magic happens.
             </p>
