       <p class="qm_tag_p">
        <span class="qm_tag_bold">
         Did you know that a 4-year-old child might ask as many as 437 questions or more every  
day?
        </span>
        This is because their brains are growing. They are at the age when a child is  
noticing their world and learning about all the nuances of their culture and other people's  
culture. They notice everything and ask why something is the way it is. For adults it could  
annoy, but it is essential for a child to understand. Children are naturally curious. They  
want to know how tall they are. It is a mark of pride for a child when they grow taller.  
They dream of being bigger and doing those special things that they have always wanted to  
do. They want to be like adults. To cultivate this curiosity by using a growth chart is a  
great way to harness the energy of asking questions into answers and wonder. The
        <a href="http://www.quickmedical.com/quick-medical-brand-wall-growth-height-chart-qm338.html" title="QuickMedical QM338 Wall Growth Chart">
         QM 338  
Wall Growth Chart
        </a>
        has polyester laminated covering for easy cleanup and measures 0-78  
inches or 0-198 centimeters. Putting this growth chart in a school would boost children's  
natural curiosity and confidence level. They could bond with their classmates who are  
growing taller. It could create a tight-knit community in which the older kids encourage the  
younger ones by making them feel content with their current height and the hope that they  
will grow taller.
       </p>
       <br/>
       <p class="qm_tag_p">
        Did you know that nutrition is so important for a child's intellectual development that  
the United States government put programs in place to make sure each child has at least one  
healthy, balanced meal a day? This meal is usually breakfast, because the relationship  
between breakfast and improved learning has been clearly shown.
       </p>
       <br/>
       <p class="qm_tag_p">
        Did you know that
        <a href="http://www.quickmedical.com/laminated_wall_growth_charts.html" title="Laminated Wall Growth Charts">
         wall growth charts
        </a>
        at QuickMedical are an affordable and valuable resources for schools and other facilities with growing children?
       </p>
