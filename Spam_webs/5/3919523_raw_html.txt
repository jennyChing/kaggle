         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Gioteck Announce Their New Range of Affordable Products at E3 2014
         </h1>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <strong>
           Gioteck brought their A game to E3 2014 as they announced a new range of products compatible across old and new-gen, the range will offer gamers innovative, well-designed products at affordable prices.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The 15 new products within this range will be released in 2014, including headsets, gaming chairs, cables and charging solutions, and will be showcasing a selection at E3 2014, taking place between 10th  12th, in the Concourse Hall.
         </p>
         <p>
          The new range highlights Giotecks focus on striking design and features that will appeal to core and casual gamers alike. Featuring wireless technology, Bluetooth, Darklight illumination and enhanced audio as well as great ergonomics, the range boasts price points starting at just 29.99 ($39.99) for new-gen stereo headsets.
         </p>
         <p>
          Below are some specific products worthy of note but there will be more products to announce over the coming days and weeks! 2014 is looking exciting with Gioteck!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Headsets:
           </em>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           HC-2 Wired Stereo Headset:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/news.xlcgamingnetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/headset_stereo_wired_pg5copy_zpse52d73c0.jpg">
           <img alt="headset_stereo_wired_pg5copy_zpse52d73c0" class="shadowround" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/news.xlcgamingnetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/headset_stereo_wired_pg5copy_zpse52d73c0.jpg?resize=640%2C1005"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Designed specifically for new-gen consoles, the wired HC-2 headset delivers quality design and competitive pricing. With high-quality digital game and chat sound, the headset also features deep cushioned ear-cups and headband for comfort during gameplay.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Compatible on PS4, Xbox One, PS3, Xbox 360, PC, PS Vita, Mac, Tablet/Mobile
          </li>
          <li>
           RRP UK 29.99 / US $39.99
          </li>
          <li>
           High quality digital game and chat sound
          </li>
          <li>
           40mm speaker drivers for depth in sound quality
          </li>
          <li>
           Flexible mic boom
          </li>
          <li>
           LED illumination
          </li>
          <li>
           Stylish design and soft touch finishes
          </li>
          <li>
           Simple set-up for plug and play experience
          </li>
          <li>
           Single cable connection to Playstation 4 game pad and Xbox One audio adaptor* using 3.5mm (*not included in box)
          </li>
          <li>
           Deep cushioned, adjustable headband
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <strong>
           HC-5 Wireless Stereo Headset:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://i1.wp.com/news.xlcgamingnetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/headset_stereo_wireless_pg15acopy_zpsad374142.jpg">
           <img alt="headset_stereo_wireless_pg15acopy_zpsad374142" class="shadowround" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/news.xlcgamingnetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/headset_stereo_wireless_pg15acopy_zpsad374142.jpg?resize=593%2C1024"/>
          </a>
