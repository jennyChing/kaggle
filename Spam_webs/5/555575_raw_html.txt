         <p>
          Having a day off of a 5-day workweek sounds like a mini-vacation but I actually spent the entire day cooking and doing some major cleaning around the house. Ever since I started working, there is just no time to clean! But seeing that I had some down time, I busted out the vacuum, the swifter, and the 409. Its seriously baffling as to how dust accumulates so quickly! No matter how often I clean, theres just so much dust that piles up on every thing around the house! Ugh.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-267">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Baked Brown Sugar Chicken Wings with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce - Amazingly crisp, baked wings served with a creamy dipping sauce!" class="size-full wp-image-5071 aligncenter" height="411" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/IMG_0584.jpg" width="650"/>

           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>

             <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
              Baked Brown Sugar Chicken Wings with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <p id="zlrecipe-prep-time">
               Prep Time
               <span content="PT15M" itemprop="prepTime">
                15 minutes
               </span>
              </p>
              <p id="zlrecipe-cook-time">
               Cook Time
               <span content="PT25M" itemprop="cookTime">
                25 minutes
               </span>
              </p>
              <p id="zlrecipe-total-time">
               Total Time
               <span content="PT40M" itemprop="totalTime">
                40 minutes
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
               Yield
               <span itemprop="recipeYield">
                4 servings
               </span>
              </p>

             </div>

            <div class="img-desc-wrap">
             <p class="t-a-c hide-card">
              <img alt="Baked Brown Sugar Chicken Wings with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce" class="photo" itemprop="image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/IMG_0592.jpg" style="width: 180px;" title="Baked Brown Sugar Chicken Wings with Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce"/>
             </p>
             <div id="zlrecipe-summary" itemprop="description">
              <p class="summary italic">
               Amazingly crisp wings baked to perfection served alongside an incredibly creamy dipping sauce that everyone will love!
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 pounds chicken wings
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 tablespoons olive oil
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 teaspoons brown sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 teaspoon garlic powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 teaspoon chili powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 teaspoon smoked paprika
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 teaspoon onion powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
              Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             </li>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9">
              For the Roasted Red Pepper Cream Sauce
             </div>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/3 cup roasted red pepper, chopped
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 ounces goat cheese
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 tablespoons sour cream
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 teaspoon salt
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ul class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              To make the dipping sauce, combine roasted red pepper, goat cheese, sour cream and salt in the bowl of a food processor until emulsified; set aside.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              In a large bowl, combine wings, olive oil, brown sugar, garlic powder, chili powder, paprika, onion powder, salt and pepper, to taste.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place wings onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes, using metal tongs to turn at halftime.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Serve wings immediately with dipping sauce.
             </li>
            </ul>
