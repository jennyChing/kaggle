  <p>
   Emphasis on typography, roomy widgets, muted color palette, one-colored or blurred background, grid or horizontal stripe layout, inornate graphics, 2d illustrations  all these can be easily attributed to
   <a href="http://designmodo.com/flat-design/">
    flat style
   </a>
   , that recently got its second rebirth.
  </p>
  <div class="figure">
   <a href="http://designmodo.com/modern-ui/">
    Microsofts Metro
   </a>
   aesthetic has captured the minds of a great deal of designers, pushing them on eschewing shadows, highlights, gradients, various effects and even textures. Such overwhelming infatuation with simplicity and ingenuousness has been reflected not only on web design, but also on mobile design, that traditionally has been known for its skeuomorphism.
   <p>
    Today contemporary mobile app UI is inspired by time-tasted Swiss design that beautifully intersects with modern Windows 8-style, that as a result gives us interface, which is generally focused on typography and huge vibrant monochromatic blocks.
   </p>

&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Hunt a Place&lt;/strong&gt; is a comprehensive exploration in flat style. Interface comprises majority of aspects of it, including blurred background, plain monotonous icons, tiny font and a lot of free space.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/Hunt-a-Place/8316605" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101430" alt="Hunt a Place" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hunt-a-Place.jpg" width="560" height="716" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;App | Trekd Concept by Thomas Le Corre&lt;/strong&gt; exudes simplicity and cleanliness, adding a spacious feeling due to relatively small type and rather huge widget areas.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/App-Trekd-Concept/7746907" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101431" alt="Trekd Concept by Thomas Le Corre" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Trekd-Concept-by-Thomas-Le-Corre.jpg" width="560" height="434" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Lucid Dreaming APP by Micha Sambora &lt;/strong&gt;immediately states the idea behind an app by means of properly chosen color palette ( which is widely associated with night and dream) and several appropriate graphical elements such as moon icon or photo of darken landscape.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/App-Trekd-Concept/7746907" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101432" alt="Lucid Dreaming APP by Michal Sambora" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Lucid-Dreaming-APP-by-Michal-Sambora.jpg" width="560" height="605" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Weather App &#8220;Outside the window&#8221; by Artem Svitelskyi&lt;/strong&gt; leverages only 2 colors. First one is used for background, and second one is responsible for highlighting rest components, including icons, pagination and type.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/Weather-App-Outside-the-window/6864179" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101438" alt="Weather App &quot;Outside the window&quot; by Artem Svitelskyi" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Weather-App.jpg" width="560" height="614" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Music App by eyal zuri &lt;/strong&gt;takes on minimal style, and showcases elegant flat audio player with neatly placed components.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/Music-App/7539433" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101434" alt="Music App by eyal zuri" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Music-App-by-eyal-zuri.jpg" width="559" height="381" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Profile Screen by Zane David &lt;/strong&gt;gets the feel of refined flat style from circular plane elements, regular icons, shadowless components, one-colored background and comparatively small lettering.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://dribbble.com/shots/889785-Profile-Sreen" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101435" alt="Profile Screen by Zane David" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Profile-Screen-by-Zane-David.jpg" width="560" height="322" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Weatherette by Fabio Basile&lt;/strong&gt; is another good example of line-inspired layout. Interface is filled with a great deal of weather information that is liberally scattered throughout all strings.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://dribbble.com/shots/547557-Weatherette" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img alt="Weatherette by Fabio Basile" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Weatherette-by-Fabio-Basile.jpg" width="560" height="370" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Invoice APP by Tomas Zeman&lt;/strong&gt; has a strong circular vibe. Majority of data enclosed in round shapes that wonderfully complemented by pale color palette.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/Invoice-APP/8369029" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101436" alt="Invoice APP by Tomas Zeman" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Invoice-APP-by-Tomas-Zeman.jpg" width="560" height="390" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;HTML5 app by Zach Robinson &lt;/strong&gt;closes our collection with its polished and clean interface. It ably draws attention by bold colors and sharp neat icons and charts.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="figure"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.behance.net/gallery/HTML5-app/6992373" rel="nofollow" title="" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-101437" alt="HTML5 app by Zach Robinson" src="http://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/HTML5-app-by-Zach-Robinson.jpg" width="560" height="386" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;Reflection&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Flat style is famous for its ability of adding extra flair of sophistication, elegance and neatness to any app interface, making it look more spacious and organized. As a rule, plain graphics and common icons skillfully interact with vivid color scheme, recreating flamboyant touches. Simplicity, absence of styles and effects give the design, at the same time, uncomplicated, and slightly intricate appearance.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Share with us your opinion about utilization of flat style in mobile application design? Which example has earned your admiration? Do you prefer elegance and ease of flat style or realism and complexity of skeuomorphism?&lt;/p&gt;
