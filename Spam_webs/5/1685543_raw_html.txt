        <p>
         Its Black Friday, are you out shopping? Boycotting Black Friday all together?
        </p>
        <p>
         Me: I hate Black Friday crowds so I never go out in it. Id much rather shop online.
        </p>
        <p>
         Anyhoo, We are still out traveling, visiting family.
        </p>
        <p>
         Wednesday of course, was Pookahs 5th birthday. He got his Ninja turtle party and had the time of his life. I really thought he was going to OD on cupcakes tho.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8102" style="width: 594px">
         <a href="http://everydayeyecandy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/image.jpg">
          <img alt="Random Friday " class="size-large wp-image-8102" height="438" src="http://everydayeyecandy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/image-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Random thoughts on a Friday
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Thursday, for the first time EVER, I cooked the ENTIRE meal and my mother was present. It was GOOD! And the food coma I was in afterwards was proof that it was. We put up my mothers Christmas tree too. Ahhhh, its the most wonderful time of the year
         <br/>
         Are you guys doing Elf on the Shelf? I always start off good, but fall off.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://everydayeyecandy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/image1.jpg">
          <img alt="image" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-8103" height="438" src="http://everydayeyecandy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/image1-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://everydayeyecandy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/image2.jpg">
          <img alt="image" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-8104" height="438" src="http://everydayeyecandy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/image2-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Anyhoo, today we are off to the in-laws to spend the rest of the holiday weekend with them. Im sure Pookah will be spoiled rotten by his uncles. But right now, Im about to go have some of my mothers biscuits. Yeah, Weight Watchers next week for sure! LOL
        </p>
        <p>
         How was your Thanksgiving? Are you out shopping today?
        </p>
