         <p>
          Like many derelict places, theres something eerily fascinating about abandonedboarding schools. The idea of the boarding school originated in the UKand somehow remains quintessentially British  the dormitories and classrooms, the refectories, the long hallways and elegant architecture. Good or bad, what goes on within the walls of such educational institutionscan live on long after the building itself is deserted. With that in mind, lets venture inside 10 abandoned boarding schools, some of them with chilling tales to tell.
         </p>
         <h3>
          10. Crookham Court Manor School
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28144" height="426" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-2.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-2.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/truebritishmetal/sets/72157635075331354" target="_blank">
            True British Metal
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/" target="_blank">
            nc-sa-4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Situated in Berkshire, England, Crookham Court Manor School closed in 1989 amidan abuse scandal that led to several members of staff being imprisoned.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28145" height="426" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-3.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-3.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/truebritishmetal/sets/72157635075331354" target="_blank">
            True British Metal
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/" target="_blank">
            nc-sa-4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          In 1988, the BBC headed an investigation into charges of ongoing child abuse at the now-abandoned school. By 1989, Crookham Court Manorhad closed, and finally, in 2012,
          <a href="http://www.getreading.co.uk/news/local-news/former-teacher-convicted-historic-child-4204522" target="_blank">
           convictions
          </a>
          began to fall. Several staff memberswere found guilty of some of the most horrific charges possible, some brought only decades later when those that had been abused finally found the courage tocome forward.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28146" height="426" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-4.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-4.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/truebritishmetal/sets/72157635075331354" target="_blank">
            True British Metal
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/" target="_blank">
            nc-sa-4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Crookham Court Manor School had once educatedthe children of military personnelstationed at the nearby
          <a href="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2014/09/5-abandoned-cold-war-airfields-britain/" target="_blank">
           RAF Greenham Common
          </a>
          . Throughout the war years members of the US Air Force also used the school, and it wasnt until 1961  after a few years of abandonment  that the building was reopened as an independently run boarding school.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28147" height="426" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-5.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-5.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/truebritishmetal/sets/72157635075331354" target="_blank">
            True British Metal
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/" target="_blank">
            nc-sa-4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Some abandoned buildings are dark and dreary, covered with graffiti or littered with garbage. Crookham is a different kind of eerie. Books still sit on their library shelves, homework is scattered across desks. Manuals and textbooks are swollen with damp and mildew, pots with their fake flowers still sit on the counters.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-6" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28148" height="426" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-6.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crookham-court-manor-school-abandoned-6.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/truebritishmetal/sets/72157635075331354" target="_blank">
            True British Metal
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/" target="_blank">
            nc-sa-4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Wallpaper is peeling and torn, rugs are faded and frayed. but its the overwhelming, unearthly feeling that the keys were turned in the locks and people  not wanting to think about what had happened inside those walls  simply turned and walked away from it all.
         </p>
         <h3>
          9. Carmel College
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28237" height="480" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/fish95th/sets/72157634706530166" target="_blank">
            fish95th
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The doors of Carmel College only closed in 1997, and for decades, it had been one of the only Jewish boarding schools in England.
          <a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/news/dismay-as-jewish-eton-closes-1268439.html" target="_blank">
           Closed
          </a>
          due to financial difficulties, the campus is deserted andovergrown, but, unlike so many other abandoned boarding schools, this one has enough neighbours to keep a careful watch over it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28238" height="480" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-2.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-2.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/fish95th/sets/72157634706530166" target="_blank">
            fish95th
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The school has an incredible history. In 1990  only seven years before it closed  it was at the top of the list of the countrys most expensive boarding schools, charging more than10,000 a year for tuition.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28239" height="480" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-3.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-3.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/fish95th/sets/72157634706530166" target="_blank">
            fish95th
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Carmel College was founded by Rabbi Dr. Kopul Rosen in 1948, and today, its still strangely well-cared for. The abandoned boarding school sits on the banks of the Thames, and the disused buildings look as though they simply need a little sprucing up before the students come back for the new term.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28240" height="897" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-4.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/carmel-college-abandoned-oxfordshire-4.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/fish95th/sets/72157634706530166" target="_blank">
            fish95th
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The school itself is a notableexample not only of post-war architecture, but of what everyone believes an English boarding school should look like. From the ornately decorative fireplaces and the dark wood staircases to the elegant carvings and fine iron lanterns, Carmel College is absolutely everything that youd hope for in a boarding school. And, thanks to its location and its neighbours, its in incredibly good shape.
         </p>
         <h3>
          8. Jacob Tome School for Boys
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="tome-school-for-boys-md-abandoned-5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28247" height="423" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/tome-school-for-boys-md-abandoned-5.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/tome-school-for-boys-md-abandoned-5.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/search/?w=56493483@N07&amp;q=tome" target="_blank">
            prolite669
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          At one time, the Jacob Tome School for Boys in Maryland  also called the
          <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2000/03/12/realestate/displacing-ghosts-of-students-and-sailors.html" target="_blank">
           Old Tome School
          </a>
           was considered one of the finest examples of Georgian architecture in the United States. Tragically, in December of 2014, a fire engulfed several of the most prominent buildings on the campus, including a clock tower that had served as a landmark for decades. After firefighters spent hours battling the blaze, all that was left of the previously ornate buildings was granite and cement.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="tome-school-for-boys-md-abandoned-7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28248" height="426" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/tome-school-for-boys-md-abandoned-7.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/tome-school-for-boys-md-abandoned-7.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/search/?w=56493483@N07&amp;q=tome" target="_blank">
            prolite669
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Jacob Tome School was founded in 1894 for children aged between kindergarten and grade 12. It operated as a prep school until 1942, when the needs of the community  and the country  changed with war. Then, it was sold to the US Navy to become a Naval Training Center.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

           <img alt="dozier-school-for-boys-florida-abandoned-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28153" height="375" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/dozier-school-for-boys-florida-abandoned-4.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/dozier-school-for-boys-florida-abandoned-4.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image: Davids Ghost via
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tpr7xtLWmqI" target="_blank">
            YouTube
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          There were plenty of other stories, many of the boys buried on the grounds. They recall being put into industrial dryers as punishment, and of witnessingothers die.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="dozier-school-for-boys-florida-abandoned-5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28154" height="352" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/dozier-school-for-boys-florida-abandoned-5.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/dozier-school-for-boys-florida-abandoned-5.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image: Davids Ghost via
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tpr7xtLWmqI" target="_blank">
            YouTube
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          State records indicate that 96 boys perished while the school was operational. Dozier School for Boys closed in 2011 due to financial reasons. Only then didthe excavations began.
         </p>
         <h3>
          3. Old Brunei Hostel
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28139" height="427" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/beggs/sets/72157626944461831" target="_blank">
            Brian Jeffery Beggerly
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/" target="_blank">
            4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Old Brunei Hostel has a couple of different names. Its also called Tanglin Hill or the Tanglin Brunei Hostel, and it sits, abandoned and odd, in an affluent area of Singapore. Opened in 1958, the boarding house was once the home of some of the most promising students from the small country of Brunei.
         </p>
         <p>
          Since Brunei didnt have its own formal education system, those that showed the most potential and promise were sent to the boarding school in Tanglin Hill. Eventually, the government of Brunei purchased the land for the hostel.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28140" height="427" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned-2.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned-2.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/beggs/sets/72157626944461831" target="_blank">
            Brian Jeffery Beggerly
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/" target="_blank">
            4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Brunei sent government officials to the school for training classes, which was also the home of a handful of Malaysian students. In spite of outside influences, the school remained a pocket of Brunei culture and creativity, celebrating uniquely Brunei holidays and organizing itsown sports teams.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28141" height="427" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned-4.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/old-brunei-hostel-tanglin-hill-abandoned-4.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/beggs/sets/72157626944461831" target="_blank">
            Brian Jeffery Beggerly
           </a>
           , cc-
           <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/" target="_blank">
            4.0
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          By the 1980s, Brunei had established its own educational system, and it no longer became necessary to send students to
          <a href="http://remembersingapore.org/tanglin-hill-brunei-hostel/" target="_blank">
           Tanglin Hill
          </a>
          . The abandoned boarding school still remains the property of the Brunei Consulate and the government, but it now stands derelict and overgrown. In spite of the No Trespassing signs, the abandoned school has become a hotspot for urban explorers and local filmmakers.
         </p>
         <h3>
          2. Mount Pleasant Indian Industrial Boarding School
         </h3>
         <p>
          The development and utilization of Native American boarding schools across the United States is another sad chapter in the unfairness visited upon the nations tribes by their government, and the Mount Pleasant Indian Industrial Boarding School is an eerie reminder of that dark part of American history.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28136" height="375" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image: via
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c5foNz810Q0" target="_blank">
            YouTube
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Established in 1894 and operational through 1934,
          <a href="http://www.cm-life.com/article/2014/01/under-the-shadow-of-history-abandoned-native-american-boarding-school-brings-to-light-one-tribes-str" target="_blank">
           the school
          </a>
          was home to children that were removed from their families and sent there under the somewhat misguided idea that they were better taken care of in an institution, where they could receive an education. The buildings still stand today, and theyre a bizarre reminder of the past not only for those that went there, but for the children and grandchildren of those that were permanently scarred by what went on behind closed doors.
         </p>
         <p>
          For some, that means breaking a cycle of violence that started with the schools. Theres little in the way of formal documents and records, and most of whats remembered has been passed down through the generations. Its mothers telling their daughters about the beatings their grandmothers had endured, its telling the story about how culture and language were taken away, how resident children were forced to conform to the American standard, rather than honouring their own, much older, traditions and beliefs.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28137" height="440" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School-2.jpg" width="750"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School-2.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image: via
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c5foNz810Q0" target="_blank">
            YouTube
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Its thought that about 200 children died during the schools years of operation, many from malnutrition. Records are all but non-existent, though, and official documents only indicate five deaths. Often, those that were about to die were allegedly sent back to their parents but they would never get there. The transfer of the school into tribal hands has facilitated a search of the grounds for the remains of students. And theyre finding them, some children no older than two years.
         </p>
         <p>
          Today, the land and the buildings belong to the tribe that suffered the most there  the Saginaw Chippewa Indian Tribe. Many of those trying to decide what to do with the old, abandoned buildings are haunted by stories of their grandparents  stories that help their own childhoods make so much more sense in retrospect.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28138" height="561" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School-3.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mount-Pleasant-Indian-Industrial-Boarding-School-3.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image: via
           <a href="http://nativenewsonline.net/currents/indian-boarding-school-exhibition-opens-ziibiwing-center-mt-pleasant/" target="_blank">
            Native News Online
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Now, though, theyre looking to rebuild  not only their lands, but their culture and their heritage. Theyre teaching their children what was never truly lost, only hidden, throughout a generation of abuse. An
          <a href="http://nativenewsonline.net/currents/indian-boarding-school-exhibition-opens-ziibiwing-center-mt-pleasant/" target="_blank">
           exhibition
          </a>
          has been opened on the site, dedicated to life at the now-abandoned boarding school.
         </p>
         <h3>
          1.St Josephs Industrial School, Letterfrack
         </h3>
         <p>
          Letterfrack is a small village out in the sprawling, seemingly endless countryside of Connemara, Ireland. In 1849, a Quaker couple moved to Letterfrack from England, purchasing a large piece of land and building
          <a href="http://www.independent.ie/opinion/analysis/st-joseph-of-letterfrack-the-cruel-saint-26248240.html" target="_blank">
           a school
          </a>
          for the local children. In 1884, the property  and the school  passed to the control of the Archbishop of Tuam, when it was decided that it would become one of 52 industrial boarding schools in the country.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="letterfrack-industrial-school-galway-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28135" height="495" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/letterfrack-industrial-school-galway-2.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/letterfrack-industrial-school-galway-2.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="http://www.childabusecommission.com/rpt/01-08.php" target="_blank">
            childabusecommission.com
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The road was paved with good intentions. Originally, it was meant to be a safe haven for children who, as Irish officials stated, could not even beg, as there was no one in the remote Connemara wilderness to beg from.
         </p>
         <p>
          And that, in the end, was one of the problems. St Josephs Industrial Schoolwas so lonely, so remote and so isolated that not only did the boys have no one outside the school to turn to, but were crushed by the aching loneliness and isolation that came with the school sentence. The school officially opened and welcomed its first 75 residents on April 1, 1886, under the management of the Christian Brothers. Because there were so few people living in Connemara, many were sent there from across the country, which made an isolated life even more lonely.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="letterfrack-industrial-school-galway" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28134" height="410" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/letterfrack-industrial-school-galway.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/letterfrack-industrial-school-galway.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image:
           <a href="http://www.childabusecommission.com/rpt/01-08.php" target="_blank">
            childabusecommission.com
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The school was like other industrial institutions of the time, with dormitories, kitchens, and a separate monastery for those in charge. Between its opening and its eventual closure in 1974, 2,819 boys called the school home  and around 99 of them would die there. Boys were orphans, destitute, or, in some cases, criminal. The decision to close the school was first brought up in 1966 and finally made in 1973, but it was only decades later that investigations into the abuse at the facility were first held.
         </p>
         <p>
          By the time the investigations started, the boys that had grown up there were elderly men; those who
          <a href="http://www.independent.ie/irish-news/letterfrack-unmarked-grave-of-boy-4-discovered-26245150.html" target="_blank">
           returned
          </a>
          to the schools grounds were horrified at the memories dredged up at the sight of the old buildings. In the 1930s, St Josephs Industrial Schoolwas the site of one of the first cases of reported sexual abuse in a Catholic institution, but the abuse was farmore widespread than a handful of isolated instances.
         </p>
         <p>
          Boys remembered the punishment for bed-wetting  they were forced to run, holding their sheets above them, ordered not to come in until they were dry. And in an area renowned for its rain, it was an endless punishment. They recounted boys being beaten senseless for the slightest offenses.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="letterfrack-industrial-school-galway-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28189" height="477" src="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/letterfrack-industrial-school-galway-3.jpg" width="640"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2015/04/eerie-abandoned-boarding-schools-of-the-world/&amp;media=http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/home/twamoran/urbanghostsmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/letterfrack-industrial-school-galway-3.jpg&amp;description=Explore 10 Creepy Abandoned Boarding Schools of the World')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <em>
           (Image: Welcome to My Videos via
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FgpNvCbh9WA" target="_blank">
            YouTube
           </a>
           )
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Far from getting an education, investigations supported allegations that the boarding school was little more than a work camp.Poor record-keeping means that details are sketchy. What they have shown is that the boys werent alone in their dilemmas, but that knowledge would be somehow bittersweet for those who had survived isolation in one of Connemaras darkest places. The old
          <a href="http://www.gmit.ie/about/letterfrack-campus" target="_blank">
           buildings
          </a>
          of Letterfrack Industrial School have since been repurposed.
         </p>
