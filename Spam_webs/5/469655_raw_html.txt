                <p>
                 Divi 1.0 was launched last year and it instantly became the most popular WordPress theme released by Elegant Themes ever. Divi has been the prime focus for the Elegant Themes team ever since it was released.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The team has just come up with the next version of the theme
                 <strong>
                  Divi 2.0
                 </strong>
                 with countless possibilities as they mention it. So, lets dig into the theme find out whats in the box.
                </p>

                </p>

                 <span class="cb-button cb-yellow cb-big cb-none">

                   Download
                  </a>
                 </span>
                </center>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Divi 2.0" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3678" height="1496" src="http://www.indexwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Divi-2.0.jpg" width="600"/>
                </p>
                <h1>
                 <div class="boxinfo">
                  Features of Divi 2.0
                 </div>
                </h1>
                <p>
                 Now, lets start the journey, when you look at the
                 <a href="http://www.elegantthemes.com/affiliates/idevaffiliate.php?id=18869&amp;url=12605">
                  demo
                 </a>
                 site for Divi 2.0 it instantly grabs your attention and makes you feel what a theme!
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  33 Robust Modules
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Modules are building blocks of any site created with Divi 2.0, you can play around with the modules to make your site unique. The team has introduced 13 brand new modules that will greatly expand upon what was previously possible with Divi making a list of 33 amazing modules.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Divi 2.0 Modules" class="alignnone wp-image-3679" height="715" src="http://www.indexwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Divi-2.0-Modules.jpg" width="554"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Vertical Navigation
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 This is an amazing feature of the theme, you can completely change the orientation of the theme and make your site look vertical.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Choosing this setting will place your navigation on the left-hand side of your page as in the examples below, this will provide different users to make their site as per their design preference.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Divi 2.0 Vertical Navigation" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3681" height="501" src="http://www.indexwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/d2-vert-nav.jpg" width="600"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Centered Logo
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Many WordPress users like to place their site logo in the center instead of traditional left side. Divi 2.0 provides you the option to change the placement of your logo easily.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Central placement of logo is great for larger logos and primary navigation that has a lot of menu items.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Divi 2.0 Center logo" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3682" height="442" src="http://www.indexwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/d2-centered-logo.jpg" width="600"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  New Header Options
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Divis header options makes it real easy for the users to customize the header section of your site quite effectively. It allows you to choose the color of your header, the placement of your logo, add an accent bar for social and contact information, and even choose between horizontal and vertical navigation.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Also, Divi 2.0 has also introduced a new secondary navigation location.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Divi Header Options" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8788" height="898" src="http://www.indexwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Divi-Header-Options.jpg" width="600"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Divi Drag and Drop Page Builder
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 The Divi Page Builder is amazing for newbie users, it helps you to create a page instantly with drag and drop interface. The module is even more amazing now with some important improvements, including the ability to duplicate a module, row, or section, and an all new Specialty Section that introduces 9 new column structures.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Divi drag and drop page builder" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8785" height="387" src="http://www.indexwp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Divi-drag-and-drop-page-builder.jpg" width="600"/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  Menu-less Landing Pages
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Good looking landing pages are the trend of the moment, with Divi 2.0 you can easily create standalone landing pages.
                </p>
                <p>
                 In this new version, there is an option for blank page, which can remove the header and footer areas from any individual page to use it as sales landing page, a maintenance mode page, a countdown/coming-soon page
                </p>
                <p>
