        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Red Velvet Chocolate Steamer
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-08T06:36:54+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 8, 2015
         </time>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

           19 Comments 
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          A luxuriously creamy
          <strong>
           chocolate
          </strong>
          steamer with a
          <strong>
           red velvet
          </strong>
          twist!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Red Velvet Chocolate Steamer is thick and rick with lots chocolate taste and that signature red velvet hue.  Perfect for warm, cozy evenings! lemonsforlulu.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15951" height="834" src="//www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer-4.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I had no intentions of posting a Valentines day recipe this early. I was still coming off a gum-drop and candy cane high when I walked into my local grocery store and noticed whatused to beChristmas had quickly been reduced to a clearance bin. The bin was pathetic; broken candy canes, ragged tinsel and generic holiday movies no one cares to watch were all crammed inside.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer.jpg">
          <img alt="Red Velvet Chocolate Steamer is thick and rick with lots chocolate taste and that signature red velvet hue.  Perfect for warm, cozy evenings! lemonsforlulu.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15954" height="900" src="//www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I think the saddest part of this whole display was this ragged box of misfit holiday itemsappeared to be swallowed up by the bright red cellophane chocolate boxes and chalky conversation hearts. It never stood a chance.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Red Velvet Chocolate Steamer is thick and rick with lots chocolate taste and that signature red velvet hue.  Perfect for warm, cozy evenings! lemonsforlulu.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15955" height="900" src="//www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         For one brief moment I thought I should probably buy at least one box of candy canes, but got side tracked by my favorite gummy SweetTarts and knew instantly that I was done with all things peppermint for another eleven months. Im ready to embrace Valentines Day and all its pink and red glory. So long ho-ho-ho, hello hubba-hubba-hubba.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Red Velvet Chocolate Steamer is thick and rick with lots chocolate taste and that signature red velvet hue.  Perfect for warm, cozy evenings! lemonsforlulu.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15952" height="900" src="//www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer-5.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         While a box of chocolates is a wonderful gift for the one you love, something warm and cozy is even better. This red velvet chocolate steamer is warm, richly thick and delicious. It starts simply enough with white milk and chocolate milk. The red velvet comes from a bit ofred velvet cake mix and chopped chocolate makes this steamer taste sinful and luxurious. Make a mug for someone you love or just for yourself, either way you win.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Red Velvet Chocolate Steamer is thick and rick with lots chocolate taste and that signature red velvet hue.  Perfect for warm, cozy evenings! lemonsforlulu.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15953" height="900" src="//www.lemonsforlulu.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Red-Velvet-Steamer-3.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tablespoons red velvet cake mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cups chocolate milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup chopped chocolate
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            red food coloring if desired
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            whipped cream and shaved chocolate for garnish
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a saucepan heat a  cup chocolate milk and the cake mix, whisking until cake mix has dissolved. Whisk in remaining milk and heat just until milk becomes warm. Whisk in chopped chocolate and continue to stir until chocolate has been incorporated and milk has thickened. Continue to heat over low heat until mixture is hot but no simmering. Add food coloring if desired. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings. Serve immediately.
           </li>
          </ol>
