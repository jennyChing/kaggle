    <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-leftRailSpanner" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
     <h1 class="grub-topOfColumn" itemprop="name" style="font-size: 27px;">
      Smores on a Stick
     </h1>
     <img height="665" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Smores-on-a-Stick-790x665.jpg" width="790"/>
     <div class="yui3-g">
      <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-well">
       <div class="grub-byline" style="margin-top: 1em;">
        Recipe by
        <a href="https://plus.google.com/113274240511284571445" rel="author">
         Jerry James Stone
        </a>
         October 13, 2013
       </div>
       <div class="grub-mainPostContent" itemprop="recipeInstructions" not-style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 23px;">
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="843" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/smores-on-a-stick/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/smores-on-a-stick" rel="attachment wp-att-4092">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love smores. Its probably the only reason I go camping. That melted chocolate and toasted marshmallow all surrounded by a crunchy graham crackerokay, now I am drooling!
        </p>
        <p>
         What were we talking about? Ehlets just make some smores, shall we?
        </p>
        <div class="yui3-g" id="grub-recipeSummary">
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-left">
          <h2>
           Ingredients
          </h2>
          <ul id="grub-ingredients">
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            6 Graham Crackers
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            4 Ounces milk chocolate
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/old-fashioned-marshmallows/">
             Simple Old-Fashioned Marshmallows (Corn Syrup Free)
            </a>
            , halved
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-right">
          <h2>
           <a class="grub-button" href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/printable/" target="_blank">
            <span class="grub-printIcon">
            </span>
            Printable View
           </a>
          </h2>
          <h2>
           Yield:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            24 s'mores
           </span>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT1H8M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
          <h2>
           Total Time
           <div style="float: right;">
            1 hour, 8 minutes
           </div>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT5M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Prep time
           <div style="float: right">
            5 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Inactive time
           <div style="float: right">
            1 hour
           </div>
          </div>
          <meta content="PT3M" itemprop="cookTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Cook time
           <div style="float: right">
            3 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         1. If your chocolate doesnt come in chunks, youll need to chop it up. You can use almost any chocolate for this but dont use chocolate chips. They tend to not melt well. Well, they meltthey just dont combine well after.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="625" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/chopped-chocolate/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/chopped-chocolate" rel="attachment wp-att-4081">
          <img alt="Chopped Chocolate" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4081" height="375" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chopped-Chocolate-600x375.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         2. Melt the 4 ounces of chopped chocolate. The double boiler method will give you the most control. If you dont have one, you can hack it like I did. Fill a small sauce pan about halfway with water and bring it to a very low simmer. Reduce the heat to low and cover with a metal bowl. Add in the chocolate and whisk it when it begins to melt.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="561" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/diy-double-boiler/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/diy-double-boiler" rel="attachment wp-att-4082">
          <img alt="DIY Double Boiler" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4082" height="336" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/DIY-Double-Boiler-600x336.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="561" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/melt-chocolate/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/melt-chocolate" rel="attachment wp-att-4083">
          <img alt="Melt Chocolate" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4083" height="336" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Melt-Chocolate-600x336.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         3. Using the melted chocolate, coat one side of each graham cracker. Store in a cool place and let the chocolate set, for about an hour.
         <strong>
          DO NOT
         </strong>
         put them in the refrigerator. Gentle cooling is what gives chocolate a uniform structure. The refrigerator will make the chocolate harden faster but once removed, it will melt slightly at room temp and risks becoming grainy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="570" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/melted-chocolate/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/melted-chocolate" rel="attachment wp-att-4084">
          <img alt="Melted Chocolate" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4084" height="342" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Melted-Chocolate-600x342.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1215" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/chocolate-graham/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/chocolate-graham" rel="attachment wp-att-4086">
          <img alt="Chocolate &amp; Graham" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4086" height="729" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chocolate-Graham-600x729.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         5. Lets make the marshmallows! Here is my recipe for
         <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/old-fashioned-marshmallows/" title="Simple Old-Fashioned Marshmallows (Corn Syrup Free)">
          corn syrup free marshmallows
         </a>
         . You only need about half for this recipe as were going to make them about  inch thick.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="673" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/homemade-marshmallow-2/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/homemade-marshmallow-2" rel="attachment wp-att-4087">
          <img alt="Homemade Marshmallow" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4087" height="404" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Homemade-Marshmallow-600x404.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         6. Cut each graham cracker into 8 equal pieces. Luckily these these are marked so its pretty easy to do.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="561" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/chocolate-mini-grahams/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/chocolate-mini-grahams" rel="attachment wp-att-4088">
          <img alt="Chocolate Mini Grahams" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4088" height="336" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Chocolate-Mini-Grahams-600x336.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         7. Using a greased knife, cut the marshmallow to the same size of the chocolate coated grahams.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         8. Now assemble the mini smores. Okay, if you want to steal a fewgo ahead. I did! They are so tasty.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="670" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/mini-smores/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/mini-smores" rel="attachment wp-att-4089">
          <img alt="Mini S'mores" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4089" height="402" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Mini-Smores-600x402.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         9. Everything is better on a stick, right? So lets do that next. I find it easiest to lay the mini smores on the table and apply some pressure with one hand, sorta making the marshmallow ooze out a bit. Then, using a twisting motion, gently penetrate the marshmallow with the stick. YesI said penetrate.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         10. Just for authenticity, I like to toast these babies with a cooking torch before enjoying.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/indoor-smores-2/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/indoor-smores-2" rel="attachment wp-att-4091">
          <img alt="Indoor S'mores-2" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4091" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Indoor-Smores-2-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1454" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/smores-on-a-stick/smores-on-a-stick-3/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/smores-on-a-stick-3" rel="attachment wp-att-4094">
          <img alt="S'mores on a Stick-3" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4094" height="872" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Smores-on-a-Stick-3-600x872.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
