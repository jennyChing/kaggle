        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          Lets start with the obvious. Howd a nice woman like you end up at maybe the scariest place in town?
         </strong>
         I was working at the Philadelphia Historical Commission when the city was considering selling Eastern State for development. With a small band of people that promoted its significance as a National Historic Landmark, we convinced the city to halt development. By then I was hooked on the site, and I have stayed with it.
        </p>
        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          When you meet someone, what is the first thing you notice about them?
         </strong>
         Probably their eyes, the windows to their souls. Oh, and I like a firm handshake.
        </p>

        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          What habit do you find most annoying in people?
         </strong>
         Dont get me started on bad drivers.
        </p>
        <div class="inset inset--minor" id="insetMinorState">
        </div>
        <!-- /.inset -->
        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          Whats the worst advice you ever got, personally or in business?
         </strong>
         Eastern State isnt Alcatraz. No one will ever come to an abandoned prison.
        </p>
        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          What would you do over again if you could?
         </strong>
         Tell my late husband that I loved him every day. I never expected to lose him so soon.
        </p>
        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          If you had to have a new career, what would you like to do most?
         </strong>
         Fix up old houses. Not too much different from an old prison, though!
        </p>
        <p class="content__segment">
         <strong>
          What are the best and worst things about young people today?
         </strong>
         Best: I love the energy of youth today and the fact that they can deal with the bombardment of noise and media. Worst: My mother told me that if I cant say something nice, dont say anything at all.
        </p>
        <div class="truncated-content fade display-none">
         <p class="content__segment">
          <strong>
           Who is your favorite neer-do-well outlaw or gangster of legend?
          </strong>
          Zorro, oops, he was really a good guy, right? How about our most famous inmate,
          <a href="philadelphia/search/results?q=Al Capone">
           Al Capone
          </a>
          !
         </p>

           Norman Johnston
          </a>
          . (We sell it in our store.)
         </p>

          </strong>
          Chinatown.

          </strong>
          Currently revisiting
          <a href="philadelphia/search/results?q=Roy Orbison">
           Roy Orbison
          </a>
          .
