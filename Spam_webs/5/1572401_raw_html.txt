          <h1>
           The Skinny on the Nokia Lumia 2520
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="page-body">
          <div class="post-date">
           Friday, December 06, 2013
          </div>
          <div class="post-body">
           <p>
            <img alt="nokia 2520 case" class="imageseoright" height="138" src="http://www.extremetech.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/nokia_lumia_2520_hero-cropped.jpg" width="250"/>
            If you decided to go another direction this year, opting for the spectacular Nokia technology in tablet form as opposed to buying Apples signature device and the best iPad case to accompany it, you wont have to worry about protection for your Lumia 2520Poetic has you covered with sturdy and slim options for tablet cases. And protection will be a major concern for owners of this fantastic tabletusers are raving for hours on end about their experience.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Why is the
            <a href="http://www.poeticcases.com/nokia-lumia-2520-1">
             Lumia 2520
            </a>
            such a hit? This alternative to the iPad, which features integrated Microsoft Office software (Windows RT 8.1), is lightweight and sleek looking, with phenomenal viewing angles and a gorgeous display via Nokias brilliant, proprietary ClearBlack technology that yields a 650 nits luminance rating. The display is so killer, the tablet is deserving of the same type of protection that the best iPad case or best Nexus 7 case offerswhich is why Poetic created versions of its GraphGrip, SlimLine and SlimBook series specifically for the Nokia Lumia 2520.
           </p>
           <p>
            Other reasons why users are looking for the best tablet cases for this stellar device are its ultra fast performance, expandable storage capability, excellent sound from the devices clever, front facing stereo speakers, and great front facing camera. A feature that sets this tablet apart from its competitors is the interfaces ability to multitask in a new way: you can use two apps at the same time and have control over where the next app you open goes.
           </p>
           <p>
            When it comes to power, the battery is long lasting and powers up extremely quickly with the devices proprietary chargeras in 80 percent in just one hour. We all know how quickly we can run through power on a tablet, so this is a very convenient feature to have.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            With all these convenient and exciting features, it makes sense that users want the same type of protection for their Nokia Lumia 2520 afforded by the
            <a href="http://www.poeticcases.com/google-nexus-7-fhd-2nd-gen">
             best Nexus 7 case
            </a>
            , iPad or other tablet coverand Poetic can make that happen.
           </p>
