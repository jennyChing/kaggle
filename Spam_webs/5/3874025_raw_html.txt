           <div class="editorial">
            <h1 class="header">
             <span class="mw-headline" id="Welcome.21">
              Welcome!
             </span>
            </h1>
            <div class="comparison">
             <p>
              This wiki documents the fictional universe created by
              <a href="/wiki/Vince_Gilligan" title="Vince Gilligan">
               Vince Gilligan
              </a>
              and his colleagues a fiction primarily led by the characters of
              <a href="/wiki/Walter_White" title="Walter White">
               Walter White
              </a>
              and his one-time lawyer,
              <a href="/wiki/Saul_Goodman" title="Saul Goodman">
               Saul Goodman
              </a>
              . Whether you're just trying to catch up on the latest episode, or trying to help us expand our content, you're very welcome here!
             </p>
             <div class="main-sub-header">
              Breaking Bad
             </div>
             <div class="center">
              <figure class="article-thumb tnone show-info-icon" style="width: 325px">
               <a class="video video-thumbnail image lightbox medium " href="http://breakingbad.wikia.com/wiki/File:Bryan_Cranston_on_Walter_White_Talking_Bad" itemprop="video" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/VideoObject">
                <img alt="Bryan Cranston on Walter White Talking Bad" class="thumbimage " data-video-key="Bryan_Cranston_on_Walter_White_Talking_Bad" data-video-name="Bryan Cranston on Walter White Talking Bad" height="182" itemprop="thumbnail" src="http://img2.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20150518194805/breakingbad/images/thumb/e/e4/Bryan_Cranston_on_Walter_White_Talking_Bad/325px-Bryan_Cranston_on_Walter_White_Talking_Bad.jpg" width="325"/>
                <span class="duration" itemprop="duration">
                 07:07
                </span>
                <span class="play-circle">
                </span>
                <meta content="PT07M07S" itemprop="duration"/>
               </a>
               <figcaption>
                <a class="sprite info-icon" href="/wiki/File:Bryan_Cranston_on_Walter_White_Talking_Bad">
                </a>
                <p class="title">
                 Bryan Cranston on Walter White Talking Bad
                </p>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              Begun in 2008 on
              <a href="/wiki/AMC" title="AMC">
               AMC
              </a>
              ,
              <i>
               <a href="/wiki/Breaking_Bad" title="Breaking Bad">
                Breaking Bad
               </a>
              </i>
              chronicled the life of
              <b>
               Walter White
              </b>
              , a terminal lung cancer patient and high school chemistry teacher.  Desperate for a way to take care of his family after his inevitable death, he turned to meth production once he discovers the fabulous, quick wealth it brings. The show ended in 2013.
             </p>
             <div class="main-sub-header">
              Better Call Saul
             </div>
             <div class="center">
              <figure class="article-thumb tnone show-info-icon" style="width: 325px">
               <a class="video video-thumbnail image lightbox medium " href="http://breakingbad.wikia.com/wiki/File:Extended_Trailer_Better_Call_Saul" itemprop="video" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/VideoObject">
                <img alt="Extended Trailer Better Call Saul" class="thumbimage " data-video-key="Extended_Trailer_Better_Call_Saul" data-video-name="Extended Trailer Better Call Saul" height="182" itemprop="thumbnail" src="http://img2.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20150110204948/breakingbad/images/thumb/d/d5/Extended_Trailer_Better_Call_Saul/325px-Extended_Trailer_Better_Call_Saul.jpg" width="325"/>
                <span class="duration" itemprop="duration">
                 02:01
                </span>
                <span class="play-circle">
                </span>
                <meta content="PT02M01S" itemprop="duration"/>
               </a>
               <figcaption>
                <a class="sprite info-icon" href="/wiki/File:Extended_Trailer_Better_Call_Saul">
                </a>
                <p class="title">
                 Extended Trailer Better Call Saul
                </p>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             A series that is at once prequel and sequel to
             <i>
              Breaking Bad
             </i>
             , 2014's
             <i>
              <a href="/wiki/Better_Call_Saul_(TV_series)" title="Better Call Saul (TV series)">
               Better Call Saul
              </a>
             </i>
             explores the life of
             <b>
              Saul Goodman
             </b>
             , Walter White's somewhat dubious lawyer. Although it contains "flash forwards" to the time following the last episode of
             <i>
              Breaking Bad
             </i>
             , it is primarily set in 2002  when the character was known as Jimmy McGill.
            </div>
           </div>
           <p>
            <br/>
           </p>
