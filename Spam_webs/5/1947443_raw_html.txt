     <!-- FEATURES -->
     <ul id="features">
      <li>
       Automatically find all the ebooks in your dropbox wherever they are and whatever the file name is
      </li>
      <li>
       Autocomplete your ebooks information (genre, summary, series, artwork)
      </li>
      <li>
       Free text search of your library
      </li>
      <li>
       Filter your library by author, serie, genre or language
      </li>
      <li>
       Read your book directly from the cloud or download them on your device
      </li>
      <li>
       All your ebooks synchronized on all your devices automatically
      </li>
      <li>
       Clean looking reader with multiple visual themes
      </li>
      <li>
       Choose your font face, size, the line height and the justification of the text
      </li>
      <li>
       Hyphenation support
      </li>
      <li>
       Synchronized bookmarks between all your devices
      </li>

   <div class="row-fluid" id="about">
    <div class="span6">
     <h2 class="page-title" id="scroll_up">
      About
      <a class="arrow-top" href="#home">
       <img src="/static/appsite/img/arrow-top.png"/>
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Librairie aims to ease the pain of ebooks management. All you have to do is
				put your ebooks in the cloud (Dropbox only for now) and Librairie will take tare of the rest for you.
      <br/>
      All your ebooks will be automatically classified and accessible from all your devices.
     </p>
     <p>
      Libriarie works with drm free epub, mobi and azw files. We will support additional formats in the future.
     </p>
