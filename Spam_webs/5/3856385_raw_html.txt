          <p>
           As promised, heres a new old comic strip! Its from 2007, and actually is one of the very first strips I drew, if I recall correctly. One thing thats immediately apparent about these early strips is the overabundance of text boxes accompanying the images. I used to have them in all of my comics actually, probably heavily influenced by A Softer World, but eventually someone told me to show, dont tell instead.
          </p>
          <p>
           Other things I remember about this comic: The title, and quite possibly the idea, came from playing Message in a Bottle by The Police in Guitar Hero 2. Also, the transparent bottle glass in the first panel took me forever to make. Oh, and the comic is of course entirely auto-biographical, hah!
