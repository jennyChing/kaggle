          Mahendra Trivedi
         </h2>
        </div>

       <div class="col12 top-mar">
        <div class="breadcrum" id="breadcrumbs">
         <div id="http://trivedieffect.com" itemref="http://www.trivedieffect.com/mahendra-trivedi/" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/Breadcrumb">

          <a itemprop="url">
           <span itemprop="title">
            Mahendra Trivedi
           </span>
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="col12 ">
        <div class="main-inner">
         <div class="about-master">
          <p>
           <img alt="Mahendra Trivedi  Founder of Trivedi Effect" class="size-full wp-image-11391 alignleft" height="200" src="http://www.trivedieffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Mahendra-Trivedi.png" title="Mahendra Trivedi  Founder of Trivedi Effect" width="150"/>
           Mahendra Trivedi is recognized throughout the world for his ability to transmit a powerful form of energy known as the
           <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/trivedi-effect/">
            Trivedi Effect
           </a>
           . The experiences of over 200,000 individuals throughout the world have demonstrated the impact of the Trivedi Effect on human wellness. Further, nearly 4,000 scientific studies have proven that the impact of this energy extends beyond humans to plants, animals, microbes and even nonliving materials. The power of these transmissions to transform living and nonliving matter for a higher purpose continues to stun the scientific community.
          </p>
          <p>
           It is no wonder that Mr. Trivedi is often referred to as the Master of Transformation.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <strong>
             The Beginning Of The Energy Transformation
            </strong>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           As a child, Mr. Trivedi exhibited unique abilities and extraordinarily high levels of perception and intelligence, including a photographic memory. He says, I always knew I was different and felt that my sojourn on this planet was for a higher purpose.
          </p>
          <p>
           As Mr. Trivedi grew up, he led a typical and normal life. As a young man in India, he graduated from engineering college, went on to work in his field of study and became a highly successful and well-respected businessman. Yet as the years passed, he grew increasingly aware of the extent of his atypical abilities.
          </p>
          <p>
           Then in 1995, Mr. Trivedi received guidance from the Intelligence of the Universe, which led him to understand that the gift he had been given was now to be used for the welfare of humankind. He didnt hesitate to give up all that was familiar and embark on this new mission.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            I know that I received this gift for a higher purpose and to seek the truth. It is simply my role to bring this energy to the world.  Mahendra Trivedi
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mr. Trivedi soon began to see the impact of his
           <a href="https://www.trivedimasterwellness.com/">
            Energy Transmissions
           </a>
           . He found that the energy he transmitted had the ability to transform human consciousness for a higher purpose and to improve human life in all ways.
          </p>
          <p>
           Mr. Trivedi says that he is always guided by what he calls a Universal Intelligence. As part of this guidance, he was directed to collaborate with researchers around the globe to validate his unique abilities. This work within the international scientific community is extensive and ongoing and constitutes an invaluable database for scientists attempting to understand the power of consciousness.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <strong>
             A Global Mission
            </strong>
           </b>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Mr. Trivedis ultimate mission, through application of the Trivedi Effect, is two-fold:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           To establish a new scientific paradigm that will bring about lasting solutions to the worlds most pressing problems and thus vastly improve the human condition.
          </p>
          <p>
           To bring a new paradigm to the field of human wellness by applying
           <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/">
            The Trivedi Effect
           </a>
            and strengthen the individuals connection to his or her own Inner Guidance System and ultimately to the infinite source of Universal Intelligence. With the establishment of this connection, individual lives are transformed to increasingly higher levels and human potential is optimized to its fullest.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <strong>
             The Science Of Consciousness
            </strong>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mahendra Trivedi is a pioneer in bridging the gap between science and consciousness. Research institutes around the world have partnered with Mr. Trivedi to scientifically test, measure, validate and document the impact of the Trivedi Effect on living organisms and nonliving materials. This profoundly exciting research has resulted in the publication of numerous substantiating articles in leading international peer-reviewed scientific journals.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <strong>
             Trivedi Effect  Transforming Individuals
            </strong>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mr. Trivedi also shares his gift through teaching thousands of people around the globe (including professional healers and health-industry workers, business professionals, celebrities, parents, and people in all walks of life) how to significantly enhance their abilities and create stronger, longer-lasting results. His students report amazing transformations in their health, self-confidence, finances, personal relationships, productivity, and much more.
          </p>
          <p>
           Mr. Trivedi has also applied this energy for the purpose of transforming three other individuals into Trivedi Masters:
           <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/alice-branton/">
            Alice Branton
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/gopal-nayak/">
            Gopal Nayak
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/dahryn-trivedi/">
            Dahryn Trivedi
           </a>
           . These individuals, along with select members of the Trivedi Healers Mastery Program, have already gained much attention and respect within the scientific community.
          </p>
          <p>
           Both Gopal and Dahryn have participated in scientific research to test their own abilities to transform living and nonliving matter. These rigorous, empirical studies have been carried out by some of the most advanced research institutes in the world, using some of the most sophisticated instruments available to science.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Trivedi Effect promises to revolutionize the human experience on Earth.
           </strong>
          </p>
