     <h1 class="entry-title">
      wine quotes to savor
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-02-18T08:11:55+00:00">
       February 18, 2014
      </time>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <img alt="Wine Quotes to savor" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-header7_blog140218" width="597"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Wine is a timeless gift, celebrated for generations. The origins of wine predate written records, but is thought to have been cultivated as early as 3100 B.C. The best part? Times havent changedeveryone (still) loves wine.
        </p>
        <p>
         In respect for National Wine Day on Feb. 18, and the
         <a href="http://www.redenvelope.com/wine-rwine">
          gift of wine
         </a>
         , weve compiled a comprehensive list of timeless quotes celebrating wine from Leonardo Da Vinci, to anonymous wine fanatics, to women wine connoisseurs. Some are sweet, some are bitter, some are downright thought-provokinglike a perfect glass of wine.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Thoughts from Historical Wine Enthusiasts
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Bottle and cork" height="594" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/bottle-and-cork_blog140218" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-style: italic;">
          photo via
          <a href="http://whoisdionysus.blogspot.com/2011/07/wine-photography-intimate-session-with.html" target="_blank">
           Wine Notes from Singapore
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Wine is the most civilized thing in the world.  Ernest Hemingway
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Nothing makes the future look so rosy as to contemplate it through a glass of Chambertin.  Napoleon Bonaparte
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Men are like winesome turn to vinegar, but the best improve with age.  Pope John XXIII
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Good company, good wine, good welcome, can make good people.  Shakespeare
        </p>
        <p>
         5. In victory, you deserve Champagne. In defeat you need it.  Napoleon Bonaparte
        </p>
        <p>
         6. Wine makes daily living easier, less hurried, with fewer tensions and more tolerance.  Benjamin Franklin
        </p>
        <p>
         7. The discovery of a good wine is increasingly better for mankind than the discovery of a new star.  Leonardo Da Vinci
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long1_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Pope%20John%20XXIII">
          <img alt="Pope John XXIII Wine Quote" class="aligncenter" height="172" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long1_blog140218" width="628"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         8. Be careful to trust a person who does not like wine.  Karl Marx
        </p>
        <p>
         9. Remember, gentlemen, its not just France we are fighting for, its Champagne!  Winston Churchill
        </p>
        <p>
         10. Wine is sunlight held together by water.  Galileo Galilei
        </p>
        <p>
         11. The discovery of wine is of greater moment than the discovery of a constellation. The universe is too full of stars.  Benjamin Franklin
        </p>
        <p>
         12. Give me wine to wash me clean of the weatherstains of cares.  Ralph Waldo Emerson
        </p>
        <p>
         13. Give me books, French wine, fruit, fine weather and a little music played out of doors by somebody I do not know.  John Keats
        </p>
        <p>
         14. Age appears best in four things: old wood to burn, old wine to drink, old friends to trust and old authors to read.  Francis Bacon
        </p>
        <p>
         15. If we sip the wine, we find dreams coming upon us out of the imminent night.  D.H. Lawrence
        </p>
        <p>
         16. It is better to hide ignorance, but it is hard to do this when we relax over wine.  Heraclitus
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long2_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Galileo">
          <img alt="Galileo Wine Quote" class="aligncenter" height="172" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long2_blog140218" width="628"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         17. When a man drinks wine at dinner, he begins to be better pleased with himself.  Plato
        </p>
        <p>
         18. Sorrow can be alleviated by good sleep, a bath and a glass of wine.  St. Thomas Aquinas
        </p>
        <p>
         19. Fan the sinking flame of hilarity with the wing of friendship; and pass the rosy wine.  Charles Dickens
        </p>
        <p>
         20. My only regret in life is that I did not drink more wine.  Ernest Hemingway
        </p>
        <p>
         21. Wine is constant proof that God loves us and loves to see us happy.  Benjamin Franklin
        </p>
        <p>
         22. Either give me more wine or leave me alone.  Rumi
        </p>
        <p>
         23. Beer is made by men, wine by God.  Martin Luther
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Anonymous Wine Lovers Speak Out
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/raise-your-glass_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Raise%20Your%20Wine%20Glass">
          <img alt="Raise your wine glass" height="571" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/raise-your-glass_blog140218" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-style: italic;">
          photo via
          <a href="http://amanacolonies.com/pages/directory/fireside-winery.php" target="_blank">
           Amana Colonies
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         24. Wine improves with age. The older I get, the better I like it.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         25. Life is too short to drink bad wine.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         26. A meal without wine is called breakfast.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         27. Love the wine youre with.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         28. You cant buy happiness but you can buy wine, and thats kind of the same thing.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         29. A good woman is like a fine red wine, once uncorked, appreciate to the full and never waste a drop!  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         30. Its not so bad if stuff happens, as long as you serve the right wine with it.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long3_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Anonymous">
          <img alt="Anonymous wine quote" class="aligncenter" height="172" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long3_blog140218" width="628"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         31. Reality is an illusion that occurs due to a lack of wine.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         32. It doesnt matter if the glass is half empty or half full. There is clearly room for more wine.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         33. Who is this moderation people are telling me to drink with?!  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         34. If you have to ask if its too early to drink wine, youre an amateur and we cant be friends.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         35. There comes a time in the day when no matter what the question is, the answer is always wine.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         36. Whichever glass you chose, may it be more full than empty.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         37. Wine is more than a beverage, its a lifestyle.  Anonymous
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Quotes from Women Who Love Wine
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/women-love-wine_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Women%20Love%20Wine">
          <img alt="Women love wine" class="aligncenter" height="418" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/women-love-wine_blog140218" width="630"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-style: italic;">
          photo via
          <a href="http://sbchic.com/2013/11/11/entertaining-dinner-in-the-apple-orchard/" target="_blank">
           Santa Barbara Chic
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         38. Wine speaks to all the senses: the eyes behold the color, tone, and shade; the nose, the bouquet, the fingers and lips caress the cool crystal; the ears delight in the subtle swishing of the liquid; the tongue rejoices in the reward of a rich harvest.  Mary Lou Posch
        </p>
        <p>
         39. Age is just a number. Its totally irrelevant unless, of course, you happen to be a bottle of wine.  Joan Collins
        </p>
        <p>
         40. Wine is a living liquid containing no preservatives. Its life cycle comprises youth, maturity, old age and death. When not treated with reasonable respect it will wicken and die.  Julia Child
        </p>
        <p>
         41. Once we hit 40, women only have about four taste buds left: one for vodka, one for wine, one for cheese and one for chocolate.  Gina Barreca
        </p>
        <p>
         42. Artists and poets still find lifes meaning in a glass of wine.  Joy Sterling
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long4_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Joan%20Collins">
          <img alt="Joan Collins wine quote" class="aligncenter" height="172" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long4_blog140218" width="628"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         43. Men are like a fine wine. They all start out like grapes, and its our job to stomp on them and keep them in the dark until they mature into something youd like to have dinner with.  Kathleen Mifsud
        </p>
        <p>
         44. Language is wine upon the lips.  Virginia Woolf
        </p>
        <p>
         45. Im like old wine. They dont bring me out very often, but Im well preserved.  Rose Fitzgerald Kennedy
        </p>
        <p>
         46. I like sweet wines. My idea has always been that when youre young, you like sweet wines; and then you get sophisticated, and you drink dry white; and then you get knowledgeable, and you drink heavy reds; and then you get old, and you drink sweet again.  Sally Jessy Raphael
        </p>
        <p>
         47. Just like becoming an expert in wine, you learn by drinking it, the best you can afford.  Julia Child
        </p>
        <p>
         48. Wine is earths answer to the sun.  Margaret Fuller
        </p>
        <p>
         49. I have dreamed in my life, dreams that have stayed with me ever after, and changed my ideas; they have gone through and through me, like wine through water, and altered the color of my mind.  Emily Bronte
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long5_blog140218&amp;url=http://www.redenvelope.com/blog/wine-quotes-to-savor?ref=redsclpngen20140218&amp;description=Wine%20Quotes%20To%20Savor:%20Rose%20Firtzgerald">
          <img alt="Rose Fitzgerald wine quote" class="aligncenter" height="172" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/wine-quotes-long5_blog140218" width="628"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         50. What I do and what I dream include thee, as the wine must taste of its own grapes.  Elizabeth Barrett Browning
        </p>
        <p>
         51. Wine is a gateway drug to environmentalism.  Katherine Cole
        </p>
        <p>
         52. There are days when solitude is a heady wine that intoxicates you with freedom, others when it is a bitter tonic, and still others when it is a poison that makes you beat your head against the wall.  Colette
        </p>
        <p>
         53. If your arteries are good, eat more ice cream. If they are bad, drink more red wine. Proceed thusly.  Sandra Byrd
        </p>
        <p>
         54. The wineit made her limbs loose and liquid, made her feel that a hummingbird had taken the place of her heart.  Jodi Picoult
        </p>
        <p>
         55. Wine is just a conversation waiting to happen.  Jessica Altieri
        </p>
