        <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-64 photo" height="64" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/eddie-avatar-75x75.jpg" srcset="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/d15c4dd6e872679aa1e0ff438fa43ca3?s=128&amp;d=mm&amp;r=x 2x" width="64"/>
        <header class="entry-header">
         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Porsche Panamera GTS by Anderson Germany
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           Written by
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/author/eddie/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             eddie
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-categories">
           in
           <a href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/category/autos/cars/" rel="category tag">
            Cars
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <img alt="Anderson modifies Panamera GTS" class="post-image entry-image attachment-content-wide wp-post-image" height="373" itemprop="image" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Porsche-Panamera-GTS-Anderson-Germany-e1343959311599.jpg" width="560"/>
         <p>
          Anderson Germany is known for taking exotic cars and blessing them with their special touch. This time around, its the Porsche Panamera GTS. The GTS model is level below the turbo and its 4.8-liter naturally-aspirated V8 engine delivers 430hp and 384 lb-ft of torque. Just because it has an abundance of power which the owner will almost never use to its full potential, doesnt mean tuners cant mess around with it to create an even more powerful beast.
         </p>
         <p>
          First things first, they technicians started off with the V8 engine by implanting software upgrades as well as a three-stage adjustable sports exhaust system. Those changes along boosted the horsepower from 430
          <span id="more-35597">
          </span>
          to 501hp. Anderson Germany techs also installed a new electronic module allowing the driver to drop the Panamera GTSs already height-adjustable suspension by another 30mm. The company also slapped on 22-inch alloy wheels with the fronts wrapped in 265/30 tires and 295/25 tires at the rear.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="imageclose-35598">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35598">
             <img alt="GTS Interior by Anderson Germany" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-35601" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Porsche-Panamera-GTS-Anderson-Interior-e1343959679322.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35598">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35598">
            <img alt="image-35598" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Porsche-Panamera-GTS-Anderson-Interior.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          On the inside of the vehicle, Anderson Germany goes with a combination of leather and Alcantara with white diamond stitching. The interior also includes white carbon fiber trim, entry sills on the doors, as well as floor lighting in the front and back with the ability to dim the lights.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="imageclose-35599">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35599">
             <img alt="Anderson Germany Panamera GTS Interior" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-35602" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Porsche-Panamera-GTS-Cabin-e1343960144968.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35599">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35599">
            <img alt="image-35599" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Porsche-Panamera-GTS-Cabin.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          They say that true beauty is within and with these engine and interior upgrades, that statement would be hundred percent correct for the Panamera GTS. But why be beautiful on the inside only? Anderson Germany took the already beautiful GTS, and basically applied minimal makeup to it. The front bumper is restyled and features a lower front spoiler and headlamp surrounds which I personally am not a fan of. The headlamps are not unattractive but in my opinion, not attractive either. The body kit also includes side skirts, rear bumper accents, diffuser, and tail light trim.
         </p>
         <p>
