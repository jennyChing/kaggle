          <p>
           With Texas being the second largest state in the country and all, it sometimes seems overwhelming when you think about just how much there is to see here! Luckily, though, there are day trip destinations scattered throughout the Lone Star State that will satisfy any travelers need for adventure. From caves to theme parks to West Texas towns known for sightings of unexplained lights flashing in the night sky, this collection of awesome day trips offers a little something for everyone.
