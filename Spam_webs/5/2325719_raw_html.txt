        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Our limb differences arent scary campaign
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn" href="http://www.bornjustright.com/author/nerdymom/">
           Jen Lee Reeves
          </a>
         </span>
         on
         <abbr class="published" title="2014-10-07">
          October 7, 2014
         </abbr>
         
         <span>
          <a href="http://www.bornjustright.com/2014/10/limb-differences-arent-scary-campaign/#comments" rel="nofollow">
           10 comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         in
         <span>
          <a href="http://www.bornjustright.com/category/2014/" rel="category tag">
           2014
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.bornjustright.com/category/camp-no-limits/" rel="category tag">
           camp no limits
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p>
         A little later this month, I plan to work with a collection of bloggers who are helping spread the word that limb differences arent scary. Why not tie our positive focus on limb differences leading up to Halloween? Im hoping this is a chance to raise more awareness about
         <a href="http://nolimitsfoundation.org" target="_blank" title="Camp No Limits">
          Camp No Limits
         </a>
         by giving everyone a chance to donate at least $5 to
         <a href="http://bit.ly/cnl2015" target="_blank" title="Camp No Limits Fundraiser">
          Camp No Limits
         </a>
         to win one of more than 15 type of raffle items.
        </p>
        <p>
         Camp No Limits is the limb difference family camp weve attended every year since Jordan was three. She loves it and we love it. The whole family gets support and knowledge about the limb difference world.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px">
         <img alt="camp-no-limits" height="334" src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5530/14333480737_06939ef993.jpg" width="500"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Hands in for camp!
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Check out this raffle awesomeness. There are many wonderful people and organizations donating cool stuff:
        </p>
        <div style="position: relative; padding-bottom: 76%; height: 0; overflow: hidden;">
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="150" id="iframe" scrolling="no" src="http://flickrit.com/slideshowholder.php?height=75&amp;size=big&amp;setId=72157648008522108&amp;caption=on&amp;counter=true&amp;trans=1&amp;thumbnails=1&amp;transition=0&amp;layoutType=responsive&amp;sort=0" style="width: 100%; height: 100%; position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0;" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         Before I share this with everyone else in the blogosphere, you get the first chance to donate to
         <a href="http://www.firstgiving.com/fundraiser/bornjustright/camp-no-limits-missouri-2015" target="_blank" title="Camp No Limits Fundraiser">
          Camp No Limits
         </a>
         and get your name into the raffle! All it takes is to donate at least $5. You get an additional raffle entry for every additional $5 you donate. If you donate more than $50, just add it up and enter the raffle again at another pay level. (For example, if you donate $100, put your name in under the $50 level along with the $25, $15, and $10)
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          How do you do it?
         </strong>
         <br/>
         STEP 1: Go to
         <a href="http://www.firstgiving.com/fundraiser/bornjustright/camp-no-limits-missouri-2015" target="_blank" title="Camp No Limits Fundraiser">
          Born Just Rights Camp No Limits 2015 Fundraiser
         </a>
         <br/>
         STEP 2: Donate to camp
         <br/>
         STEP 3: Return to this page and fill out your information this raffle box
         <br/>
         STEP 4: Wait for Halloween and the big raffle drawing
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <a class="rafl" href="http://www.rafflecopter.com/rafl/display/bc7d0ffd3/" id="rc-bc7d0ffd3" rel="nofollow">
          a Rafflecopter giveaway
         </a>
         <br/>
         <script src="//widget.rafflecopter.com/load.js">
         </script>
        </center>
        Thank you for joining in on this fundraiser. This is the first time Ive taken a raffle experience online, Im excited to see us spreading the word about camp and how limb differences arent scary. Differenceshelp teach us all how our global view of perfect is wrong. Knowing we can all go about our lives in different ways and still succeed is a pretty awesome lesson to learn.
        <p>
         <img alt="limb-differences-arent-scary" class="aligncenter" height="500" src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2947/15278258288_be7dcf8f4d.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
