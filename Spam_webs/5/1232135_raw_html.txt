           <h2>
            Tools and Materials
           </h2>
           <p>
            Paper in various colors and weights
            <br/>
            Scissors
            <br/>
            Double-sided tape
            <br/>
            Glue
            <br/>
            Wooden skewers
           </p>
           <h2>
            How-To
           </h2>
           <ol>
            <li>
             Cut paper into squares of different sizes (ours ranged from 1 1/2 by 1 1/2 inches to 4 by 4 inches).
            </li>
            <li>
             Fold diagonally to crease, unfold, then snip halfway up creases.
            </li>
            <li>
             Bend points in and attach with tape. Glue to skewers (available at most grocery stores) and stick into cake.
            </li>
           </ol>
