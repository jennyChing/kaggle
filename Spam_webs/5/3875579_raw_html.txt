        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Easy Fruit Dip | Dessert Sauce (low carb, dairy and sugar-free)
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         Posted By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/author/adrienne/" rel="author">

        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p class="no-break">
          <span class="st_sharethis_vcount" st_title="Easy Fruit Dip | Dessert Sauce (low carb, dairy and sugar-free)" st_url="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fruit-dip-recipe-dip-for-fruit-dips-sweet-dip/">

          <span class="st_stumbleupon_vcount" st_title="Easy Fruit Dip | Dessert Sauce (low carb, dairy and sugar-free)" st_url="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fruit-dip-recipe-dip-for-fruit-dips-sweet-dip/">

          <span class="st_twitter_vcount" st_title="Easy Fruit Dip | Dessert Sauce (low carb, dairy and sugar-free)" st_url="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fruit-dip-recipe-dip-for-fruit-dips-sweet-dip/" st_username="wholenewmom" st_via="wholenewmom">
          </span>
          <span class="st_pinterest_vcount" st_title="Easy Fruit Dip | Dessert Sauce (low carb, dairy and sugar-free)" st_url="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fruit-dip-recipe-dip-for-fruit-dips-sweet-dip/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_facebook_vcount" st_title="Easy Fruit Dip | Dessert Sauce (low carb, dairy and sugar-free)" st_url="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fruit-dip-recipe-dip-for-fruit-dips-sweet-dip/">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fruit-dip-recipe-dip-for-fruit-dips-sweet-dip/" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Easy Fruit Dip - Low Carb and Dairy and Sugar Free. Great as a drizzle, a dip, or eaten right off the spoon." class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-31393" data-wp-pid="31393" height="466" src="http://cdn.wholenewmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Fruit-Dip-New-Wmk-1024x682.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I love dips.
          <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/vegan-mayonnaise-vegetable-dip-recipe-egg-free-mayonnaise/" target="_blank">
           Veggie dips
          </a>
          . Fruit dips.
          <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/savory-hummus/" target="_blank">
           Hummus
          </a>
          .
          <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/fast-and-yummy-bean-dip/" target="_blank">
           Bean Dips
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          My son doesnt really like dip, but he will eat my dips on rice, plain, or spread them on breads and such.
         </p>
         <p>
          As I mentioned on my
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/WholeNewMom" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/WholeNewMom', 'Facebook page');" target="_blank">
           Facebook page
          </a>
          a few days ago, I was working on an Almond Butter Dip.
         </p>
         <p>
          Anyhow, itwas a complete success. In fact, I couldnt really keep out of it yesterday.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-11221">
          </span>
         </p>
         <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
         <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
          <!-- BEGIN JS TAG - WNM 300x250_2 &lt; - DO NOT MODIFY -->
          <script src="http://ads.pubsqrd.com/ttj?id=2570006&amp;cb=[CACHEBUSTER]" type="text/javascript">
           &lt;/SCRIPT&gt;
&lt;!-- END TAG --&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;

&lt;p&gt;I always have to find the balance between making things that are good enough to share with all of you and yet not so good that I end up eating all of it.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;(Well, I didn&#8217;t eat all of it, but&#8230;.)&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I typically don&#8217;t rush around to get a post out (gotta take it a little slow due to adrenal fatigue), but I thought you all might want to add this to your &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/super-bowl-recipes-super-bowl-food-healthy-super-bowl/" target="_blank"&gt;Healthy Superbowl Menu Galore&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I used to love those caramel apple dips, but they are &lt;strong&gt;loaded with all kinds of things&lt;/strong&gt; that I don&#8217;t want to or can&#8217;t eat anymore:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;high fructose corn syrup&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;partially hydrogenated oils&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;disodium phosphate&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;mono and diglycerides&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;potassium sorbate&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;white sugar&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Ick.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This dip, while not caramel, is really tasty. And is made of &lt;strong&gt;only healthful ingredients&lt;/strong&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;And it&#8217;s super fast to make! My kind of recipe.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!--more--&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I found several versions of nut butter based dips on a few websites and then whipped it up a few times to find the version that was a hit.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This is it. Just right sweetness with a touch of cinnamon to add the perfect extra something.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;We even made a special trip to buy some apples to go with it since we didn&#8217;t have any in the house (and I needed a nice addition for a photo :-).)&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;My youngest son was thrilled since he loves apples, has never had a fruit dip, and the Meijer stores nearby have a great horsey that you can ride on for just 1 penny (can you believe it? :-).) He rode with dad and brother while mom shopped. And we ran into some old friends.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;How nice to have extra blessings added to a mundane errand.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Ways to Use this Super Versatile Dip / Sauce&lt;/strong&gt;:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;as a fruit dip&lt;/strong&gt; for apples, pears, bananas, strawberries (&lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/health-concerns/poison-in-our-strawberries/" target="_blank"&gt;Strawberries are loaded with pesticides&lt;/a&gt;. Please choose organic or non-sprayed.)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;drizzled on top of nuts&lt;/strong&gt; (&lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/snacks/how-and-why-to-soak-and-dehydrate-nuts-and-seeds/" target="_blank"&gt;soaked and dehydrated&lt;/a&gt;, of course)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;drizzled onto oatmeal&lt;/strong&gt; or your favorite breakfast cereal (see &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/soaking-grains-the-why-and-the-how/" target="_blank"&gt;my post on soaking grains&lt;/a&gt;.)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;used as an &lt;strong&gt;alternative to syrup on pancakes or waffles&lt;/strong&gt; (I have &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/teff-waffles-or-pancakes-and-caramelized-bananas/" target="_blank"&gt;a &lt;em&gt;great&lt;/em&gt; recipe for Teff Waffles&lt;/a&gt; (sorry &#8211; no photo yet :-)!)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;spread or drizzled on top of cakes&lt;/strong&gt; like my &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/baked-oatmeal-recipes-for-oatmeal-recipes-for-oats/" target="_blank"&gt;Oatmeal Cake&lt;/a&gt; (my husband likes this option since he&#8217;s not a frosting fan at all). A friend of mine just added chopped pecans to make a &#8220;German Chocolate Cake&#8221; type of frosting for her favorite gluten free cake and she said it was great. (I&#8217;ll have to get her &#8220;favorite cake&#8221; recipe and give it a try! Promise I&#8217;ll share it with you all!)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;for &lt;strong&gt;dipping &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/perfect-basic-popcorn-recipe/" target="_blank"&gt;popcorn&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt; (or drizzle it on top!)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;as a topping for baked goods like &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/focaccia-flax-bread/" target="_blank"&gt;Focaccia Flax Bread&lt;/a&gt; or &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/oat-bran-muffins/" target="_blank"&gt;Oat Bran Muffins&lt;/a&gt;or&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;eaten by the spoonful&lt;/strong&gt;!&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;One nice thing is that you get the wonderfully rich taste of almond butter with about half the calories and fat since you&#8217;re thinning out the almond butter. Of course, these are good fats, but all things in moderation!&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;h2&gt;&lt;span style="color: #808000;"&gt;Nut Butter Fruit Dip&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Ingredients&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="padding-left: 30px;"&gt;1 cup almond butter (or other nut or seed butter. See my post on &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/homemade-nut-and-seed-butters/" target="_blank"&gt;Homemade Nut or Seed Butter&lt;/a&gt;.)&lt;br /&gt;
1 cup &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/whole-new-budget/almond-milk-recipe-how-to-make-almond-milk-almonds-milk/" target="_blank"&gt;Easiest Almond Milk&lt;/a&gt; (or other non-dairy milk such as &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/whole-new-budget/easiest-coconut-milk-recipe-improved/" target="_blank"&gt;Easiest Coconut Milk&lt;/a&gt;)&lt;br /&gt;
1 scoop (1/32 tsp) pure stevia extract powder (or 2 Tbsp other healthy sweetener. I recommend &lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0019LPM0C/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0019LPM0C&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=HN7XZUYPH4LVPGBS" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0019LPM0C/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0019LPM0C&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=HN7XZUYPH4LVPGBS', 'NuNaturals');" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"&gt;NuNaturals&lt;/a&gt;.)&lt;br /&gt;
1 Tbsp sweetener, as healthy as possible, according to your needs (I use candida-friendly sweeteners like &lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003CNOUWQ/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B003CNOUWQ&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=NLQ3MQ5Y7A4EYPTE" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003CNOUWQ/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B003CNOUWQ&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=NLQ3MQ5Y7A4EYPTE', 'xylitol');" target="_blank"&gt;xylitol&lt;/a&gt; and &lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0019LWU2K/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0019LWU2K&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=KM5KJKKD5JS6G43Q" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0019LWU2K/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0019LWU2K&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=KM5KJKKD5JS6G43Q', 'vegetable glycerine');" target="_blank"&gt;vegetable glycerine&lt;/a&gt;.)&lt;br /&gt;

1/2 tsp cinnamon&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Method&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="padding-left: 30px;"&gt;1. Place all ingredients in food processor or blender.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="padding-left: 30px;"&gt;2. Mix until combined and smooth. (This is super quick in a &lt;a href="https://secure.vitamix.com/?COUPON=06-005927 " onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://secure.vitamix.com/?COUPON=06-005927 ', 'Vitamix');" target="_blank"&gt;Vitamix&lt;/a&gt;. &lt;p style="padding-left: 30px;"&gt;3. Store whatever you don&#8217;t manage to polish off in the refrigerator.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Of course, you can alter this recipe as you like.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Some great variations include:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Use maple syrup for a more mapley taste (is that a word?)&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Add chocolate or carob for a chocolatey treat! Start with 2 Tbsp or carob or chocolate and go from there.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Use coconut milk for a coconutty / nutty blend. See my post on &lt;a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/make-your-own-coconut-milk/" target="_blank"&gt;Easiest Homemade Coconut Milk&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
