        <h1>
         Artcrank 2011 Process
        </h1>
        by
        <span class="post-author">
         <a href="http://allanpeters.com/blog/author/allanpeters/" title="Posts by Allan Peters">
          Allan Peters
         </a>
        </span>
        on
        <span class="post-date">
         Apr 30, 2011
        </span>
        
        <span>
         3:13 pm
        </span>
        <a class="post-comms" href="#comments">
         5 Comments
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://allanpeters.com/blog/?attachment_id=4019" rel="attachment wp-att-4019">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4019" height="1213" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/Screen-shot-2012-05-17-at-2.02.48-PM.png" title="Screen shot 2012-05-17 at 2.02.48 PM" width="901"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I spent a really long time illustrating my 2011 Artcrank poster and I just wanted to show you guys some of the process shots so you have an idea about how I came up with the final. Once I settled on a final idea, I started by shooting all of the crops of my bike that I wanted to use. You can see it was a pretty sweet photoshoot in our laundry room. I was a great excuse to get my bike cleaned up for the year since it was pretty dirty from last season still.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-77" height="775" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac01.png" title="ac01" width="620"/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://allanpeters.com/blog/?attachment_id=77" rel="attachment wp-att-77">

         <a href="http://allanpeters.com/blog/?attachment_id=79" rel="attachment wp-att-79">
         </a>
         <a href="http://allanpeters.com/blog/?attachment_id=78" rel="attachment wp-att-78">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-78" height="767" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac02.png" title="ac02" width="618"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://allanpeters.com/blog/?attachment_id=78" rel="attachment wp-att-78">
         </a>
         Next, I picked my crops and took the photos into illustrator where I traced them and figured out my layout + type.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-79" height="839" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac03.png" title="ac03" width="676"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-80" height="899" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac04.png" title="ac04" width="682"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, I placed every item from the original illustrator file into photoshop one by one. The reason to place each element separately was so I could do a quick selection for image crops and for texture brush crops. The textures are a combination of the actual bike photos mixed with a texture brush Ive been experimenting with.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-84" height="887" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac08.png" title="ac08" width="710"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-83" height="886" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac07.png" title="ac07" width="709"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-82" height="885" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac06.png" title="ac06" width="710"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-81" height="885" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac05.png" title="ac05" width="709"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-85" height="934" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/ac09.png" title="ac09" width="712"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Last, I picked out some
         <a href="http://www.frenchpaper.com/Index.asp" target="_blank">
          French paper
         </a>
         and PMS colors then sent my files to my buddy
         <a href="http://www.lfdept.com">
          Jamie Paul
         </a>
         out in Portland to get printed. A happy mistake that happened was that all of the heavy ink coverage from the cream color caused to paper to warp when it dried. Because of this, the registration ended up offsetting giving the final poster a really cool vintage feel. It reminds me of the registration on matchbooks from the 40s and 50s
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-86" height="848" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/FullPoster.jpg" title="FullPoster" width="670"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-87" height="830" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/Print1.jpg" title="Print1" width="670"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88" height="828" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/Print2.jpg" title="Print2" width="670"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-89" height="838" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/Print3.jpg" title="Print3" width="670"/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-90" height="834" src="http://allanpeters.com/wp-content/uploads/Print4.jpg" title="Print4" width="670"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Anyhow, If youd like to see some more pictures of the final, check out my
         <a href="http://cargocollective.com/peters#1307386/Artcrank-2011">
          portfolio
         </a>
         . And if youd like to purchase a copy, its on sale at art-a-whirl in St.Paul. Check out the
         <a href="http://artcrankpostershow.com" target="_blank">
          Artcrank
         </a>
         site for details.
        </p>
