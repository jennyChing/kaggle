     Welcome

    <p>
     Welcome at my website. My first name is Rowy. I'm a composer and I used to teach music theory
        and composition to advanced students. Although I don't teach anymore, I still take great joy in helping
        talented composers and enthusiastic amateurs.
    </p>
    <p>
     This site offers you a
     <strong>
      free
     </strong>
     advanced course in harmony for	composers, a paper on music
        	composition, on music theory, on chords, 9 lectures on composition, 6 on orchestration and a set of links
        	to teachers and books on theory, harmony, music composition, music history and orchestration. Alas, most
        	of it is in
     <a href="componeren.html">
      Dutch
     </a>
     .
    </p>
    <p>

    </p>
