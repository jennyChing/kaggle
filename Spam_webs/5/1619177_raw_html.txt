          <h1 class="entry-title">
           30-DAY WATER CHALLENGE
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="by-author vcard author">
            <span class="fn">

         <p>
          <a href="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/addtext_com_MjEwMTE5MTI3NzU1.jpg">
           <img alt="30-DAY WATER CHALLENGE" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-155" height="720" src="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/addtext_com_MjEwMTE5MTI3NzU1.jpg" width="960"/>
          </a>
          There was a time when I could run over six miles, my BMI was the lowest in my life, my shoulder and back muscles were amazing, and my collar bones protruded right below my neck in the most perfect fashion, not too bony but not covered in any soft flesh. I wish I could tell you that this was that time, but it is not.
         </p>
         <p>
          In fact I feel very removed from that time. I almost wish I was talking about my teenage years because at least then I could say, Oh, nobody looks like they did when they were a teenager, and feel okay with the decline of my shape, strength, stamina, and health. No, those days were as recent as my early 30s. I want the rest of whats left of my 40s to be the healthier version of me:
          <br/>
          By the way, here is the healthier 30 something version of me.
         </p>
         <div class="tiled-gallery type-circle tiled-gallery-unresized" data-carousel-extra='{"blog_id":1,"permalink":"http:\/\/www.notquitewonderwoman.com\/30-day-water-challenge\/","likes_blog_id":67072090}' data-original-width="700">
          <div class="gallery-row" data-original-height="700" data-original-width="700" style="width: 700px; height: 700px;">
           <div class="gallery-group" data-original-height="700" data-original-width="700" style="width: 700px; height: 700px;">
            <div class="tiled-gallery-item">
             <a border="0" href="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/30-day-water-challenge/alligator-point/">

         <p>
          Now Im not going to look back wistfully and waste my time wishing for those days. Im not going to beat myself down for the decline of my physicality or punish myself for not maintaining that level of health. That would not be productive AT ALL. But making changes, THAT is productive. And what could be easier than drinking water?
         </p>
         <p>
          There are several reasons that I am creating a water challenge to jump start my return to health. There are of course the
          <a href="http://shine.yahoo.com/healthy-living/6-reasons-drinking-water-solves-problem-143000170.html" target="_blank">
           health benefits
          </a>
          , like boosting your metabolism and fighting that mid-day fatigue. (You know what Im talking about, right?) There is a little bit of money factor. Drinking anything other than water cost money. Now, I know there are those who pay for bottles of water so this point might not help you much, but Im cool with a glass of water when Im eating out, and refilling my pop lid bottle with water from home. Not to mention the fact that there are plenty of scientific study and personal accounts of water
          <a href="http://abcnews.go.com/blogs/health/2013/11/01/can-drinking-water-really-take-10-years-off-your-face/" target="_blank">
           improving your looks
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/water2.jpg">
           <img alt="Health benefits of water" class="aligncenter wp-image-150 " height="739" src="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/water2-682x1024.jpg" width="492"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          And just maybe your liver and other organs will thank you.
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/water.jpg">
           <img alt="water" class="aligncenter wp-image-146 size-medium" height="210" src="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/water-300x210.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Now enough of the whys. Lets get to the nuts and bolts of this challenge:
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           RULE #1
          </strong>
          Anytime you (and by you I mean me) consume liquid for the next thirty days it will be water. Not apple juice, not milk, not Diet Mountain Dew, not wine, not sweet tea, not vodka but WATER, just water.
          <br/>
          <strong>
           RULE #2
          </strong>
          See rule number one.
         </p>
         <p>
          Very simple rules. And by the next 30 days I mean starting March 1, 2014 for myself, you can start whenever you like. (I actually started at the beginning of February but fell off the water wagon on the third day and realized that February was a 28 day month so that wasnt not going to work at all anyway. So Im writing this post and calling a do-over.)
         </p>
         <p>
          If anyone wants to read about how much water a person actually needs, you can click
          <a href="http://health.usnews.com/health-news/blogs/eat-run/2013/09/13/the-truth-about-how-much-water-you-should-really-drink" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          . Or want to read more about a good calculation of how much water you need
          <a href="http://www.slenderkitchen.com/how-to-calculate-how-much-water-you-should-drink-a-day/#" target="_blank">
           this was informative
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          As you see, there is no amount fits all, but it depends on weight and activity. So, heres hoping you take the 30-day water challenge with me. Ill be updating my progress with a new post soon. Please comment with your experience or tips!
         </p>
         <p>
          Happy Drinking!
         </p>
         <p>
          See my
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.notquitewonderwoman.com/30-day-water-challenge-follow-up/" target="_blank">
            follow-up
           </a>
          </strong>
          to get results!
         </p>
