          <a dsqid="11444668" href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/health/news/11444668/Study-on-penises-reveals-the-average-size-...-and-its-smaller-than-you-think.html#disqus_thread">

        <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="firstPar">
          <p>
           It's a finding that could bring relief to millions of men.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           Although six inches has long been the accepted norm for the length of a manhood, the average is actually almost a full inch smaller, research reveals.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Length
           </strong>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           Analysis of 17 studies involving more than 15,500 men revealed that the average length of an erect penis is 5.16 inches (13.12 centimetres) and 3.6 inches (9.16cm) when flaccid.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Girth
           </strong>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="related-links-float-design related_links_inline" id="tmg-related-links">
          <div class="headerOne styleOne">
           <h2>
            <span>
             Related Articles
            </span>
           </h2>
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="firstItem">
            <!-- imagePosition: centre, columnContext: oneSixth -->
            <div class="piccentre containerdiv ">
             <a class=" imageParsely" href="/men/relationships/11448864/Meet-the-man-who-popped-the-question-with-a-global-treasure-hunt.html">
              <img alt="" border="0" height="87" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03219/colorado-1_3219492g.jpg" width="140"/>
              <span class="cornerimagecentre">
              </span>
             </a>
            </div>
           </li>
           <li class="bullet">
            <p>
             <a href="/men/relationships/11448864/Meet-the-man-who-popped-the-question-with-a-global-treasure-hunt.html">
              Three continents, one marriage proposal
             </a>
            </p>
            <span class="relContDate">
             04 Mar 2015
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="body">
          <p>
           The study found the average penis had a girth of 4.59 inches (11.66 cm) when erect, and 3.66 inches (9.31cm) when flaccid.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Range
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The scale the scientists used tracks a range of penis lengths and girths, with measurements of length when erect ranging from 1.9 inches (4.8cm) to 8.3 inches (21.2cm).
          </p>
          <p>
           Psychiatrists who carried out the study said they hope the findings could help men suffering from small penis anxiety because of concern that they do not match up to the norm.
          </p>
          <p>
           Researchers from the Institute of Psychiatry said the analysis  and graphs showing the full range of sizes recorded  could provide insights on what is considered "normal" for penis length and circumference in men.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/men/active/mens-health/11347995/Penises-are-getting-smaller-fact-or-fiction.html">
             Penises are getting smaller: fact or fiction?
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           They said the findings, published in the journal BJU International, may be helpful when counselling men who are worried about their size, some of whom are so distressed that they can even be diagnosed with Body Dysmorphic Disorder.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Measuring up
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Until now, there have been no formal systematic reviews of penile size measurements and no attempts to create a graphical diagram that depicts the distribution of the size of a flaccid or erect penis.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dr David Veale, of King's College London and South London and Maudsley NHS Foundation Trust, and colleague from King's College Hospital NHS Foundation Trust, set out to create such a nomogram of male penis size measurements, covering a wide range of ages and races.
          </p>
          <p>
           A search of medical literature revealed 17 studies with up to 15,521 males who underwent penis size measurements by health professionals using a standard procedure.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/men/active/mens-health/11431842/The-Big-Small-Penis-Party-tackling-a-male-body-image-taboo.html">
              The Big Small Penis Party: tackling male body anxiety
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Dr Veale said: "We believe these graphs will help doctors reassure the large majority of men that the size of their penis is in the normal range.
          </p>
          <p>
           "We will also use the graphs to examine the discrepancy between what a man believes to be their position on the graph and their actual position or what they think they should be."
          </p>
          <p>
           <img align="left" alttext="Seventeen studies have been analysed to come up with the average measurement" caption="Seventeen studies have been analysed to come up with the average measurement" copyright="Alamy" declared-caption="" height="287" name="5-inches" photographer="" refid="3216985" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03216/5-inches_3216985c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <sup>
            Seventeen studies were analysed to come up with the measurement (Alamy)
           </sup>
          </p>
          <p>
           The study was led by the Institute of Psychiatry, Psychology &amp; Neuroscience (IoPPN) at Kings College London and South London and Maudsley NHS Foundation Trust (SLaM).
          </p>
          <p>
           The study did not draw any conclusions about differences in penis sizes across different races.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/science/science-news/11359960/Woman-on-top-is-most-dangerous-sex-position-scientists-conclude.html" target="_blank">
             Most dangerous sex positions revealed
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           One study in 2013 suggested that penis length and girth was greatest in sub-Saharan Africans, intermediate in Caucasians and smallest in East Asians.
          </p>
          <p>
           However, those findings were based on studies which did not meet the criteria of the new research, which was largely made up of Caucasian and Middle Eastern men.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Source of anxiety
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The new analysis suggests age makes little difference, although some of the studies found the flaccid lengths and circumferences of the penises of older men were greater than those of younger men.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dr Martin Baggaley, medical director at South London and the Maudsley, said: Body Dysmorphic Disorder causes a person to have a distorted view of how they look and they can spend an obsessive amount of time worrying about their appearance. This can include worries over their weight, specific parts of their body and, for men, the size of their penis. This can take over someones life and cause a great deal of distress.
          </p>
          <p>
           Hopefully this new study will help reassure those many men who are concerned with their penis size and assist clinicians dealing with BDD.
          </p>
          <p>
           The research cites a large study in 2006 which found that 85 per cent of women were satisfied with their partners penis size, but just 55 per cent of men were happy with the size of their penis.
          </p>

                        '&lt;p id="readNextLabel"&gt;MOST VIEWED IN NEWS&lt;/p&gt;';
                        
				if(settings.articleImage &amp;&amp; settings.articleImage!="null" &amp;&amp; settings.displayThumbnail){
					template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageThumbnail" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;img src="'+ settings.articleImage +'"/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;';
				}
                        
				template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageTitle" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;' + settings.articleTitle + '&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="closeBtn"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;a href="#"&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
   </script>
