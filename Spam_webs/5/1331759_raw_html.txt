       <h2>
        Granite Memorial Headstones with American Headstone Company
       </h2>
       <p>
        One of the certain unpleasantries that every living human being has to deal with is death. The death of loved ones, pets, friends, and family members is often a hard issue for many to deal with. To keep the memory of and honor the deceased person or pet, often mourners choose to adorn their grave sites with elaborate and ornament grave markers or headstones. Choosing the right granite memorial headstones is made easy with the guidance of American Headstones. Feel free to
        <a href="http://www.allheadstones.com/contact/" title="Contact American Headstone for Granite Memorial Headstones">
         contact American Headstones
        </a>
        at (949) 228-7055 for memorial and headstone purchasing information.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="granite-memorial-headstones" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-524" height="171" src="http://www.allheadstones.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/Plauche-Headstone-Layout-Praying-Hands-300x171.jpg" width="300"/>
        There are a number of different choices when it comes to the adorning of a burial site. Before a piece can be chosen, it is important to make yourself aware of the rules and regulations of the particular cemetery or burial site. Once you are aware of the requirements and restrictions that the facility may have, you can now choose which type of marker you and your budget are interested in.
       </p>
       <p>
        Granite memorial headstones are a superior choice when it comes to commemorating your loved ones resting place. Granite is durable in all types of weather and can be used in a variety of different ways. These markers can be custom made to lie flat, or can be constructed to stand vertically. They can be carved or engraved to any specifications, and are available in a variety of different pre-made designs.
       </p>
       <p>
        The designs of the granite gravestones have a large choice of sizes, texts, colors, and different thicknesses as well as unlimited wording on the stone. The pre-made designs that are offered on these stones include such things as wilderness designs, flower designs, children designs, clip art designs, and religious designs. Each category offers their clients a multitude of options to choose from. In the flower designs, there are examples such as a single rose in the center of the marker, small bunches  of roses adorning the corners of the marker, vining plants and flowers that create framing for the words on the monument. There are also other options such as decorative pinecones, decorative grasses, and an assortment of flowers and vines.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="granite-memorial-headstones" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-526" height="128" src="http://www.allheadstones.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/La-Grana-Headstone-Layout-xp-300x128.jpg" width="300"/>
        Throughout our lifetime, we will make millions of decisions. Choosing a gravestone or marker is not necessarily a decision that most people look forward to making. With the help of the professionals at American Headstones, the process of choosing a memorial is quick and easy. We will help you find the perfect stone for your burial needs and individual taste. If you are in need of a granite memorial headstone for your loved one,
        <a href="http://www.allheadstones.com/contact/" title="Contact American Headstone for Memorial Headstones">
         contact American Headstones
        </a>
        at (949) 228-7055.
       </p>
