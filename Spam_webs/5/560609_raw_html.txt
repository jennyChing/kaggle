        <h2>
         Security and Protocols
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         TorGuard offers OpenVPN, PPTP, and L2TP protocols, by default, the company offers 256 bit OpenVPN encryption on all accounts apart from for devices where OpenVPN is not supported, in which case the 128-bit PPTP is offered. It is absolutely a strapping point security wise and one that helps the company score highly on security.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Also, TorGuard is based out of Panama and they take full benefit of the countrys favorable laws on data retention to endow with better anonymity. Precisely, TorGuard does not preserve any logs whatever, or even IP addresses. Therefore, they would have not anything to hand over to authorities when accessible with a court order. Its worth pointing out, though, that the company requirements for some personal information at signup which may be used for promotional purposes.
        </p>

         Customer Service
        </h2>

         TorGuard has an normal customer support system that comprises ticketing system as well as live chat support that may go offline for extended periods of time. Furthermore, many users have expressed dissatisfaction over the lack of response when they send queries through the aforesaid system.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Pros and Cons
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         They scores highly in privacy (no logs), security (256 bit Open VPN encryption accessible by default), remarkable connection speeds and complimentary anonymous email. Clearly, the company goes a long way in protecting users privacy, even though they are not perfect in that area.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Still with privacy, users are required to provide personal details while getting register and the companys terms and conditions state that these may be used for promotional purposes. While, this turns out to be the companys major drawback.
        </p>

         Overall, some of TorGuards features and pricing plans may appear confusing at first for average users, but once they are experience to go, things work out easily
