            <p style="text-align: justify;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Cross country flight had to make an emergency landing on Wednesday because of a dog that repeatedly pooped in the plane. It not only made manypassengers sick, but the smell all over the plane was also terrible.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Passengers of US Airways, 598 ( Los Angeles to Philadelphia ) told that the flight made a diversion to Missouri when the flight attendants used all their paper towels and still the mess was un-cleaned.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             From NBC News:
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Passengers said that the large dog went to the bathroom in the planes aisle as many three times, making people nearby physically ill.
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              The second time after the dog pooped they ran out of paper towels, they didnt have anything else. The pilot comes on the radio, Hey, we have a situation in the back, were going to have to emergency land,' passenger Steve McCall told Inside Edition.
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             One of the passengers named as Chris Lawalso tweeted regarding this weird happening:
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <a href="http://www.mag4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chris-law.png">
              <img alt="chris law" class="size-full wp-image-908 aligncenter" height="531" src="http://www.mag4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chris-law.png" width="535"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <a href="http://www.mag4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chris-law1.png">
              <img alt="chris law" class="size-full wp-image-909 aligncenter" height="564" src="http://www.mag4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chris-law1.png" width="515"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <a href="http://www.mag4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chris-law2.png">
              <img alt="chris law" class="size-full wp-image-910 aligncenter" height="629" src="http://www.mag4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chris-law2.png" width="515"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Finally, the plane was landed to Philadelphia.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Of course, the social media manager of US Airways did expressed his condolences.
