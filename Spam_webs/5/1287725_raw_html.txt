        <p>
         Rita Ora is heading to 'The Voice', replacing the outgoing Kylie Minogue.
        </p>

      <div class="EchoTopic">
       <span id="intelliTxt">
        <span class="articleBody" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/info/rita_ora" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Rita Ora
          </a>
          will replace
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/info/kylie_minogue">
           Kylie Minogue
          </a>
          - who replaced Jessie J - on season 4 of BBC's singing show The Voice. The BBC has announced that the British singer is the latest big name addition to the show and will join Will.i.am, Tom Jones and Kaiser Chiefs singer Ricky Wilson in the big red chairs.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Rita Ora" height="750" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ln/20140917/rita_ora_notting_hill_170914_01/rita-ora-rita-ora-out-and-about_4377187.jpg" style="vertical-align: top; border: 1px solid black; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Rita Ora" width="500"/>
          <span style="font-size: x-small;">
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/rita-ora">
            Rita Ora
           </a>
           is heading to The Voice, where he will replace Kylie Minogue
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Writing on her Twitter page,
          <a href="https://twitter.com/RitaOra" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Rita said
          </a>
          "So excited to be part of; and for my team to win The Voice this series!!"
         </p>
         <p>
          Her fellow coach Will said: "Sir Tom Jones is our rock, Ricky is awesome and it's our pleasure to welcome the very talented Rita Ora. Rita is sweet, spicy and sassy - she's going to be a great addition to the Coaches team."
         </p>
         <p>
          Ora currently ties with Geri Halliwell as the British female with the most solo UK chart toppers, with four. She also recently made her movie debut in the adaptation of
          <em>
           Fifty Shades of Grey
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           More:
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/rita-ora-reveals-calvin-harris-purposely-stopped-her-from-performing-at-teen-choice-awards_4323334">
            Rita Ora reveals Calvin Harris prevented her from performing at Teen Choice Awards
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Voice has been a charting success since its debut series though it's yet to discover a star that has achieved mainstream success, unlike the X-Factor. The third season was won by the soulful baritone-voiced Jermain Jackman, though he's yet to release a debut album. Previous winners were Leanne Mitchell and Andrea Begley but neither enjoyed chart success.
         </p>
         <p>
          Moira Ross, The Voice UK's executive producer, said: "The Voice has gone from strength to strength with each passing year and series four promises to be bigger than ever."
         </p>
         <p>
          Season 4 of The Voice launches in January 2015, one month after The X-Factor concludes its current season.
         </p>
