     <p>
      Just like every other element of web design, color palettes follow fads that are constantly evolving. This years color trends are as diverse as they are compelling; brights and neons combine with greyscale to add some zing to clean, modern styles, while pastel palettes lend a softness to playful, cartoony designs. And from one end of the line to the other, color blocking in any hue has been enthusiastically employed.
      <span id="more-19697">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      Whats great about this new crop of color trends is that they all combine seamlessly with UI considerations, helping to distinguish important elements and break up sections as well as conveying a style or mood.
     </p>
     <h2>
      The Four Trends for 2013
     </h2>
     <p>
      <b>
       1. Greyscale With Bright Accents
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://builtbybuffalo.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://builtbybuffalo.com/', '');">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends on the Web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3448" src="//webdesignledgercom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/colortrends01.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends on the Web"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      This color scheme has greatly increased in popularity of late, and its plain to see why: with only a couple of bright hues to draw the eye, its easy to differentiate important information and interactive elements. And the simplicity and control this gives to the design makes for a polished result with a great user experience.
     </p>

       sites design
      </a>
      , the turquoise and coral colors are alternated throughout to define sections, images, and rollovers, creating a clean and intuitive design that directs attention primarily through its bold and effective color scheme.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       2. Muted Pastels
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.coullon.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.coullon.com/', '');">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends on the Web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3448" src="//webdesignledgercom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/colortrends02.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends on the Web"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      On the other end of the color trend spectrum lays the new wave of designs that feature a mix of soft, neutral tones and light pastels. This trend is all about creating an atmosphere that feels soft and friendly, and is often seen with the rise in cute, cartoony vector illustration, and hand-drawn, organic typefaces.
     </p>
     <p>
      This
      <a href="http://www.coullon.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.coullon.com/', 'example');" target="_blank">
       example
      </a>
      is a perfect intersection between the muted color palette and organic typography that are such a natural match. Theres an unusually large number of different colors being used, but because theyre all such soft tones the effect adds subtle differentiation between topics without becoming visually overwhelming.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       3. Neons and Brights
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="https://www.sailthru.com/2012" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.sailthru.com/2012', '');">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends on the Web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3448" src="//webdesignledgercom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/colortrends03.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends on the Web"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Although neon has been a big trend in fashion and interior design for a while now, its still a fresh and interesting look for web design. Its just one part of the 80s revival thats being incorporated into all different kinds of design of late.
     </p>
     <p>
      Neon colors look great when combined with each other, as sites
      <a href="https://www.sailthru.com/2012" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.sailthru.com/2012', 'like this');" target="_blank">
       like this
      </a>
      clearly demonstrate. They can also be mixed in with other brights for
      <a href="http://www.candybuffet.es/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.candybuffet.es/', 'a look');" target="_blank">
       a look
      </a>
      thats just as fun but that has less of a specific visual reference.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       4. Color Blocking
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://everlovinpress.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://everlovinpress.com/', '');">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends on the Web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3448" src="//webdesignledgercom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/colortrends04.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends on the Web"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Now for the trend thats being combined with every other color trend.
      <a href="http://www.lovecreativeblog.com/2012/08/color-blocking-web-design-trend.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.lovecreativeblog.com/2012/08/color-blocking-web-design-trend.html', 'Color blocking');" target="_blank">
       Color blocking
      </a>
      also traces its roots back to fashion design, and its as versatile as it is functional. Its not really about using any colors in particular; the point is to use crisp fields of color to define categories, sections, or single elements.
     </p>
     <p>
      A part of the larger movement towards flat vector designs, color blocking is a strong look that can often stand in for illustrations or other graphic treatments, so its a great choice for
      <a href="http://everlovinpress.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://everlovinpress.com/', 'minimalist designs');" target="_blank">
       minimalist designs
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <h2>
      Use Color Tools to Tweak the Trends
     </h2>
     <p>
      If youre hesitant about jumping into some of these new trends, youll likely benefit from one of the tools below, which can help you formulate a consistent and workable color palette while still allowing you to play around and experiment with different hues.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       <a href="http://www.colorexplorer.com/default.aspx" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.colorexplorer.com/default.aspx', 'Color Explorer');" target="_blank">
        Color Explorer
       </a>
      </b>
      offers up a comprehensive set of tools, such as color extraction, matching, and conversion, so its easy to create interesting palettes that are perfectly balanced and web safe to boot.
     </p>
     <p>
      Use the Color Matching Tool to alter RGB ratios, hue, saturation, and lightness, choosing different combination algorithms like Analogue or Spit-Complementary colors to view a selection of harmonized variations. This tool is the perfect resource if youre going for a look that uses a multitude of muted pastels.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.colorexplorer.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.colorexplorer.com/', '');">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends on the Web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3448" src="//webdesignledgercom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/colortrends05.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends on the Web"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       <a href="http://www.shutterstock.com/labs/spectrum/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.shutterstock.com/labs/spectrum/', 'Shutterstock Color Spectrum');" target="_blank">
        Shutterstock Color Spectrum
       </a>
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      Its always a great look when the color palette of a site is carried through to the photography. Striking effects can be achieved by colorizing images, but for a less-stylized option, its important to be able to find imagery with the perfect colors for your project.
     </p>
     <p>
      Use this tool as a shortcut to searching stock images by color and keyword, or use
      <a href="http://labs.tineye.com/multicolr/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://labs.tineye.com/multicolr/', 'TinEye');" target="_blank">
       TinEye
      </a>
      for the same functionality, but with images pulled from the creative commons. Theyre both great resources for finding imagery that matches perfectly with a small selection of bright or neon colors.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.shutterstock.com/labs/spectrum/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.shutterstock.com/labs/spectrum/', '');">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends on the Web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3448" src="//webdesignledgercom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/colortrends06.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends on the Web"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      One of the easiest ways to make any site look fresh and up-to-date is to make sure its on trend with its color scheme. So dont hesitate to play around with new ideas; the color palettes of this year are based on solid principles that will look good and stand up to subtle modifications for as long as theyre in use.
     </p>
