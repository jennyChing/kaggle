       <p>
        We regularly hear stuff about the most desirable and expensive cars in the world. Many, way too exotic and impossible to take out on the roads. There are 3 bikes which fit the bill perfectly. 2 of them are street legal while the 3rd is definitely not!
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         MV Agusta F4 1100 CC
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://images.nitrobahn.com.s3.amazonaws.com/cgi-bin/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/MV-Agusta-F4-1100-CC.jpg" rel="lightbox[11]">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2213" height="266" src="http://images.nitrobahn.com.s3.amazonaws.com/cgi-bin/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/MV-Agusta-F4-1100-CC.jpg" title="MV-Agusta-F4-1100-CC" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The F4CC is named after Claudio Castiglioni, the man behind theMV Agusta brand. Its a special edition bike with supreme styling and ensured exclusivity. Only 100 of them will ever be made and each will have a serial number across its steering panel. It can hit a top speed of 195mph and has a 1078cc powerplant but its not meant for the track. The company promotes the bike as a Van Gogh painting, a showpiece which you can also take for a spin. The engine is disproportionately big and this makes cornering a life and death game.
       </p>
       <p>
        It is priced, appropriately, at $135,000 and owning one would makeyou one of just a hundred people in the world to ever own an F4CC. Some may see it as a clever investment considering how the antique markets price their goods.
       </p>
       <p>
        More than just

         auto parts
        </a>
        , shop AutoAnything.com for
        <a href="http://www.autoanything.com/car-covers/20A52424A1.aspx">
         motorcycle covers
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.autoanything.com/atv-motorcycle/20A53010A1.aspx">
         motorcycle ramps
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         MTT Turbine Superbike (Y2K)
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://images.nitrobahn.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/MTT-Turbine.jpg" rel="lightbox[11]">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8018" height="267" src="http://images.nitrobahn.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/MTT-Turbine.jpg" title="MTT Turbine" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        It looks fairly ordinary and sounds nothing like a motorbike. Thats because it technically isnt one. This is only the second thing on 2 wheels to be propelled by a turbine engine. That would bethe same thing which moves a helicopter! It was produced in 2000 and so the MTT Turbine got the name signifying the times  Y2K.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its very awkward to drive because of its length and the time-lag between you giving the accelerator and it hurling itself forward makes it quite a challenge even for the most experienced pilot. It is priced at over $185,000 and for that price you get a superbike which can top 227mph and has an exhaust whichwill melt plastic. (Very useful if you want to keep people off your back at the lights). It is in the Guinness Book of Worl Records as the fastest production motorcycle ever. I have to say that it was helped by the fact that the Japanese have a self imposed 200mph speed limit.
       </p>
       <p>
        It isnt just a jet with a seat though. You have an optional passenger seat and also some interesting add-ons. There is a radar detector equipped with a laser scrambler which can get you past the cops. You can also add a camera in the back to help make parking easier!
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Dodge Tomahawk
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://images.nitrobahn.com.s3.amazonaws.com/cgi-bin/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/Dodge-Tomahawk-.gif" rel="lightbox[11]">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2998" height="199" src="http://images.nitrobahn.com.s3.amazonaws.com/cgi-bin/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/Dodge-Tomahawk-.gif" title="Dodge-Tomahawk-" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        There is no way this huge machine could be made road legal. The stats speak for themselves  An 8277cc, V10 engine which throws out a mind-numbing 500hp and 525 lb-ft of torque. The power was too much for just two wheels to handle and so they put together a new and unique design  4 wheels, 2 in the front and 2 in the rear but separated by a few inches to give the bike-like appearance.
       </p>
       <p>
        I guess Batmans two wheeled machine was modelled on this baby. Dodge claims it can top 400mph. I juts hope no one tries to verify that claim!
       </p>
