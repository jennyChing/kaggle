          <div class="csc-header csc-header-n3">
           <h1>
            PHUKET
           </h1>
          </div>
          <h5>
           Pearl of the Andaman Sea
          </h5>
          <div class="begin">
           <p>
            Thailand is a country of contrasts where ancient temples abut modern skyscrapers, and sleepy traditional villages endure just minutes from teeming cities.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="teaser pull-left">
           <p>
            <img alt="" data-htmlarea-file-table="sys_file" data-htmlarea-file-uid="48730" height="22" src="/uploads/RTEmagicC_teaser1.jpg" title="teaser1.jpg" width="29"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The pristine settings, warm hospitality, and unique culture that Phuket offers visitors are helping the Pearl of the Andaman become the worlds MICE city of the Andaman.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" data-htmlarea-file-table="sys_file" data-htmlarea-file-uid="48732" height="22" src="/uploads/RTEmagicC_teaser2.jpg" title="teaser2.jpg" width="29"/>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Great geographic diversity exists as well, perhaps best exemplified by the immaculate resort destination Phuket, where the islands verdant jungle interior rolls down to some of the planets finest beaches. Theres something for everyone here, including business travelers, and Phuket has become one of Thailands most celebrated MICE cities.
          </p>
          <p>
           As one of the countrys economic hubs, the island has everything necessary for outstanding business events including over 500 hotels in all budget ranges; an efficient international airport that welcomes direct flights from all over the globe; and a dazzling array of venues, activities, and attractions to ensure fun and fulfilling exhibitions, conventions, and team-building functions.
          </p>
          <p>
           Phuket is the premier host of domestic and regional business events like the Phuket International Boat Show, the Asia-Pacific Blood &amp; Marrow Transportation Congress, and Chinas 2012 Amway Meeting. It is poised to become a global leader too, as TCEB continues its promotional campaigns to expand on the 800 MICE events held on the island in 2012, and the 60,000 international business travelers that participated.
          </p>
          <p>
           The pristine settings, warm hospitality, and unique culture that Phuket offers visitors are helping the Pearl of the Andaman become the worlds MICE city of the Andaman.
          </p>
