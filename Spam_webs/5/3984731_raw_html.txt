        SHOCKWAVE (MD) Wins the Eighth Circuit Event #PAKC2014
       </h2>

        <p>
         Harrisburg, PA  Yesterday
         <b>
          SHOCKWAVE (MD)
         </b>
         defeated
         <b>
          Lights On (USA)
         </b>
         to win the annual PennsylvaniaKickball Classic (PAKC). The final score was 5-1 in six innings.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Pennsylvania Kickball Classic(#PAKC2014), the eighth Circuit Event of the National Tour this year attracted 15 competitive kickball teams from across the country including Virginia, Maryland, Massachusetts, DC, andPennsylvania.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Pennsylvania Kickball Classic(Co-ed) was a one-day event held on Saturday, June 7th at Stony Ridge Park in Harrisburg, PA. Over 250 players competed in a round robin group play of four games where the top 8 teams advanced to a single elimination tournament. In the end,
         <b>
          SHOCKWAVE
         </b>
         secured their second Championship and will be taking home a cash prize of $1750, the PA Champions Belt, Champion Shot Glasses from theOfficial Shot Glass Partner of The Circuit,
         <a href="http://www.ashottoremember.com">
          <strong>
           AShot to Remember
          </strong>
         </a>
         , and a $500 coupon from the Official Apparel Partner of The Circuit,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.savageultimate.com/kickball365" target="_blank">
           SAVAGE Ultimate
          </a>
          .
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         All scores for the Pennsylvania Kickball Classiccan be
         <a href="http://bit.ly/PAKC2014Scores">
          found here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         If you would like to discuss this past weekends events head to our
         <a class="broken_link" href="http://forum.kickball365.com/viewforum.php" target="_blank">
          Forum
         </a>
         and join the discussion! You can also find several tweets associated with the Event &amp; Social Parties by searching for the hashtag #PAKC2014.
        </p>
        <p>
         Kickball365 is not your daddys country club. We are the home for kickball competition in America. Kickball365 produces The Circuit, the only national, professional tour of top-flight, competitive kickball tournaments. Our tournaments are one-of-a-kind vacation experiences where teams compete to win their share of $20,000 in cash prizes.
        </p>

         Think your team can compete?
