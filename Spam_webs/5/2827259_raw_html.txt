                 <td width="47%">
                  <p class="ProductDescription">
                   Gravity - is the Balans-style reclining chair that 
is actually 4 chairs in one. Tilt forward and youll rest on plush knee-pads for 
a comfortable working position. Sit upright and it acts like a conventional arm 
chair, with the knee-pads acting as footrests. In the 3rd position, Gravity 
becomes a very relaxing lounge chair after a long hectic day. But in the 4th, 
fully-reclined position, you will enter a sanctuary of unparallel comfort for 
the mind and body - one where youll feel as if you were floating on a cloud. 
Probably the closest youll ever get to experiencing zero-gravity! Close your 
eyes and undoubtedly you fall asleep with ease. And with your legs now above 
your heart, the improved circulation can also bring soothing relief and deserved 
rest to tired feet.
                  </p>
