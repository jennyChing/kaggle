          <h2>
           These Canned Bug Flavors Include Sour Cream &amp; Onion Dung Beetles
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="references">
          <span class="mobileAuthor">
           By: Thomas Wade-West-
          </span>
          Published:
          <span content="2013-09-16" itemprop="datePublished">
           Sep 16, 2013
          </span>
           References:
          <a href="http://www.thinkgeek.com/product/1716/detail/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

         </div>

        <div class="articleText">
         For the experienced or simply curious, edible canned bugs are now available for purchase. These tasty critters are dried, sealed and flavored, making for great shock value for some, or a delicious little treat for others.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         These canned bugs come in seven different flavors including (but not limited to), Bacon &amp; Cheese Grasshoppers, BBQ Bamboo Worms and Sour Cream &amp; Onion Dung Beetles. These cute little guys are not only delicious, but packed with protein for gym-lovers. Moreover, these bugs were manufactured in a FDA-approved facility and the insects contain zero artificial colors, flavors or preservatives. In other words, these are not the bugs you find on your kitchen ceiling, but premium lab-tested bugs, optimized for flavor.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Freak your friends out by busting a tin of Wasabi House Crickets at your next lunch party.
