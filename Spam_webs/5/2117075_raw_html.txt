         <div class="description">
          <p>
           This handcarved snowflake is contoured around the finger and oh so comfortable to wear. The design is frozen to a simple split contoured band, cornered on the backside for a bit of extra design and stability.
          </p>
          <p>
           Everything about the design of this ring is cold, edged, and wintry. Six arms with multiple levels of dimensional decor fan out and away from the center. Ring measures 16.25mm at the top and 1.8mm at the bottom. Square shank is comfortable and very sturdy.  4.1 grams of sterling silver.
          </p>

          </h3>
          <div class="description">
           <p>
            Swirling eddies of water, broken bits of twig, the foam on the edge of a tidal pool... these are forms repeated in the Organic collection. Lines that appear to have grown on your finger, soft and hard edges in contrast to our brick and sawmill confined lives.
           </p>
           <p>
            This design line often overlaps other lines, such as the Root Ring or the Raw Gemstones. You might be surprised at how comfortable these are to wear, with beveled inside shanks on the rings, sandblasted or satin brushed backs on necklaces, and a bend towards the softened line. There are often little hidden 'treasure' stones sprinkled throughout the pieces.
           </p>
