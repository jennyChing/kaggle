     <h2 class="greyTitle">
      Welcome to Moretonhampstead
     </h2>
     <div class="aboutMoreton">
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Moretonhampstead Centre" height="189" src="http://www.moretonhampstead.com/File.axd?Path=%2fhome%2fUpload%2fMoreton+centre.jpg%2f" title="Moretonhampstead Centre" width="230"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong style="font-weight: bold;">
        Moretonhampstead
       </strong>
       - The Gateway to the Moor, is an ancient market town located in the centre of Devon and on the edge of Dartmoor National Park.
      </p>
      <p>
       Its fascinating history goes back over 1000 years to Saxon times. In addition to having many of its own attractions, it is the ideal base to explore some of Britains most spectacular and beautiful countryside including moorland, coast-line and the historic city of Exeter. There is a variety of accomodation to suit your needs, local shops and amenities and lots to keep you and the family occupied.
      </p>
      <p style="font-weight: bold;">
       Major attractions are the renowned:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Moreton Music Day
       </li>
       <li>
        Moretonhampstead Carnival in August
       </li>
       <li>
        Award- winning Green Hill Arts and Heritage Gallery attracting thousands of visitors each year
       </li>
       <li>
        The Motor Museum
       </li>
       <li>
        The Miniature Pony Centre
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <strong style="font-weight: bold;">
        Whatever the time of year, Moretonhampstead has something to offer you! Go to the
        <a href="http://www.moretonhampstead.com/community-groups/moretonhampstead-tourist-information-centre" title="Moreton Tourist Information">
         Moretonhampstead Information Centre page to find out more
        </a>
        or call 01647 440043.
       </strong>
      </p>
