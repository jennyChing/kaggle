          <h2 class="graf--h2 graf--first" id="19d1" name="19d1">
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--h2-strong">
            Fifty-Six
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p class="graf--p" id="520a" name="520a">
           Fifty-six was the amount of time that I was able to stomach the altercation that transpired between Ray Rice and his fianc, Janay. As I sat and watched the
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.tmz.com/2014/09/08/ray-rice-elevator-knockout-fiancee-takes-crushing-punch-video/?adid=hero1" href="http://www.tmz.com/2014/09/08/ray-rice-elevator-knockout-fiancee-takes-crushing-punch-video/?adid=hero1" rel="nofollow">
            video on TMZ
           </a>
           , I wasn't shocked that another story of domestic abuse made the news.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="4d29" name="4d29">
           It took twenty-seven seconds for Ray Rice to render her unconscious as he delivered one swift, left hook, punch to her face. She collapsed into the elevator rail and then slid down to the floor. She didn't move.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="dac9" name="dac9">
           I should have stopped watching then, but I didnt.
          </p>
          <h4 class="graf--h4" id="baab" name="baab">
           I continued to watch.
          </h4>
          <p class="graf--p" id="35cf" name="35cf">
           He pulled her lifeless body across the elevator and towards the door, with one shoe missing and her feet dragging behind her. Then, he dropped her face first on the floor with a thud. I stopped watching. I was enraged!
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="ac2c" name="ac2c">
           I was angry for every victim across the nation who has seen the hand of abuse. I was angry at TMZ for exploiting the video. I was angry at Janay Rice for marrying him a month later on
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.sbnation.com/nfl/2014/5/23/5744964/ray-rice-arrest-assault-statement-apology-ravens" href="http://www.sbnation.com/nfl/2014/5/23/5744964/ray-rice-arrest-assault-statement-apology-ravens" rel="nofollow">
            March
           </a>
           28. I was angry that she publicly lashed out towards his team and the media via
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.sbnation.com/lookit/2014/9/9/6126067/janay-rice-statement-ray-rice-ravens-nfl/in/5191279" href="http://www.sbnation.com/lookit/2014/9/9/6126067/janay-rice-statement-ray-rice-ravens-nfl/in/5191279" rel="nofollow">
            Instagram
           </a>
           , and sent the message to every child in the world that violence and abuse are what true love is.
          </p>
          <h2 class="graf--h2" id="8533" name="8533">
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--h2-strong">
            Four.
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p class="graf--p" id="e0fb" name="e0fb">
           Four is the age of Adrian Petersons son, whose body has been at the receiving end of a tree limb, a hand and a belt, in just the four short years that hes been alive.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="e938" name="e938">
           I should have known better than to go researching the latest and hottest NFL trending topic on Twitter. Its sensitive nature was clearly divulged and noted, but still I began scrolling the news.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="8058" name="8058">
           Tears filled my eyes before I even started typing. My heart was racing with anger. I knew not to read too many articles and to know when enough was enough. I was prepared this time, I thought.
          </p>
          <h4 class="graf--h4" id="5990" name="5990">
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--h4-strong">
            I continued to read and I continued to watch.
           </strong>
          </h4>
          <p class="graf--p" id="9094" name="9094">
           When I came across a few short lines, among millions of words, that left me fuming and speechless with furry, I wasn't surprised.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="a4b8" name="a4b8">
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://houston.cbslocal.com/2014/09/12/exclusive-details-on-adrian-peterson-indictment-charges/" href="http://houston.cbslocal.com/2014/09/12/exclusive-details-on-adrian-peterson-indictment-charges/" rel="nofollow">
            As he spoke with police, Petersons son said
           </a>
           ,
          </p>
          <blockquote class="graf--blockquote" id="bb0d" name="bb0d">
           Daddy Peterson hit me on my face. There are a lot of belts in Daddys closet.
          </blockquote>
          <p class="graf--p" id="030d" name="030d">
           Then, he explained to his mother that Peterson liked switches and belts and had a whipping room.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="734d" name="734d">
           I was enraged.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="0c9d" name="0c9d">
           Yesterday, my daughter slipped and fell on the bathroom floor. Today, I am still upset at myself for not being more proactive in protecting her.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="a1b9" name="a1b9">
           Thats my job as her mom. I cannot fathom disciplining my child in such a way that bruises, welts and cuts, are left on her body. I would never intentionally inflict pain upon her at all.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="c405" name="c405">
           To Adrian Peterson, though,
           <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
            thats just the way of life.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="1d5e" name="1d5e">
           So, the NFL waited a day to
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://espn.go.com/nfl/story/_/id/11537128/nfl-hires-cynthia-c-hogan-former-white-house-official-senior-vp-public-policy-government-affairs" href="http://espn.go.com/nfl/story/_/id/11537128/nfl-hires-cynthia-c-hogan-former-white-house-official-senior-vp-public-policy-government-affairs" rel="nofollow">
            announce their commencement
           </a>
           with former White House official, Cynthia C. Hogan, until one day
           <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
            after
           </em>
           Peterson delivered his preapproved and professionally articulated statement.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="21e6" name="21e6">
           A strategic move in keeping a lid on the latest domestic violence hype, and
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://houston.cbslocal.com/2014/09/15/barkley-every-black-parent-in-the-south-whips-their-kids/" href="http://houston.cbslocal.com/2014/09/15/barkley-every-black-parent-in-the-south-whips-their-kids/" rel="nofollow">
            Charles Barkley helped defend a culture of abuse
           </a>
           claiming,
          </p>
          <blockquote class="graf--pullquote pullquote" id="ab54" name="ab54">
           Whippingwe do that all the time!
          </blockquote>
          <p class="graf--p" id="0d27" name="0d27">
           Making sure to allude that its not a big deal.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="8c6b" name="8c6b">
           <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
            But it is.
           </em>
          </p>
          <h2 class="graf--h2" id="0d6d" name="0d6d">
           5.3
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--h2-strong">
            Million
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p class="graf--p" id="3113" name="3113">
           The average number of men, women and children, who will remain or become victims of abuse or neglect by the end of this year is 5.3 million.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="1fff" name="1fff">
           According to the
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.americanbar.org/groups/domestic_violence/resources/statistics.html" href="http://www.americanbar.org/groups/domestic_violence/resources/statistics.html" rel="nofollow">
            US Department of Justice
           </a>
           , 1,300,000 women and 835,000 men become victims of physical assault at the hands of their own lovers annually. Black women suffering at a rate of 35 percent higher than white women.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="01e3" name="01e3">
           An estimated 686,000 children, in 2012, according the
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://www.childwelfare.gov/can/statistics/stat_natl_state.cfm#childvictim" href="https://www.childwelfare.gov/can/statistics/stat_natl_state.cfm#childvictim" rel="nofollow">
            Child Welfare Information Gateway
           </a>
           , had newly confirmed counts of neglect or abuse80 percent of them at the hands of their own mothers or fathers.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="87eb" name="87eb">
           Approximately 3 million reports are made annually, which include more than 6
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.childhelp.org/pages/statistics" href="http://www.childhelp.org/pages/statistics" rel="nofollow">
            <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
             million children
            </em>
           </a>
           <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
            !
           </em>
          </p>
          <h4 class="graf--h4" id="8a16" name="8a16">
           But we all just continue to watch.
          </h4>
          <p class="graf--p" id="7736" name="7736">
           Meanwhile, CBS News legal analyst Rikki Klieman, an expert wants to refer to the line between discipline and abuse as a
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.cbsnews.com/news/adrian-peterson-case-line-between-discipline-and-child-abuse-a-gray-blur/" href="http://www.cbsnews.com/news/adrian-peterson-case-line-between-discipline-and-child-abuse-a-gray-blur/" rel="nofollow">
            gray blur
           </a>
           ?
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="801a" name="801a">
           We blame it on our culture and our upbringing, while pointing our fingers at the NFL and corporal punishment laws for what our own hands are doing!
          </p>
          <h3 class="graf--h3" id="a3c3" name="a3c3">
           There is a line.
          </h3>
          <p class="graf--p" id="ae25" name="ae25">
           If you are disciplining your child, you are reinforcing positive behaviors and instilling respect. When you cross the line of abuse, you're not only hurting your children, you are teaching them that it is acceptable and natural to hurt other people.
          </p>
          <blockquote class="graf--blockquote" id="06a8" name="06a8">
           All states laws permit the use of reasonable corporal punishment; simultaneously, they all prohibit non-accidentally inflicted serious injury. 
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--blockquote-anchor" data-href="http://scholarship.law.duke.edu/lcp/vol73/iss2/6/" href="http://scholarship.law.duke.edu/lcp/vol73/iss2/6/" rel="nofollow">
            Doriane Lambelet Coleman et al.
           </a>
          </blockquote>
          <p class="graf--p" id="c85d" name="c85d">
           If hitting our children results in a culture of violence and long term disorders due to trauma (e.g. Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder, racing thoughts, severe anxiety, aggressiveness, relationship issues, suicidal ideation, etc.), those
           <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
            are serious injuries.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="7b48" name="7b48">
           If you took your fist and you punched it into the wall would it hurt you? Would you feel it? Yes. If you take a tree limb, belt or wooden spoon, and hit the wall as hard as you could would it hurt you? Would you feel it? No.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="f4be" name="f4be">
           When you punish your child using physical violence, your anger has overridden your actions and you are no longer concerned by your strength or force; when your own hands are hurting and your child is bleeding and bruisedyou are abusing your child.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="a07e" name="a07e">
           <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
            That is the line.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="05fb" name="05fb">
           As a parent, you draw the line and you do not cross it because you love and do not want to hurt your child.
          </p>
          <h2 class="graf--h2" id="927c" name="927c">
           One
          </h2>
          <p class="graf--p" id="ac6b" name="ac6b">
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            One
           </strong>
           famous NFL player had to have a video go viral to get our attention.
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            One
           </strong>
           is the number of people it takes to speak out and bring attention to important causes.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="de2f" name="de2f">
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            One
           </strong>
           is the age of my only daughter, and the number of times I held her in my arms before I knew that I would fight to no end to protect her beyond measure for the rest of my life.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="5f58" name="5f58">
           There is something to be said of our nation when more than 5 million of our own citizens are being abused and neglected, and we do nothing to rise together and change it.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="a697" name="a697">
           That is...
          </p>
          <blockquote class="graf--pullquote pullquote graf--last" data-align="center" id="c65b" name="c65b">
           Until its the beginning of the football season
           <br/>
           and our favorite players start missing from the field.
          </blockquote>
         </div>
