         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           Use WordPress To Start Blogging Today
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="post-meta-author">
           <i class="fa fa-user">
           </i>
           <a href="http://www.make-it-simply-easy-life.com/author/admin/" title="">
            misel
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="tie-date">
           <i class="fa fa-clock-o">
           </i>

          <span class="post-cats">
           <i class="fa fa-folder">
           </i>

          <span class="post-comments">

           </i>

         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.make-it-simply-easy-life.com/use-wordpress-to-start-blogging/">
            <img alt="wordpress" class="alignnone wp-image-1234 size-full" height="300" src="http://www.make-it-simply-easy-life.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/wordpress.jpg" title="Wordpress" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           WordPress is popular choice for a variety of blogger. Want to start a professional-looking
           <a href="http://www.make-it-simply-easy-life.com/advice-for-running-your-own-blog/">
            blog
           </a>
           but arent sure where to start? WordPress is the solution for you. This will help you build a great blog in no time. Keep reading to gain even more knowledge.
          </p>
          <h4>
           How To Blog With WordPress
          </h4>
          <p>
           Use Alt and Title. This lets you add text to your posts. This area is going to let you put in some great SEO keywords within your content, and they also let viewers see what you have on the site when they cant access images.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its easy to incorporate videos on your blog with WordPress. While this might take a little extra preparation on your part, it is worth it. Internet users are a visual group. A video can clearly convey a message that is hard to explain with the written word; this makes video a powerful marketing tool.
          </p>
          <p>
           Avoid designs that are commonplace. It can be tempting to do this since it saves lots of time, but it will not give users the best impression of you.
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre serious about keeping up with your blog, make a posting schedule for yourself. By developing a schedule, you will remain motivated. You can even create a weeks worth of content all at once and tell WordPress when to post it to your blog.
          </p>
          <p>
           Eliminate any content that is not adding value to your blog. This makes your site user-friendly for people of integrity who visit it. Akismet is a useful plugin to filter daily website spam.
          </p>
          <p>
           Remove any special characters in the URLs of your blog posts. They can give search engines a hassle when trying to spider your site, so it is best if you remove them completely. It is also a good idea to shorten URLs to keywords.
          </p>
          <p>
           A clean permalink will make it easier to post blogs with long titles. For instance, if you have a long phrase in a URL, that can seem very long.
          </p>
          <p>
           Organize that media library often. Uploading images straight into the library is certainly appealing, but it does not take long for chaos to set in. You should start with a simple folder structure and go from there. Later, you will be able to find an image you want to use much easier.
          </p>
          <p>
           Make sure you regularly update your plugins. You can make a powerful website using plugins. But like any other software, it is important to update it. Make sure to install the updates so you can keep the plugin running smoothly.
          </p>
          <p>
           You can connect separate posts by using a plugin that allows for internal linking. This type of plugin adds lists of posts at the end of every post. These are selected based on tag relevance.
          </p>
          <p>
           Become familiar with the options and tools that are at your disposal when you blog with WordPress. Its important for you to understand what the different features do and how each option improves your webpage.
          </p>
          <p>
           Schedule posts a few days ahead for your blog in WordPress. You can set it up so that they will be posted at very specific times. Go to Edit, and then find the Publish option. Underneath, there should be something that says Publish Immediately. Next to it is a place to input time and date. Input your time in military format. Hit OK. When the screen that says schedule for pops up, choose it and click on schedule if everything is right.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dont manually approve every comment. Aksimet will take care of it all for you. Naturally, you could manually check comments, but it is easier to have them delivered to you for approval before posting. This will end up saving you quite a lot of time.
          </p>
          <p>
           When searching for plugins, check user reviews prior to selecting any. Anybody who understands how to program can create these plugins. It is important not to use plugins with lots of problems and reported issues. Plugins with a lot of downloads and high ratings are generally safe to use.
          </p>
          <p>
           Before installing WordPress, take some time for education. The more you plan, the better your results will be. You want no special characters visible within your URLs. This can make it hard for search engine spiders to search your site, so delete them.
          </p>
          <p>
           Do you want to get rid of the WordPress clutter? Eliminate a few of the boxes appearing on the page. Do this by using the Screen Options menu selection near the top portion of the screen. You will see a menu to click that gives you the ability to turn off/on what you need and dont need.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you need a host for a WordPress blog, be sure to pick on with installation tools that make the process simple. That way you will not have to worry about creating a separate database. This one click can have the host create its domain for you and its database.
          </p>
          <p>
           WordPress is used by major companies as well as individuals to build websites. The rich array of features make this tool very user-friendly to beginners, yet powerful enough for professionals who want more advanced features to build a robust website. WordPress is a limitless tool that can improve your exposure.
          </p>
