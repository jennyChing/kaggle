     <div class="row about-sunlight clearfix">
      <a href="/about/">
       <h3 class="home-section-head with-border">
        <img src="https://ds5jihom238dx.cloudfront.net/static/img/about-lock.png"/>
        <span>
         Our mission
        </span>
       </h3>
      </a>
      <div class="span9 intro">
       <p>
        The Sunlight Foundation is a national, nonpartisan, nonprofit organization that uses the
        <a href="/tools/" target="_blank">
         tools
        </a>
        of civic tech,
        <a href="/api/" target="_blank">
         open data
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/policy/" target="_blank">
         policy analysis
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/blog/investigations/" target="_blank">
         journalism
        </a>
        to make our government and politics more accountable and transparent to all. Our vision is to use technology to enable more complete, equitable and effective democratic participation. Our overarching goal is to achieve changes in the law to require real-time, online transparency for all government information, with a special focus on the political money flow and who tries to influence government and how government responds. And, our work focuses on the
        <a href="http://sunlightfoundation.com/policy/local/" target="_blank">
         local
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://openstates.org/" target="_blank">
         state
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/policy/federal/" target="_blank">
         federal
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/policy/international/" target="_blank">
         international
        </a>
        levels.
       </p>
       <p>
        These efforts have produced real results. To date, we have served more than 3 billion
        <a href="/api/usage/">
         API calls
        </a>
        (and counting), indicating how much the data we liberate is needed. Our reporting is
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL51CFDA7AB9D2F39A">
         frequently
        </a>
        <a href="http://sunlightfoundation.com/press/">
         cited
        </a>
        by the world's preeminent journalists. Our research has led to
        <a href="/policy/documents/">
         congressional hearings
        </a>
        , and our
        <a href="/tools/">
         tools
        </a>
        have stripped problematic measures from bills. And we recently
        <a href="http://sunlightfoundation.com/blog/2015/03/02/after-sunlight-foia-comprehensive-data-indexes-arrive-on-data-gov/">
         won a major victory
        </a>
        when, at our urging, the federal government agreed to begin the process of releasing all datasets held by federal agencies.
       </p>
       <p>
        As government grows ever-more complex, and as the flow of political money becomes more perverse, we will all need better and more creative tools to ensure that democracy thrives.
       </p>
