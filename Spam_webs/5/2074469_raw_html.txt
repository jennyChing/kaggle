      <h2 class="posttitle">
       <span>
        Brain Power Smoothie (Blueberry Avocado Smoothie)
       </span>
      </h2>
      <div class="postmeta postmeta2">
       <span class="cat1">
        Easy
       </span>
       <span class="cat2">
        2 mins
       </span>
      </div>
      <p class="lead">
       This Brain Power Smoothie (a.k.a. blueberry avocado smoothie) recipe is packed with delicious ingredients that are great for brain health.  Plus, it only takes minutes to make!
      </p>
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blueberry Avocado Smoothie 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17263" height="384" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-1.jpg" width="576"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Healthy eating month continues on Gimme Some Oven today with a smoothie Ive been hooked on lately. Its basically a
       <strong>
        blueberry avocado smoothie
       </strong>
       recipe, but I like to call it my
       <strong>
        brain power smoothie
       </strong>
       because its chocked
       <em>
        full
       </em>
       of tasty ingredients that are good for brain health.
      </p>
      <p>
       And lets be real  Im a total nerd, so anything smart and brainy sounds good to me.
      </p>
      <p>
       Speaking of being nerdy, Im not quite sure why this came to mind, but have I ever told you the story on here about me winning my 4th grade spelling bee? Oh yeah, I rocked it at my elementary school with the word a-u-t-u-m-n,
       <em>
        autumn
       </em>
       . So I was sent to the regional spelling bee to represent the school, but unfortunately I got eliminated on the first round. My word? (Now please picture a packed auditorium full of parents and students with an awkward old man very dryly reading the words.)
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Cleavage.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       (And yes, I asked for the definition.)
      </p>
      <p>
       I think that may have been the most scandalous moment that spelling bee crowd had seen in years. But thanks to that darned a, my spelling bee career didnt go much farther. At least I learned a new definition about splits in rock formations. :)
       <em>
        <br/>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       But I digress
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-17262">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blueberry Avocado Smoothie 6" class="aligncenter" height="864" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-6.jpg" width="576"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       So whether youre also into spelling bees, or (like me nowadays) would just like help remember where on earth you laid your keys, this brain power smoothie is here to help. Its super tasty and sweet, its easy to make, and its oh-so-good for you.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blueberry Avocado Smoothie 2" class="aligncenter size-post-full wp-image-17264" height="864" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-2.jpg" width="576"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The 6 brain health ingredients you need are:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        blueberries
       </li>
       <li>
        avocado
       </li>
       <li>
        banana
       </li>
       <li>
        chia seeds
       </li>
       <li>
        pomegranate juice
       </li>
       <li>
        ice
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Just google em. They are all fantastic food for thought. :)
       <em>
        <br/>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blueberry Avocado Smoothie 3" class="aligncenter size-post-full wp-image-17265" height="852" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-3.jpg" width="576"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       And making this banana avocado smoothie couldnt be easier. Simply add everything to a blender
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blueberry Avocado Smoothie 4" class="aligncenter size-post-full wp-image-17266" height="845" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-4.jpg" width="576"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       and blend until its nice and smooth.
      </p>
      <p>
       Then dive into that yummy berry flavor and just
       <em>
        feel
       </em>
       yourself getting smarter. S-M-A-R-T-E-R,
       <em>
        smarter
       </em>
       . :)
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blueberry Avocado Smoothie 5" class="aligncenter size-post-full wp-image-17267" height="864" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-5.jpg" width="576"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <div class="recipe hrecipe">
         <img align="right" class="photo nopin" nopin="nopin" src="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Blueberry-Avocado-Smoothie-1-160x160.jpg"/>
         <div class="printbutton">
          <a class="icon-print" href="http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/brain-power-smoothie-blueberry-avocado-smoothie-recipe/print/" title="Print Recipe">
           Print
          </a>
          <script id="wk_script" src="http://www.zlcdn.com/javascripts/wk.js" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
          <a class="ziplist-button icon-save add-recipe custom" href="http://www.ziplist.com/webkitchen/button/add_recipe?as_partner=gimmesomeoven&amp;url=http://www.gimmesomeoven.com/brain-power-smoothie-blueberry-avocado-smoothie-recipe/" target="_blank" title="Save to Recipe Box">
           Save
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="postmeta">
          <span class="yield cat3">
           Yield: About 4 cups
          </span>
         </div>
         <span class="item">
          <h2 class="fn">
           Brain Power Smoothie (Blueberry Avocado Smoothie)
          </h2>
         </span>
         <div class="summary" style="clear:left">
          <p>
           This Brain Power Smoothie (Blueberry Avocado Smoothie) is packed with delicious ingredients that are all great for brain health.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="time postmeta">
          <span class="cat1 prep">
           <strong>
            Prep:
           </strong>
           <span class="preptime">
            <span class="value-title" title="PT1M">
            </span>
            1 min
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="cat2 cook">
           <strong>
            Cook:
           </strong>
           <span class="cooktime">
            <span class="value-title" title="PT1M">
            </span>
            1 min
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="cat3 total">
           <strong>
            Total:
           </strong>
           <span class="duration">
            <span class="value-title" title="PT2M">
            </span>
            2 mins
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <h3 id="ingredients">
          Ingredients:
         </h3>
         <div class="ingredients">
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient">
            2 cups blueberries
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient">
            1 cup pomegranate juice (or any berry juice)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient">
            1 cup ice cubes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient">
            1 Tbsp. chia seeds
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient">
            1 ripe banana, peeled
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient">
            half of an avocado, peeled and pitted
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <h3 id="directions" style="clear:left">
          Directions:
         </h3>
         <div class="instructions">
          <p>
           Add all ingredients to a blender and pulse until combined and smooth. If the smoothie is too thick, add more juice. If the smoothie is too thin, add more ice.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="postmeta bottommeta">
          <span class="cat1">
           Difficulty: Easy
          </span>
          <span class="cat1 cat4">
