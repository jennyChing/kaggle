       <div class="title colordefault" style="clear: both;">
        <h4>
         Glencoe Cottages, Scotland
        </h4>
       </div>
       <div class="mbf clearfix block block-system">
        <div about="/accommodations/short-term/glencoe-cottages-scotland.html" class="node node-article node-promoted clearfix" id="node-5972" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
         <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Glencoe Cottages, Scotland" property="dc:title">
         </span>
         <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" property="sioc:num_replies">
         </span>
         <div class="submitted">
          <span content="2010-10-18T23:35:20+08:00" datatype="xsd:dateTime" property="dc:date dc:created" rel="sioc:has_creator">
           Submitted by
           <a about="/users/ed-forteau" class="username" datatype="" href="/users/ed-forteau" property="foaf:name" title="View user profile." typeof="sioc:UserAccount" xml:lang="">
            Ed Forteau
           </a>
           on Mon, 10/18/2010 - 23:35
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="user-picture">
          <a href="/users/ed-forteau" title="View user profile.">
           <img alt="Ed Forteau's picture" src="https://www.wanderingeducators.com/files/styles/userpicture/public/pictures/picture-1-1430905875.jpg?itok=FwLdHuuq" title="Ed Forteau's picture" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
          <div class="field-items">
           <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
            <p>
             Breathtaking views from every direction. The sounds of the
             <em>
              River Tay
             </em>
             running through the backyard. An ecologically progressive set of cottages that let you live in nature, whilst being close to many activities...Nestled in between mountains and river, the
             <strong>
              Glencoe Cottages
             </strong>
             are an extraordinary place to stay. From the welcome bouquet from owner
             <strong>
              Victoria Sutherland
             </strong>
             to the conversations with other guests (who were all repeat guests, which is telling), we LOVED this place.
             <em>
              <strong>
               It felt like home.
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOktfN8nI/AAAAAAAAECM/EbToq8d2pAQ/s400/_DSC0164.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              view from the patio
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOlN_QQHI/AAAAAAAAECQ/Dl04kBUyP48/s400/_DSC0177.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              welcome bouquet
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             An extremely popular location for all kinds of outdoor activities,
             <strong>
              Glencoe
             </strong>
             is a destination not to be missed. However, we loved our cottage so much that we didn't want to leave - we wanted to sit on the patio and grill our dinner, and enjoy the scenes (and sounds) of nature. Glencoe Cottages is not a just a roof over your head or a rest stop from sightseeing - it's a place to experience, to enjoy, to stay. Many people come there for hiking, mountain climbing, kayaking, sailing, skiing, and other outdoor activities. You could spend time there with friends and family, enjoying the sounds of the river Tay flowing over the rocks, or head out and know that you have a home to come to after your exertions.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOhwvBBbI/AAAAAAAAEB8/dY20CZ1lssM/s400/DSC_0090.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              view from the front door
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             From the moment we drove in, we were impressed. Glencoe Cottages has 3 cottages and a bed and breakfast. Owner Victoria Sutherland was one of the most gracious and interesting people we'd met on our travels. She was full of great stories, from tales of the
             <strong>
              <em>
               Harry Potter
              </em>
             </strong>
             films (the scenes from
             <strong>
              Hagrid's Hut
             </strong>
             were filmed at Loch Torren, at Glencoe Cottages) to mountain rescue and committed locals, to her commitment to the environment (she has won awards for the environmentally friendly cottages). You can read our interview with Victoria
             <a href="/accommodations/short-term/glencoe-cottages.html" target="_blank" title="http://www.wanderingeducators.com/accommodations/short-term/glencoe-cottages.html">
              here
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOiRcsRKI/AAAAAAAAECA/sa8-ZaY3YkY/s400/DSC_0095.JPG" style="height:400px; width:268px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              Loch Torren - hill location of Hagrid's Hut in the Harry Potter movies
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsO4n_LOQI/AAAAAAAAECU/O-QCmv9hb-g/s400/DSC_0554.JPG" style="height:400px; width:268px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              Front door
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOj_XzzpI/AAAAAAAAECI/tTXnl8V-kgM/s400/DSC_0150.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              River Tay
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             About every fifteen minutes, we exclaimed at how incredible it was here - such views! And, it felt like home. Our daughter had enough room to run and play (inside and out), and the bunk beds were calling her name. The back yard encouraged play (and bird watching). We went down the yard to the River Tay and she clambered on the rocks. There's a waterfall (with swimming hole) up and around the bend, although we didn't get there. It's a paradise for kids, who will never lack for things to explore.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOjYkZ4dI/AAAAAAAAECE/0foKAYclwhc/s400/our%20backyard%2C%20the%20river%20tay.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              River Tay
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOgi9RIsI/AAAAAAAAEB0/1l0GcjWfwuk/s400/DSC_0082.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              View from the kitchen window
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             Even though the cottages are small, the layout was very roomy, comfortable, and well-designed. The kitchen had all the accoutrements you'd need - high quality pans, dishes, and utensils. The underfloor heating was wonderful! There are comfortable accommodations for up to eight people, and an extremely fast wifi connection. The views from the huge windows were unsurpassed in all of our travels.
             <em>
              <strong>
               We'll be back...
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="Glencoe Cottages" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_z-Jgx7GeH3M/TLsOhBESIlI/AAAAAAAAEB4/YxyhaTIDaME/s400/out%20the%20kitchen%20window.JPG" style="height:268px; width:400px" title="Glencoe Cottages"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              View from the kitchen window
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             For more information, please see:
             <br/>
             <a href="http://www.homeaway.co.uk/p716646">
              http://www.homeaway.co.uk/p716646
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
