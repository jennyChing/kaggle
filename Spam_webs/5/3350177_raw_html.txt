       <h1 class="single-entry-title">
        [ The Distribution ] Stereo Boyz Drago (Remix by XtheDetective)
       </h1>
       <div class="linebreak">
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="https://soundcloud.com/thestereoboyz/drago-remix-by-xthedetective" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/soundcloud.com/thestereoboyz/drago-remix-by-xthedetective');" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Stereo Boyz Drago Brainofbmw Music" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-8959" height="300" src="http://brainofbmw.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Stereo-Boyz-Drago-Brainofbmw-Music-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Stereo Boyz release a free remix of 
         <a href="https://soundcloud.com/thestereoboyz/drago-remix-by-xthedetective" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/soundcloud.com/thestereoboyz/drago-remix-by-xthedetective');" target="_blank">
          <em>
           Drago
          </em>
         </a>
         , the fourth single off their album 
         <strong>
          <em>
           <a href="http://thestereoboyz.bandcamp.com/album/carz-clubz-theaterz" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/thestereoboyz.bandcamp.com/album/carz-clubz-theaterz');" target="_blank">
            Carz, Clubz &amp; Theaterz
           </a>
          </em>
         </strong>
         . The Remix was done by XtheDetective, a fellow Detroit bred, residing in Atlanta.
        </p>
        <p>
         XtheDetective, who is known for rugged beats with a touch of the Golden Era, put his own spin on Drago, turning it into a hard hitting, 90s Rap type track.
        </p>
        <p>
         Grab your free copy via the Stereo Boyz soundcloud. And if you havent checked out the Drago video yet, find a link to it below.
        </p>
        <p>
         The song 
         <a href="http://thestereoboyz.bandcamp.com/track/drago-ft-stereo-crew" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/thestereoboyz.bandcamp.com/track/drago-ft-stereo-crew');" target="_blank">
          Drago
         </a>
          is an ode to overcoming diversities, to being like Rocky, when the world is coming at you like Drago.
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres nothing you cant beat
        </p>
        <p>
         This song is Hip Hop. It isnt about making a song following a formula to produce a hit. This song is about putting your heart into it, about Bars and Revolution.
        </p>
