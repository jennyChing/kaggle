        soon come back on the market as a 2016 model. This is the legendary sports car that will appear with many improvements and novelties.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         2016 Mazda RX7 The exterior design and Change
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        2016 Mazda RX7 will rise, a new modified platform, and with the use of lighter materials will appear with a light weight that will be around  3,000 and possibly even less. Therefore, will show an increase aerodynamics and fuel consumption. As far as exterior design is concerned, it is still unknown how it will look, and what would be the design. However, according to rumors, will have a long veil, long and thin lights that will give an aggressive look. At the rear, fog lights will be redesigned as well. Sporty lines and new designs will surely make it popular among many potential buyers.
       </p>

       <p>
        <strong>
         Interior Design 2016 Mazda RX7
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        It is still early to talk about how the new 2016 Mazda RX7 will look into what the technology and features that will be included. However, we assume that modern equipment will be used in the design and the interior will be made only high quality materials. It will be provided with plenty of infotainment and safety systems. It is a two-seater car, with a stylish cabin and it certainly will meet the needs of enthusiasts and everyone who decides for it.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         2016 Mazda RX7 Engine and Performance
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        According to the latest rumors, brand new 2016 Mazda RX7 would have under the hood a 1.2-liter Wankel-type rotary engine that will be capable of producing around 250 horsepower. It will most likely have two rotors. Strength will be sent to the rear wheels, and it is the only option for now. It will be able to achieve acceleration from 0 to 60 mph in 5.5 seconds. The engine will be paired with a manual transmission, but will appear automatically as well which will be optional.
       </p>

       </p>

        2016 Mazda RX7 will appear on the market sometime during 2016, but nothing has yet been officially confirmed. As for the price, expected to be about $ 30,000. Hyundai Genesis and Ford Mustang will rival the mayor.
