                       <img border="0" height="387" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XbAugsYq_Ys/U5YxxoW-CiI/AAAAAAAAbjI/WJiaKjJyKHI/s1600/obama+1.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       The punchline is predictably sad and yet it still works!
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <br/>
                      And in order to have this joke the thinking Americans among us need to give a big thank you and shoutout to the ignorant ideologues who didn't just vote Obama in once, but did it twice!
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Thanks for nothing libs, two times!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Obama vs The Dictator
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-U3zyUnOA6Tc/U5YuaIkOY7I/AAAAAAAAbis/M3PpY6L0Ugo/s1600/kim.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-U3zyUnOA6Tc/U5YuaIkOY7I/AAAAAAAAbis/M3PpY6L0Ugo/s1600/kim.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Kim Jung Un had NO military experience whatsoever before Daddy made him a four-star general.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      This snot-nosed twerp had never accomplished anything in his life that would even come close to military leadership.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      He hadn't even so much as led a Cub Scout troop, coached a sports team, or commanded a military platoon.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      So he is made the "Beloved Leader" Of North Korea.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Terrific!
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <span class="s1">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <span class="s1">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       Oh crap! On second thought never mind
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7magiMsRVyw/U5YxgDkNz4I/AAAAAAAAbjA/__w4NqGPeyM/s1600/obama.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="225" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7magiMsRVyw/U5YxgDkNz4I/AAAAAAAAbjA/__w4NqGPeyM/s1600/obama.jpg" width="320"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       I'm sorry but
                      </b>
                      <b>
                       I just remembered that the United States did the same thing.
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       We took an arrogant, socialist, communityorganizer, who had never worn a uniform, and made him
                      </b>
                      <b>
                       Commander-in-Chief.
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       A guy who had never had a real job, worked on a budget, as much as managed a lemonade stand or led anything more than a racistACORN demonstration, and we made him "Beloved Leader" of the United States
                      </b>
                      <b>
                       TWICE!!!
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       I'm sorry I brought this up.
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                     </div>
                     <div class="p2">
                      <b>
                       Never mind...
                      </b>
                     </div>
