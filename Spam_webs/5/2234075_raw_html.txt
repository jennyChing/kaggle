      <h1>
       Kim Richards of RHOBH is in Rehab Again
      </h1>
      <div class="post_meta">
       Published: 1st May, 2015
       <i class="ion-ios-infinite">
       </i>
       Categorized:
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/category/alcohol-interventions/" rel="category tag">
        Alcohol Interventions
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/category/alcohol-rehab/" rel="category tag">
        Alcohol Rehab
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/category/alcoholism/" rel="category tag">
        Alcoholism
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/category/celebrities/" rel="category tag">
        Celebrities
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/category/news/" rel="category tag">
        News
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/category/women-and-addiction/" rel="category tag">
        Women and Addiction
       </a>
       <hr/>
      </div>
      <p>
       <img alt="Kim Richards of RHOBH is in Rehab Again" class="aligncenter wp-image-10906" height="404" src="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/wp-content/uploads/iStock_000020663578_Full.jpg" width="382"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Kim Richards, of
       <em>
        The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills
       </em>
       fame, is back in rehab, this not being her first time in treatment for her
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/americans-are-drinking-more-alcohol-than-ever/" target="_blank" title="Americans Are Drinking More Alcohol Than Ever!">
        alcohol addiction
       </a>
       . It seems Richards finally arrived at the decision to get help again after being confronted during an interview with Dr. Phil as well as a recent arrest for public intoxication.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.people.com/article/kim-richards-dr-phil-bombshells-rhobh" target="_blank" title="The 5 Biggest Bombshells in Kim Richards' Dr. Phil Interview">
        <strong>
         5 Revelations from
        </strong>
        <strong>
         Dr. Phil Interview
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        1.
       </strong>
       <strong style="line-height: 1.5;">
        Richards alcoholism began while caring for her mother
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Richards, 50, said she started drinking at night while caring for her now late mother, who died back in 2002. She went on to say, I realized I had started to become a person I didnt like, and I couldnt stand the sight of myself.
      </p>
      <p>
       Richards says that her drinking continued as a result of a series of personal tragedies, such as the brutal murder of her fianc John J. Collett back in 1991 and then more recently, her ex-husband Monty Brinsons current battle with cancer.
      </p>
      <p>
       She said of Collett, crying, There isnt a day that goes by that I dont think about him.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong style="line-height: 1.5;">
        2. Richards lied during the
        <em>
         RHOBH
        </em>
        reunion special
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Throughout season 5 of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, Richards insisted she was still sober, despite her erratic behavior. After taking one of her co-stars pain pills, Richards told concerned costar Lisa Rinna during the reunion special, It is not a
       <a href="http://blog.palmpartners.com/slip-vs-relapse/" target="_blank" title="Slip vs. Relapse">
        slip
       </a>
       , and it is not a
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/go-back-rehab-relapse/" target="_blank" title="Should I Go Back to Rehab after a Relapse?">
        relapse
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Richards insisted that she had not drank in over three years.
      </p>
      <p>
       However, during the interview, she admitted that that was a lie.
      </p>
      <p>
       I did have drinks a couple other times over the past few months. It was wine. I was ashamed to say anything at the reunion, embarrassed, she said. I would have loved to say to the girls, You know what, youre right, but the way they were coming at me, they didnt make it easy.
      </p>
      <p>
       Richards maintained she did not drink at all while filming the reality TV show.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong style="line-height: 1.5;">
        3. Her sister
        <em>
         did
        </em>
        reach out
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       As of the reunions taping, Richards and her sister Kyle, 46, were still estranged.
      </p>
      <p>
       On Dr. Phil, Richards admitted that the fight she had with her sister
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/depression-relapse-can-trigger-addiction-relapse/" target="_blank" title="Depression Relapse Can Trigger Addiction Relapse">
        triggered
       </a>
       her to drink a glass of vodka the night of her arrest.
      </p>
      <p>
       The bottom line is, its the overall outcome of where today is with my relationships with the people I love the most, and thats what really started getting me upset, she explained.
      </p>
      <p>
       Now, the sisters could be on their way to rebuilding their relationship.
      </p>
      <p>
       She actually texted me after my arrest, for the first time in five months, Richards said through tears. She asked me if I was okay.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong style="line-height: 1.5;">
        4. Her future with RHOBH and with co-star Brandi Glanville
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       During the interview, Dr. Phil pointed out that being in the spotlight was a catalyst for Richards relapse, and asked her if she would walk away from
       <em>
        RHOBH
       </em>
       if the pressure gets to be too much.
      </p>
      <p>
       If I cant, yes, I will, she said.
      </p>
      <p>
       Richards son Chad described her relationship with co-star Brandi Glanville, 
       <a href="http://www.orchidrecoverycenter.com/blog/toxic-friendships-6-signs-time-move/" target="_blank" title="Toxic Friendships: 6 Signs Its Time to Move On">
        Toxic
       </a>
       , probably, adding Shes a good friend for you, but you gotta be hanging around people who are supporting your sobriety.
      </p>
      <p>
       Richards responded, Shes never drank in front of me unless were at work, and all the girls are drinking.
      </p>
      <ol start="5">
       <li>
        <strong>
         She turned down Dr. Phils rehab offer
        </strong>
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
