     <h1>
      20 Must See Creative Talks for Despreneurs
     </h1>
     <ul class="stats bbotom">
      <li>
       4,920 Views
      </li>
      <li>
       September 21, 2013
      </li>
      <li>
       Vilnius, Lithuania
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="http://despreneur.com/category/inspiration/" rel="category tag">
        Inspiration
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       5 min read
      </li>
     </ul>
     <div class="socials_holder">
     </div>
     <p>
      I am all about living to inspire and be inspired. As an entrepreneur and designer I get inspired by fascinating success stories, challenges I face myself and mostly people around me.
     </p>
     <p>
      Getting that spark of marvelous energy and belief in what you are going to do is an amazing feeling that I think is the most important thing in life. You are more happy, positive and energetic when inspired which is super good for yourself and people around you. Because guess what? You inspire people surrounding you as they inspire you. Another thing that I love about inspiration that it is very contagious.
     </p>
     <p>
      For the reason of inspiring you to do what you love I collected 20 creativetalks on design, marketing, entrepreneurship, leadership, psychology that are inspiring and will make you a better entrepreneur. Let me know which one is your favorite.
     </p>
     <h3>
      Simon Sinek: How Great Leaders Inspire Action
     </h3>
     <p>
      Simon Sinek presents a simple but powerful model for how leaders inspire action, starting with a golden circle and the question Why?.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/qp0HIF3SfI4" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Tony Robbins: Why We Do What We Do
     </h3>
     <p>
      Tony Robbins discusses the invisible forces that make us do what we do.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/Cpc-t-Uwv1I" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Blake Mycoskie  The Global Leadership Summit
     </h3>
     <p>
      Blake Mycoskie tells of the birth and success of TOMS shoes and challenges the audience with his view of how capitalism can be leveraged for compassionate ministry.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/f5OCcD4qbk8" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Simon Sinek: If You Dont Understand People, You Dont Understand Business
     </h3>
     <p>
      In this wide-ranging talk, ethnographer and leadership expert Simon Sinek discusses the importance of trust, authenticity, and meaning.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/llKvV8_T95M" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Alexis Ohanian: Making Something People Love
     </h3>
     <p>
      Renowned entrepreneur and Reddit cofounder Alexis Ohanian, will inspire you to think of unique ways to connect with your customers, and to build a community of users who want your business to succeed.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/KKYkmYuk5l8" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Steve Jobs 2005 Stanford Commencement Address
     </h3>
     <p>
      Drawing from some of the most pivotal points in his life, Steve Jobs, chief executive officer and co-founder of Apple Computer and of Pixar Animation Studios, urged graduates to pursue their dreams and see the opportunities in lifes setbacks.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/UF8uR6Z6KLc" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Joshua Foer: Step Outside Your Comfort Zone and Study Yourself Failing
     </h3>
     <p>
      When most of us learn a new skill, we work to get just good enough and then we go on autopilot. We hit what journalist and bestselling author Joshua Foer calls the OK Plateau, where we have gained sufficient skills for our needs and we stop pushing ourselves.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/gWl4liX4PNw" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Jack Groetzinger: Building Your Team
     </h3>
     <p>
      It takes an outstanding team to create a successful company. In this class SeatGeek cofounder Jack Groetzinger shares strategies and tips for Passive and Active Recruiting, conducting interviews, making an offer, and finding a cofounder.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/SK27TqxV4ko" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Seth Godin: The Tribes We Lead
     </h3>
     <p>
      Seth Godin argues the Internet has ended mass marketing and revived a human social unit from the distant past: tribes. Founded on shared ideas and values, tribes give ordinary people the power to lead and make big change.
     </p>
     <div style="padding:10px; font-weight:bold; margin-top:1em; margin-bottom:1em; border-left:4px solid #2980B9">
      <span style="font-weight:bold">
       You May Also Like:
      </span>
      <a href="http://despreneur.com/inspiring-sites-of-the-week-no-14/" rel="nofollow" style="font-weight:bold;" target="_blank">
       Inspiring Sites of the Week No. 14
      </a>
     </div>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/589tH-wtCak" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Why Happiness is the New Productivity (The Story of Mindvalley)
     </h3>
     <p>
      Vishen talks about Flow: The Ultimate State of Human Existence. He then shared 10 tips on how to master this state and how to bring it to teams, families, and groups you lead.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/T8ZFU4FoNvY" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Steven Johnson: Where Good Ideas Come From
     </h3>
     <p>
      One of our most innovative, popular thinkers takes on-in exhilarating style-one of our key questions: Where do good ideas come from?
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/NugRZGDbPFU" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Drive: The Surprising Truth About What Motivates Us
     </h3>
     <p>
      This lively RSA Animate, adapted from Dan Pinks talk at the RSA, illustrates the hidden truths behind what really motivates us at home and in the workplace.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/u6XAPnuFjJc" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Web 2.0 Expo NY: Gary Vaynerchuk (Wine Library), Building Personal Brand Within the Social Media Landscape
     </h3>
     <p>
      Building Personal Brand Within the Social Media Landscape. Note: This presentation contains adult language.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/EhqZ0RU95d4" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Richard Branson: Life at 30,000 Feet
     </h3>
     <p>
      Richard Branson talks to TEDs Chris Anderson about the ups and the downs of his career, from his multibillionaire success to his multiple near-death experiences  and reveals some of his (very surprising) motivations.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/SWMqe994sNw" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Will Smith Shares His Secrets of Success
     </h3>
     <p>
      Will Smith shares his success philosophy.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/q5nVqeVhgQE" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Julie Burstein: 4 Lessons in Creativity
     </h3>
     <p>
      Radio host Julie Burstein talks with creative people for a living  and shares four lessons about how to create in the face of challenge, self-doubt and loss.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/sY0Pf_pfqCI" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Seth Godin: How to Get Your Ideas to Spread
     </h3>
     <p>
      In a world of too many options and too little time, our obvious choice is to just ignore the ordinary stuff. Marketing guru Seth Godin spells out why, when it comes to getting our attention, bad or bizarre ideas are more successful than boring ones.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/xBIVlM435Zg" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Sir Ken Robinson: Do Schools Kill Creativity?
     </h3>
     <p>
      Sir Ken Robinson makes an entertaining and profoundly moving case for creating an education system that nurtures (rather than undermines) creativity.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/iG9CE55wbtY" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      David Kelley: How to Build Your Creative Confidence
     </h3>
     <p>
      Is your school or workplace divided into creatives versus practical people? Yet surely, David Kelley suggests, creativity is not the domain of only a chosen few. Telling stories from his legendary design career and his own life, he offers ways to build the confidence to create.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/16p9YRF0l-g" width="640">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Amy Cuddy: Your Body Language Shapes Who You Are
     </h3>
     <p>
      Social psychologist Amy Cuddy shows how power posing  standing in a posture of confidence, even when we dont feel confident  can affect testosterone and cortisol levels in the brain, and might even have an impact on our chances for success.
