     <h1>
      7 Sleep Mistakes You Dont Know Youre Making and How to Fix Them
     </h1>
     <div class="single-post-meta">
      <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-55 photo" height="55" src="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/55a91885479e60d709560cdfe39d8724?s=55&amp;d=http%3A%2F%2F1.gravatar.com%2Favatar%2Fad516503a11cd5ca435acc9bb6523536%3Fs%3D55&amp;r=G" width="55"/>

         <p>
          Our bedtime routines are often formed by the life we lead. That makes sense, but our activity when were awake can often affect our sleep negatively. These seven sleep mistakes are some of the most common, so we wanted to look at how they can be fixed.
         </p>
         <p>
          To give you a point of reference:
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           22%
          </strong>
          of people sleep poorly most nights.
          <br/>
          <strong>
           47%
          </strong>
          of people say stress or worry keeps them awake at night.
          <br/>
          <strong>
           49%
          </strong>
          of people have never taken steps to help them sleep.
         </p>
         <p>
          Does the last figure sound strange to you? Good. It should. When something isnt right, we aim to fix it, and the only way sleep is different is that its much more important.
         </p>

          7 Sleep Mistakes You Dont Know Youre Making  An Infographic
