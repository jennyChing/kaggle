        <h1 class="entry-title">
         mini pumpkin pie croissants
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2011-10-11T00:01:00+00:00">
          October 11, 2011
         </span>
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://www.shakentogetherlife.com/author/CK2000ck" rel="author">
            Keri
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://www.shakentogetherlife.com/2011/10/taste-this-tuesday-fall-baking-series_10.html#comments">
           97 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <span>
           Okay, I know Ive been a little MIA around here  I promise, its not because I dont love you  I DO!! Ive looked at every comment and checked out every link from the
           <a href="http://shakentogetherlife.com/2011/09/holiday-sweet-swap-link-party-is-open.html">
            {holiday sweet swap}
           </a>
           and I am going to start doing some featuring  I swear! And I have so many ideas brewing and blooming in my head for crafty tutorials and delightful recipes to share with you!
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span>
           <br/>
          </span>
          <span>
           We actually took a four day whirlwind vacation to Dauphin Island off the coast of Alabama to spend some time with family and friends. We had absolutely GORGEOUS weather  not a cloud in the sky, breezy, mid 70s  and spent some time without TV, school, work or other distractions. I didnt realize how much we needed the time away until we were there.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span>
           <br/>
          </span>
          <span>
           And now that Ive had a little R&amp;R, I am back and you have my undivided attention! So lets get this weeks
           <strong>
            {taste this}
           </strong>
           recipe started!! This is the 3rd installment of my fall baking series and here is a little preview 
          </span>
          <span>
           <br/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div>
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-3pTmJw0WOrc/TpOAsABe1eI/AAAAAAAAA1w/d2xIcf8RDXc/s1600/PumpkinPie+FALL+BAKING+BLOG.jpg">
           <span>
            <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-3pTmJw0WOrc/TpOAsABe1eI/AAAAAAAAA1w/d2xIcf8RDXc/s400/PumpkinPie+FALL+BAKING+BLOG.jpg" width="400"/>
           </span>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          <span>
           <br/>
          </span>
          <span>
           These are pretty darn tasty, if I do say so myself
           <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.shakentogetherlife.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
           What are they, you ask? Delightfully rolled up, sinfully delicious
           <strong>
            mini pumpkin pie croissants
           </strong>
           !!
          </span>
         </p>
         <div>
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-psC5LN7XnNU/TpOI79D4_wI/AAAAAAAAA2I/piYELjsyq8Y/s1600/PumpkinPieCroissants+main+BLOG.jpg">
           <span>
            <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-psC5LN7XnNU/TpOI79D4_wI/AAAAAAAAA2I/piYELjsyq8Y/s400/PumpkinPieCroissants+main+BLOG.jpg" width="400"/>
           </span>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.shakentogetherlife.com%2F2011%2F10%2Ftaste-this-tuesday-fall-baking-series_10.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F2.bp.blogspot.com%2F-psC5LN7XnNU%2FTpOI79D4_wI%2FAAAAAAAAA2I%2FpiYELjsyq8Y%2Fs400%2FPumpkinPieCroissants%2Bmain%2BBLOG.jpg&amp;description=mini%20pumpkin%20pie%20croissants%20...%20perfect%20for%20breakfast%20or%20dessert%20%2F%2F%20%7Btaste%20this%7D%20at%20shaken%20together" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I started my croissant kick with my
          <a href="http://shakentogetherlife.com/2011/09/taste-this-tuesday-mini-chocolate.html" target="_blank">
           mini chocolate nutella croissants
          </a>
           best served warm and gooey right from the oven! and I knew I had to try some version of these for fall. So, here you have it
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-22" style="border: 1px dashed;">

            <div class="item b-b">
             <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">

             <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
              mini pumpkin pie croissants
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
             </div>
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
               Yield:
               <span itemprop="recipeYield">
                32 mini pumpkin pie croissants
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="zlclear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 tubes of Pillsbury crescent rolls
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             </li>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2">
              Pumpkin Cream Cheese Filling
             </div>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              4 oz. (1/2 block) softened cream cheese
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              One cup of canned pumpkin (not pumpkin pie filling)
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
              1-2 tablespoons pumpkin pie spice
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
              3-4 tablespoons sugar (granulated or powdered)
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             </li>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8">
              Sugar &amp; Spice
             </div>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
              4 tablespoons sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 tablespoon pumpkin pie
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Unroll each crescent roll and slice in half lengthwise.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Beat together the cream cheese, canned pumpkin, pumpkin pie spice and sugar until fluffy.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spread about 1 - 1 1/2 teaspoons of filling over each crescent roll.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Roll up, starting with the wider end.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Stir together the additional sugar and pumpkin pie spice.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Roll each mini pumpkin pie croissant in the sugar/spice mixture and place on baking sheet.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Bake at *375 for 13-15  minutes until lightly browned and baked through.  (I use baking stones, so baking times may vary.)
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-notes">
             Notes
            </p>
            <div id="zlrecipe-notes-list">
             <p class="notes">
              Adjust the sugar and/or spice to your particular tastes - you can make these as sweet or spicy as you like!  These are best served warm from the oven and can be served with ice cream or whipped cream, too!
             </p>
             <p class="notes">
              Original recipe found at shakentogetherlife.com.

         <p>
          <span>
           The best part of this recipe is rolling each little mini croissant in a sugar &amp; spice mixture that covers every inch of these little bite sized treats! Its definitely a little messy and good cause for licking your fingers when youre done!
          </span>
         </p>
         <div>
          <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_z4rmJH1EtE/TpOJJW8xb6I/AAAAAAAAA2Q/ebrUKDu5fK4/s1600/Pumpkin+Pie+Croissants+spice+BLOG.jpg">

            <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_z4rmJH1EtE/TpOJJW8xb6I/AAAAAAAAA2Q/ebrUKDu5fK4/s400/Pumpkin+Pie+Croissants+spice+BLOG.jpg" width="400"/>
           </span>

         <p>
          <span>
           These mini pumpkin pie croissants are sweet but not
           <em>
            so-much-so! S
           </em>
           ublime little bites of pumpkin pie heaven that are easy and portable and the perfect addition to any Fall celebration. Or even just because!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.shakentogetherlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/PumpkinLayerDessert-013.jpg">

          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           You will probably have some leftover filling so just pop it in the fridge. It is delicious on bagels or toast and it makes a great dip for cinnamon graham crackers, green applesand ginger snaps!
          </span>
