           ICYMI: Batman In Famous TV Scenes
          </h1>

          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Ever wonder what it might be like if the Caped Crusader was in your favorite tv scenes? I dont know if itd be exactly like this (its kind of a campy/wacky take on Batman), but its still a pretty entertaining and funny take on what it would be like. He goes from Breaking Bad to Friends,there is a pretty wide variety of shows the Dark Knight shows up in, and it is all pretty enjoyable.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
             <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/Xn1zX8tZEtE?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
             </iframe>

           </p>
           <p>
            Wanna hear a knock-knock joke?
