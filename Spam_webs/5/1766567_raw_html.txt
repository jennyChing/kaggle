                  There is no doubt that gold, 24-carat HTC One M7 caused gasps of many when it was first seen in October last year. It is a phone in which the casing is mostly done in gold, and not the so-called Golden color. The company will now give this phone through sweepstakes. All you need to do is start to follow HTC on Twitter and re-tweet about this contest, and the contest runs until 29 June at midnight. Conditions for participation are on the HTC UK Facebook page, but knowing this Sweepstakes, it is almost certain that the citizens of our country are not eligible for rewards. Estimated value of the Golden HTC One portables is 2750 pounds, which is about 3450 euros.
