        <p>
         STAY is the first book in the contemporary romance series -the Alexa Reed series. The book setting is sultry South Beach, Florida.
        </p>
        <p>
         Alexa Reed has some secrets from her past that shed rather pretend dont exist and that if she doesnt talk about them then they never really happened. Unfortunately her secrets haunt her and prevent her from moving forward towards her destiny.
        </p>
        <p>
         In Stay, Alexa is just going through the motions of life at the moment and has lived like that for almost a year until the night she finally agrees to go out with her friends to celebrate her best friends (Luke) birthday. While heading down stairs to join her girlfriends for some dancing she collides with the man that will inevitably change her life in more ways than one. Is Alexa ready to be awakened from her drama free, somewhat easy going life? As everything that was dormant for so long becomes amplified in seconds upon meeting this man, Alexa has a difficult time allowing fate to run its course.
        </p>
        <p>
         Due to the troubles and secrets from her past relationship that are resurfacing since this meeting, Alexa is having a hard time opening up about it and dealing with it and would rather run than show this man she barely knows the woman she refuses to be any longer. While, everything she is feeling is real and genuine, Alexa panics and shuts down and goes off the grid until things calm down and she can actually process all these new emotions and feelings. This method of dealing seems to work for her, but it causes concern for everyone else that truly cares for her.
        </p>
        <p>
         Julian Bauer is the Latino owner of Hotel Del Marco, which is where the club/bar is located. Julian is incredibly sexy, smart, confident, patient. He had no idea that being at the club that night would result in a collision that awakens a heated desire and lead him to an emotional and physical connection with a woman who may be the very one to test his patience and irrevocably change his life. Perfect on the outside, Julian has his own battles with the demons of his past and has remained unattached for many years.
        </p>
        <p>
         When Alexa and Julian are together everything is heightened, but things can go from extremely great to very bad in a matter of seconds. Julian believes what is between him and Alexa is real and made to last, but Alexa has a very hard time trusting this man who is everything she has ever wanted.
        </p>
        <p>
         All of this is new and exciting, yet so scary for Alexa who doesnt trust easily and wants to run when things get heavy and too much. Julian wants to be the man she runs to and not from.
        </p>
        <p>
         Will Alexa open up and give this one of a kind love a chance? Will Julian be able to convince Alexa he is the man for her to Hold On To?( book two entitled Hold On)
        </p>
