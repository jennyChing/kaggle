         <p>
          <strong>
           Where do I begin to tell you about all the things there are to love about having an online business?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          My business means a lotto me, and Im so thankful that I took the time to build it and make it one that I can be proud of.
          <span style="font-family: Arial, serif;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            <br/>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://www.carolamato.com/10-things-to-love-about-having-an-online-business/" target="_blank">
           <img alt="10 Things to Love About Having an Online Busines" class="aligncenter wp-image-155335 size-full" height="304" src="http://cdn.carolamato.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10-things-to-love-about-having-an-online-business.jpg" width="402"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Owning and operating a
          <strong>
           thriving online business
          </strong>
          means that you have arrived into the 21st Century and have all the knowledge and technology to make your dreams come true.
         </p>
         <p>
          Online businesses are every bit as demanding as brick and mortar businesses  plus, you have to have exceptional self-discipline. But, to me, there is nothing more rewarding or that can afford me ways to be creative, meet new people and be able to grow in ways I never dreamed possible.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are so many things to love about owning and running an online business. Here are just ten of them:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            You are the boss.
           </strong>
           When you run your own business, you dont have to answer to another person higher up in the company. You make the decisions, set your own schedule to work and decide your budget.
           <a href="http://www.carolamato.com/10-things-to-love-about-having-an-online-business/">
            <img alt="you are the boss" class="aligncenter wp-image-155331 size-full" height="328" src="http://cdn.carolamato.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/you-are-the-boss.jpg" width="445"/>
           </a>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="2">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Free to be creative and innovative
           </strong>
           . Its fun to be creative and to think of innovative ideas you can incorporate in your own business  especially when that business is all yours.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="3">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Your values and integrity are the foundation of your business
           </strong>
           . You dont have to adhere to the values of another business that you might not agree with if youre an employee. You set your own standards and the world judges you and your business by them.
           <div class=" sb-sp" style="height:1px; padding:0; margin:0; ">
           </div>
          </li>
         </ol>

           <strong>
            Your income is only limited to the time and effort you put into it
           </strong>
           . The skys the limit for the growth of your business and how much money you need or want to make. Your time and effort will translate to dollars in your pocket  rather than waiting for that yearly review and raise (maybe).
           <a href="http://www.carolamato.com/10-things-to-love-about-having-an-online-business/">
            <img alt="the sky's the limit" class="aligncenter wp-image-155341 size-full" height="166" src="http://cdn.carolamato.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/the-skys-the-limit.jpg" width="382"/>
           </a>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="5">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Minimal startup investment needed
           </strong>
           . You dont need a college degree or to be a tech whiz to run a
           <em>
            successful online business.
           </em>
           Later on, you can add bells, whistles and gadgets that will help your business run on a more automatic level, but in the beginning, very little is needed.
           <br class=" sb-br"/>
           <br class=" sb-br"/>
           After my domain was registered,I started with a
           <strong>
            shoestring budget
           </strong>
           of $29permonth (

             real business

            that is
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             sustainable
            </span>
            and
            <em>
             scalable
            </em>
            .
           </div>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="6">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Environmentally friendly
           </strong>
           . Running a business online can reduce your footprint on the earth. You wont be driving a car or taking a train or subway to work each day, and you can put all your business online rather than using paper and other means. (
           <em>
            I just love having NO commute!)
           </em>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="7">
          <li style="text-align: left;">
           <strong>
            Live and work anywhere in the world.
           </strong>
           Youre not stuck in a place you dont want to be because of a job. Anywhere theres Internet access is a place where you can work and enjoy online business success.
           <br class=" sb-br"/>
           <br class=" sb-br"/>
           A wonderful example of someone living and working from all over the worldis Ryan Biddulphover at
           <a href="http://www.bloggingfromparadise.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bloggingfromparadise.com/', 'Blogging from Paradise']);" target="_blank">

            </a>
           </span>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="8">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Constantly meeting new people on a global basis
           </strong>
           . How incredible that we can sit down at a desk in our own home or office and reach millions of people all over the world to teach, befriend, exchange information with and sell to. Its one of the best perks of how technology and having an online business works today.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="9">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Opportunities abound
           </strong>
           . Opportunities to grow your business and boost your income come in a constant stream online. There are opportunities to joint venture with other online entrepreneurs, new information that can mean a huge difference in the way you do business and much more. Making good connections early on is a very wise thing to do.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <ol start="10">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Realize your passion
           </strong>
           . Whatever your passion is, having an online business can help you realize it in the best way possible. If you have passion for spending time with your family, setting your own schedule helps.
           <br class=" sb-br"/>
           <br class=" sb-br"/>
           You may have a passion for crafting and can offer helpto a specific target audience in that particular market. The Internet can open the doors you need to realize your deepest passion.
