                 <p>
                 </p>
                 <div class="body field">

The Japanese government coined theterm in 1982, a translation of shinrin-yoku, whichliterally means taking in the forest atmosphere.

The concept was pioneered by high-profiledestination spas, like Canyon Ranch Living, whichlaunched in Miami Beach in 2007, and Miraval Living,which planned to open in New York City around2008. And the first wave also saw destination spaslike Red Mountain Resort &amp; Spa (Utah) and Termedi Saturnia (Italy), and numerous hotel/resort spas,add condos and villas.
