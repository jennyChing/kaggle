      <h1 class="heading-sm">
       Dividing Decimals
      </h1>
     </div>
     <div class="number_descp">
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Dividing a decimal number by another decimal number, is similar to just dividing the numbers without the decimal points.Here decimal point plays a significant role for it by determining how small or large the quotient is. The placement of the decimal point should be observed properly.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <hr/>
      <div class="panel panel-orange">
       <div class="panel-heading">
        <h3 class="panel-title no-margin">
         <i class="fa fa-paw">
         </i>
         Steps for Dividing Decimals
        </h3>
       </div>
       <div class="panel-body">
        <ul>
         <li>
          <i class="fa fa-hand-o-right">
          </i>
          Move the decimal point of the divisor to the right until the divisor will be a whole number.
         </li>
         <li>
          <i class="fa fa-hand-o-right">
          </i>
          Move the decimal point of the dividend to the right by the same number of places that the decimal point of the divisor moves. Place a zero (0) if the decimal point have moved to a decimal place without a digit.
         </li>
         <li>
          <i class="fa fa-hand-o-right">
          </i>
          Divide the dividend by the divisor with the decimal point in a new position.
         </li>
        </ul>
       </div>
      </div>
     </div>
     <hr/>
     <h2 class="learn-example-heading font-bold zero-bottom-margin font-size-20">
      Decimal Division Examples
     </h2>
     <h5 class="example padding-top_15">
      Example 1:
     </h5>
     <div class="panel-group acc-v1 learn-acc zero-bottom-margin" id="accordionOne">
      <div class="panel panel-default">
       <div class="panel-heading">
        <h2 class="panel-title">
         <a class="accordion-toggle" data-parent="#accordionOne" data-toggle="collapse" href="#collapseOne">
          Divide 13.568 / 2.12
          <i class="fa fa-chevron-left arrow-alignment">
          </i>
         </a>
        </h2>
       </div>
       <div class="panel-collapse collapse" id="collapseOne">
        <div class="panel-body">
         <h5 class="margin-left-five">
          <i class="fa fa-check-square">
          </i>
          <span>
           6.4
          </span>
         </h5>
         <h4 class="h4-heading">
          Explanation:
         </h4>
         <div class="funny-boxes funny-boxes-top-sea zero-bottom-margin explanation-padding">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Step 1:
           </strong>
           Move the decimal point of the divisor to the right until the divisor will be a whole number.
          </p>
          <p class="margin_left_30">
           `2.12` `to` `212`.
           <br/>
           The decimal point moves two decimal places to the right.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Step 2:
           </strong>
           Move the decimal point of the dividend to the right by the same number of places that the decimal point of the divisor moves.
          </p>
          <p class="margin_left_30">
           `13.568` `to`		`1356.8`
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Step 3:
           </strong>
           Divide the dividend by the divisor with the decimal point in a new position.
          </p>
          <table class="zero-border margin_left_30">
           <tr>
            <td>
            </td>
            <td>
             `6.4`
            </td>
           </tr>
           <tr>
            <td>
             `212`
            </td>
            <td class="left-top-border">
             `1356.8`
            </td>
           </tr>
           <tr>
            <td>
            </td>
            <td>
             `1272`
            </td>
           </tr>
           <tr>
            <td>
            </td>
            <td class="top-border center-text">
             `848`
            </td>
           </tr>
           <tr>
            <td>
            </td>
            <td class="center-text">
             `848`
            </td>
           </tr>
           <tr>
            <td>
            </td>
            <td class="top-border center-text">
             `0`
            </td>
           </tr>
          </table>
          <p>
           Therefore, 13.568 / 2.12 = 6.4
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
     </div>

          </p>
          <p class="margin_left_30">
           `56.1 to 561`.

           The decimal point moves one decimal place to the right.
          </p>

           <strong>
            Step 2:
           </strong>
