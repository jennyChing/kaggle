      <h2 class="title">
       Mt. Sinai, NY - Fluke Fishing
      </h2>
      <div class="meta">
       <span class="author">
        By Capt Andy LoCascio -  10/02/2006 - Short URL: http://bit.ly/13KR3KO
       </span>
       <span class="viewed">

       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="content">
       <p>
        <img alt="Fluke Fishing" class="alignleft wp-image-7301" height="188" src="http://neangling.com/wp-content/uploads/2006/10/2006MtSinaiFluke.jpg" width="251"/>
        Captain Barbara Fusco of
        <a href="http://www.luv2fishcharters.com/" target="_blank">
         Luv 2 Fish Charters
        </a>
        joins Andy and Rich for a very special dayfishing with very light tackle for
        <a href="http://neangling.com/species/fluke/">
         fluke
        </a>
        (summer
        <a href="http://neangling.com/species/flounder/">
         flounder
        </a>
        ). The guys travel out to Mt. Sinai, NY to fish the shallow waters for some hard fighting fluke. The bite is red hot and all the fish are caught using small bucktails on light spinning tackle.
       </p>
       <p>
        Fluke fishing is a critical element of the Northeast summer fishery. The regulatory climate can be frustrating and the fishery management is questionable. However, fluke (summer flounder) are prolific and continue to provide anglers with a reasonable fishery,
       </p>
       <p>
        If you have questions about any of the equipment used during the show, please
        <a href="http://neangling.com/contact-us/">
         contact us
        </a>
        .
