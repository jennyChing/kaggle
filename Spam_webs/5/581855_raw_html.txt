     <header>
      <h2 class="node__title node-title">
       <a href="/us/news/kelly-announces-first-annual-christmas-benefit-concert">
        Kelly Announces First Annual Christmas Benefit Concert
       </a>
      </h2>
     </header>
     <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
      <div class="field-items">
       <div class="field-item even">
        <p>
         Grammy Award winner Kelly Clarkson will perform her first annual benefit concert, Miracle on Broadway, at Nashvilles Bridgestone Arena on Saturday, December 20. Clarkson has tapped a star-studded special guest lineup of artist friends including Reba, Trisha Yearwood, Garth Brooks, Ronnie Dunn, Kacey Musgraves, Paramores Hayley Williams, Charles Esten, Meghan Trainor and more to sing their versions of classic Christmas favorites, as well as join her in performing original hits from her platinum selling Christmas album, Wrapped In Red.
         <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
          All proceeds will benefit The Fruition Fund of The Community Foundation of Middle Tennessee with a portion of the proceeds benefitting four of Nashvilles finest charities: Monroe Carell Jr. Childrens Hospital at Vanderbilt, Monroe Harding Childrens Home, Second Harvest Food Bank, and Thistle Farms.
         </span>
         <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
          For all the up-to-date concert information and artist announcements, stay tuned to
          <a href="http://www.KellyClarkson.com">
           www.KellyClarkson.com
          </a>
          and @KellyClarkson.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love my adopted hometown of Nashville with all of my heart and I am so honored to be able to give back to the community alongside some of my favorite artists and friends for my first annual Miracle on Broadway benefit concert! says Clarkson. I thank all of the artists joining me, as well as the people of Nashville for making me feel so welcome over the last 7 years I have called it home!"
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
          Tickets go on sale Saturday, October 18 at 10am via Livenation.com, all Ticketmaster
         </span>
         <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
          outlets, charge by phone at 1-800-745-3000 or box office.
         </span>
        </p>
