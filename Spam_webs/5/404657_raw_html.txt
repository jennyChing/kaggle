       <h1 class="entry-title">
        How-Tuesday: Last-Minute Gifts
       </h1>
       <img alt="Etsy.com handmade and vintage goods" class="main" src="https://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/gems.jpg"/>
       <div class="share-byline">
        <p class="photo-by">
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="byline">
        <img alt="julieincharge" class="avatar photo avatar-default" height="75" src="https://img0.etsystatic.com/007/0/5190440/iusa_75x75.20791550_p31n.jpg" width="75"/>
        <p class="store-by">
         Story by
         <a class="profile" href="https://blog.etsy.com/en/authors/julieincharge/">
          Julie Schneider
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="published">
         Published on
         <span class="posted">
          Dec 20, 2011
         </span>
         in
         <a class="category" href="https://blog.etsy.com/en/category/make/">
          Make
         </a>
        </p>
        <!-- get photo credits -->
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="https://blog.etsy.com/en/authors/julieincharge/">
          <img alt="julieincharge" class="author-photo" src="https://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2013/09/ap_julie1.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The holidays are nearly here! If that sentence has left you gasping for air and double-checking the calendar, sure that I am wrong, dont worry: theres still plenty of time to whip up something handmade for the loved ones on your list. In todays
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/tags/how-tuesday/">
          How-Tuesday
         </a>
         round-up, youll find a collection of 25 tutorialsto help you create special, personalized gifts.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
         Sweet Treats
        </h3>
        <div class="mceTemp" style="text-align: center">
         <dl>
          <dt>
           <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/sweet-treats.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-127778" height="376" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/sweet-treats.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </dt>
         </dl>
        </div>
        <p>
         Time to fire up your oven! Homemade baked goods are a holiday classic. Make some
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2011/cardamom-sugar-cookies/">
          cardamom sugar cookies
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2011/how-to-make-chocolates/">
          handmade chocolates
         </a>
         , or
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2011/spiced-pumpkin-seed-brittle/">
          spiced pumpkin seed brittle
         </a>
         for those with a thriving sweet tooth. Make a big batch of
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2011/eatsy-granola-with-a-free-conscience/">
          granola
         </a>
         to jar up for healthy crunchy friends (see below for jar ideas).
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
        </h3>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
         Classy Containers
        </h3>
        <div class="mceTemp" style="text-align: center">
         <dl>
          <dt>
           <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/containers.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-127825" height="500" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/containers.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </dt>
         </dl>
        </div>
        <p>
         Dress up that little something-something in the finest of handmade attire. Try
         <a href="http://www.readymade.com/projects/learn_a_lesson_in_label_making/next_step#steps" target="_blank">
          etching glass jars
         </a>
         , make your own
         <a href="http://poppytalk.blogspot.com/2010/03/artmind-tutorial-fiber-bowls.html" target="_blank">
          fiber baskets
         </a>
         , or revamp an old cookie tin into a handy
         <a href="http://www.serenitynowblog.com/2011/12/diy-decorative-chalkboard-tin.html" target="_blank">
          chalkboard container
         </a>
         that will outlive that batch of cardamom cookies and serve as a useful item around the house.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
         Cups &amp; Mugs
        </h3>
        <div class="mceTemp" style="text-align: center">
         <dl>
          <dt>
           <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/mugs.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-127826" height="497" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/mugs.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </dt>
         </dl>
        </div>
        <p>
         Once youve got a set of Pebeo paint pens made for glass or porcelain, the possibilities are endless (and pretty fast). Make a
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2010/how-tuesday-hand-painted-teacup-and-saucer-with-louchelab/">
          teacup and saucer
         </a>
         for a coffee or tea aficionado, draw a
         <a href="http://www.creaturecomfortsblog.com/home/2011/6/8/diy-simple-custom-painted-mugs-for-teachers-more.html" target="_blank">
          handwriting paper motif mug
         </a>
         for your favorite teacher, or make customized
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2010/how-tuesday-glass-painting-with-yevgenia/">
          painted glassware
         </a>
         or a
         <a href="http://doecdoe.blogspot.com/2011/02/how-i-use-porcelaine-pens.html" target="_blank">
          cross-stitch-inspired mug
         </a>
         for just about anyone on your list.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
         Paper Gems
        </h3>
        <div class="mceTemp" style="text-align: center">
         <dl>
          <dt>
           <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/paper-gems.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-127893" height="558" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/paper-gems.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </dt>
         </dl>
        </div>
        <p>
         I love paper for many reasons  its incredibly versatile, affordable, easy to recycle and repurpose, and lends itself to experiments and quick projects. Make
         <a href="http://poppytalk.blogspot.com/2011/11/diy-globe-garland.html" target="_blank">
          globe garlands
         </a>
         from recycled maps for a faraway friend with a case of wanderlust. Fold up jewel-toned
         <a href="http://www.minieco.co.uk/paper-gems/" target="_blank">
          paper gems
         </a>
         for a treasure chest effect. Print out and fold
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2008/how-tuesday-weekly-planner-with-julie-schneider-aka-yoursecr/" target="_blank">
          weekly planners
         </a>
         for a list-making fiend, or make
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2011/how-tuesday-paper-dolls/">
          paper dolls
         </a>
         for an imaginative kid.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
         Tools of the Trade
        </h3>
        <div class="mceTemp" style="text-align: center">
         <dl>
          <dt>
           <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/forthecrafty.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-127929" height="700" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/forthecrafty.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </dt>
         </dl>
        </div>
        <p>
         Why not make your artist, crafter, or designer frienda handy tool to support their practice and inspire fresh creativity? Make a
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2011/how-tuesday-make-a-hanging-notepad/">
          recycled hanging notepad
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://erinzam.com/blog/2007/06/26/match-books/" target="_blank">
          mini matchbook sketchbook
         </a>
         for notes and sketches. Create a
         <a href="http://lifeblessons.blogspot.com/2010/10/making-artwork-my-travel-map-craft.html" target="_blank">
          custom bulletin board
         </a>
         for a studio wall  just the thing for pinning up visual inspiration or even making a visual map of Etsy sales or contacts across the country. Using an old wool sweater, stitch up a
         <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/2009/how-tuesday-sweater-computer-cozy-from-refashioned-bags/" target="_blank">
          laptop sweater cozy
         </a>
         ; you can also scale this patternfit just about any device. For the crafty set, sew a
         <a href="http://alexiaabegg.squarespace.com/free-patterns-tutorials/2010/11/13/the-skinny-pincushion.html" target="_blank">
          skinny pincushion
         </a>
         designed to nestle right up against a sewing machine, or try your hand at making a
         <a href="http://rhythmofthehome.com/2011/11/make-your-own-knitting-nancy-yarn-craft/" target="_blank">
          knitting mushroom
         </a>
         , a classic knitting tool for making I-cords.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center">
         Pet Presents
        </h3>
        <div class="mceTemp" style="text-align: center">
         <dl>
          <dt>
           <a href="http://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/petlove.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-127951" height="608" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2011/12/petlove.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </dt>
         </dl>
        </div>
        <p>
         Shower a pet lover with quick gifts for their furry friends. Pay tribute to a precious pup with a
         <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/how-to/pet-silhouette-tote-bag" target="_blank">
          silhouette tote bag
         </a>
         , or whip up a
         <a href="http://www.purlbee.com/dog-leash/" target="_blank">
          handmade leash
         </a>
         with your choice of cotton webbing and ribbon for a stylish trip to the dog park. Cat toys are a fun and quick gift. Try making a few
         <a href="http://www.momdot.com/how-to-make-a-tulle-pom-pom-ball-tutorial" target="_blank">
          pom-pom toys
         </a>
         or a
         <a href="http://www.livestream.com/etsy/video?clipId=pla_3f97f060-3109-47dc-83fc-772aa457f680" target="_blank">
          catnip stuffed mouse toy
         </a>
         . Meow!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         Happy handmade holidays to you and yours!
        </p>
