      </div>

      <div class="entry clear">
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        This is for sure a unique apartment, designed and decorated by the apartments owner, architect Arash Nourinejad and his wife Kristina Lykke Tnnesen. His passion for drawing and building has given this apartment some creative solutions.Arash Nourinejad has designed and custom made the sofa in the living room that folds out over different levels. The quadratic and asymmetric sofa consists of handmade storage boxes of ash veneer. It utilizes the space and gives more seating spaces than what a normal sofa would do in this space, and creates a way more interesting corner. The
        <a href="http://www.ideastosteal.com/2011/02/overdimensjonerte-gulvlamper/" target="_blank">
         overdimensionedfloor lamp
        </a>
        creates a cool and stylish touch to it. The delightful colour palette of
        <a href="http://www.ideastosteal.com/category/farge/bla/" target="_blank">
         blue
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.ideastosteal.com/category/farge/brun/" target="_blank">
         brown
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.ideastosteal.com/category/farge/hvit/" target="_blank">
         white
        </a>
        goes throughout the apartment.
       </p>

         Restaurant Stork  Amsterdam
        </a>
       </h2>
      </div>
      <!--end post header-->
      <div class="entry clear">
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Empty industrial warehouses is the new inventive way to create new modern spaces. Use of old space and convert it in to either apartments, as we often see as great successful and beautiful projects, or commercial spaces as high quality restaurants is still a big trend.
        <a href="http://www.restaurantstork.nl/EN/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.restaurantstork.nl']);" target="_blank">
         Restaurant Stork
        </a>
        is situated in an old industrial building at businesspark De Overkant, and is overlooking the waterfront.There have been different actors involved in the development and transformation of this former warehouse.
        <a href="http://www.cube-architecten.nl/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.cube-architecten.nl']);" target="_blank">
         Cube Architecten
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.soluz.nl/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.soluz.nl']);" target="_blank">
         Soluz
        </a>
        are on contract to oversee the whole redevelopment ofthe De Overkant site. In the transformation of Stork local design studio
        <a href="http://interiorshock.nl/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://interiorshock.nl']);" target="_blank">
         Interior Shock
        </a>
        took part of what today is Europes biggest fish restaurant. They kept the raw finishes that characterises a warehouse so it still has a industrial feel, added texttiles to soften up and elements as old cable reels that helps build the seafood concept. Summertime you can have a seat outside and enjoy the sunny side of the river.
