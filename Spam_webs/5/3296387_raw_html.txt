        <p>
         Phone overuse can lead to 3 serious mental illness. Read on to discover if youre at risk before its too late.
        </p>
        <p>
         In todays society it seems almost like it is second nature to
         <a href="http://www.addictiontips.net/phone-addiction-signs/">
          have some type of cellphone or smartphone
         </a>
         in front of you all of the time.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are many people who can conduct their entire day of work and all of their socializing
         <a href="http://www.addictiontips.net/phone-addiction-symptoms/">
          without ever putting their phone down
         </a>
         , using it for everything that comes along.
        </p>
        <p>
         As smartphones become more and more prevalent in the world today
         <a href="http://www.addictiontips.net/phone-addiction-study/">
          scientists and studies
         </a>
         have taken much more of an interest in the notion of their being smartphone addiction and what type of an effect this can actually have on a person.
        </p>
        <p>
         All it takes is
         <a href="http://www.addictiontips.net/phone-addiction-facts/">
          a little bit of analysis and research
         </a>
         and you can find notions pretty easily about how using a smartphone all of the time can actually have some type of adverse effect on you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are 15 facts about the brain of a phone addict and some of the things that can happen to you as a result of being addicted to your smartphone.
        </p>
        <h2>
         1. Phone addicts run a higher risk for developing depression
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="depression phone addict" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-455" src="http://cdn.addictiontips.net/media/1-depression-phone-addict-300x250.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <div class="twitter-button single-twitter-button">

        <p>
         People are always on the lookout for ways that can lead to the path of depression.
        </p>
        <p>
         Many doctors and psychologists have developed a variety of theories and studies that showcase different reasons that can bring about depression in many individuals and one of the things they are looking closely at today is people who are suffering from some type of phone addiction.
        </p>
        <p>
         While phone addiction is not in itself classified as an actual disorder at this time, many who are studying the idea have come across those who find the compulsion to use their smartphones and cellphones all day long for everything are more susceptible to having bouts of depression in their lives.
        </p>
        <p>
         Scientists are not clear as to the exact reasoning behind this just yet, but many feel there may be a correlation between the overuse of a phone as a means of staying in touch with the world leading to feelings of isolation and loneliness, which can bring about a state of depression.
        </p>
        <h2>
         2. Those with a phone addiction are more likely to suffer from some type of Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="ocd phone addict" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-456" src="http://cdn.addictiontips.net/media/2-ocd-phone-addict-300x250.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <div class="twitter-button single-twitter-button">
         <a href="#" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'Share', 'Twitter-sharethis', '/phone-addiction/brain-phone-addiction/'); socialPopup(event, 'http://twitter.com/home?status=15+Facts+About+The+Brain+Of+A+Phone+Addict+http%3A%2F%2Fwww.addictiontips.net%2Fphone-addiction%2Fbrain-phone-addiction%2F');return false;">
          <span class="button-icon">
           <img alt="twitter icon" data-lazy-src="http://cdn.addictiontips.net/wp-content/themes/addictiontips/img/tw.png" height="20" src="http://cdn.addictiontips.net/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="20"/>

        <p>
         There does seem to be a correlation between those that use their smartphone excessively and for nearly every aspect of their life and those that may suffer from a form of OCD.
        </p>
        <p>
         Very often those with OCD need to have things highly organized in a certain way and things must be tightly regimented to run at certain times.
        </p>
        <p>
         Smartphones feed into this notion by providing you with the precision that you may need in life by giving you ways for regular notifications and alarms to help keep things running smoothly the way you may need them to run.
        </p>
        <p>
         This can lead to a compulsive behavior pattern where people may feel the need to use the smartphone for everything because it is correct and organized all of the time, never steering them in the wrong direction.
        </p>
        <h2>
         3. Phone Addicts may suffer from a lack of social skills
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="lack social skills" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-457" src="http://cdn.addictiontips.net/media/3-lack-social-skills-300x250.jpg"/>
