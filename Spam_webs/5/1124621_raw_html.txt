                 <h2 class="date-header">
                  <span>
                   Monday, July 13, 2015
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <div class="date-posts">
                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-E2NmKQjsyL0/VaAGGU55n7I/AAAAAAAADwI/cXrbV1Vrq5k/s640/IMG_2359intro.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>
                    <meta content="1751957416729779882" itemprop="blogId"/>
                    <meta content="9121269887982306774" itemprop="postId"/>
                    <a name="9121269887982306774">
                    </a>
                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://scientificseamstress.blogspot.com/2015/07/flutters-on-peasant-quick-tutorial.html">
                      Flutters on a Peasant! a quick tutorial!
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-9121269887982306774" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-E2NmKQjsyL0/VaAGGU55n7I/AAAAAAAADwI/cXrbV1Vrq5k/s1600/IMG_2359intro.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-E2NmKQjsyL0/VaAGGU55n7I/AAAAAAAADwI/cXrbV1Vrq5k/s640/IMG_2359intro.jpg" width="480"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Welcome to yet another Sisboom pattern hack! If you want to see more
                      <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/sciseamstress/" target="_blank">
                       check out our pinboards
                      </a>
                      ! We have them for all the patterns and hacks always get pinned :)
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Grab your
                      <a href="https://www.etsy.com/ca/listing/87908450/sis-boom-leighanna-easy-girls-peasant?ref=shop_home_active_2" target="_blank">
                       Leighanna peasant pattern
                      </a>
                      and let's get started!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      To make flutter sleeves you take the sleeve pattern so you know the length to cut your flutter rectangles. I just estimated on the width. enough for the casing and the hem and about 1.5 inches in between.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sHuBuGzlR7A/VZ6kNAk77FI/AAAAAAAADvI/rRk8oYwrnVc/s1600/2015-07-08%2B18.44.28.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="225" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sHuBuGzlR7A/VZ6kNAk77FI/AAAAAAAADvI/rRk8oYwrnVc/s400/2015-07-08%2B18.44.28.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Next sew the side seams together for the main dress
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kFxka47t9gk/VZ6kLGPI3xI/AAAAAAAADvA/AhU89SzcVus/s1600/2015-07-08%2B18.47.07.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="225" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kFxka47t9gk/VZ6kLGPI3xI/AAAAAAAADvA/AhU89SzcVus/s400/2015-07-08%2B18.47.07.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Grab some bias binding. single fold works best. and then pin around both arm holes and sew in that crease as sen in the second photo down here:
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_-0we33I_4g/VZ6kNdEGvyI/AAAAAAAADvM/N8mB-QHdvCk/s1600/2015-07-08%2B18.55.36.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="225" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_-0we33I_4g/VZ6kNdEGvyI/AAAAAAAADvM/N8mB-QHdvCk/s400/2015-07-08%2B18.55.36.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-IIXOhJUEWHA/VZ6kjZALfII/AAAAAAAADvY/-HDA8sUccqg/s1600/2015-07-08%2B19.00.09.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="225" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-IIXOhJUEWHA/VZ6kjZALfII/AAAAAAAADvY/-HDA8sUccqg/s400/2015-07-08%2B19.00.09.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Now you will fold the bias down and sew to enclose that edge.
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-5zZ3Kv7ttwE/VZ6kjZzGumI/AAAAAAAADvc/NQA87TUUjgg/s1600/2015-07-08%2B19.00.21.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="360" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-5zZ3Kv7ttwE/VZ6kjZzGumI/AAAAAAAADvc/NQA87TUUjgg/s640/2015-07-08%2B19.00.21.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Sew the hems on your flutters and zig zag or serge the short ends of the flutters and then fold downthe end and pin onto the sleeve hole. line up the tops with the main dress and sew along the bias binding line. (yes, this is the easy way... you could also put on and fold into the bias binding, but this was some late night sewing and for a kids item it is good for play even if not fancy inside....)
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-77dDrY1mfZY/VZ6kkKiF37I/AAAAAAAADvk/GIxb5v-SViM/s1600/2015-07-08%2B19.08.09.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="225" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-77dDrY1mfZY/VZ6kkKiF37I/AAAAAAAADvk/GIxb5v-SViM/s400/2015-07-08%2B19.08.09.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Then you just finish your top edge with the casing as per the pattern skipping the slit. and hem the bottom.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-thBfAxfJfG4/VZ6mUCiQlAI/AAAAAAAADv0/bTukcx_jWxo/s1600/2015-07-08%2B19.58.19.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-thBfAxfJfG4/VZ6mUCiQlAI/AAAAAAAADv0/bTukcx_jWxo/s640/2015-07-08%2B19.58.19.jpg" width="522"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      All done and plain adorable! I love the narrowness of this peasant - perfect for adding on some fun like free motion applique or embroidery :)
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>

                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-fvqCgFLcL0k/VaQVrR7I5dI/AAAAAAAADwc/s8n7E0_1pZY/s640/IMG_2335.jpg" width="444"/>
                      </a>
