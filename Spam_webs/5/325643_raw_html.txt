        <p>
         With a surface area of 2,289 square meters, the scarily huge vertical garden of the
         <a href="http://www.cdl.com.sg/treehouse" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Tree House
         </a>
         condominium in Singapore is certainly no shrinking violet. Its so huge, it recently won the condo a
         <a href="http://www.straitstimes.com/news/singapore/environment/story/the-worlds-largest-vertical-garden-its-right-here-singapore-2014061" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Guinness World Record
         </a>
         for having the worlds biggest vertical garden. Not that theyre useless at all: the cooling walls of green scale all the way up to the 24th floor and are targeted to help the building attain at least half a million dollars worth of savings from energy and water.
