Most of the women today face sensitive skin problems caused due to cosmetic products. Undoubtedly, you have always preferred natural products to protect, soothe and take care of your skin.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            My search for herbal or natural products acquainted me to a few good brands of beauty products. I would recommend 5 effective, organic and completely natural brands, which you cant resist having. Today Ill share with you the highlights of the zero-chemical brands, as well as my experience with each.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <strong>
             Rustic Art
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <img alt="Rustic art logo" src="http://www.jessica-jia.com/sites/default/files/images/beauty%20products/RusticArt.JPG" style="margin: 5px; border: 5px solid black;" title="Rustic art logo" width="300"/>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            I have been blown away by every product I have tried from Rustic Art ranging from soaps to moisturisers and lotions that are bound to refresh you. Gentle, yet effective, all the products are skin nourishing and perfect for anyone with sensitive or dry skin. Comprising of nourishing and calming natural extract, the products are fantastic for taking away impurities and excess oil, without stripping the skin of its natural softness.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <strong>
             Forest Essentials
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <img alt="Forest Essentials" src="http://www.jessica-jia.com/sites/default/files/images/beauty%20products/Forest-Essentials.jpg" style="margin: 5px; border: 5px solid black;" title="Forest Essentials" width="300"/>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            Enriched with all the natural goodness, the products from Forest Essentials are my personal favourites. The skin care formulations use strong natural extracts and actives, avoiding synthetic compounds to produce an amazing product range. For anyone with problem-prone skin, this one is for you (although I have fairly clear skin, still preferred using these products). Moreover, the ingredient list of the products are herbal and pure.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <strong>
             Lotus Herbals
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <img alt="Lotus Herbals" src="http://www.jessica-jia.com/sites/default/files/images/beauty%20products/lotus-logo.jpg" style="margin: 5px; border: 5px solid black;" title="Lotus Herbals" width="300"/>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            Lotus Herbals products have herbal formulations, which are inspired by Ayurveda. Containing natural herb extracts, the products are good for protecting, healing and enhancing your face, body and hair. With a wide range of products including sun blocks, lip balms and herbal face wash, you can get a fresh radiant and fair skin.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <strong>
             The Natures Co
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <img alt="The Nature's co" src="http://www.jessica-jia.com/sites/default/files/images/beauty%20products/thnaturesco.jpg" style="margin: 5px; border: 5px solid black;" title="The Nature's co" width="300"/>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            A one stop shop for an array of natural and wellness products is The Natures Co. The products embodies the goodness of nature that inspired them. Use the products including bath salts, body butters, lip balm, soap, shampoo, and other wellness products to get a wholesome, holistic natural experience.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <strong>
             Shahnaz Husain
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            <img alt="Shahnaz Husain" src="http://www.jessica-jia.com/sites/default/files/images/beauty%20products/banner-shahnaz.png" style="margin: 5px; border: 5px solid black;" title="Shahnaz Husain" width="300"/>
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            You can bask in the glory of Shahnaz Hussain products ranging from anti-aging cream to diamond exfoliating scrub. For attaining a perfect skin, Shahnaz facial kit can be an ideal choice. Furthermore, the products integrate well-being into everyday life by keeping the natural benefits intact.
           </p>
           <p class="MsoNormal">
            You can pick from the umpteen number of products to find the one that best suit your requirements. I hope you get an impeccable skin with these products!
           </p>
