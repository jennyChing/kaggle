       </i>

       <i>
        "Using a laser on traditional acupuncture points allows us to provide a non-invasive service with no pain. This is very good for people who have a phobia about needles. Its basically the same as using the needles but we use a laser instead. Laser is particularly good for treating conditions that involve damaged tissue as the laser has an effect on the micro-circulation and this helps repair that tissue. Pain relief and improvement in movement can be immediate.

        "
