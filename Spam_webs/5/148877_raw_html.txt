        <img alt="Harvest Vancouver Vegan Noodles" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-19766" height="640" src="http://www.sweetonveg.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/harvest-vancouver-vegan-noodles.jpg" title="Noodles at Harvest Community Foods " width="480"/>
        <br/>
        <em>
         Front: Udon noodles with shiso, tofu, shiitake and green onion
         <br/>
         Back: Rice noodles with hazelnut and smoked chili, SOLEfood Farms greens

       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://harvestunion.ca/" target="_blank" title="Harvest Community Foods">
         Harvest
        </a>
        is a cozy little grocery &amp; eatery on Union Street in Vancouver. The grocery items are the type of goodies I love to fill my kitchen with. The noodle dishes are perfect comfort food for a chilly day.
