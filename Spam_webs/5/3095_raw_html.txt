          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           In honor of last weeks Valentines Day, here are the top 10 cutest movie couples ever.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           10. Anna and Kristoff  Frozen (2014)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-0 el_before_av_image avia-builder-el-first avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Anna-Kristoff.gif?46e63e" title="Anna Kristoff"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He starts off ambivalent but her quirky nature and inability to shut up, wins him over in the end. They are truly adorable.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           9. Rosie and Alex  Love Rosie (2014)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-1 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Love-Rosie.jpg?46e63e" title="Love Rosie"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           They are best friends for years and years and in their heart of hearts, each of them know that they are the one. But as Rosie so eloquently puts it, stuff keeps on getting in the way.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           8. Katniss and Peeta  The Hunger Games (2012-2015)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-2 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Katniss.jpg?46e63e" title="Katniss"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           In a you-scratch-my-back-Ill-scratch-yours kind of fashion, they take it in turns to save each others lives over and over again. Perfectly matched and unfailingly cute even though theyre pretending to be in love.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           7. Alan and Joan  The Imitation Game (2014)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-3 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Imitation-Game.jpg?46e63e" title="(L-R) KEIRA KNIGHTLEY and BENEDICT CUMBERBATCH star in THE IMITATION GAME"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The only character in the whole film who really and truly understands him. Their engagement, although only one of convenience, is perfect.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           6. Allie and Noah  The Notebook (2004)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-4 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-Notebook.gif?46e63e" title="The Notebook"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           They meet when they are teenagers, fall in love but then are separated by the evil parents until they bump into each other years later. And of course, their love is rekindled.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           5. Tim and Mary  About Time (2014)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-5 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/About-Time.gif?46e63e" title="About Time"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Their relationship is just so cute. They have such a laugh together.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           4. Peter Parker and Gwen Stacy  The Amazing Spider-Man (2012 and 2014)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-6 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Amazing-Spiderman.gif?46e63e" title="Amazing Spiderman"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The scene when he asks her out, has to be one of the best-acted, awkward highschool-love scenes, ever portrayed in a film.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           3. Flynn and Rapunzel  Tangled (2010)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-7 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Tangled.gif?46e63e" title="Tangled"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           I just love everything about this film! For a Disney character, Flynn is a very realistic portrayal of masculinity in how he pretends not to care but definitely does in the end and protects her against all odds.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           2. Jack and Rose  Titanic (1997)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-8 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Leo.gif?46e63e" title="Leo"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-9 el_after_av_image el_before_av_image avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kate.gif?46e63e" title="Kate"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Irrespective of their class and aspirations in life, this tragic pair get over every barrier in their path. Until the ultimate one and they are separated forever.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align: center;">
           1. Elizabeth and Mr Darcy  Pride and Prejudice (2005)
          </h4>
          <div class="avia-image-container av-styling- avia-builder-el-10 el_after_av_image avia-builder-el-last avia-align-center " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <div class="avia-image-container-inner">
            <img alt="" class="avia_image " itemprop="contentURL" src="http://filmaware.filmaware.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Pride.gif?46e63e" title="Pride"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
