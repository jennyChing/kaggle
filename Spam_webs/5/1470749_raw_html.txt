       <center>
        <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-24934" height="575" src="http://bhmaincdn.breatheheavy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/split.jpg?eb246f" title="split" width="399"/>

       <br/>
       Britney and Jason broke up, reports
       <a href="http://www.tmz.com/2013/01/11/britney-spears-jason-trawick-break-up-split-divorce/#ixzz2HiS8sG00" target="_blank">
        TMZ.com
       </a>
       .
       <p>
        <font size="4">
         Jason and I have decided to call off our engagement, Britney says in the statement to
         <a href="http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20657207,00.html" target="_blank">
          PEOPLE
         </a>
         . Ill always adore him and we will remain great friends.
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        Adds Jason: As this chapter ends for us a new one begins. I love and cherish her and her boys and we will be close forever.
       </p>
       <p>
        <i>
         Jason didnt want to make the announcement until he was taken off the conservatorship. Interestingly, lawyers for the conservatorship are in court right now, along with Britneys dad, Jamie Spears, so its possible Jason will be removed today.
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        Sources say it was a friendly breakup because the two simply grew apart.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.breatheheavy.com/britney-signs-with-caa/" target="_blank">
         Britney also switched to CAA
        </a>
        , an agency Trawick is not involved with.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         UPDATE:
        </b>
        4:17PM PST:  Jason Trawick has just been removed as Britneys co-conservator  TMZ has learned.  Jamie Spears is now her sole conservator.
       </p>
       <p>
        Britney Spears and Jason Trawick have mutually agreed to end their one-year engagement, her rep Jeff Raymond tells People exclusively. As two mature adults, they came to the difficult decision to go their separate ways while continuing to remain friends.
       </p>
