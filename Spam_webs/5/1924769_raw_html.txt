     <h1>
      Destination Honeymoon! Greece
     </h1>
     <span class="location">
      Greece
     </span>
     <time datetime="2012-09-19">
      Posted on: 09/19/12
     </time>
     <div class="slider-wrapper theme-default">
      <div class="ribbon">
      </div>
      <ul id="slideshow">
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Blue_Palace_Crete-greece-honeymoon-620x366.png"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Blue-Palace-honeymoon-private-pool.jpg"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/mystique-greece-honeymoon-620x387.jpg"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/mystique-resort-honeymoon.jpg"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Royal-Villa-of-the-Grand-Resort-Lagonissi_1-620x412.gif"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/daois-cove-greece-honeymoon-620x465.jpg"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/grace-hotel-santorini-honeymoon.jpeg"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        <img src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/santa-marina-mykonos-honeymoon-620x415.jpg"/>
       </li>
      </ul>
     </div>
     <p>
      <em>
       Anytime I see a photograph of Greece my heart flutters a little bit. I have never been there and I am absolutely dying to go. My wonderful cousin,
       <a href="http://www.lifetimediscovery.com/" target="_blank">
        Keren Ayoun (a wedding planner &amp; travel agent based in Israel with Lifetime Discovery)
       </a>
       is back again! Last time she gave us a bit of a virtual tour of some of the
       <a href="http://venuesafari.com/venue/destination-weddings-wedding-venues-in-israel/" title="Destination Weddings: Wedding Venues in Israel">
        gorgeous wedding venues in Israel
       </a>
       , today she tells us about some great honeymoon spots in Greece (these could also make great destination wedding locations in Greece as well). Enjoy!
      </em>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.bluepalace.gr/" target="_blank">
        The Blue Palace Resort  Crete
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Blue-Palace-honeymoon-private-pool.jpg">
       <img alt="greek honeymoon blue palace with private pool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3405" height="412" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Blue-Palace-honeymoon-private-pool.jpg" title="Blue-Palace-honeymoon-private-pool" width="620"/>
      </a>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Blue_Palace_Crete-greece-honeymoon.png">
       <img alt="blue palace crete honeymoon greece" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3404" height="368" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Blue_Palace_Crete-greece-honeymoon.png" title="Blue_Palace_Crete-greece-honeymoon" width="623"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      After your guests have departed after your wedding, take time out and honeymoon at this dreamy resort nestled on the famed coast of Elounda Crete, for a memorable and tranquil time just the 2 of you. You can have your travel agent arrangeevery detail to make this both relaxing and unforgettable. Starting with a romantic gourmet breakfast, to a private boat trip discovery to the relics of Spinalonga Island.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.mystique.gr/" target="_blank">
        Mystique Hotel  Santorini
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/mystique-resort-honeymoon.jpg">
       <img alt="mystique greece resort honeymoon" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3407" height="414" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/mystique-resort-honeymoon.jpg" title="mystique-resort-honeymoon" width="620"/>
      </a>
      With a typical island-style design that preserves the nature in a climate of unabashed luxury, The Mystique, a luxury Collection Hotel is built in the worlds most beautiful scenery. A clear winner for a honeymoon to restore and reinvigorate.
     </p>
     <p>
      You might choose a honeymoon suite that opens onto sea-facing terraces for basking in the sun, to a cobblestone pathway that will lead you to the alluring beauty of the plunge pool for soothing relaxation.
     </p>
     <p>
      Enjoy breathtaking views during a wonderfully romantic candlelit supper at the Charisma Gourmet restaurant, right on the cliff edge complimented by a visit to the Secret Wine Cave where you may discover the most rare and indigenous wine selection,
     </p>
     <p>
      Hanging on the volcanic cliff above the Aegean waters, The Mystique offersbreathtaking views of theCalderaand the deep blue sea. The perfect setting for a the honeymoon you dreamed of.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/mystique-greece-honeymoon.jpeg">
       <img alt="exclusive luxury travel honeymoon greek " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3406" height="388" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/mystique-greece-honeymoon.jpeg" title="mystique-greece-honeymoon" width="621"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.lagonissiresort.gr/" target="_blank">
       <strong>
        The Grand Resort Lagonissi  Athens
       </strong>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Royal-Villa-of-the-Grand-Resort-Lagonissi_1.gif">
       <img alt="greece honeymoon wedding" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3408" height="419" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Royal-Villa-of-the-Grand-Resort-Lagonissi_1.gif" title="Royal-Villa-of-the-Grand-Resort-Lagonissi_1" width="630"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      The Grand Resort Lagonissi, your blissful honeymoon hideaway
     </p>
     <p>
      Discover the magical destination of the Apollo Beach Riviera of Athens at the outstanding and luxurious Grand Resort Lagonissi to celebrate your union together.
     </p>
     <p>
      This resort, nestled in a magnificent private peninsula, boasts 20 exclusive sandy beach coves and lush natural beauty for the newlyweds to choose from, to relax and recline in complete tranquility. To complete the day of R &amp; R, let the Thalaspa Chenot relieve you of any remaining pressures and tensions the the unique Chenot Method.
     </p>
     <p>
      From this location, it is also possible to create wonderfully exclusive and discovery excursions to Athens and other nearby destinations. Or alternatively, why not choose to join this sojourn with a couple of nights at the King George Athens, with marvelous views of the Acropolis, leaving just enough time for a private helicopter tour of the city!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.daioscovecrete.com/" target="_blank">
       <strong>
        Daios Cove  Crete
       </strong>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/daois-cove-greece-honeymoon.jpeg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3409" height="480" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/daois-cove-greece-honeymoon.jpeg" title="daois-cove-greece-honeymoon" width="640"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Nestles on a hillside of Agios Nikalaos Crete you will find the gem that is Daios Cove Luxury Resort &amp; Villas, a new luxury 5 star Beach Resort overlooking its private tranquil bay and sandy beach.
     </p>
     <p>
      Discover the flawless beaches and soak up the year round sunshine. After a day of relaxation, we invite you to an exquisite candlelit dinner at The Ocean Italian restaurant, of sophisticated and contemporary cuisine. The fresh sea breeze and a view overlooking the infinity pool and the cove, the setting makes for a perfectly romantic night between newlyweds.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.santorinigrace.com/" target="_blank">
        The Grace Hotel  Santorini
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/grace-hotel-santorini-honeymoon.jpeg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3410" height="412" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/grace-hotel-santorini-honeymoon.jpeg" title="grace-hotel-santorini-honeymoon" width="620"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      The Grace Hotel Santorini, an escape to unmatched serenity
     </p>
     <p>
      Located in the beautiful setting ofImerovigliin the northwest of the island, this hotel boasts the best vantage point for the famed and spellbindingly romantic Santorini sunsets.
     </p>
     <p>
      With a breathtaking infinity pool offering uninterrupted views, this will truly be the perfect setting for your honeymoon with memories that will last a lifetime.
     </p>
     <p>
      Renowned as a chic and intimate luxury retreat, with individually styled suites and rooms, this hotel will live up to a fairytale honeymoon
      <strong>
       .
      </strong>
      At the Grace Hotel, expecta specially prepared gourmet supper by candlelight and savour the moonlit views
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.santa-marina.gr/" target="_blank">
        Hotel Santa Marina  Mykonos
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/santa-marina-mykonos-honeymoon.jpg">
       <img alt="honeymoon in greece" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3411" height="436" src="http://venuesafari.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/santa-marina-mykonos-honeymoon.jpg" title="santa-marina-mykonos-honeymoon" width="650"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      The Santa Marina Hotel has to be
      <strong>
       the
      </strong>
      choice for jet-setters, with exotic beaches and the most entertaining nightlife, a perfect choice for the most sophisticated and glamorous honeymoon.
     </p>
     <p>
      This tranquil oasis where understated elegance meets sheer indulgence, Mykonos Hotel Santa Marina is a paradise within a paradise. Nestled on a secluded peninsula over the magical blue of the Aegean, relax together on the private deck of your spacious suite before stealing away to the tranquility of your room to experiencing ultimate relaxation.
     </p>
