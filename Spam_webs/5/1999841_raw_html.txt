         <h1 class="story-title" itemprop="name">
          DIY bamboo wall decor ideas  2 craft projects with bamboo sticks
         </h1>
         <div id="post-info">
          <time class="post-date" datetime="2013-08-12" itemprop="datePublished" pubdate="">
           August 12, 2013
          </time>
         </div>
         <div id="content-area">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           We bring to your attention an interesting
           <strong>
            bamboo wall decor ideas
           </strong>
           for your home. This amazing decoration is made of
           <strong>
            bamboo sticks
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           Bamboo wall decor ideas with bamboo sticks  How to assemble it
          </h2>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="bamboo wall decor ideas burned sticks put together" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2511" height="458" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/wall-decor-sticks-idea-bamboo-plant.jpg" title="sticks wall decor idea bamboo plant" width="535"/>
          </p>
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8708956619736340" data-ad-slot="4868048319" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Materials used in the tutorial:
           <br/>
            2 packs of 150g coconut coir
           <br/>
            1 liter of natural coconut adhesive
           <br/>
            aluminum form 2020 cm
           <br/>
            scissors
           <br/>
            1 m wire
           <br/>
            needle-nose pliers
           <br/>
            cutting pliers
           <br/>
            pair of rubber gloves
           <br/>
            8 bamboo sticks with a length of 50 cm and 3 cm in diameter
           <br/>
            4 pieces 6cm vulcanized or copper wire (1.5 mm 25 cm)
           <br/>
            hammer
           <br/>
            burner
           <br/>
            drill
           <br/>
            white glue
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Cut the bamboo in the required dimensions. Burn the sticks in your desired tone of brown to create an ineteresting effect.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="creative frame decoration bamboo chared sticks" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2506" height="349" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/creative-frame-decoration-bamboo-chared-sticks.jpg" title="creative decoration bamboo frame chared sticks" width="527"/>
          </p>
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8708956619736340" data-ad-slot="4868048319" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Cross two sticks of bamboo, leaving 10 cm from each end. Make a hole where both sticks overlap. Make another hole  5 cm from the first. Then use cutting pliers to cut pieces of 3 cm in the form of bolts.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="bamboo frame project drilling holes" class="aligncenter wp-image-2502" height="402" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/bamboo-frame-project-drilling-holes.jpg" title="bamboo sticks drilling holes assembling frame" width="626"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Put glue into the holes and insert the bolts to hold the sticks together.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="bamboo natural decor idea bolts inserts" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2503" height="407" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/bamboo-natural-decor-idea-bolts-inserts.jpg" title="bamboo wall decor idea bolts inserts" width="601"/>
           <br/>
           Use rubber bands to tie the rods as shown.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <img alt="wall decor bamboo cane diy idea" class="aligncenter wp-image-2510" height="408" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/wall-decor-bamboo-cane-diy-idea.jpg" title="bamboo sticks attatched together diy wall decor idea" width="642"/>
           <br/>
           Mix coconut fibers and natural glue. Before you start, put the rubber gloves to protect your hands. Leave the fibers for 2 hours to absorb the entire quantity of adhesive.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="art-decoration-bamboo-frame-coconut-fibers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2501" height="441" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/art-decoration-bamboo-frame-coconut-fibers.jpg" title="bamboo art decor frame coconut fibers adhensive" width="562"/>
           <br/>
           First you need to make coconut coir in a square shape. Use the appropriate container /aluminum form/ 2020 cm. Then make a pot, which we will cut into two parts. Leave the made forms to dry for 48 hours.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="bamboo plant frame arapia form" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2504" height="410" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/bamboo-plant-frame-arapia-form.jpg" title="natural plant frame arapia form" width="617"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="natural bamboo frame decor project" class="aligncenter wp-image-2508" height="438" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/natural-bamboo-frame-decor-project.jpg" title="natural frame decor project bamboo sticks coconut coir" width="631"/>
           <br/>
           Cut two 20 cm pieces of wire and use them to put together the two parts. Make a hook on the back for wall mounting.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="natural bamboo sticks coconut coir fix parts" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2509" height="365" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/natural-bamboo-sticks-coconut-arapia-fix-parts.jpg" title="sticks coconut arapia fix parts bamboo " width="582"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="diy bamboo wall decor idea" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2507" height="580" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/diy-bamboo-wall-decor-idea.jpg" title="diy bamboo sticks wall decor idea" width="552"/>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: center;">
           Bamboo wall decor ideas with a sunflower
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span id="result_box" lang="en">
            <span class="hps">
             This is another version of the project. You can make the sunflowers
            </span>
            <span class="hps">
             out of
            </span>
            <span class="hps">
             polymer
            </span>
            <span class="hps">
             clay or just to buy one.
             <br/>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8708956619736340" data-ad-slot="4868048319" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="bamboo wall decor ideas sunflower coconut coir" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2527" height="643" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/bamboo-sunflower-wall-decor-idea.jpg" title="wall decor idea bamboo sticks sunflower" width="578"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="diy bamboo sticks make sunflower" class="aligncenter wp-image-2528" height="450" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/diy-bamboo-sticks-make-sunflower.jpg" title="sunflower make wall bamboo decor" width="698"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="natural bamboo plant layout straw" class="aligncenter wp-image-2530" height="419" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/natural-bamboo-plant-layout-straw.jpg" title="bamboo natural plant layout straw" width="699"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="sunflower leaves bamboo decor project" class="aligncenter wp-image-2532" height="398" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/sunflower-leaves-bamboo-decor-project.jpg" title="bamboo decor project sunflower leaves" width="694"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="sunflower wall decor bamboo idea" class="aligncenter wp-image-2533" height="443" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/sunflower-wall-decor-bamboo-idea.jpg" title="sunflower bamboo sticks idea" width="674"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="diy natural bamboo plant ribbon cutting" class="aligncenter wp-image-2529" height="440" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/diy-natural-bamboo-plant-ribbon-cutting.jpg" title="natural bamboo plant diy ribbon cutting" width="665"/>

           <img alt="sunflower bamboo frame decoration" class="aligncenter wp-image-2531" height="468" src="http://www.diy-enthusiasts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/sunflower-bamboo-frame-decoration.jpg" title="bamboo wall decor ideas  frame decoration with sunflower" width="690"/>
