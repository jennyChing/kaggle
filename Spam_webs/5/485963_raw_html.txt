         <p>
          An engagement ring signals the start of an exciting new life between you and your spouse.Because of the importance placed on it by the bride-to-be,
          <em>
           getting just the right ring
          </em>
          can be a gut wrenching experience for the groom.With so many styles and cuts to pick from, men can
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           easily become overwhelmed
          </span>
          by the sheer amount of choice available.
         </p>
         <p>
          The engagement ring industry is constantly growing and with it new takes on the classic styles of yesteryear make their way into the market each season.Round engagement rings make up one style, however, which has endured and remained the most popular for women around the world to show off.Also sometimes know as a brilliant cut, this simple but elegant cut of diamond is always a hit and makes an easy choice for the man who just cant decide.
         </p>
         <p>
          They get the name brilliant due to their superior ability to reflect light. This makes them incredibly sparkly and bright.Round cut engagement rings can light up a room when a someone wearing one enters, which is surely a quality most women would love to posses.
          <br/>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/wXy2wBB3f-4" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Other Options
         </h2>
         <p>
          What are the competing styles available compared to round diamond engagement rings?
         </p>
         <p>
          A recent type of cut thats become popular due to singer Jessica Simpson is the pear diamond. Its actually quite similar to the round cut but has a more teardrop shape.Next in popularity to the round cut, however, is the princess cut diamond. This is a square shape cut but it also comes to a point, which is similar to the round design.Round cut halo engagement rings are another design to consider. They take a traditional round cut and place a halo of diamonds around it.
         </p>
         <p>
          And yet another way to enhance a round cut is with a solitaire diamond which helps accent its beauty within a prong setting.
         </p>
         <p>
          In the end though,
          <em>
           you cant go wrong
          </em>
          with a round cut diamond ring. There is a reason for why it has withstood the test of time and remained at
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           the top of the list
          </span>
          for diamond buyers everywhere.
         </p>

         <h2>
          Celebrities Who Wear Them
         </h2>
         <p>
          With the large variety of cuts of diamond rings available, many big celebrities still stick to the classic elegant style of a round cut diamond.Pop singer Britany spears spots a three-carat diamond round cut rings for instance.Other notable stars who wear a round cut diamond are Natalie Portman and Gisele Bundchen who both proudly display this classic and beautiful design.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Choosing the Right Kind
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ohmyrings.com/goto/www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;bbn=3367581&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;high-price=&amp;keywords=Round%20Engagement%20Rings&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;low-price=50&amp;qid=1387702759&amp;rh=n%3A3367581%2Ck%3ARound%20Engagement%20Rings&amp;rnid=386563011&amp;tag=ohm0f-20&amp;x=-180&amp;y=-702">
           <img alt="vintage round engagement ring" class="aligncenter" height="267" src="http://ohmyrings.com//HLIC/6bca07ff7659039678254cbb47e57ab7.jpg" width="307"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          Once youve decided on going with a round cut diamond, there are still questions to be answered when looking at the exact ring youll
          <a href="http://ohmyrings.com/goto/www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;bbn=3367581&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;high-price=&amp;keywords=Round%20Engagement%20Rings&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;low-price=50&amp;qid=1387702759&amp;rh=n%3A3367581%2Ck%3ARound%20Engagement%20Rings&amp;rnid=386563011&amp;tag=ohm0f-20&amp;x=-180&amp;y=-702">
           buy
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Firstly, youll need to be
          <em>
           honest about your budget
          </em>
          . The size of carat for your ring will largely be limited by how much you are prepared to spend.A good size to shoot for without breaking the bank is anywhere between 1.0  1.5 carats. This is still large enough to look impressive, but wont cost an arm and a leg.
         </p>
         <p>
          The color of the ring is important too, some women prefer gold while others will only consider silver or platinum.Make sure you talk to your significant other first about these types of details before embarking on your ring-buying journey.
         </p>
         <p>
          Good luck with your purchase and have fun buying this
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           special symbol
          </span>
          of your love.
         </p>
