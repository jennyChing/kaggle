                     <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/2015/07/mostly-homemade-mondays-week-141-urban.html">
                      Mostly Homemade Mondays - Week 141 + URBAN HOMESTEAD MEAL PLAN!
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>

                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4228427909256737881" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        Are you ready for this week's meal plan and blog hop?
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      Remember, in the meal plansome items are bolded, which indicates a homegrown or local item from our urban homestead. We have leftovers for lunch, and we rarely have desserts. Many times on weekends our lunches include eggs from the ladies.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <div>
                       <ul>
                        <li>
                         Monday - Spicy chicken and rice wraps with homemade refried beans
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Tuesday - Cobb salads
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Wednesday - Homemade frozen pizza (perhaps on the grill, if the weather is really hot)
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Thursday -
                         <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/2014/08/cracker-coated-spicy-pepper-chicken.html">
                          Homemade chicken strips
                         </a>
                         and garden green beans
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Friday - Chicken fajitas with homemade mexican rice and black beans
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Saturday - Out for dinner
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Sunday -
                         <a href="http://theelliotthomestead.com/2011/09/grilled-stuffed-portabellas-and-the-river/">
                          Grilled portabella mushrooms
                         </a>
                         stuffed withed quinoa
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <i>
                         Anyway, on to the party
                        </i>
                        :
                       </span>
                       <br/>
                       <b>
                        <br/>
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sDXiC70gUbI/VRAC4cmqC_I/AAAAAAAAI8Q/m60TzWYq93A/s1600/TSC%2BMostly%2BHomemade%2BMondays.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       <b>
                        Mostly Homemade Mondays
                       </b>
                       is a series for people that are on a journey to better living, via healthier eating and a more natural lifestyle. We realize that there are different paths that we all take to get to that place, and this bloghop celebrates that.
                       <i>
                        Whether youre a seasoned raw foodist who has banned all things unnatural, or a rookie who is starting out by cutting out junk food, wed love to hear from you! Link up with your favorite recipes, projects, crafts, or rants and raves.
                       </i>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       The Hosts:
                       <br/>
                       <div>
                        <br/>
                        <div style="text-align: center;">
                         Sarah of
                         <a href="http://beingfrugalbychoice.blogspot.com/">
                          Frugal by Choice, Cheap by Necessity
                         </a>
                        </div>
                        <div style="text-align: center;">
                         Aubrey of
                         <a href="http://homegrownandhealthy.com/">
                          Homegrown &amp; Healthy
                         </a>
                        </div>
                        <div style="text-align: center;">
                         Kelli from
                         <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/" target="_blank">
                          The Sustainable Couple
                         </a>
                        </div>
                        <br/>
                        <b>
                         Homemade Mondays
                        </b>
                        will open every Monday. You have until Thursday to make your submissions. We will share our favorite posts on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and shout it from the rooftops.
                        <br/>
                        <ul>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Link up.
                          </b>
                          Use the submission form below to add your blog posts to our gallery below. You can add as many links as youd like, as long as theyre on topic.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Whats eligible?
                          </b>
                          <i>
                           Recipes, crafts, DIY projects, gardening, fitness, green ideas or just plain old advice.
                          </i>
                          Were pretty open.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Tell us about yourselves.
                          </b>
                          Leave a comment telling us a little about your site and what youve shared. Of course this isnt mandatory, but its a good way to stand out.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Link back.
                          </b>
                          Share the love by adding a link back to this party with your readers so they can see all of these great ideas as well.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Follow our Pinterest board.
                          </b>
                          Remember to check out our
                          <b>
                           <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/grownandhealthy/mostly-homemade-mondays/" target="_blank">
                            Mostly Homemade Mondays Pinterest board
                           </a>
                          </b>
                          where we're pinning some of our favorite posts from the week.
                         </li>
                        </ul>

                     <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/2015/07/summer-strawberries.html">
                      Summer Strawberries.
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7843323333632837245" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div>
                      I think it's safe to say that we all love summer, right? Really, what's not to love? The sunshine, the long days, the warm breeze, the bountiful gardens, the birds and the bees (literally). All of those things are what I love about summer.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      I love that I am up to my eyeballs in kale, rainbow chard, and greens. I love walking out to my backyard each morning to collect eggs from the ladies, give them scraps from the scratch-made dinner the night before, and admire the green tomatoes that grow bigger with each passing day.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Not everything we enjoy can be grown on our little homestead in the city. Strawberries are a summer treat that we live for each summer.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                     </div>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/2015/07/mostly-homemade-mondays-week-140-urban.html">
                      Mostly Homemade Mondays - Week 140 + URBAN HOMESTEAD MEAL PLAN!
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3107048352400675487" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        Are you ready for this week's meal plan and blog hop?
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      Remember, in the meal plansome items are bolded, which indicates a homegrown or local item from our urban homestead. We have leftovers for lunch, and we rarely have desserts. Many times on weekends our lunches include eggs from the ladies.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <div>
                       <ul>
                        <li>
                         Monday - Roasted chicken with the last of our frozen sweet corn from last year!
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Tuesday - Chicken tacos with homemade refried beans
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Wednesday - Dinner with friends
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Thursday - Homemade frozen pizza (
                         <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/2013/10/pizza-dough-again.html">
                          dough recipe here
                         </a>
                         )
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Friday -Salad with veggies, homemade cornbread, and baked beans
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Saturday - Grilled ham steak (this is our last ham steak in the sep freeze!) and sweet potato fries
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Sunday -
                         <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/2014/07/cinnamon-rolls-from-scratch.html">
                          Cinnamon rolls
                         </a>
                         and fresh fruit (breakfast for dinner, baby!)
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <i>
                         Anyway, on to the party
                        </i>
                        :
                       </span>
                       <br/>
                       <b>
                        <br/>
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sDXiC70gUbI/VRAC4cmqC_I/AAAAAAAAI8Q/m60TzWYq93A/s1600/TSC%2BMostly%2BHomemade%2BMondays.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="160" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sDXiC70gUbI/VRAC4cmqC_I/AAAAAAAAI8Q/m60TzWYq93A/s1600/TSC%2BMostly%2BHomemade%2BMondays.jpg" width="640"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       <b>
                        Mostly Homemade Mondays
                       </b>
                       is a series for people that are on a journey to better living, via healthier eating and a more natural lifestyle. We realize that there are different paths that we all take to get to that place, and this bloghop celebrates that.
                       <i>
                        Whether youre a seasoned raw foodist who has banned all things unnatural, or a rookie who is starting out by cutting out junk food, wed love to hear from you! Link up with your favorite recipes, projects, crafts, or rants and raves.
                       </i>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       The Hosts:
                       <br/>
                       <div>
                        <br/>
                        <div style="text-align: center;">
                         Sarah of
                         <a href="http://beingfrugalbychoice.blogspot.com/">
                          Frugal by Choice, Cheap by Necessity
                         </a>
                        </div>
                        <div style="text-align: center;">
                         Aubrey of
                         <a href="http://homegrownandhealthy.com/">
                          Homegrown &amp; Healthy
                         </a>
                        </div>
                        <div style="text-align: center;">
                         Kelli from
                         <a href="http://www.thesustainablecouple.com/" target="_blank">
                          The Sustainable Couple
                         </a>
                        </div>
                        <br/>
                        <b>
                         Homemade Mondays
                        </b>
                        will open every Monday. You have until Thursday to make your submissions. We will share our favorite posts on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and shout it from the rooftops.
                        <br/>
                        <ul>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Link up.
                          </b>
                          Use the submission form below to add your blog posts to our gallery below. You can add as many links as youd like, as long as theyre on topic.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Whats eligible?
                          </b>
                          <i>
                           Recipes, crafts, DIY projects, gardening, fitness, green ideas or just plain old advice.
                          </i>
                          Were pretty open.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Tell us about yourselves.
                          </b>
                          Leave a comment telling us a little about your site and what youve shared. Of course this isnt mandatory, but its a good way to stand out.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Link back.
                          </b>
                          Share the love by adding a link back to this party with your readers so they can see all of these great ideas as well.
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <b>
                           Follow our Pinterest board.
                          </b>
                          Remember to check out our
                          <b>
                           <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/grownandhealthy/mostly-homemade-mondays/" target="_blank">
                            Mostly Homemade Mondays Pinterest board
                           </a>
                          </b>
                          where we're pinning some of our favorite posts from the week.
                         </li>
                        </ul>
                       </div>
