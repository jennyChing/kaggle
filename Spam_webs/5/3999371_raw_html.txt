        <section class="zn zn-body-text zn-body zn--idx-0 zn-has-multiple-containers zn-has-33-containers" data-containers="33" data-eq-pts="xsmall: 0, medium: 460, large: 780, full16x9: 1100" data-vr-zone="zone-1-0" data-zn-id="body-text" id="body-text">

          <div class="el__leafmedia el__leafmedia--storyhighlights el__leafmedia--">
           <div class="el__storyhighlights_wrapper">
            <div class="el__storyhighlights">
             <h3 class="el__headline">
              Story highlights
             </h3>
             <ul class="el__storyhighlights__list">
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               Powerball drawing set for Saturday night; jackpot estimated at $600 million
              </li>
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               Chances of winning are one in 175.2 million, according to Powerball
              </li>
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               Part of the allure is that everyone else is buying tickets, experts say
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
            <div class="ad ad--epic" data-ad-text="show">
             <div class="ad-ad_ns_btf_01" id="ad_ns_btf_01">
             </div>

           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Six numbers have the power to change your life.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Maybe your kid is sick and there are hospital bills to pay. Maybe you've lost your job and you're worried about making rent. Maybe you still have a job, but it sucks, and you'd really like to spend the next 50 years lying on a beach with a mai tai in hand.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Whatever your predicament, the current estimated
           <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2013/05/16/us/powerball-jackpot/index.html">
            Powerball jackpot of $600 million
           </a>
           could fix it. Which makes us wonder -- when it comes to playing the lottery, are we all just damsels in distress?
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           "Because we're in a recession, people love to have a rescue fantasy," human behavior expert Dr. Wendy Walsh
           <a href="http://newsroom.blogs.cnn.com/2012/04/01/the-psychology-of-the-lottery-player/">
            told CNN
           </a>
           last year when the Mega Millions jackpot hit $656 million. "We have the Cinderella complex -- there's a fairy godmother who's going to come in and save us."
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           We've all heard the statistics. Your chances of winning the Powerball jackpot are about
           <a href="http://www.powerball.com/powerball/pb_prizes.asp " target="_blank">
            one in 175.2 million
           </a>
           . You're more likely to die from a bee sting (one in 6.1 million), be struck by lightning (one in 3 million) or have conjoined twins (one in 200,000).
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           But people keep playing -- most likely because the thought of winning $550 million is much more fun than the thought of being attacked by a shark (one in 11.5 million).
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           "It doesn't faze them because they're in love with hope," Walsh said.
          </p>
