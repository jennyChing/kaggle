         <div class="text_article_extra">
          The fastest and most powerful Rolls-Royce to date, the new Wraith makes its debut today at the Geneva Motor Show.
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="article_body_text ">
           Its been hinted at. Its been endlessly teased. And now its here. Its the new
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/Rolls-Royce/2014_Rolls-Royce_Wraith/">
            Rolls-Royce Wraith
           </a>
           , and its finally been released at the Geneva show. The Ghost-based fastback coupe is, in the words of Rolls chief Torsten Muller-Otvos, "the ultimate gentlemens gran turismo" and "the most powerful
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/Rolls-Royce/">
            Rolls-Royce
           </a>
           in history." Substantiating that claim is a retuned version of the Ghosts 6.6-liter twin-turbo V12, producing 624 horsepower (instead of the Ghost's 563) and 590 lb-ft of torque (vs 575) for a 4.4-second 0-60 time.
           <br/>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="lazyimage share-action">
           <span class="fb-btn" data-link="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2013/3/5/Rolls-Royce-Reveals-Wraith-in-Geneva-7713250/">
            Share
           </span>
           <img alt="Rolls-Royce Reveals Wraith in Geneva" src="http://db.carbuzz.com/images2/250000/2000/100/252175.jpg"/>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="article_body_text2">
           <div align="right" class="text_article_also">
            <!--b&gt;more at CarBuzz:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;br-->
            <a href="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2015/7/14/Was-Jaguar-s-Supercar-Fighter-Just-Caught-Out-On-The-Nurburgring-7727982/">
             Was Jaguar's Supercar Fighter Just Caught ...
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2015/7/13/A-Lamborghini-And-Bentley-Crashed-In-Two-Separate-Incidents-In-The-Same-Town-On-The-Same-Night-7728052/">
             A Lamborghini And Bentley Crashed In Two S...
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2015/7/14/Does-A-Wider-Look-Suit-The-X6-Or-Has-Lumma-Design-Wrecked-Another-BMW-7728035/">
             Does A Wider Look Suit The X6 Or Has Lumma...
            </a>
           </div>
           Thatd be quick for a lightweight sports car, but the Wraith is a luxury coupe. The bold lines and fastback profile contain an assuredly unsurpassed level of opulence and refinement. Open the rear-hinged "coach" doors to find sumptuous leather, thick carpeting, the fiber-optic headliner and Canadel Panelling  the tactile wood trim that sweeps the cabin. Driver and occupants benefit from a new voice-command system and touchpad rotary controller for the infotainment system. The Wraith also introduces a new technology from
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/BMW/">
            BMW
           </a>
           that uses satellite mapping data to select from the 8-speed automatic transmission the right gear for the road ahead.
           <br/>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="article_body_text ">
           The system will find its way into other BMW Group models, but in the Wraith it's but another element that seeks to make the driving experience "effortless". At 245,000 - equivalent to $320k and significantly more than the
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/Bentley/2014_Bentley_Continental-GT/">
            Bentley Continental GT
           </a>
           Speed - it ought to be.
