        When the bright spots were first spotted, the principal investigator for the Dawn mission, Chris Russell, said it was too early to tell what had caused them.
       </p>
       <p>
        "The bright spots continue to fascinate the science team, but we will have to wait until we get closer and are able to resolve them before we can determine their source."
       </p>
       <p>
        The bright spots have sent UFO spotters and alien believers into a flutter, with online forums suggesting the cause could be anything from a heat signature from a large living organism to an artificial beacon.
