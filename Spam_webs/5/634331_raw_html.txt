          Description
         </h5>
         <div class="mt_content1">
          An exciting role to be a part of a leading company within Financial Services domain in Singapore. Our client islooking to bring on an experienced Analyst / Senior Analyst to work onImaging and ECM (Enterprise Content Management) projects. Multiple roles are available.
          <br/>
          We offer a challenging and rewarding work environment with potential for advancement and an excellent compensation and benefits package.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <h5 class="mt_title3">
          Skill-set Requirement
         </h5>
         <div class="mt_content1">
          <ul>
           <li>
            Minimum 3 years working experience within Financial Services industry with proven track record in successful delivery of projects (full project life cycle);
           </li>
           <li>
            Strong understanding of ECM touch points inbusiness processes;
           </li>
           <li>
            Technical experience in maintaining and support Imaging and ECM solution like Kofax Capture, Content Manager on iSeries, IBM Filenet;
           </li>
           <li>
            Expertise in integratingImaging and Workflow Solution, Printing Solution, ECM;
           </li>
           <li>
            Proficient with MS Excel, MS Word, MS PowerPoint, MS Visio, MS.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <br/>
          <b>
           Desirable Skills
          </b>
          <br/>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Someknowledge of Graphical User Interface (GUI), web graphical designs is a plus;
           </li>
           <li>
            Business process modelling and design preferred.
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>

          Job Responsibilities
         </h5>
         <div class="mt_content1">
          <ul>
           <li>
            Part of the project team for Imaging/Scanning, Printing and ECM projects;
           </li>
           <li>
            Analyze and translate business requirements into functional specifications for change request;
           </li>
           <li>
            Coding and ensuring that theassigned tasks are developed and onschedule;
           </li>
           <li>
            Provide user and IT operations support during and post implementation;
           </li>
           <li>
            Maintain assigned/allocated applications by trouble-shooting problems and providing effective solutions;
           </li>
           <li>
            Testing &amp; Support arrangements for work request;
           </li>

            Incidentand Problem Management &amp; Management Information Reporting.
