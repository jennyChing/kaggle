            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Aamir Khans PK Box Office Collections On Opening Day
             </strong>
             : On this Christmas Aamir Khan brings his movie
             <strong>
              PK
             </strong>
             as Christmas Gift for all. The PK Movie Box Office Collection
             <strong>
              <em>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                crossed Rs 30 crore on First Day
               </span>
              </em>
             </strong>
             .PK film industry accumulations could be more than
             <strong>
              Rs 40 crore
             </strong>
             amid
             <em>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Saturday and Sunday
              </span>
             </em>
             , however Aamirs highly anticipated film neglected to beat day 1 record set by Shah Rukh Khan-starrer
             <strong>
              Happy New Years
             </strong>
             (Rs 44.97 crore). HNY was released amid Diwali.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              PK
             </strong>
             has been released in
             <em>
              more than 5000 screen
             </em>
             all over and this is enough to make records at worldwide level. So guys, cross your fingers because Aamir is coming again with his power packed performance and also to rule at box office.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             There are lots of famous star working on this movie. So, its going to be a big hit. Movie has been released all over India and abroad on 19th December  2014. Within a week there is Christmas vacation so, chances are high for box office collection in
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Film has taken super solid begin in multiplexes in Bangalore, Pune, Mumbai and NCR. Indeed Cinepolis Patna had 98% inhabitance in morning shows and same was the situation in premium multiplexes in littler towns as well. Single screens have opened to near to half which is reasonable for such a film.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             The Opening Day Box Office Collection of the PK Movie credit must go to the director of the film Rajkumar Hirani whos vision was behind the PK and all the design and the looks of all the actors from the PK.Aamir Khan playing PK lead role with co stars, Anushka Sharma as opposite to PK and playing female lead role and Sanjay Dutt and Sudhant Singh Rajput also doing cameo role in PK movie.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             It is first time in India that the Box Office Collection of a Bollywood Movie has declared by Rentark. The Rentark is an organization that works on analyzing of Box Office Collection of Hollywood Movies. But the Director of PK, Rajkumar Hirani and Producer Vidhu Vinod Chopra has hired Rentark to check PK Movie Box Office Reviews and Collection.
            </p>
