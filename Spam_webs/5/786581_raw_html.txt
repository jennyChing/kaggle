        <p>
         On Sunday, January 9th, 2011 over 5,000 people took off their pants on subways in 48 cities in 22 countries around the world.  In New York, our 10th Annual No Pants Subway Ride had over 3,500 participants, spread out over six meeting points and ten subway lines.  Enjoy the video first and then go behind the scenes with our mission report and photos.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-2453">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_00.jpg.pagespeed.ic.YbIS-M3i0M.jpg"/>
        </center>
        <br/>
        <em>
         No Pants 2011 Subway Ride Generals
         <br/>
         (L-R) Back: Agents Todd, Shafer, Shafeek, Lindquist, Fiedler, Linder
         <br/>
         Front: Agents Lathan, Reeves, Good, Scordelis
        </em>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Digital Video:
         </strong>
         Agents Adams, Belaval, Campbell, Chalifour, Ellis, Fritz, Haskel, Heilman, Knight, Nawrot, Ng, Richardson, Thielmann
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Digital Photography:
         </strong>
         Agents Bledsoe, Chai, Chigirev, Fountain, Rosmarin, Scott, Sermoneta, Sokoler.  See their 
         <a href="http://improveverywhere.com/2011/01/09/no-pants-2011-new-york-reports/">
          flickr sets here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre unfamiliar with No Pants, you might want to first read our history of
         <a href="http://improveverywhere.com/missions/the-no-pants-subway-ride/">
          The No Pants Subway Ride
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_01.jpg.pagespeed.ic.h3V0XFB2I0.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         The crowd at Foley Square
        </center>
        <p>
         Like last year we had six meeting points around NYC two in Manhattan, two in Brooklyn, and two in Queens.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_02.jpg.pagespeed.ic.ZUj8BWXFhC.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Agent Purnell guides the crowd at the Flushing, Queens location
        </center>
        <p>
         I ran the downtown Manhattan meeting point along with three other megaphone wielding No Pants Generals.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_03.jpg.pagespeed.ic.pIneOxF31k.jpg"/>
        </center>
        <p>
         To start things off I asked the crowd who was the oldest and who was the youngest.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_04.jpg.pagespeed.ic.hgvfQph3fb.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         At 76, this guy was the oldest participant at our location
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_05.jpg.pagespeed.ic.1odbVPjP-J.jpg"/>
        </center>
        <p>
         For a moment, it seemed like a 3-year-old kid was going to be the youngest, and then this stroller was hilariously lifted up in the middle of the crowd.  Moments later shouts from the back of the crowd indicated their was an even younger baby present, 7 months.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_06.jpg.pagespeed.ic.kaKcpSvIo4.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Agent Gorden with his child on the train, both pantless
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_08.jpg.pagespeed.ic.ZgwUIKSORs.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Two of our older participants
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_07.jpg.pagespeed.ic.NpjASfZg_o.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Even a dog participated this year!
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_09.jpg.pagespeed.ic.nOBLTczFDE.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         New Yorkers reacting
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_10.jpg.pagespeed.ic.qNzsJMAzz1.jpg"/>
        </center>
        <p>
         The mission started the same way on each of the 100 cars we took over.  One person stood up, removed their pants, and exited the train to wait on the platform for the next one.  This left lots of nice moments with people by themselves in their underwear.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img src="http://improveverywhere-com.zippykid.netdna-cdn.com/images/xnopants11_11.jpg.pagespeed.ic.ILcEGBuinR.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Nice flask!
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
