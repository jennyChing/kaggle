       <p>
        Square Enix has updated its
        <a href="http://www.jp.square-enix.com/densetsu/" target="_blank">
         Densetsu countdown site
        </a>
        launched
        <a href="http://gematsu.com/2014/10/square-enix-launches-densetsu-teaser-site" target="_blank">
         last week
        </a>
        with a new background image.
        <span id="more-143138">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        The site, which is just under a day from revealing whatever it is its set to reveal, now features what appears to be headlights and the grille bar of a car or truck.
       </p>
       <p>
        When the site launched last week, Famitsu guessed it might be a
        <em>
         Racing Lagoon
        </em>
        revival, given the wording of the text on the site being similar to that of a line from the little-known and poorly-received PSone game. With the sites recent update, its looking more and more likely.
       </p>
       <p>
        The countdown ends in 22 hours.
