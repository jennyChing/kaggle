          If you followed
          <a href="http://vector.tutsplus.com/author/ben-mounsey/">
           Ben Mounsey's "Core Art Skills" Series

In the following showcase you will find a collection of 20 Designer Sketchbooks that are made for creativity (much like the designers of the books themselves.)

         <h2>
          Knitted Moleskine Cover
         </h2>
         <p>
          Moleskine Covers may not be a journal design, but it's fairly easy to find Moleskines these days. I have a few scattered about my office that could use a makeover with a cozy cover like these. The best part is that when you're done with the Moleskine you can put it into storage and cover a new one.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/listing/34254910/avalanched-handknit-Moleskine-cover">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/devaburger.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/devaburger.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/devaburger">
            Devon e.
           </a>
           - Knitted Moleskine Cover
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Notefolio
         </h2>
         <p>
          As the name suggests, a Note Folio is a folio for notes. If you're a chaotic creative that can't contain ideas to one notebook a folio is fantastic to store all your ideas before they blow away or get thrown out. The pocket section is large enough to fit napkin sketches and the book section is there if you happen to be organized enough to have your notebook with you (or need to document the scraps).
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <a href="http://poketo.com/shop/stationery?product_id=828">
           <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/poketo.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/poketo.jpg"/>
          </a>
          <p>
           <a href="http://poketo.com/">
            Poketo
           </a>
           - Notefolio
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Keyboard Notebook
         </h2>
         <p>
          If you're like me and spend a huge proportion of the day at a computer, a Keyboard Notebook is exactly what you need. You can scribble ideas and thumbnail sketches without being too far away from the comfort of your graphics software.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/nothingelegant">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/nothingelegant.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/nothingelegant.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/nothingelegant">
            Nothing Elegant
           </a>
           -
           <br/>
           Keyboard Notebook
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Cassette Tape Notebook
         </h2>
         <p>
          The cassette notebook has all the nostalgia and none of the hassle. A nice feature of these sketchbooks is a hard plastic cover that should withstand a few tussles in the locker-room or an impromptu break-dancing session.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.wednesdaygarden.com/playcassettenote.html">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/wednesday_garden.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/wednesday_garden.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.wednesdaygarden.com/playcassettenote.html">
            Wednesday Garden
           </a>
           - Cassette Tape Notebook
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Skull Journal
         </h2>
         <p>
          Journal and Sketchbook keeping is not all about design editions and subtle elegance, it's about your artwork and should be as individual as you are. So, what do you use if you're more likely to enjoy the darker side of life than skipping off though the daisies for a daydream? A Journal with a big skull and crossbones on it could be the answer.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/listing/35098575/skull-journal-crossbones-black-and">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/Derrick_Velasquez.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/Derrick_Velasquez.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/disconsolator?ref=top_trail">
            Derrick Velasquez
           </a>
           - Skull Journal
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Skeleton Notebook
         </h2>
         <p>
          Show your love for Vector art, nature and skeletons all at the same time! This notebook  is an explosion of creative talent, designed by Vector artist
          <a href="http://www.behance.net/Super-Nature">
           Nino Lee
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/listing/45835739/imaginote-handbound-hardcover-notebook">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/ROUTE121.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/ROUTE121.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/ROUTE121?ref=top_trail">
            ROUTE121
           </a>
           - IMAGINote Notebook - Skeleton
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          iPad Notebook
         </h2>
         <p>
          The cutting edge of sketchbook technology, the iPad Notebook can run infinitely without ever having to be charged and doesn't require any software. This is a very handy Notebook for Interface Designers.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.pixelpads.com/PixelPads___Buy_Awesome_Pak.html">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/pixelpad.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/pixelpad.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.pixelpads.com/PixelPads_I_Home.html">
            PixelPads
           </a>
           - iPad Notebook
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Sliced Bread Notebook
         </h2>
         <p>
          This is not just a collection of books that look like a loaf of bread, it's 12 months worth of sketching. Numbered 1 - 12, these notebooks are a clever reminder to stay creative. I haven't been able to find these for purchase, but be sure to check out
          <a href="http://www.burakkaynak.com/">
           Burak Kaynak's folio
          </a>
          for more interesting designs.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.burakkaynak.com/works/sliced-breadnotebook/">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/Burak_Kaynak.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/Burak_Kaynak.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.burakkaynak.com/">
            Burak Kaynak
           </a>
           - Sliced Bread Notebook
          </p>
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Pantone A5 Notebooks
         </h2>
         <p>
          Show your love of color with a notebook made to look like a Pantone swatch. If you're finding it hard to chose your favorite, think about how hard it must have been to chose six colors from the entire Pantone collection.
         </p>
         <div class="tutorial_image">
          <p>
           <a href="http://shop.until.com.au/index.php?main_page=index&amp;cPath=72_83_423">
            <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/pantone.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/pantone.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.pantone.com/pages/pantone/index.aspx">
            Pantone
           </a>
           - Pantone A5 Notebooks
          </p>

         <h2 style="margin-top:20px; margin-bottom:20px;">
          Further Reading
         </h2>
         <ul class="webroundup">
          <p>
           Whether it's big or small, designer or quirky, once you have chosen your perfect sketchbook it's time to put it to good use. Below you will find some further resources that will help sharpen your skill set and refresh your creative energy.
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <div>
             <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link2.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link2.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <h4>
             Group: Illustration Friday
            </h4>
            <p>
             If you're still finding it hard to make time to sketch you could consider joining a group like Illustration Friday. As the name suggests, every friday there's a new theme to draw with the final artwork (traditional, digital or sketch) compiled the following week.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.illustrationfriday.com/">
              Visit Website
             </a>
            </p>
           </li>
           <li>
            <div>
             <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link1.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link1.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <h4>
             Article: Character Design Sketching Approaches
            </h4>
            <p>
             Back in April
             <a href="http://sessions.tutsplus.com/creative/category/character-illustration/">
              Creative Sessions
             </a>
             showcased eleven days of
             <a href="http://sessions.tutsplus.com/creative/category/character-illustration/">
              Character Illustration
             </a>
             . For me, this was one of the standout articles. Take a closer look at the sketching process of twelve character illustrators, and how each one approaches the process of design differently.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://vector.tutsplus.com/articles/interviews/roughly-speaking-character-design-sketching-approaches/">
              Visit Article
             </a>
            </p>
           </li>
           <li>
            <div>
             <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link4.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link4.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <h4>
             Article: Finding Your Hand Drawn Lettering Voice
            </h4>
            <p>
             Following on from Character Illustration, Creative Sessions ran twelve days of
             <a href="http://sessions.tutsplus.com/creative/category/illustrative-lettering/">
              Illustrative Lettering.
             </a>
             In Finding Your Hand Drawn Lettering Voice,
             <a href="http://chrispiascik.com/">
              Chris Piascik
             </a>
             shares his experiences with hand rendered lettering and how to find inspiration for your own experiments with type.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://vector.tutsplus.com/articles/theory/finding-your-hand-drawn-lettering-voice/">
              Visit Article
             </a>
            </p>
           </li>
           <li>
            <div>
             <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link3.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link3.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <h4>
             Blog: Moleskinerie
            </h4>
            <p>
             An inspiring glimpse of the people and places behind the sketches and scribbles.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.Moleskinerie.com/">
              Visit Blog
             </a>
            </p>
           </li>
           <li>
            <div>
             <img data-original-url="http://vectortuts.s3.amazonaws.com/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link5.jpg" src="https://cdn.tutsplus.com/vector/uploads/legacy/articles/2010/inspir_creative_books/link5.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <h4>
             Event: The Sketchbook Project
            </h4>
            <p>
             If you consider yourself a Notebook Ninja, check out the Sketchbook Project. Participants are asked to register before the 31st October 2010. Completed Sketchbooks will be given a new home at
             <a href="http://arthousecoop.com/library">
              The Brooklyn Art Library
             </a>
             and toured as part of a massive exhibition set to take place in 2011. More details can be found via
             <a href="http://arthousecoop.com/projects/sketchbookproject/">
              Art House Co-Op
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.arthousecoop.com/projects/sketchbookproject">
              Visit Website
             </a>
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
