     <h1 title="Root Canal Process Miami">
      What is the Root Canal Process?
     </h1>
     <strong>

     </strong>
     <br/>
     <div class="relatedCategories">
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a class="single" href="what-is-root-canal-therapy.html">
         What is Root Canal therapy?
        </a>

        <a href="what-is-the-root-canal-process.html">

     <div style="margin-bottom:15px">
      Root canal therapy is a common endodontic treatment provided by dentists as a way of maintaining the natural tooth structure of an infected tooth. Problems such as these may be caused due to bacteria or neglect of the smile, or even trauma. No matter how the problem occurs, it is important to work with a dentist who has the ability to maintain the natural tooth without extracting it. Extracting an adult natural tooth should be the last attempt to restore the smile.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Drs. Marlon Pasquier, Sr. and Marlon Pasquier, Jr. of Miami Modern Dental gladly provide patients with root canal procedures. During the process, patients will have the dental pulp removed from the inside of their natural tooth. The dental pulp is a mass of tissue in the canals which contain the blood supply and nerves. By removing the dental pulp and sealing off the tooth, the structure can be maintained but the diseased or infected portion can be removed. By doing so, patients will no longer experience pain but will benefit from maintaining the natural tooth and avoiding the need for restorative services such as dentures, dental bridges, and dental implants.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Patients who may be candidates for root canal therapy are encouraged to contact Drs. Marlon Pasquier, Sr. and Marlon Pasquier, Jr. of Miami Modern Dental to learn more about the ways in which they can restore their smile even after infection occurs.
