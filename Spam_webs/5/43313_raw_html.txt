         </a>
        </h2>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">

       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p>
         <img alt="Upromise save for college" class="alignright wp-image-5959 size-medium" height="300" src="http://moneyqanda.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/save-money-while-in-college-230x300.jpg" width="230"/>
         When it comes to savingmoney forcollege, many students and parents are turning to extra jobs, grants, student loans, and scholarships. But, theres another way that you can earn money forcollege. At Upromise, theyve gone the extra mile and made it easy to save for college.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What Is Upromise?
        </h2>
        <p>
         You can join Upromise for free. Thats right, absolutely free of charge.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once youve become a member you can begin shopping on the Upromise website through featured shopping partners. These are merchants that you are already shopping with, but by buying things through the Upromise website, you can earn money for your childrens college education.
        </p>
        <p>
         You earn cash back by shopping online, going out to eat, registering your store (both drug store and grocery store) loyalty cards, booking travel arrangements, and other shopping tasks and experiences.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can also apply for the Upromise Master Card to help you earn money for college even faster. For each purchase, youll be earning cash back on your card. When you have earned cash back, you have three ways that you can use this money.
        </p>
        <h2>
         How To Use Your Upromise College Savings
        </h2>
        <p>
         The first way that you can use your savings from Upromise for college expenses is to put it into a college fund for your child or children.
        </p>
        <p>
         The second way is to pay down a student loan with the money that has accumulated in your Upromise account. And, the third way is to get your cash back returned to you via a check.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you choose to open a
         <a href="http://moneyqanda.com/529-college-savings-plans/">
          college savings plan
         </a>
         , you can set it up to directly deposit your cash back into this fund. This fund is ran through a Sallie Mae High Yield savings account.

         <a href="http://moneyqanda.com/struggling-as-an-accidental-landlord/" title="How I Am Struggling As An Accidental Landlord For The First Time">

         </a>
         , or living in it yourself, there are quite a few benefits ofa home warranty that you can take advantage of using.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Your Home Will Be Preferred By Buyers
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you want to make your home a preferred home to potential buyers, then you will want to have a home warranty plan on your home to help you attack buyers. When you have a home warranty, then the chances are there will be more potential buyers interested in buying your home.
        </p>
        <p>
         A Gallup Pollrevealed that 8 out of 10 buyers actually prefer to purchase a home that has a warranty plan. So, if you want to make your home more preferable, then get a home warranty plan for it.
        </p>

        <h2>
         Buyers Will Be Attracted To Your Home
        </h2>
        <p>
         As previously mentioned, home buyers will likely consider your home to be a preferred home when you have a warranty plan on it. Thats one of thebenefits of a home warranty.
        </p>
        <p>
         However, another one of the benefits of a home warranty is that you might end up attracting even more first-time home buyers. Many first-time buyers may not want to purchase a home without a warranty because they will not have a lot of money for things such as repairing systems, replacing systems, or even for appliances.
        </p>
        <p>
         Therefore, you can provide them with peace of mind when you have a home warranty supplied with yourhome. If you want to attract more first-time buyers, then you should consider having a home warranty plan put on your home.
