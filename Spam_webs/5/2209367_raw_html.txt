        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Leading, Following, or Hiding in the Shadows?
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-08-11T10:30:56+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          August 11, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.davidspell.com/author/davidspell/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            davidspell
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.davidspell.com/leading-following-or-hiding-in-the-shadows/#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <a href="http://davidspell.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/preachingwithnorival.jpg">
          <img alt="preachingwithnorival" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7243" height="580" src="http://davidspell.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/preachingwithnorival.jpg" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          C3 Curitiba recently had a sermon series called Next Generation Leadership. This message is about John Mark and his journey out of the shadows to becoming the leader that God had called him to be. We all go through times where we want to hide from our destiny. He was willing to learn from his mistakes and was willing to be trained by an older, wiser leader.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         John Mark was one of the leaders that we discuss in my new book,
         <em>
          New Testament Snapshots
         </em>
         . Here is how you can get your copy of the
         <a href="http://davidspell.com/a-new-book/">
          book
         </a>
         . I know that you will love it!
        </p>
        <p>
         My translator for the message was my good friend, Norival Trinidade. He is the Vice-President of the International Leadership Institute. Norival travels the globe training Christian leaders. It was an honor for me to work with him for the evening.
        </p>
        <p>
         Click
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LBw0K4daYbk&amp;list=UUqI6NiX-8WIIqi6VUe1iScw">
          here
         </a>
         to watch the message.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          My wife, Annie, and I are helping train and develop leaders in Curitiba, Brazil. Would you consider becoming a part of our support team? Just click
          <a href="https://c3church.wufoo.com/forms/brazil-mission-trip-dave-and-annie-spell/">
           here
          </a>
          . Obrigado!
         </strong>
        </p>
