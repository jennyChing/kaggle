                  <h1>
                   12.
                   <a class="external-link elf-add-back-link" href="http://www.blogher.com/frame.php?url=http%3A%2F%2Ftackorama.net%2F" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                    Tack-o-rama
                   </a>
                  </h1>
                  <p>
                   Yet another great image source. Tack-o-rama offers retro photos, fonts, and small resolution clip art for free. No registration required. They don't have a large selection, but if you are looking for a high quality retro image, this is your best bet.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   I know there are many more great resources out there. These are the ones I have depended on and have had wonderful results with. It is amazing what free programs offer these days! I hope this list helps you in your search!
                  </p>
