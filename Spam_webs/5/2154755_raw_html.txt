               <h1>
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/2011/05/20/for-the-ladies-dc-comics-hockey-stripe-babydolls/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to For The Ladies: DC Comics Hockey Stripe Babydolls!">
                 For The Ladies: DC Comics Hockey Stripe Babydolls!
                </a>
               </h1>
               <p class="published">
                Posted
                <span class="postDateTime">
                 5/20/11 2:34 pm EST
                </span>
                by
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/author/hatfield/" rel="author" title="Posts by Don Hatfield">
                 Don Hatfield
                </a>
                in
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/category/comic-books/" rel="category tag">
                 Comic Books
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/category/dc-comics/" rel="category tag">
                 DC Comics
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/category/geek-gifts/" rel="category tag">
                 Geek Gifts
                </a>
               </p>
               <br class="clear"/>
              </div>
              <div class="entry">
               <p>
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/batman_superman_hockey_babydoll600.jpg">
                 <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27449" height="373" src="http://geek-news.mtv.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/batman_superman_hockey_babydoll600.jpg" title="Batman &amp; Superman Hockey Stripe Babydoll" width="576"/>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                Ladies, are you having trouble finding an awesome superhero shirt to wear to that convention coming up? If so, you should head over to ThinkGeek and check out their new
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/dc-comics/" target="_blank" title="DC Comics">
                 DC Comics
                </a>
                Hockey Stripe Babydoll Tees. After the mad love you showed for the
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/batgirl/" target="_blank" title="Batgirl">
                 Batgirl
                </a>
                and
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/wonder-woman/" target="_blank" title="Wonder Woman">
                 Wonder Woman
                </a>
                costume shirts they decided to give you some more options when it comes to geeky threads.
               </p>
               <p>
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/flash_lantern_hockey_babydoll600.jpg">
                 <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27450" height="356" src="http://geek-news.mtv.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/flash_lantern_hockey_babydoll600.jpg" title="Green Lantern &amp; Flash Hockey Stripe Babydoll" width="576"/>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                Whether youre a fan of
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/batman/" target="_blank" title="Batman">
                 Batman
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/superman/" target="_blank" title="Superman">
                 Superman
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/the-flash/" target="_blank" title="The Flash">
                 The Flash
                </a>
                or the
                <a href="http://geek-news.mtv.com/tag/green-lantern/" target="_blank" title="Green Lantern">
                 Green Lantern
                </a>
                theyve got a new shirt waiting for you right now. Why is it called a hockey stripe babydoll you ask? Well its simple; you see the stripe that runs around the bottom of each sleeve - thats a hockey stripe. So, if you were paying attention to the Stanley Cup Playoffs and your team (like mine) failed to make it to the finals then you can just wear your Superman tee and be done with it -- thats what Im doing.
               </p>
               <p>
                Sadly, none of these shirts will make you fly, run fast, fight good or aid in your space travel needs. However, they do feature cool distressed looking logos and can take 20 of your hard-earned dollars and turn them into a t-shirt that magically arrives on your doorstep! Head over to
                <a href="http://www.thinkgeek.com/tshirts-apparel/womens/e88b/" target="_blank" title="ThinkGeek">
                 ThinkGeek
                </a>
                if one (or all) of these is the DC shirt youve been waiting for.
               </p>
