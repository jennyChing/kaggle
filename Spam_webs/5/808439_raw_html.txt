Thankfully, Baku and Ginja swimwear provide that option for me, and whats even more amazing is that a lot of theirsuits have a variety of different tops and bottoms to choose from, which gives you the luxury of choosing the right suit for your body. I think its really important to finda suit that best complementsyour figure  you become much more aware of your appearance in that little of clothing, so it better fit right!
