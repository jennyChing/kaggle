          <h1 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.interioridea.net/10-amazing-living-room-interior-design-ideas-with-concrete-walls/">
            10 Amazing Living Room Interior Design Ideas with Concrete Walls
           </a>
          </h1>
          <span class="author">
           <a href="http://www.interioridea.net/author/dragana/" rel="author" title="Posts by Dragana Markovska">
            Dragana Markovska
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="entry-audio">

          <p>
           The industrial interior design has been around for just as much time to catch everybodys attention. Whether you absolutely adore it, or you are just not so happy about it, you must admit that there is some distinctive beauty in this raw and unfinished industrial look and that it most certainly has its own charm and urban feel.
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            One of the easiest ways to achieve an industrial look at home is to use industrial materials. Concrete is a logical choice and recentlywe have witnessed a huge rise in the use of concrete in interior design. Many designers and home-owners are now using concrete in unexpected and unusual ways and it seems like there is nothing that cant be made using this versatile material. We agree that at least, a single concrete wall is something you should eventually try, so we gathered some urban designs of living room with concrete walls to draw your attention and to prove you that it indeed looks absolutely impressive. Check them out and enjoy!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-1659">
           </span>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Beautiful Living Room Design with Concrete Walls
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/rad_design_concrete_loft_04.jpg">
             <img alt="rad_design_concrete_loft_04" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1668" height="1000" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/rad_design_concrete_loft_04.jpg" width="800"/>
            </a>
            Image Credit:
            <a href="http://architizer.com/projects/loft-002/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://architizer.com/projects/loft-002/', 'architizer.com']);">
             architizer.com
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Just take a look at that brilliant mix of different kinds of textures into one perfectly harmonious whole for an urban industrial appearance of this living room. The concrete ceiling works as a contrast to all those fine materials, the sofa, the leather armchairs, the rich wooden flooor and as a central eye-catching point you have that unique light fixture. A brilliant design.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Spacious Living Room Design with Concrete Walls
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/concrete_living_room_13.jpg">
            <img alt="concrete_living_room_13" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1663" height="600" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/concrete_living_room_13.jpg" width="800"/>

           <a href="http://www.elle.es/elledeco/casas/un-bunker-de-la-segunda-guerra-mundial-convertido-en-vivienda-de-diseno" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.elle.es/elledeco/casas/un-bunker-de-la-segunda-guerra-mundial-convertido-en-vivienda-de-diseno', 'www.elle.es']);">
            www.elle.es
           </a>
          </p>

          <p>
           The spacious, bright and impressive appearance of this living room has left us speechless. We absolutely love the concrete fire place placed in the middle of the room as a focal spot, and all the stylish furniture around, that make the whole space look edgy, and still welcoming and nice.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Appealing Living Room Design with Concrete Walls
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/concrete_living_room_7.jpg">
            <img alt="concrete_living_room_7" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1662" height="1000" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/concrete_living_room_7.jpg" width="800"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Image Credit:
           <a href="http://architizer.com/projects/ukranian-loft/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://architizer.com/projects/ukranian-loft/', 'architizer.com']);">
            architizer.com
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           How can you not like this wonderful dark blue, grey and white combo since it looks so appealing? Dark blue walls to pair the concrete ceiling and soft white furniture to warm that all up and give it a doze of elegance and grace. And for an ultimate adorable look, you see all those colors on the rug- amazing.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Attic Living Room Design with Concrete Walls
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/concrete_living_room.jpg">
            <img alt="concrete_living_room" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1661" height="1000" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/concrete_living_room.jpg" width="800"/>

            tretrend.se
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           A beautiful way of turning your attic room into a fine industrial looking living room with concrete walls. Love the stylish fireplace as it gives the space warmth and elegance.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Eye-catchingLiving Room Design with Concrete Walls
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/sorrento_house_rob_mills.jpg">
            <img alt="sorrento_house_rob_mills" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1669" height="800" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/sorrento_house_rob_mills.jpg" width="800"/>
           </a>
           Image Credit:
           <a href="http://designalmic.com/sorrento-house-robert-mills-architects/sorrento-house-by-robert-mills-architects-04/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://designalmic.com/sorrento-house-robert-mills-architects/sorrento-house-by-robert-mills-architects-04/', 'designalmic.com']);">
            designalmic.com
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Concrete walls dont always look dull and lifeless, especially if you splash a pop of warm color to liven them up. Apparently, grey and orange is a brilliant color match. Love how the fireplace wood actually matches the orange art around it.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           AlluringLiving Room Design with Concrete Walls
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/sorrento_house_rob_mills_02.jpg">
            <img alt="sorrento_house_rob_mills_02" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1670" height="800" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/sorrento_house_rob_mills_02.jpg" width="800"/>
           </a>
           Image Credit:
           <a href="http://archi-now.blogspot.de/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://archi-now.blogspot.de/', 'archi-now.blogspot.de']);">
            archi-now.blogspot.de
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           This spacios living, dining and kitchen space in monochrome palette looks stylish and urban, and it is complemented with some concrete walls for an additional drama that makes the elegant furniture textures stand out even more. Concrete walls love the companion of white furniture and accesories, and you can not not agree that it looks gorgeous.
