          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pol_Pot">

         </i>

        <p>
         <img alt="Saddam Hussein" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4122" height="462" src="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Saddam_Hussein.jpg" width="640"/>

          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saddam_Hussein">

         </i>

        <p align="center">

          <a href="http://www.pbs.org/frontlineworld/stories/cambodia/tl02.html">
           500,000 Cambodian
          </a>

         </i>
        </p>

        <p align="center">
         <i>
          #10 Leopold II of Belgium: He had a personal slave nation to get him rubber and ivory from the Congo. It is estimated that

           2 to 15 million Congolese
          </a>
          died for his private exploit.
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Hideki Tojo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4141" height="817" src="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Hideki_Tojo.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <i>
          #11 Hideki Tojo: In a very Nazi-like fashion,
          <a href="http://www.moreorless.net.au/killers/tojo.html">

          , deadly experiments on humans and chemical warfare. He reigned over East Asia for a short stint.
         </i>
        </p>

         <img alt="Enver pascha" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4166" height="856" src="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Enver-pascha.jpg" width="640"/>

         </i>
        </p>

           he murdered millions of his own people
          </a>
          .
         </i>
        </p>

         <a href="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Mengistu_Haile_Mariam.jpg">

         </a>

           A dictator from Ethiopia who is responsible for the deaths of 2 million people.
          </a>
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="President Suharto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4143" height="820" src="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/President-Suharto.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>

          .

        </p>

          #20 George W. Bush
         </i>
          Again, the term genocidal is debatable when it comes to describing George Bush. But considering the death and destruction that originated from the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan, he should probably be tried for crimes against humanity (cmon no one should be above the law!). We made an infographic a few years ago that you should check out:
         <a href="http://toobbox.com/blog/child-death-toll-of-iraq-and-afghanistan/">
          Child Death Toll of Iraq and Afghanistan
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="dick cheney" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4272" height="810" src="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/dick-cheney.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <i>
          #21 Dick Cheney  Similar to how Kissinger puppeteered Nixon, many believe Cheney was the man controlling the strings when it came to George Bushs decisions..
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="us calvary man" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4255" height="945" src="http://toobbox.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/us-calvary-man.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <i>
          #22 U.S. Calvary Infantryman  This list could go on forever. But, yeah  the U.S. Calvary was instrumental in helping eliminate the Native Americans from the North American continent.
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         So what do they all have in common?
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Answer: They were/are all men!
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
        </center>
        <p>
         <i>
          by the way, Im a dude
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not sure what the world is waiting onbut dont we have enough data by now to make a fairly good guess that
         <strong>
          men make dangerous leaders?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Should we try women for a change?
        </p>
        <p>
         I say we ban men from entering world leadership positions for a good 25 year period and see what happens.
        </p>
        <p>
         Yeah I know some people will say Thatcher was like a man in this respect. But aside from the Falkland Wars, her track record was pretty clean compared to the guys above.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whos with me?
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Evolutionary Alpha-Male Psychology and the Role of Leadership
        </h2>
        <p>
         This video might be the most interesting video IN YEARS  watch it!:
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/ReRcHdeUG9Y" width="640">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         I guess without realizing it we might be adhering to the evolutionary design of the alpha-male leader. Is that why leaders are usually men? Because we are used to having the tough alpha-male type take charge? Is that because the tribe is comfortable with this system?
        </p>
        <p>
         I sure hope we can evolve past that. Maybe it worked well during hunter-gather times, but Im hoping we can move past this and design a more sensible structure for leadership.
        </p>
        <p>
         But the scary thing is: it seems that its built into our DNA and chemistry. I highly recommend you watch that entire talk (the video presented above) because it touches on how our chemistry plays a role on how we select leaders.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Some Final Takeaways
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <b>
           Never trust anyone who wears medals.
          </b>
           Notice how some of the dudes above have this thing with the self-decoration of medals. There is definitely some weird-ego shit going on.
         </li>
         <li>
          <b>
           Dont let your leaders rule for more than 4 years.
          </b>
           Hell, I would even suggest 2 years. But they wont have time to get anything done! I have some advice, you should design your government to not need ONE PERSON to get things done. The purpose of government is to serve its citizens. It should be as efficient as possible at getting things done  with 10s or 1000s of people working inside of it!
         </li>
         <li>
          <b>
           Check out the Zapatistas of Mexico
          </b>
          
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Councils_of_Good_Government">
           They have completely reversed the roles of leadership
          </a>
           to the point where being the President is basically jury duty! Thats right! Everyone has to be President for two weeks.
         </li>
         <li>
          <b>
           Why would you let one asshole ruin your life?
          </b>
           The whole idea of leaders is really odd when you think about. Why would you let the entire country and livelihood of your family depend so much
          <b>
           on one person!?!?
          </b>
          . Dont let it happen. Be instrumental in your own country design. Be the change you want to see.
         </li>
        </ul>
