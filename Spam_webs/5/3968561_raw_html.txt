       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Cinnabon Cinnapopper Pie
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-02-06T01:00:32+00:00">
         February 6, 2014
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/2014/02/06/cinnabon-cinnapopper-pie/#comments">
          16 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/cinnapopper.jpg">
          <img alt="cinnapopper" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6255" height="965" src="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/cinnapopper.jpg" width="643"/>
         </a>
         Pet peeeeeeve time.
        </p>
        <p>
         You know when you go to concerts or attractions or events and theres always
         <em>
          that person
         </em>
         filming every little detail? Except theyre filming it with the microscopic, low-grade camera on their phone AND the place is extremely loud and dark? Yeah  Filmers of Dark and Loud Events, please stop. I beg of you.
        </p>
        <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
         <center>
          <!-- /18190176/TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_1_300x250 -->
          <div id="TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_1_300x250" style="height:250px; width:300px;">
           <script type="text/javascript">
            googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_1_300x250'); });
           </script>
          </div>
         </center>
        </div>
        <p>
         Because it isnt really the fact that youre completely useless standing in the way while you attempt to film everything happening on your miniature screen. Aaand it isnt really the fact that I personally think you should be
         <em>
          enjoying
         </em>
         the actual event rather than filming it. Its actually because inevitably, you or someone like you will come into contact with me at a later date and insist I watch said video. And I can think of a handful of other things Id rather be doing (toilet paper shopping, getting a papercut, paying a toll tax) than watching your grainy, low-res video of some indistinguishable concert.
        </p>
        <p>
         You went to go see Britney Spears? Well congratulations, but you managed to take a probably pretty good concert and turn it into a 6 minute video of a tiny pixelated being making incoherent animal noises with a bunch of scratchy screaming in the background. Gee, if I knew I could experience the entire concert from your crappy camera phone, why spend the money on actual tickets?! I may as well stand awkwardly over your phone watching the vague sounds of instruments and the incomprehensible shapes of the band wiggle around as your phone struggled to capture anything meaningful of the event itself.
        </p>
        <p>
         So I apologize if Im being curt, but really  I
         <em>
          dont
         </em>
         want to see your grainy videos of the luau you saw on Waikiki or the terrible footage of Coachella. I have papercuts to get and toilet paper shopping to tend to.
        </p>
        <p>
         Something I also must tend to? Eating as much cinnamon rolls as possible. Because #Yolo. Actually, because have you
         <em>
          eaten
         </em>
         a cinnamon roll? Theyre amazing, and stopping at one is nearly impossible. Especially if theyre the gooey, chewy goodness of Cinnabon Cinnamon Rolls! You
         <em>
          know
         </em>
         you cant resist the sweet aroma of cinnamon and cream cheese icing when you walk past their store in the mall even if they know you on a first-name basis and even if you take all your first dates there whatever. Theyre simply irresistible!
        </p>
        <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
         <center>
          <!-- /18190176/TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_2_300x250 -->
          <div id="TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_2_300x250" style="height:250px; width:300px;">
           <script type="text/javascript">
            googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_2_300x250'); });
           </script>
          </div>
         </center>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DSC_0350A.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0350A" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6260" height="965" src="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DSC_0350A.jpg" width="643"/>
         </a>
         So when I found these
         <a href="http://www.cinnabon.com/goodies/grocery-goodies.aspx#1176">
          Cinnabon Cinnapoppers
         </a>
         at the store, I knew I had to make something intoxicatingly delicious with them. And what better way to create something scrumptious than to bake all those gooey, chewy cinnamon rolls into one fantastic, outrageous pie? NO BETTER WAY, THATS WHAT.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DSC_0324A.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0324A" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6257" height="965" src="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DSC_0324A.jpg" width="643"/>
         </a>
         Do yourself a service and a service to humanity and
         <em>
          make this pie.
         </em>
         Just please, dont film yourself making it at midnight on a Thursday. No one cares about the process, they just want the pieeeeeeeeee!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DSC_0335A.jpg">

           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Meanwhile in a small bowl, mix together the melted butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla, flour and cinnamon with a whisk until combined. Evenly pour the butter mixture on top and throughout the Cinnapopper Pie, coating the rolls as best as you can.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake the pie for approx. 15-20 minutes, or until the pie appears mostly set and is not super jiggly in the middle. If it remains jiggly, pop it in the oven for another 5 minutes -- take care not to over bake the pie as it will set up while it cools.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Allow the pie to cool for about 10 minutes before generously drizzling the top of the pie with the cream cheese icing. As I said in the note, you may use the icing provided in the Cinnapopper packages, or you may additionally use your favorite prepared cream cheese icing, be it from a can or homemade as an additional, heavier drizzle. Cut into wedges and serve warm! Indulge a little more by topping each warm slice with vanilla ice cream.
           </li>
          </ol>
