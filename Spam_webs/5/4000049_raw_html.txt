        <h1 class="entry-title">
         In JOLLY OL FASCIST ENGLAND, you get arrested for saying Im not a Muslim at an airport security check
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #3c78d8;">
          A pensioner who said Im not Muslim when he was asked to remove his shoes at airport security has spent more than six months facing charges for racism.
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <em>
            (What race is Islam?
           </em>
           )
          </span>
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="1414362393683_wps_2_Colchester_man_Paul_Griff" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-272752" height="422" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/1414362393683_wps_2_Colchester_man_Paul_Griff.jpg" width="634"/>
        </p>
        <h4>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2808822/Ordeal-OAP-quipped-m-not-Muslim-airport-security-stopped-Man-spends-six-months-facing-racism-charges-case-finally-dropped.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2808822/Ordeal-OAP-quipped-m-not-Muslim-airport-security-stopped-Man-spends-six-months-facing-racism-charges-case-finally-dropped.html', 'UK Daily Mail']);">
            UK Daily Mail
           </a>
          </span>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           (h/t Terry D)
          </span>
          Paul Griffith, 75,pictured above, set off the security scanners alarm at Stansted airport when travelling to Malaga for a weeks holiday.He removed his shoes as requested, but said: I am not Muslim am I?
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          A
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <em>
            (Muslim?)
           </em>
          </span>
          security guard accused him of racism and called the police, saying he was upset by the remark.One minute I am queuing up to get on a plane and the next I am confrontedby two armed policemen.
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           They said I had used racist language and took me to an office in the terminal
          </span>
          , Mr Griffith said yesterday.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="obama-airport-security" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-272762" height="432" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/obama-airport-security.gif" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          Mr Griffith was allowed to go on his trip but was arrested when he returned. He was charged with causing racially or religiously aggravated harassment, alarm or distress.When I got back I had to wait six hours before they interviewed me again,
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           arrested me and said that was being charged with causing racially aggravatedharassment.
          </span>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          I was photographed, had my finger prints taken and they also took a DNA swabfrom my mouth. Then they said I would have to go to my local police station. When I went to Colchester police station I was told I had been charged withan offense under the Crime and Disorder Act but that I could accept acaution instead.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_272760" style="width: 810px">
         <img alt='"Have a nice flight"' class="size-full wp-image-272760" height="474" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/niqab-airport-security-266908.jpg" width="800"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <strong>
           Of course you dont have to take the bag off your head, mam. Have a nice flight
          </strong>
         </p>
        </div>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          I refused to do that 
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           I had done nothing wrong and I wasnt going to admitto a criminal charge if I wasnt guilty of any crime.
          </span>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          Months later he appeared at Chelmsford Magistrates Court where he pleaded not guilty to the offence. The case was adjourned to last Thursday, but with just 24 hours before appearing, the
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           Crown Prosecution Service suddenly dropped the charge due to lack of evidence.
          </span>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          The retired hairdressing salon owner, from Colchester, Essex, yesterday criticised the police for their heavy-handedness.I have never fallen foul of the law before and the whole affair has been a complete waste of police time, the courts time, my time as well as taxpayers money.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="tsa airport security checkpoint arabs midle easterners profiling agents terrorists motivational posters" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-272759" height="600" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/tsa-airport-security-checkpoint-arabs-midle-easterners-profiling-agents-terrorists-motivational-posters.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          It has been incredibly stressful  all because I asked a question and apparently dared to use the M word.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          Deputy Chief Crown Prosecutor for CPS East of England, Frank Ferguson said: Following receipt of the evidential file a full review of the evidence took place.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          In order to successfully prosecute a charge of racially or religiouslyaggravated disorderly conduct, we first have to show that the language usedwas threatening or abusive and in these particular circumstances we couldnot show that to the high criminal standard required.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="air-infidel1412-e13903589896911" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-272754" height="591" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/air-infidel1412-e13903589896911.jpg" width="824"/>
        </p>
        <h4 class="mol-para-with-font">
        </h4>
