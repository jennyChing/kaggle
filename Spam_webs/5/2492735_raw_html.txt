          <h1 class="news-title instapaper_title entry-title" itemprop="name">
           New Wonder Woman Costume Revealed
          </h1>
          <h3 class="news-byline">
           by
           <a href="/profile/g_man/" rel="author">
            Tony 'G-Man' Guerrero
           </a>
           on
           <time datetime="2015-03-12" itemprop="datePublished" pubdate="pubdate">
            March 12, 2015
           </time>
          </h3>
          <h2 class="news-deck" itemprop="description">
           Get ready for another edition of the Great Wonder Woman Costume Debate to begin.
          </h2>
         </section>
         <section class="article-body typography-format " itemprop="articleBody">
          <p style="">
           The last time Wonder Woman got a new costume (pants and a jacket), there was quite a commotion. People are often resistant to change. It turns out in WONDER WOMAN #41, on sale in June after Convergence, she'll be getting a new look.
           <a href="http://www.hitfix.com/harpy/exclusive-wonder-womans-new-costume-is-fierce-fabulous-and-deadly" rel="nofollow">
            HitFix
           </a>
           has revealed the solicit for the issue.
          </p>
          <figure data-align="center" data-embed-type="image" data-img-src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4444129-ww_cv41.jpg" data-ratio="1.5171875" data-ref-id="1300-4444129" data-resize-url="" data-resized="" data-size="large" data-width="640" style="width: 640px">
           <a class="fluid-height" data-ref-id="1300-4444129" href="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4444129-ww_cv41.jpg" style="padding-bottom:151.7%">
            <img src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/ignore_jpg_scale_super/0/40/4444129-ww_cv41.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </figure>
          <h3>
           WONDER WOMAN #41
          </h3>
          <p style="">
           Written by MEREDITH FINCH
          </p>
          <p style="">
           Art and cover by DAVID FINCH and
          </p>
          <p style="">
           JONATHAN GLAPION
          </p>
          <div class="js-mapped-ad mapped-ad mapped-incontent_top mapped-incontent_top-dart" id="incontent_top-wrap">
           <div class="ad-wrap ad-wrap-incontent_top" id="incontent_top">
           </div>
          </div>
          <p style="">
           THE JOKER Variant cover by BRIAN BOLLAND
          </p>
          <p style="">
           On sale JUNE 17  32 pg, FC, $3.99 US RATED T
          </p>
          <p style="">
           Retailers: This issue will ship with two covers. Please see the order form for details.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p style="">
            A daring new direction begins with the arrival of a brand-new villain! But while he may be new to us, hes not new to the world he seeks to tame. And speaking of villains, Donna Troys quest to destroy Wonder Woman ratchets up another gear (if thats even possible!), while the games of the Gods bring dark portents to the ultimate Amazon!
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p style="">
           Our immediate reaction is it has a lot of red. It really has a patriotic feel to it. Hopefully those wrist spikes are removable. Change is fine. There's no reason Wonder Woman shouldn't have more than one outfit. There's no reason she has to wear the same exact outfit every single time she goes out. We particularly dig
           <a data-ref-id="4040-61184" href="/jason-fabok/4040-61184/">
            Jason Fabok
           </a>
           's recent weak in the pages of
           <a data-ref-id="4050-42488" href="/justice-league/4050-42488/">
            JUSTICE LEAGUE
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <figure data-align="center" data-embed-type="image" data-img-src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4444134-tumblr_nk7z36pim31tdrx3yo1_1280.jpg" data-ratio="0.96451204055767" data-ref-id="1300-4444134" data-resize-url="" data-resized="" data-size="large" data-width="640" style="width: 640px">
           <a class="fluid-height" data-ref-id="1300-4444134" href="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4444134-tumblr_nk7z36pim31tdrx3yo1_1280.jpg" style="padding-bottom:96.5%">
            <img src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/ignore_jpg_scale_super/0/40/4444134-tumblr_nk7z36pim31tdrx3yo1_1280.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </figure>
          <p style="">
           Hopefully we haven't seen the last of that.
          </p>
          <p style="">
           What do you think of the new outfit?
          </p>
