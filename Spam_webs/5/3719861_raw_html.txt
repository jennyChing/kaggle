        Thom Glunt

        The New Pornographers
       </a>
       "...
       <span style="color: #222222;">
        to pay homage to those brilliant 90s music videos where the artists personality is prominently featured in one long unbroken shot." I assume he's referring to
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1lyu1KKwC74&amp;feature=kp" target="_blank">
         The Verve's "Bitter Sweet Symphony"

        clip,unless there was a huge surge of Steadicam shotsin music videos?

         A.C. Newman

        and
        <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/tag/destroyer/" target="_blank">
         Destroyer
        </a>
        's Dan Bejar star in the video for their supergroup's latest power pop single, bothbringing that
       </span>
       "mid-nineties second-tier Britpop attitude" (Newman's words) to their sunglassed catwalkthrough a polite war zone.
