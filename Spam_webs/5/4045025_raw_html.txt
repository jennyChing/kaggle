      <h2 class="post_title">
       I made some Sriracha popcorn and Sriracha underwear
      </h2>
      <div class="post_body">
       <div class="pad">
        <p class="floor4">
         Ever heard of
         <a href="http://store.baconsalt.com/Bacon-Salt-3-Pack_p_13.html">
          BaconSalt
         </a>
         ?
         <a href="http://store.baconsalt.com/Baconnaise--Regular-flavor_p_50.html">
          Baconnaise
         </a>
         ?
         <a href="http://baconlube.com/">
          Bacon Lube
         </a>
         ?   
		The guy who invented those products happens to live in Seattle,
		AND he happens to have a fondness for
         <a href="/comics/sriracha">
          Sriracha
         </a>
         that almost rivals his fondness for bacon. 
		So who better to team up with when I decided to start cooking
         <a href="http://shop.theoatmeal.com/products/sriracha-popcorn">
          Sriracha Popcorn
         </a>
         ?
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Rather than making something so spicy it was inedible, we focused more on making it taste
         <em>
          good.
         </em>
         The recipe contains a mixture of garlic, peppers, salt, and has quite a kick to it.  If you eat it really fast it's got a respectable afterburn, 
		but if you take it slow it's like riding a riverboat through the jungles of dee-lish.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         You buy the popcorn from
         <a href="http://shop.theoatmeal.com">
          my shop
         </a>
         OR you can get it on
         <a href="http://baconsalt.3dcartstores.com/?AffId=3">
          J&amp;D's website
         </a>
         if you're interested in buying it with some of their delicious bacon products,  such as
         <a href="http://store.baconsalt.com/Bacon-Salt-3-Pack_p_13.html">
          BaconSalt
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://store.baconsalt.com/JDs-Bacon-Croutons_p_132.html">
          Bacon Croutons
         </a>
         , 
		and their latest creation:
         <a href="http://store.baconsalt.com/Bacon-Crispy-Bake_p_151.html">
          Crispy Bacon Bake
         </a>
         .
