       <h3>
        <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/home/you/article-1332405/Georgie-Henley-Meet-Narnia-star-whos-hailed-new-Emma-Watson.html" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
         EVERYONES favourite brat from the Narnia films is all grown up.
        </a>
       </h3>
       <section class="text-description">
        The youngest sibling of the four Pevensie children, this toothy ten-year-old was the first to stumble into the Wardrobe and discover Narnia.
        <a class="ext-link" href="http://https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nJGg2NZwYC0" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
         http://https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nJGg2NZwYC0
        </a>
        <p>
         She had a special relationship with wise old lion Aslan in 2005 film The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe and also featured in The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian three years later.
        </p>
        <p>
         The precocious little girl was played by actress Georgie Henley who is now 19 and a bit of a stunner.
        </p>
        <p>
         Georgie continued to act after her childhood stardom days appearing in BBCs Jane Eyre and a couple of small films.
        </p>
        <p>
         She currently reads English at Cambridge University.
        </p>
        <p>
         Fun fact: At the end of the first Narnia film, the Pevensie children grow up and are pictured as their adult selves. Rachel Henley, Georgies sister, played the older Lucy.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5148" style="width: 745px">
         <img alt="Narina" class="wp-image-5148 size-full" height="1080" src="http://www.magazinetoday.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/199829.jpg" width="735"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          ALL GROWN UP: Georgie is now 19 and studying at Cambridge University [FACEBOOK]
         </p>
        </div>
