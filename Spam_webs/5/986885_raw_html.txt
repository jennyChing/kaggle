   Turkey Waldorf Sandwich (a Chyrel's Kitchen Recipe)
  </font>
  <br/>
  <br/>
  Ingredients:
  <ul>
   <li>
    3/4 cup mayonnaise
   </li>
   <li>
    1/3 cup chopped celery
   </li>
   <li>
    1/3 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
   </li>
   <li>
    1 teaspoon lemon juice
   </li>
   <li>
    Lettuce
   </li>
   <li>
    3/4 pound sliced turkey breast
   </li>
   <li>
    1 medium red or green apple, thinly sliced
   </li>
   <li>
    8 slices firm white bread
   </li>
  </ul>
  Method:
  <br/>
  In medium bowl combine mayonnaise, celery, walnuts and lemon juice. Layer lettuce, turkey, apple and mayonnaise mixture between toasted bread slices.
