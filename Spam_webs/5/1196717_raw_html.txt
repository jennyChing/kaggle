      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-50002" height="450" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-22.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Australian design firm
       <a href="http://www.carr.net.au/">
        Carr Design Group
       </a>
       have completed a modular house in Kilmore, Victoria, Australia.
       <span id="more-49699">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="450" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-02.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Called Modular House, the residence comprises two buildings, each arranged along a spine housing  all services.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-16.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The main building contains the living room, dining room, kitchen, study, storage and bedrooms, connected by glazed walkways.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-06.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Split-level timber decking joins this building to the guest pavillion, which contains a living and dining area, and two bedrooms.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-05.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The house is constructed using galvanised steel columns and dark plywood panels, chosen to match the clients cattle.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-04.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Large full-height windows, the over hanging roof and three metre-wide external timber walkways frame views of the landscape.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-07.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The home is constructed using a modular design called
       <a href="http://www.intermode.com.au/">
        Intermode
       </a>
       , launched by Carr Design Group in 2007.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-11.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The house recently won the Belle Georg Jensen Best Residential Interior Award 2009.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-49731" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-17.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Here's some more information from the designers:
      </p>
      <p>
       --
      </p>
      <p>
       CARR WINS BELLE GEORG JENSEN AWARDS
      </p>
      <p>
       Prominent Australian design firm Carr Design Group has won the inaugural Belle Georg Jensen Best Residential Interior Award recognising Australias most dynamic and sophisticated residential interior.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="600" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-03.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Announced at a presentation dinner in Sydney on Thursday night, the award acknowledges the most progressive and visionary residential project.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-49729" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-15.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Celebrating innovation and recognising excellence in Australian design, Carrs Modular House for Intermode beat a field of over 300 entries nation wide and 4 finalists to succeed against much larger projects with significantly greater budgets.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="295" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-01.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Carr Design Group Director Sue Carr commented, "We are elated to have won this exciting and prestigious award."
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-18.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       "Our Modular House for Intermode represents the result of our determination to create a simple, modern, modular housing system with the key benefits of a faster, more cost effective build, total flexibility in design and a strong environmental focus.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-10.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The outstanding recognition of this project to date is exciting not only as a nod to the quality focus and design intelligence behind this work but importantly recognises professional project delivery, reliable client service and proves that a house based on modular components and construction can result in a refined, modern and well built project.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="338" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-09.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Neale Whitaker Editor-in-chief Belle noted, The Belle Georg Jensen Award recognises and rewards an Australian Design Practice for the most inspirational and successfully realised residential interior.  A stringent list of criteria ensured that entries were judged for their attention to brief, aesthetic value and commercial viability. This simple and clever structure placed against a stark landscape and the near austerity of the interior leads to an amazingly utilitarian, gallery-like feel.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="318" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-14.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       This represents Carr Design Groups second major award win for the year having won one of the highest accolades for residential design, the 2009 Interior Design Award for the beautifully crafted Courtyard House.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="263" src="http://static.dezeen.com/uploads/2009/11/dzn_Country-Victoria-Modular-House-by-Carr-Design-Group-13.jpg" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Carrs Modular House for Intermode was also Shortlisted in the 2009 Interior Design Awards and the 2009 Interior Design Excellence Awards.
      </p>
