         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lifeinabreakdown.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/New-Look.jpg">
           <img alt="New Look" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10829" height="700" src="http://www.lifeinabreakdown.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/New-Look-568x700.jpg" width="568"/>
          </a>
          I have to admit quite often I spend the day in a plain old tshirt and joggers as I often find clothes hurt (yes I know that sounds odd but with my pain receptors on over drive even the slightest of extra pressure can send them off) so I take the easy route  clothes that move with me. That being said I do love having nice clothes for my good days and the days I am off out, the days when I can feel a little bit more like normal me.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          New Look are one of my go to stores as they offer reasonably priced clothes that even if they only come out once in a while I dont feel like Im hoarding a fortune in not too often worn clothing. In fact earlier in the month I took to their internet store and stocked up on jeans and trousers so I thought I would pop together a few items to go with those that I could wear on good and bad day.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Black and Gold Embossed Ankle Cuff Pumps 22.99
          </strong>
          <br/>
          I adore the look of these with the pretty ankle cuff and the fact they are flat so I really could wear them! Plus I could see them working both with trousers / jeans or a skirt / dress.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Black and Nude Lace Skater Dress  22.99
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Im not a huge dress girl but when I do want one I know can count on the selection of
          <a href="http://www.newlook.com/shop/womens/dresses/day-dresses/_/N-9utZbxc" rel="nofollow">
           dresses at New Look
          </a>
          to find something I like, and I adore this lace skater that they have at the moment.
         </p>
         <p>
