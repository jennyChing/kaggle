        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Light &amp; Healthy Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls with Maple Frosting
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-04T07:30:45+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 4, 2014
         </time>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://fooddonelight.com/light-healthy-pumpkin-cinnamon-rolls-with-maple-frosting/#comments">
           22 Comments
          </a>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          A delicious yeasty roll made with whole wheat and pumpkin filled with cinnamon makes a lighter and healthier treat for a special brunch. Grab a Light &amp; Healthy Pumpkin Cinnamon Roll while they last.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://fooddonelight.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/healthier-pumpkin-cinnamon-rolls_3870-text.jpg">
          <img alt="Ooey, goeey and delicious brunch - Healthy and Low Calorie Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls with Cream Cheese Frosting www.fooddonelight.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13210" height="700" src="http://fooddonelight.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/healthier-pumpkin-cinnamon-rolls_3870-text.jpg" width="466"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lately every time I sit to write a post about a recipe the words of the song Its the Most Wonderful Time of the Year pop into my head. I have managed to stop myself until now. Maybe if I finally let the words out, I will quit hearing them reverberate inside my head. While I try to enjoy every day, I do love this time of year. Everyone is a little nicer and kinder. I am really going to try to enjoy this particular year. I have a feeling it will be the last Christmas with a certain magic to it. My seven-year old was asking some pointed questions about Christmas the other day. Another child has obviously been telling her something, but she wouldnt tell me who. Luckily she was happy to believe me rather than whatever she heard. (Hopefully you can infer what I am talking about without me really saying just in case little eyes are reading over your shoulder)
        </p>
        <p>
         The only hard part about the upcoming holidays, all the food! It is everywhere. Neighbors drop off delicious cookies and baked goods for us. There are parties and get-togethers, andof course, the big dinners at Thanksgiving and Christmas. Can you feel your hips expanding just thinking about it? I know I can. Healthier versions of special treats are a great way to combat the weight gain. That makes Light &amp; Healthier Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls with Maple Frosting perfect. They feel very decadent but have a lot less calories and fat than the original. This time of year that is what it is about, cutting out a few calories here and there, and that E word, exercise.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://fooddonelight.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/healthier-pumpkin-cinnamon-rolls_3858.jpg">
          <img alt="Ooey, goeey and delicious brunch - Healthy and Low Calorie Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls with Cream Cheese Frosting www.fooddonelight.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13208" height="700" src="http://fooddonelight.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/healthier-pumpkin-cinnamon-rolls_3858.jpg" width="467"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The dough is easy to work with and filled with pumpkin puree. You can bake a couple of batches together and give them as gifts to your neighbors and friends. The luscious maple frosting makes them feel so special and decadent. The cinnamon filling brings a zing to the roll plus cinnamon helps to make baked goods taste sweeter without using more sugar. Really, what would a cinnamon roll be without the cinnamon!
        </p>
        <p>
         Bake a couple of batches and Wow your friends and neighbors with your baking prowess. It is all about Thanksgiving from here on out on Food Done Light. Be sure to come back on Thursday for a lightened Loaded Mashed Potato Casserole.
        </p>

         </a>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Light &amp; Healthy Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Cook time:
            <time datetime="PT20M" itemprop="cookTime">
             20 mins
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Total time:
            <time datetime="PT2H20M" itemprop="totalTime">
             2 hours 20 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSHead">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            12 servings
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Dough:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2  tsp. active yeast
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup warm water (100-110)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1  cups (210 grams) all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1  cups (180 grams) whole wheat pastry flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup (163 grams) pumpkin puree
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup non-fat milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup light butter, melted
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tbsp. granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1  tsp. kosher salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. ground cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. ground ginger
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. ground nutmeg
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Filling:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tbsp. brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tbsp. granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tsp. ground cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Pinch nutmeg
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Glaze:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup confectioners sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tbsp. hot maple syrup
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 to 2 tsp. hot water
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a bowl of a stand mixer with a dough hook, dissolve yeast in warm water.  Let sit for 5 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add the rest of the dough ingredients.  Mix on low speed.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Turn to medium and knead for 5 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Coat a large bowl with cooking spray.  Place dough into the bowl and turn to cover with cooking spray.  Cover and let rise for about 45 minutes.  Press two fingers into the dough. If the indentation remains, dough is ready.  If not, let the dough sit for another 5 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small bowl, combine filing ingredients.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spray a work surface with cooking spray.  Roll out dough into a 12 x 10 inch rectangle.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Sprinkle cinnamon sugar over the dough and press down lightly with your hand.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Starting at the long end, tightly roll up the dough.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cut the dough into 12 equal slices.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spray a 9 x 9 inch baking pan with cooking spray.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place rolls into the baking pan.  Cover and let rise for another 25 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 375
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake for about 20 minutes or until golden brown.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place on a wire rack and cool for 5 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix ingredients for glaze and spread over hot buns.  Serve warm.
           </li>
          </ol>
