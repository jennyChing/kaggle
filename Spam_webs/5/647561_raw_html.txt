          <p>
           <strong>
            Yield: 12 Servings
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #f0f;">
            <strong>
             Ingredients:
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           8 large mint sprigs, plus 12 for garnish
           <br/>
           3/4 cup lime juice (about 1 medium lime)
           <br/>
           1/2 cup agave syrup
           <br/>
           8 cups sauvignon blanc
           <br/>
           1/2 cup peaches, sliced
           <br/>
           10 strawberries, sliced
           <br/>
           1/2 cup lemon-lime seltzer
           <br/>
           12 lime wheels, for garnish
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #f0f;">
            <strong>
             Directions:
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           In a pitcher, muddle mint with lime juice and agave.
          </p>
          <p>
           Remove mint, then add wine, peaches, and strawberries. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
          </p>
          <p>
           When ready to serve, stir well and add seltzer.
          </p>
          <p>
           Serve in wineglasses and garnish each with fresh mint and a lime wheel.
          </p>
