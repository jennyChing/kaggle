        <div class="markdown description col7" id="description">
         <div id="description-text">
          "Moon Rabbits" is an original 8 x 10 inch acrylic painting on flat canvas board. Two purple-blue ghost bunnies cavort under the pale light of the moon in the spring. Pink cherry blossom tree branches arc over a woodland scene with faintly visible rabbits sniffing among pink mushrooms and green luna moths. A ghostly scene for a haunted Easter, Ostara or Beltane, and beautiful springtime art for your decor.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Surreal, dream like nature image for animal lovers, lovers of the night and moon, pagan and purple decors.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          If you want to custom order a print in 5 x 7 or 8 x 10 sizes, please contact me. Prices would be $8 or $12, respectively and would ship in about 5 business days.

          Check my other listings for more gothic, nature, psychedelic, hippie, Halloween, fantasy and other art!

          Shipping is priority mail in the US, first class elsewhere.
