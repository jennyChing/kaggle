         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           Just Married: Jodie Foster Weds Girlfriend Alexandra Hedison
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="tie-date">
           <i class="fa fa-clock-o">
           </i>
           April 23, 2014
          </span>
          <span class="post-comments">
           <i class="fa fa-comments">
           </i>
           <a href="http://www.lovebscott.com/news/just-married-jodie-foster-weds-girlfriend-alexandra-hedison#comments">
            <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="44418 http://www.lovebscott.com/?p=44418">
             2 Comments
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           <img alt="rs_634x1024-131007103336-634.foster.cm.10713" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44419" height="1024" src="http://www.lovebscott.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/rs_634x1024-131007103336-634.foster.cm_.10713.jpg" width="634"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Jodie Foster and her girlfriend of about a year are officially married.
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54); padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            A representative for the Oscar-winning actress confirms that Foster wed girlfriend Alexandra Hedison over the weekend. Publicist Jennifer Allen offered no other details Wednesday.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54); padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            E! was the first to report that Foster tied the knot with Hedison, a 44-year-old photographer based in Los Angeles.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54); padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            Foster publicly came out in a rambling, heartfelt speech at the 2013 Golden Globe Awards, where the 51-year-old acknowledged longtime partner Cydney Bernard, with whom she has two sons. The couple ended their 20-year relationship in 2008.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54); padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            Foster previously thanked her partner at other Hollywood events but kept her sexuality private.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54); padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            E! says Foster and Hedison have been dating since last summer.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54);">
           Congrats to Jodie and Alexandra!
          </p>
          <p style="color: rgb(54, 54, 54);">
           [via
           <a href="http://bigstory.ap.org/article/jodie-foster-weds-artist-alexandra-hedison" target="_blank">
            AP
           </a>
           ]
          </p>
