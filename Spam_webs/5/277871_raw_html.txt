        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         New World Record Whitetail  The Johnny King Buck
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <h3>
         Who doesnt love big bucks, especially
         <a href="/whitetail-deer-hunts/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="trophy deer hunts">
          big typical whitetail bucks
         </a>
         that break records? A new World Record whitetail doesnt happen very often and in this case it hasnt happened since the 1990s nearly 2 decades!
        </h3>
        <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_35099" style="width: 600px;">
         <a href="http://gothunts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/New-World-Record-Whitetail.jpg">
          <img alt="New World Record Whitetail" class="size-full wp-image-35099" height="758" src="http://gothunts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/New-World-Record-Whitetail.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
          The Johnny King Buck
          <br/>
          Photo by Shane Indrebo, courtesy of www.classicantlers.com
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         The Big Buck Club announced today that the Johnny King Buck from Wisconsin is the new World Record Typical Whitetail (gross scoring) buck of all time. On April 13, the bucks final scoring came in at a
         <strong>
          gross 221-6/8
         </strong>
         beating the Milo Hansen buck from
         <a href="http://gothunts.com/category/saskatchewan-big-game-hunting/" title="Hunting in Saskatchewan">
          Saskatchewan
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         A panel of One Boone and Crockett scorers, one member of the North East Big Buck Club, and the official scorer who first scored the record in question scored the buck together. The Northeast Club mainly consists of entries from the east to mid-east but it also includes deer throughout North America. They celebrate all deer no matter where they come from.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Our goal is not to dispute Boone and Crockett. Our goal is to be consistent with the rules of a gross-scoring club, in which there is no over-emphasis on symmetry. Symmetry is purely related to net scores.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         Scoring varies between both clubs as they each have their own scoring system, but they are very close in criteria.
        </p>
        <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_35096" style="width: 597px;">
         <a href="http://gothunts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Johnny-King-Buck.jpg">
          <img alt="New World Record Whitetail" class="size-full wp-image-35096" height="609" src="http://gothunts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Johnny-King-Buck.jpg" width="597"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
          Johnny King with his massive white-tailed deer, which has been scored and officially deemed
          <br/>
          the world record by the Northeast Big Buck Club.
          <br/>
          (Photo: www.whitetailworld.com)
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         If your interested in chasing some monster whitetails inquire
         <a href="/deer-hunting-north-american-species/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Deer Hunting Guides and Outfitters">
          here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
