                     You might remember this tutorial from a
                     <u>
                      <b>

                        while ago

                     . It is my everyday neutral eyeshadow... Some like to call it a "neutral smokey eye". Truth is, I wanted to redo it because I actually took those other photos SO many years ago, before I even had this blog. Quite honestly, I think it needed an update because even though that tutorial has had about 100k pins on Pinterest, I hate the photos. So, here is a fresh new 2012 update! I simplified things for you too! Is it horrible that I pretty much still do my makeup the same way every day? I don't think so! Proves it works and is easy! Enjoy!
