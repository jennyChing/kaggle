     <section class="entry clearfix">
      <p>
       <span class="post-attachment">
        <a href="http://food-ehow-com.blog.ehow.com/files/2014/04/dsc_0522cropped.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-21203" height="413" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/640/ehow-food-blog-us/files/2014/04/dsc_0522cropped.jpg" title="DSC_0522cropped" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       I love a hearty bowl of pasta, and this particular recipe is one of my favorites. Its so creamy that you would never guess that its dairy-free. And thanks to the hearty white beans and gluten-free pasta, you dont miss the gluten either. Once you see my carefully selected list of flavorful, nutritious ingredients, your mouth will be watering.
       <br/>
       <span class="post-attachment">
        <a href="http://food-ehow-com.blog.ehow.com/files/2014/04/dsc_0116edited2.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-21211" height="730" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/640/ehow-food-blog-us/files/2014/04/dsc_0116edited2.jpg" title="DSC_0116edited2" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       Fresh zucchini, white beans, crushed red pepper flakes, ground flax seeds (rich in Omega 3s to fight inflammation) and sesame seeds (great source of non-dairy calcium) get tossed together in a fun, flavorful and savory sauce with gluten-free spaghetti for a tasty dish with a little kick. This will surely trick your taste buds into thinking you used dairy and gluten! Its just that good, and just that good for you.
      </p>
      <p>
       Enjoy!
      </p>
      <p>
       <span class="post-attachment">
        <a href="http://food-ehow-com.blog.ehow.com/files/2014/04/dsc_0094cropped.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-21207" height="930" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/640/ehow-food-blog-us/files/2014/04/dsc_0094cropped.jpg" title="DSC_0094cropped" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Spicy White Bean and Zucchini Spaghetti
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free and Vegan
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Serves 8
      </p>
      <p>
       Ingredients
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        12 oz. gluten-free pasta
       </li>
       <li>
         cup olive oil
       </li>
       <li>
        1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
       </li>
       <li>
        2 Tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
       </li>
       <li>
        2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
       </li>
       <li>
         tsp. sea salt
       </li>
       <li>
         tsp. pepper
       </li>
       <li>
        1 (15 oz.) can white beans, drained and rinsed
       </li>
       <li>
        1 large zucchini, thinly sliced
       </li>
       <li>
        1/2 cup finely chopped fresh basil leaves
       </li>
       <li>
         tsp. crushed red pepper
       </li>
       <li>
        1 Tbsp. sesame seeds
       </li>
       <li>
        1 Tbsp. Barleans organic ground flax seeds
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Directions
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        Cook gluten-free pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water according to package; drain and rinse under cool water for 2 minutes then set aside.
       </li>
       <li>
        Meanwhile, in a large bowl whisk oil, vinegar, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, sea salt and pepper.
       </li>
       <li>
        Add cooked pasta, white beans, zucchini and finely chopped fresh basil, red pepper, sesame seeds and ground flax seeds; gently toss to coat with balsamic dressing.
       </li>
       <li>
        Serve warm.
       </li>
      </ol>
