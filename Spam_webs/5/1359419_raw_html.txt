                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Gooey Butter Cake
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3761296648995658651" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-l49ckhlhGIA/UgvVjEfzjJI/AAAAAAAALFM/fm1hZSHbCxM/s1600/Gooey-Butter-Cake-Recipe-1.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Gooey Butter Cake" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-l49ckhlhGIA/UgvVjEfzjJI/AAAAAAAALFM/fm1hZSHbCxM/s1600/Gooey-Butter-Cake-Recipe-1.png" title="Gooey Butter Cake" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      Yesterday I had lunch with some good friends and I was feeling like eating a really good treat. I mean when do I not feel like eating a really good treat? anyway I decided to whip up some gooey butter cake. I hadn't made this in quite a while and let me tell you this is definitely a dessert to write home about, or write a blog post about. It's really simple to make and the results are out of this world good. Make it for your next party, or maybe just because its thursday :)...
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YQxtjTWPcxo/UgvVkBAA_9I/AAAAAAAALFc/h5hvOEBUrfI/s1600/Gooey-Butter-Cake-Recipe.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Gooey Butter Cake Recipe" border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YQxtjTWPcxo/UgvVkBAA_9I/AAAAAAAALFc/h5hvOEBUrfI/s1600/Gooey-Butter-Cake-Recipe.png" title="Gooey Butter Cake" width="432"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       Gooey Butter Cake
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="border: none; margin-bottom: 0in; padding: 0in;">
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit;">
                       First Layer:
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="border: none; margin-bottom: 0in; padding: 0in;">
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit;">
                       1 pkg.
yellow or white cake mix
                       <br/>
                       1 stick (1/2 c.) butter, melted
                       <br/>
                       1 egg
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0.2in;">
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit;">
                       Mix
together cake mix (just the mix, not the other ingredients called for
on the box) with butter and 1 egg. Pat into an ungreased 9x13 inch
cake pan.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit;">
                       Topping:
                       <i>
                        GOOEY
BUTTER
                       </i>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="border: none; margin-bottom: 0in; padding: 0in;">
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit;">
                       1 (8 oz.)
pkg. cream cheese, softened
                       <br/>
                       2 eggs
                       <br/>
                       1 (1 lb.) box confectioners'
sugar- (or 4 cups)
                       <br/>
                       1 tsp. vanilla
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit;">
                       Cream
together cream cheese, 2 eggs, powdered sugar and vanilla. Beat for 5
minutes. Pour over cake mixture, spreading to the edges. Bake at
350F for 25 minutes. Watch for edges to barely turn golden brown.
Cake will be a little jiggly and have a thin crust over the top when it's done.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       Make sure
to let the cake cool completely before serving or it will be very
runny. I usually put mine in the fridge for 5-6 hours or in the freezer for 1-2 hours if i'm in a hurry. Enjoy!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
                     </div>

                       <img alt="Gooey Butter Cake" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-94c7OveK7dA/UgvVjJoB-0I/AAAAAAAALFQ/2GBEU6qdjU0/s1600/Gooey-Butter-Cake-2.png" title="Gooey Butter Cake" width="640"/>
