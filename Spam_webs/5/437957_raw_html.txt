       <h1>
        NYCs Most Popular Design Benefit:
        <br/>
        Design On A Dime
        <br/>
        2015
       </h1>
       <span class="post-meta">
        <div class="meta-author">
         By
         <a href="http://www.sarahsarna.com/" rel="author" title="Posts by Sarah Sarna">
          Sarah Sarna
         </a>
         <a class="author-twitter" href="http://twitter.com/sarahsarna" title="Follow Sarah Sarna on Twitter">

       <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="NYCs Most Popular Design Benefit: Design On A Dime 2015" data-frizzlyposturl="http://www.sarahsarna.com/design-on-a-dime-2015/" type="hidden" value=""/>
       <div class="theiaPostSlider_header _header">
        <img alt="FLAIR Home" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-19653" height="878" src="http://www.sarahsarna.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/FLAIR_Home-620x878.jpg" width="620"/>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         THE WORLDS BEST
        </h4>
        <p class="dod_description_paragraph">
         Tonight was the New York City charity benefit that those in and outside of the design world look forward to all year.The fundraiser to benefit Housing Works,Design on a Dime, features over 60of the worldsbest interior designers, who create fabulousroom vignettes with donated new merchandise, which is then sold for 50 to 70 percent off retail pricing. Sounds amazing, right?
        </p>
        <h4 class="dod_description_paragraph" style="text-align: center;">
         OPENING NIGHT
        </h4>
        <p class="dod_description_paragraph">
         During tonights DOADOpening Night Reception, guests weregiven the first public look at the room vignettes and enjoyed exclusive preview shopping, cocktails, hors doeuvres, and live entertainment. I alsomade sureto take photos of my favorite spaces, which Ive included in the slideshow below.Most importantly, this evening will raise crucial funds to continue Housing Workshousing expansion efforts throughout New York City, providing hundreds of clients a solid foundation from which to build a successful future.
        </p>
        <p class="dod_description_paragraph">
         All Photography:
         <em>
          Sarah Sarna
         </em>
         . Above Interior Design:
         <em>
          Flair Home
         </em>
         .
        </p>
