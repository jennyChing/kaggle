             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               Yes, folks, celebrity gossip and sometimes hard-ish news site
               <em>
                TMZ
               </em>
               got its hands on photos of what looks like an Apple iPhone 6.
              </p>
              <p>
               On Friday, the site, best known for breaking celebrity news, waded deep into nerd culture and
               <a href="http://www.tmz.com/2014/08/15/apple-iphone-6-pics-photos-leak-leaked/" target="_blank">
                unveiled
               </a>
               a collection of images that may be of the rumored 5.5-inch (or 4.7-inch, it's hard to tell)
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/iphone-6/">
                iPhone 6
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/08/15/iphone-sapphire-screen-higher-price/">
                 Report: New iPhone to Have Sapphire Screen, Higher Price
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               The phone was allegedly smuggled out of a Foxconn factory in China, where Apple's iPhones are manufactured, and was photographed by a friend of an ex-factory employee.
              </p>
              <p>
               The images look a lot like others we've seen of the rumored phone. It's larger than an iPhone 5s and iPhone 5, which is pictured in some images to show size comparison. It has a body similar to an Apple fifth  generation iPod, and there's a new sensor on the front.
              </p>
              <p>
               The seven images include a shots of the device's back, front and side. It looks larger and even a bit thicker than Apple's current devices. We especially like the photos where the guy is holding the iPhone 5 and possible iPhone 6 in front of his face, which is blacked out to protect his identity.
              </p>
              <p>
               Apple, as always, has kept mum about any upcoming iPhone releases, though CEO Tim Cook promised a
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/07/23/apple-products-fall/">
                busy fall
               </a>
               , and most analysts believe the Cupertino, California, company will unveil as many as two new iPhones on
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/08/05/apple-sept-2014-event/">
                Sept. 9
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               All this, though, leaves us wondering: Will
               <em>
                TMZ
               </em>
               soon be reporting on the latest Samsung product rollout? Will it start reviewing devices? Should we expect a sit down with Tim Cook? Tell us,
               <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harvey_Levin" target="_blank">
                Harvey Levin
               </a>
               , we're all ears.
              </p>
              <p>

              </p>
