       <p>
        This 3-Piece Quilt Set, Full/Queen, Plumoffers touches of femininity and pops of color to any bedroom in your home. I personally can envision it with all white furniture and a cottage-type setting, but the way it is portrayed in the photo above is also quite lovely.
       </p>
       <p>
        Pretty plum colors are mixed with yellow and turquoise accent colors for a look that will wow! Little flowers and other designs fill this quilt with sunshine and laughter.
       </p>
       <p>
        Add some throw pillows in matching accent colors, and curtains in any of these colors, as well, to complete the color scheme theme.
       </p>
       <p>
        Other decorations to consider adding, along with this bedding set, are rugs, lamps, wall decor and more.
       </p>
       <p>
        Made of 100% cotton this bedding set includes the quilt and matching shams, and its also available in Peacock and Tangerine. All of the colors are delightful, and I would have a difficult time choosing between them. Simply click on the Buy Now button below for full details, other colors and pricing information.
