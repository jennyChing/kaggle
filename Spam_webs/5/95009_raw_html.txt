        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Currently Lovin': Lip Primer
        </h1>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
       <figure class="base-image">
        <img alt="currentlylovin-lipprimer-ft" class="attachment-big-size wp-post-image" height="700" itemprop="image" src="http://www.hellojaa.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/currentlylovin-lipprimer-ft-1050x700.jpg" width="1050"/>
       </figure>
       <p style="text-align:center; padding-top: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">
        <span style="font-size: 20px; color: #Fb4CA4;">
         
        </span>
        <span style="color: #7D7D7D; font-size: 15px; font-variant:small-caps;">
         Purchased by me, Affiliate links
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: 20px; color: #Fb4CA4;">
         
        </span>
       </p>
       <br/>
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8 column-1">
         <div class="advertisement">
          <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/ps9g" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Advertisement" height="87" src="http://www.hellojaa.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/ads-anniversarysale-2015.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <!-- .advertisment -->
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p class="icl_post_in_other_langs">
           view content in/ 
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/reviews-th/currently-lovin-lip-primer-2/?lang=th">
            Thai
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Unless Im testing the longevity, I usually layera base under my lipstick application either to cancel out my natural lip color or to prolong the wear time of the lipstick. Here are three lip primers Im lovinright now.
           <span id="more-18283">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="background-color: #e3feb1; padding: 10px; font-weight: bold;">
            MAC Lip Erase Lip Balm
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           I love this one for neutralizing my lip color so that it wont tweak the actual color of the lipstick Im wearing (I have super naturally pigmented lips!). It is flesh-toned and has a nice balmy texture that spreads easily on the lips. It sets to a tacky finish so your lipstick has something to grab on to for a longer-lasting wear. Read my full
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/reviews/mac-lip-erase-lip-balm/" target="_blank">
            review
           </a>
           here.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            BUY
           </strong>
            US$20 |
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/zwqf">
            MAC
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="background-color: #c7e1fc; padding: 10px; font-weight: bold;">
            MAC Prep + Prime Lip
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           If the lipstick Im wearing is opaque or if I dont worry about my lip color interference, then I will use this one. It has a clear balm texture that goes on transparent. You have to wait a minute or so to let it set then follow with the lipstick. It really does help extend the wear time of the lipsticksignificantly! Read my full
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/reviews/mac-prep-prime-lip/" target="_blank">
            review
           </a>
           here.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            BUY
           </strong>
           US$16 |
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/va6k" target="_blank">
            Nordstrom
           </a>
           
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/9gme" target="_blank">
            Macys
           </a>
           
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/3qii">
            Amazon
           </a>
           <img alt="" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=hellojaacom-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B002SKZS4S" width="1"/>
           
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/u3ka" target="_blank">
            MAC
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="background-color: #f5e281; padding: 10px; font-weight: bold;">
            Guerlain KissKiss Liplift
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           This one is like a mix between the other two. It is slightly fleshed-toned in the tubebut becomes mostlyclear once blended on the lips. It doesnt neutralize my lip color as well as the MAC Lip Erase Lip Balm but it does tone it down just slightly.It also leaves a tacky base for the lipstick to hold on to. What I love most about this one is the convenient and beautiful stick and the scent  oh the lovely, fruity scent! Read my mini
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/reviews/august-2013-favorites/" target="_blank">
            review
           </a>
           here.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            BUY
           </strong>
           US$37 |
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/9689" target="_blank">
            Nordstrom
           </a>
           
           <a href="http://www.hellojaa.com/nn1d">
            Sephora
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Do you have any favorite lip primer? What do you do to extend the wear time of your lipstick?
          </p>
