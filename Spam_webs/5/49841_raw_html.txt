       <p>
        Hi Dave. You don't know me but I want to buy your a beer.
       </p>
       <p>
        I know what you are thinking.
       </p>
       <p>
        "Who is this crazy person and why does she want to buy me a beer?"
       </p>
       <p>
        You see, we've met a ton of times but you wouldn'tremember me. Especially a 15 year old girl back in 1988.
       </p>
       <p>
        Now, don't get all worked up. Nothing like that happened.
       </p>
       <p>
        You see, I thought you were the coolest, most gorgeous, Chicago Cub I ever laid eyes on.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_3142" style="width: 226px">
        <a href="http://www.chicagonow.com/chi-town-mommy-mayhem/files/2015/01/IMG_1434.jpg">
         <img alt="Sigh!" class="size-medium wp-image-3142" height="300" src="http://www.chicagonow.com/chi-town-mommy-mayhem/files/2015/01/IMG_1434-216x300.jpg" width="216"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Sigh!
        </p>
       </div>
       <br/>
       I collected every baseball card you were on. I had you autograph everything I could think of every game I went to. I took a gazillion pics of you. I even started bringing you Junior Mints, which I heard were your favorite. (Obsessed much? Um, yes)
       <p>
        I cried the day you were traded.
       </p>
       <p>
        But I followed you to every team you went to. Not in the sense that I relocated. (I did move to Tampa but I was there first and
        <em>
         then
        </em>
        you came, I swear.)
       </p>
       <p>
        I crushed on you big time.
       </p>
       <p>
        In a way I still do. But it's not like that. (Well, maybe)
       </p>
       <p>
        You are my baseball idol.
       </p>
       <p>
        And you are coming back.
       </p>
       <p>
        Back home.
       </p>
       <p>
        And I can't wait!
       </p>
       <p>
        Welcome home Dave Martinez, I wanna buy you a beer!
       </p>
