          <header class="article-header">
           <h1 class="post-title">
            Jellyfish House by Wiel Arets Architects
           </h1>
           <p class="post-meta">
            Posted by
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/author/caroline/" rel="author" title="Posts by Caroline Williamson">
             Caroline Williamson
            </a>
            on 03.05.14 in
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/category/architecture/" rel="category tag">
             Architecture
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/category/main/" rel="category tag">
             Main
            </a>
           </p>
          </header>
          <div class="post-content">
           <div class="row featured-image">
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-1/" target="_self">
             <img alt="Jellyfish House by Wiel Arets Architects" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-1.jpg"/>
            </a>
            <a class="contrast-bar gallery-button" href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-1/" target="_self">
             <i class="icon ss-icon">
              forward
             </i>
             View The Photo Gallery
            </a>
           </div>
           <p>
            Situated right on the coast of Spain in Marbella, the
            <a href="http://www.wielaretsarchitects.com/en/projects/jellyfish_house/" target="_blank">
             Jellyfish House
            </a>
            , designed by
            <a href="http://www.wielaretsarchitects.com" target="_blank">
             Wiel Arets Architects (WAA)
            </a>
            , utilizes a cantilevered design so that when the homeowners are on the top floor the views of the ocean can always be seen.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-174893">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-2" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174893" height="900" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-2-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The cantilevered design holds something pretty unique  a pool on the roof with a glass bottom. The blueness of the water through the glass creates a beautiful ceiling to the outdoor space below.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-3/" rel="attachment wp-att-174894">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-3" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174894" height="399" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-3-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Spread out over four floors, the home has two staircases set up to circulate between them  a fast and a slow set of stairs. The fast stairs, which are enclosed in glass, take you from the exterior straight up to the roof, while the slow stairs are outfitted with long treads and short risers and span the length of the house, from ground floor to the roof. The slow stairs are open to the outdoor elements but are still on the interior.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-4/" rel="attachment wp-att-174895">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-4" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174895" height="399" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-4-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The house has five bedrooms, with two guest bedrooms on the bottom level that get their own private terrace.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-5/" rel="attachment wp-att-174896">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-5" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174896" height="899" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-5-600x899.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The structure of the home is made up of poured concrete thats supported by one column at the rear edge of the pool, and several smaller columns near the rear dining terrace. The rest of the walls are made up of windows allowing the suns light to filter in.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-6-dining-room/" rel="attachment wp-att-174897">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-6-dining-room" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174897" height="399" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-6-dining-room-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Accordion doors open up the interiors to the outdoor space, expanding the square footage.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-9/" rel="attachment wp-att-174901">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-9" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174901" height="900" src="http://0.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-9-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-7-pool/" rel="attachment wp-att-174898">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-7-pool" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174898" height="399" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-7-pool-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            With all staircases leading to the top, the oasis that houses the magnificent pool sits on the roof and cantilevers out 9 meters towards the southwest.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-8/" rel="attachment wp-att-174899">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-8" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174899" height="399" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-8-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The pool has an infinity edge making you feel like youre connected to the ocean out in the distance.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-8a/" rel="attachment wp-att-174900">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-8a" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174900" height="399" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-8a-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-10/" rel="attachment wp-att-174902">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-10" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174902" height="900" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-10-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Those people sitting below can be voyeurs watching the people swim up above.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-11/" rel="attachment wp-att-174903">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-11" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174903" height="900" src="http://0.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-11-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-12/" rel="attachment wp-att-174904">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-12" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174904" height="900" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-12-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-13/" rel="attachment wp-att-174905">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-13" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174905" height="900" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-13-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-14/" rel="attachment wp-att-174906">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-14" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174906" height="399" src="http://0.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-14-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            A window on the interior also lets those in the kitchen watch the swimmers.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/jellyfish-house-wiel-arets-architects/jellyfish-house-waa-15/" rel="attachment wp-att-174907">
             <img alt="Jellyfish-House-WAA-15" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-174907" height="399" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2014/02/Jellyfish-House-WAA-15-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Photos by
            <a href="http://www.janbitter.de" target="_blank">
             Jan Bitter
            </a>
            .
           </p>
