         <div class="date-outer">
          <h2 class="date-header">
           <span>
            Sunday, June 7, 2015
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="date-posts">
           <div class="post-outer">
            <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
             <meta content="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-BjRwoOoOxZE/VXN-Iojm5zI/AAAAAAAAJ3I/hLXijKdNsIg/s400/SK_title.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>
             <meta content="7522536908521187955" itemprop="blogId"/>
             <meta content="1334226427445385519" itemprop="postId"/>
             <a name="1334226427445385519">
             </a>
             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/2015/06/swtichin-kitten-1961-and-tom-jerrys.html">
               Swtichin' Kitten (1961) and Tom &amp; Jerry's kooky Gene Deitch era
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1334226427445385519" itemprop="description articleBody">
              Tom &amp; Jerry steers back into spooky territory (I previously posted on their
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/2008/10/flying-sorceress-1956.html">
               encounter with a witch
              </a>
              ) in
              <b>
               Switchin' Kitten
              </b>
              (1961).
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-BjRwoOoOxZE/VXN-Iojm5zI/AAAAAAAAJ3I/hLXijKdNsIg/s1600/SK_title.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-BjRwoOoOxZE/VXN-Iojm5zI/AAAAAAAAJ3I/hLXijKdNsIg/s400/SK_title.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              A stormy night finds Tom seeking shelter in this creepy old castle beautifully rendered in a multi-plane shot.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-w9JJf7sXkHs/VXN-bQMSjkI/AAAAAAAAJ3w/6BLlXV5CrpY/s1600/SK_castle1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-w9JJf7sXkHs/VXN-bQMSjkI/AAAAAAAAJ3w/6BLlXV5CrpY/s400/SK_castle1.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ZTn9OB1UsCI/VXN-lnpqraI/AAAAAAAAJ4Q/zCxUfZuxkmI/s1600/SK_Castle2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ZTn9OB1UsCI/VXN-lnpqraI/AAAAAAAAJ4Q/zCxUfZuxkmI/s400/SK_Castle2.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              In a blink-if-you-miss it gag, Tom is actually tossed from a passing carriage into a swamp in a rope-drawn sack. The drowning of unwanted pets in this manner was apparently a common enough occurrence to be referenced in children's entertainment (there is at least one other instance of the "drowned kittens" trope found in the Tom &amp; Jerry canon, 1949's
              <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heavenly_Puss">
               Heavenly Puss
              </a>
              ).
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-z8dCDd1J8-Y/VXN-LtuNdtI/AAAAAAAAJ3Q/gVQ8tyOx_Uw/s1600/SK-Carriage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-z8dCDd1J8-Y/VXN-LtuNdtI/AAAAAAAAJ3Q/gVQ8tyOx_Uw/s400/SK-Carriage.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Jerry, meanwhile, is living in the castle as assistant to a mad scientist performing experiments on animals. Here's his colorful array of beakers.
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-V4X4QV2dQIY/VXN-UjKCGSI/AAAAAAAAJ3g/qj84j7ASdHU/s1600/SK_labbeakers.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-V4X4QV2dQIY/VXN-UjKCGSI/AAAAAAAAJ3g/qj84j7ASdHU/s400/SK_labbeakers.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              And the mad scientist's dungeon of cats for use as subjects.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4duYpy436Ro/VXN-VjvnZDI/AAAAAAAAJ3o/mu-chzTo19M/s1600/SK_dungeonOfCats.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4duYpy436Ro/VXN-VjvnZDI/AAAAAAAAJ3o/mu-chzTo19M/s400/SK_dungeonOfCats.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              If the characterizations of Tom and Jerry seem just a little... "off" in this episode, its because this was the first time they were depicted by Czech-based animation team Rembrandt Films.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-IMvH9Dhjcj0/VXN-Sh0fF7I/AAAAAAAAJ3Y/qrYtcQfuhF4/s1600/SK_tomandJerryandskull.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-IMvH9Dhjcj0/VXN-Sh0fF7I/AAAAAAAAJ3Y/qrYtcQfuhF4/s400/SK_tomandJerryandskull.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              When
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/search/label/Hanna-Barbera">
               William Hanna and Jospeh Barbera
              </a>
              left MGM in 1957 to launch their own studio focusing on animation for television, a deal was hatched with
              <b>
               TerryToons
              </b>
              animator Gene Deitch to churn out shorts in half the time and a fraction of the budget. Deitch's Czechoslovakian animators (Deitch, himself an American, had moved to Prague in 1959) had little exposure to American animation in general, and had never seen an actual Tom &amp; Jerry short in motion, relying only on model sheets and stills for reference. The result was a bizarro-universe interpretation of Tom &amp; Jerry that sometimes reminds me of the homemade, off-model cartoon characters you might find painted on the wall of a children's day care  or on the side of a south-of-the-border supermercado.
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-w5uNKyGwHA4/VXojBiMt70I/AAAAAAAAJ8w/iv7cTxx3iy0/s1600/mexicantomandjerry.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

              <br/>
              The Gene Deitch era lasted only two years (1961-1962) before the property returned stateside under the stewardship of veteran animator Chuck Jones, so these shorts represent little more than a detour in Tom &amp; Jerry history. While reviled by many fans who felt these were merely a cheap knock-off of the real thing, I was always attracted to these cartoons because of their modern style and general strangeness, and would give them my full attention whenever they turned up on the local TV kiddie-show cartoon jukebox.

            <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
             <meta content="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_n33JN7aPzI/VQzpAbVItTI/AAAAAAAAJvs/rfUj0tDGqhk/s400/titlecard.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

             </a>
             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/2015/03/hard-times-at-disneyland.html">
               Hard Time at Disneyland
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5672482074933355434" itemprop="description articleBody">
              No, I'm not talking about the
              <a href="http://micechat.com/forums/disneyland-resort/152157-paul-pressler-era-thoughts-discussions.html">
               Pressler Era
              </a>
              (ta-dum-dum. heyyyy-oh!)
              <br/>
              <br/>
              I'm talking about
              <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hard_Time_on_Planet_Earth">
               Hard Time On Planet Earth
              </a>
              , a short-lived science fiction
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/search/label/TV">
               TV
              </a>
              show from
              <b>
               Touchstone Television
              </b>
              that lasted only 13 episodes.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_n33JN7aPzI/VQzpAbVItTI/AAAAAAAAJvs/rfUj0tDGqhk/s1600/titlecard.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_n33JN7aPzI/VQzpAbVItTI/AAAAAAAAJvs/rfUj0tDGqhk/s400/titlecard.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The year is
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/search/label/80s">
               1989
              </a>
              . An alien warrior from another galaxy is found guilty of fomenting rebellion and sentenced to serve time on a primitive planet called Earth. He'll trade his normally robot-like appearance for a human disguise that looks a lot like the square-jawed, muscular
              <a href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0184392/?ref_=fn_al_nm_1">
               Martin Kove
              </a>
              (
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/2011/08/1984-adbattlecar-warriors-of-lost.html">
               Death Race 2000
              </a>
              , The Karate Kid).
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Joining "Jesse" (that's the Earth name Kove's character adopts) throughout his sentence is "Control", a floating football-shaped robot whose voice resembles
              <b>
               Paul Reuben
              </b>
              's
              <b>
               Star Tours
              </b>
              navigator
              <b>
               RX-24
              </b>
              (the actual voice actor is
              <b>
               Danny Mann
              </b>
              , and he may very well be the first CGI character on television.)
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Z2XhAEfLg7c/VQzvpQsa7mI/AAAAAAAAJv8/9BD7duycfNw/s1600/opening-robotform.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Z2XhAEfLg7c/VQzvpQsa7mI/AAAAAAAAJv8/9BD7duycfNw/s400/opening-robotform.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Control acts as Jesse's guide and companion while Jesse serves out his term, learning the ways of Earth's strange inhabitants... and just maybe a lesson or two along the way? Control also comes with an annoying catch-phrase: "Negative outcome. Not good." which he'll deliver repeatedly throughout the show as a dead-pan, hilariously understated observation.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-2Kh7h2g5uR8/VQ0DjayzTuI/AAAAAAAAJ2g/LSebebOFFHQ/s1600/Tshirt.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-2Kh7h2g5uR8/VQ0DjayzTuI/AAAAAAAAJ2g/LSebebOFFHQ/s320/Tshirt.JPG"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <center>
               <i>
                We're going to need a lot of these, right? This catch-phrase is gonna be HUGE.
               </i>
              </center>
              <br/>
              In episode 3, "Losing Control", Jesse and Control visit an "important center of rejuvenation" that "reverses the human aging process"...
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/search/label/disneyland">
               Disneyland
              </a>
              . The whole episode amounts to a barely veiled advertisement for the park (exploiting television in this way was a Disney tradition going all the way back to 1954!)
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-2sg9yYRsixk/VQzyczOgQ9I/AAAAAAAAJwQ/RyKM3iK1Xzc/s1600/entrance-walkingup.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-2sg9yYRsixk/VQzyczOgQ9I/AAAAAAAAJwQ/RyKM3iK1Xzc/s400/entrance-walkingup.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Jesse and Control first enter Disneyland and encounter the clockwork and calliope installation that was part of a
              <a href="http://www.yesterland.com/35years.html">
               "35 Years of Magic"
              </a>
              celebration in anticipation of the park's 35th anniversary the following year.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-BLpYf-sUfWs/VQzycp8_21I/AAAAAAAAJwg/rhk468TnYH0/s1600/entrance-machine01.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-BLpYf-sUfWs/VQzycp8_21I/AAAAAAAAJwg/rhk468TnYH0/s400/entrance-machine01.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ISK5Vt7puLY/VQzycoD2MKI/AAAAAAAAJwI/m-8DW21Rydw/s1600/entrance-machine02.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ISK5Vt7puLY/VQzycoD2MKI/AAAAAAAAJwI/m-8DW21Rydw/s400/entrance-machine02.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-vXwgHI2Y9wA/VQzyclBV1hI/AAAAAAAAJwM/0hVa0sElk6M/s1600/entrance-overhead.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-vXwgHI2Y9wA/VQzyclBV1hI/AAAAAAAAJwM/0hVa0sElk6M/s400/entrance-overhead.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Over the opening credits, we are treated to flyover views of
              <b>
               Fantasyland
              </b>
              accompanied by synthesized, electronic renditions of "It's A Small World" and "When You Wish Upon a Star" that sound like cover versions you might expect to hear broadcast on the
              <b>
               Star Tours
              </b>
              radio station, K-DROID.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-jV6mDTe8IYY/VQz02sF7iEI/AAAAAAAAJww/SIYN3UrDPvM/s1600/01_monorail.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-jV6mDTe8IYY/VQz02sF7iEI/AAAAAAAAJww/SIYN3UrDPvM/s400/01_monorail.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-4bbNNAp8ymg/VQz02Yl0DsI/AAAAAAAAJws/_WvQ-epTIdU/s1600/02_skyway.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-4bbNNAp8ymg/VQz02Yl0DsI/AAAAAAAAJws/_WvQ-epTIdU/s400/02_skyway.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-TPk9i_Qw--w/VQz02ufE1LI/AAAAAAAAJxk/vfINV6hTqPU/s1600/03_autopia.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-TPk9i_Qw--w/VQz02ufE1LI/AAAAAAAAJxk/vfINV6hTqPU/s400/03_autopia.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lhPuH2eyOZ4/VQz02_S6pvI/AAAAAAAAJw0/Yd3r1zRAqC4/s1600/04_teacups.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lhPuH2eyOZ4/VQz02_S6pvI/AAAAAAAAJw0/Yd3r1zRAqC4/s400/04_teacups.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dNPdK7O5Jx8/VQz03RJZHAI/AAAAAAAAJw4/JrhOBgfW8yg/s1600/05_BTM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dNPdK7O5Jx8/VQz03RJZHAI/AAAAAAAAJw4/JrhOBgfW8yg/s400/05_BTM.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-aw8fluVKTBo/VQz03uooJ4I/AAAAAAAAJw8/Y1cQ1aQXpFs/s1600/06_Subs.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-aw8fluVKTBo/VQz03uooJ4I/AAAAAAAAJw8/Y1cQ1aQXpFs/s400/06_Subs.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6laWsyQgR0I/VQz039pb7II/AAAAAAAAJxA/Cp8Eptfwl3A/s1600/07_matterhorn.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6laWsyQgR0I/VQz039pb7II/AAAAAAAAJxA/Cp8Eptfwl3A/s400/07_matterhorn.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rzB3Vb2frZE/VQz04ME-qmI/AAAAAAAAJxE/iWVGTb-IkKY/s1600/08_dumbo.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rzB3Vb2frZE/VQz04ME-qmI/AAAAAAAAJxE/iWVGTb-IkKY/s400/08_dumbo.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The gravity-defying Control briefly ducks into a garbage can to avoid attracting too much attention...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-PtKaYQACtRk/VQz2fCeuKQI/AAAAAAAAJx0/6spjulElx54/s1600/10a_hidingInGarbage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-PtKaYQACtRk/VQz2fCeuKQI/AAAAAAAAJx0/6spjulElx54/s400/10a_hidingInGarbage.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-IEph2wv2gFM/VQz2fCj-5bI/AAAAAAAAJxw/xQSUNTRt24A/s1600/10_hidingInGarbage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-IEph2wv2gFM/VQz2fCj-5bI/AAAAAAAAJxw/xQSUNTRt24A/s400/10_hidingInGarbage.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...and in a moment that somehow got past Disney's lawyers, describes a guest's half-eaten hamburger as "minced bovine carcass and vegetation immersed in hydrogulated fat and heat", then quips "These humans will eat anything!" Now THAT'S corporate synergy!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-xOkxawVpctE/VQz2fJweCGI/AAAAAAAAJx4/EvB6Y9_rcmY/s1600/11-burgerInGarbage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-xOkxawVpctE/VQz2fJweCGI/AAAAAAAAJx4/EvB6Y9_rcmY/s400/11-burgerInGarbage.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              After noticing the
              <b>
               Mickey Mouse
              </b>
              balloons, Jesse suggests Control hover above him in a similar fashion as a disguise.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8YgzBzixa7U/VQz3KTZrwqI/AAAAAAAAJyM/QYzj6uxrDdg/s1600/12-balloons.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8YgzBzixa7U/VQz3KTZrwqI/AAAAAAAAJyM/QYzj6uxrDdg/s400/12-balloons.jpg"/>
               </a>

              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-LBPXC5WGMIQ/VQz3KRfpnOI/AAAAAAAAJyI/pDRHM-fItks/s1600/13-balloondiguise.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-LBPXC5WGMIQ/VQz3KRfpnOI/AAAAAAAAJyI/pDRHM-fItks/s400/13-balloondiguise.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              We ride the Skyway buckets, pass the original Star Tours, and the Rocket Jets...
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-A_ZC05Zwlxw/VQz3tpH0aCI/AAAAAAAAJyY/TYPQKOcKLqM/s1600/15_skyway.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-j6JB1gTu14A/VQz3tuaUezI/AAAAAAAAJy4/pWQ8bsE7IHs/s1600/16a_StarTours.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-j6JB1gTu14A/VQz3tuaUezI/AAAAAAAAJy4/pWQ8bsE7IHs/s400/16a_StarTours.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-8FGAUFkeAqo/VQz3uCM2JOI/AAAAAAAAJyg/Js6hrIeHJvo/s1600/18_rocketjets.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-8FGAUFkeAqo/VQz3uCM2JOI/AAAAAAAAJyg/Js6hrIeHJvo/s400/18_rocketjets.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rRZZ-tzHE1U/VQz3uZoiv-I/AAAAAAAAJyk/2ttSFxYYIbI/s1600/19_rocketjets.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rRZZ-tzHE1U/VQz3uZoiv-I/AAAAAAAAJyk/2ttSFxYYIbI/s400/19_rocketjets.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...then Control splits off to explore on his own, getting an up-close look at a hippo from the Jungle Cruise.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Zbq1zw26OAc/VQz4RcgRHiI/AAAAAAAAJzU/lo67mJS5vp8/s1600/20_Jungleboat.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Zbq1zw26OAc/VQz4RcgRHiI/AAAAAAAAJzU/lo67mJS5vp8/s400/20_Jungleboat.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-0csrFUSw8Rg/VQz4RXCtt7I/AAAAAAAAJzE/k54LLX4wxeA/s1600/21_Hippo.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-0csrFUSw8Rg/VQz4RXCtt7I/AAAAAAAAJzE/k54LLX4wxeA/s400/21_Hippo.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-cPGA1mhlf7I/VQz4RYdpLWI/AAAAAAAAJzI/AkCThSj8toI/s1600/22_Hippobit.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-cPGA1mhlf7I/VQz4RYdpLWI/AAAAAAAAJzI/AkCThSj8toI/s400/22_Hippobit.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Something resembling the early stages of a plot starts to develop, threatening to interrupt our Disneyland showcase, by way of a child ("Johnathan") telling his parents he wants to see "Captain EO and the Crystal Arcade". The Crystal Arcade? Mmm-kay. The
              <b>
               Peoplemover
              </b>
              is visible in the background.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-SwHauoh5euc/VQz4lcK2gCI/AAAAAAAAJzg/HJF01xlO4Pk/s1600/17_Peoplemover_family.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-SwHauoh5euc/VQz4lcK2gCI/AAAAAAAAJzg/HJF01xlO4Pk/s400/17_Peoplemover_family.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dLmcQ-mVtm0/VQz4lqz5MII/AAAAAAAAJzc/bKSUNIZDlrY/s1600/24_crystal%2Barcade.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dLmcQ-mVtm0/VQz4lqz5MII/AAAAAAAAJzc/bKSUNIZDlrY/s400/24_crystal%2Barcade.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The interior of the Crystal Arcade on Main Street strangely resembles the much larger Starcade in Tomorrowland. Johnathan plays the moving-cockpit version of Sega's
              <b>
               Afterburner
              </b>
              ...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-W5ciWXW2FEk/VQz6gKLkVZI/AAAAAAAAJzw/wn4DR8qmZqc/s1600/25_segaAfterburnercockpit.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-W5ciWXW2FEk/VQz6gKLkVZI/AAAAAAAAJzw/wn4DR8qmZqc/s400/25_segaAfterburnercockpit.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...while nearby we find
              <b>
               Blasteroids
              </b>
              ,
              <b>
               Pac-Man
              </b>
              , and a
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/search?q=star+wars">
               Star Wars
              </a>
              machine whose marque had to be partially obscured since its an intellectual property that Disney doesn't own the rights to.... for now.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-LJDRXZ-6VkM/VQz6gOXCGMI/AAAAAAAAJz0/sMs1o9FPb28/s1600/26_BlasteroidsStarWarsPacman.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-LJDRXZ-6VkM/VQz6gOXCGMI/AAAAAAAAJz0/sMs1o9FPb28/s400/26_BlasteroidsStarWarsPacman.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Control finds his way into the Crystal Arcade/Starcade and is somehow able to play the games wirelessly by projecting lasers. He takes on the game grid of
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/2011/04/tron-made-me-do-it.html">
               Tron
              </a>
              first...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-RA4Ty3C4huw/VQz6gDd7wlI/AAAAAAAAJ0I/9L-H7eOOWIo/s1600/27_Tron.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-RA4Ty3C4huw/VQz6gDd7wlI/AAAAAAAAJ0I/9L-H7eOOWIo/s400/27_Tron.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...before playing a row of machines all at once for a greater challenge.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EZZvbIZyylk/VQz6gfJwdnI/AAAAAAAAJz4/S1w3VYDenzA/s1600/29_RowOfarcademachines.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EZZvbIZyylk/VQz6gfJwdnI/AAAAAAAAJz4/S1w3VYDenzA/s400/29_RowOfarcademachines.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The exertion of
              <a href="http://the-haunted-closet.blogspot.com/search/label/videogames">
               videogaming
              </a>
              not only causes the entire arcade to blackout, but completely drains Control of his energy, who drops lifelessly to the floor, where Johnathan mistakes him for a free souvenir.  Jesse witnesses this from his Peoplemover cabin as it passes through the upper level of the arcade.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-DgECupYLIzM/VQz8g9OwmdI/AAAAAAAAJ0U/P5heYCxNX7c/s1600/30_arcadePeoplemover.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-DgECupYLIzM/VQz8g9OwmdI/AAAAAAAAJ0U/P5heYCxNX7c/s400/30_arcadePeoplemover.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Jesse needs to get Control back from that kid. Cue Chase Sequence #1! Jesse bolts out of the Peoplemover, featuring the World of Tron...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-yvdfxGs7U5A/VQz84_-JVRI/AAAAAAAAJ0c/UecoJZRnixM/s1600/32_peoplemoverTronSign.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-yvdfxGs7U5A/VQz84_-JVRI/AAAAAAAAJ0c/UecoJZRnixM/s400/32_peoplemoverTronSign.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...and follows Johnathan and family onto Big Thunder Mountain, bypassing the line by leaping the fence, scaling the rocks and dropping right into the moving train from above!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ncnU1wv68_M/VQz99xC_rGI/AAAAAAAAJ1A/ox45t0MSieg/s1600/33_BTM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ncnU1wv68_M/VQz99xC_rGI/AAAAAAAAJ1A/ox45t0MSieg/s400/33_BTM.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rr8FPtN-pYg/VQz99_ftCcI/AAAAAAAAJ0o/7YSCaojeEbU/s1600/35_BTMScalingMountain.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rr8FPtN-pYg/VQz99_ftCcI/AAAAAAAAJ0o/7YSCaojeEbU/s400/35_BTMScalingMountain.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-o1Ch40v_7lk/VQz991ZHU4I/AAAAAAAAJ0s/7LfvYyXQ_gs/s1600/37_BTMMidAir.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-o1Ch40v_7lk/VQz991ZHU4I/AAAAAAAAJ0s/7LfvYyXQ_gs/s400/37_BTMMidAir.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QO8IeoWMkj8/VQz9-NScupI/AAAAAAAAJ0w/TOmfHT12Phg/s1600/38_BTM_Landing.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QO8IeoWMkj8/VQz9-NScupI/AAAAAAAAJ0w/TOmfHT12Phg/s400/38_BTM_Landing.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The chase continues across the greens of the not quite open
              <b>
               Splash Mountain
              </b>
              (it would debut a few months later, in July '89) before Jesse is stopped by Disneyland security...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Nuly9WEm1Hc/VQz-0gS2qoI/AAAAAAAAJ1I/9stn2PkCgxU/s1600/39_Splash.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Nuly9WEm1Hc/VQz-0gS2qoI/AAAAAAAAJ1I/9stn2PkCgxU/s400/39_Splash.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-e__h2EDAYJo/VQz-0or0aJI/AAAAAAAAJ1M/9AxeRagE5pQ/s1600/40_SplashSign.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-e__h2EDAYJo/VQz-0or0aJI/AAAAAAAAJ1M/9AxeRagE5pQ/s400/40_SplashSign.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-1UPSd_0aFPY/VQz-0Td__6I/AAAAAAAAJ1Q/3_g1-ReYdiw/s1600/41_security.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-1UPSd_0aFPY/VQz-0Td__6I/AAAAAAAAJ1Q/3_g1-ReYdiw/s400/41_security.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...and invited to take a walk through
              <b>
               Disney's California Adventure
              </b>
              Version zero-point-oh (i.e. The Parking Lot.)
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-wqAYaGqiJZA/VQz_Vg5qDiI/AAAAAAAAJ1g/nsMU1kPOnF4/s1600/42_Parkinglot.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-wqAYaGqiJZA/VQz_Vg5qDiI/AAAAAAAAJ1g/nsMU1kPOnF4/s400/42_Parkinglot.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              So...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              ...about 20 minutes of plot occurs not relating to Disneyland, in which Chase Sequences #2-4 happen, before our hero is given an excuse to return for a night visit... because you haven't REALLY seen Disneyland until you've seen it at night!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-aaO6m_oCccw/VQ0Ax5Gc_2I/AAAAAAAAJ1s/HJC46KonEJE/s1600/50_spacemountain.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-aaO6m_oCccw/VQ0Ax5Gc_2I/AAAAAAAAJ1s/HJC46KonEJE/s400/50_spacemountain.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Plus, for Summer nighttime entertainment, you can't beat
              <b>
               The Main Street Electrical Parade
              </b>
              . Jesse who?
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-1Eg7vkAv-ys/VQ0Axz9DU3I/AAAAAAAAJ2E/LyQCZ7PPbrk/s1600/52_MSEP.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-1Eg7vkAv-ys/VQ0Axz9DU3I/AAAAAAAAJ2E/LyQCZ7PPbrk/s400/52_MSEP.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-klyDiYbpVN4/VQ0Ax1bwbvI/AAAAAAAAJ1w/5Grf8PYALps/s1600/53_MSEP.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-klyDiYbpVN4/VQ0Ax1bwbvI/AAAAAAAAJ1w/5Grf8PYALps/s400/53_MSEP.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8CpAiAPb9oM/VQ0AyPuPZDI/AAAAAAAAJ10/wVsYoefm8P0/s1600/53a_MSEP.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8CpAiAPb9oM/VQ0AyPuPZDI/AAAAAAAAJ10/wVsYoefm8P0/s400/53a_MSEP.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The plot conceit that brings Jesse back to the park is that he has recovered the still defective Control and believes he must return to the Crystal Arcade to rejuvenate him. Alas, the arcade is still closed from the earlier damage...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-zUYfhmtzCA4/VQ0BbXd0WEI/AAAAAAAAJ2M/4IZl7N8JJvw/s1600/51_CrystalArcadeclosed.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-zUYfhmtzCA4/VQ0BbXd0WEI/AAAAAAAAJ2M/4IZl7N8JJvw/s400/51_CrystalArcadeclosed.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              ...leaving Jesse with no choice but to recharge him with a broken electrical main cable he finds lying on the ground somewhere near Coke Corner. While reviving, Control begins to sing the lyrics to "It's a Small World" in a slow, mechanical drone that resembles
              <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2001:_A_Space_Odyssey_%28film%29">
               HAL 9000
              </a>
              's dying swan song. Nothing creepy about that.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-HV2k6zGARaI/VQ0B68HPVTI/AAAAAAAAJ2U/-WCPfIjEK6A/s1600/53_Electrifying.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-HV2k6zGARaI/VQ0B68HPVTI/AAAAAAAAJ2U/-WCPfIjEK6A/s400/53_Electrifying.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </div>
