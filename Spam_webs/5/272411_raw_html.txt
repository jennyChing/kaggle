                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Carl F. Bucherer Patravi ScubaTec Automatic Watch
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3027768012198052415" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-1MaAi0Ps_pg/VHtN18v5rDI/AAAAAAAAlgA/mZ6y9wJeni8/s1600/Bucherer_Patravi_ScubaTec_2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Carl F. Bucherer Patravi ScubaTec Automatic Watch" border="0" height="600" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-1MaAi0Ps_pg/VHtN18v5rDI/AAAAAAAAlgA/mZ6y9wJeni8/s1600/Bucherer_Patravi_ScubaTec_2.jpg" title="" width="600"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Carl F. Bucherer introduces the Patravi ScubaTec, an impressive dive watch that puts all its assets to work above and below water. Inside beats the automatic CFB 1950.1 calibre whose precision and reliability have been certified by the COSC. To ensure dive times are accurately measured for complete security, the bezel will only rotate counter-clockwise, thereby ensuring the diver's safety should the bezel be accidentally knocked. Hands and hour-markers are coated in Super-LumiNova which glows blue in the dark for underwater legibility. A helium valve releases pressure inside the case to protect against damage when resurfacing. Stylish and sporty, the Patravi ScubaTec is the ideal dive buddy.
                      <br/>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Movement
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      Mechanical automatic CFB 1950.1 calibre, COSC chronometer-certified, 38-hour power reserve
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Functions
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      Hours, minutes, seconds, date
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Case
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      Stainless steel, 44.6 mm Steel unidirectional rotating bezel with black and blue ceramic Automatic helium valve Sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating on both sides Water-resistant to 500 metres (50 bar / 1650 ft)
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Bracelet/strap
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      Stainless steel or rubber with extension, stainless steel folding clasp
