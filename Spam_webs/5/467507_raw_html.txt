        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Kids Vacation Memory Books
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-08-12T09:00:34+00:00">
          12 August, 2014
         </span>
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://whoneedsacape.com/author/lori/" rel="author">
            Lori
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">

           14 Comments
          </a>
         </span>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
          <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwhoneedsacape.com%2F2014%2F08%2Fkids-vacation-memory-books%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwhoneedsacape.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F08%2FElmersWetOnes11.jpg&amp;description=Kids%20Vacation%20Memory%20Books" rel="nobox">

         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5688571" data-link="http://whoneedsacape.com/2014/08/kids-vacation-memory-books/" data-summary="" data-title="Kids Vacation Memory Books">
         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i1.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes11.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Vacation Memory Books | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16094" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes11.jpg?resize=750%2C1010"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This shop is part of a social shopper marketing insight campaign with Pollinate Media Group Elmers and Wet Ones, but all my opinions are my own. #pmedia #CraftandCleanUp
          <a href="http://my-disclosur.es/OBsstV" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://my-disclosur.es/OBsstV', 'http://my-disclosur.es/OBsstV']);" target="_blank" title="PMedia Disclosure">
           http://my-disclosur.es/OBsstV
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Here we are at the tail end of summer break, the dog days of summer. Restlessness is settling in with my kids, probably yours too. All the fun plans for this summer have come and gone, and were left with that lag time before school starts back up. Our family recently returned from a long awaited vacation to Florida. Time spent visiting grandma, dipping our toes into the Gulf of Mexico and the Atlantic Ocean, gathering seashells and visiting the Florida Aquarium.All very memorable events to my girls ages 2 and 6.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes2.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Vacation Memory Books | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16093" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes2.jpg?resize=750%2C883"/>
          </a>
          But now were home, and were dealing with a couple of very rainy days. For the most part mom has used up her supply of rainy day activities. Luckily on a shopping trip to my local Target, I had some inspiration and was able to find some great items for an easy rainy day project that will help them remember their trip.  My arsenal consisted of inexpensive foam-cover notebooks, assorted stickers, glitter (loads &amp; loads of glitter), pictures from our trip, and a few seashells. Not to mention I purchased the ever wonderful Elmers glue and some Wet Ones. Be sure to check out the Back to School section at select Target stores to find an Elmers &amp; Wet Ones combo pack (while supplies last) which is a great bargain.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes1.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Vacation Memory Books | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16092" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes1.jpg?resize=1000%2C750"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So now the fun began! Basically I set each girl up and prepared myself for messiness to ensue. Glue, glitter and 2 little girlsIm sure you can see where this project is headed, a whole lot of mess! I wanted this to be an easy project, first step was to glue the pictures onto the pages of the book. Racquel who is 6 years old wrote a couple words on each page, and placed stickers on for decoration. We discussed the trip and the special moments we had while completing the memory book pages.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnesCollage1.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Vacation Memory Books | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16096" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnesCollage1.jpg?resize=1000%2C1000"/>
          </a>
          Meanwhile Leightyn who is 2 years old, mostly shook glitter everywhere, glued pages together with the Elmers glue and shook more glitter. You can only image how much we needed the Wet Ones, take a peek below at her pictures, she had a blast but was encrusted in glitter.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i2.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnesCollage2.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Vacation Memory Books | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16097" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnesCollage2.jpg?resize=1000%2C1000"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          When the pages inside the memory books were finished,we decorated the front cover. I simply helped Racquel write FLORIDA on the front of her book with the Elmers glue and she shook glitter all over while the glue was wet. Shethen added a couple decorative seashells from our trip. Leightyn has a smile on her cover with seashell eyes. And with loads of glitter covering both girls(and mom),we set to work with our Wet Ones, talk about easy cleanup, these were the perfect craft-time companion.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes14.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Vacation Memory Books | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16095" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ElmersWetOnes14.jpg?resize=750%2C775"/>
          </a>
          Here are their finished projects! And yes they may not be perfect, but we spent a rainy afternoon creating memory books that house our great vacation memories, which are priceless.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
