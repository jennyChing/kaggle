           <section data-src="hm">
            <h2>
             civilization
            </h2>
            <span class="snd" data-snd="C0377900">
            </span>
            <span class="pron" onclick="pron_key()">
             (sv-l-zshn)
            </span>
            <div class="pseg">
             <i>
              n.
             </i>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               1.
              </b>
              An advanced state of intellectual, cultural, and material development in human society, marked by progress in the arts and sciences, the extensive use of record-keeping, including writing, and the appearance of complex political and social institutions.
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               2.
              </b>
              The type of culture and society developed by a particular nation or region or in a particular epoch:
              <span class="illustration">
               Mayan civilization; the civilization of ancient Rome.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               3.
              </b>
              The act or process of civilizing or reaching a civilized state.
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               4.
              </b>
              Cultural or intellectual refinement; good taste.
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               5.
              </b>
              Modern society with its conveniences:
              <span class="illustration">
               returned to civilization after camping in the mountains.
              </span>
             </div>
            </div>
           </section>
           <section data-src="hc_dict">
            <h2>
             civilization
            </h2>
            (
            <span class="pron" onclick="pron_key(1)">
             svlazen
            </span>
            )
            <i>
             or
            </i>
            <b class="alias">
             civilisation
            </b>
            <div>
             <i>
              n
             </i>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               1.
              </b>
              (Sociology) a human society that has highly developed material and spiritual resources and a complex cultural, political, and legal organization; an advanced state in social development
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               2.
              </b>
              the peoples or nations collectively who have achieved such a state
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               3.
              </b>
              the total culture and way of life of a particular people, nation, region, or period:
              <span class="illustration">
               classical civilization
              </span>
              .
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               4.
              </b>
              the process of bringing or achieving civilization
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               5.
              </b>
              intellectual, cultural, and moral refinement
             </div>
             <div class="ds-list">
              <b>
               6.
              </b>
              cities or populated areas, as contrasted with sparsely inhabited areas, deserts, etc
             </div>
            </div>
           </section>
           <section data-src="rHouse">
            <h2>
             civilization
            </h2>
            <span class="pron" onclick="pron_key(1)">
             (sv  lze n)
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <i>
             n.
            </i>
            <div class="ds-list">
             <b>
              1.
             </b>
             an advanced state of human society, in which a high level of culture, science, and government has been reached.
