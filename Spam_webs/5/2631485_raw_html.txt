        <h1 class="post_title">
         <a href="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/green-tea-st-patricks-day-cake/" title="Green Tea St. Patricks Day Cake">
          Green Tea St. Patricks Day Cake
         </a>
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="post_info post_info_2">
        <span class="post_author">
         Posted by:
         <a class="post_author">

       <div class="post_content">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         St. Patricks day is just around the corner and I am going to celebrate it because I feel emotionally connected with Ireland. I lived there for quite a few years and that awesome island will always be dear to me.
        </p>
        <p>
         What better way to celebrate than making something delicious?
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipeis a green tea-flavoured cake so you wont like it if you despise green tea. I really wanted to make something green without using green food dye because that just seemed too easy. I experimented a bit with spirulina and I considered spinach but green tea turned out to be the answer this time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im using a green tea powder called matcha, which is a fine-ground type of green tea that is used in the Japanese tea ceremony. Its used in many other things though, like green tea ice-cream, cakes and other treats, so using it for cake is not, in my opinion, blasphemy. You can get this tea in good tea shops.
        </p>
        <p>
         It should not be on display as its supposed to be kept in a dark container but ask and hopefully theyll have it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5452.jpg">
          <img alt="green tea cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-485" height="437" src="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5452.jpg" width="604"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ingredients:
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 cup rapeseed oil
        </p>
        <p>
         2 eggs
        </p>
        <p>
         2 leveled Tbsp matcha green tea powder
        </p>
        <p>
         1 1/2 cup applesauce
        </p>
        <p>
         3/4 cup sugar
        </p>
        <p>
         2 cups all purpose flour
        </p>
        <p>
         2 tsp baking powder
        </p>
        <p>
         1 tsp salt
        </p>
        <p>
         icing sugar for decoration
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="green tea cake" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-478" height="241" src="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5440-300x241.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Preheat the oven to 180C/350F.
        </p>
        <p>
         In a bowl whisk the oil and eggs together with applesauce, and while still whisking add matcha green tea powder and sugar.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5442.jpg">
          <img alt="green tea cake" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-479" height="225" src="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5442-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In a separate bowl mix flour, salt and baking powder. Add these to the egg mixture and stir well.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pour into a greased round pan and bake for 40 minutes or until passes stick test.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="green tea cake" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-480" height="253" src="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5443-300x253.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once the cake cools, sprinkle with icing sugar.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5447.jpg">
          <img alt="green tea cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-481" height="477" src="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5447.jpg" width="604"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tattoomybroccoli.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/aSAM_5451.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>

         <strong>
          <span style="color: #339966;">
           Happy St. Patricks Day to Everybody!
