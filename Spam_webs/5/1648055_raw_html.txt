          <h1>
           10 Greatest Family Guy Look-alikes
          </h1>
          <div class="post_meta">
           <span class="author_avatar">
            <a href="http://thefw.com/author/ddeane/" title="Donald Deane">
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_author">
            <span>
             By
            </span>
            <a href="http://thefw.com/author/ddeane/" title="Donald Deane">
             Donald Deane
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_date">
            December 6, 2013 1:10 PM
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="social-group-top large">
           <div class="social_icons size_large">
            <a class="facebook share_link" href="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fthefw.com%2Ffamily-guy-lookalikes%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dfbshare_top&amp;t=10+Greatest+%26%238216%3BFamily+Guy%26%238217%3B+Look-alikes" target="_blank" title="Share on Facebook">
             <div class="social_icon_large">
             </div>
             <span class="social_icon">
              Share on Facebook
             </span>
            </a>
            <a class="twitter share_link" href="http://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Check+out+10+Greatest+%E2%80%98Family+Guy%E2%80%99+Look-alikes+http%3A%2F%2Fthefw.com%2Ffamily-guy-lookalikes%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dtwitter_top+%40thefw" target="_blank" title="Share on Twitter">

            <span class="social-submenu">

          <div class="the_content clearfix">
           <p>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="354" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/wY1lj1B2rxs?feature=oembed" width="630">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            Whats it like to go through life looking like a well-known cartoon character? Luckily, most of us will never know. But these unfortunate individuals all have the dubious distinction of looking like characters from 
            <a href="http://screencrush.com/tags/family-guy/">
             Family Guy
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <span id="more-94009">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            In fact, the internet abounds with doppelgangers of nearly the entire cast. Peter, Lois, Meg, Chris, even Quagmire, are all there. Yes, theres even a Stewie clone. We pity the guy who has that football-shaped head. Check out 10 Family Guy lookalikes below.
           </p>
           <h4>
            Peter Griffin
           </h4>
           <p>
            Not only does this man closely resemble Peter Griffin, but, if this photo is to be believed, he even shares the characters name. Maybe its just us, but we could easily envision this multiple-chinned guy spontaneously singing
            <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2WNrx2jq184" target="_blank">
             Surfin Bird
            </a>
             just like Peter.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94063">
            <img alt="Peter Griffin in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94063 " height="420" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/peter-griffin-real-life2.jpg" title="Peter Griffin in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             FOX/Troodi.com
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Lois Griffin
           </h4>
           <p>
            In general, Lois Griffin is the voice of reason to Peters constant hi-jinks. She has, however, had brushes with bulimia, gambling addiction, kleptomania and even an extramarital affair with President Bill Clinton. Does this woman look like Mrs. Griffin in real life? Shes pretty darn close, we think.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94064">
            <img alt="Lois Griffin in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94064" height="627" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/louis-griffin-family-guy-real-life2.jpg" title="Lois Griffin in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             CollegeHumor
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Chris Griffin
           </h4>
           <p>
            Of all the comparisons out there between Chris Griffin and real people, this one comes closest. With his shaggy hair and round face, this rotund fellow mirrors Chris in many ways. And, as an added bonus, this guy could also pass for Chris Farley or comedy writer Bruce Vilanch in a dimly lit room. Actually, were not entirely sure this isnt Bruce Vilanch.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94065">
            <img alt="Chris Griffin in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94065" height="540" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/chris.jpg" title="Chris Griffin in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Techeblog
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Meg Griffin
           </h4>
           <p>
            Poor Meg Griffin! As Family Guy has evolved over the years, nearly all the characters, with the notable exception of Quagmire, has shown an intense dislike for the teenage girl for no apparent reason. This womans resemblance to Meg is simply uncanny, from her pink beanie all the way down to her white sneakers. Though it does beg the question, Who would cosplay as Meg Griffin?
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94068">
            <img alt="Meg Griffin in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94068" height="929" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/meg-griffin-family-guy-real-life1.jpg" title="Meg Griffin in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             I-Am-Bored.com
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Stewie Griffin
           </h4>
           <p>
            Were sportscaster John Clayton and Stewie Griffin separated at birth? Well, they do have the same giant head. From what weve seen of Claytons work on ESPN, he doesnt seem to have any of Stewies megalomania or desire to take over the world  at least we hope he doesnt.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94089">
            <img alt="Stewie Griffin in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94089" height="389" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/john-clayton1.jpg" title="Stewie Griffin in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Totally Looks Like
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Brian Griffin
           </h4>
           <p>
            Although he likes his drink a bit too much, anthropomorphic family pet Brian is one of the most likeable characters in the Griffin clan. This adorable real-life pooch makes a passable Brian, but we doubt hell be authoring a failed novel called Faster Than the Speed of Love anytime soon.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94072">
            <img alt="Brian Griffin in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94072" height="601" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/brian-300x2861.jpg" title="Brian Griffin in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Perez Solomon
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Quagmire
           </h4>
           <p>
            If theres one Family Guy character you dont want to resemble, its probably Glenn Quagmire. Still, the misogynistic Quagmire has one thing going for him  plenty of luck with the ladies. So, what puts the giggity in his real-life counterpart? Maybe its his pronounced jawline or awesome flame-print shirt. Or maybe its the fact that hes clearly kidnapped that poor woman in the photo. Oh, Quagmire.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94069">
            <img alt="Quagmire in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94069" height="420" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/real-life-quagmire-family-guy2.jpg" title="Quagmire in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             FOX/Fork Party
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Herbert
           </h4>
           <p>
            With his unrequited lust for Chris, neighbor Herbert is hands down the creepiest character on Family Guy. In the episode The Courtship of Stewies Father, Herbert dons a dress and a wig and sings Somewhere Thats Green from Little Shop of Horrors while imagining a perfect life with Chris. The elderly woman here seems to capture that vibe perfectly. Maybe
            <em>
             too
            </em>
            perfectly.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94075">
            <img alt="Herbert in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94075" height="420" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/senior-citizen-totally-looks-like-herbert-the-pervert.jpg" title="Herbert in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Totally Looks Like
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Bruce
           </h4>
           <p>
            Despite a recurring role in which hes appeared as a deacon, a therapist, a medium, a lawyer, a masseur and a barman, Bruce from Family Guy isnt the best-known character. But, then again, neither is actor Jon Gries, who played Uncle Rico in Napoleon Dynamite. These two could be long-lost relatives, yes?
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94078">
            <img alt="Bruce in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94078" height="382" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/dude-300x182.jpg" title="Bruce in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Perez Solomon
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            Til Death
           </h4>
           <p>
            Some keen-eyed internet observer noticed the eerie similarities between Peters brain-damaged horse Til Death, who was named after the critically-panned Fox sitcom, and bizarre 4Chan meme Staredog. Family Guy fans will recall that the walleyed horse ultimately died of a heart attack. Staredog doesnt seem to be doing much better.
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_94094">
            <img alt="'Til Death in Real Life" class="size-full wp-image-94094" height="388" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/02/Til-Death-in-Real-Life.jpg" title="'Til Death in Real Life" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Totally Looks Like
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <h4>
            <strong>
             BONUS:
            </strong>
           </h4>
           <h4>
            Cleveland
           </h4>
           <p>
            Okay, so maybe Cleveland has graduated to his own show. But many fans have noticed a resemblance between the affable Cleveland and actor Leslie David Baker, aka the far-less-affable Stanley from The Office. (Must be the mustache.) Maybe theyre long-lost brothers. How about a Cleveland Show/'Office crossover to clear up this mystery?
           </p>
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_106511">
            <img alt="Cleveland family guy stanley the office" class="size-full wp-image-106511" height="407" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/thefw.com/files/2012/03/Cleveland-family-guy-stanley-the-office.jpg" title="Cleveland family guy stanley the office" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Fox/Getty Images
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
