             Get Ordained Free as a Dudeist Minister
            </a>
           </h1>
          </div>
          <div class="art-PostContent">
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             <strong>
              <span style="font-family: georgia; color: #6f8d52;">
               <em>
                Become a Dudeist Priest by Ordaining Fast, Free and Easy Online!
               </em>
              </span>
             </strong>
            </span>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             <strong>
              <span style="font-family: georgia; color: #6f8d52;">
               <em>
                <br/>
               </em>
              </span>
             </strong>
            </span>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: left;">
            <div class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 340px">

             </p>

            <p>
             <span class="style1">
              (Click
              <a href="http://dudeism.com/ordination-form/">
               <strong>
                here
               </strong>
              </a>
              to ordain now.)
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             Show the world that youve got what it takes to
             <em>
              take it easy
             </em>
             .As an ordained Dudeist Priest, you can minister over religious ceremonies in most U.S. States (laws vary, so check with your local County Clerk first), and assorted other countries. Preside over a wedding, funeral, or any kind of celebration with pride and authority. Or just kick back and enjoy the knowledge that youre an ordained minister at one of the most easygoing religions in the world. There are currently about300,000 Dudeist Priests world-wide. Help spread the Dude word! Ordaining is totally free and there are no obligations.
            </p>

               Proceed to Ordination Form

             </strong>

              Online Certificate
