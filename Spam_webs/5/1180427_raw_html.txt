              <p>
               At the Mobile World Congress 2015, Barcelona, both HTC and Samsung launched their new flagship smartphones. HTC has launched One M9 and Samsung has launched two smartphones Samsung Galaxy S6 and Galaxy S6 Edge. Both these smartphones fall in the same price range and with same range specifications there going to be a neck to neck fight between these two in the market. Here we are comparing both these smartphones.
              </p>
              <h3>
               Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge vs HTC One M9
              </h3>
              <h4>
               Build and Design
              </h4>
              <p>
               <a href="http://cdn.thephonebulletin.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/HTC-One-M91.jpg">
                <img alt="HTC One M9" class="aligncenter wp-image-18977 size-full" height="427" src="http://cdn.thephonebulletin.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/HTC-One-M91.jpg" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge is beautifully crafted from metal and glass. It is very light phone weighing just 132 grams and it is just 7mm thick, which is less thin than the main version. It is slightly less appropriate to hold, as its curved edges taper into the chassis and it is very smoother to use. Samsung didnt compromised with the specifications to get thin and flat back. It cleverly fit a massive 5.1 inch screen into the frame of iPhone 6 size. Galaxy S6 Edge is the worlds first phone to have the curved edge screen.
              </p>
              <p>
               HTC One M9 is comparatively very thicker than Galaxy S6 Edge with 9.6 mm thickness and weighing 157 grams.HTC One M9 has curved metal unibody and very thin bezels, it comes with 68.4% screen-to-body ratio. Samsung has changed its regular design with this new model, whereas HTC continued its old design for One M9 which is similar to its predecessors.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Display
              </h4>
              <p>
               <a href="http://cdn.thephonebulletin.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Samsung-Galaxy-S6-edge.jpg">
                <img alt="Samsung-Galaxy-S6-edge" class="aligncenter wp-image-19022 size-full" height="572" src="http://cdn.thephonebulletin.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Samsung-Galaxy-S6-edge.jpg" width="805"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Galaxy S6 Edge is little larger than HTC One M9, as the former one sports 5.1 inch display with 1440 x 2560 pixels resolution and the latter one sports 5 inch display with 1080 x 1920 pixels resolution. S6 Edges display type is Super AMOLED capacitive touchscreen protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 4 and features TouchWiz UI. One M9s display type is Super LCD3 capacitive touchscreen protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 4 and feature HTC Sense UI 7.0. Galaxy S6 Edge is the first smart phone to feature curved edge screen.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Processor and Storage
              </h4>
              <p>
               Galaxy S6 Edge is powered by 1.5GHz Quad-Core Exynos 7420 processor along with Mali-T760 Graphics processor,whereas HTC One M9 is packed with faster and powerful 64-bit Octa-coreQualcomm MSM8994 Snapdragon 810 processor along with Adreno 430 Graphics processor.The main advantage of the One M9 smart phone is that it has Qualcomm latest Snapdragon 810 chip which is available in few smart phone yet.
              </p>
              <p>
               Both these devices packs 3 GB RAM, but S6 Edge comes in three storage versions 32/64/128 GB and One M9 cones in 32 GB version, but can be expandable up to 128 GB using microSD card.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Camera and Connectivity
              </h4>
              <p>
               In camera aspect HTC camera are known for better quality than Samsung and here on the paper too HTC One M9 camera is more powerful than Galaxy S6 Edge camera. One M9 sports massive 20.7 Megapixel rear camera which no where matches Galaxy S6 Edge 16 Megapixel and it has 4 Megapixel front camera whereas S6 Edge has 5 Megapixel front camera.
              </p>
              <p>
               Coming to the connectivity options both devices features Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, Hotspot, Bluetooth Low Energy 4.1 version and many similar features. Both devices support Nano SIM only. Galaxy S6 Edge features PayPal certified fingerprint sensor and visa and mastercard certified Samsung Pay service which is introduced as competitor to Apple Pay.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Battery and Price
              </h4>
              <p>
               HTC One M9 has more capacity battery than Galaxy S6 Edge, as the former has 2840 mAh Non-removable battery whereas the latter has 2600 mAh Li-Ion non-removable battery. As the devices are yet to launch there is no confirmation yet regarding the price.
              </p>

              <h4>
               Conclusion
              </h4>
              <p>
               Both these devices fall in same price range and has almost similar specifications. In many aspects HTC One M9 took edge over Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge like camera, battery and others. But with its sleek and stylish design and also with the curved and QHD display S6 Edge is the strong competitor to HTC One M9.
              </p>
