     <h2 class="title">
      Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!
     </h2>
     <div class="post-info-top">
      <span class="post-info-date">
       Posted by
       <a href="https://plus.google.com/107978697276289899160?rel=author" rel="author" title="View all posts by Dan Kogan">
        Dan Kogan
       </a>
       on				May 9, 2015
      </span>
      <span class="addcomment">
       <a href="#respond" rel="nofollow" title="Leave a comment ?">
        Leave a comment
       </a>
       (0)
      </span>
      <span class="gotocomments">

     <div class="entry">
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Whether you are school going kid or an office going professional, mothers day holds a special part in everyones life. It is the day to make your mother special, saying thanks in a silent way for all that she has been doing throughout her life. Well, there is a lot you can do for her in NYC. Check out some amazing ideas here.
       <a href="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mothers-day.jpg" rel="lightbox[579]" title="Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!">
        <img alt="mothers-day" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-580" height="1131" src="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mothers-day.jpg" width="1697"/>
       </a>
       Being a mother is a joyous moment for every woman and from here starts her new life. Right from raising her child to educating him, a mother showers her unconditional love on her child. And, the best part is that she doesnt expect anything in return. Undoubtedly, there is nothing comparable to her in this world. Why not say a thank you to make her smile, or gift her something special or anything else, whatever you like to. Well, if you wish to celebrate the bond of a mother and a child on this Mothers Day, the best thing you can do for her is to spend some quality time with her.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <strong>
        While you are in NYC and planning for special day, here is a list of things you can do:
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <b>
        Healthy Brunch.
       </b>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mothers-day-brunch-menu2-610x300.png" rel="lightbox[579]" title="Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!">
        <img alt="mothers-day-brunch-menu2-610x300" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-582" height="300" src="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mothers-day-brunch-menu2-610x300.png" width="610"/>
       </a>
       Mothers are always fitness freak. Thus, a cool idea is to start the day with fluffy omelets or scrumptious pancakes and you can add otherdelicious brunch items. You can make her day memorable, taking her to Picholine or you can go to Cibo e Vino. If your mom loves Italian cuisine, you can switch to Morandi and it fits in your budget easily. Another option you have, Tartine, where you can get her some of the best desserts too.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <b>
        Make her laugh.
       </b>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       She made you smile throughout her life and now it is your turn. The best way out is to watch the hilarious musical starring Harriet Harris, David Burtka, Tyne Daly, and Lisa Howard. She will surely enjoy this zany show, directed by David Hyde Pierce, depicting the story of a boisterous wedding. It is a good way to spend some quality time, surrounded by some amusing characters at the Brooks Atkinson Theatre.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <b>
        Some clicks at the Central Park
       </b>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CentralPark3.jpg" rel="lightbox[579]" title="Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!">
        <img alt="CentralPark3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-584" height="982" src="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CentralPark3.jpg" width="1920"/>
       </a>
       After the amusing show; its time to walk down the central park. You can take her to The Mall. Dont forget to get some quality pictures with your mom. The beauty of wide-open spaces and the energetic atmosphere will help you have some great selfies.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <b>
        A picnic lunch.
       </b>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Picnic.jpg" rel="lightbox[579]" title="Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!">
        <img alt="Picnic" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-585" height="667" src="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Picnic.jpg" width="1000"/>
       </a>
       Now you must be hungry and your mom too. So, drive in to Washington Square Park. With artists, musicians, and passersby all around, in the Villages park, you can enjoy the sun and culture. Find a good place and grab some lip-smacking food in the Greenwich Village caf or Citarella.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <b>
        The special evening.
       </b>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Rancho-Valencia-spa-exterior-evening.jpg" rel="lightbox[579]" title="Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!">
        <img alt="Rancho-Valencia-spa-exterior-evening" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-586" height="366" src="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Rancho-Valencia-spa-exterior-evening.jpg" width="650"/>
       </a>
       What else can be better than the sunset view along the Hudson River, isnt it? You can take your mother to the lawn in Riverside Park and enjoy a glass of Sauvignon Blanc, while the sun goes down. She will surely love this and do not miss the chance to capture the cutest smile in your camera!
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <b>
        End the Day with Dinner at Nougatine.
       </b>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6505.jpg" rel="lightbox[579]" title="Mothers Day: A Day with the Special Women in NYC!">
        <img alt="IMG_6505" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-587" height="1199" src="http://www.nyhandymannyc.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6505.jpg" width="1600"/>
       </a>
       Here comes the day to its end. All is well, that ends well, so make sure to give the day a perfect ending. What about a dinner at the famous Jean Georges Nougatine. Bars are often the best place to end your days with appetizing dinner. Your mother will love the super comfortable chairs, atmosphere, stellar service, and, of course, the high-class cuisine.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Mothers Day is a special day and it is your responsibility to make your mom feel special. Dont miss the chance to bring a smile on her face!
      </p>
