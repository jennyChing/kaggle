        <a href="http://www.thevspotblog.com/2012/09/make-houndstooth-pumpkin-from-duck-tape.html">
         Make a Houndstooth Pumpkin from Duck Tape
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta">
        <div class="byline">
         <span>
          by Vivienne Wagner
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="date">
         September 11, 2012
        </div>
        <div class="comments">
         <a href="#wp-post-comments">
          Comments
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-content">
        <p>
         I love pumpkins. I love Duck Tape. I really, really love houndstooth so its only natural that I would make a Houndstooth Duck Tape Pumpkin.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ZJaacFQCAvo/UE42HVyd1DI/AAAAAAAAJok/hwrPs8_LLkk/s1600/Houndstooth+pumpkin+using+Duck+Tape.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ZJaacFQCAvo/UE42HVyd1DI/AAAAAAAAJok/hwrPs8_LLkk/s1600/Houndstooth+pumpkin+using+Duck+Tape.jpg', '']);">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="464" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ZJaacFQCAvo/UE42HVyd1DI/AAAAAAAAJok/hwrPs8_LLkk/s640/Houndstooth+pumpkin+using+Duck+Tape.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thevspotblog.com%2F2012%2F09%2Fmake-houndstooth-pumpkin-from-duck-tape.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thevspotblog.com%2F2012%2F09%2Fmake-houndstooth-pumpkin-from-duck-tape.html&amp;description=A%20houndstooth%20pumpkin%2C%20made%20from%20Duck%20Tape%20and%20a%20Dollar%20Store%20pumpkin." onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thevspotblog.com%2F2012%2F09%2Fmake-houndstooth-pumpkin-from-duck-tape.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thevspotblog.com%2F2012%2F09%2Fmake-houndstooth-pumpkin-from-duck-tape.html&amp;description=A%20houndstooth%20pumpkin%2C%20made%20from%20Duck%20Tape%20and%20a%20Dollar%20Store%20pumpkin.', '']);">
          <img alt="" border="0" src="http://assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         This is so, so, SO easy to do.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fdBwFrzkFEw/UE42PaU0SSI/AAAAAAAAJos/2SP7iqKMaxA/s1600/make+a+houndstooth+pumpkin.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fdBwFrzkFEw/UE42PaU0SSI/AAAAAAAAJos/2SP7iqKMaxA/s1600/make+a+houndstooth+pumpkin.jpg', '']);">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="210" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fdBwFrzkFEw/UE42PaU0SSI/AAAAAAAAJos/2SP7iqKMaxA/s640/make+a+houndstooth+pumpkin.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Grab yourself some fabulous Duck Tape and a Dollar Store pumpkin. Split the tape down the middle so that you have thinner strips of tape. Even though there will still be a little bubble or two, they will lay flatter this way. Start covering the pumpkin from top to bottom, trying to match up the pattern as you go. It doesnt have to be perfect youll get the houndstooth effect no matter what.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-pUgU2i7EVsM/UE42WcKDPCI/AAAAAAAAJo0/tm6hjuGnEGw/s1600/houndstooth+pumpkin.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-pUgU2i7EVsM/UE42WcKDPCI/AAAAAAAAJo0/tm6hjuGnEGw/s1600/houndstooth+pumpkin.jpg', '']);">
