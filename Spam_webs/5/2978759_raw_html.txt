        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <em style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          Desert Breath
         </em>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          is an art project created by D.A.ST. Arteam, the team of artists, which was foundedin 1995 just for purpose of creating this project. Team consists of installation artist Danae Stratou, industrial designer and architect Alexandra Stratou andarchitect Stella Constatinides.
         </span>
         <em>
          <span id="more-7201">
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Located in the eastern Sahara desert,
         <em>
          Desert
         </em>
         <em>
          Breath
         </em>
         covers the area of 100,000 square meters andexpands to the gigantic ornament made of 8,000 square meters of sand and 30-meter diameter large pool in the centre of it. Artists explain, The project is rooted in our common desire to work in the desert. In our minds eye the desert was a place where one experiences infinity. We were addressing the desert as a state of mind, a landscape of the mind. The point of departure was the conical form, the natural formation of the sand as a material. 
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <em>
          Desert Breath
         </em>
         took 2 years to complete and was finished in March 1997. Even after more than 17 years, this art installationremains visible. You canview
         <em>
          Desert Breath
         </em>
         on Google Earth by entering coordinates:272254.59N, 333748.46E (or just by clicking this
         <a href="https://maps.google.lt/maps?q=27%C2%B022'54.59%22N,+33%C2%B037'48.46%22E&amp;hl=lt&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;ll=27.380018,33.6343&amp;spn=0.011813,0.022702&amp;sll=55.1736,23.883296&amp;sspn=3.890899,11.623535&amp;t=h&amp;z=16" target="_blank">
          link
         </a>
         ).
        </p>
