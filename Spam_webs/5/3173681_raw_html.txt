        <h1 class="entry-title ">
         Alleged 5.2-inch HTC Hima panel leaked
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="posted-on">
          <i class="fa fa-calendar">
          </i>
          <a href="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/phones/htc/alleged-5-2-inch-htc-hima-panel-leaked/" rel="bookmark">

            December 9, 2014
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="byline">
          <i class="fa fa-user">

          <span class="author vcard">

           </a>
          </span>
         </span>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Twitter account @upleaks leaked that HTC would be changing the way its names it next flagships. What was once believed to be the HTC One (M9) is now thought to be called the HTC Hima. Now the devices front panel has been leaked.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel.jpg">
          <img alt="Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10518" height="904" src="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel.jpg" width="678"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel (1)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10519" height="904" src="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel-1.jpg" width="678"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel (2)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10520" height="671" src="http://news.phonesltd.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/Alleged-HTC-One-M9-front-panel-2.jpg" width="678"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The leak comes to us courtesy of French-speaking
         <a href="http://www.nowhereelse.fr/htc-one-2015-photos-facade-103258/">
          NWE
         </a>
         . According to its source, the HTC Hima will sport a 5.2-inch panel. Previous reports claimed that it would sport a 5.5-inch screen. One of the photos above show the 5-inch panel of the HTC One (M8) compared to the alleged 5.2-inch Hima panel.
        </p>
        <p>
         Taking a closer look at the panel, it seems the Himas screen will have pretty slim bezels. According to previous reports, this panel will cover a Quad HD (1440 x 2560 pixels) screen. Additionally, the smartphone is said to comes with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 805 quad-core processor under the hood. In the camera department, HTC is said to be ditching the UltraPixel camera for a high-resolution rear-facing camera.
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course, since we cant confirm if this front panel belongs to the HTC Hima, take this with a pinch of salt.
        </p>

         for further updates.
