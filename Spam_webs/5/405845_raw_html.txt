          <div class="prep-instructions nested-two-thirds">
           <h3>
            Preparation
           </h3>
           <span itemprop="instructions">
            <p>
             Preheat oven to 350F.
            </p>
            <p>
             Cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
            </p>
            <p>
             Add the eggs and vanilla and mix until combined.
            </p>
            <p>
             In a separate bowl mix the flour, salt, and baking powder.
            </p>
            <p>
             Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until well combined.
            </p>
            <p>
             Add the neon pink food dye until desired color.
            </p>
            <p>
             Add the chocolate chips.
            </p>
            <p>
             Bake at 350F for 15-25 minutes. Time will vary with each oven. You dont want them to be brown when they are done. You want them to be barely cooked through in the middle. This will keep them chewy and soft.
            </p>
           </span>
