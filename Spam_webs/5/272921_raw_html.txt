            <div class="page_paragraph">
             This test will measure your abilities in a number of areas under timed conditions, without placing significant emphasis on cultural knowledge.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Please do not use any reference materials (e.g. books, calculators, online or computer resources) or the help of anyone else in working these problems. You may use scrap paper, and pen or pencil. IT IS RECOMMENDED THAT YOU HAVE AT LEAST 2 SHEETS OF PAPER AND 2 PENS OR PENCILS, SINCE SEVERAL OF THE PROBLEMS WILL REQUIRE CALCULATIONS.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Be sure to answer every problem, even if this means guessing on some problems. There are no penalties for guessing.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The test consists of 36 multiple choice problems, and you have only 12 minutes to complete the test, so an average of 20 seconds per problem. It is important to move quickly through the test, and not spend too much time on any given problem. If the time runs out before you hit the "SCORE TEST" button, there is no penalty, and the answers you have checked at that time will be counted as your final answers. Also, you do not receive any extra points or advantages for finishing the test in less than the provided 12 minutes.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Each problem consists of a mathematical, logical, verbal, and/or spatial reasoning puzzle, and you must choose the correct answer from the choices provided.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Your estimated IQ score, general population percentile ranking, and the answers to the problems are all provided when you complete the test.
