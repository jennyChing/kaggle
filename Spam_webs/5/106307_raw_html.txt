         <img alt="2015 Alfa Romeo 4C vs. 2014 Porsche Cayman" class="aligncenter wp-image-152" height="399" src="http://res.cloudinary.com/enginepush/image/upload/v1419427590/2015_Alfa_Romeo_4C_vs_2014_Porsche_Cayman_qmniqn.jpg" title="2015 Alfa Romeo 4C vs. 2014 Porsche Cayman" width="650"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        The Romeo equiped with mid-mounted 1.7-liter turbocharged I-4 which can produce 237 hp and 258 lb-ft of torque. While The Porsche Cayman mounted 2.7-liter horizontally opposed six-cylinder engine that produces 275 hp and 213 lb-feet of torque.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Both come with six-speed transmission. The 2015 Alfa Romeo 4C vs. 2014 Porsche Cayman halted to the ground. Both car can reach 60 mph in less than 5 seconds.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        But for a long-trip duel, 2015 Alfa Romeo 4C vs. 2014 Porsche Cayman are almost at the same level. Porsche Caymen actually ahead for a bit. Its 18-inch wheels with high-performance tires, and combining with steadfastly steering accelaration make your long-trip faster and slick. I wouldnt say The Alfa Romeo couldnt do the same.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        You may be impressed by its performance in highway but, sometime its pretty hard to clutch its steering rack because its steerings sensitivity.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        The duel narrowed to the efficiency of fuel. The 2015 Alfa Romeo 4C vs. 2014 Porsche Cayman is slightly different. The Alfa Romeos fuel tank capacity is 10.5 gal; Range in miles up to 252.0/357.0 mi.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        While The Porsche has a bigger fuel tank size:11.6 gl; range in miles up to 345. It is now quite clear that The Porsche Caymen would be more pleasant for a long-trip riding than his opponent. Nevertheless, the winner of 2015 Alfa Romeo 4C vs. 2014 Porsche Cayman is still in your hand.
       </p>
