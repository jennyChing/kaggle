          <p>
           Here comes the Canadas 4th gold medal in Sochi Olympics 2014. This is the latest news update that Canadas Dara Howell has won the gold medal in womens slopestyle skiing with scoring 94.20. Meanwhile, Amercas Devin Logan won silver medal with the score of 85.40 in
           <a href="http://www.roomeetimes.com/tag/sochi-2014-olympics" target="_blank">
            Sochi 2014 Olympics
           </a>
           at Krasnaya Polyana on Tuesday February 11, 2014.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.roomeetimes.com/wp-content/uploads/Canadas-Dara-Howell-wins-Olympic-gold-in-womens-slopestyle-skiing.jpg">

           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Kim Lamarre of Canada was on third with bronze medal and she scored 85.00. Australias Anna Segal was on 4th position with score of 77.00 while Emma Dahlstrom of Switzerland was on fifth with 75.40 scores.
          </p>
          <p>
           During the final, Yuki Tsubota has suffered a heavy fall during the womens ski slopestyle. Doctors had to use a hand barrow to remove the injured athlete from the course. Tsubota was going very well in the first final run and her score was 71.06 but suddenly shesuffered a heavy fall which toooff the course on a stretcher.
          </p>
          <p>
           She finished sixth with a 71.60 first run. At the Winter Olympics 2013, Canada has now won nice medals overall.
