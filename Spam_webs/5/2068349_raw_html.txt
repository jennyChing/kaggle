         <div class="body">
          Automobile tuner Mansory has something special in store for Aston Martin. For the DB9 and DBS models, Mansory is now offering the Cyrus package.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The first time you ever see the Cyrusyou just are baffled, and completely floored at the fact that the whole car is constructed of carbon fiber. The Cyrus emphasizes the masculinity and power that the Astons possesses.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The Cyrus has newly designed front skirts and enlarged air inlets, as well as carbon fiber wing extensions and a newly designed rear skirt with an integrated diffuser. On the hood, there are two slotted power domes made from carbon fiber that send extra air into the 12 cylinder engine. The tailpipes are stainless steel and are attached to a sonorous sport exhaust system.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The Mansory Aston Martin Cyrus is stocked with a 6.0 liter, V12 naturally aspirated engine that pushes out 510 horsepower.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The car sits on 9 x 20 front wheels and 10.5 x 21 rear ultra-forged rims wrapped in 255/30ZR20 front and 295/25ZR21 rear Dunlop Sport Maxx tires. The suspension as also been adjusted so the DB9 or DBS has a lower center of gravity for improved handling while motoring.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Interior-wise, you will find carbon fiber on the dashboard, center console and steering wheel. The hand-stitched seats are absolutely gorgeous as they glow in red and black two-tonethe dashboard and inside door panels are leather wrapped as well. The Foot pedals are made of aluminum, while the door sills and head rests feature custom CYRUS logos.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Cyrus is one of the most aggressive and exclusive Aston Martin models you will ever see.
