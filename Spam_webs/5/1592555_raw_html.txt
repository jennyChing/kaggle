       <article class="post-567 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail category-howtos tag-apple tag-gadgets tag-iphone-tips tag-smartphones description-off" id="post-567">
        <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          There are three factors that make using a screen protector for your smartphone, tablet or gadget a worthwhile venture. The first is the extra protection it offers, preventing scratches and even smashes. The second is the ability to prevent glare and add extra screen privacy. The third is to improve the resale value of your devices.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          <b>
           Prevent
          </b>
          <strong>
           Scratches and Smashes with a Screen Protector
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          The first point is the most obvious of them all. A screen protector will provide better protection for your smartphone, tablet or gadget screen. A good screen protector has the ability to shield your actual glass screen from scratches. There are even screen protectors on the market that are able to prevent smashes from drops and impacts.
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/7358041/type/dlg/http://www.thinkgeek.com/product/1b90/" target="_blank">
           ThinkGeek
          </a>
          has a great screen protector that can do exactly that, minimising not only scratches but also shielding your device from smashes caused by an impact to the screen. The
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/7358041/type/dlg/http://www.thinkgeek.com/product/1b90/" target="_blank">
           RhinoShield Impact Resistant Screen Protector
          </a>
          has a 6 layer polymer film able to provide resistance to scratches and impacts, making it far less likely for your screen to smash.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_44089" style="width: 310px">
           <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/7358041/type/dlg/http://www.thinkgeek.com/product/1b90/">
            <img alt="RhinoShield Screen Protector" class="wp-image-44089 size-full" height="300" src="http://gadgetised1.wpengine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/1b90_rhinoshield_screen_protector.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            RhinoShield: Available for HTC One, iPhone 5/5C/5S, Nexus 5, Samsung Galaxy S4, or Galaxy S5
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          <strong>
           Prevent Glare and Add Privacy with a Screen Protector
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          Some screen protectors actually dont work that well in the sun, providing more reflections than the screen normally would, therefore making it difficult to see whats on your screen in broad daylight. If you want to reduce glare, there are screen protectors that have been specifically designed to do exactly that. A standard screen protector probably wont do this, so be sure to look for one that prevents glare.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          You can also add extra screen privacy using a screen protector. If you dont like people looking over your shoulder to see whats on your screen, these added privacy screen protectors can prevent exactly that. On angles other than the front facing angle you use to view your device, nothing is visible. This is particularly useful if you are always inputting pin codes and passwords that you dont want others to see.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          <strong>
           Improve Resale Value with A Screen Protector and a Case
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          If you are a fan of shiny new gadgets like myself, than you probably like to keep them in good condition. Even if you dont care so much for keeping your device in good condition, think of the resell value!
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
          In order to allow myself to upgrade to the latest products, I always make sure I keep my devices in pristine condition with the use of screen protectors and simple cases. It makes a huge difference. While some little scratches here and there are unavoidable from general use, the overall condition of all of my devices is just better since I started using protective cases and screen protectors.
         </div>
         <p>
          The trick is to sell your product, like an iPhone 4 for example, and get as much as you can for that device. The better condition you keep your device in, the more money you will get for it. That way, you can save money when upgrading to your new phone!
         </p>
         <p>
          Screen protectors and cases are cheap these days, and almost all will do the trick. While some are better than others, I have never had any issues with 5  10 dollar screen protectors off Ebay, and cases alike. There are so many to choose from that aesthetics is no longer an issue.
         </p>
         <center>
          <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

          <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-5205017202663124" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="1566105197" style="display:block">

         </center>
         <p>
          Screen protectors are barelynoticeable(I often forget mine is on and panic if I see a scratch, only to realise it hasnt even gone close to reaching the glass of the actual screen), and cases can even be customised. A simple investment like this could save you a heap of cash when upgrading your devices.
         </p>
