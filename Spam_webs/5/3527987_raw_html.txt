     <h1>
       Winner of the Danish App Awards 2012 
     </h1>
     <p>
      Join thousands of users in a fun and fast-paced multiplayer game where the objective is to connect as many consecutive blocks of the same color as you can in 2 minutes.
     </p>
     <p>
      Block Struggle is a multiplayer puzzle game available on iOS and Android. You can challenge friends and random opponents and play in up to 20 games simultaneously, or go through a challenging single player campaign.
     </p>
     <p>
      The playing field consists of 55 blocks og 5 different colors, each block having a certain value. Swipe your finger across the screen to select consecutive blocks of the same color and remove them to add their value to your score. Special blocks are included that double, triple, or even quadruple the value of a given selection of blocks, so use them wisely! When playing against friends, the game ensures that you and your opponent are given the same blocks.
     </p>
     <p>
      Every struggle is played in three rounds, and the one with the highest total score in the end wins. Every round can be played when it suits you.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Standard Features:
      </strong>
      <br/>
      - Simple and very addictive game play
      <br/>
      - Retina graphics
      <br/>
      - Global leaderboards
      <br/>
      - Chose to play against friends or be matched up against random opponents
      <br/>
      - Play in up to 5 games at a time
      <br/>
      - Offline-mode for practise
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Extra features in paid version:
      </strong>
      <br/>
      - Play up to 20 games at a time
      <br/>
      - Unique avatars for your player
      <br/>
      - No ads
      <br/>
      - No waiting times
