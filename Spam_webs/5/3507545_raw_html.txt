      <p>
       Great night out for lovers of wine and communication! Taste sorts of wine and intoduce yourself to other people. Valentine's Day is a great cause for it. Forget about Anti-Valentine's Day parties! This is an interesting way to spend a Saturday night in a classy casual atmosphere. Wines will be free and you can to drink as much as you want to! There will be delicious hot and cold snacks also, so you will not be hungry! Only fun, only happiness! And special activities will help to introduce you to others in the group.
      </p>

       <span class="address">
