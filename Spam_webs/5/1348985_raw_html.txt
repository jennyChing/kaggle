           Mardi Gras Colors New Orleans
          </h1>

           <p>
            Happy Fat Tuesday! As the Mardi Gras festivities begin lets raise a glass to one of the most colorful cities I have ever experienced- New Orleans.
            <a href="http://magedesign.com/travel-inspiration/">
             <img alt="New Orleans Napoleon House" class="aligncenter wp-image-21448 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.sensationalcolor.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/new-orleans-napoleon-house.jpg" width="400"/>
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.sensationalcolor.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/02/mardi-gras-colors.jpg">
             <img alt="Mardi Gras Colors" class="aligncenter wp-image-21460 size-full" height="58" src="http://www.sensationalcolor.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/02/mardi-gras-colors-e1422977141413.jpg" width="400"/>
            </a>
            On this night three colors reign supreme throughout The Big Easy  each one symbolizing the meaning of the holiday: purple  justice, green  faith and gold power. Im stuck in cold NY, but if I were able to transportmyself to New Orleans for Mardi Gras you would find me at the Napoleon House Cafe partaking in some king crabs, king cake and drinking a king-size glass of my favorite cocktail- a Pimms Cup.
            <span id="more-21447">
            </span>
           </p>
           <h4 style="text-align: center;">
            Pimms Cup Recipe from the Napoleon House Bar &amp; Cafe
           </h4>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Fill a tall 12 oz. glass with ice
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Add 1 1/4 oz. Pimms #1
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Add3 oz. lemonade
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Top off with 7up
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Garnish with a spear of cucumber
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            .sit back and enjoy!
           </p>
           <p>
            And while I have this fantasy going I might as well put myself in a special little only at Mardi Gras ensemble because word on the street is if you not in costume, then you better be in the colors of the day.
            <a href="http://www.net-a-porter.com/product/40561" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Mardi Gras Fashion" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21456" height="600" src="http://www.sensationalcolor.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Mardi-Gras-Fashion.jpg" width="504"/>
            </a>
            Notte by Marchesa Ruffle Dress, Lanvin Mettallic Pointed Pumps, Jimmy Choo Tube Satin Clutch, Fendi gold FF cuff all from
            <a href="http://www.net-a-porter.com/product/40561" target="_blank">
             net-a-porter.com
            </a>
            Okay this outfit is perhaps about as practical for catchings beads and doubloons on the crowded streets of the French Quarter as it is on my bank account but alas, tonight is for fun- let the fasting begin in the morning! Do you do anything special for Mardi Gras?
