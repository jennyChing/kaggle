          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This cake was a huge hit at the luncheon we had for a visiting missionary. Super moist and the frosting? Oh My Gosh! YES!
          <strong>
          </strong>
          Creamy, fluffy and decadent. And if that wasnt enough I drizzle a chocolate ganache over the top and added dark chocolate sprinkles to the sides. I found a box of dark chocolate sprinkles at Cost Plus World Market. I love that store!
         </p>

          <a href="http://www.ladybehindthecurtain.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Mocha-Devils-Food-Cake-Lady-Behind-The-Curtain-3.jpg">

            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0">
              For the Ganache:
             </div>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/3 cup semisweet chocolate pieces
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 tablespoon butter
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 teaspoon light corn syrup
             </li>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5">
              For the Cake:
             </div>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 teaspoons instant espresso coffee powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 teaspoon salt
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 cup shortening
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 large eggs
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 teaspoon vanilla extract
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 1/3 cups cold water
             </li>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16">
              For the Frosting:
             </div>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 cup butter, softened
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 teaspoons vanilla extract
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-20" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 teaspoon instant espresso coffee powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-21" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-22" itemprop="ingredients">
              6 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
             </li>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-23">
              Optional: 1 to 2 tablespoons milk
             </div>
             <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-24">
              Optional:  dark chocolate sprinkles
             </div>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0">
              For the Ganache:
             </div>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Add the chocolate, whipping cream, butter and corn syrup to a small microwave safe bowl.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Heat 1 minute and stir until melted and creamy.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Set aside to cool to room temperature.
             </li>
             <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4">
              For the Cake:
             </div>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spray
              <span class="bold">
               TWO
              </span>
              9-inch cake pans with non stick cooking spray, line the bottoms with parchment paper; spray the top of the paper with non stick cooking spray and set aside.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              In a medium bowl stir together the flour, cocoa, espresso powder, baking soda and salt; set aside.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              In a large mixing bowl beat shortening until fluffy (about 30 seconds).
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Gradually add the sugar, scrap the sides of the bowl, add eggs one at a time making sure they are incorporated; add the vanilla.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Alternate between the cold water and the flour mixture; mix until just combined.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-11" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spoon batter into the prepared pans ( a little over 2 cups each), spreading evenly.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-12" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a wooden toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-13" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Cool cake in pans
              <span class="bold">
               15 minutes
              </span>
              .
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-14" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Remove layers from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
             </li>
             <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-15">
              For the Frosting:
             </div>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-16" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              In a large mixing bowl beat cream cheese, shortening and vanilla until light and fluffy.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-17" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Add the espresso powder and cocoa powder; mix until combined.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-18" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Gradually add 1 cup of sugar at a time and beat until smooth.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-19" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              If necessary, beat in enough milk to reach spreading consistency.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-20" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Fill and frost cake layers with frosting.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-21" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spoon or pour ganache into a small zip top bag; pipe on top of the cake in a crisscross pattern.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-22" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Press dark chocolate sprinkles on the side of the cake.
             </li>
            </ol>
