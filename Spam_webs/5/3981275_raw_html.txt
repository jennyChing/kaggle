        Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches

        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Happy August, Shabby Creek friends! Its me, Claire, from
         <a href="http://alittleclaireification.com/" target="_blank">
          A Little CLAIREification
         </a>
         , back with another slow cooker favorite. This one is so easy you wont believe it which is perfect as Back To School time draws near.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.theshabbycreekcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Crockpot-Italian-Sausages-and-Pepper-Sandwiches-PM.jpg">
          <img alt="Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches - perfect for busy days!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6278" height="741" src="http://www.theshabbycreekcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Crockpot-Italian-Sausages-and-Pepper-Sandwiches-PM.jpg" width="576"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         If there is one thing my Husband loves, it is Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches. He works hard and is always cooking up delicious food that I loveso its nice to surprise him with one of his favorites every now and then.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I can toss all of the ingredients into the slow cooker andtell him not to peek or surprise him when he gets home from work.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches #recipe #slowcooker" class="aligncenter wp-image-13124 size-full" height="518" src="http://alittleclaireification.com/wp-content/uploads/2035/06/Crockpot-Italian-Sausages-and-Pepper-Sandwiches-PM2.jpg" title="Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches #recipe #slowcooker" width="576"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Since the Hubs is 100% Italian American (yep, and he still married an Irish lass), its no wonder he has such an affection for dishes that have so much robust flavor like this one. The sausages &amp; peppers are also excellent served over a little pasta too if youd prefer.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches #recipe #slowcooker" class="aligncenter wp-image-13123 size-full" height="404" src="http://alittleclaireification.com/wp-content/uploads/2035/06/Crockpot-Italian-Sausages-and-Pepper-Sandwiches-PM3.jpg" title="Crockpot Italian Sausage and Pepper Sandwiches #recipe #slowcooker" width="576"/>
         Now, I am a cheese FREAK so I like to add cheese on the bread, then the sausage and sauce and top with more cheese. You can see how deliciously melty and gooey it is. I also add more banana peppers to mine butof course that is optional.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         So heres to another easy crock pot meal that I hope you will all enjoy and I will see you again in September! Enjoy the rest of your Summer in the meanwhile!
        </p>

        <p style="text-align: left;">
         CROCKPOTITALIAN SAUSAGE &amp; PEPPERS SANDWICHES
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Ingredients
         </strong>
        </p>
        <address style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          1 pound Italian sausage links (5 sausages)
         </span>
         <br style="color: #404a62;"/>
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          2 tbsp minced garlic
         </span>
         <br style="color: #404a62;"/>
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          1 (16-ounce) bag frozen pepper stir-fry (with sliced green/red/yellow peppers and onions)
         </span>
         <br style="color: #404a62;"/>
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          1/2 cup mild banana pepper rings (drained)
         </span>
         <br style="color: #404a62;"/>
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          1 (26-ounce) jar tomato and basil pasta sauce
         </span>
         <br style="color: #404a62;"/>
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste with basil, garlic and oregano
         </span>
        </address>
        <address style="text-align: left;">
         1 tbsp italian seasoning (basil/oregano/thyme)
         <br style="color: #404a62;"/>
         <span style="color: #404a62;">
          pepper, to taste
         </span>
        </address>
        <address style="text-align: left;">
         Provolone cheese slices or shredded mozzarella for topping (optional)
        </address>
        <address style="text-align: left;">
         5 Hoagie or sandwich rolls of your choice
        </address>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Instructions
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #404a62;">
           Place sausages in slow cooker.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          Stir in the garlic, frozen pepper mix, banana peppers, tomato sauce, tomato paste, italian seasoning &amp; pepper
         </li>
         <li>
          Cover, set heat to LOW and simmer 8-10 hours (or 4-5 hours on HIGH)
         </li>
         <li>
          20 minutes before serving,wrap your hoagie rolls in aluminum foil and throw in a 350* oven for 10 minutes to warm (alternately, wrap two at a time in a paper towel and microwave for 20-30 seconds).
         </li>
         <li>
          If desired, place a slice of provolone cheese into each hoagie roll.
         </li>
         <li>
          Spoon a sausage and the red sauce pepper mixture into the hoagie rolls.
         </li>
         <li>
          Top with another slice of provolone cheese or shredded mozzarella if desired (I like cheese).
         </li>
        </ol>
