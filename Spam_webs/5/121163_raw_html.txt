               Bruce Lee has always in my opinion been a very focused individual. He was definitely a master of his craft. The key to Bruce Lee was that he always focused on being himself. Success will come when we apply the same knowledge of finding out who we really are in life. Just be yourself.
              </div>
             </li>
            </ol>

I hope sharing these two quotes today will inspire you to success in what ever your goals and aspirations my be in life. Try not to become a man or woman of success, but rather try to become a man or woman of value and success will follow.
