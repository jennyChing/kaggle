In the past five years, the CPSC (Consumer Product Safety Commission) has recalled more than 110 million household goods. Every year deaths, injuries and property damage from recalled products cost the US more than $700 billion annually. 1 child dies every two weeks from a falling TV, furniture or appliance. Learn about the hidden dangers caused by product recalls in our infographic and why its important to never ignore them.
