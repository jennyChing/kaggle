        <h2>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Intro">
          Intro
         </span>
        </h2>
        <div class="center">
         <div class="thumb tnone">
          <div class="thumbinner" style="width:382px;">
           <a class="image" href="/Images:Heart_shaped_Ear_Muffs_-_Weaved.jpg">
            <img alt="Heart shaped Ear Muffs - Weaved.jpg" class="thumbimage" height="388" src="/w/images/thumb/6/63/Heart_shaped_Ear_Muffs_-_Weaved.jpg/380px-Heart_shaped_Ear_Muffs_-_Weaved.jpg" width="380"/>
           </a>
           <div class="thumbcaption">
            <div class="magnify">
             <a class="internal" href="/Images:Heart_shaped_Ear_Muffs_-_Weaved.jpg" title="Enlarge">
              <img alt="" height="11" src="/w/skins/common/images/magnify-clip.png" width="15"/>
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         <i>
          Earmuffs, also known as earmuffles, are objects designed to cover a person's ears for protection. They consist of a thermoplastic or metal head-band, that fits over the top of the head, and a pad at each end, to cover the external ears.
         </i>
        </p>
