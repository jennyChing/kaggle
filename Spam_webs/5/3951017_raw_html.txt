       <h2>

       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28720" style="width: 426px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/mapchair.png">

       </p>

       <h2>
        Grab a Brush and some Paint
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28733" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/super-cool-painted-chairs.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: thisisknockout.wordpress.com" class="size-full wp-image-28733" height="502" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/super-cool-painted-chairs.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Thisisknockout.wordpress.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Wield a can of spray paint or use extra paint leftover from a craft project and go to town on your favorite seat. We found a great
        <a href="http://thisisknockout.wordpress.com/2012/09/14/how-to-revamp-an-old-wooden-chair/" target="_blank">
         DIY on priming and painting
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28721" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/paint-chair.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: sweetpaulmag.com" class="size-large wp-image-28721" height="833" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/paint-chair-640x833.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Sweetpaulmag.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Instead of committing to the whole chair, mark off the legs with painters tape and just spray paint the bottom.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28731" style="width: 432px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/school-chair.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: thesalvagecollection.blogspot.com" class="size-full wp-image-28731" height="640" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/school-chair.jpg" width="422"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Thesalvagecollection.blogspot.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        If youre looking for something more eye-catching, then paint an old school chair with a
        <a href="http://thesalvagecollection.blogspot.com/2012/01/creative-process-reveal.html" target="_blank">
         sleek line design
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28732" style="width: 510px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/diy_ombrechair_2.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: designsponge.com" class="size-full wp-image-28732" height="770" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/diy_ombrechair_2.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Designsponge.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        You could also fade one hue into another with an
        <a href="http://www.designsponge.com/2012/04/diy-project-shades-of-blue-ombre-chair.html" target="_blank">
         ombre chair DIY
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <h2>
        Bring on the Hardware
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28735" style="width: 370px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/studs.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: Marthastewart.com" class="size-full wp-image-28735" height="450" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/studs.jpg" width="360"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Marthastewart.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Use studs to jazz up a
        <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/274864/secrets-to-shopping-flea-markets-and-yar/@center/276964/60-days-summer#slide_8" target="_blank">
         new seat
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div>
        <a href="http://www.houzz.com/photos/119514/William-Hefner-Architecture-Interiors---Landscape-traditional-dining-room-los-angeles">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="792" src="http://st.houzz.com/simgs/aba11a9f0d4c87c5_8-3570/traditional-dining-room.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div style="color: #444;">
       </div>
       <p>
        Add door knockers or handles to the back of chairs for a handy, cool statement.
        <a href="http://www.houzz.com/ideabooks/555503" target="_blank">
         Houzz
        </a>
        list some budget-friendly options.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Dress Your Seat in Fabric
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28722" style="width: 370px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/sew-chair.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: sewcountrychick.com" class="size-full wp-image-28722" height="493" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/sew-chair.jpg" width="360"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Sewcountrychick.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Piece together scraps of fabric and sew a design onto the back of a cushioned chair. For a sewing-free option, use strong textile glue to wrap a chair in your favorite patterns.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28725" style="width: 590px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/sharpie-chair.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: littlegreennotebook.blogspot.com" class="size-full wp-image-28725" height="800" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/sharpie-chair-580x800.jpg" width="580"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Littlegreennotebook.blogspot.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Cover an ugly chair in a white slipcover then use fabric markers to
        <a href="http://littlegreennotebook.blogspot.com/2012/10/more-sharpie-les-touches.html" target="_blank">
         draw Dalmatian spots
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28726" style="width: 610px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/cross1.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: mypoppet.com.au" class="size-full wp-image-28726" height="400" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/cross1.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Mypoppet.com.au
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Update an old cane chair with a few colors of yarn and
        <a href="http://mypoppet.com.au/2012/09/how-to-cross-stitch-chair.html" target="_blank">
         create a cross-stitch pattern.
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Tie on Leather to Your Throne
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28729" style="width: 550px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/belt-chair.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: apartmenttherapy.com" class="size-full wp-image-28729" height="734" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/belt-chair.jpg" width="540"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Apartmenttherapy.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Build a new seat from old belts with this
        <a href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/how-to-diy-blakes-belted-chair-150579" target="_blank">
         DIY belted chair
        </a>
        from Apartment Therapy.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Think Outside the Seat
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28728" style="width: 580px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/swing.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: poetichome.com" class="size-full wp-image-28728" height="760" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/swing.jpg" width="570"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Poetichome.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Made from an old dining chair, this swing is an almost too-perfect spot for reading. Heres how to
        <a href="http://www.poetichome.com/2011/01/25/upcyling-dining-chairss-into-beds-swings-and-benches/" target="_blank">
         make your own
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28727" style="width: 510px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/bench_1_large.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: www.curbly.com" class="size-full wp-image-28727" height="600" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/bench_1_large.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Curbly.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Group three chairs of the same height to
        <a href="http://www.curbly.com/users/diy-maven/posts/11039-how-to-turn-chairs-into-a-bench" target="_blank">
         make a bench
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28730" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DogChairCircle1-1024x762.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: thediyvillage.com" class="size-large wp-image-28730" height="476" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DogChairCircle1-1024x762-640x476.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Thediyvillage.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Chop off the legs of your chair and create a
        <a href="http://www.thediyvillage.com/2013/01/diy-dog-bowl-chairs.html" target="_blank">
         feeding station
        </a>
        for your pets.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28740" style="width: 430px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/rocking.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: inewidea.com" class="size-full wp-image-28740" height="423" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/rocking.jpg" width="420"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Inewidea.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Your favorite seat doesnt have to remain stagnant. Convert a regular chair into a
        <a href="http://www.inewidea.com/2009/06/11/7775.html" target="_blank">
         rocking chair
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_28736" style="width: 586px">
        <a href="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/frame.jpg">
         <img alt="Photo: allthingsthrifty.com" class=" wp-image-28736 " height="867" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/frame-640x963.jpg" width="576"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo: Allthingsthrifty.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Make a classy picture frame with the
        <a href="http://www.allthingsthrifty.com/2011/04/my-gallery-wall-after-six-months-in.html" target="_blank">
         backing of a beat-up chair
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_32683" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://krrb.com/places/153-united-states?offer=chair&amp;utm_source=blog.krrb.com&amp;utm_medium=article_link&amp;utm_campaign=25_chairs&amp;utm_content=chairs_for_sale_image_link">
         <img alt="Krrb.com" class="size-large wp-image-32683" height="281" src="http://blog.krrb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Screen-shot-2013-10-24-at-10.43.19-AM-640x281.png" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Krrb.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://krrb.com/places/153-united-states?offer=chair&amp;utm_source=blog.krrb.com&amp;utm_medium=article_link&amp;utm_campaign=25_chairs&amp;utm_content=chairs_for_sale_text_link" target="_blank">
