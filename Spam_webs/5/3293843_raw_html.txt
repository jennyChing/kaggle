     <p>
      The
      <b>
       Cuejdiu oil field
      </b>
      is an
      <a href="/wiki/Oil_field" title="Oil field">
       oil field
      </a>
      located in
      <a href="/wiki/G%C3%A2rcina" title="Grcina">
       Grcina
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Neam%C5%A3_County" title="Neam County">
       Neam County
      </a>
      . It was discovered in 2010 and developed by
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Aurelian_Oil_%26_Gas&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Aurelian Oil &amp; Gas (page does not exist)">
       Aurelian Oil &amp; Gas
      </a>
      . It will begin production in 2012 and will produce
      <a href="/wiki/Oil" title="Oil">
       oil
      </a>
      . The total
      <a href="/wiki/Proven_reserves" title="Proven reserves">
       proven reserves
      </a>
      of the Cuejdiu oil field are from 150 million barrels (2010
      <sup>
       6
      </sup>
      tonnes) to 300 million barrels (4010
      <sup>
       6
      </sup>
      tonnes), and production is slated to be centered on 10,000 barrels per day (1,600m
      <sup>
       3
      </sup>
      /d).
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
