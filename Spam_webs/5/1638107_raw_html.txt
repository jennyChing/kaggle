              <p>

              </p>

              </p>
              <p>
               You can attend one or all three guided Healing Meditations absolutely FREE!
              </p>

              </p>
              <p>
               206 is located directly across the street from the Bayport Blue Point Library. look for the glass door with the purple frame next to the deli. You may park on the street or in the library/fire dept. parking lot. You neednt bring anything but your self.

               Meditation has been proven to be most beneficial in balancing and healing the body. The meditation will help you to activate your own bodys inner healing abilities. through visualization you give your self the gift of aligning your mind with your body for whole being healing.

              </p>
