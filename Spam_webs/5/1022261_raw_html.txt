        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Homework &gt; Snapchat
        </h1>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         You want to play Candy Crush Saga. Too bad, you have a 200 page thesis to write. Your brains telling you to crush candies, and that youre much more efficient when you work at the last minute.What should you do? The responsible thing, of course. Pop your phone into the kSafe. The safe locks whatever you put into it until you complete your self-set goal, or until you enter your password into the kSafe app. The container is food-safe and virtually unbreakable, making it perfect to store anything that is an obsession or a distraction.
         <span id="more-116300">
         </span>
         Be it your phone, credit cards, Xbox controller, unnecessary carbs, the kSafe will do a remarkable job keeping your kryptonite away from you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Explaining to your girlfriend that you couldnt answer her calls because your phone was locked in a smart-vault is something that youd totally have to do yourself, though.
        </p>
        <p>
         Designer:
         <a href="http://www.thekitchensafe.com/" target="_blank">
          KSafe
         </a>
        </p>
