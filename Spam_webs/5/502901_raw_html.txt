         <a href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/review-revlon-bold-lacquer-grow-luscious-mascara/" rel="bookmark" title="">
          REVIEW: Revlon Bold Lacquer by Grow Luscious Mascara
         </a>
        </h1>
        <h2 class="entry-meta">
         <time class="published updated" datetime="2014-11-11">
          November 11, 2014
         </time>
         
         <a href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/review-revlon-bold-lacquer-grow-luscious-mascara/#comments">
          <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="7429 http://www.slashedbeauty.com/?p=7429">
           4 Comments
          </span>
         </a>
        </h2>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div id="greet_block">
        </div>
        <a class="maxbutton-2" href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/disclosures" rel="nofollow">
         Affiliate Links
        </a>
        <p>
         This year, I think Ive tried more new mascaras than ever! The drugstore mascara scene has really stepped up their game and Ive been discovering many that I like. For the past couple months, Ive been testing the waters with the new Revlon Bold Lacquer by Grow Luscious mascara. I received it in my
         <a href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/july-beauty-box-5/" title="July 2014 Beauty Box 5 Review">
          Beauty Box 5 back in July
         </a>
         and finally got around to trying it. Ill admit that it didnt look like anything special, but I ended up liking it! Watch the video to hear my thoughts and see how it works in action, plus a before and after.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/mPJGVXLQUss" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        The Bold Lacquer mascara has high-shine black fibers that build length and volume while still looking natural. The end result is long, defined and thick lashes that look great. The formula applies with a wet gel consistency, but dries quickly. With that said, as I mentioned in the video, flaking does end up happening a few hours into wear time.
        <p>
         <img alt="REVIEW: Revlon Bold Lacquer by Grow Luscious Mascara" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7430" height="379" src="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/revlonbrush.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The brush is definitely the secret weapon in getting the natural look. There are plenty of bristles and they are spaced out to really comb through the lashes, separating and coating each one to avoid clumps. The tapered end also makes it easy to pick up the lashes in the corner. While this is a buildable formula that layers well, I wouldnt say that you get a super dramatic, false lash look this offers more enhancement for an every day look.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="REVIEW: Revlon Bold Lacquer by Grow Luscious Mascara" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7431" height="645" src="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/revlonboldlacquer.jpg" width="304"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I like the effect I get with the
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1wF5QI8" target="_blank">
          Revlon Bold Lacquer by Grow Luscious Mascara
         </a>
         , sans the flaking. Im wondering if my tube is stale or if perhaps I should try the waterproof version to see if that fixes the problem. Either way, I would recommend this to anyone whos looking for an affordable mascara that will give your lashes
         <em>
          oomph
         </em>
         without looking over-done. You can find it for around $8 in drugstores.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
