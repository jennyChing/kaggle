          </strong>
         </span>
        </center>
        <center>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="349" id="viddler-15bd76a2" src="//www.viddler.com/embed/15bd76a2/?f=1&amp;autoplay=0&amp;player=full&amp;secret=83608891&amp;loop=0&amp;nologo=0&amp;hd=0" width="545">

        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-size: 36px;">

         </span>
        </h1>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-size: 40px; color: #ff0000;">
          <strong>

          </strong>
         </span>
        </h1>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 16px;">
          Every day I hear from people who are JUST LIKE YOU &amp; ME who are struggling with their weight or body image. Having battled with the same 25 lbs. for years as a yo-yo dieter, I thought this lifestyle would never end. There were times that I had 4 different clothing sizes hanging my closet, and I was afraid to get rid of any of them!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 16px;">

           <strong>
            failure
