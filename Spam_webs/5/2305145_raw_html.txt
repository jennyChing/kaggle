      <h2>
       Interview  The Electric Playgrounds Victor Lucas
      </h2>
      <p>
        by Robert Sutherland (@Sutherland604)
      </p>
      <p>
       I recently had the pleasure of chatting with
       <strong>
        Victor Lucas
       </strong>
       . You may recognize Lucas from the shows
       <a href="http://www.elecplay.com/" target="_blank">
        <em>
         Electric Playground
        </em>
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.reviewsontherun.com/" target="_blank">
        <em>
         Reviews on the Run
        </em>
       </a>
       . He is one of Vancouvers premier video game personalities and his production company
       <a href="http://www.greedydocs.com/" target="_blank">
        Greedy Docs
       </a>
       has been producing
       <em>
        Elec Play
       </em>
       ,
       <em>
        RotR
       </em>
       and behind-the scenes documentaries on video games for years now.
      </p>
      <p>
       We chat about his love for video games,
       <a href="http://www.thesnipenews.com/movies/retro-batman-1966/" target="_blank">
        Batman
       </a>
       , and action figures, and I try to get to the bottom of how he comes up with the ratings for all the games, movies, toys and other fun things he reviews on a daily basis. Enjoy!
      </p>
      <p>
       Watch 
       <strong>
        Victor Lucas
       </strong>
       interview:
      </p>

       <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/wHUqKk7UP6o?fs=1&amp;feature=oembed" width="500">
