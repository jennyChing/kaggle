        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">

         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-57-52/" rel="attachment wp-att-3698">
           <img alt="2014-10-10 10.57.52" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3698" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.57.52-e1413106599713.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          I never knew how much I didnt know about chocolate. Its not just that wonderful treat that people love, its so much more.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-50-10/" rel="attachment wp-att-3717">
           <img alt="2014-10-10 10.50.10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3717" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.50.10-e1413106662148.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          Hearing the rich history of chocolate presented in this exhibit gave me a true understanding of this amazing delicacy.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-50-52/" rel="attachment wp-att-3715">
           <img alt="2014-10-10 10.50.52" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3715" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.50.52-e1413106704142.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          Touching Cacoa beans, and watching them transform into the chocolate we know and love was an interesting journey.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-51-14/" rel="attachment wp-att-3714">
           <img alt="chocolate as currency" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3714" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.51.14-e1413106778718.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          In early times, people traded cacao beans for just about anything. There value was incredible. Slaves were even traded for beans, that was a really surprising insight to learn.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-43-21/" rel="attachment wp-att-3733">
           <img alt="chocolate the drink enjoyed by kings and by the rich" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3733" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.43.21-e1413106840351.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          Kings and royalty drank chocolate, and even had specific drinks made from chocolate that they enjoyed drinking for special occasions or just because. I loved hearing about how chocolate moved from a delicacy to be enjoyed only by royalty and the rich to something so commonplace.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-44-46/" rel="attachment wp-att-3729">
           <img alt="hearing how chocolate expanded other markets " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3729" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.44.46-e1413106887781.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          The exhibit shared interesting facts, about the history of chocolate and how many lives and economies it touched. It went from Spain to European countries to worldwide. Its value was incredible and it also caused the rise and growth of many other crops like sugar.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/chocolate/2014-10-10-10-47-24/" rel="attachment wp-att-3723">
           <img alt="chocolate you can drink at home" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3723" height="338" src="http://girlfriendswithgoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2014-10-10-10.47.24-e1413106958677.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
          The exhibit showed the growth of the chocolate drink and of course, there are some of our childhood favorites represented here like Ovaltine and Quik. This turned the demand for chocolate even higher and took the drink from something only enjoyed by the rich to a drink many just couldnt live without. Most of all, it fueled an obsession with a drink that still continues today. If you want to know more about chocolate, your interested in currency and its uses, or if you want to know about how chocolate became one of Americas favorite goodies check out this exhibit at the Academy of Natural Sciences from October 11th  January 24, 2015.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
