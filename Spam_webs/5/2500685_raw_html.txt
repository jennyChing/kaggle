        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Forget Monday! At Fracture EVERY Day is Green.
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-date">
          <a href="http://blog.fractureme.com/hashtag-green-for-days/" rel="bookmark">
           <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-12-09T08:30:00+00:00">
            December 9, 2014
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="byline">
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.fractureme.com/author/dallen/" rel="author">
            Drew Allen
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p>
         <img alt="makeeverydaygreen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2863" height="640" src="http://blog.fractureme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/makeeverydaygreen.png" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Yesterday was Green Monday, the newest sibling in the retail holiday shopping days. While we applaud the candor of the day being
         <a href="http://www.ibtimes.com/where-did-black-friday-cyber-monday-come-349365" target="_blank">
          named after money
         </a>
         , it honestly made us think about how focused we are on another kind of Green.
        </p>
        <p>
         Nope. Not that either. #smh
        </p>
        <div class="tm-tweet-clear">
        </div>
        <div class="tm-click-to-tweet">
         <div class="tm-ctt-text">
          <a href="https://twitter.com/share?text=Fracture+is%C2%A0passionate+about+maximizing+the+eco-friendliness+of+our%C2%A0company+and+our+products.&amp;via=fractureme&amp;related=fractureme&amp;url=http://blog.fractureme.com/hashtag-green-for-days/" target="_blank">
           Fracture ispassionate about maximizing the eco-friendliness of ourcompany and our products.
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a class="tm-ctt-btn" href="https://twitter.com/share?text=Fracture+is%C2%A0passionate+about+maximizing+the+eco-friendliness+of+our%C2%A0company+and+our+products.&amp;via=fractureme&amp;related=fractureme&amp;url=http://blog.fractureme.com/hashtag-green-for-days/" target="_blank">
           Click To Tweet
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="tm-ctt-tip">
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         We thought that on the heels of Green Monday would be a great time to share why we do our best to
         <strong>
          make every day green
         </strong>
         at Fracture.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="fracture-glass-prints_127169351" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2859" height="533" src="http://blog.fractureme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/fracture-glass-prints_127169351.png" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Energy
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          We reduce energy usage as much as possible.
         </strong>
         Our office side is lit completely by natural light. The only time lights come on in the office when we work late at night (Mostly during the holiday rush)! This also includes keeping our A/C at a reasonable level  never too cold or too warm. We also have various means of reducing wasted energy in our production process where possible.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Waste
        </h2>
        <p>
         Weve set a dead
         <strong>
          clear culture of reducing waste
         </strong>
         , being mindful of waste and ecological concerns, our role in our community and keeping a clean shop.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Our packaging was stubbornly designed with ecology in mind
         </strong>
         , to reduce overall materials usedand to keep the materials
         <strong>
          99% renewable/recycled/recyclable
         </strong>
         cardboard and avoid bubble wrap, foam, etc. and all the while making it lightweight so it takes less energy to transport.
        </p>
        <p>
         We take
         <strong>
          extreme measures
         </strong>
         to make sure that any and all toxic and not-nice chemicals are disposed of in a proper manner. This may seem like a no-brainer, but weve found that too often it isnt.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Recycle
        </h2>
        <p>
         The product itself is made of glass, ink and foamboard. Glass thrown into the landfill isnt recycled but its
         <strong>
          very low impact
         </strong>
         ; the ink is in a solid plastic form so it
         <strong>
          doesnt leach harmful chemicals
         </strong>
         . The foamboard is something were actually working on improving because its not the best it could be.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_20141111_122857" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2861" height="600" src="http://blog.fractureme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_20141111_122857.png" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          We recycle all our glass.
         </strong>
         We go very far out of our way to do this  its purely on our own terms. It costs us time, money and energy. To date
         <strong>
          we have recycled 34,000 pounds of glass
         </strong>
          thats about the equivalent of over 75,000 beer bottles worth.
        </p>
        <div class="tm-tweet-clear">
        </div>
        <div class="tm-click-to-tweet">
         <div class="tm-ctt-text">
          <a href="https://twitter.com/share?text=To+date+Fracture+has+recycled+over+34%2C000+pounds+of+glass%21+%23EarthDay&amp;via=fractureme&amp;related=fractureme&amp;url=http://blog.fractureme.com/hashtag-green-for-days/" target="_blank">
           To date Fracture has recycled over 34,000 pounds of glass! #EarthDay
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a class="tm-ctt-btn" href="https://twitter.com/share?text=To+date+Fracture+has+recycled+over+34%2C000+pounds+of+glass%21+%23EarthDay&amp;via=fractureme&amp;related=fractureme&amp;url=http://blog.fractureme.com/hashtag-green-for-days/" target="_blank">
           Click To Tweet
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="tm-ctt-tip">
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_20141111_122804" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2860" height="600" src="http://blog.fractureme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_20141111_122804.png" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          We recycle all of our paper and cardboard material.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         We
         <strong>
          reclaim
         </strong>
         any and all prints that have been rejected in our stringent quality control inspection.
        </p>
        <p>
         We also
         <strong>
          recycle a bunch of other stuff
         </strong>
         , like cans and bottles and also all of the scrap aluminum and steel that comes out of our R&amp;D adventures.
        </p>
        <h2>
         OurGreen Goals for the Future:
        </h2>
        <p>
         Wed like to get even more energy-managed by
         <strong>
          monitoring our usage
         </strong>
         and finding ways of cutting back as much as possible.
        </p>
        <p>
         We have plans to upgrade our major equipment to much
         <strong>
          less energy intensive
         </strong>
         forms.
        </p>
        <p>
         Well be making
         <strong>
          product improvements
         </strong>
         that reduce impact on landfills when thrown away.
        </p>
        <p>
         Wed really REALLY like to
         <strong>
          become a B corp
         </strong>
         and meet all the regulations that come along with it.
        </p>
