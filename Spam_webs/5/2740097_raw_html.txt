        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Pool Noodle Crafts and Activities
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="onDate date updated">
          <i class="icon-time icon-metas" title="Date">
          </i>
          <a href="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/pool-noodle-crafts-and-activities/" rel="bookmark">
           April 27, 2015
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <i class="icon-folder-open icon-metas" title="Categories">
          </i>
          <a href="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/category/family/" rel="tag">
           Family
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/category/kids-crafts/" rel="tag">
           Kids Crafts
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Tis the season of stores overrun with
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1GK7XB5" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           pool noodles
          </a>
          , cheaper by the dozen, and so the time to look into pool noodle crafts and activities to try!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pool-noodles-crafts-activites-2.jpg">
           <img alt="'Tis the season of stores overrun with pool noodles, cheaper by the dozen, and so the time to look into pool noodle crafts and activities to try!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4145" height="950" src="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pool-noodles-crafts-activites-2.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1GK7XB5" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Pool noodles
          </a>
          are versatile, flexible, easy to cut, and huge. They are cheap and therefore perfect for giant fun.
         </p>
         <p>
          I wanted to present you with some ideas of my own but we dont have space for such large scale fun, so instead I spent days scouring the web and asking around for ideas from other bloggers. Make sure to check out the tutorial for each by clicking through. Leave a comment once youre at it
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
         </h1>
         <h1>
          <strong>
           18 Pool Noodle Crafts
          </strong>
         </h1>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          1-2. Float these
          <a href="http://ourfamilyofseven.com/pool-noodle-boats/" target="_blank">
           pool noodle boats
          </a>
          by Our Family of Seven. Or try out
          <a href="http://www.simplefunforkids.com/simple-diy-boats-in-the-water-table.html/" target="_blank">
           another great version
          </a>
          by Simple Fun for Kids.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/91.jpg">
           <img alt="Check out this fun master list of pool noodle crafts and activities for all ages! These are all so much fun!" class="aligncenter wp-image-4123 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.momsandcrafters.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/91.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
