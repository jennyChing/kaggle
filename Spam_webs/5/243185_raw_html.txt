     <p>
      The
      <b>
       Turkish Ice Hockey Womens League
      </b>
      is a womens semi-professional
      <a href="/wiki/Ice_hockey" title="Ice hockey">
       ice hockey
      </a>
      league in
      <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">
       Turkey
      </a>
      . It is operated under the jurisdiction of the
      <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Ice_Hockey_Federation" title="Turkish Ice Hockey Federation">
       Turkish Ice Hockey Federation
      </a>
      , a member of the
      <a href="/wiki/International_Ice_Hockey_Federation" title="International Ice Hockey Federation">
       International Ice Hockey Federation
      </a>
      . Each team is allowed to carry 2 import players per season. The season typically starts in late November  early December and finishes in April.
     </p>
     <p>
      The 2009-2010 season consists of 11 teams split up into three groups; Orta (Center) A and B consisting of teams from Ankara whereas the Marmara + Dou (East) group consists of teams from Istanbul, zmit and Erzurum.
     </p>
     <p>
      Champion of the league qualifies for the IIHF
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=European_Women%E2%80%99s_Champion_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="European Womens Champion Cup (page does not exist)">
       European Womens Champion Cup
      </a>
      .
     </p>
