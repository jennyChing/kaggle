          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           If you are going into the price per head business, you should know that you will be dealing with people online and that means you have to market your business using the internet. There is a lot you have to know before you start pushing and promoting your work online, and one of the first things you should get your head around is how to create the proper link for your players. There is a little more thinking that goes into it than you would probably guess.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           It should go without saying that every page of your site should have its own URL as you do not want to confuse the search engines that you are trying to rank on. You would think that this would be a common-sense sort of thing, but you would be surprise how many sites have multiple URLs going to one page and all that does is hurt your rankings. Search engines will not know what they are even looking for as players are on Google looking for your site, and that will just frustrate them enough to leave, which means you lose a client.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Make your URLs short and sweet because players do not want to have to try and remember some URL with 10 words in it. Of course, they can always bookmark it, but they may want to pass it on to someone by word of mouth and they will have an issue trying to remember it all. The URL should be descriptive and tell you what the page means, but be concise with your words and try to use a keyword or two. However, do not use too many keywords or else Google (or the other search engines) will think that it is spam and that will send you plummeting down the rankings.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           If you do have to use a few words, remember to use hyphens to separate the words instead of stuffing them all together, or some will use the period symbol. Plus signs are also useful as search engines will recognize those, but if you want the optimal result, you should be using hyphens to split up your words.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Of course, all of this can be made easier if you bring on a SEO expert who is well versed in the art of search engine optimization. They will have all the tips you need to know to create the links that search engines will pick up on. SEO is actually a specialty nowadays with the popularity of online betting and price per head sites as well, although it is something that can be used anytime you are trying to rank high on Google.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The right link can bring in a ton of visitors to your price per head site, so make sure you have your bases covered in that respect.
          </p>
