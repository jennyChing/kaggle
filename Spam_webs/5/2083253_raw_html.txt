         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           Most people will agree that in their day to day lives, they always need to have the right gadgets and other nifty items that will make their lives easier and their days smoother. There was a time when people had to bring humongous backpacks and suitcases to fit all the things they need but in todays technologically-advanced world, there are so many compact items and gadgets they can take with them and the best part is they all fit right into our pockets! Such is the wonder of technology and design innovations.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          These gadgets and items can range from the extremely high-tech to the ones that are simple yet extremely necessary and helpful. Just take a look at those smart phones with touch screens that can fit in the palm of your hand. With those, you can buy plane tickets, call your friends and family, track where you are, get directions, take and edit pictures, and so much more  all that from just one gadget that easily fits in the palm of your hand. Plus, how about those nifty Bluetooth virtual keyboards that can be attached to tablets and smart phones? With one in your pocket, you can leave the bulky laptop at home and take your work anywhere with you. There are also tiny speakers that can fit in our pocket and pack major power so you can bring the party anywhere and everywhere.
         </p>
         <p>
          Then you have other items that do not need electricity or batteries to run but still make your life easier. Items like the Knee Defender, a tiny gadget that fits into your pocket but does wonders for your knees in protecting them from reclining airplane seats or how about those Space bags for travellers who want to travel light at all times? These bags are vacuum-packed so you can lighten the load when you travel and wont have to pay extra luggage fees.
         </p>
