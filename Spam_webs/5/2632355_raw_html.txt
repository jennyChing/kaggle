     <h1>
      Design of Frank Lloyd Wrights Masterpiece
     </h1>
     <div class="meta-bar">
      Posted to
      <a href="http://flowingdata.com/category/visualization/artistic-visualization/" rel="category tag">
       Data Art
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="entry">
      <p>
       In 1934, American architect Frank Lloyd Wright designed
       <a href="http://www.fallingwater.org/">
        Fallingwater
       </a>
       , a house built partly over a waterfall. A couple of years ago, Smithsonian Magazine listed Fallingwater as
       <a href="http://www.smithsonianmag.com/specialsections/lifelists/lifelist.html">
        one of the 28 places to visit
       </a>
       before you die.
       <a href="http://www.etereaestudios.com/index.html">
        Cristobal Vila
       </a>
       , who himself has a knack for
       <a href="http://flowingdata.com/2010/05/10/dreaming-in-numbers/">
        pretty things
       </a>
       , animates the imaginary design and construction of Wright's famous building.
      </p>
      <p>
       Watch it unfold in the
       <a href="http://www.etereaestudios.com/docs_html/fallingwater_htm/fallingwater_movie_index.htm">
        animated video
       </a>
       below. Warning: after watching, you will have a very strong urge to visit.
       <br/>
       <span id="more-8270">
       </span>
      </p>
