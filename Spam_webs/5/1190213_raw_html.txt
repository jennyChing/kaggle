          <h3>
           AWAKENING I
          </h3>
          <p>
           Please read all of the following.If you wish to perform this exercise, you need to understand what you will be doing. It is important to follow the instructionsas this is somewhat advanced:
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <a class="aalmanual" href="http://amzn.to/1C5Zd75" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Third Eye">
            Third Eye
           </a>
           is directly related to the 6th
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2012/06/21/energy-workshop-part-1-foundation-and-the-chakras/" title="Energy Workshop: Part 1  Foundation and the Chakras">
            chakra
           </a>
           the psychic chakra, located on the middle of the foreheadabove the brows. It is closely associatedwith the pineal gland.The
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2012/08/13/how-to-detox-your-pineal-gland-fluoride-mercury-consciousness/" title="How to Detox Your Pineal Gland  Fluoride, Mercury, &amp; Consciousness">
            pineal gland
           </a>
           is dormant in most people,as is the true Third Eye.
          </p>
          <p>
           French Philosopher
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/search/ref=as_li_qf_sp_sr_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;index=aps&amp;keywords=Descartes%20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wakitime09-20" target="_blank">
            Rene Descartes
           </a>
           believed the pineal gland to be 
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2011/12/28/the-pineal-gland-the-seat-of-the-soul/" target="_blank" title="The Pineal Gland  The Seat of the Soul?">
            the seat of the soul
           </a>
           where mind and body met.
          </p>
          <p>
           In the average person,the
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2012/03/01/pineal-power/" target="_blank" title="Pineal Power">
            pineal gland
           </a>
           is atrophied, [calcified] and dormant.The following exercise will change that.Please read this thoroughly,as much of the exercises I write of are very advancedand can cause problems if one does not do them correctly.
          </p>
          <div align="center" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 1em;">
           <script type="text/javascript">

           The pineal gland is like a pea in size;like a shriveled raisin in most people where it remains dormant.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Opening the Pineal Gland/Third Eye
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           This is done with a specific tone and chant.You only need to do this exercise for 3 days,afterwards, it is permanent.
          </p>
          <p>
           The mantra to be used is Thoh, pronounced TOE.
          </p>
          <p>
           It must be within the correct vibration.
          </p>
          <p>
           Not deep, not high pitched,inbetween, like alto range.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            You will feel it when you hit the correct tone.
           </strong>
           So play around and dont second guess yourself. When you think youve got it, you probably do.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            1.
           </strong>
           Sit with your back straight.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            2.
           </strong>
           Breathe in through your nose and hold your breathas long as is comfortable.
           <br/>
           Open your jaws so there is a small spacebetween your top and bottom teeth.
           <br/>
           Place the tip of your tongue betweenthe space of your slightly parted teeth.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            3.
           </strong>
           Put a very light pressure onto the tonguewith your teeth.
          </p>
          <p>
           This is like the same process of saying the TH partof the English word the.
          </p>
          <p>
           Once your tongue is in position,release your breath slowly through your mouth,saying T-H-H-O-H-H in one long exhale.
          </p>
          <p>
           Say the word one time per exhale.Your tongue will be vibrating between your teeth.
          </p>
          <p>
           You should feel the air moving past your tongue and teeth.
          </p>
          <p>
           If this technique is done properly,you will feel a pressure or sensation in your jawand cheeks. The tone will also vibrate in your third eye.
          </p>
          <p>
           It may take a few seconds to adjust this,dont worry, just keep going.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            4.
           </strong>
           Do the above 5 times in a row.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            5.
           </strong>
           It is very important the above exercisebe done for 3 consecutive days, 24 hours apart.Then it is a done deal.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            What You Can Expect
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           One of the first experiences is a headacheor pressure in the center of the forehead.This sensation may also feellike it is originating from within,usually an inch or morebeneath the surface of the forehead.This is a positive indication the pineal glandis awakening and beginning to functionin a healthy manner.
          </p>
          <p>
           *Some people can experience amigrainelasting several hours, so to be safe it is recommended that you try this in the evening when you do not have work to do.The severity of the side effectswill depend on how atrophied/calcified your pineal gland isto begin with.
          </p>
          <p>
           After theoccurrenceof headache or pressurein the forehead, you may wake up one morningwith a throbbing or tingling sensation in your forehead;it may feel like a goosebump.
          </p>
          <p>
           The feeling may be very intense,like something is there.The sensation of pulsing or throbbingwill continue throughout the day, and you may hear sounds coming from within your head, such as light popping or crackling sensations.
          </p>
          <p>
           These are the finalphysiologicaleventsyou may experience after opening up your third eye.It indicates your pineal gland is awakened,functioning and alive.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Psychic Effects
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
           </strong>
           You may experience any or all of these to varying degrees:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Faster, easier learning and retention
           </li>
           <li>
            Marked increase in intuition
           </li>
           <li>
            Increased creativity
           </li>
           <li>
            Psychic gifts develop and become markedly stronger, along withmore developed ability to see or sense human auras
           </li>
           <li>
            Clairvoyance (psychic vision) opens up
           </li>
           <li>
            Clairaudience (psychic hearing) opens up
           </li>
           <li>
            Clairsentience (psychic feeling/touching) opens up
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           This is a very powerful exercise,and as with all powerful practices,your body may go through discomfort in order to adapt.
          </p>
          <h3>
           AWAKENING II
          </h3>
          <p>
           In order for this to work properly,you must have already performedthe Awakening I exercise.
          </p>
          <p>
           I included this above for those who are new.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            It is very important to wait for 10-14 days following Awakening I,before you go onto Awakening II,
           </strong>
           otherwise the exercises wont be effective. Your body needs time to adaptand get the energy flow going.
          </p>
          <p>
           This exercise is completely euphoricand intensley pleasurable.Unlike Awakening I, this should be done once a week,and given the blissful effects that are lasting when done correctly,most people will enjoy doing so.
          </p>
          <p>
           Breath in deeply and hold your breathfor a count of five.
          </p>
          <p>
           Repeat this three times.
          </p>
          <p>
           This helps you to be relaxed and focused.
          </p>
          <p>
           Now focus all of your attention upon your Third Eye.
          </p>
          <p>
           You should begin to feel a sensationsimilar to the effects of the Awakening I exercise a slight pressure or awarenessof the spot of your Third Eye.
          </p>
          <p>
           Now, take a deep breathas you did for the Awakening I exercise.
          </p>
          <p>
           Hold it for as long as comfortable do not push this  and release your breath,vibrating the word MAY.This is pronounced like the English language wordfor the month of May.
          </p>
          <p>
           It should all come out at one time- M-a-a-a-a-a-a-a-a-a-ay, gradually and slowly.
          </p>
          <p>
           It is ok to adjust your pitch.The tone should be alto.
          </p>
          <p>
           Breath in again, and repeat this five times.
          </p>
          <p>
           You need to hit a certain pitch.You will know when you do, for you will feel this in your head.Do the best you can.
          </p>
          <p>
           Now, THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT:
          </p>
          <p>
           When vibrating the word MAY,feel the energy going into your head,
           <br/>
           first into your Third Eye area,
           <br/>
           then into the middle of your brain,
           <br/>
           and then to the top of your headwhere your crown chakra is located.
          </p>
          <p>
           REMEMBER: Concentrate on your forehead (Third Eye area),
           <br/>
           then the middle of your brain
           <br/>
           and finally the top of your head.
          </p>
          <p>
           This is done for the duration of each chant.
          </p>
          <p>
           1. Breath in.
          </p>
          <p>
           2. Begin to exhale, vibrating MAY.
          </p>
          <p>
           3. Concentrate on your Third Eye.
          </p>
          <p>
           4. Concentrate on the middle of your brain.
          </p>
          <p>
           5. Concentrate on the top of your headand finish exhaling the vibration.
          </p>
          <p>
           Repeat four more times.
          </p>
          <p>
           The effects of this exercise are extremely pleasurable.
          </p>
          <p>
           A feeling of lightness can occur immediately following the exercise.
          </p>
          <p>
           Some people may feel energy or tingling inside their heads,or completely covering their heads;a slightpressurein the crown area and intense euphoria is also common.
          </p>
          <p>
           The blissful state can occur hours and even days later.
          </p>
          <p>
           This will eventually become permanentand will greatly assist in void meditation,clairvoyance, and the development of other psychic and paranormal senses.
