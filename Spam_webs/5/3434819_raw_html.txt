         <p>
          I received an email yesterday entitled Halloween Dictionary and it was one of those emails that made me LOL (laugh out loud) at my laptop. I have to be honest, nine times out of ten I delete forwarded chain emails, but I needed a pick me up yesterday, so I clicked on this one. Im glad I did.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/i-love-zombies.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-thumbnail wp-image-11560" height="150" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/i-love-zombies-150x150.jpg" title="i-love-zombies" width="150"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-11305">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Bobbing Apples:
          </strong>
          What happens when you leave your bra off while running.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Boogieman:
          </strong>
          Guy who passes time at a stoplight picking his nose.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Coffin:
          </strong>
          What you do when you get a piece of popcorn stuck in your throat.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Frankenstein:
          </strong>
          Hot dog and a mug of beer.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Full moon:
          </strong>
          What your repairman reveals when he bends over to fix your fridge.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Goblin:
          </strong>
          How you eat the Snickers bars you got for Halloween.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Invisible Man:
          </strong>
          What a guy becomes when theres housework to be done. Also, see Mr. Hyde.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Jack O Lantern:
          </strong>
          An Irish Pumpkin.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Jack the Ripper:
          </strong>
          What Jack does to his lottery tickets after losing each week.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Mummy:
          </strong>
          Who kisses the boo-boo after you scrape your knee.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Pumpkin Patch:
          </strong>
          What a pumpkin wears when trying to quit smoking.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Skeleton:
          </strong>
          Any supermodel.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Vampire Bat:
          </strong>
          What Dracula hits a baseball with.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Witch
          </strong>
          : See Mother-in-Law.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Zombie:
          </strong>
          What you look like before that first cup of morning coffee.
         </p>
         <p>
          Pretty funny, huh?
         </p>
