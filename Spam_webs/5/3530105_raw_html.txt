        <div class="format_text entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.derekfredrickson.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/google-plus-070111.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-904];player=img;">
           <img alt="The Skinny on Google+" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-905" height="150" src="http://www.derekfredrickson.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/google-plus-070111-150x150.jpg" title="The Skinny on Google+" width="150"/>
          </a>
          In case you havent noticed, theres a new kid on the social media block. And our friendly online giant, Google, brings it to us.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Ive had a lot of questions about Google+ ranging from how to use it and is it worth paying attention to
          </strong>
          . Many people out there are calling it the Facebook Killer but I dont see it like that. The social media landscape is vast and varied enough that there is (currently) enough room for another player in the game. I say  bring it on! Google is obviously a HUGE player in the online world so anything they do  you pretty much HAVE TO pay attention to it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Google+ is offered on practically all Google products (Gmail, Google.com, Google Docs, etc.), and its adoption is spreading quickly
          </strong>
          . I heard recently it attracted more than 10 million user registrations in the first two weeks (more than Facebook or Twitter during their initial launch phase.)
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           But, as a marketer  what do you need to know?
          </strong>
          Whats the lowdown on Google+ to help you with marketing and your online presence?
         </p>
         <p>
          Well, heres what you need to know as this new player starts to emerge.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           In a nutshell, Google+ enables real-time updates
          </strong>
          (like Twitter) and lets you stay in touch with circles of friends (similar to Facebook, but with easier options for creating and viewing groups). You can also check into places with it (like FourSquare).
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           So, whats in it for us marketers
          </strong>
          ? Although Google+ is still relatively new, we can already see what it has as potential from a marketing perspective.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            +1 Feature
           </span>
          </strong>
          . Similar to Facebook Like 
          <strong>
           it allows you to promote and share webpages and content online by +1 the page
          </strong>
          . So, many marketers have already started to add Googles +1 feature to content pages and websites. That means more user engagement for you from your website visitors and another method for people to spread the content and get the word out.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            SEO
           </span>
          </strong>
          . Just like with any other method of social promotion,
          <strong>
           the more +1s your content gets, the more likely Google will consider the topic relevant to those search terms
          </strong>
          . Its a recent SEO trend, which relies more on recommendations and people suggesting relevant content as opposed to black and white marketing strategies to get your content on the top pages of Google. So, over time Google will factor +1 scores into its search algorithm. When you write about a subject on Google+ and your circle (friends) Google that subject, your comments and links are displayed on page on of Google.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            User Intelligence
           </span>
          </strong>
          . The more you use Google+,
          <strong>
           the smarter the platform gets as recognizing your usage and recommending items in your News Feed
          </strong>
          . Youll start seeing increasingly relevant updates and content coming from within your network  meaning users most interested in what you have to say will find your content more easily, and will be more likely to refer to it and share it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            Influence
           </span>
          </strong>
          .
          <strong>
           Google+ is another way for users to share what theyre reading and watching
          </strong>
          . Right now, the people on Google+ are people  its not available to companies or brands yet. So, the early adopters and users of this platform are able to establish the trend and how interactive the platform is with their network. So, the biggest influencers on Google+ will become the most active and influential sharers of content.
         </p>
         <p>
          Even though Google+ is just starting out,
          <strong>
           its reach and ease of use make its implications for online marketers worth consideration
          </strong>
          . Join today and stay tuned to see where it goes!
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres an amazing secret to use Google as a marketer:
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           +1 notices are posted in everyones Gmail inbox
          </strong>
           meaning if you +1 something it has the potential to show up right in someones Gmail inbox  which is where they are spending most of their time anyway  in EMAIL. This point alone makes Google+ a must for any marketer.
         </p>
         <p>
          Until next time
          <strong>
           Learn It, Love It, Live it!
          </strong>
         </p>
