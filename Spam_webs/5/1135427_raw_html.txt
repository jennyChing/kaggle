      <h1>
       Getting to Know the Metric System
      </h1>
      <p>
       If the metric system gives you heartburn, here are a few tips to help you out.
      </p>
      <table border="1" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="2" width="491">
       <tr>
        <td colspan="2">
         <span style="font-size : xx-large;">
          The Truth
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="font-size : x-large;">
          (to within 3 or 4 significant digits)
         </span>
        </td>
        <td colspan="2">
         <span style="font-size : xx-large;">
          What you can remember
          <br/>
         </span>
         (You'll be about 10% too short.)
        </td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
        <td width="16%">
         <div>
          1 meter
         </div>
        </td>
        <td width="32%">
         = 3.280 feet
         <br/>
         = 1.094 yards
        </td>
        <td width="11%">
         <div>
          1 meter
         </div>
        </td>
        <td width="41%">
         ~= 3 feet
         <br/>
         ~= 1 yard
        </td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
        <td width="16%">

         <div>
          100 m
         </div>
        </td>

        </td>
