                <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" style="">
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   Name:
                  </strong>
                  Preston Kendig
                  <br/>
                  <strong>
                   Role:
                  </strong>
                  Client Account Coordinator, Beekeeper, Millennial
                  <br/>
                  <strong>
                   Born:
                  </strong>
                  Richmond, Virginia
                  <br/>
                  <strong>
                   Industry Specialties:
                  </strong>
                  Websites, Content Marketing, Blog Writing, Analytics and Results Tracking, New Business
                  <br/>
                  <strong>
                   Education:
                  </strong>
                  BA in History, JMU
                  <br/>
                  <strong>
                   Hobbies:
                  </strong>
                  Beekeeping, Spear Fishing, Barbecuing, Cooking, Anything Outdoors!
                 </p>
                 <!--more-->
                 <p>
                 </p>
                 <div class="caption">
                  <img alt="preston kendig impression marketing" class="wp-image-10047" height="306" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/523053/file-2968188362-jpg/blog-files/preston-cate-blue-448x600.jpg?t=1436814104432&amp;width=229&amp;height=306" width="229"/>
                  Preston, his wife Cate and dog Blue
                 </div>
                 <p>
                  As a millennial and one of the younger members of our team, the unflappable Preston injects a lot of exuberance into everything he does, which is such an interesting paradox for a guy with such an even-tempered personality. But dont let that fool you. If he has an idea, he gets excited about it, dives in and becomes totally immersed. He has his analytical days and his creative days, flip flopping back and forth comfortably between these left-brain and right-brain initiatives. He loves people, networking, meeting with clients and has a great work ethic. He's a refreshing millennial in that he still likes to wear a suit (not required here) and write hand written thank you notes.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  His extraordinary hobbies such as beekeeping and spear fishing make this Eagle Scout an interesting human being indeed. No client topic is boring to Preston as he delves into B2B and B2C business with full force in an ongoing effort to always be a more effective marketer to better serve his clients. Preston brings, above all else, a dedication to his clients that is beyond reproach. When Preston takes on a new client, he makes their passion his own.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  The interesting thing about working at Impression Marketing is how much Ive learned since I came on board and how that knowledge translates back into my own life. When a kitchen sink clogged at a dinner party, I asked the host for a plunger and a rag and, to the dismay of all, unclogged the drain in mere seconds. To those that begged how I knew this trick, all I could tell them was to start following our plumbing clients blog, commented Preston.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Creating good content for a client takes dedication and passion, as well as the ability to stop, review results and adjust accordingly. Preston demonstrates all of these qualities as he works diligently to create content for his clients that drives leads, improves SEO and helps convert leads into sales. He is also passionate about sharing with prospective clients the positive impact that digital marketing can have on their business, and helps find those that would benefit most from Impression Marketing's services.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Preston spends his free time in the great outdoors with his wife Cate and dog Blue. From hiking and kayaking, to hunting ducks, Preston is always up for an adventure, but never misses an opportunity to knock the tennis ball around with Cate or Blue! If you catch him on a rainy day, Preston welcomes challengers to the games of backgammon or foosball.
                 </p>
