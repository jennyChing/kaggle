       <div style="display:block">
        <div style="display:block">
         A few years back, I started to find success as a pumpkin carver. I made a website called
         <a href="http://www.extremepumpkins.com" target="_blank">
          ExtremePumpkins.com
         </a>
         and started to write some books. It was fun, but then when October was over I was lonely and bored (not really). I decided to work on something else. That something else was Gingerbread Houses. My friends and I created some really silly ones and I made a website about it called
         <a href="http://www.gingerbreadghetto.com" target="_blank">
          GingerbreadGhetto.com
         </a>
         . I suggest that you visit it for a laugh. I've also included a couple of the houses here as well.
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          <img alt="Gingerbread ghetto" height="429" src="images/front-view_large.jpg" style="border-top-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; border-top-style: solid; border-right-style: solid; border-bottom-style: solid; border-left-style: solid; " title="Gingerbread ghetto" width="500"/>
         </div>
         <div>
          This was my attempt at a Gingerbread Liquor Store. I guess I was tired of cutesy gingerbread houses and decided to make something a little bit darker.
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          <img alt="Injured Gingerbread Man" height="588" src="images/gingerbread-leg-cast_large.jpg" style="border-top-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; border-top-style: solid; border-right-style: solid; border-bottom-style: solid; border-left-style: solid; " title="Injured Gingerbread Man" width="500"/>
         </div>
         <div>
          I used a gingerbread man kit to make this one. Rather than cute buttons and leiderhosen I went for the leg cast and bandages.
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          <img alt="Gingerbread arson" height="464" src="images/fire-done_large.jpg" style="border-top-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; border-top-style: solid; border-right-style: solid; border-bottom-style: solid; border-left-style: solid; " title="Gingerbread arson" width="500"/>
         </div>
         <div>
          GingerbreadGhetto.com featured an entire arson scene with this gingerbread house. I like burning stuff and this one looked great after the fire was done.
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          <img alt="The Gingerbread Decaptitator" height="531" src="images/decapitator_large.jpg" style="border-top-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; border-top-style: solid; border-right-style: solid; border-bottom-style: solid; border-left-style: solid; " title="The Gingerbread Decaptitator" width="500"/>
         </div>
         <div>
          One of personal favorites was this use of a gingerbread man making kit. This was so very easy to make and has a great look. I hope you will consider making one. I call him "the decapitator".
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          <img alt="Gingerbread peep show" height="406" src="images/peep-show-boss_large.jpg" style="border-top-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; border-top-style: solid; border-right-style: solid; border-bottom-style: solid; border-left-style: solid; " title="Gingerbread peep show" width="500"/>
         </div>
         <div>
          My friend Gabby kicks ass. I paid her an incredibly small amount of money and she made me this gingerbread peep show complete with signs and money-holding, angry gingerbread men. Friends are a wonderful thing.
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          Check out
          <a href="http://www.gingerbreadghetto.com" target="_blank">
           GingerbreadGhetto.com
          </a>
          for more silly gingerbread stuff or Check out my company
          <a href="http://www.priveco.com">
           PriveCo
          </a>
          where we shot all of these photographs.
