           <p>
            These little rolls are so easy and delicious!
            <br/>
            All you need to make these are a few simple ingredients. To make the crepes, you just need some eggs, milk, flour, and butter. Ive included a crepe recipe below so that you have enough batter for this
            <a href="http://www.allfood.recipes/nutella-banana-crepes/">
             recipe
            </a>
            , but if you need a refresher on making crepes, just go take a look at my crepes post.
           </p>
           <h3>
            <span style="color: #800000">
             Ham And Cheese Crepes
            </span>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Ingredients
            </strong>
            <br/>
            <em>
             Crepes
            </em>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             1 cup all purpose flour
            </li>
            <li>
             2 eggs
            </li>
            <li>
             1/2 cup milk
            </li>
            <li>
             1/2 cup water
            </li>
            <li>
             1/4 teaspoon salt
            </li>
            <li>
             2 tablespoons butter, melted
            </li>
           </ul>
           <!-- A generated by theme -->
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

           <p>
            <em>
             Filling
            </em>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             8 ham, sliced
            </li>
            <li>
             8 cheese, sliced
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Directions
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ol>
            <li>
             In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs.
            </li>
            <li>
             Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
            </li>
            <li>
             Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
            </li>
            <li>
             Pour the batter onto the griddle, using 1/4 cup for each crepe.
            </li>
            <li>
             Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
            </li>
            <li>
             Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side.
            </li>
            <li>
             Once all crepes are made, put a slice of ham and cheese onto each center, fold sides in then roll up.
            </li>
            <li>
             Place rolled crepes back on hot pan, cook until crispy on each side.
            </li>
           </ol>
