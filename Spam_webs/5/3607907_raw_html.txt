        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8207" data-jpibfi-description="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch?         <p>
         This is a doozy of a post. Its jammed packed with goodness and goodies. We have a review of an awesome food processor from Hamilton Beach. We have a giveaway of said food processor. And then a recipe for an
         <strong>
          open face black bean cake breakfast sandwich
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets get started.
         <span id="more-8183">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <h2>
         <img alt="Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review and Giveaway" class="aligncenter wp-image-8200 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review and Giveaway" height="980" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-1.jpg" width="680"/>
        </h2>
        <p>
         First off, my
         <strong>
          review of the
          <a href="http://www.hamiltonbeach.com/food-processors-stack-and-snap-10-cup-food-processor-70720.html" target="_blank">
           Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor
          </a>
          .
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Loved it. Not only did I use it to make these black bean cakes, I also used it to make my
         <a href="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/roasted-red-pepper-white-bean-hummus/" title="Roasted Red Pepper and White Bean Hummus">
          red pepper and white bean hummus
         </a>
         . I used it to chop up veggies for a soup. And to grate my cheese for my
         <a href="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/black-bean-enchiladas-jalapeno-crema/" target="_blank" title="Black Bean Enchiladas with Jalapeno Crema">
          enchiladas
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         This baby got quite a workout from me the past month or so  and is still going strong. The only thing that may be difficult for the average user is snapping the lid closed. The first few times, I had to really put the elbow grease into it. But gradually, it became easier and easier to close. If you have trouble closing the lid snaps, try removing the food pusher first and then try again.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review and Giveaway" class="aligncenter wp-image-8198 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review and Giveaway" height="1061" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-3.jpg" width="680"/>
         <img alt="Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review and Giveaway" class="aligncenter wp-image-8199 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Hamilton Beach Stack and Snap 10 Cup Food Processor Review and Giveaway" height="984" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-2.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you are ready to use it, youll notice that the use instructions are pretty cut and dry and it would be pretty hard to mess up with this baby. No speeds to worry about  the speeds are translated instead into what you are actually doing. Slicing. Shredding. Pureeing. Mixing.
        </p>
        <p>
         And it does all of those well. Way more more power than I expected from a budget machine. Oh, did I forget to mention that? Not only is this food processor a power house, its also very reasonably priced
         <a href="http://www.hamiltonbeach.com/food-processors-stack-and-snap-10-cup-food-processor-70720.html" target="_blank">
          for $59.99 on the Hamilton Beach website
         </a>
         (this price is current as of 12/17/14.) This food processor totally has my recommendation.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lucky for you. Im giving one away, and just before Christmas too!
        </p>

        <h2>
         Open Face Black Bean Cake Breakfast Sandwich Recipe
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" class="aligncenter wp-image-8190 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" height="949" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-11.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         So weve had a review. And a giveaway. Now it is time for that recipe!
        </p>
        <p>
         And this breakfast baby is a doozy.
        </p>
        <p>
         You start off with black bean cakes mixed in the food processor, which I based off of crab cakes. I left out the mayo, because black beans and mayo just sounds gross to me. Instead, I use olive oil  just enough to hold the ingredients together, but not so much that it is nasty.
        </p>
        <p>
         I started with the cakes, and planned to stop there. But Im not the only one in my household with food weirdness. Mr. B didnt have to say it  I knew that he was going to want more than a black bean cake for breakfast.
         <em>
          (Oh, wait, thats not weird?)
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" class="aligncenter wp-image-8195 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" height="1013" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-6.jpg" width="680"/>
         <img alt="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" class="aligncenter wp-image-8194 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" height="1055" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-7.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         What better way to maximize the awesomeness of black bean cakes than to sandwich one between a piece of toast (carbs! carbs! carbs!) and some of my favorite condiments.
        </p>
        <p>
         A little more cilantro, a pinch of green onion, a dash of salsa and some sour cream  I mixed a little sriracha in it for some added kick. Brunch is served.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" class="aligncenter wp-image-8191 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch? Try this recipe for open face black bean cake breakfast sandwiches! | www.honeyandbirch.com | #brunch #breakfast #vegetarian" height="874" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/black-bean-cakes-10.jpg" width="680"/>
         <img alt="Looking for a delicious recipe for an at-home brunch?         </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Black Bean Cakes
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, separated
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 14.5-16 ounce can black beans, rinsed and drained
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 tablespoons diced green onions
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tablespoons diced cilantro
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons minced garlic
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup grated Parmesan cheese
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup panko bread crumbs
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Sandwiches
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 fried eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 pieces of toast
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 cooked black bean cakes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            diced cilantro
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            diced green onion
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            salsa
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            sour cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            salt and pepper
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In the bowl of a food processor, puree the black beans until most of the beans are chopped. You don't want it to be hummus texture - you want it to have lumps. Then add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, green onions, cilantro, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and bread crumbs. Pulse until ingredients are well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pour out the mixture onto a piece of parchment paper. Separate the mixture into 6 even portions. To do this, cut in half, then cut the halves in thirds. Form each portion of black bean mixture into a burger shape.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat the remaining olive oil over medium high heat in a skillet. Fry the black bean cakes on each side for 5-6 minutes, or until the black bean cakes are crispy on the outside. Cook no more than 2 at a time. Place on a plate, lined with a paper towel, after cooking.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           To Assemble Sandwich
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place a cooked black bean cake on top of a piece of toast. Lay a fried egg, yolk up, on top of the black bean cake. Put a dash of salsa and sour cream on egg, then sprinkle green onion and cilantro on top. Add salt and pepper to taste.
           </li>
          </ol>
