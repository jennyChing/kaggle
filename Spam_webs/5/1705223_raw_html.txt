          <p>
           Last week Floris already pointed out that
           <a href="http://rwrant.co.za/floris-exciting-films-coming-2015/" title="Floris Most Exciting films coming in 2015">
            there are great films coming out in 2015
           </a>
           and one of those films he mentioned was Marvels
           <em>
            Ant-Man
           </em>
            yesterday the much-anticipated trailer for this film was released online.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://rwrant.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Ant-Man1.jpg">
            <img alt="Ant-Man" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-40600" height="400" src="http://rwrant.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Ant-Man1-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Ant-Man
           </em>
           stars Paul Rudd, Evangeline Lilly, Corey Stoll, Bobby Cannavale, Michael Pea, Judy Greer, Tip T.I Harris, David Dastmalchian, Wood Harris, Jordi Molland Michael Douglas and is the 12th film that will be released in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The film is based on a character that has appeared in several fictional Marvel books over the years.
          </p>
          <p>
           Armed with a super-suit with the astonishing ability to shrink in scale but increase in strength, con-man
           <em>
            Scott Lang
           </em>
           (Paul Rudd) must embrace his inner hero and help his mentor, Dr. Hank Pym, plan and pull off a heist that will save the world.
          </p>
          <p>
           I have to admit that I wasnt aware of this character in the Marvel Universe, but I have to say that the trailer below looks pretty damn awesome. Its just feels a little strange seeing Paul Rudd outside his comedic comfort zone
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/xInh3VhAWs8" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           So, what do you think of this trailer? Are you rushing off to the cinema in July to watch Ant-Man or are you waiting until it gets released on DVD / Blu-ray? Leave a comment and let me know what you think. Feedback is appreciated and welcome here.
