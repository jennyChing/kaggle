     <span class="s1">
      <strong>

     </span>

     </span>

     <span class="s1">
      <strong>

      <strong>

      </strong>
      my family. The most important things in my life.

      <br/>
     </span>

     <strong>
      Wearing:
     </strong>
     my lululemons. typical uniform. I cant get enough!
    </p>

     <span class="s1">
      <strong>
       Following:
      </strong>
      Instagramers who post drool worthy handbagsand awesome food pics.
     </span>

      </strong>
      happy and content with life lately. Its a good thing

     </span>
    </p>
