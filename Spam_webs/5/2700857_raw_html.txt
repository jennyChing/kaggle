       <h1>
        10 Healthy Soup Recipes to Keep You Warm This Winter
       </h1>
       <div>
        <p>
         by
         <a href="http://my10online.com/author/sparadero/" rel="author" title="Posts by Samantha">
          Samantha
         </a>

         <a href="http://my10online.com/2015/01/10-healthy-soup-recipes-keep-warm/#comments">

        <br/>
        <p class="cunlock_first_paragraph">
         <a href="http://my10online.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/chicken-pho.jpg">
          <img alt="chicken pho" class="size-full wp-image-14479 aligncenter" height="373" src="http://my10online.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/chicken-pho.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         When its absolutely
         <em>
          freezing
         </em>
         outside, theres nothing better than sitting comfortably at home and cozyingup to a big bowl of steamingno wait,
         <em>
          scalding
         </em>
         hot soup.
        </p>
        <p>
         To help keep you warm and cozy, here are 10 healthy soup recipes for you to try:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Who doesnt love chicken noodle soup, right? You can never go wrong with this classic, comfort food soup.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/45WXFbSyGm4?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2.Clean Eating Broccoli Stem Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont throw out those broccoli stemsuse them in this delicious, super easy soup instead.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ZeiNqR4rijk?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3. Healthy Chicken Pho
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Are you a fan of Vietnamese pho? Heres an easy, healthy alternative that is absolutely guilt-free.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/3fzVY5Qu7DE?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4. Minestrone Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe makes use of a slow cooker, so making it
         <em>
          couldnt
         </em>
         be easierjust dump all your ingredients into the pot and let the slow cooker do its thing!
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Ly7pImm9DEI?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5.Easy Cauliflower Cheddar Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Creamy, cheesy, and amazing!
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/DrVgVucFTYo?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          6. Clean Eating Buffalo Chicken Chili
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Buffalo wings in a soup! Perfect for Super Bowl Sunday!
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Ou2k_P6dEMs?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          7.Roasted Butternut Squash Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         For the butternut squash lovers out there.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/rmpWkys1UEk?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          8.Immunity Boosting Green Chicken + Veggie Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Got the winter sniffles? Heres a soup thatll help boost your immunity and make you feel better a whole lot faster.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/d-MTiXBCCHc?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          9. 15-Minute Egg Drop Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         For when youre hankeringfor some Chinese food.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/eAz7adXS5rc?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          10. Garlic Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Garlic has a lot of great health benefits, but its also especially good for when you have a cold.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="375" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/b1P5HktDWWE?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
