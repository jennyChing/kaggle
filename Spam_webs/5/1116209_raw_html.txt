                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Portobello Stuffed with Creamy Scrambled Eggs
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8395747283330991878" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-msTFR3QhM6o/VIozrrRhCjI/AAAAAAAAGf0/ZC9RjUeFvps/s1600/portobello%2Bstuffed%2Bwith%2Bscrambled%2Beggs%2Bsigned.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Portobello Stuffed with Creamy Scrambled Eggs" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-msTFR3QhM6o/VIozrrRhCjI/AAAAAAAAGf0/ZC9RjUeFvps/s1600/portobello%2Bstuffed%2Bwith%2Bscrambled%2Beggs%2Bsigned.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <em>
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif;'>
                       <span style="color: black;">
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </em>
                     <br/>
                     <em>
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif;'>
                       <span style="color: black;">
                        I love it when I can make breakfast that feeds 6 people, all at the same time! Then everyone gets to eat a hot breakfast all together, including me! This is also a 'make ahead' meal that comes together quickly once the mushrooms are cooked!
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </em>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <em>
                      <span style='font-family: Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif;'>
                       <span style="color: black;">
                        I think portobello mushrooms taste similar to steak when prepared this way. So I guess you can say this is 'faux steak and eggs'! And it goes perfectly with my recipe for
                       </span>
                       '
                       <a href="http://myrecipejourney-lillian.blogspot.com/2012/07/fried-green-potatoesnot-tomatoes.html" target="_blank">
                        <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                         Fried Green Potatoes...Not Tomatoes
                        </span>
                       </a>
                       <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                        '
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </em>
                     <br/>
                     <em>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000; font-family: Georgia;">
                      </span>
                     </em>
                     <br/>
                     
                     <br/>
                     
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-8EyyknEBRvY/UifMdlUNoyI/AAAAAAAADsw/pSljp41E1XI/s1600/portobello+stuffed+with+scrambled+eggs+signed.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img alt="Portobello Stuffed with Creamy Scrambled Eggs" border="0" height="213" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-8EyyknEBRvY/UifMdlUNoyI/AAAAAAAADsw/pSljp41E1XI/s320/portobello+stuffed+with+scrambled+eggs+signed.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                         <span style='font-family: Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif; font-size: small;'>
                          <em>
                           Portobello Stuffed with Creamy Scrambled Eggs
                          </em>
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <em>
                          <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          </span>
                         </em>
                         <br/>
                         <u>
                          <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                           INGREDIENTS:
                          </span>
                         </u>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          6 five inch diameter Portobello mushrooms
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          olive oil
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          3 cloves garlic, minced
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          12 large eggs
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          4 Tbls. grated Parmesan cheese
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          2 tsp.chopped freshparsley or rosemary
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          3/4 tsp. kosher salt
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          1/2 tsp. black pepper
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          6 Tbls. butter
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <u>
                          <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                           DIRECTIONS:
                          </span>
                         </u>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Preheat broiler to 500 degrees F.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Place ovenrack about 5 inches from heating element.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Line a baking sheet with foil.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Brush both sides of mushrooms with olive oil.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Place mushrooms, gill side up,on baking sheet.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Sprinkle mushrooms with salt, pepper and garlic.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Bake mushrooms for about 5 minutes or until they start to soften.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Then turn mushrooms over and bake for another 5-7 minutes.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          The mushrooms can be made a few hours ahead and re-warmed when ready to fill.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          If serving immediately, keep warm until eggs are cooked.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          <u>
                           Make the Scrambled Eggs
                          </u>
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Whisk eggs with Parmesan cheese, parsley, 3/4 tsp. salt and1/2 tsp. black pepper in a large bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add egg mixture and stir gently with a rubber spatula until eggs start to cook. Keep doing this until all of the egg mixture is cooked but still soft. Do not let the eggs brown.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
                          Serve the warmmushrooms, gill side up, with equal amounts of scrambled eggs in each.
                         </span>
                         <br/>
