              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: purple; font-family: Verdana;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 The pattern reads like this....
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: black;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 Row 1
                 <b>
                  :
                 </b>
                 Using
                 <b>
                  both loops,
                 </b>
                 sc in 2nd ch from hook and in each of next 9 ch; sc in each of next 20 (25 - 25) sc; sc in each ch on opposite side of beg ch; ch 1, turn.
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: black; font-family: Verdana;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                  You will sc down one side of the ch 11 .... around the foot.... and back up the other side of the ch11
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-9Y2tydpBqC0/UMqh-MxcI5I/AAAAAAAAFlM/UJMvItnrS-I/s1600/continued+heel.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img bea="true" border="0" height="240" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-9Y2tydpBqC0/UMqh-MxcI5I/AAAAAAAAFlM/UJMvItnrS-I/s320/continued+heel.JPG" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-sfeyjPqWiBE/UMqiNz8AllI/AAAAAAAAFlU/1cSvWmjVgoI/s1600/finished+first+row+of+heel.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img bea="true" border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-sfeyjPqWiBE/UMqiNz8AllI/AAAAAAAAFlU/1cSvWmjVgoI/s320/finished+first+row+of+heel.JPG" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: black; font-family: Verdana;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                  And now in back loop only you will turn and sc back down to the foot... around and back up.... it will start to look like this............
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/--MutmLoSbm8/UMqi9WH3hcI/AAAAAAAAFlc/BnRuFIiFFig/s1600/Heel+half+done.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img bea="true" border="0" height="240" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/--MutmLoSbm8/UMqi9WH3hcI/AAAAAAAAFlc/BnRuFIiFFig/s320/Heel+half+done.JPG" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: black; font-family: Verdana;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                  work in this manner for the rows required for the size you're making......
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-mW2DRWWYxOY/UMqjXQWOwlI/AAAAAAAAFlk/QQokGxo950A/s1600/last+row+of+heel+before+dec.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img bea="true" border="0" height="240" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-mW2DRWWYxOY/UMqjXQWOwlI/AAAAAAAAFlk/QQokGxo950A/s320/last+row+of+heel+before+dec.JPG" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: black; font-family: Verdana;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                  Coming to the end ..... slip stitching it close...... and you're done.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Vjs0ac1MAxo/UMqknrRRm_I/AAAAAAAAFls/QbC8LCOipQQ/s1600/last+row+of+dec+on+heel.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img bea="true" border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Vjs0ac1MAxo/UMqknrRRm_I/AAAAAAAAFls/QbC8LCOipQQ/s320/last+row+of+dec+on+heel.JPG" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: black; font-family: Verdana;">
                <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <span style="color: purple; font-size: 12pt;">
                  And there ya go............ make a second one and now you have a wonderful pair of slippers to keep your feet warm!!!
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="color: purple; font-family: Verdana;">
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: purple; font-family: Verdana;">
                I sure hope this helped. If you have more questions... please ask.. I'd be happy to answer them!
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: purple; font-family: Verdana;">
                Off to get my boys movin' for the day!
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
               <span style="color: purple; font-family: Verdana;">
                Its biscuits and gravy morning at school.... YUM!!!
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">
