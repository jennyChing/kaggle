Since our fragrances are so strong, we suggest only using 1/2 ounce to 1.0 ounce of fragrance per pound of candle wax.

Coloring Suggestions: Chocolate Con Churros Wholesale Fragrance Oil:



			Candles

			Bath/Body Products
			
			
			No color
