      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       The festival seasonn has just begun and it is incomplete without a touch of all girls favourite Mehndi.
       <span id="more-44332">
       </span>
       While most prefer buying the ready made Mehndi cones, many still prefer going the traditional ritual of gathering the fresh Mehndi leaves and grinding them to a fine paste with a different mix of ingredients to attain that beautiful color which makes the pretty hands look prettier. Getting the right color is as important as selecting the right designs from the new Mehndi designs forwinter 2014 like always. Whether you choose your design inspired from the Pakistani, Arabic, African or Indian it must compliment your dressing and the festival. If you are still learning and want to know different styles of Mehndi to develop beautiful new
       <a href="http://fashionelan.com/tag/mehndi-designs/" target="_blank" title="mehndi designs">
        Mehndi designs
       </a>
       forwinter 2014 here we go:
      </p>
      <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
       Pakistani Mehndi Style
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_0010.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44342" data-lazy-src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_0010.jpg" src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif"/>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44342" src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_0010.jpg"/>
        </noscript>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       Take a Look at
       <strong>
        <a href="http://fashionelan.com/2013/04/25/amelia-mehndi-designs-2013-for-girls/" target="_blank" title="amelia mehndi designs">
         Amelia Mehndi Designs
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       These are complex combinations of Arabic and Indian style designs. Pakistani Mehndi designs are very detail oriented and fine as compared to others. Mehndi is wide used during festivals and is a must have during marriage ceremonies. Brides adorn intricate patterns on both hands it can be till the elbow or full upper and lower arms. Ladies take hours to select the best design combinations and sit equally long hours to get the design right on their beautiful hands.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       If you are looking for intricate Pakistani
       <a href="http://fashionelan.com/tag/henna-patterns/" target="_blank" title="mehndi styles">
        Mehndi Style
       </a>
       here is a beautiful design from the new winter Mehndi designs, which flows from your palms to the back of your hand. The design is intricate and best suited for new brides or about to be brides. This is an exclusive design style, make sure to choose the best organic Mehndi to get this style perfect. Allow yourself to be free for a few hours as this design will require much of your time and patience both. But is the worth al considering the unique after effect.
      </p>
      <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
       Indian Mehndi Style
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_009.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44341" data-lazy-src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_009.jpg" src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif"/>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44341" src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_009.jpg"/>
        </noscript>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       The stunning New
       <strong>
        <a href="http://fashionelan.com/2013/07/24/new-mehndi-designs-2013-for-eid/" target="_blank" title="Henna pattern ">
         HennaPattern
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Indian design styles are very diverse and are most popular for their versatility and room to combine in an end number of ways including the choice of motifs. These designs can be simplified or made intricate as the occasion demands. The typical Indian style would include cover your finger tips with Mehndi too. This gives a very beautiful appearance. It also colours your nails, which takes some time to fade off. But it can be avoided by simply applying a layer of transparent enamel, just incase you have some different enamel color plan for your nails.
      </p>
      <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
       Arabic Mehndi Styles
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_001.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44333" data-lazy-src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_001.jpg" src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif"/>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44333" src="http://fashionelan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/New-Mehndi-Designs-for-winter-2014_001.jpg"/>
        </noscript>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       Take a look some
       <a href="http://fashionelan.com/2013/10/04/indian-bridal-mehndi-designs-2013-for-girls/" target="_blank" title="wedding mehndi designs">
        <strong>
         Wedding HennaDesigns
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Compared to Pakistani or Indian style Mehndi, Arabic Mehndi design styles are easier and faster to apply. The designs mostly comprise of flower patterns and wines which run through creatively in your hands. Arabic Styles are all time favorites among young girls and a few are so fond of them that they dont mind applying all year round. The designs are simple and few tattoo style designs look cool irrespective of the occasion.
      </p>
      <p>
       Try to combine and create new Winter Mehndi designs and look gorgeous. Also make sure you pick the best Mehndi cones available in the market and avoid falling prey to chemical based ones which does give a great color but urns your upper layer of skin. Its a great festive time of the year and as like any occasion calls for little precautions.
      </p>
