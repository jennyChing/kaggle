        <p id="farm_description_abstract">
        </p>
        <div>
         <span class="fontSize3" style="font-family: comic sans ms,sans-serif;">
          Covey Rise Farms is located near Husser, Louisiana in central Tangipahoa Parish and is part of the
          <a href="http://www.coveyriselodge.com/">
           Covey RiseSportsmans Retreat
          </a>
          . The Farm started growing vegetables on 10 acres in 2009 and hasexpanded to 50 acres for the 2013 season. Our vegetables are enjoyed at over 40 of the New Orleans area finestrestaurants and by our Local Produce Club/CSA members. We grow multiple varieties of over 30 types of vegetablesthroughout our growing season which is year round. Our Local Produce Club/CSA runs in the spring and fall during heartof the growing season and includes both cool and warm weather crops. We grow conventionally under the directionof a retired LSU Agriculture Professor, who acts as our crop consultant. In 2012 we started nearby Chappapeela Farmswhich is the coolest little duck farm in the south, raising the prettiest hogs around.
         </span>
        </div>
