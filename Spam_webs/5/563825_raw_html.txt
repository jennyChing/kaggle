             <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" style="">
              <p dir="ltr">
               <img alt="how-your-customers-perceive-ads-on-google" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hubfs/408188/blog-files/how-your-customers-perceive-ads-on-google.jpg?t=1435596501774"/>
              </p>
              <p dir="ltr">
               <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
                In 2012,
               </span>
               <a href="http://www.bunnyfoot.com/blog/2014/05/user-testing-is-key-to-understanding-people/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
                BunnyFoot
               </a>
               <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
                (a usability and user experience design firm) performed a study to see if users could differentiate between paid ads and organic search results when using Googles search function. In the end, researchers found that 40% of the participants could not see a difference when looking at ads on Google.
               </span>
              </p>
              <!--more-->
              <p dir="ltr">
               <img alt="googlesearch.jpg" height="216px;" src="https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/jPX5WefOsrS1Kws1ekDdliWe4us94h0eNO-fPKCfue_mMrS4KobNxWyWeTfmFmwtsKtfHiryMtUYP-W2gZY7bfS4Fxrn0MljxGkOKDpnjJFvgqJIc6yOZicQ6WwQXWvkHw" width="624px;"/>
              </p>
              <div>
               <p dir="ltr">
                Between then and now, Google AdWords has modified their format slightly (as you can see in the infographic above). Therefore, BunnyFoot decided to redo their study to see if the results changed.
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr">
                Through a second study, researchers still found that two years later approximately one-third of regular Google Search users did not recognize the difference between a paid ad and an organic result. Further, slightly less than one-third of participants did not even know that Google used any form of advertising on their search page. The difference between then and now is only a slight decrease from 41% to 36% of participants not recognizing the ads.
               </p>
               <h3>
                What does this mean for your business?
               </h3>
               <p dir="ltr">
                <a href="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/408188/file-1132136567-jpg/blog-files/ad1-300x298.jpg?t=1435596501774">
                 <img alt="ad" class="size-medium wp-image-4305 wp-float-right" height="298" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/408188/file-1132136567-jpg/blog-files/ad1-300x298.jpg?t=1435596501774&amp;width=300&amp;height=298" width="300"/>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr">
                Because advertisements could possibly be misleading, it is important for the user to understand which results are being paid for and which results they are naturally receiving. As the most important information or most popular search result typically appears at the top of the page, having the ads naturally fit in here can be confusing for users. When Google is seamlessly blending the advertisements in with search results, it takes an extra moment to recognize the yellow Ad box and the (i) symbol next to the result (both of these are highlighted in orange on the image to the right).
               </p>
               <div>
                This shows that only using one strategy to market your business is not holistically effective. If you only display an ad, then two-thirds of Google search users will recognize that and carry onto an organic result. If you only utilize SEO, then one-third of Google search users will see an ad as a search result before they see your organic result. Therefore, in recognizing that we do not always understand how a customer interacts with websites and search engines, we can adjust accordingly.
               </div>

                 <img class="wp-float-left" height="254px;" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/RdBrWskJPtsLVavAFvYkCaTmCqiZ36Jq45zaNfufY78Ih7Axa7eEETc_CXMeJYds7nl04a5uTbS8LVAEGny5gtYnj-T2Tj5GqQpRF508NOyzavsofOtE-PcDdoV4ISPEsw" width="279px;"/>
                </div>
