               The Fish Lake also knows her seasonal cycles.
              </span>
              <span title="Il est situ entre deux volcans : le Mont McLoughlin au nord, et Brown Mountain au sud.
">
               It is located between two volcanoes: Mount McLoughlin north and Brown Mountain to the south.
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>

           <div class="ob-section ob-section-html">
            <p>
             <span id="result_box" lang="en">
              <span title="Brown Mountain est responsable de coules de lave basalto-andsitiques, anciennes de 2.000 ans, qui entourent le rive sud de Fish Lake.

">
               <span style="color:#FFA500;">
                Brown Mountain
               </span>
               is responsible of flows of basalto-andesitic lava, 2,000 years old, around the southern shore of Fish Lake.
              </span>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <span title="Le Mont McLoughlin est un cne aux parois abruptes construit au sommet d'un volcan-bouclier.">
               The
               <span style="color:#FFA500;">
                Mount McLoughlin
               </span>
               is a cone with steep walls built on top of a shield volcano.
              </span>
              <span title="Le volcan apparat il y a moins de 700 000 ans.">
               The volcano appears there are less than 700 000 years.
              </span>
              <span title="Trois tapes caractrisent son activit ruptive.">
               Three steps characterize its eruptive activity.
              </span>
              <span title="La premire se distingue par une priode explosive qui forme un cne volcanique dont la hauteur remarquable approche les 900 mtres.">
               The first is characterized by an explosive period that forms a volcanic cone whose remarkable heights approaching 900 meters.
              </span>
              <span title="La prsence d'un volcan bouclier ancestral sous ce cne pourrait expliquer cette anomalie.">
               The presence of an ancestral shield inder the cone could explain this anomaly.
              </span>
              <span title="Au cours de la deuxime tape, vers 200 000 ans BP, des coules de lave andsitique sont mises depuis le sommet du cne et l'ensevelissent.">
               During the second stage, around 200 000 years BP, of andesitic lava flows are emitted from the top of the cone and bury it.
              </span>
              <span title="La troisime tape se caractrise par des missions d'andsitique visqueuse depuis les abords du sommet, comme en tmoignent les anciennes bouches ruptives de North et South Squaw Tip sur le versant occidental, et de laves plus fluides depuis la base du volcan, essentiellement au sud">
               The third stage is characterized by viscous andesitic emissions from near the summit, as evidenced by the old eruptive vents of North and South Squaw Tip on the western side, and most fluid lava from the base of the volcano, mainly south
              </span>
              <span title=".">
               .
              </span>
              <span title="Les coules de lave les plus rcentes ont entre 25 et 30 000 ans ;">
               The most recent lava flows are dated between 25 and 30 thousand years;
              </span>
              <span title="elles sont contemporaines de la dernire glaciation.



">
               they are contemporary with the last glaciation.
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
