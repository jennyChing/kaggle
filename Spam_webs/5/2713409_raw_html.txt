        </h1>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        The links in this post may be affiliate links.  Read the

        <a id="dd_start">

        <p>
         <br/>
         Banana Bread. Oh, the sweet smell of banana bread. My husband even talked about that wonderful smell as I was mixing up this recipe. After many tries, it finally came out as I remembered banana bread to be like - moist and delicious! Dont worry, no food was wasted during the trail period!
         <br/>
         <a href="http://wholefoodmomonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Banana-Bread-Gluten-Free-Sugar-Free.jpg">
          <img alt="Banana Bread {Gluten-Free, Sugar-Free}" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1546" height="743" src="http://wholefoodmomonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Banana-Bread-Gluten-Free-Sugar-Free-1024x743.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Did you know that the first banana bread recipe was published during the Great Depression?  They did not want to waste any overripe bananas during that difficult time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now let me tell you about this banana bread..you will want to eat a lot of it. So, if you have a big family, this recipe is ideal. Otherwise, you may want to half the recipe. Or, you could make these into muffins and freeze them for later.
         <br/>
        </p>

            </div>
            <div class="duration blog-yumprint-infobox-section-data" datetime="PT50H" itemprop="cookTime">
             50 hr
             <span class="value-title" title="PT50H">
             </span>
            </div>

           <div class="blog-yumprint-infobox-section">
            <div class="blog-yumprint-infobox-section-title">

            </div>
            <div class="duration blog-yumprint-infobox-section-data" datetime="PT60H" itemprop="totalTime">

             <span class="value-title" title="PT60H">
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="0">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
             1 3/4 cups oat flour (gluten-free if necessary)
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
             1 tsp. baking soda
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
             1/2 tsp. salt
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="3">
             1/3 cup Truvia or THM Sweet Blend
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="4">
             2 bananas (mashed very well)
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="5">
             1/4 cup applesauce
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="6">
             1/4 cup Greek yogurt
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="7">
             2 egg whites
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="8">
             1 tsp. vanilla
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="1">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
             Mix dry ingredients together, then add wet ingredients and mix well.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="2">
             Pour mixture into a sprayed or greased loaf pan.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="3">
             Bake for 50 to 60 minutes or until done.
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>

             This is a THM E recipe and one serving is 1/5 of a loaf.
