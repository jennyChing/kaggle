     <h1>
      How to Play a 12 Bar Blues
     </h1>
     <p align="left">
      <strong>
       12 Bar Blues
      </strong>
      by
      <a href="http://www.freeguitarvideos.com/authors/jody-worrell.html">
       Jody Worrell
      </a>
      is a free video guitar lesson for beginners teaching one of the basic fundamentals of blues rhythm. You'll learn how to play a 12 Bar Blues chord progression in the key of E. We'll first cover the chords that make up this progression (E7, A7, B7). Next, you'll learn the order and length to play each of the chords. Jody will play this chord progression with a shuffle rhythm and demonstrate along with a backing track. To get further into blues rhythm guitar, check out Jody's
      <a href="http://www.freeguitarvideos.com/LJ_Bl/101-rhythm.html">
       Blues 101 - Rhythm
      </a>
      premium lesson.
     </p>
