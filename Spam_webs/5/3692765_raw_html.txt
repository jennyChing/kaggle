          <p>

          <p>
           This is a poll asking Yankees fans or any baseball fans who was better Joe DiMaggio or Mickey Mantle Of course either one of these men could be considered the better of the two. Most of us never saw either DiMaggio or Mantle play but we can still vote on who was the better player.
          </p>
          <p>
           DiMaggio was a thinking man on defense and did a great deal of study on opposing batters. Mickey Mantle used his athleticism to get it done in the outfield. Joe DiMaggio missed 3 seasons while in the military so only played 13 years. Joe had 2,214 hits for a .324 batting average with 361 home runs and 1,537 RBIs.
          </p>
          <p>
           Mickey Mantle played for 18 years. Mantle had 2,415 hits with a .298 batting average, 536 home runs and 1,509 RBIs. I think Mantle was the better long ball hitter but DiMaggio was the better pure hitter. I also say Joe DiMaggio was better than Mickey Mantle as an all-around baseball player.
          </p>
