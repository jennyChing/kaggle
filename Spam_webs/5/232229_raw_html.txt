             <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div class="firstPar">
               <p>
                Survival rather than Europe was expected to be Southamptons priority when the season began and, with the same advanced planning that has underpinned their progress, they are already preparing for the probable departure of Morgan Schneiderlin.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="secondPar">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sport/football/teams/tottenham-hotspur/11617904/Spurs-consider-move-for-Manchester-United-and-Chelsea-target-Nathaniel-Clyne.html" id="11617904">
                   Spurs consider move for Nathaniel Clyne
                 </a>
                </strong>
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="thirdPar">
               <p>
                An offer has been made that would make Schneiderlin the clubs highest-paid player but, having weathered the loss of five key players, a manager and a chairman last year, there is confidence that whatever happens the squad will again emerge enhanced.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="fourthPar">
               <p>
                <img alttext="Morgan Schneiderlin - Arsenal transfer news and rumours: Arsene Wenger to make 25m bid for Saints' Morgan Schneiderlin" caption="Third time lucky? Morgan Schneiderlin, the Southampton midfielder, has been targeted by Arsenal. Again" declared-caption="" height="387" name="Morgan_Schneiderlin" photographer="" refid="3296464" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03296/Morgan_Schneiderli_3296464b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
                <br/>
                <sub>
                 <strong>
                  Morgan Schneiderlin appears to be on his way out of St Mary's
                 </strong>
                </sub>
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="fifthPar">
               <p>
                Tottenham Hotspur would still like to sign Schneiderlin but failed last summer with a bid of 10million and Arsenal are now in pole position for a deal that could be concluded soon after the season ends.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="body">
               <p>
                Southampton would love to build their team around Schneiderlin but there is an understanding that he will leave if a suitable offer emerges and the club have been watching potential replacements all season.
               </p>
               <p>
                Arsenal made Schneiderlin their summer transfer priority earlier in the year but any deal is likely to hinge on whether Arsne Wengers thinking has been changed by the emergence of Francis Coquelin. A valuation that is understood to be between 25million and 30million must also be met, with Southampton adamant that there is no financial pressure to sell.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <i>
                  Did you know Telegraph Sport has an
                  <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ArsenalTelegraph?fref=ts">
                   Arsenal Facebook page
                  </a>
                  ?
                 </i>
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" id="player1" scrolling="no" src="//www.facebook.com/plugins/likebox.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FArsenalTelegraph&amp;width&amp;height=62&amp;colorscheme=light&amp;show_faces=false&amp;header=true&amp;stream=false&amp;show_border=false&amp;appId=120118784736295" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; height:62px;">
                </iframe>
               </p>
               <p>
                Southampton are also now in talks with Atltico Madrid over a potential 10 million deal that would make Toby Alderweirelds loan permanent. The final decision rests with Atltico but, should they sell, Alderweireld has indicated a desire to stay with Southampton.
               </p>
               <p>
                Similarly, the situation with Nathaniel Clyne has become more positive. A big bid from Manchester United would change things but Southampton have been encouraged by their most recent talks and United have not yet made a direct inquiry. Clynes priority is to give himself the best chance of being Englands regular right-back ahead of next summers European Championship.
               </p>
               <p>
                Whether Southampton finish fifth  and so definitely clinch Europa League football, as well as their second-best league finish  is also expected to influence any talks next week. Should they stay seventh, they would still qualify for Europe if Arsenal won next Saturdays FA Cup final against Aston Villa.
               </p>
               <p>
                <img caption="Wanted man: Nathaniel Clyne has plenty of options of where to play his football" credit="Getty Images" declared-caption="" height="387" name="Clyne" photographer="Richard Heathcote" refid="3311400" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03311/Clyne_3311400b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
                <br/>
                <sub>
                 <strong>
                  Nathaniel Clyne is another one who might be leaving Southampton
                 </strong>
                </sub>
               </p>
               <p>
                Jay Rodriguez has also signed a new contract and is now fully recovered from his knee-ligament surgery. It was decided not to risk his comeback by asking him to play again this season but, with a full pre-season, he will feel like a new signing to manager Ronald Koeman. With Graziano Pell, Sadio Man and Dusan Tadic going into their second seasons in English football, there is confidence in the attacking options.
               </p>
               <p>
                Fraser Forsters knee surgery means another goalkeeper is the main priority beyond replacing Schneiderlin or Clyne. Kelvin Davis has a year left on his contract but is 38 and there would be reluctance to go into the season with Paulo Gazzaniga as first choice.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sport/football/competitions/premier-league/11621825/Premier-League-10-questions-for-the-final-day.html" id="11621825">
                   Premier League: 10 questions for the final day
                 </a>
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                The club have also been planning for the continued integration of leading academy players. There is particular confidence that full-back Matt Targett and central midfielder Harrison Reed can join James Ward-Prowse in being home-grown first-team regulars.
               </p>
               <p>
                There is also minimal doubt over the immediate position of Koeman, who says he is committed to the cycle of a contract that has two seasons to run. The clubs strength, however, has been how power has rested not with any manager but an off-field plan overseen by director Les Reed that combines a certain idealism in the development of young players with innovation at the training ground and pragmatism in the transfer market.
               </p>

        var readNextWidget = (function(){

            var settings = {
                articleImage: "",

                currentArticleId: "",

                panelOpen: false,

            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {

                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();
