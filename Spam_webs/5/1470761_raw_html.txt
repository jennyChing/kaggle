    <h2 class="cnbc-clearfix pagehead" id="page_header">
     <a class="header_title" href="/beer-wine-spirits/">
      Beer, Wine &amp; Spirits
     </a>
    </h2>
    <div class="story-top">
     <div class="story-header-left twoCol">
      <h1 class="title" itemprop="name">
       Cheers! Craft beer hits a new milestone
      </h1>
      <div class="source" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
       <a href="/tom-rotunno/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
        Tom Rotunno
       </a>
       |
       <span class="twitter-url">
        <a href="http://twitter.com/TomRotunno">
         @TomRotunno
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <time class="datestamp" datetime="2015-03-16T17:40:16-0400" itemprop="datePublished">
       Monday, 16 Mar 2015 |  5:40  PM ET
      </time>
      <span class="sourcelogo" id="sourcelogo-cnbccom" itemprop="sourceOrganization">
       CNBC.com
      </span>
     </div>
     <div class="story-header-right">
      <div>
       <div class="share-count-container hide">
        <div class="share-total">
        </div>
        <div class="share-text">
         SHARES
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="gigyasharebar" id="social_bar_top">
       </div>
      </div>
      <a data-comments-label="Article" data-disqus-identifier="cnbcnewsstory_102509019" href="#">
      </a>
     </div>
    </div>
    <section class="cols2">
     <div class="unit col1">
      <article>
       <div class="story">
        <div class="content" id="article_body">
         <div class="group-container ">
          <div class="player embed-container cnbcvideo autoplay">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="player" data-src="http://player.cnbc.com/cnbc_global?byGuid=3000362152&amp;autoPlay=true&amp;endCardGuids=3000383239|3000383217|3000383353" frameborder="0" height="298" id="player_0_0" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" oallowfullscreen="" scrolling="no" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="533">
            <p>
             This stock is up every St. Patrick's Day
            </p>
            <p>
             CNBC's Bob Pisani reports which beer stock could move around St. Patrick's Day.
            </p>
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            Chalk up another milestone for the American craft beer industry.
           </p>
           <p>
            According to the Brewers Association, craft brewers reached 11 percent volume share in 2014, marking the first time they've reached a double-digit share of the marketplace.
           </p>
           <p>
            That's up from 7.8 percent market share the previous year, with the category likely seeing a boost from the trade group's decision
            <a class="inline_asset" href="http://www.brewersassociation.org/press-releases/brewers-association-board-meeting-produces-strategic-changes/" target="_blank">
             to expand the definition of a small or craft brewer
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            It defines craft brewers as small, meaning they produce volume of about 3 percent of U.S. annual sales; independent, or less than 25 percent owned or controlled by a noncraft brewer; and traditional, meaning the majority of its alcohol volume comes from beer with traditional ingredients.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="group-container last">
          <div class="embed-container image" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <img alt="Cans of lager are filled at the Yuengling brewery in Pottsville, Pa." height="298" src="http://fm.cnbc.com/applications/cnbc.com/resources/img/editorial/2015/03/16/102509244-94860490.530x298.jpg?v=1426542113" width="530"/>
           <meta content="http://fm.cnbc.com/applications/cnbc.com/resources/img/editorial/2015/03/16/102509244-94860490.jpg?v=1426542113" itemprop="image"/>
           <meta content="width='530'" itemprop="width"/>
           <meta content="height='298'" itemprop="height"/>
           <meta content="2015-03-16T15:54:24-0400" itemprop="datePublished"/>
           <meta content="2015-03-16T15:53:33-0400" itemprop="dateCreated"/>
           <meta content="2015-03-16T17:41:53-0400" itemprop="dateModified"/>
           <meta content="2015-03-16T15:53:33-0400" itemprop="uploadDate"/>
           <meta content="2099-12-31" itemprop="expires"/>
           <!-- TODO: defaulting to 2099 until we get the expiry date from toolbelt -->
           <meta content="jpg" itemprop="encodingFormat"/>
           <meta content="Mike Mergen | Bloomberg | Getty Images" itemprop="author"/>
           <meta content="CNBC" itemprop="publisher"/>
           <meta content="Cans of lager are filled at the Yuengling brewery in Pottsville, Pa." itemprop="caption"/>
           <div class="attribution">
            Mike Mergen | Bloomberg | Getty Images
           </div>
           <div class="caption">
            Cans of lager are filled at the Yuengling brewery in Pottsville, Pa.
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            <a class="inline_asset" href="http://www.brewersassociation.org/press-releases/craft-brewer-volume-share-of-u-s-beer-market-reaches-double-digits-in-2014/" target="_blank">
             According to the data released Monday
            </a>
            , craft brewers produced 22.2 million barrels last year, representing an 18 percent increase in volume. They also saw a 22 percent rise in retail dollar value in 2014, coming in at an estimated $19.6 billion and accounting for 19.3 percent of the market.
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="label-read-more">
             Read More
            </span>
            <a href="/2015/03/16/sam-adams-top-beer-perception-survey.html" target="_blank">
             In battle of the beers, this brand is tops
            </a>
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p>
            "With the total beer market up only 0.5 percent in 2014, craft brewers are key in keeping the overall industry innovative and growing" said Bart Watson, chief economist of the Brewers Association, in a statement.
           </p>
           <p>
            Hitting double-digit volume share is a key first step to the association's lofty goal of achieving 20 percent market share of the beer market by volume by 2020.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="label-read-more">
             Read More
            </span>
            <a href="/2015/01/23/anheuser-busch-buys-another-craft-brewer-.html" target="_self">
             Anheuser-Busch buys another craft brewer
            </a>
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p>
            That goal is believed to be part of the reason why the trade group rewrote the rules as to what counts as a craft brewery, thereby opening the doors for high-volume label Yuengling and its nearly 3 million barrels a year as well as a handful of others that were previously excluded.
           </p>
           <p>
            The data also revealed that the total number breweries in operation continues to skyrocket. The Brewers Association reported the total number of operating breweries grew 19 percent, totaling 3,464 breweries in the U.S., in 2014. Openings far outpaced closures, with 615 new brewery openings and only 46 closings.
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             Questions? Comments?

              BehindTheWheel@cnbc.com

            </em>
            <br/>
           </p>
