         <div class="article-summary summary">
          <div class="summary-text">
           <h2>
            <a href="http://www.ew.com/topic/the-big-bang-theory">
             The Big Bang Theory
            </a>
           </h2>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p class="submitted">
          Posted November 11 2014  10:38 PM EST
         </p>
         <div class="article-body">
          <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
             <p>
              Veteran character actor Carol Ann Susi, who most recently lent her voice to
              <a href="http://tvrecaps.ew.com/tv-show/the-big-bang-theory/">
               <em>
                The Big Bang Theory
               </em>
              </a>
              as the Mrs. Wolowitz
              <em>
               ,
              </em>
              has died after a brief battle with cancer, her rep confirmed on Tuesday.
             </p>
             <!--more-->
             <p>
              After moving Los Angeles from her native Brooklyn in the 1970s, Susi got her start in television on theABC series
              <em>
               The Night Stalker
              </em>
              playing Kolchaks secretary, Monique Marmelstein. She also appeared in
              <em>
               Cheers, Doogie Howser, M.D., Mad About You, Just Shoot Me, Seinfeld, Six Feet Under,
              </em>
              and more.
             </p>
             <p>
              In a statement to
              <em>
               EW
              </em>
              , Susis rep remembered Susi as a force of nature who didnt suffer fools and was fiercely loyal to her friends.
             </p>
             <div class="bc-video-player shortcode" data-lineupid="" data-playlist-size="" data-push-top="" data-size="m" data-target-id="sc-v-3888116667001-ran1841" data-videoid="3888116667001" id="sc-v-3888116667001-ran1841">
             </div>
             <p>
              
              <em>
               The Big Bang Theory
              </em>
              family has lost a beloved member today with the passing of Carol Ann Susi, who hilariously and memorably voiced the role of Mrs. Wolowitz,
              <em>
               Big Bang Theory
              </em>
              executive producers Chuck Lorre, Steven Molaro and Bill Prady wrote in a joint statement with Warner Bros. Television and CBS. Unseen by viewers, the Mrs. Wolowitz character became a bit of a mystery throughout the shows eight seasons. What was not a mystery, however, was Carol Anns immense talent and comedic timing, which were on display during each unforgettable appearance. In addition to her talent, Carol Ann was a constant source of joy and kindness to all. Our thoughts and deepest condolences are with her family during this time, and we will miss her greatly.
             </p>
             <p>
              Susi is survived by her brother, Michael Susi, and his wife, Connie.
             </p>
