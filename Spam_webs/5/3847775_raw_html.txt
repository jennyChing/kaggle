       <h1 class="title">
        <a href="http://www.1forthepeople.com/2011/11/new-music-heavenly-beat-faithless.html">
         New Music: Heavenly Beat - Faithless
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="col_main">
        <div class="post-meta">
         Posted by Wayne Flanagan
         <span class="separator">
          |
         </span>
         Thursday, November 17, 2011
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="entry">
          <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DHk_KmUzInU/TsQ_1GJ_gMI/AAAAAAAAAMM/hLIqArMKb1U/s1600/hb2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img border="0" height="540" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DHk_KmUzInU/TsQ_1GJ_gMI/AAAAAAAAAMM/hLIqArMKb1U/s640/hb2.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
           <a href="http://www.1forthepeople.com/2011/11/new-track-dive-human.html">
            From one Captured Tracks artist
           </a>
           to another, not that we're bias, but they're pretty good, aren't they? New music today from fellow Beach Fossil
           <a href="http://www.facebook.com/heavenlybeat">
            Heavenly Beat
           </a>
           , aka John Pena, with his second release "Faithless".
          </div>
          <a name="more">
          </a>
          <br/>
          Following on from previous tracks like "Desire" &amp; "Sunday", this new jam is full of Cuban flavour, except this cut hastequilainvolved (shouts to the
          <i>
           Pena Colada
          </i>
          pun). There is even a break down at the 2.20 mark, giving you just just enough time to wipe the sweat off your brow, take a sip of Heine and continue on with your now perfected two step.
          <br/>
          <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
           The 7" single is out on December 7th with an LP to follow in early 2012 via
           <a href="http://capturedtracks.com/">
            you know who
           </a>
           . Hear "Faithless" below.
          </div>
