                   Monday, June 9, 2014

                 </h2>
                 <div class="date-posts">
                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DNMLQrGqQnQ/U5Y2vjkJmoI/AAAAAAAAA84/qo8uPRwakAI/s1600/IMG_1487.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    </a>
                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://kpaffzfit.blogspot.com/2014/06/my-first-voxbox-spring-fling-box.html">
                      My first Voxbox! The "Spring Fling" box
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8006598708659031806" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <br/>
                     I signed up for a site called
                     <b>
                      Influenster
                     </b>
                     (www.influenster.com) a while ago after seeing a friend of mine mention it on FaceBook. It basically calculates your "influence" based on the number of followers you have on social media. I figured that I might as well give it a shot since there's quite a few of you who follow me on YouTube, Tumblr and Instagram. Anyways, I got picked for the Spring Fling Voxbox which included a few products to review! It was a nice little treat to receive in the mail. It came in a bright mint box with a pretty little card detailing the products that were included:
                     <br/>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DNMLQrGqQnQ/U5Y2vjkJmoI/AAAAAAAAA84/qo8uPRwakAI/s1600/IMG_1487.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="251" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DNMLQrGqQnQ/U5Y2vjkJmoI/AAAAAAAAA84/qo8uPRwakAI/s1600/IMG_1487.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-46y0NxTVn54/U5Y2u3m3PWI/AAAAAAAAA8w/s9vgW-2AEdo/s1600/IMG_1489.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="240" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-46y0NxTVn54/U5Y2u3m3PWI/AAAAAAAAA8w/s9vgW-2AEdo/s1600/IMG_1489.JPG" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3FpdOJYqLlU/U5Y2wHFgxTI/AAAAAAAAA88/InLAGKSTG5k/s1600/IMG_1490.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="240" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3FpdOJYqLlU/U5Y2wHFgxTI/AAAAAAAAA88/InLAGKSTG5k/s1600/IMG_1490.JPG" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     The following items were sent to me for free and I was not paid or compensated in any way to review them. The opinions that I express are entirely my own :)
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     As you can see, the box included almost all full-size products which was nice. I expected more "sample-y" type things. It was quite the random selection though! I was immediately most interested in the mascara and the lip balm. I had already tried the false nails previously and the "firming" lotion just didn't really strike me as interesting.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YtVmva3e23Q/U5Y2xDqA2bI/AAAAAAAAA9U/YqvfuuO37uA/s1600/IMG_1491.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YtVmva3e23Q/U5Y2xDqA2bI/AAAAAAAAA9U/YqvfuuO37uA/s1600/IMG_1491.JPG" width="228"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-yTbtNv-Nstg/U5Y2xf7QmHI/AAAAAAAAA9Y/g__Wk7FD4rc/s1600/IMG_1492.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-yTbtNv-Nstg/U5Y2xf7QmHI/AAAAAAAAA9Y/g__Wk7FD4rc/s1600/IMG_1492.JPG" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     The Softlips "Cube" seems like a very obvious attempt to compete with EOS lip balm balls, which I use frequently. I found this lip balm to be
                     <i>
                      very
                     </i>
                     scented (almost like fruity gum) and more moisturizing than EOS balms for sure! I've been using it daily as my "go-to" lip moisturizer, though I wouldn't say I'm crazy about it. It does the job, though!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-6Wbm9r6vzQY/U5Y2y5LDWvI/AAAAAAAAA9s/RsjB2yir3eo/s1600/IMG_1494.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-6Wbm9r6vzQY/U5Y2y5LDWvI/AAAAAAAAA9s/RsjB2yir3eo/s1600/IMG_1494.JPG" width="240"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      The Rimmel Scandaleyes mascara definitely didn't let me down! It really, really lengthens my lashes and provides a good amount of volume as well (and usually mascaras don't do both)! The only thing about it that I don't like is that it isn't waterproof (which I always opt for) but I'm pretty sure I'll re-purchase this mascara in waterproof once I run out since it's so good!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-AapBN030_RY/U5Y2zVO2SrI/AAAAAAAAA90/DWWADpYcfi4/s1600/IMG_1495.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-AapBN030_RY/U5Y2zVO2SrI/AAAAAAAAA90/DWWADpYcfi4/s1600/IMG_1495.JPG" width="240"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      I haven't applied these yet but I know from experience that they are very easy to do so. The "tips" also don't chip like other fake nail brands which I like. I plan on saving these for a special occasion because they do look like a professional manicure when applied carefully and can last up to a week!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ZCMsZKgRTjI/U5Y20qf1XkI/AAAAAAAAA-I/SMYUYiP8f2s/s1600/IMG_1497.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ZCMsZKgRTjI/U5Y20qf1XkI/AAAAAAAAA-I/SMYUYiP8f2s/s1600/IMG_1497.JPG" width="240"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     I reached for this moisturizer after a shower and found it to be nothing special. I didn't really observe any "skin firming" benefits like it claims, but I also probably didn't use it frequently enough. I don't want to write it off, but skin "firming" has never been something I've really looked for or expected from a lotion.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-TwoSLHmEOGo/U5Y21DQIEgI/AAAAAAAAA-M/5ou96qBYCDY/s1600/IMG_1498.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-TwoSLHmEOGo/U5Y21DQIEgI/AAAAAAAAA-M/5ou96qBYCDY/s1600/IMG_1498.JPG" width="277"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     Finally, I was a bit disappointed to see these included in the box. I've never, ever bought scented tampons because I thoroughly disagree with the message that they send young girls. It's products like these and other feminine washes that promote obsession over "odor" and promote infection (in my opinion.) So I'm afraid I won't be using these because I don't agree with inserting scented things into your body. I'll keep them around in case of emergency, but have no interest in reviewing them.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I hope that was sort of interesting for you guys? I'm also going to make a video expressing these same views but I thought a blog version might be better for those of you that prefer more straight-forward reviews!

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://kpaffzfit.blogspot.com/2014/05/getting-back-on-bandwagon-and-off.html">
                      Getting back on the bandwagon and off the beaten path
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-572518777910018796" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
                      I'm afraid I have no substantial excuse as to why I have been such a terrible,
                      <i>
                       terrible
                      </i>
                      blogger lately. I get you guys hooked with how awesome I apparently am and then leave you hanging... As much as I'd love to pin my laziness towards blogging (and pretty much everything else) on Depression (a force I consider worthy of capitalization), both you and I know that that would indeed be a cop out. After many a day wasted doing nothing of substance and compensating no physical activity with far too much food, I came to realize that the copious amounts of Chipotle I had been consuming took more effort to attain than opening my laptop and typing a couple of paragraphs would.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
                      So here I am, your humble servant, willing to take requests on topics you would like me to cover either via blog post or video. Simply comment below, tell me via Tumblr, or shoot me an email at kgsp@uw.edu (and to anyone who has emailed me in the past and has yet to receive a response... I apologize! I was caught up wallowing in self-pity but I promise to respond if you send me another!)
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-xh8u77zUUZY/U3-m3iEpN6I/AAAAAAAAA7I/Y2vva6Nj3Mg/s1600/10366306_10152377705826609_1124598529561158524_n.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-xh8u77zUUZY/U3-m3iEpN6I/AAAAAAAAA7I/Y2vva6Nj3Mg/s1600/10366306_10152377705826609_1124598529561158524_n.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      I discovered a little hiking trail the other day at a local park and loosely decided at the time that I would declare it my new "work out."
                      <b>
                       I hate working out in gym-type atmospheres.
                      </b>
                      Many of you ask me about my gym workout suggestions and, to by perfectly honest, I never know what to say. I would
                      <i>
                       so
                      </i>
                      much rather workout in nature and enjoy the satisfaction of reaching a destination then try and avoid looking at the clock in a sweaty room. Anyways my plan to ease myself back into working out was towalk there, complete both parts of the trail (it splits off towards the end) and walk home. Nothing strenuous... Well, man, am I out of shape! I huffed and puffed my way back up the trail, covered in sweat, and stopped at a couple of the more flat parts to (attempt) to catch my breath. I felt like a failure.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      Then I realized that it was this exact type of negative thinking that has been keeping me in my little bubble of depression. Why the hell was I beating myself up over getting out of the house and breaking a sweat for the first time in forever?! Rome wasn't built in a day and I certainly wouldn't get back in shape in one. Or a week. It'll take a while. But I know one thing for sure:
                      <b>
                       I'm done beating myself up. I've never been, am not, and never will be, perfect.I'm done holding myself to an impossible standard. When you do that, you miss out on the joys of the process of improvement. I'm done with never being satisfied with myself. It's the only thing holding me back from happiness
                      </b>
                      .
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      Anyways, I can't recommend ditching the gym for nature enough! There's nothing quite like the feeling of rain cooling you down once you reach your destination and can enjoy the beauty of your surroundings. I'll take that over the gym, please.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      What's your favorite outdoor activity? Let me know!
                     </div>
