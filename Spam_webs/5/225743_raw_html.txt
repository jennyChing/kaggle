       <h1 class="entry-title">
        How to Paint a Rusty Metal Shed
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-08-18T20:08:11+00:00">
         August 18, 2014
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/author/amcleod/" rel="author">
           Ashley
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/#comments">
          13 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         When we bought our house it had zero storage. No garage, no basement, an attic that isnt really usable, and no extra closets. We needed a place to store lawn equipment, paint, and general homeowner stuff, so Adams parents offered us an old metal shed that was on their property.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="moving the shed" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8751 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="315" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/moving-the-shed.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         We put it on the back part of our lot, filled it with stuff, and promptly forgot about it.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="old metal shed" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8811 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="532" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/old-metal-shed.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Or at least I forgot about it. My husband on the other hand thought it needed some TLC. He would casually say things like if you want you could paint that shed. And I would nod my head and say things like yeah, maybe all the while thinking to myself that there was
         <em>
          no way
         </em>
         he was going to talk me into painting that rusty old thing. But he kept hinting  for years  that he wanted me to paint it, and I kept dodging his subtle pleas with mumbles and side-eyes. About a year ago he got he hint and stopped asking.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Then last month 3M contacted me for a campaign theyre doing about outdoor painting projects. 3M is a great company to work with, so I racked my brain trying to think of something. I wanted to do a project that actually
         <em>
          needed
         </em>
         doing, not something like painting a pot or outdoor rug that I have no current use for. Finally I gave up and asked Adam if he had any ideas. He thought about it for about half a second, turned to me with a subtle smirk on his face and said you could paint the shed.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Dang it.he had me, and he knew it. That bastard outsmarted me. So, without further ado.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="How to paint a rusty metal shed" class="aligncenter wp-image-8807 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="696" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/How-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed.jpg" width="385"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.domesticimperfection.com%2F2014%2F08%2Fhow-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.domesticimperfection.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F08%2FHow-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed.jpg&amp;description=Humorous%20tutorial%20on%20painting%20a%20metal%20building">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="20" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pinit_fg_en_rect_gray_20.png" width="40"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Now, the
         <em>
          most important
         </em>
         thing to remember when painting a rusty metal shed is NOT to do it in late July in South Texas. Just stay inside, enjoy your air conditioning, and wait till Fall. The second most important thing is prepping the surface correctly before painting. Ive never painted rusty metal before and Google doesnt give you a clear-cut way of prepping, but rather gives you about 47 ways you could do it and tells you not to mess up. Since my shed was only lightly rusted I decided sanding it wasnt necessary and chose to wash it with TSP instead. TSP stands for trisodium phosphate, you can buy it at the hardware store and it isnt expensive at all. I had never used it before but Ive heard people sing its praises so I figured Id give it a go.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="TSP" class="aligncenter wp-image-8762 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="482" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/TSP.jpg" width="341"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Im now going to become one of those people that sings its praises, because the stuff is magic. MAGIC I tell you. It comes in powder form, you mix it with warm water, get to scrubbing, and the dirt and rust come pouring off. Literally.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="cleaning with TSP" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8758 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="315" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/cleaning-with-TSP.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Here are some before and after pictures
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Rusted metal before and after TSP" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8760 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="315" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Rusted-metal-before-and-after-TSP.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="before and after TSP" class="aligncenter wp-image-8755 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="213" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/before-and-after-TSP.jpg" width="629"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         So much better already, right? I had other prep planned for the shed, but after cleaning it with TSP we (my mom was helping me with the project) decided that it was good to go.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Next up was to prime the shed, I used
         <a href='&lt;a%20href="http://soch.us/oM7E-1M6HW"%20rel="nofollow"&gt; Behr Paint &lt;/a&gt;' target="_blank">
          Behr Premium Plus Exterior Multi-Surface Primer and Sealer
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Behr multi -surface primer" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8757 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="444" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Behr-multi-surface-primer.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I also decided to bust out my Ryobi Paint Sprayer. I had never used a paint sprayer before (so many firsts in this post!) and was hoping it would make painting the shed go really quickly. Also, my boys thought it was awesome. It looks like a gun and shoots paintwhats not to like?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="spraying an old shed" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8800 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="476" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/spraying-an-old-shed.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         The paint sprayer
         <em>
          was
         </em>
         awesome. Seriously, it took me no time at all the paint the whole shed and the finish was smooth and beautiful. That being said, there is a learning curve. I had no idea how much you needed to thin the paint (spoiler alert, its a lot), and I spent a lot of time cleaning and unclogging the sprayer.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="primed metal" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8797 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="420" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/primed-metal.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Next up was to prep the shed for paint. For this I used
         <a href='&lt;a%20href="http://soch.us/CfsE-1M6HW"%20rel="nofollow"&gt; ScotchBlue Painters Tape &lt;/a&gt;' target="_blank">
          ScotchBlue Painters Tape for Exterior Surfaces (2097)
         </a>
         and taped off the parts I wanted to keep white.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Scotch Blue Exterior tape" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8761 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="429" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Scotch-Blue-Exterior-tape.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I used Behr Marquee (in Oat Straw 740C-3)for painting the shed, which is meant for outdoor things that are going to get abused and ignored.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Behr Marquee" class="aligncenter wp-image-8756 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="516" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Behr-Marquee.jpg" width="404"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Then I got back to spraying.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         By this point it was mid-day and was getting really hot. The kinda hot where you wish you were naked but also that you were wearing ALL THE CLOTHES so that the burning rays of sun never touch your delicate skin. My peanut gallery of preschoolers had abandon me for dinosaur puzzles in the air conditioning. Smart boys. Not all that loyal, but smart nonetheless.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="power spraying a shed" class="aligncenter wp-image-8796 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="504" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/power-spraying-a-shed.jpg" width="544"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Oh, heres another paint sprayer tip. Check to make sure that the paint container is locked on before you start spraying. Even if you think its good to go, double-check you know, just in case.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="paint splatter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8759 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="442" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/paint-splatter.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         After that I moved my tape, grabbed my favorite
         <a href='&lt;a%20href="http://soch.us/IBv8-1M6HW" rel="nofollow"&gt; Wooster Brushes &lt;/a&gt;' target="_blank">
          Woosterpaint brush
         </a>
         , and painted the trim(Marquee Ultra Pure White 1850) and the doors (Marquee Subtle Touch 790E-1).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="ScotchBlue and Wooster" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8799 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="476" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/ScotchBlue-and-Wooster.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Then I called it a day, abandon the outdoor inferno, and took my sweaty, paint splattered self inside for a shower.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         We werent finished yet thoughoh no.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         The next morning my husband got up before the sun to start working on the roof. The roof, you see, was really rustedway more rusted than the rest of the shed. I had planned on just leaving it (or
         <em>
          maybe
         </em>
         painting it), but Adam decided it would be a good idea to replace it. It will be easy he said, Ill just buy some corrugated metal panels to replace it with. Lets just skip ahead to the part where I tell you this was NOT easy. As it turns out, roofs on metal sheds are quite structural. In other words, when you remove the roof the walls just kind of flop around and infuriate your husband.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="tearing off the roof" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8801 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="407" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/tearing-off-the-roof.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         So yeah, the roof replacement turned my laid back husband into an angry cursing machine. And while I felt bad that it wasnt going well, I was also thanking Jesus that replacing the roof was 100% his idea and not something I asked him to do.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="putting a new roof on the shed" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8798 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="465" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/putting-a-new-roof-on-the-shed.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Eventually he got it on though, and were not going to have to torch the shed like he had been claiming was our only option.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Okay, last step for me to add a little barn flair back onto the shed doors. For this I grabbed my trusty ScotchBlue Painters Tape and an X-acto knife. I eyeballed the design, taping off the X shape (door on the right) and then cutting out the extra tape (door on the left).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="barn doors with Scotch Blue" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8793 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="420" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/barn-doors-with-Scotch-Blue-.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Then I painted the sections between the tape
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="painting crisp lines" class="aligncenter wp-image-8795 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="479" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/painting-crisp-lines.jpg" width="404"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         And before the paint dry (very important!) I pulled all the tape off and admired my work.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="painted X doors" class="aligncenter wp-image-8806 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="488" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/painted-X-doors.jpg" width="459"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="How to paint lightly rusted metal" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8803 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="420" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/How-to-paint-lightly-rusted-metal.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Now THAT is a painted metal shed. That is what a completed project looks like after years of avoidance. I have to say, it looks goodlike really good.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="painted metal shed" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8805 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="509" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/painted-metal-shed-W.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Adam asked me very specifically to get a glamor shot of the roof, so here it is.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="New metal roof" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8804 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="493" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/New-metal-roof.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         One last side by side, because thats the best part of any project.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Before and after" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8816 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/08/how-to-paint-a-rusty-metal-shed/" height="252" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Before-and-after.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
