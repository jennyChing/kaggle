       <div class="card" data-lightbox-index="0" id="limit-access-to-just-approved-apps-and-games">
        <img alt="Android" src="//www.google.com/images/icons/product/android-32.png" title="Android"/>
        <h3>
         Limit access to just approved apps and games
        </h3>
        <div class="card-detail-main">
         <p>
          Want to share your tablet without sharing all your stuff? On Android tablets
                    running 4.3 and higher, you can create restricted profiles that limit the
                    access that other users have to features and content on your tablet.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://support.google.com/nexus/answer/3175031" target="_blank">
           Learn
                    more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="card-detail-tutorial gweb-pagination">
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/21-1.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            If youre the tablet owner, touch Settings  Users  Add user or profile.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/21-2.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Touch Restricted profile then New profile to name the profile.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/21-3.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Use the ON/OFF toggles and Settings to grant access to features, settings
                        and apps.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/21-4.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Press the Power button to return to the lock screen, then touch the new
                        profile icon.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/21-5.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Once it's all set up, the Home screen is empty. Touch the All Apps icon to
                        get started with the new profile.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="card" data-lightbox-index="1" id="set-it-and-forget-it-keep-safety-mode-locked">
        <img alt="YouTube" src="//www.google.com/images/icons/product/youtube-32.png" title="YouTube"/>
        <h3>
         Set it and forget it. Keep Safety Mode locked
        </h3>
        <div class="card-detail-main">
         <p>
          If you want to keep Safety Mode on permanently, you can lock it. Then every
                    time you access YouTube from that browser, you'll automatically be signed in to
                    Safety Mode.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/174084" target="_blank">
           Learn more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="card-detail-tutorial gweb-pagination">
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/11-1.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Scroll to the bottom of any YouTube page and click the drop-down menu in
                        the Safety section.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/11-2.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Select the On or Off option to enable or disable Safety Mode.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/11-3.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            To lock this setting, sign in to your Google Account.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/11-4.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Once you've signed in, you will have the option to lock this setting.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>

       <div class="card" data-lightbox-index="2" id="browse-the-web-without-saving-certain-information">
        <img alt="Chrome" src="//www.google.com/images/icons/product/chrome-32.png" title="Chrome"/>
        <h3>
         Browse the web without saving certain information
        </h3>
        <div class="card-detail-main">
         <p>
          When you want to browse the Web in private, enable Incognito Mode in the Chrome
                    browser on your computer, tablet or phone. In Incognito Mode, the pages that
                    you visit and files that you download arent recorded in Chromes browsing or
                    download history.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95464" target="_blank">
           Learn
                    more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="card-detail-tutorial gweb-pagination">
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/27-1.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Click the Chrome menu  File menu on the browser toolbar.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/27-2.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Select New incognito window.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/27-3.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            A new window will open with the incognito icon in the corner. To exit,
                        simply close the window.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="card" data-lightbox-index="3" id="get-family-friendly-results-from-search">
        <img alt="Google Search" src="//www.google.com/images/icons/product/search-32.png" title="Google Search"/>
        <h3>
         Get family-friendly results from Search
        </h3>
        <div class="card-detail-main">
         <p>
          By enabling SafeSearch, you can filter out most of the mature content that you
                    or your family may prefer to avoid. If an inappropriate result does sneak
                    through, you can report it to Google. We're always working to improve our
                    content filters, and this kind of feedback can help us make SafeSearch better
                    for everyone.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://support.google.com/websearch/answer/510" target="_blank">
           Learn
                    more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="card-detail-tutorial gweb-pagination">
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/12-1.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Visit the Search Settings page at
            <a href="http://www.google.com/preferences">
             www.google.com/preferences
            </a>
            .
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/12-2.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Tick the box next to Filter explicit results.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/12-3.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            Hit the save button at the bottom of the page.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="card-detail-step gweb-pagination-content">
          <img alt="" src="../safetycenter/images/tools/12-4.png"/>
          <div class="card-detail-step-caption">
           <p>
            You can click Lock SafeSearch to help prevent others from changing your
                        setting. You will be asked to enter your password.
           </p>
          </div>
