                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <p>
                   Just for fun, I'm joining in with
                   <a href="http://www.chattingatthesky.com/2015/02/27/lets-share-learned-february-2/" target="_blank">
                    Emily of Chatting at the Sky
                   </a>
                   for a link-up called What I Learned. This is a mix of treasures that I picked up along the way this month.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    <span>
                     1.
                     <span>
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                   <a href="http://www.karipatterson.com/the-problem-with-beautiful/" target="_blank">
                    <strong>
                     The Problem with Beautiful
                    </strong>
                   </a>
                   -
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   <span>
                    Im still needIng to learn to this truth, again and again, in a way that sticks.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    I forget.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    Are you feeling less than beautiful right now?
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    I hope not.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    If so, read this.
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    <span>
                     2.
                     <span>
                      <a href="https://ifttt.com/" target="_blank">
                      </a>
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                   <a href="https://ifttt.com/" target="_blank">
                    <strong>
                     IFTTT
                    </strong>
                    -
                   </a>
                   perhaps its a bit geeky, but its cool.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Maybe youve already heard of it, but just discovered IFTTT.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   It was really bothering me that when I posted Instagram pictures to Twitter, and they would only show up as links.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I set out to fix that and I found IFTTT.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   It s a free app that stands for- If. This. Then. That.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   You can create recipes that automate many tasks.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   The first recipe I added to my account was a Twitter/Instagram thing that automatically posts any pictures from my Instagram to Twitterand it shows up as a full picture.
                   <span>
                    <a href="https://ifttt.com/" target="_blank">
                    </a>
                   </span>
                   <a href="https://ifttt.com/" target="_blank">
                    Heres
                   </a>
                   more info it youre interested:
                   <span>
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    <span>
                     3.
                     <span>
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                   <a href="http://wordswag.co/" target="_blank">
                    <strong>
                     Word Swag
                    </strong>
                    -
                   </a>
                   its a fun app that allows you to create beautiful graphics.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I love it.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Ive used it for quite a few of the graphics that you see on this blog.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    <span>
                     4.
                     <span>
                      <a href="http://just-jessika.blogspot.com/2015/02/time.html" target="_blank">
                       <strong>
                        Time to Trust
                       </strong>
                      </a>
                      :
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                   
                   <span>
                    Harder than waiting, harder than running 5 miles, harder than
                    <em>
                     dancing
                    </em>
                    is resting - trusting.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    <strong>
                     Time
                    </strong>
                    , to trust.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    Im not good with the waiting either.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    Id rather being
                    <em>
                     doing
                    </em>
                    something.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    I needed to read this.
                    <span>
                    </span>
                    By Jessica Anspach, a dancer with Pacific Northwest Ballet
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    <span>
                     5.
                     <span>
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                   <a href="http://jenhatmaker.com/blog/2015/02/04/on-becoming-a-writer" target="_blank">
                    <strong>
                     On Becoming a Writer
                    </strong>
                    ,
                   </a>
                   by Jen Hatmaker
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="sqs-block image-block sqs-block-image" data-block-type="5" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1424987606166_41164">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <div class="image-block-outer-wrapper layout-caption-below ">
                   <div class="intrinsic" style="max-width:2500.0px;">
                    <div class="image-block-wrapper has-aspect-ratio" data-description="" style="padding-bottom:100.0%;">
                     <noscript>
                      <img alt="February collage" src="http://static1.squarespace.com/static/54b4474fe4b0ad2aad39ed58/t/54ef9a83e4b07741eb5c1e36/1424988828797/February+collage"/>
                     </noscript>
                     <img alt="February collage" class="thumb-image" data-image="http://static1.squarespace.com/static/54b4474fe4b0ad2aad39ed58/t/54ef9a83e4b07741eb5c1e36/1424988828797/February+collage" data-image-dimensions="2500x2500" data-image-focal-point="0.5,0.5" data-image-id="54ef9a83e4b07741eb5c1e36" data-load="false" data-src="http://static1.squarespace.com/static/54b4474fe4b0ad2aad39ed58/t/54ef9a83e4b07741eb5c1e36/1424988828797/February+collage" data-type="image"/>
                    </div>

                  </div>
                 </div>

                <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1424987606166_41383">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    <span>
                     6.
                     <span>
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                   <strong>
                    I was made to plan parties
                   </strong>
                   .
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I love everything about the process, except one thingwhatever happened to RSVP?
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Do people think its optional? Just a yes or a no, that's all I'm asking for. Were planning a sweet sixteen party for my girl. My favorite part?
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I designated myself as the official macaroon taste tester, and visited several different bakeries in my town.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Aura bakery in Kirkland has the best.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   7.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   <strong>
                    When you are teaching
                   </strong>
                   on something like trusting God, he will allow plenty of opportunities to practice what you preach.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   8.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   <strong>
                    Candlelit dinners
                   </strong>
                   in February are a good thing, every night.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   9.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   <strong>
                    Jan Karons Mitford books
                   </strong>
                   are like the mac n cheese of literature for me, my comfort food.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I already knew that, but I was reminded again after finally getting to read
                   <a href="http://www.lifeway.com/Product/somewhere-safe-with-somebody-good-P005697820" target="_blank">
                    Somewhere Safe with Somebody Good
                   </a>
                   <a href="http://www.lifeway.com/Product/somewhere-safe-with-somebody-good-P005697820" target="_blank">
                    .
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   10.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   <strong>
                    Newsletters
                   </strong>
                   .
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   This month I attempted to send my first newsletter out.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I just shared a couple of stories from my life.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Ill consider that first one my practice round.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   For nearly a week, I provided a sign up link that only I could see.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   I apologize for that. I didnt realize the problem until I looked at it on another computer, where I wasnt signed into my Squarespace account.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Oy, vey!
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Then the newsletters that went out to the people, who did manage to sign up, mostly went straight to junk mail folders.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   Ill just chalk this one up to a learning experience.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                   If you still want to sign up, the
                   <a href="http://www.dawnklinge.com/newsletter-sign-up/" target="_blank">
                    link
                   </a>
                   is now fixed.
                   <span>
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Well, that's all I've got for now! What have you been learning?
                  </p>
