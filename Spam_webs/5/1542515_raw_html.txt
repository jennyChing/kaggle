            <p>
             Have you ever thought of playing a single track on two guitars simultaneously? Yes, you read it right, simultaneously is what we are seeking for. Playing a single track on two guitars is possible but playing it simultaneously on two is strictly impossible.
            </p>
            <p>
             The video herein shows a teenage boy who practically blows the daylights out of people. Musically talented, he plays Andy McKees song Drifting on guitars. If you are wondering what is so special about him, read the above sentence once again. Did you get the clue? Yes, there you go, he masters the song over guitars and not guitar. It is his calibre of playing a single track using multiple guitars simultaneously that makes him special over others. If you dont believe us, watch this.
            </p>
            <p>
             The video is a treat for music lovers as if brings to light a novel and highly innovative playing style. Wonder what this teenage sensation would grow up to be!
