        Top 5 Tips for Curing Hives

        <p>
         Hives, technically known as urticarial, happens to be an outbreak of wheals or pale, swollen red bumps that show up suddenly. Sometimes the cause of hives is unknown, but for many individuals, they are a sign of the bodys negative reaction to a specific allergen. In most cases, individuals who experienced hives deal with itching, but hives may also sting and burn. Since hives can occur at any place on the body, they can be extremely uncomfortable. While they may only be the size of a small pencil eraser for some people, other people experience hives that are far larger. If you are dealing with hives, you may be wondering what you can do to treat them, reliving your discomfort. The following are some of the top tips for curing hives that you can use to experience relief.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Tip #1  Apply Ice Packs or Cool Compresses
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         One of the best tips you can use for curing hives is to
         <a href="http://www.hivestreatments.org/cure/cool-hives-homemade-cold-compresses/">
          apply ice packs or cool compresses
         </a>
         to the area that is affected by hives. This is considered one of the best topical remedies for treating hives naturally. If you use an ice pack, make sure you wrap it in a towel before applying it, since ice should not be applied directly to your skin. Change icepacks or cool compresses from time to time.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Tip #2  Soak in Colloidal Oatmeal or Cornstarch
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Another excellent tip you can use to help cure hives and relive your discomfort is to soak in a bath that includes either
         <a href="http://www.hivestreatments.org/cure/get-rid-of-hives/">
          colloidal oatmeal or cornstarch
         </a>
         . Oatmeal in your bath water can help to sooth the skin and reduce some of the itching that often accompanies hives. If you do not have oatmeal on hand, then you can add cornstarch to your bathwater instead for a similar result.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Tip #3  Over the Counter Antihistamines
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         You will also find that over the counter antihistamines can be very helpful for curing hives. Since hives often occur because of histamines being released within the body, antihistamines can help to eliminate them. A variety of different antihistamines are available over the counter, including Allegra, Claritin, Benadryl and Zyrtec. If you decide to treat hives with antihistamines, it is a good idea to talk to your pharmacist or physician to ensure these medications will not negatively react with other medications you may be taking.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Tip #4  Treat Affected Areas with Aloe Vera Gel
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Aloe Vera gel has many wonderful properties that make it an excellent treatment for many skin problems, including hives. This gel can help with healing, soothing the skin and relieving the pain and itch. It also offers antibacterial properties, which are beneficial to the skin as well. Try chilling the gel just a bit before applying to make it feel even more soothing to your skin when applied.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Tip #5  Brew and Drink Herbal Teas
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.hivestreatments.org/cure/can-herbal-teas-cure-hives-forever/">
          Certain herbal teas
         </a>
         can be helpful for curing hives as well. Some herbal teas offer natural antihistamine effects while others can help to reduce inflammation or reduce an allergic reaction within your body. Green tea is a great herbal tea to try to help cure hives. It has natural antihistamine effects and can be found both without
         <a href="http://www.hivestreatments.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/mint-tea.jpg">
          <img alt="Herbal tea" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-94" height="300" src="http://www.hivestreatments.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/mint-tea-200x300.jpg" width="200"/>
         </a>
         and with caffeine. Another tea to consider is licorice root tea. It helps to reduce inflammation and boosts immune system function, which can help your body to recover from hives faster. Goldenseal herbal tea has been shown to help reduce or eliminate allergic reactions, which may be beneficial for treating hives as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         While hives can be quite painful and uncomfortable, you can find a variety of ways to treat the problem. Try one of these helpful tips for curing hives and enjoy getting the soothing relief you desire.
        </p>
