         <p>
          <strong>
           Here are a few things you can expect from Google in 2014. Read more!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          2014 for google calls for focusing on its holozoic mission to be a valuable internet company that provides marketers the best medium for online advertisement. To do this, it has some of the most popular tools in its arsenal. Lets look at some of the possible areas where google is likely to flex its tech muscle and emerge out as one of the most valuable internet companies in 2014.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Tapping not just mobile internet and monetizing android
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Google has already invaded the large chunk of mobile internet users with android. But challenges from fragmentation leaves app developers earn less in android. Hence, androids kitkat will be going strong this year. If android can accommodate more devices that are in the lower left quadrant and provide uniform experience, more revenues can be generated. Even if it does not increase the revenues by large margins, there are other benefits as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The benefit of mobile search queries
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          If revenues do not change marginally by proportion, then google will not loose either. An increase in user satisfaction with its new operating system means it will be able to draw more search queries for its search engine, keeping its adword revenues coming from android by large numbers.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Expanding the android ecosystem
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          It is also speculated that android will occupy a large number of devices other than smartphone which includes gaming consoles, cameras etc. This is to expand the footprint of android and make it expand as an ecosystem, giving a strategic advantage to the operating system with reference to its size and users dependability.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The power punch of chrome
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Chrome has emerged as the most popular browser in android ecosystem and google is trying to fetch the best out of it. If you are using chrome on your PC and on your smartphone as well; it gives google collect a lot of information about you and target you with relevant ads. All your internet usage comes under the umbrella of chrome and this is what google enjoys. 2014 will have google pushing chrome into the mobile devices and unite everything under a single browser.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Rich media content consumption
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Google understands that the internet speed is increasing across majority of countries and there is a need to push for tools that again emphasizes on cross device usage along with faster buffering and media consumption. Google has recently been working on the VP9 codec in chrome and it has been backed by the major companies dealing with multimedia devices such as Sony, Toshiba, Nvidia and others. This codec allows streaming 4k videos which sounds futuristic. It also helps in streaming videos for lesser bandwidth and faster processing at the beginning. Having said that, it is obvious that youtube will emerge out as a critical tool for video consumption in 2014.
          <br/>
          YouTubehas been experimenting with editing basic editing tools as well but it has remained infant since its birth. May beGooglemight work upon that too.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Google Glass
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Google glass is an important paradigm of wearable computers and a lot of people are looking forward to have something substantial coming out of google this year. A lot has been written about the invasiveness of google glass but its applicability in specific industries is certain. Recently a google glass competitor has also popped up which unlike the glass, places the visual elements at the windshield area for a cab driver making the device safer to use.
          <a href="http://www.knowyourmobile.com/google/google-glass/21388/google-glass-release-date-specs-and-features" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Google is also in talk with DFT to make the device usage legal for drivers on the roads of London.
          </a>
          On a wholesome basis google glass can again create a lot of data that google can integrate into their database making their products such as maps and G+ even more enriching.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Playstore
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Play Store is more like a onestop digital shop now and google might start cross selling content on play store. Fitness apps might redirect you to e-books that deal with bodybuilding or similar topics. Play store is also trying to gather as many ranking and reviews possible for apps used by users which is a clear indication to accumulate maximum rankings and reviews for apps. This can help playstore emerge as a more dependable platforms as far as judging the quality of the app is concerned.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The search algorithm
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The search algorithm of google has streamlined its focus on quality content and has devalued irrelevant backlinking of pages. Earlier it used to be how many links redirect to your pages which has now changed to how many people went from those link and read the content. Making search results valuable and relevant makes things better for mobile as well as web users.
         </p>
         <p>
          Overall, google will have a lot going across its leading products in 2014 but at the root of everything, Googles focus is to strengthen its dominance upon online search queries and make android more profitable.
         </p>
