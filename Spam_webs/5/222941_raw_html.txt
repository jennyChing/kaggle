     <p>
      Healthy turkey burger recipe could be done for around 30 minutes. It is a simple yet delicious recipe that brings no worries when you serve it on the table. You go for about a minute and find out that this menu is empty. It is a really good menu. 15 minutes is enough to cook this menu but remember that this recipe is only for 12 servings.
     </p>
     <p>
      Ingredients:
     </p>
     <p>
      Ground turkey: 3 pounds
      <br/>
      Seasoned bread crumbs: 1/4 cup
      <br/>
      Finely diced onion: 1/4 cup
      <br/>
      Egg: 2, whites. Lightly beaten
      <br/>
      Chopped fresh parsley: 1/4 cup
      <br/>
      Clove garlic: 1, minced and peeled
      <br/>
      Salt: 1 teaspoon
      <br/>
      Ground black pepper: 1/4 teaspoon
     </p>
     <p>
      Steps:
     </p>
     <p>
      1.Use large bowl and mix ground turkey, onion parsley, egg whites, seasoned bread crumbs, salt, garlic and pepper. Make 12 patties form.
      <br/>
      2.Use medium skillet and cook the patties over medium heat. Turning them for once and make sure that the internal temperature could reach for about 180 degrees F or 85 degrees C.
     </p>
     <p>
      So it is easy for you to make this menu. You do not have to spend out more than an hour to make this burger for dinner party. It is recommended to be your new favorite menu when you think that you want to save your budget without sacrificing your party. It is not a matter for you to get this menu for low cholesterol diet. Even if some of your family members are carnivore, they will love enjoying this menu. Well, the ingredients are very simple, basic and easy to be found. For your variant, you can use fresh bread crumbs to solve the problem with dryness. Some people also love to add generous sprinkle of McCormicks Montreal Steak Seasoning for the topping to add more favors for the burgers. I am sure your family will forget to take regular burger when they have this Healthy turkey burger recipe.
