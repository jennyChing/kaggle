			&lt;h1&gt;Sightseeing the Beautiful of Siam Tulip Flowers at Sai Thong National Park &lt;/h1&gt;
		&lt;p class="post-meta"&gt;&lt;span class="date"&gt;August 15, 2013&lt;/span&gt; &lt;span class="author"&gt;Mayuree&lt;/span&gt; &lt;span class="cats"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/category/travel-guide/thailand-travel-guide/" title="View all posts in Thailand Travel Guide" rel="category tag"&gt;Thailand Travel Guide&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/category/travel-guide/" title="View all posts in Travel Guide" rel="category tag"&gt;Travel Guide&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
		&lt;h2&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-11363 alignleft" title="Sai Thong National Park" alt="Sai Thong National Park" src="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Sai-Thong-National-Park-1-.jpg" width="310" height="274" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;CHAIYAPHUM PROVINCE &#8211; THE POPULAR PLACES TO VISIT IN RAINY SEASON THAILAND!&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Sai Thong National Park is located on Pung Huei Mountain in Chaiyaphum Province situated in the Northeastern of Thailand. It&#8217;s evergreen forest with dipterocarp forest and mixed deciduous forest. There is popular national park for seeing Krajeaw Flower or Siam Tulip and it&#8217;s beautiful natural attractions in Thailand. Tourists can see the stunning of Krajeaw Flowers for the fields of pinkish-purple krajeaw and also the fields of white krajeaw that they will bloom at the beginning of rainy season from July &#8211; August every year.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-11364 alignright" title="Pha Hum Huet" alt="Pha Hum Huet" src="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Pha-Hum-Huet-2-.jpg" width="310" height="274" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;Moreover, tourists can also visit Pa Hin Ngam National Park in Chaiyaphum for The Siam Tulip or Dok Kra Jeaw Festival that there is start early from June &#8211; July in every year.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;OTHER ATTRACTIONS IN SAI THONG:&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
Sai Thong &lt;a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/?s=+waterfalls" title="Waterfall "&gt;waterfall&lt;/a&gt;. Visitor who stay overnight can also sightseeing Sai Thong waterfall that it&#8217;s not far only 1 kilometer from the national park office.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Pha Poa Meaung. It&#8217;s cliff with beautiful viewpoints in park, about 10 kilometer from the national park office.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Khao Pung Huai Viewpoint. it&#8217;s the highest peak of Pung Huai mountain in national park with cool weather all year round.  Pha Hum Huet. The most beautiful &amp; popular viewpoint in national park, best time to go there it&#8217;s during rainy season and cool season for seeing the wonderful sea fog in the morning.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Best Time:&lt;/strong&gt; Rainy Season from July &#8211; August every year. (recommend the visitora should stay there overnight)&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;strong&gt;Location:&lt;/strong&gt; Chaiyaphum Province, Thailand.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;strong&gt;How to get there:&lt;/strong&gt; Can take bus from Mo Chit Bus Station Bangkok route Bangkok &#8211; Phu Reun, Get off at Huay Hin Fon Market and catch bus to national park.&lt;/p&gt;
