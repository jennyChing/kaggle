          <h2>
           LinkedIn Infographic Summary
          </h2>
          <p>
           LinkedIn content marketing helps B2B marketers reach their primary goals of
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Brand awareness
           </li>
           <li>
            Lead generation
           </li>
           <li>
            Engagement
           </li>
           <li>
            Sales
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           There are 8 guidelines you should follow to get the most out of your LinkedIn content marketing:
          </p>
          <ol>
           <li>
            Have a strategy and follow it.
           </li>
           <li>
            Be concise as you deliver value  shorter content is valued.
           </li>
           <li>
            Post at the times when LinkedIn users are most active.
           </li>
           <li>
            Choose topics that appeal to your target market.
           </li>
           <li>
            Use lots of images.
           </li>
           <li>
            Avoid videos and other multimedia types on LinkedIn.
           </li>
           <li>
            LinkedIn readers like How to and Top # posts.
           </li>
           <li>
            Organize your content and make it easy to read.
           </li>
          </ol>
