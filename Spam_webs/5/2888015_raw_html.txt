         <h1 class="entry-title cb-entry-title cb-single-title" itemprop="headline">
          New Music: Common  Ghetto Dreams ft. Nas
         </h1>
         <div class="cb-byline">
         </div>
        </span>
       </div>
       <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Common-Nas-Ghetto-Dreams.jpg" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/downloads/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Common-Nas-Ghetto-Dreams.jpg');">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11247" height="629" src="http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Common-Nas-Ghetto-Dreams.jpg" width="629"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         Friday night, Nas took to
         <a href="http://twitter.com/Nas/status/87054597193072640" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/twitter.com/Nas/status/87054597193072640');">
          Twitter
         </a>
         to unleash Ghetto Dreams, The No I.D.-produced collaboration track between the Chi City MC Common and Nasty Nas himself. Impatient with his track partners apparent reluctance to drop the song so early, Nas took matters upon himself. He first called Common out on Twitter with a request, then issued an ultimatum:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/nastweet1.png" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/downloads/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/nastweet1.png');">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11248" height="169" src="http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/nastweet1.png" width="507"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/nastweet2.png" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/downloads/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/nastweet2.png');">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11249" height="205" src="http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/nastweet2.png" width="499"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         Nasir did just that and dropped the song 20 minutes later.Listen below to the first single off Commons forthcoming
         <em>
          The Dreamer, The Believer,
         </em>
         his 9
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         album and first under Warner Bros. Its expected to see a Fall release. I feel aclassicis in the making.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a class="wp-embedded-audio" href="">
          :http://thehiphopdemocrat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Common-Ft-Nas-Ghetto-Dreams.mp3|titles=Common
         </a>
        </p>
