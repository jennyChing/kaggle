        <p>
         <a href="http://thekrazycouponlady.com/?attachment_id=553195" rel="attachment wp-att-553195">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-553195" height="400" src="http://prod-cdn.thekrazycouponlady.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Ck-2.jpg?86f090" width="600"/>

        <h3>
         Honey-Garlic Chicken
        </h3>
        <p>
         4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts  $5.32
         <br/>
         3 cloves garlic, minced  $0.09
         <br/>
         1 tsp fresh or dried basil, chopped  $0.10
         <br/>
         1/2 cup soy sauce  $0.39
         <br/>
         1/2 cup ketchup  $0.25
         <br/>
         1/3 cup honey  $0.67
        </p>
        <p>
         Directions:
        </p>
        <p>
         Add all ingredients to a gallon-size freezer bag to cook at a later time, or throw into crock pot. Cook on high 3-4 hours. Serve chicken breasts whole or shred and mix with remaining juice in crock pot. Goes well over rice with broccoli on the side.
        </p>
        <p>
         Serves: 4-6
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Total Cost: $6.82
         </strong>
        </p>
