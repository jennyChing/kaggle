       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         DIY Decorative Tray
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-02-24T00:00:37+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          02/24/2014
         </time>
         Posted by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Kelly Rowe
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         It feels like its been forever since I shared a craft with all of you a couple of weeks anyway. So, Im pretty excited to have something new to share today. Decorative trays seem to be a very versatile and functional piece in home decor. Remember my
         <a href="http://livelaughrowe.com/diy-pallet-serving-tray/" target="_blank">
          pallet serving tray
         </a>
         ? Gosh, I love that tray! I used it in my Christmas decor this year too! Ive seen so many versions of decorative trays, some using frames, marble, and pallet wood (ahem). I thought it was time to try my hand at another tray a
         <strong>
          decorative tray
         </strong>
         , and this one was going to have leather handles.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="DIY Decorative Tray with leather handles at livelaughrowe.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19771" height="747" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DIY-Decorative-Tray-with-leather-handles-at-livelaughrowe.com_.jpg" width="520"/>
         Recently, as I was browsing through Consumer Crafts website, I saw a shadow box and thought it might make a great tray! I liked that it had a little depth. I also wanted to try making it with leather handles. I was tickled silly as to how fantastic it turned out. Lets take a look at how it came about.
        </p>
        <p>
         Supplies for
         <strong>
          DIY Decorative Tray
         </strong>
         :
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.consumercrafts.com/store/details/catalog/seasonal-new-just-added-items/97823" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          8.5 x 11 x 1.75 Unfinished Wood Shadow Box
         </a>
         <br/>
         Paint (color of choice)
         <br/>
         Foam Paint Brushes
         <br/>
         Leather (I used a
         <a href="http://www.consumercrafts.com/store/details/catalog/jewelry-findings-bracelets/1999-3628" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          leather bracelet
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="supplies for diy decorative tray" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19776" height="427" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/supplies-for-diy-decorative-tray.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ready? Here we go! Its pretty simple..
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Step One: Paint the Shadow Box
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         I decided to use a chalk paintMaison Blanche Furniture Paint inCreme de Menthe. I wanted to use more of a mint green, but didnt have any on hand and didnt want to run out (lazy, I know).
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="painting the shadow box" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19774" height="427" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/painting-the-shadow-box.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Step Two: Sand and Stain
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         This step is optional. I used a fine sand paper and sanded all of the edges. Then added a some stain, my version of stain for a project like this is down brown acrylic paint. I rub it along the edges with a damp cloth. I think it adds a little extra character.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Step Three:  Prep and Attach Leather Handles
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="prepping the leather handles for DIY decorative tray" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19775" height="415" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/punch-hole-into-leather.jpg" width="640"/>
         I cut two 6 pieces of leather and used a
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1enZGPh" target="_blank">
          leather hole punch
         </a>
         tool to prep the holes in the leather. The hubs drilled small holes in the box where I wanted the handles to go and we then used the post/stud of some
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1nZhBkW" target="_blank">
          rapid rivets
         </a>
         I had on hand to attach the handle to the shadow box.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="attach handles to tray" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19779" height="427" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/attach-handles-to-tray.jpg" width="640"/>
         Add a dab of wood glue for extra strength,
         <em>
          (this is optional)
         </em>
         . Once everything is ready to go, youll tap each post/stud into place. Were done! Here is a closer look at the attached leather handle (see below). I personally think the stud/post looks pretty awesome!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="a closer look at the leather handles" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19770" height="427" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/closer-look-at-leather-handles.jpg" width="640"/>
         I think it turned out lovely! What do you think? And, as I stated earlier, its so versatile and functional. It could be used in the bedroom, living room, craft room or even the bathroom.  You could paint it in any color and/or attach a different handle that fits your style  oh the possibilities!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="From shadow box to fabulousness! DIY Decorative Tray with leather handles with livelaughrowe.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19773" height="439" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/From-shadow-box-to-fabulousness-DIY-Decorative-Tray-with-leather-handles-with-livelaughrowe.com_.jpg" width="640"/>
         <img alt="DIY Decorative Tray with leather handles at livelaughrowe.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19772" height="432" src="http://cf.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Fabulous-DIY-Decorative-Tray-with-leather-handles-at-livelaughrowe.com_.jpg" width="640"/>
         I think this tray suits those ranunculus perfectly too, dont you?
         <br/>
         Im thinking these will makegreat gifts too.
         <br/>
         Well, I hope youre feeling inspired!  Happy crafting, sweet friends.
        </p>
