     <h1>
      Experience/Employment Certificate Attestation for Oman
     </h1>
     <p class="p1">
      Attestation of Experience/employment Certificates for Oman is the act of witnessing an Experience/employment certificate by authorised person / persons / Departments / authorities with their official seal and signature. This attestation also confirms that, the specified experience/employment certificate has been issued by that mentioned department and Seal and signature on that particular Experience/employment certificate is authentic.
     </p>
     <p class="p1">
      <strong>
       Experience/ Employment Certificate Attestation for Oman
      </strong>
      can be done from the issued country of the certificate. For using the  certificate in Oman, certificate should be attested from concerned Home department/MEA &amp; corresponding Embassy of the country to which the certificate holder plans to go.
     </p>
     <p class="p1">
      We can complete all the process whatever it may be whether
      <strong>
       attestation
      </strong>
      even the certificate holder is not there in the Issuing country.
     </p>
     <h2 class="main-heading">
      Documents required for Oman Employment/Experience Certificate Attestation:
     </h2>
     <br/>
     <ul class="list">
      <li>
       Original  Certificate
      </li>
      <li>
       Passport copy
      </li>
      <li>
       Visa Copy
      </li>
      <li>
       2 Photographs
      </li>
      <li>
       Authorisation letter
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p class="p1">
      <strong>
       Please Note:
      </strong>
      For Kerala originated documents applicant needs to provide request letter to home department for certificate attestation.
     </p>
