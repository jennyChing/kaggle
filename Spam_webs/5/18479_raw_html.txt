         </h2>

          The first thing you need to do is play Exo mode.
         </p>

         </p>

          He had no idea what he was talking about, but I did get over 1000 kills and played for 3 hours straight, lol.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you want to play 4 rounds, then quit, play another 5 rounds next week, etc its ok.
         </p>
         <p>
          No need to do it all at once! Once you Unlock the Zombie Map and Skins in Advanced Warfare everyone in MP will know that you are a true bad ass.
         </p>

          <li>
           This can be done in public or private matches

           You can die, lose a round or match, it doesnt matter
          </li>
          <li>
           Simply play the required number of rounds (anytime) to unlock the next tier
          </li>
         </ul>

          I unlocked all the Advanced Warfare Exo Tiers How Do I Play Zombies
