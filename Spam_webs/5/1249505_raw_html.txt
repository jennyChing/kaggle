     <h2>
      My Cup Runneth Over
     </h2>
     <div class="entry">
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.playwii.com/" target="_blank">
        <img align="left" alt="Tim" hspace="7" src="http://www.2pstart.com/images/tim.png" vspace="6"/>
       </a>
       Were not trying to say that Crackdown was only purchased for the Halo 3 beta, were just saying that people were holding onto it even after they beat it solely for the Halo 3 beta. And who can blame them? I havent personally seen the phenomenon portrayed in this comic, but lets just say I doubt EB or GameStop are going to be out of copies of Crackdown any time soon. I think they did right to include the Halo 3 beta in Crackdown. They could have included it in a really crappy game that no one wanted just to boost sales, but instead it was included in a game that appealed to the same crowd as Halo 3 and was a great game in its own right. While sales of Crackdown were certainly boosted by the promise of the chance to play Halo 3 early, Im sure gamers were happy to find out they werent just paying $60 for that chance.
