       <span class="name internal pointer" data-internal-url="http://www.ranker.com/list/walking-dead-trivia/mike-calendrillo" itemprop="name">
        42 Things You Didn't Know About The Walking Dead
       </span>
      </h1>
      <p class="relative width100" id="listCriteria">
       <strong class="rnkrCRRed">
        List Criteria:
       </strong>
       <span class="criteria">
        Vote up the most shocking, surprising, fun facts about The Walking Dead.

      <div class="relative collapse desc init grey exclude" data-csst-arrow="true" data-csst-arrowclass="ui-icon ui-icon-carat-1-s" data-csst-bg="#dddee1" data-csst-color="#000" data-csst-height="17" data-csst-id="userDesc" itemprop="description">
       <p>
        AMC's
        <span class="edS-i">
         The Walking Dead
        </span>
        has as many die-hard fans as any other television show on the planet. And if you call yourself a "Walking Dead-Head," then you know tons of little tidbits about your favorite zombie drama.But how many of these
        <span class="edS-i">
         The Walking Dead
        </span>
        facts are you actually familiar with? From where the cast members eat lunch to how many walkers are currently walking the planet, this
        <span class="edS-i">
         Walking Dead
        </span>
        list has got all of the juicy, gory details and factoids about our favorite zom-drom.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Did you know that some of the stunt doubles for
        <span class="edS-i">
         The Walking Dead
        </span>
        actors are pretty hilarious? Like Carl, who is a 15 year-old boy and has a stand-in that is a 31 year-old woman. Another fun piece of
        <span class="edS-i">
         Walking Dead
        </span>
        trivia: the zombies are actually eating ham with barbecue sauce on it, not flesh. Yum!
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <span class="edS-i">
         The Walking Dead
        </span>
        has been nominated for many awards, including the Writers Guild of America Award and the Golden Globe Award for Best Television Series, Drama. Over 17 million people watched the season five premiere, making it the most-watched drama series telecast in basic cable history. If you're reading this list, you probably already know how good the show is, but we're betting you don't know all of these fun behind-the-scenes facts! If you enjoy this fun
        <span class="edS-i">
         The Walking Dead
        </span>
        list, leave a comment and share it with your friends!
