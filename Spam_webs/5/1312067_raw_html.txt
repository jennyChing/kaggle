      <div class="post-entry">
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.frenchweddingstyle.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Aix-en-Provence_wedding.jpg">
         <img alt="Aix en Provence wedding" class="aligncenter wp-image-29697 size-full" height="898" src="http://www.frenchweddingstyle.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Aix-en-Provence_wedding.jpg" title="Aix en Provence wedding | Image by One and Only Paris, read more  http://www.frenchweddingstyle.com/wedding-in-aix-en-provence/ " width="600"/>
        </a>
        Nothing like scenes from the beautiful South of France to brighten up those Monday morning blues and that is what we are doing today, with Christina and Pauls romantic wedding in Aix en Provence planned byElegance in France.
       </p>
       <p>
        Bride Christina is half American and half French and Paul is Canadian. The couple both love art and it is thus quite naturally that they decided to seal their union in Aix en Provence, the city of the talented
        <a href="http://www.paul-cezanne.org/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.paul-cezanne.org/']);" title="Paul Cezanne">
         Paul Czanne
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        The wedding celebrations started the day before, with a rehearsal dinner that was held at an amazing venue dedicated to painting.
       </p>
       <p>
        Audrey from
        <a href="http://oneandonlyparisphotography.com/blog/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://oneandonlyparisphotography.com/blog/']);" title="One and Only Paris Photography">
         One and Only Paris Photography
        </a>
        says: 
        <em>
         The wedding venue was as beautiful as the wedding. Christina wanted something elegant with a touch of Provence. As color scheme, she chose coral to match her Valentino shoes and bridesmaids dresses.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         The ceremony was held in front of the ancient church of the Chateau de Grimaldi. Guests were treated to a cocktail reception that took place poolside amidst the ancient stone ruins.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Following the cocktail hour, guests made their way into the marquee for dinner, collecting their escort card from drawers filled of dry lavender
        </em>
        .
       </p>
