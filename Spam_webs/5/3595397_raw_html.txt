                <div align="left" class="divthougdisp">
                 <p style="float: left;">
                  <span class="contlabel" id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolder1_Labelinsdesc">
                   A team of young entrepreneurs here has designed a wearable device for children that can help parents track their wards to ensure they are safe. Called SureTrack, it comprises a phone and a GPS tracker that can be strapped on the wrist like a watch.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   When a SIM card is inserted into the device, one can receive and make calls. However, it has an application, which can be configured in such a way that the phone can take and receive calls only from specified numbers. So only parents can reach the child while strangers and telemarketers are blocked from contacting the child.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   The application can be used on a device that works on Android and iOS. Poonam Parekh, who heads the team, said the device was attempting to present a technological solution to a social problem.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   Parents can track the childs location in real time both indoors and outdoors as the device has GPS. If there is more than one child to be tracked, two separate profiles can be created on the application. Parents can configure the application on how frequently they want to be alerted on their phones or on the application where the child is (to a minimum of every second). It has a 30-hour battery.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   If parents are too busy to track the child all the time, the geofencing feature in the application allows parents designate safe zones (such as the school or playground) and they receive SMS alerts whenever the child enters or leaves that zone.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   The child can call parents or other family members on the watch with a single touch in emergency situations.
                  </span>
