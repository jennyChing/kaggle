&lt;h1&gt;Last Weekend&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Here in the US, last weekend was a long, three-day weekend. Monday was Memorial Day, a day we honor and remember all those men and women who have lost their lives in service to our country.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-4865" src="http://playgroundparkbench.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/File-May-28-9-15-42-PM-e1432862417838-226x300.jpeg" alt="File May 28, 9 15 42 PM" width="226" height="300" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;For many, it also marks the unofficial first weekend of Summer. Pools open for the season, our neighborhood beach was packed all 3 days, and families bring out their grills for the first of many summer BBQs to come. We were no different.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;While Daddy grilled burgers, the girls played outside &#8211; Lil&#8217; M is officially obsessed with watering everything, including herself, and Big M, has finally mastered the art of blowing her own bubbles!&lt;/p&gt;

  <h1>
   My Favorites from Last Week
  </h1>
  <p>
   My features this week all share a common theme: Schools out for the Summer! Am I the only one who always hears that song in my head when they say that?!? For many, Memorial Day also signals the end of the school year (or almost anyway). Last weeks favorites all share the spirit of the end of the school year and the start of long, fun-filled days of summer.
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="WLW Schools Out for the Summer Features 2015-05-29" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4864" height="650" src="http://playgroundparkbench.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/WLW-Schools-Out-for-the-Summer-Features-2015-05-29-455x650.jpg" width="455"/>
  </p>
  <p>
   First up, from Brain Power Boy,
   <a href="http://brainpowerboy.com/free-summer-activities-for-boys-part-1/">
    Fun &amp; Free Summer Activities
   </a>
   featuring Store Programs. This is the first in a 3 part series  we cant wait to see what else she has to share! I featured a few of these store programs in my post,
   <a href="http://playgroundparkbench.com/2015/01/free-activities-kids-nationwide/">
    Free Activities for Kids Nationwide
   </a>
   earlier this year, but there are definitely some new ones you should check out as well, especially for older kids!
  </p>
  <p>
   Next, summer cannot officially begin without paying homage to our amazing teachers who care for and shape the minds and hearts of our kids all school year long. Nemcsok Farms shares a great guide for the
   <a href="http://www.nemcsokfarms.com/2/post/2015/05/beautifully-simple-gifts-to-make-for-teachers.html">
    Top 10 Teacher Appreciation Gifts
   </a>
   . I think the customized wine labels and seed bombs are among my favorites!
  </p>
  <p>
   And finally, I LOVE this
   <a href="http://www.cuttingtinybites.com/2015/05/ultimate-guide-to-summer-sidewalk-chalk.html">
    Ultimate Guide to Summer Sidewalk Chalk
   </a>
   from Cutting Tiny Bites. At our house, nothing says summer like sidewalk chalk. After water play, it is probably the girls absolute favorite outdoor activity. With 50+ different DIY recipes, games, projects and more, you can choose a different chalk activity for nearly every day of the summer. Im pretty sure we need all of those chalk supplies listed at the end, especially the chalk tatoos and stencils and the bike attachment!
  </p>
  <p>
   Be sure to pay a visit to all my featured bloggers, and let them know how much their post says #WeLoveWeekends!
