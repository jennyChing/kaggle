           <header>
            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Obamas Top Ten New Years Resolutions
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-above">
             Posted on
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2011-01-03T10:18:18+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
              January 3, 2011, 10:18 am
             </time>
             by
             <span class="entry-author vcard" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
              <a class="author-link fn nickname url" href="http://www.whitehousedossier.com/author/kkoffler/" itemprop="url" title="View all posts by Keith Koffler">
               <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
                Keith Koffler
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             
             <a class="entry-comments" href="http://www.whitehousedossier.com/2011/01/03/obamas-top-ten-years-resolutions/#comments" title="Obamas Top Ten New Years Resolutions  Comments">
              6 Comments
             </a>
            </div>
            <!-- .entry-meta -->
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
            <p>
             White House Dossier has obtained a copy of President Obamas lengthy list of New Years Resolutions. We present ten of them here for your perusal
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Note to readers:
             </strong>
             There were 11 resolutions we intended to include here, but after consultations with White House officials, WHD has agreed to redact one of them for purposes of national security. Others were left off the list because they were viewed as impractical or because they appear to have been added by Michelle.
            </p>
            <p>
             Here are the remaining ten resolutions:
            </p>
            <p>
             1. Only bow to world leaders who arent funding terrorism.
            </p>
            <p>
             2. Tell Biden to stop singing to himself during Cabinet meetings.
            </p>
            <p>
             3. Stop crying every time Pelosi yells at me.
            </p>
            <p>
             4. Never leave my wallet out on the table again when Karzai is in the Oval Office.
            </p>
            <p>
             5. Allow trials by military tribunal, but only for that guy who elbowed me in the lip.
            </p>
            <p>
             6. No more late nights drinking and gambling with Hillary, Geithner, and Harry Reid.
            </p>
            <p>
             7. Take it to the Taliban by beginning new Shoot First and Ask Questions Later policy.
            </p>
            <p>
             8. Continue to allow bake sales on school premises as long as funds are used to reduce the deficit.
            </p>
            <p>
             9. Lose a little weight.
            </p>
            <p>
             10. Stop doing Michelles resolutions for her.
            </p>
