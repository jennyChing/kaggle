        <article class="x post-3351 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-art-and-design tag-artist tag-france tag-fun tag-gif tag-guillaume-kurkdjian tag-illustration tag-nantes tag-new-art tag-tumblr" id="post-3351">
         <p class="p1">
          GIFs (Graphic Interchange Formats) have come a long way since 1987. From the primitive graphic elements used in our
          <a href="http://www.theworldsworstwebsiteever.com/" target="_blank">
           first cheesy web designs
          </a>
          , they morphed into
          <a href="http://cdn0.dailydot.com/uploaded/images/original/2013/6/13/ferngif.gif" target="_blank">
           dancing babies
          </a>
          , ruled the cyber-world as the onlypre-PNG transparent image option, and eventually spawned millions of varyingly amusing
          <a href="http://33.media.tumblr.com/tumblr_m6v7o84SuM1rxevt4o1_250.gif" target="_blank">
           moving internet memes.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Yet only now, it seems, The Age Of The GIF has arrived. Astalented modern digital artists begin to realize the potential of this compressible online image sequence, the GIF has reached itslatest and greatest state of evolution: its own art form.
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          <img alt="food_truck3" height="300" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/food_truck3.gif" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          <span class="s1">
           One of the young pioneers ofthis new mediumis
          </span>
          digital artist, illustrator, photographer and animator
          <span class="s1">
           <a href="http://bisouslescopains.tumblr.com/">
            Guillaume Kurkdjian
           </a>
          </span>
          from Nantes, France. His isometric3D GIF animations aresimplistic to perfection. Throughseconds-long stories and dreamy color palettes Kurkdjianmanages to achieve one of any entertainers biggest challenges: putting a smile on audiences faces. His creations conjure a candy-colored world of innocence and care-free charm, which chimes with the artists sources of inspiration:
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
         </p>
         <div class="su-quote su-quote-style-default su-quote-has-cite">
          <div class="su-quote-inner su-clearfix">
           I love to see little houses, cabins lost in the nature and imagine stories with them, I like means of transport in general and lovely characters. My inspiration also comes from all the colorful isometric creations and animations I found on tumblr, behance or vimeo.
           <span class="su-quote-cite">
            <a href="http://www.dailydot.com/lol/morning-gif-guillaume-kurkdjian/" target="_blank">
             Guillaume Kurkdjian
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p class="p3">
          <img alt="machine3_dribbble" class="alignnone wp-image-3358 size-full" height="300" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/machine3_dribbble.gif" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          <img alt="benne" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3356" height="300" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/benne.gif" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          <img alt="maison_toit_dribbble" class="alignnone wp-image-3359 size-full" height="300" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/maison_toit_dribbble.gif" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          <img alt="tumblr_n5k5gbxyc11rn9vmdo1_r1_500" class="alignnone wp-image-3363 size-full" height="500" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_n5k5gbxyc11rn9vmdo1_r1_500.gif" width="500"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          <a href="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_inline_n085prZG6I1r5de35.gif">
           <img alt="tumblr_inline_n085prZG6I1r5de35" class="alignnone wp-image-3362 size-full" height="384" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_inline_n085prZG6I1r5de35.gif" width="500"/>
           <span class="hover_state">
           </span>
          </a>
          <a class="fancybox alignnone wp-image-3361 size-full hover_img" href="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_inline_n1col1OeUU1r5de35.gif">
           <img alt="tumblr_inline_n1col1OeUU1r5de35" height="460" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_inline_n1col1OeUU1r5de35.gif" width="500"/>
           <span class="hover_state">
           </span>
          </a>
          <a class="fancybox alignnone wp-image-3360 size-full hover_img" href="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_inline_my9lpeTwbV1r5de35.gif">
           <img alt="tumblr_inline_my9lpeTwbV1r5de35" height="470" src="http://nuomagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/tumblr_inline_my9lpeTwbV1r5de35.gif" width="450"/>
           <span class="hover_state">
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p class="p3">
          Besides Kurkdjiansadorablepersonal work, the French artist has worked with number of companies for commercial enterprises. If you would like to explore his portfolio further, check out hishis
          <a href="http://guillaumekurkdjian.com/" target="_blank">
           website
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://bisouslescopains.tumblr.com/" target="_blank">
           Tumblr
          </a>
          account.
