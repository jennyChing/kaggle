          <h3>
           L.J.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           Jenkins
          </h3>
         </div>
         <p>
          L.J. Jenkins is one of the top 35 bull riders in the world.
         </p>
         <p>
          As a competitor on the Professional Bull Riders (PBR) circuit, the 27-year-old travels all over the country riding at various events. The season lasts from January to May and then picks up again from August through October, when the PBR holds its World Finals.
         </p>
         <p>
          During these times, Jenkins is traveling and riding almost every weekend. Its a grueling schedule that leaves him with little time to sleep, but he loves what he does.
         </p>
         <p>
          I started when I was six years old. My dad did it, my family did it. Its in my blood, he said. I really had no other choice than to be a bull rider.
         </p>
         <p>
          Now on his eleventh tour, Jenkins has made some $1.7 million riding bulls at PBR events. He has also earned additional money from sponsorships.
         </p>
         <p>
          His goal: Have enough money to retire by age 30.
         </p>
         <p>
          I would probably keep going, but I dont want to be a guy thats broke up and cant get out of bed at 50, he said.
         </p>
         <p class="journal-intro">
          Here are journal entries from a day in his life:
         </p>
