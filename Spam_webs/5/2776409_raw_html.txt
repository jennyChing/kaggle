           <img alt="Travel Asia: Travel Trends &amp; Technology - Social Media - Lenovo Blogs" src="http://blog.lenovo.com/uploads/general/AsiaRooms_masthead.jpg"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           People in Asia are more likely to use technology in booking or researching travel, a recent survey finds. The survey conducted by
           <a href="http://www.asiarooms.com">
            AsiaRooms.com
           </a>
           and Lenovo found that people in Asia are up to 10 percent more likely to book leisure and business travel accommodations online compared to people in the rest of the world, while more than half in Asia search accommodation prices on mobile devices.
          </p>
