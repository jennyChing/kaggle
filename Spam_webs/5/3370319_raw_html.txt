       <div class="post_excerpt">
        <p>
         More of the posion fruit of Obamas disastrous foreign policy failure in Libya (and Egypt and Syria and Iraq and Israel .)
        </p>
        <p>
         I wonder of they used Obama-provided weapons
        </p>
        <p>
         Hey, its the sharia. Obama say, respect it!
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Filipino beheaded in Libya for not being a Muslim, By Times Live, July 21, 2014 (thanks to
          <a href="http://midnightwatcher.wordpress.com/2014/07/21/libya-islamists-behead-construction-worker-for-not-being-a-muslim/">
           Midnight Watcher)
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Suspected militiamen have beheaded a Philippine worker in Libya after singling him out because he was not a Muslim
          </strong>
          , the Philippine Foreign Department said.Department spokesman Charles Jose said this reinforced the governments order to all Filipinos to evacuate the country because of the worsening security situation.
         </p>
         <p>
          He told reporters the Filipino construction worker was kidnapped on July 15 and his beheaded remains were found in a hospital on July 20.
         </p>
         <p>
          The vehicle he was riding in was stopped in a checkpoint. There were three of them a Libyan, a Pakistani and a Filipino  and he was allegedly singled out because he was non-Muslim, said Jose.
         </p>
         <p>
          The kidnappers had initially negotiated with the Filipinos company for a $160,000 ransom but eventually they received a call from the abductors on July 20 (that) referred them to a Benghazi hospital, he added.
         </p>
         <p>
          The beheaded body was found in the hospital in an advanced state of decomposition, indicating he may have been dead even while the negotiations were going on, Jose added.
         </p>
         <p>
          He indicated that the killing prompted the Philippines on Sunday to order its estimated 13,000 nationals in Libya to leave.
         </p>
         <p>
          The threats to our Filipinos safety and security became more imminent. We would like to get them out of harms way because of this incident, Jose added.
         </p>
         <p>
          Filipinos can leave Libya by travelling by land to either Tunisia or Egypt, he added.
         </p>
         <p>
          So far 207 Filipinos have registered for evacuation and will be brought back to Manila as soon as the Philippine embassy there completes their travel requirements, he said.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
