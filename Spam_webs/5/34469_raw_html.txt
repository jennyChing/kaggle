         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/detox-drink.jpg" value="if-its-good-enough-for-gwyneth-3-crazy-celebrity-spa-detoxes">
           <img alt="detox-drink" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4921" height="301" src="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/detox-drink.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Oh Gwyneth. Dear, sweet Gwyneth. It seems that no matter what you do, even with our best interests at heart, your ideas always spark controversy. And why not start 2014 with a bang? Gwyneth Paltrows latest detox diet has whipped up a real storm online thanks to its miniscule calorie count and heavy reliance on herbal teas and hot water.
         </p>
         <p>
          But shes got one thing right. If there was ever a time of year for a detox its after Christmas. Roast turkey with all the trimmings, Christmas pudding drowned in brandy butter and cream, and the constant drip feeding of chocolates adds up to a hefty intake of fat and sugar. And all that gets washed down with more than your usual amount of alcohol, leaving your kidneys and liver screaming for mercy.
         </p>
         <p>
          We thought wed have a look at some of the crazy celebrity detoxes that have been doing the rounds, and enlist the help of detox specialists from
          <a href="http://www.purespauk.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" value="if-its-good-enough-for-gwyneth-3-crazy-celebrity-spa-detoxes">
           <strong>
            PURE Spa
           </strong>
          </a>
          to assess their effectiveness and suggest some less extreme alternatives.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/pure-spa.jpg" value="if-its-good-enough-for-gwyneth-3-crazy-celebrity-spa-detoxes">
           <img alt="pure-spa" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4923" height="304" src="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/pure-spa.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Image courtesy of PURE Spa
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           1. Gwyneths 2014 Winter Detox
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Enlisting the help of her physician Dr. Alejandro Junger, Paltrows detox focuses on drinking tons of herbal tea and eatingnot a whole lot. The food is actually high quality, with poached salmon, root vegetable salad and mixed seeds and nuts on the menu. However theres very little of it, with an average calorie intake of 300 a day. Its a 3-day detox that promises to totally cleanse your system.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           PURE Spa: This is a relatively tame celebrity diet, particularly as it consists of food with healthy nutrients such as Omega 3 (which is good for your skin). Our suggestion? Enjoy a prolonged detox for a whole month, mirroring the diet with a higher calorie intake. The most important part of any lifestyle change is happiness  stress can be detrimental not only to your mental welfare, but your physical wellbeing too.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/lemonade.jpg" value="if-its-good-enough-for-gwyneth-3-crazy-celebrity-spa-detoxes">
           <img alt="lemonade" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4924" height="313" src="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/lemonade.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           2. Beyoncs Master Cleanse
          </strong>
          <br/>
          This detox has been around for some time, since the 1950s, and even though its creator Stanley Burroughs was convicted of practising medicine without a licence and faced other criminal charges, it came back to prominence when Beyonc sang its praises. For 10 days youll consume nothing except 6 to 10 glasses of a drink comprising: water, lemon juice, maple syrup and a dash of cayenne pepper. Proponents of the diet claim it detoxes the body and reduces cravings for junk food.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           PURE Spa: A hungry stomach is an unhappy one  and whilst we support healthy eating and regular exercise here at PURE, there are alternatives to starving yourself. A spa facial is perfect for drawing out impurities in your face, whilst some heat therapy in an infra-red sauna can help you sweat out the rest. Top it off with a specialist massage and your body will feel refreshed. Whats that old phrase? Eat, spa, love?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/green-juice-diet.jpg" value="if-its-good-enough-for-gwyneth-3-crazy-celebrity-spa-detoxes">
           <img alt="green-juice-diet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4925" height="308" src="http://cdn.spafinder.com/sites/4/2014/01/green-juice-diet.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3. Giseles Marthas Vineyard Detox
          </strong>
          <br/>
          We cant promise this detox will make you look as radiant as the Brazilian supermodel, but she swears by it. This detox is 21 days long, so not for the faint hearted, and promises to help you lose 21 pounds and improve your energy levels and blood pressure. And how? A liquid diet made up of: fruit and vegetable juice, protein shakes, a variety of cleanse drinks and herbal teas and gallons of water. Oh and regular colonic treatments and coffee enemas!
         </p>
