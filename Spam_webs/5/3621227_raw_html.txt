      <h2>
       Responsive Web Design
      </h2>
      <p>
       Browsing your favorite websites on multiple devices certainly gives a sense of comfort and ease to you.  Whether you use a Smartphone, a Tablet or an iPad, only a website design compatible to all these devices would help you to browse these sites without clutter. This is where responsive
       <strong>
        website designing services
       </strong>
       come into the focus. The responsive website designing brings a coding practice that gives websites a layout and navigation that fits on the device.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Innovative Responsive Website Designing (RWD) Solutions
      </h3>
      <p>
       At BrainPulse, we understand how important your business is and how vital its device independent functionality. That is why we take your website very seriously and begins its designing phase with a philosophy based on the device-independent user interface adhere layout and design. The practice helps us to offer viewers maximum user experience on different devices. Using a responsive model, we adjust the layout of the websites as per the device screen being used. Our
       <strong>
        Responsive website designing
       </strong>
       India model is the finest combination of technologies like
       <strong>
