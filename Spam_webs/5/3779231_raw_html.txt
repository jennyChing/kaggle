        <span class="post_date" title="2011-04-20">
         April 20, 2011
        </span>
        <a class="num_comments_link" href="https://www.singularityweblog.com/legacy-ark-and-the-3rd-letter-the-dark-post-apocalyptic-sci-fi-films-of-grzegorz-jonkajtys/#disqus_thread" rel="nofollow">
         Comments
        </a>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="post_content" itemprop="articleBody">
       <div class="pf-content">
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-9624 alignright" height="126" src="https://www.singularityweblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/legacy.jpg" title="legacy" width="108"/>
         Last night I randomly discovered the amazing short sci fi films of
         <a href="http://www.3dluvr.com/jonkajtys/profile/profile.html" target="_blank">
          Grzegorz Jonkajtys
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         The three movies, that Grzegorz has posted on Vimeo in full, vary in length, style and execution. What is common is that all are situated in a post-apocalyptic setting and, in my opinion, all are strangely mesmerizing with their own unique dark beauty.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Legacy
         </strong>
         is the shortest of the 3 films. At 2 minutes and 24 seconds it manages to pack not only some cool special effects but also a powerful warning message.
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="422" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/3120879?byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=c9ff23" width="750">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ark
         </strong>
         is another post-apocalyptic animated film. The plot is set up against the background of a global virus outbreak which has forced the remaining survivors to build huge Ark ships and escape to the seas in search of uninhabited land.
        </p>
        <p>
         At 7 min long the movie packs a moving musical score as well as some Tim Burton style of dark anime.
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="422" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/3116167?byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=c9ff23" width="750">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          The 3rd Letter
         </strong>
         is Grzegorzs newest film. At 15 minutes long it is also the only non-animated one.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9629" height="168" src="https://www.singularityweblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/the-3rd-letter.jpg" title="the-3rd-letter" width="350"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Perhaps the most disturbing of all three movies, it is set up in a dystopian future where humans can survive their toxic environment only with the help of bio-mechanical pacemakers.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="422" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/11339453?byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=c9ff23" width="750">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Who is Grzegorz Jonkajtys?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-9592" height="150" src="https://www.singularityweblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Grzegorz-Jonkajtys.jpg" title="Grzegorz Jonkajtys" width="98"/>
         Grzegorz Jonkajtys was born in Warsaw, Poland, in 1972. He graduated from Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw in 1995 and has been working for advertisement, animation and feature film industry for over 12 years.
        </p>
        <p>
         While working in Poland and USA Grzegorz has contributed as vfx artist and animation lead for such films as
         <em>
          Sin City
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Hellboy
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Blade III
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Pans Labyrinth
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Mist
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          Terminator Salvation
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         He is best known for writing and directing his second film
         <em>
          ARK
         </em>
         for which he has been nominated for Golden Palm at Cannes film festival in 2007, and won Siggraph Electronic Theater Best of Show award the same year.
        </p>
        <p>
         Grzegorz lives and works in San Francisco.
        </p>

        </div>
