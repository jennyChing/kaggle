                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  3. Create a budget and live within your means. Accruing
lots of debt will only cause you to feel stressed.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  4. Kill off the monster of jealousy  and only compare
yourself with yourself.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  5. Organize your clutter and get rid of excess stuff. It
will leave you feeling calmer, and will save you lots of time!
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  6. Stay on the sidelines and dont get drawn into
pointless scenes and dramas in other peoples lives (unless its a crisis  and
you know you ought to help).
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  7. Finish what youve started - and then do something
else.
                  <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  8. Treat every person you meet with respect, and err on
the side of being gentle and kind.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  9. Accept that there are things that you cant change or
control  and focus on those things that you can change or control.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Gisha","sans-serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  10. Admit when you were wrong, then say youre sorry, and
move on.
                 </span>
                </div>
               </div>
