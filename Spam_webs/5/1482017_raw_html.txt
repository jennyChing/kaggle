                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Walk The Line :: Tramlines Festival 2014
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5038834164134659007" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <a href="http://www.tramlines.org.uk/" target="_blank">
                      Tramlines Festival
                     </a>
                     is a relatively young event held in Sheffield every year (although this is year number six, crikey!), it started off very small and in just a few years has grown and grown and grown. It features local and non-local artists alike, and is overall a really great way to spend a summery weekend.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-R6mKxEv93bA/U9UaeN3ydTI/AAAAAAAAGd4/W3YmYGWy9_k/s1600/IMG_1109.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     I know this will sound a little weird, but I didn't really have any bands in mind that I was desperate to see. I like festivals more for the atmosphere personally. I did really want to see Deap Vally, but they were on quite late and by that point I'd had a few drinks and lost track of time - dancing like a loon in Gatsby was far more fun!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I started the day at
                     <a href="http://thefolkforest.net/" target="_blank">
                      The Folk Forest
                     </a>
                     at Endcliffe Park, where we endured the sun and listened to
                     <a href="http://littlerobots.bandcamp.com/" target="_blank">
                      Little Robots
                     </a>
                     play.
