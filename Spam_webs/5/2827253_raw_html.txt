         My family eats a ton of cereal weekly. TONS! While I love cooking a big breakfast on the weekends, during the week we just dont have the time.

        <p>
         My husband alone eats probably a box of cereal a week. Mostly a bowl for breakfast, but we also love dry cereal for snack time and even cereal treats!
        </p>
        <p>
         Cereal treats are perfect, because they are easy to make, taste yummy, and are a fun alternative to cakes and other bar treats. Plus, there are just so many varieties of cereals to use.
        </p>
