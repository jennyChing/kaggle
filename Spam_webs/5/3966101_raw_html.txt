     <h2>
      The Werkstatt, 7-9 Woodbridge Street, Clerkenwell, London, EC1R 0LL
     </h2>
     <div class="clearfix">
      <p>
       <b>
        The Werkstatt - meaning workshop in German  is a show-case for the whole Sto Group and a new east London cultural establishment with a lively program of exhibitions, talks, workshops and consultations.
       </b>
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Samples resource in Clerkenwell London
       </b>
       <br/>
       A full range of Sto Groups products are at the Werkstatt for architects to play and create with, including glass and rendered rainscreen cladding, seamless acoustics, facade elements and photo catalytic interior paint coating. See
       <a href="http://www.stoviewer.com/" target="_blank" title="">
        stoviewer.com
       </a>
       for further details.
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        There to help realise your ideas
       </b>
       <br/>
       The building is intended to be all about communication and collaboration, a place for encounter and inspiration, a place of creativity: the new Werkstatt of the Sto group. If there is truth in the phrase the objective of all communication is cooperation, then the Werkstatt is the place where this process should begin, where a coming together is transformed into exchange, where the exchange is part of the creative process.
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Global presence, local knowledge
       </b>
       <br/>
       The Werkstatt also extends out from its hub in Clerkenwell to offer connections to Stos international network of technical experts with local and global knowledge. The Werkstatt is a workshop for international designers and architects to meet, hear, see, be inspired, play and create.
      </p>
