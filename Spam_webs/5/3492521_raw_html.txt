       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-02-05T13:14:37+00:00">
         February 5, 2015
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">

        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="16287961" data-link="http://wonkywonderful.com/tiramisu-white-chocolate-bread-pudding/" data-summary="" data-title="Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding">
        </div>
        <p>
         <em>
          This is a sponsored post written by me on behalf of
          <a href="http://www.kingshawaiian.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Kings Hawaiian

        <h2>
         ThisTiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding will be the star of your next party. A blend of coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate, mascarpone and soft buttery Kings Hawaiian Sweet Rolls.
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-7.jpg">
          <img alt="Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding - This individual dessert recipe is flavored with coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate and mascapone." class="alignnone wp-image-6744 size-full" height="1513" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-7.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Award season is upon us. So it is the perfect time to plan a dinner party!
        </p>
        <p>
         Forget the snowdrifts outside and cozy up for a fun night with friends and family. And of course, every party needs appetizers, desserts, drinks and a little sparkle!
        </p>
        <p>
         Kings Hawaiian is helping you to plan your own show-stopping awards party. Visit their site,
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1zcN084" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Starringroll.com
         </a>
         , for recipes and party tips. Submit your creative party ideas for a chance to
         <strong>
          WIN $10,000!!!
         </strong>
         <em>
          {see official rules below}
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         My #1 party tip is to choose some recipes that can be made the day before your party. That way you are not completely frazzled and are able to enjoy yourself at your own party.
        </p>
        <p>
         ThisTiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding is the perfect party dessert because it needs to be made the day before. Just prep, bake and then refrigerate overnight. Dress them up with masarpone and shaved chocolate for a fancy party treat!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding - This individual dessert recipe is flavored with coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate and mascapone." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6745" height="1392" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-2.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Preparing this bread pudding is beyond simple. Cut your Kings Hawaiian Rolls. Chop your favorite chocolate. Mix together. Pour liquids over bread. Bake. Refrigerate. Done.
        </p>
        <p>
         I used Kings Hawaiian Sweet Dinner Rolls. But, you can also use the Honey Wheat or Savory Butter varieties as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding - This individual dessert recipe is flavored with coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate and mascapone." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6746" height="900" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-1.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Baked to perfection. A soft flavorful center with a toasted top layer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-3.jpg">
          <img alt="Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding - This individual dessert recipe is flavored with coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate and mascapone." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6747" height="1024" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-3.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         After the bread pudding has been refrigerated overnight, use a round cookie cutter to create individual desserts.
        </p>
        <p>
         The leftover bread pudding pieces can be served in martini glasses!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Tiramisu White Chocolate Bread Pudding - This individual dessert recipe is flavored with coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate and mascapone." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6748" height="665" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/tiramisu-bread-pudding-recipe-4.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So get your party started!
        </p>

         <div class="blog-yumprint-header">

         <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-contents">
          <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="0">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Bread Pudding
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
             Original King's Hawaiian Sweet 12 Pack Dinner Rolls
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
             2 ounces White Chocolate - chopped
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
             2 ounces Milk Chocolate - chopped
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="3">
             1 Cup Heavy Cream
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="4">
             1 teaspoon Instant Espresso Powder
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="5">
             1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="6">
             3 Large Eggs - lightly beaten
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="7">
             1/2 Cup Sugar
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="1">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Dusting
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
             1 teaspoon Unsweetened Cocoa
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
             1/4 teaspoon Instant Espresso Powder
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
             1 teaspoon Powdered Sugar
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="2">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Topping
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
             8 ounces Mascarpone
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
             3 Tablespoons Powdered Sugar
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
             Grated or Shaved Chocolate for Garnish
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="3">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Bread Pudding
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Preheat oven to 350F
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
             Cut each roll into 4 pieces.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="2">
             In an 8x8 baking dish sprayed with nonstick, layer bread pieces and chopped chocolate.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="3">
             Gently press down.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="4">
             Mix cream, espresso powder, vanilla, eggs and sugar. Pour evenly over bread pieces, making sure every area is moistened with cream mixture.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="5">
             Let sit 10 minutes then gently press down to flatten.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="6">
             Bake for 30 minutes or until top is golden brown.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="7">
             Refrigerate overnight.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="8">
             Cut into individual rounds using a 3" cookie cutter. (Makes about 5 individual bread pudding rounds. Serve leftover bread pudding scraps in dishes)
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="4">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Dusting
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Mix cocoa, espresso and powdered sugar. Dust over bread puddings.
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="5">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Topping
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Mix mascarpone and powdered sugar until smooth. Refrigerate 15 minutes before serving. Top each bread pudding with a dollop of mascarpone.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
             Top with shaved or grated chocolate.
            </li>
           </ol>
