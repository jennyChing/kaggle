       <h1>
        What are Digital Heat Transfers?
       </h1>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.cdigital.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/sb10067658j-001.jpg">
          <img alt="digital heat transfer" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-93" height="225" src="http://www.cdigital.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/sb10067658j-001-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         If theres one thing that we discuss a lot at Grafixx, its the
         <a href="http://www.cdigital.com/the_process.php">
          digital heat transfer
         </a>
          but its for a good reason. We stand 100% behind the use of digital heat transfers because they far exceed all other printing methods. With up to ten
         <i>
          million
         </i>
         colors to choose from and a process that can be undertaken economically and quickly, it really is the best solution for printing on glass, plastic, and other materials. All of the benefits of digital heat transfers is a conversation for another day, though. Lets delve a little deeper in and learn more about what a digital heat transfer is and how it works!
        </p>
        <h2>
         What Exactly is a Digital Heat Transfer?
        </h2>
        <p>
         Digital heat transfers are used for
         <b>
          product decorating
         </b>
         , which is important for almost every company and organization out there. While there are many different ways to print on objects today, digital heat transfers are one of the newest, fastest, most economic ways to do so. The important question: how do they work?
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          The first thing to know is that
          <b>
           digital heat transfers
          </b>
          use heat, time, and pressure to apply a logo or other image to objects such as shot glasses, plastic water bottles, and even clothing. Its a dry process that requires minimal effort.
         </li>
         <li>
          Digital heat transfers are pre-printed graphics. You can use nearly any graphic in the world and know that youre going to get perfect quality out of it because of how the four different colors digital heat transfers use. It allows for essentially any color in the visible spectrum to be printed.
         </li>
         <li>
          There is no messy ink or cure time required for digital heat transfers, meaning its very difficult to mess anything up. Your image will look exactly like its supposed to once it is on your product. A digital heat transfer only needs heat and pressure to properly transfer the digital image to your product.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <h2>
         Why Should I Choose Digital Heat Transfers?
        </h2>
        <p>
         A digital heat transfer can save you time, money, and frustration. You will get exactly what you ask for in an economical way. You arent going to pay extra just because you want to do a short run production and youll get your product back quickly since everything is done digitally.
        </p>
