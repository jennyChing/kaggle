              <p>
               Celebrate the summer solstice weekend at the sixth instalment of the Body&amp;Soul Festival in Westmeath this coming June.
              </p>
             </div>
             <!-- Item fulltext -->
             <div class="itemFullText">
              <p>
               <a href="http://bodyandsoul.ie" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Body&amp;Soul
                </strong>
               </a>
               is back for the sixth time in
               <strong>
                Ballinlough Castle, Co. Westmeath
               </strong>
               from Friday June 19th to Sunday June 21st 2015, and the initial line-up has just been announced.
              </p>
              <p>
               So far this year were looking at:
              </p>
              <p>
               <em>
                Flight Facilities, Savages, sgeir, Austra, Goat, Nightmares on Wax, Rhye, Lamb, Matthew E White, Dan Deacon, Talaboman (John Talabot and Axel Boman), James Holden, Clark, SOHN, Mmoths, Brandt Brauer Frick, Songhoy Blues, Natalie Prass, Shit Robot, Alo Wala, Oy, New Jackson, Jonathan Toubin, Meltybrains? King Kong Company
               </em>
               and
               <em>
                Grounds For Invasion.
               </em>
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="Body&amp;Soul lineup 2015" src="/images/body-soul-2015-lineup.jpg"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               Stay tuned for more acts for the
               <strong>
                art and music festival
               </strong>
               coming soon.
              </p>
              <p>
               This year the organisers are offering a
               <a href="http://www.ticketmaster.ie/promo/51qzc6?camefrom=CFC_IE_AFF_BODYANDSOUL" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 five-tier ticket system
                </strong>
               </a>
               . Tier 1, 2, 3 have now sold out. A limited number
               <strong>
                Tier 4 tickets
               </strong>
               (139.50 incl booking fee) are currently available and
               <strong>
                Tier 5
               </strong>
               (149.50 incl booking fee) will come on sale when they have sold out. Last year the event was a total sell-out so get in quick!
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="Body&amp;Soul 2015 festival" class="cke-resize" src="/images/body-soul-2015-banner.jpg"/>
              </p>

               <strong>

                <br/>
                Co. Westmeath
               </strong>
              </p>
