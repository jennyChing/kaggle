         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <p>
           <img alt="social media content management with Mark Lerner" class="aligncenter wp-image-6522 size-full" height="300" src="http://trentdyrsmid.wpengine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/mark-lerner-interview-2_0.jpg" width="689"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mark Lerner is the Director of Marketing for Oktopost, a
           <a href="http://trentdyrsmid.wpengine.com/how-to-self-publish-maximize-sales-and-dramatically-boost-your-social-media-following-with-guy-kawasaki/" target="_blank" title="How to Self Publish, Maximize Sales, and Dramatically Boost Your Social Media Following with Guy Kawasaki">
            social media marketing
           </a>
           and content distribution platform for B2B companies. Mark is a jack-of-all-trades and his roles include social media marketing, content marketing and distribution, business development, and growth hacking.
          </p>
          <p>
           Groove Digital Marketing has recently started using Oktopost in our own business and we really enjoy it. (Hint: try it out, they offer a free trial.)
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are not familiar with re-purposing content, this interview will show you how to save a ton of time and produce a high volume of content with less effort than you think. Of course, you will also learn how to use Oktopost for social media content management and promotion.
          </p>
          <p>
           Listen now and youll hear Mark and I talk about:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            (02:30) Introduction
           </li>
           <li>
            (03:40) How did you
            <a href="http://trentdyrsmid.wpengine.com/targeted-audience/" target="_blank" title="How (and why) to Define a Targeted Audience for Your Marketing Campaign">
             define your target audience
            </a>
            ?
           </li>
           <li>
            (07:10) How did you build relationships with influencers?
           </li>
