         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-320.html" target="_blank" title="Bathroom">
          bathroom
         </a>
         is simultaneously one of the most difficult spaces in your home to light well, and the place where its most important to do so. While lighting for function and ambiance is important everywhere, inside and outside of your home, the bathroom is where you do your grooming, and the one place to get yourself looking the way you want before you step outside. Inadequate lighting in the bathroom means that not only will your morning routine be a little dark and dull, but also that you wont be putting your best face forward. So what can you do to improve task lighting in your bathroom? Consider investing in backlit mirrors.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4339" style="width: 307px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-fl3.html">
          <img alt="Fly FL3 Backlighted Mirror And Bathroom Vanity From Iotti" class="size-full wp-image-4339" height="400" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Fly-FL3-Backlighted-Mirror-And-Bathroom-Vanity-From-Iotti.jpg" title="Fly FL3 Backlighted Mirror And Bathroom Vanity From Iotti" width="297"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Fly FL3 Backlighted Mirror And Bathroom Vanity From Iotti
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Typically
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/mirrors-361.html" target="_blank" title="Bathroom Mirrors">
          bathroom mirrors
         </a>
         are surrounded by lights, with a bar of lights on top or a sconce on either side. But though these lights are bright, they inevitably cast light from above or the sides, which shadows parts of your face, making it more difficult to shave or apply makeup. Backlit mirrors like this
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-fl3.html" target="_blank" title='35.8" Bathroom Vanity Iotti FL3 from Fly Collection'>
          Fly F3
         </a>
         cast light from inside the mirror and directly in front of you, ensuring that there wont be any shadows on your face at all.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Shop Iotti Bathroom Sets:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe height="300" src="http://www.homethangs.com/shop/shopdisplayproductsblog.asp?id=384&amp;b=iotti" style="border: solid 7px #fdd4b0; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 15px;" width="645">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4340" style="width: 450px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/vigo/p/vigo-vg09003106k.html">
          <img alt="Backlighted Mirror And Wall Mounted Vanity From Vigo Industries" class="size-full wp-image-4340" height="440" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Backlighted-Mirror-And-Wall-Mounted-Vanity-From-Vigo-Industries.jpg" title="Backlighted Mirror And Wall Mounted Vanity From Vigo Industries" width="440"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Backlighted Mirror And Wall Mounted Vanity From Vigo Industries
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         So how does that work? Backlit mirrors, sometimes called backlighted mirrors or electric mirrors, are essentially mirrors with LED lights imbedded inside the mirror. Backlit mirrors like this
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/vigo/p/vigo-vg09003106k.html" target="_blank" title="Vigo VG09003106K - 31-inch single Bathroom Vanity with mirror and lighting system">
          Backlighted Mirror
         </a>
         produce a pure white light that gives a true sense of color as well as a shadow-free reflection, making it easier than ever to get a genuine sense of what youll look like outside the bathroom, even without any natural lighting.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Shop Vigo Bathroom Sets:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe height="300" src="http://www.homethangs.com/shop/shopdisplayproductsblog.asp?id=384&amp;b=vigo" style="border: solid 7px #fdd4b0; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 15px;" width="645">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4338" style="width: 410px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-fl7.html">
          <img alt="Fly FL7 Backlighted Mirror And Vanity From Iotti" class="size-full wp-image-4338" height="400" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Fly-FL7-Backlighted-Mirror-And-Vanity-From-Iotti.jpg" title="Fly FL7 Backlighted Mirror And Vanity From Iotti" width="400"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Fly FL7 Backlighted Mirror And Vanity From Iotti
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         The lights in backlighted mirrors are typically embedded in the sides or around the frame of the mirror so they wont block your reflection. That said, Ive seen some pretty cool custom backlit mirrors that have elaborate, bubbly patterns of lights that decorate (as well as light up) a full wall. In a way, something like this
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-fl7.html" target="_blank" title='40.1" Bathroom Vanity Iotti FL7 from Fly Collection'>
          Fly FL7
         </a>
         is an answer to the old Hollywood vanities and makeup mirrors, replacing the big round bulbs with more subtle circular designs, keeping the impression of the old style but giving it a modern twist.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4341" style="width: 420px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-nc3.html">
          <img alt="Concept One NC3 Electric Mirror From Iotti" class="size-full wp-image-4341" height="410" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Concept-One-NC3-Electric-Mirror-From-Iotti.jpg" title="Concept One NC3 Electric Mirror From Iotti" width="410"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Concept One NC3 Backlit Mirror From Iotti
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         However big or small the mirror or how many lights it has, and however pretty it might be, the primary function is to illuminate your face. It can be difficult to find the right balance of ambient light in a bathroom  especially if youre building a big, luxurious dream bathroom  but backlit mirrors have little or nothing to do with elaborate
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/blog/2012/02/looking-for-the-perfect-signature-touch-for-your-bathroom-try-a-chandelier-for-a-great-statement-piece-with-just-about-any-decor/" target="_blank" title="Bathroom Chandeliers">
          bathroom chandeliers
         </a>
         or any of your other
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/blog/2012/02/inspired-bathroom-lighting-how-to-brighten-and-highlight-your-beautiful-bathroom-decor/" target="_blank" title="Layered Bathroom Lighting">
          layered bathroom lighting
         </a>
         . Something like this
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-nc3.html" target="_blank" title='27.5" Bathroom Vanity Iotti NC3 from Concept One Collection'>
          Concept One Mirror
         </a>
         can more than easily replace an above the mirror light or a pair of sconces, but even in a relatively small bathroom, it wont be enough to light the whole room.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4342" style="width: 370px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-nd2.html">
          <img alt="Dune ND2 Backlighted Mirror And Vanity From Iotti" class="size-full wp-image-4342" height="360" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Dune-ND2-Backlighted-Mirror-And-Vanity-From-Iotti.jpg" title="Dune ND2 Backlighted Mirror And Vanity From Iotti" width="360"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Dune ND2 Backlighted Mirror And Vanity From Iotti
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         In that light, you want to think of backlighted mirrors as an upgrade or an add-on (like makeup mirrors) rather than a replacement or alternative for your existing lighting. That said, its one of my favorite (and oft-overlooked) little luxury upgrades that youll actually use every day. While a whirlpool tub might sit unused for weeks at a time, having a backlit mirror is something youll enjoy every time you step in front of it 
         <em>
          especially
         </em>
         if you have a dark or windowless bathroom. Even one thats only partially lit, or lit on one side, like this
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-nd2.html" target="_blank" title='41.3" Bathroom Vanity Iotti ND2 from Dune Collection'>
          Dune Backlit Mirror
         </a>
         can add a whole lot of functionality to your bathroom.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4343" style="width: 370px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-nc6.html">
          <img alt="Concept One NC6 Backlit Mirror And Vanity From Iotti" class="size-full wp-image-4343" height="360" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Concept-One-NC6-Backlit-Mirror-And-Vanity-From-Iotti.jpg" title="Concept One NC6 Backlit Mirror And Vanity From Iotti" width="360"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Concept One NC6 Backlit Mirror And Vanity From Iotti
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         If youre getting ready to do a significant bathroom remodel, this is a good time to consider backlit mirrors, too. As yet, backlit mirrors arent particularly common, but many high end bathroom designers like
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/iotti-b39272.html" target="_blank" title="Iotti">
          Iotti
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/vigo-b14720.html" target="_blank" title="Vigo Industries">
          Vigo
         </a>
         often package backlighted mirrors with some of their modern bathroom vanities, as in the
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-vanities/iotti/p/iotti-nc6.html" target="_blank" title='55.1" Bathroom Vanity Iotti NC6 from Concept One Collection'>
          Concept One NC6
         </a>
         collection above. This can make it easy (and less expensive) to get a coordinated, over-all modern looking bathroom as well as the simple task lighting you need.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4337" style="width: 343px">
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-mirrors/eglo/p/eglo-86129a.html">
          <img alt="Backlit Mirror From Eglo" class=" wp-image-4337 " height="386" src="http://blog-images.homethangs.com//2012/09/Backlit-Mirror-From-Eglo.jpg" title="Backlit Mirror From Eglo" width="333"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Backlit Mirror From Eglo
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Of course, you dont necessarily have to buy a whole bathroom set to get your hands on a backlighted mirror. A skilled DIYer with the right materials can make one on their own (though thats a whole other post for another day!), contractors can build larger or wall-sized mirrors with custom cut shapes and patterns, and of course many companies just plain offer them on their own in a variety of sizes and patterns. From simple bars of LED lights to ones with pretty patterns like this
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/bathroom-mirrors/eglo/p/eglo-86129a.html" title="EGLO 86129, MIRROR, Bath and mirror, Indoor Lighting ">
          Backlit Mirror
         </a>
         from
         <a href="http://www.homethangs.com/eglo-b28007.html" target="_blank" title="Eglo">
          Eglo
         </a>
         , backlighted mirrors provide the functionality of a makeup mirror, the light of a standard over-the-mirror fixture, and a modern touch that will make your bathroom utterly unique.
        </p>
        <p>
         What do you like about backlit mirrors? Do you need a better way to illuminate your face in the morning, or are you just looking for a functional, cool looking update for your modern bathroom? Let me know in the comments!
        </p>
