        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Workshops in Transport Company
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-date">
          <a href="http://www.yonglitaiaxles.com/blog/2014/09/30/workshops-in-transport-company/" rel="bookmark">
           <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-09-30T08:09:08+00:00">
            September 30, 2014
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="byline">
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.yonglitaiaxles.com/blog/author/admin/" rel="author">
            admin
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <img alt="afd_skoda_30t_page_1_1" class="alignnone wp-image-75 size-large" height="344" src="http://www.yonglitaiaxles.com/blog/files/2014/09/afd_skoda_30t_page_1_1-1024x543.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Procurement and logistics are the main part of any business. And in trading business this thing have a major role. Trucks, trailers, lifters and many other heavy machinery is used in logistics to carry heavy loads and transport to other places.
         <span id="more-74">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="vehicle maintenance" class="aligncenter" height="366" src="https://xrscorp.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/vehicle-maintenance-csa-basics.jpg" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Companies which have big infrastructure of transportation mostly have their own work shop for the maintenance of trucks, trailers, lifters, loaders and others. They have their own mechanics and workers who repair their trucks, trailers and moving machinery.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="truck workshop" class="aligncenter" height="200" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-k29QZMnGrHo/UFp5_ENc3cI/AAAAAAAAAIc/oenLGq3dkVo/s1600/ServiceHeaderImage.jpg" width="504"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Some companies outsource the workshop and some of them have their own workshop and their labors are on their company payroll. Outsource companies mostly buy parts form the local market but in companies who have their own workshop, contact suppliers of parts and buy it in bulk. They contact companies who manufacture truck spare parts, semi trailers parts and heavy machinery parts and buy direct from the company on bulk quantity.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="trailer axles and parts" class="aligncenter" height="700" src="http://img.servehosting.net/images/uploadedimages/products/4/6/6091534-20130620060346.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         These companies mostly buy after a strong price and competitive analysis and they build long term relation with them. This help them to get discounts on many products and also profitable for truck and
         <a href="http://www.yonglitaiaxles.com/" title="Trailer Axles and Parts">
          trailer axles and parts
         </a>
         manufacturer.
        </p>
