       <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
        Gavin had a decent day but sadly hes lost his mind
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta clearfix">
        <span class="printonly">
         Categories:
        </span>
        <p class="meta_categories">
         <a href="http://www.lostandtired.com/category/gavin/" rel="category tag">
          Gavin
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.lostandtired.com/category/my-thoughts/" rel="category tag">
          My thoughts
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="post-date-inline updated">
         <span class="value-title" title="2014-04-26T23:56">
         </span>
         <abbr class="published" title="2014-04-26T23:56:00+00:00">
          April 26, 2014
         </abbr>
        </p>
        <p class="post-author author vcard">
         by
         <span class="fn nickname">
          <a class="url" href="http://www.lostandtired.com/author/lost-and-tired/">
           Lost and Tired
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="printonly">
         <em>
          April 26, 2014
         </em>
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- Go to www.addthis.com/dashboard to customize your tools -->
       <div class="addthis_jumbo_share">
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content clearfix">
        <script>
         &lt;!--
dynamicgoogletags.update();
//--&gt;
        </script>
        <div id="triberr_endorsement">
        </div>
        <div class="g g-1">
         <div class="g-single a-13">
          Sponsored Ad
          <center>
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

           </script>
          </center>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Today went pretty well. Gavin had a good day and we didnt have any serious problems. What did happen is that he spent a great deal of time with his
         <em>
          visibly challenged friends
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Before he went to bed tonight, he told Lizze and I all about it.
        </p>
        <div class="g g-9">
         <div class="g-single a-16">
          Sponsored Ad
          <center>
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
           </script>
           <!-- Post INject 5 -->
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9840156245990287" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="3537330855" style="display:block">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
           </script>
          </center>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         I wont even try to explain this one because it made absolutely no sense and to be honest, what I did understand was pretty fucked up. This stuff is getting darker but its like hes trying to spin it positive somehow.
        </p>
        <p>
         This is something he kept repeating:
        </p>
        <p>
         blockquote&gt;I will defend. I will protect. I will not use the enemies as slaves. I will not hurt them. I will not kill them. This is my code and I live by it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.lostandtired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/wpid-wp-13985608264831.png">
          <img alt="image" class="aligncenter size-full" src="http://www.lostandtired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/wpid-wp-1398560826483.png" title="wp-1398560826483"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         He also talked about bringing
         <em>
          Shutter Flys
         </em>
         long lost love back from the grave. Apparently that is someone called
         <em>
          King Solomon
         </em>
         . Gavin made him an army of robot servants so that he could basically abuse them.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats really all I got. Lizze actually took notes and recorded the whole conversation. Check out her
         <a href="http://www.mylifebeyondlabels.com ">
          blog
         </a>
         because she will probably write about this as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         Tuesday cant come fast enough.
        </p>
        <p>
         Even if Gavin wasnt targeting Lizze and doing all those messed up things, hes still out of his mind and we have to help him with that..  None of this is his fault.  While hes responsible for the choices hes made, its not his fault that his brain is hard wired to make them in the first place.
        </p>
        <p>
         Please keep him and the rest of us in your thoughts and prayers. Were gonna need it..
        </p>
