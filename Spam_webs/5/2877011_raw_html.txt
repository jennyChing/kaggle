      The Old House Parts Company now occupies an enormous 1872 freight   warehouse in Kennebunk, Maine. It is a revolving museum of architectural salvage   including paneling, moldings, fireplace mantels, antique doors and antique   windows, cabinets, wainscoting, antique plumbing &amp; bathroom fixtures and   architectural salvage lighting fixtures for your home improvement.
      <p>
       Tom Joyal, president of Old House Parts Company and our professional staff   will work to meet your old house restoration and interior design needs. We are   experienced in dismantling and moving old barns and houses as well as designing   and fitting large spaces with architectural salvage details and genuine old   house restoration hardware.
