           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="date">

         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.starlandia.it/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Carbonara.jpg">
            <img alt="Carbonara Light" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2253" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.starlandia.it/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Carbonara.jpg?resize=800%2C532"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Living in Rome, you are always exposed to a variety of pasta dishes that leave you craving for more. Each one encompassing its own vey unique taste created with love and tradition.
          </p>
          <p>
           One of the most traditional pastas is La Carbonara, with its simple ingredients: guanciale, egg, pecorino cheese, and black pepper. But the secret to a great Roman plate of Carbonara is in how you mix the ingredients together. You want to make sure that the eggs dont scramble when you pour it onto the pasta, but leave a creamy texture along with the pecorino cheese.
          </p>
          <p>
           Check out one of my favorite Italian cooking sites,
           <a href="http://giallozafferano.it" target="_blank" title="Giallo Zafferano">
            Giallo Zafferano
           </a>
           , with a vast selection of traditional Italian recipes. The one for
           <a href="http://ricette.giallozafferano.it/Spaghetti-alla-Carbonara.html" target="_blank" title="Carbonara">
            Carbonara
           </a>
           is simple but a traditional Roman recipe. However, the one I made at home is with whole wheat pasta since I wanted to feel less guilty
           <img alt=";)" class="wp-smiley" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.starlandia.it/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif?w=800"/>
           You can choose the type of pasta that you love the most for this plate.
          </p>

I didnt start using it because of the Anti-Aging benefits, I just happened to inherit it from my Mom because she didnt like it and I loved the texture and coverage. Since then, I have stuck by it and every now and then Ill change foundation but I always keep coming back to this one. I really recommend it if you can get your hands on it since it is an Italian brand and it might be difficult to find in other countries.

It has a nice texture, 50+ SPF, and also has Vitamin C. It is a bit pricey I think for the amount that you get, I paid 28 for it, but overall it is a nice BB Cream.

Im pretty sure this is gonna be one of those products that Im going to keep buying in the future.

Wasnt too convinced when I purchased it to be honest, it was one of those products that the sales clerk recommended and so I bought it. But to my surprise I actually love how it has an exfoliator brush to really help get off any make-up on your face and the cleansing gel is also really good for taking any dirt or make-up off your face.
