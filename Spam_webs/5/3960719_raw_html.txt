       <p>
        Crews are responding to a truck vs. train crash just north of New Prague.
       </p>
       <p>
        The crash was reported at 2:02 p.m. at Scott County Road 2 and Minnesota Highway 21. The Minnesota State Patrol says injuries are reported, and air care has been called.
       </p>
       <p>
        It's unclear what the train is carrying.
       </p>
       <p>
        Matt Egan of New Prague was driving home from work in Bloomington when he heard the crash. He said the vehicle was a box truck that was pulling a flatbed trailer carrying directional boring equipment.
       </p>
       <p>
        "I could see the truck had been struck between the cab and where the box of the truck starts," Egan said. "It literally separated the cab right off the frame."
       </p>
       <p>
        The State Patrol pulled the driver from the car. Egan said he saw the man loaded into a helicopter.
