    <h1>
     Pullman Rail Journeys Has A New Website
    </h1>
    <div class="post-3688 the-post-thumbnail">
     <img alt="Travel Pullman Chicago to New Orleans" class="attachment-waterfall_thumb wp-post-image" height="290" src="http://www.ideamktg.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pullman-screenshot-290x290.jpg" width="290"/>
    </div>
    <div class="post-3688 thumb-post">
     <div class="copy">
      <p>
       Idea Marketing Group has launched a new website for Pullman Rail Journeys, a luxury rail travel experience listed as one of National Geographic's Best Tours of North American 2015. With Chicago train packages that take guests on a nostaligic ride in vintage Pullman passenger cars updated with modern conveniences, guests are taken for a journeywhere the travel is as impressive as the destination. Experience New Orleans train travel in luxury from Chicago or take a Denver train ride that you won't forget.
      </p>
      <p>
       Idea Marketing Group is workingwith Premier Rail Collection to design and develop newwebsites for each of the Premier Rail Collection railroads. Ideasetup and developed a WordPress Multisite for Premier Rail Collection as a platform for all their railroad products. Thetop level website allows them to manage each website from one login. Idea designed a custom WordPress template to showcase a consistent brandas they are each launched. Pullman Rail Journeys launches just in time for the summer season.
      </p>
      <p>
       Read the full
       <a href="http://www.prweb.com/releases/travel-pullman-web-launch/05-01-2015/prweb12691032.htm">
        Pullman Rail Journeyswebsite launch press release
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Visit the
       <a href="http://www.travelpullman.com">
        new travel website
       </a>
       for Pullman Rail Journeys.
      </p>
