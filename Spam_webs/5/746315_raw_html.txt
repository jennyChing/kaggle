        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Busy times.
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-08-25T14:21:04+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          25th August 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.patchofpuddles.co.uk/archives/author/merry" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            merry
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.patchofpuddles.co.uk/archives/13196/busy-times#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         The summer holidays are nearly over and before you know it, theyll all be back at school again. It feels like it has been a packed holiday. Last week in particular was crammed with stuff. We went to visit my sister for the day because (aside from wanting to see them!) Fran had to go to a training day for a rugby event. She has rather impressively been
         <a href="http://www.englandtouch.org.uk/wp/index.php/2014/07/future-england-stars-show-their-skills/">
          picked to play for the North of England Under 18 side in a Touch Rugby
         </a>
         event, which involved a session training in Lincoln and another one next weekend in Manchester. Ive never even BEEN to Manchester. (I dont know how we will get there as the car is in limp mode due to an inexplicable fault waaah).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.patchofpuddles.co.uk/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/franrugby.jpg">
          <img alt="franrugby" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-13197" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.patchofpuddles.co.uk/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/franrugby-112x300.jpg?resize=112%2C300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Shes quite excited about this  as is her Dad  and Im pleased too as she and Maddy have been playing contact rugby more recently and I cant say Im a fan of that. Rather illogically I dont mind Fran hurtling through the air backwards and upside down but I dont really like people banging into her 
        </p>
        <p>
         We had a lovely day with my sister and co too
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/patchofpuddles.co.uk/wordpress/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         On Monday
         <a href="http://www.projectfran.me.uk/braces/">
          Fran had her braces fitted,
         </a>
         the second to last bit of the cleft intervention she should hopefully need. You might want to pop over to her blog and read about that, since shes writing again. Its been a long old road and there is at least two years of this but shes happy to have it started now and the first wave of it hasnt been too uncomfortable. Ive never seen such an odd shaped first wire though  it went in zigzags!
        </p>
        <p>
         Through all of this Maddy was happily off at CCF camp near Ironbridge; she took part in riots, rifle training, police training, went flying and did a lot of ironing, so far as I can tell. Shes extremely composed about being away from home  my favourite thing was how thrilled Fran and Bene were when we spoke to her on the phone. Was good to get her back and I took her for a quick visit to the baby rabbits we are adopting as a friend has found herself with rather a lot of them by accident!
        </p>
        <p>
         On Wednesday Amelie needed a small eye operation to remove some
         <a href="http://www.nhs.uk/conditions/molluscum-contagiosum/Pages/Introduction.aspx">
          molluscum growing in her eyelash line
         </a>
         . Theyve been causing enormous amounts of eye itching and refusing to go on their own so surgery had to happen. Was a tedious but very simple day and all went well. Im having to keep an eye (ha) on it now though as it is a bit crusty
         <img alt=":(" class="wp-smiley" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/patchofpuddles.co.uk/wordpress/wp-includes/images/smilies/frownie.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/patchofpuddles/14976314571" title="Looooooong wait for eye surgery. #meh by Merry, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Looooooong wait for eye surgery. #meh" height="320" src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5561/14976314571_d1abbf8724_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Josie and Fran did a few days either at work or at gym as Josie was enjoying some more gym camp. I have no idea how that child can do so much exercise and not completely collapse but she seems to manage.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thursday was
         <a href="http://www.patchofpuddles.co.uk/archives/13186/the-results-are-in" title="The results are in.">
          results day
         </a>
         as previously reported. Hurrah again
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/patchofpuddles.co.uk/wordpress/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I spent quite a lot of the week trying to stop Bene from being so freaked out by spiders and beetles.
        </p>
        <p>
         After initial anxiety
         <br/>
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/patchofpuddles/14786149958" title="To combat an extreme spider phobia we are watching Spider in the Bath on our Apple TV thingy. Riveted #mummywin by Merry, on Flickr">
          <img alt="To combat an extreme spider phobia we are watching Spider in the Bath on our Apple TV thingy. Riveted #mummywin" height="320" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3921/14786149958_ff975e8314_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         he conceded that Spider in the Bath is in fact a lot of fun.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/patchofpuddles/14786356667" title="It was the best kids show ever I think. by Merry, on Flickr">
          <img alt="It was the best kids show ever I think." height="320" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3869/14786356667_e701986442_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Best show ever
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/patchofpuddles.co.uk/wordpress/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hes also really enjoying going to P
         <a href="http://www.phoenixgymnasticsacademy.co.uk/classes/">
          layGym at Phoenix
         </a>
         , which is good as we need to tire him out a bit more. Hes giving up on naps, which is slightly painful all round!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/patchofpuddles/14786377050" title="Boy on beam. #playgym #phoenixgymnastics #peterborough @phoenixgymclub by Merry, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Boy on beam. #playgym #phoenixgymnastics #peterborough @phoenixgymclub" height="320" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3852/14786377050_0f746ddb7b_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Along side that I managed to do a phenomenal amount of work on two websites, finish 3 books (the ends of each not all the way through each!) and finish the boarder on my sisters blanket. So it was a productive week, all in all.
        </p>
