             <h1 class="main-title single-title entry-title">
              Motorola G 2nd Generation  Stylish, Slim and Affordable
             </h1>
            </div>
            <div class="image-container">
             <div class="featured-image-wrapper">
              <div class="featured-image-inner">
               <a class="darken featured-image" href="http://www.handsonphones.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image2-e1427148256999.jpg" title="Click for full view">
                <img alt="Motorola G 4G" class="attachment-single wp-post-image" height="320" itemprop="image" src="http://www.handsonphones.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image2-e1427148256999.jpg" title="Motorola G 2nd Generation  Stylish, Slim and Affordable" width="403"/>
               </a>
              </div>

             <div class="authorship type-both">
              <span class="author">

             <div addthis:description="Motorola was once known for its mobile phones. It is a fact that Motorola was one of them who introduce the concept of slim mobile phones. Its mobile was known for its style and design. " addthis:title="Motorola G 2nd Generation  Stylish, Slim and Affordable" addthis:url="http://www.handsonphones.com/motorola-g-2nd-generation/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style addthis_32x32_style">
              <a class="addthis_button_twitter">

            <div class="the-content padded-panel">
             <div id="content-anchor">
             </div>
             <div class="clearfix" id="content-anchor-inner">
              <p style="text-align: left;">
               Motorola was once known for its mobile phones. It is a fact that Motorola was one of them who introduce the concept of slim mobile phones. Its mobile was known for its style and design. But sadly due to high competition in technology and strong, stable competitors like Apple and Samsung, Motorola failed to keep steady its rank in the market. For several years when other mobile companies were upgrading their smartphones, Motorola almost disappeared from the mobile market.
              </p>
              <p>
               But now, Motorola is back with its Motorola G
               <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.boostmobile.com/coverage/network-updates/4g-lte-network/" rel="boostphonemodels" title="4G LTE Network">
                4G phone
               </a>
               that is one of the lowest price smartphones with a high quality of technical specs. After a significant gap of time, Motorola came with something new, big and best. It is known by another name too, Motorola Moto G LTE.
              </p>
              <p>
               You would be curious that why I am calling Motorola 4G a real phone while there are iPhone, Samsung, and Nokia. But can these companies offer a high spec phone just in $180? No, just check out the specification of Motorola 4G below to see how Motorola does come into the market with the concept of high-quality phones in low price.
              </p>
              <h2>
               <strong>
                Motorola G Key Features:
               </strong>
              </h2>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Display:
                </strong>
                720128 pixels resolution, 4.5 inches of
                <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.wikinvest.com/concept/LCD" rel="wikinvest" title="LCD">
                 LCD screen
                </a>
                protected with
                <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.corninggorillaglass.com" rel="homepage" title="Gorilla Glass">
                 Gorilla Glass 3
                </a>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Body:
                </strong>
                Slim, 143 g weight and 129.9 x 65.9 x 11.6 mm dimensions
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Camera:
                </strong>
                5MP back and 1.5 MP front, LED flash, 720p video can be recorded
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Connectivity:
                </strong>
                As the name shows, it is 4G and LTE, GSM and HSPA
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Platform:
                </strong>
                <a class="zem_slink" href="http://code.google.com/android/" rel="homepage" title="Android">
                 Android OS
                </a>
                Kit Kat who can upgrade to Lollipop
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Storage:
                </strong>
                1 GB RAM, 8 GB built-in memory, microSD slot available
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Battery:
                </strong>
                Talk time 15 hours, browsing 10 hours and video play for 8 hours
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Colors:
                </strong>
                Available in white and black. Motorola also launched an edition of Moto G Ferrari
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Misc:
                </strong>
                FM radio, GPS, Wifi, Bluetooth, Multi-touch, Micro Sim and USB support.
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               Does not its makes you WOW that how can a swim in so much for a small price phone that comes with so many features? Lets take a deep look about the Motorola G 4G below.
              </p>
              <h2>
               <strong>
                Design  Stylish, Slim, and Tall
               </strong>
              </h2>
              <p>
               The reason Motorola 4G is popular and how does its a business changer for Motorola is the design of the phone. The design of the phone is the reason it survive in the market and compete with its competitor smartphones. Motorola G is a plastic phone with 4.5 inches of LCD screen, 16M colors, and must touch. The LCD screen is protected with Gorilla Glass 3, not just that but also the back panel of the phone is protected with scratch resistant glass. It has curved back which make it comfortable to your hand. The weight of Moto G 4G is just 143 g that is same as
               <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.virginmobileusa.com/shop/cell-phones/iphones/" rel="boostvirginphone" title="iPhone">
                iPhone 4S
               </a>
               . The design and display of Moto G 4G are overall clean, simple and efficient which makes it a stylish phone in your hand. It is unlikely to find out a smartphone like this in under $150.
              </p>
              <h2>
               <strong>
                Camera  Not much to impress
               </strong>
              </h2>
              <p>
               Overall the Motorola 4G is enough to influence the market, but its average quality camera could be a reason for many to say to it. But still it has much to impress. Check out the features below:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Back camera is 5MP with LED flash with a maximum resolution of 25921944 pixels
               </li>
               <li>
                Autofocus camera comes with several modes including HDR which enhance the quality of an image
               </li>
               <li>
                3MP front camera that is pretty good for video chat especially if you have enough light around you
               </li>
               <li>
                Moto G is not full HD but can record video at 720p resolution
               </li>
               <li>
                Facility of taking photos during video record also available
               </li>
               <li>
                You can also take panoramic pictures through Motorola 4G
               </li>
               <li>
                The image quality of the phone is average, not exquisite as you were expecting after reading amazing specs of Moto G.
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               Still confused whether to purchase Motorola Moto G 4G? Lets take a real quick comparison between good and bad points below which will help you to judge.
              </p>
              <h2>
               <strong>
                Good Points:
               </strong>
              </h2>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Android Kit Kat can be upgraded to Androids Lollipop
               </li>
               <li>
                4G LTE connectivity
               </li>
               <li>
                Stylish phone with 4.5inch of screen
               </li>
               <li>
                Low in price to its tech specs
               </li>
               <li>
                <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.sdcard.org" rel="homepage" title="Secure Digital">
                 microSD card
                </a>
                slot available
               </li>
               <li>
                Excellent battery life
               </li>
               <li>
                FM radio also available
               </li>
               <li>
                High-performance phone.
               </li>
              </ul>
              <h2>
               <strong>
                Bad Points:
               </strong>
              </h2>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Non-replaceable battery
               </li>
               <li>
                Average quality of the camera
               </li>
               <li>
                No full HD video recording, only 720p video capture
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               The price of Motorola G 4G could be varying country to country but on average it is between $150 to $190. The Ferrari edition is quite expensive due to its style and overall look, but its same like original Moto G 4G.
              </p>
              <p>
               The average quality camera is the only reason that could stop you to buy this phone. But if you are not photo capturing addictive and looking for a cheap in price but high in performance phone the Motorola G 4G is the best choice. You will hardly found a phone with LTE and 4 G in such a small price.
              </p>
