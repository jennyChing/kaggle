        <h2 class="title">
         video weekend
        </h2>
       </div>
       <p>
        <em>
         <strong>
          <span style="font-size: small;">
           As the days grow shorter, sailing videos grow more important. Here are your weekend selections from SAs staff:
          </span>
         </strong>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          The Wire
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/YQhvhPX11JY" width="560">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        An entire season of racing all over the world, and its all down to the final day of the final event: Can Morgan Larson win Brazil outright, putting a boat between Alinghi and The Wave Muscat to take the 2013 Extreme season?
       </p>
       <p>
        As much as wed love to see US skipper Larson take it, we have to admit that, with MacMillan at the helm, Muscat has been smart, fast, and consistent for the better part of two years now, and they should fairly easily claim the first back-to-back Extreme Sailing Series title in the events history after a
        <a href="http://www.extremesailingseries.com/results">
         solid, conservative, and winning star
        </a>
        t to the final event.
       </p>
       <p>
        But desperation makes for good TV, so you better watch as Larson will do everything he can to screw the Waves day up. Watch it here starting at 1300 EST/1000 PST.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Everybody, Again
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="413" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/Avz8MGehmbg?rel=0" width="551">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        One of our
        <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FWINygISxDE">
         favorite sailing videos ever is nearing a million Youtube views,
        </a>
        but Everybody  the high-speed action shoot with various ORMA 60, Open 60, kiteboards and windsurfers  is getting decidedly long in the tooth for the late night party/yacht club TV screens it is most suited for.  So weve got a new one for you; its video producers GZ Companys 2013 highlight reel, and its got everything from 12 meters to sport boats to maxi catamarans to Volvo boats. Enjoy.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Bert and I
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="309" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/EPGf77t9hRA?rel=0" width="549">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        The Bert and I
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bert_&amp;_I">
         collections of humorous maritime stories
        </a>
        may be from Downeast Maine rather than the Chesapeake, but they capture some of what Annapolis sailor Bert Jabin was all about. We remember a
        <a href="http://www.capitalgazette.com/news/annapolis/legendary-annapolis-yacht-yard-owner-and-sailor-bert-jabin-dies/article_ae8e5c0b-1488-515f-af86-7dfbeb3cb08e.html">
         leader, a legend, a mentor, a boatbuilder, a racer, and the founder of Junk Jungle Lane
        </a>
        , and our most sincere condolences to the entire Annapolis community on Berts death.
        <a href="http://forums.sailinganarchy.com/index.php?showtopic=152819">
         Share your own thoughts
        </a>
        in the thread.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Kiting With Crocs
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="309" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/78773118?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=f0003c" width="550">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        For all the talk about snakes, sharks, and jellyfish, Far North Queensland aborigines will tell you theres just one animal to be truly afraid of: The Saltwater Croc. Check out this Naish-produced video to see how Ewan Jaspan, Will Smallacomb, and Ant from Kitesafari Australiajust dont give a shit.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Hot Tomales
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="309" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/wxvxfhqRARM?rel=0" width="549">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Who says you need to wait for a big downwind day to practice wave sailing? And who says you need a surfboard to go surfing? Screw it! Grab your Zuma and do what SAer wrybread does at the mouth of Northern Californias Tomales Bay. Wanna know more?
        <a href="http://forums.sailinganarchy.com/index.php?showtopic=152766&amp;hl=">
         Ask him here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
