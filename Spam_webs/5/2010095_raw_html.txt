          <article class="content" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
           <h2>
            <span itemprop="name">
             The 11 frustrating stages of building your own website (and one way to solve your Web Presence woes)
            </span>
           </h2>
           <meta content="2013-11-6" itemprop="datePublished"/>
           <footer>
            <span class="date">
             <i class="icon-calendar">
             </i>
             November 6th, 2013
            </span>
           </footer>
           <figure class="clearfix">
            <img alt="The 11 frustrating stages of building your own website (and one way to solve your Web Presence woes)" class="img-responsive wp-post-image alignnone" src="//d335hnnegk3szv.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/cache/remote/pronto-bypronto-com/2778605521.jpg"/>
           </figure>
           <div itemprop="articleBody">
            <p class="lead">
             1. Get pumped up cause you're gonna build a website!
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Dancing-80s-Guys.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             2. But first, you need to figure out which Content Management System to use.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/I-dont-know.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             3. Ok, you've picked out your CMS. Now it's time to install your theme and plugins and figure out how to upload them through FTP.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Speechless.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             4. After spending hours failing to upload anything through your FTP client, you give up and decide you're better off just outsourcing the whole project to a developer.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Take-my-money.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             5. Then you just sit around for a while waiting for your developer to put something together.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Waiting.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             6. Several weeks later, you finally have your website designed. Yay!...oh, but wait, you still need content.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Mood-swing.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             7. So, you have two options: 1) bust out some content yourself.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Typing.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             8. Or, 2) drop some cash on a copywriter.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Throw-Money.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             9. Now, you've heard of this thing called SEO but you're not really sure what to do about it. Time to hire someone again. You get excited because he promises you #1 rankings for all your keywords! But it turns out to be a scam and you end up going nowhere.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Treadmill.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             10. Down the road a bit, you're ready to make some modifications to your site, so you go back to your developer. But they run away yelling, "On-going maintenance wasn't in our contraaaaaact!"
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Run-away.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             11. The whole experience leaves you feeling sort of like...
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/bummed.gif"/>
            </p>
            <h2>
             <strong>
              But don't worry, we're here to help
             </strong>
            </h2>
            <p class="lead">
             At Pronto, we work closely with you to make sure your site perfectly represents your business. Design, development and content are all handled by our team, so by the time we send you your completed site, you're like...
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Confetti.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             After a couple revisions to get things perfect, it's time to release your new site to the world. Your clients, friends and potential customers are blown away.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Kobe.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             Soon, you're competitors are wondering how you do it. How is your website always up to date? Where do you find the time to manage your entire Internet Presence so well? You just brush them off like it ain't no thing.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Beyonce.gif"/>
            </p>
            <p class="lead">
             A few months down the road, your SEO is solid, all your updates are taken care of and your email newsletter goes out on time each month. So sit back and relax cause we've got it covered.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.prontomarketing.com/files/2013/06/Oprah.gif"/>
            </p>
            <h2 style="text-align: center">
             <a href="http://www.prontomarketing.com/pricing/managed-website/">
              Find out how Pronto makes managing your website easy!
             </a>
            </h2>
