        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Juicy Bacon-and-Egg Cheese Breakfast Burger
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         Posted on
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-06-17T04:00:08+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 17, 2014
         </time>
         Written by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://boulderlocavore.com/author/boul7429/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Toni Dash
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/bacon-and-egg-cheese-breakfast-burger/#comments">
           20 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-BoulderLocavore-.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger - BoulderLocavore" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12461" height="894" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-BoulderLocavore-.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There is nothing like a good, juicy burger when one is in the mood and summer is certainly the season for them. Ive developed some
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/2013/06/southwestern-bacon-cheeseburgers.html">
          <strong>
           tried-and-true tips
          </strong>
         </a>
         that ensure my grilled burgers are always juicy, well-cooked but not overdone every time, making it fun to change things up with confidence about the outcome.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im really excited about these burgers today for a few reasons. The inspiration came from a delicious challenge to create a new burger as part of a
         <strong>
          Burger Week
         </strong>
         effort hosted by
         <a href="http://noshonit.com">
          NoshOn.It
         </a>
         . All week long fabulous food bloggers are creating burgers that Im convinced will fill your summer with mouth-watering options no matter what you preferences are. The parade of meat and vegetarian options are sure to dazzle and youll find links to my colleagues posting today below as a tantalizing sample of what the week will hold! To follow the week and find the other recipes, simple click on the Burger Week badge below.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-BoulderLocavore-714.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger - BoulderLocavore " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12463" height="894" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-BoulderLocavore-714.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now for these burgers! Im not sure exactly what sparked the idea of combining the best of breakfast with a burger but my muse delivered. A cheesy grilled burger, topped by a fried egg, bacon, arugula and
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/2014/06/baked-smoked-paprika-potato-chips-with-sriracha-sour-cream-dip.html">
          <strong>
           Sriracha-Sour Cream
          </strong>
         </a>
         ; do I have your attention? Perhaps the best part is when they are squeezed to eat the yolk breaks, runs down the sides of the burger just enough to add to the juiciness. I really cant image a better combination of flavors with the added crunch of arugula which wilts a bit when added on top of the warm burger. It struck me when assembling this probably qualified as hangover food as well; something to take the edge off the morning after a bit too much fun.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-patty-BoulderLocavore-701.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger - patty - BoulderLocavore " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12468" height="916" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-patty-BoulderLocavore-701.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-sriracha-sour-cream-BoulderLocavore-706.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger -sriracha-sour cream - BoulderLocavore " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12467" height="808" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-sriracha-sour-cream-BoulderLocavore-706.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-bacon-BoulderLocavore-708.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger - bacon- BoulderLocavore " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12466" height="894" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-bacon-BoulderLocavore-708.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-fried-egg-BoulderLocavore-712.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger - fried egg - BoulderLocavore " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12465" height="894" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-fried-egg-BoulderLocavore-712.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-arugula-BoulderLocavore-712.jpg">
          <img alt="Juicy Bacon-and-Egg cheese Breakfast Burger - arugula - BoulderLocavore " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12464" height="894" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Juicy-Bacon-and-Egg-cheese-Breakfast-Burger-arugula-BoulderLocavore-712.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-12438">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-283">
         <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
           <div class="item b-b">
            <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
             <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-283', 'http://boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/plugins/ziplist-recipe-plugin/'); return false" title="Print this recipe">
              Print
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="zl-recipe-link fl-r zl-rmvd" id="zl-recipe-link-283">
            </div>
            <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
             Juicy Bacon-and-Egg Cheese Breakfast Burger
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
             <p id="zlrecipe-prep-time">
              Prep Time:
              <span content="PT10M" itemprop="prepTime">
               10 minutes
              </span>
             </p>
             <p id="zlrecipe-cook-time">
              Cook Time:
              <span content="PT15M" itemprop="cookTime">
               15 minutes
              </span>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
             <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
              Yield:
              <span itemprop="recipeYield">
               1 juicy burger
              </span>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="zlclear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="img-desc-wrap">
            <div id="zlrecipe-summary" itemprop="description">
             <p class="summary italic">
              This juicy, mouthwatering burger combines the best of breakfast and a burger to become and unforgettable meal.  The egg yolk breaks when squeezing the burger to eat it allowing it to run into the other ingredients without becoming a mess.  The arugula wilts slightly though still provides a nice crunch.
             </p>
             <p class="summary italic">
              The recipe can be easily multiplied for the number of burgers desired maintaining the same ingredient quatities.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0">
             Ingredient amount per burger:
            </div>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 pound 85% Ground Beef, chilled
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup Cheddar Cheese, finely grated
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             Olive oil (to baste burgers)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             Salt and Pepper
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1-2 slices Bacon, cooked until done but still pliable; drained
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1-2 teaspoons Unsalted Butter
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 Egg
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup Arugula
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 batch
             <a class="ingredient-link" href="http://boulderlocavore.com/2014/06/baked-smoked-paprika-potato-chips-with-sriracha-sour-cream-dip.html" target="_blank">
              Sriracha-Sour Cream
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 Hamburger Bun (gluten-free or with gluten)
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Heat grill to 350 degrees.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a large mixing bowl combine ground beef, cheese, a pinch of each salt and pepper.  Work with hands to fully incorporate all ingredients.  Form patty (or patties if making multiple burgers) and refrigerate until grill is fully heated.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             When grill is heated, baste one side of burger(s) with olive oil and place face down on grill.  Close lid.  Allow to cook 3 minutes to develop a sear.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Baste the top of the burger(s) and flip over.  Do NOT press down on burgers.  Close grill lid and allow to cook an additional 6-8 minutes until juices are running clear (can cut into the top of the burger to ensure cooked to preferred doneness as well).  Remove from grill and allow to rest while cooking the fried egg.
            </li>
            <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4">
             Instructions to make Fried Egg:
            </div>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Heat a large, heavy, cast iron or non-stick skillet heat over medium-high heat and add butter to melt.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             When butter is foaming slightly swirl the pan to spread butter. Crack the egg carefully so as to not break the yolk and gently place in the heated skillet.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Allow egg to cook until the white is opaque and firm.
             <span class="bold">
              For Over Easy Egg
             </span>
             : Slide the spatula underneath the egg and gently flip to the other side. Allow to cook 30 seconds to one minute or until browned to your liking.
             <span class="bold">
              For Sunny Side Up Egg
             </span>
             : Cover pan and allow yolk to reach your desired consistency. Egg whites can be basted with the pan drippings for additional flavor and color before covering as well. Remove from pan and layer on the burger.
            </li>
            <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8">
             Assembling the Bacon-and-Egg Cheese Breakfast Burger:
            </div>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Place the hamburger on the bottom of the bun.  Add a dollop of Sriracha-Sour Cream, followed by bacon strips, fried egg, arugula and top bun.
            </li>
           </ol>
