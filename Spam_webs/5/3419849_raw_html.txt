       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Learn to make crafty holiday gifts out of bike tubes at Alchemy Goods
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.seattlebikeblog.com/2011/12/06/learn-to-make-crafty-holiday-gifts-out-of-bike-tubes-at-alchemy-goods/" rel="bookmark" title="1:36 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          December 6, 2011
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.seattlebikeblog.com/author/tfooq/" title="View all posts by Tom Fucoloro">
          Tom Fucoloro
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_6693" style="width: 355px">
         <a href="http://seattlebikeblog.com/2011/12/06/learn-to-make-crafty-holiday-gifts-out-of-bike-tubes-at-alchemy-goods/recraft-gifts/" rel="attachment wp-att-6693">
          <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-6693 " height="307" src="http://1p40p3gwj70rhpc423s8rzjaz.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/recraft-gifts-575x512.jpg" title="recraft-gifts" width="345"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Image from Hub &amp; Bespoke
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Alchemy Goods and Hub &amp; Bespoke (a SBB sponsor) are hosting an awesome-sounding party tonight (December 6) from 6-9 p.m. Learn how you can turn your used inner tubes into crafty holiday gifts with the help of a pioneering bike tube fashion company.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hubandbespoke.wordpress.com/2011/11/23/holiday-re-craft-party/">
          From Hub &amp; Bespoke
         </a>
         :
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Have you ever received a gift made by the person who gave it to you? If so, it probably made you feel pretty special knowing someone took time to craft something just for you. Well, heres a chance to give that same great feeling to people on your holiday gift list.
          <span id="more-6692">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.hubandbespoke.com/" target="_blank" title="Hub and Bespoke homepage">
           Hub and Bespoke
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://alchemygoods.com/" target="_blank" title="Alchemy Goods homepage">
           Alchemy Goods
          </a>
          invite you to join us for a re-craft session, where you can create jewelry, ornaments, picture frames, smart phone stands, and anything else you can dream up, out of wonderfully reusable bicycle innertubes.
         </p>
         <p>
          Not sure what to make? Thats okay; well have samples of previously re-crafted gift items on hand to inspire you, as well as a room full of fellow holiday elves with whom you can share ideas. Alchemy Goods will supply the tubes and the workshop, while Hub and Bespoke will provide refreshments (to get those creative juices flowing). All you have to bring are your imagination, a friend or three, and any tools or supplies you might want for ornamenting and the like (think jewelry beads, rings, rubber stamps, etc.).
         </p>
         <p>
          Details: Tuesday, December 6th, 6-9 p.m. at
          <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1723+1st+Ave+South&amp;hl=en&amp;sll=47.587462,-122.334426&amp;sspn=0.007598,0.01929&amp;vpsrc=0&amp;hnear=1723+1st+Ave+S,+Seattle,+Washington+98134&amp;t=m&amp;z=16" target="_blank" title="Alchemy Goods on Google maps">
           1723 1st Ave South
          </a>
          . No admission fee. Happy holidays!
         </p>
        </blockquote>
