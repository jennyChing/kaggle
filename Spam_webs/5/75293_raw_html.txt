         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           Mommy Monday- Taking an Adult TimeOut
          </span>
         </h1>
         <div class="entry">
          <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
           <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fmotherhoodandmerlot.com%2Fmommy-monday-timeout%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fmotherhoodandmerlot.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F08%2Fimage4-e1407098917877.jpg&amp;description=Mommy%20Monday-%20Taking%20an%20Adult%20TimeOut" rel="nobox">
            <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           Ever feel like you are so wrapped up in everything else that is happening in life that you miss out on the wonderful, little moments?
          </p>
          <p>
           Lately, this has been the story of my life. With so much going on in my husbands career as well as my own schedule and
           <a href="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/mommy-monday-balancing-motherhood-everything-else/" target="_blank" title="Mommy Monday- Balancing motherhood, and everything else!">
            of course trying to take everything upon mysel
           </a>
           f, it has felt a little bit like we are rushing everything with our girls lately.
          </p>
          <p>
           We as parents obviously dont mean to let the business of life get in the way of finding pleasure in slowing things down and taking in these rare instances, but it happens to even the most organized of us, speaking from experience.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Enjoying The Little Things
          </h2>
          <p>
           My littlest one just starting taking steps a week ago. Firsts like this always just fill our hearts with pride and joy as parents, dont they? I cant believe all of those months as a baby are now said and done and she is onto the world of mobility and adventure.
           <a href="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/image4-e1407098917877.jpg">
            <img alt="image" class="size-medium wp-image-659 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/image4-e1407098917877-183x300.jpg" width="183"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           It made me realize that we really need to take the time to watch our children grow, learn, and laugh. They are such amazing beings, no matter what age or stage of life they are in. (although some are arguably more trying for us as parents Im sure!)
          </p>
          <p>
           Doing something as simple as taking your kids on an outing for a few hours can make a world of difference in your own mood as well as theirs. They can sense when we are feeling stressed and busy.
          </p>
          <p>
           Depending on the age of the kids of course, doing something as simple as going out to eat or going to the park can be a much needed and extremely fun few hours.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/image2-e1407098986476.jpg">
            <img alt="image" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-657" height="300" src="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/image2-e1407098986476-227x300.jpg" width="227"/>
           </a>
           <a href="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/image1-e1407099032889.jpg">
            <img alt="image" class="size-medium wp-image-656 alignright" height="300" src="http://motherhoodandmerlot.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/image1-e1407099032889-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Taking my youngest down the slide for the first time and watching my oldest run through the sprinklers as she squealed with delight
           <span style="color: #993366;">
            <em>
             <strong>
              these are moments that would never want to miss, and things that I will always remember.
             </strong>
            </em>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           So if you can, stop what youre doing and take a break from the business of life. It is hard, we all have things to get done, but it is so worth it to cherish those lasting moments. They go by so quickly!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             *Heres to enjoying every special moment*
            </span>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           -Joanna
          </p>
