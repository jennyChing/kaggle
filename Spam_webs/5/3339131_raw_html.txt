     <div class="articleText" id="at_0" itemprop="articleBody">
      <p class="paragraph" data-pnum="1">
       One person is dead and another person suffered critical injuries after an apparent rockslide at a national park in Marin County Saturday evening, according to a park employee.
      </p>
      <p class="paragraph" data-pnum="2">
       Emergency medical crews with the Marin County Fire Department responded at 5:55 p.m. to a report of a rescue in the area of Arch Rock at Point Reyes National Seashore, Marin County fire Battalion Chief Mike Giannini said.
      </p>
