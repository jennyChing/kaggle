          <p>
           Anchorage AK, 21-MAY-2015  Arctic Chiropractic and Dr. Lee Waldroup DC, are pleased to announce that the chiropractor has the solutions to allow for spinal pain relief. Pain in the spine can cause pain and other symptoms throughout the rest of the body. The
           <a href="http://www.anchoragechiropractors.com">
            Anchorage Chiropractor
           </a>
           uses his knowledge of the spine and the musculoskeletal systems of the body to apply non-invasive and natural pain relief measures. Whether the pain is caused by an injury or by other conditions, chiropractic methods are able to offer solutions.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ensuring that the alignment of the vertebral column is correct is the first step for recovery and pain relief. Removing subluxations relieves pressure on the intervertebral discs and allows them to become re-hydrated. When the discs are restored to their correct position, they no longer bulge into the surrounding tissues.
          </p>
          <p>
           Soft tissue damage is another reason why there could be pain in the back near the spinal column. Muscles can be over-strained by improper lifting techniques or by injuries from falls. Poor posture can also cause pain in the muscles along the vertebral column. Regardless of the cause of spine pain, the doctor will prepare a care plan to eliminate the underlying cause for pain.
          </p>
          <p>
           Pain relief for soft tissue pain is focused on rebuilding the damaged tissue at the cellular level. Often, the body will repair itself if given the tools to do so. Nutrition, posture and exercise are all helpful in improving the circulation of blood to the injured areas. The chiropractic methods provide an improved quality of life, free of pain.
          </p>
          <p>
           Learn more about spine pain relief by visiting the web pages at
