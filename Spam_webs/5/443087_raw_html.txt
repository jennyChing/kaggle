      Hi this is Clementine. Sometimes you spot a beauty...anything, which makes you stop, which makes you forget anything else around you. This little discovery, be it a picture or a scene...or anything, just grabs you, looks into you. When this happens to me, I look around to see who else is going to see this, who else is going to feel the same way. That's how the reflection of what I just discover, reaches you. This is, reflectionof.me...of you and of everything that surrounds us. I hope you enjoy sharing what's reflected on you, like me.
