        <p>
         Super yummy glazed carrots!
        </p>
        <p>
         As you saw last week,
         <a href="http://reagantherecipehoarder.com/2015/01/09/carrot-green-pine-nut-pesto/" target="_blank" title="carrot green &amp; pine nut pesto">
          you can make pesto with carrot greens
         </a>
         . Well, you have to store your carrots by cutting off the greens first, that way they will keep longer.
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres what I did with the yummy (&amp; vibrant) carrots from
         <a href="http://facebook.com/3sisters" target="_blank" title="Three Sisters Farm Facebook">
          Three Sisters Farm
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://reagantherecipehoarder.files.wordpress.com/2015/01/photo-jan-08-7-29-01-pm.jpg">
          <img alt="super yummy glazed carrots | reagantherecipehoarder.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-903" height="660" src="https://reagantherecipehoarder.files.wordpress.com/2015/01/photo-jan-08-7-29-01-pm.jpg?w=660" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-902">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-8" style="border: 1px dotted;">
         <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
           <div class="item b-b">
            <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
             <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-8', 'http://www.reagantherecipehoarder.com/wp-content/plugins/zip-recipes/'); return false" rel="nofollow" title="Print this recipe">
              Print
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
             super yummy glazed carrots
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="zlclear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 lb carrots, peeled
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 c water, boiling
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tbsp butter
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             2/3 c brown sugar
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             After you trim the majority of the greens off the carrots, peel them, discarding the peels (or you can save them for veggie stock or compost!)
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In the meantime, boil the water in a skillet with lid.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Once the water is boiling, place the carrots in &amp; cover with the lid.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Boil for 10 minutes, until crisp-tender.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Drain.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Then melt the butter, &amp; return the carrots to the skillet.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add the brown sugar. Cover &amp; simmer on medium heat for 5-7 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cover the carrots with the glaze &amp; serve.
            </li>
           </ol>
