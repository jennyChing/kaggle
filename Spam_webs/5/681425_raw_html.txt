       <h2 class="posttitle">
        Rose Sangria Recipe
       </h2>
       <div class="postmeta">
        <em>
         posted by
        </em>
        Sommer 
        <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/rose-sangria-recipe/#comments">
         17 comments 
        </a>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="17464635" data-link="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/rose-sangria-recipe/" data-summary="" data-title="Rose Sangria Recipe">
      </div>
      <p>
       <img alt="Simple Rose Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="hiddenpinimage" height="1437" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Rose-Sangria-Recipe-on-ASpicyPerspective.com-cocktails-sangria.jpg" width="500"/>
       A blushing
       <strong>
        Rose Sangria Recipe
       </strong>
       just in time for Mothers Day! Light, lively, and PINK. Just what mom needs on her special day.
       <span id="more-36301">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Blushing Rose Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36303" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-13.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Sangria happens to be high on my list of favorites things to sip. This isnt the first sangria recipe Ive shared with you, and Im sure it wont be the last.
      </p>
      <p>
       However, I do feel its a special sangria recipe and worth trying at your earliest convenience.Unlike classic sangria made with dark Spanish wine and orange juice, this Rose (Ros) Sangria Recipe is lighter, with a tangy-sweet finish.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Vibrant Ros Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36312" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-11.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       And just look at the color!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Making Blushing Rose Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36307" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-2.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       So whats in it? So glad you asked!
      </p>
      <p>
       I started with a bottle of French Ros Wine; a crisp wine with a light citrus-floral quality. Then I added pink grapefruit juice for intenseacidity, and bourbon for sweetness and depth
      </p>
      <p>
       P.S. Bourbon and grapefruit juice are
       <em>
        meant to be.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Easy Rose Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36315" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-3.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Chambord is added to introduce a bright berry flavor, then I stirred in honey to balance out the twang.
      </p>
      <p>
       The rose sangria recipe base is a little bolder than traditional sangria, however you will not believe the depth and intrigue it provides.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Mother's Day Rose Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36316" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-6.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       As if that werent enough
      </p>
      <p>
       Fruity pink things are added to the mix to absorb all that boozy goodness.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Ros Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36309" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-7.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Amazing Ros Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36317" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-8.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Fresh watermelon, strawberries, and raspberries soak in the Ros Sangria Recipe until the base is sweet, and the fruit is
       <em>
        fully loaded
       </em>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Then stir and sip. Once you reach the bottom of the glass, a little gift is waiting for you Irresistible boozy watermelon and berries to nibble one at a time.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Rose (Ros) Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36306" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-9.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Zippy Ros Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36314" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-12.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Friends, this Rose Sangria Recipe is a winner. Its even good enough for mom.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
        This Mothers Day set your momma in a soft chair on a screened-in porch, and pour her a tall one. She deserves it!
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Rose Sangria Recipe on ASpicyPerspective.com #sangria #cocktails #mothersday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36325" height="873" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-14.jpg" width="600"/>
      </p>

      <div class="recipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
       <meta content="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/rose-sangria-recipe" itemprop="url"/>
       <img align="right" class="photo nopin" itemprop="image" nopin="nopin" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rose-sangria-recipe-14-210x210.jpg"/>
       <span itemprop="name">
        <h2 class="fn">
         Rose Sangria Recipe
        </h2>
       </span>
       <div class="time">
        <p>
         <strong>
          Yield:
         </strong>
         <span itemprop="recipeYield">
          1 large pitcher
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Prep Time:
         </strong>
         <meta content="PT15M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
         15 minutes
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="printbutton">
        <a class="print" href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/rose-sangria-recipe/print/" title="Print Recipe">
         Print Recipe
        </a>
       </div>
       <h3 id="ingredients">
        Ingredients:
       </h3>
       <div class="ingredients">
        <ul>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          750 ml bottle French Ros Wine
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 cup pink grapefruit juice
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          3/4 cup bourbon
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1/2 cup honey
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1/4 cup chambord (raspberry liqueur)
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          2 cups watermelon balls
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 1/2 cups fresh sliced strawberries
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          6 ounces fresh raspberries
         </li>
        </ul>
       </div>
       <h3 id="directions">
        Directions:
       </h3>
       <div class="instructions">
        <span itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <ol>
          <li>
           Use a
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/search/ref=as_li_qf_sp_sr_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;index=aps&amp;keywords=melon%20baller&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=aspiper-20&amp;linkId=RJJIS6S24HD7APOR" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            melon baller
           </a>
           to scoop 2 cups of watermelon balls out of a large slice of fresh watermelon. Slice the strawberries.
          </li>
          <li>
           Pour theRos Wine, grapefruit juice, bourbon, honey and chambord in a large pitcher. Stir until the honey dissolves into the mixture. (If your honey is especially thick, warm it first to thin it out before adding.) Then add the watermelon balls and strawberries. Cover the refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
          </li>
          <li>
           After at least two hours, stir and taste for sweetness. If you like your sangria on the sweeter side, stir in a little more honey. If the sangria is too strong,plan to pour it over ice. When ready to serve stir in the fresh raspberries and pour into glasses.
          </li>
         </ol>
        </span>
       </div>
