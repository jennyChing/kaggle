            I made these the other day, hoping they would become a new cookie/bar on my Christmas cookie plate. Pistachios and dark chocolate seems like a perfect pairing!  I followed the recipe exactly.  Had them in the oven for 23 minutes and they were so dry, they just crumbled and couldnt hold their shape.  I also found the pistachio flavor to be kind of muted next to the dark chocolate and wondered if keeping a little crunch from the pistachio nut might be nicer. When they are chewier, could their flavor be better?  I was, overall, disappointed in how these turned out.  And, given they arent inexpensive to make, dont know that they are worth trying again. :(
           </p>
           <p>
            Sarah
