        <h1 class="entry-title cb-entry-title cb-title" itemprop="headline">
         15 impressive DIY toy makeovers
        </h1>
        <div class="cb-byline">
         <span class="cb-author">
          <a href="http://mumsgrapevine.com.au/author/briony-walker/">
           Briony Walker
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="cb-separator">
          <i class="fa fa-times">
          </i>
         </span>
         <span class="cb-date">
          <time class="updated" datetime="2014-06-20">
           June 20, 2014
          </time>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="cb-post-meta">
         <span class="cb-category cb-element">
          <a href="http://mumsgrapevine.com.au/category/for-kids/" title="View all posts in Kids">
           Kids
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="cb-category cb-element">

         </span>
         <span class="cb-category cb-element">
          <a href="http://mumsgrapevine.com.au/category/play-time/" title="View all posts in Play">
           Play
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="cb-comments cb-element">
          <a href="http://mumsgrapevine.com.au/2014/06/15-impressive-diy-toy-makeovers/#comments">

       <section class="cb-entry-content clearfix">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mumsgrapevine.com.au/2014/05/toymakeover/" rel="attachment wp-att-64040">
          <img alt="Toy makeover" class=" size-full wp-image-64040 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/toymakeover.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         With toys now cheaper than ever, its tempting to send old, battered and worn-out toys to the tip. But many parents dont want to add to landfill dumps or let go of beloved treasures, and have found amazing new ways to upcycle old toys.
        </p>
        <p>
         With a lick of paint, some creativity and elbow-grease, they have made old toys better than new! Weve found 15 inspiring ways old classics have been given a new lease of life.
        </p>
        <h2>
         1. Batmobile
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/CosyCoupe-BatMobile.jpg">
          <img alt="CosyCoupe-BatMobile" class=" size-full wp-image-62351 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/CosyCoupe-BatMobile.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youve got to love the classic Cosy Coupe from
         <a class="external" href="http://www.tikes.com.au/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Little Tikes">
          Little Tikes
         </a>
         . A perennial favourite, youd think theres no way it could possibly be improved. These guys gave their old car a superhero makeover and we think it looks fantastic! Whats more, it only took 40 minutes of work. (via
         <a class="external" href="http://sweetcsdesigns.com/batmobile-cozy-coupe-refashion/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Sweet Designs
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <h2>
         2. IKEA play kitchen
        </h2>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/IKEA-play-kitchen-Makeover-Before-After.jpg">
          <img alt="IKEA-play-kitchen-Makeover-Before-After" class=" size-full wp-image-62387 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/IKEA-play-kitchen-Makeover-Before-After.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Not a fan of the minimalist beech wood and uncluttered lines of the
         <a class="external" href="http://www.ikea.com/au/en/catalog/products/S29874534/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="IKEA Play Kitchen">
          IKEA play kitchen
         </a>
         ? Designer
         <a class="external" href="http://jesykaditri.com/ikea-play-kitchen-makeover-hack/#more-6303" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Jesyka DItri Mars
         </a>
         gave her play kitchen a complete makeover, complete with brass hardware and marble countertop! (via
         <a class="external" href="http://jesykaditri.com/ikea-play-kitchen-makeover-hack/#more-6303" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Visual Vocabularie
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <h2>
         3. Mad Scientists Lab
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/MonsterLabRenovation.jpg">
          <img alt="MonsterLabRenovation" class=" size-full wp-image-62347 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/MonsterLabRenovation.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         One parent turned this tired plastic toy kitchen in to a mad scientists lab, with the help of some spray-paint, stickers created in Adobe Illustrator, and some ghoulish toy props. There are so many cute details, like the toxic waste storage and Scan-O-Tron. Check out the full set of pictures
         <a class="external" href="http://imgur.com/a/C0WQA#ZmsYeR6" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         . (via
         <a class="external" href="http://imgur.com/a/C0WQA#ZmsYeR6" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Imgur
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <h2>
         4. Little Tikes Playhouse
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/LittleTikesPlayhouse.jpg">
          <img alt="LittleTikesPlayhouse" class=" size-full wp-image-62376 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/LittleTikesPlayhouse.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A cheap alternative to buying a new
         <a class="external" href="http://www.tikes.com.au/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Little Tikes">
          Little Tikes
         </a>
         Playhouse is to buy an old one off eBay and spruce it up with some paint and a few plants. We love this makeover  its like a proper little country cottage! (via
         <a class="external" href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/a-little-tikes-house-gets-a-pr-155572" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Apartment Therapy
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/LittleTikesPlayhouseMakeover.jpg">
          <img alt="LittleTikesPlayhouseMakeover" class=" size-full wp-image-62377 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/LittleTikesPlayhouseMakeover.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <h2>
         5. Harrys Hardware, Gas &amp; Auto
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/HarrysHardware.jpg">
          <img alt="HarrysHardware" class=" size-full wp-image-62403 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/HarrysHardware.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its not just old toys that can be recycled into something new for kids. Kate turned an old bookshelf into an old-fashioned gas station, complete with coffee shop. The amount of thought and creative design put into this project has our jaws on the floor. (via
         <a class="external" href="http://www.katescreativespace.com/2013/01/06/welcome-to-harrys-hardware/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Kates Creative Space
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <h2>
         6. Mario Kart
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/MarioKart-Before.jpg">
          <img alt="MarioKart-Before" class=" size-full wp-image-62345 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://d37tn6c1xe3y8i.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/MarioKart-Before.jpg" width="660"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         One Dad took an old Mini Cooper Power Wheel toy car and turned it into a
         <a class="external" href="http://nintendoenthusiast.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Mario-Kart-Arcade-GP-DX.jpg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Mario Kart
         </a>
         for his Nintendo-mad son. We love the massive exhaust at the back! (via
         <a class="external" href="http://imgur.com/a/X3Err#KSGkCZ8" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Imgur
         </a>
         )
