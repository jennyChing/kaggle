                                       <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                                        <div class="field-items" itemprop="articleBody">
                                         <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                                          <p>
                                           The Charlotte
                                           <a href="/nba/team/charlotte-hornets">
                                            Hornets
                                           </a>
                                           need help at power forward and could also use a wing scorer, so the team has been making trade calls to try to fill those spots,
                                           <a href="http://grantland.com/the-triangle/charlotte-hornets-lance-stephenson-problems-how-they-might-fix-it/" target="_blank">
                                            reports Grantlands Zach Lowe
                                           </a>
                                           .
                                          </p>
                                          <p>
                                           One of the players the Hornets could look to move is shooting guard
                                           <a href="/nba/player/lance-stephenson">
                                            Lance Stephenson
                                           </a>
                                           , with Lowe saying few would be surprised if Charlotte looked into dealing him. Stephenson has only been with the team since July after leaving the Indiana
                                           <a href="/nba/team/indiana-pacers">
                                            Pacers
                                           </a>
                                           to sign a three-year, $27 million deal with Charlotte. The third year of the deal is a team option.
                                          </p>
                                          <p>
                                           Free agents who signed deals during this past offseason cant be dealt until Dec. 15.
                                          </p>
                                          <p>
                                           Lowe says that some potential candidates that Charlotte could be looking at include
                                           <a href="/nba/player/randy-foye">
                                            Randy Foye
                                           </a>
                                           or
                                           <a href="/nba/player/wilson-chandler">
                                            Wilson Chandler
                                           </a>
                                           of the Denver
                                           <a href="/nba/team/denver-nuggets">
                                            Nuggets
                                           </a>
                                           , Boston
                                           <a href="/nba/team/boston-celtics">
                                            Celtics
                                           </a>
                                           forward
                                           <a href="/nba/player/jeff-green">
                                            Jeff Green
                                           </a>
                                           , Minnesota
                                           <a href="/nba/team/minnesota-timberwolves">
                                            Timberwolves
                                           </a>
                                           swingman
                                           <a href="/nba/player/corey-brewer">
                                            Corey Brewer
                                           </a>
                                           ,
                                           <a href="/nba/player/dorell-wright">
                                            Dorell Wright
                                           </a>
                                           of the Portland
                                           <a href="/nba/team/portland-trail-blazers">
                                            Trail Blazers
                                           </a>
                                           and Phoenix
                                           <a href="/nba/team/phoenix-suns">
                                            Suns
                                           </a>
                                           power forward
                                           <a href="/nba/player/anthony-tolliver">
                                            Anthony Tolliver
                                           </a>
                                           .
                                          </p>
                                          <ul>
                                           <li>
                                            <strong>
                                             <a href="http://www.si.com/nba/2014/12/01/nba-power-rankings-grizzlies-warriors-cavaliers-west" target="_blank">
                                              DOLLINGER: NBA Power Rankings: West takes over top five
                                             </a>
                                            </strong>
                                           </li>
                                          </ul>
                                          <p>
                                           Stephenson, 24, has struggled in the first two months of the season and has not played in the fourth quarter in either of the past two games.
                                          </p>
                                          <p>
                                           He is averaging 9.6 points, 7.7 rebounds, 5.4 assists in 18 games on 37 percent shooting, including only 18-percent from behind the 3-point line, this season for Charlotte (4-14), which has lost nine games in a row and has the third-worst record in the Eastern Conference.
                                          </p>
                                          <p>

                                          </p>
