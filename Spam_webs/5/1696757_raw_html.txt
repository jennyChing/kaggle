       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Galena Jo Daviess County Illinois: Interview With a CVB
        </h1>
        <div class="comments-link">
         <a href="http://www.visitflyovercountry.com/2014/10/galena-jo-daviess-county-illinois-interview-cvb.html#comments">
          1 Reply
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .comments-link -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Annually over one million tourists visit this small town of 3,500 to enjoy its famed skiing, golfing, shopping, dining, and a very picturesque main street.
         <a href="http://galena.org/">
          Galena
         </a>
         Jo Daviess CountyIllinois is one of TripAdvisors top 10 charming small towns and is also home to many festivals and parades, perhaps the biggest of which is the annual Halloween Parade (coming October 25, 2014) which attracts nearly 15,000 spectators.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_541" style="width: 635px">
         <a href="http://www.visitflyovercountry.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/galena.jpg">
          <img alt="Galena Jo Daviess County Convention &amp; Visitors Bureau (CVB)" class="wp-image-541 size-large" height="310" src="http://www.visitflyovercountry.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/galena-1024x509.jpg" width="625"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Galena Jo Daviess County Convention &amp; Visitors Bureau (CVB)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         I recently spent some time interviewing
         <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/pub/betsy-kaage/20/438/250">
          Betsy Kaage
         </a>
         of the Galena Jo Daviess County CVB about all this Galena and what families can see, do, eat and where to stay in this gorgeous Midwestern small town:
         <span id="more-540">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Galena is one of the Midwests best kept secret tourists spots  what is it about Galena that makes it such a great destination?
          <br/>
         </b>
         <em>
          The first thing youll notice is the picturesque landscape of our Driftless Region. This area was missed by the glaciers, leaving the rolling hills and tree covered bluffs. We boast several of the highest elevations in Illinois, including the highest. Driving Highway 20 through Jo Daviess County is one of the most beautiful drives in the Midwest, with charming towns to greet you along the way. Once youre in Galena youll see the historic architecture set into the hillside along the Galena River.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s1">
          <b>
           What are the top things to do for families in Galena?
           <br/>
          </b>
         </span>
         <em>
          There is plenty of
          <a href="http://galena.org/what-to-do/history-architecture/historic-attractions-tours/">
           <span class="s2">
            history
           </span>
          </a>
          here that the kids will recognize from their studies. President Ulysses S. Grants home, the balcony at the DeSoto House Hotel where President Abraham Lincoln made a speech and remnants of the lead mining industry once prevalent in the area all make this area special. The one hour
          <a href="http://galena.org/what-to-do/history-architecture/historic-attractions-tours/">
           <span class="s2">
            trolley tour
           </span>
          </a>
          through the historic district will give a great overview.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="s1">
           Our newest parks, Gateway Park &amp; Horseshoe Mound provide outdoor fun for the family with trails through restored prairies and spectacular views of our Driftless Region. Read more &amp; see photos on our
           <a href="http://visitgalenanow.com/?p=4544">
            <span class="s2">
             blog
            </span>
           </a>
           !
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="s1">
           Activities include
           <a href="http://www.geocaching.com/">
            <span class="s2">
             geocaching
            </span>
           </a>
           , the alpine slide at Chestnut Mountain Resort, hiking &amp; biking the local trails as well as renting canoes, kayaks and paddle boards at Fever River Outfitters. We have 10 golf courses for golfers of all ages and skill levels and finally horseback riding at Shenandoah Riding Center.
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="s1">
           Contact us for a scavenger hunt when you get to town and check our calendar of events for haunted ghost tours!
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="s1">
           Shops the kids will love includeAtomic Toy Co., Poopsies, The Great American Popcorn Co. (great ice cream!) and the Galena Kandy Kitchen.
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="s1">
           The
           <a href="http://www.ptmurphy.com/Home.html">
            <span class="s2">
             P.T. Murphy Magic Show
            </span>
           </a>
           is a great show for everyone, from 13 to 113! Critically acclaimed magician P.T. Murphy will entertain, mystify, and inspire you to believe! P.T. is known for his personable style, mischievous sense of humor, and sophisticated sleight of hand which the Chicago Reader called An evening that shows magic to its best advantage up close and personal! P.T. Murphy is the co-creator of Chicagos hit show The Magic Cabaret which The Travel Channel hailed as One of Americas Best Magic Shows!
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s1">
          <b>
           When I think of Galena I always thought of wineries and shoppingbut after some research Ive discovered you are also known for skiing?
           <br/>
          </b>
         </span>
         <em>
          Yes, Chestnut Mountain Resort is perched on the hillside overlooking the Mississippi River. Chestnuts Farside Park was recently rated the Best Park in the Midwest by
          <a href="http://snowboarding.transworld.net/photos/resort-guide-2013-2014-poll-results/">
           <span class="s2">
            TransWorld Snowboarding.
           </span>
          </a>
          Theres fun for all ages and skill levels, including lessons!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="s1">
           You can also go sledding, snowshoeing and cross country skiing with Fever River Outfitters out at Eagle Ridge Resort &amp; Spa and along the Galena riverfront trails.
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s1">
          <b>
          </b>
         </span>
         <b>
          What are the best lodging choices for families?
          <br/>
         </b>
         <a href="http://www.eagleridge.com/family-time-package-,special-offers_viewItem_12-en.html">
          <span class="s2">
           Eagle Ridge Resort &amp; Spa
          </span>
         </a>
         has options to stay at the Inn or rent a house/condo. It features an indoor pool, arcade, paddle boating &amp; canoeing on Lake Galena and sledding, cross country skiing &amp; snowshoeing in the winter.
         <b>
          Bonus:
         </b>
         <a href="http://www.eagleridge.com/camp-eagle">
          <span class="s2">
           Camp Eagle
          </span>
         </a>
         &amp; pizza delivery on site!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s2">
          <a href="http://www.chestnutmtn.com/lodging_amenities.cfm">
           Chestnut Mountain Resort
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="s1">
          has rooms with views of the Mississippi. They have an indoor pool &amp; hot tub, an arcade, plus the alpine slide &amp; mini-golf in the summer and
          <a href="http://www.chestnutmtn.com/activities_daycare.cfm">
           <span class="s2">
            downhill skiing/snowboarding
           </span>
          </a>
          in the winter.
          <span class="Apple-converted-space">
          </span>
          <b>
           Bonus:
          </b>
          You can sign the kids up for
          <a href="http://www.chestnutmtn.com/activities_daycare.cfm">
           <span class="s2">
            lessons
           </span>
          </a>
          &amp; get some adult time!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s2">
          <a href="http://www.countryinns.com/offers/3136567">
           Country Inn &amp; Suites
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="s1">
          is the closest to town of the three, has
          <a href="http://www.countryinns.com/galena-hotel-il-61036-8104/ilgalena/rooms/suites/detail">
           <span class="s2">
            themed suites
           </span>
          </a>
          and a small indoor waterpark.
          <b>
           Bonus:
          </b>
          Free breakfast and you can walk to Culvers!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s2">
          <a href="http://galena.org/camping/">
           Camping
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="s1">
          is always an option! We welcome the
          <a href="http://www.blackhawkscouting.org/usgrantpilgrimage">
           <span class="s2">
            U.S. Grant Pilgrimage
           </span>
          </a>
          every spring where Boy Scouts from all over the Midwest come to Galena to celebrate the history of our town.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s1">
          <b>
           Are there any festivals or events that are must-see for families interested in visiting Galena?
           <br/>
          </b>
         </span>
         <em>
          <b>
           Spring:
          </b>
          Its fun to watch &amp; participate in
          <a href="http://www.chestnutmtn.com/events_calendar_detail.cfm?eventID=36">
           <span class="s2">
            The Slush Cup
           </span>
          </a>
          at Chestnut in March! They have music &amp; food vendors throughout the day in addition to the events.
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <b>
           Summer:
          </b>
          You cant beat the Fourth of July in Galena! Candy shops, hometown parade, fireworks and a rodeo at
          <a href="http://www.theshenandoahridingcenter.com/">
           <span class="s2">
            Shenandoah Riding Center
           </span>
          </a>
          says it all! You could travel the county &amp; find parades &amp; fireworks every night!
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <b>
           Fall:
          </b>
          <a href="http://www.galenalions.org/galena-oktoberfest.asp">
           <span class="s2">
            Oktoberfest
           </span>
          </a>
          has lots of activities for kids including the Weiner Dog Races and pumpkin decorating.
          <a href="http://galena.org/galena-fall-event-calendar/">
           <span class="s2">
            The Halloween Parade
           </span>
          </a>
          in Galena is definitely a must-see! This highly attended event lights up the night sky and adds to the holiday fun. If youre in town and dont want to miss trick-or-treating the historic houses on Park Ave. is the place to be!
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <b>
           Winter:
          </b>
          A true country Christmas is what youll find in Galena. Main St. is draped in garland and decorations and there are
          <a href="http://galena.org/holidays-in-galena/">
           <span class="s2">
            activities
           </span>
          </a>
          throughout December including
          <a href="http://galena.org/events-calendar/">
           <span class="s2">
            Living Windows
           </span>
          </a>
          , where the business owners on Main St. create live vignettes in their shop windows for the evening.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="s1">
          <b>
           And finally, where should families go to get more information or help planning their Galena vacation?
           <br/>
          </b>
         </span>
         Stay tuned for a Family Friendly tab at
         <a href="http://galena.org/">
          <span class="s2">
           galena.org
          </span>
         </a>
         . Our website provides contact information for restaurants &amp; hotels, and an up-to-date calendar of events. Click our
         <a href="http://galena.org/trip-planning-tools/">
          <span class="s2">
           Trip Planning Tools
          </span>
         </a>
         tab at galena.org for links to the Chambers in the County for details on local festivities and helpful websites. We also have a
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/galenacvb/family-fun/">
          <span class="s2">
           Family Fun
          </span>
         </a>
         Board on Pinterest!
         <br/>
         <b>
          Press:
          <br/>
         </b>
         <a href="http://www.midwestliving.com/travel/illinois/galena/">
          Midwest Living
          <br/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://galena.org/awards-honors/">
          Awards &amp; Honors
          <br/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.wisconsinparent.com/midwest-travel-geocaching-in-galena-illinois/">
          Wisconsin Parent
         </a>
        </p>

    date.setTime(date.getTime()+(days*24*60*60*1000));
    var expires = "; expires="+date.toGMTString();
  } else {
    var expires = "";
  }
  document.cookie = name+"="+value+expires+"; path=/";
}

function readCookie(name) {
  var nameEQ = name + "=";
  var ca = document.cookie.split(';');
  for(var i=0;i &lt; ca.length;i++) {
    var c = ca[i];
    while (c.charAt(0)==' ') {
      c = c.substring(1,c.length);
    }
    if (c.indexOf(nameEQ) == 0) {
      return c.substring(nameEQ.length,c.length);
    }
  }
  return null;
}

function eraseCookie(name) {
  createCookie(name,"",-1);
}

function addExpandCollapse(id, expandSym, collapseSym, accordion) {

    if (accordion==1) {
      var theDiv = jQuery(this).parent().parent().find('span.collapse').parent().children('div');
      jQuery(theDiv).hide('normal');
      jQuery(this).parent().parent().find('span.collapse').removeClass('collapse').addClass('expand');
      createCookie(theDiv.attr('id'), 0, 7);
      jQuery(this).parent().parent().find('span.sym').html(expandSym);
      //jQuery('#' + id + ' .expand .sym').html(expandSym);
    }
    expandArch(this, expandSym, collapseSym);
    return false;
  });
  jQuery('#' + id + ' span.collapse').live('click', function() {
    collapseArch(this, expandSym, collapseSym);
    return false;
  });
}
