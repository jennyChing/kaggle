          <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
           AirDroid 3 mirrors your Android smartphone on your PC
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-post-meta">
           <div class="author-link">
            Posted by
            <a href="http://sahdevgroup.ac.in/author/sahdevnewac/" itemprop="author" rel="author">
             Sahdev Group
            </a>
           </div>
           <div>
            Date:
            <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2014-12-06T10:32:58+00:00" itemprop="dateCreated">
             December 06, 2014
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="entry-cat">
            in:
            <a href="http://sahdevgroup.ac.in/category/gadgets/" rel="category tag">
             Gadgets
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="comments-link">
            <a href="#comments">

         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <div class="entry-content-data ">
          </div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            <img alt="AirDroid 3 mirrors your Android smartphone on your PC" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5967" height="359" src="http://sahdevgroup.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/airdroid-624x351.jpg" width="638"/>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            Working on tedious tasks on your phone can be quite irritating at times. Well, AirDroid 3 makes things a lot easier for you now.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            AirDroid is an applicationvia which you can manage your android device directly on a web browser, without a cable. The updated version comes in with many useful features.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            The apphas made its entry to Google Play and has added new applications for Mac OS and Windows. Earlier, you would have to log into the AirDroid server through a web browser.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            One of the new features added to AirDroid is Air Mirror.This featureallows youto mirror your smartphone on your desktop. This makes it easier for users as they can interact with their apps, simply with a mouse and keyboard.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            Initially, you could onlyreceive alerts for incoming calls and messages, but not respond to them directly via your desktop. With the new update, you can not only answer calls but you could mute or dismiss them as u like.As many users around the globe constantly useinstant messaging services, this feature turns out to be convenient as notificationsare directly pushed to the desktop in real time.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            Air Droid 3 adds in more than notifications for calls and messages. You could view notifications from any app you like and also, silent a few of them if they tend to disturb you.You could alsotransfer files, copy/paste links from web browsers as well. It also allows you to view your phonecontacts, directly from your desktop too.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            In addition, Air Droid 3 enablesend-to-end encryption for all SMS, app notifications, contact data, account credentials and other sensitive data.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            The app has been redesigned to suit the new the
            <strong>
             Android 5.0 Lollipop
            </strong>
            s Material Design.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
            The updated AirDroid 3 app is available to download from
            <strong>
             Google Play
            </strong>
            , while Mac and Windows apps can be downloaded from the company website.
           </span>
          </p>
