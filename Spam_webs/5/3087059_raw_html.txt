                   In this page, we introduce best colleges in industrial and organizational psychology, a part of Psychology major program and compare tuition costs between them. Industrial and organizational psychology (also known as I-O psychology, occupational psychology, work psychology, WO psychology, IWO psychology and business psychology) is the scientific study of human behavior in the workplace and applies psychological theories and principles to organizations. I-O psychologists are trained in the scientistpractitioner model. I-O psychologists contribute to an organization's success by improving the performance, satisfaction, safety, health and well-being of its employees.
