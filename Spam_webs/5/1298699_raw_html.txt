          CAKE exhibits tasty take on performance analytics at ad:tech NY
         </h1>
        </div>
        <div class="author">
         <img class="avatar" src="http://45.55.255.229/X4M2IR0zP3aO50OI7H0q2fyRwLo=/34x34/smart/http://45.55.255.229/user/image/100000033/lar2-jpg"/>

        <div class="bodycopy">
         <p>
          The ad:tech NY conference is filled to the brim with exhibitors. There are so many, actually, that it can get a bit overwhelming. Which ones do you make time to check out? Which ones are changing things in a major way? Thats why, before each conference, we like to take some time out to introduce some of the exhibitors that will be filling up the Javits Center. Who knows, maybe youll find something that really catches your eye ahead of time, thus saving you all of that aimless wandering.
         </p>
         <p>
          Today we discuss a wise veteran on the digital marketing stage,
          <a href="http://getcake.com/" rel="nofollow">
           CAKE.
          </a>
          The company, founded in 2007, continues to innovate and surprise even the most cynical among us. What do they do exactly? Something pretty cool. They help people make sense of the bottomless amalgam that is digital advertising.
         </p>
         <p>
          How many times have you plunked down some coin on an online marketing campaign only to have no real way to measure its success? It happens far too often, as one would imagine. Hit counts, pages viewed and all of that are nice. They give you a small idea of what is going on out there, but they are far from comprehensive. This is of particular import for small businesses, where each and every dollar spent on advertising needs to be maximized.
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats where the wizards at CAKE come in. Theyve designed a suite of software that tracks your digital advertising dollars in real time, presented in any metric you could possibly imagine. Real-time analytics and tracking are only the beginning with these folks, however. They also empower their customer base by letting the marketer control what they target on the fly. Youll never have to call someone and wait idly by as your campaign is subtly tweaked to your liking. You just open up the software and make the changes yourself. Is the campaign not making much traction in one corner of the Internet? Amp it up, or abandon it. Its all up to you.
         </p>
         <p>
          This ability to tweak on the fly is of paramount importance in todays Internet. After all, things change in a heartbeat. A video can go viral in an hour. Movements are created and destroyed in the span of an afternoon. CAKE helps marketers not only tune into that but to take advantage of it as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          So what have they got on their exhibitor plate for this years ad:tech? Well, theyll have cake pops for one. If thats not an awesome incentive to listen to what theyve got to say, we dont know what is.
         </p>
