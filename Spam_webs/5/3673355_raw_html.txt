                  <p>
                   A mobile website is just like any other website that is incorporated with browser-based HTML pages and are linked together and accessed through the internet. The major difference between a mobile website and a standard website is that it is designed for devices with small display and touch-screen interface. Just like any other website, you can view data, images, video and textual content on a mobile website. It is integrated with location-based mapping and click-to-call attributes. On the other hand, a mobile app is a software application that finds its usage on small wireless computing gadgets like tablets and smartphones. According to a recent survey, majority of the consumers prefer mobile apps over mobile websites due to their speed, convenience and ease of use.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    Mobile phone app development
                   </strong>
                   is comparatively easy as you can get free frameworks and development tools for starting the app development process. Many organizations lay emphasis on developing mobile websites for generating good amount of traffic from mobile devices, and thereby giving a mobile website more priority over an app.
                  </p>
                  <h3>
                   Difference between Mobile Website and Mobile App
                  </h3>
                  <ul>
                   <li>
                    Mobile websites are basically designed for viewing data on varied mobile devices such as mobile phones and tablets, whereas mobile applications are small software programs that run on native OS within the mobile phone.
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    A mobile app is ideal for frequent and repeated use. A mobile website is designed for mobile communication with the purpose of disseminating information to its target audience.
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Mobile websites' accessibility is dependent on wi-fi for Internet connection. A mobile app is always accessible no matter where you are as it depends on the native connectivity of your smartphone.
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    A mobile app has the ability to send push notifications directly to you. Mobile websites are static and do not feature any interactive ability.
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    In case of a mobile website, an update takes place without the user's interference. It is an automatized online process. However, in case of mobile apps, you need to download and authorize all the updates.
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Mobile websites include both the characteristics of a website and a mobile device. On the other hand, a mobile app is specifically designed for use on mobile devices only.
                   </li>
                  </ul>
