       <span itemprop="description">

         The newspaper acknowledged the article published alleged Kanye West quotes "without attribution" that were found on a satirical website, and proved to be "fictitious."

      <div class="EchoTopic">
       <span id="intelliTxt">
        <span class="articleBody" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          In the world of celebrities, it isn't' unknown for media outlets to print false reports from time to time, but it is even rarer when they acknowledge this.However, the New York Times recently admitted to publishing fake
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/info/kanye_west">
           Kanye West
          </a>
          quotes in an article titled '
          <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2014/11/23/style/fear-of-kim-kardashians-derriere.html?smid=tw-share&amp;_r=1" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Fear of Kim Kardashian's Derriere
          </a>
          .'
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Kanye West " height="656" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ln/20141101/lacma_art_film_gala_011114_main/kanye-west-kim-kardashian-2014-lacma-art-film-gala_4440169.jpg" style="vertical-align: top; border: 1px solid black;" title="Kanye West " width="500"/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: x-small;">
           The New York Times published false quotes of Kanye claiming to have a better butt than Kim
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          On Friday (Nov 21st), humour columnist Joyce Wadler was caught out when writing the satirical piece about the risks of having such a big behind like
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/info/kim_kardashian">
           Kim
          </a>
          's, in which she quoted Mr. West claiming to have a better butt than his wife's. This false quote came from a story in the
          <a href="http://dailycurrant.com/2014/11/14/kanye-i-have-a-better-butt-than-kim-kardashian-2/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Daily Currant
          </a>
          that was allegedly quoting an interview the rap star gave with Chicago radio station WGYN.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           More:
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/13-year-old-thai-girl-reportedly-declines-kim-kardashian-offer-adopt-decides-remain-close-family_4476238">
            13-Year-Old Thai Girl Reportedly Declines Kim Kardashian's Offer To Adopt Her, Decides To Remain Close To Her Family
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          "An earlier version of this column was published in error," the New York Times statement reads. "That version included what purported to be an interview that
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/kanye-west">
           Kanye West
          </a>
          gave to a Chicago radio station in which he compared his own derrire to that of his wife, Kim Kardashian. Mr. West's quotes were taken, without attribution, from the satirical website The Daily Currant. There is no radio station WGYN in Chicago; the interview was fictitious, and should not have been included in the column."
         </p>
         <p>
          Wadler wrote the article after the Kardashian sister made headlines with her
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/kim-kardashian-tries-to-break-the-internet-with-nsfw-paper-magazine-cover_4458298">
           fully nude Paper Magazine photo-shoot
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           More:
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/khloe-kardashian-supports-kim-paper-mag-shoot-kim-amazing-body-looks-incredible_4462782">
            Khloe Kardashian Supports Kim's Paper Mag Shoot: "Kim Has An Amazing Body, She Looks Incredible"
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          "How happy do you think Kim will be with her enormous can at 80?" Wadler wrote. "There she'll be, trying to negotiate narrow supermarket aisles with her wheelie cart and her massive derrire, although it is true, given her talent with the champagne glass, she may be able to put a few containers of Metamucil on it."
         </p>
