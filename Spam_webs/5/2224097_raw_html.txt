         <h1 class="headline">
          10 Reasons IUDs are the Best, As Explained By Broad City Gifs
         </h1>
         <div id="post-info">
          <div id="post-info-left">
           By
           <a href="http://articlecats.com/index.php/author/holly-richmond/" rel="author" title="Posts by Holly Richmond">
            Holly Richmond
           </a>
           on April 6, 2015
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="fb-like" data-action="like" data-href="https://www.facebook.com/articlecatsnews" data-layout="standard" data-share="true" data-show-faces="FALSE">
         </div>
         <div id="content-area">
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The IUD is fuckings best-kept secret.
          </p>
          <p>
           A whopping 99% of women with IUDs, or intrauterine devices,
           <a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/health/iud" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.womenshealthmag.com/health/iud', 'are happy with them');">
            are happy with them
           </a>
           . The tiny plastic T, just over an inch long, is almost 100% effective. Once a doctor inserts it waaaay up at the top of your uterus, where nary a dick can tread, an IUD basically puts up a sign that says STAY OUT, SPERM.
          </p>
          <p>
           (For you science nerds, it makes the mucus in your cervix thicker, which stops sperm from getting to your eggs or fertilizing them. And it makes the lining of your uterus thinner, so even if there
           <i>
            were
           </i>
           a fertilized egg, it wouldnt be able to stick to your uterine wall and turn into a kid.) Heres a map:
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3224" style="width: 560px">
           <img alt="where IUDs are uterus" class="wp-image-3224" height="390" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/iud-in-uterus-diagram.jpg" width="550"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="https://nwhn.org/2013/10/03/misconceptions-iud-it%E2%80%99s-time-move-on%E2%80%A6" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://nwhn.org/2013/10/03/misconceptions-iud-it%E2%80%99s-time-move-on%E2%80%A6', 'National Womens Health Network');">
             National Womens Health Network
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           With all this awesomeness, why do
           <a href="http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2014/05/30/forget-the-pill-iud-is-the-new-queen-of-birth-control.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2014/05/30/forget-the-pill-iud-is-the-new-queen-of-birth-control.html', 'only about 10%');" target="_blank">
            only about 10%
           </a>
           of U.S. women have one? You can thank plain ol ignorance, misinformation, and 70s health scares. (Real talk: Theyre safe. Women using an IUD have no increased risk of pelvic infection or infertility,
           <a href="http://bedsider.org/features/243-5-myths-about-the-iud-busted" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://bedsider.org/features/243-5-myths-about-the-iud-busted', 'says Bedsider');" target="_blank">
            says Bedsider
           </a>
           .)
          </p>
          <p>
           So to spread the word, here are 10reasons why IUDs are amazing, as illustrated by
           <i>
            Broad City
           </i>
           gifs. (Note: Im not a doctor. Talk to your gyno first, obvs.)
          </p>
          <h2>
           <b>
            1. They last 5-10 years
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           There are 3 IUDs on the market right now: the
           <a href="http://www.skyla-us.com/index.php" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.skyla-us.com/index.php', 'Skyla');">
            Skyla
           </a>
           , the
           <a href="http://www.mirena-us.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mirena-us.com/', 'Mirena');">
            Mirena
           </a>
           , and the
           <a href="http://www.paragard.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.paragard.com/', 'Paragard');">
            Paragard
           </a>
           . The first two have a teeny amount of hormones (progestin) and the Paragard has zero hormones (its made of copper). The Skyla is the newest and lasts three years. The Mirena lasts for five, and the Paragard lasts for 10. Thats right: up to a decade of not having to worry about getting knocked up.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3282" style="width: 510px">
           <a href="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/giphy.gif">
            <img alt="giphy" class="wp-image-3282 size-full" height="252" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/giphy.gif" width="500"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-abbi-abrams-broadcityedit-mkYm3TlEhEzss" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-abbi-abrams-broadcityedit-mkYm3TlEhEzss', 'Giphy');">
             Giphy
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <b style="line-height: 1.5;">
            2. You dont have to remember to take anything
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Forget to take the pill? Or take it several hours late and then worry that it wont work? NO MORE. You dont even have to
           <i>
            think
           </i>
           about your IUD. It just sits up in there, doing its thing. No phone alarm needed. As
           <em>
            Time
           </em>
           <a href="http://time.com/the-best-form-of-birth-control-is-the-one-no-one-is-using/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://time.com/the-best-form-of-birth-control-is-the-one-no-one-is-using/', 'notes');" target="_blank">
            notes
           </a>
           , about half of pregnancies in the U.S. are unplanned, which is way too high.If you can eliminate a lot of [unwanted pregnancies]by taking out the human ability to screw up, youve done fabulous stuff, Yale professorDr. Mary Jane Minkin told
           <em>
            Time
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3226" style="width: 560px">
           <img alt="yas queen broad city gif ilana" class="wp-image-3226" height="308" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/yas-queen-broad-city-ilana.gif" width="550"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-ilana-glazer-yaaas-1200vaoTNoXJ04" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-ilana-glazer-yaaas-1200vaoTNoXJ04', 'Giphy');">
             Giphy
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           <b style="line-height: 1.5;">
            3. You stop getting your period (in some cases)
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           With the Skyla and the Mirena, the two IUDs with hormones, your period is either lighter or goes away. (The Paragard can actually make it heavier.) The Hairpins
           <a href="http://thehairpin.com/2012/03/iuds-or-a-detailed-guide-to-long-term-sperm-scarecrows" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://thehairpin.com/2012/03/iuds-or-a-detailed-guide-to-long-term-sperm-scarecrows', 'super-thorough IUD guide');" target="_blank">
            super-thorough IUD guide
           </a>
           says 90% of women with a Mirena have lighter periods after a year, and 20% dont have a period at all. Think of all that money youll save on tampons, pads, or the DivaCupplus the ability to fearlessly wear white pants.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3227" style="width: 255px">
           <img alt="broad city gif ilana singing lift" class="wp-image-3227 size-full" height="245" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/broad-city-ilana-singing-lift.gif" width="245"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.fashionnstyle.com/articles/30488/20141110/gifs-that-sum-up-how-excited-we-are-after-watching-the-trailer-for-season-2-of-broad-city.htm" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.fashionnstyle.com/articles/30488/20141110/gifs-that-sum-up-how-excited-we-are-after-watching-the-trailer-for-season-2-of-broad-city.htm', 'Fashion &amp; Style');">
             Fashion &amp; Style
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           4.
           <b>
            Theyre almost 100% effective
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           IUDs are
           <a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/health/iud" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.womenshealthmag.com/health/iud', '99% effective');" target="_blank">
            99% effective
           </a>
           ! As one doctor
           <a href="http://well.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/06/04/the-most-effective-form-of-birth-control/?_r=0" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://well.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/06/04/the-most-effective-form-of-birth-control/?_r=0', 'told');" target="_blank">
            told
           </a>
           <i>
            The New York Times
           </i>
           , When women say to me that they want to use the pill, I say, Thats fine, but its 20 times less effective than an IUD. The only contraception thats more effective than IUDs is the birth control implant. No babies unless you want em, folks.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3231" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="broad city i am truly wet gif" class="wp-image-3231 size-full" height="282" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/broad-city-i-am-truly-wet.gif" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="https://wifflegif.com/tags/341891-ilana-glazer-im-truly-wet-holding-hot-guy-gifs" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://wifflegif.com/tags/341891-ilana-glazer-im-truly-wet-holding-hot-guy-gifs', 'WiffleGif');">
             WiffleGif
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           5. Theyre not the same as the scary 70s ones
          </h2>
          <p>
           In the 1970s, Americans were freaked out by the Dalkon Shield; not only because it sounded like a Star Trek villian, but because this IUD accidentally caused infections, miscarriages, and sometimes death. Aaaa! Thankfully, todays IUDshave been reengineered (they look like a T instead of a spider) and are much safer. But most Americans and even many doctorsdont know this, which is why IUD use in the U.S. is much lower than Europes. (
           <a href="http://www.motherjones.com/blue-marble/2012/09/why-are-iuds-unpopular" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.motherjones.com/blue-marble/2012/09/why-are-iuds-unpopular', 'One-fourth');" target="_blank">
            One-fourth
           </a>
           of Norwegian women using birth control have an IUD!)
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3263" style="width: 510px">
           <a href="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/tumblr_n1otb91MsQ1qazkdco1_500.gif">
            <img alt="piclist" class="size-full wp-image-3263" height="281" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/tumblr_n1otb91MsQ1qazkdco1_500.gif" width="500"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://picslist.com/image/broad_city/hannibal_buress/a_true_national_treasure/Well_I_got_another_cake_cooking_You_can_come_over_and_we_can_eat_it/78074896165" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://picslist.com/image/broad_city/hannibal_buress/a_true_national_treasure/Well_I_got_another_cake_cooking_You_can_come_over_and_we_can_eat_it/78074896165', 'piclist');">
             piclist
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           <b>
            6. You cant even tell its there
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           IUDs are only about an inch long. Whoever youre sexing wont be able to feel it, because its way up in your uterus. You dont have to put anything in (like the Nuva Ring) or slap anything on your arm, like the patch. Nope. Its just there, and nobody can tell! Pretty dope, right?
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3234" style="width: 632px">
           <img alt="broad city respect your dick gif" class=" wp-image-3234" height="350" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/broad-city-respect-your-dick-later.gif" width="622"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-ilana-glazer-mTbVL9Txn3Nw4" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-ilana-glazer-mTbVL9Txn3Nw4', 'Giphy');">
             Giphy
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           <b style="line-height: 1.5;">
            7. Theyre low in hormones (or have no hormones)
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           If you didnt like the hormones in the pill, or they messed you up, good news. The Paragard has zero hormones in it. And the Mirena and Skyla release about
           <em>
            one-tenth
           </em>
           as much hormones a day as the typical Ortho Tri-Cyclen Lo pill.Neither the Mirena nor the Skyla contain estrogen. Plus, IUDs dont create a false menstrual cycle like the pill does. (Learn more about the
           <a href="http://bedsider.org/features/317-all-about-hormones" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://bedsider.org/features/317-all-about-hormones', 'hormones in various types of birth control here');" target="_blank">
            hormones in various types of birth control here
           </a>
           .)
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3235" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="broad city gif nature's pocket" class=" wp-image-3235 size-full" height="281" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/broad-city-vagina-natures-pocket.gif" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="https://vproud.tv/conversation/2260/straight-out-of-natures-pocket-broad-city-returns-have-abbi-ilana-officially-become-the-funniest-duo-on-tv#related-content" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://vproud.tv/conversation/2260/straight-out-of-natures-pocket-broad-city-returns-have-abbi-ilana-officially-become-the-funniest-duo-on-tv#related-content', 'VProud');">
             VProud
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           <b>
            8. Its free with insurance or Medicaid
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Without insurance, an IUD can run you up to $1,200. Yikes. Thanks to the Affordable Care Act, though, your insurance or Medicaid
           <a href="http://www.mirena-us.com/how-to-get-mirena/insurance-coverage.php" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mirena-us.com/how-to-get-mirena/insurance-coverage.php', 'should completely cover it');" target="_blank">
            should completely cover it
           </a>
           . (Thanks, Obama, for serious!) All [insurance] plans are required to cover contraceptive methods and related services, including counseling, insertion, and removal, without copays or deductibles, says
           <a href="http://rhrealitycheck.org/article/2014/03/27/2014-the-year-of-the-iud/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://rhrealitycheck.org/article/2014/03/27/2014-the-year-of-the-iud/', 'RH Reality Check');" target="_blank">
            RH Reality Check
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Your insurance might need a doctors note explaining why you need an IUD instead of cheaper birth control, like the pill, but they legally cant deny it to you. If they do, RH Reality Check says to get in touch with the National Womens Law Center hotline for help (1-866-PILL-4-US or
           <a href="mailto:info@nwlc.org" target="_blank">
            info@nwlc.org
           </a>
           ). Free IUDs? Hell yeah!
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3236" style="width: 410px">

          <h2>
           <b style="line-height: 1.5;">
            9. Its totally reversible
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           IUDs do
           <em>
            not
           </em>
           make you infertile.As soon as your gyno takes outyour IUD, you can get pregnant. So if you just want to avoid babies until the time is right, you have nothing to worry about! Easier than getting your tubes tied and then having to reverse it.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3238" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="broad city gif abbi dance" class=" wp-image-3238 size-full" height="262" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/broad-city-abbi-dance-blue-dress.gif" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://gifrific.com/abbi-dancing-broad-city/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://gifrific.com/abbi-dancing-broad-city/', 'gifrific');">
             gifrific
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           10.
           <b style="line-height: 1.5;">
            Getting one is fast
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           It only takes about five minutes to get an IUD inserted. This IS pretty painful for most women, though; not gonna lie. Ask your OB-GYN what dosage of painkillers to take beforehand. You can definitely drive home after, and you can go back to work even, but Id use it as an excuse to lie on the couch with a milkshake and some Netflix.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3241" style="width: 255px">
           <img alt="broad city gif my legs are wide open" class="wp-image-3241 size-full" height="245" src="http://cdn.articlecats.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/broad-city-my-legs-are-wide-open.gif" width="245"/>

            <a href="http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-jaime-ilana-wexler-GT2ZhPVOvASIM" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://giphy.com/gifs/broad-city-jaime-ilana-wexler-GT2ZhPVOvASIM', 'Giphy');">
             Giphy
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           <b>
            Disclaimers
           </b>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Remember, an IUD doesnt protect you against herpes or any other STI. And during the first few months, as your body adjusts to it, sometimes you get crazy, dizzying cramps. Some people get depressed or bad acne. And
           <a href="http://www.drugdangers.com/mirena/ectopic-pregnancy.htm" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.drugdangers.com/mirena/ectopic-pregnancy.htm', 'in really rare cases');" target="_blank">
            in really rare cases
           </a>
           , you could have an ectopic pregnancy, which is when an embryo ends up in one of your Fallopian tubes instead of your uterus. Your OB GYN will explain all of this stuff.
          </p>
          <p>
           But mostly, in my experience, IUDs are right up there with cupcakes and unicorns. Get one and enjoyyears of baby-free boning!
          </p>
