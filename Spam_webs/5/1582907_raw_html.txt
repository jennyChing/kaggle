               <h2 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
                <a href="/todays-dental-of-boxborough/clear-braces-are-becoming-more-popular-with-teens-and-adults-boston-boxborough" title="Clear Braces are Becoming More Popular with Teens and Adults- Boston, Boxborough">
                 Clear Braces are Becoming More Popular with Teens and Adults- Boston, Boxborough
                </a>
               </h2>
               <div class="post-details">
                <a href="https://plus.google.com/100121229027985567781?rel=author" rel="author" target="_new">
                 Joseph Coupal
                </a>
                - Friday, July 11, 2014
               </div>
               <div class="post-body">
                <p itemprop="description">
                 <strong>
                  <img alt="" src="/images/blogs/teen-girl.JPG" style="border: 0px solid; width: 365px; height: 166px; float: right; margin-left: 10px;"/>
                  If youve always dreamed of having the perfect smile,
                 </strong>
                 braces can not only fix misaligned teeth, but also correct non-cosmetic issues such as TMJ and sleep apnea, as well as prevent gum disease.
                 <a href="http://www.boxboroughteeth.com/clear-braces.html">
                  Clear braces
                 </a>
                 , or clear aligners, are a revolutionary system of treating crooked teeth. They have changed the way patients are treated - long gone are those uncomfortable metal braces, now replaced with clear braces.
                </p>
                <p>
                 With clear braces patients can see the beginning of their new smile within the first few weeks of treatment. It is the
                 <strong>
                  ideal treatment for children and teens
                 </strong>
                 . More comfortable than traditional braces, clear braces are effective at treating mild to severe crowding.
                </p>
                <p>
                 If you are a
                 <strong>
                  teenager or adult, its not too late to have your teeth aligned
                 </strong>
                 . In fact, more and more patients are adults. Invisible braces are the ideal treatment for those whose teeth have fully erupted and whose jaws have stopped growing. Clear braces work as a series of
                 <a href="http://www.boxboroughteeth.com/clear-braces.html">
                  clear removable aligners
                 </a>
                 to straighten teeth without metal wires or brackets. Little by little, results are accomplished, and there is no mouth irritation.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The
                 <a href="http://www.boxboroughteeth.com/clear-braces.html">
                  removable braces
                 </a>
                 means you can eat and drink what you want during treatment as well as brush and floss normally.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.boxboroughteeth.com/clear-braces.html">
                  Clear aligners
                 </a>
                 <strong>
                  are one of the fastest growing treatments in orthodontics
                 </strong>
                 . Treatment with clear braces is a clear way to straighten teeth without braces using aligners which offer high level of comfort, convenience and flexibility in comparison to traditional braces. These aligners are also cost effective and take less time to complete the teeth straightening procedure. Continuous innovation in technology and increasing awareness for oral health among individuals are influencing the demand for clear aligners.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Increasing consumer awareness for oral health and the increasing emphasis we put on straight, white teeth are just a couple reasons clear aligners are increasing in popularity. For more information on clear braces,
                 <a href="http://www.boxboroughteeth.com/contact-us.html">
                  contact Todays Dental of Boxborough
                 </a>
                 .
                </p>
