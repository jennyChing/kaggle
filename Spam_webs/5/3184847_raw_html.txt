         <div class="eb-entry-body type-standard clearfix">
          <div class="eb-entry-article fd-cf" data-blog-content="" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            Maybe youve been living in
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southeast_Asia" target="_blank" title="Southeast Asia">
             Southeast Asia
            </a>
            as a digital nomad, or perhaps youre teaching there, studying, or have been traveling there for months or years on end. Whatever your reason for visiting this warm, delicious, beautiful part of the world, youre sure to identify with the following signs that youre now a Southeast Asia local:
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h2>
            1) Your day is kind of ruined because your favorite fruit shake stand is closed
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/W28tx6T.gif" title="W28tx6T.gif">
             <img alt="W28tx6T.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/W28tx6T.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="W28tx6T.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h2>
            2) You feel like a total jerk if all you have to pay with is a large bill or you dont have exact change
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/facepalm.gif" title="facepalm.gif">
             <img alt="facepalm.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/facepalm.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="facepalm.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h2>
            3) You feel like a boss because you withdraw anywhere from thousands to millions of local currency when you go to the ATM (even though this is only like $100 back home)
           </h2>
           <h2>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/poppin1.gif" title="poppin1.gif">
             <img alt="poppin1.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/poppin1.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="poppin1.gif"/>
            </a>
           </h2>
           <h2>
            4) Every meal is cause for much excitement because it will be cheap and it will be delicious
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/feeding.gif" title="feeding.gif">
             <img alt="feeding.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/feeding.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="feeding.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            5) Youve gotten kind of used to waiting 5 minutes for a page (or a .gif) to load on the slow internet
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/sad.gif" title="sad.gif">
             <img alt="sad.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/sad.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="sad.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/tired.gif" title="tired.gif">
             <img alt="tired.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/tired.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="tired.gif"/>
            </a>
           </h2>
           <h2>
           </h2>
           <h2>
            6) Youve eaten enough rice and noodle dishes to last a lifetime
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/too-full.gif" title="too-full.gif">
             <img alt="too-full.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/too-full.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="too-full.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            7) You get kind of embarrassed when unseasoned, rowdy Western tourists sit near you, hoping that the locals dont think youre together
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/dsgstd.gif" title="dsgstd.gif">
             <img alt="dsgstd.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/dsgstd.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="dsgstd.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            8) Though youve encountered all manner of bathroom, it doesnt make the bad ones any easier to deal with
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/y3PWaHz.gif" title="y3PWaHz.gif">
             <img alt="y3PWaHz.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/y3PWaHz.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="y3PWaHz.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            9) You feel pretty cool the first time youre able to order food or conduct a transaction successfully in the local language
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/vhpy.gif" title="vhpy.gif">
             <img alt="vhpy.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/vhpy.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="vhpy.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            10) You feel even cooler when the locals recognize and say hi to you when you pass by
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/dink.gif" title="dink.gif">
             <img alt="dink.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/dink.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="dink.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            11) You see something you really want at the night market, but know to play it cool so that the haggling goes more smoothly
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/5uy.gif" title="5uy.gif">
             <img alt="5uy.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/5uy.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="5uy.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            12) Unless you really want it, then youll just pay the asking price
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/give_it_to_me.gif" title="give_it_to_me.gif">
             <img alt="give_it_to_me.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/give_it_to_me.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="give_it_to_me.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            13) You realize you got a word completely wrong, and now youre buying a ticket for the wrong bus, getting extra fermented fish sauce in your soup, or some other unfortunate outcome
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/cf-dafuq.gif" title="cf-dafuq.gif">
             <img alt="cf-dafuq.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/cf-dafuq.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="cf-dafuq.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            14) You kind of, sort of, almost know what you just ordered for dinner
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a class="easyblog-thumb-preview" href="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/ewtfe.gif" title="ewtfe.gif">
             <img alt="ewtfe.gif" src="https://static.helpgoabroad.com/images/easyblog_images/778/ewtfe.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="ewtfe.gif"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            15) Despite sometimes ordering the wrong food, losing your shoes because youve had to leave them in a pile outside, getting caught in a torrential monsoon when you just went around the corner to buy water, or when things get lost in translation, youre still so dang happy to be in
            <a href="http://wikitravel.org/en/Southeast_Asia" target="_blank" title="Southeast Asia">
             Southeast Asia
            </a>
            , and wouldnt trade it for anything
           </h2>
