    <br/>
    We've teamed up with A Fistful of Fandango, to offer one lucky reader the chance to win a pair of tickets for every night of A Fistful of Fandango 2009.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    A Fistful of Fandango is now in its 3rd year takes place at the 229 Venue, Great Portland Street, in London from Wednesday 9th until Saturday 12th September 2009.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    This year's line-up includes Herman Dune, Future Of The Left, Art Brut, Hatcham Social, The Molotovs, Gaggle, Flashguns, The Chapman Family, LR Rockets, Kong, Televised Crimewave, Kasms, Factory Floor, A Experiment On A Bird In The Air Pump, Video Nasties, Lion Club, and more.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    The event showcases best new and emerging band talent featured at Fandango's Dublin Castle nights that have run over the last 9 years in Camden. This four night stand will once again see Team Fandango take over the venue to deliver six acts a night, with the three bands on the main stage and three bands in Room 2 this year hosted by Converse and Artrocker Music collaboration 'New Blood'.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    Tickets are priced at 12.50 per night, or 20 for 2 day pass, or 25 for a 3 day pass, and a 4 day pass is priced at 30. To buy tickets,
    <a href="http://www.wegottickets.com/af/4/f/968" target="_blank">
     click here
    </a>
    .

     Fistful of Fandango Website
