           20 Million LinkedIn Users Fell for the Top 10% Marketing Scheme
          </h1>
          <!-- .entry-title -->
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <h1 style="text-align: center;">
           Congrats on being the {insert percentage here} of LinkedIn!
          </h1>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3345" style="width: 420px">
           <a href="http://linkedin.com/in/jeffzelaya">
            <img alt="Jeff Zelaya - Top 1 Percent of LinkedIn Most Viewed Profiles" class=" wp-image-3345 " height="400" src="http://jeffzelaya.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Top-1-Percent-of-LinkedIn.png" title="Jeff Zelaya - Top 1 Percent of LinkedIn Most Viewed Profiles" width="410"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Top 1% of LinkedIn?
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           You think I would be happy to receive this email from LinkedIn?
          </h2>
          <p>
           Kind of hard to feel accomplished when it seems like everybody else got the same email. Facebook should follow suit with a
           <strong>
            <em>
             Congrats on being alive today
            </em>
           </strong>
           campaign. Im sure everyone will quickly flood my timeline with how excited they are that Facebook recognized their amazing achievement.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Sorry to sound so cynical, but I believe we just fell for a huge LinkedIn marketing scheme.
           </strong>
           LinkedIn sent a similar message to about 20 million people! Thats about the population of Dominican Republic, Switzerland and Jamaicacombined.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Still feeling special?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://news.yahoo.com/linkedins-4q-results-soar-past-213236143.html" target="_blank" title="LinkedIn Stock Soaring">
            LinkedIns stock is soaring
           </a>
           since its revenue have exceeded analyst forecasts for the 7th straight quarter since their IPO. In order to continue with this growth they are looking for fresh new marketing ideas and this
           <strong>
            Top 10%
           </strong>
