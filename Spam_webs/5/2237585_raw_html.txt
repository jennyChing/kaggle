        <p>
         This EPIC magical quest is only to be released June 4, 2015 (Can/US) from Shout! Factoryand again, Answer The Tullyphone gets a sneak peek. This adventure is for all ages (we recommend 12+)andthe 99 minute release includes a behind-the-Scenes Featurette. The movie is straight out of Ireland so the landscapes were nothing butphenomenal!! They couldnt havechosen a better place to shoot this magical adventure. This magical quest is guaranteed to keep you and your family glued to the screen.

         <strong>
          Synopsis
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Crumbling castles, mysterious riddles, frightening spirits, beautiful horses and a plucky, fearless heroine  director Lisa Mulcahy has lovingly burnished the most beloved coming-of-age elements in this quest film, set in the starkly beautiful landscapes of rural Ireland, set to music
         <span id="yui_3_16_0_1_1425578840891_2356" lang="DE">
          from
          <i>
           Brave
          </i>
          composer Patrick Neil Doyle.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The film stars Lucy Morton as Mickey Miller, a thoroughly modern 12 year oldNew Yorker whose great love is horses. Not surprisingly, she has little enthusiasm for her mothers plan to take Mickey and her little brother to start a new life in a run-down mill she has inherited in Longwood, a windswept Irish town at the end of the world. Mickeys presence in Longwood coincides with the return of the Black Knight, a legendary ruler who found no peace after his baby daughter was taken from him, and whose appearances always mean that something evil is going to happen to the villagers. Mickey hears and sees things that others cant, and forms an immediate bond with Silver, the proudest, haughtiest, mostuntamablehose in the stalls of a nearby castle. Realizing that she is directly linked to the story of the Black Knight, Mickey and her new friend Sean set out to redeem the knight so as to bring lasting peace to Longwood. But first she must save the lives of seven horses and thwart the evil plans of a greedy, ambitious woman, a mighty handful even for the bravest girl!!!
