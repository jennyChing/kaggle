       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Strawberry Melon Smoothie Recipe
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-05-06T09:20:08+00:00">
         May 6, 2014
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a class="fn n" href="http://missfoodiefab.com/author/kyvec/" rel="author" title="Dee Dee C">
           Dee Dee C
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://missfoodiefab.com/melon-strawberry-smoothie-recipe/#comments">
          <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="7491 http://missfoodiefab.com/?p=7491">
           16 Comments
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6832408" data-link="http://missfoodiefab.com/melon-strawberry-smoothie-recipe/" data-summary="" data-title="Strawberry Melon Smoothie Recipe">
        </div>
        <div dir="ltr">
         <div dir="auto">
          <div dir="auto">
           <div dir="ltr">
            <div dir="ltr">
             <div dir="auto">
              <div dir="auto">
               <div dir="ltr">
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Smoothies seem to have taken over the world. Every where I turn I see a strawberry smoothie, tropical smoothie, green smoothie, etc. popping up on my Instagram feed or Bloglovin.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Personally I think the current smoothie craze is another hipster fad, but its nice to see people taking an interest again in something as simple to make as a smoothie.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Ive made smoothies for some years now, but always the unhealthy way I must admit.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Sure I had fruit in my breakfast smoothies, but I also had yogurt, sugary orange juice, and sometimes rainbow sherbet.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 I know Im a mess. Dont tell anyone.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 I challenged myself to start making healthy smoothies, instead of the diabetical smoothies Ive grown accustomed to, and this Melon Strawberry Smoothie was my first attempt.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/breakfast-smoothies-healthy-smoothies-Easy-Smoothie-Recipes.jpg?4f5a35" target="_blank">
                  <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
                   <img alt="Breakfast Fruit Smoothie" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7500" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/breakfast-smoothies-healthy-smoothies-Easy-Smoothie-Recipes.jpg?resize=600%2C921"/>
                   <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://missfoodiefab.com/melon-strawberry-smoothie-recipe/&amp;media=http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/breakfast-smoothies-healthy-smoothies-Easy-Smoothie-Recipes.jpg&amp;description=Strawberry Melon Smoothie Recipe')">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 My melon and strawberry smoothie recipe turned out the bomb.com!
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 I was quite pleased with myself, actually. I thought I would miss the taste of added sugar, and I almost sprinkled a couple of packs of Splenda in it, but one sip let me know it was sweet enough on its own.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberry-smoothie.jpg?4f5a35" target="_blank">
                  <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
                   <img alt="Strawberry Smoothie " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7501" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberry-smoothie.jpg?resize=600%2C420"/>
                   <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://missfoodiefab.com/melon-strawberry-smoothie-recipe/&amp;media=http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberry-smoothie.jpg&amp;description=Strawberry Melon Smoothie Recipe')">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 It was such a simple recipe too! It only took 4 ingredients and no ice.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Yep, Im impressed with myself and my Melon Strawberry smoothie! The next time I do a detox Ill be sipping on one of these for lunch after noshing on a
                 <a href="http://missfoodiefab.com/poached-egg-breakfast-how-to-poach-an-egg/" target="_blank">
                  poached egg for breakfast
                 </a>
                 .
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberry-smoothie-recipe.jpg?4f5a35" target="_blank">
                  <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
                   <img alt="Melon Strawberry Smoothie  " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7502" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberry-smoothie-recipe.jpg?resize=600%2C921"/>
                   <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://missfoodiefab.com/melon-strawberry-smoothie-recipe/&amp;media=http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberry-smoothie-recipe.jpg&amp;description=Strawberry Melon Smoothie Recipe')">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Speaking of smoothies Ive partnered with Smoothie King to give you some tips to start making healthy smoothies at home!
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 Smoothie King has the following tips for you:
                </p>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div dir="ltr">
             <div dir="auto">
              <div dir="auto">
               <div dir="ltr">
                <ul>
                 <li>
                  Add carrots to any smoothie that has a citrus component. The orange flavor masks the carrot taste and adds an extra boost of Vitamin A.
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  Blend kale with fruit to mask the green leafy taste of the kale. Combine kale with apple and kiwi to create a delicious granny smith green apple smoothie.
                 </li>
                </ul>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://missfoodiefab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Smoothie-King-Green-Smoothies.jpg?4f5a35" target="_blank">

                </p>
