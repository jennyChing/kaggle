          <p style="text-align: left;">
           We heard you like San Francisco, so why not use your free time to also look at blogs about San Francisco?
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-5486">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Tumblr is one of those online portals that has mastered the art of taking your free time and vaporizing it into dust. One momentyou are casually browsing, and the next momenthours have passed, you have 16 new reaction GIFssaved to your phone, and a nasty case of carpal tunnel. Since we are feeling particularly masochistic today, why not encourage the vicious cycle?
          </p>
          <p>
           Here are the best San Francisco Tumblrs that you need to follow!
          </p>
          <h3>
           1.
           <a href="http://sanfranciscowaits.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sanfranciscowaits.tumblr.com/', 'SF Loves Waiting In Lines');" target="_blank">
            SF Loves Waiting In Lines
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           An hour and half wait for brunch? No problem!This city is insane  insanely in love with waiting in lines for everything. Might as well get meta and browse this Tumblr while you wait in line.
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://sanfranciscowaits.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sanfranciscowaits.tumblr.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-6080 aligncenter" height="369" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/tumblr_m4rs36VsSj1rxnfvzo1_1280.png-e1421195626341.jpg?679039" width="600"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            SF Loves Waiting In Lines
           </span>
          </div>
          <h3>
           2.
           <a href="http://dogblogsf.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://dogblogsf.tumblr.com/', 'Dogblog');" target="_blank">
            Dogblog
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           If you are remotely as obsessed with dogs as I am, you will no doubt appreciate Dogblog. Its a collection of photos of dogs tied to objects around the city and patiently waiting for their owners, along with some fun commentary.
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://dogblogsf.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://dogblogsf.tumblr.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-6496 aligncenter" height="835" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Screen-Shot-2015-01-27-at-11.14.48-AM.png?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            Dogblog
           </span>
          </div>
          <h4>
          </h4>
          <h3>
           3.
           <a href="http://whilstinsf.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://whilstinsf.tumblr.com/', 'Whilst In SF');" target="_blank">
            Whilst In SF
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Reaction GIFs to all those little San Franciscoisms. A classic.
          </p>
          <div class="mceMediaCreditOuterTemp">
           <a href="http://whilstinsf.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://whilstinsf.tumblr.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-13 at 4.31.55 PM" class="aligncenter wp-image-6086" height="86" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Screen-Shot-2015-01-13-at-4.31.55-PM.png?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 639px">
           <a href="http://whilstinsf.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://whilstinsf.tumblr.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="324" src="http://i.imgur.com/dAIuYHf.gif" width="629"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            WhilstinSF
           </span>
          </div>
          <h3>
           4.
           <a href="http://streetparkedsf.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://streetparkedsf.com/', 'Street Parked SF');" target="_blank">
            Street Parked SF
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Photo documentation ofall the classics parked on the streets of San Francisco. A car lovers dream.
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://streetparkedsf.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://streetparkedsf.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-6090 aligncenter" height="480" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/tumblr_mw2mee6Xwr1qkprbco6_1280.jpg?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            StreetParkedSF
           </span>
          </div>
          <h3>
           5.
           <a href="http://sfcitylights.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sfcitylights.tumblr.com/', 'The SF Life');" target="_blank">
            The SF Life
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           With both old and new photos and frequently updated, this ones a win-win.
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://sfcitylights.tumblr.com/post/103719452497/justinjaredphotography-long-exposure-film-photo" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sfcitylights.tumblr.com/post/103719452497/justinjaredphotography-long-exposure-film-photo', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="tumblr_nfks8ts6Hl1tk98q4o1_1280" class="wp-image-6489 aligncenter" height="432" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/tumblr_nfks8ts6Hl1tk98q4o1_1280.jpg?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            justjaredphotography via The SF Life
           </span>
          </div>
          <h3>
           6.
           <a href="http://www.soulsofsociety.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.soulsofsociety.com/', 'Souls of Society');" target="_blank">
            Souls of Society
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Like Humans of New York, but for San Francisco.
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://www.soulsofsociety.com/post/97754327941/thats-an-incredible-outfit-thank-you-how" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.soulsofsociety.com/post/97754327941/thats-an-incredible-outfit-thank-you-how', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-6470 aligncenter" height="539" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Screen-Shot-2015-01-26-at-4.38.45-PM.png?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            Souls of Society
           </span>
          </div>
          <h3>
           7.
           <a href="http://polaroidsf.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://polaroidsf.com/', 'Polaroid SF');" target="_blank">
            Polaroid SF
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           You guessed it- San Francisco as seen through a vintage Polaroid camera. Theyve been posting a polaroid daily since September 13, 2011!
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://polaroidsf.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://polaroidsf.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-6433 aligncenter" height="802" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/tumblr_nexlrm6lay1r25bkjo1_1280.jpg?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            Polaroid SF
           </span>
          </div>
          <h3>
           8.
           <a href="http://sfmoma.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sfmoma.tumblr.com/', 'SF MOMA');" target="_blank">
            SF MOMA
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           With MOMA still having another 2 years to go until it reopens, we can live vicariously through their Tumblr. Stay visually stimulated with their Submission Friday project.
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://sfmoma.tumblr.com/post/105629142037/submissionfriday-flynncameronjones-flynn-c" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sfmoma.tumblr.com/post/105629142037/submissionfriday-flynncameronjones-flynn-c', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-6441 aligncenter" height="823" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/tumblr_ng18nifTuZ1r95rijo1_1280.png?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            Flynn C Jones via Submission Friday
           </span>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           And a Sad Goodbye to
           <a href="http://sexpigeon.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sexpigeon.tumblr.com/', 'Sex Pigeon');" target="_blank">
            Sex Pigeon
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           The list wouldnt be complete without a nod to the now-defunct Sex Pigeon. It was once a wonderful combination of bizarre humor and witty commentary, but he has relocated to New York. Miss you, Sex Pigeon.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div class="media-credit-container aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
           <a href="http://sexpigeon.tumblr.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sexpigeon.tumblr.com/', '');" target="_blank">
            <img alt="Screen-Shot-2015-01-13-at-4.28.42-PM.png" class="wp-image-6084 aligncenter" height="626" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Screen-Shot-2015-01-13-at-4.28.42-PM.png.jpg?679039" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <span class="media-credit">
            Sex Pigeon
           </span>
          </div>
          <br/>
          Did we miss any crucial ones? Let us know!
