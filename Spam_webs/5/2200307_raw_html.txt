         Know About the Five Essential Elements of Acupuncture
        </h1>
        <i>
         by
         <a class="user" href="/user/peterjackson11">
          Peter J.
         </a>
         <span class="headline">
          Blogger
         </span>
        </i>
        <div class="clear">

        <div class="space_10h">
        </div>
        <div class="authorphoto">
         <span class="__pt_photo">
          <a class="user" href="/user/peterjackson11">

         </span>

        <div style="float:right;width:210px;margin-left:10px;">

        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
          Acupuncture as we all know is a traditional Chinese medicine applied
for treating a number of diseases. It is a very holistic treatment and known
for providing a very positive result for almost all types of ailments. This
method provides a detailed understanding of the entire human body. It treats
the disease from the root level and ensures with a healthy and problem free
life.
         </span>
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
         </span>
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
          In order to cure any kind of physical disorder people choose this
          <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">
           <span style="color:black">
            Chinese medicine
in NB.
           </span>
          </b>
          Most of the practitioners use five elements of acupuncture.
The main five elements that are included in this particular model are wood,
fire, earth, metal and water. While treating the health disorders the
acupuncturists regains the balance between the five elements by inserting
needles in the
          <a href="http://www.northsideacupuncture.com.au/acupuncture/" target="_blank" title="Impressive Acupuncture Treatment In NB- Northside Acupuncture">
           <b>
            acupuncture
           </b>
          </a>
          points.
          <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
           <br/>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
          <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
          <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
          Lets discuss about these five elements:
         </span>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
           <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
             <b>
              Wood
             </b>
             : This element is mainly associated with growth and its main direction is eastward. The main organs that are related to wood are gallbladder and liver. The corresponding color of this element is green and the emotion attached with it is anger. The persons who are inclined to this element are generally adventurous, prefer challenges and generally achieve best in anything where they put their mind. These people mostly suffer from irritation, headache, high blood pressure, muscle spam etc
            </p>
           </span>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
           <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
            <b>
             Fire
            </b>
            : The corresponding direction is southward and is related with warmth. The associated color is red and the emotion attached is joy. The body organs that are interlinked with fire include heart, small intestine and pericardium. The people linked to this element have vibrant spirit, intuitive, empathetic, loves excitement and drama. Some of the health issues like agitation, anxiety, insomnia, rashes, hearth palpitation etc may affect these people.
           </span>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
           <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
            <b>
             Earth
            </b>
            : The corresponding direction of earth element in at the center and the emotions attached with it is worry and the associated color is orange and yellow. Stomach and spleen are connected with earth. People who are linked with the earth element are found to suffer from self doubt and worry and are usually overprotective and easily disappointed. Symptoms like fatigue, muscle tenderness and digestive disorders are found to occur in these people.
            <br/>
           </span>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
           <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
            <b>
             Metal
            </b>
            : The emotion connected with the metal element is sadness and the color related with it is white. Lungs and large intestine are interrelated with metal. Metal type people appreciate discipline, respect reasons and generally aim at living high standards. They are generally found to suffer from dry skin, respiratory problem, poor circulation and stiff joint.
            <br/>
           </span>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="mso-ascii-font-family:
Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-theme-font:
minor-latin">
           <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
            <b>
             Water
            </b>
            : The color related with water is black and the emotion it rules is fear. Kidney and bladder are the two organs that are associated with water. The people falling in this type are clever and self reliant. They generally suffer from loss of libido, backache, loss of vision and hearing.
           </span>
          </span>
         </li>
        </ol>
