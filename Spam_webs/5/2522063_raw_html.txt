      <h1>
       Skip bins
      </h1>
      <p>
       SUEZ environnement can help you manage the removal of your residual building materials by providing convenient and reliable skip bin hire services.
      </p>
      <p>
       Skip bins are delivered and collected from your site. We ensure your
       <a href="http://www.sita.com.au/commercial-solutions/resource-recovery-recycling/construction-demolition/">
        construction waste
       </a>
       is properly disposed while maximising the recovery of recyclable materials. Help reduce your impact on the environment with SUEZ environnement skip bins.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our range of skip bins vary in sizes (from 3 to 9 cubic metres) to suit your commercial and industrial waste requirements.
      </p>
      <p>
       We also provide
       <a href="http://www.sita.com.au/media/publications/1209-SITA_RORO_Brochure.pdf/">
        Roll-on/Roll-off bins
       </a>
       (from 10 to 30 cubic metres) for businesses generating larger volumes of waste.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Need a skip bin?
      </h2>
      <p>
       To hire a skip bin or for more information on our Builders' Bin service in Victoria, or our Need A Bin service in Western Australia and South Australia, contact our Customer Service Centre on 13 13 35, or complete our
       <a href="http://www.sita.com.au/contact">
        online customer enquiry form
       </a>
