             James Robinson
            </b>
            notes, "this was an error message given by my HP A520 printer."
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>

            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            "Hot damn," wrote
            <b>
             Joshua Deltener
            </b>
            , "I can't wait until Empty Case II!"
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <img alt="" src="http://img.thedailywtf.com/images/200903/errord/emptycase.jpg"/>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            "I got this message when first connecting my Mio DigiWalker GPS unit as a USB drive,"
            <b>
             Jonathan
            </b>
            wrote.
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <img alt="" src="http://img.thedailywtf.com/images/200903/errord/MioGPSUSB.PNG"/>

            commented, "everything's easier on a Mac!"
