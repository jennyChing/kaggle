          <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.knittingknoobie.com/2014/07/country-weekend.html">
            Country Weekend
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="post-header-line-1">
          </div>
          <div class="post-body entry-content">
           This weekend we're going away to the country to see our sister in law. Actually she's my ex-sister in law. We decided that even though she and my brother in law divorced, we didn't divorce her!  She and her husband have a lovely home on a lake in upstate New York.  We'll first be going to see the Temptations and the Four Tops on Friday night. Then Saturday will be knitting by the lake and spending time with family. I think my niece will be coming over with my great niece and nephew.  I can't wait. Started packing:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-lC-cDfJScak/U9o3xMQW4ZI/AAAAAAAABw4/9OEC-mEJOUE/s1600/Weekend.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-lC-cDfJScak/U9o3xMQW4ZI/AAAAAAAABw4/9OEC-mEJOUE/s320/Weekend.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <br/>
           I'm bringing along the
           <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/sasha-sock-pattern">
            Sasha socks
           </a>
           I started knitting this week.  They're a fun knit and I can talk and enjoy company while knitting them.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           In other knitting news, my friend,
           <a href="http://www.thatbaldguyknits.com/blog/">
            Rich
           </a>
           , has started a
           <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/groups/that-bald-guy-knits">
            group on Ravelry
           </a>
           and is having a KAL with any one of his sock patterns!
           <br/>
           I love all his patterns but have yet to knit one.  I've narrowed it down to about three different patterns and I'll choose one.  The yarn is already picked out. It's Blue Moon's Socks that Rock lightweight in Sky Blue:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3ofM2pNV_I8/U9o5--mwzCI/AAAAAAAABxE/I75YB750p9M/s1600/Sky+Blue.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3ofM2pNV_I8/U9o5--mwzCI/AAAAAAAABxE/I75YB750p9M/s320/Sky+Blue.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <br/>
           Because Rich's patterns have gorgeous cables I really wanted to use a solid colored yarn which will show off the pattern best.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           So you can see that even though I haven't blogged much lately I am busy knitting!
           <div style="clear: both;">
           </div>
