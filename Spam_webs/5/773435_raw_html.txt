       <div class="wp-caption alignright first_image" id="attachment_480128" style="width: 260px">
        <img alt="Jennifer Lopez Casper Smart Breakup" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-480128" height="356" src="http://s2.gossipcop.com/up/2015/03/Jennifer-Lopez-Casper-Smart-Breakup-250x356.jpg" width="250"/>

       <p>
        <a href="http://gossipcop.com/tag/Jennifer-Lopez">
         <b>
          Jennifer Lopez
         </b>
        </a>
        and
        <b>
         <a href="http://gossipcop.com/tag/Casper-Smart">
          Casper Smart
         </a>
        </b>
        did not fake a breakup, despite a new story. According to a report thats been widely picked up, the couple never split, and only pretended to go their separate ways in an effort to help promote their projects. But
        <b>
         Gossip Cop
        </b>
        can exclusively correct this. Were told they absolutely split in June, but have remained good friends since then.
       </p>
       <p>
        The usually reliable TMZ claims it found out that Lopez and Smart only made believe they had broken up as a business move. The site says the faux split was to generate buzz for her film
        <i>
         The Boy Next Door
        </i>
        , with rumors manufactured that she might be hooking up with her co-star Ryan Guzman.
       </p>
       <p>
        The outlet notes that Lopez and Smart have been spotted several times hanging out together in the nine months since they supposedly pretended to split, and were recently photographed sharing a (very dispassionate) kiss on the American Idol set.
        <b>
         Gossip Cop
        </b>
        s impeccable Lopez insider, however, assures us they are just friends.
       </p>
       <p>
        First, it doesnt even make any logical sense that Lopez would fake a split in early June when
        <i>
         The Boy Next Door
        </i>
        was released nearly
        <i>
         eight months later
        </i>
        in January. Additionally, there would be no reason for the two to sneak around when Lopez could easily say they simply got back together, assuming one subscribes to the fake breakup theory.
       </p>
       <p>
        The truth is they havent reunited nor have they tried to pull the wool over her fans eyes for nearly a year. The same insider that confirmed to
        <b>
         Gossip Cop
        </b>
        a year ago that she wasnt dating
        <b>
         Maksim Chmerkovskiy
        </b>
        ,
        <b>
        </b>
        when a slew of lemming-like sites regurgitated that wholly inaccurate rumor, is the same source who maintains Lopez and Smart are just friends.
       </p>
       <p>
        Could they get back together
        <em>
         some
        </em>
        day? Its possible, but they havent faked a split for nine months nor are they currently dating.
       </p>
