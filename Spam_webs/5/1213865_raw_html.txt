         <p>
          <strong>
           Craft Heraldry Mod
          </strong>
          is a mod that allows you to Craft Heraldry in your game.Before installing/using these or any mods, make sure you backup your .minecraft folder, I (Vazkii), or any other modders for that matter, am not responsible in any way, shape or form for any damage dealt to, but not only, your saves, your minecraft, your computer or others.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://freedownloadminecraft.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Craft-Heraldry-Mod.jpg">
           <img alt="Craft Heraldry Mod" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12013" height="330" src="http://freedownloadminecraft.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Craft-Heraldry-Mod.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://freedownloadminecraft.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Craft-Heraldry-Mod-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Craft Heraldry Mod 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12014" height="330" src="http://freedownloadminecraft.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Craft-Heraldry-Mod-1.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          The first thing youll need is a Heraldic Scroll, that allows you to write your heraldry on a scroll.
          <br/>
          <img alt="Posted Image" src="https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/34938401/Recipes/scroll.png"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          After you customized your heraldry to the best of your design skill
          <strong>
           (note, you must press the Done button!)
          </strong>
          , its time to put it in the world. You can either hang it in a wall or put it on the floor, pick your poison, wall and floor, respectively:
          <br/>
          <img alt="Posted Image" src="https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/34938401/Recipes/wallBanner.png"/>
          <img alt="Posted Image" src="https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/34938401/Recipes/banner.png"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Craft Heraldry ModInstallation
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Youll need:
          </strong>
          Minecraft Forge
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Installation process:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Installing Forge:
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Open your Minecraft launcher.
            </li>
            <li>
             Open the Options menu near the login button
            </li>
            <li>
             Use the link in the Options menu to open the .minecraft directory
            </li>
            <li>
             Navigate to the bin folder.
            </li>
            <li>
             Open minecraft.jar and the Forge version you downloaded with appropriate archive software. (i.e WinRAR, 7-Zip)
            </li>
            <li>
             Drag all contents from Forge into the minecraft.jar
            </li>
            <li>
             Delete the META-INF folder from the minecraft.jar
            </li>
            <li>
             Close both minecraft.jar and Forge download.
            </li>
            <li>
             Go back to the .minecraft directory.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Installing CraftHeraldry:
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             While inside the .minecraft directory, create a new folder titled mods, assuming there isnt one already.
            </li>
            <li>
             Drag and drop the entire CraftHeraldry .zip file into the mods folder. (Do not extract the contents of the .zip)
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
         </ul>
