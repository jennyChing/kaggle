     <section class="content-editor">
      <p>
       <strong>
        Thunderfucker 5. Spring 2012. Pasadena, CA.
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       This one is special to me. It was purchased as an anniversary present for the customer by his wife. Very sweet, I thought. She gave me carte blanche to do whatever it was that her husband wanted. When I emailed him to find out what that was exactly, he sent me this picture..
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="500" src="http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7239/7008758949_8bac150fc4.jpg" width="398"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       So I got to work.
      </p>
      <p>
       Im being a little coy. There was more of a conversation than that.. but not too much more. The customer made it pretty clear that he was up for letting me explore. There were a few criteria. Front disk brake.. single speed.. he liked the raw steel.
      </p>
      <p>
       I sent him back a picture at some point and said, Id like to build you the bike that goes with these bars.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/fastboy/6690846173/in/photostream">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="334" src="http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7014/6690846173_bc41328021.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       He was game and Thunderfucker 5 emerged!
      </p>
