       <textarea cols="25" name="S1" readonly="" rows="19" style="font-family: Arial">
        Four friends have a favorite TV show currently running its fifth season. Oddly enough, while the four are close friends, their interest in TV shows is very different so none of the friends share a common interest in TV shows. In fact, each favorite show even airs on a different channel at a different time. Determine the name of each friend, the title of their favorite TV show, and the day of the week and time that the show airs.

1. The person who enjoyed the TV show, FBI, doesnt watch it on Sunday night. The TV show, Explorer, aired at 7 pm, but it wasnt Mikes favorite show.

2. Family Tides wasnt Als favorite TV show.

3. Rogers favorite TV show aired at 10 pm, but not on Wednesday night.

4. The TV show that aired on Tuesday night is The Z-Files.

5. Mike doesnt watch a TV show at 9 pm. Gary doesnt watch TV on Sunday night.

6. The TV show, Family Tides, aired an hour after the show that Gary liked but an hour before the show that aired on Monday night.
