Of course everyone wanted to charge their phone with this one plug, so they were constantly fighting over it. All of a sudden a magic fairy (the UPS man) brought them a magic charging station with seven (yes, 7) USB charging hubsplenty for everyone to use, not just for their phones, but for their tablets and iPads too. Not only did this magic charging station charge each electronic, it charged it super fast, up to 10 times faster than a regular USB plug! Doesnt this sound like a wonderful story?             <a href="http://www.eggtronic.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
