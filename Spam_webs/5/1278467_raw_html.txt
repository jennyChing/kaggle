     <h1 class="article-headline wordy no-hero">
      Angry Birds Developer Rovios Profits Fall 73% in 2014
     </h1>
     <div class="article-content" id="article-content">
      <div class="article-subhead">
       <div class="article-share-count-container">
        <span class="icon icon-sharrow">
        </span>
        <span class="page-share-count">
         Share.
        </span>
       </div>
       Merchandise sales down.
      </div>
      <span class="article-byline icon-pointy">
       By
       <a href="http://people.ign.com/Luke_Reilly_AU">
        Luke Reilly
       </a>
      </span>
      <aside class="ign-ad ign-ad-300x250 no-hero">
       <div class="sugarad" id="sugarad-300x250">

      <p>
       Finnish mobile developer and publisher Rovio, creator of the
       <a class="autolink" data-legacy-id="61654" href="http://www.ign.com/games/angry-birds/iphone-61654" title="Angry Birds">
        Angry Birds
       </a>
       franchise, has seen its operating profit fall from 36.5 million to 10 million for the 2014 calendar year, reports
       <a href="http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/03/19/us-rovio-results-idUSKBN0MF0AW20150319?feedType=RSS&amp;feedName=technologyNews" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Reuters
       </a>
       . Rovio's total consolidated full-year revenue was also down by 9% year on year.
      </p>
      <p>
       Rovios game revenue was up in 2014 but a steep drop in merchandise sales has had a significant impact on the companys bottom line.
      </p>
      <p>
       On the consumer product side, 2014 was not a satisfactory year, said Rovio CEO Pekka Rantala. However, the upcoming Angry Birds feature film is getting very positive reactions from major retail and license partners as well as from consumer focus groups. This will have a positive impact on licensing in the coming years.
      </p>
      <p>
       Sonys Angry Birds film, which was first hinted at way back in
       <a href="http://au.ign.com/articles/2010/08/25/angry-birds-flying-to-the-screen" target="_blank">
        August 2010
       </a>
       , is slated to hit cinemas on
       <a href="http://au.ign.com/articles/2014/12/24/sonys-angry-birds-movie-gets-a-new-release-date" target="_blank">
        May 20, 2016
       </a>
       . Directed by Clay Kaytis (supervising animator on Bolt and Tangled) and Fergal Reilly (storyboard artist for Space Jam, The Iron Giant, and Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs), the Angry Birds movie will be based on a script by The Simpson writer Jon Vitti. Its cast includes Jason Sudeikis,Josh Gad,Danny McBride,Bill Hader,Maya Rudolph, and Peter Dinklage.
      </p>
