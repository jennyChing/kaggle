                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     Dial Coconut Water Refreshing Mango Body Wash Review and Giveaway
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6671523925823235021">
                     <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="http://www.facebook.com/plugins/like.php?href=http://www.made-only-in-usa.com/2014/07/dial-coconut-water-refreshing-mango.html&amp;send=false&amp;layout=button_count&amp;show_faces=false&amp;width=90&amp;action=like&amp;font=arial&amp;colorscheme=light&amp;height=21" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; width:90px; height:21px;">
                     </iframe>
                     <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="horizontal" data-text="Currently reading:  Dial Coconut Water Refreshing Mango Body Wash Review and Giveaway" data-url="http://www.made-only-in-usa.com/2014/07/dial-coconut-water-refreshing-mango.html" data-via="madeonlyinusa" href="http://twitter.com/share">
                      Tweet
                     </a>
                     <g:plusone href="http://www.made-only-in-usa.com/2014/07/dial-coconut-water-refreshing-mango.html" size="small">
                     </g:plusone>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <i>
                       The Dial brand provided me with a sample of the product in exchange for an honest review. All of the opinions expressed are my own.
                      </i>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://www.purexinsiders.com/resource/resmgr/D_CWRM/Dial-Insiders-Web-coconutMan.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="198" src="http://www.purexinsiders.com/resource/resmgr/D_CWRM/Dial-Insiders-Web-coconutMan.png" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      Who needs a refreshing splash this summer?
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      Get those hands up in the air. Come on, don't be shy.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      Okay, if you're like me, it might be nerve racking to get those arms up after all of the sweating from summer activities. However, be prepared to make a splash with
                      <a href="http://www.dialsoap.com/" rel="nofollow">
                       Dial
                      </a>
                      Coconut Water Refreshing Mango Body Wash.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wGYSBr7xHUI/U9JyzdPUjeI/AAAAAAAAFrA/6uEVE5dtlmc/s1600/IMG_0811.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wGYSBr7xHUI/U9JyzdPUjeI/AAAAAAAAFrA/6uEVE5dtlmc/s1600/IMG_0811.jpg" width="281"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     Not only does my skin sweat a lot during the summer, it gets dry from being outside so much. I am always looking for a hydrating body wash or nourishing soap for healthy skin. But making my skin feel moisturized is only half the equation in my skin care regimen. I also want something that smells good. Since I am allergic to many floral scents, I am always on the look out for fruit based scents. I jumped at the chance to try the Coconut Water Refreshing Mango Body Wash by Dial. Who can resist the scent of coconut with mango?
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-u02W7ofBG2Q/U9JxQm_M9gI/AAAAAAAAFqs/wQ51c9saG0g/s1600/IMG_0813.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-u02W7ofBG2Q/U9JxQm_M9gI/AAAAAAAAFqs/wQ51c9saG0g/s1600/IMG_0813.jpg" width="233"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      I took the plunge.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      And it was worth it. Not only is Dial a moisturizing body was, it smells good too. I really like that it does't leave my skin dry and itchy like some soaps do. In fact, this is one of the reasons I made the switch from bar soaps to body washes a few years ago. The mango scent smells refreshing and especially suited for the summer months.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      Another thing I like about Dial's Coconut Water Mango Body Wash is the price. For around $5.00, I can get a generous size 16 ounce bottle which makes it perfect for an all around family body wash. I usually hide my body wash and let my children use bar soap but with the price on the Dial, I don't have to hide it anymore.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      After using the body wash, I'm not afraid to hold my arms in the air.
                     </div>

                      <br/>
