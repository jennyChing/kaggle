       <h1 class="entry-title">
        New 2014 Autumn Winter collection from Salvatore Ferragamo
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-08-21T21:33:28+00:00">
         August 21, 2014
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://4theloveofitaly.com/author/cf/" rel="author">
           cf
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://4theloveofitaly.com/fashion/new-2014-autumn-winter-collection-from-salvatore-ferragamo/#comments">
          4 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Salvatore Ferragamo has introduced a
         <strong>
          new line
         </strong>
         of eyeglasses and jewellery for the autumn-winter 2014/15 season. Following the recent trends seen on Italian catwalks, the Salvatore Ferragamo sunglasses feature clear cut and square shapes, with compact and thick underground appeal. The frames are entirely made with coloured variants, Zyl from Red and Blue shades, extended down the glasses, leaving space for the famous Italian brand on the side of the glasses.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2014-15-salvatore-ferragamo-eyewear.png">
          <img alt="Salvatore Ferragamo Eyewear" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1450" height="146" src="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2014-15-salvatore-ferragamo-eyewear-300x146.png" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/italian-sunglasses.png">
          <img alt="Italian sunsglasses for the 2014-2015 season" class="alignnone wp-image-1451" height="145" src="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/italian-sunglasses-300x166.png" width="262"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Meanwhile talking about Italian jewellery Salvatore Ferragamo have introduced two new jewellery items that are part of the Vara Bow product lines, they are a solid bracelet and a beautiful ring in 18 carat pink gold. On the bracelet (priced at around 4,600 Euros in Italy) resembles a Ribbon shape, meanwhile the gold ring (priced at 1,500 Euros again in Italy) duplicates itself in a bow shaoe, in a game of crossroads.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/salvatore-ferragamo-gold-ring.jpg">
          <img alt="salvatore-ferragamo gold ring for 2014 collection" class="alignnone wp-image-1452" height="189" src="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/salvatore-ferragamo-gold-ring-300x226.jpg" width="250"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/salvatore-ferragamo-gold-bracelet.jpg">
          <img alt="salvatore ferragamo gold bracelet for 2014-15 collection" class="alignnone wp-image-1453" height="191" src="http://4theloveofitaly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/salvatore-ferragamo-gold-bracelet-300x229.jpg" width="250"/>
         </a>
        </p>
