           This Son Kept his Childhood Promise and Bought his 57 Year-Old Dad a 57 Chevy
          </h1>

         <div class="post-single-content box mark-links">
          <p>
           When Mike King was a young boy, he promised his stepfather Roger that he would get him a 1957 Chevy Bel-Air for his 57th birthday. Roger had grown up in a poor family with seven children. He always dreamed of buying that car but never thought he could afford it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Take a look a Rogers reaction and you can see what this gesture meant to Roger.
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="337" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/xQLUJfArkrA?modestbranding=1;hd=1&amp;rel=0&amp;autohide=1&amp;showinfo=0" width="600">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           Clearly, Roger was overwhelmed by this gesture by his son. Mike was no rich guy either. He pulled overtime for a couple of months in order to afford this car.
          </p>
          <p>
           Several people wanted to donate to his cause, and he set up a
           <a href="http://www.gofundme.com/9zyha8">
            donation campaign.
           </a>
           Please help this guy by sharing this amazing mans gesture to his father.
          </p>
