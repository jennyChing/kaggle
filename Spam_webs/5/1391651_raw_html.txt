             <p>
              What is better than a snickerdoodle? How about no bake snickerdoodle cookie dough bites! Now we are talking. I already have a
              <a href="http://mywholefoodlife.com/2013/06/05/gluten-free-snickerdoodles-and-a-giveaway/" target="_blank">
               Gluten Free Snickerdoodle
              </a>
              on my blog, so this was only a matter of time.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://mywholefoodlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/No-Bake-Snickerdoodles-My-Whole-Food-Life.jpg">
               <img alt="No Bake Snickerdoodles - My Whole Food Life" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8119" height="1000" src="http://mywholefoodlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/No-Bake-Snickerdoodles-My-Whole-Food-Life.jpg" width="882"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              These can be made in just minutes and are gluten free! Can it get better? My daughter just loves cinnamon. In fact, to get her to drink her milk, I add some cinnamon to it. I call it Meadows Secret Milk and she drinks it right up! So these were right up her alley. I hope you enjoy them as well.
             </p>
