            <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
             <p>
              Now thats a kinglyentrance.
             </p>
             <p>
              The Iron Throne was lowered via crane at the
              <span style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">
               Tower of London for the
               <em>
                Game of Thrones
               </em>
               season 5 premiere. The HBO serieswill have itsworld premierescreening in London on Wednesday night.
              </span>
              <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
               Zai Bennett, director of the U.K. television channelSky Atlantic,
              </span>
              <a href="http://www.digitalspy.com/tv/s151/game-of-thrones/news/a636234/game-of-thrones-iron-throne-arrives-at-tower-of-london-for-world-premiere.html#~p7iSgVGDi9zWLZ" style="line-height: 1.6em;">
               reportedly told viewers
              </a>
              <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
               : Throughout its 1,000-year history, the Tower of London has been an iconic seat of power for English monarchs. As we lower the Iron Throne into the moat, ahead of Sky Atlantics world premiere of
               <em>
                Game of Thrones
               </em>
               , Im proud that we can add our own seat of power to the Towers long and rich history.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Maybe now the Queen of England
              <a href="http://www.ew.com/article/2014/06/24/queen-elizabeth-ii-visits-the-game-of-thrones-set-actual-monarch-doesnt-sit-on-pretend-seat-of-power">
               will finally sit in it
              </a>
              ?
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">
               As
               <em>
                EW
               </em>
               broke yesterday,
               <em>
                Game of Thrones
               </em>
               season 5 will have
               <a href="http://ew.com/article/2015/03/17/game-thrones-showrunners">
                storylines coming together in new and exciting ways
               </a>
               and feature more dragon action thanever berore.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Also:As impressive as the HBOs Iron Throne is (andbecause I never tire of this great illustration)heres how
              <em>
               A Game of Thrones
              </em>
              authorGeorge R.R. Martin originally describedthe Kings Landing seat of power in his books.Aegon the Conqueror had the seat forgedfrom the countlessswords of defeated enemies:
             </p>

             </p>
