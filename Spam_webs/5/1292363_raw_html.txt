        <p>
         Made completely from scratch, this chocolate and vanilla pudding pie is a breeze to make! So rich, yet so light and creamy! My family can not get enough of it!
         <br/>
         <img alt="Made completely from scratch, this chocolate and vanilla pudding pie is a breeze to make! So rich, yet so light and creamy! My family can not get enough of it!" class="aligncenter wp-image-26680 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/roxanashomebaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/chocolate-and-vanilla-pudding-pie-recipe-2.jpg?resize=667%2C1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         As soon as September rolls around, its pie season! Pumpkin pie, apple pie, pecan pie, everywhere you turn a slice a pie is tempting you!
        </p>
        <p>
         Today I bring you pie, a pie thats my familys favorite. A pie that can be made year round and every time you make it, it will be gone in no time! Chocolate and vanilla pudding pie!
        </p>
        <p>
         I love cooking and baking from scratch. But as much as I love it, there are rare days when I reach for a pre-packed-store-bough-convenient food. Although that is happens only a few times a year, there are things I would never ever choosethe pre-package route.
        </p>
        <p>
         One of them is making pudding! Homemade pudding is so easy to make and the results are way, way better than the packaged mix!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-26677">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Made from scratch chocolate and vanilla pudding pie recipe from Roxanashomebaking.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-26770 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/roxanashomebaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/chocolate-and-vanilla-pudding-pie-recipe-31.jpg?resize=667%2C1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This pie requires a couple of steps, but dont let that scare you.
        </p>
        <p>
         First you start with the crust. This time around I went with a made-from-scratch pie crust, but you could buy a pre-baked pie crust oran unbaked pie crustand bake it, or make yourpie crust using my easy recipe. Whatever works best for you
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll need all purpose flour, butter, egg yolks and iced water. Its very important when making apie crust to use cold butter (I keep mine in the freezer for 30 minutes) and very cold water. Another secret to a flaky pie crust is chilling the dough before baking it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once chilled, bake the pie crust blind for 10 minutes and uncovered for 10-12 more minutes, until slightly golden.
         <img alt="Chocolate and vanilla pudding pie recipe " class="aligncenter wp-image-26679 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/roxanashomebaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/chocolate-and-vanilla-pudding-pie-recipe-1.jpg?resize=667%2C1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         While the pie crust is cooling, its time to make the pudding. You start with the same mix for both the chocolate and the vanilla puddings.
        </p>
        <p>
         To make the pudding all is required is a light cooking on the stove. Because of that I highly recommend using pasteurized eggs! Ive mentioned before and will say it again. UsingSafest Choice Pasteurized Eggs gives me peace of mind! Now I can enjoy my half-cooked goodies without the fear of getting sick!
        </p>
        <p>
         Another thing to know aboutSafest Choice Pasteurized Eggs. If youre just starting using them, youll notice at first the egg whites dont whip, no matter how much you try.
        </p>
        <p>
         Let me tell you a little secret. Leave the egg whites for an hour or so at room temperature and theyll whip to firm peaks in no time!
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition to the eggs, youll also need cornstarch, sugar, milk, chocolate, gelatin and vanilla.
        </p>
        <p>
         For the finishing touches, youll need whipped cream! You cant go wrong with loads of whipped cream on top of your pie, right? yum!
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre still craving chocolate after trying this chocolate and vanilla pudding pie, Im sure youll find something else at this years chocolate party to satisfy your desires!
        </p>
        <p>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0">
             Pie crust
            </div>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups (240 grams) Gold Medal Flour all-purpose flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             2/3 cup (150 grams) cold butter, diced
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 Safest Choice Pasteurized Egg Yolks
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             1-2 tablespoons iced water
            </li>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5">
             Pudding
            </div>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 Safest Choice Pasteurized Egg Yolks
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tablespoons Imperial Sugar Extra Fine Granulated Pure Cane Sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tablespoons (16 grams) cornstarch
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 2/3 cups (400 ml) milk
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             5 oz (140 grams) chocolate, chopped
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 envelope gelatin
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 tablespoons water
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 teaspoons vanilla extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 Safest Choice Pasteurized Egg Whites (must be at room temperature)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup Imperial Sugar Extra Fine Granulated Pure Cane Sugar
            </li>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17">
             Topping
            </div>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
             chocolate curls
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0">
             Pie crust
            </div>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Place the flour, butter and egg yolks in a food processor and pulse until the mixture resembles bread crumbs.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Slowly, few drops at a time, add enough water until the mixture forms a soft dough.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes for up to 3-4 days.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Once chilled, roll the dough out on a floured surface and line it on the bottom and sides of a 9" pie crust, preferably with removable bottom.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Heat the oven to 375F.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Prick the pie crust all over with a fork, cover with parchment paper and fill with baking beans.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake the pie crust for 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Remove the baking beans and paper and bake for an additional 10-12 minutes until slightly golden.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cool completely.
            </li>
            <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10">
             Pudding
            </div>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-11" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             While the pie crust is cooling, make the pudding.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-12" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a medium size saucepan, whisk the milk, egg yolks, sugar and cornstarch.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-13" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cook the mixture over low heat until thickened, stirring constantly, approximately 10 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-14" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Divide the pudding into two bowls.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-15" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a heatproof bowl melt the chocolate and stir it into half of the pudding.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-16" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Spread the chocolate pudding in the pie crust, cover with a plastic wrap and refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-17" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Place the 3 tablespoons of cold water in a small bowl and sprinkle with gelatine while whisking with a fork. Set aside for 5 minutes or until spongy.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-18" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Stand the bowl in a heatproof bowl of hot water and stir until the gelatine dissolves. Never boil gelatine, as it can become stringy.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-19" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Stir the gelatin into the reserved pudding along with the vanilla extract.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-20" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a grease-free bowl, whisk the egg whites with the cream of tartar until peaks form. Slowly add in the sugar and continue whisking until firm peaks are formed.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-21" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Fold the egg whites into the vanilla pudding and spoon it over the chocolate pudding layer.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-22" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until set.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-23" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Whip the heavy cream until firm peaks and spread it over the vanilla pudding and decorate with chocolate shavings.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-24" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cut with a sharp knife and enjoy!
            </li>
           </ol>
