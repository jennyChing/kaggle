          CALORIES PER SERVING
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Peanut butter and apple quesadilla:
         </strong>
         8" whole-wheat tortilla + 1 Tbsp peanut butter + 1 sliced apple
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2. Apricot ricotta breakfast sundae:
         </strong>
         1 cup nonfat ricotta cheese + pinch nutmeg +  tsp agave nectar + 4 chopped apricots
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3. 1 egg scrambled
         </strong>
         in 1 tsp oil; 1 whole-wheat bagel thin; 1 turkey sausage link; 6 oz low-sodium vegetable juice
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Strawberry French toast:
         </strong>
         1 slice whole-wheat bread dipped in 1 beaten egg and cooked in 1 tsp butter + 1 cup sliced strawberries
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          1 cup toasted oat cereal
         </strong>
         + 1 cup 1% milk or soy milk + 2 Tbsp tart dried cherries + 1 Tbsp chopped walnuts
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          6. Egg sandwich:
         </strong>
         1 whole-wheat English muffin + 1 egg fried in 1 tsp olive oil + 1 oz Canadian bacon (about 2 slices) + 1-2 slices tomato
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          7. Pancakes and bacon:
         </strong>
         2 frozen pancakes + 1 tsp honey +  sliced banana; 2 slices turkey bacon
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          8. 1 cup bran flakes
         </strong>
         + 1 cup 1% milk or soy milk +  cup blueberries
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          9. Huevos rancheros:
         </strong>
         6" corn tortilla + 3 egg whites fried in 1 tsp olive oil +  cup black beans +  cup fresh salsa +  cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          10.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Sunflower pear waffle:
         </strong>
         1 whole-grain frozen waffle + 1 Tbsp sunflower butter (tastes like sunflower seedsloaded with protein) or any nut butter + 1 sliced pear
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          11.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Maple almond oatmeal:
         </strong>
         1 cup cooked oatmeal + 1 Tbsp almond butter + 2 tsp maple syrup
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          12.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Smoked salmon roll-up:
         </strong>
         8" whole-wheat tortilla + 1 Tbsp whipped cream cheese + 1 oz smoked salmon + 1 sliced scallion
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          13. Smoothie!
         </strong>
         1 cup nonfat plain yogurt blended with 1 cup frozen peaches +  cup calcium-fortified orange juice +  banana + 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed + pinch nutmeg
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          14. Greek yogurt parfait:
         </strong>
         8 oz nonfat plain Greek yogurt + 1 diced nectarine + 1 tsp honey + 3 Tbsp sliced almonds
        </p>
