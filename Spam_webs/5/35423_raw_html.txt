        An important lesson in parenting
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         I saw this yesterday and its actually rather thought-provoking:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://6000.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/wpid-the-magicians-son.jpg">
          <img alt="image" class=" aligncenter" height="587" src="http://6000.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/wpid-the-magicians-son.jpg" title="The-Magicians-Son.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes we need to remember that while we want our kids to grow up in our image, they need to maintain their individuality as well. I was desperate for Alex to become a world-famous footballer  after all, that was my dream as a kid, wasnt it every young boys?  but hes simply not interested. He has other things he wants to do.
         <br/>
         I tried for a while before I finally realised that hes not me, hes him. And thats just great.
        </p>
        <p>
         Fortunately, we never got as far as him having to write it down on a football and kick it to me.
        </p>
