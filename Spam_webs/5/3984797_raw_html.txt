         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Nepali songstress, singer and
           <a href="http://sangeetsansar.com/tag/composer/">
            composer
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://sangeetsansar.com/tag/subani-moktan" target="_blank">
            Subani Moktan
           </a>
           <span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_correct">
            has unveiled
           </span>
           the music
           <a href="http://sangeetsansar.com/tag/video/">
            video
           </a>
           of her
           <span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_correct">
            lastest
           </span>
           single Kati Risauchau, which is composed and written by the singer herself.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cdn.sangeetsansar.com/img/subani-moktan.jpg" target="_blank">
           <img alt="subani moktan" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter wp-image-2808" data-lazy-src="http://cdn.sangeetsansar.com/img/subani-moktan.png" data-lazy-type="image" height="347" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="682"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="subani moktan" class="aligncenter wp-image-2808" height="347" src="http://cdn.sangeetsansar.com/img/subani-moktan.png" width="682"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span id="more-2807">
          </span>
          Kati Risauchau is a simple song with very simple things to say. The song starts with gently driving Maadal and Keyboard parts interweave with the melodic voice of
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/moktan.subani" target="_blank">
           Subani
          </a>
          which clicks you right
          <span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_correct">
           way
          </span>
          and take you to the different world. The Maadal takes the lead throughout the song.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Official
           <a href="http://sangeetsansar.com/videos" target="_blank">
            music video
           </a>
           of song Kina Risauchau by
           <a href="http://sangeetsansar.com/tag/subani-moktan/">
            Subani Moktan
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-src="PGlmcmFtZSBzcmM9Ii8vd3d3LnlvdXR1YmUuY29tL2VtYmVkL0RtdDhDU1QwWTRNIiBoZWlnaHQ9IjQwMCIgd2lkdGg9IjQ2MDAiIGFsbG93ZnVsbHNjcmVlbj0iIiBmcmFtZWJvcmRlcj0iMCI+" data-lazy-type="iframe" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs="/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="400" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/Dmt8CST0Y4M" width="4600">
          </iframe>
         </noscript>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The video is directed by Rosan Lama from
          <a href="http://www.youtube.com/user/KhukuriEntOfficial?feature=watch" target="_blank">
           Khukuri Ent.
          </a>
          <span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_correct">
           and
          </span>
          Sisan Baniya which features Subani as a lead. We can find the cinematography of Rosan Lama and Sisan Baniya and Rosan Lama is behind the editing of this video. The video was shot in the office of Vortex Infosys.
          <span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_noSuggestion GINGER_SOFATWARE_correct">
           Subani
          </span>
          looks stunning in the video as usual and the video perfectly matches with the theme of the song.
         </p>
