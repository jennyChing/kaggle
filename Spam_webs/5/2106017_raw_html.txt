      <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name" title="15 Cool and Decorative Table Lamp Ideas for a Living Room">
       15 Cool and Decorative Table Lamp Ideas for a Living Room
      </h1>
      <div itemprop="articleBody">
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Building a stylish and comfortablehome is all about setting the scene. You start with color, progress with furniture, think about light and then add the details and decorative accessories. The latter two being ever-so-important to the foundation of the vision. From how your windows and light fixtures make the room dance to the way those accessories fit inside a particular space, it all has to come together to fill and transform a room into exactly what you need and want it to be. Today, were showing off 15 cool and decorative table lamp ideas for a living room. Well guide you into finding just the right choice for your home!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="contemporary living room table lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69769" height="462" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/contemporary-living-room-table-lamp.jpeg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        This room is settled in contemporary chicness but built with a masculine and almost untouchable nature with the harsh lines and organic material. This design fits in quite nicely with its soft approach that doesnt overwhelm the natural light source and its sharp, pointed fixture that plays off the rest of the accessories in the room.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="beautiful bloom table lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69754" height="525" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/beautiful-bloom-table-lamp.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        If youre feeling like your living room may need a funky, artistic touch youll need to take a good peek at these geometric beauties. They would fit quite nicely into a black and white scene or compliment a vibrant space with its timeless color combo. Theres two ways to use this one as well, opened and closed, for an even more interesting look.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="Clover industrial table lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69755" height="934" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Clover-industrial-table-lamp.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Who doesnt love an Edison syle? And with this tabletop feature you can bring this trending statement right into your home and off the ceilings. We love the golden tones and intricate nature of the bulb itself. This piece would fit nicely inside a eclectic room or one with a more industrialized overall vision.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="table lamp ideas for a living room" class="aligncenter wp-image-69756" height="525" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Drum-Table-Lamps.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        If youre looking for something simple and sweet, take a peek at this set of black table lamps. They give extra height and interesting to the focal wall of the room but theyre also subtle and slick, not taking away from the bigger features of the room, instead they merely compliment and provide function and balance.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="cool and modern table lamp design" class="aligncenter wp-image-69757" height="643" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/elegant-table-lamp.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        We are completely swooning over these charming pieces. Normally, youd see the most design in the top half of a fixture, or where the light shines  instead these table lamps have been given legs for a surprising and completely delicate bit of style.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="living room lighting ideas" class="aligncenter wp-image-69758" height="467" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/erb-macchia-table-lamp-1024x682.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Although we love the hanging type, today were all about the table version here and this one in particular sure knows how to make a splash in the most subtle of ways. Blending in with its background but still creating a beautiful scene, this bulking, yet elegant lamp was made to accent and delight.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="decorative gold table lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69759" height="720" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/golden-table-lamp-for-living-room.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        This piece looks just like a piece of art, dont you think? Take off the shade and this could be sitting in an art gallery somewhere and wed never know the difference, which is also a reason why we have fallen completely, head-over-heels in love with the design. Its fresh, its beautiful but its also very sophisticated and meant to please throughout a more formal living room.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="Jeeves table lamp design shaped like a hat" class="aligncenter wp-image-69760" height="700" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Jeeves-table-lamp-design.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        For a personalized touch to your living room, add this black, bowler hat. At first glance, it will deceive the eye, but once your guests notice their mistake, itll become a funky little focal and conversational piece! Its perfect for smaller end tables or behind the sofa!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="Leon Table Lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69761" height="951" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Leon-Table-Lamp-753x1024.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Sleek, smooth with a sophisticated and artistic presence, this style is for those rooms with a very contained and precise vision. We believe that a matching set to balance out a sharp-edged sofa would look interesting and cool under a sea of hazy grays, as well. Itll definitely give off a modern yet relaxing ambiance.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="ornate table lamp in living room" class="aligncenter wp-image-69762" height="498" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/modern-table-lamp-for-living-room-1024x728.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        One of the more traditional and homey of choices, this design definitely has an interesting yet old-age spirit happening. Theres texture, bits of color but an otherwise vintage style that cannot be ignored. And we think it looks perfect sitting beside the most comfy, leather couch weve seen in a while.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="orange table lamp shades" class="aligncenter wp-image-69763" height="475" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/orange-table-lamp-shades.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Heres another example of a pair of matching table lamps that give balance to a room. If you have a matching set of end tables or a longer sofa table that needs the accent, make sure you buy two. Again, this is a more traditional style overall, but it fits in well with this cozy, yet lbright living area.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="original design metal table lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69764" height="700" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/original-design-metal-table-lamp.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        And heres another example of a beautiful piece of art and function! Fit for a modern vision, this silver piece was made to be seen and used on a daily basis. We love its feminine essence but we love its chic and refined flair even more so. Now envision this accent in a room filled with neutral, futuristic style.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="small accent cordless table lamps for living room" class="aligncenter wp-image-69765" height="840" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/small-accent-cordless-table-lamps-for-living-room.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Like these pieces for example, although theyd easily fit into a more traditionally and neutrally styled living room, these shape gives it an extra, personalized pop.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="square table lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-69766" height="447" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/square-table-lamp-1024x654.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Another set that is perfect for balancing out the setting but this time with a more zen and feminine appeal. We love the texture and shape of these pieces and we love how they balance and blend into this creamy room. Its definitely a space made for relaxing but its also a space that was made for showing off a bit of personal style too.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="TABLE LAMP IDEAS" class="aligncenter wp-image-69767" height="482" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/TABLE-LAMP-IDEAS-1024x705.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        Youll definitely make your guests talk with this golden piece. So, it may not be the most family-oriented or family-friendly of choices, but if you have a formal living room, no children and looking for something extremely unique  this design may be exactly what youve been looking for all along for a surprising bout of masculine charm and chic styling.
       </p>
