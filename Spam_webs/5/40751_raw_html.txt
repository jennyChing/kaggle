         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://issuesurgery.com/travel-tips-and-how-to-keep-on-a-healthy-track/healthy-young-woman-running-healthy-track/" rel="attachment wp-att-851">
            <img alt="healthy-young-woman-running- Healthy Track" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-851" height="1536" src="http://issuesurgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/healthy-young-woman-running-Healthy-Track.jpg" width="958"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Traveling with the family is sure one way to spend the time together and also bond to be closer with the other family members. But before you sail the ship to bring the family to
           <strong>
            interesting travel destinations
           </strong>
           , you might need to prepare the healthy foods that will keep the kids healthy along the trips. There are many tips that you can follow to make sure that they will be healthy and good, including:
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Make sure that you can bring the kids for a walk around the travel sites after having dinner.
          </p>
          <p>
           This will be especially nice if you go to the natural tourist
           <br/>
           destination, because the view on the night will be very spectacular for the kids. Then, you can also teach them about the natural environment when you are doing this. They will also be able to enjoy the fresh air after having heavy meals on the dinner.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           If you want to make the kids stay healthy along the way to the destination, you should pack a healthy snacks for them. This will include healthy fruits, such as apples or oranges, along with fresh waters and trail mix. Take some rest once in a while to keep the energy of the kids.
          </p>
