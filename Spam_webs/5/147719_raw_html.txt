           <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
            5 makeup mistakes you undoubtedly made
           </h1>
           <div class="meta-info">
            <div class="td-post-author-name">
             By
             <a href="http://www.shefound.com/author/Isabella" itemprop="author">
              Josephine
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="td-post-views">
             <i class="td-icon-views">
             </i>

              1579
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <br/>
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

        <div class="td-pb-span8 td-main-content" role="main">
         <div class="td-ss-main-content">
          <div class="td-post-featured-image">
           <img alt="Makeup mistakes every women made once" class="entry-thumb" height="425" itemprop="image" src="http://www.shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/makeup-and-makeup-removal-681x425.jpg" title="Makeup misstakes" width="681"/>
          </div>
          <div class="td-post-content td-pb-padding-side">
           <p>
            In the name of beauty never make these makeup mistakes.
            <br/>
            <strong>
             1 Too much use of waterproof mascara
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_441" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/shaping-eye-brows" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Don't use waterproof mascara everyday" class="size-full wp-image-441" height="838" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/shaping-eye-brows.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Too much waterproof mascara
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            You really can use waterproof mascara, but not do it every day. This mascara contains generally less ingredients that take care of your lashes, it is difficult to remove with as a result you try to rub it of and take some eyelashes of as well.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             2 Shaping your brows to much
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_442" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/eyebrow-shaping" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Don't shape your eyebrows to small" class="size-full wp-image-442" height="450" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/Eyebrow-Shaping.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Dont overdo your eyebrows
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Be careful when plucking your brows. If you overdo, your hair may not come back. And that sucks. Thick eyebrows are a fashion hit. And tattooed lines above your eyes are totally so uncool. Be careful! otherwise use a template to pluck your brows.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             3 A thick layer of foundation
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_443" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/foundation-makeup" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="A thin layer breathable  foundation is better for the skin" class="size-full wp-image-443" height="600" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/foundation-makeup.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Use a foundation that breaths
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            A thick layer of foundation ensures that your pores get clogged. How do you know you have the wrong foundation? If it feels too thick, then probably it is. Opt for a light foundation or otherwise mix it with your moisturizer. There are also some good foundation mineral powders, which I prefer.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             4 Curling your lashes after the mascara
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_444" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/curling-lashes" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Don't curl your lashes after you applied mascara" class="size-full wp-image-444" height="559" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/curling-lashes.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Curling lashes after mascara
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            If you curl your lashes while you just have done your mascara, and your lashes are still wet they will definitely stick to the curler and if dry you lashes will break easily.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             5 rubbing your eyes with makeup remover
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_445" style="width: 599px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/eye-makeup-remover" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Remove eye makeup carefully to avoid damage" class="size-full wp-image-445" height="712" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/eye-makeup-remover.jpg" width="599"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Remove eye makeup carefully
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Be careful when removing your eye makeup. If you rub too hard it can irritate the skin around your eyes, or a blood vessel in your eye can burst. If you dip a cotton pad in makeup remover, hold it a minute on your eyes so that the makeup can break down
           </p>
           <p>
            If you dont have makeup remover here are
            <strong>
             3 great makeup removers
            </strong>
            you already have at home. A lot of makeup removers cause irritated and dry skin (especially in winter). But! There are at home removers, which are extremely versatile.
            <br/>
            <strong>
             3 awesome multi-taskers
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Olive oil:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_446" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/olive-oil-as-makeup-remover" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Olive oil as Makeup remover" class="size-full wp-image-446" height="459" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/olive-oil-as-makeup-remover.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Olive oil as Makeup remover
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Okay, go to the kitchen and grab the olive oil. Yep, this is really a great alternative to the standard makeup remover. Does not irritate the eyes makes your skin soft and is good for your skin. Just dip a cotton pad onto the olive oil and remove your eye makeup off.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Milk:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_447" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/clover-milk" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Milk as makeup remover is soft for the skin" class="size-full wp-image-447" height="384" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/clover-milk.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Milk as makeup remover
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            That milk is good for a sensitive and dry skin was already known, but it also removes makeup. Soak a cotton pad in milk, take your makeup off and then splash some warm water on your skin.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Baby oil:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_448" style="width: 600px;">
            <a href="http://shefound.com/beauty/skin-care/5-makeup-mistakes-undoubtedly-made/attachment/cleaning-face" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Baby oil as makeup remover" class="size-full wp-image-448" height="600" src="http://shefound.com/wp-content/uploads/cleaning-face.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             Baby oil as makeup remover
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Did you know that baby oil is one of the best miracle remedies is to remove makeup? Put a bit onto a cotton pad and wipe off everything from your face. Yes, even the most intense eye makeup. Some people claim its bad for your skin and clogs pores, this is absolute not true.
           </p>
