         <p>
          Few days ago, we made an article for the first beta version of iOS that has been successfully jailbroken. Today, in this tutorial we gonna teach you on how to jailbroken your
          <em>
           iPhone 4
          </em>
          ,
          <em>
           iPod Touch
          </em>
          ,
          <em>
           iPad
          </em>
          and
          <em>
           3GS devices
          </em>
          using the very known jailbroken tool by the iPhone Dev-Team the
          <strong>
           Redsn0w
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://blog.iphone-dev.org/">
           iPhone Dev-Team
          </a>
          just updated
          <strong>
           for Mac only
          </strong>
          to work with this iOS 5. As of now we can only
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.jeiboy.net/jailbreak-ios-5-using-redsn0w-0-9-8-on-your-iphone-4-ipod-touch-ipad-and-3gs/">
            tethered jailbreak
           </a>
          </strong>
          is possible, it means that you need to connect your device to a computer when activating, the reason is, in order to boot it into a state of jailbroken.
          <br/>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-0" href="http://www.jeiboy.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/ios-51.jpg">
           <img alt="iOS 5" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2065" height="214" src="http://www.jeiboy.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/ios-51-300x214.jpg" title="ios-5" width="300"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Note
          </strong>
          : If carrier unlock user it is not advisable to use Redsn0w, or the default iOS 5 firmware, it will cause  to break your unlock. If you arent, however, just follow these steps;
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Note
          </strong>
          : these are the supported Devices, be sure that your device is in  this list.
         </p>
         <p>
          iPod touch 3G
          <br/>
          iPod touch 4G
          <br/>
          iPad 1
          <br/>
          iPhone3GS
          <br/>
          iPhone4 (GSM)
          <br/>
          iPhone4 (CDMA)
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Download iOS 5 Beta  version
          <a href="http://www.jeiboy.net/download-ios-5-beta-1-for-developers/" target="_blank">
           click here 
          </a>
          thats appropriate for your device. It is advisable  to have  your own developer account with Apple.
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Have an Update or restore your device to iOS 5 using the iTunes 10.5 Beta,
          <a href="http://developer.apple.com/devcenter/ios/index.action" target="_blank">
           Click here 
          </a>
          to download like you would if you werent applying the jailbreak. Theres nothing to worry about because  the Redsn0w will still be able to break into it.
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Now you have  to download the latest version of Redsn0w,
          <a href="https://sites.google.com/a/iphone-dev.com/files/home/redsn0w_mac_0.9.8b1.zip?attredirects=0&amp;d=1">
           click here 
          </a>
          . Point Redsn0w to the
          <strong>
           iOS 5 Beta firmware
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Once youve finished the previous steps, check the 
          <em>
           Install Cydia
          </em>
          , most likely the reason youre jailbreaking your device in the first place, Then Click 
          <em>
           Next
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          5.
          <strong>
           Turn your device off
          </strong>
          and before proceeding leave it plugged in, otherwise the jailbreak will wont work as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          6. In this step there will be an instructions on you on how to enter DFU mode: now press and hold both 
          <em>
           Home
          </em>
           and  the 
          <em>
           Power
          </em>
           buttons for about 7-10 seconds, release the Power button but make sure that the home button is remained press for more than 10 seconds.
         </p>
         <p>
          7. Now its when we say pop out a can of your favorite soda or beer and relax, as  the
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.jeiboy.net/jailbreak-ios-5-using-redsn0w-0-9-8-on-your-iphone-4-ipod-touch-ipad-and-3gs/">
            Redsn0w Jailbreaking tool
           </a>
          </strong>
          will performs its modifications that do  not  require any user intervention. Dont think much for that, as this process will finish you will receive a clear info notification.
         </p>
         <p>
          And thats it! hope you followed the given tutorial above, but if not read this tutorial again or just check your phone and explore, maybe youve missed something.
