        <p>
         Editor Kim Shuck, author of
         <a href="http://www.spdbooks.org/Producte/9780985288389/rabbit-stories.aspx" target="_blank" title="Rabbit Stories by Kim Shuck at Spd Books">
          Rabbit Stories

         <a href="http://www.rabbitandrose.com/05rnr/05current.php" target="_blank" title="Rabbit and Rose Online Poetry Journal">
          Rabbit and Rose Online Poetry Journal
         </a>
         I. Thanks Kim!
        </p>
        <p>
         Im privileged to have my poetry included with poems by John D. Berry, Kim McMillon, Howard Miller, Jeanetta Calhoun Mish, Mary Jean Robertson, Linda Rodriguez and Brianna Lea Pruitt.
