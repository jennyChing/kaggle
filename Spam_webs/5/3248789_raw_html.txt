       <p>
        <strong>
         Imagine How Your Home Would Look and Work in the Future
        </strong>
        <br/>
        We think therell always be a place for a Fantastic Handyman, but the jobs you need him to do will certainly change over the coming years. We take a fun look at some emerging technologies that could change the way we use and maintain our homes.
        <span id="more-68">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Your Secure Home
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Soon, the worry of losing your keys will be a thing of the past. Youll control your home security through your smart phone, and check that you did lock the door any time you want. Youll be able to add friends or family to the list of people whore allowed entry if youre not there, and of course youll be able to make instant changes to your settings any time you want.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_92" style="width: 208px">
        <img alt="http://www.digsdigs.com" class=" wp-image-92 " height="147" src="http://www.fantastichandyman.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/smart-house-300x225.jpg" width="198"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         http://www.digsdigs.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Your Comfortable, Adaptable Home
        </strong>
        <br/>
        The time when you can pre-program your lights for your own personal tastes really isnt that far down the road. Youll pre-program settings for daytime, evenings, a romantic night in, or a party. Youll be able to call on the music you want and have it playing in any room you choose, and therell be at least one way to display images or TV in every room, with the help of OLED technology. Heating in your home will become much more controllable, with different temperature zones for different rooms or different settings for individual family members.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Your Home, Which Keeps You Safe
        </strong>
        <br/>
        As your house becomes more wired in and more interactive there will also be technologies for those with specific physical challenges or vulnerabilities. Audio cues for those with sight problems to help them move around their home. Visual cues for the hard of hearing. Elderly people who live alone will be able to call for help from any place they need to, not stare in frustration at the button they cant reach.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_91" style="width: 170px">
        <img alt="http://www.ianybox.com" class=" wp-image-91" height="123" src="http://www.fantastichandyman.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/led-lighting-in-house1-300x232.jpg" width="160"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         http://www.ianybox.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Your Greener Home
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        LED lights that use far less energy to start with, and automatic switch off if you forget about them when you leave the room are just the start. Super insulative materials for your walls, roof and boiler are on their way. Your home will monitor your energy consumption and will give you suggestions about how you can cut back usage without loosing comfort.
        <br/>
        Despite early teething problems with integrating home produced power into national grids, as the grids become more decentralised and intelligent, producing your own electricity from renewable sources and selling back your excess will be an ever more practical option.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Our Exciting Future
        </strong>
        <br/>
        Theres so much talk of world problems to be solved that its easy to forget what great technologies are out three just waiting to be that little bit more developed. At Fantastic Handyman well be keeping up with new developments and well be ready to help you incorporate them into your home as they become tested and affordable. Take a quick look around your home today, and ask yourself how much there is that your parents only dreamed of, and that your grandparents could never have imagined. The future has its challenges, of course, but theres good stuff to come and were looking forward to it!
       </p>
