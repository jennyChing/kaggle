         <p>
          Thanks for checking out another episode of Its a Guy Thing Podcast! This week, Mike brings up a favorite topic for all movie lovers, sequels. We discuss our favorites, our least favorites, and those that never should have been made. Amazingly enough, our top favorites are also those listed as Rolling Stones top 5! So dont subscribe to them, you can get all of your news from us!
         </p>
         <p>
          Rick brings up a topic that wouldnt normally be considered a guy thing, Musicals. The guys talk about Little Shop of Horrors, Les Miserables, and the Disney classics we all grew up with.
         </p>
         <p>
          Finally, Mario brings up the Alamo City Comic Con from October of last year and gets Rick to share some stories about some of the stars that were there. The guys also discuss how comic conventions have changed over the years and a little of whats in store for next years con!
         </p>
         <p>
          As always, if you have any questions, comments, concerns, or blather, please include them in the comments section below, email us at itsaguythingpodcast@gmail.com, or call our Google Voicemail at 210-802-1197.
