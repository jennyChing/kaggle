             <h1 class="node-title">
              Meeting Jason Dill!
             </h1>
            </div>
            <div class="content">
             <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even">
                <p>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   portraits:
                   <a href="http://curtisbuchananphoto.tumblr.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                    Curtis Buchanan
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   Interview: Benjamin Deberdt
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </p>
                <h3>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   "Nothing's shocking"
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <img alt="" src="/sites/liveskateboardmedia.com/files/field/image/JDILL_1_750.jpg"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  In between starting his board company with Anthony Van Engelen, the soon to be released
                  <em>
                   "cherry"
                  </em>
                  the Supreme video that seems to regroup a good part of "the F.A. Kids" as everybody calls the younger part of his team, and his radical art direction, Dill is the center of many a discussion these days, so sending a few questions his way made sense, plus you know he is not the kind to tick over quick bland answers.
                  <br/>
                  Proof here!
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  One song that sums up your mood, right now
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <img alt="" src="/sites/liveskateboardmedia.com/files/field/image/dill_musicmood_replacement.jpg"/>
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  One song that could work as a Fucking Awesome anthem?
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <img alt="" src="/sites/liveskateboardmedia.com/files/field/image/fa_themesong_replacement.jpg"/>
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  You and AVE were really proud of being part of the Workshop Was starting your own thing a spur of the moment reaction, or the result of a long reflection?
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="" src="/sites/liveskateboardmedia.com/files/field/image/quittingaws_500.jpg"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  What inspires you, on a daily basis?
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="" src="/sites/liveskateboardmedia.com/files/field/image/weedwomen_500.jpg"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  How does that transfer to having ideas for F.A.?
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
