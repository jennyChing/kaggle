         <p>
          Top in the list is the Mexican-America comedian; Louis C.K who was born on September 12, 1967 in Washington D.C. Louise is a writer and a director. Some of his works include; Louie, Late Night with Conan OBrien, Down to Earth and Louis C.K: Oh My God. He has won several accolades and has been nominated on several others.

           Chappelles show

          which is a sketch comedy television series. He began his career at a tender age of 14 year! In 2005, his show;
          <em>
           Chappelles Show
          </em>

          <em>
