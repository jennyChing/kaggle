      <h1>
       Earth Power Spell
      </h1>
      <p>
       Earth Power Spell - This power spell is used to
          harness the power of the Earth and allows you to carry it
          with you. This spell can be used to insert power in any
          situation or for any purpose. The spell is worked in
          harmony with the cycle of the moon and may take several
          attempts to complete.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        You will need:
       </strong>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Two beeswax candles
       <br/>
       Two small stones
       <br/>
       A small amount of soil (about  cup)
       <br/>
       A piece of white cloth (a handkerchief will do)
       <br/>
       A small bowl
       <br/>
       About one cup of river or spring water
       <br/>
       Three seeds (vegetable, flower, etc  any seeds
          will do)
      </p>
      <p>
       Light both of the beeswax candles and place them on a
          flat surface, such as a tabletop. Place the bowl between
          them.
      </p>
      <p>
       Put some soil in the bottom of the bowl about one inch
          deep. Next, pour water into the bowl so that the bowl is
          about half full. Place both stones in the bowl of water.
          Rotate the bowl, with the stones in it, one full turn.
          Extinguish the candles.
      </p>
      <p>
       Move the bowl to a location where it will be exposed
          to the light of the moon for a full cycle of the moon,
          from waxing to waning. Do not disturb the bowl and do not
          agitate the water.
      </p>
      <p>
       Once the moon has completed her cycle, remove the
          stones from the water and lay them on the white cloth to
          dry thoroughly.
      </p>
      <p>
       Drain the water from the bowl and move the bowl to a
          sunny spot, not too sunny, but where it will receive an
          ample amount of light. Allow the bowl to sit there for
          two days. On the third day, light the beeswax candles
          again and place one on either side of the bowl. Plant the
          seeds in the soil at the bottom of the bowl and then
          immediately extinguish the candles.
      </p>
      <p>
       Wrap the stones in the white cloth and lay them next
          to the bowl. Wait 27 days, adding one teaspoon of water
          per day to the soil. If a plant sprouts in the bowl, the
          spell has taken hold. If no plant has sprouted, work the
          spell again.
      </p>
      <p>
       Once the spell has taken hold, carry the stones with
          you and hold them in your right hand when power is
          needed.
      </p>
