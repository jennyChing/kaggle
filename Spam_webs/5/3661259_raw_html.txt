        <h1 class="entry-title">
         10 Signs Youre a Cat Lady
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://shenkitup.com/author/about-shenkitup/" rel="author" title="Nate Shenk">
            Nate Shenk
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="date published time" title="2011-06-21T19:45:56+00:00">
          June 21, 2011
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/2011/06/21/10-signs-youre-a-cat-lady/#comments">
           16 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="categories">
          Filed Under:
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/category/culture/" rel="category tag">
           Culture
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           <a href="http://shenkitup.com/2011/06/21/10-signs-youre-a-cat-lady/the-crazy-cat-lady1/" rel="nofollow">
            <img alt="the crazy cat lady" height="275" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/the-crazy-cat-lady11-312x383.jpg" style="border: 0pt none; float: right; padding-left: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px;" title="Cat Lady" width="225"/>
           </a>
           There are few evergreen topics that almost everyone can either identify with, or laugh at and the
           <a href="http://shenkitup.com/2010/10/06/a-day-with-andie/" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             subject of cats
            </strong>
           </a>
           is one of those topics.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           Cats are weird and unpredictable, but whats scarier are their obsessed mastersespecially when they have all the characteristics of a (
           <a href="http://shenkitup.com/2012/08/12/interesting-mental-disorders-being-lazy/" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             mental disorder)
            </strong>
           </a>
           <b>
            cat lady
           </b>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           If you, or someone you know, can answer yes to one, or more, of these signs; its not too late, theres still hope. Please, get help  youre probably a
           <i>
            cat lady
           </i>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           Here are 10 signs youre a
           <u>
            cat lady
           </u>
           , or well on your way to being one.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           1. If each of your cats has their own Facebook page.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-freaking-out.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="cat freaking out cat lady own facebook" class="wp-image-7352 aligncenter" height="333" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-freaking-out.jpg" title="cat freaking out" width="372"/>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Youre definitely a crazy cat lady if you update it regularly and never forget to write on each of their walls at least once a day, just in case they look; youre a cat lady.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If you communicate [on Facebook] more with your cats than actual human friends, its pretty safe to say youre social life is in shambles and youre a cat lady.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           2. Youre definitely a cat lady if you watch, and record, every episode of 
           <a href="http://animal.discovery.com/tv-shows/cats-101" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Cats 101
           </a>
            on Animal Planet.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/crazy-cat-lady1.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="crazy cat lady cats 101" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11393" height="273" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/crazy-cat-lady1.jpg" title="crazy cat lady" width="411"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If something prevents that (say the power goes out), you have a nervous breakdown, jump in your car and drive to the nearest hotel with cable, not satellite.
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Better make sure its pet friendly.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           3. You dont let the FedEx guy leave until hes met, and said hello to each of your seven babies; yous a scary cat lady.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/crazy-cat-lady-twilight-zone.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="crazy cat twilight zone" class="wp-image-11394 aligncenter" height="360" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/crazy-cat-lady-twilight-zone-281x450.jpg" title="crazy cat lady twilight zone" width="225"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Have you noticed the FedEx delivery guy acting oddly the past few months?
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Does he jump out of his delivery truck, sprint to your front door, toss the package and run back to his truck as if he just tossed a grenade at the front of the enemys barracks?
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If so, its most likely due to the fact that you
            <a href="http://shenkitup.com/2011/11/27/10-things-you-didnt-know-about-animals/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              completely creep him
             </strong>
            </a>
            out and cant bear to be caught in your cat twilight zone for fear of never returning.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           4. You believe that, when a cat purrs, an angel gets its wings.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-discrimination.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="cat discrimination" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7353" height="376" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-discrimination.jpg" title="cat discrimination" width="394"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If the sounds of playful meows and the feel of purr-induced vibrations makes you giddy; youre not only a cat lady, but a crazy cat lady.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            The only hope for you is shock therapy and a few hours of waterboarding. But even then the chances of recovery arent looking good.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           5. Its Friday night and youre not going out dancing, getting drinks with friends, or doing anything considered a social norm.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-playing-in-litter-box.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="cat playing in litter box" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7354" height="446" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-playing-in-litter-box.jpg" title="cat playing in litter box" width="346"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Nope, youre staying in
            <a href="http://theoatmeal.com/comics/cat_know" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              with your pets
             </strong>
            </a>
            and celebrating Mittens 8th birthday.
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            You most likely helped Whiskers and CoCo find the perfect gift for Mittens, but we wont tell, buying gifts can be a difficult thing!
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           Hopefully Mittens doesnt find out what his gifts are, or youll have to do what you did last year and take them all to PetCo for treats.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           6. You dream of cats.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/this-doesnt-even-make-sense.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="cat lady doesn't even make sense" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-7355" height="440" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/this-doesnt-even-make-sense-650x469.jpg" title="this doesn't even make sense" width="610"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If youve been taking a foreign language for years, say
            <a href="http://shenkitup.com/2011/09/26/germans-are-awesome/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              German
             </strong>
            </a>
            , and all of a sudden one morning you wake up and realize youre dreams were mostly in German, then thats a good thing.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            It means all your years of practicing has become so normal that your brain can jump from English to German, even when sleeping. Well, if you replace the German with cats, its a whole new litter box.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If you dream that everyone else, such as employees at your local grocery store, are cats that do the same duties as humans; its time to wake up and smell the catnip.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Its bad enough youre a cat lady during the day and now that the felines have infiltrated your brain, you dont have much time left.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: x-large; font-family: helvetica;">
           7. You take pictures of every angle of your cat.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/taking-a-pic-of-every-cat.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="crazy cat lady taking pictures" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11397" height="313" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/taking-a-pic-of-every-cat.jpg" title="taking a pic of every cat" width="423"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           If you spend entire afternoons pampering your cat, only to spend the entire evening hosting your very own cat-photo shootyoure a crazy photo-loving cat lady.
           <br/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           You probably, not only need Dr. Phil, but Oprah as well. Leave the catwalks and photo shoots to the professionals and models.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           8. You only hang out with cat-approved friends and refuse to even speak with people who dont donate at least fifty dollars every time they see an ASPCA commercial.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-prison.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="cat lady with a knife" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7356" height="282" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/cat-prison.jpg" title="cat-prison" width="425"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            If you couldnt afford a litter box, youd want someone to help you outwouldnt you?
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Ghetto kitty with a knife will find you.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: x-large;">
           9. When youve had a busy day of herding stray cats to add to the collection in your escape-proof fenced backyard, you decide to relax with a bottle of wine.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/czy-cat-ady-secet.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="czy cat ady secet" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11088" height="300" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/czy-cat-ady-secet.jpg" title="czy cat lady secet" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            But you could never relax until you make sure your cats are relaxed as well.
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Which you accomplish by pouring each of them a tall glass of organic goats milk (not too hot, but not too cold either).
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: large;">
           <span style="font-size: x-large;">
            10. You include your love for cats in every aspect of your online dating profile.
           </span>
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/crazy-cat-lady-collage.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="crazy cat lady collage" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11398" height="449" src="http://shenkitup.com/wp-content/uploads/crazy-cat-lady-collage.jpg" title="crazy cat lady collage" width="558"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            You do this to make sure that whoever is interested in you is completely aware that you have a passion for cats.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           But thinking logically, youll probably be single for the rest of your life so your time will most likely be better spent on Whiskers
           <a href="http://www.facebook.com/shenkitup" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             Facebook page
            </strong>
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           Dealing with, and combating PTCLD (Post Traumatic Cat-Lady Disorder) can be challenging, seemingly impossible and even scary, but the earlier you seek help, the better chance youll have at overcoming this serious psychological disorder.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           If youre an in-denial cat lady, or see nothing wrong with the fact that you, yourself, are considered a cat lady, theres no hope for you and should expect to live the rest of your days amongst litter boxes and more cats than both state and federal law permits.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: helvetica;">
           But hey, if that makes you happen; go for it. Lifes short and theres no sense in doing something so unnatural like spending time with humans.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: helvetica;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Youve probably already seen some of these, but heres a couple cat videos to paw at.
