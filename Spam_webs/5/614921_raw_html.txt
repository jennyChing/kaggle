      </h2>

      <h2 class="news-title">
       Its a Doddl
      </h2>
      <article class="post-3838 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-latest-work category-uncategorized tag-cutlery tag-design tag-doddl tag-logo tag-stationery" id="post-3838">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/doddl-1.jpg" rel="lightbox[3838]" title="It's a Doddl">
         <img alt="dodo design, doddl branding" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3840" height="360" src="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/doddl-1.jpg" width="536"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our latest work has seen us get our hands around the brand design for a new range of childrens cutlery.
       </p>
       <p>
        The uniquely designed cutlery set aims to make mealtimes easier for young children and is now starting to make real waves in the childrens market; sharing the main prize at the Women With Bright Ideas Awards and has been shortlisted for The Gadget Show Live Innovation 2015.
       </p>
       <p>
        Before all this, however, when Catherine and Rosieinitially approached us, the product was only in its very early stages.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/doddl-3.jpg" rel="lightbox[3838]" title="It's a Doddl">
         <img alt="dodo design, doddl branding " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3842" height="360" src="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/doddl-3.jpg" width="536"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The initialwork wecarried out for them was a brand name generation and strapline. Having put forward a shortlist, they settled on Doddl, a name which combined the clients surname (Dodd) witha word we associate with easy  doddle. We felt that its unique, playful spelling along with the strap line really helped to encapsulate what the cutlery was all about  making the process of eating at a young agesimpler  but stillallow for products to be added in the future.
       </p>
       <p>
        The brand identity we created draws heavily on the tactile elements of the product, but remains something that will appeal to both children and the adults making the purchase. The vibrant green asterix, suggestive of a splat of food, stands out on the purple background and is in-keeping with the playfulness of the logo.
       </p>
       <p>
        The identity was rolled out across stationery items and we are currently working on the packaging for thethree products (knife, fork and spoon).
        <a href="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/doddl-2b.jpg" rel="lightbox[3838]" title="It's a Doddl">
         <img alt="design, logo, branding doddl" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3848" height="360" src="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/doddl-2b.jpg" width="536"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        If initial reaction to the product is anything to go by, make sure you watch the space between your childs hands, because before long, there could be a
        <a href="http://doddl.com">
         Doddl product there
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        We can help bring your ideas to market and would love to hear from you,
        <a href="http://www.hillsdesign.co.uk/contact/" target="_blank">
         get in touch with us for a free consultation
        </a>
        .
       </p>
