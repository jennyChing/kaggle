           <p>
            What if you could have braces without looking like you have braces? Well you can, with
            <a href="http://dawsondental.ca/cosmetic-dentistry/invisalign-invisible-braces/">
             Invisalign Invisible Braces
            </a>
            . Its almost like there are no braces there at all. Thats why so many celebrities and famous people opt for Invisalign braces to create flashbulb-worthy smiles. So even when theyre posing on the red carpet, their clear plastic aligners are working hard to straighten their teeth  and nobody even notices!
           </p>
           <h2>
            <b>
             Check Out These Celebs, Who Have Perfected Their Teeth With Invisalign Invisible Braces
            </b>
           </h2>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3 style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
             <strong>
              Khloe Kardashian
             </strong>
            </span>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <a href="http://i1.wp.com/dawsondental.ca/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Invisalign-Invisible-Braces-khloe-kardashian.jpg">
             <img alt="Invisalign Invisible Braces Khloe Kardashian" class="size-medium wp-image-5981 alignleft" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/dawsondental.ca/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Invisalign-Invisible-Braces-khloe-kardashian.jpg?resize=230%2C230"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            To keep up with the Kardashians, Khloe turned to Invisalign. Her teeth were pretty straight already, but she wanted them to be perfect. With Invisalign express and a few trays, her smile was just the way she wanted it.
           </p>
           <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           </h3>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3 style="text-align: left;">
            <strong>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              Justin Bieber
             </span>
            </strong>
           </h3>
