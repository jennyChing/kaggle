                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       It's kind of crazy to think that it could all get purchased in the next few weeks. It's more insane to think that it could all get consumed. But really I bet there will be a lot of waste that happens also. I wish I could find some stats on this.... hold on let me go find some!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Ok I'm back!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Check out what I found.....
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <iframe height="360" scrolling="no" src="http://abcnews.go.com/video/embed?id=20676648" style="border: none;" width="640">
                      </iframe>
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <a href="http://abcnews.go.com/video">
                       More ABC news videos
                      </a>
                      |
                      <a href="http://abcnews.go.com/entertainment">
                       ABC Entertainment News
                      </a>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      On a CTV new Article i read that
                      <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                       Costumes were expected to account for $1.2 billion dollars out of the $6.9 billion spent on halloween last year.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                       It also noted that
                      </span>
                      <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                       Americans will spend $1.9 billion on it this year,
                      </span>
                      <a href="http://www.nielsen.com/us/en/newswire/2009/u-s-consumers-say-boo-to-store-brand-candy-on-halloween.html" style="background-color: white; color: #2e538f; line-height: 20px; margin: 0px; outline: none; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none;" target="_blank">
                       according to The Nielsen Company
                      </a>
                      <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                       .Thats around 600 million pounds worth of Hershey bars, lollipops, Milk Duds, Twizzlers and Clark Bars.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Crazy!!
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="background-color: white; color: #333333; line-height: 20px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Ok so with that out of my system I can move on.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       As a child I loved this holiday because of the fun of going door to door seeing how much you could collect then sorting through it all and trading with your friends and sister... But seeing this mound of junk makes me think about how this holiday is so much about consumerism and is fed by the food production industry. It's really sad that we see boxes upon boxes of unhealthyover processed food stuff when instead there really could be healthy pre packaged foods like dried fruit, nuts, healthy cookies and the like.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        <a href="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-Wsxm2-N1fhE/VDbc_olBzUI/AAAAAAAABXU/-HyL8udJcKE/s640/blogger-image-885469020.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="320" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-Wsxm2-N1fhE/VDbc_olBzUI/AAAAAAAABXU/-HyL8udJcKE/s320/blogger-image-885469020.jpg" width="320"/>
                        </a>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       In our house the
                       <b>
                        Switch Witch
                       </b>
                       comes to collect Asher's Candy.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="color: orange; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         How it works
                        </b>
                       </u>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Asher still enjoys every aspect of Halloween including having a few treats. But before he heads to bed he selects a few treats to keep and the rest he puts out for the switch witch who exchanges his treats for a toy, clothes or crafts.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       When he wakes up the next morning his new toy is in place of the treats.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin: 0px;">
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       He actually brought this up the other day and was excited about what the switch which might bring him. I think that doing this provides a nice balance between enjoying the holiday and having a loot bag of treats hanging around for weeks.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="color: orange;">
                       <b>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         How can you get the switch witch poem for yourself?
                        </span>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       You can simply
                       <a href="mailto:couponpercival@gmail.com">
                        click here
                       </a>
                       to send me your name and email address and I will email you a copy of the switch witch document that I use!
                      </span>

                         I like to print off the switch witch poem a day before halloween and put it into an envelope in our mail box.
                        </span>
                        <br/>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         The second sheet which has just the photo of the switch witch on it, I will cut out and glue to the front of the envelope.
                        </span>
                        <br/>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         Put the letter in the mail box for your child and they are sure to be excited!

                         <br/>
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         This is such a great way to reduce theamount of candy your kiddos actually eat but still allows for the fun of halloween!
