   <h1>
    Comparing dogs, men and women
   </h1>
   <h2>
    1. How Dogs and Men Are the Same
   </h2>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Both take up too much space on the bed.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both have irrational fears about vacuum cleaning.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both are threatened by their own kind.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both mark their territory.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both are bad at asking you questions.
    </li>
    <li>
     Neither tells you what's bothering them.
    </li>
    <li>
     The smaller ones tend to be more nervous.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both have an inordinate fascination with women's crotches.
    </li>
    <li>
     Neither does any dishes.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both fart shamelessly.
    </li>
    <li>
     Neither of them notice when you get your hair cut.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both like dominance games.
    </li>
    <li>
     Both are suspicious of the postman.
    </li>
    <li>
     Neither knows how to talk on the telephone.
    </li>
    <li>
     Neither understands what you see in cats.
    </li>
   </ul>
   <h2>
    2. How Dogs Are Better Than Men
   </h2>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Dogs do not have problems expressing affection in public.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs miss you when you're gone.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs feel guilt when they've done something wrong.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't criticize your friends.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs admit when they're jealous.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs are very direct about wanting to go out.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't laugh at how you throw).
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't feel threatened by your intelligence.
    </li>
    <li>
     You can train a dog.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs are easy to buy for.
    </li>
    <li>
     You are never suspicious of your dog's dreams.
    </li>
    <li>
     The worst social disease you can get from dogs is fleas. (OK, the *really*	worst disease you can get from them is rabies, but there's a vaccine for
	it, and you get to kill the one that gives it to you.)
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs understand what no means.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs understand if some of their friends cannot come inside.
    </li>
    <li>
     Middle-aged dogs don't feel the need to abandon you for a younger owner.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs admit it when they're lost.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs are color blind.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs aren't threatened if you earn more than they do.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs mean it when they kiss you.
    </li>
   </ul>
   <h2>
    3. Where Dogs Fall Down
   </h2>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Men only have two feet that track in mud.
    </li>
    <li>
     Men can buy you presents.
    </li>
    <li>
     Men don't have to play with every man they see when you take them around the block.
    </li>
    <li>
     Men are a little bit more subtle.
    </li>
    <li>
     Men don't eat turds on the sly.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs have dog breath all the time.
    </li>
    <li>
     Men can do math stuff. But then, who really needs a man to do math?
    </li>
    <li>
     Men don't shed as much, and if they do, they hide it.
    </li>
    <li>
     It's fun to dry off a wet man.
    </li>
   </ul>
   <p>
    And the reverse-----
   </p>
   <h2>
    WHY DOGS ARE BETTER THAN WOMEN.....
   </h2>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Dogs don't ask what are you thinking.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't cry.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs love it when your friends come over.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't care if you use their shampoo.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs think you sing great.
    </li>
    <li>
     A dog's time in the bathroom is confined to a quick drink.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't expect you to call when you are running late.
    </li>
    <li>
     "The later you are, the more excited dogs are to see you"
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs will forgive you for playing with other dogs.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't notice if you call them by another dog's name.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs are excited by rough play.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't mind if you give their offspring away.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs love red meat.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs can appreciate excessive body hair.
    </li>
    <li>
     Anyone can get a good-looking dog.
    </li>
    <li>
     "If a dog is gorgeous, other dogs don't hate it."
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs don't shop.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs like it when you leave lots of things on the floor.
    </li>
    <li>
     A dog's disposition stays the same all month long.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs never need to examine the relationship.
    </li>
    <li>
     A dog's parents never visit.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs love long car trips.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs understand that instincts are better than asking for directions.
    </li>
    <li>
     Dogs understand that all animals smaller than dogs were made to be hunted.
    </li>
   </ul>
