     <h1>
      Starbucks Mocha-Colored Washrooms Refreshing or Horrifying?
     </h1>
     <div class="cx-breadcrumbs">
     </div>

      <article class="post-1585 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-uncategorized tag-asheville" id="post-1585">
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1586" style="width: 778px">
         <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/2015-01-05-07.26.37.jpg">
          <img alt="FishEye women's public toilet  Starbucks Asheville." class="wp-image-1586 size-large" height="1024" src="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/2015-01-05-07.26.37-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Fish-eye photo of the womens room at Starbucks in Asheville.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Do you remember when the Starbucks on Charlotte Street was first built? There was controversy surrounding the opening, and a few bricks were thrown through the windows. Well, weve avoided it ever since. But not just because of the controversy its more an issue of location. It is inconvenient. For one thing, theres the busy morning traffic to contend with. Besides, one block up you can get a local cuppa at
         <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/2014/11/city-bakery-north-asheville-location/">
          City Bakery
         </a>
         , which is situated at a convenient traffic light. However, at Stallmans, we persist in traveling the hard road. On our recent visit to Starbucks, we found the womens single stall restroom nice enough, although not particularly inspired. If the java is too much of a jolt, the sober mocha colors of the restroom should help. The room is dimly lit by an odd-looking sconce above the mirror. It looks like an
         <a href="http://www.ikea.com/us/en/catalog/products/90155040/">
          Ikea
         </a>
         knock-off. A poster of frothy milk being poured into a coffee cup does not show off any
         <a href="http://designbeep.com/2010/04/07/40-mindblowing-latte-art-designs/">
          latte art
         </a>
         skills, but it does remind you that at the price youre paying for coffee. You deserve to get exactly the pour you want, right? Decor = 4
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, it turns out that the Charlotte Street Starbucks isnt such a less traveled path. On our recent visit there were plenty of people stopping in for a morning caffeine fix, but the womens room did not suffer from the high traffic. The cleanliness was appreciated. So we can safely say, not horrifying. Cleanliness = 7
        </p>
        <p>
         Baby-changing station: check. Theres not much else, here, however. There is a hook to hang a bag on, but we would like to have seen a table to set a drink onto. (Are we the only ones who prefer to take our cup in with us as opposed to leaving it unattended?) There is a distinct aroma from an air freshener, which we found neither pleasant nor unpleasant. Maybe well come back for a
         <span class="st">
          some vanilla bullshit latte thing
         </span>
         when traffic eases up. Amenities = 3
        </p>
        <p>
         Total = 14
        </p>

         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Odd lighting sconce at Starbucks in Asheville.
         </p>
