           <p>
            <strong>
             Apple iPhone 6 Plus: A8 + M8
            </strong>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Apple iPhone 6 Plus and
            <strong>
             <a class="bbc_url" href="http://www.spemall.com/HUAWEI-Ascend-Mate7-Smartphone-4G-LTE-Hisilicon-Kirin-925-Android-4-4-OS-Octa-Core-6-0-Inch-1920-x-1080-pixels-FHD-IPS-Screen-OTG-NFC-Dual-Cameras-3GB-32GB_g.html" title="">
              Huawei Ascend Mate7
             </a>
            </strong>
            processor are relatively some alternative. Apple iPhone 6 Plus processors is the same with Apple iPhone 6, it is Apple A8, compared to the last generation of Apple A7 processor, Apple A8 is still based on 64-bit architecture, its use 20 nanometers (A7 is 28 nm) process technology, the whole chip condensed the more than 2 billion transistors (A7 is 1 billion), but the size is smaller 13% than Apple A7 processor. In terms of performance, Apple A8 processors increases as much as 50% than A7.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span rel="lightbox">
             <img alt="15272803474_80907efed2_o.jpg" class="bbc_img" src="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8667/15272803474_80907efed2_o.jpg"/>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Apple iPhone 6 Plus is equipped with A8 (image from the verge)
            <br/>
            <br/>
            In addition, Apple A8 built-in the brand new video encoder and image signal processor, it can provide support for advanced camera and video functions, such as the new Focus Pixels, optimization of the facial recognition function, continuous automatic Focus and strengthen the noise reduction function, and so on. In addition, Apple company also introduced a new Metal technology, this technology designed to let the CPU and GPU to cooperate and optimized with each other, so as to present the image detail and complex visual effects, developers use this technology for the iPhone to produce higher fidelity host style game.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span rel="lightbox">
             <img alt="15707572738_ab042207c5_o.jpg" class="bbc_img" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7499/15707572738_ab042207c5_o.jpg"/>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            It is also equipped with the M8 movement coprocessor (image from the verge)
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Apple iPhone 6 Plus also have a M8 movement coprocessor, when you are in a state of movement, the M8 movement coprocessor will continue to record from the accelerometer, compass, gyroscope, and a new barometer of data, this split the workload of the A8, so as to improve the efficiency and extend the standby time.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span rel="lightbox">
             <img alt="15894418812_01fc8f868b_o.jpg" class="bbc_img" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7505/15894418812_01fc8f868b_o.jpg"/>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            A8 performance have 50 times of ascension than the first generation of the iPhone
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Huawei Ascend Mate7: Haisi kirin K925 + Micro processor
            </strong>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Huawei Ascend Mate7 is equipped Huawei Haisi kirin K925 processor, this is a based on ARM. Big LITTLE architecture design octa core processor, it has four 1.8 GHz core Cortex-A15 and four 1.3 GHz Cortex-A7 core. The Haisi kirin K925 processor used Global Task Scheduling, it is Big. LITTLE the most advanced work model, in this model driven, this eight core work at will and tie-in combination, so as to realize the effectiveness of more flexible and efficient performance.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span rel="lightbox">
             <img alt="15709302987_a5ce260637_o.jpg" class="bbc_img" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7463/15709302987_a5ce260637_o.jpg"/>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Huawei Ascend Mate7 is using Haisi kirin K925 octa core processor (image from Twitter)
            <br/>
            <br/>
            On the GPU, Haisi kirin K925 processor directly adopts ARM Mali-T624 graphics processing unit, it adopts innovation three pipeline architecture, it built based on using for 2D and 3D graphics quality scalable multicore solution, it support the main API, including OpenGL ES 1.1, OpenGL ES 2.0 and OpenGL ES 3.0, DirectX 11 and OpenCL 1.1. In addition, its production line in the hardware provides real IEEE double-precision floating-point math to support Full Profile and Embedded Profile and OpenCL.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span rel="lightbox">
             <img alt="15275430873_73c7634336_b.jpg" class="bbc_img" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7551/15275430873_73c7634336_b.jpg"/>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Haisi kirin K925 integrated ARM Mali-T624 graphics processing units (image from the ARM)
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span rel="lightbox">
             <img alt="15709040399_a3a2efdf5a_o.png" class="bbc_img" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7583/15709040399_a3a2efdf5a_o.png"/>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Of course, besides Haisi kirin K925 processor,
            <strong>
             <a class="bbc_url" href="http://www.spemall.com/HUAWEI-Ascend-Mate7-Smartphone-4G-LTE-Hisilicon-Kirin-925-Android-4-4-OS-Octa-Core-6-0-Inch-1920-x-1080-pixels-FHD-IPS-Screen-OTG-NFC-Dual-Cameras-3GB-32GB_g.html" title="">
              Huawei Ascend Mate7 Smartphone
             </a>
            </strong>
            is also equipped with a micro wisdom core processor, its function is similar to Apple's coprocessor M8 movement, its power consumption is lower than the Cortex-A7 core, it can be used to collect the measured data of gyroscope, sensors, etc, thus reducing haisi kirin K925 processor power consumption, which achieve the purpose to reduce the power consumption of the whole machine.
