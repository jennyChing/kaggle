         <div class="entry-content large-11 large-centered columns" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           Being best pals isnt all friendship bracelets and rainbows. You love each other like sisterswhich means you also fight like siblings, too.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://elitedaily.com/women/girl-code-rules-that-every-twenty-something-woman-lives-by/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://elitedaily.com/women/girl-code-rules-that-every-twenty-something-woman-lives-by/', 'When the rules of Girl Code');" target="_blank">
            When the rules of Girl Code
           </a>
           are broken, theres bound to be some drama. Here are the twenty most common fights that absolutely every girl has had with her friends (but it like, totally made you guys stronger, right?).
          </p>
          <h2>
           1. The I Feel Left Out Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           You feel so lame for having this argument because you havent felt this way since high school and the clique went on a trip to the mall without you. Nonetheless, you cant hold back how upset you were about being the last to know everything. Read: youre feeling insecure and want to make sure your friends didnt revoke your standing invitation to brunch.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           2. The I Caught You Sh*t-Talking Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           You were talking smack about your friend via text and accidentally sent it to her.
           <em>
            Well, this certainly did not go as planned.
           </em>
           Better throw in the towel early on, it looks like you have an evening of groveling to do.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           3. The Youve Been Drinking Too Much Friend-tervention Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           This is everyones least favorite fight  no friends want to tell their pal that she needs to slow down and no one wants to hear that shes got a drinking problem. Do yourself a solid and make sure everyone in attendance is sober, then when its all over, pop a bottle of wine!
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           4. The You Ruined My New Shirt Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Its pretty much a Rule of the Universe that on the nights when you need to be most careful, you will inevitably spill cranberry vodka on your shirt. Offer to pay for the dry cleaning and hope to G-d that your trusty laundromat can remove the glaring stain.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           5. The You Hooked Up With My Crush Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Hoes over bros is temporarily suspended, everyone takes sides, and all girl code goes out the window. B*tches be playin real dirty.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           6. The I Saw That Dress First While Shopping Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Your animal instincts really come out when fashion is involved. It doesnt matter if shes your friend, you will fight harder for this garment than a Midwestern soccer mom does over tupperware at Walmart on Black Friday.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           7. The Youre Spending Too Much Time With Your Boyfriend Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Also known as, Dont ever miss another girls night again fight.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           8. The Passive Aggressive Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           This is the fight in which the other person doesnt actually know that you are mad at her. You just are running around fuming like an aggressive idiot while the other girl is blissfully unaware of your anger. Guess who loses?
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           9. The Awkward Money Fight That Never Goes On Record
          </h2>
          <p>
           You owe her for the sushi. She owes you for the cab. Deduct that from the original bill and add in a few dollars for the bar tab and now no one is really sure who pays whom and how muchExcept you
           <em>
            know
           </em>
           she still needs to pay you for the cab. Do you ask for the money? Figure shell get the next one? No, you swallow the 10 bucks and next time casually forget to pick up that diet coke she wanted.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           10. The I Introduced You To My Friend And Now You Two Are Better Friends Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           When I said that you two should be best friends, I wasnt actually serious. Dont forget your roots!
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           11. The I Had A Bad Day And Now Im Taking It Out On You Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           If you dont like the people in your office then just yell at them, not us please! Or pretend like youre five and go to your room.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           12. The You Ditched Me At The Bar Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           I told you I needed a wingman and you totally left without telling me, said the stranded friend.
           <br/>
           I know Im supposed to feel bad because technically you arent supposed to leave a girl alone, but I dont actually feel bad because technically you made it home just fine, said the other.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           13. The Youve Been Really Selfish Lately Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Is it selfish for me to want you to stop being so selfish and pay attention to me!?
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           14. The Im Telling You A Harsh Truth Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           You know the moment you share this tough love with your friend that shes going to take the defense, but sometimes being a good friend means having to say what she doesnt want to hear. Stand your ground and remember that shell be thanking you later.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           15. The Im Calling You Out For Being A Flake Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Has it occurred to you that maybe the reason she keeps flaking is that she doesnt want to hang out with you? See number 14 above.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           16. The Compliment Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           You look amazing. No,
           <em>
            you
           </em>
           look amazing. I wish I had your legs. I wish I had your teeth. No one ever wins the compliment fight  its just a series of affirmations that the other person looks better in her skinny jeans than you do.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           17. The You Were Blackout And Said Something Messed Up Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Theres no way shes going to let this one slide. The truth always comes out when your inhibitions are lowered and youre spewing blackout hate. Congratulations! Youve just reached a new low!
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           18. The Im PMS-ing Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Wait, what are we even fighting about? I just got so heated back there for no reason. Sorry for putting you through the ringer and biting your head off. Now I feel like the jolliest person on the planet. YAY Hormones!
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           19. The Youve Been Sucking Lately Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           You cant put your finger on it, but your friend has been depressed and sucky and youre sick of her being in a funk. You know what wont motivate her to come out with you? Yelling at her for sucking.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <h2>
           20. The Were Getting Sick Of Each Other Fight
          </h2>
          <p>
           Because you two spend so much time together (duh, youre best friends!), youre bound to grow tired of each other eventually and you need some space. So you venture on without her and what do you realize? Absence makes the heart grow fonder.
          </p>
