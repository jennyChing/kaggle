        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Spooky and delicious Halloween desserts just in time for holiday. Desserts include everything from caramel apples to cheesecakes, spooky dessert dips, cookies, cupcakes and kid friendly dessert ideas. Simply Spooky treats make perfect Halloween desserts.  Its all about treats with no trickery this season with graveyards, ghouls, skeletons, witches and other classic seasonal flare. Save some of that leftover Halloween candy to top delicious desserts with this holiday!
        </p>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://www.swankyrecipes.com/gourmet-caramel-apples.html" target="_blank" title="Gourmet Caramel Apples from Swanky recipes">
          Gourmet Caramel Apples  Swanky Recipes
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Crisp fall apples dipped in caramel and chocolate then rolled in nuts and Oreo crumbs and drizzled with more chocolate. Get festive this season and hand-dip your own Chocolate and Caramel Apples. This classic fall treat is hard to resist!
        </p>
