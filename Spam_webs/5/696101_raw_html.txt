     <h1 class="post-title">
      Chic Glass Lamp from a Vase
     </h1>
     <div class="post-entry clearfix">
      <p>
       <a href="http://smallhomefurniture.com/chic-glass-lamp-from-a-vase/glass-lamp-beautiful/" title="Glass Lamp Beautiful">
        <img alt=": Glass Lamp Beautiful" height="447" src="http://smallhomefurniture.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/glass-lamp-beautiful-600x447.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Does your bedroom need more accent lighting? Just take a look around and imagine how beautiful it will be with a glass lamp on your nightstand. It can help you read better at night, and beautify the room with its design as well. But if in case you had tried hard to find a beautiful lamp that suits your fashion and bedroom design but still couldnt find one, maybe you should try to make a chic glass lamp from a vase.
       <br/>
       What you need
       <br/>
       Start preparing everything you need. They are lamp bulb, lamp kit, cord, lamp shade, glass drill, and a glass vase of course. The vase and shade design is all yours to decide, choose one which matches your bedroom design and heavy enough to hold the shades weight.
       <br/>
       How to do
       <br/>
       The first thing you need to do in making a chic glass lamp from a vase is drilling the glass vase for the cord, you will need an extra hole if you are going to use the vase upside down. Drill it with a glass drill, do it in full speed but carefully, pour water regularly on the glass to prevent overheat.
       <br/>
       Assemble the lamp kit and screw it tightly onto the glass vase. If your vase neck is too big for the lamp kit, make custom top to be placed on the neck as the lamp kit and shade foundation. Make sure that the cord is already soldered well.
       <br/>
       Chic glass lamp from a vase is really customizable to meet your fashion. You can fill the glass with sand, plastic plant, or anything you like to beautify it. Furthermore, spray paint the shade in any color that you think will match you bedroom design. All of those materials and process are much cheaper than buying a new one, and even look more amazing since you choose your own design in every part of it.
      </p>
