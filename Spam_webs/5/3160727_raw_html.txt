         </a>
        </h1>

        <p>
         Recently my 4 year old seems to have decided to become a comedian and has started telling kids jokes!
        </p>
        <p>
         These are jokes that aresomehowhilarious in the mind of a four year old, slightly more dubious to an adult, but funny to observe anyway!
         <br/>
         His sister did a very similar things when she was his age.
         <br/>
         Maybe it is a development stage for all 4 year olds? Who knows?
         <br/>
         I am not entirely sure he is set for a career in comedy, but he is a master of funny faces, and I have a feeling he is turning in to the class clown at school!
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are some of the funniest jokes he told me tonight
         <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://101thingstodowithkids.com.gridhosted.co.uk/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         <br/>
         1. Q. Why was the bear crawling on the road?
         <br/>
         A. Because he is a baby!
         <br/>
         <span id="more-4139">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Q. Why does the chicken go on the road?
         <br/>
         A. Because he wanted to get his pillow.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Q. Why does stinky pants go on the road?
         <br/>
         A. Because he wanted to get his baby pants.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Q. Why does nummny cross the road?
         <br/>
         A. Cos he wanted to get to the pigs.
        </p>
        <p>
         5. Q. Why does a four cross the road?
         <br/>
         A. Cos he wanted to get to his Millie!
        </p>
        <p>
         6. Q. Why does Jack cross the road?
         <br/>
         A. Cos he wanted to give Millie a kiss.
        </p>
        <p>
         Not sure if he is a comic genius but the crazy answers certainly made me laugh
         <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://101thingstodowithkids.com.gridhosted.co.uk/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         <a href="http://101thingstodowithkids.com.gridhosted.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/wpid-20150103_134930-e1422130628354.jpg">
          <img alt="wpid-20150103_134930.jpg" class=" size-medium wp-image-4138 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://101thingstodowithkids.com.gridhosted.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/wpid-20150103_134930-e1422130628354-169x300.jpg" width="169"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Did your little one go through a kids joke phase?
         <br/>
         Share your favourite with us below!
        </p>
