        <p>
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           Today is my 39th wedding anniversary. The secret to a loving relationship is to do the work it takes to grow peacefully and lovingly into the future.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           My wonderful regular readers know I married when I was 17 years old and pregnant; we were parents to four little girls when I was 22 years old. The odds were against us. Big time.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           How do we do it? We do the work required of relationships. Weve gotten the help we need.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           We have been to individual and couples therapy (lots of it), and we have
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; ">
          attended retreats, workshops and conferences together.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           We put each other above all else.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           We know how to have fun, be adventurous, work together and "be" together.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <o:p>
          <span style="font-size:14px;">
           <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
            We
           </span>
          </span>
         </o:p>
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           also fight, blame, get angry and begin again. Theres no perfection.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           Yet, there is constant improvement and the willingness to change and to begin again.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           When its difficult we put one foot in front of the other. We lean on each other.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <span style="font-size:14px;">
          <span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
           When its easy we celebrate, knowing hat well be together until death creates an unspoken peace.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
