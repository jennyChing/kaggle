      <div class="con-tent bor">
       <p class="tit-nm">
        ArcheAge Ranged Class with Archery Recommendation: Bloodskald / Outrider / Warpriest
       </p>
       <div class="tim-sha">
        <span class="tag">
        </span>
        <em class="sha fr">
        </em>
        <em class="tim fr">
         Posted: 09/24/2014
        </em>
       </div>
       <div class="font-nm">
        <p>
         Hey, guys, ArcheAge has been released for a week. How do you feel about the game? And which class do you prefer? Today, I want to introduce you with ranged class with Archery. They are
         <strong>
          <font color="#ff0080">
           <u>
            Bloodskald / Outrider / Warpriest.
           </u>
          </font>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Archery majors in
         <font color="#ff0080">
          <strong>
           <u>
            ranged attacks and AOE skills.
           </u>
          </strong>
         </font>
         In the following, I have listed the single targeted skills and ranged AOE skills.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="ArcheAge Ranged Class with Archery Recommendation: Bloodskald / Outrider / Warpriest" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/archery.JPG"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font color="#8000ff">
          <strong>
           Single Target skill
          </strong>
         </font>
         :
         <strong>
          <u>
           Float &amp; Snare
          </u>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Float: It is a good and powerful skill for melee fighting.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="float" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/float.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         Snare: It can cause binding effect.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="snare" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/snare.JPG"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <font color="#8000ff">
           AOE skills
          </font>
         </strong>
         :
         <strong>
          <u>
           Boneyard &amp; Concussive arrow
          </u>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="boneyard" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/boneyard.JPG"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="concussive arrow" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/concussive%20arrow.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         In the following, I will recommend you with a few Ranged class with Archery
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <font color="#8000ff" size="4">
           Bloodskald
           <br/>
          </font>
          <font color="#8000ff">
           Single target skills
          </font>
         </strong>
         :
         <strong>
          <u>
           Piercing Shot + charged bolt + Triple Slash + Charge + Precision Strike + critical discord
          </u>
         </strong>
         . The combo can cause linkage effect and deal with huge damage to foes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="bloodskald" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/bloodskald.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <font color="#8000ff">
           AOE skills
          </font>
         </strong>
         :
         <strong>
          <u>
           Whirlwind slash + Sunder Earth + Boneyard + Concussive Arrow
          </u>
         </strong>
         the combo:
         <strong>
          <u>
           Whirlwind slash + Sunder Earth
          </u>
         </strong>
         can make foes crippled together.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <u>
           Quickstep + Ode to Recovery
          </u>
         </strong>
         , when you escape, you can get blood regeneration immediately.
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <font color="#8000ff" size="4">
           Outrider
          </font>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="outrider" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/outrider.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         I have to say this class with high damage output. No matter it is melee fighting or ranged fighting, you can work it well.
         <br/>
         First of all, it is farming.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <u>
           Toxic Shot + charged bolt + piercing shot + triple slash + charge + pin down
          </u>
         </strong>
         for farming, quite OP. The skills listed above are instantaneous casting ones. The linkage effect will cause huge damage and the combo requires high operability.
        </p>
        <p>
         After the linkage effect, use overwhelm causing stun and go around to the back. Apply
         <strong>
          <u>
           shadowsmite
          </u>
         </strong>
         into use.
        </p>
        <p>
         Besides,
         <strong>
          <u>
           drop back + stealth
          </u>
         </strong>
         are good survival skills. Drop back will grant you 14m jumping back ability.
         <strong>
          <u>
           Stealth
          </u>
         </strong>
         can make you invisible to other foes. Foes and monsters can not track your trail. You can succeed in escaping.
        </p>
        <p>
         But the class has a shortcoming.
         <strong>
          <font color="#ff0080">
           That is a lack of buff and regeneration ability
          </font>
         </strong>
         . When you farm dungeons and boss, you can collocate the profession with other characters. And due to the shortcoming, you need to improve the class survivability.
        </p>
        <p>
         <font color="#8000ff" size="4">
          <strong>
           Warpriest
          </strong>
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="warpriest" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/warpriest.JPG"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This class is similar to Bloodskala in some aspect. Compared with Bloodskala, it also has some unique features.
         <font color="#ff0080">
          <strong>
           Its single target damage output is lowered a little, while its AOE damage output is improved.
          </strong>
         </font>
         You can use this class in the later period. Beside, concerning about this class, I want to specially mention two skills here.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <u>
           Review
          </u>
         </strong>
         <img alt="review" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/review.JPG"/>
         : revive an ally and restores the XP lost at death, which is really great for dungeons.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <u>
           Antithesis
          </u>
         </strong>
         <img alt="antithesis" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/antithesis.JPG"/>
         : It can be used as a defensive and attacking skill.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <u>
           Skewer
          </u>
         </strong>
         <img alt="skewer" src="http://www.itemstores.com/images/news/skewer.JPG"/>
         : High AOE damage. And It will exert remarkable damage with other skills.
        </p>
        <p>
