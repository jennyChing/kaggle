               <h1 class="article-title">
                Dear Seagate  What can I do about a fungal toenail infection?
               </h1>
              </div>
              <div class="article-meta article-meta-top">
               <span class="article-date article-meta-item">
                <span>
                 August 29, 2013
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="article-content sc" data-role="content">
              <fb:like action="like" colorscheme="light" font="lucida grande" height="65" href="http://www.seagateworld.com/2013/08/dear-seagate-what-can-i-do-about-a-fungal-toenail-infection/" layout="button_count" send="true" show_faces="true" width="450">
              </fb:like>
              <p>
               Every week we will be posting anonymous questions from our customers. Everything from correct dosage, to our farming methods, and much more.
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="http://www.dreamstime.com/stock-photography-old-personal-letter-image4307212" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1276 ov-done" height="537" src="http://www.seagateworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/dreamstime_s_4307212(pp_w659_h537).jpg" width="659"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               <em>
                <strong>
                 Dear Seagate
                </strong>
               </em>
              </p>
              <p>
               <em>
                <strong>
                 I was referred to your website for its promotion of Olive leaf. I was researching ways to lower my blood pressure, and someone who is a fan of yours said you had a product for a toenail fungal infection(which I have on one of my big toes.) Which product would I apply to the toenail? Also which product best addresses the HBP?
                </strong>
               </em>
              </p>
              <p>
               <em>
                <strong>
                 Thank you kindly,
                </strong>
               </em>
              </p>
              <p>
               <em>
                <strong>
                 Mark
                </strong>
               </em>
               <br/>
               Dear Mark,
              </p>
              <p>
               For the toenail, you can apply the
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/246/OliveViate-Topical-Skin-Relief-1-oz-Bottle.html" target="_blank">
                OliveViate
               </a>
               around the nail. It wont penetrate the actual nail so press it around the edges of the nail as good as you can. What seems to help is to also take the
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/223/Olive-Leaf-Extract-450mg-90-V-Caps-%28Buy-One-Get-One-FREE%29.html" target="_blank">
                Olive Leaf Extract
               </a>
               capsules at the same time. In most cases, most customers will eventually see new pink skin growing out of the cuticle after a couple of weeks and the bad skin beneath the nail growing out.
              </p>
              <p>
               High Blood Pressure is a whole different game. If its really very high, you need to see a doctor and get it regulated down. However if its high but not dangerously high, we attack the problem as a major change in lifestyle  go on
               <a href="http://www.knowthecause.com/index.php/get-started" target="_blank">
                Doug Kaufmanns Phase One Diet
               </a>
               and read his books about the fungal link to a lot of these sorts of problems; get rid of all the carbs, sugars, alcohol, tobacco and all the other obvious bad things. Eat a lot of organic vegetables, chicken, fish, etc. We have items that will support this diet such as 
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/226/Omega-3%2B-Shark-Liver-Oil-250mg-100-Softgels.html" target="_blank">
                Omega 3 shark liver oil
               </a>
               ,
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/218/Marine-Protein-700mg-100-V-caps.html" target="_blank">
                Marine Protein
               </a>
               , and most important
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/223/Olive-Leaf-Extract-450mg-90-V-Caps-%28Buy-One-Get-One-FREE%29.html" target="_blank">
                Olive Leaf Extract
               </a>
               for starters. If you cannot get enough good veggies, we have
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/213/Carrot-Powder-300-grams.html" target="_blank">
                freeze-dried carrot
               </a>
               ,
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/210/Broccoli-250mg-50-V-Caps.html" target="_blank">
                broccoli
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://seagateproducts.com/product/217/Lycopene-15--15mg-90-V-Caps.html" target="_blank">
                tomato
               </a>
               that will supplement the diet. Get on a regular daily exercise program, working your way up to at least one half hour a day.
              </p>
              <p>
               There is no magical pill that will counteract years of bad diet, habits and lack of exercise. But these are the textbook things you can do that will make the blood pressure eventually go down.
               <br/>
               Hope some of this helps you.
              </p>
              <p>
               Sincerely,
              </p>
              <p>
               Seagate
              </p>
