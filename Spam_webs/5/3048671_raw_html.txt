        <em>
         The Hateful Eight
        </em>
        is back on, said Sneider on Twitter, as sources tell me that Quentin has reached out to Sam Jackson in the week since he called off the movie.

        Given all the hoo-ha surrounding the original leak, we can expect QT to play this one very close to his chest, but
        <em>
         The Hateful Eight
        </em>
        might not have bitten the dust just yet
