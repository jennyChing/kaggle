        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         How to Update a Vintage Suitcase
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-08-15T10:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          August 15, 2011
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/author/nod" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Nod
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/2011/08/how-to-update-a-vintage-suitcase.html#comments">
           <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="825 http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/2011/08/how-to-update-a-vintage-suitcase.html">
            5 Comments
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p class="no-break">
         <span class="st_pinterest_hcount" st_title="How to Update a Vintage Suitcase" st_url="http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/2011/08/how-to-update-a-vintage-suitcase.html">
         </span>
         <span class="st_facebook_hcount" st_title="How to Update a Vintage Suitcase" st_url="http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/2011/08/how-to-update-a-vintage-suitcase.html">
         </span>
         <span class="st_twitter_hcount" st_title="How to Update a Vintage Suitcase" st_url="http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/2011/08/how-to-update-a-vintage-suitcase.html" st_username="@thelandofnod" st_via="@thelandofnod">
         </span>
         <span class="st_googleplus_hcount" st_title="How to Update a Vintage Suitcase" st_url="http://blog.landofnod.com/honest-to-nod/2011/08/how-to-update-a-vintage-suitcase.html">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c015434423983970c.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="1" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c015434423983970c image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c015434423983970c-800wi.jpg" title="1"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         When I found this vintage suitcase, I knew it would be perfect for my girls' dress-up clothes. And thanks to a brilliant suggestion by Jude of
         <a href="http://colourgiggles.wordpress.com/" target="_blank" title="Colour  Giggles">
          Colour Giggles
         </a>
         , I neutralized the musty smell of the case by pouring kitty litter in it and forgetting about it for several months.
        </p>
        <p>
         I recently received the new
         <a href="http://www.landofnod.com/customer-service/catalog-request.aspx" target="_blank" title="Land of Nod">
          Land of Nod
         </a>
         catalog and fell inlove with all thequirky illustrations.I took my vintage suitcase and made a happy design from an image that I found in the catalog  and you can too!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a621ba4970d.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="2" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c014e8a621ba4970d image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a621ba4970d-800wi.jpg" title="2"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Materials:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Vintage Suitcase
        </p>
        <p>
         White Plastic Contact Liner
        </p>
        <p>
         Felt
        </p>
        <p>
         Glue and brush
        </p>
        <p>
         Pen
        </p>
        <p>
         Pins
        </p>
        <p>
         Scissors
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c0154346fa5cc970c.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="3" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c0154346fa5cc970c image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c0154346fa5cc970c-800wi.jpg" title="3"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Draw or trace your pattern out of paper, pin it onto the felt and cut out your design. Glue pieces together.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f70e6970d.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="4" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f70e6970d image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f70e6970d-800wi.jpg" title="4"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Cut contact paper to size, peel the backing and carefully stick to the top of the case.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f7166970d.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="5" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f7166970d image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f7166970d-800wi.jpg" title="5"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Glue the designs onto the case.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c0153909c3667970b.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="6" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c0153909c3667970b image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c0153909c3667970b-800wi.jpg" title="6"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f729b970d.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="7" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f729b970d image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f729b970d-800wi.jpg" title="7"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f72ce970d.jpg" style="display: inline;">
          <img alt="8" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f72ce970d image-full" src="http://blog.landofnod.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/6a012876eb143a970c014e8a8f72ce970d-800wi.jpg" title="8"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Post by Helen Bird :
         <a href="http://www.curlybirds.typepad.com/" target="_blank" title="Curly Birds">
          www.curlybirds.typepad.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Checkout our talented illustrator, Blanca Gomez at
         <a href="http://www.cosasminimas.com/" target="_blank" title="Blanca Gomez">
          cosasminimas.com
