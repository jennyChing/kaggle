        <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
         It seems like we always have a birthday to celebrate at work. Last week it was Bethanys turn to celebrate. I decided to try some fun decorating ideas instead of coming up with a new recipe. I have seen several versions of marshmallow flowers, and thought that the flowers would be the perfect way to decorate some birthday cupcakes. I had so much fun creating them, and they were so easy to make. Bethany loved the
         <strong>
          Flower Power Cupcakes
         </strong>
         ! Just in case you were curious, the cupcakes were pumpkin spice flavored.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="flowercupcakes1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2075" height="304" src="http://megansmunchies.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/flowercupcakes11.jpg" title="flowercupcakes1" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <!-- 		@page { margin: 0.79in } 		P { margin-bottom: 0.08in } -->
        </p>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
         <strong>
          To make the marshmallow flowers:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Use scissors to cut large marshmallows into 4 pieces/petals.
         </li>
         <li>
          Dip one side of each petal into colored sugar of your choice.
         </li>
         <li>
          Arrange petals on top of cupcakes.
         </li>
         <li>
          Put a medium sized dot of frosting in the middle of each flower.
         </li>
         <li>
          Using small spoon, sprinkle yellow colored sugar on frosting in center of the flower.
         </li>
        </ol>
