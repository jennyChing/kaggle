                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Expat Wednesdays with Ali's Adventures
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3716663007498218982" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      Expats. Travelers who settled down for a tiny bit? Wanderers who found their place on Earth? Seekers who reached a place where they intended to be? Who are they? Would you like to meet them?
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Hint: say yes!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      The Picktures proudly presents you the guest post series:
                      <u>
                       Expat Wednesdays
                      </u>
                      ! Today, I would love to introduce you to Ali, an American expat living in Freiburg, Germany, editor-in-chief of
                      <a href="http://www.aliadventures.com/" target="_blank">
                       Ali's Adventures
                      </a>
                      blog.
                      <br/>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       Here comes her story.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       I moved to Freiburg, Germany about three years ago, just after getting married. My husband, who is also American, had already been living here for a few years, and since I already wanted to live in Europe, it made sense for me to move.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-sUFE8DLQtPs/U6nUxl9PP6I/AAAAAAAAHRA/abnw41rbNjw/s1600/Freiburg+expat+1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-sUFE8DLQtPs/U6nUxl9PP6I/AAAAAAAAHRA/abnw41rbNjw/s1600/Freiburg+expat+1.jpg" title="The center of Freiburg with Martinstor in the background" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       Freiburg isn't really on the tourist radar, but it's at the bottom of the Black Forest, which makes it a great base for outdoor activities. It's also a university town, so it has a youthful vibe and good, inexpensive food.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-nFMsjdmIBIM/U6nVJwozguI/AAAAAAAAHRM/G_1oibMJkGA/s1600/Freiburg+expat+2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-nFMsjdmIBIM/U6nVJwozguI/AAAAAAAAHRM/G_1oibMJkGA/s1600/Freiburg+expat+2.jpg" title="Rathausplatz, city hall square" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-GH8n3NSTSMg/U6nVKo8mpBI/AAAAAAAAHRU/2dea07iebjU/s1600/Freiburg+expat+3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-GH8n3NSTSMg/U6nVKo8mpBI/AAAAAAAAHRU/2dea07iebjU/s1600/Freiburg+expat+3.jpg" title="A view from Schauinsland, one of the highest peaks in the Freiburg area" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                       One of my favorite parts of town is the main market in Mnsterplatz in front of the cathedral. I love markets, and I often buy vegetables from the local farmers here.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-N_vLm69mnL0/U6nVsOk3a5I/AAAAAAAAHRY/jvNlg4sQRpU/s1600/Freiburg+expat+4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-N_vLm69mnL0/U6nVsOk3a5I/AAAAAAAAHRY/jvNlg4sQRpU/s1600/Freiburg+expat+4.jpg" title="The main market in Mnsterplatz in front of the cathedral" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-oip_KLc4zfg/U6nVtNfIXZI/AAAAAAAAHRg/pUzAalOcCIE/s1600/Freiburg+expat+5.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-oip_KLc4zfg/U6nVtNfIXZI/AAAAAAAAHRg/pUzAalOcCIE/s1600/Freiburg+expat+5.jpg" title="Mnsterplatz on a non-market day" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-wvKJ-8B4qWg/U6nWEqjJ9NI/AAAAAAAAHRo/iqghILbo-Nw/s1600/Freiburg+expat+6.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-wvKJ-8B4qWg/U6nWEqjJ9NI/AAAAAAAAHRo/iqghILbo-Nw/s1600/Freiburg+expat+6.jpg" title="The cathedral" width="418"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       Another interesting detail in Freiburg is the Bchle. These are like mini canals on the sides of the streets in the old town. They were once used to put out fires, but now they're just decoration. Legend has it that if you accidentally step into one, you will marry a local.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-LUGKdpmvGpc/U6nWPhgoQnI/AAAAAAAAHRw/X4U4ZwsqWcs/s1600/Freiburg+expat+7.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-LUGKdpmvGpc/U6nWPhgoQnI/AAAAAAAAHRw/X4U4ZwsqWcs/s1600/Freiburg+expat+7.jpg" title="A  woman stepping over a Bchle in the center of Freiburg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       I also love the old architecture. Freiburg has two remaining gates, and they're a big reminder of the history behind the city.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_ReOZeW8Se4/U6nW1sE_QkI/AAAAAAAAHR8/p8JGfNKf-dk/s1600/Freiburg+expat+8.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_ReOZeW8Se4/U6nW1sE_QkI/AAAAAAAAHR8/p8JGfNKf-dk/s1600/Freiburg+expat+8.jpg" title="Martinstor, one of the two remaining old city gates" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-fjt5wgPlQlo/U6nW3arTMOI/AAAAAAAAHSE/YsZLF_1VaIE/s1600/Freiburg+expat+9.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Freiburg, Germany" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-fjt5wgPlQlo/U6nW3arTMOI/AAAAAAAAHSE/YsZLF_1VaIE/s1600/Freiburg+expat+9.jpg" title="Schwabentor, one of the two remaining old city gates" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       My German is still pretty bad and expat life has its stresses, but overall I'm happy to live in Freiburg.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
