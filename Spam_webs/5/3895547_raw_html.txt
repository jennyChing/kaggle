        <p>
         <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
          <img alt="48 Healthier Holiday Cookie Recipes" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/Cookie-Banner.jpg?itok=VHhlZA5H"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <!--MEDIA-WRAPPER-END-1-->
        <p>
         Lets set the scene: The stockings are hung, the menorah is lit, and the cinnamon candles scent is wafting through the house. Then, the smell of freshly baked cookies emerges from the kitchen, and everyone gathers round to enjoy a batch of homemade holiday cookies.
        </p>
        <p>
         But the real Christmas miracle is that this list of cookie recipes is
         <em>
          healthier
         </em>
         ! We understand its totally time to splurge and enjoy a holiday treat (or seven), so were making it a bit easier by providing some better options. From the classic sugar cookie and gingerbread man to raspberry thumbprints and lemon drops, here are 48 cookie recipes that wont do damage to those healthy habits.
        </p>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Double Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/Simple-Peanut-Butter-Chocolate-Chip-Cookies-vegan-glutenfree.jpg?itok=-hvKKwQe" title="Photo: Minimalist Baker"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Minimalist Baker
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          1.
         </strong>
         <a href="http://minimalistbaker.com/double-peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies/">
          <strong>
           Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies
          </strong>
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         We cant
         <em>
          not
         </em>
         include this classic. This recipe is veganfilled with
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-alternatives-peanut-butter" target="_blank">
          nut butter
         </a>
         , oats, and almond mealbut we promise it puts Nabisco to shame. Grab a glass of milk and dunk away!
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          2.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/recipe-fruit-and-nut-bars-041312">
           Fruit and Nut Bar
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         These bars work for breakfast, as an afternoon snack, or an after-dinner dessert. It has protein (nuts!),
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/surprising-high-fiber-foods" target="_blank">
          fiber
         </a>
         (fruit!), and all the great taste. (So make them today!)
        </p>
        <div class="ym" data-yieldmo="ym_966591849689717803" data-yieldmo-tablet="ym_976628814724144286" id="ym_966591849689717803">
        </div>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          3.
         </strong>
         <a href="http://www.eat-yourself-skinny.com/2011/10/skinny-snickerdoodles-and-a-yummy-review.html">
          <strong>
           Snickerdoodle
          </strong>
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         These cookies are so simple, so delicious, and
         <em>
          so
         </em>
         not-so bad for you. With just the right amount of
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-cinnamon" target="_blank">
          cinnamon
         </a>
         and sugar, this holiday classic can be enjoyed anytime.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          4.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/christmas-sugar-wafers-with-vanilla-icing-10000000833344/">
           Vanilla Iced Sugar Wafer
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Gather some fitness-themed cookie cutters for this recipe! Made with egg whites and just a touch of sugar and spice, cutouts of
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/real-christmas-tree-healthier" target="_blank">
          Christmas trees
         </a>
         and holiday bells never tasted better (or less guilty).
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          5.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/espresso-crinkles-10000001860058/">
           Espresso Crinkle
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Unsweetened cocoa powder and instant espresso take center stage in this recipe. Paired perfectly with a cup of coffee, these cookies are a
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/15-healthy-and-creative-ways-get-your-caffeine-fix" target="_blank">
          caffeine-lovers
         </a>
         dream.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          6.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.skinnytaste.com/2011/09/pumpkin-spiced-oatmeal-pecan-cookies.html">
           Pumpkin Spiced Oatmeal Pecan Cookie
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Pumpkin season isnt over till we say its over. (And its not over.) Filled with
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/guide-to-healthier-grains-infographic" target="_blank">
          oats
         </a>
         and nuts, one or two of these will keep anyone satisfied till the New Year.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          7.
          <a href="http://www.cucinalibera.com/2011/11/coconut-cream-shortbread.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           Coconut Cream Shortbread
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Shortbread is usually loaded with at least one stick of butter, but these bites get their richness from coconut butter instead. Theyre also free of gluten, corn, soy, nuts, and refined sugars. Pretty impressive for a Christmas cookie, right?
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          8.
          <a href="http://lululemon.com/community/blog/holiday-baking/" target="_blank">
           Vegan Chai Cookie
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         No dairy?
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/vegan-recipe-substitutions/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          No problem!
         </a>
         With a touch of spicy chai, this recipe is guaranteed to impress any sweet tooth. Enjoy these cookies with a cup of tea and good company.
        </p>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Low-Fat Gingerbread Cookies" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/skinny-gingerbread-men_0.jpg?itok=gfb_4JN6" title="Photo: SkinnyTaste"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: SkinnyTaste
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          9.
         </strong>
         <a href="http://www.skinnytaste.com/2011/12/low-fat-gingerbread-cookies.html">
          <strong>
           Low-Fat Gingerbread Cookies
          </strong>
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Half the fat and all the flavor? Sign us up! Besides, who doesnt love to decorate these adorable gingerbread men at any holiday festivity.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          10.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/macadamia-ginger-cookies-10000001010495/">
            Macadamia-Ginger Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/macadamia-ginger-cookies-10000001010495/">
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Macadamia nuts need more love. And luckily, theres nothing
         <em>
          not
         </em>
         to enjoy with this cookie made with egg whites,
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-ginger">
          fresh ginger
         </a>
         , and a touch of honey, too.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          11.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://urbanposer.blogspot.com/2012/10/almond-flour-cut-out-cookies.html">
            Paleo Sugar Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Cavemen need cookies too, right? Using almond flour, honey, and
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/what%E2%80%99s-best-cooking-oil" target="_blank">
          coconut oil
         </a>
         , this basic holiday dessert just got
         <em>
          real
         </em>
         fancy.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          12.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://chocolatecoveredkatie.com/2011/12/13/hot-chocolate-cookies-2/">
           Hot Chocolate Cookie
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Cookies that taste like hot chocolate? We went there. Made with dates,
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/chocolate-actually-healthy" target="_blank">
          chocolate chips
         </a>
         , cocoa powder, and coconut oil, well never need to drink the liquid stuff again.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          13.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://stralayoga.com/blog/healthy-holiday-biscotti/">
           Cranberry Almond Biscotti
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         For something with a bit of extra crunch, whip up a batch of biscotti. Using spelt flour (which is lower in gluten than regular wheat and great for people with a slight gluten sensitivity),
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-baking-recipe-substitutions-infographic" target="_blank">
          applesauce
         </a>
         , and olive oil, its quite all right to reach for seconds of these treats.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          14.
          <a href="http://www.nourishingmeals.com/2009/12/soft-molasses-cookies-vegan-gluten-free.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           Soft Molasses Cookie
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         These vegan, gluten-free cookies are loaded with classic holiday flavor thanks to ground ginger, cinnamon, molasses, and vanilla. Half a cup of
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-baking-fat-swaps" target="_blank">
          unsweetened applesauce
         </a>
         makes these spicy treats nice and moist without adding too much fat.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          15
         </strong>
         .
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.stockpilingmoms.com/2012/07/gluten-free-peanut-butter-kiss-cookies-recipe/">
           Peanut Butter Kiss Cookie (Gluten-Free)
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Grab someone you love and enjoy
         <em>
          this
         </em>
         kiss under the mistletoe. Its made with rice and tapioca flour, too, so even people with
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/27-gluten-free-recipe-substitutions/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          gluten intolerances
         </a>
         can taste the love!
        </p>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Guilt-Free Pecan Pie Cookies" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/Paleo-Guilt-Free-Pecan-Pie-Cookies-vegan-Allergy-Free-Alaska.jpg?itok=-7BkWvie" title="Photo: Allergy Free Alaska"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Allergy Free Alaska
         </span>
        </span>
        <div>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           16.
           <a href="http://www.allergyfreealaska.com/2012/12/06/guilt-free-pecan-pie-cookies/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
            Pecan Pie Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          This festive flavor combination isnt just for Thanksgiving. To make these cookies, just top basic thumbprint cookie dough (made with almond flour, coconut oil, almond milk, and maple syrup) with a spiced
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/types-of-nut-infographic" target="_blank">
           pecan
          </a>
          filling.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           17.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://healthyindulgences.net/2012/08/healthier-peanut-butter-cookies-aka-fresh-peanut-butter-cookies-a-lower-sugar-high-protein-version-of-classic-pb-cookies/">
            Protein Peanut Butter Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Sometimes, peanut butter straight from the spoon isnt enough. These peanut butter treats are made with dry roasted peanuts, a touch of brown sugar, and
          <a href="http://greatist.com/fitness/protein-supplement-nutrition-guide" target="_blank">
           protein powder
          </a>
          for a high-protein, low-sugar treat. (And don't be afraid to throw in some dark chocolate chips for a lil' extra sweetness.)
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           18.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.sunnysideuprecipes.com/2009/12/healthier-shortbread-cookies.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Shortbread
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Yep, shortbread cookies dont
          <em>
           always
          </em>
          have to be full of butter. This recipe cuts out part of the fat with applesauce, still yielding a recipe thatll satisfy any taste bud.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           19.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.bobsredmill.com/recipes.php?recipe=1467">
            Almond Horn (Gluten-Free)
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Whether going
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/62-healthier-gluten-free-desserts" target="_blank">
           gluten-free
          </a>
          or not, this cookie is for you. These are low in sugar and contain no grains, but are full of all the deliciousness of a classic almond horn.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           20.
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.nbcnewyork.com/blogs/go-healthy-ny/Healthy-Holidays-Cookies-135732583.html">
             Raspberry-Chocolate Thumbprint
            </a>
           </strong>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Sweeten up the holiday season with a fruit-inspired cookie. With a hefty serving of oats, fruit, and
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-almonds" target="_blank">
           almonds
          </a>
          , everyone will be filling up on the good stuff.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           21.
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.davidlebovitz.com/2009/01/nonfat-gingersnaps/">
             Gingersnap
            </a>
           </strong>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Add a spicy bite to your next kitchen creation. (And better yet,
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-baking-fat-swaps/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           leave out the fat
          </a>
          !) These cookies are soft, chewy, and made with
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/ginger/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           fresh ginger
          </a>
          for some extra superfood love.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           22.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://chocolatecoveredkatie.com/2011/05/18/chocolate-chip-blondies-and-theyre-good-for-you/">
            Chocolate Chip Blondie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          This spin on a delicious dessert has a secret ingredient:
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/21-foolproof-vegan-recipe-substitutions" target="_blank">
           garbanzo beans
          </a>
          ! But
          <em>
           trust
          </em>
          us on this one  youll never know the difference, and your guests will never guess!
         </p>
        </div>
        <div>
         <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
          <img alt="Linzer Cookies" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/Linzer-Cookies.jpg?itok=Gxc03GoV" title="Photo: Katie at the Kitchen Door"/>
          <span class="caption">
           Photo: Katie at the Kitchen Door
          </span>
         </span>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           23.
           <strong>
            <a href="http://katieatthekitchendoor.com/2010/09/03/sorry-august/">
             Linzer Cookies
            </a>
           </strong>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Filled with fruit jam and surrounded by crushed almonds and
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/types-of-nut-infographic" target="_blank">
           hazelnuts
          </a>
          , this is a classy cookie perfect for any holiday party.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           24.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/lemon-honey-drop-cookies-10000000223017/">
            Lemon Honey Drop Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          For a sweet and sour treat, try these brightly flavored baked goods.
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-lemon" target="_blank">
           Lemon rinds
          </a>
          , honey, and plain yogurt are the stars in this recipe that make this cookie a real (healthy!) winner.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           25.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.food.com/recipe/healthy-flourless-butterscotch-brownies-58606">
            Flourless Butterscotch Brownie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Need we read past the title? When the chocolate craving hits, grab one of these brownies, no flour included.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           26.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://fitnessandfeta.com/2013/02/17/white-chocolate-peppermint-drop-cookies/">
           <strong>
            White
           </strong>
          </a>
          <a href="http://fitnessandfeta.com/2013/02/17/white-chocolate-peppermint-drop-cookies/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Chocolate Peppermint Drop Cookie
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Whats a holiday season without some peppermint?
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-recipe-substitutions/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           Swapping out some butter
          </a>
          for plain yogurt makes them healthier, and crushed candy canes make them worth going back for more!
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           27.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.healthfulpursuit.com/2011/06/guilt-free-nuts-seeds-toffee-square/">
            Toffee Square
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Nuts, seeds, and coconut make this a
          <a href="http://www.greatist.com/superfood" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           superfood
          </a>
          special in dessert form. Customize these bars with dried fruit and
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/types-of-nut-infographic/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           favorite nuts
          </a>
          , or sprinkle in some chocolate chips for extra sweetness!
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           28
          </strong>
          .
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.healthyfoodforliving.com/raspberry-coconut-oat-macaroons/">
            Raspberry Coco-Oat Macaroon
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside, and filled with coconut. What else could we ask for in a cookie?
          <a href="http://greatist.com/45-best-berry-recipes-summer" target="_blank">
           Fresh raspberries
          </a>
          add some extra sweetness (and fiber), and rolled oats keep all the ingredients together.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           29.
           <a href="http://www.health.com/health/recipe/0,,10000001862974,00.html">
            White Chocolate Holiday Bark
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          The secret to this recipe is sliced almonds and rice cereal. (Who knew?) Add melted white chocolate and
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/dangerfood-dried-fruit" target="_blank">
           dried fruit
          </a>
          for a creative twist on a classic.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div>
         <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
          <img alt="Spiced Carrot Cake Cookie" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/carrotcakecookies-23.jpg?itok=4s64Keqk" title="Photo: Averie Cooks"/>
          <span class="caption">
           Photo: Averie Cooks
          </span>
         </span>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           30.
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.averiecooks.com/2013/04/soft-and-chewy-spiced-carrot-cake-cookies.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             Spiced Carrot Cake Cookie
            </a>
           </strong>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          With grated
          <a href="http://greatist.com/carrot" target="_blank">
           carrots
          </a>
          and rolled oats as two of the major ingredients, this soft, spicy cookie is a good balance between healthy and indulgent. If butterscotch chips aren't your fave, sub in dark chocolate.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           31.
           <a href="http://realfoodforager.com/videorecipe-coconut-date-linzer-cookie/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
            Coconut Date Linzer Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          No grain is no problem for this festive, whole food-filled cookie. The dough is made from coconut flour, while the filling is made from chopped up
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfoods-a-to-z" target="_blank">
           Medjool dates
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           32.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://greatist.com/health/recipe-healthier-pumpkin-chocolate-chip-cookies/">
            Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Whole-wheat flour, a little flax, and a lotta
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-pumpkin" target="_blank">
           pumpkin
          </a>
          and dark chocolate make this a crowd pleaserespecially around the holidays!
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           33.
           <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/no-bake-coconut-delights-sugar-dairy-egg-and-grain-free/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
            No-Bake Coconut Delight
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Need a quick, easy dessert to bring to that last minute, unexpected holiday party? This five-ingredient, no-bake dessert will fit the bill.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           34.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.eatingwell.com/recipes/raspberry_bars.html">
            Raspberry Bar
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Tart raspberries plus low-fat cream cheese make for a creative holiday dessert. Enjoy as a late-night snack or the perfect start to any morning.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           35.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.thekitchn.com/my-favorite-fal-159814">
            Fig and Date Swirl
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          For some fiber with your dessert, what better than a cookie filled with figs and dates? Swap some butter for
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-greek-yogurt" target="_blank">
           low-fat yogurt
          </a>
          or applesauce, or stick to reduce-fat cream cheese to make these even better for the body.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           36.
           <a href="http://www.bonappetit.com/recipes/2011/12/peppermint-meringues">
            Peppermint Meringues
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-eggs" target="_blank">
           Egg whites
          </a>
          are useful for more than just omelets. For a light, sweet treat after a big meal, one of these festive meringues should fit the dessert bill.
         </p>
         <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
          <img alt="Raw Chocolate Pumpkin Pecan Macaroons" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/pumpkin-macaroons.jpg?itok=UlrcKVv9" title="Photo: Civilized Caveman Cooking"/>
          <span class="caption">
           Photo: Civilized Caveman Cooking
          </span>
         </span>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           37.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://civilizedcavemancooking.com/grain-free-goodies/raw-chocolate-pumpkin-pecan-macaroons/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Raw Chocolate Pumpkin Pecan Macaroon
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          These no-bake treats are practically a health food (practically). Not-so-unhealthy ingredients include unsweetened coconut, pecan flour,
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/homemade-pumpkin-puree" target="_blank">
           pumpkin puree
          </a>
          , cocoa powder, vanilla, and cinnamon.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           38.
           <a href="http://citronlimette.com/recipe_archive/desserts/fresh-cranberry-oatmeal-bars/">
            Fresh Cranberry Oatmeal Bar
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-cranberries" target="_blank">
           Fresh cranberries
          </a>
          , Greek yogurt, and orange rind give these gooey bars plenty of flavor. Whole-wheat pastry flour and rolled oats add a bit of fiber.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           39.
           <a href="http://www.veganricha.com/2012/02/tiramisu-cookies-vegan-glutenfree.html">
            Tiramisu Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Vegan and gluten-free cookies dont have to be dry, flavorless, and boring. These tiramisu sandwich cookies feature tasty ingredients like rum, vanilla, amaretto, coconut cream, and
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/why-coffee-tea-are-amazing-you-infographic" target="_blank">
           instant coffee
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           40.
           <a href="http://almondsandavocados.com/oreo-cookies-100th-birthday-i-celebrated-with-homemade-raw-oreos/">
            Homemade Raw Oreo
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Since Oreos are milks favorite cookie, theyre probably Santas favorite cookie, too. These homemade sandwiches are raw, gluten-free, and dairy-free. Pro tip: you definitely need a food processor to whip up these allergen-free bad boys.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           41.
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.food.com/recipe/healthy-oatmeal-raisin-spice-cookies-1-ww-point-282704" target="_blank">
            Oatmeal-Raisin Spice Cookie
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Here is the classic oatmeal raisin cookie with some holiday spice! Applesauce instead of butter, whole-wheat flour, and oats make this an A+ cookie.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           42.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://offbeatandinspired.com/2013/05/04/clean-eating-gluten-free-lemon-almond-butter-cookies-easy-homemade-almond-butter/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Gluten-Free Lemon Almond Butter Cookie
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Got an almond butter lover in the house? These totally DIY treats are bound to be a major hit. Grind up some almonds, then mix the butter with coconut flour, lemon zest, brown sugar, and vanilla. Theyre simple,
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/62-healthier-gluten-free-desserts" target="_blank">
           gluten-free
          </a>
          , and guaranteed to be gone in less than an hour.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           43.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://lifestooshorttoskipdessert.blogspot.com/2013/08/zucchini-chocolate-chip-oatmeal-coconut.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Zucchini Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Coconut Cookie
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Old-fashioned rolled oats, coconut flakes, and (obviously) zucchini give these bite-sized treats plenty of flavor and texture. Vanilla, chocolate chips, and brown sugar make them taste holiday-worthy.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
           <img alt="Quinoa and Almond Flour Cookies" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/healthy-almond-quinoa-cookies.jpg?itok=Ncm8i_Zr" title="Photo: Farm on Plate"/>
           <span class="caption">
            Photo: Farm on Plate
           </span>
          </span>
         </h4>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           44.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.farmonplate.com/2013/11/25/healthy-cookies-quinoa-almond-cookies/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Quinoa and Almond Flour Cookie with Chai Spiced Almond Butter
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          The name might be a mouthful, but this recipe is actually pretty straightforward. The cookies are made with quinoa and almond flour, so theyre safe for gluten-free friends. Make them into sandwich cookies by adding the spicy
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-alternatives-peanut-butter" target="_blank">
           almond butter
          </a>
          or eat em plain.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           45.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://arismenu.com/salted-caramel-mocha-cookies/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Salted Caramel Mocha Cookie
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          If the words salted caramel mocha dont have you running to the kitchen, you might not be human. With whole-wheat flour,
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/surprising-ways-use-leftover-apples" target="_blank">
           apple butter
          </a>
          , and plenty of antioxidant-rich dark chocolate, these treats are a relatively healthy way to indulge your sweet tooth.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           46.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.averiecooks.com/2013/02/peanut-butter-and-jelly-coconut-cashew-sandwich-cookies.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Peanut Butter and Jelly Coconut Cashew Sandwich Cookie
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Everyones favorite elementary school lunch gets dessert-ified in these fun, no-bake cookies. Make a basic bar cookie from ground nuts and coconut, then chill the dough and slice to form bread. Fill the mini PB&amp;Js with your favorite nut butter and jam.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           47.
          </strong>
          <a href="https://www.google.com/url?q=http://greatist.com/health/chocolate-almond-biscotti-recipe&amp;sa=D&amp;usg=ALhdy29Wr65UeuOHFmIqk8UoAVJfYIzxnw" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Chocolate Covered Almond Biscotti
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Filled with almonds, olive oil, and cinnamon and drizzled with dark chocolate, these crunchy cookies are perfect for an afternoon coffee break. Sub in whole-wheat flour to add some fiber.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           48.
          </strong>
          <a href="http://foodsforthesoul.net/2013/06/triple-threat-peanut-butter-cookies/" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           <strong>
            Triple Threat Peanut Butter Cookie
           </strong>
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          There are peanut butter cookies, and then there are
          <em>
           peanut butter cookies.
          </em>
          These treats, which are made with powdered peanut butter (such as PB2),
          <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-peanut-butter-recipes" target="_blank">
           creamy peanut butter
          </a>
          ,
          <em>
           and
          </em>
          peanut butter chips, definitely fall in the latter category.
