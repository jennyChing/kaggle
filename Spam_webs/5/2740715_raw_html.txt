         Elvis on a Rocky Road Bundt Cake Recipe ~ #BundtBakers

        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="18435518" data-link="http://www.itsyummi.com/rocky-road-bundt-cake-recipe/" data-summary="This isn't your mama's bundt cake recipe!         <p>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          This isnt your mamas bundt cake recipe!
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Its a wild and wonderful combination of Elvis Presleys favorite sandwich flavors, banana and peanut butter, with the rocky road ingredients of chocolate, marshmallows, and nuts. Grab your apron (and maybe your blue suede shoes) and come on into the kitchen and well bake up my Elvis on a Rocky Road bundt cake!
        </p>
        <div class="pw-widget pw-size-large">
         <a class="pw-button-pinterest">
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <script src="http://i.po.st/static/v3/post-%0D%0A%0D%0Awidget.js#publisherKey=3mun8vgmdjn6j41ipj6&amp;retina=true" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
         <a href="http://cdn2.itsyummi.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/rocky-road-bundt-image.jpg">
          <img alt="This Elvis on a Rocky Road bundt cake recipe will blow your mind! Peanut butter and bananas are combined with traditional rocky road flavors to create this delicious cake! #BundtBakers" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7510" height="1122" src="http://cdn0.itsyummi.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/rocky-road-bundt-image-718x1122.jpg" width="718"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Did you notice the #BundtBakers hash tag in the title of my post? Its there because Ive joined some super talented bloggers in a group called Bundt Bakers, and this is my first month participating with them.
        </p>
        <p>
         Every month, someone in the group is in charge of hosting the event and creating the topic and/or ingredient that everyones bundt cake recipe needs to be based on. This month,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://bakinginpyjamas.com/" target="_blank">
           Laura from Baking in Pyjamas
          </a>
         </strong>
         is our host. She selected our theme to be centered aroundRocky Road cookies, chocolate, marshmallow, and nuts.
        </p>
        <div class="pw-widget pw-size-large">
         <a class="pw-button-pinterest">
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <script src="http://i.po.st/static/v3/post-%0D%0A%0D%0Awidget.js#publisherKey=3mun8vgmdjn6j41ipj6&amp;retina=true" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
         <a href="http://cdn1.itsyummi.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/rocky-road-bundt-plate.jpg">
          <img alt="This Elvis on a Rocky Road bundt cake recipe will blow your mind! Peanut butter and bananas are combined with traditional rocky road flavors to create this delicious cake! #BundtBakers" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7512" height="718" src="http://cdn0.itsyummi.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/rocky-road-bundt-plate-718x718.jpg" width="718"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         As I looked through my older blog posts, I came across delicious bundt cake recipes that Ive already made. My favorites are the
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.itsyummi.com/double-chocolate-bundt-cake/">
           double chocolate
          </a>
         </strong>
         , a
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.itsyummi.com/pomegranate-lime-bundt-cake-recipe/">
           pomegranate lime
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.itsyummi.com/mini-creamsicle-bundt-cake/">
           creamsicle
          </a>
         </strong>
         flavors.
        </p>
        <p>
         The fun part of this group is that were all allowed to put our own unique twist on the theme, so as long as some of the rocky road ingredients were used, we were free to get creative. I took that invitation and ran with it!
        </p>
        <div class="pw-widget pw-size-large">
         <a class="pw-button-pinterest">
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <script src="http://i.po.st/static/v3/post-%0D%0A%0D%0Awidget.js#publisherKey=3mun8vgmdjn6j41ipj6&amp;retina=true" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
         <a href="http://cdn0.itsyummi.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/rocky-road-bundt-photo.jpg">
          <img alt="This Elvis on a Rocky Road bundt cake recipe will blow your mind! Peanut butter and bananas are combined with traditional rocky road flavors to create this delicious cake! #BundtBakers" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-7511" height="1135" src="http://cdn0.itsyummi.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/rocky-road-bundt-photo-718x1135.jpg" width="718"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I am a huge fan of peanut butter and banana flavors in cakes and muffins. Those were also the ingredients in Elvis Presleys favorite sandwich, so I thought Id combine them with the rocky road ingredients and see what kind of deliciousness I could bake up.
        </p>
        <p>
         Th only trouble that I had in creating this bundt was with the marshmallow sauce. I dont know why, exactly, but I couldnt get it to thicken up. Thats why you see mini marshmallows on top instead of a ribbon of glossy white marshmallow glaze.
         <br/>
         Ive made it successfully before, so I decided to leave it in the recipe for you. If youd rather not use it, you can join me in putting a marshmallow parade on top of the cake instead.
        </p>
        <h2>
         I think youre going to
         <strong>
          love
         </strong>
         this bundt cake recipe!

           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place sugar, peanut butter, and oil in the bowl of a hand or stand mixer. Beat on medium speed until combined, then add eggs, one at a time, mixing until combined. Turn to low speed and add mashed bananas, mixing until combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Sift together dry ingredients and mix half into banana mixture then add half of the milk, and repeat with remaining flour and milk. Gently mix or fold in chocolate chips and nuts.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pour batter into well greased and floured bundt pan. Bake for 50-55 minutes, until the internal temperature of the cake reaches 205 - 210 F, or a toothpick inserted in the center
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            of cake comes out clean or with just a few crumbs.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove pan from oven and cool cake in pan for 15 minutes before removing and transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Once cool, drizzle with chocolate ganache, peanut butter glaze, and marshmallow sauce.
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <br/>
            CHOCOLATE GANACHE
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place chocolate in a medium heatproof bowl; set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place cream in a small saucepan over medium heat and bring to a simmer (tiny bubbles around the edge of pan. (Don't let the cream come to a boil.) Pour hot cream over chocolate and let it sit until chocolate has softened, about 5 minutes. Add butter and stir until smooth. Let cool slightly before pouring or spreading over the cake.
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <br/>
            PEANUT BUTTER GLAZE
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine the peanut butter and icing sugar. Add a few drops of milk at a time until the glaze reaches a thick but pour-able consistency. Drizzle the glaze over the chocolate ganache.
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <br/>
            MARSHMALLOW SAUCE
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat marshmallows and cream in top bowl of a double boiler, folding and stirring until marshmallows are almost melted.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add vanilla and continue cooking and stirring until completely melted and well blended with cream.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Use while sauce is hot, as it will thicken as it cools.
           </li>
          </ol>

           </strong>
