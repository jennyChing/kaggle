         <div class="no-hover box content">
          <p class="lead">
           Black suit, black tie, white shirt and possibly dark sunglasses:
the American rapper's new style
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           American rapper
           <strong>
            <a href="/en/vogue-starscelebsmodels/star-news/2010/06/jessica-white" title="kanye west">
             Kenye West
            </a>
           </strong>
           started a movement
called
           <em>
            <strong>
             Rosewood
            </strong>
           </em>
           , whose suggestion is to
dress up each time of the day -
           <strong>
            like for important
occasions
           </strong>
           - which was synthesized in the sentence:
"Rosewood movement, suits all day".
           <strong>
            Black suit, black tie,
white shirt and possibly dark sunglasses
           </strong>
           , in the style of
           <em>
            Man in Black
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           f you have a look at the pictures of the group, it seems like
seeing a comeback of the Blues Brothers, without one detail:
Rosewood movement allows members to go in public without a hat. The
name they chose for the movement refers to a town in
Floridathat was destroyed in 1923 by an arson which started
after a fight between black and white citizens.
           <strong>
            A very
strong civil meaning behind this classic and elegant choice, which
catches the eye.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Rumors say that the initiative might be an
           <strong>
            intro for
Kenye's new album
           </strong>
           - soon in stores. We are all anxious to
discover the rapper's next step. Stay tuned.
          </p>
