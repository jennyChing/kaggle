               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial;">
                 The Main Drain is an adjustable urinal that attaches
to your toilet, it can be used without needing to lift the toilet seat!
                 <o:p>
                 </o:p>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-5m7MTCmkNMY/VNPI9hfXfNI/AAAAAAAAESc/jdpAHHm9SSg/s1600/1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">
                 The Main Drain is an adjustable, lightweight urinal
that attaches directly to the bowl of your toilet. It solves all your problems
about the traditional toilet set-up, but without having the need to install a
wall mounted urinal.
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">
                 How it Works
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DVc2SCEY_y4/VNPJBpyKhUI/AAAAAAAAESk/IwvJFNwe5Yg/s1600/2.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="360" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DVc2SCEY_y4/VNPJBpyKhUI/AAAAAAAAESk/IwvJFNwe5Yg/s1600/2.gif" width="640"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">
                 Liquid flows from the Main Drain head, down the hose,
through the toilet bowl clip, and into the bowl! All of this without having to
lift the seat!
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">

                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-exhA1yKDE_k/VNPKK8CaUdI/AAAAAAAAESw/7ieu8bqJmdk/s1600/3.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="360" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-exhA1yKDE_k/VNPKK8CaUdI/AAAAAAAAESw/7ieu8bqJmdk/s1600/3.gif" width="640"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">
                 Simply press the Main Drain Clip over the top of the
toilet seat until you feel the clip spring into place under the lip of the
bowl. Thats it. Its now ready for use!
                </span>
               </div>

                <span style="font-family: Arial;">

                </span>

                 <img border="0" height="360" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-s2YAAJD8Q-Q/VNPKMvV_TVI/AAAAAAAAES4/L_KRKTQhukE/s1600/4.gif" width="640"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: Arial;">

Drain follows suit. All thats needed is a few sprays of your favorite bathroom
cleaning product into the head of the Main Drain and gravity will do the rest!
No scrubbing, detaching, or flushing is required.
