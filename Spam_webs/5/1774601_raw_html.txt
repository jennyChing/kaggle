       <p>
        Heres another good one for the 4th of July or if you just have some fresh berries lying around:
        <a href="http://www.recipegirl.com/2007/05/31/red-white-and-blueberry-cheesecake-bars/">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           Red, White and Blueberry Cheesecake Bars
          </span>
         </strong>
        </a>
        .
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.recipegirl.com/2009/06/25/4th-of-july-recipes-red-white-and-blueberry-cheesecake-bars/cheesecake4/" rel="attachment wp-att-5257">
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16478" height="368" src="http://www.recipegirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/Cheesecake4.jpg" title="Cheesecake4" width="550"/>
         <br/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        Believe it or not, this is a lighter fat recipe! Yay for still getting to enjoy treats when you have to diet on the 4th of July  200 calories and 5 Weight Watcher points for a bar. Not too bad.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.recipegirl.com/2009/06/25/4th-of-july-recipes-red-white-and-blueberry-cheesecake-bars/cheesecake1/" rel="attachment wp-att-5254">
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16479" height="413" src="http://www.recipegirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/Cheesecake1.jpg" title="Cheesecake1" width="550"/>
         <br/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        You can be flexible with the berries blueberry, raspberry, blackberry (I wouldnt do strawberry). Throw in whatever is fresh and on sale.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.recipegirl.com/2009/06/25/4th-of-july-recipes-red-white-and-blueberry-cheesecake-bars/cheesecake2/" rel="attachment wp-att-5255">
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16480" height="413" src="http://www.recipegirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/Cheesecake2.jpg" title="Cheesecake2" width="550"/>
         <br/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        A foil-lined pan means you just let it cool and then lift it out gently and slice. Easy!
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.recipegirl.com/2009/06/25/4th-of-july-recipes-red-white-and-blueberry-cheesecake-bars/cheesecake3/" rel="attachment wp-att-5256">
         <br/>
        </a>
