         Chocolate Peanut Butter No-Bake Bars
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-03-07T11:33:22+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">

         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

        <h5 style="text-align: center;">
         A match made in heaven comes together in these simple no-bake bars packed full of peanut butter and chocolate!
        </h5>
        <p>
         <a href="http://momstestkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Chocolate-Peanut-Butter-No-Bake-Bars-Labeled2.png">
          <img alt="Chocolate Peanut Butter No-Bake Bars" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2401" height="925" src="http://momstestkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Chocolate-Peanut-Butter-No-Bake-Bars-Labeled2.png" width="694"/>
         </a>
         Today Im hanging out over on Pinch of DIY sharing the recipe for these delicious no-bake bars. Made with a simple crust of mixed cereals &amp; then topped with a pudding layer followed by a peanut butter frosting, they are seriously delicious and addicting. I ate one bar &amp; then sent the rest to work with my husband this morning because I knew if they stayed here in the house, Id eat the whole pan!
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://momstestkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Chocolate-Peanut-Butter-No-Bake-Bars6.png">
          <img alt="Chocolate Peanut Butter No-Bake Bars" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2402" height="925" src="http://momstestkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Chocolate-Peanut-Butter-No-Bake-Bars6.png" width="694"/>
         </a>
         So head over to Pinch of DIY and get the recipe for these
         <a href="http://pinchofdiy.com/2014/03/07/no-bake-bars/" target="_blank">
          Chocolate Peanut Butter No-Bake Bars
         </a>
         !
        </h4>
        <p>
        </p>
