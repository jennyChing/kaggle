             <p>
              Its been 20 long years since we left left Harry (
              <strong>
               Jeff Daniels
              </strong>
              ) and Lloyd (
              <strong>
               Jim Carrey
              </strong>
              ) on the road out of town. They return under direction from the
              <strong>
               Farrelly Brothers
              </strong>
              in a new adventure,
              <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2096672/?ref_=nv_sr_1">
               <em>
                Dumb and Dumber To
               </em>
              </a>
              . When Harry finds out he has a daughter, he and Lloyd embark on a trip to find her. Fraida Felcher (
              <strong>
               Kathleen Turner
              </strong>
              ) is the mystery childs mother, making the news all the more surprising.
              <strong>
               Jim Carrey
              </strong>
              and
              <strong>
               Jeff Daniels
              </strong>
              have had their own successes since the original came out in 1994 and Im hoping that their willingness to return is a sign of a quality script. Id hate to see a lazy shot at a comedy sequel based on nostalgia. The trailer seems promising to say the least; check it out below.
             </p>
             <p>
              <script src="http://www.springboardplatform.com/js/overlay">
              </script>
              <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" id="fiin001_953581" scrolling="no" src="http://cms.springboardplatform.com/embed_iframe/3811/video/953581/fiin001/filminquiry.com/10" width="560">
              </iframe>
             </p>
             <h2>
              Harry Dunne and Lloyd Christmas Return
             </h2>
             <p>
              The first scene is almost disturbing. A 20-year joke, pretending to be catatonic just to say, Got ya. The boys are back in full force. There are a few callbacks to the original movie. The infamous sheep-dog van makes its return, tearing down the road. We also see them sharing transport built for one and a return to the old apartment. Its a dash of the familiar with a helping of new material. Whos the guy in camouflage blending in with the vending machine? So random. It seems like the
              <strong>
               Farrelly Brothers
              </strong>
              really swung for the fences on this one.
             </p>
             <p>
              Check out one of the stills from the movie.
             </p>
             <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_6373" style="width: 602px">
              <a href="http://i1.wp.com/filminquiry.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/fcZi7vt-e1403991431483.jpg">
               <img alt="source: Universal Pictures" class="size-full wp-image-6373" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/filminquiry.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/fcZi7vt-e1403991431483.jpg?resize=600%2C300"/>
              </a>
              <p class="wp-caption-text">
               source: Universal Pictures
              </p>
             </div>
             <p>
              I love the original its a classically quotable film that never quite shows you all its cards. I really hope this isnt a cash grab. Its going to be nearly impossible to live up to the first outing. We have about 5 months to find out what theyve come up with.
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Dumb and Dumber To
              </em>
              will be released November 14th in the US and December 19th in the UK. For any other release dates, look
              <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2096672/releaseinfo?ref_=tt_dt_dt" target="_blank">
               here
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <strong>
                I cant wait to see it but Im keeping my expectations in check. So what do you think? Is there hope for such a long-term sequel to still be funny? Will we be tickled or just plain ticked? Will you be there opening night?
               </strong>
              </em>
             </p>
