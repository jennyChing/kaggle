       <p>
        <a href="http://www.mommytravels.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Disney-Manicure-and-Pedicure-Ideas.jpg">
         <img alt="Disney Manicure and Pedicure Ideas" class="aligncenter wp-image-3395" data-id="3395" data-wp-pid="3395" height="396" src="http://www.mommytravels.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Disney-Manicure-and-Pedicure-Ideas.jpg" width="771"/>

        <br/>

        <a href="http://www.mommytravels.net/2014/01/walt-disney-world-guide/" target="_blank" title="Walt Disney World Guide">

        is getting a Disneythemedmanicure and pedicure.
       </p>
       <p>
        I had considered doing this before, but for whatever reason never got around to it. This year I finally got my act together and managed to get mine and my daughters nails done before we went to Disney World. Mainly because once I started looking at designs on
        <a href="http://www.Pinterest.com/meaganshamy" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.Pinterest.com/meaganshamy', ' Pinterest']);" target="_blank">

        I just couldnt resist.
       </p>
       <p>
        The gal I go to for shellac manicures came up with my Minnie Mouse nail design. She painted on the bows and then added a little crystal in the middle.
       </p>
