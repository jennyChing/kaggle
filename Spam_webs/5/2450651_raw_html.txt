          Weve recently launched a gorgeous bouquet of rather
          <strong>
           unusual
           <a href="http://www.interflora.co.uk/category/roses/">
            roses
           </a>
          </strong>
          and were giving you the chance to win a vase full of them!
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.interflora.co.uk/product/rainbow-roses-vase/">
            Rainbow Roses
           </a>
           Vase
          </strong>
          contains 12 roses with petals of yellow, blue, green, purple and pink. Its the perfect gift for any celebration, but wed love to know who youd give this bouquet to.
          <span id="more-31431">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Its a very unique gift and each petal of each rose has been dyed in different colours to give the rainbow effect. These vibrant flowers are guaranteed to put a smile on your loved ones face and will certainly
          <strong>
           brighten up any room.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-31435" height="410" src="http://blog.interflora.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/C01121MSv-550x733.jpg" title="C01121MSv" width="308"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          To win a striking Rainbow Roses Vase all you have to do is leave a comment
          <strong>
           telling us who youd give it to and why.
          </strong>
          Remember, the personality of the person you suggest should reflect the vibrancy of this bouquet!
         </p>
         <p>
          Be creative with your answers and
          <strong>
           make us laugh!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The competition ends on Thursday 29th March, so get your comments in before 5pm on that day. Well then
          <strong>
           choose a winner
          </strong>
          and email them to arrange delivery of their colourful prize.
         </p>
         <p>
          Good luck!
         </p>

           Any rude or offensive entries will not be accepted.
         </p>
         <p>
           Only one entry per person is allowed.
         </p>
         <p>
           Only UK residents are eligible to enter this competition and prizes can only be delivered to a UK mainland address.
         </p>
         <p>
           The prize is as follows: 1 x Rainbow Roses Vase with next day delivery (44.99 value)
         </p>
         <p>
           To enter, comment on this blog post with the following information: who you think would enjoy receiving these flowers and why.
         </p>
         <p>
           Entries received after the closing date will not be entered into the competition. The winner will be chosen by impartial vote. Entry closes at 5pm Thursday 29th March. The winner will be selected and contacted by email before 5pm on Friday 30th March.
