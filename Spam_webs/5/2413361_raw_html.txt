      <h2>
       Stupid times require stupid solutions, says Romney
      </h2>
      <div class="date">
       27-09-12
      </div>
      <div class="introtext">
       <p>
        MITT Romney has relaunched his campaign with a direct appeal to Americas fundamental stupidity.
       </p>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="fullstory">
      <p class="no-space">
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_36040" style="width: 260px">
       <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-36040" height="400" src="//www.thedailymash.co.uk/images/stories/mittromney250.jpg" title="" width="250"/>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        If God wanted Mitt to be clever He'd have made him that way
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       A new television advert shows the Republican candidate frowning at a quick crossword, laughing hysterically at Charlie Sheens new sitcom
       <em>
        Anger Management
       </em>
       and reaffirming his Mormonism.
      </p>
      <p>
       Meanwhile, large-print newspaper ads will carry slogans including Mitt Do White House Good and Free Pizza.
      </p>
      <p>
       Speaking into a switched-off microphone in the wrong room of a conference centre in Canada, Romney said: No other candidate feel your dumbness like I be.
      </p>
      <p>
       These are stupid times, with stupid problems that need stupid solutions from a stupid man.
      </p>
      <p>
       I will do the stuff you want, whatever it is you want. Even if you dont know what you want, and I dont know either.
      </p>
      <p>
       Romney then boarded a flight to Greenland which was forced to make an emergency landing after the candidate attempted to remove one of the windows with a spoon.
      </p>
      <p>
