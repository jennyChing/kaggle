       <h1>
        Digital Experts Academy
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Create The Life You Want
       </h2>
       <p>
        Many people would welcome an opportunity to change and improve their lifestyles, whether the life changing impact is on their their personal finances and their freedom. They yearn for a powerful position where they have more time to do the things they really like and have a large amount of free time impossible for someone tied to traditional means of earning a living would enjoy.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left">
        <a href="http://tidyurl.com/uym3or">
         <img alt="digital experts academy, digital marketing training, create a digital life, digital lifestyle, digital life, the six figure mentors" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4027" height="795" src="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/files/2014/02/DEA-life.jpg" width="1411"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        I am talking about the ability to run your affiliate business virtually anywhere in the world. Simply having to work anytime you want and live anywhere in the world you like. This wish for many is really possible with the
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/digital-experts-academy/" title="Digital Experts Academy">
         Digital Experts Academy
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/the-six-figure-mentors/" title="The Six Figure Mentors">
         The Six Figure Mentors
        </a>
        . Ordinary people are taught and empowered to change their limiting traits.They are given
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/digitalbusinesslounge/personal-development-2/" title="personal development">
         personal development
        </a>
        resources to adjust their traditional approaches of doing things and transition to their ideal digital life. People wanting a change start to realize the difference of trading their talents for time and embrace new ways of growth and
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/digitalbusinesslounge/self-improvement/" title="self improvement">
         self improvement
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Become An Affiliate With The DEA
       </h2>
       <p>
        The abundance of resources and
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/%postname%/digital-business/" title="digital business">
         digital business
        </a>
        strategies presents budding entrepreneurs with rare opportunities to become successful affiliates. At the DEA many people have been given informed assistance to make a change in their lives. It has opened peoples eyes to a whole lot of unlimited possibilities. With thedigital life you design the
        <a href="http://tidyurl.com/digitalrevenue" target="_blank">
         Digital Experts Academy
        </a>
        enables its resellers to become self sufficient and help them pursue their lifestyle goals. The DEA delivers skills that empower ordinary people to achieve their financial freedomand help them improve the quality of their lives.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Having a legal
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/%postname%/digital-business-system/" title="digital business system">
         digital business system
        </a>
        teaching you skills for growing your personal finances and not having to worry about working into retirement. Through the power of the internet, leverage of digital media and application of
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/digitalbusinesslounge/digital-marketing-training/" title="digital marketing training">
         digital marketing training
        </a>
        anyone who would like to change their lives and would like to follow the instruction of leading digital business professionals and commit to building something special for them and their families.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center">
        <a href="http://thesfm.com/regdon70/?pg=entrepreneurs-unleash&amp;ty=bootcampday1&amp;t=DEA25%2F2%2F14" target="_blank">

       <h3 style="text-align: center">
        THE DIGITAL EXPERTS ACADEMY
       </h3>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center">
        <a href="http://tidyurl.com/gd7ypl">
         <img alt="digital business system, digital business startegies the digital experts academy, digital experts, digital marketing training, digital business system, create your digital life, digital lifestyle, financial freedom " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4029" height="130" src="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/files/2014/02/images-3.jpg" width="389"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        An exclusive private community of people with a common goal of building a digital life. A project of passion born from the vision of Stuart Ross and Jay Kubassek to teach adaptable digital entrepreneurs create their digital life.
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/digital-experts-academy/" target="_blank">
         The Digital Experts Academy
        </a>
        is a vehicle for moving people from the traditional to the digital economy. I understand a digital life as a life of fulfilment where people enjoy doing what they do, live the way they like and are excited about the type of work they do. Its really possible to live an affluent life with the resources at the
        <a href="http://digitalbloggers.com/regdon70/digitalbusinesslounge/digital-experts-academy/" title="Digital Experts Academy">
         Digital Experts Academy
        </a>
        .
       </p>
