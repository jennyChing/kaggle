        <p style="text-align: left;">
         As I dilly dally around the house on Saturday mornings, especially on the weekends that my children are at their dads, I like to have the television on. But there is a serious lack of quality television that I actually want to watch! I want a show that motivates me to get my house clean, gives me tips on sprucing up spaces that need a face-lift, shows that give me ideas for dinner all week. A few weeks ago I started hearing about a show on OWN that supposedly had all I was looking for. Since it was exploding on social media, I had to check it out!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.homemadesimpletv.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Home Made Simple airs on OWN at9am ET/ 8am CT on Saturday mornings
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.homemadesimpletv.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Home Made Simple on OWN" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43097" height="232" src="http://www.adayinmotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/homemadesimple.png" width="394"/>
          <span id="more-43096">
          </span>
         </a>
         <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        </p>

        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I have seen bits and pieces of a lot of episodes. When my kids are here it is harder and when they are gone, I am multi tasking. But there are clips that stick in my mind that I most definitely want to try when I can get to it.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Like turning regular tables into beautiful show pieces with mirrors and a fresh coat of paint! But my favorite clips are the ones where thy make over an entire room. Once in particular stands out as they worked together to redo a special needs childs bedroom and turn it into a coastal dream space!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Hosted by Soleil Mon Frye  she has so gone beyond the Punky years  the show introduces ideas for the home, garden, kitchen and more! There are even fabulous tips for organization and cleaning. I, for one, need all the help I can get in that area!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.homemadesimpletv.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Home Made Simple on OWN" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43098" height="401" src="http://www.adayinmotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/homemadesimple1.png" width="311"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I love this show. It is fresh and easy to follow and has awesome ideas I would have never come up with on my own!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Have you seen Home Made Simple yet? Set your DVR and dont miss this one! I really think you will love it!
