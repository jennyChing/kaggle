    <section class="e-content entry-content clearfix post" id="post_content">
     <div class="content">
      <div class="post_pagination top-pagination">
       <a href="http://wegotthiscovered.com/movies/nazis-thrills-wolves-the-13-best-horror-movies-of-2014/2/">
        <span class="next" onclick="_gaq.push([ '_trackEvent', 'TopNext', 'clickEvent', 'Clicked TopNext']);">

        </span>
       </a>
      </div>
      <!-- paged: 1 - numpages: 11 -->
      <p>
       <img alt="Top13Horror2014" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-400671" height="350" src="http://cdn.wegotthiscovered.com/wp-content/uploads/Top13Horror2014.jpg" width="640"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       While reflecting on an entire years worth of horror movies may sound like a tremendous chore, theres no better feeling than reliving some of your favorite cinematic moments after the dust has settled and no more films remain unreleased. Sure, sifting through the worst of the worst may be not be an enviable task  which I did for my
       <a href="http://wegotthiscovered.com/movies/holy-shark-13-worst-horror-movies-of-2014/">
        Worst Horror Movies Of 2014
       </a>
       feature already  but my Best Of workis where the movie Gods show their mercy. This is my exploration of the best horror had to offer this year, and my chance to tip my hat towards those horror films that remind me why a genre full of murder, mayhem, and spooky horrors provides the highest highs that Hollywood can provide.
      </p>
      <p>
       Before I get to the main list, I do want to give a few shout-outs to those films that deserve an honorable mention, even if they arent in my top thirteen. Movies like
       <em>
        Exists
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        Extraterrestrial
       </em>
       achievedsuccess through found footage formatting that dealt with their respective creatures without holding anything back, where
       <em>
        Willow Creek
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        The Houses October Built
       </em>
       did so by dialling the found footage method downwith a little more realism.
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Nurse 3D
       </em>
       is another 2014 surprise about a psychotic medical assistant who moonlights as a killer, showcasing Paz De La Huertas sexy black-widow side, which is almost as surprising as my love for
       <em>
        Almost Human
       </em>
       given how alien flicks arent exactly my favorite. Kudos to
       <em>
        The Den
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        The Banshee Chapter
       </em>
       for sufficiently creeping me out as well, rounding out the films that barely missed a numbered ranking from yours truly.
      </p>
      <p>
       This year didnt really find a tremendous amount of scares thanks to the failure of movies like
       <em>
        Annabelle
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        Ouija
       </em>
       , but 2014 was an absolute BLAST as far as horror comedies are concerned. Between a slasher musical, the latest Troma release, an anthology horror sequel, and Kevin Smiths podcast-turned-movie, silliness reigned supreme as far as horror was concerned. function setCookie(cname, cvalue, exdays) {
	var d = new Date();
	d.setTime(d.getTime() + (exdays*24*60*60*1000));
	var expires = "expires="+d.toUTCString();
	document.cookie = cname + "=" + cvalue + "; " + expires + "; path=/";
}

function getCookie(cname) {
	var name = cname + "=";
	var ca = document.cookie.split(';');
	for(var i=0; i&lt;ca.length; i++) {
		var c = ca[i];
		while (c.charAt(0)==' ') c = c.substring(1);
		if (c.indexOf(name) != -1) return c.substring(name.length,c.length);
	}
	return "";
}
