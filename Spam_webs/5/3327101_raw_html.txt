       <div itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         Yowzas! Talk about provocative. What you see above is the concept art for the upcoming Lionsgate feature
         <b>
          <i>
           Nurse 3D
          </i>
         </b>
         . The film stars
         <b>
          Paz De La Huerta
         </b>
         (
         <i>
          Boardwalk Empire, Enter the Void
         </i>
         ) as, well, a naughty nurse. The film is said to be inspired by photography from the above image's creator Tim Palen - who just hapens to be the Chief Marketing Officer at Lionsgate. Directed by Douglas Aarniokoski, the film begins shooting this September in Toronto for a release next year.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Not much is known about the script - and I don't know if I can remember a film based on photography ever wowing - but if the film is able to capture the intensity of the photo, they just might be on to something.
