         <a href="https://blog.trippeo.com/introducing-business-dashboard/" rel="bookmark">
          Sneak Peek Into Trippeos Web Dashboard
         </a>
        </h1>
        <h2 class="sub-title">
         What we're doing to change the way you think about expenses
        </h2>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="author-details">
        <ul>
         <li class="avatar-circle">
          <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-40 photo" height="40" src="https://0.gravatar.com/avatar/31facbbd075dd7886f5e0a7236469a1c?s=40&amp;d=blank&amp;r=g" srcset="https://0.gravatar.com/avatar/31facbbd075dd7886f5e0a7236469a1c?s=80&amp;d=blank&amp;r=g 2x" width="40"/>
         </li>
         <li class="author-stamp">
          <a href="https://blog.trippeo.com/author/pallian/" rel="author" title="Posts by Adarsh Pallian">
           Adarsh Pallian
          </a>
           7 months, 28 days ago
         </li>
        </ul>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         It has been a busy few months here at
         <a href="http://instagram.com/trippeohq" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://instagram.com/trippeohq', 'Trippeo HQ');" target="_blank">
          Trippeo HQ
         </a>
         and were excited to be hitting another milestone with the release of our web dashboard for businesses.
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve analyzed the entire expense management process to find where we can save you time, and were setting out to do it better than its ever been done before.
         <span id="more-637">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Across conversations with accountants in a variety of industries, a recurring story came up  that an expense solution had been purchased and implemented, yet the employees arent using it after the initial introduction, usually due to complicated workflows and/or frustrations with the UI.
        </p>
        <p>
         In Trippeos web dashboard, the first thing we did was strip out all the clutter and redundancies that are common in most other solutions. We focused on simplicity and ease of use and we hope that this will have a positive impact for the entire organization. Trippeo makes it easy for administrators to set-up their teams, company policies and of course, actual expense usage is seamlessly intuitive. Just because expense reporting has traditionally been a tedious and boring task doesnt mean it has to remain that way.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Expense Solutions Should Be Saving The Entire Company Time
        </h2>
        <p>
         Trippeos ultimate time-saving answer:
         <em>
          Ambient Automation
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         By this, were referring to the technology weve built to do everything for you automatically in the background. You can carry on with your meetings, work, and business without having to take time out of your day to complete your expense tasks. We dont even want you to be thinking about it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Ambient automation starts with the expense entry workflow. Connecting your credit card to Trippeo automatically pulls your transactions in as they happen, auto-categorizing them and organizing them in your account until youre ready to create your report. And we even took it a step further with Trippeo Events  now you can sync Trippeo with your Google Calendar, so that when your business trip to London starts on Tuesday and ends on Friday, Trippeo knows to automatically group together all expenses incurred during that time. When youre finished up with your trip, you already have an expense report ready to go with all of your relevant transactions, just waiting for you to tap and send over to your manager for approval.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats ambient automation in action, and we think youre going to love Trippeo Events  youll never have to spend more than 1 minute on an expense report ever again.
        </p>
        <h2>
         End-to-End Expense Solution
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you look at existing expense solutions youll likely see that the process within that system ends at the approval of the report. The next step, reimbursement, needs to be sent over to another system (usually the accounting software) for processing and payment.
        </p>
        <p>
         This process works just fine, but we think it could be better. Would it not be easier for employees and accountants if reimbursement could take place automatically? Rather than spend the administrative time to manually process and issue the reimbursement, what if the accounting team could set rules so that certain pre-approved employees who follow policy guidelines could be set up with automatic reimbursement that could happen overnight?
        </p>
        <p>
         I know what youre thinking, and rest assured there is absolutely no control being relinquished. With Trippeos fully customizable policies, workflows, and data analytics, administrators still retain oversight over all funds and can determine exactly how the reimbursement policy is set.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over the next few days, were opening up the web dashboard to everyone and we invite you to give it a try and let us know what you like, dislike and what we can improve on. We value every single feedback and we encourage you to get in touch and let us know your thoughts  you can even call us directly at 1-844-TRIPPEO and Ill personally pick up the phone to chat.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://trippeo.com" target="_blank">
          Sign up for access to the dashboard on our homepage
         </a>
         here and well let you know when your account is ready.
        </p>
