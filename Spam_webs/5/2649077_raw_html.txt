      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span itemprop="name">
        Mayor of London Low Carbon Entrepreneur 15,000 Prize 2014
       </span>
      </h1>
      <p class="post-meta">
       <span>
        June 12, 2014
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <div class="icx-toolbar-align-right">
        <script type="text/javascript">
         var icx_publication_id = 14236;
var icx_content_id = 24126;

       <p>
        How do you crack the age-old problem of making sure all the office lights are switched off at the end of the day, without affecting those who are working late? What about helping small scale electricity generators come together to set a price to feed back into the grid? Or even providing environmentally-friendly charging points, when you are out of the office and desperately need to charge your phone?
       </p>
       <p>
        These were just some of the problems that entrants sought to solve as part of the Mayor of Londons Low Carbon Entrepreneur prize 2014, the winners of which were announced at the Crystal on 4 June 2014, reports
        <a href="http://www.siemens.co.uk/en/news_press/press_releases/index.php?tab=2&amp;date-1-dd=03&amp;date-1-mm=04&amp;date-1=2014&amp;date-2-dd=07&amp;date-2-mm=06&amp;date-2=2014&amp;search=Searchterm&amp;division_replaced=WT&amp;division=E%2C+RN%2C+SG%2C+Sustainability%2C+WP%2C+WT" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.siemens.co.uk/en/news_press/press_releases/index.php?tab=2&amp;date-1-dd=03&amp;date-1-mm=04&amp;date-1=2014&amp;date-2-dd=07&amp;date-2-mm=06&amp;date-2=2014&amp;search=Searchterm&amp;division_replaced=WT&amp;division=E%2C+RN%2C+SG%2C+Sustainability%2C+WP%2C+WT', 'Siemens');" target="_blank">
         Siemens
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Mayor of London, Zac Goldsmith &amp; Richard Reed Hand Over15,000 Prize to Student Eco-Entrepreneur
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://1sun4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Kids-mayor-of-london-low-carbon-entrepreneur-prize-20141.jpg">
         <img alt="Kids mayor of London low-carbon-entrepreneur-prize-2014" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24223" height="433" src="//1sun4all.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Kids-mayor-of-london-low-carbon-entrepreneur-prize-20141.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <em>
         The Mayor of London with prize winners Crowd Power Plant
        </em>
        |
        <em>
         Courtesy of
         <a href="http://www.siemens.co.uk/en/news_press/press_releases/index.php?tab=2&amp;date-1-dd=03&amp;date-1-mm=04&amp;date-1=2014&amp;date-2-dd=07&amp;date-2-mm=06&amp;date-2=2014&amp;search=Searchterm&amp;division_replaced=WT&amp;division=E%2C+RN%2C+SG%2C+Sustainability%2C+WP%2C+WT" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.siemens.co.uk/en/news_press/press_releases/index.php?tab=2&amp;date-1-dd=03&amp;date-1-mm=04&amp;date-1=2014&amp;date-2-dd=07&amp;date-2-mm=06&amp;date-2=2014&amp;search=Searchterm&amp;division_replaced=WT&amp;division=E%2C+RN%2C+SG%2C+Sustainability%2C+WP%2C+WT', 'Siemens Press Photo');" target="_blank">
          Siemens Press Photo
         </a>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Sponsored by
        <a href="http://www.siemens.co.uk/answers/en/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.siemens.co.uk/answers/en/', 'Siemens ');" target="_blank">
         Siemens
        </a>
        since 2013, the Mayors annual competition encourages investment in low carbon innovations whilst nurturing Londons student entrepreneurs of the future.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://1sun4all.com/startups-accelerators/calling-londons-green-business-dragons-20000-prize-video/" target="_blank">
         Open to students and recent graduates in the Greater London area
        </a>
        , this years prize attracted over 200 entries, entering as individuals or teams.
       </p>
       <p>
        On 27th May, ten finalists pitched their ideas to a panel of judges including Zac Goldmsith MP, Dragons Dens Deborah Meaden, Innocent Drinks co-founder Richard Reed and Siemens UK CEO Roland Aurich and Martin Powell, Head of Urban Development.
       </p>
       <p>
        A week later the
        <a href="http://www.london.gov.uk/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.london.gov.uk/', 'Mayor of London, Boris Johnson');" target="_blank">
         Mayor of London, Boris Johnson
        </a>
        , joined the award ceremony at the Crystal to announce that the main prize would go to the Crowd Power Plant  a concept for an online electricity marketplace that connects producers and consumers of electricity. The two winners, Imperial College students James Winfield and Dominic Jacobson, received a 15,000 prize to help develop the idea.
       </p>
       <p>
        In addition runners up Kirsty Kenney and Harold Craston from LSE, took home 5,000 for their Solarbox idea, which aims to transform Londons iconic telephone into free green mobile phone charging points.
       </p>
       <p>
        The winning entries beat off stiff competition from other finalists whose ideas included:
       </p>
       <div>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Floating urban food farms on barges moored in the Thames
         </li>
         <li>
          A campaign for cartoon energy and carbon heroes, to inspire young people to save energy
         </li>
         <li>
          Wireless office lighting system
         </li>
         <li>
          A social logistics network
         </li>
        </ul>
       </div>
       <p>
        As part of our support for the Prize,
        <a href="http://1sun4all.com/wind-offshore-wind/dutch-offshore-project-gemini-siemens-video/" target="_blank">
         Siemens
        </a>
        also offers a number of internships to selected entrants, giving the participants the opportunity to gain valuable experience to boost their career prospects.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         The Prize has proved a great way to inspire innovation and get young people involved and active in the debate about reducing carbon emissions. The interns who have already come to Siemens through this competition have made the most of this opportunity and weve valued their input. Roland Aurich, CEO at SiemensPLC
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>

         London.gov.uk
        </a>
       </p>

        By Amber Archangel
