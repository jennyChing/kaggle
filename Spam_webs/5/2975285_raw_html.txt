        <h1 class="entry-title">
         First official run10 k, Berlin Airport Run
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://technobeast.net/first-official-run10-k-berlin-airport-run/" rel="bookmark" title="8:59 am">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2012-04-23T08:59:00+00:00">
           April 23, 2012
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://technobeast.net/author/technobeast/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Alexander Georgiev">
            Alexander Georgiev
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Its been already some time, but Im not exactly the most regular, so:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://technobeast.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-04-22-11.26.45.jpg">
          <img alt="2012-04-22 11.26.45" border="0" height="244" src="http://technobeast.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-04-22-11.26.45_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="2012-04-22 11.26.45" width="227"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The 6th Berlin Airport Run  22 April, 2012. Official Half-marathon but with a 10k option. Option which I chose for the first ever event like that for me.
        </p>
        <p>
         It was nice to prepare with a goal. You know you have to do 10k, no more, no less. 10 kilometers and you only have to beat yourself. I did it, at least  1:02:13 race time. So far, so good. What I didnt think is that there are going to be so many people  I dont really know how many thousands there were. You run on the track and you really hear the multiple drums that others shoes and the track were improvising. Very nice. You finish and by some accident your finish is matching with the one of the fastest half-marathoners. People applaud them, but you feel proud. You run faster.
         <img alt="Smile" class="wlEmoticon wlEmoticon-smile" src="http://technobeast.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/wlEmoticon-smile.png" style="border-bottom-style: none; border-left-style: none; border-top-style: none; border-right-style: none"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I finished and even forgot to stop my watch  had to kiss my girl first. She was there, right after the finish line waiting for me.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets see what is next
        </p>
