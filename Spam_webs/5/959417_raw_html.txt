      <h2 class="post-title entry-title">
       Blog It! by Molly Greene Featured Book
      </h2>
      <div class="postmeta">
       <span class="meta-date">
        <a href="http://ebookreviewgal.com/blog-it-by-molly-greene-featured-book/" rel="bookmark" title="11:07 am">
         <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2013-10-23T11:07:48+00:00">
          October 23, 2013
         </time>
        </a>
       </span>
       <span class="meta-author sep">
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="fn" href="http://ebookreviewgal.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by admin">
          admin
         </a>
        </span>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;">
       </div>
       <p>
        Youve written your book and now its time to sit back and wait for sales to soar, right? Wrong. It takes a huge amount of work for writers to get their book noticed. Weve discussed several ways to promote and market yourself and your book in the past. Marketing tools like
        <a href="http://ebookreviewgal.com/virtual-book-tour-service-now-available/" target="_blank">
         virtual book tours
        </a>
        , video book trailers,
        <a href="http://ebookreviewgal.com/let-us-interview-you-free/" target="_blank">
         author interviews
        </a>
        , guest posts, etc. are all essential techniques that every author should be utilizing.
       </p>
       <p>
        Theres another fantastic tool you should be using. If youre a writer, regardless of what type of content you write, you should be blogging. A blog is an excellent complement to your book. Its a great way to market and promote yourself by making yourself more approachable to readers.
       </p>
       <p>
        If youre new to blogging, and understand the potential power of the blog then Molly Greenes book Blog It! is for you.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe frameborder="0" height="240" marginheight="0" marginwidth="0" scrolling="no" src="http://rcm-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/cm?t=skpwoodworksc-20&amp;o=1&amp;p=8&amp;l=as1&amp;asins=B00BI15Y7E&amp;ref=qf_sp_asin_til&amp;fc1=000000&amp;IS2=1&amp;lt1=_blank&amp;m=amazon&amp;lc1=0000FF&amp;bc1=FFFFFF&amp;bg1=FFFFFF&amp;f=ifr" style="width: 120px; height: 240px;" width="320">
        </iframe>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         According to Mollys website:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
       </div>
       <p>
        <em>
         Whether youre a self-published author with titles on Amazon or selling a non-fiction book on your website, product pricing is a major component of your marketing plan and income projections. Im guessing youve spent a lot of time considering price point tactics. But lets face it, the cost of ebooks  both theory and advice  is all over the board. Whats an author to do?
        </em>
        <a href="http://www.molly-greene.com/" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Read the rest here.
         </strong>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://ebookreviewgal.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Blog-It-cover-photo.jpg">
         <img alt="Blog It! by Molly Greene" class="size-full wp-image-2085 alignright" height="239" src="http://ebookreviewgal.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Blog-It-cover-photo.jpg" width="160"/>
        </a>
        <img alt="" src="http://www.molly-greene.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Molly.headshot.jpg"/>
        Molly Greene is an Author, Blogging Specialist and Coach. Her
        <a href="http://www.molly-greene.com/" target="_blank">
         website
        </a>
        it filled with valuable tips and techniques. Personally, Ive been following Molly on
        <a href="https://twitter.com/mollygreene" target="_blank">
         Twitter
        </a>
        for some time now and I always look forward to reading what she has to say about blogging, writing and more. I highly recommend subscribing to her blog to stay on top of all the latest news!
