        How HSBC Global Connections helped my business
       </h1>

  and build contacts from South America to the Far East

        <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="firstPar">
          <p>
           Since 2010, HSBCs
           <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sponsored/business/global-connections/">
            Global Connections
           </a>
           initiative has helped UK businesses to fuel growth, find new markets overseas, and realise their full potential.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           Now in its fourth year, Global Connections aims to find the brightest stars of British enterprise, the companies with the most innovative ideas, boldest ambitions, and the determination to take their business as far as it can go. To date, more than 5,000 firms have applied to the programme.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">
          <p>
           HSBC is now seeking applications from businesses with annual turnover of between 2m and 100m. The only other requirements are that applicants have been trading for at least two years, and are based in the UK.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           For companies that fall within these criteria, the incentives for joining the contest are self-evident. Each of the 45 winners for 2014 will receive up to 6m in lending, as well as support to assist with the continued growth of their company. They also gain access to the contacts and expertise of HSBCs worldwide network.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           In the case of UK companies looking to expand their businesses abroad, perhaps approaching entirely new territories, this last prize can be particularly valuable.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="body">
          <p>
           Following a successful Global Connections trip to Mexico, regional winner Wensleydale Dairy Products set its sights on growing its export business. At present exports account for roughly 13pc of sales, but the company is now looking to increase this to 25pc.
          </p>
          <p>
           The knowledge, insight and experience gained through the HSBC scheme has helped our company grow and develop confidently, and strategically, in both domestic and export markets, says managing director David Hartley. It gives you the confidence to say, We can do this, even as a relatively small company with a phenomenally unique product.
          </p>
          <p>
           In many cases, Global Connections forums overseas have helped their participants to explore opportunities they hadnt previously considered. A visit to Istanbul helped Midbass, an audio specialist based in the West Midlands, to explore an entirely new market.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our eyes were opened to the potential export opportunities in the Middle East, says Chris Etheridge, commercial and financial director. Istanbul really is the gateway to the East and success in Turkey is a key launch pad to expand further into the region.
          </p>
          <p>
           He adds: We have placed more emphasis on emerging markets and are already seeing rapid growth in these new territories with big expectations for 2014.
          </p>
          <p>
           To date, the Global Connections programme has helped 138 companies attend its international exchanges, visiting fast-growing markets in South America, China and the Middle East, among other places. And over the past four years, successful entrants have gained access to more than 3bn in funding.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <i>
             Entries to HSBC Global Connections 2014 have now closed.
            </i>
           </strong>
          </p>

                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
