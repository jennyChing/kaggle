         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Trash to Treasure
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-07-13T12:56:57+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           13.Jul.2011
          </time>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://wenderly.com/trash-to-treasure/#comments">
            34 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/finished-cart-half.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4066" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/finished-cart-half.jpg" title="finished-cart-half" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I
          <em>
           love
          </em>
          to go antiquing.
         </p>
         <p>
          There is something so magical and medicinal about rising early with the sun, grabbing a cup of coffee and forging out in search of treasures just waiting patiently to be found.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its
          <em>
           all
          </em>
          about the hunt.
         </p>
         <p>
          The anticipationmixed with imagination that just sets my soul on fire.
         </p>
         <p>
          I LOVE it.
         </p>
         <p>
          I also love to piddle around in my flower beds. When I stumble upon a treasure that I can enjoy outside amongst my flowers beds I am filled with glee!
         </p>
         <p>
          Last summer I found this
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/2010/08/27/dining-al-fresco-with-style/">
           chandelabra
          </a>
          while antiquing and I shared how easy it is to make when talking about
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/2010/08/27/dining-al-fresco-with-style/">
           Dining Al Fresco in Style.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/back-garden-chandleier.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4086" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/back-garden-chandleier.jpg" title="back-garden-chandleier" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Today Id like to share another cherished find.
         </p>
         <p>
          A flower cart. A rustymetalflower cart.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/rusty-cart-3.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4073" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/rusty-cart-3.jpg" title="rusty-cart-3" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          But its not just any ordinary flower cart.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/rusty-cart2.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4070" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/rusty-cart2.jpg" title="rusty-cart2" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          With a little imagination, a simple sanding,
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/spray-paint-flower-cart.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4087" height="385" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/spray-paint-flower-cart.jpg" title="spray-paint-flower-cart" width="577"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          and some spray primer and paint, you, my friends, can have this
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/full-cart.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4069" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/full-cart.jpg" title="full-cart" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          a darling, charming
          <em>
           entertaining
          </em>
          cart. Its got wheels for easy transportation, two shelves to fill with all sorts of goodies and can be enjoyed for entertaining inside or out!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/full-cart.jpg">
          </a>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/finished-cart-close-up.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4071" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/finished-cart-close-up.jpg" title="finished-cart-close-up" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I even found that enamel bowl while out treasure hunting
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/close-up-drinks-on-cart.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4074" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/close-up-drinks-on-cart.jpg" title="close-up-drinks-on-cart" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Oh and that sweet vintage glass holder too! I switch out the glasses to match whatever color palette I may be using!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/antique-green-glasses.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4075" height="384" src="http://wenderly.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/antique-green-glasses.jpg" title="antique-green-glasses" width="576"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Antiquing and treasure hunting has become a hobby of mine.
         </p>
         <p>
          Go aheadventure out with an open mind and a full imagination!
         </p>
         <p>
          The possibilities are endless!
         </p>
         <p>
          Happy Hunting!
         </p>
