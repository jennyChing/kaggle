     The worlds most incredible pools
    </div>
    <div class="post-365 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-luxury-hotels-2 category-pool-spa-and-gym tag-best-hotel-pools tag-biggest-pool tag-hotel-experiences tag-largest-hotel-pool tag-luxury-pool tag-luxury-travel tag-san-alfonso-del-mar tag-worlds-biggest-pool tag-worlds-largest-pool tag-worlds-most-incredible-pool">
     <div class="content clearfix">
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="7701351" data-link="http://hotelinteriordesigns.eu/the-worlds-most-incredible-pool/" data-summary="" data-title="The world's most incredible pools">
      </div>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanalfonso.cl/" target="_blank">
        San Alfonso Del Mar Resort
       </a>
       (Chile) currently holds the record for the largest swimming pool in the world. It covers as much as eight hectares of land with a total length of one kilometer and reaches 35 meters at its deepest point. In order to have it filled over 250 million liters of seawater is pumped, filtered, and treated from the Pacific Ocean into the pool. This pool is so huge that it accommodates smaller pools near the edges. It has a Jacuzzi, a water trampoline, a glassed pyramid pool, sandy beaches on one section; and a dock for mini sailboats and kayaks.
      </p>
      <p>
       Chiles monster pool uses a computer-controlled suction and filtration system to keep fresh seawater in permanent circulation, drawing it in from the ocean at one end and pumping it out at the other.
      </p>
      <p>
       The sun warms the water to 26c, nine degrees warmer than the adjoining sea.
      </p>
      <p>
       Chilean biochemist Fernando Fischmann, whose Crystal Lagoons Corporation designed the pool, said advanced engineering meant his company could build an impressive artificial paradise even in inhospitable areas. It uses hardly any chemicals.
      </p>
      <p>
       As long as we have access to unlimited seawater, we can make it work, and it causes no damage to the ocean.
      </p>
