       <span itemprop="description">
        <p>
         Meet the 'Modern Family' cast that the producers had originally wanted.  We're pleased they didn't get their own way, because we can't imagine a better lineup than the one they have!
        </p>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="EchoTopic">
       <span id="intelliTxt">
        <span class="articleBody" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          We cant imagine a more perfect cast that the one that
          <em>
           Modern Family
          </em>
          already has. Every single of one of them has made the roles their own, almost like they were written with them in mind. So youll be surprised to hear that hardly any of them were the first choice for the parts! But who did the producers have lined up before the perfect cast finally came together?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Modern Family Alternate Cast" height="750" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ln/20130727/270713_nbc_tca_summer_press_tour_13/craig-t-nelson-nbcs-tca-2013-summer_3783759.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Modern Family Alternate Cast" width="500"/>
          <span style="font-size: x-small;">
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/craig-t-nelson">
            Craig T. Nelson
           </a>
           was first choice to play
           <em>
            Modern Family
           </em>
           patriarch Jay Pritchett
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Family patriarch Jay Pritchett was originally pegged to be played by Emmy Award winner Craig T. Nelson. Best known for his role as Hayden Fox in
          <em>
           Coach
          </em>
          and as the voice of Mr. Incredible in the 2004 animated film
          <em>
           The Incredibles
          </em>
          , producers originally approached Nelson although his pay expectations were too high. Ed ONeill, who the role eventually went to (and who just IS Jay) explained, When I read [the script] I thought, Oh boy, this is pretty good. And I called my managed and he said, Well, theyre out to Craig T. Nelson. Were grateful that they couldnt afford Nelson because no-one plays grumpy good guy Jay Pritchard quite like Ed ONeill. We're also sure that Nelson is kicking himself since Sofia Vergara could have been his onscreen wife! We wonder if he'd known that to begin with it would have made a difference?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Modern Family Alternate Cast" height="683" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ln/20140425/250414_matt_le_blanc_arrives_at_radio_2/matt-le-blanc-matt-le-blanc-arrives_4167491.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Modern Family Alternate Cast" width="500"/>
          <span style="font-size: x-small;">
           Would Matt LeBlanc have made a good Phil Dunphy?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Friends
          </em>
          actor Matt LeBlanc was first choice to play goofy Phil Dunphy. Sure, LeBlanc played Joey, who was also pretty goofy, but hes not really the right brand of adorkable in our opinion. The role ended up going to
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/ty-burrell">
           Ty Burrell
          </a>
          who totally owns it now.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/story/modern-family-bosses-to-cover-wedding-costs-in-new-york_4191584">
           <strong>
            MORE:
            <em>
             Modern Family
            </em>
            bosses to cover wedding costs in New York
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Julie Bowan wasnt even the second choice to play Jays daughter and Phils wife, Claire Dunphy.
          <em>
           3rd Rock from the Sun
          </em>
          actress
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/kristen-johnson">
           Kristen Johnson
          </a>
          and
          <em>
           Friends
          </em>
          actress
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/lisa-kudrow">
           Lisa Kudrow
          </a>
          were both frontrunners for the role. Johnson has proved herself an excellent comic actress but would she have had that edge of neuroticism to play highly strung Claire? Same goes for Kudrow. Although we would have quite liked to have seen Kudrow and LeBlanc reunited, they were great together back in the
          <em>
           Friends
          </em>
          days. Julie Bowan has all of Claires little quirks down to a tee, so she may have been third in the running but shes first in our eyes!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Modern Family Alternate Cast" height="750" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ln/20131206/i_ll_eat_you_last__a_chat_with_sue_mengers_061213_01/lisa-kudrow-bette-midler-one-woman-broadway-play_3984754.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Modern Family Alternate Cast" width="500"/>
          <span style="font-size: x-small;">
           Lisa Kudrow was a first choice to play Claire Dunphy
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          So,
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/jesse-tyler-ferguson">
           Jesse Tyler Ferguson
          </a>
          did make the final
          <em>
           Modern Family
          </em>
          cut, but did you know that he was originally set to play Cam and not Mitchell? We cant describe how happy we are that the producers found
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/eric-stonestreet">
           Eric Stonestreet
          </a>
          to play Cam and cast Ferguson as Mitchell instead. Cam is by far one of the best things about the show, we wonder if it would have been half as successful without him?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/adam-devine-modern-family-manny-andy_4227525">
           <strong>
            MORE: Reasons why Andy the Manny is our favourite
            <em>
             Modern Family
            </em>
            character
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
