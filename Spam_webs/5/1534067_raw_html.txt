       <br/>
       <br/>
       In the northern the main state, temperatures from late November to March may be on the chilly side. In this period, the AL-North region can get almost 60 days of below freezing temperatures. Central heating systems play a significant part in household comfort during this period of year. So when you will find heating problems, Alabama families in AL-North need to incur the cost of an immediate - if not emergency - repair to their central heating systems.
       <br/>

       Sometimes homeowners with this region may also do a temperature pump installation. AL-North homes that use heat pumps in conjunction with their central heating systems can realize significant savings on their energy bills.

In this respect, AL-Mid is really a transitional region. Homeowners who relied on their central heating systems would not be wrong, but would not experience exactly the same savings as people who gambled ongoing only with a temperature pump installation.
