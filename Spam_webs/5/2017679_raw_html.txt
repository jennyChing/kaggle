          </h2>
          <div class="entry-byline clearfix">

           <a href="http://cinemavine.com/star-trek-into-darkness-carol-marcus-poster">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11470" height="248" src="http://cinemavine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/carol_marcus_star_trek_into_darkness-e1367076153749.png" title="carol_marcus_star_trek_into_darkness" width="600"/>
           </a>
           Its official: Paramount will be releasing a new Star Trek Into Darkness character poster every single day up until the films release date. Today we get Alice Eves Carol Marcus and next week well be getting ones for the entire Enterprise security team, Sexy Girls 1 &amp; 2 (they exist), and Uhuras old green roommate.
           <span id="more-11469">
           </span>
           Doctor Carol Marcus is one of the leading molecular biologists in the Federationbut Im sure you already knew that.
          </p>
          <p>
           In the sequel, Kirk leads his crew on a manhunt to capture an unstoppable force of destruction and bring those responsible to justice. As our heroes are propelled into an epic chess game of life and death, love will be challenged, friendships will be torn apart, and sacrifices must be made for the only family Kirk has left: his crew. Chris Pine, Zachary Quinto, Zoe Saldana, Benedict Cumberbatch, Karl Urban, Anton Yelchin, John Cho, Simon Pegg, Alice Eve, Peter Weller, and Noel Clarke star. Star Trek Into Darkness opens in 3D and IMAX 3D on May 17, 2013.
