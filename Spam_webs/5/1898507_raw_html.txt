           <p>
            Fashion month is officially kicking off this week and we are excited to see all the fabulous new trends and looks for the Spring/Summer 2015 season. While we cant give you a ticket to the shows in New York, London, Milan or Paris, we can give you the next best thing.
           </p>

           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Jana,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://spillingtuesday.blogspot.com/">
             <strong>
              Spilling Tuesday
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Jana" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1754" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Jana.jpg?resize=300%2C221"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Black and white truly never goes out of style. So does pearls! Jana, fashion blogger of Spilling Tuesday looks chic and classy with her fabulous skirt and striped top. Her belt and awesome piles of pearls complemented her overall look.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Laura,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.spanglishfashion.com/">
             <strong>
              Spanglish Fashion
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Laura.jpg">
             <img alt="Laura" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1766" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Laura.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Laura is a fashion lover, stylist, jewelry designer, wife and blogger of Spanglish Fashion. Here she wears a pastel pink skirt, a white tank tied at the waist, a shoulder purse and to top off the look, a gorgeous pearl necklace by Laura herself. We love that she looks fresh, sophisticated and cool all at once in this fashionable look!
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Ann Kim,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.andyheart.com/">
             <strong>
              Andy Heart
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Ann-Kim.png">
             <img alt="Ann Kim" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1746" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Ann-Kim.png?resize=210%2C300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Simple lines define Ann Kims fashion tastes. The winner of Refinery 29s The Next Big Style Blogger 2012, her blog, Andy Heart, showcases a minimalist elegance and chic styles. Check out how simple pearl bracelets and rings are highlighted charmingly by a black and white outfit.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Irina Goncharova,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.yourseureka.ru/">
             <strong>
              Yours Eureka
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Irina" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1753" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Irina.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Irina shows us that simplicity still wins in fashion. Her oversized powder blue blazer and white ankle strap heels, reflective aviators and classy pearl pendant complete her look which is fresh, clean and amazing!
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Natalie Ast,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://natalieast.com/">
             <strong>
              Natalia Style
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Natalie.jpg">
             <img alt="Natalie" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1772" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Natalie.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Natalie has been following fashion ever since she was a child. Several years later, she started sharing her thoughts on fashion online and posting daily outfits to Lookbook. Now we see her personal style on her blog Nataliest. Here we see her effortlessly adorable, in a striped shirt, overalls, and a beautiful pearl necklace.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Oby Grace,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://ispyoby.blogspot.com/">
             <strong>
              I Spy Oby
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Oby" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1776" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Oby.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Pearls always look good with leather. Oby, fashion blogger of I Spy Oby shows off her smart style in a little black dress with all black shoes and bag, incorporated with layers of pearls. Her look is classy, timeless, and effortless!
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Kryz Uy,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.kryzuy.com/contact/">
             <strong>
              Thirstythought
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Kryz Uy" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1765" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kryz-Uy.jpg?resize=199%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Fashion and style blogger Kryz Uy of Thirstythought loves her DIY pearl shorts. We do, too! Her turquoise beaded necklace, bunch of gold plated bracelets and pearl shorts gives off an effortless casual look that can also be holiday-ready.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Leondra,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://fashleeunorthodox.blogspot.com/">
             <strong>
              Fash-Lee Unorthodox
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Leondra" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1768" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Leondra.jpg?resize=300%2C256"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Pearls can be embellished on denim as well as on your favorite tees! Look at what Leondra, fashion blogger of Fash-Lee Unorthodox, did to her graphic tee. She cut it into a crop top and added pearls on the neckline. That, plus a fashionable print pencil skirt, chic clutch and color-clocking heels make the perfect summer-friendly look.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Kristin Buchholz-MacKillop,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.highlandfashionista.com/">
             <strong>
              Highland Fashionista
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Kristin Buchholz-MacKillop" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1763" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kristin-Buchholz-MacKillop.jpg?resize=225%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Kristin believes in expressing your true self through fashion, and she does just that on her blog, Highland Fashionista. Here she is in her element  grey hair included  rocking a lavender dress with nude slingback pumps and a classy pair of pearl earrings to finish off a girly look.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Daniela Ramirez,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.nanysklozet.com/">
             <strong>
              Nanys Klozet
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Daniela" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1747" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Daniela.jpg?resize=270%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Rocker chick Daniela of Nanys Klozet shows off her style in leather, edgy accessories and a unique pearl crop top. She combines her burgundy maxi skirt with bold bracelets, red lipstick and the showstopper  a pearl crop top. We think this look is terrific.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Lisa Hamilton of
            </strong>
            <a href="http://seewantshop.blogspot.com/">
             <strong>
              See Want Shop
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Lisa Hamilton" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1770" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Lisa-Hamilton.png?resize=300%2C197"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Lisa considers a great fitting pair of jeans a wardrobe staple. That doesnt stop her from channeling her inner laid-back Aussie style by sporting denim cut-off shorts and a long-sleeved button down shirt, with a simple pair of pearl earrings to cap off the look.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://alanaruas.com/">
             <strong>
              Alana Ruas
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Alana Ruas" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1744" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Alana-Ruas.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Fashion blogger Alana Ruas makes lace, metallic and pearls a winning combination. Here she wears a cute baby blue and white lace dress with two layers of different pearls. She adds an oversized metallic clutch as well as white pumps to get the overall look.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Andre Judd,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://avantgardien.tumblr.com/">
             <strong>
              The Avant Gardien
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Andre Judd" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1745" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Andre-Judd.jpg?resize=300%2C224"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Andre Judd, blogger of The Avant Gardien, wowed us with his extreme styling. Check out the beautiful artsy headpiece with pearl detailing! He calls this bold look Interstellar Copper Kid. His Couture jacket, black skinny pants and platform boots truly complement his artistic pearl and copper headpiece.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Trine Kjaer,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://trineswardrobe.dk/">
             <strong>
              Trines Wardrobe
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Trine Kjaer" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1784" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Trine-Kjaer.jpg?resize=300%2C208"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            With a beauty line under her belt, Trine of Trines Wardrobe is a certified fashion icon. Her European style features uncomplicated and relaxed, yet fashion-forward outfits. Case in point: this ensemble of leather shorts from Ganni, an H&amp;M shirt, bag from Chanel, pearl earrings from Glitter, and Topshop sandals.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Kristina Bazan,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.kayture.com/">
             <strong>
              Kayture
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Kristina Bazan" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1764" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kristina-Bazan.jpg?resize=300%2C200"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Kristina wears many hats, but its her role as co-founder of Kayture, one of the most influential style blogs in the world, thats got her name out in the fashion realm. With a classically elegant style, her knack for accessorizing serves as inspiration for fashionistas the world over.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Vanessa Hong,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://thehautepursuit.com/">
             <strong>
              The Haute Pursuit
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Vanessa Hong" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1785" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Vanessa-Hong.jpg?resize=200%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            With her eye-catching avant-garde style, Vanessa of The Haute Pursuit wears and pulls off outfits that most people would normally not dare to wear. Check out how she makes this edgy black jacket and skirt ensemble work with sexy heels, a clutch, and pearl accessories.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Serena,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://theserenasaga.com/">
             <strong>
              Serena Saga
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Serena (2)" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1781" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Serena-2.jpg?resize=200%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Pearls can be fun and fabulous. Serena, blogger of Serena Saga, shows us just how. Her own carefree and fun style can be seen through her look: black leather skirt, plaid and denim button-down blouse, red accent clutch and multiple strands of pearls. Her great hair and beautiful smile also makes her overall look a sensation!
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Tricia Gosingtian,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://blog.triciagosingtian.com/">
             <strong>
              Tricia Will Go Places
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Tricia" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1783" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Tricia.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Tricia looks like a modern fairy in her dainty ensemble. We love her pearl-embellished shoes! We also love how her powder blue dress matches her footwear and side braid.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Yuni,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://kawaiiyuni.blogspot.sg/">
             <strong>
              KawaiiYuni
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Yuni" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1787" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Yuni.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Yuni, style and fashion blogger of KawaiiYuni shows is that you can make pearls work anywhere, including poolside! Look at her make a splash with her swimsuit, simple knitted hat, shades and pearl earrings. Who thought pearls would look so chic with a colored bathing suit?
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Hallie Swanson,
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.halliedaily.com/">
             <strong>
              Hallie Daily
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Hallie Daily" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1752" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.pearlsonly.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Hallie-Daily.jpg?resize=202%2C300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            If Hallie Swanson isnt already in your fashion radar, then youre missing out. Her colorful and feminine style has even landed her a spot on Vanity Fairs International Best Dressed List!
