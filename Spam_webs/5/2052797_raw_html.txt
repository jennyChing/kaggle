        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Surry Hills-based Smart Design Studio has designed the Lamble Residence, acontemporarybeach house that overlooks the beautiful Gerrigong beach in New South Wales, Australia. Constructed out oftimber cladding, a classic beach house material, the residence provides a 270 degree panoramicview of the surrounding landscape from its elevated standpoint. The housedefies conventional planning by locatingthe living room, second kitchen and master bedroom upstairs where the views are the most spectacular, with the rest of the rooms in the ground floor opening onto the extensive outdoor spaces on different sides of the house. A minimal reliance on doors lets the space flow around the cores of the joinery and blade walls, while the crisp white walls give a neutral base for art and other decorative features. Check out the photo set of theLamble Residence above.
