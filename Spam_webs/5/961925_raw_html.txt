           Built to spec for a client, this custom Harley Davidson RoadGlide started out as an 08 Electra Glide. Obviously the fairing was to be replaced, but in the process we also decided to rake out the neck and install a 26 DNA Envy rim. To wrap the massive front wheel, a custom Arlen Ness fender was used.
          </p>
