        <h6>
         1 week. Beginning of a new life. / A new life begins. / Here is the beginning of a new life.
        </h6>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         
         <a href="https://www.behance.net/gallery/20961133/MOTHER-BOOK" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.behance.net/gallery/20961133/MOTHER-BOOK', 'Mother Book']);" target="_blank" title="Mother Book">
          Mother Book
         </a>
         , created by Dentsu Nagoya,could be a truly unique gift for expecting mothers. Its a wonderful reflection of the growing bump and the baby inside of it. The book consists of 40 pages and every page is a journey towards the most important date in every womans life. The design of each page helps mothers-to-be to understand the status of their babies every week. Isnt it touchingto be reminded that, for instance, at week 13 yourbaby starts moving around using the uterine wall as a trampoline?The campaign encourages mothers to write their personal feelings on each page as the pregnancy progresses and consider the book a gift to their children.
