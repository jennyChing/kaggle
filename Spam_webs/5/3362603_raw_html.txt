         <h1 class="post-title" data-post-id="856021" itemprop="name">
          10 High-Tech Gadgets You Need in Your Bedroom
         </h1>
         <h4 class="post-meta clearfix">
          <div class="avatar nopin">
           <img alt="Samantha Tagher" class="avatar avatar-44 wp-user-avatar wp-user-avatar-44 alignnone photo nopin" height="44" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/tagher-samantha-1.jpg?fit=crop&amp;w=44&amp;h=44&amp;ver=1423172653.1" width="44"/>
          </div>
          <div class="posted-on-wrapper">
           <p>
            by
            <a href="//www.brit.co/author/samantha/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Samantha Tagher">
             <span itemprop="author">
              Samantha Tagher
             </span>
            </a>
            on
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-02-18T17:50:08+00:00" pubdate="">
             February 18, 2015
            </time>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing" style="display: none">
          </div>
         </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="post-content inner">
         <p>
          Smart homes are all the rage these days. From
          <a href="//www.brit.co/smart-bulbs/" target="_blank">
           smart bulbs
          </a>
          to
          <a href="//www.brit.co/high-tech-kitchen-appliances/" target="_blank">
           high-tech kitchen appliances
          </a>
          , the list of waysto make your home *that* much more techy is endless. The
          <a href="//www.brit.co/lowes-iris-smart-home/" target="_blank">
           home of the future
          </a>
          has certainly graced us with its presence, and we are
          <em>
           not
          </em>
          mad about it. Next up? High-tech appliances for your bedroom. Think smart mattresses, fancy nightlights and snooze-reducing alarm clocks. Scroll on through to see our recent gadgetfinds thatll make sleeping and waking that much better.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="5" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-856024" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="800" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/57.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://www.urbanoutfitters.com/urban/catalog/productdetail.jsp?id=30589527&amp;parentid=A_FURN_LIGHTING#/" target="_blank">
           Plasma Nightlight
          </a>
          : Say hello to the coolest
          <a href="//www.brit.co/nightlights/" target="_blank">
           nightlight
          </a>
          <em>
           ever
          </em>
          . The plasma-current effect it emits is all kinds of awesome.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="sleepnumber" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-856030" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="508" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/sleepnumber.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          2.
          <a href="http://www.sleepnumber.com/sn/en/Beds/Classic-Series-Beds/p/c2?tag=bom_tomsguide-20" target="_blank">
           Sleep Number c2 Bed
          </a>
          : This mattress helps you track and
          <a href="//www.brit.co/sleep-tracking-apps/" target="_blank">
           optimize your sleep
          </a>
          . Pretty cool, huh?
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="3" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-856023" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="800" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/37.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.sony.net/Products/4k-ultra-short-throw/" target="_blank">
           Sony Ultra Short Throw Projector
          </a>
          : This Sony projector allows you to turn your wall (or ceiling!) into a life-sized window or movie theater. And, if youre
          <a href="//www.brit.co/emily-henderson-small-space-interior-design-tricks/" target="_blank">
           short on space
          </a>
          , this projector is perfect for you as it sits just seven inches from the wall.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="9" class="white-image alignnone wp-image-856025 size-full" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/96.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          4.
          <a href="http://www.withings.com/us/withings-aura.html" target="_blank">
           Withings Aura
          </a>
          : Tuck this sleep sensor under your mattress, accessorize your
          <a href="//www.brit.co/diy-nightstands/" target="_blank">
           nightstand
          </a>
          with the bedside device and monitor your sleep usingyour iPhone. Sounds goodto us!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="avea" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-856026" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/avea.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          5.
          <a href="https://www.elgato.com/en/smart/avea" target="_blank">
           Elgato Avea Mood Light
          </a>
          : In the mood for some extravagant mood lighting? The Elgato Avea mood light will leave your bedroom ultra colorful.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="nest" class="white-image alignnone wp-image-856028 size-full" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/nest.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          6.
          <a href="https://store.nest.com/product/thermostat/" target="_blank">
           Nest Thermostat
          </a>
          : Weve talked about the
          <a href="//www.brit.co/nest-the-learning-thermostat/" target="_blank">
           Nest thermostat
          </a>
          before, and were still just as obsessed. Too toasty under your blankets? Make it a little cooler withyour
          <a href="//www.brit.co/make-your-smartphone-smarter/" target="_blank">
           smartphone
          </a>
          . Really, its that easy.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="samsung" class="white-image alignnone wp-image-856029 size-full" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="508" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/samsung.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          7.
          <a href="http://www.samsung.com/us/video/tvs/UN55HU9000FXZA" target="_blank">
           Samsung 4K UHD TV
          </a>
          : Because flat screen TVs are *so* 2014. The Samsung curved TV is a must for your Friday night in.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bigass" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-856076" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/bigass.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          8.
          <a href="https://store.bigasssolutions.com/fans.html" target="_blank">
           Big Ass Fans Haiku
          </a>
          : If traditional fans just arent going to cut it in your home, Big Ass Fans are perfect for you. Trust, this ones the
          <em>
           smallest
          </em>
          fan they make.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="responsive-iframe" style="padding-bottom: 56.2%">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/VlS5SMVeKn0?feature=oembed&amp;wmode=transparent" width="500">
           </iframe>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          9.
          <a href="http://lunasleep.com/" target="_blank">
           Luna Mattress Cover
          </a>
          : Make your mattressa smart bed using the Luna mattress cover, which warms your bed
          <em>
           and
          </em>
          tracks your sleep. *added to cart*
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-856214" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/126.jpg" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          10.
          <a href="http://www.ramosclock.com/" target="_blank">
           Ramos Alarm Clock
          </a>
          : To turn this bad boy off, you have to actually get OUTof bed. Sounds kind of torturous, but definitely genius for those of us who hit the snooze button one too many times.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What smart home appliances do you own? Talk to us in the comments.
          </strong>
         </p>
