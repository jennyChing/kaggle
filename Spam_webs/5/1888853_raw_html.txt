Our 2nd year in this Div is looking just as hard, with it being the most open league in years, there is no out right fav,with each team being able to beat each other. So this coming yearwe will be looking to earn promotaion.Or go one better in the cup.Season 2011/12 we got promoted, going up in 3rd place,as we just fell short of winning the league 3pts was the diffrence between the top 3.
