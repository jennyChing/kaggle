        <h1>
         J.R.R. Tolkien's Ainulindal
        </h1>
        <section class="body">
         <p>
          Adapted from Tolkien's amazing words, the first chapter of
          <em>
           The Silmarillion
          </em>
          spans the mythological creation of Middle-Earth and its gods. Told here with loving care and reverence in 54 full color pages. Original story by Tolkien, not owned by Evan Palmer.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was interviewed about the book on the
          <a href="http://truemyths.org/2015/02/26/talking-tolkien-podcast-ep-8-interview-with-illustrator-evan-palmer/" target="_blank">
           Talking Tolkien Podcast
          </a>
          and shared my thoughts on the choices made and future plans.
         </p>
        </section>
