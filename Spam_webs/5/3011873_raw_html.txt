     <h1>
      8 Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine
     </h1>
     <div class="entry">
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_74933" style="width: 560px">
       <img alt="Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine" class="size-full wp-image-74933" height="376" src="http://cdn.where.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Best-Restaurants-in-Calgary-for-Canadian-Cuisine-Rouge.jpg" title="Best-Restaurants-in-Calgary-for-Canadian-Cuisine" width="550"/>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine: #4 is the lovely Rouge (Photo:
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/berzowska/506937943/">
         berzowska
        </a>
        )
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Using locally sourced ingredients and drawing inspiration from Canadas diverse regional cuisine, these eight restaurants offer the freshest takes on home-style and high-end fare. They are not just the best restaurants in Calgary for Canadian cuisinethey are some of the best restaurants in Calgary period.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/alberta/calgary/best-restaurants-calgary-canadian-cuisine/#more-74922">
        <em>
         See the list of best restaurants in Calgary for Canadian cuisine 
        </em>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-74922">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #8 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Home Tasting Room
       </strong>
       <br/>
       A recent addition to Calgarys dining scene,
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/canadian-american/home-tasting-room-wh/">
        Home Tasting Room
       </a>
       is located in a historic sandstone building on Stephen Avenue. The concept here is small plates: order a bunch, and share them with the whole table. Larger portions include the locally sourced Okotoks chicken and Noble Farms duck breast. As the name would suggest, this is a home-style menu served in a casual setting, great for couples or foodie families.
       <br/>
       
       <em>
        Home Tasting Room,110 8 Ave. SW, 403-262-8100,
        <a href="http:///hometastingroom.ca">
         hometastingroom.ca
        </a>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/canadian-american/home-tasting-room-wh/">
         Map and reviews
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       [RELATED:
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/alberta/calgary/best-6-restaurants-near-the-calgary-airport/">
        Best 6 Restaurants Near the Calgary Airport
       </a>
       ]
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #7 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Catch Restaurant &amp; The Oyster Bar
        <br/>
       </strong>
       The citys top seafood venue sources sustainably harvested fish and shellfish from both coasts. Its elegantly appointed Stephen Avenue digs feature an upper level dining room for a sophisticated dinner experience, as well as a more informal main floor oyster bar.
       <br/>
       <em>
        Catch Restaurant &amp; The Oyster Bar, 100 8 Ave. SW, 403-206-0000 x 1,
       </em>
       <a href="http://hyatt.com/gallery/catch/index.html">
        <em>
         website
        </em>
       </a>
       <em>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/seafood/catch-restaurant-oyster-bar-wh/">
         Map and reviews
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_74935" style="width: 560px">
       <img alt="Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine" class="size-full wp-image-74935" height="350" src="http://cdn.where.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Best-Resturants-in-Calgary-Canadian-Catch.jpg" title="Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine" width="550"/>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine: #7 Catch &amp; The Oyster Bar (Photo:
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/endlisnis/237364516/">
         Endlisnis
        </a>
        )
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #6 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Selkirk Grill
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Befitting its historic setting outside the main Heritage Park gate, Selkirks menu emphasizes updated Canadian classic dishes made with regional ingredients such as Alberta beef, Yukon Arctic char,
       <a href="http://www.driviewfarms.ca/">
        Driview Farms
       </a>
       lamb and
       <a href="http://www.valbellagourmetfoods.ca/">
        Valbella
       </a>
       bacon. We recommend the Sunday prime rib dinnera sumptuous, family-friendly affair at a great price.
       <br/>
       <em>
        Selkirk Grill, 1900 Heritage Park Dr. SW, 403-268-8500,
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.heritagepark.ca/townSelkirk.php">
        <em>
         website
        </em>
       </a>
       <em>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/canadian-american/selkirk-grille-wh/">
         Map and reviews
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #5 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Blink Restaurant
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Stylish yet laid back, this Stephen Avenue spot serves exacting contemporary Canadian cuisine. The menu is delectably heavy on West Coast seafood and Alberta meats such as Noble Farms duck, Lethbridge rabbit and Angus reserve beef. Great wine list.
       <br/>
       <em>
        Blink Restaurant, 111 8 Ave. SW, 403-263-5330,
       </em>
       <a href="http://blink-calgary.com/">
        <em>
         blink-calgary.com
        </em>
       </a>
       <br/>
       
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/canadian-american/blink-restaurant-wh/">
         Map and reviews
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #4 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Rouge
        <br/>
       </strong>
       This award-winning fine dining establishment is frequently listed as one of the best restaurants in Calgary. Its located in an adorable bright red home in Inglewood. The menu changes seasonally and highlights local producers and regional fare. Bison, elk and duck are regular fixtures. The location, in a historic Calgary neighbourhood, makes for a uniquely Calgarian experience.
       <br/>
       <em>
         Rouge, 1240 8 Ave. SW, 403-531-2767,
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.rougecalgary.com/">
        <em>
         rougecalgary.com
        </em>
       </a>
       <em>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/french/rouge-wh/">
         Map and reviews
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       [RELATED:
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/alberta/calgary/hot-dining-borgo-brunch/">
        Featured Brunch: Borgo
       </a>
       ]
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #3 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Farm Restaurant
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Farm draws inspiration from the prairie homesteads of the past, both in its interior design and by celebrating local producers seasonal offerings. This southwest Calgary restaurant is best for cheese and charcuterie boards, supplied by its neighbouring sister business
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/delis-and-gourmet-market">
        Janice Beaton Fine Cheese
       </a>
       . The weekend brunch is a definite must.
       <br/>
       <em>
        Farm Restaurant, 1006 17 Ave. SW, 403-245-2276,
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.farm-restaurant.com/">
        <em>
         farm-restaurant.com
        </em>
       </a>
       <em>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <em>
        Map and reviews
       </em>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_74937" style="width: 560px">
       <img alt="Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine" class="size-full wp-image-74937" height="388" src="http://cdn.where.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Rest-Restaurants-in-Calgary-for-Canadian-River.jpg" title="Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine" width="550"/>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Best Restaurants in Calgary for Canadian Cuisine: #2. the River Caf (Photo:
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/btwashburn/7312125458/">
         )
        </a>
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #2 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        River Caf
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Perennially listed as one of the best restaurants in Calgary, River Cafs
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/attractio">
        Princes Island Park
       </a>
       setting and its fishing lodge atmosphere make it a rustic downtown getaway. Its menu emphasizes both regional game meats and sustainable farm produce.
       <br/>
       <em>
        River Caf, 25 Princes Island Park, 403-261-7670,
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.river-cafe.com/">
        <em>
         river-cafe.com
        </em>
       </a>
       <em>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/canadian-american/river-cafe-wh/">
         Map and reviews
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #1 BEST RESTAURANT IN CALGARY FOR CANADIAN CUISINE
        <br/>
        Boxwood Caf
       </strong>
       <br/>
       The River Cafs laid-back sister business is located in a cozy wooden building on the edge of the picturesque
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/activities-sports/historic-sites/central-memorial-park/">
        Memorial Park
       </a>
       in the Beltline district. The menu focuses on local and seasonal suppliers, and includes mouth-watering rotisserie chicken and porchetta sandwiches and mains.
       <br/>
       <em>
        Boxwood Caf, 340 13 Ave. SW, 403-265-4006,
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.boxwoodcafe.ca/">
        <em>
         boxwoodcafe.ca
        </em>
       </a>
       <em>
        <br/>
        
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.where.ca/listings/dining/canadian-american/boxwood-cafe-wh/">
        <em>
         Map and reviews
        </em>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
