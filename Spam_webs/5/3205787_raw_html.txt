                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     Baked Rosemary Garlic Fries (Vegetarian/Vegan)
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-622762941637711920">
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Back in February, I visited one of my
                       <a href="http://point-shoot.tumblr.com/">
                        best friends
                       </a>
                       in
                       <a href="http://www.ibelieveicanfry.com/2011/02/back-from-cleveland.html">
                        Cleveland
                       </a>
                       . One of the dishes I sampled were the "Lola fries" at
                       <a href="http://www.bspotburgers.com/">
                        B-Spot
                       </a>
                       , Iron Chef Michael Symon's burger joint in the
                       <a href="http://cleveland.about.com/od/akronandsummitcounty/p/summitmall.htm">
                        Summit Mall
                       </a>
                       (not to be confused with OUR
                       <a href="http://www.thesummitonline.com/birmingham/">
                        Summit
                       </a>
                       here in Birmingham). The Lola fries were simple; skinny-cut fresh fries, flavored simply with rosemary and sea salt.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-1mncWbGPDxs/TtTeg74vD_I/AAAAAAAADDY/UpYallEPUtM/s1600/IMG_9785.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-1mncWbGPDxs/TtTeg74vD_I/AAAAAAAADDY/UpYallEPUtM/s640/IMG_9785.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       THEY. WERE. AWESOME. Granted, so was everything else at B-Spot, from the Sriracha wings to the vanilla bean apple pie bacon milkshake. Needless to say, I decided that I HAD to make them myself. And that they needed to be baked instead of fried. And have garlic for extra flavor (sorry about the funk "breaff"). This elevate the plain old fry from a bland side item to an almost (ALMOST!) elegant complement to a meal. On top of the great taste, they're so easy, you'll never want to buy
                      </span>
                      <a href="http://www.oreida.com/products/french-fries.aspx" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       frozen
                      </a>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       again.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <b>
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Baked Rosemary-Garlic Fries
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/ibelieveicanfryrecipes/baked-rosemary-garlic-fries">
                        PRINTABLE RECIPE
                       </a>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Ingredients:
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       2 Russet potatoes, cut into thin fries
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1 Tbsp olive oil
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1/4 tsp garlic powder
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1 tsp dried rosemary
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Sea salt
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Black pepper
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Soak fries in water for 10 minutes, then drain, rinse and pat dry with paper towels.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-JKgipHCMKMs/TtTebYRqITI/AAAAAAAADCY/a7HaXmOSUfQ/s1600/IMG_9738.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-JKgipHCMKMs/TtTebYRqITI/AAAAAAAADCY/a7HaXmOSUfQ/s640/IMG_9738.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rmZlK3JrmSs/TtTeb8C58JI/AAAAAAAADCg/yBwXKOKnkxI/s1600/IMG_9745.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rmZlK3JrmSs/TtTeb8C58JI/AAAAAAAADCg/yBwXKOKnkxI/s640/IMG_9745.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-wGOslN7KtvA/TtTedWeNawI/AAAAAAAADCw/HBKmnFIyHPI/s1600/IMG_9755.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-wGOslN7KtvA/TtTedWeNawI/AAAAAAAADCw/HBKmnFIyHPI/s640/IMG_9755.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Combine the olive oil, garlic powder and rosemary. Toss potatoes in mixture to coat evenly. Season with salt &amp; pepper to taste.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kstgHA_EKSQ/TtTeco6t-EI/AAAAAAAADCo/tpnAm3ZPix4/s1600/IMG_9753.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kstgHA_EKSQ/TtTeco6t-EI/AAAAAAAADCo/tpnAm3ZPix4/s640/IMG_9753.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-u2wbAEJ-2dM/TtTeeCcK1NI/AAAAAAAADC4/ftgsbXu9kac/s1600/IMG_9756.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-u2wbAEJ-2dM/TtTeeCcK1NI/AAAAAAAADC4/ftgsbXu9kac/s640/IMG_9756.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-43WT8RkxX9E/TtTee1Vl6dI/AAAAAAAADDA/3HjDxhBdSxI/s1600/IMG_9757.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
