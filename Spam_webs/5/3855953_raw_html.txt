    <header class="page-header">
     <h1 class="page-title subtitled">
      Modern Maker Resources: Discover the best of Etsy with Uncoverly
     </h1>
     <h2 class="page-subtitle">
      A Useful Etsy Tool for the Modern Maker
     </h2>
     <div class="js-rescale-images background-container sq-background" id="js-background-container">
      <div class="js-image-container js-img-loader" data-imgurl="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.575221254_gj7c.jpg">
      </div>
      <div class="squares squares-top">
      </div>
     </div>
     <!--end #js-background-container -->
    </header>
    <!-- end page-header -->
    <div class="entry-content wrap-max-width clearfix">
     <section class="ninecol post-content clearfix">
      <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="Modern Maker Resources: Discover the best of Etsy with Uncoverly" data-frizzlyposturl="http://www.iamthelab.com/create-inspire-handmade/modern-maker-resources-discover-the-best-of-etsy-with-uncoverly/" type="hidden" value=""/>
      <p>
       With the recent changes that have occurred over at Etsy, from the redesigned homepage and the re-definition of what is handmade, many artists are wondering about its future. From what Im hearing, its not just the future of Etsy itself that has makers worried. Rather, its also
       <em>
        whether or not modern makers have a future with Etsy
       </em>
       . This is especially important, considering the
       <a href="http://www.bloombergview.com/articles/2015-01-14/is-etsy-crafty-enough-to-pull-off-an-ipo" target="_blank">
        recent news
       </a>
       that Etsy is considering an IPO this year. Seller sentiment is definitely
       <a href="http://www.marketwatch.com/story/etsys-own-sellers-unsure-if-theyd-invest----in-etsy-2015-01-14" target="_blank">
        not on Etsys side
       </a>
       at the moment.
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/198166767" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64872 size-full" height="633" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.640898468_cj10.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Geometric Glass Terrarium by
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/198166767/geometric-glass-terrarium-icosahedron" target="_blank">
        Waen
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/198166767" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       The frustrations are real and so is the apparent loss of income for some very established makers this past winter season. As a blogger, I have found Etsy increasingly difficult to navigate. The discovery features are lacking to say the least, and this is especially true when it comes to finding modern handmade goods to showcase.
      </p>
      <p>
       Last year, I wanted to find a handmade wallet and I literally spent hours hunting through the crazy over there. I finally found the wallet I wanted, and surprise, surprise, I found it from a maker not on Etsy. (
       <a href="http://www.noahmarion.com/leather-wallets/leather-origami-wallet" target="_blank">
        Thanks, Noah!
       </a>
       ) Wading through all the mass-produced items and crafty nonsense drives me nuts.
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/126046213" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64871 size-full" height="488" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.630375667_er43.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Red &amp; Blue Cotton Sash Cord Basket by
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/126046213/red-blue-cotton-sash-cord-basket" target="_blank">
        Citizen London
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/126046213" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       With all that said, you can imagine how pleased I was to receive a tweet from one of the founders of
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       , a new, super-smart, human-aided search engine just for Etsy. This just might be one of the best resources out there for discovering modern handmade goods and Im encouraging all of you LAB readers and artists to add this site to your daily must-read list.
      </p>
      <p>
       Ive been using
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       for the past few weeks and I really like what Im seeing. The homepage allows you to discover a wide array of fresh handmade goods. You can also search by category and a growing collection of shops.I had the privilege to interview Brendan, one of the founders of
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       , about the site, its mission and future. Enjoy the interview to learn more and the site and to discovery how to submit your shop.
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/210388914" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64875 size-full" height="475" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.679640811_mgjf.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Reclaimed Illuminated Wood Sculpture by
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/210388914/reclaimed-illuminated-wood-sculpture" target="_blank">
        Split Grain
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/210388914" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Im fully dedicated to showcasing modern makers on iamthelab.com and I used Etsy extensively. Searching through the morass of crafty-nonesense over there can be such a challenge, so your service is so needed.
         <br/>
         What moved you folks to start it?
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Well, I used to work on the data team for Etsy. And it was an incredible experience to work there and help build and support this marketplace of super creative individuals. I think Etsy has the most unique and awesome products to buy that you cant get anywhere else. But Etsy is a huge site  theres over 30 million listings on there. And it can be very overwhelming to browse and narrow it down. Its just too much.
      </p>
      <p>
       As a marketplace, Etsy has to be very objective. It cant reject a listing just because the photo quality is poor or if it wasnt made with the nicest supplies. And because of this, theres this huge range of quality, and it can take awhile to sift through it all to find that really special thing.
      </p>
      <p>
       This is why we made Uncoverly. We believe Etsy is the best place to buy meaningful goods on the Internet, and we think Uncoverly is the best way to discover them. We want to be this beautiful curated storefront to Etsy. We dont have millions of things (only thousands) but we think everything here is amazing.
      </p>
      <p>
       We think Amazon is the place to go for the things you need, but Etsy is the place for the things you want. And Uncoverly helps you find those things and helps you discover new things that you didnt even know about but now you also want.
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/195190385" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64877 size-full" height="563" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.622807445_smqe.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Waxed Canvas Weekend Getaway Bag Grey
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/195190385/waxed-canvas-weekend-getaway-bag-grey" target="_blank">
        Kith and Kin Store
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/195190385" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong>
         How does it work?
        </strong>
       </em>
       <br/>
       So, its part algorithm, and part hand selection. The algorithm filters down the universe of all Etsy products into a smaller, more manageable set of products that have a good shot of being really high quality (using a variety of factors such as number of views, or whether it was on the homepage, or if it favorited by someone who has a lot of followers, and several other things). Then we go through each product by hand to make sure the photo is amazing and the product is awesome. If so, it goes live on the site.
      </p>
      <p>
       And theres a small group of people who have the ability to add an Etsy item directly to Uncoverly. Its a small group now because we want to make sure that the quality of products stays super high (were hoping to expand that soon). But were working on ways for anyone to submit products and shops that were missing (and get the credit for discovering new gems!)  we just added this new page to help kickstart this process 
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/add" target="_blank">
        http://www.uncoverly.com/add
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/102998347" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64874 size-full" height="668" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.679008374_scz8.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Folded Paper Furoshikiby
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/102998347/folded-paper-furoshiki-black-japanese" target="_blank">
        The Link Collective
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/102998347" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong>
         What effect do you think it will have for modern makers?
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       I hope it brings them more business! And I hope they get exposure from a new audience of people.
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/205454278" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64873 size-full" height="1013" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.660921869_21rw.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Pluto Paper Mache Hanging Lamp by
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/205454278/paper-pulp-pendant-lamp-plutopaper-mache" target="_blank">
        CreaRe Design
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/205454278" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Any suggestions that will help makers become discovered by Uncoverly?
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       We think beautiful pictures are super important. When AirBnB was getting started, they hired professional photographers and send them to peoples apartments to take photos for them so that their listings would look great. We dont have any professional photographers on staff but we do have a keen eye for great product pictures.
      </p>
      <p>
       If youre a maker of awesome, unique products and youre not on Uncoverly, submit your things
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/add" target="_blank">
        here
       </a>
       or send us a note on Twitter (
       <a href="https://twitter.com/uncoverly" target="_blank">
        @uncoverly
       </a>
       ) or by email at hello@uncoverly.com.
      </p>
      <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/153192723" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Found on Uncoverly.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-64870 size-full" height="844" src="http://www.iamthelab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/il_fullxfull.569317474_e7pl.jpg" width="844"/>
      </a>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       ~ Geometric Glass Terrarium by
       <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/153192723/notebooks-pastel-colors-hand-screen" target="_blank">
        Antonin Plus Margaux
       </a>
       via
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/p/153192723" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       ~
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Are you considering an app or perhaps a WordPress search widget?
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       For now, we just have the website. But weve designed it so it works great on mobile devices too! An app is definitely on our bucket list. I hadnt considered a WordPress widget until you asked, but Im now going to look into that.
      </p>
      <p>
       Well, what do you think? Hop over and check out
       <a href="http://www.uncoverly.com/" target="_blank">
        Uncoverly
       </a>
       for yourselfand then ask any questions you might have for the team below. I looking forward to hearing what you think about this new resources for modern makers.
      </p>
      <p>
