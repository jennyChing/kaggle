                  <h2>
                   Background
                  </h2>
                  <div class="contentshort">
                   MySammy, LLC pioneered the Productivity Measurement Solution. It started as a software project for performance measurement for telecommuting employees working remotely. At the time, management experimented with the idea of allowing telecommuting employees to work from home through terminal server.To management's surprise there was no proper management software solution on the market to measure performance of telecommuting employees.A reliable management toolwas needed to measure productivity for the company since managers weren't able to see employees face to face and feared employees becoming complacent. Simply blocking non-productive web sites did not encourage productivity for the telecommuting employees. The key was to engage users' time. Therefore management pioneered a Productivity Measurement Solution known as MySammy.
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="fullpage-block" id="home-block-2">
                 <div class="clear-block block block-block" id="block-block-28">
                  <h2>
                   Did You Know
                  </h2>
                  <div class="contentshort">
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     56% of college students globally said that if they were offered a job at a company that banned access to social media, they would either turn it down, or ignore it.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     68% of college students globally believe companies should allow them to access social media and personal sites with their work-issued devices.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     87% of employees surf non-work-related websites while at work, and ofthem 53% engage in personal web surfing every day.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     <span>
                      <span>
                       56%
                      </span>
                      of employees do not worry about their email or Internet use being monitored at work.
                     </span>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     <span>
                      Only 35% of employees believe that surfing the Internet or sending non-work-related emails decreases productivity.
                     </span>
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="fullpage-block" id="home-block-2">
                 <div class="clear-block block block-block" id="block-block-28">
                  <h2>
                   Old Ways of Preventing Internet Abuse
                  </h2>
                  <div class="contentshort">
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     Dont allow users Internet access.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     Implement Internet surfing control solutions to limit accessible web sites.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     Implement monitoring software to look over users' activity.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     Dont allow instant message and voice over IP access.
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="fullpage-block" id="home-block-2">
                 <div class="clear-block block block-block" id="block-block-28">
                  <h2>
                   MySammy's Way
                  </h2>
                  <div class="contentshort">
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     To engage users' time and encourage productivity.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     To easily establish a quantifiable performance measurement.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     To help managers who travel often and cant always meet with their employees.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     Trust and Verify to empower users to excel.
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                   <p>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    MySammy is not interested in the exact details of users' computer activity, but focuses on productive time.
                   </p>
                  </div>
                 </div>
