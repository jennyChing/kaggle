        <h3>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="A_project_to_develop_a_comprehensive_compendium_of_knowledge">
          <center>
           <b>
            A project to develop a comprehensive compendium of knowledge
           </b>
          </center>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Welcome to
         <b>
          Citizendium
         </b>
         , a wiki for providing free knowledge where authors use their real, verified names. We welcome anyone who wants to share their knowledge by writing and improving articles on virtually any subject. Expert authors can be recognized with a special role, but membership is open to all.
        </p>
        <p>
         See
         <a href="/wiki/Special:RecentChanges" title="Special:RecentChanges">
          Recent Changes
         </a>
         an overview of articles we are writing
         <i>
          now
         </i>
         .
        </p>

          </a>

          <a href="/wiki/Category:CZ_Live" title="Category:CZ Live">

           approved
          </a>

           citable versions
          </a>

         </li>
         <li>
          Our community is collegial and congenial; everyone writes under his or her
          <b>
           verified real name
          </b>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          We
          <a href="/wiki/CZ:Myths_and_Facts#Myth:_most_Citizendium_articles_are_copied_from_Wikipedia." title="CZ:Myths and Facts">
           prefer new material
          </a>
          but allow work from other sites to be imported and improved under
          <a href="/wiki/CZ:Import" title="CZ:Import">
           certain conditions
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          For a brief guide on what Citizendium is and is not, see
          <i>
           <a href="/wiki/CZ:Myths_and_Facts" title="CZ:Myths and Facts">

           </a>
          </i>
          ; for more details, see the
          <a href="/wiki/CZ:FAQ" title="CZ:FAQ">
           frequently-asked questions
          </a>

           Eduzendium

          initiative.
