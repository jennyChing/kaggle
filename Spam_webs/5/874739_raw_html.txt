       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Have a night of fun for the whole family with these 20 New Years Eve
         <strong>
          Party Drink Recipes for Kids
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/20-New-Years-Eve-Party-Drink-Recipes-for-Kids-via-GrowingupGabel.com_.jpg">
          <img alt="20 New Year's Eve Party Drink Recipes for Kids via GrowingupGabel.com" class="size-full wp-image-14042 aligncenter" height="1200" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/20-New-Years-Eve-Party-Drink-Recipes-for-Kids-via-GrowingupGabel.com_.jpg" width="730"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         What was the first New Years Eve you remember as a kid? Mine is such a good memory. It was just the family that night, so we had our own little party filled with fun drinks and snacks. Mom let us bring our sleeping bags into the living room so we could all camp out and watch the ball drop. Which was good thinking on her part, she didnt have to move us if we didnt last till midnight.
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont know if my little guy will make it all the way till midnight this year, but I look forward to spending the evening with him either way. He always has a good time stay up later than usual andfilling his belly with special treats. This year I think we will add one of these fun party drink recipes for him him (and us) to enjoy. I know he will be thrilled to have one of these special sweet drinks.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/1-DSC_0169.jpg">
          <img alt="1-DSC_0169" class="size-full wp-image-14032 aligncenter" height="980" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/1-DSC_0169.jpg" width="561"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://theviewfromgreatisland.com/2014/08/minimal-monday-homemade-grape-soda.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://theviewfromgreatisland.com/2014/08/minimal-monday-homemade-grape-soda.html', 'Homemade (sugar free) Grape Soda');" target="_blank">
          Homemade (sugar free) Grape Soda
         </a>
         is a drink you can feel good about giving your kids!
        </p>
        <p>
         This
         <a href="http://www.copykat.com/2009/01/03/ruby-tuesdays-smurf-punch/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.copykat.com/2009/01/03/ruby-tuesdays-smurf-punch/', 'Ruby Tuesdays Smurf Punch');" target="_blank">
          Ruby Tuesdays Smurf Punch
         </a>
         copycat recipe is sure to be a big hit.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.copykat.com/2009/01/03/mock-champagne/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.copykat.com/2009/01/03/mock-champagne/', 'Mock champagne');" target="_blank">
          Mock champagne
         </a>
         is a sweet and bubbly drink that will make them feel all grown up.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.hoteatsandcoolreads.com/2012/07/sparkling-raspberry-and-blackberry.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.hoteatsandcoolreads.com/2012/07/sparkling-raspberry-and-blackberry.html', 'Sparkling Raspberry and Blackberry Lemonade ');" target="_blank">
          Sparkling Raspberry and Blackberry Lemonade
         </a>
         is perfect for any occasion.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Hot-Buttered-spiced-Cider-recipe-taste-and-tell-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Hot-Buttered-spiced-Cider-recipe-taste-and-tell-1" class="size-full wp-image-14038 aligncenter" height="908" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Hot-Buttered-spiced-Cider-recipe-taste-and-tell-1.jpg" width="605"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tasteandtellblog.com/hot-buttered-spiced-cider/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.tasteandtellblog.com/hot-buttered-spiced-cider/', 'Hot Buttered Spiced Cider');" target="_blank">
          Hot Buttered Spiced Cider
         </a>
         is a rich and delicious drink perfect for cold wintry nights.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.ratetheplateutah.com/2011/06/eat-in-butterbeer.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ratetheplateutah.com/2011/06/eat-in-butterbeer.html', 'Butterbeer');" target="_blank">
          Butterbeer
         </a>
         is a special treat for the Harry Potter lover in your family.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.anediblemosaic.com/?p=18231" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anediblemosaic.com/?p=18231', 'Sparkling Citrus Pineapple Ginger Sangria Mocktail');" target="_blank">
          Sparkling Citrus Pineapple Ginger Sangria Mocktail
         </a>
         , this drink is so refreshing and tasty kids wont even know it is healthy.
        </p>
        <p>
         When entertaining I love anything I can put in the crockpot, like this
         <a href="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/food/crock-pot-starbucks-caramel-apple-cider/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.clarkscondensed.com/food/crock-pot-starbucks-caramel-apple-cider/', 'Starbucks Caramel Apple Cider');" target="_blank">
          Starbucks Caramel Apple Cider
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Virgin-Strawberry-Margarita-Barbara-Bakes.jpg">
          <img alt="Virgin-Strawberry-Margarita-Barbara-Bakes" class="size-full wp-image-14035 aligncenter" height="1020" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Virgin-Strawberry-Margarita-Barbara-Bakes.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This F
         <a href="http://www.barbarabakes.com/2014/09/frozen-virgin-strawberry-margarita/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.barbarabakes.com/2014/09/frozen-virgin-strawberry-margarita/', 'rozen Virgin Strawberry Margarita');" target="_blank">
          rozen Virgin Strawberry Margarita
         </a>
         is a fruity and fun drink kids can enjoy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Make this
         <a href="http://www.thekitchenismyplayground.com/2014/11/holiday-mulled-cranberry-tea.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thekitchenismyplayground.com/2014/11/holiday-mulled-cranberry-tea.html', 'Holiday Mulled Cranberry Tea');" target="_blank">
          Holiday Mulled Cranberry Tea
         </a>
         with decaf tea bags and you have a warm holiday drink for the whole family.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.cravingsofalunatic.com/2013/08/italian-cherry-cream-sodas-guest-post-for-hungry-couple.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cravingsofalunatic.com/2013/08/italian-cherry-cream-sodas-guest-post-for-hungry-couple.html', 'Italian Cherry Cream Soda');" target="_blank">
          Italian Cherry Cream Soda
         </a>
         is a fun drink that is perfect for entertaining.
        </p>
        <p>
         This
         <a href="http://www.hoteatsandcoolreads.com/2013/04/berry-blue-jello-slime-drink-recipe.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.hoteatsandcoolreads.com/2013/04/berry-blue-jello-slime-drink-recipe.html', 'Berry BlueJello Slim Drink');" target="_blank">
          Berry BlueJello Slim Drink
         </a>
         is sure to have all the kids giggling in delight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/citruspunch-2.jpg">
          <img alt="citruspunch-2" class="size-full wp-image-14037 aligncenter" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/citruspunch-2.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.errenskitchen.com/citrus-punch/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.errenskitchen.com/citrus-punch/', 'Citrus Punch');" target="_blank">
          Citrus Punch
         </a>
         is a sweet and refreshing drink (and happens to be my sons favorite color at the moment.)
        </p>
        <p>
         These
         <a href="http://foodietots.com/2012/02/24/kid-friendly-oscars-party-and-mojito-mocktail/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://foodietots.com/2012/02/24/kid-friendly-oscars-party-and-mojito-mocktail/', 'Mojito Mocktails for kids');" target="_blank">
          Mojito Mocktails for kids
         </a>
         are perfect for any party.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.cometogetherkids.com/2011/06/glow-in-dark-drinks.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cometogetherkids.com/2011/06/glow-in-dark-drinks.html', 'Glow in the Dark Drinks');" target="_blank">
          Glow in the Dark Drinks
         </a>
         , even the big kids (my husband) will be excited about this drink.
        </p>
        <p>
         Using the same concept as this
         <a href="http://artsymomma.com/2013/07/red-white-blue-layered-kids-drink-4th-of-july.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://artsymomma.com/2013/07/red-white-blue-layered-kids-drink-4th-of-july.html', 'Red, White &amp; Blue Layered Drink');" target="_blank">
          Red, White &amp; Blue Layered Drink
         </a>
         , you could make a fun drink with whatever colors you want.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Sparkling-Cranberry-Punch-recipe-Taste-and-Tell-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Sparkling-Cranberry-Punch-recipe-Taste-and-Tell-1" class="size-full wp-image-14036 aligncenter" height="908" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Sparkling-Cranberry-Punch-recipe-Taste-and-Tell-1.jpg" width="605"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This
         <a href="http://www.tasteandtellblog.com/sparkling-cranberry-punch/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.tasteandtellblog.com/sparkling-cranberry-punch/', 'Sparkling Cranberry Punch');" target="_blank">
          Sparkling Cranberry Punch
         </a>
         is easy to make and very family friendly.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.anediblemosaic.com/?p=14734" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anediblemosaic.com/?p=14734', 'Limonana ');" target="_blank">
          Limonana
         </a>
         is a Middle Eastern frozen mint lemonade.
        </p>
        <p>
         This
         <a href="http://www.cravingsofalunatic.com/2012/07/shirley-temple-an-ode-to-a-legend-sundaysupper.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cravingsofalunatic.com/2012/07/shirley-temple-an-ode-to-a-legend-sundaysupper.html', 'Shirley Temple');" target="_blank">
          Shirley Temple
         </a>
         , is a glass of sweet cherry fun.
        </p>

         The colors and flavors make this
         <a href="http://www.copykat.com/2009/01/03/ruby-tuesdays-smurf-punch/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.copykat.com/2009/01/03/ruby-tuesdays-smurf-punch/', 'Sparkling Apple Cider Punch');" target="_blank">

         recipe perfect for the holidays.
