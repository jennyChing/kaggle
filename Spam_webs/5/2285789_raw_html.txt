     <h3 class="services-sub-header">
      Childrens Cases
     </h3>
     <p class="orange-bold">
      Our team of Lawyers and our wider network pride themselves on the weslth of experience held collectively in a wide range of children cases.
     </p>
     <p>
      We represent clients in complex cases, on occasions acting for the child themselves. We regularly attend all Courts in London and Kent to represent clients. We understand how important continuity is and how many of our clients find it a relief to go to Court with the Lawyer that has represented them from the outset and who is familiar with their case.
     </p>
     <p>
      There may be times when it is not possible for us to attend from within our in-house team or we have decided to instruct a specific Barrister to represent you at Court depending on experience and expertise. Anyone that we instruct on your behalf will be someone that we have worked with before and in whom we have confidence to achieve the best possible outcome.
     </p>
     <p>
      It is vital when dealing with this area to avoid a confrontational approach nor to lose sight of what is in the childs interests. The child's welfare is the paramount consideration in children cases and our team are guided by this principle. We aim to resolve issues relating to the upbringing of children in a conciliatory way from the outset. It is our experience that this approach enables parents to continue playing this role after parting from each other.
     </p>
