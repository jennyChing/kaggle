       <p>
        The Daily Banner has a Young and Restless Friday spoiler. It would be a real shame if the writers dont do this.
       </p>
       <p>
        Fen is back and he tells the Scooby Doo gang that he was being blackmailed.
       </p>
       <p>
        Yack dreams about when he first met Victor in prison. He yells out in Spanish again and Phyllis videotapes him with her phone.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_1262" style="width: 310px">
        <a href="http://www.thedailybanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Avery-and-Joe.jpg">
         <img alt="Young and Restless spoiler" class="size-medium wp-image-1262" height="184" src="http://www.thedailybanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Avery-and-Joe-300x184.jpg" width="300"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Images captured from Y&amp;R previews of 5-14-15
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Joe is doing well in therapy and Avery comes back to the Rehab after breaking up with Dylan. Joe tries to kiss her. She stops him and tells him she does not feel that same way about him. The Lunch crew at the Daily Banner thinks that once Joe has use of his legs back, the first thing he needs to do is kick her to the curb with them. She is so mean to him and she is lying to herself.
       </p>
       <p>
        Fen explains to the Police (Paul) and the Scooby Doo Gang that he cheated on a test and the next thing he knows, he is being blackmailed to spike their punch at the party. He really didnt think it was that big a deal and it would save his butt in school so he did it. He admits to feeling bad once people started dying.
       </p>
       <p>
        Sharon goes to Dylans. With teary eyes and an engagement ring on his pinky, he asks her to leave. Sharon goes home and reminisces about her times with Dylan. The next thing you know, Dylan is knocking on her door.
       </p>
       <p>
        Phyllis calls Victor and asks him to come over about Yack yelling out Victors name in Spanish. There is a cute and even humorous scene with them saying, one might think Jack is in love with Victor calling his name out in this sleep.
       </p>
       <p>
        Fen is refusing to go back to school without seeing his parents. They tell him he has to keep all of this stuff to himself and go back to college right after seeing the folks. Fen did give Paul the envelope that the drug came in that he used on his friends. The police department gets a match on the saliva used on the envelope and it is someone on the FBIs most wanted list.
       </p>
       <p>
        Victor and Yack are threatening each other and Victor blurts out that Yack is on the FBIs most wanted list. We finally find out that Yacks real name is MARCO!
       </p>
       <p>
        Dylan admits to Sharon that he has feelings for her and she admits that she has feelings for him and they make love in a very touching scene and in a bed in the sunlight.
       </p>
       <p>
        So right now it looks like the killer is YACK/MARCO.
       </p>
       <p>
        Monday looks heartbreaking for Michael, Lauren and Fen.
       </p>
