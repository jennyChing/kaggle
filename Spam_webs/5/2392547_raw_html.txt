           1991 Hercules K 180 Bundeswehr
           <br/>
           Country of Origin: Germany
           <br/>
           <font size="2">
            Click on any image to enlarge!
           </font>
          </center>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          We bought this Enduro motorcycle directly from the German Bundeswehr. You don't see the K-180 versions so often. They mostly used the smaller K-125 models. Like their civilian cousin, they have the single cylinder, 2-stroke Sachs motor. The K-180 was in excellent shape. We have had both models. The photo in the forrest is of the Hercules K-125. These were used exclusively by the German Bundeswehr.
