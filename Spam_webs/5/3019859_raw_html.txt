         Why is Support Needed to Children Autism?
        </h1>

         <p>
          Autism is a case of neuro developmental disorder affecting childs cognitive functions that can be in the form of both verbal(speech), or nonverbal(understanding)impairments.
         </p>
         <p>
          Out of the millions of child birth across the globe, atleast 2-3% of child population in every country are suspected to be autistic, where the rate of autism is found more common in a male child as compared to the female.
         </p>
         <p>
          There is no known cause for autism but the study holds the role of genetic inheritance more responsible than the environmental factors but sometimes both play equally important roles.Though the former drags one for lifetime,the latter can be overcome through intervention and imparting of major cognitive lessons and skills. In most of the case the childs developmental milestones are delayed but fades away with the growth.
         </p>
         <p>
          AUTISM CARE AND SERVICES:
         </p>
         <p>
          At the outset every new parents should learn about it and be vigilant to diagnose if their child is born with autism, it is quite easy to decipher with the childs behavior within the first two years. A certain behavioral pattern that hints at a childs possibility of being autistic can be diagnosed. Symptoms, such as failing to interact with the society or processing social information at a given point of time can be a major sign of autism.Hence a parent should take proper measures to make sure that this shouldn't transgress to severity.
         </p>
         <p>
          Services that take care and deal with autism should be consulted with and the possible solutions should be worked out at the earliest from letting the disorder woe and ruin the childs life.
          <a href="http://www.aschealthcare.co.uk/" target="_blank">
           Autism care and services
          </a>
          are provided to the patient according to the variable spectrum conditions.
         </p>
         <p>
          AUTISM SPECTRUM CONDITIONS
         </p>
         <p>
          As per on the classification, autistic
          <a href="http://www.aschealthcare.co.uk/about-autism/what-is-asperger-syndrome/" target="_blank">
           asperger treatment uk
          </a>
          can be of different repetitive or restrictive forms of behavior,Repetitive Behavior gauged under the Scale-Revised (RBS-R) and are categorizes as follows:
         </p>
         <p>
          Compulsive behavior is the mindset of following rules, such as keeping the volume of the TV in even numbers or objects in a Queue.
         </p>
         <p>
          Non Dynamic behavior is resistance to dynamism; for example, insisting that the color of the shirt not be changed or obsession with the same pattern, place,Thing.
         </p>
         <p>
          Ritualistic behavior involves monotonous and unvarying pattern of daily work, such as sleeping on the same edge of the bed. This is closely associated with sameness as mentioned above.
         </p>
         <p>
          Restricted behavior is limited in interest, activity or focus such as being occupied with a single song, game or a radio channel.
         </p>
         <p>
          Self-injury includes activities causing injury, such as eye-poking, thumb-biting, skin-scratching and picking, eye-poking etc.
         </p>
         <p>
          Stereotype is repetitive movement, making repetitive unusual sounds, eyelid flapping, head moving.
         </p>
         <p>
          AUTISM CARE CENTERS
         </p>
         <p>
          As autistic spectral conditions exacerbate at a faster rate,early diagnosis and intervention is very important, hence personalized developmental plans are framed by various healthcare centres to suit as per the patients unique autistic requirements.
         </p>
         <p>
          Healthcare units have special autism cells that take autism care of all the minor and majors of autism related issues.
         </p>
         <p>
          These healthcare or counselling centres as a long term measure incites the social handling acumen among the victims so that they can function independently in the society.
         </p>
