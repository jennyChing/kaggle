          A simple solution that will boost your sales
         </h2>
         <p>
          OKPAY is a fully-fledged payment service provider suitable for any type of online business. It is also the perfect complement to any of your current payment processing services. It makes it easy to introduce additional means of payment, and your products or services will be offered to a large, completely different category of customers  users of
          <strong>
           electronic money
          </strong>
          . Global statistics show that e-money turnover continues to grow each year and, with OKPAY, you only need to
          <em>
           take a few simple steps in order not to miss out on more sales!
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           OKPAY combines two of the best fundamental approaches to financial operations: the ability to receive
           <strong>
            e-currency payments
           </strong>

           </strong>
           .
