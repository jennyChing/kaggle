       <h1>
        Thrive in Five: Your Weekday Guide for an A+ in Back-to-School Wellness
       </h1>
       <div class="excerpt">
        This back-to-school season, make sure your kids get off on the right foot. Follow these tips to help their learning, both in school and at home. You will see them thrive in just five days!
       </div>
      </div>
     </header>
     <div class="wrap clearfix">
      <div class="sidebar">
       <h4>

       </h4>

      <div class="main">
       <div class="hentry page publish post-1 odd author-jyim" id="post-9139">
        <p>
         While summer is still in full swing in August, this month also brings about back-to-school season. To help children start the school year ahead, we are launching Thrive in Five! This five-day, holistic approach promotes health and wellness for children to help them thrive as they return to school. Find tips for helping your kids succeed at learning through the first week back, both in school and at home, including healthy eating, physical activity and recommendations for natural health products to optimize their learning.
        </p>
        <p>
         To get started, have a look at this fun video explaining many of the health challenges involved in not only learning, but growing up, too!
        </p>
        <h3>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/1V6PMCGu3g4" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Weve developed daily blogs for the first week back to school, so make sure to read each in detail. To help you stay on top of all of the information weve provided, here is an infographic summarizing the key points. Print it out, put it on your fridge, or anywhere and everywhere around the house, and stick to it! Help your kids by building these healthy habits now and theyll thank you in the years ahead.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Infographic.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-9512" height="843" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Infographic.jpg" title="Infographic" width="512"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/thrive-in-five/monday-start-strong-the-abcs-of-the-first-morning-back/https:/www.chfa.ca/thrive-in-five/monday-start-strong-the-abcs-of-the-first-morning-back/">
          Monday: Start Strong: The ABCs of the First Morning Back
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Saying goodbye to a summer filled with late nights and sleep-ins isnt easy. The first day back can be daunting, but CHFA is here to help your kids start off the week strong with expert advice for their first morning. Follow these tips to ensure your kids start the new school year energized and alert!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/thrive-in-five/tuesday-get-productive-boost-your-brainpower/https:/www.chfa.ca/thrive-in-five/tuesday-get-productive-boost-your-brainpower/">
          Tuesday: Get Productive  Boost Your Brainpower
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Memories of a fun-filled weekend often begin to fade by Tuesday, and with a full week ahead, its essential to equip your childrens brains with the necessary tools to help them make the most out of each lesson. Interestingly, studies have found Tuesday to be the most productive weekday. At CHFA, we encourage everyone to embrace this day of productivity and add some additional steps to boost your brain power.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/thrive-in-five/wednesday-build-your-mid-week-momentum/">
          Wednesday: Build Your Mid-Week Momentum
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youve made it through Monday and Tuesday, your kids are exercising, eating better, feeling good, and the end of the week is in sight. Now, we just need to get over the Wednesday hump with a smile! CHFA has gathered the top tips to help your kids stay energized and take on Wednesdays with a skip in their step.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/thrive-in-five/throwback-thursday-review-your-progress/">
          Thursday: Throwback Thursday  Review Your Progress
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youve been following our daily tips for the first week back at school, we encourage you to throw back your Thursday and reflect on your childrens progress. Determine what has been working well, and what needs more focus. You may find these tips from CHFA helpful in making the most of your holistic back-to-school efforts.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/thrive-in-five/friday-fun-day-get-ready-for-the-weekend/https:/www.chfa.ca/thrive-in-five/friday-fun-day-get-ready-for-the-weekend/">
          Friday: Friday Fun-Day: Get Ready for the Weekend!
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The school week has come to a close and its time to celebrate with some good quality family time. CHFA has some suggestions to help you make the most of your Friday and keep your kids smiling all weekend long.
        </p>
