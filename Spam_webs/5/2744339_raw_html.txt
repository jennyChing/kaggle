          <strong>
           2015 Latest Collection of Khussa Shoes Designs and Trends 
          </strong>
          There are many styles and designs in clothing items. However, only clothes dont fall in stylish look category but there are accessories too that can make you groom well. One such example being shoes and it can be some other accessories too that play a very important role to groom your personality.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Khussa Shoes Designs | Latest Trend in Pakistan &amp; India
         </h2>
         <p>
          Talking of shoes there are of variety shoes but khussa shoes is in fashon now a days. Khussa shoes are liked equally by men and women. Girls like to wear khussa shoes on their wedding festivals, such as on mayun, mehandi function and even on reception. Khussa shoes are available in market in a lot of different designs and styles. The supreme thing about khussa shoes is the material with which is it made up of. Basic material of Khussa shoes is leather while it is embellished with some other materials such as beads and sequins which are more commonly used on traditional motifs. The most impotant thing which make Khussa a uniqe shoe and very alluring is hand work beads, dubka, tilla, mirror, ribbons and many other things that are used for its manufacturing.
         </p>
         <p>
          Khussa shoes are very popular in Pakistan and India as well among men and women as it is very popular and considered as a traditional wear in South Asian culture. Indian women wear stylish khussa shoes on festivals such as Rakhi, Lori, Holi, during their pujas and also in wedding functions. Khussa shoes are best to wear with salwar kameez, pajama and froks with trousers and jeans also.
         </p>
