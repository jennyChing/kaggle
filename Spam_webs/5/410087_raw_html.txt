        <p>
         Mothers need to learn more about live online consultations and the video consultation series to determine if a Mommy Makeover is the right option for them, announces PasadenaSurgeons.com
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span>
          Pasadena, CA, United States of America  April 1, 2015 /MarketersMedia/
         </span>
         
        </p>
        <p>
         Pasadena Surgeons encourages moms who have yet to take advantage of the live consultations online and the five-part video consultation to do so now, as summer is quickly approaching. With the help of these live, one-on-one consultations, which may be completed via iPhone FaceTime or Skype, individuals speak to Shankar Lakshman, MD to determine their goals and which procedure best meets their needs. In addition, women find the five-part video consultation series to be of great assistance in narrowing the options.
        </p>
        <p>
         Women appreciate having access to these two options, as it allows them to learn more about the procedures offered and what may be of benefit to them. Many find the video consultation to be of great assistance, as it provides the same initial consultation one receives when visiting in the office. New patients learn how the surgeons work and other basic information about what they offer, Dr. Shankar Lakshman, principal surgeon and founder of Pasadena Surgeons (
         <a class="colorbox" href="http://www.pasadenasurgeons.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          www.PasadenaSurgeons.com
         </a>
         ), explains.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pasadena Surgeons wants to ensure every woman interested in learning more about a Mommy Makeover has the opportunity to do so. Offering consultations online remains a great way to do so, as the patient doesnt have to be present. She can learn all she wishes to and ask questions of a doctor, while the doctor receives the ability to share his or her expertise and artistry with the patient. The doctor explains how the procedure helps a woman get her pre-baby figure back once again.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dr. Lakshman explains the procedure may be used to remove loose skin and extra fat in the abdominal area or correct sagging breasts. Women in good physical health tend to be good candidates for the procedures, and the procedures take place simultaneously. This reduces the amount of time one needs for recovery, a concern of many new mothers caring for a young child.
        </p>
        <p>
         The video consultation series involves a conversation with Annaliza, a recent Mommy Makeover patient. The video series covers those questions most women have about a procedure of this type and those which would be covered during an in-office consultation. Women often find they now have all of the information needed to make a knowledgeable decision once they finish viewing the series.
        </p>
        <p>
         Be sure to sign up for the live one-on-one consultation and/or the five part video consultation series. The goal is to allow each woman to feel comfortable with her decision. The more one knows about the procedures, the more likely this is to be the case. In addition, one can see before and after photos to learn about the results which may be obtained, Dr. Lakshman continues.
        </p>
        <p>
         All doctors are fully licensed and board certified and also possess the experience one needs when undergoing one or more procedures of this type. All also have outstanding patient reviews and work to ensure the patients needs are met in every way. For this reason, patients have a great experience when working with Pasadena Surgeons (
         <a class="colorbox" href="http://www.pasadenasurgeons.com/cosmetic/what-is-a-mommy-makeover/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          PasadenaSurgeons.com
         </a>
         ), thus every woman considering this type of procedure should take advantage of these offerings today.
        </p>
