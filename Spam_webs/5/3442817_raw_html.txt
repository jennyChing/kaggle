            <p>
             The
             <a href="http://www.robertsradio.co.uk/Products/Internet_radios/STREAM_202/index.htm">
              Roberts Stream 202
             </a>
             is the worlds first portable DAB (digital), FM and WiFi Internet radio in one unit. Its not the prettiest thing weve ever seen, but its certainly crammed full of tech-ness. As well as featuring just about every radio flavour available, it has twin alarms, features a listen againfunction for you to savourpreviously broadcast BBC progs,canplay MP3, WMA and Real content, stream music from your PCor an external music player andruns off 6 massive D sized battery cells. Were talking radio haze here, baby. 149.99.

             <em>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             The STREAM 202 is the Worlds first truly portable DAB, FM and internet radio. Connect to radio stations around the world as well as access a listen again feature that allows you to listen to previously broadcast BBC programmes at your leisure. Browsing stations is really simple thanks to on-screen information that is automatically updated and conveniently sorted into country or genre. All you need to get started is a broadband internet connection with either a wireless router or wired Ethernet connection. You can navigate through the intuitive menu system to find what you want to listen to.
