         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          The next era in document capture: web-based capture
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          By
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.informationdynamix.com/author/petrabeck/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Petra Beck
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://www.informationdynamix.com/next-era-document-capture-web-based-capture/#respond">
            <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="262 http://www.informationdynamix.com/?p=262">
             Leave a Comment
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <div class="at-above-post addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="The next era in document capture: web-based capture" data-url="http://www.informationdynamix.com/next-era-document-capture-web-based-capture/">
         </div>
         <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Document Capture has come a long way: starting with centralized mailroom scanning applications, then branching into decentralized scanning allowing to capture documents at the earliest point of entry into the organization.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Businesses have put a lot of energy into making their document scanning applications more efficient in an effort to increase the productivity of their business process. Despite all these efforts, in many business environments document capture remains a complex and costly part of the workflow.
          <span id="more-262">
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_264" style="width: 610px">
          <a href="http://www.informationdynamix.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Web-based-capture-solving-the-document-capturing-complexity.jpg">
           <img alt="Web-based capture solving the document capturing complexity" class="size-full wp-image-264" height="269" src="http://www.informationdynamix.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Web-based-capture-solving-the-document-capturing-complexity.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Web-based capture solving the document capturing complexity
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Web-based capture technology allows to address these challenges, in particular in a large organization with many decentralized capture locations.For instance, in a bank with many branch offices deploying and updating scanning software used to be a major undertaking, tied up IT staff for weeks and was very disruptive for the users. The cost involved from an IT support point of view plus the cost for annual licenses sometimes increased by infrastructure upgrade requirements were a significant portion of the operating cost.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Web-based capture allows to deploy software updates within minutes and provides a very cost effective alternative from a licensing perspective.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          For bank clerks document scanning used to be a necessary evil as it required them to leave their customer system to access the scanning application and turn their attention away from their customers.
         </p>
         <p>
          A fully integrated
          <a href="http://uk.knowledgeshare.kodakalaris.com/kodak-info-input-web-based-capture/" target="_blank">
           web-based document capturing solution
          </a>
          allows the clerk to stay focused on their customer, capture the document without leaving their application when for example processing a mortgage application, which results in a much more positive experience for the customer.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.informationdynamix.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Web-based-capture.gif">
           <img alt="Web-based capture" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-263" height="147" src="http://www.informationdynamix.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Web-based-capture.gif" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Web-based capture technology is taking document scanning into the next phase of productive and cost efficient capture responding at the increased expectations for customer experience at the same time.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://graphics.kodak.com/docimaging/GB/en/industry_solutions/position_papers/position_papers/web_based_capture_reduces_costs/index.htm" target="_blank" title="Web Based Capture Reduces Costs">
           <strong>
            Web-based capture reduces costs, raises revenues and improves customer satisfaction. Discover how in the latest white paper published by Kodak Alaris Information Management.
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
