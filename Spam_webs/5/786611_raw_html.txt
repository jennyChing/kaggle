           <p>
            Who says brown and beige are the perfect combination for a boring space? Im about to prove to you that other than relaxing, an area that uses this colors is far from being dull! Bringing in some woodsy colors, or even sands, is a great way to add a relaxing feeling to your surroundings.
           </p>
           <a href="http://www.knowhowshedoesit.com/">
            <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-27820 " height="343" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Natural-Design-6-630x343.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           Adding plants to any room in your home can contribute to the overall fresh feeling of the space.
           <p>
            When I chose my accents for this living room I combined the raw, but elegant, wood slabswith some amber-colored recycled vases (all from HomeGoods). This might seem like a clash of materials, but as you can see, both can get along very well.
           </p>
           <a href="http://www.knowhowshedoesit.com/">
            <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-27819 " height="343" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Natural-Design-5-630x343.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           Accessorize that lifeless ottoman with a simple, natural fiber tray (mine is from HomeGoods) that you can either decorate with flowers or use to serve your guests some nice refreshments.
           <p>
            In my living room, I used one of the walls as my gallery. But, instead of framed art, I decided to hanged various baskets and mirrors all with the same principle: the circular form. I used two of them to incorporate some greens, one with an orchid and the other with air plants (so easy to take care of!). Because I love to craft, I created the golden mirror from a driftwood sculpture I previously owned. You can read the tutorial
            <a href="http://www.knowhowshedoesit.com/2013/12/re-using-driftwood.html#.UvG2SfldWSo">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <a href="http://www.knowhowshedoesit.com/">
            <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-27818 " height="343" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Natural-Design-4-630x343.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           Amber, browns, brass and sands are the main colors in this living room.
           <p>
            I love how many treasures I found at my local HomeGoods.I can honestly say that this space is my favorite spot to unwind after a hectic day. And if you dont believe me, ask this cute wooden owl (a
            <a href="http://statigr.am/viewer.php#/tag/fanfinds/" target="_blank">
             FanFinds
            </a>
            from one of my HomeGoods journeys).
           </p>
           <a href="http://www.knowhowshedoesit.com/">
            <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-27817 " height="343" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Natural-Design-3-630x343.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           This owl and the driftwood branch are part of my collection of FanFinds from HomeGoods.
           <p>
            I hope you enjoyed my natural inspired living room. Whats in your living room? Maybe you are a sea person, or an ultra modern one. Share your thoughts with me.
           </p>
