           Win Polar Frost Goodies
          </h1>
         </div>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Last weekend I decided to get up early and go for a bike ride. The weather was beautiful and the roads were fairly quiet, probably because I was cycling on country roads.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: right;">
          <a href="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/buy-online.jpg">
           <img alt="Polar Frost" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1461" height="269" src="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/buy-online.jpg" width="319"/>
          </a>
          <span id="more-1455">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          I set myself a mission, which was to cycle to Porthcurno from Newlyn and back again. About 16 miles altogether with a couple of killer hills! When I reached the turning to Porthcurno I started calculating a route home which would allow me to avoid anymore killer hills. I was enjoying the ride so I decided I would cycle to Lands End through Sennen and maybe cycle back along the A30.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Cycle Cornwall" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1459" height="600" src="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/image1-600x600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I decided that as I hadnt taken that long to get to Sennen, I would cycle to St Just then through Sanceed on to Drift before heading to Newlyn. It took me roughly 2 hours to cycle 22 miles, this is the route I took:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-shot-2014-06-01-at-15.42.48.png">
           <img alt="Cycling Sunday" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1456" height="397" src="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-shot-2014-06-01-at-15.42.48-600x397.png" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          When I got home Lulu wanted to go out for a walk. You cannot say no to this face:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/image2-e1401634589114.jpg">
           <img alt="My best friend " class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1460" height="600" src="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/image2-e1401634589114-600x600.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I took her on a walk from Newlyn to Mousehole, up through Paul and back to Penzance. This was roughly 3.9 miles. When we reached Mousehole at roughly 11:30am my friend called me to see if I would like to go horse riding and to meet her at the yard in an hour. We spent roughly 2 hours horse riding near Madron.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-shot-2014-06-01-at-15.55.54.png">
           <img alt="Newlyn to Mousehole Loop" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1457" height="349" src="http://acornishgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-shot-2014-06-01-at-15.55.54-600x349.png" width="600"/>
          </a>
          When Sunday came I was very sore, my muscles were aching. Luckily I had some
          <a href="http://www.polarfrostuk.com/">
           Polar Frost
          </a>
          to sooth the pain. Polar Frost is a cold gel which is used to treat soft tissue injuries, stress injuries, muscle tension, inflammation and rheumatic and other pain. I was impressed at how fast Polar Frost works and it smells a lot nicer than Deep Heat.
         </p>
