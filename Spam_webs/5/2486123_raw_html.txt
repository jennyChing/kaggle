        <p>
         "Will they or won't they?" It's a question as old as whatever the early Holocene version of
         <i>
          Friends
         </i>
         was (
         <i>
          Tribe
         </i>
         ?). And it's the secret rom-com within all things Detective Comics. Will Batman find true love and start a more nuclear Bat Family with the only woman who's ever really understood him? Or will that not happen so that more comic book storylines can?
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <a href="http://bit.ly/T5OrbA" target="_blank">
          <img data-img="http://i.crackedcdn.com/phpimages/article/4/2/7/281427_v1.jpg" height="539" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Come on, Bats: She's smart, sexy, disguised, and dressed in black, and she has a secret identity based on an animal, but not in
         <a href="http://www.cracked.com/photoplasty_591_27-insane-but-true-early-versions-famous-characters/?utm_source=twitter&amp;utm_medium=fanpage&amp;utm_campaign=new+article&amp;wa_ibsrc=fanpage" target="_blank">
          some weirdly literal way
         </a>
         . It's time those two crazy kids put their specific moral codes aside and got their matching insanities together. And in case you (the reader and/or Bruce) have never quite seen Catwoman as the classy lady she always has been, we asked frequent Redbubble designer
         <a href="http://www.redbubble.com/people/baznet" target="_blank">
          Baznet
         </a>
         to mash her up with that poster everyone has in their kitchen next to wine. See? She's got Le Chat Noir elegance and Le Five Finger Discount practicality. If a Wayne/Kyle wedding isn't on the horizon, they might as well stop writing plucky DA/journalist/Al-Ghul love interests for that cowled nutcase.
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1wRAIV8" target="_blank">
          <img data-img="http://i.crackedcdn.com/phpimages/article/4/0/9/281409_v1.jpg" height="539" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then again, maybe he's just a guy who can't settle down. It's hard enough being rich and handsome your whole life, let alone a traumatized crime-fighting orphan. Try behaving normally or committing to one person when you've got "
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6poX_8CT9kM" target="_blank">
          ignore Katie Holmes to wine and dine two wet models at once
         </a>
         " swag. That's why this design from Cracked fan/
         <i>
          Twin Peaks
         </i>
         superfan
         <a href="http://www.cracked.com/members/AgentCoop/" target="_blank">
          AgentCoop
         </a>
         brings together Bruce Wayne's vigilante basement and grotto-ready upstairs manor, right down to the bat-eared replacement for Playboy's
         <a href="http://www.cracked.com/article_20213_7-mind-blowing-easter-eggs-hidden-in-famous-publications.html" target="_blank">
          creepily ubiquitous bunny
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h2 class="subheading">
         There's More DC Where That Came From
        </h2>
        <p align="center">
         <a href="http://store.cracked.com/products/the-gotham-batmen" target="_blank">
          <img data-img="http://i.crackedcdn.com/phpimages/article/4/1/1/281411_v1.jpg" height="484" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         We already put together the perfect tee for summer, and for a nice game of company softball in between rounds of
         <a href="http://cracked.tumblr.com/post/66702533575/um-hello-i-still-have-my-little-batman" target="_blank">
          trying to intimidate your boss who is Morgan Freeman
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <a href="http://store.cracked.com/products/party-on-bane" target="_blank">
          <img data-img="http://i.crackedcdn.com/phpimages/article/4/1/3/281413_v1.jpg" height="484" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then we went deeper into the Christopher Nolan trilogy/
         <i>
          Star Wars
         </i>
         original trilogy/Wayne Campbell
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tbd8EAMyGwg" target="_blank">
          trilogy-in-waiting
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <a href="http://store.cracked.com/products/reaction-comics-asphalt" target="_blank">
          <img data-img="http://i.crackedcdn.com/phpimages/article/4/1/5/281415_v1.jpg" height="484" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And don't forget about Superman! That green Packard from
         <a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/nicolas-cage-superman-comic-record-2-million-sale-267770" target="_blank">
          Nic Cage's former prized possession
         </a>
         's cover sure didn't.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h2 class="subheading">
         Our Weekly Dose of Envy
        </h2>
        <p>
         Going off of that Supes shirt, there's never any point in beating yourself up over old mistakes. Yes, you could have gone for that promotion or chatted up that stranger or
         <a href="http://www.cracked.com/blog/5-superheroes-who-shouldve-gotten-movies-before-ant-man/" target="_blank">
          greenlit a standalone Wonder Woman movie that could have made you literally made of money
         </a>
         . But it's simply too late, just like it's too late for us to make any of these five great new shirt ideas ourselves. All we can do is tip our
         <a href="http://images.wikia.com/marvel_dc/images/5/55/Riddler_(Batman_1966_TV_Series)_005.jpg" target="_blank">
          1960s question-mark bowler
         </a>
         to you (the competition) and help you (the citizen) enjoy and buy these great shirts for your wearing pleasure.
        </p>
