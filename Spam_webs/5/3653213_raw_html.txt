       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Laundry Basket Drawers
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2011-10-05T08:00:00+00:00">
         October 5, 2011
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://roadkillrescue.net/author/beckiefarrant/" rel="author">
           Beckie
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         After completing a cubby storage project,
         <a href="http://asprinkleofthis.blogspot.com/2011/10/trash-to-treasure-part-2.html" target="_blank">
          Denise from A Sprinkle of This . . . . A Dash of That
         </a>
         still had a stack of 1x12s to use. The boards had originated as a bookshelf Denise got through Freecycle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://asprinkleofthis.blogspot.com/2011/10/trash-to-treasure-part-2.html" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Freecycle Boards" border="0" height="359" src="http://roadkillrescue.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Freecycle-Boards.png" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Freecycle Boards" width="538"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This time around it was the laundry room that needed organizing, and Denise followed
         <a href="http://ana-white.com/2010/11/laundry-basket-dresser" target="_blank">
          Ana Whites
         </a>
         plans for laundry basket drawers, modifying slightly to accommodate the dimensions of her repurposed wood. Laundry baskets take a chunk of space, so this is a fabulous way to get them up off the floor. I love how Denise labeled each family members basket.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://asprinkleofthis.blogspot.com/2011/10/trash-to-treasure-part-2.html" target="_blank">
          <img alt="laundry basket drawers" border="0" height="573" src="http://roadkillrescue.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/laundry-basket-drawers.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="laundry basket drawers" width="392"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Head on over to A
         <a href="http://asprinkleofthis.blogspot.com/2011/10/trash-to-treasure-part-2.html" target="_blank">
          Sprinkle of This A Dash of That
         </a>
         for the tutorial.
        </p>
