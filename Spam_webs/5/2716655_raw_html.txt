                  Christmas is coming and you cannot miss the best Christmas markets in London. Lights are everywhere and the atmosphere is truly magical. When I travel, going to markets is one of the things I love more. Ill talk you about the charm of many other markets around Europe such as the ones in the beautiful city of
                  <a href="http://www.travellector.com/tag/munich/" target="_blank">
                   Munich
                  </a>
                  . Today I will show you the best Christmas markets in London, a city I love and where I lived for a few months. I also spent a great weekend in London at Christmas. During this period, London is enchanting and markets intensify its charm.

                  <span style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;">
                   <strong>
                    For everyone (adults and children):
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <p>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    - Winter Wonderland
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . It is my favorite market. This Bavarian-style market, which takes place in
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Hyde Park
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , with 200 wooden chalets and stalls, is lit by thousands of tiny lights that will catapult you into a fairytale world. The
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    entrance to the market is free
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , but you have to
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    pay for the attractions
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  .
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Lycamobile
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  is a glittering ice rink. The
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Magical Kingdom Ice
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  is a kingdom of ice with a forest and a castle, very nice both for kids and adults.
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Giant Wheel
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  is a stunning 60-metres-high wheel where you will enjoy an enchanting view of London. It is also accessible to wheelchair users. Then there are
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Zippos Circus
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  and the
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Ice Bar
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . If your kids want to meet Santa Claus they can do it at the Santa Land from 10am to 6pm. It is free!
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/rosedavies/2108822501" target="_blank">
                   <img alt="Winter Wonderland Giant Wheel" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-969 size-large" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Winter-Wonderland-Giant-Wheel-592x444.jpg" height="444" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                   <noscript>
                    <img alt="Winter Wonderland Giant Wheel" class="aligncenter wp-image-969 size-large" height="444" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Winter-Wonderland-Giant-Wheel-592x444.jpg" width="592"/>
                   </noscript>
                  </a>
                  Bars, stalls with handmade gifts, traditional German delicacies, such as spicy sweets,
                  <em>
                   glhwein
                  </em>
                  (spicy mulled wine), beers, waffles (try waffles with white chocolate!) and more. You can find everything at Winter Wonderland. The prices are a bit expensive. When I tasted the glhwein I payed 5!
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/garryknight/11534786366" target="_blank">
                   <img alt="Winter Wonderland in London, brewery" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-970 size-large" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Winter-Wonderland-in-London-brewery-592x392.jpg" height="392" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                   <noscript>
                    <img alt="Winter Wonderland in London, brewery" class="aligncenter wp-image-970 size-large" height="392" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Winter-Wonderland-in-London-brewery-592x392.jpg" width="592"/>
                   </noscript>
                  </a>
                  My advice is to
                  <a href="http://www.hydeparkwinterwonderland.com/tickets/" target="_blank">
                   book online for the attractions
                  </a>
                  , especially for your session at skating ice rink. Try to arrive 20 minutes before your session starts. The market is open everyday from
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    November 21 to January 4 2015
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , from
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    10am to 10pm
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . It is close on Christmas Day.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="color: #000000;">
                     -
                    </span>
                    Southbank Centres Christmas Market
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . This market takes place at the
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Southbank Belvedere Road
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , along the Thames.
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/heatheronhertravels/3079941069" target="_blank">
                   <img alt="Southbank Market in London view by night" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-966 size-large" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Southbank-Market-in-London-view-by-night-592x394.jpg" height="394" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                   <noscript>
                    <img alt="Southbank Market in London view by night" class="aligncenter wp-image-966 size-large" height="394" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Southbank-Market-in-London-view-by-night-592x394.jpg" width="592"/>
                   </noscript>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/heatheronhertravels/3079941069" target="_blank">
                   <img alt="Southbank Market in London stalls" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-962 size-large" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Southbank-Market-in-London-stalls-592x394.jpg" height="394" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                   <noscript>
                    <img alt="Southbank Market in London stalls" class="aligncenter wp-image-962 size-large" height="394" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Southbank-Market-in-London-stalls-592x394.jpg" width="592"/>
                   </noscript>
                  </a>
                  About 80 wooden chalet-style stalls sell traditional German food and drink, such as
                  <em>
                   bratwurst
                  </em>
                  ,
                  <em>
                   chnitzels
                  </em>
                  , and crepes, decorations, candles, craft. There is a fab carousel at the centre of the market. The thing I like about this market is the
                  <a href="http://www.southbankcentre.co.uk/whatson/festivals-series/winter-festival-with-natwest/productions%20" target="_blank">
                   variety of events and concerts
                  </a>
                  , free or cheap, it has to offer. You can also
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    attend workshops
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  ! Take part to the free workshop 
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Get Your Knit On
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                   at the foyer area of Royal Festival Hall from
                  <strong>
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    6 December 2014 at 10am to 11 January 2015 at 10pm
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  . You will learn to knit blankets for helping old people.
                  <a href="http://www.southbankcentre.co.uk/whatson/get-your-knit-on-1000632" target="_blank">
                   More detail about this workshop here
                  </a>
                  . The market is open from
                  <strong>
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    November 11 2014 to January 4 2015, everyday from 10 am to 9 pm
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  . Friday and Saturday it is open until 10pm.
                 </p>
                 <blockquote>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    TIP
                   </strong>
                   : Southbank Centres Christmas Market is right next to the Southbank Centres Book Market, under the Waterloo Bridge, a market where you can buy old and second-hand books.
                  </p>
                 </blockquote>
                 <p>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    - Winterville 2014
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . This is a really great event held at
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Victoria Park
                   </strong>
                   ,
                  </span>
                  from
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    December 2, 2014 to January 1, 2015, from 10am to late
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . Closed on December 25 and 26. The entrance to the village is free. You can take part to free and paid events. There is a
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Ice Rink
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  and you can also buy the ticket for the
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    pantomime of Robin Hood
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , which is held at the
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Spiegeltent
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , a pavilion created for events. The cost of the pantomime is 10 per adult and 7 per child. If you are interested look at the dates here.Horse-drawn carriage rides through the Victoria Park. One of the most interesting attractions is the Wall of Death, a motordrome where Motorcycle stunt riders perform.
                  <a href="http://winterville.co.uk/" target="_blank">
                   More details about Winterville 2014
                  </a>
                  .
                  <em>
                   Photo credit winterville.co.uk
                  </em>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img alt="Horse drawn carriages Winterland" class="lazy aligncenter size-large wp-image-960" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Horse-drawn-carriages-winterland-592x652.jpg" height="652" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                 </p>
                 <noscript>
                  <img alt="Horse drawn carriages Winterland" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-960" height="652" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Horse-drawn-carriages-winterland-592x652.jpg" width="592"/>
                 </noscript>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   - Leadenhall Market
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 . It is a very ancient market with Victorian glass roof and cobbled walkways. In the past it was used to sell cheese and poultry. There are many shops, restaurants and pubs. Some scenes of
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   Harry Potter
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 have been shot here. You can buy accessories, handmade toys. What I like about this market is that there are live choral music and Christmas decorations that will make you feel the Christmas spirit.
                 <p>
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/simon__syon/11447031296" target="_blank">
                   <img alt="Leadenhall Market in London" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-961 size-large" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Leadenhall-Market-in-London-592x395.jpg" height="395" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                   <noscript>
                    <img alt="Leadenhall Market in London" class="aligncenter wp-image-961 size-large" height="395" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Leadenhall-Market-in-London-592x395.jpg" width="592"/>
                   </noscript>
                  </a>
                  <br/>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    - Apple Market
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . I love the atmosphere of Apple Market, the permanent market in Covent Garden. During Christmas time, there are big red balls hanging from the ceiling. You can buy accessories, jewelry, handmade items, souvenirs London at stalls. Then, you will be speechless looking at the beautiful Christmas tree with 150000 lights and the giant silvery reindeer in the Square
                  <strong>
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    every Saturday until Christmas from 12pm to 4pm
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/davebass5/8268255980" target="_blank">
                   <img alt="Apple Market in London red balls" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-958 size-large" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Market-in-London-red-balls-592x394.jpg" height="394" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="592"/>
                   <noscript>
                    <img alt="Apple Market in London red balls" class="aligncenter wp-image-958 size-large" height="394" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Market-in-London-red-balls-592x394.jpg" width="592"/>
                   </noscript>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                   <span style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;">
                    <strong>
                     For all designer and art lovers
                    </strong>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  :
                  <br/>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    - Dazzle @ Oxo
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . This is the market for jewelry lovers. It is held at
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Oxo Tower Wharf
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  , in
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    South Bank
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . Jewelry designers from around the world show their unique pieces of jewelry. The designers are just graduates but they have a great talent. It is held from
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    December 9 to January 11
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  .
                  <a href="http://www.dazzle-exhibitions.com/" target="_blank">
                   More details
                  </a>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    - The White Christmas Fair
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . This is a really nice market with 40 stalls for lovers of fashion, homeaccessories and design. You can also taste pizza and champagne and visit the London Gin Distillery Company (tour with free gin tastings are organized). It is
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    held at Battersea, 33 Parkgate Road, on December 6-7
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . The event will raise money for a childrens charity.
                  <a href="http://www.whitechristmasfair.co.uk/" target="_blank">
                   More details
                  </a>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;">
                     For food lovers:
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  <br/>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    - Real Food Christmas Market
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  . Dont miss this market! It takes place
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    at South Bank
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  just behind the Royal Festival Hall. It is a triumph of flavors. You can taste seasonal products such as cheese, ham, cakes, pies. You can visit it from
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    December 19 to 23
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  .
                  <a href="http://www.realfoodfestival.co.uk/" target="_blank">
                   More details about times
                  </a>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Are you ready for your best Christmas markets tour in London? Let me know if you have visited other markets in London around Christmas time.
                  <img alt=":-)" class="lazy wp-smiley" data-original="http://www.travellector.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif" src="http://www.travellector.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif"/>
                 </p>
