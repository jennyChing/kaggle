       <ol>
        <li>
         Highlighting adds so much dimension to your look! Just grab a cream or powder thats a few shades lighter than your skin tone added bonus if its a little shimmery! Everything you lighten on your face brings it forward visually so use your brush or fingers to add your highlighter to the high points of your face or places that need a little light!
         <span id="more-16272">
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         Dust it on the tops of your cheekbones to make them look higher!
        </li>
        <li>
         On the bridge of your nose to slim it!
        </li>
        <li>
         On your cupids bow above your mouth to make your lips look fuller!
        </li>
        <li>
         On the center of your chin for a sharper jawline!
        </li>
        <li>
         The center of your forehead for added dimension!
        </li>
        <li>
         The inner socket of your eye to make them look more open and awake!
        </li>
        <li>
         And right under your brow for a more pronounced eye!
        </li>
       </ol>
