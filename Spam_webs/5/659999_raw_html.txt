            <p>
             <span style="line-height: 1.3em;">
              Every couple has a story. How do you express their unique personalities, a shared interest or perhaps a quirky history to the world through one photo shoot: an engagement session?
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             Engagement photos are popular photography sessions and on the "to-do" list for most newly engaged couples. A special photo shoot all about showing off the love and special connection of a couple, oozing with romantic poses and special looks can be one of the most fun kind of shoots for a photographer, especially when the client has a specific and unique theme in mind.
            </p>
            <p>
             Engagement photo shoots can be extra fun for a couple who aren't often under the spotlight of a professional photographer, and a chance for the couple to take a creative idea and run with it.
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="line-height: 1.3em;">
              Here are some fabulously creative and bold engagement sessions we found across the web. Let us know which your favorite is! What's the funniest or coolest engagement photography you've seen?
             </span>
            </p>
