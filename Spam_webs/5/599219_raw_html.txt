                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                   Aging is a natural process which cannot be stopped and it brings along many changes in the human body, one of which is the incidence of fine lines and wrinkles over the face which many individuals find most troublesome. A lot of people, particularly women, dread these wrinkles as they ruin the beauty and reveal their age. If you are trying to find ways to remove these fine lines, wrinkles on forehead and crows' feet, the best option is to choose some holistic treatment methods which are safe and effective. Those who desire instant results opt to choose surgical treatments. But these procedures are not recommended since they have their own list of side effects besides being very expensive. The only way to have permanent remedy is by opting for natural remedies which do take time to reveal the results, but are safe and risk free.
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                   Water is a drink that is bestowed with many properties. It not just keeps the body hydrated at all times, but additionally flushes out the toxins and destroys free radicals which are responsible for the development of wrinkles on forehead. Adequate quantity of water in skin cells prevents the surface from drying up and forming wrinkles.
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                   Fast food joints encourage people to pull over and grab that pack of French fries or potato wedges. Though they arescrumptious and satisfying, these foods are considered to be ?junk? foods which do not contribute to the health of the body. If anything, these food items are harmful for health because they contain free radicals which destroy the healthy skin cells and prevent the formation of new ones. This makes the skin layers lifeless and dull, besides leading to the development of wrinkles.
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                   A good anti wrinkle cream can also be very useful in removing the wrinkles on forehead. There are a variety of anti wrinkle skin creams that are flooding the marketplace today. But the most effective ones are the skin creams which have a higher composition of holistic components that are abundant with antioxidants. Examples of the components which can work wonders on your skin are grapeseed oil and Phytessence Wakame (Japanese sea weed). These ingredients are rich in antioxidants that are capable of nullifying the harmful effects of free radicals in the body and encouraging the development of new skin cells.
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <span style='font-family: Georgia,"Times New Roman",serif;'>
                   Collagen and elastin are two enzymes which are very important for the skin. The body produces these skin proteins in sufficient quantities. But, as the person ages, these skin proteins are produced in lesser quantities which contributes to the development of crows feet, wrinkles on forehead and upper lip wrinkles. These fine lines can be eliminated by using anti aging serums which supplement the levels of elastin and collagen in your body.
                  </span>
