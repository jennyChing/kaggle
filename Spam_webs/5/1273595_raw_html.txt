        <h1>
         Bullying Poems
        </h1>
        <div class="post-desc-full">
         <ul class="post-meta-special">

         </div>

           <span class="ssbp-toggle-switch ssbp-toggle-close" data-ssbp-toggle-class="ssbp-container">

           <div class="ssbp-container" data-ssbp-lazy-direction="left" data-ssbp-lazy-effect="fade" data-ssbp-mobile-direction="down" data-ssbp-post-id="494" data-ssbp-share-text="Please share this with your friends..." data-ssbp-short-url="" data-ssbp-title="Bullying Poems" data-ssbp-url="http://nobullying.com/bullying-poems/">

           <h2>
            <em>
             <strong>
              With the child safety and bullying prevention work that were involved in at nobullying.com, weve seen circumstances with parents and children when the spoken word catches in ones throat as it sucks on every ounce of energy that makes it almost impossible to breath, much less speak, or even think. There are other times when this unspoken word becomes a mute and pathetic cry for help and understanding, but no one hears it. Silence doesnt speak. And, no ones there to dry the tears of the distraught and bullied child. Learn aboutBullying Poems Now!
             </strong>
            </em>
           </h2>
           <h3>
            And that is
            <a href="http://nobullying.com/bystanders-fight-bullying-back-through-poetry/" target="_blank" title="why we are sharing bullying poems">
             why we are sharing bullying poems
            </a>
            and poems about bullying with everyone.
           </h3>
           <p>
            This is because with bullying poems and poems about bullying, a sad incident can turn into an inspiring event
            <a href="http://nobullying.com/bullying-and-poetry/" target="_blank" title=" if the right bullying poem is read and taught and used to inspire positive actions">
             if the right bullying poem is read and taught and used to inspire positive actions
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            This happens all too often when children are bullied, and they feel violated, betrayed, frightened, and alone. Many children are so ashamed that they were somehow the victim of a bully whether its a face to face confrontation, or cyber bullying so dont tell anyone, not even their parents.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://nobullying.com/victims-of-bullying-and-poetry/" target="_blank" title="Bullying poems provide a means of expression that can be a starting point for healing">
             Bullying poems provide a means of expression that can be a starting point for healing
            </a>
            . Just like in some cases of child abuse, the child wont communicate, but he or she might find their means of expression by drawing pictures, or talking to a teddy bear or find some relief in poems about bullying and bullying poems.
           </p>
           <p>
            We believe that the following poem, a high trending piece of poems about bullying, To This Day, written by internationally acclaimed
            <strong>
             Shane Koyczan
            </strong>
            and skillfully turned into animation by 12 volunteer animators and 80 professional artists for the
            <strong>
             To This Day
            </strong>
            project will express things much better than we can.
           </p>
           <p>
            It is a touching true story written as a spoken word poem by this talented Canadian poet and writer who delves into the life-changing impact that bullying can have on a person.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Koyczan
            </strong>
            shares his hopes and insights with his Bullying Poems, My hope is [that it] would reach some of the people who were just out there looking for something to get them through another day. When I wrote the poem two years ago and people started coming to me because they just needed to talk after hearing it, I realized this is not a Canadian problem or an American problem, its everywhereI believe the bullies must be forgiven. Thats how we heal.
           </p>
           <p>
            He said that he hopes the poem and the project will promote a connectivity between those who have suffered from bullying, that they might feel less isolated. The project aims to help schools engage better with bullying and child suicide.
           </p>
           <p>
            The short and long-term impact of bullying, which are substantial, may last a life time. This has been pointed out by Senior News Editor, Rick Nauert, Ph.D. his 2007 article. Bullying and Being Bullied Results in Greater Risk of Adult Disorders.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://nobullying.com/the-psychological-effects-of-bullying/" target="_blank" title="The resulting problems may include psychiatric disorders">
             The resulting problems may include psychiatric disorders
            </a>
            , anxiety and personality disorders such as psychopathy or sociopathy. Because the problem is rapidly increasing, especially with cyber bullying on the web, a number of researchers have made strong recommended that include mental health screenings that will help to identify bullies, and the children who are or have been victims.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://nobullying.com/victims-of-bullying-and-poetry/" target="_blank" title="Poetry is an important means of expression for children">
             Poetry is an important means of expression for children
            </a>
            , parents and loved ones that can have a profound healing effect.
           </p>
