       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Top 5 trending gadgets of this week from The Gadget Flow
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/12/18/top-5-trending-gadgets-of-this-week-from-the-gadget-flow.html" rel="bookmark" title="5:41 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          December 18, 2013
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://startups.fm/author/yoda" title="View all posts by yoda">
          yoda
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/12/18/top-5-trending-gadgets-of-this-week-from-the-gadget-flow.html">
          <img alt="Top-5-gadgets-from-TheGadgetFlow" class="alignnone" height="140" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/top-5-gadgets-dec18-thumb.jpg" title="Top-5-gadgets-from-TheGadgetFlow" width="350"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         We know you are still keeping your fingers crossed because Giveaway week #38 has few more hours to get over! A 1X Hard Graft Camera Strap is worth the wait. However, since its winter party time, why not check out few more trendy gadgets at
         <a href="http://thegadgetflow.com/" target="_blank">
          The Gadget Flow
         </a>
         . We are sure you will love them.
         <span id="more-4548">
         </span>
         StartupsFM has picked out 5 gadgets for you that you must have a look at before anything else. Heres this weeks top trending gadgets.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://thegadgetflow.com/portfolio/snuglet-macbook-power-cable-connector/" target="_blank">
          Snuglet MacBook Power Cable Connector
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         MacBooks are beautiful, but it also comes with a shortcoming. The charging problem is consistent with all MacBooks. However, the snuglet ring is a good compensation to all the troubles you were facing with charging till date. This cable snugly fits in the connector port, and doesnt really interfere with your attempts to remove the connector. It doesnt let go off connection on its own.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/12/18/top-5-trending-gadgets-of-this-week-from-the-gadget-flow.html">
          <img alt="Snuglet-MacBook-Power-Cable-Connector-TheGadgetFlow" class="alignnone" height="380" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/top-5-gadgets-dec18-snuglet.jpg" title="Snuglet-MacBook-Power-Cable-Connector-TheGadgetFlow" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If your pride in owning a MacBook was getting disturbed, well, at $23, you can have a smooth MacBook experience. You can reuse the cable, remove it from time to time, and use it on another Macbook too!
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://thegadgetflow.com/portfolio/homer-simpson-slippers-men/" target="_blank">
          Homer Simpson Slippers for Men
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Guys hate the adjective Cute, but trust me they would do anything for these cute Homer Simpson Slippers. If you are one such guy who carries off ridiculous styles with great pride, then these slippers are just for you. Homer Simpson is the king, and slipping your feet into his mouth might accelerate your courage, and you might just shout out Doh!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/12/18/top-5-trending-gadgets-of-this-week-from-the-gadget-flow.html">
          <img alt="Homer-Simpson-Slippers-for-Men-TheGadgetFlow" class="alignnone" height="380" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/top-5-gadgets-dec18-simpsonslippers.jpg" title="Homer-Simpson-Slippers-for-Men-TheGadgetFlow" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         These slippers are soft, they are plush, come with non-skid soles and are just $23. Match them with Simpson PJs and boxers, and let your style speak for you! In a year when its time to party, being crazy will be an add-on.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://thegadgetflow.com/portfolio/game-boy-cartridge-soaps/" target="_blank">
          Game Boy Cartridge Soaps
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         You have games on your mind 24/7. Ahh! How you wished to take games with you even when you are having a bath! Whoosh! Wish granted. The Game Boy Cartridge Soaps are what you were waiting for. Whats more fun than washing your hands with Earthworm jim soap, or Pokemon or Tetris! At $11, your time in bathroom is added to your game hours as you slip into Kirbys Dreamland or Super Mario Land.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/12/18/top-5-trending-gadgets-of-this-week-from-the-gadget-flow.html">
          <img alt="Game-Boy-Cartridge-Soaps-TheGadgetFlow" class="alignnone" height="380" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/top-5-gadgets-dec18-cartridgesoap.jpg" title="Game-Boy-Cartridge-Soaps-TheGadgetFlow" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Though these soaps are vegan, better not bite it. You can splash and spin, but snacks time is always after bath, not during a shower!
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://thegadgetflow.com/portfolio/usb-rocket-launcher/" target="_blank">
          USB Rocket Launcher
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         ThinkGeek has brought war in classrooms or in offices. USB Rocket Launcher can be plugged to your computer via a USB cable, and launch little foam rockets to hit a target at 25 feets away. You can control your rocket with a 360 degree horizontal rotation and a 45 degree vertical tilt!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/12/18/top-5-trending-gadgets-of-this-week-from-the-gadget-flow.html">
          <img alt="USB-Rocket-Launcher-TheGadgetFlow" class="alignnone" height="380" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/top-5-gadgets-dec18-usbrocket.jpg" title="USB-Rocket-Launcher-TheGadgetFlow" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Get your own weapon at just $47, and see others backing out as you walk in! After all who messes with an armed man!
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://thegadgetflow.com/portfolio/anki-drive-starter-kit/" target="_blank">
          Anki DRIVE Starter Kit
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         What do you call a video game that has jumped into the real world? No idea? Well, its called Anki DRIVE Starter Kit. This track kit comes with a landscape of its own where intelligent racing is a common thing. The cars are aware of their location and surroundings, and have the intelligent imbibed in them to know how to race!
