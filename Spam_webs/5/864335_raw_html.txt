     <h1 class="article-headline no-hero">
      Strong Bad Comes to PC
     </h1>
     <div class="article-content" id="article-content">
      <div class="article-subhead">
       <div class="article-share-count-container">
        <span class="icon icon-sharrow">

        </span>
       </div>
       The Homestar Runner crew will be coming to the PC.
      </div>
      <span class="article-byline icon-pointy">
       By Craig Harris
      </span>
      <aside class="ign-ad ign-ad-300x250 no-hero">

      <p>
       <a class="autolink" href="http://www.homestarrunner.com">
        Telltale Games
       </a>
       , the publisher of the episodic Sam and Max series, and Videlectrix, producers of such classic hits as Peasant's Quest and Pigs on Head, have revealed that the two teams are partnering up to bring Strong Bad, Homestar Runner, The Cheat, King of Town, and the rest of the crowd to the PC this June.
      </p>
      <p>
       Strong Bad's Cool Game for Attractive People will be released in five hopefully-monthly episodic installments on the Wii. The adventures will feature point-and-click gameplay with tons of inside jokes and hidden easter eggs, a staple of the Homestarrunner.com experience. Matt and Mike Chapman are on the collaborative ticket, working closely with
       <a class="autolink" href="http://games.ign.com/objects/731/731117.html">
        Telltale Games
       </a>
       to get the humor and visual experience just right...including supplying their voices for their respective characters.
      </p>
      <p>
       Details are slim at this point -- pricing and official release dates have not been revealed.
      </p>
      <p>
       We will have more on Strong Bad's cool Game for Attractive People as this downloadable experience progresses in its development cycle. While you wait, check out what the whole Homestar Runner thing is by
       <a rel="nofollow">
        heading to the official site.
       </a>
      </p>
