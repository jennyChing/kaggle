         <h1 class="h6">
          Roll the Dice
         </h1>
         A Poem by
         <a href="http://www.writerscafe.org/silverart">
          Art Betancourt
         </a>
         <br/>
         <div style="margin-left:16px;">
          <img alt='"' class="absmiddle" height="10" src="http://www.writerscafe.org/images/quote_start.png" width="12"/>
          <h4>
           A rambling about dreams.
          </h4>
          <img alt='"' height="10" src="http://www.writerscafe.org/images/quote_end.png" width="12"/>
         </div>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <a name="writing">
         </a>
         <div class="writing-item-writing-text" id="writingNotes">
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            <br/>
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            <br/>
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            <br/>
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            No no
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            You saw a star fall into the sky
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            and you thought it would destroy the land
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Those sparks of blue and grey
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Those coils of lightning and hail
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Matched up against the black
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            of the nightly firmament
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            You were quick to unveil your armament
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Warred and scarred
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Load your guns and rifles
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            though I've never held such contraptions in mine hands
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            I saw it coming with the arrival of the comet
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            The dark underside of you setting the sky on fire
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Screaming at God
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Why why why
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            But in the game board you soon discovered
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            You have yet to go beyond square one
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            And the flashing lights and the stadium filled to the brim with beautiful women
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Cheering so loudly at your disgrace
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Cheering so sadly at the mechanics in play
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            A veritable spectacle of great demand
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            And God's arm throws the dice
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Amidst a rosy sea of cloudy lemonade
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Oh so many players moving about
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            from left to right
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            from front to back
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Motionless you stand
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            dreams of your revolution are drowned in the playful chaos
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            A mouse trapped in a maze is more likely to find its place
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            And even from the highest of peaks
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            it all looks the same
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            no matter which ever way you desire to see
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            It's that strange feeling that you get
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            That you know you wish to understand
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            That you don't know what it is it must be understood
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Just this feeling that something is not right
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            See me dance and sing!
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            We won't pray if it brings you pain
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Saying hey hey hey
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            See me dance and sing!
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            This magnetic embrace won't repel us away
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Let us watch it again
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            The sign of your premonition at the witching hour
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Looking so high up
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            you never noticed the sea has risen
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            And all manner of beasts rampage through the beach
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Come with me to the highest trampoline
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Maybe the waves won't get us here
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            'Ere we await the next roll of dice
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            And see the people dance and sing below
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Saying hey hey hey
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Don't get too attached
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            One second next we're running wild through the midnight city lights
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            The moon shining so bright
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            and all roads looking the same
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            But the path home is different this time around
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            One second next it's all dark
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            And the desert temple makes us fly
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Though we can't rise up too far
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Floating about holding hands is our mark
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            I only saw a shooting star
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            But I didn't need a wish like that
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            There's so much we cannot command
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Don't get mad
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            See me dance and sing!
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            Because no matter what
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
           <font face="trebuchet ms" size="2">
            To smile is all we can realize
           </font>
          </div>
          <div>
