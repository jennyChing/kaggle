       <p>
        <a href="/" title="Rani Mukherjee">
         Rani Mukherjee
        </a>
        is known in the industry for her cheerfulness and liveliness. But a recent incident has made this happy go lucky actress a bit upset. Once her director Sanjay Leela Bhansali, is the reason behind her unhappiness. The filmmaker has recently made a statement the actress wanted to have title Hadippa from him for which he couldnt comply as his sister Bela Segal also wanted to have the same title. He further said, They then added two words, Dil Bole with Hadippa and used it as Dil Bole Hadippa (2009). I was upset about it. I thought that wasnt done.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_62" style="width: 480px">
        <a href="http://www.ranimukherjee.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/rani-mukherjee-showing-her-back.jpg">
         <img alt="Rani Mukherjee" class="size-full wp-image-62" height="633" src="http://www.ranimukherjee.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/rani-mukherjee-showing-her-back.jpg" title="Rani Mukherjee" width="480"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Rani Mukherjee
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Rani Mukherjeewho has delivered an award winning remarkable performance in Black (2005) of Sanjay, is rather upset to learn that the director has brought this old and trivial matter to forefront. Sanjay needs to tell me why he has involved my name, probably because I mediated between Adi (producer Aditya Chopra) and him. I wanted things to happen in an amicable manner, but since they didnt, Yashraj (producers of Dil Bole Hadippa) did what they had to do, says an upset Rani.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Rani Mukherjeeand other people from industry know well that an actor has least to do with the title procuring business. How can this affect their work and how can they be a part of this business? She feels, however, that Yashraj has done nothing wrong in this concerned case of title procurement.
       </p>
       <p>
        This is not my department. An actor only acts. Sanjay is also aware of the fact that its never an actors prerogative. Yes, as a friend, I tried to mediate, but the final decision was taken by the producers, says Rani.
       </p>
       <p>
        Adding further, If Dil Bole Hadippa released, Yashraj must have got permission from the concerned authorities to go ahead with it. There are a lot of technicalities involved. You cant simply take a title, says a supporting Rani.
       </p>
       <p>
        Rani and Sanjay are good friends. Then why Sanjay dragged her name in the matter. She shoots back saying, You need to ask Sanjay why he did so. I dont know how I came into the picture. However, the Bengali beauty maintains that her relationship with Sanjay is that of Friends forever. And this equation would never change. They both are still in touch with each other and share a good rapport. Sharing her views with us on Sanjays Balck she said, That film took me to a level that would have otherwise taken me years to reach.
       </p>
