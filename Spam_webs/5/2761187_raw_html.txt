       <p>
        <em>
         September is Professional Development Month at The Muse! Check in all month for ways to boost your skills, get ahead at work, and be the best professional you can possibly be.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        With an
        <a href="https://www.themuse.com/advice/3-tools-that-tell-you-everything-you-need-to-know">
         endless number of sites, tools
        </a>
        , and resources out there, how do you know whats worth your time? Well, we know just how busy you are, so we did the legwork for you and found 99 websites thatll add value to your life and your career, guaranteed.
       </p>
       <p>
        From
        <a href="https://www.themuse.com/advice/27-productive-things-you-can-do-in-5-minutes">
         productivity advice
        </a>
        to places to go when you need a distraction, these sites will change the way you do things,
        <a href="https://www.themuse.com/advice/10-quotes-that-will-inspire-you-to-do-what-you-love">
         inspire you
        </a>
        , and, well, generally just blow your mind.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Organize Your Work
       </h2>
       <div class="image-attachment-center">
        <img alt="" src="https://tm-prod.global.ssl.fastly.net/uploaded/attachments/13362.png?v=2ba1e5"/>
        <div class="image-pin" data-image-url="https%3A%2F%2Ftm-prod.global.ssl.fastly.net%2Fuploaded%2Fattachments%2F13362.png%3Fv%3D2ba1e5">
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        1.
        <a href="https://trello.com/">
         Trello
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Trello helps you manage all your ideas and due dates and keeps track of what youre in the process of completing. Plus, it looks niceits like the Pinterest of to-do lists.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        2.
        <a href="https://hackpad.com/">
         Hackpad
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Think Google Docsonly better for collaboration. Invite as many people as you want to contribute to your docs, or set them to private so theyre just yours.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        3.
        <a href="http://coffitivity.com/">
         Coffitivity
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        What is it about a coffee shop that gives you such laser focus? Coffitivity streams the background noises of a coffee shop so you can get your creativity A-game on.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        4.
        <a href="http://faxzero.com/">
         FaxZero
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        FaxZero lets you send and receive faxes through your emailbecause nobody likes the fax machine.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        5.
        <a href="https://www.rescuetime.com/">
         RescueTime
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>

       </p>

        <br/>
       </p>

        65.
        <a href="http://www.larky.com/about">
         Larky
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Keeping track of all the perks and discounts from your various credit cards, professional organizations, and memberships can be tough, but Larkys online dashboard makes it easy.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        66.
        <a href="http://www.mint.com">
         Mint
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Link your credit cards, bank accounts, and loans to the site (yes, its safe), and youll have a total online system that tracks your spending, saving, and financial health.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        67.
        <a href="https://www.sigfig.com/">
         SigFig
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Want to get started with investing? SigFig makes the process simple, fun, and personalized to your risk tolerance.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        68.
        <a href="https://www.futureadvisor.com/">
         FutureAdvisor
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Self-described as premium investment management for everyone, FutureAdvisor not only helps you set up your portfolio, it reviews and rebalances your investments to help you achieve your goals.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        69.
        <a href="http://www.wisebread.com/">
         Wise Bread
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        If youre looking to cut your costs, head to Wise Bread for tons of tips on how to live large on a small budget.
       </p>

        <br/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        70.
        <a href="http://shenegotiates.com/">
         She Negotiates
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>

       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
       </p>

        71.
        <a href="http://money.cnn.com/">
         CNNMoney
        </a>
       </h3>
       <p>
        The most complete site for everything money-related on the web, from markets and investing to your personal spending.
