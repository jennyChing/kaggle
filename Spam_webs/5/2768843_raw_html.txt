       <p>
        If youve spent any time in the saddle theres no way you couldnt have experienced most of these.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Making It Through A Close Call Without A Scratch
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="close-call" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-607 aligncenter" height="190" src="http://i.imgur.com/RGimi9X.gif" width="450"/>
        <br/>
        Weve all had them. An almost accident that made out heart race and hands shake. Be careful out there and make sure to wear your helmet.
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Rage You Feel When You Find That Someone Has Locked Up Their Bike Through Yours
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="locked" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-614 aligncenter" height="374" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/locked.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        This has to be the rudest thing a fellow biker can do. Have a little consideration. Especially for the cyclists that are scared of car doors just like yourself.
       </p>
       <p class="alert">
        Spring is coming! Do you need new shoes for your bike? Heres are the
        <a href="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/spinning-shoes/">
         10 best pairs for the upcoming season
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <h2>
        Red Lights Only Turn Green After You Unclip
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="traffic-light-city" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-609 aligncenter" height="313" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/traffic-light-city.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Its just one of those laws of nature. You see a red light in the distance and you slow as you approach it, hoping that it will turn green before you get there. Of course that doesnt happen so you eventually unclip and put your foot down. But the second your foot hits the ground the traffic the light starts to change and you get the green.
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Longer You Ride The Better The Beer Tastes
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="beer_toast" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-610 aligncenter" height="306" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beer_toast.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Hot summer days, brisk fall mornings, it doesnt matter what the weather is like. If your riding hard during the day the beer will taste better in the evening. The longer and harder the ride the more satisfying that first beer is.
       </p>
       <h2>
        On Your Left Is Dickhead Code For Im Faster Than You
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="passing" class=" imgborder wp-image-612 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/passing.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        On your left isnt really as polite warning to let you know that there is a stealthy cyclist quickly closing in on you. What they are really saying is No sudden moves asshole because Im going to pass you without slowing down!
       </p>
       <h2>
        You Know Youre About To Get Buzzed By A Car Before It Happens
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="4feet" class=" size-full wp-image-622 aligncenter" height="398" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/4feet.jpg" width="520"/>
        <br/>
        Their a sixth sense that you develop over time. Something about the way air moves around a car when its going to pass you a littl too closely in just a second. I still cant explain that, but you know what Im talking about.
       </p>
       <h2>
        And You Use Curses That You Didnt Know You Knew
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="swearing" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-623 aligncenter" height="225" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/swearing.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Right after your fears are confirmed you let forth a string of curses that would make sailors blush and nuns leave the convent.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Leaving Work Early To Ride On That First Beautiful Day Of Spring
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="spring" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-615 aligncenter" height="297" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/spring.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        The working man is a sucker! Now the person that leaves work early (or doesnt show up at all) to ride 30 miles on the first beariful day of spring; that person has their priorities right and the world by the balls.
       </p>
       <h2>
        One Day A Car Door Will Open And There Will Be No Escape
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="doors" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-613 aligncenter" height="298" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/doors.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Cars on the side of the road are a hazard and not because youre going to rear end one. All too often the people in those cars dont check their mirrors before opening the doors and thats where youll meet your fate. Its just a matter of time.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Cars Parked In The Bike Lane. WTF!!!
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="bike-lane" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-616 aligncenter" height="333" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/bike-lane.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        The lanes are clearly marked every 50 feet or so that its a biking lane. You know youre not supposed to park there, not even for a minute. So why do they do it?
       </p>
       <h2>
        Losing The Bet You Made That You Could Squeeze In A Ride Before The Rain
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="rain" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-617 aligncenter" height="360" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/rain.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Certain times of the year there is a threat of rain almost every day. But when you feel like heading out for a ride clouds dont look too bad just yet and you know you can squeeze in a quick one. Well sometimes Mother Nature has the last laugh.
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Back Of Your Shirt After Getting Caught In The Rain
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="dirty" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-618 aligncenter" height="500" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dirty.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Dirty shirts arent just for kids riding through puddles formed from yesterdays rain. If youve been caught in the aforementioned monsoon (because it never just drizzles when youre in the saddle) you have had to come home and throw your clothes right in the washing machine.
       </p>
       <h2>
        And Then Having To Clean Your Bike
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="muddy" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-619 aligncenter" height="373" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/muddy.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        That monsoon didnt just wreck your shirt or coat. Your bike is a filthy mess and needs a good bath followed by a lube to keep it in tip top shape.
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Sickening Feeling When You Didnt See That Broken Glass Soon Enough
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="glass" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-620 aligncenter" height="281" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/glass.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Oh how we loathe broken glass in the road. Some jerk clearly didnt care and probably laughed when they heard that bottle shatter in the road. And now its your job to avoid it. Hopefully you did so with hearing that sickening crunch crunch.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Or Horse Poop
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="poop" class=" imgborder size-full wp-image-621 aligncenter" height="317" src="http://bestexerciseshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/poop.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
        Glass is worse but horse poop is pretty bad too. It ends up getting everywhere and if youre really lucky its on your back too.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Did we miss any? Let us know in the comments.
        </strong>
       </p>
