         2014 British Open: Who Will Take Home the Accolades? The Answer May Surprise You.
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <a href="http://bridalshoes.biz/2014-british-open-who-will-take-home-the-accolades-the-answer-may-surprise-you/" rel="bookmark" title="11:05 am">

           July 12, 2014
          </time>
         </a>

          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://bridalshoes.biz/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/images.jpg">
          <img alt="British Open Logo" class="alignright wp-image-19 size-full" height="114" src="http://bridalshoes.biz/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/images.jpg" title="British Open Logo" width="210"/>
         </a>
         The
         <a href="http://www.theopen.com/" title="British Open">
          British Open
         </a>
         is a golf tournament that was established in 1860 in England. It is among the four main championships played in professional golf in the world. The British open golf tournament is administered by R&amp;A which is the body in charge of golf in other countries apart from the United States and Mexico. This championship tournament is usually scheduled on the third Friday of the month of July a tradition that dates as far back as 1979. it is usually held after the Masters tournament, the famous US open and begins right before the PGA championship. Currently, the reigning British golf champion is Phil Mickelson.
        </p>
        <p>
         The championship has a rich history. In 1926, it was done at the end of two days and had four rounds. Before 1966, the last two rounds came on Fridays. From the year 1966 to 1970, the tournament had a Saturday finish and since the 1980s the tournament has had a Sunday finish. In 1951, the championship was held in North Ireland but since then, has been held in England. The winners of the tournament are usually gifted with hefty prizes whose value appreciates annually. This tournament like many other golf tournaments involves 72 holes and a cut that comes after 36 holes. This cut is limited to the best 70 players and includes ties. The British open stands out from other golf championships as there is usually a playoff which involves four holes. This playoff however may be discontinued if there is a tie between the players.
        </p>
        <p>
         Regarded as one of the most difficult of the four majors in golf, the British Open this year will kickoff in just under a weeks time. The favorites according to Stoke city bookie,
         <a href="http://bookmaker-info.com/en/bet365-profile" title="365Bet">
          365Bet
         </a>
         , are last years champion Phil Mickelson and Tiger Woods followed by Adam Scott. With the field of players being extra strong this year, its really a toss up over who will go home with the top prize and claret jug to boot.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/lqJuty1mRAE" width="420">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         If I were a betting man (and I am), my personal wager would be on Hideki Matsuyama to surprise the field and take home all the winnings. Definitely not a favorite based on what the odds at the
         <a href="http://bookmaker-info.com/en/bet365-register" title="bet365 site">
          bet365 site
         </a>
         have to say, I have a feeling that the Japanese national is due to breakout in this years tournament.
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, this is my prediction for this years tournament. What about yours? By all means, please let me know down in the comments section below.
        </p>

         </a>
        </p>
