       <h1>
        North Korean "Global Happiness Index" ranks China no. 1, USA dead last
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="entry-body">
       <p>
        <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;">
         <img alt="north-koreas-happiness-index1.jpg" class="image-right" height="563" src="http://shanghaiist.com/attachments/Jessica Colwell/north-koreas-happiness-index1.jpg" width="276"/>
        </span>
        China is the happiest place on earth(!!) according to a new global happiness index released by North Korea's Chosun Central Television. China earned 100 out of 100 points, followed closely by North Korea (98 points), then Cuba, Iran, and Venezuela. Coming in at 203rd place is America (or rather "the American Empire", ), with only 3 happiness points. South Korea got a measly 18 points for 152nd place.
       </p>
       <p>
        While their
        <a href="http://shanghaiist.com/2011/05/27/gallery_officially_representative_n.php">
         representative representatives
        </a>
        might not show the vivacity on their faces (and a few slip ups might have been
        <a href="http://shanghaiist.com/2010/11/29/horrifying_footage_of_north_korea_r.php">
         caught on video
        </a>
        ) I guess China's been right to
        <a href="http://shanghaiist.com/2011/02/21/n_korea_article.php">
         gush about Pyongyang's future
        </a>
        . Nothing says happy like government-issued
        <a href="http://www.highpeakspureearth.com/2011/02/cctv-says-lhasa-people-are-happiest-by.html">
         proclamations of happiness
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         (Image from
         <a href="http://chaoxian.com.cn/bbsthread-117570/">
          Chaoxian
         </a>
         , h/t
         <a href="http://hken.ibtimes.com/articles/153551/20110528/north-korea-happiness-index-rank-china-top-us-bottom-photos.htm">
          IBTimes
         </a>
         .)
        </em>
       </p>
      </div>
