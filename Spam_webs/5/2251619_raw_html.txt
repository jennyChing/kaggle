       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         20 Benefits from Doing Kettlebell Exercises for your Health
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_101" style="width: 302px">
         <a href="http://www.juthan.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/juthan-20-benefits-kettlebell.jpg">
          <img alt="20 Benefits of Kettlebell Exercises" class="size-medium wp-image-101" height="300" src="http://www.juthan.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/juthan-20-benefits-kettlebell-292x300.jpg" width="292"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          20 Benefits of Kettlebell Exercises
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell is one of the best workout equipment that everyone should add to his or her workout routine in order to get a leaner and tighter figure in short time. Many people especially women see this tool in the gym but they avoid using it as they are not aware of its countless benefits. The advantages of Kettlebell training cannot be denied.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Here are 20 benefits of using Kettlebell
        </p>
        <ol style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Helps to burn fat
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The common fitness goal especially for women is to burn the extra fats around the arms, thigh and waist. With high intensity exercises Kettlebell training offers the ability to kill fat in lesser time than any types of weight loss program. Kettlebell increase the metabolism which in turn decreases the body fat.
        </p>
        <ol start="2" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Strengthens muscles
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell training offers exercises for whole body movement. Its a very well known fact that Kettlebell exercises are far better than machines to improve body composition, muscle tone and strength. It also strengthens the ligaments and tendons which make the joints tougher and less prone to injury.
        </p>
        <ol start="3" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Helps to build a muscular physique
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell training helps to build a muscular physique that most men dream of. The training pares down the waist, builds broad shoulder, builds up arms and defines abdominal. So one equipment works for the whole body and does not take much time to build a muscular physique.
        </p>
        <ol start="4" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Greater cardio benefits
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell training is the most effective fitness routine available. Now most of the secret service is using Kettlebell training as repetitive KB drills offer more muscles. For an entire body cardiovascular adaption repetitive KB drills is perfect.
        </p>
        <ol start="5" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           You will see improvement in posture
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Getting Kettlebell training means your heart has to be engaged 360 degrees in order to stabilize every movement. You should be in good form when performing Kettlebell workout, you should stop and take rest if you feel that your body is deteriorating. The whole structure of your abs and back should stay straight, any forward bending that you do should come from the top of your legs or from hips. These movements will improve your posture.
        </p>
        <ol start="6" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Simplifies your life
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell makes your exercise easy and simple as you do not need to think which days you will do strength training and which days cardio as it combines both and also there is no need to think about separate upper body and lower body exercise, KBs workout covers everything.
        </p>
        <ol start="7" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Builds mobility
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Stretching practitioners develop high muscular flexibility but they do not develop muscular strength and joint stability. But Kettlebells training provides these all three- muscular flexibility, strength and joint stability.
        </p>
        <ol start="8" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Develops core strength
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         In Kettlebell you need to engage your core in almost all lifts. So this training strengthens your abs and backs like nothing else before.
        </p>
        <ol start="9" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Unifies your body
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The entire body is engaged in every Kettlebell lift, its not a single joint movement. Many muscles groups and joints are engaged in every swing, press of life which force the body always to work as a unified whole.
        </p>
        <ol start="10" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Cost effective than other fitness machines
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         You only need one tool (Kettlebell), thats all. If the weight gets light then you can get a heavier bell or holding that same KB in different way makes it harder. Continuously you will learn to increase the challenges by manipulating the timing, weight distribution, leverage etc.
        </p>
        <ol start="11" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           More coordination
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         As our body knows movement and not muscles so we become more coordinated with the use of Kettlebell. These workouts make our body to work as a whole unit. That is why athletes and body builders do not look same, athletes look different.
        </p>
        <ol start="12" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Increases mental focus
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell exercises are not just lifting up, muscle it up and then throw around. It requires your concentration, you must remain focus on the movement in each moment. As a result it improves your mental focus and co-ordination.
        </p>
        <ol start="13" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Simplifies your exercise
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Just four exercises such as swing, press, clean and snatch and their variations in limitless combinations need to practice twice or three times per week and it will keep you lean, functional and strong for the entire life.
        </p>
        <ol start="14" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Gentle rehabilitation
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Older and wiser people can be benefitted by gaining energy, strength, healing pain and gaining functionality that they lost. KB training is very flexible and simple so it merges in a good way with physical therapy routines.
        </p>
        <ol start="15" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Cures back pain
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         All the hip muscles and glutes are emphasized by the Kettlebell exercises. Not only they get stronger but also wake up and start taking part in all movements. If your glutes do not get firing at the time of lifting, your lower back muscles get overused. The low back muscles are not supposed to do the job of glutes which is the largest muscles in the body. So Kettlebell training helps our body learns correct and more powerful movements that help to do everything right. So it stops the lower back pain.
        </p>
        <ol start="16" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Fixes trashed shoulders
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebell exercises are known for fixing bad shoulders. Curing shoulders need three things-stability, mobility and strength. KB training does this exact thing.
        </p>
        <ol start="17" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Heals pains and aches
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kettlebells disappears lower back pain, heals shoulder and knee joints, increases mobility and stiffness is relieved. KB exercises reveal weaknesses and misalignments in the body and then go on to fix them.
        </p>
        <ol start="18" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Strengthens joints
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Repetitive KB swing and quick lifts work as a wonder for joints by strengthening it. Strong muscles with weak joints do nothing for you but Kettlebell training does the right thing as it makes the joints stronger and builds muscles.
        </p>
        <ol start="19" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Develops power
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         KBs develop incredible power in the lifters but with safety and simplicity.
        </p>
        <ol start="20" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Builds endurance
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         In fighting context or martial arts one of the most important skills is enduring strength which requires excellent strength or endurance. Kettlebell repetition snatches develop an aerobic threshold and a work capacity.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         So get Kettlebell training, practice the exercise twice or three times per week and stay fit for the rest of your life.
        </p>
