        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/mentality-jelly-collection/" title="Mentality The Jelly Collection">
          Mentality The Jelly Collection
         </a>
        </h2>
       </div>
       <!-- end div .post-title -->
       <div class="entry">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p style="text-align: right;">
         <span class="bk-button-wrapper">
          <a class="bk-button L center " href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/disclosure/" target="_blank">
           Press Sample
          </a>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Mentality-The-Jelly-Collection-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-The-Jelly-Collection" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5994" height="600" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Mentality-The-Jelly-Collection-w-1024x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hey dolls! Halfway through the work week! Today I have photos for you of
         <a href="http://www.mentalitynailpolish.bigcartel.com/">
          Mentality
         </a>
         The Jelly Collection. This set features nine jellies with shimmer. The formula was the same on each polish; they applied nicely, had good formulas, built up nicely, and were opaque in three coats.
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Cara is a yellow jelly packed with shimmer. I really love this shade on my complexion.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_0695-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Cara" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5993" height="733" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_0695-w-838x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_0691-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Cara" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5992" height="834" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_0691-w-736x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Jennifer is a red-leaning pink jelly filled with pink shimmer. It almost looks as if the shimmer is glowing in this polish, which makes it even prettier.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1677-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Jennifer" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5996" height="828" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1677-w-742x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1676-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Jennifer" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5995" height="788" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1676-w-779x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Jill is a pink-leaning coral jelly filled with pink shimmer. This one has a very soft look. You can tell from the photos that Jill was a little more sheer than the others in the collection.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1668-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Jill" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5998" height="684" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1668-w-897x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1665-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Jill" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5997" height="762" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1665-w-806x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Kacy is a purple jelly with blue undertones. The blue shimmer in this polish isso pretty!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1653-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Kacy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6000" height="764" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1653-w-804x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1649-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Kacy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5999" height="615" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1649-w-999x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Mona is a light green jelly with blue, gold, and silver shimmer. This one has more of a frosty appearance than the rest of the collection. Mona was also on the sheer side.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1688-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Mona" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6002" height="783" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1688-w-784x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1685-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Mona" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6001" height="764" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1685-w-804x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Nina is a turquoise jelly with shimmer. This polish just makes me want to be somewhere tropical. *sighs*
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1630-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Nina" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6003" height="822" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1630-w-747x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1634-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Nina" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6004" height="778" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1634-w-789x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Rachel is a bright blue jelly with shimmer. Blue lovers will appreciate this one.
        </p>

         </a>

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Shalon is a bright burnt orange jelly filled with gold shimmer. Hands down, this is my favorite from the collection!

        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1660-w.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mentality Tyler is a purple jelly with red undertones. Please pardon the lack of focus with these photos. I didnt notice until I was editing the photos yesterday.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1701-w.jpg">
          <img alt="Mentality-Tyler" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6010" height="625" src="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1701-w-982x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>

         <a href="http://www.refinedandpolished.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1693-w.jpg">
