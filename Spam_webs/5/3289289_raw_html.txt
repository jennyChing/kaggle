          <img alt="Comments" src="/template/ver1-0/i/share/comments.gif"/>
          <a dsqid="9200380" href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/motoring/first-drives/9200380/Lamborghini-Aventador-meets-real-life-Erins-view.html#disqus_thread">

        <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="reviewBody">
         <div class="firstPar">
          <p>
           "If you could own any car, what would you pick?" is the question I'm most asked when I tell people what I do for a living. I can't stand this question, because what they're plainly actually asking is, "Ferrari or Lamborghini?". So normally, I look them in the eye and say, "Hyundai i20", then walk off.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           From henceforth, however, there shall be but one response, and it'll be the truth: "Lamborghini Aventador". This is it: my dream car. All the practicalities of life, like money, family and transport, are now irrelevant: this tangerine rocket is the one for me.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">
          <p>
           First off, it's stunning. Easily the sexiest, maddest, most flamboyant work of art on the roads, especialy in lurid orange, with interior leather to match. What's the point of an Italian supercar that's subtle? This makes Ferraris seem so...pedestrian. That mad wedge design looks like it's cutting through the air even when it's standing still.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           But what's it like to live with (or in, if I were to sell my house in order to buy one)? Could you really just own this car and use it on a daily basis for all your transport needs? Well, yes and no. Driving it around town isn't exactly easy, but it's not as hard as I feared. Carparks are a tad tricky because, if you've left it overnight, the brakes are cold, and the engine just wants to run when first gear is engaged via the paddles. You sweep the available area at speed, wrestling the wheel, trying to look nonchalant. But out on the road, once at 30mph or more, this is serious fun. I was aurally assaulted, not by the bellowing engine behind my head, but by whoops, shouts, whistles, lorry horns blaring, cameras clicking, and three comments from consecutive passing white van men of "Want to swap?". The grins on their faces when I shrugged and said, "Sure".
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           People understand that Lamborghinis are about a sense of humour, so they smile and wave and laugh, and let you out of junctions, while ignoring the sombre-suited man in his serious Aston Martin.
          </p>
         </div>

         <div class="body">
          <p>
           And so to the ultimate test for a Lamborghini (surprisingly ignored by petrolhead mags): a supermarket on a Saturday morning. For the weekly shop, you must merely be aware of four things:
          </p>
          <p>
           1) This is a very, very wide car, so you'll need to steal a small child and park in a parent-and-child extra-wide parking bay. Or just do so without one, and pay the fine for illegitimately parking there - what do you care about fines? You just spent 230k on a car.
          </p>
          <p>
           2) This is a very, very low car, so park at the end of a row, so that when you pull out, you are not hidden by people unloading shopping from trollies and thus hit by an Audi being driven by someone who needs to perform a 10-point turn into a space because they only drive their car once a week.
          </p>
          <p>
           3) There is a very important little button on the centre console that raises the ride height of the car, so you can drive over speed humps. You will need to use this a lot, because it transforms the car from go-nowhere to go-anywhere (except for during non-existent fuel strikes - see below - when you'll be back to go-nowhere).
          </p>
          <p>
           4) There's not a lot of space in the boot at the front of the car. In fact, four bags of shopping will fill it, so make sure you get the essentials - a case of Chateau Margaux, for example.
          </p>
          <p>
           More practical things about this car: it's got four-wheel drive so you can use it to help neighbours out of snowdrifts, and then they'll be so grateful that they'll ignore their suspicions that you've either stolen the car or are a class-A dealer; its doors open upwards so you can park (a little) closer to objects than you think; it's got a jet-fighter red plastic cover for the ignition button, so you won't hit it accidentally if you're flying the car; um, it's orange so you can find it easily if you lose it; umm, it's very, very loud so people will hear you coming and not crash into you, which they might if you were in an electric car, so this is actually a more responsible purchase than a Toyota Prius.
          </p>
          <p>
           So there it is, the Aventador: worth selling your house for.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Supermarket rating: 3
           </strong>
          </p>

        var readNextCurrentArticleId = "9200380";

        /*
         Read Next Widget
         */
        var readNextWidget = (function(){

            var settings = {
                articleImage: "",
                articleTitle: "",
                articleUrl: "#",
                articleId: "",
                currentArticleId: "",
                articleList: [],
                articleListToSkip: [],
                readNextCookieName: "tmg_read_next",
                openButton: "#readNextOpen a",
                closeButton: "#closeBtn a",
                articleContainer: "#readNextArticleContainer",
                widgetContainer: "#readNextWidget",
                displayThumbnail: false,
                panelOpen: false,
                autoOpen: true
            };

            var renderTemplate = function() {
                var template = '&lt;div id="readNextWidget" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextOpen"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div id="readNextOpenInner"&gt;&lt;a href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Next Story&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span class="icn"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextArticleContainer" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticle"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleInnerWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;p id="readNextLabel"&gt;MOST VIEWED IN NEWS&lt;/p&gt;';
                        
				if(settings.articleImage &amp;&amp; settings.articleImage!="null" &amp;&amp; settings.displayThumbnail){
					template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageThumbnail" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;img src="'+ settings.articleImage +'"/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;';
				}
                        
				template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageTitle" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;' + settings.articleTitle + '&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="closeBtn"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;a href="#"&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
