          <p>
           Then, youre going to leave all the settings as they are and just enter a bid. Youre going to be bidding to show up on the first slot for your name. This will keep people searching for you away from sites like Trulia and Zillow. You should enter something around $.50-$1.00 for the bid.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Campaign_Management__Google_AdWords" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6878" height="322" src="https://easyagentpro-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Campaign_Management__Google_AdWords1.jpg" width="849"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Finally, youll enter your landing page and keywords:
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="wordpress real estate" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6879" height="717" src="https://easyagentpro-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Campaign_Management__Google_AdWords2.jpg" width="635"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What will this do?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           This means when a prospect Googles your name, they will find your landing page and website.
          </p>
          <p>
           Most realtors dont show up when their name is Googled. Sites like Trulia, Zillow, and Realtor.com steal even your name as a keyword.
          </p>
          <p>
           This simple setup in PPC prevents that traffic from finding other websites. And gets you more leads on your WordPress real estate website!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            ..the next tip is even better!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            7)Got A Hard To Beat Competitor? Run Ads Against Their Name With Your WordPress Real Estate Site
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Do you have several competitors in your markets that get lots of traffic?
          </p>
          <p>
           Great! Heres how you can easily compete:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            You can setup PPC campaigns on Google for their name!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Simply, follow the steps above. But instead of putting your name as the keyword
          </p>
          <p>
           you put your competitors page.
          </p>
          <p>
           This means your ad will show up above their page when someone Googles their name.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its a timeless ad tactic used by lots of companies online. But I rarely see realtors using it!
          </p>
          <p>
           You can easily setup ads to your website today!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            8) 3 Calls-To-Action You Need On Your SideBar
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Do you not see results from your
           <a href="http://www.easyagentpro.com/blog/real-estate-website-design/" title="Why Your Site Doesnt Get Leads (Hint: It Has Something To Do With Real Estate Website Design)">
            real estate website
           </a>
           ?
          </p>
          <p>
           The biggest problem I see on most real estate websites is a lack of flow.
          </p>
          <p>
           Realtors typically have decent
           <a href="http://www.easyagentpro.com/blog/real-estate-branding/" title="Top Real Estate Branding Ideas and Examples For Agents">
            branding
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.easyagentpro.com/blog/real-estate-slogans-50-sure-fire-ways-to-craft-a-better-brand/" title="Real Estate Slogans: 50+ Sure-Fire Ways To Craft A Better Brand With Examples And Ideas Of Catchy Taglines And Slogans">
            slogan
           </a>
           , and
           <a href="http://www.easyagentpro.com/blog/real-estate-logos/" title="Real Estate Logos  15 Free Customizable Logos For Realtors To Get Ideas And Create The Logo That Will Represent Their Brand">
            logo
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           But, whats missing is a call to action to the 3 types of landing pages that are standard across the industry.
          </p>
          <p>
           I believe realtors should use their sidebar to have calls to actions to 3 different types of landing pages.
          </p>
          <p>
           Heres what they are:
          </p>
          <ol>
           <li>
            Free Home Valuation
           </li>
           <li>
            Free Buyers Guide
           </li>
           <li>
            Free Sellers Guide
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p>
           And you can easily create 3 graphics that link to these 3 different landing pages on your sidebar. Heres an example:
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="wordpress real estate all about the tool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6880" height="650" src="https://easyagentpro-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Real_Estate_Blog_-_Coldwell_Banker___Easy_Agent_PRO2.jpg" width="1180"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Add these three calls to action on your sidebar and footer of your website today! Youll see an increased amount of traffic to these lead converting pages. And then youll start to see the leads come in.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            9) IFTTT Autoshare Creates An Automated Social Media Presence
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Do you ever set goals and then not follow through with them?
          </p>
          <p>
           I know I do
          </p>
          <p>
           For example, did you swear that youd always share your content on Pinterest and Facebook.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            But 2 weeks later find yourself not sharing it?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Of course that happens!
          </p>
          <p>
           Heres how to not let it happen again
          </p>
          <p>
           You can use IFTTT to auto share blog articles to various social media sites.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you have a WordPress real estate website, you just have to find the RSS feed.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            That typically is: yourwebsite.com/feed
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Then, you can head over to IFTTT, give them the RSS feed, and setup your content to automatically share to social media!
          </p>
