         Smores Ice Cream Cupcakes
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-05-23T17:31:12+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 23, 2013

        <p>
         Nothing says Summer like enjoying a delicious smore or ice cream cone on a warm Summer evening. We had 100 degree weather in April, yes April so lets just say weve been enjoying both of those for awhile now. Which had me thinking wouldnt it be awesome to combine smores AND ice cream into one scrumptious treat? Oh yes I did, my friends! I made Smores Ice Cream Cupcakes!
         <img alt="S'mores Ice Cream Cupcakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5108" height="896" src="http://apumpkinandaprincess.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Smores-Ice-Cream-Cupcakes-Recipe.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You already know Im a fan of ice cream cupcakes, but smores are probably my favorite treat ever! Theyre so simple yet so delicious so its no surprise that I felt like I needed to combine the two. Boy am I glad I did! The oooey gooey marshmallow combined with the ice cream and hot fudge is THE BOMB. Yes, Im getting all 90s on you here. :)
        </p>
        <p>
         For this ice cream cupcake recipe I used the
         <a href="https://www.hersheys.com/recipes/recipe-details.aspx?id=184" target="_blank">
          Hersheys Perfectly Chocolate Cake
         </a>
         recipe like I did with the
         <strong>
          <a href="http://apumpkinandaprincess.com/2013/02/triple-chocolate-cupcakes-with-ganache-frosting.html" target="_blank">
           Triple Chocolate Ganache Cupcakes
          </a>
         </strong>
         . My inspiration for the cupcakes came from the Dreyers smores ice cream I recently found at my local grocery store. I originally planned on making homemade vanilla ice cream but knew I had to try this (new to me) ice cream. Once the cupcakes were frozen I topped them with a toasted marshmallow and poured Hersheys hot fudge topping.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-9" style="border: 1px dotted;">
         <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
           <div class="item b-b">
            <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
             <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-9', 'http://apumpkinandaprincess.com/wp-content/plugins/ziplist-recipe-plugin/'); return false" title="Print this recipe">
              Print
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="zl-recipe-link fl-r zl-rmvd" id="zl-recipe-link-9">
            </div>
            <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
             Smores Ice Cream Cupcakes
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="zlclear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ol id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             For the cupcake batter:
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 3/4 cups flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 cup cocoa powder
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 tsp baking powder
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 tsp baking soda
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp salt
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 eggs
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup milk
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup vegetable oil
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tsp vanilla extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup boiling water
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
             Ice Cream:
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
             Dreyers S'mores Ice Cream
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16" itemprop="ingredients">
             Toppings:
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
             Hot fudge topping
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
             Marshmallows; toasted
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
             Graham crackers; crushed
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Preheat oven to 350 degrees and fill cupcake pan with liners.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a large bowl stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla and mix for 2 minutes. Slowly stir in boiling water.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour one tablespoon of batter into cupcake pan.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake for 10-12 minutes; cool completely.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Top cupcakes with slightly melted ice cream; freeze for 2-3 hours or preferably overnight.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             When ready to serve top with toasted marshmallow, hot fudge sauce and crushed graham crackers. Enjoy!
            </li>
           </ol>
           <div class="ziplist-recipe-plugin" style="display: none;">
            3.1
           </div>
           <a href="http://apumpkinandaprincess.com/2013/05/smores-ice-cream-cupcakes.html" id="zl-printed-permalink" title="Permalink to Recipe">
            http://apumpkinandaprincess.com/2013/05/smores-ice-cream-cupcakes.html
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Note: The ice cream cupcakes can melt fairly quick so its best to take them out of the freezer right before serving. If youre serving a large amount you could probably use a kitchen blowtorch to quickly toast the marshmallows.
