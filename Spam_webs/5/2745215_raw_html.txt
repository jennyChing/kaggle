        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Delhi Circle Rate Increased by 20%, Home Gets Costlier
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="posted-on">
          Posted on
          <a href="http://www.realtynewsindia.in/real-estate-news/delhi-circle-rate-increased-20-home-gets-costlier/" rel="bookmark" title="5:48 am">

            September 23, 2014
           </time>
           <time class="updated" datetime="2015-02-19T16:58:41+00:00">
            February 19, 2015
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="byline">
          by
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.realtynewsindia.in/author/admin/" title="View all posts by Ankit Sajwan">

        <div class="ad-inserter ad-inserter-2" style="text-align:left;margin:8px 0px;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- sidebartextads -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9701269892590012" data-ad-slot="3838744885" style="display:inline-block;width:200px;height:90px">

        <p>
         Home prices in Delhi set to increase as Delhi Government today increased the circle rates by upto 20% to check implementation of black money in sale and purchase of property.
        </p>
        <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- 46815 -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9701269892590012" data-ad-slot="4016744481" style="display:inline-block;width:468px;height:15px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.realtynewsindia.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/delhi-circle-rate.jpg">
          <img alt="delhi circle rate" class="alignleft wp-image-457 size-full" height="199" src="http://www.realtynewsindia.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/delhi-circle-rate.jpg" width="311"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lt. Najeeb Jung approved the decision of increasing the circle rates and new circle rate will come into effect from today. The circle rates of Category A which includes areas like Maharani Bagh, Friends Colony, Golf Links, Sunder Nagar, vasant Vihar &amp; Chanakyapuri is now set to 7.74 lakhs per sq.mt. as compared to 6.45lakhs per sq.mt. which means nobody buy propertyin the area below than 7.74 lakhs per sq.mt.
        </p>
        <p>
         For areas like GK,Defence Colony, South-Ex, Nizammuddin east, Kailash colony and Green park which comes under Category B the new circle rate is 2.45 lakhs per sq. mt. which was 2.05 lakhs per sq.mt. earlier. In category C the circle rate is increased to 1.60lakhs per sq.mt. from 1.33lakhs per sq.mt, The areas which comes under Category C are nizammuddin, west Malviya Nagar, Panchsheel Ext., Shankar road, CR Park and Alaknanda.
        </p>
        <p>
         The circle rate of category D is now set to 1.28 lakhs per sq.mt. which was 1.06 lakhs per sq.mt. earlier, Satya Niketan, Sarita Vihar, Kalka Ji, DDA Flats, raja garden, Vikaspuri and Janakpuri comes under Category D.
        </p>
        <p class="body">
         The rate for colonies under category E has been hiked from Rs.58,365 to Rs.70,080 per sq m. Some of the residential colonies under this category include Chandni Chowk, Hauz Qazi, Jama Masjid, Kashmere Gate and Khirki Extension.
        </p>
        <p class="body">
         For F category colonies, which includes areas like Anand Parbat, Arjun Nagar, Daya Basti, Dilshad Colony, Dishad Garden and BR Amdedkar Colony, the rate will be Rs.56,640 as against current rate of Rs.47,140.
        </p>
        <p class="body">
         Colonies falling under category G includes Amber Vihar, Dabri Extension, Dakshinpuri, Dashrathpuri and Hari Nagar Extension.
        </p>
        <div id="articleKeywords">
         The last time DDA revised circle rates was in December 2012. The increased circle rate would effect the real estate market heavily as there are speculation that the residential property would be now 20% more costlier and commercial property can get upto 60% more costlier.
        </div>
