           <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
            WORLDS SCENIC TRAIN RIDES
           </h1>
          </header>

          <div class="post-content-container">
           <div class="post-content entry-content cf" data-first="N">
            <p>
             In this fast moving world, the benefits of a train journey are often overlooked. Sure, a train journey takes a bit longer, but thats a small price to pay for a scenic view without the hassle of traffic and long security lines. The view from your seat is as much a part of the holiday as arriving at the destination.
            </p>
            <p>
             We bring to you scenic train ridesacross the world that are designed to suit your budget and preference.
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              <div class="royalSlider new-royalslider-49 rsUni rsContentSlider" id="new-royalslider-49" style="width:100%; height:500;">
               <div>
                <img alt="Splurge: The Rocky Mountaineers Journey through the Clouds, Canada" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RockyM.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RockyM-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 Splurge: The Rocky Mountaineers Journey through the Clouds, Canada
                </h3>
                <p>
                 Close to home, this Rocky Mountaineer route transports you from Vancouver to Jasper, passing through a kaleidoscope of landscapes - rolling prairie hills, glacial lakes, and snow-capped peaks of the Rockies. Splurge on GoldLeaf service for access to stellar cuisine, unlimited cocktails, and a glass dome coach that makes snapping that Instagram-worthy shot a breeze.
                 <br/>
                 Price: From CAD$1,099 per person
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Rocky Mountaineer
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="Splurge: The Rocky Mountaineers Journey through the Clouds, Canada" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rocky-Mountaineer-3.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rocky-Mountaineer-3-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 Splurge: The Rocky Mountaineers Journey through the Clouds, Canada
                </h3>
                <p>
                 Impeccable service on the Rocky Mountaineer takes your indulgence to a higher level.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Rocky Mountaineer
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="Best Value: Bernina Express, Switzerland and Italy" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Bernina-Express.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Bernina-Express-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 Best Value: Bernina Express, Switzerland and Italy
                </h3>
                <p>
                 Mention the Alps and you might expect exorbitant travel rates, but Bernina Express proves that luxury travel on a shoestring budget is possible. Lush greenery greets you through the trains large panoramic windows as you travel from St.Moritz towering glaciers to the palm trees of Tirano, Italy, on a steep track peering over mountain streams. Reservations are advised; this train is justly popular. Price: CAD $37 per person (single trip)
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Bernina Express
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="For culture: Eastern and Oriental Express Train, Singapore &amp; Thailand" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/EasternOrientalExpress.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/EasternOrientalExpress-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 For culture: Eastern and Oriental Express Train, Singapore &amp; Thailand
                </h3>
                <p>
                 The train begins its journey from Singapore traversing through gleaming skyscrapers and lush palm plantations, with pit stops at Malaysias colonial sites. As the train crosses to Bangkok, Thailand, panoramic views of villages and hill top temples offer a glimpse into the lives of the locals: farmers knee-deep in rice fields and playful children skipping to school. This 3-day journey is complemented with Oriental cuisine. 
Price: CAD$2,798 per person
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Eastern and Oriental Express Train
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="For culture: Eastern and Oriental Express Train, Singapore &amp; Thailand" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Eastern-Oriental-Express.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Eastern-Oriental-Express-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 For culture: Eastern and Oriental Express Train, Singapore &amp; Thailand
                </h3>
                <p>
                 On board the Eastern &amp; Oriental Express luxury train, the atmosphere harks back to a Colonial' age.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: o.travel.
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="For wildlife: Alaska Railroads Glacier Discovery Train, Alaska" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Alaska-Railroad.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Alaska-Railroad-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 For wildlife: Alaska Railroads Glacier Discovery Train, Alaska
                </h3>
                <p>
                 Not one to shy from competition, the Alaska Railroad plans its route along vantage locations from the shore of Turnagain Arm, where beluga whales come to feed, to the Placer Valley, a wildlife habitat populated with moose, bears, and Arctic foxes. Along the way, you can hop off the train for canoeing or take a guided hike to Spencer Lake. Price: CAD$127 per person
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Alaskarailroad
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="For when youre pressed for time: The Flm Railway, Norway" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/flamrailway2.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/flamrailway2-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 For when youre pressed for time: The Flm Railway, Norway
                </h3>
                <p>
                 This 20km train journey takes you along a winding track across ravines and through tunnels, set against a stunning vista of snow-capped peaks and farms that hang precariously onto steep slopes. Be sure to get your camera ready as the train chugs along Sognefjord  the worlds second longest fjord. This ever-changing topography is yours to admire in less than 60 minutes.
Price: CAD$72 per person
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Visit Flm
                </p>
               </div>
               <div>
                <img alt="For when youre pressed for time: The Flm Railway, Norway" class="rsImg" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Flam.jpg"/>
                <div class="rsTmb">
                 <img alt="" src="http://www.boldmagazine.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Flam-150x150.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <h3>
                 For when youre pressed for time: The Flm Railway, Norway
                </h3>
                <p>
                 View from aboard the Flm Railway train.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Photo credit: Kennymatic
                </p>
               </div>
              </div>
             </span>
            </p>
