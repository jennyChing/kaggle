      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span class="name">
        UFO Cave Paintings Found in Chhattisgarh, India
       </span>
      </h1>
      <div class="entry">
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2246" style="width: 630px">
        <a href="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/alien-cave-paintings-in-india-travel-news-tourism-photos-lol-news-luggage-online-cool.jpg">
         <img alt="alien cave paintings,BJ Bhagat,travel news" class="size-full wp-image-2246" height="410" src="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/alien-cave-paintings-in-india-travel-news-tourism-photos-lol-news-luggage-online-cool.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         This photo shows BJ Bhagat looking at the alien cave paintings.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        In Chhattisgarh state, archaeologists and the culture department are seeking NASA and Isro to help the research their UFO cave paintings. The paintings are 10,000 years old. They depict aliens coming out of spaceships and the like.
       </p>
       <p>
        These paintings are located in the Charama region, in the Kanker district. The place is home to the Bastar tribal region. The paintings are about 130 KM from Raipur. They are under the village Chadeli and Gotitola. This ancient tribe has oral tradition that archaeologist are convinced that it relates to aliens.
       </p>
       <p>
        According to folklore, their ancestors met the Rohela people. They were small sized people that came from the sky, in small round shaped flying objects. Every so often they would come and take one or two people. Those taken were never heard from again.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2247" style="width: 619px">
        <a href="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/alien-paintings-india-photos-lol-news-luggage-online-tourism-travel-cool-scenery-cute.jpg">
         <img alt="alien paintings,cave paintings,cool photos" class="size-full wp-image-2247" height="626" src="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/alien-paintings-india-photos-lol-news-luggage-online-tourism-travel-cool-scenery-cute.jpg" width="609"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         This is another one of those alien paintings. This one shows the weird Alien with a stick and the odd antennas.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        It sounds a lot like alien abduction. According to Archaeologist JR Bhagat, the paintings show aliens like you see in Hollywood. These findings suggest that ancient humans had either met aliens or had an overactive imagination.
       </p>
       <p>
        Still, why call them people form the sky and not Gods? The ancestors must have sufficient evidence to tell the difference between one and another. Still, this is all speculation.
       </p>
       <p>
        The paintings were done with natural colors. They have not faded away much, despite their age. Many show strange people holding weapon like objects, of strange shapes. Unlike their people paintings, the Rohela people do not have mouth or noses. In some paintings, they wear space suits.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2248" style="width: 643px">
        <a href="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/ufo-cave-painting-lol-news-luggage-online-travel-photos-tourism-india.jpg">
         <img alt="UFOs,alien,alien paintings" class="size-full wp-image-2248" height="410" src="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/ufo-cave-painting-lol-news-luggage-online-travel-photos-tourism-india.jpg" width="633"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         This photo shows a little UFO landing or whatever.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The depictions are too futuristic for it to be primitive invention. After all, most primitive paintings focused on depicting reality: like hunting, family, hands, animals, humanoid or animal gods, plants and the like.
       </p>
       <p>
        The aliens have the fan like antennas. Their vehicle is a tripod like the ones from War of the Worlds. Other archaeologists are also being called upon to analyze these paintings.
       </p>
