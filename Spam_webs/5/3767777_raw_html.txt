         <p style="text-align: center;">
          It may feel like the winter that will never end but you guys, Spring is coming! And that means bright beautiful colors! Ill be honest and say that things look a little drab around our home right now so I recently decided on a snow day that the kids and I needed to get a little crafty and brighten things up! While we started with the idea of this topiary being a St. Patricks Day craft, we quickly realized that its a perfect way to add color to our home any time of the year. So are you ready to get crafting?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary.jpg">
           <img alt="Rainbow Topiary" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27569" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           What You Will Need:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          2 Bag of Skittles
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Foam Craft Ball
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Wooden Mounting Stick
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Terra Cotta Pot
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Decorative Grass
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Gold Ribbon
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Paint in Blue, Gold and Yellow
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Hot Glue and Hot Glue Gun
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           How to Make It:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Start be separating your Skittles into individual colors. This is a great time to get the kids involved and let them have five bowls and divide the colors out for you.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Skittles.jpg">
           <img alt="Skittles" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27576" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Skittles.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Once your colors are divided, you can add a quick coat of paint to your white foam ball if you would like. This step is not necessary but I chose to add a yellow coat to mine so that any places that showed through would be yellow. If you do paint your foam ball, let it dry and they youre ready to get started! Using your hot glue gun, create a straight line of one color of Skittles across your foam ball. Repeat the process using the other colors, one color at a time.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Process-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Rainbow Topiary Process 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27572" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Process-1.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          When your ball is about half way covered, youll want to insert your wooden mounting stick before finishing the rest of the way around. I chose to paint my mounting stick blue but you can really choose any color or even leave it natural. All you have to do to is push your stick up into the ball. *Tip: To help you craft look more like a rainbow, turn your ball slightly askew so that the lines are not straight but more at an angle.* Then remove your stick from the hole you created, add a dab of hot glue to the end and then place it back in the hole, using the hot glue to secure it.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Process-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Rainbow Topiary Process 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27573" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Process-2.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Once your mounting stick is in place, continue with your Skittles until the entire ball is covered. Set your Skittles covered ball aside and paint your Terra Cotta Pot with a few coats of gold paint. Allow your pot to dry completely. Then place your pole in the center, add rocks around it to secure it into place and top your rocks with your fake grass.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Process-4.jpg">
           <img alt="Rainbow Topiary Process 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27575" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Process-4.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Finally, tie a piece of gold ribbon in a bow around your mounting stick and youre done!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Final.jpg">
           <img alt="Rainbow Topiary Final" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27571" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Rainbow-Topiary-Final.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This adorable craft is the perfect way to bring color to any room in your home and its a great way to ensure everyone who visits your home find a pot of gold at the end of your rainbow!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Happy crafting!
         </p>
