                  <h2 class="short-teaser">
                   The Empire Strikes Back and Star Wars: Episode VII co-writer is reportedly writing the planned Boba Fett movie...
                  </h2>
                  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                   <div class="field-items">
                    <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                     <p>
                      Here's what we know about the collection of new
                      <em>
                       Star Wars
                      </em>
                      films thus far. JJ Abrams is currently shooting
                      <em>
                       Star Wars: Episode VII
                      </em>
                      . Gareth Edwards, of
                      <em>
                       Godzilla
                      </em>
                      and
                      <em>
                       Monsters
                      </em>
                      fame, has been hired to direct one of the planned spin-off movies, which Gary Whitta has signed up to write. And then
                      <em>
                       Chronicle
                      </em>
                      's Josh Trank has been snapped up to direct a further spin-off film.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The spin-offs are set to be films that centre on specific characters within the
                      <em>
                       Star Wars
                      </em>
                      universe, with Boba Fett, Solo and Red Five reportedly lined up. And it's now being rumoured that Lawrence Kasdan - who's already co-written
                      <em>
                       Star Wars: Episode VII
                      </em>
                      - has penned the script for the Boba Fett movie.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      SchmoesKnow broke the news, quoting one of its usually reliable sources as saying that "Kasdan has written a version of the movie". Whether this is the same one that Gareth Edwards and Gary Whitta are developing is unclear. They may be on a different spin-off movie, or Whitta may be working on Kasdan for the script for Boba Fett. Either way, Kasdan and Boba Fett seem to be linked somewhere along the line.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The report also notes that Kasdan may be dipping into the
                      <em>
                       Star Wars
                      </em>
                      Expanded Universe for his Boba Fett screenplay, which has been a part of the
                      <em>
                       Star Wars
                      </em>
                      empire generally regarded as non-canon. That said, there'd be nothing to stop Kasdan using themes from one of said stories, and wrapping it into a firm part of the
                      <em>
                       Star Wars
                      </em>
                      movie world.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      More on the assorted
                      <em>
                       Star Wars
                      </em>
                      films as we hear it.
                     </p>

                      .
                     </p>
