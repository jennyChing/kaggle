         <p>
          Zoo Tycoon 2: Wild New World is a
          <a href="/wiki/Usermade_Expansion_Packs" title="Usermade Expansion Packs">
           Usermade Expansion Pack
          </a>
          being made by
          <a href="/wiki/Super_Designers_United" title="Super Designers United">
           Super Designers United
          </a>
          . It is divided into three different parts (packs) which joined form the bigger Usermade Expansion Pack to date: over 50 animales, over 70 plants and many objects and extras for Zoo Tycoon 2. The tematic of the pack is the American continent: this UXP is divided in three packs for the purpose of showing each part of America with the msot detail possible: this packs are Central America Pack, North America Pack and South America Pack.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>

         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Central_America_Pack">
           Central America Pack
          </span>
          <span class="editsection">
           <a href="/wiki/Wild_New_World?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit Central America Pack section">
            <img class="sprite edit-pencil" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D"/>
            Edit
           </a>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <figure class="article-thumb tright show-info-icon" style="width: 180px">
          <a class="image image-thumbnail" href="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/zt2modding/images/6/6d/Central_america.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20101014010940">
           <img alt="Central america" class="thumbimage " data-image-key="Central_america.jpg" data-image-name="Central america.jpg" height="254" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/zt2modding/images/6/6d/Central_america.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20101014010940" width="180"/>
          </a>
          <figcaption>
           <a class="sprite info-icon" href="/wiki/File:Central_america.jpg">
           </a>
           <p class="caption">
            CAP Boxart
           </p>
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         The
         <b>
          Central America Pack
         </b>
         (or CAP) of Wild New World is the smallest part of it. Only includes 10 animals, 9 plants and the aztec theme. This pack was released on the time span going from 16 December 2008 to 14 February 2009. The pack included:
         <p>
          <u>
           Animals:
          </u>
          1- Tehuantepec Jackrabbit 2- Desmarest's Hutia 3- American Flamingo 4- American Crocodile 5- Pantropical Spotted Dolphin 6- Green basilisk
7- White lipped peccary 8- Caribbean monk seal (the only extinct animal on the whole uxp) 9- Kinkajou 10-Nine banded armadillo
         </p>
         <p>
          <u>
           Plants:
          </u>
          1- Sacred fir 2- Montezuma cypress 3- Mexican muhly grass 4- Pillar coral 5- Maya king palm 6- Blue agave 7- White bayahonda 8- Caribbean pine
9- Bean plant
         </p>
         <p>
          <u>
           Objects:
          </u>
          1- Aztec theme (with donation box, bench, etc..) 2- Jungle pool (reptile shelter)
         </p>
         <p>
          A patch for this pack is in the works, in order to improve anf fix various aspects.

         The
         <b>
          North America Pack
         </b>
         (or NAP) of Wild New World is a pack focusing on North America. It includes 24 animals, aprox 20 plants and an undeterminated theme. This pack is actually being released and some of its content, like the Dall Sheep, the Black Bear old version and some foliage items are released.

         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="South_America_Pack">
           South America Pack
          </span>
          <span class="editsection">
           <a href="/wiki/Wild_New_World?action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit South America Pack section">

         The
         <b>
          South America Pack
         </b>
         (or SAP) of Wild New World the main piece and the most expected part of WNW. This pack has already a teaser released, which is composed by a beta version of the giant arapaima and the brazilian waterweed.
