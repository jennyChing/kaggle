       <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
        Welcome
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta clearfix">
        <span class="updated">
         <span class="value-title" title="2012-02-13T20:45">
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p>
         This site is dedicated to helping you understand and use technology.
        </p>
        <p>
         This is not a site about the latest electronic gadget, nor is it just a "how to" website. It also has a growing reference section where you can learn and understand the principles behind various technologies. Understanding the technology will make it easier to work with it, rather then just being frustrated with it - or least, you will understand why you are frustrated with technology.
        </p>
        <p>
         The site is divided into various sections or major topics to help you, namely:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geoffthegreygeek.com/category/home-av/">
          <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
           <strong>
            Home AV
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         : explains and demonstrates technologies associated with home entertainment systems: DVD players, flat screen TVs, projectors, PVRs etc. It is general in nature and does not push or mention any particular brand. The reference section explains in simple terms the principles behind the technologies, while the "How do I..." section gives general advise on connecting it all together.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geoffthegreygeek.com/category/understanding-audio/">
          <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            <strong>
             <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
              Understanding Au
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            <strong>
             <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
              d
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            <strong>
             <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
              io
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         : has a number of articles to help you understand "audio". This is designed for people wanting to learn about setting up audio systems, or understand more about getting good audio from a PA system. It is written for intelligent novices, particularly teachers, leaders, musicians and students who need to understand their sound system at school, church or group. It is also useful for PA operators, journalists, videographers and even technicians.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geoffthegreygeek.com/category/ur-the-technician/" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
           <strong>
            When you are the Technician
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         : was originally written when I was a missionary in Africa. It is designed for people who need to know how to do technical things because there is no-one else around to do it for you. The
         <a href="http://geoffthegreygeek.com/category/ur-the-technician/fundamentals/">
          Fundamentals
         </a>
         section covers the fundamental principles for understanding electrical/technical systems and is useful for everyone.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geoffthegreygeek.com/category/calculators/">
          <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
           <strong>
            Calculators
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         : is a collection of simple calculators to help better understand and use the audio and electrical principles outlined in the articles of this web site. These can be a useful learning tool by trying different values and see the changing results.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geoffthegreygeek.com/category/myths-trivia/">
          <span style="color: #1f9df6;">
           <strong>
            Myths &amp; Trivia
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         : is a fun section that looks at some of the technical myths that are often mis-understood and some short bits of technical trivia which I find interesting - let me know if you do too.
        </p>
        <p>
         I
         <object align="right" height="70" width="65">
          <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="//www.facebook.com/plugins/like.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FGeoffTheGreyGeek%3F&amp;width&amp;layout=box_count&amp;action=like&amp;show_faces=false&amp;share=false&amp;height=65&amp;appId=552334274806445" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; height:65px;">
          </iframe>
         </object>
         hope you find the information in these articles useful. If you do, please let others know about this resource - you can even click on the Facebook "like" button here. To save you checking in to see what is new, simply leave your first name and email address in the "subscription" box (on the right) and I will let you know every now again about new articles.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           "Understanding is a fountain of life to the one who has it, but stubborn fools punish themselves with their stupidity"
          </strong>
         </em>
         -
         <em>
          Proverbs 16:22 GOD'S WORD Translation (1995)
         </em>
