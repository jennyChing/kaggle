       <h1 class="postTitle">
        Full Moon View From Nile River
       </h1>
       <h2 class="author" style="margin-bottom: 2px">
        Written By:
        <span class="normalWeight">
         jeff
        </span>
       </h2>
       <h2 class="author">
        Posted On:
        <span class="normalWeight">
         November 25th, 2011
        </span>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="career break travel adventures in Egypt, Nile river, full moon" class="aligncenter" height="427" src="http://careerbreaksecrets.smugmug.com/Portfolio/Sweet-Shots/i-GkPGp8j/0/L/MG1728-L.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         By far the best part of my
         <a href="http://careerbreaksecrets.com/category/africa/egypt" target="_blank">
          trip to Egypt
         </a>
         was the three-day felucca ride up the Nile from Aswan to Luxor. There were 5 of us on our felucca: me, my friend Zoe, and 3 guides/boatsman. I thought we got lucky by being on the Nile during a full moon. But, Zoe told me later that she had planned for us to be on the Nile so we could have that as part of our experience. During the day, several large cruise boats passed us by. But, at night, it was quiet with only the stars and the moon around. It was a suh-weet experience and I got lucky enough to capture this
         <a href="http://careerbreaksecrets.com/category/suh-weet-shots" target="_blank">
          Suh-Weet shot
         </a>
         !
        </p>
