      <div class="eza-body clearfix p402_premium" id="story-body">
       <p>
        Tesla Motors built 8,763 Model S electriccarsand delivered 7,579 of them to customers during the second quarter, according to just-released second-quarter results.
       </p>
       <p>
        It expects to build about 9,000 cars this quarter, and deliver 7,800, largely due to a two-week production shutdown for retooling at its factory in
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Fremont" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: Fremont">
         Fremont
        </a>
        ,
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/California" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: California">
         California
        </a>
        --which cost it about 2,000 cars, it said.
       </p>
       <a id="eztoc18800617_4" name="eztoc18800617_4">
       </a>
       <h2>
        35,000 cars this year
       </h2>
       <p>
        But thecompanysays it is still on track to deliver about 35,000 cars during calendar 2014, its original guidance for the year's sales and leasing.
       </p>
       <div class="promo_link ezc-csm_quiz">

       <p>
        The factory upgrades will allow Tesla to produce 1,000 cars a week or more once the line is running at full speed.
       </p>

       <p>
        This article covers some of the highlights of the letter to shareholders released by the company, along with the subsequent analysts' conference call.
       </p>
       <a id="eztoc18800617_5" name="eztoc18800617_5">
       </a>
       <h2>
        Model S and Model X by end of 2015
       </h2>
       <p>
        Tesla still expects to be delivering at a rate of more than 100,000 cars by the end of 2015, whencapacityexpansion has been completed and its Model Xcrossoverhas joined the Model S in full production.
       </p>
       <p>
        The good news, said the shareholder letter, is that demand for theModelS continues to grow.
       </p>
       <p>
        "Average global delivery wait times increased because our production growth was unable to keep pace with increased demand."
       </p>
       <p>
        The total pool of Teslavehicleson the road, moreover, has covered almost 400 million miles.
       </p>
       <a id="eztoc18800617_6" name="eztoc18800617_6">
       </a>
       <h2>
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Tesla+Gigafactory" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: Tesla Gigafactory">
         Gigafactory
        </a>
        progress
       </h2>
       <p>
        Moreover, the company has broken ground in
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Nevada" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: Nevada">
         Nevada
        </a>
        on a "potential" site for its lithium-ion cell fabrication "gigafactory," which had been the subject of multiple rumors over the last few weeks.
       </p>
       <p>
        The company said it "remain[s] on track with the site selection process," and that, "Consistent with our strategy to identify and break ground on multiple sites, we continue to evaluate other locations in
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Arizona" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: Arizona">
         Arizona
        </a>
        , California,
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/New+Mexico" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: New Mexico">
         New Mexico
        </a>
        , and
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Texas" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: Texas">
         Texas
        </a>
        ."
       </p>
       <p>
        The company announced an agreement with cell partner
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Panasonic+Corporation" rel="nofollow" target="_self" title="Title: Panasonic Corporation">
         Panasonic
        </a>
        this morning to build the factory, which Tesla will operate. Panasonic will invest in production equipment for the factory, while Tesla will "prepare and provide" the building, utilities, and more.
        <span class="eos_marker" id="end-of-story" title="-30-">
        </span>
       </p>
