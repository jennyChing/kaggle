      <li>
       Click on one of the titles within that category to request a
       <u>
        FREE
       </u>
       lookup.
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       Fill out the request form completely.
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       Only
       <u>
        One (1) LOOKUP REQUEST
       </u>
       per day. (If you have 2 or 3 email accounts, please DO NOT use them all. 
Give others a chance to have their requests answered.)
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       Requests for listings on an entire
       <u>
        SURNAME
       </u>
       (A person's last or family name) will be deleted.
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       Lookup requests are answered in the order they are received. The results will be emailed to you.
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       If you do not receive a reply with in one week you may want to send your free lookup request again.
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       Databases are labeled "FREE" Lookup or "PAID" Lookup.
      </li>
      <br/>
      <li>
       You will receive an automated message letting you know that we received your Free Lookup Request and you have been 
entered in our

        Free Genealogy Resource Drawing
       </a>
