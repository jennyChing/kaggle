                      <br/>

                      They say they pay based on performance but that the average agent makes $14-$18 per hour. They say this is for "successful calls." I am not sure what makes a successful call yet. There does not seem to be any selling involved whatsoever. They say the calls are all about building good medical habits and routines. The hours are flexible. You can work as little or as much as you like between the hours of 8am and midnight.
