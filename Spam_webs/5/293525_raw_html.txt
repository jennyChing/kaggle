      <h1>
       Glow Bug  Wings Out
      </h1>
      <div class="postauthor">
       <p>
        Posted by
        <a href="http://www.elliottlemenager.com/author/admin/" title="Posts by Elliott">
         Elliott
        </a>
        on Friday, May 13, 2011 
        <a href="http://www.elliottlemenager.com/2011/05/13/glow-bug-wings-out/#comments">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="http://www.facebook.com/plugins/comments.php?href=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.elliottlemenager.com%2F2011%2F05%2F13%2Fglow-bug-wings-out%2F&amp;permalink=1" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; width:130px; height:16px;">
         </iframe>

       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="fb-like fb-social-plugin" data-action="like" data-colorscheme="light" data-enabled="true" data-font="arial" data-layout="standard" data-position="both" data-ref="wp" data-send="true" data-show-faces="true" data-width="250">

      <p>
       Today Pandora wasnt giving me anything good to listen to while I wrapped up the last couple hours at work. So, I decided to hit up twitter for suggestions and
       <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/chadschulz">
        Chad Schulz
       </a>
       sent me a link to
       <a href="http://glowbug.bandcamp.com/">
        Glow Bug
       </a>
       a local band based out of Bellingham, WA. After the first few seconds of listening I was hooked. Chad informed me that Daniel Anderson from Glow Bug actually came from Idiot Pilot and The Ghost and The Grace, I would suggest checking them out as well. Thanks Chad and now for your listening pleasure.
      </p>
