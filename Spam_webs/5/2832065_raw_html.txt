        <div class="blog-entry-text">
         <p>
          Is your fleet, like so many in the transportation industry today, facing a high driver turnover rate? If so, how do you attract and retain the best drivers? How do you make sure your drivers are well trained, informed, safe, and satisfied? The more you build good driver relationships, the more likely drivers are to stay, increasing efficiency and customer satisfaction. To help you keep your best drivers, weve developed a useful guide that explores the different reasons drivers leave fleets and the implications of high driver turnover your fleet can face. In the guide, we share tips and technology that can help improve driver recruitment and retention and ultimately save your company time and money!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Click the image below to check out our guide, Where Are All the Drivers?  Improving Retention to Keep Your Best Drivers for the Long Haul.
          </strong>
         </p>
