          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwals biography is worth a read. He is the most known name in India from last 3 years. He is the current Chief Minister of Delhi and the Founder of the
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aam_Aadmi_Party" target="_blank">
            Aam Aadmi Party
           </a>
           . He is a Ramon Magsaysay (Asias Nobel Prize)award winner and also declared as the Indian of the year by NDTV and CNN-IBN. In 2014 elections, surveys showed that he was the second choice for Prime Minister after Narendra Modi. He was an RTI activist and regarded as one of the bestsocial activist against corruption and crony capitalism.

           Arvind Kejriwal is a pure vegetarian and practicing Vipassana. He used all his Magsaysay Award money to improve his NGO Parivartan.

           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-biography.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal biography and Facts" class="aligncenter wp-image-184 size-full" height="338" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-biography.jpg.pagespeed.ce.jV_yMcBqX3.jpg" title="Arvind Kejriwal biography and Facts" width="666"/>

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Early Childhood &amp; Family
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwal was born on 16th August 1968 in Siwani, Bhiwani district of Haryana. He belongs to a financially sound middle class family. His father, Mr. Gobind Ram Kejriwal was an electrical engineer who gota lot of transfers during his service period. Arvind changed many schools because of his fathers professional transfers. His mother, Gita Kejriwal is a housewife and played a vital role in shaping his ethics and career. Arvind has a younger brother and a sister. His brother also donated a lot during formation of the Aam Aadmi Party.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-childhood.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal childhood Picture - Arvind Kejriwal Biography" class="aligncenter wp-image-185 size-full" height="253" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-childhood.jpg.pagespeed.ce.xelylxOy34.jpg" width="427"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Arvind Kejriwal Childhood
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He is married to Sunita Kejriwal, who is also an IRS officer. The two were batch mates in civil services training period and joined IRS together and later decided to marry. The couple has one girl and a boy. Harshita Kejriwal is the elder child of AK and qualified IIT exams like his father, while his son Pulkit Kejriwal is still studying in school.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Education
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He was born on Janmashtami (birth day of Lord Krishna) and one day after the Independence day of India. He was also called as Munna by his parents.Arvind was a very well known scholar from his schooldays. He did his schooling form Siwani and Ghaziabad. According to his mother, Kejriwal was so focused towards his studies that he even did his studies in bathroom if any guest arrived at home. After seeing his zeal and commitment, AKs grandfather Mangal Chand Kejriwal wanted him to be a doctor and even bought a land in Hissar, Haryanawhere he couldbuild a hospital for Arvind to practice. But Arvind wasinspired more from his seniors who joined IITs and finally he decided to do engineering from IIT only.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Schooling.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal School days picture from his biography" class="aligncenter wp-image-186 size-full" height="247" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Schooling.jpg.pagespeed.ce.93P2tR7xYg.jpg" width="331"/>
           </a>
           <strong>
            Arvind Kejriwal in School Dress
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He qualified JEE and got a seat in IIT Kharagpur. Arvind also played a vital role to stop ragging in his college. AK was a big movie lover and one of his colleague used to buy tickets on Friday for the whole group of friends.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Career
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwal joined IIT Kharagpur and did his B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering. Tata Steel hired him in 1989 to work as Mechanical Engineer in Jamshedpur. He worked there for around 3 years. He wanted to work in Social welfare department of Tata Steel but officials didnt allow him to do so. Thus finally he resigned from Tata Steel in 1992 and decided to prepare for IAS exams. In 1995, he cleared Civil Service Exams to join the public service.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind_Kejriwal-IIT.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind_Kejriwal IIT pics" class="aligncenter wp-image-187 size-full" height="251" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind_Kejriwal-IIT.jpg.pagespeed.ce.VSfDqp72Yp.jpg" width="440"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           In 2000, he took a paid leave for 2 years to pursue higher education. He later joined in 2003 but resigned after 18 months with a zeal of doing something for nation. Everybody was quite shocked in his family and relatives when they came to know that this young man left the post of Joint Commissioner of Income tax in New Delhi.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Social Activities
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Kejriwal was a social activist from the very beginning. He even met Mother Teresa and wanted to work for this society. He worked in RamaKrishna Ashram, Kolkata for few days. He spent few days in Nehru Yuva Kendra as well.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He started an NGO named 
           <strong>
            Parivartan
           </strong>
            with few other social activists. He developed a website for the same and invited people to take part in this initiative. The first man to join him with the help of this site was none other than Manish Sisodia. They together helped a lot of people to get rid off from the problems of ration, electricity, pensions and corruption. In beginning, they used banners to spread more about their social NGO Parivartan.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He protested several times in various ways against the wrongdoings of government.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-protests.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal protests" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-188" height="418" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-protests.jpg.pagespeed.ce.Je_ifM38hn.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           In 2011, he directed the Anna Movement against corruption which had shaken the whole country. The whole parliament was shocked by the power of Aam Aadmi. The movements main aim was to curb high rate of corruption in India via JanlokPal Bill. The movement was a success as it touchedthe hearts and minds of Indians and the government got ready to pass JanlokPal Bill but due to many issues, the bill could never be passed.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           He also fasted and gave Dharnas to force government to take steps on corruption but never achieved success.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Politics and Aam Aadmi Party
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Finally after trying each and every tactics, Arvind Kejriwal decided to form a political party. The party was formed on
           <em>
            <strong>
             26 November 2012
            </strong>
           </em>
           and named after a lot of consultations. Finally his party got the name on
           <strong>
            <em>
             Aam Aadmi or common man- Aam Aadmi Party
            </em>
           </strong>
           . He even fasted against Delhi Congress government of Sheila Dixit in March 2013. He opened a revolutionary campaign against Sheila Dixits government and MCD ruled BJP. The selfless volunteers and AK online supporters helped him a lot to present AAP as an alternative of BJP and Congress. He organized a lot of Nukkad Sabhas and promised to deliver on manythings which any government is unable to do till date. His speeches were very motivational and inspired a lot of Indians.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-wallpaper1.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal wallpaper" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-190" height="320" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-wallpaper1.jpg.pagespeed.ce.BjAreoOeuW.jpg" width="433"/>
           </a>
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-wallpaper.jpg">
            <br/>
           </a>
           Finally on 4th December 2013, Delhi Elections took place in which Arvind Kejriwal was standing against 3 times Delhi CM Sheila Dixit fr0m New Delhi constituency. On 8th December, Arvind Kejriwal defeated the CM Sheila Dixit by a huge margin and the Aam Aadmi Party won 28 seats. Both BJP and Congress who won 32 and 8 seats motivated AAP by giving constructive and unconditional support respectively. Thus AAP finally made government in Delhi on 28th December 2013 with the support of Congress.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Delhi-elections.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal Delhi elections - Arvind Kejriwal Biography " class="aligncenter wp-image-191 size-full" height="502" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Delhi-elections.jpg.pagespeed.ce.bxwv70ysZr.jpg" width="768"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwal took oath in Ramlila Maidan as 7th Chief Minister of Delhi. He became the second youngest person to get the thrown of CMs chair. He fulfilled the promise of electricity and water bills. As soon as he became CM, many officers got transfers and many corrupt officers burnt scam files and reports. The weaker section of society got a big relief as they didnt have to pay bribes to get their work done.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Chief-Minister.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal Chief Minister" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-192" height="400" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Chief-Minister.jpg.pagespeed.ce.4Ciwt--eTN.jpg" width="660"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            List of major work done by him in just 49 days-
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            Lowered the rates of electricity
           </li>
           <li>
            Lowered the rates of water consumption
           </li>
           <li>
            Simplified the VAT for Delhi businessmen
           </li>
           <li>
            Played a vital role to curb corruption
           </li>
           <li>
            Played a vital role to curb inflation
           </li>
           <li>
            launched anti-corruption helpline
           </li>
           <li>
            Gave an amount of 1 Crore to a martyr policemen
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwal also gave an order to power authorities to fulfill the electricity needs of country otherwise theyll bring some other power companies in Delhi. The party decided to pass JanLokpal Bill but was failed as Congress and BJP stood together against the bill passing procedure.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Finally Arvind Kejriwal resigned in 49 days on 14th Februry 2014 since he failed to get the JanLokPal Bill passed because of BJP and Congress. According to many surveys people still believe that 49 days were best but Arvind Kejriwal shouldnt have resigned. He decided to fight elections in Lok Sabha 2014 against Narendra Modi (PM candidate from BJP) but was could not get succeeded.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           After Lok Sabha elections, some former members left the party and Arvind Kejriwal even went to jail in a case of defamation against Nitin Gadkari. Everyone thought this party is going to finish soon but then Arvind decided to open Mission Vistaar to work again from grass root level.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           On 3rd August,
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/delhi-elections-will-aam-aadmi-form-government-in-delhi-again/" target="_blank">
            Arvind Kejriwal organized a rally against BJP on Jantar Mantar demanding fresh elections in Delhi
           </a>
           . This rally was a huge success for AAP as around 9K people took part in it.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           But as soon as Delhi Elections were announced, Arvind Kejriwal again rebuild his army and all surveys shows his as the most preferred Cm for Delhi in 2015 elections. He is again contesting from his New Delhi seat.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal Jantar Mantar " class="aligncenter wp-image-194 size-full" height="270" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-Jantar-Mantar.jpg.pagespeed.ce.yDKplodKbY.jpg" width="670"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            As an MLA
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwal worked a lot as an MLA. He held several Mohalla Sabhas in his New Delhi legislative constituency to know the problems of people and area. He carried out 60 projects and many projects had been successfully implemented in a short time.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Here is the report card of Mr. Kejriwal showing how he used his MLA local area development fund
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Arvind-Kejriwal-report-card.jpg">
            <img alt="Arvind Kejriwal report card" class="alignnone wp-image-1816" height="593" src="http://www.letuspublish.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/738x593xArvind-Kejriwal-report-card.jpg.pagespeed.ic.H2NiEoyjDj.jpg" width="738"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            #MufflerMan
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Arvind Kejriwal became famous as Mufflerman. The campaign of branding Arvind Kejriwal became very famous via #Mufflerman.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Awards and Accolades
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>

           </li>

           </li>
           <li>

           </li>

           </li>
