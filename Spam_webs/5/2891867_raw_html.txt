         <p>
          Dear Maria,
         </p>
         <p>
          Where did you go when the rain started falling? You took my umbrella, but I thought that you would be back. The rain started falling. I waited. It drizzled ice spikes, and pounded old men's tears.
         </p>
         <p>
          I shook like a child's temper. My skin bleach white under a blazing light. The sun raced above us cutting through the forest fog.
         </p>
         <p>
          "Maria! I loved you in the Spring of our youth, and the Summer of our adulthood. Maria! I have loved you in the falling embers of our life. It is cold here, in this storm, without you Maria! I will wait for you my love. Through the coldest winter I will wait for you! I love you my sweet Maria!"
         </p>
         <p>
          The rain pounded on. It grew angry at my will, at my defiance, at my resiliency. It roared on, and I dared to roar back. It took my arm. It took the use of my legs. It never dared to take you from my memory, my love.
         </p>
         <p>
          The eye of the storm is above me. My bones are ice, brittle shells, yet they still yearn for you. Crash into me and bring me home. I wish you would have never left ours.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our children, our six children, they live here in the town we called home. Oh God, they remind me of you every time they stop by. Samantha promises me that I will never grow old. We play chess when my mind is not foggy. I love them all, and they all think of you.
         </p>
         <p>
          My mind is going Maria. I am loosing years of the past every day. This letter will remind me of you when my aching heart is not enough. One day, this
         </p>

	//Note, we should refactor below once the messaging system is out.
	//Below is from https://gist.github.com/arbales/1654670
	var ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION, LINK_DETECTION_REGEX, MARKSAFE_FUNCTION, emojify, formatTextForHTML, linkify;

	//below regex is from: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/6927719/url-regex-does-not-work-in-javascript
	LINK_DETECTION_REGEX = /(([a-z]+:\/\/)?(([a-z0-9\-]+\.)+([a-z]{2}|aero|arpa|biz|com|coop|edu|gov|info|int|jobs|mil|museum|name|nato|net|org|pro|travel|local|internal))(:[0-9]{1,5})?(\/[a-z0-9_\-\.~]+)*(\/([a-z0-9_\-\.]*)(\?[a-z0-9+_\-\.%=&amp;]*)?)?(#[a-zA-Z0-9!$&amp;'()*+.=-_~:@/?]*)?)(\s+|$)/gi;

	ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION = Handlebars.Utils.escapeExpression;

	MARKSAFE_FUNCTION = function(str) {
	  return new Handlebars.SafeString(str);
	};
	linkify = function(safeContent) {
	  return safeContent.replace(LINK_DETECTION_REGEX, function(url) {
	  	brEnd = false;
	  	if(url.indexOf("\n")) {
	  		brEnd = true;
	  	}
	  	url = url.trim();
	    url = url.replace(/(?:\r\n|\r|\n)/g, '');
	    var address;
	    address = /[a-z]+:\/\//.test(url) ? url : "http://" + url;    
	    url = url.replace(/^https?:\/\//, '');
	    link = "&lt;a href='" + address + "' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'&gt;" + url + "&lt;/a&gt;";
	    if(brEnd) {
	    	return link + '&lt;br/&gt;';
	    } else {
	    	return link;
	    }
	  });
	};

	formatTextForHTML = function(content) {
	  var safeContent;
	  safeContent = ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION(content);
	  safeContent = linkify(safeContent);
	  safeContent = safeContent.replace(/(?:\r\n|\r|\n)/g, '&lt;br /&gt;');
	  //safeContent = emojify(safeContent);
	  return MARKSAFE_FUNCTION(safeContent);
	};

	Handlebars.registerHelper('formatTextForHTML', formatTextForHTML);
