        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Crock Pot Taco Soup
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         This post may contain affiliate links.
         <a href="http://mindofmeesh.com/disclaimer/">
          Read my full disclosure
         </a>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mindofmeesh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Crock-Pot-Taco-Soup-Sideways.jpg">
          <img alt="Crock Pot Taco Soup " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5541" height="400" src="http://mindofmeesh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Crock-Pot-Taco-Soup-Sideways.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Crock Pot Taco Soup is our family go-to meal when we are looking for something easy and quick to make that can lastfor days. Everyone likes this meal in our family with the exception of the little one, my stepson and husband love it. Another great perk about this meal is that it makes great leftovers and some would say tastes better that way! You can make this recipe using a crock pot or just by mixing it all together and leaving it on the stove top for an hour with higher heat.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mindofmeesh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Crock-Pot-Taco-Soup-Pinterest.jpg">
          <img alt="Crock Pot Taco Soup " class="aligncenter wp-image-5540" height="605" src="http://mindofmeesh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Crock-Pot-Taco-Soup-Pinterest.jpg" width="403"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 lb. Ground Beef with taco seasoning
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 can of fiesta corn
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 can ranch style pinto beans
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 15 oz can of tomato soup
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 can of cheddar cheese soup
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 can of rotel
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             can of water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 package of ranch dip mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Optional: Fritos, cheese, and sour cream for toppings.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cook ground beef and season with taco seasoning (follow directions of taco seasoning package)
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix all the ingredients including the cooked ground beef in a crock pot.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cook on low for at least 4 hours in crock pot or cook on medium on the stove in a big pot for 30 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add Fritos, cheese and sour cream to garnish your soup!
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Enjoy!
           </li>
          </ol>
