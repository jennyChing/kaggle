         Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop
        </h1>

         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6705531" data-link="http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/" data-summary="" data-title="Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop">

         <p>
          <em>
           This shop has been compensated by Collective Bias, Inc. and its advertiser. All opinions are mine alone.
          </em>

         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           #PriceChopperB2S #CollectiveBias
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Its that time again  back to school or most likely soon for many of us! What would be a great, homemade breakfast that the whole family is sure to love and get everyone off to a good start? So I thought to myself, what is one of your favorite things to eat in the morning? I love cereal, but its not like you can take a bowl of it in the car or on the school bus. Thats it! I knew I had the perfect idea! I would make a cereal bar
          <a href="http://www.pricechopper.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           recipe
          </a>
          to fill up everyones bellies in the morning.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 27" class="alignnone wp-image-2443" height="522" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-27.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-27.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I figured Id head to my local Price Chopper
          <a href="http://www.pricechopper.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           supermarket
          </a>
          to check out all of the items in the Back to School solutions center to pick up all of the ingredients for my recipe, as well as getting some great savings! I strolled down the aisles to find all of the items and ingredients for this easy dish. Theres so many fantastic food items to make back to school easier, whether its breakfast, lunch or dinner time.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 14" class="alignnone wp-image-2428" height="397" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-14.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-14.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I love that you can pair this cereal bar with a single serve Yoplait Greek yogurt, a Capri Sun juice pouch on the go, or my favorite  a glass of milk.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 12" class="alignnone wp-image-2427" height="522" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-12.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-12.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Alright, so Ive got the powdered sugar, honey, brown sugar, powdered milk, mixed nuts and parchment paper in my cart. Time to pick up a box of Honey Nut Cheerios!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 4" class="alignnone wp-image-2426" height="939" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-4.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-4.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I threw my box of Honey Nut Cheerios in the cart and headed to check out. I couldnt wait to get home to make this fun and delicious cereal bar recipe! Once I got home and got things unloaded, I put the butter and powdered sugar in my mixer. Dont worry, the complete recipes at the bottom for you!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 15" class="alignnone wp-image-2429" height="939" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-15.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-15.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Next, I slowly added the powdered milk, followed by the vanilla. I mixed all of that together, adding tablespoons of hot water, one at a time until I felt it was thinned out enough. I planned on using this as the icing for the cereal bars at the end. It was a nice finisher to this easy treat.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 17" class="alignnone wp-image-2431" height="939" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-17.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-17.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I crushed up some mixed nuts by putting them in a resealable plastic food storage bag and firmly crushing them with my rolling pin. I dumped them into the large bowl that I had poured the pre-measured Honey Nut Cheerios into. Then I poured the deliciously sticky concoction of honey, butter and brown sugar all over the nutty Cheerio mixture.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 18" class="alignnone wp-image-2432" height="939" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-18.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-18.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I was getting so hungry just looking at this and stirring it together, imagining how good it was going to taste, as I spread it out in my parchment paper lined pan.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 22" class="alignnone wp-image-2436" height="522" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-22.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-22.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Once I had it all spread out as evenly as possible, I spread the icing I made earlier over it.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 20" class="alignnone wp-image-2434" height="939" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-20.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-20.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Once I cut into the large pan and tasted it, I couldnt wait to give one to my husband. He loved it and asked for another one. I was really pleased as this was my first time making this type of recipe. What cereal would you choose to make this cereal bar recipe with?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="PC BTS 26" class="alignnone wp-image-2442" height="522" src="http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-26.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://jennunplugged.com/cereal-bar-recipe/&amp;media=http://jennunplugged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/PC-BTS-26.jpg&amp;description=Back to School Cereal Bar Recipe with Price Chopper Supermarket #PriceChopperB2S #shop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>

          <div class="ERSIngredients">
           <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup butter, softened
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup powdered sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup non-fat powdered milk
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              teaspoon vanilla extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1-2 tablespoons hot water, as needed to thin
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             ------------------------------------------------------------
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             4 cups Honey Nut Cheerios
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cups mixed nuts
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup packed brown sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup honey
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tablespoon butter
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cream butter and sugar with mixer.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add dry milk a little at a time.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add vanilla.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Mix on high until smooth, adding water as needed a tablespoon at a time.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Spread between cereal bars or use as icing, as I did.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             ------------------------------------------------------------
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Line bottom and sides of 8-inch square pan with parchment paper. If you don't have parchment paper, you can line it with foil and spray that with cooking spray to prevent sticking.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In large bowl, mix cereal and mixed nuts; set aside.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In large microwavable bowl, microwave brown sugar, honey and butter on high 2 minutes, stirring about half way between.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour over cereal mixture, stir until evenly coated.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Using the back of a spoon, press mixture into pan.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Spread icing over cereal in pan.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Refrigerate about an hour to firm them up.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Afterwards, cut them into 4x4 rows.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Store covered at room temperature up to 1 week.
            </li>
           </ol>
