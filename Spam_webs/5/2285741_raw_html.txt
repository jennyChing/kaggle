         Role Of The PHP In The Process Of Web Development
        </h3>
        <div class="blog-info">

         <div class="blog-content">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Even though there has been a sporadic change in the IT service provider companies but on an average the growth has always been positive. The credit for this positive surge clearly goes to the booming
           <a href="http://www.goigi.com/software-development.aspx" title="Software Development">
            <strong>
             software development
            </strong>
           </a>
           sector for providing the state of the art services to their clients. The software development sector over the last decade has revolutionized the IT enabled service sector by gaining the positive feedback of its users. In the late 90s the database backend development sector started to take pace. This technology literally changed the face of the sectors dependent on the databases. The core sectors involved in this are the financial and the banking institutions. The early two thousand saw that rapid emergence of the dot com bubble which literally shot up the economy world over. Out of the various avenues of ICT and software development the
           <a href="http://www.goigi.com/website-development.aspx" title="Website Development">
            <strong>
             website development
            </strong>
           </a>
           area created a potential market for the IT sector companies. A all round development of the technology and a dedicated framework for all inclusive growth has led to a wonderful execution of the various steps of the software development life cycle.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           More importantly the requirement analysis module has always been given top priority. Off late when the user trend was to go online through various ways like the social networking and the websites then the server side scripting language like PHP took shape and as a result more and more developments took place in this area. With the rapid advent of the web development sector PHP took the form of a general purpose programming language. The web frameworks and the templating engine got a boost with the mixing of PHP with HTML. With its initial shape in the 1994 PHP got developed over the years and at present the latest version is the PHP7. A PHP code embedded in an HTML document has syntaxes and semantics of the form of &lt;! DOCTYPE html&gt; for declaration part with &lt;head&gt;, &lt;body&gt; and &lt;title&gt; clearly mentioning the various segments of the code. The data types in PHP for whole numbers and well as for the floating point are platform dependent range. The functions in PHP are provided from two fronts, the first one being the core language functionality and the second from various extensions. In all there are hundreds of functions which are well documented in the online manual of the PHP development document. The sole problem associated with the PHP implementation is its dependence on various Engines. Its compatibility with most of the web servers as well as many operating systems as well as platforms has potentially increased its operating area with many relational database management systems.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           At the end the testing of the module developed in any software life cycle is a very important step. The development of state of the art software can be regarded as the incremental development of the economy.
          </p>
