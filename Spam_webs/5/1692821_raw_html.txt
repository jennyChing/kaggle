      <p>
       Coconatch is a cute little robot that does a dance whenever it detects an @reply on twitter. The robot, connected via USB, is attached to a users twitter handle and gives alerts of mentions by lighting up and wobbling from side to side.
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="361" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/JXNe0TZ6YlI" title="YouTube video player" type="text/html" width="590">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       Each Coconatch also has a unique email address and also responds to email messages sent to that address. You can have your own Coconatch by heading to
       <a href="http://www.coconatch.com/index.php" target="_blank">
        their website
       </a>
       . Itll cost you 5500 yen which is about $65.
      </p>
