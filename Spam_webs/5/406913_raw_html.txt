              <p>
               A grandfather from Lumberton, North Carolina, proved himself a force to be reckoned with after he found himself face to face with three armed men who broke into his home Monday night and allegedly attempted to rape his 19-year-old granddaughter.
              </p>
              <p>
               Kenneth Byrd, 67, and his wife were reportedly at home at around 10 p.m. when they heard a knock on the door. A man standing outside his house told Byrd he was experiencing car trouble and asked if he could have water, reports
               <a href="http://www.inquisitr.com/1558955/nc-home-invasion-grandpa-shoots-to-kill-protecting-granddaughter-from-gang-rape/" rel="nofollow">
                Inquisitr
               </a>
               . Two armed men then appeared behind him wearing black clothing, ski masks and gloves. The trio pushed Byrd aside and entered their home while demanding money, reports
               <a href="http://www.theblaze.com/stories/2014/10/23/police-say-three-men-invaded-home-and-attempted-to-gang-rape-19-year-old-but-they-didnt-come-prepared-for-grandpa/" rel="nofollow">
                The Blaze
               </a>
               . The alleged criminals reportedly took the elderly couple to the back of their home, where there is a safe, and told them to open it.
              </p>
              <p>
               But Byrd realized he couldn't just sit by when he discovered the trio had other plans, as well: they reportedly intended to gang rape his teen granddaughter.
              </p>
              <p>
               Using a gun that he retrieved from inside of his safe, the grandfather reportedly shot all three suspects and was shot in return by one of the men.
              </p>
              <div class="in-content-ad" id="in-content-ad">
              </div>
              <div class="in-content-ad" id="in-content-ad-ssl">
              </div>
              <p>
               The suspects then fled the scene in Byrds Cadillac. Two of the men  identified as Brandon Carver Stephens and Jamar Hawkins -- sought treatment at a local hospital, where police reportedly apprehended them. The third suspect  20-year-old Jamie Lee Faison, was found dead in the Cadillac.
              </p>
              <p>
               Byrd, who is being hailed as a hero, was reportedly airlifted to a hospital and is in critical condition.
              </p>
