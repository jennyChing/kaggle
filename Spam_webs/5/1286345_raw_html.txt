        <div class="fulltext-image">
         <a class="zoom-image" href="/images/wsscontent/articles/2015/02/mad-catz-r-a-t-pro-waiting-for-spring.jpg">
          <img alt="new mouse Mad Catz R.A.T Pro" src="/images/wsscontent/articles/2015/02/mad-catz-r-a-t-pro-waiting-for-spring.jpg"/>

        <div itemprop="summary">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It has come to pass! This year, at the beginning of January, Company Mad Catz introduced a play manipulator R.A.T. Pro at the exhibition CES-2015 in Las-Vegas. The line of gaming mice, the most advanced ones in the world, has been added by a new sample, to a great joy of avid gamers and e-new lovers. Portal of Gadgets-reviews simply couldnt pass by this event. The more so as we have already made the review of Mad Catz R.A.T. series.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          So, what is there a completely new in the model R.A.T.Pro?
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Lets begin with the fact that the manipulator was developed on the basis of the previous mouse concept of the same line 
          <strong>
           Mad Catz R.A.T.9
          </strong>
          . The developers proceeded and perfected their old idea of an extremely adaptive gadget-transformer. Now we can say for sure: fellows from Mad Catz succeeded to combine the fine balancing of the gadget and various custom options, the latter having been evaluated so high by company fans in the ninth and seventh models of the series.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          As in the case of Mad Catz R.A.T.9, the manipulator R.A.T. Pro allows to regulate ones weight and shape. The manipulator has been reinforced by a metal plate at its foundation and can be easily programmed for the favorite game of its master. The device is suitable well for shooters but a little worse for RPG and strategies. The evident pro for shooters lovers is an accurate aiming button realized by Mad Catz engineers.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          That is what the previous small rat presented to a novelty as inheritance.
          <strong>
           But upgrading is also here
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="Mad Catz R.A.T. Pro" height="350" src="/images/RAT_pro.jpg" style="display: block; margin: 4px auto 4px auto;" width="616"/>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The function of
          <strong>
           sensor hot-swappable
          </strong>
          has become a new option of the model. This function means a quick change of optic sensor for laser one or vice versa, if a certain game requires this. In addition to a sensor option, R.A.T. Pro includes 3 scroll wheels which are also easy to replace in few seconds. You know better what you need just now  that is how an essence of this novelty concept can be formulated.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          It is worth to pay a particular attention to a
          <strong>
           new option of mouse scroll:
          </strong>
          besides the usual vertical one, there appeared an option of space movement in the horizontal direction. The recent fantastic has been transformed into reality: now all play space has become the place under the command of gamers. The introduction of such upgrades into PC-periphery allows to raise games to a completely new level, and subordinate to oneself a whole play picture.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Along great working changes, the manufacturers didnt forget the problem of R.A.T. Pro design ergonomics. The weight of the device reduced while its durability increased. These new features will certainly be welcomed by both old and new users of Mad Catz other mice as well as by all those users who are eager to follow all novelties and compare different devices.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="gaming mouse Mad Catz R.A.T. Pro " height="297" src="/images/rat_design2.jpg" style="display: block; margin: 4px auto 4px auto;" width="479"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
