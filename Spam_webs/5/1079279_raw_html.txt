        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         5 Summer Up-Dos and How To Do Them {hair tutorials}
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-05-31T00:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 31, 2013
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/author/angie/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Angie
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         How to do Up-Dos for the summer are going to make your life so easy this season! These Up-Do
         <strong>
          <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/?cat=386" target="_blank" title="braid tutorials">
           braid tutorial
          </a>
          ,
         </strong>
         including
         <strong>
          How to Do a Sea Shell Flip Bun,
         </strong>
         How to do a Braided Sock Bun, How to do an Upside-down Braid Into-a-Bun, How to Make a Staircase bun/braid, and How to make a Twistie Bun, range from semi-easy to a bit difficult, and are easiest if you have someone to do them on you, but all are worth learning as they look fabulous in the hair! Every time my 13 year old daughter hooks me up and does my hair in any of these 5 Summer Up-Dos I get TONS of compliments on my hair!
        </p>
        <h2>
         Summer Up-Do Tutorial
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="upside down braid into bun" class="aligncenter wp-image-13805" height="576" src="http://sevenclowncircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/photo-89991.jpg" width="576"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         1.
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2013/05/upside-down-braid-into-bun.html" target="_blank" title="upside down braid into bun">
          How to Do an Upside-Down Braid Into a Bun {hair tutorial}
         </a>
         -How to Do an
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/?cat=386" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Upside-Down Braidinto a Bun
          </strong>
         </a>
         is my absolute favorite Up-Do style for the summer. My thirteen year old daughter started doing this style on herself this spring and then tried it on me this week and Im hooked. If you know how to
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2012/07/french-braid.html" target="_blank" title="french braid">
          french braid
         </a>
         , youll be able to do this style with ease, if not, heres a
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2012/07/french-braid.html" target="_blank">
          video tutorial
         </a>
         (you just have to do it upside down). It just takes 3 steps and you have a gorgeous up-do thats not fussy and lasts all day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt='How to Make a "Twisty" Bun {hair tutorial}' class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13807" height="640" src="http://sevenclowncircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/photo-89771.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         2.
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2013/05/twisty-bun.html" target="_blank" title="twisty bun">
          How to Make a Twisty Bun {hair tutorial}
         </a>
         How to Make a 
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/?cat=386" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Twisty Bun
          </strong>
         </a>
         is one of my favorite Up-Do styles for the summer. My thirteen year old daughter does this style for me and it never fails to draw tons of compliments.  The undo is perfect for the summer because it gets your hair up and off your neck so youll stay cool, and it also looks really pretty. I just love that this twistybun can be dressed up or down. Its super simple to do, too!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="sea shell flip bun" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13808" height="522" src="http://sevenclowncircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/photo-91661.jpg" width="480"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         3.
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2013/05/sea-shell-flip-bun.html" target="_blank" title="sea shell flip bun">
          How to Make a Sea Shell Flip Bun {hair tutorial}
         </a>
          How to Make a
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/?cat=386" target="_blank" title="Sea Shell Flip Bun">
          <strong>
           Sea Shell Flip
          </strong>
          <strong>
           Bun
          </strong>
         </a>
         is a great Up-Do styles for the summer because it looks so
         <em>
          summery
         </em>
         . The silhouette actually looks like a sea shell and of all the updos my daughter does for me, I have worn this one the most. It can easily go from casual to formal, and thats one of the things I love most about it. Heres how to make a Sea Shell Flip bun, with a video tutorial for making a
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J4c0omO6UNI" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           princess lace braid
          </strong>
         </a>
         in case you dont know how. You can also make this braid without the braid and it looks lovely.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="staircase bun/braid" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13809" height="514" src="http://sevenclowncircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/photo-90661.jpg" width="480"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         4.
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2013/05/staircase-bunbraid.html" target="_blank" title="staircase bun/braid">
          How to Make a Staircase Bun/Braid {hair tutorial}
         </a>
         How to Make a
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/?cat=386" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           StaircaseBun/Braid
          </strong>
         </a>
         is a fun Up-Do style for the summer, and I can almost guarantee you wont find anyone else with the same hair that day. This hairstyle requires a
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2012/07/dutch-braid.html" target="_blank" title="dutch braid">
          <strong>
           dutch braid
          </strong>
         </a>
         on a diagonal so its a bit difficult to do it on yourself. If you have a friend or daughter that loves to do hair, Id get their help with this one!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="braided sock bun" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13806" height="472" src="http://sevenclowncircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/photo-89881.jpg" width="423"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         5.
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/2013/05/braided-sock-bun.html" target="_blank" title="braided sock bun">
          How to Make a Braided Sock Bun {hair tutorial}
         </a>
         How to Make a
         <a href="http://sevenclowncircus.com/?cat=386" target="_blank" title="braided sock bun">
          <strong>
           Braided Sock
          </strong>
          <strong>
           Bun
          </strong>
         </a>
         is the most time consuming of my top Up-Do styles for the summer. Its easy to do, but takes a bit of time because it requires a few small braids within the bun and those will take a few minutes. The pictures dont really show how pretty the braids are within the bun. They really pop and add dimension to the hair do that is unexpected and super pretty. I just love the braided sock bun up-do!
        </p>
