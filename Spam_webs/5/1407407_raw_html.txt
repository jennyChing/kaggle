       <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <div class="pf-content">
         <p dir="ltr">
          Happy Sunday, darlings! Your week-in-review roundup of the best of the Internet has gotten a bit of a facelift this weekend, so feel free to show us off! This week, weve got everything from new photographs of Abraham Lincoln to tips on how to remove that super cute glittery nail polish. We never said we werent an eclectic bunch!
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
         </p>
         <div class="cb-divider clearfix">
          <span class="cb-title">
          </span>
         </div>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <span id="more-8141">
          </span>
         </p>
         <h4 dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           Haley:
           <a href="http://www.smithsonianmag.com/history-archaeology/Interactive_Seeking_Abraham_Lincoln_at_the_Gettysburg_Address.html#.UkRDyhFl6k4.twitter">
            New Photos of Abraham Lincoln
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_8146" style="width: 310px">
          <a href="http://www.smithsonianmag.com/history-archaeology/Interactive_Seeking_Abraham_Lincoln_at_the_Gettysburg_Address.html#.UkRDyhFl6k4.twitter">
           <img alt="Smithsonian Magazine's interactive feature on the new Lincoln photographs." class="size-medium wp-image-8146" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.literallydarling.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/lincoln.jpg?resize=300%2C179"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Smithsonian Magazines interactive feature on the new Lincoln photographs.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Researchers John Richter and Christopher Oakley believe theyve successfully identified my favorite president in three photos from the Gettysburg Address. Two photos are by famous Civil War photog Alexander Gardner, and one is by David Bacharach. Also captured in grainy film are several of Lincolns cabinet members, including his famous frenemy, William Seward. The best part about all this? They thought there were no more photos of Lincoln left to be found! What a great surprise.
         </p>
         <h4 dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           Katie:
           <a href="http://sh.com/a/vxaxv/how-to-remove-glitter-nail-polish">
            Peel-off-Glitter Nail Polish
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p dir="ltr">
          I realize trying to remove glitter nail polish may not be as earth-shattering as new Lincoln photos, but holy-acetone-nightmare Batman, its blown my mind. Beautylish gives us a rundown on how to get off that pesky (but fabulous) glitter nail polish that generally needs a jack hammer. Its as simple as making an Elmers glue basecoat that you paint the glitter on top of and then just peel it off when youre done. BRB, buying out the Butter London glitter section
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           Katie, again:
           <a href="http://hyperboleandahalf.blogspot.com/2013/10/menace.html">
            Menace: The Power of the Dinosaur Costume by Hyperbole and a Half
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Yeah, Im cheating and giving you two links, but yall,
          <em>
           Hyperbole and a Half
          </em>
          is BACK with an all new story and beloved Paint drawings. This time shes regaling us with the power of the dinosaur costume and how it imbued her with the strength to topple Asia (re: preschool). For any child who liked to be a menace (and who didnt?) youll relate so hard youll send your parents a belated apology note.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           Courtney:
           <a href="http://teeturtle.com/">
            Teeturtle.com
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          The cutest, nerdiest shirts youll ever see or need. Teeturtle has shirts for everything from Ninja Turtles to Pokemon to Harry Potter to Doctor Who, with a new design and guest artists EVERY week. Also, they now make hoodies and glow-in-the-dark shirts!
         </p>
         <h4 dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           Kate:
           <a href="http://www.collegehumor.com/article/6889842/if-congress-got-stuff-done-like-roommates">
            If Congress Got Things Done Like Roommates
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <a href="http://www.collegehumor.com/article/6889842/if-congress-got-stuff-done-like-roommates">
           <img alt="obama" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-8144" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.literallydarling.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/obama.jpg?resize=300%2C152"/>
          </a>
          Will Stephen brilliantly brings us a satirical set of Gmail exchanges between President Obama and various members of Congress, including Nancy Pelosi, John Boehner, Mitch McConnell, Harry Reid, Paul Ryan, and Ted Cruz. Vice President Biden, John McCain and Hilary Clinton make brief appearances. The premise: What if the shutdown talks between the President and Congress were handled classic college roommate-style?
         </p>
         <h4 dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           Vanessa:
           <a href="http://flavorwire.com/418116/50-trashy-movies-everyone-needs-to-see-before-they-die/50/">
            50 Trashy Movies Everyone Needs to See Before They Die
           </a>
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p dir="ltr">
          I absolutely love this list. Trashy movies are my lifeblood. Theres definitely some gems in there, including
          <em>
           Showgirls
          </em>
          ,
          <em>
           Pink Flamingos
          </em>
          , and
          <em>
           Valley of the Dolls
          </em>
          . Check it out, and get to watching!
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
         </p>
