       <p>
        Design and Display
       </p>
       <p>
        TheWiko Goais a handset designed for people who do not want a first-line smartphone, but acompact phone that fits in your pocketand allows you to access the most common tasks.This idea is reflected in its design, with a slightly curved shape in the corners and the habitual use of three touch buttons on the front to access menus, return to home screen or to move backwards.One of the few touches in its differential design is the ability to choose between a models that is entirely inblack, or you can opt for the model inwhitewhich gives a slightly more modern look.The full dimensions ofthe Wiko Goaare 119 x 62.6 x 11.3 mmand its weight is set at105 grams.It is not a thin mobile phone but given the size it is pretty easy to handle.
       </p>
       <p>
        In the display field, the company has opted for a TFTpanelwith a size of3.5 inchesand a simple resolution of480 x 320 pixels.This resolution yields a density of164 pip, a sufficient number to enjoy the simpler videos and apps but it is quite limited when it videos or applications that require more powerful graphics.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Camera
       </p>
       <p>
        It was said thatWikowould not put much into a60 euros/145 USD phone,but at least the company has maintained two cameras in this model.Specifically, at the back we have a camera with a resolution of2 megapixelsand a digital zoom of four.More interesting seems the front Camera of theGoa, with a decent resolution of1.3 megapixelswith which we can use videoconferencing applications likeSkypeandHangouts.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Memory and Power
       </p>
       <p>
        On the inside of this smartphone we have an ARM Cortex A-7 dual core 1 GHzprocessor,which is combined with a RAM of 512 MB.Just enough to power apps likeFacebook,WhatsApporYouTube, but it can easily fall short if you have several applications open at the same time.Yes, the fact that this device integrates Android KitKat 4.4can make an important difference in the field performance (we discussed this topic in the next paragraph).As for internal memory, it is has a good4 GB of storage space.If you dont plan on using it as a storage device for MP3songsand other media files then the device is capable of meeting all of your basic needs.If you run short, you can insert a Micro SD card of up to 32 GB.You can also draw on some of the online storage services running on the network such asDropboxorGoogle Drive.These options offer us free storage space that triples that of the mobile device, so it may be a good option to maximize capacity.
        <br/>
       </p>

       <p>
        Operating System and Applications
       </p>
       <p>
        Here we find one of the big news that comes with the Wiko Goa.And that is that this device series includes the latest version ofAndroid 4.4 KitKat, apopular platform fromGoogle.We say great news because this version is designed specifically for those smaller devices and can technically run the system smoothly, something that goes hand in hand with a device likeWiko.In terms of usage, one of the most exciting improvements is the ability to display thecover art for the songyoure listening on thelock screenof the phone.This mobile will allow us to enjoy a very pure experience ofAndroid, with a few additions specific to the brand.In addition, this platform opens up a portal to over a million different apps for users.Some of the most interesting ones and most used ones that we know of areFacebookandTwitterfor your social networks,Telegramor WhatsAppto chat with your friends orSummoners WarorCandy Crush Sagaif you plan on spending long hours playing games. Besides applications in the store, you also have preinstalled apps from Google.For example,YouTubeis a prime candidate for watching videos of a kittens life (cat videos are rather popular these days) or videos of your favorite artists.It also highlightsGoogle Mapsto find a particular address, orGmail, to manage our mail from the mobile device.In short, numerous options enable you to customize the phone to your liking with the applications and games that interest you.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Connectivity and Multimedia
       </p>
       <p>
        In the field of connections, the most notable news is the ability to work withtwo mobile networks through its variousdual SIM card slots.This is a convenience for those users who want to combine more than one number in one place for work use and for personal life, or even for those professionals working in various fields and who carry several devices with them.As expected, this phone does not support 4G networks but can achieve good speeds up to21 Mbps on 3G HSDPA networks.At home or via a hotspot we can also connect to the Internet via Wi-Fi.The connections withGPS are usedto navigate applications likeMapsorWazeandit also has Bluetooth 4.0.With this technology we can exchange files between compatible devices or synchronize your phone with accessories such as wireless speakers for improved audio quality.Within the field of multimedia, it is noteworthy that this is a very simple phone that is not designed to play video or for watching movies, but that may well serve as MP3 player or a good device to listen to the radio.

       </p>

       <p>
        Autonomy, price and reviews
       </p>
       <p>
        TheWiko Goaincorporates a battery with a capacity of1,300 milliamps.According to official data from Wiko, this handset should hold out for up to11.4 hours of talk time on 2G modeand up to6.2 hours in 3G mode.If we leave the mobile at rest it should hold out for a maximum of384 hours.In short, these are not very high numbers but with a more relaxed use of the device you could very well go a day without having to recharge the Goa.TheWiko Goais now available on the market for a very demure price60 euros.As stated earlier, this is a phone that is set at a competitive price and has a set of basic specifications that serve to meet the day to day tasks such as chatting or checking email and social media.In addition, the device has two attractions that increases interest, such as the presence of Android4.4 KitKatand the inclusion of adual SIM card slot.
       </p>
