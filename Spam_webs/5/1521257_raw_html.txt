            <img alt="BBQ Sauce" class="aligncenter wp-image-85580" height="788" src="http://www.moneysavingmadness.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/BBQ-Sauce-683x1024.jpg" width="525"/>

          <p>
           If you have a few minutes and some common ingredients you can make your own BBQ sauce from scratch. This recipe is delicious and incredibly easy to make. Just combine the ingredients, mix and serve!
          </p>
          <p>
           Say goodbye to bottled brands. Youll never want to eat those again. This sauce is healthier and and costs far less than buying bottled, and can be jarred in just 10 minutes.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span style="color: #808000;">
            <strong>
             Homemade BBQ Sauce Recipe
            </strong>
           </span>
          </h2>
          <p>
           1 1/2 cups packed dark brown sugar
           <br/>
           1 1/2 cups ketchup
           <br/>
           1/3 to 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
           <br/>
           1/4 cup water
           <br/>
           4 teaspoons dry mustard
           <br/>
           1 tablespoon garlic powder
           <br/>
           1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
           <br/>
           2 teaspoons paprika
           <br/>
           2 teaspoons fresh ground black pepper
           <br/>
           1/4 kosher salt
          </p>
          <p>
           Mix all the ingredients, cover and chill. You can use the sauce right away, but the flavors will come out once the sauce sits for a few hours or overnight. The sauce keeps in the refrigerator for months.
          </p>
