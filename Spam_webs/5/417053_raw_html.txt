     <div class="row-fluid article-intro lead">
      <h1 class="span12">
       How To: Choose the Perfect Top Coat
      </h1>
      <p>
       Top coats add staying power to any manineutral to nailartapaloozabut they rarely get the attention they deserve. This month, were turning the spotlight on this unsung, but totally crucial, member of your nail lineup.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="row-fluid article-content">
      <h2>
       Best in Show: Quick Dry Time
      </h2>
      <p>
       Got ants in your pants? Try a quick-dry product like
       <a class="track-event" href="http://www.birchbox.com/shop/zoya-fast-drops/" title="Zoya Fast Drops">
        Zoya Fast Drops
       </a>
       . These are formulated with silicones that help the solvents in nail polish evaporate faster, cutting down on what can seem like an endless wait time. Note: Even with quick-dry products, make sure to give your nails at least five minutes resting time before touching anything. A few minutes of patience beats redoing a smudged mani.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Best in Show: Strengthening Shine
      </h2>
      <p>
       Youre fighting an uphill battle if youre applying polish to dry, brittle, or peeling nails. Sound familiar? Try a top coat packed with proteins and natural botanicals to give your nails a little extra TLC. We swear by
       <a class="track-event" href="http://www.birchbox.com/shop/deborah-lippman-hard-rock-hydrating-hardener-base-top-coat/" title="Deborah Lippmann Hard Rock Base &amp; Top Coat">
        Deborah Lippmann Hard Rock Base &amp; Top Coat
       </a>
       , which gives nails that coveted glossy shine while fortifying with soy proteins and acoumea klaineana, a resin derived from West African trees that helps brittle and weak nails grow.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Best in Show: Matte Treatment
      </h2>
      <p>
       Matte top coats give your favorite shades a new outlook (and are hot for summer). Apply one or two coats of
       <a class="track-event" href="http://www.birchbox.com/shop/alessandro-top-coat-matte/" title="alessandro Top Coat Matte">
        alessandro Top Coat Matte
       </a>
       over any polish and watch it transform instantly. Try painting just the tips of your nails with matte top coat for a subtle French manicure, or apply it over glitter polish for subdued sparkle.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Best in Show: Chip Master
      </h2>
      <p>
       Nothing ruins a mani quicker than unsightly chips. You could spring for salon gel manis, but its just as easy to get the same long-lasting results at home.
       <a class="track-event" href="http://www.birchbox.com/shop/deborah-lippmann-gel-lab/" title="Deborah Lippmann Gel Lab">
        Deborah Lippmanns Gel Lab
       </a>
       is a two-step system that gives you a chip-proof, mirror-like finish. No drying lamp required.
      </p>
