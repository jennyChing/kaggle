       The Australian government is shocked at the verdict in the Peter Greste case. We are deeply dismayed by the fact that a sentence as been imposed. We are appalled by the severity of it, Foreign Minister Julie Bishop said after sentencing.
      </p>
      <p>
       It is hard to credit that court in this case could have reached this conclusion.
      </p>
      <p>
       The Australian government simply cannot understand it based on the evidence presented in this case.
      </p>
      <p>
       We understand there have been some very difficult times and there has been a great deal of turmoil in Egypt. But this kind of verdict does nothing for Egypts claim to be transitioning to democracy.
      </p>
