           <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
            Youll NEVER Drive This Mercedes. Youll Be Pleased When You Find Out Why..
           </h1>
           <div class="post-header">
            <div class="post-header-line-1">
             <span class="post-author vcard">
              <span class="fn">
               Fathi Abid
              </span>
             </span>
             <span class="post-timestamp">
              06:42
             </span>
            </div>
            <div style="float: right; line-height: normal;">
             <span class="st__large" displaytext="">
             </span>
             <span class="st_googleplus_large" displaytext="Google +">
             </span>
             <span class="st_facebook_large" displaytext="Facebook">
             </span>
             <span class="st_stumbleupon_large" displaytext="StumbleUpon">
             </span>
             <span class="st_twitter_large" displaytext="Tweet">
             </span>
             <span class="st_pinterest_large" displaytext="Pinterest">

           </div>
           <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6279664323197809762">
            <div style="color: #111111; font-family: 'Neue Frutiger W01', sans-serif; font-size: 17px; line-height: 23.7999992370605px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
             Mercedes has a vision of what the ultimate luxury car of the future will be like. Their newest concept, called the
             <a href="https://www.mercedes-benz.com/en/mercedes-benz/innovation/research-vehicle-f-015-luxury-in-motion/" rel="nofollow" style="background-attachment: initial; background-clip: initial; background-image: initial; background-origin: initial; background-position: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-size: initial; color: #f00a71; text-decoration: none;" target="_blank">
              F015 Luxury In Motion
             </a>
             , brings that vision to life.
            </div>
            <div style="color: #111111; font-family: 'Neue Frutiger W01', sans-serif; font-size: 17px; line-height: 23.7999992370605px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
             The F015 not only drives itself, but allows the front passengers to completely turn around to face the rear of the vehicle. The windows can be used as screens, along with 6 other touch screens in the cabin, to adjust car's settings and connect to the digital world. This creates what Mercedes calls a "digital arena." It's an arrangement that makes the interior more like a lounge area. It's a beautiful car and a novel concept, but it's more than that. It represents the beginning of a new era - a shift in how people relate to cars and personal transportation.
            </div>
            <blockquote style="color: #111111; font-family: 'Neue Frutiger W01', sans-serif; font-size: 17px; font-style: italic; line-height: 23.7999992370605px; margin: 1em 2em;">
             <div style="margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
              Anyone who focuses solely on the technology has not yet grasped how autonomous driving will change our society... The car is growing beyond its role as a mere means of transport and will ultimately become a mobile living space. -
              <strong>
               Dr. Dieter Zetsche, Daimler CEO
              </strong>
             </div>
            </blockquote>
            <div style="color: #111111; font-family: 'Neue Frutiger W01', sans-serif; font-size: 17px; line-height: 23.7999992370605px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
             So in the future, driving may not be something people think of 'doing' in general. It'll be more like an extension of their home. Although we'll have to wait until about 2030 for this car to become available to buy.
            </div>
            <div class="media-image" style="color: #111111; font-family: 'Neue Frutiger W01', sans-serif; font-size: 17px; line-height: 23.7999992370605px; margin-bottom: 25px;">
             <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
              The car is designed to be stylish and aerodynamic.
             </h3>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Wm78wCxfLHg/VL0UnhmU8tI/AAAAAAAAU7c/t8y5PE_1YFs/s1600/lifebuzz-cca75ea78a603634fac9f12e544eacbf-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="158" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Wm78wCxfLHg/VL0UnhmU8tI/AAAAAAAAU7c/t8y5PE_1YFs/s1600/lifebuzz-cca75ea78a603634fac9f12e544eacbf-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               Both the rear and front uses LED lights and has an automated voice that speaks to pedestrians.
              </h3>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-O-T2T38dgnY/VL0UmlftfaI/AAAAAAAAU7Q/wKXae-joYe8/s1600/lifebuzz-930a91110c845a4719f78cb74cdde61f-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="201" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-O-T2T38dgnY/VL0UmlftfaI/AAAAAAAAU7Q/wKXae-joYe8/s1600/lifebuzz-930a91110c845a4719f78cb74cdde61f-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               The front LED's can project a cross-walk for pedestrians, letting them know when it's safe to cross.
              </h3>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YrviP_8wXfA/VL0Umk0f8wI/AAAAAAAAU7M/g5bzOMKHaLk/s1600/lifebuzz-873d073298c676471a15069608a425d2-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="189" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YrviP_8wXfA/VL0Umk0f8wI/AAAAAAAAU7M/g5bzOMKHaLk/s1600/lifebuzz-873d073298c676471a15069608a425d2-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               It has both battery and fuel cell technology to get a range of up to 680 miles.
              </h3>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-GTdd9L0e3l0/VL0Upe6lQjI/AAAAAAAAU74/2mOGsuYxNAw/s1600/lifebuzz-ddd66d219a83a8780de4400d0ff3dfe7-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="line-height: 23.7999992370605px; margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: center;">
               <img border="0" height="201" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-GTdd9L0e3l0/VL0Upe6lQjI/AAAAAAAAU74/2mOGsuYxNAw/s1600/lifebuzz-ddd66d219a83a8780de4400d0ff3dfe7-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
              Some of the windows can be used as screens.
             </h3>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-g3uw6YUNaE4/VL0UmxHhWyI/AAAAAAAAU7U/-x64q53zJ5c/s1600/lifebuzz-c9c5c35bd8fafb02e3bed7ee841c0daa-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="160" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-g3uw6YUNaE4/VL0UmxHhWyI/AAAAAAAAU7U/-x64q53zJ5c/s1600/lifebuzz-c9c5c35bd8fafb02e3bed7ee841c0daa-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               There are 6 main screens which will be touch and gesture controlled.
              </h3>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-BanIA1NxA-A/VL0Un0T72vI/AAAAAAAAU7g/i1_9qt-TDgE/s1600/lifebuzz-cd180c59fb172ac155125f2e962be65c-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="291" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-BanIA1NxA-A/VL0Un0T72vI/AAAAAAAAU7g/i1_9qt-TDgE/s1600/lifebuzz-cd180c59fb172ac155125f2e962be65c-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               Each side panel lets you control the car's settings with a touch screen.
              </h3>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Co6Dx9V6HC4/VL0Ul8ERHRI/AAAAAAAAU7E/3R5_c7CfYy8/s1600/lifebuzz-137336a65b92a981426510a8bf063546-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="161" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Co6Dx9V6HC4/VL0Ul8ERHRI/AAAAAAAAU7E/3R5_c7CfYy8/s1600/lifebuzz-137336a65b92a981426510a8bf063546-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               Both of the front passenger seats can spin around to face the rear.
              </h3>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/--HTbnHrjolk/VL0UppyfQWI/AAAAAAAAU78/ET6ZBI-QLJk/s1600/lifebuzz-f27255d83e93de2e6fd8deafdf015a6c-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="161" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/--HTbnHrjolk/VL0UppyfQWI/AAAAAAAAU78/ET6ZBI-QLJk/s1600/lifebuzz-f27255d83e93de2e6fd8deafdf015a6c-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <br/>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
              All 4 doors open at 90 degree angles making is very easy to enter and exit.
             </h3>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zoiLuhy4kVQ/VL0Uo-k_DUI/AAAAAAAAU7w/OSK8hia_-Rg/s1600/lifebuzz-d8962cedd0e7fca849238b2e7e1fc009-limit_2000.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="178" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zoiLuhy4kVQ/VL0Uo-k_DUI/AAAAAAAAU7w/OSK8hia_-Rg/s1600/lifebuzz-d8962cedd0e7fca849238b2e7e1fc009-limit_2000.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
              <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </h3>
             </div>
             <h3 style="font-size: 20px; margin: 0px 0px 0.5em; text-align: center;">
              Looks pretty cozy, huh?
             </h3>
