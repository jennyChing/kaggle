      <h1 class="blogArtName">
       <a class="clsAltNormal" id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_hyperBlogDetails">
        <span class="pageHeading" id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_lblArticleName">
         Planning a Memorable End of Financial Year Event
        </span>
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="blogDatePosted">
       <span>
        Date Posted:
       </span>
       <span id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_lblDatePosted">
        2/03/2015 11:49:00 AM
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="blogPostedBy">
       <span>
        Posted By:
       </span>
       <span id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_lblPostedBy">
        Craig B
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="blogartlongdesc">
       <span id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_lblArticleDesc">
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="blogArticleTags">
       <span>
        Tags:
       </span>
       <a href="/articles?tag=eofy+event+catering" id="hyperTagLink_0">
        eofy event catering
       </a>
      </div>
      <div class="blogArtBody">
       <p>
        The End of Financial Year is fast approaching and so is the rush to find the perfect venue to celebrate the year.  An EOFY party is a great way to look back on the year and celebrate professional accomplishments.  Luckily, Laissez-faire can help!  We have a range of venues, ideas and concepts to help you with your search.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here are our tips on how to plan a memorable EOFY event!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Maritime Lighthouse" src="/Images/UserUploadedImages/173/maritimelighthouseLR_144.jpg" style="border: 0px solid;"/>
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Choose the right venue.
         </strong>
         Depending on your vision, we can help source a myriad of venues.  Have you considered the
         <a href="/venues/australian-national-maritime-museum/venue-information/venue-information">
          Australian National Maritime Museum
         </a>
         in Darling Harbour?  This waterfront venue offers spectacular harbour views.  Alternatively, if you are looking for a modern, city feel, have you thought about Harbour 220?  Located conveniently in the heart of the Sydney CBD, this venue is a perfect space to simply walk from work.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Start as early as possible.
         </strong>
         In terms of planning, it is always best to plan as early as possible.  It is easier for you to book your preferred date and venue.  If you dont have time to do the planning yourself, let us know.  We will happily do it for you!
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Think indoor venue!
         </strong>
         Keep the weather in mind when choosing a location for your EOFY event.  June is the coldest period on the calendar so an indoor venue is definitely advisable.  Fortunately, we have several indoor/outdoor venues we can offer you.
         <a href="/cafe/yots/cafe-information/yots">
          Yots at the Maritime Museum
         </a>
         in Darling Harbour offers an undercover balcony  if it increment weather, your guests can still enjoy the view and the fresh air without the event being spoiled by the rain.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Choose the right menu
         </strong>
         .
    Possibly the most important factor to consider is the menu.  We can
         <a href="/event-catering/services/menu">
          design the right menu to suit your event concept
         </a>
         .  Whether you are looking for a lovely canape menu or interactive food stations, we can make sure your guests leave your event full and happy.
        </li>
       </ol>
       <h2>
        Our Recommended Venue:
       </h2>
       <p>
        The Lighthouse Gallery at the
        <a href="/venues/australian-national-maritime-museum/venue-information/venue-information">
         Maritime Museum
        </a>
        is a perfect venue for your EOFY event.  Offering 13 metre high windows and an expansive ceiling, this awe inspiring venue will create the perfect environment for your guests.  Located right on the waterfront, this venue is the easy choice.
       </p>
