    <h1>
     Key Insights: Expert Views on Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and the Future of Jobs
    </h1>
    <div class="text ">
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.pewinternet.org/?attachment_id=11719">
       <img alt="Future of jobs" class="aligncenter wp-image-11719 size-full" height="300" src="http://www.pewinternet.org/files/2014/07/PI_14.08.06_FutureQuote_Webb.png" width="600"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       As part of our
      </em>
      <a href="http://www.pewinternet.org/packages/the-web-at-25/">
       <em>
        series of reports
       </em>
      </a>
      <em>
       marking the 25
       <sup>
        th
       </sup>
       anniversary of the World Wide Web, we canvassed a range of experts to assess what impact robotic advances and the use of Artificial Intelligence [AI] will have on societyand on human employment in particularby 2025. Nearly 1,900 expressed their views on two questions:
      </em>
      <em>
       1.) 
      </em>
      <em>
       To what degree will AI and robotics be parts of the ordinary landscape of the general population by 2025? Describe which parts of life will change the most as these tools advance and which parts of life will remain relatively unchanged. 2.) Will networked, automated, artificial intelligence (AI) applications and robotic devices have displaced more jobs than they have created by 2025? Here are some of the major points they made.
      </em>
     </p>
