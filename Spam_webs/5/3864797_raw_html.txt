        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Homemade Peppermint Oreos Recipe
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-18T04:00:32+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 18, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/author/jennifer" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jen
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/2014/12/homemade-peppermint-oreos-recipe.html#comments">
           11 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.yummyhealthyeasy.com%2F2014%2F12%2Fhomemade-peppermint-oreos-recipe.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.yummyhealthyeasy.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F11%2Fhomemade-peppermint-oreos-5.jpg&amp;description=Homemade%20Peppermint%20Oreos%20Recipe" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <em>
          Homemade Peppermint Oreos  easy cake mix cookies with a yummy peppermint filling! They make a simple but unique holiday treat.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/homemade-peppermint-oreos-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Homemade Peppermint Oreos - easy cake mix cookies with a yummy peppermint filling! They make a simple but unique holiday treat." class="aligncenter wp-image-6129 size-full" height="960" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/homemade-peppermint-oreos-5.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I shared this yummy recipe over at
         <a href="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/homemade-peppermint-oreos/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Your Homebased Mom
         </a>
         a few weeks ago and had to share it over here before the holidays were over. Especially since its Cookie Week here on YHE!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie-week.jpg">
          <img alt="Cookie Week on YummyHealthyEasy.com!!" class="aligncenter wp-image-6339" height="300" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie-week.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Speaking of Cookie Week, did you see yesterdays recipe for
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/2014/12/eggnog-cookies.html" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Eggnog Cookies
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         ? Or the
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/2014/12/30-healthy-cookie-recipe-roundup.html" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            30+ Healthy Cookie Recipe Roundup
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         the day before? Or what about the
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/2014/12/candy-cane-cookies.html" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            Candy Cane Cookies
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         from Day 1? They are all really great recipes that would be perfect to make for holiday gifts or just to snack on at home.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/homemade-peppermint-oreos-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Homemade Peppermint Oreos - easy cake mix cookies with a yummy peppermint filling! They make a simple but unique holiday treat." class="aligncenter wp-image-6131 size-full" height="960" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/homemade-peppermint-oreos-2.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So, Christmas. Can you even believe it?! Its absolutely crazy to me how quick it snuck up. One of my favorite things to do during the holiday season, is bake treats and give them to friends, nieghbors and teachers. In fact, I spent almost the entire day baking and decorating sugar cookies with my boys. Yeah. That was a LOT of work. Now I just need to make some of these Oreos to add to the cookie plates!
        </p>
        <p>
         I love making cookie plates full of a variety of different cookies. These homemade Oreos are unique and theyre super simple. The cookies are made from a cake mix, so yeah. It just doesnt get any simpler than that! No measuring sugar and flour, just dump the mix in and add a few simple ingredients. I love how they crinkle after theyre baked. Theyre soft, chewy and delicious.
        </p>
        <p>
         Add in a middle filling of creamy peppermint frosting, and you have yourself an easy (and totally cute) cookie sandwich thats the perfect addition to your holiday cookie plates. Your friends, neighbors and kids teachers will love you!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/homemade-peppermint-oreos-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Homemade Peppermint Oreos - easy cake mix cookies with a yummy peppermint filling! They make a simple but unique holiday treat." class="aligncenter wp-image-6132 size-full" height="960" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/homemade-peppermint-oreos-4.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 box Devil's Food Cake mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup melted coconut oil (could use butter or shortening instead if desired)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Tbsp. butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Tbsp. milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             -  tsp. peppermint extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 candy canes, crushed
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 375 F. Spray the bottom of a cookie sheet with cooking spray and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a mixing bowl, mix together cake mix, eggs, coconut oil and vanilla until combined. (If batter is too dry, add some water  teaspoon at a time).
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Roll batter into 1" balls and place on cookie sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes. They might look doughy but they will be perfect when they cool!
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine powdered sugar, butter, milk, and peppermint extract in a small bowl with a blender. Add in about  cup (1 crushed candy cane and fold into frosting.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread filling on one cookie then top with another. Option: Roll sides of frosting in extra crushed candy cane for serving. Enjoy!
           </li>
          </ol>
