             <h1>
              Message from Hertfordshire County Council:
             </h1>
             <p>
              <img alt="Herts county council logo" src="/images/default-source/logos/hcc-logo-colour-aug-07.tmb-thumbnail.jpg?sfvrsn=1" style="margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-right: 10px;" title="Herts county council logo"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              "We appreciate peoples patience while the work progresses and apologise for any inconvenience caused. Due to the unpredictable nature of the work, it is difficult to provide a definitive timescale for the re-opening of Wood Lane End. However it is not likely to be re-opened before the end of September. Please be aware that this is likely to change depending on how works progress. This information will be updated as work progresses.
             </p>
             <p>
              "
              <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
               Wood Lane End has been closed since February this year following the appearance of a sink hole in Oatridge Gardens. Hertfordshire County Council and Hightown Praetorian &amp; Churches Housing Association are working together to carry out repairs to both Oatridge Gardens and Wood Lane End as quickly as possible.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              "The sink hole has been filled with foamed concrete and ground investigations have been undertaken on the Oatridge Gardens estate and under Wood Lane End. These investigations revealed the need for ground strengthening and stabilisation in both roads.Once this is completed, all the local services will be reconnected, and both roads will be repaired and/or resurfaced."
