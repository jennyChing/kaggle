                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Zippers of Zoomerville Returns to Phoenix Theatre
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6127306389739928748" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://www.phoenixtheatre.org/flash.html">
                       <img border="0" height="66" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-TwARqWnbCkk/TZXsakrmiSI/AAAAAAAAAX0/M4eaPRwORXA/s400/Picture+4.png" width="400"/>

                        Phoenix Theatre
                       </a>
                       will bring back an updated (and shortened) musical spoof of Indy's annual fast-track  fever for a special one-month run, April 28-May 28th. Described as a "Gilbert and Sullivanesque celebration of flat-out folly and  full-throttle love,"
                       <i>
                        The Zippers of Zoomerville
                       </i>
                       ,
                      </b>
                      <b>
                       was written by Indy ad man Jack OHara
                      </b>
                      <b>
                       with musical score and direction from Hit City Recording's
                       <a href="http://timbrickley.com/">
                        Tim Brickley
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      <b>
                       .
                      </b>
