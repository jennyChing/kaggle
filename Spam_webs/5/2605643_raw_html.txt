       <script>
        &lt;!--

       <h3>
        Season Series: 2-2
       </h3>
       <p>
        When Lebron came back to Cleveland they were instantly favored to win the championship and a no-brainer to getthe #1 seed in the east. Well the #1 seed wentto the Hawks but we think this works out better for Cleveland.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://i2.wp.com/tonystake.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/BRAD_STEVENS.jpg">
         <img alt="BRAD_STEVENS" class=" size-full wp-image-1842 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tonystake.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/BRAD_STEVENS.jpg?resize=595%2C343"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        This was supposed to be another rebuilding season for the Celtics, but they dealt away Rondo early in the season to add a few young players and then at the trade deadline got Isaiah Thomas from the Suns, who currently leads the Celtics with 16.4 PPG. Coach Brad Stevens has done a great job getting a random collection of players thrown together to play well. Theyre made a great run during the second half of the season just to reach the playoffs.
       </p>
       <p>
        The season series between these two teams was a split at 2-2, but unfortunately for Celtics fans that wont be the case in this series. The Celtics just arent that good and Lebron takes care of business in the first round.
       </p>
       <h3>
        <b>
         Key Matchup: Lebron James vs J.R. Smith
        </b>
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://i0.wp.com/tonystake.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/LEBRON_JR_SMITH_2.jpg">
         <img alt="LEBRON_JR_SMITH_2" class=" wp-image-1841 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/tonystake.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/LEBRON_JR_SMITH_2.jpg?resize=595%2C372"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Will Lebron be able to manage J.R. Smith? Will J.R. shoot the Cavaliers out of a key game? LeBron James is going to have to keep J.R. Smith sane.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Series Prediction: Cavaliers in 4 games
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/f/f7/Cleveland_Cavaliers_2010.svg/1280px-Cleveland_Cavaliers_2010.svg.png?resize=407%2C215"/>
       </p>
