        <p>
         Australian artist
         <a href="http://www.elspethmclean.com">
          Elspeth McLean
         </a>
         paints
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mandala">
          mandala
         </a>
         stones in vibrant patterns of colorful dots both as an art form and as a meditative experience for herself. Her bold, beautiful use of color is explained in her
         <a href="http://www.elspethmclean.com/#!artist-statement/c11j">
          artist statement
         </a>
         :
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Painting is my way to find my happy place and colour is a way to express and celebrate the colours of my soul. By using bright and vivid colours and intricate dot work style, the artwork I create becomes a direct expression of my experience of life. I tend to focus on the more uplifting and beautiful aspects of this world because I think there is already enough darkness. This mindset helps me keep alert, in the moment and I try to see the positive aspect in any situation.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         McLean sells stones at
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/ElspethMcLean">
          her Etsy store
         </a>
         . More examples of her work can be found on
         <a href="https://instagram.com/elspethmclean/">
          her Instagram feed
         </a>
         .
        </p>
