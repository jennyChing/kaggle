        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Rat Rod Ford Truck Sunset Blanco River
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <a href="http://www.hotrodneyhotrods.com/category/hot-rod-photos/" rel="tag">

           <img alt="Rat Rod Ford Truck Sunset Blanco River print from VivaChas Hot Rod Art!" class="size-full wp-image-3500" height="533" src="http://www.hotrodneyhotrods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rat-Rod-Ford-Truck-Sunset-Blanco-River-800.jpg" width="800"/>

         <h2>
          Rat Rod Ford Truck
          <a href="http://fineartamerica.com/featured/rat-rod-ford-truck-sunset-blanco-river-chas-sinklier.html" target="_blank">
           Get Ur Print Here
          </a>
          Well Wait ~:0)
         </h2>
         <p>
          You know, Sunset is my time of day to shoot a Rat Rod Ford Truck. That and working more into the night-time. This habits likely due to getting a late start most days. And then ending up  after spending logistical time traveling and setting up  shooting in that Golden Hour around Sun-Down and on into the night. We like to call it the VivaChas Method! ~:0)
         </p>
         <h2 class="yt" id="watch-headline-title" style="color: #222222;">
          <span class="watch-title " dir="ltr" id="eow-title" title="Kevin Fowler   Beer, Bait &amp; Ammo">
           Kevin Fowler Beer, Bait &amp; Ammo
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Reckon we could have some music most of U have never heard b/4 this fine day. The Door sign on the side of the Rat Rod Ford in this VivaChas post inspired the musical choice. It readsCliffords Beer Bait and Ammo so this ditty from Kevin Fowler seemed a fun bit of redneckery. ~:0) Video Source:
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCP3CX8w1FQqWo6t77i1ww3A" target="_blank">
           Dixie First!
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/dYYpJBoBIVA?list=UUP3CX8w1FQqWo6t77i1ww3A" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Rat Rod Ford and the Circumference of the Sun
         </h2>
         <p>
          Such is the case with this outstanding example of a Rat Rod Ford Truckwe shot up on the Blanco River inthe Heartof our beloved Texas Hill-Country. This Beastie is fab to say the least! Now I dont care  much  about the engines in these things. Im interested in the sculptural lines  vehicle as work of art  you could say. Hate to see one with the hood up (bonnet to U metrix users  just multiply by the circumference of the Sun and youll understand) 
          <strong>
           BUT
          </strong>
           everyone always has a Big But dont they (tip-O-the-hat-to Pee-Wee Herman)  But this Rat Rod Ford Truck has a belt-driven  top-O-the-manifold  Full-Time  Super-Charger on some kind of Big Block-ol V-8 engine! It looks like some kind of Steam-Punk Machine or as close as Ull get w/o additional piping, pressure-valves and piles ofrust. Decided to shoot hood up on this rod b/c that viciousmaw just seemed the right thing to do. ~:0)
         </p>
         <h2>
          1946 Ford Pickup
         </h2>
         <p>
          Yeah this great old ride looks like a 1946 Ford Pickup to me. That huge chrome marque smack in the middle of the hood tells a story. It says  bad times R over and We R Back! Those of U who dont know history wont know why there was a Global Celebration bout this time of the Century. Others will get it. And thats a Good thing! Its an opportunity to Learn! U may have heard Ur Grandfather speak of such things. ~:0) Ad Source:
          <a href="http://www.oldcaradvertising.com" target="_blank">
           Old Car Advertising
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3253" style="width: 600px">
          <a href="http://www.oldcaradvertising.com" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Rat Rod Ford Truck Family Resemblance in Ford Truck Ad from Old Car Advertising" class="size-large wp-image-3253" height="800" src="http://www.hotrodneyhotrods.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/1946-Ford-Truck-Ad-01-590x800.jpg" width="590"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Rat Rod Ford Truck Family Resemblance in Ford Truck Ad from Old Car Advertising
          </p>
         </div>
         <h2>
          Bagged? Slammed?
         </h2>
         <p>
          One of my students didnt think the old rat rod Ford truck had wheels.  He thought the truck abandoned and resting flat on the ground. But  NO  its a Rat Rod, Low-Rider, Steam-Punk, Custom Ford P-U with Barbed-Wire trim. Its been bagged and slammed! Air Bags lift it up for driving  and drive it does  Like a Bat!  Then the Bagslower it down til its sitting on the deck like some kind of Big Dog resting between runs. And that is Slammed on the Ground.
         </p>
         <h2 class="yt" id="watch-headline-title" style="color: #222222;">
          <span class="watch-title " dir="ltr" id="eow-title" title="How to: Airbag suspension">
           How to: Airbag suspension
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Heres a Video from Tinman II Kustoms thatll explain Bagging Ur Truck! Stick with it. Its stuff worth knowing if Ud like to Slam Ur Pickup. Video Source:
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCH5rjl59TmLDJeLYO4re0wA" target="_blank">
           Tinman II Kustoms
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/s0JiznYdgbI?rel=0" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Rat Rod Ford Truck Sunset Blanco River
         </h2>
         <p>
          Now THAT is a TRUCK! In My Humble Opinion, more of em should look that way. And Im getting out and doing my part to promote these bewdies! Yeah I want that on me own Rat Rod Pickup. All I need is a few Thousand Dollars to make it happen.  Ha!    In the meantime the VivaChas Mobllget out there and make some more Hot Rod Artand keep the Adventures coming Ur way! Hey! Heres an idea. U might want to check the old interweb and see if theres a Car Show or a Car Club meeting out Ur way. Get out there. Meet new folks. Who knows  U might just make some new friends! Couldnt Hurt! ~:0) VivaChas!
         </p>
