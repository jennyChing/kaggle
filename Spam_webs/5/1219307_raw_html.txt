        <h1>
         The Soothing Sound of Waterfalls and Ponds in your Landscape
        </h1>
        <p>
         <img alt="Waterfalls and Ponds" class="imgR" src="/wp-content/themes/WLS/images/pg-waterfalls-1.jpg"/>
         Adding waterfalls and ponds to your landscape can help you recreate nature in your own backyard. With the peaceful sound of running water, and the tranquil singing of birds you will want to spend more time at home instead of vacationing in a far-away place.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Rediscover your own backyard
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Introduce natural and real looking water features
         </li>
         <li>
          Enhance your landscape with a pondless waterfall
         </li>
         <li>
          Be entertained by splashing birds
         </li>
         <li>
