            </div>
            <div class="zlclear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 cup Jif Creamy Peanut Butter
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 stick Crisco Baking Sticks All-Vegetable Shortening or 1/2 cup Crisco All-Vegetable Shortening
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/4 cups firmly packed brown sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 tablespoons milk
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tablespoon vanilla extract
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 large egg
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 3/4 cups flour
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 teaspoon baking soda
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 teaspoon salt
            </div>
           </span>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Directions
           </p>
           <span class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees and line cookie sheets with parchment paper (you don't have to use parchment paper but I always bake with it.)
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             2. Combine peanut butter, shortening, brown sugar, milk and vanilla in large bowl. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended. Add egg. Beat just until blended.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture at low speed. Mix just until blended.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             4. Drop by rounded dough balls (sized just smaller than a golf ball) 2-inches apart onto greased baking sheet. Flatten slightly in a crisscross pattern with a fork (see photo below).
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             5. Bake for 7 to 8 minutes, or until set and just beginning to brown. Cool 2 minutes on baking sheet. Remove cookies to cooling racks to cool completely. Enjoy plain, drizzled with chocolate or make little ice cream sandwiches out of them!
            </div>
           </span>
           <div class="ziplist-recipe-plugin" style="display: none;">
            3.1
           </div>
