         Handmade Holiday Gift Ideas for Under $10
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-11T09:00:45+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 11, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="11988835" data-link="http://stayingclosetohome.com/handmade-holiday-gift-ideas-10/" data-summary="" data-title="Handmade Holiday Gift Ideas for Under $10">

        <p>
         This post about handmade holiday gift ideas under $10 is brought to you by
         <a href="http://app.socialspark.com/disclosure_clicks?oid=13245550" rel="nofollow">
          Dollar General
         </a>
         . All opinions are 100% mine.
        </p>
        <p>
         Ok Halloween is past us now and soon we will be faced with the idea of giving. I love the holidays and being able to say thank you to different service providers, like the UPS and FedEx folks, teachers, sports coaches the list goes one. However, when you complete that list, it adds up so keeping gifts under $10 can be challenging but also necessary. I hit Dollar General for some Holiday Inspiration for Handmade Holiday Gift Ideas.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="holiday-gift-ideas-under-10" class="aligncenter wp-image-10993 size-large" height="853" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/holiday-gift-ideas-under-10-768x1024.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-11265">
         </span>
         I was shocked by the number of gift items they had for under $10.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_4165" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10939" height="800" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_4165-e1415039254646.jpg" width="600"/>
         ; However, I was looking for more DIY Holiday gift ideas as I think they can be more personal. I found these festive glass jars for under $2 and knew I could make layered cookie mix in the larger one but now to come up with a simple treat to go into the small ones.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         During the holidays we always get a lot of sweet items, how about something savory and delicious this year? Many years ago I received a tin of Ranch Oyster Crackers so I thought I would make those this year. They mare perfect snacks right out of the jar or they are flavorful on top of soups too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7707-e1415039245758.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_7707" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10940" height="800" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7707-e1415039245758.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I picked up everything I needed for these Ranch Oyster Cracker gifts at Dollar General, as well as a few other inexpensive gift ideas.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7710-e1415039235958.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_7710" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10941" height="800" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7710-e1415039235958.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         First makethe oyster crackers, which could not be easier.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7719.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_7719" class="aligncenter wp-image-10944 size-full" height="600" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7719-e1415039204197.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Allow them to cool completely before adding them to your jar.
        </p>
        <p>
         Add a cut tag, I used the card holders that were only $1. I cut the string to fit one jar. I will get 10 tags out of this.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_7720-e1415039195808" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11029" height="600" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7720-e14150391958081.jpg" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I told you this was simple, cheap and thoughtful at the same time.
        </p>
        <p>
         I picked up some of these gingerbread candles, only $2.50 to have on hand for hostess gifts etc. Arent they cute with the tags too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_7723" class="aligncenter wp-image-10946" height="1067" src="http://stayingclosetohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_7723-e1415039184169.jpg" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         SO no matter your budget you are sure to

          Find more Dollar General Holiday Inspiration here!

        <p>
         <strong>
          Ranch Flavored Oyster Crackers
         </strong>
        </p>
        <div class="content" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Museo Sans W01 300', 'Lucida Sans Unicode', 'Lucida Grande', sans-serif; line-height: 17px; font-size: 14px; vertical-align: baseline; width: 373px; float: none; -webkit-box-flex: 3; color: #444444;">
         <div class="Ingredients" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            1 1-oz. packet Hidden Valley
            <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 11px; vertical-align: baseline; position: relative; top: -0.4em;">
             
            </span>
            Original Ranch
            <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 11px; vertical-align: baseline; position: relative; top: -0.4em;">
             
            </span>
            Salad Dressing &amp; Seasoning Mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
             cup vegetable oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            <span style="font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit;">
             1/4 tsp garlic powder, (optional)
            </span>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            1 16-oz. box oyster crackers or 5 cups
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <h2 style="margin: 26px 0px 0.60714em; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Museo Sans W01 500', 'Lucida Sans Unicode', 'Lucida Grande', sans-serif; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: normal !important; line-height: 1.21429em; font-size: 1em; vertical-align: baseline; color: #336600; text-transform: uppercase;">
          DIRECTIONS
         </h2>
         <div class="directions" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
          <ul style="margin: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-left: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; list-style: none;">
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            Preheat the oven to 250F.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ol>
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            In a small bowl, add the oil, Hidden Valley
            <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 11px; vertical-align: baseline; position: relative; top: -0.4em;">
             
            </span>
            Original Ranch
            <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 11px; vertical-align: baseline; position: relative; top: -0.4em;">
             
            </span>
            Salad Dressing &amp; Seasoning Mix, and seasonings of choice Stir until mixed through.
           </li>
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            In a bowl, add the crackers and oil mixture.
           </li>
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            Seal the bag and toss until well-coated.
           </li>
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            Arrange the crackers on an ungreased half-sheet pan in a single layer.
           </li>
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            Bake the crackers for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden stirring after 10 minutes.
           </li>
           <li style="margin: 0.5em 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">
            Cool Completely
           </li>
          </ol>
         </div>
