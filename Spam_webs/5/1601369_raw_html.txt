&lt;div class="post-info"&gt;&lt;span class="date published time" title="2014-11-14T10:36:49+00:00"&gt;November 14, 2014&lt;/span&gt;  &lt;span class="author vcard"&gt;&lt;span class="fn"&gt;&lt;a href="https://letstalk.globalservices.bt.com/en/author/btletstalk/" rel="author"&gt;BT Lets Talk&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;span class="post-comments"&gt;&lt;a href="https://letstalk.globalservices.bt.com/en/2014/11/mobile-security-researching-risks-byod/#respond"&gt;&lt;span class="number"&gt;0&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;With working habits changing drastically, the number of companies who encourage their people to use mobile devices to complete work is on the increase. New research from BT Security, however, reveals that many organisations are falling behind when it comes to protecting their business from the security risks this presents.&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span id="more-14055"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Businesses have been quick to adopt Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) and Corporately-Owned, Personally Enabled (COPE) devices with 93 per cent of organisations allowing them. The technology gives companies and colleagues a far more flexible approach to their work and, with the increase of productivity involved, its easy to see why organisations are keen to book their ticket aboard the bandwagon.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;According to the new research, though, over two thirds of these organisations admit they have suffered a mobile security breach in the last year. On top of this, nearly all of them hold sensitive corporate data on these devices, which could expose information such as customer details to a higher risk of loss or leak.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Devoting attention to implementing a specific BYOD policy could be the first step to improving mobile security, but innovation will also be needed to ensure organisations can enjoy the benefits of BYOD and COPE with less of the risk.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter  wp-image-14056" src="https://letstalk.globalservices.bt.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/New-Picture-17.jpg" alt="mobile-security-research" width="592" height="443" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;As a global leader in communications and IT services, we can help businesses get the most from their mobile technology while also providing solutions to secure their network infrastructure against potential breaches. To learn more, &lt;a href="http://www.globalservices.bt.com/static/assets/pdf/solutions/security/BT-Security-research-on-mobile-security-threats-October2014.pdf" target="_blank"&gt;download the executive summary of our research into mobile security&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
