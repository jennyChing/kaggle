        <h1>
         <a href="http://www.carascravings.com/2013/01/are-you-ready-for-some-football-snacks.html" rel="bookmark">
          Are You Ready For Some Football SNACKS?
         </a>
        </h1>
       </td>
      </tr>
     </table>
     <p>
      To be honest, I might just be snackin on leftover cake come Superbowl time. Because its the same day as my baby shower! Which means Ill be 35 weeks pregnant. Which translates to, time really does fly.
     </p>
     <p>
      But should you require a little more sustenance and want to get it in a healthier way, I highly suggest you hit up your nearest newsstand for these two glossy covers:
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Supernbowl Snacks by Cara Lyons @Carascravings" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6895" height="560" src="http://www.carascravings.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/superbowl-snacks-2.jpg" width="560"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      cause Im featuring a few of my favorite healthy snacks in both!
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Clean Eating Magazine Superbowl by Cara Lyons @Carascravings" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6893" height="560" src="http://www.carascravings.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Clean-Eating-Magazine-Superbowl-by-Cara-Lyons-@Carascravings.jpg" width="560"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Lets start with
      <a href="http://www.cleaneatingmag.com/">
       Clean Eating
      </a>
      . Here, you can count on wholesome, natural ingredients coming together for simple, delicious, dude-approved fare. Ive health-i-fied your favorites like Buffalo Chicken Tenders and Meatball Sliders, and pulled the flavors of a classic steak n cheese into an unbelievably creamy, but guilt-free, Philly Cheesesteak Dip. Youll also get potato skins loaded with hearty tex-med ingredients, and grilled quesadillas stuffed with cheesy shrimp and artichokes. And whats a party without dessert? Ive got you (chocolate) covered with Peanut Butter Cookie Dough Bites.
     </p>
     <p>
      For a sneak peak, check out the bonus recipe for
      <a href="http://www.cleaneatingmag.com/Recipes/Recipe/BBQ-Chicken-Pizzas.aspx" target="_blank">
       Bite-Size BBQ Chicken Pizzas
      </a>
      over at CleanEatingMag.com.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Simply Gluten Free Superbowl by Cara's Cravings" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6894" height="560" src="http://www.carascravings.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Simply-Gluten-Free-Superbowl-by-Caras-Cravings.jpg" width="560"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      And for guaranteed gluten-free goodness, pick up
      <a href="http://simplyglutenfreemag.com/" target="_blank">
       Simply Gluten Free
      </a>
      for my Mexican Chicken Polenta Pizzas and Smoked Gouda &amp; Spinach Mac n Cheese Bites. Fun enough for a gal but just as dude-worthy too, I promise (I tested.) By the way if you havent heard, Simply Gluten Free is BRAND NEW and was the highest selling gluten and allergen-free magazine at Whole Foods in January! Things are just getting better and better, and I look forward to contributing a couple new recipes for each issue.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       When it comes to the Superbowl, are you all about the game, or the snacks? Or perhaps just the commercials? Gotta love em!
      </strong>
     </p>
