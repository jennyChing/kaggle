        Trade Mark Infographic
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="wp-image-3955 alignleft" height="3133" src="http://www.stephens-scown.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/FINAL-INFOGRAPHIC-web.jpg" title="Trade Mark Infographic" width="625"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Stephens Scowns specialist IP &amp; IT team has highlighted the importance of businesses registering trade marks in order to protect their brand and create a government endorsed monopoly that can last forever. For more information on registering trade marks and protecting your brand please email
        <a href="mailto:trade-marks@stephens-scown.co.uk">
         trade-marks@stephens-scown.co.uk
        </a>
       </p>

          Ben Travers

        <p class="upper">
         May 10, 2013
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="authdesc">
        <p>
         Ben is recognised as a leader in his field in the UK.  He heads up the firm's Intellectual 
Property and Information Technology team, advising clients on non-contentious and 
contentious IP and IT issues, helping them to identify,  protect  and exploit their IP.

Ben provides expert advice on Intellectual Property and IT law issues, files and prosecutes trade mark and design applications, as well as drafting and negotiating IP agreements and IT contracts. He provides expert advice on data protection, freedom of information, e-commerce, domain name disputes, online marketing and commercial contracts.

On the contentious side, Ben brings and defends IP infringement cases. He advises clients from a wide range of sectors including food and drink, nursery, pharmaceuticals, fashion, marine, leisure, publishing, media, sports and creative industries.

Ben is recognised in the independent guides The Legal 500 and Chambers.
