      <h1>
       Top 5 Popular World of Warcraft Flying Mount You Need Know
      </h1>
      <div class="tag-time">
       <div class="tag">
        <b>
         Tag :
        </b>
       </div>
       <em>
        01/08/2015
       </em>
      </div>
      <div class="news-main">
       <p>
        Since TBC open, World of Warcraft added all kinds of flying mount that players can fly in Outland freely. With the open of CTM, players also can fly in Azeroth. Now, many places you can use your flying mount except for a few places, meanwhile, the number of flying mount are increasing year by year, so which flying mount are widely loved by everyone?
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#ff0000">
          Pureblood Fire Hawk:
         </font>
        </strong>
        Pureblood Fire Hawk is very cool mount, not only looks beautiful but also very large looks very domineering! Of course, this is also related to its color, in contrast the pure Corrupted Fire Hawk which player do not like.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Drop: Ragnaros
         <br/>
         Zone: Firelands
        </strong>
       </p>
       <strong>
       </strong>
       <a href="http://imgur.com/ypXAUqC">
        <img src="http://i.imgur.com/ypXAUqC.jpg" title="WOW Pureblood Fire Hawk Mount"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#ff0000">
          Phoenix:
         </font>
        </strong>
        According to statistics, the vast majority of wow players will choose the Ashes of Al'ar mount first choice. Indeed, until now the phoenix still very cool and beautiful. Unique style and its color are loved by players, whats more, its tail dragging a long light that looks very beautiful.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Drop:Kael'thas Sunstrider
         <br/>
         Location:The Eye, Tempest Keep
        </strong>
       </p>
       <strong>
       </strong>
       <a href="http://imgur.com/kCJKeK4">
        <img src="http://i.imgur.com/kCJKeK4.jpg" title="WOW Phoenix Mount"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#ff0000">
          Invincible:
         </font>
        </strong>
        dropped by HLK, to be honest, the style of Invincible is not very nice, but it is a status symbol in WOW. In the past the price of Invincible is very high that everyone want to get it.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Drop:The Lich King
         <br/>
         Zone:Icecrown Citadel
        </strong>
       </p>
       <strong>
       </strong>
       <a href="http://imgur.com/PwMlxgY">
        <img src="http://i.imgur.com/PwMlxgY.jpg" title="WOW Invincible Mount"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#ff0000">
          Astral Cloud Serpent:
         </font>
        </strong>
        All the star things players will like very much. Astral Cloud Serpent mount is the first choice for players and is the most popular mount in MOP version. It is very like a long ghost when you riding the Astral Cloud Serpent.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Drop: Cache of Pure Energy
        </strong>
       </p>
       <a href="http://imgur.com/82HBBzX">
        <img src="http://i.imgur.com/82HBBzX.jpg" title="WOW Astral Cloud Serpent Mount"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#ff0000">
          Mimiron's Head:
         </font>
        </strong>
        In addition to Invincible, players the most like is Mimiron's Head, lovely style is very popular by players, and the mount move a bit cute, of course, the screw is also very interesting.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Drop:Yogg-Saron
         <br/>
         Location:Ulduar
        </strong>
       </p>
       <a href="http://imgur.com/I0zGSU1">
        <img src="http://i.imgur.com/I0zGSU1.jpg" title="WOW Mimiron's Head Mount"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <strong>
