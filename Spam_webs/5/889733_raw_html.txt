       <p>
        <strong>
         VA Scandal Just Another GOP Attack On Obama? Listen To This Black, Democratic Congressman Call Out The President
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Michael Graham
        <br/>
        The Natural Truth
        <br/>
        5/22/2014
       </p>
       <p>
        After living in Massachusetts for eight years and watching my local Democrats injure their spinal columns carrying water for the Obama administration, this video is truly refreshing.
       </p>
       <p>
        Congressman David Scott is one of two (TWO!) Georgia Democrats in Congress calling out Obama for failing to lead on the VA scandal, and demanding Secretary Shinseki be fired. I urge you to watch the video to hear what it sounds like when a politician puts partisanship aside and speaks from the heart.
       </p>
       <p>
        Rep. Scott has every excuse to stand quietly by. As a Democrat and a member of the Congressional Black Caucus, nobody would notice his silence. And hes surely not winning votes back home.
       </p>
       <p>
        But hes doing the right thing. And his example is a reminder that every day politicians put partisanship ahead of principles or the American people, they are making a choice to do so.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its great to be in a state where theres enough open-minded non-partisanship for that to happen.
       </p>
       <p>
        This would NEVER happen in Massachusetts.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch the video at
        <a href="//beforeitsnews.com/r2/?url=http://michaelgraham.com/va-scandal-just-another-gop-attack-on-obama-listen-to-this-black-democratic-congressman-call-out-the-president/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         The Natural Truth
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Related
        </strong>
        :
        <a href="//beforeitsnews.com/r2/?url=http://hotair.com/archives/2014/05/22/video-jay-rockfeller-ron-johnson-clash-over-accusation-that-racism-is-driving-opposition-to-obamacare" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Jay Rockefeller, Ron Johnson clash over charge that racism is driving opposition to ObamaCare
        </a>
        (video)
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Two noteworthy details here. One: Rockefeller couldnt be more casual in lobbing his grenade. Hes not out at a fundraiser with a drink in his hand, mindlessly babbling to some reporter with his guard down. Hes at an actual Senate hearing with a Republican senator sitting right in front of him, and yet its bombs away  and
         <a href="//beforeitsnews.com/r2/?url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/sen-jay-rockefeller-plays-the-race-card/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          not for the first time
         </a>
         . Congressional rules of decorum forbid swearing and personal insults, but if you want to charge the other side with racism for questioning Obamas pet boondoggle, fire away. Two: Unlike most instances where this accusation is made, the target was present and eager to respond. Watch the very beginning and then the last few minutes of the second clip to see Johnson hit back. I would have walked out if I were him, but admittedly, his approach is smarter
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        <a href="//beforeitsnews.com/r2/?url=http://www.breitbart.com/Big-Government/2014/05/21/Sarah-Palin-VA-Scandal-Future-of-Obamacare-if-Democrats-Not-Voted-Out" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Sarah Palin: VA Scandal Future of Obamacare if Democrats Not Voted Out
        </a>
        (video)
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Former Alaska Gov. Sarah Palin said President Barack Obamas Veterans Administration scandal involving 40 veterans who reportedly died after they were put on secret waiting lists represents Obamacares future if Democrats are not voted out of office.
        </p>
        <p>
         Friends, thats rationed care. Thats the VA today. Thats whats got to change, she
         <a href="//beforeitsnews.com/r2/?url=http://commonamericanjournal.com//https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10152442349783588&amp;set=a.10150723283643588.424640.24718773587&amp;type=1" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          wrote
         </a>
         Wednesday on Facebook. Thats why Congress has got to change because until we elect leaders wholl buck the march to socialized medicine known as Obamacare, Americas health care system will go the way of the VA.
        </p>
        <p>
         Palin had previously warned about the perils of socialized medicine and Obamacare before the law was enacted
        </p>
       </blockquote>
