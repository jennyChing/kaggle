        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span class="dropcap_blue">
          T
         </span>
         he one of a kind night that takes you on a whirlwind ride through the
         <span class="quoting2">
          History of House
         </span>
         is back with another massive and mouth watering line-up on S
         <span class="quoting2">
          aturday March 7th
         </span>
         at
         <span class="quoting2">
          Magna Science and Adventure Park
         </span>
         in
         <span class="quoting2">
          Sheffield
         </span>
         . Headlining across five arenas will be over 50 of only the most influential and storied DJs and Live PAs who have all made a massive contribution to house music ever since its birth back in the
         <span class="quoting2">
          80s
         </span>
         right up to the modern day.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Headliners include the likes of
         <span class="quoting2">
          Todd Terry, Farley Jackmaster Funk, Graeme Park, Sonny Fodera, Flashmob, Allister Whitehead, Rob Tissera, Jo Mills, Dale Castell, Doorly
         </span>
         alongside live PAs from the mighty
         <span class="quoting2">
          Julie McKnight
         </span>
         and a very rare full live band performance from
         <span class="quoting2">
          K Klass
         </span>
         and many more with tons of tops dance brands all involved like
         <span class="quoting2">
          Knee Deep, Rise, Love to Be, Rise, History, Knee Deep, Syncro
         </span>
         and many more.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <span class="quoting2">
          The History
         </span>
         brand has been doing great things all over the world for a while now. They host only huge and spectacular shows and with each and every one aim to educate, amaze and wow the crowds with DJs who were there at the beginning of house alongside artists who can identify to its modern crowd. As such they have delivered memorable events in the
         <span class="quoting2">
          UK
         </span>
         and
         <span class="quoting2">
          Middle East, Asia, Marbella, Ibiza
         </span>
         at the world famous
         <span class="quoting2">
          Ocean Beach
         </span>
         and many more places besides. Each party is a full on musical orgy with the added excitement of lots of live PAs and spectacular production which will light up every arena come March.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         With a selection of
         <span class="quoting2">
          VIP rooms
         </span>
         , bars and private stage areas as well as the five massive arenas, this huge house music showdown is sure to be one of the biggest and best parties you attend in all of
         <span class="quoting2">
          2015
         </span>
         and is sure to go down in history as one of the greatest ever Indoor events in the
         <span class="quoting2">
          UK
         </span>
         .
        </p>

function scrollToTop() {
    verticalOffset = typeof(verticalOffset) != 'undefined' ? verticalOffset : 0;
    element = $('body');
    offset = element.offset();
    offsetTop = offset.top;
    $('html, body').animate({scrollTop: offsetTop}, 500, 'linear');
}
