          <img alt="Christmas entertaining ideas for the hostess with minimal time" class="aligncenter wp-image-9576" height="780" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Christmas-entertaining-ideas-for-the-hostess-with-minimal-time-650x975.jpg" width="520"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Christmas-entertaining-ideas-for-the-hostess-with-minimal-time-650x975.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </center>
        The Schuplins have big plans for Christmas dinner this year! Yippee! This is the first year we are implementing our own holiday traditions in an effort to reduce our holiday stress. Gone are the days of traveling to 3 parties on Christmas day. Im over it.
        <p>
         I know some people truly enjoy party jumping, but thats way too much work with a toddler. Not to mention, I will be 34 weeks pregnant with serious cankle action, tiny bladder syndrome, and dont test my patience sleep deprivation goin on! Nobody wants in on that unless they helped put me in this joyous position.
         <em>
          *cough*
         </em>
         Michael
         <em>
          *cough*
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         So how bout I show you how Im making Christmas just as special for Michaels parents without the stress that once came with our Christmas?
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <em>
           You guessed it, Im using my secret weapon AKA
           <a href="http://www.honeybakedfoods.com/products" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            HoneyBaked Ham
           </a>
           again! They might have sponsored this post but the ideas for Christmas dinner are all mine because I developed this plan last year.
          </em>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Setting the table for a simple Christmas dinner" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9579" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Setting-the-table-for-a-simple-Christmas-dinner--650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Setting-the-table-for-a-simple-Christmas-dinner--650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </center>
        There is something about layering that really gets my hormones going so I dressed my table in traditional colors.
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <em>
           Note: Everything featured in myplace setting waspicked up during last seasons Christmas clearance.
          </em>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Easy to make cheese ball recipe for Christmas entertaining" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9578" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Easy-to-make-cheese-ball-recipe-for-Christmas-entertaining-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Easy-to-make-cheese-ball-recipe-for-Christmas-entertaining-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </center>
        I accented the ham, side dishes, and cake I picked up from the
        <a href="http://www.honeybakedfoods.com/?zip=48507&amp;zipcode=48507&amp;txtzip=48507&amp;hbhlp_id=700" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Flint, MI location
        </a>
        with two simple appetizers in festive serving dishes.
        <h4>
         Easy Cheese Ball Recipe:
        </h4>
        <ul>
         <li>
          16 ounces of cream cheese
         </li>
         <li>
          1.5 c shredded cheddar cheese
         </li>
         <li>
          .5-1 tsp garlic powder
         </li>
         <li>
          Optional- green onion
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Melt the cream cheese, mix in the extras, and put it in the fridge until firm.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Vegetables and dip served with Christmas dinner" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9582" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Vegetables-and-dip-served-with-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Vegetables-and-dip-served-with-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </center>
        See? There was little to no work involved with the appetizers and yet it still looks all fancy.
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Tips for removing the hard work from Christmas dinner" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9581" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Tips-for-removing-the-hard-work-from-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Tips-for-removing-the-hard-work-from-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </center>
        Okay, so you obviously know HoneyBaked Ham carries ham and turkey, right? Remember I served ham and turkey at my
        <a href="http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/11/thanksgiving-party-ideas/" target="_blank" title="Find all of my Thanksgiving ideas here.">
         Thanksgiving party
        </a>
        ? Did you know they also carry side dishes and desserts? Like, dont take your man child with you unless you are willing to forfeit the dessert decision making process. I didnt even get a say and I was cravingme some
        <a href="http://www.honeybakedfoods.com/products/desserts/cinnamon-walnut-coffee-cake" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         coffee cake
        </a>
        .
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Christmas dinner essentials for the hostess who want to enjoy the holiday" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9575" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Christmas-dinner-essentials-for-the-hostess-who-want-to-enjoy-the-holiday-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Christmas-dinner-essentials-for-the-hostess-who-want-to-enjoy-the-holiday-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>

        So I made up for it by picking out the side dishes I wanted.Life is one big compromise, isnt it?
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Delicious carrot cake for Christmas dinner" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9577" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Delicious-carrot-cake-for-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Delicious-carrot-cake-for-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </center>
        I personally prefer to keep centerpieces low but the little tree I purchased for Michael last year made the table so festive! I couldnt take it down once I tested it out.
        <p>
         Oh, and you NEED a lazy Susan table if you enjoy entertaining! For real.My table is beautiful and we dont have to play pass the potatoes. They must have been invented by a woman. Thats the only logical explanation.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Festive drink station for a low maintenance Christmas dinner" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9589" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Festive-drink-station-for-a-low-maintenance-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Festive-drink-station-for-a-low-maintenance-Christmas-dinner-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         And drinks? Whats a party without some sort of beverage?
        </p>
        <p>
         The drinks for our Christmas dinner matchthe food in the easy on the host with high impact department. The reindeer waters are festive, and Im able to serve something less snazzy to the grown ups because of my cute Christmas glasses.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Tips for hosting a beautiful Christmas dinner that allows you to enjoy the day with your friends and family" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9580" height="433" src="http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Tips-for-hosting-a-beautiful-Christmas-dinner-that-allows-you-to-enjoy-the-day-with-your-friends-and-family-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/&amp;media=http://horriblehousewife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Tips-for-hosting-a-beautiful-Christmas-dinner-that-allows-you-to-enjoy-the-day-with-your-friends-and-family-650x433.jpg&amp;description=Easy Christmas Dinner Entertaining | http://horriblehousewife.com/2014/12/christmas-dinner-entertaining/')">
          </span>

And if you are anything like me then this reminder will serve you well- the most important thing for any Christmas dinner celebration is to simply be present. And possibly taking a shower. People seem to appreciatewhen you shower.
