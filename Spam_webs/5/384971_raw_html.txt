       <h2>
        Working groups
       </h2>
       <p>
        Open Video Conference 2011 is structured around productive working groups. Every session will be working towards building tangible outputs, from policy papers to software and everything in between. All of the rooms are equipped with stable WiFi, projectors, markers and whiteboards. There will be no passive watchers here, just active participants.
       </p>
