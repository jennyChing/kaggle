       <h2>
        Canucks &amp; Flames fans engaged in large arena brawl after game 2
       </h2>
       <cite>
        BarDown Staff (
        <a href="https://www.twitter.com/BarDown" onclick="window.open(this.href, 'twitter'); return false;">

        We're only one week into the NHL playoffs and the two Canadian-on-Canadian match-ups have already turned into incredibly fierce affairs. 

Friday, the contentious relationship between the Calgary Flames and Vancouver Canucks turned into a violent off-ice altercation between fans after Game 2 of the first round series. The first video doesn't make the fight seem all that rough, but in the second clip, the post-scrum injuries on display tell another story.

        It's not quite clear what sparked the concourse brawl, but considering how Game 2 ended between the Canucks and Flames, we're sure emotions may have been running quite high amongst the fans on both sides. A user on
        <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/sports/comments/33388g/how_canucks_fans_celebrate_a_victory/" target="_blank">
         Reddit
        </a>
        shared a picture of the aftermath of the fight and the results weren't too pretty for one Flames fan.
        <img src="http://i.imgur.com/AGyIXh5.jpg" width="620"/>
        Here's to hoping that the pushing and other forms of physical play stays on the ice for the remainder of the Canucks versus Flames series.
