             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               Parents, when your precious angel rips you from your three hours of sleep to demand food that he won't actually eat, you'll want this f'ing book.
              </p>
              <p>
               From the author of the wildly successful "children's book"
               <em>
                Go The Fuck to Sleep
               </em>
               comes a sequel that tackles an age-old battle between parent and child:
               <em>
                You Have to Fucking Eat
               </em>
               .
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/08/27/books-with-relatable-college-characters/">
                 13 Books Every College Kid Can Relate To
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <figure class="image">
               <p>
                <img alt="YouHavetoFuckingEat" class="fullwidth" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="Akashic Books " data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!7e58" data-image="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE0LzA5LzI0L2Y4L1lvdUhhdmV0b0Z1LjVhNDc4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/451e9ede/264/YouHavetoFuckingEat.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="1224" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE0LzA5LzI0L2Y4L1lvdUhhdmV0b0Z1LjVhNDc4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/451e9ede/264/YouHavetoFuckingEat.jpg" title="YouHavetoFuckingEat"/>
               </p>
               <div class="image-credit">
                <p>
                 Image: Akashic Books
                </p>
               </div>
              </figure>
              <p>
               Author Adam Mansbach digs deep into the struggles of reasoning with a small child who refuses to perform even the most basic life skills, prompting the question, "Why the fuck won't you eat?"
              </p>
              <figure class="image">
               <p>
                <img alt="you have to fucking eat 1" class="fullwidth" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="Akashic Books" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!41f3" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE0LzA5LzI0LzhmL3lvdWhhdmV0b2Z1LmJmMmQwLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/3724d777/6c1/you-have-to-fucking-eat-1.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="1200" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE0LzA5LzI0LzhmL3lvdWhhdmV0b2Z1LmJmMmQwLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/3724d777/6c1/you-have-to-fucking-eat-1.jpg" title="you have to fucking eat 1"/>
               </p>
               <div class="image-credit">
                <p>
                 Image: Akashic Books
                </p>
               </div>
              </figure>
              <p>
               As for how Mansbach came up with the sequel's topic, he told
               <em>
                Mashable
               </em>
               : "Because it's such a fundamental thing  one of life's great joys, in fact  there's a particular frustration that comes with not being able to get your kid to eat. Trying to get a kid to put on her shoes might be crazy-making, but it's not quite the same as having her reject the basic concept of sustenance. It seemed to me like a universal experience, and one that parents would welcome the chance to commune over and laugh about."
              </p>
              <p>
               The 2011 picture book
               <em>
                Go The Fuck to Sleep
               </em>
               is a number one
               <em>
                New York Times
               </em>
               Bestseller. Though Ricardo Corts illustrated the original, the upcoming sequel is illustrated by Owen Brozman, whose work has appeared in
               <em>
                National Geographic
               </em>
               ,
               <em>
                Time Out New York
               </em>
               and Scholastic publications.
              </p>
              <p>
               While
               <em>
                You Have to Fucking Eat
               </em>
               hits shelves Nov. 12, word's still out on whether Samuel L. Jackson will be recording the audiobook.
              </p>

               </iframe>
              </p>
