          <p>
           The
           <b>
            Artificial Hive
           </b>
           is a block from
           <a href="/Ex_Nihilo" title="Ex Nihilo">
            Ex Nihilo
           </a>
           .  It is used to obtain the
           <a href="/Hive_Bee" title="Hive Bee">
            Hive Bees
           </a>
           from
           <a href="/Forestry" title="Forestry">
            Forestry
           </a>
           ,
           <a class="mw-redirect" href="/Extra_Bees" title="Extra Bees">
            Extra Bees
           </a>
           and
           <a href="/Magic_Bees" title="Magic Bees">
            Magic Bees
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span class="mw-headline" id="Recipe">
            Recipe
           </span>
           <span class="mw-editsection">
            <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
             [
            </span>
            <a href="/index.php?title=Scented_Artificial_Hive&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Recipe">
             edit
            </a>
            <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
             ]
            </span>
           </span>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Place an
           <a href="/Artificial_Hive" title="Artificial Hive">
            Artificial Hive
           </a>
           into a
           <a class="new" href="/index.php?title=Barrel_(Ex_Nihilo)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Barrel (Ex Nihilo) (page does not exist)">
            Barrel
           </a>
           filled with
           <a href="/Seed_Oil" title="Seed Oil">
            Seed Oil
           </a>
           .  This will turn it into a Scented 
Artificial Hive.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span class="mw-headline" id="Usage">
            Usage
           </span>
           <span class="mw-editsection">
            <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
             [
            </span>
            <a href="/index.php?title=Scented_Artificial_Hive&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Usage">
             edit
            </a>
            <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
             ]
            </span>
           </span>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Place the Scented Hive in the world.  It will eventually turn in to a bee hive, or it will revert back to an Artificial Hive.
          </p>
          <p>
           Bees are particular about where they live.  The Scented Hive must meet certain conditions to attract a bee.  These vary based on the species desired.
          </p>
