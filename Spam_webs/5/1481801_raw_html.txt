         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: xx-large">
          My parents separated
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.letsleepingblogslie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Depositphotos_1080788_xs.jpg">
          <img align="right" alt="Family" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.letsleepingblogslie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Depositphotos_1080788_xs_thumb.jpg?resize=468%2C312" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; float: right; border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Family"/>
         </a>
         My parents separated when I was 21-years-old. I was overseas when it finally happened  so being absent was good for me. So many parents are doing it now when kids are very young.
        </p>
        <p>
         Recently, I have had late night texts with my ex-husband. He wants me to have our daughter on a weekend night that is scheduled as his. He is demanding it. I have said no many times. I feel that his irrational thinking compromises him as a responsible adult.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          How much do separated parents tell their kids?
         </span>
         I think you have to be very careful. Remember who is the child and who is the adult. Children process things very differently compared to adults.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          What do separated parents want their kids to think?
         </span>
         That the parents are in control and that they are sorting things out to benefit everyone.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          What dont separated parents want their kids to think?
         </span>
         That one parent hates the other.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          What dont separated parents say?
         </span>
         Bad things about the other parent. Do this with friends!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          What separated parents hate?
         </span>
         Constant petty conflict with the other parent.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          Why is money often a big issue?
         </span>
         Because money is power.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff0000">
          The most important person?
         </span>
         The kids.
        </p>
