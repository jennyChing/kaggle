              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fblissfullydomestic.com%2Fhome-bliss%2Fmake-a-wire-cloche%2F11853%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fblissfullydomestic.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2008%2F10%2FSupplies-Needed-to-Buy-a-Wire-Cloche.jpg&amp;description=Supplies+Needed+to+Buy+a+Wire+Cloche">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          For this project youll need:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           chicken wire 12 in height
          </li>
          <li>
           goggles
          </li>
          <li>
           gloves
          </li>
          <li>
           wire snippers
          </li>
          <li>
           needle nose pliers
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I found a whole roll of chicken wire at a yard sale for just 25 cents.. amazing what you find at yard salesanyway.you need a piece approx. 22 long.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-140750">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-bottom-left">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <img alt="Binding the wire to form the cloche" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-140750" height="450" src="http://blissfullydomestic.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/Binding-the-wire-to-form-the-country-kitchen.jpg" width="600"/>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fblissfullydomestic.com%2Fhome-bliss%2Fmake-a-wire-cloche%2F11853%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fblissfullydomestic.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2008%2F10%2FBinding-the-wire-to-form-the-country-kitchen.jpg&amp;description=Binding+the+wire+to+form+the+cloche">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          When you cut the wire youll end up with these
          <strong>
           ends that you just wrap around the other end to join. Start by making a tube shape &amp; then work on shaping the top, which can be tricky. Some of the wire will have to be cut away to get that shape.
          </strong>
          Just keep working it &amp; crimping the wires so none are sticking out &amp; soon youll have a wire cloche. Itll end up
          <em>
           <span>
            <strong>
             <span>
              perfectly imperfect!
              <br/>
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Add a knob with a washer to hold it in place, spray paint if you like and thereyou have it.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-140751">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-bottom-left">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <img alt="Wire Cloche for a Country Kitchen Decor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-140751" height="733" src="http://blissfullydomestic.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/Wire-Cloche-for-a-Country-Kitchen-Decor.jpg" width="550"/>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fblissfullydomestic.com%2Fhome-bliss%2Fmake-a-wire-cloche%2F11853%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fblissfullydomestic.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2008%2F10%2FWire-Cloche-for-a-Country-Kitchen-Decor.jpg&amp;description=Wire+Cloche+for+a+Country+Kitchen+Decor">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Perfect for a seasonal display of mini pumpkins, ornaments, a special cupcake for your valentineor just about anything.
         </p>
