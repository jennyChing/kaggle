            <p>
             <span style="font-family:arial;font-size:18px;">
              <a href="/cfs-file.ashx/__key/communityserver-components-userfiles/00-00-00-21-03-attached+files/winner-2014.jpg">

               Giorgio Tuscani
              </a>
              !
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-family:arial;font-size:18px;">

              <em>
               <a href="/members/giorgiotuscani/?mid=17086" target="_blank">
                The Immortal Guardians of the Mortal Soul
               </a>
              </em>
              , was selected as our StudioVox company holiday card and sent out to all our StudioVox friends and business associates.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-family:arial;font-size:18px;">
              It's a stunning holiday image. We felt Giorgio's piece captured a joyous holiday spirit of love and respect for all people and hope for a glorious New Year!
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-family:arial;font-size:18px;">
              We'd like to thank Giorgio for his dedication to creativity and for allowing us to use his artwork to share our holiday message to the world. Happy Holiday's everyone!
