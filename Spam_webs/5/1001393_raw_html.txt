          <h2>
           Taig Khris' 40-Foot Eiffel Tower Skate Jump Sets World Record
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="references">
          <span class="mobileAuthor">
           By: Allison Love-
          </span>
          Published:
          <span content="2010-06-02" itemprop="datePublished">
           Jun 2, 2010
          </span>
           References:
          <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ArjfHI4ODC0&amp;feature=player_embedded" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           youtube
          </a>
          &amp;
          <a href="http://theawesomer.com/eiffel-towel-skate-jump/42597/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           theawesomer
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="articleText">
         Taig Khris' 40-foot Eiffel Tower skate jump actually looks like the most terrifying thing in the world. The man actually jumps, on roller blades no less, from 40 feet in the air onto a wooden ramp. The Eiffel Tower serves as the location for this world recording breaking roller blade stunt, but that is hardly where your attention lies.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Just watching the video actually makes me scared. The 40-foot eiffel tower skate jump would be hard to beat, seeing as he is brave enough to fly into the air without any sort of bungee cord, onto solid wood. The guy deserves a medal!
