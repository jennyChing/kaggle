&lt;/div&gt;					&lt;h1&gt;Wall You Need Is Love&lt;/h1&gt;
					&lt;span class="post-date"&gt;January 04, 2010&lt;/span&gt;
						&lt;div class="postcontenter"&gt;
							
														&lt;p&gt;Murals are making a comeback. &lt;a href="http://www.jameshotels.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.jameshotels.com']);" target="_blank"&gt;The James&lt;/a&gt;, for instance, an ber chic boutique hotel in downtown Chicago, is decked out in bold blown-up photographs: huge birch trees en route to the elevators from the lobby, abstracted textures at each floor level. In the minimal setting, they create a big impact. &lt;a href="http://www.wallyouneedislove.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.wallyouneedislove.com']);" target="_blank"&gt;Wall You Need is Love&lt;/a&gt; lets you create custom walls just like those at The James with their Wallpaper  La Carte: &lt;span id="more-17866"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;any image, any size, any scale&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="image-left"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Wall You Need is Love.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;It&#8217;s a new year - and a great time for new dcor. In the scheme of things, changing your wall-coverings is a fairly minor endeavor. Satisfy your need for nature with a life-size forest or your desire to see the world through a citiscape of Paris or landscape of Santorini. For a different look, try blowing up an image or pattern for an abstract aesthetic. You can use a photo from your own collection or choose one from the company&#8217;s extensive portfolio. Home dcor is an expression of our personality, something unique, says Patrick Pepin, founder of Wall You Need is Love.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;For residences and businesses alike, it offers the opportunity for creativity and self expression.  If it seems a bit intimidating, Wall You Need is Love works with clients individually to help with the decision-making process. Usual production time is only two weeks - allowing for early instant gratification (always a perk).&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/wall-you-need-is-love-large.jpg" alt="wall-you-need-is-love-large" title="wall-you-need-is-love-large" width="546" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-17947" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/wall-you-need-is-love-large1.jpg" alt="wall-you-need-is-love-large1" title="wall-you-need-is-love-large1" width="546" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-17948" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/wall-you-need-is-love-large2.jpg" alt="wall-you-need-is-love-large2" title="wall-you-need-is-love-large2" width="546" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-17949" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/wall-you-need-is-love-large3.jpg" alt="wall-you-need-is-love-large3" title="wall-you-need-is-love-large3" width="546" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-17950" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/wall-you-need-is-love-large4.jpg" alt="wall-you-need-is-love-large4" title="wall-you-need-is-love-large4" width="546" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-17951" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Beyond the custom wallpaper, the company also offers an Artist Canvas, using a process called Gicle on Canvas. The image is printed on an artist canvas, hand varnished and mounted on a wood stretcher - with the image wrapped around the frame eliminating the need for framing. So if the concept of covering entire walls seems daunting - or too overdone for your setting - the Artist Canvas offers an alternative. Regardless, the options are pretty much endless.&lt;/p&gt;
