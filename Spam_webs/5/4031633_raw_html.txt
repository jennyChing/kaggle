         <p>
          <strong>
           What is the best wrestling diet for weight loss?
          </strong>
          A lot of wrestlers will lose weight to get down to a lower weight class.
         </p>
         <p>
          I would like to talk about a proper way to
          <strong>
           lose weight for wrestling
          </strong>
          . This article is not about starving yourself, dehydrating yourself, or
          <a href="http://wrestleclub.com/cutting-weight-for-youth-wrestling-how-young-is-too-young/" title="Cutting Weight for Youth Wrestling  How Young is too Young?">
           what age to cut weight
          </a>
          . Crash diets will only make wrestlersweak when it comes time to compete.
         </p>
         <p>
          Wresting nutrition is an important part of a wrestlers diet plan. If youwillfollow a good weight loss plan, you will bethe best wrestler possible and still keep all of your strength.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Wrestling Diet - Best Wrestling Weight Loss Diet Plan|wrestling nutrition weight loss tips" class=" alignnone wp-image-762 size-medium" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/wrestleclub.com/wp-content/uploads/best-wrestling-weight-loss-diet.jpg?resize=300%2C224" title="Wrestling Diet - Best Wrestling Weight Loss Diet Plan for wrestlers"/>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Start Wrestling Diet Early for Best Results
         </h2>
         <p>
          Start your
          <em>
           <strong>
            wrestling weight loss plan
           </strong>
          </em>
          early. Dont wait till a week before its time to compete or rank for a match. A gradual weight loss is the
          <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20544583" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20544583', 'best wrestlers diet']);" target="_blank" title="best wrestlers diet">
           best wrestlers diet
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          A lot of wrestlers will wait to lose weight. You are hurting yourself and not achieving your full potential. This only leads to you being an average wrestler. Make healthy eatingpart of your
          <a href="http://wrestleclub.com/wrestlers-parents-develop-growth-plan/" title="Wrestling Parents Develop a Growth Plan for Your Wrestler">
           goals
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          If you are going to
          <strong>
           lose weight for wrestling
          </strong>
          , you need to start eating right at least a month in advance. Develop your weight loss plan early so you dont have to starve yourself. This will allow you to lose a small amount of weight each week.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Proper Nutrition is Important
         </h2>
         <p>
          Getting the right calories is an important part of a good
          <a href="http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/diets.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/diets.html', 'wrestling diet']);" target="_blank" title="lean meat is an important part of wrestling diet for wrestlers">
           <strong>
            wrestling
           </strong>
           <strong>
            diet
           </strong>
          </a>
          . Stay away from calorie dense foods, such as pancakes and french fries.
         </p>
         <p>
          Instead focus on nutrient dense foods like whole grains, fruits, vegetables and lean proteins.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Wrestlers need to Drink Lots of Water to Lose Weight
         </h2>
         <p>
          A lot of wrestlers learn that the best way to lose weight is to sweat it off. This is the WRONG way!
         </p>
         <p>
          You need water to support your metabolism and keep your body healthy. I recommend that you drink more water than normal. This will help you feel full, stay healthy and your body will burn more calories from the extra water.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you need to lose some water weight, you can restrict your water intake 24 hours before an event to make weight. Did you notice I didnt say stop drinking water completely? You still need water for your body to work properly.
         </p>
         <p>
          I suggest a couple of ounces of water every three hours. You should only restrict waterto drop less than two pounds.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Wrestling Weight Loss does not Include Soda Pop
         </h2>
         <p>
          Soda pop is not part of a wrestlers diet. The calories in a soda are empty calories. I have seen wrestlers that did nothing different, but stopped drinking pop and they would lose five pounds in a week. Seriously wrestlers stay away from the soda and juice!
         </p>
         <h2>
          Focus on the Right Nutrients in Proper Proportions
         </h2>
         <p>
          Here is where your
          <em>
           wrestling diet
          </em>
          should focus on the right nutrients and proportions. You need to eat the right things to promote energy and efficient weight loss.
         </p>
         <p>
          A wrestler should focus on consuming
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.webmd.com/diet/superfoods-everyone-needs" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.webmd.com/diet/superfoods-everyone-needs', 'super foods']);">
            super foods
           </a>
          </span>
          . Super foods are nutrient dense foods that promote a healthy metabolism and have anti-oxidant properties. Here is a typical list of foods my son and I will eat to lose weight.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Whole grain pasta and cereals(avoid bread)
          </li>
          <li>
           2% cheese sticks
          </li>
          <li>
           Skim Milk(Borland has a great skim milk)
          </li>
          <li>
           Low calorie yogurt
          </li>
          <li>
           Walnuts and almonds(not a lot because they are high in calories)
          </li>
          <li>
           Chicken breast, lean turkey meat, and a high quality why protein
          </li>
          <li>
           Fruits  Apples, bananas, pears, oranges, etc  Fruitprovides the necessaryvitamins, fiber, and lot of water.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h2>
          Reduce Calorie Intake to Proper Level
         </h2>
         <p>
          Reducing your calorie intake isimportant to a wrestlers diet plan. Your body requires a the right calories to drop weight and keep your strength. If you cut your calorie intake too much, your body will go into starvation mode and your metabolism will actually
          <strong>
           slow down and you will stop losing weight
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          The trick is to cut your calorie intake enough so that your body will use your fat reserves and keep your metabolism firing at top capacity. I recommend that you only drop your calorie intake to 80% of your daily requirements.
         </p>
         <p>
          For example if you need 2,000 calories a day, then your reduced calorie intake is around 1,600 calories.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you want an idea on what your calorie requirements are, you can see them
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/educational/wecan/downloads/calreqtips.pdf" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','download','http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/educational/wecan/downloads/calreqtips.pdf']);">
            here
           </a>
          </span>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          Monitor Food Intake or Workout More?
         </h2>
         <p>
          In my opinion, it is better to watch your food intake. There are 3,600 calories in a pound.
         </p>
         <p>
          Do you realize how hard it is to burn 3,600 calories? Let me give you an example: If you run 5 miles, you will burn about 1,000 fat calories. You are already practicingfive days a week. Dont make things harder on yourself, by consuming more than you need.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Keep Your Eye on the Prize
         </h2>
         <p>
          A good weight loss plan is a long-term plan. You should focus on eating right and changing your lifestyle.
         </p>
         <p>
          I know you want that big T-bonesteak or that greasy burger and fries. Keep your eyes focused on the prize. That prize is having the best wrestling season possible.
         </p>
