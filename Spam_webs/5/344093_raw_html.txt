      <h2 class="posttitle">
       18 Rules for Being a Kid
      </h2>
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <p>
       <em>
        Disclosure: This is a sponsored and compensated post from NickMom via my relationship with Quality Blue Community. All thoughts and opinions are my own. #ad #PopsicleRules
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost8.jpg">
        <img alt="18 Rules for Being a Kid #PopsicleRules #ad" class="aligncenter wp-image-23149 size-full" height="581" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost8.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       This week I am teaming up with #PopsicleRules to share my 18 Rules for Being a Kid!
      </p>
      <p>
       Lets face it most of us as kids at one point or another wished we were grown ups because in our minds they had it easy, right? I remember thinking that grown ups only had to go to work all day they didnt have to do anything difficult like homework. Then, I became a grown up and I was horribly mistaken! Being a grown up is tough business, being a grown up with kids is even more tough business as suddenly you are responsible for little people!
      </p>
      <p>
       The thing is though deep down we all hope that our kids enjoy their childhood and do not take it for granted, because now that we are grown ups we know that childhood is fleeting. In just a few short years, its gone. Life hits you, suddenly you are responsible for yourself along with others too!
      </p>
      <p>
       With that, Ive come up with 18 Fun Rules for Being a Kid that I hope both of my kids do throughout their childhood!
      </p>
      <h3>
       18 Rules for Being a Kid
      </h3>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost2.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost2" class="wp-image-23145 aligncenter" height="483" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost2-600x800.jpg" width="362"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       1. Splash in the Puddles.
       <br/>
       2. Color Outside the Lines.
       <br/>
       3. Eat a Boogerat least once. Admit ityou have.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost12.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost12" class="size-full wp-image-23153 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost12.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       4. Giggle and Laugh at Tootsone day youll be trying to hide it.
       <br/>
       5. Try to catch the bubbles then eat them until you realize its soap.
       <br/>
       6. Roll around in the mud.
       <br/>
       7. Wish on Stars.
       <br/>
       8. Chase Lightening Bugs and catch a few.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost15.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost15" class="aligncenter wp-image-23151" height="750" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost15-600x900.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       9. Let your imagination take you on wild and crazy adventures.
       <br/>
       10. Wear clothes that dont match and two different pairs of shoes. Of course, if you are my son you may put on a couple extra pair of undies too just because you want to.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost22.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost22" class="size-full wp-image-23154 aligncenter" height="399" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost22.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       11. Play in the dirt as much as possible.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost1.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost1" class="size-full wp-image-23144 aligncenter" height="669" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost1.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       12. Build a snowman and be proud of it.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost5.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost5" class="size-full wp-image-23148 aligncenter" height="589" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost5.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       13. Cry because you are cute doing it.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost3.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost3" class="size-full wp-image-23146 aligncenter" height="500" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost3.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       14. Wear masks around the grocery store and see who notices.
       <br/>
       15. Pitch a tent in the living room.
       <br/>
       16. Be any kind of superhero you want to be.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost4.jpg">
        <img alt="kidpost4" class=" wp-image-23147 aligncenter" height="623" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/kidpost4-600x757.jpg" width="494"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       17. Dream that you can be anything you want to be.
       <br/>
       18. Enjoy every ounce of your childhood, because soon youll be watching your kids enjoy theirs.
      </p>
      <p>
       Share with us! What are your rules for being a kid? Be sure to share your RULES on Social Media with the hastag #PopsicleRules to win great prizes!
      </p>
      <p>
       Enjoy those moments this weekend with the kids!
      </p>
