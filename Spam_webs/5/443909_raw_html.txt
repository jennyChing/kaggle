     <strong class="b-article__lead" itemprop="description">
      Pro-independence fighters in eastern Ukraine say they have intercepted conversations between international mercenaries in Debaltseve speaking in four different languages  English, Polish, French and some Flemish.
     </strong>
     <div class="b-article__videoclub_embed">
     </div>
     <div class="b-article__text" itemprop="articleBody">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="b-inject m-inject-min">
       <div class="b-inject__media">
        <img alt="Burnt military machinery in Uglegorsk" height="375" src="http://cdn2.img.sputniknews.com/images/101796/29/1017962923.jpg" title="Burnt military machinery in Uglegorsk" width="705"/>
       </div>
       <div class="b-inject__content">
        <a href="/europe/20150208/1017963580.html">
         Still Not a War? German Intelligence Puts Ukraine Death Toll at 50,000
        </a>
       </div>
      </div>
      Pro-independence fighters say there might be some foreign mercenaries amongthe pro-Ukrainian combatants who have been encircled nearthe city ofDebaltseve betweenDonetsk and Luhansk.
      <p>
       We have intercepted the talks infour different languages, Donetsk Peoples Republic (DPR) deputy ofdefense Edward Basurin told the journalists onMonday. Those are English, Polish, French and some Flemish.
      </p>
      <p>
       At the same time, we do not have in [our] possession any personal belongings which could serve asproof, he added. As soon aswe get any we will show them toyou.
      </p>
      <p>
       Edward Basurin has previously said that the Ukrainian forces try todestroy any proof that there are international mercenaries fighting forthe Ukrainian troops. However the DPR fighters have already got some indirect confirmation ofthe involvement offoreign fighters. On January 23, a representative ofthe pro-independence commanders said that they have discovered communication equipment and phonecards which are not being used inUkraine.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="b-inject m-inject-min">
       <div class="b-inject__media">
        <img alt="EU member foreign ministry officials stepped out against Washington's plans to provide weapons to Ukraine." height="375" src="http://cdn4.img.sputniknews.com/images/101731/45/1017314540.jpg" title="EU member foreign ministry officials stepped out against Washington's plans to provide weapons to Ukraine." width="705"/>
       </div>
       <div class="b-inject__copy">
        <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/squarcina/2087217652">
         
            Flickr/  Giampaolo Squarcina
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="b-inject__content">
        <a href="/europe/20150209/1018000778.html">
         European Governments Step Out Against US Arms to Ukraine, Warn of Possible Escalation
        </a>
       </div>
      </div>
      Debaltseve is a key transportation city inthe Donbas region and is now the scene ofthe largest battles ofthe Ukrainian conflict.
      <p>
       Forces fromthe Donetsk Peoples Republic (DNR) and Luhansk Peoples Republic (LNR) have moved in, surrounding Ukrainian infantry, artillery, and tank divisions overthe last few days.
      </p>
      <p>
       German intelligence has recently estimated that the death toll inthe southeast ofUkraine has mounted to50,000 people, ten times the officially-announced figure which has repeatedly been cited inthe press.
      </p>
      <p>
       According tothe Office ofthe UN High Commissioner forHuman Rights, the recent uptick infighting has seen casualty figures spike, with224 civilians killed and 545 wounded inthe three weeks leading upto February 1.
      </p>
