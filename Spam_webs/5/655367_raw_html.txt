     <p>
      My Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One recipe is the the sweet treat to satisfy your sweet tooth! Big enough to share.if you want
      <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
      <br/>
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-8t.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37422" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-8t.jpg" width="515"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Theres not much more that I love to do than whip up a new batch of cookies. Ive done recipes for one cookie before, but these are a bit tweaked and are my favorite yet! Crispy edges with gooey soft centers. Thats always what I look for in a cookie. Oh and packed with chocolate chips, ha!
     </p>
     <p>
      Hope you enjoy this one!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-1t.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37423" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-1t.jpg" width="538"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Your line up of ingredients.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-2.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37409" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-2.jpg" width="547"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Cream your butter and sugars.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-3.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37410" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-3.jpg" width="453"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Add your beaten egg and vanilla.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-4.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37411" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-4.jpg" width="629"/>

      Dry ingredients up next!

       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37412" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-5.jpg" width="632"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Chocolate chip time! I used mini for this one.

      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-6.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37413" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-6.jpg" width="598"/>

      Yes and yes.

     <p>
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-7.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37414" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-7.jpg" width="607"/>

      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-8.jpg">

      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-9.jpg">
       <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37416" height="675" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-9.jpg" width="523"/>

       <h2 class="item">
        <span class="fn">
         Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One
        </span>
       </h2>
      </div>
      <div class="paper-mid">
       <div class="paper-text">
        <p>
         <img alt="Giant Chocolate Chip Cookie For One" class="photo wp-post-image" height="225" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Giant-Chocolate-Chip-Cookie-For-One-8t-150x225.jpg" width="150"/>
         Prep time:
         <span class="preptime">
          5 min
         </span>
         | Cook time:
         <span class="cook time">
          15 min
         </span>
         | Total time:
         <span class="duration">
          20 min
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Ingredients
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           2 tablespoons
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           unsalted butter, softened
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           2 tablespoons
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           granulated sugar
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           2 tablespoons
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           light brown sugar, packed
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           2 tablespoons
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           beaten egg
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           1/2 teaspoon
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           pure vanilla extract
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           6 tablespoons
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           all-purpose flour
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           3 tablespoons
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           instant vanilla pudding mix
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           1/4 teaspoon
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           baking soda
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           1/4 teaspoon
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           kosher salt
          </span>
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           1/4 cup
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           mini chocolate chips
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Directions
         </span>
        </p>
        <ol class="instructions">
         <li class="instruction">
          Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. and line a large baking sheet with a silpat liner or parchment paper
         </li>
         <li class="instruction">
          Place butter and sugars into a medium bowl and stir until well combined. Add beaten egg and vanilla stirring until well combined.Add flour, pudding mix, baking soda, salt and chocolate chips. Stir until just combined. Using a large cookie or ice cream scoop, place dough onto center of baking sheet. Bake for 15-18 minutes, until golden around edges. Let cool for 15 minutes on baking sheet before removing. Serve room temperature or warm.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         Makes
         <span class="yield">
          1 Servings
         </span>
        </p>
