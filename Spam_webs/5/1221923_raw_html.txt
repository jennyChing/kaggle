                     </a>
                    </h3>

                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">
                        Walk in baths, bath lifts and walk in showers can make life easier for you, especially if you have been constrained by restricted mobility till now. These effort saving bathing solutions can make your bathing experience enjoyable again. With these innovative baths and showers you will no longer need the assistance of other people when you bathe. A walk in shower, for instance, will have strategically positioned ergonomic handles to grip in case you slip. Below we will briefly describe some of these bathing solutions so that you can choose the one that suits you.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">

                        <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-CLKk4pN17iU/TrOKHmBP-HI/AAAAAAAAAFc/uWD4yfdRJvw/s1600/walk+in+bathtub1.jpg"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-IEmB-qcXics/TrOI12kaCPI/AAAAAAAAAFE/Ft_2RqHoyI0/s1600/walk+in+bathtub.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <br/>
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">
                        <b>
                         Walk in Baths
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">
                        Walk in baths are good for those who want to soak in a bath tub without having to worry about getting in and out of the tub, or slipping on the wet tub floor. These usually have a water-tight door on the side of the bath tub that you open to get inside. These baths also have low thresholds, so you dont have to climb in or out of the tub. Just open the door and walk in. Many models are provided with power seats that can lift up to 20 stone, so you can rise easily out of the tub. Good brands of
                        <a href="http://www.nationwide-mobility.co.uk/simplicity-walk-in-bath.php">
                         <b>
                          walk in baths
                         </b>
                        </a>
                        have thermostatically controlled taps and touch control handsets for simple operation. Anti-slip floors and grip rails are other common features of walk in baths.
                       </span>
                      </div>

                      </div>

                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">
                        <b>
                         Bath lifts
                        </b>
                        <b>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">
                        These are good for those who dont really want to install walk in baths or showers, (even though installation is quite hassle free, and you can get different models to match your existing dcor) or those who have to travel quite frequently.
                        <a href="http://www.nationwide-mobility.co.uk/bathmate-bath-lift.php">
                         <b>
                          Bath lifts
                         </b>
                        </a>
                        are somewhat like portable seats that rise up and down with the use of a hand held control set. You place these inside a bath and raise it to the rim of the tub when you want to get in, or out. You lower them to bathe. Some are inflatable seats that are controlled with an attached hand set. Others are portable power seats that look like small deck chairs. These too are controlled with the help of touch-control handsets.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>

                        <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sNwCl2hZZec/TrOJycjynQI/AAAAAAAAAFU/UiWdihKiDRg/s1600/walk+in+shower1.jpg"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; font-size: small;">
                        <b>
                         Walk in Showers
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">
                        A walk in shower can often come attached to a walk in bath. It can also come as a stand-alone shower. A good walk in shower will have a low threshold, anti-slip floor, and easy controls, like thermostatically controlled lever taps. Apart from that, it ought to have grip rails and adjustable seats, in case you get tired while standing in the shower.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNoSpacing" style="color: #674ea7; font-family: Verdana,sans-serif; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: small;">

There are many online suppliers and manufacturers of these devices, so you dont even have to stir from your home to shop. Whether it is a bath lift, or a
                        <b>
                         walk in shower
                        </b>
                        or bath, you can get everything through the web. Many of these websites promise quick installation and offer different models to match the dimensions and design of your existing bathroom, so you really have no excuse to deprive yourself of these bathing solutions which will make your bathing experience truly luxurious and enjoyable.
