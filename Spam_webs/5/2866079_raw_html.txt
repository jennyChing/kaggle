     <p>
      Photographer
      <a href="http://www.jameystillings.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.jameystillings.com/', 'Jamey Stillings');">
       Jamey Stillings
      </a>
      has released a photo essay on Americas largest solar farm, the Ivanpah Solar Thermal Plant in the Mojave Desert. Stillings photographic statements portray the magnitude and magnificence of Americas sustainable energy efforts in an amazing set of stills. It is his belief that a continuation of the conversation on sustainable energy is only a good thing for the world, and it pays to appreciate what we have already achieved. His exploration of the Ivanpah facility opens the eyes to just how visually impressive a solar farm can be. Our appreciation for this structure through Stillings images will encourage continued wonder into the world of sustainable energy production. [via
