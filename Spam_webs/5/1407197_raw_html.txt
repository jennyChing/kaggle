     <p>
      <b>
       <a class="extiw" href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Science" title="w:Science">
        Science
       </a>
      </b>
      in the broadest sense refers to any
      <a href="/wiki/System" title="System">
       system
      </a>
      of
      <a class="extiw" href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Objectivity_(science)" title="w:Objectivity (science)">
       objective
      </a>
      <a class="extiw" href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/knowledge" title="w:knowledge">
       knowledge
      </a>
      . In a more restricted sense, science refers to a system of acquiring knowledge based on the
      <a class="extiw" href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/scientific_method" title="w:scientific method">
       scientific method
      </a>
      , as well as to the organized body of knowledge humans have gained by such
      <a class="extiw" href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/research" title="w:research">
       research
      </a>
      .
     </p>
