         Pia Wismar Journey with Skinny Fiber

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Ive been struggling with my weight since mid 90s, in 1999 I changed my diet, something had to happen, I was 240 lbs, so my Doc put me on a 1500 calorie diet and I lost weight but when I reached 194 lbs it just stopped For a couple of years I was between 194 and 185 lbs, and then I started to watch some people posting healthy recipes and talking about Skinny Fiber, at that
          <span class="text_exposed_show">
           point I became a member of a support group here on FB. That is the best thing I have ever done for myself for years, and finally I ordered my SF and started with them Oct. 17 2013. Now Im still a work in progress but for now Im 167 lbs gone from size 36 to a size 28 in pants I even have 2 pairs that is a size small
           <i class="_4-k1 img sp_LWp1MpKGrs1 sx_98a18a">
           </i>
           Ive lost more than 20 inches, down half my painkillers and is only taking 1 blood pressure-pill (used to take 2) I eat what I want, the only exercise I get is when I walk my dog 5 times a day for 15 to 45 minutes (each time) Im way happier, have more energy, and Ill be SBC4Life thats for sure
          </span>
         </p>
