        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Share a Coke with Friends and Family this Summer
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-14T15:58:40+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 14, 2014
         </time>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/2014/07/share-a-coke-with-friends-and-family-this-summer.html#comments">
           3 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Holly &amp; Nat
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <em>
          This shop has been compensated by Collective Bias, Inc. and its advertiser. All opinions are mine alone. #ShareitForward #CollectiveBias
         </em>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         Ever have one of those days? Nothings going right, or youre just in a funk? One of the easiest ways to snap out of it is to pay it forward and do something for
         <em>
          someone else
         </em>
         , right? Showing a friend or family member I care about them always makes me feel better and forget my own troubles. Holly and I love to come up with little gifts for friends, teachers, neighbors! ( Gifts is totally in a 3-way tie for my top Love Languages.)
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-coke-gift-ideas1.jpg">
          <img alt="share-a-coke-gift-ideas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1570" height="900" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-coke-gift-ideas1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         In the hot summer months (lets be honest  all year), one of my favorite treats is an ice cold Diet Coke. When I found out about these special edition Coca-Cola bottles, I thought they would make a perfect addition to a summer gift! These fun, personalized Share A Coke bottles were right in the checkout lines at Wal-Mart.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/personalized-coke-products-at-walmart.jpg">
          <img alt="personalized-coke-products-at-walmart" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1572" height="1050" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/personalized-coke-products-at-walmart.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         SURPRISE! I even found one with my name! You can actually go to
         <a href="http://www.shareacoke.com" target="_blank">
          Share A Coke
         </a>
         online and search to see if your name (or loved ones) are available in stores.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="share-a-coke-with-friends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1573" height="640" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-coke-with-friends.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         Here are a few ideas you can use to #ShareitForward to friends and family! Add the Sharing is Caring tag to your friends favorite drink and surprise them one afternoonmaybe youll be just the thing she needs to relax after getting all the kids down for a nap!
         <img alt=";)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif"/>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-coke-with-friends-tag.jpg">
          <img alt="share-a-coke-with-friends-tag" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1574" height="900" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-coke-with-friends-tag.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         Why not put together a bag of your friends (or in this case, my Mamas!) favorite things for a little pick-me-up? You could include a new cup, favorite treat, and something fun to read. All of these fit in a cute re-usable bag I also snagged at Wal-Mart in the gift wrap section.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/summer-gift-for-a-friend.jpg">
          <img alt="summer-gift-for-a-friend" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1569" height="450" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/summer-gift-for-a-friend.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         Do you know someone who could use a fun surprise?
        </p>
        <h2 dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         Grab the printables here:
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/2014/07/share-a-coke-with-friends-and-family-this-summer.html/printable-share-a-coke-tags" rel="attachment wp-att-1576">
          Printable Share A Coke Tags
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-Coke-tags.jpg">
          <br/>
          <img alt="share-a-Coke-tags" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1568" height="640" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/share-a-Coke-tags.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Wed love to hear in the comments how YOU are sharing with friends and family this summer!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/signature-nat.jpg">
          <img alt="signature-nat" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1227" height="85" src="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/signature-nat.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
