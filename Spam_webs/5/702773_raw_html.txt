             <strong>
              1. Where &amp; How to Place Billboards? Worst mistakes to Avoid.
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="Wrong Bill Boards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3658 size-full" data-lazy-original="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bill-boards-mistakes.jpg" height="373" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="992"/>
            </p>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Wrong Bill Boards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3658 size-full" height="373" src="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bill-boards-mistakes.jpg" width="992"/>
            </noscript>
            On average companies allocate around
            <a href="http://www.marketo.com/reports/marketing-budgets-the-strategic-allocation-model/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Marketing Budget">
             38% of their marketing budget
            </a>
            on sponsorship and bill boards. However, most marketing planners and managers dont understand that too wordy bill board adverts dont grab as much attention as they expect. Their billboards need to be
            <a href="http://10steps.sg/inspirations/artworks/50-extraordinary-and-attractive-billboards/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Best Billboards Ever">
             more visual
            </a>
            to make an impact because people dont read the messages but see perceive the message faster. Most people dont have the time to read through. Besides, large billboards are not sturdy
            <a href="http://heavy.com/comedy/2012/09/the-20-worst-billboards/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Worst Bill Boards">
             enough to bear
            </a>
            the vagaries of the elements  winds, rains and snow.
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Solutions &amp; Tips
             </strong>
              If you must include billboards and banner ads into your promotional campaign, use them wisely. They are
             <a href="http://advertising.about.com/od/advertisingglossaryb/a/The-Six-Basic-Rules-Of-Billboard-Advertising.htm" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Six Billboard Strategies">
              best placed
             </a>
             in locations such as close to restaurants, movie theaters, parks and traffic signals where people are in a more relaxed state of mind. The traditional convention is to post them close to the highways. If you think that it is the ideal spot, make sure that the message has lesser textual information and more visual message.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              2. Is Email Marketing still Viable? Could You Be Sending Spam?
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="Email Marketing in Sri Lanka" class="aligncenter wp-image-3659 size-full" data-lazy-original="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/metropilitan-spam.png" height="391" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="887"/>
            </p>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Email Marketing in Sri Lanka" class="aligncenter wp-image-3659 size-full" height="391" src="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/metropilitan-spam.png" width="887"/>
            </noscript>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Email marketing, one of the most widely used online marketing strategies since the creation of the electronic mail, was effective during its early years of application. Typically, companies used to spend around 11% of their marketing budget on email marketing, but with webmail service providers like Gmail, Yahoo! and others becoming stricter with their anti-spamming regulations; this is not a highly penetrative segment, unless you are following the terms and conditions.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             If you have chosen an email marketing service without some research and questioning, you are most probably wasting your organizations marketing funds. How? It is easy for email service providers to run campaigns without opt-in and without complying with the email spam act. They can show you fancy bulk figures but there is no assurance that those emails are even ending up in the inbox of your target audience (leave alone they are your target audience).
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Your business / Brand domain will be blacklisted by Anti spam protection service providers like
              <a href="http://www.spamhaus.org/lookup/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Blacklist Check">
               Spamhaus
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://mxtoolbox.com/blacklists.aspx" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Antispam Check">
               MxToolbox
              </a>
              and more
             </li>
             <li>
              Your domain IP maybe blacklisted by ISPs and other Internet Service protection organizations
             </li>
             <li>
              Your email ( Maybe you have given / added your contact person email ) will be added in to the
              <a href="http://www.spamcop.net/anonsignup.shtml" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Spamcop">
               spammers directories
              </a>
              .
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             All unsolicited mails end up as spam. A few years ago, you could have imagined having some of them being delivered into the recipients inbox, but things are far more complicated today.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Solutions &amp; Tips
             </strong>
              Ask the email marketing service provider from where and how they built the email list. Make sure that they send out emails only to an
             <a href="http://www.out-law.com/page-5657" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Optin">
              opt-in email list
             </a>
             . Get the facts and figures of their services for previous clients 
             <a href="http://www.copyblogger.com/37-email-marketing-tips/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Tips for Writing Emails that Get Opened">
              email open rates
             </a>
             and response rates. There are a number of other technical aspects to address  Do they have just a single mail server or IP to queue all the newsletters? Does their service comply with the spam act? All your email newsletters must have an 
             <a href="https://www.campaignmonitor.com/blog/post/3266/simple-one-click-unsubscribe-process/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Unsubscribe">
              Unsubscribe
             </a>
              link as you dont want negative branding if someone doesnt want to receive your emails. Ask if the emails could be queued to be delivered at different times for maximum efficiency.
            </p>

              3.Are You Making These Local News Site Banner Advertising Mistakes?
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="Banner Ads problems" class="aligncenter wp-image-3660" data-lazy-original="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Banner-Ad-SPAM-1024x263.png" height="232" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="900"/>

            <noscript>
             <img alt="Banner Ads problems" class="aligncenter wp-image-3660" height="232" src="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Banner-Ad-SPAM-1024x263.png" width="900"/>
            </noscript>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Whether it is digital marketing or conventional marketing, the key to succeed and make the most impact is to do it the right way. Banner advertising on local news sites is a soliddecision, but with
             <a href="http://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/horrifying-display-advertising-stats" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Banner Ads">
              so much ad space
             </a>
             and movement on the pages, are you really getting the desired attention of the readers. Most probably not! Typically, a local news site will have more than a dozen banner ads
             <a href="http://www.clickz.com/clickz/column/2164452/rotating-banners" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Rotation Banner Ads">
              running in rotation
             </a>
             . How many times will the readers be viewing your ad in a given time period? You are most likely to be wasting money if you are not using this strategy in a methodical manner.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Solutions &amp; Tips
             </strong>
              Dont start a banner ad campaign unless you have the
             <a href="http://www.businessknowhow.com/marketing/bannertips.htm" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Banner Ads tips">
              right traffic stats
             </a>
             of the website. Study their source of traffic, and the demographics of the readers  their age and languages. Then, you can choose a site that matches its readers with your target audience.
            </p>

             <strong>
              4. Should You Distribute Flyers? Hope you are Not Creating any Traffic Jam
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="Wrong Flyer Distribution method" class="aligncenter wp-image-3661 size-full" data-lazy-original="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Flyer-Distribution-Cost.jpg" height="424" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="918"/>

            <noscript>
             <img alt="Wrong Flyer Distribution method" class="aligncenter wp-image-3661 size-full" height="424" src="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Flyer-Distribution-Cost.jpg" width="918"/>
            </noscript>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Distributing flyers is one of the most cost-effective ways to advertise. However, like other conventional methods, it is not followed properly by most businesses. Almost
             <a href="http://www.jamieking.co.uk/resources/leaflet_marketing_distribution_tips.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Flyer Marketing">
              50% of your flyers will be wasted
             </a>
             as they will end up in dustbins or by the side of the road. Most people neither have the time nor the interest in distributing the flyers. Another 30% are most likely not to read your message. Besides these issues, flyer distribution can have other side effects  it can cause disturbance in traffic, cause hindrance to public movement and even irrigation. There is no opt-in and no way of telling if your message is relevant to a person.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Solutions &amp; Tips
             </strong>
              It is easy to fall for the idea of hiring some cheap and non-professional guys &amp; girls to distribute your flyers. The thing is that this can prove to be costlier than getting some experienced hands to do the job. One big advantage of this promotional method is that it is easier for your flyer distributors to know the age and sex,place,time and delivery
             <a href="http://blog.solopress.com/printing/leaflet-flyer/the-best-ways-to-distribute-your-leaflets-and-flyers/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Flyer Delivery Tips">
              method of the recipient
             </a>
             . Besides, there is a very good chance that most of them are locals.
            </p>

              5. Are you doing SMS Marketing the Right Way?or Trashing Your Brand Reputation ?
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="Sri Lanka Mobile Ads Spam" class="aligncenter wp-image-3662 size-full" data-lazy-original="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mobile-spam.png" height="280" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="900"/>

            </noscript>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             The launch of the mobile revolution followed the widespread application of SMS marketing by companies. But it has its own set of limitations too. When it comes to mobile campaign you may forget to thing about when it is the best time to send your messages. For example, your business model has limited period special offers available only after midnight.
             <a href="http://www.toptensocialmedia.com/social-media-business/ten-reasons-to-just-say-no-to-sms-marketing/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="SMS Marketing Mistakes">
              Sending SMSs
             </a>
             to your target audience during this time bracket may more be a nuisance than anything helpful. Spamming, delivery failure and privacy issues are some of the other concerns.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Solutions &amp; Tips
             </strong>
              Choose right
             <a href="http://www.simplycast.com/blog/8-sms-marketing-tips-sure-to-bring-results/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="SMS Marketing Tips">
              SMSmarketing service
             </a>
             provider that follows strict opt-in/opt-out participation rules. The messages must be sent only with the permission of the recipients and there should be the option to stop receiving the SMSs any time. Mobile marketing is cheap, fast and more personalized as long as you are doing it right.
            </p>

            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             The traditional way to reach your target audience, cold calling and automated voice calls could prove to be
             <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/today/post/article/20140326171038-4042533-cold-calling-mistakes-top-tips-to-avoid" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Cold Calling Mistakes">

             </a>
             if not followed properly. But, cold calling is a goldmine if you use it in a right way with proper
             <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/today/post/article/20130701213914-33236097-finally-the-best-times-to-cold-call-revealed" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Cold Calling Tips">

You dont want your target audience to receive your calls when they are in a serious event, for example a funeral. If they are busy with their work, asking for their feedback or making an automated promotional voice call could be nothing less than irritating. Thus, there is a good chance that your cold calling and automated voice calling campaigns are doing nothing else but damaging your business reputation. Great example Sri Lanka Pizza Hut, Dialog Messages .. Do they have your permission to send their messages and adverts? Hell no!

             </a>

When it comes to social media marketing, if it is not targeted, it is not optimized. That social media advertising is expensive makes things even more complicated.

             <strong>

             </a>
             . If your
             <a href="http://socialmedia.lk/blog/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Social Media for Sri Lanka">

             </a>

             Print media Adverts is anotherridiculously amazing wayto grab relevant usergroup attention! Newspapers have vast readerships and some of the leading dailies could take your brand to millions of eyeballs. However, Print advertising is
             <a href="http://thefinancialbrand.com/37549/6-mistakes-in-banking-advertisements/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Print Media Mistakes">
              quite expensive
             </a>
             , which makes it all the more important to do it the right way  all of it comes down to
             <a href="http://www.empowermm.com/blog1/general-industry/work-smart-tips-2013-media-plan/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Media Planing Tips">
              media planning
             </a>
             . Without proper planning your ads could be printed on the wrong pages and in the wrong sizes. If you have not prepped your business for the response to be generated from the ads, you are going to miss big on the investment and the opportunity.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Solutions &amp; Tips
             </strong>
              Identify your local audience and their behaviors, you can publish your ad on weekends when people are in a relaxed state of mind and scan through the
             <a href="http://www.maverickstrategy.com/advertising-ideas/%E2%80%9C13-format-rules-that-inject-power-in-your-newspaper-ads%E2%80%9D/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="NEspaper Ads Tips">
              entire paper
             </a>
             . However, if you are printing it on a Saturday, make sure that your business is ready to handle calls on Sunday. If not, it will be better to advertise on a
             <a href="http://www.sunprovince.com/planning/ten-steps-to-creating-effective-ads/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Ten Steps to Creating Effective Ads">
              weekday
             </a>
             . Dont expect the readers to respond to your Saturday ad on Monday  most of them will forget about the offer.
            </p>
            <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              [highlight] List of Alternative Solutions [/highlight]
             </strong>
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Marketing, without concern for the medium, needs to be done the right way. All the 8 marketing strategies mentioned above are quite powerful, when planned and delivered for optimal impact. The key is to focus on your target market, timing and using the right medium at the right time.
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              The Biggest Mistakes We See Companies make when they first hit Twitter is to think about it as a channel to push out information. 
              <strong>
               Tim ORellly &amp; Sarah Milstein
              </strong>
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Social Media Marketing
              </strong>
               Make sure that your social media campaigns are optimized and your target audience receives something in return. Develop highly engaging contents for higher and deeper interaction. For example, if you are selling insurance, give them safety tips, importance of insurance and how you can reach them better with your products, instead of just promoting your insurance products.
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong style="text-align: justify;">
               Local Search Marketing
              </strong>
              <span style="text-align: justify;">
                There are hundreds of thousands if not millions of people on the web who are searching for your product, company name, address or phone number. Build your local search presence by creating and posting informative articles about your niche and business. Content marketing can involve writing niche-focused articles and posting on leading article directories to help your company information and web pages rank higher on search engines.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong style="text-align: justify;">
               Inbound Marketing
              </strong>
              <span style="text-align: justify;">
                At a time when conventional marketing methods are becoming more and more complicated because of stricter filters and relevance barriers, inbound marketing methodologies are the solution. While traditional promotional strategies are important, it is time mid-level and top-level management and brand managers change their perception about inbound marketing methods. Improve your knowledge about online targeting marketing as the number of people searching for your business is coming through the web.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong style="text-align: justify;">
               mCommerce
              </strong>
              <span style="text-align: justify;">
                With more than a billion smart phones shipped globally last year, mobile web browsing is claimed to surpass desktop browsing this year. The number of mobile internet users (on smart phones and tablets) is growing at a staggering rate, thus creating a huge market that is now surfing the web on the go. It is time to create mobile apps that can help you manage your business relationships with your target audience. You can use apps to process a wide range of functions, from facilitating transactions to payment processing, from reports generation to sending push notifications.
              </span>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Conclusion
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="Strategy" class="aligncenter wp-image-3666 size-full" data-lazy-original="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/plan.png" height="340" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="900"/>
            </p>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Strategy" class="aligncenter wp-image-3666 size-full" height="340" src="http://www.sharanyan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/plan.png" width="900"/>
            </noscript>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             It doesnt matter what you think about the new-age media, it is becoming dominant and you cannot afford to ignore it, at least for your business. It is worth pointing out that a lack of knowledge and understanding and a veil of ignorance at the management level towards digital marketing is one of the major reasons why it is not addressed in most industry analysis and marketing plans. Contrary to the widespread belief, the transition is easy and doesnt require any sacrifices. If you are selling insurance products, it is time you allocate budget for search engine optimization (SEO) and have your product pages optimized.Find what your customers and clients NEED emotionally, not just what they want !There are a thousandsof other lessons to be learned.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="415" scrolling="no" src="http://embed.ted.com/talks/tony_robbins_asks_why_we_do_what_we_do.html" width="740">
             </iframe>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Most of your target audience is sitting in front of a screen (a desktop, laptop, tablet PC or a smart phone) and trying to search and get more information about your product offerings. If you are not up there in the search engine results, you are missing massive amount of potential business, probably much more worth than that huge billboard on the highway or those thousands of flyers could help bring. Put your business on the Internet highway and show them your brand and tell them what you can do for them! So, Tell me
