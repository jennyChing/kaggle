       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         WW Simply Filling Healthy Pizzas
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2012-06-02T16:36:41+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 2, 2012
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://lovinourchaos.com/author/abigail/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Abigail Peterson
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/#comments">
           12 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         I love pizza and have found the perfect healthier alternative to our normal and usual greasy pizza. It is simple and easy to make and is only 3-4 points on WW points plan and is completely Simply Filling, as well. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do and might I add that this is kid-approved?!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/ww-pizza-4-points/" rel="attachment wp-att-733">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-733" height="477" src="http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-Pizza-4-points-600x477.jpg" title="WW Pizza 4 points" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/&amp;media=http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-Pizza-4-points-600x477.jpg&amp;description=WW Simply Filling Healthy Pizzas')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="horizontal" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Flovinourchaos.com%2Fww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Flovinourchaos.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2012%2F06%2FWW-Pizza-4-points-300x238.jpg&amp;description=WW%20Healthy%20Pizza%20~%20Only%204%20points%20or%20completely%20Simply%20Filling!">
          <img border="0" src="http://lovinourchaos.com//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ingredients:
        </p>
        <p>
         Thomas brand Light Multi-Grain English Muffin (3 points)
        </p>
        <p>
         Tomato Sauce (0 points)
         <em>
          I used the tiny can of Hunts
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Italian Seasoning and Garlic Salt
        </p>
        <p>
         Fat Free Shredded Cheese (1/4 cup = 1 point)
        </p>
        <p>
         Optional:
        </p>
        <p>
         Turkey Pepperoni (17 slices = 1 point)
        </p>
        <p>
         mushrooms
        </p>
        <p>
         bell pepper
        </p>
        <p>
         olives
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/ww-pizza/" rel="attachment wp-att-734">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-734" height="400" src="http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-Pizza-600x400.jpg" title="WW Pizza Ingredients" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/&amp;media=http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-Pizza-600x400.jpg&amp;description=WW Simply Filling Healthy Pizzas')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I slice my english muffin in half and slightly toast the halves.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/ww-pizza-english-muffin/" rel="attachment wp-att-735">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-735" height="338" src="http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-pizza-english-muffin-600x338.jpg" title="WW pizza english muffin" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/&amp;media=http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-pizza-english-muffin-600x338.jpg&amp;description=WW Simply Filling Healthy Pizzas')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, I spoon the tomato sauce onto each half.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/ww-pizza-marinara-sauce/" rel="attachment wp-att-736">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-736" height="385" src="http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-Pizza-marinara-sauce-600x385.jpg" title="WW Pizza marinara sauce" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://lovinourchaos.com/ww-simply-filling-healthy-pizzas/&amp;media=http://lovinourchaos.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/WW-Pizza-marinara-sauce-600x385.jpg&amp;description=WW Simply Filling Healthy Pizzas')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I sprinkle a little Italian Seasoning and Garlic.
        </p>
        <p>
