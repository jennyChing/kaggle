          <p align="center" style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/topshop-55e.jpg">
            <img alt="topshop 55e" class="aligncenter wp-image-1008" height="918" src="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/topshop-55e.jpg" width="612"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <em>
            Topshop, 55
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           We totally thought this was a passing trend, and yet it seems boy jeans are going nowhere fast. So now that weve accepted them, its time to figure out how to wear them.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-1006">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Whether youre kicking back in your casuals or dolled up for a night out, if styled appropriately, the boy jean is about as versatile as they come, and definitely more comfortable than your spray-on skinnies. Were going to show you how to style your mannish denims by night, by day and for play.
          </p>
          <p align="center" style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/River-Island-50e.jpg">
            <img alt="River Island 50e" class="aligncenter wp-image-1007" height="308" src="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/River-Island-50e.jpg" width="308"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <em>
            River Island, 50
           </em>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <a href="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/new-look-29.991.jpg">
            <img alt="new look 29.99" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1009" height="414" src="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/new-look-29.991.jpg" width="979"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <em>
            New Look, 29.99
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/boyfriend-jeans-blazer1.jpg">
            <img alt="boyfriend-jeans-blazer1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1010" height="450" src="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/boyfriend-jeans-blazer1.jpg" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h5 style="text-align: center;">
           By Night
          </h5>
          <p>
           Rule Number One of wearing manpants: ensure the rest of your look is chic, tailored and sophisticated so as to avoid looking like an actual man. Opt for fail-safe tailored top and blazer in a neutral but complementary colour, then finish off the look with some open-toe sandals and statement jewellery.
           <br/>
           <a href="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/carolina-engman-boyfriend-jeans5.jpg">
            <img alt="carolina-engman-boyfriend-jeans5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1011" height="1200" src="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/carolina-engman-boyfriend-jeans5.jpg" width="800"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h5 style="text-align: center;">
           By Day
          </h5>
          <p>
           For a casual daytime look, polish up your favourite white runners and team your boy jeans with an on-trend pastel overcoat. Keeping the colour palette and accessories simple and chic; finish off the relaxed look with a casual beanie hat.
           <br/>
           <a href="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/boyfriend-jeans-questione-di-stile2.jpg">
            <img alt="boyfriend-jeans-questione-di-stile2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1012" height="1896" src="http://blog.opsh.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/boyfriend-jeans-questione-di-stile2.jpg" width="1280"/>
