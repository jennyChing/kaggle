              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               A final year engineering project is the most significant academic work for a B. Tech and BE students. When it comes to the choice of the project, ECE and EEE engineering students have a number of choices because of
               <a href="https://www.elprocus.com/list-of-200-ece-projects-for-engineering-students-in-2014/" target="_blank">
                various technologies
               </a>
               . Final year engineering projects for ECE and EEE students selection could be in a host of categories like embedded system, automation, wireless power, VLSI, communication, GSM, solar, Wi-Fi, simulation, Bluetooth, Zigbee etc. From these broad categories, there are various technologies for ECE and
               <a href="https://www.elprocus.com/list-of-72-simple-mini-projects-for-eee-students/" target="_blank">
                EEE projects
               </a>
               . Every student must have the basic concepts of these technologies and you have to decide which category to use. This article lists the
               <a href="https://www.elprocus.com/top-10-electronics-projects-ideas/" target="_blank">
                final year engineering projects
               </a>
               for ECE and EEE students
              </p>
              <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_28641" style="width: 490px">
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Final-year-engineering-projects-for-ece-and-eee-students-1.png">
                <img alt="Final Year Engineering Projects For ECE and EEE Students" class="size-full wp-image-28641" height="300" src="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Final-year-engineering-projects-for-ece-and-eee-students-1.png" width="480"/>

              <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
               Final Year Engineering Projects for ECE and EEE Students
              </h2>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               ECE and EEE are one of the famous branches of engineering. Here the list of final year ECE and EEE projects for B. Tech students to identify the best suitable project for them is listed below. These ideas will most helpful for the students who are not confident of how to get started with the project.
              </p>
              <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
               PLC based Intelligent Traffic Control
              </h4>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               This project is used to control the traffic in a junction. But, sometimes we can find that the traffic signals fail to control the traffic efficiently when a particular path has got full traffic than the other paths This situation makes that exact path more Crowdy than the other paths. By using this project, we can measure the density of the traffic by counting the vehicles in each path and then assign different time slots to different paths according to the vehicles. It is also very difficult for the traffic police to observe the whole situation around the clock. So,here PLC(
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/understanding-a-programming-logic-controller/" target="_blank">
                programmable logic controller
               </a>
               ) is used for automation, which counts the number of vehicles in each path. PLC is the most appropriate controller due to its ease of programming and reprogramming and roughness.
              </p>
              <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_28643" style="width: 455px">
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/PLC-based-Intelligent-Traffic-Control.jpg">
                <img alt="PLC based Intelligent Traffic Control" class=" wp-image-28643" height="318" src="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/PLC-based-Intelligent-Traffic-Control.jpg" width="445"/>
               </a>
               <p class="wp-caption-text">
                PLC based Intelligent Traffic Control
               </p>
              </div>
              <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
               Robotic Wheel Chair based on Flex Sensor
              </h4>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               Wheelchairs are used for the people whose walking is difficult, illness, disability or injury. This project helps people to direct without the help of the other person. It comprises of a flex sensor which is connected to a microcontroller to drive the motor in the preferred direction by the disable person operating it.
              </p>
              <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_28645" style="width: 304px">
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Robotic-Wheel-Chair-based-on-Flex-Sensor.jpg">
                <img alt="Robotic Wheel Chair based on Flex Sensor" class="size-full wp-image-28645" height="356" src="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Robotic-Wheel-Chair-based-on-Flex-Sensor.jpg" width="294"/>
               </a>
               <p class="wp-caption-text">
                Robotic Wheel Chair based on Flex Sensor
               </p>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               The flex sensor generates an analog signal when the resistance of the chair increases or decreases depending on the direction of the bends, then the microcontroller converts analog signal into digital signal using inbuilt
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/analog-digital-converters/" target="_blank">
                analog to digital converte
               </a>
               r. For various touch positions, dissimilar values will be generated. Depending on the direction, angle corresponding ADC values are calculated by the microcontroller and the motor moves in the desired direction.
              </p>
              <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
               Android ADK based Security System and Home Automation
              </h4>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               The main concept of this project is to design a
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/home-automation-systems-applications/" target="_blank">
                security system
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/home-automation-systems-applications/" target="_blank">
                home automation
               </a>
               for old age and handicapped people, those who are unable to control home appliances and also to give an indication in dangerous situations. The main advantages of using this project are we can reduce human efforts, time saving and energy efficiency. This project uses a standalone
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/simulation-process/" target="_blank">
                embedded system
               </a>
               board, ADK (android accessary development kit) at home. Home appliances are connected to the input/ output ports of the embedded system and their signal is delivered to the ADK. Here, accessary development kit (ADK) &amp; communication are established b/n the android mobile and ADK
              </p>
              <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_28640" style="width: 442px">
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Android-ADK-based-Home-Automation.jpg">
                <img alt="Android ADK based  Home Automation" class=" wp-image-28640" height="214" src="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Android-ADK-based-Home-Automation.jpg" width="432"/>
               </a>
               <p class="wp-caption-text">
                Android ADK based Home Automation
               </p>
              </div>
              <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
               Prepaid Electricity System based on Smart Card
              </h4>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               The main purpose of this project is to design a prepaid electricity system that can control the amount of electricity spent by the user. This is achieved by interfacing energy meter using
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/working-of-smart-card/" target="_blank">
                smart card technology
               </a>
               . This project uses an energy mete, microcontroller, LCD, relay, LED indicator and buzzer. The consumed electricity is stored in the internal memory of the microcontroller. The recharged amount is stored in the internal memory of the smart card. When the recharged amount becomes zero, then the microcontroller turns OFF all the loads by giving an alarm sound. And the user has to recharge the smart card to further electricity usage.
              </p>
              <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_28644" style="width: 430px">
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Prepaid-Electricity-System-based-on-Smart-Card.jpg">
                <img alt="Prepaid Electricity System based on Smart Card" class=" wp-image-28644" height="310" src="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Prepaid-Electricity-System-based-on-Smart-Card.jpg" width="420"/>
               </a>
               <p class="wp-caption-text">
                Prepaid Electricity System based on Smart Card
               </p>
              </div>
              <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
               GSM based Wireless Load Controller
              </h4>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               The aim of this project is to design a wireless load controller using
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/gsm-architecture-features-working/" target="_blank">
                GSM modem
               </a>
               . GSM is a low cost device for monitoring and remote control through a mobile phone. It consists of four contact closure inputs and two relay outputs. These outputs are used to control different loads like pumps, control lighting and central heating boiler. The inputs can be connected to the flood detectors, security sensors &amp; thermostats. An elective interface lets communicate with an increasing range of security &amp; fire sensors, panic switches and heating thermostats for temperature control.
              </p>
              <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_28642" style="width: 406px">
               <a href="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GSM-based-Wireless-Load-Controller.jpg">
                <img alt="GSM based Wireless Load Controller" class="size-full wp-image-28642" height="317" src="http://www.elprocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GSM-based-Wireless-Load-Controller.jpg" width="396"/>
               </a>
               <p class="wp-caption-text">
                GSM based Wireless Load Controller
               </p>
