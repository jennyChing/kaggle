               <div class="field-step-header">
                Follow These Five Easy Steps
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="panel-pane pane-entity-field pane-node-field-how-to-step video__steps">
              <div class="pane-content">
               <div class="field-collection-container clearfix">
                <ol class="field-how-to-step">
                 <li>
                  <div class="field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full">
                   <div class="field-how-to-header">
                    Fold napkin in half vertically
                   </div>
                   <div class="field-how-to-step-text">
                    <p>
                     Lay napkin out on a flat surface and fold in half vertically, making sure that the corners are aligned.
                    </p>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div class="field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full">
                   <div class="field-how-to-header">
                    Fold the bottom half of the napkin up
                   </div>
                   <div class="field-how-to-step-text">
                    <p>
                     Grasp the bottom of the napkin and fold it up so the bottom edge rests a little more than halfway up the napkin. Fold the new bottom up so it reaches the same line.
                    </p>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div class="field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full">
                   <div class="field-how-to-header">
                    Flip and fold to the middle
                   </div>
                   <div class="field-how-to-step-text">
                    <p>
                     Flip the napkin over and smooth it out. Fold the left side in so the edge rests at the napkins new center.
                    </p>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div class="field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full">
                   <div class="field-how-to-header">
                    Tuck corner between the folds
                   </div>
                   <div class="field-how-to-step-text">
                    <p>
                     Pick up the right corner and tuck it under the short folds on the left-hand side. Align the corners so the bottom and right sides of the napkin are straight.
                    </p>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div class="field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full field-collection-view-final">
                   <div class="field-how-to-header">
                    Flip the napkin and place utensils
                   </div>
                   <div class="field-how-to-step-text">
                    <p>
                     Smooth the napkin, then flip it over and arrange the fork, knife, and spoon in the pouch.
                    </p>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                 </li>
                </ol>
               </div>
