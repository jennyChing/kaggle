	&lt;div class="post-pad single"&gt;
		
		&lt;h1&gt;Ex-Bury Your Dead Singer Releases Lounge Cover of Nirvana&#8217;s &#8220;Come As You Are&#8221;&lt;/h1&gt;
		
		
		&lt;!--================= META ===================--&gt;
		&lt;div class="meta single"&gt;
			&lt;time id="desktop"&gt;Tuesday, February 25th, 2014 at 11:30AM&lt;/time&gt;
			&lt;time id="mobile"&gt;02/25/14 at 11:30AM&lt;/time&gt;
			&lt;span class="author"&gt;By &lt;a href="http://www.metalsucks.net/author/vince-neilstein/" title="View All Posts by Vince Neilstein"&gt;Vince Neilstein&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/div&gt;
		&lt;!--================ /META ===================--&gt;
		
		
		
		&lt;!--================ CONTENT =================--&gt;
		&lt;div class="post-body"&gt;
		
			
		&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.metalsucks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/myketerry.jpg"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-139417" alt="Myke Terry" src="http://www.metalsucks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/myketerry.jpg" width="600" height="400" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;script src="https://static.urx.io/units/web/ffabffc4-a672-4347-8e30-5755eebf89ba.min.js"&gt;
       </script>
       <p>
        Yet another
        <a href="http://www.metalsucks.net/2014/02/12/cover-lordes-tennis-court-needs-breakdowns/" title="This Cover of Lordes Tennis Court Needs More Breakdowns">
         lost opportunity
        </a>
        ! Given Bury Your Deads reputation for breakdowning their breakdowns with some more breakdowns, Id fully expect ex-vocalist Myke Terry (2007-2011) to pepper his cover of Nirvanas Come As You Are with a healthy dose of ya know. Instead hes taken a more srs direction.
       </p>
       <p>
        But at least its not just a straight cover; it plays kind of like a Portishead lounge version of the Nirvana classic with some neat ambient sound effects and vocal harmonies. Also, who knew this guy could sing so well?? Dat falsetto! Kurt Cobains statue is probably
        <a href="http://www.metalsucks.net/2014/02/24/statue-made-kurt-cobain-weep/" title="This Statue Would Have Made Kurt Cobain Weep">
         crying joyous tears of pure heroin
        </a>
        right about now. Someone get this guy in a band in which he can utilize his vocals talents in a way other than background noise for breakdowns ASAP.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/tracks/135721019&amp;color=ff5500&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=false&amp;show_artwork=true" width="100%">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         [via
         <a href="http://www.theprp.com/2014/02/24/news/former-bury-your-dead-vocalist-myke-terry-streams-his-cover-of-nirvanas-come-as-you-are/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.theprp.com/2014/02/24/news/former-bury-your-dead-vocalist-myke-terry-streams-his-cover-of-nirvanas-come-as-you-are/', 'The PRP']);" target="_blank">
          The PRP
         </a>
         ]
        </em>
       </p>
