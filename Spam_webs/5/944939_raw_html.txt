             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://ifitshipitshere.blogspot.com/2010/11/incredible-beluga-superyacht-emperor-of.html">
               The Incredible Beluga Superyacht, Emperor Of The Seven Seas
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <script src="http://w.sharethis.com/button/sharethis.js#publisher=0d650ffe-1ec7-4395-883b-24e806d21a65&amp;type=website" type="text/javascript">

              <br/>
              <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_zqFoq3qej2c/TMoR86UO1lI/AAAAAAABjj4/v_82pxlCFBc/s1600/beluga-1.jpg" onblur="try {parent.deselectBloggerImageGracefully();} catch(e) {}">
               <img alt="" border="0" height="450" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5533254830322013778" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_zqFoq3qej2c/TMoR86UO1lI/AAAAAAABjj4/v_82pxlCFBc/s640/beluga-1.jpg" width="640"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              The
              <a href="http://www.pure-sang.com/">
               Puresang
              </a>
              Naval Division collaborated with
              <a href="http://www.glass-deco.com/">
               Glass Deco
              </a>
              to conceive of this modern super yacht concept which was unveiled last month at the
              <a href="http://www.monacoyachtshow.com/">
               2010 Monaco Yacht Show
              </a>
              . The joint product of the design group and decorative glass company derives its shape from the white round-headed Beluga whale and a traditional fishing boat.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_zqFoq3qej2c/TMoRyzhARhI/AAAAAAABjjw/j8RWM_tfoXs/s1600/Beluga-2.jpg">

              </a>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_zqFoq3qej2c/TMoQSQB7RmI/AAAAAAABjiw/nl9QoR_9WjM/s1600/Beluga-6.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" height="399" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5533252997904811618" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_zqFoq3qej2c/TMoQSQB7RmI/AAAAAAABjiw/nl9QoR_9WjM/s640/Beluga-6.jpg" width="640"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              The modern  and luxurious 90 meter long concept includes multiple enclosed glass  rooms or conservatories, full sized swimming pool with jacuzzi, helipad, large open living room, dining room, bar, six sleeping cabins and automatic opening rooftops and  dinghy compartments.
