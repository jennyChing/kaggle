              <p>
               Winter has announced itself emphatically, with the rain pelting down, resulting in the large patio doors being closed and the pool, now only visible through the glass. This is one of the realities that we have to deal with every year; we may not be able to swim, but we can, as a present to the impending summer, spruce up the old pool deck, giving it a much more appealing aesthetic presence.
              </p>
              <p>
               It is, however, imperative that you choose a company that is reputable, with a proven track record of unrivalled excellence. For over three decades, SA paving has been the brick paving measuring stick to which all other companies are measured.
              </p>
              <p>
               Using only apex quality bricks to build masterpieces, to which clients will attest, SA Paving has earned the right to be a member of the Concrete Manufacturers Association (CMA).
              </p>
              <p>
               You can be assured that all the workmen have gone through an extensive training programme and are equipped to produce world-class results. Quality control is a serious tenet of the companys ideology and therefore whenever a job has been contracted, a site foreman will always be at the location to coordinate the paving and a construction manager will oversee the process.
              </p>
              <p>
               Meticulous preparation and consultation with you is conducted before the decking of the pool begins; you are also able to discuss any alterations that you feel may be needed.
              </p>
              <p>
               If your pool happens to be situated on a slant, you dont need to worry about the possibility of the bricks cracking or coming loose from the foundation because SA Paving only uses cement grouting, adding extra support and strength.
              </p>
              <p>
               Furthermore, SA Paving also offers a sealing option, which will provide the pool deck with further protection as well as ensuring that the colour does not fade.
              </p>
              <p>
               For more information about how to get your pool deck ready for the summer season, visit
               <a href="http://www.sapaving.co.za">
                SA Pavings official website
               </a>
