        <header class="entry-header">
         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          10 Life-Changing Facts About Fear
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           Posted By:
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://gailbrenner.com/author/gail/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Gail Brenner
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p>
          <img alt="freefromfear1" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2770" height="413" src="http://gailbrenner.com/wp-content/uploads/freefromfear1.jpg" title="freefromfear1" width="252"/>
          <em>
           Note
          </em>
          :  This article is a follow-up to the
          <a href="http://theboldlife.com/2010/12/limits-flourish-face-fear/">
           guest post
          </a>
          I wrote for
          <a href="http://theboldlife.com/">
           The Bold Life
          </a>
          (thanks, Tess!) about how to flourish in the face of fear.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          The presence of fear is a sure sign youre trusting in your own strength.
          <br/>
          ~A Course in Miracles
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre a human being, chances are you experience fear. These bodies we live in are built for survival, and fear is the gatekeeper.  It protects us, keeping us safe and secure by making us wary of any potential threat that might come our way.  Fear breeds caution, vigilance, and suspicion.
         </p>
         <p>
          Which is fine if a hungry lion is chasing you.  But if your intention is to live in the abundance that is always here, to lead with the heart, to be open to the depth and breadth of what might be possible in your life, then fear deserves your attention.
         </p>
         <h3>
          A Sacred Choice
         </h3>
         <p>
          Simply said, running from fear doesnt work.  If we avoid turning to face it, it will nip at our heels forever.  What does this mean?  We live a fear-led life, choosing partners, jobs, and friends out of fear.  Habits and addictions run wild because we are afraid of meeting our feelings.  We feel separate and alienated, while deep inside, we recognize the echo of truth whispering softly.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here is my question to you as we move into 2011.  What do you want?  I mean what do you
          <em>
           really
          </em>
          want for this precious life you have been given?  If you are committed to knowing yourself fully, to living fully, then get to know fear.  Start with these facts, then step aside and let your life unfold in all its glory.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Facts About Fear
         </h3>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Fear-motivated thoughts are all about cant.
           </strong>
           They create a negative, imagined scenario about the future.  Heres the truth: you dont know what is going to happen, so these thoughts cant possibly be true.  Buy into these thoughts, and you are inviting limitation.  Let them float on by, and you will see what is actually true for you.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Fearful thoughts are designed to keep you safe and limited
           </strong>
           .  They are not wisdom, and they are not truth.  You get to choose what to follow.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Fear always includes physical sensations
           </strong>
           .  Learn to recognize these, and receive them as they are with an open heart. Channel the energy of fear into excitement and enthusiasm.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Fear makes us think that something negative will happen
           </strong>
           , when the truth is that we dont know what is going to happen.  Become comfortable with not knowing so that fear doesnt rule you.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Resisting fear strengthens it
           </strong>
           .  The antidote is awareness  being willing to directly experience fear as it appears to you in the moment, recognizing the thoughts and physical sensations.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            The goal is not to get rid of fear
           </strong>
           , as you dont have the power to make this happen.  But you do have the power to change the way you relate to fear.  Learn to receive it with curiosity and a loving heart, get to know how it spins thoughts that deflate the things you are enthusiastic about.  But dont feel like something is wrong or you have failed if it continues to appear.  Simply meet it lovingly every time.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            A surge of fear tends to arise directly after a moment of truth
           </strong>
           .  Say that an idea appears in your mind about something youd love to do.  Soon after, you might notice that your mind is filled with reasons why you cant or shouldnt do it.  Recognize that this is fear speaking.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Recognizing the presence of fear allows you to make conscious decisions
           </strong>
           .  You have the clarity to see what fear is guiding you to do, and you can consider what you really want.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Fear is not the enemy
           </strong>
           .  It can be the voice of reason, caution, and practicality that serves you well at times.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            It takes energy to resist fear
           </strong>
           .  Getting to know it and allowing it to be lets your body and mind relax, as the fight is over.  This opens a space for creativity, wonder, awe, love, beauty, inspiration.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          Learn about fear.  Know it so well that it cant sneak up on you.  Free yourself from the chains of fear, and every moment of your life will shine.
         </p>
         <p>
          How have you dealt with fear?  Does it hold you back?  What happens when you embrace fear?  Id love to hear
         </p>
