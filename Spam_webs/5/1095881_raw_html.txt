     <p>
      The Old Man is me, and Im going West. Its still 26 daysaway, and the anticipation is killing me.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10661" style="width: 910px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/attachment/20150327164858/" rel="attachment wp-att-10661">
       <img alt="20150327164858" class="wp-image-10661 size-large" height="719" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/20150327164858-900x719.jpg" width="900"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       El Morro National Monument (2004)
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      Usually, when Ive announced trips in the past, I have a pretty well-designed itinerary to detail. This trip is quite different, and that is quite a departure for me. My inherent OCD combined with my love of planning have, on past trips, limited my ability to see where the road takes me. So far, when planning for this trip, Ive yet to make a reservation that cant easily be cancelled. This should allow me maximum flexibility.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10662" style="width: 910px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/copy-of-20150327164901/" rel="attachment wp-att-10662">
       <img alt="Copy of 20150327164901" class="wp-image-10662 size-large" height="612" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/Copy-of-20150327164901-900x612.jpg" width="900"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Montezuma Castle National Monument, Arizona
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10658" style="width: 826px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/attachment/20150327164748/" rel="attachment wp-att-10658">
       <img alt="20150327164748" class="wp-image-10658 size-large" height="1024" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/20150327164748-816x1024.jpg" width="816"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Glen Canyon Dam
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      Much of this trip will retrace my very first National Parks trip in 2003. Id come up with the idea in the spring to try and visit all of the National Park units in the lower 48 states. In May of that year, I headed out on the first of many long road trips. It was the last trip I would shoot with a *gasp* film, *gasp again* point and shoot camera. I couldnt believe how many pictures I shot on this trip when I went to have them developed afterward. 618 pictures total, which was a lot for the time, but is usually less than a days total on my last 4 trips now.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10654" style="width: 745px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/attachment/20150327164235/" rel="attachment wp-att-10654">
       <img alt="20150327164235" class="wp-image-10654 size-large" height="1024" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/20150327164235-735x1024.jpg" width="735"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Helicopter Flight over the Grand Canyon
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10656" style="width: 910px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/attachment/20150327164645/" rel="attachment wp-att-10656">
       <img alt="20150327164645" class="wp-image-10656 size-large" height="717" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/20150327164645-900x717.jpg" width="900"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       The Mittens, Monument Valley Navajo Tribal Park, Arizona
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10657" style="width: 826px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/attachment/20150327164738/" rel="attachment wp-att-10657">
       <img alt="20150327164738" class="wp-image-10657 size-large" height="1024" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/20150327164738-816x1024.jpg" width="816"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Zion National Park, Utah
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      I will be revisiting many of the Southwests most well-known and beloved parks that I visited on that trip- Grand Canyon, Zion, Arches, Canyonlands, Capitol Reef, Monument Valley- which Ive long looked forward to photographing with a real camera. I can not wait to see some of the HDR images I can capture in these places. I will also be visiting quite a few parks I somehow missed on previous trips- Petroglyph NM, Casa Grande Ruins NM, Navajo NM &amp; Cedar Breaks NM.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10663" style="width: 490px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/dsc02067/" rel="attachment wp-att-10663">
       <img alt="DSC02067" class="wp-image-10663 size-full" height="640" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/DSC02067.jpg" width="480"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Las Vegas Selfie from 2004
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10664" style="width: 650px">
      <a href="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/go-west-old-man/dsc02495/" rel="attachment wp-att-10664">
       <img alt="DSC02495" class="wp-image-10664 size-full" height="480" src="http://onmyfeetorinmymind.com/wp-content/uploads/DSC02495.jpg" width="640"/>
      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Lowest Point in the lower 48, from 2004
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      Ive certainly got some highlights planned- hopefully I can pull them all off. Among those are a photo tour of Antelope Canyon, a Padres game in San Diego, a flight over the Grand Canyon, a boat trip to the Channel Islands and visiting microbreweries in Albuquerque, Flagstaff and, of course, San Diego.
     </p>

      </a>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
