       <p>
        <em>
         <small>
          <img alt="Mashable" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-16018" height="50" src="http://d2ul9qo86z2b9q.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Mashable.png?697c4e" width="207"/>
          This post is excerpted from a recent
          <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/02/21/readyforzero-debt-tool/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://mashable.com/2013/02/21/readyforzero-debt-tool/', 'Mashable article');" target="_blank">

       <p>
        Generation Y is the most educated generation yet  lets talk school loans. The average person has four debts, and this degree-holding generation is now responsible for billions of unpaid college loans. Now theres a better way to manage it all.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="https://www.readyforzero.com/auth?c=blog_body1mash#signup" target="_blank">
         ReadyForZero
        </a>
        is an online tool that helps you control and ultimately reduce your debt. Founded by Rod Ebrahimi and Ignacio Thayer, this Ycombinator company isnt your average financial service (so dont tune out yet!).
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-16009">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Were not financial services guys, admits Ebrahimi, who says the idea for
        <a href="https://www.readyforzero.com/auth?c=blog_body2mash#signup">
         ReadyForZero
        </a>
        came from his girlfriends search for a website to help her pay off her debts. When they couldnt find what she was looking for, Ebrahimi and Thayer created it.
       </p>
       <p>
        A few years later, the entrepreneurial duo has helped people pay off more than $30 million dollars in debt
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe height="240" scrolling="no" src="https://www.readyforzero.com/iframe/debt-consolidation" style="width: 522px; height: 470px;" width="320">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        So whats the algorithm for paying off your debt? Ignacio shares the golden rule: The highest, most painful debt first.
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         <small>
          Jesse Draper is creator and host of
          <a href="http://mashable.com/follow/videos/valleygirltv/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://mashable.com/follow/videos/valleygirltv/', 'The Valley Girl Show');">
           The Valley Girl Show
          </a>
          , through which shes become a spokesperson for startups and helped pioneer the way of new media content distribution. Formerly a Nickelodeon star, Draper is now CEO of
          <a href="http://valleygirl.tv/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://valleygirl.tv/', 'Valley Girl');" target="_blank">
           Valley Girl
