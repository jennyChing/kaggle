           <p>
            <a href="http://www.geeksofdoom.com/2013/05/08/watch-now-superhero-president-battles-aliens-in-new-saints-row-4-trailer/2013-05-07-saints_row_4" rel="attachment wp-att-214804" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Saints Row IV Image" class="c" height="333" src="http://www.geeksofdoom.com/GoD/img/2013/05/2013-05-07-saints_row_4-533x333.jpg" title="Saints Row IV" width="533"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Are you a gamer who also happens to have an extra $1 million to blow on anything you want? If so, you might just be interested in a new special edition of the upcoming video game
            <em>
             <strong>
              Saints Row IV
             </strong>
            </em>
            , which just so happens to cost exactly $1,000,000.
           </p>
           <p>
            Thats right, a special editionof a video gamethat costs one MILLION dollars (I know what youre expecting me do hereits not gonna happen). The outrageously dubbed Super Dangerous Wad Wad Edition (or Million Dollar Pack) is very real, and though you might think its a joke the Wad Wad Edition actually backs up the price tag with some insanely cool goodies, including a freakin Lamborghini!
           </p>
           <p>
            Continue below for the full rundown of what else comes with this unparalleled
            <em>
             Saints Row IV
            </em>
            special edition comes with.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span id="more-229637">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            In addition to a Commander in Chief Edition of the game, of course, and the
            <a href="http://static.cargurus.com/images/site/2009/12/08/19/21/2009_lamborghini_gallardo_coupe-pic-5840629049543659332.jpeg" target="_blank">
             Lamborghini Gallardo
            </a>
            ($191,000 to $248,000), your $1 million will also score you a Virgin Galactic
            <a href="http://www.virgingalactic.com/" target="_blank">
             space flight
            </a>
            ($250,000), seven nights in the Royal Suite at
            <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RLeB8FtP5rk" target="_blank">
             Burj Al Arab Hotel
            </a>
            in Dubai ($22,900 per night), a week for two at the
            <a href="http://www.jeffersondc.com/" target="_blank">
             Jefferson Hotel
            </a>
            in Washington, DC, first class flights to both Dubai and DC, a new Toyota Prius with a year of insurance, and much more.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.geeksofdoom.com/2013/08/13/very-real-1-million-saints-row-4-special-edition-includes-lamborghini-trip-to-space-plastic-surgery/2013-08-13-saints_row_4" rel="attachment wp-att-229639" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Saints Row IV Super Dangerous Wad Wad Edition" class="c" height="340" src="http://www.geeksofdoom.com/GoD/img/2013/08/2013-08-13-saints_row_4-533x340.jpg" title="Saints Row IV Super Dangerous Wad Wad Edition" width="533"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Not that many of us has $1 million to spend on this awesome special edition, but only one of these is available. Its also unknown how much all of these bonuses add up to if you were to just go off and do them separately. But then of course youd have to spend the $60 to get the game.
           </p>
