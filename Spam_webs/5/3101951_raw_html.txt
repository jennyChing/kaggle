       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Professional massage is a great way to deal with neck pain and pain in the back and shoulders. But not always have the time and money to afford a professional massage.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Physiotherapist Nadia Perez from
        <strong>
         Los Angeles
        </strong>
        can help you with the following
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/15-drinks-that-will-help-with-your-daily-difficulties/" title="15 Drinks that Will Help with your Daily Difficulties">
         three simple techniques
        </a>
        that can help in such situations, and they are simple to practice.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/pain-in-neck.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="pain in neck " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1153" height="528" src="http://www.everydayinbox.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/pain-in-neck-1024x872.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
        Massage by yourself on your neck
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        This technique is recommended in situations where you have severe pain in the neck and you cant turn your head to the side. All you need is a
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/for-a-fit-body-5-things-that-you-have-to-count-but-not-calories/" title="For a Fit Body: 5 Things That you Have to Count, But not Calories!">
         yoga cube or harder sponge and a tennis ball
        </a>
        . In one hand take the cube and the other ball. Stand on one foot from the wall while you lev leg thrown back. Hold the cube against the wall, before your right shoulder and place the ball between the cube and bend your neck. Slowly press so your body will be closer to the wall. Then repeat the exercise on the other side of the neck.
       </p>
       <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
       <div style="float:left;margin:10px 10px 10px 0;">

       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/uper-back.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="upper back " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1154" height="513" src="http://www.everydayinbox.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/uper-back-1024x848.jpg" width="620"/>

       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
        Massage by yourself on your upper back
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Pain in the neck often associated with back pain. So when we massaging the neck it is recommended to massage your back too whether you feel pain in it or not.
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/water-apple-and-cinnamon-an-ideal-combination-for-weight-loss/" title="Water, Apple and Cinnamon  an Ideal Combination for Weight Loss!">
         Stand with your back facing the wall.
        </a>
        Place the tennis ball between the top of the right shoulder and spine and good lean your body to the wall. The hand made a circular motion that will enable the internal muscles to massage.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/3.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="Stretching " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1155" height="668" src="http://www.everydayinbox.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/3-951x1024.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
        Stretching of the neck and chest
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Continuous use of mobile phones, computers and improper sleeping forcing us to drop our neck forward which leads to stiffness and pain in the body. Therefore occasionally need to do
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/7-walnuts-a-day-can-help-you-against-many-diseases/" title="7 Walnuts a Day Can Help You Against Many Diseases!">
         stretching of the neck and back
        </a>
        . Stand with your hands placed behind your back and lift your head slowly stretching the whole body back. Do not relax stomach and hold the spine in a neutral position. Hold the body in this position
        <strong>
         20 to 30 seconds
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
