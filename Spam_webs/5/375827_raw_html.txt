            <p>
             Connected Living Room (CLR) is a concept where network-based devices are used for infotainment purposes. The CLR concept enables one to share music, photos, and videos among various consumer devices such as computers, set-top boxes, TV systems, smart phones, home theater systems, gaming consoles, and other multimedia devices.
            </p>
            <p>
             APAC looks to be a promising market for Connected Living Room devices. With the transition from analog to digital systems gaining pace (thanks to government institutions), APAC will be the biggest market for Connected Living Room devices. In 2012, APAC grossed $62.85 billion, and is expected to grow to $86.29 billion by the end of 2018.
            </p>
