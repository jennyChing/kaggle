        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Fudge Cake Recipe
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-02-27T16:44:47+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 27, 2011
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/author/robyn/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Robyn
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/fudge-cake-with-peanut-butter-frosting/#comments">
           25 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         This fudge cake recipe makes a perfect, rich chocolate cake recipe. Simple to make, this fudge cake pairs well with so many frosting recipes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2011/02/fudge-cake-peanut-butter-frosting-1.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-1212 aligncenter" height="399" src="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2011/02/fudge-cake-peanut-butter-frosting-1-602x399.jpg" width="602"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course, I love it paired with my
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/2011/02/27/peanut-butter-frosting-recipe/#axzz2vEG8dU00" target="_blank">
          peanut butter frosting
         </a>
         . It is one of my all-time favorite flavor combinations. Its rich, decadent, and to die for. If you like peanut butter and chocolate, I cant recommend it enough. Of course, any frosting recipe works perfectly, too!
        </p>

          Fudge Cake Recipe

          This fudge cake makes a delicious, rich chocolate dessert.

           <span itemprop="author">
            Robyn Stone | Add a Pinch

           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            12
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 ounce baking chocolate
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            pinch salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup chocolate chips
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 325 Fahrenheit.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Prepare 8 inch square or small baking pan by buttering and lightly flouring.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Melt butter and chocolate over low heat in a small saucepan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from heat and add sugar.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Beat in eggs.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add flour, baking powder, and salt.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix well.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add vanilla and chocolate chips. Mix well.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pour batter into prepared pan and bake 35 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from oven and cut while hot.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Do not remove until cooled.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            You may leave fudge cake in the pan, spread lightly with desired frosting or remove from cake pans and serve.
           </li>
          </ol>
