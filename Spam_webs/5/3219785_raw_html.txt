            And it's not like the lineup is getting stale either. On the contrary, there seem to be newer, hotter players coming on the tour every single day, many of them from overseas. In fact, there are seven women on this list whose last name ends in "ova." That's almost a quarter of the list!
           </p>
           <p>
            I know you're all used to seeing these kinds of lists that inevitably have Anna Kournikova topping it off with her fine self. That's all well and good, but I'm more of a "what have you done for me lately" kind of guy. That's why I'm singling out the players who are still playing, and playing at the top of their game no less.
           </p>
