         <p>
          <a class="ext-link" href="http://datingheadshots.com/blog/tag/online-dating-controversy/" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="Online dating controversy">
           <img alt="Online dating controversy" class="alignright size-full wp-image-575" height="205" src="http://datingheadshots.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/dating_controversy.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 0 0 0 10px; border: 1px solid #ccc;" width="275"/>
          </a>
          Weve been discussing some of the complaints people have with online dating sites and how daters can spot and protect themselves against predatory sites.
          <strong>
           If youre new to online dating, it can be hard to tell the good guys from the crooks.
          </strong>
          Consumer protection agencies say the best way to protect yourself is to be a proactive consumer and take steps to prevent problems before they occur. Its like your grandmother used to say, An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure.
         </p>
         <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
         </div>
         <p>
          Before giving your personal information to an online dating site or signing a contract, review the Better Business Bureaus Reliability Report on the dating service at
          <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.bbb.org/us/Find-Business-Reviews/" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="www.bbb.org">
           www.bbb.org
          </a>
          . Consumer advocates recommend asking these questions when you interview an online dating service:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           What is your minimum enrollment period?
          </li>
          <li>
           What is the monthly fee? Is there an additional membership fee or any fees for other services?
          </li>
          <li>
           Once its posted, can I update my online dating profile and
           <a href="http://www.datingheadshots.com/" title="Dating Headshot">
            dating headshot
           </a>
           ? How do I do this and is there a charge or limit to the number of revisions that can be made?
          </li>
          <li>
           What is your cancellation procedure and how do I stop monthly deductions if I cancel?
          </li>
          <li>
           If I am dissatisfied with your service, what can I do?
          </li>
          <li>
           Ask for contact information for 3 or 4 current members in your area. Contact them and ask about their experiences with the dating service.
          </li>
         </ol>
