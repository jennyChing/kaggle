        <p>
         I noticed recently that I dont have any recipes for drinks on this blog so what better way to start a new category then with homemade sparkling strawberry lemonade. To be honest, I had never attempted homemade lemonade before. Growing up, I was always a fan of the powdered stuff, but have since found it way too sweet for me. Making it at home allows you to adjust it to your tastes. For this recipe, I used fresh strawberries, lemons, a quick simple syrup and some sparkling water. A little sweet, a little tart and very refreshing.

         <h1>
          The Recipe
         </h1>
         <meta content="Deseree Kazda" itemprop="author"/>
         <meta content="A sweet strawberry lemonade perfect for a hot summer day. " itemprop="description"/>
         <meta content="http://images.lifesambrosia.com/food/sparkling-strawberry-lemonade.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
         <meta content="Drinks" itemprop="recipeCategory"/>
         <meta content="American" itemprop="recipeCuisine"/>
         <ul class="amount_time_display">
          <li class="value_container" itemprop="recipeYield">
           <i class="icon-food">
           </i>
           Serves 4
          </li>
          <li class="value_container">
           <i class="icon-clock">
           </i>
           Prep time:
           <time datetime="PT0H30M" itemprop="prepTime">
            30 min
           </time>
          </li>
          <li class="value_container">
           <i class="icon-clock">
           </i>
           Cook time:
           <time content="PT0H10M" itemprop="cookTime">
            10 min
           </time>
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <div class="ingredients">
         <div class="ingredient_group">
          <p class="recipeheadings">
           Ingredients:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            6 lemons
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 to 1 1/2 cups simple syrup (recipe follows)
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 (16 ounce) container strawberries, hulled and halved
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 quart sparkling water
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            ice cubes
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            mint for garnish
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="ingredient_group">
          <p class="recipeheadings">
           For the Simple Syrup
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 part sugar
            <br/>
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 part water
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="instructions">
         <p class="recipeheadings">
          Instructions:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Combine sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat.
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Bring to a boil. Stir until sugar has dissolved.
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Cool completely.
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Roll the lemons back and forth a couple of times on the counter to release the juices. Cut the lemons in half juice into a bowl. Remove any seeds that may have fallen into the juice.  You should get about 1 cup of lemon juice.
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Using an immersion blender or regular blender, blend the strawberries with the 1/2 cup of the simple syrup, until smooth.
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Pour lemon juice, strawberry puree, 1/2 cup simple syrup and sparkling water into a pitcher. Add more simple syrup if you want a little more sweetness. Add ice cubes. Chill until ready to drink.
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Place more ice cubes in a glass, pour in chilled lemonade, garnish with mint sprig. Enjoy!
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p class="note">
          Enjoy!
         </p>
