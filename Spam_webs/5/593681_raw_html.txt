      <p>
       So it begins.
      </p>
      <p>
       Earlier this month,
       <em>
        Suicide Squad
       </em>
       director David Ayer gave fans a vague
       <a href="http://www.altpress.com/news/entry/heres_our_first_look_at_jared_leto_as_the_joker" target="_blank">
        first look
       </a>
       at Jared Leto as the Joker.
      </p>
      <p>
       Yesterday afternoon, Ayer returned to his Twitter in light of (essentially) the75-year anniversary ofBatman'sfirst comic book issue, which is also commonly known as the Joker's debut, (via
       <a href="http://badassdigest.com/2015/04/24/this-is-the-new-dc-movieverse-version-of-the-joker/" target="_blank">
        Badass Digest
       </a>
       ). The post, which features Leto from the chest-up, is captioned with, "The Suicide Squad wishes you a Happy Anniversary Mr. J! #Joker75 #SuicideSquad @WarnerBrosEnt @DCComics." Check it out for yourself below.
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Suicide Squad
       </em>
       hits theaters August 5, 2016.
      </p>
