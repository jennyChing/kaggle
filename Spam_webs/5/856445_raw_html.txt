        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Kitchen Remodeling increases the Value of Frederick County Homes
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          <a href="http://www.handyandylofstrand.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Kitchen-Remodeling-Frederick-County.jpg">
           <img alt="Kitchen Remodeling Frederick County" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-194" height="300" src="http://www.handyandylofstrand.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Kitchen-Remodeling-Frederick-County-230x300.jpg" title="Kitchen-Remodeling-Frederick-County" width="230"/>
          </a>
          When it comes to home improvement, Frederick County homeowners have a lot of decisions:
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.handyandylofstrand.com/blog/basement-remodeling-contractor-frederick-county/" style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          basement remodeling
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          , build a deck,
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.handyandylofstrand.com/blog/bathroom-remodeling-can-benefit-frederick-county-home-several-ways/" style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          bathroom remodeling
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          , etc. Each project has its own unique set of benefits. And each project will impact the value of your home in its own unique way. But which home improvement project will make the biggest impact? The answer:
         </span>
         <b style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          Kitchen Remodeling
         </b>
         <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          . With an average
         </span>
         <b style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          return on investment (ROI)
         </b>
         <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          of 74.2-82.7%, according to
         </span>
         <i style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          Remodeling
         </i>
         <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          magazines annual Cost vs. Value report, kitchen remodeling is one of the best ways to increase the value of your home. Compared to other potential projects, the kitchen ranks in the upper echelon of recouped costs. But that is just the beginning
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         When it is time to sell your home, a recently-remodeled kitchen is a
         <b>
          HUGE selling point
         </b>
         . Most potential homebuyers look for two things when buying a home: an updated bathroom and a remodeled kitchen. Conversely, nothing can send potential homebuyers running for the hills quicker than an ugly, out-of-date kitchen.
         <i>
          Yuck!
         </i>
         And with the current state of the real estate market, a little kitchen remodeling can go a long way!
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         Additional Benefits of Kitchen Remodeling
        </h2>
        <ol style="text-align: left;">
         <li>
          Allows you to customize your kitchen your way
         </li>
         <li>
          Increased storage space
         </li>
         <li>
          More counter space
         </li>
         <li>
          <b>
           And More!
          </b>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         Frederick County Kitchen Remodeling Contractor
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Andy Lofstrand, better known in home improvement circles as Handy Andy, is a
         <a href="http://www.handyandylofstrand.com/blog/need-remodeling-contractor-handy-andy-services-frederick-county/">
          remodeling contractor
         </a>
         in Frederick County who specializes in light kitchen remodeling. , Andy can handle everything from crown molding and casings to drywall and plaster repair, baseboards, custom trim work, and so much more. Andy goes the extra mile to ensure his clients are completely satisfied with their projects. It is this dedication to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and personalized service that has allowed Andy to thrive in such a competitive home improvement industry.
        </p>

         <strong>
          <i>
           Choose elegance. Choose quality. Choose Handy Andy!

        </p>
