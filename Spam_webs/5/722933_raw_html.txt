                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-109EH7TMOxQ/U0PWZgB6p2I/AAAAAAAAPn4/Ot_eu3s6pFo/s1600/IMG_9037.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://www.joules.com/" rel="nofollow">
                      Joules
                     </a>
                     is one of those great British family brands that creates lovely pieces for any season. I love their approach to simple style as well as their use of pretty floral prints. They have a great understanding of combining practicality and style together to make useable but chic clothing that is perfect for a picnic to a date night, walks along to beach or the school run, so when they asked me to review of piece of clothing from their summer collection I couldn't wait. I went with this simple denim tunicthat is so comfortable and relaxed but tailored at the waist to give you some shape and I am really impressed with the
                     <a href="http://www.joules.com/Womens-Clothing/Tunics" rel="nofollow">
                      tunics from Joules
                     </a>
                     I can see myself wearing this at some festivals this year and it's very well made and I am sure it was last for many years to come.
                     <br/>
                     I have paired it up with my red cheeky shoes to give a pop of colour and my Radley bag and east silver cuff bracelet for a chic daily look.
