     <div class="item-description">
      <p>
       I love aprons! I have aprons for all occasions. Craft aprons, sewing aprons, baking aprons... the list goes on and will increase as I get busy in the New Year.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Everybody loves daisy's. But pink polka dot daisy's are even better! This very versatile apron would be great used as a market, craft or gardening apron and is sturdily constructed in a good quality poly cotton.Created from cotton and poly/cotton seersucker cotton (slightly bubbly, textured surface - very vintage!) in pinks and green and features a appliqued daisy on the pocket in pink and white polka dots .It has  long straps and lovely frill at the bottom.Very cute! The pocket is lined and generous enough to hold all the essentials and the ties long enough to tie in a big bow at the back or around to the front on a smaller waist. Pocket includes a hidden loop for clipping your keys or phone to for security.
       <br/>
       Wear this cute half apron around the waist or lower at the hip for a longer look.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Apron body -  (approx) 19" at the top flaring out to approx 29" at the bottom (width) x 21' length Suits most sizes.
       <br/>
       Straps - 33 1/2" each from the edge of waist band
       <br/>
       Pocket - fully lined - 6" x 8 1/2"
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Poly cotton
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Normal cool wash
       <br/>
       Cotton/steam press
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Hand made in Western Australia
       <br/>
      </p>
