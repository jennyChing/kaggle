       <h1>
        Spring 2014 Trend Alert
       </h1>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makemechic.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/midi-dress-trending-now-spring-fashion-dresses-makemechic.jpg">
         <img alt="midi-dress-trending-now-spring-fashion-dresses-makemechic" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6642" height="505" src="http://www.makemechic.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/midi-dress-trending-now-spring-fashion-dresses-makemechic.jpg" width="619"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h1>
        <b>
         Midi Dresses
        </b>
       </h1>
       <p>
        As we transition into warmer temperatures, we need to find the perfect dress to complement the time between winter and spring. The hottest
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <span style="color: #ff99cc;">
          <a href="http://www.makemechic.com/just-in.html">
           <span style="color: #ff99cc; text-decoration: underline;">
            spring 2014 trends
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        for dresses are midi dresses. In case you havent heard, midi dresses are the ones that are neither maxi nor mini. The length of a midi dress falls below your knees or somewhere mid-calf. The sophisticated style of this dress is a great tribute to the late 50s and early 60s.
       </p>
       <h1>
        <b>
         Popular Midi Dress Prints
        </b>
       </h1>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <span style="color: #ff99cc;">
          <a href="http://www.makemechic.com/womens-fashion-apparel/dresses.html">
           <span style="color: #ff99cc; text-decoration: underline;">
            Midi dresses
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        are perfect numbers to don from day till night. We are in love with the playful floral prints and bright colors that are prominent styles this season! For spring 2014, designers like Gucci and Jimmy Choo explore a whole new level of gorgeousness with these mid-length dresses. Want to try out this trendy look but cant afford designer brands? Well, its your lucky day! Here at Makemechic.com, we offer designs from classy, retro, and grungy to modern that will fill your closet with the latest prints and designs without emptying out your wallet. Clearly this trend fits just about anyone. This style is both sophisticated and sexy; the prints will call attention to you without overdoing it. No matter what your height or size, as long as you find the right midi, you can never go wrong.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makemechic.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/makemechic-how-to-wear-midi-dress.jpg">
         <img alt="makemechic-how-to-wear-midi-dress" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6643" height="644" src="http://www.makemechic.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/makemechic-how-to-wear-midi-dress.jpg" width="619"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h1>
        <b>
         The Perfect Shoes
        </b>
       </h1>
       <p>
        Once you have the right midi dress for you, youll need the perfect pair of shoes to pull your look together. For a tougher and edgier look, pair your favorite midi dress with chunky heeled
        <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff99cc;">
         <a href="http://www.makemechic.com/boots/ankle-boots.html">
          <span style="color: #ff99cc; text-decoration: underline;">
           ankle boots
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
        or creepers. Add some studs to complete that tough look! For a classy and even more sophisticated look, pair your midi dress with classic pumps and a sleek clutch for a nighttime event. If you are bold and unafraid of taking risks, don bright, neon-colored designs to make a funky pattern! There are limitless ways you can style your midi dress to create the perfect look for you.
       </p>
      </div>
