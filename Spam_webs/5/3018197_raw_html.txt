     <h2>
      <a href="http://thebeautydepartment.com/2014/11/the-hair-flip/">
       THE HAIR FLIP
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="entry">
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_16995" style="width: 522px">
       <img alt="photos/post/graphic design: Kristin Ess" class="size-full wp-image-16995" height="730" src="http://97.74.65.162/wp-content/uploads-c/2014/11/mastering-a-hair-flip-from-the-beauty-department-dot-com.jpg" width="512"/>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        photos/post/graphic design: Kristin Ess
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Everyone is all about this 90s inspired hair flip right now and were not gonna lie it makes us pretty happy. And while some can really nail that flip naturally, others may need a little help. Today were going to show you exactly how we achieve a flip with volume, wave and hold. Here we go
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://thebeautydepartment.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/how-to-do-a-hair-flip-via-thebeautydepartment.jpg">
        <img alt="how to do a hair flip via thebeautydepartment" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16993" height="1224" src="http://97.74.65.162/wp-content/uploads-c/2014/11/how-to-do-a-hair-flip-via-thebeautydepartment.jpg" width="512"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        Youre probably going to need some mousse at the root. If you need more volume and lift, this will be essential. Weve been crushin on
        <strong>
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1xzK2Ne" target="_blank">
          THIS ONE
         </a>
        </strong>
        because its got good hold and gives great separation without being too product-y. A little bit goes a long way. Were able to do all our roots with this one handful.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         The trick to a great hair flip is to blow dry everything and curl everything the opposite way that you want to wear it
        </strong>
        . So for example, if you planto flip your hair to the right, youll want to do all these steps with your hair going to the left. Then youll switch it up at step 7! Okay, so useyour blow dryer (on a non-ionic setting if yours comes with the option, as ionic dryers tend tosmooth more than create volume) and give some lift with your fingers at the root. Flip from side to side as you dry and even flip upside down for a minute or two. You just want to getall of the hair lifted up and going away from the scalp.
       </li>
       <li>
        Use MY
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1zwHAdw" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          NEW FAVORITE WAND
         </strong>
        </a>
        to create some soft subtle waves.
       </li>
       <li>
        Youll needa nice large hair pin like
        <a href="http://bit.ly/ReedClarkeGoldPins" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          THIS ONE
         </strong>
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        After your hair is all waved up, wrap it into a
        <strong>
         very high
        </strong>
        bun and push the pin through. Let that set while you get ready. This creates additional volume because the hair is now directed upward and cooling/setting while you get ready.
       </li>
       <li>
        When youre dressed and makeup is done and youre ready to roll, take the pin out and let the hair fall. Give it a good veil of light old hairspray.
       </li>
       <li>
        Flip everything to the opposite direction!
       </li>
       <li>
        Give it one more good round of hairspray because its 1997.
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       Are you flipping out?? Show us your hair flip please!! Tag us @TheBeautyDept on the gram, okay!?
      </p>
