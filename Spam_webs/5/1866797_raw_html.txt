          <h1 class="documentFirstHeading">
           <span class="" id="parent-fieldname-title">
            Careers in Parapsychology/Psychical Research
           </span>
          </h1>
          <p class="documentDescription">
           <span class="" id="parent-fieldname-description">
           </span>
          </p>
          <div class="" id="parent-fieldname-text">
           <div id="impMainColumn">
            <p>
             Opportunities for making a full-time career in parapsychology are
very rare, since there are presently many more qualified people wishing
to work in the field than there are funds for their support.
Opportunities do exist, however. We suggest the following link to the
Consciousness Research Laboratory for current information on obtaining
a degree or job in parapsychology:
             <a href="http://www.psiresearch.org/para3.html#seventeen">
              http://www.psiresearch.org/
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             We also offer two articles which give some valuable suggestions to
those who are contemplating such a career.The articles are old but the
information is still relevant. For electronic copies please access
below:
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="internal-link" href="resolveuid/c1b888f9fc12aa79b967d3fdeae0db60" title="Schmeidler.pdf">
              Hints to Aspiring Parapsychologists
             </a>
             ,
Gertrude R. Schmeidler, Ph.D.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="publicationslinks/stanford.pdf">
              Parapsychology as a
Career
             </a>
             , Rex G. Stanford, Ph.D.
            </p>
            <p>
             In addition, John Palmer atThe Rhine Research Center has
witten an article titled
             <a class="external-link" href="http://www.spr.ac.uk/Education_in_Parapsychology.pdf">
              Education
in Parapsychology
             </a>
             that speaks to students contemplating a
career in parapsychology. We offer the link here because we feel
that the site is particularly informative and helpful. However,
we do not endorse Palmer's claim that, "Most parapsychologists....feel
that the evidence from real-life (or
             <em>
              spontaneous cases
             </em>
             , as we
officially call them) is not as good as evidence obtained in the
laboratory."On the contrary, wefeel that evidence
from spontaneous cases is
             <em>
              better
             </em>
             evidence as it is derived
from what really happens in the world rather than contrived
circumstances.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="internal-link" href="resolveuid/74fc59f2e5c3257ed268c4ba968084b6" title="Carlos Alvarado">
              Carlos Alvarado
             </a>
             Ph.d., one of the
researchers here at DOPS, also has valuable information to offer
regarding careers in parapsychology.
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <div>
               <a class="external-link" href="http://www.pflyceum.org/64.html">
                What is
Parapsychology?
               </a>
              </div>
             </li>
             <li>
              <div>
               <a class="external-link" href="http://www.pflyceum.org/71.html">
                Parapsychology as a
Profession
               </a>
              </div>
             </li>
             <li>
              <div>
               <a class="external-link" href="http://www.pflyceum.org/72.html">
                Getting to Work in
Parapsychology
               </a>
              </div>
             </li>
             <li>
              Reflections on Being a Parapsychologist
             </li>
             <li>
              <a class="external-link" href="http://www.pflyceum.org/91.html">
               The
Historical Perspective in Parapsychology
              </a>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             <a class="internal-link" href="resolveuid/94cdea5c74ce18a12ecf3a160927833d" title="Nancy's bio">
              Nancy Zingrone
             </a>
             Ph.d., is another of our
researchershere at DOPS, who offers important information about
basic literacy in the field of parapsychology. In this article,
she outlinesthe body ofliterature which serves as a solid
foundation for those students and professionalswho aspire to
build a career inthe field of
parapsychology.
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <div>
               <a class="external-link" href="http://www.pflyceum.org/106.html">
                What Basic Literacy in the
Field of Parapsychology Can Do For You
               </a>
              </div>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             <strong>
              University Based Research Units
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             DOPS is one of a little over a dozen University-based research units
in the world investigating similar paranormal phenomena.Some of
theotherresearch centersare at
             <a class="external-link" href="http://www.princeton.edu/~pear/">
              Princeton
University
             </a>
             , the
             <a class="external-link" href="http://veritas.arizona.edu/index.htm">
              University of Arizona
             </a>
             ,
the University of Edinburgh, the
             <a class="external-link" href="http://www.psy.uva.nl/resedu/pn/res/ANOMALOUSCOGNITION/anomal/shtml">
              University of Amsterdam
             </a>
             , and the University of Hertfordshire
in England. The researchers at the University of Virginia's
unithave a special interest in studying the evidence for
survivalafter death.
            </p>
            <p>
             For more information about the psychical research being done at
other research facilities throughout the world, please refer to
thelist of
             <a class="external-link" href="http://www.pflyceum.org/20.html">
              Accredited Colleges and
Universities in the United States and the United
Kingdom
             </a>
             .found on the Parapsychological
Foundationwebsite.
            </p>
