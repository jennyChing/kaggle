         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Discover the best and most reputed animal bedding manufacturers in India
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="posted-on">
           Posted on
           <a href="http://www.kompass.in/animal-bedding-manufacturers-in-india/" rel="bookmark">
            <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2014-05-16T10:13:48+00:00">
             May 16, 2014

            <time class="updated" datetime="2014-05-16T10:21:00+00:00">

           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="byline">
           by
           <span class="author vcard">

             Kompass India

           </span>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://www.kompass.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Vaighai-Agro-Products-Ltd.02.jpg">
           <img alt="Vaighai Agro Products Ltd.02" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-15029" height="184" src="http://www.kompass.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Vaighai-Agro-Products-Ltd.02.jpg" width="651"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is essential to have an animal bedding of good quality for maintaining the health of the domestic animals. Vaighai Agro Products Limited is a very reputed name among the animal bedding manufacturers. They provide high quality animal bedding facilities to their clients. Their main motive is to deliver high quality animal bedding products according to their customer and meet all their demands at a very cost effective rate. Animal bedding is very important to keep your livestocks healthy, that is why they are committed to provide clean and eco-friendly
          <a href="http://www.kompass.in/vaighai-agro-products-ltd/animal-beddings.html">
           animal bedding products
          </a>
          to our customers.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Animal bedding products offered by Vaighai Agro include:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul style="text-align: justify;">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Coco Peat
           </strong>
           :
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Coco Peat is also known as coir fibre pith or coir dust, it is produced as a by-product during the extraction of coconut fibres. The main advantage of using it for animal bedding is that it is resistant to bacterial and fungal growth. It has proved to be an excellent garden soil as it has the pH of 5  6.8 and also it also have exceptional water retaining capacity. Coco peat has turned out to be environmental friendly animal bedding material as it is 100% biodegradable. They offer supreme quality coco peat which helps to keep your animals happy and healthy.
         </p>
         <ul style="text-align: justify;">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Corn Cob Meal:
           </strong>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The central core of a maize ear is called as Corn Cob and it is the part of the ear upon which kernels grow. It is successfully used for feeding and bedding animals. It can be also used as a substrate for mushroom cultivation. Vaighai Agro provide 100% non GMO corn cob meal which keeps your pets healthy.
         </p>
