          <div class="x-content-band vc" id="x-content-band-3" style="background-color: transparent;">
           <div class="x-column vc two-thirds" style="">
            <h3 class="h-custom-headline h2" style="margin-top: .5em !important;">
             <span>
              High Visual Impact
             </span>
            </h3>
            <p>
             Throughout the years, cold air inflatables have continuously proven to be highly effective marketing tools. The simple fact is, few other products can offer as much visual impact in such an easy to use, cost-effective package. For over 35 years, businesses, events and organizations have relied on Next Generation Inflatables &amp; Displays to maximize their marketing efforts, with high visibility cold air inflatables.
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="x-column vc one-third" style="">
            <img alt="Cold Air Inflatable " class="x-img x-img-rounded none" src="http://nextgenerationinflatables.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/10-CFL-bulb-cold-air-inflatable-copy.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="x-content-band vc" id="x-content-band-4" style="background-color: transparent;">

           </div>
           <div class="x-column vc two-thirds" style="">
            <h3 class="h-custom-headline h2" style="margin-top: .5em !important;">
             <span>
              Vision Into Reality
             </span>
            </h3>
            <p>
             Cold air inflatables are extremely customizable, typically ranging in size from about 5 feet to upwards of 70 feet. We use several different fabric weights depending upon the implementation, which vary from lighter weight vinyl coated nylon, to ultra heavy-duty 18oz vinyl coated polyester. All of our materials provide exceptional durability as well as UV and fire resistence. There are many pantone colors available in addition to custom logos, artwork and full digital printing. Our talented design team can turn your vision into a reality.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
