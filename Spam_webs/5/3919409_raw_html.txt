                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     4 best bandages to cover that gaping wound on your face
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      <span class="post-icons">
                      </span>
                      <div class="post-share-buttons goog-inline-block">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7816441832178724456" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Last week, I had
                     <b>
                      <a href="http://jenvillamere.blogspot.com/2011/04/6-reasons-why-having-cyst-removed-is.html">
                       a cyst problem
                      </a>
                     </b>
                     that required me to wear a bandage on my face.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Here are some of the bandage options I evaluated:
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      1. Tastee bandages
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-WsbFnc_1E54/TbithCZkwuI/AAAAAAAAAH4/733BnMxT34w/s1600/bacon_bandages.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-WsbFnc_1E54/TbithCZkwuI/AAAAAAAAAH4/733BnMxT34w/s1600/bacon_bandages.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     The most delicious bandages around. My nightmares are inspired by imagining what the free prize contained within might be.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     $4.95 US at
                     <b>
                      <a href="http://www.neatoshop.com/product/Bacon-Strip-Bandages">
                       neatoshop.com
                       <span id="goog_771218234">
                       </span>
                       <span id="goog_771218235">
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </b>
                     . (They ship to Canada for the same fee as to a US destination. Free shipping on Canuck orders $75 and up. But that's a lot of bandages.)
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      2. Wise old bandages
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8H796IkBTTw/Tbiu1gp_ybI/AAAAAAAAAH8/8CKgZXpNxlk/s1600/owl_bandages.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8H796IkBTTw/Tbiu1gp_ybI/AAAAAAAAAH8/8CKgZXpNxlk/s1600/owl_bandages.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     Having to wear a bandage on your face makes you look like an idiot, but associating yourself with owls makes you seem smart.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Available in-store or
                     <a href="http://www.urbanoutfitters.com/urban/catalog/productdetail.jsp?id=18158535x&amp;color=002&amp;itemdescription=true&amp;navAction=jump&amp;search=true&amp;isProduct=true&amp;parentid=SEARCH+RESULTS">
                      <b>
                       online at Urban Outfitters,
                      </b>
                     </a>
                     $7 CND.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      3. Hipster bandage
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-sAlEZIiSJI0/TbiwxsH5zBI/AAAAAAAAAIA/x7x5oscJKMg/s1600/mustache_bandage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-sAlEZIiSJI0/TbiwxsH5zBI/AAAAAAAAAIA/x7x5oscJKMg/s1600/mustache_bandage.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     Because hipsters love their mustaches. And I love jokes about free mustache rides.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     When I get a cyst on my upper lip (which will inevitably happen), I will rock one of these for sure. See you on the subway!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     $7 CND in-store and
                     <b>
                      <a href="http://www.urbanoutfitters.com/urban/catalog/productdetail.jsp?id=18158535k&amp;color=005&amp;itemdescription=true&amp;navAction=jump&amp;search=true&amp;isProduct=true&amp;parentid=SEARCH+RESULTS">
                       online at Urban Outfitters
                      </a>
                     </b>
                     .
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      4. Dilly of a pickle
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-IbncJAT8rHQ/Tbixu2eD1YI/AAAAAAAAAIE/MDDN1SNFo9U/s1600/pickle_bandages.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-IbncJAT8rHQ/Tbixu2eD1YI/AAAAAAAAAIE/MDDN1SNFo9U/s1600/pickle_bandages.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     I think these would look really nice on my arm. I think they'd look extra-alarming on anyone's face. I wish they were scented!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Also: these include a free toy, which spooks me.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     $4.95 US at
                     <b>
                      <a href="http://www.neatoshop.com/product/Pickle-Bandages">
                       neatoshop.com
                      </a>
                     </b>
                     (cheap shipping to Canada).
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Which bandage would you choose to wear on your face for a week?
                     <a href="http://oh-canada-box.myshopify.com/products/villamere-box">
                     </a>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://oh-canada-box.myshopify.com/products/villamere-box">
                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-NRGHW0cASJ4/VPi2ALf4-DI/AAAAAAAADmI/exwJf7xRPpc/s1600/villamere_box2.gif"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
