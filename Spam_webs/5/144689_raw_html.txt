         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Cocktail Apprentice Program New Orleans Application
         </h1>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://talesofthecocktail.com/sponsors/bar-smarts/attachment/barsmarts-logo-black-options-r1/" rel="attachment wp-att-14195">
           <img alt="BarSmarts-Logo-Black-options-r1" height="150" src="http://talesofthecocktail.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/BarSmarts_Logo_2013.jpg" width="150"/>
          </a>
          <img alt="TOC-CAPlogo (Black)" height="125" src="http://talesofthecocktail.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/TOC-CAPlogo-Black-620x581.jpg" width="134"/>
          <img alt="Pernod Ricard" height="150" src="http://talesofthecocktail.com/wp-content/uploads/sponsor-logos/PernodRicard.gif" width="150"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
           The Cocktail Apprentice Team is selected on cocktail recipe submissions, as well as their answers to questions relating to their experience in the industry. The program, initially developed to give talented bartenders the chance to learn from veterans of the industry, is made up of a diverse group of individuals to include experienced bartenders, as well as bartenders new to the creative mixologist world. The program gives innovative bartenders the opportunity to develop and perfect their skills.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Since the start of the Cocktail Apprentice Program, hundreds of promising bartenders have had the opportunity to polish their craft at Tales of the Cocktail. Bartenders also get the chance to work on large-scale events, which they may not have the opportunity to do normally. The team is essential to the success of seminars and tasting rooms, working behind the scenes to create flawless events with succulent cocktails.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          In 2014 The New Orleans Culinary and Cultural Preservation Society (NOCCPS) introduced the Tuition Reimbursement Program encourages and supports Cocktail Apprentice alumni who enroll in college, university, technical school courses, or hospitality courses in order to obtain an Associates, Bachelors, Masters, Doctoral, technical degree, or a certificate of completion that has a grade.
          <a href="http://talesofthecocktail.com/cap-tuition-reimbursement-program-information-request-reimbursement-form/" title="Cocktail Apprentice Tuition Reimbursement Program">
           Click Here
          </a>
          for more information about the Tuition Reimbursement Program.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Also,NOCCPS offers former Cocktail Apprentices opportunities through the Medical Assistance Fund, B.A.R. Scholarships and the CAP Scholarship Program that awards grants in various projects bartenders are interested in pursuing.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            The Selection Process
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          The cocktail apprentice leadership team uses a multi-stage review process based on a point system to select the program finalists. The first several rounds are conducted blind, awarding points based solely on essay responses. Applicant names and any recommendations are not considerations for this part of the process. Those who move on to the second round are then awarded more points based on event experience. For the third step of the review the leaders remove their blinders and give weight to other factors in order to create a staff balanced between regions and levels of experience.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">

         </p>
