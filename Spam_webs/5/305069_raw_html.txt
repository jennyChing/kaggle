              <h2>
               2016 BMW M2 Release Date
              </h2>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               This specific Bavarian car creator has many fans across the world and most of them will be very interested to see new sport coupe which BMW model is a new entry-level high-performance coupe also it will come since
               <a href="http://www.autopricerelease.com/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 2016 BMW M2
                </strong>
               </a>
               . This specific model, which is some type of a successor connected with 1M limited function, will have most necessary characteristics that you sports car will need. It will possess very aggressive glimpse, exciting interior, which will be full of hi-tech attributes, excellent performances and it will have great energy economy, which is nowadays important approximately other mentioned attributes.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               Like every model out of this company, 2016 BMW M2 are going to be characterized by extremely attractive design. Though it will use exactly the same design language since other BMW model, it will possess its unique attributes too. Beside regular design tweaks which separate M by standard models, it will use the F21 software, like future 2-Series versions. The 2016 M2 are going to be made of a few extra lightweight supplies, which are earmarked for only few models like M3 CRT or i8. Big section of the construction of these vehicles consists of carbon-fiber-reinforced plastics, which provides significant weight savings. This big fat loss guarantees not solely great driving encounter, but also large fuel savings.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               With M versions, it is just about all about routines. This model undoubtedly wont be a exception. The
               <a href="http://www.autopricerelease.com/category/bmw/" target="_blank">
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 2016 BMW M2
                </span>
               </a>
               use the same engine that can be obtained from 235i model. Its a 3. 0 liter straight six twin turbo motor, 2016 M2 can produces somewhere around 380-400 horsepower. When it comes to performances, this means until this coupe can sprint from 0 to 60 mph inside of 4. 2 mere seconds, while top speed are going to be over 150 mph.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               It is however early to predict extra precise launch night out, but we expect that
               <a href="http://www.autopricerelease.com/2015-mercedes-benz-cls-class-release-date/" target="_blank">
                <em>
                 2016 BMW M2
                </em>
               </a>
               are going to be available in later 2015, with an amount of 55. 000 cash.
