      <h4>
       Pros &amp; Cons:
      </h4>
      <p>
       <strong>

       </strong>
      </p>

      <p>

       </strong>

      <h4>
       Features:
      </h4>
      <p>
       <em>
        Stock Slots Pro
       </em>
       is a financial stocks management tool presented with a casino slots thematic. You first add your investments. Note:U.S. stock symbols (from your 401K, mutual funds, or stock purchases). These can be searched if you do not know the companys symbol, then you enter the number of shares, thereafter you can see your investment portfolio list. There is a bit more research you can gather about the stock from this screen including; last quote, change amount &amp; percentage, range, daily volume &amp; average volume, plus market cap.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://www.androidtapp.com/stock-slots-pro/stock-slots-pro-symbol-research/" rel="attachment wp-att-65393">
        <img alt="Stock Slots Pro - Symbol Research" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-65393" height="444" src="http://www.androidtapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Stock-Slots-Pro-Symbol-Research-250x444.png" title="Stock Slots Pro - Symbol Research" width="250"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The main feature, however, allows you to
       <em>
        Spin
       </em>
       the slot machineand see a monetary value plus graph of your investment gains or losses. Note: these are only represented during active trading days and times. So if you spin it multiple times in an active day you will see a traditional chart. Additional features include the ability to track
       <em>
        Trends Points
       </em>
       , which compares stocks from your last set trending point until the current time. Alternatively, you can see a more traditional table of your stocks under the Today portion of Trending.
      </p>
      <p>
       Overall, its novel app for glancing at your portfolio and not intended for executing trades, buying or selling. I felt the user interface and experience suffered a bit from not being as responsible as it should be- as if it were a web app versus native app. At times, the company search does not work or slow to filter companies.
      </p>
