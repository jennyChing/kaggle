You can collect screenshots from his laptop and even log microphone sounds to make sure that phone call wasnt from his boss. If you are still wondering how to catch a cheater, you must download and install
            <a href="http://www.mobistealth.com/" target="_blank">
             MobiStealth
            </a>
            on his phone. The power of the spy is now in your hands for less than $100. You dont have to spend a small fortune hiring a private investigator when you have this kind of advanced tech available in such a low price.
           </p>
           <h2>
            How to Catch a Cheater #6: Find my iPhone
           </h2>
           <p>
            If you are not willing to spend any budget for your investigation, theres always free solutions for you. Luckily, we know all the ways to satisfy your appetite when it comes to the big question: how to catch a cheater? Find my iPhone is the free service powered by Apple that allows you to recover your beloved smartphone or tablet if it was lost or stolen. You can do the same if you think that your man is lost or stolen by another woman. Just fire up the application and check out his exact location. When your husband comes back, just ask him where youve been. If he lies, you will know. Make sure you take a screenshot that shows his whereabouts and the time / date, so that he cant dispute it on the courts. Did I hear a divorce lawyer name coming up?
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="How to Catch a Cheater" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-356" height="419" src="http://www.lovedatingdoc.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/how-to-catch-a-cheater-mistake.jpg?2c65a3" width="419"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            How to Catch a Cheater #7: Remote Access of his Webcam
           </h2>
           <p>
            Dont be scared, we are not going to suggest you read 10 books of hacking and cracking in order to activate his webcam remotely. All you have to do is download and install a simple program called Nanny Cams. Find some time when your spouse is not around on his laptop and make sure you run the program. Job done. Next time he has his laptop with him on a business meeting, you will be able to switch on his webcam remotely and see for yourself the ugly truth. Warning, you might not like what youll see, so its better to call a friend to support you the first time you use it. Be prepared for anything.
           </p>
           <p>
            It worked for so many women in the past, its time you utilize this knowledge and find out how to catch a cheater. Technical skills is not what youll need most. Its the inner strength to accept reality and over come the fear of the unknown. If you are becoming suspicious, there is no harm to activate some of these simple tricks and find out the truth about your lying boyfriend.
           </p>
