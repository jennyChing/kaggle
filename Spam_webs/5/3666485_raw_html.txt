                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ElZyuNDCqp4/UYlAcM-he-I/AAAAAAAADCI/o_3z2mwpS5k/s1600/DSC_0748.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Baked Potatoes
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4679724411776294212" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ElZyuNDCqp4/UYlAcM-he-I/AAAAAAAADCI/o_3z2mwpS5k/s1600/DSC_0748.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: center;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ElZyuNDCqp4/UYlAcM-he-I/AAAAAAAADCI/o_3z2mwpS5k/s1600/DSC_0748.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      Ingredients
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      7 red potatoes
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      1 Tbsp olive oil
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      1/2 tsp sea salt
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      1/2 tsp ground pepper
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      1/2 tsp garlic powder
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      1/2 tsp thyme
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      1/2 tsp rosemary
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      Prep time: 5 minutes
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      Stove top cook time: 7 minutes
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      Bake time: 45 minutes
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      Makes 4 servings.
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      Original recipe found
                      <a href="http://www.jamieoliver.com/recipes/vegetables-recipes/perfect-roast-potatoes">
                       here
                      </a>
                      .
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        { STEP 1 }
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cut up red potatoes into 1/2 inch pieces.
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-kkYX74DQS88/UYlAcNHjGII/AAAAAAAADCA/jF5g47xJh_w/s1600/DSC_0713.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: center;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-kkYX74DQS88/UYlAcNHjGII/AAAAAAAADCA/jF5g47xJh_w/s1600/DSC_0713.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        { STEP 2 }
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Place cut potatoes into a pot and cover with water. You want the potatoes to just be covered, do not drown them. Add some salt, cover with lid and bring to a boil. Let boil for 7 minutes.
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wWvZ5N6rKe8/UYlAxnStpTI/AAAAAAAADCc/OueVhRpaLbo/s1600/DSC_0731.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: center;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wWvZ5N6rKe8/UYlAxnStpTI/AAAAAAAADCc/OueVhRpaLbo/s1600/DSC_0731.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        { STEP 3 }
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Drain the potatoes and let sit in a colander for 3 minutes. Shake a few times to get all the water out.
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-_uf4x7sCxIY/UYlAxiRAL0I/AAAAAAAADCY/J0ZgjK5ZzVQ/s1600/DSC_0734.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: center;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-_uf4x7sCxIY/UYlAxiRAL0I/AAAAAAAADCY/J0ZgjK5ZzVQ/s1600/DSC_0734.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        { STEP 4 }
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Place the potatoes on a baking sheet and coat with olive oil. Sprinkle salt, pepper, thyme, rosemary, garlic powder. Shake the baking sheet to make sure everything is coated. Spread the potatoes out so they do not touch each other, this way they will cook more evenly.
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-26a2HtcbXXw/UYlBytxfFRI/AAAAAAAADDA/p50kc5eEh0E/s1600/Fotor0507200056.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-26a2HtcbXXw/UYlBytxfFRI/AAAAAAAADDA/p50kc5eEh0E/s1600/Fotor0507200056.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        { STEP 5 }
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Bake for 40-45 minutes, turning half way through until brown and crispy!
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>
                     <center style="text-align: justify;">
                     </center>

                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-YZz3qAvrgcs/UYlAcUXFmaI/AAAAAAAADCE/cvRVTVwU_Wc/s1600/DSC_0767.jpg"/>
                      </a>
