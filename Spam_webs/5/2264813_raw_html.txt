      </h2>

       <p>
        <a href="http://sunriseboxes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Titos-Vodka-21.jpg">
         <img alt="two piece setup box" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1416" height="403" src="http://sunriseboxes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Titos-Vodka-21.jpg" width="375"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Tito Vodkas product branding reflects the culture of Austin itself. The emphasis of Austin and Texas stars is prevalent throughout the design. For a Two Piece Set Up box, the packaging and product are sure to catch your eye. The outer packaging gives a more tactile experience. When you break the seal to open the box, you reveal the bottle, giving Titos a very high premium feel.
       </p>
       <p>
        Ibelieve the Two Piece Set Up box with this design will go a long way todistinguish Tito Vodkas branding efforts and separate them from the competition.
       </p>
