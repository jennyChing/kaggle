           <p>
            What are two common worries that plague parents? All parents worry about their kids wandering away and getting lost. Nothing is scarier to a parent than turning around and not seeing their child. Parents of kids with food allergies, worry what their kids are served when they are at school or friends homes. The Sync Smartband from ActvContent, will help parents rest easy.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The Sync Smartband
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Smartband is worn on your child's wrist and is connected by low energy Bluetooth to the ActiveContent Sync app in your Apple or Android device.
           </p>
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="3" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense1">

           </div>
           <p class="cb-split">
            The bands come in a choice of five kids colors and have a 2 - 3 month battery life. They are also water resistant.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             How will the Smartband allow me to worry less?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Sync Smartband helps you monitor your child's location and store information on your child's health.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Location
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Set a digital safety zone when out with your kids, and you will be
             <strong>
              alerted
             </strong>
             if they wander outside it. You can set the range for approximately 150 ft outdoors, 50 ft indoors.Any parent will know the horror of going to a park, store or other public place, and not seeing you child, even for a few seconds. The panic that strikes is overwhelming.
            </li>
            <li>
             If somehow your child does manage to wander off (you forgot to set a zone, or he was running, etc.), you can use the
             <strong>
              locator
             </strong>
             to find him.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Health
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Add allergy, emergency contact and all other medical information about your child into the Sync Smartband. Even though your Sync is private, you can give a password to anyone that may need it, such as a babysitter, teacher, daycare provider, etc.
           </p>
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="2" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense2">
           </div>
           <p class="cb-split">
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Other Features of the Sync Smartband
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             You can monitor up to 8 devices at once.
            </li>
            <li>
             You only pay for the Smartband and that is it. No data plan is needed.
            </li>
            <li>
             If your child removes the Sync Smartband, you will be alerted.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Other Uses for the Sync:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            1. The Smartband can be used by adults also. It comes in two sizes, ages 2 to 10 years old, and 11 plus which includes adults. Adults can use it to keep all their medical information. And an even better use would be for very elderly, to not only store all their medical info, but possibly to have their children be able to track them.
           </p>
           <p>
            2. Schools and Daycare Centers - Both schools and daycare centers have to deal with medical and allergy issues all the time. And I know from being a class mother many times on school field trips, it was nerve racking making sure all the kids stayed where they were supposed to be. These Smartbands are a perfect fit for students during the school day.ActvContent is working with schools to make this happen.
           </p>
