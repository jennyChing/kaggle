          <h3 style="text-align: center;">
           25 Healthy Snack Ideas
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           So, how are those New Years resolutions coming along? What about the one you made about getting healthy: did you start? Are you staying on track? Finding things you can eat without feeling deprived?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           If the answer is yes, then congratulations! If the answer is no, well then, join the club. As you know, one of my
           <a href="http://www.remodelaholic.com/2013/01/goals-2013/" target="_blank">
            goals for 2013
           </a>
           is to work off my blogger butt. Easier said than done! Lifestyle changes are H-A-R-D to make, especially when they involve my tastebuds. Since I really do love to eat, the tricky thing for me is to find healthy foods that are filling and flavorful so that I dont feel like Im missing out. Ive been making a list of tasty, nutritious snacks that I can munch on during the day, and Im happy to share it with you. Here, youll find all kinds of snacks, from nuts and veggie chips to delicious popsicles!
          </p>
