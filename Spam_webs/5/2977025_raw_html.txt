      <h1>
       <a href="http://www.aishakristine.com/events/how-to-host-an-amazing-cocktail-party.html">
        How to Host an Amazing Cocktail Party
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="aishaentry">
       <center>
        <a href="http://www.aishakristine.com/sponsorship-details.html">
         <img data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.aishakristine.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Ad-Space-Ad.png"/>
        </a>
       </center>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         If you want to gather people together  family, friends or business colleagues  but you dont want to organize a large, full-scale dinner party, then a cocktail party is a great solution. You can time it for the early part of an evening and expect it to be over in two to three hours. Here are a few tips on how to make sure your guests have a great time and are delighted by your most stunning social event of the year.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="how-to-host-an-amazing-cocktail-party" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9652" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.aishakristine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/how-to-host-an-amazing-cocktail-party.png?w=700"/>
         <br/>
         <span id="more-9651">
         </span>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Venue
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Depending on the season and available space, you can opt for a garden party held outdoors on the lawn. This is generally a less formal affair in terms of dress code, as people are likely to be seated on garden furniture rather than fine upholstery, so bear this in mind when planning your invitations. Always have a backup plan in case the weather takes a turn for the worse. If youre holing a party indoors, make sure you choose a room or rooms with sufficient space for the number of guests, as cramped conditions are uncomfortable and will not be appreciated.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Cocktail-Party-Venue-Ideas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9660" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.aishakristine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Cocktail-Party-Venue-Ideas.jpg?w=700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Food
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Most cocktail party hosts provide snacks to accompany drinks along the lines of tapas  small savory dishes typically served with drinks in a Spanish bar. This is a good idea if your party is pre-dinner. Its possible to find or make a range of simple yet tempting appetizers that look attractive and taste delicious. Popular, stylish hors doeuvres often include finger foods incorporating smoked salmon, asparagus tips, cheese or fruit. It is worth making sure that some snacks are wholly vegetarian or vegan, to take into account the dietary requirements of those you know as well as any unexpected, last-minute additions to the guest list.
        </p>
        <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
         <br/>
         <center>
          <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

          </ins>

          </script>
         </center>
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="gourmet-cupcakes-to-try" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9657" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.aishakristine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/gourmet-cupcakes-to-try.jpg?w=700"/>
        </p>
        <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
         <center>
          <div class="pf_ads" id="pf_43352">

          </div>
         </center>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Drinks
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the fun things to do when planning a cocktail party is to decide in advance which cocktails you will supply while also making sure that you have some adventurous options for teetotalers. You can then create a menu listing the drinks and their ingredients and encourage your guests to try something new. For example, a Manhattan uses bourbon or rye whiskey, sweet vermouth and a cherry garnish. If you prefer to use Canadian whisky, look for a label spelled whisky without the e and youll know you have the genuine version rather than a substitute.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Cocktail-Drinks-for-Parties" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9658" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.aishakristine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Cocktail-Drinks-for-Parties.jpg?resize=700%2C350"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         In fact, the quality of the alcohol you serve is of central importance at a cocktail party  skimp on this at your peril, as it may result in drinks that will disappoint and ultimately spoil the effect you have gone to such trouble to achieve. Dont use any old white rum in a
         <a href="http://www.bacardilimited.com/our-heritage/the-early-years" target="_blank">
          Bacardi Cuba
         </a>
          it wont be the real thing unless you choose Bacardi.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Atmosphere
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Finally, give some thought to the atmosphere you want to create and adjust the music and lighting to enhance this effect. Soft background music usually works well, or even a guest piano player if space permits. You wont, for instance, get a civilized, professional networking event with blaring sound and flashing lights.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Cocktail-Party-Ideas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9656" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.aishakristine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Cocktail-Party-Ideas.jpg?resize=700%2C400"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         When all your preparations are done, you can adopt the role of the attentive host and relax, knowing your cocktail party is a success.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
