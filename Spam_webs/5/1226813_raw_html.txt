     <h1>
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/31/disney-finds-its-prince-for-maleficent/" rel="bookmark">
       Disney Finds Its Prince For Maleficent
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="authmetasingle">
      Posted
      <abbr class="timeago" title="2012-05-31 14:00:09Z">
       Thursday, May 31st, 2012 10:00 am GMT -4
      </abbr>
      by
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/author/janice-kay/" rel="author" title="Posts by Janice Kay">
       Janice Kay
      </a>
      <span class="sharetwhm">
       <span class="st_twitter_hcount" st_title="Disney Finds Its Prince For Maleficent" st_url="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/31/disney-finds-its-prince-for-maleficent/" st_username="ScienceFiction" st_via="ScienceFiction">
       </span>
       <span class="st_facebook_hcount" st_title="Disney Finds Its Prince For Maleficent" st_url="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/31/disney-finds-its-prince-for-maleficent/">
       </span>
       <span class="st_sharethis_hcount" st_title="Disney Finds Its Prince For Maleficent" st_url="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/31/disney-finds-its-prince-for-maleficent/">
       </span>
      </span>
     </div>
     <div style="clear:both;">
     </div>
     <p>
      <img alt="Brenton Thwaites" class="alignright wp-image-64377" height="236" src="http://cdn1.sciencefiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/Brenton-Thwaites.png" style="margin: 8px;" title="Brenton Thwaites" width="339"/>
      With filming to begin soon, there was one person who was missing in the tale of 
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/02/13/disneys-maleficent-film-is-next-up-for-angelina-jolie/" target="_blank">
       Maleficent
      </a>
       and that is the Prince who awakens Princess Aurora with a kiss. It now looks like her prince has come in the form of Aussie actor Brenton Thwaites.
     </p>
     <p>
      Thwaites is well known on Australian TV for his role as Stu Henderson in the series Home and Away, but he will soon be seen on the American screen in the upcoming Lifetime movie remake of The Blue Lagoon. (Yes, you read right! The same movie that starred Brooke Shields and Christopher Atkins 32 years ago.)
     </p>
     <p>
      Thwaites will be joining an impressive cast with
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/02/13/disneys-maleficent-film-is-next-up-for-angelina-jolie/">
       Angelina Jolie
      </a>
      in the title role of evil fairy Maleficent and
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/03/09/elle-fanning-in-negotiations-to-star-opposite-angelina-jolie-in-maleficent/">
       Elle Fanning
      </a>
      as his princess in sleeping er waiting. Also cast in the film is
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/08/disneys-maleficent-adds-6-more-to-join-angelina-jolie-and-elle-fanning/">
       Imelda Staunton
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/08/disneys-maleficent-adds-6-more-to-join-angelina-jolie-and-elle-fanning/">
       Miranda Richardson
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/08/disneys-maleficent-adds-6-more-to-join-angelina-jolie-and-elle-fanning/">
       Sam Riley
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/08/disneys-maleficent-adds-6-more-to-join-angelina-jolie-and-elle-fanning/">
       Lesley Manville
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/08/disneys-maleficent-adds-6-more-to-join-angelina-jolie-and-elle-fanning/">
       Kenneth Cranham
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/08/disneys-maleficent-adds-6-more-to-join-angelina-jolie-and-elle-fanning/">
       India Eisley
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Still in the pre-production stage, Maleficent sounds like Disney is planning a film worthy of the fairy tale and taking a page out of Wicked by telling the story from the evil fairys point of view. If this film is half as good as 
      <a href="http://sciencefiction.com/2012/05/19/snow-white-and-the-huntsman-featurette-centers-on-charlize-therons-evilness/" target="_blank">
       Snow White and the Huntsman
      </a>
       seems to be, there may be need of an evil sorceress run off. Queen Ravenna or Maleficent?Whos the evilest one of all?
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Maleficent is slated to be released on March 14, 2014.
      </strong>
     </p>
