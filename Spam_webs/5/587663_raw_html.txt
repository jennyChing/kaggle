        A Virtual Trip to Lugoj
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">

        <div style="float: right; margin-right: 10px;">
         <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="vertical" data-hashtags="Banat,features,lugoj,Timis+River" data-url="http://www.rounite.com/2009/05/03/lugoj/" data-via="argophilia" href="https://twitter.com/share">

        <p>
         Lugoj, is a city in Timi County, Banat, western Romania, situated on both banks of the Timi River. The river divides the city in two quarters: the Romanian on the right and the German on the left.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.rounite.com/2009/05/03/lugoj/lugoj-view-from-the-timis-river/" rel="attachment wp-att-294">
          <img alt="Lugoj, view from the Timis River." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-294" height="240" src="http://www.rounite.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/lugoj-view-from-the-timis-river.png" title="Lugoj, view from the Timis River." width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The city was the home town of famous Dracula actor
         <a href="http://www.rounite.com/2008/08/20/romanian-americans/">
          Bela Lugosi
         </a>
         <u style="display:none">
          <a href="http://thesevenyearplan.com/?movie_commando">
           Commando buy
          </a>
         </u>
         , a Romanian-Hungarian native, whose real name was Blasko. Lugosi is a derivative of Lugos, the towns Hungarian name.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.rounite.com/2009/05/03/lugoj/bela-lugosi/" rel="attachment wp-att-295">
          <img alt="Bela Lugosi" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-295" height="240" src="http://www.rounite.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/bela-lugosi.png" title="Bela Lugosi" width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lugoj is inhabited by Romanians, Hungarians, Germans, Roma and Ukrainians and other nationalities  with a 82.9 percentage of Romanian inhabitants.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.rounite.com/2009/05/03/lugoj/lugoj/" rel="attachment wp-att-296">
          <img alt="Lugoj, view of the center." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-296" height="240" src="http://www.rounite.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/lugoj.png" title="Lugoj, view of the center." width="590"/>
         </a>
         <strong style="display:none">
          <a href="http://chessasia.net/?in_her_shoes">
           In Her Shoes dvd
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lugoj is a beautiful, quiet little city, home of the Eparchy of Lugoj, an important religious establishment for the Romanian Church United with Rome, Greek-Orthodox.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.rounite.com/2009/05/03/lugoj/lugoj-church/" rel="attachment wp-att-297">
          <img alt="Church in Lugoj" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-297" height="240" src="http://www.rounite.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/lugoj-church.png" title="Church in Lugoj" width="590"/>
         </a>
         <strong style="display:none">
          <a href="http://johnquiggin.com/?summer_school">
           Summer School movies
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         The images below were offered by our StumbleUpon friend
         <a href="http://rotariu.stumbleupon.com/">
          Sebastian Rotariu
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <form style="display:none">
         <a href="http://interactivehug.com/?insomnia">

        </p>
