           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/about-me/disclosure-policy/" target="_blank" title="Disclosure Policy">
            May contain affiliate links.
           </a>
          </h6>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-033.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 033" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23015" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-033-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           Chocolate banana bread, whats not to like? I love just about anything with chocolate, add some bananas and it makes it seem a little healthier, right? Oh wellthats what I tell myself when a eat a big slice for breakfast. Actually in this recipe which I found here. I substituted the cocoa powder with
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Foods-Alive-Organic-Carob-Powder/dp/B00HEY0OW4/ref=sr_1_6?s=grocery&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1415705299&amp;sr=1-6&amp;keywords=carob+powder" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Carob powder
           </a>
           . Never heard of it? I won a contest over at
           <a href="http://www.itsaharleyyylife.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Its a Harlyyy Life
           </a>
           and one of the products was
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Foods-Alive-Organic-Carob-Powder/dp/B00HEY0OW4/ref=sr_1_6?s=grocery&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1415705299&amp;sr=1-6&amp;keywords=carob+powder" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Carob Powder
           </a>
           . I had never heard of it. So I did a Google search and found out what an amazing product this is. I was skeptical of course. I have heard this song and dance before. Sure it tastes like chocolate.
           <img alt=";)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif"/>
           I was pleasantly surprised. It is as good and obviously much better for you as cocoa powder but without all the negatives like caffeine and theobromine Read more about the health benefits
           <a href="http://www.gilead.net/health/carob.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
           . I was quite amazed, no one was able to tell the difference. Its definitely going to be a staple in my home.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           Youll need:
          </h3>
          <h3>
           1/2 cup butter, softened
           <br/>
           1 cup
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/sucanat/" target="_blank" title="Sucanat">
            Sucana
           </a>
           t/sugar
           <br/>
           2 eggs
           <br/>
           3 bananas mashed
           <br/>
           1 teaspoon vanilla extract
           <br/>
           1/1/2 cups whole wheat flour/white flour
           <br/>
           1 teaspoon baking soda,
           <br/>
           2 tablespoon
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Foods-Alive-Organic-Carob-Powder/dp/B00HEY0OW4/ref=sr_1_6?s=grocery&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1415705299&amp;sr=1-6&amp;keywords=carob+powder" target="_blank">
            carob powder
           </a>
           / unsweetened cocoa
           <br/>
           1/2 cup sour cream light
           <br/>
           1/2 cup chocolate chips
          </h3>
          <h3>
           In a bowl cream your butter, sugar, eggs and bananas together.Add vanilla extract and mix in.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-003.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 003" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23008" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-003-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Sift in the flour, baking soda, cocoa powder.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-005.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 005" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23010" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-005-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Mix until well blended.
           <br/>
           Stir in sour cream.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-008.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 008" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23011" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-008-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           Fold in chocolate chips.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-009.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 009" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23012" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-009-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Pour into a greased 95 loaf pan.
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-012.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 012" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23013" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-012-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Bake in a preheated 350 F for 1 hour or a inserted toothpick comes out clean.
           <br/>
           Cool and eat.
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-032.jpg">
            <img alt="Chocolate Banana Bread 032" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-23014" height="483" src="http://mamaldiane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chocolate-Banana-Bread-032-1024x768.jpg" width="644"/>
           </a>
          </p>
