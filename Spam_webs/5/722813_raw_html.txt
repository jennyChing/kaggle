     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright" height="309" src="http://www.whatdoesitmean.com/mgc1.jpg" width="280"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      A grim report prepared by Commander-in-Chief of the Air Force
      <a href="http://eng.mil.ru/en/structure/forces/air/management.htm" target="_blank">
       Lieutenant-General Viktor Bondarev
      </a>
      on the just completed scientific mission of North America carried out by
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tupolev_Tu-95" target="_blank">
       4 Tupolev Tu-95 strategic aircraft
      </a>
      and
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ilyushin_Il-78" target="_blank">
       2 Ilyushin Il-78 aerial refueling tankers
      </a>
      that electronically swept for magnetic anomalies from Alaska to California warns that a catastrophic event may be nearing for this region.
     </p>
     <p>
      US officials, it should be noted, characterized this purely scientific mission as a 
      <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/russian-bombers-california-2014-6" target="_blank">
       bombing run
      </a>
       that came within 50 miles of California, but which their Air Forces were able to repel by their launching of F-15 fighter jets.
     </p>
     <p>
      This report, however, states that this scientific mission was necessitated by a severe mysterious magnetic anomaly detected by the
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kosmos_2473" target="_blank">
       Kosmos 2473
      </a>
      satellite on
      <a href="http://helenair.com/news/local/swarm-of-earthquakes-in-yellowstone-park/article_2f4833d7-7b0b-55ce-8ab4-1839da7dee20.html" target="_blank">
       3 June occurring in the Yellowstone region
      </a>
      of the Western United States which resulted in what is called an earthquake swarm.
     </p>
     <p>
      Most important to note about the 3 June Yellowstone magnetic anomaly, this report continues, are that satellite measurements show it being precipitated by the
      <a href="http://www.alaskapublic.org/2014/06/09/earthquakes-swarm-the-brooks-range/" target="_blank">
       mysterious earthquake swarm hitting the Brooks Range mountains in Alaska
      </a>
      , and which seismologists are
      <a href="http://www.alaskapublic.org/2014/06/09/earthquakes-swarm-the-brooks-range/" target="_blank">
       still at a loss to explain
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      The information relating to the linking of these two events, this report says, was further verified by the United States Geological Survey (
      <a href="http://www.usgs.gov/" target="_blank">
       USGS
      </a>
      )
      <a href="http://mrdata.usgs.gov/magnetic/" target="_blank">
       magnetic anomaly maps and data for North America
      </a>
      showing a strange magnetic disturbance/ripple emanating from
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brooks_Range" target="_blank">
       Brooks Range
      </a>
      and ending at Yellowstone on 3 June, both of these areas, it is important to note, being part of the
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rocky_Mountains" target="_blank">
       Rocky Mountains
      </a>
      that stretch more than 4,830 km (3,000 miles) from the northernmost part of British Columbia, in western Canada, to New Mexico, in the southwestern United States.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright" height="130" src="http://www.whatdoesitmean.com/mgc2.jpg" width="280"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Of grave concern to Russian military authorities relating to these events, General Bondarev says in his report, was the catastrophic effect they had on the advanced 
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnetoception" target="_blank">
       magnetoception
      </a>
      
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inertial_navigation_system" target="_blank">
       inertial navigation systems
      </a>
      employed by many US-NATO-Russian warplanes which use these highly sophisticated aircraft flight devices.
     </p>
     <p>
      Though no Russia military aircraft were near the disturbed magnetic zone emanating our from Yellowstone on 3 June, this report says, two US military aircraft were at its boundaries in the Southern California region on 4 June while this event was still active causing them both to crash.
     </p>
     <p>
      The two
      <a href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2014/06/04/military-jet-crash-california/9989629/" target="_blank">
       US fighter jets crashing on 4 June, this report continues, were identified
      </a>
      as a US Navy F-A-183 that went down when the pilot was attempting to land aboard the carrier Carl Vinson, and a US Marine Harrier AV-8B jet that crashed into a residential community in Imperial, about 90 miles east of San Diego, both of them occurring within hours of each other.
     </p>
     <p>
      This report notes that no civilian aircraft would have been affected by this magnetic anomaly as only the most advanced military aircraft employ these geomagnetic-satellite coordinated flight systems which enable them to hug the terrain not unlike the
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnetoception" target="_blank">
       magnetic systems used by birds and insects to navigate
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright" height="136" src="http://www.whatdoesitmean.com/mgc3.jpg" width="280"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Russian concerns relating to magnetic anomalies, it is important to note, are related to the
      <a href="http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2005/12/1215_051215_north_pole.html" target="_blank">
       rapidly shifting north magnetic pole
      </a>
      which since 2005 has been moving at a rate of 40 kilometers (25 miles) a year from Arctic Canada toward Siberia.
     </p>
     <p>
      Frightening
      <a href="http://www.zengardner.com/the-shift-is-on-magnetic-north-pole-now-racing-a-mile-a-day/" target="_blank">
       independent research
      </a>
      from last year (2013) further warns that this shift is still picking up speed and according to this researcher should reach Siberia in at least within 2 years. [See video
      <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nbh9VI2PL0I" target="_blank">
       HERE
      </a>
      (banned in US)]
     </p>
     <p>
      One of the effects of the rapidly shifting magnetic north pole being noticed the most, this report notes, are the airport runway systems being disrupted because of it, and as we can read one such 2011
      <a href="http://www.infowars.com/magnetic-north-pole-shifts-forces-runway-closures-at-florida-airport/" target="_blank">
       example which occurred in the US
      </a>
      :
     </p>
     <p>
      Tampa International Airport was forced to readjust its runways Thursday to account for the movement of the Earths magnetic fields, information that pilots rely upon to navigate planes. Thanks to the fluctuations in the force, the airport has closed its primary runway until Jan. 13 to change taxiway signs to account for the shift, the Federal Aviation Administration said.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright" height="161" src="http://www.whatdoesitmean.com/mgc4.jpg" width="280"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      The poles are generated by movements within the Earths inner and outer cores, though the exact process isnt exactly understood. Theyre also constantly in flux, moving a few degrees every year, but the changes are almost never of such a magnitude that runways require adjusting, said Paul Takemoto, a spokesman for the FAA.
     </p>
     <p>
