      <h2 class="head headline2 mg-1">
       Instructions
      </h2>
      <ol>
       <li>
        <p>
         Attach one or two strands of yarn on to your crochet hook. Double strands will create a heavier, warmer blanket.
        </p>
        <div class="clearfix">
         <figure>
          <a data-href="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/720x960/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/70/4/242774.jpg" data-modal-content="" data-modal-credit="" data-type="modal">
           <img class="mg-4 fl" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/98x65-01/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/70/4/242774.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Make a chain of four stitches.
        </p>
        <div class="clearfix">
         <figure>
          <a data-href="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/720x960/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/70/6/242776.jpg" data-modal-content="" data-modal-credit="" data-type="modal">
           <img class="mg-4 fl" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/98x65-01/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/70/6/242776.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Join with slip stitch to form a ring.
        </p>
        <div class="clearfix">
         <figure>
          <a data-href="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/720x960/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/70/8/242778.jpg" data-modal-content="" data-modal-credit="" data-type="modal">
           <img class="mg-4 fl" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/98x65-01/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/70/8/242778.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Crochet eight single crochets in the ring. Slip stitch to join.
        </p>
        <div class="clearfix">
         <figure>
          <a data-href="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/720x960/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/80/1/242781.jpg" data-modal-content="" data-modal-credit="" data-type="modal">
           <img class="mg-4 fl" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/98x65-01/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/80/1/242781.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Crochet two single crochets in each single crochet around. Slip stitch to join.
        </p>
        <div class="clearfix">
         <figure>
          <a data-href="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/720x960/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/80/3/242783.jpg" data-modal-content="" data-modal-credit="" data-type="modal">
           <img class="mg-4 fl" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/98x65-01/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/80/3/242783.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Crochet one single crochet in each single crochet around. Slip stitch to join.
        </p>
        <div class="clearfix">
         <figure>
          <a data-href="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/720x960/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/80/4/242784.jpg" data-modal-content="" data-modal-credit="" data-type="modal">
           <img class="mg-4 fl" src="//img-aws.ehowcdn.com/98x65-01/ds-cdn-write/upload/2000/700/80/4/242784.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Continue crocheting Step 6 and step 7 until the blanket is the size that you want.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Make a slip stitch in every single crochet around the outer edge of the blanket. Cut the yarn about three inches from the last slip stitch. Weave the end into the blanket to hide it.
        </p>
       </li>
      </ol>
