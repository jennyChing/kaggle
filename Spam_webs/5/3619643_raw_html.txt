         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Delicate Pressed Flower Sculptures by Ignacio Canales Aracil
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content content">
         <p>
          This collection of pressed flower sculptures is byIgnacio Canales Aracil.He creates each
          <a href="http://inspirationhut.net/tag/sculpture/" target="_blank">
           sculpture
          </a>
          by continually tightening the drying wild flowers around a sculptural base, this process takes months of work anda lot of training. Ignacio's delicate creations areincluded in a group exhibition at theGalera Luca Mendoza in Madrid until the end of February 2015.
