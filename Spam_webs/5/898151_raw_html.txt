        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Fourth of July Cupcake Recipe
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-05-29T16:35:59+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 29, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Katie
           </span>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <em>
          Are you looking for a Fourth of July dessert recipe that reminds you of fireworks?! Be sure to check out these firecracker Fourth of July cupcakes. The secret ingredient in the frosting is something your guests will love!
         </em>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Fourth of July Cupcakes Recipe" class="aligncenter wp-image-16556 size-full" height="900" src="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/firecracker-cupcakes-5-of-10.jpg" title="Fourth of July Cupcakes Recipe" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <link href="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/firecracker-cupcakes-5-of-10.jpg" itemprop="image"/>

          <tr>
           <td>
            <span class="item ERName" itemprop="name">
             Fourth of July Cupcake Recipe

         <div class="ERIngredients">
          <div class="ERIngredientsHeader ERHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul class="ingredients">
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 box vanilla cake mix**
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 small bag of red Pop Rocks
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 small bag of Blue Pop Rocks
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tablespoons milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tablespoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2.5 cups powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2.5 sticks butter, softened
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="ERInstructions">
          <div class="ERInstructionsHeader ERHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol class="instructions">
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Prepare vanilla cake mix according to box instructions for cupcakes. Let cool completely before cooling.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine all ingredients (except for pop rocks) in a bowl and mix with a hand mixer or stand mixer until smooth and creamy.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Divide buttercream into two different bowls. Mix blue pop rocks in with one bowl and red with another.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Frost cupcakes and enjoy!
           </li>
          </ol>
         </div>
         <div class="endeasyrecipe" style="display: none" title="style000">
          3.3.3070
         </div>
        </div>
        <br/>
        <p>
         I always enjoy Fourth of July  its a great reason to celebrate the freedoms we enjoy, those who protect them for us, as well as to enjoy some fun time outdoors BBQing, swimming, and watching fireworks!
        </p>
        <p>
         Last year we were visiting my sister in Rexburg, and we had so much fun going to a local parade and then watching fireworks on her trampoline. The summer before that, we were visiting my parents in Colorado, and we enjoyed a yummy breakfast with a bunch of family members.
        </p>
        <p>
         This year we are going to be in Lewiston, Idaho, visiting my grandparents and to help celebrate my grandpas birthday. Its quite a long drive up there, but we are getting really excited to go. It will be a fun weekend with my parents, little brother, and a few other members of our extended family.
        </p>
        <p>
         Wherever we end up spending Fourth of July, without a doubt there will be yummy food. We typically have the traditional fare of hotdogs, hamburgers, and potato salad, along with fresh fruit like watermelon. However, the highlight for me is always the dessert. I enjoy coming up with a fun and yummy idea that is patriotic (last year I loved making this
         <a href="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/recipe/red-white-and-blue-fruit-pizza/" target="_blank">
          Red, White and Blue Fruit Pizza
         </a>
          so yummy.)
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Firecracker Cupcakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16555" height="400" src="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/firecracker-cupcakes-4-of-10.jpg" title="Firecracker Cupcakes" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         A few days ago, I decided to try and experiment with a few ideas, and I came up with this firecracker cupcakes. Why are their firecracker? Well, the frosting has red and blue pop rocks in them! I tried putting them in the batter as well, but that didnt work out as well. But the pop rocks in the frosting were a fun surprise. I just made these with a vanilla cake mix (though I doctored it up a little bit by using butter instead of oil, and one more egg than the recipe called for), but the frosting was to DIE for! Seriously so yummy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Vanilla Cake Mix" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16553" height="900" src="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/firecracker-cupcakes-2-of-10.jpg" title="Vanilla Cake Mix" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         So if you are looking for a tasty Fourth of July dessert recipe, definitely consider this one! We made them when my brothers family came over, and they disappeared quickly!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Fourth of July Cupcakes Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16559" height="400" src="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/firecracker-cupcakes-8-of-10.jpg" title="July Dessert Recipe" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres another great Fourth of July recipe that uses Pop Rocks 
         <a href="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/recipe/red-white-and-blue-popcorn/" target="_blank">
          Red, White, and Blue Popcorn
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Fourth of July Cupcakes Recipe" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16567" height="1024" src="http://www.clarkscondensed.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/redwhiteandblue2-768x1024-768x1024.jpg" title="Fourth of July recipe" width="768"/>
        </p>
