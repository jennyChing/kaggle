         <header class="entry-header">
          <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
           DIY Mercury Glass Votive
          </h1>
          <p class="entry-meta">
          </p>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Im all about the faux, bout the faux, no real.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Im all about the faux bout the faux, no real.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Sorry I cant get that song out of my mind.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I am sure a few of you know which song.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Anyway it seems I am sharing lots of tutorials lately on how to make faux projects.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Like my
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/2015/02/diy-chalky-paint-porcelain-roses/">
            faux porcelain roses
           </a>
           , or my
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/2015/02/diy-shabby-tole-chandelier/">
            faux tole chandelier
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Guess what?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Today I am going to show you how to make faux mercury glass
           <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           With this DIY mercury glass votive tutorial.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/faux-mercury-glass-votive.jpg">
            <img alt="faux mercury glass votive" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8872" height="900" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/faux-mercury-glass-votive.jpg" width="574"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Well I actually shared something similar a month ago, when I shared how to use silver leaf on a glass table.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           You can read about that
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/2015/01/add-silver-leaf-glass/">
            HERE
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I am sharing this very simple project because I want to show you how easy it is to create something from nothing.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Open your eyes to what is around you while you are thrift shopping or at a garage sale.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           These votives, well actually only one is a votive the other is a brandy snifter.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Total cost for both .50!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Yes the silver leaf is a little pricey, but you can make a lot with very little.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Let me show you.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Lets star with the supplies.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Supplies
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           -Candle votive
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           -Silver leaf 3 sheets ( I am using imitation silver leaf sold as 5.5. x 5.5 sheet sizes)
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
            Metal Leaf Adhesive
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           -Sponge brush
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           -Metal Leaf brush ( I used a disposable stain brush)
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           All supplies can be purchased at a craft store.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Supplies below are optional:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           -Muddy Pond Wax by Heirloom traditions or Antiquing medium.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            Before we get started lets talk safety.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            ** I dont know if this is flammable, if you decide to use a real candle with this you are at your own risk. Follow manufacturers instructions, and please read my disclosure policy**
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-1-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (1 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8854" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-1-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Are you ready to get started?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           This is super easy, but a little messy.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           First apply the metal leaf adhesive to your votives, using a spong brush.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           This will ruin a good brush, trust me use a sponge brush.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I am sharing this from experience.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-2-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (2 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8855" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-2-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Let this fully dry.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           When its dry, the adhesive will look opaque.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-3-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (3 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8857" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-3-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Next its time to start applying your silver leaf.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I dont like perfection, so I carefully pick up each piece and place it on in no particular fashion.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Dont do this near a fan, open window, or breeze, this stuff will fly everywhere!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Try not to breath..just kiddingsort of.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           You will see once you work with it how delicate it is.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-8-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (8 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8856" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-8-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Take your leaf brush (or in my case I used a disposable stain brush), and start lightly brushing off the excess.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Dont worry about doing anything wrong, you cant.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Just have fun and create!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           After you cover your whole piece you are done!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           You can spray on a silver leaf finish spray thats supposed to protect it, but I always skip that step since it is flammable and stinky.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-12-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (12 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8858" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-12-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           At this point you are done.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Guess what?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I couldnt leave it alone, I wanted it to look a little more aged with a little patina.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           So, I first took out my antiquing medium.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-14-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (14 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8859" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-14-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I applied a small amount, a pea size amount, to an old t-shirt rag and wiped it on my votive.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Hmmmm not enough patina, so next I pulled out my Heirloom Traditions wax.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Remember I dont follow rules, I just create.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-15-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (15 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8860" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-15-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           With my t-shirt rag I added just a touch of wax to my votive.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-16-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (16 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8861" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-16-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Now I am starting to see a little patina.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           And a little on my hands too.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I kept working at it until I was happy with the look I wanted.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           And secretly even though I am finished with these, I may go back and add a little more.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Do you do that?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Go back and tweak things?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Sometimes I cant leave things alone I just keep tweaking until I get the look I want.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           So here is the finished two pieces.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-20-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (20 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8862" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-20-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           In that brandy snifter/votive I have a faux candle.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           They make really pretty and realistic looking faux candles now.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I love using faux votives over real votives.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I never have to worry about my house burning down if I forget about it.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I always remember to blow out my scented candles, but I worry that I will forget about a candle votive.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-23-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (23 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8863" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-23-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Not sure I like this size faux candle in my shorter votive.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Well, lets take a closer look at the patina.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-26-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (26 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8864" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-26-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Isnt that pretty!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I love how perfectly imperfect it looks.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-27-of-35.jpg">
            <img alt="Silver Leaf mercury glass candle votive (27 of 35)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8865" height="506" src="http://www.whitelacecottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Silver-Leaf-mercury-glass-candle-votive-27-of-35.jpg" width="900"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           This brandy snifter makes a perfect candle votive dont you think?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
