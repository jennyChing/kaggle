       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Homemade Coconut Salt Scrub
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-09-14T05:58:31+00:00">
         September 14, 2012
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://wonkywonderful.com/author/nicole-harris/" rel="author" title="Nicole Harris">
           Nicole Harris
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://wonkywonderful.com/homemade-coconut-salt-scrub/#comments">
          82 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="16287961" data-link="http://wonkywonderful.com/homemade-coconut-salt-scrub/" data-summary="" data-title="Homemade Coconut Salt Scrub">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          My Dirty Little Secret . . . I Have Dry, Cracked, Nasty Old Hag Feet!
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          In my defense, I live in flip-flops 8 months out of the year and having sexy feet is not high on my priority list.
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          I attempted to get a picture of my cracked heels to prove just how neglected my feet truly are. But, do you know how impossible it is to take a picture of your own feet without looking like a hobbit? It is probably just as well that I could not get a decent picture. That is an image you do not want to see.
         </span>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/5723481_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/5723481_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 500px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
           <span style="font-size: 13px;">
            Well, it is time to take care of my little problem. I have noticed DIY Scrubs on
           </span>
           <a href="http://pinterest.com/wonkywonderful/" style="font-size: 13px;">
            Pinterest
           </a>
           <span style="font-size: 13px;">
            and just how simple they are to make. Its just a matter of deciding if you want a salt or sugar scrub and what type of oil and essential oil scent you prefer.
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/3525562_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/3525562_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 550px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         I chose to make a Salt Scrub with Coconut Oil and Coconut Scent. I also used Vitamin E Oil because I have a ton of it stockpiled from my pregnancy. Apparently I thought that I would need to bathe in Vitamin E to prevent stretchmarks.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/4697901_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/4697901_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 550px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Just mix your Course Sea Salt or Sugar with your Oils at a 2:1 ratio. For example I mixed 1 cup Salt with 1/4 cup Coconut Oil and 1/4 cup Vitamin E Oil. If you are only using one oil mix 1/2 cup Oil with 1 cup Salt. Then add 3-4 drops of Essential Oil for fragrance and stir it up.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/114903_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/114903_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 550px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Put your scrub into a container and youre done. I Know, Right?  Easy Peasy!
         <br/>
         And it is perfect for a quick and cheap last minute gift. Put it in a jar, tie a ribbon around it, attach a label and you have a fancy and luxurious gift.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/5079866_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/5079866_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 550px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Store bought scrubs can cost ten to twenty dollars or more. This comes out to about two or three bucks depending on the ingredients and that is including the cost of the jar. Luckily none of my family and friends read my blog  because guess what they are getting for Christmas and Birthday presents this year!
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/8800467_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/8800467_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 550px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         This also works great as a Shower Body Scrub too. Just be sure to scrub the heck out of your bathtub afterwards so nobody slips and breaks a hip.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div style="padding-top: 10px; padding-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0; margin-right: 0; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/8761653_orig.png" rel="lightbox">
           <img alt="Homemade Coconut #SaltScrub - wonkywonderful.com" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/8761653_orig.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 500px;"/>
          </a>
          <div style="display: block; font-size: 90%;">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         So there you have it.
         <br/>
         My once Hideous Hobbit Feet are now Sexy and Silky Smooth.OhLaLa.
        </div>

            <p>
             Hi Alexis! I would recommend melting the coconut oil just enough to make it liquid. That way it is easier to mix with the salt. It will become solid again and the scrub will become like a paste. You can either use it that way, place your jar into a bowl of warm water before use or substitute some of the coconut oil for a liquid oil such as olive or grapeseed. I hope you enjoy!
