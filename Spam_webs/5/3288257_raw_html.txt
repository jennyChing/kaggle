      <h2>
       Celebrity Mii contest!
       <span class="date">
        DEC 06 2006
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The
       <a href="http://wii.nintendo.com/">
        Nintendo Wii
       </a>
       includes a nifty editor for making the avatars that you play with, which are called Miis.
       <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rr3ops-eFT8">
        Here's a video demonstrating how the editor works
       </a>
       . The editor is suprisingly powerful for how simple it is and almost right away, people began
       <a href="http://gamevideos.com/video/id/7638">
        making celebrity Miis
       </a>
       ...early efforts included Michael Jackson and Liza Minnelli. Some of the best celebrity Miis I've seen are
       <a href="http://flickr.com/photos/peepeehead/315370082/">
        Spike Lee
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://flickr.com/photos/daveandfaye/314594065/">
        Borat
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://flickr.com/photos/daveandfaye/314593850/">
        Steve Martin
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://flickr.com/photos/daveandfaye/314593478/">
        Amy Sedaris as Jerri Blank
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="http://www.thewiiexperience.com/charlie-brown-mii-neo-mii-gw-mii-and-more/">
        Charlie Brown
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Meg and I set out to do a John Lennon Mii last nght, but as soon as we saw these eyes, we switched to Paul McCartney:
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://flickr.com/photos/megnut/315340358/">
        <img alt="Paul Mccartney Mii" height="315" src="http://also.kottke.org/misc/images/paul-mccartney-mii.jpg" width="420"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Not too shabby for a few minutes work, but I know you can do better. So, I'm having a contest to see who can make the best celebrity Mii. The rules are as follows:
      </p>
      <p>
       1. All Miis must be made with the Nintendo Wii editor, not
       <a href="http://www.joystiq.com/2006/10/02/roll-your-own-mii-pre-wii/">
        this Flash editor
       </a>
       (which is cool, but not the same).
      </p>
      <p>
       2. No cheating! Make your own Mii, don't just copy someone else's.
      </p>
      <p>
       3. I love your mom, but she's not a celebrity. Frances Bean, you can ignore this rule.
      </p>
      <p>
       4. You retain exclusive worldwide rights to your Mii and its image, save for giving me permission to post it on kottke.org as part of the contest.
      </p>
      <p>
       5. Judging will be done by me and possibly a panel of "celebrity" judges if I can scrounge some up. The family and friends of the judges can enter, but will be held to much higher standards than everyone else, just as in real life.
      </p>
      <p>
       6. Only two entries per person. (And don't enter two in your own name and then have your friend email in two more. Pick your best two, send 'em in, and take your chances.)
      </p>
      <p>
       7.
       <strike>
        Entry deadline is Monday, December 11th at 11:59 pm ET.
       </strike>
       The entry deadline has been moved to Wednesday, December 13th at 11:59 pm ET. I will announce the winner at some time shortly after that.
      </p>
      <p>
       To enter, make your Mii, take a photo of it on the screen (make sure the Mii is clearly visible in the photo), and send a link to the photo to
       <a href="mailto:jason@kottke.org?Subject=Celebrity Mii Contest">
        jason@kottke.org with a subject line of "Celebrity Mii Contest"
       </a>
       (no quotes). You can also send attachments but because of my spam situation, I cannot guarantee that they will get through to me...send a
       <em>
        link
       </em>
       to your entry to make sure. There will be some still-as-yet-unspecified prize (I'm thinking a Wii game or something like that) awarded to the winner. Good luck!
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Update:
       </b>
       <a href="http://www.kottke.org/06/12/more-on-the-celebrity-mii-contest">
        More information on prizes (including a cool Mii statuette for the winner) and judges here
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Update:
       </b>
       The contest is over and
       <a href="http://www.kottke.org/06/12/celebrity-mii-contest-results">
        the results are here
       </a>
       .
      </p>
