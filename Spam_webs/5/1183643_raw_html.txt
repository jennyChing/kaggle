&lt;p&gt;Cake Batter Popcorn coated in cake mix, white chocolate, and sprinkles will satisfy your craving for something sweet and crunchy. Make a batch for family movie night or a birthday celebration and watch faces light up.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Not only is this popcorn delicious, it&#8217;s hard not to smile when you look at sprinkles. They are an immediate mood elevator, bringing out the kid in all of us.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/cake-batter-pop-corn-17.jpg"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8567 pib-hover-img" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/cake-batter-pop-corn-17.jpg" alt="Cake Batter Popcorn" width="525" height="825" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/cake-batter-popcorn/" data-pin-media="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/cake-batter-pop-corn-17.jpg"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Ever since I made &lt;a title="Cake Batter Milkshakes" href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/cake-batter-milkshakes/"&gt;Cake Batter Milkshakes &lt;/a&gt;for my daughter&#8217;s birthday, I&#8217;ve been coming up with ways to use what was leftover of my box of yellow cake mix. First it was &lt;a title="Cake Batter Pancakes Recipe" href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/cake-batter-pancakes/"&gt;Cake Batter Pancakes &lt;/a&gt;and now Cake Batter Popcorn. I&#8217;ve come to the conclusion that one can never have enough cake batter in their life.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/cake-batter-pop-corn-1.jpg"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8568 pib-hover-img" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/cake-batter-pop-corn-1.jpg" alt="Cake Batter Popcorn" width="525" height="825" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/cake-batter-popcorn/" data-pin-media="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/cake-batter-pop-corn-1.jpg"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I like to pop my own popcorn. It&#8217;s easy to do in a large pot with a little Vegetable oil. But you could use microwave popcorn instead. All it takes is a simple mixture of cake mix, melted white bark or chocolate, and sprinkles to turn boring popcorn into a fun and exciting treat.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The mixture will be a little thick, and really the best way to coat the popcorn is to use your hands. It gets a little messy and you have to work fast before it sets up. This is a perfect task for kids. Just make sure they wash their hands first! Adding a little vegetable shortening to the mixture would help make it a little easier to spread.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Cake Batter Popcorn is best eaten soon after it is made. Most likely it will disappear in a hurry.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;div class="ERSCategory"&gt;Recipe type: &lt;span itemprop="recipeCategory"&gt;Snack&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;div class="ERSServes"&gt;Serves: &lt;span itemprop="recipeYield"&gt;5&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;/div&gt; &lt;div class="ERSIngredients"&gt; &lt;div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading"&gt;Ingredients&lt;/div&gt; &lt;ul&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;10 cups popped popcorn&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;8 ounces white bark or white chocolate&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;&frac12; cup yellow cake mix&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;&#8531; cup sprinkles&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;2 teaspoons nonpareils&lt;/li&gt; &lt;/ul&gt; &lt;div class="ERSClear"&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;/div&gt; &lt;div class="ERSInstructions"&gt; &lt;div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading"&gt;Instructions&lt;/div&gt; &lt;ol&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Line a sheet pan with parchment paper and spread out popcorn on top.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Chop white bark so that there are no pieces bigger than 1 ounce. Place in a microwave safe bowl. Microwave on high for 30 seconds. Stir. Continue microwaving for 20 second intervals, stirring in between until completely melted.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Stir cake batter into melted white bark and add sprinkles.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Using a spoon, drizzle melted white bark on top of popcorn. It will probably be too thick to drizzle, so just glop it out. Use a spatula or your hands (easiest way) to evenly coat the popcorn in the white bark mixture.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Sprinkle with nonpareils.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Let it set up for a few minutes before eating.
