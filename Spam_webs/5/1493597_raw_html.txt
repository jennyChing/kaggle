       <h2 class="post-Heading single-heading">
        Ampera wins second Auto Green Award  Auto Express magazines award ceremony London
       </h2>
       <ul class="entry-meta blog-border">
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.autosmodel.com/autos-news/" rel="category tag">
          Autos news
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span class="author_meta">
          By
          <a href="http://www.autosmodel.com/author/kaili/" rel="author" title="Posts by Kaili">
           Kaili
          </a>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span class="dates">
          27 Sep 2013
         </span>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="clear">
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="18393579" data-link="http://www.autosmodel.com/ampera-wins-second-auto-green-award-auto-express-magazines-award-ceremony-london/" data-summary="" data-title="Ampera wins second Auto Green Award - Auto Express magazines award ceremony London">
        </div>
        <p>
         Auto Express magazines award ceremony was held in London recently. It included all the green cars with magnificent performances, it was difficult to decide which car would win at one time but Vauxhalls revolutionary model
         <a href="http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/vauxhall/ampera/65163/vauxhall-ampera" target="_blank" title="Vaushall Ampera">
          Vauxhall Ampera
         </a>
         outmatched all yet again.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Manufacturers are working harder than ever to make their cars more eco-friendly, so its testament to Vauxhalls brilliant Ampera that wins our Green Award for the second year on the trotit is still the best.
         </strong>
          Steve Fowler (Vauxhall official)
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.autosmodel.com/ampera-wins-second-auto-green-award-auto-express-magazines-award-ceremony-london/vauxhallamperrar-wins-green-card/" rel="attachment wp-att-1702">
          <img alt="Vauxhallamperrar-wins-green-card" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1702" height="390" src="http://www.autosmodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Vauxhallamperrar-wins-green-card.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Vauxhalls director showed his gratitude over winning the award and said that they are upbeat as Ampera has embraced the award for the second consecutive year. It is a trendsetter in the true sense of the word not only for the company but also for the entire automotive industry.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <span id="more-1698">
         </span>
         Working
        </h2>
        <p>
         Ampera is superbly eco-friendly and efficient. Driving it is a joy and no one could think of discomfort while riding on it. CO2 emission of Ampera remains 27g /km that is an outstanding emission rate .Its battery ranges from 25/50 miles; however a small generator is attached to it to boost its range to 310 miles. A type-2 cable charger is also available with it to charge it at any public place.
         <a href="http://www.autosmodel.com/test-a-car-before-to-buy-it-test-drive-tips/">
          Test drive
         </a>
         proof that not a drop of petrol is needed when you are in the EV mode.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Performance
        </h2>
        <p>
         Come to its performance now, Critics agree that there are no flaws in it. It gets you instant torque the moment you begin to drive it. One thing that they pinpointed was its handling that is a bit (excessively) squishy but no worries, as it works well at high speeds. It will run smooth and calmly; there is no concept of any wind or road noise in it. It is truly a revolutionary car as its wheels are all-electric unlike
         <a href="http://www.autosmodel.com/top-10-hybrid-cars/">
          other hybrid
         </a>
         wheels that is available in three trim levels.
        </p>
        <p>
         Though Ampera is not as attractive as other rivals are but it promises a serene cabin, solid quality equipment and roomy seats plus cargo, what else do we need?
        </p>
        <h2>
         Price Tag &amp; Warranty
        </h2>
        <p>
         Precious things are always expensive and Vauxhall Ampera is precious. It is because quality material inside and out are used in it. It offers a lifetime warranty that means you are tension-free after buying this prestigious vehicle for the entire life. Price sticker of 2013 car ranges from 34,995 to 38,995 pounds.
        </p>
