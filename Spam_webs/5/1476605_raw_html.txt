             <p>
              Toaster Strudels are a very sad food because of an interesting indication that they offer about anyone's personality. Think about this: a person's ability to enjoy a toaster strudel and to respect it as a legitimate, delicious food shares a direct correlation with his or her level of childhood wonder left in them. If you are no longer capable of enjoying a toaster strudel, you have lost all of the inherent childhood goodness left in you. All of it. Thus, when I smoke weed, my childhood is returning to me en masse.
             </p>
