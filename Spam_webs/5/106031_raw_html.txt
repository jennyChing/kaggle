         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Win a Family Trip for 4 From Atlantic Luggage and Packing Tips for Family Travel
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <h2>
          <a href="http://kidsareatrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/atl-newyork-sandiego.jpg">
           <img alt="New York-San Diego" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1616" height="464" src="http://kidsareatrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/atl-newyork-sandiego.jpg" width="1170"/>
          </a>
         </h2>
         <h3>
          This is a sponsored post written by me on behalf of
          <a href="http://izea.it/l17XY" rel="nofollow">
           Atlantic Luggage
          </a>
          for
          <a href="https://izea.com/" rel="nofollow">
           IZEA
          </a>
          . All opinions are 100% mine.
         </h3>
         <h3>
          If you havent heard, Atlantic Luggage is having an amazing sweepstakes to win a trip for a family of 4 to San Diego or New York City, now through November 15, 2014. Before you pack your bags, keep reading to see why Atlantic Luggage may be the right choice for your next vacation.
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span id="more-1611">
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Our family loves to travel and we know it isimportant to have luggage you can trustwhen you are traveling with kids. Atlantic Luggage has been producing quality luggage since 1919 and is helping to make family travel easier with their lightweight and affordable travel luggage sets. With a variety of colors and styles, and unique features, Atlantic Luggage makes packing for family trips easy, no matter the destination.
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Fe522cec-93cc-4e51-88d9-e80b246e3b89_zpsab18058d" src="http://i1301.photobucket.com/albums/ag108/chelseaf2/fe522cec-93cc-4e51-88d9-e80b246e3b89_zpsab18058d.png"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          One thing I have learned over the years is the importance of having a plan when you pack for your family vacation. We are THAT family who does not believe in dragging along more than one suitcase for our family. That is why Atlantic Luggage is a perfect fit when we travel.Atlantic Luggage has innovative features such as the Link2Go system, which allows you to connect multiple pieces of luggage for easy one handed rolling. Also, their 360-degree 4-Wheel Spinners allows for easy guiding and steering through busy terminals. Just make sure your kids dont run into anyone! Atlantic Luggage has thought of it all, taking the struggle out of traveling for the whole family.
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="D10dd084-915e-4bdc-a409-43413f25907a_zps5ec7f6e2" src="http://i1301.photobucket.com/albums/ag108/chelseaf2/d10dd084-915e-4bdc-a409-43413f25907a_zps5ec7f6e2.png"/>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Here are some of our packing tipsto keep you from overpacking on your next trip:
         </h2>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           1. Use packing cubes for each child
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Assign each child a colored cube and pack their clothes in the cube. When you arrive at your destination, take out the cube, and place it right into the drawer. If you have to change locations during your trip, all the clothes go back into the cube, zip it up, throw it into your suitcase, and go.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           2. Roll your clothes
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Many people have heard this before. If you are not doing this yet, start now. Rolling your clothes saves space in your luggage. Period.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           3. Check the weather before you go
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Instead of winging it, be sure to check the weather so you are only taking the clothes you need. Why take sweaters when it will be 80 degrees?
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           4. Pack a clothesline or stay somewhere with a washing machine
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          One of the easiest ways to lighten your load is to stay in a place where you can do your laundry. No one likes to do laundry on vacation, but who really likes to do laundry when they get home?
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           5. Dont pack a bunch of toys or books in your suitcase
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Suitcase space is valuable real estate. Limit your kids to toys that can travelin your carry-on and download books on an electronic device if possible.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <span style="font-size: 14px;">
            <strong>
             Heresthe sweepstakes information you have been waiting for:
            </strong>
           </span>
          </em>
         </p>
