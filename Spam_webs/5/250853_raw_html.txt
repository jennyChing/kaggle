      <h1 class="entry-title">
       Play Area Line Marking Specification
      </h1>
      <div class="the-content">
       <p>
        Play area surfacing contractors can install line marking for macadam playground surfaces in the UK, the playground markings come in a range of different graphic flooring designs with fun and educational games and sports for children of all ages. These graphics are called thermoplastic markings and are similar to the designs found on Wetpour rubber play areas; however they are made from plastic which is heated and stuck to the macadam floor surface, this therefore gives a brighter and more appealing effect which engages kids and encourages educational play.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Thermoplastic Play Area Line Marking Application
       </h3>
       <p>
        These thermoplastic play area designs are permanent, very durable, hard wearing, especially in comparison to the standard painted playground surface markings. Thermoplastic line marking can be applied to any play area surfacing with ease in a short period of time to reduce disruption for kids and parents at schools and clubs. In addition, the installation of thermoplastic play area line marking is non toxic and does not contain lead or chromates, these qualities make the play surface safe for children to use and be in contact with. Once applied, thermoplastic playground graphics can be easily cleaned and maintained with a wide fanned pressure washer or a soft brush, this means there is no extra expense involved with the cleaning and upkeep of the play area line markings. This simple maintenance makes thermoplastic line marking more cost effective than paint graphics which require repainting after a while.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Playground Marking Graphic Designs
       </h3>
       <p>
        The playground surface graphics are available in a huge range of colours and designs which can be customised to suit any school or club, for example playground line marking contractors would be able to apply a school logo or bespoke colour scheme to the macadam to give a more personal effect and to fit a range of specifications and budgets. Individual play surfacing construction specialists will be able to offer prices and give quotes tailored to different surface requirements including and special requests you may have regarding installation of personalised playground graphic designs.
       </p>
       <p>
        Some of the graphics and surface games available include traditional playground activities like hopscotch and what time is it Mr. Wolf? as well as clocks, maps, safety and road signs, number grids for maths games and letters for alphabet and word games which can be specially chosen to meet different specifications. These activities all combine the aspect of educational learning linking to school curriculums, as well as providing an enjoyable experience for the children. Line marking for different sports such as football, tennis and netball can also be installed by play surfacing contractors; this helps schools to meet the standards of recommended physical education activities. Some of the designs which can be provided also include brightly coloured animals and cartoon characters to stimulate childrens imaginations for a more fun and engaging learning experience which would offer a change from conventional classroom learning.
       </p>
       <p>
        These play surface designs can be bespoke made for individual schools, clubs and public play areas to offer specialised playground games and activities, costs and prices can vary between different contractors and companies based on the specification and budget of the project and quotes can be offered to suit individual circumstances and locations.
       </p>
