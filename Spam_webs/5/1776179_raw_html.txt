    <h1>
     <strong>
      Art In Wax
     </strong>
     : Encaustic Art By Hazel Rayfield
    </h1>
    <div id="post-1509">
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.artinwax.co.uk:/blog/art-in-wax-on-ampersand-art-supplies-encausticbord-video-demonstrations/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Art In Wax on Ampersand Art Supplies Encausticbord Video Demonstrations">
       Art In Wax on Ampersand Art Supplies Encausticbord Video Demonstrations
      </a>
      ::
      <span class="post">
       Posted by: Hazel on June 25th, 2014
      </span>
     </h2>
     <div>
      <h1>
       Working with Ampersand Art Supply Encausticbord
      </h1>
      <p>
       <img alt="Hazel Rayfield Artist" class="imageright" height="222" src="http://www.artinwax.co.uk:/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/hazelrayfield-film-image-artinwax.jpg" width="285"/>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Video demonstrations Encausticbord
      </h2>
      <p>
       When I was approached by Karyn at
       <a href="http://www.ampersandart.com/">
        Ampersand Art Supply
       </a>
       earlier this year to create some demonstrations of me working with their wonderful products for their YouTube channel, I was really excited.
      </p>
      <p>
       I love to paint in hot wax and have used the Encausticbord panels and cradle in the past when I
       <a href="http://www.artinwax.co.uk/blog/encaustic-starter-kit-review/">
        reviewed the R&amp;F Handmade Paints Starter kit
       </a>
       on this blog last year and the kit included some of the panels and a cradle.
      </p>
      <p>
       The Encausticbord has not been readily available in the UK other than in the kits as far as I am aware, but I am informed that Ampersand Art Supply who are based in Buda Texas, are looking to have UK supplier later this year.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Always up for a challenge
      </h2>
      <p>
       If you have read my blog before you know that I love to see what can be done in hot wax and I love to challenge myself and try new things, so when this opportunity came I was very excited to be working with Ampersand and with these wonderful encaustic supports.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Working with Ampersand Encausticbord
      </h2>
      <p>
       This is what Ampersand Arts Supply say about Encausticbord .
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        A Creative Collaboration between Ampersand and R&amp;F Handmade Paints
        <br/>
        This creative collaboration began in 2008 when the two manufacturers, authorities in their respective fields of wood painting panels and encaustic paint colors, started working together to dream up the perfect surface for encaustic paints. This combination of Ampersands archival panel coating technology and R&amp;Fs expertise in encaustics led to Encausticbord, a unique wood panel that has the tooth to hold encaustic and mixed media unlike any other surface.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       Quote taken June 2014 courtsey of
       <a href="http://www.ampersandart.com/encausticbord.html">
        www.ampersandart.com/encausticbord.html
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Making the videos
      </h2>
      <p>
       My husband Adrian, a great amateur photographer in his own right, filmed as I painted. We are not professional film makers, we got the giggles a few times and had a few out-takes too  but I have a passion for painting Art In Wax and I hope this comes over in the videos.
      </p>
      <p>
       The two videos created especially for Ampersand Art Supply are now live and can be seen online on the
       <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/ampersandvideos">
        Ampersand Art Supply YouTube Channel
       </a>
       . I have posted the direct links to my demonstrations below :
      </p>
      <p>
       I am using the wonderful and amazing colour wax paints by
       <a href="http://www.rfpaints.com/">
        R &amp; F Paints
       </a>
       in these films.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Two video demonstrations
      </h2>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Cradled Encausticbord
       </strong>
       :
       <br/>
       This film shows me working on an Encausticbord cradle which is 6 x 6 inches and a prefect size for this subject, in this film I demonstrate the stages of how I painted a Sunflower head.
      </p>
      <p>
       The video can be watched here :
       <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C9csQ1nZ16s">
        Ampersand Art Supply : Hazel Rayfield Cradled Encausticbord
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Artist Trading Cards in Encaustic
       </strong>
       :
       <br/>
       In this film I demonstrate the techniques working in miniature, onto Encaustic ACEO / ATC 2.5 x 3.5 inch size Encausticbord panels which are great fun to work with. Here I create three pieces using different styles and technique, from painting directly onto the surface, to a mixed media piece and a colorful abstract.
      </p>
      <p>
       The video can be watched here :
       <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uzVX4FVoZFQ">
        Ampersand Art Supply : Hazel Rayfield Artist Trading Cards in Encaustic
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       The finished pieces
      </h3>
      <p>
       These are the finished pictures, this is been a great challenge / project. Ampersand Art Supply and their products have made painting my Art In Wax a pleasure.
      </p>
      <div class="center">
       <img alt="art in wax on ampersand art supply bords" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1511" height="1024" src="http://www.artinwax.co.uk:/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ampersand-art-video-blog-artinwax-hazelrayfield.jpg" width="800"/>
      </div>
      <h4>
       A Fun Project
      </h4>
      <p>
       This has been a great fun project, I hope you like the videos and watching me paint in this style of hot wax.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you would like to comment below that would be great.
      </p>
      <p>
       Thanks for visiting my blog today.
      </p>
      <p>
       Hazel
      </p>
