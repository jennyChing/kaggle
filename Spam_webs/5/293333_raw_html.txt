        <h2 class="date-header">
         11/30/2013
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-disney_princess_costumes entry-category-fantasy_costumes entry-category-gypsy_costumes entry-author-briellecostumes entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a0134804df3cb970c019b006ff266970c">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/brielles-costume-wardrobe/2013/11/belles-battle-outfit-version-2.html">
            Belle's Battle Outfit Version 2
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             Greetings,
            </p>
            <p>
             For this costume my customer was in need of an outfit inspired by Belle's battle garb worn in the tv show Once Upon A Time.
            </p>
            <p>
             This is the second costume I have created like this. The first one can be seen
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/brielles-costume-wardrobe/2013/02/belles-battle-outfit.html" target="_self">
              here
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The original costume looks like this...
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c017c372eb5ed970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Belle1" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c017c372eb5ed970b image-full" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c017c372eb5ed970b-800wi" title="Belle1"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             My customer is using her own leather gloves and leggings. (not pictured here)
            </p>
            <p>
             *click on the pictures for larger views of the details*
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b00703d6c970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Belle2" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019b00703d6c970c" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b00703d6c970c-500wi" title="Belle2"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The undershirt has a fairytale hood and features a low neckline with full sleeves.
            </p>
            <p>
             I used a very pretty and soft organic hemp fabric for the undershirt because I wanted it to feel magical. This fabric is also perfect for tucking into leggings.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b0070a91b970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Belle4" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019b0070a91b970d" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b0070a91b970d-500wi" title="Belle4"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The bodice was created from a very high quality deep wine colored suede fabric edged with dark brown linen and tapestry accents. Lacing up the front allows a custom fit.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b00706c13970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Belle5" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019b00706c13970b" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b00706c13970b-500wi" title="Belle5"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </p>
            <p>
             I fully lined the bodice with a soft brown linen as well to give it a pretty feel and texture.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b0070a99c970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Belle1" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019b0070a99c970d" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b0070a99c970d-500wi" title="Belle1"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The fairytale sleeves are my favorite part of the costume! It gives the whole look a very "Belle" feel. The belt was created from silk, suede, trim and rows of all metal button accents.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b00704023970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Belle3" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019b00704023970c" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019b00704023970c-500wi" title="Belle3"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Thank you for looking at this Belle Inspired Costume.
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              <strong>
               Interested in your own Once Upon Time garments?
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             Contact me for details.
            </p>

        <h2 class="date-header">
         09/24/2013
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-disney_princess_costumes entry-category-fantasy_costumes entry-author-briellecostumes entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff932890970d">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/brielles-costume-wardrobe/2013/09/rapunzel-inspired-dress-.html">
            Rapunzel Inspired Dress
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             For this dress the inspiration was Rapunzel from the Disney Princess movie "Tangled".
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91ec9e970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Inspired by" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91ec9e970b" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91ec9e970b-500wi" title="Inspired by"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Details on the gown include lace up bodice, double layered skirt with painted designs, puffed sleeves and sparkly pink lower sleeves. Sparkly pink buttons up the back make it easy to wear the dress.
            </p>
            <p>
             Of course the costume wouldn't be complete without Pascal on the shoulder.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91ed2a970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="IMG_0873" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91ed2a970b" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91ed2a970b-500wi" title="IMG_0873"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The dress is made of a beautiful shimmer shine satin bridal satin. The color is more lavendar in real life. It just reads on camera as more purple. All the lace has a shimmer glitter finish.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff921ac3970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="IMG_0898" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff921ac3970c" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff921ac3970c-500wi" title="IMG_0898"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             A full circle skirt is perfect for twirling during a dance or hanging out in the tower.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff921b46970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="IMG_0885" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff921b46970c" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff921b46970c-500wi" title="IMG_0885"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The designs around the hem and the front of the skirt were all hand painted based off of the designs on her dress in the movie and at the Disney Parks.
            </p>
            <p>
             I also added crystal rhinestones in the center of the flowers of the designs for an added touch of sparkle.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91eef3970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="IMG_0900" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91eef3970b" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff91eef3970b-500wi" title="IMG_0900"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Sparkly pink buttons up the back close the dress with ease and add a whimsical touch.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff951772970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="IMG_0919" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c019aff951772970b" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c019aff951772970b-500wi" title="IMG_0919"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Interested in your own Disney Princess costume? Contact me for details!
            </p>
            <p>
             Thanks for looking at my costumes!
            </p>

        <h2 class="date-header">
         11/01/2011
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-disney_princess_costumes entry-category-narnia_costumes entry-author-briellecostumes entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a0134804df3cb970c0153943b03c4970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/brielles-costume-wardrobe/2011/11/susans-farwell-gown-this-gown-was-inspired-by-the-one-worn-by-susan-in-the-movie-the-chronicles-of-narnia-prince-caspian.html">
            Susans Farwell Gown
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             This gown was inspired by the one worn by Susan in the movie The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian.
            </p>
            <p>
             Although Susan is seen wearing this dress for a short time, I think it is the most memorable because it is the last one she wears in Narnia.
            </p>
            <p>
             Here is what the movie gown looked like...
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ed187970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Movie Susan" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ed187970c image-full" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ed187970c-800wi" title="Movie Susan"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             My version of her gown is an inspired by version, I do not claim to have it identical in every way, but similar. I changed the neckline so that it is a bit more princess like and the back features gold eyelets.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ef0f7970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Front" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ef0f7970c image-full" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ef0f7970c-800wi" title="Front"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The costume consists of a crinoline, full skirted underdress with embellished sleeves and then the over dress is laced up with a functional under corset.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ef362970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Back" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ef362970c" height="417" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380ef362970c-500wi" title="Back" width="446"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The White Under Dress is accented with an off the shoulder neckline,  princess seams and an invisible zipper. The sleeves feature band of a  golden fabric trim and lower puffed sleeves tightened with golden fabric  cuffs closed with a delicate gold button. The lower hem of this under  dress has a slight train in the back achieved with a mermaid like panel.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380f2868970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Front3" height="268" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0154380f2868970c-500wi" title="Front3" width="447"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The Blue and Gold Over gown is made with a fully functional corset  underneath. Closed with gold plated corset busks and boned up each  lining seam, the back laces tight with gold eyelet and a silken cord.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0162fd90f022970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Front2" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c0162fd90f022970d" height="655" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0162fd90f022970d-500wi" title="Front2" width="445"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             This over gown is made of a heavy taffeta type fabricgown is fully lined in the same color.
            </p>
            <p>
             The front is outlined with the golden fabric trim and is split in the front to allow the silken under gown to show through the buttons on the cuff are very Narnia inspired.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0162fd9639fc970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Smiling" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0134804df3cb970c0162fd9639fc970d image-full" height="418" src="http://briellecostumes.typepad.com/.a/6a0134804df3cb970c0162fd9639fc970d-800wi" title="Smiling" width="559"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Looking for your own Narnia gowns? Contact me for details.
            </p>

             Want to see more pictures? Check out the
