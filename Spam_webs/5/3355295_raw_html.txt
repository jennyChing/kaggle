             <h2>
              <strong>
               Get
              </strong>
              <strong>
               that Classic Living Room
              </strong>
             </h2>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-019.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-456" height="600" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-019.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="900"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              Classic living room is what most of the people have. It has its own rules, like huge furniture, side tables, lamps and sometimes fireplaces too. You can mix beautiful, timeless elements and make your living room, magnificent with a classical look. It is easy to create and makes your guests feel welcome.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-08.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-445 size-full" height="736" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-08.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="982"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <h3>
              <em>
               Furniture Types for Classic Living Room
              </em>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <em>
               <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-020.jpg">
                <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-457 size-full" height="600" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-020.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="782"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
              </em>
              <br/>
              Furniture is the key for classic living room. Huge chairs with a little XX century features, sofa with curved arms and of course, coffee table. A coffee table can be in glass or wood, but mostly the room should be in wooden furniture with ornaments. Side tables are must-have too. They could be for lamps or flowers.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-018.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-455 size-full" height="600" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-018.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="825"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-017.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-454" height="600" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-017.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="899"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-016.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-453" height="500" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-016.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="800"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              Everything depends on owners taste. Dont be afraid to combine furniture with different patterns. Classic living room can be in neutral colors with small touches of color and texture. This way you can add some vintage characteristics.
             </p>
             <h3>
             </h3>
             <h3>
              <em>
               Add
              </em>
              <em>
               a Fireplace
              </em>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-014.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-451 size-full" height="453" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-014.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="725"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              White fireplace in the middle of two plaid chairs make the living room look formal, but also soft and welcoming. Moreover, the best way to have cozy rooms with masculine look is to add a black fireplace with dark wooden furniture in the room. This way, living room looks formal, homey, beautiful and classic.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-015.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-452 size-full" height="453" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-015.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="725"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <h3>
             </h3>
             <h3>
              <em>
               Classic Living Room Colors
              </em>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <em>
               <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-013.jpg">
                <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-450 size-full" height="736" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-013.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="982"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
              </em>
              <br/>
              For classic living room, colors are simple and cozy. You can choose neutral cream tones, but other colors could be added throughout accessories. Blue and White combination makes the room feel warm and bigger. Also, beige and various tones of brown are brilliant. The wallpaper could be in various ornaments, like floral or damask patterns.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-012.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-449" height="736" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-012.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="982"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              Dont be afraid to stick to a single-
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/living-room-wallpaper-ideas" title="Living Room Wallpaper Ideas from My Living Room Ideas">
               color palette
              </a>
              . In this case, beige is your color with dark woods, in order to create a glamorous classic living room.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-011.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-448 size-full" height="736" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-011.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="982"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-010.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-447" height="736" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-010.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="982"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-09.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-446" height="736" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-09.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="982"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              A classic living room defines a typical home and makes most people feel at home even when far from home. The tips above are simple, why not try
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/small-sitting-room-decor-tips" title="Small Sitting Room Decor Tips">
               to create
              </a>
              one in your own home and eliminate that coldness and emptiness that may be in your living room.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-01.jpg">
               <img alt="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas photo" class="aligncenter wp-image-438 size-full" height="765" src="http://www.mylivingroomideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Classic-Living-Room-Designs-01.jpg" title="Classic Living Room Designs | My Living Room Ideas" width="1000"/>
