                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       Lights
Camera Action. Welcome to Hollywood. Heres a place where the stars shine 24
hours a day and 7 days a week.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

place. But, we all know the actresses run the show.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>
                     </div>

                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

Brad Pitt without Angelina Jolie? Captain America is a chump without Scarlett
Johansson standing beside him. I learned early in life that behind every great
man is a great woman.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>

                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

                      </span>

                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       The role
of the actress is to bring the character to life. She has to dive into the part
like a synchronized swimmer. I admire actresses for their crafts.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       If you
want to make it in Hollywood, you cant fear rejection. Rejection is part of the
game. Who knows? You might go on 100 auditions and still not get a job.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       Dont
give up or get discouraged. Remember the goals you have set. No one said being
an actress was easy.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>
                     </div>

                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       My
favorite actresses belong to the
                       <a href="http://www.sfsite.com/columns/amy26.htm" target="_blank">
                        science fiction and fantasy
                       </a>
                       genre. Although I
love all kinds of movies, fantasy resonates with my idea of an unstoppable
movie. In a sense, science fiction is unregulated, unlimited, and unbelievable.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>

                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;">

all, science fiction and fantasy movies are really hard to produce. Most movies
in this genre have enormous budgets. As a result, sometimes you hit; and,
sometimes you miss.
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>

film and get at the heart of the story. A great movie critiques society.

                       </o:p>

said this is a mans world, but it would be nothing without a woman or a
little girl. Hollywood is an actors paradise but it would be nothing without
an actress.

all Hollywood actresses are underpaid. Click

highest paid actresses. I admire actresses for their tenacity.
