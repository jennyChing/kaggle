     <h1 itemprop="headline">
      Youre invited to an Otherworldly Dinner Party
     </h1>
    </header>
    <div class="meta left">
     <!-- Author avatar -->
     <p class="avatar">
     </p>
     <!-- Author name -->
     <p class="author">
      By
      <a href="http://www.messynessychic.com/author/vanessa/" rel="author">

    <div class="content left" itemprop="articleBody">
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="oustandingbeachdinnerparty" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-53480" height="560" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/oustandingbeachdinnerparty.jpg" width="756"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <em>
       <strong>
        T
       </strong>
      </em>
      hisseemingly infinite white table
      <span style="color: #222222;">
       emerging fromthe ocean waves stopped me in my internet-scouring tracks today. I had to know. Is this real? And if so, how do I get invited? The originof the photo was not cited but it didnt take me long to track down its source and discover that not only is this otherworldly dinner party actually a thing that exists on this earth, but you and I are invited 
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="outstanding6" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-53482" height="576" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/outstanding6.jpg" width="960"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <span style="color: #000000;">
       <a href="http://www.outstandinginthefield.com/" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         Outstanding in the Field
        </strong>
       </a>
       is a roving culinary adventure  literally a restaurant without walls. Setting their signature pristine white table amidst the most heavenly of earths backdrops is kinda their thing. For 15 years, this traveling feast has been inviting diners to eatonmountain tops, islands, ranches, in sea caves, greenhouses and vineyards. In thesummer of 1999, founder and native Californian chef Jim Denevan came up with the idea of setting a long table on a farm and inviting some people round for anopen-air feast, in celebration of the farmer and the gifts of the land. His idea was to inspire a conversation at the table that could re-connect diners to the land and the origins of their food, and to honour the local farmers and food artisans who cultivate it. Oh and to have the most outstandingdining experience of your life. That too.
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="outstandingplates" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-53506" height="942" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/outstandingplates1.jpg" width="954"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      The way it works is to reserve your chosen dinner eventonline (a
      <span style="color: #000000;">
       schedule is released on the website every year on the first day of Spring) and wait to receiveyour invitationabout two weeks prior to the event, which will include details such as dress code and directions to the sometimes secret locations. On arrival, youll be greeted with some wine to help mingle with the other dinner guests and then take a little tour of the hosting farm or site. Youll also meet the local producers who provided the ingredients. Then its time to chow down at the iconic table, family style, passing down bowls of deliciously fresh ingredients and eatingalongside thefarmers, producers, culinary artisans, and fellow guests.
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <span style="color: #000000;">
       The Outstanding crew have already made ten coast-to-coast tours of North America, visited 45 of the United States and set their table in a total of nine countries.
      </span>
      You can find the list of the
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.outstandinginthefield.com/events/?action=tribe_list&amp;tribe_paged=1&amp;tribe-bar-date=2014-01-01" target="_blank">
        2014dinners and reserve online here
       </a>
       .
      </strong>
     </p>

A celebrated chef from the region will be putting his heart and soul into your dinner this is not your typical catering mush. Oh no, this dinnerwill change the way you look at food.Heres a few snaps from past dinners
      </span>

       plate,
      </em>
      where each guest is encouraged to
      <span style="color: #222222;">
