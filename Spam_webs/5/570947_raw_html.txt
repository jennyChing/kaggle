          <p>
           A few weekends ago I ended up in Malibu with a good friend, and on the way back from the beach we decided to stop by for some wine. Along the way we ran across Rosenthal, Malibu Wines. It was not their actual estate, but their tasting room. Their estates are located further north.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/merosenthalrs-Malibu-164_wm.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1780" height="203" src="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/merosenthalrs-Malibu-164_wm-300x203.jpg" title="Me outside of Rosenthal" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           We pulled in and were surprised to see how easy parking was, and then walked in. It was a very casual and comfortable environment, and we walked up to the tasting table.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/rosenthanmenu_wm.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1781" height="205" src="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/rosenthanmenu_wm-300x205.jpg" title="rosenthanmenu_wm" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The hostess explained to us how it all worked and we briefly browsed through their menu. We decided on a flight, which included 6 tastes (of course, we got a taste of muscat at the end too!)
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/rosenthal-Malibu-151_wm.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1782" height="300" src="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/rosenthal-Malibu-151_wm-204x300.jpg" title="rosenthal, Malibu 151_wm" width="204"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           They started us out with their Surfrider Rose, in which that pale pink color came to life as you sipped on your glass. We quickly moved onto their 2 chardonneys. The first was my favorite with their Surfrider Chardonney, a taste of vanilla was brought into the wine which I absolutely loved. My friends favorite was the Rosenthal Chardonney, in which you could taste a bit of lemon and pear mixed together.
          </p>
          <p>
           We then moved onto the reds. When I started my wine journey I had no taste for reds. The past few months I have slowly begun to find reds that I enjoy, and thats telling me my taste for wine is starting to improve, and Im beginning to appreciate more of it.
          </p>
          <p>
           The first red we tried out was their Surfrider Merlot, and let me tell you, I cant do Merlots quite yet. Theyre far too much for me yet.
          </p>
          <p>
           There were two other reds given to us, their unique variety the Petit Verdot and then their Caberet. I really enjoyed their caberet and was happy to walk away with a red I enjoyed.
          </p>
          <p>
           But my favorite came after the tasting was over with the sweet, sweet muscat, and I even walked home with a bottle of my own.
          </p>
          <p>
           Rosenthal has a great backyard where you sit out back. Youre invited to bring food into the establishment and make a day hanging out with friends as you sip on your favorite bottle of wine or two or three. They even had a stage where performers were putting on a show.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, Rosenthal has become my new favorite place to stop by after a day of work to sit back and unwind.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/stage1.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1784" height="300" src="http://mngirlinla.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/stage1-212x300.jpg" title="stage" width="212"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Have you been to Rosenthals before? What are your thoughts?
          </p>

           </em>
