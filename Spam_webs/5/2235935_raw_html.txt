           <h2 class="graf--h2 graf--first" id="title" name="title">
            PM AuraA Tool That Merges Project Management And Time Tracking!!
           </h2>
           <h4 class="graf--h4 graf--last" id="subtitle" name="subtitle">
            An Ideal Combination Of Project Management And Time TrackingBy PM Aura!!
           </h4>
          </div>

        </div>

        <div class="section-content">
         <div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn">
          <p class="graf--p graf--first" id="5f46" name="5f46">
           No project is a childs play, whether small or big! Before launching it or propelling it into the market, a robust layout is created that navigates the business into a right direction. It becomes extremely tedious if only manpower is allocated the work following the conventional procedures. A business can work more effectively if it has a technology back-up to handle its ongoing processes along with the work force. With lots of projects pouring in the business, it becomes really difficult to manage even if separate managers are hired for each and every project.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="93c2" name="93c2">
           To serve project needs and organizational resource constraints, a
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            Project Management Software
           </strong>
           can serve as an all-round solution empowering managers to monitor and handle projects efficiently.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="4d41" name="4d41">
           No project can be made successful without effective planning and implementing the same in a systematic manner. Indeed, appropriate task allocation, checking on customers satisfaction, adhering to deadlines, etc. are the key areas that need to be worked upon before triggering it.
           <br/>
           The question arises,
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">
             What is it that brings systematic and organized approach towards executing project tasks
            </em>
           </strong>
           ??
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="baf0" name="baf0">
           The two most required aspects to have a successful business are:
           <br/>
            Precised management of allocated projects
           <br/>
            Effective time tracking for deadline adherence and individual task time scheduling
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="4f16" name="4f16">
           The equation goes like:
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="2f8c" name="2f8c">
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            Effective Project Management + Accurate Time Tracking = Successful Business
           </strong>
          </p>
          <figure class="graf--figure" id="825a" name="825a">
           <div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 400px; max-height: 350px;">
            <div class="aspect-ratio-fill" style="padding-bottom: 87.5%;">
            </div>
            <img class="graf-image" data-height="350" data-image-id="1*AMZds7NsQ79xicUMSUr8dg.jpeg" data-width="400" src="https://d262ilb51hltx0.cloudfront.net/max/800/1*AMZds7NsQ79xicUMSUr8dg.jpeg"/>
           </div>
          </figure>
          <p class="graf--p" id="699e" name="699e">
           The company essentially needs the time record of the employees that are engaged in a particular project. An effective time tracking tool is a must to monitor time spent on each task. Another benefit of using a time tracking tool is that it renders convenience in computing salaries and consulting fees. Moreover, it also creates a clear time log of resources and reports that can be viewed by the clients for detailed assessment.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p graf--last" id="8787" name="8787">
           For an
           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.pmaura.com/project-management/" href="http://www.pmaura.com/project-management/" rel="nofollow">
            <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
             Effective Project Management
            </strong>
           </a>
           , it becomes cumbersome to detach these two components, time tracking and project management, in order to render a merged dynamic solution.
           <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">
            PM Aura
           </strong>
           renders a great project management solution to its users, serving an ideal combination of project management as well as time tracking. It offers a great opportunity to the customers to get dual benefits at single cost that turns out to be a great deal. In addition, it gives a powerful and dynamic platform for task management, document management, collaboration, generating reports and email notifications serving an ideal interface for the project management.
          </p>
