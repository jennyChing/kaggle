        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Do You Ever Wonder What the Father is Really Like?
        </h1>
        <p>
         Do you ever wondered what the Father is really like?
        </p>
        <p>
         Its too easy for us to look at our own fathers, or the greek god Zeus, or Santa Claus, and assign those attributes to our heavenly Father. But that view would be distorted.
        </p>
        <p>
         Jesus says, 
         <em>
          Anyone who has seen me has seen the Father.
         </em>
         
         <a class="scripturizer" href="http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+14%3A9" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+14%3A9', 'John 14:9 (NLT)');" target="_blank" title="Bible Gateway">
          John 14:9 (NLT)
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats because Jesus was the exact representation of the Father. As Bill Johnston says; Jesus is perfect theology.
        </p>
        <p>
         He only did what He saw the Father do
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          For I have come down from heaven to do the will of God who sent me, not to do my own will.
          <a class="scripturizer" href="http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+6%3A38" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+6%3A38', 'John 6:38');" target="_blank" title="Bible Gateway">
           John 6:38
          </a>
          New Living Translation (NLT)
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         He only said what He heard the Father say
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          I dont speak on my own authority. The Father who sent me has commanded me what to say and how to say it. And I know his commands lead to eternal life; so I say whatever the Father tells me to say.
          <a class="scripturizer" href="http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+12%3A49-50" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+12%3A49-50', 'John 12:49-50');" target="_blank" title="Bible Gateway">
           John 12:49-50
          </a>
          New Living Translation (NLT)
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         So lets spend some time looking at Jesus so we can see the Father more clearly. These are thoughts that I consider when I wonder at and about the Father.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Jesus  Liked to Party
         </strong>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          The Son of Man, on the other hand, feasts and drinks, and you say, Hes a glutton and a drunkard, and a friend of tax collectors and other sinners! But wisdom is shown to be right by its results.

          (New Living Translation)

         <p>

          <a class="scripturizer" href="http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=Psalm+17%3A7-8" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=Psalm+17%3A7-8', 'Psalm 17:7-8');" target="_blank" title="Bible Gateway">
           Psalm 17:7-8
          </a>
          (New International Version)
         </p>
        </blockquote>

         <strong>
          Jesus  Didnt Come to Condemn
         </strong>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          God sent his Son into the world not to judge the world, but to save the world through him.
          <a class="scripturizer" href="http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+3%3A17" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=John+3%3A17', 'John 3:17');" target="_blank" title="Bible Gateway">
           John 3:17
          </a>
          (New Living Translation)
         </p>

For the mountains may move and the hills disappear, but even then my faithful love for you will remain. My covenant of blessing will never be broken, says the Lord, who has mercy on you.

          <a class="scripturizer" href="http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=Isaiah+54%3A9-10" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://biblegateway.com/bible?version=NLT&amp;passage=Isaiah+54%3A9-10', 'Isaiah 54:9-10');" target="_blank" title="Bible Gateway">

          (New Living Translation)

           Psalm 103:2-3
