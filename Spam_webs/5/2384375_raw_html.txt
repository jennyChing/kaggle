         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          From Cannabis to Mint, there are hundreds of common herbs, flowers, berries and plants that serve all kinds of medical and health purposes.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Here are seven of the topmedicinal plants that can help with:anti-inflammatory, anti-fungal, insect repellent, antibacterial, detoxification, fever reduction, antihistamine and pain relief.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Cannabis
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Mostly legal until 1970 when it became classified as a hard drug. No one thought of it as a dangerous or illicit drug until the 20th century. In fact, the Declaration of Independence is written on it.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Hemp is a remarkable and renewable plant, offering all kinds product and food uses that surpass cotton and plastic.
          <a href="http://www.askmen.com/sports/health/20b_mens_health.html" target="_blank">
           Health benefits
          </a>
          are well documented, from depression,anxiety relief, reduced blood pressure, pain alleviation and even glaucoma treatment. It is not addictive, does not kill brain cells and is not a gateway drug  in fact, when pot is more available, studies show that the use of hard drugs like heroin and cocaine actually decreases.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Lady Ferns
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Grab some and roll it up between your palms. The juices released will quickly ease stinging burns and can alsoease minor cuts and burns. Bracken fern are similar to lady fern and will work as well.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Tansy
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tansy" target="_blank">
           Tansy
          </a>
          is an old-world aster and remedy, used for flavoring beer and stews as well as repelling insects.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Rubbing the leaves on the skin provides an effective bug repellent, but tansy can also be used to treat worms.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is said to be poisonous when extracted, but a few leaves are not harmful if ingested.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <a class="grouped_elements" href="http://dereklovellcoaching.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Mint-leaves-2007.jpg" rel="tc-fancybox-group701" title="Mint-leaves-2007">
           <img alt="Mint-leaves-2007" class="wp-image-703 alignleft" height="190" src="http://dereklovellcoaching.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Mint-leaves-2007.jpg" width="254"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Mint
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Mint is famous for soothing headaches, fighting nausea, calming the stomach and reducing nervousness and fatigue.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Korean mint, also called Indian mint and hyssop, is a fairly effective antiviral, making it useful for fighting colds and the flu.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Whatever continent youre on, some type of mint is usually to be found. Eat whole, garnish food or make tea to get the all purpose health benefits.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="alfalfa" class="wp-image-1373 at_include alignleft" height="189" src="http://cdn3-www.webecoist.momtastic.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/alfalfa.jpg" title="alfalfa" width="253"/>
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Alfalfa
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://www.homeremediesweb.com/alfalfa_health_benefits.php" target="_blank">
           Alfalfa
          </a>
          is incredibly rich in minerals and health-promoting nutrients and compounds. With roots that grow 20 to 30 feet deep, alfalfa is considered the father of all plants. (It also contains a high amount of protein for a green.) It can treat morning sickness, nausea, kidney stones, kidney pain and urinary discomfort.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is a powerful diuretic and has a bit of stimulant power, helping to energize after a bout with illness. Its a liver and bowel cleanser and long-term can help reduce cholesterol.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         </h2>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Sage
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://www.whfoods.com/genpage.php?tname=foodspice&amp;dbid=76" target="_blank">
           Sage
          </a>
          is an incredibly useful herb, It is anti-inflammatory, anti-oxidant, antifungal,aids digestion, relieves cramps, reduces diarrhea, dries up phlegm, fights colds, reduces inflammation and swelling and kills bacteria. Sage apparently even brings
          <a href="http://health.howstuffworks.com/wellness/natural-medicine/herbal-remedies/sage-herbal-remedies.htm" target="_blank">
           color
          </a>
          back to gray hair.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="red-clover" class="wp-image-1382 at_include alignleft" height="185" src="http://cdn1-www.webecoist.momtastic.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/red-clover.jpg" title="red-clover" width="257"/>
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #00ccff;">
           Red Clover
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The plants reddish pink blossoms can be used for coughs and colds, but they are an excellent detoxifier and blood cleanser as well.
         </p>
