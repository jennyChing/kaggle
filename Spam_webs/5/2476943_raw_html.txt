        From Right Wing Watch:

       <p>

       <blockquote>
        <p>
         During a presidential candidate forum hosted by an Iowa homeschool group yesterday, Ted Cruz
         <a href="https://caffeinatedthoughts.com/2015/04/cruz-huckabee-jindal-and-santorum-speak-to-iowa-homeschoolers-video/">
          lashed out
         </a>
         at the gay community for waging a jihad against so-called religious freedom laws in states such as Arkansas and Indiana.
        </p>
        <p>
         Cruz, speaking at a panel moderated by conservative talk show host Steve Deace, who
         <a href="http://mediamatters.org/blog/2015/04/01/ia-radio-host-steve-deace-lashes-out-at-pence-i/203132">
          regularly castigates
         </a>
         the 
         <a href="http://www.rightwingwatch.org/content/steve-deace-gay-rainbow-jihad-put-jesus-jail">
          Rainbow Jihad
         </a>
         , told the crowd of homeschooling activists that they should fear the jihad that is being waged right now in Indiana and Arkansas, going after people of faith who respect the biblical teaching that marriage is the union of one man and one woman.
        </p>
        <p>
         By
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NmoGgziNTS0&amp;feature=youtu.be">
          condemning this gay jihad,
         </a>
         Cruz said, he could bring people together to defend religious freedom.
        </p>
        <p>
         Other likely candidates present at the forum included Gov. Bobby Jindal and past Iowa caucus victors former Gov. Mike Huckabee and former Sen. Rick Santorum.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
