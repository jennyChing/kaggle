      <div class="post-left">
       <img alt="post-3298 thumbnail" height="400" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/670x400-Olivias-Overlook-Berkshires-Credit-Tim-Grafft-MOTT.jpg" width="675"/>
       <p class="author">
        Posted by the Mass Office of Travel and Tourism
       </p>
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <p>
        With more than 1,400 lodging destinations statewide  fitting any comfort or luxury in mind  as far as finding the right place to stay goes, its truly all here.
       </p>
       <p>
        Today on MassFinds, we invite your to explore the warm, rustic side of Massachusetts, where a number of resorts and inns combine unforgettable hospitality, and the natural side of the state.
       </p>
       <p>
        So, to avoid having to give up the comfort of your own home on your next getaway, consider one of these eight Massachusetts inns by region:
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         The Berkshires
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.redlioninn.com/rli/meadowlark.html" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Red Lion Inns MeadowlarkSuite
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         , Stockbridge, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Once the studio of famousAmerican sculptor Daniel Chester French  best known for sculpting the famous seated Lincoln of the Lincoln Memorial in Washington D.C. Meadowlark is now a picturesque cottage, located 3-miles from the actual Red Lion Inn, in a charming bungalow among a grove of maple and hemlock trees. The Suite includes two bedrooms, each with a queen bed, a living room with a sofa bed, a dining area, a small kitchen, a private bath, and a deck. For rates and availability,
        <a href="https://booking.ihotelier.com/istay/istay.jsp?HotelID=17402" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_3302" style="width: 310px">
        <img alt="" class=" wp-image-3302 " height="199" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Shaker-Mill-Inn-copy.jpg" title="Shaker Mill Inn copy" width="300"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Shaker Mill in via Facebook
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.shakermillinn.com" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Shaker Mill Inn
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         , WestStockbridge, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        This cozy Berkshires bed and breakfast inn is notable for luxurious accommodations at affordable prices, and is conveniently located just a short drive from Tanglewood, as well as the Stockbridge and Lenox Theaters. You can take a virtual tour
        <a href="http://shakermillinn.com/shakermillinn/_auto/flash/shakermillinn_shakermillinn.html" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        , and for reservations,
        <a href="http://www.shakermillinn.com/rooms-rates/" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.warfieldhouseinn.com" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Warfield House Inn atValley View Farm
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         , Charlemont, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Located on 530-acres of breathtaking farmland, overlooking the Mohawk Trail and Deerfield River, this family farm offers traditional Bed and Breakfast stays, in addition to a number of experiential activities  everything from llama treks and horse trail rides to maple sapgathering. For reservations,
        <a href="http://www.warfieldhouseinn.com/bed_and_breakfast/reservations" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Warfield-House-Inn-copy.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3300" height="477" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Warfield-House-Inn-copy.jpg" title="Warfield House Inn - Charlemont MA" width="720"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        The Warfield House Inn atValley View Farm via
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Warfield-House-Inn-at-Valley-View-Farm/162510413784863" target="_blank">
         Facebook
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Central Massachusetts
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.restfulpaws.com/index.php" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Inn at Restful Paws
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         ,Holland, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        A unique, pet-friendly bed and breakfast, The Inn at Restful Paws is the perfect retreat for travelers with pets. Featuring a number of pet oriented activities and amenities (like a bone shaped swimming pool!), guestscan take inall of the pleasures that theQuinebaugandShetucketRiver Valleys National Heritage Corridor have to offer. For rates and availability,
        <a href="http://www.restfulpaws.com/rooms.htm" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Greater Boston/Metrowest
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://innonthehorsefarm.com" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Inn on the HorseFarm
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         , Sudbury, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        An 1880 Queen-AnneVictorian Farmhouse secluded on nine acres of woods, pond, stream, willowtrees, stonewalls, and rolling pasture, all located in the historic town of Sudbury. For rates and availability,
        <a href="http://innonthehorsefarm.com/roomsandrates.html" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Cape Cod
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://lighthouseinn.com" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Lighthouse Inn
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         , West Dennis, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Situated on nine quiet, oceanfront acres, The Lighthouse Inn offers a private sandy beach, a heated pool,tennis, a childrens activity program, and a full service restaurant withpanoramic ocean views. Accommodations are provided in Cape-style guesthouses, with one-,two- and three-bedroom cottages. For availability,
        <a href="https://secure.iqres03037.com/iqreservations/asp/IQHome.asp" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Lighthouse-Inn.jpeg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3306" height="720" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Lighthouse-Inn.jpeg" title="Lighthouse Inn" width="960"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        The Lighthouse Inn via
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/lighthouseinn" target="_blank">
         Facebook
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.whalewalkinn.com" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Whalewalk Inn &amp; Spa
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         ,Eastham, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        One of the most secludedinns on the Cape, Whalewalk features 16 spacious, Cape-styled accommodations, which are spread throughout six buildings on a peaceful three-acrecompound of lawns, gardens, and meadows. Tucked away on a countryroad,Whalewalk also offers nearby access tomodern conveniences, attractions, and fine dining when desired. For availability,
        <a href="https://secure.rezovation.com/Reservations/CheckAvailability.aspx?s=yfILaNWE14" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         South of Boston
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://fivebridgeinn.com/2-BB/index.html" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Five Bridge Inn
         </strong>
        </a>
        <strong>
         , Rehoboth, MA
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Unique colonial character, and a location deepin the middle of 900 acres of preserve land, the Five Bridge Inn makes for an experience folks never seem forget (70% of the B&amp;B visitors are often repeats). For rates and amenities,
        <a href="http://fivebridgeinn.com/6-Videos/inn-grounds_01.html" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        To explore more of Massachusetts many lodging properties,
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/find/lodging/" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        . Whats your favorite way to relax, and unwind with a rustic retreat? Tell us in the comments below!
       </p>
