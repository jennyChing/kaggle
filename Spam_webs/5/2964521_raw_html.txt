       <div class="letter_right">
        <h2 style="font-weight:lighter;letter-spacing:1px;">
         ABOUT THE UNIVERSE TALKS (TUT)
        </h2>
        <br/>
        <div class="float_right" style="margin-left:20px;width:287px;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="float_left" frameborder="0" height="198" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/uVJbGrtyUmM" style="max-width: 287px;">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        Were host to a community of 700,000 like-minded thinkers, "Tutters," from around the world and were best known for:
        <ul style="margin-left:20px;">
         <li>
          <a href="/Inspiration/nftu">
           <i>
            Notes from the Universe
           </i>
          </a>
          e-mailings.
         </li>
         <li>
          Mike Dooleys self-improvement
          <a href="/tutshop/index.php/books.html">
           books
          </a>
          and
          <a href="/Education/mike-live">
           events
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/Humanity/our-story">
           Infinite Possibilities
          </a>
          Train the Trainer conferences.
         </li>
         <li>
          Our twice a year
          <a href="/Adventure/wow-tours">
           WOW Tours
          </a>
          !
         </li>
        </ul>
        <br/>
        <p>
         <b>
          What We Believe
         </b>
         <br/>
         TUT, which stands for The Universe Talks, believes that everyone's special, that every life is meaningful, and that we're all here to learn that dreams do come true. We also believe that "thoughts become things," and that imagination is the gift that can bring love, health, abundance, and happiness into our lives.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          What We Do
         </b>
         <br/>
         Through our products and events we remind others as much as ourselves of lifes fundamental truths: that life is magical, that we are adored and powerful, and that dreams really do come true.
         <a href="/account/register">
          Joining is FREE
         </a>
         and begins with taking the oath to receive our free daily emails,
         <a class="italic" href="/Inspiration/nftu">
          Notes from the Universe
         </a>
         . In addition to sending out the
         <i>
          Notes
         </i>
         ,
         <a href="/about/mikedooley/">
          Mike Dooley
         </a>
         , founder and author of the
         <i>
          Notes
         </i>
         ,
         <a href="/Education/mike-live">
          hosts talks around the world
         </a>
         on the nature of reality, the law of attraction, creative visualization, the power of thought, and how to create major life changes by leveraging the Universe and engaging life's magic. At this website we host a
         <a href="/tutshop/">
          boutique
         </a>
         and an ever growing list of free resources.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/Humanity/our-story">
          Infinite Possibilities Train the Trainer
         </a>
         is our latest adventure  a project designed to train others how to reach those in our society who have not yet been reached with lifes truths, principally, that our "Thoughts Become Things."
         <!-- Learn how you can become a &lt;a href="/shop/product.php?productid=607&amp;cat=10"&gt;Certified Infinite Possibilities Trainer&lt;/a&gt;!&lt;/p&gt; -->
        </p>
        <p>
         Finally, twice a year we host
         <a href="/Adventure/wow-tours">
          WOW (Wonders of the World) Tours
         </a>
         to some of the most exotic and fascinating places on the planet.
        </p>
        <h2 style="font-weight:lighter;letter-spacing:1px;">
         OUR STORY
        </h2>
        <br/>
        <img alt="TUT" class="center" src="/images/img_about_tuttdays.jpg"/>
        <p>
         In 1989, 2 brothers and their cool mom, launched TUT (then it stood for "Totally Unique T-shirts," now it stands for "The Universe Talks") selling t-shirts and souvenirs to tourists out of a pushcart in Orlando, Florida, with no idea of where we were actually headed. 11 years later, in the year 2000, after selling 1 million of our "Totally Unique T-shirts" through a small chain of our own stores and international distributors, we closed operations and launched todays TUTs Adventurers Club, a web based philosophical club for understanding and living the adventure of life. Using the email addresses we had collected from visitors to our retail stores, we started sending out "Monday Morning Motivators" (inspirational poems taken from our bestselling t-shirts). These weekly emails soon became daily emails, which finally evolved into
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.tut.com/Inspiration/nftu">
           Notes from the Universe
          </a>
         </em>
          short and humorous reminders of lifes magic and our divinity, still the mainstay of all we now do.
        </p>
        <p>
         For us, the most inspiring part of our story was discovering that by setting ourselves into motion, toward the realization of our dream, we also set into motion the entire "Universe" and all of its wisdom, which then, and only then, could conspire tirelessly on our behalf (even taking us "off course" at times) to avail us of treasures and rewards beyond our greatest expectations.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks for coming by, happy trails, and tallyho!
        </p>
