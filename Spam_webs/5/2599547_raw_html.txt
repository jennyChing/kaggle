        Lunch with Philadelphia: Simple Cheesy Ham Sandwich
       </span>

      <!-- #breadcrumbs -->
      <div id="left-area">
       <article class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p class="post-meta">
         Posted By
         <a href="http://romanianmum.com/author/otilia" rel="author" title="Posts by Otilia">
          Otilia
         </a>
         on Jun 3, 2014 |
         <span class="comments-number">
          <a href="http://romanianmum.com/2014/06/lunch-with-philadelphia-simple-cheesy-ham-sandwich.html#respond">
           11 comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         In my previous life I used to spend lots and lots of hours sitting on a chair in a front of my computer at work. Most days I even forgot to drink water or eat as I was so busy!
        </p>
        <p>
         In my previous life, I used to hate warming and preparing my lunch at work. But that was before I discovered Philadelphia cream cheese. Then my life became easier . I dont like using butter in my sandwiches but love this cream cheese! I always buy the low fat variety because although I am addicted to cheese I need to watch my waistline.
        </p>
        <p>
         I have a couple of suggestions for you but for today here is my favourite sandwich of all time (my girls and my husband favourite too):
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/udirMA12VR0" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         And as you can imagine the recipe is very simple too:
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Simple Cheesy Ham Sandwich
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1 slice of wholemeal bread
        </p>
        <p>
         Philadelphia
        </p>
        <p>
         A couple of thin slices of cheddar
        </p>
        <p>
         1 slice of chicken ham
        </p>
        <p>
         1 leaf of lettuce
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Take one slice of wholemeal and spread as much Philadelphia cream cheese as you wish. I like a lot! Cut the slice in two.
         </li>
         <li>
          On one of the slices lay the cheese, then the lettuce and last the ham.
         </li>
         <li>
          Cover with the other half slice.
         </li>
         <li>
          Put it on the plate and enjoy!
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Simple! I love eating it while drinking some yoghurt  it goes better down the throat.
        </p>
        <p>
         My girls enjoy it while drinking some warm milk while my husband loves drinking a big glass full of ice and cola.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_7216.jpg">
          <img alt="Simple Cheesy Ham #Sandwich" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2689" height="558" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_7216-1024x682.jpg" width="838"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         How do you like your Philadelphia sandwich?
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/100314_logo_horspack_PMS_vect1.png">
          <img alt="philadelphia logo" class="aligncenter wp-image-2686 " height="259" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/100314_logo_horspack_PMS_vect1-1024x485.png" width="548"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Stayed tuned with the Romanian Mum Blog though as there is at least one more recipe coming by the end of the week!
        </p>
        <p>
         For more simple and yummy recipes do watch Philadelphias video too:
