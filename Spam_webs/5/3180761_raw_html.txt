       <h1 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://www.codawpro.com/about/" rel="bookmark">
         About
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p>
         Hello all and welcome to CoDAWPro.com,
        </p>
        <p>
         Im Vaakoh, or at least thats what Im known as on the Internet and on multiplayer games. Im a seasoned Call of Duty player with several years of experience. Ive been playing since the original Modern Warfare (CoD 4). No, I havent been there since day 1, but Ive been a dedicated fan of the series for a long time now.
        </p>
        <p>
         Enough about me lets talk about this site. I made this website with the intention of providing every little detail possible for the franchises new title, Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare. This is a new era of CoD and Im here to help you get acclimated to it.
        </p>
        <p>
         On this website youll find everything from the basics of the game to advanced tips and strategies to help you become a master of CoD: AW in online multiplayer, EXO survival mode, and, yes, zombies.
        </p>
        <p>
         Everything you need to know about Advanced Warfare will be on this site. Ill be doing full reviews of every weapon, showing off the best class setups to use, giving you a heads up on every update and DLC that launches, and everything else related to the game. Basically, CoD AW Pro is here to be your one-stop source of information for the game.
        </p>
        <p>
         Since were all gamers and have super short attention spans Ill end this here. I hope you enjoy the site. Feel free to comment on any of the articles, add to them, criticize them, call me a noob its all cool. I welcome any and all comments. And there may be a minor thing here and there that I screw up. Correct me on it. I wont mind a bit.
        </p>
        <p>
         See you scrubs online.
        </p>
        <p>
          Vaakoh
        </p>
