         <h1 class="span6">
          LOL! Lena Dunham Shares All the Deets of Her Dream Wedding, Circa 2002Including a Wedding Dress Illustration
         </h1>
         <div class="byline-container top">
          <div class="byline">
           By
           <cite class="vcard author">
            <a href="/contributors/kim-fusaro">
             Kim Fusaro
            </a>
           </cite>
          </div>
          <div class="date-time-stamp">
           June 10, 2013
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="social-container">
          <div class="sharrre-tools">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="article-text">
         <p>
          <img alt="lena-dunham-getting-naked" data-dam-id="2000000002276190" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/sex-love-life/2013/05/lena-dunham-getting-naked-w724.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Girls
          </em>
          star Lena Dunham
          <a href="http://instagram.com/p/aCILsEi1A1/#">
           Instagramed
          </a>
          an illustration of her dream wedding dress, circa 2002, and I'm kind of obsessed.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="lena-dunham-dream-wedding-circa-2002-wedding-dress-girls-celebrity-weddings-0610" data-dam-id="2000000002296143" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/weddings/2013/06/lena-dunham-dream-wedding-circa-2002-wedding-dress-girls-celebrity-weddings-0610-w724.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
          The caption reads: "An upsetting document from 2002, back when I was really fiendin' to get hitched." (Lena would have been 16 or 17 then!) Please note the over-the-elbow gloves and the tulle hair-a-ma-bob, which has a long beige feather leaping out of it. And are those knee socks?
         </p>
         <p>
          In case you can't read it, the musical selection includes "Live performances from the White Stripes, Belle and Sebastian, and Sade" and the menu features a "pasta bar, tofurkey and stuffing, and tropical drinks."
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           How sweet is this illustration? What would your dream wedding, circa 2002, include? How 'bout when you were 16?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          If I married the
          <strike>
           loser
          </strike>
          <strike>
           schmuck
          </strike>
          <strike>
           total shithead
          </strike>
          guy I was dating in 2002, the wedding would have included a lot of house music and vodka (him) and a live performance by the Dave Matthews Band (me). I probably would have been divorced by 2003. My dream wedding as a 16-year-old was nonexistent. Anything past junior prom was the distant, distant future.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
