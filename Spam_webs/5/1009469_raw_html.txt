        <div class="article-content js-sticky-ad">
         <p>
          Versatile
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/artists/yeah-yeah-yeahs" target="_blank">
           Yeah Yeah Yeahs
          </a>
          frontwoman Karen O has signed to Cult Records  the label founded by
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/artists/the-strokes#timeline" target="_blank">
           the Strokes
          </a>
          lead singer Julian Casablancas in 2009  and will release her first solo album, titled
          <em>
           Crush Songs
          </em>
          , on September 9th. A vinyl-only special edition will also be available prior to the official release and will include drawings and handwritten lyrics.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="inStoryLink" href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/lists/100-best-songs-of-2013-20131204/yeah-yeah-yeahs-sacrilege-19691231-" target="_blank">
           See where "Sacrilege" From Yeah Yeah Yeahs
           <em>
            Mosquito
           </em>
           Fell in our Best Songs of 2013 List
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Karen O recorded the lo-fi set around 2006 and 2007, when Yeah Yeah Yeahs released their Grammy-nominated second album
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/albumreviews/show-your-bones-20060320" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Show Your Bones
           </em>
           .
          </a>
          "When I was 27, I crushed
          <em>
           a lot
          </em>
          ,"the 35-year old vocalist explained in a statement. "These songs were written and recorded in private around this time. They are the soundtrack to what was an ever-continuing love crusade."
         </p>
         <p>
          In his role as Os label boss, Casablancas shared some kind words about the record, saying, "So excited, lucky and proud to be involved with such a classic album  I just want to listen to it all day."
         </p>
         <p>
          Though the Yeah Yeah Yeahs havent officially announced a follow-up to 2013s
          <em>
           Mosquito
          </em>
          , O has been venturing out on her own for some time. Most recently, she received a "
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/karen-o-and-ezra-koenig-team-for-dreamy-moon-song-song-premiere-20140210" target="_blank">
           Best Original Song" Oscar nomination
          </a>
          for "The Moon Song," her contribution to Spike Jonzes
          <em>
           Her
          </em>
          later released as a duet with Ezra Koenig. Singing [that] song with Ezra felt like fulfilling a wish of my die-hard romantic junior high self, O told
          <em>
           Rolling Stone
          </em>
          earlier this year.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lest he be out-multitasked, Casablancas will release his
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/julian-casablancas-and-the-voidz-set-tyranny-release-date-20140623" target="_blank">
           second solo record
          </a>
          alongside his new backing band, The Voidz, on September 23rd. The album, titled
          <em>
           Tyranny
          </em>
          , will also come out on Cult Records, and marks his first solo release since 2009s
          <em>
           Phrazes for the Young
          </em>
          and most recent record since the Strokes 2013 LP
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/albumreviews/comedown-machine-20130318" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Comedown Machine
           </em>
          </a>
          . The Strokes recently played their
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/30-best-things-we-saw-at-governors-ball-2014-20140609/best-homecoming-the-strokes-0308904" target="_blank">
           first shows together in three years
          </a>
          , including a
          <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/30-best-things-we-saw-at-governors-ball-2014-20140609/best-homecoming-the-strokes-0308904" target="_blank">
           classics-filled homecoming performance
          </a>
          earlier this month at New Yorks Governors Ball.
         </p>
