          <p>
           Volunteering in Nepal has become the part of nepals tourism upto this day. Tourists generally come in tourist visa and stay long upto 5 months volunteering in nepal in various programs like

           </a>
           ,volunteering in nepal monastery,

            volunteering in nepal school
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://volunteersummernepal.org/veterinaryvolunteernepal" target="_blank" title="volunteering in nepal animals">
            volunteering in nepal animals
           </a>
           ,volunteer nepal farm, volunteering in nepal elephants,volunteer in nepal hospital,
           <a href="http://volunteersummernepal.org/buddhist-monastery-volunteering-program-in-nepal" target="_blank" title="volunteering in nepal buddhist monastery">
            volunteering in nepal buddhist monastery
           </a>
           , volunteering in nepal construction,volunteer nepal conservation,
           <a href="http://volunteersummernepal.org/nepalagriculturalvolunteerprogram" target="_blank" title="volunteering agriculture in nepal">
            volunteer nepal agriculture
           </a>
           and other various programs for volunteering in nepal experience. Volunteering in Nepal and India can be life changing experience if you choose right volunteer organization in nepal that offers volunteering in nepal for free only asking some money for food, accommodation and tours. Volunteering in nepal for nepalese can become good or bad volunteering in nepal experience based on the program and organization you choose.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are looking volunteering abroad in nepal, know about third eye volunteer foundation nepal which has been able to establish itsself different from other volunteer organizations of nepal. Many companies say that volunteer in nepal for cheap or low cost volunteering in nepal but make sure that the information is true and you are not paying much or by hidden ways. Volunteer nepal disability or doctors volunteering in nepal programs are much in demand and seems contributing.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are thinking i want to volunteer in nepal in your gap year, plan few months ahead which helps to settle things in organized way. Is volunteering in nepal safe can come in your mind when you plan on your mind to go nepal for volunteering. Nepal is safe for volunteering despite of internal political instability and political turmoils as nepal is in the process of great transformation. No any tourists are kidnapped or killed or disrespected or made to feel disgrace in nepal. When people come nepal and are used to with her clture, they say i dont want to go back. Learn many things from nepal helping nepal joining volunteering programs in nepal. The best volunteer program to join in nepal for genuine experience is teaching english volunteering in nepal staying with local host family in countryside.
          </p>
          <p>
           Volunteer works are available in kathmandu nepal but we encourage you to join programs that are located in vilages of nepal where your expections meets. Kathmandu is good for few days tours but not a great place to volunteer based on the law of necessity and simple healthy living. There are volunteering opportunities in kathmandu nepal and in rural areas and many organizations offer you teaching programs to english private schools of kathmandu which keeps not much meaning. Volunteering in nepal pokhara has been choice of many volunteers becuase of its beauty and pleasant climate.
          </p>
          <p>
           Single female volunteers can come to volunteer in nepal and they need not to worry on if it is unsafe place. Volunteers also should follow the manners and etiquities which makes them feel more belonging to the local culture and community. The things also depends on how you act so always follow the middle way path.
          </p>
