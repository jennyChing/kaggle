          <p>
           Ed Boone of NetherRealm studios marked this Friday the 13thon twitter by revealing thatJason Voorhees  the supernatural killer and star of the
           <i>
            Friday the 13th
           </i>
           franchise ofhorror
           <i>
           </i>
           movies  will be making his bloody debutin the upcoming fighting game
           <em>
            Mortal Kombat X.
           </em>
          </p>
          <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" width="550">
           <p>
            Mortal Kombat X Kombat Pack character #1 is.
            <a href="https://t.co/bqh306qcBi">
             https://t.co/bqh306qcBi
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
             Ed Boon (@noobde)
            <a href="https://twitter.com/noobde/status/576376348006084609">
             March 13, 2015
            </a>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
           </script>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Mortal Kombat
           </em>
           is no stranger to guest characters and
           <em>
            Mortal Kombat X
           </em>
           looks to continue that trend. Jason Voorhees is expected to appear as DLC in the Kombat Pack which is included in the Kollectors Edition and Premium Editions of the game, or can be purchased separately for $29.99. The pack will contain three other characters, including two classic fighters and anotherspecial guest  which may or may not be
           <a href="https://twitter.com/noobde/status/484811943354441728" target="_blank">
            Predator
           </a>
           . But hopefully its not, because Alien is definitely better and can kick Predators ass.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Mortal Kombat X
           </em>
           is set to release this April 14th on PS4, PS3, Xbox One, Xbox 360, and PC.
          </p>
          <p>
           What do you think of this reveal? Does Jasons inclusion make you any more excited for the game? Are you ready to
           <em>
            ch-ch-ch-ah-ah-ah
           </em>
           all over the competition? Let us know in the comments!
          </p>
