       <div class="entry" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Casting-on is a challenge for anyone learning how to knit. This cast-on method uses two needles, and produces a smooth, elastic edge.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Want more knitting instructions? Check out the book this was excerpted from: "How to Use, Adapt, and Design Knitting Patterns" (Barron's).
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Source:
         </strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0764145029/ref=as_li_tf_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=craftfocom-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0764145029" target="_blank">
          http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0764145029/ref=as_li_tf_tl...
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <h4 class="step_header_full">
         Step 1
        </h4>
        <div class="step-pic">
         <img alt="" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/256/thumb.?1379051721"/>
         <a class="zoom_image" href="#" rel="step_1256">
          Zoom Image
         </a>
        </div>
        <div id="orginal">
         <img alt="Zoom" class="hidden" id="step_1256" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/256/zoom.?1379051721"/>
        </div>
        <div itemprop="articleSection">
         <p>
          Make a slip knot with a short tail on one needle and hold this in your left hand (if you are right-handed).
          <br/>
          Insert the other needle as if to knit, wrap the yarn from the ball counterclockwise around the tip, and pull the new stitch through the first loop.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <h4 class="step_header_full">
         Step 2
        </h4>
        <div class="step-pic">
         <img alt="" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/257/thumb.?1379051723"/>
         <a class="zoom_image" href="#" rel="step_1257">
          Zoom Image
         </a>
        </div>
        <div id="orginal">
         <img alt="Zoom" class="hidden" id="step_1257" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/257/zoom.?1379051723"/>
        </div>
        <div itemprop="articleSection">
         <p>
          Slip the new stitch onto the left needle in the direction shown. Two stitches are now on the left needle.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <h4 class="step_header_full">
         Step 3
        </h4>
        <div class="step-pic">
         <img alt="" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/258/thumb.?1379051724"/>

        </div>
        <div id="orginal">
         <img alt="Zoom" class="hidden" id="step_1258" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/258/zoom.?1379051724"/>
        </div>
        <div itemprop="articleSection">
         <p>
          Now insert the right needle between the two previous stitches (not through the stitch loop), wrap the yarn as before, and pull through to make another stitch.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <h4 class="step_header_full">
         Step 4
        </h4>
        <div class="step-pic">
         <img alt="" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/259/thumb.?1379051726"/>
         <a class="zoom_image" href="#" rel="step_1259">
          Zoom Image
         </a>
        </div>
        <div id="orginal">
         <img alt="Zoom" class="hidden" id="step_1259" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/craftfoxes-assets/steps/images/000/001/259/zoom.?1379051726"/>
        </div>
        <div itemprop="articleSection">
         <p>
          Slip the new stitch onto the left needle as step 2. Repeat from step 3 as many times as required.
         </p>
        </div>
