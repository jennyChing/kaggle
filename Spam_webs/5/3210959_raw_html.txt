        </a>
       </h2>
      </div>
      <div class="post-content clear">
       <p>
        One of our Fourtheye (and
        <a href="http://toolnavy.com">
         Toolnavy
        </a>
        ) regulars
        <strong>
         Kanaduh
        </strong>
        managed to smuggle a recording device into a recent
        <strong>
         Tool
        </strong>
        sound check, and he managed to capture the band jamming something a little new, as well as a few older classics.  Enjoy!
       </p>
       <p>
        (Needless to say its a little spoilerish if you havent already heard them this tour)
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Update
        </strong>
        : Clip has been removed at the bands request.
