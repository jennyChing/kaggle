       <h2 class="item-title color-a detail">
        Top 10 Blog Posts from The Week: 9/8-12 - PPA Today
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-asset asset hentry" id="entry-2518">
        <div class="asset-header">
         <h1 class="asset-name entry-title" id="page-title">
          Top 10 Blog Posts from The Week: 9/8-12
         </h1>
         <div class="asset-meta">
          <span class="byline vcard">
           By
           <address class="vcard author">
            <a class="fn url" href="http://www.ppa.com">
             Professional Photographers of America (PPA)
            </a>
           </address>
           on
           <abbr class="published" title="2014-09-11T21:11:45+00:00">
            September 11, 2014  9:11 PM
           </abbr>
          </span>
          <span class="separator">
           |
          </span>
          <a class="permalink" href="http://www.ppa.com/ppa-today-blog/inspiration/top-10-blog-posts-from-the-wee-2.php" rel="bookmark">
           Permalink
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="asset-content entry-content">
         <div class="asset-body">
          <a href="http://www.ppa.com/ppa-today-blog/assets_c/2014/07/blog_roundup_graphic-thumb-300x300-1774-thumb-500x500-1793-thumb-500x500-1813-thumb-350x350-1860-thumb-500x500-1867.jpg">
           <img alt="Thumbnail image for Thumbnail image for Thumbnail image for Thumbnail image for Thumbnail image for blog_roundup_graphic.jpg" class="mt-image-right" height="350" src="http://www.ppa.com/ppa-today-blog/assets_c/2014/09/blog_roundup_graphic-thumb-300x300-1774-thumb-500x500-1793-thumb-500x500-1813-thumb-350x350-1860-thumb-500x500-1867-thumb-500x500-1929.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 0 0 20px 20px;" width="350"/>
          </a>
          <div>
           With the first full week of September under your belt, it can be easy to get caught up in the back-to-school crazy and miss out on some pretty awesome blogs from around the interwebs! Here are our top 10!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/jeffrey-shaw/leverage-your-lack-of-focus_b_5725044.html">
            <b>
             Use Your Lack of Focus to Your Advantage
            </b>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div>
           PHOTOGRAPHER'S LIKE SHINY THINGS: Ever feel a little distracted? Learn how to use it to your advantage with this great article by Jeffrey Shaw (he was one of the speakers at Imaging USA 2014) and the Huffington Post. If you can keep your focus until the end, you'll be sure to learn to leverage your wandering mind!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://petapixel.com/2014/09/08/wildlife-photographer/#more-144356">
             I Am Not A Wildlife Photographer...
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           INSPIRATION: Here's a little inspiration from the folks at PetaPixel! Photographer Joseph W. Nienstedt talks about the 20+ types of photographer he isn't and the results might strike a nerve! Check out his beautiful images and story here.
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://www.npr.org/2014/09/09/346840505/9-ridiculously-cute-underwater-puppies-youre-welcome?utm_source=facebook.com&amp;utm_medium=social&amp;utm_campaign=morningedition&amp;utm_term=nprnews&amp;utm_content=20140909">
             Underwater Puppies
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           PUPPIES! How could we resist? Photographer Seth Casteel is back with his follow up book to Underwater Dogs with the only thing more adorable, Underwater Puppies. Let this serve as an inspiration to be open to what your client (in this case, Buster, the dog) wants! And as a bonus,
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/video.php?v=10152751758373689&amp;fref=nf">
            check out his video on Facebook!
           </a>
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://www.thelawtog.com/new-volunteer-photography-contract/">
             Volunteer Photography Contract
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           GOOD BUSINESS: Do you ever use volunteer second shooters? This contract from the Law Tog is perfect for you! Maintain your relationships with your free labor with this easy-to-customize form.
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://www.stuckincustoms.com/2014/09/11/burning-man-and-the-dust/">
             Burning Man And The Dust
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           CLEAN GEAR: Trey Ratcliff from Stuck in Customs gives you his quick tips for suriving Burning Man (and the dust. So. Much. Dust.) How can you capture great images without ruining your best gear? He has the answers here!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://digital-photography-school.com/what-everybody-ought-to-know-about-posing-for-portraits/">
             What Everybody Ought to Know About Posing for Portraits
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           POSING TIPS: Digital Photography School has a quick list of their nine favorite posing guides for portrait photographers from around the interwebs (think of it as a round up with in the round up)! If you're just starting off in this line of work, be sure to check them out!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://www.phototips.biz/2014/09/a-timeapse-of-timelapse.html?utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+blogspot%2FilUfF+%28phototips.biz%29">
             A Timelapse of a Timelapse
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           NO WORDS, JUST WATCH: We're getting pretty meta on this one with the guys at PhotoTips. Here's a timelapse of a timelapse with Maciej Tomkow in Zakynthos, Greece. Not only is it beautiful, but educational to see how he makes his photography work! We had a few "Oh! THAT'S how they do that" moments!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <a href="http://contrastly.com/landscape-composition-tips/?utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_source=FeedPress&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+contrastly+%28Contrastly%29">
            <b>
             10 Must-Know Tips for Composing Landscapes
            </b>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div>
           TIPS FOR LANDSCAPE: Thinking about trying your hand at landscape photography? Join Christina Harmon from Contrastly as she shares her top 10 favorite tips for composing landscapes!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           <b>
            <a href="http://www.featureshoot.com/2014/09/black-and-white-portraits-capture-the-joy-of-jumping/">
             Black and White Portraits Capture the Joy of Jumping
            </a>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
           DIFFERENT PERSPECTIVE: Jump for joy! Capture the moment in mid-air with Tomas Januska and his series entitled "Gravity". According to the article, each subject jumped between 150 and 600 (SIX HUNDRED) times for the camera. The results are stunning!
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
          <div>
           There you have it! The top posts from around the interwebs. What sorts of photography blogs light up your idea bulb? Let us know on
           <a href="http://theloop.ppa.com/communities/viewdiscussions/viewthread/?GroupId=577&amp;MessageKey=6c3bb9fa-8448-406a-b585-478fe06ef957">
            theLoop
           </a>
           !
          </div>
          <div>
           <br/>
          </div>
