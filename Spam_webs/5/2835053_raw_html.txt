      <h1>
       On-site Data Destruction
      </h1>
      <div class="post-748 page type-page status-publish hentry">
       <div class="entry">
        <h2>
         Shred Hard Drives On-site Before They Leave Your Custody With Our Mobile Data Destruction Vehicle  The Data Annihilator
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="The DDV truck engages in mobile data destruction at a local business" class="alignnone wp-image-763 size-full" height="363" src="http://www.synetictechnologies.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DDV.jpg" width="820"/>
         When regulations and chain of custody liability prevent your hard drives and associated media from leaving your facility for data destruction, let Synetic Technologies bring our data destruction vehicle to you. Our mobile data destruction truck is called The Data Annihilator, and for good reason.
        </p>
        <p>
         The first one of its kind in the Kansas City metro area, The Data Annihilator is a self-contained mobile hard drive shredder. There is a lab in the front of the truck where your hard drives and associated media are loaded onto a conveyor belt, which carries the assets to the shredder. The shredders four powerful, rotating cylinders grind the pieces until they are small enough to fit through a screen into a collection bin. The materials are rendered breach-proof and then recycled according to strict R2/RIOS policies that protect your data and the environment.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <img alt="DDV2" class="alignright size-full wp-image-765" height="211" src="http://synetictechnologies.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DDV2.jpg" width="314"/>
         The Data Annihilator eliminates your chain of custody concerns.
        </h3>
        <p>
         HIPPA, HITECH, PHI, PCI, PII, FACTA, GLBA and Sarbanes-Oxley regulations governing industries such as banking, finance, healthcare and government present chain of custody problems for companies. Synetics on-site data destruction solves those challenges, giving you tailored solutions that meet your internal and federal and state compliancy needs.
        </p>
        <p>
         60% of all data breaches occur at the business associate (your vendors) level, trusting data destruction requirements to anyone other than a company that is vetted, audited and certified can be expensive after a data breach. Synetics on-site data destruction ensures your assets never leave your sight before they and the data they contain are destroyed, eliminating the threat of a breach.
        </p>
        <h3>
         A digital camera system records the entire on-site data destruction process.
        </h3>
        <p>
         The Data Annihilator is equipped with a digital camera system. Cameras capture the conveyor belt leading to the shredder in the back of the truck. Another records our trained, experienced technicians handling your hard drives and media as they drop the materials on the conveyor belt. A camera in the rear of the truck records the materials being shredded and shifted through the screen into the container for recycling. Clients are welcome to watch the entire process live from inside the trucks lab or via a web feed to a tablet in the comfort of their offices.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once the on-site hard drive destruction is complete, Synetic provides a Certificate of Destruction and a DVD video (if requested) for confirmation of proof. An audit report is also available, customized to your unique specifications to ensure
         <a href="http://www.synetictechnologies.com/solutions/it-asset-management/" title="IT Asset Management">
          appropriate IT asset management
         </a>
         from start to finish.
        </p>
        <h3>
         When it comes to destroying hard drives on-site, weve thought of everything.
        </h3>
        <p>
         Absolutely nothing is required on your part for our on-site data destruction service. Our technicians and truck need only a parking space or dock access. We take care of the rest. From the generators that power the shredder and HVAC system to the way we reconfigured the vents for improved ventilation, The Data Annihilator is effective, safe and even environmentally conscious with
         <a href="http://www.synetictechnologies.com/solutions/responsible-e-waste-recycling/">
          our zero landfill policy
         </a>
         . Watch it in action below:
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/3Uq3nRMgidQ?feature=oembed" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
