       <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
        <a href="http://sgcafe.com/2013/05/cast-stella-jogakuin-koutouka-c%c2%b3-bu-revealed/">
         Cast of school action anime C-bu revealed
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta-top entry-meta">
        <div class="row">
         <div class="col-md-6">
          <div class="post-date">
           Posted on
           <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2013-05-07T20:30:38+00:00">
            May 7th, 2013
           </time>
          </div>
          <div class="post-author">
           by
           <strong class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://sgcafe.com/author/sunsoftware/" rel="author">
             SunSoftware
            </a>
           </strong>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="col-md-6">
          <ul class="post-categories">
           <li>
            <a href="http://sgcafe.com/category/news/" rel="category tag">
             #News

            <a href="http://sgcafe.com/category/anime/" rel="category tag">

            </a>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="post-content entry-content">
        <iframe frameborder="0" height="90" id="acc65a80" name="acc65a80" scrolling="no" src="http://pikachu.vr-zone.com.sg/www/delivery/afr.php?zoneid=21&amp;cb=INSERT_RANDOM_NUMBER_HERE" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; width: 100%; margin-bottom: 20px;" width="728">
         <a href="http://pikachu.vr-zone.com.sg/www/delivery/ck.php?n=a6a6c6f6&amp;cb=INSERT_RANDOM_NUMBER_HERE" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" border="0" src="http://pikachu.vr-zone.com.sg/www/delivery/avw.php?zoneid=21&amp;cb=INSERT_RANDOM_NUMBER_HERE&amp;n=a6a6c6f6"/>
         </a>
        </iframe>
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://images.sgcafe.net/2013/05/C3bub.png" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://images.sgcafe.net/2013/05/C3bub.png', '']);" target="_blank">
          <img alt="C3bub" class="aligncenter wp-image-47245" height="408" src="http://images.sgcafe.net/2013/05/C3bub-640x435.png" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The next school action anime from the renowned studio Gainax is coming this summer, and among the voice talent joining them are big names like Miyuki Sawashiro and Yui Makino.
         <span id="more-47237">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Stella Jogakuin Koutouka C-bu
         </em>
         (
         <em>
          Stella Womens Academy, High School Division Class C
         </em>
         , not to be confused with
         <em>
          C
         </em>
         , the 2011 love comedy) is centered around a high school girl named Yura Yamato who enters Stella Womans Academy, where she joins a club called C (C-cube), where members compete inmilitary survival games with airsoft guns. The anime is based on the manga of the same name written by Ikoma and drawn by Momoka Midorito.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://images.sgcafe.net/2013/05/C3bu.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://images.sgcafe.net/2013/05/C3bu.jpg', '']);">
          <img alt="C3bu" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-47247" height="300" src="http://images.sgcafe.net/2013/05/C3bu.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The latest issue of Monthly Young Magazine revealed the cast of this upcoming anime, which you can see below. Curiously, it includes stars like Miyuki Sawashiro, as well as a complete newcomer.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Yura Yamato: Yui Makino (Sakura from
          <em>
           Tsubasa Chronicle
          </em>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          Sonora Kashima: Miyuki Sawashiro (Shinku from
          <em>
           Rozen Maiden
          </em>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          Karila Hatsuse: Ai Kayano (Saori from
          <em>
           Girls und Panzer
          </em>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          Honoka Mutsu: Chiwa Saitou (Homura from
          <em>
           Puella Magi Madoka Magica
          </em>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          Rento Kirishima: Rima Nishizima (actress/model debutingas voice actress)
         </li>
         <li>
          Yachiyo Hinata: Madoka Yonezawa (Ui from
          <em>
           K-ON!
          </em>
          )
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Earlier this year, the studio Gainax also revealed the staff of the anime:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Director: Masayoshi Kawajiri (background artist debuting as director)
         </li>
         <li>
          Character Designer: Manami Umeshita (
          <em>
           Medaka Box
          </em>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          Music Composer: Koutarou Nakagawa (
          <em>
           Hayate no Gotoku!
          </em>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          Animation Studio: Gainax (
          <em>
           Neon Genesis Evangelion
          </em>
          )
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         They also revealed a teaser video featuring girls holding firearms in dramatic poses and catchy jazz music.
        </p>
        <div class="arve-wrapper arve-normal-wrapper arve-youtube-wrapper " itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/VideoObject" style="background-image: url(//img.youtube.com/vi/i_eR6iBjXJ4/0.jpg);">
         <div class="arve-embed-container">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="arve-inner arve-hidden" data-src="//www.youtube.com/embed/i_eR6iBjXJ4?iv_load_policy=3&amp;modestbranding=1&amp;rel=0&amp;wmode=transparent&amp;autoplay=1" frameborder="0" id="arve-iframe-1" mozallowfullscreen="" scrolling="no" webkitallowfullscreen="">
          </iframe>
          <button class="arve-inner arve-play-background arve-iframe-btn" data-target="arve-iframe-1">

        </div>

         Fans of Gainax or cute girls fighting with replica guns should keep an eye onC-bu when it airs this July.
