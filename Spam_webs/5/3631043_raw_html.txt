       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Missing Bear and the Hansel &amp; Gretel House
       </h1>
       <p class="post-meta">
        <span>
         Posted by:
         <a href="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/author/admin/" title="">
          Ruth Crilly
         </a>
        </span>
        <span>
         on  March 15, 2015
        </span>
        <span>
         <a href="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/2015/03/15/missing-bear-and-the-hansel-gretel-house/#comments">
          18 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </p>
       <div class="clear">
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="hansel and gretel house" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28884" height="433" src="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IMG_9647-2.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         1) Just to fill you in on where Im at with life: Ive moved. Not through choice, necessarily, but because my
         <a href="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/category/house/">
          house
         </a>
         is being stripped out, renovated and extended. All at once. I petitioned to be left in residence, just my desk and computer left in situ like some kind of workspace island in a sea of debris and builders bums, but everyone involved thought it best that I enjoyed the remainder of my pregnancy in peace and without the risk of steelwork falling down on my head. So here we are  family AMR, minus Mr Bear the cat  in our little barn in the middle of absolute nowhere. No phone reception, shaky internet  its a wonder I havent lost my marbles and its only been a week!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="farm life" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28883" height="433" src="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IMG_9649-2.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This is the view from my spare bedroom window (picture of spare room above, it makes me thinkof the little house that Hansel and Gretel might have lived in!): I am
         <em>
          literally on a farm
         </em>
         ! Keep an ear out for sounds of mooing in my upcoming
         <a href="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/category/videos/">
          videos
         </a>
         ..
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="mr bear the british shorthair cat" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28894" height="433" src="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IMG_4773.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         2) I wanted to clarify that Mr Bear has
         <em>
          not
         </em>
         gone on his holidays because Im pregnant: hes had to be temporarily moved because we couldnt find a single short-term let that allowed cats and that had availability. Actually, there was
         <em>
          one
         </em>
         , but it was 53 miles away. Not ideal. So Mr Bear is currently enjoying the undivided attention that my parents are no doubt giving him  apparently his favourite game is playing chase up and down the stairs and he shouts at them if they dont join in! Ive asked my Mum to send a weekly Mr Bear picture so that we can keep the
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.amodelrecommends.com/2012/03/18/sunday-tittle-tattle-2/">
           Sunday Tittle Tattle
          </a>
         </em>
         tradition alive  the first one is above. Sleeping on the windowsill above the kitchen sink.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">

          <strong>
           Read more Sunday Tittle Tattle posts
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
        </p>
