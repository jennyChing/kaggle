   <h1 class="post-title">
    Costs of Building a Data Center
   </h1>
   <div id="main">
    <div id="content">
     <div class="post" id="post-6304">
      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        Do you have a growing stack of servers hanging out in a closet in the back of your office or stuffed into the maintenance closet of your office building?
       </p>
       <p>
        Do you constantly have to re-set the server for your SaaS? Or have you ever had to do server maintenance because the server got too hot or there was damage to your building?
       </p>
       <p>
        Instead of going the way of Facebook and building a data center, consider leasing. The short and long term benefits of leasing from a hosted data center will help you sleep late at night and keep your servers happy too!
       </p>
       <p>
        Check out our infographic on the costs of building a data center and see why leasing is so much better!
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://mapadatacenter.com/wp-content/uploads/V4_CostofDataCenter-1.jpg">
         <img alt="building vs. leasing a data center" class="aligncenter wp-image-6305" height="1728" src="http://mapadatacenter.com/wp-content/uploads/V4_CostofDataCenter-1.jpg" width="490"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
       </p>
       <p>
        Embed Code
        <br/>
        <textarea cols="70" rows="6">
         <img alt="building vs. leasing a data center" border="0" src="http://mapadatacenter.com/wp-content/uploads/V4_CostofDataCenter-1.jpg" title="Cost of Building a Data Center" width="100%"/>
         <br/>
         Brought to you by:
         <a href="http://mapadatacenter.com/">
          Map a Data Center
         </a>
        </textarea>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Benefits
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Leasing
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         predictable cost
        </li>
        <li>
         add-on capability
        </li>
        <li>
         run by professionals
        </li>
        <li>
         other lessees
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Building
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         complete control
        </li>
        <li>
         work in close proximity
        </li>
        <li>
         low risk of location loss
        </li>
       </ul>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Costs
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Leasing
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Up-front fees
        </li>
        <li>
         network cross-connected fees
        </li>
        <li>
         monthly lease payments
        </li>
        <li>
         power bills
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Building
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         up front fees
        </li>
        <li>
         complex fire and suppression systems
        </li>
        <li>
         purchase of equipment
        </li>
        <li>
         network connection cost
        </li>
        <li>
         power bills
        </li>
        <li>
         staff
        </li>
        <li>
         annual facility and infrastructure maintenance
        </li>
       </ul>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Risks
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Leasing
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Possibility of being forced to leave the facility
        </li>
        <li>
         outgrowing facility
        </li>
        <li>
         lease not renewed
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Building
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Size miscalculationbuilding too little or too much
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
       </p>
