                      <blockquote>

                       <a href="http://www.bleubirdvintage.com/collections/ladybird">
                        bluebird vintage
                       </a>
                       this week. the stock is beautifully presented! i love that detailed measurements are offered and the prices aren't too shabby either. international shipping available.

                      </blockquote>

                       <br/>
                      </blockquote>

                       <a href="http://zenhabits.net/brief-guide/">
                        this
                       </a>
                       is a quick guide which links to various amazing articles about how to lead a more fulfilling life.

                      </blockquote>
