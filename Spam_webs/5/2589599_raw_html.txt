        <p>
         Party planning doesnt need to be stressful. Alleviatesome of the stress of hosting for the Big Gameby adding the IN-VINCE-IBLE pizza and sandwichto your game day menu from your local Big Ys Pizza and Sub shop!
        </p>
        <p>
         IN-
         <strong>
          VINCE
         </strong>
         -IBLEas in defensive lineman
         <strong>
          Vince Wilfork
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre a football fan, youre probably familiar with Vinces name. And you may have also seen recent news reports on his heroismfor helping a woman out of her overturned Jeep.
        </p>
        <p>
         But did you also know that hes a spokesperson for
         <strong>
          <a href="http://bit.ly/1wkd2by" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Big Y
          </a>
         </strong>
         ? Check out this funvideo.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/VGdcnCdjtHM?list=PLcxamblKHfPMRc900t7EqgtuTBOXd7z2g" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <strong>
          <a href="http://bit.ly/1upX63f" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           IN-VINCE-IBLE pizza and sandwich
          </a>
         </strong>
         feature the same delicious ingredients. These game day party favorites are loaded with ham,pepperoni, hard salami, capicola hot ham, and cheese; and then topped with tomatoes, green peppers, roasted red peppers, onions, banana peppers and basil pesto!  The pizza is also made with Big Ys fresh pizza dough (that same dough that I used to make my game day Barbeque Chicken Stromboli, which you might remember from a couple weeks ago) and their signature sauce. And since these are pre-made, theyre perfect last minute additions to your Big Game party menu.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont they look delicious?! I can tell you from personal experience that they are!
        </p>
        <p>
         The Big Y  Vince Wilfork relationship goes beyond simply selling products though. Vince has teamed up with Big Ys Living Well Eating Smart Wellness Team, as well as registered dietitians Carrie Taylor and Andrea Samson, to help tackle diabetes. Having watched his father battle the disease for years, Vince has devoted his foundation to finding a cure for diabetes!
        </p>
        <p>
         But back to the IN-VINCE-IBLE sandwich and pizza
        </p>
        <p>
         Theyre not just for game day party events.
        </p>
        <p>
         The other day I picked them up at Big Ys Pizza &amp; Sub shop for my daughters high school track team. Everyone is always so friendly there, and they made my order while I shopped. I was only picking up a few things, so it was a pleasant surprise to return to the counter and find it ready to go so quickly!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.adishofdailylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Pizza-and-Sub-Dept-at-Big-Y.png">

           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
