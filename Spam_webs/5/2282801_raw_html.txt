   <h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">
    List of school-related attacks
   </h1>
   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
    <div id="siteSub">

    </div>

    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <p>
      This is a
      <b>
       list of school-related attacks
      </b>
      . These are attacks that have occurred on school property or related primarily to school issues or events. A broad definition of the word
      <i>
       attacks
      </i>
      is used for this list so as to include public attacks or one's self (
      <a href="/wiki/Suicide" title="Suicide">
       suicide
      </a>
      ).
     </p>
     <p>
      The list is divided into three main chronological sections according to school age:
      <a href="/wiki/Primary_education" title="Primary education">
       primary
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Secondary_education" title="Secondary education">
       secondary
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="/wiki/Higher_education" title="Higher education">
       post-secondary
      </a>
      . Additionally, a list is provided detailing foiled school-related attack plots that resulted in a conviction.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
