       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-02-25T06:00:21+00:00">
         February 25, 2015
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/2015/02/25/mini-baileys-chocolate-cheesecake-trifles/#comments">
          13 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lifeloveandsugar.com%2F2015%2F02%2F25%2Fmini-baileys-chocolate-cheesecake-trifles%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lifeloveandsugar.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F02%2FBaileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles5.jpg&amp;description=Mini%20Baileys%20Chocolate%20Cheesecake%20Trifles" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         These Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles are full of chocolate and Baileys flavor! I am totally in love with the fun mix in textures. Between no bake cheesecake, cookie crumbles and whipped cream  these trifles have it all!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles - layers of Baileys chocolate cheesecake, Baileys whipped cream and crushed Oreos! So yummy and perfect for St. Patrick's Day!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11587" height="855" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles5.jpg" width="575"/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles - layers of Baileys chocolate cheesecake, Baileys whipped cream and crushed Oreos! So yummy and perfect for St. Patrick's Day!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11584" height="870" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles2.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You know those days where you totally think youre killin it? At least in one aspect. And then
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, that happened to me the other day. I bought all the stuff to make dinner in the slow cooker for a night the latter part of last week. I try to have a slow cooker meal at least one night a week  usually Wednesday, since thats the night we go to small group.
        </p>
        <p>
         Well for some reason I just could not remember to put everything into the slow cooker in the morning. Twice I forgot to do it. Twice.
        </p>
        <p>
         #fail
        </p>
        <p>
         So finally Monday morning, I was all ready to go. The meat needed to be used since wed been out of town for the weekend and I couldnt use it. So I put the stuff I needed right in the middle of the counter Sunday night so that there was no way Id forget the next day.
        </p>
        <p>
         And I didnt! I put everything into the pot and felt awesome about it. I couldnt wait to get home to dinner already made, leaving me more time to bake.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I walked in the door after work and realized that while I had put everything into the slow cooker, I never turned the thing on. FAIL! For the third time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its the story of my life lately. Too many things swirling around in my head at one time. Too many fails.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles - layers of Baileys chocolate cheesecake, Baileys whipped cream and crushed Oreos! So yummy and perfect for St. Patrick's Day!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11583" height="852" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles.jpg" width="575"/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles - layers of Baileys chocolate cheesecake, Baileys whipped cream and crushed Oreos! So yummy and perfect for St. Patrick's Day!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11586" height="868" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles4.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Fortunately it was something I could throw on the stove and cook and it turned out fine. The hubs was happy with it, so that was what mattered.
        </p>
        <p>
         Clearly I need chocolate to make me feel better though. These Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles will do just fine. Not only are they chocolate, but the are spiked.
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I sure hope you love Baileys as much as I do. I have used it in so many recipes 
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/2013/02/26/st-patricks-day-baileys-irish-cream-puppy-chow-2/">
           Baileys Puppy Chow
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/2014/03/04/baileys-chocolate-pie/">
           Baileys Chocolate Pie
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/2013/03/05/no-bake-baileys-irish-cream-cookie-balls/">
           No Bake Baileys Cookie Balls
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="om/2014/03/06/baileys-chocolate-cookies/">
           Baileys Chocolate Cookies dipped in white chocolate
          </a>
          .
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         I decided I wanted to use it in some cheesecake, but keep it kind of simple and layer it in a jar/cup. I love the way textures can be combined when you pile everything into a cup or jar. Here are our layers in this particular dessert:
        </p>
        <p>
          Thick, creamy Baileys chocolate cheesecake  no bake, I might add
         <br/>
          Light and lovely Baileys whipped cream
         <br/>
          Crumbly, crushed Oreo cookies
        </p>
        <p>
         So many things to love with two layers of each. The Baileys, the chocolate  yum! I used dark cocoa, so these have a very deep chocolatey flavor. You could certainly use regular cocoa, if you prefer.
        </p>
        <p>
         These cheesecake trifles would make an awesome treat for St. Patricks Day, or for any time youre looking to spice things up with some Baileys action. I mean really, is there ever a bad time for Baileys? Not in my opinion!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles - layers of Baileys chocolate cheesecake, Baileys whipped cream and crushed Oreos! So yummy and perfect for St. Patrick's Day!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11585" height="865" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles3.jpg" width="575"/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles - layers of Baileys chocolate cheesecake, Baileys whipped cream and crushed Oreos! So yummy and perfect for St. Patrick's Day!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11588" height="863" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baileys_Chocolate_Cheesecake_Trifles6.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

         <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
           <div class="item b-b">
            <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
             <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-253', 'http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/plugins/zip-recipes/'); return false" rel="nofollow" title="Print this recipe">

            <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
             Mini Baileys Chocolate Cheesecake Trifles
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
             <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
              Yield:
              <span itemprop="recipeYield">
               4 trifles
              </span>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="zlclear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             <span class="bold">
              Cheesecake layer
             </span>
             <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="//stats.ziprecipes.net/piwik.php?idsite=1&amp;rec=1" style="width: 1px; height: 1px; border:0" width="1"/>
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             16 oz cream cheese, room temperature
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup cocoa (I used Hershey's Special Dark)
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             4 tbsp Baileys
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             <br/>
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             <span class="bold">
              Whipped cream and cookies
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup heavy whipping cream, cold
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tbsp Baileys
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 tbsp cocoa
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 tbsp powdered sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             15 Oreo cookies, crushed
            </div>
           </span>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <span class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             1. Beat cream cheese, sugar and cocoa together in a bowl until smooth.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             2. Add Baileys and mix until well incorporated. Set aside.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             3. In another bowl, whip heavy whipping cream until it starts to thicken.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             4. Add Baileys, cocoa and powdered sugar and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             5. To make the trifles, divide half of the cookie crumbs evenly between the four cups (or jars) and spread into an even layer in the bottom of the cups.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             6. Pipe, or spoon, an even layer of cheesecake filling on top of the cookie crumbs in each of the four cups. You should only use about half of the filling.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             7. Pipe, or spoon, an even layer of whipped cream on top of the cheesecake filling in each of the four cups.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             8. Divide remaining cookie crumbs between the four cups and evenly distribute the second layer of crumbs on top of the whipped cream. You can keep a little bit of crumbs aside, if you want to use them to sprinkle on top of the finished trifles.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             9. Pipe, or spoon, remaining cheesecake filling on top of cookie crumbs, distributing evenly between the four cups.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             10. Top each trifle with a swirl of whipped cream and a few more cookie crumbs, if you kept some aside in step 8.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             11. Refrigerate until ready to serve. I recommend setting them out for about 15 minutes prior to eating, so that they aren't too firm.
            </div>
           </span>
