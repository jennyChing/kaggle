         <div id="description-text">
          Bermuda Blue Stud Earrings  6mm Swarovski Crystal Cube Titanium Post Earring  Hypoallergenic ALS Awareness Jewelry
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Breathtaking vintage Swarovski Crystal cube in Bermuda Blue, a Swarovski special effect that shifts from bright aqua blue to sea green shades.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          These crystals measure 6mm wide, and are mounted to the earring findings by a flattened corner, so you view the color in through the opposite corner!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The crystals are secured to earring findings made of surgical stainless steel pads with titanium posts.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Stainless steel ear nuts.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Photos 1-3 taken in direct sunlight.  Photos 4-5 taken in filtered artificial light.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Titanium is an inert metal and does not react to commonly found chemicals, such as chlorine, making it absolutely hypoallergenic -- No more allergic reactions or green skin!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The earring pad is a little larger than the flat section of the crystal, but it is not visible from the front of the crystal.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          **Adhesive is an industrial strength, jewelry grade adhesive. It is waterproof, but I recommend removing the earrings for bathing or sleeping. Some adhesive may appear around the cup part of the earring finding - it will not show through the front of the earring. Do not pull on the adhesive or the earring may dis-assemble. As it is winter, if earrings arrive cold, allow to warm to room temperature before removing from earring card.**
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          I created these earrings in honor and memory of my dear friend Kristy's mother, Ruth, and her grandmother. Ruth's favorite color was blue, hence the fiery brilliance of the Bermuda Blue Swarovski crystal featured in this design. Both of these amazing women passed away due to ALS, aka Lou Gehrig's disease, which is a 100% fatal neurodegenerative disease.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Every 90 minutes an American dies of ALS.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Part of the proceeds from the sale of each of these ALS earrings will be donated to The ALS Association's Walk to Defeat ALS Warren / Youngstown event.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The ALS Association is the only non-profit organization dedicated to finding a cure, and providing comfort and compassion to those diagnosed with ALS and their families. For more information, visit http://www.alsa.org/
