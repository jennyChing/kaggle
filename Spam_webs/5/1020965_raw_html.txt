        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          One of the first content-specific things that one always thinks about when starting up a brand new blog is the first batch of content. Those first few posts which are supposed to help kick off the blog with a bang.
         </p>
         <p>
          These are often called pillar posts.
         </p>
         <p>
          In this Office Hours call with VIP members, I started off by talking about the strategies you should keep in mind while creating those first few pillar posts all with an eye on the goal of actually generating revenue from the blog.
         </p>
