        <h1 class="entry-title">
         UNIT:Extinction Announced by Big Finish, starring Jenna Redgrave as Kate Stewart
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="author vcard">
          By
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://exiledingeeksville.com/author/geeksville/" title="View all posts by David Vandervliet">
           David Vandervliet
          </a>
          <span class="bl_sep">
           
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="onDate">
          <a href="http://exiledingeeksville.com/2015/02/09/unitextinction-announced-by-big-finish-starring-jenna-redgrave-as-kate-stewart/" rel="bookmark">
           February 9, 2015
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <a href="http://exiledingeeksville.com/category/audio-drama/" rel="tag">
           Audio Drama
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://exiledingeeksville.com/category/television/doctor-who/" rel="tag">
           Doctor Who
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://exiledingeeksville.com/category/features/" rel="tag">
           Features
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Big Finish, the production company producing original audio dramas based on the classic 20th century version of
          <em>
           Doctor Who,
          </em>
          starring all the surviving original cast members, since 1999 has just gotten their first license from the BBC to produce audios set in the New Series. Just not featuring any of the new series Doctors.
         </p>
         <div class="box roundedAll grid_9 news">
          <div class="newsItem content noBorder">
           <div class="body">
            <div class="newsbody">
             <h4>
              Big Finish is delighted to announce that Kate Stewart and her UNIT team from the Doctor Who TV series will be starring in a brand new series of audio dramas, in a licensing deal with BBC Worldwide.
             </h4>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              Big Finish will be producing 4 Four hour box sets, starring Kate, and her team as they fight to defend the Earth from all invaders, remember always, that 
              <em>
               science leads
              </em>
              . The first box set,
              <a href="http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/unit---extinction-1208" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/unit---extinction-1208', 'UNIT: Extinction ']);" target="_blank">
               UNIT: Extinction
              </a>
              will be released in November of this year, and all four are available as a bundle for pre-order from the Big Finish
              <a href="http://www.bigfinish.com/bundles/start/101/0" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bigfinish.com/bundles/start/101/0', 'website']);" target="_blank">
               website
              </a>
              as either CDs or digitial download, with all pre-orders getting a copy of
              <em>
               <strong>
                Nicholas Courtney
               </strong>
              </em>
              s memoirs
              <a href="http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/the-nicholas-courtney-memoirs---a-soldier-in-time-1016" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/the-nicholas-courtney-memoirs---a-soldier-in-time-1016', 'A Soldier in Time']);">
               A Soldier in Time
              </a>
              as a free download.
              <a href="http://exiledingeeksville.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/UNIT-New-Series-BF.jpg">
               <img alt="UNIT-New-Series from Big Finish" class="alignright wp-image-7877" height="275" src="http://exiledingeeksville.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/UNIT-New-Series-BF-300x207.jpg" width="400"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              Kate is the daughter of
              <strong>
               <em>
                Brigadier Lethbridge-Stewart
               </em>
              </strong>
              , and Head of Scientific Research at the
              <em>
               <strong>
                Unified Intelligence Taskforce,
               </strong>
              </em>
              which investigates and confronts alien attacks on the planet Earth. The role is portrayed by
              <em>
               <strong>
                Jemma Redgrave
               </strong>
              </em>
              (
              <em>
               Dracula, Frankie, Cold Blood
              </em>
              ) who has appeared opposite
              <em>
               Matt Smith
              </em>
              and
              <em>
               Peter Capaldi
              </em>
              on television, and returns for the audio series.
             </p>
             <p>
              
              <em>
               Were thrilled that Jemma is on board for our brand new UNIT adventures
              </em>
              , says series producer
              <em>
               <strong>
                David Richardson,
               </strong>
              </em>
              a
              <em>
               nd we feel privileged to work within the universe of New Series Doctor Who for the first time. UNIT: Extinctionwill showcase all the excitement, drama and wit that viewers of Doctor Who love.
              </em>
              
             </p>
             <p>
              
              <em>
               Having worked with the greatly missed Nicholas Courtney and his magnificent portrayal of the Brigadier since the early days of Big Finish, it is wonderful to see the return of UNIT under the Brigs daughte
              </em>
              r, says executive producer Jason Haigh-Ellery. 
              <em>
               We are all very excited about bringing UNIT back to Big Finish with the next generation and look forward immensely to working with Jemma again, who appears in this months Doctor Who main range release
              </em>
              <a href="http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/mistfall-867" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bigfinish.com/releases/v/mistfall-867', 'Doctor Who  Mistfall']);">
               <em>
                Doctor Who  Mistfall
               </em>
              </a>
              <em>
               .
              </em>
              
             </p>
             <p>
              
              <em>
               Im really excited to hear Jemma and her team battling to save the Earth,
              </em>
               says executive producer
              <em>
               <strong>
                Nick Briggs.
               </strong>
              </em>
              
              <em>
               Theyve been such an important part of the many exciting things to come out of the Doctor Who TV series in recent years
              </em>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              Additional cast members for the first box set, UNIT: Extinction, will be revealed later. This first box set release comprises four hours of adventure, in which Kate and her team confront an alien invasion by the
              <em>
               <strong>
                Nestene Consciousness
               </strong>
              </em>
              and its army of plastic
              <em>
               <strong>
                Autons
               </strong>
              </em>
              .
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
