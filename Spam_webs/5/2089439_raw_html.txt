          Natalie Portmans iTunes Mix
         </h1>
         <ul class="post-details clearfix">
          <li>

            Mike
           </a>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Natalie Portman has compiled a collection of her favorite tracks for iTunes.
         </p>
         <p>
          Proceeds from the collection, titled
          <em>
           Big Change: Songs for FINCA
          </em>
          , will benefit FINCA International, a charity organization that provides financial services to the worlds lowest-income entrepreneurs, for which Portman acts as a Ambassador of Hope.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center">
          <img border="1" height="355" src="http://i11.photobucket.com/albums/a180/spraygraphic/natalie-portman.jpg" width="310"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Natalie Portman has a killer taste in music, so you know this mix will be alright  shes a big Yeah Yeah Yeahs fan.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here is is the tracklisting:
          <br/>
          1. Be Good 
          <a href="http://www.myspace.com/tokyopoliceclub" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.myspace.com']);" target="_blank">
           Tokyo Police Club
          </a>
          <br/>
          2. My Night With the Prostitute From Marseille [exclusive]  Beirut
          <br/>
          3. Plaid Lined Jacket  Tom Brosseau
          <br/>
          4. Tudo Bem Malandro  Curumin
          <br/>
          5. Australia (Bjorn Yttling Mix) [exclusive] 
          <a href="http://www.crushable.com/terminal-5-presents-the-shins/" target="_blank">
           The Shins
          </a>
          <br/>
          6. Aint No Reason  Brett Dennen
          <br/>
          7. Turnaroundturnmeon  Sean Hayes
          <br/>
          8. Oh Yes, Another Mother  Thee More Shallows
          <br/>
          9. The Beast  Angus &amp; Julia Stone
          <br/>
          10. Paddys Gone  Antony &amp; the Johnsons
          <br/>
          11. Idle Ties  Vetiver
          <br/>
          12. Broken  Norah Jones
          <br/>
          13. Theres Always Something Happening [exclusive]  Devendra Banhart
          <br/>
          14. What Is a Soul? [exclusive]  M. Ward
          <br/>
          15. Forgiveness Figg (Bethany Hotel Blues)  Wooden Wand
          <br/>
          16. How We Landed [exclusive] 
          <a href="http://www.myspace.com/roguewave" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.myspace.com']);" target="_blank">
           Rogue Wave
          </a>
         </p>
