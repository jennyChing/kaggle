       <p>
        Autonomous vehicles are what our society strives for and envision. In movies, we see the future as being carted around in a self-driving car like in Minority Report, Total Recall, Blade Runner, Demolition Man, and iRobot. Speaking of robots, self-driving cars are pretty much like a robot chauffeuring people around and for some, this is incredible, while others are skeptical of robots taking over the world.
       </p>
       <p>
