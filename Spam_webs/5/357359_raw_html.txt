         <span style="color: #ff0000;">

It is so easy to use, even my 2 year old can help
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cf.laurenkellynutrition.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         </span>

         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          I started with a basic frozen yogurt recipe. I wanted to use fresh strawberries from our local farmers market and only used 3 Tablespoons of sugar in the entire recipe. Compare that to Breyers or Edys! My frozen yogurt has just 4
          <strong>
           INGREDIENTS!
          </strong>
          Woooohooooo! Again, I am thrilled about my new prize
         </span>
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cf.laurenkellynutrition.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          <strong>
           Strawberry Frozen Yogurt
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h2>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          Ingredients
         </span>
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           3/4cupreduced fat milk of your choice
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           1/4 cup turbinado sugar (or sweetener of your choice)
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           4cups vanilla non fat greek yogurt
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           2cups strawberries,pureed
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          Directions:
         </span>
        </h2>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <div>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            In a medium bowl, mix the milk and sugar until the sugar is dissolved (about 1 to 2 minutes on low speed).
           </span>
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Stir in the yogurt and strawberry puree.
           </span>
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Freeze according to manufacturers instructions. (I spooned it into a tall, upright plastic container to place in the freezer).
           </span>
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           <a href="http://cf.laurenkellynutrition.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/straw-fro-yo1.jpg">

           </a>
          </div>
         </li>
        </ol>
