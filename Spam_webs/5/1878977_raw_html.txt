      <article class="post clearfix cat-1156 cat-927 post-6798 type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-kitchen category-my-favorite-finds tag-craigslist tag-piano-island tag-video" id="post-6798">

       <h1 class="post-title">
        <a href="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/repurposed-antique-piano-kitchen-island/">
         Want to see our kitchen island? I THINK YOU ARE GOING TO LIKE IT. *video*
        </a>
       </h1>
       <!-- /post-title -->
       <!-- /post-image -->
       <!-- post-content -->
       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         Last summer I saw a posting on Craigslist. I felt like my teeth were being electrocuted, which is how you know something good is happening.
         <br/>
         Or that you are about to have a seizure.
        </p>
        <p>
         I went to show Paul, and he said we are
         <em>
          definitely
         </em>
         not getting a piano.
        </p>
        <p>
         This is exactly what I was hoping he would say, because who does not lovebeing the one to reveal an exciting plot twist?
        </p>
        <p>
         I shouted
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          SURPRISE!
         </span>
         <br/>
         We are DEFINITELY getting a piano!
        </p>
        <p>
         I thrust my fist into the air to indicate action and piano-getting.
         <br/>
         I said AT DAWN WE RIDE.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I marched up and down the street with my gong, announcing Hereye! Hereye! I SHALL HAVE ALL OF THE GIANT FANCY THINGS.
        </p>
        <p>
         Also, for the record, only someone who does not read my blog would mistake this for a piano. CLEARLY, this is
         <a href="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/our-old-house-kitchen-design/" target="_blank">
          our kitchen
         </a>
         island.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://victoriabarnes.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/antique-furniture-repurposed-into-kitchen-island.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[6798]">
          <img alt="Were repurposing this AMAZING piece of antique furniture into our KITCHEN ISLAND its the first step in our old Victorian house DIY kitchen remodel." class="alignnone wp-image-6814 size-full pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/antique-furniture-repurposed-into-kitchen-island.jpg" data-pin-url="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/repurposed-antique-piano-kitchen-island/" height="1188" src="http://victoriabarnes.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/antique-furniture-repurposed-into-kitchen-island.jpg" width="638"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-6798">
         </span>
         I explained my idea to Paul at length which took about five seconds.
         <br/>
         Here it is, for your consideration.
        </p>
        <p>
         IDEA:
         <br/>
         Acquire item of giant fanciness.
         <br/>
         Put the giant fanciness in the kitchen.
         <br/>
         The End.
        </p>
        <p>
         I guess I did not explain it right, because Paul said  dont you think a
         <em>
          piano
         </em>
         in the kitchen might be a little bit
         <em>
          much?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         This confused me, because what? A
         <em>
          little
         </em>
         bit much? No. I think it would be a LOT much
         <em>
          much like myself.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Besides, since when have I shied away from committing to something ridiculous? If I dont use this, I will have to re-examine everything I hold to be trueabout myself.
        </p>
        <p>
         I may need to go to therapy where a primary topic of self-examination will be analyzing why I married a man who wants me to LIVE WITHOUT ALL OF THE GIANT FANCY THINGS.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://victoriabarnes.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/repurposing-antique-furniture-into-kitchen-island1.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[6798]">
          <img alt="Were repurposing this AMAZING piece of antique furniture into our KITCHEN ISLAND its the first step in our old Victorian house DIY kitchen remodel." class="alignnone wp-image-6816 size-full pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/repurposing-antique-furniture-into-kitchen-island1.jpg" data-pin-url="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/repurposed-antique-piano-kitchen-island/" height="960" src="http://victoriabarnes.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/repurposing-antique-furniture-into-kitchen-island1.jpg" width="638"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Paul thought about this. Then he went back and rifled through his rolodex of anticlimactic responses.
        </p>
        <p>
         He said  well, its an interesting idea. Lets think about it for a little whileTo you, this might seemreasonable. To me, it isacovert attack on my mental health.
        </p>
        <p>
         Paulknows perfectly well that thinking about things for a little while is my very least favorite activity It is so much more exciting and satisfying to just GO. MORE. FASTER.
         <br/>
         GO DOG GO.
         <br/>
         DISCO!
        </p>
        <p>
         I wanted to fling myself on the floor and foam at the mouth to express the extent to which he was hampering my acquisition process and harming my very existenceBut I had not finished looking on Craigslist.
        </p>
        <p>
         So I just whispered Vive la Rvolution!
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I scurried off to get into my French peasant costume and polish my pitchfork out in the barn while planning my revolt.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Libert, galit, fraternit craigslist!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://victoriabarnes.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/repurposing-antique-furniture-into-kitchen-island.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[6798]">
          <img alt="Were repurposing this AMAZING piece of antique furniture into our KITCHEN ISLAND its the first step in our old Victorian house DIY kitchen remodel." class="alignnone wp-image-6809 size-full pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/repurposing-antique-furniture-into-kitchen-island.jpg" data-pin-url="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/repurposed-antique-piano-kitchen-island/" height="1119" src="http://victoriabarnes.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/repurposing-antique-furniture-into-kitchen-island.jpg" width="638"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Usually the revolt takes a couple of days.
         <br/>
         This one took months.
         <br/>
         <em>
          Months.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Stalemate. After impasse. After deadlock.
        </p>
        <p>
         Fortunately, I amtrained for this: this entire
         <a href="http://victoriaelizabethbarnes.com/how-we-ended-up-buying-and-restoring-an-old-victorian-house/" target="_blank">
          house project
         </a>
         has been an extended drill session where Ive eschewed all reasonable housewares and set fire to anything from Homegoods.
        </p>
        <p>
         And ultimately, I guess Paulforgot to ask himself the one really, really, really important question:If this piano exists, DO I THINK VICTORIA WILL CHOOSE ANYTHING ELSE?
        </p>
        <p>
         ***
        </p>
        <p>
         NOW, a video and a note to say:I LOVE you people. YOU total strangers who GET the riding at dawn and the gong and the giant fanciness xoxoxo times 10,000.
         <br/>
         love,
         <br/>
         VEB
        </p>
