             <p>
              <strong>
               Nepal Earthquake: Over 2,500 died:-
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Reportedly it has been confirmed by agencies that over 2,500 peoplehave been found dead in Nepal. The rescuers are busy digging the pilled up bodies to clear the space for the ones trapped. Its really very amusing to see those bodies lie out there and no one able to recognize the bodies. Nepal on Saturday was rocked by a 7.9-magnitude earthquake. It was the highest recorded since 81 years.
             </p>
             <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_544" style="width: 848px">
              <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.everydsports.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/nepal-earthquake-rescue-02.jpg">
               <img alt="People try to dig out bodies from under the rubble of a destroyed building after an earthquake hit Nepal, in Kathmandu, Nepal, 25 April 2015. The epicentre was 80 kilometres north-west of Kathmandu, United States Geological Survey. Strong tremors were also felt in large areas of northern and eastern India and Bangladesh.  EPA/NARENDRA SHRESTHA  ATTENTION EDITORS: GRAPHIC CONTENT." class="size-full wp-image-544" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.everydsports.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/nepal-earthquake-rescue-02.jpg?resize=838%2C559"/>
              </a>
              <p class="wp-caption-text">
               People try to dig out bodies from under the rubble of a destroyed building after an earthquake hit Nepal, in Kathmandu, Nepal, 25 April 2015. A 7.9-magnitude earthquake rocked Nepal destroying buildings in Kathmandu and surrounding areas, with unconfirmed rumours of casualties. EPA/NARENDRA SHRESTHA ATTENTION EDITORS: GRAPHIC CONTENT.
              </p>
             </div>
             <p>
              Everyone rushed to
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://whc.unesco.org/en/list/121" rel="unesco" target="_blank" title="Kathmandu Valley ">
               Kathmandu valley
              </a>
              which got heavily crowed and resulted in over 2,500
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cadaver" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Cadaver">
               dead bodies
              </a>
              . The whole civilization and vegetation crumbled into pieces. This was a huge hit to Nepal. It was good to see the
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Developed_country" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Developed country">
               developed countries
              </a>
              to come in support of Nepal and help them to get out of this devastating situation. Whole Nepal is terrorized and there was not only 1 earthquake that hit Nepal but there were many others to follow. Approximately there were 9 to 10 more
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earthquake" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Earthquake">
               earthquakes
              </a>
              felt after the first huge one.
             </p>
             <p>
              Nepal was not the only country to suffer. Even in northern
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=28.6133333333,77.2083333333&amp;spn=10.0,10.0&amp;q=28.6133333333,77.2083333333 (India)&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation" target="_blank" title="India">
               India
              </a>
              the death count has reached up to 500. All the schools and colleges have been closed. People are reportedly sent to safer places and
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_India" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Government of India">
               Indian government
              </a>
              is taking active part in it. This Nepal Earthquake has come as an black cloud to the whole
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Asia" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="South Asia">
               southern Asia
              </a>
              . Both the countries are in huge panic.
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.narendramodi.in/" rel="homepage" target="_blank" title="Narendra Modi">
               Narendra Modi
              </a>
              the
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_India" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Prime Minister of India">
               Indian Prime Minister
              </a>
              has sent a big team for rescue operation in Nepal and also alerts whole of
              <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_India" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="North India">
               North India
              </a>
              to move to slower places. The metros and trains in India have also been stopped as a safety measure.
             </p>
             <p>
              There was an urgent meeting called by the Prime Minister to take immediate action on this natural calamity. There have been few buildings that got crumbled in India in the earthquake. The Nepal Prime Minister has asked for help from neighboring countries. Indian Prime Minister gave a historic comment in the latest interview. He said,
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               I will wipe tears of Nepal.
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Government is providing hugecompensation to all the affected people in Nepal and the people in North India are also getting sufficient money to rebuild they homes and start new work.
             </p>
             <p>
              However the weather department has alerted all the people to move to safer places as they are chances of another earthquake with a higher magnitude to hit again. May god bless all and may the affected people rest in peace.
             </p>
