      <h1>
       <a href="http://www.skiptomylou.org/2010/02/18/a-drawstring-bag-tutorial/">
        A Drawstring Bag Tutorial
       </a>
      </h1>
      <a class="surprise_me" href="/?random">
       <span>
        Surprise Me!
       </span>
      </a>
     </header>
     <p>
      I was needing a little gift bag to hold some note cards for a sweet friend who has been spending way too much time at the hospital and I thought a fabric drawstring pouch would be perfect.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" src="file:///Users/georgehopper/Library/Caches/TemporaryItems/moz-screenshot.png"/>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/Drawstring-Bag.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11454" height="575" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/Drawstring-Bag.jpg" title="Drawstring Bag" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I used a piece of fabric 6 1/2 inches by 21 inches, but it can be made any size!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag1.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11455" height="421" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag1.jpg" title="DrawstringBag1" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Turn each end under 1/4 inch and stitch along the edge.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag2.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11456" height="197" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag2.jpg" title="DrawstringBag2" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      With right sides together stitch sides together using a 1/2 inch seam, stopping two inches from the top.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag4.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11457" height="633" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag4.jpg" title="DrawstringBag4" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Pink edges and press side seams open. At the top where the side seam is open, stitch around the opening on each side.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag5.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11458" height="480" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag5.jpg" title="DrawstringBag5" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold top edge down 1 inch and stitch all the way around close to the bottom edge.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/Drawstringbag6.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11459" height="262" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/Drawstringbag6.jpg" title="Drawstringbag6" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Cut two pieces of ribbon or cording about 22 long each. Feed one piece through casing and back out the other casing coming out on the same side of the bag.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag7.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11460" height="144" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag7.jpg" title="DrawstringBag7" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Repeat with second cord on opposite side.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag8.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11461" height="138" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag8.jpg" title="DrawstringBag8" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Tie the string ends on each side together. (You can hind the knots in the casing part if you work at it)
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag9.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11462" height="197" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag9.jpg" title="DrawstringBag9" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fill the bag with some goodies and you have a nice little treat!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag10.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11463" height="479" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/DrawstringBag10.jpg" title="DrawstringBag10" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
