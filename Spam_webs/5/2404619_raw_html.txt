      <h3>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       I managed to work and
       <a href="https://maqtoob.com/tools/category-travel" target="_blank">
        travel
       </a>
       around the world for 3 years with these 22 basic but essential things in my backpack.I hope the list will help you once you make the bold decision to set off on the road and make the world your office.
      </h4>
      <h3>
      </h3>
      <h3>
       1. Send a scanned copyof your passport, international drivers license, credit cards, and international health insurance to your email. And of course don't forget to take the originals along.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="375" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/802_12b3c_file-20150119-18335gbnrrn6yybm.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <h3>
      </h3>
      <h3>
       2.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Deuter-Traveller-Travel-Pack-Anthracite/dp/B002AL8LI0/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624555&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=deuter+traveller+70%2B10+backpack" target="_blank">
        Deuter Traveller 70 + 10 Backpack
       </a>
        acomfortable backpackto carry, with enough space for all the items needed for a long-distance trip.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="542" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/611_a3313_file-20150120-1258ta67la8uqgak.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       3. Unlock your smartphone and download sometravel apps (e.g. MapsWithMe,
       <a href="https://maqtoob.com/tool/entrain-yourself" target="_blank">
        Entrain Yourself
       </a>
       , XE Currency,
       <a href="https://maqtoob.com/tool/tripit" target="_blank">
        Triplt
       </a>
       , Google Translate, Skyscanner).It can also substitute your torch as well as photo and video cameras.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="493" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/909_30543_file-20150120-1643c5crageg7t3b.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <h3>
      </h3>
      <h3>
       4. A light laptop, together with a
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Portable-Flexible-Light-Laptops-Notebooks/dp/B002WI8GG8/ref=sr_1_6?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624609&amp;sr=8-6&amp;keywords=light+laptop" target="_blank">
        portable USB LED
       </a>
       (if you are a Microsoft user), allows you to work in the dark, especially if you plan to sleep in dorms.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="500" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/973_8dec8_file-20150120-12148l83tvkhxzcl.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <h3>
      </h3>
      <h3>
       5. With
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/High-Resolution-Display-Adaptive-PagePress-Sensors/dp/B00GDQ7CYC/ref=sr_1_13?s=electronics&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421758331&amp;sr=1-13&amp;keywords=kindle+7th+generation" target="_blank">
        Kindle
       </a>
       you can take tons of books with you on a single ultralight device.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="333" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/161_b0197_file-20150120-1229h4ds38d8unaf.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       6.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Plugable-Gigabit-Ethernet-Network-Adapter/dp/B00AQM8586/ref=sr_1_8?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625364&amp;sr=8-8&amp;keywords=adapter+usb+lan" target="_blank">
        Adapter USB LAN
       </a>
       and a LAN cable. For example, many hotels in Japan don't have WI-FI, only LAN Internet connection. So if you have a McBook Air, you'll definitely need that adapter.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="453" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/757_73c99_file-20150120-1318my6f9h653lyq.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       7.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Bose-SoundTrue-Headphones-On-Ear-Style/dp/B00IUICOF8/ref=sr_1_18?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625648&amp;sr=8-18&amp;keywords=bose+headphones" target="_blank">
        Headphones
       </a>
       with a microphone are essential for video conferences. Also, sometimes you dont feel like talking to people, so having a while for yourself listening to some good music may come in handy.
      </h3>
      <h3>
      </h3>
      <h3 style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="" height="500" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/588_33e4b_file-20150120-1306dmjlqtyye7uv.jpg" width="500"/>
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       8.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/MAXAH%C2%AE-Protector-Universal-Worldwide-Charger/dp/B00E0FZSQC/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624953&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=plug+converter" target="_blank">
        Plug Adapter
       </a>
       . Some of them have got a USB slot to charge your cellphone.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="442" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/996_156ab_file-20150120-1347s28vw92sdlwc.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       9.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Organizer-Storage-Packing-Bags-GoToBag/dp/B00CF5K698/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625743&amp;sr=8-2&amp;keywords=plastic+envelopes+travel" target="_blank">
        Water Resistant Bags
       </a>
       to protectyour electronic devices and documents (passport, insurance) from the rain.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="344" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/255_71d5b_file-20150120-131065wanz11wtda.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       10.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Combination-Improved-Security-Suitcase-Accessories/dp/B00QNHY078/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625147&amp;sr=8-2&amp;keywords=padlock+travel" target="_blank">
        Padlocks
       </a>
       to protect your valuables. Look for one with a combination lock in case you lose the key.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="374" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/58_256b6_file-20150120-1339svf8zp291qfq.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       11.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Mens-Leather-Money-Belt-Size/dp/B007EHN94Q/ref=sr_1_15?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624671&amp;sr=8-15&amp;keywords=money+belt" target="_blank">
        Money Belt
       </a>
       to secure and discreetly store money andcopies of your documents.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="259" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/287_8a223_file-20150120-1431rg7pet6abumh.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       12.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Moleskine-Passion-Journal-Travel-Large/dp/8862936257/ref=sr_1_8?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625000&amp;sr=8-8&amp;keywords=travel+journal" target="_blank">
        Journal
       </a>
       .Keep track of your stories, write downyour ideas.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="500" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/748_9525d_file-20150120-1405slaehbzuebbs.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       13.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Ultra-Bright-LED-Headlamp-Flashlight/dp/B00H26CGTI/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624982&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=Headlamp" target="_blank">
        Headlamp
       </a>
       .Dimmerensures you get the exact light you need for any use with the push of a single button.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="500" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/68_9b438_file-20150120-1414baylnxrgvp2t.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       14.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Etymotic-Research-ETY-Plugs-Protection-Earplugs/dp/B0044DEESS/ref=sr_1_6?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625089&amp;sr=8-6&amp;keywords=ear+plugs" target="_blank">
        Earplugs
       </a>
        great if you can't stand snorers in dorms or if you want to have a nap on public transport.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="500" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/237_73db2_file-20150120-1457v5883121nlae.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       15.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Adventure-Medical-Kits-World-Travel/dp/B0026MRZSK/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625685&amp;sr=8-2&amp;keywords=emergency+kit+travel" target="_blank">
        Medical Kit
       </a>
       .Some of them also contain a language kit to help overcome language barriers. Dont forget to add yourown regular medicines.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="351" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/930_f5d4c_file-20150120-1515rzvcfrf31sqy.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       16. The inevitable
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Victorinox-Swiss-Champ-Pocket-Knife/dp/B0001GS19U/ref=sr_1_4?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421766439&amp;sr=8-4&amp;keywords=swiss+army+knife" target="_blank">
        Swiss Army knife
       </a>
       .It may be prohibited in some countries (e.g. Japan).
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="511" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/222_c471b_file-20150120-1535fwuf348j6adj.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       17. Collapsible water bottle
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Platypus-PlusBottle-Liter-Closure-Cap/dp/B002EQDP96/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624925&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=water+bottle+platypus" target="_blank">
        Platypus
       </a>
       :80% lighter and more compact than an empty hard bottle of equal volume.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="666" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/726_fd001_file-20150120-152919qtfhdqqns2.png" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       18.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Diercon-Personal-Water-Filter-Purifier/dp/B00IZZZEKS/ref=sr_1_10?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624868&amp;sr=8-10&amp;keywords=Lifestraw+water+filter" target="_blank">
        Lifestraw water filter
       </a>
       , just in case of a long hike or a dodgy water tape.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="333" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/609_b43d9_file-20150120-1659wbb4c94hpqpn.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       19.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/StanSport-705-Stansport-Single-Mosquito/dp/B001E1JKFU/ref=sr_1_6?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625277&amp;sr=8-6&amp;keywords=mosquito+net+treated" target="_blank">
        Treated Mosquito Net
       </a>
       .Protect yourself from infected mosquitoes.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="334" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/750_0696f_file-20150120-16569j9hzevvhe5j.jpeg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       20.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Sea-Summit-Reactor-Extreme-Thermolite/dp/B003BIT004/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421625033&amp;sr=8-3&amp;keywords=sleeping+bag+liner" target="_blank">
        Sleepingbag liner
       </a>
       :compact, light, strong, easy to wash and fast to dry. Perfect for avoidingdirty-looking beddings athostels.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="638" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/137_3d120_file-20150120-1636dt29wh12hr4h.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       21. Maximize space in your backpack with
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Shacke-Pak-Packing-Organizers-Laundry/dp/B00KKXCJQU/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624813&amp;sr=8-3&amp;keywords=packing+cubes" target="_blank">
        Packing cubes
       </a>
       .
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="500" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/740_bee1f_file-20150120-1644wmp1ksx97s6s.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       22.
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Delicol-Assorted-Seamless-Bandanna-Headwrap/dp/B00J55PZR4/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421624778&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=8-%09Buff+Handkerchief" target="_blank">
        BuffHandkerchief
       </a>
       which can be used as a headband, hairband, bandelet, hat, neckband, armband, waistband, skirt and so on.
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" height="775" src="https://a61708ea39919d6700b0-3269735c2c9a3486a13a2853770c3e6f.ssl.cf3.rackcdn.com/35_01216_file-20150120-160129jknfncq8k2.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h4>
      </h4>

       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Icebreaker-Sleeve-Shirt-Black-Medium/dp/B00AF65ZJK/ref=sr_1_4?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421798537&amp;sr=8-4&amp;keywords=t-shirt+icebreaker" target="_blank">
        longsleeve tee
       </a>
       , 3
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Icebreaker-Short-Sleeve-T-Shirt-Medium/dp/B00AF66M9W/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421798537&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=t-shirt+icebreaker&amp;pebp=1421798543990&amp;peasin=B00AF66M9W" target="_blank">
        t-shirts
       </a>
       , a windproof jacket, a fleece jacket, a pair of detachable pants, a pair of sunglasses,
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/X-BIONIC-Marathon-Running-White-US8-5-US10/dp/B007CHM7KU/ref=sr_1_2?s=sporting-goods&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421798692&amp;sr=1-2&amp;keywords=x+socks" target="_blank">
        socks
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Icebreaker-Mens-Anatomica-Relaxed-Boxers/dp/B00DJ9BF12/ref=pd_sim_sg_2?ie=UTF8&amp;refRID=0DA4E7W3KZMJPTG0S1FX" target="_blank">
        underwear
       </a>
       , a pair of walking shoes, a pair of sandals (I recommend
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Teva-Closed-Sandal-Turkish-Coffee/dp/B003TU14EO/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421798094&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=Teva+Dozer&amp;pebp=1421798097335&amp;peasin=B003TU14EO" target="_blank">
        Teva Dozer
       </a>
       ), a pair of flip-flops, and a
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Absorbent-Microfiber-Sports-Fitness-Towel/dp/B00QESFALG/ref=sr_1_27?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1421798165&amp;sr=8-27&amp;keywords=microfiber+towel+travel" target="_blank">
        microfiber towel

       .
