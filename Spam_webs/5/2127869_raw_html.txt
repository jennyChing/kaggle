                    $("#newsletter-signup-form .required-icon").each(function(i, el) {
                        setTimeout(function() {
                            $(el).fadeIn(200);
                        }, (i * 300));
                    });
                
                    setTimeout(function() {
                        $('#newsletter-signup-form .legend').fadeIn(200);
                    }, ($("#newsletter-signup-form .required-icon").length * 300));
                });
            });
                      
            
            // Check for proper email formatting
            $('#newsletter-email').blur(function() {
                if (!$(this).val().match(/.+\@.+\..+/i) &amp;&amp; $(this).val().length &gt; 0) {
                    if (!$(this).hasClass('error')) {
                        $(this).addClass('error');     
                        var p = $(this).position();           
                        $(this).after('&lt;div class="field-error" style="margin-left: ' + p.left + 'px"&gt;&lt;div class="field-error-arrow"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="field-error-text"&gt;Not a valid email format. Did you forget something?&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;');
                    }
                    $('#newsletter-submit').attr('disabled','disabled');
                    $('#newsletter-signup-form form').attr('disabled','disabled');
                    
                } 
            });
            $('#newsletter-email').keyup(function(){
                if ($(this).val().match(/.+\@.+\..+/i)) {
                    if ($(this).hasClass('error')) {
                        $(this).removeClass('error');
                        if ($(this).next().hasClass('field-error'))
                            $(this).next().remove();
                    }
                    $('#newsletter-submit').removeAttr('disabled');
                    $('#newsletter-signup-form form').removeAttr('disabled');              
                }
            });
            
            // let a person know when the emails don't match
            $('#newsletter-re-type-email').keyup(function(){
                var pattern = new RegExp('^' + $(this).val(), 'i');
            
                if (!$('#newsletter-email').val().match(pattern)) {
                    if (!$(this).hasClass('error')) {
                        $(this).addClass('error');                
                        var p = $(this).position();           
                        $(this).after('&lt;div class="field-error" style="margin-left: ' + p.left + 'px"&gt;&lt;div class="field-error-arrow"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="field-error-text"&gt;Email doesn\'t match&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;');
                    }
                    $('#newsletter-submit').attr('disabled','disabled');
                    $('#newsletter-signup-form form').attr('disabled','disabled');
                } else {
                    if ($(this).hasClass('error')) {
                        $(this).removeClass('error');
                        if ($(this).next().hasClass('field-error'))
                            $(this).next().remove();
                    }
                    $('#newsletter-submit').removeAttr('disabled');
                    $('#newsletter-signup-form form').removeAttr('disabled');
                }
            });
            
            $('#newsletter-signup-form input').keyup(function(){
                if ($(this).hasClass('clear-error-on-keyup')) {
                    $(this).removeClass('clear-error-on-keyup').removeClass('error');   
                 
                    if ($(this).next().hasClass('field-error'))
                        $(this).next().remove();
                }
            });

       <p>
        <b>
         <img alt="obama" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1233215" height="283" src="/files/2014/09/obama.jpg" width="596"/>
         Michael Savage
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Ebola is Obamas Katrina, Savage told his listeners this week.
       </p>
       <div class="left_prom_160 desktop-only">
        <div class="desktop-only" id="div-gpt-ad-story160LeftSide">
         <script type="text/javascript">
          googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('div-gpt-ad-story160LeftSide'); });
         </script>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Not only is the president mishandling the crisis, said Savage, hes compounding it by preparing a secret surge of millions of immigrants from Central America, Liberia and Haiti, where tuberculosis is endemic (
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2VFoSufGTec">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        Savage also watched the media coverage of the terror attack at Canadas Parliament Buildings with a growing sense of disgust.
       </p>
       <p>
        This is how a country dies, he observed. This is how a civilization dies.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its a result of the vermin on the left who have debased and deballed the West for the last 40 years. We all know exactly whats really happening, Savage said. But if you speak the truth, youre a racist (
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0J_ac2k88cU">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Rush Limbaugh
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Limbaugh theorized that terrorists like the Muslim murderer in Ottawa are driven to attack their own nations after years of self-loathing socialist propaganda.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are subjected enough and regularly to an onslaught of how rotten the United States is, Limbaugh said, how guilty the United States is, if you are subjected to a daily tirade of all the injustices and all the unfairness and all the discrimination and all the inequality and all of the inequity, at some point youre going to snap if youre not well adjusted to begin with. And who knows then what could become attractive to you as you seek to put action to your thoughts, become a hero or what have you? (
        <a href="http://www.breitbart.com/Breitbart-TV/2014/10/23/Limbaugh-Ottawa-Shooter-Example-of-Mentally-Unstable-Driven-Insane-by-Socialist-Propaganda">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <div class="module widget_wnd_prom_widget mobile-only">
        <div class="promo-300x250 mobile-only" id="wndm_story_midcontent_300x250">
         <script type="text/javascript">
          googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('wndm_story_midcontent_300x250'); });
         </script>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Limbaugh also commented on the growing phenomenon of female teachers seducing their students.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its amazing the number of female teachers, Limbaugh said. Thats not supposed to happen, right? That isnt supposed to be able to happen. This is supposed to be the other way around. Its certainly not the template. Its a mystery how this happens.
       </p>
       <p>
        He added, It never ever happened when I was in school, damn it.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Aaron Klein
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Ebola isnt the only disease Americans need to worry about, says investigative broadcaster Aaron Klein. He revealed that Dengue fever, chikungunya and other exotic infectious diseases should also be considered looming threats.
       </p>
       <p>
        Klein looked in the global alert issued by the World Health Organization after an outbreak of the polio-like Enterovirus D68, which has already killed 5 children in America.
       </p>
       <p>
        He also investigated reports that illegal aliens from Central America brought with them a newly discovered and aggressive mosquito thats a known transmitter of deadly viruses.
       </p>
       <p>
        Israelis are moving to Berlin, of all places, claiming that the cost of living in Israel is too high. Israels Agriculture and Rural Planning minister spoke to Klein about this fascinating story (
        <a href="https://soundcloud.com/aaron-klein-radio">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Mark Levin
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        The Democrat Party is a disaster, Levin declared this week.
       </p>
       <p>
        Every one of these incumbent Democrats who are now pretending they dont support Obama supported him over 90, 95 percent, he pointed out. They supported Obamacare, they supported amnesty, they supported this massive deficit spending and this debt. And all this is coming home now, and it is a disaster. The countrys gone to hell under this president, under the Democrat Party. Everybody knows it (
        <a href="http://www.foxnews.com/on-air/hannity/transcript/2014/10/22/mark-levin-election-day-2014-democrat-party-disaster">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        A self-described leftist called in to Levins show, and he had a hard time defending his beliefs under interrogation by the host. It makes for entertaining listening (
        <a href="http://therightscoop.com/mark-levin-schools-a-leftist-caller/">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Laura Ingraham
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Obama cant admit it, said Ingraham, but his presidency is finished.
       </p>
       <p>
        However, she added, this doesnt mean the Republicans should expect to coast to victory in the midterms.
       </p>
       <p>
        The GOP has to stand for something, she cautioned. To grow the Republican party, candidates have to be able to convincingly articulate what does work when it comes to our economic policies, our foreign policies, border policies (
        <a href="http://www.newsmax.com/newswidget/ingraham-midterms-obama/2014/10/21/id/602067/?Dkt_nbr=159DB-1&amp;nmx_source=Five_Feet_Of_Fury&amp;nmx_medium=widget&amp;nmx_content=226&amp;nmx_campaign=widgetphase2">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        Ingraham backed New Hampshire candidate Scott Brown as one of those candidates, praising his populist message on immigration (
        <a href="http://www.lauraingraham.com/mediabits">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Glenn Beck
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        While pausing to make fun of his guests accent, Glenn Beck praised author Mark Steyn as one of the few individuals courageous enough to fight against radicalized Islam, at great personal cost.
       </p>
       <p>
        On the show to promote his new book, The (Un)documented Mark Steyn, the author talked about everything from the terror attack in Ottawa to J.R.R. Tolkiens handwritten reply to a letter hed written to him as a boy.
       </p>
       <p>
        Mark, I dont know why youre not on more, Beck said at the end of the interview. I thoroughly enjoy you. Youre really truly one of the bravest men alive today because you will not shut up or sit down. I hope that continues (
        <a href="http://www.steynonline.com/6623/maternal-and-queenly">
         Free audio
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
