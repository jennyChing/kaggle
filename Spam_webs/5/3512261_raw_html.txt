   <h2>
    London Street Art
   </h2>
   <div class="galleryheader">
    <!-- BEGIN user desc -->
    <p align="center">
     This is a sub gallery of my Street Art Gallery.
     <br/>
     It contains the street art objects I was searching for and found in London.
     <br/>
     London is a city, so rich with street art that I`ve decided to open an own gallery.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     I was lucky to had still the chance to visit the Leake Street tunnel next to Waterloo Station
     <br/>
     which will be closed in September 2008.
     <br/>
     And I spent hours there to capture the beautiful works from the cans festival this year.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     It`s easy to take the fotos but I know it`s hard to create the street art works.
     <br/>
     So this gallery will be my tribut to the amazing street artists too!
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Thanks to Ai Li for leading  me with her tip to this exciting place for street art freaks like me - this gallery is dedicated to you!.
    </p>
    <br/>
