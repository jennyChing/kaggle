         <p dir="ltr">
          Live-AdZ is an augmented reality app which will provide an interactive experience to the users. Users of this app can get a feel of 4D
          <a href="http://www.augmentedrealitytrends.com/category/augmented-reality">
           augmented reality
          </a>
          . Interaction will be on a completely new level. The app has been powered by Klokwerks. It leverages the power of advanced 3D digital graphics as well as the power of tablet or mobile technology to provide branding. The users of the Live-AdZ AR app will get an intuitive learning experience.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           The Future of Print
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          The app delivers images, audios, videos and 3D animation overlaid directly on printed advertisings. Users can also get to experience these animations, videos, etc. even in the world around us. Interaction of users with the virtual world will be taken to a new level with this app. The interactive experience of the users happens in real time with realistic 3D objects that can be viewed using the AR app. The videos that users can see are in full HD.
         </p>
