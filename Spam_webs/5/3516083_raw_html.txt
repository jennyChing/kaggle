       <div class="embed_wrapper clear_both" contenteditable="false">
        <iframe frameborder="0" height="351" src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/10322228?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0" width="468">
        </iframe>
        <span class="caption public_hide">

       </div>
       <p data-ic-marker="4a3na2_1398">
        Helmut Smit is our new favorite artist/designer, even if
        <a href="http://helmutsmits.nl/design/flamma">
         FLAMMA
        </a>
        was the only project he'd ever done (it isn't, by the way, and they are all stellar). This brilliant Ikea Hack "harks back to one of humanity's basic needs: making fire." As Ikea does not sell matches or lighters, Smits has taken cues from the wartime necessity of burning furniture to keep warm, and created a fire from an Ikea rope, hanger, wine rack, egg cup, napkins, and floral embellishments.
       </p>
       <p data-ic-marker="0d8dw1_6104">
        The video above is worth every minute, not to mention a good lesson in building fires, an art lost on most of us.
       </p>
       <p data-ic-marker="0o4fn8_4364">
        Visit Smit's site
        <a href="http://helmutsmits.nl/">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
