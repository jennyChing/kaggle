          <h1 class="pag-title">
           7 Dental Marketing Ideas to Quickly Generate 100 New Patients
          </h1>
          <div class="divider">
          </div>
          <p class="texto3">
           These dental marketing actions are taken for all of our dental clients, and for just one of our clients, 100 new patients were acquired in just over nine months.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <iframe frameborder="0" height="350" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/bUTCRQ3VYSI" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </center>
          <p class="texto3">
           In todays market, you must have a
           <a href="http://crawfordandobrien.com/chrisad-vs-crawford-and-obrien/">
            strong internet marketing campaign
           </a>
           to bring in new patients and crush your competition. Many people use the Internet to search for local services like dentists. A weak online presence will result in the loss of a huge pool of potential patients. However, a rudimentary website isnt enough to generate a steady stream of new clients. Instead, a website that engages prospective customers, since most people only stay on any given website for 10 seconds before deciding to act on something that interests them. Before customers even reach that decision point, let us help you lead them reach your site through several smart and targeted marketing efforts, like the following:
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://crawfordandobrien.com/get-quote/">
            <img alt="7 Dental Marketing Ideas" class="aligncenter wp-image-1723" height="1908" src="http://crawfordandobrien.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/7DentalMarkeingIdeas.jpg" title="7 Dental Marketing Ideas" width="720"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p class="texto5">
           Embed this infographic on your site.
          </p>
          <p>
           <textarea class="texto6">
            &lt;a href=http://crawfordandobrien.com/7-dental-marketing-ideas/ title=7 Dental Marketing Ideas to Generate 100 New Patients &gt;&lt;img src=http://crawfordandobrien.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/7DentalMarkeingIdeas.jpg alt=dental marketing ideas title=7 Dental Marketing Ideas to Generate 100 New Patients width= height=/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;a href=http://crawfordandobrien.com/7-dental-marketing-ideas/&gt;dental marketing ideas &lt;/a&gt;
           </textarea>
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 1  CREATE DIRECT RESPONSE OFFERS
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           Decide on a specific area or demographic for a campaign and create direct mail such as postcards or letters to encourage a visit. Regardless of the delivery method, a direct response offer creates an incentive for an immediate action. Ideally, an offer for a free exam on their first visit or 20% off a teeth whitening will put a smile on their face and turn into an office visit. If the offer is good enough, and you are targeting the right people, it should convert into a positive ROI.
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 2 IMPROVE YOUR SOCIAL MEDIA
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           Social media has become a major tool in any marketing campaign, but you need more than just a profile on all the popular sites. A
           <a href="http://crawfordandobrien.com/social-media-marketing/">
            good social media campaign
           </a>
           begins with a profile on all the major social media sites, such as Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and YouTube. Integrating social media into your website can help keep customers up to date, as well as allowing you to continually engage with them. Posting quality content, which can include a range of information from posts about your practice to general industry news, will emphasize your commitment and reinforce your qualifications by showing you know your stuff.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           For example, the best Facebook posts include a picture and just enough information to elicit a response from the reader. Figuring out the types of information and content your target audience enjoys will result in more likes, and an increase in the likelihood that they will share those posts to their personal networks.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           Additionally, social media sites make it very easy to target ads to a particular demographic. By taking advantage of this vehicle of advertising, you can reach out to the people that will most likely act on something you post; which will make the most out of your advertising and marketing budget.
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 3  START BLOGGING
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           Having a blog on your website is another great way to generate new patients. Regular blogging adds high quality, unique content to your site on a continual basis. Each blog post can be optimized for search engine optimization (SEO), meaning keywords in your posts will result in your website being one of the top returns from internet searches. Additionally, a blog can be integrated into your social media sites to keep those sites alive with new content.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           A well written, interesting blog will help bring users to your site which could lead to them visiting your practice for all of their dental needs. Blog posts about dental hygiene tips, an explanation of various dental procedures, interesting facts about dental procedures, and news about new technological advances are some interesting options for topics. You can also include personalized information about your practice, dentists, and hygienists.
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 4  IMPROVE YOUR SEO
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           <a href="http://crawfordandobrien.com/dental-seo/">
            Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
           </a>
           is the backbone of any Internet marketing campaign. In order to be found by potential customers, your website must rank very highly on all the search engines, such as Google, Bing, and Yahoo. Most people never look past the first page of a search result, so if you are not on the first page, you might as well be invisible.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           By having a website optimized for search engines, your site will be one of the first results a potential customer sees. Keywords, metadata, and coding that make it easy for search engines to find your site, all work together to build a strong ranking. Additionally, having other websites link to yours will improve your SEO. For dental practices, a strong local SEO is important since most customers are from the geographical area around your practice.
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 5  GOOGLE ADWORDS PPC
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           Advertising is integral to marketing, but you can too often find yourself advertising in the wrong locations and wasting your money. Google Adwords Pay Per Click (PPC) will get you noticed by many people without breaking your marketing budget.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           PPC prevents unwanted costs by charging users only when the ad is actually clicked. There are many services out there that offer PPC advertising services. However, Google Adwords is one of the biggest networks, giving you a large reach at reasonable prices.
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 6  HIGHLIGHT CUSTOMER REVIEWS
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           When people look for a new dentist, the most important things they look for are customer reviews and testimonials. Many people dont like going to the dentist or are even afraid. Accommodation, timeliness, and the performance of high quality procedures, like root canals and cosmetic dentistry, are key factors that prospective patients are going to look to reviews to validate.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           You should ask your satisfied customers to go to sites like Yelp and Google+ to write their positive review and testimonial. Not only will this help potential patients feel secure in coming to your practice, it will also help improve your SEO. Having many reviews on these sites increases your SEO rankings and provides more than one place for you to be found on the internet.
          </p>
          <h2 class="pag-title2">
           DENTAL MARKETING IDEA No. 7  COMPETITIONS AND OFFERS
          </h2>
          <p class="texto3">
           Competitions are a fun way to bring in new patients. You can offer goods or services as a reward for a particular competition, like achieving a specific number of likes on Facebook. This will inspire people to take action. Social media sites like Facebook and Twitter are great places to offer special deals for dental procedures. Everyone loves getting a discount, and theyre a great incentive for a first visit without costing your practice very much.
          </p>
          <p class="texto3">
           <strong>
            With a cohesive Internet marketing campaign that includes these seven features, you can tap into the huge pool of potential online customers and further grow your business.
           </strong>
           Let us help you implement these ideas or ones that are fairly similar to help you see new patients at your front door. Dont waste your time on marketing strategies and methods that arent generating new patients. We can help you get started on these marketing tools right away with one simple click right here. Theres nothing to lose, so why not just take us up on an offer from a hassle free strategy session.
          </p>
