                     Designer bags are not just symbols of luxury and elegance; they are in fact sturdy, efficient companions for everyday use. Many designer brands offer less expensive models designed for regular use in the workplace.  When purchasing a bag for the office, take into consideration the following qualities:

                      Durability
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Most authentic designer bags are durable enough for the office.  Choose bags that are made of high-quality leather: these were treated substantially, and have undergone various stress tests to withstand wear and tear.  They are scratch- and fireproof, and can be used extensively without one worrying about damage due to constant bumping and dangling.
