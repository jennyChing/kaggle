         Sometimes the craving for a single slice of
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthier-cake-recipes">
          chocolate cake
         </a>
         will
         <em>
          not
         </em>
         go away. And when it's just you, your PJs, and a full Netflix queue, baking an entire cake or batch of brownies seems like overkill. (And you know you'll be tempted to eat a little more here and a swipe of icing there.) But while its usually not hard to pare down a dinner that serves four to serve one, how in the world do you split an egg into fifths?
        </p>
        <p>
         Answer: You dont. Instead, you make one of these single-serving desserts. No more worry about portion control with
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthier-pie-012313">
          apple pie
         </a>
         or eating an entire batch of snickerdoodle
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthier-holiday-cookies">
          cookies
         </a>
         in a week, tops. With these recipes, you get to eat the entire thing! (Just remember: These are dessertsand some are pretty indulgent. So resist the urge to make them every night.)
        </p>
        <h3>
         Cakes and Cupcakes
        </h3>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Funfetti Cupcake" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/funfetti-cupcake11_thumb_0.jpg?itok=A5R-I46C" title="Photo: Chocolate-Covered Katie"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Chocolate-Covered Katie
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         1.
         <a href="http://chocolatecoveredkatie.com/2012/01/02/funfetti-cupcake-for-one/">
          Funfetti Cupcake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Lifes better with sprinkles, and if you bake at all, chances are you have the ingredients on-hand to whip up a speckled cupcake any time. No need for a mixall you need is flour, sugar, vanilla, and a few other staple dessert ingredients.
        </p>
        <h4>
         2.
         <a href="http://www.crazyforcrust.com/2014/01/double-chocolate-peanut-butter-mug-cookie/">
          Double Chocolate Peanut Butter Mug Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         While this is called a cookie, the consistency is like a cake. Ready in less than five minutes, it turns out rich and fudgy with a peanut butter and chocolate center thats perfectly ooey-gooey. Leave out the PB if youre aching for plain chocolate cake. Its still insanely good.
        </p>
        <div class="ym" data-yieldmo="ym_966591849689717803" data-yieldmo-tablet="ym_976628814724144286" id="ym_966591849689717803">
        </div>
        <h4>
         3.
         <a href="http://heather-maclean.com/hungry/just-one-serving-ny-style-cheesecake/">
          Three-Inch Cheesecake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This one takes a little patience since it bakes for 20 minutes and then should chill for an hour. But you get to eat an entire (mini) cheesecake! Top with your favorite fresh fruit such as berries or cherries.
        </p>
        <h4>
         4.
         <a href="http://www.heathersfrenchpress.com/2014/04/single-serving-carrot-cake-mug.html">
          Carrot Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Cinnamon and nutmeg spice up the classic springtime dessert in this lightened-up version. Top your mug cake with
         <a href="http://greatist.com/greek-yogurt">
          Greek yogurt
         </a>
         rather than cream cheese frosting and crushed walnuts or pecans for a pretty presentation.
        </p>
        <h4>
         5.
         <a href="http://www.theglowingfridge.com/chocolate-chip-cupcake-for-one-and-oil-free-january/">
          Chocolate Chip Cupcake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Vegan and oil-free, this cupcake uses almond milk and applesauce as binders and can be made with any gluten-free flour. The coconut cream frosting just may become your go-to cake topper since its rich and fluffy.
        </p>
        <h4>
         6.
         <a href="http://spoonuniversity.com/recipe/pumpkin-cake-one/">
          Pumpkin Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         When theres no time to bake a pie, make a cakein the mircrowave. With only 3/4 tablespoon sugar, this cake is sweet without overpowering the delicious pumpkin and cinnamon flavors.
        </p>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Breakfast Strawberry Shortcake" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/BreakfastShortcake-1475-tm.jpg?itok=kFQvAVrg" title="Photo: The Chic Life"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: The Chic Life
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         7.
         <a href="http://thechiclife.com/2012/05/breakfast-strawberry-shortcake-for-one-recipe.html">
          Breakfast Strawberry Shortcake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This lightly sweet recipe is as appropriate for the morning as it is for an evening treat thanks to some healthy
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/83-healthy-recipe-substitutions">
          ingredient substitutions
         </a>
         . Spelt flour packs four grams of protein and fiber into a quarter cup and has a nutty flavor, and just a few fresh strawberries provide almost 30 percent of your daily vitamin C. Top with Greek yogurt rather than whipped cream, natch.
        </p>
        <h4>
         8.
         <a href="http://www.thekitchenwhisperer.net/2014/02/01/gooey-brownie-chocolate-mug-cake-one/">
          Gooey Brownie Chocolate Mug Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This is definitely a #treatyoself (or share on movie night with your BFF). And worth every. Single. Bite. The fudgy brownie is dotted with chocolate chips and why not drizzle caramel sauce on top? Cook carefully to your desired donenesswe recommend gooey or super gooey.
        </p>
        <h4>
         9.
         <a href="http://www.heathersdish.com/1/cinnamon-streusel-coffee-cake-for-one-in-a-jar/">
          Cinnamon Streusel Coffee Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The best part about coffee cake is the sweet, crunchy topping. Layering it with the cake batter means double the deliciousness, and baking in a mason jar adds a touch of cute. Add cream cheese frosting if you wish. (We know you do. We do too.)
        </p>
        <h4>
         10.
         <a href="http://pinkrecipebox.com/one-bowl-chocolate-cupcake-for-one/">
          One-Bowl Chocolate Cupcake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Beat, fold, bake, and youve got a moist, sweet chocolate cupcake for one! The addition of chopped chocolate makes it richer than regular cake, and dont neglect the saltjust a pinch enhances the other flavors.
        </p>
        <h4>
         11.
         <a href="http://kirbiecravings.com/2013/10/10-minute-microwave-cheesecake-mug-cake.html">
          Microwave Cheesecake Mug Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Proof that cheesecake can be made without an oven (it just may not be quite as creamy), this recipe cooks quickly and looks like the real thing after you slide it out of the mug. Let it cool an hour in the fridgewarm cheesecake just doesnt taste like cheesecake.
        </p>
        <h4>
         12.
         <a href="http://chocolatechocolateandmore.com/2012/07/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-mug-cake/">
          Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Mug Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Ditch the boxed mixes and their questionable ingredients. All you need are six things and less than five minutes to make a single-serving cake. Mini chocolate chips mean melty goodness in every bite.
        </p>
        <h4>
         13.
         <a href="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/2013/01/single-serve-banana-cake/">
          Single Serve Banana Cake
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This dessert is gluten-free and has zero added
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/sugar-really-bad-us">
          sweeteners
         </a>
         the banana and unsweetened almond milk add enough naturally. Add dark chocolate chips since their rich flavor goes so well with bananas.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Cookies
        </h3>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Deep-Dish Chocolate Chip Cookie" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/deep-dish-chocolate-chip-cookie.jpg?itok=17iH9a2H" title="Photo: Edible Perspective"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Edible Perspective
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         14.
         <a href="http://www.edibleperspective.com/home/2013/5/22/single-serving-deep-dish-chocolate-chip-cookie-vegan-gluten.html">
          Deep-Dish Chocolate Chip Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         How do you make cookies even better? You make them deep dish. Using oat flour and almond meal keeps these gluten-free and coconut oil replaces butter or eggs, so theyre vegan. Bake to your desired doneness, whether you like chewier or crispier.
        </p>
        <h4>
         15.
         <a href="http://lacreativitedelafille.blogspot.com/2013/10/xxl-dark-chocolate-sea-salt-brownie.html">
          XXL Dark Chocolate and Sea Salt Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Because more chocolate is always better, this dessert has cocoa baked in and dark chunks dotting the top along with a generous sprinkle of sea salt. Soft and fudgy, its the stuff chocolate dreams are made of.
        </p>
        <h4>
         16.
         <a href="http://domesticdeepthought.blogspot.com/2011/02/uni-baker-one-awesome-peanut-butter.html">
          Awesome Peanut Butter Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         With PB as (rightfully) one of the two main ingredients (flour is the other), this cookie is richly
         <a href="http://greatist.com/peanut-butter">
          peanut-buttery
         </a>
         , and the light sugar coating makes the outside perfectly crisp.
        </p>
        <h4>
         17.
         <a href="http://www.workitmom.com/articles/detail/199909/single-serve-snickerdoodle-cookies">
          Snickerdoodle Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Theres something about straight-from-the-oven cinnamon-sugar cookies thats just unbeatable. And since nuking already-baked cookies can lead to burnt disaster (not that wed know), this single-serve recipe is a welcome addition to any snickerdoodle fans arsenal.
        </p>
        <h4>
         18.
         <a href="http://carrotsncake.com/2013/07/gluten-free-egg-free-cookie-dough.html">
          No-Bake Cookie Dough
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Lets be real: Everyone has eaten raw cookie dough. But this recipe lets you enjoy that rich decadence without any fears of salmonella or an upset stomach. Its a genius combo of almond butter, almond flour, vanilla, honey, salt, and chocolate chips. And it has a surprising 12 grams of protein!
        </p>
        <h4>
         19.
         <a href="http://www.fannetasticfood.com/2013/10/30/microwave-pumpkin-oatmeal-cookie-for-one/">
          Microwave Pumpkin Oatmeal Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This cookie is truly wholesome. In addition to the fiber from the oats and pumpkin, it has some omega-3s from flaxseed, a little protein from nut butter, and natural sweetness from bananas and raisins. The spicy treat turns out soft and chewy like a muffin top.
        </p>
        <h4>
         20.
         <a href="http://carrotsncake.com/2012/09/microwavable-nut-butter-cookie-for-one.html">
          Microwavable Nut Butter Cookie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The combination of honey, cinnamon, and
         <a href="http://greatist.com/eat/peanut-butter-and-beyond-nutrient-packed-alternatives">
          nut butter
         </a>
         cant be beat. Add in two more ingredients (plus toppings like dried fruit, nuts, or chocolate, if you wish), and in two minutes, you have a moist, craveable, and Paleo-friendly cookie.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Puddings and Mousse
        </h3>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Healthy Chocolate Mousse" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/far-healthy-chocolate-mousse-recipe.jpg?itok=nFK4afTw" title="Photo: iFoodReal"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: iFoodReal
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         21.
         <a href="http://ifoodreal.com/healthy-chocolate-mousse-recipe/">
          Chocolate Mousse
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Having leftover
         <a href="http://greatist.com/eat/recipes/pumpkin-chia-seed-pudding">
          chia seed pudding
         </a>
         on hand makes this super speedy to create. Blend it up with sweet dates and cacao, then top with melted chocolate and nuts or berries for a gorgeous treat that can be made vegan.
        </p>
        <h4>
         22.
         <a href="http://www.southerninlaw.com/2012/08/quick-and-easy-stovetop-rice-pudding-recipe-healthy-gluten-free.html">
          Stovetop Rice Pudding
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Leftover rice (use brown for more nutrients) from yesterday's dinner can become dessert tonight! Customize this recipe using your desired milk and sweetener to taste. We like adding raisins and cinnamon too.
        </p>
        <h4>
         23.
         <a href="https://melbournemummy.wordpress.com/2013/06/09/mums-treat-moist-butterscotch-pudding-for-one/">
          Moist Butterscotch Pudding
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Say hello to
         <em>
          real
         </em>
         instant pudding! Made from ingredients you recognize (and likely have in your pantry and fridge), this is made in five minutes and requires one mug for easy cleanup. So long, Jell-O.
        </p>
        <h4>
         24.
         <a href="http://www.nutritiontothr1ve.com/2013/07/17/raspberry-mousse/">
          Raspberry Mousse
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This is one dessert thats suitable for pretty much any diet!
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/superfood-chia">
          Chia seeds
         </a>
         make this more like pudding than typical mousse, but its still delish. Stir them with fruit, sweetener (if using), lemon zest, and hot water. Let that sit until thick, then whip in chilled coconut cream.
        </p>
        <h4>
         25.
         <a href="http://www.tip.kitchen/threads/cauliflower-rice-pudding.1218/">
          Cauliflower Rice Pudding
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Paleos like rice pudding too, and using cauliflower rice lets them satisfy the craving. Using coconut milk and oil plus shredded coconut lends a tropical note as well as medium-chain fatty acids, which studies suggest are beneficial for
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/genius-ways-to-use-coconut-oil">
          cholesterol levels
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h4>
         26.
         <a href="http://thefitveganginger.blogspot.com/2014/05/silky-peanut-butter-pudding.html">
          Silky Peanut Butter Pudding
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The secret in this velvety, three-ingredient dessert? Silken
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/healthy-tofu-recipes">
          tofu
         </a>
         . The peanut butter is all you taste, though. Add sweetener as desired, and eat up 13 grams of filling protein in your bowl.
        </p>
        <h4>
         27.
         <a href="http://mainlinegardening.com/learn-post/healthful-chocolate-pudding-anyone/">
          Healthful Chocolate Pudding
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         If you havent tried
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/39-deliciously-unexpected-healthy-avocado-recipes">
          avocado
         </a>
         pudding yetyoure seriously missing out. Next time you have half a green fruit left over from making guacamole, puree it with banana, cocoa, milk, and honey or agave, then get ready to dig into rich, chocolatey, satisfying creaminess.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Frozen Treats
        </h3>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Banana Soft Serve With Toasted Seeds and Nut Butter Topping" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/Banana-Soft-Serve-With-Toasted-Seeds-and-Nut-Butter-Topping.jpg?itok=RgjYe-E6" title="Photo: Apples Under My Bed"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Apples Under My Bed
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         28.
         <a href="http://applesundermybed.com/2013/11/06/banana-soft-serve-with-toasted-seeds-and-nut-butter-topping/">
          Banana Soft Serve With Toasted Seeds and Nut Butter Topping
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         This recipe tops
         <a href="http://greatist.com/health/our-banana-ice-cream-made-only-bananas">
          banana ice cream
         </a>
         with nut butter, sunflower seeds, pepitas, and flaxseed for a creamy and crunchy treat. We think its better as a dessert or snack than breakfast since its not super heavy in protein, but eat whenever you want it!
        </p>
        <h4>
         29.
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/2013/05/15/strawberry-coconut-soft-serve/">
          Strawberry Coconut Soft Serve
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Not into bananas? Make homemade soft serve from coconut milk by pureeing it with frozen berries. All are great sources of fiber, so mix up your faves!
        </p>
        <h4>
         30.
         <a href="http://www.hungryhealthygirl.com/tag/coconut-cookie-dough-banana-soft-serve/">
          Coconut Cookie Dough Banana Soft Serve
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Cookie dough ice cream doesnt only come in the freezer aisle. Coconut flour and almond milk combine with a few other ingredients to create moist "cookie dough" thats mixed into a soft serve "ice cream," made from bananas and more almond milk for a hint of nut.
        </p>
        <h4>
         31.
         <a href="http://christinerobyn.com/2014/04/09/recipe-peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-ice-cream/">
          Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Ice Cream
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Weve got a thing for faux ice cream made from frozen bananas, what can we say? Its low-cal and often needs no additional sweeteners. Which means you can add things like PB and chocolate to make it taste indulgent without it being anything like the nutritional equivalent of eating a pint of the real stuff.
        </p>
        <h4>
         32.
         <a href="http://www.heathersfrenchpress.com/2013/03/single-serve-healthy-raspberry-ice-cream.html">
          Healthy Raspberry Ice Cream
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Theres frozen yogurt and Greek yogurt and Greek fro yo. So why not make Greek yogurt ice cream at home? Its so simple: Blend up the creamy stuff with any frozen fruit and maple syrup or honey (or omit sweetener; fruit often has enough).
        </p>
        <h3>
         Pies, Cobblers, and Crisps
        </h3>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Deep-Dish Apple Pie" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/deep-dish-apple-pie.jpg?itok=TZ3yauzM" title="Photo: Oh Lady Cakes"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Oh Lady Cakes
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         33.
         <a href="http://www.ohladycakes.com/2013/05/single-serving-deep-dish-apple-pie-for-thom-with-love.html">
          Deep-Dish Apple Pie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Pie in a mug is great (see below), but sometimes you want the real deal. This takes some work, but once the smell of cinnamon
         <a href="http://greatist.com/apples">
          apples
         </a>
         starts wafting through your kitchen, and especially when you take the first bite, youll realize its all worth it. Cute as it is, skip the heart-shaped top crust if youre more into the filling.
        </p>
        <h4>
         34.
         <a href="http://snappygourmet.com/2012/08/01/one-minute-blueberry-cobbler/">
          One-Minute Blueberry Cobbler
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Add in the (minimal) prep work, and this dessert comes together in six minutes. Plus blueberries contain anthocyanins, those pigments that keep your heart and eyes healthy, and that may product against cancer, diabetes, and neurological diseases
         <cite class="citation-reference" data-cite-reference="">
          <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1082903/">
           AnthocyaninsMore Than Natures Colours
          </a>
          . Konczak, I and Zhang, W. Journal of Biomedicine and Biotechnology, Dec 1, 2004; 2004(5): 239240.
         </cite>
         .
        </p>
        <h4>
         35.
         <a href="https://attentionstarvedfoodblog.wordpress.com/2012/04/21/pecan-pie-in-a-mug/">
          Pecan Pie in a Mug
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The best part of pecan pie is the gooey, buttery inside, so skip the crustand the hassleand make this! As with other nuts, pecans are a good source of plant sterols, which have been shown to lower cholesterol.
        </p>
        <h4>
         36.
         <a href="http://jensfavoritecookies.com/2014/08/25/peach-cobbler-for-one/">
          Peach Cobbler
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Those pre-portioned cups of peaches packed in juice are good for this recipe, but fresh or frozen peaches work just as well. Toss with flour, sprinkle with oats and brown sugar, and dot with butter, then bake! So easyand so good when topped with ice cream.
        </p>
        <h4>
         37.
         <a href="http://lacreativitedelafille.blogspot.com/2012/05/single-serving-apple-crisp.html">
          Apple Crisp
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Microwave sweets just dont get old. This one cooks up to be caramel-y, chewy, and soft. Its like biting into fall any time of year.
        </p>
        <h4>
         38.
         <a href="http://www.thekitchn.com/recipe-pumpkin-pie-in-a-mug-recipes-from-the-kitchn-212557">
          Pumpkin Pie in a Mug
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The texture might not be quite a pie (its more soft and spongy), but the taste is still all there in this 10-minute recipe. Gingersnap cookies are the base for the quick crust, lending even more spice to the super
         <a href="http://greatist.com/pumpkin-recipes">
          pumpkin
         </a>
         -y dessert.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Bars, Breads, and Balls
        </h3>
        <span class="media media-element-container media-g_full_width_inline_image">
         <img alt="Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls" class="media-element file-g-full-width-inline-image" src="http://greatist.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_main/public/Single-Serving_Pumpkin_Cinnamon_Rolls-7.jpg?itok=2Ako1CSf" title="Photo: Healthy Aperture"/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo: Healthy Aperture
         </span>
        </span>
        <h4>
         39.
         <a href="http://healthyaperture.com/blog/post/global-garden-single-serving-pumpkin-cinnamon-rolls">
          Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Cinnamon rolls are like burritostypically sold in size gigundo. This appropriately portioned recipe also uses oat and whole-wheat flours, plus Greek yogurt for the icing, so theyre a tad healthy too. Eat with a spoon, or remove from the ramekin to pull apart with your hands.
        </p>
        <h4>
         40.
         <a href="http://www.lovefromtheoven.com/2012/07/14/brownie-a-la-anytime-single-serving-brownies-made-in-the-microwave/">
          Brownies a la Anytime
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         No need to bake a whole pan of brownies since theyre best hot and gooey. This comes out rich and dark. Add chocolate chips or nuts if youre into that, and serve straight from the (microwave) oven a la mode.
        </p>
        <h4>
         41.
         <a href="http://witandwhistle.com/2012/09/04/peanut-butter-balls-for-one/">
          Peanut Butter Balls
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         You could make a batch of truffles or cake balls and restrain yourself. Or just follow this recipe that makes three balls, just the right amount. The balls take three ingredients and then get a coating of melted semisweet chocolate.
        </p>
        <h4>
         42.
         <a href="http://chefambershea.com/2011/01/22/the-5-minute-single-serving-raw-vegan-blondie/">
          Raw Vegan Blondie
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Raw desserts can call for many ingredients and lots of time, but this dessert breaks that standard. Six ingredients and five minutes, and its ready. Its a splurge because of the nuts, but they also provide omega-3 fatty acids if you use walnuts and
         <a href="http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/002423.htm">
          magnesium
         </a>
         which is vital for your bones, immunity, heart, and moreif you use cashews.
        </p>
        <h4>
         43.
         <a href="http://blog.dollhousebakeshoppe.com/2011/01/3-minute-single-serving-banana-bread.html">
          Banana Bread
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The banana and oil (or applesauce) in this recipe guarantees bread so super moist you certainly need a spoon to eat it. Its vegan too!
        </p>
