        <header class="post-title">
         <h2>
          <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/pastel-wedding-at-crooked-willow-farms/" rel="bookmark" title="Pastel Wedding at Crooked Willow Farms">
           Pastel Wedding at Crooked Willow Farms
          </a>
         </h2>
         <h4 class="categories">
          <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/category/weddings/vintage-weddings/" rel="category tag">
           Vintage Weddings
          </a>
         </h4>
        </header>
        <section class="post-content">
         <div class="right-part">
          <div class="content-area">
           <p>
            How can one not fall in love with this wedding from
            <a href="http://www.lauramurrayphotography.com/" onclick="javascript: pageTracker._trackPageview('/photographers/lauramurrayphotography');" target="blank">
             Laura Murray Photography
            </a>
            ? We were sold as soon as we saw the gorgeous wooden altar decorated with flowers and pastel-colored watercolor pinwheels. Keep an eye out for repeated pinwheel elements  mini versions even show up on the table number frames!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/ruffled_galleries/pastel-colorado-wedding/" target="blank">
             <img alt="pinwheel ceremony altar" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_238.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            From the florists of
            <a href="http://www.coricook.com/" onclick="javascript: pageTracker._trackPageview('/shops/coricook');" target="blank">
             Cori Cook Floral Design
            </a>
            :
            <em>
             Jenise and Max chose a color palate of soft pastels like robins egg blue, peaches, blush pinks, ivories, and butter yellows. Many of the details of the wedding were handmade with love by her talented sisters and mother. Her mother handmade the bridesmaid dresses and the flower girl dress, designed in robins egg blue. The groom and groomsmen wore grey tailored suits with a robins egg blue tie. Jenises gown was a stunning fit! It was an ivory, form-fitting lace gown with a v neck and jaw-dropping keyhole back. Handmade pinwheels designed by her crafty sister, Karleen Quakenbush, adorned walls, table numbers, the escort card display, and flower containers.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/ruffled_galleries/pastel-colorado-wedding/" target="blank">
             <br/>
             <img alt="teal chevron invitations" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_035.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="lace wedding dresses" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_052.jpg"/>
            <img alt="white satin wedding shoes" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_016.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="blush pink boutonnieres" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_074.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="yellow teal invitations" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_028.jpg"/>
            <img alt="yellow teal invitations" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_032.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="pinwheel ceremony altar" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_345.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="white hair accessories" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130013.jpg"/>
            <img alt="pastel wedding bouquets" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130012.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             Jenise is a talented floral designer at Cori Cook Floral Design, so flowers were a priority and main focus of dcor. With the colors and style in place, Jenise let us run wild with her floral dcor. Lush, garden-style blooms like coral peonies, caf au lait dahlias, garden roses, poppies, hydrangea, and astible were combined with wooden boxes and milkglass pedestaled containers for her centerpieces. Her arch was adorned with curly willow, florals, and water-colored pinwheels.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/ruffled_galleries/pastel-colorado-wedding/" target="blank">
             <br/>
             <img alt="bride and groom" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130009.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="teal dress bridesmaids" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_187.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="pinwheel wedding altar" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130003.jpg"/>
            <img alt="pastel grooms boutonnieres" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130011.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="pinwheel escort card display" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_256.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="pastel wedding ideas" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_262.jpg"/>
            <img alt="pastel flower arrangements" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/IMG_8048.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             A value from the beginning of the wedding planning process was to create a cohesive design aesthetic starting with paperware and ending with flowers. Jenise and Max had custom save the dates and initiations designed by Paper + Ink, marrying their soft color palette with a touch of kraft paper and screen printed chevron. Details were present through the wedding including hand-stamped programs and escort cards tied in striped yellow bakers twine.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/ruffled_galleries/pastel-colorado-wedding/" target="blank">
             <br/>
             <img alt="bride and groom" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130010.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="teal flower girls" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_176.jpg"/>
            <img alt="teal bridesmaids dresses" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_196.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="indoor barn receptions" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07110013.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="pastel wedding ideas" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_207.jpg"/>
            <img alt="watercolor wedding ideas" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_244.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             The bride and her familys love of dance was displayed throughout the evening with a variety of choreographed  routines, the best of which was a surprise to everyone: a sassy father-daughter dance including a medley of significant songs that they share together.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/ruffled_galleries/pastel-colorado-wedding/" target="blank">
             <br/>
             <img alt="pastel wedding decor" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07110014.jpg"/>
             <img alt="pastel wedding decor" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/07130002.jpg"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="newlyweds first dance" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_566.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="pastel wedding ideas" class="left" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_281.jpg"/>
            <img alt="white lace cakes" class="right" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_273.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="nighttime barn weddings" src="http://ruffledblog.com//vintage-wedding-blog/image-resend-for-jenise-maxs-colorado-wedding/W68 Jenise Max_602.jpg"/>
           </p>
