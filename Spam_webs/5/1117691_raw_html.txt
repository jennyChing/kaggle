          <h1 class="post-title">
           <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Hot for the Holidays! Sofia The First Royal Prep Academy{VIDEO}">
            Hot for the Holidays! Sofia The First Royal Prep Academy{VIDEO}
           </a>
           <div class="comments">
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/#comments">
             0
            </a>
           </div>
          </h1>
          <small>
           by
           <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/author/gyb/" rel="author" title="Posts by Lisa Arneill">
            Lisa Arneill
           </a>
           <a href="https://plus.google.com/+LisaArneill3" plugin="Google Plus Authorship" rel="author" title="Google Plus Profile for +">

          <div id="getsocialmain">
           <p>
            Since hitting the small screen 2 years ago, Sofia The First has been one of the most popular shows on television for kids 3+. Initially, the toys were slow to come, but this year we have seen some really great sets for little fans of the show.
           </p>
           <p>
            Debuted at Toy Fair earlier this year, the
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00IVFCINS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Royal Prep Academy
            </a>
            is by far one of my favorite play sets to be released from the collection.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy/" rel="attachment wp-att-94216">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94216" height="380" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-575x380.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The biggest set they have created to date, the
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00IVFCINS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Royal Prep Academy
            </a>
            has 9 rooms across 3 floors. Not only does it include a lot of little accessories, but there are lots of fun features that the designers have built right in to the set.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-94217">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94217" height="380" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy--575x380.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Starting at the top there is a garden area to hold the flying horses. The Prep Academy comes with just Sofia and Clover, but parents can purchase
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00IVP13NO" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             a second set($39)
            </a>
            that includes Minimus, Sofias horse, Amber and James, plus some of their school mates.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-additional-characters/" rel="attachment wp-att-94226">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy additional characters" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94226" height="396" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-additional-characters-575x396.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Just over from the garden there is an art studio and then an area, that appears to be the library- but is actually a surprise room.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-art-room-and-library/" rel="attachment wp-att-94218">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy - art room and library" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94218" height="380" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-art-room-and-library-575x380.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            On the second floor the prep academy has a study, music class andsorcery class. In the music class the piano pops up to reveal a little animal when kids go to play it. The blackboard in thesorcery class drops down to show some interesting potions being created.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-sorcery-class/" rel="attachment wp-att-94225">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy - sorcery class" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94225" height="380" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-sorcery-class-575x380.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            After taking the spiral staircase down to the main floor students can visit the conservatory, have lunch in the dining room or take a swirl on the dancefloor in the ball room!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-sofia-and-clover-dancing-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-94227">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy - sofia and clover dancing" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94227" height="380" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-sofia-and-clover-dancing--575x380.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            My favorite part of the set, however, is the elevator. Designed to look like a throne, Sofia and her classmates can move from floor to floor easily with just the twist of a handle! As she hits each floor the set recites different messages from the headmistresses Flora, Fauna and Merryweather!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-elevator/" rel="attachment wp-att-94220">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy - elevator" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94220" height="380" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-elevator-575x380.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            With more than 25 accessories, 2 characters and lots of different places to play,
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00IVFCINS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="sofia the first prep academy">
             the Prep Academy
            </a>
            is one of my favorite toys picks for this Holiday Season.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/12/01/hot-for-the-holidays-sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academyvideo/sofia-the-first-royal-prep-academy-front/" rel="attachment wp-att-94228">
             <img alt="sofia the first Royal Prep Academy - front" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-94228" height="389" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/sofia-the-first-Royal-Prep-Academy-front-575x389.jpg" width="575"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            I have put together a video to show off the whole set! Please watch it below:
           </p>

            <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/XIgd8zjKaiE" width="560">
