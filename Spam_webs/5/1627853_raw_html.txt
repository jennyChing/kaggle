       <h1 class="blogtitle entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
        <span itemprop="name">
         Altitude Comedy Festival Reaching All New Heights: March 18th  22nd, 2013
        </span>
       </h1>
       <div class="clear">
       </div>
       <div class="featuredimage">
        <div class="slider-wrapper theme-default">
         <div>
          <img alt="Altitude Comedy Festival Reaching All New Heights: March 18th  22nd, 2013" class="scale-with-grid" data-thumb="http://www.comedychords.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Altitude-1213-700x325.jpg" src="http://www.comedychords.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Altitude-1213-700x325.jpg" title=""/>

        <div addthis:title="Altitude Comedy Festival Reaching All New Heights: March 18th  22nd, 2013 " addthis:url="http://www.comedychords.com/news/altitude-comedy-festival-reaching-all-new-heights-march-18th-22nd-2013/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style addthis_32x32_style">
         <a class="addthis_button_preferred_1">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_preferred_2">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_preferred_3">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_preferred_4">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_compact">
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Altitude
         </strong>
         brings an all new meaning to
         <em>
          Aprs
         </em>
         <em>
          -
         </em>
         <em>
          ski
         </em>
         . What better way to wind down after a day of gliding down the slopes than a perfect snow storm of high-calibre comedians. The highly successful festival is all set to make a welcome return to the Austrian ski resort of
         <a href="http://www.mayrhofen.at/en" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mayrhofen.at']);" target="_blank">
          Mayrhofen
         </a>
         , from
         <strong>
          March 18-22.
         </strong>
         The event, created by
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.andrewmaxwellcomedian.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.andrewmaxwellcomedian.com']);" target="_blank">
           Andrew Maxwell
          </a>
          ,
         </strong>
         the Irish comedy powerhouse, has added an exceptional line-up of some of the circuits top dogs.
         <span id="more-6571">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Maxwell is ecstatic at the thought of sharing a line-up of circuit celebrities such as the even-brilliant
         <strong>
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Bishop_%28comedian%29" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://en.wikipedia.org']);" target="_blank">
           John Bishop
          </a>
          ,
         </strong>
         and BBC Radio 4 favourite and Maxwells old partner in crime
         <strong>
          Marcus Brigstocke
         </strong>
         who commented that the line-up this year literally blew him away and that
         <em>
          This is truly growing into one of the best festivals in the world. I seriously cannot wait! Bring it on!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Many names are yet to be announced, including a secret headliner  one of the worlds top comedians, filling international arenas since his meteoric rise to fame in the 90s. Any ideas? We cant wait to find out. Even though the line-up is not yet finalised its already looking quite the beast of a festival with appearances confirmed from
         <strong>
          Daniel Sloss
         </strong>
         , who youll have seen on pretty much any stand-up show on TV in the past couple of years, whilst well be treated to some of the worlds best Improv from the likes of
         <strong>
          Phill Jupitus,
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Andy Smart
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          Steve Frost.
         </strong>
         The Festival will also play host to some of last years most successful comedians including
         <strong>
          Craig Campbell, Benny Boot, Brendon Burns, Phil Nichol, Howard Marks
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          Rob Beckett,
         </strong>
         who, following his stint hosting ITVs Im a Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here Now! Heads from the sweltering jungle to the icy Austrian slopes
         <strong>
          .
         </strong>
         Theres also some welcome fresh meat this year with Irelands newest comedy starlet
         <strong>
          Keith Farnan,
         </strong>
         and festival virgin
         <strong>
          Kai Humphries
         </strong>
         (recently seen supporting Daniel Sloss).
        </p>
        <p>
         The Austrian slopes will also welcome comedians of a more international scope with appearances from
         <strong>
          Paul Provenza
         </strong>
         (The Aristocrats, Northern Exposure &amp; The West Wing) and
         <strong>
          Troy Conrads
         </strong>
         hit LA show
         <strong>
          SETLIST,
         </strong>
         the worlds first ever improv stand-up show! Altitude has also revealed a very special debut appearance from one of Germanys top comedians
         <strong>
          Michael Mittermeier.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Not content with this already mega line-up, youll need to keep your ear to the ground by joining
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AltitudeComedyFestival" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.facebook.com']);" target="_blank">
          Altitudes new Facebook page
         </a>
         for more information. Make a note in your diary for March 4
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         as a big announcement is planned.
        </p>
        <p>
         Despite all the comedy we mustnt forget was a beautiful ski resort Mayrhofen is, boasting choice restaurants, luxurious hotels, more than inviting spas and offers access to the entire Zillertal super ski area with 639km of piste and 167 lifts and gondolas. Surely its clear now why this resort was chosen as the perfect location for one of the worlds top five international comedy festivals. The resort caters for all levels and intentions, whether youre a group of friends daring to tackle the black runs followed by all-night fun or just slipping away for a romantic break, Altitude will pamper your every need with its extreme ski/comedy festival fusion.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.comedychords.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Alt12.png">
          <img alt="Alt12 Altitude Comedy Festival Reaching All New Heights: March 18th  22nd, 2013" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6572" height="175" src="http://www.comedychords.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Alt12.png" title="Alt12" width="398"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         All ticket information is available
         <a href="http://www.ticketline.co.uk/altitude-festival#tour" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.ticketline.co.uk']);" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         with a full week Festival wristband only setting you back 177.50 (inc. Booking Fee). Not only that but groups of 10 or more can take advantage of special discount tickets  Call 0844 888 9991 if buying 10 tickets or more and get them for 150 each (plus booking fee).
        </p>
        <p>
         The Festival will also be offering accommodation packages which include the Hotel, Bed &amp; Breakfast for 5 nights (based on 2 sharing) and the week wristband.
        </p>
        <p>
         For full details on the event please visit the official Altitude Festival website:
         <a href="http://www.altitudefestival.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.altitudefestival.com']);" target="_blank">
          www.altitudefestival.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <h5>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           UPDATE:
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h5>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Altitude Festival
         </strong>
         is extremely proud to be able to include
         <strong>
          Eddie Izzard
         </strong>
         as headliner to join
         <strong>
          John Bishop
         </strong>
         as one of this years headline acts! Having been hailed as the lost Python by none other than John Cleese he is bound to bring the house down this year in Mayrhofen! Watch this space as more top acts are added to the already extensive list.
        </p>
