      <article class="post-135 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-nutrition tag-benefits-of-coconut-water tag-coconut-water-is-good tag-health-benefits-of-coconut-water" id="post-135">
       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         15 Positives Of Drinking Coconut Water
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_136" style="width: 310px">
         <img alt="coconut-water-juthan" class="wp-image-136 size-medium" height="225" src="http://www.juthan.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/coconut-water-juthan-300x225.jpeg" width="300"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Coconut Water Benefits
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Coconut water is the best delicious substitute of water and it is a good thirst quencher. If you drink one glass of coconut water on a hot sunny day the feeling you get is awesome. But calling this coconut water only a stimulating drink would be wrong as it can do more than that. Read on more to know the various positive effects of drinking coconut water.
        </p>
        <ol style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Helps in weight loss
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Of course coconut water contains some amount of calories but the percentage is very less which cant add extra pounds on your weight. Also the rich nutrition of coconut water makes you full so the appetite is suppressed, thus it helps to reduce some weight.
        </p>
        <ol start="2" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Controls blood pressure
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The level of blood pressure is increased when there is a disproportion in the balance of electrolytes. Coconut water is that healthy drink which contains all the nutrition in exact amount. So it is recommended by some doctors to drink coconut water in every morning so that the amount of all elements in our body is maintained at right proportion.
        </p>
        <ol start="3" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Enhances hydration
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         All the sportsman are seen to drink various kinds of sports drink and energy drink for hydration as we tend to lose minerals fluid during heavy exercise and sports. Most of the energy drink and sports drink contain some amount of potassium, sodium and huge amount of sugar. But if all those drinks are replaced by coconut water then the nutrition that our body will get is 294 mg potassium (double the amount that is presented in energy drink and sports drink), only 5 mg sugar and 25 mg sodium. So the benefit is huge.
        </p>
        <ol start="4" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Good remedy for hangover
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Coconut water is the best remedy to cut down hangover. It can settle the stomach upset that is caused due to extra drink. Also if huge amount of electrolytes is removed from your body due to frequent urine and vomiting then drink more coconut water and it will replace all the elements in body.
        </p>
        <ol start="5" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Helps in digestion
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Digestion can also be handled by coconut water. If you are suffering from constant indigestion then coconut water can be a great help there also. As it is full of fiber so is able to reduce the acid reflux and indigestion. Coconut water gives quick relief from indigestion.
        </p>
        <ol start="6" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Well-suited with human blood
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Coconut water can rehydrate human body in emergency cases. In so many poor countries till now coconut water is given for saving human lives. This is used for quick rehydration as it is isotonic to human plasma. It is a good substitute of saline water that was used in the earlier days to treat the soldiers during war.
        </p>
        <ol start="7" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Good for skin
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Drinking coconut water has a great effect on the skin also. It contains a compound called cytokinins which is a great anti-aging element. This element prevents the pre mature aging spot by its excellent formula. Also skin become spotless and more firmed if you keep on drinking coconut water every day. Topical applying of this on skin is very useful to remove acne spot and other black spot from skin.
        </p>
        <ol start="8" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Good for heart
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Coconut water reduces the blood pressure level in our body. It has magnesium and potassium in it which keeps the function of heart ongoing. Thus drinking coconut water can reduce the chances of heart attack and heart stroke. So drink coconut water and stay safe.
        </p>
        <ol start="9" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Boosts the metabolism rate
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The other great benefit of drinking coconut water is that it increases the metabolism rate in body and more metabolism rate means more fat burning. Also the amount of carbohydrate in coconut water is very less but still it has the capability to make you full. Thus again help in weight loss.
        </p>
        <ol start="10" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Healthy hair
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         If you are suffering from hair fall, dandruff and lice then start drinking coconut water regularly and get rid of all the hair problems. Coconut water helps in growing the hair with removing all the problems such as hair fall, dandruff and lice. Also if you can wash hair with coconut water then it brings shine and glow in hair.
        </p>
        <ol start="11" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Strengthens immune system
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         With performing so many acts coconut water is also able to improve the immune system of our body. As it has anti bacterial and anti viral properties so is able to boost up the immune system.
        </p>
        <ol start="12" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Helpful for pregnant women
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Coconut water is full of potassium, magnesium, protein, vitamin, carbohydrate, chloride, electrolyte etc. Also it is a good source of vitamin C, fiber, calcium and so many things that are very helpful for human body. Dehydration, tiredness and fatigue are treated by this water. With all these benefits coconut water helps the pregnant women to stay fit with preventing morning sickness, nausea, acidity and constipation during pregnancy.
        </p>
        <ol start="13" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Reduces muscle cramp
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Coconut water is the big medicine for muscle cramp. Muscle gets cramped only when there is deficiency of potassium in our body. So it is important to maintain the amount in order to prevent this. Coconut water contains great amount of potassium that is 294 mg in a glass and this is enough to fulfill the deficiency of potassium in human body. So drinking coconut water can help you to reduce the frequency of muscle cramp.
        </p>
        <ol start="14" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Prevents formation of kidney stones
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Kidney stone is best treated by coconut water. Huge amount of potassium presented in this can dissolve the kidney stones. Also it plays a key role to prevent the formation of kidney stones and urine alkalizing.
        </p>
        <ol start="15" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Controls diabetes
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Regular drinking of coconut water is able to control diabetes by regulating the blood circulation. It prevents the formation of plaque in the blood vessels thus maintains the blood circulation. Coconut water also fights against atherosclerosis.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         So the benefits of drinking coconut water are numerous and it can save you from various diseases. If you want to stay healthy then start having one glass of coconut water every day.
        </p>
