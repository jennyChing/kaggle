           <p>
            <span id="bodycontent_0_ArticleDescription">
             Not to brag, but we are masters of get-out-the-door-and-on-with-your-day beauty. Give us just three minutes and we can make ourselves look presentable. In five, we can turn presentable into looking pretty good. And in ten minutesWell, were pretty much ready to hit the red carpet at the Oscars. OK, maybe were not
             <em>
              that
             </em>
             skillful, but we do know how to maximize our mirror time. Its all about prioritization. The order you apply your favorite products is important. If you only have a mere 180 seconds before you simply must leave the house (we all know public transportation waits for no one), dont start with eyeliner. Here, let us show you our masterful ways
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <img alt="5 minute makeup" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1905839" height="1947" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/the_3_5_and_10_minute_face_bop_2.jpg" width="600"/>
             <h2>
              The 3-Minute Face
             </h2>
             <strong>
              15 seconds: Primer.
             </strong>
             We started our look with
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.dermablend.com/makeupprimer/SkinPerfector-Pigment-Correcting-Primer.htm" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Dermablend SkinPerfector Pigment Correcting Primer
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             .
             <br/>
             <strong>
              1 minute: Eyelash Curler.
             </strong>
             We pumped our
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.shuuemura-usa.com/product/EYELASH-CURLER,default,pd.html" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Shu Uemura Eyelash Curler
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             three times on each eye.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              1 minute: Mascara.
             </strong>
             We applied one generous coat of
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.lancome-usa.com/Hypnose-Drama/1000371,default,pd.html?dwvar_1000371_color=Excessive%20Black&amp;cgid=makeup-mascara#start=4" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Lancme Hypnse Drama
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             to the top lashes.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              45 seconds: Foundation.
             </strong>
             We used our fingers to apply Hourglass Cosmetics Immaculate Liquid Powder Foundation, but we also love a good
             <a href="http://www.makeup.com/bb-cream-vs-cc-cream/" target="_blank">
              BB cream
             </a>
             .
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <h2>
              The 5-Minute Face
             </h2>
             <strong>
              1 minute: Concealer.
             </strong>
             We blended
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.maybelline.com/Products/Face-Makeup/Concealer/dream-lumi-touch-highlighting-concealer.aspx" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Maybelline Dream Lumi Touch Highlighting Concealer
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             under the eyes.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              30 seconds: Blush.
             </strong>
             We dusted
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.lorealparisusa.com/en/Products/Makeup/Face/Blush/True-Match-Blush.aspx?shade=Tender-Rose-C3-4" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                LOral True Match Blush in Innocent Flush
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             on the apples of the cheeks.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              30 Seconds: Bronzer.
             </strong>
             We used a
             <a href="http://www.makeup.com/face-makeup-brush-guide/" target="_blank">
              fan brush
             </a>
             to lightly sweep
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.thebodyshop-usa.com/makeup/bronzing/honey-bronzing-powder.aspx" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                The Body Shop Honey Bronze Bronzing Powder
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             across the temples.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <h2>
              The 10-Minute Face
             </h2>
             <strong>
              1 Minute: Highlighter.
             </strong>
             We used
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.dermablend.com/highlighter/Quick-Fix-Illuminator.htm" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Dermablend Quick Fix Illuminator in Pearl
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             to highlight the brow bones and cheekbones.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              2 minutes: Eyeliner.
             </strong>
             We lightly lined the eyes with
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.emcosmetics.com/waterliner-intense-color-eyeliner/DW-WATERLINER.html?dwvar_DW-WATERLINER_color=S1176800" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                em Cosmetics Waterliner in Black Night
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             .
             <br/>
             <strong>
              1 Minute: Mascara.
             </strong>
             We added
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.maybelline.com/Products/Eye-Makeup/Mascara/illegal-length-fiber-extensions-mascara.aspx" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Maybelline Illegal Length Fiber Extensions Mascara
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             to the bottom lashes.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              1 Minute: Lipstick.
             </strong>
             A swipe of
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://www.urbandecay.com/urban-decay/lipstick%2C-lip-stain-%26-gloss/revolution-lipstick/378.html" target="_blank">
               <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
                Urban Decay Revolution Lipstick in Native
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             finished off our 10-minute face.
             <br/>
             <strong>
              <br/>
              How many minutes did you spend doing your makeup this morning?
             </strong>
             Tell us in the comments below!
            </span>
