        <p style="text-align: justify">
         Young and beautiful with a fantastic voice to rock, singer, songwriter and actress Selena Gomez has more to celebrate than just her good looks. The pop star has superb style and she consistently alternates between red-carpet sophisticated and the trendy girl-about-town look. On the red carpet, she prefers a very subtle and polished look and opts for couture gowns with simple figure hugging silhouettes but when she step out of the spot light she goes for an effortlessly chic look with a pair of denim cut-offs and crop tops or slashed denim pants, tank top or tee with a leather jacket combo.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         On Thursday, July 10, 2014, the pretty actress was spotted taking a stroll on the streets of NYC sporting a look that combined her love for silhouette gowns with the grunge factor. Selena was dressed in a sparkly gray jersey midi dress and brought in the tough-girl look with a quilted leather jacket and a pair of black lace-up boots. The 21-year old star finished off her look with a pair of round sunglasses and a pair of black studs. She kept her make-up minimal with just a hint of pink blush and pink toned lips. Her tousled hair fell in waves about her shoulders.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         Love Selenas pretty-meets-grunge look? Then, its time for you get a dress just like her. Our suggestion would be to go with a simple navy blue stripe tulip dress. Dress it down with a pair of lace-up booties, leather jacket and a pair of dark sunglasses to get the look.
