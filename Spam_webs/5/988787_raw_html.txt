               <h1 class="post-tile entry-title" itemprop="name">
                Giyilebilir Robot Kuratas Basna Tantld
               </h1>
               <div class="mom-post-meta single-post-meta">
                <span class="author vcard">
                 Yazar:
                 <span class="fn">
                  <a href="http://www.sanalmakina.net/author/admin/">
                   Kaya Ali Uymaz
                  </a>
                 </span>
                </span>
                <span>
                 Tarih:
                 <time class="updated" datetime="2012-07-30T23:59:48+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
                  Temmuz 30, 2012
                 </time>
                </span>
                <span>
                 Kategori:
                 <a href="http://www.sanalmakina.net/category/teknoloji/robot-teknoloji/" title="Tm yazlara bak: Robot">
                  Robot
                 </a>
                </span>
                <span>
                 <a href="http://www.sanalmakina.net/giyilebilir-robot-kurutas-basina-tanitildi/#comments">

                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="entry-content">
                <p>
                 Suidobashi Heavy Industries isimli bir Japon firmas son Kuratas adndaki robotunu basna tantt.
                 <br/>
                 <iframe frameborder="0" height="343" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/2TlSshPH2as" width="610">
                 </iframe>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <iframe frameborder="0" height="343" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/jxLBKeIWIHU" width="610">
                 </iframe>
                 <br/>
                 <span id="more-4596">
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 600 bin sterlinlik burobotun en byk zellii dakikada 6bin plastik mermi atabiliyor. Ateleme sisteminin almas iin ise sadece kullancsnn glmesi yeterli. Deiik bir zellik ama alma ekli bu. Belki de bu dmanlarn kmseme yntemi olabilir.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Dakikada 6 bin mermi atabilen, 16 farkl rengi bulunanKuratas, 3G teknolojisiyle Iphonela kontrol edilebiliyor.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Wataru Yoshizaki ve Kogoro Kuratann rettiirobotun siyah-pembe olan iin ekstra 60 sterlin demek gerekiyor.
                </p>
                <p>
                 2010 ylndan bu yana sren almalar sonucu retilen dev robot Kuratas, 4 metre uzunluunda ve pilot hari 4 ton arlnda.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.sanalmakina.net/wp-content/uploads/Suidobashi-heavy-industry-kurutas.jpg">
                  <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4597" data-lazy-src="http://www.sanalmakina.net/wp-content/uploads/Suidobashi-heavy-industry-kurutas.jpg" height="426" src="http://www.sanalmakina.net/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Suidobashi heavy industry kurutas" width="640"/>
                  <noscript>
                   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4597" height="426" src="http://www.sanalmakina.net/wp-content/uploads/Suidobashi-heavy-industry-kurutas.jpg" title="Suidobashi heavy industry kurutas" width="640"/>
                  </noscript>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 A Japanese company called Suidobashi Heavy Industries recently revealed its 13-foot mech robot named Kuratas. Kuratas is the brainchild of artist Kogoro Kurata and robotics researcher Wataru Yoshizaki. Kuratas can be controlled in three ways  either through a controller, a master slave, or an iPhone. The robot runs on an operating system called V-Sido and it is outfitted with a launcher and a twin Gatling gun. To operate the robots heavy artillery, the user only needs to smile to fire 6,000 bullets per minute.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Sounds too good to be true doesnt it? Apparently while we have pretty much covered almost every robot around, Kuratas appears to be a concept to us for now. But I have to say that the robot looks very surreal and realistic, judging from the video above. The Kuratas mech robot was recently showcased at the Wonder Festival in Chiba, Tokyo. Suidobashi Heavy Industries is reportedly planning to mass produce Kuratas in the future, but I reckon that you take that with a grain of salt in hand.
                </p>
