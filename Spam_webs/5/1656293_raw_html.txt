     <div class="entry-content">
      <p>
       Even though my daughters are getting older, they still sometimes say things wrong that warm my heart.For instance, the big thing hanging in our dining room that Santa will fill with small goodies? Its a socking, rather than a stocking. And when my little one is cold, shes sugarin rather than shivering. I know theyll go away one day, but I kind of want to hang onto those phrases forever.
      </p>
      <p>
       Moms in BabyCenters community shared some of the absolutely adorable things their kids say wrong. Awww with me, wont you?
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        My son is two, I love it when he says snissors (scissors).  hippybelcher
       </li>
       <li>
        Both of my kids say hangaber for hamburger.  BabyParlo
       </li>
       <li>
        My daughter has a pair of yoga pants and she calls them her yogurt pants.During the summer she called her bathing suit a baby soup and she used to call strawberries, strawbabies.  Wenders11
       </li>
       <li>
        My daughter says aminal for animal, I love that.  mag1119
       </li>
       <li>
        I love how my son calls mashed potatoes, Mash-ah-tatos.  MommaAnon
       </li>
       <li>
        My son (4) refers to any day in the past as lasterday. H also says Who body as in who body wants to play with me?  SuperBecca
       </li>
       <li>
        Papates is my three year olds pancakes.  urbanspock
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Hungry for more? Check out this

         very long list
        </a>
        of funny things kids say wrong.Have one to add? Share it with us!
       </strong>
