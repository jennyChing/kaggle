     <article class="post-content post-2166 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail category-review tag-146 tag-air-freshner tag-batman tag-candy-cane tag-captain-america tag-december tag-door-hanger tag-figure tag-ghostbusters tag-groot tag-guardians-of-the-galaxy tag-joker tag-loot-crate tag-marvel tag-pop tag-socks tag-stickers tag-tetris tag-the-simpsons tag-vinyl tag-wallet">
      <div class="entry-content">
       <div class="post-item-content image-above">
        <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
        <p>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Dec2014LootCrateBoxClosed.jpg">
          <img alt="Dec 2014 Loot Crate Box Closed" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-2175" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Dec2014LootCrateBoxClosed-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         For the month of December 2014,
         <a href="http://mbsy.co/6HQHl" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://mbsy.co/6HQHl', 'Loot Crate']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Loot Crate website">
          Loot Crate
         </a>
         is celebrating their first anniversary with their Anniversary themed box. For those who arent aware, Loot Crate is a monthly subscription service that caters to geeks and fans of pop culture, comics books and video games. Every month, Loot Crate offers a theme and teases it throughout the month to subscribers without ever spoiling the surprise of what lies inside. Loot Crate also happens to change the size and shape of their box every month according to what lies inside and this month, the box was their biggest yet, coming close to (but not quite) the same size as Nerd Blocks offerings. Loot Crate promises a value of $40 every month!
        </p>
        <p>
         If you want to avoid spoilers, I suggest you stop reading now. Without further delay, heres what SolarNovas Loot Crate contained:
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/BatmanPopCap.jpg">
          <img alt="Batman Pop Figure" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-2173" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/BatmanPopCap-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         Loot Crate Exclusive Joker/Batman Mashup Pop! Vinyl Figure
        </h2>
        <p>
         This exclusive Pop! Vinyl figure is simply amazing. The colors are vivid and bright and the fact that this figure is exclusive to Loot Crate subscribers is a serious win for the subscription service. This item represents how competing services can make significant gains by offers truly unique and compelling items to their subscribers. SolarNova absolutely loves this figure. Value: $9.99
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/AirFreshner.jpg">
          <img alt="Captain America Air Freshner" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-2169" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/AirFreshner-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00K8VQJ9M/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00K8VQJ9M&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=fami05-20&amp;linkId=RSA3ECMSKAB4LEG7" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00K8VQJ9M/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00K8VQJ9M&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=fami05-20&amp;linkId=RSA3ECMSKAB4LEG7', 'Vanilla Scented Captain America Air Freshner']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Air Freshner on Amazon">
          Vanilla Scented Captain America Air Freshner
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Well, its an air freshner. Not much can be said about this and frankly, its not a compelling product but the air freshner is made of solid material and has a very good quality printed image on it. One side shows Captain Americas emblematic Adamantium shield and the other side shows off the first Avenger in his full glory. Value: $4.75
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/TetrisStickers.jpg">
          <img alt="Tetris Stickers" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-2180" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/TetrisStickers-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00K0GIN0I/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00K0GIN0I&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=fami05-20&amp;linkId=DTDUAJCW27X3D7MV" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00K0GIN0I/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00K0GIN0I&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=fami05-20&amp;linkId=DTDUAJCW27X3D7MV', 'Tetris Sticker Set']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Tetris Sticker set on Amazon">
          Tetris Sticker Set
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         There are 147 stickers in this set and theyre pretty tiny but theyre pretty awesome too. The stickers come in a variety of Tetrimino shapes and colors and are reflective and bright. They complement
         <a href="http://famigami.com/review/nerd-block-jr-girls-unboxing-review-dec-2014/" target="_blank" title="Nerd Block Jr. Girls Unboxing &amp; Review (Dec 2014)">
          Kiwis notebook
         </a>
         pretty well too. Im not sure that SolarNova is going to make much use of these but if not, his sister will be more than happy to oblige. Value: $8.99
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/GrootSocks.jpg">
          <img alt="Groot Socks" class=" size-thumbnail wp-image-2183 alignleft" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/GrootSocks-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         Loot Crate Exclusive Baby Groot Guardians of the Galaxy Socks
        </h2>
        <p>
         These socks are amazing! They fit a mens size 9 to 12 foot and feature everyones favorite Guardian, dancing Groot! The lower half of the sock is a dark greenish grey. SolarNova needed new socks and these are sure to be a favorite to wear. Value: Unknown
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/SimpsonsWallet.jpg">
          <img alt="Simpsons Wallet" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-2179" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/SimpsonsWallet-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0093JFUPO/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0093JFUPO&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=fami05-20&amp;linkId=ZXXZRRFSUCW3LYY5" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0093JFUPO/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0093JFUPO&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=fami05-20&amp;linkId=ZXXZRRFSUCW3LYY5', 'DynomightyThe Simpsons Wallet']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="The Simpsons wallet on Amazon">
          DynomightyThe Simpsons Wallet
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         SolarNova seemed pretty happy to get this item but I found it to be unsuitable for my own needs. The wallet is made of a paper material and features as many The Simpsons characters as possible throughout. It has standard slits for paper bills and receipts as well as two side pockets to keep cards but I cant imagine it being capable of holding more than a handful of them at a time. The wallet did contain coupons for 10% off at Dynimighty so theres some extra value for those that were looking to save some money on their store. Value: $15.00
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DoorHanger2.jpg">
          <img alt="Door Hanger 2" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-2178" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DoorHanger2-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         Loot Crate Exclusive Ghostbusters Door Hanger
        </h2>
        <p>
         Who doesnt like Ghostbusters? This door hanger is neat, but ours was bent at the top end probably due to shifting in the mail. I wont fault Loot Crate for that though. Now SolarNova can tell us to stay out of his room. Great. One side feature the Stay Puft Marshmallow Man and the other features the four Ghostbusters presumably capturing a ghost, streams uncrossed of course. Value: Unknown
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/BatmanComic.jpg">
          <img alt="Batman Comic" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-2172" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/BatmanComic-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         Loot Crate Exclusive Cover Batman Endgame Comic Issue #36
        </h2>
        <p>
         Heres another high quality Batman item in the box! Ive heard great things about the Endgame series and the inclusion of an issue in this box is pretty neat. Too bad it had to be issue 36 instead of #1. But wait,t heres more! The issue also comes with three digital comics of Batman: Arkham Unhinged! Value: $5.99
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/CandyCane.jpg">
          <img alt="Candy Cane" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-2174" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/CandyCane-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         Broken Candy Cane
        </h2>
        <p>
         Since it was December, Loot Crate packed in this awesome broken candy cane treat. SolarNova was stoked to give this a try since hes never had a broken one before. He said it tasted the same as a regular one so he wasnt really impressed. Hey, its the thought that counts, right? Value: $0.10
        </p>
        <p>
         The box also featured an exclusive Loot Crate button and magazine (like every month), though this months button had a different look to it due to the anniversary.
         <a href="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/AnniversaryButton.jpg">
          <img alt="Anniversary Button" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-2170" height="150" src="http://famigami.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/AnniversaryButton-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
         </a>
         The usual look it an orange circle containing an image inspired by the months theme but this month, its all about the gold! If you havent noticed, this months box was packed with exclusive items and aside from the total disaster that was the candy cane, I think this months Loot Crate was their best weve had yet even if the total value wasnt all that impressive at around $45 and coming in closer to $50 if we take into account those items I couldnt get a price for.
        </p>
        <p>
         Loot Crate starts for as low as $11.67 per month on a 6-month subscription or for $13.37 per month on a month-to-month basis. Head on over to the
         <a href="http://mbsy.co/6HQHl" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://mbsy.co/6HQHl', 'Loot Crate website']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Loot Crate website">
          Loot Crate website
         </a>
         using promo code
         <strong>
          SAVE3
         </strong>
         for $3 off your first box. The January theme is Rewind and the countries of France, Finland and New Zealand can now subscribe as well so join in on the fun!
        </p>
