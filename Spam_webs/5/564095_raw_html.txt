         <p>
          Ive often said on here that Gracie is a little
          <a href="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/?s=fashionista" target="_blank">
           fashionista
          </a>
          . She honestly is. She LOVES to dress up and she LOVES to make makeup.
          <a href="http://www.walmart.com/search/?query=%23flashmob&amp;cat_id=0" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Markins
          </a>
          gave her a chance to try out their awesome line of makeup this week and she fell in love with it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0040.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="IMG_0040" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-47229" height="225" src="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0040-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>

            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Gracie was sent Markwins #flashmob gotta move eye makeup kit to try out. She loved all the colors. She loves playing with being able to play with different colors. Markwins made this very easy with their 3 tiered eye shadow compact. They made it super convenient and they must know how hard it is for tweens and teens to find room in their bathrooms. Really, they
          <em>
           HAVE
          </em>
          the room, its just a mess (in Gracies case).
         </p>
         <p>
          She was very excited to be able to try out the bright eye liner and to be able to add dazzle with the glitter. Its not something she would normally have me buy. She was honestly in hog heaven. At first she applied the makeup lightly. Later, she went a little bolder. It think by the end, she will be bold and brazen with it!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0041.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="IMG_0041" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-47230" height="225" src="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0041-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.sonyashappenings.com/gracie-loved-her-markwins-flashmob-eye-makeup-kit/&amp;media=http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0041-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Gracie Loved Her Markwins #Flashmob Eye Makeup Kit')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0042.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="IMG_0042" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-47231" height="225" src="http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0042-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.sonyashappenings.com/gracie-loved-her-markwins-flashmob-eye-makeup-kit/&amp;media=http://www.sonyashappenings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/IMG_0042-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Gracie Loved Her Markwins #Flashmob Eye Makeup Kit')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I appreciated how compact they were once you removed them from the package. I liked that the eye shadow was tiered and that the eye liner would stand up. Like I said, Gracies bathroom isnt the neatest, so we
          <strong>
           NEED
          </strong>
          things that work well in messy areas. Please dont think that I dont have her clean her bathroom either. She just walked thru here emptying her trash. Shes a good girl, she just is
          <strong>
           MESSY
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          This would be a great gift for any tween or teen. I know that Gracie really loved her kit. Her friends will have a ball with it too when they visit. If you happen to be looking for a present for girl around Gracies age (13), check these out!
