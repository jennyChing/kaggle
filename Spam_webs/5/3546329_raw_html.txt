        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         February is one of the best months for pool and billiards tournaments. Three of the main tournaments are held on or near the west coast. Amateurs who are willing to take their game to the next level should look into attending at least one of these premier events. Whether you love to shoot or just love to watch, these events are sure to have something for everyone.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Pool and Billiards Events" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-1161" height="150" src="http://blog.qstix.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Qstix-Cue-Sticks-150x150.jpg" title="Pool and Billiards Events in February" width="150"/>
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <b>
           2/15  2/16
          </b>
          -The 18th Annual Jay Swanson Memorial 9  Ball Tournament will be held at the Hard Times in Bellflower, California. The entry fee for the tournament is $88. The field is limited to 192 players. Entry forms can be submitted and fees paid at www.ctsondemand.com/. The annual Jay Swanson Tournament is dedicated to the memory of one of the most well loved pool players of all time.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <b>
           2/24  3/2
          </b>
          -The 21st Annual US Bar Table Championships will be held in Reno Nevada at the Grand Sierra Resort and Casino. The entries fees vary for 8, 9 and 10 ball leagues, as well as singles and team play. Entry forms and fees can be submitted online at www.ctsondemand.com/. Packages are available that include the entry fees for the tournament as well as lodging at the resort.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <b>
           2/24  2/28
          </b>
          -The 15th US Open One Pocket Championship will be held in Reno Nevada. This yearsopen will be at the Grand Sierra Resort and Casino.The entry for the event is $300. The Grand Sierra Resort offers packages that include tournament fees for each event plus lodging for each night. Fees and online sign up forms can be submitted to www.ctsondemand.com/.
         </li>
        </ul>
