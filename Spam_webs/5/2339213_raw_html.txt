      <div class="clear greyLinks abstractText larger">
       <span itemprop="description">
        <p>
         The 35-year-old actress is fourth months pregnant with her and hubby Brian Hallisay's second child.
        </p>
       </span>

      <div class="EchoTopic">
       <span id="intelliTxt">
        <span class="articleBody" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Earlier this month news broke that
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/jennifer-love-hewitt">
           Jennifer Love Hewitt
          </a>
          and her husband Brian Hallisay are
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/jennifer-love-hewitt-is-pregnant-actress-expecting-second-child-with-with-husband-brian-hallisay_4526302">
           expecting their second child together
          </a>
          , and although the 35-year-old actress' due date is not yet known, she is really excited about welcoming her second child into the world.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Jennifer Love Hewiit " height="770" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ln/20140718/070115_file_jennifer_love_hewitt/jennifer-love-hewitt-2014-television-critics-association-summer_4524356.jpg" style="vertical-align: top; border: 1px solid black;" title="Jennifer Love Hewitt " width="500"/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: small;">
           Hewitt is pregnanct with her and hubby Brian Hallisay's second child
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Hewitt, who shares a 13 month-old daughter, Autumn James, with Hallisay, is luckily having a trouble-free pregnancy so far, much like her experience the first time she was carrying a baby.
         </p>
         <p>
          "Jennifer is almost four months pregnant and doing great," a source close to Hewitt told
          <a href="http://uk.eonline.com/news/617838/jennifer-love-hewitt-doing-great-during-second-pregnancy-and-thrilled-about-expanding-her-family" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           E!News
          </a>
          . "They are loving being a family and excited about expanding. Jennifer knows having two children under two is going to be a lot of work, but she loves the idea of having her kids be close in age. Being a mom is the most important thing to her and she's thrilled about being pregnant again."
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           More:
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/i-know-what-you-did-last-summer-remake_4375332">
            Does Anybody Want a Remake of 'I Know What You Did Last Summer'?
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The 'Ghost Whisperer' actress and her hubby, who secretly tied the knot in November 2013 shortly after the birth of their first child, are equally as excited for Autumn as she is preparing to become an older sister.
         </p>
         <p>
          "She's head over heels for her daughter, Autumn, and can't wait for her to have a sibling," the insider continued. "Jennifer and Brian are often at home with Autumn and spend as little time away from her as possible. When Jennifer has been working on Criminal Minds, a nanny brings Autumn to the set every day. They play in her trailer while Jennifer is filming and then when she has breaks, she gets to see the baby."
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           More:
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/article/jennifer-love-hewitt-criminal-minds-series-regular_4268941">
            Jennifer Love Hewitt To Go Undercover With Series Regular Role On "Criminal Minds"
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          When announcing her second pregnancy, Hewitt's rep released a statement, which read, "Jennifer and Brian are thrilled to be expecting their second child later this year."
         </p>
