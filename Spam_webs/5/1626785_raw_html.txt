   <header>
    <p>
     <span>
      <i class="fa fa-folder-open">
      </i>
     </span>
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/tips/be-inspired/" rel="tag">
      Be Inspired
     </a>
     ,
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/destinations/usa/new-york/" rel="tag">
      New York
     </a>
    </p>
   </header>
   <div class="content">
    <!-- Content -->
    <p>
     <b>
      Its hard to believe you can get anything for free in a place like
      <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/thingstodo/new-york" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/thingstodo/new-york', 'New York']);" title="things to do in New York">
       New York
      </a>
      , but if you know where to go, you can easily spend a whole day (or more) discovering this amazing city  without spending any money!
     </b>
    </p>
    <p>
     These are 8 of my favourite free things to do in New York:
    </p>
    <h3>
     St Pauls Chapel
    </h3>
    <p>
     This is the place is where a lot of firefighters and rescue workers slept for weeks after 9/11 and is filled with memorabilia to that effect. Located right by the
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/tickets/heroes-of-the-world-trade-center-tour" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/tickets/heroes-of-the-world-trade-center-tour', 'World Trade Center']);" title="World Trade Center">
      World Trade Center
     </a>
     in Lower Manhattan, its free to enter and is a must-visit if you want to know more about 9/11.
    </p>
    <h3>
     Staten Island Ferry
    </h3>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/staten.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/staten.jpg', '']);">
      <img alt="staten" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1079" height="199" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/staten-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     In a city as bustling as NY, who imagined you could get any kind of transport for free? Most tourists who get on this ferry dont actually want to get to Staten Island  they do it because it goes right past the
     <b>
      Statue of Liberty
     </b>
      something a lot of people pay good money for!
    </p>
    <h3>
     Coney Island
    </h3>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/coney.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/coney.jpg', '']);">
      <img alt="coney" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1080" height="225" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/coney-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     This is one of the quirkier parts of New York and has loads to do that wont cost you a dime. Theres a beach, a fairground and fireworks at the boardwalk every Friday, as well as tons of annual events including a 4th of July hot-dog-eating contest and a Mermaid Parade!
    </p>
    <h3>
     Walk the high line
    </h3>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/walk.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/walk.jpg', '']);">
      <img alt="Highline Park New York City, Highline Park New York City" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1081" height="200" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/walk-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     Built on a former railroad, this 1-mile urban park has plenty to grab your attention, with glorious views across the water to New Jersey (no honestly!) or the striking graffiti art that pops up along the way. Add in the observation areas, water features, lounging spots, a bar and beautiful plants and you could easily lose track of time floating above Lower West Manhattan.
    </p>
    <h3>
     Central Park
    </h3>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/central.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/central.jpg', '']);">
      <img alt="central" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1082" height="225" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/central-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     You can spend the best part of a day just strolling through this gigantic park. It has a zoo (which you have to pay to enter, but its not a lot) and make sure you visit Strawberry Fields  a living memorial to John Lennon.
    </p>
    <p>
     Theres always something going on too  like performances from a choir or street-dancers (who have been known to pick people from the audience to join in!), and youre sure to recognise spots and locations from the movies as you walk along the paths.
    </p>
    <h3>
     Window-shopping
    </h3>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/window.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/window.jpg', '']);">
      <img alt="window" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1083" height="199" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/window-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     When it comes to shopping, New York is officially the worlds best city. Thats not so great if youre trying to keep your credit card under control  but just taking a walk down 5th Avenue with its fabulous window displays is an experience in itself  and can help to relieve those retail cravings!
    </p>
    <p>
     Want that instant kid again feeling? Head to
     <b>
      FAO Schwarz
     </b>
     toy shop with its famous Big Piano. There are usually queues to play it but its a NY staple! Even if youre not buying, you can easily spend hours looking at all the awesome toys, and there are always staff giving demonstrations!
    </p>
    <p>
     <b>
      If you know any other free things to do in NY, make sure you let me know!
     </b>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
