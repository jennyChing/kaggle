        <p>
         If youre a student at one of the universities in the Greater Toronto Area, you really should check out A1 GTA Cabs student services. You can get a taxi in Bolton, Vaughan, Oshawa, Caledon or anywhere around GTA at an affordable rate to take you (and your friends) where you need to go.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether youre trying to get to class on time or you and some friends would like to go out for a couple of beers at happy hour, we know that you want to have your taxi in Caledon or anywhere in the Toronto area on time, clean, and spacious. For group trips, we have 14-passenger vans and SUVs that will accommodate you and your friends or colleagues wherever you need to go via
         <a href="http://www.a1gtacab.com/blog/our-services-extend-to-bolton-oshawa-and-caledon/" target="_blank">
          taxi in Oshawa
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre tired of waiting for the bus in winter cold or summer heat and depending on an unreliable transit schedule to get you to class or work on time, A1 GTA has the solution for you. You dont have to spend a ton of money buying your own car. Instead, just get an A1 GTA
         <a href="http://www.a1gtacab.com/blog/taxi-or-cab-service-in-vaughan/" target="_blank">
          taxi in Vaughan
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether you need to get to work, school, or a party, and you dont your own personal car, you can get there quickly, safely, and in style with an affordable and reliable taxi in Bolton. A1 GTA Cab understands students transportation needs, and we work hard to make sure that we can always meet them and take you wherever you need to go, when you need to be there.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont trust your transportation needs to buses and luck; Call A1 GTA Cab for fast and easy transportation anywhere in the Greater Toronto Area.
