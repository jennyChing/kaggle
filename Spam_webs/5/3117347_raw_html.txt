                 <p>
                  <a href="http://blog.gessato.com/2014/12/05/blue-and-red-vector-print-series-for-gusto-robusto/blue-and-red-vector-print-series-for-gusto-robusto-1/" rel="attachment wp-att-123103">

                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="http://www.gustorobusto.com" target="_blank">
                   Gusto Robustos series n.2
                  </a>
                  is a quartet of digital vector artwork printed in Pantone shades of blue and red. The collection includes artwork by four Italian artists: Gloria Pizzilli, Sara Penco, David Sossella, and Mirko Cmia. Their work here explores humanity, nature, and industrialism while playing with Japanese and pop imagery. Detailed with vivid colors and sharp lines, the graphics are typographically printed on museum-quality Fedrigoni paper and promise to gain value over time. At present, the miniature prints are priced at 25,00, and the larger, limited editions go for 150,00.
                 </p>
