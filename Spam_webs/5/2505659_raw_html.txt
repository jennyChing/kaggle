       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span style="color: #0000ff;">
         <b style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          WhatsApp  Largest Acquisition by Facebook
         </b>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <img alt="WhatsApp Aquisition" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-807" height="73" src="http://www.techrobo.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/WhatsApp-Aquisition.png" width="200"/>
        WhatsApp a mobile messaging app has been acquired by Facebook Inc. for $ 19 Billion. The deal was announced on Wednesday, 19, 2014. It is largest acquisition by social network giant Facebook.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Facebook said that $ 4 billion amount will be paid in cash and $12 billion in stocks. And as per terms and conditions all 55 employees of WhatsApp including its founder will be granted restricted stock worth $3 billion.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Mark Zuckerberg Facebook founder announced this deal on their wall by stating:
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Im excited to announce that weve agreed to acquire WhatsApp and that their entire team will be joining us at Facebook.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        I am going to share screenshot of Mark Zuckerberg Facebook wall post regarding the announcement of this acquisition. Below is the screenshot.
        <span id="more-806">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <img alt="whatsapp " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-808" height="506" src="http://www.techrobo.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/whatsapp.png" width="587"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span style="color: #0000ff;">
         <b>
          Why Zuckerberg acquired WhatsApp?
         </b>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        WhatsApp is one of the most popular mobile/Smartphone messaging app having 450 million user and 190 million active user around the world. The app is handling 10 billion messages each day. The number of WhatsApp users are increasing every day.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Apart from this, approximately 400 million photos are shared every day by WhatsApp user. So, this app was becoming very popular among Smartphone user and tablet users.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <b>
          Mark Zuckerberg Interested in WhatsApp Since 2012!!  A Report
         </b>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Mark Zuckerberg was interested in this app since 2012 because according to a source we came to know that Mark first reached out to WhatsApp founder Jan Koum in 2012 spring.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg and WhatsApp founder Jan Koum met at a coffee shop in Los Altos, California, USA and both spoke for an hour. After this, meeting they continue meeting and discussing.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Finally now we know that WhatsApp has been acquired by Facebook Inc. According to me, the main reason behind this acquisition is huge amount of active user and popularity of WhatsApp. Apart from this, Mark Zuckerberg also told that I am impressed with the unique service which is being offered by WhatsApp. They provide free message option/feature for 1 year and this is really amazing.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        From today all WhatsApp employee will work for Facebook and for the improvement of WhatsApp mobile messaging application.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        So, if you are a user of WhatsApp application then enjoy because now you will also see some better improvement in this app.
       </p>
