        <h1>
         Syndication/Engagement Groups
        </h1>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/YP3LQXEmkIE?rel=0" width="560">
         </iframe>
         <br/>
         In this video, I talk about syndication groups and engagement groups. I share how they
         <br/>
         can help you begin to get more views and clicks even. Video engagement is important as
         <br/>
         well as blog syndication, so if you can find a few of these groups I suggest getting in them
         <br/>
         and following the rules. Once you do and start posting your content, your views will grow.
        </p>
        <p>
         I share how you can do this and then talk about the two key social networks that I use
         <br/>
         daily to build and grow with this method. A lot more people should be doing this because
         <br/>
         it is such a great way to spread your web out.
        </p>
