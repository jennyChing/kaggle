         Tips You Will Need to Know to Set Up a Safe Online Betting Site
        </h1>

          <p>
           If you are looking to become an
           <a href="../bookie">
            online bookie
           </a>
           , then your focus would obviously be your website. One of the types of competition you will have to deal with is a betting exchange. But you can use the challenges of betting on an exchange as a model to develop your new online betting business. Customers who find exchanges too risky will need to see something from your website that will convince them that they will be safe betting with you. There are plenty of safety tips you can follow that will make people want to bet on your website.
          </p>
          <p>
           When people sign up for your
           <a href="http://www.payperhead.com/sportsbook">
            online sportsbook
           </a>
           , they will obviously sign up using their name and some of their information. But you will need a system that stores the betting data offshore and assigns customers PIN numbers they can use instead of their names. This may seem like a long way to go to insure privacy, but your customers will appreciate it when they know that they can bet safely and without attaching their personal names to their betting activity.
          </p>
          <p>
           Customers who sign up for a betting exchange have to give their financial information and their personal information. If you want to attract clients to your betting website, then you need to leave their financial information out of the equation. You need to set up a website that allows customers to enjoy their betting action, but keep the financial transactions off the betting website. This will not only help to add a layer of security to your website, but it will also help you to strengthen your relationships with your clients.
          </p>
          <p>
           Since your website will be available to customers all over the world, you will need a toll free customer service phone number that is staffed by multi-lingual betting experts. Your staff of betting experts can answer questions for your customers and they can even take bets over the phone. It is part of offering all of the customer service angles you will need to attract clients. You should also allow customers to use their PIN numbers when calling customer service instead of their names.
          </p>
          <p>
           All of this security, as well as the safety offshore betting, are something that betting exchanges do not offer. But you can offer all of this to your customers when you subscribe to the
           <a href="../price-per-head">
            price per head
           </a>
           system. The price per head system has its offices offshore, where customer data can be safely stored. When you use the price per head system, your clients are assigned a PIN number that they use whenever they call customer service or place bets.
          </p>
          <p>
           The best part about the price per head system is that your customers never have to give their financial information to sign up for an account. All of the financial transactions happen directly between you and your clients. It strengthens your client relationships and gives your clients the peace of mind they need to use your website.
          </p>
