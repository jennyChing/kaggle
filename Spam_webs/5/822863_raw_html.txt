          <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-22 photo" height="22" src="https://1.gravatar.com/avatar/40b5f33ed0fe2d7e2770cdcc2535b7ad?s=22&amp;d=mm&amp;r=g" srcset="https://1.gravatar.com/avatar/40b5f33ed0fe2d7e2770cdcc2535b7ad?s=44&amp;d=mm&amp;r=g 2x" width="22"/>

         SoundCloud Voices  Hear the election
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          SoundCloud Voices is a weekly Community feature focusing on new spoken word creators found on SoundCloud. It might be a podcast, radio show, audio book, interview, audio messages, a poem, anything! Keep an eye out every Thursday for a new post.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10748" height="600" src="https://soundcloud-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Election2012_cmyk.jpg" title="Election2012_cmyk" width="600"/>
          <br/>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Love em or hate em, the U.S. elections have taken up a large portion of our Twitter, Facebook, and SoundCloud feeds over the past few weeks. As we bid farewell to araucouspolitical season, we thought wed highlight some of the great content that popped up on SoundCloud around election day.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         First, the final speeches from the Mitt Romney and Barack Obama. One victory, one concession.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F66459845?" width="100%">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F66454585?" width="100%">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Steven Kastenbaum, a reporter for CNN, was at Romneys headquarters as the results came in. He captured the scene as the mood turned from jovial to somber.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F66444972?" width="100%">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         CNNs Nova Safo captured a few moments of jubliation at President Obamas HQ, moments after the final results were announced.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F66445676?" width="100%">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         This weeks election wasnt all presidential however. Many other positions and issues, from senate races to marijuana legalization, were voted on. Democracy Now produced a great summary of the broader election results.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F66522888?" width="100%">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre inclined to see the lighter side of politics, check out Laughspins Dylan Gadinos talk with Jordan Peele and Keegan-Michael Key at Comedy Centrals official Indecision election night party.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F66503788?" width="100%">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

        </p>
