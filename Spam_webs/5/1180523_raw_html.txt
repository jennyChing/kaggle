                   <u>
                    Did You Know?
                   </u>
                  </b>
                 </h3>
                 <h5>
                  Adult Link was considered to make an appearance in The Wind Waker
                 </h5>
                </center>
                <font size="2">
                 <table align="left" border="1" bordercolor="#000000" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" noshade="">
                  <tr>
                   <td>
                    <img alt="Adult Link" height="164" src="/dyk/media/009a.jpg" width="95"/>
                   </td>
                  </tr>
                 </table>
                 A number of months ago, a Japanese company released a product called "The Zelda Box." Inside this curious little box was exclusive information, media and figures from Nintendo's classic GameCube game, The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Included in these items were prints of concept art for the game, one of which reveals that the team was considering having an adult Link in the Wind Waker.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 There's absolutely nothing else to say on the matter, but it's interesting to think how the inclusion of an adult Link would've changed the game's story, game mechanics and even public perception.
                 <br/>
