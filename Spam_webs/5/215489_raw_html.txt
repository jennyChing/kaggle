           Delysia Chocolatier Wins Gold!!!
          </h1>
          <div class="post-meta">

          <img alt="delysia12" class="attachment-large wp-post-image" src="//i1.wp.com/www.crazyforconfections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/delysia12.jpg?fit=1024%2C1024"/>

         <div class="post-content cf">
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/crazyforconfections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/patel.jpg">
            <img alt="patel" class="aligncenter size-thumbnail wp-image-182" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i1.wp.com/crazyforconfections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/patel-150x150.jpg?resize=150%2C150"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Celebrated premium chocolate artisan
           <a href="delysia.com">
            <strong>
             Delysia Chocolatier
            </strong>
           </a>
           has been awarded two Gold Medals at the 2014
           <strong>
            International Chocolate Salon Awards
           </strong>
           . The Austin-based chocolatier won top honors in the Most Unique category for its wildly-popular Carrot Cake and White Peach Balsamic truffles, respectively, at the Salons Top Truffle Artistry Award Competition. The International Chocolate Salon Awards recognizesthe finest in artisan and premium chocolate and confections.
          </p>
          <div>
           Additionally,
           <strong>
            Delysia
           </strong>
           (
           <wbr>
           </wbr>
           pronounced:
           <em>
            [ duh  lis    uh ]
           </em>
           ) was bestowed two Silver Medals in the Best Taste and Flavor category  for the brands Mexican Mol and White Peach Balsamic truffles  as well as two Bronze Medals; awarded to the White Peach Balsamic truffle in both the Best Ingredient Combinations and Best in Salon Truffle divisions.Earlier this year,
           <strong>
            Delysia Chocolatier
           </strong>
           was named as one of the
           <strong>
            <em>
             Best Chocolatiers and Confectioners in America
            </em>
           </strong>
           by the Salon.
           <strong>
            Delysia
           </strong>
           was one of only ten chocolatiers to receive the coveted six-star rating and is the only Texas-based chocolate company to land the perfect appraisement. For more information on the International Chocolate Salon, visit
           <a href="http://nomnom-pr.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=17c4d56c37573b8b84def0fe3&amp;id=9e4243494f&amp;e=61f94401a9" style="color: #6dc6dd;" target="_blank">
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             this location
            </span>
           </a>
           .
          </div>
          <div>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/crazyforconfections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/delysia12.jpg">
            <img alt="delysia12" class="aligncenter size-thumbnail wp-image-181" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i2.wp.com/crazyforconfections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/delysia12-150x150.jpg?resize=150%2C150"/>
           </a>
           There are no words to describe how amazing it feels to be among the best in the country, said
           <strong>
            Delysia
           </strong>
           founder and chocolatier, Chef
           <strong>
            Nicole Patel
           </strong>
           . Being once again recognized by the International Chocolate Salon is such a huge honor.
          </div>
