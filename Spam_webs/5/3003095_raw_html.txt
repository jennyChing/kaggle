            <div class="article-content-inner">
             <h2>
              Lasagna Cupcakes
             </h2>
             <p class="author">
              As prepared by
              <span class="name">
               Girl Who Ate Everything
               <span class="author">
                ,
               </span>
              </span>
              <span class="date" content="2011-03-31" itemprop="datePublished">
               March 31, 2011
              </span>
             </p>
             <div class="generated-content" itemprop="articleBody">
              Cupcakes are coming in all shapes and sizes these days but seriouslyLasagna Cupcakes? Is that even possible? It is! These Lasagna Cupcakes are easily made using all of the same ingredients in your classic lasagna with layers of meat, three types of cheeses, and pasta.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Usually lasagna looks like a big gooey mess, but when its in cupcake form it looks elegant and refined. Wonton wrappers are used as the pasta element in this lasagna, eliminating the nightmare and logistics of trying to make pasta noodles cupcake sized.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Wonton wrappers are fresh little sheets of pasta that are perfectly sized for these lasagna cupcakes and because of their thinness, they cook up super fast. Just take a stack of wonton wrappers and place a drinking glass on top. Using a knife, cut around to the glass to make circle shapes.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://www.tablespoon.com/recipes/lasagna-cupcakes-recipe/1/" title="Lasagna Cupcakes Recipe">
               <img alt="Lasagna Cupcakes" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7874" height="501" src="/-/media/Images/Articles/PostImages/2011/03/week5/2011-03-31-lasagna-cupcakes-wonton-500.jpg" width="500"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Next, assemble layers of pasta, cheese, meat, and sauce in the muffin tins to make your lasagna cupcakes.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://www.tablespoon.com/recipes/lasagna-cupcakes-recipe/1/" title="Lasagna Cupcakes Recipe">
               <img alt="How to Make Lasagna Cupcakes" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7877" height="501" src="/-/media/Images/Articles/PostImages/2011/03/week5/2011-03-31-lasagna-cupcakes-assemble-500.jpg" width="500"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              I dont know about your family, but in my family when we eat lasagna, we all fight over the corners of the pan because of the crispy and chewy texture. With these cupcakes, every single one has all the yummy qualities of those corner pieces.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://www.tablespoon.com/recipes/lasagna-cupcakes-recipe/1/" title="Lasagna Cupcakes Recipe">
               <img alt="Lasagna Cupcakes Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7878" height="700" src="/-/media/Images/Articles/PostImages/2012/01/week1/2011-03-31-lasagna-cupcakes-row-500.jpg" width="500"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Just sink your teeth into the melted cheese and pasta in these lasagna cupcakes!
