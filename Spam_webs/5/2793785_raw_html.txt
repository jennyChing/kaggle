            Jathi $100 VISA Gift Card Giveaway
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="post-header-line-1">
          </div>
          <div class="post-body" id="post-2738565661666223114">
           <style>
            #fullpost{display:inline;}
           </style>
           <p>
            Spending time with our loved ones is so important.  Learn more about neat app that makes spending time with your kids, spouse, friends, siblings, parents, or other loved ones even more special!
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Plus, enter for a chance to win a $100 VISA gift card!
            <br/>
            <br/>
           </p>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WyJG8zhFzXk/UbX_6eEM2sI/AAAAAAAAIJQ/naC_7XMjFao/s1600/jathi.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WyJG8zhFzXk/UbX_6eEM2sI/AAAAAAAAIJQ/naC_7XMjFao/s320/jathi.jpg" width="285"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <br/>
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            <br/>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://jathi.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Jathi
            </a>
            , short for "joined at the hip", is a new app set to launch soon! It is a mobile-only iPhone app that lets you create an e-invitation called a J-vite that is activity focused. The J-vite is a custom-designed e-invitation you can send to a friend or loved one  just for the two of you.
           </div>
           <br/>
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            So if  you would like to plan a special get together with your daughter, mom, husband, ect., this app will allow you to send a personalized detailed e-invitation in just minutes! I know you might be thinking, "I can just send a text." This is more than just a text....this is a special e-invitation when you want to say, "Hey, I want to spend time one-on-one with you because you are special to me." Even for activities as simple as an ice cream date, wine on the deck after the kids are in bed, or dinner and a movie night out.  You can make them a special time by making an extra special e-invitation for this one person you care to spend your time with.
           </div>
           <br/>
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            Jathi will be free to download and explore.  You can see a
            <a href="http://jathi.com/tour/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             tour of the Jathi App here
            </a>
            . For a limited time, you will be able to use the free trial option, so you can send a J-vite free of charge! Then after your free trail 3 J-vites cost just $1 or 8 J-vites for $2.00.
           </div>
           <br/>
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            Make sure to sign-up for the
            <a href="http://jathi.com/blog/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Jathi Blog Newsletter
            </a>
            to keep up to date on the launch date.
           </div>
           <br/>
           The Jathi App features includes:
           <br/>
           <ul>
            <li>
             a broad range of activity ideas and options to select from
            </li>
            <li>
             beautiful artwork created by international artists, or add your own
            </li>
            <li>
             options for just the right sentiment to go with your J-vite, or create your own
            </li>
            <li>
             scheduling calendar
            </li>
            <li>
             message board to post comments and photos before and after the activity
            </li>
           </ul>
