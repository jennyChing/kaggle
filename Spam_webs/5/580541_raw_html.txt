       <a class="autolink" href="http://games.ign.com/objects/026/026417.html">
        Valve
       </a>
       Software, during the Games for Change festival in New York City today.
      </p>

        Portal 2
       </a>
       was released on April 19, 2011.
      </p>
      <p>
