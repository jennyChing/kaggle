      <p>
       So youve decided to treat yourself to an upgrade and invest in every homeowners dream: Hardwood flooring. Hardwood flooring has been synonymous with classic style and luxury for decades, and it is one of the most popular flooring types around. But if you are accustomed to caring for a different type of flooring, whether that means carpeting, vinyl, or tile, it may take learning a few tricks to really get the hang of living with hardwood. Read on to find out more!
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://www.verticalconnection.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/flooring-and-cabinets.jpg">
        <img alt="hardwood-floor" class="alignleft wp-image-1107 size-full" height="385" src="http://www.verticalconnection.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/flooring-and-cabinets.jpg" width="349"/>
       </a>
       Hardwood Flooring 101: The Basics To Know
      </h2>
      <p>
       There are a few tips and tricks that homeowners can employ when their hardwood floors are first installed. Unlike a super low-maintenance flooring option, such as vinyl, hardwood floors require a little extra attention to stay looking their best.
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Place protective pads on furniture. Chair legs and the feet of other pieces of furniture could potentially scrape or gouge the surface of your hardwood floors. By simply fixing a small felt pad to the bottom of these things, you reduce that risk of damage.
       </li>
       <li>
        Wipe up spills. While an overturned glass of water may not seem like a threat, you will want to clean it or any other liquids up off of your hardwood floors as soon as they are spilled. Excessive exposure to moisture could cause the wood to buckle or warp.
       </li>
       <li>
        Learn how to maintain your flooring. The finish of hardwood is what gives it its signature shine and beauty. Depending on the wood type and finish type, different treatments can be used to maintain this shine. Always be sure to use a cleaner made specifically for hardwood flooring. Check out our
        <a href="http://www.verticalconnection.com/wood-floor-care/">
         maintenance page
        </a>
        for more detailed cleaning instructions.
       </li>
       <li>
        But dont worry too much! Though there are extra steps involved in taking care of hardwood floors, it is important to remember that hardwood is one of the most durable materials out there. By choosing hardwood, you are making a great investment in your home!
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Do you have questions about switching to hardwood floors? Get in touch with the experts at The Vertical Connection today!
