             <div class="itemIntroText">
              <p>
               Another eclectic selection of interesting things to do over the coming seven days on the beautiful, sun-soaked French Riviera.
              </p>
             </div>
             <!-- Item fulltext -->
             <div class="itemFullText">
              <p>
               We start off this week with a visit to
               <strong>
                Monaco
               </strong>
               to spend an evening in the company of the
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.opmc.mc/index.php?id_concert=350" target="_blank">
                 Orchestre Philharmonique de Monte-Carlo
                </a>
               </strong>
               . They will be performing works by
               <em>
                Smetana
               </em>
               ,
               <em>
                Martin
               </em>
               , and
               <em>
                Dvok
               </em>
               , featuring
               <strong>
                Sol Gabetta
               </strong>
               on cello, with musical direction by
               <strong>
                Krzysztof Urbanski
               </strong>
               . The concert, which is part of the
               <strong>
                Grande Saison series
               </strong>
               , takes place in the
               <strong>
                Auditorium Rainier III
               </strong>
               at 6pm on Sunday, 4th May. Tickets range in price from 16 to 32 and may be
               <a href="http://billetterie.opmc.mc/index.php?c=performance&amp;m=bookmap&amp;performance=992" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 reserved online
                </strong>
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               A new and innovative
               <strong>
                dance and virtual unreality spectacle
               </strong>
               entitled '
               <a href="http://www.nice-acropolis.com/agenda_detail.php?id=743" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Mr &amp; Mme Rve
                </strong>
               </a>
               ' comes to the
               <strong>
                Acropolis in Nice
               </strong>
               this Saturday, 3rd May. Performed by the
               <strong>
                Compagnie Pietragalla-Derouault
               </strong>
               , in conjunction with
               <strong>
                Dassault Systmes
               </strong>
               , The 3DEXPERIENCE company, the show is choreographed and directed by
               <strong>
                Marie-Claude Pietragalla
               </strong>
               and
               <strong>
                Julien Derouault
               </strong>
               . The great
               <strong>
                Laurent Garnier
               </strong>
               provides the musical score. The performance takes place in the
               <strong>
                Salle Apollon
               </strong>
               at 8:30pm. Tickets range in price from 45 to 65 and are available from all the usual outlets or may be
               <a href="http://www.ticketnet.fr/fr/manifestation/mr-et-mme-reve-billet/idmanif/286786" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 reserved online
                </strong>
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="Fte des Main in Cimiez in Nice in 2013" src="/images/fete-des-mai-2013.JPG"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               The annual Niois
               <a href="http://www.nice.fr/Actualites/Fete-des-mai" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Fte des Mai
                </strong>
               </a>
               starts today, Thursday, 1st May, and continues throughout the month, with activities scheduled every
               <strong>
                Sunday
               </strong>
               under the olive trees of the
               <strong>
                Jardins des Arnes de Cimiez
               </strong>
               . There will also be celebrations taking place in the various
               <strong>
                quartiers of Nice
               </strong>
               each Saturday over the coming weeks. Fun times for all the family guaranteed!
              </p>
              <p>
               The
               <a href="http://www.riviera-buzz.com/lifestyle/lgbt/item/625-in-out-film-festival-2014.html" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 6th annual In&amp;Out Gay and Lesbian Film Festival
                </strong>
               </a>
               , the theme of which this year is 
               <em>
                Je taime, mon non plus
               </em>
               , comes to an end this Sunday, May 3rd.The programme this year boasted an
               <strong>
                impressive lineup
               </strong>
               of films, readings, performances and exhibitions. Full details of the remaining events are available on the
               <a href="http://www.inoutfestival2014.com" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 festival website
                </strong>
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               The
               <strong>
                42nd Antibes Art Fair
               </strong>
               , or the
               <a href="http://www.salon-antiquaires-antibes.com" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Salon d'Antiquits, Brocante et Art Moderne
                </strong>
               </a>
               , continues in
               <strong>
                Port Vauban
               </strong>
               , coming to a conclusion next Monday, 5th May. This event is one of the most important on its kind in the world of
               <strong>
                French antiques
               </strong>
               , and attracts interest from both national and international collectors. The Salon is open everyday from 10:30am to 7:30pm and admission is 10, with reductions available for students and people of reduced mobility.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <!-- JoomlaWorks "AllVideos" Plugin (v4.4) starts here -->

                 <div class="avPlayerContainer" style="width:680px;">

                   </iframe>
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <!-- JoomlaWorks "AllVideos" Plugin (v4.4) ends here -->
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               The phenomenon that is
               <strong>
                Riverdance
               </strong>
               touches down in the
               <strong>
                Palais Nikaia in Nice
               </strong>
               tomorrow, Friday 2nd May, with its
               <a href="http://riverdance.com/france/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 20th Anniversary Tour
                </strong>
               </a>
               . A dance spectacular already seen by some
               <strong>
                23 million people
               </strong>
               in 350 cities worldwide, this tour is billed as '
               <em>
                The Original ... The Best'.
               </em>
               <a href="http://www.ticketnet.fr/fr/manifestation/riverdance-billet/idmanif/289807#" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Tickets are still available
                </strong>
               </a>
               and range in price from 35.50 to 64
              </p>
              <p>
               European champions
               <a href="http://www.rctoulon.com/en/page/accueil/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 RC Toulonnais
                </strong>
               </a>
               , who have once again secured their place in the
               <strong>
                Heineken Cup final
               </strong>
               in Cardiff later this month, face
               <strong>
                Stade Franais
               </strong>
               at the
               <a href="http://www.allianz-riviera.fr/actualite/articles/rc-toulon-stade-francais-paris-187" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Allianz Riviera
                </strong>
               </a>
               at 2:40pm this Saturday, 3rd May. This is the final
               <strong>
                Top 14
               </strong>
               match of the season.
              </p>
              <p>
               Our local
               <strong>
                soccer teams
               </strong>
               are both in action over the coming days.
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.ogcnice.com/fr/" target="_blank">
                 OGC Nice
                </a>
               </strong>
               travel to
               <strong>
                FC Sochaux-Montbliard
               </strong>
               for their 5pm encounter this Sunday, 4th May, while
               <a href="http://www.asm-fc.com/fr/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 AS Monaco
                </strong>
               </a>
               welcome
               <strong>
                EA Guingamp
               </strong>
               to the
               <strong>
                Stade Louis II
               </strong>
               for their 7pm fixture on Wednesday, 7th May..
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>

                   </iframe>
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <!-- JoomlaWorks "AllVideos" Plugin (v4.4) ends here -->

              </p>
              <p>
               For all the
               <strong>
                movie buffs
               </strong>
               out there, be sure to check out the
               <a href="http://www.rivieraradio.mc/sectional.asp?id=36863" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 listings for all the latest movies
                </strong>
               </a>
               in English throughout the region, courtesy of our friends at
               <strong>
                Riviera Radio
               </strong>
               .

              <p>
               Don't forget
               <a href="https://www.facebook.com/DavesTriviaNight" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Dave's Trivia Night
                </strong>
               </a>
               , every Wednesday night from 8:30pm in
               <strong>
                Blue Whales pub in Vieux Nice
               </strong>
               . Teams of five, free entry, and
               <strong>
                Happy Hour
               </strong>
               until midnight -- definitelythe best value in town!
              </p>

               So, that's the

               for this week.Have a great weekend and week ahead, and do remember to keep checking back with

                Riviera Buzz
