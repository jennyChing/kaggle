     <h3>
      Belgium Beers
     </h3>
     <p>
      Cheers!  Welcome to our web shop where we offer you the best Belgian beers. We invite you to take 
a closer look at our online store. We at trappistbeershop.com are an experienced and trustworthy 
worldwide distributor of the best Belgian beers. The beer we sell are not ?ordinairy? Belgian 
beers, but all Trappist Belgian beers. Only monasteries, by following certain strict criteria,
 are entitled to brew Trappist beer mainly to preserve the ancient traditions and excellent 
quality. You can imagine that, because of this, Trappist beers are all great products with 
perfect tastes.
     </p>
     <p>
      At trappistbeershop.com we offer you Westmalle, Rochefort, Zundert, Orval, La Trappe, Chimay, 
Engelszell, Hamont Achel and Westvleteren. As you can see, almost too much to choose from! You
 can order single bottles or six packs but also cases and even special glasses which fits the 
specific beer. When you decide to buy Belgian beers at our web shop your order will be handled 
with the outmost care and you will receive your order within 3 ? 7 days. And delivery guaranteed 
because we will send you a new package in case of loss or being held by customs. When ordering 
you will also receive a tracking to follow your order online 24/7. So please take a look at our 
web shop and enjoy! We are very confident you will not be disappointed.
     </p>
