            <img alt="Pretty In Pink Garden Art by Omaste Witkowski owFotoGrafik.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-3866 size-large" height="600" src="http://owfotografik.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/pretty-in-pink-garden-art-by-omaste-witkowski-owfotografik-com.jpg?w=800" width="800"/>
           </a>
          </h2>
          <h2 style="text-align:center;">
           Pretty In Pink Garden Art
          </h2>
          <p>
           I really love flowers and the Peony is no exception. This pretty pink example of one was found in my Pacific Northwest garden. I look forward to their vibrant blooms in the spring/summer and it is always a treat to see just how many blooms are ushered in each year.
          </p>
          <p>
           I am really enjoying combining my photography and using a layering technique to isolate bloom from background. I enjoy the painted effect in the back and the clarity and light of a photograph on the main subject. Lots of fun incorporating textures into photographs.
           <br/>
           <a href="http://omaste-witkowski.artistwebsites.com/featured/pretty-in-pink-garden-art-by-omaste-witkowski-omaste-witkowski.html">
            <img alt="Pretty In Pink Garden Art by Omaste Witkowski" src="http://omaste-witkowski.artistwebsites.com/displayartworkartistwebsites.html?id=12739928&amp;width=250&amp;height=187" style="border:none;" title="Pretty In Pink Garden Art by Omaste Witkowski"/>
           </a>
          </p>
