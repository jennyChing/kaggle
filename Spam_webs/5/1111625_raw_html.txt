        <h1>
         Stress During the Holidays
        </h1>
        <p>
         <img alt="5 ways to relieve stress during the holidays" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6847" height="637" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/5-ways-to-relieve-stress-during-the-holidays.jpg" width="494"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Raise your hand if you deal with stress on a daily basis! I bet many of you have all kinds of stresses, including stress caused by money, relationships, health, work, and, dare I say, kids. What causes the most stress for you?
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          What about during the holidays? Does your stress increase then?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         I know it increases for me, and I can only imagine how it snowballs for others during the very busy holiday season.
        </p>
        <p>
         So how do you relieve stress during the holidays?
        </p>
        <h1>
         5 Ways to Relieve Stress During the Holidays
        </h1>
        <p>
         Here are 5 ways to relieve stress during the holidays:
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          1. Think Happy Thoughts
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6864" height="370" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/1.jpg" width="545"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This might seem like a crazy tip since were talking about the holidays, but think about all of the times you get stressed during the holidays: planning parties, buying gifts, preparing meals, having guests in your home, driving to stores, etc. Are all of those times stress-free? Things can get hectic pretty fast, especially when you have other things going on at work or with the kids.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         If you feel like youre getting stressed during the holidays, close your eyes (not while driving, of course), take a deep breath, and think of something that makes you happy. It could be anything! You could think about your favorite vacation spot, your kids laughter, springtime, the tinkling of jingle bellswhatever floats your boat!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- In Text Small Horizontal -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-4076531311739857" data-ad-slot="7926229487" style="display: inline-block; width: 468px; height: 60px;">
         </ins>
        </p>
        <p>
         <script>
          // &lt;![CDATA[
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
// ]]&gt;
         </script>
        </p>
        <h3>
         2.
         <strong>
          Relieve Stress Naturally
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="ways to relieve holiday stress" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6853" height="113" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2-.jpg" width="346"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Oneway to relieve stress during the holidays is to use aromatherapy whilerelaxing the tension out of your muscles. Think: its like yoga, minus the stretching and plus soothing scents.
         <strong>
          <a href="http://pinchmedough.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Pinch Me Therapy Dough">
           Pinch Me Therapy Dough
          </a>
         </strong>
         isjust that.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pinch me dough" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6858" height="416" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/pinch-me-dough.jpg" width="492"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           What is Pinch Me Therapy Dough?
          </em>
         </strong>
         Its a pliable dough that is infused with spa quality oils. Its very soft,and, depending on scents you prefer, smells heavenly. It comes in six varieties:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Tropics
         </li>
         <li>
          Ocean
         </li>
         <li>
          Beach
         </li>
         <li>
          Earth
         </li>
         <li>
          Spa
         </li>
         <li>
          For Men
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           How Does It Work?
          </em>
         </strong>
         When you knead the dough between your hands, you are using the muscles in your arms and relaxing them. You also breathe in the soothing aromas as you knead the dough. Both actions can distract you from whatever it is that is stressing you out.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks to the generous folks at Pinch Me and Mom Buzz Media, I was sent a Pinch Me Dough to review. I chose the Beach scent because when I think of relaxing, I think of laying on a beach, listening to the rolling waves as they reach the shoreline (note: this is a memory prior to having kids).
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pinch me therapy dough review" class="aligncenter wp-image-6860" height="400" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/pinch-me-therapy-dough-review.jpg" width="400"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres the description of Beach Pinch Me Therapy Dough:
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <em>
           Get mentally swept away to the seaside getaway youve been dreaming about.BEACH exudes the relaxing aroma of the sand, sun, and surf. No sunscreen required.
          </em>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         Lets start with the scent. The description above nails it on the head. When I first inhaled the aroma, I immediately thought of sunscreen and warm sand. I LOVE the smell of a good sunscreen, and the thought of warm sand is just so inviting. (Just writing about it calms me down!)
        </p>
        <p>
         The dough itself feels like a super soft childrens play dough. It has a smooth, creamy texture that is pleasant to roll between your fingers. Some of the scent of the aroma can linger on your hands without being oily, which is nice because the memories brought on by the scent stays with me for a while.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="relieve stress naturally" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6859" height="435" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/pinch-me.jpg" width="606"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I found that just a scoop of the dough was enough for me to feel the effects. Too much of the dough was too much for my hands to move comfortably.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pmtd" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6861" height="397" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/pmtd.jpg" width="560"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pinch Me Dough can be easily kept anywhere in your home or even taken on the go. I keep mine in the kitchen where I often separate myself for a few minutes from the chaos that is my home. I pull out the dough for a few minutes to relax before moving onto my next task for the day.
        </p>
        <p>
         I am keeping it nearby during the holidays, especially on the bitterly cold nights after a long day. I will let it take me to the beach, under an umbrella, toes in the warm sand, waves gently rolling.
        </p>
        <p>
         Having a jar of Pinch Me Dough can help you relieve any stress you feel during the holidays! Visit

        <h3>
         <strong>
          3. Go for a Walk or Get Exercise
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="go for walk" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6863" height="412" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/go-for-walk.jpg" width="543"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Simply going for a walk outdoors can help to relieve stress during the holidays. The combination of fresh air and your increased heart rate can help rejuvenate you to take on your next task. This can be especially helpful when you are in a crowded home with family and friends and just need to step out for a while.
        </p>
        <p>
         Any kind of exercise canhelp. Dont pick anything too strenuous (unless that helps you!) or you run the risk of injury, bringing on more stress. A Zumba class or another type of fitness class may be a fun way to relieve stress. Invite your friends or family to join you!
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          4.Accept help
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="toddler helping in kitchen" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6865" height="414" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/toddler-helping-in-kitchen.jpg" width="614"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you like to try to manage everything all on your own? That in itself can cause a ton of stress. Find someone to help you with your tasks.
        </p>
        <p>
         Make a list of what you would like to accomplish. Ask a friend or family member to take on some of the tasks, like shopping for recipe ingredients, wrapping presents, cleaning and decorating your home (a good task for kids!).
        </p>
        <p>
         While others are helping, try not to be managing
         <em>
          how
         </em>
         they help. No, they may not be doing things your way, but at least things are getting done!
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          5. Go with the Flow
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="go with the flow" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6866" height="414" src="http://mommyknowswhatsbest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/go-with-the-flow.jpg" width="545"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can make all kinds of lists and check them twice, but things can change in the blink of an eye. Be ready to go with the flow. All your planning may not go as..wellplanned.
        </p>
        <p>
         Instead of getting all worked up about a change in plans, go with the flow and readjust. Find ways to work around any unexpected event and make the most of what you have to work with.
        </p>

           What Are Your Favorite Ways to Relieve Stress During the Holidays?

         Leave a comment telling me your ways to relieve stress during the holidays!
