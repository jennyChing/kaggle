          <h2>
           <span class="mw-headline" id="Tips">
            Tips
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="section_text" id="tips">
           <ul>
            <li>
             Omit the spices and add 2 teaspoons vanilla. Glaze the warm doughnuts with vanilla or chocolate frosting.
            </li>
            <li>
             Use an electric fryer to make the task easier.
            </li>
            <li>
             An easy, thrifty "doughnut" is to take a tube of refrigerated biscuit dough and remove the biscuits. Poke a hole in each refrigerated biscuit and fry the biscuits in hot oil.
            </li>
            <li>
             Drop the unchilled dough by teaspoonfuls into the hot oil. These make wonderful and easy "drop" doughnuts. Much easier to make and just as good tasting.
            </li>
