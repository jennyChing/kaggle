        <p>
         Youve got to admit, pillow tutorials to sewists are mugs to a regular Joe. No matter how many of them you have, you cant help yourself from collecting one every time it catches your eye. We understand, and were here to enable you even more. The editors at AllFreeSewing happen to adore an easy pillow tutorial (and mugs, in fact), and today were featuring 8 Patterned Pillow Tutorials We Want You to See.
        </p>
        <p>
         You wont find these
         <a href="http://www.allfreesewing.com/Sewn-Pillows" target="_blank" title="Sewn Pillows">
          DIY pillow designs
         </a>
         on
         <a href="http://www.allfreesewing.com" target="_blank" title="AllFreeSewing">
          AllFreeSewing
         </a>
         just yet; theyre newly discovered and were absolutely in love with them. Best of all, all eight of these handmade
         <a href="http://www.allfreesewing.com/Sewn-Pillows" target="_blank" title="Sewn Pillows">
          pillow tutorials
         </a>
         are totally on trend with the latest fad of prints and patterns. Super stylish patterned pillows allow you to keep the major pieces in your house, like couches and chairs, timeless and neutral. Instead of picking a print for your love seat that youll hate ten years from now, stick with a solid color and infuse life into your home with
         <a href="http://www.allfreesewing.com/Sewn-Pillows" target="_blank" title="Sewn Pillows">
          DIY pillows
         </a>
         . Small but strong accents like those created by these pillows prints can keep a rooms dcor from feeling overwhelmed and busy while still appearing fresh.
        </p>
