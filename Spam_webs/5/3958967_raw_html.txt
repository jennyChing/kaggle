       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://itsaklife.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/recipe.jpg">
          <img alt="recipe" class="size-medium wp-image-156 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://itsaklife.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/recipe-275x300.jpg" width="275"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hey yall!
        </p>
        <p>
         Today, where I live, a big cold front is heading in. My favorite hot drink for freezing days, like the ones were fixing to have, is spiced tea. My Granny D. (my moms mom) and Mom always made this during the fall and winter seasons.
        </p>
        <p>
         To make spiced tea, you will
         <strong>
          need
         </strong>
         :
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 cups Tang
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2- 1 cup Sugar (depending on how sweet youd like it, I use 1/2 cup)
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 cup Powdered Unsweetened Tea (caffeinated or decaffeinated)
         </li>
         <li>
          1 teaspoon Cinnamon
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 teaspoon of Cloves (optional)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://itsaklife.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/spiced-tea.jpg">
          <img alt="spiced tea" class="size-medium wp-image-157 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://itsaklife.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/spiced-tea-277x300.jpg" width="277"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You will want to combine all of the ingredients listed above in a container that will allow for you to store it. After combining the ingredients, be sure to give it a good stir and mix everything together.
        </p>
        <p>
         To finish making spiced tea, I usually heat up a coffee mug filled with water and then add about 6-7 spoonfuls of the mix to the hot water. If youd like it a little stronger or not quite as strong, then you can add or subtract the amount of spoonfuls. Stir it up and your spiced tea is ready!
        </p>
        <p>
         My mom likes to sometimes add a splash of lemon to hers. I havent ever tried it, but she says it makes it taste even better.
        </p>
        <p>
         I hope you guys enjoy some spiced tea this fall and winter! What are some of your favorite hot drinks for cold days?
        </p>
        <p>
         -Kailey
        </p>
