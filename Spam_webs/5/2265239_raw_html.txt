                &lt;header&gt;
                    &lt;h2&gt;&lt;a href="http://yahoo.tumblr.com/post/52805929240/yourname-yahoo-com-can-be-yours"&gt;yourname@yahoo.com Can Be Yours!&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
                &lt;/header&gt;
                &lt;section&gt;
                    &lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;By&nbsp;&lt;span&gt;Jay Rossiter, SVP, Platforms&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="p1"&gt;Over the last few months, we&rsquo;ve made exciting changes to some of your favorite Yahoo! products, like Flickr, Mail, Weather, the Homepage and Search. Today, I&rsquo;m excited to share with you our next big push: we want to give our loyal users and new folks the opportunity to sign up for the Yahoo! ID they&rsquo;ve always wanted.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="p2"&gt;&lt;span&gt;If you&rsquo;re like me, you want a Yahoo! ID that&rsquo;s short, sweet, and memorable like &lt;/span&gt;&lt;a href="mailto:albert@yahoo.com"&gt;&lt;span class="s1"&gt;albert@yahoo.com&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span&gt; instead of &lt;/span&gt;&lt;a href="mailto:albert9330399@yahoo.com"&gt;&lt;span class="s1"&gt;albert9330399@yahoo.com&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span&gt;. A Yahoo! ID is not only your email address, it also gives you access to content tailored to your interests &ndash; like sports scores for your favorite teams, weather in your hometown, and news that matters to you.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="p2"&gt;&lt;span&gt;So, how are we making these Yahoo! IDs available? We&rsquo;re freeing up IDs, that have been inactive for at least 12 months, by resetting them and giving them a fresh start.&nbsp;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span&gt;In mid July, anyone can have a shot at scoring the Yahoo! ID they want. In mid August, users who staked a claim on certain IDs can come to Yahoo! to discover which one they got.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="p2"&gt;&lt;span&gt;What if you haven&rsquo;t logged into Yahoo! for over a year, but want to keep your Yahoo! ID? It&rsquo;s easy. All you have to do is log on to any Yahoo! product before July 15th.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="p2"&gt;&lt;span&gt;We&rsquo;ve got a lot more in store that will make your everyday routines even more inspiring. We&rsquo;re happy to have you as a part of the Yahoo! community!&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
