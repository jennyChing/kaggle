          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop It.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Thats it .
          </p>
          <p>
           Thats all its ever been.
          </p>
          <p>
           Huge industries and genreshave risen up to tell you this very thing.
          </p>
          <p>
           The simplicity of the statement is unfathomable to many.
          </p>
          <p>
           The true depth of its meaning too grand to comprehend.
          </p>
          <p>
           Yet no education is needed.
          </p>
          <p>
           No lifetimes of understanding required.
          </p>
          <p>
           Takethese simple wordsat face value and apply as needed.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Over-eating and your weight out of control, creating health issues and clothes no longer fit?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop over-eating and love yourself enough to do whatyou know you needto do. Dont know what to do? Go ask someone getting the results you want.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Are your relationships bad and you find yourself in constant strife with others and life is basically miserable?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop being the drama queen/king and choose the path of peace instead. Stop looking for attention. No one has time for that, most of all yourself. Instead, work on your health, goals, hobby orany other area of your lifethat need attentionratherthan causing problems for yourself and others.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Spending out of control?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop over spending and start saving.See how much you can keep instead of how much you can spend between each pay.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Always thinking negatively and drawing bad situations to yourself?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop torturing yourself and others with your negative, self-deprecating talk. No one wants to hear it. You cant afford to hear it either.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Getting up each morning in a bad mood, hating to go to work and because of this you make everyones life miserable because youre miserable?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop the bad attitude. You have a job. If you dont like the job, find a new one. Cant find a new one? Learn to like/love the one you have. Start creating a side income/business until things change. Read online resources such as Pam Slims blog
           <a href="http://www.escapefromcubiclenation.com/pamela-slims-blog/">
            Escape From Cubicle Nation
           </a>
           to help you tostart thinking about what else is possible. Juststop torturing everyone around you because of your choices. Just stop it.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Walking around confused about life? Have no idea what you want to do or be or create?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop the confusion thats rolling around in your head. Cant do it on your own? Get help. Find assistance. Hire a coach. Janet Bray Attwoods,
           <a href="http://www.thepassiontest.com/about-the-passion-test/janet/">
            The Passion Test
           </a>
           is a great place to start.Just stop using the excuse of confusion as why your life isnt working ormoving ahead. The next time youre asked how youre doing in this regard tell them youre well on your way to finding your purpose and mean it!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Always walking around telling the same story of failure to anyone that will listen?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           No one wants to hear it. No one has that kind of time. Nor do they have the energy. If they are listening, theyre just being polite and counting the minutes until they can escape.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           People always taking advantage of you? Wasting your time, energy and compassion? Are you known as the sucker amongst friends, family and peers?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stop it.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Grow up and grow a backbone while youre at it. You have a job to do. The job of finding your purpose and passion. Get to it and stop being one of those people.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           See where Im going with this?
          </p>
          <p>
           All you have to do is stop what isnt working.
          </p>
          <p>
           Put a stop to it once and for all.
          </p>
          <p>
           Need help stopping whatever is not working? Go findit. Help exists everywhere, you simply have to ask.
          </p>
          <p>
           However, its so simple most wont do it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Why?
          </p>
          <p>
           Because were trained to complain, find fault, and look for excuses as to why we cant
           <a href="http://www.amazon.ca/gp/product/1936719010/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=15121&amp;creative=330641&amp;creativeASIN=1936719010&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=persdevedai0c-20">
            do the work
           </a>
           . Wesee the negative in everything and everyone and believe everything has to be complicated in order for it tocontain value.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dont be one of those people.
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop being powerless.
          </p>
          <p>
           Stop it.
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/downtownandaround.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/stop-worrying.png">
            <img alt="stop worrying" class="aligncenter wp-image-1462" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/downtownandaround.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/stop-worrying.png?resize=350%2C317"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Pamela Sylvan  Mojo Maker
          </p>
