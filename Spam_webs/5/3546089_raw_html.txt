		&lt;h2&gt;&lt;img class="alignright size-full wp-image-286688" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/010915-paris-attacks_map_11-e1425927997926.png" alt="010915-paris-attacks_map_1" width="400" height="228" /&gt;&lt;span style="color: #434343; background-color: #ffe8a5;"&gt;Four people including a French MUSLIM female police officer have been taken into custody in connection with the January Islamic terror attacks in Paris.Those arrested are believed to be associates of Amedy Coulibaly, who stormed a kosher supermarket and killed four Jews in Porte de Vincennes as well as another female police officer and a jogger.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-286687" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/4406646.jpg" alt="4406646" width="645" height="320" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;h4&gt;&lt;span style="color: #073763;"&gt;The man is believed to have met with Coulibaly in the days leading up to his attack on the Jewish kosher store in Porte de Vincennes, in which he killed four people.According to AFP two others who had links to Coulibaly were also being questioned by officers in Paris.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h4&gt;
