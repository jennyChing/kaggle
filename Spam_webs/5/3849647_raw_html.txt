                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     Lacquer Legion On Holiday theme for June
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2615415887044334992">
                     <div style="text-align: right;">
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        *Nothing to disclose
                       </i>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: right;">

                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      Hey there Polish Peeps!!! Happy Monday!! I hope everyone had a great weekend! We had my youngest's First Birthday Party yesterday and after cleaning up the smash cake mess, I was exhausted!!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      Today I have a mani that I'm so excited about!! Today is the Lacquer Legion and the theme for this month was "On Holiday". Instead of posting something vacation themed, I figured I would go for the literal sense and get out some July 4th nail art and go with the upcoming holiday!!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      This is what I used:
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kp6euTYx8Zw/U7GpGtm_gNI/AAAAAAAAFB8/tBJxMuptRFc/s1600/Lacquer-Legion-July-4th-nail-art.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="386" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kp6euTYx8Zw/U7GpGtm_gNI/AAAAAAAAFB8/tBJxMuptRFc/s1600/Lacquer-Legion-July-4th-nail-art.jpg" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://frompolishwithlove.blogspot.com/2014/06/barielle-keys-collection-for-summer.html">
                        Barielle Miami Heat
                       </a>
                       , Mentality stamping polish in white,
                       <a href="http://frompolishwithlove.blogspot.com/2014/05/kbshimmer-early-summer-2014-collection.html">
                        KBShimmer Low &amp; Be Bold
                       </a>
                       , damp makeup wedge sponge, Different Dimension Indepen-dance, Pueen 56 plate, Zoya Purity
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       I started off with 2 thin coats of Purity and using a damp makeup wedge sponge, I applied Miami Heat and Low and Be Bold. I sponged that on top of the white while moving the sponge up and down to get a good blend. I did this about 3 times to get the level of opacity that I wanted!! After each nail, I did clean up so it wouldn't be hard to remove as if I would have waited til the end, I then applied Sally Hansen InstaDri and waited around an hour!
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YgQXxpnI1Dc/U7GrMvuFcnI/AAAAAAAAFCI/md8Dp83RhgU/s1600/Red-white-blue-gradient-july-4th-nail-art.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Gradient-KBShimmer-Low-&amp;-Be-Bold-Barielle-Miami-Heat-nail-art" border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YgQXxpnI1Dc/U7GrMvuFcnI/AAAAAAAAFCI/md8Dp83RhgU/s1600/Red-white-blue-gradient-july-4th-nail-art.jpg" title="Gradient-KBShimmer-Low-&amp;-Be-Bold-Barielle-Miami-Heat-nail-art" width="544"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       I then took Indepen-Dance! from Different Dimension and applied it over my index and pinky! I just love this patriotic glitter!! This is limited edition for this year and be sure to look for more swatches of this tomorrow!
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-VDDomq50OYU/U7Gtbm6ls7I/AAAAAAAAFCU/VN6g9w6tSfs/s1600/Different-Dimension-Indepen-Dance-swatch.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img alt="Gradient-KBShimmer-Low-&amp;-Be-Bold-Barielle-Miami-Heat-nail-art-Different-Dimension-Indepen-Dance-swatch" border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-VDDomq50OYU/U7Gtbm6ls7I/AAAAAAAAFCU/VN6g9w6tSfs/s1600/Different-Dimension-Indepen-Dance-swatch.jpg" title="Gradient-KBShimmer-Low-&amp;-Be-Bold-Barielle-Miami-Heat-nail-art-Different-Dimension-Indepen-Dance-swatch" width="588"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       Different Dimension Indepen-Dance!
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">

                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-CT6dvIAsi-E/U7GuIbuGdkI/AAAAAAAAFCc/ZJR_9VYrfpw/s1600/Mentality-white-stamping-Pueen-56-july-4th.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
