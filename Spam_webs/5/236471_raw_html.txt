        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Internet addiction continues to rise in Japan
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <img align="right" alt="hikikomori geek" border="0" class="alignright size-full wp-image-69451" height="124" src="http://news.3yen.com/wp-content/images/hikikomori_100x124.jpg" style="margin: 10px 15px 10px 15px" title="hikikomori neet" width="100"/>
         <br/>
         Japanese experts are blaming the the increasing quality of digital content and the plague of smartphone/
         <a href="http://news.3yen.com/2014-07-06/keitai-zombies-of-tokyo/">
          <em>
           keitai
          </em>
          zombies
          <small>
           <em>
            (3Yen 2014-07-06)
           </em>
          </small>
         </a>
         for the continuing increase of Net addicts, who often prefer the Internet over other essential activities such as sleeping (or sex).
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Internet addiction rising in Japan
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://www3.nhk.or.jp/nhkworld/english/news/20141015_17.html">
           NHK WORLD Oct. 15, 2014
          </a>
          <br/>
          A panel of experts in Japan says more than 4.2 million people in the country are addicted to the internetaddiction is up 50 percent from the last survey 6 years ago.
          <br/>
          Addiction is suspected when people neglect their work or household chores as a result of spending too much time online.
          <br/>
          <a href="http://www3.nhk.or.jp/nhkworld/english/news/20141015_17.html">
           <em>
            More
           </em>
          </a>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         <a href="http://youtu.be/voGp3ONUm1c?t=15m16s">
          <img alt="otaku-life-8AM" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-69444" height="275" src="http://news.3yen.com/wp-content/images/otaku-life-8AM.jpg" title="otaku-life-8AM" width="350"/>
          ..
          <br/>
          <small>
           <em>
            Click to play. (in Japanese)
           </em>
          </small>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Solution?
         </strong>
         <br/>
         The Japanese government needs a crash program to provide all Internet junkies with the iFeel_IM  the Virtual Hugging Vest has been created at Toyohashi University of Technology.
         <br/>
         As shown below, the iFeel_IM can simulate the feel of a hug or shrug using the tight vest and electronic sensors and vibrotactile actuators.  The iFeel_IMs website describes that the vest can provide
         <em>
          hikomori
         </em>
         /socially-isolated geek with: The quickened thump of an angry heart beat, a spine-tingling chill of fear, or that warm-all-over sensation sparked by true love  all can be felt even as your eyes stay glued to a computer screen.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://news.3yen.com/2010-04-10/sad-ifeel_im/">
          <img alt="iFeel_IM Affective Haptics in Emotional Communication" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15145" height="320" src="http://news.3yen.com/wp-content/images/iFeel_IM-Affective-Haptics.jpg" title="iFeel_IM Affective Haptics in Emotional Communication" width="299"/>
          ..
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For more about this perfect solution, refer to the previous report on the
         <a href="http://news.3yen.com/2010-04-10/sad-ifeel_im/">
          3Yen
          <small>
           <em>
            (2010-04-10)
           </em>
          </small>
          :
          <span style="font-size:1.2em">
           <strong>
            sad iFeel_IM
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
