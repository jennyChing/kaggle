                 <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" style="">
                  <p>
                   Music has the power to touch people in many ways  it acts as a release for those who create it and can deliver endless inspiration and motivation to those listening. Studies show that exposure to music at an early age helps encourage creativity and that it can also aid in the development of essential verbal and problem solving skills. Music also has the power to connect people through common experience and help us get through difficult situations. When two art forms as influential like tattoos and music mix only magic can result from it.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <img alt="Misfits_Oksana" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1963387180-jpg/Misfits_Oksana.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Killer Misfits tattoo by
                    <a href="http://www.tattooschool-art.com/tattoo-school-testimonials-reviews">
                     A.R.T. grad Oksana Weber
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <!--more-->
                   With todays technology you can easily carry thousands of your favorite songs in your purse or pocket so you can have instant access to the perfect song that inspires, motivates or comforts you. With todays advancements in tattooing you can carry a piece of your favorite song, band or singer with you at all times on your body for that extra kick of motivation.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   For most people it is something in the story of a song or even a single line that makes the most powerful impact. This is why a single lyric, direct quote or even the title of a song can make an excellent subject for your new tattoo.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <img alt="10606403_709246315828684_7933532425270375120_n" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1963387250-jpg/10606403_709246315828684_7933532425270375120_n.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Demi Lovata Dark Clouds Lyrics Tattooed by
                    <a href="http://www.tattooschool-art.com/tattoo-apprenticeship-blog">
                     A.R.T. Grad Pete Doty
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    <img alt="I_am_my_own_creation_Otep" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1959039588-jpg/I_am_my_own_creation_Otep.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Super Clean Script of Otep Lyrics from the Song Self Made by an artist at our
                    <a href="http://www.tattooschool-art.com/tattoo-apprenticeship-tattoo-school-programs">
                     Philadelphia location
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    <img alt="Sunshine_temptations_oksana" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1963422340-jpg/Sunshine_temptations_oksana.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Classic Temptation's Lyrics tattooed by
                    <a href="http://www.tattooschool-art.com/tattoo-school-testimonials-reviews">
                     shop manager Oksana Weber
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    <img alt="Shatter_Me_Lindsey_Stirling" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1963422370-jpg/Shatter_Me_Lindsey_Stirling.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Lyrics from Lindsey Stirling's Shatter Me by an artist in
                    <a href="http://www.tattooschool-art.com/tattoo-apprenticeship-tattoo-school-programs">
                     our New Jersey shop
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Those who write and play music often gain inspiration, relieve stress or express themselves through the creative process. This process opens up even more possibilities for cool, crazy, unique and meaningful tattoos.
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <img alt="Microphone_delvirus" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1962641222-jpg/Microphone_delvirus.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Awesome microphone cover-up by
                    <a href="http://www.tattooschool-art.com/tattoo-apprenticeship-blog">
                     A.R.T. grad Delvirus
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    <img alt="Note_BAS" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1963447475-jpg/Note_BAS.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Small but powerful music note from
                    <a href="http://newjersey.bodyartsoul.com/">
                     Body Art and Soul
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Music tattoos dont have to be small or subtle either. With the right artist you can go as and as
                   <a href="/blog/8-impressive-tattoos-show-why-its-good-to-think-big-with-your-ink">
                    big and bold
                   </a>
                   as you want in order to show your love for a particular band or artist.
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <img alt="Axl_Miguel" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/138307/file-1962666322-jpg/Axl_Miguel.jpg?t=1436797442745"/>
                  </p>
                  <p style="text-align: center;">
                   <strong>
                    Outstanding Axl Rose portrait by
                    <a href="http://newjersey.bodyartsoul.com/">
                     Miguel Prada
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Music and tattoos are two amazing methods of expression. If you have a song that particularly motivates or moves you, hit up your local shop to talk to an artist about turning that magical verse or album cover into an incredible new tattoo!
                  </p>
                 </span>
