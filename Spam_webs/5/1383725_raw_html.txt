        <h1>
         Complete Guide to Alexa Ranking
        </h1>
        <p>
         Here are the topics that covers under the guide to Alexa Ranking. Follow these topics and make your blog stands in good rankings.

        <h2>
         1. What is Alexa:
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Alexais California based company
         </li>
         <li>
          Alexa was acquired by Amazone
         </li>
         <li>
          Alexa Defines themselves a Web information company
         </li>
         <li>
          Alexa Rank will be updated every day
         </li>
         <li>
          Lesser the number rank you get better the ranking
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         2. How does Alexa Works
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Alexa gives the rank to blog/website depending upon the traffic
         </li>
         <li>
          They rank every website depending upon several factors
         </li>
         <li>
          They always count the traffic from the no. of system which are having Alexa Toolbar in their browsers
         </li>
         <li>
          Some times, even though you got high traffic your alexa rank will not be improved
         </li>
         <li>
          It need the traffic from the users who are having alexa rank.
         </li>
         <li>
          Alexa Rank showglobal rank and also rank in your country
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         3. Alexa Tool Bar
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Install the Alexa Tool bar on your browsers to get the updates of your web rank
         </li>
         <li>
          You can install the Alexa Tool bar from the official website of Alexa
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         4. Importance of Alexa Rank
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          If you want to get direct advertisers then Alexa Rank matters
         </li>
         <li>
          It is very useful for Affiliate Marketing
         </li>
         <li>
          If your blog is having good alexa rank then advertisers will directly contact you.
         </li>
         <li>
          It is only the factor why we need to improve the Alexa rank.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         5. Tips &amp; Tricks to improve Alexa Rank
        </h2>
        <p>
         Here is the complete guide to
         <a href="http://techfresherspoint.com/increase-boost-alexa-rank/" target="_blank" title="How To Improve Alexa Rank">
          improve your Alexa Rank
         </a>
         . Factors need to consider to improve Alexa rank to your blog is given below
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Install Alexa Tool bar on your browser
         </li>
         <li>
          Get Traffic from Bloggers
         </li>
         <li>
          Write articles related to Blogging, Internet Marketing
         </li>
         <li>
          Write Quality and Quantity Posts
         </li>
         <li>
          Share your posts on Blogger Groups, Blogger Communities
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h3>
         Conclusion:
        </h3>
        <p>
         If you are having daily traffic from Bloggers, then automatically your Alexa ranking will be boosted. So, try to write articles related to Blogging, SEO, Internet Marketing.
