          <h1>
           Virgo  People born between August 22 and September 22
          </h1>
          <p>
           <b>
            Virgo
           </b>
           is the sixth astrological sign in the Zodiac, originating from the constellation of Virgo. In western astrology, the sign is no longer aligned with the constellation as a result of the precession of the equinoxes. In astrology, Virgo is considered a feminine, negative (introvert) sign. It is also considered an earth sign and is one of four mutable signs. Virgo is traditionally ruled by the planet Mercury. Being the sixth sign of the zodiac, Virgo has been associated with the astrological sixth house.
          </p>
          <div class="ad-float-rigth">
           <script type="text/javascript">
            &lt;!--

          <p>
           Individuals born when the Sun was in this sign are considered Virgo individuals. Under the tropical zodiac, the Sun is in Virgo roughly from August 24 to September 22. The Sanskrit name of Virgo in Hindu astrology is Kanya.
          </p>
          <p>
           Based on Elements within the Zodiac, Virgo is considered compatible with Taurus, Capricorn and Virgo itself.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Virgo ( August 22  September 222)
          </h2>
          <p>
           No one on earth could possibly be such a pure and perfect goodies as you pretend to be an yet, you have the nerve to criticize other peoples faults and failings. You are born to serve others, you are quite, reserved, methodical and practical. You can be relied upon safely. With power of discrimination coupled with clear insight, cool and intellectual abilities, you have power of insight. You can not be carried away by emotions.
          </p>
          <p>
           A combination of tact and diplomacy will help you to have success every where. You are modest and conservative. You prefer intelligence than romance and pleasure. You are shy and not demonstrative in love of details which is your another trait. Your negative sides are fault finding and nagging which be kept under control. You are modest, retiring and not inclined to come to lime light when you are in close contact with many people.
          </p>
          <p>
           You have good endurance, not easily contented, changeable nature and environment, often change their residence. Methodical, but have wander lust in your nature. Will have a pleasant and peaceful domestic life. In love affairs, you will hardly succeed due to your fault finding nature and rarely passionate. You are sensitive, be not so economical that your domestic life becomes hell.
          </p>
          <h2>
           FEMALE
          </h2>
          <p>
           You are intelligent, wise, accommodating and of adjusting nature. Homely, keep peace at home and are God fearing. Will run the home in a regulated way. Sex is a mean idea for you which not upset your married life, so you are advised to mould yourself. You will have limited number of children and will provide them all facilities of life at any cost. ADVICES You should not be sensitive, critics, conversationalist and chatty. Avoid too much calculating nature. You must have self confidence and avoid nature of changeability. You should be a good judge and do not seek others advice to confuse yourself but stick to single mans advice. Revengeful and long sustained resentment be avoided.
          </p>

          <h2>
           ADVICES
          </h2>
          <p>
           The downside to being a Virgo is they can worry about small matters or fine points, often getting bogged down in detail they can miss the big picture. Virgos are generally very talkative and can reveal too much of themselves or bore people with too much detail. They pride themselves on giving advice which can make them unpopular if they start criticizing their friends. Of course, they dont do it to be critical or nasty, they genuinely feel they are helping. You can be sure that Virgo has analysed you thoroughly and really just wants to share what he or she knows.
          </p>
          <h2>
           THINGS TO WATCH OUT FOR
          </h2>
          <p>
           Lucky days are Wednesday, Friday, Monday and Thursday. Sunday, Tuesday and Saturday are not favourable. Lucky colours are pale green, lime, olive, mixtures of yellow and green, white and emerald whereas red, blue and black be avoided. Lucky numbers are 2, 3, 5, 6 &amp; 7, passive are 4 and 9 whereas disagree numbers are 1 and 8.
          </p>
          <h2>
           LUCKY DAYS, NUMBERS AND COLOURS
          </h2>
          <p>
           Bright colours as yellow, gold and sunny, orange, red and green are lucky colours. Avoid blue and black colours. Numbers 1, 4, 5, 6, and 9 are lucky. Avoid 2, 7, and 8 whereas 3 is passive. Sundays, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday are favourable whereas Monday, Saturdays be avoided for important works. Keep fast on Monday.
          </p>
