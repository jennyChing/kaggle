        <h1>
         Hunger threatens millions in Sahel region of West Africa
        </h1>
        <h5>
         The situation
        </h5>
        <div class="captionright">
         <img alt="Plan is on the ground working to help children and families in the Sahel cope during the crisis." src="http://plancanada.ca/view.image?Id=6393"/>
         <p>
          Drought threatens millions of people in West Africas Sahel region.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         More than 18 million people across the Sahel region of West Africa are threatened with a humanitarian crisis that has risen to epic proportions. What started as a drought and food shortage has deteriorated into a deadly storm of hunger, disease and despair.
        </p>
        <p>
         The combination of erratic rains, poor harvests, recurring drought and rising food costs has caused food shortages in countries across the region, like
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/Burkina-Faso">
          Burkina Faso
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/Mali">
          Mali
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/Niger">
          Niger
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/Cameroon">
          Cameroon
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/Senegal">
          Senegal
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         This food shortage is further worsened by conflict, cholera and locust infestation  with more than four million children sitting right in the bullseye, Plans Roland Berehoudougou says from West Africa.
        </p>
        <p>
         We know that the earlier we act, the more lives we can save. And now, with your help, we can save even more lives.
        </p>
        <h5 class="morelink">
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/Sahel-crisis">
          Please make a donation today to help us save lives.
         </a>
        </h5>
        <h5>
         The solution
        </h5>
        <p>
         Plan has been working with our partners to develop a coordinated response to the food crisis in the region.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now we need to intensify the response before this crisis becomes catastrophic, and so that we can save the lives of families and children who are hungry for food and our attention, said
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/page.aspx?pid=1932">
          Rosemary McCarney
         </a>
         , President and CEO of Plan Canada.
        </p>
        <p>
         Your support will help us reach more families and continue to:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Feed children
          </strong>
          through Plan's food distribution network, school meal initiatives and food voucher programs.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Save children
          </strong>
          now suffering the effects of malnutrition with health care and specialized nourishment.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Protect children
          </strong>
          during migrations that leave them vulnerable to hunger, disease and traffickers.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h5 class="morelink">
         <a href="https://plancanada.ca/SahelCrisis">
          Donate now!
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; outline: 0px; font-size: 12px; vertical-align: baseline; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; color: #675c53; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 18px; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;">
         Plan is proud to participate in the
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         <a href="http://humanitariancoalition.ca/" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; outline: none; font-size: 12px; vertical-align: baseline; background-color: transparent; color: #0073cf; text-decoration: none; background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;">
          HUMANITARIAN COALITION
         </a>
         , comprised of Plan Canada, CARE Canada, Oxfam Canada, Oxfam-Qubec, Save the Children Canada, five of Canadas largest relief agencies. By joining our efforts, the HUMANITARIAN COALITION will increase the impact of Canadian humanitarian responses and reduce administrative costs.
        </p>
        <p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; outline: 0px; font-size: 12px; vertical-align: baseline; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; color: #675c53; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 18px; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;">
         To find out more, visit the HUMANITARIAN COALITION website at
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.together.ca/" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; outline: none; font-size: 12px; vertical-align: baseline; background-color: transparent; color: #0073cf; text-decoration: none; background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;">
          www.together.ca
         </a>
         .
        </p>
