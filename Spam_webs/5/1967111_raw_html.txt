        <h1 class="article-title" itemprop="headline">
         Five Reasons To Enter The Small Business Challenge
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- end article header -->
       <br class="clearfix"/>
       <div class="post-img">
        <img alt="15_00243-05-Lede-800x600" class="attachment-single-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="600" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Lede-800x600.jpg" width="800"/>
       </div>
       <section class="article-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         The Small Business Challenge allows Canadas brightest entrepreneurs to showcase their unique business ideas and share their biggest challenges. Now in its fifth year, the contest attracts more than 1,000 entries annually. In celebration of this milestone, weve worked with The Globe and Mail to make our fifth year bigger and better.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are five great reasons to enter this years Small Business Challenge.
         <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-1.jpg">
          <br/>
          <img alt="15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-1" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-5225" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-1.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="15_00243-05-Infographic1-715x250" class=" wp-image-5219 alignright" height="90" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Infographic1-715x250-300x105.png" width="257"/>
         Most new small businesses are self-funded or financed by family and friends. But, sometimes these businesses need additional funding to overcome hurdles and get off the ground. Thats where The Small Business Challenge can help. Imagine how your small business could grow with $100,000.
        </p>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-2.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-2" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-5226" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-2.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         <img alt="15_00243-05-Infographic2-715x250" class=" wp-image-5220 size-medium alignright" height="105" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Infographic2-715x250-300x105.png" width="300"/>
         Its not just our finalist who wins big! To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the contest, weve added more than $50,000 in awards to help even more of Canadas smartest entrepreneurs drive their big ideas.
        </p>
        <p>
         This year:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Four runners-up will receive $10,000 plus a TELUS technology pack valued at $2,000
         </li>
         <li>
          10 regional winners will receive a TELUS prize package with an estimated value of $1,500
         </li>
         <li>
          Three most promising start-ups will receive $5,000 worth of marketing services and mentorship from
          <a href="https://www.agency59.ca/">
           Agency 59
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          50 honourable mentions will be featured in The Globe and Mail
          <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-3.jpg">
           <img alt="15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-3" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-5227" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-3.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <img alt="15_00243-05-Infographic3-715x250" class=" wp-image-5221 size-medium alignright" height="105" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Infographic3-715x250-300x105.png" width="300"/>
        In order to survive and grow, small business owners need to look for every opportunity to gain exposure and promote their business. This year, throughout the Small Business Challenge, 68 businesses will be showcased in The Globe and Mail and recognized on our social media channels; helping build brand awareness and attract new customers.
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-4.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-4" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-5228" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-4.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <img alt="15_00243-05-Infographic4-715x250" class=" wp-image-5222 size-medium alignright" height="105" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Infographic4-715x250-300x105.png" width="300"/>
        To enter the Small Business Challenge, small business owners need to propose a solution to their biggest obstacle. At the heart of it, entrepreneurs are asked to reflect on the values of their company and its long-term strategy. We live in a fast-paced world and small business owners often dont make the time to step away from running their business to consider the bigger picture. Even if youre not one of the winners, youll gain invaluable insight into your business.
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-5.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-5" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-5229" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Subhead-700x250-5.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         <img alt="15_00243-05-Infographic5-715x250" class="alignleft wp-image-5223 size-medium" height="105" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/15_00243-05-Infographic5-715x250-300x105.png" width="300"/>
         We believe its imperative for businesses to give back to their communities, so weve added something new to the grand prize this year. In an effort to encourage all organizations to be philanthropic and promote our philosophy of giving where we live, were providing the grand prize winner an additional $10,000 to donate to a local charity of their choice.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over the last five years, The Small Business Challenge has helped innovative Canadian companies overcome their challenges and reach extraordinary heights. Were accepting
         <a href="http://business.telus.com/en/campaigns/2015-small-business-challenge?CMP=EDM_sEMA_cTheChallenge_cdTheChallenge5Reasons_ad">
          submissions
         </a>
         until June 1.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sound like a contest for you or someone you know?
         <a href="http://business.telus.com/en/campaigns/2015-small-business-challenge?CMP=EDM_sEMA_cTheChallenge_cdTheChallenge5Reasons_ad">
          Enter here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/06FR.png">
         <img alt="06FR" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-5024" height="83" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/06FR.png" width="714"/>
        </a>
       </section>
