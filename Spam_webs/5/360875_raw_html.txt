         </h1>

         <p>
          I am sick to death of clients coming to me with websites built by web designers who do not know the basics of SEO.
         </p>
         <p>
          Some clients spend thousands of pounds and end up with a site that needs to be re coded, simply because title tags have not been included, or a H1 tag or an effective internal link structure.
         </p>
         <p>
          Yet go onto the unmentioned Cornwall Web Designer website and Effective SEO services are offered.
          <span id="more-401">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Do not offer what you cannot deliver.
         </p>
         <p>
          It is so simple to learn the basics, go to my list of
          <a href="http://www.cornwallseo.com/search/index.php/2007/02/17/enormous-list-of-the-best-how-to-do-seo-links/">
           how to learn basic seo
          </a>
          and read up. It doesnt even cost money.
         </p>
         <p>
          But if you cant do it, dont say you can. Get a professional in. Doesnt have to be me, one of the best SEO companies in the UK live in Cornwall, go see what they say, and then come to me, 
         </p>
         <p>
          There are a few SEO friendly web designers in Cornwall though,
          <a href="http://www.richardquickdesign.com/">
           Richard Quick Designs
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.webfooteddesigns.co.uk/">
           Web Footed Designs
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.fourdegreeswest.co.uk/internet/webdesign.htm">
           Four Degrees West
          </a>
          , to name a few.
         </p>
         <p>
          I am sure there are others, but this is a blog about Cornish search engine marketing, not Cornish Web Design. Actually if you are a Cornish Web Designer get in touch and lets chat about giving your clients a superb SEO service.
         </p>
         <p>
