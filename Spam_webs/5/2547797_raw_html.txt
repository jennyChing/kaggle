                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Slice the Cake
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7172412818955566664" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-OX6iOBJRGag/U7DpT59gAHI/AAAAAAAAA2k/epb9VROTIRU/s1600/PIANO+CAKE_checkerboard.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-OX6iOBJRGag/U7DpT59gAHI/AAAAAAAAA2k/epb9VROTIRU/s1600/PIANO+CAKE_checkerboard.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Back in college, my ultimate dream job was to be a cake slicer. You know, someone who slices cakes at a pastry or coffee shop. NO KIDDING. you could ask some of my friends about it. :-)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Ten years later, never in my wildest dreams that I would be slicing cakes (to think that I had
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <u>
                         <b>
                          zero
                         </b>
                        </u>
                       </span>
                       skills in cooking and baking). And to add to that, I am making the cakes myself! :-) I never kept my hopes high in chasing my dreams. After all, I was only in college taking up Information Technology (IT) and I was just
                       <i>
                        only
                       </i>
                       dreaming for that job. How would an IT student like me be slicing cakes as a profession? That would be silly. Except, that is, slicing a cake on a birthday is fairly reasonable. :-)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Let me reiterate...
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <u>
                        Never in my wildest dreams that I would be making cakes myself.
                       </u>
                       From slicing cakes to baking cakes. How cool is that?
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       A dream that was once just floating in the air like bubbles. A dream that I thought was dead and I had buried it deep down. A dream I thought that would stay unreachable has now become a reality. A dream, I must say, I am living it now wide awake!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Isn't that amazing? Isn't God amazing? He materializes every dream He has for me in His own time at His own pace. Indeed, when God is your partner, better make your
                       <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                        <span style="font-size: small;">
                         dreams
                        </span>
                        <b>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                       <b>
                        <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                         big
                        </span>
                       </b>
                       . For in Him there are no limits! What seems unreachable is reachable to Him. What seems impossible to the human eye is possible to Him!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       I may not be there yet, but from where I am standing now, I can see that
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         opportunities are endless with God
                        </b>
                       </u>
                       ! :-)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Delight in the Lord, indeed, and He will give you the desires of your heart. Desires in which what God desires, you also desire. God also honors those dreams you have for yourself because after all, He is the One who gave them to you.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       God desires to fulfill His plans for your life and the dreams that you have. So do not limit God of what He can do by limiting your own knowledge of Him.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       I, of course, cannot say that I have a vast knowledge of Him, yet. There is more to know about Him. And He desires that I will come to spend time with Him more for me to know Him better.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       And what is the relation of me knowing Him with my cake slicing?
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         He delights.
                        </b>
                       </u>
                       He delights to see me reach my full potential in the same way I am delighting in what I do, that is, all for His glory.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-U8EE1XeXoMY/U7DqmBRWn4I/AAAAAAAAA2w/IVjzarnLdaA/s1600/sheslley+bakes.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-U8EE1XeXoMY/U7DqmBRWn4I/AAAAAAAAA2w/IVjzarnLdaA/s1600/sheslley+bakes.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Others might say, I have an average dream. Well for them, maybe yes. But to me, it is something. It is
                       <b>
                        passion
                       </b>
                       . Waking up everyday and baking some cakes is something more than work or hobby. It's
                       <b>
                        <u>
                         passion
                        </u>
                        .
                       </b>
                       I do my thing in the kitchen with all effort and time. And every time I am done with my tasks I feel I have already achieved some great things. I get that feeling of satisfaction whenever I work in the kitchen that no amount of my previous jobs have given me. It's more than the profit. It's more than the business. It's more than awards and rewards.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         PASSION
                        </b>
                       </span>
                       .
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Passion for what I do.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Passion to wake up everyday checking mails and messages from people who have taken interests in my products.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Passion to bake cakes, design them, package them, and give them to customers.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       It gives me the kind of joy that no amount of office work has given me.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       Seriously...
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       It is always a joy to me knowing that my customers appreciate what they eat and that they have enjoyed them.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       This dream. This skill. This passion. This work of art. Everything has been dabbed with God's grace. And because one day at a time I am realizing my once dead dream, I give thanks, truly and deeply, to my One and only
                       <b>
                        <u>
                         Business Partner, GOD.
                        </u>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                       All glory and honor belongs to Him!
                      </span>
                     </div>
