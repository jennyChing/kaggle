         <h1 class="title">
          Healthcare Analytics Adoption Model
         </h1>
        </div>
        <section id="breadcrumbs">
         <div class="breadcrumbs">
          <a href="https://www.healthcatalyst.com">

           Healthcare Analytics Adoption Model
          </strong>
         </div>
        </section>
        <div class="healthcare_analytics_adoption_model" id="system">
         <article class="item">
          <div class="content clearfix">
           <p>
            <a class="button-primary" href="/white-paper/healthcare-analytics-adoption-model">
             Read a More In-Depth Explanation Here
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Why Healthcare Analytics?
           </h2>
           <p>
            Healthcare in the United States and other parts of the world has slowly been progressing through three waves of data management: data collection, data sharing, and data analytics. So far, the data collection and sharing waves, characterized by the urgent deployment of electronic health records and health information exchanges, have failed to significantly impact the quality and cost of healthcare. And despite the current hype about
            <a href="/big-data-in-healthcare-made-simple">
             big data
            </a>
            being the next big thing in other industries, the reality in healthcare is that we are just beginning to have the necessary analytics capabilities that enable system-wide quality improvement and cost reduction efforts. The real promise of analytics lies in its ability to transform healthcare into a truly data-driven culture.
           </p>
           <p>
            Healthcare Analytics can be confusing, even overwhelming without a systematic framework for guidingyour approach and priorities.The following framework, called the
            <a href="/white-paper/healthcare-analytics-adoption-model">
             Healthcare Analytics Adoption Model
            </a>
            , was developed by a cross-industry group of healthcare industry veterans as a guide to classifying groups of analytics capabilities, and provide a systematic sequencing to adopting analytics within health system organizations.A successful and sustainable analytics strategy requires building foundational elements of the model first in order to support the upper levels of the model in later years .
            <a data-lightbox="" href="#contributors" title="Analytics Adoption Model Contributors">
             View Contributors List
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Purpose
           </h2>
           <p>
            The model borrows lessons learned from the HIMSS EMR Adoption Model, and describes an analogous approach for assessing the adoption of analytics in healthcare. The Healthcare Analytics Adoption Model provides:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             A framework for evaluating the industrys adoption of analytics
            </li>
            <li>
             A roadmap for organizations to measure their own progress toward analytic adoption
            </li>
            <li>
             A framework for evaluating vendor products
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            We believe that this Analytics Adoption Model will enable healthcare organizations to fully understand and leverage the capabilities of analytics and so achieve the ultimate goal that has eluded most provider organizations  that of improving the quality of care while lowering costs and enhancing clinician and patient satisfaction.
           </p>
           <h2 style="text-align: center;">
            The Eight Levels of the Analytics Adoption Model
            <br/>
            (click to reveal each level)
           </h2>
           <div class="wk-accordion wk-accordion-aam clearfix" data-options='{"style":"aa-model","collapseall":0,"matchheight":1,"index":500,"duration":500,"width":"auto","order":"default"}' data-widgetkit="accordion" id="accordion-2059-558b4c605c2dc">
            <h3 class="toggler i">
             Level 8 - Personalized Medicine &amp; Prescriptive Analytics
            </h3>
            <div>
             <div class="content wk-content clearfix">
              <h4>
               <em>
                Contracting for &amp; managing health
                <br/>
               </em>
              </h4>
              <p style="language: en-US; line-height: normal; text-indent: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed; mso-vertical-align-alt: auto; mso-line-break-override: none; word-break: normal; punctuation-wrap: hanging; margin: 0pt 0in 0pt 0in;">
               <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: bold; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                Personalized Medicine &amp; Prescriptive Analytics:
               </span>
               <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: bold; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 Analytic motive expands to wellness management, physical and behavioral functional health, and mass customization of care.
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline; mso-text-raise: 0%; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 A
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 nalytics expands to include NLP of text, prescriptive analytics,
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline; mso-text-raise: 0%; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 and
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 interventional decision support.
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 Prescriptive analytics are available at the point of care to improve patient specific
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: #404040; mso-color-index: 1; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline; mso-text-raise: 0%; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: text1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: #404040; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">

                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: white; mso-color-index: 0; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline; mso-text-raise: 0%; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: background1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: white; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  Data governance expands to raise the data literacy of the organization and develop a data acquisition strategy for Levels 4 and above.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
              </ul>
             </div>
            </div>
            <h3 class="toggler c">
             Level 2  Standardized Vocabulary &amp; Patient Registries
            </h3>
            <div>
             <div class="content wk-content clearfix">
              <h4>
               <em>
                Relating and organizing the core data
               </em>
              </h4>
              <p style="language: en-US; line-height: normal; text-indent: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed; mso-vertical-align-alt: auto; mso-line-break-override: none; word-break: normal; punctuation-wrap: hanging; margin: 0pt 0in 0pt 0in;">
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold;">
                 Standardized Vocabulary &amp; Patient Registries:
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                  Master vocabulary and reference
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  data
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                  identified and standardized
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                  across disparate
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  source system content in the data warehouse.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  N
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                  aming,
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  definition, and
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                  data types
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  are consistent with local standards.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  Patient registries
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  are defined solely
                 </span>
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; color: white; mso-color-index: 0; mso-font-kerning: 12.0pt; language: en-US; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline; mso-text-raise: 0%; mso-style-textfill-type: solid; mso-style-textfill-fill-themecolor: background1; mso-style-textfill-fill-color: white; mso-style-textfill-fill-alpha: 100.0%;">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  on ICD billing data.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 Data governance forms around the definition and evolution of patient registries and master data management.
                </span>
               </li>
              </ul>

            </h3>
            <div>
             <div class="content wk-content clearfix">
              <h4>
               <em>
                Foundation of data and technology
               </em>
              </h4>
              <p style="language: en-US; line-height: normal; text-indent: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed; mso-vertical-align-alt: auto; mso-line-break-override: none; word-break: normal; punctuation-wrap: hanging; margin: 0pt 0in 0pt 0in;">
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 Enterprise Data Warehouse
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold;">
                 :
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial;">
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  At a minimum,
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  the following data are co-located in a single data warehouse, locally or hosted: HIMSS
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  EMR Stage 3 data, Revenue Cycle, Financial, Costing, Supply Chain, and Patient Experience.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  Searchable
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  metadata repository is available across the enterprise.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  Data content includes insurance claims, if possible.
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  Data warehouse is updated within one month
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                  of source system changes
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  .
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  Data governance is forming around the data quality of source systems.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  The EDW reports organizationally to the CIO.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
              </ul>

            </h3>
            <div>
             <div class="content wk-content clearfix">
              <h4>
               <em>
                Inefficient, inconsistent versions of the truth
               </em>
              </h4>
              <p style="language: en-US; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed; mso-line-break-override: none; word-break: normal; punctuation-wrap: hanging;">
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold;">
                 Fragmented Point Solutions:
                </span>
                <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal;">
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; font-style: normal;">
                  Vendor-based
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; font-style: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  and internally developed applications
                 </span>
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  are used to address specific analytic needs as they arise.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  The fragmented point solutions are neither co-located in a data warehouse nor otherwise architecturally integrated with one another.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  Overlapping data content leads to multiple versions of analytic truth.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  Reports are labor intensive and inconsistent.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                <span style="color: #000000;">
                 <span style="font-size: 9pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: normal; vertical-align: baseline;">
                  Data governance is non-existent.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
              </ul>
