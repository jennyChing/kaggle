       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Opening Soon: The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train in New Fantasyland
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-05-08T13:03:05+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 8, 2014
         </time>
         By:
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Tonya
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.thetravelingpraters.com/seven-dwarfs-mine-train-in-new-fantasyland/#comments">
           12 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <img alt="The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train in New Fantasyland scheduled to open to the public on May 28th" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8662" height="675" src="http://www.thetravelingpraters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/450x675xThe-Seven-Dwarfs-Mine-Train-in-New-Fantasyland-scheduled-to-open-to-the-public-on-May-28th.jpg.pagespeed.ic.JZCm7bLtj-.jpg" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Last week I attended a press event as a
         <a href="http://www.travelingmom.com/">
          Traveling Mom
         </a>
         writer to get a sneak peek at the new Seven Dwarfs Mine Train in New Fantasyland. This special attraction, the final component and crown jewel of New Fantasyland, pays tribute to one of Disneys most loved classic films,
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00003CXCQ/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00003CXCQ&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=livetheadve-20">
          Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=livetheadve-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00003CXCQ" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         <strong>
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pass for New Seven Dwarfs Mine Train in New Fantasyland" class="aligncenter wp-image-8665" height="450" src="http://www.thetravelingpraters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Pass-for-Seven-Dwarfs-Mine-Train-ride-in-New-Fantasyland.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         As you enter the ride, youll stroll through an interactive covered queue where you can sort gemstones in a digital sluice box, dip your hands in a musical water fountain and twirl buckets of gemstones to create magical animations on the ceiling of the vault.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Interactive Queue in the Seven Dwarfs Mine Train Ride" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8664" height="300" src="http://www.thetravelingpraters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Interactive-Queue-in-the-Seven-Dwarfs-Mine-Train-Ride.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Though the ride is not slated to officially open until May 28th, I was able to board the mine cart and travel the length of the track not once, but two times, once in the first cart and again in the last, each ride offered a little bit different experience.
         <strong>
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         The family-style roller coaster premieres an innovative new ride system that allows the cars to swing back and forth as they travel down the twists, turns and drops of the track. The swinging feature creates a smooth ride free of jerks. If you want to feel the swinging a bit more and want a little bit faster ride, settle in to the back carts.
         <strong>
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train in New Fantasyland scheduled to open to the public on May 28th" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8663" height="400" src="http://www.thetravelingpraters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Seven-Dwarfs-Mine-Train-Opening-May-28th.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This ride provides just the right amount of thrill with appearances along the way from characters from the classic film. Warning- by the end of the ride youll be singing
         <i>
          Heigh-Ho, Heigh-Ho its off to work we go
         </i>
          which may stick inside your head for days to come.
         <strong>
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This much anticipated ride is sure to be a popular feature of New Fantasyland. If youre planning a trip to Walt Disney World, make sure you utilize the new Fastpass+ to save on wait times.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Are you planning a trip to Walt Disney World? Is this a ride that youll take with your family?
         </strong>
        </p>

        </p>
