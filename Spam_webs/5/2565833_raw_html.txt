          <section class="article-content">
           <p>
            Even in Westeros, these looks could kill.
           </p>
           <p>
            Makeup artist
            <a href="https://twitter.com/AllisonChaseMU" target="_blank">
             Allison Chase
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://mashable.com/category/photography/">
             photographer
            </a>
            Matt Barnes collaborated to create customized looks based on nine houses from the HBO series
            <a href="http://mashable.com/category/game-of-thrones/">
             <em>
              Game of Thrones
             </em>
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <div class="see-also">
            <p>
             See also:
             <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/08/18/hbo-branded-game-of-thrones-fans/">
              All Men Must Ink: How HBO Tattooed 111 'Game of Thrones' Fans
             </a>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            From House Baratheon's majestic stag-inspired model to House Bolton's gruesomely flayed man, every look perfectly encapsulates each family's motto in the series.
           </p>
           <p>
            Chase used her skills as a makeup artist to express her love for the show. Each face took an hour to create, after days of daydreaming, meticulously researching characters and practicing application before the shooting day. Unsurprisingly, the work comes from a true, dedicated fan of the series.
           </p>
           <p>
            "I wanted to stay in the
            <em>
             Game of Thrones
            </em>
            zone and stay in denial that the season was over," Chase told
            <em>
             Mashable
            </em>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            More of Chase's makeup work can be found on her
            <a href="http://allisonchasemakeup.wordpress.com/" target="_blank">
             website
            </a>
            . Matt Barnes works in Palmerston North, New Zealand, and more of his photography, including more from the
            <em>
             Game of Thrones
            </em>
            makeup series, can be seen on his professional
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/MattBarnesPhotography" target="_blank">
             Facebook
            </a>
            page.
           </p>
