            <p>
             A
basic difference between laptops and desktops is that laptops can be powered by
the adaptors which are provided with the machine but in addition the laptops
can be used away from the power source as well that is they can be used when
they are not plugged in to an electricity outlet. This mainly separated the
laptops from the everyday use home desktop personal computers. In the beginning
the
             <b>
              <a class="" href="http://www.hafeezcentre.pk/Laptops/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               laptops
              </a>
             </b>
             used to be rugged and very task specific so people thought of them
as something very specific to special tasks or the personnels related to that
task so they were limited to the commercial banking use or military use. But
with the passage of time and remarkable innovations the laptops became smaller
and they got better performing components. Along these factors plus the fact
that they got cheaper and affordable made the laptops more and more powerful
and popular in the general public.
            </p>
            <p>
             Now there are various kinds of laptops available
in the market namely; full size laptop, notebook, palm top, pen PC. Ultra slim
PC, rugged laptop and tablet PC. Then there is a specific kind of laptops whose
performance is no lesser than any other desktop PC and they are called desktop
replacement. These high performance machines are fast, quick and efficient and
they have large screen displays like any other desktop. The basic parts of the
motherboard that make up the performance machinery of a laptop are relatively
smaller in size and the fact that they have to be regulated to check the heat
flow as well as avoid over-heating. Due to the above mentioned factors the
performance of the laptops is a little bit less than an ordinary PC.
            </p>
