             You asked for it, you got it, Pix!
             <br/>
             <br/>
             I have the upholstered pvc on now. I just took a staple gun to it and that took care of the issues I was having with the leather. There is a picture here of the spring loaded hinges, the 2" wedge you see in that pic is on the ceiling. It's a weird looking pic. The dark one is the legs.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             These are taken with my camera phone - sorry for the blur. The last one is a hub I just got off of ebay. The Bcr2000s are plugged into that. The bcf2000s all use their own usb plug on the PC - They must in Mackie Emulation mode.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The bcrs set in there and are held there by gravity. The bcfs on the bottom are trapped between the front molding and the spring loaded pvc in front. 4 units squeeze in with NO side to side play.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             What you see here is all working like a champ.
