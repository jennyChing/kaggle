       <h2 class="alt">
        What are the "
        <em>
         DriverPacks
        </em>
        "?
       </h2>
       <p>
        Each DriverPack is a
        <u>
         pack
        </u>
        age of
        <u>
         driver
        </u>
        s for a
              particular category of hardware. There are for example
              DriverPacks for Sound devices and for Graphics devices. For each
              of these DriverPacks we include
        <em>
         every
        </em>
        device driver of
              that category. On top of that, we solve tricky problems 
              such as driver conflicts  for you.
       </p>
       <p>
        They are intended to be used while creating a Windows 2000,
              XP, 2003, Vista or Windows 7 installation disc/image.
              We also provide you with the software to make that as simple as
              possible.
       </p>
       <p>
        Your Windows installation disc doesn't have drivers for all
              hardware, and over time the drivers it does have get
              out-of-date.
       </p>
       <p>
        We've built this to
        <strong>
         simplify the lives of network
              administrators and employees of pc shops
        </strong>
        , but also
              enthusiast pc users.
       </p>
       <p>
        Sounds cool? Then explore our
        <a href="/downloads">
         downloads
        </a>
        and come and say hi in our
        <a href="http://forum.driverpacks.net">
         forum
        </a>
        !
       </p>
