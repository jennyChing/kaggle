        <p>
         I once heard someone say that having children is like living in a frat house; theres always something sticky, something is always getting broken, and there is always someoneup at night. Isnt that the truth?! Motherhood is not easy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Signs that you're a mom of little ones" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2954" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.wellnourishednest.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15signs.jpg?resize=600%2C900"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Today, this one is for you, moms of little crawlers, droolers, and cuddlers! Just when you think youre the only one whose house is a wreck and hasnt showered in 3 days, think again! Here are the top signs that youre a mom of little ones!
        </p>
        <p>
         You know youre a mom of little ones when
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          1. The car organization aisle excites you.
         </p>
         <p>
          2. You no longer respond to your real name.
         </p>
         <p>
          3. When you find an outfit you like, you wear it multiple timesa week.
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Youve forgotten what silence in the car sounds like. In fact, it now makes you uncomfortable.
         </p>
         <p>
          5. Your purse could double as a carry-on suitcase.
         </p>
         <p>
          6. You find yourself frequently saying, Sorry about the mess.
         </p>
         <p>
          7. At any given time, there is a load of laundry on the couch, in the dryer, on the bedroom floor, or accidentally left overnight in the washer.
         </p>
         <p>
          8. You find yourself spelling out words like crap or stupid even when your kids arent around.
         </p>
         <p>
          9. You frequently find sippy cups behind furniture and dread opening them.
         </p>
         <p>
          10. Youve successfully mastered going to the bathroom with a baby in a backpack carrier and a toddler at your feet.
         </p>
         <p>
          11. Bathroom words are part of your normal conversations with friends.
         </p>
         <p>
          12. Every time you get on the phone, suddenly its as if youre surrounded by a marching band, a WWE throw-down, and a tribe of howler monkeys at meal-time.
         </p>
         <p>
          13. You decide if a shirt is dirty by whether or not it has spit-up on it.
         </p>
         <p>
          14. You no longer get to decide what music gets played in the car.
         </p>
         <p>
          15. You can no longer be on time anywhere but find comfort in the fact that your friends will more than likely be late as well.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <h1>
         Can you relate?
        </h1>
        <p>
         Moms everywhere, just in time for Mothers Day, wed like to celebrate YOU!
