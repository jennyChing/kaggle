    <p id="keypoint">
     Want to know about Leo? There are reasons why Lions are the undisputed King of the animal kingdom; they are large, powerful
               creatures that will always have to come in ahead of lesser animals.
    </p>

    <p id="firstline">
     No way would they let the creatures like tiger's or cheetah's steal the show; there ego's couldn't handle
               it. No, the Lion has to be the star and will never willingly share the spotlight. They're the undisputed King of Beasts,
               and they'll never let you forget it.
    </p>
    <p>
     Leo does not need to be the center of your world... as long as they're the center of your universe! Anything less will
               wound their pride and cause them to be grumpy the rest of the day. And grumpy lions have a tendency to bite the hand that
               feeds them.
    </p>
    <p>
     Lions are warm and caring by nature, with very generous spirits. Leo's have a tendency to see the world from a very
               young age in shades of black and white, good versus bad. They make opinions based on these views, and seldom change them as
               they grow older.
    </p>
    <p>
     Lions are bossy, not because they mean to be but just because they're never wrong. (Or so they like to think). They are
               filled with the most absolute confidence that they know what's best for everyone and what they don't know won't
               make a difference.
    </p>
    <p>
     When Lion's lose their temper, they roar. Thankfully they're usually good natured and slow to anger. However, this
               is a fire sign so displays of temper are to be expected from time to time.
    </p>
    <p>
     Lions are proud creatures. They take great pride, not just in themselves but in their homes and in their families. Although
               they're willing to buy a small starter home when they're first getting into homeownership, they will quickly want to
               trade up and purchase something bigger that will have more room for the family that they will one day want to have.
    </p>
    <p>
     Leo's do better than most at striking a balance between work and play. You don't have to worry about them spending
               too much time at the office. They're naturally very active and exercise for them is something they enjoy and they often
               incorporate it into their hobbies.
    </p>
    <p>
     Work is something Leo's will do well at. They have the drive and determination to excel at whatever they put their mind
               to. Their need for recognition will push them to rise to the top of whatever field they choose to enter. However, Leo's
               tend to go into area's that are professional and somewhat conservative.
    </p>
    <p>
     Leo's can be quite scrupulous with their money and how they spend it, but never for very long. Their generous nature
               will get them into financial trouble. Not just because they're generous to their friends, but because they believe in
               being generous to themselves. In fact, Leo's enjoy treating themselves to the very best luxuries that money can buy and
               they don't mind putting it on a credit card if they have to.
    </p>
    <p>
     To learn more about Leo, use the links in the navigation on the left side of this page.
    </p>
