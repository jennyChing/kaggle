         <img alt="Collections 101" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/175699/175699"/>
        </p>
        <p class="contentdivider">
         <i>
          Dont worry, this isnt one of those courses where your eyes glaze over and you end up facedown on your desk. Its a quick primer on the amazing and ginormous collections of content iStock has to offer thatll take your work to the next level.
         </i>
        </p>
        <h1 style="margin-top: 120px;">
         Lets start with the Vetta Collection
        </h1>
        <p>
         If youre looking for something fresh, this is the place. Carefully chosen by our top dedicated team of creatives for exceptional quality and artistic direction,
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/search/vetta/#fbae44f">
          Vetta
         </a>
         (Italian for peak) is the original stock collection. Elevate your designs, with richly-detailed photo, video and illustration files priced as low as 35 credits.
        </p>
        <img alt="Lets start with the Vetta Collection" class="collectionheader" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202089/202089"/>
        <h1>
         Original files, only from iStock
        </h1>
        <p>
         iStock is the original source for top-notch stock content you just cant find anywhere else, so when you search, youre not going to find the same old stale content on our site. At iStock, we work exclusively with some of the best stock creators to bring you tons of
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/search/exclusive/search/?facets={%2235%22:[],%2234%22:[%221%22,%227%22,%228%22],%221005%22:%22true%22#4c3db10">
          hand-picked photo, illustration, video and audio files
         </a>
         . Million of files you wont find anywhere else means millions of ways to stand out.
        </p>
        <img alt="Original files, only from iStock" class="collectionheader" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202090/202090"/>
        <h1>
         Don't miss out on Hulton Archive
        </h1>
        <p>
         Be sure to check out our addictive collection of retro images. And if you think retro only means black and white, color yourself wrongour
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/search/portfolio/2134884/?facets=%7B%2225%22%3A%226%22%7D#ddb5764">
          Hulton Archive
         </a>
         features awesome imagery through the 70s as well.
        </p>
        <img alt="Don't miss out on Hulton Archive" class="collectionheader" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202091/202091"/>
        <h1>
         Enter the world of our Illustrations Collection
        </h1>
        <p>
         When its time to remove the shackles of reality, where every stroke and every line reflects your wildest dreams brought to life. Whether you need an incredibly angry octopus or someone literally floating on cloud nine, our
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/illustration">
          Illustration Collection
         </a>
         will take your work where it needs to go.
        </p>
        <img alt="Enter the world of our Illustrations Collection" class="collectionheader" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202097/202097"/>
        <h1>
         Now lets talk about the Video Collection
        </h1>
        <p>
         Weve inspired tons of devotion to our motion from video editors looking to incorporate rad elements and special effects to their projects. But theres also more traditional stock footage for when you need
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/video">
          video
         </a>
         of business scenes, healthcare and people just being real people.
        </p>
        <img alt="Now lets talk about the Video Collection" class="collectionheader" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202092/202092"/>
        <h1>
         Pump up the volume with our Audio Collection
        </h1>
        <p>
         Need an explosion, a bass line, a drum pattern, a screaming chicken? Multi-media production pros find it all here plus, it spans all popular musical genres and is guaranteed royalty-free. This
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/audio">
          music
         </a>
         is created exclusively for iStock, and it rocks.
        </p>
        <img alt="Pump up the volume with our Audio Collection" class="collectionheader" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202093/202093"/>
        <h1>
         Tell your story with our Editorial Collection
        </h1>
        <p>
         Of course no content overview would be complete without mentioning our
         <a href="http://www.istockphoto.com/editorial">
          Editorial Collection
         </a>
         and its thousands of unaltered shots of the people, places, events and brands making news in the world. Here youll find pics of everything from Brad Pitt to the latest iPad, helping you provide context for your newspaper or magazine article, blog post or any other non-commercial use. (Just dont use these images to sell something!)
        </p>
        <img alt="Tell your story with our Editorial Collection" class="contentdivider" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202094/202094"/>
        <div style="padding-bottom: 30px;">
         <h1 style="width:368px; float:left; padding-top:15px;">
          Awesome content at awesome prices
         </h1>
         <p style="width:358px; float:left; padding-right: 10px;">
          At iStock, were all about giving you access to amazing content at amazing prices that are sure to fit any budget. Finding the right imagery, illustrations, audio and video at the right price is a no-brainer with tools like our magic price filter. 
    Look for this when you start searching:
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <img src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202096/202096"/>
         </p>
         <img alt="Awesome content at awesome prices" src="http://i.istockimg.com/generic_image_view/202095/202095" style="padding-top: 5px;"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         Thats all for now, class. Your only homework is to check out these collections and ace your next campaign.
        </p>
