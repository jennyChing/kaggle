        <p>
         <i>
          (My boyfriend has often expressed his theory that when a woman says Its fine, you know that it most definitely is NOT fine.)
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         So, I want to have a joint birthday party with [Friend] but I have to wait until he gets home from his job in [Other Country]. Which means the first Saturday we can do it is your birthday.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Boyfriend:
         </b>
         Youre going to have a party, on my birthday, for other peoples birthdays?
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         Well, I was gonna say that it could be for you, too
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Boyfriend:
         </b>
         It would be all your friends, though.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         So invite your friends, too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Boyfriend:
         </b>
         But
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         Look, do you want me to do it a different day? I can just do it a different day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Boyfriend:
         </b>
         <i>
          *looking away*
         </i>
         No, its fine
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         Did you just give me the girl Its fine?
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Boyfriend:
         </b>
         Oh, s*** I did, didnt I?
        </p>
