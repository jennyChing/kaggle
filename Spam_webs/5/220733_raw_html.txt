       <section class="stag-section stag-intro-text">
        <p>
         <span class="stag-dropcap stag-dropcap--squared" style="font-size: 125px; line-height: 125px; width: 125px; height: 125px;">
          S
         </span>
         eduction is 100% an emotional process. Dont let anyone tell you otherwise. All of the lines and jokes and games and appearances we agonize and obsess over are simply tools to create a desired emotional outcome. Theyre a means, not an end.
        </p>
       </section>
       <p>
        The Greatest Feeling in the World is when you and a person are so emotionally intimate that the physical limitations of your body become stifling. You yearn to melt one another and mix yourselves into some kind of bodily fluid concoction. Sex is a by-product of this process, of course  the ultimate act of physical intimacy. A last ditch effort to become one with one another.
       </p>
       <p>
        When you have The Greatest Feeling in the World, conflicting schedules fall by the wayside. Second guessing one anothers intentions is as absurd as returning money you just won in the lottery.
       </p>
       <p>
        The mens dating advice industry creates entire systems, layering theory upon theory, all for the banal goal of making their penises slightly wetter than they were before. It sells men short. Our internal lives go so much further than that. These men dont even see that sex isnt the cause, its the effect. Find the Greatest Feeling in the World and wonderful, mind-blowing sex happens as a side-effect.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its always been assumed that it took dating someone months or even years to reach this place. But it can be reached in a matter of hours. And she doesnt even have to speak your language
       </p>
       <p>
        11PM: Buenos Aires. Tourist bar. Mostly Europeans and a few Americans (boring), but also a smattering of South American travelers (more interesting). I seek out the most beautiful girl there. Found her. She looks Brazilian  as only Brazilians have a smorgasbord of mixed genetics like her and upon further inspection, she is way, way overdressed for this place (typical Brazilian behavior) and doesnt shy away from eye contact when made (only Brazilians stare like that).
       </p>
       <p>
        Shes talking to two guys. Cant tell if theyre with her or if they are randoms. Im busy scavenging for munchies, as I hadnt eaten yet that night.
       </p>
       <p>
        We catch eye contact again. Yep, has to be Brazilian. I interrupt their conversation, You must be Brazilian. She smiles, Yes, how did you know? in broken English. The guys are definitely not with her. They try to remain engaged in the conversation, but its apparent within a few minutes that Im the only one she wants to talk to. They drop off like dead flies.
       </p>
       <p>
        1AM: Dancing. Spinning. Falling (figuratively, not literally). Light kissing. Were inseparable now. The bubble is forming. A bath of oxytocin in our brains. No one else dares speak to us. Were either dancing together or Im sitting with my arms wrapped around her. We physically dont disconnect, ever.
       </p>
       <p>
        She speaks A+ Portuguese, B+ Spanish and C- English; I speak A+ English, B- Spanish and C- Portuguese. Conversations are a linguistic jungle-gym, falling in and out of all three languages. We dont understand half of what the other one says. But verbal misfires are more than compensated with smiles, eye contact and touch. The missed words almost help in a way. As they just make the emotions and implications that much stronger.
       </p>
       <p>
        3AM: Leaving the club now. We lost track of the main group we were with hours ago, although we dont really care. We made a token effort to figure out which bar they went to, but really it was an excuse for us to do something together. Taxi!
       </p>
       <p>
        5AM: At my hostel, with my laptop, we buy tickets for a getaway at a beach resort about 5 hours outside Buenos Aires. We book a room on the beach together and plan on leaving the next day. Weve known each other for four hours.
       </p>
       <hr class="stag-section stag-divider stag-divider--dashed"/>
       <p>
        Were on day three of our little tryst right now. This is the first time Ive done such an insta-vacation with a girl. And Id be lying if I said I wasnt a little nervous going through with it. But its been fine  wonderful even. Our bubble has slowly faded, as the long days together have forced reality to settle in a little bit. Were not in love. Were not going to get married and have a million babies. We probably wont even keep in touch for that long. And thats OK.
       </p>
       <p>
        But we still love each others company. We still laugh together, dance together in inappropriate places, and overdress everywhere we go and make heads turn.
       </p>
       <p>
        On the beach, with her perfect body and tiny thong bikini, she rubs sun tan lotion on me every afternoon. Every night we tangle ourselves in sheets and limbs until we cant stay awake anymore.
       </p>
       <p>
        We are each others idealizations. We look at each other and see our dreams and desires. And for a brief moment of our lives we appear to each other as everything we need. We speak to each other in broken Spanish. We create memories together. And if for only a week we occasionally share The Greatest Feeling in the World.
       </p>
