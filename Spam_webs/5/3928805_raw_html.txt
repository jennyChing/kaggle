            <div class="td-post-text-content">
             <p>
              A random funny text to a stranger got me to thinking What would other random strangers reply if they got a random text from an unknown number.
             </p>
             <p>
              306 wrong numbers. Thats how many texts I sent this time, all with an upbeat and positive message.
             </p>
             <p>
              I chose known prefixes for cell phones in my area, and I sent the message out (a labor of love which took a couple of days, LOL).
             </p>
             <p>
              Most of those I sent it to either didnt reply or simply told me I had the wrong number. This screen capture (from
              <a href="http://www.danoah.com/2014/04/217-wrong-numbers.html">
               the first Wrong Numbers post
              </a>
              ) pretty much sums up those ones:
             </p>

              </div>
             </div>
             <p>
              But
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Some
              </em>
              replies were a little (or a lot) more incredible, hilarious, or powerful than that.
             </p>

              Here are 21 of them.
