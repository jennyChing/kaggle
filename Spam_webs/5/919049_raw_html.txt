            <p>
             Our faces are attacked daily by various environmental factors, making exfoliation a key to great skin. If you have sensitive skin, many exfoliants can be abrasive and harmful to your skin. This all-natural DIYavocado face cleanser will purify and exfoliate your face without dangerous and unnecessary chemicals.
            </p>
            <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->

            <p>
             <strong>
              Key Ingredients &amp; Why They Work:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Avocado 
             </strong>
             Avocados containamazing benefits for the skin. The antioxidant properties will help combat free radicals, which are a major cause of wrinkles and skin damage. Vitamins C and E, along with various fatty acids, also help protect and firmthe skin.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Eggs 
             </strong>
             Eggs are great for skin tightening, oil removal, and whitehead removal. The protein in eggs is also nourishingand healing.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Milk 
             </strong>
             Milk has lactic acid, which is an terrificnatural exfoliator.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              What to Do:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             1. In a bowl mix 1 egg, 1 avocado (remove skin and pit) and 1/2 cup of whole milk. If you have a lactose intolerance, try a substitute milk variation.
            </p>
            <p>
             2. Using a fork or whisk, mix the ingredients until they are smooth and even throughout.
            </p>
            <p>
             3. Apple the mixture mixture on your face and neck. Gently massage it in for 2-3 minutes.
            </p>
            <p>
             4. Use warm water to rinse off the mixture and pat your face dry with a clean towel.
            </p>
            <p>
             5. Apply a natural moisturizer of your choice.
             <strong>
              <a class="amzn-pp-active" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008WG7X8Q/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=211189&amp;creative=373489&amp;creativeASIN=B008WG7X8Q&amp;link_code=as3&amp;tag=skims01-20&amp;linkId=HXOFFMPOMJHA7YXX" target="_blank">
               100% African Shea Butter
              </a>
             </strong>
             is always a great option.
            </p>
            <p>
             6. You can store any excess in the fridge. Be sure to air seal it tight.
            </p>
