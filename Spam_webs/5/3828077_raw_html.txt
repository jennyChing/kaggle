          <a class="glossaryLink" data-tooltip='&lt;br /&gt;&lt;p&gt;Growth Expectations: Related classifications include Expected Decline, Substandard and Strategic Play.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;p&gt;Companies that exhibit both higher historical revenue growth (over 3 years) and higher expected earnings growth (P/E) than peer median, suggesting market confidence in sustained superior earnings growth.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.capitalcube.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/merck-nyse_mrk-superior-growth-expectations.png"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6235" title="merck (nyse_mrk) superior growth expectations" src="http://www.capitalcube.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/merck-nyse_mrk-superior-growth-expectations.png" alt="merck (nyse:mrk) has superior growth expectations" width="599" height="444" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;br /&gt;' href="http://www.capitalcube.com/blog/index.php/glossary/superior/" target="_blank" title="Glossary: Superior">
           superior

          relative to its peers.

         <h2>
          Betting Heavily on the Future
         </h2>
         <div>
          Cree's annualized rate of change in capital of 27.9% over the past three years is greater than the peer median of 16.3%. This relatively high investment has generated a less than peer median return on capital of 4.0% averaged over the same three years. The relatively high investment and low current returns lead us to believe that the company is betting heavily on the future.

         <h2>
          Company Profile
         </h2>
         <div>
          Cree, Inc. develops and manufactures semiconductor materials and devices based on silicon carbide, gallium nitride, silicon and related compounds. Its product line includes blue and green LED chips, LED Components, LED Modules, LED lighting, power switching devices and radio frequency devices. The company was founded by Calvin Carter Jr., John Palmour, Neal Hunter, Eric Hunter and John Edmond in 1987 and is headquartered in Durham, NC.
