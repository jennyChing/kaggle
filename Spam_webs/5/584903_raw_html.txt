        A scholarship is financial support awarded by an academic institution to a student on the basis of academic merit. At Henley Business School we award a limited number of scholarships on our MBA programme to students who have achieved excellence in their prior academic studies. A key consideration in our selection of Scholarship awards will therefore be the degree to which we believe the candidate will make a significant contribution to the quality of the Henley MBA programme. It is likely that award winners will have a UK first class Honours Degree or its international equivalent from a reputable, leading University or academic institution. Scholarship applications are considered by an academic panel of Henley Business School and their decision is final and not open to discussion or appeal by applicants.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Study Subjects and Level:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Scholarships are awarded to study any MBA courses at Henley Business School. The Scholarships are obtainable for studying master degree at Henley Business School.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Eligibility:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        A key consideration in our selection of Scholarship awards will therefore be the degree to which we believe the candidate will make a significant contribution to the quality of the Henley MBA programme. It is likely that award winners will have a UK first class Honours Degree or its international equivalent from a reputable, leading University or academic institution. Scholarship applications are considered by an academic panel of Henley Business School and their decision is final and not open to discussion or appeal by applicants.
        <br/>
        -Must therefore have received an unconditional offer from Henley Business School before the following deadlines in order to be considered by the panel.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Scholarship Value:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The scholarships will cover 50% tuition fee.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Selection criteria:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The scholarships will be awarded on the basis of academic merit.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         How to Apply?
        </strong>
        The applying mode is by post.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Application Deadline:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The application deadlines for Flexible Executive MBA are 30th June 2014 and 31st July 2014 for September intake.
       </p>
