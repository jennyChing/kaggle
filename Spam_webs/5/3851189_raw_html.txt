       <div class="entry-content bborder" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         The Tiny House movement is gaining momentum as an antithesis to rising housing pricesas tiny homes offer a sustainable and affordable alternative.As tiny homes are so small and versatile, they can be placed just about anywhere and moved as the mood strikes. Even if you outgrow your tiny home, it makes a great guest house and can also be sold easily. Tiny homes free owners of mortgages, rent and the kind of overheads that would otherwise keep them tied to their cubicles. Greenmoxie is building our own tiny home this summer and we have tiny home fever! Here are 20 of our favorite tiny homes just for you.
