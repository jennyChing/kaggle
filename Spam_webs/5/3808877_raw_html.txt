           So excited, in fact, that the man already plans to open his second Rallys store after the Eastlake business is up and running.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           A new franchise is set to be built on the site of the former Burger King on Chardon Road in the Shoppes at Willoughby Hills (formerly Loehmanns Plaza) area. The current structure on the property will be razed.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           If plans go smoothly, the western Lake County store could open by late summer.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           Talking up the Rallys brand, which in recent years has been awarded several industry awards, including Best Drive-Thru in America by QSR Magazine and the Hot! Again Award from Nations Restaurant News, Stallman said he sees a lot of similarities with McDonalds, his new competitor.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           Rallys menu features burgers, fries, shakes and other inexpensive food prepared and served quickly. However, its claim to fame are the flavors of casual dining brought into a fast-food environment, according to Jennifer Durham, vice president of franchise development for Rallys.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           When people taste our food, the expectation is higher than you would get from a Burger King, McDonalds or Wendys, she said, adding that highly seasoned and bold-flavored fare created with quality ingredients add value to Rallys product.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           The 904-square-foot building allows for drive-through and walk-up service, but doesnt include a dining room. A covered patio offers an outdoor eating option in good weather.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           Durham noted that the absence of a dining room means less of an investment cost for franchisees and that 70 percent to 80 percent of fast food is typically delivered via drive-throughs.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           Stallmans stores with a single drive-through lane are examples of the new Rallys building prototype, an alternative to their double drive-through system.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           A customers time from arrival at the speaker to driving away with order in tow should take less than three minutes.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           Not only do we have those targets, we meet them, Durham said. The team is making the order as youre placing it. Nothing sits under a heat lamp.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <!-- start articleparagraph1.pbo -->
          <p>
           Currently Ohio boasts 60 Rallys restaurants, with 19 in the Cleveland market.
          </p>
          <!-- end articleparagraph1.pbo -->

          <p>
           In 1999, Checkers and Rallys restaurants merged to create the largest double drive-through chain in the U.S.
