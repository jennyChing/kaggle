            <h1 class="blog-title">
             <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/">
              Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies
             </a>
            </h1>
            <div class="blog-info-wrapper">
             <div class="blog-date">
              posted on : September 28, 2014
             </div>
             <div class="blog-tag">
              <span class="blog-info-head">
               tag :
              </span>
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/apples/" rel="tag">
               Apples
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/applesauce/" rel="tag">
               Applesauce
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/maple-syrup/" rel="tag">
               Maple Syrup
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/oatmeal/" rel="tag">
               Oatmeal
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="blog-author">
              <span class="blog-info-head">
               by :
              </span>
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/author/shelly/" rel="author" title="Posts by Shelly">
               Shelly
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="blog-comment">
              <span class="blog-info-head">
               comment :
              </span>
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/#comments">
               27
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="clear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="blog-content">
             <p>
              These Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies are perfectly sweet!
              <br/>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13682" height="814" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/applesauceoatmeal.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/applesauceoatmeal.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Yep. More apples. yall.
             </p>
             <p>
              If youre looking to overdo a seasonal ingredient, youve come to the right place. Because Im not of the opinion there is too much of a good thing in pretty much any aspect of life. Good things can come in abundance. I wont hate it, thats for sure.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span id="more-13680">
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              I mean has anyone ever heard someone say, oh gosh..please, dont make me THAT rich. Thats just TOO MUCH MONEY.
             </p>
             <p>
              Or, please I dont really want to have THAT many cute purses. Too many cute accessories might be hazardous.
             </p>
             <p>
              No one ever says that. Ever.
             </p>
             <p>
              So my pointapples. Theyre good, theyre in season and I plan on WERKing them OUT.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13689" height="793" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4218.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4218.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              I thought I would try to make a slightly lighter cookieand sub in some applesauce for the butter. What ended up happening was I got a delicious cookie, with less butterbut I wouldnt exactly call it light. There is a whole mess of brown sugar in thereand of course I made a delicious maple glaze to ice them with.
             </p>
             <p>
              BUT I will say they are pretty tasty with a cup of coffee in the morning. Which basically has nothing to do with anything but I thought I would mention it.
              <br/>
              So lets talk details.I mixed in some applesauce right into the sugar and butter. It kind of looked a little bit curdled and I thought I might have messed upbut I diint. Just press on.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_4188" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13683" height="380" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4188.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4188.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Add in your flour, oats etc and youll have normal cookie dough.
             </p>
             <p>
              Plus throw in a few apples for good measure.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_4191" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13684" height="737" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4191.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4191.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Drop it on a lined baking sheet and bake!
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_4193" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13685" height="778" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4193.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4193.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              The glaze is also super simplejust powdered sugar, maple syrup and a little heavy cream.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_4198" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13686" height="735" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4198.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4198.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              When the cookies are done drizzle the icing all over
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_4206" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13687" height="763" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4206.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4206.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              You could even make the icing a bit thicker by adding less cream and spread the icing on top, if youd prefer a thicker layer.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13688" height="751" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4211.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/iced-applesauce-oatmeal-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_4211.jpg&amp;description=Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              And there you have it..a loaded appley cookie. With icing. Its a good day.
              <br/>
             </p>
             <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
              <link href="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/applesauceoatmeal.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
              <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
               Iced Applesauce Oatmeal Cookies
              </div>
              <div class="ERSClear">
              </div>
              <div class="ERSTopRight">
               <div class="ERSSavePrint">
                <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
                 <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://cookiesandcups.com/easyrecipe-print/13680-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Print
                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
               makes 30 cookies
              </div>
              <div class="divERSHeadItems">
              </div>
              <div class="ERSIngredients">
               <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
                Ingredients
               </div>
               <ul>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 4 Tbsp butter, room temp
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 cups light brown sugar
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 egg
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                  cup applesauce
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 tsp vanilla
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                  tsp baking soda
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                  tsp kosher salt
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 cups old fashioned oats
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 2 cups flour
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                  cup small diced Granny Smith apples (about 1 small apple)
                </li>
               </ul>
               <div class="ERSSectionHead">
                Icing
               </div>
               <ul>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 cup powdered sugar
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 2 Tbsp real Maple Syrup
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 3 Tbsp heavy cream
                </li>
               </ul>
               <div class="ERSClear">
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="ERSInstructions">
               <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
                Instructions
               </div>
               <ol>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Preheat oven to 350
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Line baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Mix butter and sugar together with mixer until combined, about 2 minutes. Add in egg, applesauce, vanilla, baking soda and salt and mix until incorporated, about another minute. Mixture will look slightly curdled.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Turn mixer to low and add in oats and flour, mixing until combined. Stir in apples.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Drop dough by heaping tablespoon onto lined pan, about 2 inches apart and bake for 10-12 minutes until tops are set.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Transfer cookies to wire rack to cool.
                </li>
               </ol>
               <div class="ERSSectionHead">
                Icing
               </div>
               <ol>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Mix all icing ingredients in a bowl until smooth and then drizzle over top
                </li>
               </ol>
               <div class="ERSClear">
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
               <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
                Notes
               </div>
               <div class="ERSNotes">
                optional addition ~  tsp cinnamon added when you mix in salt and baking soda.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Store airtight for up to 3 days
                <br/>
                Adapted from Martha Stewart
               </div>
               <div class="ERSClear">
