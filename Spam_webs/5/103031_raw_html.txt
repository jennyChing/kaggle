         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8454" height="500" src="http://craziestgadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/panasonic-sc-sp100.jpg" title="panasonic sc sp100" width="500"/>
          <br/>
          Theres plenty of iPod speaker docks out there and most them are shaped like a box.  Or maybe a rounded box.  Big freakin deal.  Boooring.  The Panasonic SC-SP100 is way more exciting than the completely non-memorable name would imply.  Its got a sexy rounded shape, almost evoking a seashell.  Yes I just implied that a seashell is sexy (any real mermaids out there, drop me an email please- I have a sexy proposition for you- raaaoooar.).  It puts out 20W of power and connects to your computer with a USB cable for synching.
          <br/>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8455" height="500" src="http://craziestgadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/panasonic-sc-sp100-front.jpg" title="panasonic sc sp100 front" width="500"/>
          <br/>
          Theres a 2 1/2 Dual Passive Radiator and a 2 1/2 Subwoofer that work combine to reproduce full bodied sound from an unobtrusive and portable speaker.  The Panasonic SC-SP100 costs about
          <a href="http://www.bhphotovideo.com/c/product/676092-REG/Panasonic_SC_SP100_SC_SP100_Audio_System_for.html">
           $115 at B&amp;H
          </a>
          and elsewhere.
