           <em>
            <strong>
             When Two Icons Merge
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="marilyn monroe, chanel No.5, tv ad, marilyn monroe chanel no.5 commercial, iconic, beauty blog nz, fashion blog nz, style blog nz, beauty media nz, fashion media nz, angie fredatovich, gurlinterrupted, fashion, beauty" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3600" height="423" src="http://gurlinterrupted.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-10-16-at-12.24.47-PM-300x423.png" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           After recently announcing Marilyn Monroe as the new face of Chanel No. 5, the French fashion house has now unveiled the new ad campaign which uses actual footage of Miss Monroe. The commercial features anaudio recording of a 1960 interview between Georges Belmont, who at the time was the editor of
           <em>
            Marie Claire
           </em>
           , and Monroe, in which she famously stated that she wore nothing but Chanel No. 5 to bed. Monroe statesYou know, they ask me questions. Just an example: What do you wear to bed ? A pajama top? The bottoms of the pajamas? A nightgown? So I said, Chanel No. 5, because its the truth And yet, I dont want to say nude. But its the truth!.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3599" style="width: 522px">
           <img alt="marilyn monroe, chanel No.5, tv ad, marilyn monroe chanel no.5 commercial, iconic, beauty blog nz, fashion blog nz, style blog nz, beauty media nz, fashion media nz, angie fredatovich, gurlinterrupted, fashion, beauty" class="size-full wp-image-3599" height="640" src="http://gurlinterrupted.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Marilyn-Monroe_copyright-Philippe-Halsman-Magnum-Photos.jpg" width="512"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            1952. Marilyn MONROE, a young American actress under contract with 20th Century Fox, posing in her small apartment for the cover of LIFE Magazine.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Both the print campaign and TV commercial are in black and white and Chanel has released three exclusive images featuring Marilyn with the perfume, however theres still no confirmation as to which will be used throughout the actual campaign.
          </p>
