           <font color="#8c7093">
            <h2>
             Is a Bartending Job Right For You? Take This Quiz To Find Out!
            </h2>
           </font>
           Being a bartender is not for everyone - if you hate having fun and getting paid to socialize, stay in your cubicle. But if you're up for the challenge, take this quiz to find out if you should pursue a
           <a href="https://www.bartending101.com/dvdorder.aspx?CID=True">
            bartending career
           </a>
           .
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <h2>
            Is becoming a bartender in your future?
           </h2>
           <ol>
            <li>
             <b>
              Do you enjoy being social on the job?
             </b>
             <br/>
             A.	Yes! I love meeting new people all of the time.
             <br/>
             B.	Yes, but I prefer laid back conversations with a variety of people.
             <br/>
             C.	No, I don't really like talking to people - at work or at home.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </li>
            <li>
             <b>
              Do you like working in a variety of settings - like at events?
             </b>
             <br/>
             A.	I'm all for trying out new things and going to different places.
             <br/>
             B.	As long as the setting is pretty casual, I'm there.
             <br/>
             C.	The thought of leaving my cubicle scares me.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </li>
            <li>
             <b>
              Do you prefer a flexible schedule or a fixed routine?
             </b>
             <br/>
             A.	The more flexible, the better.
             <br/>
             B.	I don't mind a fixed routine, but adding some flexibility sounds nice!
             <br/>
             C.	I'm not a huge fan of change.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </li>
            <li>
             <b>
              Do you want to learn practical and profitable skills, like how to deal with people?
             </b>
             <br/>
             A.	I love taking on the challenge of learning new things.
             <br/>
             B.	Of course, life is all about learning.
             <br/>
             C.	I dislike both people and profits.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </li>
            <li>
             <b>
              Is it important to you to be rewarded for good customer service?
             </b>
             <br/>
             A.	As in tips? Yes - show me the money!
             <br/>
             B.	A little extra cash never hurt anyone.
             <br/>
             C.	I like the consistency of my always-low salary.
             <br/>
            </li>
           </ol>
           <b>
            If you answered mostly A...
           </b>
           <br/>
           Being a bartender at a trendy nightclub or hot downtown spot is for you! The fast-placed nightlife and the draw of a new crowd every night is right up your alley. And, the flexible schedule and big tips you'll draw in by being comfortable with your customers isn't so bad either. Plus, you can always hire yourself out to work the hottest parties too!
           <a href="http://www.bartending101.com/bartender-membership.aspx">
            Become a bartender
           </a>
           - it's the job that takes your social life to a whole new level.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            If you answered mostly B...
           </b>
           <br/>
           Being a bartender at a laid-back bar or neighborhood pub would be your ideal job. The relaxed conversations with patrons, casual atmosphere and flexible schedule would be a perfect fit, not to mention the tips from customers who love your friendly face. Experience all the
           <a href="http://www.bartending101.com/whybartending.aspx">
            benefits of being a bartender
           </a>
           - it's the perfect mix of laid-back crowds and on-the-job fun!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            If you answered mostly C...
           </b>
           <br/>
           Having fun is really not your thing. Being a bartender may not be right for you.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Seriously, bartending jobs are right for pretty much anyone, from any walk of life.
           <br/>
