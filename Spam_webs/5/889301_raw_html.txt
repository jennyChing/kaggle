       <img alt="Hulu selling its Japan business to Nippon TV" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-262" data-lazy-original="http://www.techpeel.com/resources/uploads/2014/02/Hulu-Japan-Acquired-Nippon-TV.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" title="Hulu selling its Japan business to Nippon TV"/>

       <img alt="Hulu selling its Japan business to Nippon TV" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-262" src="http://www.techpeel.com/resources/uploads/2014/02/Hulu-Japan-Acquired-Nippon-TV.jpg" title="Hulu selling its Japan business to Nippon TV"/>

      <p>
       <strong>
        Hulu
       </strong>
       announced in a
       <a href="http://blog.hulu.com/2014/02/27/an-international-update-from-hulu-in-japan/" target="_blank">
        blog post
       </a>
       today that it will be selling its video on demand services in Japan to
       <strong>
        Nippon
        <a href="http://www.techpeel.com/television/">
         Television
        </a>
        Network Corporation
       </strong>
       (Nippon TV).
      </p>
      <p>
       Hulu started its operations in Japan 3 years ago and now it feels that it has reached a point where it is best to sell the
       <a href="http://www.techpeel.com/business/">
        business
       </a>
       to some company who will carry forward it with more efficient strategy.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-274">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       From the blog post:
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Im confident that the Hulu
        <a href="http://www.techpeel.com/business/">
         business
        </a>
        in Japan is in very good hands, and Nippon TV will take the service to new heights, with the added benefit of allowing us to focus on our growing
        <a href="http://www.techpeel.com/business/">
         business
        </a>
        here in the U.S. Thank you to all of our customers and partners across the globe for your continued loyalty and enthusiasm for Hulu.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
