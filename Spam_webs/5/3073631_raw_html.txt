          <p>
           If a person has frequent headaches then it can said with certainty that the person knows about what is causing the headaches and what triggers it, which can be any of the following that is stress, sleep problems, improper diet, allergy, sinus issues or strain to eyes but there is one more aspect which many people do not think about which is the posture of a person, and oxygen deficiency that bring on a headache. Many people have rounded shoulders with head jutted in a forward direction when sitting or standing. Poor posture has an adverse affect on respiratory system and blood circulation to brain causing muscle tension and hence headaches. If a person has tension headaches, then yoga might be the answer. Yoga goes a long way in helping tension headaches by way of relaxing muscles in head, back, neck. It bolsters circulation to brain and upper part of body, and improves posture. The best time to treat a headache is when there is a slight sign of it and before muscles start spasming.

           <h2>
            Yoga for Headaches: 5 Best Asanas
           </h2>
          </div>
          <p>
           To reduce headaches, the yoga poses described below can be inculcated into daily exercise regimen.
          </p>
          <div class="sub_subheading">
           <h2>
            Yoga Simple Seated Twist Pose for Treating Headaches
           </h2>
          </div>
          <p>
           To do this pose you can do the following:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Sit straight in edge of chair.
           </li>
           <li>
            Place feet firmly on floor.
           </li>
           <li>
            Cross right leg on top of left at knee.
           </li>
           <li>
            Take right arm to back of chair and left hand to right knee.
           </li>
           <li>
            Turn from stomach to right while exhaling.
           </li>
           <li>
            Keep shoulders downward and chest open.
           </li>
           <li>
            Relax the eyes and jaw.
           </li>
           <li>
            Hold this position for about 10 seconds and take deep breaths.
           </li>
           <li>
            Return to original position and do the same for the other side.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="sub_subheading">
           <h2>
            Yoga Grabbing Opposite Elbow Pose for Treating Headaches
           </h2>
          </div>
          <p>
           To do this pose you can do the following
          </p>
          <div class="center">
           <script type="text/javascript">

           <li>

           </li>
           <li>
            Relax eyes and jaws.
           </li>
           <li>
            Hold this position for about 10 seconds and take deep breaths.
           </li>
           <li>
            Come back to original position.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="sub_subheading">
           <h2>
            Yoga Gomukhasana with Arms Pose for Treating Headaches
           </h2>
          </div>
          <p>
           To do this pose you can do the following:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Stand with feet firmly placed on floor but slightly apart from each other.
           </li>
           <li>
            Lift right arm near the right ear.
           </li>
           <li>
            Bend right elbow.
           </li>
           <li>
            Reach the right hand behind back with palm facing backwards placing it between shoulder blades.
           </li>
           <li>
            Bring left arm behind back and clasp it with the right hand.
           </li>
           <li>
            If your shoulders are tight, yoga belt can be used for this.
           </li>
           <li>
            Maintain this position for about 10 seconds taking deep breaths.
           </li>
           <li>
            Come back to original position and repeat it on the other side.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="sub_subheading">
           <h2>
            Yoga Garudasana for Arms Pose for Treating Headaches
           </h2>
          </div>
          <p>
           To do this pose you can:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Wrap arms around torso with right arm below left as if hugging yourself.
           </li>
           <li>
            Bring hands up with left elbow resting in right elbow.
           </li>
           <li>
            Rotate hands such that palms are facing each other.
           </li>
           <li>
            Relax eyes, jaws, and shoulders.
           </li>
           <li>
            Maintain like this for about 10 seconds taking deep breaths.
           </li>
           <li>
            Come back to neutral and do the same thing with the opposite hand.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="sub_subheading">
           <h2>
            Yoga Side Stretches Exercise for Treating Headaches
           </h2>
          </div>
          <p>
           To do this exercise you can:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Sit on edge of chair with feet apart.
           </li>
           <li>
            Place the feet firm on floor.
           </li>
           <li>
            Lift arms near the head.
           </li>
           <li>
            Grab the left wrist using right hand.
           </li>
           <li>
            While exhaling, slowly stretch to right side while pulling the left arm and wrist with right hand.
           </li>
           <li>
            Press bodyweight to left buttock.
           </li>
           <li>
            Hold this position for about 10 seconds taking deep breaths.
           </li>
           <li>
            Return to neutral and do the same thing for the other side.
           </li>
          </ul>
