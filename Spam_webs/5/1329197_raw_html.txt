        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Hope Soap: Saving South African Children
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-10-21T12:00:28+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 21, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://findingjustice.org/author/ericdye/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Eric Dye
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://findingjustice.org/hope-soap-saving-south-african-children/#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <img alt="Hope Soap" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-12884" height="310" src="http://findingjustice.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Hope-Soap-Screenshot.png" width="580"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This is so awesome!
        </p>
        <p>
         In the Western world, I think we often overthink how to help those in need. I had no idea that something like hand washing would have such a profound and life changing effect!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-12883">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/mwjXY001nM8?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Incredible, right?
        </p>
        <p>
         I am
         <em>
          so
         </em>
         guilty when it comes to taking things for granted. My family and I all wash our hands multiple times a day, with clean water and soap and hardly ever give it a second thought. Such a basic necessity, made fun, and brought to vulnerable children that otherwise may not have had access to it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Hope Soap is genius!
        </p>
        <p>
         Learn more about
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/media/set/?set=a.451248308286151.1073741825.191269890950662&amp;type=1" target="_blank">
          Hope Soap
         </a>
         and the organization this was done through,
         <a href="http://blikkiesdorp4hope.co.za/" target="_blank">
          Blikkiesdorp 4 Hope
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         SOURCE:

          Ads of the World
         </a>
        </p>
