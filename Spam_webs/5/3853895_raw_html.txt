          Welcome to Day #6 of Christmas Week, a multi-blogger event co-hosted by Kim of

         <p>
          Today is our last day but we are going out with a bang. I thought I would toast the week with a cocktail. Something simple, elegant and festive red in color. I had such fun tasting black cherry vodka cocktails at an event this past summer. I wanted to make something with black cherry vodka for you today.
