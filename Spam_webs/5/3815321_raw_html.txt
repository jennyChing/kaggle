       <h2 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/save-the-date-right/" rel="bookmark">
         Save-the-Date Right
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/save-the-date-right/" rel="bookmark" title="9:23 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          July 14, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">
          Jordaan Sanford
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Save-the-date cards have almost become essential these days. The reason is they allow you to establish a pretty accurate headcount of who will be attending the wedding way ahead of time. However, when you send save-the-dates out, you want to make sure youre reminding your guests who have not responded to do so. This process has the potential to linger and that is something you want to stay away from.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/wedding-invitations" target="_blank">
           Save-the-date cards have evolved
          </a>
          from their days of just including the location, date, and wedding names. They can perhaps contain more information than the actual invitation, and thank goodness they have. It takes time for all your guests to be able to plan, schedule, and organize getting to your wedding. This is what makes a save-the-date so important. It allows your guests to know of your upcoming wedding in advance. Including information such as local hotels, travel details, and a link to your wedding website. All of these give your guests the ability to start scheduling and organizing their trip. Its great to include this information within your save-the-date because you dont want to wait till you give your invitations out. Including sufficient information that is of use to your guests is a game changer.
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/wedding-invitations" target="_blank">
           <img alt="save-the-date" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5438" height="667" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/savethedate1.jpg" width="1000"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Now you dont want to go overboard with your information. How can you go overboard? Including your registry in your save-the-date. This is highly inappropriate to print this on your card. There are places for your registry, such as your online wedding website. Your guests shouldnt feel obligated to give you gifts and putting it on your card will send the wrong message. When you receive a gift you want it to be genuine and not make your guests feel like they are obligated.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Although cards are the standard for save-the-dates this is not the only outlet you can use. One that really stands out is a
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/magnets" target="_blank">
           save-the-date magnet.
          </a>
          This is a great alternative because it sticks and constantly reminds your guests of your big day. Magnets will not get lost in piles, they will be stuck somewhere that can be seen and always in the front of your guests minds. Magnets are possibly the best alternative because of this reason alone.
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/wedding-invitations" target="_blank">
           <img alt="save-the-date" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5439" height="667" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/savethedate2.jpg" width="1000"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Many people dont have graphic design skills and that is completely ok. Here at Overnight Prints we have expert designers that know and understand the ins and outs of graphic design. Or you can have the opportunity of speaking with a designer and having them create your dream save-the-date. Or you can choose from our
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/wedding+postcards?g=postcards" target="_blank">
           many quality templates and create your own.
          </a>
          No matter what Overnight Prints will help you obtain your ideal card.
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/wedding-invitations" target="_blank">
           <img alt="save-the-date" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5440" height="1500" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/savethedate3.jpg" width="2100"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          You dont have to stick to the standard style. This is your time to shine and you can
          <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/savethedate2.jpg">
           <br/>
          </a>
          express your relationship to your guests and set the tone for your wedding. Grab the attention of your attendees by making a unique card that stands out from the rest. Create a magnet, or show the love for comics you both have. It doesnt matter! The sky is the limit. Whatever you do, dont be plain and boring, your wedding happens once, so make sure you do it your own personal and unique way.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

      <div class="post-5421 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-business-cards category-everything-print-las-vegas category-fonts category-welcome-bloggers tag-business-cards tag-circle-business-cards tag-creative tag-design-services tag-overnight-prints" id="post-5421">
       <h2 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/a-business-card-different-than-the-rest/" rel="bookmark">
         A Business Card Different Than The Rest
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/a-business-card-different-than-the-rest/" rel="bookmark" title="9:49 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          July 6, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">
          Jordaan Sanford
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Why be standard? When you can express your uniqueness to every individual you come across? That is the question you should ask yourself before starting to create your business card. There are plenty of standard, black and white, lets face it, boring business cards floating around out there. That doesnt mean you should blend in with the crowd or just try to be conservative. Business cards are essentially an extension of who you are and what you bring to the table. You dont want your business card to read exactly the same as all of your competitors. If you want that job, if you want to make a lasting impression; it all
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
           starts with a business card.
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
          <img alt="creative-business-15" class="aligncenter wp-image-5424" height="513" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/creative-business-15.jpg" width="597"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          As stated previously, a business card is an extension of who you are. So, ask the question who are you? What makes you special? Then transfer it over onto paper. One way you can do this is using bright and bold colors. Rich blues, gold, and bright reds are some examples. However, you dont want to just have one of those colors make up the entire card. If done this way, your card will be overwhelming and in-creative. The trick is to mix and match colors, but really think about what colors complement each other. If you go to Google and look up complimenting colors, you will find a lot of information. Turquoise mixed with white looks very elegant, bright, and peaceful. Red and light blue also complement each other quite nicely, the complementing colors are endless. What colors represent your personality and express who you are? This is what it comes down to.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          So we all know the stereotypical 3.5 x 2 business card. Must we stick to the standard? NO! Overnight Prints has a plethora of
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
           non-standard business cards
          </a>
          . Why not completely change the shape of your business card to lets say a 3 x 3 Circle Business Card? Or what about a 2.5 x 2.5 Square Business Card? Or maybe you can still keep that standard size but spice it up with extra thick 34pt card stock with our FAT Business Card. These cards automatically stick out with either their distinguished shape or superior thickness. Whoever you give your card to will notice and remember you just by the quality, shape, and texture of an Overnight Prints business card. Once you place your color using 4:4 full color printing, your card will already be expressing quality and uniqueness and these are two factors that matter.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/squarebusinesscards" target="_blank">
          <img alt="creative-business-15" class="aligncenter wp-image-5425" height="329" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/creative-business-151.jpg" width="540"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Now, going back to color. They dont have to be extremely bold to be considered unique. They can also be subdued, light, and tranquil. It depends on again who you are, or maybe your job position, and what you do. If you work by the beach or are for example a yoga instructor, you might want to use those lighter tones. In this case the lighter tones work perfectly. It expresses relaxation, peace, and if your job embodies this, you dont want your business card to communicate and entirely different message.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Of course, the most important part is placing your information and contact on the card. So, this brings us to choosing the correct font. There are an abundant, almost innumerable amounts of fonts circulating. Knowing this, there is one that fits effortlessly and accurately for everyone.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>

          </span>

           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">

          Your font should coincide with who you are and what you do. These are only font suggestions, if you find others that you love, dont be afraid to choose those. Business cards are all about expression and being true to you.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          If you have never thought about your business card or just rushed through the process. Its time to really think about your business card. You want to make great impressions not average ones, and you want to get that job, not be disregarded. Business cards are not only essential they are critical and vital in establishing relationships and making connections that last.

        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/new-custom-t-shirts-designer-is-available/" rel="bookmark" title="8:17 am">

         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">

        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">
          Jordaan Sanford
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008080;">
           The six reasons why you should use custom t-shirts
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Whether your business is just starting up or has been around for quite some time, marketing is a paramount factor for how a business performs. Often times marketing and advertising can get expensive and if your business is small or starting up you probably dont want to overspend. There are many cost-efficient methods that can propel a business to the top. One of them is
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
           custom t-shirts
          </a>
          . The benefits of using custom t-shirts to market your business are great. If youre looking to gain awareness and get the word out about your business there is no better way to do it.
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Walking billboard over Real Billboard
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          When you give a shirt out with your business design or logo on it, your shirt becomes literally a walking billboard. Im sure we have all come across billboards, they capture our attention and they make us interested. This is exactly what a quality custom t-shirt can do for your business; capture attention and gain interest. If your business is small or just starting up, having a marketing tactic that is able to do both of these aspects at once is incredible. Billboards are highly expensive, especially if you have them in a high trafficked area. Custom t-shirts are affordable and they can be in so many places at once, what could be better? It allows you to advertise to many people just like a billboard but without the huge price tag.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          When you give these shirts out to potential or current customers, you have the ability to speak personally and get to know your customers. Being able to create and establish those personal relationships are vital and are often missed, but custom t-shirts will help you make these relationships and also great impressions.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
          <img alt="custom-t-shirt-15" class=" wp-image-5395 aligncenter" height="382" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/custom-t-shirt-15.jpg" width="573"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Cheaper way to advertise
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          These days there are so many options for marketing and advertising. Its easy to get confused, lost, and overspend. What many businesses dont realize is having a simple mindset and not overthinking is often what is best. Custom t-shirts may seem simple but they have
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
           a lot to offer your business.
          </a>
          They only cost a few dollars per shirt, so your business can buy as many as needed without having to worry. The great thing about shirts is they can be worn over and over. Just think, your business can be advertised for over a year or more! This marketing tactic, although cheap which i like to call cost-efficient, can effectively advertise your business, extend your reach, and for long periods of time.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
          <img alt="custom-t-shirt-15" class="aligncenter wp-image-5401" height="392" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/custom-t-shirt-152.jpg" width="588"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Customize your shirts, your way
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          This marketing tactic lets you do it your way. Choose designs, place your logo, and add your company tagline. The sky is the limit and you can really maximize it. You may have come across a promotional company t-shirt, and thought that looks terrible. Many businesses think this is how they always look and think they dont want their business associated with poor-quality. NOT TRUE! As long as you find a reliable printing company that is established and prints quality, such as Overnight Prints, you will be good to go. Overnight Prints has just released a custom t-shirt option and you will not only receive quality but work with expert designers.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          When it comes to quality, Overnight Prints is dedicated in ensuring every customer that they will leave with a product they love. If youre looking for quality and perfection, Overnight Prints is your destination.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
          <img alt="custom-t-shirt-15" class="aligncenter wp-image-5398" height="453" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/custom-t-shirt-151.jpg" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           <strong>
            Many different options
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          You can really maximize the use of custom t-shirts by disbursing them in many different ways. Give them to your employees, this will make your team feel unified while promoting your business. If your company ever participates in a trade show, fair, or expo, you can either sell your custom t-shirts or hand them out. These shirts can be used for social media contest winners, and you can use them at public relations events. The concept of the custom t-shirt is to expand and maximize your exposure. By utilizing all of these options your business will reap the benefits.
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Advertise for longer
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Billboards, posters, and brochures all have a limited amount of time to advertise. With quality custom t-shirts you can advertise your business for an extended amount of time. If you make a great quality and well-designed t-shirt, customers will want to keep wearing your shirt. What does this mean for your business? Your advertising can go on and on. The longer amount of time you can advertise, the greater the awareness and sales.
         </span>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           ALSO
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Great for non-profit or volunteer organizations
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
          <img alt="custom-t-shirts-15" class=" wp-image-5410 alignleft" height="270" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/custom-t-shirts-153.jpg" width="270"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
          <img alt="custom-t-shirts-15" class=" wp-image-5411 alignright" height="271" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/custom-t-shirts-154.jpg" width="271"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Appeal meets functionality
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Custom t-shirts offer customers and your business the best of both worlds. Shirts are both appealing and carry functionality, these two great qualities reflect on your business. Its unbelievable one can receive both of these characteristics without paying costly prices and that is what makes custom t-shirts an optimal marketing strategy.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          If you havent considered custom t-shirts,
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/custom-t-shirts" target="_blank">
           you should ASAP
          </a>
          . They work perfectly for businesses just starting up and for small businesses. Take a look at our new custom t-shirt line and start optimizing your advertising!
         </span>
        </p>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #008000; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           How To Increase Productivity In The Workplace
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Companies and businesses around the world are constantly thinking about how to focus their team and how to reach their fullest productivity, at least they should be. There are so many ways you can get your team to work harder and more proficiently like perhaps yelling at them to do so. JUST KIDDING! Yelling is among the worst ways to get your workers to do a better job. This is called the fight-or-flight method, a study done by psychologists for many decades, has shown that this method will make the person that senses danger be able to fight better however they will stop thinking. This method works well if you want to scare your employees away or make them turn into Mike Tyson, but doesnt help with productivity.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #008000; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           Productivity Apps Show Promise
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          If you really want your employees to work their hardest and become dedicated to every single task, productivity apps may show promise. We live in a very technological age and many people carry around smart phones. This can turn into something great for your company. If you go to your app store and search productivity, there are endless amounts of apps you can find to help better your productivity levels. Even though there are many, I wanted to figure out which ones are the best and what they can do for a company. All of the apps i have chosen show they can be strongly effective in different areas of productivity.
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #008000; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            30/30 Productivity App
            <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity.jpg">
             <img alt="company-productivity " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5344" height="149" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity.jpg" width="188"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          This app from Binary Hammer can be used on both the iPhone and the iPad. If youre a major procrastinator and you know it, this app was made for you. 30/30 is effortless to use and understand. When you first open the app you will be greeted by a clock counting down the time it should take you to finish your next task. You can apply as many tasks as you need to and select the time you want to complete them in. Once you decide and finalize your tasks, its time to lock them in and press the Go button. This app will get your heart racing and wanting to beat the clock, and in turn finish work.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity1.jpg">
           <img alt="company-productivity " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5345" height="177" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity1.jpg" width="284"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Use this app for absolutely any task in the workplace, maybe returning customer phone calls, or finishing a blog post. No matter what the task is, this app will push you to get it done. Just by selecting your tasks and setting a time, gives you enough pressure to want to get it done. What you thought was a long time will turn into not enough if you lose focus. 30/30 makes you not only work faster, but makes you realize how precious time is and to learn how to get things done without falling into those endless distractions. If you want to stop procrastinating and get daily tasks done with proficiency, this app will force you to be productive. Another plus with this app, its FREE! This means all of your employees can use this app and have no excuses.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">

There are so many options for customization and that is the part I loved. When creating a new task you can label it, color the label, and set an icon to it. This allows the user to easily differentiate and understand what he or she needs to do now, what is coming up, and also provides a more personal experience.
         </span>
        </p>

          <strong>
           <span style="color: #008000; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">

          </strong>

        </ul>

This is great for companies to have because being able to share viable information instantly, no matter where you are is a necessity. Many note taking and sharing apps require you to go outside of the app, not Huddle. They capture the essence of productivity by allowing the user to stay within the app. They wanted to give the user an outlet to really become productive and not be susceptible to outside app distractions, making the app stray away from being productive. This app is available on both iOS and Android apps, users can also download the app onto a desktop. If you have or run a company being able to access an app on various mediums is a plus.

           <img alt="company-productivity" class=" size-full wp-image-5347 alignright" height="174" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity2.jpg" width="290"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>

         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">

What makes Huddle extremely functional is its capability to integrate with many other applications such as Tibbr, Photoshop, Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and SharePoint. Keep you up-to-date within your network and be able to view new documents and files as they are submitted. The reason I thought this app was better than the rest was its ability to allow users to stay within the app along with being able to use it with many programs. Huddle also creates personal notifications so you know exactly when something is associated with you, so you can respond quicker. Huddle also comes with an agenda, this means you can stay current with your daily tasks and never miss a beat.
         </span>

         </li>

         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          The reason I loved this app is because its different and in any company you will have employees that think different. If your company has designers and artist they might want to actually draw things out and not type. This app provides more productivity for those who think creatively and need to take notes or jot down ideas.

Inkflow lets you draw seamlessly without any glitches. Users can have the freedom to re-size the images using a selection tool. What is really neat about this is normally when you re-size an image you lose quality, with Inkflow youre able to transform the sizing of images and still obtain crisp and clear quality.

The app allows you to zoom in and zoom out to change and edit details. This app is for the creative minds and makes it effortless to share with other employees.

You will always get reminded when adeadline is approaching, so you never are late or miss an important task.

Having many choices was the main reason this app stood out to me along with app structure. Set reminders, look at group folders, communicate with employees, have access from anywhere, and allow all employees to keep up with tough schedules by creating notifications.           This last app is perfect for the organization aspect of your company, because in order to be productive you must be organized.
          <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity8.jpg">
           <img alt="company-productivity " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5357" height="203" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/company-productivity8.jpg" width="248"/>
          </a>
          DropBox is an app that keeps documents and files all in one place and allows groups to be created so they can all view and add files. Do you carry a USB or hard drive? Well, thanks to DropBox you dont have to anymore. Anything that you copy into DropBox is instantly uploaded online and saved. So, if youre using your iPhone you can view it and if you switch over to your iPad you can view it. No matter where you are you can view your documents and start working on it.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          This app is highly useful and is extremely clear and easy to understand. You can use DropBox to upload your photo stream, share documents, share images, delete or move files, and edit from wherever you may be.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          These apps are the perfect way to get your employees engaged and also doing more work, faster. In this day and age its all about technology and when companies imply that into the workplace, it might just make all the difference. Remember for all of your business printing needs choose Overnight Prints for the
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/" target="_blank">
           ultimate printing experience.
          </a>

         What To Consider When Creating A Logo And How Our Design Services Can Help
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/what-to-consider-when-creating-a-logo-and-how-our-design-services-can-help/" rel="bookmark" title="8:29 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          June 12, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">

        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">

         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
          When you think of logos you most-likely think of the great ones, the ones that are memorable, and stick with you. When you start thinking about
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-maker" target="_blank">
           creating a logo
          </a>
          for your company remember that it has to be unique enough to make a lasting impression. Creating a one-of-a-kind logo is not by any means easy, it requires time, patience, and many steps. Before you start creating your very own logo make sure you dont forget to consider these key points.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ac37de; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          1.) What colors best suit your company?
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Colors are a way of expression and they bring out emotions in everyone. All colors bring out different emotions and you need to choose colors that represent what your company stands for. If you choose red as your color, it will be active, passionate, and intense. One example is Red Bull, they use the color red and its a perfect choice for their company since Red Bull is supposed to supply their customers with energy which will keep them active throughout the day.
           </span>
          </span>
          <br/>
          <hr/>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           So just what do these colors express? Here is an infographic to further explain some of the most popular colors.
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-maker" target="_blank">
          <img alt="logo-design " class="aligncenter wp-image-5305" height="690" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design7.jpg" width="276"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; color: #ac37de;">
          2.) Look at your selection of fonts
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           Fonts are very similar to colors because they also make us feel a range of emotions. If your company is energeticand fun you will want to go with a font that expresses those emotions. If your company is supposed to express strength and being bold such as Under Armour, its probably best to not choose a cursive, floral font. Selecting a font is just as important as the color because of the emotion factor. Its all about matching who and what your company is with a font that can convey that message instantly to your customer.
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ac37de; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          3.) Understanding the many different logo types
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           This can be a bit tricky because there are so many types of logos and many aspects to consider. You have to examine how it will look on all of your products, your overall company collateral, and last but not least consider the emotional response your customers will have. The emotional response all comes back to choosing the right colors and fonts. A company logo isnt just a symbol, its the core of the company. In many cases its the first thing your customers see and you want them to respond in a positive way. Lets go over some of the top types of logos.
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #3366ff; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          1. Lettermark Logo
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Lettermarks are using the initials of the company or the first letter of the company. Many companies such as HP and Chanel use this method because its more appealing and using the whole name would be too long, or the name is hard to pronounce. These are all reasons why companies would choose the Lettermark type of logo. Take ABC for example, its full company name is American Broadcasting Company, using the full name is too
         </span>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          long.
         </span>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Creating a lettermark for certain companies makes it easier to remember.
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-design" target="_blank">
          <img alt="logo-design " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5288" height="768" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design5.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; color: #3366ff;">
          2. Symbol Logo
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Using a symbol is harder to come up with but often times creates more of an impact. If youre planning on having a pretty big business having a symbol logo is the way to go. The reason is symbol logos are much easier for people to remember because they are simple and unique enough. The fun part about symbol Logos is that you can create clever twist within them that point out what your company does. Just take companies like
         </span>
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          theGoodwill and Baskin Robbins for Example.
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design.jpg">
          <img alt="logo-design " class=" wp-image-5280 alignleft" height="376" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design.jpg" width="289"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design1.jpg">
          <img alt="logo-design " class=" wp-image-5281 alignright" height="305" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design1.jpg" width="257"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #3366ff; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          3. Wordmark Logo
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          These are going to be your custom designed fonts that spell out the whole name of the company. These typically use a creative arrangement that will leave not only a lasting impression but a logo that is unique. This type of logo solely represents the company on its use of creatively adding flare to the original name. This type of logo can be easily read and understood since they are using the exact company name.You have to be prepared to get innovative and think of something new, because you want the finished product to inspire your customers and one of the best examples up-to-date is Visa.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-maker" target="_blank">
          <img alt="logo-design" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5310" height="250" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Visa-logo.jpg" width="478"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #3366ff; font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          4. Emblem Logo
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          The emblem logo is continually growing. This logo uses the company name within a detailed design. Emblem logos give you the best of both worlds, meaning that you can use the full name of your company and a design that will also give you distinction. This type of logo is very popular because it combines the two, from famous ones to the newest they are all great.
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-maker" target="_blank">
          <img alt="logo-design " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5286" height="160" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design4.jpg" width="160"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design3.jpg">
          <img alt="logo-design" class=" wp-image-5285 aligncenter" height="203" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design3.jpg" width="351"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-maker" target="_blank">
          <img alt="logo-design " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5291" height="142" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/logo-design6.jpg" width="135"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5321" style="width: 1034px">
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-maker" target="_blank">

Colors and fonts are the first aspect of the logo you should think about. Perhaps these two characteristics are the most important. Before diving into logo design really do your research and apply it to your company and last but not least dont be afraid to use designers, and preferably
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/logo-design" target="_blank">

        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          If youre a small business we understand it might be a little difficult to gain new customers. Your business may need some form of advertising that sticks, is large in size, and attention grabbing. These three key factors are all ideal for advertising. Overnight Prints has a product that encompasses all three of these, what product? Our Custom Car Magnets!
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/carmagnets" target="_blank">
           I guess youre in luck.
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          No matter where you are if you have a small business, obtaining new customers and boosting your awareness is very high on the importance scale. There are many reasons why using Car Magnets are an absolute must for a small business but I will list five today.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/carmagnets" target="_blank">
          <img alt="car-magnet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5262" height="768" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/car-magnet.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #4c88a6;">
            Why You Should Use Car Magnets For Advertising
           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
           <strong>
            They are the perfect size.
           </strong>
           Yes, youcould use a vehicle wrapbut sometimes those look unprofessional and also will hurt the resale value of your car quite a bit, because they are permanent. Vehicle wraps are also pretty expensive. On the other hand you have bumper stickers which are cheap to make but lack the professionalism as well as visibility. Car Magnets are the perfect size to advertise your business. They are also removable so you can place them on different cars and it wont hurt the resale value.
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/carmagnets" target="_blank">
           <img alt="car-magnet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5264" height="768" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/car-magnet1.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
           <b>
            They can seamlessly fit your business.
           </b>
           Car Magnets can brandyour business by using different shapes and styles. Lets say youre a dentist and you want to advertise your new office. You could have a magnet shaped as a tooth to place on the side of your car with contact information and an address within it. You can take full control over shapes, colors, and sizes and make your brand stand out from the rest.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
           <strong>
            Overnight Prints makes magnets that can weather the storm.
           </strong>
           You have tobecareful here, dont just go anywhere to buy your car magnets because when youre caught in unfortunate weather you dont want your magnet to crumble. We use magnets that will look great through the years and have a strong durability. The magnets are made out of 12 mil super thick laminate that will last through all kinds of weather and withstand road wear. You can rest assured that you will receive top-quality because we Demand Perfection and offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee to all of our customers.
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/carmagnets" target="_blank">
           <img alt="car-magnet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5265" height="768" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/car-magnet2.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
           <strong>
            You can transfer them to other vehicles.
           </strong>
           This is great when you have multiple cars and you need to switch. You can easily remove it and transfer it over to another vehicle in minutes.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
           <strong>
            Cost-efficient price for advertising.
           </strong>
           Our
           <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/carmagnets" target="_blank">
            car magnets are very affordable
           </a>
           and are made with quality in mind. You are getting superior quality without having to spend an arm and a leg. There is nothing like getting to advertise your business without spending a fortune.
          </span>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          With Overnight Prints Custom Car Magnets you are getting a great quality magnet that is also cost-efficient. If you are a small business and are looking for cheap and effective ways to advertise your business Car Magnets do both. Take your business with you wherever you go and start building a larger and stronger customer base for your small business. Go to
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/" target="_blank">
           Overnightprints.com
          </a>
          to start creating your Custom Car Magnets today!
         </span>
        </p>

       <h2 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/stand-out-from-the-crowd-by-using-spot-uv/" rel="bookmark">
         Stand Out From The Crowd By Using Spot-UV
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/stand-out-from-the-crowd-by-using-spot-uv/" rel="bookmark" title="10:03 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          June 8, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">
          Jordaan Sanford
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Are you tired of using the same standard looking print products? If you are, all it takes is
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
           one simple addition
          </a>
          to completely change the look of your business card. Im talking about Spot-UV. If you have never heard of Spot-UV, its a shiny added layer applied to a design or portion of print products such as business cards, postcards, and posters.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Spot-UV will give attention and dramatize an important part of your card or product in which you want to emphasize. Any time light hits the Spot-UV it shines, shimmers, and stands out. For instance, if you have your company logo, company name, or design you can put the spotlight onto this and bring it out by giving that section a high-gloss, very luminous effect.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          We were extremely happy to have the chance to work with Ben Kwok, who is an amazing visual designer and illustrator. If you want to know more about Ben, please read our previous blog post entitled
          <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/artistic-genius-ben-kwok-speaks/" target="_blank">
           Artistic Genius Ben Kwok Speaks.
          </a>
          In todays blog we used Bens outstanding and highly intricate work to showcase the strong and eye-catching effect of Spot-UV.
         </span>
         <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-151.jpg">
          <img alt="spot-uv-bioworkz" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5172" height="300" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-151-277x300.jpg" width="277"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Spot-Gloss-Tiger-White-150ppi.jpg">
          <img alt="spot-uv-bioworkz" class="aligncenter wp-image-5242" height="495" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Spot-Gloss-Tiger-White-150ppi.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          We took Bens Tiger Helm design and placed it on one of our 4 x 6 postcards. As you can see the difference from photo 1 and photo 2 is that the Tiger Helm is shining and is really highlighting the detail of the design. We loved this illustration because you can see the Spot-UV reflect the light and shine off the design while the rest of the card is standard, and does not have gloss. The design as you can see completely stands out from the card and immediately grasps your attention. Spot-UV is a very unique finishing gloss because it can
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/postcards" target="_blank">
           point out the detail of any design.
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
          <img alt="spot-uv-bioworkz" class=" wp-image-5241 aligncenter" height="445" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Spot-Gloss-Owl-White-150ppi.jpg" width="556"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          This is the back of our standard business card. The owl is coated with a layer of luscious Spot-UV. The Owl against the white card completely stands out and exposes the very fine details of the illustration, and all it takes is a splash of our Spot-UV. The back of any business card is extremely valuable. The reason is you have all of your contact information in the front, so no need to state the same on the back. Show who you are and your personality on the back side and you will have yourself an unforgettable business card. Since Ben is an illustrator its important for him to of course showcase his art and work and the back of the card does it nicely. While Spot-UV brings the card to life it also protects it from wear and tear and keeps it looking new.Dont let the back of your business card go to waste.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/postcards" target="_blank">
          <img alt="spot-uv-bioworkz" class="aligncenter wp-image-5245" height="364" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/5-x7-postcard.jpg" width="273"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
          <img alt="spot-uv-bioworkz" class=" size-full wp-image-5247 aligncenter" height="1401" src="http://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Bioworkz_postcardsnew1.jpg" width="2100"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Spot-UV isnt only for business cards, it can be used for an array of printing products and as we can see it greatly enhances the overall look.TheOrnate Elephant, was placed on our 5 x 7 postcard and also is using Spot-UV. As you can see in the first image of theOrnate Elephant, is shining quite a bit. If youre looking for something out of the ordinary and highly impressive, Spot-UV can enhance anything from a company name to a highly intricate design such as the illustrations we used from visual designer and illustrator Ben Kwok. Start using our one-of-a-kind Spot-UV today and
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/postcards" target="_blank">
           see the difference.
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>

        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/establishing-a-design-establishes-your-company/" rel="bookmark">
         Establishing A Design, Establishes Your Company.
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/establishing-a-design-establishes-your-company/" rel="bookmark" title="12:39 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          June 4, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">
          Jordaan Sanford
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Design is what generates appealing and eye-catching products. If you have an amazing product but fail to use quality design most people will lose interest and begin looking elsewhere. Design is art and it not only compliments a well-made product but makes it more engaging and draws customers in.
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          A great product or business will not reach its fullest potential without design work. In any business its important to create and
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/designservices" target="_blank">
           establish a design
          </a>
          that is unique and meaningful for that specific company. Overnight Prints will work with each customer and talk about what their company means to them and what they want to convey to customers. Then, they will come up with a design that can build up a product or business.
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/businesscards" target="_blank">
           <img alt="design services" class="aligncenter wp-image-5193" height="314" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/design-services3-300x200.jpg" width="472"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px;">
          <b>
           What goes into a full design service.
          </b>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #808080; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           If you want to
          </span>
          <a href="https://www.overnightprints.com/designservices" target="_blank">
           re-create your business from top to bottom
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           the full design service from Overnight Prints which is called, Build Your Brand With Us, will do just that. If you need a simple design revision or a full design for your brand, Overnight Prints does it all. You can choose from three different design service options for your specific needs. If you have any questions and are interested in our design services just email our team and give a couple of brief details of what youre looking for at designservices@overnightprints.com. Once you email you will be receiving a response shortly after from one of our expert designers.
          </span>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Logo Design
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/designservices" target="_blank">
           <img alt="design services" class=" size-medium wp-image-5183 alignleft" height="200" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/design-services-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
          <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/design-services.jpg">
           <img alt="design services" class=" size-medium wp-image-5183 alignnone" height="200" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/design-services-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h3>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Work with designers to achieve a logo that seamlessly integrates what your company stands for and the message you want to convey. Build a logo that will capture attention, represents your brand, and serves as a symbol of your standards and company values. Our team will work with you to create a logo that not only represents your business or company accurately but a logo that has a timeless quality. If you have an idea or concept our design team can bring it to life.
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Create a Web Design
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Having an online presence that looks great and is true to your company can effect your company tremendously. Capture potential customers and existing customers full attention by showcasing your products and business in a beautiful and simplistic light.
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Implement clear communication between you and your customer, establish credibility, and engage your audience within your
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/web-design" target="_blank">
           websites artistry.
          </a>
          If you have an idea or a vision of what you want your website to look like we can make it come to fruition. If you have a great product or business let the world know about it and have easy access to view your products and services whenever they want.
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Marketing Collateral
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          Having a fluid and flowing design throughout all of your products makes your business look more reliable, professional, and appealing.
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h4>
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/marketing" target="_blank">
           <img alt="design services" class=" size-medium wp-image-5187 aligncenter" height="200" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/design-services1-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          If you have completely different designs this can possibly throw the customer off and make your business look disorganized and unprofessional. Overnight Prints can make brochures, postcards, posters, and more all match with cohesive designs. Build your credibility by presenting your customers with a beautiful and efficient look and feel to all of your marketing products. You may be surprised with how much well-designed marketing tools contribute to making great impressions.
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h3>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
          If you have an idea or a design in mind and need help creating it, let our expert designers guide you through the process. Our design experts can help you create a strong and effective marketing collateral for your business and make it unique and meaningful to your company. If you dont have marketing tools that showcase your business and what it has to offer, customers will not take you seriously and rightfully so. Marketing collateral of course can boost sales but it also can tell the story of your business. In this day and age many people read reviews because they want to hear of others experiences and if they should trust a product or company. By placing a clients testimonial within your collateral shows that youre trustworthy and supply an excellent service or product.
          <a href="http://www.overnightprints.com/letterhead" target="_blank">
           <img alt="design services" class=" wp-image-5188 alignright" height="212" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/design-services2-300x200.jpg" width="318"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          Marketing collateral has the potential to carry any brand or business because it connects the buyer and the seller. Effective marketing collateral creates an impression and has a strong influence over purchasing decisions. There are so many businesses out there that dont realize or understand what a paramount and strategic asset it can be. They neglect showcasing their past customer experiences and what they have done as a company. These companies are stagnate and arent shaping the buyers perceptions. The door is open for you and your business to take advantage of this when others are not.
         </span>
        </h3>

      <div class="post-5152 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-everything-print-las-vegas category-postcards category-welcome-bloggers tag-art tag-ben-kwok tag-business-cards tag-design tag-illustration tag-overnight-prints tag-postcards" id="post-5152">
       <h2 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/artistic-genius-ben-kwok-speaks/" rel="bookmark">
         Artistic Genius Ben Kwok Speaks
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/artistic-genius-ben-kwok-speaks/" rel="bookmark" title="10:23 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          June 3, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.overnightprints.com/author/janell-awong/" title="View all posts by Jordaan Sanford">
          Jordaan Sanford
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           There is something about art and designing that captures the heart and makes us feel in awe, and that is what Ben Kwok provided us. He is a graphic artist and illustrator who was born in Taiwan but currently lives in Los Angeles, California. He is passionate about his work and has an undying love for art that is transferred over into every drawing and every illustration that he creates. However, getting to where he is now took some time and took overcoming many obstacles. He loves creating pieces that are filled with complexity and losing himself in the flow of his art. His highly detailed work is what stunned us along with his close attention to detail.
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           We are thrilled that we had the opportunity to interview the artistic genius, and also combine his beautiful artwork with some of our products which will be in our next blog.
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="font-family: Verdana, Geneva;">
           <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            What made Ben Kwok become anillustrator?
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <h4>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           I remember vividly at the age of 7 I wanted to be a veterinarian or a cartoonist. I wanted to be a vet because I absolutely love animals and wanted to work with them for a living. Who knew there was so much education that was required to become a vet and then there was the option of being a cartoonist
          </span>
         </h4>
        </blockquote>
        <h6>
         <span style="font-family: Verdana, Geneva;">
          <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-153.jpg">
           <br/>
          </a>
          <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Ornate-Lion.jpg">
           <br/>
          </a>
          <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-151.jpg">
           <img alt="bioworkz-15" class=" size-medium wp-image-5172 alignleft" height="300" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-151-277x300.jpg" width="277"/>
          </a>
          <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-152.jpg">
           <img alt="bioworkz-15" class=" size-medium wp-image-5173 alignleft" height="300" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-152-219x300.jpg" width="219"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </h6>
        <h6>
        </h6>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <blockquote>
         <h4>
         </h4>
         <h4>
          I actually really liked comic books at the time, but I didnt know the difference so I just went with cartoonist. Fast forward to college, I was still not sure what I wanted to do, so I started off as a Graphic Designer. After my first semester at school, I hated it all. I wanted to draw and not play with fonts and layouts. Thats when I discovered ILLUSTRATION; I never looked back. I get to actually draw with traditional mediums, pencil on paper, paint on canvas, everything made sense. Besides, drawing was the only thing I was good at, so it was easy for me to make the decision to do something art related.
         </h4>
         <h4>
         </h4>
        </blockquote>
        <h4>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          <strong>
           What are some of the most memorable designs/art you have made? And what made them so memorable?
           <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-154.jpg">
            <img alt="bioworkz-15" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5175" height="300" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-154-245x300.jpg" width="245"/>
           </a>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <blockquote>
         <h4>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           <strong>
            The Ornate Elephant
           </strong>
           is my most memorable illustration. I keep telling this story, but I feel like its important to share. To back track a bit I hated my job as a graphic artist at a company I will not mention. So I decided to do something about it and look for another job. I was able to find another job and started working for a new company. After only 1 week they decided to lay me off. I was so angry, bitter, lost, and just beaten up by the apparel industry. Since I had no plan B, I decided to just draw for fun. To draw whatever I wanted, whenever I wanted and however I wanted with no one to answer to. Thats what gave birth to my Ornate Elephant drawing. I decided to post it online and thats when it started getting traction. People really liked the illustration so I decided to focus all of my attention into this style of artwork. What made the Ornate Elephant most memorable was the fact that I drew something for myself and I got to fall in love with art all over again. Even when I was unemployed, had no options, I was so happy and in peace because I found my passion again.
          </span>
         </h4>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         <a href="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-155.jpg">
          <img alt="bioworkz-15" class=" size-medium wp-image-5176 aligncenter" height="300" src="https://blog.overnightprints.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bioworkz-155-246x300.jpg" width="246"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
          <strong>
           Talk about your influence by H.R Giger and Alfonse Mucha?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <blockquote>
         <h4>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           I fell in love with H.R. Giger while I was in high school and discovered Alfonse Mucha in college. I really love the dark complex images that Giger created.
          </span>
         </h4>
         <h4>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
           The fusion of machine and flesh really spoke to me. I love how much detail, texture, and darkness he puts into his artwork. His style was known as Bio-mechanics, so since I loved his work so much, I decided to take on the moniker BIOWORKZ, because thats what all the cool kids were doing back in the day. Then I stumbled upon Alfonse Muchas work and it totally blew my mind. His work is almost the exact opposite of Gigers work. It has lots of rich colors, soft curving lines, with lots of focus on the flow of the lines. Muchas work inspired me to play with different line widths and the way every line flows seamlessly into each other. Both Giger and Mucha are masters at what they do and I would like to reach their greatness one day. Giger brought us Bio-mechanics, Mucha brought us Art nouveau, and I would like to be known as the person that brought Ornate artwork into the mainstream.
          </span>
         </h4>
        </blockquote>
