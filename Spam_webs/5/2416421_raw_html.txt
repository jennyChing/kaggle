       <p>
        <!-- Disney Imagicademy: Creative Arts / Clever Girls Snippet -->
       </p>
       <p class="none">
        <script data-cfasync="false" src="http://member.clevergirlscollective.com/track?u=11488&amp;g=979" type="text/javascript">
        </script>
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.clevergirlscollective.com/pixel/p.png?a=campaign&amp;gid=979&amp;uid=11488" style="display: none; width: 1px; height: 1px;"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <!-- END Clever Girls Snippet -->
       </p>
       <p>
        Do you let your younger kids play learning apps on the iPad? I think its a great learning tool, especially for young children. We recently found
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1xGcWdd" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Disney Imagicademy
        </a>
        . Its a new learning app designed for families with children ages 3 to 8 to inspire learning through creating, making and doing. Their latest app is
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1CjjySB" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Mickeys Magical Arts World
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/disney-mickey-label.jpg">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5007" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/disney-mickey-label.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        In
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1CjjySB" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Mickeys Magical Arts World
        </a>
        , children are invited to join Mickey and his friends in the most imaginative place around, as they explore a fully immersive world of creative arts.
        <span id="more-5000">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0099.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5009" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0099.png"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Explore storytelling, improvisation, costume design, character development and role play with Donald; Consider concepts of rhythm, tempo, patterns and melodies with Mickeys Musical Marching Band; Experiment with symmetry, balance and proportion as they design buildings with Minnie; and play with oodles of virtual arts and crafts with Goofy!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0101.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5017" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0101.png"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        My youngest just LOVES it. She has a wild imagination, and I love seeing the new things she creates using this app. There is really so much they can do with this one. She can play with musical instruments, create crazy buildings, or make animals out all sorts of materials and textures. Herabsolutefavorite is creating movies.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0102.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5014" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0102.png"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Children can create their own characters that can star in animated movies alongside beloved Disney characters like Mickey and Minnie, while they experiment with line, shapes and texture in Mickeys Drawing Studio. Your creation even goes to the hair salon, which is so much fun!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0103.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5015" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0103.png"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Molly likes the magic movie for her creations to star in the best. Here her character is taking a bow after Mickeymade her disappear.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5008" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0098.png"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Disney Imagicademy is the first comprehensive brand of interactive and connected early learning experiences that combines the Disney characters and stories families love, with a research-driven curriculum developed in conjunction with top academics and education experts.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0105.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5018" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_0105.png"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        To enhance your childs experience, theres is also a free follow along companion app for parents 
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1wnGVrg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         <i>
          Disney Imagicademy Parents
         </i>
        </a>
        . Not only does it offer tips for your child to play with the app, it offers so much more!The app will give parents their daily dose of, What can I do today to help my child grow?
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_7655.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class=" wp-image-5013 aligncenter" height="861" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_7655.png" width="484"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        There are ideas for you to make fun imaginative play things for your kids. I just love the crafty ideas that are shared every day. This sandwich idea out of melted beads was my absolute favorite! I cant wait to head to the craft store for the supplies.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_7656.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class=" wp-image-5011 aligncenter" height="861" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_7656.png" width="484"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Theres also ideas for fun art projects your child can make, too! Plus, there areways to share your childs creations with friends and family.
       </p>
       <p>
        My daughter flipped over this caterpillar idea, and now I have a use for all those paper towel tubes Ive been saving.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_7653.png">
         <img alt="Disney Imagicademy Mickey's Magical Arts World App | The TipToe Fairy #DisneyImagicademy #CG" class=" wp-image-5012 aligncenter" height="861" src="http://thetiptoefairy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_7653.png" width="484"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Theres going to be much more to
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1xGcWdd" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Disney Imagicademy
        </a>
        coming soon. They will also have books and other physical products that will be available for back-to-school 2015. The apps and products cover key subject matters that are important for early childhood development, including math, creative arts, science, reading and social and emotional skills.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1CjjySB" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Mickeys Magical Arts World
        </a>
        is now available on the App Store.
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1wnGVrg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Disney Imagicademy
        </a>
        apps are available exclusively for iPad and can currently be found on Apples App Store in the Kids, Education and Entertainment categories. Your kids will love it. You can download Mickeys Magical Arts World
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1CjjySB" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         HERE
        </a>
        and the parents app
        <a href="http://clvr.li/1wnGVrg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         HERE
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        What are your favorite apps for your children to learn and grow?
       </p>
