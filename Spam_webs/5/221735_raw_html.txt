          <p>
           <a href="http://www.flooringamerica.com/" rel="dofollow">
            Flooring
           </a>
           is one of the biggest and more important additions you can make when it comes to home design. Flooring can either make or break a room, so its important to thoroughly consider the many flooring materials available before making a decision. Below are a few of the most basic flooring products.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="SAMSUNG CAMERA PICTURES" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6861" height="422" src="http://www.tidbitsofexperience.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Area-Rug-3.jpg" width="466"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Carpet
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           Carpet is one of the single most popular choices for homeowners for a myriad of reasons. The biggest reason being comfort. Carpet also adds a lot of beauty to any room, and comes in a large selection of colors and styles.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Hardwood
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           The beauty of hardwood is undeniable. Hardwood flooring is a timeless flooring option that never goes out of style. Hardwood is also one of the most durable flooring options available. With proper care, hardwood flooring can last an entire lifetime, and usually gets more beautiful with age.
          </p>
          <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
          </div>
          <p>
           <b>
            Laminate
           </b>
          </p>
          <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
          </div>
          <p>
           Laminate floors are growing in popularity as they are an excellent, and more affordable substitute for hardwood floors. These floors are durable, easy to install, and look great.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Vinyl
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           Todays vinyl flooring is designed to look like more expensive flooring products. Vinyl floors are perhaps the most durable and cost effective flooring solutions on the market.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Tile
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           Tile flooring is classic, beautiful, and incredibly low maintenance. With a large selection of styles and choices, tile flooring can add a lot of beauty to your home.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What do you find to be your favorite type of flooring?
