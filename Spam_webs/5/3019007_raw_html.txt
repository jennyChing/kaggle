        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Hot Spiced Apple Cider Oatmeal in the Slow Cooker
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="time">
          October 29, 2013
         </span>
         <span class="author">
          by
          <a href="http://eatathomecooks.com/author/sacagawea" rel="author" title="Posts by Tiffany">
           Tiffany
          </a>
         </span>
         <a class="post-edit-link">
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
          <a class="external" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Featathomecooks.com%2F2013%2F10%2Fhot-spiced-apple-cider-oatmeal-slow-cooker.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Featathomecooks.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F10%2FHot-Spiced-Apple-Cider-Slow-Cooker-Oatmeal.jpg&amp;description=Hot%20Spiced%20Apple%20Cider%20Oatmeal%20in%20the%20Slow%20Cooker" rel="nobox" target="_blank">
           <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="pf-content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://eatathomecooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Hot-Spiced-Apple-Cider-Slow-Cooker-Oatmeal.jpg">
            <img alt="Hot Spiced Apple Cider Slow Cooker Oatmeal" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11104" height="750" src="http://eatathomecooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Hot-Spiced-Apple-Cider-Slow-Cooker-Oatmeal.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I am on a roll with slow cooker oatmeal. Weve been loving it lately!
          </p>
          <p>
           This time, I decided to make use of the apple cider in the fridge. A mug of hot spiced cider is a winter favorite. So it has to be good for a late autumn breakfast too, right?
          </p>
          <p>
           Sweet apple cider, brown sugar, cinnamon and other spices transform a plain bowl of oatmeal into holiday breakfast special.
          </p>
          <p>
           We dont get snowed in here in Kentucky very often, but Im going to keep this breakfast in mind just in case we get a big snowfall this winter. I cant think of anything more comforting than waking up to world wrapped in white and having a bowl of this spiced oatmeal to go with the view. Cozy!
          </p>
          <p>
           I dont have an ingredient photo for you, but all the ingredients are very common.
          </p>

            <div class="ERSSavePrint">

             Hot Spiced Apple Cider Oatmeal in the Slow Cooker

           </div>
           <div class="er-recipe-details">
            <div class="er-half-width er-float-left">
            </div>
            <div class="er-half-width er-float-left" itemprop="nutrition" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/NutritionInformation">
             <p>
              Serves:
              <span>
               4-6 servings
              </span>
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
           <div class="er-photo-details">
            <div class="er-photo-container">
             <img itemprop="image" src="http://eatathomecooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Hot-Spiced-Cider-Slow-Cooker-Oatmeal1.jpg"/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSIngredients">
            <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 cup steel cut oats
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 cups apple cider
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 cups water
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               cup brown sugar (or less, if you don't like it too sweet)
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 tsp. cinnamon
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               tsp. allspice
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               tsp. cloves
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              pinch of nutmeg
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSInstructions">
            <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spray a small slow cooker with cooking spray.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Combine all ingredients in crock.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Cook 3-5 hours on low.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Serve with milk.
             </li>
            </ol>
