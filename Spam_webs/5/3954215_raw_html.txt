       <div class="post-title">
        <h2>
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/top-data-center-events-and-conferences-usa/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to 37 Top 2015 Data Center Events and Conferences in the USA">
          37 Top 2015 Data Center Events and Conferences in the USA
         </a>
        </h2>
       </div>
       <!--/post-title -->
       <div class="post-date">
        <em>
         On 05.06.15, In
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/cloud-computing-news/" rel="category tag">
          Cloud Computing
         </a>
         , by
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/author/william/" rel="author" title="Posts by William Toll">
          William Toll
         </a>
        </em>
       </div>
       <p>
        At the hub of nearly every organization is a data center. There are now 8.6 million data centers worldwide. As the worlds businesses and citizens demand more technology and connectedness, data centers and data center managers must be able to keep up. Companies and their system administrators now are facing the challenge of storing unparalleled amounts of data. This translates to budget issues, security challenges, and time-consuming management activities that corporations may or may not consideras demand for capacity increases.To top it off, data center operators face enormous challenges in maintaining data security and preventing data theft and loss. Data Center events help data center managers find solutions they can implement today and in the future.
       </p>
       <p>
        The critical nature of data centers and the fact that there are new decisions facing corporations as the public cloud computing deployment model rapidly becomes the new normal to run existing and new workloads, places more eyes and budget on all things related to the data center. We here at ProfitBricks have compiled our list of top data center events and conferences in the USA, in order to help you connect with thought leaders and industry experts so that you can manage your data centers and understand the connection to the cloud a little more easily. We also included events that have concluded for 2015, so that you can mark your calendars and plan to attend in 2016.
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Please note, we have listed our top data center events and conferences in the USA
         <strong>
          in no particular order
         </strong>
         .
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         1.
         <a href="http://www.hpcuserforum.com/" target="_blank">
          HPC User Forum
          <br/>
         </a>
        </strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/idchpcuserforum" target="_blank">
         @idchpcuserforum
         <br/>
        </a>
        September 8-10
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Denver (Broomfield), CO
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/HPC-User-Forum.png">
         <img alt="HPC User Forum" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5941" height="175" src="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/HPC-User-Forum-300x175.png" width="300"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        In 1999, market analyst firm IDC and users and vendors in the technical the high-performance computing (HPC) industry created the HPC User Forum, a service that identifies buyer/user requirements and vendor capabilities within the market. HPC User Forum holds two full-membership meetings per year in the United States, plus two annual meetings in international locations. Typically held in April and September, the HPC User Forum events include networking opportunities and technical sessions that address issues of importance to the HPC industry, including data and data centers.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Cost to Attend:
        </strong>
        Contact for registration price
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         2.
         <a href="http://cloudslam.org/" target="_blank">
          CloudSlam
          <br/>
         </a>
        </strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/CloudSlam" target="_blank">
         @CloudSlam
         <br/>
        </a>
        Date TBD
        <br/>
        <b>
         Location TBD
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CloudSlam.png">
         <img alt="CloudSlam" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5932" height="179" src="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CloudSlam-300x179.png" width="300"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        CloudSlam is a premier cloud computing event and has evolved to become an international network of cloud computing thought leaders that links thousands of organizations and individuals from around the world. Attendees connect with cloud and business specialists and learn how to integrate their data centers and cloud strategy.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Cost to Attend:

        Contact for registration price
       </p>

       <p>
        <strong>
         3.
         <a href="http://puppetconf.com" target="_blank">
          PuppetConf
          <br/>
