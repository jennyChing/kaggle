        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         ROASTED GREEN BEANS WITH BACON A MUST TRY!
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-05-16T06:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 16, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.stonegableblog.com/author/yvonne/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            StoneGable

         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

           27 Comments

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-R-lRfvhxDwk/U3UEKCgyb_I/AAAAAAAAqcA/v0Mok_g0vqM/s1600/ROASTED+GREEN+BEANS+WITH+BACON-TITLE+PAGE-stonegableblog.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-R-lRfvhxDwk/U3UEKCgyb_I/AAAAAAAAqcA/v0Mok_g0vqM/s1600/ROASTED+GREEN+BEANS+WITH+BACON-TITLE+PAGE-stonegableblog.jpg" width="426"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div>
           There should be a disclaimer with this recipe
           <i>
            Please dont think this is just another green bean recipe with bacon. It is so much more than that! If you dont try these you will be missing out on one of lifes little culinary miracles
           </i>
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           I could eat these green bean everyday! They are that good! And if truth be told, Im not a natural born veggie lover! Most carnivores like me arent. Vegetables need to be spruced up a bit for me to love them and these roasted green beans with bacon are at the top of my food to eat list!
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           Theres a little secret to making theses beans fabulous and Im sharing it with you today
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-i5sYncyMlAU/U3UFSWHzOQI/AAAAAAAAqcI/zF_vLStWVjE/s1600/roasted-green-beans-close+up-stonegableblog.com.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-i5sYncyMlAU/U3UFSWHzOQI/AAAAAAAAqcI/zF_vLStWVjE/s1600/roasted-green-beans-close+up-stonegableblog.com.jpg" width="422"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div>
           Just looking at these make me hungry for them!!!
          </div>
          <div>
           These beans are insanely easy to make and are over-the-top delicious!
          </div>
         </div>
         <div>
          Dont forget to make them with bacon!
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          <div>
           <img alt="" border="0" class=" aligncenter" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-DWbIZVDotoo/U3UHWD_PeoI/AAAAAAAAqcQ/_8yMLHW7W2M/s1600/Roasted-green-beans-with+bacon-bacon-stonegableblog.com.jpg" width="422"/>
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div>
           And roast the green beans in the bacon fat. YUMMY!
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-2lhP29dRkao/U3UHygl4xcI/AAAAAAAAqcY/KX-3Bcd-LYA/s1600/roasted-green-beans-with+bacon-green+beans-stonegableblog.com.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-2lhP29dRkao/U3UHygl4xcI/AAAAAAAAqcY/KX-3Bcd-LYA/s1600/roasted-green-beans-with+bacon-green+beans-stonegableblog.com.jpg" width="422"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div>
           And top with crumbled bacon and diced tomatoes!
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-WLuU41fGnQg/U3UI1QigRjI/AAAAAAAAqco/hLPKEHK8h2g/s1600/roasted-green-beans-with+garnish-stonegablelbog.ocm.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-WLuU41fGnQg/U3UI1QigRjI/AAAAAAAAqco/hLPKEHK8h2g/s1600/roasted-green-beans-with+garnish-stonegablelbog.ocm.jpg" width="422"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe blog-yumprint-medium-image blog-yumprint-numbered-methods " itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe" yumprintrecipe="qaeS">
            <img class="blog-yumprint-google-image" itemprop="image" src="http://www.stonegableblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/ROASTED-GREEN-BEANS-WITH-BACON-TITLE-PAGE-stonegableblog1.jpg" style="display:block;position:absolute;left:-10000px;top:-10000px;"/>
            <div class="blog-yumprint-photo-top" style="background-image: url(http://www.stonegableblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/ROASTED-GREEN-BEANS-WITH-BACON-TITLE-PAGE-stonegableblog1.jpg)">

            <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-title" itemprop="name">
             ROASTED GREEN BEANS WITH BACON
            </div>
            <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-published" itemprop="datePublished">
             2015-05-14 18:46:20
            </div>
            <img class="blog-yumprint-photo-top-large" src="http://www.stonegableblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/ROASTED-GREEN-BEANS-WITH-BACON-TITLE-PAGE-stonegableblog1.jpg"/>
            <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-summary" itemprop="description">
             Crips oven roasted green beans infused with smoky bacon flavor.
            </div>
            <div class="blog-yumprint-header">
             <div class="blog-yumprint-stars-reviews" color="rgb(145,196,20)" count="0" emptycolor="#BEBEBE" highlightcolor="#a4de17" itemprop="aggregateRating" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/AggregateRating" rating="0">

              <div class="blog-yumprint-write-review">

             <div class="blog-yumprint-save blog-yumprint-action">

               Save Recipe

             <div class="blog-yumprint-print blog-yumprint-action">

             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="blog-yumprint-spacer">
            </div>
            <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-contents">
             <div class="blog-yumprint-photo-middle" style="background-image: url(http://www.stonegableblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/ROASTED-GREEN-BEANS-WITH-BACON-TITLE-PAGE-stonegableblog1.jpg)">
             </div>
             <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="0">
              <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
               Ingredients
              </div>
              <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
               <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
                8-10 slices of bacon (I use thick cut hickory bacon)
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
                1 lb fresh green beans ( I use haricot vert, a french green bean. I pronounce it like harry-corvair close enough Im not french!)
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
                tomatoes for garnish, optional
               </li>
              </ol>
             </div>
             <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="1">
              <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
               Instructions
              </div>
              <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
                Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
                Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil. Lay bacon in a single layer. Cook until crispy done. My bacon takes about 15-20 minutes.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="2">
                Remover the bacon to a paper towel lined dish.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="3">
                Trim the stem end of the beans, if needed. I only do this if they beans have been sitting in the frig for a couple of days.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="4">
                Put the green beans in the pan and roll them around to coat with bacon fat.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="5">
                Roast for 10 minutes with the rack in the center of the oven, or until the are still very crisp but have lost that raw green taste. Toss once during cooking.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="6">
                Turn on the broiler leave the rack where it is. Watch the beans so they dont burn, and broil for up to  4 minutes. This will give some a little char! YUMMY! *If there is too much bacon drippings you may want to spill it out before broiling so it does not catch on fire.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="7">
                Meanwhile, chop up a few pieces of bacon and tomatoes, if desired.
               </li>
               <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="8">
                Plate and put the bacon along the center of the green beans. Top with tomatoes in the same way.
               </li>
              </ol>
             </div>
