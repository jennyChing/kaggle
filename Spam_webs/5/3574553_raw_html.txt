              <h1 class="main-title single-title entry-title">
               Trend Alert! Add Personality to Your Home with Painted Furniture
              </h1>
             </div>
             <div class="authorship-row clearfix padded-panel">
              <div class="authorship type-both">
               <span class="author">
                by
                <a class="styled" href="http://blog.homes.com/author/sonya-barker/">
                 Sonya Barker
                </a>
               </span>
               <span class="date">
                September 11, 2014
               </span>
              </div>
             </div>
             <style type="text/css">
              #rating-anchor-wrapper {display:none;}
             </style>
             <div class="the-content padded-panel">
              <div id="content-anchor">
              </div>
              <div class="clearfix" id="content-anchor-inner">
               <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
                <img alt="Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trends-Collage" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-94834" height="672" src="http://blog-cdn.static-homes.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/09/Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trends-Collage.jpg" width="600"/>
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr">
                One of the biggest, and probably the most wonderful trends in home dcor is painted furniture. Painted furniture is great because it can be adapted to any style and any room; plus, it works really well for adding personality to your home or apartment. You still get to have colorful options that are not permanent changes to your space.
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
                <img alt="Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-Coffee-Table" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-94831" height="397" src="http://blog-cdn.static-homes.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/09/Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-Coffee-Table.jpg" width="600"/>
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr">
                So what are some ways you can incorporate painted furniture into your homes decor? Glad you asked! Here are three easy ways:
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
                <img alt="Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-TV-Cabinet" class="alignnone wp-image-94833" height="400" src="http://blog-cdn.static-homes.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/09/Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-TV-Cabinet.jpg" width="300"/>
                <img alt="Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-Red" class="alignnone wp-image-94832" height="400" src="http://blog-cdn.static-homes.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/09/Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-Red.jpg" width="265"/>
               </p>
               <ol>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Paint It Yourself!
                 </strong>
                 Painting furniture is a huge trend for people trying their hand at their very own DIY project. Its a fun way to show your artistic side. It also allows you to choose your very own furniture piece and the paint color. If you are not a do-it-yourself type of person, thats fine  painting furniture isnt for every one. But if youd like to take a stab at it, heres an awesome
                 <a href="http://blog.homes.com/2013/03/homes-com-diy-experts-revamping-an-old-chair/" rel="me">
                  DIY chair painting tutorial
                 </a>
                 .
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Contact a Pro!
                 </strong>
                 If painting your own furniture caused a panic attack, no worries! There are many people around your community you can hire to do it for you. If you have a piece of furniture that could use a makeover, try performing an internet search or ask some friends for references (maybe your friend knows a closet DIYer!). Be sure to see some of the work before you hire someone. Everyone has a different painting style, and you dont want to end up with a vintage, distressed piece when you love modern.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Shop Your Favorite Retailer!
                 </strong>
                 Would you rather find your furniture already painted and ready to go? Many retail stores have caught onto the trend and have many options already. Another great way to find painted furniture is to browse markets where local vendors sell items they have created.
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
                <img alt="Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-Aqua-Stool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-94830" height="528" src="http://blog-cdn.static-homes.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/09/Homes.com-Painted-Furniture-Trend-Aqua-Stool.jpg" width="600"/>
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr">
                Painted furniture is a fantastic trend in home dcor and one that has been going for a while, but keeps getting more popular! No matter what decorating style you love, there will be many options available where painted furniture is concerned. Still on the fence? If you are unsure about painted furniture, start with a small piece, such as a side table. You cant go wrong there!
               </p>
               <p dir="ltr">
                Check out more decor trends like
                <a href="http://blog.homes.com/2014/08/home-decor-trend-going-ombre/" rel="me">
                 ombre
                </a>
                on the Homes.com
                <a href="http://blog.homes.com" rel="me">
                 blog
                </a>
                !
               </p>
