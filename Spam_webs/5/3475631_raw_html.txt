          HTC Mocks Samsung with a Facebook Post
         </h1>

        <div class="post-single-content box mark-links">
         <p>
          We all know that HTC is quite famous for producing premium looking and elegant smartphones. But there are smartphone manufacturers who are famous for producing smartphones with plastic body (we would no like to name any). Every company is trying to get the better of other and in this race they at times mock each other in a way that brings smile on the face of many people.
         </p>
         <p>
          Today HTC mocked Samsung in a quite different way which definitely brought smile on the faces of many. HTC posted a photo of HTC One M8 and bandaids on their
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/HTC/photos/a.477210308083.252809.101063233083/10152958144313084/?type=1">
           official Facebook page
          </a>
          with a tagline 
          <em>
           The cure for bad designs
          </em>
          . This photo was clearl targeted to Samsung because Samsung Galaxy S5 has perforated back which somewhat resembles to a band-aid.
         </p>
         <div class="adsense adsense-midtext" style="text-align:center;margin:12px">

           (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
          </script>
         </div>
         <p>
          Heres what HTC posted.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://smartphonetechie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/10480988_10152958144313084_2290644723832378509_n.jpg">
           <img alt="10480988_10152958144313084_2290644723832378509_n" class="aligncenter wp-image-214 size-full" height="358" src="//smartphonetechie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/10480988_10152958144313084_2290644723832378509_n.jpg" width="720"/>

         <p>
          This must have hurt Samsung badly, eh?
