        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Twelve Meatball Recipes Youll Love
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         last updated:
         <time class="entry-modified-time" datetime="2015-03-04T11:00:11+00:00" itemprop="dateModified">
          March 4, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://wearychef.com/author/wearychef/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Andi Gleeson
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-categories">
          Filed Under:
          <a href="http://wearychef.com/category/roundups/" rel="category tag">
           Recipe Roundups
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://wearychef.com/twelve-meatball-recipes/#comments">
           12 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         This list of twelve
         <em>
          <strong>
           Meatball Recipes
          </strong>
         </em>
         has everything a meatball lover could want including teriyaki, cheese stuffed, and Buffalo meatballs to name a few!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="This list of twelve Meatball Recipes has everything a meatball lover could want including teriyaki, cheese stuffed, and Buffalo meatballs to name a few!" class="aligncenter wp-image-11500 size-large" height="857" src="http://3qdpb11ix4d120cz5d15ubwe.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/12-meatball-recipes-600x857.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <!--Ad Injection:top-->
        <div style="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:10px;">
         <center>
          <div style="text-align:center; background-color:#E3EBD8; padding:10px;">
           <!-- /31859105/mid_article_top -->
           <div id="div-gpt-ad-1434983222715-1">
            <script type="text/javascript">
             googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('div-gpt-ad-1434983222715-1'); });
            </script>
           </div>
          </div>
         </center>
        </div>
        <p>
         Who doesnt love meatballs? They are so versatile! Besides putting them in spaghetti, which is always a hit, you can use them in sandwiches, pizza, with gravy, or serve them on their own as an appetizer. Nothing but good things happen when you have delicious spheres of seasoned meat.
        </p>
        <p>
         I looked up meatballs on
         <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meatball">
          Wikipedia
         </a>
         and was so interested to find that so many countries have their own signature version of meatballs. A few fun facts I learned:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          In Afghanistan, meatballs sometimes include plum seeds to add tartness.
         </li>
         <li>
          Albanian meatballs usually contain feta cheese. (Yum!)
         </li>
         <li>
          Chinese meatballs called Lions Heads can be up to 10cm in diameter!
         </li>
         <li>
          Finnish meatballs are sometimes made with reindeer meat.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Wherever youre from, Im sure youll enjoy these twelve meatball recipes. Some of them feature homemade meatballs, and others use frozen prepared meatballs as a shortcut.
