         <br/>
         <a href="http://wp.me/p2WNE9-2IU" target="_blank">
          Chevron and Posie Fall Wreath
         </a>
         via Nest of Posies
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.bombshellbling.com/fall-hedgehog-wreath/" target="_blank">
          Fall Hedgehog Wreath
         </a>
         via Bombshell Bling
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.thelilypadcottage.com/2014/08/fall-wreath.html" target="_blank">
          Fall Bittersweet Wreath
         </a>
         via The Lily Pad Cottage
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.aglimpseinsideblog.com/2014/08/fall-burlap-wreath.html" target="_blank">
          Fall Burlap and Plaid Wreath
         </a>
         via A Glimpse Inside
         <br/>
         <a href="http://anightowlblog.com/2014/08/easy-fall-hydrangea-wreath.html" target="_blank">
          Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath
         </a>
         via A Night Owl Blog
         <br/>
         <a href="http://sweetcsdesigns.com/burlap-flower-cone" target="_blank">
          Burlap Flower Cone
         </a>
         via Sweet Cs Designs
        </center>
        <strong>
         So much Fall inspiration, right?
        </strong>
        I love that we have options for styles and tastes across the board. If youre here to check out how I created our Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath, lets get started!
        <h3>
         What youll need:
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Floral craft ring
         </li>
         <li>
          Hydrangea stems (numbering the number of holes in your floral craft ring)
         </li>
         <li>
          Wire cutters
         </li>
         <li>
          Burlap ribbon
         </li>
         <li>
          Drill (as needed)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath | anightowlblog.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17085" height="1595" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-2.jpg" width="665"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/08/easy-fall-hydrangea-wreath.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-2.jpg&amp;description=Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         And the steps:
        </h3>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Snip your hydrangea stems off about an inch and a half below the flower using your wire cutters.
         </li>
         <li>
          Then take a look at the stem width compared to your floral craft ring. For our ring the holes were not quite large enough for the stems to feed through, so we simply took a drill with a bit matching the width of the hydrangea stem to widen the holes a bit. Easy peasy.
         </li>
         <li>
          Then feed your hydragea stems through the holes in the floral and bend down to hold in place.
         </li>
         <li>
          Repeat this steps to fill every hole in the floral craft ring with hydrangea stems, alternating colors if using more than one!
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath | anightowlblog.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17086" height="1595" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-3.jpg" width="665"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/08/easy-fall-hydrangea-wreath.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-3.jpg&amp;description=Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thenloop that ring of hydrangea goodness with a large burlap ribbon and youre all set! A truly
         <strong>
          Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath | anightowlblog.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17077" height="981" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-4.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/08/easy-fall-hydrangea-wreath.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-4.jpg&amp;description=Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath | anightowlblog.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17078" height="981" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-5.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/08/easy-fall-hydrangea-wreath.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-5.jpg&amp;description=Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath | anightowlblog.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17079" height="981" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-6.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/08/easy-fall-hydrangea-wreath.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Easy-Fall-Hydrangea-Wreath-6.jpg&amp;description=Easy Fall Hydrangea Wreath')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          This wreath literally took minutes to create.
         </strong>
         No glue, no hours of wrapping  my kind of wreath! And I think it looks just as nice as so many crazy expensive hydrangea wreaths you can find out there. And trust me, this is one you can recreate too! With the flatness of the floral craft ring in the back, the wreath lays against the door just perfectly too.
        </p>

         <img alt="anightowlsignature-2" height="99" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/anightowlsignature-2.jpg" width="200"/>
