     <h1>
      Seattle Folk: Emerald City Comic-Con!
     </h1>
     <h3 class="date">
      Wednesday, December 26, 2007 - 07:06 AM
     </h3>
     <div class="content">
      If you live in and around Seattle, fun news:  I'll be up there May 10 and 11th, for the big
      <a href="http://www.emeraldcitycomicon.com/">
       Emerald City Comic-Con
      </a>
      .
      <p>
       Emerald City Comic-Con was a blast the last time I went up there, and Seattle has a huuuge Sheldon audience (the biggest in the U.S., interestingly) so it's gonna be really good fun to get up there.
      </p>
      <p>
       I'll be exhibiting with my three buddies from
       <a href="http://www.halfpixel.com/">
        Halfpixel
       </a>
       , and we'll be doing a joint panel-talk on the "How To Make Webcomics" book.  Those three make me laugh like nuthin' else, so I'm looking forward to it.
      </p>
      <p>
       So if you're in or around Seattle, make plans to
       <a href="http://www.emeraldcitycomicon.com/tickets.php">
        attend
       </a>
        it really is gonna be a fun 'con to go to.
      </p>
     </div>
