                 <h4 class="block-title">
                  <span>
                   WHAT YOU DO
                  </span>
                 </h4>
                 <div class="td_mod_wrap">
                  <ol>
                   <li>
                    Start by taking half of the black olives and slicing them in half
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Then slice (1) round of carrot for every pair of olives that you have
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Cut a small triangle slice out of the carrot round (Save this as it will be the penguins beak)
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Now take a 1/2 TSP scoop of cream cheese and place it in between 2 olive halves
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Take the beak and insert it in the opening of an uncut olive
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    To assemble start by placing the penguin head through the toothpick, followed by the body and into the feet!
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Congratulations your done!
                   </li>
                  </ol>
