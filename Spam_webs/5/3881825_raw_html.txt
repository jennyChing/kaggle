        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Exciting New Additions Coming Our Way
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-27T14:21:38+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 27, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.perfectenoughforus.com/author/alexsmumma" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Krystal
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.perfectenoughforus.com/2015/01/exciting-new-additions.html#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <script>
         &lt;!--
dynamicgoogletags.update();
//--&gt;
        </script>
        <div class="wwsgd" style="display:none;">
         <p style="border:thin dotted black; padding:3mm;">
          If you're new here, you may want to subscribe to my
          <a href="http://www.perfectenoughforus.com/feed/">
           RSS feed
          </a>
          . Thanks for visiting!
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Back in November last year I decided to take on a new challenge. I have always loved writing in just about any form. I still have scraps of paper from when I was young with little snippets of a poem or verse that I had written. Writing was something I had done to cope with various stages of my life  it was a way to remove the chaos from my mind and organise it on paper.
        </p>
        <p>
         I had dabbled in blogs before, started a few that I never followed through with for various reasons and never really felt a passion for them. Looking back I think the biggest problem I had was that I was over editing myself. The first one I had started I tried to remain anonymous, the second one was work orientated and the third was too personal. None were quite the right fit for me.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.perfectenoughforus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/www.perfectenoughforus.com-5.png">
          <img alt="There are some exciting new additions coming our way here at Perfect Enough For Us. This is going to be a mammoth year and I have never been more excited. " class=" wp-image-311 alignright" height="638" src="http://www.perfectenoughforus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/www.perfectenoughforus.com-5-620x930.png" width="425"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I had the idea for Perfect Enough For Us  there are so many things in our life that arent the way we envisaged they would be, but we make things work and our life really is Perfect Enough For Us. Its okay not to be everyone elses standard of perfect.
        </p>
        <p>
         Since starting this little venture in mid November 2014, Perfect Enough For Us has grown faster and bigger than I could have ever imagined. Since I first hit publish we have had over 10,000 page views and so far for January we have already doubled our stats for December. We are growing fast and we are learning so much along the way.
        </p>
        <p>
         We have a lot of projects that are being put together behind the scenes and I think its the right time now to share some of them with you. I am so ridiculously beyond excited I can hardly contain myself. So heres a few of the incredibly exciting new additions that are coming up:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          eBook
         </strong>
          After writing The Story of Alexander I realised that there was a missing link of information for parents of NICU babies. The hospital gives you some information, you can Google and read others stories too, but I wanted to have one go to place that would give parents the practical information that need to survive NICU. This little project has been in the works for a month or so now and is ticking along really well  I have had a fantastic amount of input from other NICU parents that has given the book a real sense of substance and definitely added a lot more information.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youd like to be first on the list to receive the eBook you can subscribe to Perfect Enough For Us now (either using the subscription bar at the top of the page or the subscription box at the right hand side of the page) and the eBook will be emailed to you as soon as it is released. I will be offering it for free for a period of time so make sure you get in early to secure your copy which should be finished by the end of February 2015.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Guest Posts 
         </strong>
         I have been incredibly lucky over the last few months to have come into contact with some amazing fellow bloggers who have inspired and motivated me along the way. In the blogging world its always nice to have some fresh new content and different perspectives offered on your site as well as giving your readers a little variety. Because of this I will be featuring Guest Posts here on Perfect Enough For Us, as well as guest posting for other inspirational blogs. Keep an eye out for the first one over the next few weeks!!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          New Blogging Series 
         </strong>
         Since starting my blog I have noticed that there is a lot of information on how to create a blog, and a lot of information about how to monetize that blog and take it to the next level but there seems to be less information about what to do once your blog is live. What do you do next?
        </p>
        <p>
         I am lucky enough to be able to spend a lot of time on my blog  I am obsessed with the whole topic of blogging (just ask my friends, family and poor Steve who hears about blogging all the time). I research, constantly. I read books, blogs and anything I can get my hands on to increase my knowledge. And then  I give it a go.
        </p>
        <p>
         As of February Ill be starting a weekly series documenting what I have learned about blogging, what is working for me and what isnt, as well as tips and tricks from other blogging experts. I want to share with you all the things I am learning as I go along. I want to take all the research I have done and present it in a compact way that means you get all the information you need without having to spend copious amounts of hours reading and sorting through information.
        </p>
        <p>
         I am so excited about this series and already have so much information I want to share. Keep an eye out for the first post going live on Wednesday 4
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         February.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         
        </p>
        <p>
         There are a few other projects in the pipeline to be released as the year progresses. I am so excited about where Perfect Enough For Us is heading and would absolutely love your feedback. What do you want to see on here? What do you love about this site? Leave your comments below or use the
         <a href="http://www.perfectenoughforus.com/contact" title="Contact">
          Contact
         </a>
         form to let us know what you think.
        </p>
