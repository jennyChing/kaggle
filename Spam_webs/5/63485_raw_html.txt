    <header class="header-single container has-img">
     <h1 class="entry-title">
      15 Easy Grilled Desserts
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-08-25T16:31:16+00:00">
       August 25, 2014
      </time>
      <p class="byline author vcard">
       By 
			Shari's Berries
      </p>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <!-- /.hero -->
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <p class="lead">
        It's time for one last summer hurrah! And we know you're not going to just slap some burgers and hot dogs on the grill and call it a Labor Day. Summer deserves a big (and delicious) finish, like strawberry ricotta pizza, espresso doughnuts and more amazing grilled desserts.
       </p>
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Peaches With Honey-Ginger Cream Cheese Fluff
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2494" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled+peach+UC+edited1.jpg">
          <img alt="Photo courtesy of A Kitchen Addiction" class="size-full wp-image-2494" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled+peach+UC+edited1.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of A Kitchen Addiction
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         This simply delicious
         <a href="http://www.a-kitchen-addiction.com/grilled-peaches-with-honey-ginger-cream-cheese-fluff/">
          A Kitchen Addiction
         </a>
         recipe uses the grill to caramelize the natural sugars in fresh peaches. The slight tang of honey-ginger cream cheese fluff complements the fruits sweetness.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Chocolate Strawberry Croissant
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2495" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-nutella-croissant-strawberry-shortcakes-ohsweetbasil.com-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Nutella Strawberry Croissant" class="size-full wp-image-2495" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-nutella-croissant-strawberry-shortcakes-ohsweetbasil.com-4.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Oh, Sweet Basil
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         Dont toss those leftover brunch croissants. Take a cue from
         <a href="http://www.ohsweetbasil.com/2014/07/grilled-chocolate-strawberry-croissants-recipe.html">
          Oh, Sweet Basil
         </a>
         and toast them on the grill. Then top them off with chocolate hazelnut spread and whipped cream.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled N Glazed Strawberries
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2496" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-glazed-strawberries-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Photo courtesy of Crme de la Crumb" class="size-full wp-image-2496" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-glazed-strawberries-5.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Crme de la Crumb
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lecremedelacrumb.com/2014/07/grilled-n-glazed-strawberries.html">
          Crme de la Crumb
         </a>
         makes freshstrawberry skewers even more irresistible with a quick and easy vanilla cream cheese glaze.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Pineapple and Strawberries With Bourbon White Chocolate
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2497" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-pineapple-and-strawberries-with-bourbon-white-chocolate-glaze-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled-pineapple-and-strawberries-with-bourbon-white-chocolate-glaze-4" class="size-full wp-image-2497" height="1152" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-pineapple-and-strawberries-with-bourbon-white-chocolate-glaze-4.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of A Zesty Bite
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         Bourbon white chocolate sauceand an unexpected secret ingredient (psst  its white chocolate coffee creamer), give these fruit skewers from
         <a href="http://www.azestybite.com/grilled-pineapple-strawberries-skewers-bourbon-white-chocolate-glaze/">
          A Zesty Bite
         </a>
         a hint of decadence.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Espresso Glazed Coconut Doughnuts
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2498" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-Espresso-Glazed-Coconut-Doughnuts-with-Mocha-Coconut-Ganache-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled-Espresso-Glazed-Coconut-Doughnuts-with-Mocha-Coconut-Ganache-1" class="size-full wp-image-2498" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-Espresso-Glazed-Coconut-Doughnuts-with-Mocha-Coconut-Ganache-1.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Half Baked Harvest
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.halfbakedharvest.com/grilled-espresso-glazed-coconut-doughnuts-with-mocha-coconut-ganache/">
          Half Baked Harvest
         </a>
         remixes this breakfast favorite by first grilling the doughnuts, and then dunking them in homemade mocha coconut ganache.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Pineapple With Cinnamon Sugar Glaze
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2499" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-Pineapple-Cinnamon-Sugar-Glaze-Recipe.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled-Pineapple-Cinnamon-Sugar-Glaze-Recipe" class="size-full wp-image-2499" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-Pineapple-Cinnamon-Sugar-Glaze-Recipe.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Lollyjane
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         Serve these grilled pineapples from
         <a href="http://lollyjane.com/grilled-pineapple-cinnamon-sugar-glaze/">
          Lollyjane
         </a>
         as a side dish with grilled pork or chicken. Then, after everybodys finished the main course, add a scoop of ice cream and turn them into a simply refreshing dessert.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Stone Fruit &amp; Almond Mascarpone Dip
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2521" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/GrilledFruitwithAlmondMascarpone-018-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled Fruit with Almond Mascarpone " class="size-full wp-image-2521" height="534" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/GrilledFruitwithAlmondMascarpone-018-2.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Christina Lane-Dessert for Two
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.dessertfortwo.com/2014/07/grilled-stone-fruit-almond-mascarpone-dip/">
          Dessert for Two
         </a>
         breaks out of her usual mold with this party-friendly recipe made with peaches, plums, apricots and pluots.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Figs With Vanilla Ice Cream and Strawberry Sauce
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2522" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilledFigs.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled Figs" class="size-full wp-image-2522" height="1199" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilledFigs.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of The Hungry Australian
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://hungryaustralian.com/2013/02/grilled-figs-with-vanilla-ice-cream-and-strawberry-sauce">
          The Hungry Australian
         </a>
         pairs fresh, grilled figs with ice cream drizzled with strawberry sauce for a cool, sweet dessert that only takes five minutes to make.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Pears With Cinnamon Drizzle
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2524" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled_pears_cinnamon_honey_drizzle_a_house_in_the_hills_2.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled Pears with Cinnamon Drizzle " class="size-full wp-image-2524" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled_pears_cinnamon_honey_drizzle_a_house_in_the_hills_2.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of A House in the Hills
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <a href="http://ahouseinthehills.com/2014/02/25/grilled-pears-with-cinnamon-drizzle/">
          A House in the Hills
         </a>
         discovers that warm cinnamon honey glaze is the Best. Thing. Ever.,especiallywhen drizzled over grilled pears.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Pound Cake With Berries
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2525" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-Poundcake-with-Blueberries-026-533x800.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled-Poundcake-with-Blueberries" class="size-full wp-image-2525" height="1201" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grilled-Poundcake-with-Blueberries-026-533x800.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Paula Jones
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         For a simple-yet-elegant way to round off any meal, try Paula Jones grilled pound cake topped with yogurt, blueberries, fresh mint and powdered sugar. See the full recipe on the blog
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/2013/06/01/grilled-pound-cake-with-berries/">
          Bellalimento.
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Pizza With Fresh Ricotta &amp; Strawberries
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2526" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-pizza-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled Pizza with Fresh Ricotta &amp; Strawberries" class="size-full wp-image-2526" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-pizza-5.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo Courtesy of Food Babbles
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         A heavenly dessert (or breakfast according to
         <a href="http://foodbabbles.com/2011/07/grilled-pizza-with-fresh-ricotta-strawberries/">
          Food Babbles
         </a>
         ) this sweet twist on pizza features ricotta cheese made from scratch and strawberries soaked in sugar and Grand Marnier.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Pound Cake With Sweetened Mascarpone &amp; Berries
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2528" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-pound-cake-135.jpg">
          <img alt="Grilled Pound Cake " class="size-full wp-image-2528" height="1193" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-pound-cake-135.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Sugar and Cloth
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         This fool-proof dessert from
         <a href="http://sugarandcloth.com/2014/03/eats-grilled-pound-cake-with-sweetened-mascarpone-berries/">
          Sugar &amp; Cloth
         </a>
         doesnt require much measuring and takes about 10 minutes, or even less if you buy a pound cake from the store.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Fruit Pizza With Hazelnut Spread
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2529" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grillled-Fruit-Berry-Pizza.jpg">
          <img alt="Grillled-Fruit-Berry-Pizza" class="size-full wp-image-2529" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Grillled-Fruit-Berry-Pizza.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Lollyjane
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         It all starts with pre-made cookie dough. Then
         <a href="http://lollyjane.com/grilled-fruit-pizza/">
          Lollyjane
         </a>
         adds rich, sweet hazelnut spread and fresh fruit for a crowd-pleasing dessert that you can whip up in seconds.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Grilled Angel Berry Skewers
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2530" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-angel-berry-skewers2.jpg">
          <img alt="Angel Berry Skewers" class="size-full wp-image-2530" height="1201" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/grilled-angel-berry-skewers2.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo courtesy of Living Better Together
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         A salute to fresh summer berries, this no-fuss recipe from
         <a href="http://www.livingbettertogether.com/2014/06/grilled-angel-berry-skewers.html">
          Living Better Together
         </a>
         is full of natural flavors and sweetness.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Vegan Deconstructed Boozy Mocha Drenched Grilled Banana Coconut Cream Pies
        </h4>
        <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2531" style="width: 810px">
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Vegan-Deconstructed-Boozy-Mocha-Drenched-Grilled-Banana-Coconut-Cream-Pies.-16.jpg">
          <img alt="Vegan-Deconstructed-Boozy-Mocha-Drenched-Grilled-Banana-Coconut-Cream-Pies.-16" class="size-full wp-image-2531" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Vegan-Deconstructed-Boozy-Mocha-Drenched-Grilled-Banana-Coconut-Cream-Pies.-16.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
          Photo Courtesy of Half Baked Harvest
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         The name pretty much says it all. Luckily this boozy, chocolaty, creamy creation from
         <a href="http://www.halfbakedharvest.com/vegan-deconstructed-boozy-mocha-drenched-grilled-banana-coconut-cream-pies/">
          Half Baked Harvest
         </a>
         is easier to make than it is to say.
        </p>
        <h4>
         All up in Your Grill
        </h4>
        <p>
         We want all the details. Whats your favorite grilled summer dessert? Was it on our list? Let us know in the comments.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
