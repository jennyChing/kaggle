         <p>
          On June 29, 2011, The Bridge Project opened Richard III starring The Old Vic Artistic Director
          <a href="/people/Kevin-Spacey/">
           Kevin Spacey
          </a>
          at The Old Vic in London. The production will embark on an international tour including the Athens &amp; Epidaurus Festival, Spain's Centro Niemeyer, Hong Kong Arts Festival, and Singapore Repertory Theatre, arriving at BAM's Harvey Theater in New York on January 10, 2012. The production will take its final bow at BAM on March 4, 2012. Check out Spacey as Richard III below!
         </p>
         <p>
          Now playing The Old Vic through September 11, tickets will be available through the BAM box office this fall. The Bridge Project is again being directed by Academy Award winner
          <a href="/people/Sam-Mendes/">
           Sam Mendes
          </a>
          , with whom
          <a href="/people/Kevin-Spacey/">
           Kevin Spacey
          </a>
          worked on the film American Beauty, for which they both won BAFTA and Academy Awards.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Bridge Project is a unique three-year series of co-productions by BAM, The Old Vic, and Neal Street, devoted to producing large-scale, classical theater for international audiences.
         </p>
         <p>
          The complete
          <a href="/people/Acting-Company/">
           Acting Company
          </a>
          is as follows:
          <a href="/people/Maureen-Anderman/">
           Maureen Anderman
          </a>
          (Duchess of York),
          <a href="/people/Stephen-Lee-Anderson/">
          </a>

           Stephen Lee
          </a>
          Anderson (Sir
          <a href="/people/Richard-Ratcliffe/">
           Richard Ratcliffe
          </a>
          ),
          <a href="/people/Jeremy-Bobb/">
           Jeremy Bobb
          </a>
          (Catesby &amp; 2nd Murderer &amp; 2nd Citizen ),
          <a href="/people/Nathan-Darrow/">
           Nathan Darrow
          </a>
          (Lord Grey &amp; Richmond),
          <a href="/people/Jack-Ellis/">
           Jack Ellis
          </a>
          (Lord Hastings),
          <a href="/people/Haydn-Gwynne/">
          </a>
          <a href="/people/Haydn-Gwynn/">
           Haydn Gwynn
          </a>
          e (Queen Elizabeth), Chuk Iwuji (Buckingham),
          <a href="/people/Isaiah-Johnson/">
           Isaiah Johnson
          </a>
          (Rivers &amp; Lord Mayor),
          <a href="/people/Gemma-Jones/">
           Gemma Jones
          </a>
          (Queen Margaret),
          <a href="/people/Andrew-Long/">
           Andrew Long
          </a>
          * (King Edward IV &amp; Bishop of Ely), Katherine Manners (Young Prince Richard),
          <a href="/people/Howard-Overshown/">

          <a href="/people/Simon-Lee/">

          </a>
          (Lord Stanley),
          <a href="/people/Annabel-Scholey/">

          </a>
          (Lady Anne),
          <a href="/people/Kevin-Spacey/">

          </a>
          (Richard, Duke of Gloucester), Gavin Stenhouse (Dorset &amp; Urswick), Hannah Stokely (Edward), and
          <a href="/people/Chandler-Williams/">
           Chandler Williams
          </a>
          (George, Duke of Clarence).
         </p>
         <p>
          Richard III will play the Athens &amp; Epidaurus Festival July 29-31, 2011, theHong Kong Arts Festival September 16-18, 2011, Centro Niemeyer, Spain September 28 - 1 October and theSingapore Repertory Theatre November 17-26, 2011.
