      <p>
       <em>
        Puzzled Episodes 1 to 4
       </em>
       is another series of short and simple
       <a href="http://www.freegamesnews.com/en/?cat=38" title="Room Escape">
        Room Escape
       </a>
       games developed last year by
       <a href="http://www.freegamesnews.com/en/?s=Dom+Chapman&amp;searchbutton=Search" target="_self">
        Dom Chapman
       </a>
       , the author of the
       <a href="http://www.freegamesnews.com/en/?p=1061" target="_self">
        <em>
         Trapped series
        </em>
       </a>
       . You are again stuck in a closed room and you will have to find and combine several objects to get out of here.
      </p>
      <p>
       Dont forget to press the spacebar if you want to put an item away. Each episode is recommended for a 10-15 minute break.
      </p>
