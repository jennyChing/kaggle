  <meta content="Other Ingredients
&amp; Equipment You'll Need:
Scale
Microwave safe glass container
Large Knife
Several Mixing Spoons
(wood or stainless
steel)
Spatula
Microwave
Rubbing alcohol in a spray bottle
Stick Blender
Stovetop
Pots (for double broiler method)
Several Mixing Bowls
Bat embed mold (or Halloween themed)
SugarWax
PaperTapeSmall Paint BrushToothpicks (3) 4oz. Natures Garden Fragrance BottlesTotal Recipe for Bath
Melt Bottoms
29 grams- Shea
Butter
29 grams- Mango Butter
57 grams- Cocoa
Butter
3 grams- Beeswax
2 grams- Beet Root Powder
6 grams- Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance 

Total Recipe for Herbal Packs
19 grams- Lavender Flowers
Whole
10 grams- Jasmine Flowers Whole
9 grams- Juniper Berries Whole 


Total Recipe for Bubble Fizz
113 grams- Baking
Soda
58 grams- Citric
Acid
9 grams- Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance 


Total Recipe for Purple Sugar
50 grams- White
Sugar
10 drops- FUN Soap Colorant Ultramarine Violet

Bath Melt &quot;Glue&quot;
24 grams- Cocoa Butter
2 grams- Beeswax

Total Recipe for RIP
Paint
2 grams- Arrowroot
Powder
1 grams- Vegetable Glycerin
20 drops- Fun Soap Colorant Black Oxide





Total Recipe for the
Bat Embeds
45 grams- Diamond
Clear Melt and Pour Soap
2 grams- Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance
20 drops- FUN Soap Colorant Black OxideClean
&amp; Sanitize your work area and all of your packaging materials. It is
suggested that you wear gloves, protective clothing, and a hair net while
preparing this recipe. 

Prep
the Base: 

1.&nbsp;&nbsp; Weigh out all of your butters and beeswax from the
total recipe for Bath Melt Bottoms.&nbsp; 

2.&nbsp;&nbsp; Begin melting the butters and
beeswax using the double boiler method.&nbsp;
Heat until completely melted. 

3.&nbsp; Add your beet root powder and stick
blend thoroughly.&nbsp; You want the herb to
be completely incorporated throughout the entire melted mixture.&nbsp; This allows for your finished bath melts to
have a nice bold color. 
&nbsp;
4.&nbsp; Add your fragrance oil and stir.&nbsp; 

5.&nbsp; Once blended, set out both of your
cupcake cherry on top molds.&nbsp; Fill
approximately 1/4 of each cupcake bottom (for 3).&nbsp;
Place the remaining bath melt mixture back on low heat for about 10- 15
minutes.&nbsp; Stir occasionally.&nbsp; 

6.&nbsp; Prep your (3) 4oz
bottles by completely wrapping the bottom half portion with wax paper.&nbsp; Secure the wax paper with tape.


7.&nbsp; Once the bath melt mixture in the
mold has started to harden, gently put one of each of your 4 oz. bottles in the
center of each mold opening.&nbsp; Give the
bath melt mixture that is still on the stovetop one last good stir, and
carefully pour the remaining mixture along the side of the bottle to completely
fill the molds.&nbsp; Allow this to fully set
up.

Prep
the Bats:

8.&nbsp; In your microwave safe glass container, weigh out and melt 45 grams of Diamond Clear&nbsp; Melt and Pour Soap in the microwave 30 seconds at a
time. Stir gently. Keep melting until all soap is in a liquid state.


9. &nbsp;Next,
add 20 drops FUN Soap Colorant- Black Oxide and mix well.&nbsp; Then, add the 2 grams Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance Oil; stir well.&nbsp; Once incorporated, fill (3) bat embed
cavities and spritz with alcohol.&nbsp; Set aside and allow to fully set up.&nbsp; 

Prep
the Tea Bags:

10.&nbsp; In one of your mixing bowls, weigh
out your lavender flowers, jasmine flowers, and juniper berries.&nbsp; Stir well to blend.&nbsp; 

11.&nbsp; Get three pull string tea bags and
gently stuff each one with the herbal mixture.&nbsp;
Pull the strings and tie bag shut.&nbsp;
Set this aside.&nbsp; 

Prep
the Bubble Fizz:&nbsp; 

12.&nbsp; In a mixing bowl, weigh out your
baking soda, and citric acid.&nbsp; Stir well,
and break up any clumps that may be in your mixing bowl.&nbsp; Once mixture is blended well, add 9 grams of
Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance Oil and thoroughly incorporate.&nbsp; Then, set aside.&nbsp; 

Prep
the Purple
Sugar:&nbsp; 

13.&nbsp; In another
mixing bowl, weigh
out your sugar.&nbsp; Next, add 10 drops Ultramarine violet FUN
Soap Colorant.&nbsp; Stir this well with a
spoon.&nbsp;&nbsp; You want to ensure that all of
the sugar has a purple&nbsp; hue to it.&nbsp; Then, set aside. Prep the &quot;RIP&quot; Paint:&nbsp; 

14.&nbsp; In a
small mixing bowl, weigh
out your vegetable glycerin
and arrowroot powder.&nbsp; Blend this
together to make a thick paste.&nbsp; Then,
add 20 drops of FUN Soap Colorant Black Oxide.&nbsp;
Stir again until all of the color is incorporated into the paste.&nbsp; Set aside.&nbsp;


&nbsp;Prep the Bath Melt &quot;Glue&quot;:&nbsp; 

15. Weigh out your cocoa butter and beeswax.&nbsp; Begin melting the two together using the double boiler method.&nbsp; Heat until completely melted.&nbsp; Stir.&nbsp; Once both
ingredients are melted, keep this on low heat stirring occasionally.&nbsp; 

Assembly:&nbsp; 

16.&nbsp; Lay each of your filled tea bags flat, with the string side towards you.&nbsp; Then,&nbsp;
using a small paint brush, paint the letters &quot;RIP&quot; towards the opposite
side of the string end.&nbsp; Allow this to
harden. 

17.&nbsp; Once your bath melt bottoms are completely
hardened, gently remove the bottle from the wax paper in each mold.&nbsp; Then, very gently pull each wax paper from
the molds as well.&nbsp; Once both are
removed, carefully remove each bath melt bottom from the mold.&nbsp; Then, stick the bath melt back in its mold so
that you can decorate it without it melting in your hands. 

18. Now, get your bubble fizz bowl, and carefully pack the bottom of the bath
melt with the mixture.&nbsp; Do not completely
fill.&nbsp; Once the bubble fizz is packed
down, place one tea bag (string side down) into the opening.&nbsp; Then, place even more bubble fizz around the
tea bag to secure it in place.&nbsp;


19.&nbsp; Next, using a spoon and working
quickly, place some of the bath melt &quot;glue&quot; on top of the bubble fizz and
around the tea bag.&nbsp; Then, quickly spread
the purple sugar on top of the melted &quot;glue&quot; to completely cover the bath melt
bottom.&nbsp; Repeat with all (3) bath
melts.&nbsp; Allow this to fully set up.&nbsp; 

20.&nbsp; Pop the bat embeds out of the mold
while you are waiting for the sugar to adhere and harden as the &quot;glue&quot; sets
up.&nbsp; 

21. Place your bat embed in front of the RIP side of the tea bag.&nbsp;&nbsp; Carefully, spoon some bath melt &quot;glue&quot;
behind the bat alongside the tea bag.&nbsp;
Gently push the bat back in place and hold for a few seconds.&nbsp; Once secure, reinforce any weak areas with
more bath melt &quot;glue&quot;.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 

Please
Note:&nbsp; It is okay if some of the bath melt &quot;glue&quot; gets on the
bat or purple sugar.&nbsp; We had no problem
cleaning this up with a toothpick after the bath melts were completely
finished.&nbsp; 

22.&nbsp; Once the bath
melts have completely set up, use a toothpick to clean up any visible &quot;glue&quot;
.

23.&nbsp; Finally, carefully angle your
cupcake mold on the side and gently ease the bath melt out of the mold.&nbsp; 

Your Berry Bewitching Bath Brews are now ready to use.&nbsp; Enjoy!&nbsp;


To
Use:&nbsp; Place one
bath melt into the bath tub under warm running water.&nbsp; Only one bath melt is needed.&nbsp; 

Be Careful:
&nbsp;Your bath
tub will be slippery from the butters once the bath melt has melted. 

Please
Note:&nbsp; These bath melts will begin to melt just from physical contact
with the skin.&nbsp; It is not advised to ship
these during the hotter temperature months.&nbsp;

       <h2>
        Directions:
       </h2>
       <div>
        <u style="text-underline:single">
         Other Ingredients
&amp; Equipment You'll Need
        </u>
        <u style="text-underline:single">
         :
        </u>
        <p style="language:en-US;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-left:0in;
text-align:left;direction:ltr;unicode-bidi:embed;mso-line-break-override:none;
word-break:normal;punctuation-wrap:hanging">
         Scale
         <br/>
         Microwave safe glass container
         <br/>
         Large Knife
         <br/>
         Several Mixing Spoons
(wood or stainless
steel)
         <br/>
         Spatula
         <br/>
         Microwave
         <br/>
         Rubbing alcohol in a spray bottle
         <br/>
         Stick Blender
         <br/>
         Stovetop
         <br/>
         Pots (for double broiler method)
         <br/>
         Several Mixing Bowls
         <br/>
         Bat embed mold (or Halloween themed)
         <br/>
         Sugar
         <br/>
         Wax
Paper
         <big>
          <br/>
         </big>
         Tape
         <big>
          <br/>
         </big>
         Small Paint Brush
         <br/>
         Toothpicks
         <br/>
         <small>
          (3) 4oz. Natures Garden Fragrance Bottles
         </small>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed;">
         <u style="">
          Total Recipe for Bath
Melt Bottoms
          <br/>
         </u>
         29 grams- Shea
Butter
         <br/>
         29 grams- Mango Butter
         <br/>
         57 grams- Cocoa
Butter
         <br/>
         3 grams- Beeswax
         <br/>
         2 grams- Beet Root Powder
         <br/>
         6 grams- Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <u style="">
          <br/>
          Total Recipe for Herbal Packs
         </u>
         <u style="">
          <br/>
         </u>
         19 grams- Lavender Flowers
Whole
         <br/>
         10 grams- Jasmine Flowers Whole
         <br/>
         9 grams- Juniper Berries Whole
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <u style="">
          Total Recipe for Bubble Fizz
         </u>
         <br/>
         113 grams- Baking
Soda
         <br/>
         58 grams- Citric
Acid
         <br/>
         9 grams- Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <u style="">
          Total Recipe for Purple Sugar
          <br/>
         </u>
         50 grams- White
Sugar
         <br/>
         10 drops- FUN Soap Colorant Ultramarine Violet
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <u style="">
          Bath Melt "Glue
         </u>
         <u style="">
          "
         </u>
         <br/>
         24 grams- Cocoa Butter
         <br/>
         2 grams- Beeswax
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <u style="">
          <br/>
          Total
         </u>
         <u style="">
          Recipe for RIP
Paint
          <br/>
         </u>
         2 grams- Arrowroot
Powder
         <br/>
         1 grams- Vegetable Glycerin
         <br/>
         20 drops- Fun Soap Colorant Black Oxide
        </p>
        <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed;">
        </p>
        <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed;">
         <u style="">
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Total Recipe for the
Bat Embeds
          <br/>
         </u>
         45 grams- Diamond
Clear Melt and Pour Soap
         <br/>
         2 grams- Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance
         <br/>
         20 drops- FUN Soap Colorant Black Oxide
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Clean
&amp; Sanitize your work area and all of your packaging materials. It is
suggested that you wear gloves, protective clothing, and a hair net while
preparing this recipe.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Prep
the Base:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         1. Weigh out all of your butters and beeswax from the
total recipe for Bath Melt Bottoms.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         2. Begin melting the butters and
beeswax using the double boiler method.
Heat until completely melted.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         3. Add your beet root powder and stick
blend thoroughly. You want the herb to
be completely incorporated throughout the entire melted mixture. This allows for your finished bath melts to
have a nice bold color.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         4. Add your fragrance oil and stir.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         5. Once blended, set out both of your
cupcake cherry on top molds. Fill
approximately 1/4 of each cupcake bottom (for 3).
Place the remaining bath melt mixture back on low heat for about 10- 15
minutes. Stir occasionally.
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p style="language:en-US;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-left:0in;
text-align:left;direction:ltr;unicode-bidi:embed;mso-line-break-override:none;
word-break:normal;punctuation-wrap:hanging">
         6. Prep your (3) 4oz
bottles by completely wrapping the bottom half portion with wax paper. Secure the wax paper with tape.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         7. Once the bath melt mixture in the
mold has started to harden, gently put one of each of your 4 oz. bottles in the
center of each mold opening. Give the
bath melt mixture that is still on the stovetop one last good stir, and
carefully pour the remaining mixture along the side of the bottle to completely
fill the molds. Allow this to fully set
up.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Prep
the Bats:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         8. In your microwave safe glass container, weigh out and melt 45 grams of Diamond Clear Melt and Pour Soap in the microwave 30 seconds at a
time. Stir gently. Keep melting until all soap is in a liquid state.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <br/>
        9. Next,
add 20 drops FUN Soap Colorant- Black Oxide and mix well. Then, add the 2 grams Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance Oil; stir well. Once incorporated, fill (3) bat embed
cavities and spritz with alcohol. Set aside and allow to fully set up.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Prep
the Tea Bags:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        10. In one of your mixing bowls, weigh
out your lavender flowers, jasmine flowers, and juniper berries. Stir well to blend.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        11. Get three pull string tea bags and
gently stuff each one with the herbal mixture.
Pull the strings and tie bag shut.
Set this aside.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Prep
the Bubble Fizz:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        12. In a mixing bowl, weigh out your
baking soda, and citric acid. Stir well,
and break up any clumps that may be in your mixing bowl. Once mixture is blended well, add 9 grams of
Berry Bewitching Brew Fragrance Oil and thoroughly incorporate. Then, set aside.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Prep
the Purple
Sugar:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        13. In another
mixing bowl, weigh
out your sugar. Next, add 10 drops Ultramarine violet FUN
Soap Colorant. Stir this well with a
spoon. You want to ensure that all of
the sugar has a purple hue to it. Then, set aside.
        <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed;">
         <br/>
         Prep the "RIP" Paint:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         14. In a
small mixing bowl, weigh
out your vegetable glycerin
and arrowroot powder. Blend this
together to make a thick paste. Then,
add 20 drops of FUN Soap Colorant Black Oxide.
Stir again until all of the color is incorporated into the paste. Set aside.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Prep the Bath Melt "Glue":
         <br/>
         <br/>
         15. Weigh out your cocoa butter and beeswax. Begin melting the two together using the double boiler method. Heat until completely melted. Stir. Once both
ingredients are melted, keep this on low heat stirring occasionally.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Assembly:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         16. Lay each of your filled tea bags flat, with the string side towards you. Then,
using a small paint brush, paint the letters "RIP" towards the opposite
side of the string end. Allow this to
harden.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         17. Once your bath melt bottoms are completely
hardened, gently remove the bottle from the wax paper in each mold. Then, very gently pull each wax paper from
the molds as well. Once both are
removed, carefully remove each bath melt bottom from the mold. Then, stick the bath melt back in its mold so
that you can decorate it without it melting in your hands.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         18. Now, get your bubble fizz bowl, and carefully pack the bottom of the bath
melt with the mixture. Do not completely
fill. Once the bubble fizz is packed
down, place one tea bag (string side down) into the opening. Then, place even more bubble fizz around the
tea bag to secure it in place.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         19. Next, using a spoon and working
quickly, place some of the bath melt "glue" on top of the bubble fizz and
around the tea bag. Then, quickly spread
the purple sugar on top of the melted "glue" to completely cover the bath melt
bottom. Repeat with all (3) bath
melts. Allow this to fully set up.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         20. Pop the bat embeds out of the mold
while you are waiting for the sugar to adhere and harden as the "glue" sets
up.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         21. Place your bat embed in front of the RIP side of the tea bag. Carefully, spoon some bath melt "glue"
behind the bat alongside the tea bag.
Gently push the bat back in place and hold for a few seconds. Once secure, reinforce any weak areas with
more bath melt "glue".
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Please
Note: It is okay if some of the bath melt "glue" gets on the
bat or purple sugar. We had no problem
cleaning this up with a toothpick after the bath melts were completely
finished.
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p style="language:en-US;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-left:0in;
text-align:left;direction:ltr;unicode-bidi:embed;mso-line-break-override:none;
word-break:normal;punctuation-wrap:hanging">
         22. Once the bath
melts have completely set up, use a toothpick to clean up any visible "glue"
.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         23. Finally, carefully angle your
cupcake mold on the side and gently ease the bath melt out of the mold.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Your Berry Bewitching Bath Brews are now ready to use. Enjoy!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         To
Use: Place one
bath melt into the bath tub under warm running water. Only one bath melt is needed.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Be Careful:
Your bath
tub will be slippery from the butters once the bath melt has melted.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Please
Note: These bath melts will begin to melt just from physical contact
with the skin. It is not advised to ship
these during the hotter temperature months.
