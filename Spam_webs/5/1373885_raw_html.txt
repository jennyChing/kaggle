           <a href="http://blog-content.emeals.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/cake_pops.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://blog-content.emeals.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/cake_pops.jpg', '']);">
            <img alt="Easy Valentine's Day Red Velvet Cake Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6639" height="328" src="http://blog-content.emeals.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/cake_pops.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           This Red Velvet Cake Bites recipe makes a large batch  perfect for school or a Valentines Day party. If you have extras, freeze in airtight containers up to one month.
           <br/>
          </p>
          <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
           <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTopRight">
            <img itemprop="image" src="http://blog-content.emeals.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/cake_pops.jpg" width="205"/>
            <div class="ERSSavePrint">
             <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
              <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://blog.emeals.com/easyrecipe-print/6637-0/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://blog.emeals.com/easyrecipe-print/6637-0/', 'Print']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               Print
              </a>
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="divERSHeadItems">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSIngredients">
            <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 box red velvet cake mix
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              Egg, oil and water called for on cake mix package
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              16 oz container cream cheese frosting
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              16 oz pink, red, and/or white candy melts
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              Lollipop sticks
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              Foam block, to serve as a stand, optional
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              Optional Garnish: assorted Valentines Day sprinkles
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSInstructions">
            <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Prepare cake according to package instructions. Cool completely.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Crumble cake into a large bowl. Add frosting, stirring to combine.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Roll into 1-inch balls, and place on baking sheets. Refrigerate overnight.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Melt candy melts in microwave on medium power, stirring until smooth.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Dip the end of 1 lollipop stick in melted candy and insert into 1 cake ball.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Dip cake ball into melted candy, allowing excess chocolate to drip off. Place on a baking sheet lined with wax paper, or poke into a foam block to dry. Immediately sprinkle with sprinkles if desired.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Repeat procedure with remaining cake balls. Let stand until coating is set.
             </li>
            </ol>
