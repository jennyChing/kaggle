        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Hometown, Downtown Engagement: Erika &amp; Jared in Orlando
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          on
         </em>
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-03-11T09:26:14-04:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          March 11, 2014
         </time>
         <em>
          by
         </em>
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link local-link" href="http://floridiansocial.com/author/laura/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Laura McGlynn
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-categories">
          <em>
           in
          </em>
          <a class="local-link" href="http://floridiansocial.com/category/weddings/" rel="category tag">

         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         A gorgeous couple, stunning urban locations, and joy and love all
         <em>
          over
         </em>
         the place - everything about today's session from
         <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.cptphotography.com" onclick="this.target='_new';" rel="external nofollow">
          Concept Photography
         </a>
         is just absolute perfection.
        </p>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29304" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-03.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29303" height="1800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-02.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29305" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-04.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <p>
         Erika and Jared both grew up in Orlando, so it was a natural choice for both their engagement session and wedding day; says Erika,
         <em>
          "Having the wedding in Orlando reminds us of where we got our start."
         </em>
        </p>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29306" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-05.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29309" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-08.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <p>
         The two met the summer before their freshman year of high school, in PE class... in a way that is just TOO dreamy. While running around the track, Erika's knee gave out, and Jared came to the  rescue - he picked her up and carried her to the nurse.
        </p>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29310" height="1800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-09.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29308" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-07.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29311" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-10.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29312" height="1800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-11.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <p>
         They had a few classes together over the years, but didn't start dating until junior year. After attending USF and graduating together (he in biomedical sciences, her in chemical engineering) the two are both currently working on their Masters degrees.
        </p>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29313" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-12.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <img alt="downtown Orlando engagement by cptphotography.com" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-29314" height="800" src="http://floridianweddings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Orlando-Engagement-Concept-Photography-13.jpg" width="1200"/>
        <p>
         Jared popped the question in the summer of their junior year in college. From Erika:
         <em>
          He tricked me into thinking we were traveling to go get pictures on location, although he never told me exactly where we were going. We arrived at Bok Tower Gardens where I was pleasantly surprised given how beautiful the area was. He walked me through the numerous flowers where we finally came upon a clearing where there was a little stool set up with all kinds of little chocolates and sparkling cider as well as a custom quilt with our picture on it sprawled on the grass.
         </em>
        </p>
