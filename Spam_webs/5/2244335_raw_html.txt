             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2015/06/happiness-is-morning-walks-on-beach.html">
               Happiness is Morning Walks on the Beach
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6450544132928961062" itemprop="articleBody">
              I am learning what happiness means to me. Over the weekend, I took a trip to Myrtle Beach and felt the happiness of morning walks on the beach.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-KxxvhwdvOY8/VXZaDoNlb4I/AAAAAAAAIIM/3kzDKiODk4E/s1600/IMG_5449.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-KxxvhwdvOY8/VXZaDoNlb4I/AAAAAAAAIIM/3kzDKiODk4E/s640/IMG_5449.JPG" width="480"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-rYOQp8UoIGY/VXZaDkretvI/AAAAAAAAIII/MBSv2aZpmoA/s1600/IMG_5469.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-rYOQp8UoIGY/VXZaDkretvI/AAAAAAAAIII/MBSv2aZpmoA/s640/IMG_5469.JPG" width="480"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6HgsPvitv80/VXZaDxmoLxI/AAAAAAAAIIQ/AIqiGJWJMe0/s1600/IMG_5563.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6HgsPvitv80/VXZaDxmoLxI/AAAAAAAAIIQ/AIqiGJWJMe0/s640/IMG_5563.JPG" width="480"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Each morning, I rose early and spent time leisurely strolling the beach. I soaked in the sights, sounds and the rising sun. I was surprised at the number of people out that early. I was thrilled to see their smiling faces and the enjoyment they were getting out of their own treks.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-foT1S1AIDzY/VXZZ7boTA9I/AAAAAAAAIIA/EJ7mppCeb74/s1600/IMG_5476.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-foT1S1AIDzY/VXZZ7boTA9I/AAAAAAAAIIA/EJ7mppCeb74/s640/IMG_5476.JPG" width="480"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              I was also able to take in the energy from the crashing ocean waves, meditate and do some yoga. The overwhelming washing over of love I felt in these moments was my bliss on this trip.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fYyNOSSTUTQ/VXZZun4FEAI/AAAAAAAAIH4/OmE3dFBp_-A/s1600/IMG_5584.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fYyNOSSTUTQ/VXZZun4FEAI/AAAAAAAAIH4/OmE3dFBp_-A/s640/IMG_5584.JPG" width="480"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              Happiness was also spending time with my lovely friends. Both of whom mean the world to me.
              <br/>

              This weekend was all about me and my inner guide.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              I decided to take a road trip and simply listen to my spirit on where to go and it was fucking amazing.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              All week, I had this yearning to visit the Spillway. It's this badass waterfall near where I went to college. It has been a place of peace, serenity and renewing for me for many years. When I arrived, it was breathtaking.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Since it was a little flooded, the waters were rushing and misty. The entire time I was there I felt surrounded by love and peace. I did some meditating and listened to what spirit had to say then I cleansed my hands in the water. I released some heavy junk there! I instantly felt refreshed and renewed. My soul needed that badly.

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2015/04/you-learn-by-living.html">
               You Learn By Living
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3735874491459143096" itemprop="articleBody">

                My Year with Eleanor
               </a>
              </i>
              and
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/You-Learn-Living-Eleanor-Roosevelt-ebook/dp/B004U6WYJU/ref=sr_1_1?s=digital-text&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1429443298&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=you+learn+by+living" style="font-style: italic;" target="_blank">
               You Learn by Living
              </a>
              .
              <br/>
              <br/>
              In this time, I have accomplished things I never dreamed of. I went
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2014/06/i-went-skydiving.html" target="_blank">
               skydiving
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2014/04/i-ran-my-first-5k.html" target="_blank">
               ran my first 5K
              </a>
              , went to
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2015/02/i-went-to-mardi-gras.html" target="_blank">
               Mardi Gras
              </a>
              and began taking care of myself better than I ever have. My mantra has been,
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <i>
                "You must do the thing you think you cannot do."-Eleanor Roosevelt
               </i>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <i>
                <br/>
               </i>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               The quote has meant so much to me, I recently had it tattooed:
              </div>

               <br/>
              </div>

               <br/>
              </div>

                  <a href="https://instagram.com/p/0099icv7r7/" style="color: #c9c8cd; font-family: Arial,sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: 17px; text-decoration: none;" target="_top">

               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                Through my living, I have learned a great deal about myself, realized what's important to me and have taken serious ownership over my life. The experiences I threw myself into, gave me gifts far beyond what I could have imagined. This was mostly because I have chosen to be present in my life and show up for myself.
               </div>

                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                During this time, I gained the strength to move on from a relationship that had not been serving me for quite some time. Yup, I got a divorce. It is
                <i>
                 part
                </i>
                of the reason I have slowed down my posting over the past six months.
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                The other reason I have slowed down is because I have been out among the world living my life. While I love writing this blog, I had felt an obligation to "keep up" so to speak. I was getting overwhelmed, found myself comparing to others and just genuinely not happy with the experience anymore. About 3 months ago, I decided to blog when I felt like it and to allow it to flow how it would.
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <br/>

               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                I hope you all examine your life and go out there and live, too. It's an amazing experience.

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2015/03/100-pure-vanilla-bean-body-wash-and.html">
               100% Pure Vanilla Bean Body Wash and Lotion Review
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-391218589053484373" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div style="text-align: right;">
               affiliate links
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               Let's talk about body care for a moment. For me, having soft supple skin is really important. I'm not sure how or why it's been this way but I have always found a need to use a nourishing body wash and lotion to achieve this. If not, I'm scale city.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               Last May, I picked up 100% Pure's
               <a href="http://www.100percentpure.com/collections/gels-and-washes/products/vanilla-bean-hydrating-body-wash" target="_blank">
                Vanilla Bean Hydrating Body Wash
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/loadRetailerProductPage?id=468073694&amp;pid=uid7076-23178226-93" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                Body Cream
               </a>
               . My Ulta was running a BOGO sale, so that's why I picked them up. Otherwise, they're $16+ a pop, which is way outside of my budget.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               100% Pure is a company that derives their products from natural ingredients. The tout that their products are free of toxic ingredients. They are a cruelty free company. The products are available
               <a href="http://www.100percentpure.com/" target="_blank">
                online
               </a>
               or at Ulta.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4n3S3R5zgyA/VQ69rCGP7oI/AAAAAAAAH9A/BksLbjUvtD8/s1600/100.percent.pure.body.wash.1.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="100% Pure Vanilla Bean" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4n3S3R5zgyA/VQ69rCGP7oI/AAAAAAAAH9A/BksLbjUvtD8/s1600/100.percent.pure.body.wash.1.png" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ALOanMUAvMY/VQ69qoGCc_I/AAAAAAAAH88/CofhHFAroLQ/s1600/100.percent.pure.body.wash.2.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="100% Pure Vanilla Bean" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ALOanMUAvMY/VQ69qoGCc_I/AAAAAAAAH88/CofhHFAroLQ/s1600/100.percent.pure.body.wash.2.png" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The body wash is still in my shower and has yet to be completely used up (I have a few more pumps left). I'm on 10 months with it! This was a huge surprise to me because body washes typically see 3-4 months in my shower. This wash pumps out into a foam. I need about 2-3 pumps to cover my whole body. The scent is a light vanilla and smells quite a bit like ice cream. It dissipates rather quickly, though. I don't find it particularly hydrating. It's a very average body wash. Due to cost and it not being a holy grail, it's not something I'm picking up again. The
              <a href="http://www.100percentpure.com/collections/gels-and-washes/products/vanilla-bean-hydrating-body-wash" target="_blank">
               body wash is $16
              </a>
              for 17 ounces.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/--sGO3_fA6Sk/VQ69qA68UwI/AAAAAAAAH80/AR8a6alqgVI/s1600/100.percent.pure.lotion.1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="100% Pure Vanilla Bean" border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/--sGO3_fA6Sk/VQ69qA68UwI/AAAAAAAAH80/AR8a6alqgVI/s1600/100.percent.pure.lotion.1.jpg" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-UPeJG9tzxd4/VQ69rtitARI/AAAAAAAAH9I/X_vAYIv0t6M/s1600/100.percent.pure.lotion.2.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="100% Pure Vanilla Bean" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-UPeJG9tzxd4/VQ69rtitARI/AAAAAAAAH9I/X_vAYIv0t6M/s1600/100.percent.pure.lotion.2.png" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              The lotion, on the other hand, lasted me only through summer. I had such mixed feelings about it. There were days I loved it and days I loathed it. When my skin was very thirsty, it absorbed this so quickly I couldn't spread it around. Therefore, I would have to use a ton of product. When my skin was fine, I had no issues. Either way, I did not find this moisturizing enough. For summer weather, it worked but I feel in the winter I would have been scaly. The scent is the same as the wash and doesn't linger on the skin. I chose not to purchase another bottle. The
              <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/loadRetailerProductPage?id=468073694&amp;pid=uid7076-23178226-93" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               lotion is $17
              </a>
              for 8 ounces.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Overall, I really love 100% Pure's philosophy and ingredients. These are very safe products to use for those who prefer a more natural lifestyle. I won't be purchasing them again due to the fact that I can find products that are just as good for less money. If you happen to run into a BOGO sale on their products, I recommend giving them a shot at least.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              xo, arielle

          <div class="date-posts">
           <div class="post-outer">
            <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
             <meta content="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Zj9jo2w_LNc/VPRPixth8sI/AAAAAAAAH7Q/Hi9MsVGaai0/s1600/pacifica.perfume.1.png" itemprop="image_url"/>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://www.beautybyarielle.com/2015/03/pacifica-indian-coconut-nectar-perfume.html">
               Pacifica Indian Coconut Nectar Perfume
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2060218774007719520" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div style="text-align: right;">
               affiliate links
              </div>
              <br/>
              I know I haven't been updating at all lately. I have a lot going on in my personal life that has prevented me from doing so. I'm going to try to post when possible, so know that I haven't forgotten about BBA!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              When I switched to natural products, I knew that perfumes were one of the most chemical laden product out there. I was shopping at Ulta and the only thing I could find that was remotely close to being clean was the
              <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/loadRetailerProductPage?id=468106243&amp;pid=uid7076-23178226-93" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               Pacifica Roll-On Perfumes
              </a>
              . I took a sniff of the available scents and went with Indian Coconut Nectar.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Zj9jo2w_LNc/VPRPixth8sI/AAAAAAAAH7Q/Hi9MsVGaai0/s1600/pacifica.perfume.1.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Pacifica Perfume" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Zj9jo2w_LNc/VPRPixth8sI/AAAAAAAAH7Q/Hi9MsVGaai0/s1600/pacifica.perfume.1.png" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               Pacifica's roll-on perfume comes in 14 scents. I picked this one up for $12. The full size are $22 and there are 18 scents.
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               Pacifica touts that these are 100% vegan and cruelty free as well as paraben, phthalate and propylene glycol free. EWG rates this as a 4 (moderate hazard).
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-deaurilEHGc/VPRPjeyPf9I/AAAAAAAAH7U/nR3CC5MFu0s/s1600/pacifica.perfume.2.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Pacifica Perfume" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-deaurilEHGc/VPRPjeyPf9I/AAAAAAAAH7U/nR3CC5MFu0s/s1600/pacifica.perfume.2.png" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               I chose
               <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/loadRetailerProductPage?id=468106243&amp;pid=uid7076-23178226-93" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                Indian Coconut Nectar
               </a>
               because I prefer spicy scents. Of what was a available, this one had the least floral or sweetness to it. There are times I have caught this smelling a little like cotton candy, though. For the most part, it's light but a little sultry. I plan to pick up the Island Vanilla next (I absolutely love it!!) but it's been out of stock every time I'm at Ulta.
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-DuZpfOwofmE/VPRPjpy4hVI/AAAAAAAAH7Y/Z3HlOhrrZsI/s1600/pacifica.perfume.3.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Pacifica Perfume" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-DuZpfOwofmE/VPRPjpy4hVI/AAAAAAAAH7Y/Z3HlOhrrZsI/s1600/pacifica.perfume.3.png" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               I have been happy with the roller-ball application, too. It makes it very simple plus, it's been very easy to travel with. I have been using it since June and still have at least half of the product left. It's not the longest lasting perfume I have ever used, though.
              </div>
              <br/>
              What is your favorite natural perfume?
              <br/>
              <br/>
              xo, arielle
