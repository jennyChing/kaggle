        <h2>
         Tips on Carpet Stain Removal
        </h2>
        <div class="entry">
         <div addthis:title="Tips on Carpet Stain Removal " addthis:url="http://www.clickandclean.com.au/tips-on-carpet-stain-removal/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">
          <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count">
          </a>
          <a class="addthis_button_tweet">
          </a>
          <a class="addthis_button_pinterest_pinit">
          </a>
          <a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style">
          </a>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="Carpet Cleaners Melbourne" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-159" height="167" src="http://clickandclean.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Carpet-Cleaners-Melbourne-300x218.jpg" title="Carpet Cleaners Melbourne" width="230"/>
          There are many different types of stain that your carpet can get. Sometimes it is really hard to get rid of them. Of course, there is no recipe for removing any type of stain. But there are some things which would help you in most of the cases. Here they are.
          <span id="more-157">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          When you clean stains, it is very important to try acting immediately.
          <a href="http://carpetcleaninginsydney.net/" target="_blank">
           Carpet cleaning experts in Sydney
          </a>
          advise you to start the cleaning process as fast as possible. That will ensure the cleaning process will be as effective as possible.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The first step is to get a cotton cloth and remove as much of the spill as you can. Do not rub the stain, just dab it. Be careful not to spread the stain and make the situation worse. If you are dealing with some kind of spillage, you can use some paper towels. Put a stack of them on the affected spot and press hard to absorb the liquid.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Once you have removed the excess of the material, you can continue with preparing a cleaning solution. Mix some liquid detergent with water. Get a soft brush and scrub the affected area with the solution. Be careful not to scrub too hard to avoid causing damage to the carpet fibres.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          When the stain is gone, you need to rinse with plenty of water and dry the area. You can use your hair dryer for that.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Dealing with stains can be very difficult. If you cannot do it on your own, you should ask some professional company for help.
          <em>
           <strong>
            Carpet cleaners within Sydney
           </strong>
          </em>
          would be a very good choice.
         </p>
