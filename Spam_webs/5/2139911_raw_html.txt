        <p>
         It is no longer news that zorrovox has parted ways with A+. now they have collaborated with a cccam account service provider to produce a one year special account for their box. This one year account is in the form of a standard cccam account, but it cant wwork on any other decoder apart from zorro box. For the account to be used on zorro box z3 successfully, you need to download a special software and load it to your zorro box. The software will be uploaded in this article.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i1.wp.com/lemmymorgan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/zorro-box-z3.jpg">
          <img alt="picture of zorro box z2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4400" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/lemmymorgan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/zorro-box-z3.jpg?resize=284%2C177"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Liek i said, the account is in form of a cccam account and it will be loaded like a cccam account. However, this account cannot work for any other decoder except zorro. The account is for one year, and it is generally cheaper than any standalone cccam account you can think of.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         HOW TO LOAD NEW ACCOUNT TYPE INTO YOUR ZORRO BOX Z3 DECODER
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         From whomever you are buying the account, he must supply you with the software as a matter of compulsion. The reason being that without the software, the account will be useless. The software will be loaded via usb upgrade same way you load a software into qsat, tlink, g6 and strong decoders. To load the account, you complete the followings;
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Download the software:
          <a href="https://www.dropbox.com/s/ypqv06wc7zz6dqn/lemmy_20140523_zorro%20box%20.rar">
           <span id="pyxl7170163582580466601">
            lemmy_20140523_zorro box .rar
           </span>
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          Then Upgrade the decoder with the software provided
         </li>
         <li>
          Now go to menu&gt;&gt;Setting&gt;&gt;XCAM
         </li>
         <li>
          Select  Sharing 
         </li>
         <li>
          go to Sever 1
         </li>
         <li>
          Then fill the columns with the details of your account
         </li>
         <li>
          sever ip : ************ hiden
          <br/>
          &gt; port must be:  13000
          <br/>
          &gt; user : ( it is blank ,will show after you put username)
          <br/>
          &gt; pwd : ( it is blank ,will show ************* after you input pwd)
          <br/>
          of course you need to use a string 3g, wifi or lan internet to power this for maximum enjoyment. Note this account is not the end itself, it is(like every other accounts subject to downtimes or service disruption) But by and large, you will get value for your money.
         </li>
