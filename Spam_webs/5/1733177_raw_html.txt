                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="https://plus.google.com/111560657218468059256/posts">
                    JACQUELINE MEDINA
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   E- Cig Reviews: Before Buying, Know t Type of E-Cigarette Warranty That Should You Choose
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  E Cigars have been in the mainstream since last year and thats the reason why there are lots of e-cig reviews about this device. For those who want to quit smoking and for some who want to find a healthy alternative for smoking, e cigars could be a good idea.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  When it comes to finding the perfect e-cigarette, you need to take some time, as there are so many brands, models, and types available. Moreover, you will also consider how it works, its price and take into account the warranty of an e-cigarette. Every company of e-cigar has a different warranty policy, which can usually be of any length of time. Some companies provide 30 days, one year, or even lifetime warranty that can replace defective parts of your devices even for as long as the terms of the previous sales are met. But the question now is, how can you know which one is right for you? Should you buy an e-cigar with a lifetime warranty or not?
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="http://smokechat.com">
                   <img alt="5896655259510547572.jpg" height="316" src="http://h.kiwibox.com/ip/5896655259510547572.jpg" title="User image" width="525"/>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  As said earlier, there are some companies who provide lifetime e-cigarette warranties if you sign up for their automatic home delivery system. Each company has a different set of conditions. Just by having a warranty, it only means that you will receive a certain amount of refills every month. This offer is specially created and designed for people who dont want to run out of cartridges or accessories and continue enjoying their electronic cigarette.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="http://smokechat.com">
                    E-Cig Reviews
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                  provides another important program that you need to consider is the lifetime warranty. Usually, many people often misled by the term lifetime. As a matter of fact, most of them when they hear lifetime warranty, it means to them that this program will be offered forever, but the fact is, if you say lifetime, it accessible for the lifetime of the product.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img alt="5896655408939825390.jpg" height="500" src="http://h.kiwibox.com/ip/5896655408939825390.jpg" title="User image" width="500"/>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  It is very important to know what type of warranty you should pay attention because some brands offer only 5 replacements even they offer a lifetime warranty. Through e-cig reviews you can also have wide vision about different prices of batteries at companies that offer lifetime warranties which offer short-term coverage. Usually, when it comes to choosing the extended warranty, you will pay much more in advance for batteries.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  If you hate dealing with complicated warranties, you can choose a basic warranty. A basic warranty can avoid complications as this type of warranty can last anywhere from 30 to 90 days. Plus, you are not required to send the defective parts back before receiving a replacement. In a basic warranty, you are paying around $10 for batteries, instead of $30 as in the case of long term warranties. Many companies will send a replacement for defective parts even though your warranty expired and they are quite flexible in that matter.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Overall, both lifetime and short coverage
                  <strong>
                   <a href="http://www.encyclopedia.com/topic/Warranty.aspx">
                    warranties
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                  have their perks and you need to find the one that suits your needs better. It should be the one that allows you to enjoy your electronic cigarette for a long term.
                 </p>
