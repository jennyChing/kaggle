                       <div style="margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 0.5em; text-align: justify;">
                        <em>
                         <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                          Skill is an ability to accomplish complex activities by incorporating knowledge based ideas. The skillset possessed by an employee is an essential prerequisite before grasping a responsible position in an organization. However, the skills necessary for success are often not included in academic curriculums but are required to be nurtured through individual practical implementations.
                         </span>
                        </em>
                       </div>

                      <div class="itemFullText" style="background-color: white; line-height: 22.22222137451172px;">

                        <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                         There is a huge skill gap between educationally acquired knowledge and the industrial requirements expected from an individual at the workplace. Moreover, in the current scenario of Indias rapidly evolving and rising economy a huge resource of skilled people are required to sustain its future economic ambitions. What so ever organizations serve as a platform that provides skills with which an individual can build and develop skills necessary to be successful.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 0.5em; text-align: justify;">
                       </div>
                       <ul style="text-align: justify;">
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                          The current industrial environment requires making use of formally educated young generation of Indias workforce by providing them on job and practical training. Fortunately, in the rapidly evolving industrial environment organizations serve as the base for acquiring practically usable knowledge. The correct career skills can be developed by identifying and following the patterns of skill implementations in the organizations.
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                          New technologies are being implemented consistently to enhance the market competency levels of the organizations. In order to be competent and productive, it has become mandatory for each employee to update their skill levels with the practical knowledge on latest technologies. Hence, acquiring knowledge in a consistently evolving organizational environment fueled by technological innovations gives an edge to the employees for succeeding further with every new change.
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                          Improvising individual intelligence with the help of knowledge should be the passion of each employee who aspires to be successful in an organizational environment. The global economic structure is getting more dependent on intelligent mental efforts rather than the mere physical hard work. Hence, it is necessary for the working individuals to concentrate more on structuring their intelligence levels synchronized with technological advancements.
                         </span>
                        </li>
                       </ul>

                        <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif; text-align: justify;">

As a highly populated nation, India can only succeed as an economic giant in the global market when evolution of individual intelligence and an knowledge seeking work environment is encouraged with an all round effort.
