           <p>
            We need
            <strong>
             5000
            </strong>
            to rejuvinate hidden corners of Regents Canal in London
           </p>
          </div>
          <!-- PLAYGROUND ORIGINAL BLOCK -->
          <div class="mstd-content-block">
           <p>
            <strong>
             The Regents Canal, which cuts straight through the heart of London, is over 200 years old. We need your help to transform it into an urban oasis for people and wildlife.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            We need 5,000 to
            <strong>
             make a lasting difference to this area.
            </strong>
            We are looking to re-establish plant life in neglected pockets of the Regents Canal and reclaim areas of this urban towpath through the planting of shrubs and bushes that will encourage more wildlife and people to visit.
           </p>
           <p>
            The makeover will include planting fruit and nut treesso thatmembers ofthe communitycan enjoy free scrumping, alongside
            <strong>
             wildflower seeds and bulbs
            </strong>
            which will bring some much needed colour to the towpath throughout the seasons.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              5
             </strong>
             could buy a flowering shrub with berries
            </li>
           </ul>
           <hr/>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              20
             </strong>
             could provide an apple tree
            </li>
           </ul>
           <hr/>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              100
             </strong>
             will pay for enough vegetable and herb plants to supply community grow-your-own containers
            </li>
           </ul>
           <hr/>
           <p>
            By making a donation today, you can help us host climbing night-scented flowering plants on trellis to
            <strong>
             encourage bats into the area
            </strong>
            and screen off heritage-protected walls from damage by graffiti. Or you could help us provide community
            <strong>
             grow-your-own opportunities
            </strong>
            along the canal-side. We need to raise 5,000 so planting can begin.
            <strong>
             Please donate today.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Support this project now and play your part in preserving this special corridor through London for generations to come.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>

           <p>
            There are pockets along the Regents Canal that, for various reasons, have been neglected and forgotten over time. We want to reclaim these for local communities, visitors and wildlife by clearing out the litter and damage, and breathing new, green life back into them. Not only can we support populations of bumblebees, butterflies, dragonflies and other insects but we can also encourage birds and bats to make their homes in the heart of our capital.
           </p>
