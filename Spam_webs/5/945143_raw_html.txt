         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           Teen Wolfs Meagan Tandy: A Model for Self-Empowerment
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="post-meta-author">
           Posted by:
           <a href="http://thehollywoodbillboard.com/author/quinn-vincent-hough/" title="">
            Quinn Vincent Hough
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="tie-date">
           08/11/2014
          </span>
          <span class="post-comments">
           <a href="http://thehollywoodbillboard.com/teen-wolfs-meagan-tandy/#respond">
            0
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           <strong>
            EXCLUSIVE |
           </strong>
           Is there a soul in Hollywood more dedicated to the fans than
           <em>
            Teen Wolf
           </em>
           s
           <strong>
            Meagan Tandy
           </strong>
           ? I caught
           <span id="more-2714">
           </span>
           up with the MTV star last Friday as she navigated LAs San Fernando Valley and we discussed social media, the misunderstandings of beauty and her potentially iconic MTV character, Braeden.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tandy was raised 50 miles east of Los Angeles and understands the persuasive power of Hollywood on the youth of America. In fact, the graduate of Rancho Cucamongas Chaffey College was recently presented with her alma maters Alumni of the Year Award for her enduring social work.
          </p>
          <p>
           I wasnt expecting it at all, she said. The cool thing about is that I didnt get it for being an actress. I created and founded my own program called Girl Talk, which is for girls of ages 11 to 16, and its always good to give back in some sort of way. I dont do it for recognition, and for them to recognize me was just really awesome.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tandy, a former Miss California USA, developed her Hollywood skills in college while studying film production, and although she has nothing but admiration for her
           <em>
            Teen Wolf
           </em>
           followers, the MTV fan favorite acknowledges that self-respect, and not beauty, is the key to a happy life.
          </p>
          <p>
           There are girls trying to model themselves after
           <strong>
            Kim Kardashian
           </strong>
           or
           <strong>
            Britney Spears
           </strong>
           , and think, thats what beauty is. Lets face it: we (actresses) are wearing make-up, and weve got hair extensions, so they have to understand that were putting it on too.
          </p>
          <p>
           The day before our conversation, Tandy was the focus of the Twitter hashtag #MeaganAppreciationDay. After some Teen Wolf-Twitter investigation, I presented Tandy with a tweet from one of her most prolific fans, @TeenWolfHub, who was filled with a blend of affection and surprise: I love Meagan Tandy so much and I didnt even know this was a thing.
          </p>
          <p>
           You know whats funny? I know exactly who that isI believe his name is Cole and hes one of my biggest fans. Hes so sweet, she said.
          </p>
          <p>
           After our interview, Tandy sent out a series of tweets to her fan base, including @TeenWolfHub, and even reached out to the fortunate fellow a couple days later. Tandy welcomes the Wolfpack with open arms after a somewhat troubling experience as bad girl Lulu Pope on ABC Familys
           <em>
            Jane By Design
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           If I only had 100 fans for
           <em>
            Jane By Design
           </em>
           , I could tell you that 96 of them hated me, she said. Its definitely flipped around. Once you have fans, you get it. I love them to death. Theyve been really amazing.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tandy has slowly generated her own following as Braeden, the loveable (and dangerous) mercenary every Monday on MTVs
           <em>
            Teen Wolf
           </em>
           , and kept Twitter abuzz with a badass motorcycle arrival in the season 4 premiere earlier this year. I asked Meagan if she received any specialized training for her role.
          </p>
          <p>
           No, not at all, she answered. Ive done Tae Bo, Ive done boxing, but thats about it. Everything that you see I literally have learned right there on the spot.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tandys career was in full bloom after placing 3
           <sup>
            rd
           </sup>
           -runner up in the 2007 Miss USA pageant, and her life-long passion for film production and acting led to an audition as
           <strong>
            Denzel Washington
           </strong>
           s daughter in
           <strong>
            Tony Scott
           </strong>
           s 2010 film
           <em>
            Unstoppable
           </em>
           . After six weeks of waiting, Tandy landed the prized role in what would ultimately be the finale of Tony Scotts long and superlative career.
          </p>
          <p>
           That man, honestly, was such a ball of fire, Tandy said. He literally had a vibrant, fun spirit about him  absolutely hilarious. Every time we went on set he always made it fun and was just silly, which was cool because Im a silly person myself.
          </p>
          <p>
           After working with a first-rate director, I asked Tandy if she was curious about stepping behind the camera and utilizing the skills of her collegiate days.
          </p>
          <p>
           Absolutely. Thats how I got into acting, she said. I was literally creating my own video projects with our video camera at home, and took two years of film production. I love being behind the camera. Its fun to edit, its fun to direct a scene and its fun to put your creative mind behind it.
          </p>
          <p>
           When it comes to acting, she is an ardent supporter of former co-star Denzel Washington, along with the Fresh Prince himself,
           <strong>
            Will Smith
           </strong>
           , and believes that
           <strong>
            Chadwick Boseman
           </strong>
           , the star of both
           <em>
            42
           </em>
           and
           <em>
            Get On Up
           </em>
           , could be the next big thing.
          </p>
          <p>
           I feel like he is getting some really good roles right now, and not a whole lot of people know about him, she said. As far as a young African-American man, it seems to be him. Hes coming up.
          </p>
          <p>
           What can we expect from Braeden moving forward?
          </p>
          <p>
           You can expect Braeden to soften out a little bit, Tandy said. Shes tough but youre going to see a slight shift in her. Shes supposed to care about the pack and whats going on. Its not about the money anymore. Now its about who shes working with.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tandy has only appeared in a handful (or two) of
           <em>
            Teen Wolf
           </em>
           episodes, but has established herself as one of the most exciting and promising actresses on television. She wakes up early for scar treatment, and through her invaluable work with Girl Talk, Tandy is all too familiar with the psychological scars that affect young women worldwide.
          </p>
          <p>
           #MeaganAppreciationDay
           <em>
            is
           </em>
           a real thing, however a true recognition of Meagan Tandy the human being would be to look beyond all the glitz and glamour of Hollywood.
          </p>
          <p>
           Follow Meagan Tandy on
           <a href="https://twitter.com/meagantandyhttps:/twitter.com/meagantandy">
            Twitter
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://instagram.com/meagantandy">
            Instagram
           </a>
          </p>
