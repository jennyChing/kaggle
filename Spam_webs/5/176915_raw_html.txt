         <p>
          Last week, I shared my
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/a-favorite-find/">
           favorite find
          </a>
          so far this spring. In that post, I told you that I had picked up another fabulous piece at that same sale.
         </p>
         <p>
          This post is all about that piece.
         </p>
         <p>
          This dresser was hidden in the garage with a ton of glassware on the top. I didnt even know if it was for sale so I asked. The guy said, Yes! He gave me another incredibly, low price and I immediately said, Sold.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is what this dresser looked like when I bought it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16843" height="647" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Unlike the
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/a-favorite-find/">
           other piece
          </a>
          I bought at that sale, this dresser needed some help.
         </p>
         <p>
          When I looked at it closer, it looked like they painted it a cream color and then added stain over the paint???
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser1.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16844" height="1200" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser1.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Whatever they were trying to do, it didnt work.
         </p>
         <p>
          Just like my favorite piece, I gave this dressera good bath. As I was washing it, the paint was coming off. I started thinking that maybe I could strip this dresser easily and stain it instead of painting it.
         </p>
         <p>
          Now, I have stripped and stained a few pieces in the past and I know why people opt to just paint pieces. Painting is a lot easier. Stripping and staining involves A LOT of time and work, but it can be the most rewarding job in the end.
         </p>
         <p>
          I decided to go for it with this dresser.
         </p>
         <p>
          The paint did come off fairly easily in most places, but then I was left with really dark spots in certain places. After I removed all the stripper, I sanded it all.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is what I ended up with.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser2.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16845" height="1180" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser2.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I didnt even notice there was a design on the middle drawer when I bought this piece.
          <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          As you can see, the paint didnt come off in a few places.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser4.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16847" height="1029" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser4.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I was okay with that. Perfect and I arent good friends anyway.
         </p>
         <p>
          I thought I would run with staining it and see what I ended up with. The worst case scenario would be that I would have to paint the dresser. I was most worried about the dark spots and how the stain would look over them. Would it cover them?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser3.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16846" height="1200" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser3.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This is how it came out!
         </p>
         <p>
          I could not be happier!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/final.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16857" height="1200" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/final.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I am coming to the finishing stages of making over my daughters room and this dresser has been a great addition.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser6.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16849" height="1200" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser6.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          To be honest, I love that you can still see some of the white paint in places.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser7.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16850" height="1200" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser7.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ill take
          <em>
           imperfect
          </em>
          any day. It has so much character.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser8.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16851" height="1196" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser8.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="801"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          For some reason, these pictures are showing the dresser kind of blotchy, but it isnt at all. The stain really looks nice and doesnt have all the variation that is showing up in the pictures.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser5.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16848" height="1327" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser5.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser10.jpg">
           <img alt="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" class="alignnone wp-image-16853" height="791" src="http://www.mycreativedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/dresser10.jpg" title="A Dresser Makeover Without Paint mycreativedays.com" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I only have a few more things to do with my daughters room and then it will be done. I will be sharing more of the projects we did in her room soon!
         </p>
         <p>
          Stay tuned!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
