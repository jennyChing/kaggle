      <h1>
       Harmony Day
      </h1>
      <div id="post-2">
       <p>
        Harmony Day (21 March) celebrates Australias cultural diversity. Its about inclusiveness, respect and a sense of belonging for everyone.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span class="highlight">
         Thank you for celebrating our 15
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         birthday!
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Thank you, for celebrating Harmony Days 15
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        birthday.
       </p>
       <p>
        Events and activities were celebrated across Australia, from the smallest child care centres to the largest businesses, sharing the message everyone belongs.
       </p>
       <p>
        To showcase the last 15 years of Harmony Day we are creating a Hall of Fame on our website and want your images and stories. Please provide us with the following information:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         your historical images with captions in 70 words or less
        </li>
        <li>
         include the year and the place the images where captured
        </li>
        <li>
         send them to our email
         <a href="mailto:harmonyday@dss.gov.au">
          harmonyday@dss.gov.au
         </a>
         by Friday, 31 May 2015.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        To continue to get updated information on Harmony Day activities and stories please follow Follow or like us on
        <a href="http://www.facebook.com/harmonydayAUS" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.facebook.com']);">
         Facebook
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.twitter.com/harmonyday_AU" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.twitter.com']);">
         Twitter
        </a>
        or
        <a href="http://www.instagram.com/harmonyday_AU" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.instagram.com']);">
         Instagram
        </a>
        .
       </p>

        <h2>
         Recipes for Harmony
        </h2>
        <p>
         <em>
          <a href="/schools/recipes-for-harmony/">
           Recipes for Harmony
          </a>
         </em>
         brings the world to classrooms by introducing  students to diverse Australians, their culture and traditional dishes in a fun  and interactive way. The online resource features recipes, cultural profiles  and personal stories from every-day and high profile Australians such as AFL  player Bachar Houli and Masterchef contestant Alice Zaslavsky.
        </p>
        <p>
         A teachers resource includes  example lesson plans, classroom activities and worksheets for primary school  students from foundation through to late primary school years.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Recipes for Harmony
         </em>
         was  funded by the Australian Government
         <a href="http://www.dss.gov.au" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-widget','http://www.dss.gov.au']);">
          Department  of Social Services
         </a>
         and the
         <a href="http://www.scanlonfoundation.org.au" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-widget','http://www.scanlonfoundation.org.au']);">
          Scanlon Foundation
         </a>
         , as part of Harmony Day  and a
         <a href="http://www.tasteofharmony.org.au/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-widget','http://www.tasteofharmony.org.au']);">
          Taste of Harmony
         </a>
         celebrations.
        </p>
