        My trip to Brookdale Treeland Nurseries Limited (BTN) {PC Insiders Report}

        <p>
         This pastWednesday, I had the wonderful opportunity to visit Brookdale Treeland Nurseries Limited (BTN) located nearSussex inNew Brunswick.
        </p>
        <p>
         BTN is a wholesale operation and what you may not know is they supply tropical plants, perennials,evergreens, trees and shrubs found in our Atlantic SuperstoreFlower Markets.
         <span id="more-14066">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/037-edited-w-sig-resized.jpg">
          <img alt="037 edited w sig resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14904" height="450" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/037-edited-w-sig-resized.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         {This is just a small corner of the very largeBrookdale Treeland Nurseries Limited operation near Sussex, NB}
        </p>
        <p>
         Brookdale Treeland Nurseries Limited have five locations across the country (so they are the suppliers for your Loblaw store too!) and have been inoperation since 1983. They are one of the largest growers of nursery stock and perennial plants inCanadaand their products are available in all Loblaw Companies Limited garden centres.
        </p>
        <p>
         I was thrilled when I learned the plants and trees I had been purchasing from the Atlantic Superstore garden centres were grown so close to home. Im not going to lie, I always thought the garden centreproductsfound inAtlanticSuperstores were grown in Ontario and then shipped to Atlantic Canada.
        </p>
        <p>
         Brookdale Treeland Nurseries Limited is recognized for the innovative and creative products that it grows which are designed for todays consumer. They have been a proud member of the team of Loblaw growers for nearly 20 years.
        </p>
        <p>
         This yearBTNis thrilled to present many exclusives you will only find under the Presidents Choice label, plus exciting products and new plant colours that will take your breath away!
        </p>
        <p>
         On my recent visit to BTNI had the opportunity to see many of the gorgeous plants thatBTN supplies for the
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/presidentschoice/app_665841670098226" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Insiders Report
         </a>
         . I have included some descriptions and also some photos of many of them including:
        </p>
        <p>
         PC Oleander Braid (New &amp; Exclusive to PC) Three oleander plants growing together with intertwining trunks is spectacular! This plant is like a spectacular bouquet with a mild fragrance.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/059-edited-resized.jpg">
          <img alt="059 edited resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14896" height="600" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/059-edited-resized.jpg" width="453"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ~ Intertwining Trunks ~
        </p>
        <p>
         PC Gardenia/PC Hibiscus Braid/PC Mandevilla  last year the fragrant Gardenia, the vividtri-colour Hibiscus and the pink, yellow centered Mandevilla were all very popular and they are back to transform your deck or patio into a tropical oasis!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/004-edited-w-sig-Hibiscus-resized.jpg">
          <img alt="004 edited w sig Hibiscus resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14902" height="406" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/004-edited-w-sig-Hibiscus-resized.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ~ PC Hibiscus Braid ~ {The vibrant colours are stunning!}
        </p>
        <p>
         PC Haskap 2 in 1  (New &amp; Exclusive to PC)Until about a year ago I was not familiar with the Haskap berry so I was very excited to see this plant. The plump and juicy Haskap berries taste like a cross between blueberries, raspberries and Saskatoon berries. This year the breeder suggested planting 2 compatible varieties in one pot for proper cross-pollination, to ensure lots of fruit. One of the earliest fruits to ripen, they are perfect for jams, baking or straight from the bush!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/PicMonkey-Collage-resized.png">
          <img alt="PicMonkey Collage resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14905" height="300" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/PicMonkey-Collage-resized.png" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ~PC Haskap 2 in 1 ~ {Be sure you check to be sure you have both plants in the one pot}
        </p>
        <p>
         PC DoubleBerryPlanter (New &amp; Exclusive to PC) Why choose between juicy blueberries and succulent strawberries when you can enjoy both in the new double berry planter; perfect for the deck or patio!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/069-edited-w-sig-resized.jpg">
          <img alt="069 edited w sig resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14898" height="488" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/069-edited-w-sig-resized.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ~ PC DoubleBerryPlanter ~
        </p>
        <p>
         PC Clematis Esme/PC ClematisAbilene/PC Clematis Planter on Trellis (New &amp; Exclusive to PC) You dont need a big backyard to grow these beautiful plants, these come in a decorator planter already in flower and climbing up a mini trellis. All you need to do is place it on your deck, patio, balcony or porch and enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/024-edited-with-sig-Clematis-resized.jpg">
          <img alt="024 edited with sig Clematis resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14903" height="489" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/024-edited-with-sig-Clematis-resized.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ~ PC ClematisAbilene on trellis ~ {I LOVE how the actual plant looks exactly like the plant pictured on the tag!}
        </p>
        <p>
         PC Patio Accents, Evergreen Planters  PC asked its growers to create gorgeous and affordable evergreen planters as a great new planter option. These long low planters would be perfect as an accent or would also make a super divider!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/076-edited-w-sig-resized.jpg">
          <img alt="076 edited w sig resized" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14899" height="541" src="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/076-edited-w-sig-resized.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ~ One of thePC Patio Accents, Evergreen Planters ~
        </p>
        <p>
         I had a wonderful morning at Brookdale Treeland Nurseries andI want to say a special thank you to Joe Sturgeon who was kind enough to take a couple hours from his very busy day to give me comprehensive and very informative tour of their spacious wholesale garden operation.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oldermommystillyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/18B369325ED7EDE30F777565E7D2727A.png">

         </a>

         </left>
        </p>
