        </a>
       </strong>
       . Optimized for race tracks, this GTR boasts 986 horsepower using the same powertrain as available in McLaren P1, which typically produces 890 horsepower. Once in the market, this will be the most powerful car manufactured by McLaren Automotive.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-2297">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       Besides just tweaking the engine,
       <strong>
        <a href="http://cars.mclaren.com/home/models_link/McLAREN%20P1/mclaren-p1-gtr.html" target="_blank" title="McLaren P1 GTR">
         McLaren P1 GTR
        </a>
       </strong>
       encompasses several other modifications in the overall bodywork. The most prominent of these is the 80mm wider front track coupled with a newly added front splitter that seems quite bigger than expected. The retractable wing on the rear is replaced by a fixed height wing, which allows greater down force with increased aerodynamics.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://cdn.dubaicars.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/McLaren-P1-GTR.jpg" target="_blank" title="McLaren P1 GTR">
        <img alt="McLaren P1 GTR" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2298" height="417" src="http://cdn.dubaicars.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/McLaren-P1-GTR.jpg" width="625"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The P1 GTR has gone much lower and features a unique set of 19 inch motorsport alloy wheels. Moreover, it derives an onboard air jacking system from 650S GT3 for convenient tyre change. As the engine capacity has been increased by almost 100 horsepower, brakes and engine cooling system are also upgraded accordingly.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://cdn.dubaicars.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/McLaren-P1-GTR1.jpg" target="_blank" title="McLaren P1 GTR">
        <img alt="McLaren P1 GTR" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2299" height="417" src="http://cdn.dubaicars.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/McLaren-P1-GTR1.jpg" width="625"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       With this new GTR concept, the British automaker aims to take its most significant production car to the race track. This track-only car is announced to celebrate their victory in 24 Hours of Le Mans, two decades ago.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://cdn.dubaicars.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/McLaren-P1-GTR2.jpg" target="_blank" title="McLaren P1 GTR">
        <img alt="McLaren P1 GTR" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2300" height="417" src="http://cdn.dubaicars.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/McLaren-P1-GTR2.jpg" width="625"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       McLaren P1 GTR will go for manufacturing next year, after the production of street-legal P1 is finished. Although the automaker has not communicated the exact number of units to be produced, we can confidently infer it would be limited and lower than that of
       <strong>
        <a href="http://cars.mclaren.com/home/models_link/McLAREN%20P1/introduction.html" target="_blank" title="McLaren P1">
         McLaren P1
        </a>
       </strong>
       (375 units). However, according to an official it could be 50 units or more, but nothing is confirmed.
      </p>
      <p>
       The McLaren P1 GTR will cost around GBP 1.98 million in UK and will be exclusive to McLaren P1 owners only.
      </p>
