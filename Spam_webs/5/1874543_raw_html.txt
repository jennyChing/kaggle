        <p>
         <a href="http://cdn2.business2community.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Little-Rascals.jpg">
          <img alt="The Little Rascals" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-998363" height="408" src="http://cdn2.business2community.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Little-Rascals.jpg" width="600"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         In 1994
         <em>
          The Little Rascals
         </em>
         movie debuted and the movie poster for the film quickly became a classic. While the original cast of the TV series had long grown up, the new actors in the film did a great job of portraying the original cast in a new, more modern film version of the once popular TV series.
        </p>
        <p>
         In honor of the films 20th anniversary, the original cast got together for a remake of their original poster.
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres the original Little Rascals poster:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cdn2.business2community.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Little-Rascals-original.jpg">
          <img alt="The Little Rascals Original Poster" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-998364" height="894" src="http://cdn2.business2community.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/The-Little-Rascals-original.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
