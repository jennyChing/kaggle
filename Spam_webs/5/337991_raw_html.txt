&lt;div class="intro"&gt;

	&lt;p&gt;Finding the right gift, one that really means something to a person, can be hard. It requires thought. Care. Focus. And when your brain is hijacked by holiday season planning, travel, and partiesits especially challenging.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;/div&gt;

&lt;/div&gt;


&lt;link rel="stylesheet" href="/t/etc/clientlibs/attthread/articleParagraph.css" type="text/css"/&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;

    &lt;div id="module-author" class="article-author"&gt;

        &lt;/div&gt;
    &lt;/div&gt;

&lt;link rel="stylesheet" href="/t/etc/clientlibs/attthread/articleAuthor.css" type="text/css"/&gt;

&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="articleParagraph section"&gt;


&lt;div class="article-body"&gt;

    &lt;p&gt;The easy answer is to not wait till the last minute. Heard that before, right? Year-round gift idea gathering can be really helpful, and for this I say: Get a good note-taking app on your phone so you can jot down an idea whenever it comes to you. I use &lt;a href="http://evernote.com/" target="_blank"&gt;Evernote&lt;/a&gt;. It lets me add pictures and links to my notes, but there are lots of other good &lt;a href="http://www.att.com/t/thread/simplify/technology?anchor=applicationrecommendation" target="_blank"&gt;note-taking apps&lt;/a&gt;, too.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;/div&gt;


&lt;link rel="stylesheet" href="/t/etc/clientlibs/attthread/moduleCommon.css" type="text/css"/&gt;

&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="articleParagraph section"&gt;


&lt;div class="article-body"&gt;

    &lt;p&gt;Another tip: Read online reviews about your gift ideas. And try to do it soon after the idea comes to you, so you know whether to keep it on your list.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;/div&gt;



&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="articleParagraph section"&gt;


&lt;div class="article-body"&gt;

    &lt;p&gt;But even though we know better, many of us still tend to procrastinate (in fact, this article is due on my editors desk tomorrow). I dont have an antidote for procrastination, but I can share my humble strategy for making the gifts I giveeven if theyre last minutemore meaningful. Its simply to focus on peoples interests. If your cast of characters is anything like mine, maybe I can help you with some gift ideas.&lt;/p&gt;
