        <h2 class="post-title">
         <a href="http://flowingmotion.jojordan.org/2011/06/07/where-are-those-qualities-of-bravery-and-sharp-compassion-in-this-group/" rel="bookmark" title="Where are those qualities of bravery and sharp compassion in this group?">
          Where are those qualities of bravery and sharp compassion in this group?
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <span class="post-date">
          <a href="http://flowingmotion.jojordan.org/2011/06/07/where-are-those-qualities-of-bravery-and-sharp-compassion-in-this-group/" title="Where are those qualities of bravery and sharp compassion in this group?">
           June 7, 2011
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="date-sep">
          /
         </span>
         <span class="post-author">
          <a href="http://flowingmotion.jojordan.org/author/JoJordan/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jo Jordan">
           Jo Jordan
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="date-sep">
          /
         </span>
         <a href="http://flowingmotion.jojordan.org/2011/06/07/where-are-those-qualities-of-bravery-and-sharp-compassion-in-this-group/#comments">
          1 Comment
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
       <!-- /post-header -->
       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         Theres courage involved if you want
         <br/>
         to become truth. There is a broken-
        </p>
        <p>
         open place in a lover. Where are
         <br/>
         those qualities of bravery and sharp
        </p>
        <p>
         compassion in this group? Whats the
         <br/>
         use of old and frozen thought? I want
        </p>
        <p>
         a howling hurt. This is not a treasury
         <br/>
         where gold is stored; this is for copper.
        </p>
        <p>
         We alchemists look for talent that
         <br/>
         can heat up and change. Lukewarm
        </p>
        <p>
         wont do. Halfhearted holding back,
         <br/>
         well-enough getting by? Not here.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Rumi
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         If anyone knows where this was published, could you let me know so I can add proper links here?
         <br/>
         Many thanks.
         <br/>
        </p>
