          <h2>
           Scientist Says Aliens Nuked Martians and We May be Next
          </h2>
          <div class="details">
           <ul>
            <li class="comments-link">
             <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/2014/11/scientist-says-aliens-nuked-martians-and-we-may-be-next/#comments">
              <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="39180 http://mysteriousuniverse.org/?p=39180">
               <strong>
                35
               </strong>
               Comments
              </span>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/2014/11/22/">
              <span class="icon-3">
              </span>
              November 22, 2014
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/author/paulseaburn/" rel="author">
              <span class="icon-4">
              </span>
              Paul Seaburn
             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="cl">
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           Why do many of the photographs of the surface of Mars appear to be of structures that look like theyve been destroyed and covered with dust? According to a new book by Dr.
           <a href="http://astroreview.com/author/john-e-brandenburg-ph-d" target="_blank" title="John E. Brandenburg Ph.D.">
            John Brandenburg
           </a>
           , its because two ancient Martian civilizations were annihilated by other aliens using nuclear weapons. Whats worse, we could be next.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_39183">
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/castle.jpg">
            <img alt="Image of what appears to be the ruins of a structure on the Martian surface." class="size-large wp-image-39183" height="237" src="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/castle-570x237.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Image of what appears to be the ruins of a structure on the Martian surface.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Brandenburgs book is called 
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Death-Mars-Discovery-Planetary-Massacre/dp/193914938X" target="_blank" title="Death on Mars: The Discovery of a Planetary Nuclear Massacre">
            Death on Mars: The Discovery of a Planetary Nuclear Massacre
           </a>
            and will be published in February 2015. He reviews portions of his findings at the
           <a href="http://www.monmsci.net/~psaps/" target="_blank" title="2014 Annual Fall Meeting of the American Physical Society">
            2014 Annual Fall Meeting of the American Physical Society
           </a>
           and in a paper to be published in the
           <a href="http://iopscience.iop.org/1475-7516" target="_blank" title="Journal of Cosmology and Astroparticle Physics">
            Journal of Cosmology and Astroparticle Physics
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Dr. Brandenburg has previously theorized that the red color of Mars and the radioactive substances in its soil are the result of a
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/2012/02/exraterrestrial-atomic-war-evidence-of-a-nuclear-blast-on-mars/" target="_blank" title="Exraterrestrial Atomic War: Evidence of a Nuclear Blast on Mars?">
            thermonuclear explosion
           </a>
           from natural causes. He now says that the high concentration of Xenon-129 in the Martian atmosphere and uranium and thorium on the surface are remnants of two unnatural nuclear explosions, most likely triggered by alien invaders.
          </p>
          <p>
           Who were these aliens invading and eventually wiping out? Brandenburg believes Mars once had a climate like Earth and was inhabited by two civilizations  one in a region called Cydonia Mensa and another at Galaxias Chaos. Why these two regions?
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            Analysis of new images from Odyssey, MRO and Mars Express orbiters now show strong evidence of eroded archeological objects at these sites.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/map.jpg">
            <img alt="map" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-39182" height="355" src="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/map-570x355.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The images of what appear to be structures in those areas are indeed mounting, although some, like the so-called face on Mars in Cydonia have been attributed to shifting sand.
          </p>
          <p>
           What does all this mean to Brandenburg?
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            Taken together, the data requires that the hypothesis of Mars as the site of an ancient planetary nuclear massacre, must now be considered.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Brandenburg further speculates that the
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fermi_paradox" target="_blank" title="Fermi paradox">
            Fermi Paradox
           </a>
           is due to a powerful alien race wiping out other alien civilizations before they can contact any others. He presents this as a reason to visit Mars and study what happened there so we can be prepared for an attack.
          </p>
          <p>
           A trip to Mars would answer a LOT of questions, Dr.Brandenburg. His dots are interesting but pretty far apart. Do you think hes managed to connect them?
          </p>

                </p>
