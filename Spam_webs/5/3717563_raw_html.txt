                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Chicken soup for convalescence
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-582658692097277645" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        From
ancient time people of many nations believed that chicken soup will help a
person recovering from sickness. Even today many take chicken soup to combat
common cold and influenza. We hear often patients say that they had never been
well since their last illness such as bad flu, accident, operation, tummy bug
and many other kinds of infection. This is because we do not recuperate our health
after sickness. We take the pill and then we go off to work. Getting better
takes time and appropriate food that will help you to really get better.
Chicken soup is one of such foods. The ingredients such as garlic, pepper,
ginger, cumin seeds etc all help to make the chicken soup a real convalescent
food. Protein from the chicken and the antioxidants from the vegetables help
the body in making necessary antibodies to fight the infection. It is easily
digestible and is absorbed in the system quickly.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       </div>
                       <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                        <tbody>
                         <tr>
                          <td style="text-align: center;">
                           <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jE1zKnn0SLc/VAwZWFlP3NI/AAAAAAAACF8/4ErXAIHFEEs/s1600/IMG_3725.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                            <img alt="Chicken soup" border="0" height="475" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jE1zKnn0SLc/VAwZWFlP3NI/AAAAAAAACF8/4ErXAIHFEEs/s1600/IMG_3725.JPG" title="Chicken soup for convalescence " width="640"/>
                           </a>
                          </td>
                         </tr>
                         <tr>
                          <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                           Chicken soup
                          </td>
                         </tr>
                        </tbody>
                       </table>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Today I am
going to make a simple chicken soup using the legs of chicken for convalescence.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Serves: 4
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Preparation
time: 30 minutes
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Cooking
Time: 40 minutes
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Ingredients
                        <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                         <tbody>
                          <tr>
                           <td style="text-align: center;">
                            <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-tHqihZcrxw0/VAwZtp9N4WI/AAAAAAAACGE/vBkGlfoSIUs/s1600/Thei00182.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                             <img alt="Vegetables for the soup" border="0" height="225" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-tHqihZcrxw0/VAwZtp9N4WI/AAAAAAAACGE/vBkGlfoSIUs/s1600/Thei00182.jpg" title="Chicken soup for convalescence " width="400"/>
                            </a>
                           </td>
                          </tr>
                          <tr>
                           <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                            Vegetables for the soup
                           </td>
                          </tr>
                         </tbody>
                        </table>
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        1 young chicken
(1 month old) (150 grams)
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        1 Onion chopped
coarsely
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        100 grams French
beans sliced thin
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        100 grams
carrots sliced into  inch cubes
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                       <br/>
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        1 rib of celery
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        1 teaspoon
cumin seed
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                         teaspoon
salt
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                         spoon
pepper powder
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        3 cloves
garlic sliced thin
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        10 grams
ginger shredded
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        1 cup
vegetable broth
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        3 cups
water
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                        <tbody>
                         <tr>
                          <td style="text-align: center;">
                           <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XApg1Ju6tpw/VCcSdM9Y9xI/AAAAAAAACko/JHkZtcoBo-Y/s1600/Thei00153.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                            <img alt="Chicken" border="0" height="208" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XApg1Ju6tpw/VCcSdM9Y9xI/AAAAAAAACko/JHkZtcoBo-Y/s1600/Thei00153.jpg" title="Chicken soup for convalescence " width="320"/>
                           </a>
                          </td>
                         </tr>
                         <tr>
                          <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                           Chicken
                          </td>
                         </tr>
                        </tbody>
                       </table>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <h4 style="text-align: left;">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Preparation
                       </span>
                      </h4>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Cut chicken
into large chunks. Do not remove the bones.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <h4 style="text-align: left;">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Method
                       </span>
                      </h4>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        In a large
saucepan add onion, carrot, beans, cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, water,
vegetable broth and chicken pieces and salt. Bring the items to the boil.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Lower the
heat to low and cover the pan.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Simmer for
30 minutes.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Take off
the heat.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Let the
soup cool for 10 minutes.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Take the
chicken pieces and pull out the flesh from the bones.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Discard the
bones and add the meat back into the stock pot. If you think there may be tiny pieces of bones left in the soup you can always strain the soup and boil again with only the flesh.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Bring to
the boil. Taste for seasoning and adjust.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Take the
soup off the heat and ladle into a soup bowl and serve while hot. If the patient cannot take solid, strain the soup and serve. Today I used only the leg which can be taken without pulling and boiling again.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Note:
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        The steam
from the hot soup will relieve congestion in the nose and cold and flu. If the
person is already sick make the soup taste a little stronger by adding more
garlic, ginger and pepper. You can also use leeks to give a better flavor. You can also serve rice or bread along with the
soup.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        We are not
frying the onions as usual because we want to avoid oil during the convalescent
period.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span lang="EN-US">
                        Oil may
upset the stomach of the patient. We also chose a month old chick because it
will not have any fat.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <span lang="EN-US" style='font-family: "Calibri","sans-serif"; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-bidi-font-family: Latha; mso-bidi-language: TA; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-bidi; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-language: EN-US; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;'>
                       You can also add cooked rice to the soup and cook
it till the rice becomes porridge. The rice will give additional strength to
the patient and over cooking the rice will enable it to be digested easily.
                      </span>
