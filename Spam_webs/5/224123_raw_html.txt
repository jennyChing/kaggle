addthis_bubble_style"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;p&gt;Ballhaus Riviera in Berlin Grnau is one of prettiest ruins I&#8217;ve been to so far. Most of the abandoned buildings in Berlin used to be some kind of factory or boring office building, where there&#8217;s not much more to see but plain, rotten walls and graffiti. Ballhaus Riviera on the other side used to be a beautiful ballroom building where people went to have a good time dancing to live bands. Some of this place&#8217;s glory can still be seen today, even though it has not seen a dance since it was closed 1991, after the German reunification.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;span id="more-250"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;div class="GARD gard_async_mode" id="google-ads-0T5r" &gt;&lt;script async="async" src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"&gt;

  <p>
   Youngsters from the neighborhood sure didnt refrain from vandalizing the ballroom building and the adjacent guesthouse (Gstehaus), but they didnt manage to demolish the beautiful stuccoed ceiling nine meters above their heads in the ballroom. Only time made the paint flake off from it, which only adds to its beauty in my opinion.

  <div style="color: #000000;">
   Some random facts and links regarding Ballhaus Riviera:
  </div>
  <div style="color: #000000;">
   <ul>
    <li>
     Built 1890
    </li>
    <li>
     Denkmal (protected monument) since 1977
    </li>
    <li>
     Closed 1991, after the German reunification
    </li>
    <li>
     Since then its falling apart. Rumors are that the current owners let it decay on purpose so they can tear it down eventually.
    </li>
    <li>
     From August through October 2000, there was a Biergarten here, but didnt last long.
    </li>
    <li>
     More photos at
     <a href="http://digitalcosmonaut.com/2013/111-places-in-berlin-ballhaus-riviera/" target="_blank">
      digitalcosmonaut.com
     </a>
     .
    </li>
    <li>
     More photos at
     <a href="http://www.abandonedberlin.com/2013/10/ballhaus-grunau-abandoned-ballroom.html" target="_blank">
      abandonedberlin.com
     </a>
     .
    </li>
    <li>
     <a href="http://www.berliner-zeitung.de/berlin/gesellschaftshaus-und-riviera-in-koepenick-verfallen-die-ausflugslokale,10809148,25895850.html" target="_blank">
      Article
     </a>
     (german) in Berliner Zeitung.
    </li>
    <li>
     A
     <a href="http://www.riviera-retten.de/" target="_blank">
      local group
     </a>
     trying to rescue the property from decay.
    </li>
   </ul>
  </div>
