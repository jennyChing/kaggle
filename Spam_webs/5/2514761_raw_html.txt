        Hey Nick Young ...
       </h4>
       <h3 class="hf3-case">
        Dolphin Show Was Epic Mistake
       </h3>
      </div>
      <h5 class="article-posted-date">
       2/16/2015 9:26 AM PST BY TMZ STAFF
      </h5>
      <div class="all-post-body group article-content" itemprop="articleBody">
       <div class="primary-image-swipe">
        <span>
         EXCLUSIVE
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a class="lightbox-link" href="http://www.tmz.com/videos/0_qy81i5xz">
         <img alt="021615_eric_balfour_launch" src="http://ll-media.tmz.com/2015/02/16/021615-eric-balfour-launch-3.jpg"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         Eric Balfour
        </strong>
        is a HUUUGE Lakers fan ... he's also a huge animal rights activist ... which is why the former "24" star was so devastated when he found out about a scary incident involving
        <a href="http://www.tmz.com/person/nick-young/" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Nick Young
         </strong>
        </a>
        and a dolphin.
       </p>
       <p>
        The L.A. Lakers star has been joking around about the incident all week ... claiming a
        <a href="http://espn.go.com/los-angeles/nba/story/_/id/12308743/nick-young-los-angeles-lakers-recounts-death-dolphin-tale" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          dolphin tried to kill him
         </strong>
        </a>
        during one of those "ride the dolphins" experiences in Cabo San Lucas.
       </p>
       <p>
        Balfour says he's glad Young survived -- but he's bummed out Young and
        <a href="http://www.tmz.com/person/iggy-azalea/" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Iggy Azalea
         </strong>
        </a>
        decided to participate in the attraction in the first place.
       </p>
       <p>
        "When people unknowingly go to those shows, what they're actually contributing to is a dolphin slaughter in Taiji, Japan where hundreds of dolphins are killed, captured, separated from their families, and we're trying to stop that because its pretty f**king uncool."
       </p>
       <p>
        Balfour says he's giving Young a pass ... this time ... but says he should know better in the future.
       </p>
