                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Scandinavian Style - House Tour
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3983171164865071614" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Scandinavian-style interior design is a great way to style your house using simplistic beauty. Lest take a tour trough this house.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QRYDEOowYmk/VHKedFTiebI/AAAAAAAAHMA/ecC3k-uCiwk/s1600/FotorCreated.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="240" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QRYDEOowYmk/VHKedFTiebI/AAAAAAAAHMA/ecC3k-uCiwk/s1600/FotorCreated.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      If youre a person who doesnt collect a lot of clutter and
wants a clean, chic look for your place, take a look at this style. The home
pictured here uses Scandinavian style to create a natural, fresh feeling.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Throughout the whole place, the walls are colored white.
This opens up the room and creates consistency throughout. Bright or dark
colors can often close off a room. They can work when going for a snug
atmosphere, but with this were going for a much freer environment.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      To add to that, be sure to use plenty of natural light.
Natural light is the best lighting when it comes to simplicity. It makes the
room easy to look at. Scandinavian style is great for homes with large windows
throughout, to be sure plenty of natural light gets in.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-RGUXp4kSnDs/VHKejzrG5uI/AAAAAAAAHMM/h8jf_idX-qE/s1600/1SFDC3D238DF55C149018363066A867F724D.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="239" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-RGUXp4kSnDs/VHKejzrG5uI/AAAAAAAAHMM/h8jf_idX-qE/s1600/1SFDC3D238DF55C149018363066A867F724D.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/--kacSV5DUB0/VHKejQMqjGI/AAAAAAAAHMI/e3U72PzANZM/s1600/2SFD385C774022FB4B35A6C2D0E8B0592BC3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="239" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/--kacSV5DUB0/VHKejQMqjGI/AAAAAAAAHMI/e3U72PzANZM/s1600/2SFD385C774022FB4B35A6C2D0E8B0592BC3.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-N_e93lV4i88/VHKekSzdOoI/AAAAAAAAHMQ/mJygONg4AJY/s1600/3SFD7A911EDEC03D48D89AE6341DC0024B3E.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="239" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-N_e93lV4i88/VHKekSzdOoI/AAAAAAAAHMQ/mJygONg4AJY/s1600/3SFD7A911EDEC03D48D89AE6341DC0024B3E.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_N08n1rPYyk/VHKekwil8LI/AAAAAAAAHMY/gaknmKS2dzE/s1600/4SFDCE3D69FE842842EC8E9081BFC66D4888.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="239" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_N08n1rPYyk/VHKekwil8LI/AAAAAAAAHMY/gaknmKS2dzE/s1600/4SFDCE3D69FE842842EC8E9081BFC66D4888.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      You can see the bedroom is free from clutter. With
light-colored wood floors to compliment the white walls, a simple tan rug
frames the bed. The focus on the room is on the bed and the light above it.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      The black bedding contrasts the white walls, but isn't as
heavy as a bright color would be. The light above the bed hangs from the
ceiling and has a modern look. With plenty of natural light, the ceiling lights
are more about style than functionality. If youre designing your home this
way, have some fun with this.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Instead of closets or dressers, metal clothing racks fit the
scene better. Theyre generally easier to sort through and add to the look of
simplicity. A bulky dresser can really weigh down the feeling of the room.
Other dcor, such as a bedside table could match this as well. You can see next
to the clothes rack theres a metal table with a plant. Its simple with a
style almost like a warehouse  functional and to the point.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-jM8G3HhhapE/VHKemenk7oI/AAAAAAAAHMk/TaFglndEJrs/s1600/SFD2BD6208823D1476880478D91538CF52A.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="239" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-jM8G3HhhapE/VHKemenk7oI/AAAAAAAAHMk/TaFglndEJrs/s1600/SFD2BD6208823D1476880478D91538CF52A.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-dIjYiXvertw/VHKeoMeDbiI/AAAAAAAAHMw/MZ-IGgOxmgU/s1600/SFD8A61A8565E0749939D153D2194A0162C.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="239" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-dIjYiXvertw/VHKeoMeDbiI/AAAAAAAAHMw/MZ-IGgOxmgU/s1600/SFD8A61A8565E0749939D153D2194A0162C.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      If you take a walk out to the living room, you can see a lot
of similarities in style. The plain white walls and large windows give the
illusion of there being a lot of space. Again, we have certain pieces that are
given focus by using black color  the rug, the couch, and some of the pillows.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Again, we have warehouse style furniture, like the wheeled
dolly as a centerpiece and coffee table. Just like in the bedroom, we have a
hanging light that pulls the room together. We also have plants that give the
room life and breathe freshness in.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Unlike in the bedroom, we have a few extra accents that make
it a little busier. A turquoise puff and poster, which, match stand out from
the rest of the room. If youre looking to make your Scandinavian-style home
more vibrant, think about adding an accent in a bright color. Be sure to keep
it simplistic, leaving the rest of the dcor fairly neutral. You can see with
this home, theyve used pieces, like antlers for a center piece, which maintains
the neutrality of the room.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
