       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         No Ka Oi Mai Tai: The Best Mai Tai in Maui
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-07-01T16:39:44+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 1, 2011
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.mommymusings.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Stefanie Fauquet
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div addthis:title="No Ka Oi Mai Tai: The Best Mai Tai in Maui " addthis:url="http://www.mommymusings.com/no-ka-oi-mai-tai/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">
         <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count" rel="nofollow">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_tweet" rel="nofollow">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_pinterest_pinit" rel="nofollow">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style" rel="nofollow">
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="Maui Mai Tai" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7650" height="527" src="http://www.mommymusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/maitai.jpg" title="maitai" width="350"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           No Ka Oi
          </em>
          is a Hawaiian phrase that can been seen and heard all over Maui.
         </strong>
         It simply means The Best.
        </p>
        <p>
         It was only fitting that my first night in Hawaii would include a mixology class at
         <a href="http://www.westinmaui.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          The Westin Maui
         </a>
         to make one of their signature cocktails:
         <strong>
          No Ka Oi Mai Tai
         </strong>
         . The Mai Tai is a staple in Hawaii; its kind of like a margarita in Mexico you cant travel there without ordering at least one
         <em>
          (or in my case, half a dozen!).
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         After two flights totaling 10 hours and a 45 minute drive to Kaanapali, I was in need of a drink.
        </p>
        <p>
         And this Mai Tai really was
         <em>
          The Best
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          So what makes this Mai Tai so special?
         </em>
         Its all in the rum.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Westin Maui has an exclusive partnership with
         <a href="http://www.haleakaladistillers.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Maui Rum
         </a>
         , who produces a special pineapple flavored rum just for The Westin Maui. It cant be purchased anywhere else and is not sold outside of ordering it in a drink.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="maui pineapple rum" height="461" src="../wp-content/uploads/2011/07/mauipineapplerum.jpg" title="mauipineapplerum" width="350"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Even though you can only order this drink at The Westin Maui, I know you wanna know how its made!
        </p>
        <h2>
         Na Ka Oi Mai Tai Recipe
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 oz Maui Pineapple Rum
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 oz Grand Marnier
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 oz Cruzan Coconut Rum
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Muddle liquor with 2 1/2 oz freshly made sugar-lime syrup with 2 fresh limes wedges + 2 pineapple wedges + 2 orange wedges.
         </li>
         <li>
          Strain and pour over crushed ice in a snifter glass. Top with a float of Maui Dark Rum &amp; garnish with pineapple leaves.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         If youll notice, the shape of the glass with the pineapple leaf garnish makes it look like a pineapple 
         <em>
          so clever!
         </em>
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         For those who want to make this drink at home, you can substitute any pineapple rum for Maui Pineapple rum. The bartender suggested Cruzan, which is an inexpensive alternative. The sugar-lime syrup is just a simple syrup that you can make on your stove top with sugar, water and lime. {
         <a href="http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/simple-syrup/detail.aspx" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Heres a recipe
         </a>
         }.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Trust me on this one, this is the best Mai Tai in or out of Maui!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="the best mai tai maui" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7712" height="407" src="http://www.mommymusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/mauimaitai.jpg" title="mauimaitai" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Cheers!
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.mommymusings.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Special thanks to the Maui Visitors Bureau &amp; The Westin Maui for hosting my visit and stay in Maui.
         </em>
        </p>
