       <h2>
        The Magic of Spring and Butterflies
       </h2>
       <div class="post-content">
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          Tinker Bells Butterfly House at the Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival
         </em>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span id="more-10972">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg1.jpg">
          <img alt="Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival - Tinker Bell's Butterfly House" class=" wp-image-11034 aligncenter" height="315" src="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg1.jpg" width="261"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Welcome to those of you joining me from
         <a href="http://disneyworldonabudget.blogspot.com/2014/03/epcot-flower-garden-festival-2014.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://disneyworldonabudget.blogspot.com/2014/03/epcot-flower-garden-festival-2014.html', 'Disney with Babies, Toddlers, and Preschoolers']);">
          Disney with Babies, Toddlers, and Preschoolers
         </a>
         and those of you just hopping aboard. I am the 2nd stop on our
         <a href="http://magicalblogorail.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://magicalblogorail.com/', 'Magical Blogorail']);">
          Magical Blogorail
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         The
         <strong>
          <a href="https://disneyworld.disney.go.com/events-tours/epcot/epcot-international-flower-and-garden-festival/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://disneyworld.disney.go.com/events-tours/epcot/epcot-international-flower-and-garden-festival/', 'Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival']);">
           Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival
          </a>
         </strong>
         just exudes spring. The flowers, the topiaries, the outdoor cooking, the kids play areas, etc. They all make you want to enjoy the outdoors and stop to smell the roses.
        </p>
        <p>
         But my favorite attraction is the butterfly house. Once known as
         <strong>
          Minnies Butterfly House
         </strong>
         it has now been taken over by a little fairy known for wearing green and being friends with some fellow named Peter. Thats right  Tink.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <hr class=" sb-hr" size="3" style="background:black"/>
        <br/>
        <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
        </script>
        <!-- Disney Travel Tips - Leaderboard -->
        <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3254012191950547" data-ad-slot="8470233318" style="display: inline-block; width: 728px; height: 90px;">
        </ins>
        <script>
         // &lt;![CDATA[
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
// ]]&gt;
        </script>
        <br/>
        <hr class=" sb-hr" size="3" style="background:black"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Tinker Bells Butterfly House,
         </strong>
         Presented by HGTV, is a walk thought attraction that showcases hundreds of butterflies. These butterflies are very busy and have even been known to land on guests. This year it is located near the Imagination Pavilion on the walkway towards the World Showcase.
        </p>
        <p>
         Take the time to watch these beautiful creatures and if you are a photographer, get your camera out. You will walk away with some gorgeous shots! Dont worry about getting too close. These little guys are used to the fuss and sometimes I feel like they are actually posing for the shot.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <a href="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg2.jpg">
          <img alt="Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival - Tinker Bell's Butterfly House" class="wp-image-11035 alignnone" height="362" src="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg2.jpg" width="242"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg3.jpg">
          <img alt="Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival - Tinker Bell's Butterfly House" class="wp-image-11036 alignnone" height="264" src="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg3.jpg" width="396"/>
         </a>
        </center>
        <center>
        </center>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg6.jpg">
          <img alt="Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival - Tinker Bell's Butterfly House" class="aligncenter wp-image-11044" height="306" src="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg6.jpg" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         They even have cocoons on display at different stages of development. Some are always ready to hatch so if you are lucky you may see a butterfly being born. We were not that lucky this day but you can tell some had been.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are usually one to two Cast Members in
         <strong>
          Tinker Bells Butterfly House
         </strong>
         at any time. These Cast Members are also knowledgeable about what types of butterflies are flying around you. So stop and ask them a question, or two, or three. They would love to answer them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg4.jpg">
          <img alt="Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival - Tinker Bell's Butterfly House" class="aligncenter wp-image-11037" height="384" src="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg4.jpg" width="512"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <hr class=" sb-hr" size="3" style="background:black"/>
        <br/>
        <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
        </script>
        <!-- Disney Travel Tips - Leaderboard -->
        <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3254012191950547" data-ad-slot="8470233318" style="display: inline-block; width: 728px; height: 90px;">
        </ins>
        <script>
         // &lt;![CDATA[
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
// ]]&gt;
        </script>
        <br/>
        <hr class=" sb-hr" size="3" style="background:black"/>
        <p>
         Dont be concerned if you let one of the butterflies out. As they mature Disney hopes they enter the park and begin helping in flower pollination. It is a win-win. Disney gets pollinators and the butterflies get to live at Walt Disney World.
        </p>
        <p>
         And since this is Tinker Bells domain, be sure to keep an eye out for some fairy houses intermingled between the butterflies. Its fun to see how the fairies live and what they make their houses from.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg7.jpg">
          <img alt="Epcot International Flower and Garden Festival - Tinker Bell's Butterfly House" class="aligncenter wp-image-11045" height="383" src="http://www.capturingmagicalmemories.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/fg7.jpg" width="379"/>
         </a>
         In addition to butterflies, this year they have added a
         <b>
          Hummingbirds at Home,
         </b>
         a new exhibit presented by the National Audubon Society located near Test Track. Like Tinker Bells Butterfly House the hummingbird garden is walk through and included tips on how to attract these magical creatures that seem to defy gravity as they migrate from Central America each year.
        </p>
        <p>
         For more information click here for the
         <a href="https://wdpromedia.disney.go.com/media/wdpro-assets/parks-and-tickets/tours-and-experiences/epcot-international-flower-and-garden-festival/F&amp;G_Guide_2014_Final-R1.pdf?24022014130745" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://wdpromedia.disney.go.com/media/wdpro-assets/parks-and-tickets/tours-and-experiences/epcot-international-flower-and-garden-festival/F&amp;G_Guide_2014_Final-R1.pdf?24022014130745', '2014 Epcot Flower and Garden Festival Guide']);">
          2014 Epcot Flower and Garden Festival Guide
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Thank you for joining me today. Your next stop on the Magical Blogorail Loop is
         <a href="http://iamadisneynerd.com/epcot-flower-festival-hgtv-presenters/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://iamadisneynerd.com/epcot-flower-festival-hgtv-presenters/', 'I Am a Mommy Nerd']);">
          I Am a Mommy Nerd
         </a>
         .
        </p>

        </p>
