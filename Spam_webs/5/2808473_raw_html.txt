       <h1 class="page-title">
        Tooth Whitening
       </h1>
      </div>
      <!-- /content-heading -->
      <p>
       A brighter white smile is one of the most common dental desires. With easy, comfortable tooth whitening treatments, you dont have to put up with a smile you arent happy with.
      </p>
      <p>
       Persistent stains from drinking coffee, tea and red wine develop over years and often leave your smile looking dull and discolored. Tobacco use can also make your teeth turn dark and yellow.
      </p>
      <p>
       Tooth whitening treatments can brighten and freshen your smile and remove years of stains, providing you with a younger, rejuvenated smile. At Dental Boutique, we offer both in chair and take home teeth whitening procedures. Both options are safe and harmless.
      </p>
      <h2>
       ZOOM Tooth Whitening
      </h2>
      <p>
       At Dental Boutique we offer ZOOM Tooth Whitening treatments, these treatments are popular due to being safe and effective.
      </p>
      <p>
       When you visit for an in practice ZOOM Tooth Whitening treatment the dentist will apply a gel liquid to your teeth that will then be activated by the ZOOM light. With this treatment, you can whiten you teeth by up to 8 shades in a single appointment.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Your tooth whitening questions answered
      </h3>
      <p>
       Many patients have questions regarding tooth whitening, for your convenience we have included some FAQs below:
      </p>
      <div class="list-accordion js-collapse has-mb">
       <p class="list-accordion-title">
        Can I use some kind of store-bought tooth whitening product?
       </p>
       <div class="list-accordion-content">
        <p>
         You could, but dont expect dramatic or consistent results. In fact, dont expect any results at all from whitening toothpaste. The store-bought kits have all-purpose trays which oftenwill not fit your unique teeth. That means the whitening agent will be in contact with your teeth in an off-and-on fashion, giving you patchy results. Also, the whitening gel contain a weaker active agent than what Cosmetic Dentist Dr Chan and Dr Sim can provide for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         The whitening toothpastes actually make your teeth more yellow in the long run, because they have abrasive ingredients like baking soda which gradually scrape off your white enamel, revealing more of the yellow dentin layer underneath.
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- /list-accordion-content -->
       <p class="list-accordion-title">
        Does tooth whitening hurt?
       </p>
       <div class="list-accordion-content">
        <p>
         Not typically, although some people have extra sensitive teeth and might feel some mild discomfort. At Dental Boutique, Dr Sim and Dr Chan will check your teeth to ensure theyare healthy enough to have a whitening procedure started, and the team at Dental Boutique will prescribe necessary pre-treatment procedures to reduce sensitivity.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether you are having a chair side tooth whitening; or take home kit, a desensitizing gel can be applied to neutralize the effect of the whitening gel.
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- /list-accordion-content -->
       <p class="list-accordion-title">
        How white could my teeth become after tooth whitening? Is it worth doing?
       </p>
       <div class="list-accordion-content">
        <p>
         Teethtypically become six or eight shades whiter, whether you have a chair side whitening procedure, or a take-home kit. The team at Dental Boutique will takephotos before and afteryour whitening procedure. The difference will be quite obvious.
        </p>
        <p>
         Usually, the only teeth that dont respond dramatically are those with very deep staining, too deep for the whitening agent to reach. Those deep stains were formed as the teeth developed in childhood, often as a side effect of a medication such as tetracycline. If you have this type of staining, you might consider dental bonding or porcelain veneers.
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- /list-accordion-content -->
       <p class="list-accordion-title">
        How long will my teeth stay white after the whitening treatment?
       </p>
       <div class="list-accordion-content">
        <p>
         That will depend on:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Your daily dental hygiene; and
         </li>
         <li>
          Your daily diet
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         If you drink a lot of coffee or red wine, for instance, these pigmented drinks will stain your teeth more quickly than if you drink them only occasionally. The same goes for any strongly pigmented food, such as blackberries or blueberries. However, if you brush your teeth well after each meal or snack, and floss daily, your chances of keeping your teeth white are much higher.
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- /list-accordion-content -->
      </div>
