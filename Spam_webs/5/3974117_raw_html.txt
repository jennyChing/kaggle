           <div itemprop="articleBody">
            <p>
             <br/>
             We never realized such beautiful watch existed, until a rich bloke comes by and drops the quid for it. This statement was in reference to the Vintage Rolex Daytona in 18kt was sold at the worlds leading watch auctioneer, Antiquorums Spring Geneva Auction a couple of days back. Made in 1985 and extremely rare, the wristwatch dazzles in 18kt yellow gold and features a round button chronograph.
            </p>

             The bezel is set with 28 baguette diamonds and the dial is swathed with 240 diamonds and nine sapphires. The stunning wristwatch fetched 3,60, 000 Swiss Franks. (Approximately US$315,000 including buyers premium)
