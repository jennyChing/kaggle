     <div class="description" itemprop="description">
      <h2>
       Dive El Gouna with Emperor Divers
      </h2>
      <p>
       Emperor Divers is conveniently located on the Mvenpick's private beach and is a PADI Five Star Instructor Development Centre/BSAC Travel Partner. With our dedicated daily RIB you can quickly reach great dive sites in comfort without missing too much 'resort' time or take a little longer with our daily dive boats. We offer free try dives in our warm pool so you can 'try before you buy' - ideal for this family-friendly resort.
      </p>
      <p>
       Free transfers are provided from all hotels in the resort.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Dive El Gouna
      </h2>
      <p>
       One of our main attractions is the different types of diving on offer nearby; from deep walls to shallow reefs and a choice of wrecks at Abu Nuhas. Special trips can be booked in resort to the Rosalie Moeller and the Thistlegorm. This is also a great location for both learners and experienced divers with day diving, day trips, learning to dive or taking your diving further with our welcoming and highly knowledgeable team of dive instructors and guides.
      </p>
