         <h3 class="entry-header">
          You Can Help: 10 Easy Ways to Help Shelter Dogs
         </h3>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="entry-body">
           <h2 class="date-header">
            10/05/2010
           </h2>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              <a href="http://blogs.discovery.com/.a/6a00d8341bf67c53ef0133f4d92c18970b-pi" style="float: right;">
               <img alt="Buggie Beagle Mix available for adoption at Petfinder" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341bf67c53ef0133f4d92c18970b " src="http://blogs.discovery.com/.a/6a00d8341bf67c53ef0133f4d92c18970b-400wi" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 5px; width: 325px;" title="Buggie Beagle Mix available for adoption at Petfinder"/>
              </a>
             </span>
             October is Adopt-A-Shelter-Dog Month: How You Can Help
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            There are more dogs in need than ever these days and our friends at
            <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/before-pet-adoption/october-is-adopt-a-shelter-dog-month.html" target="_blank" title="Petfinder.com">
             Petfinder.com
            </a>
            have put together a great list of ten simple ways we can all help the tens of thousands of helpless, orphaned and abandoned pets that are seeking loving forever homes. We understand that not everyone can necessarily adopt a pet, but there are many ways you can help. Read on to learn how and please spread the word!
           </p>
           <p>
            ps.
            <em>
             If you're interested in learning more about cute as a bug
            </em>
            <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/petdetail/17569231?rvp=1">
             <em>
              Buggie the Beagle mix
             </em>
            </a>
            <em>
             , at right, who is available for adoption, check out her profile page at
            </em>
            <a href="http://">
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/petdetail/17569231?rvp=1">
             <em>
              Petfinder.com
             </em>
            </a>
            <em>
             )
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            1)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Donate your Facebook status
            </span>
            . Just paste this message into the "What's on your mind?" box at the top of your page: "October is Adopt-A-Shelter-Dog Month. Save a life: Adopt a dog! http://www.petfinder.com"
           </p>
           <p>
            2)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Tweet, retweet and repeat
            </span>
            the following (or your own brilliant message): "October is Adopt-A-Shelter-Dog Month. Save a life: Adopt a dog! http://www.petfinder.com"
           </p>
           <p>
            3)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Contact your local shelter or rescue group
            </span>
            (you can search for groups near you on
            <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/awo/index.cgi?action=location">
             Petfinder
            </a>
            ) and ask if they have a donation wish list or other flyer they'd like to you to post around your office or neighborhood. They may be holding special events for
            <a href="http://blogs.discovery.com/daily_treat/2010/10/things-to-consider-before-you-adopt-a-dog.html">
             Adopt-A-Shelter-Dog Month
            </a>
            which you can help promote.
           </p>
           <p>
            4)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Share an adoptable dog story
            </span>
            or a Petfinder dog-adoption
            <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/adoption-stories/">
             Happy Tail
            </a>
            on your blog, Facebook or Twitter page each day of the month.
           </p>
           <p>
            5)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Sign up as a foster parent or shelter volunteer
            </span>
            then tell your friends how great it is. Contact your local shelter or rescue group, or register in our volunteer database.
           </p>
           <p>
            6)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Add a
             <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/widgets-banners/">
              Petfinder widget
             </a>
            </span>
            or banner to your Web site or blog.
           </p>
           <p>
            7)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Share your stories:
            </span>
            Write an op-ed about the importance of pet adoption for your local paper.
           </p>
           <p>
            8)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Contact your local shelter or rescue group and offer to photograph their adoptable pets
            </span>
            and upload the pics to Petfinder.
           </p>
           <p>
            9)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Donate
            </span>
            to your local shelter or rescue group or to the
            <a href="http://www.petfinderfoundation.com/">
             Petfinder.com Foundation
            </a>
            in honor of Adopt-A-Shelter-Dog Month.
           </p>
           <p>
            10)
            <span style="color: #407f00;">
             Educate Others
            </span>
            : Pass on an understanding of the importance of pet adoption to the next generation. Talk to your kids, nieces, nephews, grandchildren and other up-and-comers about animal shelters and why Adopt-A-Shelter-Dog Month, and pet adoption in general, is important.
           </p>
           <p>
            See this list and more adoption awareness tips at
            <a href="http://www.petfinder.com/before-pet-adoption/october-is-adopt-a-shelter-dog-month.html" target="_blank" title="Adopt-a-Shelter-Dog Month at Petfinder.com">
             Petfinder.com
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             Did we leave anything out? Give us your own ideas!
            </em>
           </p>
