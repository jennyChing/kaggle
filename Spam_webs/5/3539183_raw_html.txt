       <div class="post clearfix">
        <h1 class="title">
         Topsail Beach, NC
        </h1>
        <p class="post-meta">
         Posted  by
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/author/paula/" rel="author" title="Posts by Paula">
          Paula
         </a>
         in
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/category/travel/" rel="category tag">
          Travel
         </a>
         |
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/2012/08/07/topsail-beach-nc/#comments">
          22 comments
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bellalimento.com%2F2012%2F08%2F07%2Ftopsail-beach-nc%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bellalimento.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2012%2F08%2Fphoto17.jpg&amp;description=Topsail%20Beach%2C%20NC%20" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span class="rdetector_placeholder_before">
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo17.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-12228" height="553" src="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo17-1024x1024.jpg" title="Topsail Beach" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The beach is my happy place. Ive always been in awe of the power and beauty of the ocean. I could sit on the beach for hours and hours just watching, listening and of course napping. Napping is inevitable. The ocean calms me. No matter how much chaos and craziness is in my life I feel calm beside the ocean. Its as if takes all of my stress and washes it away with the tide. I need more beach time in my life. Plain and simple.
         <span id="more-12227">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo20.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-12231" height="717" src="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo20-764x1024.jpg" title="Topsail" width="535"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This past weekend we tagged along with the hubs on one of his work trips to Jacksonville. We stayed in nearby Topsail beach so we could maximize our beach time. Quite frankly I dont know why we havent thought to tag along before now. Oh yes work. Im sure it was because we were TOO busy between work and activity commitments. Im striving to work smarter and not harder so we can enjoy more trips like this one.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo21.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-12232" height="553" src="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo21-1024x1024.jpg" title="Topsail Beach" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In case you were wondering, I captured all of these photos with my Iphone. What you didnt see is me almost getting knocked over knee deep in the ocean trying to get said shots. True story. Topsail beach is clearly beautiful. It goes on for miles (or so it seems) and is a shell collectors dream. The public access was fantastic. Free parking and even showers to rinse the sand off before returning to the car. Okay well we all know ALL the sand isnt coming off but it was nice to be able to rinse off. There were hardly any crowds. We pretty much had the beach to ourselves. August is the perfect time to hit the beaches.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo19.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-12230" height="717" src="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/photo19-764x1024.jpg" title="Topsail Beach" width="535"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The boys are definitely drawn to the water too. They loved jumping over the waves,
         <del>
          throwing sand at each other
         </del>
