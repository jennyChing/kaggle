               </span>
              </b>
             </a>
             <span style="">
              is Apple
trademarked technology which can offer services in indoor positioning system. It
is aptly considered as low power, low cost transmitters which work on Bluetooth
Low Energy or Bluetooth Smart technology. It is compatible with the devices
running on Bluetooth v4.0 supporting dual mode. Best part of this technology
which sets it apart from others is its context-aware, location-aware and
pervasive small wireless sensing abilities. Practical usage of this technology
is that it can pin point the location of users in a retail store. This helps
the retailers to send the details of nearby products, offers, deals and

customer. It can also enable payments at POS i.e. Point of Sale.

             <span style="">

Android operating system based devices. NFC will work similar to Bluetooth but
it can transfer the information just by tapping with other NFC enabled device.
iBeacon technology will be helpful for the retailers to communicate with their
customers about nearby deals and offers. It can be very much useful than NFC. iBeacon
helps the retailers to communicate with huge number of shoppers while NFC
enables communication between two devices.

some differences also. Both of them work for finding out nearby devices. GPS
uses this technology to find out the current location while iBeacon can be used
for locating a device or product in indoors. While GPS can be very much useful
to track the route in a new place, this latest technology will be very much
useful to find out a specific item in the new premises.

mode.
