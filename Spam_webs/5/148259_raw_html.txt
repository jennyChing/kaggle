       <p>
        <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?om=0&amp;iwloc=addr&amp;f=q&amp;ll=-16.5004126%2C-151.7414904&amp;hl=en&amp;z=4&amp;ie=UTF8" id="aptureLink_54dr01lvwF" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?om=0&amp;iwloc=addr&amp;f=q&amp;ll=-16.5004126%2C-151.7414904&amp;hl=en&amp;z=4&amp;ie=UTF8', 'Bora Bora']);">
         Bora Bora
        </a>
        had always been at the top of my must-visit list. Id seen countless pictures of this famous atoll in so many publications and it always looked stunning, both on the ground and from the air. So, when I boarded the flight from
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tahiti" id="aptureLink_i9FBRqhUu4" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tahiti', 'Tahiti']);">
         Tahiti
        </a>
        , I hoped and prayed that Id chosen the right side of the plane for optimal views of
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bora%20Bora" id="aptureLink_qbMezyiADx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bora%20Bora', 'Bora Bora']);">
         Bora Bora
        </a>
        before landing there. Alas, I was on the wrong side! After four unforgettable, totally mesmerising days there, it was time to fly back to Tahiti.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_14652" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="aerial-view-bora-bora-lagoon-photo" class="size-full wp-image-14652" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/IMG_1157.jpg" width="640"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2009/04/plane-views-bora-bora/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/IMG_1157.jpg&amp;description=Plane views: Bora Bora')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The Bora Bora lagoon right after take-off!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        This time, I paid great attention to the direction in which the plane landed because that most probably means that the plane will take-off in the same direction. Based on this observation, I chose a window seat on the right side of the plane (its free seating) and was thrilled when the plane did take off in the desired direction. The views of the brilliant multi-coloured lagoon, Mt. Otemanu, and the overwater villas of the various luxury resorts were totally breathtaking!
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_14653" style="width: 650px">

         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
