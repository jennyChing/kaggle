                  <p>
                   Have you ever woken up with a craving for a particular food? Im talking a bone deep, must have, nothing-will-stop-you-from-having-it food craving. Moments like that are pretty rare for me, but the day before my 31st birthday, I woke up with an intense craving for the Black Forest Trifle my Momma used to make!
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <a href="http://busybeingjennifer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Delicious-Black-Forest-Trifle-on-BusyBeingJennifer.com_.jpg">
                    <img alt="Delicious Black Forest Trifle on BusyBeingJennifer.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5122" height="800" src="http://busybeingjennifer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Delicious-Black-Forest-Trifle-on-BusyBeingJennifer.com_.jpg" width="1000"/>
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Of course I couldnt remember exactly how my Mom used to make this Trifle. I knew there was pie filling and cake and some sort of chocolate bar oh! And pudding but after that I was stuck! So I facetimed my sister and we discussed. Pretty sure I transferred my craving via facetime to Emily.  Between the 2 of us we were able to remember the other ingredients and work out the layering sequence.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <a href="http://busybeingjennifer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Perfect-Black-Forest-Trifle-BusyBeingJennifer.com_.jpg">
                    <img alt="Perfect Black Forest Trifle - BusyBeingJennifer.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5123" height="791" src="http://busybeingjennifer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Perfect-Black-Forest-Trifle-BusyBeingJennifer.com_.jpg" width="1000"/>
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Immediately after ending our call I headed to the grocery store for all the necessary Black Forest Trifle ingredients. Trifle tastes best when its allowed to sit and gel together after a few hours, or even better, over night, and I NEEDED to have this trifle on my birthday!
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <a href="http://busybeingjennifer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Yummy-Black-Forest-Trifle-BusyBeingJennifer.com_.jpg">
                    <img alt="Yummy Black Forest Trifle - BusyBeingJennifer.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5124" height="1010" src="http://busybeingjennifer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Yummy-Black-Forest-Trifle-BusyBeingJennifer.com_.jpg" width="1000"/>
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                  </p>
                  <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-3">
                   <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">

                     <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
                      <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                       Starting with a layer of cake, layer the trifle ingredients - Cake, Pie Filling, Whip Cream, Pudding, and a sprinkling of chocolate diced chocolate bar.

                   I know I have an awesome MIL. But honestly, the multitude of dishes is my least favorite part of making desserts like this. SO not only did I get the trifle I was craving, I also didnt have to clean the kitchen when I was done! Yep!! Im super spoiled  Dont worry, I paid her back with some Black Forest Trifle of her own
