      <td valign="top">
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Gomez &amp; Bikker lawyers have been involved with aircraft registration and financing transactions representing clients from all over the world in Aruba for the last 20 years. Our lawyers are well versed in the regulatory aspects of aircraft registration in Aruba as well as in representing owners, financiers, lessors and operators of Aruba-registered aircrafts.
       </p>
       <p>
        Gomez &amp; Bikker has played a major role in realizing the accession of Aruba to the Cape Town Convention and works closely with the Department of Civil Aviation of Aruba and the Registry of Aruba to optimize the registration, certification and the security interests of Aruba-registered aircrafts.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its long list of international publications can be found in all leading aviation finance law and aviation industry journals. Over the years Gomez &amp; Bikker has provided lectures and seminars in all major venues worldwide.
       </p>
