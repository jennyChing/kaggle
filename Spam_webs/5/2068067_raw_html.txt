      </h2>
      <p>
       Every environment and organization is different. And within each environment there may be a wide range of access points to control with a variety of operational needs to consider. With CLIQ, youll be able to build an access control solution that effectively addresses your specific needs and is tailored to your location and operations. View which combination of CLIQ products makes up your ideal access control solution.
      </p>
      <div>
       <div>
        <div>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_1">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci5.googleusercontent.com/proxy/vHsEOnOgCFt6uctBHIGXcR5IouofAV5X2ned1ApPOqAG2FgHtAfd3A3dPnsTmrDFh5Nq3TfQ5FbSDYzBqyOPzF2D2uYkQ70QIp6x_5nnALRj-CJL36hsltp5LgdTg1u-HDmc7vEPB5JrO-JbyvUXA6Flf8HPUn--PFLR9hyxYdtTYLEeUraekg=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_cylinders%26padlocks.jpg?itok=dh7ZY9ra" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Cylinders &amp; Padlocks -
          </b>
          based on Mul-T-Locks renowned High Security mechanical platforms, the electromechanical CLIQ Cylinders and Padlocks maintain authorization lists to ensure only enabled keys have access. Additionally, it collects and stores information for audit trail events. Available in a wide range of applications including Euro (double or single), Rim, Mortise, K.I.K, padlocks, switch cylinders and more, the CLIQ Cylinder can be deployed almost anywhere.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_2">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci3.googleusercontent.com/proxy/v4f5dWwpApwH-aWOB0z8qy17YvWGY98hXV_Zkftt5Uog5XT82kaOE5T7fONaplFlxHb-ShjyF6wyIxAgWos-dHXnHw5ag5HR6vnwudnSZFWm5_RvjHHldIp0Wrgli9jWHlJpJ_m656fYFM2xVLvLHXbpLqLLGoUH=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/WatchLock.jpg?itok=0njiUb4x" width="125"/>
          <b>
           WatchLock
          </b>
          
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          
          <b>
          </b>
          <b>
           -
          </b>
          combines multiple technologies  GPS, GSM, and patented mechanical and electromechanical platforms  to create an intelligent locking solution. It provides flexible access control over your assets and information about when and where your assets have been accessed. With audit trails available from both WatchLock and CLIQ, youll have accurate records about all assets under WatchLock CLIQ protection.Ideal for remote, hard to reach or mobile assets, this comprehensive solution gives you the control you want and the information you need to enhance the protection level of your assets and premises.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_3">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci4.googleusercontent.com/proxy/8GvAZXOzCgsZfKt0kpfCJvFXyi8Ade2MsFlOhOONCzeRAXHT_1OYXUo6N2XBLiymBsYl-GiCuxWctIZrKr6mOmATxM3zngH7T6ybCnL6ghbAnGmY2S65LuIgmwO7j3e3FW6QB5WVSzPkH561khqc28KUofaNPttGMVzQSmc=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_user_keys.jpg?itok=PkMSRGUp" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           User Keys -
          </b>
          are electromechanical keys used with CLIQ cylinders. All keys feature LED indicators that signal for authorizedand unauthorized access, out-of-schedule use and low battery notice. Select the CLIQ keys or a combination of them based on your needs.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           CLIQ Quartz Key  can be programmed with specific time schedules, they also capture audit trail data of up to 130 events.
          </li>
          <li>
           CLIQ QuartzXT Key  is used with CLIQ Remote and can store up to 1000 events for audit trails. The key can also be programmed with specific time schedules.
          </li>
          <li>
           CLIQ Quartz+ Key  is used with CLIQ Remote and can store up to 1000 events for audit trail and be programmed with specific time schedules. In addition, the Quartz+ has an additional feature that allows system administrators to predefine a list of authorized cylinders that limits the Quartz+ key to open only those designated cylinders.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_4">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci3.googleusercontent.com/proxy/VmlN3xbv6TtaFPW2NID16nya6OIdAvQr3Mem6sxt4Ew0R5d2HTMmLh-2yfD2zQ_J6-Zoz8bP_c6KNw8M1MTeWIxmbk3nnGOVhdGyg7BVx2YHtVyCTyt4ZJrq8F7i_pmQ6Yaarz318dtPbLOONdQ0-OurO84SA3oMW4n6=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_manager.jpg?itok=OowC0BOk" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Manager Software -
          </b>
          installed on your local network, CLIQ Manager Software is used for the daily management of your CLIQ system. Designed for secure access, login to the software is fortified and only possible with the C-Key and authorized PIN code. With CLIQ Manager Software, system administrators can manage and create access permissions; audit trails; time-based authorizations; reports and much more.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_5">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci4.googleusercontent.com/proxy/9nje0FaLg10pxRmK-k_36bzEWALbhYHm__eWv3XdBDMguGP2ujFSVkOramfc5vsPr0N29-JFbOts9D0Jl2dT0lvV7QrrNsyu-KX2XSXI_uzMST7YGVNNEuEpkZM1PWWp_WJPkGADpD0Sen1vK8Zpzlx7zFPyjIDRdpq-Gg=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_software.jpg?itok=gH0Ov_hq" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Web
          </b>
          <b>
           Manager Software
          </b>
          <b>
           -
          </b>
          is an easy-to-use, web-based software program that allows system administrators to manage their CLIQ Remote platform from any computer, anywhere. In addition to daily functions such as managing and creating access permissions, audit trails, time-based scheduling and reports, the program also supports remote programming devise management; key programing and key and personnel management.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_6">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci6.googleusercontent.com/proxy/cB3fXDXcnFEKvl4m1wf6E3Wp25uqDS5T2QkAZTta4D5z2UC50kiWGKRETvWgOYRYfDej3zAUfro68iCUCpj7so6e5U0MqlUnoT5QowHRzCE9c5Cb0dJmlvfm9ZSWhFj0KSnA036OzCpkydY1N7TCUm4DQhDYhJD2Hz8waao=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_control_C.jpg?itok=sM1lad5I" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Control
          </b>
          <b>
           Key (C-Key)
          </b>
          <b>
           -
          </b>
          provides secure access for transferring information between the systems software and its cylinders, such as updating key authorization lists within the cylinder and the retrieval of audit trail info from cylinder to software. Used for unlocking electronic communication and not actual access points, every CLIQ system has at least one C-Key referred to as the Master C-Key. Additional C-Keys can be added to the system.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_7">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci5.googleusercontent.com/proxy/BFDQEsIE6MZvzXtSWO-82ssGjbRnZE-84vWP9V12intJNbLZ27Ci1R0xbWC_uXFcaOOlvPIV7eLlio9M_XttRcxiH04BcxkJn2l0P0A34kKrYEa-sDiF9yFD06-w8G1slZdxZ1wYSJb-8tH2RXIADF5xmiCv7OxS1kan5tcay2E=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Local_Programming.jpg?itok=BSxLIV0S" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Local Programming
          </b>
          <b>
           Device (PD)
          </b>
          <b>
           -
          </b>
          is used to connect CLIQ keys and cylinders to the systems management software CLIQ Manager and/or CLIQ Web Manager. The PD provides secure communication that can update CLIQ keys with the latest authorizations and scheduling; retrieve and upload audit trails; and transfer tasks from the CLIQ software to your C-Keys (Control Keys).
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_8">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci5.googleusercontent.com/proxy/FOjn2_OHwqDhupXNoRUnqAX_B0i3Ff2EXOKV04QiNfvx351DXqL152MvuaLhEP5KCNlSFMJX_1s1DXmZyMvPu5CmdRrVQroA_4VUaYZmxywEfJ3Vv7iDCwm4oUN7onMVWw3NN68ZmTXvOLYLadHVP8Fw_cl_y2_amqjDx3Gzu5Cx1sGa=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_wall_programming.jpg?itok=G44QkYC6" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Wall Programming
          </b>
          <b>
           Device (WPD) -
          </b>
          always online, the WPD has its own IP address that enables you to remotely program your CLIQ keys including making authorization and scheduling changes; retrieval of audit trails; revalidation of keys, sync of time settings; updating CLIQ keys firmware and more.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="14b7f37c25e5cf8e_9">
          </a>
          <img alt="" height="103" src="https://ci3.googleusercontent.com/proxy/4VDBhe3cMLrbhs3goYPwt3yf13-J6Cql_LnJXzzLCAYGSr9xnUlSqNlAXjRhwGPp6TEauy31zZh0qiL0TUYo0nyBU3VxOol74oYO2hhr5zHLvQP4-k7nOL0nqjYZNV-Vk1ncjVtmORaSWH0f58mHj3AgQuharqst1E81kQ=s0-d-e1-ft#http://www.mul-t-lock-cliq.com/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/Cliq_mobile_0.jpg?itok=bl2JPTZk" width="125"/>
          <b>
           CLIQ
          </b>
          <b>
           Mobile Programming
          </b>
          <b>
           Device (MPD) -
          </b>
          extremely efficient, the MPD allows you to program CLIQ keys on-the-go using a cellular phone or computer as a modem. With it, you can easily make authorization and profile changes; revalidate keys; retrieve audit trails; sync time settings and more  when and where you need to.
         </p>
