Well, nowadays such wedding rings not only will be an imagination. Some jewellery stores create wedding rings which are inspired from characters of Disney princesses.        Usually, when we look at engagement rings or wedding rings, generally they are custom projects which are costumed especially to the person wearing them. However, these are definitely different. You can go to a site called Gemvara and using their customization tool, you can create your own Disney princess wedding ring! Thus, go and create the compliment for your fairy tale wedding.Disney Princess Wedding Rings

      <p>
       Disney Princess Wedding Rings In the spring of 2010, a line of new rings was just released. It consists of six Disney-engagement rings, and all of them are inspired by princesses in Disney stories. Even the names of the rings are the same with the princesses in Disney stories, such as Snow White, Cinderella, Belle, Sleeping Beauty, Ariel, and Jasmine. There are even wedding bands to match each one. The creator of these Disney engagement and wedding rings said that subtle references to the personalities and characters of each princess can be seen from each piece of the collection. For example, to suggest Cinderellas ethereal grace and charm, her engagement ring and wedding ring feature pave and mill grain borders. For Ariels engagement ring and wedding ring, to suggest an art deco influence and her bold characteristic, there are baguettes and clean lines. Each ring from this collection costs from $4380 to $5460.Disney Princess Wedding Rings

       Kirstie Kelly, a clothing designer who made a beginning of a line of Disney character-inspired bridesmaid and flower girl dresses and wedding gowns, has added to the collection a line of character-inspired engagement and wedding rings. The six engagement rings with matching wedding bands, all are inspired from the Disney princesses, are set under the label Kirstie Kelly for Disney by Mouawad. She unveiled the collection in New York at the Intercontinental Barclay Hotel as a part of Briday Fashion Week.Disney Princess Wedding Rings
