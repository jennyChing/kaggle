             <div class="itemIntroText">
              <p>
               Un festival cest trop court, Nices short movie festival,is back in the city this October, and the 13th edition promises to be bigger and better than ever.
              </p>
             </div>
             <!-- Item fulltext -->
             <div class="itemFullText">
              <p>
               The
               <a href="http://www.nicefilmfestival.com/court-metrage/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 2013 festival
                </strong>
               </a>
               features no less than 83 singular, innovative or unpublished films, representing more than 30 countries, and four official competitions:
               <strong>
                The European Competition
               </strong>
               ,
               <strong>
                Shot Here
               </strong>
               (films shot and produced in the Provence-Alpes-Cte dAzur region),
               <strong>
                Experience
               </strong>
               (experimental film competition, video art, new cinema) and
               <strong>
                Short Animation
               </strong>
               (animated films from French language film schools).
              </p>
              <p>
               From the 15th to 20th of October, the
               <a href="http://www.nicefilmfestival.com/court-metrage/grille-des-programmes/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 movies will be shown
                </strong>
               </a>
               in many venues throughout town, from the
               <strong>
                Rialto
               </strong>
               and the
               <a href="http://www.mercury-cg06.fr" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Cinma Mercury
                </strong>
               </a>
               to the
               <a href="http://www.villa-arson.org" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Villa Arson
                </strong>
               </a>
               and the
               <strong>
                M.A.M.A.C.
               </strong>
               , and the various winners will be selected by a
               <strong>
                professional jury
               </strong>
               but also by the
               <strong>
                audience
               </strong>
               who will be able to vote after each showing for the
               <strong>
                Prix du Public
               </strong>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="Short film festival 2013 in Nice" src="/images/short-film-festival-poster-2013.jpg"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               The main goals of this festival are to bring together the best of the
               <strong>
                young European creators
               </strong>
               and to explore the
               <strong>
                richness
               </strong>
               of the continents cinema, honouring a different country each year. After the
               <strong>
                Czech Republic
               </strong>
               in 2012, the guest of honour of this new edition is
               <a href="http://www.nicefilmfestival.com/court-metrage/gros-plan-sur-le-danemark/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Denmark
                </strong>
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               Moviegoers will thus have a chance to discover, or rediscover as the case may be, movies by
               <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl_Theodor_Dreyer" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Carl Theodor Dreyer
                </strong>
               </a>
               ,
               <strong>
                Jorgen Leth
               </strong>
               and
               <a href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001885/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Lars Von Trier
                </strong>
               </a>
               , as well as by new up-and-coming directors.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <!-- JoomlaWorks "AllVideos" Plugin (v4.4) starts here -->
                <div class="avPlayerWrapper avVideo">
                 <div class="avPlayerContainer" style="width:680px;">
                  <div class="avPlayerBlock" id="AVPlayerID_e17e6f7e_124895135">
                   <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="383" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/6143hgZ2_fQ?rel=0&amp;rel=0&amp;fs=1&amp;wmode=transparent" title="JoomlaWorks AllVideos Player" width="680">
                   </iframe>
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <!-- JoomlaWorks "AllVideos" Plugin (v4.4) ends here -->
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               But the
               <strong>
                six-day event
               </strong>
               is not just a competition, it is also a big
               <strong>
                celebration
               </strong>
               and for the duration of the festival, many workshops, meetings of authors and producers, round tables, debates and concerts will also take place every day.
              </p>
              <p>
               In 2012, Un festival cest trop court attracted
               <strong>
                8,000 spectators
               </strong>
               , so do not miss your chance to
               <a href="http://www.fnacspectacles.com/place-spectacle/manifestation/Film-UN-FESTIVAL-C-EST-TROP-COURT--FNI13.htm#" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 participate
                </strong>
               </a>
               in this fun and interactive movie fest this year.
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="Court mtrage film festival in Nice" src="/images/court-metrage-logo.jpg"/>
              </p>
