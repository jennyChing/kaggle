             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://www.lostgarden.com/2007/02/happy-valentines-day-turtles-all-way.html">
               Happy Valentine's Day:  Turtles all the way down
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.lostgarden.com/gfx/TurtlesAllTheWayDown-small.jpg" style="margin: 0px auto 10px; display: block; text-align: center;"/>
              <br/>
              Here is the painting I made for Valentine's Day this year.  If you haven't planted a big juicy smacker on someone's passionately pleasurable lips,  you now have a mission.  Precious moments of nibbling and kissing are 'a wasting!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              The painting title comes from a hilariously metaphysical physics
              <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turtles_all_the_way_down" onclick="return top.js.OpenExtLink(window,event,this)" target="_blank">
               joke
              </a>
              .  This is the sort of thing that kept all the science students chortling late into the night as we crunched vector fields and fudged probability envelopes.
              <span>
               <br/>
              </span>
              <span>
               <span style="font-style: italic;">
               </span>
              </span>
              <blockquote>
               <span>
                <span style="font-style: italic;">
                 A well-known scientist (some say it was Bertrand Russell) once gave a public lecture on astronomy. He described how the Earth orbits around the sun and how the sun, in turn, orbits around the centre of a vast collection of stars called our galaxy.
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span>
               </span>
               <span style="font-style: italic;">
                At the end of the lecture, a little old lady at the back of the room got up and said: "What you have told us is rubbish. The world is really a flat plate supported on the back of a giant tortoise."
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-style: italic;">
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-style: italic;">
                The scientist gave a superior smile before replying, "What is the tortoise standing on?"
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-style: italic;">
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-style: italic;">
                "You're very clever, young man, very clever," said the old lady. "But it's turtles all the way down!"
               </span>
               <br/>
              </blockquote>
              With love and respect,
              <br/>
