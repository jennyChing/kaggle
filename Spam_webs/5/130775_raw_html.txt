         The 12 Steps of the Mindfulness Meditation Practice
        </font>
       </h2>
       <p align="justify">
        The 12 Steps of the Mindfulness Meditation Practice are the foundation of the Institutes unique approach to learning this time-tested ancient practice. They begin with learning some of the basic principles of mindfulness meditation, such as the Four Noble Truths and the Five Hindrances.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        Then you will learn the proper techniques of the meditation practice, so you can make quick and steady progress toward calming your mind and overcoming stress. The remaining steps show you how to make mindfulness a way of life, so you can realize true inner peace. Download a printable copy
        <a href="http://www.mindfulnessmeditationinstitute.org/download.php?file=The 12 Steps of the Mindfulness Meditation Practice.pdf" target="_top" title=" The 12 Steps of the Mindfulness Meditation Practice ">

           here
          </b>
         </font>
        </a>
        .

        <b>
         Mindfulness Meditation Made Simple: Your Guide to Finding True Inner Peace.

        This powerful book explains the 12 Steps in detail, and shows you how to get the most from your meditation practice. Download your free sample chapter

       <h2>
        <font color="6C59FF">
         Goal Statement Exercise
        </font>
       </h2>
       <p align="justify">
        Most successful people establish goals for themselves. This increases the likelihood that they will actually accomplish them. If youre serious about your meditation practice, then youll make the commitment to practicing diligently and consistently.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        A goal statement is a powerful tool to help you get started with your mindfulness meditation practice, and stay on track. This simple exercise will make it easy for you to prepare your own statement, and show you to use it for maximum effectiveness. Download the exercise
        <a href="http://www.mindfulnessmeditationinstitute.org/download.php?file=GoalStatementExercise.pdf" target="_top" title="Goal Statement Exercise">
         <font color="blue">
          here
         </font>
        </a>

       <h2>
        <font color="6C59FF">
         Writing Meditations
        </font>
       </h2>
       <p align="justify">
        You can
        <b>
         change a lifetime of habits in less than a week
        </b>
        with these powerful writing meditations. This simple exercise can be done in just 10 minutes a day.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        Simply copy the them by hand in a notebook or journal, for about 10-15 minutes a day. Any time of the day is fine. After a few days, youll notice how your thinking and behavior are changing without any conscious effort.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        Here are free printable copies of our life-changing writing meditations.
        <strong>
         Share them freely with friends and family members
        </strong>
        who you would like to see find true happiness in their lives. The writing meditations will help them transform their lives in just a matter of days.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <u>
          Healing, relationship, and sociability
         </u>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        The
        <a href="http://www.mindfulnessmeditationinstitute.org/download.php?file=Loving-Kindness Writing Meditation.pdf" target="_top" title="Loving-Kindness Writing Meditation">
         <font color="blue">
          loving-kindness writing meditation
         </font>
        </a>
        will help you heal the wounds from your past by changing your attitudes about other people. It will help you become more understanding and forgiving of other people for their transgressions. The meditation will also help you become more sociable and outgoing.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <u>
          Weight loss and smoking cessation
         </u>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        The
        <a href="http://www.mindfulnessmeditationinstitute.org/download.php?file=Mindful Consumption Writing Meditation.pdf" target="_top" title="Mindful Consumption Writing Meditation">
         <font color="blue">
          mindful consumption meditation
         </font>
        </a>
        will help you overcome stubborn habits such as smoking and overeating. It does this by helping you become more mindful of the things you put into your body, and by giving you the inner strength to let go of nutrients and substances that arent serving you well.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <u>
          Staying focused on your spiritual path
         </u>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        <a href="http://www.mindfulnessmeditationinstitute.org/download.php?file=Living the Mindfulness Meditation Practice.pdf" target="_top" title="Living the Mindfulness Meditation Practice">
         <font color="blue">
          Living the mindfulness meditation practice
         </font>
        </a>
        . This comprehensive writing meditation will help you stay focused by reinforcing in your subconscious the ideals of your meditation practice. It covers your goals, the practice, loving-kindness, relationships, mindful consumption, and carrying the message of the practice to others.
        <br/>

          Cancer Recovery
