    <div class="g_txt_5">
     Noida, India, February 14,2015/Free-Press-Release.com/ -- Welcome to the Supertech Golf Village, which has the most current styling and a residential venture which has been developed by Supertech Group at Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida. It is  a residential township, which can boast of a new as well as ultra modern residential apartments in Greater Noida. It is located on the Yamuna Expressway of Greater Noida and providing the buyers with 1, 2 &amp; 3 BHK Apartments which have been set up in sizes ranging from 690 to 1440 Sq. Ft. It is a township which has been sprawled over an area of 11 acres at Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida, and a location that is easily accessible from all parts of Greater Noida and also very well connected to all important places in Greater Noida as well as neighboring areas of Delhi/NCR.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Since it is situated in Noida, it has loads of exciting elements such as the Night Safari, f1 Motor Track as well as an 18 + Hole Golf Course so that you are able to live a life full of escapade in the city. Noida is a city of NCR which is able to offer its inhabitants with a vibrant life.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     The township is only  ten minutes drive away from the metro station and this is why the different territories are not difficult to commute while you stay here. Noida-Greater Noida connects Delhi/NCR with this outlandish golf colony. Gautam Buddha University, Pari Chowk, shopping arcades and excitement zone all lie in the area which makes connectivity more hassle free.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     It is a township, which is spread across 100 sections of lush green land where more than 90% of spaces are decked up with rich common greens. When you stay here, you will be able stay here in a more secured lifestyle inside the gated neighborhood.
