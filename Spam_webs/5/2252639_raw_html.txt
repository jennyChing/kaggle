      <h1>
       Forging Tools
      </h1>
      <p>
       One of my many endeavors has been to blacksmith. It has taken me a while to gather the tools to get started. Now that I have the fun has begun. When you forge and look down in the fire. It is so hot, above 2000 degrees, that you can place a poker into what was rock and has become molten rock. This heat is doing your bidding to manipulate iron into a new shape. As a wood worker I wait for the tree to grow. As a blacksmith I only wait for the smelter to produce my material.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/lonmin_smelter.jpg">
        <img alt="lonmin_smelter" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-967" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/lonmin_smelter.jpg?resize=225%2C239"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       All of our tools and anything we used that was made of any metal was produced by a smith. If it was of metal they made it. Metal was so precious that they would burn a structure and retrieve the nails from it after it burned.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/rosehead.jpg">
        <img alt="rosehead" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-968" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/rosehead.jpg?resize=500%2C375"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       Many of the tools we consider modern were forged by the smith.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://i1.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/vintage-adjustable-tools-by-whiteforge.jpg">
        <img alt="vintage-adjustable-tools-by-whiteforge" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-969" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/vintage-adjustable-tools-by-whiteforge.jpg?resize=450%2C360"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       I have a few basic smithing tools to make that I need to use to produce the hardware I want. And my intention is to create them myself.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://i1.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/l.jpg">
        <img alt="smithtools" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-970" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/l.jpg?resize=400%2C300"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       I am looking to build nails, pulls, hinges and other decorative pieces.
      </p>
      <p>
       I have just gotten my hands on some coke. This is coal that they have cooked the impurities out of. It burns clean and very hot. A have a british friend of mine that tells me they toasted there crumpets over it a s a child.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://i1.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Petroleum_Coke_Indonesian_Steam_Coal_South_African.jpg">
        <img alt="Petroleum_Coke_Indonesian_Steam_Coal_South_African" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-974" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Petroleum_Coke_Indonesian_Steam_Coal_South_African.jpg?resize=600%2C400"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       It requires a lot of oxygen. A ole style hand blower will not produce enough air to sustain the fire. They would mix coal with it in the ole days to keep the fire going.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://i0.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/2880-A.jpg">
        <img alt="2880-A" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-975" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/chiselandforge.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/2880-A.jpg?resize=563%2C800"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       Now I would like any tips and ideas that you can give me. I am learning this and love to learn more.
       <br/>
       I was playing around with my software and made a movie trailer. I will show that first then the second video will be a few tools I have made.
       <br/>
       The holdfasts in the video are a test set. They are to small in diameter and have not been heat treated properly. Thank you for your time and patience.
       <br/>

      </p>
      Please be careful. Some of the things I do are dangerous. It is not my fault if you get hurt. Use your brain, be safe.
