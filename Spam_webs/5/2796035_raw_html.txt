        <h1 class="postitle">
         Worry Free Handmade Soap &amp; Skincare
        </h1>
        <div class="imagess_box">
        </div>
        <div class="single_metainfo">
         by Dinah Wulf
        </div>
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="15198337" data-link="http://www.diyinspired.com/worry-free-handmade-soap-skincare/" data-summary="" data-title="Worry Free Handmade Soap &amp; Skincare">
        </div>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          <em>
           Worry Free Handmade Soap and Skincare,
          </em>
          is a sponsored review by
          <a href="http://bambuearth.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Bambu Earth
          </a>
          . All opinions are 100% my own.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20116" height="432" src="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.diyinspired.com/worry-free-handmade-soap-skincare/&amp;media=http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care.jpg&amp;description=Worry Free Handmade Soap and Skincare')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          When I received a package in the mail from Bambu Earth, I couldnt wait to open it. I ripped off the tape and opened the flaps to find this
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20117" height="432" src="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-1.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.diyinspired.com/worry-free-handmade-soap-skincare/&amp;media=http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-1.jpg&amp;description=Worry Free Handmade Soap and Skincare')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          My first thought was how cool! my second thought was this company is going over and above their mission to live a simpler more sustainable life. To be honest, I think Ill be a copycat and print my business cards on the backs of cereal boxes.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          Five years ago,
          <a href="http://bambuearth.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Bambu Earth
          </a>
          products were born. Made by hand with local ingredients in Southern California, they make a worry free handmade soap and skincare line.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          <strong>
           The Bambu Earth skin care package includes:
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           Tea Tree Cleanser
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           Rosemary Toner
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           Petitgrain Facial Cream
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           Herbal Sugar Glow
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           Herbal Buttermilk Mask
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           Tea Tree Facial Soap
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          My personal favorite is the organic tea tree facial soap. The soap wrapper is seeded and can be planted to grow flowers! The alcohol free Petitgrain facial cream and herbal sugar glow feels great on your skin and is non-greasy and feeling super soft.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20118" height="438" src="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-2.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.diyinspired.com/worry-free-handmade-soap-skincare/&amp;media=http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-2.jpg&amp;description=Worry Free Handmade Soap and Skincare')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          The Cleanser is formulated with simple, effective ingredients without harsh chemicals or alcohol. The Toner is formulated with organic apple cider vinegar to balance your skins PH level while organic alcohol-free witch hazel acts as a natural astringent.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bambu-Earth-Skin-Care-3.jpg">
