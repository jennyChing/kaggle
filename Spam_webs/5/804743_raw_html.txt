         <div id="description-text">
          Thanksgiving Pilgrim woman charm // Lampwork with clear amber Czech glass
          <br/>
          <br/>
          This Thanksgiving pilgrim charm features a cheerful lampwork pilgrim woman framed with two faceted Czech glass beads in clear amber with AB finish for lots of colorful sparkle. Each lampwork pilgrim is unique. This particular little lady has a sweet smirky smile. These would make great hostess gifts!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The charm is strung on a silver-plated lobster clasp. I oil and clean all clasps before sending to ensure they are easy to use.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The first photo shows your charm. The second photo shows one of these charms on a purse zipper pull; the third photo shows my little gaggle of pilgrim charms all together. Get a set and you can have a happy couple!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          All metals used in my shop are lead free and nickle free.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Charm Length: 2 inches including clasp.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Your charm will come attractively boxed to gift or to keep.
