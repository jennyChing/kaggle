                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Rags (2012) Review
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7159530899332763987" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-JA2_J-1zQ8w/U7gOD4tKjuI/AAAAAAAAFvY/sUOacDpYFC8/s1600/Rags+(2012).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-JA2_J-1zQ8w/U7gOD4tKjuI/AAAAAAAAFvY/sUOacDpYFC8/s1600/Rags+(2012).jpg" width="213"/>
                     </a>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <b>
                       Description:
                      </b>
                      Rags is an American musical comedy television film. It follows the story of Charlie Prince (Max Schneider) and Kadee Worth (Keke Palmer) in a gender switched version of the story of Cinderella.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <b>
                       Director:
                      </b>
                      Bille Woodruff
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <b>
                       Writers:
                      </b>
                      Jason Fuchs
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Elizabeth Hackett
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Hilary Galanoy
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <b>
                       Music:
                      </b>
                      Rodney Jerkins
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Andre Lindal
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Travis Sayles
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <b>
                       Starring:
                      </b>
                      Max Schneider (Charlie Prince)
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Keke Palmer (Kadee Worth)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Drake Bell (Shawn/the Fairy Godfather")
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Avan Jogia (Finn)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Robert Moloney (Arthur)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Zac Santiago (Diego)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Christina Sicoli (Martha)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Keenan Tracey (Andrew)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Burkeley Duffield (Lloyd)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <b>
                        Reception:
                       </b>
                       The film received 3.5 million views on its opening night and 4.6 million in total. It became one of Nickelodeon's highest rated original films. It stayed at the top of the iTunes charts as best musical for over a month.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <b>
                        My Review:
                       </b>
                       I am so glad that this movie actually happened. It was really good and the talent in it was pretty impeccable. It is sad that a lot of people will never even give it the time of day just because it is a Nickelodeon movie. People need to start appreciating Nickelodeon because it is very underrated. Instead of thanking Nickelodeon for finding and growing talent such as Ariana Grande, Keke Palmer, Max Schneider and Elizabeth Gillies, people say that they are hurting their reputation by being on this network. News flash, they would not even have a reputation to lose if it was not for Nickelodeon and if you watch Nickelodeon shows and movies you may just think that it is not such a bad network after all.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       The first thing I noticed in Rags was the cast. I had been a fan of Max Schneiders YouTube covers and music for a while before I saw the movie. He is an incredible talent and he did a very good job in this movie. Charlie Prince (Max Schneider) and Kadee Worth (Keke Palmer) had beautiful chemistry together. The movie reminded me of Keke's performance in
                       <a href="http://www.musicalmoviereviews.com/2014/06/joyful-noise-2012-review.html" target="_blank">
                        Joyful Noise (2012)
                       </a>
                       . I did prefer Joyful Noise but they are both favorites of mine so I enjoy this one a lot too.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       The music in Rags is really good. I found it very different to most of the other music I have heard before but there was something pretty authentic about it. Well, of course that does not include the music that was purposefully rubbish to prove a point about what artistry really is. Point proven. I had two favorite songs. The first one came from the scene where Charlie went into Bernie's thrift shop to make a payment on his mothers old piano and he played "Nothing Gets Better Than This" and started dancing around the shop. The second is when Charlie took Kadee somewhere in the streets of New York and got her to sing with shades on so no one would recognize her and she sang "Look At Me Now" beautifully.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Honestly, there were parts of the movie that seemed pointless and awkward but it all kind of worked out by highlighting the parts of the movie that were good. Max Schneider is trying to release some music at the moment and I really hope he finds some more luck and more people get the chance to listen to him.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       This was a great movie and I hope you find the time to watch it. It would be a very good movie for the family to watch and it has a really authentic feel to it.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>

                       Thank you for reading my review. Please comment below and tell me what you think of the movie. Suggestions for new reviews are also welcome.
