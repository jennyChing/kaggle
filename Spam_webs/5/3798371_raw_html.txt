        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         If you are not sure where to take your little darlings so they can enjoy an out-of-this-world, wacky, outrageous and truly exciting meal, you are in luck: we have picked a few of our best from all over  the worlds best themed restaurants for kids.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         There are so many great things about being a kid when it comes to entertainment  the theme parks, the animal adventure parks and the greatest themedrestaurants for kids. Who wouldnt want to be a pirate during dinner or enjoy a banquet fit for a king while dressed as one  and thats just the parents!
        </p>
        <h1>
         The Worlds Best Themed Restaurants for Kids
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Chessington Zufari Bar &amp; Grill" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-18408" src="http://goingcrazywannago.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Chessington-Zufari-Bar-Grill.bmp"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Chessingtons Zufari Bar &amp; Grill  Chessington World of Adventures, UK
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Whats nice about this place is not only that it is brand new, it is geared towards the whole family. Perched right over the Wanyama Reserve, you and your family can look down over the animals as they go about their day-to-day activities. Munch on African-themed food right from the sizzling grill or the fresh salad bar, while looking down on antelopes, zebra and their friends in the reserve below.
        </p>
        <h2>
         World of Warcraft  Beijing, China
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This is perfect for the World of Warcraft junkie who can barely tear themselves away from the computer for a night out with the rest of the family. The restaurant was designed with features taken directly from this iconic game and fans will be delighted, feeling as if they have walked right into the game itself. From the front entrance to the menu, every surface displays familiar scenes and faces from the game. And if that is not enough, you can check out snippets of the Massive Multiplayer Online game on TV screens right throughout the venue.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Pirate's Dinner Adventure" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-18409" src="http://goingcrazywannago.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Pirates-Dinner-Adventure.bmp"/>
        </h2>
        <h2>
         Pirates Dinner Adventure  USA
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This is without doubt one of the most exciting dinner adventures for any age group  kids and adults alike: an interactive, acrobatic pirate-themed show complete with a feast fit for any budding buccaneer. Climb aboard an 18th century Spanish galleon that is anchored in a sparkling lagoon, surrounded by no less than six other ships. Be immersed in a mind-blowing show with cannon blasts, stunts and swashbuckling for a theatrical dinner that your kids will talk about forever afterwards.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="The Redwoods Treehouse" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-18407" height="283" src="http://goingcrazywannago.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/The-Redwoods-Treehouse.jpg" width="400"/>
        </h2>
        <h2>
         Treehouse Restaurant  New Zealand
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Right in the heart of Auckland, New Zealand is the Redwoods Treehouse Retreat. A stunning feat of architectural genius, a dramatic bulb-like structure peers some 10m up into the treetops accessible by a winding walkway. The Treehouse is the ultimate venue for an amazing dinner, and will be something your kids have never before seen in their lives and possibly will never see again: a truly remarkable, once-in-a-lifetime experience.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Rainforest Cafe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-18410" src="http://goingcrazywannago.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Rainforest-Cafe.bmp"/>
        </h2>
        <h2>
         Rainforest Caf  London and USA
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The Rainforest Caf is the ultimate adventure-themed restaurant to take your family. Choose from areas like the Gorillas, Aquarium, Butterfly Room or book to sit in the Elephants Area. Great, healthy jungle food is on the menu surrounded by the sights and sounds of the thick of the rainforest, and this is one venue that will be a lifetime hit with your family.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What are your favorite themed restaurants for kids?
        </h2>
