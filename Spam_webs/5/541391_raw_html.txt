       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         BeautyBeam: Putting A Spotlight On Your Polishing
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/beautybeam-putting-a-spotlight-on-your-polishing/" rel="bookmark" title="7:40 PM">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-06-09T19:40:29+00:00">
           June 9, 2014
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/author/kimberlyp/" rel="author" title="View all posts by kimberlyp">
            kimberlyp
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Sometimes, I want to say What will they think of next? when I see new beauty gadgets. The
         <a href="http://thebeautybeam.com/">
          BeautyBeam
         </a>
         is one that had me wondering why it took so long to come up with this idea. Its a little flashlight for your nail polish bottle!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0053.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0053" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4007" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0053-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0054.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0054" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4008" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0054-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Like a miners lamp, you can strap this right on the handle and put a trouble light right on your work
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0057.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0057" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4011" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0057-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And, if you need a tutorial, you can download BeautyBeams app
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0058.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0058" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4012" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0058-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Even if you do your nails in a well-lit area, putting some light right at the point of work can make it just a bit easier. This is a tiny gadget, so youll want to keep it in a safe spot. There are two pieces
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="4c4c4070-d97e-478e-a39a-bf738d9fa0f6">
          <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="2c338a95-f198-4c46-a4c7-c202bfefb481">
           to
          </span>
         </span>
         it; the light itself and the
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="9fb135d1-1ddd-4c69-9499-a13f4059556d">
          <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="16040c45-c3b6-4a31-95bb-e907d9c2b223">
           silcone
          </span>
         </span>
         sleeve that holds it on the bottle. The little button is the on/off switch
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0023.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0023" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3991" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0023-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0024.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0024" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3992" height="778" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0024-768x1024.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I brought out an array of different polishes to test the BeatyBeam on. Heres the bottles I picked
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0021.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0021" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3990" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0021-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I will admit, it took me a moment of wrestling with the
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="87588752-4b8a-40f6-85cc-ed33ab0f1026">
          <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="3ffc4123-801c-4543-a53c-5512c59afecb">
           silcone
          </span>
         </span>
         sleeve to stretch it over the polishs handle and get the light in place. But, after I had switched it out over numerous bottles, I did get it down and was fairly quick with it. Heres what it looks like on a bottle of polish
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0046.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0046" class="aligncenter wp-image-4005 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0046-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0044.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0044" class="aligncenter wp-image-4004 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0044-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0041.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0041" class="aligncenter wp-image-4003 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0041-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0040.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0040" class="aligncenter wp-image-4002 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0040-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0039.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0039" class="aligncenter wp-image-4001 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0039-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0038.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0038" class="aligncenter wp-image-4000 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0038-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0037.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0037" class="aligncenter wp-image-3999 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0037-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0031.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0031" class="aligncenter wp-image-3996 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0031-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once the light is
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="eb77d82d-b423-42e6-bcd7-9c64f1b2b5e9">
          <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="55850568-a97e-43ee-ac39-71bed4fb2b72">
           snuggly
          </span>
         </span>
         in place, turn it on and then remove the polishs top from the bottle
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0047.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0047" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4006" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0047-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll want to make sure that the light isnt too far down that it gets in the way of dipping the brush back into the bottle too
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0035.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0035" class="aligncenter wp-image-3997 size-medium" height="225" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0035-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The light wasnt as bright as I thought it would be. However, as you can tell, Im taking these photos
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="1bf5ee73-88e3-4dec-b024-08ff5b0393d8">
          <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="fd779913-f7ca-4297-aa1d-6f5dc2e156bd">
           at
          </span>
         </span>
         a sunny window. There is just enough of a light beam to make it to the end of the brush and shine right where you are polishing
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beautybeam-on.jpg">
          <img alt="beautybeam on" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4014" height="439" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beautybeam-on-1024x771.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0029.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCF0029" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3995" height="438" src="http://libbyspinkvanity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DSCF0029-1024x768.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         As I was removing the BeautyBeams
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="5a25b6f6-3c81-4cbf-8114-e524667c23bb">
          <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="da9379d6-54db-420d-8327-45e70510f615">
           silcone
          </span>
         </span>
         sleeve, I noticed that it was quite easy to stretch to hold the light in place. It didnt slide around.
        </p>
        <p>
         One thing that I learned when I was in nail tech school was to not hunch over while doing nails. As a professional, doing that every day would be murder on your back! So, I have found that I do sit up straight, but lean into the table while I paint my nails. However, if I leaned my face down even closer to my nails, I did see that the light make a better visual impact on the area I was polishing.
        </p>
