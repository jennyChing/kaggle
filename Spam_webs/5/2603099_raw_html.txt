                <strong>
                 Ingredients:
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
                </li>
                <li>
                 spoonful of honey
                </li>
                <li>
                 fresh lemon juice (use 1 lemon)
                </li>
                <li>
                 300 ml/ 10 oz water
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Preparation:
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                Put water on stove until it boils. After it boils, let it stand and cool for awhile. Do not add ingredients in hot water because they will lose their quality. After water cools down add
                <a href="http://www.healthyfoodhouse.com/health-benefits-and-uses-of-cinnamon/" title="cinnamon">
                 cinnamon
                </a>
                and
                <a href="http://www.healthyfoodhouse.com/the-amazing-benefits-of-honey/" title="honey">
                 honey
                </a>
                and let it rest for 15/20 minutes. Before consuming this drink add fresh lemon juice (take 1 or half a lemon). After it rests for awhile drink 150 ml in the morning and drink the rest 150 ml before bedtime.
               </p>
               <p>
                Let this be your everyday routine because experiences have shown it helps losing weight.
