         <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
          eVokalization  Free Live Pack by PerforModule
         </h1>
         <div class="byline">
          <abbr class="published" title="Friday, August 1st, 2014, 9:45 am">
           August 1, 2014
          </abbr>
           by
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://sonicbloom.net/en/author/madeleine/" title="Madeleine Bloom">
            Madeleine Bloom
           </a>
          </span>
           in
          <span class="category">

            Freebies
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           Generally, the freebies on Sonic Bloom are made by me. This time though I've got a great free Live Pack from Animus Invidious a.k.a.
           <a href="http://performodule.wordpress.com/" target="_blank" title="PerforModule">
            PerforModule
           </a>
           for you. "eVokalization" contains one melodic multi-synth Instrument Rack and one beatbox Drum Rack made out of the musician eVokal's processed voice. The pack also includes a selection of free-to-use beatbox loops created by eVokal off-the-cuff.
           <span id="more-5797">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           The samples were recorded at 96kHz sample rate via Kaoss Pad into a Firewire audio interface. The Instrument Rack is a 3-voice synth made out of eVokal's voice. It contains a digeridoo-like bass sound evolving over time, a human voice playing "round robin" samples so that every note is slightly different and there are slight pitch scoops and fluctuations for an extremely "human" feel, and a clear, smooth, angelic voice which almost sounds like a string section or a pad. The Drum Rack contains individual beat box hits with sounds ranging from thumping kicks to shimmering hi-hats.
          </p>
          <p>
           The "eVokalization" Live Pack is self-installing and works for Live 9.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Listen to the demo:
          </h3>
          <p>
           <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/tracks/155784731%3Fsecret_token%3Ds-y1382&amp;color=ff5500&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=false&amp;show_artwork=true&amp;show_comments=true&amp;show_user=true&amp;show_reposts=false" width="100%">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           If clicking it to download results in a text-file, simply right-click and choose 'Save Link as...'. To install, just double-click the alp-file and choose the installation path when prompted by Live.
          </p>

          </p>
