      Lip Sync Battle Recap: Stephen Merchant vs. Malin Akerman
     </h1>
     <div class="mom-post-meta single-post-meta">
      <span class="author vcard">
       Posted By:
       <span class="fn">
        <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/author/todd-betzold">
         Todd Betzold
        </a>

     <div class="entry-content">
      <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="16167190" data-link="http://gossipandgab.com/72456/lip-sync-battle-recap-stephen-merchant-vs-malin-akerman" data-summary="Of all the episodes of Lip Sync Battle on Spike, this is one of them that I was excited to see as Stephen Merchant takes on &quot;Dirrty&quot; by Christina Aguilera while wearing leather chaps! It was hilarious in the Spike's Lip Sync Battle previews and I want to see it all play out, as Stephen takes on Malin Akerman, who I also happen to love. Check out all the details below in our Lip Sync Battle recap!" data-title="Lip Sync Battle Recap: Stephen Merchant vs. Malin Akerman">
      </div>
      <p>
       Of all the episodes of
       <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/lip-sync-battle" target="_blank" title="Lip Sync Battle">
        <em>
         Lip Sync Battle
        </em>
       </a>
       on Spike, this is one of them that I was excited to see as
       <strong>
        Stephen Merchant
       </strong>
       takes on Dirrty by
       <strong>
        Christina Aguilera
       </strong>
       while wearing leather chaps! It was hilarious in the
       <em>
        Spikes Lip Sync Battle
       </em>
       previews and I want to see it all play out, as Stephen takes on
       <strong>
        Malin Akerman
       </strong>
       , who I also happen to love. Check out all the details below in our
       <em>
        Lip Sync Battle
       </em>
       recap!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/72456/lip-sync-battle-recap-stephen-merchant-vs-malin-akerman/lip-sync-battle-2" rel="attachment wp-att-72467">
        <img alt="Lip Sync Battle Spoilers - Malin Akerman" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-72467" height="333" src="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Lip-Sync-Battle-Spoilers-Stephen-Merchant-vs-Malin-Akerman-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/71757/lip-sync-battle-recap-hoda-kotb-vs-michael-strahan" target="_blank" title="Lip Sync Battle Recap: Hoda Kotb vs. Michael Strahan">
        <strong>
         Last week on
         <em>
          Lip Sync Battle
         </em>
         2015
        </strong>
       </a>
       , it was a battle of the morning show personalities, as
       <strong>
        Hoda Kotb
       </strong>
       took on
       <strong>
        Michael Strahan
       </strong>
       . While it was fun to watch, Hoda took home the championship belt. She did a good job, but was a little nervous. Michael was working that stage, but his lip syncing sucked!
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #ff0000;">
        <strong>
         <i>
          Warning: This post contains spoilers on Lip Sync Battle
         </i>
        </strong>
       </span>
       <em>
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <strong>
          tonight on Spike, so stop reading now if you dont want to know what happened!!!
         </strong>
        </span>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Stephen Merchant is so awkward, he makes it so fun to watch him perform! And I love Malin and wish
       <em>
        Trophy Wife
       </em>
       was still on ABC! Lets get to these performances now!
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #0000ff;">
        <strong>
         ROUND 1
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Malin Akerman 
       </strong>
       She performs Pour Some Sugar On Me by
       <strong>
        Def Leopard
       </strong>
       ! This was awesome and she is so good! She was working that stage and that mic stand and she was looking quite sexy. Stephen said it is not fair and he didnt know they could use their sex appeal!
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Stephen Merchant 
       </strong>
       He performs Hero by
       <strong>
        Enrique Iglesias
       </strong>
       . He gets into the character big time and he is taking us on the serious side for this performance, but he is just as amazing. He awkwardly performs it to a guy in the audience, which is why I love him!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <center>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/SUBlLZ1FVH0" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </center>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #0000ff;">
        <strong>
         ROUND 2
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Malin Akerman 
       </b>
       She performs Talk Dirty To Me by
       <strong>
        Jason Derulo
       </strong>
       . She talks some major smack before this one, but she definitely backs it up! She brought out the backup dancers and they were bringing the sex tonight. She busted out some dance moves and I just love this woman!
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Stephan Merchant 
       </strong>
       He performs Dirrty by
       <strong>
        Christina Aguilera
       </strong>
       . They are keeping it dirty with this second round, huh? I swear, I had a smile on my face for this entire performance. He is so good and no shame coming out in that outfit! Malin did great, but you have to give him props for this one!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <center>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zZdxlVbziYo" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </center>
      <p>
       <strong>
        RESULTS 
       </strong>
       The audience votes and Stephen gets the victory for that last performance alone and takes home the championship belt!
      </p>
