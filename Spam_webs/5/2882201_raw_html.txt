       <h1>
        2014 Infiniti Q60  a powerfully refined sport coupe
       </h1>
       <div id="articles">
        <div class="byline">
         By
         <a href="/users/aaront" title="View user profile.">
          Aaron Turpen
         </a>
         , Sun, October 27, 2013
        </div>
        <div class="all-attached-images">
         <div class="image-attach-body image-attach-node-5035" style="width: 251px;">
          <a href="/image/2014-infiniti-q60-awd-aaron">
           <img alt="2014 Infiniti Q60 AWD (Aaron)" class="image image-news3 " height="251" src="http://www.futurecars.com/sites/default/files/images/2014 Infiniti Q60S - house leftside 2 AOA800px.news3.jpg" title="2014 Infiniti Q60 AWD (Aaron)" width="251"/>
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         The 2014 Infiniti Q60 sport coupe is remarkably refined and smooth without sacrificing the sportiness and excellent handling that is expected in a true speedster. The Q60 holds the road and propels down the road with an excellent feel while also managing a smooth, luxurious drive in traffic and for the distance.
        </p>
        <h2>
         The manufacturer says
        </h2>
        <p>
         <em>
          Your Infiniti transforms the reality of traffic with technology that will help effortlessly manage your speed and maintain your distance to the car ahead. When traffic slows, Intelligent Cruise Control will automatically slow your vehicle to help maintain a safe distance, then resume your set cruising speed when traffic clears.
         </em>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Specs:
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Manufacturer:
          </strong>
          Infiniti
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Year, Model:
          </strong>
          2014 Q60 AWD
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Class, Type:
          </strong>
          Luxury Sport Coupe
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Propulsion system:
          </strong>
          3.7L VVEL V6, 7spd Automatic
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Base Price:
          </strong>
          $40,400
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           MSRP as tested:
          </strong>
          $52,055
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Availability:
          </strong>
          Now
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         Overview
        </h2>
        <p>
         Sport coupes tend to come in two stripes: they're either very sporty and uncomfortable or they're comfortable and not very sporty. The 2014 Q60 changes that, however, offering both sportiness and comfort in one package, rivaling the best that the Germans can offer in the same regard.
        </p>
        <p>
         Highway comfort comes in the form of the extra gear that dampens road noise by lowering RPM levels and giving a smooth, even ride at speed.  Formerly the G37 Coupe, which is already a well-designed sport coupe, the 2014 Infiniti Q60 is a further refinement of that design.
        </p>
        <p>
         It begins with a 3.7-liter V6 with VVEL that produces a memorable exhaust note and beautiful sounds when pressed, but stays quiet when on the highway at cruising speeds. This engine couples to a seven-speed transmission that glides through the gears evenly and without complaint. The top gear comes into play on the highway, lowering RPM levels from the engine to hold speeds, thus reducing fuel consumption and noise levels to make for a more comfortable ride.
        </p>
        <p>
         The all-wheel drive model we drove (a 2WD and manual option are available) delivers 18 mpg in the city and 25 mpg on the highway, which is better than many in this category. The 2WD option raises those numbers to 19/27 city/highway while the manual transmission lowers efficiency to 17/25. In a three-hour road trip on the freeway, averaging nearly 70 mph, we managed 27 mpg overall in our 2014 Infiniti Q60 Coupe AWD.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What we like
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Powerful, responsive, yet comfortable.
         </strong>
         Unlike others in this segment, the Q60 delivers all three.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Beautiful styling
         </strong>
         that is both simple and elegant, denoting the refinement this car enjoys inside and out.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What we don't
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Not the most 'green' of cars
         </strong>
         , but none in this segment are. The sport coupe segment aims for luxury and power, not green cred, but even here, the Infiniti Q60 delivers good fuel economy in comparison to the competition.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Conclusions
        </h2>
        <p>
         The 2014 Infiniti Q60 is a great combination of power and refinement and will please most who are interested in a luxury coupe. It's comfortable enough to drive everyday, yet exciting enough to make for a great weekend "fun car" as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Test Period Length and Limitations
         </strong>
         <br/>
         The 2014 Infiniti Q60 AWD was driven for about a week as a manufacturer's loan. A total f 478 miles were put on the car in a mixture of highway, freeway, and city driving in the Southeastern Wyoming and Denver, Colorado areas.
        </p>
