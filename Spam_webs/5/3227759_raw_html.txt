             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Use Smaller Plates
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <font color="#2A2A2A">
               Instead of eating your meal on a full sized plate, use a
               <strong>
                smaller plate
               </strong>
               .
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               The smaller plate will not hold as much as the bigger plate and makes it easier to control the stomach.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               After eating a meal,
               <em>
                wait
               </em>
               about 20 minutes before going for a second serving.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               This gives the time needed to allow the food you just ate to settle. If you need to, walk away from the table and find something to keep you busy in the 20 minute interim.
              </font>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div class="wsite-image wsite-image-border-none" style="padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;margin-right:0;text-align:left">
               <a>
                <img alt="Picture" src="/uploads/1/6/6/6/16669516/9892407_orig.jpg" style="width:100%;max-width:316px"/>
               </a>
               <div style="display:block;font-size:90%">
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Add Some Lean Protein In Your Diet
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <font color="#2A2A2A">
               You may not have noticed this but
               <strong>
                you will feel hungry for longer
               </strong>
               if you eat protein.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               I
               <u>
                would not
               </u>
               go as far as the Atkins Diet where most of what you eat is basically all animal protein.
               <span>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               This is for the reason that all animal protein sources lack the essential variety of nutrients.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <strong>
                Acceptable protein sources are
               </strong>
               ; salmon, tilapia, tuna, tofu, beans, and skin less chicken. Chicken breasts being a better part of the chicken to eat as the rest of the chicken tends to be more
               <em>
                fatty
               </em>
               .
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <font color="#8D2424">
                 <strong>
                  <span>
                  </span>
                 </strong>
                </font>
               </span>
               <font color="#8D2424">
                <strong>
                 Note
                </strong>
               </font>
               : if you are trying to lose weight 
               <u>
                <em>
                 <strong>
                  do not resort to protein supplements/shakes
                 </strong>
                </em>
               </u>
               .
              </font>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div class="wsite-image wsite-image-border-thin" style="padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;margin-right:0;text-align:left">
               <a>
                <img alt="Picture" src="/uploads/1/6/6/6/16669516/5804616_orig.jpg" style="width:100%;max-width:495px"/>
               </a>
               <div style="display:block;font-size:90%">
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Consume a Variety of Soup
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <font color="#2A2A2A">
               Believe it or not, soup can actually help
               <strong>
                prolong
               </strong>
               the feeling of hunger.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               This is because soup takes longer to digest than regular whole food.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                </span>
               </span>
               I wouldnt eat just soup but it can be a great idea to fit it in for lunch every day or every other day. The more
               <strong>
                thick
               </strong>
               you make the soup you make 
               <em>
                the better
               </em>
               .
              </font>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div class="wsite-image wsite-image-border-thin" style="padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;margin-right:0;text-align:left">
               <a>
                <img alt="Picture" src="/uploads/1/6/6/6/16669516/4214692_orig.jpg" style="width:100%;max-width:906px"/>
               </a>
               <div style="display:block;font-size:90%">
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Have a Smoothie For a Snack
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <font color="#2A2A2A">
               I don't believe in making
              </font>
              <font color="#2A2A2A">
               a diet out of only juices and smoothies. However, it would not be a bad way for a quick snack.
               <br/>
               <span>
                <br/>
                <span>
                 Not to mention that it can
                 <em>
                  prolong hunger.
                 </em>
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
              </font>
              <span>
               <br/>
               <span>
                <font color="#2A2A2A">
                 <strong>
                  Just like soup
                 </strong>
                 (blended vegetables with added water and spice), smoothies and home made juice could work in the same way.
                </font>
                <br/>
                <span>
                 <br/>
                 <span>
                  <font color="#24678D">
                   <strong>
                    Quick Tip:
                   </strong>
                  </font>
                  <font color="#2A2A2A">
                   Avoid the use of
                   <em>
                    dairy
                   </em>
                   in your smoothies if you choose to blend one.
                  </font>
                  <br/>
                  <span>
                   <br/>
                   <span>
                    <font color="#8D2424">
                     <em>
                      (dairy can be the one thing holding you back from losing weight =&gt;
                      <a href="http://www.russnagamori.com/1/post/2013/12/dairy-lactose-and-the-holy-grail-of-weight-loss.html" target="_blank">
                       Click Here
                      </a>
                      to find out how dairy is not necessary.)
                     </em>
                    </font>
                    <br/>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div class="wsite-image wsite-image-border-thin" style="padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;margin-right:0;text-align:left">
               <a>

             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Last But Not Least...
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <font color="#2A2A2A">
               <strong>
                Avoid processed sugar at all costs.
               </strong>
               <br/>
              </font>
              <span>
               <br/>
               <span>
                <font color="#2A2A2A">
                 If you have not noticed, we begin to feel
                 <em>
                  hungry
                 </em>
                 after eating
                 <em>
                  sugar
                 </em>
                 .
                 <br/>
                </font>
                <span>
                 <br/>
                 <span>
                  <font color="#2A2A2A">
                   This also includes; store bought fruit juices, cookies, bakery, candy, chocolate, etc.
                   <br/>
                  </font>
                  <span>
                   <br/>
                   <span>
                    <font color="#2A2A2A">
                     Even though dark chocolate is
                     <em>
                      supposed
                     </em>
                     to be good for you (in small amounts) it could make feeling hungry which could also lead to
                     <strong>
                      overeating
                     </strong>
                     .
                     <br/>
                    </font>
                    <span>
                     <br/>
                     <span>
                      <font color="#2A2A2A">
                       <font color="#8D2424">
                        <strong>
                         Tip
                        </strong>
                       </font>
                       : If you crave sugar -
                       <strong>
                        <em>
                         eat some whole fruit!
                        </em>
                       </strong>
                      </font>
                      <br/>
                      <span>
                       <br/>
                       <span>
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                    </span>
