     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Hello Kitty Roller Rescue
       </b>
      </i>
      <span style="font-weight: normal">
       (
       <span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">
        
       </span>
       <span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">
        ,
       </span>
       <i>
        Har Kiti no Pikopiko Daisakusen
       </i>
       <sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint">
        <a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets">
         <span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">
          ?
         </span>
        </a>
       </sup>
       , lit. "Hello Kitty's Picopico Mission")
      </span>
      is an
      <a href="/wiki/Action-adventure_game" title="Action-adventure game">
       action-adventure
      </a>
      -
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Racing_game" title="Racing game">
       racing
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">
       video game
      </a>
      developed by XPEC Entertainment. The game is based on the
      <a href="/wiki/Hello_Kitty" title="Hello Kitty">
       Hello Kitty
      </a>
      characters. The game also features exclusive character designs by Hunter Roberson and Rhett Deal.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Hello_Kitty_Crystal_Edition">
       Hello Kitty Crystal Edition
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Kitty:_Roller_Rescue&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Hello Kitty Crystal Edition">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <div class="hatnote">
      See also:
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Xbox_special_limited_editions" title="Xbox special limited editions">
       Xbox special limited editions
      </a>
     </div>
     <p>
      A Hello Kitty Crystal Xbox was released with
      <a href="/wiki/Sanrio" title="Sanrio">
       Sanrio
      </a>
      in
      <a href="/wiki/Singapore" title="Singapore">
       Singapore
      </a>
      , to commemorate the release of the game on the Xbox. The special edition console was translucent with a pink and orange
      <a href="/wiki/Hello_Kitty" title="Hello Kitty">
       Hello Kitty
      </a>
      picture covering the X on top of the case. A limited production run of 550 units was sold at a retail price of S$99 (US$61), if you purchased certain selected Samsung LCD TVs during a promotion. Included with the Hello Kitty Crystal console was a matching Crystal Controller S and a copy of
      <i>
       Hello Kitty Mission Rescue
      </i>
      .
     </p>
