      <header class="entry-header wrapper">
       <div class="entry-meta">

       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         Prioritizing Passion: Day 7
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
       <img alt="Stacy Eden - Prioritizing Passion Day 7" class="attachment-big-size wp-post-image" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.valleyhype.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Stacy-Eden-Prioritizing-Passion-Day-7.jpg?resize=922%2C639"/>
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8">
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <div class="bottomcontainerBox" style="">
           <div style="float:left; width:90px;padding-right:10px; margin:4px 4px 4px 4px;height:30px;">
            <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="http://www.facebook.com/plugins/like.php?href=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.valleyhype.com%2Fprioritizing-passion-day-7%2F&amp;layout=button_count&amp;show_faces=false&amp;width=90&amp;action=like&amp;font=verdana&amp;colorscheme=light&amp;height=21" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; width:90px; height:21px;">
            </iframe>
           </div>
           <div style="float:left; width:75px;padding-right:10px; margin:4px 4px 4px 4px;height:30px;">
            <g:plusone href="http://www.valleyhype.com/prioritizing-passion-day-7/" size="medium">
            </g:plusone>
           </div>
           <div style="float:left; width:85px;padding-right:10px; margin:4px 4px 4px 4px;height:30px;">
            <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="horizontal" data-text="Prioritizing Passion: Day 7" data-url="http://www.valleyhype.com/prioritizing-passion-day-7/" data-via="valleyhype" href="http://twitter.com/share">
            </a>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div style="clear:both">
          </div>
          <div style="padding-bottom:4px;">
          </div>
          <p>
           I am writing this in 30 minutes. Thats it. Ten minutes for editing and twenty for writing. No brainstorming needed because today
           <a href="http://www.valleyhype.com/?s=Prioritizing+Passion">
            <em>
             Prioritizing Passion
            </em>
           </a>
           is taking on the theme of 30 minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Because its incredible what you can accomplish in 30 minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its also amazing what you can stand to do for 30 minutes. Just stay at this boring party for 30 minutes. Just babysit your bratty cousin for 30 minutes. Just work on that huge looming monsterous project that gets more complicated with each layer you pull back on it for 30 minutes. Then its on to the next thing. Which cant be worse than your bratty cousin.
          </p>
          <p>
           A huge part of my job as CEO and Creative Director at
           <a href="http://www.valleyhype.com/?s=clutch+jewelry">
            Clutch Jewelry
           </a>
           is social media. Even just two years ago I could not have cared less about this stuff. Now I couldnt be a bigger fan. Instagram has connected me with huge name designers, LA models, photographers, and most importantly commercial buyers. Twitter opens the dialogue with national publications and is every musicians favorite social media outlet. Most importantly they all allow me to connect with customers on personal level.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its about sales. And its also about relationships. That is, ultimately, why I do what I do. Im here to enrich the world with art. And nothing could be a better example of that than a happy Clutch customer. Or as I like to call them, Game Shifters.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ok, enough gushing about social media. Yes its awesome and its free. But its also a time suck and can be used and often is used for mind numbing (or worse; ex-stalking) purposes.
          </p>
          <p>
           So 30 minutes of social media is all Ive allotted for the day on my
           <em>
            Default Diary
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Yep, its killing me. Ill probably bump it up to an hour once Phoenix Fashion Week is over and I can breath again (for a week until LA Fashion Week of course). But its part of the
           <em>
            Default Diary
           </em>
           , so for now Im sticking to it. Its 30 minutes I will have saved and be spending on practicing the harmonica or making Clutch Crowns or taking the dog for a walk.
          </p>
          <p>
           That might not make me as popular. My Klout score may go down. But seven days into following my
           <em>
            Default Diary
           </em>
           to a fault has me feeling like every moment counts. I only have 30 minutes, so Im going to get down to business. Time is money. Its valuable. And Im starting to spend it more wisely.
          </p>
          <p>
           And now my 30 minutes is up, so I leave you with a quote by Sharon Lechter.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            Dont try to be successful. Every day, when you wake up, try to be the person that deserves the wildest success you can imagine.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
