       <h2>
        Mercedes Unveil New Compact Car, The CLA Shooting Brake
       </h2>
      </div>
      <div class="single-content clearfix">

       <p>
        Mercedes-Benz have disclosed details on their fourth generation compact car the CLA Shooting Brake AMG. What in essence is a wagon version of the Mercedes CLA sedan, the new CLA Shooting Brake will be a more high-performance vehicle than the model it is based from. Buyers can look forward to additional room inside the car.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.thecarfansite.com/wp-content/uploads/mercedescla21.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2028">
         <img alt="mercedescla2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2028" height="406" src="http://www.thecarfansite.com/wp-content/uploads/mercedescla21.jpg" width="750"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        From increased headroom and more cargo space in the back, the new CLA will appeal to speed freaks and large families alike. Both diesel and petrol alternatives will be available at launch, with the most powerful of the lot being the 2.0 litre turbocharged engine with 355 hp, and with Mercedes unique 4Matic all-wheel drive system which will come standard on all models. The CLA45 AMG model will be available as the OrangeArt Edition, with orange around the radiator grille, rear mirrors and apron trim, also with chrome tailpipes giving the car a sleek and sophisticated overall finish.
       </p>
