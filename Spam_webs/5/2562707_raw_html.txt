       <h1 class="heading">
        Your Annual Black Friday Shopping Survival Kit  Handled!
       </h1>
       <p class="post-byline">
        By
        <span class="post-author">
         <a href="http://mblog.macys.com/author/jovel-phillips/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jovel Phillips">
          Jovel Phillips
         </a>

        </time>
       </p>

      <figure class="feature">
       <img alt="main" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" height="575" src="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/11/main_23.jpg" width="745"/>
       <figcaption>

      <p>
       Were just days away from
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.macys.com/campaign/social?campaign_id=572&amp;channel_id=1">
         Black Friday
        </a>
       </strong>
       and our minds are in total game mode! After all, its the one time of the year when shopping becomes a full-on sport. But before you even think of leaving the house, take a look at our trusted survival kit to make sure youre in it to win it all day long.
      </p>
      <p>
       Whether youre ready to go when the doors open on Thursday night or opt to wait til dawn the next day, you can bet on chilly conditions outside. Rather than being weighed down by a heavy coat, try a quilted zip-up jacket. Its insulated enough to keep you warm, but wont slow you down once you get inside and start shopping.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/11/image1_15.jpg">
        <img alt="image1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10134" height="750" src="http://iot.macy.s3.amazonaws.com/wp_media_content/2014/11/image1_15.jpg" width="558"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        The Extra Defense
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Long lines are a given on Black Friday. As you anxiously wait for the doors to open, a cozy infinity scarf and leather gloves will help the seconds pass much more comfortably. More important, the less time you spend concentrating on the cold, the more focus youll have for mapping out the most strategic routes through the store.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        The Shoes
       </strong>
       <br/>
       High heels? Leave them at home. Youll need a pair of kicks that can help you keep up with the pace. Wedged sneakers will give your pups some comfort as they carry you (and all those goods) from the racks to the register.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        The Wristlet Wallet
       </strong>
       <br/>
       To get the most out of the day, youve got to be able to slide past the competition with ease. Bumping into other hungry shoppers with a huge tote bag is a foul waiting to happen. Instead, a cute wristlet is great for holding on to the essentials  credit cards, coupons, cell. This DKNY convertible style doubles as a crossbody, a helpful fix when you have your hands full.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        The Juice
       </strong>
       <br/>
       This year, your smartphone plays a vital role in your shopping success. It holds your mobile wishlist (who does paper anymore?), serves as a stopwatch for those awesome in-store specials, and is your Plan B when someone snatches that last cashmere sweater from the shelf (*cough, cough* macys.com!). You cant afford to let it die in the middle of the madness. A portable charger is non-negotiable.
      </p>
