           <b>
            Alexi Torres
           </b>
          </a>
          has a unique technique of
          <b>
           oil painting
          </b>
          . His works are mainly feautring leaves, feathers and other organic materials and his subjects appear to be knitted or woven. Below you will see his series of oil paintings on canvas;
          <a href="http://www.alexitorres.com/P-We-buy-G-S.htm" target="_blank">
           <b>
            We buy Gold and Silver
           </b>
          </a>
          , showcasing well-known artists on the face of silver or gold coins. Make sure to check out his website to see his other works too.
