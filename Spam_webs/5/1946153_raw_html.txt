     <h1 class="detail_title">
      How to Make a Colorful Watch Pillow with Turquoise Beads
     </h1>
     <p class="detail_subtitle">
      Summary: This time come with me to make a colorful watch pillow with turquoise beads for your beloved watch. Keep reading to learn how to make it step by step!
     </p>
     <div class="Art_detail_banner">
      <img alt="How to Make a Colorful Watch Pillow with Turquoise Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/article_main_image/1561/ba3af061-55c8-4fb4-900d-a653067f3883-680-330.jpg" title="How to Make a Colorful Watch Pillow with Turquoise Beads"/>
     </div>
     <div id="articleProductList">
     </div>
     <div class="art_detail_text">
      <div class="art_detail_content">
       <p>
        A watch pillow is a great choice to store your watch when it is not being worn. A handmade watch pillow is obviously better than a store-bought one. To make a personalized watch pillow, the first step is to choose a piece of cloth you like, then prepare a soft material like quilting. Next, you should have some beautiful embellishments; I used colorful turquoise beads here. Here we go!
       </p>
       <h2>
        Materials and tools for making a colorful watch pillow:
       </h2>
       <p>
        12mm
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/beads/wholesale-Turquoise/0-286-1.html">
         Turquoise
        </a>
        Beads Strand, Flat Round, Mixed Color
        <br/>
        Cotton Cloth, Flower, Purple
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/beads/wholesale-Sewing-Thread-Cord/0-464-1.html">
         Sewing Thread
        </a>
        <br/>
        Quilting
        <br/>
        Needle
        <br/>
        Scissors
        <br/>
        Hot Glue Gun (Not Shown)
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="materials and tools for making a colorful watch pillow" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/f1629925-575e-4169-ac42-1711b57127f8.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h2>
        How to make a colorful watch pillow with turquoise beads?
       </h2>
       <h3>
        Step 1: make a watch pillow case and attach beads
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, cut out a piece of rectangular cloth which is fit for your watchband and fold it in half with wrong side out. Running stitch any two openings perpendicular with each other;
        <br/>
        2nd, turn the cloth inside out through the remaining opening;
        <br/>
        3rd, you will see the watch pillow case in an early form;
        <br/>
        4th, glue or sew three turquoise beads at each corner of the pillow case. Remember to leave seam allowance at the opening side.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="make a watch pillow case and attach beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/acf6ff5a-d92f-49ed-91fe-64892eeb8c4c.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Step 2: stuff the pillow case
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, stuff moderate quilting into the pillow case;
        <br/>
        2nd, sew the opening with hidden stitch.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="stuff the pillow case" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/bc7d5efb-2ea8-4402-a83e-5e822e45bba7.jpg" style="height:200px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Hope you like it!
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="finished colorful watch pillow" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/66ee5fbe-b7c8-4b9c-a974-bfe5e298f6b0.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Now youve learnt how to make a watch pillow, its time to give your watch a home!
       </p>
