       <p>
        The Eureka Springs School of the Arts (ESSA) has established the vision of being a premier art school in Mid-America. We feel that we are poised to develop that potential due to our rich history, gifted leadership, and the support of the community. We have created achievable goals that move us towards that vision in a concise and clear manner. By thinking about ESSA as an internationally known art school, we feel that we are positioning ourselves to create a rich and vital art education experience for all those that participate.

     <h1>
      Acrylic Painting
      <br/>
      May 29-June 1
     </h1>
     <h2>
      Denise Ryan
     </h2>
     <p>
      All levels
      <br/>
      Studio
      <br/>
      4-Day Workshop (9am-4pm)
      <br/>
      Tuition: $220
      <br/>
      Materials: Instructor List will be provided
     </p>
     <p>
      Students will be introduced to three basic techniques in the use of acrylic paint. The workshop will cover realism (2 days), abstraction(one day), and painting with a knife(one day) with the intent of providing the student with a foundation to further their education in painting.
     </p>
     <p>
      Denise Ryan (Eureka Springs, AR) has a Master's degree in Fine Art with a major in Painting and a minor in Art History She has been painting for forty years and loves to share what she has learned.
      <a href="http://www.deniseryanart.com" target="_blank">
       www.deniseryanart.com
