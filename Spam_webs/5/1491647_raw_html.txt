      <h4>
       <font face="Verdana" size="2">
        <br/>
       </font>
       <b>
        <font color="#800080" face="Verdana" size="4">
         Mini Mochi Flower Garden 
		Shawl -
         <br/>
        </font>
        <font color="#800080" face="Verdana" size="2">
         granny 
		style motif crochet
        </font>
       </b>
      </h4>
     </blockquote>
     <font face="Verdana">
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Your Granny Squares never looked so good with so little 
effort! The color changes are built into the yarn!
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Materials:
        <font size="2">
         <br/>
         Yarn:
        </font>
       </b>
       <font size="2">
       </font>
       <b>
        <a href="../../yarns/mini-mochi-balls.html">
         <font size="2">
          Crystal Palace 
Mini-Mochi
         </font>
        </a>
       </b>
       <font size="2">
        ,
        <b>
         8 - 50 gr. balls
        </b>
        :
        <br/>
        model shown used 4 different colors: 3 balls #104 fern rainbow, 2 balls #101 
"intense rainbow", 2 balls #102 "flame rainbow" &amp; 1 ball #106 "strawberry-lime" 
(or make the shawl in all one color, it'll still have lots of variation in 
colors)
        <b>
         <br/>
        </b>
        <a href="../../yarns/mini-mochi-balls2.html">
         See the newest 8 colors of Mini 
Mochi here.
        </a>
        <b>
         <br/>
         Crochet Hook
        </b>
        size F
        <br/>
        <a href="MochiPlus-CrochetGardenWrap.html">
         [See a
         <b>
          Mochi Plus
         </b>
         version of 
this Shawl using an H hook - Here]
        </a>
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Dimensions:
       </b>
       <font size="2">
        48" along outside edge, 18" at front edges. 
Motif 4" square
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Flower Garden Motif
        <font size="2">
        </font>
       </b>
       <font size="2">
        (make 19 in #104 "fern 
rainbow" for garden border, 45 in other 3 flower shaded colors)
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 6, join with sl st to form a ring. Ch 3 (counts as a dc), 
15 dc in ring. Join with sl st to top of beg ch 3 (16 dc).
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 1, sc in same st, (ch 7, skip 3 dc, sc in next dc) three 
times, ch 7, join with sl st to beg sc.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 1, in next ch-7 space make 2 sc, 2 hdc, 2 dc, 3 trc, 2 dc, 
2 hdc, 2 sc (petal made),
        <br/>
        ch 1, make petals in next 3 ch-7 loops in same manner. Join with sl st in beg ch 
1.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 6, skip 2 sc and 1 hdc, sc in next hdc, ch 3, skip next 2dc 
and trc, sc in center trc,
        <br/>
        Ch 3, skip trc and 2 dc, sc in next hdc, ch 3, dc in ch-1 space between petals.
        <br/>
        Continue in this manner around, ending join with sl st in 3rd ch of beg ch 6.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 3, 3 dc in each ch-3 space, dc in next sc, 3 dc in next 
ch-3 space, (dc, ch 2, dc) in sc at center of petal. Continue in this manner 
around. Join with sl st to top of beg ch 3.
        <br/>
        Fasten off.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Join motifs as shown in diagram by placing right sides 
together and slip stitching thru back loops only of both motifs, using #104 
"fern rainbow" 
along outside edge for border.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        <b>
         Finishing:
        </b>
        Join #104 "fern rainbow" with sl st to corner space at top of 
left edge (see drawing). Ch 3, dc in same space. Dc in each dc around, working in 
back loops only, making (2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc) in corners. At center neck, dc to 
corner, skip corner spaces and dc in next dc (decrease made).
        <br/>
        End 2 dc in last corner, join with hdc to top of beg ch 3.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 3, dc in each dc around 4 sides only, working (2 dc, ch 2, 
2 dc) in corner spaces.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font size="2">
        Ch 1, do not turn. Work 1 row reverse sc around, working 2 
reverse sc n corner spaces.
        <br clear="all"/>
        <br clear="all"/>
       </font>
      </p>
