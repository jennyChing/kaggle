               <div class="field-items">
                <div class="field-item even">
                 <p>
                  The weather is getting cooler, the days are shorter and classes have officially begun. Whether we want to or not, its time to transition from sandals and brightly-colored outfits to items that are a little more practical for a blustery October day. Swap out those sandals for something that will actually take you through the seasons; a cute pair of boots or a to-die-for tote are all you need to transition your summer wardrobe to fit fall fashions! Adding the perfect transitional accessories to your closet is a great way to make any outfit look chic and timeless, regardless of your personal style!
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/VJybh3">
                   <img alt="" class="media-image" height="420" src="http://www.hercampus.com/sites/default/files/styles/full_width_embed/public/2014/09/05/Hero_3_phillip_riding.jpg?itok=A_WhUN7Q" width="620"/>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Whether youre coveting a classic leather bag or a pair of winter-ready (but still on-trend) boots for harsh New England winters,
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/VJybh3">
                   FRYEs
                  </a>
                  <strong>
                  </strong>
                  uncompromising qualityand luxurious materials make it easy to find that perfect leather pick to transition you to your fall wardrobe. Rock them with sundresses or shorts
                  <em>
                   and
                  </em>
                  your coziest sweaters (when the time comes).
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   If youre looking for something classic:
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/1lklMMY">
                   <img alt="" class="media-image" height="574" src="http://www.hercampus.com/sites/default/files/styles/full_width_embed/public/2014/09/05/76247%20HARNESS%206_%20gau.jpg?itok=7FCIk6r-" width="620"/>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Try the
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/1lklMMY">
                   Harness 6
                  </a>
                  ($258) boot from FRYE. These boots are a shorter twist on a favorite and perfect for the cooler fall weather. A must-have for any Northeastern collegiette, the Harness 6 boot is durable enough for running around campus and comfy enough for long weekend walks in the city. These would work perfectly with dark-washed skinny jeans and a comfy oversized sweater!
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   If youre looking for something casual:
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/1wfujFx">
                   <img alt="" class="media-image" height="1013" src="http://www.hercampus.com/sites/default/files/styles/full_width_embed/public/2014/09/05/DB660%20STITCH%20TOTE%20bud.jpg?itok=j8acRy7D" width="620"/>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Made for the busy collegiette, the
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/1wfujFx">
                   FRYE Stitch Tote
                  </a>
                  ($288) is the essential carryall bag for fall! Whether youre toting around your laptop, books or favorite weekend purchases, the Stitch Tote has space for everything the collegiette on the go would want to carry. Versatile and classic, you can pair the Stitch Tote with just about anything, from your internship outfit to your favorite pair of skinnies. The best part? It comes in
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/1wfujFx">
                   seven different colors
                  </a>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   If youre looking for something durable:
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/XE2Zld">
                   <img alt="" class="media-image" height="583" src="http://www.hercampus.com/sites/default/files/styles/full_width_embed/public/2014/09/05/77590%20SABRINA%206G%20LACE%20UP%20sdl.jpg?itok=Z365AMfa" width="620"/>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Who said rugged boots cant be stylish? The
                  <a href="http://bit.ly/XE2Zld">
                   Sabrina 6G Lace Up
