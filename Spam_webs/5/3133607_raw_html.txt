        <p>
         When you fall madly in love, you look back at your life and wonder why did you concern yourself with all those other guys that were just not right for you.
         <br/>
         Do you remember the conversation with your girlfriends saying Can you please just get rid of him? He is ugly, useless, using you, cheating on you, making you miserable, and you will find someone better., but you never did?
         <br/>
         You think Why did it take me so long to figure this one out?
         <br/>
         Love can actually be fun, love is actually ease and love is sticking together no matter what life throws at you because you want to be there with him.
         <br/>
         This is what love feels like..
        </p>
        <h3>
         1. Natural ease and fun element
        </h3>
        <p>
         When you are in the right relationship for you there is a natural ease between you and him. There is an air of confidence inside of you that this just feels right. That this time everything will work out, it is a sort of knowing, kind of like listening to your inner guides and they are all peaceful and happy.
         <br/>
         There are no major issues in your relationship that need to be solved or addressed. They seem to just naturally untangle themselves as your relationship moves along.
         <br/>
         I had a friend who was in her mid-thirties when she got with her boyfriend. He had been married before and she was slightly concerned he would never marry again, but his behavior always put her at ease.
         <br/>
         Christmas 2013 he asked her to marry him, she said yes! When youre falling in love the obstacles have a tendency to disappear. Your relationship has a natural, fun dynamic. Now my friend is pregnant with her first child on the way.
        </p>
        <h3>
         2. Obstacles have brought you closer together
        </h3>
        <p>
         Maybe when you started dating you both where seeing other people? They disappeared off the scene. Maybe you stay at his house very regularly, now youre practically moved in, but nobody has a problem. You have your first fight and then you laugh about how silly and unnecessary it all was and the conversation after the fight leads to setting up some boundaries and ground rule, a compromise.
         <br/>
         When you are in love obstacles are there to test the strength of your relationship all these obstacles either bring you closer together or further apart. If your relationship gets stronger, you can be sure you are in the right place.
        </p>
        <h3>
         3. Hes not perfect
        </h3>
        <p>
         As the relationship evolves you start to realize that your god-like boyfriend is still cute, funny and has you wrapped around his little finger. But you also notice he is not perfect, he as his weird habits and unusual habits, you dont love all his funny habits but they dont drive you crazy either. You just laugh and smile and realize this comes with the territory and that is okay.
        </p>
        <h3>
         4. There is not a single doubt in your mind
        </h3>
        <p>
         If your doubt, your doubting for a reason. The number one thing people doubt and overlook is attraction. Attraction gets confusing, there is so much mixed advice out there. Let me put it in simple form. You need a good guy with an edge; you need a good guy you are attracted too.
         <br/>
         When your head and heart is peaceful because you have no doubts in the world you are indeed in the right relationship for you and are falling madly in love.
        </p>
        <h3>
         5. He keeps passing the test
        </h3>
        <p>
         In relationship there are a lot of obstacles to get through, he needs to past the test. One day youre talking on the sofa while watching a DVD and you talk about your favorite white dress, what your dream house would look like and how many kids. The conversation does not faze him, he happily joins in your girly conversation and talks about how he would love the same.
        </p>
        <h3>
         6. You dont seek others approval
        </h3>
        <p>
         Everybody friend you dont like, you always need a second opinion. You rush them over to meet your friends, especially your best friend and if he doesnt pass the test or you need major persuasion to like him. That is not your guy!
         <br/>
         But when you fall madly in love you dont need your friends approval. You already know he is the right guy for you instead when you call your friend she hears and sees that your happy and cant wait to meet him when you a spare moment to interact in each other busy life.
         <br/>
         When you are in love you dont need to ask, you dont need to question it. You just know. It is always good to read about the destination before you get there so you get an idea of what real love really looks like.
        </p>
        <h3>
         7. Saying I love you does not feel forced
        </h3>
        <p>
         Think back to all your previous relationships, do you remember the time you told a guy you loved him because he said it to you and in the back of your mind you were thinking how am I going to get out of this mess. A classic mistake we make and I have certainly done this in the past is you are with a guy who is a good person and you are hoping the attraction will show up later. It doesnt!
         <br/>
         When you are madly in love saying I love you feels right and feels congruent because youre thoughts, your words, and your actions are all in harmony. You are with the right guy because saying I love you feels right, feels good and with an air of confidence you know he feels the same.
         <br/>
         There is no doubt, just a knowing, you dont need others approval because you trust your own, he keeps passing the test because he just gets you. You cant imagine your life without him because reverting back to the old ways seems hilarious.
         <br/>
         My dear if you tick all these boxes you are madly in love with the right guy for you I would know I am living it and so can you. Dont settle for anything less.
        </p>
