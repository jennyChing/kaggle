         <strong>
          No Churn Pumpkin Pie Ice Cream recipe
         </strong>
         with you.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pumpkin pie ice cream ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-66747" height="333" src="http://savingdollarsandsense.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/pumpkin-pie-ice-cream-ingredients.jpg" width="500"/>
         <strong>
          INGREDIENTS
         </strong>
         <br/>
         8 oz Heavy Whipping cream
         <br/>
         14 oz Sweeteend Condensed Milk
         <br/>
         3/4 cup Pumpkin Puree
         <br/>
         1 tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
         <br/>
         1/2 tsp Vanilla.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <img alt="pumpkin pie ice cream process" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-66746" height="333" src="http://savingdollarsandsense.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/pumpkin-pie-ice-cream-process.jpg" width="500"/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          DIRECTIONS
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
         <br/>
         Gradually add condensed milk while continuing to beat.
         <br/>
         Stir in the pumpkin, vanilla and spice.
         <br/>
         Freeze for at least 4 hours.
         <br/>
        </p>
