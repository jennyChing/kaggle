      <p>
       When Harry met Sally, the two constantly quarreled. But when Prince Harry met Missy Franklin, she got a royal treat  her own birthday serenade.
      </p>
      <p>
       The British prince joined in a rousing chorus of "Happy Birthday" on Friday evening, wishing the Olympic swimming champion a happy 18th birthday at a private golf club south of Denver.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341292!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537179.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341292.1368289054!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537179.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Franklin, who just celebrated her 18th birthday, said being serenaded by the British prince was the perfect conclusion to her birthday festivities
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       The veteran combat helicopter pilot and third in line to the British throne is in the Centennial State to meet with wounded soldiers who are competing in the Paralympic-style Warrior Games.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341298!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537177.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image v">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341298.1368289079!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537177.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Franklin reacts as the British Ambassador to the U.S. Sir Peter Westmacott and Britain's Prince Harry sing happy birthday to her.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       The ceremonies began Saturday evening at the Olympic Training Center in Colorado Springs. While at the games, Prince Harry sat in a circle of 12 sitting volleyball team members, batting the ball around amid whoops and laughter.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341296!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537183.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341296.1368289072!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537183.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Franklin, center, later wrote on her Instagram page that it was an 'honor' to meet Prince Harry, left.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       Franklin called meeting the grinning prince "the perfect way to end my 18th birthday," saying on her Instagram page that it was "an honor meeting him."
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341420!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168556745.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="Prince Harry congratulates LCPL Maurillia Simpson at the Warrior Games in Colorado Springs, Colorado." src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341420.1368305808!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168556745.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Arthur Edwards/Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Prince Harry congratulates LCPL Maurillia Simpson at the Warrior Games in Colorado Springs, Colorado.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       The swimmer wowed during last year's London Olympics after winning four gold medals and a bronze at the age of 17, skyrocketing her to international acclaim.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341421!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168556764.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="Prince Harry plays sitting volleyball with British soldiers during the third day of his visit to the United States." src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341421.1368305130!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168556764.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Arthur Edwards/Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Prince Harry plays sitting volleyball with British soldiers during the third day of his visit to the United States.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       But Harry charmed more than just the Olympic champion, sharing his charisma with dignitaries, students, military personnel, and British expatriates at the posh clubhouse Sanctuary, a private golf course.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341422!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168556797.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image v">
        <img alt="Prince Harry meets injured British soldiers as he attends the Paralympic-style Warrior Games." src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341422.1368305181!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168556797.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Arthur Edwards/Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Prince Harry meets injured British soldiers as he attends the Paralympic-style Warrior Games.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       He also met with Paralympic champion Jessica Long, who has a collected 12 gold medals from the past three games. She told London's
       <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/prince-harry/10050766/Prince-Harry-raises-a-glass-to-birthday-girl-Missy-Franklin.html">
        Telegraph
       </a>
       newspaper: "My London gold is now my favorite medal because he touched it."
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341290!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/usa-britain.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="Harry worked the room at a reception at the Sanctuary Golf Course in Sedalia, on Friday night, charming Colorado officials." src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341290.1368289046!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/usa-britain.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         POOL/REUTERS
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Harry worked the room at a reception at the Sanctuary Golf Course in Sedalia, on Friday night, charming Colorado officials.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       Colorado politicians like Gov. John Hickenlooper, Sen. Michael Bennet, and Denver Mayor Michael Hancock were all in attendance, according to the
       <a href="http://www.denverpost.com/breakingnews/ci_23220057/prince-harry-arrives-colorado-feted-at-sanctuary-reception">
        Denver Post
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341302!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537186.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341302.1368289095!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168537186.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Harry (right) talks with piano player Jon Parker at the reception Friday night.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       The prince is on a seven-day tour of the U.S., which started with a visit to Washington, where he met with first lady Michelle Obama to discuss one of his patronages, an anti-landmine organization that was also supported by his mother, the late Princess Diana.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341288!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168503991.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341288.1368289039!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168503991.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Earlier Friday, Harry visited a medical center in Bethesda, Maryland treating soldiers wounded in battle; he is pictured speaking with Staff Sgt. Timothy Payne (right), who lost his legs in an IED explosion in Afghanistan.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       While in the nation's capital, Harry also visited Arlington National Cemetery and honored the American and foreign dead by placing a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341287!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168506201.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341287.1368289033!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168506201.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Harry poses with a wounded soldier during his visit to the Military Advanced Training Center at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center treating wounded soldiers undergoing physical therapy on May 10 in Bethesda, Maryland.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       He also placed a wreath in Section 60  where troops from the recent conflicts in Afghanistan and Iraq are buried. The tombstone Harry chose, which was selected at random, was for Army Specialist Michael Stansbery Jr., 21, who was killed by an IED in Afghanistan in 2010 while on foot patrol.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341289!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168526702.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341289.1368289042!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168526702.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         According to a statement from St. James Palace in London, Harry is attending the Warrior Games in part because he believes those wounded in combat deserve recognition.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       According to a statement from St. James' Palace in London, Harry is attending the Warrior Games in part because he believes those wounded in combat deserve recognition.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341285!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168500945.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
       <figure class="a-image h">
        <img alt="" src="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1341285.1368289025!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_970/168500945.jpg"/>
        <span class="a-credit" itemprop="copyrightHolder">
         Pool/Getty Images
        </span>
        <h2 class="a-caption" itemprop="description">
         Also on Friday, Harry placed a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknowns during the second day of his visit to the U.S. at Arlington National Cemetery.
        </h2>
        <a class="a-enlarge" href="#">
        </a>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <p>
       "He has said before how humbled he feels by the extraordinary courage and fortitude shown by those servicemen and women who have made huge sacrifices for their country, and to whom we all owe a considerable debt of gratitude," the statement read. St. James Palace serves as the official residence of the British Royal Family.
      </p>
      <p>
       The prince returned from Afghanistan in January following a 20-week deployment where he served as a co-pilot and gunner on an Apache helicopter.
      </p>
      <p>
       So far, the prince has remained devoted strictly to his royal duties. But during his last visit to the U.S. in 2012, the bachelor caused an international stir when he was photographed frolicking naked around a Las Vegas hotel with an unidentified female.
      </p>
      <p>
       Harry said after the incident: "It was probably a classic example of me probably being too much army, and not enough prince."
      </p>
      <p>
       His current seven-day tour comes as the 87-year-old Queen Elizabeth II is slowly scaling back public appearances and royal duties, handing them down to Harry's father, Prince Charles, and of course, Harry himself.
      </p>
      <p>
       Later in his visit, the prince will travel to parts of New Jersey damaged in last October's Superstorm Sandy, swing by New York City, and conclude his trip by competing in the Sentebale Polo Cup match in nearby Greenwich, Conn.
      </p>
