                Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) is one of the largest state-owned steel making company based in New Delhi, India and one of the top steel makers in world. SAIL traces its origin to the Hindustan Steel Limited (HSL) which was set up on 19 January 1954. The registered office was originally in New Delhi. It moved to Calcutta in July 1956, and ultimately to Ranchi in December 1959.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                SAIL operates and owns 5 integrated steel plants at Rourkela, Bhilai, Durgapur, Bokaro and Burnpur and 3 special steel plants at Salem, Durgapur and Bhadravathi. SAIL is a public sector company, owned and operated by the Government of India and Incorporated on 24 January 1973 SAIL is one of India's fastest growing Public Sector Units.
