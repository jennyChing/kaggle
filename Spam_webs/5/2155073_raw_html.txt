       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         DIY Silhouette Ornament with Glitter
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-27T01:52:02+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 27, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Becca
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/diy-silhouette-ornament-glitter/#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6696466" data-link="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/diy-silhouette-ornament-glitter/" data-summary="" data-title="DIY Silhouette Ornament with Glitter">
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          There are affiliate links in this post.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="DIY Glitter Ornament using your Silhouette" class="aligncenter wp-image-17630" height="650" src="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/diy-silhouette-ornament-1-682x1024.jpg" width="433"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love how easy it is to customize thisDIY Silhouette ornament! Using aSilhouette, you can createquotes and personalize these ornaments for each of your friends and family.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Supplies Needed:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=silhouette%20black%20vinyl&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;tag=oucrbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps&amp;linkId=GYUP4DVDS4AQSC5J" target="_blank">
           Black Vinyl
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/mn/search/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=silhouette%20black%20vinyl&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;tag=oucrbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps&amp;linkId=K4VUJ33KJXAMPLM6" target="_blank">
           Contact Transfer Paper
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=clear%20ornament&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=i%3Aaps%2Ck%3Aclear%20ornament&amp;tag=oucrbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps&amp;linkId=7V3AMA6O7UD7H5NW" target="_blank">
           Clear Ornament
          </a>
          (Glass or plastic)
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=gold%20glitter&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=i%3Aaps%2Ck%3Agold%20glitter&amp;tag=oucrbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps&amp;linkId=FMLNKPMUURYGPVXD" target="_blank">
           Gold Glitter
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00H4PC8Y0/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00H4PC8Y0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=oucrbo-20&amp;linkId=XACNZF2OYYV5CTSN" target="_blank">
           Pledge Floor Cleaner with Shine
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Glitter Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <div class="code-block code-block-1" style="margin:8px 0px;">
         <center>
          <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
          </script>
          <!-- Mid-Post 300x250 -->
          <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-0500417073067646" data-ad-slot="3036623673" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
          </ins>
          <script>
           (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
          </script>
         </center>
        </div>
        <p>
         1. Remove the cap from your ornament and pour the Pledge solution into the center of the ornament. A little solution goes a long wayyou dont need much. Slowly swirlthe solution around the inside of the ornament until well coated, then place the extrasolution back into the Pledge bottle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="DIY Silhouette Ornament with Glitter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17624" height="432" src="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Gold-Glitter2-1.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         2.Pour a generous amount of glitter into the center of your ornament and swirl it around slowly to ensure youre covering the entire inside of the ornament with glitter. I used a funnel to pour glitter into myornament. Once its well coated, pour the excess glitter back into the jar.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Allow the ornament to dry for a few minutes andcarefully apply the cap and clip back inside the ornament.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="DIY Silhouette Ornament with Glitter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17627" height="500" src="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Gold-Glitter4-1.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Silhouette Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Head over to the Silhouette Design Studio and typeup whatever saying youd like on your ornament. If youre making the ornament for a friend, consider
         <a href="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/sharpie-and-glitter-coffee-mug-gift/" target="_blank">
          checking out their Pinterest board for some of their favorite quotes
         </a>
         . We love the movie Elf, so I wanted his quote Smilings my favorite. Once you choose your favorite font(I used the font Digs my Hart which can be downloaded for free
         <a href="http://www.kevinandamanda.com/fonts/freescrapbookfonts/digs-my-hart/" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         ),set the sizeto 1.993 x 2.892.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Load your black vinyl onto theSilhouette cutting mat and feed it into your machine. In the cut settings make sure your selected material is vinyl and your blade in the machine is set to a 2. Click Send to silhouette and let the cut begin.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once the cut is completed, remove it from your Silhouette machine and weed out the excess vinyl.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Youll have toapply contact transfer paper over yourquote soit can beapplied to the ornament. Once its transferred, eyeball the placement and firmly press it down onto the ornament.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Finished DIY Silhouette Ornament with Glitter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17628" height="433" src="http://mycrazygoodlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/FinishedOrnament-1.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This is a fun and easy project that can be done in batch and given as gifts this holiday season!
        </p>
