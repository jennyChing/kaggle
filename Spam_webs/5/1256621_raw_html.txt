      <h1>
       Go Retro with a Karaoke Party this Summer!
      </h1>
      <div class="entry">
       <p class="postmetadata-postdate">
        <small>
         July 11, 2014
        </small>
       </p>
       <p>
        With so many of these long summer days ahead of us, youre going to need some ideas about how to fill your time. Many of your friends and family are on vacation and looking for entertainment; why not give it to them? Throwing a retro-themed karaoke party with the help of Golden Productions is the best way to put some sizzling fun back into your summer!
       </p>
       <p>
        The first step to throwing a retro karaoke party is picking an era of music to feature. Do you want your guests crooning to 1950s hits, rocking out to the best songs of the 70s, or breaking out the hairspray and crimping iron to prepare for a night of 80s power ballads? Think of your guests and what some of their favorite songs are; sometimes people are a little bit shy about getting up on stage and singing, so a familiar tune might make it easier for them to banish their nerves and access their inner pop idol!
       </p>
       <p>
        Another benefit of a retro karaoke party is that the songs played are probably ones that everyone already knows. People wont waste time flubbing over lyrics and squinting at the screen; theyll be free to belt it out like theyre on Broadway and have a fantastic time doing it!
       </p>
       <p>
        Whats the finishing touch to a successful retro karaoke party? The costumes! Have guests dress to match the period of music youre focusing on. From poodle skirts to hippie beads to gold spandex, celebrate all of the past decades style hits (and misses!) along with the hits from the radio. You can even provide some elements of the costumes, such as hats and wigs, to make sure that everyone gets in on the fun.
       </p>
       <p>
        Want to get planning your retro karaoke party? Call on Golden Productions today to get started!
       </p>
