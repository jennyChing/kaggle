          <img alt="Thin Mint and Andes Mint Rice Crispy Treats" class="aligncenter wp-image-3081" height="717" src="http://www.yourcupofcake.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Thin-Mint-and-Andes-Mint-Rice-Crispy-Treats-682x1024.jpg" width="477"/>

        <p>
         The Story:
         <br/>
         When people find out I bake, they always say want to come and help. But lets be real, they dont want to help, they want to help.
        </p>
        <p>
         They want to sit there at the counter, watch me, distract me, taste everything so much theres nothing left for photos, give me baking advice, tell me what I should make for
         <strong>
          them
         </strong>
         , watch me do the dishes and then take all the treats home on a plate.
        </p>
        <p>
         Usually Ill humor them and invite them to my kitchen, but I warn them that it comes with a price.
        </p>
        <p>
         If anything goes wrong when Im baking, I will blame them. It doesnt matter if they were across the room, upstairs, or sleeping on the couch, I will find some hilariously insane way to blame them for what went wrong.
        </p>
        <p>
         But if you cant take the heat, get out of the kitchen.[end-story]
        </p>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0">
             1st Layer:
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 C. chocolate rice cereal
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             5-6 oz. marshmallows (half the normal sized bag)
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 Tbsp. butter
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp vanilla extract
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             15 Thin Mints, roughly chopped
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7">
             2nd Layer: Peppermint White Chocolate Fudge
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             6 oz. cream cheese, soft
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 C. powdered sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp vanilla extract
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 tsp. peppermint extract
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/3 C. melted white chocolate (melts or baking chocolate work best)
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 C. Andes Mint Pieces
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15">
             3rd Layer:
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 C. chocolate rice cereal
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
             5-6 oz. marshmallows (half the normal sized bag)
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 Tbsp. butter
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp vanilla extract
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-20" itemprop="ingredients">
             15 Thin Mints, roughly chopped
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-21" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-22">
             Chocolate Ganache:
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-23" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 3/4 C. chocolate chips
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-24" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 C. + 2 Tbsp. whipping cream
            </div>
           </span>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Directions
           </p>
           <span class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             1. Prepare a baking dish by greasing it with butter.  (It also helps to line with parchment and let the flaps hang over so that when you're all done you can life the entire thing out to cut.)
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             2. 1st Layer: Measure out cereal and place in a bowl.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             3. In a large bowl, place marshmallows, butter and vanilla extract.  Microwave and stir until smooth.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             4. Pour in cereal and stir until coated. Add in cookie pieces and stir again.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             5. Dump unto pan and pat down evenly on the bottom.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             6. 2nd Layer: Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla and peppermint extract until smooth.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             7. With the mixer slowly beating, stream in melted white chocolate. Beat until smooth.  (If there are a few chunks of chocolate, don't fret, it will still taste great!)
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             8. Dump over 1st layer and smooth over evenly. Dump Andes Mint Pieces and pat down into fudge.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             9. 2nd Layer: Repeat steps 2-5.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             10. Ganache: Melt chocolate and cream in the microwave and stir until smooth.  Pour over the top layer and spread evenly.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             11. Let set for about 1 hour, or you can speed up the process in the fridge.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-11" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             12. Cut and enjoy!
            </div>
           </span>
