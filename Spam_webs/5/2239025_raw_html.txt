       <p>
        Meet Alek, Anjali and Anya.
       </p>
       <p>
        Theyre a young couple with a little dog.
       </p>
       <p>
        And theyve been building they own mortgage-free tiny home on a 820 trailer.
       </p>
       <p>
        It was designed in SketchUp and every little bit of the process has been documented on
        <a href="http://tiny-project.com/blog/?affiliates=42" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://tiny-project.com/blog/?affiliates=42', 'their blog']);" target="_blank">

        </li>
        <li>

         Kitchen
        </li>

         Washer/Dryer
        </li>
