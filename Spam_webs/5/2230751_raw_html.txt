                    <h2 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Quotes about Strength from Swami Vivekananda
                    </h2>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3456973599735585749" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <p>
                      Here are 50 quotes about strength from Swami Vivekananda.
                     </p>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      Quotes about Strength and Courage
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Think of your own body, and see that it is strong and healthy; it is the best instrument you have. Think of it as being as strong as adamant, and that with the help of this body you will cross the ocean of life. Freedom is never to be reached by the weak. Throw away all weakness. Tell your body that it is strong, tell your mind that it is strong, and have unbounded faith and hope in yourself.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-8Ufc2mRrkPk/VPDHNfaBqwI/AAAAAAAABKw/1JLTBm8tWJY/w877-h589-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-throw-away-weakness.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about strength throw away weakness" border="0" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-8Ufc2mRrkPk/VPDHNfaBqwI/AAAAAAAABKw/1JLTBm8tWJY/w877-h589-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-throw-away-weakness.png" title="Vivekananda quotes about strength throw away weakness" width="500"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <p>
                      All weakness, all bondage is imagination. Speak one word to it, it must vanish. Do not weaken! There is no other way out.... Stand up and be strong! No fear. No superstition. Face the truth as it is!
                     </p>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <p>
                      Stand up, be bold, be strong. Take the whole responsibility on your own shoulders, and know that you are the creator of your own destiny. All the strength and succor you want are within you.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-NOt_9YZw_QY/VPDOVYMn6ZI/AAAAAAAABLo/hEyjGN93RZM/w938-h672-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-take-responsibility.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about strength take responsibility" border="0" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-NOt_9YZw_QY/VPDOVYMn6ZI/AAAAAAAABLo/hEyjGN93RZM/w938-h672-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-take-responsibility.png" title="Vivekananda quotes about strength take responsibility" width="500"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <p>
                      Stand and die in your own strength, if there is any sin in the world, it is weakness; avoid all weakness, for weakness is sin, weakness is death.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If you think yourselves weak, weak you will be; if you think yourselves strong, strong you will be; if you think yourselves impure, impure you will be; if you think yourselves pure, pure you will be. This teaches us not to think ourselves as weak, but as strong, omnipotent, omniscient.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Never be weak. You must be strong; you have infinite strength within you. How else will you conquer anything? How else will you come to God?
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/-KuqeWH8ZhnE/VPEDqrBBfMI/AAAAAAAABMM/cY94E2ffbyg/w938-h722-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-never-be-weak.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about strength never be weak" border="0" src="https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/-KuqeWH8ZhnE/VPEDqrBBfMI/AAAAAAAABMM/cY94E2ffbyg/w938-h722-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-never-be-weak.jpg" title="Vivekananda quotes about strength never be weak" width="500"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <p>
                      The strong individual, even if he is thrown amidst the worst conditions of place or time, overcomes them and affirms his own strength. Not only so, all the heavy burdens heaped upon the individual, the acting agent, are being made lighter and lighter in the course of time, so that any individual, however weak he may be in the beginning, is sure to reach the goal at the end if he assiduously applies himself to gain it.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Hold on to your own ideas with strength and purity, and whatever obstructions may now be in your way, the world is bound to listen to you in the long run.
                     </p>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      Strength in Restraint
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Many times in the rushing forward we lose much strength.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Non-resistance is the greatest strength. In meekness and mildness lies the greatest strength. In suffering is greater strength than in doing. In resisting one's own passions is far higher strength than in hurting others.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Which requires the greater strength, letting go or restraining? The calm man is not the man who is dull. You must not mistake Sattva for dullness or laziness. The calm man is the one who has control over the mind waves. Activity is the manifestation of inferior strength, calmness, of the superior.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The highest manifestation of strength is to keep ourselves calm and on our own feet.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-8Ur8nzJMwbI/VPDHOyhFH2I/AAAAAAAABK4/kID18aGQSjE/w1100-h679-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-keep-calm.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about strength keep calm" border="0" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-8Ur8nzJMwbI/VPDHOyhFH2I/AAAAAAAABK4/kID18aGQSjE/w1100-h679-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-keep-calm.jpg" title="Vivekananda quotes about strength keep calm" width="550"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      Quotes about Strength in Life
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      The best guide in life is strength.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The sign of life is strength and growth.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The greatest weakness often insinuates itself as the greatest good and strength.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      We are forever trying to make our weakness look like strength, our sentiment like love, our cowardice like courage, and so on.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      In doing good we do good to ourselves and to others as well; and, like all other forces in man, these forces of good and evil also gather strength from outside.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Power and things like that will come by themselves. Put yourself to work, and you will find such tremendous power coming to you that you will feel it hard to bear. Even the least work done for others awakens the power within; even thinking the least good of others gradually instills into the heart the strength of a lion.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      We think we are going to get security, strength, wisdom, happiness from the outside. We always hope but never realize our hope. Never does any help come from the outside.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-4Qwrgj-wDHk/VPHmpD2rQPI/AAAAAAAABNA/iu1Y-cfr2yw/w1063-h705-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-help.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about strength and help" border="0" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-4Qwrgj-wDHk/VPHmpD2rQPI/AAAAAAAABNA/iu1Y-cfr2yw/w1063-h705-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-help.jpg" title="Vivekananda quotes about strength and help" width="500"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <p>
                      External helps and methods, forms, ceremonies, creeds, doctrines, all have their right place and are meant to support and strengthen us until we become strong. Then they are no more necessary.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Ay, let every man and woman and child, without respect of caste or birth, weakness or strength, hear and learn that behind the strong and the weak, behind the high and the low, behind every one, there is that Infinite Soul, assuring the infinite possibility and the infinite capacity of all to become great and good.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The education which does not help the common mass of people to equip themselves for the struggle for life, which does not bring out strength of character, a spirit of philanthropy, and the courage of a lion - is it worth the name? Real education is that which enables one to stand on one's own legs.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Our greatness is greater than our weakness; the good things are stronger than the evil things.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-5vhccXZTVEo/VPHiddhpTAI/AAAAAAAABMk/UoB8aY2D6z4/w938-h489-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-good-is-stronger.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about strength and good things" border="0" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-5vhccXZTVEo/VPHiddhpTAI/AAAAAAAABMk/UoB8aY2D6z4/w938-h489-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-good-is-stronger.jpg" title="Vivekananda quotes about strength and good things" width="550"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <p>
                      Without doubt, for purity is strength, goodness is strength.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The sign of vigor, the sign of life, the sign of hope, the sign of health, the sign of everything that is good, is strength. As long as the body lives, there must be strength in the body, strength in the mind, [and strength] in the hand.
                     </p>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      Quotes about Inner Strength
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Strength does not come by thinking of weakness all the time. The remedy for weakness is not brooding over weakness, but thinking of strength. Teach men of the strength that is already within them.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The stronger the will, the less the yielding to the sway of the emotions.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      What you want is character, strengthening of the will.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Why should a man be moral and pure? Because this strengthens his will. Everything that strengthens the will by revealing the real nature is moral.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      It is the strong mind that hews its way through a thousand difficulties.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-8hBD4Iw9iF4/VPJn-VOWe5I/AAAAAAAABNU/Jc1347gQU58/w928-h770-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-strong-mind.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda strength quotes about a strong mind" border="0" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-8hBD4Iw9iF4/VPJn-VOWe5I/AAAAAAAABNU/Jc1347gQU58/w928-h770-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-strong-mind.png" title="Vivekananda strength quotes about a strong mind" width="500"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      Spiritual Strength Quotes
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      The world is a grand moral gymnasium wherein we have all to take exercise so as to become stronger and stronger spiritually.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Every one of us, every being, has as his own background such a reservoir of strength, infinite power, infinite purity, infinite bliss, and existence infinite - only these locks, these bodies, are hindering us from expressing what we really are to the fullest.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The chaste brain has tremendous energy and gigantic will-power. Without chastity there can be no spiritual strength.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If there is no strength in the body and mind, the Atman cannot be realized.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Repetition of Om and self-surrender to the Lord will strengthen the mind, and bring fresh energy.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Day and night say, "I am He". It is the greatest strength; it is religion.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      A spiritually strong and sound man will be strong in every other respect, if he so wishes.
                     </p>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-zMUDXFYAy20/VPDkNXZLCHI/AAAAAAAABL4/jVIj23MeCYw/w1000-h629-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-spiritually-strong.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vivekananda quotes about spiritual strength" border="0" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-zMUDXFYAy20/VPDkNXZLCHI/AAAAAAAABL4/jVIj23MeCYw/w1000-h629-no/vivekananda-strength-quotes-spiritually-strong.jpg" title="Vivekananda quotes about spiritual strength" width="550"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <p>
                      Obey the scriptures until you are strong enough to do without them; then go beyond them. Books are not an end-all. Verification is the only proof of religious truth.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Truth is strengthening. Truth is purity, truth is all-knowledge; truth must be strengthening, must be enlightening, must be invigorating.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The search for truth is the expression of strength - not the groping of a weak, blind man.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Infinite strength is religion and God.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The attention to forms, to formulas, to the everyday details of religion, and to rituals, may sometimes be laughed at; but nevertheless, within them is strength.
                     </p>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      More Quotes about Strength
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Strength is the medicine for the world's disease. Strength is the medicine which the poor must have when tyrannized over by the rich. Strength is the medicine that the ignorant must have when oppressed by the learned; and it is the medicine that sinners must have when tyrannized over by other sinners.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      We must not look down with contempt on others. All of us are going towards the same goal. The difference between weakness and strength is one of degree; the difference between virtue and vice is one of degree, the difference between heaven and hell is one of degree, the difference between life and death is one of degree, all differences in this world are of degree, and not of kind, because oneness is the secret of everything.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If you are a strong man, very good! But do not curse others who are not strong enough for you. ... Everyone says, "Woe unto you people!" Who says, "Woe unto me that I cannot help you?" The people are doing all right to the best of their ability and means and knowledge. Woe unto me that I cannot lift them to where I am!
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      It is not the whole truth to say that fortune favors the enterprising man; behind there must be the strength that comes of the Divine Mother.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      They talk a great deal of the new theories about the survival of the fittest, and they think that it is the strength of the muscles which is the fittest to survive. If that were true, any one of the aggressively known old world nations would have lived in glory today, and we, the weak Hindus, who never conquered even one other race or nation, ought to have died out; yet we live here three hundred million strong!
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Are you strong? Do you feel strength? - for I know it is truth alone that gives strength.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Perfect strength will have no activity in this world; it only is, it does not act.
                     </p>
                     <br/>
