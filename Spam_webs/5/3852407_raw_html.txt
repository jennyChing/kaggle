                      <div class="field-item even">
                       These tips will help you with packing and hiring a moving company.
                      </div>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="paragraphs-items paragraphs-items-field-paragraph-blocks paragraphs-items-field-paragraph-blocks-full paragraphs-items-full">
                     <div class="field field-name-field-paragraph-blocks field-type-paragraphs field-label-hidden clearfix">
                      <div class="field-items">
                       <div class="field-item even" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           Most people will have to pack up shop and move at some point or another. While starting over in a new home is exciting, packing your life into small, cardboard boxes is not fun.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Whether you plan to relocate down the street, or across the country, the following tips can aid you in your move.
                          </p>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field-item odd" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <h3 class="readable-header">
                          1. Develop a master plan before you move
                         </h3>
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           Sit down with everyone in your household and put together a master to-do list.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           This should include a strategy for packing, contacting all relevant service providers and determining if you'll need a professional moving service.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            RELATED:
                            <a href="http://www.angieslist.com/articles/how-much-does-it-cost-hire-movers.htm">
                             How Much Does it Cost to Hire Movers?
                            </a>
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field-item even" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <h3 class="readable-header">
                          2. Get started on your move early
                         </h3>
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           Moving is a long and involved process, so its extremely important to give yourself plenty of time to prepare. If you choose to hire a professional moving company, you need to make preparations in advance.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Call potential moving companies as early as possible, and weekend moves require even more notice. If you want to move on a Friday, Saturday or Sunday, call at least a month in advance. If you're moving Monday-Thursday, a two-week notice should suffice.
                          </p>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field-item odd" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <h3 class="readable-header">
                          3. Choose your moving method
                         </h3>
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           You can handle everything yourself, or
                           <a href="http://www.angieslist.com/articles/what-you-need-know-about-hiring-mover.htm" target="_blank">
                            hire professional movers
                           </a>
                           to pack, transport and unpack everything for you. Many people opt for intermediate options, such as hiring movers to take only the largest, most awkward items, or packing up everything themselves and letting the movers drive the truck to the new home.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Consider the cost of these services as well as your schedule. One of the main factors that will influence the cost of a move is whether it's classified as interstate or intrastate. Interstate moves involve crossing state lines and intrastate moves stay within the same state.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           According to estimates provided by the
                           <a href="http://www.moving.org" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                            American Moving &amp; Storage Association
                           </a>
                           , the average cost of an interstate move is about $5,630, based on an average weight of 7,100 pounds and distance of 1,200 miles.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           The average intrastate move costs $1,170, based on the same weight and a crew of four. Prices may fluctuate, depending on where you live.
                          </p>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field-item even" property="">
                        <div class="floating-box-block entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-floating-box-block view-mode-full floating-box-block-right floating-box-block-width-large">
                         <div class="floating-box-block-image">

                       <div class="field-item odd" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <h3 class="readable-header">
                          4. Make an informed decision before hiring a mover
                         </h3>
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           If you decide to hire professionals for any stage of the move, be diligent in your search. Check Angies List to find highly rated moving companies in your area. Make sure your movers are licensed with the
                           <a href="http://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/" target="_blank">
                            Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration
                           </a>
                           (FMCSA) or, if applicable, with your state government.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           For interstate moves, the FMCSA legally requires moving companies to obtain licensing and display their U.S. Department of Transportation number in any advertising, provide arbitration if consumer complaints cant be resolved amicably, inform homeowners of their rights and furnish an accurate summary of their complaint handling procedures, including a phone number.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Get bids from at least three different companies to help make sure you are getting the best price, but know that saving money isn't everything. Most moving companies will provide a binding or nonbinding estimate.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           A binding estimate is a written agreement made in advance with your mover that clearly describes all services provided. It guarantees the total cost of the move based on the quantities and services written in the estimate.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           A nonbinding estimate is what the mover believes the cost will be based upon the estimated weight of the shipment and additional services required. However, the final charges are based on the actual weight of your shipment, and the services provided by the movers.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Some movers offer a guaranteed-not-to-exceed estimate, which allows the consumer to pay the binding estimate or the actual cost, whichever is lower.
                          </p>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field-item even" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <h3 class="readable-header">
                          5. Pack and transport important items yourself
                         </h3>
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           Even if you're using professional movers, there are some important items you should keep on your possession. These include essential, everyday things like toiletries, cleaning supplies, kitchen utensils and medications.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            RELATED:
                            <a href="http://www.angieslist.com/articles/how-prepare-and-pack-move.htm">
                             How to Prepare and Pack for Moving
                            </a>
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           In addition, keep important records such as birth certificates, passports, financial documents and Social Security cards safely on your person or in your vehicle. Valuable possessions like jewelry should also travel with you, ideally in a secure container.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Bring tools such as tape, writing utensils and box cutters along on moving day.
                          </p>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field-item odd" property="">
                        <div about="" class="ds-1col entity entity-paragraphs-item paragraphs-item-basic-content view-mode-full clearfix" typeof="">
                         <h3 class="readable-header">
                          6. Pack efficiently the items you plan to move
                         </h3>
                         <div class="readable-text">
                          <p>
                           Pack items from the same room together, and in the same boxes. Place items securely in each box, with the heaviest things on the bottom, and make sure the boxes can close.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Don't overload boxes with too many items because it slows down the process, and makes it impossible to stack boxes in the moving truck.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Schedule time to disassemble large items prior to moving day, and keep a supply of used cardboard on hand for extra padding.
                          </p>

                      <div class="field field-name-comment-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden clearfix">
                       <div class="field-items">
                        <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                         <p>
                          When you are planning a move between September 15th and May 15th two weeks notice should be sufficient for most movers.  But if you are planning to move during the busy summer months, May 15th through September 15th, you should plan AT LEAST one month in advance.  Movers book up early during this period and if you wait too long you may not be able to find a mover who can accommodate your moving dates.
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          Also, it is good to have the mover out BEFORE you start packing.  There are two reasons for this; number one - you will get a more accurate estimate of the weight, and number two - your mover can give you tips on how to pack specific items and where to get the best pricing on packing material.
                         </p>
