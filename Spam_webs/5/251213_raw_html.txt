     <p class="post-content post-content-justify" itemprop="articleBody">
      Hey guys!!!
      <br/>
      <br/>
      I was craving some biscuits today, fluffy butter filled biscuits but i know i`m trying to eat healthier and that doesn`t exactly fit the healthy bill sadly so i hit up my lifesaver google (i google everything) for some healthy recipes i found on onhttp://www.easybakingtips.com/fluffy-biscuits.html and changed up a few stuff to make it a little more personal.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      I`ll only be including the recipe with my tweaks you can check out the original recipe in the source link or above..
      <br/>
      <br/>
      1 cup whole wheat flour
      <br/>
      1 teaspoon baking powder (next time i will be using yeast instead )
      <br/>
      a pinch of salt
      <br/>
      1 tbsp dried parsley.
      <br/>
      1/2 and onion chopped
      <br/>
      about 2 green onions chopped
      <br/>
      2 figs of garlic also chopped.
      <br/>
      3 tbsp EVOO
      <br/>
      1/4 cup non-fat yogurt whipped
      <br/>
      1/2 tsp honey
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      *Preheat over to 350
      <br/>
      * Mix the flour, salt baking powder together then add all the other dry ingredients
      <br/>
      * Making a well in the mixture add all wet ingredients and mix until all ingredients are incorporated nicely..
      <br/>
      *Make dough into a ball and roll into a circle (the thinner you roll the thinner your biscuits the thicker you roll the thicker your biscuits will be)
      <br/>
      * Cut dough using a cookie cutter or a glass
      <br/>
      *Bake for about 20 min or until brown.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Hint the fluffiness of your biscuit depends on the thickness..
      <br/>
      <br/>
      If you all have any biscuit recipes please share. This one was pretty good and easy but i`m always looking for new recipes.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      thanks for reading
