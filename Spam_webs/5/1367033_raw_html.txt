         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name headline">
          Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME
         </h1>
         <div class="subhead">
          <span class="postauthortop author vcard">

        <div class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="description articleBody">
         <p class="no-break">
          <span class="st_facebook_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_twitter_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/" st_via="JustinLaurenXO">
          </span>
          <span class="st_pinterest_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_stumbleupon_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_googleplus_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_reddit_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_email_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
          <span class="st_sharethis_large" st_title="Travel Blogger Thursday  A&amp;Z of Backpack ME" st_url="http://justinpluslauren.com/travel-blogger-thursday-backpack-me/">
          </span>
         </p>
         <div id="triberr_endorsement">
         </div>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 710px">
          <a href="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-In-Torres-del-Paine_Chile.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-center" height="532" src="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-In-Torres-del-Paine_Chile.jpg" width="710"/>
          </a>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Torres del Paine, Chile
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Ashray and Zara, better known as A&amp;Z, write for the travel blog,
          <a href="http://bkpk.me" target="_blank">
           Backpack ME
          </a>
          and were honoured to interview them for this weeks
          <a href="http://justinpluslauren.com/category/travel-blogger-thursday/" target="_blank">
           Travel Blogger Thursday
          </a>
          ! They are a couple who met in Dubai in 2011, who have since decided to sell all of their belongings, pack their bags, and travel the world. We hope that you enjoy learning more about this adventurous duo!
         </p>
         <h4>
          What inspires you to travel and what do you enjoy most about traveling?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We like going to new places, experience different contexts, observing how people go about their daily lives in opposites side of the planet. Travel is our school. It is our home and it is what we do. Right now, it would be impossible to picture us any other way.
         </p>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 710px">
          <a href="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-In-South-America.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-center" height="472" src="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-In-South-America.jpg" width="710"/>
          </a>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           A&amp;Z in South America
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <h4>
          How did you both meet and is travel involved?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We met in Dubai, in the United Arab Emirates. We were not traveling there, but rather working. We dated for a while and we were very happy to have a love for travel in common. We were
          <br/>
          <a href="http://bkpk.me/why-i-left-dubai-and-wont-come-back-part-12/" target="_blank">
           increasingly discontent with life in Dubai
          </a>
           we didnt care for the superficiality and materialism
          <br/>
          that Dubai tends to be well-known for. We both wanted to see the world and we couldnt be happier that we were in the same page at the same time!
         </p>
         <h4>
          Where is your favourite destination and why?
         </h4>
         <p>
          It is impossible to name a favorite destination in the world. Seriously, how can you compare
          <br/>
          lounging in a paradise-like beach in Goa with with eating delicious tacos in Mexico City? Or trekking in icy Patagonia with spending an afternoon telling little kids in Ethiopia what life is all
          <br/>
          about in India? Every place has its own ups and downs. As visitors, we want to see, do and learn as much as possible while were in a given destination  no matter where in the world we might be.
         </p>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 678px">
          <a href="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Yee-Peng-Lights-Festival-in-Thailand.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-center" height="451" src="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Yee-Peng-Lights-Festival-in-Thailand.jpg" width="678"/>
          </a>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Yee Peng Lights Festival in Thailand
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <h4>
          When did you start to travel, and where did you first visit?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We left Dubai in the fall of 2011 and our plan was to
          <a href="http://bkpk.me/category/latin-america/" target="_blank">
           travel around Latin America
          </a>
          for a year. After brief stops in Ethiopia, India and some countries in Europe, we finally landed in Mexico almost in time for a Caribbean Christmas. We spent the entire year in Latin America, just as planned. We visited Mexico, Cuba, Costa Rica, Brazil, Ecuador, Chile, Peru, Bolivia, and still had time for a few weeks in the USA. After that we returned home, to India and Portugal, and realized that this had only been the beginning. So we got married, re-planned our lives for the immediate short-term, managed to establish ourselves working on the go and havent stop traveling to new places ever since!
         </p>
         <h4>
          Why did you start your blog, and what do you enjoy most about blogging?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We decided to start Backpack ME because we figured our travel experiences could only be enriched if we shared them with other people. Maybe others out there, planning to do something similar, could learn from what we have already gone through. Or they could simply get inspired and understand that yes, it is possible to live this life-style. We didnt know many bloggers from India or Portugal either, or multicultural couples like us. So we wanted to specially reach out to people similar to us, from countries that arent particularly known for having a long-term travel culture. This has become one of Backpack MEs mottos: travel, no matter where you come from!
         </p>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 710px">
          <a href="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Making-friends-in-Ethiopia.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-center" height="472" src="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Making-friends-in-Ethiopia.jpg" width="710"/>
          </a>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Making friends in Ethiopia
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <h4>
          What do you like to bring back from the places youve visited?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We do have at least one souvenir from each place weve been to. But normally it has to be something small, because we travel for months in a row and we dont even have a house where put all of the things we get. Normally, we get handicrafts. And, sometimes, we pack a bunch of rare to find ingredients to cook later in India or Portugal  particularly while traveling in Asia.
         </p>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 710px">
          <a href="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Tasting-Asia-in-Singapore.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-center" height="472" src="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Tasting-Asia-in-Singapore.jpg" width="710"/>
          </a>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Tasting Asia in Singapore
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <h4>
          Where do you plan on visiting next?
         </h4>
         <p>
          Right now were in Chile but not for long. We have plans to head to India for a while, followed by Portugal  we like visiting our families and friends back home from time to time. After that, its
          <br/>
          still an enigma. But knowing us, it will probably end up being a spur of the moment adventure, somewhere exciting and with good food! So many possibilities are spoiling us and we cant seem to decide with much time in advance. But keep on checking
          <a href="http://bkpk.me/travel-plans/" target="_blank">
           Backpack ME
          </a>
          and who knows where youll find us next!
         </p>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 710px">
          <img alt="" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-center" height="472" src="http://justinpluslauren.com/wp-content/gallery/travel-blogger-thursday-2/Zara-Road-tripping-in-Arizona.jpg" width="710"/>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Road tripping in Arizona
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Thank you so much for the awesome interview, A&amp;Z! Be sure to visit their blog at
