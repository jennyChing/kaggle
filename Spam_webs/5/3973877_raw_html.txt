Opposing Coaches on the 19&lt;sup&gt;th&lt;/sup&gt; season of The Ultimate Fighter reality TV series, BJ Penn and Frankie Edgar will share the same Octagon for the third time at the TUF 19 Finale. The card is set for July 6 at the Mandalay Bay Event Center in Las Vegas. Nev.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Here are my TUF 19 Finale predictions:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;B.J. Penn vs. Frankie Edgar&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Frankie(16-4-1, 4 KOs) couldnt have been any more dominant during his first two fights against Penn (16-9-2, 7 KOs), and theres no reason to think their third encounter will be any different. Frankies utilized his crisp boxing, speed, and footwork to earn the two relatively easy decisions, and that was back when B.J. was in his prime.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Factor in the fact Penns cardio  which has always been an issue throughout his MMA career  seems to be even worse nowadays, and its hard to see the prodigy emerging victorious from this TUF 19 Finale bout.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;Prediction: Edgar wins via TKO.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;Kevin Lee vs. Jesse Ronson&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Lee (7-1-0, 4 submissions) put on a valiant effort during his UFC debut, but it wasnt enough to get him past Al Iaquinta. His opponent Ronson (13-4-0, 6 KOs) will also be looking for his first win inside the Octagon, currently sporting a 0-2 record in the Ultimate Fighting Championship.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Ronson seems to be the technically superior striker on paper, but he might have issues controlling where the fight takes place against an opponent like Lee. It should be a close competitive fight, but I cant go against Lee given how good he looked during his fight against Iaquinta.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;Prediction: Lee wins via decision.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;Remaining TUF 19 Finale Predictions:&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Corey Anderson defeats Matt Van Buren&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Dhiego Lima defeats Eddie Gordon&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Justin Scoggins defeats Dustin Ortiz&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Derrick Lewis defeats Carlos Augusto Filho&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Sarah Moras defeats Alexis Dufresne&lt;/p&gt;
