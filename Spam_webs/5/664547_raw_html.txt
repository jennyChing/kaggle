         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Designer Sylvain Viau Imagines the Hover Cars We Were Promised
          <span class="post-date">
           by
           <a href="http://thisiscolossal.com/about" rel="author external" title="Visit Christopher Jobsons website">
            Christopher Jobson
           </a>
           on April 21, 2015
          </span>
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54596" height="518" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-1.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          For his ongoing series
          <a href="https://www.behance.net/gallery/17907655/Flying-cars" target="_blank">
           Flying Cars
          </a>
          , French designer
          <a href="http://www.svdesign.fr/" target="_blank">
           Sylvain Viau
          </a>
          digitally edits photographs of cars into sleek, wheel-less hover cars that appear to float just above the ground. Viau not only uses his own photography to create these sci-fi cars, but is fortunate to claim many of the actual cars among his own collection. He originally worked only with 80s Citron vehicles because of their classic space-age design, but has continued to branch out over the last few months to include cars from Peugeot, Toyota, and Renault. You can see many more
          <a href="https://www.behance.net/gallery/17907655/Flying-cars" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          . (via
          <a href="http://www.designboom.com/art/sylvain-viau-flying-cars-04-21-2015/" target="_blank">
           Designboom
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="update">
           Update:
          </span>
          Photographer
          <a href="http://renaud-marion.squarespace.com/air-drive/" target="_blank">
           Renaud Marion
          </a>
          created a similar series of works in 2013.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54597" height="531" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-2.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54598" height="527" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-3.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-4" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54599" height="531" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-4.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54600" height="535" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-5.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-6" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54601" height="523" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-6.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-7" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54602" height="530" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-7.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="hover-8" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54603" height="531" src="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hover-8.jpg" width="800"/>
