         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Death by Chocolate  Seriously Chocolatey Orange &amp; Vanilla Cake
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="posted-on">
           Posted on
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/death-by-chocolate-cake/" rel="bookmark">
            <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2014-12-24T14:13:24+00:00">
             24 December, 2014
            </time>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="byline">
           by
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/author/margot/">
             Margot
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="posted_in">
           in
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/category/occasions/for-him/">

        </header>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          [recipe adapted from
          <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/The-Golden-Book-Chocolate-Recipes/dp/1845432673/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=coffandvani-21&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=4PWDZPVRYALNM6C2&amp;creativeASIN=1845432673" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           The Golden Book of Chocolate
          </a>
          ]
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="death by chocolate cake recipe" class="alignnone wp-image-28593 size-large" height="835" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/death-by-chocolate-cake-recipe-622x835.jpg" title="Death by Chocolate - Seriously Chocolatey  Orange &amp; Vanilla Cake" width="622"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          This is seriously chocolatey cake recipe using 775 g darkchocolate spread between cake dough, frosting and the ganache.
         </p>
         <p>
          It is very rich but delicious, moist, havetexture similar tomarcepan thanks to the almond flour and is filled with scent of orange liqueur and vanilla.
         </p>
         <p>
          I have adapted this recipe from mentioned above book but I have changed proportions of the dough, filling to the frosting to suit my needs.
         </p>
         <p>
          I have also added vanilla sugar to make it more fragrant.
         </p>

           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});

         </div>
         <h3>
          INGREDIENTS
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            for the chocolate cake
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           375 gquality bittersweet / dark chocolate (broken into pieces)
          </li>
          <li>
           1 cup plain flour
          </li>
          <li>
           1 tsp baking powder
          </li>
          <li>
            tsp / pinch of salt
          </li>
          <li>
            cup ground almonds / almond flour
          </li>
          <li>
           185 g / about bar unsalted butter
          </li>
          <li>
           1 tbs unsalted butter for greasing the tin
          </li>
          <li>
           1 cup of golden caster sugar
          </li>
          <li>
           3 tbs
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/real-vanilla-sugar/">
            vanilla sugar
           </a>
           , best homemade
          </li>
          <li>
           4 large / 6 smaller eggs (separated)
          </li>
          <li>
           4 tbs orange liqueur (I used Grand Marnier)
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            for the filling
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
            cup quality orange marmalade (I used fine cut but clear would be the best)
          </li>
          <li>
           4 tbs orange liqueur (I used
           <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Grand-Marnier-70cl/dp/B0058ISARE/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=coffandvani-21&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=WPKILK7BYVMJOFN2&amp;creativeASIN=B0058ISARE" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Grand Marnier
           </a>
           )
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <em>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            for the chocolate frosting
           </span>
          </em>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           250 g quality bittersweet / dark chocolate (broken into pieces)
          </li>
          <li>
           125 g / half a bar unsalted butter
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            for the chocolate ganache
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           150 ml extra thick double cream
          </li>
          <li>
           150 g quality bittersweet / dark chocolate (grated)
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h3>
          EQUIPMENT
         </h3>
         <ul>
          <li>
           9 (22,8 cm) round non stick cake tin with removable trays (
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/how-to-line-base-of-spring-form-tin/">
            lined with baking paper
           </a>
           )
          </li>
          <li>
           spatula
          </li>
          <li>
           food processor with bowl
          </li>
          <li>
           egg whisk and bowl for the egg whites
          </li>
          <li>
           another bowl for dry ingredients
          </li>
          <li>
           small pot and metal bowl for the double boiler
          </li>
          <li>
           saucepan for the ganache and then orange marmalade
          </li>
          <li>
           cake stand or disposable cake base
          </li>
          <li>
           piping syringe
          </li>
          <li>
           grater
          </li>
          <li>
           strong thread
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="death by chocolate cake from scratch" class="alignnone wp-image-28594 size-large" height="704" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/death-by-chocolate-cake-835x704.jpg" title="Death by Chocolate - Seriously Chocolatey Orange &amp; Vanilla Cake" width="835"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          METHOD
         </h3>
         <ol>
          <li>
           Preheated oven to 180C (356F).
          </li>
          <li>
           Grease the baking tray.
          </li>
          <li>
           Melt the chocolate in the double boiler and leave aside.
          </li>
          <li>
           Combine dry ingredients: flour, almonds, baking powder and salt.
          </li>
          <li>
           In a food processor beat butter, sugars and egg yolks.
          </li>
          <li>
           In meantime beat also egg whites using the whisk.
          </li>
          <li>
           Once content ofthe food processor and mixed well, slowly fold in also egg whites and dry ingredients.
          </li>
          <li>
           Add also the liquor and transfer whole mixture to the baking tray.
          </li>
          <li>
           Place in the oven and bake for about 45 minutes.
          </li>
          <li>
           In meantime prepare ganache heating cream in a small saucepan and adding grated chocolate, continuouslystirring. Once the ganache is ready put it in the fridge until needed.
          </li>
          <li>
           Once the cake is done and cooled down cut it in half using the thread to create two layers.
          </li>
          <li>
           In a small saucepan heat the marmalade until all runny, stir in also the orange liqueur.
          </li>
          <li>
           To assemble the cake, place one layer of the cake on the cake stand, spread over the orange marmalade, cover with second layer.
          </li>
          <li>
           In a double boiler melt chocolate and butter for the frosting. Spoon mixture over the cake, let until the whole cake is covered.
          </li>
          <li>
           Once the frosting is set, use piping syringe and ganache to decorate the cake.
          </li>
          <li>
           Leaveinthe fridge for at least one hour to set.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            This recipe makes 12  16 portions of cake.
           </em>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="death by chocolate cake recipe UK " class="alignnone wp-image-28595 size-large" height="742" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/death-by-chocolate-cake-recipe-uk-835x742.jpg" title="Death by Chocolate - Seriously Chocolatey Orange &amp; Vanilla Cake" width="835"/>
         </p>
