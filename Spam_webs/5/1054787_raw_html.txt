           <h1>
            Creepy Kids in Creepy Vintage Ads
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <div class="left" style="display:block;">
             By Tim Urban
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <!-- end meta -->
          </header>
          <div class="entry-content-wrap">

               <em>
                [Originally posted August 2013. Updated June 2014.]
               </em>
               <div>
                Message to every American currently between the ages of 55 and 110: I know your secret.
               </div>
               <div>
               </div>
               <div>
                You had an incredibly weird childhood.
               </div>
               <div>

               </div>
               <div>
               </div>
               <div>
