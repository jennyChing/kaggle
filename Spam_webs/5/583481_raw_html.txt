      <p>
       Here at OnlineBookClub.org we take pride in featuring books that you might not otherwise see. We know you don't need to be told about the Harry Potters and Twilights that everyone is talking about. We find the up-and-coming gems--the books by authors everyone is going to be talking about next year. Today's feature is a book that our own in-house professional review team gave 4 out of 4 stars,
       <strong>
        <em>
         The Final Frontier
        </em>
        by H M Irwing
       </strong>
       .
      </p>
      <h2>
       About the Book - The Final Frontier
      </h2>
      <p>
       Sim is a bio-engineered weapon created for a sole purpose, to stop the end of time. Schooled in the prestigious Venetor Defence Academy run by the insular Johuan race, Sim was taught survival by the very best of them. But unwitting through the means of her budding powers, having caused the death of a particularly sadistic mentor at the academy, Sim is on the run. Escaping Johua, her birth-land and scooped up by the Ovions a virtuos formidable Johuan protector of the Awakened Worlds, Sim embarks on an unusual journey of discovery of herself, her new found three siblings and her creator and sire, Bimas. The Ovion are searching for Bimas, a mad-scientist, whose missing invention may be the cause of an impending apocalypse of cosmic proportions. The journey brings Sim and the Ovions the discovery of the existence of the Johuans genetically superior cousins, an angel-like beings called the Heilor. The discovery of the growing seed of political corruption amongst the echelons of Johua who intends to obliterate the Ovions and their new found cousins, the Heilors and amass the wealth of the Awakened Worlds for Johua. And most importantly the discovery of the existence of a remote and obscure plant called Earth.
      </p>
