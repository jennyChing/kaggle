        <p>
         I rendered this voodoo doll tattoo design (scroll down) in Photoshop a few months back. It was an experiment to get down and dirty with Photoshop as an artistic medium and get some feedback.
        </p>
        <p>
         I might do more tattoo designs in Photoshop in the future. Keep up with
         <a href="http://www.tattoodev.com/">
          tattoodev
         </a>
         by subscribing to rss in the sidebar or like the
         <a href="http://www.facebook.com/tattoodev">
          facebook page
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Im about to tattoo this design on a regular customer and I figured I would post the image I created from a sketch in case it interests anyone else as a tattoo reference.
        </p>
        <p>
         I am giving it away free. All I ask if that you do not use it for commercial purposes. Ive already done that by the way.

        <p>
         As you can see, this image is of a voodoo doll with pins puncturing his heart that is bleeding out onto a spool of thread into a reflecting pool of blood at the bottom.
