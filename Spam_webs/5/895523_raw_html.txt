      <div class="eza-body clearfix p402_premium" id="story-body">
       <p>
        <span class="eza-dateline all_caps" id="dateline">
         Washington 
        </span>
        The
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/United+States" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         United States
        </a>
        said on Wednesday that
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/North+Korea" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         North Korea
        </a>
        had agreed to a moratorium on nuclear tests and long-range missile launches and to allow nuclear inspectors to visit its
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Yongbyon" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         Yongbyon
        </a>
        nuclear complex to verify a halt to all nuclear activities including uranium enrichment.
       </p>
       <p>
        The U.S. announcement paves the way for the possible resumption of six-party disarmament negotiations with
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Pyongyang" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         Pyongyang
        </a>
        and follows talks between U.S. and North Korean diplomats in
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Beijing" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         Beijing
        </a>
        last week.
       </p>
       <p>
        "To improve the atmosphere for dialogue and demonstrate its commitment to denuclearization, the DPRK has agreed to implement a moratorium on long-range missile launches, nuclear tests, and nuclear activities at Yongbyon, including uranium enrichment activities," the
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/U.S.+Department+of+State" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         State Department
        </a>
        said in a statement.
       </p>
       <div class="promo_link ezc-csm_gallery">
        <span class="recommended_designation">
         Recommended:
        </span>
        <a href="/Photo-Galleries/In-Pictures">
         <span class="gal kicker">
          In Pictures
         </span>
        </a>
        <a href="/Photo-Galleries/In-Pictures/Kim-Jong-un-Mystery-Man">
         <span class="story_link">
          Kim Jong-un: Mystery Man
         </span>
        </a>
       </div>
       <p>
        "The DPRK has also agreed to the return of
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/International+Atomic+Energy+Agency" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         IAEA
        </a>
        inspectors to verify and monitor the moratorium on uranium enrichment activities at Yongbyon and confirm the disablement of the 5-MW reactor and associated facilities," it said.
       </p>
       <div class="pull_right p402_hide" id="story-embed-column">

       <p>
        The Democratic Peoples
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/South+Korea" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         Republic of Korea
        </a>
        (DPRK) is North Korea's official name.
       </p>
       <p>
        The State Department said that in return the United States was ready to finalize details of a proposed food aid package of 240,000 metric tonnes of nutritional assistance, and that more aid could be agreed based on continued need.
       </p>
       <p>
        "The United States still has profound concerns regarding North Korean behavior across a wide range of areas, but today's announcement reflects important, if limited, progress in addressing some of these," a State Department statement said.
       </p>
       <p>
        It said
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Washington%2c+DC" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         Washington
        </a>
        reaffirmed that it did not have hostile intentions toward North Korea and was prepared to take steps to improve bilateral ties and increase people-to-people exchanges.
       </p>
       <p>
        The announcement followed the sit-down negotiations between the United States and with North Korea last week in Beijing, the first such meeting since the death of its longtime leader
        <a class="inform_link" href="/tags/topic/Kim+Jong-il" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
         Kim Jong-il
        </a>
        in December.
       </p>
       <p>
        The U.S. special envoy for North Korea, Glyn Davies, told reporters those talks made some progress on issues including nuclear non-proliferation, North Korea's demands for food aid and other issues at the heart of regional tension. The talks are aimed at laying the groundwork for renewed six-party disarmament negotiations with North Korea, whose ties with South Korea have deteriorated, especially after deadly attacks on the South in 2010.

        </span>
       </p>
