       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="post-meta">
       <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author posted">
        Posted on
       </span>
       <a href="http://dubselector.com/dub-in-t-o/dubn-it-up-in-toronto/" rel="bookmark" title="1:14 am">

      <!-- end of .post-meta -->
      <div class="post-entry">
       <p>
        Gladly, there has been quite a resurgence of dub happening in the city. Thanks to some hard working promotion and selecting by the likes of Pressure Drop &amp; K-Zar, there have been monthly events that have been nothing but amazing. So much so, that Ive decided to revive the reviews on the site. There has been no activity here since 2008, but I intend to revive and archive some of the writings that first appeared on the site, dating back to 2005.
       </p>
       <p>
        In addition, Ill have the backing of Laurence T. Roberts providing design support to the site, so even if the reading gets a bit dry, at least well look good!
       </p>
       <p>
        Stay posted.
       </p>
