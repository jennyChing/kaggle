       <h1 class="title">
        {Holiday Recipe} Coconut Milk Hot Chocolate
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta">
        <span class="small">
         By
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://friskylemon.com/author/allienic/" rel="author" title="Posts by Allison">
           Allison
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="small">
         on
        </span>
        <abbr class="date time published" title="2012-12-18T04:59:58-0400">
         December 18, 2012
        </abbr>
        <span class="small">
         in
        </span>
        <span class="categories">
         <a href="http://friskylemon.com/category/sweet-treats/">
          Sweet Treats
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- Top of post -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-7778098591739049" data-ad-slot="2057427614" style="display:inline-block;width:728px;height:90px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
         I have a secret list of things.
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, I have lots of secret lists of things.
        </p>
        <p>
         One of these secret lists is titled Must Haves for my Dream Home.
        </p>
        <p>
         You see, Im manifesting my dream homeits somewhere out there.
        </p>
        <p>
         On the top of this list is:
        </p>
        <h2>
         Fireplace/Wood Stove
        </h2>
        <p>
         I love the smell of wood smoke.
        </p>
        <p>
         I love the idea of curling up by a warm fire.
        </p>
        <p>
         I love the idea of M doing wood stove/fireplace maintenance so that I can keep all of my fingers and avoid third degree burns
        </p>
        <h3>
         Back to the curling up by a warm fire.
        </h3>
        <p>
         The warmth of a fire is so cozy and comforting to me.
        </p>
        <p>
         Especially during the holiday season.
        </p>
        <p>
         I imagine myself curling up with my book and a mug of hot cocoa with some Nat King Cole Christmas carols playing the in background.
        </p>
        <p>
         So I decided to make some hot chocolate.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Coconut milk Hot Chocolate as seen in the
         <a href="http://hannahmarcotti.com/2012/11/03/the-holiday-joy-up-2012/">
          Holiday Joy Up
         </a>
         .
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4470_1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5922" height="400" src="http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4470_1.jpg" title="IMG_4470_1" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://friskylemon.com/2012/12/18/holiday-recipe-coconut-milk-hot-chocolate/&amp;media=http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4470_1.jpg&amp;description={Holiday Recipe} Coconut Milk Hot Chocolate | http://friskylemon.com/2012/12/18/holiday-recipe-coconut-milk-hot-chocolate/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         This recipe is delicious and so easy to make.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4463_1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5923" height="400" src="http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4463_1.jpg" title="IMG_4463_1" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://friskylemon.com/2012/12/18/holiday-recipe-coconut-milk-hot-chocolate/&amp;media=http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4463_1.jpg&amp;description={Holiday Recipe} Coconut Milk Hot Chocolate | http://friskylemon.com/2012/12/18/holiday-recipe-coconut-milk-hot-chocolate/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         I use the term recipe very loosely. I find that everyone likes their hot chocolate a little different, so theres lots of room for creativity.
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          {Holiday Recipe} Coconut Milk Hot Chocolate
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://friskylemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/IMG_4470_1.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://friskylemon.com/easyrecipe-print/5921-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Allison Nichols
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup canned organic coconut milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 to 2 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder (depending on how chocolatey you want it)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             to 1 tsp grade B maple syrup (depending on how sweet you want it)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Cacao nibs or cinnamon for sprinkling (optional)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small saucepan, bring your water and coconut milk to a simmer.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Whisk in the cocoa powder, vanilla and maple syrup.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Once well combined, pour into your mug and top with cacao nibs or cinnamon if you like.
           </li>
          </ol>
