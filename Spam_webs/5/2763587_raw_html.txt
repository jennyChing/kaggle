    <font color="#2A188B">
     The democracy
illusion
    </font>
   </center>
  </h2>
  <blockquote>
   <font face="Times" size="+2">
    The democracy has ceased to
   be a reality.
   </font>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     The chairmen of the organisations
   which hold the real power are not elected, and the public is not
   informed about their decisions.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     The room for maneouvre of the
   nation-states is getting more and more restricted by economic
   international agreements about which people have never been
   neither consulted nor informed.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     All these treaties, elaborated
   during the last five years (GATT, WTO,
    </font>
    <a href="http://www.oecd.org/daf/cmis/mai/maindex.htm">
     <font face="Times" size="+2">
      MAI
     </font>
    </a>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     )
   are directed at a single aim: the transfer of political power from
   states to non-elected organisations.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     A violent takeover would have
   certainly caused a revolution. That is why it have been decided to
   keep the appearances of democracy, and
    </font>
    <font color="#5A6FE8" face="Times" size="+2">
     <b>
      shift the
   real power toward new
   centers
     </b>
    </font>
    <font color="#4B59CE" size="+2">
     .
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font color="#5A6FE8" face="Times" size="+2">
     <b>
      The citizens
   are still voting, but their votes have been deprived of all
   meaning. They vote for leaders in charge of practically
   nothing
     </b>
    </font>
    <font color="#4B59CE" face="Times" size="+2">
     .
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     And if the political programs from
   "right" and "left" are getting so similar, it's just because there
   is nothing to decide anymore.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font color="#4B59CE" face="Times" size="+2">
     To sum up, we
   don't have the choice the dish, but we are free to choose the
   sauce. The dish is called "new slavery", with right chilli sauce
   or left bitter-sweet sauce
    </font>
    <font size="+2">
     .
    </font>
   </p>
  </blockquote>

     The removal of information
news
    </font>

  </h2>
  <blockquote>
   <font face="Times" size="+2">
    Since the beginning of the
   90's, the information news are progressively desappearing from
   great audience medias.
   </font>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     As for elections, the TV news
   still exist, but thet have been deprived of their meaningfull
   contents.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     An average TV news bulletin
   contains a maximum of 2 or 3 minutes of real information. The rest
   is made of magazine subjects, anecdotal reports, short news items,
   interviews of the public, and reality-shows about ordinary
   life.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     The editorials or analysis have
   almost completely been removed.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     The information remains in
   newspapers, read by only a few people.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     The removal of information is the
   tangible sign that our political system have already changed of
   nature.
    </font>
   </p>
  </blockquote>

   <font face="Times" size="+2">
    The leaders of the economic
   power come from the same worlds, belong to the same social

   same views and the same interests.
   </font>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     So they share the same vision of
   what the ideal future world should be.
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font face="Times" size="+2">
     Therefore it is natural for them
   to agree on a strategy and synchronize their respective actions
   toward common global objectives, inducting economic situations
   that are favourable for the fulfilment of their
   objectives:
    </font>
   </p>
   <blockquote>
    <img align="bottom" height="12" src="../Images/BlueBall.jpg" width="12"/>
    <font size="+1">
     Weakening
      of the gouvernments. Deregulation. Privatization of the public
      services.
    </font>
    <p>
     <img align="bottom" height="12" src="../Images/BlueBall.jpg" width="12"/>
     <font size="+1">
      Disengagement
      of states from the economy (including education or police,
      destined to become frofitable markets for private
      compagnies)
     </font>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img align="bottom" height="12" src="../Images/BlueBall.jpg" width="12"/>
     <font size="+1">
      Maintaining
      of a hight level of unemployment, sustained by relocations and
      globalization. This increase the economic pressure on the
      salaried workers, who are then ready to accept any wages or
      working conditions.
