        <h3>
         IFTTT
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="IFTTT" class="size-full wp-image-6263 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://bloggingwizard.adamconnell1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/top-blogging-productivity-tools-ifttt.jpg" width="400"/>
         <a href="https://ifttt.com/" target="_blank">
          IFTTT
         </a>
         stands for if this then that.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its a tool that allows you to create connections between various online services, anything from Facebook to Evernote. These are called
         <em>
          channels
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         You can search for
         <em>
          recipes
         </em>
         , this link connect channels together to perform automated tasks.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are a few examples:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <em>
           If you get a new Twitter follower, they are invited to connect on Linkedin.
          </em>
         </li>
         <li>
          <em>
           If I get an email through Gmail that has an attachment, save it to my Google Drive account.
          </em>
         </li>
         <li>
          <em>
           If a new blog post is published (via the RSS feed you choose), add it to my Pocket account.
          </em>
         </li>
         <li>
          <em>
           If I post a photo to Instagram, publish it to my Tumblr account.
          </em>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          How it will help you be more productive:
         </strong>
         Automate countless tedious tasks that would otherwise take you a very long time.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Price:
         </strong>
         Free.
        </p>
