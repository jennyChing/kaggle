         <p>
          This year has been a fantastic one for the UK car market. Estimations have been smashed month after month as the slow, but steady, economic recovery has bolstered consumer confidence and therefore their investment in new vehicles.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-749">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          With UK new car registrations falling just short of 2 million between January the 1st and October the 31st this year has seen a 10% increase on the year before. It is believed that the number will hit 2.25 million by the end of this year. But what cars have been stealing the lions share of those sales? And how much would they set you back to lease from us? Find out all that, and more, here!
         </p>
         <h2>
          The Top 10 Best Selling Cars of 2013 (So far)
         </h2>
         <h3>
          10. Peugeot 208  approx. 35,000 units
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Peugeot-208-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg">
           <img alt="Peugeot 208 for best selling cars of 2013 article" class="wp-image-755 aligncenter" height="397" src="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Peugeot-208-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg" width="560"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          Number 10 on the list comes in the form of the Peugeot 208 supermini. Given an average of 4 stars out of 5 by WhatCar? users the 208 holds a respectable chunk of the lucrative supermini market with its unique styling, competitive pricing and low running costs.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://carleasingmadesimple.com/business-car-leasing/peugeot/208-hatchback/">
           Lease the Peugeot 208
          </a>
          with us from 131.92 per month
         </p>
         <h3>
          9. Volkswagen Polo  approx. 36,000 units
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Volkswagen-Polo-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg">
           <img alt="Volkswagen Polo for best selling cars of 2013 article" class="wp-image-759 aligncenter" height="398" src="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Volkswagen-Polo-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg" width="560"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Volkswagen Polo has been a firm favourite for many years. With a rating of 4 out of 5 stars by both WhatCar? and its users it seems its popularity is unlikely to dwindle any time soon. One of the least supermini looking of the superminis on the list the Polo bridges the gap between Golf looks and small car credentials.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://carleasingmadesimple.com/business-car-leasing/volkswagen/polo-hatchback/">
           Lease the Volkswagen Polo
          </a>
          with us from 125.83 per month
         </p>
         <h3>
          8. BMW 1 series  approx. 37,000 units
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/BMW-1-Series-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg">
           <img alt="BMW 1-Series for best selling cars of 2013 article" class="wp-image-750 aligncenter" height="396" src="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/BMW-1-Series-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg" width="560"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          The first BMW to appear on our list, the 1 Series will tick a lot of boxes for many. Of the smaller vehicles it is one of the more executive cars with fairly classic BMW styling in a much smaller package. Following the trend towards more economical vehicles the 1 Series boasts a great MPG. It was also awarded 4 out of 5 stars by both WhatCar? and its readers.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://carleasingmadesimple.com/business-car-leasing/bmw/1-series-hatchback/">
           Lease the BMW 1 Series
          </a>
          with us from 189.98 per month
         </p>
         <h3>
          7. BMW 3 series  approx. 37,000 units
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/BMW-3-Series-for-top-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg">
           <img alt="BMW 3-Series for top selling cars of 2013 article" class="wp-image-751 aligncenter" height="398" src="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/BMW-3-Series-for-top-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg" width="560"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          The 3 Series is the 1 Series bigger, older and slightly more imposing brother. WhatCar?s Executive Car of the Year and owner of a coveted 5 star rating the 3 Series is by far the most executive and business like vehicle on the list.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://carleasingmadesimple.com/business-car-leasing/bmw/3-series-saloon_6/">
           Lease the BMW 3 Series
          </a>
          with us from 266.41 per month
         </p>
         <h3>
          6. Nissan Qashqai  approx. 45,000 units
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Nissan-Qashqai-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg">
           <img alt="Nissan Qashqai for best selling cars of 2013 article" class="wp-image-754 aligncenter" height="399" src="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Nissan-Qashqai-for-best-selling-cars-of-2013-article.jpg" width="560"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          As the original compact-crossover the Nissan Qashqai has sold over 2 million units worldwide since its 2007 launch. Demand for it doesnt seem to be abating either. 4 star ratings all round come from WhatCar? and as one of our top sellers we know just how happy our customers are with it. It has also seen a redesign for a 2014 release, find out more information about the
          <a href="http://news.carleasingmadesimple.com/the-new-nissan-qashqai-2014-has-been-revealed/">
           new Nissan Qashqai
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://carleasingmadesimple.com/business-car-leasing/nissan/qashqai-hatchback/">
           Lease the Nissan Qashqai
          </a>
          with us from 145.99 per month
         </p>
         <h3>
          5. Volkswagen Golf  approx. 56,000
         </h3>
         <p>
