            </h1>
           </div>

            Make sure these dining room updates are on your wish list.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="row" id="post-4118">
           <div style="position: relative;">
            <p>
             <img alt="dining-room-wish-list" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4124" height="500" src="http://www.luxdeco.com/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/dining-room-wish-list.jpg" width="940"/>

        <div class="body-container">
         <article class="article-body padding16">
          <h2 class="normalcase globalmargin margintop24 text-center">
          </h2>
          <div class="row">
           <div class="col-sm-12 post-4118">
            <p>
             Of the 365 dinners you have every year, December 25ths is undoubtedly the zenith of your gastronomic adventures. Christmas dinner  this most holy and looked-forward-to of family gatherings  is your
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/dining-room/" target="_blank">
               dining room
              </a>
             </u>
             s finest moment. You spend weeks perfecting the menu, another week or so tracking down the perfect bird (of the poultry variety, that is) and you make sure that youve covered all the bases  turkey and chicken to satisfy those who can tell the difference, a spare dessert in case something goes awry and crackers which hopefully wont put someones eye out.
            </p>
            <p>
             Before all that hard work though, dont go forgetting that setting the scene is just as important as how well your food goes down. Do as all smart host and hostesses with the mostest do and think ahead about prepping your
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/dining-room" title="Dining Room Furniture">
               dining room
              </a>
             </u>
             for the celebrations of the festive season  as well as the revelries of the New Year  in advance.
            </p>
            <p>
             Decked out in a pristine dinner service, glistening crystalware and fabulous centrepieces, your table spread should be as palette-whetting as your cuisine. Not to mention the foundations to any great entertaining experience  an extendable table large enough to fit your family and friends, comfortably
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/dining-room/dining-chairs" title="Stylish Dining Rooms">
               stylish chairs
              </a>
             </u>
             that look like theyve come straight from a Michelin star restaurant and a
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/cabinets-storage/sideboards/" target="_blank">
               sideboard
              </a>
             </u>
             to house
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/serveware/" target="_blank">
               dishes
              </a>
             </u>
             during courses.
            </p>
            <p>
             So whether youre a traditional turkey and all the trimmings type or a minimalist haute cuisine foodie, get ready to eat, drink and have a merry Christmas with these hand-picked pieces.
            </p>
            <h2 style="text-align: center;">
             Traditional Glamour
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="PalazzoPitti-Sideboard-2" class="size-full wp-image-4134 aligncenter" height="386" src="http://www.luxdeco.com/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/PalazzoPitti-Sideboard-2-e1382112370111.jpg" width="778"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             For a look that really sparkles, think Christmas dinner with all the gold trimmings. Starting with a rich wooden table like
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/brands/selva" target="_blank">
               Selva
              </a>
             </u>
             s
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/palazzopitti-dining-table" target="_blank">
               Palazzo Pitti
              </a>
             </u>
             design means you can afford to load up on gilded accents.
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/brands/moser" target="_blank">
               Moser
              </a>
             </u>
             s
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/catalogsearch/result/?q=splendid" target="_blank">
               Splendid dinner service
              </a>
             </u>
             is an easy way to work the look and wont look out of place all year round.
             <br/>
            </p>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <h2 style="text-align: center;">
             Perfectly Purist
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="Big-Table---Venere" class="size-full wp-image-4130 aligncenter" height="341" src="http://www.luxdeco.com/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Big-Table-Venere-e1382112212716.jpg" width="778"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             For fans of the non-traditional, a minimalistic palette is a winner. Let your festive fare speak for itself by serving it up in
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/brands/rosenthal" target="_blank">
               Rosenthal
              </a>
             </u>
             s crisp white, chef-designed
             <u>
              <a href="http://www.luxdeco.com/catalogsearch/result/?q=In.gredienti" target="_blank">
               In.gredienti
              </a>
             </u>
             porcelain plates alongside these statuesque crystal champagne flutes. Colour can be used as an accent but go sparingly for a bold impact.
             <br/>
            </p>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <h2 style="text-align: center;">
             Sophisticated Palette
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="z-waldorf-table-2_2x" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4135" height="442" src="http://www.luxdeco.com/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/z-waldorf-table-2_2x.jpg" width="780"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Keep the fashionable contingent as well as the foodies happy this year by having a very (sophisticated) Versace Christmas. The brands iconic Medusa-clad crystalware will be an instant conversation starter whilst the monochrome button-tufted Dolly dining chair will add a touch of winter white to your decor.
             <br/>
            </p>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <h2 style="text-align: center;">
             Contemporary Classic
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="HARRY-dinner-table-ALLEGRA-dinner-chair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4133" height="508" src="http://www.luxdeco.com/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/HARRY-dinner-table-ALLEGRA-dinner-chair.jpg" width="780"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Dark woods and winter are a style no-brainer. With plenty of depth and drama, the high-gloss Harry dining table crafted from Macassar ebony  and matching chair will fill your dining room with a comforting sense of intimacy perfect for smaller get-togethers. Keep tableware flawless and simple.
             <br/>
            </p>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <div class="products">
            </div>
            <h2 style="text-align: center;">
             Monochrome Elegance
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="f495eed2a1baa313a1572b845f4a7650" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4132" height="495" src="http://www.luxdeco.com/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/f495eed2a1baa313a1572b845f4a7650-e1382112162839.jpg" width="778"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Black and white  the ever-in-style colour duo  is an effortlessly classic look for any time of the year but will certainly wow your guests this yuletide. As an alternative to the usual red and green, imagine the Anastasia bookcase suitably bursting with sparkling silver and the Jacopo table equipped with equally shiny silverware.
             <br/>
            </p>
