        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Lasagna Grilled Cheese
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-08-20T20:02:32+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          August 20, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://eatbetterspendless.com/author/bethcran4d/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Beth Cranford
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://eatbetterspendless.com/lasagna-grilled-cheese/#comments">
           12 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="4660740" data-link="http://eatbetterspendless.com/lasagna-grilled-cheese/" data-summary="" data-title="Lasagna Grilled Cheese">
        </div>
        <a href="http://eatbetterspendless.com/affiliate-disclosure/" target="_blank">
         <img alt="Affiliate Disclosure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2776" height="75" src="http://eatbetterspendless.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Affiliate-Disclosure.png" width="600"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         I was having a somewhat stressful Saturdayand Saturdays are supposed to be stress-free! I had so much to do and I had no lunch plans. And because Im big on meal planning (you may have noticed) I get really frustrated when I dont have one. I hate to go out for burgers or something worse just because I didnt have a good plan. So, I was frustrated.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then my daughter said Mom, Ill take care of lunch.
         <br/>
         Stress. Gone.
        </p>
        <p>
         And then this shows up in front of me!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Lasagna Grilled Cheese; Creamy ricotta, gooey mozzarella, fresh basil and tomato. A delicious update to your grilled cheese sandwich." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1813" height="990" src="http://eatbetterspendless.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Vertical.jpg" title="Lasagna Grilled Cheese" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not kiddin, my mood went from very low to pretty much giddy.
         <br/>
         Lunch was not only taken care of it was delicious!
         <br/>
         Yes, I was overtly grateful.
        </p>
        <p>
         From the buttery crisp crunch of the grilled bread to the gooey cheese and fresh tomatoes and basil
         <br/>
         What could be wrong with this sandwich? (Im awaredont tell me, Im choosing to ignore the whiteness of the bread and I have no problem with all that cheese.)
        </p>
        <p>
         If you need a change of pace, try it, youll like it!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Lasagna Grilled Cheese" class="aligncenter" height="475" src="http://eatbetterspendless.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Horizontal1.jpg" title="Lasagna Grilled Cheese" width="645"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Lasagna Grilled Cheese
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div>
          <div class="ERSPhoto">
           <img itemprop="image" src="http://eatbetterspendless.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Vertical.jpg" width="205"/>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTopRight">
           <div class="ERSSavePrint">
            <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
             <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://eatbetterspendless.com/easyrecipe-print/1807-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              Print
             </a>
            </span>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Gooey cheese, crisp, buttery bread, fresh tomatoes and basil. Yum!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 slices of multigrain italian bread (or bread of choice)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Butter (about 2 TBSP)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup ricotta cheese
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup shredded mozzarella cheese
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8-10 leaves of fresh basil, chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            5-7 cherry tomatoes, chopped
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Chop the tomatoes and basil.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread half of the ricotta cheese on each of two pieces of bread.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Follow the ricotta with the tomatoes and basil.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add the shredded mozzarella and top with the other piece of bread.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Butter the top piece of bread and add the sandwich, buttered side down, to a hot grill pan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            butter the top bread and turn when the first side is golden brown.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Grill second side until golden brown.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Eat while hot.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="endeasyrecipe" style="display: none" title="style003">
          3.2.2158
         </div>
        </div>
