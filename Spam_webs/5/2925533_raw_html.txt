       <div class="blurb" itemprop="description">
        Nicole Richie is one serious Britney Spears fan! Check out the video of Nicole and a male friend having an impromptu dance-off to the singer's
        <em>
         Slave 4 U
        </em>
        hit.
       </div>
       <div class="body" data-article-body="">
        <p>
         <img alt="" height="268" src="http://cdn.sheknows.com/articles/2011/06/nicole-richie-britney-dance.png" style="float: right; margin-left: 8px; margin-right: 6px;" width="200"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Proving that imitation is the most sincere form of flattery,
         <a href="/tags/nicole-richie">
          Nicole Richie
         </a>
         went head-to-head in a
         <a href="/tags/britney-spears">
          Britney Spears
         </a>
         dance-off just hours before her big
         <em>
          <a href="/entertainment/articles/833773/britney-spears-femme-fatale-costumes-hot-or-not">
           Femme Fatale
          </a>
         </em>
         concert.
        </p>
        <p>
         So who won the Nicole Richie
         <em>
          Slave 4 U
         </em>
         dance-off? Watch the video below and judge for yourself!
        </p>
        <p>
         Nicole Richie shared the video of her best Britney moves Monday night, writing on her official
         <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/nicolerichie" target="_blank">
          Twitter page
         </a>
         , "In honor of my queen, @BritneySpears -- Can't wait to dance tonight!"
        </p>
        <p>
         It appears the mother of Harlow and Sparrow with
         <a href="/entertainment/articles/821366/nicole-richie-and-joel-maddens-wedding-photo-released">
          husband Joel Madden
         </a>
         was hanging out with friends before heading to Britney's concert when someone got the bright idea for a dance-off. Lucky for us cameras were rolling and Nicole was in the mood to share her Britney tribute!
        </p>
        <p>
         The video, titled
         <em>
          Britney Dance Off
         </em>
         , starts off with Nicole's words, "What was that, Scotty? Do you REALLY want to challenge me to a Britney dance-off? Okkaaaaaayyyy..." From there,
         <em>
          Slave 4 U
         </em>
         music takes over, Nicole and Scotty dance head-to-head, and she appears to out-move and outlast her worthy opponent. "Who's next?" the video concludes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Judging by her moves, something tells us this isn't Nicole's first time working on the
         <em>
          Slave 4 U
         </em>
         choreography!
        </p>

         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/HaWpE953XDE?rel=0" width="640">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Are you impressed by Nicole Richie's Britney dance video?
