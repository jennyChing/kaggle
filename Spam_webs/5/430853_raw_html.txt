          <p style="text-align: justify;">
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Dualityis an art experiment that binds a passage of words into two different narratives.
          </p>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Art, music and literature can have an intrinsic influence on how we see the world.Individuals hear music and see art differently. Based on ouremotions, experiences and individuality,the narrative can be propelled intodifferent directions. A writer can explore sadness, rootlessness or loss which could in turn inspire a visual artist to see hope, desire or beauty.
          </p>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           With the intent to explore this concept further we invited fifty Vancouver based emerging and established artists to contribute to this exploration. Twenty-five writers, poetsand songwriters shared a sentence, a lyric or a scribble from their note book. The words were then anonymously paired with a visual artist along with a blank archival rag sheet and seven days to bring tangibility to their words.
          </p>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <h2 style="text-align: left;">
           Words canelicitmany emotions;
Dualitywill set out to explore this sentiment.
          </h2>
