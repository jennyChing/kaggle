           <div class="flexslider boxed_shadow" id="single_slider">
            <ul class="slides" data-autoplay="true">
             <li class="" id="slide_1">
              <img height="480" src="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Insurance-640x480.jpg" width="640"/>

           <div class="on_colored twelve centered columns" id="single_post_content">
            <div class="nch-lb-mediacontent" data-wrap="post3273">
             <p>
              <em>
               Our data for the insurance aggregator market shows that, since March 2014, Compare the Market and Money Supermarket have experienced a declining share of voice  how can they turn this troubling trend around?
               <span id="more-3273">
               </span>
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              With the March number plate change encouraging new car purchases (and hence searches for car insurance)  people rush to keywords like car insurance. However, in April of last year, big players like Money Supermarket and Compare the Market took historically large hits to their respective performances. Lets take a close look at this trend and see how these companies can stay ahead this March.
             </p>
             <h3>
              The March Descent
             </h3>
             <div class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2583" style="width: 500px;">
              <figure>
               <a class="nch-lightbox" data-width="860" href="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/TopCompSOV.png" rel="group1" title="Top insurance aggregators share of voice, Adthena data">
                <img alt="" src="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/TopCompSOV.png" style=""/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               <figcaption style="color: grey; font-size: 10px;">
                Top insurance aggregators share of voice,
                <a href="http://adthena.com" target="_blank">
                 Adthena
                </a>
                data
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              As you can see from the share of voice graph above, in 2014, Money Supermarket suffered through significant losses following a March peak. While Compare the Markets fate was similar, it managed to bounce back significantly, overtaking its main competitor, GoCompare, for the majority of the year.
             </p>
             <p>
              As
              <a href="http://www.glassbusiness.co.uk/news-and-insights/blog-march-vs-september/" target="_blank">
               Glass Business
              </a>
              explains, the new registrations issued by the DVLA in March are more attractive to customers because they more clearly show the cars age, translating to a huge peak in new car sales.
             </p>
             <p>
              According to the
              <a href="http://www.smmt.co.uk/category/news-events/news/news-registration-cars/" target="_blank">
               Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders
              </a>
              , dealers sold 464,824 vehicles in March 2014, the years highest total, compared to 68,736 in February.
             </p>
             <p>
              This month, therefore, represents an extremely competitive time for PPC. But that doesnt explain why Compare the Market and Money Supermarket suffered so terribly afterwards.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Keywords
             </h3>
             <div class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2583" style="width: 500px;">
              <figure>
               <a class="nch-lightbox" data-width="860" href="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/KeywordTraffic.png" rel="group1" title="Top insurance aggregators keyword traffic, Adthena data">
                <img alt="" src="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/KeywordTraffic.png" style=""/>
               </a>
               <figcaption style="color: grey; font-size: 10px;">
                Top insurance aggregators keyword traffic,
                <a href="http://adthena.com/">
                 Adthena
                </a>
                data
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              As you can see, traffic for the keywords car insurance and cheap car insurance rocked back and forth in March, the former dropping from 64,000 clicks to 24,000. Still, all of the top competitors  with the exception of Money Supermarket  bid on these keywords.
             </p>
             <p>
              Insurance, however, proved quite a bit more stable, establishing itself a useful bolt-hole once the March rush was over for competitors trying to claw back some revenue on other types of insurance.
             </p>
             <p>
              The overall estimated cost per click shot through the roof in March, then plummeted afterwards along with overall spend. In March, brands spent seven times the February total on cheap car insurance, with Money Supermarkets jump in spend proving even more extreme.
             </p>
             <p>
              So did Money Supermarket burn its PPC cash in the prime month, forcing it to tighten its belt in the months that followed?
             </p>
             <div class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2583" style="width: 500px;">
              <figure>
               <a class="nch-lightbox" data-width="860" href="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GoCompare-ComparetheMarket-MoneySupermarket.png" rel="group1" title="GoCompare, ComparetheMarket, MoneySupermarket, keyword head to head, Adthena">
                <img alt="" src="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GoCompare-ComparetheMarket-MoneySupermarket.png" style=""/>
               </a>
               <figcaption style="color: grey; font-size: 10px;">
                GoCompare, ComparetheMarket, MoneySupermarket, keyword head to head,
                <a href="http://adthena.com/" target="_blank">
                 Adthena
                </a>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              As you can see from the head-to-head above (comparing the keywords on which the top three brands bid) their keyword portfolios are pretty similar, so its likely that the difference in performance is down to the weight of bids across their whole keyword sets.
             </p>
             <div class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2583" style="width: 500px;">
              <figure>
               <a class="nch-lightbox" data-width="860" href="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GoCompareKeywords.png" rel="group1" title="GoCompare keyword traffic, Adthena data">
                <img alt="" src="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/GoCompareKeywords.png" style=""/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               <figcaption style="color: grey; font-size: 10px;">
                GoCompare keyword traffic,
                <a href="http://adthena.com/">
                 Adthena data
                </a>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              As such, it looks like Money Supermarket and Compare the Market blew a whole load of cash in March, grabbing traffic from keywords that soared during this month, then holding back afterwards.
             </p>
             <p>
              Looking at GoCompares performance, we see that, while clicks from car insurance fell in April, they bid such that insurance hits stepped into the breech. The traffic is there; the two giant brands just arent bidding on it.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Boosting Efficiency
             </h3>
             <p>
              So, how can Money Supermarket and Compare the Market restrain their spend while getting results this March, allowing them to keep it up in April and beyond? Working on the ad copy might help.
             </p>
             <div class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2583" style="width: 500px;">
              <figure>
               <a class="nch-lightbox" data-width="860" href="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Adcopy.png" rel="group1" title="Insurance aggregators top ads, Adthena">
                <img alt="" src="http://d1yxk5wee00vef.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Adcopy.png" style=""/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               <figcaption style="color: grey; font-size: 10px;">
                Insurance aggregators top ads,
                <a href="http://adthena.com/">
                 Adthena
                </a>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              Take a look at the copy above: only one of Money Supermarkets ads made the top 10. Great ad copy is indispensable  the right wording will boost your quality score and render clicks cheaper. Meanwhile, once Google sees that a particular ad is doing well, your CPC for that ad will drop as well.
             </p>
             <p>
              Large brands like Money Supermarket and Compare the Market risk, in creating a huge campaign with lots of ads and a wad of cash, that some of the all-important PPC subtlety could be lost. So learn from their mistakes and check out Adthenas ad copy analysis to keep your PPC subtle and cheap, and the money saved on the CPC can then be pumped into other keywords.
             </p>
             <p>
              It looks like our two top brands adopted a drastic hit-and-run approach for the car insurance season  forcing them to dial it back somewhat over the following months. Resources are finite, of course, but the dropoff doesnt have to be this extreme.
             </p>
             <p>
              Adthenas competitive analysis system can locate opportunities and niches that put your PPC ahead of the game and help you to predict and master seasonality such as new car registrations.
             </p>
