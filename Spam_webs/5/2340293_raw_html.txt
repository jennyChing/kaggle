             <p>
              Leaving for college makes you excited. Youre finally out on your own for the first time. Nobodys around to tell you what to do, and you can finally make decisions and take care for yourself. The only problem? Living in the dorms. These usually small, not-so-fancy rooms could use some improvement. So, its up to you to make the dorm a much more livable place. After all, youre the only one taking care of you now. Luckily, you can find all kinds of gadgets that improve dorm room living. Lets check out a few of them right here.
             </p>
             <h2>
              1. Microwave
             </h2>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
               <img alt="Microwave - Dorm Room Essentials" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11019" height="406" src="//www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Microwave-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x406.jpg" width="610"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/&amp;media=http://www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Microwave-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x406.jpg&amp;description=7 Gadgets College Students Need in Their Dorms | http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              I cant tell you how many times I wished I had gotten a microwave for my dorm. Especially if youre on a meal plan, which is generally standard for dorm living, you might have some trouble, or too many limitations when finding a late night snack. Having a microwave certainly opens up your options. Also, you dont want to have to go all the way to the cafeteria every time you want some hot food. Plus, now you have a way to heat up any leftovers you take home.
             </p>
             <p>
              You can find perfectly suitable microwaves for incredibly cheap prices. No need to go all out on the most expensive one you can find.
             </p>
             <h2>
              2. Nice Speakers
             </h2>
             <p>
              Nice speakers are an absolute requirement. You dont have to get huge ones; in fact, the school probably wouldnt appreciate it too much if you did. If you can afford wireless ones, though, that certainly helps, as dorms dont always provide great room or surface space for all of your things. Just make sure you dont blast music at inappropriate times and get kicked out.
             </p>
             <h2>
              3. Good Laptop
             </h2>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
               <img alt="Typing on a Laptop - Dorm Room Essentials" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11020" height="406" src="//www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Typing-on-a-Laptop-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x406.jpg" width="610"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/&amp;media=http://www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Typing-on-a-Laptop-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x406.jpg&amp;description=7 Gadgets College Students Need in Their Dorms | http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              These days, you cant really go off to college without a laptop. In fact, the quality of your laptop can make an incredible difference in your experience with schoolwork. Most work required of students happens on the computer anyways. Youll get a school e-mail, get assigned online tests and quizzes, have to post to discussion boards, and refer to previous lectures as many professors these days post their lectures online for review.
             </p>
             <p>
              Not to mention, with a laptop, you can keep yourself pretty entertained throughout the school year. And dont worry, not all laptops break the bank. If you want to find a cheaper, but perfectly suitable laptop for school, check out these great
              <a href="http://shop.lenovo.com/us/en/landing_pages/promos/ideapad/weekly-sale" target="_blank">
               Lenovo laptop deals
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <h2>
              4. Roomba
             </h2>
             <p>
              With nobody there to clean your room (or to tell you to), youve got to take charge of it yourself. You dont want to end up sleeping in your own filth. So whats the easiest way to deal with your laziness and lack of a parent? The
              <a href="http://store.irobot.com/family/index.jsp;jsessionid=jtg1SwBYG7vLkdgBx2fCx3LpTNyv6pThR8KvQnSLHbYnXQkGppWX!1351223845?categoryId=2501652&amp;s=A-ProductAge">
               iRobot Roomba 530
              </a>
              . With its built-in dirt detection technology, this little gadget scoots itself around your floor and vacuums up all of your dust and dirt. Its like they made it specifically for college students.
             </p>
             <h2>
              5. Coffee Maker
             </h2>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
               <img alt="Coffee Pot - Coffee Maker - Dorm Room Essentials" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11021" height="403" src="//www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Coffee-Pot-Coffee-Maker-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x403.jpg" width="610"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/&amp;media=http://www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Coffee-Pot-Coffee-Maker-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x403.jpg&amp;description=7 Gadgets College Students Need in Their Dorms | http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Trust me; this saves you a fortune. Many students dont realize how much they love coffee until they start taking those touch college classes early in the morning, and have those late nights studying for finals. You dont want to have to spend all your money at Starbucks every day. This way, you dont have to leave your room if youre in the middle of a long study session to get coffee, its right at your fingertips. Plus, with a coffee maker, you can share with your roommate, which theyll greatly appreciate  just be sure to check with your RA to make sure youre allowed to have one in your room!
             </p>
             <h2>
              6. Excellent Alarm Clock
             </h2>
             <p>
              An alarm clock seems obvious, but youve got to make sure you have one that works well for you. Especially if youre embracing the party side of college, youll need a clock that works well enough to get past the post-alcohol ears of yours in the morning and get to your brain. Phones dont always give the necessary volume for this.
             </p>
             <p>
              Its not like highschool, you dont simply get a slap on the wrist for missing an important class. Professors dont always care whether you show up, and many of them wont help you out if you miss anything important. So just make sure its loud!
             </p>
             <h2>
              7. Good Headphones
             </h2>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
               <img alt="Good Headphones - Dorm Room Essentials" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11022" height="405" src="//www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Good-Headphones-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x405.jpg" width="610"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/&amp;media=http://www.survivingcollege.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Good-Headphones-Dorm-Room-Essentials-610x405.jpg&amp;description=7 Gadgets College Students Need in Their Dorms | http://www.survivingcollege.com/7-gadgets-college-students-need-in-their-dorm-rooms/')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              For those times when speakers arent appropriate, youll need some decent headphones. Music offers one of the only ways to keep you awake, motivated, and in good spirits while simultaneously working on homework. So, when its too late or early to use your speakers, or when youre stuck in the library, your headphones will keep you company. Not to mention all the long ten or twenty minute walks to class. Having headphones for the walk makes it sound much more appealing.
             </p>
             <p>
              Make sure to prepare yourself for your dorm experience by picking up some of these excellent gadgets!
             </p>
