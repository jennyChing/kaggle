            <p>
             To make these shortbread cookies first beat 2 egg yolks and vanilla essence. Add 100 g of softened butter in it. Add 80 g sugar and beat again. After that gradually add 200 g flour into this mixture and knead a dough. Mix cocoa powder in half of the dough. Roll each dough and cut discs with a cookie cutter or small glass. Make a circle in each discs centre with a bottle cap. Make button holes with a straw. Put all the shortbread cookies on a baking tray and bake them in the oven until done.
            </p>
