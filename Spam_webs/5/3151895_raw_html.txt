       <div class="post-37460 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-news-2 tag-allison-janney tag-bella-thorne tag-mae-whitman tag-robbie-amell tag-the-duff" id="post-37460">
        <h2 class="title">
         The DUFF  censored for an entire scene! Another film cut by a distributor, not the BBFC
        </h2>
        <div class="postdate">
         Posted by
         <strong>
          Dom Robinson
         </strong>
         on  March 23rd, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="entry">
         <p>
          <a alt="the-duff" class="alignright size-full wp-image-35245" height="785" href="http://domrobinson.co.uk/dvd-fever/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/the-duff.jpg" rel="lightbox[37460]" width="530">
           <img alt="the-duff" class="alignright size-full wp-image-35245" src="http://domrobinson.co.uk/dvd-fever/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/the-duff.jpg" width="180"/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           The DUFF
          </strong>
          is a forthcoming daft comedy, released in the UK on April 6th, which doesnt refer to where-is-she-now actress Hilary Duff, Johnny Vegas-starring
          <em>
           Ideal
          </em>
          sitcom creator Graham Duff, nor Homer Simpsons favourite tipple, but actually stands for Designated Ugly Fat Friend, when a high school senior instigates a social pecking order revolution after finding out that she has been labeled the DUFF in question, to her prettier more popular friends.
         </p>
         <p>
          Starring
          <strong>
           Mae Whitman
          </strong>
          as Bianca  the DUFF, plus
          <strong>
           Bella Thorne, Robbie Amell
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           Allison Janney
          </strong>
          , this made me laugh a fair bit so it looks worth a watch upon its UK release or so I thought.
         </p>
         <p>
          Unfortunately, its yet another film which has been censored. And while its usually 20th Century Fox doing the dirty deed  the latest example being
          <a href="http://dvd-fever.co.uk/kingsman-the-secret-service-another-film-censored-for-uk-cinemas-by-20th-century-fox/" target="_blank">
           Kingsman
          </a>
           this time its Entertainment One UK Ltd.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.bbfc.co.uk/releases/duff-2015" target="_blank">
           The BBFC confirmed:
          </a>
          <span id="more-37460">
          </span>
          <br/>
          <em>
           This film was originally seen for advice, at which stage the company was advised it was likely to receive a 15 classification but that their preferred 12A could be achieved by removing a scene involving strong sex references. When the film was submitted for formal classification, this sequence had been removed and the film was classified 12A.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Their site also says that more info will be available on March 27th, but they only ever post the content of whats left, not whats cut which is described above.
         </p>
         <p>
          Hopefully the Blu-ray and DVD release will be uncut. but dont bet the farm on it. Perhaps import the US release when the time comes? At leas Blu-ray discs arent region-locked. Then again, neither are DVDs once you set your machine to circumvent them.
         </p>
         <p>
          Check out the trailer below and click on the poster for the full-size image:
          <br/>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="343" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/eraXtuAnv64" width="610">
          </iframe>
