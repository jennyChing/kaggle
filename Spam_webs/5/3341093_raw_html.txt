        <p>
         <strong>
          Aeropostale
         </strong>
         a brand from New York now conquers Manila and even other parts of the country. Last Saturday, I went all the way from the South to the North to witness the opening of
         <strong>
          P.S. from Aropostale
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         P.S. from Aropostale offers trend-right merchandise at compelling values for girls and boys ages 4 to 12.
        </p>
        <p>
         The event was held at the open area of UP Town Center. We were welcomed by a fun, colorful and carnival like design of the stage.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="540" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale1_zps7e08f9c1.jpg" width="720"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         There were activity booths where kids and kids at heart can enjoy. I had fun creating my own canvass bag, look at my finished product.
         <span id="more-3933">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale3_zps719b3333.jpg" width="691"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         If Ren was with me, I know he would have enjoyed doodling at this wall.
         <em>
          Ang galing ng mga artists!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="625" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale2_zpsc02ebd1b.jpg" width="692"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Bizu catered the event and they served overflowing finger foods.
         <em>
          Hindi mo kailangan mag-antay, ang bilis ng refill at service nila.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         There were also booths of ice cream, hotdog and nachos. I am one happy child at the event with these stuff around me.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="540" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale13_zpsc8ef248f.jpg" width="720"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The music was brought by these
         <span class="userContent">
          DJ duo D+Cash They were really awesome,
          <em>
           ang galing!
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="540" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale15_zpsff7b39a3.jpg" width="720"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         It rained during the event, but that did not stop the fashion show. Here are the clothes worn by the cute kids and young adults at the event.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="913" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale11_zpsc7762984.jpg" width="731"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          and here is one of my favorite part, these two kids wowed the audience with their super cool dancing steps.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="1004" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale12_zpsabbf6107.jpg" width="770"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Congratulations P.S from Aropostale!!!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="540" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale4_zps92d34c22.jpg" width="720"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         After the fashion show, because of the rain still pouring down, the movie viewing of How to Train a Dragon 2 did not push through.
        </p>
        <p>
         Me and my blogger friend Peachy decided to check out more clothes at the store after the fashion show. These were the clothes that was caught by my camera.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://i9.photobucket.com/albums/a53/martinezren/Aeropostale14_zpsfad90005.jpg" width="901"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thank you so much P.S. fromAropostale and Nuffnang PH for having me.
        </p>
