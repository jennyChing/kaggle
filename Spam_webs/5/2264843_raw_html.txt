        <h2>
         Pumpkin Seed Blender Bread  Paleo, GAPS
        </h2>
        <h4>
         Ingredients
        </h4>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 1/2 cups
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003J9W0UC/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B003J9W0UC&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=itsaloloth-20&amp;linkId=OMQQBDNQOKDSMDST" target="_blank">
           pumpkin seeds
          </a>
          , preferably soaked overnight and dehydrated
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 cup
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/coconutoil" target="_blank">
           coconut oil
          </a>
          , ghee, or butter (remember to cook and bake with healthy fats!)
         </li>
         <li>
          3 eggs 
          <strong>
           OR
          </strong>
           if staying away from egg whites, use 3 egg yolks and be sure to add:
          <strong>
           1/3 cup water
          </strong>
          blended with
          <strong>
           1 tablespoon
           <a href="http://lovelovething.com/gelatin" target="_blank">
            grass-fed gelatin
           </a>
          </strong>
          , blended till frothy, to the ingredients in your blender. (
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000E39LYO/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000E39LYO&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=itsaloloth-20&amp;linkId=HX435BBIG4UWUSTV" target="_blank">
           I use this to blend
          </a>
          and I love it! Its so inexpensive and convenient!)
         </li>
         <li>
          3 tablespoons
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/rawhoney" target="_blank">
           honey
          </a>
          or a little more to taste
         </li>
         <li>
          1 teaspoon
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/cinnamon" target="_blank">
           cinnamon
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          1 teaspoon
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/vanilla" target="_blank">
           vanilla
          </a>
           learn
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/homemade-vanilla/" target="_blank" title="Homemade Vanilla">
           how to make your own vanilla extract
          </a>
          !
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 teaspoon
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/seasalt" target="_blank">
           sea salt
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h4>
         Equipment
        </h4>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/blender" target="_blank">
           high speed blender
          </a>
          , or a
          <a href="http://lovelovething.com/foodprocessor" target="_blank">
           food processor
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          silicone spatula or long-handled wooden spoon
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0042H925Q/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0042H925Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=itsaloloth-20&amp;linkId=BE4NYZ26F4AWAWAI" target="_blank">
           bread pan
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Just dump everything in your blender or food processor, blend, and bake! MMM!!!" class="aligncenter wp-image-3441 size-full" height="600" src="http://lovelovething.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_5774-e1409804414422.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://lovelovething.com/pumpkin-seed-blender-bread-paleo-gaps/&amp;media=http://lovelovething.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_5774-e1409804414422.jpg&amp;description=Pumpkin Seed Blender Bread (paleo, GAPS)')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">

        <h4>
         Instructions
        </h4>
        <ol>
         <li>
          This is so simple! Combine all ingredients in blender or food processor, and blend/process until the seeds are thoroughly blended into the mixture. If youre using a high-speed blender, start at low and work your way up to high speed. It will turn quite thick and pasty  thats okay! Scrape with a spatula/spoon into a greased bread pan.
         </li>
         <li>
          Bake at 350 for 1 hour. Its done when a knife or toothpick inserted will come out clean. Allow to cool. Drizzle each slice with a little honey before serving.
         </li>
        </ol>

         Notes
        </h4>

         You can add a banana in there if you want to! Or any fruit or dried fruit.
