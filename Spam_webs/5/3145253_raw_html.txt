           </span>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              The Power Of The Heart by Baptist de Pape: Finding Answers by Opening Your Heart
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5148558920339671107" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="background-color: white; font-family: trebuchet, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 16px;">
                <i>
                 This is a sponsored post for SheSpeaks/The Power of The Heart
                </i>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-CsC12iCgYzk/VBdslgzb_MI/AAAAAAAAJuI/ikkubt2p9hI/s1600/photo%2B(62).JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-CsC12iCgYzk/VBdslgzb_MI/AAAAAAAAJuI/ikkubt2p9hI/s1600/photo%2B(62).JPG" width="300"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                I have always been one of those people who believe things happen for a reason. After reading Baptist de Pape's new book
                <i>
                 <a href="http://www.thepoweroftheheart.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  The Power Of The Heart
                 </a>
                 ,
                </i>
                I've come to realize that things not only happen for a reason but because I followed my hearts path.
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                "
                <i>
                 The Heart is the only thing we can trust.
                </i>
                " Maya Angelou
               </span>
              </div>
              <a name="more">
              </a>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                Baptist de Pape had just finished law school and was about to start his legal career with a very lucrative deal at a prestigious law firm. However, something inside him just didn't feel right with taking the offer. After watching an interview of Eckart Tolle with Oprah Winfrey, de Pape changed the course of his life completely. In the clip, Tolle suggested that we not only ask what we want from life but what life wants from
                <i>
                 us
                </i>
                . Baptist de Pape listened to his heart and embarked on a journey to create a book and film on the power of the heart. In this film/book he interviewed 18 masters of the heart including Maya Angelou, Deepak Chopra, Jane Goodall, John Gray and even Eckart Tolle himself. Each of these "Masters" contributed their love, wisdom and intelligence to this book to help us (the readers) find our hearts calling.
                <i>
                 The Power of The Heart
                </i>
                is not only a beautifully written piece by powerful spiritual teachers but it is also a guide for us all to find our purpose in life by listening with our hearts.
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <object class="BLOGGER-youtube-video" classid="clsid:D27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0" data-thumbnail-src="https://ytimg.googleusercontent.com/vi/jobk3Usi73k/0.jpg" height="366" width="420">
                <param name="movie" value="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/jobk3Usi73k&amp;source=uds">
                </param>
                <param name="bgcolor" value="#FFFFFF">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" height="366" src="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/jobk3Usi73k&amp;source=uds" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="420">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                Like I said earlier, I believe things happen for a reason. When I agreed to review this book, I knew nothing about it. The title appealed to me, so I agreed. Two days after receiving the book, while coaching my daughters soccer game, a shooting took place near the field. The girls were actually in the middle of the game when 15-20 shots were fired off. You see we live near an area that has been national news recently because of the protests/unrest that's occurring. The shooting that took place was an isolated incident but the fact that tensions are already high, this incident added fuel to the fire. My poor girls were truly traumatized by it and if matters were not bad enough, the league we play in decided our games were to continue on the same field. They actually wanted us to continue playing on the same field after only ONE week. When I received this news my heart immediately sank, I knew it was
                <b>
                 not
                </b>
                right, my heart was telling me so!
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                I expressed my feeling to the representatives of the league and felt I was hitting road blocks at every turn. The league had other options, there were schools in neighboring areas that offered their fields to us but the league reps declined. We were also not getting support from the community, like I said the area has been receiving a lot of negative attention in the news and the people in the community have been trying to save its repetition. I can not blame them, I understand that by us deserting the field it could potentially hurt the struggling area even more-so but I felt that staying there would damage the girls. My heart was telling me to "think of the children" even though I was getting pressure from everyone else. And then I happened on a quote from
                <i>
                 The Power Of The Heart
                </i>
                :
               </span>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-JgbROk1W0pg/VBeGptwYUyI/AAAAAAAAJuU/64JEwwluI38/s1600/PowerofHeart2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-JgbROk1W0pg/VBeGptwYUyI/AAAAAAAAJuU/64JEwwluI38/s1600/PowerofHeart2.jpg" width="480"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                And I knew what I had to do. I contacted every coach in the league and told them our story, they understood and was uneasy about returning to that field as well. We band together and each wrote the league requesting a move or we would forfeit the season. They had no choice but to move us. It was a victory; it was a victory for the coaches who wanted to protect the emotional well being of the girls, it was a victory for the parents who thanked us for looking out for their children but most importantly it was a victory for those little girls. They love soccer, they play their hearts out every week and all they ask for is to feel safe in this chaotic world. Now they can begin to heal at a location they feel safe in.
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                As you can tell, it was a tough week but thank goodness for
                <i>
                 The Power of The Heart
                </i>
                ! All I can say is thank you Baptist de Paul for following your heart and writing this book, thank you for providing me with a guide to following mine and thank you for giving me the courage to stand up for those little girls.
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                "To connect to your heart, become a child." -Paulo Coelho
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                Try
                <b>
                 The Three Blessing Exercise
                </b>
                from the book:
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="background-color: white; font-family: trebuchet, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 16px; text-align: left;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <i>
                 Every night for a week write down what went well, or three blessings. The three things that went well can be small or large, unimportant or important. Then next to each item answer the question "Why did this go well?"
                </i>
               </span>
              </div>
              <ul style="background-color: white; font-family: trebuchet, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 16px;">
               <li style="text-align: left;">
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <i>
                  Did you feel more grateful?
                 </i>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li style="text-align: left;">
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <i>
                  Do you see the differently during this week?
                 </i>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li style="text-align: left;">
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <i>
                  Did you feel more relaxed?
                 </i>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li style="text-align: left;">
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <i>
                  Did you feel happier?
                 </i>
                </span>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <i>
                 The Power Of The Heart
                </i>
                book and movie will be available October 7th. Visit
                <a href="http://www.thepoweroftheheart.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 The Power Of The Heart website
                </a>
                to preorder the book and find more information on the movie, book and author. Follow on
                <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thepoweroftheheart" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 Facebook
                </a>
                and/or
                <a href="https://twitter.com/powerotheart" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 Twitter
                </a>
                for all up to date news.
               </span>
