  <article class="post-2804 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-halloween tag-children tag-costume tag-halloween tag-kids">
   <div class="hero">
    <img alt="Sweet Halloween Costumes" class="hero-img" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/HeaderHero1.jpg"/>
    <header class="header-single container has-img">
     <h1 class="entry-title">
      Sweet Halloween Costumes for Kids
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-09-26T06:00:57+00:00">
       September 26, 2014
      </time>
      <p class="byline author vcard">
       By 
			Shari's Berries
      </p>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <!-- /.hero -->
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <p class="lead">
        We love treats, and kids, so this Halloween we decided to put the two together with these easy-to-make, crazy cute costumes for toddlers. Our favorite, of course, is the strawberry. Although ... we think it'd be even sweeter dipped.
       </p>
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <h4 style="text-align: left;">
         Pink Princess Cake
        </h4>
        <h5 style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Cake Costume" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2812" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/girl-in-cake-costume.jpeg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 style="text-align: center;">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://creativelychristy.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
          Creatively Christy
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         This crafty
         <a href="http://creativelychristy.blogspot.com/2013/11/halloween-costumes-2013.html" target="_blank">
          cake costume
         </a>
         gets its shape from cardboard, poster board and duct tape, things you probably have at home. Its decorated with pink felt and white fleece, plus straws for candles and multicolored felt scraps for the sprinkles.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Milk &amp; Cookies
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Milk &amp; Cookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2813" height="692" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/cookie-and-milk.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 style="text-align: center;">
         Photo Courtesy of
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/TheCostumeCafe?ref=l2-shopheader-name" target="_blank">
          TheCostumeCafe
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         Got milk? And cookies? This
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/106627930/baby-costume-toddler-costume-cookie?ref=shop_home_active_6" target="_blank">
          chocolate-chip cookie costume
         </a>
         from Etsy is easy to fit and comfortable to wear. Plus, its roomy enough for warm layers if the October weather turns chilly.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Sprinkle Doughnut
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Sprinkle Doughnut Costume" class="aligncenter wp-image-2814" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/doughnut-costume.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 align="center">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://www.throughtheeyesofthemrs.com/" target="_blank">
          Through the Eyes of the Mrs.
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         Boston Creme, chocolate glazed, frosted with sprinkles, powdered with jelly  transform your tot into their favorite doughnut with this
         <a href="http://www.throughtheeyesofthemrs.com/2013/10/diy-donut-costume.html" target="_blank">
          do-it-yourself doughnut costume
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Candy Corn
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Candy Corn Costume" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2815" height="1339" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/candy-corn-costume.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 align="center">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://www.chicaandjo.com/" target="_blank">
          Chica and Jo
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.chicaandjo.com/2010/10/18/candy-corn-costume/" target="_blank">
          Candy corn
         </a>
         is a Halloween classic. This colorful, yet simple, costume with matching treat bag is sure to be a hit at any party.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Paper Cupcake
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Paper Cupcake Costume " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2817" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/toilet-paper-cupcake.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 align="center">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://www.mommytesters.com/" target="_blank">
          Mommy Testers
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         Heres another adorable
         <a href="http://www.mommytesters.com/2013/10/easy-diy-cupcake-costume-last-minute.html" target="_blank">
          costume made from super-soft toilet paper
         </a>
         . Yes, toilet paper. This mom discovered that soft toilet tissue is the perfect texture for creating fluffy cupcake frosting. Made from craft paper and toilet tissue, this ones for you last-minute costume designers.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Ice Cream Cone
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Ice Cream Cone Costume" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2818" height="1200" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ice-cream-costume.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 align="center">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://modernkiddo.com/" target="_blank">
          Modern Kiddo
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         Since theres not too much fitting involved, this
         <a href="http://modernkiddo.com/4th-annual-modern-kiddo-best-homemade-costumes-parade/" target="_blank">
          ice cream costume
         </a>
         can be saved for younger siblings and used year after year. Made with felt and yarn, its easy to customize. If theyd rather be an ice cream cone with whipped cream and a cherry, simply swap out sprinkles for white cotton and a felt cherry.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Sweet Strawberry
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Strawberry Costume" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2819" height="938" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/strawberry-costume.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 align="center">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://stuffaverylikes.com/" target="_blank">
          Stuff Avery Likes
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         This
         <a href="http://stuffaverylikes.com/happy-halloween/" target="_blank">
          strawberry costume
         </a>
         is cheerful and bright, especially compared to those dark, moody minions of ghosts and goblins. Just right for a little cherub. Perfect for the pre-walking set, the soft felt will keep babies comfy.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Gum Ball Machine
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Gumball Machine Costume" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2820" height="987" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/gumball-machine-costume.jpg" width="800"/>
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://inchmark.squarespace.com/" target="_blank">
          inchmark
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         This charming old-school
         <a href="http://inchmark.squarespace.com/inchmark/2011/10/31/gumball-machine.html" target="_blank">
          gumball machine costume
         </a>
         will evoke the nostalgia of days past. Grandma and grandpa will love it.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Felt Strawberry Cupcake
        </h4>
        <h5 align="center">
         <img alt="Strawberry Cupcake Costume" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2821" height="1194" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/strawberry-cupcake.jpg" width="800"/>
        </h5>
        <h5 align="center">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://notsoaveragemama.com/" target="_blank">
          Not So Average Mama
         </a>
        </h5>
        <p>
         If your little girl loves dressing up, shell love this fancy
         <a href="http://notsoaveragemama.com/2011/10/15/the-cutest-cupcake-ever/" target="_blank">
          felt cupcake
         </a>
         . Colorful and festive, it will also make her easy to spot trick or treating on a dark Halloween evening.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Do you have a favorite sweet Halloween costume?
        </h4>
        <p>
         Or, is there one that you would love to make, but you havent done it yet? Tell us your favorites in the comments  and let us know how much your kids loved them, too!
        </p>
       </div>
      </main>
