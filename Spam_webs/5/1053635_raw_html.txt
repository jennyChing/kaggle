         <p>
          Hi Krmbal fans!
         </p>
         <p>
          Weve got a brand new product in the shop. Its one I know lots of people have been waiting for. We finally have
          <a href="http://krmbal.storenvy.com/collections/294177-art-prints/products/5137556-zombie-t-rex-5x7-art-print">
           Zombie T-Rex 57 art prints
          </a>
          ! Our infamous Zombie T-Rex is printed in full color and should fit in most standard 57 frames.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://krmbal.com/wp-content/uploads/ZombieTRex5x7.jpg">
           <img alt="Zombie Dinosaur" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-984" height="300" src="http://krmbal.com/wp-content/uploads/ZombieTRex5x7-226x300.jpg" width="226"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Like all Krmbal art prints, this one is printed on 100% recycled card stock. Its made in the USA from a FSC mix from responsible sources and even made using wind power.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is the perfect alternative to a Zombie T-Rex tee for kids who love Zombie T-Rex, but cant fit into our adult sizes. Plus, Zombie T-Rex makes an excellent door guardian, keeping kids rooms safe from any imaginary troublemakers.
         </p>
         <p>
          Of course, Zombie T-Rex is also great for home or office decor, for anyone who appreciates the awesomeness of the undead King of Dinosaurs. Hed certainly make your desk the coolest one in the office and hed probably guard your stapler pretty well too!
         </p>
         <p>
          Im pretty excited to finally bring this art print to the shop and expand our selection of 57 prints! Hope you guys are as excited as I am!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          PS  Zombie fans, if you havent heard

           RE: Your Brains by Jonathan Coulton
          </a>
          , go give it a listen now! 
         </p>
