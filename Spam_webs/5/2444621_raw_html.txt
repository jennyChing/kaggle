         </h1>

         <div class="wwsgd" style="display:none;">
          <p style="border:thick double pink; padding:3mm;">
           Welcome to MassholeMommy.com. You should subscribe to my
           <a href="http://massholemommy.com/feed/">
            RSS feed
           </a>
           and follow my

         <p>
          While we were in San Diego in April, we had a chance to make a stop at the The Reuben H. Fleet Science Center, which features more than 100 interactive science exhibits. My boys love that sort of thing and I wanted to take them if we ended up having a little extra time, which we did.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-outside.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center outside" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23678" height="347" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-outside-1024x512.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          When I initially told my sister in law, who lives in San Diego, that I wanted to take my boys there, the first thing she said was that the Museum of Science in Boston was much better. So,I actually read a few reviews about this place before we went because her statement had a me a little worried.
         </p>
         <p>
          Many of the reviews that I read were by adults that went to the museum and didnt enjoy it because it was geared towards children. Well, duh. That sort of museum
          <em>
           is
          </em>
          intended for children, so those had me a little baffled. And there were quite a few of them, which also made me scratch my head. But anyways, all the reviews that I read by adults that were also parents said that the Fleet Science Center was awesome, so we went.
          <span id="more-23669">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Let me just say that I am so glad we did. My boys had the best time there and it just goes to show that to each his own.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-2.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 2" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23670" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-2-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-3.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 3" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23671" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-3-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-4.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 4" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23672" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-4-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-5.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 5" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23673" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-5-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-6.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 6" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23674" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-6-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-7.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 7" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23675" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-7-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-8.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 8" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23676" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-8-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-9.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center 9" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23677" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center-9-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center1.jpg">
           <img alt="fleet science center1" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23679" height="695" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/fleet-science-center1-1024x1024.jpg" width="695"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Yes, I know..I took a lot of pictures.
         </p>
         <p>
          It was much smaller than the MOS in Boston, but it was still a great little museum. Other than the fact that there was a field trip there the day we went &amp; the place was under construction, so it was packed. Fire hazard packed, but they cleared out after a while and we almost had the place to ourselves after that. They really shouldnt have had any field trips there until the construction was over.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you are ever in the area, it is a cool place to check out with the kiddos. Its located at 1875 El Prado in San Diego, CA (right inside Balboa Park).
         </p>
