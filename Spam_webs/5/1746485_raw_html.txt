         6 Essential Travel Promotional Products
        </h1>
        <ul class="post-meta clr">

        <p>
         <img alt="travel-image" class="aligncenter wp-image-49" height="233" src="http://scorepromotions.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/travel-image.png" width="350"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         A trip should always be a smooth and enjoyable one, whether business or pleasure. However, we all go through similar situations such as finding a matching pair of socks or forgetting to pack a certain something. Even worse, you are running out of time to catch your flight. Well, have no fear! Let Score Promotions help you!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span id="more-48">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Here are the 6 must-have travel promotional products:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/product-info/24437/2200-mah-power-station-mobile-power-bank#.VG0OMvnF92I" target="_blank" title="Promotional Power Bank">
           Light-Weight Power Bank
          </a>
          <em>
           :
          </em>
         </strong>
         We have all been through the stress of a low battery alert from our cell phones. Prevent this from happening by carrying a power bank. It acts as a backup charger for your phone without having to find an actual outlet. Great for emergency use and you wont miss a single call or email again!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/products/office-and-technology/speakers/" target="_blank" title="Promotional Bluetooth Speaker">
           Bluetooth Speaker
          </a>
          :
         </strong>
         After a long day of travel, play some tunes and relax in your hotel room. Simply do this by connecting a Bluetooth speaker to your phone and BAM! You can easily listen to your whole playlist for hours.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/product-info/24348/salzburg-luggage-tag/products::bags-and-totes::luggage-and-roller-bags/" target="_blank" title="Custom Luggage Tags">
           A Bright and Bold Luggage Tag
          </a>
          :
         </strong>
         Imagine standing around the conveyor belt waiting for your luggage to arrive. Little did you know, your luggage has already arrived, but you didnt even notice because every other luggage was black, just like yours. Just attach an awesome bold and bright personalized luggage tag to your bags and avoid this situation so that you can get home in no time.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/product-info/24808/bobble-18-5-oz-filtered-water-bottle/products::drinkware-and-food::sport-and-water-bottles/#.VG0PlvnF92I" target="_blank" title="Filtered Water Bottle">
           Reusable Filtration Bottle
          </a>
          :
         </strong>
         Why pay $5 for bottled water at the hotel when you can get a personalized reusable filtration bottle that can be used up to 300 times? Its eco-friendly and a big money saver!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/product-info/13945/bugatti-ferrara-genuine-leather-overniter-briefcase-on-wheels/products::bags-and-totes::luggage-and-roller-bags/#.VG0P8fnF92I" target="_blank" title="Carry On Bags">
           A Super Efficient Carry-On Bag
          </a>
          :
         </strong>
         Save money on your overweight luggage and make your trip more efficient with a fashionable carry-on bag. Since you will be bringing your belongings with you on the plane, you will never have to worry about losing your luggage again!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/products/bags-and-totes/cosmetic-cases/" target="_blank" title="Promotional Cosmetic Case">
           Cosmetic Case
          </a>
          :
         </strong>
         If you really want to get organized and find your toiletries in one place, put it in a cosmetic case and it will definitely make your life easier.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Love these must-have travel essentials? Find them all at
         <a href="http://www.scorepromotions.ca/" target="_blank" title="Score Promotions Canda">
          scorepromotions.ca
         </a>
         !
        </p>
