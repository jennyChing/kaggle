                     It's been a long time coming, but today I
                     <u>
                      finally
                     </u>
                     cut ties from several folks that had no place in my life.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I had held back on this decision for years under the guise of
                     <i>
                      professionalism.
                     </i>
                     Simply put, I wanted to be the bigger person.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     But...It was time.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Seeing photos, tweets and Facebook updates that have bordered on bullying at best, or shared embellished lies at worst, have become negative distractions that I can't afford to waste more time with.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I have
                     <i>
                      beyond
                     </i>
                     proven that I am the bigger, and better, person. I don't need, nor want to,
                     <i>
                      go there.
                     </i>

                      <br/>

                      <br/>
                      Then I had a glass of wine. Went shopping. Came back home to only ask myself:
                      <i>
