                     <br/>
                     The Lakers won only their second game of the season last night. It was the first game of the season for
                     <b>
                      Nick Young
                     </b>
                     , who credited the "swag effect" for the win.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Swaggy P, as Young is also known as, said in his post game interview that the swag effect just rubbed off on everyone and that's why they won the game. Whatever it is I'm sure the Lakers would like more of it.
