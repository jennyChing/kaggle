					&lt;h2&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.blogofthegods.com/2007/02/08/debbie-schlussel-inspires-new-commandment-from-god/" rel="bookmark"&gt;Debbie Schlussel Inspires New Commandment from God&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
                    &lt;div class="byline"&gt;Posted by &lt;a href="http://www.blogofthegods.com/author/god/" title="Posts by God"&gt;God&lt;/a&gt; on February 8th, 2007                    in &lt;a href="http://www.blogofthegods.com/category/atheists/" title="View all posts in Atheists" rel="category tag"&gt;Atheists&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://www.blogofthegods.com/category/new-commandments-of-god/" title="View all posts in Commandments" rel="category tag"&gt;Commandments&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://www.blogofthegods.com/category/sycophant-god-flatterers/" title="View all posts in Sycophants" rel="category tag"&gt;Sycophants&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
					&lt;div class="entry"&gt;
						&lt;p&gt;The vapid conservative heroine who &lt;a href="http://www.blogofthegods.com/2007/02/06/good-little-sheep-condemn-atheism-on-cnn/"&gt;condemned atheists on CNN&lt;/a&gt; last week has &lt;a href="http://www.debbieschlussel.com/archives/2007/02/when_atheists_a.html"&gt;answered&lt;/a&gt; their bitter reaction with a post on her blog &lt;em&gt;so insightful&lt;/em&gt; it validates almost every blonde joke in the books:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;Something happened over the last 24 hours. Beginning last night, my inbox became populated with vile hate-mail from atheists. No skin off my back.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;But it is entertaining and amusing.  It&#8217;s hard to believe their letters because they were all attacking me for &lt;a href="http://www.debbieschlussel.com/archives/2007/01/live_from_new_y.html"&gt;my appearance on CNN&#8217;s &#8220;Paula Zahn Now,&#8221; a week ago&lt;/a&gt;, but coincidentally each letter claims the sender just watched me on CNN. First of all, the video of that segment appears nowhere on the net. Believe me, if it did, I&#8217;d link to it.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://www.blogofthegods.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/02/debbie2.thumbnail.jpg" alt="debbie2.jpg" class="padded" align="left" /&gt;I commend Debbie for resisting the temptation to learn about technology, for not following George W. Bush down the path of sin that leads to discovering the Google on the Internets.  Had she taken that road, she might have Googled &#8220;&lt;a href="http://www.google.com/search?sourceid=navclient-ff&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;rlz=1B2GGGL_enUS175US175&amp;q=paula+zahn+atheists"&gt;Paula Zahn atheists&lt;/a&gt;&#8221; and noticed that a &lt;a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fPHnXrU5JzU"&gt;YouTube video&lt;/a&gt; of her appearance is the top search result.  This brings Me to the first of many New Commandments I hope to deliver through My blog.  It shall be known hereafter as the Schlussel Commandment:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span id="more-35"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote class='commandment'&gt;&lt;p&gt;Thou shalt not research thine claims.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Getting your facts right leads to nothing but trouble, and if taken far enough can drive you to atheism.  Instead, make something up to please Me, and you shall be rewarded, but only after you die.  No, really.  Trust Me.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;I don&#8217;t mind receiving the atheist hate mail, since I know that in a few years, many of these same people will either be Muslim extremists (redundant) or helping the country fall further in its fight against the creep of Islamic imposition on America&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Right on!  Atheists and Muslims hold the &lt;strong&gt;exact same ideology&lt;/strong&gt;, which is that My nephew Jesus is not their saviour.  There is a very fine line between complete rejection of all things supernatural and devout acceptance of the world&#8217;s most elaborate and demanding major religion.  Nobody is more susceptible to Allah&#8217;s propaganda than people who see through Mine, and people who don&#8217;t want to go to church once a week are dying to kneel on a magic carpet and pray toward Mecca five times a day.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Debbie is so smart for pointing that out.  I&#8217;m glad CNN realized she belongs on an expert panel, while a lesser network might have assigned her to a straightjacket.  Unfortunately their good judgement did not last &#8212; they&#8217;ve called &lt;a href="http://richarddawkins.net/"&gt;noted atheist Richard Dawkins&lt;/a&gt; to appear in rebuttal on the same show tonight at 8:00pm.  What can this Oxford professor and evolutionary biologist, with his &#8220;doctorates&#8221; in &#8220;zoology&#8221; possibly have to add to a discussion about the origins of life that has not already been covered by this Wisconsin-educated lawyer?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;Look at famous atheists and what happened to them.  &lt;a href="http://www.debbieschlussel.com/columns/column052804.shtml"&gt;Adam Gadahn a/k/a Azzam Al-Amriki&lt;/a&gt;&#8211;now a top Al-Qaeda video &#8220;personality&#8221;&#8211;was raised by his hippie Jewish father and equally bizarre gentile mother as an atheist. And look how he turned out. Ditto for hippie-spawn John Walker Lindh.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Debbie again follows My newest Commandment, refusing to let facts get in the way of a good point.  While Adam Gadahn was a &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adam_Gadahn"&gt;Christian of Jewish descent who converted to Islam&lt;/a&gt; and John Walker Lindh was a &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Walker_Lindh"&gt;Catholic who converted to Islam&lt;/a&gt;, Debbie doesn&#8217;t let that stop her from listing these two men to show how all atheists run off to join Islamic terrorists.  Even&lt;strong&gt; I&lt;/strong&gt; couldn&#8217;t be that illogical, and I wrote the &lt;em&gt;Bible&lt;/em&gt; for chrissake.  If she had limited herself to &lt;a href="http://www.celebatheists.com/index.php?title=Main_Page"&gt;the facts&lt;/a&gt;, she would have had to write something like this:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;Look at famous atheists and what happened to them.  Douglas Adams, Lance Armstrong, Isaac Asimov, Dave Barry, Warren Buffett, Francis Crick, Richard Feynman, Jodie Foster, Bill Gates, H.P. Lovecraft, Sir Ian McKellen, Jack Nicholson, Ron Reagan Jr., Carl Sagan, Steve Wozniak&#8230;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;That&#8217;s almost enough to make Me not believe in Me &#8212; imagine what it would do if the public knew!  This leads Me to a clarification I&#8217;ve been meaning to make about one of My original Ten Commandments:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This one has been taken way out of context and interpreted as, &#8220;Thou shalt not lie.&#8221;  Why do you think I put &lt;em&gt;against your neighbor&lt;/em&gt; in there?  &lt;strong&gt;It&#8217;s okay to lie about other people.&lt;/strong&gt;  Also, if you&#8217;re doing it to promote My word, you can go ahead and lie about your neighbor too.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;More from Debbie:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;&#8230; as I pointed out on CNN, atheists are on the attack against religion and G-d only when Christians and Jews are involved, not when Muslims and Islam are.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Pay attention, kids.  This is the kind of divine statement you can only make if you resist the temptation of the Google!&lt;/p&gt;
