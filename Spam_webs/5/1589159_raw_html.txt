        <h1 class="title">
         Summery Chicken Salad
        </h1>
        <p class="post-meta">
         Posted  by
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/author/paula/" rel="author" title="Posts by Paula">
          Paula
         </a>
         in
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/category/recipes/" rel="category tag">
          All Recipes
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/category/poultry/" rel="category tag">
          Poultry
         </a>
         |
         <a href="http://www.bellalimento.com/2009/07/13/summery-chicken-salad/#comments">
          17 comments
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bellalimento.com%2F2009%2F07%2F13%2Fsummery-chicken-salad%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bellalimento.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2009%2F07%2Fchickensalad_wm-400x296.jpg&amp;description=Summery%20Chicken%20Salad" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span class="rdetector_placeholder_before">
         </span>
         <img alt="chickensalad_wm" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1577" height="296" src="http://www.bellalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/chickensalad_wm-400x296.jpg" title="chickensalad_wm" width="400"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I know weve all had Chicken Salad before. Most Ive had are heaping with mayonnaise (something Im not a big fan of but can deal with in small amounts), chicken, and little else. Not mine! I cant tell you how many people have asked me for this recipe after they have tasted this chicken salad. Its very different &amp; oh so refreshing with all of the fruit inside. I can always tell that they cant quite figure out what that extra little flavor is. So I just blurt out, The secret ingredient is CURRY powder!
        </p>
        <p>
         If you dont want to cook the chicken just for the salad, you can also make this with leftovers from roasted or grilled chicken, OR you could even use 2 large cans of chunk chicken breast (drained of course).
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-1560">
         </span>
         Its beautiful served on a bed of lettuce. Delicious in pita bread or served up on a toasted hoagie bun. Or if youre like me just eaten straight out of the bowl  LOL! Its even better the second day (if theres any left) because the flavors have had a chance to get to know each other a little better!
        </p>
        <p>
         If you happen to like mayonnaise feel free to add a little more, if you really like Curry powder go a little heavier on the sprinkling. If you prefer grapes throw those in as well! Either way you combine it, its the perfect addition to your summer menu!
        </p>
        <p>
         Does your chicken salad have a secret ingredient?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="signature" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-114" height="80" src="http://www.bellaalimento.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/signature.gif" title="signature" width="160"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #008080;">
          <strong>
           What youll need:
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         3-4 chicken breasts  cooked &amp; cubed (enough for your family)
         <br/>
         1 apple  diced
         <br/>
         1 small box of raisins
         <br/>
         1 can of pineapple chunks  drained
         <br/>
         1/4 cup mayonnaise
         <br/>
         1 tsp. curry powder
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #008080;">
          <strong>
           What to do:
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         In a large bowl, add your chicken, apple, raisins &amp; pineapple. Add mayonnaise &amp; stir. Sprinkle curry powder on top. Stir together well. Refrigerate for 30 minutes prior to serving.
        </p>
        <p>
         Buon Appetito!
        </p>
