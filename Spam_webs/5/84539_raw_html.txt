Sherriff"&gt;R. C. Sherriff&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Claudine_West&amp;action=edit" class="new" title="Claudine West"&gt;Claudine West&lt;/a&gt; and &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eric_Maschwitz" title="Eric Maschwitz"&gt;Eric Maschwitz&lt;/a&gt;. &lt;/div&gt;
