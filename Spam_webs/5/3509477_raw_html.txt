         Listerine 21- Day Oral Care Challenge Starts February 13th #listerine

         <span class="st_pinterest_hcount" st_title="Listerine 21- Day Oral Care Challenge Starts February 13th #listerine" st_url="http://www.mykindarain.com/reviewsgiveaways/listerine-21-day-oral-care-challenge-starts-february-13th-listerine.html">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         I have never made it a secret that I have horrible teeth. Many cavities as a child, lots of fillings, and a lot of time spent at the dentist. Even now as an adult I have had two very painful root canals recently. One root canal was simply because the tooth decayed so much that it just literally fell out of my mouth.
        </p>
        <p>
         NOT Good.
        </p>
        <p>
         As I have gotten older and had these horrible teeth issues continue I have tried to take much better care of my teeth. I do a lot of flossing. As a matter of fact, I am the mom KNOWN to always have flossers with her. Even at softball tournaments. I still struggle with brushing my teeth twice a day however. I nail the night brush but the morning brush is always forgotten in the craziness of getting up and ready for the day.
        </p>
        <p>
         This 21 day challenge is going to be just that for me, a challenge. I am determined to make sure I brush my teeth twice a day for 21 days as well as use Listerine mouth wash products to make sure I get 100% clean since brushing alone misses 75% of your mouth!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.listerine.com/21days">
          <img alt="21 day" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6672" height="311" src="http://www.mykindarain.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/21-day.jpg" width="1015"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Taking the
         <a href="http://www.listerine.com/21days">
          Listerine 21 day Challenge
         </a>
         can also help children in need. For every person who posts a Swish Selfie to Listerine Brands Facebook Page, Listerine will make a $5 contribution to Oral Health Americas Smile Across America Program, up to $85,000 toward their goal of connecting 210,000 children with needed oral health services in 2014.
        </p>
        <p>
         Another added bonus to posting your Swish Selfie? One winner will be chosen to win $500!
        </p>
        <p>
         Here is a picture of my boys doing their Swish Selfie.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mykindarain.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/boyslisterine.jpg">
          <img alt="boyslisterine" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6678" height="406" src="http://www.mykindarain.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/boyslisterine.jpg" width="547"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So what do you say? Who wants to join me in this 21 day Challenge?
        </p>
        <p>
         All you have to do is head to
         <a href="www.target.com">
          Target
         </a>
         to purchase Listerine products like I have below! And YES that is kids mouth wash my boys are using! Get your kids on board with the challenge! It is Childrens Dental Health Month after all!
        </p>

        </p>
        <p>
         Next you can check in with us at our Listerine Twitter party happening Tuesday, February 24th at noon ET (11am CT/ 9amPT). Join us using the hashtag #Listerine to chat for one hour about the 21 day Challenge, your experiences and thoughts, tips for good oral hygiene and more!
