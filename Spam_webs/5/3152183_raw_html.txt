         <h4>
          <a href="http://justforeignpolicy.org/act/iran-deal-letter">
           Does your Rep support the Iran talks?
          </a>
         </h4>
         <p>
          151 in the House have signed the Schakowsky letter commending the Iran framework agreement and supporting continued talks to bring about a final deal! This is huge. Of the 151 signers, 146 are voting members, enough to uphold a presidential veto of any legislation Republicans might try to pass to sabotage a deal. That includes any attempt to block President Obama from waiving sanctions after a deal has been concluded, which is what Republicans have been trying to do with such legislation as the Corker-Menendez bill.

        <h1 class="page_title">
         Spread the word about MoC's supporting an end to the blockade of Gaza!
        </h1>
        <div id="content_inner">
         <div class="node-blog node" id="node-1563">
          <div class="submitted">
           Submitted by Megan Iorio on 26 September 2014 - 5:50pm
          </div>
          <div class="content clear-block">
           <p>
            On September 8, Rep. Danny Davis from Chicago became the third Member of Congress to issue
            <b>
             <a href="http://www.suntimes.com/opinions/letters/29762211-474/letter-writers-logic-offbase-on-births-out-of-wedlock.html#.VCXbTCldXA4" target="_new">
              a public statement
             </a>
            </b>
            calling for an end to the blockade of Gaza, joining Reps. Keith Ellison and Barbara Lee.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Davis statement came as a result of grassroots engagement led by the Chicago-based Committee for Just Peace in Israel and Palestine (CJPIP). Their story is an inspiration to us all, showing us that, with perseverance and persuasion, it is possible to turn Members of Congress out in favor of justice in Israel/Palestine.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Israelis and Palestinians are scheduled to begin their next round of talks in late October. With Hamas and the Palestinian Authoritys announcement of a unity agreement and the transfer of control of Gaza to the Palestinian Authority, the ground is set for an agreement to end the blockade. All thats left is to build political will.
           </p>
