        <p>
         You know what they say: Its not the size that matters  its how you use it! What was once a row of ordinary postboxes has now been repurposed to become Melbournes smallest art gallery, called
         <a href="http://mailboxartspace.com/" rel="nofollow">
          MAILBOX ART SPACE
         </a>
         . Located at 141 Flinders Lanes entrance foyer, the 19 restored mailboxes all have glass fronts and LED lights, allowing passers-by to stop and take a peek at the exhibit.
         <!--more-->
        </p>
