        <h2>
         Split Tunneling
        </h2>
        <p>
         PureVPN is the only service provider with the Split Tunneling feature. It greatly assists in speeding up the VPN performance. This technique involves splitting the bandwidth; routing only specific traffic over the secure &amp; unrestricted VPN network while allowing normal traffic to directly access the internet. For instance, if YouTube is blocked in your region and Google is not; then you only require the VPN service to access YouTube and not Google  with this marvelous feature offered by PureVPN you can specify the website or web services where you want traffic to be routed through VPN while all other traffic will directly go to the internet; hence providing you a much faster connectivity.
        </p>
