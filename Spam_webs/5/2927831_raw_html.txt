         <p>
          Today I went to the cup noodle factory (&amp; museum) in Osaka, Japan. Its quite far out of the city, but after I read that
          <strong>
           you get to make your own cup noodle
          </strong>
          for 300
          <span>
           (1.70/$2.90), I just
           <em>
            had
           </em>
           to go there. You go through the whole process of decorating a cup, adding the dry noodles, soup powder &amp; your choice of 4 dry ingredients (I did pork, onion, garlic &amp; narutomaki/decorative chicks). Then the cup is all sealed up (you even get to operate the machine to seal and fill it) and you pop it into an inflatable bubble bag with a red cord to take home with you!
          </span>
         </p>
