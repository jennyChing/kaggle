             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              Zoya's Entice Fall 2014 Collection Review
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-278099287866386247">
              <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                Press Samples
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                Hello dolls! Today I have the
                <a href="http://zoya.com/content/category/Zoya-Entice-Ignite-Fall-2014.html">
                 Zoya's Entice Fall 2014 Collection
                </a>
                to share with you. This beautiful collection is full of 6 very enticing fall colors.
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-We4e0qmAWvM/U_NRoZCgxbI/AAAAAAAAIj4/UhBAD3nO48c/s1600/IMG_3001.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-We4e0qmAWvM/U_NRoZCgxbI/AAAAAAAAIj4/UhBAD3nO48c/s1600/IMG_3001.JPG" width="640"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
               </div>
               <a name="more">
               </a>
               <br/>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                Let's start with the first color I just had to try,
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <b>
                  Claire
                 </b>
                </span>
                .
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-oYiDf6_CWgQ/U_NSCwrjCJI/AAAAAAAAIkA/YjH3W9NUirM/s1600/IMG_2825.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-oYiDf6_CWgQ/U_NSCwrjCJI/AAAAAAAAIkA/YjH3W9NUirM/s1600/IMG_2825.JPG" width="480"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                Claire is described as a warm, rich burgundy. Burgundy is such a terrific fall color and Claire really is perfect, can you imagine the leaves changing already? Formula was perfect with 2 coats.
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/--Y7iDALXR-o/U_NWK-wEyuI/AAAAAAAAIkQ/HqibssEP52A/s1600/IMG_2827.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/--Y7iDALXR-o/U_NWK-wEyuI/AAAAAAAAIkQ/HqibssEP52A/s1600/IMG_2827.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4ITegpx3zKc/U_NWK6H00mI/AAAAAAAAIkM/VqQlTKc2yXk/s1600/IMG_2830.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4ITegpx3zKc/U_NWK6H00mI/AAAAAAAAIkM/VqQlTKc2yXk/s1600/IMG_2830.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Next, we have
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Nyssa
                </b>
               </span>
               .
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-pYey7DX3lGI/U_NWbCsRcEI/AAAAAAAAIkc/xbqj1FB89WA/s1600/IMG_2940.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-pYey7DX3lGI/U_NWbCsRcEI/AAAAAAAAIkc/xbqj1FB89WA/s1600/IMG_2940.JPG" width="480"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Nyssa is described as a milk chocolate brown. I am in love with all the chocolate browns this season. Nyssa is such a warm chocolate color with a wonderful formula that applying it seriously feels like putting chocolate on your fingers. This was 2 coats.
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-8EtDnmohXek/U_NXiFZTBII/AAAAAAAAIko/RXw2YQPpHFg/s1600/IMG_2947.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-8EtDnmohXek/U_NXiFZTBII/AAAAAAAAIko/RXw2YQPpHFg/s1600/IMG_2947.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-90qqre6gAoY/U_NXiIxQGdI/AAAAAAAAIkk/FFPSuJSfWCg/s1600/IMG_2948.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-90qqre6gAoY/U_NXiIxQGdI/AAAAAAAAIkk/FFPSuJSfWCg/s1600/IMG_2948.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Now, we have
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Veronica
                </b>
               </span>
               .
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-pNINybacmXU/U_NX5PbSAkI/AAAAAAAAIk4/tKw8Y4APKhI/s1600/IMG_2952.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-pNINybacmXU/U_NX5PbSAkI/AAAAAAAAIk4/tKw8Y4APKhI/s1600/IMG_2952.JPG" width="480"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Veronica is described as brilliant red wine. To me it's a pretty red-tone berry color. Like many of the Veronica's I know she's feisty, I had a bit of flooding with my middle finger and clean up wasn't the easiest but she's worth it. This was 2 coats.
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-TCkxqLo1FlA/U_NZY1srbDI/AAAAAAAAIlE/FuGCizYVUpc/s1600/IMG_2956.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-TCkxqLo1FlA/U_NZY1srbDI/AAAAAAAAIlE/FuGCizYVUpc/s1600/IMG_2956.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-OJeWM_NQnAg/U_NZZqWHCDI/AAAAAAAAIlM/sDXJmn_rmRo/s1600/IMG_2957.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-OJeWM_NQnAg/U_NZZqWHCDI/AAAAAAAAIlM/sDXJmn_rmRo/s1600/IMG_2957.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Now, we have
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Geneviev
                </b>
               </span>
               .
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-fUCaoWeQfCk/U_NZsQWNtHI/AAAAAAAAIlU/OeHIpcDuvd8/s1600/IMG_2962.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-fUCaoWeQfCk/U_NZsQWNtHI/AAAAAAAAIlU/OeHIpcDuvd8/s1600/IMG_2962.JPG" width="480"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Geneviev is described as a leather grey. This color reminds me of Humpback whales. It's that bluish deep grey, it's a very majestic color, I can see myself wearing this colors a lot this season.
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-fsfoI99zA-g/U_Na_nLv14I/AAAAAAAAIlg/A6hE3ebUVCE/s1600/IMG_2973.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-fsfoI99zA-g/U_Na_nLv14I/AAAAAAAAIlg/A6hE3ebUVCE/s1600/IMG_2973.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-cVkVsWrDTOI/U_Na_stLXZI/AAAAAAAAIlk/wFR7dUxIc-s/s1600/IMG_2974.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-cVkVsWrDTOI/U_Na_stLXZI/AAAAAAAAIlk/wFR7dUxIc-s/s1600/IMG_2974.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Next, we have
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Margo
                </b>
               </span>
               .
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EKK8o4dkdGw/U_NbQn69MDI/AAAAAAAAIlw/kAiDMzpT9wU/s1600/IMG_2976.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EKK8o4dkdGw/U_NbQn69MDI/AAAAAAAAIlw/kAiDMzpT9wU/s1600/IMG_2976.JPG" width="480"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Margo is described as a medium red plum. Compare to Veronica this is a purple toned berry or plum. Unlike Veronica this formula was perfect, no flooding issues.
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-p8JSjM6lWGQ/U_Ncl-8_koI/AAAAAAAAIl8/hS7k537Q5ic/s1600/IMG_2985.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-p8JSjM6lWGQ/U_Ncl-8_koI/AAAAAAAAIl8/hS7k537Q5ic/s1600/IMG_2985.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rNzPUj5hneI/U_Ncl31ytmI/AAAAAAAAImA/43_cblmYWVo/s1600/IMG_2986.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rNzPUj5hneI/U_Ncl31ytmI/AAAAAAAAImA/43_cblmYWVo/s1600/IMG_2986.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Last but not least, we have
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Ryan
                </b>
               </span>
               .
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-xa64bWV2M68/U_Nc1_vyBsI/AAAAAAAAImM/9isk1fzsRuY/s1600/IMG_2988.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-xa64bWV2M68/U_Nc1_vyBsI/AAAAAAAAImM/9isk1fzsRuY/s1600/IMG_2988.JPG" width="480"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               Ryan is described as a classic indigo colors, this color was originally released during NYFW for Timo Weilland's AW14 Collection. Be still my beating heart... This is a beautiful cream/jelly. Since it's a deep blue I highly recommend wearing a nice base coat or doubling down just to be safe so your nails don't stain just in case. This for me was a show stopper.
               <br/>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gNSoyR03Ld8/U_NeB6EO2qI/AAAAAAAAImY/GC34lywBX8s/s1600/IMG_2993.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gNSoyR03Ld8/U_NeB6EO2qI/AAAAAAAAImY/GC34lywBX8s/s1600/IMG_2993.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-0_TUBVyTyeE/U_NeCCIaHVI/AAAAAAAAImc/-9ZMkKnXzN8/s1600/IMG_2995.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-0_TUBVyTyeE/U_NeCCIaHVI/AAAAAAAAImc/-9ZMkKnXzN8/s1600/IMG_2995.JPG" width="240"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               At the end of the day for me this collection hit fall on the head, it's very enticing and the colors are very beautiful. Even though I had a bit of a power fight with Veronica I'd still pick her up. Are the leaves changing colors yet?
               <br/>
               <br/>
               So what are your thoughts on this collection? Are any colors you need for fall? Let me know.
