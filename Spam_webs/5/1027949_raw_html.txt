      <div id="wiki">
       For a song with an identical title, see Yellow (Nicholas Tse song).

Yellow is a song by English alternative rock band Coldplay. The band wrote the song and co-produced it with British record producer Ken Nelson for their debut album, Parachutes (2000). The songs lyrics are a reference to band vocalist Chris Martins unrequited love, and its musicscape is built on a varied instrumentation.

The song was released in June 2000 as the second single off Parachutes, following Shiver, and the lead single in the United States. The single reached number four in the UK Singles Chart, giving the band their first top-five hit in the United Kingdom.
