         <h3 class="title">
          The Reality of We Got Married
         </h3>
         <span class="metadata">
          Posted by NessieSenpai on August 27th, 2013 under
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/2am/" rel="tag">
           2AM
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/2pm/" rel="tag">
           2PM
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/a-pink/" rel="tag">
           a pink
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/fx/" rel="tag">
           f(x)
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/fandoms/" rel="tag">
           fandoms
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/idols/" rel="tag">
           idols
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/jinwoon/" rel="tag">
           Jinwoon
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/khuntoria/" rel="tag">
           Khuntoria
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/naeun/" rel="tag">
           naeun
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/nichkhun/" rel="tag">
           Nichkhun
          </a>

           reality TV
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/shinee/" rel="tag">
           shinee
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/taemin/" rel="tag">

          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/victoria/" rel="tag">
           Victoria
          </a>

          <a href="http://jakwave.co.uk/tag/we-got-married/" rel="tag">
           we got married
          </a>
         </span>
         <div class="category-tags">
          <div class="category-tag-wrapper">
           <div class="category-tag-text">
            K-TV/Movies
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="entry">
          <p dir="ltr">
           <a href="http://www.jakwave.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/we-got-married-logo.jpeg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.jakwave.co.uk']);">
            <img alt="we-got-married-logo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-847" height="261" src="http://www.jakwave.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/we-got-married-logo.jpeg" width="543"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           So I got caught a couple of months ago.
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           I am very much ashamed to admit this but I was swept up in the weird phenomenon that is We Got Married, the Korean reality show that puts together idols and general famous people as a couple and have them live out their daily lives as a newly married couple would. Apparently. I mean, Im not married but the way that some of the couples act on the show I am pretty sure people in love in real life would be a bit more believable than that.
           <span id="more-846">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           There are three couples on the show currently: the quirky pair who are actually a couple in real life (and who I like because you can see their love is real) Jo Jung Chi and Jung In, Jinwoon from 2AM and Koo Joon Hee , an actress who even though she acts quite touchy-feely with Jinwoon (something that makes a refreshing change on Korean TV, even though it is entirely fake- a point I am going to reenforce a lot in this article so bear with me) is somewhat unhealthily obsessed with Baro from B1A4 (not that I blame her- Baro is smoking hot) and then the series newcomers; SHINees Taemin and A Pinks Na Eun whose combined presence on the show actually makes me ill because they are so forced that it makes you wonder how people believe that this show is real (SHINee and A Pink stans, dont hate me please!)
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_848" style="width: 561px">
           <a href="http://www.jakwave.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Taemin-and-Naeun-Couple.png" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.jakwave.co.uk']);">
            <img alt="Damn Squib couple more like." class="size-full wp-image-848" height="287" src="http://www.jakwave.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Taemin-and-Naeun-Couple.png" width="551"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Damp Squib couple more like.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p dir="ltr">
           But I was caught for around three weeks. I think it was a week or so into the show for the newly-created TaEun couple and
           <a href="http://www.allkpop.com/article/2013/06/taemin-blushes-at-the-thought-of-kissing-na-eun-on-we-got-married" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.allkpop.com']);" target="_blank">
            AllKpop made a pos
           </a>
           t about Taemin pestering this poor, clearly uninterested girl on whether he was getting a kiss as his birthday present. I was curious and decided to watch an episode and got hooked. I didnt believe anything of course (apart from the one actual couple on there who seemingly werent that popular with the audiences at the time because they were older and not idols- you have to think that the main audience are young girls between the ages of 15-19 with nothing better to do but simper over their Oppa and his fake wife) but as an entertainment programme with silly challenges and great comedy moments; mainly from the older couples teasing the young and sometimes blissfully unaware Taemin and Naeun.
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           After a couple of weeks I got bored. Perhaps I was tired of the forced relationships or I was getting annoyed that the older and only real couple were getting overshadowed because they werent idols and I wanted more of them and less of the saccharine aegyo of the younger couples. Maybe I woke up and remembered that this was the kind of reality TV that I LOATHED.
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           However, it is popular. And there are hardcore fans who will defend their favourite pairing almost to the death. A good example of this are those who still obsess over Khuntoria (the fan pairing name for 2PMs Nichkhun and F(x)s Victoria and not, as I first thought, a clever pun describing the Holy Grail of kpop genitalia). They had left the show way back in 2011 yet there are still Tumblr accounts and fanfiction dedicated to their true and obvious love. Even when they have been paired with other people (Nichkhun was rumoured to be with SNSDs Tiffany and Victoria most recently with TXVQs Changmin) there will still be the hardcore fangirls who would argue that its all lies and Khuntoria is forever -throws up-
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_849" style="width: 410px">
           <a href="http://www.jakwave.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/khuntoria.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.jakwave.co.uk']);">
            <img alt="Don't let the goo-goo eyes fool you... its all lies!" class="size-full wp-image-849" height="250" src="http://www.jakwave.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/khuntoria.jpg" width="400"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Dont let the goo-goo eyes fool you its all lies!
           </p>
          </div>
          <p dir="ltr">
           But perhaps I am being made a fool of in thinking that these girls (and lets face it, they are all girls) are taking this too seriously. Perhaps they are indeed all aware of the fact that it is only just a TV fabricated for our entertainment and not in the serious field of trying to set up Kpop idols in relationships. Perhaps they are doing what we all do when our favourite fictional pairing on TV or in film are happy together (Ross and Rachel from Friends, John Paul and Craig from Hollyoaks, etc)- purely being wistful and enjoying the sweet moments of them together. Well, at the very least that is what I hope their understanding of it is though some of the reactions do scare me a little.
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Maybe I will dip into We Got Married again sometime in the future when there is a new couple/when TaEun is finally over and they leave the show (again TaEun fans, dont hate me but MY GOSH they are so boring! Give me some much more entertaining Key instead!) But until then, I shall just stick to my dramas and variety shows. I deal with enough false romances in real life so I dont want to watch it in action on TV too.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
