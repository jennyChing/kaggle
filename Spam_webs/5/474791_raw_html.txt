          <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            When trying to introduce the Cape Town-born and based artist
            <a href="http://jadeklara.co.za/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://jadeklara.co.za/', 'Jade Klara');" target="_blank">
             Jade Klara
            </a>
            to you, I find myself thinking back to a lyric that has been highly significant in my own life, perhaps yours too. Im guilty for using it before in a past article, but this time it resonates much stronger. Its from Goldfrapps Fly Me Away':
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             This crazy life, this crazy world
             <br/>
             Were living in is magical
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            Years might pass by, but I refuse to let life follow suit. This doesnt always require going on extravagant trips abroad, or overpriced This and Thats Recently its been a change of scenery. Different faces and new ideas. Consistency in old dreams. Music, always. Hope, again. Jades characters remind me of this voyage were all on, a journey that can be dark, but is always magical And is sometimes best enjoyed alone.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Portrait_2014_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Portrait_2014_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4838" height="400" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Portrait_2014_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             You have said before that magic can happen  what do you identify as something magical and when did you last experience something of this nature?
            </strong>
            <br/>
            I think magic is a concept thats transcends the ordinary, its something that changes or moves you, in a weird way.
           </p>
           <p>
            The most recent magical experience happened about a month ago when I was fast asleep and I woke to something patting my feet. It was still dark but I could make out a dark shape sitting on the end of my bed. Then the shape meowed. I switched on the light and there was a fat black cat. Not so much magic, more of nocturnal house breaking. He bit me and left.
           </p>
           <p>
            In connection to my next question, please first consider these two quotes:
           </p>
           <p>
            Never fall in love with an idea. Theyre whores. If the one youre with isnt doing the job, theres always, always, always another.
            <br/>
             Chip Kidd
           </p>
           <p>
            A man may die, nations may rise and fall, but an idea lives on
            <br/>
             John F. Kennedy
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Is a creative idea different to political ideas or is an idea in essence the same in its definition
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            An idea is an idea regardless of context. An idea is recognizing a spark of intuition as something important and as something with purpose. That said, I feel a little sorry for Chip Kidds ideas, Im sure they have little idea-families to support.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Incredibly_Slow_Haunting_of_Ghost_Sloth_Poster_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_The_Incredibly_Slow_Haunting_of_Ghost_Sloth_Poster_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4841" height="848" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Incredibly_Slow_Haunting_of_Ghost_Sloth_Poster_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Incredibly_Slow_Haunting_of_Ghost_Sloth2_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_The_Incredibly_Slow_Haunting_of_Ghost_Sloth2_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4842" height="469" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Incredibly_Slow_Haunting_of_Ghost_Sloth2_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Before studying graphic design you studied literature. What triggered this change and do words/language still play an important role in your work  as inspiration and/or a significant part of the final piece?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            I love stories, and both art and literature are stories. They create worlds where there is magic and beauty and pain, a space where you can explore yourself through analogies and metaphors. I love how books can transform how you think and how you understand life. Its the transformative power that I love about literature and is what I have found doing what I do now. When I need inspiration I have pages and pages of verses and quotes and exerts and words that inspire me.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Black_Ghost_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Black_Ghost_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4829" height="848" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Black_Ghost_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Wooing__Booing_of_Cassandra_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_The_Wooing_&amp;_Booing_of_Cassandra_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4846" height="831" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Wooing__Booing_of_Cassandra_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Black_Snow_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Black_Snow_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4830" height="578" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Black_Snow_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Tiger__Lily_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Tiger_&amp;_Lily_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4847" height="836" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Tiger__Lily_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Black_Woods_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Black_Woods_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4831" height="849" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Black_Woods_The_Black_Winter_Collection_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Zombie_Dog_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Zombie_Dog_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4848" height="835" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Zombie_Dog_Poem_Series_The_Witching_Hour_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             You have previously commented that you have an affection for ugly creatures. When I look at your characters in your work  especially in
             <em>
              Black Winter Collection
             </em>
             and
             <em>
              Poem Songs
             </em>
              I get a sense of wanderlust and strength in their solitude from the human realm. It makes me questions my own ideas of what I find is beautiful and what is ugly Can the one even exist without the other? What are your thoughts on this?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            I do, I love little beings that look a bit wrong. Beauty can be a bit boring and so I naturally route for the aesthetic underdog. I love ugly dogs.
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             The concepts of beauty and ugliness exist as poles to one another with the walrus in the center, sitting there like a great equalizer.
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Bat_Screen_Print_A2_2012_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Bat_Screen_Print_A2_2012_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4828" height="848" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Bat_Screen_Print_A2_2012_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Do you have a soft spot for one of your characters?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Batty. I love that guy. When I wrote his story I fell in love with him, I picture him singing sea shanties and exploring the world with his goofy face.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Have you ever met one of your characters in your dreams? Who would you like to meet and what would you ask him/her?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            I wish I could say I have. Perhaps I should seek out a desert shaman and learn the art of lucid dreaming. If this works I would like to meet the girl I draw, and give her a hug. She looks like she needs one.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             From illustration to painting to design and also sculpture  you work in a range of mediums; is there a connection/similar theme albeit the technique being different?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            I like to explore different mediums, it doesnt always work out *sculpture, cough cough, but its important to stretch your legs and see what an idea looks like using different techniques. Learning is growing, and growing is fun, unless your pants stop fitting. I think even though the medium may colour the idea, the ideas I work from are always connected.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             What have you learned from experience that didnt learn whilst studying?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Technique comes from practice and thats not something one learns, it is a discipline that you gain by working at it everyday. I loved studying, its a time where youre blank and excited to fill your space with newly discovered things and I think its something we should carry through.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Fly_Panda_Fly_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Fly_Panda_Fly_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4835" height="714" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Fly_Panda_Fly_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Aurora_Marauders_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre1.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Aurora_Marauders_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4827" height="852" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Aurora_Marauders_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre1.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Lilith__Blob_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_Lilith_&amp;_Blob_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4837" height="752" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_Lilith__Blob_Art_Prints_2010_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             There is a particular bond between animals and humans present in your work. Is this companionship a reflection on a broader discourse of the relationship between man and nature, and that we are losing respect as we become ever-more immersed in a life of consumption and easy access.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Yes, of course. We place so many things between us and the world that it becomes difficult to have meaningful connections. I love a narrative of simple, innocent companionship. Its a quiet moment of connection. Its nice to feel we are still a part of this world.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Albert Einstein quoted that Imagination is more important than knowledge. What are your thoughts on this?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            I think imagination is something that grows, and feeds as an outward force, whereas knowledge is more myopic, if that makes sense? If one is looking to know more about something, that something is dissected until it is understood, whereas imagination seems to be more limitless. That said, both are important, one needs imagination to dream of space travel and knowledge to build a ship.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Coral_Girl_Silk_screen_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Coral_Girl_Silk_screen_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4844" height="848" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Coral_Girl_Silk_screen_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Exhibition_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Exhibition_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4845" height="517" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Exhibition_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Bind_print_on_Hahnemule_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg">
             <img alt="Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Bind_print_on_Hahnemule_2013_beautifulbizarre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4843" height="749" src="https://beautifulbizarre.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Jade_Klara_The_Witching_Hour_Bind_print_on_Hahnemule_2013_beautifulbizarre.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Your work seems to reflect an imagination that is at times fierce and wild and other times dreamy and playful. What have you been imagining lately?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            To be honest Im at a stage where Im trying to be practical. I am imagining a life where I go back in time and choose to study law or actuarial science. Of course in these imaginings, I am a tiger, with reading glasses and an aptitude for arithmetic.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Any words to leave us dreaming about?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            This comes from the book
            <em>
             Norwegian Wood
            </em>
