It Mexico Surfing Holidays Mexico Surfing Vacations Mexico Surfing Travelis bordered by the United States on the north and by Guatemala and Belize on the south.
     </p>
     <p>
      With almost 2500 Miles of Pacific coastline facing west, Mexico is a magnet to swell and having a deep water trench to funnel this energy just 
		  offshore it has become a classic surfing destination. With dozens of beaches and unbeatable swells that vary considerably in face height 
		  (all creating glassy surfing conditions) means that the surfing is great for both beginners and professionals alike who love the feeling only 
		  experienced when riding the crest of a wave. You can expect surfing holidays waves in Mexico ranging from knee high to a few feet overhead, 
		  depending on the swell and which point you are surfing. The amazing thing is that even at knee high, Mexico Surfing Holidays Mexico Surfing 
		  Vacations Mexico Surfing Travel waves are perfect and can be ridden for hundreds of yards on a longboard. You will get more practice actually 
		  riding a wave here ten times more than at a normal wave in southern California. If you really love to surf, this is the place.
     </p>
