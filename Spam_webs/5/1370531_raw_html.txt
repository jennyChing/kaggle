         <h1 class="title">
          7 Ultimate Luxury Travel Accessories
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <abbr class="date time published" title="2014-06-20T00:59:28+0000">
          06/20/2014
         </abbr>
        </div>
        <section class="entry">
         <p>
          <img alt="lv3 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27364" height="537" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/lv3-2.jpg" width="720"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Long gone are the backpacking days, the days of traveling rough, bouncing from hostel to hostel along the gringo trail. These days its all about traveling in style, sampling the finer things in life, basking in the best that luxury travel has to offer.From stylish hand luggage to theessentialinflight technology, we round up
            <em>
             <strong>
              the 7 ultimate
              <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/miami/">
               luxury travel accessories
              </a>
              you cannot do without,
             </strong>
            </em>
            just in time for summer in the northern hemisphere.
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          |Bags Are Packed and Ready to Go|
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="suitcase 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27332" height="1024" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/suitcase-2.jpg" width="700"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre a pro-jetsetter, you know that traveling in style means mixing design, comfort and functionality. Packing well is key, and a piece of carry-on luggage thats easy to maneuver through airports makes your trip to the gate that much easier. Thefolks at
          <a href="http://bree.com/men-s/travelling-bags/DIE-ZEIT-10.html?id=000000000257280010" target="_blank" title="Bree">
           Bree
          </a>
          , a German bag design company that focuses on innovation and utility, have the perfect suitcase for you. Its incredibly light, super modern and easy to roll through security thanks to high-tech Japanese castors (technical talk for wheels).With this mix of style and simplicity, its the perfect bag for
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/p/corporate-housing">
           corporate travelers
          </a>
          or even weekend get-a-ways to say,
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/lp/south-beach-luxury-apartments-for-rent/">
           Miami Beach
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          |All Digital Everything|
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="Collage2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27341" height="1000" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Collage2.png" width="1333"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          We live in a digital world, and a very modern digital world at that. Gone are the days of paperbacks, discmans, and boomboxes. Its a good thing the Caribbean can keep up, because its time to break out the
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/lp/cartagena-vacations/">
           Cartagena vacation luxury travel accessories
          </a>
          this summer, and were not talking beach balls and sandcastles. Get those apps on that iPad and the books downloaded on the Kindle. Be the party starter wherever you go with premium travel speakers by
          <a href="http://www.beoplay.com/Products/Beolit12?_ga=1.77693719.280573825.1403203126#at-a-glance">
           Band &amp; Olufsen
          </a>
          . But the real beach toy this year is the Go Pro Hero, ready to capture all your active moments on video.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         </h2>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          | Plugged In |
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="powerdock 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27344" height="960" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/powerdock-2.jpg" width="1280"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          You cant be geeked out with all your electronics without power. Be sure to stow away the Griffin Powerdock in your suitcase for the ultimate charger. This is especially useful for the
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/p/corporate-housing">
           corporate traveler
          </a>
          or large family needing to liven up many devices. The Powerdock charges up to 5 electronics at once, saving you from hunting around your
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/miami/#/search">
           luxury apartment rental
          </a>
          for multiple power sockets.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          | Travel Safe With Your Travel Safe |
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="safe 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27346" height="683" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/safe-2.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          It happens all the time. You just think itll never happen to youuntil it does. Theft in foreign lands is beyond prevalent, so be safe and lock it up. No matter if youre seeing the
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/lp/vacation-in-buenos-aires/">
           sights in Buenos Aires
          </a>
          or lounging on the warm
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/costarica/#/search">
           beaches of Costa Rica
          </a>
          , your luxury travel accessories need their very own luxury travel safe! The
          <a href="http://www.doettling.com/en/modern-safes/guardian.html">
           Dottling Guardian
          </a>
          safe is so secure, a thief could take a sledgehammer to it without gaining access to the valuables inside. Talk about high impact resistance.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          | Towel Off |
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="MAS 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27375" height="984" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/MAS-2.png" width="1774"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats right folks, even the beaches of Jersey shore can feel like some exotic destination when youre laying on these towels from
          <a href="http://www.maslinandco.com/store">
           Maslin &amp; Co
          </a>
          . The plush towels are in the shape of animal skins, offered in an array of colors, and can be carried with a unique leather carrier strap  both fashionable AND functional, exactly how the jetsetters do. This would go swimmingly on the sandy shores of
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/lp/luxury-vacation-rentals-in-south-beach-miami">
           South Beach
          </a>
          , martini in hand and laying on your cozy yet comfy and favorite
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/lp/luxury-vacation-rentals-in-south-beach-miami">
           luxury travel accessory in Miami
          </a>
          . You can thank us later.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          | Diaries of a Globe-Trotter |
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="hermes 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27353" height="1500" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/hermes-2.jpg" width="1500"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          They say memories last forever, but it sure would help to write them down in the most beautiful notebook ever created. Hermes is an extremely established luxury travel brand, known for their fine luggage and quality goods. Jot down your travels in this calfskin covered
          <a href="http://usa.hermes.com/leather/notebooks/ulysse/ulysse-pm/notebook-ulysse-pm-grouped-20012.html">
           notebook
          </a>
          so you may look back on fond memories of yesteryear in the written word.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          | Give Me A Break |
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="chair 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-27355" height="600" src="http://culture.oasiscollections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/chair-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Go ahead, take a seat. Take a load off your feet. Objets Nomades for Louis Vuitton elevates the art of travel to new heights with this limited edition travel furniture and accessory. The total 16 piece travel inspired collection merges wanderlust, innovative design, and of course, luxury. May this suitcase-turned-foldable chair inspire you to book that dream vacation. We think this look would be oh so chic on the beaches of Brasil. You deserve that break. Relax in style with your ultimate
          <a href="http://oasiscollections.com/lp/ipanema-holiday-apartments        ">
           luxury travel accessory in Rio de Janeiro
          </a>
          .
         </p>
