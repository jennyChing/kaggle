       <article class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p class="post-meta">
         Posted |
         <span class="comments-number">
          <a href="http://momanddadcentral.com/5-minute-recipe-yummy-complete-nutrition-smoothie-for-kids-of-all-ages-and-adults/#respond">
           2 comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/smoothie.jpg">
          <img alt="smoothie" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-310" height="384" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/smoothie.jpg" width="535"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Main nutritional benefits:
         </strong>
         This smoothie can be used as a super nutritional meal substitute, because is it so rich in nutrients and absolutely delicious, especially because the oat milk is naturally sweet . It is an excellent source of Calcium, Protein, Omega complex, vitamins A, B complex, C, E, K, Iron and minerals.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Classification:
         </strong>
         Vegan Vegetarian
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 cup of Vanilla Oat Milk ( regularly milk or other vegetarian milks can be used, but increase the amount of banana for sweetness)
         </li>
         <li>
          1/3 Medium banana
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 Apple
         </li>
         <li>
          A few blueberries ( 6 or 7 medium ones)
         </li>
         <li>
          A few leaves of spinach (4 or 5 fresh or frozenbaby spinach)
         </li>
         <li>
          A pinch of wheat Germ
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 Teaspoon of Cashew Nut Butter, peanut butter or sunflower seed butter ( can be omitted for those with allergies)
         </li>
         <li>
          <em>
           Optional:
          </em>
          A pinch of shelled Hemp Seeds ( for a boost of Omega fats)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Preparation:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
          Using a
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00AN9UJ68/ref=as_li_tf_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00AN9UJ68&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=simplytaty-20">
          hand blender
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=simplytaty-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00AN9UJ68" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         or food processor, blend all the ingredients very well until there are no chunks or lumps.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Serve:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Younger babies will enjoy this fed to them with a spoon. Older kids will have fun having this in a special cup with a cool straw.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Recommended for ages:
         </strong>
         8 months and up. For young babies it is advisable that you test the ingredients for allergies individually, especially nuts and berries, before feeding them the smoothie.
        </p>
