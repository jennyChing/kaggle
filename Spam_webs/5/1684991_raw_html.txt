         <h1>
          Spin Chill review  Portable beer chiller
         </h1>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Beer taken cold is better for many. However, when you need your beer it might not been as cold as it should. You do not have to wait. You can make it cold instantly with Spin Chill. Spin Chill makes beer cold instantly. You do not have to wait or take it warm while you do not like warm beer. You can make it cold and have it just like you wanted it. Within a minute, your beer will be cold just like you want it and you can have it.
          <a href="http://asseenonpcitems.com/spin-chill-reviews-portable-beer-chiller/">
           Read more about spin chill reviews
          </a>
          below .
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="SpinChill Portable Beer Chiller" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-42" height="573" src="http://kitchenkm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Spin-Chill-Portable-Beer-Chiller-image-1.png" width="570"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
          How Spin Chill works ?
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          This is not magic but pure science.
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c0__La30IXE">
           Spin chill
          </a>
          may be made of a power drill attachment specially designed to fit over the top of the bottle or can. You have it fixed on the bottle and lay the bottle down on its side inside an ice chest the power the drill by turning it on. The other one has no-drill attachment. The device works by having the container spun which introduces convention to both sides and the outer surface of the container. The heat rate transfer will then be increased resulting to faster cooling.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          With this effect the normal process of cooling which involves movement of heat from the can to the cooler environment is expedited. The cooling is twenty time faster than normal with the spin chill. The quality of the beer remains the same.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
          Features of spin chill
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt=" Spin Chill " class="alignleft size-full wp-image-44" height="406" src="http://kitchenkm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/SpinChill-Portable-Beer-Chiller-image-4.png" width="267"/>
          The device uses 4 AA batteries for the hands-free type. You do not need to be closer to a power connection to operate it
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is small and light in weight. It can be easily carried even in the pockets. This makes it portable and easy to carry for outdoor events
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is easy to use. There is no technical requirements to be able to understand it and use it. All you have to do is carry it and turn it one when you need your beer cooler.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Benefits of spin chill
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The spin chill does not shake the container during the process. this way, it does not increase the foam formation that would have been experienced.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It cools any beer, soda, or wine by having it spun inside ice-bucket . You can use it with any bottled beverage
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The device is durable and reliable. You do not have to worry about breakdowns
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It does not make a lot of noise making it ideal for use during fishing and other quiet moments without worrying about noise.
         </p>
         <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
          How much spin chill cost ?
