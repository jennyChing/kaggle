      <p>
       <a href="http://Zerply.com" target="_blank" title="zerply">
        Zerply
       </a>
       is a
       <strong>
        network for the worlds top creative talent in TV, film, and games.
       </strong>
       It is not exactly a new platform but it does deserve the extra awareness. In fact Zerply comes to fit in this new category of
       <strong>
        dedicated social networking platforms
       </strong>
       by offering a new way to connect talent in the entertainment industry.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Also Read:
       <a href="http://wersm.com/25-facts-you-really-should-know-about-linkedin/" target="_blank" title="25 Facts You Really Should Know About LinkedIn">
        25 Facts You Really Should Know About LinkedIn
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Initially launched in 2010 as a network and portfolio site for creative professionals in general, the platform has recently repositioned as
       <strong>
        an invite-only community and resource for the top creative talent in TV, film, and games.
       </strong>
      </p>
      <div class="tm-tweet-clear">
      </div>
      <div class="tm-click-to-tweet">
       <div class="tm-ctt-text">
        <a href="https://twitter.com/share?text=.%40Zerply+is+an+invite-only+community+for+the+top+creative+talent+in+TV%2C+film%2C+and+games.&amp;via=WeRSM&amp;related=WeRSM&amp;url=http://wersm.com/meet-zerply-the-linkedin-for-artists/" target="_blank">
         .@Zerply is an invite-only community for the top creative talent in TV, film, and games.
        </a>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a class="tm-ctt-btn" href="https://twitter.com/share?text=.%40Zerply+is+an+invite-only+community+for+the+top+creative+talent+in+TV%2C+film%2C+and+games.&amp;via=WeRSM&amp;related=WeRSM&amp;url=http://wersm.com/meet-zerply-the-linkedin-for-artists/" target="_blank">
         Click To Tweet
        </a>
       </p>
       <div class="tm-ctt-tip">
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
       Say you are an artist, actor, comedian, musician or game designer. You will often find it quite hard to get connect to the right people in order to find your next gig. This is where Zerply comes in.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Christofer Karltrop
       </strong>
       , co-founder of
       <a href="http://Zerply.com" target="_blank" title="zerply">
        Zerply
       </a>
       , puts it like this:
      </p>
      <div class="tmnf-sc-quote">
       <p>
        It shouldnt be so hard for talent and companies to find each other. We built Zerply to make the process of transitioning from one production to the next seamless for recruiters and creative pros alike. Our mission is to change the face of hiring in TV, film, and games for the better.
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       To register onto
       <a href="http://Zerply.com" target="_blank" title="zerply">
        Zerply
       </a>
       you will however need to be invited as the platform still operates as an
       <strong>
        invite-only community
       </strong>
       . So to get on Zerply, simply click on the apply for membership button, fill-in your details and information on why you really should be on the platform and the good guys will get back to you quickly.
      </p>
      <p>
       This process has been chosen by the founders to ensure
       <strong>
        Zerply remains a high quality and dedicated network.
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://wersm.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/wersm_zerply.png" rel="prettyPhoto">

       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       What do you think of dedicated social media platforms? Are you registered on any of them? What has your experience been like?
       <strong>
        Share your story in the comments.
