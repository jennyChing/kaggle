          <a href="http://laughingspatula.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sour-Cream-Coffee-Cake1.jpg">
           <img alt="Sour Cream Coffee Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2154" height="600" src="http://laughingspatula.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sour-Cream-Coffee-Cake1.jpg" width="400"/>

         <p>
          So after all my talk about weight loss in my last post,
          <a href="http://laughingspatula.com/skinny-tomato-and-roasted-red-pepper-soup/">
           Skinny Tomato Soup
          </a>
           here I am posting a fairly decadent recipe. Well, I do it for you my friends. I do it because I know, deep in your heart of hearts, you do not want skinny stuff thrown in your face every week while we are gearing up for the Holidaysam I right? Do I know you? I think so.
         </p>
         <p>
          We went all out on this one.  No light dairy products, no reduced sugar  its just big. Big flavors, big calories and everyone you know is going to love you big for making it!
         </p>
         <p>
          This is a great make ahead loaf for the holidays. I wouldmake it no more than a day in advancebakery items have a short shelf life.cant be helped. But I made it on a Sunday and took the photos on Mondayand it was darn tasty!
         </p>
         <p>
          Now, if only you were here to eat the rest.its just sitting there all lonely and stuff. I guess its time for a trip to the neighbors. Theylove it when Iamdieting. Ding Dong  Honey, get the door. Its that lady that used to be fat  she brings us the most delightfulstuff!. So happy I can be of service.sharing the chub, one neighbor at a time.
         </p>
         <p>
          NOTES: I used pecans butwalnuts or almonds would work just as well. This recipe doubles perfectly.Ifyou want 2 loaves or a 9 x 13 size cake pan  just double the ingredients and it will work fine.

          <div class="ERSIngredients">
           <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             <strong>
              Cake
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup butter - room temp
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 eggs
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 teaspoon vanilla extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup sour cream
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 teaspoon baking powder
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              teaspoon baking soda
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              teaspoon salt
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             <strong>
              For Streusel Topping
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup brown sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 teaspoons cinnamon
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              teaspoon salt
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 tablespoons butter - chilled
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup chopped pecans
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             <strong>
              Maple Icing
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup powdered sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tablespoons maple syrup
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tablespoon milk
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Preheat oven to 350. Spray 9 x 5 loaf pan with non stick spray.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <strong>
              For Cake:
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Using mixer. Cream together butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time. Add vanilla and sour cream. Mix until well combined.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In separate bowl - combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add to wet ingredients.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <strong>
              For Streusel topping
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix with pastry cutter or fork until well combined and butter is the size of small peas.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour half the cake batter into the prepared loaf pan and top with half the streusel mixture. Add remaining batter and top with streusel mixture. (this makes a lot of streusel so go heavy with it!)
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake for about 40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <strong>
              Icing
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Mix all icing ingredients together and drizzle over cooled cake.
            </li>
           </ol>
