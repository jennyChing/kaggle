       <h3>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Texture_and_music_replacement:">
         <b>
          Texture and music replacement
         </b>
         :
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Project M allows individuals to put new character and stage textures, custom music, and various other things in the game. Doing so is very simple.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please refer to these guides:
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="external free" href="http://www.smashboards.com/showthread.php?t=232455&amp;" rel="nofollow">
         http://www.smashboards.com/showthread.php?t=232455&amp;
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="external free" href="http://www.smashboards.com/showthread.php?t=245410&amp;" rel="nofollow">
         http://www.smashboards.com/showthread.php?t=245410&amp;
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Kitty Corp Meow Mix has tons of pre-made models for you to download and put on your SD card:
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="external free" href="http://forums.kc-mm.com/Gallery/BrawlView.php" rel="nofollow">
         http://forums.kc-mm.com/Gallery/BrawlView.php
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <i>
         Note:
        </i>
        Textures generally work, but Vertex textures tend to rename bones on characters resulting is messed up animations. Test them before using them with friends/wifi.
       </p>
