Consequently, nuclear war occurs that shatters the peace of the earth and makes it a wasteland. The heroes of this sequel emerge after the nuclear war and are to involve into a fight with an army of dinosaurs and shape-shifting reptilians to protect the earth and for the survival of the humanity.
