           <div class="slider-title">
            <h2>
             What is Citizen Space?
            </h2>
           </div>
           <p>
            We take the best elements of a coffee shop (social, energetic and, yes, coffee) and the best elements of a workspace (productive, functional) and combine them to give indie workers the chance to have their own
            <a href="http://citizenspace.us/membership/" target="_self">
             affordable space
            </a>
            . Its a place to collaborate. Get creative. Inspire a fellow entrepreneur. Make business deals. Meet new people. And even get some work done.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="slide" id="slide-2" style="display:none">
           <img alt="" class="alignright slider-image" src="http://citizenspace.us/OrsonWelles/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/CS-interior.jpg" width="300px"/>
           <div class="slider-title">
            <h2>
             Work from here.
            </h2>
           </div>
           <p>
            We offer a large, open work space with dedicated desks as well as communal tables. Other perks include a kitchen, living room area, phone booth, and conference room. Our citizens include startups, software engineers, web developers, social media strategists, designers, writers, consultants and more. Some set-up shop here full-time; others just stop by to work for the day. All benefit from working among like-minded entrepreneurs. Become a
            <a href="http://citizenspace.us/membership/" title="citizen">
             citizen
            </a>
            or just
            <a href="http://citizenspace.us/membership/">
             drop in
            </a>
            and try it out.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="slide" id="slide-3" style="display:none">
           <img alt="" class="alignright slider-image" src="http://citizenspace.us/OrsonWelles/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/IMG_0038.jpg" width="300px"/>
           <div class="slider-title">
            <h2>
             Attend an Event.
            </h2>
           </div>
           <p>
            Citizen Space plays host to meetups, launch parties, demo nights, startup mixers, and other professional development events. Members can host an event for free. And we occasionally let non-members
            <a href="http://citizenspace.us/events/rent-space">
             rent
            </a>
            out the space. Check out our
            <a href="http://citizenspace.us/events/calendar">
             calendar
            </a>
            of events and come on by.
           </p>
          </div>
