         <img alt="Banana Pudding Cheesecake with a vanilla wafer crust." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9832 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cheesecake/" height="575" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/banana-pudding-cheesecake-4.jpg" width="575"/>

        <p>
         This creamy dessert flavored with fresh banana is a combination of two of my favorite desserts- cheesecake and banana pudding. Im a huge fan of banana pudding anything and everything. In the past Ive made
         <a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-pancakes/" title="Banana Pudding Pancakes">
          Banana Pudding Pancakes
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-bars/" title="Banana Pudding Bars">
          Banana Pudding Bars
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cupcakes/" title="Banana Pudding Cupcakes">
          Banana Pudding Cupcakes
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cake-recipe/" title="Banana Pudding Cake Recipe">
          Banana Pudding Cake
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-ice-cream/" title="Banana Pudding Ice Cream">
          Banana Pudding Ice Cream
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Banana Pudding Cheesecake with a vanilla wafer crust." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9833 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cheesecake/" height="863" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/banana-pudding-cheesecake-6.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Banana Pudding Cheesecake starts off with a vanilla wafer crust  because its not banana pudding without nilla wafers
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cheesecake/" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         . Just to make sure theres enough Nilla Wafer goodness a ring of whole vanilla wafers is placed along the circumference of the springform pan. If you wanted even more vanilla wafers in your Banana Pudding Cheesecake, you could also do a layer of them in the middle of the filling.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Banana Pudding Cheesecake with a vanilla wafer crust." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9834 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cheesecake/" height="814" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/banana-pudding-cheesecake-29.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The original recipe, which came from
         <a href="http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/banana-pudding-cheesecake" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/banana-pudding-cheesecake', 'Southern Living']);" target="_blank">
          Southern Living
         </a>
         , called for baking the crust before the filling is added, but I skipped this step and it turned out fine. No need to do extra work :). Plus I find crusts made out of vanilla wafers can get a little hard to cut through if they are precooked.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Banana Pudding Cheesecake with a vanilla wafer crust." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9835 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cheesecake/" height="950" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/banana-pudding-cheesecake-31.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The filling is creamy and rich with just the right amount of banana flavor. Top the cheesecake off with whipped cream and vanilla wafers and you have a showstopper southern dessert that would be perfect to serve for Easter.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Banana Pudding Cheesecake with a vanilla wafer crust." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9836 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/banana-pudding-cheesecake/" height="863" src="http://spicysouthernkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/banana-pudding-cheesecake-1.jpg" width="575"/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cups finely crushed vanilla wafers
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup finely chopped pecans
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup butter, melted
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            17 vanilla wafers
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 ripe bananas, diced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 ripe banana, mashed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tablespoon lemon juice
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2-3 tablespoons light brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 (8-oz.) packages cream cheese, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 large eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            whipped cream and vanilla wafers for garnish
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a medium bowl, stir together crushed vanilla wafers, chopped pecans, and melted butter. Press into bottom of a greased 9-inch springform pan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stand 17 vanilla wafers around edge of pan with rounded sides facing outward. Press them gently into the crust.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small saucepan, combine bananas and lemon juice. Stir in brown sugar, using 2 tablespoons if your bananas are super ripe and 3 tablespoons if they are medium ripe. Cook over medium heat until brown sugar dissolves, about 1 minute. Remove from heat.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese at medium speed until smooth, about 3 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Gradually add granulated sugar.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add eggs, one at a time and stopping to scrape down sides of bowl after each addition. Do not over beat. Only beat until most of yellow disappears before adding next egg.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Beat in vanilla extract.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir banana mixture in until evenly mixed. Pour batter into springform pan on top of crust.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake for 45 to 55 minutes, until center is almost set. Jiggle the pan to check.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Turn oven off, open door about 6 inches and leave cheesecake in oven to cool for about 1 hour.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove cheesecake to counter to cool for another 30 minutes. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 8 hours before serving.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Garnish with whipped cream and vanilla wafers.
           </li>
          </ol>
