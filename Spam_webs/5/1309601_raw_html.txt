       <div class="headline_area">
        <h1 class="headline">
         7 Ways the Cloud is Changing the Mobile Industry
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="byline small">
        <div class="cmeta_area">
         <div class="cmeta_aimg">
          <img alt="Melissa" class="avatar avatar-90 photo" height="90" src="//www.bluecloudsolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Melissa_avatar_1421962265-90x90.jpg" width="90"/>
         </div>
         <div class="cmeta_ainfo">
          <div class="cmeta_alink">
           <a href="http://www.bluecloudsolutions.com/author/melissa-wood-reed/" rel="author" title="Posts by Melissa">
            Melissa
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="cmeta_date">
           September 24, 2013
          </div>
          <div class="cmeta_cats">
           <a href="http://www.bluecloudsolutions.com/category/articles/" rel="category tag">
            Articles
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="cmeta_coms">
           <a href="http://www.bluecloudsolutions.com/articles/7-ways-cloud-changing-mobile-industry/#comments">
            0 Comments
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="clr">
         </div>

       <div class="post_content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         As more people become aware of the cloud and what it can do for them, it is completely revolutionizing the mobile industry. When talking about the cloud on mobile devices, most mean the ability to save pictures, files, and more from their devices to the Internet. These files can then be opened from any device anywhere in the world, as long as it has a connection to the Internet. Here are seven ways the cloud is changing the mobile industry:
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: right;">
         Reducing Required Memory
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 13px;">
          <img alt="samsung-galaxy-s4-smartphone" class="wp-image-4136 alignleft" height="215" itemprop="image" src="//www.bluecloudsolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/samsung-galaxy-s4-smartphone.jpg" width="359"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Were getting to a point in mobile device technology where people want more memory on every piece of technology, but devices are limited on what they can handle. However, because of the ability to save things in the cloud, users dont have to worry about this nearly as much.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Before you could have 1,000 pictures on your phone, and then have to decide if you should delete some of them or get a new game. Now, you can save the pictures to one of the many cloud storage apps available on the
         <a href="http://www.t-mobile.com/cell-phones/samsung-galaxy-s-4.html" target="_blank">
          T-Mobile Samsung Galaxy S4
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         Improving Innovation
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Since mobile app developers are able to now create apps that can be stored on the cloud, there are more
         <a href="http://beforeitsnews.com/science-and-technology/2011/08/three-ways-the-cloud-is-changing-the-mobile-industry-995572.html" target="_blank">
          options for innovation
         </a>
         . In the past, developers have been strictly limited to the specs of specific devices. Today, these same developers are able to expand what they do, simply because more can be saved online.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         Taking Things From Mobile to Desktop
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         One of the biggest reasons that most people decide to use cloud storage is because theyre able to take any number of files from their cell phone to their desktop, and beyond. This is beneficial to everyone, from business people to stay-at-home parents. Being able to bring files along with you and present them on any type of device from a smartphone, tablet, or even desktop computer completely changes the way we look at saving files. You no longer need to worry about how to get presentations from one place to another.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         Sharing Becomes Easier
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="mobile sharing" class=" wp-image-4137 alignright" height="247" src="//www.bluecloudsolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/GalaxySIIISBeam_1-300x247.jpg" width="300"/>
         Have you ever sat with friends and family and had to pass around a cell phone so that everyone could see the pictures on the device? Instead of doing this, most mobile cloud storage apps allow you to give others a link to view your files. In some cases, such as with DropBox, the person clicking on the link doesnt even have to have an account to see the files youre sharing. With this option, you can easily share almost anything you create or find with anyone in the world.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         Quicker Indication of Problems
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Many cloud based apps are already available that connect to appliances and more. These apps allow users to find out if there is a problem with the appliance. Some battery powered cars also have this ability, and the apps will alert the user if their car battery is about to die. This means that youll be able to fix problems before they become crucial. Being able to have an almost
         <a href="http://online.wsj.com/ad/article/cloudcomputing-changelives" target="_blank">
          instant diagnostics tool
         </a>
         on your mobile device is a great way to save time and energy, trying to figure out if you need a new appliance, or if youre able to fix the one you have.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: right;">
         Less Stressful Travel and Road Trips
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="mobile road trip" class="wp-image-4138 alignleft" height="240" src="//www.bluecloudsolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/aaa_travel_information_gas.png" width="250"/>
         Have you ever taken a road trip that had multiple cars involved? If so, you know how stressful it can be. With some of the cloud technology available, a group of students were able to design an app that would allow multiple cars to keep track of each other on the road. With this app, youll never again have to worry about how far ahead of the rest of the group youre getting. Instead, you can ensure that everyones on track, or see if theres been a problem in the group.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         In Developing Countries
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Countries that are developing have had a hard time with things like phone lines. They are difficult to get to many people, and they get expensive. With cloud options, more people are using cell phones, and able to get information to and from themselves. Because of this, developing countries are starting to rely on cloud technology to advance themselves. This will lead to these countries becoming even more advanced than originally thought possible in a short time.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         There are many benefits to cloud technology, and its making a huge change in the global marketplace. From cloud storage apps to helping developing counties, this technology will help move us into the next decade.
        </p>
