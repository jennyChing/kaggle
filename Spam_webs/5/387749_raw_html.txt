     <p>
      Let the cookie baking season begin! Not like I needed the Holidays to bake cookies, but it seems a bit more fun during the Christmas season doesnt it?
     </p>
     <p>
      This is a simple, one bowl chocolate lovers cookie that is perfect for any Christmas plate. Your friends and neighbors will LOVE you when these are given as gifts.
     </p>
     <p>
      Hope you enjoy! xoxo
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6359/" rel="attachment wp-att-18241">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18241" height="400" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6359.jpg" title="IMG_6359" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Here is your 5 ingredient line up, cant beat that!
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6368/" rel="attachment wp-att-18242">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18242" height="536" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6368.jpg" title="IMG_6368" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      In no particular order, place your ingredients into a large mixing bowl.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6370/" rel="attachment wp-att-18243">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18243" height="466" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6370.jpg" title="IMG_6370" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      I started with the cake mix.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6372-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-18244">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18244" height="581" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6372.jpg" title="IMG_6372" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Pour in your melted butter.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6377/" rel="attachment wp-att-18245">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18245" height="466" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6377.jpg" title="IMG_6377" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Add your eggie.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6388-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-18246">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18246" height="446" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6388.jpg" title="IMG_6388" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Add your chocolate chips. I chose the minis.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6390/" rel="attachment wp-att-18247">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18247" height="466" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6390.jpg" title="IMG_6390" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      YUM!
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6397/" rel="attachment wp-att-18248">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18248" height="466" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6397.jpg" title="IMG_6397" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Time for the Reeses Peanut Butter Cups.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/rpbc/" rel="attachment wp-att-18249">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18249" height="351" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/RPBC.jpg" title="RPBC" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Place 25 of them in a ziplock bag and give them a good smash.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/chopped/" rel="attachment wp-att-18250">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18250" height="351" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/chopped.jpg" title="chopped" width="700"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Once smashed, add them right to the bowl.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6425-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-18251">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18251" height="645" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6425.jpg" title="IMG_6425" width="550"/>

      <a href="http://picky-palate.com/2011/12/01/chocolate-cake-reeses-peanut-butter-chunk-cookies/img_6428/" rel="attachment wp-att-18252">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18252" height="602" src="http://picky-palate.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/IMG_6428.jpg" title="IMG_6428" width="700"/>

      Enjoy!

      <h2>
       Chocolate Cake Reeses Peanut Butter Chunk Cookies
      </h2>
     </div>
     <div class="paper-mid">
      <div class="paper-text">
       <p>
        1 box devils food cake mix
       </p>
       <p>
        1 stick (8 tablespoons) butter, melted (if dough seems too dry, add 2 more Tbsp melted butter)
       </p>
       <p>
        1 large egg
       </p>
       <p>
        2 cups mini chocolate chips
       </p>
       <p>
        25 mini Reeses Peanut Butter Cups
       </p>
       <p>
        1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. and line a large baking sheet with parchment or a silpat liner.
       </p>
       <p>
        2. Place all ingredients into a large bowl mixing until combined. I find it easiest to mix with hands. If dough seems too dry, you can add 2 additional Tablespoons melted butter. With a medium cookie scoop, scoop dough onto baking sheet placed 1 inch apart. Bake for 13-15 minutes until cooked through. Let cool for 10 minutes on baking sheet before transferring to cooling rack.
       </p>
       <p>
        Makes 3 dozen cookies
       </p>
