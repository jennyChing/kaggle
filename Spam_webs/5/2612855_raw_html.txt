     <h1 class="grub-topOfColumn" itemprop="name" style="font-size: 27px;">
      Russian Green Tea Cookies
     </h1>
     <img height="471" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Russian-Green-Tea-Cookies-790x471.jpg" width="790"/>
     <div class="yui3-g">
      <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-well">
       <div class="grub-byline" style="margin-top: 1em;">
        Recipe by
        <a href="https://plus.google.com/113274240511284571445" rel="author">
         Jerry James Stone
        </a>
         October 29, 2013
       </div>
       <div class="grub-mainPostContent" itemprop="recipeInstructions" not-style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 23px;">
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="597" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/russian-green-tea-cookies-4/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/russian-green-tea-cookies-4" rel="attachment wp-att-4158">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive been wanting to cook with matcha green tea for quite some time but I could never make up my mind on what to make. I love the flavor of it but I really wanted to show off the color too. Sure, I could use it to make
         <em>
          anything
         </em>
         greenbut I wanted something unique. And look how beautiful these powdery little delights are.
        </p>
        <p>
         These are one of my favorite holiday cookies and this fun and earthy spin on Russian tea cakes is as much fun to look at as it is to eat!
        </p>
        <div class="yui3-g" id="grub-recipeSummary">
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-left">
          <h2>
           Ingredients
          </h2>
          <ul id="grub-ingredients">
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Cup Butter, unsalted
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Teaspoons vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Cup confectioner's sugar
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Cups Flour, sifted
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Tablespoons Matcha Green Tea
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Teaspoon Salt
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Cup Pistachios, chopped
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-right">
          <h2>
           <a class="grub-button" href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/printable/" target="_blank">
            <span class="grub-printIcon">
            </span>
            Printable View
           </a>
          </h2>
          <h2>
           Yield:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            2 dozen cookies
           </span>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT1H30M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
          <h2>
           Total Time
           <div style="float: right;">
            1 hour, 30 minutes
           </div>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT10M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Prep time
           <div style="float: right">
            10 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Inactive time
           <div style="float: right">
            1 hour
           </div>
          </div>
          <meta content="PT20M" itemprop="cookTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Cook time
           <div style="float: right">
            20 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <h2>
           Oven Temperature:
           <span>
            325 F
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Recipe Types:
           </h2>
           Baking, Dessert
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Diet:
           </h2>
           Vegetarian
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees F.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. In the bowl of an electric mixer, cream one cup of butter along with 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="733" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/matcha-green-tea/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/matcha-green-tea" rel="attachment wp-att-4128">
          <img alt="Matcha Green Tea" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4128" height="440" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Matcha-Green-Tea-600x440.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. In a separate bowl, combine  cup of confectioners sugar, 2 cups of flour,  teaspoon salt and 2 tablespoons matcha green tea. Gradually add it to the butter mixture until just combined.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Mix in the  cup of chopped pistachios. What is it about pistachios? I simply love their color.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="665" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/pistachios/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/pistachios" rel="attachment wp-att-4129">
          <img alt="Pistachios" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4129" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Pistachios-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         5 Shape the dough into 1-inch balls and place them 2 inches apart on parchment-lined baking sheets.
        </p>
        <p>
         6. Place the baking sheets in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to firm up the dough. This will help them keep their roundish shape during baking. Of course, if you dont care about that, just bake em.
        </p>
        <p>
         7. Bake for 20 minutes, or until edges are very lightly browned.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1215" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/rolling-cookies/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/rolling-cookies" rel="attachment wp-att-4130">
          <img alt="Rolling Cookies" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4130" height="729" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rolling-Cookies-600x729.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         8. Remove the cookies from the baking sheets and roll them in powdered sugar while still hot. Place them on wire racks for 30 minutes to cool and roll cookies in powdered sugar again before serving.
        </p>
        <p>
         Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="436" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/russian-green-tea-cookies-2/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/russian-green-tea-cookies-2" rel="attachment wp-att-4132">
          <img alt="Russian Green Tea Cookies-2" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-4132" height="261" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Russian-Green-Tea-Cookies-2-600x261.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1517" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/russian-green-tea-cookies/russian-green-tea-cookies-3/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/russian-green-tea-cookies-3" rel="attachment wp-att-4136">

         </a>
        </p>
