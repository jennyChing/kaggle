        <h1>
         What is the worlds deadliest creature?
        </h1>
        <p>
         Its not a big animal, and it doesnt have sharp teeth. You wont find it caged up at the zoo or lurking in the snow  nope! It may surprise you to learn that the worlds deadliest creature is the mosquito.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="An infographic outlining the worlds deadliest creature  the mosquito, which kills close to 1 million people every year." src="https://plancanada.ca/image/_new2015/newsupdates2015/2015_MalariaDayInfograph_UPDATED_LH_v3_forweb.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <h5>
         A tiny creature with a deadly bite
        </h5>
        <p>
         Believe us, its true! Mosquitoes kill nearly 1 million people every year. Thats more than sharks, snakes, hippopotamuses, grizzly bears and crocodiles combined. If youre shocked, wait until you hear whats next.
        </p>
        <p>
         Close to 600,000 of those deaths are caused by
         <a href="http://plancanada.ca/page.aspx?pid=4641">
          malaria-infected mosquitoes.
         </a>
         90% of all malaria-related deaths occur in Africa. Sadly, most of those deaths occur among children under the age of 5.
        </p>
        <h5>
         You can help fight against malaria
        </h5>
        <p>
         But its possible to fight these malaria-infected mosquitoes. All it takes is one
         <a href="https://plancanada.ca/Giftsofhope/shopexd.asp?id=38" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'clickthroughFromPlan','link', 'GOH-bednets']); ">
          anti-malaria bed net
         </a>
         to make a difference in the lives of children and families in Africa. Sleeping under these long-lasting anti-malaria bed nets can protect 2 children for up to 3 years.
        </p>
        <p>
         The bed nets are treated with a long-lasting insecticide that repels and kills malaria-infected mosquitoes, making bed nets one of the most effective and cost-efficient way to protect against malaria.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bed nets save lives. You can too!
        </p>
        <h5 class="morelink">
         <a href="https://plancanada.ca/Giftsofhope/shopexd.asp?id=38" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'clickthroughFromPlan','link', 'GOH-bednets']); ">
          Buy a bed net, today
         </a>
        </h5>
