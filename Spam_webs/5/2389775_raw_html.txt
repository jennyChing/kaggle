       <p>
        Are you worried on how to make your house look modish but you lack the cash to accomplish your dreams? You can now redecorate your room perfectly just by incurring low amount of cash. These are some of the best tips that will enable you make your room look attractive and sophisticated by spending less.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Use stickers
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <a data-rel="lightbox-0" href="http://solitarytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/kitchen-Wall-Stickers-Decoration-Idea.jpg">
         <img alt="kitchen-Wall-Stickers-Decoration-Idea" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-35" height="571" src="http://solitarytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/kitchen-Wall-Stickers-Decoration-Idea.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <img alt="" src="http://www.designbolts.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Beautiful-Wall-Stickers-Wall-Art-Decals-to-decor-bedrooms.jpg"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Stickers are very attractive on any wall. They will enable you decorate your room to look attractive. Since there are umpteen pretty stickers and of different colors online, you can therefore find the best stickers that will match the color of your walls. To individuals who are football lovers can find wide range of these stickers. For example, you can find sticker of your favorite team at low costs to decorate your room.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Use of graffiti
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <img alt="" src="http://image.melodyhome.com/images/upload/1(1088).jpg"/>
        <br/>
        The use of graffiti is one of the best and cheap decorating ideas to make your house splendid. They are very attractive and easy to craft. You will not spend much to come up with the art you may need on your walls. They will create smart design in your house hence some of the best interior design to decorate you house.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Gorgeous magazine pages
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <img alt="" src="http://www.lenzflare.net/img/2014/6/licious-modern-wall-paint-ideas.jpg"/>
        <br/>
        Look out for high quality and attractive pictures you may love in the magazines. From the magazines, you will come across wide range of quality pictures ranging from attires, films, bands and even beautiful houses. These will provide smart cover on your walls and thereby creating that splendid appearance in your house. The magazine pages will not cost you much despite their attractive nature.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Blackboard paint
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <img alt="Blackboard Paint" src="http://indierocket.com/chalks5.jpg"/>
        <br/>
        The blackboard paint is a perfect interior design for your house. With the blackboard paints, you can create smart decoration on the wall and from it you can create other smart features. For example, creating your own calendar and even playing point for the hangman. This will cost you very less amount but at the end you will have achieved the best designs within your house.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Use of doodling
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Doodling is another smart decoration idea that you can use to make your room look modish and attractive. It is all simple because you just need to apply the skills of doodling on your book and finally you will come up with perfect and high quality designs. This can be done annually to change the decoration within your room.
       </p>
       <p>
        Fabric, fairy lights, paint colors, rugs, palaroids and posters are other design ideas you can use to decorate your room at low costs.
       </p>
