        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.solutel.sa/blog/wp-content/uploads/Website-protocols.jpg">
          <img alt="Web design protocals " class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-32" height="225" src="http://www.solutel.sa/blog/wp-content/uploads/Website-protocols-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The success or failure of a website depends on utility factor and how user friendly the website is. Its the end user who decides everything as he is the one who has the control over the mouse. And hence it is so very important to design a web site keeping in mind a user-centric design. There is no point to have an amazing feature on the website if the user just cannot use it.
        </p>
        <p>
         For us to design an
         <a href="http://www.solutel.sa/it/web-designing.php">
          <strong>
           effective Web design
          </strong>
         </a>
         , its very important for us to understand the interaction that the users have with websites, their thought process and their behavior pattern. Quality &amp; credibility of the content is what a user looks for. Even if the website is not so good to eyes with its design, it still gains a lot of traffic for its high quality content and Traffic. The other most important thing to understand is that the users scan through the site and do not read it. They are very impatient as they choose the first reasonable/accessible option which they think will lead to their goal. They do not look for the quickest way instead follow their intuition.
        </p>
        <p>
         A lot of these factors should be taken into consideration to make sure that the Website has an impact on all its users. And for me to make an effective website, I follow the following 5 protocols.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1)
         </strong>
         The users are impatient and so to make the website real effective it becomes very important to make sure that the users do not think. If the navigation and the website architecture is not designed properly, it makes it difficult for the end user to understand how the website and its functionality works. A clear navigation path , more visual clues and a self-explanatory structure will make the user not think and makes his experience enjoyable.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2)
         </strong>
         Focusing on Users attention management is one of the important keys. Not all content will grab the same attention, image content is more eye catching than the text, conveying the right message with the image is a key as it keeps the user to think less and makes it easily understandable.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3)
         </strong>
         Simplicity is the sign of success. A statement well suited statement for an effective website. Users are not there only to check out the design, there motto is for the right information. Complexity will not help the cause of the end user, and so make sure website design is plain and simple yet elegant.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4)
         </strong>
         Communication of the right information is also one of the major keys when it comes to effective
         <a href="http://www.solutel.sa/it/development.php">
          <strong>
           Website development
          </strong>
         </a>
         . Navigation, screen layout &amp; relationship are the key components for an organized structure. The User Interface should be matched to the users capability. It should keep in balance the readability, typography, multiple views, texture, feel and color in order to communicate effectively.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5)
         </strong>
         Conventions are major components when it comes to gain the confidence of the users, their trust, reliability and to prove your credibility. Understand what the users are expecting from the website navigation, text structure &amp; Search placement etc. If conventions are used appropriately, then the users will be able to achieve a not too specific objective even if they do not understand a word of it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bottom Line, follow these protocols and make an extra ordinary effective website.
        </p>
