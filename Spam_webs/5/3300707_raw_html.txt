        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups
        </h1>
        <span style="font-size: 10pt;">
         This post may contain affiliate links or sponsored content, please read our
         <a href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/disclosure-policy/" target="_blank">
          Disclosure Policy.

        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-06-30T20:21:27+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 30, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/author/savoryexperiments/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Savory Experiments

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Before preparing it today, Ihadnt had JELL-O since I was a child. My mother used to make orange flavor with mandarin oranges mixed in. I just loved it!  As Ive gotten older the gelatin concoction lost its luster, but Im bringing it back!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Red-White-and-Blue-JELL-O-Cups.jpg">
          <img alt="Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups - simple, healthy and patriotic! Make these for 4th of July! | #4thofjuly | www.savoryexperiments.com" class="alignnone wp-image-3185" data-wp-pid="3185" height="800" src="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Red-White-and-Blue-JELL-O-Cups-737x1024.jpg" width="576"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Did you know you can make white JELL-O? Actually it isnt really JELL-O, because that is a brand, but using unflavored gelatin and milk, you can achieve a creamy white middle layer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/JELL-O-Cups.jpg">
          <img alt="Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups - simple, healthy and patriotic! Make these for 4th of July! | #4thofjuly | www.savoryexperiments.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-3187" data-wp-pid="3187" height="800" src="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/JELL-O-Cups-680x1024.jpg" width="532"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups are perfect for any BBQ, but especially 4th of July, Memorial Day or Labor Day. Easy to make, using only one sauce pan and easy to clean up, use disposable plastic cups- they are sure to please your crowd. Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups can be made up to a day ahead of time, but for best results wait to garnish with fresh fruit and whipped cream until right before serving. Also make sure to check out my
         <a href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/red-white-and-blue-cherry-pie/">
          Red, White and Blue Cherry Pie
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/american-flag-sugar-cookie-cake/">
          All American Sugar Cookie Cake
         </a>
         and my
         <a href="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/american-flag-cake/">
          Pinterest FAIL American Flag Cake
         </a>
         !
         <br/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://www.savoryexperiments.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/JELL-O-Cups-5-1024x906.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSSaveBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSSaveBtn bigoven" href="">

           </span>
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">

          </div>

         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Red, White and Blue JELL-O cups are a simple and festive addition to any 4th of July celebration.
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 3-ounce package blue berry JELL-O
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 3-ounce package cherry JELL-O
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups boiling water, divided
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 packet Knox unflavored gelatin
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup milk, divided
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tablespoons sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Whipped cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Strawberries and blueberries for garnish (optional)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 plastic wine cups
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Start by emptying contents of blue berry JELL-O into a small bowl. Stir for 2 minute with 1 cup boiling water. Allow to cool for 5-10 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place plastic wine glasses onto a small tray or plate (this just makes it easier to move them in and out of the refrigerator.) Equally divide blue berry JELL-O mixture between four cups. Place in the refrigerator for 15-20 minutes or until JELL-O has start to set and can be touched.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Meanwhile, start preparing white layer by mixing one packet Know unflavored gelatin in a small bowl with  cup cold milk. Heat remaining milk,  cup, over high heat until just before boiling, remove. Do not scald. Add 3 tablespoons sugar and  teaspoon vanilla. Stir until dissolved and mix with cold milk and gelatin mixture. Stir until well mixed and allow to cool.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Evenly pour white gelatin over blue layers. Place back into the refrigerator for 15-20 minutes or until it starts to set.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Next, prepare red cherry JELL-O by emptying contents into a small bowl. Stir for 2 minute with 1 cup boiling water. Allow to cool for 5-10 minutes. When white layer has set, equally divide red JELL-O over white layer. Return to refrigerator to set for 3-4 hours.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Do not garnish until ready to serve, especially if it is a hot day, the whipped cream will melt fast! (Just take a look at the wilting cream in these photos and that was only 10 minutes!) Give each cup a dollop of whipped cream, a wedge of strawberry and a blueberry.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Enjoy your Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups!
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
          <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
           Notes
          </div>
          <div class="ERSNotes">
           Having an adults only party?? Make your Red, White and Blue JELL-O Cups vodka laced. To modify, soak fruit in vodka in an airtight container for 12 hours, drain. For red and blue JELL-O layers, use only  cup boiling water to mix gelatin. Allow to cool slightly before stirring in  cup vodka. You can also purchase alcoholic whipped cream if you so choose.
          </div>
