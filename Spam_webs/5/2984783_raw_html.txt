        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Candy Corn Pizza
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-10-16T15:47:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 16, 2013
         </time>
         |
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.sotipical.com/candy-corn-pizza/#comments">
           3 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Here is a fun way to enjoy your pizza this time of year. We make it every year around Halloween and its very {simple} to make!
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <b>
          Candy Corn Pizza
         </b>
        </div>
        <div>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Pizza Crust
          </li>
          <li>
           Pizza Sauce
          </li>
          <li>
           Shredded Mozzarella
          </li>
          <li>
           Shredded Cheddar
          </li>
          <li>
           Pepperoni or Tomato Slices
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
         Start with your pizza crust. I used a prepared crust, so I brushed olive oil and a little chopped garlic on it first. Then I added the pizza sauce. In this case, I just used leftover spaghetti sauce.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
         Next plan your candy corn layers! Begin with a center circle of cheese. Looking back, I should have used the mozzarella in the center since candy corn has white tips.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
         Then add either pepperoni, sliced tomatoes or just plain sauce for the red(orange) later.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
         Last is the yellow layer. Add the remaining cheddar cheese to the outer circle.( This is where I used the white.) Bake according to directions on the package. I baked for 10 minutes at 450deg.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
         Cool for about 5 minutes, then slice into candy corns!
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: justify;">
        </div>
