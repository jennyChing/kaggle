             <p>
              <img alt="Police arrest poacher who was filmed shooting close to Ghaxaq - CABS" height="412" src="http://gozonews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Police_checks_shotguns_CABS.jpg" style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; WIDTH: 720px; DISPLAY: block; HEIGHT: 412px; MARGIN-LEFT: auto; MARGIN-RIGHT: auto" title="Police arrest poacher who was filmed shooting close to Ghaxaq - CABS" width="720"/>
              This morning a team of the Committee Against Bird Slaughter (CABS) filmed a poacher while he was shooting at birds during the closed season close to Ghaxaq, the organisation said.
             </p>
             <p>
              The video footage was passed on to the Administrative Law Enforcement who identified the man within hours.
             </p>
             <p>
              According to CABS the man admitted to having fired the shots and was taken to the ALE headquarters for further interrogation.
             </p>
             <p>
              CABS said that its teams recorded a total of 137 shots fired after the Prime Minister closed the hunting season last Tuesday.
             </p>
             <p>
              Most of the shots were heard in the countryside around Safi, Hal-Far, Handaq, Marsaskala and Ghaxaq.
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               File photo by CABS shows police officers during an inspection of a hunters shotgun on Gozo last week.
              </p>
