        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Last week, we saw Continuing Claims decrease  proof, said the green shooters, of the imminent economic recovery.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Only, not so much:
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Those of you (who can still afford the luxury of) a trusty Bloomberg will note the exhaustion rate for jobless benefits 
          <strong>
           EXHTRATE
          </strong>
           reveals that people are not leaving the pool of continuing unemployment claims because they are getting new jobs; Rather, they are leaving because they have exhausted their benefits.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          They are now unemployed AND broke. That is hardly a green shoot . . .
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #ffffff;">
          &gt;
         </span>
        </p>
