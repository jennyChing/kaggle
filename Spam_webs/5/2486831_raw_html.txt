           <p>
            Would you fuck the inside of a foot?
           </p>
           <p>
            Some people would, apparently, because the Vajanklea sex toy in the shape of a foot with a vagina attached (as seen in our
            <a href="http://www.vice.com/read/give-your-valentine-a-fox-fur-butt-plug-456" target="_blank">
             Valentine's Day Gift Guide
            </a>
            )has been bankrolled. Last month, the item caused an international furor when news websites started reporting on it in a state of torrid incredulity. But now that the media frenzy has died down, what lies in store for the Vajankle?
           </p>
           <p>
            What many don't know, as Bronwen Keller of
            <a href="http://sinthetics.com/new-product-the-vajankle/" target="_blank">
             Sinthetics
            </a>
            the company that makes the Vajankleinforms me, is that the Vajankle was in fact created in 2013 when a repeat buyer contacted the company and asked if he could put a vagina on one of the feet they'd already manufactured. "He explained he wanted to be able to see and massage the foot while having sex with it," Keller says, "and so the Vajankle was born."
           </p>
           <p>
            Why, then, wasn't it until 2015 that the Vajankle shot to fame? Keller thinks the foot may have piqued the interest of the internet because an individual named Dixie De La Tour, founder of sex storytelling show
            <a href="http://bawdystorytelling.com/" target="_blank">
             Bawdy Storytelling
            </a>
            , began talking about the product on Twitter. And yet, despite the ensuing media attention, Sinthetics has probably sold fewer than 100 Vajankles to date.
           </p>

            Sinthetics don't just make vagina feet. No one could make a living thus. They specialize in a variety of alarmingly lifelike manikins (they prefer not to use the term "dolls," though many of their customers are comfortable doing so), and the Vajankle becomes less creepy the more time you spend on the website.
