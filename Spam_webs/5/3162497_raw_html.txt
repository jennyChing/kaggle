    <h3>
     <a href="http://real-estateresources.tumblr.com/post/74223170860/email-just-got-a-whole-lot-better">
      Email Just Got A Whole Lot Better
     </a>
    </h3>
    <p>
     <em>
      Did you know an average person spends nearly 30% of their time reading and responding to email?
     </em>
    </p>
    <div class="post_body">
     <p>
      Theres no way around it, Im a heavy email user and I wouldnt be half surprised if I spend nearly 45% of my time processing email. Ive accepted that email is a huge part of my life and a vital form of communication.
     </p>
     <p>
      What I cannot accept is the insane amount of unimportant, useless email I receive. Get a spam filter you say? Its not spam, its email Id like to see eventually, but it shouldnt be interrupting my day and distracting me.
     </p>
     <p>
      On a quest to find a solution, I quickly accumulated a notepad full of failed attempts, including Gmail Tabs.
     </p>
     <p>
      I knew I would need a solution that did several things:
     </p>
     <ol>
      <li>
       Prioritize my email based on importance
      </li>
      <li>
       Work seamlessly with my existing workflow
      </li>
      <li>
       Weed out unimportant email without removing it entirely
      </li>
      <li>
       Was compatible with my phone and tablet
      </li>
      <li>
       *Bonus* Automated my messy filter configuration
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
      As I reached my breaking point (bloodshot eyes, overgrown facial hair, nervous twitch, etc) a friend pointed me in the direction of a service called,
      <a href="http://www.sanebox.com/">
       SaneBox.
      </a>
      It is without a doubt the best thing to happen to email, well, since email was invented.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="image" src="http://33.media.tumblr.com/1c8ca94b578359efa30280c95540ff0d/tumblr_inline_n0n8va1x3G1sv2nv7.png"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      SaneBox is smart email management tool that analyzes your email relationships and decides whats important based on your past behavior. It then automatically filters out unimportant email into a separate folder (check) and summarizes it in a daily digest so you dont miss anything (check). This means your inbox is filled exclusively with important/high-priority email. It literally saves me at least 2 hours every day (check).
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="image" src="http://33.media.tumblr.com/d7d46b6096943bede978cfba622727d5/tumblr_inline_n0n8vpYBFS1sv2nv7.png"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      As if youre not salivating already, SaneBox also has a full suite of time saving features such as: SaneBlackhole - enables you to unsubscribe from annoying emails with 1-click, Snooze folders - allows you to snooze untimely emails until later, Automatic follow-up reminders - receive notifications when an important contact didnt respond to your email. SaneBox also works on any email service, client or device just by adding a folder (check &amp; check).
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       If youre sick of an overloaded inbox, give
       <a href="https://www.sanebox.com/">
        SaneBox
       </a>
       a try.
      </strong>
     </p>
