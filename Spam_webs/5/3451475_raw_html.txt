					&lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;
					&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;The Chakras are energy centres in our body. A healthy person has chakras that allow energy to flow through them without any problems. If there are blockages in any of the chakras we can have problems in that area of the body. For example, someone who has a persistent cough may disruption in energy flow through the throat chakra. Each chakra relates to a particular area of life.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;The throat chakra, for example, relates to self-expression, the truth and communication. A person with disrupted energy flow through the throat chakra may have issues with communication. Perhaps they are scared of communicating their true feelings and this may result in physical symptoms manifesting in the throat area. Chakra healers believe healing a chakra can help ease physical symptoms that may be manifesting because of the blocked energy.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; The Seven Chakras&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;span style="color: #ff0000;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 1 &lt;/strong&gt;  &lt;strong&gt;The Root or Base Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is red and is located at the base of the spine. It represents feeling grounded and our basic needs for survival, such as food, shelter and money.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; &lt;span style="color: #ff9900;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 2  The Sacral Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is orange and is located in the abdomen below the naval. It represents being open to new experiences and abundance.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; &lt;span style="color: #ffff00;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 3  Solar Plexus Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is yellow and is located in the abdomen above the naval. It represents confidence and self-sufficiency.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; &lt;span style="color: #339966;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 4  The Heart Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is green, located in the centre of the chest and represents our ability to give and receive love.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; &lt;span style="color: #0000ff;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 5  The Throat Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is blue, located in the middle of the neck and represents communication, truth and self-expression.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; &lt;span style="color: #99ccff;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 6  The Third Eye Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is indigo, located in the middle of the forehead and represents intuition and being able to focus on the greater scheme of things rather than the smaller details.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; &lt;span style="color: #cc99ff;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt; Chakra 7  The Crown Chakra &lt;/strong&gt;This is violet, located at the top of the head and represents the ability to connect to Spirit.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Chakra Healing&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
A chakra healer is specially trained to bring balance to each of the chakras. This will help the healing energy to flow through the body without any blockages that may cause physical symptoms. Some healers will use coloured crystals, the same colour as each chakra, to balance each chakra. Others will use reiki or some other form of healing energy to bring the chakras back into correct alignment.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;If you want to balance your chakras yourself, get into a meditative state by relaxing and taking deep breaths in and out and imagine a healing light entering your root chakra. It is orange when it enters and it changes colour to match each chakra as it moves up your body. Let the light circle clockwise like a small, coloured Catherine wheel as it moves to each chakra.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;The above chakra balancing exercise is more effective when combined with a discussion about blockages in a particular chakra. The healer, on noticing a blockage, may try to understand any issues in your life that may be causing the blockage. If you feel you may have a blockage in a particular chakra, read what it represents and see if you can identify anything going on in your life that may need to change.&lt;/p&gt;
