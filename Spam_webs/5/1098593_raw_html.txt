          <h1>
           Vans OTW and Mister Cartoon Team Up
          </h1>
          <div class="article-meta">
           <span class="date">
            09/28/11
           </span>
           <span class="sep">
            /
           </span>
           <span class="author">
            Author: Jason Hellinger
           </span>
          </div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 16px;">
            Vans OTW and Mr. Cartoon proudly present the Art of Lettering, a limited edition book featuring Cartoons personal photography. T
           </span>
           <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 16px;">
            he book was created in conjunction with Mr. Cartoons Art of Lettering seminar held in Los Anges this summer. During the seminar, Cartoon discussing how lettering has impacted his life  from graffiti art to vintage sign painting and modern day low rider script as well as illustration airbrushing and his concepts of tattooing. There will be a limited run of 818 books available at OTW retailers worldwide.
           </span>
          </p>
