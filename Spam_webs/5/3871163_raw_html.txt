         <p>
          A daybed is a great room furnishing that is useful for sleeping, sitting and lounging. It can also be used as a sofa.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/1u9kNlD">
           Elegant daybeds
          </a>
          add style with a simple, sleek or tailored look. Using a space saving daybed is a good idea when you need to make a small space functional. Quality daybeds are available that coordinate with your budget, taste and design needs.
         </p>
         <p>
          A modern daybed is an awesome consideration for a small bedroom. It will save space and is a good place to take a nap. Comfort, design and brand are features to consider when shopping for a
          <a href="http://bit.ly/1u9kQOe">
           modern daybed
          </a>
          . Daybed styles include mission, modern, sleigh, canopy and traditional. Their prices range from $300 to over $700. White, black, cherry, espresso, mahogany and maple are beautiful daybed finishes. Other decorative finishes and materials are metal, upholstered and wicker. Affordable daybeds offer many modern decor options. These are daybeds in medium to dark wood tones. A chrome silver or brass daybed has unique elegance in an updated bedroom setting. A trundle style daybed is a comfortable place for overnight guest. Trundle, storage and full size are also options to look for.
         </p>
         <p>
          <u>
           Decorating with a Daybed
          </u>
         </p>
         <p>
          An elegant daybed is a great inspiration for a decorative theme. A comfortable, contemporary
          <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/ebarnes33/beautiful-daybeds/">
           daybed
          </a>
          looks cozy and inviting. It can be the centerpiece for a romantic or a casual theme. This look features sumptuous, luxurious daybed linens and pillows. Room accents that coordinate with a daybed includes: a cozy floor rug, color and patterned modern curtains and a side table. A stool for beverages, decorative lamp and colorful pillows are personal touches in a decor that features a daybed.
         </p>
         <p>
          Soft, plush accent pillows, a blanket throw and modern daybed bedding is stylish elements for a daybed room scheme. Daybed linens in beautiful colors and textures are a wonderful layer of design. And they enhance a bedroom setting with plush and relaxing effect.
         </p>
         <p>
          A decorative area floor rug, coordinating drapes, modern lighting and artwork are stylish accents for a spare room space. The bed style, wood or metal finishes, color or animal print bedding and a faux fur throw are special design elements in a room centered with a daybed. Other decorative touches include: candles, a tray, indoor plant or floral arrangement. So whether you have a daybed in a small apartment or basement bedroom, your decorating options offer a chance to design a fun, daybed inspired makeover.
          <br/>
         </p>
