       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Grilled Artichokes
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-22T08:00:55+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 22, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/author/rwhiting/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Renee
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/side-dishes/grilled-artichokes/#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         I love artichoke season! I also love that we live in CA and artichokes grow throughout the state. In fact, if you google Artichoke Capital of the World up pops Castroville and its only a 30 minute drive from us.
        </p>
        <p>
         I know that not everyone likes artichokes, and I can understand that. Theyre kind of weird to eat. But if you can make it through enough of the leaves and get to the heart of the choke, oh my gosh, its delicious!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-4.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Grilled Artichokes-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1284" height="680" src="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-4.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.joyineveryseason.com/side-dishes/grilled-artichokes/&amp;media=http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-4.jpg&amp;description=Grilled Artichokes')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Even though I titled this post Grilled Artichokes, I did start off byboiling them until just tender. I love just a plain boiled/steamed artichoke, but seasoning and grilling them brought them to a whole new level.
         <span id="more-1280">
         </span>
         Once they were just tender, I cut them in half, seasoned them with melted butter, crushed garlic, and my
         <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/main-dishes/bbq-spice-blend/" target="_blank">
          BBQ Spice Blend
         </a>
         , and placed them on the grill.
        </p>
        <p>
         My plan was to serve them with a lemon aioli, but I was out of lemons, so I opted for some plain mayonnaise with a dash of BBQ Spice on it. It was perfect!! Do you like mayo with your artichokes??
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Grilled Artichokes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1281" height="453" src="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-1.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.joyineveryseason.com/side-dishes/grilled-artichokes/&amp;media=http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-1.jpg&amp;description=Grilled Artichokes')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Grilled Artichokes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1282" height="679" src="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-2.jpg" width="453"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.joyineveryseason.com/side-dishes/grilled-artichokes/&amp;media=http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-2.jpg&amp;description=Grilled Artichokes')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-3.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Grilled Artichokes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1283" height="680" src="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-3.jpg" width="453"/>

           </span>

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.joyineveryseason.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Grilled-Artichokes-5.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

        <p>
         I hope that next time youre in the mood for artichokes that youll try grilling them. If you do, Id love to hear what you think.

           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 TBSP butter (per artichoke)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 clove garlic (crushed) per artichoke
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            BBQ Spice Blend
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Mayonnaise
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Rinse artichokes and trim stem. Cut off a few of the small outer small leaves.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place artichokes in large pot, covered with salted water. Cover.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bring pot to boil, then reduce to simmer.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cook for about 30 minutes or until knife easily inserts into center of artichoke. Time will vary depending on size of artichoke.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            When artichokes are just tender, remove from pot, drain, and slightly cool. Cut in half lengthwise and remove the middle section right above the heart.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Melt butter with crushed garlic. I used 2 TBSP and 2 garlic cloves for two large artichokes. Measurements are approx.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Brush butter and garlic liberally onto cut side of artichoke.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Season with BBQ Spice Blend.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place artichokes, cut side down onto med/high grill.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Grill for 10-15 minutes or until they get golden with a few grill marks.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Enjoy your artichoke with mayo, melted butter, or fresh lemon.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="endeasyrecipe" style="display: none" title="style001">
          3.2.2802
         </div>
        </div>
        <br/>
        How to eat an artichoke: To eat, pull off a leaf and scrape the meat off the tender end with your front teeth. Dip the ends of the leaves in lemon juice and melted butter if desired.
