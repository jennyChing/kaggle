               <br/>
               Back in 2009, we designed a video booth using a MacMini and coded software in MaxMSP. It was ahead of its time but finicky to setup. As filmmakers our aim was totake the interviewer out of the equation and let participants interview themselves. Playing with the line between private &amp; public space, the booth captured answers of an openness and honesty that warmed our little storytelling hearts. So we built more booths  big and small, around the world  and collected stories from people from all over.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               When iPads began usinga HDfront-facing camera we decided to spread the love and set it free in app form  the Video Booth App.You now have the tools to explore stories and create an accessible environment for expression. Expect only the best sort of open, direct and unforgettable answers to questions.
