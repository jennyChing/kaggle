           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-content">

             A lie can travel halfway around the world while the truth is still putting on its shoes
            </a>
            . That thought flashed across my mind when I came across yet another recent news report filled with inaccuracies in the Oregonian, the largest daily newspaper in Oregon.
           </p>
           <p>
            The story is about a suspect involved in an accident, and police found several empty packets of

           </p>
           <p>

           </p>
           <p>
            Its laughable. The problem is that reporters can be lazy, and pick up the inaccuracies of those who were inaccurate before them. So the lies get compounded, passed along without any independent verification of the truth of such a claim.
           </p>
           <p>
            Invariably, the kratom community responds immediately to set the writer straight, and that happened in Oregon like its happened all over the country.

             Turns out Im not the only person who thinks so. Try a Google search on the topic and start reading. And weeping.
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
             It sucks even more if youre a fat kid.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Fat kids get picked on, all the time. Fat kids get teased, punched and picked last for pretty much everything, from sports activities to the school play. Unless the play is about fat kids.And look at these factoids:
            <a href="http://www.diabetes.org/advocacy/advocacy-priorities/prevention/preventing-obesity-diabetes.html" target="_blank">
             80 percent of fat kids become fat adults
            </a>
            . Fat adults are at higher risk of cardiovascular disease, diabetes and cancer. And those who were obese as children are twice as likely to die before age 55 than kids who were not overweight.
           </p>
           <p>
            If youre the parent of a fat kid, do you know whos to blame?You are. So quit killing your fat kid.
           </p>
           <p>
            Maybe that sounds harsh, but its true most of the time. All of those excuses we use  She has a slow metabolism. Hes just big-boned. Its a genetic problem that runs in the family  simply dont fly. The only genetic problem that comes up in most families is trying to get their fat kid into his jeans.

          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://lovefooty.net/are-you-considering-an-online-high-school-diploma/" rel="bookmark">
            Are You Considering An Online High School Diploma?
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            The introduction of the internet into education has helped change the way that students enroll in classes. One way that it has changed the world is that students can now choose to attend online high school versus the traditional high school experience. For some students, this is the best forum for learning and achieving their diploma or
            <a href="http://www.excelhighschool.com/ged-online-prep.html">
             GED online
            </a>
            . Here are just a few of the many benefits that come from learning via an online high school.
           </p>
           <p>
            First off, those who choose to learn at an online high school have the freedom to learn when they want instead of when the school does. For students that have to work to support a family or are attempting to save money so that they can attend college the 7 to 3 schedule of high school may not work. An online high school remedies this issue because students can learn at any time of the day or night so long as they keep up with their studies.
            <a href="http://www.careerstep.com/blog/personal-referral/flexibility-convenience-benefits-of-online-education">
             This flexibility allows students to complete their high school education that otherwise might be forced to drop off
            </a>
            .
