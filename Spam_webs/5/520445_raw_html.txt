          <p>
           Back in the good golden days, all one needed to play a great game was a stick and some mudthese days even playgrounds and parks have DJ boothsyep, this is where you get introduced to Fono. Designed by Yalp, a Dutch enterprise, the Fono is a public DJ booth that allows a person to play, mix, and FX tracks in the most uncomplicated manner. The solar-powered booth has about 14 light-up touch panels, one of which is a wireless dock. Place your smartphone on the dock, open your playlists and get the party started.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Fono is primarily aimed at the teeny generation, in order to get the young things connected to music and to encourage them to start DJing earlyhmmmsounds legit, but older teens will probably have a spin or two on this solar console.
