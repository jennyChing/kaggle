        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Poor Inventory Management Could be Ruining Your Profit &amp; Reputation
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <a class="post-edit-link">
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p class="entry-tagline">
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Poor-Inventory-Ruin-Proffit-Reputation-051415-banner" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7831" height="400" src="http://buzz.waspbarcode.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Poor-Inventory-Ruin-Proffit-Reputation-051415-banner1.jpg" width="940"/>
          When a business grows, a lot of small business owners
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/optimize-inventory-management/" target="_blank">
           feel pressured to keep their focus
          </a>
          on the day-to-day challenges of the business. That means seemingly mundane tasks, like
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/inventory-control">
           inventory management
          </a>
          , gets pushed aside. While counting inventory and restocking shelves might not seem like it should be a top priority, not making it one is a detrimental mistake.
         </p>
         <p>
          According to the U.S. Small Business Administration, a poorly managed inventory system is one of the major reasons small businesses fail, yet 46 percent of small businesses with 11 to 499 employees dont have any kind of system to track their goods. Not only does this lead to production shortages, it also takes a big hit on customer satisfaction ratings. Other times, companies find that they have too much stock on hand, leading to steep wastes much like
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/5-lean-inventory-principles/" target="_blank">
           burning money,
          </a>
          as Marcus Lemonis assured on his CNBC show
          <i>
           The Profit
          </i>
          . In all cases, the companys reputation is at stake and the high costs dig into the business bottom line.
         </p>

         <p>
          Additionally, employees end up wasting countless hours searching for misplaced items, especially if an error is made during
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/maturing-excel-inventory-management/">
           manual tracking
          </a>
          . Not surprisingly, human errors are frequently made, a skilled operator averages one error in every 300 keystrokes. Once a data entry mistake is made, its hard to detect and remove, oftentimes resulting in more costly errors and devastating the companys entire supply chain management and accounting systems.
         </p>
         <p>
          Forward-thinking companies fully understand that a proper inventory management system has the power to maximize sales and minimize costs, yielding higher profits.
         </p>
         <p>
          A 2014 IBM report found that 54 percent of Chief Supply Chain Officers surveyed admitted to exploring inventory strategies to reduce costs. According to Microsofts Midsize Business Center, companies that make inventory a priority end up decreasing their inventory costs by
          <a href="http://www.scanco.com/the-true-cost-of-poor-inventory-management/">
           as much as 35 percent
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" width="810">
          <tbody>
           <tr>
            <td height="25" valign="top" width="810">
             <hr/>
             <h5>
              <em>
               Before [our inventory control system], each person was probably wasting two to four hours a week tracking inventory, recalled Spare. [Now] When someone comes in looking for a piece of equipment, we can instantly look at the database, see what we have, where it is and what we need to order
              </em>
             </h5>
             <hr/>
            </td>
           </tr>
          </tbody>
         </table>
         <p>
          Take for example the
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/case-studies/inventory-control/bay-area-air-quality-management-district" target="_blank">
           Bay Area Air Quality Management District
          </a>
          which, until recently, struggled to keep accurate counts of its extensive stock of replacements parts sourced through nearly 100 suppliers. Before implementing enterprise-level
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/inventory-control" target="_blank">
           inventory control software
          </a>
          , the district replied on Microsoft Excel, resulting in technicians wasting valuable hours every week looking for
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/stockroom-catastrophe-excel-bad-inventory-tracking/" target="_blank">
           needed parts
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Poor Inventory Management Could be Ruining Your Profit &amp; Reputation" class="size-medium wp-image-7814 alignleft" height="315" src="http://buzz.waspbarcode.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/twitter-051415-161x315.jpg" width="161"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Even worse, with no central tracking method, the district couldnt give management an accurate answer when asked how their budget was being used, continued Bibeau.When a technician needed a replacement part, he would spend between two and three hours calling around to the various field technicians looking for it, said Bryan Bibeau, a senior air quality instrument specialist for the Ambient Monitoring group. Since we replace between four and five parts a week, as a group we were wasting between 500 and 750 hours a year just tracking down parts.
         </p>
         <p>
          Another example of a business that turned things around is the University of Oxford, which up until recently was only using 
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/4-reasons-excel-inventory-management-doesnt-work/" target="_blank">
           a few spreadsheets
          </a>
           here and there to run its entire telecom department, said Jemima Spare, IT Support Officer for Telecoms, which meant only if you could physically see the stock you needed, then it could be used. Since then, the university has buckled down with an inventory control system equipped with barcodes, labels, serial number details, and an automated database that tracks each item thats taken from stock.
         </p>
         <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" width="810">
          <tbody>

            <td height="25" valign="top" width="810">

                <span style="color: #3366ff;">
                 What is Inventory Turnover
                </span>
               </a>
              </span>
             </h5>
             <hr/>
            </td>
           </tr>
          </tbody>

         <p>
          But simply implementing inventory control software isnt enough. The most strategic business owners know that its crucial to choose the right system for their business and keep up with trends at the same time. What are your competitors using? What are the pros and cons of their system? What do you want in an inventory management system? These are all questions business owners should ask themselves.
         </p>
         <p>
          Once you find a system that fits your business model, properly train your staff to use the new system. If your staff isnt informed, the money you spent just went down the drain.
         </p>
         <p>
          Small business owners who understand their bottom line knows that
          <a href="http://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/inventory-management-101/" target="_blank">
           poor inventory control
          </a>
          can destroy your financial security in no time. Not using a formal, up-to-date, technological system makes little sense considering the massive growth opportunities available to companies smart enough to implement an effective inventory management system.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           How would properly training your staff on best inventory tracking practices to save your business time and money?
          </b>
         </p>
