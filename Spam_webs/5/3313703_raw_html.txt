       <div class="post_header_wrapper">
        <h1 class="post_title">
         Xiaomi smartphones can steal bank card data via NFC
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="post_detail">
        June 12, 2014
			    							By
        <a href="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/author/paganinip">
         PierluigiPaganini
        </a>
       </div>
       <br class="clear"/>
       <br/>
       <!-- Begin each blog post -->
       <div class="post_wrapper" style="padding-top:0;">
        <div class="post_inner_wrapper">
         <div class="post_wrapper_inner">
          <div class="post_inner_wrapper">
           <g:plusone href="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/25691/hacking/xiaomi-nfc-steal-bank-card-data.html" size="standard">
           </g:plusone>
           <div class="fcbk_share">
            <div class="fcbk_button">
             <a href="http://www.facebook.com/197355730374940" target="_blank">
              <img alt="Fb-Button" src="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/facebook-button-plugin/images/standard-facebook-ico.png"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="fcbk_like">
             <fb:like href="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/25691/hacking/xiaomi-nfc-steal-bank-card-data.html" layout="button_count" share="false" show_faces="false" width="450">
             </fb:like>
            </div>
           </div>
           <h2>
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="cad27426-7bf6-473b-9a0b-8e1e85149d65">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="51ed592f-77f5-4824-8eec-8f888830e936">
              Chinese woman
             </span>
            </span>
            accidentally discovered that its Xiaomi
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="cc423d92-f853-4606-be17-5a5e63f7a0ed">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="c8eab3d6-2e00-4a86-b786-8d17bf82daa5">
              smartphone
             </span>
            </span>
            has the capability to steal bank card data via near field communication.
           </h2>
           <p>
            A report issued by
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             the
            </span>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Nanjing-based Yangtse Evening News states that
            </span>
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="8b00d1f0-1b66-4d83-a24d-465ce93eeb37">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="8dc25886-1655-431a-9141-443c25ef883a">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="8e31b16f-f74c-4f98-8d70-b7f30aad99aa">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="1a2138db-8b05-41f1-93de-0aedfef957f2">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="bd60dbf9-83c4-471c-ae61-1f181387a86e">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="be245217-98b9-45c9-92aa-980a10b65495">
                  <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="e1653a09-fa6f-4efe-a88f-e8810d712b8a">
                   <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="1574ec44-1d08-43bc-8cce-bd4a5210874b">
                    <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="105b7754-a7c0-4e13-8029-71df3e3caffa">
                     smartphones
                    </span>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            produced
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             by Chinese Xiaomi are able to
            </span>
            steal bank card data from wireless connections. Rumors reports that a
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             woman from Nanjinghas revealed to
            </span>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             the newspaper that her new Xiaomi
            </span>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="6af39870-f04f-4121-9fae-9238c430e392">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="3e2a20a5-a648-4fcd-ae95-240c809de77d">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="4c2215a9-f27d-4a10-9888-db41cec1757c">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="43d8f72d-625e-470d-ae3b-e9f369390058">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="af1784c1-b8aa-44d0-801a-0c03e8e9b981">
                  <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="9d1b7480-bc87-4049-b4b2-607415c3c96c">
                   <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="7bb79687-48eb-4e5b-ad73-deec5146dc20">
                    <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="db442baa-afcd-4be1-827e-fe8142d671b7">
                     <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="a6b2b88e-bfd3-4660-9a8f-f02e63fbcd51">
                      smartphone
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             managed to automatically pick up private account details from a bank card stored in close proximity.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            The woman,
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="50978cc3-7c25-467d-bfa4-673c2060ab21">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="41f4a8e5-9342-4388-900f-b94c6650e61d">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="f65ff660-4433-43c2-bd8b-7174a2dc60de">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="fe6e761c-66f7-4cac-b6ee-162895d90e53">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="bd8212b4-8070-4e8c-b3e5-213f328784dc">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="340e86b6-762d-4486-b350-b40b265c478f">
                  <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="bfac3530-077a-4d30-b718-2deecc56ebee">
                   <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="1336e7b8-f967-418b-aad0-88e6ce39a663">
                    <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="562fb114-fac5-4c54-bc76-59ec14276223">
                     surnamed
                    </span>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            Feng, was surprised by noticing that the data was displayed directly on the display of her device, the data sent to the
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="39762a07-17b6-47da-b281-f8431653f067">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="dd21f0dc-51ae-46dc-9ed2-72013338b10d">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="53f54636-8a39-44e7-ae31-78899d095844">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="e561a15b-61c3-45c1-bbee-7a5f73220822">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="128ea38c-8491-4839-b12a-ab8d61ee0de8">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="1bfce1b7-65bd-4f83-a94f-f325613cf872">
                  <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="ddbc55ae-9ae8-44e4-a45c-dbef6228a834">
                   <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="2e40df84-e824-4f79-86f3-2755aa2d5123">
                    <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="6739b0b6-5ef7-494a-849e-44b391c3338d">
                     smartphone
                    </span>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            included the card number stored in close proximity and the accounts last 10 transactions with related amounts and locations.
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <em>
              Feng, who said she had not accessed her bank account on her phone or entered her password, initially thought it may have been the work of spyware, though she soon realized it was an automatic function because her bank card could still be read even after she closed all running applications.
              <a href="http://www.wantchinatimes.com/news-subclass-cnt.aspx?id=20140610000087&amp;cid=1103" target="_blank" title="http://www.wantchinatimes.com/news-subclass-cnt.aspx?id=20140610000087&amp;cid=1103">
               states
              </a>
              the
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="c888f3c3-b01b-46f4-bb9f-5ca149d8b495">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="8f82f6de-461d-4c51-ac41-de942b3671a7">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="4438ed6c-4b8f-466e-85cf-9fcd5924b43a">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="ce5040cb-6625-4331-850e-86feb38ee430">
                  <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="16e45238-0f5b-41b3-88f8-89d941b620b7">
                   <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="3129fbe5-50ce-4c4f-bc6f-e18e0ea1f989">
                    <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="bf15bfcc-27d4-4098-82fa-6865093ccc24">
                     <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="5842648b-9798-4b65-9578-ee8ddadecfc3">
                      WChina
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
              Times.
             </em>
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            The journalists at Yangtse Evening News decided to verify Fengs revelation and confirmed that
            <a href="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/5090/hacking/nfc-business-opportunities-security-and-privacy-issues.html" target="_blank" title="NFC, business opportunities, security and privacy issues">
             near field communication
            </a>
            (NFC) mode had been activated on her Xiaomi device.
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <em>
              <span style="color: #444444;">
               Near field communication(NFC) is a set of standards forSmartphonesand similar devices to establishradiocommunication with each other by touching them together or bringing them into close proximity. The standard describes a radio technology that allows two devices to communicate at a short distance, no more than a few centimeters, allowing the exchange of information quickly and safely. I reported in a previous post
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="f2600748-62f3-4268-a5bf-b2b2090e6ddc">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="279b14bd-0c9f-4f21-9cbd-e980f154d19c">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="fef65ab5-baaf-47ba-be9b-58fab3e1e6e4">
                  <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="5b084492-2e16-4a3d-9ebb-4f64c2cf4ace">
                   <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="3a47e3b0-1862-4779-9878-6821d5cdc197">
                    <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="cab5f1ab-9785-4919-9073-583e6041518c">
                     <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="672dcc1c-9b0b-4ded-8eec-5bf7fccce8f8">
                      in
                     </span>
                    </span>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               NFC standards.
              </span>
             </em>
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            The disconcerting discovery made by the experts in charge of the Yangtse Evening News newspaper is that the Fengs phone couldretrieve details from a microchip bank card automatically in two seconds from within a range of about 10 centimeters.
           </p>
           <p>
            Now image a classic scenario when in a crowded place, an attackerpass very close to your wallet, in this context, he can stealpersonal information from
            <a href="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/25134/cyber-crime/chip-and-pin-cloning.html" target="_blank" title="Chip-and-PIN cards easily cloning with the pre-play attack">
             bank cards
            </a>
            without the victims knowledge. We have discussed many times onthe security issues related to the NFC and possible abuses of the technology.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Xiami-mobile-2.jpg">
             <img alt="Xiaomi mobile 2" class="aligncenter wp-image-25699 size-full" height="304" src="http://securityaffairs.co/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Xiami-mobile-2.jpg" width="450"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Feng declared to be shocked by the behavior of the
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="ab04d7c3-1911-461c-86a1-d5f0265acdb7">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="e1e83796-1814-4e4a-97f8-fea0909909e2">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="0485d79a-74ea-4444-8559-e5478c54309b">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="3501b441-9086-4f30-a80c-cb5cd17cb95b">
                <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="6cf48941-7f7d-41c6-84ab-7c0d5776164e">
                 <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="a537026a-a702-4fc4-b9ed-8aa6d13af5bb">
                  smartphone
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            and she believed Xiaomi should have warned its customers of this potentially serious security flaw that could expose them to the theft of personal information from bank cards.
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <em>
              She said when she called customer support she was told that she could simply switch off the NFC function if she had concerns.
             </em>
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            I have no possibility to test the above feature
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="2617dd9b-b8f3-4811-8aa3-00eb2973b0c5">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="6957136f-f14a-40f1-8f9e-8319a19bb1f6">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="ad85f82a-ecf2-40a0-ae15-c6e77f30a3ff">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="da733103-1a8a-4907-ae49-526cd2bb4e5a">
                at
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            time Im
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="ad11ba7e-66aa-43fe-a7cb-e2c45ae886d1">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="f03d24f4-4a2d-4d82-99e4-bad31a74dfd2">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="ed400002-1dcc-4174-9fb7-611b18cf1257">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="50f411f3-24d3-49aa-9211-2b282c109c91">
                writing but
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            I consider the reply provided by customer support not adequate, a bad actor in fact could be able to serve a malware on victims mobile theoretically could turn on the NFC feature and listen to others bankcard in close proximity to steal the data and send them to a C&amp;C controlled by
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="5991ac5d-6e44-4c14-85ff-51d06fc81687">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="5b1d35f1-a768-467e-aaa3-4110b5d8c1ff">
              <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="98f9a0eb-5f73-444e-9c40-3d58cae5ac35">
               <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="68d340fd-db9d-4d55-8d0c-9a3bf81bb2f2">
                attacker
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Really worrying, lets hope
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Xiaomi and other manufacturers will consider seriously the NFC security issues.
            </span>
           </p>
