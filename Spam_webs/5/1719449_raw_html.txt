         <h1 class="story-title" itemprop="name">
          Smoked Paprika &amp; Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings
         </h1>
         <div id="post-info">
          By
          <span class="author" itemprop="author">
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/author/mewinebrenner/" rel="author" title="Posts by mewinebrenner">

         <div id="content-area">
          <p>
           I saw these cute little rainbow fingerling potatoes at a farmers market in Easton, MD. They lady selling them ensure me that these would be the best sweet potatoes Ive ever eaten. She included a variety of sweet potatoes and said a quick roast in the oven and theyd be perfect! I of course was sold the instant I saw that they were miniature in size and came in a variety of colors. But the fact that this farm lady was so intent on me buying them, made me think they were particularly special.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings1-e1414972578739.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="roasted sweet potatoes, sweet potato fingerlings, smoked paprika, side dish recipes, potato recipes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5608" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings1-e1414972578739.jpg?resize=620%2C411"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://beanafoodie.com/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/&amp;media=http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings1-e1414972578739.jpg&amp;description=Smoked Paprika and Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings11-e1414972603196.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="roasted sweet potatoes, sweet potato fingerlings, smoked paprika, side dish recipes, potato recipes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5618" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings11-e1414972603196.jpg?resize=620%2C411"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://beanafoodie.com/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/&amp;media=http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings11-e1414972603196.jpg&amp;description=Smoked Paprika and Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           And they were! I roasted these buggers in the oven with some smoked paprika, thyme, cumin and of course a heavy handed sprinkle of salt. Excuse my french, but these were the best damn sweet potatoes Ive ever eaten! And I dont even really like sweet potatoes. As in occasionally Ill eat them in the deep fried french fry form  maybe. But not baked, not in a pie, not candied and definitely not covered in any cinnamon sugar concoction. No sir, I do not like sweet potatoes. (OK, that was definitely a little like Dr. Seuss, but I just spent time reading Green Eggs &amp; Ham to my nephew soits in my head).
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings4-e1414972626352.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="roasted sweet potatoes, sweet potato fingerlings, smoked paprika, side dish recipes, potato recipes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5611" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings4-e1414972626352.jpg?resize=620%2C411"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://beanafoodie.com/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/&amp;media=http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings4-e1414972626352.jpg&amp;description=Smoked Paprika and Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings9-e1414972654816.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="roasted sweet potatoes, sweet potato fingerlings, smoked paprika, side dish recipes, potato recipes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5616" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings9-e1414972654816.jpg?resize=620%2C411"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://beanafoodie.com/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/&amp;media=http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings9-e1414972654816.jpg&amp;description=Smoked Paprika and Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           As does Sam, I actually decided to try these sweet potatoes and was delighted at the flavor! Just slightly sweet, but the smokey paprika and cumin and earthy thyme really made these sweetroots delicious. They cooked very quickly due to their small size. And even better, since they are younger potatoes, their skins are thin and perfectly good to eat. No peeling required!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings6-e1414972789139.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="roasted sweet potatoes, sweet potato fingerlings, smoked paprika, side dish recipes, potato recipes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5613" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings6-e1414972789139.jpg?resize=620%2C411"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://beanafoodie.com/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/&amp;media=http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings6-e1414972789139.jpg&amp;description=Smoked Paprika and Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings12-e1414972830444.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="roasted sweet potatoes, sweet potato fingerlings, smoked paprika, side dish recipes, potato recipes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5619" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings12-e1414972830444.jpg?resize=620%2C411"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://beanafoodie.com/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/&amp;media=http://beanafoodie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/sweetfingerlings12-e1414972830444.jpg&amp;description=Smoked Paprika and Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           If youre not a sweet potato fan, Id highly recommend this recipe. I promise, itll be something you really enjoy!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://beanafoodie.com/recipes/smoked-paprika-thyme-sweet-potato-fingerlings/" target="_blank" title="Smoked Paprika &amp; Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings">
             Smoked Paprika &amp; Thyme Sweet Potato Fingerlings
            </a>
            :
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Ingredients:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            1 lb sweet potato fingerlings  cleaned and sliced in half length wise
           </li>
           <li>
            1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
           </li>
           <li>
            1 tsp salt
           </li>
           <li>
            1/2 tsp pepper
           </li>
           <li>
            1 tsp smoked paprika
           </li>
           <li>
            1/2 tsp cumin
           </li>
           <li>
            1 tsp dried thyme
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           Instructions:
          </p>
          <ol>
           <li>
            Preheat oven to 425F.
           </li>
           <li>
            Place cleaned fingerlings on a large sheet tray or baking dish. Toss with olive oil and all the spices ensuring the potatoes are thoroughly coated.
           </li>
           <li>
            Lay out potatoes with cut side facing down  this will ensure a nice brown caramelization. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the potatoes are fork tender. Serve immediately!
           </li>
          </ol>
