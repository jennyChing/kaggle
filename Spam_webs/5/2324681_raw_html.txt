        <h2>
         <strong>
          Deluxe Yoga Retreat in Positano
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         Sole Yoga Holidays is overjoyed to return for our 5th retreat at La Selva, nestled high amongst the glorious cliffs of Positano, Italy. This year, we are bringing back our "deluxe" package with more activities and goodies! At the eco-resort, La Selva, nature reigns supreme, surrounded by olive groves, lemon orchards and gorgeous coastal views.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h6>
         <strong>
          Powerful yoga retreat in a heavenly place
         </strong>
        </h6>
        <p>
         This is a true yoga retreat where we all share accommodations. It is what Sole Yoga Holidays calls its "Adult Summer Camp" Retreat! Daily powerful, Ashtanga flow yoga is practiced on an outdoor deck with stunning views of Positano or inside a natural rock room with floor to ceiling windows. The shared rooms are rustic, charming and quiet in the evening. The meals are fresh, filling, incredibly delicious and 100% vegetarian made with ingredients from the land.Our wonderful hosts also make homemade jams, jellies, teas and honey that we get to indulge in daily!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

          Vacation highlights

         <h3>
          <strong>
           Sole Yoga Holidays Positano, Italy Yoga Program
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          We will practice on either the outside deck with breathtaking views of the Amalfi Coast or in a covered yoga room with floor to ceiling windows that open fully to enjoy the gorgeous, natural beauty of La Selva's surroundings. In between yoga, hike down into town for swimming or shopping or take the bus into other adorable Amalfi Coast towns, like Amalfi, Praiano or Ravello. You can also hike around the famous trails or for the more adventurous, try rock climbing with experienced, local guides.Daily power Ashtanga yoga will be led by instructor Shari Hochberg, Owner of Sole Yoga Holidays.We try to have lights out by 10.30 so we can fully enjoy each exciting and activity-filled day.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>

         <h3>
          <strong>
           Excursions
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          In between yoga, hike down into town for swimming or shopping or take the bus into other adorable Amalfi Coast towns, like Amalfi, Praiano or Ravello. You can also hike around the famous trails or for the more adventurous, try rock climbing with experienced, local guides.
         </p>
         <p>
          We will go on a 6-hour boat ride excursion one day through the gorgeous grottos of the coast and then a stopover in picturesque Capri. Another excursion includes a beach day, followed by late-lunch at famous Positano restaurant, Chez Black right at the sea!And let's not forget that everyone gets a pair of custom-made, leather sandals.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you wish to go out in the evenings in Positano, we offer a wonderful yogi/meals/excursion package without accommodations at La Selva. Inquire for more information.

         <h3>
          Shari Hochberg
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          Shari Hochberg will guide you through daily strengthening and invigorating Ashtanga lesson in the morning and late afternoon. Shari discovered the unlimited and positive effects of a consistent yoga practice over 12 years ago in San Francisco, California. Her journey included numerous disciplines, but it wasnt until Ashtanga that she found her true yoga passion and was certified in 2010 in Florence, Italy.
         </p>
         <p>
          She now enjoys teaching a vigorous, playful, and challenging Ashtanga Flow Class accompanied by an equally dynamic playlist. She hopes through her teaching she can inspire others with the grace and love that she has found. Shari has been leading yoga retreats and holidays for over 2 years in amazing location from Belize to India and Morocco, and all over her adopted and gorgeous country, Italy.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           Location
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          The retreat location is 30 minutes hike up from the town of Positano, but close enough for a few hours of beach time or shopping every day during your free time. This retreat is for an intermediate yogi and athlete as La Selva is only reachable by foot. This gorgeous location allows for a quiet, relaxing and private week-long retreat. The location does not allow for hiking when it is dark. If you wish to go out on the evenings in Positano, we offer a wonderful yogi/meals/excursion package without accommodations!Please note that the location does not allow for hiking when it is dark.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>

         <h3>
          <strong>
           Accommodation
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          All of the charming, natural rooms are shared on this retreat. There are 2 large bedrooms with 5 beds and two smaller rooms with 2 beds. The beds are comfortable and the rooms, although simple in decor are spacious with plenty of room for your belongings.
         </p>
         <p>
          The larger bedrooms have bathrooms with shower in the rooms and the smaller rooms use the shared facilities outside the rooms. There is no air conditioning, but in the evenings, the temperatures cool down and delicious breezes filter through the rooms. Linens and towels are provided.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>

          <strong>
           Food
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          The food is locally grown and freshly prepared at La Selva. Special requests can be accommodated upon request. We are served an incredible variety of fresh salads, homemade granolas, warm and cold dishes of seasonal vegetables, rice and pasta. We will have special organic teas and delicious desserts every evening.In fact one of everyone's favorite discussions after yoga is always, "What do you think is on the menu today?"
         </p>
         <p>
          Each day the menu is as follows:
         </p>

          <li>
           Light breakfast (teas, coffee, fruit)
          </li>
          <li>
           Brunch (warm and cold dishes, salads, pastas, fruits-menu changes daily for something new each meal)
          </li>
          <li>
           Dinner (homemade pastas, cheeses, warm dishes, salads plus wine, teas and dessert)
          </li>
         </ul>
