            <h1>
             See the best of British with Europcar and the Tour de France!
            </h1>
            <h2 class="author">
             <time class="updated" datetime="2014-07-03T15:59:22+00:00" pubdate="">
              July 3, 2014
             </time>
            </h2>
            <p class="byline author vcard">
             By
             <a class="fn" href="http://blog.europcar.co.uk/author/emmaweed/" rel="author">
              Emma Weed
             </a>
            </p>
           </div>
           <!--= /post-header -->
           <div class="post-main">
            <div class="featured">
             <img alt="Yorkshire - Tour de France 2014" class="attachment-213x213 wp-post-image" height="166" src="http://blog.europcar.co.uk/assets/sites/7/2014/07/thumbnail.jpg" width="213"/>
            </div>
            <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
             <p>
              You dont need to travel to France to get your fill of cycling fever this summer. This years Tour de France kicks off this weekend, right here in beautiful Blighty. To mark the occasion, weve created these extra special maps for Stages 1-3 of The Grand Depart 2014 from Leeds to
              <a href="http://www.europcar.co.uk/car-hire-UNITED_KINGDOM-LONDON.html" target="_blank">
               London
              </a>
              , so you can be sure youre up to scratch on your two-wheeled facts and stats.
             </p>
             <p>
              So have you ever wondered how many sheep it would take to form a continuous row of furry followers from
              <a href="http://www.europcar.co.uk/car-hire-UNITED_KINGDOM-LEEDS.html" target="_blank">
               Leeds
              </a>
              to Harrogate? Lost sleep over the number of stacked margarine containers it would take to equal the lofty heights of Buttertubs Pass? Pondered how many Yorkshire Puddings you could safely work off as a contender for Le Tours Stage 1 yellow jersey?
             </p>
             <p>
              Probably not. But we bet you are now, right?
             </p>
             <p>
              Check out our fantastic maps to find out more and get your fill of Le Tour trivia. And if youre feeling inspired to grab a slice of the action for yourself,
              <a href="http://www.europcar.co.uk/" target="_blank">
               pick up a hire car from your nearest Europcar outlet
              </a>
              and head down to one of our
              <a href="http://blog.europcar.co.uk/2014/07/01/the-ten-best-places-to-watch-the-tour-de-france-in-britain/" target="_blank">
               ten top spots to see the Tour de France in Britain
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              We dont know about you, but we dont plan on missing a moment
             </p>
