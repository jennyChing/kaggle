   <h1>
    Pope Saint John Paul II
   </h1>
   <div class="category_filter">
   </div>
   <div class="category_items">
    <div class="cat_desc">
     <p>
      Pope John Paul II was head of the Catholic Church for nearly 27 years. During that time, Pope John Paul II held a great influence over world affairs. He was named to the Time 100 list both in the 20th and 21st centuries. Pope John Paul II traveled the World over spreading the Catholic Churchs influence into third world nations and fighting the materialism of developed countries. He is referred to as Pope John Paul II The Great, and will be beatified by his successor, Pope Benedict XVI, on May 1, 2011. His feast day is October 22 - the day he was formally inaugurated to the papacy.
     </p>
     <p>
      "O God, who are rich in mercy and who willed that the Blessed John Paul II should preside as Pope over your universal Church, grant, we pray, that instructed by his teaching, we may open our hearts to the saving grace of Christ, the sole Redeemer of mankind. Who lives and reigns. Amen."
     </p>
