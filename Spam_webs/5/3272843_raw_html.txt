      <h1>
       Automobile Accidents
      </h1>
      <h2>
       Do You Need an Experienced Bronx Car Accident Lawyer?
      </h2>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       No matter how good your driving is, sometimes, accidents are unavoidable  especially if other Bronx drivers arent as attentive to driving responsibly.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Navigating the streets and thoroughfares of Brooklyn, Bronx, and the boroughs of New York City is a hazardous proposition for anyone. You may successfully avoid an accident for years, but the perils are always there. As too many accident victims can attest, the consequences of a collision can be life-changing. From property damage to medical bills to loss of income, victims can spend years recovering from the accident. Some will even experience symptoms for the rest of their lives. An experienced accident lawyer knows these facts and also knows what to seek in terms of compensation when taking your accident case to court.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       If you have been seriously injured in any type of auto accident in which you were not at fault, consult a Bronx car accident lawyer at the law firm of Macaluso &amp; Fafinski, P.C. for help making critical decisions about your future. We may be able to recover money you need to pay for medical expenses, replace lost wages, and deal with pain and suffering. You will not owe attorney fees unless we succeed in obtaining compensation for you.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       To Receive Compensation, Fast Action is Critical
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Macaluso &amp; Fafinski, P.C. are winning, experienced auto accident attorneys in Bronx that victims can rely on to ensure that they meet the necessary legal requirements associated with a car accident lawsuit. Some of these matters are highly time-sensitive, including:
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Filing claims and supporting documents with the insurance carrier responsible for your short-term medical expenses, including no-fault applications
       </li>
       <li>
        Obtaining police reports from the precinct where your accident occurred
       </li>
       <li>
        Contacting witnesses and obtaining statements concerning their observations regarding the accident
       </li>
       <li>
        Taking photographs of the scene
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Injury Lawyer for All Types of Vehicular Accidents: Cars, Motorcycles, Trucks, Bicycles, Pedestrian
      </h3>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Macaluso &amp; Fafinski, P.C. has represented injured drivers, passengers, cyclists, and pedestrians. Accident investigation is a critical element in building a successful injury case, and we employ proven experts who can assess vehicle damage, establish accident causality, and uncover all essential evidence. The Macaluso &amp; Fafinski team has successfully worked on many varied types of serious vehicle accidents, including:
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Motorcycle accidents, which commonly cause very serious injuries
