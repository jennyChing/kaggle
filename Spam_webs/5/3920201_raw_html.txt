                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     <a href="http://www.juliaandjulieta.com/2010/09/fresh-watermelon-salad-with-feta-cheese.html">
                      Fresh watermelon salad with feta cheese
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content">
                     This watermelon salad is easy, fresh and super tasty. It's great for warm days, when you don't feel like turning on the oven.
                     <br/>
                     It's also perfect for outdoor dining, or pic-nics!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <iframe frameborder="0" height="360" src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/15188545?color=ff9933" width="640">
                     </iframe>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://vimeo.com/15188545">
                      Greek watermelon salad
                     </a>
                     from
                     <a href="http://vimeo.com/juliaandjulieta">
                      Julia and Julieta
                     </a>
                     on
                     <a href="http://vimeo.com/">
                      Vimeo
                     </a>
                     .
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Here's the list of ingredients:
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <ul>
                      <li>
                       watermelon
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       roasted pine nuts
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       fresh basil
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       feta cheese (or goat cheese)
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       dressing: olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper
                      </li>
                     </ul>
                     <div>
                      Enjoy!
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
