      <div class="heading alignleft">
       <h2>
        <a href="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/2013/06/18/diy-terrarium-escort-cards-by-kelli-murray/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to DIY terrarium escort cards by Kelli Murray">
         DIY terrarium escort cards by Kelli Murray
        </a>
       </h2>
       <small>
        categories
        <a href="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/category/diy-wedding/" rel="category tag">
         DIY Wedding
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/category/favors-gifts/" rel="category tag">
         Favors + Gifts
        </a>
       </small>
      </div>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54761" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-00.jpg" height="800" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54761" height="800" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-00.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        Were so stoked to have Mrs.
        <a href="http://www.kellimurray.com/" target="_blank">
         Kelli Murray
        </a>
        back with us today. We swear, one day were going to share enough of her pretty DIYs to style an entire wedding (not a bad idea)! Today though, is all about the wedding guest and showing them to their table with these fab terrarium escort cards, that can also double as wedding favors. Theyre easy to make, everyone will love them, and we bet youll get addicted and make a few extras for your home. Heres what cha need
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54767" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-1.jpg" height="399" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54767" height="399" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-1.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Supplies:
        </strong>
        <br/>
         Corked round glass bottle
        <br/>
         moss
        <br/>
         fake flowers
        <br/>
         black cardstock
        <br/>
         cotton string
        <br/>
         white pigment ink pen
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54768" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-2.jpg" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54768" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-2.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        If you are making a lot of these, round up all your supplies and make a little assembly line. Invites your friends (or your mom!) to help you put them together. Because really, the assembly will be the hardest part.
       </p>
       <p>
        Step 1: Uncork your glass jar. Fill it with moss about half way up and add a single faux flower.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54765" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-5.jpg" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54765" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-5.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54772" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-9.jpg" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54772" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-9.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy " data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-10.jpg" height="802" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" height="802" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-10.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        Step 2:Printthis
        <i>
         <a href="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Banner-Template.pdf">
          Banner Template
         </a>
        </i>
        to create your customized banners. Cut them out and punch a small hole at the center top where your string will go.
       </p>
       <p>
        Step 3:If your banners are black, as mine are, use a white pigment ink pen to write the names of your guests. Let the ink dry.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54770" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-4.jpg" height="800" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54770" height="800" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-4.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        Step 4: Wrap these name banners around your assembled terrariums with cotton string.and voil!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-54771" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-6.jpg" height="800" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY Terrarium escort card | by Kelli Murray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-54771" height="800" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Faux-Terrarium-DIY-6.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        You can totally customize these to your event too. Play around with different flowers, colors you know, just have fun with it!
       </p>
