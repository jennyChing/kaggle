          <p>
           Completing our preview to the
           <a href="http://hypebeast.com/tags/neighborhood">
            NEIGHBORHOOD
           </a>
           x
           <a href="http://hypebeast.com/tags/coca-cola-zero">
            Coca-Cola Zero
           </a>
           collaborative collection, the brands own Shinsuke Takizawa comes forward with imagery to their dual-branded multi-geared bicycle. Inspired by those traveling under the night sky, the all-black two-wheeler presents each companys name on its frame with a bright red tire sitting cleanly in the back. The NEIGHBORHOOD x Coca-Cola Zero Road Bike is expected to release September 14 at HOODS Hong Kong and HOODS Beijing in conjunction with their capsule collection.
          </p>
