          <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
           <p>
            Braids
            <a href="http://www.bridalguide.com/beauty-fitness/hair/new-wedding-hair-trends" target="_self">
             <strong>
              dominated the bridal runways
             </strong>
            </a>
            this season  from Monique Lhuillier to Alita Graham to Winnie Couture. We love how the versatile technique can be used in a free-flowing, wavy 'do or as a finishing touch on a sleek topknot. Think you don't have the skills to do your own wedding-day hair? Think again. After scouring YouTube for the best tutorials, we found five easy-to-follow videos featuring gorgeous styles that are all wedding-appropriate.
            <strong>
             The best part: Each of these videos is under five minutes
            </strong>
            . So part your hair in three, and let's begin!
           </p>
           <h3>
            Bohemian Braids
           </h3>
           <p>
            A low-laying braid looks gorgeous over wavy hair. No French-braiding required!
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="400" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/GQfXbPZhgno?rel=0" width="600">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Braided Bun
           </h3>
           <p>
            The secret to this perfect bun, topped off with a wraparound braid? A hair doughnut.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="400" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/Ut72eX579gE?rel=0" width="600">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Mermaid Side Braid
           </h3>
           <p>
            This mermaid style is suprisingly easy to achieve. If you have shorter or thinner locks, consider investing in a set of natural-looking extensions to add length and volume.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="400" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/8xZWRwfib7U?rel=0" width="600">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Braided Updo
           </h3>
           <p>
            Dress up the look by adding a flower or a sparkly hairpiece.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="400" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/rf2oZwSehWQ?rel=0" width="600">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Knotted Braid Updo
           </h3>
           <p>
            Sleek and sophisticated, this knotted braid will keep your hair in place for a long night of dancing.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="400" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/5cwx_UQQWho?rel=0" width="600">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            Stay tuned for our roundup of romantic hairstyle tutorials!
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Tell us: Which hairstyle will you try?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Paulette Safdieh
           </p>
