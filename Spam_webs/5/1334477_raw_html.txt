      <h1 class="title">
       <a href="http://www.home-designing.com/2009/04/bizarre-house-that-slides" rel="bookmark" title="Bizarre House That Slides!">
        Bizarre House That Slides!
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="postdate">
       <ul class="pdate">
        <li id="pmonth">
         Apr
        </li>
        <li id="pday">
         19
        </li>

         2009

        <a class="drmm" href="http://www.home-designing.com/tag/drmm" title="dRMM Tag">

      <div class="entry">
       <div class="uniq-class">
        <div class="simplepost">
         <p>
          Okay, it is not every day that you come across a home like this. Sliding House, by
          <a href="http://www.drmm.co.uk/">
           dRMM Architecture
          </a>
          does something that we have never seen or heard any another house do  it slides.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-drmm.jpg">
           <img alt="sliding house drmm" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2826" height="370" src="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-drmm.jpg" title="sliding house drmm" width="537"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          At first glance, the form looks nothing special and gives you the idea of a regular timber barn until you realise that the entire outer skin (the wooden part) is capable of sliding back and forth.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-3.jpg">
           <img alt="sliding house architecture" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2828" height="430" src="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-3.jpg" title="sliding house architecture" width="580"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Here is a short video in which the owner Ross Russel explains the concept of the house and shows how the sliding takes place.
         </p>
         <p>
          <object height="491" width="580">
           <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/ZxmvRDTELy8&amp;hl=en&amp;fs=1">
           </param>
           <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
           </param>
           <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
           </param>
           <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="491" src="http://www.youtube.com/v/ZxmvRDTELy8&amp;hl=en&amp;fs=1" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="580">
           </embed>
          </object>
         </p>
         <p>
          <!-- Begin Google Adsense code -->
          <script type="text/javascript">
           &lt;!--
google_ad_client = "pub-9618854083971312";

          </script>
          <script src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
          <!-- End Google Adsense code -->
         </p>
         <p>
          A leaf from the designer dRMMs book:
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           The brief was a self-build house to retire to in order to grow food, entertain and enjoy the landscape. The site offered a combination of rolling England and agricultural Holland, restrained by stringent local Planning parameters for rural development. A genuine appreciation of vernacular farm buildings shared by architect and client led to a manipulation of the local timber framed and clad shed idiom.
          </p>
          <p>
           The outcome is three conventional building forms with unconventional detailing, radical performance, and a big surprise. A linear building of apparent simplicity is sliced into three programmes; house,
           <a class="autobesttag" href="http://www.home-designing.com/tag/garage" rel="nofollow" title="See the tag: garage (3 posts)">
            garage
           </a>
           and annexe. The garage is pulled off axis to create a courtyard between the three. The composition is further defined by material and colour; red rubber membrane and glass, red and black stained larch.
          </p>
          <p>
           The surprise: the separated forms can be transformed by a 20 ton mobile roof/wall enclosure which traverses the site, creating combinations of enclosure, open-air living and framing of views according to position. This is an autonomous structure; steel, timber, insulation and unstained larch spanning recessed railway tracks. Movement is powered by hidden electric motors on wheels integrated into the wall thickness. The tracks can be extended in the future should the client wish to build a swimming
           <a class="autobesttag" href="http://www.home-designing.com/tag/pool" rel="nofollow" title="See the tag: pool (2 posts)">
            pool
           </a>
           , which may need occasional shelter.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sliding House offers radically variable spaces, extent of shelter, sunlight and insulation. The dynamic change is a physical phenomenon difficult to describe in words or images. It is about the ability to vary the overall building composition and character according to season, weather, or a remote-controlled desire to delight.
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          <!-- Begin Google Adsense code -->
          <script type="text/javascript">

          </script>
          <script src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
          <!-- End Google Adsense code -->
         </p>
         <p>
          At night, the home produces some interesting light patterns with the slide:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-night.jpg">
           <img alt="sliding-house-night" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2830" height="417" src="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-night-582x417.jpg" title="sliding-house-night" width="582"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Besides the visual awe that the slide creates, it gives the control for its residents to adjust the heating,
          <a class="autobesttag" href="http://www.home-designing.com/tag/lighting" rel="nofollow" title="See the tag: lighting (5 posts)">
           lighting
          </a>
          and mood of the home to suit any weather.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-winter.jpg">
           <img alt="sliding-house-winter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2829" height="361" src="http://cdn.home-designing.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sliding-house-winter.jpg" title="sliding-house-winter" width="537"/>
          </a>
         </p>
