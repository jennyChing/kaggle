   <h1>
    Velvet Ribbon
   </h1>
   <p>
    Velvet ribbons have a luxurious touch and feel. Single and Double face velvet ribbon is made of high quality nylon filaments and goes into continuous dyeing machine. All Velvet ribbons are high quality, good shape, fine texture, and high color fastness to wet and dry rubbing, widely used in garment and gift packing. Printing on velvet tapes is rare and usually only by silk screen method.
   </p>
