                 'Pretty Little Liars' Recap: 'Into The Deep'
                </a>
               </h2>
               <p class="published">
                Posted
                <span class="postDateTime">
                 8/7/13 9:45 am EST
                </span>
                by
                <a href="http://hollywoodcrush.mtv.com/author/jeanbentley/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jean Bentley">

               <p>
                <img alt="Pretty Little Liars" class="thumbnailmain" src="http://hollywoodcrush.mtv.com//wp-content/uploads/hc/2013/08/prettylittleliars.jpg"/>
                What in the what just happened, you guys? "
                <a href="http://hollywoodcrush.mtv.com/tag/pretty-little-liars/">
                 Pretty Little Liars
                </a>
                " is often chock full of mysteries that are paired with other mysteries that are perpetrated by someone in a hoodie and black leather gloves, but the ending of "Into the Deep" was particularly bananas. What is even going on here? One thing's for certain: CeCe Drake is definitely a bad guy. Well, maybe. Nothing is definite on this show.
               </p>
               <p>
                Let's start at the end and work our way back: Because this is "PLL," they're probably contractually obligated to put at least one regularly appearing cast member in mortal danger. This week, Jenna's the one who drew the short straw, because after hemming and hawing to Shana about how she was super-duper scared of someone and really didn't want to be around any of the liars, she was found floating facedown in the lake. Emily jumped in and saved her, thankfully, because the last thing Rosewood needs is another dead body.
