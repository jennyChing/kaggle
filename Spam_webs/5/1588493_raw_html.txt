      <h2 class="singleh2">
       <a href="http://www.moderncat.net/2010/09/02/marmalade-wallflower-is-a-big-hit/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Marmalade Wallflower is a Big Hit">
        Marmalade Wallflower is a Big Hit
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="date-comments">
       <p class="fl">
        Thu, Sep 2, 2010
       </p>
       <p class="fr">
        <a href="http://www.moderncat.net/category/archives/other/coupons-discounts/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Coupons &amp; Discounts">
         Coupons &amp; Discounts
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.moderncat.net/category/archives/products/scratching/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Scratching">
         Scratching
        </a>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <img alt="Wallflower Cat Scratcher" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9369" height="414" src="http://www.moderncat.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Wallflower1.jpg" title="Wallflower Cat Scratcher" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Ive never had my own
        <a href="http://www.marmaladepets.com/shop/moderncat_exclusive.html" target="_blank">
         Wallflower cat scratcher from Marmalade Pet Care
        </a>
        , but I finally had a chance to test one out and I absolutely love it. Its so great that it makes me even more sad that these are being discontinued! I always thought that they were smaller, but the Wallflower scratcher is actually a substantial size at 34 long and 3.5 wide. When you mount it on the wall according to the directions, it is the perfect height for cats  even big cats  to get a good stretch while scratching. And its so beautiful, like a piece of modern sculpture.
       </p>
       <p>
        Heres Ando giving a little demonstration:
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Wallflower Modern Cat Scratcher" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9370" height="565" src="http://www.moderncat.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Wallflower2.jpg" title="Wallflower Modern Cat Scratcher" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        You can also order the
        <a href="http://www.marmaladepets.com/shop/moderncat_exclusive.html" target="_blank">
         Wallflower
        </a>
        without the wall clips and use it on the floor. Some cats, like Mackenzie, prefer to scratch horizontally.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Wallflower Modern Cat Scratcher from Marmalade Pet Care" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9371" height="288" src="http://www.moderncat.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Wallflower3.jpg" title="Wallflower Modern Cat Scratcher from Marmalade Pet Care" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        I have word that there are not very many Wallflowers left in the
        <a href="http://www.marmaladepets.com/shop/moderncat_exclusive.html" target="_blank">
         Marmalade inventory clearance
        </a>
        , so if you want one, this is your last chance. The Wallflower is available to Moderncat readers for just $23.10 and the wall clips are $17.60, both prices include shipping. Order one today
        <a href="http://www.marmaladepets.com/shop/moderncat_exclusive.html" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
