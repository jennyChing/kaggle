          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Teenage is really a messy and also a crucial part of everyones life. Being a Teenager is really hard. This crucial part of our life can actually determine how our entire future can be..!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           If we give up to the unusual problems and conditions we face during this period, we would eventually be going to mess up our entire life..!
           <span id="more-2071">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Science Behind Teenage Brain Explained
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           One of the main problem of teenage is Increase in every emotion of our life. Be it anger, hatred, happiness, sexual feelings etc., its teenage where all this things grow exponentially high and leads to several problems (sometimes no problems..!) to the teenage guy.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Theres a lot to explain. So please why dont you watch the video? Thats really better than me explaining to you!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           In the above video, Hank from the YouTube channel
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/scishow" target="_blank">
            SciShow
           </a>
           , explains the exact science and mysteries involved within the teenage brain.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.factsnfakes.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Teenage-Brain.jpg">
            <img alt="Teenage Brain" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2075" height="426" src="http://www.factsnfakes.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Teenage-Brain.jpg" width="575"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           So if youre a parent and next time when you scold your teenage for his less grades or stupid things done by him, once remember this video. And if youre a teenager, then remember every point explained in this video and start enjoying your life now, instead of wasting it inside a room.
          </p>
