        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Chinese Plum Sauce Recipe
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2009-09-16T16:04:11+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          September 16, 2009
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://savorysweetlife.com/author/alice/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Alice Currah
           </span>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <img alt="plumsauce2 Chinese Plum Sauce Recipe" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2181 pib-hover-img" height="760" src="http://savorysweetlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/plumsauce2.jpg" title="plumsauce2" width="526"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of my husbands favorite Chinese condiments is plum sauce. He dips EVERYTHING in it and who could blame him? The combination of sweet and sour with a touch of heat makes the perfect companion to springs rolls, pot stickers, fried chicken, and duck dishes. This sauce is incredibly easy to make and will have you wondering why you never made it before!
         <span id="more-2178">
         </span>
         Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #800040; font-size: small;">
          Chinese Plum Sauce Recipe
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #800040;">
           INGREDIENTS:
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1 cup of plum jam (Heres a very simple and easy recipe for
         <a href="http://savorysweetlife.com/?p=2122">
          homemade plum jam
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         1 clove of garlic, minced
        </p>
        <p>
         2 teaspoons of ginger, minced
        </p>
        <p>
         2 tablespoons of white vinegar
        </p>
        <p>
         1 tablespoon of
         <a class="broken_link" href="http://www.huyfong.com/no_frames/garlic.htm">
          Asian Chili Garlic sauce Huy Fong
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #800040;">
           DIRECTIONS:
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Combine all the ingredients and cook on the stove top on medium heat until everything is boiling. Remove from heat and serve (or allow to cool and refrigerate). Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="garlicginger Chinese Plum Sauce Recipe" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2184 pib-hover-img" height="366" src="http://savorysweetlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/garlicginger.jpg" title="garlicginger" width="526"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="plumsauceingredients Chinese Plum Sauce Recipe" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2185 pib-hover-img" height="351" src="http://savorysweetlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/plumsauceingredients.jpg" title="plumsauceingredients" width="526"/>
        </p>
        <p>
