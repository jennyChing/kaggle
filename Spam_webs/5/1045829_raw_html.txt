        <div class="heading">
         <h2>
          <a href="http://larkcrafts.com/jewelry-beading/uyehara/">
           Q&amp;A with Gordon K. Uyehara, metal clay master artist!
          </a>
         </h2>
         <small>
          July 31, 2012, 16:14 pm Posted by
          <a href="/about-contact#jewelry">
           Lark Jewelry &amp; Beading
          </a>
         </small>
        </div>
        <div class="cl">
        </div>
        <div class="entry">
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_40210" style="width: 235px">
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-40210 " height="300" src="http://www.larkcrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/gordon-uyehara-225x300.jpg" title="gordon uyehara" width="225"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Gordon K. Uyehara at Bead&amp;Button
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.honudream.com" target="_blank" title="Gordon Uyehara website">
           Gordon K. Uyehara
          </a>
          is a fabulous artist working in metal clay. He teaches, creates, interacts with the metal-clay community online, and inspires with his beautiful, elegant designs. He is the author of
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank" title="Metal Clay Fusion">
           Metal Clay Fusion: Diverse Clays, Detailed Techniques, Artful Projects
          </a>
          . Learn more about Gordon at his website
          <a href="http://www.honudream.com" target="_blank" title="Gordon Uyehara website">
           www.honudream.com
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-51600" height="300" src="http://www.larkcrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Metal-Clay-Fusion-254x300.jpg" title="Metal Clay Fusion" width="254"/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           Why metal clay, Gordon? How did you discover this medium, and why is it such a good fit for you?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          There was just something about it that set my imagination off. I could spend hours working with it and then when I wasnt, my mind was still engaged to the point of obsession.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           How do you describe your work and your aesthetic sensibility?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Id describe my work as organic and nature-inspired, with sculpted features, in restrained refinement.
         </p>
         <p>
          Artistically, its an ongoing process about observing the world around you and learning how to express yourself in every aspect of living.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_47450" style="width: 247px">
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-47450 " height="300" src="http://www.larkcrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Screen-shot-2012-01-18-at-11.55.32-AM-237x300.png" title="Poison Pill Ring from Metal Clay Fusion" width="237"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Poison Pill Ring from Metal Clay Fusion
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What are your hopes for the book
           <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank" title="Metal Clay Fusion">
            Metal Clay Fusion
           </a>
           ? What do you want your readers to receive in practical and artistic terms?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          I would like the book to inspire as many people as possible. I hope readers will try different techniques and that the book will help them discover their own artistic voice. And I always hope artists are thoughtful as they create.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What most impresses you about how metal clay is maturing as a medium today? And in which areas do you think growth is still most obviously needed?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          I think the amount of experimentation people are willing to undertake is impressive.
         </p>
         <p>
          Growth is needed in all areas. Having said that, it is proceeding just fine, under its own momentum.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_47414" style="width: 248px">
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-47414 " height="300" src="http://www.larkcrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Screen-shot-2012-01-18-at-11.53.32-AM-238x300.png" title="Bronze, Silver &amp; Pearl Pendant from Metal Clay Fusion" width="238"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Bronze, Silver &amp; Pearl Pendant from Metal Clay Fusion
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Im not going to make you name your favorite metal clay artists, but would you name a few who you think are doing exciting new things, and say what it is about each that you find exceptional?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          There are a lot of artists doing terrific work. As far as exciting new things, a few people come to mind:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.jade-orchid.com/www.jade-orchid.com/Home.html" target="_blank" title="Wanaree Tanner website">
           Wanaree Tanner
          </a>
          : youthful exuberance with a willingness to experiment, coupled with a keen aesthetic sense and natural talent
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.artinsilver.com/" target="_blank" title="Hadar Jacobson website">
           Hadar Jacobson
          </a>
          : tireless innovator with super accessible projects
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.samanthabraundjewellery.com/" target="_blank" title="Samantha Braund website">
           Samantha Braund
          </a>
          : wild and beautiful shell-scapes (as they look to me) combined with wire-wrapping and stone setting
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.lbjewelrydesigns.com/" target="_blank" title="Lisa Lynn Barth website">
           Lisa Lynn Barth
          </a>
          : outstanding metal clay and leatherwork/knotting combinations
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Speak to someone who has worked with other materials but not with metal clay: Please explain what you find most appealing about the medium.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          I like that it is pliable and picks up deep texture effortlessly. Plus, it is easily sculpted. The entire transformative process is fun. It can be used in a myriad of ways.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_47439" style="width: 215px">
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-47439 " height="300" src="http://www.larkcrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Screen-shot-2012-01-18-at-11.53.52-AM-205x300.png" title="Bronze Clay Mask Pendant" width="205"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Bronze Clay Mask Pendant from Metal Clay Fusion
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Do you feel a community has developed in metal clay, with artists really knowing one another and the work intimately, offering ideas, feedback, and support? Or is that still nascent?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          There is definitely a real community where ideas are shared freely. But it is also still very young. So it is both.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What do you do for fun, Gordon? And what are a few things that might surprise people about you?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Other than playing with clay, I like to relax outside in the sun, play guitar, and watch movies. It might surprise people that I have a mischievous side that I struggle to keep under wraps.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           In what ways does your home state of Hawaii influence your work?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The flora and fauna definitely play a partto what extent I do not know.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_47430" style="width: 192px">
          <a href="http://www.larkcrafts.com/bookstore/?isbn=9781600596971" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-47430 " height="300" src="http://www.larkcrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Screen-shot-2012-01-18-at-11.53.00-AM-182x300.png" title="Bronze Asian Bell from Metal Clay Fusion" width="182"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Bronze Asian Bell from Metal Clay Fusion
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Whats next? Where do you see yourself going from here professionally? What are your biggest ambitions that youre willing to share?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Im not sure. The possibilities are endless, and you never know where youll end up. Im just going to keep creating and see what life presents before me.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Thank you, Gordon!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Mahalo!
         </p>
