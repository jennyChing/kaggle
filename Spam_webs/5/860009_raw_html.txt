         <p>
          In 2015, we thought we would have flying cars and skateboards that can hover off the ground
          <span id="more-57640">
          </span>
          . Our reality may not match up with
          <em>
           Back to the Future II
          </em>
          just yet, but 2015 did bring a surprise we werent quite ready for and thats $99 flights to Europe courtesy of
          <a href="http://wowair.com/" target="_blank">
           WOW Air
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Recently,
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/2014/11/09/wow-air-is-making-flights-to-europe-as-cheap-as-99/" title="Wow Air is Making Flights To Europe As Cheap as $99">
           WOW Air announced
          </a>
          that they will launch service to Reykjavik,
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/?s=iceland&amp;x=0&amp;y=0" target="_blank">
           Iceland
          </a>
          from
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/?s=boston&amp;x=0&amp;y=0" target="_blank">
           Boston
          </a>
          and Baltimore later this year with rates as low as $99 each way. That rumor is confirmedon May 8th, 2015 round trip service from Baltimore for just $99 will be available four days a week and will extend to five days a week after June 3rd.
         </p>
         <p>
          With more people heading to
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/?s=iceland&amp;x=0&amp;y=0" target="_blank">
           Iceland
          </a>
          on vacation theres no doubt that these seats will fill in no time. Especially whenReykjaviks Keflavik Airport (KEF) serves as a great hub to accessing the rest of
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/2015/01/07/the-10-best-events-and-festivals-to-travel-for-in-2015/" target="_blank" title="The 10 Best Events and Festivals To Travel For In 2015">
           Europe
          </a>
          . If you need to book additional flights
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/2015/01/08/the-10-best-websites-to-help-you-book-cheap-flights/" title="The 10 Best Websites To Help You Book Cheap Flights">
           we highly recommend these websites
          </a>
          for booking the cheapest and best flights available. Youll find flights as low as
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/2015/01/08/the-10-best-websites-to-help-you-book-cheap-flights/" title="The 10 Best Websites To Help You Book Cheap Flights">
           $70 from Iceland to London
          </a>
          (with EasyJet)to help you explore the rest of Europe.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you need a hotel,
          <a href="http://infinitelegroom.com/2015/02/11/best-websites-for-booking-cheap-hotels/" target="_blank" title="The 10 Best Websites to Help You Book a Cheap Hotel">
           we recommend one of these 10 websites
          </a>
          for finding the best deals on hotels.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Via [

          </em>
         </p>
