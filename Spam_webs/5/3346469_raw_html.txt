     <h1 class="entry-title">
      18 Red, White &amp; Blue Desserts
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-06-25T15:04:05+00:00">
       June 25, 2014
      </time>
      <p class="byline author vcard">
       By 
			Shari's Berries
      </p>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <!-- /.hero -->
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <p class="lead">
        The Fourth of July means different things to different people. Some families show their patriotism by flying the United States flag in their yard, while others choose to privately honor the holiday. Of course, plenty of people also celebrate with barbecues and parties. If that's your style, then don't forget to serve these red, white, blue and delicious July 4th desserts!
       </p>
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <h4>
         Red, White and Blue Tart
        </h4>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Red, White and Blue Tart" class="aligncenter" height="685" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Tart-1024x685.jpg" width="1024"/>
         If you prefer your dessert a little on the lighter side, this tart is perfect. This treat pairs sweet berries with the flavors of pineapple and honey. Source:
         <a href="http://cookeatenjoyrepeat.blogspot.com/2011/07/red-white-and-blue-tart.html" target="new">
          Cook. Eat. Enjoy. Repeat.
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Patriotic Cheesecakes Topped With Raspberry Gelee and Fresh Berries
        </h4>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Patriotic Cheesecakes topped with Raspberry Gele and Fresh Berries" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/No-bake-cheesecake-1024x1024.jpg" width="1024"/>
         This no-bake dessert is simpler than it appears. Raspberry gelee and fresh berries add a bit of summer sophistication to this rich cheesecake. Source:
         <a href="http://www.hungryhappenings.com/2012/06/patriotic-cheesecakes-topped-with.html" target="new">
          Hungry Happenings
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White and Blue Trifle
        </h4>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Red, White and Blue Trifle" class="aligncenter" height="979" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/anthony-_-dessert-015-1024x979.jpg" width="1024"/>
         Raspberries and blueberries are always summer favorites, which is why this trifle is just right for a festive July 4th barbecue. Source:
         <a href="http://pattiwest.blogspot.com/2011/07/red-white-blue-dessert.html" target="new">
          Its All Good.
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White &amp; Blue Dipped Oreos
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Red, White &amp; Blue Dipped Oreos" class="aligncenter" height="721" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Red-White-Blue-Chocolate-Dipped-Oreo-Cookies-1024x721.jpg" width="1024"/>
         Everyone loves Oreos, right? This recipe reinvents the childhood favorite with patriotic flair. Simply dip the cookies in red, white and blue candy melts. Source:
         <a href="http://www.cookingmamas.com/red-white-blue-dipped-oreos.html" target="new">
          Cooking Mamas
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White and Blue Velvet Cupcakes
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Red, White and Blue Velvet Cupcakes" class="aligncenter" height="682" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RWB-star-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
         What do you get when you cross a sugar cookie with a cupcake? Red, white and blue velvet cupcakes! Sugar-cookie-flavored cupcake batter is topped with almond cream cheese frosting. Decorate them any way you like to make a fabulous and festive dessert. Source:
         <a href="http://javacupcake.com/2013/07/patriotic-desserts-round-up/" target="new">
          JavaCupcake
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Blue Velvet Cupcakes With White Coconut Frosting and Strawberry Syrup Drizzle
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Blue Velvet Cupcakes with White Coconut Frosting and Strawberry Syrup Drizzle" class="aligncenter" height="735" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Blue-Velvet-Cupcakes.jpg" width="1024"/>
         Want a dessert that tastes decadent, but is actually on the healthier side? These cupcakes deliver. Theyre whole-grain, gluten-free, low in sugar and high in protein. Source:
         <a href="http://dessertswithbenefits.com/healthy-july-4-dessert/" target="new">
          Desserts with Benefits
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White &amp; Blue Dessert Pizza
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Red, White &amp; Blue Dessert Pizza" class="aligncenter" height="768" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/fruit-pizza-1024x768.jpg" width="1024"/>
         This fruit pizza recipe uses almond flour and pine nuts for a healthy twist on the popular dessert. Source:
         <a href="http://yourlighterside.com/2012/01/low-carb-red-white-and-blue-dessert-pizza-puts-pizzazz-in-picnics-2/" target="new">
          Your Lighter Side
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White &amp; Blue Rice KrispieTreats
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Red, White &amp; Blue Rice Krispie Treats" class="aligncenter" height="682" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rice_Krispies2-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
         Give this kid favorite a patriotic makeover. The adults wont be able to resist these red, white and blue Rice Krispie treats either. Source:
         <a href="http://www.therecipenut.com/2012/05/red-white-and-blue-rice-krispie-treats.html" target="new">
          The Recipe Nut.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White and Blue Salad
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Red, White and Blue Salad" class="aligncenter" height="893" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/red-white-blue-salad-008_crop1-1024x893.jpg" width="1024"/>
         A mix of buffalo mozzarella, fresh berries and orange, this salad suits sophisticated tastes. If youre having an adults-only party, add a splash of Grand Marnier. Source:
         <a href="http://mamaguru.com/red-white-and-blue-salad/" target="new">
          mamaguru
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Ricotta Cheesecake
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Ricotta Cheesecake" class="aligncenter" height="682" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cake-whole-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
         The sweetness of this cheesecake teams well with savory barbecue. Roasted pistachios, mascarpone and lemon are just a few of its complex flavors. Source:
         <a href="http://www.askchefdennis.com/2010/07/my-ricotta-cheesecake-a-red-white-and-blue-dessert/" target="new">
          Chef Dennis
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Red, White and Blue Ombre Cake
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Red, White and Blue Ombre Cake" class="aligncenter" height="639" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ombre-cake.jpg" width="639"/>
         This chic and colorful cake takes time to prepare, but your guests salute to your baking skill will make it worth it. Source:
         <a href="http://thepurplemixer.com/2012/07/02/red-white-and-blue-ombre-cake/" target="new">
          The Purple Mixer
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         4th of July Cake Pops
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="4th of July Cake Pops" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cake-pops-1024x1024.jpg" width="1024"/>
         These cake pops are decorated in their Independence Day best. Bake them from scratch, or take a shortcut and decorate our
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/cake-pops-">
          vanilla cake pops
         </a>
         with red, white and blue frosting and sprinkles. Source:
         <a href="http://rumblytumbly.com/2011/06/11/4th-of-july-cake-pops/" target="new">
          Rumbly in my Tumbly
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Stars and Stripes Forever Cookies
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Stars and Stripes Forever Cookies" class="aligncenter" height="768" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/star-cookies-1024x768.jpg" width="1024"/>
         More than delicious cookies, these also make great table decorations. Theyre sure to be a big hit with guests of all ages. Source:
         <a href="http://pinkapronbaker.blogspot.com/2011/07/stars-and-stripes-forever-cookies.html" target="new">
          pink apron baker
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Patriotic Trifle Dessert
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Patriotic Trifle Dessert" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Patriotic-strawberry-trifle-WM-1024x1024.jpg" width="1024"/>
         This trifle is as beautiful as it is delicious. Ripe berries topped with sweet whipped cream create a refreshing dessert for hot summer days.Source:
         <a href="http://www.the-girl-who-ate-everything.com/2010/05/patriotic-trifle-and-giveaway.html" target="new">
          The Girl Who Ate Everything
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         4th of July Cookie Bars
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="4th of July Cookie Bars" class="aligncenter" height="785" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/4th-of-july-sugar-cookie-squares-006-1024x785.jpg" width="1024"/>
         These cookie bars can be decorated to look like flowers, flags or any other patriotic design youd like. Theyre easy, fun and delicious. Source:
         <a href="http://www.creationsbykara.com/2011/06/4th-of-july-cookie-bars.html/" target="new">
          Creations by Kara
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         4th of July Pops
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="4th of July Popsicles" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Popsicles-1024x1024.jpg" width="1024"/>
         The sun is out and the temperature is high. What better way to cool down than with pops made from homemade french vanilla ice cream and raspberry puree? Source:
         <a href="http://itsybitsyfoodies.com/4th-of-july-popsicles/" target="new">
          Itsy Bitsy Foodies
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Firecracker Berry Frozen Yogurt Pops
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Firecracker Berry Frozen Yogurt Pops" class="aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/FirecrackerBerryFrozenYogurtPops-1-682x1024.jpg" width="682"/>
         These fresh fruit and yogurt pops are cool, healthy, delicious plus as vibrant as a Fourth of July firecracker. Source:
         <a href="http://www.thecomfortofcooking.com/2013/06/firecracker-berry-frozen-yogurt-pops.html" target="new">
          The Comfort of Cooking
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         Star-Spangled Strawberries
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Star-Spangled Berries" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2082" height="704" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/berries-1024x704.jpg" width="1024"/>
         Perfect for summers all-American celebrations, our
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/star-spangled-berries-sme" target="new">
          Star Spangled Berries
         </a>
         are dipped, delicious and easy. Just tell us where the party is, and well deliver.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Whats Your Fourth of July Favorite?
        </h4>
        <p>
         Whats your favorite red, white and blue dessert? Let us know in the blog comments.
        </p>
