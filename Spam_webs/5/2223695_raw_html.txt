        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Guest post: Is Diagon Alley Doable This Summer?
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-30T05:59:30+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 30, 2014
         </time>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           This is a guest post from a travel agency specializing in Orlando theme park vacations.
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://lastmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/daigon2.jpg">
          <img alt="daigon2 Guest post: Is Diagon Alley Doable This Summer?" class="aligncenter wp-image-3588" height="393" src="http://lastmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/daigon2.jpg" title="Guest post: Is Diagon Alley Doable This Summer?" width="524"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youve got a Harry Potter-obsessed brood, theyre probably clamoring to visit Diagon Alley over at Universal Studios Florida. Unfortunately, so is half the planet. Youre not going to avoid the crowds any time in the near future, even during the off-season. Still, the fall crowds should be somewhat lower as kids go back to school, so wait it out if you can.
        </p>
        <p>
         What if your family just wont wait? If they really, really want to hit Diagon Alley during the summer, heres how to stay sane amidst the crowded insanity:
        </p>
        <p>
         Roll everyone out of bed early and get to the park at opening time. Yes, it will be packed with other early birds, but the crowd builds even more as the day wears on. If riding Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts is a must-do, head over there first. The morning line will be nothing compared to the afternoon rush.
        </p>
        <p>
         Gringotts melds roller coaster and dark ride technology in new and intriguing ways. Unfortunately, that means that Universal has been having trouble with it since before Diagon Alley officially opened. It make break down while youre in line, which significantly adds to the wait time. Expect the bugs to get smoothed out later in the year, but its likely to run sporadically and break down a lot this summer.
        </p>
        <p>
         Gringotts has a single rider line, so thats an option if your kids are old enough and dont feel the need to see the entire queue. Single riders may still wait up to an hour or more, but thats nothing compared to the three and four hour waits that the ride racks up on the busiest days.
        </p>
        <p>
         Depending on the time you arrive at Universal Studios and the days crowd level, you might not get right in to Diagon Alley. If you;re turned away and given a return time for later, enjoy the other rides and attractions with minimal waits. One good thing about Diagon Alley is that its significantly cutting the wait times for other rides, like Transformers, Simpsons, Men in Black, and pretty much everything else at Universal Studios Florida. People are drawn to Harry Potter and blinded to everything else around it. That means you and your kids can ride whatever you like in the rest of the park with a fraction of the usual summer wait times.
        </p>
        <p>
         Those lowered waits are also good when you all reach your tolerance level in Diagon Alley. Yes, its exquisitely detailed, and your little wizards and witches will undoubtedly want to spend lots of time exploring, but the heat and wall to wall people will eventually drive them (and you) to their tolerance limit. Once youve had enough, you have the rest of the day to explore both Universal Studios and Islands of Adventure at your leisure while everyone else is still in the Wizarding World.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre nearby and Diagon Alley is a day trip for you, you dont have to worry about finding a hotel. If youre coming in for vacation, you can save money by booking a vacation package through a site like
         <a href="http://www.orlandovacation.com" target="_blank">
          www.OrlandoVacation.com
         </a>
         . The site has discounted packages, and youll even get free days on your ticket, depending on your length of stay. The website has details of all the hotel options and latest offers.
        </p>
