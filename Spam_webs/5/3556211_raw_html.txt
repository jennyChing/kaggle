     <p>
      <br/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       act_romegiallis underground pavilion contains a sunken swimming pool
      </strong>
      <br/>
      photography 
      <a href="http://www.marcellomariana.it/" target="_blank">
       marcello mariana
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       charged with adding an indoor pool to an existing home in northern italy, local practice
       <a href="http://www.actromegialli.it/" target="_blank">
        act_romegialli
       </a>
       built a subterranean pavilion containing both leisure and fitness facilities.
      </strong>
      with its location informed by the sites natural topography, the new structures glazed faade opens on to an artificial lake populated with buoyant water lilies.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-02.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      the independent dwelling is reached by a timber pathway
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       to achieve a feeling of continuity throughout the scheme, small white ceramic tiles were chosen to line the pool as well as its neighboring surfaces.
      </strong>
      the front of the pavilion houses a sliding door that disappears into a structural cavity, maximizing the intimate relationship with the surrounding parkland.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-03.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      the structure is integrated into the sites natural topography
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       the independent space is reached externally by a path that emphasizes a profound sense of seclusion and privacy.
      </strong>
      internally, the pavilions sequential geometric volumes can be accessed via an underground tunnel that leads directly from the main house.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-04.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      a new relationship is formed between the main house and the sunken pavilion
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       in addition to this sunken sanctuary, a glazed conservatory was added as an extension of the existing property  an early 19th century building renovated ten years previously.
      </strong>
      sitting one meter above the ground plane, the residence had very little connection with the surrounding parkland. consequently, the client desired an enclosed space from which garden views could be enjoyed.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-05.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      the glazed faade opens on to an artificial lake filled with water lilies
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-06.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      white ceramic tiles have been chosen to line the pool and its adjacent surfaces
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-07.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      the pavilion comprises a series of distinct volumes
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-08.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      curved surfaces are clad with the same white tile as the pools lining
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-09.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      fitness areas are also included as part of the scheme
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-10.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      a connecting underground tunnel leads directly from the main house
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-11.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      a glazed conservatory was also added as an extension of the early 19th century property
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="act_romegialli la piscina del roccolo swimming pool italy designboom" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-265120" height="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/act_romegialli-la-piscina-del-roccolo-swimming-pool-italy-designboom-12.jpg" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      the enclosed space offers sweeping garden views
     </p>
