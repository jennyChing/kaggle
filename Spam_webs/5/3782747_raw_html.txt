         <h2 class="entry-header font-entryheader">
          <span class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.zooborns.com/zooborns/2015/01/baby-turtles-saved-from-illegal-trafficking-.html">
            Baby Turtles Saved From Illegal Trafficking
           </a>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <h5 class="date-header font-dateheader">
          <span class="entry-date">
           <a href="http://www.zooborns.com/zooborns/2015/01/baby-turtles-saved-from-illegal-trafficking-.html">
            January 11, 2015
           </a>
          </span>
         </h5>
         <div class="entry-body font-entrybody">
          <p>
           <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01bb07d75f0f970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
            <img alt="10872821_10152902873899178_5903419042370506900_o" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a010535647bf3970b01bb07d75f0f970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01bb07d75f0f970d-800wi" title="10872821_10152902873899178_5903419042370506900_o"/>
           </a>
           Eight baby Turtles confiscated from wildlife traffickers are safe in their new home at the
           <a href="http://www.mississippirivermuseum.com/">
            National Mississippi River Museum &amp; Aquarium.
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b62b970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
            <img alt="10887253_10152902873729178_6360453697146035213_o" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b62b970b image-full img-responsive" src="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b62b970b-800wi" title="10887253_10152902873729178_6360453697146035213_o"/>
            <br/>
           </a>
           <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01bb07d75f39970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
            <img alt="10404400_10152902875244178_4757642265170990123_n" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a010535647bf3970b01bb07d75f39970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01bb07d75f39970d-800wi" title="10404400_10152902875244178_4757642265170990123_n"/>
           </a>
           <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b62b970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
            <br/>
           </a>
           <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b684970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
            <img alt="10429310_10152902875009178_7858583633860942406_n" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b684970b image-full img-responsive" src="http://www.zooborns.com/.a/6a010535647bf3970b01b7c733b684970b-800wi" title="10429310_10152902875009178_7858583633860942406_n"/>
           </a>
           Photo Credit: National Mississippi River Museum &amp; Aquarium
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            The young Wood, Blandings, and Loggerhead Musk Turtles are part of a shipment of more than 200 hatchlings intercepted by the United States Fish &amp; Wildlife Service (USFWS). The Turtles were bound for export to China, where they would feed the demand for Turtle meat, exotic pets, and Traditional Chinese Medicine.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Thousands of Turtles and Tortoises are sold illegally every day in markets throughout China and Southeast Asia. The International Union for Conservation of Nature, or IUCN, states that up to 50 percent of Asian Turtle species are now Endangered, and that number is rising. In fact, Tortoises and freshwater Turtles are the most threatened of any major group of terrestrial (land-dwelling) vertebrates  more than mammals, birds, or amphibians.
          </p>
          <p>
           Visit the
           <a href="http://www.fws.gov/international/wildlife-trafficking/index.html">
            USFWS website
           </a>
           to learn more about how illegal wildlife trafficking threatens species around the globe.
          </p>
