               </a>
               .

              <p>

                according to TMZ.

              <p>
               Charvet and Burke attempted to obtain a restraining order against Fleiss after he allegedly made physical threats and continually harassed the couple by blasting barking noises towards their home.
              </p>

               Fleiss was reportedly uncooperative when police had initially interviewed him about the complaints against him.
