         <h1 class="post-title" data-post-id="631193" itemprop="name">
          You Wont BELIEVE the Things You Can Now 3D Print For Your Home
         </h1>
         <h4 class="post-meta clearfix">
          <div class="avatar nopin">
           <img alt="Alexis Monson" class="avatar avatar-44 wp-user-avatar wp-user-avatar-44 alignnone photo nopin" height="44" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/monson-alexis-11.jpg?fit=crop&amp;w=44&amp;h=44&amp;ver=1423172653.1" width="44"/>
          </div>
          <div class="posted-on-wrapper">
           <p>
            by
            <a href="//www.brit.co/author/alexis_monson/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Alexis Monson">
             <span itemprop="author">
              Alexis Monson
             </span>
            </a>
            on
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-03-06T11:50:06+00:00" pubdate="">
             March 6, 2014
            </time>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing" style="display: none">
          </div>
         </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="post-content inner">
         <p dir="ltr">
          The 3D printed world is moving and shaking. The number of products you can buy is growing by the second. Some are
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-couture/" target="_blank">
           wearable
          </a>
          . Some are
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-november/" target="_blank">
           edible
          </a>
          . Some are
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-printing-feb-2014/" target="_blank">
           medical
          </a>
          . And some are for your home. For the time being, it seems there are more not-so pretty things out there than stunners, but thats why weve done a little research for you (as we like to do) and curated this list of the most beautiful home products you can buy. Get ready to impress all your friends by telling them, Bee tee dubs, thats 3D printed.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631200" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/1-bottle-openers.jpg" title="1-bottle-openers" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://www.storenvy.com/products/5072285-cleo-teal-bottle-opener" target="_blank">
           Bottle Openers
          </a>
          ($82): Beer and 3D printing have met. And trust us. They get along real well. Oh, and yes its true. 3D printed metal is one of the coolest things to have ever happened.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631201" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/2-Roses-2-Main.jpg" title="2-Roses-2-Main" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          2.
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-roses-tutorial/" target="_blank">
           Rose Bouquet
          </a>
          : This past V-day was somewhat major for the inhabitants of San Francisco. Why? Because we
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-rose-delivery/" target="_blank">
           delivered
          </a>
          these roses out to the masses. (via
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-roses-tutorial/" target="_blank">
           Brit + Co.
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631202" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="482" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/3-mod.jpg" title="3-mod" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          3.
          <a href="http://www.finedininglovers.com/blog/curious-bites/3d-printed-porcelain-kitchenware/" target="_blank">
           The Mod Collective
          </a>
          : Some triple-dee printed items feel a little too 3D printed. These are not those items. These are pretty. The use of technology is just a bonus. (via
          <a href="http://www.finedininglovers.com/blog/curious-bites/3d-printed-porcelain-kitchenware/" target="_blank">
           Fine Dining Lovers
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631203" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="478" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/4-king-kong.jpg" title="4-king-kong" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          4.
          <a href="http://lokolo.eu/kabel-king-kong/" target="_blank">
           Kabel King Kong
          </a>
          ($12): Berlin designed and made, this swinger can come to our lighting party any day of the week.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631204" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/5-jumbo.jpg" title="5-jumbo" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          5.
          <a href="http://www.yankodesign.com/2014/01/24/introucing-jumbo/" target="_blank">
           Jumbo
          </a>
          : And since were on the topic of ridiculously cute 3D printed housewares, heres a little elephant that we think should be your new toothbrush holder. (via
          <a href="http://www.yankodesign.com/2014/01/24/introucing-jumbo/" target="_blank">
           Yanko Design
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631205" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="320" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/6-hearts.jpg" title="6-hearts" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          6.
          <a href="https://www.rinkak.com/item/palabola/6268616437661696" target="_blank">
           Lovely Heart Paper Cup Holder
          </a>
          ($30): You know what we should all stop doing? We should all stop using paper sleeves. Theyre wasteful. And more importantly, not nearly as adorable as this.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631206" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/7-snap-lamp.jpg" title="7-snap-lamp" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          7.
          <a href="http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:27062" target="_blank">
           Snap Together Mini Lamp
          </a>
          : MakerBot is our preferred choice of 3D printer. Right now, they retail for around $2,100, which by no means is cheap, but it is closer to becoming accessible to the masses than any other models out there. And this little snap together lamp they created? Its like LEGOs met home decor, and all things are suddenly right in the universe. (via
          <a href="http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:27062" target="_blank">
           MakerBot
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631207" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="485" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/8-Pi-Pencil-Holder.jpg" title="8-Pi-Pencil-Holder" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          8.
          <a href="http://www.shapeways.com/model/567973/pi-pencil-holder-small.html?li=productGroup&amp;materialId=6" target="_blank">
           Pi Pencil Holder
          </a>
          ($44): Math nerds, get ready to get pumped. This pencil holder is made of the first 546 digits of pi. We dont know about you, but all of a sudden were crazy for a slice of banana cream.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631208" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/9-Snow-12-Close.jpg" title="9-Snow-12-Close" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          9.
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-snowflake-garlands/" target="_blank">
           Snowflake Garlands
          </a>
          : Were not trying to brag, but you guys do know that we have a few 3D printers at
          <a href="http://makeshop.brit.co/" target="_blank">
           Brit + Co SF
          </a>
          , right? Yeah, we made these with them. Aint no thang. (via
          <a href="//www.brit.co/3d-snowflake-garlands/" target="_blank">
           Brit + Co.
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631209" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="432" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/10-wall-cleat.jpg" title="10-wall-cleat" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          10.
          <a href="http://www.shapeways.com/model/263704/wall-cleat-1.html?li=productGroup" target="_blank">
           Wall Cleat
          </a>
          ($65): Until someone finds a way to make cords beautiful, we will continually try to find ways to tuck them away and make them unassuming.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631210" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="416" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/11-endless-chair.jpg" title="11-endless-chair" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          11.
          <a href="http://www.thebohmerian.com/2011/06/the-endless-chair-made-of-recycled-refrigerator-plastic-designed-by-dutch-furniture-designer-dirk-van-der-kooij/" target="_blank">
           The Endless Chair
          </a>
          : We dont like to go around throwing out the word epic (okay, maybe we do), but this chair is just that. Dutch furniture designer Dirk Vander Kooij created a modern look using eco-technology. Youd never guess this little seat was made out of plastic from recycled refrigerators. Coooooool. (via
          <a href="http://www.thebohmerian.com/2011/06/the-endless-chair-made-of-recycled-refrigerator-plastic-designed-by-dutch-furniture-designer-dirk-van-der-kooij/" target="_blank">
           The Bohmrian
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631211" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/12-twisted-tri.jpg" title="12-twisted-tri" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          12.
          <a href="http://3dea.openhouse.me/3dea-features-haute-3d-printed-fashion-and-design-from-rotterdams-designshop/" target="_blank">
           Twisted Triangle
          </a>
          : &amp; Design Shop out of Rotterdam knows exactly how to dovetail good design with technology. And here is an example of it in all its glory. (via
          <a href="http://3dea.openhouse.me/3dea-features-haute-3d-printed-fashion-and-design-from-rotterdams-designshop/" target="_blank">
           3dea
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631212" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="484" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/13-room-divider.jpg" title="13-room-divider" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          13.
          <a href="http://www.architonic.com/pmsht/macedonia-space-divider-freedom-of-creation/1142789" target="_blank">
           Macedonia Space Divider
          </a>
          : Room
          <a href="//www.brit.co/room-dividers/" target="_blank">
           dividers
          </a>
          are oh-so useful. We love open floor plans, but sometimes its nice to create little nooks for yourself. And this looker can do just that. (via
          <a href="http://www.architonic.com/pmsht/macedonia-space-divider-freedom-of-creation/1142789" target="_blank">
           Archi Tonic
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631213" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="543" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/14-old-pot.jpg" title="14-old-pot" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          14.
          <a href="http://www.jeplus.at/?q=content/berlin-old-pot-new-top" target="_blank">
           Old Pot, New Top
          </a>
          : We kind of make a sport out of
          <a href="//www.brit.co/tag/repurpose" target="_blank">
           repurposing
          </a>
          . So we can totally dig this Austrian-based designers beat. She took old porcelain pieces that had missing or broken tops and gave them new dapper little hats. (via
          <a href="http://www.jeplus.at/?q=content/berlin-old-pot-new-top" target="_blank">
           Julia Landsiedl
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631214" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="470" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/15-matthijs.jpg" title="15-matthijs" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          15.
          <a href="http://www.matthijskok.nl/cubify/" target="_blank">
           Plant Pot + Bookend
          </a>
          : We like the way Matthijs Kok thinks. Rather than putting bright pops of colors in the obvious places, he hides them away, making for one lovely backdrop for your favorite
          <a href="//www.brit.co/terrarium-hacks/" target="_blank">
           succulent
          </a>
          and one of the
          <a href="//www.brit.co/gold-bear-bookends/" target="_blank">
           best bookends
          </a>
          weve ever seen. (via
          <a href="http://www.matthijskok.nl/cubify/" target="_blank">
           Matthijs Kok
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631215" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/16-erragatory.jpg" title="16-erragatory" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          16.
          <a href="http://showstudio.com/shop/product/eragatory_thee_spoon" target="_blank">
           Eragatory Thee Spoon
          </a>
          ($347): This is one for the wedding registry. And at that price, you better have very generous and well-to-do guests attending your nuptials.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631216" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/17-vegtable.jpg" title="17-vegtable" width="645"/>
         </p>
