      <a href="http://nbaa14.mapyourshow.com/6_0/exhibitor/exhibitor-details.cfm?ExhID=305064">
       VezTek USA is exhibiting
      </a>
      this year at the National Business Aviation Associations annual trade show in Orlando FL. If you will be attending this event, please stop by at
      <strong class="hilite">
       Booth # 1846
      </strong>
      . We would love to meet you and let you know what we have to offer when considering mobility and digital marketing options for your FBO business.  reads an email from the company sent this morning to a number of Fixed-Base Operators.
      <p>
       NBAAs 2014 Business Aviation Convention &amp; Exhibition (NBAA2014) returns to Orlando, FL  October 21, 22, 23, 2014. Consideredthe worlds largest and most important business aviation event is alsoranked as the fourth largest tradeshow in the U.S., NBAA2014 will feature over 1,000 exhibitors showcasing the latest products and services in the industry, nearly 100 side-by-side business aircraft at the static display, opportunities to network with 25,000 business aviation professionals and dozens of education sessions.
      </p>
      <p>
       VezTek USA mobile solutions company helps FBOs draw their mobile strategy and roadmaps for developing and launching mobile and web applications; as well as devising strategic roll out plans to provide a hassle free technology adoption experience for the FBO staff and their clients. VezTek FBO service division comprises Apple and Google certified developers as well as seasoned pilots and FBO industry veterans; that unique combination provides the
       <strong class="hilite">
        NBAA member firm
       </strong>
       with a valuable insight into the unique needs of FBOs and their clients
      </p>
      <p>
       
       <strong>
        <strong class="hilite">
         Careful planning is key to leveraging mobile technologies for turning an FBO into a high performance business
        </strong>
       </strong>
       . VezTek USA plans to announce a comprehensive set of initiatives aimed at helping FBOs seamlessly integrate mobile and web applications in their marketing and business operations.  said Sani Abdul-Jabbar, managing director. VezTeks six person team will present hands on demonstration of its FBO-focused mobile and web solutions featuring Monterey Jet Center and other FBOs.
      </p>
      <p>
       NBAA2014 pre-show events begin this weekend and the exhibit halls open at 10 a.m. on Tuesday, immediately following the opening-day general session at 8:30 a.m. in the southhall. Meanwhile, some 100 aircraft will be at the static display hosted by Showalter Aviation at Orlando Executive Airport, with the Bombardier Learjet 85, production-interior-outfitted Cessna Citation Latitude and Embraer Legacy 450 making their debut.
      </p>
