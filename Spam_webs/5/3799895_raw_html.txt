            <header>
             <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
              How saying no will change your life
             </h1>
             <div class="meta-info">
              <ul class="td-category">
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/category/lifestyle/" style="background-color:#c324e2;">
                 Lifestyle
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/category/lifestyle/health/" style="background-color:#c324e2;">
                 Health
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/category/staff/" style="background-color:#727272;">
                 Staff
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/category/staff/mariela-de-la-mora/">
                 Mariela De La Mora
                </a>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <div class="td-clear-author">
              </div>
              <span class="td-block-author">
               by
               <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/author/mariela-delamora/" itemprop="author">
                Mariela De La Mora
               </a>
               -
              </span>
              <meta content="UserComments:0" itemprop="interactionCount"/>
              <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2015-03-19T11:54:34+00:00" itemprop="dateCreated">
               Mar 19, 2015
              </time>
              <meta content="UserComments:0" itemprop="interactionCount"/>
              <div class="entry-comments-views">
              </div>
             </div>
            </header>
            <p class="td-sub-title">
             Don't let fear cause you to accept standards that don't make you happy.
            </p>
            <div class="td-post-featured-image">
             <figure>
              <a data-caption="www.reddit.com" href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/rsz_nobaby.jpg">
               <img alt="Daily News Service" class="entry-thumb td-modal-image" height="462" itemprop="image" src="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/rsz_nobaby-700x462.jpg" title="rsz_nobaby" width="700"/>
              </a>
              <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
               www.reddit.com
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
            </div>
            <div class="td-post-text-content">
             <p>
              The power of No is undeniable. Saying no takes courage to say because it isnt always well received. And you may doubt whether youre doing the right thing. Too often, we stay in unhappy situations until an outside force intervenes to close those doors for us and push us in directions we should have taken on our own. But why leave your fate in the hands of others?
             </p>
             <p>
              The term
              <em>
               blessing in disguise
              </em>
              is often used to describean outside force that drives you in a positive direction you wouldnt have otherwise taken. If you dont like your situation, save yourself by saying no. Dont wait for a blessing in disguise because it will probably never come.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Thats whyno is so powerful.
              </strong>
              Think about this:
             </p>
             <p>
              How many times have you said yes to relationship standards that fell short of your expectations?
             </p>
             <p>
              to a friend who asked for favors but wouldnt do the same in return?
             </p>
             <p>
              or to a job you hated because you were afraid you couldnt find better?
             </p>
             <p>
              Saying no means taking control of whatyou will not accept, and not leaving that decision to someone else.
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               Sayingno requires not being driven by fear.
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              It often means rejecting an option even when you have no alternative to trade it for.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/d6f91faa70cdcf749fd9e15c1fbbbbd167e940bf5d5addcdd6ba93685f5b9cac.jpg">
               <img alt="Daily News Service" class=" size-full wp-image-5389 aligncenter" height="358" src="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/d6f91faa70cdcf749fd9e15c1fbbbbd167e940bf5d5addcdd6ba93685f5b9cac.jpg" width="600"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Saying no can change four important aspects of your life:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               1. Dating and relationships
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Youve probably made the mistake of hanging on to a toxic partner, or subscribing to relationship standards that didnt make you happy. Im not suggesting you should always get what you want, but there should always be reciprocity.
             </p>
             <p>
              You have to know when to invest and when to walk away. And if youre waiting for someone else to fall madly in love with you and steal you away, youre doing it wrong.
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Relationships arent like jobs
              </em>
               you cant interview for a new onewhile holding on tothe one you have. And if you
              <em>
               are
              </em>
              , youre not ready to be in a relationship in the first place.
             </p>
             <p>
              Dont be afraidto stand on your own and endrelationships that dont meet your standards. In fact, by latching on to the
              <em>
               wrong
              </em>
              ones,youre guaranteeing your own misery by ensuring youre unavailable to recognize and receive the
              <em>
               right
              </em>
              one.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               2. Friendships
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Say no to friendships of superficiality or convenience. Every friend should enhance your life in some way. Friendsshould do for you as you do for them, not just when its convenient for them. The key, again, is reciprocity. Free your time from superficial relationships so you can dedicate it to those who enrich your life.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               3. Personal development
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              A sign of maturity is putting less value on short-term gratification. The most worthwhile payoffs are not immediate. They take time, effort and discipline.
             </p>
             <p>
              Sometimes that means saying no to a destination wedding invitation because youre trying to save money for a car, or to pepperoni pizza at 1am because youre trying to eat healthier.
             </p>
             <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5390" style="width: 500px;">
              <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/tumblr_n5mv2vfnwQ1tttpjdo1_500.jpg">
               <img alt="Daily News Service" class="size-full wp-image-5390" height="500" src="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/tumblr_n5mv2vfnwQ1tttpjdo1_500.jpg" width="500"/>
              </a>
              <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
               Me on a diet
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
             <p>
              <strong>
               4. Career
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Jobs are SO much like relationships! And thatmakes job hunting like dating.
             </p>
             <p>
              Know what you bring to the table and what you expect from your employer. If you treat the interview like a first date rather than an audition, youll ask yourself whether this is a job you actually want rather than blindly selling yourself into something thats not a match.
             </p>
             <p>
              Pay attention because how a company treats its candidates during the interview process reflects how they treat their employees:
             </p>
             <p>
              Do they value your time? Do they treat the interview like an audition (one-directional) or a conversation (two-directional)? Are they transparent or do they treatyou like a poker opponent?If itfeels like youre forcing it, you probably are.
             </p>
             <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_5392" style="width: 820px;">
              <a href="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/5lsCFTJ.jpg">
               <img alt="Daily News Service" class="size-full wp-image-5392" height="585" src="http://www.dailynewsservice.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/5lsCFTJ.jpg" width="820"/>
              </a>
              <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
               www.reddit.com
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Above all, remember
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              The hardest part is having the confidence to realise
              <em>
               your options are not limited to whats in front of you
              </em>
              , becauseyou can create new options.
             </p>
             <p>
              If you let fear drive your decisions, youll miss the right opportunities because youll be too busy putting a bandage on the yeses that should have been no.
             </p>
