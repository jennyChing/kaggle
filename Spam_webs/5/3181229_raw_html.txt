                         half double crochet (hdc)
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td>
                         treble (tr)
                        </td>
                        <td>
                         double crochet (dc)
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td>
                         double treble (dtr)
                        </td>
                        <td>
                         treble (tr)
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td>
                         triple treble (trtr)
                        </td>
                        <td>
                         double treble (dtr)
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td>

                        </td>
                        <td>
                         skip
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td>
                         tension
                        </td>
                        <td>
                         gauge
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td>
                         yarn over hook (yoh)
                        </td>
                        <td>
                         yarn over (yo)
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                      <u>
                      </u>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #9fc5e8;">
                       <u>
                        <span style="font-size: medium;">
                         Stitch Abbreviations
                        </span>
                       </u>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <table border="1" cellpadding="2">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td valign="top" width="50%">
                         st(s) = stitch(es)
                        </td>
                        <td valign="top" width="50%">
                         htbl- half treble
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td valign="top" width="50%">
                         <br/>
                         dtbl cross
                        </td>
                        <td valign="top" width="50%">
                         dtbr- double treble
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td valign="top" width="50%">
                         tbl cross
                        </td>
                        <td valign="top" width="50%">
                         ch - chain
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <span style="font-size: medium;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                      trbl cross - to treble cross you skip a stitch then treble, then wrap wool as you would to start a new stitch but go back into the skipped stitch and treble
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                      dtbl cross - is the same as above but a double treble stitch you are creating and twisting.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                      blo- is working into the back loop
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                      I used a 4 mm hook for this pattern.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <h4>
                      <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                       Cast on 12 sts
                      </span>
                     </h4>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                       Row 1
                      </span>
                      .
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       Chain 2 to make first "htrb" then htbl the next 11 sts - 12 in total
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                       Row 2.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       Ch3 tbl 1, then tbl cross into the next 8 stitches, making 4 twists, trbl normally into the last 2 stitches.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        Row 3.
                       </span>
                       Ch 2 then htbl into the blo until end.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        Row 4.
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         Ch 2 htbl until end.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          Row 5.
                         </span>
                         Repeat row 3.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        * Row 6
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          .
                         </span>
                         Ch 4 then dtbl next st, dtbl cross in next 8 stitches then dtbl in last two stitches
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          * Row 7.
                         </span>
                         Ch2 htbl into blo until end.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         Repeat these two rows until row 30 or until big enough to fit your head ending with the htbl row.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          Next row.
                          <span style="color: #444444;">
                           Ch 2 htbl until end.
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          <span style="color: #444444;">
                           <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                            Next row.
                           </span>
                           Ch 2 htbl into blo until end.
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          <span style="color: #444444;">
                           <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                            Next row.
                           </span>
                           Ch 3 tbl 1, tbl cross the next 8 sts , tbl in last 2 sts.
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #3d85c6; font-size: x-large;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                        <span style="color: #444444;">
                         <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                          <span style="color: #444444;">
                           <span style="color: #3d85c6;">
                            Next row.
                           </span>
                           Ch2 htbl into blo until end - cast off and sew edges together.
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
