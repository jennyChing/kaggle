               <h1 class="in-article">
                Man Raped Woman to Learn English in Prison
               </h1>
               <div class="abstract">
                <div class="author clearfix">
                 By
                 <a ach="" achh="" class="userlink" href="/user/1705" onmouseover="djp.user.tt(this,event);" pg="5">
                  Chris V. Thangham
                 </a>
                 Jan 21, 2009            in
                 <a class="department" href="/crime">
                  Crime
                 </a>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="abstract">
                The ex-convict told a psychiatrist that he raped a woman so that he could be well-fed, housed and taught English at the prison. The judge is planning to impose a life sentence on him.
                <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="abstract">
                <div style="padding-bottom:10px">
                 <script type="text/javascript">
                  djp.ga_fill("300x250-Big-Box-ATF");
                 </script>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="article-column">
                <div class="rs_skip">
                </div>
                <div class="body">
                 Alim Majlat, 35, was released from the Romanian jail last June after serving time for theft and attempted robbery. He entered United King to visit his brother. Soon after he was sent to prison for eight weeks in London for burglary.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 Days after release he attacked the 21-year-old woman, who was waiting on a platform at Wakefield Kirkgate station. He raped her and robbed her mobile phone, purse and bracelet on Oct. 12 last year.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 He was then arrested days after a nationwide British Transport Police appeal.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 The court-appointed psychiatrist interviewed Majlat and found that he raped her so that he could learn English inside prison and also be well-fed and housed.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 The judge Alistair Maclean hearing the case was dumbfounded to hear this explanation
                 <a href="http://www.yorkshireeveningpost.co.uk/news/Wakefield-rapist-wanted-jail-English.4891029.jp" target="_blank">
                  asked
                 </a>
                 his defense counsel, Mark McKone whether it was true or not.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 McKone replied to the judge:
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 <q>
                  He does say words similar to that.
                 </q>
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 Judge McCallum then questioned him:
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 <q>
                  That's bizarre ... Is it going to be part of the mitigation that the reason for committing this offence was to get into an English jail so he had somewhere to sleep and learn the language?
                 </q>
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 McKone replied that it wont be the mitigating factor but that is what his client had explained to him.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                 Judge McCallum wants prosecutors to investigate Majlats past history in Romania. He plans to impose a life sentence for Majlat.
                 <div style="padding-bottom:15px;">
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
