							&lt;h1&gt;Are These Foods Killing Your Brain?&lt;/h1&gt;
							&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.thedailylife.com/cognizine"&gt;&lt;img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-547" alt="Cognizine" src="http://49krjibwenxxrtcj3fdxjbk9r.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/CG_600x350_cognizine_BGS-300x175.jpg" width="300" height="175" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;Did you know that Alzheimer&#8217;s is ranked as our #1 fear? And for good reason.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;As anyone over 40 knows forgetting things and feeling less mentally sharp often becomes an unavoidable side effect of getting older.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;There are currently 3,000 food additives in the US food supply.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Here&#8217;s the 5 that are &lt;a href="http://www.thedailylife.com/cognizine"&gt;killing your brain&lt;/a&gt;&#8230;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;1. MSG (monosodium glutamate)&lt;/strong&gt; &#8211; MSG raises the amount of glutamate acid in the brain, which can cause death of nerve cells.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;What&#8217;s even worse is now food manufacturers are trying to hide this ingredient with clever and innocent-sounding names; hydrolyzed protein, vegetable protein, soy protein isolate, soy protein concentrate, whey protein, and natural flavoring, spices, enzymes, autolyzed yeast extract, stock, broth and carrageenan.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Make sure to avoid this excitotoxin &#8211; it literally stimulates the brain cells till they die. One of the common sources is bouillon cubes.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;2. Aspartame&lt;/strong&gt; &#8211; Warning! This Sweetener May Affect Your Memory, and Worse&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In spite of studies that show animals fed aspartame developed seizures and brain tumors, aspartame is FDA approved and added to a lot of food and drink products.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Currently, there are 92 categories of complaints filed against aspartame with the FDA. This one is easy to avoid since it&#8217;s clearly labeled.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;3. Sucralose&lt;/strong&gt; &#8211; Another sweetener that taste just may be too good to be true.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Sucralose reduces the good gut bacteria by 50% allowing your body to become toxic. This leads to damaging effects on the hippocampus, where memories are stored.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;4. Diacetyl (Butter Flavoring)&lt;/strong&gt; &#8211; Who doesn&#8217;t love a bag of popcorn?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In fact, 17.3 billion quarts are popped each year! But the home-popped variety contains the butter flavor additive diacetyl.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Diacetyl is able to cross the blood-brain barrier, a defense which prevents harmful substances from entering the brain. It causes beta-amyloid clumping which is a significant indicator of Alzheimers.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You wont see diacetyl on the label, but if you see artificial butter flavor or natural flavors you should avoid it at all costs.&lt;/p&gt;
