         Thats actually bad to see how media has made BAD meaning of this term "HACKER"

         Here are some excerpts from ccc.de
         <br/>
         on what HACK / HACKER / HACKING is
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Hacking is not a crime
         <br/>
         	A crime needs criminals to take place
         <br/>
         	Criminals pursue financial goals
         <br/>
         	Criminals do not share knowledge
         <br/>
         	Hackers pursue higher goals
         <br/>
         	Hackers share knowledge
         <br/>
         	Hackers are not criminals
         <br/>
         	They need to explain complicated things
         <br/>
         	Using media to educate the public
         <br/>
         	Hackers are concerned citizens and artists
         <br/>
         	Hacker Ethics reflect principles of culture
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Hackers Ethics
         <br/>
         	Access to computers should be unlimited and total
         <br/>
         	Always yield to the Hands-on Imperative!
         <br/>
         	All information should be free
         <br/>
         	Computers can change your life for the better.
         <br/>
         	Hackers should be judged by their hacking...
         <br/>
         	You can create art and beauty on a computer.
         <br/>
         	Every good hacker is an artist
