        <h1 class="cat_article_title">
         6 Shocking Drunk or Drugged Up Public Displays By Celebrities
        </h1>
        <div style="height:5px;">
        </div>
        <div class="single_article_content" id="article_content">
         <p>
          <a id="dd_start">
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          We all know that no one is perfect. That doesnt mean its not humorous to sometimes see our favorite celebrity make a drunken debacle of themselves in public. Here is a list of 6 of the most shocking displays celebrities have put on for the world to see.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Reese Witherspoon
         </h3>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/g9fwe_NEerE?feature=oembed" width="500">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Reese Witherspoon isnt normally known as analcohol drinking wild woman. However recently the world saw this humoring side of her. She was with her husband in Atlanta when a cop stopped them and put him in hand cuffs. Reese, being the loyal wife she is, stuck up for her husband. She tried the give the cop a piece of her mind asking Do you know who I am? They were both arrested as a result. She was arrested for dis-orderly conduct and he was taken in for drunk driving.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Prince Harry
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          What happens in Vegas stays in Vegas. Unless of course you are the royal Prince Harry. Last year the Prince was having a good time in Vegas playing a friendly game of strip billiards in his hotel suit. Photos were taken and leaked all over the internet. The pictures are of him bearing hugging woman while both being completely naked. Prince Harry later commented that he felt as though he let his family down.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Steve-O
         </h3>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="375" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/_rafFvbCf4Y?feature=oembed" width="500">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The world is expects nothing less than absurd gestures from Jackass star Steve-O. He has done some outrageous stunts for the TV show and Movies and that translated in to his personal life as well. There are many instances of Steve-O being drunk in public. The most notable is when he appeared on Too Late with Adam Carolla. He was falling all over his chair and yelling obscenities at the top off his lungs. Steve-o eventually went to an
          <a href="http://www.12keysrehab.com/inpatient-alcohol-rehab.html" target="_blank">
           inpatient rehab for alcoholics
          </a>
          to get treated for a drug and alcohol addiction.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Mariah Carey
         </h3>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="375" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/sleaSKmd5Js?feature=oembed" width="500">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Mariah Carey is often look at as being loopy and outgoing. She isnt often look at as being a drunken mess. However it did happen in 2010, at an award show, in front of the world. She got up to accept her award for Best Supporting actress and had a hard time finding her words and giggling a lot. She talked about many different thing, but this was not your typical thank you speech.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Kayne West
         </h3>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/OeuHxZURHls?feature=oembed" width="500">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Kayne West is the ring leader in whats goes down as one of the most iconic moments at the MTV Video Music awards. Taylor Swift has just won an award for Best Female Video and is about to give her speech. From out of the crowd a drunken West appears and interrupts her entire speech. He simply wanted to express his feelings that Beyonce had one of the best singles of all time. West disappears again and poo Swift is left standing on stage speechless.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Amanda Bynes
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Amanda Bynes has taken a page from Brittney Spears book recently. Over the past year she has made a spectacle of her self pulling off some outrageous stunts. The most recent incident is when she set fire to a strangers driveway. She was found sitting in the grass next to the driveway, pantless, with her gasoline soaked dog. When someone passing by stopped to put the fire out she wandered off to a liquor store down the street. She was eventually found but no charges were pressed against her. She was taken to a mental hospital where she is being treated for a
          <a href="http://radaronline.com/exclusives/2013/09/amanda-bynes-leaves-ucla-being-treated-psychiatric-disorder/" target="_blank">
           psychiatric disorder
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Seeing people drunk and disorderly in public is not an uncommon appearance. However its not often we see our poised and proper celebrities have these moments. Do you have an embarrassing display of public drunkenness youd rather forget?
         </p>
