&lt;p&gt;Respawn Entertainment has announcedTitanfall: Frontiers Edge, the secondof three planned content packs for Titanfall.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The DLC introduces three new maps for multiplayer, including the isolated mining outpost of Dig Site, the exclusive beach resort of Haven, and the mining hub on the side of a mountain known as Export.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Frontiers Edge will be available for $9.99 on the Xbox One, Xbox 360, and PC. Alternatively, you can purchase the Titanfall Season Pass which will cost $24.99.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Titanfall&#8217;s first DLC pack, titled Expedition, was released on May 15. No release date for the second DLC pack was given, but we&#8217;ll update you when more information is revealed.&lt;/p&gt;
