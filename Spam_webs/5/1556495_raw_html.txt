        <p>
         A Kansas woman may have taken her fear of spiders too far on Friday.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Fire crews told The Hutchinson News that Ginny M. Griffith, 34, was charged with aggravated arson after lighting a fire in order to kill a spider.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The woman told officers she used a cigarette lighter in order to ignite some towels on fire, and burn the spider. Instead, Griffith ended up lighting her duplex on fire.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Crews found multiple points of origin for the fire, which they quickly put out. They say the duplex really only suffered smoke damage.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         No word on if the spider made it out safely.
