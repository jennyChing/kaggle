        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Social Media tips: 15 Pinterest tips to help you grow
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-25T15:25:22+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 25, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

        <p>
         As a blogger social media is so important to the growth of my site but it can be confusing. Thats why I like to write posts about social media tips. That way I can share what Ive learned and hopefully help you avoid some of the confusion. I love Pinterest. It brings my blog so many readers. I know with recent changes though some bloggers lost traffic and some bloggers just dont know how to use Pinterest well. So here are my social media tips when using Pinterest.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://afreshstartonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pinterest.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="15  tips to help you grow even with Pinterest's changes" class="aligncenter wp-image-3603 size-large" height="1024" src="http://afreshstartonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pinterest-697x1024.jpg" width="697"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://afreshstartonabudget.com/15-pinterest-tips-to-help-you-grow-despite-pinterests-recent-changes/&amp;media=http://afreshstartonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pinterest-697x1024.jpg&amp;description=Social Media tips: 15 Pinterest tips to help you grow')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          This postwill be focusing on Pinterest.So to start things off here are my top 10 favorite Pinterest Tips! These arent new tips but they are tried and true tips that I have done or currently do to maximize my Pinterest.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          1.
          <strong>
           Share who you are with your followers
          </strong>
          . Include your picture and fill out the about section so people know who they are following.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          Here is mine as an example.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          <a href="http://afreshstartonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/pinterest.png">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="pinterest" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1978" height="164" src="http://afreshstartonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/pinterest-1024x164.png" width="1024"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://afreshstartonabudget.com/15-pinterest-tips-to-help-you-grow-despite-pinterests-recent-changes/&amp;media=http://afreshstartonabudget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/pinterest-1024x164.png&amp;description=Social Media tips: 15 Pinterest tips to help you grow')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          2.
          <strong>
           Comment on pins.
          </strong>
          Not a lot of people comment so the comments stand out and people who might not be following you yet might take notice and the people you are following probably dont get a lot of comments. Its a nice way to increase interaction with you and your followers.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          3.
          <strong>
           consistently pin things
          </strong>
          You dont want your followers to forget you are on Pinterest. I try to get on Pinterest everyday even if just to post a couple things and do a little re-pinning.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          4.
          <strong>
           Dont over pin!
          </strong>
          If youve been on Pinterest before where someone has pinned 100 similar items at once and it takes up most of your feed, you know thats annoying,, try not to be annoying.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          5.
          <strong>
           Schedule Pins!
          </strong>
          If you can try a pinning service to schedule pins. Im a fan of
          <a href="http://analytics.tailwindapp.com/" target="_blank">
           Tailwind
          </a>
          . You can schedule pins for the next week. I pay for the monthly service. I believe its $10 a month and Tailwind schedules the posts for when my Pinterest pins are seen the best.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          5.
          <strong>
           Your board is most important.
          </strong>
          It should be the first board people see when the click on your Pinterest Page. If its not, move your boards around. You want to drive people to your site and make it easy to find your content.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          6.
          <strong>
           Organize your boards
          </strong>
          . Mine are my board first. Then seasonal boards like the current Holiday. Then boards related to what I blog about (social media, organization) Then less used boards ( other holidays, boards that are more personal). Then Group boards.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          7.
          <strong>
           Put your group boards at the bottom!
          </strong>
          People dont want to scroll through all your group boards. so group them together at the bottom.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          8
          <strong>
           .Join Group boards!
          </strong>
          This helps more people see your pins but you need to post regularly to them, I need to get better about this. Im in a few group boards but I dont join every board Im invited to because they dont fit me.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          9.
          <strong>
           Group boards arent as important as they used to be
          </strong>
          . With the changes in how pins are shown now group boards dont matter as much as they did. I cut down my group boards to the ones I really enjoy being a part of. The ones that relate best to my blog or that I get a lot of repins from. Im just more selective now.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          10.
          <strong>
           Label your pins.
          </strong>
          The new search makes this more important than ever. So include popular key words in your pin descriptions.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          11.
          <strong>
           Link your Pinterest with your Twitter and Facebook!
          </strong>
          Here is a great article about it
          <a href="http://www.ohsopinteresting.com/pinterest-tip-linking-facebook-to-pinterest-for-businesses/" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          !
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          12.
          <strong>
           Have a pin it button on your site!
          </strong>
          Make it easy for your readers to pin your content. There are a million tutorials on this so search for one that works for you.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          13.
          <strong>
           Grow your Pinterest.
          </strong>
          The best way to do this is to try to like 10 new pinners daily and like and comment on multiple pins daily. Get your account being seen.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          14.
          <strong>
           Update old posts for Pinterest
          </strong>
          . Create pinnable images for older posts to get them pinned on your board and drive traffic to them. Tall iages work best I have found. Canva even has a pinterest size template.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
          15.
          <strong>
           Clean up your boards.
          </strong>
          Rename them and add boards descriptions using those keywords I mentioned earlier. It will help you come up in searches.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>

          Id love for you to Follow me on

           Pinterest
          </a>
