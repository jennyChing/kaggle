    <p>
     <strong>
      Graukse  a mountain delicacy.
     </strong>
     <a href="http://www.gasthaus.it/en/valuable-information/dishes-with-a-long-history/grey-cheese.html" target="_blank">
      Graukse
     </a>
     or grey cheese, is a product of the frugal existence in the mountains where nothing goes to waste and everything is put to good use. As milk was used for butter, grey cheese was produced with the remaining skimmed milk without adding rennet. This cheese is popular in eastern Tyrol and has been awarded the slow food residio prize  an award for nearly forgotten, high-quality regional products with traditional processing techniques. Grab a cracker and give it a try.
    </p>

Local fare will be served in Alpine huts across the mountains meaning that a whole day can be spent walking from hut to hut to enjoy the delicacies on offer within.
