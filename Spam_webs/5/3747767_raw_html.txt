                       <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wL5ikSTLij0/VTJOK_Yz5dI/AAAAAAAAZBU/2CfS_qYlNHQ/s1600/Mason%2BJar%2BSolar%2BLights.jpg" title="Mason jar lighting using the tops of solar lights and a few gems to reflect the soft lighting for your patio or yard    http://www.myturnforus.com/2015/05/mason-jar-solar-lights.html" width="488"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      I love yard lights twinkling in the summer nightilluminatingsoft romantic areas around theyard.  And one of my favorite things to do in the evenings is sit outside and enjoy the cool night breeze and watch and listen to nature.  I have several tables set around the Ranch yard where we can sit and have coffee or a beverage and just enjoy the end of the day and
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      I needed a light on these tables.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      I wanted a soft light that I wouldn't have to light, but was heavy enough not to blow away during our spring winds.  I hadn't been able to solve this until one of my little lights down at the front gates was destroyed by fallingdebris
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      during a windstorm.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-TQY7eCEDa3I/VVB3pkU5AeI/AAAAAAAAZMk/NPDLvsmeNGU/s1600/Broken%2BLight.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-TQY7eCEDa3I/VVB3pkU5AeI/AAAAAAAAZMk/NPDLvsmeNGU/s640/Broken%2BLight.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      I remembered that my sweet friend down the road (ok 8 miles down the road) had made the cutest little mason jar lights from the tops of her solar lights when the bases was damaged.  I saved the top of the light andgatheredseveral other supplies.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wGhtqY1AsQA/VTJSyqxTEfI/AAAAAAAAZBg/axHL7bBr0xs/s1600/Materials%2Bneeds%2Bfor%2BMason%2BSolar%2BLights.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wGhtqY1AsQA/VTJSyqxTEfI/AAAAAAAAZBg/axHL7bBr0xs/s1600/Materials%2Bneeds%2Bfor%2BMason%2BSolar%2BLights.jpg" width="560"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Supplies needed:
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Top of a solar yard light
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Wide Mouth Mason Jar (or any size mason jar that the ring will fit your solar light)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       2 cups of glass gems (for weight)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       gorilla glue
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Cx-mZ0R29JY/VTJYDjETPTI/AAAAAAAAZB0/AcvDvCLUCWQ/s1600/glass%2Bgems.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Cx-mZ0R29JY/VTJYDjETPTI/AAAAAAAAZB0/AcvDvCLUCWQ/s1600/glass%2Bgems.jpg" width="480"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Pour the glass gems in the jar filling about halfway (about 2 cups of gems for a quart size wide mouth jar). Filling only halfway leaves room for the light to shine and the gems to reflect the light.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-uN2YLi3t4gI/VTJXGNUb2lI/AAAAAAAAZBs/tgtuYw3dO2Q/s1600/Bottom%2Bof%2BSolar%2Blight.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-uN2YLi3t4gI/VTJXGNUb2lI/AAAAAAAAZBs/tgtuYw3dO2Q/s1600/Bottom%2Bof%2BSolar%2Blight.jpg" width="480"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Remove the battery and cover. Center the mason jar lid ring on the bottom of the solar light making sure you do not cover the opening for the battery. Not to worry about replacing batteries since the sun recharges the battery every day and you will enjoymaintenanceFree lights every night!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       (but if the batteries ever are not able to recharge, then simply replace with a new AA battery, my lights are almost two years old and I have never replaced them yet but I know I can if needed)
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Glue in place
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-4uOaxcnUExI/VTJZcuhimaI/AAAAAAAAZCA/wQI0BC-aDvY/s1600/ring%2Bon%2Bsolar%2Blight.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-4uOaxcnUExI/VTJZcuhimaI/AAAAAAAAZCA/wQI0BC-aDvY/s1600/ring%2Bon%2Bsolar%2Blight.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Replace battery and cover, and if needed file the corners off the cover or just leave the cover off. Screw the solar light on the mason jar and place outside and enjoy your evening filled with the soft lighting.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Is1o2a59s10/VTJbolet0AI/AAAAAAAAZCM/OrwaWhPxO3U/s1600/Jar%2Bat%2Bnight.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Is1o2a59s10/VTJbolet0AI/AAAAAAAAZCM/OrwaWhPxO3U/s1600/Jar%2Bat%2Bnight.jpg" title="Mason jar lighting using the tops of solar lights and a few gems to reflect the soft lighting for your patio or yard    http://www.myturnforus.com/2015/05/mason-jar-solar-lights.html" width="568"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       See how gorgeous these lights are with the gems reflecting the light!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       I purchased several more of these lights and removed the tops to make lights for other areas.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-y02A3X93fpo/VUBA4yA3uiI/AAAAAAAAZHs/ah5EpPzNhUg/s1600/New%2Bsolar%2BLights.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-y02A3X93fpo/VUBA4yA3uiI/AAAAAAAAZHs/ah5EpPzNhUg/s1600/New%2Bsolar%2BLights.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       They are sitting around on tables and on the BBQ Pit around the Ranch yard. I just love them and the price was even better.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Solar yard light prices range from $3.98 to 6.98 (these are the $6.98) and I had the mason jars already and even had the glass gems so the price was awesome!!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Now I know you wish to see the object of my inspiration!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       My friend Angie's lights!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       She used wire and made little hangers on each jar and they are hanging in her flower beds bordering the sidewalk.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       Just gorgeous!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">

                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gaF2mOeZkyg/VTJfLL1qMqI/AAAAAAAAZCY/e6nKrz5XtUo/s1600/Angie's%2BMason%2BJar%2Blight.jpg" width="466"/>
                      </a>
