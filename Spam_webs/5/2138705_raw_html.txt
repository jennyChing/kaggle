         <p>
          I have read about and have written posts on how to keep yourself cool during the summer months. We have had such record breaking temperatures it has been vital to make sure we take care of ourselves when we are out in this heat. What many of may not consider is how animals fare when it is this hot outside. If you are a pet owner, you probably know some of these tips but you may not be aware of them all. I thought it was a good idea to have a little refresher safety course onhow to protectour dogs during the summer.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="6 Ideas To Keep Your Dog Cool This Summer pin" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73870" height="630" src="http://makobiscribe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/6-Ideas-To-Keep-Your-Dog-Cool-This-Summer-pin.jpg" width="348"/>
         </p>
         <h2>
          6 Ideas To Keep Your Dog Cool This Summer
         </h2>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Time Your Walks:
           </strong>
           Even though it is hot, your pup still needs to get his exercise. Make sure you take him out either early in the morning or later in the evening, when it is cooler.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Watch For Dehydration:
           </strong>
           Dogs cannot sweat, so you need to watch for signs they are not hydrated enough. If you seen your pooch panting or drooling excessively, is lethargic and has bloodshot eyes, they are dehydrated.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Keep Your Dog Hydrated:
           </strong>
           You must make sure your puppy gets enough to drink when you are out in this heat. Make sure you carry at least one bottle of water and if possible, a portable water bowl, and let your pup drink as much as they need to.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Let Them Dig:
           </strong>
           Your dog will find ways to stay cool, and digging in the dirt is one of them. Find a shady area in the park and let your pup dig there.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Take Them Swimming:
           </strong>
           There is no better reason to get in the pool then to cool you and your dog off! Dogs love to swim, so this is a great time to cool them off and let them play at the same time.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Get Creative:
           </strong>
           If need be, get creative when finding ways to keep your dog cool. One of the most unique products I have seen to keep your dog cool is the Aspen Pet Self-Cooling Mat, distributed from Petmate.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          <img alt="Keep Your Dog Cool In The Summer" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73869" height="472" src="http://makobiscribe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Keep-Your-Dog-Cool-In-The-Summer.jpg" width="630"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I think of my pets as part of our family, so I take great care in what we buy for them. We buy organic foods for them to eat, so I am ensured they are getting a healthy and well balanced meal. This is important for them to grow strong and have a healthy coat, teeth, and organs.

They offer solutions to the traditional travel crates, bedding, toys, collars, leashes, food and water bowls and more.
