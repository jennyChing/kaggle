           <section class="ft-entry">
            <p>
             Since texting has become the preferred method of communication for just about everyone except your Nana, its easy to start finding little things that people do that drive you nuts. Some of you may not even realize you do these awful things, so lets take care of these offenses before your friends and family exile you. Here are the 12 most annoying type of texters.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Never Ending Typing Bubble
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 0.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-0.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             I dont know if you sat on your phone and accidentally pressed a single key or if youre typing a novel, but that ominous floating bubble of mystery is driving everyone insane.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Rapid Fire
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 1.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-1.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             If you have a message you want to send, send it all at once. I dont need 800 alerts because you insist on hitting send after every four words.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Group Text Side Conversation
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 2.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-2.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             A group text can be a great way to help plan an event or for a group to catch up with each other, but the problem is when a side conversation starts and wont end. Have you ever looked down and say 87 new messages only to realize none of them are to you and its just two of your friends mindlessly chatting in a group text? Stop that.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Dismissive K
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 3.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-3.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             By now you should definitely know that sending K as the reply is just about the worst thing you can do. Its even worse when the person texted you a long message that should warrant a response thats at least a sentence.
            </p>
            <h6>
             THE UNINTENTIONAL SCREAMER
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 4.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-4.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             If you are intending to scream this text at me, then youre doing a great job. If youre just trying to communicate, please remove your finger from the caps lock button. Thanks.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Ignorer
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 5.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-5.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             I know you saw my text. Why are you completely disregarding it and talking about something else, you monster?
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Super Abbreviator
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 6.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-6.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Youre more than welcome to abbreviate a word here and there, but when there are no vowels in a sentence, it may be time to start expanding your vocabulary.
            </p>
            <h6>
             Non-Stop Emojis
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 7.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-7.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <div class="via_source">
             via
             <a href="http://jessicabari.com/sexting-emojis-new-sex/" target="_blank">
              Jessicabari
             </a>
            </div>
            <p>
             Just like with the abbreviations, a few here and there are perfectly fine. However, if there are more emojis than words in your conversation, please stop. Were all begging you.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Suspense Builder
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 8.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-8.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Just tell me what youre going to say! Does hey mean youre upset? Does it mean youre happy? Are we hanging out? Are you coming to kill me? I DONT KNOW!
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Phone Call Screener
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 9.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-9.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             If you want to talk, just call. Dont send that creepy message that makes it feel like youre about to ruin my life. No one has ever gotten that text and assumed it meant anything good.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Follow Up
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 10.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-10.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             If someone didnt respond to your text, theyre either busy at the moment or they dont want to respond. Its never a good idea to send 14 messages in a row trying to identify a service problem in their area. Most of the time they were just busy and now you look like an insane person.
            </p>
            <h6>
             The Non-Texter  THE WORST ONE OF ALL
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="The 12 Most Annoying Type Of Texters We All Deal With 11.png" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-12-Most-Annoying-Type-Of-Texters-We-All-Deal-With-11.png?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <div class="via_source">
             via
             <a href="http://www.javipas.com/2010/06/15/android-vs-ios-un-estudio-de-usabilidad/" target="_blank">
              Javipas
             </a>
            </div>
            <p>
             Im not completely anti-phone call, but if theres something that would take 6 seconds to text and you choose to call me instead, Ill mark your name out of my will.
            </p>
