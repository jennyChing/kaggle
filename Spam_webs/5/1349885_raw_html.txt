     <h1 class="entry-title">
      23 Fresh Strawberry Recipes
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-07-16T15:54:10+00:00">
       July 16, 2014
      </time>
      <p class="byline author vcard">
       By 
			Shari's Berries
      </p>
     </div>
    </header>

   <!-- /.hero -->
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <p class="lead">
        If you live in warmer climates, like the lucky residents of California or Florida, strawberries are a year-round treat. For everyone else, summer heralds the beginning of strawberry season. As fun as it is to eat berries plucked from the patch, there's nothing like using fresh-from-the-farm strawberries in your recipes. Juicy, naturally sweet and bright red, they add a lovely flavor and color to any dish. From strawberry poppy-seed pancakes to strawberry balsamic pizza, our round-up has unique and delicious recipes for every meal.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div>
         <h4>
          1. Strawberry Pretzel Salad Parfait
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2220" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Strawberry Pretzel Salad Parfait" class="size-full wp-image-2220" height="699" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strawberry-pretzel-parfait.jpg" width="800"/>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Photo courtesy of: Dessert for Two
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          If you like dessert for breakfast, youll love this strawberry pretzel parfait layered with brown sugar yogurt. Its easy to make and is the perfect balance of salty and sweet. Recipe via
          <a href="http://www.dessertfortwo.com/2013/04/strawberry-pretzel-parfait/" target="_blank">
           Dessert for Two
          </a>
          .
         </p>

          2. Roasted Strawberry Muffins
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2206" style="width: 810px">

          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Photo courtesy of: Two Recipes.
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          These strawberry muffins are moist and sweet without being too cakey. The intense flavor of the roasted fruit tastes like baked summer. Recipe via
          <a href="http://www.tworecipesblog.com/2013/07/roasted-strawberry-muffins.html" target="_blank">
           Two Recipes
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h4>

           Photo courtesy of: Dine and Dish
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Can pancakes capture the taste of summer? The bright citrus flavor of these lemon pancakes say yes, especially when you top them with a generous helping of fresh diced strawberries. Recipe via
          <a href="http://dineanddish.net/2010/04/finally-beyond-plain-recipe-lemon-poppy-seed-pancakes/" target="_blank">
           Dine and Dish
          </a>
          .

         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2208" style="width: 810px">

           Photo courtesy of: eCurry  The Recipe Blog
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Infused with ginger, chili pepper and spices, this strawberry chutney is incredibly versatile. Spread it on crackers and sourdough bread or stir it in yogurt. Recipe via

           eCurry  The Recipe Blog
          </a>

         </h4>

          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>

          </a>

           Photo courtesy of: The Cafe Sucre Farine
          </figcaption>

           The Cafe Sucre Farine
          </a>
          .

           Photo courtesy of: The Floating Kitchen
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>

          <a href="http://www.floatingkitchen.net/roasted-strawberry-and-asparagus-salad-with-strawberry-tahini-dressing/" target="_blank">
           The Floating Kitchen
          </a>
          .

          8. Strawberry and Basil Crostini
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2212" style="width: 810px">

           Photo courtesy of: Cookin Canuck

           Cookin Canuck
          </a>
          .

          9. Strawberry Napoleon
         </h4>

           Photo courtesy of: Driscolls
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          This sweet strawberry dessert is so good that no one will guess its made with store-bought pie dough. Bake heart-shaped pastry and layer with whipped cream and fresh fruit. Recipe via

           Driscolls
          </a>
          .

          10. Honey-Sweetened Strawberry Sauce

         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2200" style="width: 810px">

         <p>

          <a href="http://www.texanerin.com/2012/06/honey-sweetened-strawberry-sauce.html" target="_blank">

          11. Strawberry Pie Sour Cream Crumb Bars

         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2218" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Strawberry Pie Sour Cream Crumb Bars" class="size-full wp-image-2218" height="912" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strawberry-pie-sour-cream-crumb-bars.jpg" width="800"/>

           Photo courtesy of: The Recipe Critic
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Youll love the swirls of strawberry pie filling in this sour cream crumb bar. Although the recipe will work with just about any fruit filling, strawberry is our favorite. Recipe via
          <a href="http://therecipecritic.com/2013/05/strawberry-pie-sour-cream-crumb-bars/" target="_blank">
           The Recipe Critic
          </a>
          .

          12. Strawberry Mango Fruit Roll-ups
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2215" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Strawberry Mango Fruit Roll-ups" class="size-full wp-image-2215" height="600" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strawberry-mango-fruit-roll-ups.jpg" width="800"/>

           Photo courtesy of: Meatballs and Milkshakes
          </figcaption>

          <a href="http://meatballsandmilkshakes.com/strawberry-mango-fruit-roll-ups/" target="_blank">
           Meatballs and Milkshakes
          </a>
          .

          13. Roasted Strawberry, Chickpea and Asparagus Couscous
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2205" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Roasted Strawberry, Chickpea and Asparagus Couscous" class="size-full wp-image-2205" height="606" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/roasted-strawberry-chickpea-asparagus.jpg" width="800"/>

           Photo courtesy of: Vanilla and Spice
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Couscous transforms this light salad into a hearty entree. Toss chickpeas, fresh herbs and roasted strawberries with a few tablespoons of balsamic vinegar for a dish thats filling but not heavyjust right for summer. Recipe via
          <a href="http://www.vanilla-and-spice.com/2012/06/roasted-strawberry-chickpea-asparagus.html" target="_blank">
           Vanilla and Spice
          </a>
          .

          14. Strawberry Banana Sorbet

         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2211" style="width: 810px">

           Photo courtesy of: Butter, with a side of Bread
          </figcaption>

          Sorbet is a wonderful way to enjoy berries that are at the peak of sweetness. This recipe mixes the traditional fruit puree with several ripe bananas for a creamier concoction that it is divine. Recipe via
          <a href="http://www.butterwithasideofbread.com/2012/07/strawberry-banana-sorbet.html" target="_blank">
           Butter, with a side of Bread
          </a>
          .

          15. Grilled Strawberry Shortcake Kebabs
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2198" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Grilled Strawberry Shortcake Kebabs" class="size-full wp-image-2198" height="534" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/grilled-strawberry-shortcake-kabobs.jpg" width="800"/>

           Photo courtesy of: From Away

          <a href="http://www.fromaway.com/cooking/grilled-strawberry-shortcake-kababs" target="_blank">
           From Away
          </a>
          .

          </figcaption>

          <a href="http://teaspooncomm.com/teaspoonofspice/2012/04/peanut-butter-strawberry-jelly-bread-improv-challenge/" target="_blank">

           Sugar Mama Cooks
          </a>
          .

          <a href="http://strawberrysue.com/2010/12/roasted-asparagus-salad/" target="_blank">
           Strawberry Sue and the Florida Strawberry Growers Association

           Buttercream Blondie

          20. Strawberry Chicken Salad With Warm Orange Vinaigrette

           Photo courtesy of: Barefeet In The Kitchen

          <a href="http://www.barefeetinthekitchen.com/2013/07/strawberry-chicken-salad-with-warm-vinaigrette-recipe.html" target="_blank">

          21. Strawberries and Cream Sandwiches

          Strawberries and cream is a classic combination that never fails. This version, made with whipped cream and graham crackers, is easy to put together for a last-minute dessert. Recipe via
          <a href="http://alidaskitchen.com/2012/06/16/strawberries-and-cream-sandwiches/" target="_blank">
           Alidas Kitchen
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h4>
          22. Strawberry Peach Jam
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2217" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Strawberry Peach Jam" class="size-full wp-image-2217" height="534" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strawberry-peach-jam.jpg" width="800"/>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Photo courtesy of: The Busty Baker
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          This delightful fruit jam is great for beginners because it only makes two half-pint jars. You can make it again and again, experimenting with flavors to find your perfect spoonful. Recipe via
          <a href="http://ladolcivita.blogspot.com/2012/08/strawberry-peach-jam.html" target="_blank">
           The Busty Baker
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h4>
          23. Strawberry Popsicles
         </h4>
         <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2219" style="width: 810px">
          <img alt="Strawberry Popsicles " class="size-full wp-image-2219" height="1046" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strawberry-popsicles.jpg" width="800"/>
          <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
           Photo courtesy of: The Little Epicurean
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Join the fancy frozen pop trend by blending fresh fruit in unique flavor combinations. Or, keep it simple with a sweet strawberry puree and a squeeze of lemon juice. Either way, this is a summer staple. Recipe via
          <a href="http://www.thelittleepicurean.com/2013/05/strawberry-popsicles.html" target="_blank">
           The Little Epicurean
          </a>
          .
         </p>
