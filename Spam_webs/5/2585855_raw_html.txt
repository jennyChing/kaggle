        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-29T07:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 29, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://hellonatural.co/author/deborah-harju/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Deborah Harju
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://hellonatural.co/diy-anti-cellulite-oil-honey-cellulite-massage/#comments">
           1 Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Cellulite can affect each of us, but thats no excuse to throw our hands in the air with defeat. Eating healthy, staying hydrated and exercising are essential, but sometimes we need a little more hands on help. The battle with those dimples and lumps can be wagedwith this intensehoney massage technique and a simple to make anti-cellulite oil.
        </p>
        <h1>
         Honey Cellulite Massage
        </h1>
        <p>
         Honey is a wonderful elementthat helps eradicatecellular waste from our body. Applying honey directly to the skin absorbs internal toxins,increases circulation and delivers a healing dose of antioxidants to repair skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellonatural.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Honey-cellulite-massage.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage" class="aligncenter wp-image-42238 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/hellonatural.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Honey-cellulite-massage.jpg?resize=650%2C910" title="DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellonatural.co/diy-anti-cellulite-oil-honey-cellulite-massage/&amp;media=http://hellonatural.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Honey-cellulite-massage.jpg&amp;description=DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage | http://hellonatural.co/diy-anti-cellulite-oil-honey-cellulite-massage/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Start with clean, dry hands and apply a thin layer of honey to the area of skin with cellulite, about 2 teaspoons. Drag your hand back and forth in each direction, lifting your hand off your skin with each pass. Each time it will be more and more difficult to lift your hand away from your body. You can increase pressure throughout the 5-10 minute massage, however beware that if you use intense pressure bruising might result.
        </p>
        <p>
         Expect some redness as youve just forced blood circulation to increase to this area. The blood brings nutrients and oxygen and takes away toxins from the tissues and muscles. Rinse with warm water and applythe following anti-cellulite oil. This massage can be done several times a week.
        </p>
        <h1>
         DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil
        </h1>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellonatural.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cellulite-Oil-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage" class="aligncenter wp-image-42237 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/hellonatural.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cellulite-Oil-21.jpg?resize=650%2C910" title="DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellonatural.co/diy-anti-cellulite-oil-honey-cellulite-massage/&amp;media=http://hellonatural.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cellulite-Oil-21.jpg&amp;description=DIY Anti-Cellulite Oil + Honey Cellulite Massage | http://hellonatural.co/diy-anti-cellulite-oil-honey-cellulite-massage/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Juniper oil is known to clear waste from the body and increase circulation while orange oil relieves fluid retention and swelling. Rosemary will help with edema and swelling as well.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Orange Rosemary Anti-Cellulite Oil
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1/2 cup jojoba oil
         </li>
         <li>
          15 drops orange essential oil
         </li>
         <li>
          15 drops rosemary essential oil
         </li>
         <li>
          15 drops juniper essential oil
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Combine the three essential oils with jojoba oil as your carrier oil. Apply to cellulite and gently massage into skin. Store oil in a dark, glass bottle to keepfresh andshake before each use.
        </p>
        <p>

          9 at-home remedies

         .
        </p>
