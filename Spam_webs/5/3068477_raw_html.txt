        <h3>
         What is Manchester Buddhist Centre?
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span class="larger">
          Manchester Buddhist Centre has taught Buddhism and meditation to thousands of people since1977
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Centre is bringing an ancient tradition alive in the modern world as part of the
         <a class="noiframe" href="http://www.manchesterbuddhistcentre.org.uk/general-info/about-triratna">
          Triratna Buddhist Community
         </a>
         (formerly Friends of the Western Buddhist Order).
        </p>
        <p>
         We are well known for our friendly and informal style of teaching, exploring what Buddhism can teach us in the modernwest.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <em>
           I saw people who exemplified Buddhist principles  not as atheory, but as something alive
          </em>
          <br/>
          Centre regular
         </p>
        </blockquote>
       </div>
