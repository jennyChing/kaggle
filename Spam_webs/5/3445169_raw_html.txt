    <p>
     In this modern world fireplace in the home isnt made to keep the home residents stay warm but only to add the aesthetic look for the home interior. Actually we dont need to ask a contractor to build a fireplace for our home since we can make our own DIY fireplace mantel.
    </p>
    <p>
     At first we need to prepare the backer board, dentil molding, prefabricated accents and paints. The second step is to attach the backer board against the wall and attach the dentil molding right on the bottom of the backer board.
    </p>
    <a class="lm" href="http://verycoolliving.com/simple-ways-to-make-diy-fireplace-mantel/nice-fireplace-mantel-decoration-ideas/" title="Nice Fireplace Mantel Decoration Ideas">
     <img alt="Nice Fireplace Mantel Decoration Ideas Decoration, Interior Simple Ways to Make DIY Fireplace Mantel" height="860" src="http://verycoolliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Nice-fireplace-mantel-decoration-ideas.jpg" width="647"/>
    </a>
    <p>
     The third step is to attach the decorative molding on the dentil molding surface tightly. The fourth step is to attach the prefabricated accents on the backer board to make it looks beautiful. Here you need to be extra careful and pay attention to the detail simply because the overall result of the project determined by this complicated step.
    </p>
    <a class="lm" href="http://verycoolliving.com/simple-ways-to-make-diy-fireplace-mantel/fireplace-mantels-vintage-design-ideas/" title="Fireplace Mantels Vintage Design Ideas">
     <img alt="Fireplace Mantels Vintage Design Ideas Decoration, Interior Simple Ways to Make DIY Fireplace Mantel" height="486" src="http://verycoolliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/fireplace-mantels-vintage-design-ideas.jpg" width="647"/>
    </a>

    <p>
     The last step is to paint the wood surface entirely using white or wooden colors. Choose the paint that is designed specifically for the respective material and has outstanding durability to avoid worn out after short time. Before making the DIY fireplace mantel youre advised to measure the space where youd like to set the fireplace at.
    </p>
