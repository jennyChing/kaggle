             <h1 class="main-title single-title entry-title">
              Rocksmith 2014 has a Bullet for your Valentine
             </h1>
            </div>
            <div class="authorship-row clearfix padded-panel">

              </div>
              <input data-essbishovercontainer="" data-essbispostcontainer="" data-essbisposttitle="Rocksmith 2014 has a Bullet for your Valentine" data-essbisposturl="http://www.thexboxhub.com/rocksmith-2014-has-a-bullet-for-your-valentine/" type="hidden" value=""/>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.thexboxhub.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/bullet-for-my-valentine-header.jpg">
                <img alt="bullet for my valentine header" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11175" height="360" src="http://www.thexboxhub.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/bullet-for-my-valentine-header-553x360.jpg" width="553"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               This weeks Rocksmith 2014 Edition DLC is out now.fancy learning a bit of Bullet For My Valentine?
              </p>
              <p>
               As always, youll find a number of tracks now available to learn, play and just downright enjoy, all of which can be purchased individually or indeed as part of a song pack.
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Hearts Burst Into Fire
               </li>
               <li>
                Your Betrayal
               </li>
               <li>
                Hand Of Blood
               </li>
               <li>
                Tears Dont Fall
               </li>
               <li>
                Scream Aim Fire
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               The above can be picked up for 2.39. The
               <a href="http://marketplace.xbox.com/en-GB/Product/Bullet-For-My-Valentine-Song-Pack/f771b6d9-f810-4895-86ad-53168c5ed9f8?cid=search" target="_blank">
                full song pack
               </a>
               (includes all the songs) can be picked up for 9.59.
              </p>
              <p>
               Rocksmith 2014 Edition is out now on Xbox One and Xbox 360. Make your way to the
               <a href="http://marketplace.xbox.com/en-GB/Search?query=rocksmith%202014&amp;SortBy=ReleaseDate" target="_blank">
                Xbox Games Store
               </a>
               or download the tracks via the game.
              </p>
