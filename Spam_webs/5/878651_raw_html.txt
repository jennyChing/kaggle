    <h1>
     New Releases for The Week of Dancing with Spiders
    </h1>
    <div class="articlemeta clearfixright">
     <a class="PhotoWrap" href="/profile/361/CB" title="CB">
      <img alt="CB" class="ProfilePhoto ProfilePhotoMedium" src="http://icrontic.com/wordpress/uploads/userpics/011/n6E8IKFHBGF4F.png"/>
     </a>
     <span class="author">
      <a href="http://icrontic.com/profile/361/CB">
       CB Droege
      </a>
     </span>
     <span class="date">
      24 Oct, 2011 at 1:54pm ET
     </span>
     <span class="category">
      Article published in
      <span class="unmeta">
       <a href="/categories/gaming-articles">
        Gaming
       </a>
      </span>
     </span>
    </div>
    <div class="content">
     <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_15895" style="width: 640px">
      <img alt="Dance Central 2 screenshot" class="size-large wp-image-15895 " height="354" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/20111024-DC2screen-630x354.jpg" title="Dance Central 2 screenshot" width="630"/>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Dance Central 2
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-15900" height="127" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/xbox-kinect1.png" title="xbox kinect" width="190"/>
      This is a big week for releases, as we stert to get into the early parts of the holiday season. First up, lets look at
      <strong>
       Dance Central 2
      </strong>
      .
      <em>
       Dance Central
      </em>
      has become a very popular game among Kinect owners, bringing in audiences who would never have played dance games before. Why? Because its the first dance game that cannot be gamed. The Kinect makes ittrulyaccurate, and the feedback is strong. Its not just about moving the motion-sensitivecontrollersto acertainangle, which is easy to do without actually dancingthis game needs the player to actually get the moves right across their entire body to be successful. The only real drawback to the game was that it was solo onlydespite the adverts which showed groups of four dancers playing along, there was no multi-player mode to get people going at a party. Enter Dance Central 2. The new game has more tracks and more dancers and what-not, of course, but the big thing is that it allows two dancers to play side-by-side in either competitive or co-operative dance modes. Now, when yoursignificantother wants to play
      <em>
       Dance Central
      </em>
      with you, there is no excuse not to.
      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0050SYYEK/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=icrontic-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373&amp;creativeASIN=B0050SYYEK">
       Dance Central 2
      </a>
      <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=icrontic-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B0050SYYEK&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
      is $49.99 on Amazon
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_15897" style="width: 640px">
      <img alt="The Haunted: Hell's Reach screenshot" class="size-full wp-image-15897 " height="310" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/20111024-THHRscreen.png" title="The Haunted: Hell's Reach screenshot" width="630"/>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       The Haunted: Hell's Reach
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a href="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/windows-steam-xbox-ps3.png">
        <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-15909" height="127" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/windows-steam-xbox-ps3.png" title="windows steam xbox ps3" width="190"/>
       </a>
       The Haunted: Hells Reach
      </strong>
      is a third-person over-the-shoulder adventure game with a demonic theme. The gameplay is similar to
      <em>
       Left 4 Dead
      </em>
      , with demons instead of zombies to fight. Players can get through alone, but the game seems intended for four-player co-operative play. There are more than 20 demon types to fight, and lots of weapons which can be upgraded for even more variety. No info about weather the A.I. of the enemies is similar to
      <em>
       L4Ds
      </em>
      Director A.I., but it does have a versus mode in which another four players take on the role of the demons, attempting to defeat the players. Anadditionaldeath-matchstyle mode is called Demonizer. In this mode a group of players compete to be the last alive, while dead human players are turned into repeatedly respawning demons. The Haunted: Hells Reach is available for
      <a href="http://store.steampowered.com/app/43190/" target="_blank" title="The Haunted: Hell's Reach on Steam">
       $17.99 on Steam
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005NH862Y/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=icrontic-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373&amp;creativeASIN=B005NH862Y">
       $19.99 on Amazon
      </a>
      <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=icrontic-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B005NH862Y&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
      , and is coming to Xbox Live as well as the PlayStation Network.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_15896" style="width: 640px">
      <img alt="Kirby's Return to Dreamland screenshot" class="size-full wp-image-15896 " height="357" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/20111024-KRDscreen.png" title="Kirby's Return to Dreamland screenshot" width="630"/>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Kirby's Return to Dreamland
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-15899" height="127" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/wii.png" title="wii" width="190"/>
      After Kirbys successful comeback in
      <em>
       Kirbys Epic Yarn
      </em>
      last year, it was only a matter of time before we saw another new Kirby, and here he is in
      <strong>
       Kirbys Return to Dreamland
      </strong>
      . As the name implies, this game is a return to a more classic Kirby with his more traditional suck-attack and power absorbtion abilities. Of course with a whole new set of enemies, his potential powers are widely varying. An alien ship has commanderkeened into Dreamland, and Kirby and his friends Meta Knight, King Dedede, and Waddle Dee, set out to help him get his ship back together. The graphics are similar to
      <em>
       New Super Mario Wii
      </em>
      , and so is another aspect: The multiplayer, which is drop-in drop-out for up to four players. Interestingly, each player is not required to pick a differnt character, as one might expect. If all four players want to be Kirby, so be it. Thats a lot of Kirby, though, so Id be careful with that.
      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0050SVGW8/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=icrontic-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373&amp;creativeASIN=B0050SVGW8">
       Kirbys Return to Dream Land
      </a>
      <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=icrontic-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B0050SVGW8&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
      is available on Amazon for $49.99.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_15894" style="width: 640px">
      <img alt="Centipede: Infestation screenshot" class="size-large wp-image-15894 " height="448" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/20111024-CIscreen-630x448.jpg" title="Centipede: Infestation screenshot" width="630"/>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       Centipede: Infestation
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-15902" height="127" src="http://gaming.icrontic.com/files/2011/10/wii-3ds.png" title="wii 3ds" width="190"/>
      The Icrontic Spotlight this week weaves back and forth through the grass to reach
      <strong>
       Centipede: Infestation
      </strong>
      . You think you know what its like to wait a long time for a sequel? This one is for the ages. The story is the same, as far as I can tellyoure either a tiny dude who has to defend a garden from insects or a normal dude who has to defend a giant garden from giant insects. Either way, theres this centipede guy and his spider friends, and they want to eat you. This time theyve brought some other bugs along also. The gameplay is essentially a 3D co-op tower defender, a
      <a href="http://gaming.icrontic.com/reviews/dungeon-defenders-creates-a-whole-new-genre-of-multiplayer-gaming/" target="_blank" title="Dungeon Defenders review on Icrontic">
       gameplay genre which is quickly becoming very popular
      </a>
      . In this game there are 35 levels over 7 environment types, and a variety of weapons, towers, and bugs to play with. The co-op mode is for two players only, and works split-screen on the Wii or over the 3DS wi-fi connection with one cartridge. Centipede: Infestation is
