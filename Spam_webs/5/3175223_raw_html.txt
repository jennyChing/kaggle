          California Measles Outbreak Likely Imported to United States
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <span class="entry-meta-content">
           <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
            <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.thegatewaypundit.com/author/jim-hoft/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
             <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
              Jim Hoft
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-meta-content">
           Jan 25th, 2015 9:13 am
          </span>
          <span class="entry-meta-content">
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.thegatewaypundit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/immigrants-warehouse.jpg">
           <img alt="immigrants warehouse" class="aligncenter wp-image-283025" height="316" src="http://www.thegatewaypundit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/immigrants-warehouse.jpg" width="421"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          Illegal alien children wait in detention at a Texas facility before being shipped across the US. (
          <a href="http://www.breitbart.com/texas/2014/06/05/leaked-images-reveal-children-warehoused-in-crowded-us-cells-border-patrol-overwhelmed/">
           Breitbart Texas
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          With the Disneyland measles outbreak expected to grow, California hospitals and doctors offices are preparing for new cases and a flood of questions from patients worried about getting the disease. So far
          <a href="http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/Bay-Area-hospitals-doctors-prepare-for-the-6036168.php">
           68 cases of measles
          </a>
          have been reported in California this year, the bulk of them in people who visited  or had contact with someone who visited 
          <a href="http://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/disney-measles-outbreak-could-get-worse-experts-warn-n291426">
           two Disney theme parks
          </a>
          in Anaheim. The outbreak could get much worse.
         </p>
         <p>
          In 2011, there were
          <a href="http://www.fortherecordmag.com/archives/071612p22.shtml">
           222 confirmed Measles cases
          </a>
          in the United States,
          <strong>
           200 were linked with importations,
          </strong>
          including 72 that were linked with importations from other countries.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           So is this latest outbreak linked to the influx of illegal immigrant children welcomed to America by the Obama administration?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Dr. Elizabeth Lee Vliet
          </strong>
          warned about this possibility at
          <a href="http://www.themonitor.com/opinion/commentary-deadline-diseases-crossing-border-with-unaccompanied-minors/article_86f79a7c-f80a-11e3-b5ff-0017a43b2370.html">
           The Monitor
          </a>
          back in June 2014:
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           A flood of illegals has massively surged at our Southwestern borders. The economic impact of medical care, education and incarceration for illegals forced on taxpayers is bankrupting Arizona.
          </p>
          <p>
           Why are such swarms now entering the United States illegally, particularly children? Newspapers in Mexico and Central and South America are actually describing U.S. open borders, encouraging people to come with promises of food stamps or amnesty. It is textbook Cloward-Piven strategy to overwhelm and collapse the economic and social systems, in order to replace them with a new socialist order under federal control.
          </p>
          <p>
           Carried by this tsunami of illegals are the invisible travelers our politicians dont like to mention: diseases the United States had controlled or virtually eradicated: tuberculosis (TB), Chagas disease, dengue fever, hepatitis, malaria and measles. I have been working on medical projects in Central and South America since 2009, so I am aware of problems these countries face from such diseases
          </p>
          <p>
           Vaccine-preventable diseases like chicken pox, measles and whooping cough spread like wildfire among unvaccinated children. Other illnesses, along with scabies and head lice, also thrive as children are transported by bus and herded into crowded shelters  courtesy of the federal government. And treatment costs are borne by taxpayers.
          </p>
          <p>
           There are many unanswered questions about this invasion of illegals with unaccompanied children:
          </p>
          <p>
            Who are paying for so many impoverished people and young children to get to the U.S. border from Central and South America?
           <br/>
            Why is our government preventing Border Patrol agents from speaking out?
           <br/>
            Why is our government not enforcing our immigration laws as other countries do?
           <br/>
            Why are federal officials transporting illegal immigrants from Texas to Arizona?
          </p>
          <p>
           Illegal immigration is not a victimless crime. All taxpayers and all patients are its victims.
           <strong>
            Diseases dont stop at borders. Diseases dont discriminate based on political affiliation.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Today it is Arizona and Texas. Tomorrow it will be your state,
           </strong>
           your medical care, your health, your life and your children. Demand Congress stop this flagrant lawlessness at our borders.
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          The Canadian government blames the current US outbreak on
          <a href="http://travel.gc.ca/travelling/health-safety/travel-health-notices/measles">
           imported cases.
          </a>
         </p>
