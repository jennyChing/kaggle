               Refreshing Homemade Peach and Strawberry Ice Cream

              <p>
               Ive stumbled upon an interesting homemade ice cream recipe. And just as I never miss a chance to try every recipe that consists of fresh fruits or vegetables, Im eager to try this one too.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.healthylifeworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com_.jpg" rel="gallery_group">
                <img alt="peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-320" height="705" src="http://www.healthylifeworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com_.jpg" width="595"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               The
               <strong>
                ingredients
               </strong>
               for this yummy ice cream are:
              </p>
              <p>
               150 g fresh strawberries
              </p>
              <p>
               2 ripe peaches
              </p>
              <p>
               10 large mint leaves
              </p>
              <p>
               3 tablespoons of sorbet
              </p>
              <p>
               360 ml cold water
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               These ingredientsshould beenough to make 8 ice creams.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.healthylifeworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/refreshing-peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com_.jpg" rel="gallery_group">
                <img alt="refreshing-peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-321" height="447" src="http://www.healthylifeworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/refreshing-peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com_.jpg" width="595"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                Preparation:
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               Place the strawberries in the blender along with 1 tablespoon of sorbet and 120 ml of water.
              </p>
              <p>
               Blend it until the ingredients are well mixed.
              </p>
              <p>
               Take 8 ice cream moulds (you can use plastic cups with suitable form) and fill 1/3 of them with the strawberries mixture.
              </p>
              <p>
               Place an
               <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=ice%20cream%20sticks&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;sprefix=ice%20cream%20st%2Caps%2C256&amp;tag=item_ref04-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps&amp;linkId=KA7OT4WDGEAKCBZ2" target="_blank">
                ice cream wooden stick
               </a>
               in eachmould and leave them in the freezer for 10-15 minutes, until the mixture starts to solidify.
              </p>
              <p>
               Cut the peaches in half, remove the seeds and cut them into smaller pieces.
              </p>
              <p>
               Put the peaches in the blender, along with the mint, 2 tablespoons of sorbet and the remaining water.
              </p>
              <p>
               Blend it until the mixture becomes homogeneous.
              </p>
              <p>
               Add the mixture into the moulds and freeze it all along.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                Tip:
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               To make the sorbet, combine an equal amount of water and sugar and warm it over low heat until the sugaris melted.
              </p>
              <p>
               Leave it until its cooled down.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.healthylifeworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/tasty-peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com_.jpg" rel="gallery_group">
                <img alt="tasty-peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-322" height="324" src="http://www.healthylifeworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/tasty-peach-and-strawberry-ice-cream-www.healthylifeworld.com_.jpg" width="595"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Call your friends to taste this refreshing homemade ice cream. Im sure they will love it.
              </p>
