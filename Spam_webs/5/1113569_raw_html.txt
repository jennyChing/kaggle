      <p>
       Writer and director James Gunn had the ease of mind knowing that hed have a chance to make
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2/">
        <strong>
         <em>
          Guardians of the Galaxy 2
         </em>
        </strong>
       </a>
       for Marvel Studios before the first even hit theaters. He was able to announce it with star Chris Pratt at Comic-Con International last summer and actually
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-galaxy-2-story-james-gunn/">
        began writing the sequel
       </a>
       the day the first came out.
      </p>
      <p>
       As one of the more open and interactive filmmakers in the comic book movie business, Gunn isnt shy about teasing or confirming certain details about his workwith fans on social media or with the pressin interviews.Even though
       <em>
        GOTG 2
       </em>
       wont be in theaters until 2017, weve already learned quite a few details, especially when it comes to characters.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-540234">
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="advert zergnet">
       <span>
        From The Web
       </span>
       <div id="zergnet-unit-in-article">
       </div>
       <script>
        /*&lt;![CDATA[*/document.write('\

                        znscr.parentNode.insertBefore(zergnet, znscr);\
                    })();\
                &lt;\/script&gt;\
            ');/*]]&gt;*/
       </script>
      </div>
      <p>
       We know, for instance, that the second
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy
       </em>
       will see the entire core cast of five heroes return alongside
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-returning-characters/">
        Nebula (Karen Gillan), The Collector
       </a>
       (Benicio Del Toro),
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-blu-ray-michael-rooker-interview/">
        Yondu (Michael Rooker)
       </a>
       and hisright-hand man, Kraglin (Sean Gunn). We can expect Thanos (Josh Brolin) to make an appearance as well alongside a few
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-returning-characters/">
        other familiar faces
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       We also know that
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-galaxy-2-ronan-nebula-groot-son/">
        Ronan the Accuser
       </a>
       (Lee Pace) who seemingly died at the end of Gunns first Marvel film may not be dead after all, but he definitely wont be in the sequel. As for
       <em>
        new
       </em>
       characters, thats where things get interesting.The biggest we know about so far is Peter Quill a.k.a.
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-star-lord-dad-mystery/">
        Star-Lords dad
       </a>
        a character who Gunn confirmed will be different than in Marvel Comics but who will be very important to Quills story going forward.
      </p>
      <h2>
       New Characters Are On The Way
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-2-Characters-From-Marvel-Comics.jpg">
        <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Characters From Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-2-Characters-From-Marvel-Comics.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h196-q90-c380:196/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h294-q90-c570:294/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 51.6129032258%;">
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Characters From Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/wp-content/uploads/Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-2-Characters-From-Marvel-Comics.jpg" title="Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Characters From Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character"/>
         </noscript>
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       We also know from what Gunn told us while promoting
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy
       </em>
       that the main team will include at least two female characters this time around. We can assume both Star-Lords dad and thisnew female member of Star-Lords crew are characters from Marvel Comics, even if theyre different, and its possible one of them might be James Gunns personal favorite character  That or this tweet from the weekend where James Gunn held a Twitter Q&amp;A is hinting at
       <em>
        another
       </em>
       character about to be introduced into the Marvel Cinematic Universe:
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <p>
       From Gunns words, it sounds like whoever this character is, their first introduction will be in
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy 2
       </em>
       . As for the
       <em>
        who
       </em>
       of it, looking back on what Gunn has said about projects hed like to make for Marvel we can make a few guesses.
      </p>
      <p>
       We know for instance the project he initially wanted to make for Marvel was
       <em>
        Hit Monkey
       </em>
       . He spoke about that at length when
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-set-interview-james-gunn/">
        chatting with us on the set of
        <em>
         Guardians of the Galaxy
        </em>
       </a>
       in 2012 (he actually wasnt interested in
       <em>
        Guardians
       </em>
       at first). The other Marvel fave for Gunn is the villain team-up series
       <em>
        Thunderbolts
       </em>
       , a project which Marvel Studios boss Kevin Feigeteased that Gunn could make if hes successful with
       <em>
        GOTG
       </em>
       .
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-james-gunn-post-credits/">
        We asked Gunn about
        <em>
         Thunderbolts
        </em>
        too
       </a>
       but he said hes now interested in sticking with the
       <em>
        Guardians
       </em>
       for a while.
      </p>
      <h2>
       ROM: Spaceknight
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/Rom-Spaceknight-by-artnomad.jpg">
        <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Rom Spaceknight by artnomad 570x878 Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Rom-Spaceknight-by-artnomad-570x878.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h585-q90-c380:585/,481:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/,768:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/,1024:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/,481:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/,768:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/,1024:slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 154.035087719%;">
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Rom Spaceknight by artnomad 570x878 Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w570-h878-q90-c570:878/wp-content/uploads/Rom-Spaceknight-by-artnomad-570x878.jpg" title="Rom Spaceknight by artnomad 570x878 Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character"/>
         </noscript>
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Another Marvel Comic James Gunn frequently namedrops is
       <em>
        ROM: Spaceknight
       </em>
       , and this ones quite unique. ROM wasnt created by Marvel Comics and instead was licensedfromParker Brothers who had acquired the character from its original creators. Marvel ran a ROM: Spaceknight series for 75 issuesover an eight-year span until the rights reverted back in 1986.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <blockquote align="center" class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
       <p>
        
        <a href="https://twitter.com/TweetTinyTim">
         @TweetTinyTim
        </a>
        : which Marvel character do you wish you could reclaim the rights to so they could appear in your films? ROM Spaceknight!
       </p>
        James Gunn (@JamesGunn)
       <a href="https://twitter.com/JamesGunn/status/571824244012654592">
        March 1, 2015
       </a>
      </blockquote>
      <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
      </script>
      <p>
       Since then, Marvel Comics hasnt been able to use the character but had built so much lore around him that they still can use. Feige spoke briefly about ROM
       <em>
        to
       </em>
       <em>
        ScreenCrush
       </em>
       last year following a question about thepotential of using the character in Marvel movies.
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        I think we have Spaceknights, but we dont have Rom. I think they did some standalone Spaceknights comics at some point. ROM, I believe, was a licensed deal. Like G.I. Joe and Transformers back in the day.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       Thats important to remember because the Galadorian Spaceknights (cyborgs created on Galador to protect the planet from their archenemies, the Dire Wraiths) had a major role to play in the more recent Marvel cosmic stories,specifically during the Dan Abnett and Andy Lanning years where they re-introduced the Guardians of the Galaxy (for which the moviewas based on). Their run actually ended with Star-Lord helping start a new team called of cosmic heroes called the Annihilators who would replace the Guardians of the Galaxy after their final, devastating mission to save the universe. There was a Spaceknight on that team named Ikon and their first mission was to save Galador.
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        I almost made it work. The Guardians were more than I could ever have hoped for! But the universe is also more dangerous than I ever imagined! Look around! This is the kind of thing that can happen!
       </p>
       <p>
        The galaxy doesnt need Guardians! It doesnt need brave souls! If its going to survive another one of these, its going to need absolute bad-asses! Its going to need the biggest guns of all! Cosmic heavy hitters as scary as the menaces they have to face!
       </p>
       <p>
        Its going to need flarking annihilators! Peter Quill
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       ROM however, despite rights issues, did make a cameo appearance in 2013s Age of Ultron crossover event in Marvel Comics and the year prior, Hasbro licensed the rights to sell new ROM toys. Whether or not Marvel Studios has since acquired the film rights or made a deal to use ROM in the movies, it wont be publicly announced if said character actually
       <em>
        will
       </em>
       show up in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, but knowing Marvelat least owns the Spaceknights and their homeworld and enemies, etc., makes it possible that they could show up  or at least be referenced  in
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/james-gunn-guardians-of-the-galaxy-franchise-spinoffs/">
        <em>
         Guardians of the Galaxy
        </em>
        sequels/spinoffs
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Sadly, were not counting on ROM the Spaceknight making an appearance though.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Who Its NOT Going To Be
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Nova-Richard-Rider.jpg">
        <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Nova Richard Rider Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Nova-Richard-Rider.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h196-q90-c380:196/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h294-q90-c570:294/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 51.6129032258%;">
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Nova Richard Rider Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Nova-Richard-Rider.jpg" title="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Nova Richard Rider Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character"/>
         </noscript>
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Before we start listing somepossibilities based on pure speculation, we can at least remove a few possibilities from what Gunn has said in the past.
      </p>
      <p>
       Carol Danvers,previously known as Ms. Marvel and who now carries the
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-captain-marvel/">
        Captain Marvel
       </a>
       title, is getting her own solo movie on Phase 3 of the Marvel Cinematic Universe but she will not appear in
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy 2
       </em>
       . Gunn made it clear that he doesnt wantotherEarth characters to take away from that unique aspect of Peter Quill.
      </p>
      <p>
       Another character Gunn spoke with us about in the past is Bug, a member of the 2008-2010
       <em>
        GOTG
       </em>
       team from the comics, but like Rom, hes alsonot owned by Marvel. Gunn told us he wanted to use Bug in the first movie but simplycould not.
      </p>
      <p>
       Nova  Similar to Danvers, Nova (both the original Richard Rider version and the new Sam Alexander version) is also Earth-based. Given how Nova Corps was introduced and handled in the
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy
       </em>
       movie, were not even sure the comic book version of this organization (where Nova Centurions can fly and have super powers) will be adapted to the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-no-nova/">
        James Gunn also flat out said that doesnt want to use Novaright now.
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Who It Could Be
      </h2>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_479774" style="width: 630px">
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Adam-Warlock.jpg">
        <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Adam Warlock Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Adam-Warlock.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h196-q90-c380:196/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h294-q90-c570:294/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 51.6129032258%;">
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Adam Warlock Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Adam-Warlock.jpg" title="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Adam Warlock Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character"/>
         </noscript>
        </span>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Adam Warlock
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       We dont know if the character Gunn is referring to is a member of the team in the comicsbut our first guess is to go through our list of
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-characters/">
        12 characters we want to see in
        <em>
         Guardians of the Galaxy
        </em>
        sequels
       </a>
       and eliminate the ones we know it cannot be. Our best remaining guesses for who could realistically show up in
       <em>
        GOTG 2
       </em>
       andcould qualify as a fave of Gunn include Adam Warlock, Mantis, Moondragon and Phyla-Vell.
      </p>
      <p>
       Warlock is a crucial character in stories involving big bad supervillain Thanos, and given that
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/thor-2-dark-world-easter-eggs-trivia-comic-references/">
        Warlockscocoon
       </a>
       was featured in the first movie, it would be strange for him
       <em>
        not
       </em>
       to appear in the movies.These movies are the least faithful to the Marvel Comics and are going to deviate even further going forward  to the point of
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-original-story/">
        Marvel claiming its risky
       </a>
       (not from a financial standpoint, but from a story standpoint for fans).
      </p>
      <p>
       As for the other three, were expecting at least one ofMantis, Moondragon and Phyla-Vell to show up in the sequel as a new member of the team. All three are crucial characters to the 2008-2010 run of
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy
       </em>
       comics and all had important roles to play in the bigger picture story. Moondragon for instance, is the daughter of Drax who is presumed dead.
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_479775" style="width: 630px">
       <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Phyla Vell Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Phyla-Vell.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h196-q90-c380:196/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h294-q90-c570:294/,481:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,768:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/,1024:slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 51.6129032258%;">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Phyla Vell Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w620-h320-q90-c620:320/wp-content/uploads/Marvel-Comics-Guardians-of-the-Galaxy-Character-Phyla-Vell.jpg" title="Marvel Comics Guardians of the Galaxy Character Phyla Vell Guardians of the Galaxy 2 Will Introduce James Gunns Favorite Character"/>
        </noscript>
       </span>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Phyla-Vell as Quasar
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       A more obscure choice that comes to mind as well isGenis-Vell, brother of Phyla-Vell. Genis wasnt a member of the Guardians like her sister was, but like his sister (and eventually, Carol Danvers), he did once occupy the position ofCaptain Marvel and we know that this character title is in play in the MCU. Why we say Genis over the original Captain Marvel (Mar-Vell)is because Genis-Vell was actually once a member of the
       <em>
        Thunderbolts
       </em>
       and we already established that Gunn loves that property.
      </p>
      <p>
       Perhaps that last one is a stretch, but Marvel Studios, in all their long-form storytelling plans, needs to start planting seeds for Captain Marvel to make sense, and right now,
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy
       </em>
       is the only full-on space-based property running that can do that effectively.
      </p>
      <p>
       It could also be one of the original
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy
       </em>
       from the 60s comics who hail from the distant future
      </p>
      <p>
       And who knows, maybe this character  whoever it ends up being  could be played by
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/marvel-studios-al-pacino-cast/">
        Al Pacino, a self-admitted fan of
        <em>
         Guardians of the Galaxy
        </em>
       </a>
       who actually met with Marvel to talk about a potential role. Thistweet isnt a coincidence:
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <blockquote align="center" class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
       <p>
        
        <a href="https://twitter.com/adelutzap1982">
         @adelutzap1982
        </a>
        : Which actor would you like to work with? Al Pacino.
       </p>
        James Gunn (@JamesGunn)
       <a href="https://twitter.com/JamesGunn/status/571822894239465473">
        March 1, 2015
       </a>
      </blockquote>
      <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
      </script>
      <p>
       What character(s) should be introduced in
       <em>
        Guardians of the Galaxy 2
       </em>
       and which character do is James Gunn teasing as one of his favorites that hes going to introduce? Share your thoughts in the comments!
      </p>
