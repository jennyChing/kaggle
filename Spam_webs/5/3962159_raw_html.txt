If you've been following us on social media then youll probably recognize 
this office. If you havent been following us stop what youre doing right 
now and show us some love! Like many of our followers you may be wondering 
how to achieve a similar look in your own office. Well today well break 
down the decor behind the photo and share a few tips so you can replicate 
this look.

Click to enlarge

 Color

Lets start with the color combination. To duplicate the color you see on 
the walls Behr Paint offers a pretty good copycat called Windrush. If you 
cant seem to get your hands on a bucket of Behr Paint be sure to look for 
a gray with brown undertones. We suggest you use Behrs Heather Gray with a 
semi- gloss finish to paint the walls of the built in bookshelf. As for the 
molding surrounding the bookshelf continue to use the Windrush Gray. If you 
want a distressed finish on the bookshelf molding you can blot the wood 
with a metallic brownpaint. The designers of this office chose gold, 
powder blue, and pinks with gold highlights to add a few pops of color 
throughout this very gray room.

LIGHTING

The designers of this room decided to add an ornate Aidan Gray Italian 
Wedding Chandelier. The officeappears to havecan lights that provide a 
lot more lighting. Howeverthe chandelier is definitely the focal point of 
the room and a must have to complete this look. While the original piece is 
very beautiful it is also very pricey. So if youre looking to recreate the 
look for less we found a great dupe for 1/3 of the costhere.

  DESK

Now for the most important part of any office, the desk.The designers 
chose to use a French inspired writing desk with a dark stain. We searched 
for a while to find the desk used in the inspiration photobut we could not 
find it anywhere. Howeverwe were able to find a few look a likes that will 
give the same look.Hooker FurnitureandEthan Allenprovide our favorite 
look alike desks.

 CHAIRs

Overstock.com provided a nice andcomfy chairthat embodies the French 
style in the inspiration photo and the right amount of comfort and lumbar 
support youll need while youre sitting at your desk. Any visitors that 
stop by your office will be happy to take a seat in the beautiful powder 
blue chateau arm chairfrom Wisteria.









Floors

Rugs USAs Edison Rug is almost an exact replica of the rug found in the 
inspiration photo. Be sure to purchase the white rug with the gray lines 
and not the other way around.

 Accessories

The designers put the finishing touches on the room by stocking the shelves 
of the book case with leather bound books, gold vases and ornaments, as 
well as a white orchid on the edge of the desk. It appears that some of the 
books on the shelf have been spray painted a pinkish-gold. As for the 
curtains they were custom made by Cote de Texas. However if you are not 
looking to spendmoney oncustom made window treatments you can follow one 
of our favorite cost cutting tips. Custom drapes are determined by the 
number of folds in the curtains, more folds means more fabric, and more 
fabric means more money. Pick up multiple packs of grayish brown curtains 
off the rack from somewhere like Target. Group the curtains on one end of 
the curtain rod to create the drama and high end look that you would get 
from custom made drapes." property="og:description"/>

                 <h2>
                  Color
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  Lets start with the color combination. To duplicate the color you see on the walls Behr Paint offers a pretty good copycat called Windrush. If you cant seem to get your hands on a bucket of Behr Paint be sure to look for a gray with brown undertones. We suggest you use Behrs Heather Gray with a semi- gloss finish to paint the walls of the built in bookshelf. As for the molding surrounding the bookshelf continue to use the Windrush Gray. If you want a distressed finish on the bookshelf molding you can blot the wood with a metallic brownpaint. The designers of this office chose gold, powder blue, and pinks with gold highlights to add a few pops of color throughout this very gray room.

                 <h2>
                  Accessories
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  The designers put the finishing touches on the room by stocking the shelves of the book case with leather bound books, gold vases and ornaments, as well as a white orchid on the edge of the desk. It appears that some of the books on the shelf have been spray painted a pinkish-gold. As for the curtains they were custom made by Cote de Texas. However if you are not looking to spendmoney oncustom made window treatments you can follow one of our favorite cost cutting tips. Custom drapes are determined by the number of folds in the curtains, more folds means more fabric, and more fabric means more money. Pick up multiple packs of grayish brown curtains off the rack from somewhere like Target. Group the curtains on one end of the curtain rod to create the drama and high end look that you would get from custom made drapes.
