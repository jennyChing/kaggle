          <p>
           The beauty of movies is that they take us to a different world without us doing anything more than buying a ticket, a bucket of popcorn and settle ourselves in comfy chairs in huge air-conditioned halls. We do not sometimes realise that those who make movies, and those who are involved in it, often have to go through gruelling sessions of takes and retakes. Dont they, therefore, deserve a quick rest, a distressing chat or a beer? We think that the man playing the baddie is a baddie in real life, too. Isnt he is supposed to beat the hell out of our hero? The world of movies is full of mysteries which can only be revealed by pictures from behind the scenes and screens.
