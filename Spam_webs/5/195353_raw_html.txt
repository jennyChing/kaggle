          <div style="text-align: justify;">

           </a>
           l, a company, a manufacturer and leading distributor of tires in Southeast Asia. Gajah Tunggal tires that offer high-quality production for vehicles like cars and motorcycles, as well as for the inner tubes, and also industrial. Other than that, Gajah Tunggal is also a producer and distributor of rubber related products include tire thread, inner tube, flap, o-rings, synthetic rubber, and many more. Gajah Tunggal was established in 1951, when the founding of the Gajah Tunggal begin begin by producing bicycle tires. Then since the founding, the Gajah Tunggal here grew rapidly to become a leading tire manufacturer terintregitas in Southeast Asia.
