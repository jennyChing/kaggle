       <h2>
        Omnik sparked on Intersolar 2014 in Munich
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span class="date">
         Date: 06/06
        </span>
        <span class="viewed">
         Viewed (
         <b>
          1240
         </b>
         )
        </span>
        <span class="label">
         Label:
         <a href="http://www.omnik-solar.com/news/tag/solar_inverter">
          solar inverter
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.omnik-solar.com/news/tag/Intersolar">
          Intersolar
         </a>
        </span>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="content">
       <p align="left" style="text-align:left;">
        <span>
         <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
          Omnik New Energy
         </span>
        </span>
        <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"'>
         <span style="font-family:Tahoma;font-size:12px;">
          brought its new inverters to Intersolar in Munich,
Germany from June 4
          <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"'>
           <span style="font-family:Tahoma;font-size:12px;">
            <sup>
             th
            </sup>
           </span>
          </span>
          to 6
         </span>
         <span style="font-family:Tahoma;font-size:12px;">
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          . There are1141 companies from 48
countries and places attending this year. Omnik products won great popularity
in this expo.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p align="left" style="text-align:left;">
        <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"'>
         <span style="font-family:Tahoma;font-size:12px;">
          <img alt="" src="/uploads/image/1836936411.jpg"/>
          <br/>
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p align="left" style="text-align:left;">
        <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
         Intersolar is known as the largest and the most important professional
solar exposition, gathering all the famous companies in solar industry around
the world. Since its establishment in 2011, this is the 4th time that Omnik
attended such event. Each year, the new
        </span>
        <span>
         <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
          products
         </span>
        </span>
        <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
         published by Omnik always left deep impression on
busniess partners.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p align="left" style="text-align:left;">
        <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
         <img alt="" src="/uploads/image/1463816694.jpg"/>
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
         Omnik, the only Sine-German PV Company in China, has
established a worldwide market, accounting for 40% of the market in the Netherlands.
In this expo, Omnik exhibited its self-developed inverters, including Hybrid
Inverter, Single Phase Inverter, Three Phase Inverter and Micro Inverter.
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
         <img alt="" src="/uploads/image/21000053122.jpg"/>
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size:12px;font-family:Tahoma;">
         The brand new Hybrid Inverter became the highlight in
Intersolar. It has 98.3% conversion efficiency; combines charge controller,
storage inverter and solar inverter into one. It can automatically judge
on-grid/ off-grid mode, connect into smart grid and realize peak load shifting.
Omnik Micro Inverter also attracted great attention among European companies. The
soaring popularity in Intersolar fully verified Omniks great brand impact in
European market.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
