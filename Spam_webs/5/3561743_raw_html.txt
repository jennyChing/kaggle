       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Im so excited to be working with Microsoft again. Yes Im a Windows Champion and Ive been asked to help promote the Surface 2 coming out next week. Im going to be at the local Suface 2 release party at a local store and you can win a gift card. To pre-register for the event you can sign up on the
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/415238225242722/">
          Facebook Page
         </a>
         . I will be there because I am purchasing a Surface 2 pro for my husband.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          DETAILS:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         What: Microsoft Stores Surface Challenge Midnight Launch Event!
        </p>
        <p>
         Where: Bellevue Square Mall
        </p>
        <p>
         When: Monday, Oct. 21, 10 p.m.  12:30am; consumers can pick-up their Surface 2 or Surface Pro 2 at midnight
        </p>
        <p>
         Who: Anyone can join!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/image26.png">
          <img alt="image" border="0" height="259" src="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/image_thumb27.png" style="border-left-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom-width: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto 5px; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border-top-width: 0px" title="image" width="433"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Arrive at 10pm for the opportunity to go head-to-head in a series of fun challenges for a chance to win a trip to the Orlando store opening complete with a live performance by Pitbull. The challenge winner from each store will be whisked away that morning to meet Pitbull and enjoy VIP treatment at this special concert event! Win or lose, youll still get the exclusive opportunity to celebrate the launch of the brand new Surface 2 and Surface Pro 2 at the stroke of midnight and check out the devices before anyone else. So come on out and experience the most productive tablet on the planet!
        </p>
        <p>
         Enter to Win
         <br/>
         1 of 3 $50 Microsoft Store Gift Cards
        </p>
        <p>
         To Enter:
        </p>
        <p>
         You must be able to pick up your prize that night
        </p>
        <p>
         And leave a comment telling me what you would get at the Microsoft store with your $50.00 gift card.
        </p>
        <p>
         Extra entries:
        </p>
        <p>
