        Bruschetta Chicken
       </h1>

       <div class="pf-content">
        <div class="at-above-post addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Bruschetta Chicken" data-url="http://askgeorgie.com/bruschetta-chicken/">

        <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
        </div>
        <p>
         Tis the season for grilling! Bring on the sunshine, and family and friends, the long weekends, and the lean protein.
        </p>
        <p>
         How to make grilled chicken interesting without adding lots of work? Add a flavorful tomato-basil topping.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://askgeorgie.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Bruschetta-Chicken.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4446" height="487" src="http://askgeorgie.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Bruschetta-Chicken-1024x780.jpg" title="Bruschetta Chicken" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe is a fabulous way to use tomatoes and basil in the summertime, when theyre at the peak of flavor. Its also incredibly easy and pleases just about everyone. You can mix up the topping the day before and refrigerate until its time to cook the chicken. If you end up with too much tomato mixture, serve it on toasted bread or toss with cooked, chilled grains or whole wheat pasta for an easy cold salad.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 large tomato, chopped
         </li>
         <li>
          1 large garlic clove, crushed or minced
         </li>
         <li>
          2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
         </li>
         <li>
          1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
         </li>
         <li>
          1/8 teaspoon salt
         </li>
         <li>
          10 large basil leaves, chopped
         </li>
         <li>
          2 (5 ounce) chicken breasts
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ol>
         <li>
          In      a medium bowl, combine tomato, garlic, oil, vinegar, salt, and basil. Stir      and set aside. (Can be prepared up to 24 hours before serving time and      refrigerated.)
         </li>
         <li>
          Season      chicken lightly with salt and pepper and cook on indoor or outdoor grill      for 8-10 minutes or until cooked through. Place one breast on each plate      and divide tomato mixture on top.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         Serves 2
        </p>
        <p>
         Per serving: 223 calories, 6 g total fat, 1 g saturated fat, 4 g total carbohydrate, 1 g dietary fiber, 35 g protein
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          <strong>
           Enjoy!
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
