       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Bacon Shortbread Cookies {3 Ingredients!}
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-02-15T11:05:13+00:00">
         February 15, 2015
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/BaconShortbreadCookies.jpg">
          <img alt="BaconShortbreadCookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8244" height="965" src="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/BaconShortbreadCookies.jpg" width="643"/>
         </a>
         Im gonna keep this short and sweet, just like todays recipe.
        </p>
        <p>
         And
         <em>
          honnnney
         </em>
          todays recipe is the bees knees. Its
         <em>
          bacon-wrapped cookies, people.
         </em>
         And its pretty darn phenomenal if I do say so myself.
        </p>
        <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
         <center>
          <!-- /18190176/TheDomesticRebel_Mid_Post_1_300x250 -->

        <p>
         This recipe (which I struggle to deem a recipe) isnt really one at all. It was adapted from The Pioneer Womans famous
         <a href="http://thepioneerwoman.com/cooking/2007/12/flashback_1981_-_holiday_bacon_appetizers/" target="_blank">
          Bacon-Wrapped Crackers
         </a>
         which I have had and yes, they are as divine as they sound.
        </p>
        <p>
         Basically, the recipe consists of this: sprinkle club crackers with brown sugar. Wrap coated crackers in bacon. Bake low and slow for a couple hours. Consume gleefully. See? Easy.
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe is the same thing, minus the club crackers and adding shortbread cookies because everything is vastly improved when cookies are involved. These poppable, bite-sized treats are sweet, salty, crunchy and buttery and absolutely perfect for dessert, breakfast, snacks, gifts, or mid-day pick-me-ups on a Sunday. Or you know, perfect all the time.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_0191AA.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0191AA" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8247" height="965" src="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_0191AA.jpg" width="643"/>
         </a>
         So if youre craving something absolutely outrageous and smothered in bacon, this is your dessert.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now if you dont mind me, I have a batch of cookies I need to attend to.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_0188A.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0188A" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8246" height="957" src="http://thedomesticrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_0188A.jpg" width="638"/>
         </a>

        </p>

           Ingredients

           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">

           Instructions

          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 250 degrees F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil and place a metal cooling rack on top. Mist the cooling rack with cooking spray and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Working carefully, sprinkle about 1 teaspoon of brown sugar onto each shortbread cookie, and wrap the cookie with the bacon piece. Place the cookies onto the wire rack and repeat with remaining cookies.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake for approx. 60-90 minutes or until the cookies smell fragrant and the bacon appears done. Cookies will be a darker colored brown and that's okay as long as they don't appear burnt. Cookies also may appear to crack slightly; this is normal.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cool cookies completely before serving.
           </li>
          </ol>

As the bacon cooks, the grease soaks into the crisp shortbread cookies, absorbing all that lovely flavor. They are just so. stinkin. delicious and I cant recommend you making them enough!
