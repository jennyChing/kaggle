        <h1 class="title">
         The 12 Best Internet Memes and Viral Videos of 2010
        </h1>
        <div class="byline">

         <a class="ovr" href="/writers?name=lindsay+eanet">

       <div class="copy entry">
        <p>
         Within the past several years, weve seen a new cult of personality emerge: the internet celebrity. From the beloved (
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=charlie+bit+me&amp;aq=f">
          the Charlie bit me kids
         </a>
         ) to the notorious (
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=jessi+slaughter&amp;aq=f">
          Jessi Slaughter
         </a>
         ), no one is safe from the eyes (and tweets) of a totally wired and easily amused public. Two thousand ten was an especially banner year for memes, viral videos and lovable YouTube personalities who charmed and attention-sought their way onto the late-night talk show ciruit. Below, we salute our favourites:
        </p>
        <p>
         <big>
          12. I Hope This Gets to You/Viral Love Letter
         </big>
         <br/>
         Walter C. May and his band, The Daylights, made this video for Mays girlfriend, with the hopes that it would reach her virally, through an organic chain of tweeting and sharing and passing along. It did, and she loved it, and the world went Awwwwww.
        </p>
        <p>
         <object height="340" width="560">
          <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/EjgtxVxE14A?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US">
          </param>

          <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">

          <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="340" src="http://www.youtube.com/v/EjgtxVxE14A?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="560">
          </embed>
         </object>
         <big>
          11. Panda Cheese/Never Say No To Panda
         </big>
         <br/>
         Panda Cheese is an actual edible dairy product popular in Egypt and the Middle East. But after Arab Dairy, the company that makes Panda Cheese, launched this series of advertisements in which a friendly-looking panda wordlessly intimidates hospital patients, supermarket shoppers and others into buying the product, soon, the whole world would have a hankering for Panda Cheese.
        </p>
        <p>
         <object height="385" width="480">
          <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/v6iHCFiSqIw?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US">

          </embed>
         </object>
         <big>
          10. Inception Memes
         </big>
         <br/>
         Youre waiting for a train.
         <a href="http://www.geekosystem.com/inception-memes/">
          J/K ITS HERE
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <big>
          9. Prancing Cera
         </big>
         <br/>
         A simple photo of Michael Cera prancing during the filming of
         <em>
          Scott Pilgrim vs. The World
         </em>
         , taken by director Edgar Wright, turned into a mass flurry of Photoshopping and puns on the word prance, by the thousands. We liked it so much,
         <a href="http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2010/08/variations-on-a-meme-prancing-cera.html">
          we turned it into a gallery
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <big>
          8. Sad Keanu
         </big>
         <br/>
         Sad Keanu is sad. Wont someone cheer him up? Or at least Photoshop him into various situations which may point to or alleviate his sadz?
         <a href="http://sadkeanu.tumblr.com/">
          Thank you.
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <object height="385" width="480">
          <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/kcsemGUl0xM?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US">
          </param>
          <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
          </param>
          <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
          </param>
          <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="385" src="http://www.youtube.com/v/kcsemGUl0xM?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="480">
          </embed>
         </object>
         <big>
          7. Old Spice Guy
         </big>
         <br/>
         Look at your man. Now back to him. Now back at your man. Now back to him. This meme gave an extra jolt of energy (like fighter jets and punching!) to actor Isaiah Mustafas career (
         <a href="http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2010/07/old-spice-guy-to-appear-in-horrible-bosses-and-chu.html">
          look for him in
          <em>
           Horrible Bosses
          </em>
          early next year
         </a>
         ) and was parodied everywhere, from the Emmy Awards (by the creators of
         <em>
          Modern Family
         </em>
         ) to
         <em>
          Sesame Street
         </em>
         (Grover used the ad to teach the kids about the word on, as in Im on a horse.). Old Spice Guy even
         <a href="http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2010/07/watch-the-man-your-man-could-smell-like-respond-to.html">
          responded to individual users on Twitter
         </a>
         . The tweets are now diamonds!
        </p>
