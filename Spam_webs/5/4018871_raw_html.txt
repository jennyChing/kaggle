                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Adovia Facial Serum With Dead Sea Minerals Delivers Minerals &amp; Nutrients Deep Into Skin
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1062879744037472614" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      Disclosure: I got this product as part of an advertorial.
                      <a href="http://www.tomoson.com/?code=TOP40f7f10b61c65ed086bd153ca7d69942" rel="nofollow">

                         <br/>
                         <br/>
                         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                          <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Oig6ICAjzbw/U5UReQ4YaJI/AAAAAAAAF4Y/HnkxwqoMSxY/s1600/100_3583.JPG" width="240"/>
                         </div>
                         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                          <br/>
                         </div>
                         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                          I am a beauty junkie, I love trying new products. I know the importance of taking care of the skin on my face and love pampering my face with lotions. The ingredients in
                          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/b00khgik54" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                           Adovia Facial Serum
                          </a>
                          made me want to try it, as they sounded so healthy andluxurious. Adovia Facial Serum is made with vitamin C, Green Tea, and Dead Sea Minerals. The company describes the product as an anti-aging and skin lifting facial serum. That sounded like pampering to me and I wanted to see how it worked on my skin.
                         </div>
                         <br/>
                         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ADFpFQf5AsM/U5URf_l3O6I/AAAAAAAAF4g/WTaR8G0LED0/s1600/100_3584.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                           <img border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ADFpFQf5AsM/U5URf_l3O6I/AAAAAAAAF4g/WTaR8G0LED0/s1600/100_3584.JPG" width="240"/>
                          </a>
                         </div>
                         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                          <br/>
                         </div>
                         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                          The serum comes in a nice glass bottle and has a pump on it to make it easy to apply to your face. The serum comes out as a silky lotion that feels so smooth on you face. The lotion is not oily and absorbed quickly into my skin making my face feel hydrated and soft. The serum is gentle on the skin and will deliver " Anti Wrinkle Results" over time unlike some other products that I have used that have irritated my skin. The Vitamin C is a great antioxidant that protects the skin from sun damage and can also reverse sun damage. When I used Adovia Facial Serum, my skin felt so good. I felt like I visited a spa.
                         </div>
