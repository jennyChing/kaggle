        <h1 class="entry-title">
         How to use PCSX2 the Playstation 2 Emulator
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p class="reapernotice">
         Update: Please check the new version of this post at
         <a href="http://www.reaper-x.com/2010/04/12/how-to-configure-pcsx2-ps2-emulator/">
          <strong>
           how to configure pcsx2
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now im going to put some guide on how to configure PCSX2 to try out the PS2 Emulator. And since few people are asking is their system can play ps2, then the answer is no. You cant actually play ps2 games on your pc right now (dunno for a real high end system which is using Dual Core Processor, SLI Graphics Card, etc, but to most usersjust wait for it), but if you want to help the PCSX2 team, you can do it by testing out PCSX2 and reporting any bugs you found to them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-58">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         First you must have PS2 Bios in order to try out the PCSX2 (you can find it easily on the internet) ;) and dont forget toput it into Bios Directoryin your PCSX2 Directory
        </p>
        <p>
         and the next step would be running the PCSX2 Executable and configure it like this (maybedifferent depending on your computer configuration, especially to those who are using SSE2 Enabled Processor, such as Pentium 4, Athlon64, etc)
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Configuration Screen of PCSX2
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="PCSX2 Configuration" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/scrpcxs2.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Graphic Plugin Configuration Screen
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="PCSX2 Graphic Plugin" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/pcsx2-2.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Controller Configuration (since i dont own a joystick, im using Keyboard)
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="PCSX2 Controller Plugin" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/pcsx2-3.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         4. CDVD Plugin Configuration
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: if you dont have ASPI installed on your system use Win2k/XP IOCTL SCSI Command and set it into your DVD Rom Drive (if youre not using ISO Image)
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="PCSX2 CDVD Plugin" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/pcsx2-4.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         5. The next step would be putting your PS2 Games on your DVD ROM and choose to Run CD from File Menu  and pray hope itll work :P and in my case i was running Dragon Ball Z Budokai Tenkaichi PAL Version
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Dragon Ball Budokai Tenkaichi on PCSX2" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/tenkaichi-1.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Dragon Ball Budokai Tenkaichi Screenshot 2 on PCSX2" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/tenkaichi-2.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Dragon Ball Budokai Tenkaichi 2 PAL on PCSX2" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/db-tenkaichi2-1.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Final Fantasy 12 oin PCSX2" src="//img.reaper-x.com/blogs/ff12pcsx2.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         And thats it  youre finished ;)
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: If you got an error message saying
         <em>
          error opening gs plugin
         </em>
         or something like that, most likely youre trying to use ZeroGS Pluginand your vga card is not a Pixel Shader 2.0 Capable Card (ZeroGS require Pixel Shader 2.0 ++ Capable Card) .. and the only solution is using GSDX Plugin as the graphic plugin or get a new graphic card
        </p>
       </div>
