         <p>
          Okay ladies, lets get serious and be honest with ourselves for a minute. Weve all experienced it but never talk about it  losing our favorite pair of panties to an unfortunate monthly mishap. Or worse, wearing worn out granny panties for a week because were terrified of ruining the cute new ones we just bought. Not too good for the old ego is it? It happens to the best of us and its frustrating really, it just plain sucks. But guess what? Its time to get excited because it doesnt have to be this way  we dont have to be improperly protected during our periods anymore. We can rise up and make a change ladies, and we can save those undies! (
          <em>
           Okay this is where you all jump out of your seat and squeal with glee!
          </em>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ooh.li/51f4c9e" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I know youre all wondering, what is this revolutionary invention that is going to make periods less of a hassle for women across the nation? Well let me tell you, its
          <a href="http://ooh.li/51f4c9e" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            U by Kotex with 3D capture core
           </span>
          </a>
          . These bad boys have a one-of-a-kind center that locks away liquid to help stop leaks, thus making it possible for all ladies to save the undies! Not only do we no longer have to deal with the sadness associated with losing that favorite pair of designer panties (
          <em>
           or maybe the sadness is just hormones
          </em>
          ), either way, we will also save money by not having to replace our undergarments as often (
          <em>
           and saving money is always good right?
          </em>
          ). Sounds like a win-win to me. And quite possibly the most important benefit,
          <a href="http://ooh.li/51f4c9e" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            U by Kotex with 3D capture core
           </span>
          </a>
          makes it possible to always be completely confident no matter what time of the month it is. If were not worried about pesky leaks, we can better spend our time focused on things that truly matter (
          <em>
           like wearing our cute clothes instead of our designated period wardrobe
          </em>
          ).
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ooh.li/51f4c9e" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img alt="UbyKotexSavetheUndies" class="aligncenter wp-image-1659 size-large" height="389" src="http://horseshoes-n-handgrenades.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/UbyKotexSavetheUndies-1024x726.png" width="550"/>
          </a>
         </p>

         </p>
         <p>
          So ladies of the world, if you want to keep your wardrobe intact, save your hard earned cash, and shine with confidence regardless of what day it is, remember to use
