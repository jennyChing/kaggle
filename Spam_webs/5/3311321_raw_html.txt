          and I can save a ton of ideas {I truly think if you could possibly weigh my boards they would weigh several tons} and when I cant sleep or I need something to soothe my nerves I head on over to Pinterest and either pin a frenzy of new pins or go through my old ones. I had seen similar earrings to these and thought I would try to make a pair. They were easier than I thought they would be and I love how they turned out!
         </p>
         <p>
          Supplies:
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14542">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-2.png">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14542" height="494" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-2.png" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-2.png&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Soft and fairly thin leather scrap {think selvage ~ if you have an old leather handbag, wallet, coat, etc., save that leather for a craft like this}
         </p>
         <p>
          Gold Craft Paint {optional} and paint brush
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14543">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-3.png">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14543" height="667" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-3.png" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-3.png&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          French wire for earrings
         </p>
         <p>
          Sewing machine
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14548">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-9.png">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 9" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14548" height="433" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-9.png" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-9.png&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Small pair of sharp fabric scissors
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14544">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-4.png">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14544" height="549" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-4.png" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-4.png&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Draw the outline of a feather on a piece of cardboard and cut out.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14545">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-5.jpg">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14545" height="392" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-5.jpg" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-5.jpg&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Trace pattern onto the wrong side of the leather.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14546">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-7.png">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14546" height="454" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-7.png" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-7.png&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Use small sharp scissors to make fringes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14547">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-8.jpg">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 8" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14547" height="422" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-8.jpg" width="650"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-8.jpg&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Sew a straight stitch down the left of center then back up to the top of the leather feather.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14550">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Leather-Earrings-from-Cupcakes-and-Crinoline.png">
              <img alt="Feather Leather Earrings from Cupcakes and Crinoline" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14550" height="914" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Leather-Earrings-from-Cupcakes-and-Crinoline.png" width="609"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Leather-Earrings-from-Cupcakes-and-Crinoline.png&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          5. Make a small hole at the top of the earring and run ring through this hole and then add the french wire.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pin-holder aligncenter" id="pin-wrapper-14549">
           <span class="pin-wrapper pin-wrapper-top-right">
            <span class="pin-image-wrapper">
             <a href="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-11.jpg">
              <img alt="Feather Earrings_Leather 11" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14549" height="433" src="http://cf.cupcakesandcrinoline.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Feather-Earrings_Leather-11.jpg" width="542"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="pin-bundle-wrapper">
             <span class="pin-button-container pin-button-container-none">
              <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="none" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2F2014%2F02%2F07%2Fmake-feather-earrings%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fcupcakesandcrinoline.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F02%2FFeather-Earrings_Leather-11.jpg&amp;description=">
               <img border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="pin-clear">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          6. If desired, brush a light coating of the gold paint on the bottom one-third of your earrings. *Note that once this is dry you will need to gently separate the fringes.
         </p>
         <p>
          These would be pretty to wear in the spring and summer with a prairie skirt or jeans and a light weave ruffled-shirt and cowboy boots!
         </p>
         <p>
          I hope you enjoy!
