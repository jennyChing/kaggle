       <strong>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-size: 11.8181819915771px; line-height: 1.2;">
        TheTown of
       </span>
       Camillus
       <span style="font-size: 11.8181819915771px; line-height: 1.2;">
        is the proud home to many museums, historical sites, and excellent recreational facilities. The Town has a population of over 25,000 residents.
       </span>
       Camillus
       <span style="font-size: 11.8181819915771px; line-height: 1.2;">
        is within close proximity to the City of Syracuse and the crossroads of upstate New York, providingeasy access to all Central New York locations, as well as the Thousand Islands, the Adirondacks, and Finger Lakes regions of the state via major highways passing through the area.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <strong>
        School:
       </strong>
       Camillus New York has one high school (West Genesee), two middle schools (West Genesee, Camillus) and four elementary schools:East Hill,Onondaga Road, Split Rock and Stone hedge.
       <strong>
        School District:
       </strong>
       <a href="http://www.westgenesee.org/" target="_blank">
        <strong>
         West Genesee Central School District
        </strong>
       </a>
       <strong>
        .
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <strong>
        Shopping &amp; Dining:
       </strong>
       Camillus New Yorkhas a strong business base, with enterprises stretching the entire length of West Genesee Street, from Fairmount to the furthest western Town border, all of whom provide convenient and safe access to shopping, dining, and services to fill almost every conceivable need. Twoshopping malls (Fairmount Fair, Camillus Commons) are located in the Town of Camillus. There are numerous chain restaurants in the area, which includesApplebee's Neighborhood Grill, Denny's, Friendly's, IHOP, Moes Southwest Grill and Panera Bread. Local restaurants include the Inn Between, Top O' the Hill, Plainville Farms, Victors Inn, Green Gate Inn &amp; French Kiss Restaurant, Shanghai Chinese, DiBellos Pizza, Boom Boom Mex Mex, and Ahern Pub.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <strong>
        Parks/Lakes/Recreation:
       </strong>
       The Town of Camillus owns and maintains over 500 acres of park land that provide a staggering array of recreational choices for every season, including a small lake, a community swimming pool, and an enclosed skating arena amongst other offerings. There arefive
       <strong>
        golf courses
       </strong>
       in the Camillus area (Westvale, West Hill, Pine Grove, Camillus and Popes Grove). Anglers can fish for trout in
       <strong>
        Nine Mile Creek
       </strong>
       (25 mile stream from Otisco to Onondaga Lake)which runs through the Town of Camillus.
      </p>

      </p>
