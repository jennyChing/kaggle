          <p>
           Capture every moment of wonderful celebrations and merry making with the whole family and friends with the HD C310 Portable Webcam, 5MP, Black. This special device offers clear and bright pictures of up to 5 MP which you can send to your family and friends. The high definition C310 Portable Webcam is a perfect device to take high quality videos of precious moments which are perfectly clear with sharp images and striking colors that you can share on Facebook by uploading it. This Portable Webcam which can be connected to the laptops or desktop computer with high speed USB, can give you a chance to view your videos is spite of poor lighting because it has automatic light correction. Another important feature is the HD 720p which allows you to make high definition calls even in a noisy background because of special features which can eliminate background noise. It is also compatible with Skype, Yahoo Messenger and other IM applications.
           <span id="more-3291">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           The HD C310 Portable Webcam has a universal clip which can connect laptops, CRT or LCD monitors which makes it comfortable for the user to easily make a safe connection with the devices/ gadgets. Since the portable webcam is compatible to Skype, yahoo messenger and other IM applications, you can easily share pictures and videos which can also be saved for future references. Pictures taken during special events or occasions like birthdays, anniversaries, graduations, reunions and other celebrations which happen only once in a lifetime are perfectly captured by the high definition C310 portable webcam. Reminiscing or recalling special moments can be made more meaningful as you watch videos and pictures. Each picture and videos seem real and can bring you back to the good old days.
          </p>
          <p>
           HD C310 Portable Black Webcam 5MP is a portable device helps you get connected. It has a built in microphone which makes it possible for you to receive and make HD calls in widescreen. Every call you made is clear and loud which make communication very effective.
          </p>
          <p>
           The webcam weighs only 9.6 oz. Its dimension is 6x 3x 8.2 which makes it easy to handle and carry anywhere you go. Its portability gives you a convenient way to take pictures or videos any way you want.
          </p>
          <p>
           The HD C310 Portable Black Webcam 5 Mega Pixel can be purchased online and can be delivered right at your doorstep because of its free shipping package.
