        <h1>
         Textures Messengers: Djent Gone Acoustic
        </h1>
        <!--================= META ===================-->
        <div class="meta single">
         <time id="desktop">
          Friday, June 20th, 2014 at 3:20PM
         </time>
         <time id="mobile">
          06/20/14 at 3:20PM
         </time>
         <span class="author">
          By
          <a href="http://www.metalsucks.net/author/axl-rosenberg/" title="View All Posts by Axl Rosenberg">
           Axl Rosenberg
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!--================ /META ===================-->
        <!--================ CONTENT =================-->
        <div class="post-body">
         <p>
          Textures didnt arentthe first djent band to go the
          <em>
           Unplugged
          </em>
          route 
          <a href="http://www.metalsucks.net/2013/09/09/vildhjarta-you-dimman/" target="_blank">
           Vildhjarta did on the track Dimman
          </a>
           but they are the best!
         </p>
         <script src="https://static.urx.io/units/web/ffabffc4-a672-4347-8e30-5755eebf89ba.min.js">
         </script>
         <p>
          Thislive, acoustic version of their song Messengers, from the album
          <em>
           Silhouettes
          </em>
          , is terrific  the original has some really heavy parts, but the band has made the necessary adjustments without sacrificing the tracks integrity. Check it out:
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="__youtube_prefs__" frameborder="0" height="187" id="_ytid_69985" mozallowfullscreen="" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/Y_-9jUhkrKU?enablejsapi=1&amp;autoplay=0&amp;cc_load_policy=0&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;loop=0&amp;modestbranding=0&amp;rel=1&amp;showinfo=1&amp;playsinline=0&amp;autohide=2&amp;theme=dark&amp;color=red&amp;wmode=opaque&amp;vq=&amp;controls=2&amp;" type="text/html" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="282">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          The acoustic Messengers will be available as a digital download today in Europe, June 22 in the UK, and June 24 in the U.S.
         </p>
         <p>
          And heres the original, on the off chance youve never heard it before
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="__youtube_prefs__" frameborder="0" height="240" id="_ytid_44919" mozallowfullscreen="" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/V8FecJLXV3o?enablejsapi=1&amp;autoplay=0&amp;cc_load_policy=0&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;loop=0&amp;modestbranding=0&amp;rel=1&amp;showinfo=1&amp;playsinline=0&amp;autohide=2&amp;theme=dark&amp;color=red&amp;wmode=opaque&amp;vq=&amp;controls=2&amp;" type="text/html" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="282">
