             <a href="http://i2.wp.com/triedandtasty.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/cowboy-caviar-01.jpg">

             Its Super Bowl Sunday! Although I dont give much of a care for football, I will tell you Im excited for two reasons. 1) My dad LOVES the Seattle Seahawks. Hes been a fan since before I was born (so hes not one of those bandwagon fans). As soon as I learned they were going to the Super Bowl I immediately called him up to hear his excitement. Im happy for him, and hope that is team does well! 2) game day food!!!! The appetizers, party snacks, and finger foods are what I live for on this day whether I watch the game or not!
            </p>
            <p>
             Ever heard of Cowboy Caviar? You may have or you may have not. Its pretty much just a bunch of diced up veggies coated with a delicious dressing to make one of my favorite dips of all time. I was first introduced to this by my friend at work who had brought it for a pot luck years ago. It was then that I realized what I had been missing all the year before.. and now, Ill never have to go without again because Ive got the recipe! Its super simple: chop, drizzle, stir. Can you get much easier than that?! I think not! If you dont have time to make this for the big game, no worries  its delicious any day of the year!
