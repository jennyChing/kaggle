     <p>
      The Irredeemable Shag of
      <a href="http://onceuponageek.com/">
       Once Upon a Geek
      </a>
      has been posting photos from past
      <a href="http://www.dragoncon.org/">
       Dragon*Cons
      </a>
      in advance of years convention. Among them: This 2007 photo of a fan dressed as the
      <a href="http://www.hyperborea.org/flash/appendix-alternate.html#dk2">
       future Barry Allen
      </a>
      from Frank Miller and Lynn Varleys
      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1563899299?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=hyperborea-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=1563899299">
       <i>
        Batman: The Dark Knight Strikes Again
       </i>
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://onceuponageek.com/" title="Dark Knight Flash_74 by Once Upon a Geek">
       <img alt="Dark Knight Flash_74" class="aligncenter" height="500" src="http://st3.speedforce.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/4943360122_de6a6dcf08.jpg" width="375"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I dont think Ive seen anyone in this costume before.
