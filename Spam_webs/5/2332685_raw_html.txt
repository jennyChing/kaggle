        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Easiest Ever Peanut Butter Blossoms
        </h1>
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="18487137" data-link="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/peanut-butter-blossoms/" data-summary="" data-title="Easiest Ever Peanut Butter Blossoms">
        </div>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-12-19T00:00:40+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 19, 2013
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/author/paula/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Paula
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/peanut-butter-blossoms/#comments">
           16 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div class="wwsgd" style="display:none;">
         <p style="border:thin dotted black; padding:3mm;">
          Welcome! To find more fun posts like this please
          <a href="http://pinterest.com/PaulaRollo">
           follow me on Pinterest
          </a>
          . Thanks for visiting!
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         My friend Sarah recently told me how to make these super easy peanut butter blossoms, and so we tried making the cookies last week. They are truly the easiest cookies Ive ever made! Little Man was able to do pretty much all of it by himself! The cookies are also naturally gluten free, and can be dairy free if you exclude the kiss on the top!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Easiest-Ever-peanut-Butter-Blossoms.png">
          <img alt="Easiest Ever peanut Butter Blossoms" class="size-full wp-image-4685 aligncenter" height="550" src="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Easiest-Ever-peanut-Butter-Blossoms.png" width="423"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1 cup Peanut Butter
         </strong>
         <em>
          (make sure your brand is gluten free, if need be)
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1 cup sugar
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1 egg
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Hersheys kisses
         </strong>
         <em>
          (Hersheys Milk Chocolate Kisses should be gluten free, but
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           if you need the cookies to be dairy-free also, leave the kisses of
          </span>
          f)
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mix the peanut butter, sugar and egg together in a bowl. The mixture may look a little bit grainy, thats fine.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Easy-cookie-recipe-for-kids.jpg">
          <img alt="Easy cookie recipe for kids" class="size-full wp-image-4687 aligncenter" height="521" src="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Easy-cookie-recipe-for-kids.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Make the dough into 1 inch balls and arrange on a baking sheet. You can put them moderately close together, as they wont spread much.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
        </p>
        <p>
         When you remove them from the oven place a hersheys kiss on top of each cookie (unless you are going for the dairy-free version)! Let them cool and enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Easiest-cookies-ever.jpg">
          <img alt="Easiest cookies ever" class="size-full wp-image-4688 aligncenter" height="550" src="http://www.beautythroughimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Easiest-cookies-ever.jpg" width="415"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
