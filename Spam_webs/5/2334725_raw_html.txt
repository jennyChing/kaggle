       <p>
        <img alt="" class="center imgcap" src="http://i.somethingawful.com/u/elpintogrande/may09/tatswat/24.jpg" width="250"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         I thought we were doing a thing about bad tattoos, not the greatest tattoo.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         Facts about the dude with this tattoo: never wears a shirt, walks backwards everywhere, sees shit through the bear face's eyes.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         Is never fucked with; basically never stops yelling.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         Not all that muscular, but draws immeasurable physical and sexual strength from the tattoo.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         This isn't even a tattoo, a shaman just rubbed a hot rock all over his back and this is what happened.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         It was like metaphysical paint-by-numbers. Smoked some peyote, saw infinity, bear on back.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         Now carries spirit of Yellbear within him; uses it to get out of parking tickets.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         Sir, this is a tow-away zo-- RRUUUHHHHHH!
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         The traffic cop just shrinks smaller and smaller until he turns into a tiny tattoo that finally disappears into the bear's flared nostril.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         Yellbear also uses this power to suck in beers.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         No matter how many beers it drinks, Yellbear's owner doesn't get fatter, Yellbear just gets more powerful.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         Walks into Safeway... RRRRUUHHHHHH... frozen food section is now completely out of Totino's Pizza Rolls.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         Drives past Taco Bell....RRRRRRRRRUHHHHHHHH...passenger seat piled with sacks full of chili cheese burritos.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         Pulls up at a light next to some pretty girls...RRRRRRRUHHHHHHHHH...girls flushed, satisfied.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         Uh oh, it's 5:30, Yellbear missed Cops! RRRRUUUUUUUHHH! Another episode!
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         That's not really a power. I think Raiders fan or die 4 u could conjure up more cops episodes.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         In certain situations, whether or not it's a power isn't relevant.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         Man, this sucks. Now I really badly want some pizza rolls and a cops marathon.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         You have to earn that shit with a spirit quest, or else earn it by getting really drunk and calculating that it's cheaper to pass out in the 24-hour tattoo parlor than in the hotel.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         I would pass out at a Raiders game wearing a Chiefs jersey if it meant I would wake up from my coma in six weeks with a yellbear to call my own.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_red">
           Dave:
          </span>
         </b>
         RRRRUUUHHHHH! Bob Barker tattoo turns into big titty angel.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <span class="color_blue">
           Zack:
          </span>
         </b>
         RRRRRRUUUUUHHHHH! Departed wife skeletonizes.
         <br/>
        </p>
       </blockquote>

       <div class="lining">
        <p>
         Fashion SWAT... the fashion industry is obsessed with impracticality. We know that what designers create was never meant to be worn by the grimy masses, but that doesn't somehow diminish how ridiculous many of these costumes are. Make no mistake, they are costumes, and like a Halloween prize pageant we will turn our discerning gaze on the grievous fashion misfires of Paris, Milan, and New York. We're not pulling any punches, and we're definitely not interested in making any friends. We're Joan Rivers without Melissa Rivers to temper our screeching. We're the Fashion Police in jack boots. We are Fashion SWAT.
