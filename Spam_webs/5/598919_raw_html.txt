      <h1>
       Celebrity Jewelry Trends 2015
      </h1>
      <div class="info">
       Posted on April 29, 2015
                 by 
                    
                        LuAnn Caprio
                    
                 
            |
       <a href="/blogs/news/20289537-celebrity-jewelry-trends-2015#commentsection">
        0 comments
       </a>
      </div>
      <div class="pagecontent contentsection">
       <div class="content-block">
        <p>
         Layering, Mixing, Personalizing, '70's, '80's, Midi's - all on trend celebrity jewelry styles for 2015! Let's face it, our favorite celebrities set many of the trends we grow to love and make into our own jewelry styles. This year, we are seeing jewelry layered on and personalized - the perfect way to show your unique style. Mixing fine jewelry, with fashion jewelry is another great way to create a new look and pair the old with the new.
        </p>
        <p>
         Layering midi rings is a trend seen on celebs like Taylor Swift, seen recently in Vogue wearing this CZ Double Band layered Ring.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/collections/monogram-gold-rings" title="Gold Monogram Rings">
          <img src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/midis_large.jpg?4631370055163970190"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/products/cz-double-band-ring-taylor-swift-vogue-magazine" title="Taylor Swift Vogue Magazine CZ Ring">
          <img height="382" src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/taylor-swift-collage_1024x1024_large.jpg?801500581806527836" width="382"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Layering longer necklaces and chokers creates a stunning, vintage yet trendy look. A '70's throwback, this current trnd is also extended to layering with mixed metals.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/collections/gold-monogram-necklaces" title="Gold Monogram Necklaces layered">
          <img height="366" src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/mini-monogram-necklace_large.jpg?10051625687334470849" width="387"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/collections/sterling-silver-monogram-necklaces" title="Sterling Silver Monogram Necklace">
          <img height="363" src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/mixed-metal-layering_large.jpg?7283178895353316575" width="397"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The '80's, Madonna style, arm party is on trend in 2015. Layer up your bracelets with the ultimate arm candy. Wear your fine jewelry with your fashion jewelry - the lines are blurred and we are loving it!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/collections/monogram-gold-plated-jewelry/products/gold-filigree-monogram-bangle-bracelet" title="Personalized Monogram Bangle Bracelet">
          <img alt="" src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/gold-pink-bangles_large.jpg?11888182966870154781" style="float: none;"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/collections/monogram-gold-bracelets/products/gold-monogram-birthstone-bangle-bracelet" title="personalized birthstone cuff bracelets">
          <img src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/birthstone-jewelery-monogrammed_large_large.jpg?8033585622643770506"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p>
         Personalization! Layering! It's the trend and we love it all. These ideas will give any outfit an updated look and more. We are always on the hunt for the next trend, and our favorite celebrities are often in the forefront setting those trends.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bemonogrammed.com/collections/gold-engraved-necklaces/products/engraved-gold-bar-necklace-clare-of-the-bachelor-reese-witherspoon" title="Jessica Alba gold bar necklace">

          <img height="415" src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0267/2891/files/kourtney7_2d95ca59-cfb2-42c1-8847-ded82ab49d5c_large.png?17058544645675585283" width="412"/>
         </a>
        </p>
