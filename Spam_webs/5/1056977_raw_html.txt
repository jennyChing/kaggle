       As readers of my columns over the years can attest, I am a major fan  of mashup videos. If you are not familiar with them, a mashup video is  one that a person has taken two or more different films and have edited  and combined into one very interesting video.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The other day, Jill called to me, saying she had found a video that  you had to see to believe and was she ever right! A YouTube user named
       <a href="http://www.youtube.com/user/LM1313" rel="nofollow">
        LM1313
       </a>
       has taken two  videos with absolutely nothing in common, and has, through the use of  careful editing and synchronization, has turned them into a video piece  that one would think was originally done that way, if you were not  familiar with the two subjects.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       What this talented individual has done is to take video from the  cartoon show, My Little Pony, and edit a video to which he has added  the audio track from, of all shows, Dr. Horribles Sing-Along Blog.  The result is a cartoon that many folks are enjoying.
