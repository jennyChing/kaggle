surgical procedure process is appropriate for patients who need to remove about
1.7 pounds from each breast. This process can lead to a projecting, stunning
breast shape with limited scarring. Another important aspect of
          <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">
           Vertical Reduction Mammoplasty
          </b>

related to the body's ability to shrink skin and thereby reshape the breasts.
In certain cases, a revision procedure may be needed based on the amount of
skin that was unable to shrink.

breasts. Scarring is minimal major cuts or incisions are not necessary. The
liposuction cannula is inserted through a tiny incision which heals soon
without leaving any major marks. There are also no sensory changes in the
nipple-areola complex. This process is ideal for more youthful patients with
adequate skin tone. Moreover, this process is best for those patients who are
looking for a complete solution for heavy, oversized breasts. The surgical
procedure helps to lift the breasts by a significant degree, thereby reducing
breast size by over fifty percent.
