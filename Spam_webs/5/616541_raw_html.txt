         <h1 class="single_title" itemprop="name">
          Packaging Spotlight: Big Peat Whiskey
         </h1>
        </div>
        <!-- ///////////////////////////// -->
        <div class="post-body" style="position:relative;">
         <!-- feature image -->
         <p>
          <a href="http://drinksfeed.com/2015/03/17/packaging-spotlight-big-peat-whiskey/" id="big_top_pic" rel="bookmark" style="text-decoration:none" title="Packaging Spotlight: Big Peat Whiskey">
           <div>
            <img alt="Packaging Spotlight: Big Peat Whiskey photo" border="0" height="600px" itemprop="image" src="http://cache.winetimes.co/wp-content/blogs.dir/1/files/2015/03/zoom-whisky-big-peat-1764.jpg" style="width:100%; height:auto" title="Packaging Spotlight: Big Peat Whiskey" width="600px"/>
           </div>
          </a>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <!-- ///////////// -->
         <div itemprop="articleBody" style="margin:0% 2%;">
          <div style="width:200px; float:right; text-align:right">
           <a class="" href="/punt/?p=twitter&amp;id=244820" target="_blank" title="Tweet">
            <img src="http://cache.winetimes.co/images/icons/twitter_32.png" width="32px"/>
           </a>
           <a class="" href="/punt/?p=pinterest&amp;id=244820" target="_blank" title="Pin">
            <img src="http://cache.winetimes.co/images/icons/pinterest_32.png" width="32px"/>
           </a>
           <a class="" href="/punt/?p=facebook&amp;id=244820" target="_blank" title="Share">
            <img src="http://cache.winetimes.co/images/icons/facebook_32.png" width="32px"/>
           </a>
           <a class="" href="/punt/?p=google&amp;id=244820" target="_blank" title="Plus">
            <img src="http://cache.winetimes.co/images/icons/gplus_32.png" width="32px"/>
           </a>
           <img height="32px" src="http://cache.winetimes.co/images/punt.gif" width="32px"/>
          </div>
          <p>
           Big Peat is a feisty Islay character but has a sweet side too  he carries a big peaty (phenolic), beachy, oceanic, rather ashy selection of malts only from the island of Islay.
           <span id="more-244820">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stirling agency Pocket Rocket Creative has designed the packaging for Douglas Laing &amp; Co. whisky brand Big Peat.
          </p>
          <p>
           It features an illustration of the Big Peat character reacting to the smoky, peaty and medicinal flavours of the Islay Blended Malt.
          </p>
