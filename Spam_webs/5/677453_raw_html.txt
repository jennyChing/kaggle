       <div class="pg-rail-tall__body" itemprop="articleBody">
        <section class="zn zn-body-text zn-body zn--idx-0 zn-has-multiple-containers zn-has-36-containers" data-containers="36" data-eq-pts="xsmall: 0, medium: 460, large: 780, full16x9: 1100" data-vr-zone="zone-1-0" data-zn-id="body-text" id="body-text">
         <div class="l-container">
          <div class="el__leafmedia el__leafmedia--storyhighlights el__leafmedia--">
           <div class="el__storyhighlights_wrapper">
            <div class="el__storyhighlights">
             <h3 class="el__headline">
              Story highlights
             </h3>
             <ul class="el__storyhighlights__list">
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               Mereso Kilusu was a child bride. Now she campaigns against child marriage
              </li>
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               Changing attitudes in traditional communities is key, says Kilusu
              </li>
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               'We need to talk to families about how girls can contribute to their livelihoods,' she says
              </li>
              <li class="el__storyhighlights__item el__storyhighlights--normal">
               Report: Nine of the 10 countries with the world's highest rates of child marriage are in Africa
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
            <div class="ad ad--epic" data-ad-text="show">
             <div class="ad-ad_ns_btf_01" id="ad_ns_btf_01">
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Nine of the 10 countries with the world's
           <a href="http://www.who.int/mediacentre/news/releases/2013/child_marriage_20130307/en/" target="_blank">
            highest rates of child marriage
           </a>
           are in Africa: Niger, Chad and Central African Republic, Guinea, Mozambique, Mali, Burkina Faso and South Sudan, and Malawi.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           My country, Tanzania, did not make the list. But in traditional Maasai communities like mine, marrying off girls is very common.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           I was married at 13 to a man in his 70s.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           It happened during Christmas break. My father told my school that I had died. Even if he hadn't, I would have been forced to leave when I got pregnant because that was the law at the time.
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
           <img alt="Mereso Kilusu" class="media__image media__image--responsive" data-demand-load="not-loaded" data-eq-pts="mini: 0,  xsmall: 221,  small: 308,  medium: 461,  large:  781" data-src-full16x9="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-large="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-medium="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-horizontal-large-gallery.jpg" data-src-mini="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-hp-video.jpg" data-src-mini1x1="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-topics.jpg" data-src-small="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-story-top.jpg" data-src-xsmall="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-story-body.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"/>
           <div class="img__preloader">
           </div>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Mereso Kilusu" class="media__image" src="http://i2.cdn.turner.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/130308111537-mereso-kilusu-story-top.jpg"/>
           </noscript>
           <div class="media__caption el__gallery_image-title">
            Mereso Kilusu
           </div>
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           I gave birth to my first child within a year. I had no professional prenatal care and no trained medical assistance during delivery. I had to depend on my husband and his other wives for guidance. It was a very painful experience. Every time I became pregnant after that I felt sick and scared. Because of all these difficult births I have a hard time controlling my bladder and it can be painful to urinate.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Today, I am a mother of five at 29 years old.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           <a href="http://edition.cnn.com/2013/03/07/business/international-womens-day-inspiring-words/">
            International Women's Day: Whose words inspire you?
           </a>
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           In communities like mine, age is not understood as a number. Our traditional values dictate girls are meant for marriage, and when the men decide we are biologically ready, we are married.
          </p>
          <div class="el__image--standard js__image--standard">
          </div>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
           Marriage is sometimes a way of forming and cementing relationships. But it is also a way of earning money.
          </p>
          <p class="zn-body__paragraph">
