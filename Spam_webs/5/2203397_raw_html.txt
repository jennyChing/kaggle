       <div class="post-1856 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-attracting-women category-beginner-advice category-muscle-building category-workouts tag-compound-exercises tag-exercises tag-isolation-exercise tag-physique-that-attracts-women tag-shaping-your-body tag-shoulders tag-strengthen-your-core tag-workout entry">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The Best Workout Options for the Best Body
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://musclepyramid.com/author/jasonparis" rel="author" title="Jason Paris">
            Jason Paris
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div id="getsocialmain">
          <p>
           When it comes to transforming your body and building a physique that attracts women, nothing works like a well designed
           <a href="http://musclepyramid.com/let-me-share-some-weight-lifting-experience" target="_blank" title="Let Me Share Some Weight Lifting Experience">
            weight training
           </a>
           program.That is not to say that you dont have to eat right and do cardio, because that is important too. But its the workout routine that makes all the difference in shaping your body.
          </p>
          <p>
           You can eat right and do all the cardio in the world but that will not get you that V-shape and broad shoulders that women find so attractive and masculine. Sure, it will get you lean and let your abs show through, but you will just be that skinny guy that gets sand kicked at him on the beach.
          </p>
          <p>
           You have to lift weights or do some kind of
           <a href="http://musclepyramid.com/is-resistance-training-better-than-bodyweight-training" target="_blank" title="Is Resistance Training Better than Bodyweight Training?">
            resistance training
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            In this article you will discover:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            What exercises to include in your workout
           </li>
           <li>
            How to add intensity to your workout
           </li>
           <li>
            What muscles to target for the best body
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h2>
           Not All Programs Are Created Equal
          </h2>
          <p>
           Unfortunately finding the right program can be difficult.You get suggestions, and advise coming at you from all angles.There is the old guy in your gym that likes to tell you of methods he used back in the day.Of course there is the guy you have seen for the past year who fancies himself a trainer, but he looks the same as he did the first time he tried to share his wisdom with you. Lets not forget all the muscle magazines with the personal workouts of some of the biggest guys in the world.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1860" style="width: 441px">
           <img alt="Best Workout Options for Best Body - Fitness Models" class="size-full wp-image-1860" height="518" src="http://musclepyramid.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/best-workout-options-for-best-body-fitness-models.png" title="Best Workout Options for Best Body - Fitness Models" width="431"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Fitness Models
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           But all that advise is of no use to you.You are not looking to be the biggest guy in the gym, you arent on steroids, and you want your body to look better, not stay the same year after year.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Your Workout Program is Everything
          </h2>
          <p>
           Your weight training is what shapes you.It is critical that you follow a plan that works, and can get you the look you want.The random advise you get from the guys in your gym or the fitness magazines may be well meaning but it is not going to get you to where you want.
          </p>
          <h2>
           The Look
          </h2>
          <p>
           Across all cultures there seems to be a common body type that the majority of women find to be the most attractive.Many of Hollywoods leading men have this look, often getting them crowned sexiest man alive.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1861" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="Best Workout Options for Best Body - Daniel Craig" class="size-full wp-image-1861" height="507" src="http://musclepyramid.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/best-workout-options-for-best-body-daniel-Craig.png" title="Best Workout Options for Best Body - Daniel Craig" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Daniel Craig
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Im not an anthropologist but I believe women looked for guys that could provide for them.That meant hunt and protect their family.A guy with broad shoulders, a full chest and muscular arms looked as though he could handle himself and protect his family.
          </p>
          <p>
           I guess this is in our DNA, as women still find those same characteristics attractive today.Women love guys with wide shoulders, muscular arms and a full chest.
          </p>
          <p>
           A workout program that targets those muscle groups will create a physique other men respect and women love.
          </p>
          <h2>
           The Best Exercises For the Most Attractive Physique
          </h2>
          <p>
           When it comes to adding size nothing works like compound exercises.That is because they use multiple muscle groups and allow you to move the most amount of weight, a perfect combination for building muscle fast.
          </p>
          <p>
           Isolation exercises have their place too.For added muscle in select areas isolation movements are your best option. Because they only use one muscle group the weight is lower and you can really target individual muscles.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Shoulders and Traps
          </h3>
          <p>
           When it comes to building shoulders and traps nothing beats the muscle clean and press. With this exercise you also get the added bonus of getting your triceps worked.
          </p>
          <p>
           The isolation exercise that will bring out more definition in your shoulders is the lateral raise.It will finish off that nice round cap and help widen your shoulders.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Chest
          </h3>
          <p>
           To build a full chest the incline bench press is your best option.That doesnt mean you have to ditch the bench press but the incline is better because it will develop more of your upper chest to help it look more full.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you want to isolate your chest to make it have more cuts and increase its density, flys work perfectly.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Upper Back
          </h3>
          <p>
           Hands down, pull ups are by far your best option to widen your back.To create that v shape taper you need to be wide at the shoulders and back, but thin in the waist.
          </p>
          <p>
           To isolate the back lat sweeps work extremely well for adding width.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Legs
          </h3>
          <p>
           Developing your legs is not as important as the other muscle groups, but still a necessary component.The deadlift and squat by far the best exercises for this situation.Not only do they work your legs but they will help strengthen your core, shoulders, arms and back.
          </p>
          <p>
           Deadlifts and squats provide enough work thus an isolation exercise isnt necessary.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Arms
          </h3>
          <p>
           Your arms get lots of work doing your pulling and pressing exercises. But that doesnt mean you wont want to do a little extra.
          </p>
          <p>
           Nothing will build your triceps like dips. Some people are against bench dips because they are too hard on your shoulders.So, you may wanna go for parallel bar dips.
          </p>
          <p>
           For an isolation exercise that will engrave that horseshoe into your arms, use the overhead triceps extension.
          </p>
          <p>
           If your biceps need more attention stick with an isolation exercise, your best, ez curls. With an ez-curl bar you have the option to change grips making it less stressful on your elbows.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Improving Your Workout
          </h2>
          <p>
           The best way to
           <a href="http://musclepyramid.com/do-you-make-these-7-mistakes-when-you-work-out" target="_blank" title="Do You Make These 7 Mistakes When You Work Out?">
            improve your workout
           </a>
           is by adding intensity.The easiest way to add intensity is to get more work done in the same, or shorter amount of time. If you dont change anything about your workouts, increase the intensity by pushing to get it done faster. That is a bare minimum.But if you are interested in getting the most out of your workouts, give these intensity-boosting techniques a try.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Super Sets
          </h3>
          <p>
           If you are not currently superseting your exercises, start now.This one factor will totally change your workouts.They will become more intense and much quicker.
          </p>
          <p>
           Non-competing muscle groups make the best supersets.They allow you to get the most amount of work done in the shortest time, but allow you to maximize the weight used for both exercises.
          </p>
          <p>
           A great example would be to superset a muscle clean and press with a pull-up. Do your set of muscle cleans, rest 30 seconds then move on to your pull-ups. Take another 30 second break, then go back to your muscle clean.Repeat this process until youre done your sets.
          </p>
          <p>
           This works so well because youre using different muscles for each exercise. When doing your muscle clean and press, traps, triceps, and delts are the major muscles used. These muscles get to rest as you use your lats and biceps to do the pull ups.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Compound Sets
          </h3>
          <p>
           A compound set is doing two or more exercises in a row utilizing the same muscle group.They work well with the smaller assistance exercises like your biceps and triceps.
          </p>
          <p>
           Here is a quick example for the next time you train your triceps.
          </p>
          <p>
           Select two or more of your favorite triceps exercises.I like dips so that will be my first exercise.The second exercise is the overhead triceps extensions. This is a good option because these two movements work the muscle from different angles, putting the stress in slightly different parts of the muscle for
           <a href="http://musclepyramid.com/my-trainer-said-that-i-have-to-get-bigger-to-have-more-sex" target="_blank" title="My Trainer Said that I Have to Get Bigger to Have More Sex">
            maximum definition
           </a>
           .If you like, feel free to add in a third exercise, but its not necessary.
          </p>
          <p>
           This is how you would do it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Start off by doing a set of dips. Its best to start of with the most difficult exercise first. Immediately after your set of dips, dont rush, instead pick up your dumbbells and get your triceps extensions in.That combination makes one set.
          </p>
          <p>
           Generally two to three sets like this is sufficient.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Conclusion
          </h2>
          <p>
           Nutrition and cardio are important but your weight training workout is what is going to transform and shape your body. Because of this it is important you follow a program that will deliver results and help you build the body you want.
          </p>
          <p>
           Your program has to address all the attributes that women consider attractive like wide round shoulders, muscular arms, a full chest, and a V-tapper.Your typical bodybuilding program just isnt designed to deliver those kinds of results.Because of that you need a program with the right mix of exercises.
          </p>
          <p>
           Pull-ups, incline bench press, and the muscle clean and press are critical exercises for developing the Hollywood sex symbol or fitness model look. Add to that a great intensity by getting more work done in less time with supersets, and you have yourself a killer workout program.
          </p>
