     <p>
      <b>
       Sour Diesel
      </b>
      , also known as
      <b>
       Sour D
      </b>
      , is a
      <a href="/wiki/Cannabis_sativa" title="Cannabis sativa">
       Sativa
      </a>
      strain of
      <a href="/wiki/Cannabis_(drug)" title="Cannabis (drug)">
       cannabis
      </a>
      . It is a cross between 91 Chemdawg and a super skunk/northern lights. The strain produces a fast acting, cerebral high. Sour Diesel is easily identified by its extremely pungent sour scent as well as its uplifting, mood enhancing effects.
     </p>
     <p>
      The origins of Sour Diesel point to a Chemdawg grower in Colorado who intentionally bred the strain and gave clones from the best plant to other growers. Others say the strain was a mistake, developed from a few seeds found in a large bag of Chemdawg that were grown and crossed with Super Skunk somewhere on the east coast.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Medical_uses">
       Medical uses
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sour_Diesel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Medical uses">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Sour Diesel is highly effective in relieving stress, anxiety, and pain. Also, there is some suggestion that it may be helpful in alleviating the symptoms associated with chronic depression.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]

       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Characteristics">
       Characteristics
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sour_Diesel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Characteristics">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      This strain is known to contain upwards of 20% of the active ingredient, THC,.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-other_3-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-other-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Appearance">
       Appearance
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sour_Diesel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Appearance">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Sour Diesel's flowers are light green with moderate orange hairs. Trichome production is very high in this strain. This produces buds that are covered in a frosty white layer of THC. The leaves of Sour Diesel are thin and the plant grows tall like most sativa strains.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
       <a href="#cite_note-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Growing_environment">
       Growing environment
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sour_Diesel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Growing environment">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Sour Diesel stretches in the first three weeks of flowering. By maturity Sour Diesel could reach 6 feet indoors. Flowering time is approximately twelve weeks indoors, while outdoor plants will finish in late October. It can be grown indoors and outdoors with average yields ranging between 450 and 550 grams per square meters.
     </p>
