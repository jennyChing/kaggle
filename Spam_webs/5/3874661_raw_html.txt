      Dr. Linchun Willa Liu
     </h2>
     <!-- print help and any error messages -->

     <div class="" id="node-1469">
      <div class="node-inner">
       <div class="meta">
       </div>
       <div class="content">
        <div class="field field-type-filefield field-field-biography-image-file">
         <div class="field-items">
          <div class="field-item odd">
           <img alt="" class="imagecache imagecache-biography imagecache-default imagecache-biography_default" height="163" src="http://www.uleth.ca/prenticeinstitute/sites/prenticeinstitute/files/imagecache/biography/Liu-photo-2.jpg" title="" width="130"/>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Dr. Linchun Willa Liu joins the Prentice January 2013 as a Post-Doctoral  Fellow. Her Ph.D. (2010) is from the University of Toronto in  Sociology of Education and Equity Studies. Her research interest  include population health particularly among Chinese immigrants to  Canada, food security and many conference papers. From 2010-12, she was  lead researcher for a study funded by the Heart and Stroke Foundation  on Chinese Ontarians Living with Heart Disease and Stroke.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dr.  Liu will work for the two years of her post-doctoral fellowship on her  research project on health needs and practices among older Chinese  immigrants. As well, she will collaborate on various ongoing research  projects at the Prentice Institute.
        </p>
        <p>
         contact:
         <a href="mailto:willa.liu@uleth.ca">
          willa.liu@uleth.ca
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
