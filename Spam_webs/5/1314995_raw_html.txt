       <p>
        18 months ago BPG Werks was commissioned to build a Shredder that could Go-Faster. We had very few restraints and we found a small 48 hp rotary engine that was small enough to fit inside the existing Shredder frame. Although many modifications were made to both strengthen and stabilize the Shredder, the overall footprint is only 10% larger than our standard production model. This High performance Shredder has a top speed of +60 mph and truly is capable of flying over virtually any terrain.
       </p>
       <p>
        We just brought one of these machines to Travis Pastranas house to see what was possible and by the end of the first day, the guys were backflipping the rotary Shredder with ease. Shortly after, the riders were doing variations of Can Cans and Madonnas and it became apparent that these Shredders can do almost any motocross, jet ski and skateboard tricks. This will open up a whole new type of trick category that combines both power and board sports in a freestyle riding experience.
       </p>
