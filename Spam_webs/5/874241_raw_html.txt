         <p>
          We recently took a trip into the Museum of Science in Boston with the boys. Its one of their favorite places to go because of all the hands-on learning they get to do there. They think theyre playing, but theyre really learning at the same time!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston.jpg">
           <img alt="museum-of-science-boston" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22649" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          There are three floors of exhibits, so its usually a jam packed day whenever we go there.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-exhibit-halls.jpg">
           <img alt="museum-of-science-boston-exhibit-halls" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22654" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-exhibit-halls.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
          <span id="more-22648">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          As soon as we got there, the boys ran right for the space capsule:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-space-capsule.jpg">
           <img alt="museum-of-science-boston-space-capsule" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22674" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-space-capsule.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Then it was on to the Electricity Theater. Not only does it have the worlds largest indoor Van de Graaff generator, but there are all sorts of weather and electricity exhibits in that section of the museum.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-largest-van-de-graff.jpg">
           <img alt="museum-of-science-boston-largest-van-de-graff" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22661" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-largest-van-de-graff.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-electricity.jpg">
           <img alt="museum-of-science-boston-electricity" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22653" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/museum-of-science-boston-electricity.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
