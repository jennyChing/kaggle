           <p>
            Tracking a phone number of an unknown person can be difficult. Yet the task is not impossible. With extensive research you might be able to track down a number. Reverse phone lookup is a simple, but effective process. This process enables you to simply type the phone number of a person in search engine box to track it down. However, the process may not yield result on the first go. There are some handy ways to look for a phone number online. This article will give you an idea of how to track down someones phone number using online resources.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Google It
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            In this era, we use Google for everything. So, it is of little wonder that people will use this search engine to track down others phone numbers as well. Earlier looking for the location of a phone number had been easy. You could have gotten useful result just by typing the number in Google phonebook. However, after the Google phonebook shut down, the task of tracking phone number has become more difficult. Yet, with little research and effort, you can still track a number down using Google and online phone directories.
           </p>

           <p>
            <strong>
             Some Useful Tips
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Make sure to type the entire phone number of the person in the search box. Start with the area code when you are typing the number. This is a great way of getting useful search result.
           </p>
           <p>
            Once you have hit the enter button, Google will come up with various search results. You may find that the particular number is listed in various directories and websites. It may seem a bit difficult to sort out the directories and websites. However, since the Google phonebook shut down, the process of tracking down a phone number has become more difficult.
           </p>
           <p>
            If the number you are searching for belongs to a business organization, the search may show the name of the company in first five results.
           </p>
           <p>
            The matter may get difficult when you are searching for the number of a private place or if you are looking for a cell phone number. By nature people look for anonymity and they dont like to have their private information appear in search boxes. Therefore, the number may not come along with the residential address. However, if you are lucky and the person has entered the phone number in some website else, like a social media site, you are likely to get a lot of information about the person.
           </p>
           <p>
            Google can be used to track down both the address and phone number of a person. Type the full name of a person along with the zip code. This may yield effective result and you may get name, phone number and address with Google map.
           </p>

             The Bad News Is
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>

           <p>
            <strong>
             Use Online Phone Directory Instead
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            There are various reasons why people think twice before entering their personal information online. The growing threat of stalking and identity theft has made everyone cautious. Due to this reason, finding sensitive information like private phone number, residential address and current location has become difficult.
           </p>
           <p>
            If Google search result has failed to provide you proper details, you can also try online phone directories to search for phone numbers, addresses and email addresses of a person.
           </p>
           <p>
            However, the online directories value the users privacy and for that reason, they have given the option of hiding phone numbers or email addresses. If anyone is looking for the hidden information, the site will send a notification mail seeking approval from the particular profile owner. Only after getting the approval the site shares the requested details.
           </p>
           <p>
            So, the next time you are searching for a phone number or residential address, try online phone directories. Due to the sense of security, people share their personal information without any reluctance.
