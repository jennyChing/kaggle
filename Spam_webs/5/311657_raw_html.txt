         <span content="Amtrak's National Train Day is coming May 12th! {B. toys giveaway}" itemprop="title">
         </span>
         <span content="giveaway~kids" itemprop="categories">
         </span>
         <span content="free events for families~National Train Day~train" itemprop="tags">
         </span>
         <span content="wordpress.org" itemprop="srcType">
         </span>
         <span content="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/2012/04/amtrak-national-train-day-may-12/" itemprop="permalink">
         </span>
         <span content="" id="wpLogin" itemprop="wpLogin">
         </span>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          <a href="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/National-Train-Day-2012-Logo_reasonably_small.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1928" height="128" src="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/National-Train-Day-2012-Logo_reasonably_small.jpg" title="National-Train-Day-2012-Logo_reasonably_small" width="128"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          Are you looking for a great event for kids? Amtrak's Fifth Annual National Train Day is coming to over 75 locations around the US! National Train Day is an annual event that has family-friendly festivals and kid-focused events, teaching kids about trains and what makes train travel special. Check out the National Train Day website to find a location near you, as well as the events that will be taking place at each location.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          Check out some of the awesome entertainment that will be happening at select locations (check website to find where these events are going to take place.)
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li style="text-align: left; ">
           fun, interactive, and educational activities for kids  teaching children how trains work!
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: left; ">
           tours of freight and commuter trains and beautiful private railroad cars  for adults and children!
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: left; ">
           Dora the Explorer will be present in select locations!
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: left; ">
           Radio Disney will be emceeing all of the events!
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: left; ">
           Amtrak will be giving away free t-shirts and other memorabilia on-site for adults and children!
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          Train passengers are also encouraged to submit videos, pictures, and stories at NationalTrainDay.com,
          <a href="http://facebook.com/NationalTrainDay" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://facebook.com/NationalTrainDay', 'National Train Day on Facebook');" rel="nofollow">
           National Train Day on Facebook
          </a>
          , and @natltrainday on Twitter for a chance to win prizes!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          <strong>
           B.
          </strong>
          toys is excited to be the official toy partner of the Amtrak National Train Day! We love
          <strong>
           B.
          </strong>
          toys!
          <strong>
           B.
          </strong>
          toys will be showincasing their awesome building block toys  the Elemenosqueeze and Stackadoos at the kids' activity stations at the festivals.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          The Elemenosqueeze and Stackadoos toys are perfect for building trains, cities, and anything else you can imagine!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left; ">
          <a href="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Elemenosqueeze_pic-1.jpg" rel="" style="" target="" title="">
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1926" style="width: 280px">
            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-1926 wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter" height="270" src="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Elemenosqueeze_pic-1.jpg" style="" title="Elemenosqueeze_pic 1" width="270"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Elemenosqueeze
            </p>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left; ">
          <a href="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Stackadoos_pic-1.jpg" rel="" style="" target="" title="">
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1927" style="width: 394px">
            <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-1927 wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter wp-caption aligncenter" height="256" src="http://www.captainfussybuckets.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Stackadoos_pic-1-1024x682.jpg" style="" title="Stackadoos_pic 1" width="384"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Stackadoos
            </p>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          <strike>
           To celebrate National Train Day,
           <strong>
            B.
           </strong>
           toys wants to give away one set of Stackadoos to one Life With Captain Fussybuckets reader! Simply enter in the Rafflecopter form below!
           <span style="color:#ff8c00;">
            Giveaway will end the night before National Train Day, on May 11, 2012 at 11:01 PM, central time.
           </span>
          </strike>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          <span style="font-size: 14px; color: rgb(255, 140, 0); ">
           Giveaway has ended and Rochel R. is the winner! Congratulations!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center; ">
          <span style="color: rgb(255, 140, 0); font-size: 14px; ">
