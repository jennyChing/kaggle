        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p>
          Rainbow Bark is a super easy, no-bake recipe that would fit in with a St. Patricks day theme (though you really could enjoy it any time of the year). Its also a recipe that kids can help out with towards the end by breaking up the cooled bark into smaller pieces. Its almost too pretty to eat!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.simplystacie.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/rainbow-bark-1.jpg">

          <div class="ERSIngredients">
           <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup Mini M&amp;M's
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1  cup broken pretzel pieces (I used small round pretzels, but small stick would be good too)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 lb. Wilton White Chocolate
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             Multi-Color nonpareil sprinkles
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Line a 9x13 baking sheet with wax paper or foil.Spread  cup of the mini M&amp;M's and the broken pretzel pieces onto the lined cookie sheet.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Melt the white chocolate in a microwave safe dish stirring every 30 seconds until melted. Let cool 1-2 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Poor the melted chocolate evenly over the cookie, M&amp;M and pretzel mixture. Spread out with a spatula if needed.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Before the mixtures 'sets' sprinkle the remaining mini M&amp;M's and the green and white sprinkles on top.Place cookie in the fridge to set.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Remove and break into smaller pieces.
            </li>
           </ol>
