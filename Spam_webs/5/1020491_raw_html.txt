         <h1 class="entry-title">
          You Are The Everlasting God
         </h1>
         <div class="post-info">
          <span class="date published time" title="2008-01-26T13:37:39+00:00">
           January 26, 2008
          </span>
          by
          <span class="author vcard">
           <span class="fn">
            <a href="http://www.wordjourney.com/author/admin/" rel="author">
             Matthew C. Keegan
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           I am fairly new to the YouTube/GodTube scene, having chosen not to spend an endless amount of time previewing uploaded videos. For the times that I am able to get to the site, I have found some truly amazing work. Plenty of comedy pieces, lots of automotive demos, and so much more.
          </p>
          <p>
           A number of Christian artists have had their songs uploaded to YouTube too. One artist whom I admire is
           <a href="http://www.lincolnbrewster.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.lincolnbrewster.com/', 'Lincoln Brewster']);" style="font-weight: bold">
            Lincoln Brewster
           </a>
           who sings Everlasting God a song that points to the majesty of our God and encourages believers to find their strength in and through Him. I like how he ends the song with a quote from Isaiah which is read by his four year old son.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you want to find out more about Charles Dean, then please visit his
           <a href="http://www.myspace.com/charlesbare" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.myspace.com/charlesbare', 'MySpace']);" style="font-weight: bold">
            MySpace
           </a>
           page for more information. You can also find a write up in
           <a href="http://www.christianitytoday.com/music/artists/lincolnbrewster.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.christianitytoday.com/music/artists/lincolnbrewster.html', 'Christianity Today']);" style="font-weight: bold">
            Christianity Today
           </a>
           that gives more information about Lincoln Brewster.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, without further adieu I bring to you Lincoln Brewster courtesy of Charles by way of GodTube:
          </p>
          <p>
           <code>
            <embed align="middle" allowscriptaccess="sameDomain" flashvars="viewkey=93b0591f6240af2b6209" height="270" name="godtube" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" quality="high" src="http://godtube.com/flvplayer.swf" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="330" wmode="transparent">
            </embed>
           </code>
          </p>
