     <h2 class="posttitle">
      Triple Chocolate Cheesecake Bars
     </h2>
     <div class="postmeta">
      <em>
       posted by
      </em>
      Shinee
      <em>
       on
      </em>
      April 13, 2015
      <div class="ccount">
       <a href="http://www.sweetandsavorybyshinee.com/triple-chocolate-cheesecake-bars/#comments">
        48

     <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
     <p>
      Creamy and rich, these triple chocolate cheesecake bars are such an eye-candy and are bound to satisfy your sweet tooth!
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Creamy and rich, these triple chocolate cheesecake bars are such an eye-candy and are bound to satisfy your sweet tooth! " class="aligncenter wp-image-6309 size-large" height="900" src="http://www.sweetandsavorybyshinee.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Chocolate-Cheesecake-Bars-3-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Oh my god, you guys, Im beyond excited to share this recipe with you today. Its anultimate treat for chocoholics, like you and me, who also happened to love cheesecake.
     </p>
     <p>
      Ladies and gents, are you ready for these
      <strong>
       stunning
      </strong>
      triple chocolate cheesecake bars? Rich and creamy white and dark chocolate cheesecake mixtures swirled over deliciously contrasting chocolate Oreo crust.Do I need to say more?
      <span id="more-6306">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      I created this recipe for
      <a href="http://foodandwineconference.com/2015-dixie-crystals-chocolate-dessert-recipe-contest/" rel="nofollow">
       Dixie Crystals Chocolate Dessert Recipe Contest 2015
      </a>
      .
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.dixiecrystals.com" rel="nofollow" title="Dixie Crystals">
        Dixie Crystals
       </a>
      </strong>
      is one of platinum sponsors of
      <a href="http://foodandwineconference.com" rel="nofollow">
       Food &amp; Wine Conference
      </a>
      , that will take place in Orlando, FL in July 17-19. Im so excited to attend this conference, as itll be my very first blogging conference! The winning dessert of the contest will be afeatured dessert at the conference. Now, howawesome is that? If mytriple chocolate cheesecake bars win, itll sure please many people. Guaranteed!
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Creamy and rich, these triple chocolate cheesecake bars are such an eye-candy and are bound to satisfy your sweet tooth! " class="aligncenter wp-image-6307 size-large" height="900" src="http://www.sweetandsavorybyshinee.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Chocolate-Cheesecake-Bars-1-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      From the look of it, it might seem a bit complicated, but rest assured it is super easy to make these cheesecake bars.
     </p>
     <p>
      Chocolate Oreo cookies are ourshortcut, basically an express ticket to perfectly crumbly, chocolate-y crust.
     </p>
     <p>
      For the filling, we will use softened brick of cream cheese. I might sound like a broken record, but its essential for perfectly smooth cheesecake filling. Always let the cream cheese soften to room temperature before moving forward.
     </p>
     <p>
      Now, for the beautiful swirled presentation, we will need to melt white and dark chocolates separately and mix them with cream cheese mixture in separate bowls. I melt the dark chocolate in a double boiler over simmering water, and the white chocolate in the microwave.
      <strong>
       Quick tip for melting white chocolate:
      </strong>
      I get way better success when I finely chop the white chocolate before microwaving. The tiny chocolate curls heat more evenly and melts quicker.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Triple chocolate cheesecake bars- step by step photo directions" class="aligncenter wp-image-6314 size-full" height="500" src="http://www.sweetandsavorybyshinee.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/triple-chocolate-cheesecake-bars.jpg" width="500"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      During thetesting, I found that making more of dark chocolate mixture creates the best-swirled presentation. When I had equal amount of dark and white cheesecake mixtures, the bars lackedthedramatic swirled look.
     </p>
     <p>
      See? Isnt that an easy-peasy dessert? Theyre totally mind-blowing delicious! My sis-in-law said death-by-chocolate cheesecake bars. They are intense, rich, creamy with nice texture contrast from the crust! A must-try, for sure.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Creamy and rich, these triple chocolate cheesecake bars are such an eye-candy and are bound to satisfy your sweet tooth! " class="aligncenter wp-image-6308 size-large" height="900" src="http://www.sweetandsavorybyshinee.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Triple-Chocolate-Cheesecake-Bars-2-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Hope you enjoyed the recipe. And wish me luck with the contest!
     </p>

      <h2 itemprop="name">
       Triple Chocolate Cheesecake Bars
      </h2>
      <div class="summary" itemprop="description" style="clear:left">
       <p>
        Creamy and rich, these triple chocolate cheesecake bars are such an eye-candy and are bound to satisfy your sweet tooth!
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="time">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Yield:
        </strong>
        <span itemprop="recipeYield">
         16
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Prep Time:
        </strong>
        <meta content="PT20M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
        20 min
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Cook Time:
        </strong>
        <meta content="PT25M" itemprop="cookTime"/>
        25 min
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Total Time:
        </strong>
        <meta content="PT45M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
        45 min
       </p>
      </div>
      <h3 id="ingredients">
       Ingredients:
      </h3>
      <div class="ingredients">
       <p>
        For Crust:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         9oz (250gr) chocolate Oreo cookies
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         5 tablespoons (70gr) unsalted butter, melted
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        For Filling:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         8oz (225gr) cream cheese, softened
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          cup (65gr) plain Greek yogurt, at room temperature
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/3 cup (65gr) sugar
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 large egg, at room temperature
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         2 tablespoons (15gr) all-purpose flour
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/8 teaspoon salt
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         4oz (115gr) bitter- or semi-sweet chocolate
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         4oz (115gr) white chocolate
        </li>
       </ul>
      </div>
      <h3 id="directions" style="clear:left">
       Directions:
      </h3>
      <div class="instructions" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
       <ol>
        <li>
         Preheat the oven to 350F (177C). Line 8in (20cm) square baking pan with a foil, leaving 2 sides hanging over the edges.
        </li>
        <li>
         In a food processor, pulse Oreo cookies until fine crumbs. In a medium bowl, mix the cookie crumbs with melted butter. Press the crumb mixture in the bottom of prepared baking pan. Set aside.
        </li>
        <li>
         In a mixing bowl with whisk attachment beat the cream cheese, Greek yogurt and sugar until smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes. Add the egg, flour, vanilla extract and salt. Mix until just combined, about a minute.
        </li>
        <li>
         In separate bowls, melt the dark and white chocolates until smooth, using a double boiler or microwave, or both (*See thenotes below).
        </li>
        <li>
         Pour about 2/3 of the cream cheese mixture into melted dark chocolate. And mix the remaining cream cheese mixture into melted white chocolate. Using a spatula, mix both mixtures until smooth.
        </li>
        <li>
         Spoon both mixtures in an alternating pattern into the cookie crust in the baking pan. Swirl the two mixtures with a toothpick, or skewer. Tap on the counter a few times to release the bubbles. Bake the cheesecake until its set on the edges and slightly wobbly in the center, for about 23-25 minutes. Cool the cheesecake to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Cut into 16 squares, before serving.
        </li>
       </ol>
      </div>
      <div class="notes">
       <p>
        To melt both chocolates simultaneously, I melted white chocolate in the microwave according to the directions on the packaging (Microwave for 30 seconds at high and stir. Then another 3o sec and stir. If needed, microwave for additional 10 sec until its nice and smooth.) I recommend to finely chop the white chocolate before microwaving, it helps to melt better and faster that way.
       </p>
       <p>
        As for the dark chocolate, I placed the chocolate pieces in a heatproof bowl and melted the chocolate over simmering water, aka double boiler.
       </p>
      </div>
