    <h1>
     Pet Odor Removal
    </h1>
    <p>
     <img alt="" class="pic-frame" height="386" src="images/dog-on-carpet.jpg" width="580"/>
     <br/>
     Pet urine is a very common  problem on oriental and other fine rugs. Understanding the molecular  construction of pet urine is an important step to removing pet  odors. Sanitation bath is a standard process in all GARC rug  cleaning procedures.
    </p>
    <p>
     While pet odor removal is  possible, most pet urine stains may be permanent color damage. Pet urine  can break down the dye in wool, silk and cotton rugs, thus causing dye  migration.After insuring the pet odor removal process, GARC will  apply a protective coating to help protect against dry, water-based, and  oil-based soils when professionally cleaned  periodically.
    </p>
