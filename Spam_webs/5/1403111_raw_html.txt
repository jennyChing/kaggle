&lt;h4&gt;Bajaj Pulsar 375 Price: Rs 1,75,000 (approx.)&lt;/h4&gt;
&lt;h4&gt;Bajaj Pulsar 375 Launch Date: January 2014&lt;/h4&gt;
&lt;h4&gt;Bajaj Pulsar 375 Feature (expected):&lt;/h4&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Floral leaf sort disc brakes&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;New DTS i engine&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Six speed gear transmission&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;375 cc displacement&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Liquid cooling engine&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Alloy wheel&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Electric start&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;4 Stroke engine&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The release date of &lt;strong&gt;Pulsar 375&lt;/strong&gt; is not yet officially announced. It is expected that it will use the new DTS i engine with a single cylinder engine of 373.2 cc with a water-cooled technology and triple spark which will produce a power of 40 Bhp and a torque of 30 Nm with a six speed transmission system. Floral leaf sort disc brakes are used in this bike with a 4 stroke engine. It will be running on alloy wheels.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Pulsar 375&lt;/strong&gt;can reach 0-100 km/h in less than 7 seconds with a top speed of 180 km/h.&lt;/p&gt;
