             <a href="http://www.kindsnacks.com/">
              KIND
             </a>
             is a brand that makes many flavors of fruit and nut bars. I had tried a couple of KIND bars in the past and really liked them. After checking out their website and seeing that they have many flavors, I wanted to try some more.
             <br/>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-vCbJ8w6mN24/UJe5J0j-UJI/AAAAAAAAFYM/5pSYSX-kWy0/s1600/dark+chocolate.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="108" rea="true" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-vCbJ8w6mN24/UJe5J0j-UJI/AAAAAAAAFYM/5pSYSX-kWy0/s320/dark+chocolate.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             The first bar that I tried was the dark chocolate cinnamon pecan. This bar was awesome. It was firmer than some other bars like this but I liked that. It only contains 5 grams of sugar which is amazing because it definitely tastes much sweeter than that. Each bar has 200 calories, 7 grams of fiber and 5 grams of protein. I found this bar to be very filling and only ate about 2/3 of it in one sitting. It was very satisfying because it was a sweet fix that was healthy.
             <br/>
             <div style="text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-eEyPtApJolA/UJe6AWVW2uI/AAAAAAAAFYU/YqpG9zqBaDo/s1600/pb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img border="0" height="108" rea="true" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-eEyPtApJolA/UJe6AWVW2uI/AAAAAAAAFYU/YqpG9zqBaDo/s320/pb.jpg" width="320"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             The other bar that I really enjoyed was the peanut butter and strawberry bar. This contains peanuts, almonds, soy crisp and strawberry pieces. It really did have that PB&amp;J flavor and was delicious. This bar has 190 calories, 4 grams of fiber, 7 grams of protein and 10 grams of sugar.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             What really impressed me about the KIND bars is that they are a lot lower in sugar than I would have expected. I love that they are full of healthy fats and are filling for a snack.
