          <h1 class="content">
           Distinctive Features of Islam in Comparison with other Religions
          </h1>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="block text" id="block-54a52c10a672ad4817000010">
        <div class="inner">
         <div class="view clearfix">
          <div class="content">
           Islam is a comprehensive religion which encompasses all the solutions of this universe. It describes complete way of life including social, economical, political and all other ways which are necessary for the living of life. Islamic education is not only helping to attain blessings of Allah but also improves the way of living
           <br/>
           the life. In this article we will try to explore the unique features of Islam which are beneficial for the living of life.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Islam is a Universal
           </b>
           <b>
            Religion
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           When we are comparing the religion of Islam with other religions then the first thing which outstand the Islam is the universality because Islam focus on betterment of whole humanity while other religions focus on specific communities. Islam is not a racist religion in fact it respects the dignity of every individual and this is proven in the last sermon (Khutba) of Holy Prophet (P.B.U.H) where they said,
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            O ye people! Allah says, O people We created you from
           </b>
           <b>
            one male and one female and made you into tribes and nations, so as to be known
           </b>
           <b>
            to one another. Verily in the sight of Allah, the most honored amongst you is
           </b>
           <b>
            the one who is most God-fearing. There is no superiority for an Arab over a
           </b>
           <b>
            non-Arab and for a non-Arab over an Arab or for the white over the black or for
           </b>
           <b>
            the black over the white except in God-consciousness.
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           This last sermon was the best speech is favor of humanity in which Allah not said only Muslims but for all human kind that no one is superior for him except those who are pious.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Naturally and Logically
           </b>
           <b>
            Authentic
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Islam is a naturally authentic religion with everything can be logically proven in The Holy Quran. If someone has any doubts about Islam then he can read The Holy Quran with its explanation to clarify any kind of myths and misconceptions. Islamic education is logically and scientifically proven and one of the examples is the Process of purification which is called wudu/ablution.
           <br/>
           It is scientifically and logically proven that if someone performs wudu on daily basis then he can save himself from different harmful disease. Wudu is a complete sequential process which every Muslim performs before offering prayer. The complete process of wudu can be learned through different sources but we recommend you to use the android application
           <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.quranreading.kidswuduseries&amp;hl=en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            <b>
             Kids Wudu Series which can help you to learn the step by step wudu
            </b>
            <b>
             procedure
            </b>
           </a>
           .
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Justice for All
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            O My slaves, I have forbidden injustice for myself and
           </b>
           <b>
            forbade it also for you.  So avoid being unjust to one another. (
           </b>
           <i>
            Sahih Muslim
           </i>
           <b>
            )
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The key feature of Islam which distinguishes it from other religions is the justice system. Islam focuses on rapid and equal justice for all. The social and economical justice of Islam is proven with the era of Holy Prophet Hazrat Umar (R.A) where everyone was given equal right regardless of religious affiliation.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Conclusion
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Islam focuses on betterment of humanity and never disrespects other religions. Individual acts of someone portrays the negativity of his own mind but it cannot be judged as the representation of whole religion therefore we should understand the core of Islam and that can only be understand by following the path of Holy Prophet (P.B.U.H) written in Holly Quran.
