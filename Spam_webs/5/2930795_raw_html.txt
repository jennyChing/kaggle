         </a>
         in Marengo, Indiana; and
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/2013/07/lincoln-boyhood-national-memorial/" rel="nofollow">
          Lincoln Boyhood National Memorial
         </a>
         in Clay, Indiana, to name a few. Out of all the places that weve visited this summer, my son loved Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park the most. This 40,000 square feet water park is beautifully enclosed in a glass structure. Its also one of the
         <strong>
          few
         </strong>
         water parks in the world that features a retractable roof!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Patoka-Lake.jpg">
          <img alt="Patoka Lake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14430" height="500" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Patoka-Lake.jpg" width="751"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         French Lick has quickly become one of my favorite places to visit! Aside from it being famously known as the hometown of Larry Bird, French Lick is small, quaint, historic, yet very beautiful and scenic, with mountains of lush green trees, old barns, rolling hills, and Patoka Lake. This weekend, thanks to
         <a href="http://www.usfamilyguide.com/" rel="nofollow">
          US Family Guide
         </a>
         and Big Splash Adventure,  I had the second opportunity to visit French Lick, Indiana. However, I was able to expand my adventure even further by being provided with overnight stay in their Double Splash room, including 4 water park passes, and a full hot breakfast. I was also given 4 tickets to Shotz, their Lazer Tag and Miniature Golf facility, and 4 tickets to French Lick Ziplines at Wilstem Ranch.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Big-Splash-Adventure-.jpg">
          <img alt="Big Splash Adventure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14426" height="1200" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Big-Splash-Adventure-.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>

        <p>
         Although my certificate covered up to 4 people, which would be me, my husband, and two of our children; my son had school the day we were scheduled for our overnight stay at the Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park &amp; Resort. Since I didnt want my son to miss school, especially during his first week, my son was with my mother while my daughter stayed with my mother-in-law. Therefore, my husband and I were about to enjoy a short, yet relaxing overnight getaway sans children  a first!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Welcome-to-Big-Splash-Adventure-Indoor-Water-Park.jpg">
          <img alt="Welcome to Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14444" height="755" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Welcome-to-Big-Splash-Adventure-Indoor-Water-Park.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Upon our arrival, we were welcomed by the welcome bear outside of Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park &amp; Resort.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Inside-Big-Splash-Adventure-Indoor-Water-Park-Resort-.jpg">
          <img alt="Inside Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park &amp; Resort" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14445" height="500" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Inside-Big-Splash-Adventure-Indoor-Water-Park-Resort-.jpg" width="755"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Inside, we were greeted by a warm, family-friendly, and welcoming tropical atmosphere.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0035.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0035" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14449" height="755" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0035.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         To our left, stood the Seaside Market, a gift shop that I made a mental memo to myself to later on check once we were settled in.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Calypso-Market.jpg">
          <img alt="Calypso Market" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14450" height="500" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Calypso-Market.jpg" width="639"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Alongside the front desk, stood the Calypso Market, which wasnt a real market, of course, but it was something to add to their family-friendly tropical atmosphere.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Front-Desk-at-Big-Splash-Adventure-Indoor-Water-Park-Resort-.jpg">
          <img alt="Front Desk at Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park &amp; Resort" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14446" height="500" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Front-Desk-at-Big-Splash-Adventure-Indoor-Water-Park-Resort-.jpg" width="518"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Big-Splash-Adventure-Wristband.jpg">
          <img alt="Big Splash Adventure Wristband" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14447" height="500" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Big-Splash-Adventure-Wristband.jpg" width="755"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The staff was just as warm and welcoming.  Although our room was ready, they needed our information (i.e. drivers license and license plate number) for verification purposes. Once this was all verified, they gave us both wristbands for guest identification, which doubled as a key to get into our room and their fitness facility.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0014.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0014" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14451" height="755" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0014.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Big-Splash-Room.jpg">
          <img alt="Big Splash Room" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14452" height="500" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Big-Splash-Room.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Considering it was just the two of us, there wasnt any sense of getting the Double Splash room, which included two queen sized beds. The Big Splash was all we needed  a comfortable and beautiful king bed. It also included a sleeper sofa, a 32 inch flat screen television, coffee maker, coffee samples, complimentary organic sample sized bath products, iron, ironing board and hair dryer, wireless internet, and wired high speed internet connection.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0047.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0047" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14454" height="755" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0047.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         By the time we got settled in, we werent up for going to the Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park until today. Instead, yesterday evening, we trekked to
         <a href="http://www.frenchlickshotz.com/" rel="nofollow">
          Shotz
         </a>
         . Shotz is a unique place for the whole family enjoy. It had been AGES since my husband and I both played miniature golf. We had a choice between 18 hole outdoor Miniature Golf Course or a 9 hole indoor Miniature Cosmic Golf Course. We opted for the first and had fantastic fun without taking our golf game seriously by not keeping score. We also enjoyed the peaceful scenery  the sound of running water from one of their seven waterfalls, large rock formations, and bridges. After completing our fun game of mini golf, we were feeling spontaneous and decided to try out a game of lazer tag for the first time ever! It was quite a workout, mentally and physically, but it was so much fun  Id definitely do it again!
        </p>
        <p>
         We were tired and worn out by the time we made it to our room, but we managed to watch a couple of episodes of
         <em>
          My Strange Addiction
         </em>
         on Netflix on my laptop. We could barely keep our eyes opened after that and was quickly lulled into a blissful slumber.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0051.jpg">
          <img alt="DSC_0051" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14455" height="755" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC_0051.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This morning, we ate a complimentary hot breakfast from their buffet at their restaurant called
         <em>
          The Boatyard
         </em>
         . The food was decent, and the perfect way to jumpstart the long day that we had ahead of us.
        </p>
        <p>
         Overall, our experience was fun and memorable, and hope to visit again! In fact, I know Ill be visiting French Lick again before the year is over since I have yet to experience ziplining at French Lick Ziplines at Wilstem Ranch.
        </p>
