       <div class="post-text">
        <p>
         I just wanted to let all you know, that I am now engaged to be married to my beautiful girlfriend, Yisha.
        </p>
        <p>
         Why am I posting this here, at a search blog?  Well, because of the way I did it.
        </p>
        <p>
         I proposed via Ask Jeeves.  I brought Yisha to my office, ask her to search on
         <a href="http://web.ask.com/web?q=yisha+tversky" target="_blank">
          her name
         </a>
         .  While she was doing that I kneeled behind her with the ring and flowers.  She typed in her name into Ask Jeeves, full name, and up came a special Smart Answer (thanks to Jim Lanzone and team) with the proposal.
        </p>
        <p>
         I don't have much time to explain it, so go to
         <a href="http://www.yishaandbarry.com/idea.php" target="_blank">
          the idea
         </a>
         page for more information.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img alt="ask-proposal.gif" height="236" src="https://www.seroundtable.com/archives/ask-proposal.gif" width="443"/>
        </center>
        <p>
         Also try
         <a href="http://web.ask.com/web?q=rustybrick+engagement" target="_blank">
          rustybrick engagement
         </a>
         for some other smart searches.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks again to Jim Lanzone, Scott Grieder, Steve Orr, Daniel Read, and the whole Ask Jeeves team!
        </p>
