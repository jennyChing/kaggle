       #1 - Shingeki no Kodomo
      </div>
      <div class="cc-publishtime">
       posted Jul.29.14 at 11:29 pm
       <br/>
      </div>
      <div class="cc-newsbody">
       <p>
        Welcome to Let's Speak English, my comic about my adventures teaching English in Japan! Ifinally made a dedicated site for it :)
       </p>
       <p>
        Shingeki no Kyojin (Known as Attack on Titan in the west) is a big media fad here at the moment. I was super surprised to learn that my fifth graders were really into it since it's a pretty violent/scary show.
       </p>
       <p>
        But by this point it's friggin everywhere. You can buy shaving razors where the ad is a bunch of the SnK kids shaving a giant's beard. It's pretty novel.
       </p>
