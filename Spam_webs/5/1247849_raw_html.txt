      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        In todays world, 24 hours in a day is just not enough. Were all so busy multi-tasking in our own worlds, that having time to read a book is almost a luxury.
        <strong>
         Enter audiobooks.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        You can listen to audiobooks anytime, anywhere. While jogging, working, driving On your smartphone, laptop, tablet The format makes reading books easy-peasy, and often free. Needless to say, we love audiobooks.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.supermoney.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/vladstudio_googlelibrary_1024x768_signed.jpg">
         <img alt="Free Audio Books for the Self-Motivated" border="0" height="364" src="http://www.supermoney.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/vladstudio_googlelibrary_1024x768_signed_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="Free Audio Books for the Self-Motivated" width="480"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Today, we have for you a huge collection of amazing audiobooks that are available free to download. So go grab them today, and share the wisdom!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.supermoney.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/The_Evolution_of-an_Entrepreneur_Jack_Nadel_Veteran_Business_Author-e1373381616951.png">
         <img alt="The_Evolution_of-an_Entrepreneur_Jack_Nadel" border="0" height="214" src="http://www.supermoney.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/The_Evolution_of-an_Entrepreneur_Jack_Nadel_Veteran_Business_Author-e1373381616951_thumb.png" style="background-image: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="Free Audio Book The_Evolution_of-an_Entrepreneur_Jack_Nadel" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        1.
        <a href="http://www.bookrix.com/_ebook-jack-nadel-the-evolution-of-an-entrepreneur/">
         The Evolution of An Entrepreneur  Jack Nadel
        </a>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.supermoney.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/artofwar.jpg">
         <img alt="artofwar" border="0" height="415" src="http://www.supermoney.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/artofwar_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="artofwar" width="271"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        2.
        <a href="http://www.librophile.com/audiobook.html#!/summary/facbb5ff358d348f9ff79315b2a444af/Art of War, The (version 2)">
         Art of War  Sun Tzu
        </a>
       </h2>
       <h2>
        3.
        <a href="http://www.bookrix.com/_ebook-dr-daniel-crosby-risk-management-ebook/">
         Risk Management  Dr. Daniel Crosby
        </a>
       </h2>
