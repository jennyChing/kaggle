        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Valentines Day Tea Giveaway
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-04T15:20:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 4, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.pbfingers.com/author/peanutbutterfingers/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Julie
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/2015/02/04/valentines-day-tea-giveaway/#comments">
           1,932 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p id="top">
        </p>
        <p>
         Its time to heat things up around here!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celestial-Seasonings-Valentines-Day-Tea.jpg">
          <img alt="Celestial Seasonings Valentine's Day Tea" border="0" height="633" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celestial-Seasonings-Valentines-Day-Tea_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Celestial Seasonings Valentine's Day Tea" width="456"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Grab a hot mug of tea and settle in because I have a warm and festive giveaway coming your way this afternoon brought to you by this cuddly guy:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celestial-Seasonings-Sleepytime-Bear.jpg">
          <img alt="Celestial Seasonings Sleepytime Bear" border="0" height="646" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celestial-Seasonings-Sleepytime-Bear_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Celestial Seasonings Sleepytime Bear" width="411"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Celestial Seasonings Sleepytime Bear! He visits our house on an almost-nightly basis when Ryan and I make a hot mug of Sleepytime tea before bed. Love that stuff!
        </p>
        <p>
         But todays giveaway isnt focusing on our beloved Sleepytime tea Theres something new out there from the Celestial Seasonings family and I must say Im a big-time fan.
        </p>
        <p>
         In January of this year,
         <a href="http://www.celestialseasonings.com/" target="_blank">
          Celestial Seasonings
         </a>
         launched three new indulgent teas in keepsake gift tins that are being sold exclusively at Target stores nationwide. The teas will only be available for a limited time and come in three Valentines Day-friendly flavors:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <font color="#ca3c51" size="4">
            Red Velvet Rooibos Tea
           </font>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Red-Velvet-Tea.jpg">
          <img alt="Red Velvet Tea" border="0" height="405" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Red-Velvet-Tea_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Red Velvet Tea" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          A satisfying tea that lets you have your cake and drink it, too!
         </em>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <font color="#ca3c51" size="4">
            Strawberry Champagne Black Tea
           </font>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Strawberry-Champagne-Tea.jpg">
          <img alt="Strawberry Champagne Tea" border="0" height="395" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Strawberry-Champagne-Tea_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Strawberry Champagne Tea" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          A sweet, fruity tea reminiscent of champagne, but without the bubbles!
         </em>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <font color="#ca3c51" size="4">
            Chocolate Caramel Herbal Tea
           </font>
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/chocolate-caramel-tea.jpg">
          <img alt="chocolate caramel tea" border="0" height="395" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/chocolate-caramel-tea_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="chocolate caramel tea" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          A sultry swirl of salted caramel and chocolate flavors thats sure to satisfy any sweet tooth!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive been regularly making myself a hot mug of tea to sip in the late afternoon for years and last month, I began working the three new limited-edition flavors into my tea rotation. I adore all three and find each one flavorful, comforting and almost dessert-like! The strawberry champagne flavor is my favorite, thanks to the wonderfully fruity aroma and authentic strawberry flavor. Its also fantastic served over ice!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Strawberry-Champagne-Tea-Celestial-Seasonings.jpg">
          <img alt="Strawberry Champagne Tea Celestial Seasonings" border="0" height="395" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Strawberry-Champagne-Tea-Celestial-Seasonings_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Strawberry Champagne Tea Celestial Seasonings" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive enjoyed adding milk to the Chocolate Caramel Herbal tea to make a Chocolate Caramel Tea Latte that feels like a little mid-day treat. Highly recommended! (Just heat up some milk and froth with a frother before pouring it on top of a hot mug of tea. Simple as that!)
        </p>
        <p>
         The keepsake tins that the teas come in at Target are also absolutely adorable!! I have been using them for makeup and office supplies. I love the unique designs and think the tea + tin combo would make such a fun little Valentines Day gift for the tea lover in your life!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/office-supplies-tin.jpg">
          <img alt="office supplies tin" border="0" height="395" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/office-supplies-tin_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="office supplies tin" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And now here is your chance to win a lovely giveaway from the Celestial Seasonings team
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <font color="#ca3c51" size="4">
           Giveaway
          </font>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         One PBF reader will receive all three keepsake tea tins and two initial mugs: One for you and one for someone special in your life!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celestial-Seasonings-Giveaway.jpg">
          <img alt="Celestial Seasonings Giveaway" border="0" height="415" src="http://www.pbfingers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celestial-Seasonings-Giveaway_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Celestial Seasonings Giveaway" width="589"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         To enter this giveaway, please leave a comment below telling me about which new limited edition flavor you are most excited to try: Red Velvet Roobios Tea, Strawberry Champagne Black Tea or Chocolate Caramel Herbal Tea. Let me know!
        </p>
        <p>
         I will randomly select a winner on Friday morning.
        </p>
        <p>
         Good luck!
        </p>
