       <p>
        We often cover controversial but super important topics here like
        <a href="http://lovindublin.com/dublin/8-best-places-peaceful-poo-dublin">
         the best places to have a poo in peace
        </a>
        in the city and
        <a href="http://lovindublin.com/dublin/10-best-places-get-ride-dublin">
         the best place to get the ride in Dublin
        </a>
        . Important investigative journalism at its very best we say! So today we wanted to help the frisky couples amongst you who either don't have somewhere quiet to enjoy an intimate moment or just like seizing any lustfulopportunity. Here are the top 10 places to get a sneaky ride when out and about in Dublin...
       </p>
       <h3>
        10th.The Woods In Ticknock
       </h3>
       <p>
        One for the outdoorsy types amongst you. Park up and go on a quick 10 minute hike to one of the secluded spots in the woods. An ideal spot would be behind one of the large rocks that can conceal you as you admire the view of Dublin.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for : Young kids doing zip lining near the car park as they have a higher view and you don't want their parents chasing after you.
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Ticknock" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Ticknock.png?mtime=20141106111849" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Ticknock.png?mtime=20141106111849 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        9th.Swan Cinema - 2pm Midweek Showing
       </h3>
       <p>
        You'll want to get here during the day and pick the worst film you possibly can. If you are really lucky you might just be the only 2 people in there. Worst case there will be a couple of other people but it's big enough to find a quiet corner where you can figure out the best way to approach the mission.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for : Creepy student with his flashlight coming up through the aisles.
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Swan" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Swan_1.png?mtime=20141106111914" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Swan_1.png?mtime=20141106111914 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        8th. Toilets in Crackbird
       </h3>
       <p>
        This ticks so many boxes because the toilets are upstairs away from all the punters and are very spacious. With the pumping music downstairs and there is no chance anybody will be onto you. Order the bucket of chicken before you head up so as you have some much needed sustenance when you come back down beaming.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for: Other frisky couples that have the same idea!
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Crackbird" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Crackbird.png?mtime=20141106111944" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Crackbird.png?mtime=20141106111944 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        7th.Rooms By The Hour -Dublin Airport Hotel
       </h3>
       <p>
        Dublin airport probably sounds like an unlikely spot for the ride but if you need a little privacy and don't want to be forking out for a room for an entire night they rent rooms by the hour. Anywhere else in the city that would be frowned upon, but you'll blend in with the crowd hereas you go about your sneaky business.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for : Charges rising by the hour so get the business done in 59 minutes and get out as quick as you can. Romance can wait for another day.
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Dublin-airpot-hotel" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Dublin-airpot-hotel.jpg?mtime=20141106112009" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Dublin-airpot-hotel.jpg?mtime=20141106112009 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        6th.Up The Aras At Phoenix Park
       </h3>
       <p>
        One of the largest open parks in the world so we should be using it for more than jogging or walking the dog. Find a nice secluded spot and bring a blanket so when you are putting in a performance worthy of your country you can admire the Aras as well
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for : A Deer's antler poking you in a painful place.
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Screen-Shot-2014-11-06-at-10.58.58" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Screen-Shot-2014-11-06-at-10.58.58.png?mtime=20141106112038" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Screen-Shot-2014-11-06-at-10.58.58.png?mtime=20141106112038 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        5th.Chester Beatty Library
       </h3>
       <p>
        Intellectual people need to ride too and the good thing about these sorts of buildings is that the toilets and secluded aisles are often super clean and well kept. You are going to keep your emotions under control though as any noise will quickly get you caught immediately.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for : Paper cuts
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Chester" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Chester.jpg?mtime=20141106112112" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Chester.jpg?mtime=20141106112112 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        4th.Brown Thomas Changing Rooms
       </h3>
       <p>
        The key here is to grab lots of clothes giving the impression you plan to buy themand to walk into the changing rooms as if you own them with your partner there to "give you a second opinion". Time isn't going to be your friend here so get straight down to business.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for: Super helpful sales assistants popping in to offer advice or different sizes
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Screen-Shot-2014-11-06-at-11.01.21" sizes="100vw" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWideMobile/Screen-Shot-2014-11-06-at-11.01.21.png?mtime=20141106112219" srcset="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/_blogWide/Screen-Shot-2014-11-06-at-11.01.21.png?mtime=20141106112219 870w"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        3rd.The Museum Building Rooftop Trinity
       </h3>
       <p>
        Students are at it all the time with their overactive libido, spare time and alcohol drinking so you'll fit in here no matter what age you are. You could pick lots of nice places in Trinity but up on the roof of the Trinity Museum is a top spot with ample gorgeous views for you both.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for: A partner who suffers with vertigo
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Trinity" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/Trinity.jpg?mtime=20141106113059" width="800"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        2nd.The Backseat Of Your Car - Howth / Killiney Hill
       </h3>
       <p>
        You might be in the early stage of the relationship and not even have a bed to call your own but that doesn't mean your partner doesn't want to dream big. Clean up the car, push the seats as far forward as you possibly can before you pick them up and head to one of the two richest suburbs in Dublin. As you get down to business look at the sea, the big houses and dream about your future life together.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for: Bono
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="Howth" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/Howth.jpg?mtime=20141106112434" width="800"/>
       </div>
       <h3>
        1st.Disabled Toilets In Copper Face Jack's
       </h3>
       <p>
        You probably aren't going to have a long and meaningful relationship with the person if you are stooping to this level but let's brush that aside for now. Let's also forget the fact that a disabled person might need the toilets and just roll with the passion. You won't feel good about yourself after this one, but needs must.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch out for : The other couples queueing for exactly the same thing.
       </p>
       <div class="content__image content__image--">
        <img alt="196036 318367674945768 666298948 n" src="http://cloud.lovindublin.com/images/uploads/2014/11/196036_318367674945768_666298948_n.jpg?mtime=20141106112140" width="550"/>
       </div>
