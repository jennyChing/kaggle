       <div class="articleMainText">
        <p>
         Inspired by the FGR4 Typhoon fighter jet, Christopher Ward has updated their C1000 Typhoon FGR4 watch in a predominantly black color scheme. The most sophisticated aviation watch Christopher Ward has ever produced, the C1000 Typhoon FGR4 Ceramic notes a matte black ceramic case thats incredibly light, yet 7x stronger than steel. Its so strong, in fact that only a diamond can scratch it. Inside, a second titanium case housesa modified version of theETA Valjoux 7750 chronograph movement, with the backplate, crown and twin pushers all IPK black-coated titanium to match the sleek black styling. Completing the package, acharcoal black dial with SuperLuminova numerals and a high density black webbing strap both pay homage to the C1000 Typhoon FGR4s aircraft inspired design elements.
        </p>
