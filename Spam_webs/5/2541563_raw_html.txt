        <p>
         <img alt="doily cross stitch" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19311" height="700" src="http://thecraftymummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/doily-cross-stitch-700x700.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Stitching is so relaxing.
        </p>
        <p>
         After finishing my rainbow cross stitch blocks, I was keen to have another small stitching project. I was missing that little bit of relaxation at the end of each day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="cross stitch doily-001" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19312" height="699" src="http://thecraftymummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/cross-stitch-doily-001-700x699.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         So I designed a new rainbow project!
        </p>
        <p>
         This Rainbow Doily was inspired by all the crochet mandelas that I was seeing on my Instagram feed at the time. In fact I was going to call it a mandela, but in the end I feel somehow like it reminds more of a lacy doily.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="doily cross stitch rainbow-001" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19313" height="699" src="http://thecraftymummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/doily-cross-stitch-rainbow-001-700x699.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is stitched in the same colours as my other rainbow projects on a scrap of Aida I had lying around.
        </p>
        <p>
         What would you do with it now? Im thinking it will become the centre of a mini quilt, but Id love to hear your suggestions.
        </p>
