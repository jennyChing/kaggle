              </p>

              <p>
               Ginos East South Loop is located in the heart of the historic Printer's Row district, not far from museum campus.
              </p>

               Ginos East was voted a must-visit while in Chicago by TripAdvisor in 2015. Come enjoy legendary deep dish pizza at our neighborhood sports bar.
