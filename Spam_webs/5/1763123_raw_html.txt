             <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div class="firstPar">
               <p>
                Arsne Wenger has admitted that he will start next season without star striker Alexis Snchez should Chile progress to the latter stages of the Copa America in the summer.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="secondPar">
               <p>
                The tournament will be staged in Snchezs home country from June 11 to July 4 but the break in the English domestic season runs only between May 30, the date of the FA Cup final, and the start of the 2015-16 Premier League season on Aug 8.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="thirdPar">
               <p>
                <img caption="" credit="Alex James/JMP/REX Shutterstock" declared-caption="" height="387" name="NathanielChalobahreu" photographer="Alex James/JMP/REX Shutterstock" refid="3274924" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03274/NathanielChalobahr_3274924b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
                <br/>
                <strong>
                 <sub>
                  Alexis Sanchez has been a revelation for Arsenal this season
                 </sub>
                </strong>
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="fourthPar">
               <p>
                Wenger, the Arsenal manager, likes to give his players at least four weeks of summer holiday, meaning any player who reaches the final of the Copa America would not be expected to return for pre-season until the week before the league starts.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="fifthPar">
               <p>
                Arsenal also have David Ospina and Gabriel potentially involved for Colombia and Brazil respectively, and Wenger will apply the same principle should either of those nations reach the final. Fifa rules allow nations to call up players 14 days before the start of an international tournament but Wenger said that Arsenal had an agreement that players would be available for the FA Cup final.
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="body">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 
                 <a href="/sport/football/teams/arsenal/11576686/Arsenal-will-not-be-forced-into-giving-Jack-Wilshere-bumper-wage-increase-after-Manchester-City-interest.html" id="11576686">
                  Arsenal will not be forced into giving Wilshere bumper wage increase
                 </a>
                </strong>
                <br/>
                <strong>
                 
                 <a href="/sport/football/teams/arsenal/11576418/Arsene-Wenger-accuses-Jose-Mourinho-of-disrespecting-Arsenal.html" id="11576418">
                  Arsene Wenger accuses Jose Mourinho of 'disrespecting' Arsenal
                 </a>
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                Of Snchez and the Copa America, Wenger said: Thats not good news because they finish very late and if they go very far he will miss the start of the season.
               </p>
               <p>
                Wenger has blamed Arsenals failure to challenge Chelsea more strongly for the title on the clubs start to the season and he implied that
                <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/teams/arsenal/11576418/Arsene-Wenger-accuses-Jose-Mourinho-of-disrespecting-Arsenal.html" target="_blank">
                 <strong>
                  Jose Mourinho had shown him a lack of respect
                 </strong>
                </a>
                by calling his team boring for their failure to win the league since 2004.
               </p>
               <p>
                <img caption="" credit="Getty Images" declared-caption="" height="386" localcaption="Chelsea v Arsenal - Premier League...LONDON, ENGLAND - OCTOBER 05:  Managers Arsene Wenger of Arsenal and Jose Mourinho manager of Chelsea clash during the Barclays Premier League match between Chelsea and Arsenal at Stamford Bridge on October 4, 2014 in London, England.  (Photo by Shaun Botterill/Getty Images)" name="wengermourinho" photographer="Shaun Botterill" refid="3287317" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03287/wengermourinho_3287317b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
                <br/>
                <strong>
                 <sub>
                  Arsene Wenger has suggested Jose Mourinho lacks respect
                 </sub>
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/teams/arsenal/11564103/Arsenal-0-Chelsea-0-match-report-Jose-Mourinho-shuts-out-Gunners-to-edge-team-closer-to-the-title.html" target="_blank">
                 Arsenal supporters chanted Boring, boring Chelsea
                </a>
                during the goalless draw at the Emirates last Sunday, with Mourinho responding by arguing that it was far more boring to have not won the league for more than a decade. The biggest thing for a manager is to respect other managers  some people have to improve on that, said Wenger.
               </p>
               <p>
                Asked if Mourinho needed to conduct himself with more dignity, Wenger added: Everybody lives with his own internal problems and I live with mine. You can have incidents with other managers sometimes. It gets a bit heated when you fight directly with them, but in the end I believe time heals and the important thing is to respect each other as much as you can.
               </p>
               <p>
                
                <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/teams/arsenal/11570612/Arsenal-players-how-do-they-rank-by-work-rate.html">
                 <strong>
                  Who has worked hardest for Arsenal this season?
                 </strong>
                </a>
                <br/>
                <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/teams/arsenal/11575921/Arsenal-transfer-news-and-rumours-Gunners-in-advanced-talks-to-sign-Real-Madrid-goalkeeper-Iker-Casillas.html">
                 <strong>
                   Transfer Talk: Gunners in advanced talks to sign Casillas
                 </strong>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                Arsenal are 10 points behind Chelsea and Wenger agreed that contrasting injury records had perhaps been the critical difference between the teams. I think so but its easy to say, he said. It took us some time to find a good balance in the team and we dropped points because of that. We faced a big challenge because Chelsea didnt drop their level. Overall, we feel we have made progress.
               </p>
               <p>
                When you fight for the championship, you win some games because you smell you can do it. [The gap in points] doesnt always represent the value of the team. The gap is the gap and they have been more consistent.
               </p>
               <p>
                <sub>
                 <strong>
                  <img alttext="Arsenal will not be forced into giving Jack Wilshere bumper wage increase after Manchester City express interest" caption="Money matters: Man City want to lure Jack Wilshere to Etihad with bumper salary" copyright="GETTY IMAGES" declared-caption="" height="387" name="jack-wilshere" photographer="" refid="3288173" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03288/jack-wilshere_3288173b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
