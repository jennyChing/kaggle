       </a>
      </h2>
      <div id="topper">

       <p>
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9192" height="255" src="http://quartersnacks.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/mta-sucks.jpg" title="mta-sucks" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you have spent an excess of 24 hours in New York City, you are more than aware that the MTA is the worst organization in America. But beyond the common misery of rotting in a train station at 3 A.M., desperately waiting for a light to appear at the end of the tunnel for an hour (and having it turn out to be a service train that doesnt take passengers) plus everything in between (service interruptions, no weekend service, probably no service on weekdays either, no service after midnight, minimal rush hour service, tickets for taking up more than one seat, overcrowded trains, not enough trains, fare hikes, thoughts of suicide, thoughts of mass murder, etc.), the
        <b>
         ONE
        </b>
        redeeming fact of the MTA, is that it is often an inadvertent comedy show. A comedy show of the trife variety.
       </p>
       <p>
        Another week, another crazy subway video. This week, its some guy licking his shoe for a few minutes. Next week, it will be something else. Heres a look back at some of the MTAs trifer moments throughout the past year. Please weigh in on the trifest one of all.
