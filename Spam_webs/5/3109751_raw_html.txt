          <p>
           Corporate identity influences the success of any business, with unique, distinctive brands leading by example across all sectors. With 93% of all human communication being non-verbal, theres a lot to be said for the power of visuals - and the benefits that come with a commanding brand.
          </p>

         <!-- Item fulltext -->
         <div class="itemFullText">
          <p>
           Today were challenging the misconception that brand identity and logo design are synonymous. Many branding agencies may find it easier to sell their services by presenting increased sales as the product of your business and their logo design combined  but as the professional landscape changes, so too does the recipe for success.
          </p>
          <p>
           And today a brand identity equals logo design plus a whole lot more.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Building a Brand
          </h3>
          <p>
           Logo design alone does not make a cohesive, communicative brand - and many businesses have the false impression that a professionally designed logo constitutes a brand identity overhaul.
          </p>
          <p>
           As one component of a fully formed, aesthetically airtight brand identity - logo design has the potential to communicate your brand values from the forefront. However, the downfall of many enterprises is the assumption that logo design  of even the most superlative quality  constitutes, rather than contributes to, a memorable brand.
          </p>
          <p>
           Plastering an incongruous logo on to the face of a business at best offers a short term fix for a more intrinsic issue. Companies looking to reinvent themselves via new and improved brand design are often tempted to skip the brand-building and head straight to logo. But no corporate logo has the power to single-handedly build a brand  and businesses prepared to settle for conveyor belt logos are doomed to dullness.
          </p>
          <p>
           Brand management involves not only the design but the positioning and implementation of an unforgettable brand  and logo design that perfectly complements your brand identity as a whole makes standing apart in your niche infinitely easier. Rather than pinning all of your professional hopes on one very fixed and ultimately static logo design, the aim is to create a world of things which communicates your brands values while offering audiences a chance to interact and involve themselves.
          </p>
          <p>
           Traditional logo design is notoriously antisocial, with sales-driven companies focused on shouting into the void rather than engaging in a dialogue with their customers. But by considering how your brand identity as a whole expresses opinions and invites responses, your brand can become a part of the conversation without coming off like a fox in the henhouse.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Communicating Messages
          </h3>
          <p>
           A powerful corporate identity distinguishes brands from businesses, and relevant, resonant logo design is just one essential component of a magnetic brand. One of the key benefits of having your company logo designed professionally is the chance to present your business as one entity with a single, audible voice.
          </p>
          <p>
           Clichs like a picture speaks a thousand words may be tired and uninspiring at this point, but classics like this have earned their place in the mainstream consciousness. Company logo design is an ideal medium for conveying brand messages without verbal constraints, especially since advertising overkill is an instant turn-off for even the most loyal customer base.
          </p>
          <p>
           Rather than drowning your audience in a plethora of marketing messages, let your brand identity do the talking. Logo design done well embodies your businesss core values and makes a valid, unique statement - rather than contributing to the industry noise.
          </p>
          <p>
           During our
           <a href="/projects/world-aids-day-campaign-creative" title="AIDS Trust Branding">
            work with the National AIDS Trust
           </a>
           (NAT), we experienced first-hand the impact that refining and streamlining promotional messages can have. Keeping things simple doesnt just differentiate your brand in a saturated market, but also ensures that your core values arent lost in a torrent of competing messages.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Changing the Game
          </h3>
          <p>
           Every business experiences its own kind of metamorphosis. And while the journey itself varies from one company to another, the truth is that every business must be prepared to stay ahead of the industry curve  even if that means adapting and enhancing a longstanding brand image. Logo design is inextricably linked to brand identity and offers companies in a transition period the chance to draw themselves a new face.
          </p>
          <p>
           A wariness to fix what you dont think is broken is only natural - but with so many businesses held back by the nostalgia of their original image, convinced that changing direction means losing customers, it seems counter-productive to cling on to the past when an opportunity to evolve presents itself.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dated brands are condemned to monotony  and the consequences of preserving perceived brand value far outweigh the risks that come with taking your brand into a new and potentially very different phase. Thomas International had concerns about updating their brand image, cautious about losing their existing brand value while eager to move forward. Through
           <a href="/projects/thomas-international-brand-identity" title="Logo design">
            strategic logo design
           </a>
           and effective brand management, we were able to help the recruitment company evolve, retaining the brands core idea.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Logo Design + Brand Identity" height="416" src="/images/Blog%20jpgs/blog-images/Thomas.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="666"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre ready to bring your business to the forefront, finding a professional branding agency is the key. At
           <a href="/#home" title="Web Design + Branding London">
            Shaw+Skerm
           </a>
           , we understand the power of brand-building - and if youre looking for logo design that makes a statement, weve got it covered. Drop us an email to start
           <a href="/what-we-do/brand-identity#branding-agency" target="_blank" title="Brand Identity and Design">
            building your brand identity
           </a>
           today.
          </p>
