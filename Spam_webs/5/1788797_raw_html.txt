         <h1 class="post-title" data-post-id="606496" itemprop="name">
          30 Recipes for the Ultimate Valentines Day Breakfast in Bed
         </h1>
         <h4 class="post-meta clearfix">
          <div class="avatar nopin">
           <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-300 photo legacy-userphoto nopin" height="300" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/userphoto/541.jpg" width="300"/>
          </div>
          <div class="posted-on-wrapper">
           <p>
            by
            <a href="//www.brit.co/author/bkosten/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Bethany Kosten">
             <span itemprop="author">
              Bethany Kosten
             </span>
            </a>
            on
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-01-13T19:50:35+00:00" pubdate="">
             January 13, 2014
            </time>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing" style="display: none">
          </div>
         </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="post-content inner">
         <p>
          Nothing says I love you more than heartwarming fare especially when its served in bed. Valentines morning is the perfect excuse to indulge in
          <a href="//www.brit.co/100-layer-cake/" target="_blank">
           berry-infused pastries
          </a>
          and
          <a href="//www.brit.co/12-heart-treats/" target="_blank">
           heart-shaped treats
          </a>
          so delicious, youll wish every day was a special breakfast-in-bed affair. So dish up a little love for your sweetie with these 30 romantic breakfast-in-bed recipes.After all, isnt the way to anyones heart through their stomach?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607060" rel="attachment wp-att-607060" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607060 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="429" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/nutella-french-toast-645x429.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://www.quickneasyrecipes.net/nutella-french-toast-with-strawberries/" target="_blank">
           Nutella French Toast with Strawberries
          </a>
          :The only thing better than Nutella is Nutella and strawberries. These flavors compliment one another perfectly. Obsessed!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607062" rel="attachment wp-att-607062" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607062 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/bacon-645x645.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          2.
          <a href="http://www.wendyseewendydo.com/2012/02/i-heart-bacon.html" target="_blank">
           Bacon Hearts with Maple + Dijon Glaze
          </a>
          : Make this. Why? Its bacon. Your sweetie will heart you when you bring bacon to bed. Pair it with eggs and toast for more well-rounded fare. (via
          <a href="http://www.wendyseewendydo.com" target="_blank">
           Wendy See Wendy Do
          </a>
          )
          <span style="text-align: center;">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607112" rel="attachment wp-att-607112" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607112" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="967" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/double-chocolate-doughnuts-645x967.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.hungrygirlporvida.com/blog/2013/01/18/double-chocolate-doughnuts-w-chocolate-sprinkles/" target="_blank">
           Double-Chocolate Heart Doughnuts
          </a>
          : Bust out the chocolate sprinkles!Make these doughnuts to score brownie points if your sweetie is a chocaholic.(via
          <a href="http://www.hungrygirlporvida.com" target="_blank">
           Hungry Girl pro Vida
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607048" rel="attachment wp-att-607048" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607048 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="602" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Egg-in-a-Basket-645x602.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          4.
          <a href="http://www.petitekitchenesse.com/2012/01/22/egg-in-the-basket/" target="_blank">
           Valentines Day Egg in a Basket
          </a>
          : If your honey likes breakfast hearty, try this Valentines Day-style dish. It takes traditional eggs &amp; toast to a whole new level. (via
          <a href="http://www.petitekitchenesse.com" target="_blank">
           Reclaiming Provincial
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607042" rel="attachment wp-att-607042" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607042 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="653" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/french-toast-in-a-cup-645x653.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          5.
          <a href="http://prudentbaby.com/2012/01/entertaining-food/2-minute-french-toast-in-a-cup/" target="_blank">
           2-Minute French Toast in a Cup
          </a>
          : Its a cup o joe its a cup o soup, its a cup o French toast? Thats right. Put a smile on your honeys face with French toast in a cup. The genius to this recipe is that you dont have to pull out any fancy kitchen gear,
          <em>
           and
          </em>
          youre done in two minutes tops. Double score! (via
          <a href="http://prudentbaby.com" target="_blank">
           Pretty Prudent
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607183" rel="attachment wp-att-607183" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607183" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="746" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/pancake-kabobs1-645x746.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          6.
          <a href="http://www.designlovesdetail.com/2013/01/29/valentines-inspired-breakfast-recipe/" target="_blank">
           Mini Pancake Kabobs
          </a>
          : Everythings better on a stick! Heart-shaped pancakes are stacked high between layers of fluffy whipped cream and strawberry slices to create this breakfast treat. (via
          <a href="http://www.designlovesdetail.com" target="_blank">
           Design Loves Detail
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607105" rel="attachment wp-att-607105" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607105" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/cannoli-stuffed-french-toast-645x430.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          7.
          <a href="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/cannoli-stuffed-french-toast/" target="_blank">
           Cannoli Stuffed French Toast
          </a>
          : Your breakfast favorite just got a Valentines Day makeover. This has to be
          <em>
           the
          </em>
          most delicious way to eat french toast.Did we mention that mini chocolate chips are involved? To. Die. For. (via
          <a href="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com" target="_blank">
           Two Peas and Their Pod
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607061" rel="attachment wp-att-607061" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607061 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="967" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/simple-strawberry-smoothies-645x967.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          8.
          <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2011/06/simple-strawberry-smoothies.html" target="_blank">
           Simple Strawberry Smoothies
          </a>
          : Blend together three ingredients, and voil youve got breakfast! Strawberry jam is the secret to the surprisingly intense strawberry flavor. (via
          <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com" target="_blank">
           A Spicy Perspective
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-608064" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="386" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/eggs-in-clouds1.jpg" title="eggs-in-clouds" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          9.
          <a href="http://whatshouldieatforbreakfasttoday.com/post/52621670498/baked-eggs-with-bacon-and-parmesan" target="_blank">
           Eggs in Clouds
          </a>
          : Who wouldnt want to wake up to this savory surprise? Take your relationship to new heights with eggs in clouds. Delish. (via
          <a href="http://whatshouldieatforbreakfasttoday.com" target="_blank">
           What Should I Eat For Breakfast Today
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607055" rel="attachment wp-att-607055" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607055 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="969" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/baked-strawberry-doughnuts-645x969.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          10.
          <a href="http://www.cookingclassy.com/2013/02/baked-strawberry-doughnuts/" target="_blank">
           Baked Strawberry Doughnuts
          </a>
          : Fresh strawberries lend unexpected pizzazz to these sugared-pink beauties. Bake them ahead of time and drizzle on the glaze on just before serving. (via
          <a href="http://www.cookingclassy.com" target="_blank">
           Cooking Classy
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-608063" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="395" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Red-Velvet-Crepes-recipe-Taste-and-Tell-1-645x4291.jpg" title="Red-Velvet-Crepes-recipe-Taste-and-Tell-1-645x429" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          11.
          <a href="http://www.tasteandtellblog.com/red-velvet-crepes/" target="_blank">
           Red Velvet Crepes
          </a>
          : Theres something incredibly romantic about red velvet crepes. Who can resist em? (Not us!) (via
          <a href="http://www.tasteandtellblog.com" target="_blank">
           Taste and Tell
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607044" rel="attachment wp-att-607044" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607044" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="860" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/cracked-egg-pizza-645x860.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          12.
          <a href="http://www.prouditaliancook.com/2010/02/mini-cracked-egg-pizza.html" target="_blank">
           Mini Cracked Egg Pizza
          </a>
          : Put some heart into your homemade breakfast with a heart-shaped breakfast pizza (and add your sweeties favorite toppings!). We cant wait to try it! (via
          <a href="http://www.prouditaliancook.com" target="_blank">
           Proud Italian Cook
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607094" rel="attachment wp-att-607094" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607094" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="804" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/hot-choc-heart-mallows1-645x804.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          13.
          <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/333081/hot-chocolate-with-marshmallow-hearts?crlt.pid=camp.mwKCD3jqfmvY" target="_blank">
           Hot Chocolate with Marshmallow Hearts
          </a>
          : Linger in bed a bit longer with your sweetie and cozy upover a cup of hot cocoa. The mini heart-shaped mallows will melt hearts! (via
          <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com" target="_blank">
           Martha Stewart
          </a>
          )
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="//www.brit.co/?attachment_id=607049" rel="attachment wp-att-607049" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-607049 aligncenter" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="429" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/cinnamon-rolls_hearth-magazine1-645x429.jpg" width="645"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          14.
          <a href="http://www.hearthmagazine.com/tin-can-cinnamon-rolls/" target="_blank">

          : Not-your-average-sweet rolls, these cinnamon-infused buns are served with unexpected rustic flair. Wrap it with a ribbon to add a more personal touch. (via
          <a href="http://www.hearthmagazine.com" target="_blank">
           Hearth
          </a>
          )
         </p>
