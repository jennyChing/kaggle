      <p>
       I played a nice Dominion match recently. This is my current record up to not and will update if there is a better match in future. This match took only 7 minutes and 33 seconds. We won with 463-0. Have to mention that full enemy team was active (no disconnections or
       <a class="glossaryLink " data-tooltip="Away from keyboard." href="http://www.agnilam.com/lol-dictionary/afk/" target="_blank" title="Glossary: AFK">
        AFK
       </a>
       ). I was playing
       <a class="glossaryLink " data-tooltip="Example: Soraka and I are bot against Malphite bot and Nunu bot." href="http://www.agnilam.com/lol-dictionary/bot/" target="_blank" title="Glossary: bot">
        bot
       </a>
       and was in control of both bot turrets. But ended up with 2 kills and 0 deaths 4 assists.
      </p>
