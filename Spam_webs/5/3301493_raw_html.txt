       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Top 10 Baby Learning Games
        </h1>
        <div class="comments-link">
         <a href="http://thetailoredmama.com/2014/06/17/top-10-baby-learning-games/#respond">
          <span class="leave-reply">
           Leave a reply
          </span>

       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5459614" data-link="http://thetailoredmama.com/2014/06/17/top-10-baby-learning-games/" data-summary="" data-title="Top 10 Baby Learning Games">
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Disclaimer: Affiliate links may be used in this post.
         </em>
         <a href="http://thetailoredmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/LearningGamess.jpg">
          <img alt="baby learning games" class="wp-image-649 size-large aligncenter" height="862" src="http://thetailoredmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/LearningGamess-689x1024.jpg" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Babies are ever-changing in their interests, arent they? It seems like Ive got a hundred toys that I rotate for my daughter, and she usually only stays interested for an hour or so. Ive recently figured out that she likes things that are much more engaging than a ball or a stuffed animal. She wants something that grabs her attention and keeps it, rather than just sits there. This list is a perfect compilation of baby learning games and toys that your little will enjoy for days!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Lamaze-Play-Freddie-Firefly-Along/dp/B000I2Q0F4/ref=sr_1_4?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403054163&amp;sr=8-4&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Lamaze Play &amp; Grow Firefly
          </a>
          
         </strong>
         Audrey has and LOVES this toy! She got it around 6 months old and still plays with it at 9 months old! Its one of the best toys we own and it was perfect for dinner entertainment. Whenever we go out and shes in the high chair, its the go to toy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Fisher-Price-Brilliant-Basics-Babys-Blocks/dp/B0089W1IGG/ref=sr_1_6?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403054775&amp;sr=8-6&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Fisher-Price Brilliant Basics Babys First Blocks
          </a>
         </strong>
          Blocks are a great, engaging toy. I may have mentioned this before but we have a lady who comes every other week to give us activities to do with Audrey to boost her development and make sure shes on track (for Arizonans, its called Healthy Families and is provided free by the state, check it out if you have the option!) and she brings blocks a lot for Audrey. Oddly enough, even though she cant stack them (yet) she has tons of fun making music with them and looking at the different colors!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Fisher-Price-See-Say-Farmer-Says/dp/B001D0C70C/ref=sr_1_12?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403054918&amp;sr=8-12&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Fisher-Price See n Say The Farmer Says
          </a>
         </strong>
          This is one of the most exciting, engaging toys ever! I had it as a child, and Audrey loves toys like this that teach her sounds and animal noises!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Lamaze-Cloth-Book-Peek-A-Boo-Forest/dp/B0043D28B4/ref=sr_1_9?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403055213&amp;sr=8-9&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Lamaze Cloth Book
          </a>
          
         </strong>
         This toy is amazingly engaging! Its got the crinkly sound that babies love and are easily entertained by!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Earlyears-Roll-Swirl-Ball-Ramp/dp/B004IZWQYQ/ref=sr_1_18?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403056721&amp;sr=8-18&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Earlyears Roll n Swirl Ball Ramp
          </a>
         </strong>
          I (and Audrey, because ya know, thats who matters here) love this toy! Its great for entertainment purposes and keeps them entertained for longer than a few seconds or minutes. Its also a great teacher of the cause and effect concept so it makes for one of our favorite baby learning games!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          6.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Baby-Einstein-Sea-Dreams-Soother/dp/B006YBHE6M/ref=sr_1_23?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403056877&amp;sr=8-23&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Baby Einstein Sea Dreams Soother
          </a>
         </strong>
          Not only is this extremely engaging, but its calming to baby! It also fits universally to any crib which is an added bonus!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          7.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/VTech-Touch-Swipe-Baby-Phone/dp/B00CS16EJU/ref=sr_1_31?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403057060&amp;sr=8-31&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           VTech Touch and Swipe Baby Phone
          </a>
         </strong>
          This is such an adorable toy for babies! Be careful with this one because they might learn how to unlock your real phone though!  Otherwise its a perfect addition to your rotation of baby learning games!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          8.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/VTech-80-027501-Rhyme-Discover-Book/dp/B00FEJPCNC/ref=sr_1_26?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403057181&amp;sr=8-26&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Rhyme &amp; Discover Book
          </a>
         </strong>
          This one is pretty self explanatory as far as baby learning games go. They learn rhymes and get to flip through this fun toy for all kinds of learning! Audrey really enjoys this one! The lights are engaging and there are super fun sing along songs which are awesome!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          9.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Fisher-Price-Laugh-Learn-Crawl-Around/dp/B00CS3XAAY/ref=sr_1_39?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403057508&amp;sr=8-39&amp;keywords=baby+toys">
           Fisher-Price Laugh and Learn Crawl Around Car
          </a>
         </strong>
          This one is a bit pricy, but if you can swing it, definitely worth it! I absolutely love the part where they can put the shapes through their respective holes. It also allows them to crawl in and be super interactive! One of the best baby learning games around, for sure.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          10.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Fisher-Price-Brilliant-Basics-Stack-Roll/dp/B000LSZVJQ/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1403057780&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=baby+stackable+bowls">
           Fisher-Price Brilliant Basics Stack &amp; Roll Cups
          </a>
         </strong>
          Audrey got the pleasure recently of trying these at her friends house and she LOVED them! Between learning about stacking, colors, and being able to bang them together to make noise, it was super engaging and she learned a ton by using them. One of our favorites, for sure, and were buying them ASAP!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
