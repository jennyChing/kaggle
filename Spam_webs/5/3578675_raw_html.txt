             <h4>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               We present you the natural way, face masks (facial mask) for a shiny face skin !
              </span>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               <img alt="Face masks, Facelifting" class="alignnone wp-image-169 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/healthyss.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/images_articles_tips_and_tricks_Skincare_facemasks_facemasks_big.png?resize=600%2C600"/>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Below you will find the easy way to do it your self face masks.
             </p>
             <div class="advads-3" id="advads-1425325368" style="float: left; margin-top: 15px; margin-right: 35px; margin-bottom: 15px; margin-left: 1px; ">
              <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

               (adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({});

             </div>
             <p>
              <strong>
               <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #3366ff;">
                 Pumpkin and yogurt
               </span>
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Ingredients: Yogurt, boiled pumpkin, honey, olive oil and cinnamon
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Preparation: Mix 2 teaspoons of yogurt with 100 grams of cooked pumpkin, half a teaspoon of honey, half a teaspoon of olive oil and a little cinnamon on the top of the teaspoon.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Apply the mask on previously nice cleansed face and leave on for 10 minutes. Wash your face with water and enjoy in the beautiful skin. Calcium, zinc and vitamin B from the yoghurt and beta carotene from the pumpkin perfectly repairs skin and cinnamon improves blood circulation in the skin. Your face will shine.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="color: #3366ff;">
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                  Banana and sour cream
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Mash half a banana and mix it with a spoonful of sour cream. On clean face, apply the previously prepared mask and leave it to stay for half an hour (30 minutes). Wash your face with warm water and dry thoroughly with a cloth.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="color: #3366ff;">
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                  Mask of banana and honey
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Mash half a banana and add a spoonful of honey. Mix and apply it on the face and neck that are previously washed and remained wet. Leave it to act for 15 minutes, and then rinse the face gently with water.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="color: #3366ff;">
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                  Mask of banana and milk
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Mash half a banana and add tablespoon of milk. Mix the mixture and apply it on your face. Leave to act for 15-20 minutes. Then rinse with warm water.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               <span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #3366ff;">
                 Bonus mask from orange
               </span>
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
               Cut the orange in half. Drain the first half and mix it with brown sugar and olive oil to get a thick paste. With the other half of the orange go through the dry parts of the face, rubbing a few minutes. Then apply the mixture of orange, sugar and oil on the face and gently rub your face. Rinse your face with warm water.
              </span>
             </p>
