           <h1 class="story-body__h1">
            Happiness on the rise globally, survey finds
           </h1>
           <div class="story-body__mini-info-list-and-share">
            <ul class="mini-info-list">
             <li class="mini-info-list__item">
              <div class="date date--v2" data-datetime="30 December 2014" data-seconds="1419954036">
               30 December 2014
              </div>
             </li>
             <li class="mini-info-list__item">
              <span class="mini-info-list__section-desc off-screen">
               From the section
              </span>
              <a class="mini-info-list__section" href="/news/world">
               World
              </a>
             </li>
            </ul>
           </div>
           <div class="story-body__inner" property="articleBody">
            <figure class="lead media-landscape full-width has-caption">
             <img alt="A handout picture taken by Prince Harry shows Lesothan children posing for a photograph at a herd boy school supported in Mokhotlong, Lesotho (December 2014)" class="js-image-replace" height="351" src="http://ichef-1.bbci.co.uk/news/624/media/images/79978000/jpg/_79978871_79978870.jpg" width="624"/>
             <figcaption class="media-caption">
              <span class="media-caption__text">
               Respondents in Africa were the happiest and most optimistic about the new year
              </span>
             </figcaption>
            </figure>
            <p class="story-body__introduction">
             Happiness is on the rise globally, according to an end-of-year survey of 64,000 people in 65 countries.
            </p>
            <p>
             The market research and polling organisation
             <a class="story-body__link-external" href="(http://www.wingia.com/en/services/end_of_year_survey_2014/global_results/8/45/)">
              WIN/Gallup found
             </a>
             that 70% of respondents were content with their life - a 10% increase from last year.
            </p>
            <p>
             Fiji was the happiest nation, with 93% of residents expressing contentment, and Iraq the least happy with 31%.
            </p>
            <p>
             The survey found that Africa was the happiest region, with 83% of people saying they were happy or very happy.
