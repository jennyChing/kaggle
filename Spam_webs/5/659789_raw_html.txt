      </p>

       <strong>

       </strong>

One can give a 100% assurance that you cant find a heaven like Australia anywhere in the world, One in four of Australia's 22 million people were born outside Australia. Over 26.8% of Australia's population was born overseas, making it the most immigrant friendly nation on earth.
      </p>

       Seven seas Edutech provides services which assist you in study abroad, visa and other immigration related services. Our team members and immigration agents will completely assist you to get the Australian Immigration visa with most comfortable manner without any hassle.

       Apart from these if you require accommodations of your own choice, our agents also helps you get one. But it is also important to select under which category you want to apply for the visa. Some of the categories are listed;
      </p>
      <h3>
       <a href="/immigration/australia-business-visa.php">
        Australia Permanent residency visa
       </a>
       :
      </h3>
      <p>
       Holder of permanent residency visas may remain in Australia indefinitely,A 5-year initial visa is granted alongside the permanent residency. Until the initial visa expires, the visa holder may leave and re-enter Australia freely. A notable feature of the permanent residency visa is that, even after the initial visa expires, the holder is entitled to stay in Australia indefinitely without breaching immigration regulations. Permanent residents enjoy many of the rights and privileges of citizens, including access to free or subsidised legal and health services. They do not have the right to vote in federal elections.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189)
       </strong>
       <br/>
       The "General Skilled Migration Program" is mainly for skilled migrants, This visa is for points-tested skilled workers who are not sponsored by an employer or family member or nominated by a state or territory government, It allows you to live and work in Australia as a permanent resident.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)
       </strong>
       <br/>
       This visa is for points-tested skilled workers who are nominated by a state or territory government agency,
  It allows you to live and work in Australia as a permanent resident.
       <br/>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <a href="/immigration/australia-business-visa.php">
        Australia Business Visa
       </a>
       :
      </h3>
