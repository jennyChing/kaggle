         <p>
          Google has been identified as the most visited site of 2011 in US, with Facebook coming second, according to the survey conducted by Nielsen
         </p>
         <p>
          This survey shows that more than 153 million visitors clicked on Google branded pages each month of year 2011 whereas 138 million users visited Facebook.
         </p>
         <p>
          Yahoo attracted 130 million visitors each month and came third, the BBC report warned that if youth continued to turn away from web-based email then yahoos tally might be at risk.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.techieapps.com/google-beats-facebook-and-identified-as-the-most-visited-site-of-2011-in-us/google-vs-facebook/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-outbond', 'http://www.techieapps.com/google-beats-facebook-and-identified-as-the-most-visited-site-of-2011-in-us/google-vs-facebook/', '']);" rel="attachment wp-att-5208">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5208" height="259" src="http://www.techieapps.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/google-vs-facebook-350x259.jpg" title="" width="350"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The survey has been carried out with the data collected between January and October, visits from home and work computers are included in the final list. It involved a sample from a global panel of 200,000 people.
         </p>
         <p>
          Although Google left Facebook behind in the most popular web brand race but its social network Google+ came far behind from Facebook in Nielsens ranking of the most popular social networks.
         </p>
         <p>
          Google+ came eighth with 8.02m unique monthly visitors in the most popular social networks list.
         </p>
         <p>
          YouTube has been recognized as the most popular site for online videos which attracted more than three times the number of monthly visitors as the music video service Vevo.
         </p>
         <p>
          The survey carried out by Nielsen prepared a list for the number of visitors to various websites:
