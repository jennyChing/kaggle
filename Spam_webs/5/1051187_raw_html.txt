      <h1 class="omc-post-heading-standard">
       My TROFAST studio
      </h1>
      <div class="pw-widget pw-counter-horizontal pw-horizontal" pw:="" pw:image="" pw:url="http://www.ikeahackers.net/2013/06/my-trofast-studio.html">
       <a class="pw-button-facebook pw-look-native">
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="pw-button-googleplus pw-look-native">
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="pw-button-pinterest pw-look-native">
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="pw-button-post-share">
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="pw-button-twitter pw-look-native">
       </a>
      </div>
      <div>
       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-yHTZWOf4xvk/Ube83laZojI/AAAAAAABE0Y/g5k_9KfOt6A/s1600/panchina-734599.jpg">
        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-yHTZWOf4xvk/Ube83laZojI/AAAAAAABE0Y/g5k_9KfOt6A/s640/panchina-734599.jpg" width="550"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <p>
       <b>
        Materials:
       </b>
       Trofast boxes, jig saw, sand paper, screws
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Description:
       </b>
       Ikeas toys storage boxes were used both to cover the ceiling, and to create the libraries.  With a jig saw I cut the bottom of the boxes which were then fixed to the wall, alternating with closed  boxes with LEDs inside.  I fixed to the ceiling the smaller boxes over adhesive strips of LEDs for a very effective ceiling light!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a name="more">
       </a>
      </p>
      <div>
       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/--JXRtNq7C1w/Ube84unwn6I/AAAAAAABE04/tonNfwOXsYA/s1600/taglio%2Be%2Bmontaggio-738269.jpg">
        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/--JXRtNq7C1w/Ube84unwn6I/AAAAAAABE04/tonNfwOXsYA/s640/taglio%2Be%2Bmontaggio-738269.jpg" width="550"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <div>
       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-jAr5ejvovlc/Ube83WLdstI/AAAAAAABE0Q/j6DmGuJonUQ/s1600/scatole-733092.jpg">
        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-jAr5ejvovlc/Ube83WLdstI/AAAAAAABE0Q/j6DmGuJonUQ/s640/scatole-733092.jpg" width="550"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <div>
       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-bq6vbRNRYgw/Ube831AHgOI/AAAAAAABE0k/ji1x8fIYdaE/s1600/visuale-735812.jpg">
        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-bq6vbRNRYgw/Ube831AHgOI/AAAAAAABE0k/ji1x8fIYdaE/s640/visuale-735812.jpg" width="550"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <div>
       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7dua_9aaNWI/Ube84XRV6pI/AAAAAAABE0w/MXf9oNLee9Q/s1600/bancone2-737004.jpg">
        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7dua_9aaNWI/Ube84XRV6pI/AAAAAAABE0w/MXf9oNLee9Q/s640/bancone2-737004.jpg" width="550"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <p>
       The desk is obtained from two old Ikea tables. On the wood I fixed the boxes, while the legs become a shelf for the glass top beneath which are placed TROFAST boxes used as drawers.
      </p>
      <p>
       See more of the
       <a href="http://mommo-design.blogspot.it/2013/06/mommo-design-studio.html">
        Trofast studio
       </a>
       .
      </p>

       ~ Rosandra, Italy
