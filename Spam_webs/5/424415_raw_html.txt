         <h1 class="post-title">
          Is Andy Bianchini the next Madeon?
         </h1>
         <!-- Go to www.addthis.com/dashboard to customize your tools -->
         <div class="addthis_jumbo_share">
         </div>
         <!-- post content -->
         <div id="mainpostcontent">
          <p>
           Im hearing a lot of complex composition in Andy Bianchinis productions that remind me of
           <a href="http://www.onlythebeat.com/tag/madeon" target="_blank">
            Madeon
           </a>
           . This is another amazing aspect of electronic dance music that continues to amaze me everyday. Biachini and Hugo (Madeon) grow up on opposite sides of the world, Andy in Brazil and Hugo in France, yet they wind up producing similar styles of edm. Music really is a universal language. And Id be interested to know if Bianchini lists Madeon as an influence.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Andy Bianchini" class="wp-image-20813 size-medium alignleft" height="300" src="http://cdn.onlythebeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Andy-Bianchini-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The track I really want to share with you today is the first track from Andys recently released EP Need You/California Breeze on Nueva Records. I heard
           <em>
            Need You
           </em>
           on Myon &amp; Shane 54s International departures and had to stop what I was doing to share this with you. Andy is a new face on the scene and with productions like this, I cant wait to see where his career takes him.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            Andy Bianchini  Need You
           </span>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <iframe frameborder="no" height="350" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/playlists/34051793&amp;color=00ADEF&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=true&amp;show_artwork=true&amp;show_comments=false&amp;show_user=false&amp;show_reposts=false" width="100%">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           Diggin around his soundcloud I found a few bootlegs and remixes that are available for Free Download and definitely worthing sharing. Let me know which ones you like best!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            Andy Bianchini  Refixes, VIPs &amp; Bootlegs [FREE DOWNLOADS]
           </span>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <iframe frameborder="no" height="350" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/playlists/31000990&amp;color=00ADEF&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=true&amp;show_artwork=true&amp;show_comments=false&amp;show_user=false&amp;show_reposts=false" width="100%">
           </iframe>
          </p>

           <span style="color: #3366ff;">

          <p>
           <iframe frameborder="no" height="166" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/tracks/147954943&amp;color=00ADEF&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=true&amp;show_artwork=true&amp;show_comments=false&amp;show_user=false&amp;show_reposts=false" width="100%">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           Nueva lists Andys
           <em>
            Need U
           </em>
           as Progressive Trance on
           <a href="https://soundcloud.com/only-the-beat">
            Soundcloud
           </a>
           , a genre we dont have a specific category for on this site, but a hastag Im seeing pop up more and more on
           <a href="https://soundcloud.com/only-the-beat">
            Soundcloud
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           What do you think about Andys sound? Do you agree with me about his Madeon-ish style? Let me know in the comments below!
