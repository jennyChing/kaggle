      <p>
       <font style="font-size:1.2em;">
        A village floats through the clouds like a mystical island in this amazing picture.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font style="font-size:1.2em;">
        Taken by amateur photographer Vittorio Poli, it shows a town emerging eerily from between the clouds in the Asiago Plateau in northern Italy.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font style="font-size: 1.2em;">
        Mr Poli, 31, who lives in the area, was returning from a trip up a nearby mountain at dusk when the mist came rolling in.
       </font>
      </p>

        'It was about 5.15pm and I was still about 1,000 metres up when my eyes were caught by a sea of fog that was slowly enveloping my little country, like a floating island.
