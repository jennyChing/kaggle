                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     From Pinterest to Real Life - How to Recreate a Pinterest Look in Real Life
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-428209927644527864" itemprop="articleBody">
                     Who else gets inspired by great outfit ideas on Pinterest? Who else gets disappointed knowing that those great skinny pieces all laid out on the white background will never look that great on your curvy body? Stinks - right? But there's hope. Use the inspiration you find on Pinterest to create your own looks.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-bqgHAhLMnXs/VLZeh3vy5LI/AAAAAAAAJqc/Qn4iBkemihU/s1600/From%2BPinterest%2BTo%2BReal%2BLife.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="From Pinterest to Real Life - How to Recreate a Pinterest Look in Real Life" border="0" height="400" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-bqgHAhLMnXs/VLZeh3vy5LI/AAAAAAAAJqc/Qn4iBkemihU/s1600/From%2BPinterest%2BTo%2BReal%2BLife.jpg" title="From Pinterest to Real Life - How to Recreate a Pinterest Look in Real Life | My Scraps" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     Say what, you may be asking. . .take a Pinterest look you love and take a long hard look at it. What about it do you love? Is it the color palette? Is it the accessories? Once you identify the main thing drawing you in, think about what you own that is similar. Need something? Add it to your shopping list for next time you're kicking up your wardrobe or there's a great sale at your favorite store.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Don't get me wrong, I love to shop and I wish I had the budget to recreate all those great Pinterest looks. But my reality is that I have to do the best I can with what I have, and be smart about adding new pieces to my wardrobe. Then I can indulge in the occasional splurge and spoiling of myself.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     So I wanted to share how I put these tips into action for myself. Here's a link to a
                     <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/69102175506639530/" target="_blank">
