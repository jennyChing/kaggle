        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         #Hashtag Instagram Printables {for Project Life}
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2012-11-09T19:15:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 9, 2012
         </time>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://sisterssuitcaseblog.com/2012/11/hashtag-printable-cards-for-project-life.html#comments">
           17 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Holly &amp; Nat
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         Are you lovin Instagram like we are? Im addicted. I could spend hours looking at random peoples pictures. (Weird, huh?) Really though, its been so fun to get to know fellow bloggers and friends through this fun app. We cant wait to meet some of our new friends at SNAP in April!
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-xPAGaDd1NTo/UJ1Jr-SCc7I/AAAAAAAAGQA/5BfCH8WuT_o/s1600/hashtag_journaling_cards.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-xPAGaDd1NTo/UJ1Jr-SCc7I/AAAAAAAAGQA/5BfCH8WuT_o/s640/hashtag_journaling_cards.jpg" width="442"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         One of my favorite parts of Instagram? Hashtags. #iloveagoodhashtag
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         So I whipped up some new printables that are #instagood. Okay, I cant help it.
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-FVmcCl_WAc0/UJ1JltYdaeI/AAAAAAAAGP4/eM3kl_nJw6U/s1600/free_printable_cards.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="442" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-FVmcCl_WAc0/UJ1JltYdaeI/AAAAAAAAGP4/eM3kl_nJw6U/s640/free_printable_cards.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Click through to get these fun printable cards!
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a name="more">
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          Get the free printable PDF
         </span>
         <a href="https://docs.google.com/open?id=0B7u0z9INCiFCeC02R2JVbldVMEU" target="_blank">
          <span style="font-size: large;">
           here
          </span>
         </a>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          !
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         All I did was trim down an Instagram pic and adhere it to the card.Need help getting your photos off of your phone? Check out our tips here:
         <a href="http://www.sisterssuitcaseblog.com/2012/08/how-to-print-phone-instagram-pics-for.html" target="_blank">
          How to Print Instagram Photos
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.sisterssuitcaseblog.com/2012/08/how-to-print-phone-instagram-pics-for.html" target="_blank">
         </a>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ODJWmNgvjUk/UJ1J8xIPSLI/AAAAAAAAGQY/mFqxfSzVVRk/s1600/ilovemyjob_hashtag_card_example.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="442" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ODJWmNgvjUk/UJ1J8xIPSLI/AAAAAAAAGQY/mFqxfSzVVRk/s640/ilovemyjob_hashtag_card_example.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         You can also add a little flair with some Washi tape!
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-wxB7YJQEBvA/UJ1KCoJZu_I/AAAAAAAAGQg/fW6kHtPrtpQ/s1600/instagood_hashtag_card.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-wxB7YJQEBvA/UJ1KCoJZu_I/AAAAAAAAGQg/fW6kHtPrtpQ/s640/instagood_hashtag_card.jpg" width="442"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         The Hashtag cards were designed for my
         <a href="http://www.sisterssuitcaseblog.com/2012/08/what-is-project-life.html" target="_blank">
          Project Life
         </a>
         album. They are 34 inches and fit perfectly in the standard
         <a href="http://astore.amazon.com/mysisssui-20/detail/B005TYRHIA">
          Design A Page Protectors
         </a>
         . (And a bunch of other sizes.)
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Wru4W8Y9RnM/UJ1KPHyHtAI/AAAAAAAAGQ0/HqFb5vY-PFk/s1600/prject_life_page_example.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="442" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Wru4W8Y9RnM/UJ1KPHyHtAI/AAAAAAAAGQ0/HqFb5vY-PFk/s640/prject_life_page_example.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         I rounded the corners of the cards after printing and trimming. All you professional scrappers out there, I know my corner rounder is prehistoric.
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-mb_d0aIjc6I/UJ1J20z4e8I/AAAAAAAAGQQ/9gVNw_3psSg/s1600/how_to_trim_cards.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="442" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-mb_d0aIjc6I/UJ1J20z4e8I/AAAAAAAAGQQ/9gVNw_3psSg/s640/how_to_trim_cards.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Not a scrapbooker? You can also use these cards as a fun way to display your favorite Instagram pics!
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-HN5vUuCgMv0/UJ1Jxd3FZ4I/AAAAAAAAGQI/2XOq3aIbStw/s1600/hashtag_project_life_cards.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img border="0" height="442" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-HN5vUuCgMv0/UJ1Jxd3FZ4I/AAAAAAAAGQI/2XOq3aIbStw/s640/hashtag_project_life_cards.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </div>
