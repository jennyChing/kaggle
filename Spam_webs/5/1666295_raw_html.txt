        <div class="entry-category-idea entry-category-small_business entry-category-visitor_pass entry-author-leighann entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea73e43970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           Visitor Pass: Product Packaging Tips [Katie Kukulka]
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <div>
             <strong>
              <em>
              </em>
             </strong>
             <div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889afe9f970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="Katie" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889afe9f970d" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889afe9f970d-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
                Hi  Freckled Nest readers! I'm Katie of
                <a href="http://www.igobykatie.com/" target="_blank">
                 I go by Katie
                </a>
                &amp;
                <a href="http://darlingtonia.bigcartel.com/" target="_blank">
                 Darlingtonia  Moccasin Company
                </a>
                &amp; today I am here to share some custom packaging  tips for all of you online handmade entrepreneurs out there!
               </span>
              </div>
             </div>
             <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                One  of the greatest things about being a handmade business owner is having  the ability to deliver really amazing &amp; creative products to  customers. Why not take that creativity &amp; make your packaging  really shine? After all, your packaging is the first thing your customer  physically interacts with so make that first impression a lasting one!  Here are a few tips to inspire you...
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a2e11970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
                 <img alt="Katie_Packaging5" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a2e11970c" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a2e11970c-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging5"/>
                </a>
                <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889acebf970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
                 <img alt="Katie_Packaging1" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889acebf970d" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889acebf970d-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging1"/>
                </a>
               </div>
              </div>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #ffffff;">
              .
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
               Custom Packaging 
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                Customizing  the outside of your shipping boxes as well as your interior packaging  can be a really great way to really wow your customer &amp; make  them feel great about supporting your small business. I've also found  when I am in line at the post office people read my boxes &amp; ask  me where they can find what I sell!
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Rubber Stamps
                </strong>
                -These are so easy to use &amp; to get custom made, it's silly! Just head over to
                <a href="http://www.rubberstamps.net/" target="_blank">
                 Rubberstamps.net
                </a>
                &amp; order yourself one up to 6x6"!
               </div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 What Kind?
                </strong>
                -I order my packaging boxes in bulk from
                <a href="http://www.uline.com/" target="_blank">
                 uline.com
                </a>
                which  come flat &amp; are super affordable. I choose not to use USPS flat  rate boxes unless I am shipping super heavy stuff. I've found shipping  in my own boxes to be the same price if not lower.
               </div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Screen printing
                </strong>
                -I screen print all of my packaging for
                <a href="http://ww.darlingtoniamoccasins.com/" target="_blank">
                 Darlingtonia Moccasin Co
                </a>
                ,  which is a great way to embellish a stack of boxes quick. If you've  never screen printed before I would stick to using stamps or a
                <a href="http://www.diylife.com/2007/12/14/diy-definitions-print-gocco/" target="_blank">
                 print  gocco
                </a>
                for smaller packages if you've got one.
               </div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Interior Packaging
                </strong>
                -Inside  each shipping box you will likely have your final jewelry box or in my  case shoe bag. Make your interior packaging really stand out any way you  can, with custom stickers, stamps, embossed lettering (which you can do  yourself with embossing powder that you can pick up at any craft  store!), ribbon &amp; etc. When it comes to using fabric bags - I make  my own using cotton muslin I pick up by the bolt when it goes on sale  &amp; embellish those too. If you wrap your items in tissue paper make  sure it matches your branding in color &amp; add something special to  tie it all together.
               </div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Shopping Bags
                </strong>
                -  When it comes to shopping bags get creative! Dissect a store bought  bag, use the pattern &amp; make yourself some newspaper bags like  the one pictured above. Or add a little handmade touch instead of just  your business name... Like using yarn &amp; a hole punch to embroider a  monogram on the front.
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a3213970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
                 <img alt="Katie_Packaging3" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a3213970c" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a3213970c-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging3"/>
                </a>
               </div>
              </div>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
              <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea7684d970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
               <img alt="Apple2" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea7684d970b" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea7684d970b-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Apple2"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
             </span>
             <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
               Thank You Cards 
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 Thank  you cards are another way to stand out &amp; you should you always  include one with each order. Here are some neat ways to say thanks...
                </div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                  .
                 </span>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <strong>
                  Vintage postcards&amp; stationary
                 </strong>
                 -Theses  can be such fun thank you cards, especially if you write in pencil so  the customer can reuse them - it's like a freebee! I've found vintage  stationary &amp; post cards for pennies at a yard sales &amp; antique  shops.
                </div>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <strong>
                  Print your own
                 </strong>
                 -I  print my own thank you cards using my photo printer &amp; double sided  matte photo paper. It can be super affordable if you've got the right  paper (check out
                 <a href="http://www.redriverpaper.com/" target="_blank">
                  redriverpaper.com
                 </a>
                 - awesome &amp; cheap). I also include a little reminder of my policies too.
                </div>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: left;">
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <strong>
                  Custom Post Cards
                 </strong>
                 -  pick yourself up some promotional post cards to use as thank you cards.  There are tons of sites that offer custom printing services like
                 <a href="http://www.vistaprint.com/" target="_blank">
                  vistaprint.com
                 </a>
                 ,
                 <a href="http://www.moo.com/" target="_blank">
                  moo.com
                 </a>
                 &amp;
                 <a href="http://www.4by6.com/" target="_blank">
                  4by6.com
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                  .
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889ad248970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
                  <img alt="Katie_Packaging2" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889ad248970d-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging2"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <div style="margin: 0px; text-align: left;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px; text-align: left;">
                <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
                 Promotion
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 Put  the cherry on top of your custom packaging sundae by reminding your  customers where they found you &amp; how to find you again!
                </div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                  .
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <strong>
                  Freebies
                 </strong>
                 -  I hand out pocket mirrors with each moccasin order, they are a fun gift  &amp; promote my business at the same time. I've also seen folks hand  out a variety of really cute custom stuff.
                </div>
               </div>
               <div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <strong>
                  Business Cards
                 </strong>
                 -Have some! Weather you make them yourself with a custom stamp ;) or order a batch from
                 <a href="http://www.moo.com/" target="_blank">
                  moo.com
                 </a>
                 like I do - always include one with each order.
                </div>
                <div style="margin: 0px;">
                 <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                  .
                 </span>
                </div>
               </div>

               <img alt="Katie" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889afe9f970d" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889afe9f970d-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie"/>

              <br/>
              <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">

               </a>
               &amp; today I am here to share some custom packaging  tips for all of you online handmade entrepreneurs out there!
              </span>
             </div>
            </div>
            <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
             <div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               One  of the greatest things about being a handmade business owner is having  the ability to deliver really amazing &amp; creative products to  customers. Why not take that creativity &amp; make your packaging  really shine? After all, your packaging is the first thing your customer  physically interacts with so make that first impression a lasting one!  Here are a few tips to inspire you...
              </div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                .
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a2e11970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="Katie_Packaging5" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a2e11970c" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a2e11970c-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging5"/>
               </a>
               <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889acebf970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="Katie_Packaging1" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889acebf970d" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889acebf970d-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging1"/>
               </a>
              </div>
             </div>
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
            <span style="color: #ffffff;">
             .
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
              Custom Packaging 
            </span>
            <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
             <div style="text-align: left;">
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               Customizing  the outside of your shipping boxes as well as your interior packaging  can be a really great way to really wow your customer &amp; make  them feel great about supporting your small business. I've also found  when I am in line at the post office people read my boxes &amp; ask  me where they can find what I sell!
              </div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                .
               </span>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: left;">
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <strong>
                Rubber Stamps
               </strong>
               -These are so easy to use &amp; to get custom made, it's silly! Just head over to
               <a href="http://www.rubberstamps.net/" target="_blank">
                Rubberstamps.net
               </a>
               &amp; order yourself one up to 6x6"!
              </div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <strong>
                What Kind?
               </strong>
               -I order my packaging boxes in bulk from
               <a href="http://www.uline.com/" target="_blank">
                uline.com
               </a>
               which  come flat &amp; are super affordable. I choose not to use USPS flat  rate boxes unless I am shipping super heavy stuff. I've found shipping  in my own boxes to be the same price if not lower.
              </div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <strong>
                Screen printing
               </strong>
               -I screen print all of my packaging for
               <a href="http://ww.darlingtoniamoccasins.com/" target="_blank">
                Darlingtonia Moccasin Co
               </a>
               ,  which is a great way to embellish a stack of boxes quick. If you've  never screen printed before I would stick to using stamps or a
               <a href="http://www.diylife.com/2007/12/14/diy-definitions-print-gocco/" target="_blank">
                print  gocco
               </a>
               for smaller packages if you've got one.
              </div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <strong>
                Interior Packaging
               </strong>
               -Inside  each shipping box you will likely have your final jewelry box or in my  case shoe bag. Make your interior packaging really stand out any way you  can, with custom stickers, stamps, embossed lettering (which you can do  yourself with embossing powder that you can pick up at any craft  store!), ribbon &amp; etc. When it comes to using fabric bags - I make  my own using cotton muslin I pick up by the bolt when it goes on sale  &amp; embellish those too. If you wrap your items in tissue paper make  sure it matches your branding in color &amp; add something special to  tie it all together.
              </div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <strong>
                Shopping Bags
               </strong>
               -  When it comes to shopping bags get creative! Dissect a store bought  bag, use the pattern &amp; make yourself some newspaper bags like  the one pictured above. Or add a little handmade touch instead of just  your business name... Like using yarn &amp; a hole punch to embroider a  monogram on the front.
              </div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                .
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="margin: 0px;">
               <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a3213970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="Katie_Packaging3" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a3213970c" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b0154327a3213970c-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging3"/>
               </a>
              </div>
             </div>
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
            <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
             <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea7684d970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Apple2" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea7684d970b" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b01538ea7684d970b-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Apple2"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
              Thank You Cards 
            </span>
            <span style="font-family: trebuchet ms,geneva; font-size: 10pt;">
             <div style="text-align: center;">
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                Thank  you cards are another way to stand out &amp; you should you always  include one with each order. Here are some neat ways to say thanks...
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Vintage postcards&amp; stationary
                </strong>
                -Theses  can be such fun thank you cards, especially if you write in pencil so  the customer can reuse them - it's like a freebee! I've found vintage  stationary &amp; post cards for pennies at a yard sales &amp; antique  shops.
               </div>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Print your own
                </strong>
                -I  print my own thank you cards using my photo printer &amp; double sided  matte photo paper. It can be super affordable if you've got the right  paper (check out
                <a href="http://www.redriverpaper.com/" target="_blank">
                 redriverpaper.com
                </a>
                - awesome &amp; cheap). I also include a little reminder of my policies too.
               </div>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Custom Post Cards
                </strong>
                -  pick yourself up some promotional post cards to use as thank you cards.  There are tons of sites that offer custom printing services like
                <a href="http://www.vistaprint.com/" target="_blank">
                 vistaprint.com
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="http://www.moo.com/" target="_blank">
                 moo.com
                </a>
                &amp;
                <a href="http://www.4by6.com/" target="_blank">
                 4by6.com
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <a href="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889ad248970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
                 <img alt="Katie_Packaging2" src="http://frecklednest.typepad.com/.a/6a0120a887a2d5970b014e889ad248970d-600wi" style="width: 600px;" title="Katie_Packaging2"/>
                </a>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: center;">
              <div style="margin: 0px; text-align: left;">
               <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                .
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="margin: 0px; text-align: left;">
               <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: andale mono,times;">
                Promotion
               </span>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                Put  the cherry on top of your custom packaging sundae by reminding your  customers where they found you &amp; how to find you again!
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Freebies
                </strong>
                -  I hand out pocket mirrors with each moccasin order, they are a fun gift  &amp; promote my business at the same time. I've also seen folks hand  out a variety of really cute custom stuff.
               </div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <strong>
                 Business Cards
                </strong>
                -Have some! Weather you make them yourself with a custom stamp ;) or order a batch from
                <a href="http://www.moo.com/" target="_blank">
                 moo.com
                </a>
                like I do - always include one with each order.
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #ffffff;">
                 .
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                <em>
                 Now  that I've spilled my secrets &amp; shared some of my resources I hope I've  inspired you to come up with some of your own awesome new packaging  ideas-- now go out &amp; get creative!
                </em>
               </div>
               <div style="margin: 0px;">
                xo,
                <a href="http://www.igobykatie.com/" target="_blank">
                 Katie
