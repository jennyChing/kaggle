           Hey fellow beer drinkers, I believe we are all in agreement that warm beer, or even just sort of cold beer can be the most disgusting thing on this planet. Sure, weve drank it, and well probably do it again sometime, but not if we can help it, because now we have the Beer Freeze: Self-Chilling Beer Glass.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           This stunning and functional creation is crafted from BPA-free plastic, the glasss built-in proprietary cooling gel will ensure that your brew, no matter what kind, will stay nice and cold for those hot days. Check out the creation below and speak your mind on it after the jump. Get yours right

            here
