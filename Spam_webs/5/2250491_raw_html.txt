      <h3>
       <strong>
        <a class="post-title" href="/internet-marketing-budgets-will-increase-2015/">
         Internet marketing budgets will increase in 2015
        </a>
       </strong>
      </h3>
     </div>
     <div class="col-md-12">
      <div class="tags tags-blog">
       <hr/>
       <div class="row">
        <div class="col-md-3">
         <p>
          <span class="color-font">
           <i class="fa fa-user">
           </i>
          </span>
          <a href="/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by webadmin">
           webadmin
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-4">
         <p>
          <span class="color-font">
           <i class="fa fa-clock-o">
           </i>
          </span>
          November 24, 2014
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-3">
         <p>
          <span class="color-font">
           <i class="fa fa-folder-open">
           </i>
          </span>
          <a class="comments" href="/internet-marketing-budgets-will-increase-2015/#comments">
          </a>
          <a href="http://www.massoftind.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Blog">
           Blog
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-2 text-right">
         <p>
          <span class="color-font">
           <i class="fa fa-comments-o">
           </i>
          </span>
          <a class="comments" href="/internet-marketing-budgets-will-increase-2015/#comments">
           0
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <hr/>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="col-md-12">
      <p>
       A very recent survey report has revealed that in 2015, internet marketing budgets will increase by 8%. The survey conducted by Gartner, collected data from over 315 companies from media, retail, finance, transportation, hospitality, manufacturing, finance and technology sectors. Most of these companies have more than $500 million annual revenues. 68% of them had separate digital marketing budgets for 2014.
      </p>
      <p>
       In general, companies allocate certain percentages of their annual revenues for
       <a href="/services/digital-marketing-india/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        digital marketing
       </a>
       . But that does not mean the trend is always directly proportional. Survey findings have got examples where lower revenue generators spent more than the companies with voluminous revenue figures. Almost 30% of the companies allocated 13% of their revenues for digital marketing in 2014 but on an average it was 9% of the revenue. When surveyors probed to learn about companies plans for 2015, they found that almost every company is looking forward to make a hike in its internet marketing budget. 50% of these companies are going to increase the budgets by 10.4% next year. From among the rest, some of them are even planning to raise it by 13.6%.
      </p>
      <p>
       This increasing trend was predictable as the web has the highest potentiality across all the mediums, in terms of expansion. So, to keep pace, companies are bound to stretch themselves as much as possible to ensure greater ROI. That is why when we started in 2001, since then internet marketing had been our focus. Today we have a dedicated and industry-experienced team and a number of digital marketing packages with customization options open. We know that what is best for A will not necessarily be so for B.
      </p>
