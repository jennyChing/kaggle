          <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
           Bacon-Wrapped Honey Sriracha Chicken Bites
          </h1>
          <p class="entry-meta">
           Posted By
           <span>
            
           </span>
           <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
            <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/author/hers0108/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
             <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
              Lee Hersh
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p>
           Serve theseBacon-Wrapped Honey Sriracha Chicken Bites on a piece of apple for the most delicious, paleo-friendly appetizer that only takes 30 minutes to make!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon11.png">
            <img alt="Serve these Bacon-Wrapped Honey Sriracha Chicken Bites on a piece of apple for the most delicious, paleo-friendly appetizer that only takes 30 minutes to make!" class="alignnone wp-image-10477 size-full" height="1049" src="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon11.png" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Bacon. Sriracha. Chicken.
          </p>
          <p>
           Nuff said.
          </p>
          <p>
           This is one of those recipes that I had not planned on shooting (I usually plan out my content calendar weeks in advanced &lt;crazy, but its what works for me), but that I thought of making after having some leftover chicken breasts and chicken in the fridge. Turns out my lovely lady Lexi from
           <a href="http://lexiscleankitchen.com/">
            Lexis Clean Kitchen
           </a>
           has
           <a href="http://lexiscleankitchen.com/2014/12/26/bacon-wrapped-sriracha-lime-chicken-bites/">
            a very similarrecipe
           </a>
           . I think my mind secretly memorizes all of Lexis paleo goodness 
           <span id="more-10468">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           So these bitesWOULD BE PERFECT FOR Da Soops Bowl (apparently we arent aloud to say the actual words because its trademarkedfun fact Friday).
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon3.png">
            <img alt="Serve these Bacon-Wrapped Honey Sriracha Chicken Bites on a piece of apple for the most delicious, paleo-friendly appetizer that only takes 30 minutes to make!" class="alignnone wp-image-10474 size-full" height="950" src="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon3.png" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           These little bites of heaven are SO EASY TO MAKE!
          </p>
          <p>
           All you have to do is marinade your chicken pieces for a few minutes in your sriracha honey concoction and then roll them up in bacon pieces. Then, you pop the whole thing in the oven for just under 25 minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Boom chicka pow.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon1.png">
            <img alt="Bacon-Wrapped Honey Sriracha Chicken Bites" class="alignnone wp-image-10472 size-full" height="1048" src="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon1.png" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           You can use any kind of bacon you want. I used a paleo-friendly, center-cut bacon that doesnt have all those gross hormones and addednasties in it. Originally I thought an apple smoked bacon would have been perfect, then I decided to throw a piece of actual apple on there.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gold star.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon.png">
            <img alt="Bacon-Wrapped Honey Sriracha Chicken Bites" class="alignnone wp-image-10471 size-full" height="1004" src="http://fitfoodiefinds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/honey-sriracha-lime-chicken-bacon.png" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>

            <div class="ERSServes">
             Serves:
             <span itemprop="recipeYield">
              30 bites or about 10 servings
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSIngredients">
            <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              10 pieces of center cut bacon
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 large chicken breast, organic if possible!
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               cup sriracha
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               lemon wedge
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 teaspoons honey
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              salt and pepper to taste
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 apple
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSInstructions">
            <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              First, preheat oven to 400F. Then, line a baking sheet with tin-foil and set aside.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Next, prep marinade by mixing together sriracha, lemon juice, honey, and salt and pepper. Then, slice chicken breast up into bite-size pieces, making sure all of the pieces are relatively the same size. I got about 30 pieces out of the two breasts. Then, place the chicken pieces in the marinade and mix.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Prep bacon by slicing into thirds. You should have a total of 30 mini strips to match the 30 chicken pieces. Then, place a piece of chicken in the center of a piece of bacon and roll. Place on tin foil seam down. Repeat.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Bake at 400F for 20-25 minutes or until the bacon is at desired texture.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Serve with a thin slice of apple and a toothpick.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSNutrition nutrition" itemprop="nutrition" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/NutritionInformation">
            <div class="ERSNutritionHeader">
             Nutrition Information
            </div>
            <div class="ERSNutrionDetails">
             Serving size:
             <span itemprop="servingSize">
              3
             </span>
            </div>
