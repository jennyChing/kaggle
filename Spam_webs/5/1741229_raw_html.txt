           Cat Care Tips For The New Cat Owner
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="post-meta-author">
           <i class="fa fa-user">

          <p>
           <a href="http://www.make-it-simply-easy-life.com/cat-care-tips-new-cat-owner/">
            <img alt="cat" class="alignnone wp-image-1285 size-full" height="338" src="http://www.make-it-simply-easy-life.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/cat.jpg" title="Cat" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Cats are curious and temperamental. They can take you one day and that they love you the next. They confuse and even infuriate you at times; however, but they also bring much joy. The following article offers some excellent tips about life with your cat's confusing behavior.
          </p>
          <h4>
           Useful Cat Care Tips
          </h4>
          <p>
           Be certain your cat gets regular vet visits.Cats need routine annual check-ups and wellness checks just like people. Cats need to be seen by a vet right away if they are having any issues.
          </p>
          <p>
           You may want to have a microchip put into your cat. Even indoor cat may get out of your home at some point. A tagged collar might help get kitty home, for instance if it gets snagged in some branches. Microchips are as tiny and contain all your contact information.
          </p>
          <p>
           Cats are frequently nocturnal animals. This means nighttime is an active during the night. This will keep them from waking you up at night and from pouncing on feet under the cats away while you're trying to sleep because they won't be jumping all over you.
          </p>
          <p>
           Cats can spend hours grooming themselves and their fur clean. Hairballs are more common for long-haired cats.There are special foods which help with this problem. Some cat food brands have formulations to prevent or reduce hairballs, which will benefit you and your cat.
          </p>
          <p>
           Be considerate of your cat's ears when traveling. You may enjoy it, but they enjoy softer sounds. To ensure your cat's trip is pleasant, keep the stereo low - or perhaps off entirely.
          </p>
          <p>
           Brush your cat at regular basis. This keeps the cat's natural oils in their fur and stimulates blood flow in the skin healthy. It can also reduce the amount of loose hair that is about ready to fall out. This can keep hairballs and their associated medical problems.
          </p>
          <p>
           Consider getting your cat a special drinking fountain. Streaming water is your cat to take in.You have noticed that a dripping sink faucet quickly attracts their attention. A fountain is a more environmentally-friendly way to allow your cat to drink naturally.
          </p>
          <p>
           Is cat hair all over every surface in your home? Cats shed a ton, so it is important to brush them often. If your cat sheds often, it's important to brush then frequently. This will also help their coat from becoming tangled or matted.
          </p>
          <p>
           Take your cat for vaccinations on a regular basis to keep him healthy and strong.Your cat needs these periodic checks and parasites.
          </p>
          <p>
           Never give your cat a medication that was formulated for use by humans. If you have a sick cat it is best to take them to someone who specializes in animal sickness, you should take them to the vet and administer whatever he provides. Giving cats medication meant for humans is very dangerous and could even cause death.
          </p>
          <p>
           Cats have a very sensitive nose and notice the smallest things. Don't be discouraged if you find that your cat is not interested in these things right away.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dogs normally pant on a way to release heat from their bodies. Be sure you get in touch with a vet, particularly if your pet has suffered from respiratory issues.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you were home all the time when you first adopted your cat and are now going to be out of the home, your cat will get lonely when you go back to work. One possible solution to ending your cat's loneliness is to adopt a cat from getting lonely is to pair it up with another cat.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dry food is a good choice for cats. Kittens need wet food because it's easier to chew. As they grow older, the harder texture of dry food keeps their teeth strong.
          </p>
          <p>
           Place a sticky tape on your furniture. This will really make your cat stop scratching the furniture. There is specific tape available that is specifically designed for this purpose.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are concerned that your cat might be exposed to things such as fungus, catching fleas or being exposed to fungus, or any kind of fungus then the cat shouldn't go outside. While issues can affect your cat while inside as well, they are more likely to experience this issues if they spend a lot of time outside.
          </p>
          <p>
           Think about your cat just be indoors. Outdoor cats are known to have a shorter life expectancy and they come in contact with diseases. Some of the illnesses can also be passed along to members of the household. Let your cat rest in a place where it can look out a window so that they can get the feeling of light; this will help satisfy his or her outdoor needs.
          </p>
          <p>
           You cat has a collar with tags on all the time. Cats aren't stupid animals - they may try to leave if they're given the chance. By having a tag collar on your cat, you can be more confident that you will be reunited if the cat escapes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Some people who own cats may wish to keep their cat well-groomed and bathe them to keep them cleaner. Unless the cat is extremely dirty, a bath is not needed.Cats spend a good job of their time cleaning themselves. Cats are very concerned on their appearance!
          </p>
          <p>
           Don't let your cat get into the habit of swatting people or swat at humans. Teach them this from happening later on.
          </p>
          <p>
           Many people don't realize they are allergic to cats. Some breeds of cats trigger less likely to agitate such allergies. A better choice would a female cat or a Siberian.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you own a cat that spends time outside, be sure there is a space that is safe for him. A backyard with a high fence may be of some help, but remember that cats can jump very high and climb with ease. You can always add chicken wire around the fence's top to help keep kitty within your property.
          </p>
          <p>
           Cats are often hard to understand, but they still make wonderful pets. The above advice will allow you to give them a better life. When you know how to take care of your cat, you'll both be happier.
          </p>
