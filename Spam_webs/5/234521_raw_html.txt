          Intex Aqua Power HD Smartphone With 4000mAh Battery
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">

          <a href="http://gadgetbuzz.in/author/limey/" rel="author" title="Posts by Limey">

         </p>
         <img alt="Intex Aqua Power HD Smartphone With 4000mAh Battery" height="675" src="http://gadgetbuzz.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Intex-Aqua-Power-HD-Smartphone-118357_800x675.jpg" width="1080"/>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <h4>
           Intex Aqua Power HD smartphone with 5-inch 720x1280 display powered by 1.4GHz processor alongside 2GB RAM and 13-megapixel rear camera.
          </h4>
          <div style="float:left;margin-right:5px;">
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
           </script>

          <h3>
           Overview:
          </h3>
          <p>
           Intex Technologies today launched its Aqua Power HD smartphone at a price of Rs 9,444. The Aqua Power HD is powered by a
           <strong>
            4000mAh battery
           </strong>
           and claims to provide up to 20 hours of talk time and 500 hours of standby life. The smartphone runs on a 1.4GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6592M CPU with 2GB RAM and 16GB internal memory which can be expanded up to 32GB.
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             <a href="http://gadgetbuzz.in/" style="color: #0000ff;" target="_blank">
              Intex Aqua Power HD smartphone
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           is for people who want their smartphones to be offering more in terms of battery power. In a press statement, the company promises that the smartphone will provide up to 20 hours of talk time and about 500 hours of standby time.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_4862" style="width: 280px">
           <a href="http://gadgetbuzz.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Intex-Aqua-Power-HD-Smartphone.jpg">
            <img alt="Intex Aqua Power HD Smartphone" class=" wp-image-4862" height="307" src="http://gadgetbuzz.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Intex-Aqua-Power-HD-Smartphone.jpg" width="270"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Intex Aqua Power HD Smartphone
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Lets have a look at the other
           <strong>
            specifications of the Intex smartphone
           </strong>
           . Intex Aqua Power HD sports a 12.7-cm (5-inch) HD display and offers dual-SIM support. On the memory front, the company has fitted a 2GB RAM and 16GB of internal storage along with a microSD card slot. The smartphone runs on
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             <a href="http://products.androidxiphone.com/elephone-g5-5-5-android-smartphone-android-4-4-kitkat/" style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;" target="_blank" title="Android 4.4.2 Android KitKat">
              Android 4.4.2 Android KitKat
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           but is upgradeable to Android 5.0 (Android Lollipop).
          </p>
          <p>
           On the camera front, the
           <strong>
            Android smartphone
           </strong>
           comes with a 13MP rear shooter and a 5MP front camera. It runs on a 1.4GHz octa-core processor. In a company statement, Sanjay Kumar Kalirona, mobile business head, Intex Technologies, said, An upgrade in the Aqua Power Range, the HD version comes with an enhanced screen, memory and camera to name a few features. At Intex, it is our effort to constantly understand and deliver on customer expectations.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Intex Aqua Power HD Colours:
          </h3>
          <ul>
           <li>
            White
           </li>
           <li>
            Black
           </li>
           <li>
            Blue
           </li>
           <li>
            Grey
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h3>
           Intex Aqua Power HD specifications:
          </h3>
          <ul>
           <li>
            5-inch (1280 x 720 pixels) OnCell IPS display
           </li>
           <li>
            1.4 GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6592M processor
           </li>
           <li>
            2GB RAM, 16GB internal memory, expandable memory up to 32GB with MicroSD
           </li>
           <li>
            Android 4.4 (KitKat), upgradable to Android 5.0 (Lollipop)
           </li>
           <li>
            Dual SIM
           </li>
           <li>
            13MP rear camera with LED Flash
           </li>
           <li>
            5MP front-facing camera
           </li>
           <li>
            3.5mm audio jack, FM Radio with recording
           </li>
           <li>
            3G HSPA+, WiFi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.0, GPS
           </li>
           <li>
            4000 mAh battery
           </li>
          </ul>
