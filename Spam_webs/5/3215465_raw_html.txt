          <span class="mw-headline" id="Tips">
           Tips
          </span>
         </h2>
         <div class="section_text" id="tips">
          <ul>
           <li>
            Jack Skellington is characterized by his skeleton-like appearance. Don't be afraid to stretch his proportion by adding longer limbs, thinner and longer fingers, etc.--it adds to the characters appearance.
           </li>
           <li>
            You can also forgo the pinstripes for a flat black suit. It's easier and doesn't hurt the image, since Jacks outfit isn't too elaborate in the first place.
           </li>
           <li>
            Make it as stitchy and edgy as possible for a better "Burton" effect.
           </li>
          </ul>
