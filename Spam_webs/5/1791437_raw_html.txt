      <p>
       A dad uploaded a
       <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ehLggF12_8" title="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ehLggF12_8">
        YouTube video
       </a>
       of his family's shocked reaction when they received a healthy baby boy  instead of a girl!
      </p>
      <p>
       Kyle and Danielle Williams had an ultrasound performed in October 2014 and were told to expect a baby girl, which would have been their second daughter. They even picked out a name, but when the baby finally arrived in early March, they were shocked to discover they had their first son.
      </p>
      <p>
       Danielle, Kyle and the entire family were in shock.
      </p>
      <p>
       On YouTube, the family writes they are no stranger to having rare things happen to them. Their first child, who is now two years old, suffered from a rare blood disorder at birth. In fact, she's one of only 800 cases in North America. They're using the latest incident as an opportunity to raise awareness of Diamond Blackfan Anemia:
       <a href="http://www.peytonspals.org" title="http://www.peytonspals.org/">
        www.peytonspals.org
       </a>
      </p>
