                 <div class="content">
                  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">

               <div class="pane-content">
                <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                 <div class="field-items">
                  <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                   <p>
                    Beyond Spots &amp; Dots has claimed four wins in Higher Ed Marketing Reports 30th Annual Educational Advertising Awards, the largest educational advertising competition in the nation. The agency was recognized for its efforts in executing advertisements across various media.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    In the Magazine Advertising Series category, Beyond Spots &amp; Dots garnered a gold award. Additionally, the agency earned a silver award in Radio Advertising Series for a Pandora Radio campaign. Merit awards were also achieved in both the New Media and Newspaper Advertising Series categories, for internet advertising of academic and transfer programs.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Beyond Spots &amp; Dots competed against 2,000 entries representing 1,000 colleges and universities from across the country. The 30th Annual Educational Advertising Awards featured 30 competition categories and was judged by the editorial board of the Higher Education Marketing Report, as well as marketing and advertising professionals, creative directors and higher education marketers.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    For more information, please visit
                    <a href="http://www.hmrpublicationsgroup.com/">
                     www.hmrpublicationsgroup.com
                    </a>
                    .
                   </p>
                   <p>
                   </p>
                   <h2>
                    About Beyond Spots &amp; Dots
                   </h2>
                   <p>
                    Established in 2006, Beyond Spots &amp; Dots is a full-service advertising agency in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania that manages all facets of advertising and marketing. The agency offers PR, corporate branding, creative design, social media management, web development, media planning/buying as well as outdoor, online, TV, radio and print advertising. Beyond Spots &amp; Dots also provides market research, search engine optimization, search engine marketing and video production services. Learn more at
                    <a href="http://www.beyondspotsanddots.com/">
                     www.beyondspotsanddots.com
                    </a>
                    .
                   </p>
