           <p style="text-align: center;">
            I am pretty much loving all Pre-Fall 2014 collections. Ive always been a huge fan of Missoni and this
            <strong>
             <em>
              <a href="http://www.wwd.com/runway/pre-fall-2014/review/m-missoni" target="_blank" title="M Missoni Pre-Fall 2014">
               M Missoni Pre-Fall 2014
              </a>
             </em>
            </strong>
            collection has not disappointed. From the leather thigh-high boots to the all white ensembles. And lets not forget about their always amazing patterns that Missoni brings out every year. The color palate of purple, yellow and turquoise that they chose is one that gets my inspiration thoughts rolling with outfit ideas. The good thing with Missoni is that their clothes never seem to go out of style. Always using very classic styles that keep me more and more intrigued when new collections come out. I would die if I could get my hands on that white coat. And I would of course pair it with amazing white thigh-high boots just the way they styled it. I was also drawn to a few of the mini dresses. The sleeveless tweed dress is one of my favorites. This look is so versatile in the sense that you could go elegant or you could go casual. M Missoni Pre-Fall 2014 collection is beyond and even though its a year away Im already thinking of how Im going to get my hands on this collection.
           </p>
