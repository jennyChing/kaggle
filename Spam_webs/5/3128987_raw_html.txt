            Four Very Eco-Friendly Fashion Designers
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="meta single">
           <span class="meta-author">
            <a href="http://livinggreenmag.com/author/richardtaos/" rel="author" title="Posts by Editor Post">
             Editor Post

          <p>
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19627" height="311" src="http://livinggreenmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/stella-mccartney-shoe.jpg" title="stella-mccartney-shoe" width="468"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            By Shaun Chatman
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           When you hit the mall or the Internet in search of an outfit that flatters your body and produces that new you feeling, do you think about the environmental impact of the designers you choose?
          </p>
          <p>
           Mainstream manufacturing techniques often involve unnecessary waste, so its good to be conscientious of the ones with smaller environmental footprints. Here are four hot designers that, in addition to making you look good, are also eco-friendly.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19628" height="311" src="http://livinggreenmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/stella-mccartney-lingere.jpg" title="stella-mccartney-lingere" width="468"/>
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Stella McCartney
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Around the world, the name Stella McCartney is synonymous with beautiful, quality clothing, but this company also one of the most eco-friendly designers out there.
          </p>
          <p>
           In their spring and summer collections for 2013, the shoes feature biodegradable soles. Another example of their commitment to the planet is in the lingerie they produce, which employs both organic cotton and recycled metal.
          </p>
          <p>
           Part of the companys
           <a href="http://www.stellamccartney.com/experience/us/stellas-world/sustainability/company-statement/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.stellamccartney.com']);" target="_blank">
            mission statement
           </a>
           says, We will continue to consider the impact we have on the planet as we design clothing, open stores, and manufacture products.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19629" height="311" src="http://livinggreenmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/carrie-parry.jpg" title="carrie-parry" width="468"/>
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Carrie Parry
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Even though its one of many fashion designers in New York City, Carrie Parry also stands out for its responsible environmental practices; for example, the company received the Ethical Fashion Forums 2011 Innovation Award.
          </p>
          <p>
           Since 2011, this designers eco-friendly ways have not wavered. Among the
           <a href="http://www.carrieparry.com/blog/cat/principles/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.carrieparry.com']);" target="_blank">
            guiding principles
           </a>
           listed on its website are a precautionary approach to environmental challenges and encouragement of the development and diffusion of environmentally friendly technologies.
          </p>
          <p>
           When you look at Carrie Parrys designs, you dont just see the ubiquitous recycling logo. The fashions are wearable, feminine, and timeless.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19630" height="311" src="http://livinggreenmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/loomstate.jpg" title="loomstate" width="468"/>
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Loomstate
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           This designer is another major name thats also big on being eco-friendly. Loomstate focuses on the use of sustainable materials such as tencel fabrics, and it uses manufacturing techniques that keep waste to a minimum.
          </p>
          <p>
           One of their lines, called 321, is particularly cool in that each piece can be worn five different ways. With the potential for
           <a href="http://www.mrcostumes.com/Womens-Halloween-Costumes-C16.aspx" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mrcostumes.com']);" target="_blank">
            a variety of womens costumes
           </a>
           from just one garment, thats another great way that Loomstate is extending an olive branch to environmentalists.
          </p>
          <p>
           Loomstate also strives to increase awareness of the need for sustainability; the company has made a mission of educating others on how to help preserve our planet for future generations.
          </p>
          <p>
           An example of this is their
           <a href="http://actnatural.loomstate.org/search?q=zero+waste" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://actnatural.loomstate.org']);" target="_blank">
            Zero-Waste Project
           </a>
           , which paired design students with Loomstate to create a garment that was truly zero-waste.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19631" height="311" src="http://livinggreenmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/100-percent-ny.jpg" title="100-percent-ny" width="468"/>
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            100% NY
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Image via
           <a href="http://www.100percentny.com/shop/tree-notebook" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.100percentny.com']);">
            100% NY.com
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Despite the undeniably concrete feel of New York City, it could still be considered a green city due to the omnipresent rooftop gardens and, of course, the huge and beautiful Central Park. While New York City is not yet 100-percent eco-friendly, the brand 100% NY strives to be so.
          </p>
          <p>
           The company tries to make use of every single bit of their fabric. In addition, it uses natural dyes and believes strongly in local production.
          </p>
          <p>
           These four designers are among the hottest on the market today. And not only are they hot and happening, but they also believe in getting everything they can from the Earth without
           <em>
            taking
           </em>
           all they can from it.
          </p>

           </strong>
           is a well published author on many authority sites. He lives in Dunedin, FL, and spends his free time playing with his kids or his collection of gadgets.
