      <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name" title="10 Modern Four Poster Beds Styles and Pictures">
       10 Modern Four Poster Beds Styles and Pictures
      </h1>
      <div itemprop="articleBody">
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        For a bit of dramatic and power in the home design, think about adding a four-poster bed into the mix. If youre looking for a new furnishingor a way to create a more stylish and appealing master space, this may be your ticket to boldness and fashionable energy. We suggest you have the right kind of room to put it in, an area with enough height and space that wont swallow it whole. But, other than that, a four-poster bed can easily transform your master suiteinto something incredible special and indulgent. Lets take a look at 10 contemporary options that will knock your socks off!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="beautiful four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69946" height="424" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beautiful-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="beautiful four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        This modern example fits this particular room perfectly but can also fit easily into a variety of contemporary looks and styles. The black is always a good neutral to play with and the circular detail adds a pinch of femininity within all the masculine, sharp drama. Accent the frame with bedding that matching your vision for the right type of blending and meshing.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="classic four poster bed" class="aligncenter wp-image-69947" height="506" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/classic-four-poster-bed.jpg" title="classic four poster bed" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        A piece with a more worldly style, this examplecan be seen in a mix of decorativestyles from super modern to eclectic toAsian inspired. Again, we love the black foundation as its a perfect neutral for a contemporary vision. We also love how this particular piece has a beautiful, detailed headboard full of life and interest, bringing an artistic appeal into the space.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="classic four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69948 size-full" height="550" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/classic-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="classic four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        How can you not fall in love with this golden beauty? It may be dipped in a delicate color but the contrast between its dressing and bulkier, sturdy bones makes for a fun and interesting style. Of course, a piece this chunky deserves enough spaceto call home. Notice the the golden, silver and grey combination makes such a beautiful and relaxing atmosphere.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="contemporary four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69949" height="466" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/contemporary-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="contemporary four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Add a bit of contemporary girlish charm into your houseby finding a chic framedipped in a creamy white. This entire room has a modern edge but its made with a less dramatic and masculine edge than its predecessors. We love the mustard and almond combination that runs across the bedroom but we love even more how this ideaacts as the focus to the space.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="four poster beds for women" class="aligncenter wp-image-69950" height="411" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/four-poster-beds-for-women.png" title="four poster beds for women" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Similar to the one right above, this white themehas a feminine infusion and a wonderfulenergy swirling around. The bit of drama that the posters add make it even more special space, because its not too big andfits this slender room a lot better than a bulkier design would. And like we said above, the bedding of choice helps to dress the foundation to blend with its surroundings.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="glass four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69951" height="700" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/glass-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="glass four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Did you just fall in love? Because at first glance we fell right to the floor. This beautiful mirrored producthas the pop, power and tasteto truly create a magical masterthat youve been dreaming of. It will go with any color combination and textures that strike your fancy and will definitely fit inside a more modern vision.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="silver four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69952" height="597" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/silver-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="silver four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Heres a beautiful design thats tons of fun and has tones to notice and talk about. From the swirling risersto the scalloped edging, this furnishingwill no doubt because the focal center point, no matter what colors you decide to choose or overall theme to follow. Silver helps create a contemporary flair, but the delicate details add a welcoming bout of girlishness into the space.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="traditional four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69953" height="511" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/traditional-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="traditional four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        This piece is a stunner but it definitely needs the room to shine. It would swallow a normal sized bedroom right up and itwouldnt be appreciated as it should be. Instead, create a sleek and clean room and then place a piecesuch as this instead so you can truly see all the details it holds. It bodes well within a monochromatic theme though, dont you think?
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="transitional four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69954" height="525" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/transitional-four-poster-beds-1024x768.jpg" title="transitional four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Although the overall design of this exampleis quite sleek it has a more traditional and warmappeal than others on the list. If youre looking for something to blend in with your family home and not adding too much overwhelming style, this may be the pick for you. Itll still give you a bout of stylish ease but without the fuss!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="center-image">
        <span class="image-wrapper">
         <img alt="white four poster beds" class="aligncenter wp-image-69955" height="491" src="http://cdn.furniturefashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/white-four-poster-beds.jpg" title="white four poster beds" width="700"/>
         <span class="image-social-share">
          <span class="image-social-button image-pinterest">
           Share on Pinterest

       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Wouldnt this quiant and charming suggestion look great in your studio apartment or hipster house? This piece will fit inside small layouts, which is nice because not everyone has a huge and spacious area to play with. That being said we love it as it still give the bits of drama that make a design statement. The neutral, white foundation also helps to make it a very versatile piece for a variety of different interior design styles.
