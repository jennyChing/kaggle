        <div class="mw-content-ltr mw-content-text" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
         <div style="display: none;">
         </div>
         In the section below, leave a link to a page and
         <b>
          your
         </b>
         signature with timestamp (not the name of the pasta's author). Please make sure you add your signature, as nominations without one will be discarded. Only nominate any given pasta
         <u>
          <i>
           <b>
            ONCE
           </b>
          </i>
         </u>
         as there is a voting method for deciding.
         <p>
          Please do not nominate your
          <i>
           own
          </i>
          pages. Do not nominate "popular" pages. Do not nominate troll or horrible troll pastas. Do not nominate pages in the
          <a href="/wiki/Category:Suggested_Reading" title="Category:Suggested Reading">
           Suggested Reading
          </a>
          category. Do not nominate any pastas nominated last month. Any such nominations will be invalidated. Do not nominate
          <a href="/wiki/Category:PotM" title="Category:PotM">
           previous winners
          </a>
          or
          <a href="/wiki/Category:Spotlighted_Pastas" title="Category:Spotlighted Pastas">
           spotlighted pastas
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Keep in mind that as the story will be featured on the home page of the wiki, NSFW stories should not be nominated.
         </p>
         <p>
          Only
          <i>
           ONE
          </i>
          nomination per user. If there's more than one pasta that you feel deserves Pasta of the Month, please either contact an admin about it or wait until next month to nominate the next one.
         </p>
         <p>
          Please don't leave comments next to your vote. The story will more than likely do a better job of convincing the reader to like it than you would giving your opinion. Also, it makes the nomination page look sloppy.
         </p>
         <p>
          Nominations will be open until the 15th of the month. This gives everyone ample time to nominate pages and then about 15 days to vote.
         </p>
