          The earth receives more energy from the sun in 1 hour than is used in 1 year. With the rise in popularity of solar energy many homeowners have begun equipping their homes with solar panels. In 2014, its projected that new solar electric capacity will generate enough clean energy to power 1.13 million homes. From saving money to helping the environmentthere are many benefits to equipping your home for solar energy.
