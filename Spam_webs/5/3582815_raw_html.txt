         <div id="description-text">
          REDUCED PRICE Titled "Autumn Magic"  This original Exclusive Acrylic Mixed Media Painting is  in honor of the Autumn magic rituals and festivities.  This piece is a storybook tale in a fantasy world created by painting with acrylic paints, fabric paints and enamels and adding buttons, fabric, feathers, recycled jewelry, beads and other odds and ends. Click for more items:
          <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/AbetArt4U" target="_blank">
           https://www.etsy.com/shop/AbetArt4U
          </a>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Many layers of color, texture and costume jewelry pieces of unknown age on canvas.
          <br/>
          The size is 24x8 inches and the depth is 1/2 inches
          <br/>
          The painting continues on the sides and is ready to hang.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The colors are hues of browns, tans, bronze, gold, green and silveron canvas.
          <br/>
          This fantasy painting is ready to hang with a champagne painted barrier on the back to cover various areas where jewelry pieces have been secured to the canvas.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          There are beautiful buttons of unknown age and recycled jewelry pieces, a wooden button with a brass bird figure. There are two silvered colored dangle pieces from a recycled necklace, one on the top right and one on the bottom in the middle. The 3-D Shaman has a face made from a silver colored button, a jewelry piece necklace, black tattered fabric hair, a silver button shield and a yellow and black dyed feather.  This piece is unsigned, please let me know if you wish it to be signed.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          For adult use only, not recommended for children.
