            FILIPINO PESCETARIAN DISHES RECIPE ROUND UP
           </p>
           <p>
            Holiday is over but that does not stop us, Pinoys, from eating! The aftermath of over-indulging ourselves from the countless dinners and parties brought by the season is now haunting us in the form of those extra-shedded fats. Guilt might leave us though to settle for fish/seafood and veggies and lessen sweets and fatty foods for the meantime. So, here is a round-up of some Filipino pescetarian homecooked dishes with little or no red meat for our post-holiday enjoyment. Click on the links for the recipes. DON'T FORGET TO CLICK ON THAT SHARE BUTTON, your friends might need them!
