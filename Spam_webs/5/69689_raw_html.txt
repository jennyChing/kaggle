      <h1>
       5 Surefire Ways to Increase Business Efficiency
      </h1>
      <h2>
      </h2>
      <div class="date-links">
       By
       <a href="http://www.theepochtimes.com/n3/author/chris-grasso/">
        Chris Grasso
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.theepochtimes.com/n3/author/chris-grasso/?post_type=all">
        Curious Chris' Musings
       </a>
       | September 9, 2014
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="top-story">
      <div class="singlepage top-story-R" style="width:auto">
       <!--Category Start-->
       <div class="category-bg entry" id="artcontent">
        <p>
         The more efficient you make your business, the more opportunities you have to become successful. An efficient business makes the most of its time and resources. By focusing on these 5 surefire ways to increase business efficiency, you should find your business reaching goals sooner than expected.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Use Organized Workstations
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="" src="http://farm5.staticflickr.com/4122/4746911330_d36d035bc1.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <!--[endif]-->
        </p>
        <p>
         Image via
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/smemon/4746911330/sizes/m/in/photolist-8etamN-8eta9b-4GhjhX-4h6c7n-6xbLPD-97M52w-2UzVnp-7jGtzE-asNYgw-4TUyu3-4nMPKL-5E8otF-9AZK27-cWB9vs-97HWCk-7tafRw-7taf8Q-7tafv7-7tairu-7t6gg2-7t6i96-7t6iUa-7tai5J-7t6hK8-7t6kEZ-7taerj-7tah3C-7tahNd-7taiLf-5XmuYF-5XmuX2-4TQjyn-cADEoL-cWRvv5-BnFsp-4zxdut-656orT-cWAN8C-cWBu5S-cWAApU-cWAWfs-cWB3As-cWBjvj-cWB6jS-cWBD3N-cWArm1-cWACJQ-cWBbX3-cWAkeS-cWBJEs-cWBAJC/" target="_blank">
          Flickr
         </a>
         by Sean McEntee
        </p>
        <p>
         An organized workstation makes it easier for employees to do their jobs. Whether they work at a computer or a factory, organized workstations give employees easier access to the tools that they need to do their jobs. If a cluttered desk distracts an employee for a few minutes each day, then youve lost productivity that could push your business closer to success.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over a year, five lost minutes a day adds add up to over 20 hours for a full-time employee. Seen from that perspective, a cluttered desk starts to look more like a speed bump.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Set Daily Production Goals
        </h2>
        <p>
         Daily production goals establish expectations within your business. Without production goals, employees dont know whether they are fulfilling their duties. Some employees might even become less productive if they dont have expectations to meet.
        </p>
        <p>
         Production goals create a map that helps employees know where they are and if they are meeting their obligations. If you dont have them, even managers cant determine whether employees are working well. It becomes a guessing game for everyone in the organization.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Use Positive Management Strategies
        </h2>
        <p>
         The benefits of performance management become most obvious when you focus on positive reinforcement that rewards employees for meeting goals instead of punishing those who do not.
        </p>
        <p>
         A survey of companies in the UK show that
         <a href="http://news.cnet.com/U.K.-survey-IT-managers-blamed-for-staff-malaise/2100-1022_3-6222631.html" target="_blank">
          37 percent
         </a>
         of those with open, positive management styles have higher productivity and overall success. The same survey shows that 56 percent of declining companies use bureaucratic management styles that make workers feel disenfranchised.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Get Feedback From Employees
        </h2>
        <p>
         Employees present a perspective to the company that managers and owners overlook.Giving them the opportunity to express their thoughts and concerns gives you a chance to spot inefficiencies that you would never see without their help. When a manager always focuses on the big picture, she can miss little opportunities that could improve efficiency.
        </p>
        <p>
         Employees can show you relatively simple ways to reduce costs, make communication easier, and cut the time it takes to complete projects.You just have to give them a chance to speak their minds.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Upgrade Equipment
        </h2>
        <p>
         Old equipment often cant compete with the efficiency of newer models. Consider that Microsoft requires that computers have a minimum
         <a href="http://computer.howstuffworks.com/ram5.htm" target="_blank">
          128MB of RAM
         </a>
         to use Windows XP. Windows 3.0 only required
         <a href="http://support.microsoft.com/kb/58317" target="_blank">
          192K of free memory
         </a>
         . That shows you that todays computers have come a long way since those released in the 1990s. If youre using old computers, then your business is wasting time every day. Having companies who can take care of IT work and developing becomes very important in todays technological world. A group like
         <a href="http://www.asapy.com/">
          Asapy development company
         </a>
         can help you be more efficient without having to hire internally.
        </p>
        <p>
         Other types of equipment might also need upgrades depending on your industry. You should compare your options and upgrade costs to decide when it makes sense to buy new equipment.
        </p>
        <p>
         What surefire ways have you used to increase your businesss efficiency? Have they ever failed you?
        </p>
