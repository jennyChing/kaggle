      <h2 class="entry-title">
       Puritans Run Amok  Schools ban Anne Frank and Websters Dictionary
      </h2>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        Since when is one parent complaining about a book enough to get it pulled from schools?
       </p>
       <p>
        In seemingly unrelated incidents, a
        <a href="http://rawstory.com/2010/01/school-district-pulls-anne-franks-diary/">
         Virginia school district
        </a>
        has yanked Anne Franks famous diary off its curriculum and shelves, because of a short passage about genitalia:
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         There are little folds of skin all over the place, you can hardly find it. The little hole underneath is so terribly small that I simply cant imagine how a man can get in there, let alone how a whole baby can get out!
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        But if that werent silly enough, a
        <a href="http://www.pe.com/localnews/menifee/stories/PE_News_Local_W_sdictionary22.414bdf0.html">
         school district in Southern California
        </a>
        has pulled the Merriam Webster dictionary from all school shelves because (Im not making this up), it has an entry for oral sex (defined as oral stimulation of the genitals).
       </p>
       <p>
        Both incidents were caused by a single complaint from a parent.
       </p>
