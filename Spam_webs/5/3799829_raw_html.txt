          Turning Point in Life
         </h1>

          <span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25">

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Life is really full of surprises. Today, you wake up, spending all your time with your family, with such happy faces that no one in town could ever paint to. It has its own ups and downs, which may sometimes lead you to the point of having such opposite mood with the one you have yesterday, and the one that youll be having tomorrow. And even in your life, hitting you in just a tick of the clock, which may let you live for one more day, or end your life, leaving you no chance to do what you still havent finished.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-360">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Meet Imari; a very care-free lady, extrovert and acts as if she doesnt have any of the problems in the world. Everybody loves her for her optimism, and being a very reliable person. She has the good traits that everybody wants to have. However, she is an atheist- a nonbeliever of Christ.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Imari has a scheduled week-long trip, and before she left, her closest friend, who happened to be a Christian gave her a Bible. Since shes in a hurry, she put the Bible in her bag, left, and enjoyed her week. But who would have thought that after spending so much time, enjoying her life, Imari will meet an accident- an accident that changed her life from being an atheist, into a faithful and loyal believer of Christ.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           When Imari was heading her way home, sleeping soundly while the bus is running, she was awakened by the sudden noise of the people, panicking for the bus loss its brake. Imari doesnt know what to do until the bus fell on a cliff that killed a lot of passengers. Imari, grasping her breath, struggling to go out of the bus to save her life, saw some of the passengers struggling just like her, and have lost some parts of their body. Imari couldnt last what she saw by that time and cried while pleading for help from the Lord until she fainted. While unconscious, Imari saw a group of angels rescuing her from the incident, and when she woke up in the hospital, beside her was the Bible given to her by her friend. The rescuers said that while she was out of conscious, she was holding the Bible in her hand tightly. It made Imari cry because the last time she remembered is that she just put the Bible in her bag and ignored it for the whole duration of her trip. And remembering the angels she has seen while she was unconscious, Imari decided to believe in Christ and do whatever pleases Him.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Imari was definitely blessed to be given a chance to know God. She almost lost her life in the incident. However, God has a lot of plans for her on earth and has given her another chance to live for Him. But we should also remember that life is not always like what happened to Imari. There are people who died without even hearing the name of Jesus. The reflection of Imaris turning point in life thus leaves a call for all of us who claim to be believers and followers of Chirst; to do everything that we can to make Christ known, especially to the unreachable ones.
          </p>
