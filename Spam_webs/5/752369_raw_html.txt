         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_125" style="width: 235px">

           <img alt="Hand gripper" class="wp-image-125 size-medium" height="300" src="http://www.modernhealthclub.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/pencil_sketch_1423045449016-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>

         <p>
         </p>
        </center>
        This is a one kind of small instrument.You can hold in your palm and press it and release it.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         procedure:
        </strong>
        <p>
          Hold the hand gripper strongly with the palm of left hand.
        </p>
        <p>
          Give the support of left hand near the elbow with the help of right hand.
        </p>
        <p>
          Thereafter press the hand gripper completely and release it slowly.
        </p>
        <p>
          First day 10 to 30 time and you can increase according to your capacity.Similarly do for right hand.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Advantage:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1) You can make strong your wrist.
        </p>
        <p>
         2) Get good shape of your hand.
        </p>
        <p>
         3) This is good exercise for fingers and stamina of palm.
        </p>
