           <p class="mrb">
            <span class="published">
             Updated January 01, 2014.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Screening for possible depression or mania is very simple and could save your life. Screening tests will reveal if you have symptoms that require evaluation by a physician.
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Difficulty:
            </b>
            Easy
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Time Required:
            </b>
            5 minutes
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Here's How:
            </b>
           </p>
           <ol>
            <li>
             The screening quizzes you will be taking are found online. Read the directions here before proceeding.
            </li>
            <li>
             <p>
              Select which quiz you want to take and right click to open it in a new window or tab. There are two available:
              <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="1" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/cs/diagnosis/l/bldepscreenquiz.htm">
               depression
              </a>
              and
              <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="2" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/od/bipolar/a/bipolarordep.htm">
               mania
              </a>
              .
             </p>
            </li>
           </ol>
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="2" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense1">
           </div>
           <div class="radlinks ads-half" data-character-limit="null" data-display-inline="true" data-display-label="false" data-num-links="5" id="radlinks3">
           </div>
           <div data-defer="onPageLoad" id="videoPlaceholderLarge">
           </div>
           <ol class="cb-split">
            <li>
             Read the directions for that test.
            </li>
            <li>
             Read each question carefully and select your answers by clicking on the radio button beside it with your mouse.
            </li>
            <li>
             After you have answered all questions, you will see a button at the bottom of the page labeled "Score My Depression Questionnaire." Click this.
            </li>
            <li>
             You will be given an evaluation of your current level of depression or mania and instructions about whether you should seek a professional evaluation.
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            <b>
             Tips:
            </b>
           </p>
           <ol>
            <li>
             Bookmark the screening tests and use them weekly to track your progress.
            </li>
           </ol>
