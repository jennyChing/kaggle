                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     SPECIAL MESSAGE about Shuja'iya in Gaza
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2883024984342148603" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div style="background-color: white; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, 'lucida grande', tahoma, verdana, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.359999656677246px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        h/t
                        <a href="http://israeltheviewfromhere.blogspot.co.il/" target="_blank">
                         Barry Shaw
                        </a>
                        via
                        <a href="http://rjstreets.com/" target="_blank">
                         Real Jerusalem Streets
                        </a>
                        .
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       in Gaza has been identified by the IDF and Shin Bet intelligence as an area rife with Hamas and Islamic Jihad terror hideouts. These terror groups store weapons in
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       , launch rockets from
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       , and dig tunnels to be used forterrorattacks from underneath
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       .
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="background-color: white; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, 'lucida grande', tahoma, verdana, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.359999656677246px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       The IDF warned the residents of
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       to leave for several days. Leaflets were dropped, thousands of phone calls were made,and text messages were sent, telling the residents to evacuate the area. While thousands of residents managed to get out, others were prevented from vacating the area by Hamas. Hamas prevented civilians from leaving the area because, like the residents themselves, Hamas knew that the IDF intended to target and eradicating several years worth of stockpiled weapons and the significant terror infrastructure they have built in this heavily populated urban neighborhood.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
                      Shuja'iya: Neighborhood for civilians, fortress for Hamas terrorists.
                      <a href="http://t.co/HUhAKvDuWN">
                       pic.twitter.com/HUhAKvDuWN
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                       IDF (@IDFSpokesperson)
                      <a href="https://twitter.com/IDFSpokesperson/statuses/490833439222804481">
                       July 20, 2014
                      </a>
                     </blockquote>
                     <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
                     </script>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="background-color: white; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, 'lucida grande', tahoma, verdana, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.359999656677246px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       It is from
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       that hundreds of rockets have been launched into Israel.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="background-color: white; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, 'lucida grande', tahoma, verdana, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.359999656677246px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       Hamas asked for and received a humanitarian ceasefire, a lull in the fighting in
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       . Israel agreed to a humanitarianceasefire of two hours, from 1:30-3:30 PM. During this time, Hamas brought in the foreign press corps to tell them about the "genocide" in
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       . Many civilians were killed and wounded because Hamas prevented them from leaving.The press reported the so-called "genocide" without mentioning that several dozen terrorists were the real targets and were among the many killed and wounded.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="background-color: white; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, 'lucida grande', tahoma, verdana, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.359999656677246px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">
                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       The humanitarian ceasefire gave the terrorists an opportunity to treat and evacuate the wounded and to move their weapons and explosives from the area, ahead of the serious fighting expected to take place in this neighborhood later today. It is believed that ambulances were used both to remove the wounded AND the weapons stockpile from
                       <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px;">
                        Shuja'iya
                       </span>
                       duringthe ceasefire period.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div id="fb-root">
                     </div>

                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       The ceasefire was still meant to be in effect when Gaza launched rockets into Israel, at 2:15 PM. These rockets were intercepted by the Iron Dome system. IDF artillery responded shortly after, shooting several rounds of shells into Gaza.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="background-color: white; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, 'lucida grande', tahoma, verdana, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.359999656677246px; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 1em;">

                      <span style="color: #444444;">
                       This is the third ceasefire in the current conflict hat Hamas has failed to honor.
