                     Walking Down Memory Lane with Big G Cereals, WinCo and the new Ibotta App #giveaway
                    </h3>

                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       When I was younger (much younger!), I remember spending every Saturday night at my grandparent's house. My cousins and I, with some of my aunts would get up every Sunday morning for church. As kids, my cousins and I would argue every Saturday night about who got what sleeping bag. My gramma would tell us kids numerous times to be quiet and to go to sleep. We would stay up playing, making noises and talking  just like most kids our ages! I have very fond memories of those sleepovers. On Sunday morning, we would all get up and make ourselves breakfast. One of my favorite things about breakfast at my grandparent's house was that they always had the "fun" breakfast cereal  you know, the stuff my mom wouldn't buy for us at home!! One of my favorites as a child (and still today!) was Honey Nut Cheerios! My second choice was always Cinnamon Toast Crunch - now, I love this cinnamon cereal for late night-snacking! My cousins loved picking their favorite cereal too!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <center>
                       <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/lovebakesgoodcakes/13988427068" title="Walking Down Memory Lane with Big G Cereals, WinCo and the new Ibotta App #sponsored #WinCoCerealDeals by Jamie, on Flickr">

                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <center>
                       <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/lovebakesgoodcakes/14195178553" title="Walking Down Memory Lane with Big G Cereals, WinCo and the new Ibotta App #sponsored #WinCoCerealDeals by Jamie, on Flickr">

You can save up to  $14.50 on your favorite cereals!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <center>
                       <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/lovebakesgoodcakes/14151907756" title="Walking Down Memory Lane with Big G Cereals, WinCo and the new Ibotta App #sponsored #WinCoCerealDeals by Jamie, on Flickr">
                        <img alt="Walking Down Memory Lane with Big G Cereals, WinCo and the new Ibotta App #sponsored #WinCoCerealDeals" height="640" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7327/14151907756_0e1365b29d_z.jpg" width="480"/>

                      <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       My family and I were in the Glendale, AZ area recently and we decided to stop and check out
                       <a href="https://tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/4ZquR/O83T5/c?p=WcY0f&amp;i=OxhW2&amp;r=p%2FrXUx2OONeSjec%2BHI7xwWTjB9HXjEgi" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

                       . I love discovering new places (especially ones that save me money!!) and I remember seeing all the great deals on the
                       <a href="https://tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/4ZquR/5sMPx/c?p=WcY0f&amp;i=OxhW2&amp;r=VbhY3l3IY7dbe0Jme1rr4i6KkuvEHZZMxtC00%2F3EeHWFByHl1dEqDA%3D%3D" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                        Ibotta app
                       </a>

It conjured up all sorts of memories for me as I watched my kiddos get excited about picking out which General Mills cereal they wanted to eat.
