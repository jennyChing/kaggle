           <h5 class="heading p-col">
            Ingredients
           </h5>
           <ul class="content ingredients-list">
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             4 large Idaho baking potatoes, scrubbed clean
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tsp
             <a data-inlink="UB6HQs7FZCB-0hCeuYObvw==" href="/od/foodhealthinformation/a/oliveoilhealth.htm">
              olive oil
             </a>
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tsp kosher salt
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             6 slices bacon, cooked, drained, and crumbled
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1-1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided use
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup
             <a data-inlink="Mx8dFxgElPRwISA47ehKLQ==" href="/od/milkproducts/a/sourcreamtips.htm">
              sour cream
             </a>
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup chopped chives or green onions
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Kosher salt, to taste
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Freshly ground pepper, to taste
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Sweet Hungarian paprika (optional)
            </li>
           </ul>

          <div class="preparation expert-content-text" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           <h5 class="heading-style-a p-col">
            Preparation
           </h5>
           <p>
            Preheat oven to 400 F.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Rub
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="1" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blpotatoes.htm">
             <b>
              potatoes
             </b>
            </a>
            with olive oil and sprinkle with kosher
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="2" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa042202a.htm">
             <b>
              salt
             </b>
            </a>
            . Prick in several spots with a fork. Place on a shallow baking pan 6 inches apart and bake until center is soft, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            When
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="3" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blpotatoes.htm">
             <b>
              potatoes
             </b>
            </a>
            are cool enough to handle, slice each one in half lengthwise. Carefully scoop out the soft flesh, leaving 1/4-inch rim around the
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="4" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blpotatoes.htm">
             <b>
              potato
             </b>
            </a>
            skin, and place flesh in a mixing bowl.
           </p>
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="3" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense1">
           </div>
           <div class="radlinks ads-half" data-character-limit="null" data-display-inline="true" data-display-label="false" data-num-links="5" id="radlinks3">
           </div>
           <p class="cb-split">
            Set skins aside to stuff later.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Mash the
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="5" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blpotatoes.htm">
             <b>
              potatoes
             </b>
            </a>
            with a fork or masher (do not use a mixer). A few small lumps are good for flavor and texture, so do not try to get them perfectly smooth. Stir in
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="6" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa082701a.htm">
             <b>
              bacon
             </b>
            </a>
            bits, 1 cup shredded
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="7" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/topicsubdairy.htm">
             <b>
              Cheddar cheese
             </b>
            </a>
            ,
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="8" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa040802a.htm">
             <b>
              sour cream
             </b>
            </a>
            ,
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="9" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa022398.htm">
             <b>
              chives
             </b>
            </a>
            ,
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="10" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa042202a.htm">
             <b>
              salt
             </b>
            </a>
            , and
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="11" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa091100a.htm">
             <b>
              pepper
             </b>
            </a>
            .
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Spoon or pipe mashed
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="12" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blpotatoes.htm">
             <b>
              potato
             </b>
            </a>
            mixture into the potato skins and return to the baking tray. Sprinkle with reserved 1/2 cup
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="13" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/topicsubdairy.htm">
             <b>
              Cheddar cheese
             </b>
            </a>
            ,
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="14" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blparmesan.htm">
             <b>
              Parmesan cheese
             </b>
            </a>
            , and optional
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="15" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/weekly/aa052101a.htm">
             <b>
              paprika
             </b>
            </a>
            . (May be made in advance to this point. Cover and refrigerate or freeze.)
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Return to the 400-degree F. oven and bake about 30 minutes until
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="16" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/library/archive/blpotatoes.htm">
             <b>
              potatoes
             </b>
            </a>
            are lightly browned and heated through.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Yield: 8 servings as a side dish
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <sub>
             Photo  2006 Peggy Trowbridge, licensed to About.com, Inc.
            </sub>
            <br/>
            <br/>
           </p>
