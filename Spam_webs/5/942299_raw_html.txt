     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Walk the Angry Beach
       </b>
      </i>
      , also known as
      <i>
       Hollywood After Dark
      </i>
      and
      <i>
       The Unholy Choice
      </i>
      , is an
      <a href="/wiki/Exploitation_film" title="Exploitation film">
       exploitation film
      </a>
      starring
      <a href="/wiki/Rue_McClanahan" title="Rue McClanahan">
       Rue McClanahan
      </a>
      as a stripper who aspires to become a movie actress, but ends up being exploited by the industry.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="DVD_releases">
       DVD releases
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Walk_the_Angry_Beach&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: DVD releases">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      In 2005, the film was featured in the finale of the television series,
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Deadly_Cinema" title="Deadly Cinema">
        Deadly Cinema
       </a>
      </i>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      In 2006, the film was released as a double feature DVD with another McClanahan film,
      <i>
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Rotten_Apple" title="The Rotten Apple">
        The Rotten Apple
       </a>
      </i>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      In 2007, the film was featured in the direct-to-DVD series,
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/The_Film_Crew" title="The Film Crew">
        The Film Crew
       </a>
      </i>
      .
     </p>
