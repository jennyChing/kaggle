            <img alt="Smoked Tomato Michelada Popsicle Cocktail" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2402" height="1350" src="http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomato-Michelada-Pop-Cocktail.jpg" width="900"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://dinexdesign.com/popsicle-week/&amp;media=http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomato-Michelada-Pop-Cocktail.jpg&amp;description=Popsicle Week: Smoked Tomato Michelada Popsicle Cocktail')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Theres something about spending an afternoon riding beach cruisers that makes us thirstyfor beer.
          </p>
          <p>
           Where cervezasarent always first on our brunch or happy hour menus, there is no denying those yeasty bubbles and crisp refreshment that only a frosty pint can soothe on a hot seaside day.
          </p>
          <p>
           Inspired by a recent getaway on the sandy stretches of San Diego weve put together a Michelada unlike any other  its smoked, full of spiceand it involves popsicles.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Smoked Tomato Michelada Pop Cocktail" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2404" height="600" src="http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Popsicle-Week-Smoked-Tomato-Lime-Cocktail.jpg" width="900"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://dinexdesign.com/popsicle-week/&amp;media=http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Popsicle-Week-Smoked-Tomato-Lime-Cocktail.jpg&amp;description=Popsicle Week: Smoked Tomato Michelada Popsicle Cocktail')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Full disclosure: this probably is not a popsicle wed recommend eating on its own.
          </p>
          <p>
           We are definitely fans of smoked tomato sorbets when used in the appropriate application (were looking at you
           <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://gomighty.com/TheCuisinerd/movida-melbourne/" rel="external nofollow" title="">
            Movida
           </a>
           ), but this popsicle was created with the full intention of being diluted in a large mug of beer. Its packed with powerful slow smoked heirloom tomatoes, two kinds of hot sauce (because hot sauce is San Diegos love language), jalapeo slices, fresh lime juice and if that wasnt intense enough, weve dipped them in a coarsecracked pepper and a dusting of cayenne heat.
          </p>
          <p>
           In a word, you
           <em>
            need
           </em>
           that beer to calm this savory sassycle.
          </p>
          <p>
           Lets get into this and onto our weekend.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomatoe-Michelada-Popsicle-Cocktail.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Smoked Tomatoe Michelada Popsicle Cocktail | Dine X Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2405" height="1350" src="http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomatoe-Michelada-Popsicle-Cocktail.jpg" width="900"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://dinexdesign.com/popsicle-week/&amp;media=http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomatoe-Michelada-Popsicle-Cocktail.jpg&amp;description=Popsicle Week: Smoked Tomato Michelada Popsicle Cocktail')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <div class="sc-separator type-double">
          </div>
          <address>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Popsicle Week: Smoked Tomato Michelada Pop
            </strong>
           </em>
          </address>
          <address>
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Makes 8 Pops/ Beer Cocktails
           </span>
          </address>
          <address>
           <span style="font-size: small;">
           </span>
          </address>
          <address>
           <strong>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             Popsicle
             <em>
             </em>
             Ingredients
            </span>
           </strong>
          </address>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              <i>
               3 large heirloom tomatoes
              </i>
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              3 limes, juiced
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              1 TBSP Cholula Hot Sauce
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              1 TBSPTapatio Hot Sauce
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              1 TSP Celery Salt
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              16-20 Jalapeo slices
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <address>
           <strong>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             Popsicle
             <em>
              Directions
             </em>
            </span>
           </strong>
          </address>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              <i>
               Smoke tomatoes for 1 hour on using a
               <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005LRKF7U/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B005LRKF7U&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thecuis06-20&amp;linkId=NUCLCMNHF7SW4KTE" rel="external nofollow" title="">
                BBQ smoker kit
               </a>
               &amp;
               <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000CBJCQ/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0000CBJCQ&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thecuis06-20&amp;linkId=TLNOVUO2JELNU42T" rel="external nofollow" title="">
                hickory chips
               </a>
              </i>
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              Once tomatoes cool, extract juice through a cheese cloth or mesh strainer in a bow
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              Discard skins and pulp
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              In a bowl, add and stir lime juice, hot sauce and celery salt with fresh cracked pepper
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              Add mixture to popsicle
              <em>
               molds and place thin jalapeo slices on the sides
              </em>
             </span>
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             Freeze over night
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             <em>
              Serving Directions
             </em>
            </span>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <address>
             Release pops from mold and dip into a mixture of course cracked black pepper andcayenne pepper
            </address>
           </li>
           <li>
            <address>
             Pour Mexican Beer of choice into a large mug, add a squeeze a fresh lime and let that pop go for a dip anddissolve
            </address>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="sc-separator type-double">
          </div>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Smoked Tomato Michelada Pop Cocktail Recipe | Dine X Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2408" height="1350" src="http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomato-Michelada-Pop-Cocktail-Recipe.jpg" width="900"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://dinexdesign.com/popsicle-week/&amp;media=http://dinexdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Smoked-Tomato-Michelada-Pop-Cocktail-Recipe.jpg&amp;description=Popsicle Week: Smoked Tomato Michelada Popsicle Cocktail')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Whooo! That is a surprising smack on the lips. Are you ready to take a smoked and spicy plunge this weekend? We are thinking this might be a new summer staple and there are still 4 more in our our cooler.
          </p>
          <p>
           Just let us know when to expect you stopping by.
          </p>
          <div class="sc-separator type-double">
          </div>
          <address>
           Its
           <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://witandvinegar.com/popsicleweek" rel="external nofollow" title="">
            popsicle week!
           </a>
           And that means there are 40 MORE unique popsicles making your weekend freezer seem a little cramped. Check out everyone who participated over on
           <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://witandvinegar.com/popsicleweek" rel="external nofollow" title="">
            Wit And Vinegar
           </a>
           and let us know what youre planing on popping below!
          </address>
