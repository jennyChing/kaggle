       <div class="article-story-body">
        <div class="body parsys">
         <div class="articlerelatedphotogallery section">
         </div>
         <div class="articlerelatedvideogallery section">
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           A week after Mayor John Tory
           <a data-councillor="John Tory" data-img="/councillors/John.Tory.9x2.jpeg" data-position="Mayor" data-ward="Mayor" href="http://www.thestar.com/content/thestar/news/politics/policard.html#councillor/johntory">
            (open John Tory's policard)
           </a>
           suggested
           <a href="http://www.thestar.com/news/gta/2015/03/12/mayor-john-tory-vows-toronto-city-staff-shakeup.html">
            someone would be held to account
           </a>
           , the TTC has fired two senior managers in charge of the over-budget, delayed Spadina subway extension.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           TTC chief capital officer Sameh Ghaly and chief project manager Andy Bertolo  both long-term executives  were let go Thursday.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           After careful and lengthy consideration, I have determined that a change in leadership within the engineering, construction and expansion group, including the
           <a href="http://www.thestar.com/news/queenspark/2015/03/06/kathleen-wynne-chides-ttc-for-cost-overruns-on-spadina-subway-extension.html">
            Toronto-York Spadina Subway Extension
           </a>
           project, is necessary, said TTC CEO Andy Byford in a memo to staff mid-afternoon.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Saying, Torontonians have a right to expect better, Tory issued a statement later signaling the potential for more dismissals in the future.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           I've been clear about the need for much stronger culture of accountability at city hall . . . From time to time that will and should mean personnel changes, as it would with any other organization. TTC CEO Andy Byford has my full support in his efforts to restore public confidence in the management of the Toronto-York Spadina Subway Extension, said the mayor, who is in Austin on city business.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Ghaly earned $257,600 and Bertolo made $202,581, according to the 2013 provinicial list of public salaries published in compliance with the Public Sector Salary Disclosure Act.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           The subway opening had already been delayed once when the Star reported in August that it likely wouldn't meet its second scheduled opening date in 2016. The project was plagued with issues ranging from funding delays and the death of a construction worker to contractor disputes and engineering issues.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Two weeks ago,
           <a href="http://www.thestar.com/news/queenspark/2015/03/06/spadina-subway-extension-400m-over-budget.html">
            the Star revealed the six-stop extension from Downsview Station to the Vaughan Metropolitan Centre was about $400 million over its $2.6 billion budget
           </a>
           .
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           On Friday the TTC will issue a report outlining a revised construction schedule and costing. It will likely include the possibility of opening the line only as far as York University  a suggestion that has been vehemently opposed by Transportation Minister Steven Del Duca, who represents Vaughan.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Councillor and TTC board chair Josh Colle
           <a data-councillor="Josh Colle" data-img="/councillors/Josh.Colle.9x2.jpeg" data-position="Councillor" data-ward="Ward 15 Eglinton-Lawrence" href="http://www.thestar.com/content/thestar/news/politics/policard.html#councillor/joshcolle">
            (open Josh Colle's policard)
           </a>
           acknowledged Thursday that public projects in the city  many not managed by the TTC  notoriously run over time and over budget. It's a problem, he said, that has maybe not been dealt with decisively enough.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Saying he supports Byford in making some difficult decisions, Colle said the changes at the TTC speak to a level of accountability that people want.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p annotation="note">
           TTC board member councillor Joe Mihevc
           <a data-councillor="Joe Mihevc" data-img="/councillors/Joe.Mihevc.9x2.jpeg" data-position="Councillor" data-ward="Ward 21 St. Paul's" href="http://www.thestar.com/content/thestar/news/politics/policard.html#councillor/joemihevc">
            (open Joe Mihevc's policard)
           </a>
           said he had known Ghaly and Bertolo as good civil servants, who did their best in difficult situations.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           It is tragic that someone has to take the fall, he said.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           While Byford has responsibility for staffing decisions, The question from the Toronto Transit Commission's point of view is, is it the right call and what does this say about how we need to manage the leadership at the TTC?
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Other city councillors said action was needed in light of the overages on Spadina.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           If a project went $400 million over budget in the private sector there would be accountability, said Deputy Mayor Denzil Minnan-Wong
           <a data-councillor="Denzil Minnan-Wong" data-img="/councillors/Denzil.Minnan.Wong.9x2.jpeg" data-position="Councillor" data-ward="Ward 34 Don Valley East" href="http://www.thestar.com/content/thestar/news/politics/policard.html#councillor/denzilminnanwong">
            (open Denzil Minnan-Wong's policard)
           </a>
           , who sits on the Toronto Transit Commission.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           Obviously something's gone wrong, said fellow TTC member councillor John Campbell
           <a data-councillor="John Campbell" data-img="/councillors/John.Campbell.9x2.jpeg" data-position="Councillor" data-ward="Ward 4 Etobicoke Centre" href="http://www.thestar.com/content/thestar/news/politics/policard.html#councillor/johncampbell">
            (open John Campbell's policard)
           </a>
           .
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           He dismissed any suggestion that Ghaly and Bertolo were scapegoats, saying he suspects a certain amount of complacency in the civil service.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           I have enough confidence in Andy Byford and his senior team to think he probably would have a clear understanding to assign performance with outcomes, said Campbell.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           At this point I have very little confidence in the TTC to manage a major infrastructure project, said councillor Josh Matlow
           <a data-councillor="Josh Matlow" data-img="/councillors/Josh.Matlow.9x2.jpeg" data-position="Councillor" data-ward="Ward 22 St. Paul's" href="http://www.thestar.com/content/thestar/news/politics/policard.html#councillor/joshmatlow">
            (open Josh Matlow's policard)
           </a>
           , a vocal opponent of the Scarborough subway.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
           The enormous over-runs on Spadina are inexcusable, he said, adding, There needs to be responsibility taken.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
          <p itemprop="articleBody">
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="text combinedtext parbase section">
