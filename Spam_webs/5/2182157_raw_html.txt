   <h1>
    Toms Favourite Comments
   </h1>
   <p>
    The journalistic life is an exposed one. This isnt helped by having the opinions I seem to have, which normally results in some pretty amazing comments. So: This is a segment I like to call: Toms Favourite Comments. These comments are from a
    <a href="http://wegotthiscovered.com/gaming/10-ways-gaming-destroying-life/#.U9Kmp2f6Np4.twitter" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://wegotthiscovered.com/gaming/10-ways-gaming-destroying-life/#.U9Kmp2f6Np4.twitter', 'July 25th 2014']);">
     July 25
     <sup>
      th
     </sup>
     2014
    </a>
    . article.
   </p>
   <p>
    1) Simple and elegant like a sophisticated wedding dress.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a href="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/1.png">
     <img alt="shit post is shit" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1470" height="75" src="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/1.png" width="542"/>
    </a>
   </p>
   <p>
    2) I love how misguided the rage is.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a href="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2.png">
     <img alt="asshole" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1471" height="113" src="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2.png" width="503"/>
    </a>
   </p>
   <p>
    3) Numbers 10, 6 and 5 are my favourite.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a href="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/3.png">
     <img alt="nope" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1472" height="425" src="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/3.png" width="509"/>
    </a>
   </p>
   <p>
    4) The Archer has seemed to have missed the disclaimer I had that the beginning of my article:
   </p>
   <blockquote>
    <p>
     <em>
      Also, dont take it too seriously
     </em>
    </p>
   </blockquote>
   <p>
    With one hell of a rant. Bravo sir.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a href="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/4.png">
     <img alt="the archer" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1473" height="398" src="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/4.png" width="506"/>
    </a>
   </p>
   <p>
    5) However, whilst The Archers comment was a fantastic one, the winners comment pretty much summed up my article in one. Maybe he should have written it. Well done Wes Blackwood.
   </p>
   <p>
    <a href="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/5.png">
     <img alt="wes blackwood" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1474" height="123" src="http://half-empty.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/5.png" width="601"/>
    </a>
   </p>
