          <p>
           What would you do if you suddenly found out, the advice youd always been given about what it takes to get an awesome body was well, WRONG?
          </p>
          <p>
           And what if I told you once you STOPPED doing these so called fitness truths, youd FINALLY be able to start seeing some real results would the truth finally set you free?
          </p>
          <p>
           As a certified personal trainer and nutritionist whose helped hundreds of people get the body of their dreams in record time, I know a thing or two about fitness
          </p>
          <p>
           So let me start by debunking 5 of the most common myths in fitness today which are destroying your chances of ever being toned and sculpted  and I guarantee youre probably doing at least ONE of these (if not all) right now!
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="mistake no 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1914" height="60" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/no-1.jpg" style="border: 0;" width="373"/>

          <p>
           <img alt="long sessions of cadio" class="size-full wp-image-1512 alignright" height="150" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/long-sessions-of-cadio.jpg" style="margin: 0 0 8px 8px; border: 0;" width="120"/>
           We all know that cardio is important for fat loss but heres a big tip for you  not all cardio was created equal. Doing long sessions of steady-state cardio not only eats away at your workout time (and is boring) but it also eats into your muscle too!
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre trying to get a great physique you need to be maintaining your muscle and only burning fat. Long cardio sessions only allow you to continually burning fat for a maximum of 3 to 4 hours after your workout. But, Ill reveal a foolproof method which continues to burn fat for up to 24hours or more plus youll be shocked at how little time you spend actually training!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="doing isolated body exercises" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1915" height="60" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/no-2.jpg" style="border: 0;" width="305"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="isolated exercise" class="size-full wp-image-1513 alignright" height="150" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/isolated-exercise.jpg" style="margin: 8px 0 8px 8px; border: 0;" width="120"/>
           When youre training for fat loss, the REAL secret is NOT working muscles in isolation, but rather working several muscle groups together to maximize how much energy and therefore fat youre burning. Compound exercises exert much more effort leading to increased fat loss. Plus, working several muscles together has the knock-on effect of drastically reducing your workout time.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="doing sit ups and ab exercises to get a six pack" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1916" height="61" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/no-3.jpg" style="border: 0; margin-top: 70px;" width="475"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="sit ups" class="size-full wp-image-1514 alignright" height="150" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sit-ups.gif" style="margin: 8px 0 8px 8px; border: 0;" width="120"/>
           If youve been trying to sculpt your stomach by doing loads of sit ups and other ab exercises you need to stop NOW, because these exercise cant burn off the layer of fat sitting on your belly! Yes, ab exercises will help tone the muscles, but they cant in themselves burn fat.
          </p>
          <p>
           Instead, your focus should be on doing specific fat burning exercises to burn the fat from your entire body  including your abs! Having a specific, solid action plan to burn off the layer of fat from your body is an absolute must if you want to get fabulous abs  and in a minute Ill show you exactly how to do this and get seriously sculpted abs at the same time.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="doing the same workouts week after week" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1917" height="60" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/no-4.jpg" style="border: 0;" width="439"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="bench-press" class="size-full wp-image-1515 alignright" height="150" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/bench-press.jpg" style="margin: 8px 0 8px 8px; border: 0;" width="121"/>
           When you keep doing the same workouts week in week out, your body adapts and becomes used to ityoull hit a plateau, get stuck and quickly become de-motivated. Simply changing routines and varying your intensity will keep your body guessing and will prevent your body from hitting the dreaded plateau!
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="over training, working out for too long or too often" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1918" height="61" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/no-5.jpg" style="border: 0; margin-top: 70px;" width="532"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="over training" class="size-full wp-image-1516 alignright" height="150" src="http://lovesexyabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/over-training.gif" style="margin: 8px 0 8px 8px; border: 0;" width="120"/>
           Rest is just as important as training, as this is when your body grows and repairs muscle tissue. Lack of sufficient rest will prevent your body from repairing itself and becoming fitter and stronger. Also quantity never beats quality, so if youre training correctly using scientifically proven methods, then you really dont need to do more than 3  4 sessions a week.
          </p>
          <p>
           So there you have it  5 very common but dangerous fitness myths which really will prevent you from getting the body you want
          </p>
          <p>
           So now you know what you shouldnt do, youre probably wondering what you SHOULD do instead right?
          </p>
          <p>
           If you have been training for weeks and are still wondering why you are not seeing the changes you want in your body it is time for a change. I will show you exactly how to super charge your workouts and burn serious amounts of fat and get an amazingly lean and ripped body in no time at all. This easy to do method will cut your workouts in half and literally turn your body into a fat burning machine.
          </p>
