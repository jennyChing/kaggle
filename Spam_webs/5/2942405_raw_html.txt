          The construction industry in the US has been suffering for the last 3-5 years and it is mainly attributed to the economic unrest during 2007-2008.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          You may have heard of the increased in foreclosure rates during 2007-2008, which led to the subprime crisis.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          The US government induced funds through special loans as part of the rescue plans but up until now, the industry is still suffering.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
         </div>
         <div>
          Nevertheless quality in construction should not be compromised.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          Using high quality diamond tools such as
          <a href="http://www.gilatools.com/diamond-core-bits.html">
           diamond core bits
          </a>
          and diamond blades should always be top of mind when it comes to your construction needs.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          The impact of the unrest should not impact the quality of work and the worth of the structures that you are constructing.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          Having the right diamond tool is an excellent investment and is one way of showing your clients that you value quality.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          Anyways, working with dependable tools will translate to faster and more accurate jobs.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          You will definitely get your moneys worth and will absolutely give you more projects!
         </div>
         <div>
          Although the construction industry has taken a huge hit in the past 5 years, private construction is heading upwards according to Ken Simonson, the chief economist of Associated General Contractors of America.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          This is a good indication of a rising industry.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          TAKE ADVANTAGE!
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
         </div>
         <div>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/3.bp.blogspot.com/--Q2Yb4YO3oU/T8hX0hv6eMI/AAAAAAAAAMY/QeyW8IS65KY/s1600/Economic+turmoil+2.png" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://i0.wp.com/3.bp.blogspot.com/--Q2Yb4YO3oU/T8hX0hv6eMI/AAAAAAAAAMY/QeyW8IS65KY/s1600/Economic+turmoil+2.png', '']);" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">

          </a>
          During times of crisis, there is always an
          <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
           <i style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">
            opportunity
           </i>
           that opens
          </b>
          .
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          You can niche your construction business by providing homes, buildings and other construction projects at a minimal cost but with utmost quality.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          You can do this by making sure that your people are working efficiently; thus a need for high quality diamond tools.
         </div>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: Cambria; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US;">
           It all depends on
           <i style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">
            how you perceive
           </i>
           the current situation around you.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           How you react to the challenges of these times will make a huge difference.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Its either you see challenges as an impediment or an opportunity.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Nonetheless dont loose the quality of your work.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Choose only the best partners.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Invest on high quality and dependable diamond core bits and other diamond tools for your construction needs.
