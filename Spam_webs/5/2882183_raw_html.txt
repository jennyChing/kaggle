             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://www.keepingitsimplecrafts.com/2013/10/monster-paper-bag-puppets.html">
               Monster Paper Bag Puppets
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <a data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.keepingitsimplecrafts.com%2F2013%2F10%2Fmonster-paper-bag-puppets.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Flh5.ggpht.com%2F-mxweXmHBSnY%2FUlyfwqmKekI%2FAAAAAAAASzI%2FxGheZFSjyhk%2Fmonster%252520paper%252520bag%252520puppets_thumb%25255B1%25255D.jpg%3Fimgmax%3D800&amp;description=Keeping%20it%20Simple%3A%20Monster%20Paper%20Bag%20Puppets">
               <img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               Lately my kids have been really wanting to do some kind of craft. I have been busy lately and haven't done one with them for a couple weeks. So I came up with this idea. It was super easy to do, very little prep and the kids had a blast.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-_pghg7ASEWA/UlyfuV6881I/AAAAAAAASzA/aQ8MnZ8DeZI/s1600-h/monster%252520paper%252520bag%252520puppets%25255B3%25255D.jpg">
                <img alt="monster paper bag puppets" border="0" height="513" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-mxweXmHBSnY/UlyfwqmKekI/AAAAAAAASzI/xGheZFSjyhk/monster%252520paper%252520bag%252520puppets_thumb%25255B1%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; border-right: 0px; border-top: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="monster paper bag puppets" width="754"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               All you need is some paper bags, paper, glue and
               <a href="http://www.craftprojectideas.com/" rel="nofollow">
                wiggly eyes
               </a>
               .
               <br/>
               <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-9799r-1Ckg4/Ulyfyw1eSoI/AAAAAAAASzQ/m0tBYLTabV4/s1600-h/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525282%252529%25255B10%25255D.jpg">
                <img alt="monster puppets (2)" border="0" height="471" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-xB_IoWzz418/Ulyf0_8UtwI/AAAAAAAASzY/qrbLIxargR0/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525282%252529_thumb%25255B7%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; border-right: 0px; border-top: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="monster puppets (2)" width="704"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               I let everyone do their own thing and make their own puppet.
               <br/>
               <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-6tUTCfm2EOM/Ulyf7wYgizI/AAAAAAAASzg/vt2lfz5Odko/s1600-h/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525283%252529%25255B8%25255D.jpg">
                <img alt="monster puppets (3)" border="0" height="471" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-IfuKKf5AmQs/Ulyf9duHIzI/AAAAAAAASzo/jzHtq32B0xI/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525283%252529_thumb%25255B5%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; border-right: 0px; border-top: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="monster puppets (3)" width="704"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               Here are my two oldest boys': Iron Man and Herobrine from MineCraft.
               <br/>
               <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-eHvEymCTYHY/Ulyf_JqxqLI/AAAAAAAASzw/1iA5-0wAPb0/s1600-h/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525281%252529%25255B11%25255D.jpg">
                <img alt="monster puppets (1)" border="0" height="529" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-PmtXFrtI2ng/UlygAUMO3YI/AAAAAAAASz4/Gh5GsaR6ROc/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525281%252529_thumb%25255B8%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; border-right: 0px; border-top: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="monster puppets (1)" width="354"/>
               </a>
               <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-odNagS5gb8M/UlygBo4g2eI/AAAAAAAAS0A/mpltP_STOGI/s1600-h/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525284%252529%25255B9%25255D.jpg">
                <img alt="monster puppets (4)" border="0" height="529" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-DUjAmIvkplY/UlygDeFv7aI/AAAAAAAAS0I/8Z2laAv-yLw/monster%252520puppets%252520%2525284%252529_thumb%25255B6%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; border-right: 0px; border-top: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="monster puppets (4)" width="354"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               I would love it if you followed along.
              </div>
