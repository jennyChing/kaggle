           <h2 class=" small_title " style="color:#">
            Why the Ralph Lauren Girls Fashion Show Reminded One Mom of Her Own Childhood
           </h2>
           <h3>
           </h3>
          </div>
          <div class="contentWrapper">
           <p>
            The
            <a href="http://www.elizabethstreet.com/style/Ralph-Lauren-girls-show-2013?section=home%20" target="_blank">
             Ralph Lauren Girls Fall Fashion show
            </a>
            , which Jessica Alba recently hosted at the Wolffer Estates in Sagaponack, NY, was a throwback for some of the moms in attendance.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            For early school day mornings, Elliott Grace will undoubtedly be wearing Fair Isle sweater dresses with her Mary-Janes," says New York mom and UK-transplant,
            <a href="http://www.elizabethstreet.com/mom/alice-ryan" target="_blank">
             Alice Ryan.
            </a>
            My all-American little girl must channel her mother's British heritage from time to time! Fair Isle knits remind me of my childhood, and chilly mornings in London walking with my mother to school.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            From braided hair to
            <a href="http://www.elizabethstreet.com/style/ralph-lauren-girls-shana-zappa?section=home%20" target="_blank">
             leopard prints
            </a>
            mixed with plaid shirts and wooly hats, there was a trend for every mother-daughter fashionista duo.
            <br/>
            Shop the looks from the show in the slideshow above, and tell us what your favorite look from the show was in the comments below.
           </p>
