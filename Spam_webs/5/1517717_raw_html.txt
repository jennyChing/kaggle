       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Preteen Boys room REVEAL: Gray, Orange, Blue (decorating boys room)
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-08-10T08:10:00+00:00">
         August 10, 2012
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/author/clev4586/" rel="author">
           Tracie
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-23.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (23)" border="0" height="399" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-23_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (23)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is hard to believe.my bear is in 7th grade this year. He is turning into such a kind and funny young man. I was thrilled to work on his room with him this summer. We made it into a space that is creative and funbut at the same time peaceful and chill as he would say. I have a lot of projects to share with you from this space.here are some pictures of the reveal..
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-4.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (4)" border="0" height="733" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-4_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (4)" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Before the room was brown and kind of beat up. We took some time to fix the wallsI repainted all the trim also. The carpet needs to be replacedbut we got a fun new area rug for the time being.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-3.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (3)" border="0" height="733" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-3_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (3)" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         His large sleigh bed took up so much room.so I decided to replace the bed. I made this bed with
         <a href="http://columbiaforestproducts.com/PureBond">
          Purebond plywood
         </a>
         and some steel pipe. It is a hip new look.for not much cash. Easy project too! The bed is framed with some cedar planks that I nailed to the wall. In the center is some fun action shots of him playing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-2.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (2)" border="0" height="146" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-2_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (2)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The gray on the wall plays nicely with the pops of blue and orange. It feels very fresh and fun!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-1.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (1)" border="0" height="525" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-1_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (1)" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The chill zone is a definite favorite. The orange futon can pull out for any overnight guest. It has also become nice spot to sit and read. All his year books are displayed on a custom made shelf. Above that I painted a square blue and stuck on some corkboards for him to collect is snapshots .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-6.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (6)" border="0" height="399" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-6_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (6)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-5.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (5)" border="0" height="399" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-5_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (5)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-8.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (8)" border="0" height="473" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-8_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (8)" width="338"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-7.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (7)" border="0" height="459" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-7_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (7)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-10.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (10)" border="0" height="399" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-10_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (10)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-11.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (11)" border="0" height="473" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-11_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (11)" width="315"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-15.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (15)" border="0" height="451" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-15_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (15)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-14.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (14)" border="0" height="450" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-14_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (14)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-18.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (18)" border="0" height="382" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-18_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (18)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/2012/08/diy-light-do-it-yourself-light/">
          custom made lighting
         </a>
         is one of my favorites. I will have a post coming soon!! So easy, uber chic, cheap and fun!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-19.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (19)" border="0" height="473" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-19_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (19)" width="446"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-21.jpg">
          <img alt="gray and orange boys room (21)" border="0" height="136" src="http://cleverlyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/gray-and-orange-boys-room-21_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="gray and orange boys room (21)" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The room is a true reflection of the bear. It is so fun, creative and full of all the things he loves.
        </p>
        <p>
         Where I found some things for this room:
        </p>
        <p>
         The color on the walls is Cement Gray by Martha Stewart (glidden paints)
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.target.com/p/sofa-bed-futon-orange/-/A-12184629">
          Orange futon is from Target
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pbteen.com/products/basketball-hoop-wall-mural/">
          Bball mural is PBTeen
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.overstock.com/Home-Garden/Dali-Native-Bamboo-Roman-Shade-33-in.-x-74-in./3297232/product.html">
          Bamboo Shades from Overstock.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedepot.com/webapp/wcs/stores/servlet/ProductDisplay?storeId=10051&amp;langId=-1&amp;catalogId=10053&amp;productId=203284343&amp;R=203284343">
          Jean area rug from the Home Depot
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Bedding is Tommy Hilfiger at TJMaxx
        </p>
        <p>
         All pillows are from Target or Homegoods
        </p>
        <p>
         More projects coming soon from this room redo!
