    <h2>
     <a href="http://m-sewing.com/" title="sewing patterns">
      Home
     </a>
     /
     <a href="http://m-sewing.com/patterns-catalog/women.html" title="Women">
      Women
     </a>
     /
     <a href="http://m-sewing.com/patterns-catalog/women/dresses.html" title="Dresses">
      Dresses
     </a>
     / #5160 Special Occasion Slip Dress
    </h2>
    <img alt="example - #5160 Special Occasion Slip Dress" border="0" src="http://m-sewing.com//public/images/patterns/1435.gif" style="float:left;"/>
    <img alt="preview - #5160 Special Occasion Slip Dress" border="0" src="http://m-sewing.com//public/images/patterns/1434.gif" style="float:right;"/>
    <div style="clear:left;">
     <p>
      <b>
       Difficulty:
       <span class="diff">
        **
       </span>
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       You will need:
      </b>
      Lightweight, well-draping fabric for dress, lining fabric, 1 zipper.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       Attention!
      </b>
      Fabric consumption varies depending on the size of garment. First of all, print out paper patterns and lay them out at the width of fabric/ knit you plan to use, to see how much fabric you will need.
     </p>
     <p>
      <i>
       When sewing the garment, pay attention to notches- they must coincide.
      </i>
     </p>
     <h4>
      Cutting:
     </h4>
     <h5>
      Fabric for Dress:
     </h5>
     <p>
      1. Top Back - 2 pieces
      <br/>
      2. Top Front  1 piece with fold
      <br/>
      3. Front Panel  1 piece with fold
      <br/>
      4. Back Panel  2 pieces
      <br/>
      5. Shoulder Strip 2 pieces
     </p>
     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Note:
       </b>
       Front Panel and Back Panel pieces must be cut at bias.
      </i>
     </p>
     <h5>
      Lining Fabric:
     </h5>
     <p>
      6. Top Back - 2 pieces
      <br/>
      7. Top Front  1 piece with fold
      <br/>
      8. Front Panel  1 piece with fold
      <br/>
      9. Back Panel  2 pieces
     </p>
     <h4>
      Instructions:
     </h4>
     <p>
      1.Sew darts, spread allowances of darts evenly to both sides and press them open.
     </p>
     <p>
      2. Sew side seams, sew top of the dress to bottom.
     </p>
     <p>
      3. Sew center back seam from bottom to notch marking zipper. Sew zipper in.
     </p>
     <p>
      4. Fold shoulder strips in half lengthwise, face side inside, sew at a long side, turn out and press.
Baste shoulder strips to corners of Top Fronts.
     </p>
     <p>
      5. Sew darts at lining pieces. Sew lining pieces together.
     </p>
     <p>
      6. Place the dress and lining dress face-to-face. Turn under seam allowances of lining at center back and pin them to neckline. Sew along armholes and neckline edges, picking the shoulder strips. Turn lining to the inside; hand-sew lining to zipper tape. Hand-sew lining to seam allowances of waist (seam attaching top to bottom).
     </p>
     <p>
      7. Try the dress on, adjust the length of shoulder strips. Hand-sew loose ends of strips under the top corners of Top Back.
     </p>
     <p>
      8. Let the dress hang for one day, then even out hem if necessary.
     </p>
     <p>
      9. Press hem allowance to the inside and hand-sew with hemming stitches.
Hem lining 1.5 cm (6/8) shorter than dress itself.
     </p>
