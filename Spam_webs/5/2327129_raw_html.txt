        <header class="entry-header">
         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Startling 2D Face Art  Womens Faces Transformed into Paintings
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           Written by
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/author/chad/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Chad
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-categories">
           in
           <a href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/category/art-photography/" rel="category tag">
            Art &amp; Photography
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <img alt="face-painting-women-surreal01" class="post-image entry-image attachment-content-wide wp-post-image" height="960" itemprop="image" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/face-painting-women-surreal01.jpg" width="640"/>
         <p>
          The interesting thing about art is that everyone interprets it differently.
         </p>
         <p>
          When people look at these women, some see fine art  emulations of famous paintings, and something of beauty to be admired. Other people, well they see this artwork as something slightly different. Something probably best left to the dark reaches of our minds, and the niche sites of the internet.
         </p>
         <p>
          But back to the topic at hand;
          <strong>
           women painted to look like paintings
          </strong>
          , and in particular, famous paintings and pop-culture icons. I dont know the artist responsible for the first piece, but I certainly recognize it. After all, it was hanging in my friends parents basement for many years. In any case, theyre all somewhat recognizable, and perhaps youll agree that theyre all pretty impressive.
         </p>
         <h2>
          But are they Real?
          <br/>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Define real what is real? If real is what you can see and touch, well this artwork may or may not be that. Have a look through, and Ill explain a little later.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="imageclose-65148">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-65148">
             <img alt="incredible face painting" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-65155" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/face-painting-women-surreal08-566x849.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>

          </a>

           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Imagine an entire movie where the actors are painted like this, and the sets are digitally altered to match. It might look like a moving painting, and if you ask me, that would be pretty cool. I dont mean stereo-scoped like
          <a href="https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/scanner_darkly/" target="_blank">
           A Scanner Darkly
          </a>
          , but instead an actual painted style.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a name="imageclose-65149">
           <div class="lb-album">

             <img alt="woman painted face" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-65154" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/face-painting-women-surreal07-566x849.jpg"/>

           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Im a sucker for pixelized styling, so let me just say that though this isnt my favorite, I like it a lot. Now, enjoy the rest, and well meet up at the end to discuss my confusion.
         </p>

          <a name="imageclose-65150">

         <p>
          Im confused, and Im going to tell you why. You see, these faces were painted by Valeriya Kutsan, and photographed by Alexander Khokhlov. They mention using paint, and this and that, to acheieve the effect, but they also mention doing careful retouching as well. So I ask again, are these real, or are they mostly done digitally?
         </p>
         <p>
          I truly want to know, and I also want to
          <strong>
           hear your opinion in the comments below!
          </strong>
         </p>
