    <p>
     Start with four raindrops falling on a few different surfaces. Multiply that sound sequence by itself, and then that product by itself a couple more times. In under a minute you have the sound of a mid-afternoon shower right at your fingertips. Sound artist
     <a href="http://yugop.com/">
      Yugo Nakamura
     </a>
     does just that in his inspired short, Amaoto no Yurai, or The Origin of the Sound of Rain.
