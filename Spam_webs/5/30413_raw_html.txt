       <h6>
        <em>
         OH THE FOOLISHNESS OF YOUTH
        </em>
       </h6>
       <p>
        What man can resist the most powerful force in the universe? The force to reproduce. Simply stated thats the force which menmust deal with their entire lives. Oh, we fill our lives with other things but "
        <a class="populated" href="/title/reproduce" title="reproduce">
         the force
        </a>
        " is always working on us. It lies dormant in our bodies for our first few years but then it springs to life as a seed. And from theseed is born the
        <a class="populated" href="/title/monster" title="monster">
         monster
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="populated" href="/title/History" title="History">
         History
        </a>
        tells the story. The
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Bible" title="Bible">
         Bible
        </a>
        records several stories of interest. First,
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Adam+and+Eve" title="Adam and Eve">
         Adam and Eve
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Adam was beguiled by Eve. However you interpret this story, theirs was a complex relationship. First Adam was created alone. Then because of his loneliness,
        <a class="populated" href="/title/God" title="God">
         God
        </a>
        created Eve. Then everything went to hell. They were both kicked out of the
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Garden+of+Eden" title="Garden of Eden">
         Garden of Eden
        </a>
        as their punishment. Was "the force" behind all their undoing? Maybe....no, probably. They did have two sons,
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Cain+and+Abel" title="Cain and Abel">
         Cain and Abel
        </a>
        . However, Cain killed Abel out of
        <a class="populated" href="/title/jealousy" title="jealousy">
         jealousy
        </a>
        . What a mess.
       </p>
       <p>
        Next, we read of
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Samson" title="Samson">
         Samson
        </a>
        and
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Delilah" title="Delilah">
         Delilah
        </a>
        . Samsons long hair was the source of his strength but Delilah knew about "the force". Samson didnt stand a chance and neither do we.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Antony+and+Cleopatra" title="Antony and Cleopatra">
         Antony and Cleopatra
        </a>
        ? You remember their story.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="populated" href="/title/John+F.+Kennedy" title="John F. Kennedy">
         John F. Kennedy
        </a>
        and
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Marilyn+Monroe" title="Marilyn Monroe">
         Marilyn Monroe
        </a>
        ?
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Bill+Clinton" title="Bill Clinton">
         Bill Clinton
        </a>
        and
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Monica+Lewinsky" title="Monica Lewinsky">
         Monica Lewinsky
        </a>
        ?
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="populated" href="/title/J.+Howard+Marshall+II" title="J. Howard Marshall II">
         J. Howard Marshall II
        </a>
        and
        <a class="populated" href="/title/Anna+Nicole+Smith" title="Anna Nicole Smith">
         Anna Nicole Smith
        </a>
        ?
       </p>
       <p>
        And we as men are going to conquer " the force"? I think not.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here are somewords of wisdom for us.
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Recognize that "the force" is greater than we are and greater than well ever be.
        </li>
        <li>
         Dont be too hard on yourself: its a male problem we all have. (well, atleast those of us who are
         <a class="populated" href="/title/heterosexual" title="heterosexual">
          heterosexual
         </a>
         )
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Dont be too smug in thinkingwe have "the force" under control. Pretty girls are aligned with "the force" and they have evolved in ways that take advantage of us.
        </li>
        <li>
         If all else fails, we should find an ugly girlwith whom we can be happy. Have some kids. Have a life.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        Remember if it werent for pretty girls plus "the force" none of us would be here.
       </p>
