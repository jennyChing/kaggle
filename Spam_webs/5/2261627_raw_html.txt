         </span>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          My youngest loves to craft. In fact, pretty much every afternoon he heads for our cabinets, pulls out the glue gun and looks at me expectantly. However, his thing here recently has been making crafts that can be his pets. Hes been asking me to make a bunny for quite some time and I just hadnt com up with the way that I wanted to do it. But as he was waiting patiently the other day, I happened across one of my favorite crafting supplies  pom poms  and inspiration hit me. Out of just a few items that we already had at home, we were able to put together an adorable bunny craft that is perfect for little hands! Heres how you can make your Easy Pom Bunnies too!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Easy-Pom-Bunny-Easter-Craft-for-Kids.jpg">
           <img alt="Easy Pom Bunny Easter Craft for Kids" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27769" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Easy-Pom-Bunny-Easter-Craft-for-Kids.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            What You Will Need:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          White Pom Poms in Small, Medium and Large Sizes
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Googly Eyes
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Pink and White Crafting Foam Sheets
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Pink Pipe Cleaners
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Hot Glue and a Hot Glue Gun
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Pom Bunny Craft 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27770" height="741" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-1.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            How to Make It:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Start by cutting out ear shapes from both the white foam and the pink foam. They dont have to be perfect and you can free hand them  just make sure the pink parts are small enough to fit into the white ears. Once they are all cut out, glue your ears together. Then glue your googly eyes to your medium sized ball. Next glue your ears to the top of that same ball.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Pom Bunny Craft 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27771" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-2.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Once your head is ready, put a small dab of glue on the bottom and secure it to the top of your large pom pom.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-3.jpg">
           <img alt="Pom Bunny Craft 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27772" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-3.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Make sure it stands on its own and then glue two of your smaller pom poms on the front for feet and one on the back for a tail! Finally cut short pieces of your pipe cleaner and glue two together for each side of the face.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-4.jpg">
           <img alt="Pom Bunny Craft 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27773" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-4.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Add hot glue to the end of your pipe cleaner vs and secure them to each side of the bunnys face for whiskers. And thats it! Your done!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-5.jpg">
           <img alt="Pom Bunny Craft 5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27774" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-5.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Now if your kids are older and want to do this craft alone, just use regular glue so there is no chance of them getting burned! But if you have little ones like I do, youll be thrilled to watch them stare in wonder as you help them place together their new bunny friend!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Then all thats left to do is play away with your family of bunnies that are sure to make Easter more fun!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-6.jpg">
           <img alt="Pom Bunny Craft 6" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27775" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Bunny-Craft-6.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Happy Crafting!
         </p>
