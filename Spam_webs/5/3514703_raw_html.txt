            This is definitely the stuff cyclists dream about, especially if you consider the lack of respect and attention they receive from drivers while theyre pedaling the streets, minding their own business without bothering anybody, not even the environment.
           </p>

            <img alt="hisZGpy" class=" size-full wp-image-416 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i0.wp.com/gipsy.ninja/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/hisZGpy.gif?resize=640%2C331"/>

           <p>
            The UK has over 19,000 accidents involving cyclists each year, most of them happening during the night when visibility levels are very low.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://i2.wp.com/gipsy.ninja/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/3skQMrf.gif">
             <img alt="3skQMrf" class=" size-full wp-image-419 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i2.wp.com/gipsy.ninja/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/3skQMrf.gif?resize=640%2C334"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Given the fact that everybody wants to live in a greener city and an eco-friendly world, we have to pay more attention to cyclists because they are part of the people that are making the world a better and safer place to live in.
           </p>
           <p>
            The invisible spray is the simple answer to this very complex problem. Now drivers will be able to see cyclists during the night, and cyclists will be able to pedal away freely, without the fear of getting hit or even killed by a car.
           </p>
