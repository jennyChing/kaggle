          <span class="print-link">
          </span>
          <div class="field field-name-field-image field-type-image field-label-hidden">
           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even">
             <img alt="Provenal Roasted Potatoes" src="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/roast_potatoes.png?itok=rcy7HIpB" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
            </div>

          <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
             <p>
              Simplicity is the key for most good things in life and that includes what happens in the kitchen. What's easier than a hunk of warm baguette, fresh berries, and a half wheel of Brie? Nothing! And those three make a perfect picnic, especially for an afternoon lazing under a parasol on a quiet Ontario beach.
             </p>
             <p>
              Okay, maybe the daydreams have taken hold, BUT THE COLD'S GOTTEN TO ME, PEOPLE, and I'm
              <strong>
               &lt;this&gt;
              </strong>
              close to waving the white flag.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/food/20130319/potato-peeling-tips-from-the-canned-soup-mom?s=bp">
               <strong>
                <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u25/lips_icon.gif"/>
                Quick Tips To Make Peeling Potatoes Easier
               </strong>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              When I start picturing warm afternoons  it's a Canadian winter survival tool  I almost always think of holidays with my family, and many of those were spent in the south of France. The south of France smells of oregano and thyme warmed by the sun, it's a breeze that cools the air under olive groves, and it's meals the family can quickly prepare together so that more time can be spent playing outdoors. Nothing complicated, no matter what the cookbooks and movies might show us.
             </p>
             <p>
              When you're stuck for a side dish to a
              <a href="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/blogs/katja-wulfers-around-the-table/20150118/pork-tenderloin-with-apples-brie" target="_blank">
               beautiful meal
              </a>
              you've spent time preparing, the last thing you have time for is a 100-step process that results in a lukewarm reception by hungry hordes. I have yet to meet a person who doesn't like Provenal roasted potatoes.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Ingredients
              </strong>
             </p>
             <div>
              5 medium Yukon Gold potatoes
             </div>
             <div>
              1/3 cup olive oil
             </div>
             <div>
              1 teaspoon minced garlic or 1-2 cloves finely chopped
             </div>
             <div>
              1/2 - 3/4 level teaspoon salt
             </div>
             <div>
              1/2 level teaspoon pepper
             </div>
             <div>
              1/2 teaspoon Herbes de Provence (a blend of dried thyme, rosemary, basil, oregano, and marjoram)
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <p>
              <img alt="roast potatoes, herb roasted potatoes, easy roasted potatoes, easy potato recipe, best potato recipes, Around The Table, katja wulfers" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/DSC_5467.jpg" style="width: 420px; height: 279px;"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               <img alt="roast potatoes, herb roasted potatoes, easy roasted potatoes, easy potato recipe, best potato recipes, Around The Table, katja wulfers" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/DSC_5505.jpg" style="width: 420px; height: 554px;"/>
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Directions
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/article%20flower%20%281%29.jpg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Peel and cut potatoes into chunks.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/article%20flower%20%281%29.jpg" style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Pour olive oil over potatoes and add garlic.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/article%20flower%20%281%29.jpg" style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and Provenal herbs. Toss lightly and set aside until oven reaches 400F/205C.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/article%20flower%20%281%29.jpg" style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Place potatoes onto a baking sheet covered in parchment paper and roast for 30-35 minutes, or until golden.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               Makes 4 servings.
              </b>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Bon apptit!
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <img alt="roast potatoes, herb roasted potatoes, easy roasted potatoes, easy potato recipe, best potato recipes, Around The Table, katja wulfers" src="/sites/default/files/u5046/DSC_5515.jpg" style="width: 420px; height: 563px;"/>
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              Another French classic that turns potatoes into a love potion  It's
              <em>
               conceivable!
              </em>
               is
              <a href="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/blogs/katja-wulfers-around-the-table/20141123/leek-potato-and-carrot-soup" target="_blank">
               <strong>
                Leek, Potato, and Carrot soup
               </strong>
              </a>
              , especially when topped with bacon.
             </p>
