      <h2 class="posttitle">
       Difference Between Obama and Bush foreign policy
      </h2>
      <p class="postmetadata">
        Categorized under
       <a href="http://www.differencebetween.net/category/business/" rel="category tag">
        Business
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.differencebetween.net/category/miscellaneous/politics/leaders/" rel="category tag">

       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.differencebetween.net/category/miscellaneous/politics/" rel="category tag">

       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.differencebetween.net/business/difference-between-obama-and-bush-foreign-policy/" title="Difference Between Obama and Bush foreign policy">
        Difference Between Obama and Bush foreign policy
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="entry clearfloat">
       <p>
        <strong>
         <img alt="obama-bush" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4790" height="83" src="http://cdn.differencebetween.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/obama-bush.jpg" title="obama-bush" width="200"/>
         Obamas foreign policy vs Bush foreign policy
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Many critics have stated that Americas foreign policy was not going to change despite a change in the countrys leadership. However, the foreign policies adopted by different presidents have differed in various respects. Certain variations can also be seen between the foreign policies of the Obama and Bush administrations.
       </p>
       <p>
        Both the Bush administration and the Obama administration have committed to a foreign policy that ensures the safety of Americans. However, the Bush presidency was marked by tensions in all spheres. In the first few months of his presidency, Obama has not faced any serious foreign policy issues. He has vowed to commit himself and his administration to a foreign policy that ensures the safety of Americans.
       </p>
       <p>
        Bush was clear in declaring war on Iraq stating that the country was a threat to the world. His foreign policy was to pump more troops into
        <a href="http://www.differencebetween.net/miscellaneous/politics/difference-between-afghanistan-and-iraq-war/" title="iraq war vs afghan war">
         Iraq
        </a>
        . But Obama has stated clearly that his mission will be to end the war in Iraq and focus on the challenges faced by that country. He has said the mission of US forces in Iraq will change drastically and that they will be engaged in training, equipping and advising the Iraqi Security Forces to meet future challenges.
       </p>
       <p>
        Regarding Americas attitude towards Muslims, Obama has been clear stating that America is not an enemy of the
        <a href="http://www.differencebetween.net/miscellaneous/difference-between-muslims-and-arabs/" title="muslim vs arab">
         Muslim
