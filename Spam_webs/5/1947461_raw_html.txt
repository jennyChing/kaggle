              <a href="https://www.facebook.com/organicpools" style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: #e03f00;" target="_blank">
               David Pagan Butler
              </a>

             <p style="color: #777777;">
              What makes Davids ponds so special is that they are actually something called
              <a href="http://www.organicpools.co.uk/" style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: #e03f00;" target="_blank">
               Organic Pools
              </a>
              . These are natural swimming pools that allow the growth of wildlife and are environmentally friendly.
             </p>
             <p style="color: #777777;">
              <em style="font-weight: inherit;">
               The nutrient level in an organic pool is carefully restricted so competition for the limiting nutrient (usually phosphorous) is fierce. In these circumstances, pond plants outperform algae, keeping itsuppressedand barely hanging in at the margins. A pond, low in nutrients, is a healthy environment forwildlife. An organic pool is teeming with life.
              </em>
             </p>
             <div class="gallery galleryid-42992 gallery-columns-1 gallery-size-full" id="gallery-2" style="color: #777777;">
              <div class="gallery-item" style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit;">
               <div align="center">
                The organic pools are created in such a way so that they are safe to swim in, but dont restrict plant growth.
               </div>
               <div class="gallery-icon landscape" style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit;">
                <a href="http://www.viraldoza.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/natural-pool3.jpg">
