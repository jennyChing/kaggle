     <h1>
      How Long Can I Live With Nephrotic Syndrome
     </h1>
    </div>
    <div class="con_l2" id="zoom">
     <p>
      <span>
       <img alt="How Long Can I Live With Nephrotic Syndrome" src="/uploads/allimg/131121/6-131121105S2532.png" style="margin: 10px; width: 395px; height: 311px; float: left;"/>
       In most cases,
       <a href="http://www.igancure.com/nephrotic-syndrome/" target="_blank">
        <u>
         Nephrotic Syndrome
        </u>
       </a>
       relapses frequently and if left uncontrolled, it is more possible to develop into Kidney(Renal) Failure, even death. Facing with such terrible situation, patients always can not help asking : how long can I live with nephrotic syndrome on earth? Is there a treatment to prolong my life span effectively? Well, the answer is expected to be showed in the below content.
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       How long can I live with nephrotic syndrome?
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Firstly, we have to say there is no a certain time to this question because patients life span can be affected by various factors and the life expectancy of nephrotic syndrome varies from individual to individual. Among these factors, patients disease severity, daily diet, healthy care and treatment are the most important. Of course, if you want to know more informations just depend on your own illness condition, please send your blood test, urine test, physical examination,etc to
      <a href="mailto: igacure@gmail.com">
       igacure@gmail.com
      </a>
      as soon as possible, after a careful study, we will design a treatment plan just for you.
     </p>
     <p>
      To be honest, how long patients with nephrotic syndrome can survive mostly depends on the treatment method.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       1. Traditional therapy
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      In general, the conventional hormone therapy can relieve the symptoms of nephrotic syndrome like heavy loss in urine, edema ( swelling), hyperlipidaemia, hypoproteinemia at a fast speed indeed. However, once the hormone dosage is reduced or even stopped, this disease is more likely to relapse, what is worse, every relapse is a big trauma to the kidneys. This is because the hormone can only suppress the abnormal immune response, but can not clear up the immune complexes deposited in kidneys at all. To get rid of its disadvantages, the latest treatment
      <a href="http://www.igancure.com/Immunotherapy.html" target="_blank">
       <u>
        Immunotherapy
       </u>
      </a>
      is introduced into the treatment of nephrotic syndrome. If you want to know how does this therapy work, please click
      <a href="http://www.igancure.com/nephrotic-syndrome-treatment/172.html">
       Immunotherapy For Nephropathy Syndrome
      </a>
      , this article may be a great help to you.
     </p>
