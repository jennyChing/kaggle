                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Jeans basket weave into textured vest/jacket
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7949878021965982141" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Supplies: Pair of husband's old jeans.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-E3BMYnIsKXg/T5OGI_IZtWI/AAAAAAAABq8/1I6nKC1WF0Y/s1600/DSC_0297.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="520" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-E3BMYnIsKXg/T5OGI_IZtWI/AAAAAAAABq8/1I6nKC1WF0Y/s640/DSC_0297.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X-WIIYbItjY/T5OGe41yn2I/AAAAAAAABrE/CBONZlw79No/s1600/DSC_0355.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="594" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X-WIIYbItjY/T5OGe41yn2I/AAAAAAAABrE/CBONZlw79No/s640/DSC_0355.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YexN0IRvQPQ/T5OHCihNknI/AAAAAAAABrM/vr0cvqZg_9w/s1600/DSC_0354.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YexN0IRvQPQ/T5OHCihNknI/AAAAAAAABrM/vr0cvqZg_9w/s640/DSC_0354.JPG" width="524"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      Started to basket weave my husband's terribly old jeans. I wanted to make a jacket but ran out of denim for the sleeves. For now it is an amazingly textured vest. Look forward to finding another pair to add sleeves.
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-YxCdes-ndrM/T5rDyPSTbQI/AAAAAAAABs4/Tejyi-db-u8/s1600/2266b60a-9e52-4608-b07d-8f049e5a486awallpaper.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-YxCdes-ndrM/T5rDyPSTbQI/AAAAAAAABs4/Tejyi-db-u8/s640/2266b60a-9e52-4608-b07d-8f049e5a486awallpaper.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      Cut denim into panels. Rip strips leaving an edge intact. Cut extra strips and weave. Cut pattern. Pin all edges and sew all around...Put together.
                     </div>
