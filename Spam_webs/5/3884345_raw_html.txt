             <h1>
              The Current Presents: Chvrches
             </h1>
             <input id="title225492393" type="hidden" value="The Current Presents: Chvrches"/>
             <input id="modelShortUrl225492393" type="hidden" value="http://n.pr/1dEww0u"/>

              <p>
               The Scottish synth-pop trio
               <a href="http://www.npr.org/search/index.php?searchinput=chvrches">
                Chvrches
               </a>
               recently made its second visit to Minneapolis in three months. While in town, its members stopped by The Current's studios in St. Paul to perform songs from
               <em>
                The Bones of What You Believe
               </em>
                including this intimate version of the rousing anthem "Recover."
              </p>
