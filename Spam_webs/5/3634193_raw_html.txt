          Know the Eligibility Criteria of Canada Student Visa

         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://www.blog.opulentuz.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Canada-student-visa.jpg">
           <img alt="Canada Student visa" class="alignleft wp-image-8394" height="250" src="http://www.blog.opulentuz.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Canada-student-visa-300x283.jpg" style="margin: 0px;" title="Canada Student visa" width="300"/>

          Many students from abroad come to Canada every year with an intention of acquiring a degree which is internationally acclaimed. This move improves their chances of fetching them the right job in their near future. A
          <strong>
           study in Canada
          </strong>
          exposes you to the best in-built technology and contemporary teaching methodology. Canada is one of the leading immigration countries for such students looking out to develop and improvise on their knowledge and skills.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Canada is a very safe country, with clean ambience and strong infrastructure. The people here are very warm and friendly towards outsiders which make
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.opulentuz.com/immigration/canada/canada-student-visa-guidance/24" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.opulentuz.com']);" target="_blank" title="Indian Students in Canada">
             <span style="color: #0000ff;">
              Indian students in Canada
             </span>
            </a>
           </strong>
          </span>
          feel at home. If you too wish to be a part of all this and have this once in a lifetime experience of a multicultural environment,
          <strong>
           apply Canada Student Visa.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Canada Student visa
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          A
          <strong>
           Study Permit to Canada
          </strong>
          allows you to pursue your education in the country. However, you do not need it in case your program is of six months or lesser duration. Once you have the
          <strong>
           Student visa for Canada
          </strong>
          , you can opt for a renewal in case you want to continue with your studies. For full-time students at a recognized institution, you are allowed to work part-time on campus.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Canada Student Visa Requirements
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Following are some of the eligibility criteria for obtaining a Student Visa to Canada:-
         </p>
         <ul style="text-align: justify;">
          <li>
           Applicants of certain countries need to furnish biometric details
          </li>
          <li>
           Proof of medical check-up and police clearance certificate
          </li>
          <li>
           Valid passport to show your intention of going back to your native country after the completion of your course
          </li>
          <li>
           Sufficient funds to support yourself
          </li>
         </ul>
