        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Valentines Breakfast Tray
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-02-10T05:18:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 10, 2013
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Spoonful of Sugar
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         -
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/2013/02/valentines-breakfast-tray/#comments">
           8 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" data-pin-height="32" data-pin-shape="round" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Faspoonfulofsugardesigns.com%2F2013%2F02%2Fvalentines-breakfast-tray%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F3.bp.blogspot.com%2F-8S7EZWf1_Ds%2FUReLEdRzcFI%2FAAAAAAAAO54%2FkeTnQFsxeFQ%2Fs1600%2FValentines%2BTray%2B%2B%2B%2B%2B2.jpg&amp;description=Valentines%20Breakfast%20Tray" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9sHxoBjXtN0/URdy54vOA6I/AAAAAAAAO4M/oO0Vyk5wTts/s1600/Valentines+Tray++++1.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="542" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Valentines+Tray++++1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         If I am lucky, I usually score breakfast in bed twice a year  on Mothers Day and my birthday (both of which usually fall in the same week!). I usually need to do some pre-planning (such as stock up on croissants etc) to ensure a lovely breakfast. Of course, now that Sarah is very capable in the kitchen, she often whips up something special.
        </p>
        <p>
         When I was shopping at Target last week, I spotted some sweet Valentines inspired
         <a href="http://shop.target.com.au/p/red-heart-mug/P52951794" target="_blank">
          coffee mugs
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://shop.target.com.au/p/heart-shaped-plate/P52950216" target="_blank">
          heart shaped plates
         </a>
         . This got me to thinking that a Valentines Day breakfast in bed would be a great way to start the day. To make it really easy for Mr Sugar, I have the tray all decorated and ready to go.
        </p>
        <p>
         I gave some plain white
         <a href="http://shop.target.com.au/p/4-pack-napkins-white/P47486966" target="_blank">
          cotton napkins
         </a>
         , a Valentines make-over. I raided my ribbon stash and found three co-ordinating ribbons  white grosgrain, red polka dot, and gingham hearts.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-UOA7bFG0FHc/URd1icrKsUI/AAAAAAAAO4o/ot8KTycX9ro/s1600/Valentines+Napkins.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Valentines+Napkins.jpg" width="610"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I cut the ribbons so that they were the 1 inch longer than the width of the napkin. The ribbons were pinned 1/2 inch from thebottomedge of the napkin and stitched in place, approximately 1/8 inch apart. Ateachend, 1/2 inch of ribbon was tucked under.
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-2tUy2RrAw-U/URd3_9BqHnI/AAAAAAAAO5M/8GgtHFqH9Cc/s1600/Valentine+Napkin+31.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="500" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Valentine+Napkin+31.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com//2012/03/spray-paint-project/" target="_blank">
          A white tray
         </a>
         was lined with a pink tea towel and the Valentines napkin. The new Valentines coffee mug and plate are all washed and ready for brekkie.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-CMuwl6Gak1U/UReKYxlCKKI/AAAAAAAAO5o/GsBJCe0xzJI/s1600/Valentines+Tray+++3.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="500" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Valentines+Tray+++3.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         We found a sweet red polka dot porcelain spoon at
         <a href="http://www.plazastyle.com/minipla/" target="_blank">
          Minipla
         </a>
         (in Japan) which will be perfect for spooning some jam on our croissants.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-u49ltwKPb-k/UReKYl2AQ4I/AAAAAAAAO5s/8QxWkQ9etXM/s1600/Target+Coffee+Mug++1.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Target+Coffee+Mug++1.jpg" width="456"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         It is the little things that show someone you care. Setting a pretty tray for breakfast in bed makes it extra special.
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-8S7EZWf1_Ds/UReLEdRzcFI/AAAAAAAAO54/keTnQFsxeFQ/s1600/Valentines+Tray+++++2.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="440" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Valentines+Tray+++++2.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
