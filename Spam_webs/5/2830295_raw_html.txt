      <article class="post-1096 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail tag-adult-beverage tag-alcoholic tag-beverages tag-mixed-drink entry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Chocolate Russian
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-16T16:26:21+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 16, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/author/marilynclark" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            MarilynClark
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/2014/12/chocolate-russian.html#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         It was shortly after my 21st birthday that my girlfriends and I went out and I had my first White Russian. I was in heaven. The creaminess combined with kahlua with the punch from the vodka.yummo! I am putting together some of my menu items for the upcoming holidays and I got to thinking, what if we gave the old White Russian a chocolaty twist?  Yup, a Chocolate Russian!
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <span id="more-1096">
         </span>
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-collage.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Russian" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1176" height="650" src="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-collage.jpg" width="395"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         Now before I continue, this is for all of us that are 21 and overoh, and drink responsibly and never drink and drive, okay? Now, lets make a Chocolate Russian, shall we?
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         Ingredients:
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 oz
          <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/2013/11/baileys-irish-cream-homemadeholidays.html">
           Baileys Irish Cream
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          2 oz
          <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/2013/12/homemade-kahlua-kahlua-sugar.html">
           Kahlua
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          1 oz Vodka
         </li>
         <li>
          4 oz Chocolate milk
         </li>
         <li>
          Ice cubes
         </li>
        </ul>
        <div>
         <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-ingredients.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Russian Ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1177" height="573" src="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-ingredients.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <p>
         As always, start off by measuring out all your ingredients. My life is full of distractions so I find it very helpful to have each ingredient ready and set aside before I actually add them to my glass.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-measured-ingredients.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Russian Measured Ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1178" height="488" src="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-measured-ingredients.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, if you have a cocktail shaker, add all your ingredients and give them a good shake. Pour your drink into a glass and serve.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you dont, just add your ingredients in your glass and give them a good stir. Serve.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolat-Russian-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Russian" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1179" height="603" src="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolat-Russian-2.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Doesnt this look absolutely delicious. Heres a birds-eye view. Are you drooling yet? Its definitely a yummy way to end your day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Russian" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1180" height="488" src="http://www.4you-withlove.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/chocolate-russian-1.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         If you like this twist, check out my
         <a href="http://www.4you-withlove.com/2013/07/party-thyme-beat-heat-raspberry.html" title="Rasberry Amaretto Sour">
          Raspberry Amaretto Sour
         </a>
         . It too is absolutely delicious and I bet your holiday guests would love it as well!
        </div>
