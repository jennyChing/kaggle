       </div>
       <h3>
        A YOUNG woman died in the bath after checking Facebook on her iPhone.
       </h3>
       <section class="text-description">
        Evgenia Sviridenko, 24, had been charging the phone and scrolling through her Facebook account as she took a soak.
        <p>
         Her flatmate noticed she had been gone some time and when she checked she found Evgenias body floating in the water.
        </p>
        <p>
         Yaroslav Dubinina said: I noticed that she had been gone a while and when I couldnt raise her I opened the door and saw her floating on the water looking really pale.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4981" style="width: 1162px">
         <img alt="girl-phone-bath-died" class="wp-image-4981 size-full" height="768" src="http://www.magazinetoday.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/girl-phone-bath-died-196841.jpg" width="1152"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          SOCIAL MEDIA: Evgenia was checking her Facebook on her phone while in the bath [CEN]
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         I saw her phone which was on the charger and at the bottom of the bath, and I unplugged it and pulled her out.
        </p>
        <p>
         Her body was still shaking from the shock.
        </p>
        <p>
         The 23-year-old called for an ambulance immediately and told a team of doctors what had happened.
        </p>
        <p>
         Police are investigating why the phone charger may not have cut out and would not reveal whether it was a licensed product or not.
        </p>
        <p>
         Evgenia and Yaroslav both lived together in the Russian city of Moscow.
        </p>
        <p>
         The young womans death comes days after a 16-year-old girl died in Russias Bashkortostan Republic after taking her charging phone into the bathroom as she was taking a shower.
        </p>
        <p>
         source
         <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.cnn.com" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          CNN
         </a>
        </p>
       </section>
