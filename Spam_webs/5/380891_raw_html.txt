          <h1>
           The Modern Girls Guide to the Bar Dating Scene
          </h1>
          <div class="article__header-info">
           <b>
            <time datetime="2014-09-11" pubdate="">
             September 11th, 2014
            </time>
           </b>
          </div>
          <div class="clear">
          </div>
          <div class="article__header-ancillary">
           <aside class="article__header-author">
            <div class="article__header-author-copy">
             <a href="http://www.welovedates.com/blog/author/anna/">
              <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-96 photo" height="96" src="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/1b66f86b8da279b3f6ffe663dde0c1d4?s=96&amp;d=mm&amp;r=g" srcset="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/1b66f86b8da279b3f6ffe663dde0c1d4?s=192&amp;d=mm&amp;r=g 2x" width="96"/>
             </a>
             <h4>
              By
              <b>
               <a href="http://www.welovedates.com/blog/author/anna/">
                Anna Wickham
               </a>
              </b>
             </h4>
             <p>
              Anna Wickham is a blogger and digital nomad who writes about culture, travel, music, and dating from a millennial perspective. When she's not writing, she enjoys rapping at karaoke, reading non-fiction books, and trying every cheeseburger in America.
             </p>
            </div>
           </aside>
           <aside class="article__trustpilot">
            <div class="trustpilot-widget" data-businessunit-id="4ec29f040000640005118a27" data-locale="en-GB" data-style-height="100px" data-style-width="150px" data-template-id="53aa8807dec7e10d38f59f32" data-theme="light">

        <div class="article__body">
         <div class="article__content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.welovedates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/photodune-2330593-young-people-in-bar-having-fun-s.jpg">
            <img alt="young people in bar having fun" class="alignnone wp-image-18331 size-full" height="632" src="http://www.welovedates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/photodune-2330593-young-people-in-bar-having-fun-s.jpg" width="948"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I know, I know. Going to a bar to meet guys is
           <i>
            so
           </i>
           1994. But no matter how convenient and easy online dating is, sometimes you want to get out of the house and meet guys IRL.
          </p>
          <p>
           If youve been online dating, it can seem intimidating to meet guys in the flesh. No carefully-planned text messages and emails. Just regular conversations in which theres a real possibility of saying something embarrassing or having food in your teeth.
          </p>
          <p>
           Although meeting guys in bars gets a bad rap, I have never found myself in an unsafe situation. As long as you watch your drink, come with a group of friends, and dont go home with anyone that you just met, a bar can be a great place to find love. Some people think that online dating can be just as dangerous. Be cautious and do not trust strangers, no matter what method of meeting people you use.
          </p>
          <p>
           People also love to say that no good guys hang out in bars, but youre great and youre there, arent you? Why cant the same be true for men?
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Why Meet Guys in Bars?
          </h2>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Unlike online dating, you cant hide behind your computer screen, and you actually have to wash your hair in order to meet people in bars. But despite these minor downsides, there are some advantages as well.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            1. You
           </strong>
           <b>
            can immediately know whether or not youre attracted to someone
           </b>
           . When online dating, its sometimes hard to tell whether or not you are actually attracted to someone until you meet in person, and it could take 4 or 5 emails back and forth before you decide to meet. Think of all the time youve sunk in that one person, just to meet in person and realize that youre not really into each other. Skip all that drama, mama, and just meet a guy in person.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <b>
             2. Guys in bars arent necessarily looking for a relationship
            </b>
            .
           </b>
           In pre-online dating days, this might have been considered a disadvantage. But after meeting so many guys online who are players or serial online daters, Im keen to meet a guy who might just happen to be single and out to have a good time, just like me.
           <b>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            3. There are distractions to aid you
           </b>
           . Besides alcohol (ie  the best social safety net for human interactions since the beginning of time), there are often other things going on in bars, such as live music, that can help alleviate the awkwardness if theres a lull in the conversation.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Can I Buy You a Drink?
          </h2>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           One of the oldest pickup lines in the book is for a guy to offer to buy you a drink at the bar in order to talk to you. This sometimes makes guys seem like they are trying to buy your attention, and it makes some women uncomfortable. But it doesnt have to put a damper on things. Heres how to handle it, no matter how you feel about the convention or the guy.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
             The first and most important rule to remember
           </b>
           <b>
            : you do not owe a guy
           </b>
           <b>
            <i>
             anything
            </i>
           </b>
           <b>
            if he buys you a drink
           </b>
           . Im sure all of you ladies already know this, but its always good to have a reminder.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
             You dont have to say yes
           </b>
           . Not comfortable with a guy buying you a beverage? You definitely dont have to say yes, even if you are interested. Simply reply, No thanks, I already have one, or even more directly, Im not really comfortable with that. Its not a big deal, and you dont have to say it in a way that embarrasses him (unless thats what you were going for). Remember, you are in control.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
             If youre not interested in him, its best to decline
           </b>
           . Again, its not that you would owe him your attention if he got a drink for you, but if youre not interested, why even go there? Politely decline.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
             Did you see the drink being made?
           </b>
           If you didnt, or it was not brought to you by a bartender, do not drink it. Period. If this guy is decent, hell understand.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
             If youre interested and ok with someone buying you a drink, go for it
           </b>
           . Want to get to know this guy, and you have no qualms with having a drink bought for you? Well then, have fun and do your thang.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
             Why not buy him a drink instead?
           </b>
           Dont act like hes the best thing thats ever happened to you. But playing it cool and turning the tables on him will definitely be memorable and come across as confident.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           How to Make Friends Out of Strangers at the Bar
          </h2>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Despite how intimidating it sounds, its actually not difficult to make friends with strangers at a bar. Just follow these tips.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <b>
            The Female Flock Effect
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Ladies, you may not realize this, but looking all cute with your lip gloss, your cocktail, and a cloud of heavenly-smelling perfume, you and your friends are intimidating as hell. Guys are risking being publicly rejected by swallowing their pride and walking up to you.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you see a guy stealing glances at you from across the bar and youre reasonably certain hes interested, why not sashay over there and say hi? Sure, you might be risking a little embarrassment yourself if youre wrong, but chances are that you arent wrong, and hell be grateful and impressed that you introduced yourself.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Be Strategic
          </h3>
          <p>
           If you spot a guy that youd like to meet, here are some great ways to make it happen.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            1. Ask for advice about a drink
           </b>
           . If you see a guy youd like to approach at the bar, ask him about something drink related. (You are at a bar, after all). Ask him what hes drinking. Ask him if he comes there often, and if so, what he recommends. Ask him how he feels this bar compares to others in town. Is he from around here? So many possibilities!
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <b>
             2. At karaoke? Ask for (or offer!) some song advice
            </b>
            .
           </b>
           If youre at karaoke, youll have tons of great opportunities to talk to a guy youre interested in, especially if he is actually bold enough to sing. Compliment him on the song he chose, or ask him who sings it if youre not sure.
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre a karaoke veteran (like I am), let him know that theres a secret list of new song choices if you ask the karaoke DJ. Trust me, he will not be offended to receive unsolicited advice from a confident, cool girl.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <b>
             3. If youre hitting it off, ask him and his friends to sit with you
            </b>
            .
           </b>
           Its time be brave and take some risks here. Youre not asking them to move into your apartment. Its just one evening, and it will definitely shake up your usual Friday night.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            4. If youre rejected, brush your shoulders off
           </b>
           . It happens to the best of us. But better to try and fail than to regret not trying the next day. So get out there and start meeting some great people!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Other Tips to Nab That Dude
          </h2>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <b>
             1. Play some games.
            </b>
           </b>
           No, I dont mean mind games, although those are always fun, too. I mean bar games  go somewhere with darts, pool tables, air hockey, or shuffleboard and meeting guys will be approximately 10 times easier. (There are studies somewhere. Maybe.)
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            2. Dress to _____
           </b>
           . I was wrong to think I could get through this entire guide without talking about what youre wearing. Heres how you should dress: wear whatever the hell you want. I dont really suggest dressing like this or like that in order to attract men. Dress like
           <i>
            you
           </i>
           , whatever that may be, and you will meet men who are interested in
           <i>
            you
           </i>
           . Simple.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            3. Have fun, for real.
           </b>
           Instead of spending your energy looking like youre having fun across the bar, invest that energy in
           <i>
            actually
           </i>
           having a good time. Youll smile more naturally and youll generally look your best.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <b>
             4. When you are into a guy, use body language to show it.
            </b>
           </strong>
           There are lots of ways you can signal to a guy that youre interested: touch his arm or hand subtly when you talk to him, or maybe even pay him a cute compliment. You can also make your body a mirror image of his if his legs are crossed, cross yours in the same direction. Oh, and one more thing: do not prop your head or chin with your elbow on the table. It makes you look like you are bored, even if youre not.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_18334" style="width: 310px">
           <a href="http://www.welovedates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/photodune-2240926-bored-woman-s.jpg">
            <img alt="Bored woman" class="wp-image-18334 size-medium" height="200" src="http://www.welovedates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/photodune-2240926-bored-woman-s-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            I am so. bored.
           </p>
          </div>
          <hr/>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           So What Have We Learned Today, Kids?
          </h2>
          <p>
           Basically, we can boil it down to the following points:
          </p>
          <p>
            Meeting guys in bars is a perfectly viable method, and if you know what youre doing, it can be fun too.
          </p>
          <p>
            Youre going to have to get out of your shell. Just going to the bar in itself is a bold step in this day and age of dating from home. But beyond that, you are never going to meet anyone if you stay closely packed among a crowd of your friends. If you want this to work, youre going to have to break from the group, strut over to the bar, and ask that cute guy what he recommends you have to drink.
          </p>
          <p>
            There lots of ways to start a conversation, but youre going to have a much easier time if theres a pool table, a dart board, a live band, or a karaoke microphone nearby.
          </p>
          <p>
            Be your fabulous, confident self and have a good time. Youll radiate beauty and men wont be able to help but be interested.
          </p>
          <p>

            <strong>
             What are your favorite ways to start a conversation with a stranger in a bar? What tips did we miss?
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
