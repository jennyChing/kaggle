       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Chocolate Peppermint Truffles
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2011-12-16T14:09:15+00:00">
         December 16, 2011
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.bakerita.com/chocolate-peppermint-truffles/#comments">

         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          These Chocolate Peppermint Truffles are made with just three ingredients!! The chocolate peppermint cookie truffle filling is creamy and refreshing. These will fly off the dessert plate.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="These Chocolate Peppermint Truffles are made with just three ingredients!! The chocolate peppermint Oreo truffle filling is creamy and refreshing. These will fly off the dessert plate." class=" wp-image-1287 aligncenter" height="387" src="//www.bakerita.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/IMG_2368-1024x683.jpg" title="These Chocolate Peppermint Truffles are made with just three ingredients!! The chocolate peppermint Oreo truffle filling is creamy and refreshing. These will fly off the dessert plate." width="580"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         As the holidays approach, time seems to fly by and panic settles in as we try to finish all the errands that need to be completed. What the holidays really are about though, is family. Spending time with one another, giving and receiving from the people who love you most in the world. Whether its your drives-you-crazy siblings, your nagging mother, your sweet grandfather, or your 5th cousin who you havent seen in 8 years, holidays are a time to show the love you feel for one another. What better way to show someone you care than with candyhomemade candy at that?
        </p>
        <p>
         In this busy time of year, these are easy and fun to make. Kids can help form the balls and decorate, creating a fun activity for the kiddos on break. And trust me  youll enjoy the benefits. These are minty, smooth, delicious, and absolutely gorgeous. Easy and impressive? Perfect for this time of yearor any time of year!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-1277">
         </span>
         Grab the THREE ingredients (yes, only three) and take some time to spend time with your family and whip these together. If youre in the giving mood, throw some in a pretty box and
        </p>
        <div style="display: none;">
         <a href="http://buysteroidss.org/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://buysteroidss.org/', 'buy steroids');" title="buy steroids">
          buy steroids
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         deliver them to a neighbor, family member, or coworker. Theyll be grateful. Treasure this time of year, sing some carols, have a wonderful holiday season, and enjoy these truffles!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bakerita.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/IMG_2407.jpg">
          <img alt="These Chocolate Peppermint Truffles are made with just three ingredients!! The chocolate peppermint Oreo truffle filling is creamy and refreshing.          <br/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Chocolate Peppermint Truffles
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="//www.bakerita.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/IMG_2368-1024x683.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSSaveBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSSaveBtn bigoven" href="">
             Save
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.bakerita.com/easyrecipe-print/1277-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            40 truffles
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, room temperature
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 (16 ounce) box Candy Cane JoJo's (from Trader Joes) or mint-flavored Oreos
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 (10 ounce) bag of white candy melts
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Sprinkles (optional)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Dark chocolate, melted (optional)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Directions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a food processor, grind cookies until they are a fine powder. In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese and Oreos until no white streaks of cream cheese remain.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Shape into about 40 tablespoon-sized balls. Place in the refrigerator for at least 45 minutes. They can be refrigerated up to overnight.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Melt white candy melts and dip truffles. Place on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet to set.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            If desired, sprinkle on sprinkles before the outside sets or wait until they are set and drizzle on dark chocolate. Keep refrigerated.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
