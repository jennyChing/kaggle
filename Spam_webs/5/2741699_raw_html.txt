Take the experience to the next level, by attending one of the full-day immersive workshops covering SEO, PPC, local search or international search.
         </p>
         <p>
          Workshops will be held Thursday, June 4, 2015 at the Bell Harbor International Conference Center. Check out the line-up:
         </p>

         <div class="row">
          <p>
           <a href="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/advanced/local-search-advantage">

          <p>
           Local Search Advantage is a deep dive on local marketing, covering all of the topics necessary to be found by buyers when theyre looking to purchase. Even if youre an expert in traditional SEO or paid advertising, successful local search marketing has unique requirements you wont want to overlook. This full-day workshop, conducted by local search authority and Search Engine Land Contributing Editor
           <a href="http://searchengineland.com/author/greg-sterling">
            Greg Sterling
           </a>
           will offer practical advice and best practices for digital marketers, brands and business owners of all sizes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Topics covered include:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Google My Business: Everything You Need to Know Now
           </li>
           <li>
            Local SEO Strategy &amp; Key Tactics (Bonus: Apple Maps Clinic)
           </li>
           <li>
            Mobile SEO, SEM and Attribution
           </li>
           <li>
            New Rules for Local-Mobile Search
           </li>
           <li>
            And much more
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h4>
           
           <a href="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/advanced/local-search-advantage">
            More Info
           </a>
           
           <a href="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/advanced/register">
            Sign Up Now
           </a>
          </h4>

          <p>
           <a href="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/advanced/social-ppc-psychographic-display-intensive">
            <img alt="Social PPC &amp; Psychographic Display Intensive @ SMX Advanced 2015 - Seattle, WA" class="pull-left margin-ten" src="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/_images/events/sppdi_logo.png" width="180"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/advanced/social-ppc-psychographic-display-intensive">
            Social PPC &amp; Psychographic Display Intensive  Starts at 9:00 am
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Despite the fact that
           <a href="https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&amp;rct=j&amp;q=&amp;esrc=s&amp;source=web&amp;cd=6&amp;cad=rja&amp;uact=8&amp;ved=0CC0QFjAF&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.emarketer.com%2FArticle%2FSocial-Network-Ad-Spending-Hit-2368-Billion-Worldwide-2015%2F1012357&amp;ei=ZJRLVfnwGcaCsAXp-YHwDw&amp;usg=AFQjCNHKD9obRdphChaB1JgEAG6nNYPLdQ">
            social network ad spend is expected to hit $23.68 billion worldwide
           </a>
           , many advertisers just arent getting the bang for the buck that they expect. Social PPC &amp; Psychographic Display Intensive focuses on the strategies, hands-on techniques and immediately- actionable tactics to accelerate ROI in your social ad campaigns. If youre a social advertiser, this full-day workshop, conducted by social media guru
           <a href="http://searchengineland.com/author/marty-weintraub">
            Marty Weintraub
           </a>
           is totally for you, whether youre a publisher, ecommerce site, direct response marketer or undertake lead generation.
          </p>
          <p>
           Youll take away tactics to:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Define and cultivate audiences, inject them deep into your sales funnel, and convert them to customers.
           </li>
           <li>
            Generate more attributable conversions faster and at a lower cost
           </li>
           <li>
            Drive awareness from influencers (journalist, bloggers, prominent community members), enhancing your PR effectiveness
           </li>
           <li>
            Conduct multivariate social voice testing, much the way SEOs use search PPC to improve SEO funnels
           </li>
           <li>
            And much more
           </li>
          </ul>

           <a href="http://searchmarketingexpo.com/advanced/social-ppc-psychographic-display-intensive">
