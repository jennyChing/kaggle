            <p>
             Denver BroncosquarterbackPeyton Manningpassed Brett Favre for the leagues record for career touchdown passes Sunday night against the San Francisco 49ers with his 509th career scoring pass.
            </p>
            <p>
             Mannings8-yard pass toDemaryius Thomaswith 3:09 remaining in the first halfbroke the record.
            </p>
            <p>
             The Broncos honored Manning with a
             <a href="http://www.denverbroncos.com/multimedia/videos/Congratulations_Peyton_Manning/65cf0b65-92c1-4873-86dd-a776bc1dc8c8" target="_blank">
              video message
             </a>
             fromFavre, John Elway and several of Mannings teammates.
            </p>
            <p>
             Manning threw a three-yardtouchdown pass toEmmanuel Sanderswith 8:29 second left in the first quarter for his 507th career scoring pass, along with a 39-yard touchdown passtoWes Welkerwith 2:57 left in the opening quarterto tie the record.
            </p>
