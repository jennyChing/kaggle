          <!--post-image-->
          <p>
           Heres who is officially in talks for the new
           <b>
            <i>
             <a href="http://bloody-disgusting.com/tag/ghostbusters/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://bloody-disgusting.com/tag/ghostbusters/', 'Ghostbusters');">
              Ghostbusters
             </a>
            </i>
           </b>
           , as reported by Variety:
           <b>
            Kristen Wiig
           </b>
           , pictured above,
           <b>
            Melissa McCarthy
           </b>
           ,
           <b>
            Kate McKinnon
           </b>
           and
           <b>
            Leslie Jones
           </b>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Paul Feig is directing with Katie Dippold penning the script.
          </p>
          <p>
           Several sources say Jones is likely to play a government official similar to the role William Atherton played in the original.
           <span id="more-3329761">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Sounds like this is becoming a SNL parody sketch instead of something inspired.
          </p>
          <p>
           Allegedly,
           <a href="http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3324902/peter-dinklage-ghostbusters-villain-historical-figures-haunt-new-york/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3324902/peter-dinklage-ghostbusters-villain-historical-figures-haunt-new-york/', 'the plan is to land Game of Thrones fav Peter Dinklage');">
            the plan is to land Game of Thrones fav Peter Dinklage
           </a>
           for the villain role. He would play the ghost of a murderer (sort of like Wes Cravens Shocker, actually) who resurrects a ghost army based on historical characters.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Crossing-streams-ghostbusters-33868486-2453-1553" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3313175" height="392" src="http://cdn.bloody-disgusting.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Crossing-streams-ghostbusters-33868486-2453-1553-1024x648.jpg" width="620"/>
          </p>
