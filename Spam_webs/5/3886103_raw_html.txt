             Iceland

          <p>
           Southwest Iceland has an incredibly diverse set of landscapes. Green valleys, lunar-like terrain, lava fields, hot springs and bird cliffs can all be explored over the course of a four-day tour. This trip also has time for a dip in the Reykjaviks famous Blue Lagoon and a hike to the top of the volcano crater on the island of Heimy.
          </p>
          <p>
           Costing from 1334pp, the price includes flights from Heathrow, transfers, accommodation with breakfast, entrance to the Blue Lagoon and services of a bus and guide, all based on two sharing. Departs July 3,17,31 and August 14 2013.
          </p>

          <p>
           Stockholm, Helsinki and St Petersburg are all magnificent cities, and its only by visiting them in a short space of time that youre able to pick up on the subtle characteristics shared by all three. This seven-night trip manages to squeeze in all three. It is also includes a visit to Gamla Stan (Stockholms old town) and a cruise along the Neva River. The St Petersburg leg includes a visit to the Winter Palace, and theres time to shop for crystals and leather in Helsinkis busy market.
          </p>

          <p>
           Sometimes you hanker for the buzz of the city. Other times you just wish for the peace of tranquillity of the countryside. This 11-night tour of Norway, Sweden and Finland provides a mix of the two. Highlights include Norways beautiful UNESCO-listed Geirangerfyord (our featured image) and Flm Railway, Gamla Stan (Stockholms enchanting old town with its narrow cobbled streets and numerous antique shops) and Uspenski Cathedral in Helsinki.

Based on two sharing. An optional three-night St. Petersburg extension is also possible at the end of the tour. Prices for the 14-night itinerary start from 4217pp. Departs July 2 and 23 2013.
