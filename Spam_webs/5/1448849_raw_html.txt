            <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.youngcons.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/090414_sr_benghazi_640.jpg">
             <img alt="090414_sr_benghazi_640" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24988" height="493" src="http://www.youngcons.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/090414_sr_benghazi_640.jpg" width="876"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Former Army Ranger Kris Paronto and four other security operators who were responsible for guarding the CIA station in Benghazi, Libya are opening up about what really happened on the ground.
           </p>
           <p>
            From
            <a href="http://www.theblaze.com/stories/2014/09/04/bombshell-claim-security-operators-on-the-ground-in-benghazi-say-they-were-told-to-stand-down-and-thats-not-all/">

           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             If you guys do not get here, we are going to die!
            </strong>
            a diplomatic security agent shouted over the radio, the commandos say in their new book, according to the New York Times.
           </p>
           <div class="inline-ad cf">
            <span>
             <div class="textwidget">
              <div id="div-gpt-ad-1426883459323-6" style="width:300px; height:250px;">

            </span>
           </div>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            Within moments the team, comprised of former members of American Special Forces teams and hired as private contractors, was ready to deploy and move to the compound, a mere mile away.
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             Five minutes, were ready,
            </strong>
            Kris Tanto Paronto, a former Army Ranger, told Fox News. It was thumbs up, thumbs up, were ready to go.
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             However, according to the team, a top CIA officer in Benghazi, who they refer to as Bob, told them to stand down.
            </strong>
            
           </p>
           <div class="widgets_on_page" id="Widgets_on_Pages_8">
            <ul>
            </ul>

           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             Were starting to get calls from the State Department guys saying, Hey, were taking fire, we need you guys here, we need help,
            </strong>
            Paronto added.
           </p>
           <p>
            After 30 minutes of waiting, the team deployed in defiance of the order. The five security operators believe Ambassador Stevens and others would still be alive if they were never told to stand down.
           </p>
           <p>
            Paronto: Ambassador Stevens and Sean [Smith], yeah, they would still be alive, my gut is yes.
