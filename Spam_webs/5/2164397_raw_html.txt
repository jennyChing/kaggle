&lt;p class="entry-meta"&gt;&lt;time class="entry-time" itemprop="datePublished" datetime="2014-02-08T12:52:19+00:00"&gt;February 8, 2014&lt;/time&gt; by &lt;span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"&gt;&lt;a href="http://preventionrd.com/author/nrmintuos/" class="entry-author-link" itemprop="url" rel="author"&gt;&lt;span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name"&gt;Nicole Morrissey&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;span class="entry-comments-link"&gt;&lt;a href="http://preventionrd.com/2014/02/dark-chocolate-chunk-coconut-key-lime-pound-cake-weekly-menu/#comments"&gt;12 Comments&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;/p&gt;&lt;/header&gt;&lt;div class="entry-content" itemprop="text"&gt;&lt;input class="jpibfi" type="hidden" &gt;&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;a title="Dark Chocolate Chunk Coconut Key Lime Pound Cake 1 by preventionrd, on Flickr" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/73943144@N04/12387274304/"&gt;&lt;img alt="Dark Chocolate Chunk Coconut Key Lime Pound Cake 1" src="//farm8.staticflickr.com/7423/12387274304_e453e23a32_z.jpg" width="432" height="640"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;When Mr. Prevention and I got married, he quickly learned that I &lt;em&gt;hate&lt;/em&gt; cleaning and I quickly learned that that would be an issue. Some of our friends say that a cleaning service saved their marriage and I&#8217;d venture to say we&#8217;re not far off. Every other Friday when we return to a squeaky clean house, Mr. Prevention and I are both much happier, calmer individuals. Plus, I very rarely cook on Friday evenings, so my kitchen is nearly guaranteed to stay clean for a full 24 hours.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;The duties that get us from one cleaning to the next are pretty much divvied up between outside and inside chores &#8212; Mr. Prevention covers the outside and I tackle the inside.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;a title="Dark Chocolate Chunk Coconut Key Lime Pound Cake 2 by preventionrd, on Flickr" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/73943144@N04/12386971563/"&gt;&lt;img alt="Dark Chocolate Chunk Coconut Key Lime Pound Cake 2" src="//farm8.staticflickr.com/7429/12386971563_aef33ac70a_z.jpg" width="640" height="432"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;That is, of course, only applicable when Mr. Prevention is around. And seeing as he travels for work, I do get stuck with my fair share of outdoor chores&#8230;some of which simply can&#8217;t be pushed off until he returns. If we get a foot of snow&#8230;well, it&#8217;s got to be removed. Luckily, such masses of snow have never been an issue&#8230;until now. This horrible, horrible, no-good winter. Old news, I know.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;Before leaving last week on business, Mr. Prevention gave me a full hands-on tutorial on how to use the snow blower.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;em&gt;No, I&#8217;d never blown snow before. I&#8217;ve also never mowed a lawn. I know, I know&#8230;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;a title="Dark Chocolate Chunk Coconut Key Lime Pound Cake 3 by preventionrd, on Flickr" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/73943144@N04/12386813725/"&gt;&lt;img alt="Dark Chocolate Chunk Coconut Key Lime Pound Cake 3" src="//farm8.staticflickr.com/7296/12386813725_a813b31891_z.jpg" width="432" height="640"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;But I used the snow blower, albeit not very proficiently. I was able to get out and to work and that&#8217;s what really matters. But not after a few pile ups on the highway and black ice all 32 miles there. And back.&lt;/p&gt;
