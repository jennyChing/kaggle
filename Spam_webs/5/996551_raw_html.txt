          <header class="entry-header">
           <h1>
            10 Types of 30-Year-Old Single Guys
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <div class="left" style="display:block;">
             By Tim Urban
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <!-- end meta -->
          </header>
          <div class="entry-content-wrap">
           <div class="entry-content">
            <div dir="ltr">
             <p>
              30-year-old guys are a curious bunch.
             </p>
             <p>
              Find me a group of 30-year-old men and Ill pick out one overgrown frat dude living with roommates, another guy who just dropped his two kids off at school, a few who are well into their careers and a couple soul-searchers looking for work. Some will tell you that theyve finally figured it all out and some more will say they feel hopeless for the first time in their lives. Its a motley crew.
             </p>
             <p>
              But perhaps the motliest part of this crowd is the ever-growing group of 30-year-old single guys. If you want a case study in humanity, 30-year-old single guys have pretty much all the bases covered. Lets examine some of the common types:
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                1) The Total Package
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/total-package1.png">
               <img alt="total package" class="aligncenter wp-image-2332" height="397" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/total-package1.png" width="387"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The Total Package is smarthe went to a top college. The Total Package is an athlete, a musician, and an avid traveler. The Total Package is handsomeand you better believe hes well-groomed.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The Total Package has a hell of a career going, but dont you for a second suggest that The Total Package would be a workaholicThe Total Package is a family man.
             </div>
             <p>
              Theres just one thing The Total Package seems to be having a hard time findinga girl worthy of his greatness.
             </p>
             <p>
              Yes, the woman fit for The Total Package will be the ultimate icing on his cake of perfection. He imagines her oftengorgeous as they come, she turns heads; bursting with charm and charisma, she lights up every room she enters; shes a brilliant rising star in her career and beloved by her many friends. And thats just her public personaat home, shes fantastic in bed, a spectacular cook, loving, selfless, and devoted. Oh and she also speaks French, plays tennis, sings beautifully, reads voraciously and shes a history buff. His Juliet.
             </p>
             <p>
              Unsurprisingly, The Total Package is single. Hes immersed in a fierce battle between his superhuman standards and his terror of being 40 and singlebecause 40 and single is not supposed to be part of The Total Packages story.
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
               <b>
                2) The New Lease On Life Guy
               </b>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/lease-on-life.png">
               <img alt="lease on life" class="aligncenter wp-image-2327" height="272" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/lease-on-life.png" width="330"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              As long as anyone can remember, The New Lease On Life Guy had been dating his longterm girlfriend. He never seemed that happy in the relationship, but everyone just assumed they would eventually get married. Now, after a long and difficult breakup, The New Lease On Life Guy has reemerged with a bang and is suddenly acting like he just got called down on The Price Is Right. Hes not really sure how to be single but hes goddamn happy he is, and hes sure as hell going out tonight.
             </p>
             <p>
              Hes also the arch-nemesis of The Resigned Fiance, whos in an equally unhappy relationship but just kind of kept going with it, unable to resist the sweet, sweet inertia, and who most certainly does not want to hear about The New Lease On Life Guys latest exploits.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                3) The Guy Who Has To Marry Someone Of The Same Ethnicity Or His Parents Will Never Speak To Him Again
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/ethnicity.png">
               <img alt="ethnicity" class="aligncenter wp-image-2324" height="270" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/ethnicity.png" width="419"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              Its hard enough finding someone to be your life partner, and this guys parents are really not making things any easier. He tried to rebel briefly, but after his last girlfriend was not allowed in his parents house, causing her to cry, he gave up on that.
             </p>
             <p>
              Hed also really appreciate it if his mother would stop setting him up on dates.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                4) The Misogynist
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/misogynist.png">
               <img alt="misogynist" class="aligncenter wp-image-2328" height="322" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/misogynist.png" width="302"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              The Misogynist hates women, and women hate The Misogynist. The Misogynist doesnt know a whole lot about the other gender, but he can tell you the exact number of them hes slept with214.
             </p>
             <p>
              He did quite well with girls back in his earlier days when many were in their attracted to assholes phase, but lately, only those with the lowest self-esteem seem to gravitate towards him.
             </p>
             <p>
              The Misogynists close cousin is The Perpetual Cheater. Theyre different but they understand each other.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                5) The Guy Who Peaked Too Early
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/peaked-early.png">
               <img alt="peaked early" class="aligncenter wp-image-2330" height="312" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/peaked-early.png" width="461"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              Back in the day, The Guy Who Peaked Too Early had everything a 17-year-old girl could ever dream of. His sky-high confidence carried him smoothly through college, and no one was surprised when he landed a smart, sweet, beautifulgirlfriend in his early 20s. But The Guy Who Peaked Too Early was just getting started. There was a field that needed to be played, and he broke up with his girlfriend when he was 24.
             </p>
             <p>
              Now its seven years later, his hair got bored and left, and his high school lacrosse glory isnt part of the conversation that much these days. And hes noticing that girls like his ex-girlfriend dont seem to be all that into him anymore. Realizing this about five years after everyone else, he takes a deep sigh and cranks his standards down a few big notches.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                6) The Guy Whos Finally a Good Catch
               </span>
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Finally-a-Good-Catch.png">
               <img alt="Finally a Good Catch" class="aligncenter wp-image-2325" height="315" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Finally-a-Good-Catch.png" width="377"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              On the other side of the coin, after losing some weight, getting decent clothes, and having early career success, The Guy Whos Finally a Good Catch is getting more attention each week than he got in his first 25 years combined. Girls find it endearing that such an appealing guy has managed to maintain his humility, when its actually just that hes assuming every girl is out of his league at all times.
             </p>
             <p>
              Once his new situation starts to sink in, he enters an unfortunate newphase, stressing his male friends out by doing things like winking at them over the shoulder of a girl hes dancing with and offering them a fist pound when an attractive girl walks by on the street.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
               <b>
                7) The Normal Guy Who Just Hasnt Met The Right Girl Yet And He Really Wishes People Would Stop Looking At Him With Those Pitying Eyes
               </b>
              </span>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/normal-guy1.png">
               <img alt="normal guy" class="aligncenter wp-image-2333" height="381" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/normal-guy1.png" width="334"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              Ah, The NGWJHMTRGYAHRWPWSLAHWTPE. TheNGWJHMTRGYAHRWPWSLAHWTPEis enjoying his life. He likes his job, helikeshis friends, and he likes being single just fine. Hes in no rush to be in a relationship and feels totally confident that at some point, hell meet the right girl and get married.
             </p>
             <p>
              Hes also not quite sure why everyone who knows him is trying to figure out what the problem is. His parents are worried, never wasting an opportunity to ask him if hes been dating anyone. His friends want to help, setting him up on dates every chance they get. He appreciates all the unsolicited support, but he also thinks it would be pretty great if everyone stopped thinking there was something wrong with him.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                8) The Aggressively Online Dating Guy Who Cant Believe Hes Not Married Yet
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cant-believe-not-married1.png">
               <img alt="can't believe not married" class="aligncenter wp-image-2323" height="259" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cant-believe-not-married1.png" width="482"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              The opposite of the previous guy, The Aggressively Online Dating Guy Who Cant Believe Hes Not Married Yet cant believe hes not married yet. Through high school, college and his twenties, he was always The Guy With A Girlfriend. He spent years enjoying pitying his single friends, and somehow, hes now 30 and single.
             </p>
             <p>
              He has four online dating profiles, and when people ask him if hes dating anyone, he explains that hes just too busy with his career right now for a relationship.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
               <b>
                9) The In-The-Closet Guy
               </b>
              </span>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/in-the-closet.png">
               <img alt="in the closet" class="aligncenter wp-image-2326" height="320" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/in-the-closet.png" width="253"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              The In-The-Closet Guy is
              <i>
               so
              </i>
              close to beingthe perfect catchhes handsome, hes well-dressed, and he has a great job. Hes funny, articulate, and charming.The
              <i>
               only
              </i>
              tiny little inconvenience is that hes not attracted to females whatsoever.
             </p>
             <p>
              His antithesis is TheNGWJHMTRGYAHRWPWSLAHWTPE, whos had just enough of the theories about him being gay, since hes completelystraight and, for the hundredth time, just hasnt met the right girl yet and is really very okay with being single right now.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: 24pt;">
                10) The Guy Who Has Just Fully Quit At This Point
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <div>
              <a href="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/quit.png">
               <img alt="quit" class="aligncenter wp-image-2331" height="299" src="http://28oa9i1t08037ue3m1l0i861.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/quit.png" width="445"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              The Guy Who Has Just Fully Quit At This Point never tried that hard in the first place, but at least there used to be a semblance of effort. He doesnt like going to bars, refuses to try online dating, and both the bong and the X-Box are back in the living room following their brief stint in the closet after his friend gave him a pep talk one day four months ago.
             </p>
             <p>
              Deep down, The Guy Who Has Just Fully Quit At This Point is pretty frightened about a lot of things, but his fear manifests itself in indifferent denial, and passivity usually prevails. There is only one way that things change for The Guy Who Has Just Fully Quit At This Point, and thats to find himself squarely in the sights of The Girl Who Relentlessly Pursues. Until then, the whole thing isnt really his issue.
             </p>
