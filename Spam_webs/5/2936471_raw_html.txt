         <br/>
         <br/>

        <p>
         *
         <em>
          Im super excited to have the lovely Kristyn, from
          <a href="http://www.glamour-zine.com/" target="_blank">
           Glamour-Zine
          </a>
          , here to chat with yall today! Kristyn happens to be a favorite of mine in beaut blog land. Ive been visiting her site for quite sometime, so I thought having her stop by and share a little somethin, somethin with you all would be super fun! You can find so much on her site from
          <a href="http://www.glamour-zine.com/how-to-rock-in-style-for-under-100-summer-edition-with-kohls/%20" target="_blank">
           how to rock in style for under $100
          </a>
          to thorough reviews of
          <a href="http://www.glamour-zine.com/bite-beauty-matte-creme-lip-crayons-review-photos-swatches/" target="_blank">
           lipsticks
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.glamour-zine.com/nars-duo-eyeshadow-kauai-review-photos-swatches/" target="_blank">
           eyeshadows
          </a>
          !Shes a busy mom so this super easy
          <strong>
           5 Minute Makeup
          </strong>
          routine is something Im sure she needs to use all the time. Ill let her take it away!!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         As a busy mommy of two, I know that time is of the essence. I also know that many women feel like giving up on looking good once they have children but I am here to tell you, you dont have to! Even if youre a busy working mom, constantly on the go like me, there is still time to look your best. I know that every second counts when you have kids, so on days when I just dont have more than 5 minutes to spare; I have perfected a 5-minute or less makeup routine that never fails.
        </p>
        <p>
         Between changing diapers, feeding the kids, and packing diaper bags or back packs, not much time is usually left for us. The key to having a good 5-minute makeup routine that never fails is using multi-tasking products that really work. This is my key secret to looking fresh and polished in a matter of minutes. When I have more than 5 minutes to get my makeup in place, my face routine is different and I take the time to switch up my products, but this is my go to routine for those times when I need to get it done in a hurry.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, I have tried hundreds of products over the past few years and these are my Holy Grail items that I currently use to give me a complete and flawless face in 5 minutes or less.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.hairsprayandhighheels.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/5-minute-makeup-.png">
          <img alt="5 Minute Makeup! " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5312" height="2334" src="http://www.hairsprayandhighheels.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/5-minute-makeup-.png" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 1: Prep  50 Seconds
        </p>
        <p>
         Foundation is the most important step in my opinion, which is why I spend a good amount of time on it.  I love using my YSBB
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=435563306&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Your Skin But Better CC Cream
         </a>
         with SPF 50+. Talk about a multi-tasking product. This is your full coverage foundation, brightening color, anti-aging serum, pore minimizing primer, dark spot corrector, moisturizing day cream, plus UVA/UVB broad spectrum physical only SPF 50+ all in one easy step!
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 2: Conceal  55 seconds
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 3: Contour, Blush &amp; highlight
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you add foundation you need to add back the life and dimension into your face. I do this by contouring, high lighting and adding blush. When I am in a crunch for time, I like to use palettes that include all three so I dont have to reach for three different products. I love using my IT Cosmetics
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=434800984&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Vitality Face Disc
         </a>
         because it includes a Luxe High Performance Anti-Aging matte bronzer, luminating powder, and vitality blush that all look very natural and pretty on my skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         It takes me 20 seconds to contour, 15 seconds to higlight and 10 seconds to add blush.
        </p>
        <p>
         * For a diagram on how to contour and highlight check out this awesome post
         <a href="http://www.hairsprayandhighheels.net/2013/12/contour/" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 4: Brows  40
        </p>
        <p>
         Brows are a total must for me and I simply cant leave the house without them done. Brows frame a persons face and if you dont have time for anything else, I suggest at least defining your brows. My brows alone use to take me 5 minutes when I lined them with a pencil, filled them in with a powder and kept them in place with a brow gel. Well thats now a thing of the past, my IT Cosmetics
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=442643138&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Brow Power Waterproof Perfecto
         </a>
         takes the place of five products; a waterproof brow pencil, brow fill-in powder, taming brow gel, brow enhancing treatment conditioner and brow brush and its all housed in one super easy-to-use automatic pencil.
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 5: Eyes  70 Seconds
        </p>
        <p>
         I always go for a natural look with my eye shadow when I only have 5 minutes to get my makeup on. I use just three colors from my
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=441149483&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          NAKED 3 palette
         </a>
         . Strange all over my lid, Limit in the crease and Nooner in the outer V. This takes me 25 seconds. I then curl my lashes and add a coat of the mascara which takes me 30 seconds. Currently, Ive been using the
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=435734993&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Too Faced Better Than Sex
         </a>
         Mascara. Lastly, I line the top and bottom of the inner rims of my eyes with
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=336311846&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Pur Minerals
         </a>
         to brighten up my eyes. This takes me 15 seconds.
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 6: Lips  30 seconds
        </p>
        <p>
         If its a busy day and I know I will be on the go with no time to reapply lip products through the day I always reach for a long-lasting Lip product. The three products I rotate between are the
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=432927261&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          BITE Beauty Cashmere Lips
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=451426782&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Too Faced Melted Lipstick
         </a>
         and the
         <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=381739166&amp;pid=uid8144-23292988-58" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Make Up For Ever Aqua Rouge
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         *You can check out a review of the Too Faced Melted Lipsticks
         <a href="http://www.glamour-zine.com/faced-melted-liquifiied-long-wear-lipsticks-review-photos-swatches/" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         ; with swatches!
        </p>
        <p>
         Well there you have it, my 5 minute or less makeup routine!
        </p>
