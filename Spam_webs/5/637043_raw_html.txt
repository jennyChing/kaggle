       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Buddy  English Shepherd Pet Portrait
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-06-18T07:48:35+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 18, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://justpaintitblog.com/author/colleen/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Colleen
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/2014/06/buddy-english-shepherd-pet-portrait/#comments">
           12 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Shepherd-Portrait.jpg">
          <img alt="English Shepherd Portrait" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13155" height="810" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Shepherd-Portrait.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         Meet Buddy!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_9517.jpg">
          <img alt="English Shepherd Portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13156" height="717" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_9517-768x1024.jpg" width="538"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Doesnt he look like the sweetest dog ever?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1940.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13159" height="717" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1940-764x1024.jpg" width="535"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I have it on good authority he
         <em>
          is
         </em>
         one of the sweetest dogs, because I follow his
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/PrettyHandyGirl" target="_blank">
          mom on Facebook
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://instagram.com/prettyhandygirl" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         where she occasionally shares posts about Buddy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I would expect that her dog would be awesome because
         <a href="http://www.prettyhandygirl.com/" target="_blank">
          Brittany
         </a>
         is truly one of the kindest and generous people I know. Im so lucky that I get to call her my friend.So to be able to paint her dog was a real treat, especially since Brittany is an accomplished artist herself. #nopressure Also, this is the first english shepherd portrait Ive done, or shepherd of any kind!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I asked Brittany to email me some of her favorite pics of Buddy, which she did.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy_walk.jpg">
          <img alt="English Shepherd Portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13157" height="717" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy_walk-768x1024.jpg" width="538"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Her only request was if I could capture Buddy with his ears up and mouth open, as this is when hes happy. The only thing missing from this pic above is a wagging tail, right? I dont know how she even shot this because I imagine Buddy jumping up and down, excited to go for a walk.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Or maybe hes one of those dogs who actually sits on command. I dont have one of those.
         <span class="wp-font-emots-emo-happy">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I was so excited to draw Buddy, I totally forgot to take step-by-step photos. Plus, I wasnt really ready to write a colored pencil tutorial. Now, with a few under my belt, I might share some pencil tips on my next pet portrait. I think youll love drawing with pencils, especially on animals.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I have a few in-progress photos that I shared on
         <a href="http://instagram.com/muralmaker1" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         . Are you following me there? If you like seeing step-by-step pics, I almost always share there.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-1a.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13160" height="640" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-1a.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I follow a lot of artists on Instagram and I notice that they draw portions of an animal and work their way down. I used to color block the whole thing and then add detailing later.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         The one thing I do now is get the eyes in early. They always bring life to the piece and its like I can feel the pet, which is special to me.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I may or may not actually talk to the animal while Im drawing him or her. (Ill never tell.)
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-1b.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13161" height="602" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-1b.jpg" width="536"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I started working my way down with Buddys portrait. It felt a little odd at first but now I wouldnt do it any other way.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-1c.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13162" height="640" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-1c.jpg" width="531"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Mainly because I rest my hand on the canvas as Im sketching and, since these are oil-based pencils, I manage to smear everything under my hand.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Yeah. Its lovely. #not
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-2.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13163" height="407" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-2-1024x678.jpg" width="614"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Once I erased all of the smears I took a fine tip liner brush and acrylic paint and started adding the wispies.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-4.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13164" height="614" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-4-803x1024.jpg" width="482"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         No matter how much I sharpen my pencil  and I sharpen them a lot  I cant seem to get the tiny fine lines like I do with a paint brush.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-5.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13165" height="407" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-5-1024x678.jpg" width="614"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Of course, the fact that I did this on canvas rather than paper makes a big difference. You can see the weave of the canvas close up but not from far away.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-6.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter wp-image-13166" height="708" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Buddy-6.jpg" width="566"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This is an 8 x 10 inch canvas so its pretty small, but large enough to still get in most of the details too.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Shepherd-Portrait1.jpg">
          <img alt="english shepherd portrait" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13167" height="750" src="http://justpaintitblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Shepherd-Portrait1.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         What a joy it is to paint someones best friend. For a long time Ive been wanting to do something special for
         <a href="http://www.prettyhandygirl.com/" target="_blank">
          Brittany
         </a>
         . Not just because shes a dear friend but because she was amazing after
         <a href="http://justpaintitblog.com/2012/08/real-life-angels-2/" target="_blank">
          my husband died
         </a>
         . Shes a special, special person who is always giving to others.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I hope youll take a minute and go visit her blog,
         <a href="http://www.prettyhandygirl.com/" target="_blank">
          Pretty Handy Girl
         </a>
         . It is full of awesome DIY projects of all kinds. Seriously, if I ever have a question, PHG is the first place I look!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         So Buddy is the second blogger pet Ive done, and there are more to come! If this keeps up, I might have to do a Bloggers Dogs (and kitties) series of some kind. Wouldnt that be fun? We could play a mix n match game  match the pet to the blogger  or something.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          You just keep thinking, Butch. Thats what youre good at.
         </em>
         Name that movie.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Im off to start the next pet portrait. Any guesses which breed this one will be? Ill give you a hint  its a dog. I little one.
         <span class="wp-font-emots-emo-happy">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Have a great rest of the week!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: x-large;">
          <em>
           Colleen
