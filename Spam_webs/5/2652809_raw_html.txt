            <p>
             There is nothing more refreshing on a hot summer day than enjoying a big bowl of ice cream; unless, of course, you would rather have a race with the drips and have a scoop or two on a cone. No matter which way you prefer to eat your ice cream, you will enjoy it even more if you make it yourself.
            </p>
            <p>
             Browse through this collection of amazing ice cream recipes, but dont look for traditional flavors like vanilla, chocolate and strawberry! You can, instead, satisfy your sweet tooth with bubble gum ice cream, easy lemon ice cream, or root beer ice cream. If you are feeling adventurous you can whip up a batch of candied bacon ice cream, Idaho potato ice cream, or oatmeal ice cream.
            </p>
