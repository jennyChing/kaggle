                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Blood Orange Olive Oil Cake
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                     <div class="under-title">
                      <span class="post-comment-link">

                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-9178385647007523553" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <article itemscope="" itemtype="http://nik.io/v1/schema/Article">
                      <span content="http://www.chefnextdoorblog.com/2015/05/blood-orange-olive-oil-cake.html" itemprop="permalink">
                      </span>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                        <i>
                         This cake is light, tender and full of fresh citrus flavors. It's so delicious, it won't last long!
                        </i>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-GW7rrrI9x7k/VUgsuAGEyII/AAAAAAAAGlw/4UGzSPoD4aU/s1600/Blood%2BOrange%2BOlive%2BOil%2BCake-3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-GW7rrrI9x7k/VUgsuAGEyII/AAAAAAAAGlw/4UGzSPoD4aU/s640/Blood%2BOrange%2BOlive%2BOil%2BCake-3.jpg" width="438"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                        I love citrus fruits. I also love to bake with citrus fruits. They make everything so light and flavorful, and I never feel guilty about eating dessert. This time of year is perfect for all things citrus. From oranges to lemons to tangerines, I want to devour every single one.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                        I received some beautiful blood oranges in my recent
                        <a href="https://michigan.doortodoororganics.com/">
                         Door to Door Organic
                        </a>
                        s delivery, and could hardly wait to make something with them. It took me a bit to decide what to do, but I am incredibly excited over the recipe I developed.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EOqKaTHqDrc/VUgnqPz-ODI/AAAAAAAAGlg/xqoP29heE1A/s1600/fruit.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EOqKaTHqDrc/VUgnqPz-ODI/AAAAAAAAGlg/xqoP29heE1A/s640/fruit.jpg" width="426"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       This cake is so simple to make, and you should have everything you need in your pantry already. Well, maybe except for the oranges. I also used the gorgeous
                      </span>
                      <a href="https://michigan.doortodoororganics.com/shop/products/green-meadow-certified-organic-eggs" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       Green Meadow Organic Eggs
                      </a>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       from my DTDO order. I adore these eggs and include them in my order every single week.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-o6LegTQ4BtU/VUgsz7MI9-I/AAAAAAAAGl4/sS_19a7DEXQ/s1600/Blood%2BOrange%2BOlive%2BOil%2BCake-2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-o6LegTQ4BtU/VUgsz7MI9-I/AAAAAAAAGl4/sS_19a7DEXQ/s640/Blood%2BOrange%2BOlive%2BOil%2BCake-2.jpg" width="426"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                        Again, this cake is simple, but perfectly delicious. Made with olive oil, it's the most light and tender cake I've had in a long time. And the citrus flavors just shine through. This cake would be perfect for Cinco de Mayo or Mother's Day, but honestly you don't even need a reason to make it. Just be sure to make it soon.
                       </span>
                      </div>

                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       4 b
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       lood oranges
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1/2 cup
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       extra virgin olive oil
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       4
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       eggs
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1 cup
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       sugar
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1 cup
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       cake flour
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1 Tbsp
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       baking powder
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1/2 tsp
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       salt
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1 1/2 cups
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       powdered sugar
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="amount" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       1
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                      <span class="name" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                       lime
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9-inch round cake pan with non-stick baking spray and set aside.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Zest all of the oranges and juice one of them. Whisk the zest, juice and olive oil in a small bowl until well combined.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        In the bowl of a stand mixer, beat the eggs until light and fluffy. Carefully beat in the sugar until combined.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt, then slowly add to the mixer. Add the oil/citrus mixture and beat just until incorporated.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 50 minutes, or until a cake tester inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool on a rack for 15 minutes, then remove from the pan and allow to cool completely.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        While the cake is cooling, make the icing. Zest and juice the lime. Whisk that together with the powdered sugar until smooth. Drizzle over the top of the cooled cake. Slice and serve.
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="background-color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Bon Appetit!
                       </span>
                      </div>
