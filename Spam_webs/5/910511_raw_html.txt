        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Litbloc: Google Glass App Lets You Make A Sex Tape
        </h1>
        <div class="comments-link">
         <a href="http://litbloc.com/litbloc-google-glass-app-lets-make-sex-tape/#respond" title="Comment on Litbloc: Google Glass App Lets You Make A Sex Tape">
          <span class="leave-reply">
           Leave a reply
          </span>
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .comments-link -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         In mid-2013, an app designed to stream porn between Google Glass users was launched.
         <span id="more-44842">
         </span>
         As expected Google moved quickly to squash the presence of such content, modifying its developer policy to read, We dont allow Glassware content that contains nudity, graphic sex acts, or sexually explicit material. Despite Googles strict policy, a team of London-based developers developed an app which allows wearers to view of their night activities from all angles through a Sex with Glass app.
        </p>
        <p>
         Couples can record themselves when they are having sex, with the ability to live stream the vision between the two partners, given they are both wearing the sets of eyewear. Moreover, Sex with Glass can be connected to a smartphone to allow for viewing from various angles.
        </p>
        <p>
         Users can direct the app using voice commands, by saying, OK Glass, its time, then when users want to stop recording and/or streaming, OK Glass, pull out. How appropriate.
        </p>
        <p>
         We want to change the way people experience things, the website states. We love using design and strategy to disrupt at scale. So when we asked ourselves the question How can we make sex more awesome with Google Glass? we came up with Sex with Glass.
        </p>
        <p>
         As if this wasnt enough, the app allows users to play music to set the mood, request suggestions for sex positions, and set different levels of lighting to create just the right ambiance. When the night of passion draws to a close, the app compiles the footage to show the users a video. Your very own custom-made sex tape. Talk about improved love making.
        </p>
        <p>
         Given the work Sex with Glass is doing, naturally issues of privacy and hacking come into play. In an effort to placate these fears, the developers have also built a function into the app which destroys the footage after five hours, though this clearly still allows for potential invasion of privacy before that.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its unclear if Google will integrate this app. Meanwhile, the team is developing a free iPhone app to give users a glimpse of the Sex with Glass experience. For more information click the link below.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
