       <h1 class="entry-title">
        2015 Topps Finest Football Will Shout Back to 95
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-05-12T22:09:44+00:00">
         May 12, 2015
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/author/rmueller/" rel="author">
           Rich Mueller
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div id="within-post">
        <div class="widget simple_ads_manager_widget" id="simple_ads_manager_widget-19">
         <div class="widget-wrap">
          <div class="sam-container sam-place" data-sam="0" id="c7955_87_19">
           <a href="http://shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=739847&amp;u=193947&amp;m=28250&amp;urllink=&amp;afftrack=" target="_blank">
            <img alt="Find the Perfect Gift for Dad This Father's Day from SportsMemorabilia.com" border="0" src="http://static.shareasale.com/image/28250/FathersDay468x60.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         2015 Topps Finest Football will arrive just as the NFL regular season is hitting its stride. Topps has released some preliminary information on the product, which should retail at around $100 per box.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Aaron-Rodgers-base.jpg">
          <img alt="Aaron Rodgers 2015 Topps Finest Football" class="alignleft wp-image-52553" height="255" src="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Aaron-Rodgers-base.jpg" width="182"/>
         </a>
         The company will take the throwback route, going back 20 years to utilize the 1995 Finest design for o
         <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Mike-Evans-2015-Topps-Finest-Refractor-Auto1.jpg">
          <img alt="2015 Topps Finest 1995 design Mike Evans" class="alignright wp-image-52554" height="279" src="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Mike-Evans-2015-Topps-Finest-Refractor-Auto1.jpg" width="199"/>
         </a>
         ne of its insert subsets. Veterans, retired greats and rookies will be featured on cards with autographed versions numbered to 20 (
         <strong>
          Pulsar Refractors
         </strong>
         are #d to 10.
         <strong>
          SuperFractor Parallels
         </strong>
         are 1/1).
        </p>
        <p>
         Each master box of Finest Football will contain a total of 60 cards including:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1 Rookie Autograph Patch card
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1 Autographed Jumbo Relic card
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4 Finest Atomic Refractor rookie cards
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2 1995 design Finest rookie cards
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Rookie-Refractor-Auto-Jameis-Winston.jpg">
          <img alt="Jameis Winston Rookie Refractor auto 2015 Finest" class="alignleft wp-image-52552" height="195" src="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Rookie-Refractor-Auto-Jameis-Winston.jpg" width="274"/>
         </a>
         Autographed content will include serial numbered
         <strong>
          Rookie Refractor Autograph Variations
         </strong>
         which are on-card autograph image variations of the base rookie cards on chrome refractor technology with:
        </p>
        <p>
         Blue Refractor Parallel: #d to 25.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor Parallel: #d to 15.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pulsar Refractor: #d to 10.
        </p>
        <p>
         SuperFractor Parallel: #d 1/1
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Finest Atomic Rookies Refractor Autograph Die Cuts
         </strong>
         , featuring a perimeter die cut, chrome refractor technology and an
         <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Rookie-Refractor-Die-Cut-Kevin-White.jpg">
          <img alt="Kevin White 2015 Topps Finest Refractor DieCut" class="alignright wp-image-52547" height="260" src="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Rookie-Refractor-Die-Cut-Kevin-White.jpg" width="186"/>
         </a>
         on-card signature and these parallels:
        </p>
        <p>
         Blue Refractor: #d to 25.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor: #d to 15.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pulsar Refractor: #d to 10.
        </p>
        <p>
         SuperFractor: #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Rookie Refractor Autograph Patches
         </strong>
         include an autograph and a patch on refractor technology with these also possible:
        </p>
        <p>
         Blue Refractor Parallel: Numbered.
        </p>
        <p>
         Gold Refractor Parallel: Numbered.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Amari-Cooper-2015-Finest-Rookie-Refractor-Auto.jpg">
          <img alt="2015 Topps Finest Rookie Refractor Autograph Amari Cooper" class="alignright wp-image-52551" height="195" src="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Amari-Cooper-2015-Finest-Rookie-Refractor-Auto.jpg" width="274"/>
         </a>
         Diamond Refractor Parallel: Numbered.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pulsar Refractor Parallel: Jumbo Patch  #d to 35.
        </p>
        <p>
         X-Fractor Parallel: Jumbo Patch  #d to 20.
        </p>
        <p>
         STS Refractor Parallel: Jumbo Patch  #d to 15.
        </p>
        <p>
         BCA Refractor Parallel: Jumbo Patch  #d to 10.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor Pigskin NFL Shield Parallel: #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor NFL Shield Logo Parallel: #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor Swoosh Logo Tag Parallel: #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor Jersey Swoosh Logo Parallel: #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         SuperFractor Team Patch Parallel: Seq #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Autographed Jumbo Relic Refractors
         </strong>
         are numbered rookie and veteran autographs with a jumbo relic swatch. Look for
         <a href="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Football-Relic-Jumbo-Odell-Beckham.jpg">
          <img alt="Odell Beckham 2015 Topps Finest Jumbo Relic Odell Beckham" class="alignright wp-image-52550" height="204" src="http://www.sportscollectorsdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/2015-Topps-Finest-Football-Relic-Jumbo-Odell-Beckham.jpg" width="286"/>
         </a>
         these:
        </p>
        <p>
         Blue Refractor Parallel: Numbered.
        </p>
        <p>
         Gold Refractor Parallel: Numbered.
        </p>
        <p>
         Diamond Refractor Parallel: Numbered.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pulsar Refractor Patch Parallel: #d to 35.
        </p>
        <p>
         X-Fractor Patch Parallel: #d to 20.
        </p>
        <p>
         STS Patch Parallel: #d to 15.
        </p>
        <p>
         BCA Patch Parallel: #d to 10.
        </p>
        <p>
         Red Refractor Nike Swoosh Parallel: #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         SuperFractor Team Patch Parallel: #d 1/1
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Dual Autograph Dual Patches
         </strong>
         will feature two players with a relic and an autographfrom each (#d to 10). There will also be a SuperFractor #d 1/1.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Triple Autograph Triple Patches
         </strong>
         will have three players with a relic and autograph of each, numbered to ten with a 1/1 SuperFractor hiding in one case.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Quarterback Quartet Autograph Patch Book Cards
         </strong>
         will have four quarterback autographs and patches on a refractor book card. Numbered to 5.
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres also a
         <strong>
