                     <br/>
                     And then, because I have no decency, I sent the whole shebang over to Kim at
                     <a href="http://wp.me/p2eiH5-2Zt" target="_blank">
                      <b>
                       Cravings of a Lunatic
                      </b>
                     </a>
                     to use as a guest post.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Well,
                     <i>
                      okay
                     </i>
                     , I didn't email her the actual ice cream. Just words and photos.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     So, gawk at this photo of the caramel sauce.
                     <i>
                      Ooooh
                     </i>
                     , thick caramel sauce, ready for drizzling. Or for eating right off the spoon. We don't want to talk about how many times I did
                     <i>
                      that.
                     </i>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-AxC9Pcibl2A/U7n0h65qmbI/AAAAAAAAQaY/UQztzaRmmOA/s1600/baileys+caramel+sauce.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-AxC9Pcibl2A/U7n0h65qmbI/AAAAAAAAQaY/UQztzaRmmOA/s1600/baileys+caramel+sauce.JPG" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     And then take a closer look at this ice cream layered with caramel. Pretty ice cream, flavored with Baileys, along with a deeper-flavored caramel with Baileys. It's a match made in ... uh ... a glass.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YlRLLs294cA/U7n0h5dQfoI/AAAAAAAAQak/rHPvRpyHbGI/s1600/baileys+ice+cream+sundae.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YlRLLs294cA/U7n0h5dQfoI/AAAAAAAAQak/rHPvRpyHbGI/s1600/baileys+ice+cream+sundae.JPG" width="266"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     And then when you're done gawking, go visit Kim for the recipe. Because I
                     <i>
                      know
                     </i>
                     you want to make it!
