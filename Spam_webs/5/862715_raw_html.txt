    <h1>
     Foxcatcher  Promo Teaser
    </h1>
   </div>
   <div class="coll">
    <!-- description -->
    <p>
     <strong>
      source:
     </strong>
     <a href="http://youtu.be/pDCP9uaqs_s">
      RegalMovies.com
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      added: Thu, Sep 26th '13
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p>
     For the first time in his career, Steve Carell takes a dark and serious turn as John duPont, the real-life paranoid schizophrenic philanthropist who shot and killed Olympic wrestler/coach Dave Schultz, in the upcoming drama "Foxcatcher," which will likely have many wondering if the funnyman can pull off a heavy dramatic role. Certainly, he appears downright creepy in the film's absolutely stunning debut trailer. It's safe to say, you'll never look at Steve Carell the same way again.
    </p>
    <p>
     "Foxcatcher" was directed by Benett Miller (whose credits include Moneyball and Capote) and also stars Channing Tatum, Mark Ruffalo and Sienna Miller.
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      Update:
     </strong>
     Sony Pictures Classics has just announced that they have delayed the film's release, essentially shifting it out of this year's award season and into 2014, says
     <a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/bennett-millers-awards-contender-foxcatcher-637536">
      The Hollywood Reporter
     </a>
     .
    </p>
    <p>
     The reason for the date shift? The filmmakers, apparently, did not have enough time to finish the film this year, which left them with the only option to push back the release date until sometime in 2014. Bummed? We are, too.
    </p>
    <!-- detail box -->

       synopsis:

      <br/>
      Steve Carell steps into dramatic territory with this docudrama about John du Pont, the schizophrenic millionaire who infamously shot and killed his friend and Olympic Gold Medal wrestler Dave Schultz (Mark Ruffalo) before locking himself in his mansion as police officers negotiated his surrender for two days.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       directed by
      </strong>
      Benett Miller
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       starring

      Steve Carell, Mark Ruffalo, Channing Tatum, Sienna Miller, Anthony Michael Hall, Vanessa Redgrave
     </p>

      <strong>
       release date
      </strong>
      2014
     </p>
