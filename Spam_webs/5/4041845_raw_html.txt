       <p>
        <img alt="1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-91114" height="602" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/1.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        A artista australiana
        <a href="http://www.iphoneogram.com/u/233517214" target="_blank">
         CJ Hendry
        </a>
        desenha com tcnica impressionante e muita pacincia ilustraes de objetos to reais que parecem saltar aos olhos.
        <!--more-->
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="2" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-91115" height="599" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/2.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="3" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-91116" height="601" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/3.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="4" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-91117" height="600" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/4.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="5" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-91118" height="600" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/5.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        De forma que parecem fotografias em p&amp;b granuladas, os desenhos criados por Hendry so feitos meticulosamente com pontilhados de canetas nanquim.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="6" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-91120" height="603" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/6.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="7" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-91122" height="604" src="http://www.ideafixa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/7.jpg" width="600"/>
