         KB Homes  How A Smart Home Will Change MealTime [Infographic]
        </h1>

         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/how-a-smart-home-will-change-mealtime.jpg">
          <img alt="kb homes - how a smart home will change meal time" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5847" height="2210" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/how-a-smart-home-will-change-mealtime.jpg" width="720"/>

        <p>
         KB Home put together a great infographic that will show you how a smart home will revolutionize your mealtimes. You will be surprised. Just imagine this: you had a tough day at work and all you want is to eat something really good and relax. But, after such a long day, who has a time to prepare dinner? Well, what if we told you that your appliances will make your tasty dinner by themselves and it will be ready when you come home? You can just use your smart-phone controllable slow cooker, even though you are not in your kitchen. You can switch it on or off, change the temperature, just by using your smartphone!
        </p>
        <p>
         What else can a smart home do? You can use smart chopping board to prepare meals much faster than usual. This Wi-Fi enabled board uses an interactive multifunctional touch screen to weigh ingredients for you or even suggest recipes based on what are you chopping. You dont have to think how much eggs you have to use if you need to scale down the quantities, smart chopping board will do that for you!
        </p>
        <p>
         Smart homes are definitely saving our money and time, which is truly great. Listen to this. A new cooking device will allow you induction cooking, which means that only your pan is getting heated, while the rest of the hob remains cool. Well, that is definitely cool! There is also a built-in Android tablet included in the oven top that will let you view your recipes or monitor the oven from your phone. Thats not all  there are further innovations in the pipeline that will blow your mind. We are talking about cooking tops with 3D technology, palm scanners to assess nutritional needs and much more. Smart kitchens are definitely a dream place for busy people!
        </p>
