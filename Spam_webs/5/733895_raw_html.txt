           <h2 class="subheader" itemprop="alternativeHeadline">
            Top 10 Cars of All Time
           </h2>
           <p>
            <!--pagetitle:Top 10 Cars of All Time-->
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.autoguide.com/auto-news/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Top-10-Cars-of-All-Time-.jpg">
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Cars have shaped the world we live in by giving mobility to the masses. Over the past century and beyond there have been plenty of revolutionary vehicles, not to mention quite a few clunkers.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <!--more-->
           </p>
           <p>
            Throughout its history the automotive industry has produced literally dozens of revolutionary cars. But remember, there are only 10 spaces on this list so everything wont fit. Did your beloved Plymouth
            <a href="http://www.autoguide.com/gallery/gallery.php/v/main/events/festivals-of-speed-st-petersburg-2012/Festivals-of-Speed-Plymouth-Superbird-04.JPG.html">
             Superbird
            </a>
            make the cut? What about Hondas scrappy little
            <a href="http://www.autoguide.com/auto-news/tag/honda-crx">
             CR-X?
            </a>
            Keep on reading to find out what the Top 10 Cars of All Time really are.
           </p>
