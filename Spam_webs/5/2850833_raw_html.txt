          <h1 class="title single-title">
           5 Best ways of conceal freckles
          </h1>
          <div class="post-info">
           By
           <a href="http://healthluv.com/author/shailender/" rel="nofollow" title="Posts by shailender">
            shailender
           </a>
           On 22 January 2014 In
           <a href="http://healthluv.com/category/caring-tips/" rel="nofollow">
            How to Care
           </a>
          </div>
         </header>
         <!--.headline_area-->
         <div class="post-single-content box mark-links">
          <span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="50">
          </span>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="Beautiful Girl with Freckles" class=" " height="310" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/oIMAn4875-gpuON27LpUE8httHMxMpcSkQ0KwW9J6BE=w535-h347-no" title="Beautiful Girl with Freckles" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Girl with Freckles
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           People usually give contradictory views about the word freckles when you mention it. Those who do not have the little spots consider them extremely adorable and they treat them as a statement of character instead of thinking of them as blemishes while those who have them want to remove them by doing almost anything that they can because they hate seeing them.
           <span id="more-748">
           </span>
           Freckles are brownish, red, or yellow colored melanin spots. They are often small and flat and their size is approximately that of a nail head. These spots usually appear on people who have red hair or are fair skinned. However, almost all people can get freckles if they expose themselves to the sun for a long time. If you have freckles and you are working very hard to eliminate them, the following tips would be very useful for you because they can enable you to conceal them.
          </p>
          <h2>
           How to conceal freckles
          </h2>
          <p>
           If you really want to conceal freckles, you can do so by using the following 5 easy steps.
          </p>
          <h3>
           1. Slather up on your SPF protection
          </h3>
          <p>
           This helps you to avoid any more brown spots. Sunblock helps in preventing brown blemishes from developing and hence, it is important to mention how important and essential the aforementioned step is.
          </p>
          <h3>
           2. Use cream foundation with a full coverage
          </h3>
          <p>
           You may have to opt for heavy-duty foundations if you do not use enough coverage on your usual foundation. The consistency is very thick. However, it is not only heavy duty enough for use on theatre or shoots and even very hard for covering blemishes such as port wine stains but it is also perfect for coverage.
          </p>
          <h3>
           3. Use a corrector with peach color
          </h3>
          <p>
           This helps in neutralizing the brown pigments of the freckles that you have (or any other blemish that is brown in color). You should apply the corrector before your concealer but after your foundation. You should use a concealer brush to apply it before blending lightly by using your ring finger or sponge in a stippling motion. If you want to achieve a flawless look in photos and in person, you should know that blending is necessary at all times.
          </p>
          <h3>
           4. Apply a yellow-based concealer over the corrector by using a concealer brush
          </h3>
          <h3>
           5. Use a dusting of loose or sheer powder to top off
          </h3>
          <p>
           You should remember that the testers serve a specific purpose and hence, you should try them before you buy them. You should be careful when you buy the products and ensure that you try them before walking around the mall throughout the day in order to see their reaction with your skin, if they last and how they feel on your face. You should then buy them as soon as you are convinced. You should not allow yourself to be bullied into buying the products once you try them. You are entitled to say no and you should test them before shelling out. In addition, the foundation might already work for covering major freckles and hence, spending your money on the correctors or concealers may not be necessary.
          </p>
          <p>
           Finally, you should make an effort to work with what you have or seek medical attention if the aforementioned steps do not help you to achieve your desired results.
          </p>
