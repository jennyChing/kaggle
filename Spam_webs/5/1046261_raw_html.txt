                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Photo Friday: Where Word Nerds and Design Nerds Meet
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6179265035854468930" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Word nerds and design nerds rejoice! I think I found the point where you two meet. I stumbled across this amazing collection on Brain Pickings today of unusual and rarely used words turned into bold graphics and the literature major in me swooned. Obscure words and pretty graphics make up most of my life.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Phw3KKog4vw/UXrSmHe7PEI/AAAAAAAAIG4/j5FNhF4NG40/s1600/word-nerd-1.JPG"/>
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     This project, put together by
                     <a href="http://theprojecttwins.com/?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=26&amp;Itemid=15">
                      Project Twins
                     </a>
                     , grabbed me instantly. Mostly because I spent a good deal of time trying to guess the words on my own and let me just say, these are in fact some obscure words. It was still fun though. I just picked out a few of my favorite word/design combos, but you can check out the whole
                     <a href="http://www.brainpickings.org/index.php/2012/07/02/project-twins-unusual-words/">
                      A-Z set on Brain Pickings
                     </a>
                     .
