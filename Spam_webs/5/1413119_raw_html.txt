       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Aloha Sunrise Protein Cake Bars
       </h1>
       <p>
        <span class="text-detail">
         By
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard text-emphasize">
         <a href="http://blog.questnutrition.com/author/guest-author/" rel="author" title="Posts by Guest Author">
          Guest Author
         </a>

        </span>
        <span class="cat-links text-emphasize">

       </p>
      </header>
      <div class="fb-like" data-action="like" data-font="" data-href="http://blog.questnutrition.com/aloha-sunrise-protein-cake-bars/" data-layout="standard" data-send="true" data-width="450" show_faces="true" style="margin: 0px 0px 0px 0px;">
      </div>
      <div style="float: right; margin: 0px 0px 0px 0px">
       <g:plusone href="http://blog.questnutrition.com/aloha-sunrise-protein-cake-bars/">

      </div>
      <p>
       <a href="http://d39cy8ulqxbvhv.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/alohasun-procakebar-Quest3sm.jpg">
        <img alt="alohasun-procakebar-Quest3sm" class="aligncenter wp-image-7816" height="270" src="http://d39cy8ulqxbvhv.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/alohasun-procakebar-Quest3sm.jpg" width="360"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       These White Chocolate Macadamia Raspberry Cake Bars are high in protein, fruit and of course vegetables. A neigh cheese-cakeish compote but without any heavy cheese ingredients. You will get a lite yet flavorful fruity punch combined with a cookie crunch in every bite of this protein treat. This is surely a post workout delectable or can be enjoyed at any part of the day.
      </p>
      <p>
       Spoiler Alert!: A spoon and a fork may both be required.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Prep Time:
       </span>
       35 minutes
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Bake Time:
       </span>
       3-4 minutes
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Ingredients:
       </span>
       <br/>
       Cookie Crunch Layer:
       <br/>
       2 servings of White Chocolate Raspberry Quest Bars
       <br/>
       2 tbsp of Organic Raspberry Fruit Spread
       <br/>
       1/4 cup of Raw Organic Macadamia Nuts (Crushed)
       <br/>
       1/3 cup of Fresh Organic Raspberries
      </p>
      <p>
       Creamy Cake Layer:
       <br/>
       1 scp of Plant-Based Protein (Natural)
       <br/>
       1 scp of Organic Brown Rice Protein (Vanilla)
       <br/>
       1 cup of Unsweetened Organic Cashew Milk (Vanilla)
       <br/>
       1 tbsp of Organic Chia Seeds
       <br/>
       2 tbsp of Gluten-Free Coconut Flour
       <br/>
       1/4 tsp of Organic Grounded Vanilla Bean
       <br/>
       1/2 tsp of Organic Grounded Cinnamon
       <br/>
       2 tsp of Fresh Squeeze Organic Lemon Juice
       <br/>
       3/4 cup of Raw Organic Yellow Squash (Chopped)
       <br/>
       1/2 cup of Frozen Organic Bananas
      </p>
      <p>
       Toppings:
       <br/>
       1/4 cup of Raw Organic Macadamia Nuts (Crushed)
       <br/>
       1/3 cup of Fresh Organic Raspberries
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Directions:
       </span>
       <br/>
       1. Cookie Crunch Layer: Roll out white chocolate raspberry quest bars with rolling pin and roll into square. Microwave for 30sec if needed. Next, place flatten quest bars into a 9x9in square pan and bake 3-4min or until lite-brown. Let cool and then add a thin layer of raspberry fruit spread on top. Set aside.
      </p>
      <p>
       2. Creamy Cake Layer: Combine all ingredients under cake layer into a blender and blend until smooth. Next, pour cake layer into 9x9in pan with quest bar layer at the bottom and apply evenly.
      </p>
      <p>
       3. Toppings: sprinkle crushed macadamia nuts and then raspberries and store in freezer to set overnight.
      </p>
      <p>
       4. Finally, take out pan, thaw, then cut into bars and dig in with fork or spoon if needed.
      </p>
