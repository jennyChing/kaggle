     <p>
      <b>
       Al-Sabinah
      </b>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">
       Arabic
      </a>
      :
      <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">
       <big>
        
       </big>
      </span>
      ) is a town in southern
      <a href="/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">
       Syria
      </a>
      , administratively part of the
      <a href="/wiki/Rif_Dimashq_Governorate" title="Rif Dimashq Governorate">
       Rif Dimashq Governorate
      </a>
      , located southwest of
      <a href="/wiki/Damascus" title="Damascus">
       Damascus
      </a>
      in the western
      <a href="/wiki/Ghouta" title="Ghouta">
       Ghouta
      </a>
      . Nearby localities include
      <a href="/wiki/Ashrafiyat_Sahnaya" title="Ashrafiyat Sahnaya">
       Ashrafiyat Sahnaya
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Darayya" title="Darayya">
       Darayya
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Muadamiyat_al-Sham" title="Muadamiyat al-Sham">
       Muadamiyat al-Sham
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Sayyidah_Zaynab" title="Sayyidah Zaynab">
       Sayyidah Zaynab
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Al-Hajar_al-Aswad" title="Al-Hajar al-Aswad">
       al-Hajar al-Aswad
      </a>
      . According to the
      <a href="/wiki/Central_Bureau_of_Statistics_(Syria)" title="Central Bureau of Statistics (Syria)">
       Syria Central Bureau of Statistics
      </a>
      , al-Sabinah had a population of 62,509 in the 2004 census.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBS_1-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-CBS-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      A
      <a href="/wiki/Palestinian_refugee" title="Palestinian refugee">
       Palestinian refugee
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Refugee_camp" title="Refugee camp">
       camp
      </a>
      was established in the town in 1948.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>

      </sup>
