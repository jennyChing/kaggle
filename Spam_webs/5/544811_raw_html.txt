            <p>
             Converting an old barn into a home is quite a project in itself, but adding a new living space onto that barn can be a very ambitious undertaking.
             <a href="http://www.puigpujolarchitectures.fr/realisations/logements/extension-dune-grange-lesponne-2010.html">
              PPA Architects
             </a>
             created a stunning modern addition to this barn conversion in Lesponne in the heart of picturesque Hautes-Pyrnes, France.
            </p>

           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-36736" height="311" src="http://assets.dornob.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/barn-extension-ppa-architects.jpg" title="barn extension ppa architects" width="468"/>

           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-36740" height="312" src="http://assets.dornob.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/french-barn-extension1.jpg" title="french barn extension" width="468"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The barn on the property had already been converted to a gorgeous vacation home with rich wood floors, exposed timber beams, and classic stone walls. The incredible living space needed some extra room, however, for a modern family and their guests to stretch out into.
           </p>

           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-36742" height="312" src="http://assets.dornob.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/ppa-architects-french-barn-extension1.jpg" title="ppa architects french barn extension" width="468"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The extension project added on a new living area that both complements and perfectly offsets the traditional stone architecture of the original barn. With a green roof, glass walls and stacks of wood that act as half-walls, the extension incorporates nature in a superbly sophisticated fashion.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-36743" height="311" src="http://assets.dornob.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/barn-extension-france1.jpg" title="barn extension france" width="468"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-36744" height="312" src="http://assets.dornob.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/beautiful-french-barn-add-on1.jpg" title="beautiful french barn add on" width="468"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The stone and wood from the barn continue to play a large part in the interior of the newly-extended living space. However, there is also a very modern element enhanced by contemporary furnishings. The enormous glass walls allow residents to look out from the work space or guest bedroom over the truly awe-inspiring surroundings.
