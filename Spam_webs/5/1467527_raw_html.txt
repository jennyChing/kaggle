            <div class="blog-content">
             <p>
              These Potluck Oreo Bars will easily be a crowd favorite! They start with a dense chocolate cookie base (made with a cake mix) and are topped with a dreamy Oreo Cream Cheese Mousse!
              <br/>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Potluck Oreo Bars ~ These are so dreamy, they start with a cake mix and are topped with a creamy Oreo mousse!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12743" height="800" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/oreobars.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/oreobars.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              So my husband has taken up juicing
             </p>
             <p>
              He went out and bought a juicer and is now all about new juices.
             </p>
             <p>
              This means he spends infinity dollars on produce daily. Because how much juice do you think comes out of a head of broccoli? Like 2 teaspoons. So basically to get enough juice to fill a Dixie cup you have to buy 45 heads of broccoli.
             </p>
             <p>
              And 45 heads of broccoli is expensive.
             </p>
             <p>
              Also are Dixie cups still a thing? I used to drink from them all the time when I was a childand now I cant even remember the last time I SAW one.
             </p>
             <p>
              I mean, really they are kind of annoying, they only hold like 1/4 cup of liquid.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span id="more-12694">
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Anyhow, back to my husbands juice.
             </p>
             <p>
              Like I said you have to buy a LOT of veggies and fruit to actually make a glass, which means my refrigerator is filled with kale and spinach and beets and broccoli and things with weird odors.
             </p>
             <p>
              I have no room left for pitchers of lemonade or coffee creamer. Its becoming an issue.
             </p>
             <p>
              And might I just say when my husband makes this juice, its the color of poop after youve been to a birthday party and eaten a piece of cake with a giant green flower.
             </p>
             <p>
              Yeah, theres a visual for you.
             </p>
             <p>
              So it looks sick, smells weird and takes up all my lemonade space.
             </p>
             <p>
              But its healthy. So theres that.
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              Anyway, the whole point of my story is that I made these Potluck Oreo Bars, which need to be refrigerated, but I had no room in the fridge for my Oreo bars because of all the kale, which forced me to eat all the bars.
             </p>
             <p>
              So basically my husbands juice is making me fat.
             </p>
             <p>
              Thanks for that.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Potluck Oreo Bars, simple and easily a crowd favorite!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12742" height="751" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1609.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1609.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              The beauty of these bars is that they start with a cake mix. Which again, I know some of you hate. But its just the nature of the beast today.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Base starts with a chocolate cake mix.." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12744" height="760" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1578.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1578.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              You use the cake mix as a thick base and then top it with a creamy cheese mixture
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Topping the base with a chocolate cream cheese mixture!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12745" height="794" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1581.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1581.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Bake it up like this
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="The baked base for Potluck Oreo Bars" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12747" height="775" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1591.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1591.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Then chop some Oreos.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Chopped oreos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12746" height="797" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1587.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1587.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              And mix it with more cream cheese deliciousness.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Oreo Cream Cheese filling!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12748" height="746" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1594.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1594.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Mix that with Cool Whip. Yes, I know this recipe is from the devilcake mix, Oreo AND Cool Whip.
             </p>
             <p>
              Spread that on top
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="topping the bars..." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12749" height="793" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1596.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1596.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              So pretty!
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Finishing off the Oreo Bars with the Oreo Cream Cheese mousse!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12750" height="786" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1602.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1602.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              And then more Oreosbecause yes.
             </p>
             <p>
              And then you should eat. And eat.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Potluck Oreo Bars...a delicous cookie base that starts with a cake mix, topped with a light Oreo Mousse!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12741" height="802" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1604.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/potluck-oreo-bars/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_1604.jpg&amp;description=Potluck Oreo Bars')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Then have some juice
              <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <br/>
             </p>

               <div class="ERSSavePrint">

              <div class="ERSIngredients">
               <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
                Ingredients
               </div>
               <ul>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 box chocolate cake mix
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                  cup butter, melted +  cup butter, melted
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 2 eggs, divided
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 (8 oz) brick cream cheese
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 4 cups powdered sugar
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 Tbsp vanilla
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 2 Tbsp Cocoa powder
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 (8 oz) container Cool Whip, thawed
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 12 Double Stuf Oreos, chopped
                </li>
               </ul>
               <div class="ERSClear">
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="ERSInstructions">
               <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
                Instructions
               </div>
               <ol>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Preheat oven to 350
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Spray a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray, set aside.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 In bowl, stir together cake mix,  cup butter (1 stick), melted and 1 egg until smooth.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Press mixture into the bottom of your prepared pan.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 In bowl of stand mixer beat cream cheese, vanilla and powdered sugar together until smooth. Remove half of the mixture from the bowl and reserve for later (eyeball it). In the remaining cream cheese mixture, add cocoa powder,  cup butter, melted and remaining egg. Beat until smooth.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Spread this mixture over top the cake mix layer. Bake for 30 minutes until just set.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Allow to cool completely.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 When base is completely cool, stir in half of your chopped Oreos to the remaining cream cheese mixture. Then fold in thawed Cool Whip until smooth.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Spread this all over your cooled base. Sprinkle with remaining chopped Oreos.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Chill for at least an hour. Cut into squares when ready to serve.
                </li>
               </ol>
