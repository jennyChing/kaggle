           <h4>
            Ever wonder what marijuana looks like up close and personal?
           </h4>
           <p>
            Now you can see how cannabis appears to the scientists who study it, thanks to a new book called
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Cannabis-Under-Microscope-Exploration-Medicinal-ebook/dp/B00J7ZL5TY/">
             Cannabis Under The Microscope: A Visual Exploration of Medicinal Sativa and C. Indica
            </a>
            by Ford McCann.
           </p>
           <p>
            The book features over 170 images of cannabis in its full glory, taken with optical and scanning electron microscopes.
           </p>
