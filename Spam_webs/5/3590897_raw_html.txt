          <p class="p1">
           <span class="s1">
            Comedian Jon Stewart is set to leave his role as host of Comedy Centrals The Daily Show later this year. Stewart confirmed the news to his studio audience at the end of his program on Tuesday. The decision will see him conclude 16 years involvement with the parody newscast. Hugely popular with the 18-35 demographic, The Daily Show is regularly cited as one of the primary news sources for that age group. Its unclear what the next step is for Stewart, thought the two-time Oscar host made his directorial debut with the film Rosewater last year
