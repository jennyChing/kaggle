         Frank Ocean Grammy Recap/Live Performance
        </h1>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="272" src="http://www.dailymotion.com/embed/video/k1UqiMkqHrrl623NyO5" width="480">
         </iframe>
         <i>
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         By now youve probably heard the exciting news  Frank Ocean has won
         <strong>
          two
         </strong>
         of the Grammys that he was nominated for:
         <em>
          Best Urban Contemporary Album
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          Best Rap/Sung Collaboration.
         </em>
         This would be a monumental achievement in any artists career. And of course, in addition to taking two Grammys home, Frank Ocean gave a stunning performance of his song
         <em>
          Forrest Gump
         </em>
         accompanied by visuals reminiscent of the
         <em>
          Forrest Gump
         </em>
         movie. Like promised, Frank kept his word, and indeed rocked a yellow tux at the grammys. Watch the video of Franks performance above and let us know what you thought of tonights winners.
         <br/>
         <em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         In a short interview with MTV, Frank also lets us know that he is around 12 or 13 songs deep into his next project. Who knows when well get any more info on it, but knowing Frank, well most likely get some news about it out of the blue. Stay tuned!
        </p>
        <div style="background-color: #000000; width: 520px;">
         <div style="padding: 4px;">
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="288" src="http://media.mtvnservices.com/embed/mgid:uma:video:mtv.com:878860/cp~id%3D1701354%26vid%3D878860%26uri%3Dmgid%3Auma%3Avideo%3Amtv.com%3A878860" width="512">
          </iframe>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Congratulations to
         <a href="https://twitter.com/frank_ocean">
          @frank_ocean
         </a>
         and the rest of Odd Future. Wolf gang!
        </p>
