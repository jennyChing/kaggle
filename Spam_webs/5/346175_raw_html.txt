            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
             After years of renting I'm excited to announce that we finally purchase our very first home right here in sunny orange county California. I've always wanted to own my own home so this is pretty much a dream come true. Our new home is a Mediterranean-style home with three bedrooms, a huge backyard with a swimming pool with a barbecue area and a very spacious kitchen.
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
             We just moved in two weeks ago and have been very busy unpacking. The place is still semi-furnished but we still have to buy a couch, coffee table and a bunch of other stuff. It's so nice to finally have your own place and not have to deal with a landlord or neighbors. I also want can to have a lot of space for Kim to run around in the backyard when she gets older. The area is very upscale and safe with a great school district (one of the main reasons why we chose this location).
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
             Moving into our own house has been a good experience benefits also been stressful to say the least. It's always been our dream to live in our own home but to be honest it's been kind of difficult since we don't have perfect credit it had to apply for a home loan twice. The first time we were denied by
             <a href="https://www.bankofamerica.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              Bank of America
             </a>
             because my husband's credit had some dings on it but we finally got approved by
             <a href="http://www.loandepot.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              loanDepot
             </a>
             and were able to take advantage of lower interest rates. Yayyy!!!
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
             I am thinking of painting the Kim's bedroom pink with a pink/white princess theme. It will be very girlie and feminine and almost like a mini castle. We're thinking of decorating the living room contemporary style with more earth tones (but still cozy). Not the really cold, minimalistic look; more like West Elm style.
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            </p>
            <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
             Packing with such a huge pain; you never realize how much stuff you actually happen until you have to pack it up boxes. Thankfully we were able to hire helpers and movers from Craigslist which made things a lot smoother. I'll post photos of our new home as soon as were settled in. Thanks for checking in!
            </p>
