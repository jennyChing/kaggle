         <div class="post-body">
          <p>
           <a class="zoom" href="http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79d2f92ea135f9004d32/Learning Campus Model.jpg">
            <img alt="Learning Campus Model.jpg" height="334" src="http://cdn.cstatic.net/gridnailer/500x/http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79d2f92ea135f9004d32/Learning Campus Model.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <span class="credit">
            [Images via the
            <a href="http://www.chicagobotanic.org/projects/learningcampus">
             Chicago Botanic Garden
            </a>
            ]
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           As if on schedule for this spring warmth, the
           <strong>
            Chicago Botanic Garden
           </strong>
           in Glencoe recently broke ground on its new, $26 million Regenstein Foundation Learning Campus, the
           <a href="http://www.chicagoarchitecture.org/2015/05/06/chicago-botanic-garden-breaks-ground-on-new-26-million-campus/">
            Chicago Architecture Blog reports
           </a>
           . The seven-acre learning campus includes a 1.5-acre learning garden as well as a new, 25,000-square-foot education center, designed by
           <strong>
            Booth Hansen
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <div class="post-kicker">
           <span class="extended">
            <a href="http://chicago.curbed.com/archives/2015/05/07/chicago-botanic-garden-breaks-ground-on-learning-campus.php#more">
             More renderings and a virtual flyover  &gt;&gt;
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="post-more" id="more">
           <p>
            <a class="zoom" href="http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79eff92ea108a6006497/Education Center Model 2.jpg">
             <img alt="Education Center Model 2.jpg" height="334" src="http://cdn.cstatic.net/gridnailer/500x/http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79eff92ea108a6006497/Education Center Model 2.jpg" width="500"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="zoom" href="http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79eff92ea108a6006494/Education Center Model 1.jpg">
             <img alt="Education Center Model 1.jpg" height="224" src="http://cdn.cstatic.net/gridnailer/500x/http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79eff92ea108a6006494/Education Center Model 1.jpg" width="500"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            With sustainability input from the Rocky Mountain Institute, the education center expects
            <strong>
             LEED Platinum
            </strong>
            certificationthe highest sustainability rating conferred by the US Green Building Councilbased on its use of recycled and low-emission materials, solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and other sustainability strategies, all of which will be tracked in real-time. Landscape design for the center is being developed and adapted by Mikyoung Kim along with Jacob/Ryan Associates, and the campus is expected to be open by
            <strong>
             summer 2016
            </strong>
            . Meanwhile, the remaining 385 acres of the botanic gardens are green, blooming, and open for business.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="zoom" href="http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79d2f92ea135f9004d35/Learning Campus Schematic Design.jpg">
             <img alt="Learning Campus Schematic Design.jpg" height="375" src="http://cdn.cstatic.net/gridnailer/500x/http://cdn.cstatic.net/images/gridfs/554b79d2f92ea135f9004d35/Learning Campus Schematic Design.jpg" width="500"/>
            </a>
           </p>
