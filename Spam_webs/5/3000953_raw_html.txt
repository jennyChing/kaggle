                            Find out more
                           </a>
                           .
                          </strong>
                         </p>
                         <p class="MsoNormal">
                         </p>
                         <h2>
                          <strong>
                           2nd Globalizer on Economic Inclusion in Latin America in Buenos Aires, Argentina from  September 25-27 2015.
                          </strong>
                         </h2>
                         <p>
                          Building on the success of the 1st
                          <a href="/economic-inclusion-LA">
                           Globalizer on Economic Inclusion in Latin America
                          </a>
                          we are now launching the 2nd round on this critical topic. Once again Ashoka will partner with the Swiss Development Cooperation (SDC) to support social entrepreneurs in Latin America working for economic inclusion. The Globalizer Summit in Buenos Aires will bring together leading Ashoka Fellows with globally scalable innovations for poverty alleviation. Their solutions will be market-driven, have proven track records and involve entire communities to alleviate poverty. Further topics that will be explored and built on at this Summit include strengthening financial mechanisms and corporative structures across the region to increase the impact of inclusive businesses.
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          These social influencers are ready to expand their innovation's impact beyond localized organizations. They have varied strategies for increasing economic inclusion, such as:
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          
                          <a href="https://www.ashoka.org/fellow/angie-ferrazzini" target="_blank">
                           Angie Ferrazzini
                          </a>
                          , whose initiative
                          <a href="http://www.sabelatierra.com/" target="_blank">
                           Sabe La Tierra
                          </a>
                          fosters direct exchange between local producers, micro entrepreneurs and consumers, promoting the concept of agricultural sustainability and conscious consumption
                          <br/>
                          
                          <a href="https://www.ashoka.org/fellow/javier-lozano" target="_blank">
                           Javier Lozano
                          </a>
                          , whose chain of
                          <a href="http://www.clinicasdelazucar.com/" target="_blank">
                           Clinicas del Azcar
                          </a>
                          - by making use of advanced technology and diagnostic and treatment innovations - offers high-quality, yet affordable medical care to treat diabetes for populations that could otherwise not access it
                          <br/>
                          
                          <a href="http://usa.ashoka.org/fellow/michael-turner" target="_blank">
                           Michael Turner
                          </a>
                          , whose organization
                          <a href="http://www.perc.net/" target="_blank">
                           PERC
                          </a>
                          is leading a national and global effort to dramatically increase financial inclusion by promoting the responsible use of regular non-financial payment and other data in existing credit assessment tools
                          <br/>
                          
                          <a href="https://www.ashoka.org/fellow/vicente-fenoll" target="_blank">
                           Vicente Fenoll
                          </a>
                          , whose platform
                          <a href="https://www.kubofinanciero.com/Kubo/Portal/index.xhtml" target="_blank">
                           kubo.financiero
                          </a>
                          represents the first financial community in Mexico specialized in Crowdfunding and Peer to Peer (P2P) Lending and is regulated by the National Banking and Securities Commission
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          The Globalizer Summit in Argentina matches these "ready to globalize" economic inclusion models with the strategic resources they need to rapidly and effectively achieve continental and global scale.
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          In the next months leading up to September's Globalizer Summit, the participating Ashoka Fellows will build their scaling strategies with an advisory team of senior business executives and strategy consultants. At the Summit itself, global business leaders will meet with Fellows and critically analyze their scaling strategies while also offering Fellows the strategic support and insight needed to enable these innovations to increase their impact dramatically.
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          The Summit's Ecosystem Day will take a broader view by having participants explore barriers faced by economic inclusion initiatives while scaling their impact. A broad alliance of various stakeholders will brainstorm together on how existing gaps in the support ecosystem could be filled.
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          Throughout the Globalizer Summit, Ashoka Fellows will engage in peer-to-peer exchange to facilitate collaboration on their shared work towards economic inclusion.
                         </p>
