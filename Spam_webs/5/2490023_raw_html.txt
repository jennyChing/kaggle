                    <meta content="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5FYGrGUHLdc/VDXiEjaIfjI/AAAAAAAADIw/aA1hHFF6bJ4/s1600/pumpkin-179527_1280.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     5 Reasons American Bloggers Should Visit Australia
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6950854922514821151" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      Okay, so you know I love all my American blogger friends right? Well I do! But... this time of year makes you all go a little crazy! You all just can't stop posting about Fall, pumpkin spice lattes, Halloween and even Christmas! Yes... I said it... you all... NOT ya'll! In my opinion getting away from the word ya'll is reason enough to visit Australia but here are my top 5 reasons American Bloggers should visit Australia:
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5FYGrGUHLdc/VDXiEjaIfjI/AAAAAAAADIw/aA1hHFF6bJ4/s1600/pumpkin-179527_1280.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="360" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5FYGrGUHLdc/VDXiEjaIfjI/AAAAAAAADIw/aA1hHFF6bJ4/s1600/pumpkin-179527_1280.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <b>
                        1. Pumpkins
                       </b>
                      </div>
                      Seriously... we do have pumpkins here too! I just had pumpkin soup for breakfast and it's Spring here! WTH? It's a world gone topsy turvy! I have 2 pumpkins in the kitchen ready to go. I eat them but I don't decorate them or with them... I know I'm totally weird! Which brings me to my next point:
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-WS_mhoXaYgQ/VDXkqGOmg4I/AAAAAAAADI8/yoUg7rAZ69o/s1600/ae7a7451-6536-4026-9408-56c017b956c6.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="440" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-WS_mhoXaYgQ/VDXkqGOmg4I/AAAAAAAADI8/yoUg7rAZ69o/s1600/ae7a7451-6536-4026-9408-56c017b956c6.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       2. Real Coffee
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      Man oh man have I been inundated with instagram pics of your Starbucks coffees lately. What is your obsession with pumpkin lattes? You do get that there's no actual pumpkin in there right? Anyway on to my main point, Starbucks had to close 61 of it's 87 shops here because in Australia their coffee isn't considered good enough. Actually, it's not even considered coffee... more like coffee flavoured water. If you're interested you can
                      <a href="http://www.abc.net.au/news/2008-08-07/32188" target="_blank">
                       get the full story of why Starbucks just doesn't cut it in Australia
                      </a>
                      .
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DRsi56XF7j4/VDXn2GVwS5I/AAAAAAAADJI/FDewXj_p30c/s1600/chairs-270621_1280.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-DRsi56XF7j4/VDXn2GVwS5I/AAAAAAAADJI/FDewXj_p30c/s1600/chairs-270621_1280.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       3. Vintage Shopping
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      In Australia, especially Tasmania we have less of the big corporate chain stores and more of the little guys! Except on Sundays of course! There are heaps of cute little shops to find vintage wares, antiques, collectibles and little trinkets everywhere, in the city centres and in the 'burbs. I have a number of favourites that I frequent and recently found a new amazingly priced antiques and second hand store where I purchased the most gorgeous vintage dresser that I'm now working on so stay tuned! BTW if you'd like to hit me up with your stripping techniques please shoot me an email! That's wood stripping by the way, just to clarify!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-LuCqHlHiLw0/VDXq3-YG5HI/AAAAAAAADJU/QpfxESjMsKk/s1600/halloween-187784_1280.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="424" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-LuCqHlHiLw0/VDXq3-YG5HI/AAAAAAAADJU/QpfxESjMsKk/s1600/halloween-187784_1280.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       4. Halloween
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      Halloween has finally made it's way to Australia and now we have it too! Okay so it's not exactly the big night that it is in the states but for the first time ever I'm going to have to stock up on lollies for the neighbour kids this year. Last year I got lucky and just happened to have some bags of sweets lying around to hand out when they knocked so this year I'm going to be prepared!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-4518vj1o8Wc/VDXrgLZvroI/AAAAAAAADJc/zFhxyjPrmLI/s1600/busselton-51434_1280.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-4518vj1o8Wc/VDXrgLZvroI/AAAAAAAADJc/zFhxyjPrmLI/s1600/busselton-51434_1280.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       5. Christmas
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      I know it's not your wonderful white Christmas that you all know and love but this is what Christmas in Australia looks like and it's beachy bliss baby so start saving and enjoy the
                      <a href="http://www.ozforex.com.au/personal/travel-card" target="_blank">
                       high USD/AUD exchange rate
                      </a>
                      right now.
                      <a href="http://www.ozforex.com.au/personal/travel-card" target="_blank">
