       <div class="entry-header">
        <h2 class="entry-title">
         The Renovation And Extension Of An 18th Century House To Include An Office
        </h2>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <a href="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/" rel="bookmark" title="3:29 am">
         <time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-05-26T03:29:46+00:00">
          May 26, 2015
         </time>
        </a>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="The Renovation And Extension Of An 18th Century House To Include An Office" data-frizzlyposturl="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/" type="hidden" value=""/>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/" rel="attachment wp-att-115635">
          <img alt="Kirchplatz Office + Residence by Oppenheim Architecture + Design" class="alignnone wp-image-115635 size-full" height="533" src="http://www.contemporist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/contemporary-design_250515_17.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span id="more-115621">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #ffffff;">
          .
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.oppenoffice.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.oppenoffice.com', '\nOppenheim Architecture + Design');" target="_blank">
          <br/>
          Oppenheim Architecture + Design
         </a>
         have completedthe renovation and extension of a farmhouse, built in 1743 in Muttenz, Switzerland, to include an office and private residence.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #ffffff;">
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/contemporary-design_250515_02/" rel="attachment wp-att-115623">
          <img alt="Kirchplatz Office + Residence by Oppenheim Architecture + Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115623" height="533" src="http://www.contemporist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/contemporary-design_250515_02.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         The architects description
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          The design of this adaptive re-use project was born initially out of a design competition initiated by the City of Muttenz/Basel. The design was based on the renovation of an historic farmhouse situated within the historic center core of the city. The original farmhouse was constructed in 1743.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/contemporary-design_250515_04/" rel="attachment wp-att-115625">
          <img alt="Kirchplatz Office + Residence by Oppenheim Architecture + Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115625" height="1200" src="http://www.contemporist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/contemporary-design_250515_04.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Today the converted farmhouse serves as an office for an architectural design company, provides community meeting space, and serves as a compelling link to a new, adjacent private residence.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/contemporary-design_250515_06/" rel="attachment wp-att-115627">
          <img alt="Kirchplatz Office + Residence by Oppenheim Architecture + Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115627" height="533" src="http://www.contemporist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/contemporary-design_250515_06.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          The new design aimed to provide a fresh interpretation to the existing traditional features of the historic farmhouse building and its interior.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.contemporist.com/2015/05/26/the-renovation-and-extension-of-an-18th-century-house-to-include-an-office/contemporary-design_250515_09/" rel="attachment wp-att-115651">
          <img alt="Kirchplatz Office + Residence by Oppenheim Architecture + Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115651" height="533" src="http://www.contemporist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/contemporary-design_250515_09.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          This is achieved by creating new openings for natural daylight and by using a crisp white finish in the interiors, which juxtapose against the texture of the old wood and through the way in which the spaces open up, overlap, and merge together with one another.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
