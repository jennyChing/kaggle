     </time>
     <span class="author">
      by Jones Oil
     </span>
     <div class="linksalpha-email-button" data-desc="Autumn has arrived, meaning social media is awash with status updates from disgruntled people who wish it was still summer, can't get warm and are sick of the rain already.      <p>
      Autumn has arrived, meaning social media is awash with status updates from disgruntled people who wish it was still summer, cant get warm and are sick of the rain already. The sudden drop in temperature might tempt you to stay indoors, but head outside and youll find stunning scenery. Autumn is, after all, one of the most beautiful seasons of the year. Weve compiled a gorgeous gallery of some of autumns most impressive aesthetic treats to inspire you. Now: wrap up warm, head outside and see the limited edition beauty before its gone. Then come home to a cosy house, comfy sofa and warming food. Bliss!
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong style="line-height: 1.6em;">
       1. Climbing the Walls
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="384" src="http://perfect-nature.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/autumn-is-awesome-photos6.jpg" width="576"/>
      <a href="http://perfect-nature.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/autumn-is-awesome-photos6.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://perfect-nature.com']);" rel="nofollow" style="line-height: 1.6em;">
       Image source
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      This cottage in Wales looks stunning, enveloped as it is in beautiful climbing plants.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong style="line-height: 1.6em;">
       2. Pretty Portmeirion
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="433" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lHHO1UG8YFI/UJaMeAapF_I/AAAAAAAAA38/gxJjZuhRclw/s1600/DSCN0023.JPG" width="577"/>
      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lHHO1UG8YFI/UJaMeAapF_I/AAAAAAAAA38/gxJjZuhRclw/s1600/DSCN0023.JPG" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://1.bp.blogspot.com']);" rel="nofollow">
       Image source
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      You could be forgiven for thinking this breathtaking photograph was taken in the Far East, but its Wales, again. This is the Chinese Lake, tucked in the woodland at picturesque coastal village, Portmeirion.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong style="line-height: 1.6em;">
       3. Work of Art
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <img alt="" height="382" src="http://kindadukish.files.wordpress.com/2013/11/dsc_0152.jpg?w=640&amp;h=423" width="576"/>
      <br/>
      <a href="http://kindadukish.files.wordpress.com/2013/11/dsc_0152.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://kindadukish.files.wordpress.com']);" rel="nofollow">
       Image source
      </a>
