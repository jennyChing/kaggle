  <article class="post-4046 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-recipes">
   <div class="hero">
    <img alt="" class="hero-img" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pouring-juice-smoothies-in-glasses-with-lime-and-mint-hero-e1428104306865.jpg"/>
    <header class="header-single container has-img">
     <h1 class="entry-title">
      4 Easy &amp; Fresh Smoothie Ideas
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2015-04-06T00:38:36+00:00">
       April 6, 2015
      </time>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         Getting a healthy breakfast together on the fly or a quick lunch between errands isnt always easy. But weve got a solution: a bright, healthy smoothie. They go together quickly in a blender and provide a way to sneak fruit and veggies into your pickiest eaters. Best of all, they feel like a real treat rather than a hasty meal time compromise. If you havent been exercising your blender regularly, here are a few bright, colorful choices to get you started.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="chopped fruiton cutting board with kitchen knife" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4047" height="667" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chopped-fruiton-cutting-board-with-kitchen-knife-e1428104054953.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Berry Picking
          </span>
         </b>
         <b>
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Nothing adds eye appeal to a smoothie as quickly as bright, colorful berries. If you load your freezer with them when theyre in season or on sale, youll always be ready to conjure up a quick breakfast from the blender. Frozen berries can actually be better in a smoothie than fresh, because you dont need to dilute the mixture with added ice. Mix up a kid-friendly blueberry-pomegranate smoothie. Your youngsters wont care that its loaded with antioxidants, but theyll love its gloriously purple color. Remember to put on an apron before you start to protect your clothing against splashes and stains.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="fresh juice smoothies with mixed berries in mason jar glass" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4049" height="667" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/fresh-juice-smoothies-with-mixed-berries-in-mason-jar-glass-e1428104016549.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Break up a medium banana  like the berries, its even better if its frozen  and drop it into your blender. Add a half-cup each of pomegranate juice and whole blueberries and buzz them in your blender until theyre mixed. Add a cup of plain or vanilla yogurt and process it until the mix is smooth. If you go with plain yogurt the smoothie will be pretty tangy, so you might want to add a spoonful of honey. If your fruit isnt frozen, add a handful of ice as needed.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="mixed berries and sugar in bowl" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4050" height="667" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/mixed-berries-and-sugar-in-bowl-e1428103991540.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Divide the smoothie among sturdy
         <a href="http://www.personalcreations.com/mason-jars-psemasn" target="_blank">
          Mason drinking jars
         </a>
         and hand them out to the clamoring kids. If you have an oversize blender, you can make larger batches in the same proportions. Otherwise, if you need more portions, just make multiple batches.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="fresh juice smoothie with mixed berries" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4048" height="619" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/fresh-juice-smoothie-with-mixed-berries-e1428103969949.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           A Glass of Sunshine
          </span>
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Purples a good breakfast color for kids, but as a grown-up its a lot easier to face something with a warmer, sunnier color. Something tropical, maybe, with lots of bright flavors. Youll want roughly 2 cups of golden-yellow tropical fruits, either fresh or frozen. Choose from sweet pineapple, diced mango, orange segments or really ripe persimmons  whatever is available. Chop the fruit coarsely on your favorite cutting board, and youre ready to begin.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pouring juice into pitcher carafe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4052" height="667" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pouring-juice-into-pitcher-carafe-e1428103924460.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The orange juice version is more virtuous and has a deeper, warmer color. Whichever one you choose, divide the smoothie among tall, elegant drinking glasses and enjoy the liquid sunshine.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pouring juice into glasses from pitcher carafe" class="aligncenter wp-image-4051" height="690" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pouring-juice-into-glasses-from-pitcher-carafe-e1428103862306.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Going Green
          </span>
         </b>
         <b>
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Green smoothies are typically a love em or hate em proposition. If youre a booster, you love the option of incorporating healthy green vegetables into a breakfast beverage. If youre among the unpersuaded, the notion of drinking something green at breakfast might be a difficult pill to swallow. You might find the notion easier to wrap your mind around if you use lime juice as your main flavoring. Lime-flavored foods are often green, so its a good way to trick your brain and tummy into going the healthy route.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pouring juice smoothies in glasses with lime and mint" class="aligncenter wp-image-4054" height="667" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/pouring-juice-smoothies-in-glasses-with-lime-and-mint-e1428103784160.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Break up a medium-sized banana into your blender, along with 2 cups of mild-tasting baby spinach. Add the juice of a lime and a cup of coconut water or half coconut water and half coconut milk. Whiz them together in your blender, with or without added ice, until the mixture is smooth. If you want to make it even heartier and healthier, theres plenty of wiggle room for add-ins. A handful of quick oats adds fiber and makes your smoothie more filling, for example. For protein, add a tablespoon or two of your favorite protein powder or replace part of the coconut milk with Greek-style yogurt. Pour the smoothie into a tall Pilsner glass, where its foaming surface will look right at home.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="green smoothies in glasses with lime" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4053" height="722" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/green-smoothies-in-glasses-with-lime-e1428103691547.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Sip Some Feel Good
          </span>
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Sometimes you might need your smoothie to offer more than a quick pick-me-up. If youre feeling poorly, whip up a blender full of cold, tasty therapy. A cranberry-orange smoothie fits the bill perfectly. Cranberries are loaded with vitamin C, and gingers good for settling your stomach and reducing inflammation. When you put them together, along with a bit of honey to soothe your throat and offset the tartness of the berries, youve got a clear winner.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="juice smoothies in glasses with sandwiches" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4055" height="613" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/juice-smoothies-in-glasses-with-sandwiches-e1428103651702.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Break up a frozen banana into your blender, along with 1/2 pound of fresh or frozen cranberries and half an orange. Add 1 or 2 tablespoons of shredded ginger, 2 or 3 of honey and 1/2 cup of yogurt or coconut milk  probiotic yogurt might be helpful, if youre really unwell  and process the mixture in your blender until its smooth. If you dont have a frozen banana on hand and arent using frozen berries, feel free to add a handful of crushed ice. Its all good. Pour the tangy, comforting drink into a stemless
         <a href="http://www.personalcreations.com/personalized-wine-glasses-psewgls" target="_blank">
          wine glass
         </a>
         , just to make it a little more special. Youll need all the positive reinforcement you can get with a nasty bug dragging you down.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           A Few Quick Tips
          </span>
         </b>
         <b>
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         One thing to remember when youre making smoothies is that theyre cold, and that means moisture will probably condense on the outside of the glass and drip onto your table. Its best to set your glasses on coasters to protect the furnitures finish. When youre serving kids, set down the coaster with a flourish and make a production of it. They might not care about the furniture, but they do love feeling special. A well-chosen garnish or two can help, as well. Just keep back a piece or two of the fruit youve used in the smoothie and use the fruit to decorate the rim. If youve made a smoothie with berries, thread a few of them onto a toothpick or cocktail pick and balance it on the rim.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="juice smoothies in glasses on tray" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4056" height="667" src="http://www.personalcreations.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/juice-smoothies-in-glasses-on-tray-e1428103530347.jpg" width="1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now that weve talked about some ways to work out your blender, tell us, what are your favorite healthy, kid-friendly smoothie combinations?
        </p>
       </div>
