       <p>
        Finally its time for a drywall inspection
        <a href="http://oahu-handyman.com/Drywall-Plastering-Honolulu/Custom-Textures/index.html" target="_blank">
         ceilings
        </a>
        need a seamless touch up screws and Sheetrock are separating at all joints on rafters 24 inches from one end to the next.
       </p>
       <p>
        in the early 1960s and 70s homebuilders were accustomed to different types of interior installation products, moreover it wasnt until the early 1970s
        <em>
         <strong>
          general construction contractors
         </strong>
        </em>
        started installing sheet rock or as some would call it drywall in all interior walls as far as insulation was concerned
        <strong>
         <em>
          drywall/sheet
         </em>
        </strong>
        rock up until today is probably your best selection for installation materials.
       </p>
       <p>
        because sheet rockers used a different method when hanging drywall especially in single panel homes, it was done differently, vice versa in todays technology
        <a href="http://oahu-handyman.com/Drywall-Plastering-Honolulu/Custom-Textures/index.html" target="_blank">
         drywall installers
        </a>
        Honolulu Oahu use 1 5/8 screws in between all interior wall studs in other words every 16 inches you install five screws from top to bottom, usually a sheet of drywall is about 8 feet in length and 4 feet and with leaving five uprights that need to be screwed down, in between the 4 x 8 sheet rock material you have to make sure you install at least five screws 8 inches apart inside every stud that will guarantee your ceilings and rafters wont shake loose the sheet rock over the next 10 to 15 years or so.
       </p>
       <p>
        as a homeowner, for example lets say you just bought an older home that was built in the 70s, suddenly you take a look at the ceiling and you discovered dimples every 8 inches apart, this is a direct indication drywall is in the 1970s used nails to hang drywall instead of screws the way your you are built
        <a href="http://oahu-handyman.com/Drywall-Repair-Service/index.html" target="_blank">
         drywall installation

       </p>
