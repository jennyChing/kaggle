              <p>
               <strong>
                Before any new episode of
Doctor Who we release a short synopsis which gives an idea of what will be
happening in the adventure
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               For Deep Breath, the official
synopsis reads, When the Doctor arrives in Victorian London he finds a
dinosaur rampant in the Thames and a spate of deadly spontaneous combustions.
Who is the new Doctor and will Claras friendship survive as they embark on a
terrifying mission into the heart of an alien conspiracy? The Doctor has
changed. Its time you knew him.
              </p>
              <p>
               Of course, youll be able to watch
the whole thing when Doctor Who returns to BBC One on Saturday, 23 August, or
you can visit a
               <a href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/galleries/p023y0sw">
                gallery of images for the episode
               </a>
               now!
              </p>
