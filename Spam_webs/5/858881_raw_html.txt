          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Since beginning of Telugu cinema, comedy is an important part and most of the Telugu directors used different comedy track in films. When it comes to Rajendra Prasad time, most of his films are based on comedy and audiences used to watch them without fail and without considering hit or flop concept.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Days are passed by Allari Naresh has occupied the place of Rajendra Prasad and entertaining audiences for last one decade. But now audiences are watching comedy films with gripping story. If the film does not have enough content, audiences are simply ignoring them. That is the reason why he has been facing repeated flops like Yamudiki Mogudu, Action 3D, Kevvu Keka, Laddu Babu and Jump Jilani.
