                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     5 reasons why a dog is like a kid!
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3684767073496764617" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      Some of you may be surprised to hear it, because I don't talk about her very often, but I have a daughter! She's our first baby, almost 8 1/2 years old, 15 lbs and SUCH a cutie!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      Say HI to Honey, our Havanese! ;)
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-2EfvgTfWak0/VOvYHsNArAI/AAAAAAAAGFs/3sqmeMQrE1M/s1600/Untitled%2Bdesign%2B(1).png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-2EfvgTfWak0/VOvYHsNArAI/AAAAAAAAGFs/3sqmeMQrE1M/s1600/Untitled%2Bdesign%2B(1).png" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      She is 100% part of the family, totally my husband's girlfriend and the laziest love bug you've ever seen! She sleeps most of the day away (on a
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.boppy.com/" target="_blank">
                        Boppy pillow
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      that was a baby gift when my 5 year old was born - she snagged it during week 1! lol).....and then the night too! She loves dirty laundry, scrambled eggs &amp; playing around (if she's not sleeping!)!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      My kids LOVE animals and I feel like it is such a gift for them to grow up with a pet (we have a fish too but I won't bore you with those details!) - they just love her &amp; she loves them!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      So I call Honey my daughter because she is like having another kid....
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-hkQvfIPgVkM/VOvY-CtBJeI/AAAAAAAAGF0/uYjiWlLNv1A/s1600/Add%2Bsubtitle%2Btext.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-hkQvfIPgVkM/VOvY-CtBJeI/AAAAAAAAGF0/uYjiWlLNv1A/s1600/Add%2Bsubtitle%2Btext.png" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        1. They sleep in your bed.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      I'm lucky that my kids have
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        no
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      desire to even hang out in my bed, but boy does my dog make up for it! I think that she thinks it is HER bed! And for a 15-pound small-sized dog she takes up a WHOLE lotta space!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        2. They want a bite of everything you're eating.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      They just wait around - by your feet - just praying that something - anything - will drop! (How many have you have hidden in the bathroom or closet so you didn't have to share with your kid (or dog)?!?) What's yours is theirs, right?!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-1zAtxOIEXEA/VOvaU5yZIOI/AAAAAAAAGF8/bDcDvfuDKss/s1600/Untitled%2Bdesign%2B(2).png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-1zAtxOIEXEA/VOvaU5yZIOI/AAAAAAAAGF8/bDcDvfuDKss/s1600/Untitled%2Bdesign%2B(2).png" width="400"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                         The night we got her - she was 8 weeks old!!!
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <b style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <i>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        3. You can't leave them home alone overnight.
                       </span>
                      </i>
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      Going on a trip? You have
                      <strike>
                       something
                      </strike>
                      someone else to worry about...who's watching your pup? Regular walks, meals, play time - they need a babysitter, too!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        4. Even if they're potty trained they have the occasional accident.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      Kids, dogs, it's all the same.... Do you ever feel like you're ALWAYS dealing with poop, or is it just me?!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-aUMkNZS-DVc/VOvhCQLA83I/AAAAAAAAGGM/WU6bdOP_Vk8/s1600/Untitled%2Bdesign%2B(3).png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-aUMkNZS-DVc/VOvhCQLA83I/AAAAAAAAGGM/WU6bdOP_Vk8/s1600/Untitled%2Bdesign%2B(3).png" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        5. They give you unconditional love!
                       </i>
                      </b>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      And how?! They LOVE you always and always have your back - that applies to kids &amp; dogs! You're their hero, their protector, their whole world!!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      Do you have a dog? Are they a part of your family or "just a pet"?
                     </span>
