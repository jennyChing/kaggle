          <a href="http://somethingturquoise.com/2015/01/15/glitter-wedding-ideas/">
           Defined Details | You Can Never Have Too Much Glitter
          </a>
         </h2>
        </div>
        <hr/>
        <i>
         <img alt="diamond" src="/assets/images/icons-post/diamond.jpg"/>

       <div class="wp-content">
        <p>
         <strong>
          glitter
         </strong>
         |glitr| noun. 1. bright, shimmering, reflected light:
         <em>
          the blue glitter of the sea
         </em>
         . 2. a glint in a persons eye indicating a particular emotion:
         <em>
          the scathing glitter in his eyes
         </em>
         . 3. tiny pieces of sparkling material used for decoration:
         <em>
          heels trimmed with sequins and glitter.
         </em>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         Many brides are a little glitter crazy, so glitter happens to be the theme of todays fresh new article that we are calling
         <em>
          defined details
         </em>
         . Each month Ill be breaking down specifically themed detail ideas for weddings with ultra-pretty pictures! There is something so glamourous and romantic about glitter especially when its done right, so here are 9 fab ideas on how you can incorporate
         <strong>
          glitter
         </strong>
         into your modern wedding.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Defined Details -- Glitter Wedding Ideas" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-28831" height="856" src="http://somethingturquoise.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Defined-Details-Glitter-Wedding-Ideas.jpg" width="660"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Image Credits:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Glittered Wedding Heels:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://ligaphotography.com/">
           Liga Photography
          </a>
          via
          <a href="http://www.weddingchicks.com/2014/12/08/peach-and-gold-plantation-wedding/">
           Wedding Chicks
          </a>
          . Purchasing glitter heels or getting crafty and adding glitter to your wedding shoes is a very good idea! Youll feel like Dorothy only better. Want to make your own? Check out our tutorial
          <a href="http://somethingturquoise.com/2013/10/04/diy-glitter-high-heels/">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Bride and Groom portrait in glitter:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://ourlaboroflove.com/">
           Our Labor of Love
          </a>
          via
          <a href="http://ruffledblog.com/black-tie-st-augustine-wedding/">
           Ruffled
          </a>
          . Wow. This is for the hardcore glitter lover. Stunning but lots of work.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Glitter Wedding Cake:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.cottonandcrumbs.co.uk/">
           Cotton and Crumbs
          </a>
          . Whether it be large chunky glitter or darling little sparkles, glitter wedding cakes are a crazy huge trend right now and a terrific way to glam up your dessert!
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Glitter Table Numbers:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://hunterryanphoto.com/">
           Hunter Ryan Photo
          </a>
          via
          <a href="http://www.artfullywed.com/real-couples/weddings/aqua-pink-gold-glitter-diy-wedding/">
           Artfully Wed
          </a>
          . Buy almost any type youd like from
          <a href="https://www.etsy.com/search?q=glitter%20table%20numbers">
           Etsy
          </a>
          or make your own, glitter table numbers are a fun way to add sparkle to all your tables. A fairly simple project since you only have to make 10-20 and all you need is decoupage and glue. Make sure to seal with two coats of decoupage glue so that you dont loose a sparkle!
         </li>
