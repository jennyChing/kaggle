          <p>
           <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Trends-to-stop-in-2015-Stop-the-Madness.jpg">
            <img alt="A very angry aggressive woman is clenching her fists in rage" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11118" height="400" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Trends-to-stop-in-2015-Stop-the-Madness.jpg" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The year 2015 is almost here and with it, 2014 will be gone. There are several trends that have crept up over the last year that we really dont need to see more of in 2015. Its a fresh, new year and its time to put a few things to rest and see them no more.
          </p>
          <p>
           Check out this list of 5 trends I dont want to see in 2015.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_11119" style="width: 640px">
           <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/No-More-Butts-e1419867964807.jpg">
            <img alt="no more butt pics on the internet" class="wp-image-11119 size-large" height="734" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/No-More-Butts-879x1024.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            You didnt think I was going to show a butt pic did you?
           </p>
          </div>
          <h3>
           Butt Pictures
          </h3>
          <p>
           Kim Kardashian didnt really break the Internet with pictures of her butt on the cover of a magazine. After she posed with her posterior exposed, a number of mimic pics were floating around the Internet. We dont need to see any more. Butt pictures are never a good trend and what kind of message does it send to the youth of America?
          </p>
          <p>
           Either way, Kardashians rear end sets a dangerous precedent: Will all celebrities start turning to show us their backside? Its certain to backfire (no pun intended, maybe) when we start seeing posed pictures of hairy man butts peeking at us everywhere we turn.
          </p>
          <p>
           Nobody needs to see that, so please stop.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/No-More-Twerking.jpg">
            <img alt="Miley Cyrus stop twerking" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11120" height="412" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/No-More-Twerking.jpg" width="600"/>
           </a>
           Twerking
          </h3>
          <p>
           After Miley Cyrus twerked her scrawny back side in front of the world, it became a thing. Not really sure what kind of a thing, but it became a thing. Now everyone who has a butt thinks they can and must twerk. If theres a camera around and a YouTube account handy then its even better. How about lets not see this as a trend in 2015? Can I get an, Amen?
          </p>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Stop-Crowd-Shooting-e1419869516685.jpg">
            <img alt="Anonymous crowd walking on a street in New York" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11121" height="420" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Stop-Crowd-Shooting-1024x683.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           Crowd Shootings
          </h3>
          <p>
           Shootings at malls, schools, shopping centers, restaurants, streets, and more, have become too much of a common trend in recent years. Pick up any newspaper or check out any online news source on any given day and youre bound to find a news piece about someone who decided to take out a crowd of people for some insane reason. Lets not see any of these things in 2015. Realistic? Probably not. Worth hoping for? You bet.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Stop-Rioting-e1419869698814.jpg">
            <img alt="trends of 2014 stop riot rioting" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11122" height="461" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Stop-Rioting-1024x750.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           Riots
          </h3>
          <p>
           Perhaps 2014 will be known as the year of the riots. Too many cities and towns have seen riots and unrest, violence and looting. Entire neighborhoods have been burned down and destroyed as people have tried to make a statement. What statement? Many probably dont even know. How about in 2015, instead of rioting and destroying things, lets find way to put our time to good use. Lets rebuild things and spend time helping others.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Selfies-trend-2014.jpg">
            <img alt="Selfies trend" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11123" height="448" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Selfies-trend-2014-1024x728.jpg" width="630"/>
           </a>
           Selfies
          </h3>
          <p>
           Selfies may be a trend that just wont die, but please, can we stop in 2015? Sure, its fine to take a selfie once in awhile when you want to show off your new hairstyle, but the selfie-a-minute attitude that has become to prevalent in our social media-driven society has just got to stop. Overuse of selfies could be a sign of the rampant narcissism that has become the unfortunate norm for today.
          </p>
          <p>
           There are plenty of other trends that need to end in 2015. At least for these five, lets give it a rest.
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            What trend do you want to see stop in 2015?
           </strong>
          </h3>
