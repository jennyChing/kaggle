           The Real Cost Of Doc Brown's Mansion
          </h1>
         </header>
         <div class="article__content">
          <div class="widget__wrapper js-widget js-widget--desktop js-widget--tablet js-widget--mobile visible--desktop visible--tablet visible--mobile" data-widget-id="0">

             <em>
              Back To The Future
             </em>
             fan would want to live in a place like this, but just how much money would it set you back?

            </p>

             Take a look below and find out if it's in your price range.
