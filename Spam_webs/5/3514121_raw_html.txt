       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masque
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-26T07:54:09+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 26, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.beautybymissl.com/author/leelor" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Leelo
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.beautybymissl.com/2015/02/macadamia-natural-oil-deep-repair-masque.html#comments">
           4 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masqueis something that many love and I have heard so many great things about it. And so when Hairtrade gave me an opportunity to try it then of course I said YES! 
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8287" style="width: 660px">
         <a href="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_00041.jpg">
          <img alt="Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masque" class="wp-image-8287 size-full" height="529" src="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_00041.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          PR sample; All opinions are my own
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Untitled.png">
          <img alt="Untitled" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7950" height="40" src="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Untitled.png" width="71"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masqueis described as a revitalizing hair reconstructor that will penetrate and rebuild damaged hair for improved shine and elasticity. A combination ofMacadamia and Argan oil interact with tea tree oil, chamomile oil, aloe and algae extracts rejuvenate and rebuild the hair, leaving it deeply nourished for improved elasticity and shine with long-lasting conditioning benefits. This hair masks paraben free formula is promised to deeply nourish, condition and reconstruct hair.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_0009.jpg">
          <img alt="Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masque" class="aligncenter wp-image-8288 size-full" height="433" src="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DSC_0009.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         My opinion ofMacadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masque
        </h2>
        <p>
         Since I have used this hair mask only for two weeks (4 times) then I cant give you very thorough review, but I can share with you my first impressions of this mask (but dont worry Ill update this post once the jar is empty  ). So this hair mask comes in a plastic jar that has very unique and eye-catching design. The mask itself is nice, thick and creamy and smells wonderful. It is easy to apply this hair mask to my hair, but rinsing it out takes bit more time. After I have applied the mask on to my hair, it makes my hair instantly softer. This hair mask reminds me of those hair treatments that come with LOreal and some Garnier hair dyes  you know the ones that just do their magic and make your just-color-treated and somewhat damaged hair nice and soft again
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.beautybymissl.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.hairtrade.com/macadamia-deep-repair-masque-500ml-p-4438.html" target="_blank">
           Macadamia Deep Repair Masque
          </a>
         </strong>
         makes my hair so incredibly soft and easily manageable  I have no problems with tangles and my hair is shiny. When I used this hair mask for the first time then I just couldnt stop myself touching my hair all the time, because they just felt so soft.
