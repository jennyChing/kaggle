             <a href="http://www.everythingfab.com/2011/04/hair-inspiration-half-and-half-how-to.html">
              Hair Inspiration: The Half and Half ~ How To
             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="post-header-line-1">
            </div>
            <div class="post-body" id="post-88013650013882670">
             <style>
              #fullpost{display:inline;}
             </style>
             <p>
              <a href="http://s1198.photobucket.com/albums/aa454/everythingfab1/?action=view&amp;current=387fa8e1.jpg" target="_blank">
               <img alt="Photobucket" border="0" src="http://i1198.photobucket.com/albums/aa454/everythingfab1/387fa8e1.jpg"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              ... inspired by the very sexy Brigitte Bardot, this half updo is all about old Hollywood glamour. Perfect look for when you are feeling romantic...! Great for a party but I also like balancing a super relax look of jeans and a simple t-shirt with a more polished hair look!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://s1198.photobucket.com/albums/aa454/everythingfab1/?action=view&amp;current=4502fe2f.jpg" target="_blank">
               <img alt="Photobucket" border="0" src="http://i1198.photobucket.com/albums/aa454/everythingfab1/4502fe2f.jpg"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              1. Using a large-barrel curling iron, create loose curls around your head from your ears down.
              <br/>
              2. Part your hair in the middle. Use your fingers to separate and loosen the curls.
              <br/>
              3. Starting halfway down the part, begin spritzing your roots with hairspray. Continue to the crown.
              <br/>
              4. Tease the sprayed hair with a fine-tooth comb, to create height and volume.
              <br/>
              5. With a brush gently smooth the teased hair. Gather the sides up into a half ponytail.
              <br/>
              6. Secure the sides with pins, then use your fingers to gently shape curls into place.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://s1198.photobucket.com/albums/aa454/everythingfab1/?action=view&amp;current=a5cf4d34.jpg" target="_blank">
               <img alt="Photobucket" border="0" src="http://i1198.photobucket.com/albums/aa454/everythingfab1/a5cf4d34.jpg"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              ...put some shine spray and voila...you are ready to go!
