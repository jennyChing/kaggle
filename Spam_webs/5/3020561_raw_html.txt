   MG 00Q The Amazing QANTA Ver.Zack Project by Syahlan Razack
  </h2>
  <p>
   <em>
    From the modeler
   </em>
  </p>
  <blockquote>
   <p>
    <em>
     I just want to share my latest work from the OOseries. At first, there is no plan in kitbashing this kit, I just suddenly thought of doing so. To build this kit, I used two (2) MG OO Qanta and LBX Achilles, I added some panel lines and pla plates in creating this. As for the colour scheme, it was inspired from the Amazing Exia colour scheme.
    </em>
