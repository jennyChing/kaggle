          <a href="http://safehouse.com.ph/services/">
           <font size="3">
            <b>
             Warehouse for Lease
            </b>
           </font>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:justify">
           You dont just answer to
           <a href="http://safehouse.com.ph/services/">
            warehouse for lease
           </a>
           ads out of the blue. Usually, these huge and spacious structures function as
           <b>
            storage rooms
           </b>
           . Some convert it to a showroom, gym, shopping centre, office, and other business establishments. In other countries, they use it for residential purposes. With a
           <b>
            warehouse
           </b>
           , all you have to do is simply plan an outline, create divisions, and transform the place the way it must function.
          </div>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:center">
           <a href="http://safehouse.com.ph/services/">
            <img border="0" minion_name="user_content_image" url="http://safehouse.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Banner1_Revised.jpg"/>
            <link href="http://safehouse.com.ph/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Banner1_Revised.jpg" rel="image_src"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:center">
           Photo from Safehouse Storage Philippines
          </div>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:justify">
           The need for a
           <b>
            storage room
           </b>
           arises when a business realizes that the demand of their customers grows. This is evident if the business is a supplier of a particular product or merchandise. However, bear in mind that you cant just store your supplies inside on a whim. A detailed and strategic plan is necessary. Should this plan include an implementation of a
           <b>
            Warehouse Management System
           </b>
           (WMS)?
          </div>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:justify">
           In this era of modernization, manual labor is slightly more a thing of the past (albeit not completely). This results to an automated and paperless execution of business operations. In a
           <b>
            warehouse setting
           </b>
           , a WMS is a software application that supports the day-to-day operations in a
           <b>
            warehouse
           </b>
           , according to TechTarget. Imagine searching a tiny parcel in a sea of large boxes by foot. This method is a tedious and time-consuming process that will greatly affect the business. WMS is a tool that simplifies and organizes the inventory and delivery of goods.
          </div>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:justify">
           It is advisable that it must be part of the planning stage already if you decided to use such application. The designing of the
           <b>
            warehouse
           </b>
           layout must be coordinated with the programming of the WMS.  If not, it defeats the purpose of the WMS as an instrument of
           <b>
            centralization of warehouse
           </b>
           activities or operations. WMS software could be one of the components of an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system or just a stand-alone application.
          </div>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:justify">
           Today, most WMS use a sophisticated technology to locate a package easily without browsing the entire record or walking through a maze of racks. One of the novel examples is the RFID or radio frequency identification. Through its antennae, it transmits radio frequency signal to find the item, which has a transponder attached to it, and the transponder returns a reply to the antennae.
          </div>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <div style="text-align:justify">
           Using such high-end system, it would greatly help the growth of the companys profit. Aside from that, it improves the handling of
           <b>
            inventory of goods
           </b>
           , provides real-time record-keeping, faster turnaround time in terms of delivery of goods and improves labor productivity. With the right staff, incorporating a WMS application would be an everyday lifesaver.
          </div>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          For more information on warehousing and storage industry, check out
          <a href="http://safehouse.com.ph/">
           safehouse.com.ph
          </a>
