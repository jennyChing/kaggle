      <h3 class="title-post">
       This Teen Is Getting Suspended For Earning Almost $22k From His Side Business Between Classes
      </h3>
      <div class="header-bottom">
      </div>
      <!-- header-bottom -->
     </header>
     <div class="entry-content clearfix">
      <div style="text-align:center;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;margin-top:8px;margin-bottom:8px;">
       <img src="http://capitalismisfreedom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/bar2.jpg"/>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align:center;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;margin-top:8px;margin-bottom:8px;">
       <!-- /169723673/topbanner -->
       <div id="div-gpt-ad-1432056361744-0">

      <p>
       <a href="http://capitalismisfreedom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/kid-sells-candy-01.jpg">
        <img alt="kid sells candy 01" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27336" height="334" src="http://capitalismisfreedom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/kid-sells-candy-01.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Tommie Rose is the back-alley Willy Wonka of his high school.Rose,15, has been threatened with suspension for
       <a href="http://www.mirror.co.uk/news/uk-news/schoolboy-15-threatened-suspension-raking-4665390" target="_blank">
        buying candy and soda in bulk
       </a>
       , and selling it to his classmates at a high markup during free periods.So far, the Salford, England boy has earned nearly $22,000 dollars.
      </p>
      <p>
       After years of watching competitive business television shows like The Apprentice, Rose began considering ways to start his own operation.
      </p>
      <p>
       Three years ago, he sold his first piece of candy and began building a business model.The young entrepreneur hopes to make enough money to pay fora business degree from a top university.
      </p>
      <p>
       Since the beginning of Roses business, hes hired two school-age employees, whom he pays about $8.50 each day.However, Roses teachers have never been impressed with his business acumen.
      </p>
      <div style="text-align:center;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;margin-top:8px;margin-bottom:8px;">

        (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
       </script>
      </div>
      <p>
       Roses previous school,Oasis Academy, suspended him for 10 days in 2011 for the black market trades. His current institution,Buile Hill High School, is threatening to do the same.
      </p>
      <p>
       James Inman, the schools headteacher, says Buile Hill has a very strict healthy eating policy thats promised to parents when they enroll their children.Rose is ruiningit by selling candy between classes.
      </p>
      <p>
       He continued,
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        We admire this pupils entrepreneurship but school is not the place to set-up a black market of fizzy drinks, sweets and chocolates.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       But, Roses parents, Gary and Tracy, dont see anything wrong with their intelligent son trying to start his career a little early.
      </p>
