           </a>

               </li>

             </div>
            </li>

Read carefully when filling out any form to make sure you're not agreeing to receive catalogs.

         <div class="section tips sticky ">
          <h2>
           <span class="mw-headline" id="Tips">
            Tips
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="section_text" id="tips">
           <ul>
            <li>
             When calling, don't be rude to the person who answers the phone, even if you're annoyed about receiving something. He or she is paid to answer the phone and is not personally responsible for your receiving it. But do, if necessary,
             <a href="/Be-Persistent" title="Be Persistent">
              be persistent
             </a>
             , in addition to
             <a href="/Be-Kind" title="Be Kind">
              being kind
             </a>
             .
            </li>
            <li>
             Keep a recycling bin or trash can by your mailbox or wherever you sort mail, so that you can immediately recycle any junk you do receive.
            </li>
            <li>
             When all else fails, recycle the junk. If you have a shredder, anything printed with black and white ink is usually safe for the compost heap. You can also use them as kindling in your fireplace. If you're inclined toward crafts, you could also
             <a href="/Make-Paper" title="Make Paper">
              make paper
             </a>
             or use your junk mail in
             <a href="/Make-a-Collage" title="Make a Collage">
              collage
             </a>
             or
             <a href="/Decoupage" title="Decoupage">
              decoupage
             </a>
             .
            </li>
            <li>
             Sending junk mail back in the reply envelope may feel good, but it is not a particularly effective way to get off mailing lists. It is better simply to request to be removed.
            </li>
            <li>
             Use an electronic calendar to set renewal reminders. If you learn that your removal request expires after a period of time, place a reminder to renew it in your calendar now. Most electronic and online calendars include reminder functions and can take dates far into the future.
            </li>
           </ul>
