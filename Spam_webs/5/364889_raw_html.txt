         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Last Minute DIY Vampire Costume for Halloween
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="posted-on">
           Posted on
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/vampire-costume-for-halloween/" rel="bookmark">
            <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2014-10-30T09:23:18+00:00">
             30 October, 2014
            </time>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="byline">
           by
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/author/margot/">
             Margot
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="posted_in">
           in
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/category/occasions/halloween/">
            Halloween
           </a>
          </span>

        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="Halloween dress up DIY vampire accessories " class="alignnone wp-image-27344 size-full" height="602" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/halloween-2014-vampire-600.jpg" title="Last Minute DIY Vampire Outfit for Halloween" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          MATERIALS
         </h3>
         <ul>
          <li>
           any shiny dress (I got this Lipsy midnight blue silk dress with a missing belt for couple of pounds in charity shop)
          </li>
          <li>
           1 m of wide / black elastic with sequins (about 2 in our local fabric store)
          </li>
          <li>
           couple of red beads(on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           spider or other scary pendant(on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           buckle (we managed to find heart shaped one for 0.25in our local fabric store)
          </li>
          <li>
           vampire teeth (on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           black wig (on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           fake blood (from supermarket 1)
          </li>
          <li>
           dark eye shadow(on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           black eyebrow pencil(on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           black stockings and shoes (on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           black lace or button(on hand)
          </li>
          <li>
           black thread and needle(on hand)
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="vampire choker necklace with spider red black " class="alignnone wp-image-27345 size-full" height="400" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/vampire-halloween-outfit_0007-600.jpg" title="Last Minute Halloween Vampire Choker Spider Necklace" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          METHOD
         </h3>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1. For the choker necklace you will need piece of the elastic just a bit longer than fitting tightly around the neck.
          </li>
          <li>
           2. Secure cutting line with the thread and connect piece of lace on each end to be able to tie it with a bow, or attach button on one side and make loop on other.
          </li>
          <li>
           3. Decorate the necklace sewing on some beads and attaching the pendant.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="vampire bracelet red black Halloween scary" class="alignnone wp-image-27348 size-full" height="400" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/vampire-halloween-outfit_0009-600.jpg" title="Last Minute DIY Vampire Accessories for Halloween" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           4. Making the bracelet is the easiest part, all you need to do is to cut piece of elastic just long enough to go around the wrist and sew it together, it is stretchyso no need for any closure.
          </li>
          <li>
           5. Decorate with beads.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="fancy dress Halloween kids girls DIY belt red black" class="alignnone wp-image-27349 size-full" height="400" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/vampire-halloween-outfit_0013-600.jpg" title="Last Minute DIY Halloween Vampire  Fancy Dress Outfit for Girls" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           6. To make the belt use the rest of elastic, sew one side onto the buckle, then just secure the other end from shedding the sequins and voil, vampire outfit is almost ready.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h3>
          NOTES
         </h3>
         <p>
          To complete the look use dark eye shadow around the eyes, black pencil on the eye brows, some fake blood on the lips, black stockings and shoes, vampire fangs and scary wig.
         </p>
