        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Review | Insurgent by Veronica Roth
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2012-09-12T10:06:08+00:00">
          2012-09-12
         </span>
          by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://www.attackofthebooks.com/author/britt/" rel="author">
            Brittany
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://www.attackofthebooks.com/review-insurgent-by-veronica-roth/#comments">
           <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="597 http://www.attackofthebooks.com/?p=597">
            1 Comment
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p class="leftAlignedImage">
          <a href="http://www.goodreads.com/book/photo/11735983-insurgent" itemprop="image" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.goodreads.com/book/photo/11735983-insurgent', '');" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="Insurgent (Divergent, #2)" class="alignleft" data-recalc-dims="1" id="coverImage" src="http://i1.wp.com/d.gr-assets.com/books/1325667729l/11735983.jpg?resize=180%2C272"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="leftAlignedImage">
          <strong>
           <em>
            Synopsis from Goodreads:
           </em>
          </strong>
          <em>
           In this sequel to the bestseller
           <a href="http://www.attackofthebooks.com/review-divergent-by-veronica-roth/">
            Divergent
           </a>
           , war now looms as conflict between the factions and their ideologies grows. And in times of war, sides must be chosen, secrets emerge, and choices will become more irrevocableand even more powerful. Transformed by her decisions, haunted by grief and guilt from those decisions, radical new discoveries, and shifting relationships, Tris must fully embrace her Divergence, even if she does not know what she may lose by doing so.
          </em>
         </div>
         <div class="leftAlignedImage">
         </div>
         <div class="leftAlignedImage">
          Book two of the Divergent Series delivered just as book one (
          <em>
           Divergent
          </em>
          ) did. I loved it. It was fast-paced, and action packed. I really enjoyed getting an in-depth look at some of the factions we learned little about in book one. I also thought that Roth did a good job continuing the development of the characters we met in
          <em>
           Divergent
          </em>
          while also introducing us to new characters.
         </div>
         <div class="leftAlignedImage">
         </div>
         <div class="leftAlignedImage">
          I cant wait for book three in this series which does not come out until next year (2013).
          <em>
           Insurgent
          </em>
          has a cliff hanger ending. If you liked
          <em>
           Hunger Games
          </em>
          , youll probably really like this series as well.
         </div>
         <div class="leftAlignedImage">
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           _____________
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Overall Rating:
           </strong>
           <img alt="4 of 5 stars false" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/s.gr-assets.com/assets/layout/stars/red_star_4_of_5-5405838cef8c4750ec26237ed6595e7c.png?resize=75%2C15" title="4 of 5 stars, really liked it"/>
           1/2
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Parents guide
           </strong>
           : I would only give this book to mature teens and adults.
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Sex
            </strong>
            : kissing throughout the books, some sensuality, it is implied that a couple has sex
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Violence
            </strong>
            : quite a bit, teens using guns, people are shot and killed, drugs are used on people to make them hallucinate
            <strong>
             <br/>
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Adult Themes
            </strong>
            : teens get tattoos and drink alcohol
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
