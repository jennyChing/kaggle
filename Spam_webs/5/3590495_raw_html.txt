          <img alt="tumblr" class="synved-share-image synved-social-image synved-social-image-share" height="32" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/plugins/social-media-feather/synved-social/image/social/regular/64x64/tumblr.png" style="display: inline; width:32px;height:32px; margin: 0; padding: 0; border: none; box-shadow: none;" title="Share on tumblr" width="32"/>
         </a>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Keeping your dorm room clean will help you stay focused. It will also help you be more organized and keep your brain from feeling cluttered.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean1.jpg">
           <img alt="dorm room 1" class="aligncenter wp-image-8758 size-medium" height="200" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean1-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1. Make your bed in the morning- it takes 2 minutes and makes your room look a lot neater!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean2.jpg">
           <img alt="dorm room 2" class="aligncenter wp-image-8759 size-medium" height="300" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean2-294x300.jpg" width="294"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          2. Fold your laundry right away! Making sure you have time to fold and put away your laundry once its done is important for making sure your dorm is not filling up with dirty laundry and your dresser is not exploding.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean3.gif">
           <img alt="dorm room 3" class="aligncenter wp-image-8760 size-medium" height="300" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean3-280x300.gif" width="280"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          3. Take out your trash once a week- or as soon as it gets full, whichever comes first. This will not only keep your room neat, but nice smelling too!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean4.jpg">
           <img alt="dorm room 4" class="aligncenter wp-image-8761 size-medium" height="300" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean4-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          4. Do your dishes immediately after youre done eating in your room. Then put them away. This will also keep your dorm from smelling. (In this vein, also clean out your fridge once a week.)
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean5.jpg">
           <img alt="dorm room 5" class="aligncenter wp-image-8762 size-medium" height="225" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean5-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          5. If you need motivation- Invite members of the opposite gender to come hang out. Nothing will motivate you to make sure your dorm room is neat than a guy/girl coming over!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean6.jpg">
           <img alt="dorm room 6" class="aligncenter wp-image-8763 size-medium" height="200" src="http://blog.ecampus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/clean6-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
