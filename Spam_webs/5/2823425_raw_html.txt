         <h2 class="blog__title">
          How to Talk Like an Intellectual
         </h2>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="blog__content clearfix">
         <p>
          The reification of the natural is homologous with the engendering of the gendered body.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youve probably had that very thought, or one like it, but you couldnt find the words to express it. Be an intellectual wallflower no longer!
         </p>
         <p>
          With the University of Chicago Writing Programs
          <a href="http://writing-program.uchicago.edu/toys/randomsentence/write-sentence.htm">
           academic-sentence generator
          </a>
          you can baffle them with your bombast.
         </p>
         <p>
          Or, if youre too lazy to string together your own sentence, let
          <a href="http://writing-program.uchicago.edu/toys/randomsentence/index.htm">
           Pootwattle, the Virtual Academic,
          </a>
          do the work for you.
          <a href="http://writing-program.uchicago.edu/toys/randomsentence/index.htm">
           Smedley, the Virtual Critic,
          </a>
          responds with an automatically generated counter-argument.
         </p>
         <p>
          Go ahead, give it a try!
          <em>
           Don Troop
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
