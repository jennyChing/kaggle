          Yoast WordPress SEO Sitemap Error
         </a>
        </h1>
        <p class="byline vcard" style="margin:0px 5px 0px 15px;">
         <span class="author" style="color:#58595b;">
          Steven Soehl
         </span>
         <span class="updated published" style="color:#58595b;">
          PostedApril 22, 2015
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <section class="entry-content ">
        <p>
         Well, the other day I was checking in on Google Webmaster tool and noticed that one of our sites was experiencing crawl errors. I preceded to bring up the that site's sitemap generated by Yoast WordPress SEO to find the following:
        </p>
        <div style="padding: 15px; margin-left: 20px; border: 1px solid #666;">
         This page contains the following errors:error on line 2at column 6: XML declaration allowed only at the start of the document
         <br/>
         Below is a rendering of the page up to the first error.
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         After some research, the cause of the error is white space either preceding your opening php tag (&lt;?php) or after your closing tag (?&gt;). First place to check is your theme's functions.php file. Open the file in your editor of choice and check to see if your opening and closing tags are free of white space.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
