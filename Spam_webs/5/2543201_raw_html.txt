         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.1smile.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/invisalign-glasgow.jpg">
            <img alt="invisalign-glasgow" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-8607" height="175" src="http://www.1smile.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/invisalign-glasgow-300x175.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
           Our practice in Glasgow believes that your smile can change your confidence and can have an impact on your oral health.
          </p>
          <p>
           We provide a treatment called invisalign which straightens your teeth using a series of nearly invisible removable aligners that are custom-made specifically for you. Generally you can expect to replace each aligner every two weeks and as you do so your teeth will move slightly each time that you do until your teeth are in the final position that you want.
          </p>
          <p>
           The benefits of invisalign are:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            They are customised for your teeth
           </li>
           <li>
            They are comfortable
           </li>
           <li>
            They are effective
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            .
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            .
           </span>
           These are clear and removable as well which means that you dont need to worry about people noticing that you are undergoing treatment at all.
          </p>
          <p>
           Before we begin treatment for patients we will take an impression of your teeth to check whether or not your teeth can be straightened using the invisalign system. If you are suitable for this treatment then a detailed treatment plan will be developed.
          </p>
          <p>
           When you begin this treatment at our practice in Glasgow you will be expected to wear each set of custom-made aligners in two week increments. During this time we will ask you to return to us for occasional check-ups, this is so that we can monitor your progress.
          </p>
          <p>
           The treatment times for invisalign can vary depending on the complexity of each straightening issue so our dentists can talk to you about your situation and goals to determine the best way forward for you.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you live in Glasgow and would like to discuss this type of treatment then you should get in touch with us and make an appointment to see one of our dentists.
          </p>
