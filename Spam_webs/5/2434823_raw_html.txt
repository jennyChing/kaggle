      <h1>
       Cuba
      </h1>
      <p>
       The Cuba shutters range is third up in our interior window shutter products.
      </p>
      <p>
       It is our entry level hardwood shutter manufactured from a mix of sustainable hardwoods with a medium density fibreboard (MDF) frame. This combination of materials allows strength whilst reducing weight and provides a cost effective option for hardwood shutters. All shutter panels are finger jointed which is a more economical way of manufacturing.
      </p>
      <p>
       An advantage of Cuba over the Antigua and Bermuda ranges it that 4 panels can be hinged together which is ideal for large windows and expanses.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Cuba features:
      </h3>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Made from basswood in a medium density fibreboard (MDF) frame
       </li>
       <li>
        Available in 6 standard white paint finishes (no stains or custom colours)
       </li>
       <li>
        Choose from 4 louvre sizes (47mm, 64mm, 76mm and 89mm)
       </li>
       <li>
        Available with a range of tilt rod options (offset, central and hidden)
       </li>
       <li>
        Suitable for large windows and expanses
       </li>
       <li>
        Suitable for custom window shapes
       </li>
       <li>
        Available with remote control - ideal for roofs
       </li>
      </ul>
