     <p align="left">
      <strong>
       <font face="Trebuchet MS" size="4">
        Orion
    (Or-rye-en)
       </font>
       <font face="Trebuchet MS" size="3">
        1)
       </font>
      </strong>
      The Hunter, a
    constellation lying on the celestial equator between Canis Major and Taurus, containing
    the bright stars Betelgeuse and Rigel and the home star system of the Orion Barrier
    Alliance
      <strong>
       2)
      </strong>
      any member of a race of humanoid, sentient beings who are
    characterized by green to white skin, native to some star systems found in the
    constellation Orion
      <strong>
       3)
      </strong>
      the longest running Classic Star Trek fanzine
    press in fandom
     </p>
     <font size="4">
      <p>
      </p>
     </font>
     <font face="Trebuchet MS" size="4">
      <strong>
       fanzine (fan-zeen)
      </strong>
     </font>
     <font face="Trebuchet MS" size="3">
      a fan-produced non-profit publication that is
    written by fans, illustrated by fans, edited by fans, and printed by fans out of their
    love for the material; often referred to as a 'zine' (zeen, as in maga
      <u>
       zine
      </u>
      ) by
    those who read them
     </font>
     <p>
      <font face="Trebuchet MS">
       Welcome to the home of one of fandom's last bastions of
       <em>
        Classic
    Trek
       </em>
       fandom! Orion Press has served fandom since 1979, and now hosts one of the
    largest collections of
       <em>
        Classic Star Trek
       </em>
       fan fiction on the Internet. On this
    website, you will find a variety of material designed for the discriminating
       <em>
        Star Trek
       </em>
       fan. All we ask is that you drop us a line, and let us know how we're doing, how we can
    improve, and what you liked or disliked about the site, the stories, the articles and the
    like. We want to hear from you!
      </font>
     </p>
     <p>
      <font face="Trebuchet MS">
       And hey, be sure and check out
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.projectpotemkin.com">
         Project: Potemkin
        </a>
       </strong>
       , a fan film series
    we're setting in the Orion Universe. The first nine productions are ready for viewing.
      </font>
     </p>
     <p>
      <font face="Trebuchet MS">
       Lastly, we're in the process moving the website to:
       <a href="http://www.orionpressfanzines.com">
        www.orionpressfanzines.com
       </a>
       ! Please notify me
    of any errors at
       <a href="mailto:randy@orionpressfanzines.com">
        randy@orionpressfanzines.com
       </a>
       ,
    I'll get at them immediately. Please pass along the main website link to your friends. We
    don't want to lose any readers during this transition.
      </font>
     </p>
