     <div class="entry">
      <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="4831435" data-link="https://blog.shareaholic.com/introducing-shareaholic-affiliate-links/" data-summary="" data-title="Earn Revenue In Your Sleep With Shareaholic Affiliate Links">

      <p>
       Wouldnt it be great if you were rewarded every time you recommended your favorite products to your friends and followers? Even better: what if these rewards were generatedautomatically, without any complicated set-up or maintenance?
      </p>
      <p>
       We think thatsounds awesome, which iswhy we addeda new feature that
       <em>
        automatically
       </em>
       monetizes the
       <em>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <strong>
          existing
         </strong>
        </span>
       </em>
       links on your site.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Introducing Affiliate Links
      </h3>
      <p>
       Our brand new Affiliate Links app turns your sites
       <strong>
        existing links
       </strong>
       into potential rewards. Now, anytime your readers make a purchase at participating e-commerce siteslike Amazon and eBay based on links from your site, you get compensated. The more traffic you drive, the more money you get!
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Heres how it works:
       </strong>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Affiliate Links are
        <em>
        </em>
        integrated into your site and are
        <strong>
         native to your readers experience
        </strong>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        Existing links are
        <strong>
         automatically monetized*
        </strong>
        ,so theres no additional installation required.
       </li>
       <li>
        Already participating in anaffiliate program? Dont worry,
        <strong>
         we do not overwrite existing affiliate or partner links, neither does the App create anynew links.
        </strong>
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       For more information, visit the complete
       <a href="http://support.shareaholic.com/hc/en-us/sections/200528823-Affiliate-Links">
        FAQs for Affiliate Links
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        *
        <span style="color: #4d4d4f;">
         The Federal Trade Commission requires that you disclose to your readers when you endorse a product or service and have a material connection to the seller.
         <a href="http://support.shareaholic.com/hc/en-us/articles/201814675" target="_blank">
          Click here to learn more
         </a>
         .
        </span>
       </em>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Make MoneyIn Your Sleep
      </h3>
      <p>
       The best part about about this feature is that thereis no extra work. No installation, no special links. If you already use Shareaholic on your site, then your links are automatically enabled. Better yet,getting paid is simple:Just
       <a href="https://shareaholic.com/account" target="_blank">
        connect your PayPal account
       </a>
       !*
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-align: center; font-size: 12px;">
        <em>
         *Payments andinitialearnings report will be distributedon November 1st, 2014 (
         <a href="http://support.shareaholic.com/hc/en-us/articles/201364089-Affiliate-Links-Payment-FAQ-s" target="_blank">
          learn more about payments
         </a>
         ). Coming soon, youll also be able to view your earnings in adashboard in your Shareaholic Website Settings.Stay tuned!
        </em>
       </span>
      </p>
      <hr/>
      <h3>
       Manage Your Affiliate Link Settings
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         Turn Affiliate Links On/Off
        </span>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="https://shareaholic.com/login" target="_blank">
         Log in
        </a>
        to your Shareaholic account
       </li>
       <li>
        Click on the
        <a href="https://shareaholic.com/publisher_tools" target="_blank">
         Website Settings
        </a>
        button at the top right
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <img alt="shareaholic_settings" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-19937" height="56" src="https://blog.shareaholic.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/shareaholic-settings.png" width="279"/>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        In the Affiliate Links section, move the toggle
        <strong>
         LEFT
        </strong>
        to turn it
        <strong>
         OFF
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         RIGHT
        </strong>
        to turn it
        <strong>
         ON
        </strong>
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <img alt="affiliate_toggle" class="alignnone wp-image-19938" height="55" src="https://blog.shareaholic.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Affiliate-Links-Toggle.png" width="516"/>

      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         Connect your PayPal account

        Enter your PayPal email address
       </li>
