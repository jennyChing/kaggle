      Apologies for the delay, Timeflies bring us a fan submitted idea of TV shows. In standard form, Res samples some themes, writes down some random tv shows on small pieces of paper and lets Cal do the rest. You can tell Res is still surprised by some of the words Cal strings together as he bends back in awe.
