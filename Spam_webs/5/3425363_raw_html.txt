          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.resultswithmaggie.com/complete-guide-to-affiliate-marketing-and-targeted-traffic/" title="Nothing Held Back: Complete Guide To Affiliate Marketing and Targeted Traffic">
            Nothing Held Back: Complete Guide To Affiliate Marketing and Targeted Traffic
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <span style="font-family: verdana, geneva;font-size: 12pt">
             Affiliate marketing is one of the most popular ways to make money online and there are many things that anyone can do to take advantage of it.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             But since affiliate marketing is one of the well-known ways to earn money, it is also misunderstood. More often than not, those who dont understand it usually fail to generate any income.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             It simply isnt as easy as slapping on some products from other companies on your website, and hope the sales will flood in.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             This article is meant to show you some of the possibilities of generating an income without creating your own products.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Im talking about finding products to promote and making commissions from them.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             I also want to emphasize the power of traffic and also show you how you can get floods of people to your page. In the end, its all about you and how honest you are with your marketing.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             This guide will help you get a head start on affiliate marketing and show you the proper way to promote products without making the common mistakes marketers make.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              What you will learn:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              What is affiliate marketing
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              What it isnt
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              How to get started
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Where to find affiliate products and successful companies
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Write useful content and honest reviews
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Know your target audience
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Traffic
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Be patient
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Keep trying different things
             </span>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              What is affiliate marketing?
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Affiliate marketing is simply a way to make money online by promoting a service, product or a site that isnt yours. As an affiliate, you are rewarded on a performance-based structure that allows you to earn money based on your advertising efforts.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             For example:
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             If you promote a product from Amazon and someone makes the purchase by clicking on your link, you earn a percentage off the total sale. They have an affiliate program called
             <a href="https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/">
              Amazon Associates
             </a>
             , and you can promote any product.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You basically get a link that has your specific affiliate code on the end of it.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              What affiliate marketing isnt:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             It isnt a way to get rich overnight. You cant simply slap on a few products onto your website, and wait for some magic to happen. Anything that has rewards takes work, and affiliate marketing is no different.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You may not see sales for weeks, months, or maybe even years.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Okay if you dont get a sale after a year, then you know youre doing something wrong. But it simply requires patience and skills. It isnt simple as you should be unique in the way you promote any product.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You have to be honest, or you may break your visitors trust.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              Is affiliate marketing easy money?
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             While it is one of the most lucrative ways to earn an income online, it is important to understand that it isnt easy money.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Most people who try it usually fail and give up because they are missing one of these key factors:
            </span>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Targeted traffic (not just random traffic that has no interest in what youre promoting)
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Understanding the products you promote
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Finding products that actually work
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Building relationship and trust with visitors
             </span>
            </li>
            <li>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
              Good sales copy writing skills
             </span>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Of course there is always the chance that if you push too hard with your affiliate marketing efforts, or promote low quality products, youll have issues with trust and your reputation. You cant expect to burn your visitors with something that is low quality or plain doesnt work, and have a happy ending.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Thats never going to benefit anyone, especially you!
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             How can you get started with affiliate marketing?
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             While you may just have a social media page where you can promote products or service, it is highly recommended that you have your own website.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             If youre not sure how you can start your own website or you think its difficult, there are tons of YouTube videos that show you how to do so within one hour, like this one:
             <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8Jv47_VIBOQ">
              https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8Jv47_VIBOQ
             </a>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             After you know where youll be promoting services or products, you should choose a niche. It may seem easy to promote random products, but if youre going to sell baby products to young mothers, you probably wont want to promote hunting gear.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             See where Im going with this?
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Choose your niche, preferably one with lots of products. Health, wealth and relationships are the 3 biggest ones, so narrow it down to something where you can just solve one problem rather than dozens.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              Where to find affiliate products?
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">

            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">

           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              Write useful content and reviews
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Okay so youve got your affiliate products in mind, and your website is hopefully ready, now youll want to have some content. Now there used to be a time where you could just write a few paragraphs, make a bazillion bucks and be ranked on Google for the number one spot for that particular product.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             But Google is smart, and they are always adapting.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Keywords matter, people will want to find your site according to their search, but you will have to write for those people, not just for Google.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             So write useful content.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Solve their problem and tell them what they can do to solve it. Make the product as the centerpiece, throw in your link a few times in the post, dont be shy.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Youre going through the effort of writing a nice, in-depth post of something useful for them.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Hopefully you know the product or service well, so well that you may have tried it or seen it in action. Youll want to write reviews as people are always looking for reviews.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You wont get far without writing an HONEST review.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Hey yea this product is perfect, no drawbacks, buy it now wont work. Youll want to write the good AND the bad of the product. If you dont, theyll notice it and they wont trust you as much.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Oh yeah, keep your target audience in mind. If youre going to write for dentists who are looking for dental equipment, you wont want to write as if you were talking to a toddler.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Youll want to place banners and links where you think people will see it. Obviously at the bottom of everything is probably not optimal, but keep trying different places without being too spammy.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              Get traffic
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You can have the best content, the best recommended products/services, but if its a ghost town, what good does it do for anyone? You have tap into the internets flood of traffic.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You wont magically grow into the millions overnight, (let me know if you do!), but you will have to try.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             One of the best ways to get traffic with minimal effort is by guest posting. Guest posting is a way to connect with other established websites as you write content for them.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Usually theyll plug in your website so people can check out what youve got. Take advantage of influencers and well established websites because they already have audiences!
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              Try different things
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You wont want to try one thing, and hope that it would be the answer to it all, and just let it be. Youll want to try different things. Maybe your sales copy isnt convincing them, or maybe your reviews arent honest enough.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Whatever it is, youre bound to fail but thats when you can take the advantage of succeeding. Position yourself right from the beginning so youre on the right track. People are smart, they want honest affiliates, not robots.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             So be a human!
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <strong>
              Be patient
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Patience is a virtue; youve got to have it. Any affiliate will tell you that this takes time. Nothing happens overnight and that youll want to be patient or you wont give yourself a chance to see success.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Like I said, it may take months before you see your first sale. Until then, youve got lots of work and testing to do!
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family: verdana, geneva">
             <span style="font-size: 18.6666660308838px">
              <b>
               Wrapping it up
              </b>
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             So now you know all about affiliate marketing, youll want to get started right away. Action is the first step to anything. Youll want to have an email marketing plan as well because email is one of the best ways to stay in touch with your visitors. Its also great for building rapport and trust.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             You wont want to advertise and bug them about that one product you want them to buy, but youll want to solve their problem by showing them that you understand what they are looking for.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Treat every visitor like a friend, get to know them and learn what you can do to improve their experience.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;font-family: verdana, geneva">
             Most affiliate marketers fail because they dont care about their visitors. Care about them, and show them the way to a more awesome life!
            </span>
           </p>
