     <h2 style="text-align: center;">
      <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
       Before You List Your Wheels For Sale
      </span>
     </h2>
     <div align="left">
      <h3>
       i. Clean your wheels
      </h3>
      <div style="margin-left: 1em;">
       Potential online buyers interested in your wheels are going to request pictures at some point before they send you money.  Being prepared with clear photos of clean wheels makes your job as a seller easier.  Your wheels will be more marketable and chances are potential buyers are going to be willing to pay more.
      </div>
      <h3>
       Before Washing:
      </h3>
      <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/HowToCleanWheels">
       <img alt="How to Clean German Wheels" class="alignleft size-full" height="467" src="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Wheel Shipping Photos/How to Clean BMW Wheels.jpg" title="How to Clean BMW Wheels.jpg" width="700"/>
      </a>
      <h3>
       After Washing:
      </h3>
      <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/HowToCleanWheels">
       <img alt="How to clean BMWs wheels" class="alignleft size-full" height="467" src="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Wheel Shipping Photos/Clean BMW Contour Staggard Wheels.jpg" title="Clean BMW Contour Staggard Wheels.jpg" width="700"/>
      </a>
      <p>
      </p>
      <center>
       <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/HowToCleanWheels">
        How to Clean Wheels
       </a>
       shows quick &amp; effective cleaning techniques before listing and packaging your wheels.
      </center>
      <h3>
       ii. Take pictures of your cleaned wheels
      </h3>
      <div style="margin-left: 1em;">
       Wheels are susceptible to many structural and aesthetic flaws not limited to things like: curb rash, cracks, dents, dings, bends and oxidation. With clear pictures you can show the potential buyers the exact condition of the wheels, instead of having to answer 10 emails with the same questions.
      </div>
      <p>
       <img alt="How to take pictures of wheels" class="alignleft size-full" height="467" src="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Wheel Shipping Photos/Photographing wheel for sale.jpg" title="Photographing wheel for sale.jpg" width="700"/>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <center>
        <u>
         Listing Your Wheels
        </u>
       </center>
      </h2>
      <div align="left">
       <h3>
        iii. Choose where to list your wheels online
       </h3>
       <div style="margin-left: 1em;">
        How quickly do you want to sell these wheels? Are you willing to ship the wheels? Is there a specific car forum for the type of wheel your selling?
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Craigslist_vs_eBay_vs_forums">
         <img alt="eBay vs Craigslist vs Forums" class="alignleft size-full" height="360" src="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Wheel Shipping Photos/eBayForums.jpg" title="eBayForums.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Craigslist_vs_eBay_vs_forums">
         Craigslist vs eBay vs forums
        </a>
        explains the Pros &amp; Cons of selling on Craigslist, eBay &amp; Car Forums.
       </center>
       <div align="left">
        <h3>
         iv. Creating Your Listing
        </h3>
        <div style="margin-left: 1em;">
         Buyers want to know exactly what theyre buying, if the wheels will fit their car &amp; application.  The wheel specifications are likely on the back of the wheel or a quick google search will lead you to them. Tell potential buyers why youre selling the wheels; be clear and forthright about any imperfections.
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="How to create a for sale listing" class="alignleft size-full" height="540" src="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Wheel Shipping Photos/porsche.jpg" title="Selling Porsche Wheels Online" width="700"/>
        </p>

          v. How to Package &amp; Ship Wheels w/ Tires
         </h3>
         <div style="margin-left: 1em;">
          Shipping wheels with tires can be costly if you dont choose the right shipping provider &amp; you pay someone else to package them.

         <p>
          <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/HowToShipWheelsWithTires">
           <img alt="How to package and ship wheels" class="alignleft size-full" height="467" src="http://howtoshipwheels.com/Wheel Shipping Photos/PackagedandReadytoshipwheelswithtires.jpg" title="PackagedandReadytoshipwheelswithtires.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <a href="http://howtoshipwheels.com/HowToShipWheelsWithTires">
           How To Ship Wheels With Tires

          shows you how to  avoid the high cost of shipping and handling that most people pay when shipping wheels, rims and tires.

           vi. How to Package &amp; Ship Wheels w/o Tires

           vii. How to Package &amp; Ship Tires

           viii. Choosing a Shipping Provider
