            <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
             Preview: 30th annual Mich. HPV Rally - May 17-18, 2014
            </h3>
            <div class="post-header">
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4958528278602255617" itemprop="description articleBody">
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: 12px;">
              </span>
              <br/>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-FxRJaPZ3zDY/Ux5uLjw5HsI/AAAAAAAAA6Q/Uqc8i69fbvo/s1600/100_1052.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-FxRJaPZ3zDY/Ux5uLjw5HsI/AAAAAAAAA6Q/Uqc8i69fbvo/s1600/100_1052.jpg"/>
               </a>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               The rally will be held at the Waterford Hills sports car racing track onthe Oakland County Sportsmen's Club grounds in Clarkston, near Pontiac -the location since 1986.
              </span>
             </div>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              The
              <i>
               oldest such event
              </i>
              in North America is open to riders of all humanpowered vehicles  recumbents, regular bicycles, tandems and handcycles.
              <br/>
              There are classes for streamlined, unstreamlined cycles, tandems, women,youth and tricycles. The rally is conducted using Human Powered RaceAmerica rules (
              <a href="http://www.recumbents.com/hpra/rulesand.htm" style="color: #0000cc; outline-color: initial; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial;" target="_blank">
               www.recumbents.com/hpra/rulesand.htm
              </a>
              ). Note: HPRA rulesrequire all vehicles to have a mirror or mirrors enabling rear vision toboth sides.
              <br/>
              The track is 1.4 miles around, with nine turns and one hill. Sunday races use a shorter course without the hill.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              <b>
               ENTRY FEES:
              </b>
              One day only, $30; both days, $40 (includes $10 MHPVAmembership);
             </span>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              college/high school teams with several riders, $100
             </span>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              . $1 of each entry fee goes to support the League of MichiganBicyclists. Test ride pass, $10, which includes MHPVA membership.Spectators free.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              Even if you don't want to compete, come and see some unusual and somevery fast bicycles and tricycles. Prize money will be awarded to topfinishers in each class.
              <br/>
              Concession stand with hot food will be serving lunch on Saturday andpossibly on Sunday, breakfast and lunch.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              Schedule of events, subject to change:
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              <b>
               SATURDAY, May 17
              </b>
              <br/>
              Registration and technical inspection starts at 8 a.m.
              <br/>
              One-hour time trial (streamliner, streetliner classes) - 9:30-10:30 a.m
              <br/>
              One-hour time trial (stock, junior, women, tandem) - 11 a.m.-noon
              <br/>
              Lunch
              <br/>
              Hill climb/coast down (all classes) - 1:30-2:30 p.m.
              <br/>
              1-kilometer standing start time trial - 3-4 p.m.
              <br/>
              Urban transportation contest - in afternoon.
             </span>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
              Scores will be based on finishing position in the hill climb, coast-down and obstacle course, plus vehicle evaluation. For instance, if there are 10 competitors, the fastest in the hill climb gets 10 points; second fastest, 9 points, etc. In the evaluation, 1 point is awarded for each feature: Lights, front and rear (1 point for each); fenders, front and rear (1 point for each); reflectors; daytime visibility; cargo carrying ability (minimal, one grocery bag, or two grocery bags  maximum 3 points), rearview mirrors, security against theft, convenience (size, getting on/off or in/out), weather protection, horn, bell, tools, tire pump, spare inner tube or tube repair kit and "anything else."
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              6 p.m. (approximate)  Trackside barbecue. Choice of hamburgers, hot dogs or chicken, plus salad and soft drinks. Paper plates, napkins, etc., will be provided. Minimum donation of $5 per person.
             </span>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              (Note: This is a change from what was previously listed of either bring your own food to barbecue, plus supplies, or eat at a nearby restaurant.)
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              Followed by  Dessert (provided) and an opportunity to reminisce about 30 years of HPV racing.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              <b>
               SUNDAY
              </b>
              <br/>
              200-foot sprints, flying start (all classes) - 8:30-9:30 a.m.
              <br/>
              Tricycle race - 10 a.m.
              <br/>
              Road race 1 (faster vehicles, 20 laps, 12 miles, no hill) - 10:30 a.m.
              <br/>
              Road race 2 (slower Vehicles, 15 laps, 9 miles, no hill) - 11 a.m.
              <br/>
              Awards ceremony by 1 p.m. (hopefully)
              <br/>
              Location: Oakland County Sportsmen's Club: 4770 Waterford Road, Clarkston,
              <br/>
              MI 48346. For a map of the track, visit
              <br/>
              <a href="http://www.waterfordhills.com/downloads/facilitymap.pdf" style="color: #0000cc; outline-color: initial; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial;" target="_blank">
               http://www.waterfordhills.com/downloads/facilitymap.pdf
              </a>
              or
              <br/>
              <a href="http://www.mapmyride.com/routes/fullscreen/179549320/" style="color: #0000cc; outline-color: initial; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial;" target="_blank">
               http://www.mapmyride.com/routes/fullscreen/179549320/
              </a>
              For additional information, contact: Mike Mowett, 586-863-3902 or
              <br/>
              <a href="mailto:mowett@aol.com" style="color: #0000cc; outline-color: initial; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial;">
               mowett@aol.com
              </a>
              , or Mike Eliasohn, 269-982-4058 or
              <a href="mailto:mikethebike@acd.net" style="color: #0000cc; outline-color: initial; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial;">
               mikethebike@acd.net
              </a>
              .
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              <b>
               TO GET TO THE RALLY:
              </b>
              <br/>
              From I-75, get off at exit 91. Take M-15 south to Dixie Hwy. (US-24). Turnleft, continuing south about 1 mile and turn left onto Waterford Road, thenproceed to track.
              <br/>
              If coming from the west, take U.S. 23 north (or south) to M-59 (HighlandRoad). Go east on M-59 to Airport Road, then left (north) to US-24. Turnleft, then immediately right onto Waterford Road. Go about a half-mile totrack.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              <b>
               PLACES TO STAY:
              </b>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <b>
               <br/>
              </b>
              MOTELS (with approximate distances/direction from Waterford Hills track)
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              Clarkston - Clarkston Motor Inn, 6853 Dixie Hwy. (US-10), 248-625-1522, 12rooms, 2 miles northeast.
              <br/>
              Clarkston - Olde Mill Inn of Clarkston, 5835 Dixie Hwy., 248-623-0300.Across Dixie Highway from Waterford Road leading to track. This is theclosest motel to the track.
              <br/>
              Hartland - Best Western of Hartland, 10087 M-59 at US-23, 810-632-7177, 61rooms. About 18 miles west.
              <br/>
              Waterford  Comfort Inn, 7076 Highland Road (M-59), 248-666-8555, 111rooms. About 3 miles southwest.
              <br/>
              Waterford  Waterford Motel, 2201 Dixie Hwy. at Telegraph Road,248-338-4061, 50 rooms. About 6 miles southeast.
              <br/>
              Waterford  Holiday Inn Express, 4350 Pontiac Lake Road, 248-674-3434,83 rooms. About 7 miles southwest.
              <br/>
              Whitmore Lake - Best Western of Whitmore Lake, 9897 Main St. (off US-23,exit 53), 734-449-2058, 61 rooms. About 33 miles southwest.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              CAMPING
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              Free camping available overnight Friday and Saturday at the WaterfordHills Sportsman Club, site of the HPV rally, starting at 6 p.m. Friday.Restrooms, showers available and possibly electrical hookups.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              STATE CAMPGROUNDS (
              <a href="http://www.michigan.gov/dnr" style="color: #0000cc; outline-color: initial; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial;" target="_blank">
               www.michigan.gov/dnr
              </a>
              , then click on "camping and
              <br/>
              recreation," then "camping and harbor reservations." Reservation can be
              <br/>
              made online.
              <br/>
              Highland Recreation Area, 5200 E. Highland Road (M-59), White Lake,248-889-3750. Two miles east of Highland.
              <br/>
              Holly Recreation Area, 5300 McGinnis Road, Holly, 248-634-8811. Five mileseast of Holly.
              <br/>
              Ortonville Recreation Area, 5779 Hadley Road, Ortonville, 810-797-4439.Four miles northeast of Ortonville.
              <br/>
              Pontiac Lake Recreation Area, 3480 Teggerdine Road, White Lake,248-625-9112. Closest to Waterford Hills track, about 4 miles west.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <br/>
              OAKLAND COUNTY: Groveland Oaks County Park, 14555 Dixie Hwy., White Lake, northeast ofHolly, 248-625-9112.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
