          It Happens.
         </h1>
         <div class="post-header-meta">
          <span class="the-author">

       <div class="entry-content clearfix" id="content-4588">
        <div class="twitter-share">
         <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="none" data-size="large" href="https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=http%3A%2F%2Fichilly.com%2Fit-happens&amp;via=iChilly&amp;related=ichilly%3AChilly">
          Tweet
         </a>
        </div>
        <p style="color: #000000; text-align: justify;">
         Sometimes people say and/or do really mean and hurtfulstuff. It rocks our world. It exposes our insecurity. We feel betrayed, wounded, alone and unwanted. And, the more we focus on the offense, the deeper it sinks into our hearts. Then, words like: unfair, untrue or undeserved leap to the forefront of ourminds. We allow ourselves to become victims. We communicate to our trusted friends how we have been wounded. Yet, their pity does little to help
        </p>
        <p style="color: #000000; text-align: center;">
         Insults, accusations, backstabbing 
         <strong>
          IT HAPPENS!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #000000; text-align: justify;">
         What if we didnt fear it or try to stop
         <strong>
          it
         </strong>
         ? What if we didnt defend ourselves? What if we embraced
         <strong>
          it
         </strong>
         ?
        </p>
        <p style="color: #000000; text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Love&gt;Hate copy" class="alignright wp-image-4591" height="192" src="http://ichilly.com/wp-content/uploads/LoveHate-copy-300x231.jpg" width="249"/>
         Persecution or opposition is a mark of every true Christian The Old Testament prophets like Amos, Jeremiah, Ezekiel and Daniel; all experienced it. So did the New Testament apostles. And down through the centuries, including today, authentic Christians who refuse to distort or dilute the gospel of grace have had to suffer for their faithfulness. The good news of Christ crucified is still a scandal (Greek, skandalon = stumbling-block), grievously offensive to the pride of our generation.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Im NOT suggesting that wepursue 
         <strong>
          IT
         </strong>
           that would be insanity  but, if were real, weWILLexperience it. Wewonthave to pick a fight  attack upon our lives, familiesand character areunavoidable. But as westand up and through such an onslaught, Christs power becomes more and more evident to the cynical &amp; sarcastic culture hiding in the shadows of a lie.
         <span id="more-4588">
         </span>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <span class="verse v11" data-usfm="MAT.5.11">
            <span class="wj">
             <span class="content">
              Jesus said,
              <strong>
               God blesses you when people mock you and persecute you and lie about you and say all sorts of evil things against you because you are my followers.
              </strong>
             </span>
            </span>
            <span class="content">
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="verse v12" data-usfm="MAT.5.12">
            <span class="wj">
             <span class="content">
              <strong>
               Be happy about it! Be very glad! For a great reward awaits you in heaven. And remember, the ancient prophets were persecuted in the same way.
              </strong>
              Matthew 5:11-12
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Whoa, be very glad for 
         <strong>
          it
         </strong>
         ! And, in the meantime, listen to what God says about you!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Soon light will reveal all until then, lets press on.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #000000; text-align: right;">
         Yours in the Fight and the Victory,
         <strong>
          <em>
           C
          </em>
          <em>
           hilly
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="line-height: 32px; color: #808080;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           For discussion, in the comments
          </span>
          :
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          1.
         </strong>
         Have you recently experienced attack, betrayal or disappointment? How are you dealing with it?
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2.
         </strong>
         How do we rejoice when stuff sucks &amp; hurts?
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3.
         </strong>
         What can you (will you) do today to bless those who persecute you?
        </p>
