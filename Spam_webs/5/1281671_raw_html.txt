     <h2>
      Bow and Bay Windows
     </h2>
     <p class="intro" style="text-align: justify;">
      Add character to your home and gain extra living space with our beautiful bay &amp; bow windows.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Enhance your view with our stylish bow and bay windows. Offering a contemporary design, our uPVC windows feature conservative trim detailing. This preserves period charm whilst adding an attractive, yet subtle, modern twist.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      When you choose a bow or bay window from your local Double Glazing Network Dealer, you will benefit from the following:
     </p>
     <h3>
      Equal Glass Area
     </h3>
     <p>
      Additional dummy sashes are fitted to create equal sightlines, enhancing the appearance of your home.
     </p>
     <h3>
      Bow-Bay Conversion
     </h3>
     <p>
      Transform a flat window into a wonderful bow-bay and create a heightened sense of space.
     </p>
     <h3>
      Structural Bay Poles
     </h3>
     <p>
      Where required, bay poles can be carried through the cill onto a hidden bearing plate to take structural loads.
     </p>
     <h3>
      Joint Coupling
     </h3>
     <p>
      Clever interior and exterior joint couplings are designed to prevent dust traps forming to make upkeep easy.
     </p>
     <h3>
      Welded One-Piece Cill
     </h3>
     <p>
      The external cill is welded together to create a single complete cill underneath all of the uPVC frames.
     </p>
     <h3 style="color: #5f625d;">
     </h3>
     <h3 style="color: #5f625d; text-align: justify;">
     </h3>
     <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
      Attractive Features
     </h3>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Liniar bow and bay windows are available in a range of styles including 3 facet, 5 facet or box bay. With slim sightlines you benefit from increased light entering your home and enhanced views outwards. A Liniar UPVC bow and bay window is an excellent choice for preserving period charm with a stylish contemporary design.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      The Liniar bay window also offers a joint coupling feature which cleverly combines interior and exterior couplings, designed to prevent dust clusters from gathering in your home. A perfect way to keep a low maintenance household.
     </p>
     <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
      The uPVC Bay Window
     </h3>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      A beautiful choice, Bay windows let in a vast amount of natural light making a room appear bigger and brighter. These uPVC windows provide a wide, non obstructed view creating a feeling of spaciousness. Designed to project outwards from a room, their unique structure will look impressive from both the interior and exterior of your property.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Bay windows look great anywhere in the home. Popular places include kitchens, living rooms and bedrooms. A popular window style, the bay window brings added light and depth, opening up your home. Their added footage provides you with extra space for ornaments or even additional seating. A bay window can be turned into a tiny cove complete with pillows. A deep shelf low to the ground will set the perfect scene.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Bay windows are often seen as the main feature of a house, capturing the eye of visitors and home buyers alike. Replacing your old bay window can breathe life back into your property, eliminating draughts and increasing the overall value.
     </p>
     <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
      The uPVC Bow Window
     </h3>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      The uPVC bow window features a seamless curved design and typically combines four or more casement windows which join together to form an arch.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      This unique design comprises four or more casement windows which join together creating a gradual, curved look. This round, soft angled design is also known as a curved bay window. The uPVC bow window projects beyond the exterior wall of a property, providing a wider, scenic view.  Bow windows can be installed on any level and like the bay window, they have fantastic home value potential.
     </p>
