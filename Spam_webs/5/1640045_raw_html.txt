       <h2>
        <strong>
         Urijah Faber Scouting Report
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Vitals
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        56 145 lbs (Featherweight)
        <br/>
        69 reach, Orthodox
        <br/>
        May 14, 1979
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Record
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        32-8 (UFC: 8-4)
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Current Streak
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        1 straight loss
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Training
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        NCAA D1 wrestler
        <br/>
        Brown belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Championships Held
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        King of the Cage Bantamweight Champion: 2004-2006 (four successful title defenses)
        <br/>
        WEC Featherweight Champion: 2006-2008 (six successful title defenses)
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Strengths
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
         as experienced as they come  18 title fights
        <br/>
         incredibly tough  fought with two broken hands one fight
        <br/>
         excellent submission skills
        <br/>
         finishes fights
        <br/>
         great chin (no pun intended)
        <br/>
         amazing cardio  always pushing the pace
        <br/>
         strong wrestler
        <br/>
         solid in the clinch
        <br/>
         good at avoiding/defending strikes
        <br/>
         has only lost twice in a non-title fight in his career
        <br/>
         fast hands
        <br/>
         very disciplined in training and diet
        <br/>
         good ground and pound
        <br/>
         big and strong for a bantamweight
        <br/>
         superb submission defense  has never been tapped out
        <br/>
         comfortable switching stances during a fight
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Weaknesses
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
         not much knockout power
        <br/>
        <strong>
         -
        </strong>
        not overly accurateon his strikes
        <br/>
        <strong>
         -
        </strong>
        gets hitalmost more often than he dishes out
        <br/>
        <strong>
         -
        </strong>
        poor takedown &amp; takedown defense success
        <br/>
        <strong>
         -
        </strong>
        hasnt been able to get over the hump in championship fights lately
        <br/>
        <strong>
         -
        </strong>
        best years are probably behind him
        <br/>
         doesnt throw many kicks
        <br/>
         very susceptible to be attacked via leg kicks
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <br/>
         Synopsis
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The sun is probably close to setting on the The California Kids storied MMA career, so enjoy his exciting fights while you can.
       </p>
