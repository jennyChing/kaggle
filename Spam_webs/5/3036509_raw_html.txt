         <p>
          The good old boys at the Texas-based Starwood Motors have been wowing us for years with their custom Jeep work, so why would 2015 be any different? For their first act of the year, the talented customizers present the Jeep Nomad.
         </p>

         <p>
          A customized Jeep Wrangler, this thing is just as menacing as the brands previous builds  and has the function to match its form. Thats because underneath all those bolt-ons is a fully capable Rubicon Unlimited. Its been outfitted with a Teraflex long arm suspension system, Fox shocks and steering stabilizer, Anti Rock sway bays, RCV axles, and a whole lot more to help it tackle the off-road trails of the backcountry. On the outside, weve got a Smittybilt Overland rack complete with a
          <a href="http://hiconsumption.com/2015/03/best-rooftop-camping-tents/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://hiconsumption.com/2015/03/best-rooftop-camping-tents/', 'roof-mounted tent']);" title="Rolling Homes: The 7 Best Rooftop Camping Tents">
           roof-mounted tent
          </a>
          for weekend excursions, full front and rear LED lighting, custom storage compartments, winch, generator, and a portable fridge/freezer. Now we know why its called the Nomad. Expect to spend $107,888 for this bad boy. [
          <a href="http://www.starwoodmotors.com/projectjeeps/nomad.php" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.starwoodmotors.com/projectjeeps/nomad.php', 'Purchase']);" target="_blank">
