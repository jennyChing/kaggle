     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        The Last Mimzy
       </b>
      </i>
      is a 2007
      <a href="/wiki/Science_fiction_film" title="Science fiction film">
       science fiction
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Adventure_film" title="Adventure film">
       adventure
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">
       drama film
      </a>
      directed by
      <a href="/wiki/Robert_Shaye" title="Robert Shaye">
       Robert Shaye
      </a>
      and loosely adapted from the
      <a href="/wiki/1943_in_literature" title="1943 in literature">
       1943
      </a>
      science fiction short story "
      <a href="/wiki/Mimsy_Were_the_Borogoves" title="Mimsy Were the Borogoves">
       Mimsy Were the Borogoves
      </a>
      " by
      <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Padgett" title="Lewis Padgett">
       Lewis Padgett
      </a>
      (the
      <a href="/wiki/Pseudonym" title="Pseudonym">
       pseudonym
      </a>
      of husband and wife team
      <a href="/wiki/Henry_Kuttner" title="Henry Kuttner">
       Henry Kuttner
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/C._L._Moore" title="C. L. Moore">
       C. L. Moore
      </a>
      ). The film features
      <a href="/wiki/Timothy_Hutton" title="Timothy Hutton">
       Timothy Hutton
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Joely_Richardson" title="Joely Richardson">
       Joely Richardson
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Rainn_Wilson" title="Rainn Wilson">
       Rainn Wilson
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Kathryn_Hahn" title="Kathryn Hahn">
       Kathryn Hahn
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Michael_Clarke_Duncan" title="Michael Clarke Duncan">
       Michael Clarke Duncan
      </a>
      , and introducing Rhiannon Leigh Wryn as seven-year-old Emma Wilder and
      <a href="/wiki/Chris_O%27Neil_(actor)" title="Chris O'Neil (actor)">
       Chris ONeil
      </a>
      as ten-year-old Noah.
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">
       Plot
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Last_Mimzy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Presented as a story by a group of students and their teacher Lena that takes place in the distant future,
      <i>
       The Last Mimzy
      </i>
      is the story of the attempt by humans in the distant future to avert a catastrophic
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ecological_disaster" title="Ecological disaster">
       ecological disaster
      </a>
      that has destroyed their world.
      <a href="/wiki/High_tech" title="High tech">
       High tech
      </a>
      devices disguised as
      <a href="/wiki/Toy" title="Toy">
       toys
      </a>
      are sent back in time to Noah and Emma Wilder, children living in early 21st century
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seattle,_Washington" title="Seattle, Washington">
       Seattle
      </a>
      . The "toys" are incomprehensible to Emma (Rhiannon Leigh Wryn) and Noah (
      <a href="/wiki/Chris_O%27Neil_(actor)" title="Chris O'Neil (actor)">
       Chris O'Neil
      </a>
      ), except for what appears to be a
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stuffed_animal" title="Stuffed animal">
       stuffed
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Rabbit" title="Rabbit">
       rabbit
      </a>
      . Sensing the paranormal strangeness of the devices, the children initially keep their discovery secret from their parents.
     </p>
     <p>
      Emma becomes telepathically connected to the stuffed rabbit, named Mimzy, and learns how to operate the devices. Interaction with the devices causes the children to develop advanced knowledge,
      <a href="/wiki/Genius" title="Genius">
       genius
      </a>
      -level
      <a href="/wiki/Intelligence" title="Intelligence">
       intelligence
      </a>
      and
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Psionic" title="Psionic">
       psionic
      </a>
      abilities. Because of her psychic connection with Mimzy, Emma's development of her abilities is much stronger than her older brother's; she is the only one to display
      <a href="/wiki/Empathy" title="Empathy">
       empathy
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Telepathy" title="Telepathy">
       telepathy
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Levitation_(paranormal)" title="Levitation (paranormal)">
       levitation
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="/wiki/Psychokinesis" title="Psychokinesis">
       telekinesis
      </a>
      (though Noah can also telepathically communicate with her once she has telepathically contacted him). Emma is also the only one who can use the Spinners, strange spinning, telekinetically floating rocks that produce a force-field. Noah's strong psionic/physic abilities are developed through a card and a
      <a href="/wiki/Seashell" title="Seashell">
       seashell
      </a>
      . He gains increased intelligence, knowledge, telepathy, empathic communication with, and control over,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arthropods" title="Arthropods">
       arthropods
      </a>
      . He can also use the card to enable him to telekinetically
      <a href="/wiki/Teleportation" title="Teleportation">
       teleport
      </a>
      objects through a small
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dimensional_rift" title="Dimensional rift">
       dimensional rift
      </a>
      after staring at it and observing his surroundings. At one point, Noah becomes somewhat envious of his sister's psionic prowess, but she reveals to him that even though she is the Chosen One, he is her Chosen Engineer and she cannot "build the bridge to the future" without him.
     </p>
     <p>
      The children's unusual psionic and mental abilities and Emma's attachment to Mimzy alert their parents and schoolteachers to the devices. Later, Noah accidentally fuses the card with a blue blob, turning it into a Generator that causes a
      <a href="/wiki/Power_outage" title="Power outage">
       power black-out
      </a>
      over half the state of Washington, alerting the
      <a href="/wiki/Federal_Bureau_of_Investigation" title="Federal Bureau of Investigation">
       FBI
      </a>
      to their activities. The family is held for questioning by Special Agent Nathaniel Broadman (
      <a href="/wiki/Michael_Clarke_Duncan" title="Michael Clarke Duncan">
       Michael Clarke Duncan
      </a>
      ), and it is revealed that Mimzy is actually a highly advanced form of
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Synthetic_life" title="Synthetic life">
       artificial life
      </a>
      utilizing
      <a href="/wiki/Nanotechnology" title="Nanotechnology">
       nanotechnology
      </a>
      created by
      <a href="/wiki/Intel" title="Intel">
       Intel
      </a>
      . Mimzy has brought a message from humanity's distant future. Emma explains that
      <a href="/wiki/Pollution" title="Pollution">
       pollution
      </a>
      has corrupted humanity's
      <a href="/wiki/DNA" title="DNA">
       DNA
      </a>
      . Many rabbits like Mimzy have been sent to the past, but none have successfully returned. Mimzy is the last one remaining, but is now dying. The reason for the other Mimzys' deaths is that the Chosen Ones before Emma had no Engineers (like Noah) to help build the bridge across time. Mimzy explains to the children that they must use the toys as a time machine to return it to the future with uncorrupted 21st century human DNA, which the people of the future can use to correct the damage to their DNA caused by ecological problems.
     </p>
     <p>
      Despite attempts by the FBI to prevent them, Noah and Emma use their psionic abilities to escape so that they can activate a
      <a href="/wiki/Time_travel" title="Time travel">
       time portal
      </a>
      . Before leaving, Mimzy absorbs a
      <a href="/wiki/Tears" title="Tears">
       tear
      </a>
      from Emma, thus providing the DNA required to restore humanity's corrupted DNA. Emma is almost sucked into the future with Mimzy, but Noah grabs Emma and pulls her out of the portal just as Mimzy is sent back to its own time. The world has become a beautiful place, where humanity has integrated into the constructed
      <a href="/wiki/Ecosystem" title="Ecosystem">
       ecosystems
      </a>
      . The story ends with Emma's teacher (Julia Arkos) calling on Emma in class, asking what she did over her weekend break. Emma simply smiles.
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="The_.22Toys.22">
       The "Toys"
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Last_Mimzy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title='Edit section: The "Toys"'>
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      The "toys" are high-tech devices sent from the future by a scientist to obtain uncorrupted DNA. They were sent in a box that has three compartments despite its outside appearance suggesting it has only one. The floor of the first compartment opens to access the second; the floor of the second compartment opens to access the third. The "toys" all have special abilities and are able to give Emma and Noah genius-level intelligence and psionic abilities of their own.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Mimzy - an intelligent object based on nanotechnology, in the form of a brown and white stuffed rabbit, which can telepathically connect with certain individuals.
      </li>
      <li>
       Green card - a green-colored card with endless, moving light lines that make it seem fractured. To most people, it looks like a small, black rectangular slab. It can enable users to teleport objects after staring at it and observing their surroundings. It can also fuse with the blue blob to turn it into a Generator.
      </li>
      <li>
       Seashell - a white seashell-shaped object that allows the user to hear across longer distances and over a wider range of frequencies than normal.
      </li>
      <li>
       Blue blob - a blue blob that can fuse with the green card, turning it into a Generator.
      </li>
      <li>
       Spinners - a large black rock that can break into smaller rocks. The rocks can spin slightly off the ground, and when set spinning in a circle, they create a visible force-field that allows the user to view the time period from which the "toys" came.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      The "toys" can be used to send Mimzy back to its original time period. To do this, the green card is first fused with the blue blob to create a generator. Then, the spinners are used to create a force field. Finally, the generator must be used to create energy and blast it at the force field, giving it the power needed for the transmission. This can only be done once. After that, the remaining "toys" break and cannot be used again.
     </p>
