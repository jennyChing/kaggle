           Designing the icon is one of the most crucial parts of mobile app development because for many users, its the first interaction he or she has with your brand. Its the first visual experience the user will have and we all know you can only make a first impression once. That tiny, little square must convey what your app does, be unique and be memorable. If its beautiful, people will love it. Theyll be drawn to it. And ding, ding, ding! This makes for an important piece to building a successful app.
          </p>

           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/golf-spain-hd/id458975022?mt=8">
            Golf Spain HD
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/golf_spain_hd.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1475" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/golf_spain_hd.jpeg?w=300&amp;h=300" title="Golf_Spain_HD" width="300"/>
           </a>
           The designer created an icon that looks remarkably like golf bag. I love the shading and different textures add depth to the design.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">

           </a>
          </p>

           Since the app only uses a few colors, you can really focus on the different shades. Its so clever and unique to create a pint glass out of the square shape.

           </a>
           I love the weathered look of this design. It reminds of the type of material a tea bag is made out of, which is perfect for this app.

            Pillboxie

           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/pillboxie.jpeg">

           </a>

           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           App icons that look like other objects are simply clever and cool. I found myself examining this app longer than most because its ingenious.
          </p>

           </span>

            My Travel App
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/my-travel-app.png">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1479" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/my-travel-app.png?w=300&amp;h=300" title="My Travel App" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           I love the mix of vibrant colors, clever words on the passport and the smart use of space. The icon is designed to look like a suitcase and it actually does.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>

           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/wall_of_memories.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1489" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/wall_of_memories.jpeg?w=300&amp;h=300" title="Wall_of_Memories" width="300"/>
           </a>

           This app is all about creating a new way to view photos on your smartphone. The icon design mimics the UX using a similar layout thats simple, crisp and beautiful.

           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/wunderlist-logo.png">

           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           The icon design uses three colors and communicates the idea of a to do list thats fun. Users can customize the design of the app once its open, but the outline of the red star remains the same. I love that the red is an integral part of the icon.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/green-kitchen-healthy-vegetarian/id466252999?mt=8">
            Green Kitchen
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/green-kitchen.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1476" height="175" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/green-kitchen.jpeg?w=175&amp;h=175" title="Green Kitchen" width="175"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Its simple. Its beautiful. It simply communicates that this app is about healthy eating.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/postale/id436737730?mt=8">
            Postale
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/postale.png">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1485" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/postale.png?w=300&amp;h=300" title="Postale" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Keen attention to detail rules in this icon design. The postage stamp and weathered envelope edges make for a nostalgic feel and it works for this design.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="http://photoforge2.com/">
            Photoforge 2
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/photoforge-2.png">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1481" height="288" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/photoforge-2.png?w=288&amp;h=288" title="Photoforge 2" width="288"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           While this app is another photo editing app, the design icon is unique. It features a gorgeous image and includes a technical piece with the gridlines.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="http://ramotion.com/applications/pick-n-roll/">
            PicknRoll
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/pick-n-roll.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1499" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/pick-n-roll.jpeg?w=300&amp;h=300" title="pick 'n' roll" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Its cute and it cleverly integrates a basketball outline into the hat, giving the user an indication about what this app is all about.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="http://pollogr.am/">
            Pollogram
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/pollogram.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1484" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/pollogram.jpeg?w=300&amp;h=300" title="Pollogram" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           This icon design makes you curious about what this app does. Its memorable, too.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="http://infinitekind.com/syncspace">
            SyncSpace
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/syncspace.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1487" height="278" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/syncspace.jpeg?w=300&amp;h=278" title="SyncSpace" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           I absolutely love this design. It just makes sense because it communicates that its a creative app used for inputting information.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="http://www.easychefapp.com/">
            EasyChef
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/easychef.png">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1474" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/easychef.png?w=300&amp;h=300" title="EasyChef" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Its got easy in the title of the app, so the design had to be simple. The checkered table cloth detail communicates that its an app for foodies.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/our-wedding-planner/id473527023">
            Wedding Planner
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/wedding-planner-for-ipad.png">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1490" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/wedding-planner-for-ipad.png?w=300&amp;h=300" title="Wedding Planner for iPad" width="300"/>
           </a>
           This app is pretty. What woman wouldnt want to download something with this gorgeous design?
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="http://www.thephotocookbook.com/">
            The Photo Cookbook
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/photo_cookbook.jpeg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1480" height="300" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/photo_cookbook.jpeg?w=300&amp;h=300" title="Photo_Cookbook" width="300"/>
           </a>
           The icon design communicates that its a cooking app, but upon further review, you can see that the food highlighted in the center is actually a polaroid photo. Yet again, another clever app design.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/accordeon/id364741802?mt=8">
            Accordon
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <a href="/blog/images/2012/10/accordeon.png">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1468" height="228" src="https://kinveyposts.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/accordeon.png?w=300&amp;h=228" title="Accordeon" width="300"/>
           </a>
           Heres a design that turns another object into an app icon. The textures, colors and shapes create a beautiful image.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#ffffff;">
            
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:left;">
           Use these stunning designs as inspiration to design your own beautiful app icons. Wed love to hear about some designs that you love  let us know in the comments!
          </p>
