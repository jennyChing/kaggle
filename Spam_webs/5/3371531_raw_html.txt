         Popsicle Stick Furniture.  This stuff is crazy amazing.
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2011-10-20T08:59:18+00:00">
          October 20, 2011

         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5645450" data-link="http://yellowtennessee.com/2011/popsicle-stick-furniture-this-stuff-is-crazy-amazing/" data-summary="" data-title="Popsicle Stick Furniture. This stuff is crazy amazing. ">
         </div>
         <p>
          We made a little trip to Hobby Lobby over the weekend. I swear I could spend hours and hours and hundreds of dollars in that store.  I wanted to get some things so we could do craft projects with Genghis.  Just because mama home schools doesnt mean I dont want cute little things made by him.  Included in my purchase was a box of 1,000 Popsicle sticks.  Surely we can use them for something.
         </p>
         <p>
          Off to Google I go and I stumble upon
          <a href="http://www.riffstick.net/" target="_blank">
           David Hrobowski
          </a>
          .  He uses Popsicle sticks to make furniture!  Wow.
         </p>
         <p>
          Like this dining room table:
          <br/>
          <a href="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Table.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3820" height="333" src="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Table.jpg" title="Table" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Lamps:
          <br/>
          <a href="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/lamp.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3817" height="410" src="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/lamp.jpg" title="lamp" width="273"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Table-and-lamps.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3821" height="410" src="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Table-and-lamps.jpg" title="Table and lamps" width="273"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Sconce:
          <br/>
          <a href="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/sconce.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3819" height="410" src="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/sconce.jpg" title="sconce" width="273"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Mirror:
          <br/>
          <a href="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/mirror.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3818" height="333" src="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/mirror.jpg" title="mirror" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          And even a chair:
          <br/>
          <a href="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/chair.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3816" height="332" src="http://yellowtennessee.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/chair.jpg" title="chair" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I think we will stick with the prerequisite Popsicle house.
         </p>
