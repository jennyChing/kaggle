      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span>
        I have a background in Art and Design and I love to draw, paint, print, craft and generally get messy. I love nothing more than to surround myself with the things that make me happy, colour, texture, fabrics and threads.  I have a love of mid-century design, craft, illustration and a strange obsession with all things Pac-Man. I use recycled materials wherever possible, turning once loved items into something new. I am inspired by nature, fairytales, the woods and the magical creatures that live within them.
       </span>
      </p>
      <br/>
     </div>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <span>
       A few years ago my life took a challenging and unusual turn when I became ill with a balance disorder called uncompensated Labryinthits. This meant I felt severe dizziness all of the time and struggled to focus on everyday tasks I previously took for granted. Unfortunately I had to give up my job and put on hold my aspirations of being an artist to concentrate on my health. During my time in recovery as I adjusted to my balance disorder, I started sewing and made brooches from old jeans and shirts. I gave them to family members as gifts, whatever the occasion, I found it helped with my condition and I just couldn't stop making. I began to turn my character sketches into fabric brooches and embroidery hoop art. Before I knew it I was surrounded by my creations and had run out of space! I decided to start selling them online and Dizzy Miss James was born.
