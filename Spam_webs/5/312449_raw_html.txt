         <h1>
          Christopher Lee Releases a Heavy Metal Album on his 91st Birthday
         </h1>
        </div>
        <span class="blog-time" style="float:left;">
         May 27, 2013
         <span class="lowercase">
          at
         </span>
         7:57 am  by
         <a href="mailto:webmaster@theonering.net">
          admin
         </a>
         -
        </span>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CF6Y1D4/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CF6Y1D4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet">
          <img alt="Amazon.com_ Charlemagne_ Omens of Death_ Christopher Lee_ Music" class="alignright size-full wp-image-72038" height="298" src="http://www-images.theonering.org/torwp/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Amazon.com_-Charlemagne_-Omens-of-Death_-Christopher-Lee_-Music.jpg" width="297"/>
         </a>
         What will you be doing on your 91st birthday? If you are Sir Christopher Lee, you are releasing a heavy metal albumbased on the life of the French ruler Charlemagne.Recorded with Judas Priest guitarist Richie Faulkner,
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CIVOVB8/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00CIVOVB8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
          <em>
           Charlemagne: The Omens of Death
          </em>
         </a>
         is the follow-up to Lees 2010 release
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005EY3W2U/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B005EY3W2U&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
          <em>
           Charlemagne: By the Sword
          </em>
          <em>
           and the Cross
          </em>
         </a>
         . One can only hope to be so active in their Nineties![
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CIVOVB8/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00CIVOVB8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
          Audio CD
         </a>
         ] [
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CF6Y1D4/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CF6Y1D4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
          MP3 Download
         </a>
         ] [
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CIVOVGS/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CIVOVGS&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
          Vinyl
         </a>
         ]
        </p>
        <p>
         Lee was born in Belgravia, Westminster, Englandon May 27th 1922  making him exactly 91 years old today. We all know his work as
         <a href="http://www.theonering.net/torwp/the-hobbit/characters/saruman/" title="Saruman">
          Saruman
         </a>
         in
         <em>
          The Hobbit
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          The Lord of the Rings
         </em>
         films, but did you know Sir Lee holds the world record for most film acting roles ever ! We at TheOneRing.net would like to continue to offer a good pint of well-earned ale as a toast to a true master of his craft! A better portrayal of Saruman there will never be!Cheers!
        </p>
        <p>
         [
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CIVOVB8/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00CIVOVB8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
          Audio CD
         </a>
         ] [

          MP3 Download
         </a>
         ] [
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CIVOVGS/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CIVOVGS&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theoneringnet" target="_blank">
