        <h1 class="entry-title">
         15 Wake Up Early Quotes To Get Your Inspired
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         by Kosio Angelov  Get free productivity training
         <span style="color:#ff8601">
          <span class="tve-leads-two-step-trigger tl-2step-trigger-2814">

         <div class="tve-tl-cnt-wrap">
          <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Article">
           <span itemprop="articleBody">
            <a id="dd_start">
            </a>
            <p>
             Waking up early can be a daunting task, especially when
             <a href="http://blog.highperformancelifestyle.net/how-to-wake-up-early/" target="_blank">
              you first attempt it
             </a>
             . Nevertheless, being an early riser can significantly boost your personal productivity so it is an essential routine.
            </p>
            <p>
             Here are some wake up early quotes that help motivate you when getting up at the break of dawn (or earlier):
             <span id="more-8">
             </span>
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              <em>
               1) Lose an hour in the morning, and you will be all day hunting for it.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Richard Whately
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               2)
               <em>
                
               </em>
               One key to success is to have lunch at the time of day most people have breakfast.
               <em>
                
               </em>
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Robert Brault
             </p>
             <p>
              3)
              <em>
               Life is too short, she panicked, I want more. He nodded slowly, Wake up earlier.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Dr. SunWolf
             </p>
             <p>
              4)
              <em>
               The sun has not caught me in bed in fifty years.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Thomas Jefferson
             </p>
             <p>
              5)
              <em>
               Every morning is a beautiful morning.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Terri Guillemets
             </p>
             <p>
              6) The time just before dawn contains the most energy of all hours of the day. This has helped me become an early riser and an early doer. When I wake to see that its light out already, I feel the world has started without me.
              <br/>
              ~Terri Guillemets
             </p>
             <p>
              7)
              <em>
               It is well to be up before daybreak, for such habits contribute to health, wealth, and wisdom.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Aristotle
             </p>
             <p>
              8)
              <em>
               I would have it inscribed on the curtains of your bed and the walls of your chamber: If you do not rise early you can make progress in nothing.
              </em>
               ~William Pitt Chatham
             </p>
             <p>
              9)
              <em>
               The difference between rising at five and seven oclock in the morning, for forty years, supposing a man to go to bed at the same hour at night, is nearly equivalent to the addition of ten years to a mans life.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Philip Doddridge
             </p>
             <p>
              10) The early morning has gold in its mouth.
              <br/>
              ~Benjamin Franklin
             </p>
             <p>
              11)
              <em>
               Early to bed and early to rise, makes a man healthy, wealthy and wise.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~Benjamin Franklin
             </p>
             <p>
              12)
              <em>
               Every morning in Africa, a Gazelle wakes up. It knows it must run faster than the fastest lion or it will be killed. Every morning a Lion wakes up. It knows it must outrun the slowest Gazelle or it will starve to death. It doesnt matter whether you are a Lion or a Gazelle when the sun comes up, youd better be running.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~ Author Unknown
             </p>
             <p>
              13)
              <em>
               Early rising not only gives us more life in the same number of years, but adds, likewise, to their number; and not only enables us to enjoy more of existence in the same time, but increases also the measure.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~ Caleb C. Colton
             </p>
             <p>
              14)
              <em>
               I never knew a man come to greatness or eminence who lay abed late in the morning.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~ Johnathan Swift
             </p>
             <p>
              15)
              <em>
               Morning is an important time of day, because how you spend your morning can often tell you what kind of day you are going to have.
              </em>
              <br/>
              ~ Lemony Snicket
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             <strong>
              What is your favorite early wake up quote? Share it in the comment section below:
             </strong>
            </p>
