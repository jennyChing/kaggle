          <h1 class="h1__text" itemprop="headline">
           One man's love of vintage Honda motorcycles spawns new museum
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="video featured videoWrapper">
          <style>
           .embed-container { position: relative; padding-bottom: 56.25%; height: 0; overflow: hidden; max-width: 100%; } .embed-container iframe, .embed-container object, .embed-container embed { position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%; }
          </style>
          <div class="embed-container">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/Cmw3LB-lvvg">
           </iframe>
          </div>

         <div class="copy clearfix">
          <p class="byline pull-left">
           <span class="author hcard">
            <span class="author-headshot photo">
             <a class="url" href="/bloggers/jonathon-ramsey/">
              <img class="img-responsive" onerror="this.onerror=null;this.src='http://o.aolcdn.com/os/wheels/img/profile_placeholder.png';" src="http://www.blogsmithmedia.com/www.autoblog.com/v/38aae4bef673b85facb94068826aea94849f8f44/img/editors/johnathon-ramsey.jpg"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="btn-group">
             <a class="active btn btn-ultralight btn-xxs" href="https://plus.google.com/106439108937561172980">
              <span class="text-dark abicon abicon-google-plus">
              </span>
             </a>
             <a class="btn btn-ultralight btn-xxs" href="http://www.autoblog.com/bloggers/jonathon-ramsey/rss.xml">
              <span class="text-dark abicon abicon-feed">
              </span>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="fn clearfix" itemprop="author">
             <a href="/bloggers/jonathon-ramsey/">
              Jonathon Ramsey
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <div itemprop="articleBody">
           In 1977, David Silver got a
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/honda/">
            Honda
           </a>
           SS50 moped - it was the last year of unrestricted mopeds for riders just 16 years of age in the UK. That two-wheeler made him a fan, and starting his
           <a class="injectedLinkmain" href="http://www.autoblog.com/honda/">
            Honda
           </a>
           parts business in 1986 made him a part of the Honda family.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           He started collecting, with the idea that he'd eventually open a museum. During a visit to another amateur collector's trove in Pennsylvania, the Brit got the chance to purchase the 125 bikes the American had gathered, and his museum aspirations hit the fast-forward button.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           He's in the process of building a proper home for the bikes in Suffolk, it should open later this year. There will be everything from the first Cub F engine that people could attach to bicycles in 1952, to two examples of the CB92 Benly Super Sport that showed how fast and how good a 125cc bike could be, to the first Fireblade that dropped in 1992 - our CBR 900RR, to the original Honda Dream. Honda says it could be one of the finest collections outside the company's museum in Japan.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           You can watch Silver tell his story in the video above, and there's more on what's he'll have in an article in Honda's
           <em>
            Dream
           </em>
           magazine.
