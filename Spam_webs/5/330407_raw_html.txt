           <header>
            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Felipe Femur (2013)
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-above">
             Posted on
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2014-10-23T07:24:48+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
              October 23, 2014
             </time>
             by
             <span class="entry-author vcard" itemprop="author" itemptype="http://schema.org/Person" itemscope="">
              <a class="author-link fn nickname url" href="http://www.felipefemur.com/author/david/" itemprop="url" title="View all posts by David">
               <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
                David
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             
             <a class="entry-comments" href="http://www.felipefemur.com/felipe_femur/#comments" title="Felipe Femur (2013)  Comments">
              0 Comments
             </a>
            </div>
            <!-- .entry-meta -->
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
            <p>
             <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Hugyu.FelipeFemur" target="_blank">
              <img alt="felipefemur" class="size-full wp-image-361 aligncenter" height="238" src="http://www.felipefemur.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/felipefemur.jpg" width="925"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <span style="font-weight: 300; color: #333333;">
              Meet Felipe Femur, a skeleton, who lives in a closet. Join Felipe on his quest to slay boredom and find happiness, and read along as he learns something new about himself, meets interesting friends, and participates in a challenging game of risk and reward. Discover several fun levels, while trying to get the highest score you can.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="font-weight: 300; color: #333333; text-align: justify;">
             Designed for children to improve their interest in reading. Serves as a perfect substitute for bed time stories in our modern age of technology.
            </p>
            <p style="color: #555555;">
             <strong style="font-weight: bold; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Felipe Femur Features:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <ul style="color: #555555;">
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Easy and fun to play
             </li>
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Crisp, clean graphics and smooth game play
             </li>
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Several uniquelevels
             </li>
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              A humorous, light hearted story about alonely skeleton and his spooky friends
             </li>
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Help screen and short tutorial
             </li>
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Cute characters and beautiful details
             </li>
             <li style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; color: inherit;">
              Fun sounds and music to immerse you Felipes interesting world
             </li>
            </ul>
           </div>
