     <h5>
      Investment Wallets
     </h5>
     <p>
      Your wallet says a lot about you. Whether plain, colourful, sleek or bulky, the small accessory used to carry your cash is as much of an indication of personal style as a new bag or the latest trainers.
     </p>
     <p>
      While it may seem like a small piece a kit to some, investing in a decent wallet is a savvy decision  a timeless, high quality design can genuinely last you a lifetime.
     </p>
     <p>
      So, if youre thinking of upgrading your wallet to something a little more special, take a look at our top ten luxury picks
     </p>
     <h6>
      1. Scotch &amp; Soda Leather Billfold Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      As the king of Amsterdam couture, Scotch &amp; Soda combines rustic-feel design with rich, modern detailing in this uncomplicated wallet.
     </p>
     <p>
      Inside youll find space for cards, cash and coins, while on the outside, opulent gold branding does all the talking.
     </p>
     <p>
      At 50, this investment piece wont break the bank either.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=QmrL3jZ9kXA&amp;subid=&amp;offerid=236989.1&amp;type=10&amp;tmpid=5135&amp;u1=news&amp;RD_PARM1=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.asos.com%2F&amp;RD_PARM2=pgeproduct.aspx%3Fiid%3D4629320%2526TTP%3D2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        ASOS
       </a>
       and the brands
       <a href="http://www.scotch-soda.com/gb/en/home" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        webstore
       </a>
       .
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=QmrL3jZ9kXA&amp;subid=&amp;offerid=236989.1&amp;type=10&amp;tmpid=5135&amp;u1=news&amp;RD_PARM1=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.asos.com%2F&amp;RD_PARM2=pgeproduct.aspx%3Fiid%3D4629320%2526TTP%3D2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Scotch &amp; Soda Leather Billfold Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/scotch-soda-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h6>
      2. Saint Laurent Babycat Leather Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      This fierce wallet is not to be messed with. Its animal print upper brings a rebellious streak to the sharp and simple black inner, crafted in Italy from smooth leather.
     </p>
     <p>
      With a coin pouch, card slots and space for notes, this statement piece is everything a good wallet should be and more.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=QmrL3jZ9kXA&amp;subid=&amp;offerid=219891.1&amp;type=10&amp;tmpid=7769&amp;u1=news&amp;RD_PARM1=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.harrods.com%2Fproduct%2Fbabycat-leather-wallet%2Fsaint-laurent%2F000000000004619203%3Fcat1%3Dnew-accessories%2526cat2%3Dnew-accessories-men-bags-and-wallets" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Harrods
       </a>
       , priced 310.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=QmrL3jZ9kXA&amp;subid=&amp;offerid=219891.1&amp;type=10&amp;tmpid=7769&amp;u1=news&amp;RD_PARM1=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.harrods.com%2Fproduct%2Fbabycat-leather-wallet%2Fsaint-laurent%2F000000000004619203%3Fcat1%3Dnew-accessories%2526cat2%3Dnew-accessories-men-bags-and-wallets" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Saint Laurent Babycat Leather Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/saint-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h6>
      3. Comme des Garons Zip-Around Leather Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      We cant help but love the soft sky blue shade of this zip-around wallet from Comme des Garons.
     </p>
     <p>
      The gold zip and branding complements the uplifting shade perfectly, while its small form makes it perfect for slipping into your jeans pocket.
     </p>
     <p>
      Prepare for compliments when youre seen whipping this beauty out.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://out.fashionbeans.com/mrportercdg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Mr Porter
       </a>
       , priced 105.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://out.fashionbeans.com/mrportercdg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

     <h6>
      4. Barney And Taylor Tarlton Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      Manchester-born brand Barney and Taylor brings modern living to the forefront in its Tarlton wallet  not only does it offer and unusually large selection of card slots, but it also includes a removable holder in the front section. Were thinking this would be perfect for a travel card or to slip in your pocket separately on a night out.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at the brands
       <a href="http://barneyandtaylor.com/mens/wallets/the-tarlton-484" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        webstore
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.present-london.com/barney--taylor/black-tarlton-wallet/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Present London
       </a>
       , priced 110.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://barneyandtaylor.com/mens/wallets/the-tarlton-484" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Barney And Taylor Tarlton Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/barney-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h6>
      5. Il Bussetto Bi-fold Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      For fans of rich simplicity, Italian artisanal label Il Bussetto is at your service.
     </p>
     <p>
      Crafted from 100 per cent vegetable tanned leather and dyed using natural colours, this bi-fold wallet is as thoughtful as it is striking. Were drawn to this eye-catching ochre shade, but its also available in brown, red and olive green.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://goodhoodstore.com/store/12674" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        The Goodhood Store
       </a>
       , priced 95.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://goodhoodstore.com/store/12674" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Il Bussetto Bi-fold Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/ocrewallet.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h6>
      6. Paul Smith Graphic Garage Interior Billfold Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      Unassuming on the outside, its the interior of this Paul Smith wallet that really pops. Printed with a vintage-feel garage scene, this vessel for cash almost feels like a piece of portable art.
     </p>
     <p>
      You wont be able to resist opening it up and sharing your wallets little secret with all your friends, we guarantee it.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at Paul Smiths
       <a href="http://www.paulsmith.co.uk/uk-en/shop/mens/accessories/wallets/men-s-black-leather-graphic-garage-interior-billfold-and-coin-wallet.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        webstore
       </a>
       , priced 170.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://www.paulsmith.co.uk/uk-en/shop/mens/accessories/wallets/men-s-black-leather-graphic-garage-interior-billfold-and-coin-wallet.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Paul Smith Graphic Garage Interior Billfold Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/paul-smith-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>

     <h6>
      7. Maison Martin Margiela Patent Leather Billfold Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      When we think of Maison Martin Margiela, cutting-edge design is always the first thing that springs to mind.
     </p>
     <p>
      Things are no different where its leather goods are concerned, with this patent wallet customisable by taking a lighter to it (if you dare) to change its colour.
     </p>
     <p>
      At 235, youll want to make sure your hand is steady enough!
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=3539&amp;awinaffid=32880&amp;clickref=news&amp;p=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.selfridges.com%2Fen%2Fmaison-martin-margiela-patent-leather-billfold-wallet_388-83037373-S55UI0062SX8927961%2F%3FpreviewAttribute%3DWhite" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Selfridges
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=2083&amp;awinaffid=32880&amp;clickref=news&amp;p=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.oki-ni.com%2Faccessories%2Fmaison-martin-margiela-11-men-s-white-inflammable-leather-wallet-mmm2337wht.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Oki Ni
       </a>
       .
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=3539&amp;awinaffid=32880&amp;clickref=news&amp;p=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.selfridges.com%2Fen%2Fmaison-martin-margiela-patent-leather-billfold-wallet_388-83037373-S55UI0062SX8927961%2F%3FpreviewAttribute%3DWhite" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Maison Martin Margiela Patent Leather Billfold Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/maison-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>

     <h6>
      8. A.P.C. Large Leather Zip Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      Stepping away from the traditional wallet silhouette, this soft look option from Parisian label A.P.C. brings a touch of originality to the table.
     </p>
     <p>
      Combining its signature minimal design approach with dedication to high-end fabrics, the brand has created something that feels fresh, luxurious and a little bit fun.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://www.endclothing.co.uk/catalog/product/view/id/248654/s/a-p-c-large-leather-zip-wallet-pxaod-f63081-iac/category/7/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        END Clothing
       </a>
       , priced 159.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://www.endclothing.co.uk/catalog/product/view/id/248654/s/a-p-c-large-leather-zip-wallet-pxaod-f63081-iac/category/7/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="A.P.C. Large Leather Zip Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/apc-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>

     <h6>
      9. Smythson Gresham Collection Money Clip Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      In a countdown full of leather wallets, we couldnt let London label Smythson be forgotten. Its line-up of simple and professional looking wallets is perfect for the businessmen and his hectic lifestyle.
     </p>
     <p>
      This brown goatskin option includes a built-in money clip for those who really need to keep their cash strapped down.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Available at
       <a href="http://www.smythson.com/brown-gresham-money-clip-wallet.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Smythson
       </a>
       , priced 190.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <a href="http://www.smythson.com/brown-gresham-money-clip-wallet.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       <img alt="Smythson Gresham Collection Money Clip Wallet" class="white" src="http://static.fashionbeans.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/smythson-wallet-1.jpg"/>
      </a>

      10. Lanvin Midnight Blue Leather Wallet
     </h6>
     <p>
      For a touch of true luxury, look no further than Lanvin. This midnight blue wallet combines tonal textures, cut-out detailing and embossed branding to bring a little sparkle to its quintessentially minimalist form.

     <p>
      Perfect for those who want a departure from the standard black or brown, but still want to keep things classy.
