       Energy Healing for
       <br/>
       Enneagram Type 4 - The Individualist
      </h1>
      <div class="notthere">

      </div>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       <a href="../chakras/open.php" title="How to do chakra meditations">
        Chakra meditations
       </a>
       for
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="../chakras/open.php#chakra1" title="Root chakra meditation">
         Root (1) chakra
        </a>
        feeling more at home in reality
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="../chakras/open.php#chakra3" title="Navel chakra meditation">
         Navel (3) chakra
        </a>
        for assertiveness and self-esteem
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="../chakras/open.php#chakra4" title="Heart chakra meditation">
         Heart (4) chakra
        </a>
        to be more compassionate toward self and others
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <a href="../acupressure/howto.php" title="How to apply acupressure">
        Acupressure
       </a>
       on points
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LU-1" title="Acupressure point Lung 1">
         LU-1
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LU-3" title="Acupressure point Lung 3">
         LU-3
        </a>
        or
        <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LU-9" title="Acupressure point Lung 9">
         LU-9
        </a>
        for internal emptiness, making contact to one's inner worth, sadness
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LI-4" title="Acupressure point Large Intestine 4">
         LI-4
        </a>
        for letting go of grief
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LIV-3" title="Acupressure point Liver 3">
         LIV-3 (reduce)
        </a>
        for counteracting depression and letting go of feelings
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#SP-6" title="Acupressure point Spleen 6">
         SP-6
        </a>
        to heal effects of too much thinking on the body, smoothing the flow of emotions
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <hr/>
      <h3>
       Feeling at home in reality
      </h3>
      <p>
       <a href="type4.php">
        Fours
       </a>
       tend to escape reality into a world of idealized fantasy; this weakens their sense of feeling at
home in reality. Their tendency to feel unappreciated can also increase this sense of alienation.
Strengthening the
       <a href="../chakras/open.php#chakra1" title="Root chakra meditation">
        Root chakra
       </a>
       can help to repair the damage caused by the Four's lack of presence in the physical world.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Self-esteem
      </h3>
      <p>
       As Fours often focus on what they perceive to be their defects, they tend to lack self-confidence
and to have low self-esteem. The resulting lack of assertiveness can be counteracted by doing
       <a href="../chakras/open.php#chakra3" title="Navel chakra meditation">
        Navel chakra
       </a>
       meditations. This will also help with the Four's tendency to withdraw.
      </p>
      <h3>
       The heart
      </h3>
      <p>
       Opening the Heart chakra can help Fours to be more compassionate toward themselves and
others. A strenghthened
       <a href="../chakras/open.php#chakra4" title="Heart chakra meditation">
        Heart chakra
       </a>
       helps them to connect better to people and to cease obsessing about themselves.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Sadness and depression
      </h3>
      <p>
       Fours are known for their tendency to hold onto sadness and melancholy; they frequently suffer
from depressive episodes. The acupressure points discussed below are useful for treating these
difficulties.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Self-worth
      </h3>
      <p>
       The acupressure points
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LU-1" title="Acupressure point Lung 1">
        LU-1
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LU-3" title="Acupressure point Lung 3">
        LU-3
       </a>
       and
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LU-9" title="Acupressure point Lung 9">
        LU-9
       </a>
       are useful for treating the damage that is done to the body from feeling too much sadness. They
are also helpful for increasing resistance to such feelings and for increasing a sense of self-worth.
      </p>
      <p>
       As the Four's lack of a firm a sense of identity and feelings of diminished self-worth are partly
rooted in a feeling of internal emptiness, working with the appropriate acupressure points can be
helpful for treating the underlying problem.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Letting go of feelings
      </h3>
      <p>
       Fours tend to hold onto their feelings, especially feelings of melancholy and sadness.
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LI-4" title="Acupressure point Large Intestine 4">
        LI-4
       </a>
       is especially indicated for letting go of such feelings, particularly feelings of loss.
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LIV-3" title="Acupressure point Liver 3">
        LIV-3
       </a>
       is also a useful point to work with when it is necessary to release pent up emotions of all sorts.
As the inability to let go of one's feelings is a major cause of depression, working with
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#LIV-3" title="Acupressure point Liver 3">
        LIV-3
       </a>
       is an especially beneficial point to access.
      </p>
      <p>
       Fours find working with point
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#SP-6" title="Acupressure point Spleen 6">
        SP-6
       </a>
       to be beneficial for several reasons. Like LIV-3,
it has some effect in smoothing the flow of emotions, but it doesn't require the specific
acupressure technique (reducing) that is used for LIV-3;
it can simply be pressed. Also,
       <a href="../acupressure/points.php?type=4#SP-6" title="Acupressure point Spleen 6">
        SP-6
       </a>
       is indicated for counteracting the fatigue that is often caused by too much thinking, whether
it be caused by worrying or endless fantasizing.
      </p>
