     <h2 class="title">
      Clevelanders in Hotpants, Its a Beautiful Sight
     </h2>
     <div id="stats">
      <span>
       24 August 2011
      </span>
      <span>
      </span>
      <span>
       <span class="dsq-postid" rel="6018 http://rustwire.com/?p=6018">
        No Comment
       </span>
      </span>
     </div>
     <div class="entry clearfloat">
      <p>
       Whoa. Cleveland is getting pretty hip. Check out this video from
       <a href="http://www.vimeo.com/danielkrivenki">
        Dan Krivenki
       </a>
       of
       <a href="http://www.cranksetrides.com/">
        Crank Set Rides
       </a>
       :
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe frameborder="0" height="225" src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/28079671?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0" width="400">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://vimeo.com/28079671">
        Hot Pants Bike Ride  Cleveland  8-20-2011
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Ive been wanting to write about Crank Set for a while. This organization, led by Dan and his girlfriend Lindsey Bower, has been hosting themed bike rides in Cleveland for more than a year now.
      </p>
      <p>
       Earlier this summer, they hosted a nerd ride. Let me tell you, it was a fun experience seeing drivers try to make sense of 100 something adults dressed as nerds riding around on bicycles in the middle of a Saturday.
      </p>
      <p>
       Around Halloween, they host a zombie ride. Lindsey also organized a womens-only ride, appropriately held once a month, called Two Wheels and Heels.
      </p>
      <p>
       Crank Set  Lindsey and Dan  are doing a great public service for Cleveland. They have created a series of fun events that are green, healthy and super fun. They are helping make bicycling cool. They are helping make bicycling visible. Soon Crank Set will be a nonprofit. Lindsey is even reducing her work load to 30 hours at her job so she can devote more time to it.
      </p>
      <p>
       These two make me proud to live in Cleveland! I think this is something that would be fun and would work in many of our cities.
      </p>
      <p>
       -A.S.
      </p>
