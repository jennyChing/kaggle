       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Rice Salad
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://zrecipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/rice-salad-zr.jpg">
          <img alt="Rice Salad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62" height="491" src="http://zrecipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/rice-salad-zr.jpg" width="585"/>
         </a>
         This recipe,
         <strong>
          Rice Salad
         </strong>
         , can be a good replacement for a bowl of soup! If you want, you can use this salad as a side dish for cold meat or cold fish. Its quick to make and very nice! Children will surely love it.
        </p>
        <div style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-4947814141550636" data-ad-slot="8616354987" style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
         This recipe serves four people. You can change the amount of ingredients proportionally to your needs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           1 1/3 cups (10.58 ounces /300 grams) of rice
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           1/2 cups ( 3.5 ounces / 100 grams) of peas
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           Vinegar, salt and pepper to your taste
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           3.5 ounces (100 grams) of ham
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           2 peppers or baked carrots
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          How to make Rice Salad:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           First, you should cook the rice, season it with salt and dont forget to stir it so it doesnt stick. It should cook for about 15 minutes.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           After its cooked, remove the rice from the heat and clean it with water until it loses its starch.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           Then, slice the ham into small pieces.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           Put the rice, the peas, the ham and the chopped carrot or peppers in a container (it can be a bowl).
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           Season it at your taste and mix the ingredients.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           You may decorate the dish with boiled eggs.
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="-webkit-text-stroke-color: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0.15px;">
           The rice salad is ready to be served!
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
