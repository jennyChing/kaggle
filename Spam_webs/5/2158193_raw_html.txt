            I suppose this is give-or-take the 900th comic. Because of the navigation snafu, we jumped ahead a bit in the numbering. But I also know Ive occasionally done multiple comics for single updates in the past. Who knows? It really shouldnt be that complicated. Lets just move on and make 1,000 an actual milestone. Dont think about it too much. Wibbly-wobbly timey-wimey stuff.
