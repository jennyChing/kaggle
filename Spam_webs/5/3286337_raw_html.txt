In order to achieve these goals, I also need different goals that will support my running goals and give me the
         <a href="http://www.v8juice.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.v8juice.com', 'morning energy');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

         that I need to get everything done.
        </p>
        <p>
         I decided on three goals that support my big goals. These are the daily things that I will do that will help me cross the finish line (literally cross the finish line, in my case) and successfully complete my huge 2015 goals.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3 Ways I Will Reach My Goals in 2015:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Get regular activity each day.
          </strong>
          I dont mean running everyday or a hard workout. I know the importance of proper rest to allow my body to repair and rejuvenate. On my rest days from running, I will do activities that further support this. It could be a light workout, strength training, yoga, biking or anything else that Id like to do. It may even be something as simple as walking my dog around the block. I will make sure that I get intentional movement each day.

          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Start each day with a nutritious breakfast.
          </strong>
          I always start the day with a nutritious breakfast. It truly is the most important meal of the day for me. Eating a good breakfast each morning helps me start the day off on the right foot and makes me more likely to stay on track with my eating the rest of the day.          </li>
        </ul>

         <li>
          <strong>
           Maintain a healthy weight.
          </strong>
          Over the years, I have figured that I have an ideal weight range at which I run very well. My goal this year is to stay within this weight range. I dont vary much from it on a normal basis anyway and its well within the healthy range for my height and build but a few pounds make a difference in how I run. In addition to starting the day with a nutritious breakfast, to maintain a healthy weight I will also need to eat healthy snacks and a healthy lunch and dinner. Not only does this help me maintain a healthy weight, I just feel better too and have the energy and motivation to get my workouts done.
