                              <h1 class="pt-title">
                               <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/2014/03/garlicky-avocado-grilled-cheese-with-tomato-pesto/" title="Garlicky Avocado Grilled Cheese with Tomato Pesto">
                                Garlicky Avocado Grilled Cheese with Tomato Pesto
                               </a>
                              </h1>
                             </div>
                             <div class="metabar">
                              <div class="metabar-pad">
                               <em>
                                <time class="date time published updated sc" datetime="2014-03-19T02:00:43+00:00">
                                 March 19, 2014
                                </time>
                                
                                <span class="post-comments sc">

                            <p>
                             <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
                              Id have to admit, this post is all about the goo. And maybe a little avocado to compliment the garlicky plot.
                             </span>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-040-2.jpg">
                              <img alt="Garlicky Avocado Tomat Pesto Grilled Cheese Sandwich" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10280" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-040-2.jpg" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
                              I love cheese. And if youve followed this blog for a nanosecond youd know why. Maybe thanks to
                              <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/2012/08/blt-grilled-cheese-recipe/" target="_blank">
                               this
                              </a>
                              and
                              <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/2013/04/strawberry-bruschetta-grilled-cheese/" target="_blank">
                               this
                              </a>
                              and
                              <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/2013/04/cobb-grilled-cheese/" target="_blank">
                               this.
                              </a>
                             </span>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             We all have our weaknesses.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             But recently Ive felt a disconnect. I felt somethings been missing. Ive missed my grilled cheese.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Maybe its because I know that Grilled Cheese Month is nearly here. So I started brainstorming some tasty combos I hope youll love. Hey, tell me, what do you love, anyway? The comments below are a great place to say.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-022-2.jpg">
                              <img alt="Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-022-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10284" height="400" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-022-2.jpg" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-022-2.jpg">
                              <span id="more-10274">
                              </span>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-044-2.jpg">
                              <img alt="Garlicky Avocado Tomat Pesto Grilled Cheese Sandwich" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10277" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-044-2.jpg" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             This year, Ill be judging the
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/2013/04/spinach-and-artichoke-grilled-cheese-and-grilled-cheese-academy-contest/" target="_blank">
                              Grilled Cheese Showdown
                             </a>
                             againkeep your eyes peeled for a big announcement where youll be able to join the party. Coming soon.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             But really my weakness lies in my favorite pitted fruit of the gods, the almighty avocado.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             The key to this recipe is the herbed up garlic butter. Its pretty much a must have on any savory griller.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             So lets make a decadent cheesy avocado sandwich to seal the cheesy deal. I did remember the cheese, right?
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Im such a romantic that way.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <strong>
                              About the recipe:
                             </strong>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             The key to this recipe is the butter mix. Adding garlic, onion and pretty much any herb to give a little jump to the crust is a do not miss. The butter mix makes the bread pieces crispy, crusty and perfectly garlicked that will only showcase the creamy center.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             When it comes to store bought sun dried tomato pesto, I use
                             <a href="http://www.delallo.com/products/sun-dried-tomato-pesto" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                              this store bought brand
                             </a>
                             from Delallo. But if you want to make your own,
                             <a href="http://www.bakeyourday.net/sun-dried-tomato-pesto/" target="_blank">
                              Cassies recipe
                             </a>
                             <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                             </span>
                             is my go-to-get-the-delicious-flavor done recipe to make. I went the easy route this way around and used the store bought brand.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             I used Wisconsin Cheeses Provolone, a cheese that is extra melty. The proof is in the pudding aka these photos. If youre planning on using a provolone yourself, be sure to allow for extra post-cooking melting time aka once you pull from the pan. Because this cheese will keep melting. And depending on how much you use, it could look neat and tidy, or like a gooey, delicious, mouthwatering mess. I errored on the mess. But, whatever. It tastes divine.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-042-2.jpg">
                              <img alt="Garlicky Avocado Tomat Pesto Grilled Cheese Sandwich" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10279" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-042-2.jpg" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
                             <link href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avocado-Tomato-Pesto-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich-foodiecrush.com-042-2.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
                             <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
                              Garlicky Avocado Grilled Cheese with Tomato Pesto
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSClear">
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSTopRight">
                              <div class="ERSSavePrint">
                               <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
                                <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/easyrecipe-print/10274-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                 Print
                                </a>
                               </span>
                              </div>
                             </div>
                             <div class="divERSHeadItems">
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSIngredients">
                              <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
                               Ingredients
                              </div>
                              <ul>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                3 tablespoons salted butter
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                2 teaspoons onion powder
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1 teaspoon garlic powder
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1 teaspoon kosher salt
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1 teaspoon dried thyme
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                2 slices whole wheat white bread, or bread of your choice
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1 tablespoon sun dried tomato pesto
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 avocado, mashed
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                4-5 slices provolone cheese
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1 teaspoon olive oil
                               </li>
                              </ul>
                              <div class="ERSClear">
                              </div>
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSInstructions">
                              <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
                               Instructions
                              </div>
                              <ol>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                In a small bowl, combine the butter, onion powder, garlic powder, kosher salt, dried thyme and mash together.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                Spread onion garlic butter mix on one side of each slice of bread, then flip the bread buttered side down.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                Layer the bottom slice of bread with a few slices of provolone, then add the mashed avocado and the sun dried tomato pesto. Top with the remaining provolone cheese and the other piece of bread. Spread with the butter mixture.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                Place a large non-stick pan on a burner over low and add the olive oil and a bit of the extra butter mixture. Add the sandwich and cover with a lid. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until golden, checking in to see that bread is slowly cooking and doesn't get too brown.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                Flip sandwich to the other side and cook for 2-3 minutes or until bread is toasty golden and the cheese has melted. The second side will cook faster than the first so watch it carefully. Enjoy hot.
                               </li>
                              </ol>
