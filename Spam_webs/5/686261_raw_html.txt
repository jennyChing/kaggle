          <img alt="" class=" size-full wp-image-49558 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://www.tsbmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/wildban.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          In my younger and more vulnerableyears, I used to think that only some girls were really into sex.
         </p>
         <p>
          Especially if Id talk to my buddies who were really good at getting girls. A lot of them, were maybe able to attract a girl, but they could never keep them around. And even though they might sleep with them once, theyd usually get bored.
         </p>
         <p>
          But more than anything, as good as they were at picking up girls and getting numbers, they didnt have girls begging for sex.
         </p>
         <p>
          Some of them would even complain that they couldnt get girls to give them blowjobs, among other things.
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats because they werent bringing out the girlswild side.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was the opposite. When I was younger, I wasnt very good at the pick up- I didnt get good at that until after college. However, Ive always been able to get girls coming back after sex, and Ive never been left wanting for more during the sex itself.
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats because I acknowledged early on that every girl has a wild side.
         </p>
         <p>
          Sure, youll just have naturally better sex with some girls, but the truth is that most girls, if even secretly, want to get as crazy in the bedroom as you do.
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres how to make it happen.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
          Lead by example.
         </h1>
         <p>
          You have to let women know that its safe to get sexy with you.
         </p>
         <p>
          And theres a really monkey-see-monkey-do aspect to this.
         </p>
         <p>
          For example, if youre expecting blowjobs, be prepared to go down on her (and make it good).
         </p>
         <p>
          Theres also an attitude you need to have. You need to be in the right mind-set and treat her the way you want to be treated.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is of course baring anything extreme that you might seriously need to discuss and have a safe-word for before hand.
         </p>
         <p>
          But in general, if you dont censor or restrain yourself, youre going to see her warm up to your way of thinking.
         </p>
         <p>
          Be yourself in the bedroom.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
          Build up and anticipation.
         </h1>
         <p>
          Think of a womans sex drive like a roller-coaster.
         </p>
         <p>
          The first part is this huge build up, before tumbling into an wildly exciting ride.
         </p>
         <p>
          And the longer the climb to the top is, the more intense the rest of the ride will be.
         </p>
         <p>
          Foreplay an oral sex are all well and good, but you always want to be taking your time to get there.
         </p>
         <p>
          You have to make her want it. And
          <em>
           wanting
          </em>
          always comes from
          <em>
           waiting
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Subtly can be your friend at times.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are ways to do this outside the bedroom as well (which well get to), so that by the time youre alone, shes already pouncing on you.
         </p>
         <p>
          But for example, working your way down from her lips to between her legs and then back up just before giving her oral sex and then maybe back down and not quite giving her oral sex yet and then maybe giving her oral sex for a bit before going back up for a little bit, and so on, isnt a bad start.
         </p>
         <p>
          Pacing, build up, creating anticipation, making her wait and making her
          <em>
           want
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Again, think of that climb to the top of the roller-coaster. The longer it goes on for, the faster it gets your heart beating.
         </p>
         <p>
          So the more you put in, the more youll get back.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
          Ask and encourage.
         </h1>
         <p>
          Once youve got her wound up, you still need to let her know that its safe and desirable for her to do certain things and to go wild on you.
         </p>
         <p>
          You will be amazed what saying things like;
         </p>
         <h3>
          I want you to _____ 
         </h3>
         <h3>
          I love it when you _____ 
         </h3>
         <p>
          And, one of my favourites;
         </p>
         <h3>
          You can do whatever you want.
         </h3>
         <p>
          Which is great, because its totally non-specific, and may not lead to her doing anything different right that second, but it makes her completely comfortable doing just about anything with you. It leads to her feeling comfortable to do all things shes always secretly wanted to.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
          Give her an orgasm.
         </h1>
         <p>
          An orgasm is like an affirmation that a girl made the right decision to have sex with you.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its also an affirmation that she made the right choice getting wild with you; that it was the right thing to do to show you that side of her.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its what gets her literally addicted to being that way with you.
         </p>
         <p>
          So, job one is always making her cum. Which shouldnt be that surprising. Think about the lengths youll go to for a girl to make you cum. Now intensify that be the merit that not everyone you have sex with can do that to you.
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats how valuable a non-self produced orgasm is to women.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is how you flip a script too by the way, because now shell be trying to turn you on as often as possible so that you can keep making her cum.
         </p>
         <p>
          Generosity goes a long way. Take care of your lady friend.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
          Dont just stop at the bedroom.
         </h1>
         <p>
          Dont be afraid to limit your dirty talk to just in the bedroom.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its easy to keep things hot and heavy in the bedroom, but you dont want things to fizzle out in the time in-between.
         </p>
         <p>
          Dont find yourself getting into a mundane
          <a class="kblinker" href="http://www.tsbmag.com/texting-girls" title="More about texting ">
           texting
          </a>
          cycle.
         </p>
         <p>
          For every hows your day? you should be sending a seriously, cant wait to get you alone later.
         </p>
         <p>
          And use R rated language. Especially in text. It has much more of an impact. It sticks out and separates you from every other text in her phone.
         </p>
         <p>
          This will do some of your build up work for you. But you want her to be thinking about you when youre not around.
         </p>
         <p>
          Call back to things she did or that you did that she loved.
         </p>
         <p>
          All of these things together, make you have instant sexual chemistry, and ultimately, thats the most important thing.
         </p>
         <p>
          If a woman feels that she can be herself with you, and let loose, and you validate that decision, youll get her doing things with you that shesnever done with anyone before.
         </p>
