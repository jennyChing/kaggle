                <h2>
                 About Me
                </h2>
                <div class="widget-content">
                 <a href="https://www.blogger.com/profile/01049853988877974310">
                  <img alt="My Photo" class="profile-img" height="80" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XZzberWDOIg/TyB1i7UdFRI/AAAAAAAAACI/AMv2btN_ixw/s80/man%2Band%2Bsea.jpg" width="53"/>
                 </a>
                 <dl class="profile-datablock">
                  <dt class="profile-data">
                   <a class="profile-name-link g-profile" href="https://www.blogger.com/profile/01049853988877974310" rel="author" style="background-image: url(//www.blogger.com/img/logo-16.png);">
                    symphonyforlove
                   </a>
                  </dt>
                  <dd class="profile-textblock">
                   I think  that meditative and inspiring  thoughts and words have  the power to start a fire in a person's life. It can alter the way we see ourselves. It can change the way we see the world.
It dares us to break free from the safe strategies of the cautious mind; it calls to us, like the wild geese, as Mary Oliver would say, from an open sky. It is a magical art, and always has been -- a making of language spells designed to open our eyes, open our doors and welcome us into a bigger world, one of possibilities we may never have dared to dream of.

Ive found that the best way to reconnect with how beautiful life can be is to give something away. It doesnt have to be anything material. It can be a compliment, a smile, or a positive intention or thought for someone else. Sometimes the greatest gift you can give is sincerely giving your presence.
                  </dd>

                <h2 class="title">
                 The language of Beauty
                </h2>
                <div class="widget-content">
                 <center>
                  <img g="" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_HijhRK2BkjA/SojngaRe6YI/AAAAAAAAApM/5gApw51zwXI/s400/nature+oil+paintings.jpg" style="border:6px ridge black" width="171"/>
                  <font color="springgreen">
                   <b>
                    <font size="4">
                     Its amazing to me how when I stop the incessant mental chatter, and actually just see things, I am blown away by how beautiful they are. The trees swaying, the leaves blowing around on the ground, the waves crashing on the shore. The simplest of things have the most profound beauty.
                    </font>
                   </b>
                  </font>
                 </center>

                  <font size="4">
                   <font color="lemonchiffon">
                    My task which I am trying to achieve is, by the power of the written word, to make you hear, to make you feel  it is, before all, to make you see. That  and no more, and it is everything. If I succeed, you shall find there according to your deserts: encouragement, consolation, fear, charm  all you demand; and, perhaps, also that glimpse of truth for which you have forgotten to ask.
                    <font color="red">
                     Joseph Conrad
