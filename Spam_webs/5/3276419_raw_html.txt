        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         skincare products im loving right now.
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-08-14T13:00:05+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          14 August, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://girlmeetslife.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Gracie
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://girlmeetslife.com/2013/08/skincare-products-im-loving-right-now/#comments">
           5 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         If you havent noticed, Im a
         <em>
          really
         </em>
         big believer in taking care of your skin by keeping it
         <a href="http://girlmeetslife.com/2013/06/my-skincare-routine-number-one-beauty-tip/">
          moisturized
         </a>
         . Im constantly trying new lotions, face moisturizers, and any skincare products I can get my (well-moisturized) hands on. Here are some new ones I recently tried that Im loving right now.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Eucerin
         </strong>
         has been my go-to brand of lotion for years. Nothing leaves my skin feeling softer and looking healthier! Thats why I was so excited when they sent me a handful of their new products to try.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="375" src="http://girlmeetslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/IMG_1635.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; display: inline; padding-right: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I loved each and every one of these, but my favorites were
         <a href="http://www.eucerinus.com/products/soothe/eucerin-original-healing-soothing-repair-lotion.html">
          <strong>
           Eucerin Original Healing Soothing Repair Lotion
          </strong>
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.eucerinus.com/products/face-care/eucerin-daily-protection-spf-30-moisturizing-face-lotion.html">
          <strong>
           Eucerin Daily Protection Moisturizing Face Lotion
          </strong>
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         The Soothing Repair Lotion goes on so smoothly and feels great on my skin. I apply right after I get out of the shower and felt a difference in my skin after just a few uses.
        </p>
        <p>
