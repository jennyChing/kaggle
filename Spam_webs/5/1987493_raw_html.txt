       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Flirting Tips for our Female Online Dating Members
       </h1>
       <div class="title-meta">
        September 26th 2014
        <span class="by-author">
         by
         <span class="author vcard">

       <p>
        <img alt="online dating flirting" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-471" height="350" src="http://blog.adults.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/online-dating-flirting.jpg" width="687"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        You have found a potential date through
        <a href="http://adults.co.uk" title="online dating">
         online dating
        </a>
        and now you want to make a good impression in person. The best way to do this is to speak the universal language of interest in the opposite sex  flirting. When done the right way, flirting can be a fun and powerful way to open the lines of communication between two people. Whether youre looking for a full blown romantic relationship, or just some casual fun, knowing how to flirt effectively makes it easier to capture the attention of your online dating match
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Why flirting is important
         <br/>
        </b>
        Men may be interested in getting to know you better, but they are often hesitant to make the first move. This is because of a fear of rejection. Even if you have gotten to know each other online first, it can still be a daunting time when you meet up for real. Women must show that they are open and approachable. Flirting begins with a eye contact. When your eyes meet for the first time, you send unspoken messages through your body language, the expressions on your face and if youre talking, the tone of your voice. This tells him if the odds are in his favour for making a connection with you, or if you may make him look silly by turning cold.
        <span id="more-464">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         How to flirt online
         <br/>
        </b>
        When
        <a href="http://blog.adults.co.uk/online-dating-profiles/" title="online dating profiles">
         setting up your online dating profile
        </a>
        , use photos that show you as yourself, someone who knows themselves but also knows how to enjoy life. Use pictures that show you involved in activities in which you are smiling and having a great time. When you make an online connection, allow the man to take the lead. When its your turn, ask him to talk about himself and then complement him. Not too much because hell think youre phony. After youve broken the ice a bit, use humour when flirting. A great way to do this tastefully is to talk about any common interests or hobbies you may have. Take a quick peek at his profile so you will know more about him. Keep it light-hearted and fun above all.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         How to flirt from the moment you enter the room
         <br/>
        </b>
        So you are now ready to meet your online dating match for the first time in an agreed location. When your eyes meet, smile at him and stand with an open posture that says you are interested and approach him to greet him. Remember you have already got to know each other to a certain level so dont be nervous, this will reflect badly in some cases. You may want to gently play with your hair. This move is a powerful message that men generally respond well to. Make sure that whatever setting you have chosen for your date, that it allows for you to be close and get to know each other better. There is nothing worse than a loud or lively setting for a first date.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Let him lead
         <br/>
        </b>
        Allow him to take the lead as men tend to back off from pushy women. Be responsive to his questions and answer him in ways that keep the conversation going. Yes and no answers send the message that youre not interested, but when you give answers that allow him to add more, hes likely to take things further. If he tells a joke, laugh with him and make eye contact. Dont make the mistake of trying too hard or showing obvious interest as men do enjoy the thrill of the chase. On the flip side to this though, dont be too distant as he may be confused as to why you ever agreed to a date after chatting through online dating for so long. A few subtle looks and flirtatious comments should do nicely to keep him interested.
       </p>
       <p>
        Avoid saying things that may be offensive. You can share jokes and lightly tease, but keep it classy. If youre using a webcam, dress appropriately, but lean forward when you are talking. This will signal that you have an interest. If he leans in you can play the inching closer game and both have a good laugh over it. Flirting should be fun and leave both parties feeling happier when the conversation ends.
       </p>
