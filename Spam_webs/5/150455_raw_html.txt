        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Pumpkin Yogurt Cake
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-09-26T22:56:09+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          09/26

         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6820276" data-link="http://www.giverecipe.com/pumpkin-yogurt-cake.html" data-summary="" data-title="Pumpkin Yogurt Cake">
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Pumpkin Yogurt Cake | giverecipe.com | #cake #pumpkin #yogurt #greekyogurt #fall" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8152" height="1027" src="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-1.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Pumpkin Yogurt Cake
         </strong>
         is another recipe that shows my pumpkin obsession these days. Well, I can honestly say that I dont have any problem with my pumpkin craziness since I do love baking with it. Everything started after I made
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/homemade-fresh-pumpkin-puree.html" target="_blank" title="Homemade Fresh Pumpkin Puree">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff6600;">
            Fresh Pumpkin Puree
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         . It is so good and I love its flavor and vibrant color so much that I can put it in anything you can imagine. Ive made pancakes, cornbread, cookies and chocolate chip bread using this puree so far. And I made a classic yogurt cake with a pumpkin twist this time.
         <span id="more-8151">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Pumpkin Yogurt Cake | giverecipe.com | #cake #pumpkin #yogurt #greekyogurt #fall" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8153" height="1027" src="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-2.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         I seved this Pumpkin Yogurt Cake with a caramel sauce, which perfectly matches with the pumpkin flavor. Well, for me there are some ingredients that go wonderfully with pumpkin and caramel is one of these. The others are chocolate and spices like cinnamon, cardamom, ginger and nutmeg. So Im mostly pairing pumpkin with these if Im making something sweet. Have you tried anything savory with it? If you havent yet, check out my
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/roasted-chicken-drumsticks-pumpkin.html" target="_blank" title="Roasted Chicken with Pumpkin">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff6600;">
            Roasted Chicken with Pumpkin
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         and vegetarian
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/pumpkin-stew-lentils-chickpeas.html" target="_blank" title="Pumpkin Stew with Lentils and Chickpeas">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff6600;">
            Pumpkin Stew with Lentils and Chickpeas
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         to have an idea.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-3.jpg">
          <img alt="Pumpkin Yogurt Cake | giverecipe.com | #cake #pumpkin #yogurt #greekyogurt #fall" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8154" height="1027" src="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-3.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Back to this divine pumpkin bundt cake, the first word I can use to describe it is soft, fluffy or airy. Well, you get the idea, almost melting in your mouth. And it is super moist due to the pumpkin puree and greek yogurt inside it. Id love to thank Chungah of Damn Delicious for her recipe,
         <a href="http://damndelicious.net/2012/10/31/pumpkin-bundt-cake-with-pumpkin-glaze-bundtamonth-and/" target="_blank" title="Pumpkin Bundt Cake with Pumpkin Glaze">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff6600;">
            Pumpkin Bundt Cake with Pumpkin Glaze
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         . This is absolutely a wonderful fall cake, so good that I want to make it any time of the year; just as she suggests. I just slightly changed her recipe. I reduced the amount of sugar, if you like your cake very sweet, use  cup sugar.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Pumpkin Yogurt Cake | giverecipe.com | #cake #pumpkin #yogurt #greekyogurt #fall" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8155" height="1027" src="http://www.giverecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pumpkin-Yogurt-Cake-4.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         I excluded the pumpkin glaze from her recipe and I simply used caramel sauce instead. You can use whatever glaze you like. If you arent sure what to make as a dessert for Thanksgiving, try this Pumpkin Yogurt Cake and watch how it is gone in seconds.
        </p>

           <span itemprop="author">

           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            8
           </span>
          </div>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 cups all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tablespoon cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons vanilla powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 and  cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 large eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup
            <a href="http://www.giverecipe.com/homemade-fresh-pumpkin-puree.html" target="_blank" title="Homemade Pumpkin Puree">
             pumpkin puree
            </a>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup Greek yogurt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Caramel sauce for glaze
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 F (175C).
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Butter a 12-cup bundt pan and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl, combine flour, cinnamon, baking powder, salt and vanilla powder.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a seperate bowl, beat butter and sugar for a few minutes until fluffy.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Beat in eggs, one at a time. Mix until well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Whisk in pumpkin and yogurt.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Slowly add the flour mixture and stir with a spatula just until incorporated.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pour the batter into the bundt pan and bake for 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from the oven and let cool for about 15 minutes. Remove the cake from the pan on a wire rack.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Drizzle caramel sauce over the top of the cake right before serving.
           </li>
          </ol>
