         <p>
          Although it might have seemed as if
          <em>
           <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Bayonetta-2-Nintendo-Wii-U/dp/B00DC7G1WE/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=videogamenetwork-20&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=I34EZYJHIIRQNZ66&amp;creativeASIN=B00DC7G1WE">
            Bayonetta 2
           </a>
          </em>
          had been out for over a week now thanks to its stellar reviews and positive news all around,
          <em>
           Bayonetta 2
          </em>
          has officially hit store shelves today Oct 24. The first review hit the net via EDGE over a week ago with a shockingly perfect score of 10; many other sites followed suit awarding the game with perfect or near perfect scores.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Bayonetta 2
          </em>
          is currently averaging a 91 on
          <a href="http://www.metacritic.com/game">
           Metacritic
          </a>
          , only behind an iPhone game
          <em>
           Banner Saga
          </em>
          and a rehash version of last years PS3 game,
          <em>
           <a href="https://www.amazon.com/The-Last-Us-Remastered-PlayStation-4/dp/B00JK00S0S?psc=1/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=videogamenetwork-20&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=45O467E2WOKYJONP&amp;creativeASIN=B00JK00S0S">
            The Last Of Us
           </a>
          </em>
          . With that said, were not taking anything away from
          <em>
           The Last of Us
          </em>
          on the PS4 its technically the highest rated game this year on Metacritic, but its also not a new game  weve seen it and beat it last year on the PS3, which is why we believe that
          <em>
           Bayonetta 2
          </em>
          is the top dog thus far. What do you guys think? Will
          <em>
           Super Smash Bros.
          </em>
          Wii U get anywhere near this score?
         </p>
