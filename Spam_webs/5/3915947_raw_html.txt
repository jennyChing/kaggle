       <p>
        <a href="http://cdn.zoknowsgaming.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Sony-Vaio-L-Series.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-32927 alignleft" height="162" src="http://cdn.zoknowsgaming.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Sony-Vaio-L-Series-550x385.jpg" title="Sony-Vaio-L-Series" width="231"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Sony wants you to know that you can be both productive and entertaining with their new Sony VAIO Series All-in-One desktop. This powerhouse series lineup features several intriguing features that are sure to make anyone take a second look. Perhaps the most noted is the inclusion of 3D technology. According to the press release, The Sony VAIO L Series features a full HD 3D display and lets you enjoy 3D Blu-ray Disc movies and PC games in 3D. You can also connect to a PlayStation3 entertainment system via HDMI and enjoy 3D gaming on the 24-inch full HD display. One pair of USB rechargeable, active shutter 3D glasses are included with the L Series desktop.
       </p>
       <p>
        As we said earlier, Sony thinks you can be productive with the VAIO L series, as well as get some entertainment from the desktop. On the productive and power side of things, users can expect:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Quad Core Intel Core i7 processor for visibly smart performance at its best (select models)
        </li>
        <li>
         Windows 7 Professional (select models)
        </li>
        <li>
         Windows 7 Home Premium (select models)
        </li>
        <li>
         Dedicated NVIDIA graphics with 1GB VRAM (select models)
        </li>
        <li>
         Sleek and sexy All-in-One design from any angle
        </li>
        <li>
         Compact All-in-One design, wireless keyboard and mouse offer a clutter-free workspace
        </li>
        <li>
         Wait less and transfer data faster with USB 3.0 technology
        </li>
        <li>
         HD web camera powered by Exmor sensor offers great low-light performance and superb video chatting quality
        </li>
        <li>
         VAIO Care software makes PC maintenance simple
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        As far as entertainment goes:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Explore the web, music, photos and more with a touch of a finger
        </li>
        <li>
         Multi-touch controls offer a simple, intuitive way to explore your PC
        </li>
        <li>
         Edge Access technology incorporates touchable controls into the displays bezel
        </li>
        <li>
         Experience 2D and 3D Blu-ray Disc movies (select models) and games in stunning Full 1080p HD
        </li>
        <li>
         Enjoy your photos and videos in 3D with the 3D photo and video viewer
        </li>
        <li>
         Enjoy rich, full audio with S-FORCE 3D Front Surround Sound technology
        </li>
        <li>
         Connect your PS3 system or set-top box via HDMI input
        </li>
        <li>
         Experience your content in a whole new way with Media Gallery software
        </li>
        <li>
         A true All-in-One solution with a PC, Blu-ray Disc player (select models), HDTV and DVR (select models) in one sophisticated wall-mountable package (mount sold separately).
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        The Sony VAIO L series will come in two colors  black or a premium white Signature Collection model, and cost you around $1,420. The desktop will be available for pre-sale on July 13, 2011. For those wanting to pre-order, visit
        <a href="http://www.sonystyle.com/lseries" target="_blank">
         www.sonystyle.com/lseries
        </a>
        .
       </p>
