       <p>
        The Cake Cafewas designed by
        <a href="http://www.2bua.com/" target="_blank">
         2B.GROUP
        </a>
        and islocated in Kiev. The interior was created in four months  from the first sketch to the store opening.
       </p>

        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Inside we find a lot of different elements.The diversity of images and forms presented in the 80 square meters main hall has only been achieved due to the fact that most of the design elements have been handcrafted.This diversity in the interior design was called by the designer as an indie design.
       </p>
