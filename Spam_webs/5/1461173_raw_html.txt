        Ahh, denim. Let us count the ways. There are the no-brainer skinnies for any lazy day, the comfortable jean jacket perfect for all seasons, and the summer cutoffs that are seriously festival-worthy. It's true that all of our beloved blue crushes have coveted spots in our closet, but denim is more than a fabric. It takes to our bodies perfectly, like no other piece of clothing can.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Then, there's fragrance. Finding the perfect scent  or, rather
        <em>
         any
        </em>
        scent  is a difficult task. Will it work with your body's chemistry? Is it a nice balance between flowery and woodsy? Will it capture the olfactory playfulness
        <em>
         and
        </em>
        sexiness of who you are? Is this all too much to ask?
        <br/>

        <a href="http://luckybrand.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Lucky Brand
        </a>
        to sort us out on both the wardrobe and fragrance front. The expert brand in easy-going denim has introduced Lucky Darling, the new scent of ginger, red currant, wisteria, and missoula wood that obviously nails the perfume trifecta of fruity, flowery, and musky. Ahead, Lucky Brand shows us how to pair Lucky Darling with three everyday denim looks  no Canadian tuxedos here!
