         Prepositions
        </span>
       </h2>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Wrong: He is sitting on his table.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Right
         </strong>
         : He is sitting at his table.
        </li>
        <li>
         Wrong: I live at Hyderabad.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Right
         </strong>
         : I live in Hyderabad.
        </li>
        <li>
         Wrong:He went with the car
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Right
         </strong>
         : He went by car.
        </li>
        <li>
         Wrong:He died from swineflu
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Right
         </strong>
         : He died of swineflu
        </li>
       </ul>

         :The book contains five hundred and fifty pages
        </li>
        <li>
         Wrong:Heasked that where I lived
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Right
         </strong>
         : He asked where I lived.
        </li>

         Wrong:Unless you do not work hard, you will not succeed
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Right
         </strong>
         : Unless you work hard, you will not succeed.
