        <p>
         Simply stated, Thunder yet again proved its stance on quality and lighting expertise as expressed through 1000 watts of pure efficiency. The Grow Light Kit comes equipped with one 1000-watt super HPS bulb and 1 MH bulb, 24-hour dual outlet grounded timer for the ballast lights, ratchet hangers, 1000-watt ballast with adjustable parameters, one Sunmax large white air-cooling reflector, and so much more it will leave you wondering just how the company could provide so much at such a price point.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thunder also covers your kit with a 5-year Manufacturers Warranty to reinforce its confidence in a product that seems to be sweeping the review boards with its uniformly positive ratings, being the product that doesnot produce ambivalence in the minds of experts and seasoned users alike. As stated, youre sure to find your own confidence within the Thunder 1000 Watt HPS Grow Light Starter Kit, since. Staying under the $300 price range, the 1000 Watt HPS Grow Light kit carries an with it an impressive versatility. You may be able to build your own kit for a lesser price, but
         <strong>
          DIY = no warranty
         </strong>
         , so you will have to bear all costs of your self-built light repairs yourself.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1000 Watt Super HPS Bulb and MH Bulb
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thunder covers these bad boys under their 5-Year Manufacturers Warranty. Should anything go wrong, your defective set will be replaced with new bulbs. Hence you can purchase these lights with complete confidence.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Thunder 24-Hour Dual Outlet Grounded Timer
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         You have the freedom of controlling two devices with one timer which is nice, especially with an override switch to prevent anything from shutting off inadvertently. . Though convenient, would this mean I need to monitor the lights just as often to ensure they do, in fact, stay operational? Further experimentation to follow!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1000 Watt Ballast
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         With this high-luminous capacity, you have as much or as little lighting as needed with adjustable wattage settings. 120V and 240V compatibility allows this lighting kit to be used anywhere in the world. Protected by the 5-Year Manufacturers Warranty, the ballast has varying bulb dimming options and maintains its status as one of the better choices to make when considering this type of investment. The growing environment can be unpredictable, but having this sort of control ensures that you control the outcome of your gardens growth potential to a large extent.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Sunmax Large Air Coolable Reflector
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Unlike most coolable reflectors utilized for hydroponics-based endeavors, the Sunmax reflector amplifies the amount of light it is capable of reflecting using its state of the art ingenious, tempered glass design. The reflecting panels are housed within a heavy-duty steel casing, which is reassuring, but 40 lbs of lighting equipment can be a bit of a hassle to move around or set up. Execute careful planning for where you would like this to end up!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5-Year Manufacturers Warranty
         </strong>
        </p>

         From the above analysis, it will surely appear that Thunder is serious about their product quality. Further proof of this can be seen from the quality of the warranty which covers it. . Im sure their limitations have yet to be tested with what they can and cant fix, but only time will tell. At the time of writing, I am very pleased with this lighting kit! It has a wide range of applications, which ensure that it is available for use even when the environment has to be changed drastically at short notice.
