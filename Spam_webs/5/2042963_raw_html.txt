  <p>
   People looking to lose unwanted body fat jump from one weight loss tactic to another.
  </p>
  <p>
   Often times these tactics involve the latest diet craze or fad.
  </p>
  <p>
   When one doesnt work after a few days of using it then they jump to the next tactic  then the next.
  </p>
  <p>
   Sound familiar?
  </p>

   One problem with this approach is that they never establish a
   <strong>
    weight loss foundation
   </strong>
    a platform, so to speak, from which they can launch a successful weight loss strategy  one that will not only work over time, but one they will be able to stick to for life.
  </p>
  <p>
   The solution is simple. Simply put in a little time and effort to build the proper foundation using my 5 step strategy below.
  </p>
  <p>
   Do these 5 things to lose weight BEFORE you even go on a diet.
  </p>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="how-to-build-a-weight-loss-foundation">
  </a>
  <h2>
   How to Build a Weight Loss Foundation
  </h2>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="1-focus-on-getting-started-not-finishing">
  </a>
  <h3>
   1. Focus On Getting Started, Not Finishing
  </h3>
  <p>
   Its often recommended that you visualize your desired outcome when working towards a goal. In the case of weight loss, you may visualize yourself fitting back into the jeans you wore in high school or finishing a 5K.
  </p>
  <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
  <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
   <center>
    <!-- Ad Unit [18001] Green Thickies-300x250-2 -->

  </div>
  <p>
   But sometimes this visual can be daunting and leave you paralyzed without making any progress.
  </p>
  <p>
   Simply getting started working towards your goals can be the most difficult part of the process.
  </p>
  <p>
   Can you relate?
  </p>
  <p>
   Instead of focusing on the 10, 20, 30+ pounds you have to lose and being daunted by the task, just start doing small things that will get you closer to overall success.
  </p>
  <p>
   Doing simple things such as cutting out bread, starting to workout with weights and get rid of all the junk in your house are great places to start.
  </p>
  <p>
   Once you do enough of these things they will begin to add up and snowball. Before you know it you will be picking up momentum and the goal of losing so much weight wont feel as daunting.
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Take Action:
   </strong>
   Just do something, no matter how small, right now to get you started working towards your goal.
  </p>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="2-progress-not-perfection">
  </a>
  <h2>
   2. Progress, Not Perfection
  </h2>
  <p>
   Not only can the simple act of getting started paralyze you, but the thought of doing everything perfectly can also stop you dead in your tracks.
  </p>
  <p>
   Have you ever considered starting a new diet but then realize you have a social gathering a few days away so you put off starting until after the event?
  </p>
  <p>
   If yes, then you know what I mean.
  </p>
  <p>
   However, having this kind of mindset needlessly wastes valuable time you could be using to move closer to achieving your weight loss goals.
  </p>
  <p>
   You see, to lose weight you dont have to be perfect.
  </p>
  <p>
   You do have to make some serious lifestyle changes but being perfect at everything you do isnt one of them.
  </p>
  <p>
   If you focus on eating real food and being active every single day you can afford to attend a party and indulge a little bit.
  </p>
  <p>
   You dont have to completely avoid your favorite foods all together. You just have to avoid them enough to see the needle on your bathroom scale move down.
  </p>
  <p>
   Having this mindset will allow you to keep moving forward and working towards your weight loss goals.
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Take Action
   </strong>
   : Focus on progress not perfection when it comes to losing weight.
  </p>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="3-control-negative-self-talk">
  </a>
  <h2>
   3. Control Negative Self Talk
  </h2>
  <p>
   Admittedly, I have a very loud voice in my head telling me I suck and that I will fail.
  </p>
  <p>
   Ive never really admitted that to anyone, people think I am very strong and confident. And really, I am those things.
  </p>
  <p>
   But I hear my own voice telling me otherwise, especially when I have a lofty goal set for myself.
  </p>
  <p>
   Ive tried and tried over the years to make that voice completely go away, to no avail. So Ive had to learn to minimize her voice and not listen to what she says.
  </p>
  <p>
   Whats your negative voice in your head telling you?
  </p>
  <ul>
   <li>
    That you dont deserve to be lean and healthy?
   </li>
   <li>
    That you dont have the will power to stick to new, healthy habits?
   </li>
   <li>
    That you could never get back into the jeans you wore in high school?
   </li>
  </ul>
  <p>
   You MUST figure out a way to ignore this voice like I did.
  </p>
  <p>
   The best way Ive found to accomplish this is to simply take action and do the work necessary to meet your weight loss goals.
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Take Action
   </strong>
   : Ignore the resistance in your head in the form of self sabotaging thoughts by taking massive action towards your goals.
  </p>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="4-social-support">
  </a>
  <h2>
   4. Social Support
  </h2>
  <p>
   Social support is what you get when you surround yourself with people who will help motivate you and hold you accountable.
  </p>
  <p>
   There are a few ways you can go about enlisting social support:
  </p>
  <p>
   First, get a workout buddy. This works great when it is a friend who you can schedule workouts with throughout the week. This can be a great time to catch up and spend time together while motivating each other to keep working hard.
  </p>
  <p>
   The best part is that it is very hard to cancel on your friend if you dont have a good excuse.
  </p>
  <p>
   Second, you must get the support of your spouse and everyone else who you live with.
  </p>
  <p>
   For example, if youre going to start eating a real food dietthen you must make sure that your husband and your children help keep processed and other fake foods out of the house.
  </p>
  <p>
   Theres nothing worse that your significant other housing a bowl of your favorite ice cream every night while you suffer watching them.
  </p>
  <p>
   Third, you could hire a Personal Trainer at your local gym or find an online weight loss coach you can check in with regularly. They could also provide you with an effective weight loss strategy if you dont have one for yourself.
  </p>
  <p>
   No man or woman is an island and we do occasionally need a little extra help. So make sure to take advantage.
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Take Action:
   </strong>
   Find a workout buddy and/or weight loss coach and make sure everyone in your house supports your weight loss efforts.
  </p>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="5-know-your-powerful-why">
  </a>
  <h2>
   5. Know Your Powerful Why
  </h2>
  <p>
   Your powerful why is what I call your ultimate reason for wanting to lose weight and reclaim your health.
  </p>
  <p>
   You need a really powerful reason WHY you want to lose weight.
  </p>
  <p>
   It has to be something so motivating that you wont let ANY obstacle stand in your way of achieving it.
  </p>
  <p>
   Otherwise, no external motivation in the world can kick you in the behind enough to meet the goal if it really isnt what you want to do.
  </p>
  <p>
   Although I saved this step for last I believe knowing your why is THE single most important step you can take when trying to lose your unwanted body fat.
  </p>
  <p>
   Period.
  </p>
  <p>
   The problem is, most people have no idea how to discover what their powerful why really is. In fact, many of my weight loss clients *think* they know what it is but come soon to realize they were way off.
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    To help you discover YOUR powerful why, I developed a step by step strategy you can grab for FREE here.
    <a class="snppopup" href="http://bit.ly/1dL8yhr">
     http://bit.ly/1dL8yhr
    </a>
   </strong>
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Action item:
   </strong>
   Discover your why  your passionate reason to lose weight, improve your health and feel better that you can be passionate about.
  </p>
  <a class="mwm-aal-item" name="wrapping-things-up">
  </a>
  <h2>
   Wrapping Things Up
  </h2>
  <p>
   Using my 5 step strategy above for developing your weight loss foundation is the key to our long term weight loss success  even before you go on a diet.
  </p>
  <p>
   In particular, discovering your powerful why can single handedly keep you motivated enough to start your journey and provide you with the will power to overcome any obstacles that might get in your way.
  </p>

   Feel free to leave any questions you have in the comments below and I will be happy to answer them for you.
