       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: .0001pt; line-height: normal; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: .5in 1.0in 1.5in 2.0in 2.5in 3.0in 3.5in 4.0in 4.5in 5.0in 5.5in 6.0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;">
        <span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">
         This Friday brings Chicago singer-songwriter, Angel Olsen, to
         <a href="(http://www.whiterabbitcabaret.com/White_Rabbit_Cabaret.html)">
          White Rabbit Cabaret
         </a>
         . Her debut LP,
         <i>
          Half Way Home
         </i>
         , served as a breakthrough in 2012. Olsen was subsequently signed to Bloomington label and indie heavyweight
         <a href="http://www.jagjaguwar.com/artist.php?name=olsenangel">
          Jagjaguwar
         </a>
         where she will release a follow-up early next spring.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: .0001pt; line-height: normal; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: .5in 1.0in 1.5in 2.0in 2.5in 3.0in 3.5in 4.0in 4.5in 5.0in 5.5in 6.0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;">
        <span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: .0001pt; line-height: normal; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: .5in 1.0in 1.5in 2.0in 2.5in 3.0in 3.5in 4.0in 4.5in 5.0in 5.5in 6.0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;">
        <span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">
         Olsen cut her teeth as a backup vocalist for Will Oldhams
         <a href="http://www.bonnieprincebilly.com/">
          Bonnie Prince Billy
         </a>
         . Her prowess as a solo performer became evident in 2010 with
         <i>
          Strange Cacti EP,
         </i>
         initially released on cassette and later on vinyl by
         <a href="http://batheticrecords.com/">
          Bathetic Records
         </a>
         . I caught Olsens set at
         <a href="http://thebishopbar.com/">
          The Bishop Bar
         </a>
         in Bloomington earlier this summer. Her warbling vocals and stark arrangements forced the near-capacity audience to stand at rapt attention throughout, despite an uncooperative microphone. She lightened the tension over the audio issues with witty stage banter built upon self-deprecating humor.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: .0001pt; line-height: normal; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: .5in 1.0in 1.5in 2.0in 2.5in 3.0in 3.5in 4.0in 4.5in 5.0in 5.5in 6.0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;">
        <span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: .0001pt; line-height: normal; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: .5in 1.0in 1.5in 2.0in 2.5in 3.0in 3.5in 4.0in 4.5in 5.0in 5.5in 6.0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;">
        <span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">
         Rest assured, Olsens talent extends beyond her singular voice. Her lyrics cut to the core, wielding a knife of scorned love, heartbreaking loss and vulnerability. The singer recently stopped by NPR Music to record one of their 
         <a href="http://www.npr.org/series/tiny-desk-concerts/">
          Tiny Desk Concerts
         </a>
         . According to reports, the performance featured tracks from her next LP. If this is case, we can safely expect to hear some new material on Friday at The Rabbit. Advanced tickets are available for $10 via
         <a href="http://www.mokbpresents.com/event/319343-angel-olsen-indianapolis/">
          MOKB Presents
         </a>
         .
        </span>
       </p>
