         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <img alt="Jack Ryan DVD Release &amp; Cheesy Spinach &amp; Bacon Dip Recipe! #JackRyanBluRay | Optimistic Mommy" class="aligncenter wp-image-6276" height="823" src="http://optimisticmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/jack-ryan-jackryanbluray.jpg" width="583"/>

         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Speaking of appetizers, I have one that Im sure your husband will enjoy while watching
          <em>
           Jack Ryan
          </em>
          ! We all lovecheese and bacon, right? How about a Cheesy Bacon Dip! This is super easy to make and will sure to be a hit during your action movie marathon!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <img alt="Jack Ryan DVD Release &amp; Cheesy Bacon Dip Recipe! #JackRyanBluRay | Optimistic Mommy" class="aligncenter wp-image-6280" height="602" src="http://optimisticmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cheesy-bacon-dip-jackryanbluray.jpg" width="903"/>
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Cheesy Bacon Dip
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <em>
           Ingredients:
          </em>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1 Pound Pasteurized Cheese Product (Such As Velveeta)
          </li>
          <li>
           4 Ounces Cream Cheese
          </li>
          <li>
           1 Can (10 Ounces) Tomatoes &amp; Green Chiles
          </li>
          <li>
           Bacon Bits
          </li>
          <li>
           Tortilla Chips, Cut Up Vegetables
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <em>
           Directions:
          </em>
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           Place all of the ingredients in a microwave-safe bowl, except for the tortilla chips and cut-up vegetables.
          </li>
          <li>
           Microwave on high for 5 minutes until cheese product is melted and well blended, stirring after 3 minutes.
          </li>
          <li>
           Serve with tortilla chips and cut-up vegetables. Enjoy!
