          <span class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://recipes.terra-americana.com/2010/11/spicy-grilled-corn.html">
            Spicy Grilled Corn
           </a>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <div class="entry-body font-entrybody">
          <p>
           <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://terra-americana.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55022dfc588340191047c8278970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false">
            <img alt="Spicy Grilled Corn" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55022dfc588340191047c8278970c" src="http://terra-americana.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55022dfc588340191047c8278970c-500wi" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Spicy Grilled Corn"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: 13pt;">
            This is a fantastic way to taste the other side of corn. On the grill the sugars boil as the kernels sear. Basted with butter and sprinkled with spice; there is nothing left to do but enjoy.
           </span>
          </p>
          <div id="caption">
           <div>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: 13pt;">
               6 ears corn, husked and cleaned
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: 13pt;">
               1/2 cup butter, softened
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: 13pt;">
               6 Tbsp
               <a href="http://recipes.terra-americana.com/2010/06/bayou-blackening-balm.html">
                Bayou Blackening Balm
               </a>
               or
               <a href="http://recipes.terra-americana.com/2010/06/picante-passion.html">
                Picante Passion
               </a>
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: 13pt;">
               1/4 cup
               <a href="http://www.cookinglight.com/cooking-101/essential-ingredients/queso-fresco-00400000001122/" target="_self">
                queso fresco
               </a>
               or
               <a href="http://recipes.terra-americana.com/2012/03/a-simple-recipe-for-creamy-goat-cheese-ch%C3%A8vre.html" target="_self">
                chevre
               </a>
               (optional)
              </span>
             </li>
            </ul>
           </div>
           <div>
            <br/>
            <span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: 13pt;">
             Peel back husks from the corn, remove strings and leave just a few layers of husk on the corn. Spread butter or margarine over each ear of corn. Sprinkle
             <a href="http://recipes.terra-americana.com/2010/06/bayou-blackening-balm.html">
              Bayou Blackening Balm
             </a>
             or
             <a href="http://recipes.terra-americana.com/2010/06/picante-passion.html">
              Picante Passion
             </a>
             lightly over each ear, or to taste. Fold corn husks back over the corn. Place on grill for 25 minutes, turning occasionally. Peel back husks, sprinkle with cheese and enjoy!
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
