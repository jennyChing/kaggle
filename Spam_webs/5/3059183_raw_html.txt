      <p>
       Ashworth gives you all the info you could possibly need to be inspired.
      </p>
      <p>
       Celebrating natural, ruggedly beautiful golf courses, Ashworths Golf/Man
       <a href="http://bit.ly/jdbandon" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://bit.ly/jdbandon', 'site']);" target="_blank">
        site
       </a>
       has set out on the roads less-traveled to some of the newest and most impressive links-style destinations in the U.S. Places like Bandon Dunes in Oregon, Gamble Sands in Washington, and Streamsong in Florida. The kinds of courses that take a while to get to, and then make you seriously consider never going back home.
      </p>
      <p>
       But if youre not able to actually get to Bandon or Streamsong this weekend, simply visit
       <a href="http://bit.ly/jdbandon" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://bit.ly/jdbandon', 'Golf/Man']);" target="_blank">
        Golf/Man
       </a>
       , Ashworths award-winning digital journey to discover each of these courses. Created with the fashion-forward player in mind, Golf/Man celebrates style, friendship and exploration with a unique mix of content, including films, articles and photos. From the travel, to the food, to the courses themselves, Ashworth delves into the world of the golfer, otherwise known as the Golf/Man.
      </p>

       To help celebrate the launch of Golf/Man, Ashworth created Our Kind of Golf, a short film outlining the brands approach to the game. Watch below.
