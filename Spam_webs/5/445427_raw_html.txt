         <h1>
          2015s Best Laptops for Linux
         </h1>
         <p>
          The idea of freedom is what drove the development of
          <a href="http://www.laptophub.net/best-laptops-linux/" target="_bank" title="Best Laptops for Linux">
           <strong>
            Linux
           </strong>
          </a>
