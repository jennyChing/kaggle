         <p>
          Diversity in Devices, Applications and Technology has given rise to testing times for the Operators. How would front end and back end support team keep pace with the new developments? Are they cross selling or up-selling the right products? Can they afford to misinform the customer with their lack of knowledge?
         </p>
         <p>
          Learning management solution is a flexible SCORM (Sharable Content Object Reference Model) compliant, multilingual Learning Management System that enables Telcos and MVNOs to consolidate their learning content into a seamless integrated library- online, virtual or live. LMS manages everything right from enrollment, to delivery, test scores, tracking, reporting, individual development plans, competencies &amp; certifications along with highly personalized and customized learning environment.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="LearnMax-Info" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1326" height="263" src="http://www.kochartech.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/LearnMax-Info-1024x397.png" width="680"/>
         </p>
