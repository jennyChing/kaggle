&lt;div style="background-color: white; line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Size of Baby:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;3 3/4 lbs. &nbsp;and 16 1/2 inches long&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="color: #999999; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;b style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; line-height: 21.5599994659424px; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: inherit;"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Gender:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;i style="color: #999999;"&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/i&gt;Our baby girl! SRC!&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; color: #999999; line-height: 21.5599994659424px; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; color: #999999; line-height: 21.5599994659424px; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Movement:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;All the time. She has really been keeping me up at night because that is when she moves the most. It's so sweet, but I'm afraid she is going to be like her big brother and be a night owl.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Sleep:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;Who needs sleep?&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; color: #999999; line-height: 21.5599994659424px; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;b style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="background-color: white; line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Workouts:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;This week, I have walked a lot more because it has been so nice outside. I've walked about 2 miles a day and I've still been completing my TIU workouts with a lot of stretching and yoga thrown in. It has made me feel a lot better when I stretch.&nbsp;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="color: #999999; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Maternity Clothes: &lt;/b&gt;I've been rocking my Nike shorts most of this week. It has been warmer and it has been so nice to wear shorts.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: inherit;"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Symptoms: &lt;/b&gt;Luckily I haven't had any more painful Braxton Hicks this week. I've been trying to drink lots and lots of water to keep hydrated so I don't have anymore pain. It has definitely helped.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: inherit;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Cravings/Aversions:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;Just sweets every now and then. My cravings still haven't been crazy. I even made it three day in a row this week without any sweets at all. :)&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="color: #999999; line-height: 21.5599994659424px; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;b style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;div style="margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Missing Most:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;Sleep. And my abs. Because they are officially gone. At least for the next couple of months. I am having a really hard time doing any sort of ab workouts now, although I haven't given up completely yet.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="color: #999999; line-height: 21.5599994659424px; margin: 0px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="line-height: 21.5599994659424px;"&gt;
&lt;span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);"&gt;&lt;b style="color: #999999;"&gt;Nursery:&nbsp;&lt;/b&gt;I shared a sneak peek at &lt;a href="http://www.logancan.com/2015/03/baby-girls-nursery-decor-blockaholic.html"&gt;our nursery&lt;/a&gt; this week! Did you catch it?&nbsp;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
