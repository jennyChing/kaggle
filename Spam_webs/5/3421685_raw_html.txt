             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               The royal baby deserves a regal shindig.
              </p>
              <p>
               This heir to the British throne is being ushered into the world with a flurry of
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/07/22/twitter-royal-baby/">
                funny tweets
               </a>
               and a swelling crowd in front of the hospital. Even if you can't afford to jet off to England to get closer to the celebration, there are still plenty of ways to burn off some of your excitement.
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/07/11/weird-royal-baby-merchandise/">
                 8 Weirdest Pieces of Vintage Royal Baby Merchandise
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               Just remember when planning your royal birthday party: it's important to strike a good balance between baby-based and British-related festivities.
              </p>
              <h3>
               1. Rock out to a club remix of "God Save the Queen."
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="420">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/AdIZ6RlKWoE?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/AdIZ6RlKWoE?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="420">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               Or opt for a low key slow dance to the original.
              </p>
              <h3>
               2. Watch the first five minutes of
               <em>
                Monty Python and the Meaning of Life.
               </em>
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="560">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/3RYu8fcAD0Q?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/3RYu8fcAD0Q?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="560">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               Since Kate Middleton is such a high profile patient, they'll probably have at least three machines that go ping.
              </p>
              <h3>
               3. Eat a baby-themed meal.
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="420">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/u6lChkMu2V8?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/u6lChkMu2V8?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="420">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               If baby-back ribs are too fancy, just load up on baby carrots.
              </p>
              <h3>
               4. Track down a baby bottle pop.
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="420">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/la6d5FafgTk?version=3&amp;hl=en_US&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/la6d5FafgTk?version=3&amp;hl=en_US&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="420">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               Somewhere there's a CVS that still sells them.
              </p>
              <h3>
               5. Host an encore presentation of the royal wedding.
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="560">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/DTd2Ex60XiQ?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/DTd2Ex60XiQ?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="560">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               Feeling a little nostalgic is natural.
              </p>
              <h3>
               6. Put on
               <em>
                Love Actually
               </em>
               and close your eyes.
              </h3>
              <p>
               <a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Love-Actually.gif">
                <img alt="Love Actually" class="fullwidth" data-width="480" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA3LzIyLzI4L0xvdmVBY3R1YWxsLmJhYzFkLmdpZgpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/6164f517/3f0/Love-Actually.gif"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               <em>
                Image courtesy of Tumblr,
                <a href="http://alltheliesihavetold.tumblr.com/" target="_blank">
                 alltheliesihavetold
                </a>
               </em>
              </p>
              <p>
               This is the closest you can come to being in England without traveling to England.
              </p>
              <h3>
               7. Watch the birth scene from
               <em>
                Knocked Up
               </em>
               on mute and invite your friends to do the voice overs in British accents.
              </h3>
              <p>
               <a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Knocked-Up.gif">
                <img alt="Knocked Up" class="fullwidth" data-width="245" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA3LzIyL2ZmL0tub2NrZWRVcC5hMWViNC5naWYKcAl0aHVtYgkxMjAweDk2MDA-/cc3d0c73/e57/Knocked-Up.gif"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               <em>
                Image courtesy of Tumblr,
                <a href="http://hazzaway.tumblr.com/" target="_blank">
                 hazzaway
                </a>
               </em>
              </p>
              <p>
               With the role of well meaning guy who's ordered out of the delivery room played by imaginary Prince Harry.
              </p>
              <h3>
               8. Listen to a Spice Girls medley while watching YouTube videos of adorable infants.
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="420">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/gJLIiF15wjQ?version=3&amp;hl=en_US&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/gJLIiF15wjQ?version=3&amp;hl=en_US&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="420">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               UK babies will work best, but children from outside the Commonwealth will do.
              </p>
              <h3>
               9. When the birth announcement is finally set out, play the soundtrack to
               <em>
                Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
               </em>
               .
              </h3>
              <p>
               <object height="315" width="560">
                <param name="movie" value="//www.youtube.com/v/IpYHbdh9yTM?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0">
                </param>
                <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
                </param>
                <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
                </param>
                <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/v/IpYHbdh9yTM?hl=en_US&amp;version=3&amp;rel=0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="560">
                </embed>
               </object>
              </p>
              <p>
               Whispering "you are the chosen one" is optional.
              </p>
