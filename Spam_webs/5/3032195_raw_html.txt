                       Affirmation repetition
                      </h1>
                      <div class="metabar">

                    <p align="justify">
                     Affirmations are probably the easiest and simplest technique to influence and affect the conscious mind.
                    </p>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      Keep it simple
                     </li>
                     <li>
                      Think about what you are wanting as you say your affirmation
                      <a href="http://www.thebestthoughts.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/273912_in_our_hands_4.jpg">
                       <img alt="affirmation repetition" class="alignright size-full wp-image-499" height="300" src="http://www.thebestthoughts.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/273912_in_our_hands_4.jpg" title="affirmation repetition" width="296"/>
                      </a>
                     </li>
                     <li>
                      Repetition is how a thought becomes a belief
                     </li>
                     <li>
                      Train yourself to say the affirmation often
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <p style="text-align: justify;">
                     Through continuous repetition you will feel the reality of your desire. The more you see what you want happening the more likely it will become a happening.
                    </p>
                    <p style="text-align: left;">
                     <strong>
                      This process is fun.
                     </strong>
                    </p>
                    <p style="text-align: justify;">
                     Dont allow yourself to become in a state of need, get frustrated,or angry when your thoughts are of your affirmation, as those thoughts will deter the desire.
                    </p>
                    <p style="text-align: justify;">
                     When you are doing affirmations you are influencing the thoughts that occur in your mind.
                    </p>
                    <p style="text-align: justify;">
                     Think it, feel it, affirm it, and the results will occur.
                    </p>
