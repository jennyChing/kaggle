                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-KgHXpUPGfi0/Uw1-VOai8OI/AAAAAAAAB5M/M2vFczasTzg/s1600/DSC09350.JPG" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Temecula Wine Tasting in Limo Bus Style
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7009918436227587081" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-KgHXpUPGfi0/Uw1-VOai8OI/AAAAAAAAB5M/M2vFczasTzg/s1600/DSC09350.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-KgHXpUPGfi0/Uw1-VOai8OI/AAAAAAAAB5M/M2vFczasTzg/s1600/DSC09350.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                      Temecula
is Southern Californias wine region.
                      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                      </span>
                      But Temecula doesnt necessarily have a reputation for producing the
best wine.
                      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                      </span>
                      Since Temecula is an easy
hour drive north of San Diego, I am always looking for those special Temecula
wineries that defy the bad reputation and surprise with their excellent
wine.
                      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                      </span>
                      I found one of those surprises in
Danza del Sol Winery.
                      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        I previously joined a
                        <a href="http://www.passportsandcocktails.com/2013/10/aall-in-limo-brewery-tour-san-diego.html" target="_blank">
                         San Diego limo brewery tour
                        </a>
                        with Aall In Limo &amp; Party Bus, so I
was thrilled when Aall In Limo &amp; Party Bus asked me to be their guest again, this time to test
out the newest addition to their fleet, the limo bus.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        We drove in style and comfort to Temecula in
the spacious and plush limo bus.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Our day
of wine tasting appropriately started with a bottle of sparkling wine from one
of the Temecula wineries in the limo while we drove to our first stop, Danza
del Sol Winery.
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-r9nARr4RQp8/Uw1-SFkogZI/AAAAAAAAB40/idS2tGuJ7Xg/s1600/DSC09325.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img alt="Aall In Limo Limo Bus Temecula Wine Tour" border="0" height="266" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-r9nARr4RQp8/Uw1-SFkogZI/AAAAAAAAB40/idS2tGuJ7Xg/s1600/DSC09325.JPG" title="Limo Bus" width="400"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                         <span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">
                          <b>
                           The roomy interior of our Aall In Limo &amp; Party Bus limo bus ride.
                          </b>
                         </span>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        Danza
del Sol is the fifth oldest winery in Temecula.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Previously Filsinger Vineyards and Winery founded in 1978, the winery
was later purchased in 2010 by the current owner, Robert Olson.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        The oldest vines on the property are
Sauvignon Blanc planted in 1972.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        This is
also where the first Gewrztraminer grapes were planted in Southern California.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        The winerys other signature grapes are
Orange Muscat and Syrah.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Danza del Sol
is a 35 acre estate vineyard, meaning all the wine is grown, crushed,
fermented, bottled, and aged on the premises.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Because Danza del Sol is an estate vineyard and produces a relatively
small number of cases, their wine can only be obtained at the winery or through
the wine club.
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        Danza
del Sol Winery kindly hosted a tour of their winery in addition to our wine
tastings so we could learn more about the origins of the wine.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        We started our tour outside with a view of
the vines.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        The vines were not very
picturesque in February as bud break occurs in March when everything starts to
turn green again.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        It was a foggy
morning, which illustrated why Temecula is a good grape growing region.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Laura Burnham, the tasting room manager,
explained it is actually this mist and fog that makes Temecula such a thriving
wine growing region.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Temecula is only
about 20 miles inland from the ocean.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        The fog comes in from the west, hits the mountains in the east, and
cascades down into the valley.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        The heat
in the valley causes the grapes to create more sugars, which later turn into
alcohol, which is why Temecula wines are stronger than wines from other
regions.
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        We
entered the tank farm where fermentation occurs and wine is stored.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Danza del Sols wines are fermented in
stainless steel, which helps enhance the aromatic profiles.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Laura gave us a taste of their 2013 Tempranillo,
which had completed the fermentation process but had not yet been aged.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        Tasting a new wine prior to aging was a fun
new experience for me.
                        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                        </span>
                        The 2013
Tempranillo was bright purple with a very fruity, grapey taste and a prominent tannin
finish, yet it was surprisingly drinkable.
                       </span>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                       <tbody>
                        <tr>
                         <td style="text-align: center;">
                          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-L5IPtk8CoEo/Uw1-Q1e09mI/AAAAAAAAB4o/ferSVEj8XyY/s1600/2014+Temecula+wine+tour+%25287%2529+copy.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                           <img alt="Danza del Sol 2013 Tempranillo Temecula California" border="0" height="266" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-L5IPtk8CoEo/Uw1-Q1e09mI/AAAAAAAAB4o/ferSVEj8XyY/s1600/2014+Temecula+wine+tour+%25287%2529+copy.jpg" title="2013 Tempranillo" width="400"/>
                          </a>
                         </td>
                        </tr>
                        <tr>
                         <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                          <span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">
                           <b>
                            Tasting Danza del Sol's 2013 Tempranillo before aging straight from the tank.
                           </b>
                          </span>
                         </td>
                        </tr>
                       </tbody>
                      </table>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         We then tasted the aged 2011 Tempranillo so we could taste the difference
the aging process makes.
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         The 2011
Tempranillo was a darker garnet with a nice fruity aroma and a more mellow taste
with less tannin.
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         Laura explained tannin
allows wine to age well and mellows with aging.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                         <tbody>
                          <tr>
                           <td style="text-align: center;">
                            <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ctuZMKiaImE/Uw1-U_tTB9I/AAAAAAAAB5I/psN-9XaeXNA/s1600/DSC09345.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                             <img alt="Danza del Sol 2011 Tempranillo Temecula California" border="0" height="266" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ctuZMKiaImE/Uw1-U_tTB9I/AAAAAAAAB5I/psN-9XaeXNA/s1600/DSC09345.JPG" title="2011 Tempranillo" width="400"/>
                            </a>
                           </td>
                          </tr>
                          <tr>
                           <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                            <span style="color: black; font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">
                             <b>
                              Tasting Danza del Sol's 2011 Tempranillo in the tank farm.
                             </b>
                            </span>
                           </td>
                          </tr>
                         </tbody>
                        </table>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                          After
completing our tour of the winery we entered the tasting room to sample more of
the wines,starting with four of
Danza del Sols white wines.
                          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                          </span>
                          The 2011
Sauvignon Blancis fresh, light, and crisp with a somewhat grassy
aroma.
                          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                          </span>
                          The 2011 Viognier has a strong green
apple aroma and a crisp flavor.
                          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                          </span>
                          The 2011
Roussanne is an award winnerwith a very buttery feel.
                          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                          </span>
                          The 2011 Sinfonia, another award winner, is a
blend of Viognier, Roussanne, and Danza del Sols signature Orange Muscat with
a citrus taste and a sweet but fresh finish.
                          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                          <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-foqyVMFsg2I/Uw1-RSQt1sI/AAAAAAAAB4s/WXGjRfJpDz0/s1600/2014+Temecula+wine+tour+%25289%2529.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                           <img alt="Danza del Sol Winery Temecula California" border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-foqyVMFsg2I/Uw1-RSQt1sI/AAAAAAAAB4s/WXGjRfJpDz0/s1600/2014+Temecula+wine+tour+%25289%2529.JPG" title="Walter" width="212"/>
                          </a>
                          <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                           We
also sampled four of Danza del Sols red wines.
                           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                           </span>
                           The 2010 Grenache is a light-bodied red which tastes best slightly
chilled and actually has an unusual aroma of root beer, though we couldnt put
our finger on what it was until reading the notes.
                           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                           </span>
                           The 2010 Mourvdre is a great wine for Thanksgiving
with cranberry aromas and a spicy clove and nutmeg finish.
                           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                           </span>
                           The 2011 Barbera is a more rustic red with a
deep fruit flavor.
                           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                           </span>
                           The 2011 Trilogy is a
Meritage blend with a silky finish.
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         We
ended our tastings with Danza del Sols sweeter wines.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         The 2012 Tres Ros is another award winning
blend of Cinsaut, Zinfandel, and Sangiovese.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         The Ros is sweet with a pronounced strawberry flavor.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         The 2012 Gewrztraminer is an award winner
which is sweet but not too sweet.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         The 2012
Sabrosa, a late harvest Sauvignon Blanc made from the old estate vines, is a
sweet after-dinner wine, but is not syrupy.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         The Espumosa is Danza del Sols semi-sweet sparkling wine with a crisp
melon flavor.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         I
am hard-pressed to visit a winery and not bring any bottles home with me, so I purchased some of our favorites, the Sinfonia, Mourvdre, Barbera,
and Sabrosa.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         I even brought a bottle of
the Sinfonia to a gathering that evening, and it was a hit with all.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         Danza del Sols wine is both exceptional in
flavor and very affordable, with most bottles ranging from $20 to $30 without a
wine club discount.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                        <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                         If
you are planning a wine tasting trip to Temecula, be sure to include a visit to
                         <a href="https://www.danzadelsolwinery.com/" nofollow="" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.danzadelsolwinery.com/']) rel=" target="_blank">
                          Danza del Sol Winery
                         </a>
                         .
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         In addition
to wine tasting, you can join a guided tour of the winery.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         If planning a wine tasting trip with family
and friends, book
                         <a href="http://www.aallinlimo.com/wine-tours/" nofollow="" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.aallinlimo.com/wine-tours/']) rel=" target="_blank">
                          Aall In Limo &amp; Party Bus
                         </a>
                          limo bus, which comfortably seats up to
20 passengers.
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         The commute will fly by,
and of course there is the added benefit of having a designated driver while
you sip champagne and reminisce about all the great Temecula wines you
discovered.
                         <i style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">
