         <p>
          Hello Love,
         </p>
         <p>
          Hello first love. Hello joy, pleasure, despair and sorrow. I miss you.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was lying in bed next to my wife and child when you slipped into my thoughts, unboxing a thousand things I've carefully hidden in a dark place. Places I've avoided to go to, songs I've avoided to hear, people that we used to know.
         </p>
         <p>
          And everything else I drowned in alcohol.
         </p>
         <p>
          I slept with the bittersweet of nostalgia and guilt last night.
         </p>
         <p>
          I realised that we have been apart for ten years. My memories of your hair in my hands, you lips on me and mine on you, are like photos that refused to fade away. There is something about first loves that lingers harder than others. I've had many before and after you, but none raised me to a higher mountain and threw me into the darker void.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was so afraid of your rejection that you were the one that grabbed my arm and held my hand in the cinema, to this day I have no hope of remembering what was on the screen. I still can't believe I had to ask for permission to kiss you. The first touch of a first love was such a bliss that the aftertaste never quite goes away.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was the first you had inside you, we were slow, gentle, I remember how you winced in pain, how your body tensed up, you gripped my wrists and told me to continue. Your pain was your pleasure when I was its reason. Being inside you was like finding a home for the first time in my life. A single point in the universe that I could call my

I deprived you of my affection and made you beg for it. And you did. Oh you did. You let me have you wherever and whenever I wished. You let me bend and twist you in ways you've never before. Perhaps somewhere in that chaos we were looking for that moment of innocence again, but by then the pain you gave made you despise yourself for my hollow pleasure.

	  return new Handlebars.SafeString(str);
	};
	linkify = function(safeContent) {
	  return safeContent.replace(LINK_DETECTION_REGEX, function(url) {
	  	brEnd = false;
	  	if(url.indexOf("\n")) {
	  		brEnd = true;
	  	}
	  	url = url.trim();
	    url = url.replace(/(?:\r\n|\r|\n)/g, '');
	    var address;
	    address = /[a-z]+:\/\//.test(url) ? url : "http://" + url;    
	    url = url.replace(/^https?:\/\//, '');
	    link = "&lt;a href='" + address + "' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'&gt;" + url + "&lt;/a&gt;";
	    if(brEnd) {
	    	return link + '&lt;br/&gt;';
	    } else {
	    	return link;
	    }
	  });
	};

	formatTextForHTML = function(content) {
	  var safeContent;
	  safeContent = ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION(content);
	  safeContent = linkify(safeContent);
	  safeContent = safeContent.replace(/(?:\r\n|\r|\n)/g, '&lt;br /&gt;');
	  //safeContent = emojify(safeContent);
	  return MARKSAFE_FUNCTION(safeContent);
	};

	Handlebars.registerHelper('formatTextForHTML', formatTextForHTML);
