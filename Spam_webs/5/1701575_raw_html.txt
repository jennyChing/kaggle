         <h1>
          <a href="http://www.weightlossdietwatch.com/diet-plans/gallbladder-diet-for-controlling-the-problems-in-the-gallbladder/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Gallbladder Diet For Controlling The Problems In The Gallbladder!">
           Gallbladder Diet For Controlling The Problems In The Gallbladder!
          </a>
         </h1>
         <p class="postinfo">
          Posted on Feb 11, 2008 |
          <a href="http://www.weightlossdietwatch.com/diet-plans/gallbladder-diet-for-controlling-the-problems-in-the-gallbladder/#comments">
           Comments 0
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="entry clearfix">
          <p>
           <img align="right" alt="Gallbladder diet" class="img1" src="http://www.weightlossdietwatch.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/01/gallbladder-diet.jpg"/>
           <strong>
            Gallbladder diet
           </strong>
           includes the high fiber diet which absorbs excess amount of water and this  makes your bowel motions more bulky.
          </p>
          <p>
           You have to avoid foods that worsen diarrhea; these foods  include caffeine in tea and coffee, dairy products, spicy and fatty foods.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Following foods are involved in gallbladder diet:
          </h3>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Gallbladder diet
           </strong>
           will not shrink the gallstones but this diet will help you in excretion of  gallstones into gut.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you have gallstone problems, you should take more  starchy foods such as bread, cereals and rice. You should take fruits and  vegetables for fiber to your body.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gallbladder diet which provides relief from gallbladder pain  are vegetables, fruits, dark leafy greens, legumes, nuts and any other foods  which are related to vegetable kingdom. In this vegetable kingdom, there is no  cholesterol.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you want to stop your body from creating more and more  gallstones, then you have to avoid taking cholesterol content foods in your  diet. You have to follow low fat diet; if you are suffering from gallbladder  disease.
          </p>
          <p>
           You should substitute your salad dressings with vinegar and  olive oil. To follow Gallbladder diet, you should not take large meals during  night time.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Following gallbladder diet when your gallbladder is removed:
          </h3>
          <p>
           Gallbladder is a sac that will store the bile which is made  by liver. After you have completed taking the meal, the gallbladder squeezes  the bile and stores in the intestine.
          </p>
          <p>
           The bile in the intestine is mixed with the food and fat.  Then bile participates in the digestion of that food and transfers digested  food from intestine into body.
          </p>
          <p>
           When gallbladder is removed, the bile which is made by liver  is not stored between meals. As soon as bile is produced by liver, it moves  into the intestine. Now bile should still mix with food and fat.
          </p>
          <p>
           If your gallbladder is removed you will not have much bile  but this bile will be enough for digesting and transfer of fat. It is not  required to limit the amount of fat if your gallbladder is removed.
          </p>
          <p>
           If the gallbladder inside your body will not produce enough  bile, then fat foods which you have consumed are not digested and you will feel  ill and suffer from diarrhea.
          </p>
          <h3>
           You should not take following diet when you are suffering from gallbladder  pain:
          </h3>
          <p>
           You will suffer from gallbladder pain due to gallstones.  Gallstones are hard calcium or bile pigments which are formed due to the excess  cholesterol in the bile. These gallstones are cholesterol deposits, so avoid  eating cholesterol foods for overcoming the pain of gallbladder.
          </p>
          <p>
           The cholesterol foods include animal foods, dairy foods  (butter, cream, milk, cheese and many other products). You should avoid taking  carbonated drinks as this trigger the gallstone movement and enhances the pain.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gallbladder not only prevents you from gallstones but also  acts against heart disease, cancer, diabetes, obesity and other overweight  related problems. Anyone who is suffering from gallbladder problem can follow  this diet. You can also prefer gallbladder diet as healthy weight diet.
          </p>
