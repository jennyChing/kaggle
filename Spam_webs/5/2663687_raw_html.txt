      <h1 class="post-title">
       Jewellery Organisation &amp; Display
      </h1>
      <p class="metadata">
       <span class="date">
        25 Jan 2013
       </span>
       <a class="comments" href="http://www.alinaloves.com/2013/01/jewellery-organisation-display.html#disqus_thread">
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.alinaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/12596073927872540_4WyjgZEN_c.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       I adore looking at other peoples jewellery, trinkets and accessories, whenever I have an opportunity (be it in real life or online). They say so much about this certain person, dont you think? I also love seeing how ladies display their precious jewels and how they keep their necklaces and bracelets untangled (still a struggle for me). Filling up vintage teacups with little gems, lining up bracelets or necklaces on an antique bust, or having a dedicated stand on a dresser table are such original ways of displaying all the precious pieces of jewellery that we may have. I especially love these images and how the jewellery is prettily featured on them.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.alinaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/33988172158011583_ds3fz3KX_c.jpg"/>
