      <h4 style="text-align: center;">
       Without even knowing it, Ive been on an
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        <strong>
         anti-selfie campaign
        </strong>
       </span>
       for over a year now.
      </h4>
      <h4 style="text-align: center;">
       I startedtravelling solo in January of 2014 and have since visited more than 50 countries in all 6 continents. After 12 months had passed I returned home to see my family, packed my bags, and took off again.
      </h4>
      <h4 style="text-align: center;">
       Whilst at home I realised a certain trend had taken off. Becoming aware of such a trend made me equally nauseas and come to a sudden realisation that THAT was what all those sticks were about.
      </h4>
      <h4 style="text-align: center;">
       Ahh yes, the selfie.
