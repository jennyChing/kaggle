       Obama Seeks Dem Alliance With Islamic Groups During WH Meeting
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="post-content">
      <p>
       <img alt="obamairan" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-212659" height="310" src="http://weaselzippers.us/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/obamairan-550x310.jpg" width="550"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Yeah, Barack, they already vote Democrat.
      </p>
      <p>
       Via
       <a href="http://dailycaller.com/2015/02/05/obama-seeks-alliance-with-islamic-groups/" target="_blank">
        Daily Caller
       </a>
       :
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        President Barack Obama quietly met with a group of Muslim political activists in the White House Feb. 4 to get them to support the Democrats political machine.
       </p>
       <p>
        The meeting didnt focus on U.S. foreign policy or Islamic jihadi attacks. Instead, it was Obamas outreach to a growing Democratic-affiliated political constituency, similar to other meeting with environmental, African-American or Latino political groups. []
       </p>
       <p>
        The immigrant Muslim population has grown by roughly 1 million since 2000, up to almost 2.7 million in 2013. The population has grown because the federal government has accepted many migrants from war-torn Muslim countries, such as Somalia and Syria.
       </p>
       <p>
        Naturally, the U.S.-based Islamist groups want something in exchange for their cooperation with Obamas Democratic Party.
       </p>
       <p>
        They want federal jobs for their allies, diplomatic support for allied Islamist movements in the Middle East, the stigmatization of criticism of Islam, plus the legal authority for U.S.-based Islamists to independently police zealous U.S.-based Muslims who are preparing to become jihadis.
       </p>
