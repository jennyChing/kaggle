There is a small breakfast table in the kitchen that two can dine at with additional seating at the breakfast bar area and dining table.
            <br/>

            <br/>

This bedroom also offers a private balcony that is perfect to watch the spectacular sunsets. Both of these bedrooms share a jack-n-jill style bathroom with two sinks and a shower/tub combination. The second floor also has a laundry area with a full size washer and a full size dryer.
            <br/>

This bedroom also has its own private bathroom with a shower/tub combination. All beds are freshly made for your stay and bath towels are provided.
