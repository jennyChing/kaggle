      (&lt;span itemprop='rating' itemscope itemtype='http://data-vocabulary.org/Rating'&gt;&lt;span itemprop='average' class='bld'&gt;0&lt;/span&gt; out of &lt;span itemprop='best' class='bld'&gt;5&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;, &lt;span itemprop='votes' class='bld'&gt;0&lt;/span&gt; ratings)&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br class='clear'&gt;&lt;div class='editor-module'&gt;&lt;div class='module module-container' id='module1863872'&gt;&lt;div class='article-text'&gt;&lt;p&gt;There are many winter sports that have gained the attention of outdoor sports lovers these days. One of the most interesting winter sports is outdoor ice climbing. If you are familiar with rock climbing, then you can assume that ice climbing is somewhat similar to rock climbing. If you want to enroll for one of the short term ice climbing courses, you will be mostly provided by the gear. However, if you want to pursue ice climbing as a career, you will have to make sure that you rise from the beginner level to the expert level.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;There are some very good reputed mountaineering courses that are really good for beginners as well as for experts. Depending on your level, you can enroll for the desired course and make sure that the objectives are met. The &lt;b&gt;winter mountaineering courses&lt;/b&gt; have to be booked a few weeks in advance because there is a huge demand for the same. When you are enrolling for any winter mountaineering course, you will have to make sure that you have a good look at the terms and conditions.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;What are the security arrangements? What are the transportation costs? What are the hidden costs? These are some of the questions for which you will have to get an answer. Once you have an answer to these questions, you can then decide to enroll for the desired mountaineering course. If you are a beginner, you will have to be warned that mountaineering is not child&rsquo;s play and you have to be mentally and physically prepared to take on the challenges. More than the climbing, you will have to take on the atrocities of nature and difficulties of the hostile terrain.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;When you have to battle the harshness of the icy terrain, you will be testing your skills and physical endurance. That will be the time that you will get to know your true power. Always make sure that you have the best trainer or expert by your side to give you perfect guidance to come out of all difficulties and problems. There are several lessons that you will learn from the Snow &amp; ice mountaineering courses. You will understand that surviving in the cold climate is not so easy. There are some techniques that the trainers will teach you, which you have to keep in mind all the time.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;There are some specific mountaineering activities that are available to you. Depending on your interest, you can select the ones that are most liked by you. If you are suffering from asthma or heart related problems, then you will have to make sure that you consult your doctor before you enroll for any of the mountaineering courses. Some of the most popular options for mountaineering are: Abseiling, Aerial Assault Course, Canyoning, Rock Climbing, Skiing &amp; Snowboarding, Snow &amp; Ice Climbing, Telemark Skiing, and Winter Mountaineering.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;If you are at a beginner level, you will have to make sure that you have sufficient information related to the mountaineering or ice climbing techniques. The trainers of the mountaineering courses will make sure that you get the best knowledge in the right manner and in the stipulated timelines.
