           <h2 class="article-header">
            <a href="/16-use-a-bread-maker-to-put-some-pizzazz-in-everyday-meals">
             Use a Bread Maker to Put Some Pizzazz in Everyday Meals
            </a>
           </h2>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            <img alt="" height="480" src="/images/delicious-bread.jpg" width="640"/>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            Making bread by hand is fun. Its a great workout for your arms and shoulders, and its fun to watch the bread rise when the yeast starts doing its work. But you need a lot of extra time to make bread by hand, and if youre like me, you dont have that kind of time very often. I had a bread maker that was given to me as a present, and it was sitting on the shelf collecting dust. I decided that using a bread machine might be less fun than rolling the dough on a breadboard by hand, but it was still better than eating bland store-brand bread. Bread from the store is filled with all sorts of chemicals and preservatives I was trying to avoid, too.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            Bread machines have come a long way in the last few years. If you had a bread maker a ten or twenty years ago, you probably remember that it really didnt save all that much time over making the bread by hand, and most of the machines made really strangely shaped loaves. Thats all changed.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            <a href="http://breadkitchenstory.com/">
             <img alt="bread machine reviews" height="480" src="/images/peaking-from-the-bread-machine.jpg" width="640"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            Todays modern bread machines let you dump all the ingredients together instead of a lot of premixing. The loaf pans pop out for easier cleaning, too. Even though theres a lot less hands-on time, a modern bread machine turns out a loaf with the shape and consistency of the best bread you can buy at the supermarket, and it tastes fresh and wholesome hot out of the machine.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            Most people give up too easily when learning to use their bread machine. It can be hard to get the ingredients and the timer just right, but once youve tried a few loaves, its as easy as making a pot of coffee. After you begin to turn out nicely browned loaves, its time to tinker with the ingredients to make the bread into a real treat.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            <img alt="" height="428" src="/images/peanut-butter-jelly-sandwich.JPG" width="640"/>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            One of my favorite additives to perk up my loaves is sweetened condensed milk. I put in about one-half a cup in a regular recipe, and it gives the bread a silky consistency and extra flavor that makes a simple peanut butter and jelly sandwich into a banquet. You can cut the bread extra thick to give your sandwiches more body, and the leftovers make fantastic croutons and French toast, too.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
            Your bread maker can probably make other related foods. I use mine to make pizza dough while Im away at work. The bread machines timer makes perfect pizza dough in a few hours, ready for me the minute I walk in the door.
           </p>

            </a>
           </h2>

            <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: medium; line-height: normal;">
             The planet is full of numerous interesting in which some merely have no rational explanation to their origin and ending. There are lots of strange spots in the world but the strangest of all are guaranteed to make you go gaga. The idea of ideal retirement varies between individuals but there's one retirement hometown that made it into the news recently. This retirement hometown is referred to as The Villages and is reputed to be one of the largest retirement communities on the globe. Found in a location like Florida, The Vilages seems like an enjoyable retirement area. But this is far from the reality on the earth with The Hamlets having been thrust into the limelight due to the sheer variety of scandals and sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) reported there. There are classes given through the year and distinguished guest lectures comprise the likes of Deepak Chopra and Wayne Dyer.
