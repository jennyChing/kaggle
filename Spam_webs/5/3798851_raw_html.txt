         <div id="description-text">
          The story of this bracelet named Vignetta:
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Vignetta is a good listener.  She looks you deep in the eyes and peers into your soul. She is also the one who seems to be having the best time in a group at a party.  You can hear her joyous laughter through the noise of the crowd. Her name means little vine in French. She is a red wine lover, a loyal friend, she will dance with you until dawn or will be equally as interested in deep conversation with you until sunrise depending on the mood of the evening.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Part of my Wishes and Dreams Collection, I hand wire linked this bracelet with various red beads of garnet, red dyed serpentine jade, freshwater pearl, agate, crystal, and quality Czech glass, finishing it with a grape leaf toggle clasp.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Bracelet measures 7 and  inches long and is about  inches at it's widest point.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Arrives in a bow-wrapped gift box and pretty organza jewelry pouch, perfect for gift giving!
          <br/>
          Colors of gift box and pouch may vary.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/shimmershimmer" target="_blank">
           https://www.etsy.com/shop/shimmershimmer
          </a>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Quality jewelry and accessories each with their own flair to suit any personality and style for gift giving or a treat for yourself. All pieces here are professionally and lovingly created by me, and many are one-of-a-kind designs, thus giving you a unique item made especially for you. You are guaranteed quality made items, which come from my experience of creating and selling my crafts professionally to various boutiques and galleries for about 13 years.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Since I have so much experience by working in a bead store part time for about 13 years, and visiting the Tucson, AZ gem show often, which is the largest gem show in the world, I have a lot of knowledge about identifying gemstones and other bead materials. Living in Arizona, I am especially knowledgeable about Turquoise gemstones and being able to tell what is real, genuine Turquoise, and what is not. I have also had the chance to get to know certain gemstone bead sellers and learn about their sources and how to identify what the beads and stones are made of.
