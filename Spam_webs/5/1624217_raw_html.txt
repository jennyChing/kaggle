         <h2 class="post-title">
          Most Famous Non-traditional Engagement Rings
         </h2>
         <span class="byline">
          <time datetime="2014-03-04 11:47:25 +0000" pubdate="">
           January  6, 2014
          </time>
          <i>
           by
          </i>
          <a href="http://thoughts.com/profile/angarajewelry">
           angarajewelry
          </a>
         </span>
        </header>
        <div class="post-body">
         <div class="post-images">
         </div>
         <p>
          Celebrities always go above and beyond when it comes about a perfect proposal. From a beautiful holiday proposal theme to a rocking
          <strong>
           sapphire and diamond engagement ring
          </strong>
          , nothing could be usual. Of course, diamonds are a traditional choice for the proposal bling and even the A-list divas also love to flaunt them on the sacred finger. But it is not the ultimate choice for them.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Lately, many celebrities have ditched a diamond engagement ring for a gorgeous gemstone ring. While it was an off the track thing a decade ago, it is now a well established trend. Below we have listed most popular non-diamond engagement ring which have created a stir amongst brides. Each of the rings is an inspiration, and contributed a lot to the
          <a href="http://www.vogue.com.au/brides/trends/galleries/think+different+19+unconventional+engagement+rings,27591">
           unconventional rings trend
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong style="">
           Sapphire Engagement Ring:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong style="">
           <img height="260" src="http://www.blueborrowednew.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Elizabeth-Hurley-Engagement-Ring-e1325098289962.jpg" style="vertical-align: middle;" width="259"/>
           <img height="262" src="http://robbinsbros.files.wordpress.com/2010/06/jenna-bush-engagement-ring.jpg?w=300&amp;h=204" style="float: right;" width="383"/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Talking about non-diamond proposal rings  we have to start with the most famous sapphire engagement ring in the history. And what else except the
          <a href="http://www.cbsnews.com/news/kate-middletons-engagement-ring-is-princess-dianas/">
           18-carat sapphire and diamond engagement ring of Lady Diana
          </a>
          could be the most popular gemstone ring in the history of proposal jewels? We love
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diana,_Princess_of_Wales">
           Princess Diana
          </a>
          and her exotic choice. When Kate Middleton accept Prince Williams proposal with the same ring, it suddenly became a rage. The ring with center
          <strong>
           oval sapphire of 12 carats
          </strong>
          surrounded by 14 round brilliant diamonds was set in 18K white gold by Garrard. Even today, instead of the simple and traditional halo design, the ring is extremely popular amongst brides. The ring values around 85,000.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <strong style="">
           <img height="324" src="http://img2.timeinc.net/people/i/2012/stylewatch/blog/120305/elizabeth-smart-300x400.jpg" style="vertical-align: middle;" width="243"/>
           <img height="327" src="http://blog.helloonline.com/myweddingscrapbook/files/2012/11/princess-anne.jpg" style="float: right;" width="381"/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Do you know who else flashes
          <a href="http://www.aboutgemstonejewelry.com/sapphire-rings/blue-sapphire-rings-vs-pink-sapphire-rings">
           blue sapphire rings
          </a>
          ? Well, we have a long list for this competitor of diamond and it includes
          <a href="http://blog.hellomagazine.com/myweddingscrapbook/2012/11/the-engagement-rings-adorning-the-hands-of-the-british-royals.html">
           Princess Anne
          </a>
          ,
          <strong>
           Penelope Cruz
          </strong>
          ,
          <strong>
           Heather Mills
          </strong>
          ,
          <strong>
           Elizabeth Hurley
          </strong>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.glamour.com/weddings/blogs/save-the-date/2013/06/glees-matthew-morrison-engaged.html">
           Renee Puente
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.yourengagement101.com/blog/2012/02/elizabeth-smart-picks-a-sapphire-engagement-ring/">
           Elizabeth Smart
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://robbinsbros.wordpress.com/2010/06/25/celebrities-choose-colored-diamond-engagement-rings/">
           Jenna Bush
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong style="">
           Ruby Engagement Ring:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong style="">
           <img height="410" src="http://picturespaintedbythesun.files.wordpress.com/2011/11/royal_couple-copy.jpg" style="vertical-align: middle;" width="697"/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Alike sapphire, ruby rings are also a favored choice for engagement rings. The romantic appeal of the fiery red hues of ruby along with its remarkable history gives the brides more reasons to choose this July Birthstone. While royals like
          <strong>
           Princess Margaret
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           Sarah Ferguson
          </strong>
          received ruby and
          <a href="http://ezinearticles.com/?Things-to-Know-Before-Buying-a-Diamond-Engagement-Ring&amp;id=5453322">
           diamond engagement rings
          </a>
          , it was
          <a href="https://twitter.com/JessicaSimpson">
           Jessica Simpson
          </a>
          who re-introduced the lovely gem as the proposal stone. The singer picked a beautiful three-stone ring with center ruby of 5 carat flanked by diamonds totaling 5 carats.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong style="">
           Emerald Engagement Ring:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong style="">
           <img src="http://www.hellomagazine.com/imagenes//celebrities/201204177762/halle-berry-engagement-ring/0-36-616/halle-berry-ring--z.jpg" style="vertical-align: middle;"/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Halle Berry
          </strong>
          is happily married to Olivier Martinez and proudly flaunting her exotic
          <strong>
           emerald engagement ring
          </strong>
          . Do you know her special ring was created after great efforts and inputs from Martinez! The ring highlights a beautiful emerald set in yellow gold with a petite
          <a href="../what-should-you-look-for-in-each-of-the-4cs-of-diamonds">
           diamond
          </a>
          on each side. It also has a secret message inscribed on the inner side of the band. The ring is considered to be a perfect proposal piece but do you know there are few other
          <strong>
           famous emerald rings
          </strong>
          to get inspiration. Check out the famous rings of Jackie Kennedy and Wallis Simpson.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Be it
          <a href="http://www.angara.com/rings/sapphire-rings.html">
           natural sapphire rings
          </a>
          or fine emerald rings, they all make a stunning alternative to the
          <strong>
           diamond engagement ring
          </strong>
          , so you can choose any of them for your special day.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
