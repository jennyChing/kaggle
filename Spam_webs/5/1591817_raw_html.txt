          <span class="post-meta">
           March 13, 2015
          </span>
          <h1>
           No Knead Brioche Brown Butter Buns
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="post-content clearfix">
          <p>
           <img alt="No Knead Brioche Brown Butter Buns // The Sugar Hit" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4201" height="533" src="http://www.thesugarhit.com/wp-content/uploads/No-Knead-Brioche-Brown-Butter-Buns.jpg" width="800"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           This no-knead dough is so delicious, youre going to want to make a sleeping bag out of it. And I just might. And then Im gonna make a pillow out of that glaze and go to sleep in a sticky, buttery puddle of happiness.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           These are a classic cinnamon bun, made with no-knead brioche dough, and topped with a brown butter glaze. And that glaze is the stuff of dreams, I tell ya. And the dough is too. Theres a real sleep/dream theme to this post so far, isnt there? Lets go with it.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="No Knead Brioche Brown Butter Buns // The Sugar Hit" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4202" height="1200" src="http://www.thesugarhit.com/wp-content/uploads/No-Knead-Brioche-Brown-Butter-Buns-1.jpg" width="800"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Actually, you know what it is? I think Im tired. My man-friend and I went and checked out
           <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1791528/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1791528/', 'Inherent Vice']);" target="_blank" title="Inherent Vice">
            Inherent Vice
           </a>
           last night, and then came home and watched most of
           <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0104452/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0104452/', 'Housesitter']);" target="_blank" title="Housesitter">
            Housesitter
           </a>
           , which was on TV. So we had a lazy, late night.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Inherent Vice? Pretty good. Great cast, I love the 1970 vibe, thought the acting was great, but the story was a little meandering. Housesitter? Goldie Hawn + Steve Martin = amazing. Either of those two in anything and Im there.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           But today Im just generally tired, and Im pretty much over-the-moon about the fact that its Saturday tomorrow and I can sleep to my little hearts content. Im gonna sleep so hard, my pillow wont know what hit it.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="No Knead Brioche Brown Butter Buns // The Sugar Hit" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4204" height="1200" src="http://www.thesugarhit.com/wp-content/uploads/No-Knead-Brioche-Brown-Butter-Buns-3.jpg" width="800"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Before I go to bed, though, Im gonna stir together another batch of this dough, so that I can bake more brioche in the morning. And yes, I mean stir. Like in a bowl, with a spoon. This recipe is from the good folks at
           <a href="http://www.artisanbreadinfive.com/2008/09/24/brioche-dough-recipe-and-all-of-its-wonderful-uses" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.artisanbreadinfive.com/2008/09/24/brioche-dough-recipe-and-all-of-its-wonderful-uses', 'Artisan Bread in 5 Minutes a Day']);" target="_blank" title="Brioche Dough Recipe">
            Artisan Bread in 5 Minutes a Day
           </a>
           , and it is truly a miracle. The only change I made was to switch the honey out for plain sugar.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           So I suggest you do as Im going to, and get out a wooden spoon for a batch of this dough. And then after a quick roll and a few lazy hours of resting, you too can enjoy these glorious buns. And that (plus sleeping) is what Saturday mornings are all about, yo.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>

            No Knead Brioche Brown Butter Buns

           <div class="divERSHeadItems">
            <div class="ERSAuthor">
             Author:
             <span itemprop="author">
              Sarah Coates
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="ERSServes">
             Serves:
             <span itemprop="recipeYield">
              12
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSIngredients">
            <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               Batch No Knead Brioche Dough (link above in post)
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSSectionHead">
             For the filling:
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               stick (50g) butter
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               cup brown sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 tsp cinnamon
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               cup caster sugar
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSSectionHead">
             For the glaze:
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 tbsp (50g) butter
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 cup (130g) icing sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              Milk
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSInstructions">
            <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              First, make the dough as per the instructions on Artisan Bread. Stir together the ingredients, and then refrigerate overnight.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              The next morning, take the dough from the fridge, and roll it out into a large square, just under  inch thick.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Melt the butter for the filling and spread it over the dough, and then sprinkle over the sugars and cinnamon.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Roll up the dough tightly into a log, and slice into twelve buns. Place them onto a lined baking sheet, cover loosely with plastic and leave to rise for 1-1.5 hours.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Preheat the oven to 350F/180C and bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through. Set the buns aside to cool completely.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              To make the glaze, place the butter into a small saucepan over a medium heat. Melt the butter, and then continue to cook it, swirling the pan, until the butter foams up and turns a deep brown.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place the icing sugar into a small bowl, add the butter, and stir together, adding a splash of milk or as much as you need to make a glaze consistency.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Drizzle the glaze over the buns and serve.
             </li>
            </ol>
