         20 Facts You Never Knew About Antarctica
        </h2>
       </div>
       <!-- /post-header -->
       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         This post is about the remotest continent of the world  the highest, driest, windiest, emptiest, coldest place on earth. Here we present you 20 cool facts about Antarctica!
         <span id="more-2606">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. You cannot work in Antarctica unless your wisdom teeth and appendix are removed.
        </p>
        <p>
         Surgeries are not made at the stations in Antarctica, so you should have your wisdom teeth and appendix removed even if theyre absolutely healthy.
        </p>
