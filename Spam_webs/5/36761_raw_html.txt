        <h1 class="entry-title">
         5 Best Budget Friendly Beach Hotels in Miami
        </h1>
        <ul class="post-meta">
        </ul>
       </header>
       <div class="post-thumbnail">
        <a href="http://www.travelwithamate.com/5-best-budget-friendly-beach-hotels-miami/">
         <img alt="Crest Hotel Miami" class="attachment-post wp-post-image" height="240" src="http://cdn3.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/crest3.jpg" width="320"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Miamis South Beach is a favourite vacation spot for millions. Who wouldnt love the pristine beaches, warm ocean water, art-deco architecture, and boutique shopping? Celebrities flock there by the dozens, opening stores and filming reality television shows. And dont forget the people watching. Some of the most interesting characters on the east coast are found along Collins Avenue or in
         <a href="http://lincolnroadmall.com/">
          <span style="color: #042eee;">
           Lincoln Road Mall
          </span>
         </a>
         . Here are a few places that wont break the bank while you vacation like a star.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Hawaii Hotel Miami
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-25358" height="217" src="http://cdn4.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/663433_33_b-290x217.jpg" width="290"/>
         Hawaii Hotel Miami is right off A1A near the Normandy Shores Golf Club. This oceanfront hotel offers free Wi-Fi, continental breakfast, and beach access, making this place a steal. Enjoy the North Shore Open Space Park just a few blocks to the north. Take a short walk to Fifis Place for some great seafood. The place is small but the portions are generous. Little Brazil on Collins Avenue is exactly what it sounds like. This delightful restaurant serves traditional Brazilian food in a comfortable, classy atmosphere. Check out
         <a href="http://www.gogobot.com/miami--hotels">
          <span style="color: #1255cc;">
           Miami hotel reviews
          </span>
         </a>
         , and youll see this hotel is one of the most well-reviewed in the area.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Hawaii Hotel Miami" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-25356" height="545" src="http://cdn1.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/hawaii_ibiza_11-763x545.jpg" width="763"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Lorraine Hotel
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="Lorraine Hotel Miami" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-25360" height="192" src="http://cdn4.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2241284-Lorraine-Hotel-Hotel-Exterior-1-DEF-290x192.jpg" width="290"/>
         Lorraine Hotel is at 2601 Collins Avenue in Miami Beach. This hotel is family friendly and offers a free airport shuttle. Complimentary continental breakfast, wireless internet access, complimentary beach towels, and a furnished balcony are just a few of the features included in this beachfront find. Check out Collins Park for a fun and relaxing afternoon. The Indomania Restaurant is on site and a popular favorite among the locals. This area is known for its unique boutique shops full of clothing, local art, and souvenirs.
        </p>
        <h2>
         SoBe Hostel
        </h2>
        <p>
         South Beach Hostel is one of the cheapest ways to stay in Miami Beach. Accommodations start at $19 per person per night and include breakfast, daily housekeeping, lockers in every room, and pick-up service from the Miami International Airport. The beach is only two blocks away and you are within walking distance of hundreds of shops, bars, and restaurants. The Hangout Bar is on the ground floor of the SoBe Hostel for those days you just want a retreat from the hustle and bustle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="166902" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25359" height="266" src="http://cdn1.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/166902.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Crest Hotel Suites
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="Crest Hotel Miami" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-25354" height="217" src="http://cdn3.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/crest3-290x217.jpg" width="290"/>
         This art deco gem is so beautiful you wont believe your eyes, or the price. Just steps from Lincoln Road Mall, this delightfully restored 1939 boutique hotel has it all. An outdoor swimming pool, on site caf, and beach club access including lounge chair, umbrella, and cabana rentals are just a few of the amenities provided to make your stay more enjoyable.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Ocean Reef Suites
        </h2>
        <p>
         The 225 square foot studios are ideal for one or two people, while the 400 square foot suite can accommodate up to four. Every room has a galley kitchen stocked with plates and utensils, a microwave, and a mini refrigerator. This cost-saving feature means you dont have to pay restaurant prices for every meal. Bring your leftovers back and reheat them for another meal. Wi-Fi is free and so is the airport shuttle to this Collins Avenue bargain.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Ocean Suites Miami" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-25357" height="572" src="http://cdn1.travelwithamate.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/1157995412-763x572.jpg" width="763"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now that you know that
         <a href="http://www.timeout.com/miami/things-to-do/20-essential-things-to-do-in-miami">
          <span style="color: #042eee;">
           Miami Beach
          </span>
         </a>
         is not just for the rich and famous, you just need to decide which lodging is right for you.
        </p>
