        <p>
         Toys, or toyz should I say, have been slowly pushing their way into the adult market for a while now. From tiny smoking rabbits to fully fledged skeletal animals there is no doubt that these toys are definitely not for kids.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether its your thing or not, the craftsmanship that goes into making and personalizing these tiny little works of art is still pretty damn impressive. Pictured above is one of my personal favourites, Jason Freeny. His quirky, tongue-in-cheek toys based on popular characters never fail to make me smile and his
         <a href="http://web.mac.com/moistproduction/flash/index.html">
          website
         </a>
         includes some fun free wallpapers and step-by-step pictures of how he creates his pieces.
        </p>
