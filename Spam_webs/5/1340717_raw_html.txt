        New Entry

       <p>

       </p>
       <p>
        Friends hit Netflix on January 1st much to its fans delight and theres a good reason for that. Friends remains a show that you can watch over and over again with it ever growing stale or boring. Its an absolute testament to how good NBC comedy was back when this show was airing. Its tightly crafted character circle, real life scenarios and excellent dialogue means this show will most likely remain relevant for decades to come. Its streaming in full on Netflix and its well worth a go if youre new to the series.
       </p>

        <a class="button external" href="//www.netflix.com/AddToQueue?movieid=70153404&amp;qtype=INSTANT&amp;trkid=13752289&amp;authURL=1425660186291.sombhOKsrhmWXdrH1NN72oVNS%2B8%3D&amp;section=WATCHNOW" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

        New Entry

       </p>
       <p>
        Although short this modern adaptation of the classic Sherlock Holmes tales packs one hell of a punch. Split into smaller episodes similar to Black Mirror, the series follows Sherlock, played by Benedict Cumberbatch with his trusty sidekick Dr. Watson whos played by Hobbit star, Martin Freeman. Join them both solving mysteries in todays city of London. One of the absolute finest BBC series in existence.
       </p>

        <a class="button external" href="//www.netflix.com/AddToQueue?movieid=70202589&amp;qtype=INSTANT&amp;trkid=13752289&amp;authURL=1425660200981.hgiZkHDCiG1mOIZssRCe2yCq%2FAw%3D&amp;section=WATCHNOW" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

Jackson Jax Teller is a man in his early thirties struggling to find a balance between his old life of being in a biker gang and his new roles of being a father. A great cast and a great script scored Sons of Anarchy a golden globe which is why we have it in our top 25 TV series list.

He then builds an empire around the business dragging his family into quite a mess.
