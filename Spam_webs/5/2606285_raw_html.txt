        Reeses Peanut Butter Fluff Crepes Recipe
       </h1>

        <a href="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reeses-crepe.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="reese's crepe" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-467" height="930" src="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reeses-crepe-682x1024.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Is it breakfast or is it dessert? This yummy gooey Reeses Peanut Butter Creme Fluff Crepe is decadent and truly the peanut butter lovers dream!! Go ahead and have it for an indulgent breakfast  I wont tell! These are a lot easier to make than I thought they would be, and they turn out wonderfully! Would make for an impressive dessert if you are having guests! I think that it makes a great late night dessert for two, as well! Heres how to make these delish Reeses Peanut Butter Creme Fluff Crepes yourself:
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/20140522_126.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="20140522_126" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-470" height="371" src="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/20140522_126-1024x614.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <span style="color: #008080;">
         Crepes:
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        1 1/4 Cups Flour
        <br/>
        1 1/2 Teaspoons Sugar
        <br/>
        1/4 Teaspoon Salt
        <br/>
        1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
        <br/>
        3 Large Eggs
        <br/>
        2 1/4 Cups Milk (2%)
        <br/>
        3 Tablespoons Butter, Melted
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/20140522_75.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="20140522_75" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-471" height="412" src="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/20140522_75-1024x682.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Mix together the flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder until well blended. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well. Slowly pour in the milk and butter and mix for about 2 minutes until batter is smooth and thin.
       </p>
       <p>
        Heat nonstick pan over medium heat. The pan is key for making crepes. Spray the pan liberally with a nonstick cooking oil between each crepe. Pour 3/4 cup of batter into ~9 inch heated pan and pick the pan up to quickly swirl the batter around the pan until the edges are slightly creeping up around the sides. You want the batter to be coated evenly around the bottom of the pan. The crepe will cook quickly, and begin to create bubbles in the batter. Cook about 1-2 minutes on one side, then with a large flipper lift the edges around the crepe until you can completely get under the crepe. Lift the entire crepe and flip. Cook for about 1 minute on the other side.
        <br/>
        **Cooking temperatures could vary depending on your stove**
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/20140522_83.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="20140522_83" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-472" height="657" src="http://meetkristy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/20140522_83-965x1024.jpg" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Lift the crepe and set aside to continue to the next one. Makes about 8 crepes.
       </p>
       <h3>
        <span style="color: #008080;">
         Making the divine Peanut Butter Fluff Filling:
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        1 1/2 cups chopped Reeses Cups
        <br/>
        1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
        <br/>
        12 oz whipped topping
        <br/>
        Blend peanut butter and whipped topping gently until you have an amazing peanut butter whipped topping.
       </p>
       <p>
        Spread 1/3 Cup peanut butter fluff over crepe and add 1-2 tablespoons of chopped Reeses Cups. Roll the crepe up and add whipped topping, 1-2 more tablespoons of chopped Reeses Cups then drizzle with Hersheys chocolate syrup.
        <br/>
        Serve warm and enjoy!
       </p>
