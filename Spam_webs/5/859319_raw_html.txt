       <h1 class="entry-title">
        5 Coolest Job Picks You Might Be Looking For This week
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/08/23/5-coolest-job-picks-you-might-be-looking-for-this-week.html" rel="bookmark" title="2:25 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          August 23, 2013
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://startups.fm/author/yoda" title="View all posts by yoda">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/08/23/5-coolest-job-picks-you-might-be-looking-for-this-week.html">
          <img alt="Top-5-startup-jobs" class="alignnone" height="120" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/startupjobs-22aug-thumb.jpg" title="Top-5-startup-jobs" width="280"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         StartupsFM team is back with a list of top 5 coolest Jobs for this week. We like to keep you updated of everything happening in the startup world and jobs are something you might be searching for! We like to do all the searching for you so that you can sit back and go through the hot job picks of this week!
         <span id="more-3088">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/08/23/5-coolest-job-picks-you-might-be-looking-for-this-week.html">
          <img alt="top-5-most-trending-startup-jobs" class="alignnone" height="380" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/startup-jobs-21aug.jpg" title="top-5-most-trending-startup-jobs" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://blog.peerindex.com/careers/back-end-developer/" target="_blank">
          Back-end developer at PeerIndex
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/08/23/5-coolest-job-picks-you-might-be-looking-for-this-week.html">
          <img alt="peerindex-people-centric-social-analytics" class="alignnone" height="47" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/PeerIndex-logo.png" title="peerindex-people-centric-social-analytics" width="180"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          London
         </strong>
         based startup
         <a href="http://www.peerindex.com/" title="peerindex-homepage">
          PeerIndex
         </a>
         is a social web analytics company. It works mostly to draw the graph identifying who are influential and in what context do they influence the most. It is looking for an individual who can build massively scalable systems on distributed computing environments.
        </p>
        <p>
         You should know:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Core Java
         </li>
         <li>
          Hadoop Big Data stack.
         </li>
         <li>
          SQL
         </li>
         <li>
          Linux
         </li>
         <li>
          JVM
         </li>
         <li>
          AWS
         </li>
         <li>
          HBase
         </li>
         <li>
          Concurrent Programming
         </li>
         <li>
          Algorithm design
         </li>
         <li>
          Artificial Intelligence
         </li>
         <li>
          NLP
         </li>
         <li>
          Machine Learning
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <em>
          Theres just 20% research, 10% IT and 70% of engineering here!
         </em>
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.superawesome.tv/#careers.html" target="_blank">
          Senior .net Developer at SuperAwesome
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/08/23/5-coolest-job-picks-you-might-be-looking-for-this-week.html">
          <img alt="superawesome-leading-discovery-platform-for-kids-and-teens-in-UK" class="alignnone" height="58" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/superAwesome-logo.png" title="superawesome-leading-discovery-platform-for-kids-and-teens-in-UK" width="214"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.superawesome.tv/#home_layout_1.html" title="superawesome-homepage">
          SuperAwesome
         </a>
         is building a top discovery platform for kids between age 8-14 in UK and US. They are currently looking for a Senior .net developer who will contribute to their
         <a href="http://www.superawesome.tv/#who are we?/swapit.html" title="swapit-website">
          Swapit
         </a>
         website. It is a small but highly experienced team. SuperAwesome is moving really fast. If you think you can pace up with them, here you go with the job details-
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Expectations from you
         </strong>
         :
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          From front-end Javascript and CSS to database and SQL server, you need to be ready to work on all aspects of the system depending on requirements.
         </li>
         <li>
          Create, design and implement new features to enhance engagement for kids and teenagers as well as make it fun!
         </li>
         <li>
          Comfortable with a cross-functional work environment.
         </li>
         <li>
          Experienced commercially in C# and ASP.NET MVC, as well as front-end skills-CSS/Javascript/JQuery/related UI technologies.
         </li>
         <li>
          Comfortable with Linq to SQL, NHibernate or similar ORMs.
         </li>
         <li>
          SQL server and Couch DB are bonus!
         </li>
         <li>
          Continuous integration &amp; Deployment with Git, SVN, TeamCity.
         </li>
         <li>
          Aware of test-Driven Development and Lean Startup technologies.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Qualities appreciated, but not really mandatory
         </strong>
         :
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Knowledge about Agile methodology
         </li>
         <li>
          TDD and BDD experiences.
         </li>
         <li>
          Experience with node.js and Express.js, Python/Django/ Redis/other NoSql
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         The company is based in
         <strong>
          London and Dublin
         </strong>
         . This job has its posting in London.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="https://www.optimizely.com/jobs" target="_blank">
          Technical Support Representative at Optimizely
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://startups.fm/2013/08/23/5-coolest-job-picks-you-might-be-looking-for-this-week.html">
          <img alt="optimizely-website-optimization-made-easy" class="alignnone" height="47" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.startups.fm/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Optimizely-logo.png" title="optimizely-website-optimization-made-easy" width="218"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.optimizely.com/" title="optimizely-homepage">
          Optimizely
         </a>
         is an online optimization platform helping businesses to identify the right content for the right people at the right moment. In less than a span of 2 years, Optimizely has names like Starbucks, Disney and Marketo among its 4000+ paying customers.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          San Francisco
         </strong>
         based, this startup is looking for a Technical Support Representative with the following
         <strong>
          skills
         </strong>
         :
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Deliver happiness to every customer you interact with.
         </li>
         <li>
          Become a subject matter expert in Optimizely technology
         </li>
         <li>
          Respond to customer feedback and bug reports via email, live chat and phone.
         </li>
         <li>
          Debug customer issues and create reproducible test cases for engineers to resolve.
         </li>
         <li>
          Maintain ownership of cases until resolution, drive other teams as needed and set customer expectations
         </li>
         <li>
          Author knowledge-based articles / technical notes for our customers.
         </li>
         <li>
          File enhancement requests and work with product management to translate business needs to product requirements.
         </li>
         <li>
          Pioneer innovative ways to help our customers become even more successful with Optimizely by improving our resources, processes and customer interactions.
         </li>
         <li>
          You need to have a technical degree from a 4 year university (e.g.: computer science/ engineering/mathematics/etc).
         </li>
         <li>
          1-3 years of experience in technical support.
         </li>
         <li>
          Enjoy playing with Javascript, html and CSS.
         </li>
         <li>
          Led projects earlier from scratch to the finishing line.
         </li>

         <strong>
          perks

         </li>

          Unlimited vacation
         </li>
         <li>
          Clipper Cards for commute
         </li>
         <li>
          Company events: karaoke, show-n-tells, ski trips, retreats
         </li>
         <li>
          Top-of-the-line MacBook Pro and 30 monitor  in fact, any gear you want that will improve your productivity.
         </li>

He/she will be provided with all the expenditures that include travel and lunch, and extended perks like bonus! On the basis of your performance during the internship period, the company will decide whether you can join the team on a full time basis.
