        <p>
         I am so happy that our Pitbull dog named Maggie tried a new dog treat from Pedigree. It is called the Stackerz. It is in stick and has the filet mignon and bacon flavor in it. It has the real meat as the one ingredients.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <a href="http://s1356.photobucket.com/user/wifetoalineman/media/Reviews/snackerz_zps193d95d6.jpg.html" target="_blank">
          <img alt=" photo snackerz_zps193d95d6.jpg" border="0" src="http://i1356.photobucket.com/albums/q734/wifetoalineman/Reviews/snackerz_zps193d95d6.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </center>
        <br/>
        How do you think our Maggie likes it? You can see it from the pictures. I also included a video of her tasting her new dog treat. We seldom give her new treat. Now that we know that she likes the Stackerz, we will buy her this new dog treat.
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <a href="http://s1356.photobucket.com/user/wifetoalineman/media/Reviews/stackerz_zps6ab0e86c.jpg.html" target="_blank">
          <img alt=" photo stackerz_zps6ab0e86c.jpg" border="0" src="http://i1356.photobucket.com/albums/q734/wifetoalineman/Reviews/stackerz_zps6ab0e86c.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </center>
        <br/>
        Maggie is one special pitbull dog that we love the most, She came into our lives unexpectedly. She also helps our little girls dog allergy getting better. Maggie only deserve the best treat and that is from Pedigree Stackerz. She also had a dry food from Pedigree as well.
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
