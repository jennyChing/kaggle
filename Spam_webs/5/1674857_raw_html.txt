      <article class="post-2844 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail category-make tag-12-days-of-christmas tag-card-stock tag-christmas tag-cricut tag-design-space-star tag-garland tag-gift-bags tag-ornaments tag-paper-craft tag-twelve-days-of-christmas entry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Twelve Days of Christmas Gift Bags
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-19T10:40:26+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 19, 2014
         </time>
         |
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://albiongould.com/twelve-days-christmas-gift-bags/#comments">
           5 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Christmas is fast approaching and Team Ten is helping you get ready with some Twelve Days of Christmas crafts for the final round of
         <a href='&lt;a%20href="http://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=650298&amp;u=950558&amp;m=51766&amp;urllink=&amp;afftrack="&gt;Cricut Explore Machine + Basic Starter Set&lt;/a&gt;' target="_blank">
          Cricut
         </a>
         s Design Space Star! My girls take after me and love little packages, so I came up with a Twelve Days of Christmas Gift Bag project thats easy to make and has an extra surprise.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/twelvedays.jpg">
          <img alt="Twelve Days of Christmas Gift Bags and Garland" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2848" height="699" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/twelvedays-1024x699.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1911693_847155438680675_4849083728207235565_n.jpg">
          <img alt="12 Days of Christmas with Design Space Star Team 10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2849" height="960" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1911693_847155438680675_4849083728207235565_n.jpg" width="672"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For my gift bags, youll need:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          a selection of pretty cardstock
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href='&lt;a%20href="http://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=650298&amp;u=950558&amp;m=51766&amp;urllink=&amp;afftrack="&gt;Cricut Explore Machine + Basic Starter Set&lt;/a&gt;' target="_blank">
           Cricut Explore
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          scoring tool
         </li>
         <li>
          bakers twine
         </li>
         <li>
          tissue paper (optional)
         </li>
         <li>
          chocolates (highly recommended!)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Attach your scoring tool in the A slot. This was my first time using it, and I wish I had discovered it sooner! While the blade is cutting your project, the scoring tool is marking where you fold it, so you dont need to worry about straight lines or messing it up. Genius!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4939.jpg">
          <img alt="Scoring Tool" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2850" height="1024" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4939-682x1024.jpg" width="682"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I also figured out another neat trick (Im full of them today!). I used textured cardstock for this project, and found that if I put it on the mat with the texture down, the cut was cleaner and I could see the scoring lines easily. I used the Bow Treat Bag and Christmas Light designs in
         <a href='&lt;a href="http://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=604304&amp;u=950558&amp;m=51766&amp;urllink=&amp;afftrack="&gt;Cricut Design Space&lt;/a&gt;' target="_blank">
          Design Space
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4942.jpg">
          <img alt="bow bags" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2851" height="682" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4942-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4945.jpg">
          <img alt="Christmas lights" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2852" height="1024" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4945-682x1024.jpg" width="682"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The idea behind this is to make a bag and a light for each of the twelve days of Christmas. Put a light in each bag, along with some tissue paper. Im adding a chocolate in each one as an additional treat! Each day, pull out your light (while nibbling your chocolate), and add it to your bakers twine. At the end of the twelve days, you have a lovely little garland! Im making a set for each of my girls in their favorite colors.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4956.jpg">
          <img alt="Twelve Days of Christmas Gift Bags and Garland" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2853" height="699" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/DSCF4956-1024x699.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Please check out the rest of Team Tens Twelve Days of Christmas crafts! Theres a lot of inspiration for the Christmas season!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1911693_847155438680675_4849083728207235565_n.jpg">
          <img alt="12 Days of Christmas with Design Space Star Team 10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2849" height="960" src="http://albiongould.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1911693_847155438680675_4849083728207235565_n.jpg" width="672"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://onekriegerchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2-Days-of-Christmas-Ideas-created-with-the-Cricut-Explore-from-Cricut-Design-Space-Star-Team-10-CDSSTeam10.jpg">
          <img alt="!2 Days of Christmas Ideas created with the Cricut Explore from Cricut Design Space Star Team 10 #CDSSTeam10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8212" height="1000" src="http://onekriegerchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2-Days-of-Christmas-Ideas-created-with-the-Cricut-Explore-from-Cricut-Design-Space-Star-Team-10-CDSSTeam10.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
