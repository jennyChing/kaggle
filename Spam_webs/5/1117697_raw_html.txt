             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               If you crave the comfort of a fast food meal, but are embarrassed at the thought of a drive-thru  or worse, don't have a car  consider your first world problems over.
              </p>
              <p>
               Save yourself the torture and still get all the taste you crave by making your favorite greasy foods at home. The recipes vary in both difficulty and ingredients, but if you're dedicated
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/diy/">
                DIY
               </a>
               -er, you should be up for the challenge.
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/01/31/fast-food-wine-pairings/">
                 8 Wines to Pair With Your Fast Food
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               You may even be surprised at how easy it is to make your own food. For example, the Big Mac special sauce isn't really that special  it's just a blend of spices, mayo and mustard.
              </p>
              <p>
               You can do it. We have faith.
              </p>
