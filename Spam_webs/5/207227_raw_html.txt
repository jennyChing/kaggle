           <h2>
            Property description
           </h2>
           <p>
            Waterfront cottage directly on the beautiful Bras dOr Lake. Stunning sunsets!
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The property is magnificent as a starting point to explore wonderful Cape Breton Island!
            <br/>
            The cottage has a living room, sunroom, fully euqipped kitchen, one 3-pc bathroom and two bedrooms with AC / heat units. The gas fireplace guaranties cozy evenings.
            <br/>
            There are two lake view decks with patio furniture, one with a BBQ.
