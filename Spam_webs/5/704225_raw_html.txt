           <h1 class="entry-title margin-bottom-30">
            Lisa Bloom: Documents Reveal Grand jury Voted to Indict JonBenet Ramseys Parents in 1999
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta margin-bottom-30 hidden-print" style="display:none">
            <span class="posted-on">
             <time class="entry-date published pull-left" datetime="2013-10-28T12:39:56+00:00">
              Oct 28, 2013
             </time>
             <time class="updated hidden" datetime="2013-10-28T13:53:37+00:00">
              Oct 28, 2013
             </time>
            </span>
            <span class="byline">
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://nakedlaw.avvo.com/author/lisa-bloom">

             </span>
            </span>

           <!-- .entry-meta -->
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p class="entry-thumbnail margin-bottom-30">
            <img alt="Lisa-Bloom-JonBenet" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="434" src="http://nakedlaw.avvo.com/files/2013/10/Lisa-Bloom-JonBenet-710x434.png" width="710"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Recently released court documents show that the parents of JonBenet Ramsey, the murdered 6-year-old beauty queen, were initially facing felony charges.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="" allowtransparency="true" class="wistia_embed" frameborder="0" height="375" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" name="wistia_embed" oallowfullscreen="" scrolling="no" src="//fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/i6crv1n6fd" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="500">
            </iframe>
            <script async="" src="//fast.wistia.net/assets/external/E-v1.js">
            </script>
           </p>
           <p>
            Hi, Im Lisa Bloom for
            <a href="http://www.avvo.com" title="Avvo">
             Avvo.com
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Theres a new document thats been unsealed in the JonBenet Ramsey case out of Colorado thats raising a lot of questions today. JonBenet was a little six year old beauty queen who was murdered in her own home back in 1996. And the crime has never been solved. Her parents were initially suspects in the case. Investigators looked at them like any family member in order to rule them out. And initially they werent able to do so. In 2008 the parents were completely cleared of any wrong doing in the matter based on new DNA evidence that indicated a stranger had come into the home.
           </p>
           <p>
            Lets go back again to the 1990s because what were learning now is that in 1999 a grand jury had actually recommended that felony charges, child abuse resulting in murder, be brought against the parents. And at that time, the district attorney who I assume reviewed everything in the case took a look at that grand jury indictment and said no. The district attorney did not want to go forward and press charges against the parents. And as I said, in 2008 a second district attorney reviewed the matter and determined that the parents were victims and we not perpetrators.
           </p>
           <p>
            So this is an interesting new piece that shows that at least in one point and time the parents could have faced criminal charges. But I see it more as a historical document in the case. But yes, initially the parents were under suspicion, but since then they have been completely cleared. And this doesnt raise any new evidence to suggest that the parents were guilty of wrong doing in the case. Unfortunately, the killer of this little girl has never been found.
           </p>
           <p>
            Im Lisa Bloom for
            <a href="http://www.avvo.com" title="Avvo">
             Avvo.com
            </a>
            .
           </p>
