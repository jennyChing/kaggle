&lt;div class="articleContent"&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Top National Football League quarterbacks are superstars and celebrities. Defenses win Super Bowl championships, an opinion that was reinforced and in a big way last February. A strong running back can, to the credit of those athletes, be the backbone of an offense capable of winning a title.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Take last season&#8217;s Seattle Seahawks as an example. Yes, the Seattle defense was downright dominant during the team&#8217;s Super Bowl run, but make no mistake about the fact that the &#8216;Hawks would not have won it all without having their starting running back on the roster.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The NFL is certainly a passing league, but there is still a place in pro football for dynamic running backs. Here are the 10 highest-paid NFL RBs for the 2014 season.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;i&gt;Cap numbers via &lt;a href="http://www.spotrac.com/rankings/nfl/cap-hit/running-back/limit-25/"&gt;Spotrac&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

          <h2>
           <b>
            10. C.J. Spiller  Buffalo Bills: $5,916,666 cap hit
           </b>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2894519" style="width: 3726px">
           <div class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Timothy T. Ludwig-USA TODAY Sports Images" data-image="wp-content/uploads/2014/08/USA_C.J.-Spiller.jpg" data-size="0:cdn/320/227/90/cw/,321:cdn/450/319/90/cw/,481:cdn/718/510/90/cw/,768:cdn/864/613/90/cw/" data-size2x="0:cdn/480/341/90/cw/,321:cdn/675/479/90/cw/,481:cdn/1077/765/90/cw/,768:cdn/1296/920/90/cw/" data-src-base="http://static7.therichestimages.com/" style="padding-bottom: 71.044133476857%;">
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Timothy T. Ludwig-USA TODAY Sports Images" src="http://static7.therichestimages.com/cdn/1296/920/90/cw/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/USA_C.J.-Spiller.jpg" title="Timothy T. Ludwig-USA TODAY Sports Images"/>
            </noscript>
           </div>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Timothy T. Ludwig-USA TODAY Sports Images
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Spiller has been the subject of some trade rumors following what was, for him, somewhat of a down season. He averaged half of a yard less per carry (4.6) than his career average. Spiller rushed for only two touchdowns in 15 contests. He had zero receiving scores.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Bills have thus far denied all reports that they are shopping Spiller. For Buffalo, seeing what Spiller can do in the teams offense could be wise. Dynamic wide receiver Sammy Watkins was drafted by the club in May, and Buffalo having an improved passing attack could lead to Spiller having a bounce-back season.
          </p>
