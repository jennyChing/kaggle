         <p>
          Tech workers who produce motherboards sometimes have a little fun, even though the odds are high that their work will never be seen by anyone. Redditor jazzmonster
          <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/funny/comments/2b79of/a_hidden_message_underneath_the_circuit_board/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            posted a picture
           </strong>
          </a>
          of a motherboard
          <a href=" http://imgur.com/DDvRl4F" target="_blank">
           and said,
          </a>
          I opened my broken Trace Elliot amp to have a look at the PCB and there was a hidden message printed underneath. Now, this is no good reason to start tearing your electronic equipment apart, but if you ever need to, check for hidden messages while youre there.
         </p>

             I was doing a little reverse engineering of a circuit board from Jack Deville - taking each component off and tracing the connections to get an idea how the board worked, and under the main chip was a message 'Concede Defeat, Retain Pride'.  I can't imagine too many people would see that, but it made my day.
