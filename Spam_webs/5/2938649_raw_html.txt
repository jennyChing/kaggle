        This Angry Yoga Teacher Is the Tragically Funny Debbie Downer of Yoga Class (VIDEO)

      <div class="post_content" itemprop="articleBody">
       <p>
        Have you seen this? Its really something. Angry Yoga has been making the rounds on the interwebs and for good reason. Its a total hoot, you guys.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="22min-AngryYoga" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-46239" height="281" src="http://yogadork.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/22min-AngryYoga-500x281.jpg" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        This parody video is from a show called This Hour Has 22 Minutes a long-running Canadian
        <a href="http://www.cbc.ca/22minutes/" target="_blank">
         comedy series
        </a>
        on CBC.In the sketch, the yoga teacher eschews any kind of light and fluffy stuff (you know
        <a href="http://yogadork.com/2011/11/15/top-10-yoga-cue-translations-flutter-my-what/" target="_blank">
         what we mean
        </a>
        ) and shoots right for the real talk. Well, mostly she complains about her problems and the worlds impending doom, which is ironic, funny (also, sad because its true), and from a certain point of view, strangely refreshing in a what if Louis CK taught yoga kind of way.
       </p>
       <p>
        Man buns, the apple butter police, the greedy government  all open targets for this angry yoga teacher, whos just airing her grievances and saying what a lot of us might
        <em>
         feel
        </em>
        like saying sometimes, but dont.
       </p>
       <p>
        And releaseand just feel the weight of the world on your back, and its crushing you. But stay in the present because there is no futurebecause the government sold it to the highest bidder!
       </p>
       <p>
        How delightful!This woman is basically the Debbie Downer of yoga, and if we didnt laugh, wed cry.
       </p>
       <p>
        As it turns out, the video is a parody PSA were supposed to believe is from Canadas Medicinal Pot Growers: Sometimes yoga doesnt cut it. Ask your doctor if marijuana is right for you. Oh, Canada.
       </p>
       <p>
        Watch the funny sketch below, and if you like it, follow it up with the Angrier Yoga and Blue Yoga sequels.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/BH0l4FlZT-A?rel=0" width="560">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Just go ahead and take your fist, roll it into a ball and shove it into your mouth and just exhale into a little scream.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/TUM51JNkb3M?rel=0" width="560">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Yoga on Blue Monday is just the pits!
       </p>
