          <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
           In and Around Orlando: Family Attractions for Kids of All Ages [graphic]
          </h1>
          <div class="meta">
           <span class="date updated">
            June 16, 2014
           </span>
           <span class="categories">
            <a href="http://blog.ihg.com/iconic-pictures" rel="category tag">

           <h2>
            <a href="http://blog.ihg.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Orlando-Infographic1.png" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://blog.ihg.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Orlando-Infographic1.png', '']);">
             <img alt="Orlando Infographic" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-571" height="5903" src="http://blog.ihg.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Orlando-Infographic1.png" width="1000"/>
            </a>
           </h2>
           <h2>
            A Few Points Of Interest To Look Into During Your Trip To Orlando, FL
           </h2>
           <h3>
            Kissimee
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Old Town
              </strong>
               Check out specialty stores, amusement rides, food and fun
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.gatorland.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.gatorland.com', 'Gatorland ']);">
               <strong>
                Gatorland
               </strong>
              </a>
               Try out the Gator Zip Line, visit the petting zoo or enjoy an animal show
             </li>
            </ul>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <a href="http://www.celebration.fl.us" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.celebration.fl.us', 'Celebration']);">
             Celebration
            </a>
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Farmers Market
              </strong>
               Visit the open-air market Sunday mornings
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Boardwalk
              </strong>
               Take a walk and enjoy the scenery
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Town Center
              </strong>
              In the summer, grab a bite to eat and relax while the kids splash in the pool. In the fall, watch the lamp posts rain leaves, and frolic in the snow over the holidays
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.sweetescapecelebrationspecials.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.sweetescapecelebrationspecials.com', 'Sweet Escape Celebration']);">
               <strong>
                Sweet Escape Celebration
               </strong>
              </a>
               Eat at an authentic European style bakery and deli
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Lakeside Park
              </strong>
               Take a break from Orlandos hustle and feed the ducks
             </li>
            </ul>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            International Dr.
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.funspotattractions.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.funspotattractions.com', 'Fun Spot']);">
               <strong>
                Fun Spot
               </strong>
              </a>
               Visit the arcade, hop on a ride or race go-karts
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.ripleys.com/wp/orlando/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ripleys.com/wp/orlando/', 'Ripleys Believe It or Not']);">
               <strong>
                Ripleys Believe It or Not
               </strong>
              </a>
               Explore a variety of artifacts and displays in unique galleries that pay tribute to the odd and strange
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.wonderworksonline.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.wonderworksonline.com', 'Wonderworks']);">
               <strong>
                Wonderworks
               </strong>
              </a>
               Discover the science centers interactive exhibits, from natural disasters to space to physical challenges
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Mini Golf
              </strong>
               Ahoy, me hearties! Tee up at Pirates Cove Adventure Golf
             </li>
            </ul>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Downtown Orlando
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.nba.com/magic" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.nba.com/magic', 'Orlando Magic']);">
               <strong>
                Orlando Magic
               </strong>
              </a>
               For all NBA basketball lovers
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Lake Eola
              </strong>
               Enjoy a picturesque view of the city, rent a boat, or dine at one of the restaurants around the lake
             </li>
            </ul>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <a href="https://www.kennedyspacecenter.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.kennedyspacecenter.com/', 'Kennedy Space Center']);">
             Kennedy Space Center
            </a>
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Lunch With An Astronaut
              </strong>
               Meet a NASA astronaut while enjoying a chef-prepared buffet lunch
             </li>
             <li>
              <strong>
               KSC Up-Close Tours
              </strong>
               Go behind the scenes and get up close to key areas in the worlds largest working spaceflight facility
             </li>
            </ul>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
