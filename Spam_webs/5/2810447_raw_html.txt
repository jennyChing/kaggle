        Emani Minerals review
       </h2>

      <p>
       Ive been seeing Emani Minerals reviews all over the internet, and Ive been drying to try this brand. Yes I am officially, 100%, makeup-spoiled! I was so excited to have the chance to do an Emani Minerals review, and this is one case where the reality lived up to the hype. Keep reading to hear the details of my
       <strong>
        <a href="http://dearcrissy.com/emani-minerals-makeup/" title="Emani Minerals Makeup Review  Love Collection">
         Emani Minerals makeup
        </a>
       </strong>
       review!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Emani Minerals Makeup Review" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1146" height="364" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/emani21.jpg" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       I received quite a selection of products for my Emani Minerals review.
      </p>
      <p>
       I was able to create a complete look using the Emani products and tools, and I was very pleased with the warm summery result that I achieved. I have to admit that I was a little skeptical of Emani Minerals at first, as they boast makeup that is 100% vegan. In fact, Emani Minerals products are
       <strong>
        vegan &amp; gluten friendly, cruelty free, not tested on animals, and the paper goods are biodegradable and 100% recycled (25% post &amp; 75% pre-consumer)
       </strong>
       . I mean, wow. Being such an amazing green makeup option, there are certainly drawbacks in quality, right? WRONG!
      </p>
      <p>
       First off, Emanis
       <a href="http://www.emani.com/Nude-Beige-G10-(Face)-pd-113_1.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Pressed Mineral Foundation
       </a>
       is lovely, and allows you to create a natural look by applying just a little, or a gaining fuller coverage by applying additional layers. Again, this is a vegan product, and the Pressed Mineral Foundation is infused organic rosemary and grape seed extract. Its so nice to know that the makeup Im applying to my face is actually great for my skin!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Emani Minerals Makeup Review" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1145" height="340" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/emani11.jpg" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       I was also particularly impressed with Emanis
       <a href="http://www.emani.com/Mineral-Hybrid-Cream-p-115.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Mineral Hybrid Cream
       </a>
       , which is completely paraben-free, talc-free, gluten-free,
       <em>
        and
       </em>
       is handmade! The Hybrid Cream is made of natural waxes and oils, and is an absolutely unique choice for eyes, cheeks, and lips. You have to try this blend of shimmers and mineral pigments for yourself to fully realize how beautiful it is.
      </p>
      <p>
       The Mineral Hybrid Cream is also a great base for your eyelids when you are using the
       <a href="http://www.emani.com/Crushed-Mineral-Color-Dust-p-114.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Crushed Mineral Dust
       </a>
       . The Crushed Mineral Dust comes in a variety of gorgeous colors, and Id like to have every single one of them!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Emani Minerals Makeup Review" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1147" height="329" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/emani31.jpg" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       I also have to mention a few of the other products that I am in love with, including the
       <a href="http://www.emani.com/Organic-Lip-Shine-p-125.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Organic Lip Shine
       </a>
       , that smells good enough to eat, the
       <a href="http://www.emani.com/Duo-Eye-Shadow,-Filler-Liner-p-118.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Duo Eye Shadow, Filler, and Liner
       </a>
       my new go-to choice for a beautiful smoky eye lineand the awesome Soy Infused Mascara!
      </p>
