     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Danger 5
       </b>
      </i>
      is an
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Australian" title="Australian">
       Australian
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Action_film" title="Action film">
       action
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Comedy" title="Comedy">
       comedy
      </a>
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Television_series" title="Television series">
       television series
      </a>
      which premiered on
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SBS_One" title="SBS One">
       SBS One
      </a>
      on 27 February 2012.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      It is set in a bizarre 1960s interpretation of
      <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">
       World War II
      </a>
      and follows a group of five international spies on a mission to kill
      <a href="/wiki/Adolf_Hitler" title="Adolf Hitler">
       Adolf Hitler
      </a>
      and thwart his plans of world domination.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      The
      <a href="/wiki/Men%27s_adventure" title="Men's adventure">
       Men's adventure
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Pulp_magazine" title="Pulp magazine">
       pulp magazine
      </a>
      -inspired series was created by Dario Russo and David Ashby. On 11 September 2014, the
      <i>
       Danger 5
      </i>
      crew announced that series 2 had been delayed due to unforeseen international news events and the recent
      <a href="/wiki/Islamic_State_of_Iraq_and_the_Levant" title="Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant">
       ISIS
      </a>
      actions.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      They also released the series two trailer on the same day.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
       <a href="#cite_note-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      The second series eventually began airing on
      <a href="/wiki/SBS_Two" title="SBS Two">
       SBS 2
      </a>
      on 4 January 2015, and is based in the mid-1980s.
     </p>

     </h2>
     <h3>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Development">
       Development
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Danger_5&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Development">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h3>
     <p>
      After the success of the web series
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Italian_Spiderman" title="Italian Spiderman">
        Italian Spiderman
       </a>
      </i>
      created by Alrugo Entertainment (Russo, Ashby, Tait Wilson, Will Spartalis and Boris Repasky), Australian broadcaster
      <a href="/wiki/Special_Broadcasting_Service" title="Special Broadcasting Service">
       SBS
      </a>
      became interested in turning the project into an
      <a href="/wiki/Interstitial_program" title="Interstitial program">
       interstitial television show
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-autogenerated1_5-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-autogenerated1-5">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        5
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      Due to ownership and copyright issues within the production team, the project fell through. Nonetheless, SBS were still interested in Russo and Ashby and in 2009 offered a development deal for a new show.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">
       <a href="#cite_note-6">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        6
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      Russo and Ashby submitted three concepts that, regardless what was chosen, they would be happy to make.
      <i>
       Danger 5
      </i>
      was the chosen concept, the most ambitious and dense of the three.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-autogenerated1_5-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-autogenerated1-5">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        5
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
