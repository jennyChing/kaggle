      <img alt="Snack Girl Cherry Cheesecake Bites" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-2184" data-jpibfi-description="Snack Girl Cherry Cheesecake Bites" height="413" src="http://www.emilybites.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Guest-Cherry-Cheesecake-Bites-620x413.jpg" width="620"/>

     <p>
      Hi, Im Lisa! Because I write
      <a href="http://www.snack-girl.com/">
       Snack Girl
      </a>
      I receive all sorts of requests for low calorie recipes. One reader wanted me to make a lighter version of cheesecake.
     </p>
     <p>
      Cheesecake? Nooooooo.I am not a miracle worker! But, I did come up something that will remind you of cheesecake.
      <span id="more-2183">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      Make these Cherry Cheesecake bites in all of five minutes.Wash them, dip them in a mixture of cream cheese and powdered sugar, dip them again in mashed graham crackers  and you got a treat that will hit that cheesecake sweet spot.
     </p>
     <p>
      If you cant find cherries, this will work with strawberries or even slices of banana.
     </p>
     <p>
      You can refrigerate these for a party later in the day. I would eat them the same day that I make them because the graham crackers will get soggy.
     </p>

        <img align="right" class="photo nopin" data-jpibfi-description="" height="125" nopin="nopin" src="http://www.emilybites.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Guest-Cherry-Cheesecake-Bites-190x145.jpg" width="125"/>
        <span class="item">
         <h2 class="fn">
          Cherry Cheesecake Bites
         </h2>
        </span>
        <div class="time">
         <p>
          <strong>
           Yield:
          </strong>
          <span class="yield">
           20 bites
          </span>
         </p>
        </div>
        <h3 id="ingredients">
         Ingredients:
        </h3>
        <div class="ingredients">
         <ul>
          <li class="ingredient">
           20 fresh cherries
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient">
            cup whipped cream cheese
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient">
            cup powdered sugar
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient">
           4 graham crackers
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <h3 id="directions" style="clear:left">
         Directions:
        </h3>
        <div class="instructions">
         <ol>
          <li>
           Wash cherries and put them in a small bowl. Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar together until mixture forms a paste. Place graham crackers in a plastic bag and crush with the back of a wooden spoon. Put cracker crumbs in another small bowl.
          </li>
          <li>
           One cherry at a time, dip cherry into cream cheese mixture and then graham cracker crumbs. Place on plate or baking sheet and refrigerate until ready to eat or eat immediately.
          </li>
         </ol>
        </div>
