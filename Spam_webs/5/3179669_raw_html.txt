       <h1>
        Get Started on Home Automation with Your Security System
       </h1>
       <div class="entry">
        <div addthis:title="Get Started on Home Automation with Your Security System " addthis:url="http://www.marylandsecurityalarms.com/blog/get-started-home-automation-security-system/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">

        <p>
         <a href="http://www.marylandsecurityalarms.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/custom-home-automation.jpg">
          <img alt="security system" class="alignright wp-image-832" height="156" src="http://www.marylandsecurityalarms.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/custom-home-automation-300x251.jpg" width="186"/>
         </a>
         Home automation has evolved from a niche pipe dream into a quickly growing enterprise and its not hard to see why. Home automation can save Maryland homeowners a significant amount of money on their energy bills and afford them additional control over their HVAC, home theatre, and home security systems. But many homeowners struggle on taking the first step. Where does home automation start? Chesapeake System Services, your Maryland home automation provider, is here with the reasons why automating your security system is the best place to begin this process.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Double-Duty Sensors
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         The most fundamental piece of any Maryland security system is the sensor. Motion sensors can detect trespassers on your lawn or in your house, contact sensors tell you if a door or window is left open or opened by an intruder, etc. Sensors in a home security system alert you to the presence of activity when there shouldnt be any. But they can also play a vital part in total home automation. The same sensors that form the bedrock of your security system can also be configured to simultaneously perform other tasks. A motion sensor that detects unwanted intruders while youre at work can also tell your home theatre system to turn on your favorite channel or radio station when you get home, or automatically turn on your HVAC system.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Gain Interface Experience
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Home automation can be controlled through a variety of interfaces including touch panel, keypad, and mobile app. Getting used to controlling your Maryland home through these applications can seem tricky at first. When you start out using a security system you will be forced to interact with it several times a day, building experience and gaining confidence in your ability to use the system. This makes adding additional layers of home automation a snap and will allow you to control them comfortably upon first use.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          An Affordable First Step
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Many Maryland homeowners worry about immediately investing in a complete home automation system only to find out they dont like it. Because home security systems are relatively inexpensive compared to other elements of home automation, they can be an affordable way to test the waters and see if home automation is definitely for you. When youre ready you can add additional elements like lighting or HVAC control at a pace that works for you.
        </p>
