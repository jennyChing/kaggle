          <p>
           So many exciting things are happening over the coming months and better yet, some new games are int he pipeline, maybe nearly here is better suited. The new highly anticipated GTA V is nearly here on both Playstation and Xbox; anyone who is a fan of the GTA franchise would no doubt be looking forward to the release and if you are still contemplating, give the game a quick search on YouTube, that should hopefully put you in the excited category.
          </p>
          <p>
           Grand Theft Auto is a classic and has everything it takes to keep gamers entertained and locked on to their controllers; it has the women, cars, money and most importantly the game play alongside the graphics is just too much to describe in words. If you are like me, the cost is something that perhaps holding you back,
           <a href="http://www.musicmagpie.co.uk/blog/article.asp?id=813" target="_blank" title="GTA V musicMagpie competition">
            GTA V musicMagpie competition
           </a>
           is currently happening, and it gives you the chanceof winning yourself a collectors edition of the game as-well as a Limited Edition PlayStation 3, so not only do you get the game, you also get a console to play it on.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/17wVptd" target="_blank" title="GTA V musicMagpie competition">
            <img alt="game piece - gta comp buttona 2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10081" height="69" src="http://www.gadgetsboy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/game-piece-gta-comp-buttona-2.jpg" width="373"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           GTA V is the gaming release of the year and with the collectors edition, youll be getting the best of everything GTA has to offer; as-well as the game itself, youll benefit from exclusive downloadable content, custom characters for GTA Online, a collectable SteelBook complete with exclusive Artwork, a Blueprint map, a New Era GTA V Snapback cap and a GTA V Security Deposit Bag. If all that wasnt enough then youll get to play the game on a limited edition PlayStation 3!
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre one of the only people on the planet who hasnt heard much about GTA V, its set to take gaming to a whole new level. Set in the fictionalised city of San Andreas, the games storytelling focuses on the pursuit of the almighty dollar. For the first time in the history of the franchise, the game will have a multi-character structure focusing on Michael, Franklin and Trevor; three very different people who come together to pull off a series of heists.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gamers will be able to switch perspectives whenever they like and drop in on any of the characters to see what they are up to. As-well as the central missions, theres the usual truck load of side missions to get to grips with, or you can just treat the city as your very own playground  so what are you waiting for? Just click the button above to enter our competition and you could be causing mayhem in San Andreas before you know it!
