              <p>
               Working from home can be very rewarding, both financially and in terms of lifestyle. Flexible hours, no sitting in traffic, reduced costs, the list goes on. Best of all, you can assume that everything is covered in your home and contents insurance, right? Wrong.
              </p>
              <p>
               Working from home carries risks that arent necessarily covered under your home and contents policy. You may need business-specific insurance, such as:
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <a href="#Public Liability Insurance" style="color:red;">
                 Public liability insurance.
                </a>
               </strong>
               If you have customers or suppliers visiting, no matter how infrequently, this insurance offers protection against the small but serious risk that someone could injure themselves on your premises.
               <a href="http://businessinsurancehub.com.au/basics/what-public-liability-insurance">
                Public liability insurance
               </a>
               can also protect you if youre found responsible for damage to their property.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <a href="#Business Interruption Insurance" style="color:red;">
                 Business interruption insurance
                </a>
                .
               </strong>
               <a href="http://businessinsurancehub.com.au/basics/what-business-interruption-insurance">
                Business interruption insurance
               </a>
               can cover your ongoing business costs when your business operations are interrupted as a result of damage to your property or burglary.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                Income protection
               </strong>
               can help provide valuable financial support by covering part of your monthly remuneration or share of business income while you cant work due to sickness or injury.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                Accidental injury insurance
               </strong>
               can help you cover medical bills, loss of income or other costs by paying out a lump sum for specific accidental injuries.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <a href="#Money Insurance" style="color:red;">
                 Money insurance
                </a>
                .
               </strong>
               If you get paid in cash, you can insure money itself against loss through burglary, armed hold-up or fire. The money may be in transit or at your premises. Check your policy for details.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <a href="#Property Damage Insurance" style="color:red;">
                 General property insurance
                </a>
                .
               </strong>
               You can choose to cover valuable portable items (eg, mobile phones and laptops) that you usually carry around with you in the course of your business. Even travel overseas may be covered, depending on the policy. Its worth considering if you work from home but travel frequently.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <a href="#Equipment Breakdown Insurance" style="color:red;">
                 Equipment breakdown insurance
                </a>
                .
               </strong>
               This insurance covers the breakdown of equipment, such as computers. If, for example, you run a catering business from home and your refrigeration unit breaks down, your policy could cover you for the resulting deterioration of your consumable stock.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <a href="#Tax Audit Insurance" style="color:red;">
                 Tax audit insurance
                </a>
                .
               </strong>
               You might not welcome an audit by the Australian Taxation Office, but at least you can take out a policy to help cover your accountants fees.
              </p>

      <div class="block block-views" id="block-views-hub-glossary-block">
       <div class="content">
        <div class="view view-hub-glossary view-id-hub_glossary view-display-id-block view-dom-id-0047b57f29e8f787cc0612fb30993d72">
         <div class="view-header">
          <h2>
           The Jargon
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="view-content">
          <div class="views-row views-row-1 views-row-odd views-row-first">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Property Damage Insurance">
              Property Damage Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              Insurance cover for the repair or replacement of your building, business contents or stock for specified events such as fire, storm, lightning, explosion or earthquake.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="views-row views-row-2 views-row-even">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Public Liability Insurance">
              Public Liability Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              Public liability insurance covers your legal liability for personal injury, death or property damage to a third party that occurs as a result of your business activities.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="views-row views-row-3 views-row-odd">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Equipment Breakdown Insurance">
              Equipment Breakdown Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              Equipment breakdown insurance covers you for the cost of repairing or replacing your mechanical and electrical equipment and certain electronic devices following a breakdown.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="views-row views-row-4 views-row-even">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Business Interruption Insurance">
              Business Interruption Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              Business interruption insurance covers you for loss of gross profit resulting from an interruption to your business caused by an insured event such as a fire.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="views-row views-row-5 views-row-odd">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Money Insurance">
              Money Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              Money insurance covers loss of money through burglary, armed hold-up or fire at your premises or in transit.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="views-row views-row-6 views-row-even">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Tax Audit Insurance">
              Tax Audit Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              Tax audit insurance covers you for the professional fees (including accounting and legal costs) associated with an Australian Taxation Office (ATO) audit of your business.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="views-row views-row-7 views-row-odd views-row-last">
           <div class="views-field views-field-field-glossary-term">
            <div class="field-content">
             <a name="Personal Accident and Injury Insurance">
              Personal Accident and Injury Insurance
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="views-field views-field-body">
            <div class="field-content">
             <p>
              This insurance covers you against loss of income in the event of an accident and/or illness that prevents you from working.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
