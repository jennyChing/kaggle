         <section class="article-body-wrap">
          <div class="body-text clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
           <h5 style="text-align: right;">
            <b>
             Sponsored by
            </b>
            <a href="http://www.orias.com.au">
             StarTrack
            </a>
           </h5>
           <span class="dropcap2">
            I
           </span>
           <p>
            m a sucker for a cute crafty shop. I used to spend every cent of my pocket money at the markets when I was a kid. Friendship bracelets, cute little pens with stuff stuck all over them, notebooks, stickers and anything at all with a teddy bear on it I was all over it. Now that Im a grown up, Etsy (and online in general) is where I spend all my pocket money and by pocket money I mean the money I should be spending on food and toilet paper. I just love shopping online because I dont have to see people and I can do it from the couch with wine and no pants.
           </p>
           <p>
            In honour of lazy couch shopping, Im partnering with the
            <a href="http://www.orias.com.au">
             StarTrack Online Retail Industry Awards
            </a>
            (StarTrack ORIAS) to celebrate awesome Australian online retailers, which is beyond awesome. I love supporting local businesses as well as the economy so this partnership is a dead set dream. I bet youre wondering what the
            <a href="http://www.orias.com.au">
             StarTrack ORIAS
            </a>
            are. Theyre basically the Oscars but for online retailers. I hope the award is a statue of a little gold person in a little gold bed placing bids on eBay at 3am on a tiny little gold phone. That would be rad and accurate. Anyway, theyve been around since 2010 but this year for the first time ever they are introducing the Peoples Choice Award which means that you get to vote for your favourite Australian online stores.You could even win one of ten $1000 gift cards (T&amp;Cs apply). Voting closes 1 June, with finalists announced 17 June and the ultimate winner announced 23 July.
           </p>
           <p>
            To celebrate the online Aussie shops that I love, Ive popped together a list of my current favourite (and cutest) Australian Etsy stores. Enjoy!
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.683744556_7c63" class="aligncenter wp-image-19270 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19270" height="257" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.683744556_7c63.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            I just adore these little 3D printed and hand finished vessels from
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/shop/EachToOwn">
             EachToOwn.
            </a>
            Im half tempted to order a dozen and have them on hand for my mates birthdays. How much would you love to get one of these delivered to your desk with a teeny bunch of flowers and a cupcake? Perfection.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.572671066_ka2z" class="aligncenter wp-image-19260 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19260" height="402" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.572671066_ka2z.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Theres a whole faux glam movement going on at the moment that Im just loving. Did you know that in the jewellery design world theres unwritten rule that evening earrings drop from a stud (like the earrings pictured from
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/208779945/double-drop-earrings-lush-glitter?ref=shop_home_active_15">
             EachtoOwn
            </a>
            ) and casual earrings usually drop from hooks? These would super fab with a ball gown or on the ears of a quirky bride I reckon.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.743633300_ogf7-e1430105408239.jpg">
             <img alt="il_570xN.743633300_ogf7" class="aligncenter wp-image-19263 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://www.smaggle.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.743633300_ogf7-e1430105408239.jpg" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19263" height="384" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.743633300_ogf7-e1430105408239.jpg" width="400"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            If I worked in a corporate environment, Id totally make whacky blazers my thing. Tailored and classic but a little bit kooky. This jacket from
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/226633033/triassic-jacket-blue-green?ref=shop_home_active_16">
             ApartofmeAPOM
            </a>
            is gorgeous. They have the cutest prints in that shop  I love their
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/225638206/dinosaur-printed-skirt?ref=shop_home_active_12">
             dinosaur print skirt
            </a>
            too.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.684756214_es1s" class="aligncenter wp-image-19264 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19264" height="400" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.684756214_es1s.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Im not really one for fashion trends but I do get excited whenever statement necklaces make a comeback because I get a chance to stock up!
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/211770951/stella-blue-rope-simple-necklace?ref=shop_home_active_24">
             YellowTeapot
            </a>
            have some of the coolest statement necklaces Ive seen in years. Chunky (essential) but still streamlined and minimalist. Theyre necklaces that compliment your outfit, not dominate it you know?
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.619527840_ife2" class="aligncenter wp-image-19266 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19266" height="267" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.619527840_ife2.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Im not going to lie surrealist art usually confuses me or scares me. Im a dead set realist so I usually cant figure it out but theres something so calming about
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/187485096/float-on-print-art-print-illustration?ref=shop_home_active_20">
             AlexisWinter
            </a>
            prints. Shes got beautiful, weird little prints with all kinds of woodland creatures. Theyre dead cheap too so theyd make amazing little gifts.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.679909174_8rpy" class="aligncenter wp-image-19267 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19267" height="453" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.679909174_8rpy.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/210501325/life-planner-goals-journal-bucket-list?ref=shop_home_feat_2">
             MiGoals
            </a>
            is such an awesome little business to keep your eye on.Theyre a company that sell very simple and very awesome goals journals. Have a squiz at the inside of one of the journals and it will give you a
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/210501325/life-planner-goals-journal-bucket-list?ref=shop_home_feat_2">
             step by step guide
            </a>
            for solidifying a goal, justifying it and then reaching it. I have their day planner desk pad (and I LOVE it!) but next on my list is definitely one of their journals.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.738326891_btsu" class="aligncenter wp-image-19268 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19268" height="573" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.738326891_btsu.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            I have a craft store in my suburb that teaches night classes on how to make these macrame planters. Its adorable  there are always about eight super cute hipster looking girls, wearing clogs, drinking wine and crafting. Ive been meaning to pop along to one of these classes for months but I havent had the chance so when I saw these from
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/214774954/dip-dyed-orange-handmade-ombre-macrame?ref=shop_home_active_12">
             Scoutgathers
            </a>
            pop up in my recommended Etsy feed I was stoked. The perfect way to tide me over until I get to make my own.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.496590565_taqq" class="aligncenter wp-image-19265 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19265" height="287" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.496590565_taqq.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Im having a big moment in grandma style cushions and I just love this fabulous cross stitch printed cushion from
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/161316239/vintage-style-printed-cushion-cover-with?ref=shop_home_active_3">
             Esthersandler
            </a>
            . Im currently working on a crochet granny square cushion and I think this one would look rather excellent next to it.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="il_570xN.699840594_36kd" class="aligncenter wp-image-19269 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.smaggle.com/2015/05/08/australian-etsy-stores/" data-wp-pid="19269" height="601" src="http://g3yxi3w953w3xjjaj1xcbuay.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/il_570xN.699840594_36kd.jpg" width="400"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            How adorable is this cup and spoon friend pair from
            <a href="https://www.etsy.com/au/listing/215452358/cup-and-spoon-friends-ceramic-porcelain?ref=shop_home_active_6">
             Vanessabeanshop?
            </a>
            I think this would be the perfect mug to have a mug cake in! Ive been dying to try this
            <a href="http://www.babble.com/best-recipes/nutella-mug-cake/">
             Nutella mug cake
            </a>
            recipe for ages!
           </p>
           <p>
            Even though Etsy is absolutely my favourite online haunt Ive got at least four or five other stores that Ill be nominating. Adore Beauty is fantastic  theyre quick, cheap, reliable and you get a Tim Tam with every order. Im not even kidding. An actual Tim Tam. I also heard an amazing story about the
            <a href="http://www.birdsnest.com.au/">
             Birdsnest
            </a>
            girls from a friend of mine. She needed a dress for a wedding and they sent the wrong size so one of the employees of Birdsnest drove the correct size dress to Canberra from their warehouse in Cooma. How amazing is that?
           </p>
           <p>
            Dont forget to cast your vote for your favourite Australian online retailer
            <a href="http://www.orias.com.au">
             today
            </a>
            . Small retailers and independent sellers can be nominated so dont stick the mainstream suspects.
           </p>
