                  <h1 id="node-title">
                   Gigabit Ethernet Design Kit Connects the World
                  </h1>
                  <div class="panel-pane pane-node-created quiet icon-time-gray small no-title block quiet icon-time-gray small" id="time-stamp">
                   <div class="pane-content">
                    Thu, 03/06/2014 - 5:25pm
                   </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="panel-pane pane-token pane-node-field-author icon-author-gray small quiet no-title block icon-author-gray small quiet" id="author-stamp">
                   <div class="pane-content">
                    Melissa Fassbender, Associate Editor
                   </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="panel-pane pane-entity-field pane-node-field-product-company-profile icon-company no-title block" id="company-stamp">
                   <div class="pane-content">
                    <div class="field field-name-field-product-company-profile field-type-node-reference field-label-hidden view-mode-_custom_display">
                     <div class="field-items">
                      <div class="field-item even">
                       <a href="/company-profiles/texas-instruments-inc">
                        Texas Instruments, Inc.
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="field-item odd">
                       <a href="/company-profiles/elysium-inc">
                        Elysium Inc.
                       </a>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="panel-pane pane-block pane-block-17 no-title block" id="pane-subscriptionTextAd">
                   <div class="pane-content">
                    <div class="icon-mail">
                     <a href="http://subscribe.advantagemedia.com/pdd_ods/landing.aspx?cmpid=textadincontent" target="_blank">
                      Get today's design engineering headlines and news - Sign up now!
                     </a>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="panel-pane pane-node-body clear article left no-title block">
                   <div class="pane-content">
                    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden view-mode-_custom_display">
                     <div class="field-items">
                      <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                       <p>
                        <strong>
                         <a href="http://www.lantiq.com/digital-home/gigabit-ethernet/gigabit-ethernet-phy/xwaytm-phy11g-gigabit-ethernet-phy/" target="_blank">
                          Gigabit Ethernet Design Kit Connects the World
                         </a>
                         <br/>
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <em>
                         Demo kit to adds high speed connectivity to any home appliance.
                        </em>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Lantiq and Wrth Elektronik eiSos have announced a jointly developed design kit for the implementation of Gigabit Ethernet in a broad range of products. The kit provides all necessary information to understand the demands of an Ethernet hardware design, along with a 1 Gbps Ethernet demonstration board.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        The USB-powered demo board connects to PCs with data rates of up to 1 Gbps bi-directional. Using the Lantiq XWAY PHY11G in a small VQFN-48 package (7 x 7 mm), the board saves energy due to compliance with IEEEs energy efficient Ethernet standard.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <a href="http://www.aimess-products.de/en/" target="_blank">
                         <br/>
                         <strong>
                          An Intelligent Inline Measuring System
                         </strong>
                        </a>
                        <strong>
                         <br/>
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <em>
                         The R3Dscan infrared scanner made by AiMESS measures lightweight materials without having to spray them first.
                        </em>
                        <strong>
                         <br/>
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        The innovative fringe projection system detects lightweight materials, such as carbon fiber, glass fiber and fiber-reinforced plastics, without having to treat the surface of the object to be measured, enabling measurements in the production cycle. The system differs fundamentally from the white light scanners normally used for 3D surface data acquisition.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Unlike conventional fringe projection systems, AiMESS 3D scanner does not analyze the reflection, but the energy absorbed by the object to be measured, and can scan transparent, dark, or reflective surfaces.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <a href="http://manifoldbuilder.com/" target="_blank">
                         <strong>
                          Interface Enables Users to Step Inside Design
                         </strong>
                        </a>
                        <strong>
                         <br/>
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <em>
                         With the introduction of the Manifoldbuilder.com digital parts catalog, Smartflow customers no longer have to go through the process of requesting 3D CAD models directly from the Burger &amp; Brown.
                        </em>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        The new site allows users to step inside the inventory, giving them the power to configure and create 3D CAD models of Smartflow manifolds to their exact specifications.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        The digital parts catalog was created by CADENAS PARTsolutions, who specializes in driving revenue for industrial manufacturers with digital catalogs and 3D CAD models.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <a href="http://www.ti.com/tool/ek-tm4c1294xl?DCMP=mcu-tivc-ek-tm4c129xl-en&amp;HQS=mcu-tivc-ek-tm4c129xl-pr-tf-en" target="_blank">
                         <strong>
                          The First IoT-Capable LaunchPad in the TI Ecosystem
                         </strong>
                        </a>
                        <em>
                         <br/>
                        </em>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <em>
                         Quickly connect to the Internet with evaluation tool based on Tiva TM4C1294 MCU with integrated Ethernet MAC+PHY.
                         <br/>
                        </em>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Texas Instruments (TI) has announced the latest addition to its microcontroller (MCU) LaunchPad ecosystem  the Tiva C Series Connected LaunchPad.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        The Internet of Things (IoT) platform enables engineers and makers to rapidly prototype a wide range of cloud-enabled applications, bringing connectivity to any new or existing LaunchPad-based application.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Along with the on-board Ethernet MAC +PHY, analog integration and connectivity options, the Tiva C Series Connected LaunchPad contains two BoosterPack XL plug-in interfaces.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <strong>
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <a href="http://elysiuminc.com/products/cadfeature/" target="_blank">
                         <strong>
                          New Browser-Based Automation
                         </strong>
                        </a>
                        <strong>
                         <br/>
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <em>
                         Enhancements provide a more streamlined, user-friendly and efficient process for CAD/CAM/CAE/PLM translations.
                         <br/>
                        </em>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Elysium has announced the release of CADfeature 11.3, a new browser-enhanced, feature-based program for repairing, migrating, and reporting on product engineering data that is exchanged within manufacturing organizations and their supply chains.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        The newest version of CADfeature addresses long-sought industry goals of perfecting and speeding the creation of master CAD models used to build products ranging from appliances to medical devices, automobiles, aircraft, and more.
                       </p>
