       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Youre Not Going To Die
       </h1>
       <header class="entry-header">
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="posted-on">
          <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2012-05-15T07:42:10+00:00">
           May 15, 2012
          </time>
          <time class="updated" datetime="2015-03-04T11:19:32+00:00">
           Mar 4, 2015
          </time>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <img alt="not going to die" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="813" src="http://danielle-dowling.com/content/uploads/2012/05/not-going-to-die.png" width="650"/>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         IF you dont get the job,
         <br/>
         the call-back,
         <br/>
         the promotion.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you max out your credit-cards,
         <br/>
         wipe out your savings,
         <br/>
         lose every cent in your checking account.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          youll still be ok.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         If your lover leaves you or
         <br/>
         the relationship you have tirelessly nurtured withers and ends
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          youll survive.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you flub your words,
         <br/>
         regretfully overstep your bounds,
         <br/>
         feel the sting of unprovoked or provoked criticism.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          the world wont stop turning.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll be OK because youre always OK.
         <br/>
         <em>
          Have
         </em>
         always been OK.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Examine your past.
          <br/>
          Take the 30,000 foot view of lifes trajectory.
          <br/>
          When have you
          <em>
           not
          </em>
          landed on your feet?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Has it been challenging, crushing, painfully debilitating?
         <br/>
         Sure.
        </p>
        <p>
         Maybe you needed support.
         <br/>
         You called the doctor, your best friend, the headhunter.
         <br/>
         You poured a stiff drink or packed your bags for a yoga retreat.
        </p>
        <p>
         But you are resilient.
         <br/>
         A survivor.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://ctt.ec/74afj" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           You have chutzpah running through your veins and
           <br/>
           everything about your history proves it.
           <img alt="Twitter_logo_blue" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6934" height="25" src="http://danielle-dowling.com/content/uploads/2014/09/Twitter_logo_blue-e1410818556872.png" width="30"/>
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So wince in shame.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Lose it all + Start over.
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Get beat by the competition.
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll be ok.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          But more than that, youll be a stronger, smarter, fiercer version of yourself.
          <br/>
          And thats way,
          <em>
           way
          </em>
          better than being just OK.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         +++++++++++++++
         <br/>
         <b>
          I am the proud author of
          <a href="http://danielle-dowling.com/my-book-soul-sessions" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Soul Sessions: A 5 Week Guide to Crafting Greater Joy + Making Big Things Happen
           </em>
          </a>
          . Need one on one inspiration?
          <a href="http://danielle-dowling.com/work-with-me" target="_blank">
           Hire me.
          </a>
          You wont regret it.
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         photo credit:
         <a href="http://laurenconrad.com/blog/2012/02/friday-favorites-43/" target="_blank">
          Lauren Conrad
         </a>
        </p>
