       <p>
        <span id="more-123">
        </span>
        <br/>
        <!--noteaser-->
        <br/>
        A puzzle game where you have to make with the body of your hero exactly the same figure as a figure displayed on the advancing wall.
       </p>
       <p>
        Move and twist the figure so it matches the figure on the advancing wall in this human tetris like game!
       </p>
       <p>
        Bust 7 walls and move up a level where it gets even more interesting.
        <br/>
        You can also speed up walls if you think you get the required position and so earn some bonus points.
       </p>
       <p>
        Always check the distance and extra time gauges on the statusbar to know how much time you still have for rotating the figure!
       </p>
       <p>
        Use Mouse to click and hold a body part to move it.
        <br/>
        Press shift-key to reset the figure.
        <br/>
        Press space-bar to speed up the wall.
       </p>
