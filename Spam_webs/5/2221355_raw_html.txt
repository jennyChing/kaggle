         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Liverpool midfielder Philippe Coutinho admits he is training to make up the deficiency of not scoring enough goals for Liverpool. Coutinho got outstanding form during recent months and he was declared the Reds Capital One Cup semi-final man of the match in Chelsea defeat on Tuesday. However this season saw the Brazilian scoring only two times, in 3-2 QPR win in October and 2-2 Arsenal home draw before Christmas. Coutinho said we can improve in so many things. He added that he was working to assist his team fellows even further and trying to improve his

           <strong>
            goal scoring skill

          because he thinks that is part of his role and he hopes to perform well on that.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://blog.gofootballtickets.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Philippe-Coutinho-Liverpool.jpg" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Philippe Coutinho - Liverpool" class="aligncenter wp-image-364 size-full" height="320" src="http://blog.gofootballtickets.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Philippe-Coutinho-Liverpool.jpg" width="540"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The Brazilian further said his coaches are working with him on specific tasks to improve his final touch and he does post training work whenever possible to improve his shooting skill. A superb attack on Chelsea defence was tackled by home goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois because of Coutinhos frustration in front of goal at Stamford Bridge. Liverpool midfielder provide only two assists from creation of 20 chances in the current Premier League season and has attempted 28 shots from outside the box out of 48 shots with a shot accuracy of 48%.
