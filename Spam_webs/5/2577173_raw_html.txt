                 <h2 class="date-header">
                  <span>
                   Friday, 13 February 2015
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <div class="date-posts">
                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-qX0g24qU5Ik/VN0fHxnxhWI/AAAAAAAACVE/P9lxMjXRxDg/s1600/valentines%2Bday%2Blook%2Bfront%2Bpage.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <meta content="4778205754391000841" itemprop="postId"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Valentine's Day | Make up look
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4778205754391000841" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-qX0g24qU5Ik/VN0fHxnxhWI/AAAAAAAACVE/P9lxMjXRxDg/s1600/valentines%2Bday%2Blook%2Bfront%2Bpage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="494" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-qX0g24qU5Ik/VN0fHxnxhWI/AAAAAAAACVE/P9lxMjXRxDg/s1600/valentines%2Bday%2Blook%2Bfront%2Bpage.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      In the spirit of Valentine's Day, I attempted at creating a look that suits the special day! The month still has the chill of winter so I wanted to incorporate the wintery colours in with the look as well as the pinks for valentines. I could have gone mad and added a Red lip, but decided to go for a softer, more romantic look rather than sexy.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NMvEVRHYH0Q/VN0h5oDX7QI/AAAAAAAACVY/i6RtakPFoGM/s1600/closeup.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="266" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NMvEVRHYH0Q/VN0h5oDX7QI/AAAAAAAACVY/i6RtakPFoGM/s1600/closeup.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      For the eyes I used the silver colour from my
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.boots.com/en/Sleek-MakeUp-i-Divine-Eyeshadow-Palette-Vintage-Romance_1417377/" target="_blank">
                        Sleek I-Divine Eyeshadow Palette Vintage Romance
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      (apt I know) and swept the colour all over the eye base concentrating on getting into the corner of the eye. With a brighter colour in the inner corner will make any eye appear brighter and bigger.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I then needed to add some depth to the eye so I added an exaggerated liner flick using the black from the
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.boots.com/en/No7-Stay-Perfect-Eyeshadow-Duo_1273269/" target="_blank">
                        No7 Eyeshadow duo Moonlight Shadows
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      ending in line with where my brow ends. The idea of the look is not to appear too harsh so using my blender brush I blended the line concentrating more on the outer corner and blending it inward.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      So far the eye look is looking more wintery than romantic so I added the dark pink colour from my
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.feelunique.com/p/Pur-Minerals-The-Jupiter-Palette" target="_blank">
                        Jupiter Ascending limited edition palette
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      just above the crease of my eye, mainly because my eyes are slightly hooded and this needs to happen in order for the eyeshadow to be noticed. I quite like this subtle hint of pink it gives the look an almost wintery love story appeal.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-thVEIl1068Y/VN0iVvIfhHI/AAAAAAAACVg/ZihGEOweKc8/s1600/eyes%2Bdown.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="232" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-thVEIl1068Y/VN0iVvIfhHI/AAAAAAAACVg/ZihGEOweKc8/s1600/eyes%2Bdown.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      To finish the eye look off I added a black pencil liner across the upper lash line and ran the pink eyeshadow along the lower lash line. For this look too much black liner would make the eyes look to dramatic for what I had intended and runs the risk of making my eyes looks smaller. I then applied some mascara it didn't really matter which one I used as all it was needed for was to define my own lashes before adding false ones. The false lashes I used were
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.boots.com/en/Eylure-Cheryl-lashes-Sexy-Seniorita_1623426/" target="_blank">
                        Eylure Cheryl lashes - Sexy Seorita
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      . These lashes seemed to add the right amount of definition I was looking for without being too over the top, complimenting the look perfectly.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-LjpY-cXeAQ0/VN0gk5G4wbI/AAAAAAAACVQ/X7Y2dOfxp9U/s1600/Lashes%2B.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="365" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-LjpY-cXeAQ0/VN0gk5G4wbI/AAAAAAAACVQ/X7Y2dOfxp9U/s1600/Lashes%2B.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      To complete the whole look I added a
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.boots.com/en/Revlon/" target="_blank">
                        Revlon
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      Pink Lipstick (forgive me as I believe the lipstick in question has now been discontinued) I just really loved the colour pink and the texture of it. I thought that it fitted perfectly with the romantic look I was going for, proving not all valentine's makeup needs to have a red lip!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-u-BqwaLjSWE/VN0kCHSnlPI/AAAAAAAACVs/tVEM6etr0B4/s1600/TADAH.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-u-BqwaLjSWE/VN0kCHSnlPI/AAAAAAAACVs/tVEM6etr0B4/s1600/TADAH.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I love this look as I think it is totally wearable on a day or evening date with that special someone!
                     </div>
                     <div class="" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
