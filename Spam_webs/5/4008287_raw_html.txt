        <p>
         If you love all things Mickey Mouse, you will love this easy to create Mickey Mouse holiday ornament. With just three bulbs you can create the famous profile of Mickey, perfect for hanging on your tree or gifting to a Disney fanatic in your life. Plus its super easy to make with the kids!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="DIY Mickey Mouse Ornament" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12067" height="907" src="http://mommadjane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/mouseorn.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Take a peek below at how you can get started crafting your own.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Supplies needed:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="craft ornament supplies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12066" height="907" src="http://mommadjane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/058.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 medium sized ornament bulb
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 identical small sized ornament bulbs
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Hot glue, glue gun
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Scissors
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Ribbon for hanging
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Marker
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Feel free to use a color combo that works for you. We chose red, but if you want you can use black or any other color that suits your dcor.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Begin by creating two small dots on the top of your ornament where you want to attach the ears. This will help you place them on easily and evenly.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Add a dab of glue to the side of the smaller ornaments. Press them on the dots you created to act as ears. As you place them, keep in mind that the top of the small ornaments (where the hooks would go) to the back so they are not seen.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Disney Christmas ornament" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12064" height="907" src="http://mommadjane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/054.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Cut a length of ribbon and tie it to the top of the newly crafted ornament. Choose a color that compliments it and it sturdy enough for hanging.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          See how simple that was? Your Mickey Mouse ornament is now ready to be hung.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Mickey Mouse Christmas Ornament" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12065" height="907" src="http://mommadjane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/055.jpg" width="680"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Gather your supplies and give this easy and inexpensive Disney themed holiday craft a try!
        </p>
