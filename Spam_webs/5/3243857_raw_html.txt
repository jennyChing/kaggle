         <br/>
          Compete for the top score and challenge friends in Game Center
         <br/>
          Share your scores and achievements on Facebook and Twitter
         <br/>
         <br/>
         GAME MODES
         <br/>
          Moves Mode: Score as many points as you can in 20 moves
         <br/>
          Timed Mode: Score as many points as you can in 60 seconds
