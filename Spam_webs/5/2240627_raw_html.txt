     <h1 class="grub-topOfColumn" itemprop="name" style="font-size: 27px;">
      the girl &amp; the figs Lavender Mojito
     </h1>
     <img height="526" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Lavender-Mojito-Main-790x526.jpg" width="790"/>
     <div class="yui3-g">
      <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-well">
       <div class="grub-byline" style="margin-top: 1em;">
        Recipe by
        <a href="https://plus.google.com/113274240511284571445" rel="author">
         Jerry James Stone
        </a>
         March 20, 2013
       </div>
       <div class="grub-mainPostContent" itemprop="recipeInstructions" not-style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 23px;">
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/the-girl-the-figs-lavender-mojito/lavender-mojito-main/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/lavender-mojito-main" rel="attachment wp-att-1995">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Last year I spent time with some really amazing people including Sondra Bernstein and her crew from the girl &amp; the fig, the fig cafe and ESTATE, all located in Sonoma Valley.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pictured below are Sondra and Chef John at the Imagery Estate farm. The beets that Sondra is holding made their way into
         <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/the-girl-the-figs-beet-and-carrot-cocktail/" title="the girl &amp; the figs Beet and Carrot Cocktail">
          a very tasty beet and carrot juice cocktail
         </a>
         enjoyed by us later that day. But Sondra admits, they havent gotten it all figured out just yet. This is a learning process. For example, the farm is booming with leeks at the moment and therefore, so is the menu at all three restaurants (oops!). Next time around they will pace out their plantings to better balance the menu.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/the-girl-the-figs-beet-and-carrot-cocktail/sondra-and-john/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/sondra-and-john" rel="attachment wp-att-1666">
          <img alt="Sondra and John" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1666" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Sondra-and-John-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I apologize for the noise in the video, it was super busy that day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="338" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/6bfuDxcXlMM" width="600">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <div class="yui3-g" id="grub-recipeSummary">
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-left">
          <h2>
           Ingredients
          </h2>
          <ul id="grub-ingredients">
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            4 Ounces light Rum
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            20 fresh mint leaves
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Limes, juiced
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Ounces
            <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/lavender-simple-syrup/">
             Lavender Simple Syrup
            </a>
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            Club Soda
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            fresh Lavender, for garnish
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-right">
          <h2>
           <a class="grub-button" href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/the-girl-the-figs-lavender-mojito/printable/" target="_blank">
            <span class="grub-printIcon">
            </span>
            Printable View
           </a>
          </h2>
          <h2>
           Yield:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            2 Lavender Mojitos
           </span>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT5M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
          <h2>
           Total Time
           <div style="float: right;">
            5 minutes
           </div>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT5M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Prep time
           <div style="float: right">
            5 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Recipe Type:
           </h2>
           <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe-type/cocktail/">
            Cocktail
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Main Ingredient:
           </h2>
           Lavender
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Diet:
           </h2>
           <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/diet/vegan/">
            Vegan
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         1. Add torn fresh mint leaves to a pint glass.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1500" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/the-girl-the-figs-lavender-mojito/the-girl-and-the-fig-lavender-mojito/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/the-girl-and-the-fig-lavender-mojito" rel="attachment wp-att-1997">
          <img alt="the girl and the fig Lavender Mojito" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1997" height="900" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/the-girl-and-the-fig-Lavender-Mojito-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Add lime juice and lavender syrup and muddle the ingredients.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/strawberries-stuffed-with-honey-lavender-cream-cheese/dried-lavender/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/dried-lavender" rel="attachment wp-att-1961">
          <img alt="Dried Lavender" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1961" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Dried-Lavender-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Add ice, the rum and give it a shake.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Top it off with the club soda and some fresh lavender.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1500" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/the-girl-the-figs-lavender-mojito/lavender-mojito-full/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/lavender-mojito-full" rel="attachment wp-att-1996">

         </a>
        </p>
