               <h2>
                PURPLE OFTEN LOOKS BEST WITH
               </h2>
               <p>
                Menswear has now fully embraced pattern and colour over the past few years, with alot of men becoming more confident in there use of colour.So it should come as no surprise that purple seeing men experimenting with purple. Purple often looks best with Riviera-inspired neutrals such as white, cream and navy. However, shades of grey and green can also work extremely well.

                ROBERTO CAVALLI
