        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Even More Gift Ideas for Teenage Boys
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         The teen years are so hard for gift buying. I find it a little easier to shop for girls but not only are boys a little more difficult, this age is is hard in general. No longer a child that plays with toys but also not quite an adult that would appreciate kitchen gadgets.
        </p>
        <p>
         My oldest son just turned fifteen yesterday and finding gifts for him for his birthday and Christmas hasnt been an easy task. When I ask him what he wants he just shrugs his shoulders and tells me books. He is into sci-fi and fantasy books so thankfully I have a little to go on there but as his mother I would like to give him something a little more creative. These are some ideas that Ive come up with for my son and hopefully it will help you come up with some ideas as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Even more gift ideas for teenage boys. Teens are hard to buy for, especially boys. This list of ideas will hopefully give you some ideas when your mind is blank." class="aligncenter wp-image-16254" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/gift-ideas.png?resize=468%2C351" title="Even more gift ideas for teenage boys. Teens are hard to buy for, especially boys. This list of ideas will hopefully give you some ideas when your mind is blank."/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span id="more-16242">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         Fathead
        </h2>
        <p>
         Whether the teen on your gift list is into sports, video games, or anything in between you are sure to find a
         <a href="http://www.fathead.com/" target="_blank">
          Fathead
         </a>
         that would be perfect for him. If not, you can create your own forthe perfect one for his room. Take a look at
         <a href="http://i1.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/4X6A2270.jpg?resize=640%2C427" target="_blank">
          my sons Fathead
         </a>
         to see how cool they look in a teens room.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="RealBig_Tall_BlueBench_Temp" class="aligncenter wp-image-16256" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/RealBig_Tall_BlueBench_Temp.jpg?resize=444%2C444"/>
        </p>
        <h2 class="milo" id="yui_3_8_1_1_1418076018947_1162">
         William Shakespeares Star Wars Trilogy
        </h2>
        <p>
         (aff. link)
         <a href="http://track.flexlinks.com/a.ashx?foid=1048597.221087&amp;fot=9999&amp;foc=1&amp;foc2=603523&amp;url=http%3a%2f%2fwww.barnesandnoble.com%2fw%2fwilliam-shakespeares-star-wars-trilogy-ian-doescher%2f1120552736%3fean%3d9781594747915" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          This set
         </a>
         includes:
         <em>
          Verily, A New Hope, The Empire Striketh Back,
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          The Jedi Doth Return
         </em>
         . When I saw this the titles made me laugh but I figured it was just something silly to capitalize on the Star Wars franchise. After reading the reviews it sounds like they are legitimately good books.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="download" class="aligncenter wp-image-16253" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/download.jpg?resize=267%2C424"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Kindle
        </h2>
        <p>
         This is always a perennial favorite for the reader in your life. If your teen doesnt read as often as youd like the convenience of a
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Kindle-Glare-Free-Touchscreen-Display-Wi-Fi/dp/B00I15SB16/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1418077043&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=kindle" target="_blank">
          Kindle
         </a>
         may get them to read more.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="kb-slate-02-sm._V324779286_" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16255" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/kb-slate-02-sm._V324779286_.jpg?resize=320%2C282"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Magic the Gathering
        </h2>
        <p>
         If the teenage boy on your listis into RPGthen they may enjoy
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/ref=nb_sb_ss_c_0_5?url=search-alias%3Dtoys-and-games&amp;field-keywords=magic%20the%20gathering&amp;sprefix=magic%2Caps%2C209" target="_blank">
          Magic the Gathering
         </a>
         . My son gets together with a group of friends during his free period and lunch to play this card game. I dont pretend to understand it but he likes it and thats all that matters.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="download (1)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16252" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/download-1.jpg?resize=300%2C184"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         New Driver Kit
        </h2>
        <p>
         (aff. link) Included in
         <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/link?id=mvtsWhL/3Nk&amp;offerid=54404.21785&amp;type=2&amp;murl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.uncommongoods.com%2Fproduct%2Fyou-got-your-drivers-license-kit%3Futm_medium%3Daffiliates%26utm_source%3Dlinksharefeed%26utm_campaign%3D21785" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          this kit
         </a>
         for the newly licensed driver on your list isa tire gauge, an emergency flashlight, and a cloth cell phone pouch to prevent texting while driving) to the playful (a colorful fuzzy dice keychain
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="21785_zoom1" class="aligncenter wp-image-16248" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/21785_zoom1.jpg?resize=446%2C446"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Power Card
        </h2>
        <p>
         (aff. link) In this age of technology there arent many teenagers who dont have a cell phone or MP3 player that needs to be charged and an
         <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/link?id=mvtsWhL/3Nk&amp;offerid=54404.25355&amp;type=2&amp;murl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.uncommongoods.com%2Fproduct%2Fpower-card%3Futm_medium%3Daffiliates%26utm_source%3Dlinksharefeed%26utm_campaign%3D25355" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          on-the-go charger
         </a>
         is a great thing to have on hand. Since boys dont carry a purse, this slim power card will fit in a wallet or back pocket for convenient charging.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="25355_zoom1" class="aligncenter wp-image-16249" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/25355_zoom1.jpg?resize=376%2C301"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Bedroom remodel
        </h2>
        <p>
         Does your sons room need some updating from a childs space to a teenagers space? This year my son got a new bed to give his room a more grown up look. He still had the headboard that I bought him when he was about six so now his little brother has that old headboard and he has a new bed with storage and will carry him through college and beyond.
         <a href="http://theantijunecleaver.com/2014/10/the-great-bedroom-redo/" target="_blank">
          This is how his room looks now
         </a>
         an actual teenagers room. A new black comforter will be under the tree this year to complete the transformation.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="captains bed" class="aligncenter wp-image-16251" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/captains-bed.png?resize=432%2C269"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         T-shirts
        </h2>
        <p>
         I dont know about the teen in your life but mine lives in t-shirts, even now in December. Thankfully we live in a relatively moderate climate. He loves shirts that are funny, quirky, and especially from his favorite bands.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="avenged" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16250" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/avenged.png?resize=396%2C402"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Art set
        </h2>
        <p>
         Do you have a teen that loves to draw? Why not give him an
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Gallery-Studio-Quality-Mediums-Guaranteed/dp/B008AH6PPE/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1418162236&amp;sr=8-3&amp;keywords=art+set" target="_blank">
          art set
         </a>
         that will fuel his creativity?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="7897f9bffbfc09f77f6789fefb44ac02" class="aligncenter wp-image-16247" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/7897f9bffbfc09f77f6789fefb44ac02.jpg?resize=444%2C444"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Carnivorous Plants
        </h2>
        <p>
         Because why not? Ive always thought that having a
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Dune-Craft-Strange-Plant-Collection/dp/B003BK3U7Q/ref=sr_1_5?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1418160589&amp;sr=8-5&amp;keywords=carnivours+plants" target="_blank">
          venus fly trap
         </a>
         would be cool and I think my son would get a kick out of it as well. Plus its an all natural way of keeping your teenage boys room bug-free (I kid, I kid).
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="61uhhoglXhL" class="aligncenter wp-image-16246" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/61uhhoglXhL.jpg?resize=366%2C366"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Perplexus Epic
        </h2>
        <p>
         For the puzzle lover, the
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Perplexus-BL300-Epic/dp/B004H1V5RU/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1418160733&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=perplexus+epic" target="_blank">
          Perplexus Epic
         </a>
         will give your teen hours of fun and probably a little frustration. See it in action
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aIEmsgOV6lM" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="51JJuItmd6L._SY450_" class="aligncenter wp-image-16244" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/51JJuItmd6L._SY450_.jpg?resize=305%2C356"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Goodminton
        </h2>
        <p>
         Portable and compact,
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Goodminton-Worlds-Easiest-Racket-Game/dp/B00LWDRQ6A/ref=sr_1_1?s=toys-and-games&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1418158608&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=goodminton" target="_blank">
          Goodminton
         </a>
         will give you and your teenager hours of outdoor fun and exercise. Without a net to set up, you can bring this game along with you anywhere and have a blast.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="9ba8ac08513509aff5518a230de6f25f" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16243" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/theantijunecleaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/9ba8ac08513509aff5518a230de6f25f.jpg?resize=300%2C360"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Books
        </h2>
        <p>
         Books are always a good bet for the reader on your gift list. I am very thankful that my son wants mostly books this year. For some book ideas check out my post on
         <a href="http://theantijunecleaver.com/2012/06/pre-teen-suggestions/" target="_blank">
          Pre-teen and Teen Book Suggestions
         </a>
         as well as my Pinterest board.
        </p>
        <p>
         For more ideas check out my previous pre-teen and teen gift idea posts.
