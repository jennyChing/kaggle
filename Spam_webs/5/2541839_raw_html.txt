           , which is the most advanced form designer currently available. After googling it, he lamented that it took him all day to code! Which is exactly why the product exists.
          </p>
          <p class="graf--p" id="daa4" name="daa4">
           The Form Designer allows you design beautiful forms easily and painlessly. Forget the hours and hours of hunching over your screen trying to code a form. Our problem-solving engineers have done that so you dont have to. Simple point-and-click technology makes form designing a breeze -select fonts, colors, backgrounds, custom images, logos and more in seconds.
          </p>

           Forms created with the Form Designer are responsive by default, so you know that your form will look just as good across all mobile devices.

           <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.jotform.com/form-designer/" href="http://www.jotform.com/form-designer/" rel="nofollow">
            Form Designer
           </a>
