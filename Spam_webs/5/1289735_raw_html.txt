       <p>
        We're lucky to have Jenny Lewis in the studio to celebrate the release of her new album,
        <em>
         The Voyager.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        From her time as a child actor to her 13 years spent as co-head of Rilo Kiley, Jenny Lewis has never stopped putting forth great artistic work. Her past two solo albums were in a league of their own, and the third,
        <em>
         The Voyager
        </em>
        , is no exception.
       </p>
       <p>
        As we will hear today, this album took her a while, as insomnia and the death of her father threw her for a loop. She started working with Beck on the song "Just One Of The Guys," but it was Ryan Adams who brought forth the performances on
        <em>
         The Voyager
        </em>
        as producer. Today we'll hear four songs from this new album.
       </p>
