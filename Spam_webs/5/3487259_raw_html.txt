        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Fence Board Planked Wall How To
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-06-01T15:31:54+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 1, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/author/admin" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Shannon at Fox Hollow Cottage
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Fence Board Planked WallHow To
         </span>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Our latest update here at the cottagehas been to add aplanked wall treatment. With a Fox Hollow Cottage twist.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Our planked wallhas a slightly different vibe then you get with the smooth boards and very tight joints lots of folks are using. A bit rougher its got a little more barn wood, farmhouse, rustic flavor.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Low-Cost-Wood-Plank-Wall-Treatment-DIY-Home-Improvement.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Low Cost Wood Plank Wall Treatment DIY Home Improvement" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7584" height="1230" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Low-Cost-Wood-Plank-Wall-Treatment-DIY-Home-Improvement-410x1230.jpg" width="410"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Low-Cost-Wood-Plank-Wall-Treatment-DIY-Home-Improvement-410x1230.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Since budgetis always a factor and we try to keep our costs down, we went the least expensive route. After doing some material cost research that turned out to be1 x 6 pre stained Douglas Firfence boards.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Do I like the color? No. Does it matter? No.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          I knew it was getting painted, so I didnt care one bit. Plus, it was super cheap.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          The entire walls worth of wood costus only $28.00  WIN!
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          So if cheap and easy is your thing.. keep reading!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Using fence boards means less cutting and less measuring.. both of which will make your project go faster. We only had to make our length cuts. The boards were already the perfect width. So zip-zip and on you get to move on to the next step.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          I meant to share this much sooner. I dont know where my days fly away too? If you find them, send them back this way would you!?
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Jim and I have been hard at work giving our combination powder bath and laundry room a makeover. You can see where we started
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/05/scratching-the-10-year-redecorating-itch-with-sherwin-williams-paints.html" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
           </strong>
          </span>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          And get a little peek of our progress while I share our new planked wall!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-DIY-Tutorial.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall - DIY Tutorial" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7528" height="800" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-DIY-Tutorial.jpg" width="800"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-DIY-Tutorial.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          *As with about every project we undertake here at the cottage, we had a problem. Our wall is solid concrete. Yay. So fun. So instead of being able to use a nail gun.. and a construction adhesive. We had to depend on an adhesive alone. It just makes it a bit more challenging. If you can install yours with nails. Lucky you!!This should be even easier!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          <strong>
           SUPPLIES:
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #333333;">
           1 x 6 Fence Boards
          </span>
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #333333;">
           Saw (Chop Saw, Miter Saw, Table Saw or even a Radial Saw will work)
          </span>
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #333333;">
           Sanding Block
          </span>
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #333333;">
           Construction Adhesive (youll need what is beat suited to your particular walls)
          </span>
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Spacers (we used quarters, they worked perfect)
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Paint and Paint Brush
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Trim
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          <strong>
           Optional:
          </strong>
          If you have electrical outlets, youll have to address those.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Youll need new boxes or extension boxes to pull the outlet out and make it flush with the new wall. If you do not haveelectricalknowledge, please consult a professional.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Outlet-Instalation.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall - Outlet Instalation" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7526" height="752" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Outlet-Instalation.jpg" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Outlet-Instalation.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-New-Outlet-Instalation.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall - New Outlet Instalation" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7527" height="752" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-New-Outlet-Instalation.jpg" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-New-Outlet-Instalation.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          TIP:
         </strong>
         Paint your wall before you install your boards. Whatever color you use can/may show though your spacing so youll want that uniform. I used white. I knew I would be using white paint to finish my wall so this made sense for us.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          TIP:
         </strong>
         Start with a smooth, uniform wall. We had to do some repair to ours before we started to accomplish this. Again, old house. So much fun. (were used to it)
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Measure your wall and assess your material needs.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Gather supplies.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Plan out your pattern, this will dictate your cuts.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Make your cuts.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          If you plan to paint and want a finished look Paint it now, BEFORE you install it.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Knock Off Rough Edges with a Sanding Block
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-3.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7531" height="811" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-3.jpg" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-3.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I gave ours a soft, slightly rounded finish.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall" class="aligncenter wp-image-7530" height="308" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall.jpg" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          We installed our wall witha
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           running
          </span>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           bond
          </span>
          pattern. That means your pattern runs 2  3  2. One row with two boards, the next with three, the next with two, repeat. Seams meeting up every other row.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Use a level to ensure a straight line across your wall and start your first course working off that.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Start applying your boards to the wall at the bottom. We began with a two board run for our first course.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Project.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall Project" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7532" height="1053" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Project.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Project.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          We placedour adhesive on the back of the boards in a long, wavy S pattern.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Firm even pressure and a little wiggle as you push it onto the wall should do the trick.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          If you can, use a nail gun to further anchor your boards to the wall.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Place your spacers.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Tutorial.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall Tutorial" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7533" height="1053" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Tutorial.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-Tutorial.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Install your next run, checking for level.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Place spacers, install next board, repeat.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          When you reach the ceiling line. You are done with your wall!
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: left;">
          Trim to taste.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-During.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Rustic Wood Fence Board Plank Wall - During" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7536" height="1053" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-During.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rustic-Wood-Fence-Board-Plank-Wall-During.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          TIP:
         </strong>
         We knew our wall was not level (big surprise!) so we planned to end ourlast course, and cover it with our trim.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          There was so much to do in this room that we simply painted this particular wall, and hung a giantmirror when we moved in.We were busy dealing with thewindow to nowhere. Crazy, CRAZY scabbed in wall.. an exposed hot water heater.. the floor.. oh, and the rest of the house too.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          You can see why it didnt make the top-ten back then!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Before-bathroom-diy-makeover-reno.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Before bathroom diy makeover reno" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7542" height="488" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Before-bathroom-diy-makeover-reno.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Before-bathroom-diy-makeover-reno.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Back to the wall at present. Sorry! Memory lane sidetrackedme there for a second.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          With the less than perfect finish of our boards, I was not shooting for a smooth, polished look. I letthefinishof the wood set the tone and decided to do a white wash.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          I used my paint at full strength and applied with a brush. I worked some into the seams, but did not go for full coverage. With the pre-stained finish I suggesta primer coat first to seal and prevent bleed through. Then paint to taste.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          I applied three coats of my white wall color and called it good. I like the less than perfect look. Its got a good feel to it. I debated leaving it natural, but this room has no windows, so it was a short-lived thought.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DIY-Plank-Wall-with-Fence-Boards.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="DIY Plank Wall with Fence Boards" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7537" height="399" src="http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DIY-Plank-Wall-with-Fence-Boards.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://foxhollowcottage.com/2014/06/fence-board-planked-wall-how-to.html&amp;media=http://foxhollowcottage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DIY-Plank-Wall-with-Fence-Boards.jpg&amp;description=Fence Board Planked Wall How To')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          <strong>
           This is a weekend project.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          We got it done in two days total work time. With Jimmy cutting and me sanding the boards, to the install, new outlet, me painting and popping some trim in. Not counting our special situation waiting for the adhesive to dry. If we had nailed in, I could have painted right away. Waiting was smart though, I sure didnt want any boards falling off the wall! So far so good. Its been up for weeks now and everything is staying put beautifully.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          As always, thanks for stopping in to see what we are up to. We are so happyto say goodbyeto that ugly wall we inherited.I hope this was a helpful post that might work for a room in your home as well!!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          ~Shannon
         </span>
        </p>
