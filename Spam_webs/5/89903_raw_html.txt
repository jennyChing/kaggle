        <p>
         Westgate Residence by Kurt Krueger Architect:
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          The Westgate Residence is a renovation of a 1948 home found in Brentwood, CA in which the goal was to architecturally update the developing by means of the use of new components, spatial definition and openings, but with out rising the total square footage or developing envelope.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Located near a pretty active street intersection with an abundance of foot targeted traffic, the challenge was to produce a peaceful dwelling from an current constructing shell. Because the home owners are a retired couple, ease of function and total practicality had been just as essential as the total style. Our mission was to seamlessly mix these demands in a seamless and poetic way.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Rather than including much more location with a 2nd floor, the property was stored to one particular degree. The 50x100 good deal meant that the present footprint has currently been maximized, delivering a first floor region at two,200 square feet. Nevertheless, the residence had excellent bones, allowing us to pierce new window, door, and skylight openings, delivering more organic light. Walls and needless partitions had been eliminated, allowing the main area to come to feel as one particular big room, but within it, containing smaller zones.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Security was an critical issue to think about along with the constrained outside area, so a courtyard with retractable sunshades have been created to enclose the front yard. Douglas fir wood slats are placed at this kind of a height that individuals on the sidewalk degree are not able to see in, but those at the greater courtyard level can see out.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          The heightening of the senses was critical during, and informed the selection of wealthy resources with tactile attributes. Douglas Fir wood was utilized for the siding as well as the doors and windows with copper gutters and downspouts lining the exterior eaves. Walnut wood flooring and cabinetry are consistent throughout the residence. Limestone paving is used in the courtyard, whilst Argentinian cobblestone announces the driveway and entrance. Water-jet stone is utilised at at the fireplace, Kitchen backsplash and in the Master Bathroom, enabling a customized developed grooves into the stone.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Attention to specifics create special elements of shock and wonder to the tiny area. One of these details was the creation of a dam-less level shower exactly where the slab appears to float as the water falling off the edges and disappearing. One more was the use of white panels on the casework that can slide, tilt and re-positon to hide the television or any undesirables on the shelves.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Fountains and water elements include white noise to reduce the sound of street traffic whilst the landscaping was made to screen, nevertheless give a sense of calm and reflection.
         </em>
        </p>
