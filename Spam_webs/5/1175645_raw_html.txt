        <p>
         <span lang="EN-US">
          8, 18, 28, 38, 48, 54, 68, 80, 84, 88, 99, 168 &amp; 108 are all good numbers.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-US">
          You can choose which you like, but try to have he number balanced with yin and yang energies.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          For example
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           3388, 1618 and 1328
          </span>
          are all very auspicious, because they have a combination of two yang numbers and two yin numbers.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          Such combinations are balanced and very lucky to have them in your life.
         </span>
        </p>

         <span lang="EN-GB">

         </span>
        </p>
        <h2 center="" id='\"193_dont-worry-if-you-ha_1\"' style='\"text-align:'>
         <strong>
          Dont worry if you have unlucky number!
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h3 id='\"193_cure-number-1_1\"'>
         <strong>
          <span lang="EN-US">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            Cure Number 1.
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          Paint
          <strong>
           two red dots
          </strong>
          near the number.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          For example if you have an unlucky 4 or 14 in the number of your house, then just paint it like 4 or 14 with numbers in
          <a href="http://messagenote.com/interior-inspiration/11-black-interior-images" title="black">
           black
          </a>
          and the dots in red.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          A dot or zero symbolizes nothingness; a pair of them in red will nullify any unluckiest associated with the number.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          <strong>
           This cure only works if the numbers are written down
          </strong>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          For mobile or telephone numbers, you need to actually change the numbers to change your luck.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-GB">
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 id='\"193_cure-number-2_1\"'>
         <strong>
          <span lang="EN-US">

          </span>

        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-US">
          Good and cheap cure is to draw a circle
          <strong>
           around your number
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span lang="EN-US">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            Cure Number 3.
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span lang="EN-US">
