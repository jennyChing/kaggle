                 A video featuring A-list celebrities  some with tough-guy street cred -- sends a powerful message about the difference between consensual sex and rape.
                </p>

               <!--teaser-->
               <div class="body-remainder">
                <p>
                 <iframe border="0" class=" oembed-iframe oembed-iframe-center" frameborder="0" height="270" src="http://oembed.prosepoint.net/sites/all/modules/oembed_iframe/oeif.php?url=http://youtu.be/xLdElcv5qqc&amp;endpoint=http://www.youtube.com/oembed" width="480">
                 </iframe>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Posted on the White House website, the video features President Barack Obama, Vice President Joe Biden, Daniel Craig, Benicio Del Toro, Dul Hill, Seth Meyers and Steve Carell. It was released just as the U.S. Department of Education posted its list of colleges and universities under investigation for possible violation of federal law over the handling of sexual violence and harassment complaints.
                 <br/>
                 <a href="http://www.ed.gov/news/press-releases/us-department-education-releases-list-higher-education-institutions-open-title-i">
                  The list
                 </a>
                 includes several ivy-league schools, along with prestigious private colleges and public universities.
