    Top 5 Flip-Phones
   </h1>

     <p>
      When choosing your next mobile handset, you'll want to pay particular attention to the top flip-phones that are available on the market today. Flip-style phones are some of the most popular options due to their small size and simplicity. The top designs offer the latest technology as well as a durable design that can withstand practically anything.
     </p>

     <h2>
      Current Top Five Flip-Phones
     </h2>
     <p>
      Not all of these phones are going to be available in all areas and individual differences will dictate whether or not any of them are suitable to your needs. Technology can move at a breakneck pace, so keep an eye out for the latest and greatest that the major phone companies have to offer. The top five flip-phones, as of October of 2011, are listed below. They each approach slightly different segments of the market, offering something different and unique to consumers who appreciate the convenience of a flip-style phone.
     </p>
     <h3>
      1. BlackBerry 9670
     </h3>
     <p>
      When most people think about the
      <a href="/Who_Makes_the_Best_Smartphones" title="Who Makes the Best Smartphones?">
       BlackBerry devices
      </a>
      produced by Canada's Research in Motion (RIM), they think about the wide "bar" style phone with a full QWERTY keyboard. RIM has since expanded its lineup to include different types of smartphones as well, including both touchscreen and flip models. The
      <a href="http://us.blackberry.com/smartphones/blackberrystyle/?CPID=KNC-kw419152{MatchType}_p6&amp;HBX_PK=rim|1b45244e-d0b4-6f88-9bfc-0000426b655c#!phone-specifications">
       Blackberry 9670
      </a>
      is an innovative, flip-style smart phone that is sleek and stylish. It features a camera with five megapixels, a QWERTY keyboard, and Blackberry's 6 OS Web Browser. It can email and text and allows you to see all messages in one list. Just like with most smartphones, this style will let you stay up-to-date with the best apps. This flip-phone is available from wireless carriers such as
      <a href="http://www.boostmobilestore.com/bpdirect/boost/PhoneList.do?action=view&amp;id=BB9670">
       Boost Mobile
      </a>
      and
      <a href="http://us.blackberry.com/smartphones/blackberrystyle/?providerID=885#!where-to-buy">
       Sprint
      </a>
      . It retails for $199.99.
     </p>
     <div class="image right" style="width: 138px">
      <a href="/image/146497~AT&amp;T_SGH-a847.jpg" rel="" title="Samsung Rugby II">
       <img alt="Samsung Rugby II" src="http://cf.ltkcdn.net/cellphones/images/std/146497-136x350-AT&amp;T_SGH-a847.jpg"/>
      </a>
      <div class="caption">
       Samsung Rugby II
      </div>

     <h3>
      2. Samsung Rugby II
     </h3>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.att.com/shop/wireless/devices/samsung/rugby-ii-black.html">
       Samsung Rugby II
      </a>
      is sold by AT&amp;T and retails for $269.99 but is often featured for $99.99 with a new two-year contract. This phone has push to talk ability as well as text, email, web browsing and a camera and music player. It is designed according to military specifications and can withstand elements such as rain, dust and humidity. This phone is also Bluetooth compatible.
     </p>

     <h3>
      3. Samsung Convoy 2
     </h3>
     <p>
      Samsung's
      <a href="http://www.samsung.com/us/mobile/cell-phones/SCH-U660CNAVZW">
       Convey 2
      </a>
      model is another great flip-style phone.. This phone can withstand shock, dust, sun and sand, making it durable enough to take anywhere. It has dual microphones with noise cancelation, push to talk features and an extended battery life. In addition, it can take photos and video, play music and understand voice commands. This phone is available through Verizon Wireless and retails for $249.99, but with a two-year Verizon contract is priced at $79.99.
     </p>
     <div class="image right" style="width: 192px">
      <a href="/image/146498~SCH-u640.jpg" rel="" title="Samsung Convoy">

     <h3>
      LG Accolade
     </h3>
     <p>
      The
      <a href="http://www.lg.com/us/mobile-phones/LG-VX5600.jsp">
       LG Accolade
      </a>
      is popular choice for those wanting a small and simple style phone. It has a camera with image editor, one-touch speakerphone and is Bluetooth compatible. In addition, this little phone has a web browser, email and instant messaging. It is available through Verizon and is priced at $219.99 but with online discounts and a new two-year contract, it is free.
     </p>
     <h3>
      LG Revere
     </h3>
     <p>
      The
      <a href="http://www.verizonwireless.com/lg-revere.shtml">
       LG Revere
      </a>
      is a great phone for those looking for something sleek and simple. It can browse the web, email and text as well as take pictures with its 1.3 megapixel camera. It also has an image editor feature and the ability to directly load pictures online and to Facebook. This phone can be purchased through Verizon and is free after online discounts and a new two-year contract.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Using a Flip-Phone
     </h2>
     <p>
      Just as fashion trends change over time, the same can be said about the world of cell phones. Personal preferences change with the times and this has indeed happened with mobile phones as well. Many cell phone manufacturers started to produce more touchscreen phones and fewer "clamshell" style flip-phones. Even though touchscreen phones may be a very popular option, there are still many great and interesting clamshell phones available.
     </p>
     <p>
      There are many reasons that some people prefer flip-phones to touchscreen models. Those who work and keep their phone in a pocket may like the security of the cover and the overall durability of the design. Others many like the how the cover protects the screen or the compact size. Just like with any other style of phone, flip-phones may get updates from time to time and new styles are introduced as others get discontinued. Using a flip-style phone remains a popular option for many and choosing one of the top models will guarantee that you are presented with the latest in technology as well as a cool design.
     </p>
