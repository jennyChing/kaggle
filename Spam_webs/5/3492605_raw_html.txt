             Hows your holiday shopping going? OK, wait! Dont slam your laptop shut in a fit of despair. You are more than fine on time. The biggest shopping day of the year, Black Friday, hasnt even hit yet. Plus, according to last years numbers, more than 80% of shoppers will still be on the hunt, for a few items at least, come mid-December. Phew! With that panic attack averted, we want to make sure youre prepared to shop, whether that happens next week or December 24th. Here weve rounded up our favorite tips for saving cash online. Now go get em!
             <br class="blank"/>
             <br class="blank"/>
             <h3>
              1. Scan for deals.
             </h3>
             All right, heres a shameless plug: if you check out
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com" target="_blank">
              Chippmunk.com
             </a>
             before you shop, youll know where all the best deals are on the web. That way, you wont waste time looking for a coupon after the fact, like when you already have your heart set on that Chicago Bears-themed nutcracker. As Jiminy Cricket said, Always let your coupons be your guide. Fine, he might have said conscience, but we still think hed approve.
             <br class="blank"/>
             <br class="blank"/>
             <h3>
              2. Use a coupon.
             </h3>
             Speaking of coupons, use them! It seems so obvious, but its easy to forget once youre on a roll and breezing through checkout. Dont leave that promo box empty!
             <br class="blank"/>
             <br class="blank"/>
             <h3>
              3. Stack your coupons.
             </h3>
             This is what seasoned shoppers call using more than one coupon at once. Not all sites will let you do it, but some that will include
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com/victorias-secret-coupons/" target="_blank">
              Victorias Secret
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com/dsw-coupons/" target="_blank">
              DSW
             </a>
             , and
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com/kohls-coupons/" target="_blank">
              Kohls
             </a>
             . Just be sure to enter your coupons in the right order. If you have a percentage-off coupon, enter that first and then a dollar-amount coupon to maximize your savings.
             <h3>
              4. Keep your eye on the calendar.
             </h3>
             Online retailers tend to offer sales at the beginning of the month (usually around the 2nd), and at the end of the month (around the 27th or 28th). Plan accordingly.
             <h3>
              5. Compare similar stores.
             </h3>
             Think outside the box and feel free to stray from your usual retailers. For example,
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com/1800-flowers-coupons/" target="_blank">
              1800-Flowers
             </a>
             might have the exact same pair of sneakers as
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com/teleflora-coupons/" target="_blank">
              Telefora
             </a>
             . And even if they both offer the shoes for the same price, one website may have a better coupon than the other. So whats an easy way to compare offers? Funny you should ask. Chippmunk will help you compare prices lickety-split.
             <h3>
              6. Sign up.
             </h3>
             Register on your favorite retail sites and theyll send you savings. Some stores will give you a 25% discount just for entering your info. Better yet, just sign up to our
             <a href="http://eepurl.com/vgeF9" target="_blank">
              Chippmunk email
             </a>
             and we'll wade through all the retailer emails and deals - then send you one simple email a week. No spam, we hate that, too!
             <h3>
              7. Never pay for shipping.
             </h3>
             Plenty of stores will offer free shipping when you hit a certain dollar amount, like $49 or $99. If not, you may be able to nix the shipping fee by picking your product up at a local store. If all else fails, check
             <a href="http://www.chippmunk.com" target="_blank">
              Chippmunk
             </a>
             for free-shipping coupon codes. Right now, our favorite free shipping offers are from
