        <h2 class="subtitle ellip curpo mgtop_20 w620" itemprop="alternativeHeadline" onclick="$(this).toggleClass('ellip')" title="The long awaited film is going to start shooting in a few months">
         The long awaited film is going to start shooting in a few months
        </h2>
        <div itemprop="articleBody">
         <p class="intro">
          <strong itemprop="description">
           A brand new report has appeared online claiming that the
           <a href="http://news.softpedia.com/news/World-of-Warcraft-Patch-5-4-Siege-of-Orgrimmar-Gets-Main-Feature-List-376001.shtml" target="_blank">
            World of Warcraft
           </a>
           movie will begin filming in January of 2014 and that the primary location will be Vancouver, located in the British Columbia region of Canada.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="mgbot_20">
          The World of Warcraft movie has been stuck in development limbo for many years, as while there were producers and directors associated with the film, it didn't actually begin its filming process.
         </p>
         <p class="mgbot_20">
          Now, with new Director Duncan Jones, the movie has received a 2015 launch date and, according to a report from website
          <a href="http://www.genibits.com/world-of-warcraft-movie-filmed-in-vancouver/" target="_blank">
           Genibits
          </a>
          , the filming process kicks off in January of 2014.
         </p>
         <p class="mgbot_20">
          The primary shooting location will be Vancouver, British Columbia, a popular pick for many U.S.-based studios because it offers advantageous locations, taxes, and many professionally-trained actors.
         </p>
         <p class="mgbot_20">
          As of yet, however, no other details about the World of Warcraft movie are known.
         </p>
