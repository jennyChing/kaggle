         <p>
          Imagine sending your children with a healthy lunch of leftovers consisting of roast beef, potatoes, carrots, oranges and milk and having the school tell you that the lunch is unbalanced.
         </p>
         <p>
          Imagine then that they REQUIRE that your children eat Ritz Crackers and also fine you $10 for not sending a balanced lunch.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is happening.
         </p>
         <p>
          More often than you realize.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://theysmell.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/20131119-073311.jpg">
           <img alt="20131119-073311.jpg" class="size-full aligncenter" src="http://theysmell.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/20131119-073311.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #ccffff;">
           Image source:
           <a href="http://gawker.com/mom-gives-kids-homemade-lunch-school-forces-them-to-ea-1466822586/@neetzanz" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            <span style="color: #ccffff;">
             Gawker.com
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          I believe that as parents we have the right to not only choose what to feed our children, but to also be notified if someone is going to question our choices.
         </p>
         <p>
          I live in the US and this incident was in Canada but
          <a href="http://www.momdot.com/a-doctors-note-for-gmos/" target="_blank">
           similar stories
          </a>
          are emerging here as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          I would be pretty upset if someone questioned the lunch I packed my child but forcing them to eat something I do not approve of
          <strong>
           crosses the line
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          What about allergies? Dietary restrictions? Personal choice?
         </p>
         <p>
          The bottom line is that Ritz Crackers are full of GMOs, trans fats, high fructose corn syrup, artificial colors (yellow #6 which has been shown to cause ADHD and contain known carcinogens), and even more chemicals. Read more about ingredients in Ritz Crackers products here:Whats in it?:
          <a href="http://theysmell.com/read-the-label-ritz-bits-cheese-crackers/" target="_blank">
           Ritz Bits Cheese crackers
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          How is adding a chemical lab-created concoction healthier than the lunch she provided?
         </p>
