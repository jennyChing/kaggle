        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Why Award Season Matters to Jewelers
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         Posted on
         <a href="http://www.halsteadbead.com/blog/2012/01/11/why-award-season-matters-to-jewelers/">
          January 11, 2012
         </a>
         by
         <a class="url fn" href="http://www.halsteadbead.com/blog/author/halsteadbead/" rel="author" title="Posts by Hilary Halstead Scott">
          Hilary Halstead Scott
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.halsteadbead.com/blog/2012/01/11/why-award-season-matters-to-jewelers/red-carpet-illustration-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-1088">
          <img alt="2012 Upcoming Award Shows" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1088" height="300" src="http://www.halsteadbead.com/blog/images/Red-Carpet-Illustration-300x300.jpg" title="2012 Upcoming Award Shows" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Award season is here. Love it or hate it, there is no denying that Hollywood award shows drive fashion trends. The red carpet gowns and baubles we see for the next two months can drive accessory sales for several years. For jewelry designers these events are a great opportunity to monitor
         <a href="http://www.halsteadbead.com/Shopping-Cart/Products/Sterling%20Silver/" target="_blank" title="Jewelry making supplies for current trends">
          jewelry making
         </a>
         trends and draw inspiration for collections. So, mark your calendars, these are the fashion events you should not miss:
        </p>
        <p>
         January 15 Golden Globe Awards
        </p>
        <p>
         January 29 SAG Awards
        </p>
        <p>
         February 12 Grammy Awards
        </p>
        <p>
         February 26 The Academy Awards / Oscars
        </p>
        <p>
         The blogosphere will be abuzz with chatter about fashion, gossip and results for days after each event. Be sure to check the Halstead Jewelry Blog and our Facebook page for our take on the jewelry highlights. Add your comments and join the fun!
        </p>
