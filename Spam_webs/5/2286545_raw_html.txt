       <h1 class="entry-title">
        How To Remove Veneer (Buffet Transformation!)
       </h1>
       <div align="left" class="post-date">
        09.22.2010
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Ooo Ooo OOO! Ive been so excited to share this one for a long time! This buffets awesomeness is twofold.
        </p>
        <p>
         a) we refinished it ourselves.
         <br/>
         b) it was free
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats right. FREE.
        </p>
        <p>
         Ill tell you the story in a moment, but I just cant resist the affects of a before and after shot. So here they are kids!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnjywlRQ8I/AAAAAAAAC60/VKrR5rFpZps/s640/IMG_6040.JPG" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnkaesyHII/AAAAAAAAC68/nG5IV6wHKkM/s1600/IMG_6633.JPG" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnkaesyHII/AAAAAAAAC68/nG5IV6wHKkM/s1600/IMG_6633.JPG', '']);" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnkaesyHII/AAAAAAAAC68/nG5IV6wHKkM/s640/IMG_6633.JPG" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         We went to pick up this run-down little
         <a href="http://mrssywassink.blogspot.com/2010/09/eloise.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://mrssywassink.blogspot.com/2010/09/eloise.html', 'vanity']);">
          vanity
         </a>
         that we had found on craigslist. And as the man ushered us through his house to show us the vanity we passed by THE BUFFET. And I thought to myself awesome. I wish we could take
         <i>
          that
         </i>
         instead of the vanity. I mentioned to the man (because I have a big mouth),
        </p>
        <p>
         Me: I wish you had this buffet for sale on craigslist.
         <br/>
         Him: actually it IS on craigslist.
         <br/>
         Me: 
         <i>
          Seriously?!?!?!
         </i>
         well I didnt see it on there.
         <br/>
         Him: its listed under the free section.
        </p>
        <p>
         Husband performs CPR.
        </p>
        <p>
         So we cleaned her up, gave her a little love you know the story. And can I say that there something incredibly validating and gratifying for me to bring an item from bane to blessing. Its my fave.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlCZAcHBI/AAAAAAAAC7E/hiYMjlvdDnA/s640/IMG_6038.JPG" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The top surface had some SERIOUS chipping veneer issues. But the wood beneath was in good shape and had a beautiful wood grain, so we went at it with a putty knife and chipped away at it till it was gone! All you need is a little elbow grease!
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlJr9nCZI/AAAAAAAAC7M/fgyAqpKYXZ0/s640/IMG_6211.JPG" width="640"/>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlR9NrS4I/AAAAAAAAC7U/cXLBmH4S92k/s640/IMG_6250.JPG" width="640"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlY3u1oOI/AAAAAAAAC7c/PwRYPOHelQ8/s640/IMG_6385.JPG" width="640"/>
         <br/>
         We refinished but not without a little wine.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlebbn60I/AAAAAAAAC7k/_49fO_lHxE0/s1600/IMG_6389.JPG" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlebbn60I/AAAAAAAAC7k/_49fO_lHxE0/s1600/IMG_6389.JPG', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnlebbn60I/AAAAAAAAC7k/_49fO_lHxE0/s640/IMG_6389.JPG" width="432"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Found us some sweet new hardware at Hobby Lobby (my other lover). Wood puttied up the old holes, and drilled our own:
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnmN_oonbI/AAAAAAAAC7s/eb-4Pe00FT4/s640/IMG_6394.JPG" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Slapped on some paint.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="419" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnuLKSJ1NI/AAAAAAAAC-U/DjMWMBIjQ3U/s640/IMG_6551.JPG" width="640"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         Being the amateurs that we are, we glazed using our blackish/grayish glaze, without thinking that the top surface is stained dark brown, and the hardware is also in the brown family. After glazing, I was extremely disappointed in the outcome. Doesnt it look a little cartoonish? And BLUE? Hate it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnm1bF0flI/AAAAAAAAC78/SlBZVMb8Ahg/s640/IMG_6567.JPG" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         After a trip to town, we reglazed with brown, and I actually love the two-toned glaze weve got going on. Much better. Can you tell the difference? I can.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xMuiFhIkgag/TJnm-QJoC3I/AAAAAAAAC8E/XppMEUzKqUo/s640/IMG_6570.JPG" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         So there you have it folks!
