                <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" style="">
                 <h3 class="p1">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <img align="right" alt="tips" src="http://www.reputationx.com/hs-fs/hub/435812/file-2135288805-png/tips.png?t=1428600341383&amp;width=353" style="font-size: 13px; width: 353px; float: right;" width="353"/>
                  </span>
                 </h3>
                 <h3 class="p1">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   Your mugshot could define you for centuries. Use these tips to make sure its a good one!
                   <!--more-->
                  </span>
                 </h3>
                 <p class="p1">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   Thousands of years in the future, long after humankind has ventured out into the stars, the last remnants of the Internet will still exist on servers somewhere. On those servers will be
                   <strong>
                    the sole surviving image of you
                   </strong>
                   that encapsulates all of your hopes, dreams and accomplishments as a personand that photo
                   <strong>
                    may just be your mug shot
                   </strong>
                   .
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="p1">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   Removing damaging images from the Internet is tough to do, so make sure you follow these tips to
                   <strong>
                    make that special photo one youre proud of
                   </strong>
                   .
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <h2 class="p3">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Plan your arrest photo
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <p class="p1">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   Arrest images can be damaging in search results so plan ahead for a more successful mug shot. Before heading out for the night, check yourself in the mirror and ask yourself,
                   <strong>
                    how will I look in my mug shot tonight?
                   </strong>
                   If the answer is
                   <strong>
                    awesome!
                   </strong>
                   then youre ready to go.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <h2 class="p3">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Pre-arrest checklist
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <p class="p1">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   We created this handy checklist for you. It contains tips for you to remember when you encounter the police photographer you will eventually meet.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <h4 class="p5">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    <img align="right" alt="dear-police-photographer" src="http://www.reputationx.com/hs-fs/hub/435812/file-2134469742-png/dear-police-photographer.png?t=1428600341383&amp;width=379" style="width: 379px; float: right;" width="379"/>
                    Tips for you
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                 </h4>
                 <ul class="ul1">
                  <li class="li1">
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    <strong>
                     Dont wear white.
                    </strong>
                    The surprise of seeing flashing lights behind you
                    <strong>
                     could cause you to spill red wine on your shirt
                    </strong>
                    . Surprisingly, many police stations do not keep a supply of Wine Away in stock.
                   </span>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                  </li>
                  <li class="li1">
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    <strong>
                     Dress up!
                    </strong>
                    Youre being arrested, so dress up a bit. Even the guiltiest Wall Street banker looks like a fine, upstanding citizen when dressed nicely. Gents, wear a collared shirt and tie, if possible. Ladies, opt for fashionable business attire.
                   </span>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                  </li>
                  <li class="li1">
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    <strong>
                     Look directly at the lens.
                    </strong>
                    You dont want to look guilty, even if you are. People who dont make eye contact automatically look guilty. So look confidently into the camera and not at your shoes (if you happen to be wearing them).
                   </span>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                  </li>
                  <li class="li1">
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    <strong>
                     Use eye drops.
                    </strong>
                    Handcuffs make applying eye drops infinitely more difficult - trust us we know. So try to squeeze in a few just as you see the cops start pointing, drawing their sidearms and then running after you.
                   </span>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                  </li>
                  <li class="li1">
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    <strong>
                     Look friendly.
                    </strong>
                    Dont grin, but do look friendly. Happy, confident people look like winnersand you
                    <em>
                     are
                    </em>
                    a winner!
                   </span>
                  </li>
                 </ul>
                 <p>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Tips for the police photographer
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  <br/>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   Remember to fill in the blank below
                   <strong>
                    before
                   </strong>
                   your photo shoot! Keep this information with you if ever you feel that nagging suspicion that you might meet a police photographer that evening.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   But seriously...
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   At least one contributor to this post has been arrested and it's less fun than everyone says it is. But we are quite serious about that mugshot. Its important to look good (and not too drunk) because it will almost certainly show up in your search results. If its a good looking image and you're dressed well, most people may not notice that it is posted on a mugshot website if it is just returned in image results. But if its obvious in the picture you're being arrested it will quickly become, and remain, a problem. If it is a problem you'll need to either get it
                   <a href="/reputation-management-remove-bad-search-results-and-more">
                    <span style="color: #000000;">
                     removed
                    </span>
                   </a>
                   , or
                   <a href="/suppress-negative-search-results">
                    <span style="color: #000000;">
                     suppressed
                    </span>
                   </a>
                   in search results in order to clean up your online reputation. Mugshot removal is tough, and sometimes its impossible to completely get rid of.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </span>
