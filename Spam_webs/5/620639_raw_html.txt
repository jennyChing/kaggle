                    <meta content="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7IrqYpNzFhM/U4M74PUupRI/AAAAAAAAGK0/HiYjYla5jag/s1600/terror-underlying.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Day 453 - Seeing Myself As A Failure Part 2
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3419871250457865568" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     In the previous post I was extrapolating upon the point of
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/life-review-seeing-through-the-eyes-of-the-mind">
                      seeing
                     </a>
                     myself a
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-i-am-not-a-failure-part-51">
                      failure
                     </a>
                     and having that point resonate throughout my
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/an-ordinary-woman-part-1-life-review">
                      experience
                     </a>
                     of myself on this Earth and come through within the sight of what I have put my effort into. This comes through within most of my actions usually because throughout the
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/time-and-money-life-review">
                      time
                     </a>
                     that I have been
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/anna-viktor-discovers-the-physical-we-are-here">
                      here
                     </a>
                     I have accepted myself to degrade myself with
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/life-review-how-thoughts-bombard-the-physical-and-destroy-self">
                      thoughts
                     </a>
                     and have accepted myself to be
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/atlanteans-why-the-mind-is-inferior-to-the-superiority-physicality-part-44">
                      inferior
                     </a>
                     to the
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/can-you-ever-trust-your-mind-reptilians-part-127">
                      mind
                     </a>
                     , inferior to myself, and inferior to others. So as I have accepted these
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-thought-and-thinking-a-deliberate-creation-of-control-part-105">
                      thoughts
                     </a>
                     to accumulate I have accepted this definition of myself as
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/mind-being-body-kryon">
                      being
                     </a>
                     a failure and/or a fuck up and then this point comes across within my actions and
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/the-structural-resonance-veno">
                      resonance
                     </a>
                     .
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7IrqYpNzFhM/U4M74PUupRI/AAAAAAAAGK0/HiYjYla5jag/s1600/terror-underlying.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7IrqYpNzFhM/U4M74PUupRI/AAAAAAAAGK0/HiYjYla5jag/s1600/terror-underlying.jpg" width="226"/>
                     </a>
                     When I take a look at this point as to where it has developed from I see that it comes from the perspective of the system.
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/man-made-meaning-reptilians-part-256">
                      Meaning
                     </a>
                     a failure based on systemic perspectives such as
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/the-novelty-of-poverty-as-the-road-to-wealth-life-review">
                      wealth
                     </a>
                     or grades within the education system or such as goals or aspirations or other such things. I see that I started to believe this from myself starting in Grade 4 when we started to get homework in school. I had absolutely no
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/a-chat-with-desire">
                      desire
                     </a>
                     to go home from school and work on homework. I saw school for school and home
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-time-is-consequence-part-32">
                      time
                     </a>
                     for things that I would like to do with the tools and availability that I had. So each
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/time-dimensions-2013-future-of-consciousness-part-17">
                      time
                     </a>
                     that we got home work I would
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/physical-resistance-relationship-success-support">
                      resist
                     </a>
                     it immediately  because I never saw it as purposeful or any
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/atlanteans-when-energy-has-more-value-than-life-part-40">
                      value
                     </a>
                     within the point of homework - I only saw it as something that was being pushed on me that I did not want to do. This obviously led to marks that were not up to par with the standards of that time and I remember believing that since I had no ambition and no
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-how-the-mind-motivates-physical-movement-part-79">
                      motivation
                     </a>
                     to move myself within the point of homework and since I was getting the grades that I was getting that I was a failure and a low-life, someone who would not accomplish anything in
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-the-game-of-life-part-96">
                      life
                     </a>
                     nor ever be motivated to make `something` of myself within the
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-guidelines-through-the-maze-of-the-world-system-part-20">
                      world system
                     </a>
                     . At that point in
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-time-consciousness-s-worst-enemy-part-67">
                      time
                     </a>
                     I would relate and compare myself to my class mates and
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-the-question-part-7">
                      question
                     </a>
                     why it was that I was that way, why it was that I was getting bad grades and was not able to keep up with the other kids within that class given the specific material that we were learning. From this point on, or if not, earlier, I defined myself as a failure and a fuckup in relation to society and defined myself as
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/life-review-i-m-not-good-enough">
                      less than
                     </a>
                     others within points of
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/life-review-comparison-judgement-and-competition">
                      comparison
                     </a>
                     within points of value.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     A point within this point is self-spite. Since I have little value of myself as a being in relation to this point of self-hate or seeing myself as a failure spite comes in to play within this equation. I will see a mistake that I have made or I will see myself in relation to another person or a point as inferior and from this just get down on myself for a while. I will think thoughts about how I am
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/uselessness-part-1">
                      useless
                     </a>
                     , how I am worthless, how I do not contribute much to bettering myself or this lifetime for any
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/atlanteans-establishing-individuality-part-14">
                      individual
                     </a>
                     and essentially beat up on myself pretty damn hard within thoughts. This results in seeing myself as a failure in relation to myself, in relation to others and in relation to life. From seeing myself as a failure I will accept this definition of myself and
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-facing-choice-part-1-part-107">
                      decide
                     </a>
                     that I do not deserve anything
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/the-little-bit-of-good-frees-me-from-all-the-bad-life-review">
                      good
                     </a>
                     with
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/where-were-you-in-this-life">
                      in this life
                     </a>
                     time and aim to destroy my life. I will think about anything that I hold dear within my
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-the-obvious-secret-reality-or-illusion-part-18">
                      reality
                     </a>
                     and set out to destroy it, I will set out to destroy all
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/relationship-success-support-introduction">
                      relationships
                     </a>
                     within my life and aim for a point of complete
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/atlanteans-isolation-part-12">
                      isolation
                     </a>
                     from everyone - aim for being
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-my-relationship-with-aloneness-part-59">
                      alone
                     </a>
                     with myself with no one around to generate any
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reaction-prevention-quantum-mind-self-awareness">
                      reactions
                     </a>
                     from myself and as well, so that I can sit in a self-pity party with myself
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I have found that when I am in this point I enjoy
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/self-forgiving-hate-atlanteans-support-part-81">
                      hating
                     </a>
                     myself. I
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/looking-for-joy-life-review">
                      enjoy
                     </a>
                     bringing myself further into the despair and actually, as I said, AIM to destroy the
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-relationships-as-illusion-of-control-part-24">
                      relationships
                     </a>
                     in my life. As I will think about destroying the
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/relationship-system-relationship-success-support">
                      relationships
                     </a>
                     I will feel
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-the-force-of-good-part-1-part-94">
                      good
                     </a>
                     within the point of
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/why-do-i-keep-feeling-this-way-reptilians-part-188">
                      feeling
                     </a>
                     bad or making my life more of a hell then what I already perceive it as. I think about
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/isolating-myself-within-an-interest-life-review">
                      isolating myself
                     </a>
                     from everyone and everything and I feel `good` about doing this, feel like it is something that I want.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I see that I have created a
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/relationship-system-practical-support-relationship-success-support">
                      relationship
                     </a>
                     with self-hate to be pleasurable, like masochistic. I have derived pleasure from
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/the-metaphysical-secrets-of-imagination-self-judgment-as-punishment-part-14">
                     </a>
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/how-do-i-stop-punishing-myself-the-metaphysical-secrets-of-imagination-part-20">
                      punishing myself
                     </a>
                     for seeing myself as a failure and almost want to see myself as a failure so I can go into this point of self-spite and destroy the
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-why-do-relationships-exist-part-35">
                      relationships
                     </a>
                     within my life.  From this point I then
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/life-review-acceptance-and-allowance-vs-the-decision">
                      decide
                     </a>
                     to move into this point. I decide to
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/the-forces-behind-love-and-hate-atlanteans-support-part-79">
                      hate
                     </a>
                     myself further and Decide to create a little slice of hell for myself. I do this because I find it a bit pleasurable, pleasurable to take all things
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/life-review-seeing-the-good-when-only-the-bad-prevails">
                      good
                     </a>
                     from my reality so I sit at the bottom of my reality where I can just sit with myself for a while, kinda like bringing myself back to a starting point of nothing and in this nothingness recreate myself again because I have not liked what I have created thus far.  Cool, I am glad that I wrote that because that pretty much sums up the point that I was looking for = bringing myself to a point of nothingness because I do not like what I have created as myself or I do not enjoy an aspect of my reality. From that point I will look to bring myself back down to nothingness so I can restart creating myself, but this is never what happens. I only ever make things a hell of a lot
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/making-matters-worse-reptilians-part-230">
                      worse
                     </a>
                     than what they needed to be. I make my life worse off then what I really wanted because I went into a point of spite to destroy my life - believing that I wanted to destroy my life because of going into that feeling
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     This point has never worked out the way I wanted it to or the way that I saw it
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/what-is-sex-physical-self-movement-vs-mind-experience-part-26">
                      moving
                     </a>
                     . I saw taking my reality down as a `good` thing, as a point of re-birthing myself but that was never the case. What happened was that I was so within the point of emotional
                     <a href="https://eqafe.com/p/reptilians-how-to-manipulate-part-8">
                      manipulation
                     </a>
                     and self-spite/self-hate that I would actually believe that I what I was doing was what I wanted to give to myself.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I will continue in the next blog
