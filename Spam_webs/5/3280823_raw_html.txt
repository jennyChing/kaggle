              <p>
               Based on film permit data from the
               <a href="http://www.nyc.gov/html/film/html/home/home.shtml" target="_blank" title="Mayor's Office of Film">
                Mayors Office of Film, Theatre, and Broadcasting
               </a>
               , this map displays the filming locations of all moviescenes, 17,241 in total, filmed between 2011 and 2013 (excluding thosethat were filmed in parks or on private property).
              </p>
              <h3>
               <a href="http://metrocosm.com/web/film-permits-map-nyc.html" title="Filmed in NYC">
                View interactive map
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               Breaking it down by neighborhood, here are the top five most filmed areas:
              </p>
              <p>
               #1:
               <strong>
                Midtown
               </strong>
               , Manhattan  842 Permits
               <br/>
               #2:
               <strong>
                Williamsburg
               </strong>
               , Brooklyn  771 Permits
               <br/>
               #3:
               <strong>
                Financial District
               </strong>
               , Manhattan  644 Permits
               <br/>
               #4:
               <strong>
                Harlem,
               </strong>
               Manhattan  432 Permits
               <br/>
               #5:
               <strong>
                Greenpoint
               </strong>
               , Brooklyn  409 Permits
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               Looking at the map, a few of densest areas immediately jump out.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://metrocosm.com/web/film-permits-map-nyc.html">
                <img alt="Film Map NYC Dense Locations" class="aligncenter wp-image-51 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/film-map-nyc-dense-locations.png?resize=656%2C429"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <h3>
               Times Square
              </h3>
              <p>
               <a href="http://i0.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/resident_evil.jpg">
                <img alt="Resident Evil Retribution - Times Square" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-58" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/resident_evil.jpg?resize=748%2C339"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Perhaps the most-filmed location in New York City aside from Central Park, Times Square is immediately recognizable as abackdrop to some of the biggest films from recent years.
              </p>
              <p>
               Recent films include: Resident Evil: Retribution (pictured), The Amazing Spiderman 2, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Now You See Me, Thanks for Sharing.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <h3>
               Kaufman Astoria Studios
              </h3>
              <p>
               <a href="http://i2.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bourne_legacy.jpg">
                <img alt="Bourne Legacy - Kaufman Astoria Studios" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-55" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bourne_legacy.jpg?resize=702%2C396"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               One of the largest film studios on the East Coast. Kaufman Astoria Studios recently opened backlot makes it the only film studio in New York with an outdoor stage facility.
              </p>
              <p>
               Recent films include: The Bourne Legacy (pictured), The Secret Life of Walter Mitty, The Immigrant.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <h3>
               Financial District
              </h3>
              <p>
               <a href="http://i2.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/dark_knight_rises.jpg">
                <img alt="The Dark Knight Rises - Financial District" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/dark_knight_rises.jpg?resize=768%2C353"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               With iconic locations includingWall Street and Founders Hall as well as several quiet, old-fashioned-looking streets, the Financial Districtis one of New Yorks most popular locations for film shoots.
              </p>
              <p>
               Recent films include: The Dark Knight Rises (pictured), The Wolf of Wall Street, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <h3>
               47th Regiment Armory
              </h3>
              <p>
               <a href="http://i0.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/amazing_spider_man_2.jpg">
                <img alt="The Amazing Spider-Man 2 - 47th Regiment Armory" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-52" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/metrocosm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/amazing_spider_man_2.jpg?resize=768%2C389"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               One of 22 remaining armories in New York City, the 47th Regiment Armory is one of the largest open indoor spaces in the City,making it an ideal location for the construction of large indoor movie sets.
              </p>
              <p>
               Recent films include: The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (pictured), Noah.
              </p>
              <p>

               </a>
              </p>
