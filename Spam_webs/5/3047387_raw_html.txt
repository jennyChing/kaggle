       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Sharpie mugs, $20 Christmas gifts, and the elephant in the room
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-12-13T17:11:34+00:00">
         December 13, 2014
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/author/amcleod/" rel="author">
           Ashley
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/#comments">
          24 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          *This post was sponsored by the BHG Live Better Network. All opinions are 100% my own.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youve ever used pinterest then chances are youve seen these 
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/search/pins/?q=sharpie%20mug&amp;term_meta%5B%5D=sharpie%7Ctyped&amp;term_meta%5B%5D=mug%7Ctyped" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.pinterest.com/search/pins/?q=sharpie%20mug&amp;term_meta%5B%5D=sharpie%7Ctyped&amp;term_meta%5B%5D=mug%7Ctyped', 'sharpie mugs']);" target="_blank">
          sharpie mugs
         </a>
         . The idea is that you buy a cheap plain mug, personalize it with a sharpie, and voila! Crazy easy personalized coffee cups. Now, Ive been DIYing for a while now and I can tell you one thing for sureif it sounds too good to be true, it probably is. (You can actually apply that to life in general, not just DIY.) I mean, sharpies are great and all, but theyre not magic. They may be nearly impossible to get off a couch, wall, or clothing, but there is no way you can write on a glazed mug with one and expect to stay put, even when you wash it. NO WAY.
        </p>
        <p>
         So this month the Better Homes and Gardens challenge was to put together some gift ideas for under $20. Now, you can put together a decent gift for under twenty bucks, but its not going to be mind-blowing or anything. Unless, of course, something sentimental or homemade is included. Enter the sharpie mug.
        </p>
        <p>
         I spent some time reading through websites that had tried and failed at this in an attempt to come up with a method that actually worked. I found a few that had success stories and combined them all the make a mug that is indestructible.
        </p>
        <p>
         First I bought some
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-18-oz-Porcelain-Tall-Mug-White/25875601" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-18-oz-Porcelain-Tall-Mug-White/25875601', 'plain white coffee mugs']);" target="_blank">
          plain white coffee mugs
         </a>
         for $3.88 a piece
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="plain white coffee mugs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9539 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="298" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/plain-white-coffee-mugs.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         and an
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Sharpie-Oil-Base-Fine-Paint-Markers-Fine-Marker-Point-Type-Gold-Ink-1-Each-SAN35544/40172963" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Sharpie-Oil-Base-Fine-Paint-Markers-Fine-Marker-Point-Type-Gold-Ink-1-Each-SAN35544/40172963', 'OIL BASED sharpie paint pen']);" target="_blank">
          OIL BASED sharpie paint pen
         </a>
         . The oil based part is key here. You can also use a
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Sharpie-Oil-Base-Fine-Paint-Markers-Fine-Marker-Point-Type-Gold-Ink-1-Each-SAN35544/40172963" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Sharpie-Oil-Base-Fine-Paint-Markers-Fine-Marker-Point-Type-Gold-Ink-1-Each-SAN35544/40172963', 'DecoArt glass marker']);" target="_blank">
          DecoArt glass marker
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Pebeo-Porcelain-150-ANTHRACITE-BLACK-Fine-Bullet-Tip-China-Glass-Marker/39103760" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Pebeo-Porcelain-150-ANTHRACITE-BLACK-Fine-Bullet-Tip-China-Glass-Marker/39103760', 'Pebeo porcelain pen']);" target="_blank">
          Pebeo porcelain pen
         </a>
         , but the Sharpie was easy find and cheap, so thats what I went with.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="oil based gold sharpie" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9537 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="342" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/oil-based-gold-sharpie.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I cleaned the cups with alcohol, taped off the top (because I didnt want any marker where you mouth touches) and added some scrapbooking letters.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="sharpie mug prep" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9540 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="562" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/sharpie-mug-prep.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I let the kids go to town and make mugs for each of theirteachers.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="sharpie mugs as teacher gifts" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9541 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="442" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-as-teacher-gifts.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ack, theyre just so cute, right? Levi is grinning away while sporting some sweet bed head, and Elijah is over there acting like he is painting the Mona Lisa.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="how to make personalized coffee cups" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9535 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="386" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/how-to-make-personalized-coffee-cups.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         After they were finished I peeled off the scrapbook letters with a pair of tweezers let them dry for a day before baking. Also, I learned a little too late that if you have any mess ups you can fix them with some rubbing alcohol and q-tips before they dry. We definitely has some bleeding under the letters. Oh well, it adds to the made by a preschooler effect, which is an integral part of this gift.
        </p>
        <p>
         Next I baked the mugs by putting them in a COLD oven, setting it to 400 for an hour, then letting them cool completely before taking them out. This worked great for setting themexcept that it turned the paint from a beautiful gold to a brownish bronze color. Bummer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="baking sharpie mugs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9532 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="426" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/baking-sharpie-mugs.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         And then, since I was combining ALL the things that people said had worked, I taped them off again and sprayed the painted part with clear sealer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="clear coat on sharpie mugs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9534 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="369" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/clear-coat-on-sharpie-mugs.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         BAMpersonalized, indestructible mugs for the kids teachers.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="How to sucessfully make a personalized sharpie mug" class="aligncenter wp-image-9536 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="540" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/How-to-sucessfully-make-a-personalized-sharpie-mug-.jpg" width="388"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I even scrubbed them before taking that pictures (by hand) and noting budged. Which is good, because I would have prettydisappointed if it had.
        </p>
        <p>
         To finish the gifts I headed off the Walmart and bought the things I would like to get as a gift. I have pretty great taste so it seems like no fail method of Christmas shopping.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Beter Homes &amp; Gardens at Wamart" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9533 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="408" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Beter-Homes-Gardens-at-Wamart.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Obviously I love whites and neutrals, blankets, and things that smell like lemon.
        </p>
        <p>
         So here are a few personalized gift ideas that are all under $20
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Personalized gifts under $20" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9543 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.domesticimperfection.com/2014/12/sharpie-mugs-20-christmas-gifts-and-the-elephant-in-the-room/" height="630" src="//www.domesticimperfection.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Personalized-gifts-under-20.jpg" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         1.
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/search/?query=better%20homes%20wax%20warmer&amp;cat_id=4044_133012_1090556" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/search/?query=better%20homes%20wax%20warmer&amp;cat_id=4044_133012_1090556', 'wax warmer']);" target="_blank">
          wax warmer
         </a>
          $10.00, wax scents  $2.00 (x2),
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-18-oz-Porcelain-Tall-Mug-White/25875601" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-18-oz-Porcelain-Tall-Mug-White/25875601', 'Coffee mug']);" target="_blank">
          Coffee mug
         </a>
          $3.88
         <strong>
          Total  $17.88
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         2.
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-Velvet-Plush-to-Sherpa-50-x-60-Throw/38363693" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-Velvet-Plush-to-Sherpa-50-x-60-Throw/38363693', 'throw']);" target="_blank">
          throw
         </a>
         -$12.88, mug  $3.88
         <strong>
          Total  $16.76
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         3.
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-Cotton-Blanket/38234535" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-Cotton-Blanket/38234535', 'blanket']);" target="_blank">
          blanket
         </a>
          $16.96, mug  $3.88
         <strong>
          Total  $20.84
         </strong>
         (whoops!)
        </p>
        <p>
         4. picture frame  $9.96,
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-13-Ounce-Candle-Zesty-Lemon-Peppercorn/35121178" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.walmart.com/ip/Better-Homes-and-Gardens-13-Ounce-Candle-Zesty-Lemon-Peppercorn/35121178', 'candle']);" target="_blank">
          candle
         </a>
          $3.97, mug  $3.88
         <strong>
          Total  $17.81
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I cant even believe I made it through that post without mentioning the elephant in the room. Its such a huge elephant I can barely see to type around it. So here we go, lets just get it out in the open.
        </p>
        <p>
         Today is my 30th birthday.
        </p>
        <p>
         30. 30! Im not quite sure when that happened, but here I am30. I was tempted to be bummed about it for a while, but then I realized that Ive been acting like Im in my 30s for about a decade now. Im been married for over ten years, have (nearly) three children, joyfully drive a nerdy minivan, and the thought of going out past about 8pm exhausts me. I was meant to be in my 30s.
        </p>
