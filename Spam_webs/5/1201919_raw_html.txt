             <div class="article-title-wrap">
              <h1 class="article-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
               BROWNIE POPS
              </h1>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="article-content sc" data-role="content" itemprop="articleBody">
             <div class="pf-content">
              <p>
               <strong>
                I decided that in lieu of St. Patricks Day, I should make something GREEN . . . and pink and orange and purple!
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-alignnone">
               <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-1088 alignnone img-downsized img-gd-downsized ov-done" data-src-2x="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00311(pp_w1528_h1012).jpg" height="506" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00311-897x595(pp_w764_h506).jpg" title="DSC_00311" width="764"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay alignnone" style="width:764px;height:506px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               I really did start out  just intending to make GREEN Brownie pops . . . see
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1101 img-downsized img-gd-downsized ov-done" data-src-2x="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_002612(pp_w1556_h1078).jpg" height="539" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_002612-897x622(pp_w778_h539).jpg" title="DSC_00261" width="778"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:778px;height:539px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               But . . . then those other colors were taunting me, and I caved! These Brownie pops were fun to make. They really were quite easy and are very yummy. I threw all good sense out the window when I decided to jump in and make these. You see . . . my husband doesnt like brownies unless they are mostly nuts! Hes nuts! I ate one . . . said YUM . . . and gave the Brownie Bouquet away!
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Do you want to make these? Heres how:
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Step one: Make the Brownies.
                <br/>
               </strong>
               Make them according to the recipe or package directions. Bake them on parchment paper so you can remove them from the pan completely. Moist, fudgy brownies work best.With a greased knife, cut off the crusts and cut into small bars. Let them cool to where they are easy to handle.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Parchment paper makes this process so simple.
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-1097 aligncenter ov-done" height="358" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_0002-500x358.jpg" title="DSC_0002" width="500"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:500px;height:358px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              Cut Off All the Yummy edges.
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1102 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00031-499x332.jpg" height="332" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0003" width="499"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:499px;height:332px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              Cut Into small squares . . . like this
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1103 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00073-500x354.jpg" height="354" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0007" width="500"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:500px;height:354px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Step two: Mold the brownies into balls.
                <br/>
               </strong>
               If your brownies are moist and fudgy, they will cling together easily. Grease your hands to keep them from getting too sticky. Try to roll the top of the brownie into the center of your ball. The crackly top has dry edges that make it hard to make your ball nice and round.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               just like this . . They really arent as dark as they seem to look.
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1104 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00083-500x363.jpg" height="363" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0008" width="500"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:500px;height:363px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Step three: Insert craft sticks into each ball.
                <br/>
               </strong>
               Go ahead and puch them almost all the way down to the bottom. You want to make sure you dont lose your brownie ball while dipping. Let them cool completely before you start dipping them in chocolate.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1105 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_0012-500x332.jpg" height="332" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0012" width="500"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:500px;height:332px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Step four:
               </strong>
               <strong>
                Dip the brownies in melted chocolate.
               </strong>
               <br/>
               To melt the chocolate wafers, microwave in 15 second intervals stirring after each until they are smooth. Do not over do it. If you cook the chocolate it will not set up very pretty. Dip the brownie pops into the melted dark chocolate completely. We dont want to see any of the brownie. Mugs work very well for these. They are the perfect size and the thick mug holds the heat in so that the chocolate stays warm for a while. Let the chocolate set before you start dipping the pops in the white chocolate. They will no longer look shiney and will get a dull appearance when the chocolate is set up.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               I used Wilton Candy Melts, but you could also find these in bulk. I like to dip things in mugs.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1107 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00145-500x332.jpg" height="332" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0014" width="500"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:500px;height:332px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Dip . . . Dip!
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1108 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00181-500x359.jpg" height="359" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0018" width="500"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:500px;height:359px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Lay each pop on Parchment Paper to cool and harden. Dont worry if they arent perfect. Mine certainly were not!
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1109 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_0020-499x483.jpg" height="483" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0020" width="499"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:499px;height:483px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Step five: Dip the brownies in melted white chocolate.
               </strong>
               <br/>
               Only dip the pops 80% of the way into the white chocolate. It looks nice to see the dark chocolate layer peeking out. Let the white choclate set up a little bit before dipping them any sprinkles. If the chocolate is to drippy the sprinkles will drip right off your pop.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Get Your sprinkles ready.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1113 ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_00221-499x368.jpg" height="368" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0022" width="499"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:499px;height:368px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Sorry, I had chocolate up to my ears, and didnt get a picture of the white chocolate process. Its the same as the milk chocolate,
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               but you only dip 80% of the brownie pop, leaving a bit of the Milk Chocolate showing.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                Step six: Roll the brownies in sprinkles.
                <br/>
               </strong>
               Standard paper muffin liners worked well to set out all my sprinkles. Only dip them half way up the pops so you can still see the white chocolate layer. Roll them in thesprinkles if you have to.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               If you are not going to be serving them right away wrap each one individually in plastic wrap and then tie with ribbons.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
              </p>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1111 img-downsized img-gd-downsized ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_003111-897x595(pp_w778_h516).jpg" data-src-2x="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_003111(pp_w1556_h1032).jpg" height="516" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_00311" width="778"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:778px;height:516px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <strong>
               A secret look inside!
              </strong>
              <div class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
               <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-1112 aligncenter img-downsized img-gd-downsized ov-done" data-lazyload-src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_0034-897x591(pp_w778_h512).jpg" data-src-2x="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_0034(pp_w1556_h1024).jpg" height="512" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" title="DSC_0034" width="778"/>
               <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:778px;height:512px;">
                <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                 <img alt="" height="20" src="http://www.justgetoffyourbuttandbake.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                I hope you all have a great St. Patricks day. Make these with the kids . . . you will go nuts, and the kids will have a ball!
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                I still plan to post the Puff Pastry Marshmallow cups, but thought Id be green first.
               </em>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                Come back soon.

                      I used Milk Chocolate as well.  If it seems too thick, just add a teaspoon of vegetable oil to the melted chocolate. Add more if needed to get the
                      <br/>
                      consistency that works well for you.  Also, before sticking the sticks into the brownie or cake balls, dip the end of the stick in the melted chocolate, then
                      <br/>
                      into the balls. Refrigerate the balls with the sticks inserted, so that the chocolate sets up. This will help secure them, so that they dont come off.
