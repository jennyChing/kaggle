         <div style="text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
          Hi all! First off, I want to say thank you to all of you who rated our project online today! Wow!!!! You guys are really helping us out!!!! Thanks so much!!!!! Now for my latest project! Ive mentioned that early Christmas decorating is a family tradition going way back! We have always put the trees up right after Halloween and it feels right to me! So here I am again, early November decorating my Christmas Tree!
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
          I had to start from scratch this year because ALL of our ornaments were strewn across Hwy 114 on moving day (note to self ~ do not put a lightweight rubbermaid container in the bed of a truck). Thankfully they were of no sentimental value! I was going to revamp them anyway to match my new color scheme!
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGi_XTRrI/AAAAAAAACgg/awKXsNvZx8M/s1600/img_6525.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGi_XTRrI/AAAAAAAACgg/awKXsNvZx8M/s1600/img_6525.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGi_XTRrI/AAAAAAAACgg/awKXsNvZx8M/s400/img_6525.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          Here is the first of the replacements!
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGtNh3H9I/AAAAAAAACgo/BDQ7kwIu2Kg/s1600/img_6495.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGtNh3H9I/AAAAAAAACgo/BDQ7kwIu2Kg/s1600/img_6495.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGtNh3H9I/AAAAAAAACgo/BDQ7kwIu2Kg/s400/img_6495.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          Im sure you all have these!
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGy09uRhI/AAAAAAAACgw/_VMfSpIOAwA/s1600/img_6501.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGy09uRhI/AAAAAAAACgw/_VMfSpIOAwA/s1600/img_6501.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYGy09uRhI/AAAAAAAACgw/_VMfSpIOAwA/s400/img_6501.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          Pair it with a bunch of cream colored buttons and a hot glue gun!
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYG6O8n4pI/AAAAAAAACg4/7971m0oBXjk/s1600/img_6502.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYG6O8n4pI/AAAAAAAACg4/7971m0oBXjk/s1600/img_6502.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYG6O8n4pI/AAAAAAAACg4/7971m0oBXjk/s400/img_6502.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          Cut 6 long and 6 shorter pieces using a pair of wire cutters
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHC8GybrI/AAAAAAAAChA/r6kz0pijGEw/s1600/img_6503.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHC8GybrI/AAAAAAAAChA/r6kz0pijGEw/s1600/img_6503.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHC8GybrI/AAAAAAAAChA/r6kz0pijGEw/s400/img_6503.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          and hot glue each piece around a large button alternating sizes.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHGhKU5gI/AAAAAAAAChI/NJJ1bXv_Xjo/s1600/img_6505.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHGhKU5gI/AAAAAAAAChI/NJJ1bXv_Xjo/s1600/img_6505.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHGhKU5gI/AAAAAAAAChI/NJJ1bXv_Xjo/s400/img_6505.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          more hot glue.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHT102NsI/AAAAAAAAChQ/Dtt6TLEaxn8/s1600/img_6506.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHT102NsI/AAAAAAAAChQ/Dtt6TLEaxn8/s1600/img_6506.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          and sandwich the wires with another large button.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHY0xqPDI/AAAAAAAAChY/6dIj6Sz2w90/s1600/img_6509.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHY0xqPDI/AAAAAAAAChY/6dIj6Sz2w90/s1600/img_6509.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHY0xqPDI/AAAAAAAAChY/6dIj6Sz2w90/s400/img_6509.jpg"/>

         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          Glue the buttons to each wire creating a pattern
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHfiY86uI/AAAAAAAAChg/N7tlSbNQx4g/s1600/img_6510.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHfiY86uI/AAAAAAAAChg/N7tlSbNQx4g/s1600/img_6510.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHfiY86uI/AAAAAAAAChg/N7tlSbNQx4g/s400/img_6510.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          and flip! Copy the same pattern on the reverse side.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          Now for the hangin
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHu7Vhs9I/AAAAAAAAChw/Pb-7AzxkFao/s1600/img_6512.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHu7Vhs9I/AAAAAAAAChw/Pb-7AzxkFao/s1600/img_6512.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYHu7Vhs9I/AAAAAAAAChw/Pb-7AzxkFao/s400/img_6512.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

         </div>

         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYH715xJOI/AAAAAAAACh4/Guf2sZagjnY/s1600/img_6513.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYH715xJOI/AAAAAAAACh4/Guf2sZagjnY/s1600/img_6513.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYH715xJOI/AAAAAAAACh4/Guf2sZagjnY/s400/img_6513.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          and coil up to the tip of the wire.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYH-78DIDI/AAAAAAAACiA/8WMm13zpYwo/s1600/img_6514.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_cduEct3OnPA/SwYH-78DIDI/AAAAAAAACiA/8WMm13zpYwo/s1600/img_6514.jpg', '']);" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
