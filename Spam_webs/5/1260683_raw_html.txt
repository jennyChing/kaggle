            <br/>
            Sometimes, there are people who think that healthier meals are less tastier. But what they dont know is that there are a lot of ways to cook meals that are full of nutrients without compromising its flavorful taste. All you have to do is to replace some ingredients or change the way to prepare it. Now that the holiday season has come, the tendency for us to gain some in our waistline has come as well. But worry no more, you can still stick to a healthy eating habit with these tips.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <b>
             Be smart in choosing which is which
            </b>
            <br/>
            It is true that todays market are filled with processed foods that are most likely not healthy for you. This is why you need to carefully choose the ones youre going to purchase. Read the labels. Know the difference between wheat and non wheat. Educate yourself that not all carbs are bad for you because there are
            <a href="http://www.quora.com/Rasha-Tiozon/Posts/Eat-More-Carbs-Good-and-Healthy-Ones-I-Mean" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             <u>
              healthy carbs
             </u>
            </a>
            for you to enjoy too.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <b>
             Add more colors
            </b>
            <br/>
            Colors, I mean fruits and vegetables. If you dont like to eat a meal with only veggies as the ingredient, then add them to your favorite meals instead. If you have just switched into a healthy lifestyle, you are probably not used to eating these too. So, instead of suddenly switching your entire meal plan, why not adjust a little day by day. Add a little portion of orange, yellow, or green colors to your usual meals.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <b>
             Keep an eye on the portions
            </b>
            <br/>
            Its okay to give in to the temptation of your favorite meals. But take note of the serving size. If you are trying to lower down your weight then it is just fair to lower down the serving size of your meals. Instead of the typical two cups of rice, try to trim it down into one.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <b>
             Enhance using natural herbs and spices
            </b>
            <br/>
            Watch out of your usage of salt and sugar, those little additions to your meals might turn into a little addition to your unhealthy scale. Why not change your cooking routine that uses
            <a href="http://www.ihcginjections.com/blog/herbs-spices-will-help-lose-weight/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             <u>
              natural herbs and spices
             </u>
            </a>
            instead of the instant seasonings? Though this may take more effort for preparing, the taste of your meal will surely improve and be more natural.
