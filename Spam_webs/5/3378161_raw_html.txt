          <div class="clearboth">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="clearboth">
         </div>
         <div class="mk-single-content" itemprop="mainContentOfPage">
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.dirtyredhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/FallenLeader-scaled.gif">
            <img alt="Priorities" class="wp-image-367 size-medium alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.dirtyredhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/FallenLeader-scaled.gif?resize=300%2C188"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I just read the 
           <a href="http://livingnotwanting.com/about-me/" target="_blank" title="Living Not Wanting ~ about me">
            about me
           </a>
           , on the blog 
           <a href="http://livingnotwanting.com/about-me/" target="_blank" title="Living Not Wanting ~ about me">
            Living Not Wanting
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           In it she says, a year ago I decided I am tired of being angry and pissed off all the time.
          </p>
          <p>
           Simple, but profoundly insightful.
          </p>
          <p>
           This is a thought that indicates that shes made a decision to change. And will.
          </p>
          <p>
           Are you ready for a change?
          </p>
          <div class="tm-tweet-clear">
          </div>
          <div class="tm-click-to-tweet">
           <div class="tm-ctt-text">
            <a href="https://twitter.com/share?text=Are+You+Ready+For+Change%3F&amp;url=http://www.dirtyredhat.com/are-you-tired-of-being-pissed-off-all-the-time/" target="_blank">
             Are You Ready For Change?
            </a>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a class="tm-ctt-btn" href="https://twitter.com/share?text=Are+You+Ready+For+Change%3F&amp;url=http://www.dirtyredhat.com/are-you-tired-of-being-pissed-off-all-the-time/" target="_blank">
             Click To Tweet
            </a>
           </p>
           <div class="tm-ctt-tip">
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           It isnt something that happens overnight.
          </p>
          <p>
           Like getting healthier and fitter, like getting wiser, like becoming wealthier; like most things, it takes time and happens gradually.
          </p>
          <p>
           The little efforts and nudges each day of your life will manifest over time to become your life.
          </p>
          <p>
           Maybe it is too big a challenge to imagine being like Matthieu Ricard (hes been called the happiest man in the world), being happy 100% of the time, but you can stop being pissed off all the time.
          </p>
          <p>
           Start small.
          </p>
          <p>
           Practice
           <a href="http://www.dirtyredhat.com/gratitude/" target="_blank" title="Gratitude">
            gratitude
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Practice tolerance.
          </p>
          <p>
           Smile. Inside and out.
          </p>
