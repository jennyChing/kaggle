       <h3>
        MBBS Admission in Green Life Medical College Dhaka
       </h3>
       <p>
        GLMCH is a tertiary medical service center with 400 bed. It offers specialty and super specialty services, including state-of-the-art diagnostic facilities to ensure the delivery of holistic patient care. The hospital is staffed with dedicated and highly competent members of the medical fraternity along with trained personnel who work with sophisticated state-of-the-art equipment.
       </p>

        <em>
         Dhaka University
        </em>

        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Started on 2005, Green Life Medical College Hospital now has 14 full fledged departments to provide specialty and super specialty services.
       </p>

       <p>
        <i>
         <b>
          <i>
           <b>
            Admission
           </b>
          </i>
          Criteria for Indian Students
         </b>
        </i>
        <br/>
        1. Equivalence of Foreign Degree:
       </p>
       <p>
        Must have passed qualifying examinations (HSC/ A level) or equivalent examination.
       </p>
       <p>
        SSC/ 0 level or equivalent examination passed not before 2012
       </p>
       <p>
        HSC/ A level or equivalent examination passed not before 2014
       </p>
       <p>
        Minimum aggregate GPA in SSC or equivalent and HSC or equivalent : 7.00 (seven)
       </p>
       <p>
        Minimum GPA either in SSC or equivalent or in HSC or equivalent: 3.0
        <br/>
        Minimum GPA in biology: 3.5
        <br/>
        For calculation of GPA against the marks obtained in SSC/ 0 level
        <br/>
        or equivalent exam, only top 5 subjects are considered for
        <br/>
        calculation of GPA.
        <br/>
         Therefore, GPA in 0 level = (total marks obtained in top five subjects)  5
        <br/>
        For calculation of GPA against the marks obtained in HSC/A level or equivalent
        <br/>
        exam, only 3 subjects (Physics, Chemistry and Biology) are considered for calculation
       </p>
       <p>
        of GPA. Qualifying grade in each subject individually is C.
        <br/>
        Therefore, GPA in A level = (total marks obtained in three subjects)3
       </p>
       <p>
        2. Prospectus and Application Form:
       </p>
       <p>
        Three (3) copies of duly filled application forms along with the following documents should be sent.
        <br/>
        Copy of passport
        <br/>
        Six (6) passport size photographsAll certificates duly attested by the competent authority.
       </p>
       <p>
        3. Application Fee:
        <br/>
        Each applicant must attach with his/her application a non-refundable Demand Draft of US $ 50 (fifty)
       </p>
       <p>
        4. Only the applications processed through Smile Education Consultancy India or through the Edu Care Career NepalMissions in Dhaka of respective countries will be considered for admission. Applications must reach the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Dhaka within the application deadline, which will be published inthe websites of DGHS, Bangladesh (www.dghs.gov.bd), Consulate of Bangladesh (www.bangladeshconsulatela.com)and
        <a href="http://www.mbbsbangladesh.com/contact-us/" title="Contact Us">
         <strong>
          www.mbbsbangladesh.com
         </strong>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        5. Applications should be submitted in the prescribed form (to be available from DGHS website:www.dghs.gov.bd). Attested copies of certificates and mark-sheets etc. have to be submitted along with the application. All certificates and mark-sheets must be attested. No application will be accepted without attestation.
       </p>
       <p>
        6. To ensure citizenship of foreign students, attested copies of passport must be submitted along with the application. The certificates/mark sheets and other testimonials need to be attested.
       </p>
       <p>
        7. Candidates interested in seeking admission in GMC, need to mention the name of Green Life Medical College in their application.
       </p>
       <p>
        8. Successful applicants will have to produce all the original academic certificates mark-sheets/transcripts during their admission.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Accommodation Facilities
        </strong>
        <br/>
        Accommodation Facilities are available for Foreign students.
