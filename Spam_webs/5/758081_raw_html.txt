    <div id="author-info">
     <p>
      Albert Einstein (14 March 1879  18 April 1955) was a theoretical physicist, philosopher and author, widely regarded as one of the most influential and best known scientists and
      <a href="http://quotabl.es/quotes/tagged/intellectual">
       intellectuals
      </a>
      of all time. A German-Swiss Nobel laureate, he is often referred to as the father of modern physics. He received the 1921 Nobel Prize in
      <a href="http://quotabl.es/quotes/tagged/physics">
       Physics
      </a>
      "for his services to Theoretical Physics, and especially for his discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect".
      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_Einstein">
       Read more on Wikipedia...
      </a>
