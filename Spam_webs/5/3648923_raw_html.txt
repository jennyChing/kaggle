        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Simple art ideas :: painting on foil
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-04-02T19:13:09+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          02/04/2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/author/cathy_a_toner" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Cathy James
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/simple-art-ideas-painting-foil#comments">
           7 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Heres one of our favourite
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/category/art-and-craft" target="_blank" title="simple art ideas kids">
          simple art ideas
         </a>
         
         <strong>
          painting on foil
         </strong>
         , which is lots of fun, great for sensory play, and creates gorgeous collaborative art.
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/simple-art-ideas-preschool-painting-on-foil.jpg">
          <img alt="Simple art ideas for kids :: painting on foil" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19220" height="900" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/simple-art-ideas-preschool-painting-on-foil.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #008080;">
          Simple art ideas :: painting on foil
          <span id="more-19212">
          </span>
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Sometimes you need a quick rescue remedy  something thats easy to set up, will engage a group of children, and hopefully perk up a gloomy day or grumpy kiddos. This simple set up of painting on foil is one of those activities. All you need is a roll of kitchen foil, some paint, and either brushes or hands to create some art.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/simple-art-ideas-painting-on-foil.jpg">
          <img alt="Simple art ideas for preschool, toddlers and kids :: painting on foil" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19213" height="675" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/simple-art-ideas-painting-on-foil.jpg" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I laid out our roll of foil on the table, fastening it in place with a little sticky tape at the corners. The children used paint brushes today, so I set out a palette with their pick of colours, but you might like to squirt some paint straight onto the foil and swoosh it around with your hands  super sensory play.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7758.jpg">
          <img alt="painting on foil" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19214" height="500" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7758.jpg" width="334"/>
         </a>
         Then its all-join-in time. The foil is an interesting surface to paint on  quite different to paper, which is most likely what your children are used to. The foil is very smooth, so the paint glides, and sits on the surface rather than being absorbed a with paper. The shiny surface adds an extra sensory element, and it makes a noise as you paint on it too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7763.jpg">
          <img alt="simple art ideas for kids painting" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19215" height="500" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7763.jpg" width="334"/>
         </a>
         And you can make all sorts of gorgeous patterns, from waves
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7772.jpg">
          <img alt="painting on foil" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19216" height="334" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7772.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         to follow-the-leader dots
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7774.jpg">
          <img alt="easy painting ideas for preschool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19217" height="334" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7774.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         and mandala shapes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7799.jpg">
          <img alt="easy painting ideas for preschool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19218" height="334" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7799.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         Laid out on the table, the foil can be a canvas for a big collaborative piece, with everyone painting their own section, or overlapping across the foil, layering the paint and colours as you go.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7803.jpg">
          <img alt="simple art ideas for kids" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19219" height="500" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_7803.jpg" width="334"/>
         </a>
         <span style="color: #008080;">
          More simple art ideas
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         For more simple art ideas, take a look at our pretty
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/spring-art-kids-watercolor-flowers" target="_blank" title="apring art kids watercolor flowers">
          spring flowers
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/spring-art-kids-watercolor-flowers">
          <img alt="Beautiful spring art for kids: try this watercolor flowers technique" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19102" height="600" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/spring-art-for-kids-watercolor-flowers.jpg" width="401"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Enjoy some sensory play with
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/color-mixing-activities-shaving-foam" target="_blank" title="color mixing activities shaving foam">
          color mixing activities with shaving foam
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://nurturestore.co.uk/color-mixing-activities-shaving-foam">
          <img alt="Color mixing activities : hands-on learning with sensory play" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19058" height="500" src="//nurturestore.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/color-mixing-activities.jpg" width="334"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
