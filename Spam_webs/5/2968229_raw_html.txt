       <h1 class="headline">
        Box Set John Goodman &amp; the Coen Brothers
       </h1>
       <span class="post-byline">
        By
        <a href="http://theinterrobang.com/author/kyleadamski/" rel="author" title="Posts by Kyle Adamski">

        <div id="content-area">
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="GOODMAN2" class=" wp-image-99625 alignnone" height="435" src="http://mypullzone.orangepopmediall.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/GOODMAN2-580x435.jpg" width="580"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Box Set takes a look at those great pairings between writers, actors, musicians, artists, directors, etc., that arent just one and done projects, but span years, sometimes even decades. Follow these collaborations between iconic figures as they developed together in their respective art forms.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          The
          <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="http://theinterrobang.com/tag/coen-brothers/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with coen brothers">
           Coen Brothers
          </a>
          and
          <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="http://theinterrobang.com/tag/john-goodman/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with john goodman">
           John Goodman
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          For over 25 years, Joel and
          <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="http://theinterrobang.com/tag/ethan-coen/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with ethan coen">
           Ethan Coen
          </a>
          have teamed up with John Goodman to bring us not only some of Goodmans most iconic roles, but easily one of the greatest film characters of all time in Walter Sobchak from The Big Lebowski. Starting with 1987s Raising Arizona through 2013s Inside Llewyn Davis, the Coens have continuously utilized Goodman in roles both great and small that showcase his amazing talents.
         </p>

          <h3 class="footer-widget-header">
           About The Interrobang
          </h3>
          <div class="textwidget">
           <p>
            Finding comedy in everything and everything in comedy.
           </p>
           <p>
            interrobang, interrobang  [in-ter-uh-bang]  (often represented by ?! / !?), is a nonstandard punctuation  designed to combine the question mark (?) and the exclamation point (!), indicating a mixture of query and interjection, as after a rhetorical question.
           </p>
           <p>
            Focusing on the most interesting ! and ? in life, theinterrobang.com is a place to talk about the comedy in everything, and everything in comedy.  From street corners to  theaters, arenas, print, television, film, or even the White House, if it's funny, or should be, we're talking about it. We also continue to bring you everything happening in the news that's worth discussing, the best recommendations on the internet, and interviews with the most creative people on the planet.
           </p>
           <p>
            Interested in contributing?
            <a href="http://theinterrobang.com/contact-us/">
             Hit us up on our contact page.
