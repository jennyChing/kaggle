       <div class="tagline">
        Rock 'n Roll, running and lung cancer: Kristinas personal crusade in her stepdads memory
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="description">
       <p>
        Throughout her life, Kristina Lindblad has watched countless friends and family members battle various forms of cancer. From as young as seven, she remembers watching her mother support friends fighting the disease.
       </p>

      <div class="story-meta">
       <ul class="list-unstyled">
        <li>
         <img class="meta-icon" src="https://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/themes/edh-portal-2014/assets/img/sidebar-spark.png"/>
         <h5>
          The Spark
         </h5>
         <p>
          Currently living in Florida, Kristina took the opportunity to honor her late step-dad in her first race since he's passed by going home to race in the Rock 'n' Roll Virginia Beach Half Marathon where her support system of family and friends live.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li>
         <img class="meta-icon" src="https://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/themes/edh-portal-2014/assets/img/sidebar-action.png"/>
         <h5>
          The Action
         </h5>
         <p>
          Training this summer in the 100 plus degree weather in Florida has not been easy but hasn't stopped Kristina's drive to train and complete the race in Virginia Beach to raise money and awareness for lung cancer.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li>
         <img class="meta-icon" src="https://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/themes/edh-portal-2014/assets/img/sidebar-result.png"/>
         <h5>
          The Result
         </h5>
         <p>
          When Kristina crosses the finish line, she will have not only raised money in honor of her step-dad but will have pushed herself to her limits in the city she calls home.
         </p>
        </li>
       </ul>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="main-col">
      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        <span id="more-1055">
        </span>
        Her stepfather, who had raised her and her sister, was diagnosed with stage four lymphoma when she was in her 20sand beat the odds and won the battle. But a few years later, cancer struck againthis time, it was lung cancer. Four days before his 67th birthday, her stepfather lost his battle.
       </p>
       <p>
        As she grew up, doing everything she could to combat cancer became a personal crusade for Kristina. She quickly realized with all the various charity races out there, running could help accomplish more than just staying in shape. It was a great way to keep herself fit while raising awareness and funds for each of the different cancers that had impacted her loved ones lives. Along with her sister, she raced to raise money for breast cancer, lymphoma and several other diseases.
       </p>
       <p>
        But there was one glaring omissionthere were no races that fundraised for lung cancer, the illness that had claimed her stepfathers life.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         The stigma surrounding lung cancerthat its something they brought on themselves by smokingmeant there were no races to run, she explained.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        So, when she decided to run the
        <a href="https://www.everydayhero.com/us/runnraise-rock-n-roll-marathon-series/" target="_blank">
         Rock n Roll Marathon
        </a>
        in her hometown of Virginia Beach, she was ecstatic when she reached the registration page. Instead of having one dedicated charity, she had the option to sign up with
        <a href="https://www.everydayhero.com/us/runnraise-rock-n-roll-marathon-series/" target="_blank" title="Run'n'Raise with everydayhero in the Rock'n'Roll Marathon">
         everydayhero
        </a>
        and fundraise for the cause that meant the most to her.
       </p>
       <p>
        Instead of fundraising for just one kind of cancer, though, she decided to help the families feeling its impact. She selected the
        <a href="http://www.ccfot.org">
         Cancer Care Foundation of Tidewater
        </a>
        as the cause she was going to run and raise money for through
        <a href="https://rocknrollvabeach2014.everydayhero.com/us/kristina" target="_blank">
         her fundraising page
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         People dont understand how challenging it can be for a family facing these kinds of medical bills, she said. We were fortunate and had good insurancebut not every family has the money needed for treatment.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        The Cancer Care Foundation of Tidewater also happens to help those in the Virginia Beach area, Kristinas hometown, so she is helping an organization that provides services directly to the people she knows and loves.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Helping a hometown charity hits a lot closer for me because I was born and raised in Virginia Beach, she said. So many of the people I know that have been afflicted with cancer are from the area, so I know its helping them.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        If you want to rock out for a cause close to your heart, you now can thanks to a rockin partnership between the Rock n Roll Marathon Series and everydayhero.

        </a>
        and register today to Run n Raise for more than 50 local and national charities!
