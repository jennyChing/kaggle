          </a>
         </h1>

         <div id="triberr_endorsement">
         </div>
         <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_5899" style="width: 510px">
          <a href="http://guide2travel.ca/2014/07/10-things-to-do-in-southern-saskatchewan-in-the-summer/ss06-2/">
           <img alt="Southwest Saskatchewan" class="wp-image-5899" height="501" src="http://guide2travel.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/ss061-550x551.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Southwest Saskatchewan. Photography by Douglas E. Walker. Photo credit to Tourism Saskatchewan/Douglas E. Walker.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          July is one of the best months to visit Saskatchewan! Our
          <a href="http://www.currentresults.com/Weather/Canada/Saskatchewan/temperature-july.php" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.currentresults.com/Weather/Canada/Saskatchewan/temperature-july.php', 'daytime temperature']);" target="_blank">
           daytime temperature
          </a>
          averages 25 or 26 degrees (77 to 79 F) Celsius, followed by colorful sunsets (around 9:15 p.m.) with gradual cooling through the twilight hours (full dark near 11 p.m.), so we have long, gorgeous days.
         </p>
         <p>
          Since half of the
          <a href="http://www.currentresults.com/Weather-Extremes/Canada/sunniest-places.php" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.currentresults.com/Weather-Extremes/Canada/sunniest-places.php', 'sunniest places in Canada in July']);" target="_blank">
