        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Oreo Balls No Bake Dessert!
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2013-02-21T08:00:55+00:00">
          21 February, 2013
         </span>
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">

         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://whoneedsacape.com/2013/02/oreo-balls-no-bake-dessert/oreo-balls/" rel="attachment wp-att-6899">
           <img alt="Oreo Balls No Bake Dessert ~ Who Needs a Cape?" class="aligncenter wp-image-6899" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Oreo-balls.jpg?resize=1050%2C696"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So, my husband was listening to the radio and the DJs were RAVING about some
          <a href="http://whoneedsacape.com/2013/05/peanut-butter-oreo-truffles-no-bake-dessert/" target="_blank" title="Peanut Butter Oreo Truffles (No Bake Dessert)">
           Oreo Balls
          </a>
          that a listener had sent to them. They said all you needed was a package of Oreos, a package of cream cheese and some chocolate. My husband told me this would make a great blog  aka I would like you to make those for me! SoI did.
         </p>
         <p>
          I used Peanut Butter Oreos  YUM. But you really could use any Oreo you want (mint would be awesome!) I also used microwavable chocolate for dipping. SCORE  thank you to the people (Bakers Chocolate) who invented that. But if you need to you can melt your own chocolate in a double broiler
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-155" style="border: 1px dashed;">
          <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
           <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
            <div class="item b-b">
             <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
              <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-155', 'http://whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/plugins/ziplist-recipe-plugin/'); return false" title="Print this recipe">
               Print
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="zl-recipe-link fl-r zl-rmvd" id="zl-recipe-link-155">
             </div>
             <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
              Oreo Balls No Bake Dessert
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
             </div>
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
             </div>
             <div class="zlclear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="img-desc-wrap">
             <p class="t-a-c hide-print">
              <img alt="Oreo Balls No Bake Dessert" class="photo" data-recalc-dims="1" itemprop="image" src="http://i1.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Oreo-balls.jpg" title="Oreo Balls No Bake Dessert"/>
             </p>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 package of Oreos (Peanut Butter is what I used)
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 8 ounce package of cream cheese - softened
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              Chocolate melted for dipping
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place cookies in food processor, blend until they are fine crumbs (You could break the cookies in a bag if you don't have a processor)
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Add in cream cheese in smallish chunks and blend until combined (you could use a hand mixer)
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Form into balls and place on wax papered cookie sheet.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Put into fridge and allow to set (1/2 hour)
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Dip into chocolate and place back on wax paper
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Refrigerate until set
             </li>
            </ol>
