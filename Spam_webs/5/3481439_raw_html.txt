             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              Flea Market Memories
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5384933572167314267" itemprop="description articleBody">
              Dh &amp; I love to "sale" &amp; "flea" every weekend. This is how I find so many of my garden, house &amp; shop treasures. Today, instead of the usual jaunts through the south shore, we decided to venture into Queens (NY) &amp; visit the flea market at Aqueduct Raceway. Boy, were we in for a surprise! We never realized how big it is, nor how diverse in ethnicity &amp; cultures it was. We did a lot of bargain shopping &amp; we will definately re-visit!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCarsP_h2ZI/AAAAAAAABHE/8Ry7fcp1kTs/s1600/aqu.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5487261972693113234" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCarsP_h2ZI/AAAAAAAABHE/8Ry7fcp1kTs/s400/aqu.jpg" style="MARGIN: 0px 10px 10px 0px; WIDTH: 317px; FLOAT: left; HEIGHT: 308px; CURSOR: hand"/>
              </a>
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCar5ThmCZI/AAAAAAAABHM/6828ByRSNr0/s1600/aqu1.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5487262196979599762" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCar5ThmCZI/AAAAAAAABHM/6828ByRSNr0/s400/aqu1.jpg" style="MARGIN: 0px 0px 10px 10px; WIDTH: 319px; FLOAT: right; HEIGHT: 292px; CURSOR: hand"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              This got me to thinking of how many other flea markets we've frequented over the years - many out of state, stopping as we pass them, but none were like the ones we visited as teenagers and young adults - like the one of a kind Roosevelt Raceway flea market
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCasenYiQ6I/AAAAAAAABHU/oO2IQnYhlz4/s1600/FLEA-MARKET--det--222.jpg">
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCauXlscgXI/AAAAAAAABHk/DPvMqJqVOPI/s1600/roosevelt_raceway_sign.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5487264916276281714" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCauXlscgXI/AAAAAAAABHk/DPvMqJqVOPI/s400/roosevelt_raceway_sign.jpg" style="WIDTH: 400px; HEIGHT: 225px; CURSOR: hand"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCauohlAfHI/AAAAAAAABHs/W3-nkK824s0/s1600/1996_july_roosevelt_raceway.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5487265207229119602" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCauohlAfHI/AAAAAAAABHs/W3-nkK824s0/s400/1996_july_roosevelt_raceway.jpg" style="WIDTH: 400px; HEIGHT: 225px; CURSOR: hand"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Sadly the raceway was demolished to make way for "senior housing" &amp; more stores.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Then there was Bethpage Farmers Market - which I went to with the parents, almost every weekend for fruits &amp; vegetables, fish &amp; other assorted goodies. Guy &amp; I used to visit there a lot too - one of our favorite resturants, The Crystal Ship, was located there
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCava73wPsI/AAAAAAAABH0/j0Xb9P4NRtU/s1600/bethpage+farmers.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5487266073280528066" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCava73wPsI/AAAAAAAABH0/j0Xb9P4NRtU/s400/bethpage+farmers.jpg" style="WIDTH: 400px; HEIGHT: 278px; CURSOR: hand"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Mysteriously, this caught fire &amp; burned to the ground &amp; Lowes now stands there.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Finally our current favorite, The Bellmore flea market. It is held at the train station every week, alternately being sponsored by The Bellmore Kwians Club &amp; The Bellmore Synagogue - one of the best!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_SfBxOd0TRq8/TCawSeDW8GI/AAAAAAAABH8/HjSURMjZJW0/s1600/bell+flea.jpg">
