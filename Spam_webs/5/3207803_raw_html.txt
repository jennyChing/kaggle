          <h3>
           What to do when a cop wants to search your car
          </h3>
          <p>
           Even if you think you have nothing to hide, giving a police officer consent to search your vehicle could have unintended consequences. Law enforcement officers have a huge advantage in these situations and knowing how to exercise the rights you have during a police stop is the first step in leveling the playing field. Check out this great video from
           <a href="http://www.flexyourrights.com/" target="_blank">
            flexyourrights.com
           </a>
           .
