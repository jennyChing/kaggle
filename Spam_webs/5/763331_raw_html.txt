        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Freescale  Your One Stop Shop for Reliable Embedded Processing Solutions
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="author vcard" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <span itemprop="name">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.just-connectors.com/blog/author/futurism/" itemprop="url" rel="author" title="View all posts by Joe Faruqui">
             Joe Faruqui

         </span>
         <span class="meta-separator">
          
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.just-connectors.com/blog/freescale-your-one-stop-shop-for-reliable-embedded-processing-solutions/" rel="bookmark" title="12:13 pm">

         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
        <div align="justify">
         <img alt="Freescale Semiconductor" class="wp-image-538 alignleft" height="99" src="http://www.just-connectors.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/freescale-semi.jpg" width="266"/>
         <strong>
          Make the World a Smarter Place:
         </strong>
         Freescale, headquartered in Austin, Texas, designs, researches, develops, manufactures, and sells its product portfolios in 20 different countries. Freescale is a leader in embedded processing solutions for the automotive, consumer, industrial and networking markets.
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           What are the Products Offering by Freescale?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Their product lines consist of the following:
         </p>
         <ul style="text-align: justify;">
          <li>
           <strong>
            Microcontrollers
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             8-bit Microcontrollers, 16-bit Microcontrollers, Digital Signal Controllers, Qorivva 32-bit MCUs, MAC7xxx 32-bit automotive, Kinetis MCUs, ColdFire+ 32-bit MCUs, ColdFire 32-bit MCUs. Microcontrollers are small computers on a single integrated circuit containing a processor core, memory, and programmable input/output peripherals.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Processors
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             QorlQ Processing Platforms, C29x Family of Crypto Coprocessors, PowerQuicc Communication Processors, Host and Integrated Host Processors, Image Cognition Processors. They are hardware inside a computer that carries out the instructions of a computer program by fulfilling the basic arithmetical, logical, and input/output operations of the system.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Analog and Power Managemen
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Freescale gives robust, dependable, high-performance analog, mixed-signal, and power solutions that bridge real world signals with joined processor intelligence enabling entire embedded system solutions.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            RF Power and RF Lower Power
           </strong>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Sensors
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Battery sensors, magnetic sensors, touch sensors, pressure sensors, acceleration sensors, and gyroscope. This new sensor design is the right combination of intelligent integration to deliver smarter applications.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Software and tools
           </strong>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Hardware Development, software development, run-time software, special adapters, programmers. Freescale allows a wide variety of applications with a wide range of software offerings, from drivers and simple demonstration software to extremely integrated vertical solutions.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Committed to Deliver Only the Best and Innovative Products at Unbeatable Price Range:
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Freescale has an extensive history of technology development and has generated some of the most world changing inventions. They invest over $800M dollars a year in research and development to enable smallest-geometry, richly optimized and higher-performance products. Freescale is committed to being the best semiconductor company in the world by giving the highest levels of product quality, delivery and service. At the moment, they are currently ranked 7th among semiconductor sales leaders in the United States, and ranked 16th worldwide.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Consider Partnering with ASAP Semiconductor for Freescale Products:
          </strong>
          <br/>
          ASAP Semiconductor, through its proprietary website Just Connectors, is a leading
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.just-connectors.com/manufacturer-freescale_semiconductor.aspx" target="_blank" title="Freescale Semiconductor Connector Parts">
            distributor of all Freescale products
           </a>
          </strong>
          . Please contact ASAP Semiconductor today at
          <a href="mailto:sales@just-connectors.com">
           sales@just-connectors.com
          </a>
          or at our toll free number1-714-705-4780and one of our experienced sales representatives will be ready to assist you.
         </p>
