        <h1 class="post-title">
         Corn &amp; Avocado Salad
        </h1>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <span class="date">
          June 21, 2011
         </span>
        </div>
        <p>
         Inspired by
         <em>
          Real Simples
         </em>
         3-Ingredient Meals, I made this last night:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://ramshackleglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/IMG_36451.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-465860117" height="299" src="http://ramshackleglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/IMG_36451-1024x682.jpg" title="IMG_3645" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         CORN &amp; AVOCADO SALAD (
         <em>
          serves 4 as a side
         </em>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         Boil 2 ears of sweet corn until just cooked. Use a sharp knife to remove kernels, and toss in bowl with one diced avocado and three thinly sliced scallions. Dress with 3 tbsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp lemon juice, sea salt, and freshly-ground pepper.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://ramshackleglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/IMG_36484.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-465860118" height="299" src="http://ramshackleglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/IMG_36484-1024x682.jpg" title="IMG_3648" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         (Served alongside chicken, pea &amp; mushroom stir-fry over rice. The pairing made absolutely no sense but nicely used up all the veggies I had in my refrigerator, and was completely delicious.)
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out this months
         <em>
          Real Simple
         </em>
         for more amazing 3-ingredient recipes (next up to try: Chocolate Pound Cake w/ Ricotta).
        </p>
