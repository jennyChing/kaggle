        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Breaking News: CMS Extends Deadline for Meaningful Use Attestation
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://meaningfuluse.edublogs.org/2015/03/05/breaking-news-cms-extends-deadline-for-meaningful-use-attestation/" rel="bookmark" title="3:45 pm">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-03-05T15:45:13+00:00" pubdate="">
           March 5, 2015
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://meaningfuluse.edublogs.org/author/anjummudassar/" rel="author" title="View all posts by anjummudassar">
            anjummudassar
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) have announced a new date for Meaningful Use attestation deadline.
        </p>
        <p>
         Eligible professionals (EPs), participating in the Medicare incentive program (only), now have time till March 20, 2015 to attest for Meaningful Use rather than the previous deadline of February 28, 2015.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://meaningfuluse.edublogs.org/files/2015/03/Meaningful-Use-CureMD-w04jid.jpg">
          <img alt="Meaningful Use-CureMD" class="aligncenter wp-image-5" height="228" src="https://meaningfuluse.edublogs.org/files/2015/03/Meaningful-Use-CureMD-w04jid-300x138.jpg" width="495"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This relaxation is intended to bring relief to physicians by giving them extra time to submit their Meaningful Use data. However, CMS has urged practitioners to take advantage of the extra time and begin attesting as soon as they can.
        </p>
        <p>
         For further read visit
         <a href="http://blog.curemd.com/breaking-news-cms-extends-deadline-for-meaningful-use-attestation/" target="_blank" title="Meaningful Use- CureMD">
          http://blog.curemd.com/breaking-news-cms-extends-deadline-for-meaningful-use-attestation/
         </a>
        </p>
