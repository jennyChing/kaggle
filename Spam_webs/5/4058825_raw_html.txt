         <p>
          <b>
           "Stevonnie"
          </b>
          is the fusion of
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Steven" title="Steven">
           Steven
          </a>
          and
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Connie" title="Connie">
           Connie

          <a href="/wiki/Alone_Together" title="Alone Together">
           Alone Together
          </a>
          ".

         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Appearance">
           Appearance
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Stevonnie is currently the shortest fusion in the series. They are taller than
          <a href="/wiki/Amethyst" title="Amethyst">
           Amethyst
          </a>
          and
          <a href="/wiki/Pearl" title="Pearl">
           Pearl
          </a>
          , but are a few inches shorter than
          <a href="/wiki/Garnet" title="Garnet">
           Garnet
          </a>
          . They have an average/athletic build consisting of Connie's slim figure with Steven's chubby features. They have dark curly hair that reaches to their thighs, and a complexion slightly darker than Steven's. Their attire consists of Steven's star shirt layered under a cropped version of Connie's cyan dress, Steven's jeans morphed into cutoff shorts, and bare feet. Stevonnie appears to be a teenager, similar in height to the other teenage characters (
          <a href="/wiki/Lars" title="Lars">
           Lars
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/wiki/Sour_Cream" title="Sour Cream">
           Sour Cream
          </a>
          , etc.), if not taller. Stevonnie is considered to be very beautiful, as the citizens of Beach City seem to be in awe of their appearance and personality.
         </p>

         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Personality">
           Personality
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Stevonnie's personality is unique. They are the first fusion between a half-Gem and a non-Gem, and they do not have a single, distinct mind apparent in other
          <a href="/wiki/Fusion_Gems" title="Fusion Gems">
           Fusions
          </a>
          . Stevonnie shares the minds and personalities of
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Connie" title="Connie">
           Connie
          </a>
          and
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Steven" title="Steven">
           Steven
          </a>
          , and they frequently alternate in their control of Stevonnie's behavior and thoughts. They carry the free spirit, friendliness, and fun-loving attitude of Steven and the courtesy, awkwardness, and intelligence of Connie. While Steven is the primary instigator of the fusion, Connie appears to be the dominant personality.
         </p>
         <p>
          After Stevonnie shows their dance moves at the rave party and notice that everyone stopped dancing, they become nervous and had a panic attack. This suggests that Stevonnie may have social anxiety disorder, and Steven or Connie (most likely Connie) experiences this as well.
         </p>
         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Abilities">
           Abilities
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Stevonnie, like all fusions, appears to possess both super-human strength and agility although in very modest quantities; nowhere near the capabilities of even a non-fused Gem. This is evident by the fact that their dance moves during the rave would require degrees of strength and agility above that of a human and more similar to that of a Gem.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is most likely a result of
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Steven" title="Steven">
           Steven
          </a>
          , being a half-Gem, inheriting a fraction of his
          <a href="/wiki/Rose_Quartz" title="Rose Quartz">
           mother's
          </a>
          super-human strength and agility as he is occasionally shown to be capable of feats of strength which would be impossible for a human boy of his age (such as forcing the
          <a href="/wiki/Evil_Spirit" title="Evil Spirit">
           possessed breakfast monster
          </a>
          into the pool of lava in "
          <a href="/wiki/Together_Breakfast_(episode)" title="Together Breakfast (episode)">
           Together Breakfast
          </a>
          ", tearing an
          <a href="/wiki/Meat_Beat_Mania" title="Meat Beat Mania">
           arcade machine
          </a>
          apart with his bare hands in "
          <a href="/wiki/Arcade_Mania" title="Arcade Mania">
           Arcade Mania
          </a>
          ", lifting
          <a href="/wiki/Jenny" title="Jenny">
           Jenny
          </a>
          in "
          <a href="/wiki/Joy_Ride" title="Joy Ride">
           Joy Ride
          </a>
          ", and punching the door hard enough to activate the air bag, and dent the inside of
          <a href="/wiki/Greg%27s_Van" title="Greg's Van">
           Greg's van
          </a>
          in "
          <a href="/wiki/The_Return" title="The Return">
           The Return
          </a>
          "). Most likely, Stevonnie would not be able to pull out Steven's/Rose's Shield
Due to the fact Stevonnie is 25% gem.
         </p>

         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">
           Trivia
          </span>
         </h2>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <a href="/wiki/Amethyst" title="Amethyst">
            Amethyst
           </a>
           came up with the name "Stevonnie".
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie is the second
           <a href="/wiki/Gems" title="Gems">
            Gem
           </a>
           not to be named after a gemstone (the first being
           <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Steven" title="Steven">
            Steven
           </a>
           ) as well the first fusion not named after a gemstone.
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie is the first hybrid fusion shown, and is also the firstfusion with only one gemstone, due to the fact that Connie doesn't have one.
          </li>
          <li>
           They are the secondfusion that does not have an extra set of arms, the first being
           <a href="/wiki/Garnet" title="Garnet">
            Garnet
           </a>
           , and the third being
           <a href="/wiki/Rainbow_Quartz" title="Rainbow Quartz">
            Rainbow Quartz
           </a>
           .
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie is the second fusion to only have two eyes, the first one being
           <a href="/wiki/Opal" title="Opal">
            Opal
           </a>
           .
          </li>
          <li>
           They are the third Gem to not feature their weapon, the first being
           <a href="/wiki/Lapis_Lazuli" title="Lapis Lazuli">
            Lapis Lazuli
           </a>
           and the second being
           <a href="/wiki/Peridot" title="Peridot">
            Peridot
           </a>
           .
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie is the first fusion to be fused by accident as there was no intent to fuse in either "
           <a href="/wiki/Alone_Together" title="Alone Together">
            Alone Together
           </a>
           " or "
           <a href="/wiki/We_Need_to_Talk" title="We Need to Talk">
            We Need to Talk
           </a>
           ."
           <ul>
            <li>
             They are also the second fusion not to show their weapon,
             <a href="/wiki/Alexandrite" title="Alexandrite">
              Alexandrite
             </a>
             being the first,
             <a href="/wiki/Malachite" title="Malachite">
              Malachite
             </a>
             being the third, and Rainbow Quartz being the fourth.
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie's portrayed age may have been created by combining
           <a href="/wiki/Connie_Maheswaran" title="Connie Maheswaran">
            Connie
           </a>
           's and
           <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Steven" title="Steven">
            Steven
           </a>
           's respective ages.
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie's gemstone is the same as Steven's (and
           <a href="/wiki/Rose_Quartz" title="Rose Quartz">
            Rose Quartz's
           </a>
           ).
          </li>
          <li>
           Stevonnie is the second fusion shown that did not include
           <a href="/wiki/Amethyst" title="Amethyst">
            Amethyst
           </a>
           .
           <ul>
            <li>
             The first is
             <a href="/wiki/Garnet" title="Garnet">
              Garnet
             </a>
             , revealed in "
             <a href="/wiki/Jail_Break" title="Jail Break">
              Jail Break
             </a>
             ".
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           Like
           <a href="/wiki/Malachite" title="Malachite">
            Malachite
           </a>
           and
           <a href="/wiki/Alexandrite" title="Alexandrite">
            Alexandrite
           </a>
           , Stevonnie did not seem to have their own personality at first, as their first few lines were Steven and Connie talking. After a few seconds, unlike the other two, Stevonnie's dominant personality had become Connie's.
          </li>
          <li>
           According to
           <a href="/wiki/Rebecca_Sugar" title="Rebecca Sugar">
            Rebecca Sugar
           </a>
           , Stevonnie is a "...metaphor for all the terrifying firsts in a first relationship, and what it feels like to hit puberty and suddenly find yourself with the body of an adult, how quickly that happens, how it feels to have a new power over people, or to suddenly find yourself objectified, all for seemingly no reason since youre still just you..."

            </a>
           </sup>
          </li>
         </ul>
