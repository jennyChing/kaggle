         <a href="http://tinaramchandani.com/#/holiday-house-nyc/" target="_blank">
          Holiday House NYC
         </a>
         and it was one of the best experiences. I met so many wonderful people and participated in a great cause. This year, the Holiday House NYC is back and invited 23 amazing designers to redecorate a beautiful house in the Upper East Side. Like every year, all events proceeds benefit The Breast Cancer Research Foundation. The showhouse is open until this Saturday and if you havent already visited you really should try to stop by! Heres a quick preview of 10 rooms that caught our eye.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          25th Anniversary Room
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Matthew Patrick Smyth for Traditional Home
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         In order to celebrate the 25th Anniversary of Traditional Home Magazine and the 125th Anniversary of Schumacher,
         <a href="www.matthewsmyth.com" target="_blank">
          Mr. Smyth
         </a>
         created astunningsitting room, full of fine art, antiques and mid-century finds.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/25th-Anniversary-Room.jpg">
          <img alt="25th Anniversary Room" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14777" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/25th-Anniversary-Room.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Galerie des Glaces
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Amy Lau for Baccarat
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         This year, Baccarat, the famous luxury French brand, celebrates its 250th anniversary.In honor of this grand anniversary
         <a href="www.amylaudesign.com" target="_blank">
          Amy Lau
         </a>
         created a Versailles inspired dining room, full of winter and Christmas items.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Galerie-des-Glaces.jpg">
          <img alt="Galerie-des-Glaces" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14778" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Galerie-des-Glaces.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Kara Mann Design
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="www.karamann.com" target="_blank">
          designer
         </a>
         wanted to mix modern pieces with old ones to created a unique space.The single loop
         <a href="http://www.rollandhill.com" target="_blank">
          Roll &amp; Hill
         </a>
         light in the center of the room is a pleasant surprise!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Kara-Mann-Design.jpg">
          <img alt="Kara Mann Design" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14779" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Kara-Mann-Design.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Kentucky Derby Day
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Patrick James Hamilton
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Last year
         <a href="http://askpatrick.blogspot.com" target="_blank">
          Patrick James Hamilton
         </a>
         created a St. Patricks Day room that was layeredwith an immense amount of detail at every turn.This year he created an equally detailed room but with a completely different look! The space is filled withderby day inspired furniture. Our favorite part? The amazing oil painting above the turquoise divan.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Kentucky-Derby-Day.jpg">
          <img alt="Kentucky Derby Day" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14780" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Kentucky-Derby-Day.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Mischievous Night
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Louis Navarette
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.louisnavarretedecoration.com" target="_blank">
          Louis Navarrete
         </a>
         wanted, through modern and antique furniture, to mix two different styles: Downtown Abbey and Comicon. The result is stunning and makes the room fun and stylish, both at the same time.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Mischievous-Night.jpg">
          <img alt="Mischievous Night" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14781" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Mischievous-Night.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Mothers Day
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Alyssa Kapito and Vivian Muller
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         This Upper East Side
         <a href="www.kapitomuller.com" target="_blank">
          duo
         </a>
         created the perfect room to spend Mothers Day. They cleverly mixed modern midcentury and classical pieces.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Mothers-day.jpg">
          <img alt="Mother's day" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14782" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Mothers-day.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Personal Day
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Caleb Anderson Design
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="www.calebandersondesign.com" target="_blank">
          Caleb Anderson
         </a>
         , who recently received the IFDAs 2014 Rising Star Award, created a unique and very personal room with an amazing library and a daybed to lounge while reading the surrounding books.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Personal-Day.jpg">
          <img alt="Personal Day" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14783" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Personal-Day.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Reflection
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Natalie Kraiem Interiors
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         During the holidays, everyone should take some time for reflection. In order to show that
         <a href="http://www.nataliekraiem.com" target="_blank">
          Natalie Kraiem
         </a>
         and Sabrina Brinbaum juxtaposed reflective materials to create the perfect space for contemplating.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Reflection.jpg">
          <img alt="Reflection" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14784" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Reflection.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Summer Solstice
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Laura Krey
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Have you ever wondered how your place would look like with custom-commissioned art and handcraft? Well open your eyes and take notes because
         <a href="www.laurakreydesign.com" target="_blank">
          Laura Krey
         </a>
         created an entry you would probably enjoy spending time in, and all the details are really inspiring.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Summer-Solstice.jpg">
          <img alt="Summer Solstice" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14785" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Summer-Solstice.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Winter Solstice
         </strong>
         <em>
          by Noella Ibanez for Gaggenau
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         This kitchenette is probably one the most eye-catching
         <a href="www.gaggenau.com/us/" target="_blank">
          rooms
         </a>
         in the whole house, thanks to the galaxy ceiling, which adds a very uniquelight to the space. Who wouldnt enjoy en early coffee break in such a cozy place.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Winter-Solstice-Kitchenette.jpg">
          <img alt="Winter Solstice Kitchenette" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14786" src="http://lifeinsketch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Winter-Solstice-Kitchenette.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         We hope youll have a chanceto visit Holiday House NYC this year. If you do please tweet/share and let us know!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
