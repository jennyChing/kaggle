          </a>
           the webs best self-serve ad marketplace for Christian websites  has greased up its operation and is dishing up a more palatable name:
         </i>
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">
          <i>
           Bacon Ads
          </i>
         </a>
         <i>
          .
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         BELLINGHAM, Wash. (April 1, 2015)  Beacon Ads, the Internets leading
         <a href="http://beaconads.com">
          <img alt="Bacon-Web-Ad_100x800" class="alignright wp-image-2102 size-full" height="600" src="http://natesmoyer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Bacon-Web-Ad_100x800.png" width="100"/>
         </a>
         advertising marketplace for Christian websites, has changed its name to the more meaty Bacon Ads.
        </p>
        <p>
         Everythings better with bacon, said Nate Smoyer, Bacon Ads Manager. So why not skim the fat off our name and make it something you can really sink your teeth into? At Bacon Ads, were all about bacon care of business. We pride ourselves on offering Grade A advertising services that cut through the baloney and get straight to the meat of things.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">
          Bacon Ads
         </a>
         helps web advertisers reach their audience, track performance, and manage dozens of adsall from one dashboard. It also connects publishers with more than 1,500 websites, giving them complete control over the ads placement, price, and size.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont let those other advertising services scare you off with warnings about your cholesterol, said Aaron Armstrong from
         <a href="http://www.bloggingtheologically.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015">
          Blogging Theologically
         </a>
         . 
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">
          BaconAds.com
         </a>
         gets results  and its delicious.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Internet agrees  if you want to bring home the bacon and manage your ads with a smart, simple dashboard, theres no better choice than Bacon Ads.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://beaconads.com">
          <img alt="Bacon-Web-Ad_BaconGraph" class="alignleft size-large wp-image-2105" height="557" src="http://natesmoyer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Bacon-Web-Ad_BaconGraph-1024x725.png" width="788"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive been a fan of
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">
          BaconAds.com
         </a>
         for years now, said Jason Whitehorn, blogger at
         <a href="http://weleadworship.com/">
          WeLeadWorship.com
         </a>
         . I once tried to switch to another platform, you know  cold turkey. But I soon discovered that turkey bacon just wasnt the same. Will I give up BaconAds.com again? Yeah  when pigs fly.
        </p>
        <p>
         Web ads have never been so delicious: check out
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">
          BaconAds.com
         </a>
         today.
        </p>
        <p>
         ###
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          About Beacon Ads, Now Bacon Ads
         </b>
        </p>
        <p class="p2">
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">
           Bacon Ads
          </a>
         </span>
         , formerly
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">

         </a>
         , is the worlds leading advertising marketplace for Christian websites and advertisers. In addition to providing a beautiful dashboard that tracks impressions, clicks, and click-through rates,
         <a href="https://beaconads.com/?utm_source=press&amp;utm_medium=release&amp;utm_content=baconads&amp;utm_campaign=promo-beaconads2015#baconads">

         now gives your website the irresistible aroma of one of the finest foods known to man: Bacon.
