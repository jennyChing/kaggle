              <h2>
               <a href="http://www.congressrental.co.za/blog/news-and-events/high-quality-conference-equipment-on-rent">
                High Quality Conference Equipment on Rent!
               </a>
              </h2>
              <ul class="info">
               <li class="user">
                By Tech Guys
               </li>
               <li class="tags">
                1Tags
               </li>
               <li class="comments">
                0 Comments
               </li>
              </ul>
              <div class="blog-content">
               <p>
                Important business decisions are made at board meetings, with in-depth brainstorming, conventional discussions and debates. All the attendees offer their views in order to reach an agreement so that the goals discussed and debated can be executed. What makes a conference successful and worthy of attending? Impressive presentations by speakers and the agenda for the meeting contribute to the success of a conference but the arrangement of the audiovisual system also plays an essential role.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                We at Congress Rental are the leading suppliers of conference and simultaneous interpretation equipment in Africa. We are the only company in the conferencing industry to offer sales and rentals to our prestigious clients. Using only high quality branded equipment, such as BOSCH, we make your conference worth attending.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                We have numerous conference systems available for your selection, allowing you the flexibility to choose the system which best suits your venue.
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 DCN New Generation
                </li>
                <li>
                 CCS 900 Ultro
                </li>
                <li>
                 Wirless Microphone System
                </li>
                <li>
                 Concentus NG System, As well as many other award-winning BOSCH systems
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                A successful conference is measured not only by professional presentations, productive debates and conclusive discussions but also by the use of sophisticated conferencing equipment and gadgetry such as, microphones, speakers, lights, projectors, screens, computers, laptops and quality sound systems. High quality sophisticated pieces of equipment facilitate clear exchange of words and messages being delivered during the meeting. Every conference needs a little zest and the right audio visual equipment will definitely deliver that.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Visuals add an element of elegance to conferences like no other. With the combination of crystal clear sound and the high definition visuals, you can be assured that your delegates will walk out of your conference more satisfied, informed and impressed.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Today, there are multiple professional firms that rent out
                <a href="http://www.congressrental.co.za/conference" target="_blank">
                 conference equipment
                </a>
                to various business organizations that need the latest equipment for their conference needs. All you need to do is find the right service provider. We take immense pride in declaring ourselves to be the only company which provides 100% customer satisfaction. We assure you that your guests will definitely be delighted with your event or meeting.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                We offer tailor-made services catering to the organizations specifications and preferences. For the smooth functioning of board meetings, presentations and corporate conferences, we provide you with the best solutions. Various personalized packages designed for digitized conference systems, professional microphones for multiple applications, voting systems, video screens, projectors and other platforms are beyond compare.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                We offer both temporary and permanent Public Address (PA) options. Crystal clear sound and sound penetration is what any public venue or event requires. Congress Rental proudly uses the best technology available in the industry.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Our qualified engineers and technicians identify your specifications and accordingly set up the equipment. They have vast experiences in the field. Our team allows peace of mind to the client regarding technicalities of the AV equipment and installation of the systems.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Catering to individual and organizational needs, we rent out products that meet your specific requirements, ensuring that your conference goes on without any technical glitches.
               </p>
              </div>
