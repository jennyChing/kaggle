             <h4 class="entry-title text-center">
              Enter Our Moving With Arpin Contest Today!
             </h4>
             <div class="post-meta-box">
              <div class="entry-meta text-center">
               <span class="entry-date">
                <time>
                 Feb 06, 2015
                </time>
               </span>
               <span class="category">
                <i class="fa fa-tags">
                </i>
                <a href="http://www.arpin.com/index.php/blog/C3">
                 From the Arpin Team
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <!-- /.entry-meta -->
             </div>
             <!-- /.post-meta-box -->
             <div class="entry-content">
              <p>
               <br/>
               The third annual Moving With Arpin video contest is back!
               <img alt="" src="http://www.arpin.com/images/uploads/Screen Shot 2015-02-06 at 5_52_38 PM.png" style="width: 100px; height: 63px; margin: 8px; float: right;"/>
               Our contest has been very popular the past two years and we cant wait to see what contestants have in store for 2015. To make it fresh and switch things up a bit, weve changed some of the rules and where were hosting it.
              </p>
              <p>
               In the past, we uploaded all entries to our official YouTube page, where the winners would be based on popular vote. However, we noticed that third party sources were being used to elevate like and view count. We realize this is unfair and have changed where were hosting the contest.
              </p>
              <p>
               This year, everything will take place on our official
               <a href="http://www.facebook.com/arpinvanlines">
                Facebook
               </a>
               page. Videos are to be submitted though a short form and the video you submit will be hosted on your own personal
               <a href="http://www.youtube.com/arpinvanlines">
                YouTube
               </a>
               page. Once the submission period is over, voting will occur in the same area.
              </p>
              <p>
               We will disqualify any entry that we find is using a 3
               <sup>
                rd
               </sup>
               party source to increase votes. The same applies if we find out a video was created by a 3
               <sup>
                rd
               </sup>
               party source and not by the individual submitting it.
              </p>
              <p>
               Here are some important dates to remember:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                February 2  March 27: Submissions are accepted
               </li>
               <li>
                April 1  April 30: Voting period
               </li>
               <li>
                May 4: Winners will be contacted
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               All prizes will remain the same as last year:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                1
                <sup>
                 st
                </sup>
                Place: $3,000
               </li>
               <li>
                2
                <sup>
                 nd
                </sup>
                Place: $500
               </li>
               <li>
                3
                <sup>
                 rd
                </sup>
                Place: $250
               </li>
               <li>
                Arpins Choice: $1,000
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               We love holding this contest because we leave it up to you about why someone should choose Arpin as his or her mover. Its exciting to see the different viewpoints and we enjoy this just as much as you do. If you need any ideas, check our
               <a href="http://www.youtube.com/arpinvanlines">
                YouTube
               </a>
               page for past entries.
              </p>
              <p>
               Have fun and good luck!
              </p>
