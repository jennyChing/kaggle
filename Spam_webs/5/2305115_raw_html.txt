        <h1>
         We are offering a Pre-Order on The Drawings of Bob Peak!
        </h1>
        <p>
         Now that weve fully funded the Kickstarter Campaignweremaking the book available to pre-order now. It will ship immediately after all of the Kickstarter pledge books have been distributed, around the 3rd week in September, 2015
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          THE DRAWINGS OF BOB PEAK
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1067" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://www.bobpeak.com/product/the-drawings-of-bob-peak-pre-order/">
          <img alt="The Drawings Of Bob Peak Book Cover" class="wp-image-1067" height="339" src="http://www.bobpeak.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DrawingsOfBobPeakBookCover-960.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          The Drawings of Bob Peak Book Cover
         </p>
        </div>
        <h3 class="null">
         <strong>
          About the new book.
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Two years ago, I produced a large coffee-table book on 
         <em>
          The Art of Bob Peak.
         </em>
          The book was a Labor of Love and something I just had to do in celebration of my fathers illustrious career. The book proved to be an overwhelming success with an endless stream of orders throughout the United States andaround the World. As a follow-up, I thought Bob Peak enthusiasts, artist, illustrators, art students, and designers would be interested in a book showcasing 
         <strong>
          <em>
           The Drawings of Bob Peak
          </em>
         </strong>
         . This 160 page oversized soft cover book (14 x 11) curated collection of his best drawings is a comprehensive look into a rarely viewed side of Bob Peak with never before seen charcoal, graphite, art market, pen, ink, pencil, and pastel drawings from the maestro himself. This oversized book will take on the look and feel of the actual artwork with drawings large enough to study and admire the draftsmanship of the artist Bob Peak. Produced by Art Works Fine Arts Publishing which also produced the original 
         <em>
          The Art of Bob Peak
         </em>
          book, this new book will be the same high quality that you expect and I require. I will not produce anything surrounding the legacy of my father Bob Peak that does not meetwith the highest quality and standards. I appreciate your pledge backer support.
        </p>

        </h3>
        <h3 class="null">

        <h3 class="null">
         Support the Kickstarter Campaign and getthe new Bob Peak Book:
        </h3>
        <p>
         <strong>
          THE DRAWINGS OF BOB PEAK.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Two years ago, I produced a large coffee-table book on 
         <em>
          The Art of Bob Peak.
         </em>
          The book was a Labor of Love and something I just had to do in celebration of my fathers illustrious career. The book proved to be an overwhelming success with an endless stream of orders throughout the United States andaround the World. As a follow-up, I thought Bob Peak enthusiasts, artist, illustrators, art students, and designers would be interested in a book showcasing 
         <strong>
          <em>
           The Drawings of Bob Peak
          </em>
         </strong>
         . This 160 page oversized soft cover book (14 x 11) curated collection of his best drawings is a comprehensive look into a rarely viewed side of Bob Peak with never before seen charcoal, graphite, art market, pen, ink, pencil, and pastel drawings from the maestro himself. This oversized book will take on the look and feel of the actual artwork with drawings large enough to study and admire the draftsmanship of the artist Bob Peak. Produced by Art Works Fine Arts Publishing which also produced the original 
         <em>
          The Art of Bob Peak
         </em>
          book, this new book will be the same high quality that you expect and I require. I will not produce anything surrounding the legacy of my father Bob Peak that does not meetwith the highest quality and standards. I appreciate your pledge backer support.
        </p>

         Bob Peak  Four Decades of American Illustration  at The Weisman Museum of Art
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.bobpeak.com/bob-peak-master-of-the-movie-poster-exhibit-at-pepperdine-university/" rel="bookmark" title="4:39 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          April 4, 2014
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.bobpeak.com/author/the-art-of-bob-peak/" title="View all posts by The Art of Bob Peak">
          The Art of Bob Peak
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_770" style="width: 260px">
         <a href="http://www.bobpeak.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/ThoroughlyModernMillie250x239.jpg">
          <img alt="Thoroughly Modern Millie" class="wp-image-770 size-full" height="239" src="http://www.bobpeak.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/ThoroughlyModernMillie250x239.jpg" width="250"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <em>
           Thoroughly Modern Millie
          </em>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Were pleased to announce a new Bob Peak gallery event at the Frederick R. Wesiman Museum of Art at Pepperdine University.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          This exhibition will feature original art by Bob Peak. Many of these works are on loan from private collections and will be shown to the public for the first time at this event.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         These pieces reflect the full range of his career  from his early work in advertising and fashion, to the famous posters he designed for movies such as
         <em>
          My Fair Lady
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Thoroughly Modern Millie
         </em>
         , and
         <em>
          Apocalypse Now
         </em>
         to his work for a variety of national clients, including
         <em>
          Time
         </em>
         magazine,
         <em>
          Cosmopolitan
         </em>
         , and
         <em>
          Sports Illustrated
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Exhibit will runMay 10  August 3, 2014
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Please join us for theOpening Reception
          <br/>
         </strong>
         Saturday, May 10, 2-4 pm
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://arts.pepperdine.edu/museum/2013-2014/bob-peak.htm">
          <img alt="WeismanRevisedTimeImg" class="aligncenter wp-image-747 size-full" height="491" src="http://www.bobpeak.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/WeismanRevisedTimeImg.jpg" width="736"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         This exhibition was organized by the
         <br/>
         Frederick R. Weisman Museum of Art
         <br/>
         Pepperdine University
         <br/>
         24255 Pacific Coast Highway
         <br/>
         Malibu, CA 90263
        </p>
        <p>
         Museum hours: Tuesday through Sunday, 11 AM  5 PM
        </p>
        <p>
         Closed Mondays and the Fourth of July
         <br/>
         No admission fee
         <br/>
         General information: 310-506-4851
         <br/>
         Museum staff: 310-506-7257
         <br/>
         <a href="arts. pepperdine.edu">
          arts. pepperdine.edu
         </a>
        </p>
