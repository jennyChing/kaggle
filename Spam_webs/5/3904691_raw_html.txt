       <div class="headline_area">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Capturing Sweet Memories
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           Mrs. BWF
          </span>
         </span>
         on
         <abbr class="published" title="2014-05-24">
          May 24, 2014
         </abbr>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Recently
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CanonCanada" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/CanonCanada', 'Canon Canada']);" target="_blank">
          Canon Canada
         </a>
         contacted me about their
         <a href="http://www.canon.ca/inetCA/products?m=gp&amp;pid=24222" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.canon.ca/inetCA/products?m=gp&amp;pid=24222', 'Rebel SL1']);" target="_blank">
          Rebel SL1
         </a>
         and a
         <a href="http://yourbabymoments.ca/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://yourbabymoments.ca/', 'new app']);" target="_blank">
          new app
         </a>
         they are launching. They asked if Id like to try it out. Now, Im a 100% iPhone pic kind of gal, but thought, Hey, why not? I have a lot of kids and am always around cute babies!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         I started playing with it and absolutely fell in love. I am in just the beginning stages of learning all this Rad Rebel has to offer, so I can not wait until I know more!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         A close up of my sweet girl.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sweet Girl, Cannon SL1" class="aligncenter wp-image-17928" height="493" src="http://birthwithoutfearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Sweet-Girl-Cannon-SL1.jpg" width="740"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Something I am already madly in love with is the continuous shootingoption. When you choose this option, you can holddown the shutter button and it will take photo after photo. SomethingI cant do on my iPhone as it needs to readjust after each shot! This comes in handy with 5 young children, especially my youngest spirited child. I managed to get a full tantrum on camera and its adorable (on film, not in person).
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Expression of the spirited child, Cannon SL1" class="aligncenter wp-image-17927" height="748" src="http://birthwithoutfearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Expression-Cannon-SL1.jpg" width="748"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Potatoes, Cannon SL1" class="aligncenter wp-image-17925" height="494" src="http://birthwithoutfearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Potatoes-Cannon-SL1-e1400979870361.jpg" width="745"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         After just a few hours with this camera in my hand, Ive realized how many family memories and fun, sweet moments I have not been capturing and I am so glad I can now change that.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jumping, Canon SL1" class="aligncenter wp-image-17929" height="500" src="http://birthwithoutfearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Jumping-Canon-SL1.jpg" width="748"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         These eyes are seeing the world through this amazing piece of equipment, including myself. Beautiful imperfections.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Imperfections, January Harshe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17930" height="500" src="http://birthwithoutfearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Imperfections.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         To be continued
        </p>
        <p>
         *This post sponsored in part by Canon Canada. All pictures taken with a Canon Rebel SL1 and Canon 50 mm lens and opinions are my own.
        </p>
