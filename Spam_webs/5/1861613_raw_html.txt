       <p>
        Hi everyone! First off, I'd just like to thank for even taking the time to read this and find out more about this project, because that in itself means a lot to me. Edventure is an educational and leadership program that allows students to travel to rural parts of the world and help make a difference. The Thailand program, which is what this kickstarter is about, is the program that I hope to be able to attend. I chose this program specifically because of the conservation work with the elephants, and it would be the greatest experience to be able to work hands on with them. One of my greatest passions is to help save animals and I have grown an extreme love for elephants. I plan on working them later on in my career and I hope to one day own an elephant sanctuary like the ones in Tennessee and Maine. Elephants are an endangered species that need to be protected and my lifelong goal is to do that, and Edventure gives me that opportunity. I plan on attending this program in July of 2016.
       </p>
       <p>
        Edventure Thailand is a 21 day program in which we (the participants) are given the privilege to perform conservation and humanitarian work in rural villages in parts of the country.
       </p>
       <p>
        The first week, we will be living and working on an elephant farm. We will be feeding, bathing, and performing daily activities with elephants. Many of these elephants have suffered abuse from circuses, zoos, and logging. We will assist in rehabilitating them and be their companions throughout this entire week.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img src="https://images.indiegogo.com/file_attachments/875252/files/20140922172600-329801_353026164782413_540662938_o.jpg?1411431960" style="max-width:620px;"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        During the second week, we will be working very closely in a village in Thailand. We will be working in schools and teaching children English and other subjects, enabling them to have future job opportunities in and possibly outside of the country. English has become an essential language in Thailand due to the high volume of tourists in recent years. Education is very important, and these children don't have access to education in a way that we do in the United States. Many children have to walk several kilometers to get to school and during rainy seasons, they are often unable to get to school. Giving these children the education they so rightly deserve gives them the chance to further their education and purse other dreams.
       </div>
