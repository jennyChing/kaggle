      <p class="summary">
       This is a short poem I wrote about the older generation after having seen my grandmother suffer. They maybe old but they're still human.
      </p>
      <br/>
      <div class="chapter_contents">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Submitted:
        </strong>

        <strong>
         Reads:

       <br/>
       <div class="content" id="mainContent">
        <div class="postingContent">
         <p>
          <span>
           <span style="font-size:16px;">
            Faded
  and hollow is all people see,
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A shadow of what I used to
  be.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A mind that knows not of things
  that have gone,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A light that's gone out after it
  brightly shone.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A burden that's carried is all that
  they'll see,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A faded old template of what used
  to be me.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A mind scattered with memories now
  struggles for a name,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           A feeling that each day is always
  the same.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           Behind the lines that now litter a
  once perfect face,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           Is a smile that sits wistful with
  humility and grace.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           When stood by that mirror what do I
  see?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           Is that flicker of what I remember
  was me.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           An a pair of worn out tired
  eyes,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           Glisten back like velvet
  skies.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           An although it's with frailty I
  carry myself,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           I still have dignity if not my own
  health.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           Remember my plight for one day it's
  your own,
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size:16px;">
           For when you've gotten older and
  your life's been outgrown.
          </span>
         </p>
