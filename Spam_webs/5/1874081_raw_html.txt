         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Architectural CAD / BIM Training Courses
         </h1>
         <p>
          Suitable for Architects, Interior Designers, Civil Engineers, Construction Professionals as well Civil &amp; AutoCAD Drafting Professionals, our Architectural CAD &amp; BIM Training Courses include
          <b>
           AutoCAD
          </b>
          Training Courses,
          <b>
           Revit
          </b>
          <b>
           Architecture
          </b>
          Training Courses,
          <b>
           3D Studio Max &amp; VRay
          </b>
          Training Courses as well as comprehensive
          <b>
           Architectural BIM
          </b>
          Training Programs. Our architectural courses provide candidates with software knowledge and skills that are aligned to best architectural practices in the world, thus helping them get ahead of the competition.
         </p>
         <h2>
          AutoCAD Training Courses
         </h2>
         <p>
          We provide comprehensive AutoCAD Training Courses for beginner, intermediate and advanced users alike. In the basic courses, users get to know the commands of the software and are introduced to design development, construction documents and other design tools. Candidates going for advanced courses get to know added functions and customisation of object styles and features. Our architectural training courses are delivered by trainers who have practical experience of architectural drafting and modelling for actual projects.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Revit Architecture Training Courses
         </h2>
         <p>
          We offer Revit Architecture Training courses to users of all levels.i.e from beginners to experts. For novices, we have a basic Revit Architecture Training course that will familiarise users with essential tools and enable them to create full 3D architectural project models and set them up as working drawings. For users with a basic knowledge of Revit Architecture, we have Advanced Revit Architectural course that covers the advanced features of Revit Architecture as well as the advanced detailing and documentation techniques.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Architectural BIM / Internship Training Program
         </h2>
         <p>
          Gain valuable expertise as well as get invaluable work experience by enrolling in our Certified Professional in Architectural BIM (Building Information Modelling) Technologies training program. This course features an intensive 3 month classroom training program covering the entire 2D / 3D / BIM Workflow process as well as a 2 month paid Internship to gain valuable experience. For more information, please
          <b>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <br/>
            <a href="http://www.xscadtraining.com/bim-training-courses/">
             click here
            </a>
           </span>
          </b>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          Architectural CAD Technologies / Internship Training Program
         </h2>
         <p>
          Gain valuable expertise as well as get invaluable work experience by enrolling in our Certified Professional in Architectural Computer Aided Design Technologies training program. This course features an intensive 3 month classroom training program covering the entire Design Development, Documentation &amp; Presentation Workflow process. For more information, please
          <b>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <a href="http://www.xscadtraining.com/bim-training-courses/">
             click here
            </a>
           </span>
          </b>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          3D Visualization &amp; Rendering
         </h2>
         <p>
          XS CAD provides some of the best construction-related 3D rendering solutions in India. Our courses use this experience and prepare our students for rendering to meet global quality standards. These cover aspects including lighting, scene setting, interior design and landscape design, camera movement, and animation. Our courses include training on software such as
          <b>
           3D Studio Max, VRay
          </b>
          (for advanced and photorealistic rendering)
          <b>
           &amp; Adobe Photoshop.
          </b>
         </p>
