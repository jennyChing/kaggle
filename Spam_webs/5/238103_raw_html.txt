         <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.familyfreshmeals.com%2F2012%2F08%2Fquick-easy-mini-meatloaf-cupcakes-with.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F2.bp.blogspot.com%2F-LHZ6D3qND50%2FUDQpPSSLUgI%2FAAAAAAAAEVo%2FvFZCpiDxHek%2Fs640%2Fimg_2476b.jpg&amp;description=Quick%20%26%20Easy%20Mini%20Meatloaf%20Cupcakes%20(with%20vegetarian%20option)">
          <img alt="" border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png?a6eb9b" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <!--oldmore-->
        </div>
        <p>
         <a name="more">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          My wonderful neighbor Megan was telling me about these
          <em>
           little meatloaf
          </em>
          cupcakes she would have as a kid. As soon as she told me the story about them, I
          <strong>
           knew
          </strong>
          what I was
          <a href="http://www.familyfreshmeals.com/recipes">
           making for dinner
          </a>
          that night.
         </span>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
         </span>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          The original idea came from a Kraft recipe I got a while back, but I had to switch things up a bit to make a
          <em>
           <a href="http://www.familyfreshmeals.com/category/vegetarianvegetarian-option">
            vegetarian friendly
           </a>
          </em>
          version for my hubby.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          That being said, the
          <em>
           <strong>
            pictures are of the vegetarian version using
           </strong>
          </em>
          <strong style="line-height: 13.183333396911621px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">
           <em style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">
            MorningStar Farms Meal Starters Grillers Recipe Crumbles
           </em>
          </strong>
          , but I give directions for both
          <strong>
           meat
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           meatless
          </strong>
          versions.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          So yes, your meat will look different.
          <strong>
           For you real meat folks out there.you do use raw ground beef
          </strong>
          . Feel free to use
          <strong>
           <em>
            your favorite meatloaf
           </em>
          </strong>
          recipe.this is just a
          <em>
           quick and easy
          </em>
          version you can
          <a href="http://www.familyfreshmeals.com/category/crockpot">
           whip up in no time
          </a>
          !
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          Here is what you needFor 12 individual meatloaves:
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-0PBrOYJ5HNM/UDQuxIL25DI/AAAAAAAAEWY/peL5hGKVeGE/s1600/img_2436a.jpg" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <span style="font-size: x-large;">
           <img alt="" border="0" class="alignright" height="225" src="//1.bp.blogspot.com/-0PBrOYJ5HNM/UDQuxIL25DI/AAAAAAAAEWY/peL5hGKVeGE/s400/img_2436a.jpg?a6eb9b" width="312"/>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          -1 lb. extra-lean ground beef (
          <em>
           or vegetarian crumble as pictured
          </em>
          )
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           1 package of stuffing mix
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           1 egg
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           1/2 cup of bbq sauce (divided)
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           1/2 cheddar cheese or cheese blend
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           1 tsp garlic powder
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           1 cup of water
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           salt and pepper
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          For optional mashed potato topping
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
           3 cups of prepared mashed potatoes and1/4 cup of frozen peas.
         </span>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: large;">
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.familyfreshmeals.com%2F2012%2F08%2Fquick-easy-mini-meatloaf-cupcakes-with.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F1.bp.blogspot.com%2F-JgQDq0XrHig%2FUDQzskxsT_I%2FAAAAAAAAEW4%2F56YvpUYUjXc%2Fs640%2FPicMonkey%2BCollagemini2.png&amp;description=Quick%20%26%20Easy%20Mini%20Meatloaf%20Cupcakes%20(with%20vegetarian%20option)">
          <img alt="" border="0" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png?a6eb9b" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-JgQDq0XrHig/UDQzskxsT_I/AAAAAAAAEW4/56YvpUYUjXc/s1600/PicMonkey+Collagemini2.png" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="//1.bp.blogspot.com/-JgQDq0XrHig/UDQzskxsT_I/AAAAAAAAEW4/56YvpUYUjXc/s640/PicMonkey+Collagemini2.png?a6eb9b" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Season beef to taste with salt and pepper. Mix beef (or veggie crumble), stuffing mix, egg, garlic powder, 1/4 cup of bbq sauce, and water in a large bowl.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         2. Spray muffin tin with non-stick cooking spray. Divide mixture into 12 muffin tin cups and use your finger to make anindentationin each muffin.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         3. Fill the indents evenly with the remaining 1/4 cup of bbq sauce.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         4. Bake for about 25-30 minutes (or for vegetarian version, for 20 minutes.) Top each evenly with shredded cheese and bake for 5 more minutes.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         5 Let cool for 5 minutes. This step is for those who are doing the optional mashed potato topping. I used a ziplock bag with a cut off tip to frost the meatloaves.Simply fill bag with mashed potatoes, cut off corner of the bag and frost
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-EUTnb0_Jta8/UDQ1r6Yg0DI/AAAAAAAAEXA/e7PgI3-jjSE/s1600/img_2486a.jpg" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="144" src="//3.bp.blogspot.com/-EUTnb0_Jta8/UDQ1r6Yg0DI/AAAAAAAAEXA/e7PgI3-jjSE/s200/img_2486a.jpg?a6eb9b" width="200"/>
         </a>
         Top with peas and serve right away!
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         My family sure loved these little bundles of goodness! Hope you enjoy too! XO Corey
        </div>

          <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png?a6eb9b" title="Pin It"/>

            1. When using the veggie crumblers, do you thaw them before using?
            <br/>
            2. I find that stuffing mix often has meat in it (dry broth of some sort), I have usually found other stuffing mix that omits meat but find that the mixture is very thick and very moist when adding all the other ingredients. I can typically make about a half of a muffin tin. I assume that I would just match the dry amounts when substituting? Your thoughts?
