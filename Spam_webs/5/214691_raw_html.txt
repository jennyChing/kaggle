         <div class="post-content description " itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           In 2010 Switzerlands Lake Lucerne region got a newly renovated 5 star boutique hotel, the Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort in Vitznau. It is located right on the shores of the lake and via its garden it offers direct access to the lake and a stunning view of the Swiss alps.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-2650">
           </span>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Boutique Hotel Feeling on Lake Lucerne, Switzerland
          </h2>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div>
           <img alt="Light-Reflexion in Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Reception" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-2695" data-lazy-src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Light-Reflexion-Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Reception-200x132.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="132" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" title="Light-Reflexion in Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Reception" width="200"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Light-Reflexion in Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Reception" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-2695" height="132" src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Light-Reflexion-Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Reception-200x132.jpg" title="Light-Reflexion in Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Reception" width="200"/>
           </noscript>
          </div>
          <p>
           From the moment you enter the
           <a href="http://www.vitznauerhof.ch" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Hotel Vitznauerhof
           </a>
           you will feel a pampering boutique ambiance. The lobby and especially the bar feature stylish furnishing and arts decoration with perfectly blending colours. The staff is very attentive, friendly and helpful.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <img alt="Lounge and Reception at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort, Lucerne, Switzerland" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignnone size-large wp-image-2709" data-lazy-src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Lounge-Reception-Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Lucerne-Switzerland.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" title="Lounge and Reception at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort, Lucerne, Switzerland"/>
          </p>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Lounge and Reception at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort, Lucerne, Switzerland" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-2709" src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Lounge-Reception-Hotel-Vitznauerhof-Lucerne-Switzerland.jpg" title="Lounge and Reception at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort, Lucerne, Switzerland"/>
          </noscript>
          <br/>
          <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2697" style="width: 450px">
           <img alt="Katja relaxing at the Bar at Vitznauerhof" class="lazy lazy-hidden size-large wp-image-2697" data-lazy-src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Katja-Hotel-Bar-Vitznauerhof-Lucerne.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" title="Katja relaxing at the Bar at Vitznauerhof"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Katja relaxing at the Bar at Vitznauerhof" class="size-large wp-image-2697" src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Katja-Hotel-Bar-Vitznauerhof-Lucerne.jpg" title="Katja relaxing at the Bar at Vitznauerhof"/>
           </noscript>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Katja relaxing at the Bar at Vitznauerhof
           </p>
          </div>
          <h2>
           Hotel Room and Suites
          </h2>
          <p>
           The superior lake view rooms are convenient and modern and the bathroom is generous. Especially the large shower. WLAN is speedy and complementary in all rooms.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2674" style="width: 450px">
           <img alt="Double Room at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort" class="lazy lazy-hidden size-large wp-image-2674" data-lazy-src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Vitznauerhof-Double-Room.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" title="Double Room at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Double Room at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort" class="size-large wp-image-2674" src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Vitznauerhof-Double-Room.jpg" title="Double Room at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort"/>
           </noscript>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Double Room at Hotel Vitznauerhof Vitalresort
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Several suites with spectacular lake views are available as well.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Breakfast &amp; Dining by the Lake Lucerne
          </h2>
          <p>
           One of the restaurants at the Vitznauerhof is the Inspiration. It serves food made only from fresh, seasonal and regional products. We had a delicious five course dinner served by very friendly and humorous staff. Every course is prepared with accuracy and composed very creatively. I liked my dinners cognac sauce finish a lot!
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Inspiration-Restaurant-Vitznauerhof-Vitalresort-Lucerne-Switzerland" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignnone size-large wp-image-2711" data-lazy-src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Inspiration-Restaurant-Vitznauerhof-Vitalresort-Lucerne-Switzerland.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" title="Inspiration-Restaurant-Vitznauerhof-Vitalresort-Lucerne-Switzerland"/>
          </p>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Inspiration-Restaurant-Vitznauerhof-Vitalresort-Lucerne-Switzerland" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-2711" src="http://travelmemo.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/mimo/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Inspiration-Restaurant-Vitznauerhof-Vitalresort-Lucerne-Switzerland.jpg" title="Inspiration-Restaurant-Vitznauerhof-Vitalresort-Lucerne-Switzerland"/>
          </noscript>
          <p>
           The available breakfast variety seems more healthy than advisable. I choose vital and energy and ran strongly forever. It thought.
