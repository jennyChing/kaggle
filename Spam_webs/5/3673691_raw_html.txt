         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Baby Tattooville Inside Out: A Private Affair Made Public at the Riverside Art Museum
         </h1>
        </div>
        <div class="authorbox2">
         by Nastia Voynovskaya
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://hifructose.com/2012/10/09/baby-tattooville-inside-out-a-private-affair-made-public-at-the-riverside-art-museum/" rel="bookmark" title="9:48 pm">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2012-10-09T21:48:07+00:00">
           October 9, 2012
          </time>
         </a>
        </div>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content" id="single">
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i1.wp.com/farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8072093681_07d0b91515_z.jpg?resize=584%2C438"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Brandt Peters
        </p>
        <p>
         In conjunction with the VIP art retreat
         <a href="http://babytattooville.wordpress.com/" target="_blank">
          Baby Tattooville
         </a>
         at the historical Mission Inn last weekend, the
         <a href="http://www.riversideartmuseum.org/current-exhibitions/baby-tattooville-inside-out-a-private-affair-made-public/" target="_blank">
          Riverside Art Museum
         </a>
         recently premiered the exhibit Baby Tattooville Inside Out: A Private Affair Made Public. For the annual Baby Tattooville, a small group of popular contemporary artists spend a weekend with a handful of collectors discussing art, creating original works and participating in special panels and events. This years participating artists included Brandt Peters, Kathie Olivas, Brandi Milne, RISK, Nathan Ota, Nouar, Rob Reger, Ragnar, Coop and Mark Frauenfelder. For art lovers who couldnt attend the exclusive event, the Riverside Art Museums related group show runs until December 14.
        </p>
