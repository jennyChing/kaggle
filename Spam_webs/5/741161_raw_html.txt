      <h2 class="article-title">
       The Best of2014
       <span class="article-meta">
        <span class="article-date">
         December 31, 2014
        </span>
        <span class="article-category">
         <a href="http://jetsetfam.com/category/galavanting-2/" rel="category tag">
          Escapades

      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       2014 was not bad whatsoever. In fact, it was the first yearin a long time that I felt was kind to us. Not fantabulously flawless, butit certainly did not deliver the sucker punches like previous years. This is the first year Im not trying to own the new year. Start fresh. Im just going to purge a bit here and there, change a few things and hope that 2014 sorta just blends seamlessly into 2015.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       As I began to tearsheets out of magazines for this years annual vision board, I became reminiscent of everything I enjoyed in 2014. I thought it would be a great time-capsule type of blog post that would document our year-in-review. This was a big year for The JetSet Family. We continuouslyset goals, raised the bar and broke records. And it was all made possible thanks to our partners, sponsors, contributors, web guru, and YOU!
