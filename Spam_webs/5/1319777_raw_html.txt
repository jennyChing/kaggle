        <h1 class="entry-title">
         What Are the Benefits of Drinking Black Tea
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Black Tea-juthan" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-183" height="300" src="http://www.juthan.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Black-Tea-juthan-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         Black tea and green tea both are produced from camellia sinesis but the main difference between them is in the processing. Both are processed in different manner. Black tea is more oxidized which is the reason it contains huge amount of caffeine and loaded with aroma. The flavor is long lasting and if you retain the tea for more than a year, the flavor still exists. The methods of black tea production involve first plucking the tea leaves and then they are dried to remove all the moistures from it. The leaves are then rolled with machines or manually in the exposure of high temperature. When the leaves are oxidized fully they are sorted according to the sizes. Black tea is able to deliver so many health benefits and for this reason doctors recommend to drink black tea without adding milk or sugar in it. Know here the various health benefits of drinking black tea.
        </p>
        <ol style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Lowers cardiovascular problems
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Cardiovascular disease is strongly related with the consumption of black tea. This relation has also been proved in a study conducted at Boston University in 2001. According to that study black tea is able to slow down the endothelial vasomotor dysfunction in patients who all are suffering from coronary artery disease. Endothelial vasomotor dysfunction is that which stimulate the coronary artery diseases and make the situation uncontrollable. But regular drinking of black tea can stop the dysfunction of that endothelial vasomotor.
        </p>
        <ol start="2" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Body gets huge anti oxidants
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Polyphenol is the anti-oxidants that are found in black tea. This anti oxidant is useful to control various types of severe diseases such as brain disease, hearing loss, Parkinsons disease etc. So if you plan to drink black tea daily then you can protect yourself from various diseases.
        </p>
        <ol start="3" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Releases stress
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         All types of tea black tea, green tea and oolong tea are well known for their stress releasing trait. Black tea cant reduce the stress rather it helps you to come back in the normal level by regulating the stress hormone cortisol. High stress gives rise to heart attack also. So it is important to reduce the stress after a hectic work schedule which black tea is able to do.
        </p>
        <ol start="4" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Strengthens bones
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         BMD refers to bones mineral density. Lower BMD increases the risk of bone fractures and osteoporosis while higher BMD promotes healthy bones. Bones tend to loss its density with ages and they get fractured very easily. But if you continue drinking black tea for years, it helps to maintain a proper BMD and strengthen the bones. This thing has already been proved by a study.
        </p>
        <ol start="5" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Prevents tooth decay, plaque and cavities
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Antioxidants in black tea are very useful that prevent the development of cavities bacteria in mouth, stop the formation of plaque and prevent tooth decay. Thus black tea can make the tooth healthy and protect from any kind of infections.
        </p>
        <ol start="6" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Reduces muscle soreness
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Theaflavin is a powerful antioxidant which is found in the black tea. This anti-oxidant can remove the muscle soreness very quickly. So if you do lots of heavy exercises then try to consume black tea on regular basis to get rid of the frequent muscle soreness.
        </p>
        <ol start="7" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Promotes healthy heart
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Black tea can help you to gain a healthy heart. A research in 2009 called as Green and black tea consumption and the rate of stroke-a meta analysis revealed that people who consume an average of 3 cups of black tea daily or more than that have 21% less chances of being attacked by stroke while others who consume only 1 cup black tea per day have more chances of stroke.
        </p>
        <ol start="8" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Prevents cancer
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Catechins and polyphenol are the two important and powerful antioxidants that can prevent many types of cancer. A study has said that women who drink black tea regularly have less chances of developing ovarian cancer than others.
        </p>
        <ol start="9" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Lessens the risk of diabetes
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Type-2 diabetes can be well controlled by the regular consumption of black tea. Black tea contains rich antioxidants that maintain the blood sugar level and insulin in blood. The antioxidants try to reduce the level of blood sugar by its powerful properties and thus lower the risk of type-2 diabetes.
        </p>
        <ol start="10" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Promotes healthy immune system
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Black tea has alkylamine antigens in it which improves the immune system in our body. Also there is one more compound called as tannins found in black tea that fights against various viruses such as stomach flu, influenza and keep us protected from them.
        </p>
        <ol start="11" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Helps in digestion
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Tannins presented in black tea are also able to promote a better digestive system. These tannins have great effect on controlling the gastric and intestinal illness and thus maintain a good digestive system.
        </p>
        <ol start="12" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Provides energy
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         If you are feeling down and low in energy, drinking one cup of black tea can give you quick energy to start with the schedule again. Proper amount of caffeine in black tea enhances the blood flow in the brain but does not over stimulate it so that it cannot create heart problem.
        </p>
        <ol start="13" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Fights against the free radicals
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Our body is full of free radicals and all should be removed properly from body otherwise that can create cancer, various brain diseases and they also damage the DNA in our body. Our body is made to remove the free radicals but not 100%. Black tea is very effective to absorb that free oxygen radical quickly from our body.
        </p>
        <ol start="14" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Provides hydration
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Yes black tea is able to provide hydration so drink more black tea for avoiding dehydration and stay safe.
        </p>
        <ol start="15" style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <strong>
           Recovers from radiation
          </strong>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Black tea is able to help our body to recover from radiation. Tea can stop the cellular degeneration which is caused due to the exposure of radiation.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         So the benefits of drinking black tea are numerous and it is observable that who all have the habit of drinking black tea regularly are very safe and protected from various severe diseases.
        </p>
