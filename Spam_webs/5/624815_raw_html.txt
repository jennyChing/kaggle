          <p class="news_title">
           Path of Exile 1.2 Rangers Passive Tree Tips for Physical Damage/ Elemental Damage / Critical Bow
          </p>
          <p class="tagdata">
           <span class="new_tag">
           </span>
           <span class="new_data">
            09/03/2014
           </span>
          </p>
          <div class="new_txton">
           <p>
            <font color="#ffffff">
             With the update of 1.2 passive tree in Forsaken Masters, many passive tree points have been changed. In the 1.2 passive tree, you may see some added critical rate&amp; elemental resistance as well as MP. Pick up what you want. When you reach level 50, you can utilize elemental bow. But because of GMP, your relative damage will be much lowers. Therefore, in the initial stage, I suggest you can use
            </font>
            <font color="#000000" style="BACKGROUND-COLOR: #ffff80">
             <strong>
              Fork + Chain + other gems.
             </strong>
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#ffffff">
             <font color="#8000ff">
              <strong>
               Main skills suggestion
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </font>
             1. Single Target:
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               Frenzy
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </font>
             2. AOE skill:
            </font>
            <font color="#ffff80">
             <strong>
              Lighting Arrow / Ice Shot
             </strong>
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#ffffff">
             1.
             <strong>
              <font color="#8000ff">
               Single Target:
              </font>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <font color="#ffff80">
               Frenzy + Add projectile physical damage + faster attacks + power charge on critical strike + empower charge
               <br/>
              </font>
              <font color="#8000ff">
               Reason:
              </font>
             </strong>
             When you attack foes with frenzy, you will get a frenzy charge. With the charges in passive tree, I will obtain 7. The attacking speed is quite fast. This is a primary choice for boss-killing.
             <br/>
             Power charge on critical strike is based on the 1 power charge +2.5% critical strike rate. My passive tree will grant me with 4 charges. The critical strike damage is quite huge.
             <br/>
             2.
             <strong>
              <font color="#8000ff">
               AOE skills
              </font>
             </strong>
             :
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               lighting arrow / ice shot + faster attacks + LMP + weapon elemental damage + blood magic
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </font>
             Reason:LMP will make you with 2 more arrows. Weapon elemental damage will be enhanced greatly due to auras. Blood rage: MP will be lowed because of auras.
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#ffffff">
             <strong>
              <font color="#8000ff">
               What attributes that Ranger lacks?
               <br/>
              </font>
             </strong>
             The answer definitely is
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               elemental resistance, HP/MP, defense and a good main-handed weapon
              </strong>
             </font>
             .
             <br/>
             Therefore, I want to give you a few suggestions to build 1.2 rangers bow build.
             <br/>
             1. Utilize at least
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               3 auras
              </strong>
             </font>
             ! At the same time, equipment with resistance will be much better, because in the third difficulty, your resistance will be reduced much. Therefore, pile up the resistance to at least 70.
             <br/>
             2. Pick up equipment with high
             <strong>
              <font color="#ffff80">
               HP
              </font>
             </strong>
             . In the passive tree, pick up blood points as many as possible. Cruel-2000HP, Merciless-3000HP.
             <br/>
             3. Do not use the gems named
             <strong>
              <font color="#ffff80">
               lighting arrow
              </font>
             </strong>
             or
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               ice shot
              </strong>
             </font>
             before level 70. Use
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               Fork + pierce + faster attacks + LMP + added physical damage
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </font>
             4. As we all know that Rangers lack of defense. Then you can choose equipment with high defense or high evasion to make up the shortcoming. Beside, there is a passive tree point named
             <font color="#ffff80">
              <strong>
               Acrobatics
              </strong>
             </font>
             which is good to use in the initial stage.
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#ffffff">
             <img alt="Path of Exile 1.2 Rangers Passive Tree Tips for Physical Damage/ Elemental Damage / Critical Bow" src="http://www.pathofexileitems.us/images/news/acrobatics.JPG"/>
             <br/>
             40%+30% spell evasion is quite OP.
             <br/>
             Besides,
             <strong>
              <font color="#ffff80">
               Ondar's Guile
              </font>
             </strong>
             is also quite fit for ranger. You will have double chance to dodge the projectile attacks.
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Path of Exile 1.2 Rangers Passive Tree Tips for Physical Damage/ Elemental Damage / Critical Bow" src="http://www.pathofexileitems.us/images/news/Ondars%20Guile.JPG"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#ffffff">
             5. Buy a bow with
             <a href="http://www.pathofexileitems.us/currency">
              pathofexile currency
             </a>
             . The damage will be 9x-25x and the attacking speed is 1.4. The cost will not be expensive. The attacking speed of the weapon is the most important.
             <br/>
            </font>
           </p>
