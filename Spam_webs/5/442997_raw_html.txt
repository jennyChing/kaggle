           <!-- &lt;a class="blog" href="https://www.joomplace.com/blog/html5-flipping-book-helping-to-make-mediterranean-within-reach.html"&gt;HTML5 Flipping Book Helping to Make Mediterranean Within Reach&lt;/a&gt; -->
           HTML5 Flipping Book Helping to Make Mediterranean Within Reach
          </h1>
          <small class="jb-category-bgk">
           Category:
           <a href="/blog/category/joomplace.html">
            JoomPlace
           </a>
          </small>
          <!-- Post Content Text -->
          <div class="postContent">
           <div class="jb-post-content clearfix">
            <div class="jb-article-image-details">
             <img alt="" src="/blog/media/com_joomblog/0/html5_joomla_flipping_book.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <em>
              This post is a part of the
              <a href="http://www.joomplace.com/blog/success-stories.html" target="_blank">
               Success Stories Series
              </a>
              , featuring tips on how to use JoomPlace products and services. Today we've decided to show you an example of
              <a href="http://www.joomplace.com/joomla-components/html5-flipping-book.html" target="_blank">
               Html5 Flipping Book
              </a>
              implementation. Since the day of release, that happened 2 months ago, the component has been successfully installed on more than 100 websites all over the world. And here is an example from Croatia  Marina&amp;Shipyard Punat.
             </em>
            </p>
            <a name="readmore">
            </a>
            <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
            </h4>
            <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
             Mediterranean within reach
            </h4>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             While developing extensions we are always thinking about types of Joomla! websites where the components can be used and how they can be used. And it makes us proud when we see great examples of components' implementations.
             <a href="http://www.marina-punat.hr/index.php?option=com_html5flippingbook" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              Marinara &amp; Punat
             </a>
             is one of such great illustrations.
             <br/>
             Visiting Marina &amp; Punat website you plunge into Mediterranean life and start dreaming of spending your next vacations not far from the coast of the Mediterranean Sea: numerous beaches, rich history, unique culinary experience and much more. Even the companys slogan 
             <strong>
              Mediterranean within reach. Relax and explore with us!
             </strong>
              eggs you on packing your bags!
            </p>
            <h4>
             HTML5 Flipping Book implementation
            </h4>
            <p>
             HTML5 Flipping Book extension is used for presenting Companys news as digital magazines. Before installing HTML5 Flipping Book component Marinara News publications were presented in the form of PDF files.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="marinara news" src="http://www.joomplace.com/blog/media/com_joomblog/0/Marinara_pdf.png" style="width: 600px; height: 532px;"/>
             <br/>
             With Flipping book extension the publications turned into real magazines with flipping pages, colorful images and informative articles. The publication opens in a new tab (there are 2 more display modes available) against the background of the Mediterranean Sea view (the interface is customizable).
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="joomla flipping book" src="http://www.joomplace.com/blog/media/com_joomblog/0/Joomla_flipping_book.png" style="width: 600px; height: 401px;"/>
             <br/>
             A website visitor can easily read Marina news on the go, using any of his mobile devices, and share the magazine with his friends using social media buttons.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="flipping book mobile" src="http://www.joomplace.com/blog/media/com_joomblog/0/Flipping_book_mobile.png" style="width: 600px; height: 786px;"/>
             <br/>
             Do you have your own example of HtML5 Flipping Book implementation? Share it in the comments below.
             <br/>
             Are you interested in having your website reviewed?
             <a href="http://www.joomplace.com/support/helpdesk/department/marketing.html" target="_blank">
              Submit a ticket
             </a>
             for Marketing department.
             <br/>
             Lets share your experience with others!
            </p>
