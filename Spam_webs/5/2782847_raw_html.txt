         </h1>

         <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           Pinterest
          </strong>
          is one of the major trending social media concepts in recent times. It was tactical on developers path to release it at the exact point where people want a change from social media like Facebook, Twitter, Google + since all of these were just mostly text based. Although all of them supported images, videos etc they werent explicitly enough for pictures sharing. However Instagram and Pinterest were quite different and very useful.
          <span id="more-5245">
          </span>
          This actually helps users to get a clear view of pictures first and then move on to the concept. Instagram is apparently more in to pictures and casual while Pinterest is the professional solution you need to take up.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Using Pinterest asa social media source for your organizations promotion and sharing is simply awesome.
          <em>
           It is not only convenient but also
           <em>
            profitable
           </em>
           .
          </em>
          If you are wondering how Pinterest will help you to advance your organization then here are the two methods on how to use it efficiently in order to get advancements by preferring it.
         </p>
         <ol style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Are you very much interested in getting more traffic driven to your website?
            </strong>
           </em>
           Then all you should do is to consider using Pinterest. The true fact is that Pinterest drives more referral traffic than LinkedIn, Google+ and YouTube combined together. In order to get proper benefit from this, the Pinterest users must first need to be attracted to your pins very much and they must re pin them on to their personal boards. The highly re-pinned and successful pins on Pinterest always share common traits and characteristics in common between them. They generally combine great images along with clear content to solve a particular problem or to inspire and offer something enviable and appealing to the interest in an activity.
          </li>
          <li>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Use Group Boards:
            </strong>
           </em>
           You can use group boards and collaborate with the other popular Pinners who already have a huge fan following base. This will help to expose your brand virally and thus your content reaches more people easily. By this method your followers will be able to see what your contributors pin and vice versa. And what they pin will directly reflect its impact on your business. This is all about so called simple trust between parties which is very important. The collaborative partnership taken should generally be beneficial to both the parties. This method with cautious thoughts will be a great and excellent way for getting in front of a much more large audience.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          If you find it too tiring with all the
          <a href="http://www.apptha.com/blog/category/social-media/" title="social media">
           social media
          </a>
          handling then you may have Pinterest like extension attached to your website. This way the users will be able to share video, images etc along with content without any fuzz or haziness.
          <strong>
           Joomla
          </strong>
          offers all the website users with a Pinterest like extension. This will provide a special Social Pinboard on your page and this is completely customizable and elegant.
