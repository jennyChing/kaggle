                         Two small things:
                         <div>
                          <br/>
                         </div>
                         <div>
                          Firstly, I've meant to post for a while that if you are in the US (or have figured out a way to give a computer the impression you're in the US) you can watch the whole of
                          <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-style: italic;">
                           The Prisoner
                          </span>
                          , the Patrick McGoohan series that was such a part of my mental landscape growing up, legitimately online at
                          <a href="http://www.amctv.com/originals/the-prisoner-1960s-series/">
                           http://www.amctv.com/originals/the-prisoner-1960s-series/
                          </a>
                          . I think it's great that they've put it up. As a teenager, before videos, I collected all three of the
                          <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-style: italic;">
                           Prisoner
                          </span>
                          novels, just to try and get back to the Village, and was unsatisfied by all of them. Finally got to watch them again on Channel 4 in the mid 80s, and they were just as good, and as odd, as I had remembered.
                         </div>
                         <div>
                          <br/>
                         </div>
                         <div>
                          And secondly, Miss Maddy is interviewed by The Fabulous Lorraine, over at Lorraine's blog. Well, it made me smile.
                          <a href="http://lorraineamalena.blogspot.com/2009/02/interview-with-maddy-gaiman.html">
                           http://lorraineamalena.blogspot.com/2009/02/interview-with-maddy-gaiman.html
                          </a>
                         </div>
