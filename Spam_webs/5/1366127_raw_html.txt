          <h1 class="title">
           Beef and Barley Stew
          </h1>
          <p class="meta-info">
           Jan 20, 2015
           <span>
            by
           </span>
           <a href="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/author/foodyschmoody/" rel="author" title="Posts by foodyschmoody">
            foodyschmoody
           </a>
          </p>
          <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="17936689" data-link="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/beef-barley-stew/" data-summary="" data-title="Beef and Barley Stew">
          </div>
          <h2>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            Beef and Barley Stew 
            <em>
             A hearty stew featuring beef, mushrooms and barley that was created by a pot roast mishap.
            </em>
           </span>
           <br/>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="Beef and Barley Stew" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2852" height="434" src="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beef-and-barley-stew3-w-watermark.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            Last week I made a pot roast and it was.O.K.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            Thats it, it was okay. It wasnt great and it wasnt terrible. I didnt even get a chance to take photos. I was that unimpressed. The flavor of the sauce was good but it just didnt come together right. It seemed like it made sense and it seemed as though it should be great. I wasnt ready to give up on this recipe yet.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            Fast forward 3 days. I am in the grocery store and stew meat is on sale and the wheels started turning
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Beef and Barley Stew" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2853" height="975" src="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beef-and-barley-stew-with-watermark.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            I knew that I had to try the recipe again but as a stew. I would change up a few things and it would be great!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            <em>
             <strong>
              Spoiler Alert
             </strong>
            </em>
             I was right. It was great!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            I typically load my beef stew with potatoes. But as you know, Ive been trying to lighten things up. Not that this is a light recipe but I figured using
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004T33Y8O/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004T33Y8O&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=VONHT4APB5QJ43BH" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             barley
            </a>
            instead of potatoes has to be a tad bit better.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Beef and Barley Stew" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2855" height="787" src="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beef-and-barley-stew2-w-watermark1.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            I only used about 1 cup of chopped mushrooms because my family doesnt love mushrooms and I figured I was already pushing my limit with sneaking in mushrooms (Dont forget, I had just fed them my
            <a href="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/loaded-vegetable-chicken-ragu/" target="_blank" title="Loaded Vegetable Chicken Ragu">
             Loaded Vegetable Chicken Ragu
            </a>
            that has mushrooms), so you can adjust your amount of mushrooms to suit your taste.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Beef and Barley Stew" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2857" height="434" src="http://foodyschmoodyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beef-and-barley-stew4-w-watermark.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; font-size: 12pt;">
            I know, a lot of you are looking for
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004P2NG0K/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004P2NG0K&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=3CJHOEQXWK3TOEC2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             slow cooker
            </a>
            recipes and Im sorry. I made this on my stove top. Im sure you could make it in a
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004P2NG0K/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004P2NG0K&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=3CJHOEQXWK3TOEC2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             slow cooker
            </a>
            but Im not exactly sure at what point you would add the
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004T33Y8O/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004T33Y8O&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=VONHT4APB5QJ43BH" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             barley
            </a>
            . Maybe Ill experiment with that soon. But for now, just save this for a cold Sunday afternoon when youve got time to let something simmer for a while.
           </span>
          </p>

           <div class="ERSIngredients">
            <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 T cottonseed oil (or canola)
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1.5 lb beef stew meat, cubed
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 T cornstarch
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               tsp salt
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               tsp black pepper
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 sweet onion, diced
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 C mushrooms, roughly chopped
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 garlic cloves, minced
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 T rosemary, freshly chopped
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 bay leaf
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              32 oz beef broth
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 C
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004T33Y8O/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004T33Y8O&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=O6LJNHGP3HUV5TVT" target="_blank">
               pearl barley
              </a>
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              9 oz frozen peas, defrosted
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSInstructions">
            <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place beef cubes in a
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001715PN8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B001715PN8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=NCUAWBMVSOHGDTJO" target="_blank">
               bowl
              </a>
              . Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle cornstarch over the meat and toss to coat.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Heat oil in a
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001AS94XK/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B001AS94XK&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=foscimjuco-20&amp;linkId=EJO3C6Y5VD7KZTOJ" target="_blank">
               large pot
              </a>
              , over high heat. Add meat to pot and brown on all sides (about 5-7 minutes total). Remove meat and set aside.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Return pot to burner and reduce to medium/high. Add onions, mushrooms, garlic and cook just until starting to soften, about 4 minutes. Add rosemary and bay leaf.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Pour in broth, cover and simmer (on low heat) 1 hour. After 1 hour, stir in barley, recover and continue to simmer another 45 minutes. Stir in defrosted peas and cook uncovered 10 minutes.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Serve hot.
             </li>
            </ol>
