      <div class="article-content" style="padding-top: 20px;">
       <p>
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         Kardashian Klass is now open and today's lesson is ALL about YOU.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Its shocking how quickly an intimate encounter transforms into a shark jumping monstrosity. What's even worse is how
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         inflated fame is polluting our minds and diminishing our feminine power.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Lets face it; we are living in a financially obsessed society. It cant be vital to our growth to fixate on what someone else has in their wallet. Shouldnt we be fixated on finding innovative solutions, to stuff our own? Not to mention, when fame is acquired with no skill or intellect, it sends the message to young women that using your back, is far more valuable than using your brain.
       </p>
       <p>
        How empowering is that?
       </p>
       <p>
        Are we ready to wash our rights down the drain? Do we wish to revert back, sit pretty and rest on our laurels? I will be the first to admit that
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         I too was confused by the phenomenon and popularity of the Kardashian brand.
        </span>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Is being easily hypnotized cute?
        </li>
        <li>
         How did this happen to our culture?
        </li>
        <li>
         Is this the new face of girl power?
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        I took it upon myself to dissect the Kardashian brand to see why 20,000,000 young women (and men) are
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         glued to Instagram and Twitter
        </span>
        to watch what they say and what designers theyre jet setting around the world wearing.
       </p>
       <p>
        What you do with inspiration is as important as the inspiration itself.
       </p>
       <p>
        We must realize, these girls are not rags to riches. They were born with silver spoons and dashed around the elite since they were Tweens. A famous father, a famous step-father and a
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         "Platinum Rolodex"
        </span>
        filled with the whos who of 90210, long before they signed the contract with Ryan Seacrest to sashay around Calabasas primping &amp; promoting for E Entertainment!
       </p>
       <p>
        $100 Million Dollars later the obsession has exploded and rivals British Royalty, Bona Fide Movie Stars &amp; Sport Icons.
       </p>
       <p>
        Does it matter?
       </p>
       <p>
        YES!
       </p>
       <p>
        We make &amp; influence 85% of purchasing decisions, so its important that how, and why we spend our money is scrutinized from time to time.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         Furthermore, its important that we pick and choose our role models with a fine tooth comb and emulate actions and crave lifestyles that are inflated with substance!
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        I too have guilty pleasures, but I put them in a pretty little box I like to call, perspective. Sit pretty &amp; ponder this.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-weight:bold;">
         Does it matter what Kim, Khloeor Kourtney are wearing?
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Who theyre dating?
       </p>
       <p>
        Who they marry?
       </p>
       <p>
        How many babies they deliver?
       </p>
       <p>
        AND
       </p>
       <p>
