         <div class="thecontent" itemprop="articleBody">
          <h6>
           NYX Powder Blush in Taupe,7.99. No press samples featured.Full disclosure policy
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/relationship-brands-bloggers-explained/" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </h6>
          <p>
           If Im any kinda gal, Im a slapdash contour kinda gal. I dont think its an area where effort matches reward. You can go full Kim K and paint stripes across your face, but youll probably just look like youve gone full Kim K and painted stripes across your face, you know?
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/NYX-TAUPE-POWDER-BLUSH-REVIEW-SWATCH-2.jpg">
            <img alt="NYX TAUPE POWDER BLUSH REVIEW SWATCH 2" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1968" height="375" src="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/NYX-TAUPE-POWDER-BLUSH-REVIEW-SWATCH-2-1024x768.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I like a blurry wash of colour just under my cheekbone, something that exaggerates the natural shadow of my, eh, cheek pit? Cheek hollow? Cheek nook? Well go with cheek nook. Thats where NYX Powder Blush in Taupe comes in.
           <span id="more-1967">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           I picked it up because its a cult favourite of ghost-faced beauty bloggers. State side, NYX is one of the most chatted about budget brands.
          </p>
          <p>
           Powder Blush in Taupes texture is grand  nothing to write home/on a blog about. Not too dusty or powdery, but not mega creamy. Grand.
          </p>
          <p>
           The selling point is its shade. Taupe is cool and leans a bit grey. It mimics the natural darkness thats cast when your cheek bone protrudes over your cheek nook. Thats a tricky match especially for very pale complexions.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/NYX-TAUPE-POWDER-BLUSH-REVIEW-SWATCH-3.jpg">
            <img alt="NYX Powder Blush in Taupe" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1969" height="375" src="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/NYX-TAUPE-POWDER-BLUSH-REVIEW-SWATCH-3-1024x768.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Theres no warmth, shimmer or glow to tell tales. Its altogether very subtle and forgiving. Im no dab hand with contour, and I really find it hard to feck up with this. Colour me impressed!
          </p>
          <p>
           Heres
           <a href="http://www.vivaadonis.com/2014/06/nyx-in-ireland.html" target="_blank">
            link on Viva Adonis
           </a>
           to all of NYXs Irish stockists, in case you want to give it a whirl too.
          </p>
