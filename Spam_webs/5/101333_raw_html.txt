       <div class="txt step-body">
        Well, a dayarrivedand a rise up the tent. This is a double wall tent. I rise it up or down in 4 minutes.
        <br/>
        Total fabric weight: inner part with bottom 932 gr + outer layer 623 gr.
        <br/>
        Tent rolled for transport : pack islength30 cm x 17 cm.
        <br/>
        Frombeggingof work till finish I spent about 7 days 2 hours a day.
        <br/>
        Price depend on the material but compared with similar commercial tents range 300/500 US D. I got mine for about 90US D.
        <br/>
        It is possible to use zip connection between inner part and outer part to for separate use according warm or cold weather,
        <br/>
        but I sew it fixed to each other.
        <br/>
        I like to be warm. Always. Especially on mountain top.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        This was made for me.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        You can make your own. For you. Good luck!
