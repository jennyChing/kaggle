        <p>
         Summertime and the living is easy.
        </p>
        <p>
         At least when you visit
         <a class="external" href="http://www.mollymoonicecream.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Molly Moons Homemade Ice Cream
         </a>
         in Seattle!
        </p>
        <p>
         This neighborhood ice cream parlor actually has 2 locations  in Wallingford and on Capitol Hill. And both always have a line of people streaming out the door. With good reason. At Molly Moon you can be sure youll find interesting flavors that seem to change every time you visit.
        </p>
        <p>
         A few examples are:
         <br/>

         <br/>
         <li>
          Theo Coconut Kiss
         </li>
         <br/>
         <li>
          Salted Caramel
         </li>
         <br/>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Nmmm Nmmm.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bonus facts about Molly Moon: There are always 2 vegan sorbet flavors available at their locations for you non-dairy types and the company does their best at sourcing locally made products for their ice cream!
