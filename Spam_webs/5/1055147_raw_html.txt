          <p>
           Were taking a quick detour from our regular format for Fashion Friday. Today were rockin a new hair do and Im sure youre wonderingwhat? Why? Honestly thats what everyone asked me. Truth be told  I dont know. Newborns get their heads shaved in Sri Lanka and most parts of South Asia and from what Ive gathered its to ensure that it comes back thicker. Little Monkey had a lot of hair when she was born and then by the three month mark it got very splotchy so the hubs agreed to have it shaved off. With Baby Boy he barely had any hair and a lot of cradle cap so off it went. Traditionally the cut happens by a river so that the hair can wash away I still have Little Monkeys tufts. Should I head to Niagara Falls or Lake Ontario and pollute the stream?

        <h4>
         Yashy

        <p>
         <img src="http://www.babyandlife.com/wp-content/uploads/et_temp/2010-20418_90x90.jpg" style="border: 0pt none; float:left; padding-right:10px; padding-bottom:10px"/>
         Born in Sri Lanka raised in Dubai, Bahrain, Pakistan, U.S, Australia and England, I now find myself settled in Toronto. Passions include wine, dining out, cooking, cocktailing, travelling, hunting for random fun events in the city and now exploring all things baby related. With two kids under the age of three we continue to explore everything that fascinates us! Our goal is to encourage young families to maintain the pre baby life, post baby. Having dined out and traveled to over 33 cities in 8 countries since the first baby was born years ago, we encourage you to travel and enjoy the culinary offerings in your own city!
        </p>

         Chris
        </h4>
        <p>

         When Yashy launched Baby and Life while on Mat Leave in 2011, I never envisioned being able to maintain such an audacious, at times hectic, life outside of our Downtown Toronto haven. As Yashy and I have continued to explore new activities and culinary experiences inside and outside of Toronto, I knew I needed to begin telling our stories from the Dads perspective. Our hope is that you will find this site to be both informative and inspirational, and if you have kids, or plan to, and are concerned that life as you know it ends at parenthood, we are here to tell you why that simply isnt true.
