                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                       Having the snob affect is great but so is saving your money
so dont run to pay the highest you can. Shiraz by Benefactor cellars is a
barebones whine that retails for under $5. If youre buying wine for the price
then you might as well just add it to your collection of overpriced shoes and
jackets. However, if youre buying to try new tastes such as that of South
Eastern Australia put your savings in an interest bearing account and pick up a
bottle.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                       This wine celebrates those who have passed away. It is
readily apparent from the skeleton label design and red coloring that it is
something you should pick up for a Halloween party. Regardless, the wine is
about life and celebration even though it has a unique artistic decor.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">

                       Nose: Ripe berry and chocolate
                      </span>
                     </div>

                     </div>

                       Mouth: Ripe berry, tobacco, cherry, spice and currant.
Slightly pungent and wet.
