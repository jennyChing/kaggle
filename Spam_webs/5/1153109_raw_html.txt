           Hallway Makeover  Final Reveal!

         <p>
          A few words came to mind every time Id walk up our stairs. Ugly. Ewww. Gross. It was brown. As a matter of fact, the entire living room was also painted this color when we moved in. The previous owners had a real thing for brown. Just, no.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Before-Collage.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Before Collage" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-584" height="600" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Before-Collage.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I decided to brighten it up and give it some character. I painted it all bright white and painted the window mullions black. I really LOVE black + white when its done right. My dining room is black and white too. I feel like the walls sort of turn into a neutral. Anyway, back to the hallway.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-Pinterest.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal Pinterest" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-594" height="800" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-Pinterest.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
          <center>
           <!-- Javascript tag: -->
           <!-- begin ZEDO for channel:  houseologie.com , publisher: houseologie.com , Ad Dimension: Medium Rectangle - 300 x 250 -->

         <p>
          The planked wall is probably my favorite thing in the whole hallway. Its imperfect and rustic with a little glam. White planks + brassy nail heads = Awesome. So pretty!!
         </p>
         <p>
          I was never sure what to do with this little built-in alcove in the wall. It used to have a phone jack in it! We dont even have a land line but our house was built in the 1950s. I thought sitting something in it would just get knocked over (three kids and two cats, it happens a lot) so I decided to make a
          <a href="/diy-mini-hanging-planter/" target="_blank" title="DIY Mini Hanging Planter">
           mini hanging planter
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-588" height="791" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-2.jpg" width="593"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-3.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-589" height="800" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-3.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="/picture-frame-makeover-a-diy-fail/" target="_blank" title="Picture Frame Makeover + A DIY Fail">
           picture frames I painted gold
          </a>
          are filled with Van Morrison lyrics from the song These are the Days. It has extra special meaning to my husband and I so I typed the words out and printed them for just a little personal touch.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-7.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal 7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-593" height="800" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-7.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-6.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal 6" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-592" height="800" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-6.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-5.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal 5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-591" height="931" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-5.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I wanted a rug up here but didnt want to spend much money on it. Again, I decided to make my own! You can see the
          <a href="/4-diy-rug-with-tassels/" target="_blank" title="$4 DIY Rug With Tassels">
           DIY Rug with Tassels here
          </a>
          . I also built the blanket ladder for around $5! Its not perfect but itll do just fine
          <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
          Ill post about it next week!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DIY-Rug-with-Tassels-2.jpg">
           <img alt="DIY Rug with Tassels 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-574" height="800" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/DIY-Rug-with-Tassels-2.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres the other end of the hallway where our
          <a href="/from-closet-to-reading-nook/" target="_blank" title="From Closet to Reading Nook">
           Reading Nook
          </a>
          is in one closet and a bedroom and bathroom in the other two doors.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-8.jpg">
           <img alt="Hallway Makeover Reveal 8" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-601" height="800" src="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-8.jpg" width="600"/>

          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hallway-Makeover-Reveal-9.jpg">

          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://houseologie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Macrame-Hanging-Planter.jpg">

         <p>
          Im pretty much in love. Wish my photography skills could do it more justice! Stay tuned next week, Ill have lots more details and tutorials
