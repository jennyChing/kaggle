              <h2 class="entry-title">
               Fashion &amp; Style: Lauren
              </h2>
              <div class="entry-meta">
               <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
                By
               </span>
               <span class="author vcard">
                <a href="https://www.blogger.com/profile/09519038961864718554" rel="author" title="author profile">
                 Jem Lawren - Culture Of Venus
                </a>
               </span>
               <span class="meta-sep">
                |
               </span>
               <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-entry-date">
                Published
               </span>
               <span class="entry-date">
                <a href="http://www.cultureofvenus.com/2013/12/fashion-style-lauren.html" title="2013-12-23T16:15:00-08:00">
                 Monday, December 23, 2013
                </a>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div class="entry-content" id="post-body-8024030892167184544">
               <p>
               </p>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dUWlgypDX_w/UrjPob57eZI/AAAAAAAAA-o/MKWzdSB1po4/s1600/White+Crop+Top%252C+Skater+Skirt.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dUWlgypDX_w/UrjPob57eZI/AAAAAAAAA-o/MKWzdSB1po4/s1600/White+Crop+Top%252C+Skater+Skirt.jpg"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  Introduce yourself!
                 </span>
                </strong>
                <strong>
                 <span lang="EN-GB" style='font-family: "Arial","sans-serif";'>
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span lang="EN-GB" style='font-family: "Arial","sans-serif"; font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;'>
                  Hi! My name is Lauren and I am the creator of the blog and YouTube
channel, A Lovely Side Project, which focuses on my two passions in life,
fashion and nutrition.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  How would you
describe your sense of style?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  I have fashion A.D.D; my style varies from bohemian
to edgy to preppy to ridiculously girly. It depends on how I feel, what Im
doing, and where I am going. One theme that is constant is comfort (minus my
shoes). Usually, I end up preppy for work and bohemian to edgy for weekend
casual. Nighttime is a free-for-all. I almost always go with my first instinct
(even if I am getting a side eye from my boyfriend).
                 </span>
                </strong>
                <strong>
                 <span lang="EN-GB" style='font-family: "Arial","sans-serif"; font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;'>
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-s5eGtUbyfpo/UrjPmy2saxI/AAAAAAAAA-c/mXqmAjzmA84/s1600/Pleated+Leather+Skrit.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-s5eGtUbyfpo/UrjPmy2saxI/AAAAAAAAA-c/mXqmAjzmA84/s1600/Pleated+Leather+Skrit.jpg"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  Where do you get your
inspiration for each look?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  Inspiration is something that I feel very strongly
about. I have the magazine clippings to prove it! I have been ripping out fashion
pictures since I was in high school and now have very large binders (divided by
season) that house my inspiration pics. I find inspiration not only in print
magazines, but also from street style pics, other bloggers, movies and television
(SATC, Gossip Girl), and my friends!
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  Whats your Go-To
outfit for when you need to get ready in a hurry?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  All black everything- motorcycle boots, skinnies,
and a leather moto jacket. A pop of color usually comes in the form of a hat
(or beanie) on my head.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-j03_NEc748w/UrjPj6vif_I/AAAAAAAAA-I/0w83ShaAKNg/s1600/Monochromatic+Navy.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-j03_NEc748w/UrjPj6vif_I/AAAAAAAAA-I/0w83ShaAKNg/s1600/Monochromatic+Navy.jpg"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  What are the three
favorite things in your closet?
                 </span>
                </strong>
                <strong>
                 <span lang="EN-GB" style='font-family: "Arial","sans-serif"; mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;'>
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  My black moto jacket (which I am constantly reaching
for), a perfectly pink Diane Von Furstenberg dress I scored at a Neiman Marcus
Last Call a few months ago (I have yet to photograph it for the blog, but I do
have a picture of it on my Instagram account, linked below), and a
white/purple/blue dip dye Henri Bendel tote I purchased over the summer.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-MdkchxK95u8/UrjPnPf67uI/AAAAAAAAA-g/Z16GHllxmhk/s1600/Booties+with+Cutoffs.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-MdkchxK95u8/UrjPnPf67uI/AAAAAAAAA-g/Z16GHllxmhk/s1600/Booties+with+Cutoffs.jpg"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  What are some of your
favorite shops or websites for clothes, shoes and accessories?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  I love Nordstrom, Shopbop, Zara, Madewell, J.Crew, and
Lord &amp; Taylor (I find great deals at L&amp;T). I am a definite bargain
hunter, but quality has become very important to me. I am starting to realize
that splurging on something that you will wear 100 times might be a better way
to go than wasting $20 on something you only wear once.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-81Ds67AxX44/UrjPmwhtRaI/AAAAAAAAA-Y/UXb1scKkkbM/s1600/High+Waisted+Polka+Dot+Pants.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-81Ds67AxX44/UrjPmwhtRaI/AAAAAAAAA-Y/UXb1scKkkbM/s1600/High+Waisted+Polka+Dot+Pants.jpg"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  What fashion or
styling tips would you give to someone who wants to improve their sense
ofstyle?
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  Start with inspiration. Visit fashion blogs (peruse
them on Bloglovin), look at magazines, visit entertainment websites to check
out celebrity style. Figure out a few people whose style you cant get enough
of and who have a similar body shape to yours. Start making a list of items
that you would like to add to your wardrobe. Do you love Jessica Albas
high-waisted bell bottom/fedora look? Do you own high-waisted bell bottoms? Do
you own a fedora? Keep this fashion wish list in your purse at all times so you
can glance at it when you find yourself shopping.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-QRCrQfkBV34/UrjPpbwqGpI/AAAAAAAAA-w/r0V6dPwKb_4/s1600/White+with+Bow+Tie.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-QRCrQfkBV34/UrjPpbwqGpI/AAAAAAAAA-w/r0V6dPwKb_4/s1600/White+with+Bow+Tie.jpg"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div style="background: white; margin-bottom: 6.0pt;">
                <strong>
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
                  Is there a
blog/webpage or Youtube channel where we can find you?
                 </span>
                </strong>
                <strong>
                 <span lang="EN-GB" style='font-family: "Arial","sans-serif";'>
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </strong>
