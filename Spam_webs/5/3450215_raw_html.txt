               <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
                Brace yourself: youre about to take an adventure of a lifetime!
               </h2>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                A lot of people believe that reaching this age can be a challenge. This is also the stage where you could achieve your
                <a href="http://iwbodywraps.com/earn-money-the-it-works-way-with-the-ultimate-body-applicator-wrap-aka-that-crazy-wrap-thing">
                 lifes primal fun and excitement
                </a>
                . Would you agree? Life is just extraordinary. However, with all these things going on around us, enjoying the time of your life being 20 can be crucial. In other words, its difficult to stay focused on one thing. You can focus on getting a degree yet losing concentration on becoming fit. You have to admit, staying healthy can be difficult. In order to transform those melancholic days to brighter ones, read on and pump up your energy levels.
                <span id="more-4702">
                </span>
               </p>
               <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                10 Commandments of a Woman In Her 20s:
               </h3>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Ask any adult woman and she would tell you that her 20s was her most fun and exciting years. All you need is to increase your feel good hormones. In short, increase your endorphin levels to make these things happen. The key is to start with these 10 habits. No pressure. Its about making yourself feel and look great. Get ready!
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 I. Train Your Muscles.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                There are a lot of benefits to working out. As you strengthen your muscles, you should be able to ease your back pain, get a good nights rest, burn those excess calories, and heres one thing that would excite you  increase your metabolism. Dont people blame having a slow metabolism whenever they have gained too much weight? Overall,
                <a href="http://iwbodywraps.com/it-works-products">
                 your health will improve
                </a>
                as you begin your strength training.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 II. Hydrate Yourself. Drink Water.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Its about time to change that habit of drinking soda or alcohol. Drink water instead and replenish your body. You need to. Imagine how much water your body contains. You need to replace what has been lost. Think about how a dry ground begins to crack. This is one of the reasons your skin would begin to wrinkle and sag.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 III. Introduce New Kinds of Exercise.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                If you do the same exercise or things over and over again, your body will tend to hit a plateau. Notice how some people do not even lose any pounds no matter how much they exercise and work out. Vary your work outs. Perhaps you can jog today, do yoga tomorrow, and take a hike the next. Be in control. This is your body that were talking about.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 IV. Rest Days are Essential.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Give your body a time to recover and replenish. Machines experience wear and tear if you dont turn them off. You may want to feel good tomorrow, but dont strain yourself. No, it doesnt mean that if you have worked out for 24 hours nonstop that you will burn those calories and fats. You could not make this happen overnight. It takes time. Enjoy every single day of it.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 V. The Key is to have a Proper Form.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Dont risk yourself from getting injuries. Your trainer may teach you how to perform your exercises, listen and follow them. You may want to consider weight lifting, but make sure you have the proper form.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 VI. Use Right Equipment.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Even if you exercise or workout in your home, practice using the right equipment. Again, you wouldnt want to incur injuries. Furthermore, with the right equipment, you will be able to recognize its efficiency.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 VII. Its Time to Refuel.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Right after your workout, replenish your body. You will crave for protein and carbohydrates. Refuel your body with the right amount of food. Carbohydrates are energy boosters. Meanwhile, protein repair your stretched and torn muscles. Get enough dose of them.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 VIII. Stretching has its Own Meaning.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                3-5 minutes of stretching is enough to prevent injuries and at the same time tighten your muscles. Never forget to stretch after you exercise.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 IX. Achieve Good Posture.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                Pilates, yoga, or whatever your trainer would teach you to achieve good posture. This should not only accelerate your feel good hormones, but your confidence as well.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 X. Enjoy and Have Fun!
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                This isnt a hard thing to do. Learn to love your age, your body and most of all your life. Start building your muscles now! Stay young and healthy.
               </p>
