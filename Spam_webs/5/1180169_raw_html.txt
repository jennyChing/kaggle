              <p>
               Redditors are hailing this as "the worst local commercial I have ever seen." While that may be true for
               <a href="http://www.reddit.com/user/topofthecc" target="_blank">
                topofthecc
               </a>
               , we can probably all agree that it's pretty bad and probably the worst you'll see today.
              </p>
              <p>
               The back-to-school ad, for a
               <a href="http://www.shopeasthills.com/" target="_blank">
                shopping center in St. Joseph, Missouri
               </a>
               , has many cringe-inducing elements, including '80s, Fat Boys-style rapping, off-key singing and perhaps most of all, ultimately unsuccessful attempts to muster enthusiasm.
              </p>

                Reddit discussion
               </a>
               , the mall hasn't enjoyed the attendance it used to  a plausible claim since retail foot traffic is falling everywhere in the age of Amazon. Perhaps this so-bad-it's-good ad will give East Hills a splash of ironic fame and once again draw the crowds.
