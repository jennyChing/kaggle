        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Top 4 First Class Cabins
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="author vcard">
          By
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.trakdot.com/blog/author/admin/" title="View all posts by admin">
           admin

          <span class="bl_sep">

         <span class="bl_categ">

         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_454" style="width: 430px">
          <a href="http://blog.trakdot.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/SingaporeAir.jpg">
           <img alt="Singapore Air First Class" class=" wp-image-454 " height="238" src="http://blog.trakdot.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/SingaporeAir.jpg" width="420"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Singapore Air First Class
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          If youre going to pay for First Class, we highly suggest going with one of these top 4. Forget about leg room and awkward sleeping positions, youll want to move into these posh airplane seats.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           Singapore Air First Class:
          </strong>
          Believe it or not, First Class is only level two on Singapore Airlines. This luxury seat is 35 inches wide and offers gourmet dining options, a built in cinema and the option to fold-out your seat into a bed at any time. Oh and if you want a sleeper suit and bedroom slippers, you can snag those too!
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_455" style="width: 570px">
          <a href="http://blog.trakdot.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/SingaporeFirstClass.jpg">
           <img alt="Singapore Air First Class" class=" wp-image-455 " height="317" src="http://blog.trakdot.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/SingaporeFirstClass.jpg" width="560"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Singapore Air Suite Class
          </p>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           Singapore Air Suite Class:
          </strong>
          If youre looking for even more comfort on Singapore Air, Suite Class takes you to another level of Singapore Air luxury. Much like the cabin on a cruise ship, this includes a bed, sliding doors and all the fine dining, slippers and movies you want.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
