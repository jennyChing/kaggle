           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even">
             <img alt="Slow Juicing: A Great Way To Drink Your Vegetables" src="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/green-goddess-juice-recipe.jpg?itok=-bt6Goki" typeof="foaf:Image"/>

          <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
             <p>
              Juicing is the latest trend in healthful living, and it really is a delicious way to pack in all those nutrients from fruits and veggies.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.newegg.ca/Home-Outdoors/Store" target="_blank">
               Newegg At Home
              </a>
              sent mea
              <a href="http://www.newegg.ca/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16896922001" target="_blank">
               Fagor Slow Juicer
              </a>
              to try out and one to give away. In the following video I'm going to show you how easy it is to use, and tell you all about the amazing benefits of slow juicing. I'm also going to show you how to make a delicious and nutrient-packed Green Goddess juice that will make you feel beautiful from the inside out.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Watch this video to learn about the benefits of slow juicing:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/o2L0xVVWSYw" width="420">
              </iframe>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Green Goddess Juice
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Ingredients:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <div>
              2cups spinach
             </div>
             <div>
              1/2 medium field cucumber
             </div>
             <div>
              1/2 medium lemon
             </div>
             <div>
              1 apple
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u18/article%20flower.jpg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Wash vegetables thoroughly.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u18/article%20flower.jpg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Rinse spinach but do not pat dry.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u18/article%20flower.jpg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Peel lemon and chop into 3-inch pieces.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u18/article%20flower.jpg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Chop apple and cucumber into 3-inch pieces.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u18/article%20flower.jpg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px;"/>
              Feed into the juicer, alternating spinach with the other ingredients.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              Enjoy!
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              <strong>
               Makes 1 serving
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div>
