     <p>
      <b>
       Bessemer Venture Partners
      </b>
      is an American global
      <a href="/wiki/Venture_capital" title="Venture capital">
       venture capital
      </a>
      firm with offices in
      <a href="/wiki/Silicon_Valley" title="Silicon Valley">
       Silicon Valley
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">
       New York
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">
       Boston
      </a>
      , Herzliya, Israel, and Mumbai and Bangalore,
      <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">
       India
      </a>
      . Since its inception in 1911, Bessemer has provided capital to companies such as Box,
      <a href="/wiki/Pinterest" title="Pinterest">
       Pinterest
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Diapers.com" title="Diapers.com">
       Quidsi
      </a>
      (Diapers.com),
      <a href="/wiki/Skype" title="Skype">
       Skype
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Staples_Inc." title="Staples Inc.">
       Staples Inc.
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/VeriSign" title="VeriSign">
       VeriSign
      </a>
      and Dicks Sporting Goods. More than 100 Bessemer companies have gone public, including recent IPOs from
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/cornerstone-ondemand" rel="nofollow">
       Cornerstone
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/broadsoft" rel="nofollow">
       BroadSoft
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/linkedin" rel="nofollow">
       LinkedIn
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/yelp" rel="nofollow">
       Yelp
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/millennial-media" rel="nofollow">
       Millennial Media
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/eloqua" rel="nofollow">
       Eloqua
      </a>
      and
      <a class="external text" href="http://www.bvp.com/portfolio/lifelock" rel="nofollow">
       LifeLock
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Bessemer Venture Partners is the longest-standing venture capital practice in the United States. In 1911,
      <a href="/wiki/Henry_Phipps,_Jr." title="Henry Phipps, Jr.">
       Henry Phipps
      </a>
      established a family office with his share of the proceeds from the sale of
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Carnegie_Steel" title="Carnegie Steel">
       Carnegie Steel
      </a>
      . Bessemer currently manages more than $4 billion of venture capital. The firm raised a $1.6 billion fund in 2011.
     </p>
     <p>
      Bessemer partners include Kent Bennett, Ed Colloton, David Cowan, Byron Deeter, Brian Feinstein, Alex Ferrara, Bob Goodman, Adam Fisher,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chris_Gabrielli" title="Chris Gabrielli">
       Chris Gabrielli
      </a>
      , Vishal Gupta, Felda Hardymon, Steve Kraus, Ethan Kurzweil, Jeremy Levine, Siddharth Nautiuyal, Trevor Oelschig, Umesh Padval, Rob Stavis and Subu S.V.
     </p>
