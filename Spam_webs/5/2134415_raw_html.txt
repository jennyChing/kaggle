       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1974" height="1080" src="http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_1.jpg" width="720"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/eats/korean-pork-tacos/&amp;media=http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_1.jpg&amp;description=Korean Pork Tacos')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Happy Cinco de Mayo! I hope you have your sombrero ready because Im doing Korean Pork Tacos today. I actually made this two weeks ago when we first tested our new grill. Boy, it was a tough one!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1975" height="480" src="http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_2.jpg" width="720"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/eats/korean-pork-tacos/&amp;media=http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_2.jpg&amp;description=Korean Pork Tacos')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         We never owned a BBQ grill before but finally decided to buy a Traeger BBQ Grill for our new house. Dinner was supposed to start at 6pm, and I thought that we would have enough time if we start grilling at 4pm. Little did we know that we had to preheat the grill for an hour before we could use it. So, there we were with our meats, doing nothing but waiting for the grill to start heating up. Finally we were able to start cooking at 5.15pm, but voila, the grill worked perfectly! And luckily, the butcher was able to thinly sliced the pork so it didnt take us too long to cook it.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_3.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1976" height="480" src="http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_3.jpg" width="720"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/eats/korean-pork-tacos/&amp;media=http://www.brunchwithjoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/brunchwithjoy_koreanporktacos_0515_3.jpg&amp;description=Korean Pork Tacos')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Ive also made homemade kimchi using the recipe from Maangchi. Kimchi is easy to make but very time consuming. Nevertheless, Im super happy with the result. The kimchi also works well with this spicy pork as a perfect way to celebrate Cinco de Mayo.
        </p>

           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a bag, marinate the pork belly with water, salt, sugar, and 1-inch ginger for 24 hours.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix the sauce and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            When ready, rinse under cold water and pat dry.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat the grill for high heat.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Brush the pork belly with sauce and place pork on the grill. Cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, until juices run clear.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Serve pork belly over taco, top with kimchi and toasted sesame seeds.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
          <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
           Notes
          </div>
          <div class="ERSNotes">
           For crunchiness, add cucumber pickles on top.
          </div>
