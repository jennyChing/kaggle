      <h1 class="title">
       Retro Electronics Amazing Paper Art
      </h1>
      <div class="meta">
       <a href="http://molempire.com/author/leetsquivel/" rel="author" title="Posts by Leetsquivel">
        Leetsquivel
       </a>
       | July 11, 2011 |
       <a href="http://molempire.com/2011/07/11/retro-electronics-amazing-paper-art/#comments">
        Comments (2)
       </a>
      </div>
      <div class="content clearfix">
       <p>
        Two artschool grads from Nancy, France had a terrific idea to create old electronic items out of paper. Be it a cassette, a walkman, an old telephone or a Nintendo controller, these things are just so colorful and well-made that you wish they were real.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5918" height="394" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art6-594x394.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5917" height="351" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art5-594x351.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5915" height="395" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art2-594x395.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="size-full wp-image-5922 aligncenter" height="576" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art9.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="458"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="size-full wp-image-5919 aligncenter" height="457" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art7.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="561"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5921" height="401" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art8-594x401.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Lucie Thomas and Thibault Zimmermann, whove studied graphic design and go by the name of
        <a href="http://www.zimandzou.fr/">
         Zim and Zou
        </a>
        , are pretty much paper handling experts. This project is just one of the many paper sculptures, installations and graphic designs that theyve done in their studio based in France.
       </p>
       <div class="single-banner">

       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5924" height="337" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art11-594x337.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5923" height="431" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art10-594x431.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5926" height="407" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art13-594x407.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>

       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="size-full wp-image-5920 aligncenter" height="697" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art14.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="518"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Zim and Zou Spectacular " class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5925" height="395" src="http://molempire.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/retro_electronics_paper_art12-594x395.jpg" title="Zim and Zou Spectacular " width="594"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        According to Zim and Zou, this project, named Back to Basics, took one year to develop and its still ongoing. So if youre curious of what goes on in the process of making these tiny paper treasures, you can have a look at this video theyve posted and maybe start making your own, if youre up to it:
       </p>
       <iframe frameborder="0" height="386" src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/25776682" width="594">
       </iframe>
