                     Singer Taylor Swift insuring her legs
                    </h3>

                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        Singer Taylor Swift is
reportedly insuring her legs for $40 million.
                        <a name="more">
                        </a>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <span lang="EN">
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 18.3999996185303px;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          It is believed that the 25-year-old's team looked into how much her legs were
worth in case anything happened during her routines.
                         </span>
                        </div>
                       </span>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          "It seems like a ludicrous sum, but if something was to happen to her
legs, Swift wouldn't be able to give her signature stage performances,"
said a source, reports mirror.co.uk.
                         </span>
                        </div>
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          <br/>
                         </span>
                        </div>
                       </span>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-PNcgFiWt42Q/VQBn40wqFMI/AAAAAAAAt28/kGPWjZ0_tgg/s1600/Taylor%2BSwift.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img alt="Singer Taylor Swift is reportedly insuring her legs for $40 million" border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-PNcgFiWt42Q/VQBn40wqFMI/AAAAAAAAt28/kGPWjZ0_tgg/s1600/Taylor%2BSwift.jpg" title="Singer Taylor Swift" width="202"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          "Her $200 million career would be in big trouble," added the source.
                         </span>
                        </div>
                       </span>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          The singer, who had showed off her legs in a short suit at The Brit Awards last
month, was believed to be "embarrassed" once she found out how much
her legs were worth.
                         </span>
                        </div>
                       </span>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          "She thought her legs were possibly worth a million. But $40 million
shocked her. She's even a little embarrassed about it," said the source.
                         </span>
                        </div>
                       </span>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                        <div style="text-align: justify;">
                         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                          Her team is now said to be completing the paperwork in time for her world tour,
which begins in May.
