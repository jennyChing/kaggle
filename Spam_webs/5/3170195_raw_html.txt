         <h1 class="title single-title">
          Day 19 to 21 of Surviving Change. When life gets difficult, stop and think, do not fight it
         </h1>
         <!-- End Title -->
         <!-- Start Post Meta -->
         <div class="post-info">
          <span class="theauthor">

            30 Day Journey

          <span class="thecomment">
           <a href="http://www.survivingchange.co.za/day-19-to-21-of-surviving-change-when-life-gets-difficult-stop-and-think-do-not-fight-it/#comments">

        <div class="post-single-content box mark-links">
         <p>
          When life gets difficult, stop and think, do not fight it. My third week of the Surviving Change journey would be best described as an iceberg. Only 10% is visible while the rest is underwater. The week felt difficult and I struggled a lot, but it forced me to stop and think. Now, sitting here reflecting on the past week on a Sunday night, I realised why I had to be slowed down to stop and think.
         </p>
         <p>
          I did fight the slowdown as best I could because if I want to bring financial freedom to millions of people using my methodology, I have to make it a success myself first. I have proven that it works by building a new business in 14 days that already got $10,000 worth of orders and the work has already begun. However, I want it to be more than that. I would be happy with $100,000 in 30 days. Having this in the back of my mind does drive you to extremes and I really started to feel tired and sometimes unfocused.
         </p>
         <p>
          Friday, I tried my best to get anything going, but I was dead tired and had a headache of note which was with me already by the time I woke up. The pressure of reching more success was on me the whole time and then I also had to prepare for the Toastmasters area contest in the humorous and impromptu speaking categories. I am glad to report that I won in both categories.
         </p>
         <p>
          I only realised later in the day that I had to accept the state of affairs and slow down. As soon as I realised I had to accept that things would happen on their own time, the headache disappeared. The world has many strange ways to give us feedback on what we have to do.
         </p>
         <p>
          Life will happen at its own pace and there is nothing I or we can do about it. Trying to force things to happen is futile. The only thing we can do, is to take consistent action. You can have vision boards. You can watch the movie
          <em>
           The Secret
          </em>
          hundreds of times and you can pray and keep praying but nothing will happen if you do not uphold your part the bargain. Thats the only control we have in this deal of life and destiny.
         </p>
         <p>
          Consistent action can take on many forms  actual work, meetings or even just sitting and thinking or even spending time with your children and playing. If the action you are taking feels right inside and life flows from that action, you are on the right track.
         </p>
         <p>
          I know what I have to do in this life. I am going to change the world and bring financial freedom to millions of people. Every day I am learning, reading, thinking and connecting and doing my bit in this game of life. If life feels difficult, I am travelling in the wrong direction. If life is flowing like a river, I am moving in the right direction.
         </p>
         <p>
          Life at the end of last week felt difficult and there was no flow. It resulted in me feeling tired and lifeless and not having any real progress from what I can see. I simply had to stop and think about my journey. The progress that life decided I needed would not be presented in the outside world but rather inside me. And now, after standing back for a few days it is as clear as day why I had to stop, think and grow.
         </p>
         <p>
          In order for me to have the success I want, I have to become that person of success first. Success is a mindset first and foremost. The changes within you impacts how you do and act in the world and in turn then change your world around you. In simple English, in order to be a millionaire you have to think like a millionaire. If you were already thinking like a millionaire, you would be one already today. Are you a millionaire? What needs to change for you to become one? What thinking and habits you hold on to is withholding this success from you?
         </p>
         <p>
          Today will be different; I will slow down a bit and do what I can do today. If I cannot get to everything that I want to get to, that is fine  then it was not supposed to happen today. Let life lead the way, because the future life has planned for me is bright.
         </p>
