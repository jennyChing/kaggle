     <p>
      Schindelhauer BFS ThinBike, a design masterpiece on wheels.We have a problem, a serious bike problem. Some people collect baseball cards and old motorcycles, we collect bikes. Whenever we see a cool new concept or design we want to share it with the world. Berliner Fahrradschau and Schindelhauer Bikes have collaborated on a special limited edition, the BFS ThinBike. This polar white edition of 25 numbered pieces features exclusive BFS branding, matching front rack, and included wall mount for easy storage. If winning the 2014 Red Dot Design award and iF Design Award doesnt wet your appetite, the ingenious fold away handlebars and pedals certainly will. As avid urban
      <a href="http://lumberjac.com/tag/commuter/" target="_blank" title="Commuters">
       commuters
      </a>
      were always looking at new designs that make our lives just a little bit easier. Details like the custom fenders, dynamo front light, and a bell integrated into the brake lever make us appreciate the meticulous effort that went into designing this masterpiece. Only one question remains, will a case a beer fit on the front rack?
     </p>
