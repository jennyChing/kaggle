      <p>
       The Downtown Boathouse is an all-volunteer nonprofit organization dedicated to providing free public access to the harbor in New York City through public kayaking programs. Our mission is to encourage safe public use of the harbor waters of New York City and thereby provide residents of this space-constrained city with increased recreational opportunities. Our operations are made possible exclusively through volunteers and with your public donations.

          <div class="sqs-block-content">
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Free Kayaking
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            We offer free sit-on-top kayaks for public use in protected Hudson River embayments at each of our locations throughout our season (May-October). We give brief instruction and provide all necessary safety equipment as well as changing rooms, lockers and locks, bike locks, sun block, and first aid equipment.
           </p>
           <p>
            This is by far our most popular program for adults and children alike. It provides an opportunity to create a personal connection with our waterfront,
            <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
             regardless of age, income or location, and
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
             it also serves to educate our communities about how far we have come in cleaning up the Hudson River.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Rules - Free Kayaking
           </h3>
           <p>
            <strong>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Before You Go Out
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
             Sign a waiver (good for the year)
            <br/>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              You must know how to swim
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              Put on a lifejacket
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             On the Water
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
             Stay within the bay (between piers)
            <br/>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              Keep away from sides and wall
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              Come back in 20 minutes
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              Come back in if called
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              No swimming
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Under Eighteen
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
             One child per boat
            <br/>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              Guardian must be present
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.68em;">
              Under 16, must go with guardian
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
