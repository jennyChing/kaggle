               <p>
                Loveless? Still havent found your one true love or perfect soul mate? Well, maybe, you should try dating a girl who
                <strong>
                 thrives on adventures and traveling
                </strong>
                . From having
                <strong>
                 wondrous and meaningful conversations
                </strong>
                to experiencing a ton of
                <strong>
                 breathtaking escapades
                </strong>
                , there are surely a lot of perks of dating a girl who loves to travel and explore the world.
               </p>
               <p>
                Im not a love expert, by any means, but trust me, she will sweep you off your feet with her free spirit as well as the mesmerizing sparkle in her eyes, whenever you say: lets go.
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="female traveler " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7230" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/ThinkstockPhotos-469560291.png" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <h2>
                <strong>
                 Reasons to date a girl who loves to travel
                </strong>
               </h2>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 1
                </span>
                She is witty, intelligent and enthralling
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="conversation " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7232" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/istock_000053030442_large.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                There is never a dull moment with a girl who loves to travel. Whether shes urging you to take an exciting adventure in a Safari jungle or sharing stories of her epic backpacking escapades, she always has a way to enthrall you. Plus, shes got the wit and charm to make your conversations more fun and engaging.
               </p>
               <p>
                A girl who loves to travel is often intelligent, curious and inquisitive. After all, traveling is one of the best forms of education. With enthusiasm and passion, she can talk for a number of hours about her backpacking trips and adventures abroad.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 2
                </span>
                Life never gets boring with a girl who loves to travel
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="fun and adventure " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7233" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/ThinkstockPhotos-178493982.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                Just when you think that your life is heading towards the mundane, she will come up with a trip that will create magic and a spark of excitement in your life. She will
                <a href="http://trekeffect.com/">
                 plan a trip
                </a>
                , and will even have the budget and flights sorted out. She is, then, going to take you to places where youve never been to, allowing you to discover a lot of new things that could an extra element of fun and excitement in your life.
               </p>
               <p>
                A girl who loves to travel also recognizes every opportunity for adventure, excitement and fun. She is always keen to try something new and unusual with you, whether abroad or at home. From building houses in Africa to skydiving in
                <a href="http://blog.trekeffect.com/35-reasons-dubai-on-bucket-list">
                 Dubai
                </a>
                , she has a sense of adventure that is practically limitless, and youll never get bored with her.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 3
                </span>
                Shes sprightly and active
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="an active female traveler " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7234" height="397" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/cropped-dsc_0174-1.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                Whether shes hiking the Himalayas or taking a stroll around the neighborhood, she is the kind of girl who wants to be active at all times. While she does love the occasional stops to
                <a href="http://www.starbucks.com/">
                 Starbucks
                </a>
                , she is someone that pushes you outside comfort zone, to help you become a better, stronger and healthier version of yourself.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 4
                </span>
                She is fun-loving and spontaneous
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="spontaneous girl who loves to travel " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7235" height="399" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/ThinkstockPhotos-469037111.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                Travelers, in general, are always up for a blast. And they rarely say no to things that are new and fun, which makes her a perfect fit to a dude whos into a good-time and spontaneous relationship.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 5
                </span>
                A girl who loves to travel is adaptable and easy-going
               </h3>
               <p>
                After many delays, missed flights, bad restrooms, bad street food and wrong turns, it is safe to say that she is adaptable and easy-going. She is ready for any bumps on the road metaphorically and literally. When life throws a curve ball at her, she smacks it right out of the ballpark with flying colors.
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="adaptable girl " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7237" height="260" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/complicated.gif" width="499"/>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 6
                </span>
                She isnt materialistic
               </h3>
               <p>
                There is no need for you to spend a great deal of money on a girl who loves to travel. As her boyfriend or suitor, you wont have to buy her extravagant gifts like a ritzy car or a state-of-the-art
                <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00QLDRXJG/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00QLDRXJG&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=trekeffect-20&amp;linkId=6Y3Q5JEMUIETSV6M%22%3eiPhone%206%20Plus%20Case,%20Maxboost&reg;%20%5bVibrance%20Series%5d%20iPhone%206%20Plus%20(5.5)%20Case%20%5bLifetim">
                 smartphone
                </a>
                , just to impress her or make her happy. As a matter of fact, a simple yet meaningful gift likea photo frame of you and her traveling will sweep her away into bliss.
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="non-materialistic girl " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7239" height="270" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/giphy2.gif" width="500"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                Looking for the perfect present for a globetrotting girl? Here are a couple of cool travel equipment that traveling gals would love to have.
               </p>
               <p class="clearfix">
                <a class="buy" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B006QF3TW4/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B006QF3TW4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=trekeffect-20&amp;linkId=TLVJDEBURRVVGA52" target="_blank">

                 Reviews
                </a>
               </p>
               <p class="clearfix">
                <a class="buy" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00K8I4D2A/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00K8I4D2A&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=trekeffect-20&amp;linkId=ZYQSZWKBF2YTWHF5" target="_blank">

                 Reviews
                </a>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 7
                </span>
                She is easy to please
               </h3>
               <p>
                A girl who loves to travel certainly knows how to appreciate the small and simple things in life. She loves to listen to the chirping birds near your window, or watch the gleaming stars at night. She even goes nuts over the incense sticks that will remind her of the smells in Bali, or the wasabi candies that bring back memories of her unforgettable gastronomic experience in
                <a href="http://blog.trekeffect.com/25-ways-enjoy-japan-budget">
                 Japan
                </a>
                .
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 8
                </span>
                She can tolerate your untidy room
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="girl with untidy room " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7240" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/o-MESSY-TEEN-BEDROOM-facebook.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                A girl who loves to travel wont mind dating a partner who has an untidy room. In fact, tolerating your messy space could be a piece of cake for her. If she can stay in a hostel dorm with a bunch of unwashed backpackers in Peru or take a piss in a nasty squat toilet in Southeast Asia, then she can definitely endure all the clutter in your room.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 9
                </span>
                She knows who exactly she is
               </h3>
               <p>
                Traveling, in a lot of different ways, lets people discover themselves. And of course, a girl who travels a lot knows herself more than anybody else.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 10
                </span>
                Shes a good problem solver
               </h3>
               <p>
                It is inevitable for travelers, especially the ones who spend a few months on the road, to run into a few problems. And while most people would freak out when they miss a bus or get pick-pocketed, she has the ability to think in a rational way, and find solutions to solve the problem.
               </p>
               <p>
                With her as your dating partner, you will always have a reliable person wholl offer a great piece of advice, every time youre dealing with a major problem in your life. Not to mention, she can react rationally to issues, whenever they arise in your relationship.
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="problem solved " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7242" height="220" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/tumblr_mbw3f0NtpC1ry34ar.gif" width="500"/>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 11
                </span>
                Shes open-minded
               </h3>
               <p>
                The more she immerses herself into other cultures, the more open-minded she becomes. Instead of looking at other places and people as weird, she simply learns to view them as something different. So, if youre girlfriend is a travel junkie, you dont have to worry about her judging you, and the other unusual things that you want to try. With her, you can be as weird and idiosyncratic as you can be.
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="weird girl " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7247" height="215" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/living-bayarea-21.gif" width="500"/>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 12
                </span>
                Your buddies will like her
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="conversation friends " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7245" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/5a958f20_Conversation-Starters.xxxlarge_2x.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                You will rarely bump into a traveler who is unfriendly and shy. Months of sharing hotel dorms with strangers and talking with foreigners have given her the ability to make friends with anyone in any situation and anywhere. You wont have to worry about your friends not liking her because she is social and doesnt have problems making friends instantly.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 13
                </span>
                She is hard-working
               </h3>
               <p>
                She is probably the hardest working gal you will ever meet in your lifetime. She will take a couple of jobs or more, to
                <a href="http://blog.trekeffect.com/20-sure-fire-ways-help-travel-funds-grow">
                 fund her trips and adventures
                </a>
                . Whats more, she shares her travel experiences to the world via a blog, and converts it into an extra source of income.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 14
                </span>
                Good financial management skills
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="girl computing her expenses" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7248" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/young-woman-working-in-home-office-2.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                Speaking of funds, travelers are also quite proficient, when it comes to financial management. Not only do they spend their money wisely, but they can find a myriad of ways to generate funds on the road as well.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 15
                </span>
                She isnt clingy
               </h3>
               <p>
                She isnt the kind of girl who wants to know everything you do. She wants to be with you because she likes you and it is fun, and not because she needs to be with you at all times. Unlike most lonely women and men, she wont use her dating partner to fill a void.
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="Independent woman " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7250" height="274" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/large.gif" width="500"/>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 16
                </span>
                She will leave a positive mark in your life
               </h3>
               <p>
                Even if your relationship wont work out, she will still leave a lasting positive mark in your life. She can change your life for the better, as well as make you a more exciting and well-rounded dude. Furthermore, she can introduce you to a whole lot of new theories and ideas that will make your wiser.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 17
                </span>
                She is beautiful in her own special and unique way
               </h3>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="beautiful backpacker " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7252" height="400" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/how-to-beat-the-backpacker-blues-2.jpg" width="709"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                She can dress up for you, put on a fragrant perfume and wear glittering jewelry on occasion. But, she will feel more comfortable, beautiful and confident in her shorts and vest tops, with her hair tied back. Her skin is freckly and tanned, and she sports a number of hippy bracelets around her wrists and ankles that she has collected from her globetrotting journey. She is a wanderer, and she is indeed a beautiful creature in her own way.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                <span class="bulleted">
                 18
                </span>
                Shes loving and thoughtful
               </h3>
               <p class="img_wrap">
                <img alt="beautiful female traveler " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7251" height="401" src="//blog.trekeffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/backpacking_in_europe_tips.jpg" width="710"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                She may be on the road a lot, but a girl who loves to travel never forgets you. You may not hear her voice for a few days, but she constantly writes how she has missed you in her blog or diary. She thinks about you every time she goes to sleep, and she tells all the backpackers in her hostel dorm on how great and wonderful you are. Moreover, she will do her best to find a place with WiFi, so she can call you on Skype or send you a message on Whatsapp.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                So, do you think shes worth it? As far as Im concerned, she is a sweet girl who, in many ways, can have a positive impact in your life. With her great traits and optimistic outlook, I even think shes a wifey material. Sure, you are going to be in a wild and crazy ride, but shes that special someone who you truly want to spend your entire lifetime with. Of course, she is also a great travel companion that will help feed your wanderlust.
               </p>

                Want to make her happy? Then, plan a trip with her for FREE usingTrekeffect!
