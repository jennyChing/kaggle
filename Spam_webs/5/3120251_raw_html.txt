      <h2 class="title">
       Middle Schooler Suspended over Death Note
      </h2>
      <div class="clearfloat" id="stats">
       <span class="left">
        Submitted by
        <a href="http://www.dailyotaku.com/author/admin/" title="Posts by Zero">
         Zero

       <span class="right">

      </div>
      <div class="entry clearfloat">
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://www.dailyotaku.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/2424995662_8bff376e08_o.jpg">
         <img alt="2424995662_8bff376e08_o" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1987" height="480" src="http://www.dailyotaku.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/2424995662_8bff376e08_o.jpg" title="2424995662_8bff376e08_o" width="550"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Death note strikes again;
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         The Argus-Press newspaper reports on Friday that a 14-year-old middle school student from Owosso, Michigan was suspended indefinitely after another student found a Death Note created by the 14-year-old boy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span id="more-1986">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Owosso Police Department Deputy Director Michael Rau said the notebook contained two students names and times, but the listed times had already passed.
        </p>
        <p>
         The student who discovered the notebook had given it to a teacher, who then gave it to school administrators on Tuesday. The county prosecutors office is now handling the incident, but both Rau and the schools principal, Rich Collins, said that they believe no student was in danger.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        This isnt the first time a student has been suspend over bringing a Death Note to school, and I have a feeling it wont be the last time.
       </p>
       <p>
        A question for the readers, do you think the suspension was justifiable or not?
       </p>
