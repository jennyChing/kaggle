8. For vegetarian, Ive used approx 3 oz of spinach (cooked in 1T water and drained) OR approx 3 oz of frozen broccoli florets instead of bacon.

10. Right now I am making one with 2 oz frozen broccoli florets AND 2 oz bacon with cheddar cheese &amp; frozen chopped onion.  I let the pie crust, onion, and broccoli sit out of the freezer for 1 hour firstPefrect!
