        <h1 class="post-title">
         Office Tub Chairs  Its Time to Plan your Office Refurbishment
        </h1>
        <section class="details">
         <time class="posted_at" datetime="2013-11-15">
          Posted on Nov 15 by andy
         </time>
         <aside class="filed_in">
          <strong>
           Filed in:
          </strong>
          <a href="/blog/categories/commercial-tub-chairs">
           Commercial Tub Chairs
          </a>
         </aside>
        </section>
        <img alt="Office Tub" src="/system/images/W1siZiIsIjIwMTMvMTEvMTUvMTUvMjYvNTIvOTcyL29mZmljZV90dWIuanBnIl1d/office-tub.jpg" style="display:block; margin:0 auto; float:none;  padding-bottom:20px;" title="Office Tub"/>
        <p>
         Its important that everyone enjoys the space they work in as much as they can.  After all, we spend so much time in the work environment and quite often, we spend more time at work than we do at home.  In the past, office furniture was bought from an office supplies company and where there might have been 2 choices of colour for an office chair, it was obligatory for the whole office to have the same colour to keep the colour scheme consistent.  At some point in time, as with most things, there was probably a fund backed project to assess what people thought about the space they work in - whether it made them feel happy, sad, uplifted or demotivated and if indeed they were proud to call it their workspace.  Being proud of where you work is very important and in itself, this is a great motivator.
        </p>
        <p>
         Larger companies are able to employ the services of an interior designer, someone who brings colours and textures together on mood boards and then presents these to a Board of Directors for the final approval and choice of colours.  Not every company can go to an expense such as this, where budgets need to be very carefully considered and perhaps not all new pieces of furniture or even the decorating can be undertaken at the same time.  This is where the purchase of
         <strong>
          office tub chairs
         </strong>
         comes into its own.  You may be in an environment where your colour scheme needs to be in more reserved colours perhaps, black, brown or cream and this is where
         <a href="http://www.tubchairs.com">
          TubChairs.Com
         </a>
         can provide you with several choices of
         <a href="http://www.tubchairs.com/t/category/contract-tub-chairs?locale=en" style="text-decoration:underline;">
          contract tub chairs
         </a>
         to meet those requirements.  If your thoughts are to bring two colours into your office interior design but you are worried that the colours wont blend together, why not take advantage of our free swatch service.  We can send you a selection of swatches and you can mix and match to find what suits you best.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you have a working environment where your manager asks for your input and/or suggestions and your working space is conducive to colour, then office tub chairs in bright colours or perhaps with a funky pattern is a fun route to go down when replacing your tired old office chairs.
        </p>
        <img alt="Spottytub" src="https://www.tubchairs.com/system/images/W1siZiIsIjIwMTMvMTEvMTUvMTUvMDkvMjUvOTE1L3Nwb3R0eXR1Yi5qcGciXV0/spottytub.jpg" style="display:block; margin:0 auto; float:none;  padding-bottom:20px;" title="Spottytub"/>
        <p>
         Imagine this funky office tub chair in Ditto Verdi fabric either in your main office or reception area!  What a fantastic improvement on the chrome and black fabric chairs of the past.  So many companies are choosing this chair in their break out area and their interview rooms as it brings great style and interest to the room and is enormously comfortable to sit on with good support for for your back and arms.  It certainly brings a happy feel to the office, and thats what everybody wants in their workplace.
        </p>
