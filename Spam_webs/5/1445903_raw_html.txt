       <h2 class="step-title">
        Step 1: The only step!
       </h2>
       <div class="photoset" data-entry-id="SDJVA6OFA190M9J" data-entry-url="/id/Easy-camper-van-conversion/step1/The-only-step/" id="photoset-SDJVA6OFA190M9J">
        <div class="row" style="width:600px;">
         <a class="photoset-link" data-fancybox-group="photoset-gallery-SDJVA6OFA190M9J" data-fancybox-href="http://cdn.instructables.com/FN2/1VR6/FA190M9O/FN21VR6FA190M9O.LARGE.jpg" data-href="/file/FN21VR6FA190M9O" data-notes="" href="/file/FN21VR6FA190M9O" id="photoset-link-FY2LTSLFA190M9V-0" style="">
          <div class="photo-container" style="width:600px;">
           <img alt="Picture of The only step!" class="photoset-photo id_FN21VR6FA190M9O " data-notes="" src="http://cdn.instructables.com/FN2/1VR6/FA190M9O/FN21VR6FA190M9O.MEDIUM.jpg" style="width:600px;"/>
          </div>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="row" style="width:600px;">
         <a class="photoset-link" data-fancybox-group="photoset-gallery-SDJVA6OFA190M9J" data-fancybox-href="http://cdn.instructables.com/F4M/8XMA/FA190M9N/F4M8XMAFA190M9N.LARGE.jpg" data-href="/file/F4M8XMAFA190M9N" data-notes="" href="/file/F4M8XMAFA190M9N" id="photoset-link-F7QMGS0FA190M9W-1" style="">
          <div class="photo-container" style="width:600px;">
           <img alt="000_0001.jpg" class="photoset-photo id_F4M8XMAFA190M9N lazyphoto" data-notes="" data-original="http://cdn.instructables.com/F4M/8XMA/FA190M9N/F4M8XMAFA190M9N.MEDIUM.jpg" src="/static/img/pixel.png" style="width:600px;"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="000_0001.jpg" class="photoset-photo" src="http://cdn.instructables.com/F4M/8XMA/FA190M9N/F4M8XMAFA190M9N.MEDIUM.jpg" style="width:600px;"/>
           </noscript>
          </div>
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="txt step-body">
        Get four milk crates, and a piece of 18mm ply to fit the van. Remember - it must accommodate the inside diameter of the van door, or: make sure you cut it small enough to fit through the door!
        <br/>
        <br/>
        You don't want it to cover the entire area, because that makes it hard to get in and out.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Then, place crates strategically and place board on top. I fill the crates with bottles of water (essential for my van which doubles as a high performance spa pool at times), tools, the jack, coolant, (as above) and other extraneous crap. This will weight them down a bit more too, as the entire arrangement does tend to leap into the rear view mirror when navigating potholes. However, once you pack the rest of your crap - surfboards and kiting gear and three square meters of sand - under the platform, they will move around less.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Step three - enjoy!
