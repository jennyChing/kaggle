        <p>
         Halloween is that time of year where you can be anyone, or anything your heart desires. Sometimes those costumes can be outrageously priced. DIY Halloween Costumes are not only a great way to save money, but a great project to get the entire family involved in.
        </p>
        <p>
         Making your own Halloween Costumes isnt as hard as you may think. You can also find inspiration by looking around your house for items that dont get much use. Thrift shops and yard sales are a good way to find materials for your DIY Halloween Costumes. Weve scoured the web for DIY Halloween Costumes and found 100 that are sure to rock your socks!
        </p>
