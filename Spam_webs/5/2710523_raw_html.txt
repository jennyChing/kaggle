       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/wp-content/uploads/5-TIPS-ON-HOW-TO-EAT-LESS.jpg">
        <img alt="5 TIPS ON HOW TO EAT LESS" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1388" height="225" src="http://www.lapizfashion.com/wp-content/uploads/5-TIPS-ON-HOW-TO-EAT-LESS-300x225.jpg" title="5 TIPS ON HOW TO EAT LESS" width="300"/>
       </a>
       Its a sad fact that many women today eat far overmuch, and while women perhaps concerned with how they look if theyve gained weight from overeating, this habit can also have serious health effects. Eating overmuch sugar can interfere with the bodys production of glucose which in turn can direct to
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/how-to-manage-diabetes-in-5-steps/" target="_blank">
        diabetes
       </a>
       . Too much deep-fried foods can clog the arteries with cholesterol and lead to heart and circulatory troubles. The body is also meant to handle only so much food at a time so overeating can lead to indigestion, acid reflex disease, and further painful conditions.
      </p>

      <p>
       Eating less is often easier said than done. Food is a commodity in various parts of the world, to be purchased and sold for a profit. The more that those food manufacturers sell, the more benefit they have. That`s why they construct food taste as good as possible with little relation to healthy ingredients, and spend millions of dollars on advertising that food. While its hard to cut back on how much you eat, it can be done.
       <strong>
        View 5 tips to get you started.
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        1. Prepare Less
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       This might look obvious, but if you prepare heaping helpings of food, youre going to eat those helpings! Get habitual to making half a sandwich for lunch with a piece of fruit, to making one egg in the morning with one piece of toast, and to cooking little for dinner and other meals overall.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        2. Fill Up on Low-Calorie Snacks Between Meals
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Frequently a person will overeat at a meal as they deprive themselves of food for hours between meals, so theyre hungry when they do sit down to eat. Have some ounces of cheese, a hardboiled egg, or a part of fruit between meals to fill up.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        3. Keep Busy
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Eating can be a hobby, something to maintain you busy or to keep you from getting bored. If youre absorbed in
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/5-ways-to-work-out-at-home/" target="_blank">
        fun hobbies
       </a>
       or go out for a walk in the evening, youre less potential to sit down and eat just to keep yourself pleased.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        4. Eat More Protein
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Protein has a chemical in it that directs a signal to the brain, saying it that youre full. Other foods dont have this chemical, so its gentler to overeat on foods that hold sugar, white flour and other starches, and oils. Think about how this is true; its hard to overeat chicken or pork but not that difficult to eat overmuch bread or candy! Stick to lean protein options so you dont increase weight or clog your arteries with cholesterol.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        5. Write Down What You Eat
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Be very careful about this; dont just imagine, but
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/why-evening-workout-is-ideal-for-you/" target="_blank">
        weigh
       </a>
       and measure your food and keep track. Once you check how much you eat daily and weekly, you perhaps very motivated to cut back to healthier portions!
      </p>
