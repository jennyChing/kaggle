You need to check-in onlineand print your boarding pass to escape penalty charges if one needs to be obtained at the airport. Emails warning of the penalties and your on-board baggage permissible weight and size are sent out to you so if you fail to comply you only have yourself to blame.
        </p>
        <p>
         Food is available in flight or you could take your own food on-board but drinking your own alcohol is not permitted. A good meal deal is usually available at the airport which would save you money against Ryanairs prices on-board.
