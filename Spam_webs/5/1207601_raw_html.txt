      <h1>
       Tour a Colorful Family Villa in Qatar Designed by Katharine Pooley
      </h1>
      <p class="meta">
       Posted July 09, 2014 in
       <a href="/daily-features/category/top-designers-at-work">
        Top Designers At Work
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/daily-features/category/house-tours">
        House Tours
       </a>
       by
       <a href="/daily-features/contributors/dering-hall">
        Dering Hall
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="desc">
       <p>
        London-based interior design firm
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/katharine-pooley">
         Katharine Pooley Limited
        </a>
        re-designed the interior of this extensive four bedroom private villa in Qatar's capital city of Doha for a young family with three children. The firm is known for its exquisite attention to detail, which can be seen in all areas of the
design, from the beautiful embroidery on the curtains and sofa panels to the
bespoke billiards table clad in shagreen to match the doors.
       </p>
