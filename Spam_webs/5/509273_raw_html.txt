                      It will better to secure
                      <b>
                       whole website
                      </b>
                      with
                      <b>
                       HTTPS (SSL Certificate)
                      </b>
                      rather that only checkout page.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Reason:
                      </b>
                      When a user visit an E-Commerce website to buy a product, user enter his details for registration such as (Name, Email Address, Product Delivery Address, Mobile Number, Age, Etc...) and during
                      <b>
                       Checkout
                      </b>
                      user enters his financial details.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      As per
                      <span class="qlink_container">
                       <a href="/Symantec">
                        <b>
                         Symantec
                        </b>
                       </a>
                      </span>
                      <b>
                       's
                      </b>
                      Report each single text, image or documents shared over internet needs security, if you only secure the
                      <b>
                       Checkout
                      </b>
                      that means you are only encrypting user's financial information. But user's registration information may compromise under
                      <b>
                       cyber attack
                      </b>
                      as you have not encrypted your whole website.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Study shows if an E-commerce website not secured with
                      <b>
                       HTTPS
                      </b>
                      , users are not felling safe to shared their financial information, this may affect website's business as well.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      So I must say it will be better to secure your whole E-Commerce website with (HTTPS) SSL Certificate.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      EV SSL Certificate is the only solution to give highest encryption level and security to your website. It goes green in all web/mobile browsers and display organization name as well which indicates the E-Commerce business is legitimated and verified by trusted Certificate authority, this improves user's trust and confidence to shared their personal and financial details with you.
