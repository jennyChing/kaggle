            <h2 class="item-description" itemprop="description" style="font-size: 12px; font-weight: bold;">
             The indictment of Bobby Shmurda and 12 other members of GS9 is now underway, with police displaying the guns the confiscated and accusing the crew of 24 shootings.
            </h2>
            <br/>
            <section itemprop="articleBody">
             <p>
              As details continue to emerge in the case of
              <a class="text-word" href="http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/bobby_shmurda/profile/" target="_blank">
               Bobby Shmurda
              </a>
              &amp; GS9, it looks even more grim for their chances of getting out of this unscathed. Their indictment is now underway, and thanks to some tweets from people inside the courtroom, we're now getting a better picture of the case.
             </p>
             <p>
              First off, Channel 4 reporter Andrew Siff gives us a picture of the 21 guns that were taken from Shmurda and Co.
             </p>
             <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" width="400">
              <p>
               Cops say these guns belonged to
               <a href="https://twitter.com/BobbyShmurdaGS9" rel="nofollow">
                @BobbyShmurdaGS9
               </a>
               and
               <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/GS9?src=hash" rel="nofollow">
                #GS9
               </a>
               gang 21 weapons seized
               <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/NBC4NY?src=hash" rel="nofollow">
                #NBC4NY
               </a>
               <a href="http://t.co/GTAYHWgjTT" rel="nofollow">
                pic.twitter.com/GTAYHWgjTT
               </a>
              </p>
               Andrew Siff (@andrewsiff4NY)
              <a href="https://twitter.com/andrewsiff4NY/status/545678544169615360" rel="nofollow">
               December 18, 2014
              </a>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              XXL then
              <a href="https://twitter.com/XXL/status/545682890420674561" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               reported
              </a>
              that the D.A. is seeking $2 million bail for Shmurda, and
              <a href="https://twitter.com/XXL/status/545682297500872705" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               alleging
              </a>
              that GS9 are crips and responsible for 24 shootings in Brooklyn. "Upon arrest," they
              <a href="https://twitter.com/XXL/status/545685156061659136" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               added
              </a>
              , "Shmurda had 2guns on him" and three more were found in the stairwell of the studio.
             </p>
             <p>
              Stay tuned for more updates.
             </p>
             <p>
              [
              <a href="http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2014/12/bobby-shmurda-2-million-bail/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               via
              </a>
              ]
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               [Update: Official Charges Released]
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2014/12/official-charges-against-bobby-shmurda-and-gs9/?utm_source=rss&amp;utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_campaign=official-charges-against-bobby-shmurda-and-gs9" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               XXL
              </a>
              now has a full list of the charges brought against the 13 members of GS9, and it's a doozy.
             </p>
             <p>
              Shmurda himself is charged with one count of conspiracy (second degree), three counts of conspiracy (fourth degree), one count of reckless endangerment (second degree), two counts of criminal possession of a weapon (second degree) and one count of criminally using drug paraphernalia (second degree), to which he pleaded "not guilty." Rowdy Rebel has been hit with one count of conspiracy (second degree), two counts of conspiracy (fourth degree), one count of attempted murder (second degree), one count of attempted assault (first degree), counts of reckless endangerment in the first and second degree, and four counts of criminal possession of a weapon (second degree).
             </p>
             <p>
              Check out a picture of GS9's literal rap sheet in the gallery above.
             </p>
             <p>
              <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="349" src="http://hnhh.co/youtube-embed/gE60v7XlJNY?show_controls=1&amp;playlist=DoEyWhpp_8A,1yEwg_wvbLU,yGp0RRB_y9A,jBIgPMzk_Oo&amp;autoplay=" width="620">
              </iframe>
             </p>
