       <section class="article-body">
        <p>
         <span>
          Drinking plays a large role on
          <em>
           Game of Thrones
          </em>
          . Not as large as violent swordplay or bare, pendulous breasts, but close.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         So in honor of the March 31st premiere, we gave three of the citys most talented chefs a few bottles of
         <a href="http://www.ommegang.com/#!limited_release">
          Iron Throne Blonde Ale
         </a>
          Brewery Ommegangs hoppy, citrusy, Lannister-inspired brew.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then we asked them to craft
         <em>
          GOT
         </em>
         -inspired recipes that pair well with the beer.
        </p>
        <p>
         And so behold, your viewing party essentials:
        </p>
        <div class="board board-300by250 board-mobile">
         <div class="board-inner" id="div-gpt-ad-1412352247885-0">
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.ommegang.com/#!limited_release">
          <img align="left" src="http://s1.insidehook.com/032513-game-of-_1363987274.jpg"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://s1.insidehook.com//pdfs/harlow_1364242074.pdf">
          Hounds Tooth Sandwich
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Created by Danny Ye,
          <a href="http://harlownyc.com/">
           Harlow
          </a>
         </em>
         <a href="http://s1.insidehook.com/rep1_(1)_1363988330.pdf">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         When the scarfaced warrior looks to quench his belly's hunger, Ye says about
         <em>
          GOT
         </em>
         s burn-scarred badass, The Hound, he turns to a man-wich. This ribeye with white kimchi and egg grinder is just the ticket, though he cautions: You may need a cigarette after.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://s1.insidehook.com//pdfs/2_GOT_1364223754.pdf">
          Crispy Artichokes with Parsley and Pancetta
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Created by Brendan McHale,
          <a href="http://www.tastingtable.com/entry_detail/national/4327/A_new_Test_Kitchen_to_call_home.htm">
           Tasting Table Test Kitchen
          </a>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         McHale was inspired by would-be king Stannis Baratheons new sigil, a stag inside a flaming heart. Frying the artichoke, youve got your flaming heart, with the pancetta standing in for the stag. Super herb-acious and funky, a great match for the fresh blonde ale.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://s1.insidehook.com//pdfs/3_GOT_1364223779.pdf">
          Beer-Braised Pork Belly with Kimchi and Tempura Cilantro
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Created by Adam Slamon,
          <a href="http://rogueandcanon.com/">
           Rogue &amp; Canon
          </a>
