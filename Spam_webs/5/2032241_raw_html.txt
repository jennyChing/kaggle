         <p>
          <a href="http://fashionforpassion.org/engagement-rings-vs-wedding-rings/engagement-rings-and-wedding-rings-set/" rel="attachment wp-att-3375">
           <img alt="Engagement Rings and Wedding Rings set" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3375" height="868" src="http://fashionforpassion.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Engagement-Rings-and-Wedding-Rings-set.jpg" width="654"/>

         <p>
          What engagement ring should always be used even if the marriage 3 years later? According Ring Fiance history indicates that the person is financially ready to marry a woman to her family. Engagement ring signifies that a woman had been requested and not open again for others. While the wedding ring is a symbol that a woman or a man is married. What do you think?
         </p>
         <p>
          Rings engagement wedding ring then? The engagement ring or wedding ring too? If you do not know if he would say Yes, buy the engagement ring. But if you have discussed marriage before applying then buy a wedding ring. The wedding ring is not worn (be given) at the time of application but worn during the wedding ceremony. Usually the wedding ring is less luxurious than the engagement ring and have the same models.
         </p>
         <p>
          When a man proposed to his partner, he gave an engagement ring, whether the man is also wearing an engagement ring? Usually only a woman wearing engagement ring to signify the status that he has been spoken for. Is it really true engagement ring wedding ring or separated from their partner? Engagementring including stand-alone ring. while a pair of wedding rings is a ring that was given at the time of the wedding and engagement rings worn together.
         </p>
         <p>
          So, it depends on each individual whether to buy an engagement ring or wedding ring directly. But keep in mind that the wedding ring used only during the wedding ceremony. Its okay to apply with a wedding ring when it is definitely. If you intend to postpone the wedding engagement ring purchase.
         </p>
         <p>
          Happy Wedding!
