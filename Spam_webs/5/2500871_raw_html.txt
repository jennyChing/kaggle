       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Samsung Electronics America, Inc., is announcing their collaboration with Ubisoft, developer of Assassins Creed and numerous other popular games, to offer a free download of their latest game, Assassins Creed Unity, with the purchase of either a Samsung UD590 28 monitor, or with the purchase of a Samsung 850 PRO series SSD. These Samsung PC peripherals are ideal for gaming, and allow gamers to enjoy a much more immersive gaming experience with games such as Ubisofts Assassins Creed Unity.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-LfaM]" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-group="22414" data-slb-internal="0" href="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Assassins-creed-unity-banner-4.png">
         <img alt="Assassins creed unity banner 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22433" height="274" src="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Assassins-creed-unity-banner-4.png" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        The UD590s 4K ultra-high definition screen offers gaming enthusiasts a stunning visual experience. At a resolution of 38402610, the UD590 has four times the pixels and resolution of standard full HD displays. The 28 screen features eight million pixels, creating one of the most detailed and clearest pictures ever. Its one billion colors is 64 times that of conventional monitors, and its one millisecond response time creates the smoothest action, even in the fastest moving scenes.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-LfaM]" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-group="22414" data-slb-internal="0" href="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Sasmung-U28D590D-monitor.png">
         <img alt="Sasmung U28D590D monitor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22420" height="413" src="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Sasmung-U28D590D-monitor.png" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        According to Ron Gazzola, vice president of marketing for Samsung Electronics America, Our monitors create a one-of-kind, immersive gaming experience and our SSDs ensure quick load times and robust performance for drastically improved gameplay. The UD590 premier monitor and the 850 PRO SSD will bring Assassins Creed Unity to life and allow players to experience the game in a completely new way. Were happy to be providing this extremely popular offering to our customers.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-LfaM]" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-group="22414" data-slb-internal="0" href="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/assassins-creed-logo.png">
         <img alt="assassins-creed-logo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22434" height="349" src="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/assassins-creed-logo.png" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Samsungs 850 PRO SSD gives gamers a more engaging and faster playing experience, offering enhanced graphic and visual effects, and loads game levels twice as fast as mechanical hard disk drives (HDDs). Choppy play and slow performance are eliminated by the drives superior multi-tasking capabilities. The Samsung 850 PRO SSD allow gamers to spend less time waiting, and more time playing. Featuring Samsungs ground-breaking V-NAND technology, the 850 PRO delivers the highest level of performance of any SSD utilizing the SATA III (6Gb/s) interface.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-LfaM]" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-group="22414" data-slb-internal="0" href="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Sasmung-SSD-850-PRO.png">
         <img alt="Sasmung SSD 850 PRO" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22421" height="914" src="//techxcdn2.thessdreview1.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Sasmung-SSD-850-PRO.png" width="620"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        The Assassins Creed Unity download is available with the purchase of the 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB capacities of the Samsung 850 PRO SSD. The 850 PRO SSD has an MSRP of $229.99 for the 256GB capacity, $429.99 for the 512GB capacity, and $699.99 for the 1TB capacity. The UD590 monitor is available now at an MSRP of $599.99
       </p>
