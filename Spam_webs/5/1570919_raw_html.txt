        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         Russian Kulich
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This month the theme at Bread Bakers isEaster, Passover and Springtime Breads from Around the World. I decided to try a Russian Kulich bread that is popular at Easter that I found over at
         <a href="http://www.razzledazzlerecipes.com" target="_blank">
          Razzle Dazzle Recipes
         </a>
         that Id been meaning to make. We were also challenged to make a bread we hadnt made before and this fits the bill.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This bread still highlights Easter in Russia. People bring a loaf to church for the priest to bless and then serve it as part of dessert following Easter Dinner. The frosted top represents a church dome with snow on it. It is supposed to be baked in a 1 lb. coffee can and I didnt have one so I used a 9 inch cake pan instead.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://thatsmyhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/kulich-3f.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>

             <p class="summary italic">
              Russian Kulich is a sweet yeast bread made with candied fruit, cardamon and almonds. It is popular in Russia at Easter and is served with dessert.
             </p>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tablespoon active dry yeast
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup warm water (105-115)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup warm milk (105-115)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 cup sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 teaspoon almond extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 teaspoon salt
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1-1/2 tablespoons grated lemon peel
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             2-3 cups flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 beaten eggs
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cups slivered almonds
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup chopped candied mixed fruit
            </li>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14">
             <span class="bold">
              Icing
             </span>
            </div>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16" itemprop="ingredients">
             about 1 tablespoon water
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 teaspoon softened butter
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
             couple of drops almond extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cups toasted slivered almonds
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Dissolve yeast in warm water. Set aside for 5 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Combine warm milk, sugar, butter, vanilla, almond extract, cardamom, salt, and lemon peel in a
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             large bowl. Add 1-1/2 cups flour, yeast mixture, and eggs. Mix thoroughly.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add almonds and candied fruit. Add enough remaining flour to make a soft dough.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Knead on lightly floured surface until smoothabout 10 minutes. Place in greased bowl, turning to coat top. Cover; let rise in warm place until doubleabout 1-1/2 hours. Punch down dough. Shape into a ball; try to get a nicely rounded top. Place in well-greased 1-pound coffee can. Cover; let rise in warm place until doubleabout 45 minutes. (I baked it in a 9 inch cake pan.)
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake in a preheated 350F over 35-40 minutes or until done. Cool on wire rack.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Mix thoroughly confectioners' sugar, water and butter. Spoon icing over top of kulich. Top with the roasted almonds.
            </li>
           </ol>
