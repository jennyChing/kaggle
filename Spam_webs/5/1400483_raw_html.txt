        <h1 class="entry-title">
         National Tire Safety Week
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           By
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         When youre in the tire business like RightTurn is, youre also in the safety business. Thats why we go to great lengths to educate our readers and customers on safety. Right now through June 8 is a particularly good time to talk about tire safety. Thats because its the Rubber Manufacturers Associations (RMA) National Tire Safety Week.
        </p>
        <p>
         Why have a National Tire Safety Week? Well, tires are the only parts of a passenger vehicle that actually touch the road. All of a vehicles safety and performance transfer from the vehicle to the road through the tires. For that reason, its important to bring awareness to the many safety issues regarding tires.
        </p>
        <h2>
         About the RMA
        </h2>
        <p>
         Founded in 1915, the RMA is the national trade association for US tire manufacturers. The RMA has grown to be an excellent source of information for its various members and for the general public. On
         <a href="http://www.rma.org/index.cfm" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.rma.org']);" target="_blank" title="rma.org">
          the RMA website
         </a>
         , you can find a great deal of safety resources and educational materials about tires. You can even order a free National Tire Safety Week kit.
        </p>
        <h2>
         About National Tire Safety Week
        </h2>
        <p>
         For this years National Tire Safety Week, the RMA website has everything from brochures that explain how to perform basic tire maintenance to
         <a href="https://www.rma.org/tire_safety/tire_maintenance_and_safety/quizzes/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.rma.org']);" target="_blank" title="rma.org | Tire Safety Quizzes">
          tire safety quizzes
         </a>
         . (No big deal, but I got a 100%. What did you get?) If you want to delve even deeper into the world of tires, you can find out how tires are made and whats inside a tire.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are some pretty shocking statistics on the National Tire Safety Week website. For example, fewer than half of all drivers check the condition of their tires on a regular basis. That gives you an idea of why its so smart to take some time to learn about your tires and how to help maximize their safety benefits.
        </p>
        <h2>
         RightTurn &amp; National Tire Safety Week
        </h2>
        <p>
         Here at RightTurn, were supporting National Tire Safety Week by bringing you some important facts and simple tire maintenance instructions. Visit
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/RightTurnNow?fref=ts" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.facebook.com']);" title="RightTurn on Facebook">
          our Facebook page
         </a>
         and like us to get tire safety facts every day this week. Be sure to share the info with your friends and loved ones. Remember, sharing is caring! (No seriously, it is. Especially when you share something that can help make someones life safer.)
        </p>
