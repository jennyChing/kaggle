        <p>
         Dear fellow CPA studier,
        </p>
        <p>
         (Im done with the exams- but I consider us colleagues)
        </p>
        <p>
         Are you wondering how you should study for the CPA exam?
        </p>
        <p>
         Or which CPA study guides you should use?
        </p>
        <p>
         The truth is, never before has there been as high-quality CPA study guides and review courses as we have right now. You can watch video lectures from your laptop wherever youre at, and you can access test banks with thousands of questions with high-quality answer explanations.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thank goodness we dont just have textbooks with thousands of questions in them we have to slog through at the same time the CPA exams are still incredibly difficult to study for and pass.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pass rates stay the same or are even lower in some cases then they were a few years ago
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://d881dkrbn6rgf4p6v72bscdv0h.hop.clickbank.net/?tid=SBCPAPROS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <em>
           How to study for the CPA exams
          </em>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <b>
          How to study for the CPA exams
         </b>
         
        </p>
        <p>
         The truth is, the old way of studying is not the best way. You probably dont have a year or more of your life that you want to flush down the toilet trying to pass the CPA exams
        </p>
        <p>
         I studied for FAR the old way the first time I took it, and I spent months studying only to fail. After that attempt, I made up my mind that I was going to figure out a way to study less and pass faster I refused to let the CPA exams take me the typical 9 to 15 months to pass.
        </p>
        <p>
         No way.
        </p>
        <p>
         While I was taking the test, seeing it in person gave me several aha moments about the best way to study for the other CPA exam sections.
        </p>
        <p>
         After that first attempt, I implemented my crazy ideas and I ended up passing all 4 sections very quickly, and I didnt fail another section.
        </p>
        <p>
         When people at my firm found out how fast I had passed, I started getting a lot of questions about how I did it. (you see, Im not one of the sharpest but I did finish my exams before the sharpest kids I got hired with, and thats why people were shocked)
        </p>
        <p>
         So Ive taken my exact CPA study tips, study strategies, and study methods, and put them all in this course for you
         <span style="text-decoration: underline">
          How to study for the CPA exams
         </span>
         -Superfast CPA system.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://d881dkrbn6rgf4p6v72bscdv0h.hop.clickbank.net/?tid=SBCPAPROS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           pass the CPA exam
          </span>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         This course will literally help you shave entire months off of how long it takes you to pass the CPA exams
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         WARNING: The study methods are very different than what youve been taught, and it will be a little uncomfortable when you first put these methods into practice. But like anything else, youll get used to it, and youll definitely get used to all the time you save.
        </p>
        <h4 class="box-title">
         <a href="http://d881dkrbn6rgf4p6v72bscdv0h.hop.clickbank.net/?tid=SBCPAPROS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Superfast CPA system
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Heres some of what youll learn in the Superfast CPA system
         <br/>
         Youll learn the optimal order to take the sections, and why taking certain sections first will make the other sections easier
         <br/>
         Youll learn the several reasons why the traditional way of studying is flawed, and how to approach the entire study process in a much more effective and efficient way
         <br/>
         Youll learn the correct balance of practicing questions and watching lectures that will eliminate wasted time and zoning out
         <br/>
         Youll learn how to breeze through most lessons in minutes instead of hours, and end up knowing the material even better
        </p>
