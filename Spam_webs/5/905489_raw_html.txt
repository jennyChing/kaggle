       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         3-Ingredient Banana Ice Cream
        </h1>
        <a class="YUMMLY-YUM-BUTTON" href="//yummly.com" rel="nofollow">
         Yum
        </a>
        <script src="https://www.yummly.com/js/widget.js?wordpress">
        </script>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-05-29T15:20:12+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 29, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://wifemomgeek.com/author/Donella Crigger/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Donella Crigger
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://wifemomgeek.com/3-ingredient-banana-ice-cream/#comments">
           13 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-icecream-pin.jpg">
          <img alt="3 Ingredient Banana Ice Cream" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-full wp-image-1875" data-lazy-src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-icecream-pin.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="1050" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="700"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="3 Ingredient Banana Ice Cream" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1875" height="1050" src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-icecream-pin.jpg" width="700"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Its in the 80s here, and we didnt have any ice cream. Gasp! I decided to take matters into my own hands and make some myself. Technically, this banana ice cream isnt real ice cream. Its mashed bananas, marshmallows and a splash of milk. But if you love bananas, youll love this frozen treat.
         <span id="more-1874">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-ice-cream-nochoc.jpg">
          <img alt="banana-ice-cream-nochoc" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-full wp-image-1876" data-lazy-src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-ice-cream-nochoc.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="467" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="700"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="banana-ice-cream-nochoc" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1876" height="467" src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-ice-cream-nochoc.jpg" width="700"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its super simple to make. First, cut three bananas up into large chunks. Cover them with plastic wrap, and put them in the freezer for about 3 hours.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/chopped-bananas.jpg">
          <img alt="chopped-bananas" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-full wp-image-1877" data-lazy-src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/chopped-bananas.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="467" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="700"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="chopped-bananas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1877" height="467" src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/chopped-bananas.jpg" width="700"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, put the chopped bananas, marshmallows and a couple splashes of milk into the blender a little bit at a time. I used 10 regular-sized marshmallows total, but you could use about 1 cup of mini marshmallows, too. Just leave them out of the blender and mix them in after your bananas are pulsed.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mashedbanans.jpg">
          <img alt="mashedbananas" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-full wp-image-1878" data-lazy-src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mashedbanans.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="467" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="700"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="mashedbananas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1878" height="467" src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/mashedbanans.jpg" width="700"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If marshmallows arent your thing, add some nuts or chocolate chips or vanilla cookies. Some peanut butter would be tasty, too. You cant go wrong, really.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once everything is mixed up, put it in the freezer (again, covered with plastic wrap) for an additional 2-3 hours.
        </p>
        <p>
         Serve with chocolate syrup. YUM!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-ice-cream.jpg">
          <img alt="banana-ice-cream" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-full wp-image-1879" data-lazy-src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-ice-cream.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="467" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="700"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="banana-ice-cream" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1879" height="467" src="http://wifemomgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/banana-ice-cream.jpg" width="700"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
        </p>

          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 ripe bananas
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            10 regular-sized marshmallows
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2-3 splashes of milk as needed
           </li>
          </ul>

          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Chop bananas into large chunks.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place plastic wrap over the banana chunks, and freeze for 3 hours.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add banana chunks, marshmallows and a splash of milk to a blender a little at a time.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pulse until mostly smooth (You don't want the marshmallows to disintegrate entirely).
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Put back in the freezer for 2-3 hours, or until ice cream is firm.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Top with chocolate sauce, whipped cream, nuts or other toppings.
           </li>
          </ol>
