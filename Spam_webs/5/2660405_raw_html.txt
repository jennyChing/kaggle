          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Skype Partners with DonorsChoose.org for Better Classrooms
          </h1>
          <p class="meta-line">
           <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://www.techvocal.com/2012/03/22/" rel="bookmark" title="12:08 pm">
            <i class="icon-left fa fa-calendar-o">
            </i>
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2012-03-22T12:08:02+00:00" itemprop="startDate">
             March 22, 2012
            </time>
           </a>
           <span class="meta-label comment-label">
            <i class="fa fa-comment">
            </i>
            <a href="http://www.techvocal.com/skype-partners-with-donorschoose-org-for-better-classrooms/#respond">
             Comment
             <i class="">
             </i>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <span class="smartlib-category-line">
           <a href="http://www.techvocal.com/category/tech-news/" rel="category tag">
            Tech News
           </a>
          </span>
         </header>
         <div class="smartlib-single-image-container">
          <img alt="Skype Partners with DonorsChoose.org for Better Classrooms" class="attachment-wide-image wp-post-image" height="281" src="http://www.techvocal.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Skype-Partners-with-DonorsChoose.org-for-Better-Classr.jpg" width="400"/>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           There are many technology companies that are working hard to bring better
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.techvocal.com/category/gadgets/" target="_blank" title="Gadgets">
             gadgets
            </a>
           </strong>
           and equipments to the classrooms and now Skype has decided to take a step forward in that direction. Recently,
           <a href="http://blogs.skype.com/en/2012/03/skype_and_donorschooseorg_part.html" target="_blank" title="Skype has announced their partnership with DonorsChoose.org">
            Skype has announced their partnership with DonorsChoose.org
           </a>
           that would allow people to provide online charity to teachers and students that need help. Skype has already been providing Skype in the classroom to various students and schools across the globe where millions of students get education through online technologies.
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.techvocal.com/tag/skype/" target="_blank" title="Skype">
             Skype
            </a>
           </strong>
           did mention that they are trying to make better technologies that can help students and teachers to reach various organizations and firms for their help.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.techvocal.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Skype-Partners-with-DonorsChoose.org-for-Better-Classr.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2478" height="281" src="http://www.techvocal.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Skype-Partners-with-DonorsChoose.org-for-Better-Classr.jpg" title="Skype Partners with DonorsChoose.org for Better Classrooms" width="400"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Sharing more information about their new partnership with
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.donorschoose.org/" target="_blank" title="DonorsChoose.org">
             DonorsChoose.org
            </a>
           </strong>
           the company said that with this project they will be providing $250,000 in the hands of various teachers so that they can come up with various classrooms that can provide better learning experiences. DonorsChoose.org is also asking donors to provide laptops, netbooks and webcams to all those students that need these gadgets so that they can enjoy virtual classrooms. On the other hand, Skype is also working on how they can build better funding projects that can help students in various countries to have positive impact of the virtual education and classrooms that they have provided.
          </p>
         </div>
