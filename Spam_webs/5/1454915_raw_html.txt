            <h2>
             <span class="goog-text-highlight">
              More light  more space
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>

             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Large windows provide excellent lighting in the daytime, and light, in turn, visually expands the space of the room.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Wall with bookshelves
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/wall-with-bookshelves/" rel="attachment wp-att-4861">

            </p>
            <p>
             The wall that serves as a bookcase, is a great option for small apartments, where every detail is extremely important to make the most useful and functional.
            </p>

             <span class="goog-text-highlight">
              Translucent walls
             </span>
            </h2>

            </p>

            <p>

            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/open/" rel="attachment wp-att-4866">
              <img alt="Open" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4866" height="410" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Open-600x410.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             By combining a few small areas of premises, you can organize a spacious combined room that will serve several functions.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Attic as an extra room
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/attic-as-an-extra-room/" rel="attachment wp-att-4867">
              <img alt="Attic as an extra room" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4867" height="389" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Attic-as-an-extra-room-600x389.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             For smaller homes are characterized by a loft that can be converted, if desired, under the additional bed or a modest relaxation room.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Retractable Ceiling Bed
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/retractable-ceiling-bed/" rel="attachment wp-att-4868">
              <img alt="Retractable Ceiling Bed" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4868" height="700" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Retractable-Ceiling-Bed.jpg" width="524"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span class="goog-text-highlight">
              Standard bed takes up too much space and is often unaffordable luxury for the owners of small apartments.
             </span>
             Retractable bed located on the ceiling, is unusual and, at the same time convenient option for small spaces.
            </p>
            <h2>
             <span class="goog-text-highlight">
              The space under the stairs
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/the-space-under-the-stairs/" rel="attachment wp-att-4869">
              <img alt="The space under the stairs" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4869" height="600" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/The-space-under-the-stairs-600x600.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/the-space-under-the-stairs-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-4870">
              <img alt="The space under the stairs" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4870" height="700" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/The-space-under-the-stairs1.jpg" width="477"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span class="goog-text-highlight">
              In small private homes too much useless space remains under the stairs.
             </span>
             Resourceful designers have proposed to use it for recreation areas or placing additional drawers and lockers.
            </p>
            <h2>
             <span class="goog-text-highlight">
              Folding doors
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/folding-doors/" rel="attachment wp-att-4871">
              <img alt="Folding doors" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4871" height="432" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Folding-doors-600x432.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             For swing doors must be prudent to leave some free space, so that they can open and close. Sliding interior doors  is not only beautiful, but also very practical.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Glass walls
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/glass-walls/" rel="attachment wp-att-4872">
              <img alt="Glass walls" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4872" height="399" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Glass-walls-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Fully transparent walls visually enlarge the space of small floor space. On the other hand, you can forget about privacy.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Mirror  elegant designer trick
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/mirror-elegant-designer-trick/" rel="attachment wp-att-4873">
              <img alt="Mirror - elegant designer trick" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4873" height="700" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Mirror-elegant-designer-trick.jpg" width="466"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             To visually enlarge a small space living room, you need to hang a large mirror, which will reflect the window.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Plants  extra paint in interior
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/plants-extra-paint-in-interior/" rel="attachment wp-att-4874">
              <img alt="Plants - extra paint in interior" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4874" height="450" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Plants-extra-paint-in-interior-600x450.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Apart in the living plants in pots, you can paint and a little spice up the interior. In this case, the main thing is not to overdo it, so as not to turn the room into a greenhouse.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Kitchen cabinet with open shelves
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/kitchen-cabinet-with-open-shelves/" rel="attachment wp-att-4875">
              <img alt="Kitchen cabinet with open shelves" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4875" height="700" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Kitchen-cabinet-with-open-shelves.jpg" width="500"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Large bookcase with open shelves  extra storage of kitchen utensils, as well as an incentive for training organization and attention to detail.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Partitions instead of walls
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/partitions-instead-of-walls/" rel="attachment wp-att-4877">
              <img alt="Partitions instead of walls" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4877" height="600" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Partitions-instead-of-walls-600x600.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/partitions-instead-of-walls-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-4878">
              <img alt="Partitions instead of walls" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4878" height="372" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Partitions-instead-of-walls-600x372.jpeg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Replacing the bulky original wall partitions in the form of book shelves or shelves with plants, you can transform the interior is interesting and visually expand the space.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Curtains instead of walls
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/curtains-instead-of-walls/" rel="attachment wp-att-4879">
              <img alt="Curtains instead of walls" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4879" height="450" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Curtains-instead-of-walls-600x450.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/creative-decoration/15-great-ideas-saving-living-space/attachment/curtains-instead-of-walls-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-4880">
              <img alt="Curtains instead of walls" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4880" height="646" src="http://interiordesignsmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Curtains-instead-of-walls1-600x646.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
