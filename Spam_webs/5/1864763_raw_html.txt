          <h2>
           The Hello Kitty Pop Up Pirate Toy is Barrels of Fun
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="references">
          <span class="mobileAuthor">
           By: Angela Rajic-
          </span>
          Published:
          <span content="2013-12-12" itemprop="datePublished">
           Dec 12, 2013

           References:

           strapya-world

         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="articleText">
         In the 1970s Pop Up Pirate was a very popular game with children, and now it's getting a new lease on life thanks to this Hello Kitty Pop Up Pirate game.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The game's redesign with the popular feline anime character spices up the barrel and the game pieces with a lot of pink and bows. The players must slide pink-bowed plastics swords into the slots of a hot-pink barrel until the pirate (fully decked out in a pink bandanna and heart-shaped eye patch) pops out of the barrel.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         This Hello Kitty Pop Up Pirate game is great fun for small children, and the Kitty theme will attract many more girls to these extremely entertaining game. A fun and creative mashup, this Pop Up Pirate game is a great tribute to a retro favorite.
