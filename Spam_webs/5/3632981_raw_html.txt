      <a href="http://jazzyvegetarian.com/blog.html/oat_and_date_breakfast_cupcakes/">
       <h2>
        <span class="news_title">
         Oat and Date Breakfast Cupcakes!
        </span>
       </h2>
      </a>
      <!-- Meta data -->
      <span class="date">
       Posted on April 19, 2014
      </span>
      <!-- Post text -->
      <div class="notes">
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="EASTER_CUPCAKES_WEB_1.jpg" height="289" src="http://www.jazzyvegetarian.com/images/EASTER_CUPCAKES_WEB_1.jpg" title="EASTER_CUPCAKES_WEB_1.jpg" width="446"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <span style="color: #ff6600;">
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Oat and Date Breakfast Cupcakes
          </span>
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Makes 6 Cupcakes
       </p>
       <p>
        ShhhI wont tell if you don't. These festive little breakfast cupcakes are chock-full of
        <em>
         good for you
        </em>
        ingredients like oats, bananas and dates, but they taste decadently delicious! Oh yes  they take less than ten minutes to prep for the oven, making your morning meal a breeze to prepare!
       </p>
       <p>
        2 medium ripe bananas
       </p>
       <p>
        3 tablespoons maple syrup
       </p>
       <p>
        2/3 cup rolled oats
       </p>
       <p>
        1/3 cup unsweetened shredded dried coconut
       </p>
       <p>
        2 tablespoons raw unsalted sunflower seeds
       </p>
       <p>
        6 medjool dates, pitted and diced
       </p>
       <p>
        Preheat the oven 400 degrees F. Line a six-cup standard muffin tin with paper liners.
       </p>
       <p>
        Put the bananas and the maple syrup in a large bowl and mash with a potato masher until smooth. Add the oats, coconut, sunflower seeds and diced dates and stir to combine.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="EASTER_BLOG_DATES.jpg" src="http://www.jazzyvegetarian.com/images/EASTER_BLOG_DATES.jpg" title="EASTER_BLOG_DATES.jpg"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Mound the mixture into the prepared muffin cups using a round scoop or spoon. Bake for 20 to 24 minutes, or until the cupcakes are slightly golden on top. Put the tin on a wire rack and let cool for 12 to 15 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="EASTER_CUPCAKES_WEB_3.jpg" height="270" src="http://www.jazzyvegetarian.com/images/EASTER_CUPCAKES_WEB_3.jpg" title="EASTER_CUPCAKES_WEB_3.jpg" width="446"/>
       </p>
