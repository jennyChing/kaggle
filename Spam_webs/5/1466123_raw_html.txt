         <a class="embed-infog" data-link="http://blog.mint.com/consumer-iq/say-yes-to-paying-less-for-a-wedding-dress-0513/?cid=soc_infogrph-embed_Say+Yes+to+Paying+Less+for+a+Wedding+Dress " href="http://blog.mint.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Say-Yes-to-Paying-Less-for-a-Wedding-Dress-Mint.com_1.jpg" title="http://blog.mint.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Say-Yes-to-Paying-Less-for-a-Wedding-Dress-Mint.com_1.jpg">
          <span>
           Embed Infographic
          </span>
         </a>
        </li>
       </ul>
      </figcaption>
     </figure>
     <p dir="ltr">
      First comes love, then comes marriage. Then comes the mountain of wedding debt youll now have to pay.
     </p>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Last year, couples spent an average of $28,427 on weddings and nuptial-related events, according to
      <a href="http://blog.theknot.com/2013/03/07/average-wedding-cost-2013/" target="_blank" title="Opens in new window: http://blog.theknot.com/2013/03/07/average-wedding-cost-2013/">
       TheKnot.com
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p dir="ltr">
      While the venues, the engagement ring and even the entertainmentcan costthousands of dollars, the wardrobe isnt far down the list.
     </p>
     <p dir="ltr">
      The average cost of the dress: more than $1,200!
     </p>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Here are some tips to saying yes to paying less for a wedding dress:
     </p>
     <h2 dir="ltr">
      Online
     </h2>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Dozens ofwebsites offer new wedding dresses tailored to your taste and your budget. Some examples:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         TheKnot.com
        </strong>
        . Sign up for the free website to browse thousands of dresses. The site offers a custom search that can browse popular collections and sort by price. You can also search for dresses for other members of the wedding party.
       </p>
      </li>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         Brides.com
        </strong>
        . This site also offers a custom search,andhastips on how to find the right dress for your body type.
       </p>
      </li>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         BestBridalPrices.com
        </strong>
        . While the search isnt as user-friendly as the other two sites, there are more options for dresses that cost $200 or less.
       </p>
      </li>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         Amazon.com
        </strong>
        . If you didnt think Amazon had wedding gowns, you were wrong.
       </p>
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Chances are, youregoing to wear your gown onlyonce. Buy one secondhand and thensell itafter you say I do. Even if you have to pay for dry cleaning, its still less expensive than a brand-new dress.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         OnceWed.com.
        </strong>
        Grab designer dresses without paying designer prices. I found at least seven Vera Wang dresses for less than $300.
       </p>
      </li>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         PreOwnedWeddingDresses.com
        </strong>
        . I found dressesbyCarolina Herrerafor as low as $600. Note that sellers pay a one-time $25 fee for posting the listing.
       </p>
      </li>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         Tradesy.com
        </strong>
        . This site was formerly known asRecycled Bride.
       </p>
      </li>
      <li dir="ltr">
       <p dir="ltr">
        <strong>
         Ebay.com
        </strong>
        .Narrow your search by price, size and color. For some, you may have the option to bid orbuy it now.Check out the groovy vintage dresses in your search.
       </p>
      </li>
     </ul>
     <h2 dir="ltr">
      Rent
     </h2>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Some bridal shops that sell dresses also rent them, although there are also specialized boutiques that offer rental-only dresses.
     </p>
     <p dir="ltr">
      While renting eliminates the hassle of selling a dress you bought,look out for a damage deposit oftena $200 upfront fee.
     </p>
     <h2 dir="ltr">
      Around town
     </h2>
     <ul dir="ltr">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Consignment shop.
       </strong>
       Keep a lookout forconsignment shops that specialize in wedding dresses.
      </li>
      <li>
       <strong>
        Thrift store.
       </strong>
       This may take some extra digging, but secondhand stores carry all kinds oftreasures, and wedding dresses are no exception. Use
       <a href="http://www.thethriftshopper.com/" target="_blank" title="Opens in new window: http://www.thethriftshopper.com/">
        The Thrift Shopper
       </a>
       to search for a store near you.
      </li>
      <li>
       <strong>
        Discount department stores.
       </strong>
       If youre looking for a nontraditional dress, browse Ross, Marshalls, T.J. Maxx and even Target for less expensive dresses that arent strictlythe wedding type. A less formal dress can work,depending on the ceremony setting.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <h2 dir="ltr">
      From the family
     </h2>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Does your mother, grandmotherorsister have a wedding dress she kept? What about friends who have tied the knot? You could land adress for only thecost ofdry cleaning anda bit oftailoring.
     </p>
     <p dir="ltr">
      Wheredid you getan affordablewedding dress? Tell us your story in the comments below.
     </p>
