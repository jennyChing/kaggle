         We have created a visual step by step guide to help you learn about the basics of crochet. Whether youre new to stitching and would like to learn how to crochet or if youre a knitter looking to diversify your skills today well teach you how to get started crocheting!
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/howtocrochet.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3708" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/howtocrochet.jpg" height="970" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="800"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to Crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3708" height="970" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/howtocrochet.jpg" width="800"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Make A Slip Knot
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Before we begin well need to start with a slip knot. Hold a loop of yarn in your right hand, with the yarns crossed. Pick up the strand that is still attached to the ball. This is underneath the cut end of the yarn and is called the tail.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/1-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3693" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/1-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3693" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/1-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         Pinch the strand of yarn thats still attached to your ball of yarn and pull through the loop on your right hand, holding the ends in your left hand.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9-1.jpg">
          <br/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3694" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3694" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         Place this loop on your crochet hook, youve created a slip knot! Pull the ends of the yarn to tighten the knot on your
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/176" target="_blank">
          crochet hook
         </a>
         . Now were ready to crochet.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/4-1.jpg">
          <br/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/3-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3695" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/3-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3695" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/3-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Chain Stitch
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 1: Bring the yarn over your
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/176" target="_blank">
          crochet hook
         </a>
         , making sure that the yarn is coming from behind the hook and towards you over the hook.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/4-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3696" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/4-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3696" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/4-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 2: Pull this loop through the stitch on your hook. Youve chained one stitch!
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/5-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3697" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/5-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3697" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/5-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Continue to chain stitch until you have enough stitches for your pattern, or youre happy with the width of your crochet piece.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/6-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3698" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/6-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3698" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/6-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Single Crochet: First Row
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 1: Skip the first stitch down from your hook, and insert your crochet hook into the second stitch from the hook, inserting through the center of the stitch.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/7-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3699" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/7-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3699" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/7-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 2: Bringthe
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/8" target="_blank">
          yarn
         </a>
         over the hook, making sure that the yarn is coming from behind the hook and towards you.
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3700" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3700" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 3: Pull this loop through your chain stitch, youll now have two stitches on your crochet hook.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3701" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3701" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 4: Bring the yarn over the crochet hook again, then pull the loop through both stitches on your hook.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3702" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3702" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 5: Youll be left with one stitch on your hook, youve just completed your first single crochet stitch! Insert your hook into the next stitch, and repeat from Step 2 until youve worked a row of single crochet into all of your chain stitches.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/11-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3703" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/11-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3703" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/11-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Single Crochet: Next Row
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This is what your piece should look like after completing your first single crochet row.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/12-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3704" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/12-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3704" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/12-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 1: Turn your work, and chain 1 stitch by bringing the yarn over the hook, then pulling it through the stitch on your hook. This will give height to the edge of your work so that youll have straight edges and clean corners.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/13-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3705" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/13-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3705" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/13-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step 2: Insert your hook into the first stitch of the first single crochet row, going through the center so that youll have two strands over your hook. Single crochet as before, going back to Steps2-5 of the Single Crochet First Row.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/14-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3706" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/14-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3706" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/14-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         All consecutive rows are started like the this, and the single crochet is the same throughout. Its important to remember that on the first row you skip the first chain stitch, and then on consecutive rows you chain 1 stitch at the beginning of each row.
        </p>
        <p>
         To finish cut your yarn, and pull the stitch on your hook up until the cut end of the yarn comes out the other end of your work.
         <a href="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/15-1.jpg">
          <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3707" data-lazy-src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/15-1.jpg" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="900"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="How to crochet by Alpaca Direct" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3707" height="596" src="http://blog.alpacadirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/15-1.jpg" width="900"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         Dont forget when learning a new skill it will take some practice. Soon youll be crocheting like a pro! Have you tried crochet? Wed love to hear your thoughts on learning to crochet.
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-after-post" style="display: none !important;">
