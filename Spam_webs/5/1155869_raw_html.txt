          <p>
           <strong>
            17.03
           </strong>
           The spaceship travelled over 60,000 miles today before safely touching down in the Pacific Ocean.
          </p>
          <p>
           The US navy will now help us recover Orion and bring it back to either the USS Anchorage or USNS Salvor.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="" copyright="NASA" declared-caption="" height="287" name="capsule sea" photographer="" refid="3129627" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03129/capsule_sea_3129627c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>

            16.41 Reuters
           </strong>
           reports from Cape Canaveral on a "near-bullseye" landing:
          </p>

           <i>
            <img align="left" alt="Quote" height="40" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/01817/quotes_1817837a.gif" width="45"/>
            Orion survived a searing, plunge through the atmosphere, heating up to 4,000 degree Fahrenheit (2,200 degree Celsius) - twice as hot as molten lave - and experiencing gravitational forces eight times stronger than Earth's.
           </i>
          </p>
          <p>
           <i>
            Over the next few minutes, a total of 11 parachutes deployed to slow Orion's descent, including three gigantic main chutes that guided the spaceship to a 20-mph (32 km/h) splash-down 630 miles (1,014 km) southwest of San Diego, California, at 11:29 a.m. EST (1629 GMT).
           </i>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16.33

          </p>

          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16.29
           </strong>
           100 feet to splashdown... and Orion has landed.
          </p>
          <p>
           Confirmation coming through that it is upright in the water.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Nasa commentator's metaphors are becoming a little more eccentric - "A bullseye splashdown for America's newest spacecraft"
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16.28
           </strong>
           Perfect conditions at ocean level for the landing, with low winds reported. Orion is now passing down through the clouds.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="" declared-caption="" height="287" name="orion chute" photographer="" refid="3129552" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03129/orion_chute_3129552c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16.26
           </strong>
           Amazing footage as Orion sheds its initial parachutes and releases the three main parachutes, slowing further and approaching the Earth's surface.
          </p>
          <p>
           "There is your new spaceship America", says Nasa.
          </p>
          <p>
           It is now at 4,000 feet.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16.24
           </strong>
           Orion is descending. It has slowed to sub-sonic speed - i.e. below the speed of sound - and is preparing to jettison its forward cover, allowing the release of parachutes (see
           <strong>
            15.52
           </strong>
           ). It is now at 35,000 feet.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="Orion re-entry simulation" copyright="NASA" declared-caption="" height="287" name="orion entry" photographer="" refid="3129539" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03129/orion_entry_3129539c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16.18
           </strong>
           Time to splashdown is in seven minutes, with thrusters now being fired to maintain control of Orion as it reenters the Earth's atmosphere. It is currently at 120,000 feet and is experiencing 3Gs of force, which is expected to rise to 8.3G during reentry.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            15.52
           </strong>
           Orion's landing in the Pacific is due in just over half an hour.
          </p>
          <p>
           So how does it work? As the below video explains, the landing component of the spaceship - in which astronauts would be located - will use 11 parachutes at different stages of descent to slow down sufficiently for a safe landing.
          </p>

          </p>

            15.24
           </strong>
           Orion is Nasas first new vehicle for space travel since the Space Shuttle programme was terminated in 2011. Nasa is hoping to put astronauts back on the Moon by 2020. After that, it is hoped that Orion will journey to Mars in the 2030s.
          </p>
          <p>
           Astronaut Rex Walheim, who was aboard the final shuttle flight in 2011, said the Orion programme could one day ensure humans become a multi-planetary species.
          </p>
          <p>
           You have that excitement back here at the Kennedy Space Center and its tinged with even more excitement with whats coming down the road, he said.

           Here's a breakdown of today's mission and some key facts:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
           The Orion craft will circle the Earth twice before re-entering the atmosphere at 20,000 mph.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
           It is the first mission since the Apollo moon landings to take a spacecraft built for manned flight into deep space beyond the limit of orbiting satellites.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
           During the 4.5-hour flight Orion will travel 3,609 miles from Earth, 15 times the distance to the International Space Station.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
           Nasa hopes to use Orion to put astronauts back on the moon by 2020 and take them to Mars in the 2030s. A midway mission to an asteroid is also on the cards.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
           Orion will launch from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station on the end of a Delta IV Heavy, the largest US rocket currently in service.

        /*
         Read Next Widget
         */
        var readNextWidget = (function(){

            var settings = {
                articleImage: "",
                articleTitle: "",
                articleUrl: "#",
                articleId: "",
                currentArticleId: "",
                articleList: [],
                articleListToSkip: [],
                readNextCookieName: "tmg_read_next",
                openButton: "#readNextOpen a",
                closeButton: "#closeBtn a",
                articleContainer: "#readNextArticleContainer",
                widgetContainer: "#readNextWidget",
                displayThumbnail: false,
                panelOpen: false,
                autoOpen: true
            };

            var renderTemplate = function() {
                var template = '&lt;div id="readNextWidget" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextOpen"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div id="readNextOpenInner"&gt;&lt;a href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Next Story&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span class="icn"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextArticleContainer" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticle"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleInnerWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;p id="readNextLabel"&gt;MOST VIEWED IN NEWS&lt;/p&gt;';
                        
				if(settings.articleImage &amp;&amp; settings.articleImage!="null" &amp;&amp; settings.displayThumbnail){
					template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageThumbnail" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;img src="'+ settings.articleImage +'"/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;';
				}
                        
				template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageTitle" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;' + settings.articleTitle + '&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="closeBtn"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;a href="#"&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
