          <h1>
           Behold the New Worlds Strongest Beer: 67.5 Percent ABV Snake Venom From Brewmeister
          </h1>
          <div class="post_meta">
           <span class="author_avatar">
            <a href="http://97rockonline.com/author/bprescott/" title="The Brad">
             <img src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/97rockonline.com/files/2013/10/250x250_Brad1.jpg?w=40&amp;h=40&amp;zc=1&amp;s=0&amp;a=t&amp;q=89"/>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_author">
            <span>
             By
            </span>
            <a href="http://97rockonline.com/author/bprescott/" title="The Brad">
             The Brad
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_date">
            October 25, 2013 2:00 PM
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="social-group-top large">
           <div class="social_icons size_large">
            <a class="facebook share_link" href="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2F97rockonline.com%2Fnew-worlds-strongest-beer-the-67-5-abv-snake-venom-from-brewmeister%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dfbshare_top&amp;t=Behold+the+New+World%26%238217%3Bs+Strongest+Beer%3A+67.5+Percent+ABV+%26%238216%3BSnake+Venom%26%238217%3B+From+Brewmeister" target="_blank" title="Share on Facebook">

          <div class="the_content clearfix">
           <figure class="aligncenter" id="attachment_185119">
            <img alt="Lewis and John of Brewmeister" class="size-full wp-image-185119" height="291" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/97rockonline.com/files/2013/10/brewmeisters.png" title="Lewis and John of Brewmeister" width="630"/>
            <figcaption style="width:630px;">
             Facebook
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Nearly a year ago to the day,
            <a href="http://www.brewmeister.co.uk/">
             Brewmeister
            </a>
            brewed up the Armageddon with a record ABV of 65 percent.The Scottish brewery has recently outdone themselves with their latest creation, Snake Venom, which has an ABV of 67.5 percent! Thats about ten-times what you are getting out of your favorite domestic beerwater and even more than most whiskeys!
           </p>
           <p>
            <span id="more-185099">
            </span>
            Although it is a beer by title, you wouldnt want to be putting down a six pack of these things. One, because you would die before you could open your third beer (please no one accept that challenge) and two, it aint cheap. You can
            <a href="http://www.therealalecompany.co.uk/real-craft-ale-beer/ales-by-brewery/brewmeister.html">
             purchase both of Brewmeisters beers from The Real Ale Company
            </a>
            for around $80/bottle.
           </p>
           <hr/>
           <dl class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_185103" style="width: 310px;">
            <dt class="wp-caption-dt">
             <img alt="World's Strongest Beer - 6.75% ABV 'Snake Venom' by Brewmeister" class=" wp-image-185103" height="200" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/97rockonline.com/files/2013/10/630x420_SnakeVenom-300x200.png" title="World's Strongest Beer - 6.75% ABV 'Snake Venom' by Brewmeister" width="300"/>
            </dt>
           </dl>
           <h6>
            <a href="http://www.therealalecompany.co.uk/snake-venom-the-world-s-strongest-beer-67-5-1-x-275ml-bottles-brewmeister.html">
             Snake Venom  The Worlds Strongest Beer
            </a>
           </h6>
           <p>
            <em>
             67.5%  1 x 275ml Bottle  50.00 ($80.78)
            </em>
            <br/>
            <em>
             This unique beer is made with a plethora of malts including peat smoked malt, acid malt and chocolate malt. It is fermented using two types of yeast  ale yeast and champagne yeast in order to deliver a higher alcohol content. It is then painstakingly freeze concentrated.
            </em>
           </p>
           <hr/>
           <dl class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_185109" style="width: 310px;">
            <dt class="wp-caption-dt">
             <img alt="Armageddon from Brewmeister - World's Second Strongest Beer" class="size-medium wp-image-185109" height="200" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/97rockonline.com/files/2013/10/630x420_Armageddon1-300x200.png" title="Armageddon from Brewmeister - World's Second Strongest Beer" width="300"/>
            </dt>
           </dl>
           <h6>
            <a href="http://www.therealalecompany.co.uk/armageddon-the-world-s-strongest-beer-65-1-x-330ml-bottles-brewmeister.html">
             Armageddon  The Worlds 2nd Strongest Beer
            </a>
           </h6>
           <p>
            <em>
             65% ABV  1 x 330ml Bottle  55.00 ($88.86)
            </em>
            <br/>
            <em>
             Armageddon  65% Worlds strongest beer which is made by freezing the beer and removing the water. It is then aged for 2-3 months. Notes of caramel and oak.
            </em>
           </p>
