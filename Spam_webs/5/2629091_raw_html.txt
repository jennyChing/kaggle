      <h1 class="entry-title">
       <a href="http://charlesandhudson.com/diy_city_guide_portland/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to DIY City Guide: Portland">
        DIY City Guide: Portland
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="post-meta">
      </div>
      <!-- /.post-meta -->
      <div class="clearfix post-1184 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-diy category-diy-city-guides tag-diy-city-guide tag-oregon tag-portland" id="post-1184">
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         <img alt="portland-diy-tools.jpg" height="215" src="http://www.charlesandhudson.com/archives/portland-diy-tools.jpg" width="480"/>
         <br/>
         Portland remains an outpost of great design, bleeding edge technology, and fervent do-it-yourselfers. It seems that the rest of the country has finally caught onto to the sustainable lifestyle and eco-friendly transportation that Portland has long been known for. With help from Paige at
         <a href="http://www.reliableremodeler.com/blog/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.reliableremodeler.com/blog/', 'Reliable Remodeler']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Reliable Remodeler
         </a>
         , weve gathered some of the top resources in Portland for DIY enthusiasts.
         <br/>
         More from Paige:
         <br/>
         <em>
          When it comes to Portland Im passionate about sharing our amazing local, independent businesses and resources that showcase what I love about this city: our unique sense of community, our love of being green, and of course our desire to support local quality products!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-1184">
         </span>
         <br/>
         As with all of our DIY City Guides, please share any stores, vendors, or contractors you feel weve left out.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Hardware Stores
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.hippohardware.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.hippohardware.com/', 'Hippo Hardware']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Hippo Hardware
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=1040+East+Burnside,+Portland,+OR+97214&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,16032916146294366350&amp;ei=m6a2ScH-CpnMsAOakvDyCA&amp;t=h&amp;z=16" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=1040+East+Burnside,+Portland,+OR+97214&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,16032916146294366350&amp;ei=m6a2ScH-CpnMsAOakvDyCA&amp;t=h&amp;z=16', '1040 East Burnside, Portland, OR 97214']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          1040 East Burnside, Portland, OR 97214
         </a>
         , 503-231-1444
         <br/>
         This store is a Portland favorite, and is often listed in tour books and guides of the city. A large building on Burnside, the main North/South Portland thoroughfare, Hippo Hardware is easy to spot. Its the building with the dancing yellow hippos on the outside. Inside, there are no hippos, but you will find three stories of antique, reproduction and recycled hardware, moldings, and lighting fixtures. Have a project? The dedicated and creative Hippo Hardware staff is helpful and has lots of vision to help you find yours.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.rejuvenation.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.rejuvenation.com/', 'Rejuvenation ']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Rejuvenation
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1100+SE+Grand+Avenue,+Portland,+OR+97214&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=yqa2SY-zGoHwsAP805HrCA&amp;ll=45.516677,-122.660744&amp;spn=0.005984,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1100+SE+Grand+Avenue,+Portland,+OR+97214&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=yqa2SY-zGoHwsAP805HrCA&amp;ll=45.516677,-122.660744&amp;spn=0.005984,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr', '1100 SE Grand Avenue, Portland, OR 97214']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          1100 SE Grand Avenue, Portland, OR 97214
         </a>
         , 503-238-1900
         <br/>
         Rejuvenation is the name that first comes to mind when I think of quality hardware in Portland. Lots of choices, and locally owned, this place is fun to visit, even if you cant always afford everything you want.  I am a big fan of their Satellite Modern series which Ive even featured on my blog.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Antique Hardware
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.schoolhouseelectric.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.schoolhouseelectric.com/', 'School House Electric']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          School House Electric
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;source=s_q&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=330+SE+M+L+King+Blvd,+Portland,&amp;sll=45.524721,-122.664886&amp;sspn=0.011966,0.027637&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;ll=45.524781,-122.663898&amp;spn=0.011245,0.027637&amp;t=h&amp;z=15&amp;iwloc=addr" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;source=s_q&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=330+SE+M+L+King+Blvd,+Portland,&amp;sll=45.524721,-122.664886&amp;sspn=0.011966,0.027637&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;ll=45.524781,-122.663898&amp;spn=0.011245,0.027637&amp;t=h&amp;z=15&amp;iwloc=addr', '\nrel=&quot;nofollow&quot;&gt;330 SE  MLK Jr. Blvd, Portland, Oregon 97214']);" target="_blank">
         </a>
         rel="nofollow"&gt;330 SE  MLK Jr. Blvd, Portland, Oregon 97214, ph: 503-230-7113
         <br/>
         Sometimes I get jealous that New York gets all the cool design and hardware stores. But once in a while I remember that while Portland cant beat the sheer size of NYC, we sometimes get stores that even they would be jealous of. Schoolhouse Electric is one of those places. Their vintage reproduction lighting options are some of the most beautiful fixtures Ive seen. They have a great online presentation, but in person, they truly stand out in a room.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://houseofantiquehardware.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://houseofantiquehardware.com/', 'House of Antique Hardware']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          House of Antique Hardware
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=802+N.E.+Davis+Street,+Portland,+Oregon,+97232&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=yKW2SYXnAonYsAPkjLz2CA&amp;ll=45.525788,-122.657354&amp;spn=0.005983,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?q=802+N.E.+Davis+Street,+Portland,+Oregon,+97232&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=yKW2SYXnAonYsAPkjLz2CA&amp;ll=45.525788,-122.657354&amp;spn=0.005983,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr', '802 N.E. Davis Street, Portland, Oregon, 97232']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" v="">
          802 N.E. Davis Street, Portland, Oregon, 97232
         </a>
         , 888-223-2545
         <br/>
         House of Antique Hardware manufactures its own line of affordable reproductions, complete with a hand-finishing process that replicates the timeworn look of the originals. If youd rather have the real thing, the companys hardware sleuths can get authentic antique pieces for you, too. They also offer custom manufacturing of mortise locks, escutcheons, and doorknobs.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Lighting
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Eleek Inc.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=2326+N.+Flint+Ave,+Portland,+OR+97227&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,13798480492568589202&amp;ei=3Ki2Sar8AoKEsAPvgIXpCA&amp;ll=45.542337,-122.668405&amp;spn=0.005981,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=2326+N.+Flint+Ave,+Portland,+OR+97227&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,13798480492568589202&amp;ei=3Ki2Sar8AoKEsAPvgIXpCA&amp;ll=45.542337,-122.668405&amp;spn=0.005981,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A', '2326 N. Flint Ave, Portland, OR 97227']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          2326 N. Flint Ave, Portland, OR 97227
         </a>
         , ph: 503-232-552
         <br/>
         Eleeks more than 100 fixtures designed by Eric Kaster, the vast majority can be wired for LED bulbs. The new lamping technology is far more energy-efficient than compact fluorescent. All of Eleeks fixtures are handcrafted in their Portland factory.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Salvage Center
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.rebuildingcenter.org/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.rebuildingcenter.org/', 'Rebuilding Center']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Rebuilding Center
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=3625+N.+Mississippi+Ave.,+Portland,+OR+97227&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=BKe2Sb2oHpGUsAO7l7zlCA&amp;ll=45.550542,-122.675722&amp;spn=0.00598,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?q=3625+N.+Mississippi+Ave.,+Portland,+OR+97227&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=BKe2Sb2oHpGUsAO7l7zlCA&amp;ll=45.550542,-122.675722&amp;spn=0.00598,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr', '3625 N. Mississippi Ave., Portland, OR 97227']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          3625 N. Mississippi Ave., Portland, OR 97227
         </a>
         , 503-331-1877
         <br/>
         The Rebuilding Center is Portlands premiere recycling and salvage center. Not only is the building an impressive, artistic, recycled masterpiece, but the inside holds every kind of material you would need for a home remodeling project: windows, doors, cabinets, sinks, and all kinds of hardware. The center also offers classes in making furniture, jewelry and other crafts from recycled materials.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Paint Stores
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.powellpaintcenter.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.powellpaintcenter.com/', 'Powell Paints']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Powell Paints
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=5205+SE+Powell+Blvd.,+Portland,+OR+97206&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,16113332142412176047&amp;ei=TKe2SeWbB5mMsQOIz5jyCA&amp;t=h&amp;z=16" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=5205+SE+Powell+Blvd.,+Portland,+OR+97206&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,16113332142412176047&amp;ei=TKe2SeWbB5mMsQOIz5jyCA&amp;t=h&amp;z=16', '5205 SE Powell Blvd., Portland, OR 97206']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          5205 SE Powell Blvd., Portland, OR 97206
         </a>
         , 503-620-7522
         <br/>
         This paint stores offers a really great service called Designer Saturdays where you can meet with a color consultant for free. Reserve a spot online and come in with your pictures and fabric swatches to get some ideas for how to best design your homes interior. Choosing the right color can be frustrating, and I love that Powell Paints offers this helpful service.
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Tool Libraries
         </strong>
         <br/>
         What I love about Portland is that we pride ourselves on community. Heres a great example of cool neighborhood projects: our two tool libraries. As with regular libraries you can receive a free library card to check out tools from their arsenal, as long as you live within their geographic parameter. North and Northeast Portland is the home of fixer-upper real estate, so being able to borrow from over 500 tools for any of your weekend projects can be gods send, and a money saver. Weekend warriors rejoice!
         <br/>
         +
         <a href="http://www.northportlandtoollibrary.org/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.northportlandtoollibrary.org/', 'The North Portland Tool Library']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          The North Portland Tool Library
         </a>
         : 2209 N. Schofield, Portland, OR 97217, ph: 503-823-0929
         <br/>
         +
         <a href="http://www.neptl.org/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.neptl.org/', 'Northeast Portland Tool Library']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Northeast Portland Tool Library
         </a>
         : 5431 NE 20th Ave, Portland, OR 97211
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Nurseries and Landscaping
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.pistilsnursery.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.pistilsnursery.com/', 'Pistils Nursery ']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Pistils Nursery
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=3811+N.+Mississippi+Ave,+Portland,+OR&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=z6e2Sc3uL5KasAPb5-jvCA&amp;ll=45.551999,-122.67585&amp;spn=0.00598,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?q=3811+N.+Mississippi+Ave,+Portland,+OR&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;split=0&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=z6e2Sc3uL5KasAPb5-jvCA&amp;ll=45.551999,-122.67585&amp;spn=0.00598,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr', '3811 N. Mississippi Ave, Portland, OR']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          3811 N. Mississippi Ave, Portland, OR
         </a>
         , ph: 403-288-4889
         <br/>
         Even if you dont have major landscaping to do, this place is gorgeous and fun to visit. If youre simply looking for a garden hose or cheap pair of gloves, this is not the place to come. Its a boutique, with a thoughtfully chosen stock of beautiful and interesting things. All kinds of plants, mulch, fertilizer, terrariums, and you can even buy chicks for your own urban chicken coop. They hold inexpensive classes on bee keeping, chicken keeping, worm composting and even raising goats, as well as providing landscape design services. I always buy my veggie starters here.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.portlandnursery.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.portlandnursery.com/', 'Portland Nursery ']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Portland Nursery
         </a>
         <br/>
         SE Stark:
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=5050+SE+Stark,+Portland,+OR+97215&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,4190555396170039838&amp;ei=G6i2Sfe2A5GYsAOOwqHwCA&amp;ll=45.521368,-122.611585&amp;spn=0.005984,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=5050+SE+Stark,+Portland,+OR+97215&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,4190555396170039838&amp;ei=G6i2Sfe2A5GYsAOOwqHwCA&amp;ll=45.521368,-122.611585&amp;spn=0.005984,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A', '5050 SE Stark, Portland, OR 97215']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          5050 SE Stark, Portland, OR 97215
         </a>
         , ph: 503-231-5050
         <br/>
         SE Division:
         <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=9000+SE+Division,+Portland,+OR+97266&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,915504927311185926&amp;ei=OKi2SfT1MpHItQOsn7j5CA&amp;ll=45.506648,-122.570493&amp;spn=0.005985,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=9000+SE+Division,+Portland,+OR+97266&amp;fb=1&amp;split=1&amp;gl=us&amp;cid=0,0,915504927311185926&amp;ei=OKi2SfT1MpHItQOsn7j5CA&amp;ll=45.506648,-122.570493&amp;spn=0.005985,0.013819&amp;t=h&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A', '9000 SE Division, Portland, OR 97266']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          9000 SE Division, Portland, OR 97266
         </a>
         , ph: 503-788-9000
         <br/>
         A classic Portland resource, some might call the Portland Nursery the Powells books of the landscaping world.  In the heart of inner SE Portland, this is the antithesis of Pistils. While still thoughtful and with great stock, this place is bigger and more reminiscent of a larger scale landscaping store. While it may be easy to go out to the nearest Home Depot, we Portlanders like to support our local businesses, hence our love of the two Portland Nursery locations.
