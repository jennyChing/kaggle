       <h3 style="text-align:center;">
        Time pushes closer to the end
        <br/>
        Of what once was the work I tend.
        <br/>
        Integrity driven. That never failed.
        <br/>
        Nonetheless, the plans curtailed.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Magic and illusion long part of my life.
        <br/>
        That seems no match for the constant strife.
        <br/>
        Counting out time most memories fade,
        <br/>
        Except for the ones of the friends I made.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        After all is said and done,
        <br/>
        Things remembered that were fun.
        <br/>
        And while I've sat and wondered why,
        <br/>
        It matters not, so... goodbye.
       </h3>
