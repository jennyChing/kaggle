          <h1>
           4 Most Advanced Ancient Civilizations
          </h1>
          <div class="entry">
           <p>
            <!-- Start Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic -->
            <!-- End Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic -->
            We can thank our ancient ancestorsfor many of the modern conveniences we enjoy today. But, there is more to thesecivilizationsthan their contributions to art and architecture
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <img alt="GreatWall" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-188" height="199" src="http://baidun.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/GreatWall3-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
             Ancient China 2100  221 BC
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Along with the Four Great Inventions of Ancient China  printing, paper-making, the compass and gunpowder  the Ancient Chinese have also been credited with inventions ranging from kites to toilet paper! The first recorded observations of solar eclipses and comets were also made in China.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Chinese also brought the concept of time-keeping into our lives through Shadow Clocks  forerunners of the sundial which first appeared in China around 2,500 years ago.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <img alt="ancient-egypt" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-189" height="268" src="http://baidun.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/ancient-egypt-300x268.jpg" width="300"/>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Ancient Egypt3150  31 BC
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #2f2e2e;">
             The Ancient Egyptians are world renowned for their early advances inMath and Architecture; but did you know that they were forward-thinking in the field of medicine? With the harsh living and work conditions of early Egypt, parasitic diseases and malaria were prevalent near the Nile River, with the added danger of crocodiles and hippos.There is evidence that the Ancient Egyptians had knowledge of anatomy, injuries and practical treatments
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Moreover, the Egyptians were ahead of their time in regards to gender equality. Men and women were represented equal before the law, and women played significant roles in society.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <img alt="parthenon1" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-190" height="203" src="http://baidun.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/parthenon1-300x203.jpg" width="300"/>
             Ancient Greece 800 BC  146 BC
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Although well known for their sophistication in Architecture, Math and Astrology, the Ancient Greeks took great strides in the literary world too  playwright Aeschylus invented drama through the idea of dialogue, Sophocles is credited with developing irony as a literary technique, and Euipedes used plays to challenge social norms  something that has continued to the present day.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <img alt="maya civilization" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-191" height="202" src="http://baidun.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/science-022412-004-617x416-300x202.jpg" width="300"/>
             Maya Civilization 2000 BC  early 16th Century
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Maya are well known for havingbrilliance astrology and outstanding mathematicians, credited with the invention of zero and measuring the length of a solar year with great accuracy.What is less well known is the sophistication of the methods they used to produce food  with permanent raised fields, wild harvesting, forest gardens and terracing supporting the population  practices that modern-day Maya people continue today.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
