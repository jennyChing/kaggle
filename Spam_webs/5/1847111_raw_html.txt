        <p>

        </p>
        <p>
         Grammy
         <sup>
          
         </sup>

         </a>
         , on
         <span class="xn-chron">
          Thursday, March 19, 2015
         </span>
         at
         <span class="xn-chron">
          7:30 p.m.
         </span>
         local time, presented by Front Row Centre Events, SpectiCast, Serious Ent., AMG, Rap-A-Lot Records &amp; Young Empire Music Group.

        <p>

         <span class="xn-person">
          Young Money
         </span>
         (Cash Money/Universal) imprint during a live, sold-out concert at
         <span class="xn-location">
          Torontos
         </span>

         <span class="xn-person">
          James Prince
         </span>
         and his son
         <span class="xn-person">
          Jas Prince
         </span>

        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="xn-person">
          Jas Prince
         </span>

I told him during our first conversation I would make him a star. This film documents the beginning of that journey.
        </p>

         <span class="xn-location">
          Toronto
         </span>
         -based rapper Drake now holds the record for the most number one hits on
         <i>
          Billboard
         </i>

         <i>
          Billboard
         </i>
         s Hot 100 chart. After signing to Universal, Drake released the album Thank Me Later
         <i>
          
         </i>
         (2010) which reached sales of 1.5 million and produced the top ten hit Over. 2011 saw the release of the multi-platinum album Take Care
         <i>
          
         </i>
         and Drake winning a Grammy for the Best Rap Album. His 2012 Club Paradise Tour was the highest grossing hip-hop tour, bringing in excess of
         <span class="xn-money">

         </span>

         <i>

         </i>

         <span class="xn-money">

         </span>

         <i>
