         <p>
          Feathers are in big time right now, so as soon as this lovely submission landed in my inbox, I was smitten  so many cute ideas involving feathers! In fact, today is going to be all about feathers! :) This shoot was designed by
          <a href="http://www.CoutureEventsSD.com" target="_blank">
           Couture Events
          </a>
          and captured by a bunch of photographers (all listed at the end of thefeature).Since Bohemian is all about the feathers  we say, the more the better! They used feathers for the invitations, place cards, escort cards, menus, and guestbook as well as in the bouquet andboutonniere. Hope this shoot helps inspire youBohemianbrides with some new ideas for your big day!
