         <div class="itemFullText">
          <p>
           Leave it to Russia to think of something so genius, that FOX Sports will bite off their style and
           <strong>
            never
           </strong>
           give them credit. Forget Compustrike or FightMetric, it's all about this
           <strong>
            in-fight exp system
           </strong>
           so we can see our favorite fighters level up right before our very eyes.
          </p>
          <p>
           So from what I can discern, a flying knee is worth 150exp, low kick is worth 15exp, a knockout is worth 1500exp, any strike to the head is valued at 30exp. Arrows has also installed a
           <strong>
            combo meter
           </strong>
           similar to Killer Instincts that
           <strong>
            multiplies
           </strong>
           the value of strikes. If you get the crowd on your side, it's worth
           <strong>
            2000exp
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Look, the entire concept is genius, and we're damn happy they're advertising on the front page of MiddleEasy. See, we don't mess around with our sponsors. Now check out this clip to see how the EXP system plays out.
