       12 Shoes for 12 Loves by Sebastian Errazuriz
      </h1>
     </div>

       <p>
        Artist and designer
        <a href="http://www.meetsebastian.com/305226/3835758/anti-design/12-shoes-for-12-lovers" target="_blank">
         Sebastian Errazuriz
        </a>
        has never been one to be afraid of pushing the boundaries and his latest project entitled 12 Shoes for 12 Lovers does just that.  He has created a series of exquisitely-designed shoes that deal with his past relationships in a uniquely artistic and open way. The series, called 12 Shoes For 12 Lovers, features shoes designed to reflect each of his 12 exes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Each beautiful high-heel shoe comes with a brief anecdote about or description of the girl in Errazurizs past that it is dedicated to. Some of the descriptions are beautiful and flattering, while others are not quite as nice. However, they are all powerful  the shoes seem like an act of deliberate public self-exposure, and Errazuriz seems more vulnerable to these women from his past.
       </p>
