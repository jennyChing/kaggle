Check out these super cute little Aussies:



Baby Kangaroo &amp; Wombat



A true Australian icon this baby Kangaroo, known as a Joey, poses with his best mate, baby Wombat.



Baby Echidna



Did you know Echidnas (sometimes known as spiny anteaters) are one of only two egg-laying mammals in existence?



Baby Crocodiles



Ok, so by the time these man-eating salt water crocodiles reach maturity they will be anything but cute! Although you have to admit as babies they have a certain toothy charm about them.



Baby Emu



The Emu is the second tallest bird in the world, reaching well over 6 ft in height. Although newly hatched chicks, like this cute little guy, stand at only 5 in tall.



Baby Koala



Surely one of the cutest animals in the world, it's hard to look at a Koala and not want to give it a cuddle!



Baby Platypus



With it's duck bill, beaver tail and otter feet the Platypus must rank as one of the strangest animals on earth. In fact when the Platypus was first discovered early scientists thought it was a hoax!



Baby Tasmanian Devil



When early Australian settlers first heard the blood curdling screams and snarls coming from the Tasmanian forests in the dead of night, they thought it was the work of Devils. I'm sorry, but it's hard to imagine being terrified by these cute little guys.
