       <h2 style="text-align: left;">
        Andaman
       </h2>
       <h3 style="text-align: left;">
        Island Tourism
       </h3>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Famed for its fabulous beaches, world-class diving , unspoiled reefs, and picturesque pristine locations in the middle of nowhere, the Andaman Islands are one of the best beach gateways of India. The sparkling turquoise waters are encircled by primeval tropical jungleand innumerable exotic flowers , animals and birds. Bursting with colorful fish and kaleidoscopic corals, the crystal-clear waters of the Andaman Sea features some of the worlds lushest marine reserves  perfect for snorkeling and scuba diving. Although the archipelago embraces some 300 islands, only a dozen or so are open to tourists.
       </p>
       <p>
        Best beaches:Radhanagar Beach, Corbyns Cove, Ramnagar Beach, Merk Bay Beach, Guitar Island Beach,Karmatang Beach
       </p>
       <p>
        To Reach: Andaman is connected to the mainland by flights and shipsfrom Kolkata, Chennai and Vishakapatnam ,India.
       </p>
       <p>
        Best Time : November  April
       </p>
