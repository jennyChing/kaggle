                     <a href="http://www.ultimatehousewife.com/2015/06/7-easy-ways-to-decorate-your-bedroom.html">
                      7 Easy Ways to Decorate Your Bedroom!
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7573050515939053858" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      By Jane Blanchard
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">

                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-5ejx4Z1O4Pc/VYMpgrnprDI/AAAAAAAAA3I/aJirNTJ_ASw/s1600/63b8de5cbdba5724c7bafb0993edbeb7--gallery.jpeg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="425" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-5ejx4Z1O4Pc/VYMpgrnprDI/AAAAAAAAA3I/aJirNTJ_ASw/s640/63b8de5cbdba5724c7bafb0993edbeb7--gallery.jpeg" width="640"/>

                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      The bedroom is one of the most overlooked rooms in the home
in terms of decorating. But why? Its where you spend one-third of your time
and should be as comfortable, sumptuous, and well-designed as possible. Its
literally your sanctuary where you can find peace, rest, and relaxation in
addition to getting a good nights rest.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Obviously, if your undecorated bedroom isnt cozy its
harder to unwind from a long day and rest well. Here are seven incredibly easy
ways to decorate your bedroom and turn it into the relaxing retreat you
deserve.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       1. Easy Bedding Decor
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Consider adding a stylish comforter or duvet if youre not
using one already. A new comforter or duvet cover with matching European shams
will set the tone of the room and instantly give it a fresh look that can take
it from ordinary to outrageously gorgeous.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       2. Prints
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Use harmonizing prints on the bed linens, window treatments,
lampshades, and even artwork. Unify them with similar patterns and colors while
keeping mindful of the scale as well.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       3. Proper Lighting
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Keep your overall bedroom lighting somewhat subdued. Bedside
table lamps or soft wall sconces should be just close enough for well-lit
bedtime reading. However, be sure to use three-way bulbs to easily adapt the
lighting for your task or mood. Also, many homeowners today like to install
beautiful chandeliers in the center of their bedroom to create a sense of
elegance and romance.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       4. Pillows Galore
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Stack pillows on the bed, arm chairs, or chaise lounge in
your bedroom without shying away from a mix of eye-catching patterns and
colors. Combine both small and large prints, geometrics, and florals; mix in an
ample amount of solid ones as well to keep all those busy patterns anchored.
Although pillows should be cute and comfy, they shouldnt use more than half
the bed space. A few sets of properly sized bed pillows, a neck roll, and a few
small matching pillows should be all you really need.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       5. Dresser Dress Up
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Painting or wallpapering the front of your bedroom dresser
or the drawers of your nightstand is a super easy way to add new design
elements and fresh color. A modern floral paper accent can easily convert a
once drab dresser into a sweet feminine classic. Follow through on your
makeover by replacing worn drawer pulls and handles with attractive new ones.
This easy trick adds style and personality to inexpensive or plain furniture.
Its little things like this that often make the most impact in a room.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       6. Wall Space
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Does your bedroom wall have a big, bare, blank problem?
Try hanging your favorite patterned quilt on the wall or behind the bed to
serve as an alternative headboard. The pattern will add interest, style, and a
unique quality to the room and gives the room a feeling of complete coziness.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <b>
                       7. Shift the
Furniture
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Sometimes even the slightest shift in furniture can add new
life to a once boring room. Think outside the box on this one and try different
furniture configurations to suit your own personal tastes. Keep in mind that
the headboard should be the first thing you see when you enter the room. Its
just good feng shui. While youre rearranging your furniture, flip and rotate
your mattress for even wear.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Prior to giving your bedroom a makeover, eliminate anything
that doesnt encourage sleep and relaxation, including the TV and all other
electronics. At the very least, consider hiding any stressful clutter in
stylish boxes and baskets.
