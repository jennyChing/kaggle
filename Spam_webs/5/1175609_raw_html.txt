     <p>
      <br/>
      This is a
      <b>
       list of
       <a href="/wiki/Legendary_creature" title="Legendary creature">
        legendary creatures
       </a>
       from
       <a href="/wiki/Mythology" title="Mythology">
        mythology
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Folklore" title="Folklore">
        folklore
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Fairy_tale" title="Fairy tale">
        fairy tales
       </a>
      </b>
      , sorted by their classification or affiliation. Creatures from modern
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fantasy_fiction" title="Fantasy fiction">
       fantasy fiction
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Role-playing_game" title="Role-playing game">
       role-playing games
      </a>
      are not included.
     </p>
