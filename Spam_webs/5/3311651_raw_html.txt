               <h3 class="article-title">
                What does "Florida" mean?
               </h3>
               <p>
                Florida was named
                <em>
                 Pascua Florida
                </em>
                by explorer Ponce de Leon on Easter in 1513. Translation: means "Flowery Easter" or "Flowering Easter" (after Spain's "Feast of the Flowers" Easter celebration).
               </p>
