            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             Baltimore Ravens quarterback Joe Flacco got married recently. Its a great way to spend a lockout, I guess. Some of the wedding photos have been released and this one has gotten a
             <a href="http://sports.yahoo.com/nfl/blog/shutdown_corner/post/Joe-Flacco-makes-his-wife-play-center-in" target="_blank">
              bit of buzz
             </a>
             :
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             With a certain Chicago Bears quarterback recently engaged to a particular reality TV starlet, you would think this picture could give them some ideas for fun poses. I know if gave me some ideas. Think Kristin will go for a picture like this? Do you think any of Jays Bears teammates besides Greg Olsen will be groomsmen? These are the questions that beg me to say  END THE DAMN LOCKOUT ALREADY!
            </p>
