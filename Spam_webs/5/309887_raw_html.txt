        <p>
         I have been partnering with
         <a href="http://www.mccormick.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          McCormick
         </a>
         for a few years now. It is a match made in heaven! I get to use amazing quality products and share delicious and creative recipes and they um they well. I am starting to thinkthat I got the better end of this partnership.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont tell them I love my work so much ok? Our secret!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="McCormick Logo Surprise-Inside Cake!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45315" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="600" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/mccormickcake.jpg" width="388"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         McCormick is celebrating their 125th birthday on Sept. 28th. I dont know about you, but I had no idea that they had been around for 125 years! There is something to be said for longevity and dependability in a huge company like that.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="McCormick Logo Surprise-Inside Cake!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45316" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="600" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/mccormickcake1.jpg" width="505"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         In honor of their big day I decided to make them a cake. Its what I do ok? I wanted it to be fun,
         <em>
          really
         </em>
         fun, and really personal. The outside of my cake is covered in hundreds of rainbow sprinkles! Dont you love the clean lines and color in this cake?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="McCormick Logo Surprise-Inside Cake!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45317" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="400" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/mccormickcake2.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Add to that a fancy little white frill around the top and we are golden. Golden I tell you!
        </p>
        <p>
         Or rainbow. More like rainbow.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Add to that a fancy little white frill around the top and we are golden. Golden I tell you!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45318" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="600" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/logo-cake-1.jpg" width="465"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         And here is the surprise! I made the McCormick logo inside the cake!
        </p>
        <p>
         It was not easy. But it was a labor of love.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Add to that a fancy little white frill around the top and we are golden. Golden I tell you!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45319" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="600" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/logo-cake-2.jpg" width="491"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I have to tell you, when I cut into the cake I was so pleased! SO pleased.  could not contain my excitement and I texted off a picture to a close friend. She was as excited as I was and thats when I knew that this was a special cake. And who better to honor?
        </p>
        <p>
         While I happen to think that this may be one of the prettiest cakes I have ever made, it is also the tastiest. I preferthis amazing
         <a href="http://iambaker.net/the-perfect-white-cake/" target="_blank">
          White Cake
         </a>
         recipe, but with a twist.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="McCormick Extra Rich Vanilla" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45321" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="516" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/mccormick-vanilla.jpg" width="506"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         McCormick just released this wonderful new Extra Rich vanilla that is 25% richer than classic vanilla extract. It adds something really special to my cakes and frostings!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="McCormick Logo Surprise-Inside Cake!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45324" data-pin-description="Logo Surprise-Inside Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="493" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/logo-cake1.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you want to know more about how I made this surprise-inside cake please do check out my book,
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/006219531X/ref=cm_sw_su_dp?tag=authorweb-20" target="_blank">
          Surprise-Inside Cakes
         </a>
         ! I also have a little
         <a href="http://iambaker.net/easter-egg-surprise-inside-cake/" target="_blank">
          video here
         </a>
         that helps to detail the process.
        </p>
        <p>
         Happy Birthday McCormick! Looking forward to another 125 years!
        </p>
