        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Asparagus season will be here before we know it. This Asparagus, Bacon &amp; Goat Cheese Tart is a perfect recipe for fresh asparagus. My girlfriend Mary made this for my birthday and it was soooooo good. I know youll love it too. Scroll down to see howshe made it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Asparagus-Bacon-Goat-cheese-tart.jpg">
           <br/>
           <img alt="Second Chance to Dream: Asparagus, Bacon &amp; Goat Cheese tart" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12775" height="667" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Asparagus-Bacon-Goat-cheese-tart.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Ingredients:
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul style="text-align: justify;">
          <li>
           5 slices bacon
          </li>
          <li>
           1 shallot, finely chopped
          </li>
          <li>
           1 bunch asparagus (about 1 lb.) tough ends trimmed, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
          </li>
          <li>
           1 tomato chopped
          </li>
          <li>
           3 eggs whipped
          </li>
          <li>
           3/4 cup of milk
          </li>
          <li>
           1 pie crust
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 pound soft goat cheese
          </li>
          <li>
           salt and freshly ground black pepper
          </li>
          <li>
           shredded parmesan to garnish
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Heres what you do:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          1. Cut your bacon in bite sized pieces. Fry your bacon. When cooked remove from pan and place on paper towel to drain grease.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/fry-bacon.jpg">
           <img alt="fry bacon" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12772" height="558" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/fry-bacon.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Saute your asparagus until tender.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cook-asparagus.jpg">
           <img alt="cook asparagus" class="aligncenter wp-image-12768 size-large" height="592" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cook-asparagus-500x592.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Add your shallots and tomatoes and cook for 1 min.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cook-asparagus-and-tomatoes.jpg">
           <img alt="cook asparagus and tomatoes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12767" height="667" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cook-asparagus-and-tomatoes.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Whip your eggs. Add milk and mix eggs and milk together. Mix in the bacon, asparagus,tomatoes &amp; crumbled goat cheese.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mix-ingredients.jpg">
           <img alt="Mix ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12777" height="667" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Mix-ingredients.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          5. Place pie crust into your pie pan. Add theeggmixture andbake at 350 for 30 minutes or until itis set in the center.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crust1.jpg">
           <img alt="crust" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12779" height="643" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/crust1.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          6. Sprinkle the tart with shredded parmesan cheese. Let set for 5 min. to cool down.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/asparagus-tart-2.jpg">
           <img alt="asparagus tart 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12773" height="667" src="http://secondchancetodream.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/asparagus-tart-2.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Enjoy!! It is SUPER Yummy!!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">

          </a>
