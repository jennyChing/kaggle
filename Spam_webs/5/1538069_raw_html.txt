         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B007HCCNJU/ref=gs_htljs" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/dp/B007HCCNJU/ref=gs_htljs', 'Kindle']);">
          Kindle
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
         <img alt="101-kindle" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-86564" height="300" src="http://www.1stwebdesigner.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/101-kindle.jpg" width="600"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         This is one of the earliest generations of Kindle, and its actually the best Kindle at $69. In my opinion, Kindle Fire is less useful. If youre planning on buying a Kindle, be sure to check older versions like this one.
        </p>
        <h2>
         102.
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B009NVVQWS/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B009NVVQWS&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B009NVVQWS/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B009NVVQWS&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20', 'LED Fluorescent Message Board']);">
          LED Fluorescent Message Board
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
         <img alt="102-led-flourescent-message-board" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-86565" height="300" src="http://www.1stwebdesigner.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/102-led-flourescent-message-board.jpg" width="600"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         A great addition to anyones nightstand either for cheer notes or for a to-do list the next morning.
        </p>
        <h2>
         103.
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0081J2LRC/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0081J2LRC&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0081J2LRC/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0081J2LRC&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20', 'Robotic Ball for iOS and Android']);">
          Robotic Ball for iOS and Android
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
         <img alt="103-robotic-ball" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-86566" height="300" src="http://www.1stwebdesigner.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/103-robotic-ball.jpg" width="600"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         Something fun to have. A robotic ball that runs on Android and iOS, you can control it remotely. Purpose? Fun!
        </p>
        <h2>
         104.
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CW7KK9K/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CW7KK9K&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CW7KK9K/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CW7KK9K&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20', 'Pulse Wireless Activity Tracker + Sleep and Heart Rate Monitor']);">
          Pulse Wireless Activity Tracker + Sleep and Heart Rate Monitor
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
         <img alt="104-pulse-wireless-activity-tracker" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-86567" height="300" src="http://www.1stwebdesigner.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/104-pulse-wireless-activity-tracker.jpg" width="600"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         Are you sleeping well? Is your heart pumping like its supposed to? Sleep and heart problems are common to web designers because they just sit most of their working hoursand while at home.
        </p>
        <h2>
         105.
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0050SZD18/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0050SZD18&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0050SZD18/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0050SZD18&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=1stwebdesigne-20', 'OUYA']);" rel="nofollow">
          OUYA
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
         <img alt="105-ouya" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-86568" height="300" src="http://www.1stwebdesigner.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/105-ouya.jpg" width="600"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         <del>
          OUYA is a virtual reality gaming console that will be the next big thing for gaming. For decades, gamers have been yearning for virtual reality and now its here. I am certain that everyone, even non-gamers, will love this. Its the coolest item to have in this list, I swear.
         </del>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.oculusvr.com/order/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.oculusvr.com/order/', 'Oculus Rift']);">
          Oculus Rift
         </a>
         is a virtual reality gaming console (this is the real one, OUYA is different).
        </p>

         To End
