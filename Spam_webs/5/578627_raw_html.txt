       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Watch Dogs PC Recommended Specs
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-author">
         <a href="http://gamerattitude.com/author/halil-mehmet/">
          Hal
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="meta-date">
         05/04/2014
        </span>
        <span class="meta-cats">
         <a href="http://gamerattitude.com/category/news/" rel="category tag">
          News
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://gamerattitude.com/category/pc/" rel="category tag">
          PC
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- END ENTRY META -->
      </header>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <div class="post-thumbnail featured-content image-above">
        <img alt="Watch Dogs PC Recommended Specs" class="wp-post-image" data-rel="" height="0" src="" width="0"/>
       </div>
       <div class="post-item-content image-above">
        <script>
         &lt;!--
dynamicgoogletags.update();
//--&gt;
        </script>
        <p>
         Watch Dogs has gone up forpre-order on Steam and it shows theminimum and recommended PC specs for the game.
         <a href="http://store.steampowered.com/app/243470/" target="_blank">
          <br/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Minimum:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         OS: Windows Vista (SP2), Windows 7 (SP1) or Windows 8 (Please note that we only support 64 bit OSs.)
        </p>
        <p>
         Processor: Intel Core 2 Quad Q8400 @ 2.66Ghz or AMD Phenom II X4 940 @ 3.0Ghz
        </p>
        <p>
         Memory: 6 GB RAM
        </p>
        <p>
         Graphics: DirectX 11 graphics card with 1 GB Video RAM  Nvidia Geforce GTX 460 or AMD Radeon HD 5770
        </p>
        <p>
         DirectX: Version 11
        </p>
        <p>
         Hard Drive: 25 GB available space
        </p>
        <p>
         Sound Card: DirectX 9.0c Compatible Sound Card with Latest Drivers
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Recommended:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         OS: Windows Vista (SP2), Windows 7 (SP1) or Windows 8 (Please note that we only support 64 bit OSs.)
        </p>
        <p>
         Processor: Eight core  Intel Core i7-3770 @3.5 GHz or AMD FX-8350 X8 @ 4 GHz
        </p>
        <p>
         Memory: 8 GB RAM
        </p>
        <p>
         Graphics: DirectX 11 graphics card with 2 GB Video RAM  Nvidia Geforce GTX 560 ti or AMD Radeon HD 7850
        </p>
        <p>
         DirectX: Version 11
        </p>
        <p>
         Hard Drive: 25 GB available space
        </p>
        <p>
         Sound Card: DirectX 9.0c Compatible Sound Card with Latest Drivers
        </p>
        <p>
         Going by the looks of needing a Minimum of a Quad Core CPU to play, it seems
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          that it will be a verydemanding game to run.
         </span>
        </p>
