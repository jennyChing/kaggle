       <p>
        The Williams-Sonoma free junior chef classes are simply amazing. We were extremely happy to check out their recentValentines Day treats. Charm also got a chance to learn how-to make some very cool no bake sweet treats just in time for Valentines Day. The classes are inspired byJennifer Tyler Lee, creator of the award-winning food game Crunch a Color and author of The 52 New Foods Challenge cookbook.
       </p>
       <h2>
        FREE Junior Chef Classes
       </h2>
       <p>
        During the class kids made three easy, no-bake treats that are perfect for Valentines Day! The no bake class was suitable for ages 513. Whats great about these classes are they show kidshow fun and easy cooking can be while learning aboutdelicious healthy recipes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Kids will learn how-to make things frommain courses to desserts and snacks, with plenty of tasting along the way. Fun for parents to watch along and also get involved.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.williams-sonoma.com/pages/store-events/store-events/junior-chef-classes.html">
         Williams-Sonoma junior chef classes
        </a>
        are free and available at all stores; class times vary by store location.Class sizes are limited so make sure to call your local store to register today.
       </p>

         CharmPosh.com givesWilliams-Sonoma junior chef classes an A+ !
