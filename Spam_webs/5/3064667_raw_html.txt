       <h2 class="hf1">
        Jermaine Jackson
       </h2>
       <h4 class="hf2">
        Drops Big Bucks On Ferrari
       </h4>
       <h3 class="hf3-case">
        During MJ Jury Deliberations
       </h3>
      </div>
      <h5 class="article-posted-date">
       10/5/2013 7:05 AM PDT BY TMZ STAFF

      <div class="all-post-body group article-content" itemprop="articleBody">
       <div class="primary-image-swipe">
        <span>
         EXCLUSIVE
        </span>
       </div>
       <img alt="1040_jermaine_jackson_ferrari" src="http://ll-media.tmz.com/2013/10/04/1040-jermaine-jackson-ferrari-3.jpg"/>
       <strong>
        Jermaine Jackson
       </strong>
       may have put the cart before the Ferrari this week ... he bought this wildly expensive whip while the MJ jury was deciding whether to give his family as much as $40 billion.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Jermaine went to
       <strong>
        Calabasas Luxury Motorcars
       </strong>
       Monday to pick up the 2009 Ferrari California, which was listed at $169,995. Sources connected to Jermaine tell us he was able to wheel-and-deal the dealer down to the low 160s.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Jermaine didn't have to plunk down the entire amount. He traded in his Rolls Royce and got around $90k. We're told he paid the balance -- just over $70k.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       As we reported ... if
       <strong>
        Katherine
       </strong>
       would have won her lawsuit against AEG, Jermaine and her other kids would likely have been the beneficiaries of the judgment once she died. But it was not meant to be.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The only hope left is suing Dr. Murray ... which would put him in the Ford Focus market.
