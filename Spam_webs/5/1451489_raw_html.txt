         <h1 class="title">
          5 Problems with Travelling
         </h1>
         <div id="content-area">
          <div class="node node-type-blog build-mode-full clearfix" id="node-137">
           <div class="content">
            <blockquote>
             <p dir="ltr">
              <span>
               To my mind, the greatest reward and luxury of travel is to be able to experience everyday things as if for the first time, to be in a position in which almost nothing is so familiar it is taken for granted.  Bill Bryson
              </span>
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             Create a checklist of places you want to see in the
             <strong>
              <a href="http://www.goldenskytravelandtours.com/about-us">
               Philippines, mark that travel spot
              </a>
             </strong>
             and set the date of the beginning of your endless journey. Of all the vices, traveling is one of the most expensive, yet make you acquire treasure that no monetary value can amount to. Before you pack your stuff, prepare yourself with these travel side effects :
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              It is addicting
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Passion and love for travel are the personal heroine of travelers. After setting foot on one destination, they just cant seem to control themselves for booking another flight. It is addicting in a sense that it makes you crave for more adventure. Visiting places, discovering stuff beyond your imagination, and seeing with your own very eyes how majestic the world is, are what fuel your system to continue your journey.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              It makes you demanding
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             If you havent traveled before, after your first flight, youll surely have a hard time getting rid of travel hangover. You start to question why theres only 24 hours each day, 7 days a week - 2 days for weekend, a couple of holidays, and 365 days a year. Unconsciously, you become more aware how important every second is, then the rest is history. You will learn how to balance your time, social life and finances, so that you can fit in your tight schedule your next escapade.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Gives you sleepless nights
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             When reality is better than dreams, it makes it impossible for one to fall asleep. Time is precious, that you dont have the luxury to just sleep or sit in the corner waiting for things to happen. Having a plan of places to visit with definite date leaves you excited, makes your brain process thoughts continuously and the next thing youll realize is that the moons no longer up - its already morning.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              One day millionaire habit
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Money is meant to be spent. Thrifty is just a word for certified travel addicts. You have to reward yourself for every job well done, even if it means you have to spend your entire payslip for an overnight stay in one popular travel spot. Traveling is expensive, you will have to spend a lot to be in places on your checklist, and thats a sad fact.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              It breaks your heart and makes you cry
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Awe-inspiring travel destinations will break your heart and make you shed tears over and over again. You cant help but be emotional upon seeing how magical each place is. This time, you will be crying tears of joy. Or once its time to leave, you cant help but have your heart broken by this unfortunate reality.
            </p>
            <p>
             Theres more beauty that we have yet to discover, but little is our time to explore each destination. Since time and youth is not on our side, better start while you still have the energy and enthusiasm. Go and check any of the vacation packages made available by accredited Philippine travel agencies. Its the time to travel and explore!
            </p>
            <p>
             <span>
              <img alt="Wanderlust-travel" height="437" src="https://fbcdn-sphotos-c-a.akamaihd.net/hphotos-ak-prn1/72628_520725007978594_1326984837_n.jpg" title="Wanderlust-travel" width="900"/>
             </span>
            </p>
