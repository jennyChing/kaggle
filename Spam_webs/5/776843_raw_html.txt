          <p class="p1">
           In the countrys crowded restaurant industry, creativityhappens to come in handy when trying to attract business.
          </p>
          <!-- In Post -->
          <div id="ad_unit_medium_rectangle_in_post">
           <script type="text/javascript">
            googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('ad_unit_medium_rectangle_in_post') });
           </script>
          </div>
          <p>
           Coming up with a unique and memorable name for an establishment could make a huge difference to potential customers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Why would anyone want to write a review for whats-the-name-of-that-place-again? See.
          </p>
          <p class="p1">
           <img alt="Boring Restaurant Name" class="size-full wp-image-621 aligncenter" height="360" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Jackie-Chan.jpg" width="480"/>
          </p>
          <p class="p2">
           Here are a couple of places listed on the
           <a href="http://www.looloo.com" target="_blank" title="looloo Restaurant Reviews">
            looloo app
           </a>
           with some clever monikers meant to be memorable by being funny. Some are clever, some are witty, while some might leave you wanting to scratch your eyeballs out.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/39289-ang-kat-tea" target="_blank" title="Ang Kat Tea">
            Ang Kat Tea
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_622" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Ang-Kat-Tea" class="wp-image-622 size-full" height="125" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Ang-Kat-Tea.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/angkat.tea?fref=ts">
             Ang Kat Tea on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           There are a ton of tea places nowadays, and business owners love playing around with the word tea. While places like
           <a href="http://looloo.com/explore?q=serenitea" target="_blank" title="Serenitea">
            Serenitea
           </a>
           , and
           <a href="http://looloo.com/explore?q=infinitea" target="_blank" title="Infinitea">
            Infinitea
           </a>
           sound so serious, we liked that this particular tea joints name was a lot more playful and actually chose a Tagalog phrase!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/31577-brew-ha" target="_blank" title="Brew Ha!">
            Brew Ha!
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_623" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Brew-Ha" class="wp-image-623 size-full" height="175" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Brew-Ha-2.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="http://www.smsupermalls.com/smcitybacolod/food/3768/brewha">
             smsupermalls.com
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           Were pretty sure this coffee shop in Bacolod didnt choose their name with the intention of scaring anyone off (
           <em>
            bruha
           </em>
           means witch in Filipino), but you cant deny that it does have quite a ring to it. Give the place a try and you could find yourself under the spell of their bewitching brews!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/38467-bun-of-brothers" target="_blank" title="Bun of Brothers">
            Bun of Brothers
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_624" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Bun-of-Brothers" class="wp-image-624 size-full" height="81" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Bun-of-Brothers.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="http://facebook.com/BunBrothersMarikina" target="_blank" title="Bun of Brothers on Facebook">
             Bun of Brothers on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           Its name obviously a play on the WW2 miniseries
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Band_of_Brothers_(TV_miniseries)" target="_blank" title="Band of Brothers Miniseries">
            Band of Brothers,
           </a>
           the Marikina-based burger joint is actually pretty popular in the area. Its been around for just over a year so you probably wouldnt call it battle-tested, but their selection of gourmet burgers might be a good alternative to the usual fast food fare.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/38935-eat-my-gf" target="_blank" title="Eat my GF">
            <b>
             Eat My GF
            </b>
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_625" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Names-Eat-my-GF" class="wp-image-625 size-full" height="357" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Names-Eat-my-GF.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/EAT-MY-GF-Garlic-Fries/209255229090929" target="_blank" title="Eat my GF on Facebook">
             Eat my GF on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           Using a bit of shock factor by being a little
           <i>
            bastos
           </i>
           , you cant help but be at least a LITTLE bit curious about the place. Apparently, GF stands for their Garlic Fries, which the owner modeled after his favorite game time snack from California.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/11363-ken-afford" target="_blank" title="Ken Afford">
            <b>
             Ken Afford
            </b>
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_633" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Ken-Afford" class="wp-image-633 size-full" height="201" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Ken-Afford.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="http://kainankorner.blogspot.com/2012/04/ken-afford.html" target="_blank" title="Ken Afford from kainankorner.blogspot.com">
             kainankorner.blogspot.com
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           A staple in the area (and also featured in our
           <a href="http://insights.looloo.com/fearless-food-guide-katipunan/" target="_blank" title="Fearless Food Guide to Katipunan">
            Fearless Foodies Field Guide to Katipunan
           </a>
           !), Ken Afford is exactly that  good cheap eats accessible to the student population nearby. Not much has changed over the years, and were pretty sure youll be able to get their sisig and free bulalo soup for many more to come!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/39466-meats-match" target="_blank" title="Meats &amp; Match">
            Meats and Match
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_626" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Names-Meats-&amp;-Match" class="wp-image-626 size-full" height="226" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Names-Meats-Match.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="http://facebook.com/meatsandmatch" target="_blank" title="Meats and Match on Facebook">
             Meats &amp; Match on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           As the name implies, the Pampanga-based restaurant lets you choose from their different meats for a quick and affordable meal. For a simple no-frills eatery, the creativity is admirable  theyve even got a hashtag! #saucydito
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/explore?q=johnny%27s+fried+Chicken" target="_blank" title="Johnny's Fried Chicken">
            Johnnys Fried Chicken: The Fried of Marikina
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_627" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Johnny's-Fried-Chicken" class="wp-image-627 size-full" height="152" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Johnnys-Fried-Chicken.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Johnnys-Fried-Chicken/137597779609508" target="_blank" title="Johnny's Fried Chicken on Facebook">
             Johnnys Fried Chicken on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           Marikina has always been known for their shoemaking. Leather footwear may be the pride of Marikina, but apparently, Johnnys Fried Chicken is campaigning to trump that! Its a homey place with a simple
           <i>
            carinderia
           </i>
           vibe about it, but one reviewer says the chicken could hold its own against Maxs even!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/38171-nacho-fast" target="_blank" title="Nacho Fast">
            Nacho Fast
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_629" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Nacho-Fast" class="wp-image-629 size-full" height="212" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Nacho-Fast.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="http://www.jeepneygang.com/bola/lists/signs-in-da-pilipins.html" target="_blank" title="Nacho Fast on jeepneygang.com">
             jeepneygang.com
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           What do you call cheese that isnt yours? Nacho Cheese! Were guessing the places owner took his or her cue from this when he named the restaurant.Okay, so its not the most clever of names, but we appreciate the effort of making a movie snack nacho joint sound funner!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/14822-obeertime" target="_blank" title="Obeertime">
            Obeertime
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_628" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Obeertime" class="wp-image-628 size-full" height="92" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Obeertime.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Obeertime-Since-1993/209349429159502" target="_blank" title="Obeertime on Facebook">
             Obeertime on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           <i>
            Dear, late ako uuwi, nag-obeertime ako eh.
           </i>
           If you say it fast enough, shell never be the wiser. Unlike your usual roadside
           <i>
            inuman,
           </i>
           this bars name is just clever enough to be witty without being
           <i>
            too
           </i>
           tacky. We also give it props for taking overtime (ugh) and turning it into something to look forward to!
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/16840-pork-barrel-bar-and-grill-restaurant" target="_blank" title="Pork Barrel Grill and Restaurant">
            Pork Barrel Grill and Restaurant
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p class="p1">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Pork-Barrel" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1549" height="147" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Pork-Barrel.jpg" width="400"/>
          </p>
          <p class="p1">
           With the pork barrel scam being such a hot topic these days, were not sure how much of a negative vibe this places name sends out. If you believe in no such thing as bad publicity though, it cant hurt! Now, how ironic would it be to see Janet Napoles dining here??
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="http://looloo.com/p/30145-tri-mo-shawarma-co" target="_blank" title="Tri Mo Shawarma Co">
            Tri Mo Shawarma Co.
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_632" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="Funny-Restaurant-Name-Tri-Mo-Shawarma-Co" class="wp-image-632 size-full" height="238" src="http://3c9bl93o71m619w9kn2rfwinkdh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Funny-Restaurant-Name-Tri-Mo-Shawarma-Co.jpg" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Photo taken from
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/trimoshawarma" target="_blank" title="Tri Mo Shawarma Co on Facebook">
             Tri Mo Shawarma Co on Facebook
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p class="p1">
           Pretty straightforward, this one  they want you to try their shawarma! This hidden gem in QC has actually gotten
           <em>
            a lot
           </em>
           of good reviews, some mentioning the long lines people put up with for the food. Definitely worth a TRI if youre in the QC area!
          </p>
          <p class="p1">
           Did we miss anything? And yes, we know theres a carinderia called
           <em>
            Cooking ng Ina Mo
           </em>
           .
          </p>
