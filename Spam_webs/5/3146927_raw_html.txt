                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Disney Duo: Space Mountain
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5641383074227731120" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: right;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <a class="button black" href="http://www.xoxojen.com/p/info.html">
                        Press Sample
                       </a>
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     Disney Theme Parks and movies both hold a special place in my heart. From my very first theater experience of watching Ichabod and Mr. Toad with my uncle to my most recent cross country theme park vacation, Disney is definitely special to me. I was so excited to have the chance to review a Disney ride inspired polish duo made by Sweet Heart Polish and Literary Lacquers. The set is limited edition and is part of Sweet Heart Polish's ongoing Disney Duo series.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Blast off through an energy tunnel pulsating with blinking lights and ascend a 180-foot high mountain. At the top, launch into the inky blackness of the dark universe, the mysterious sounds of space and celestial music filling the void. Travel through space, whizzing past stars, comets and meteors. Feel the push and pull of gravity as you re-enter through a worm hole and return safely back to the space station.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     These polishes were inspired by the Space Mountain ride which is one of my favorite rides at Magic Kingdom.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Sirius is made by Sweet Heart Polish and is a royal blue holographic polish with silver shimmer sprinkles, like space dust. The color on my nail seems more saturated in person than in my photos, it's a very vibrant and vivid royal blue. I wore two coats and topcoat for extra shine.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="xoxoJen's swatch of Sweet Heart Polish Sirius" src="http://i474.photobucket.com/albums/rr101/bbcutsie/MUA/sweetheartpolish-sirius-swatch1b.jpg" title="xoxoJen's swatch of Sweet Heart Polish Sirius"/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="xoxoJen's swatch of Sweet Heart Polish Sirius" src="http://i474.photobucket.com/albums/rr101/bbcutsie/MUA/sweetheartpolish-sirius-swatch2b.jpg" title="xoxoJen's swatch of Sweet Heart Polish Sirius"/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="xoxoJen's swatch of Sweet Heart Polish Sirius" src="http://i474.photobucket.com/albums/rr101/bbcutsie/MUA/sweetheartpolish-sirius-swatch3b.jpg" title="xoxoJen's swatch of Sweet Heart Polish Sirius"/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The Literary Lacquers Polish, Very Sirius, is a lilac glitter holo packed with holographic sparkle and a smattering of tiny silver holo stars. I wore three coats for opaque coverage but it also makes a very pretty topper in one or two coats over your favorite shade.
                     <a href="http://www.myboyfriendhatesmakeup.com/2014/07/le-literary-lacquersweet-heart-polish.html">
                      Lacey
                     </a>
                     shows it worn over Sirius and it's beautiful that way!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="xoxoJen's swatch of Literary Lacquers Very Sirius" src="http://i474.photobucket.com/albums/rr101/bbcutsie/MUA/literarylacquer-verysirius-swatch1.jpg" title="xoxoJen's swatch of Literary Lacquers Very Sirius"/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="xoxoJen's swatch of Literary Lacquers Very Sirius" src="http://i474.photobucket.com/albums/rr101/bbcutsie/MUA/literarylacquer-verysirius-swatch2.jpg" title="xoxoJen's swatch of Literary Lacquers Very Sirius"/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="xoxoJen's swatch of Literary Lacquers Very Sirius" src="http://i474.photobucket.com/albums/rr101/bbcutsie/MUA/literarylacquer-verysirius-swatch3.jpg" title="xoxoJen's swatch of Literary Lacquers Very Sirius"/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The duo will be released at both
                     <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/LiteraryLacquer">
                      Literary Lacquer
                     </a>
                     and
                     <a href="http://www.sweetheartpolish.com">
                      Sweet Heart Polish
                     </a>
                     web shops at 2pm Central time on Saturday July 5th.

    navigate:function(keyword){ 
        clearTimeout(this.setting.playtimer) 
        this.setting.totalsteps=100000 //if any of the nav buttons are clicked on, set totalsteps limit to an "unreachable" number 
        if (!isNaN(parseInt(keyword))){ 
            this.showslide(parseInt(keyword)) 
        } 
        else if (/(prev)|(next)/i.test(keyword)){ 
            this.showslide(keyword.toLowerCase()) 
        } 
        else{ //if play|pause button 
            var slideshow=this 
            var $playbutton=$(this.setting.navbuttons).eq(1) 
            if (!this.setting.ispaused){ //if pause Gallery 
                this.setting.autoplay[0]=false 
                $playbutton.attr({title:'Play', src:simpleGallery_navpanel.images[1]}) 
            } 
            else if (this.setting.ispaused){ //if play Gallery 
                this.setting.autoplay[0]=true

    showslide:function(keyword){ 
        var slideshow=this 
        var setting=slideshow.setting 
        var totalimages=setting.imagearray.length 
        var imgindex=(keyword=="next")? (setting.curimage&lt;totalimages-1? setting.curimage+1 : 0) 
            : (keyword=="prev")? (setting.curimage&gt;0? setting.curimage-1 : totalimages-1) 
            : Math.min(keyword, totalimages-1)

                        setting.playtimer=setTimeout(function(){slideshow.showslide('next')}, setting.autoplay[1])

            }) //end callback function

        setting.bglayer=(setting.bglayer==0)? 1 : 0 
        setting.curimage=imgindex

            setting.$descpanel.css({visibility:'visible'}) 
            setting.descdiv.innerHTML=setting.imagearray[imgindex][3] 
        } 
        else if (setting.longestdesc!=""){ //if at least one slide contains a description (feature is enabled) 
            setting.descdiv.innerHTML=null 
            setting.$descpanel.css({visibility:'hidden'})
        } 
    },

        var setting=this.setting

    }
