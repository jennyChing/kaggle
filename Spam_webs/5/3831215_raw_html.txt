       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Homemade Crme de Menthe
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         Posted on
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-11-29T11:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 29, 2011
         </time>
         Written by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://boulderlocavore.com/author/boul7429/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Toni Dash
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://boulderlocavore.com/homemade-creme-de-menthe/#comments">
           50 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-1-71BXebfTI/TtQ5yGqcwhI/AAAAAAAACvA/osNtnCiKHwk/s1600/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1085-1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1085-11.jpg" width="428"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          I recently got my mitts on my Moms recipe file to pilfer for interesting recipes that match my seasonal food focus and for any family treasures.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          We are fortunate to have handwritten recipe cards back to my Great Grandmother which has proven to be a gift revealed to have access to favorites passed down via the dining tables (and cocktail glasses) of multiple generations.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          Weve pieced together decades of food-related memories for our family and our friends who have taken part in various relatives, kitchen exploits.
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          You might expect when going through a mothers recipe file that youd find a favorite family apple pie recipe, maybe the old faithful lasagna well remembered from ones youth, or a favorite holiday cookie recipe.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          Apples dont fall far from the tree my friends so I suspect it would not shock you that in addition to the family culinary nostalgia I found a number of DIY liqueur recipes.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          Quelle surprise.
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Uq1SKofQpd4/TtQ7S43fFCI/AAAAAAAACvI/996z-gGFvec/s1600/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1059.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1059.jpg" width="314"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           When I found a recipe for
           <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
            Crme de Menthe
           </strong>
           (mint liqueur) I was thrilled.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           With
           <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
            Grasshopper Pie
           </strong>
           being in new rotation and the mint-i-ness of the season setting in, this seemed a fun concoction perfectly timed.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Most importantly Im pleased to report, its good!
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           In a head to head taste test, it beat out the store purchased bottle of Crme de Menthe.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           The color is the same, the depth of mint identical but its a smidge smoother than the purchased Crme de Menthe.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           It takes mere minutes to assemble and has a whopping 5 ingredients.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           What a perfect time to whip up a batch for entertaining, gifts or just for your own enjoyment!
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             CRME DE MENTHE
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <em style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">
          <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
           <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
            Makes:
            <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
            </span>
            10-11 cups
           </span>
          </span>
         </em>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           8 cups water
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           8 cups sugar
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           1 pint 190 proof grain alcohol (I used Everclear brand)
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           3 tablespoons peppermint extract
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           1 tablespoon green food coloring
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo2; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
            1.
           </span>
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Combine the sugar and water.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Heat until the sugar dissolved (happens quickly; no need to boil it), stirring frequently to hasten the process.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Allow to cool.
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt 0.5in; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo2; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-latin;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
            2.
           </span>
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           Once cool add the grain alcohol, peppermint extract and green coloring.
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-MPJDhLVusXg/TtQ7i1upxdI/AAAAAAAACvQ/hon6fo9Tq98/s1600/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1055.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1055.jpg" width="428"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           Now that you have it, what can you do with it?
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Grasshopper Cocktail
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           Essentially a chocolate mint cocktail!
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           1 part Crme de Menthe
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           1 part Crme de Cacao
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt 0.5in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -0.25in;">
         <span style="font-family: Symbol; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
          <span style="mso-list: Ignore;">
           
           <span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           1 part cream
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
         <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
          <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
           Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with crushed ice.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Shake and pour into a martini glass. (Tip: for the bright green color, use clear or light Crme de Cacao.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           The darker version will still be delicious but the color of the drink is more like drab spinach.not so appetizing).
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="text-indent: -0.25in;">
        </div>
        <div style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-vk6SwTBoa6Q/TtQ79g5I8_I/AAAAAAAACvY/j7kF__BW3so/s1600/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1074.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="640" src="//boulderlocavore.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Boulder+Locavore+creme+de+menthe+1074.jpg" width="428"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
          <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
           A
           <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
            Grasshopper
           </strong>
           cocktail and shots of Crme de Menthe in edible candy cane shot glasses
          </span>
