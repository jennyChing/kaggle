     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Oh, My God
       </b>
      </i>
      (stylised as
      <i>
       <b>
        Oh, My God!!
       </b>
      </i>
      ) is an Indian film
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bollywood_films_of_2008" title="Bollywood films of 2008">
       released on 5 December 2008
      </a>
      . The film is about an employee who puts more time trying to get people to invest in a money-making scheme.
     </p>
     <p>
      <br/>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">
       Plot
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Oh,_My_God_(2008_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Vinay Pathak stars as Rajendra, a salesman who wants to make it big in life but through ethical ways. Suman (Divya Dutta) is Rajendra's wife, who stands by her husband. As Rajendra continues to fail in his various money-making schemes, Suman pleads with God to help her husband.
     </p>
     <p>
      God (played in human form by Saurabh Shukla) tries to help Rajendra become successful but is constantly frustrated as Rajendra fails to recognize the opportunities.
     </p>
