         <h2>
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Ruby_and_Sapphire" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Pokmon Ruby and Sapphire">
           Pokemon Ruby and Sapphire
          </a>
          remakes lets players create their own gyms
         </h2>
         <p>
          Excitement around Pokemon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire has been raised up a notch again.
          <a href="http://www.pokemon.com/us/pokemon-news/the-secret-is-out-on-super-secret-bases/">
           The Pokemon Company put out an announcement
          </a>
          that has been spreading like wildfire. Word has come down about changes to the secret bases that were so popular in the original versions of the game. The secret bases will be getting a host of new features in the remakes of the game. There will be a whole new level of connectivity between players in their secret bases. Players will also have greater control than every how their bases are set up.
         </p>
         <p>
          The most interesting change to the secret base formula though is battling. Pokemon R/S originally let players battle each other in their bases by connecting their game boys. The remakes of these classic
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_%28video_game_series%29" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Pokmon (video game series)">
           Pokemon games
          </a>
          will take it a step further by giving players a whole new level of choice.
         </p>
         <p>
          When playing Pokemon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire players can set up their bases to act like gym. Trainers will be able to set up mazes, traps, and decide what types of battles are allowed. It will give players the feeling of running a
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gameplay_of_Pok%C3%A9mon" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Gameplay of Pokmon">
           Pokemon Gym
          </a>
          complete with challengers.
         </p>
         <p>
          Thanks to the 3DS ability to connect players together the new Pokemon R/S will let you have challengers from all over. Now players will be able to continue challenging gym leaders even after finishing the main story.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Pokemon Company has even developed perks to encourage players to use these new secret base features. Becoming secret pals with other players by visiting their bases opens up special skills. Details on this are scarce but the Pokemon Company gave a little insight. Becoming secret pals lets you share decorations, level up a chosen Pokemon, and speed up the hatching of an egg.
         </p>
         <p>
          As you connect and battle with more people more special skills will become available to you. It is all designed to encourage players to come together to use their secret bases to grow their teams. Being able to build your own Pokemon Gym and gain special skills is just the start. Omega Ruby &amp; Alpha Sapphire have a lot more to offer players secret bases.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1342" style="width: 650px">
          <a href="http://iwbag.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Hoenn-Region.jpg">
           <img alt="Pokeymon Omega - Hoenn Region" class="wp-image-1342 size-full" height="415" src="http://iwbag.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Hoenn-Region.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Pokeymon Omega  Hoenn Region
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Capture the flag is one of them. Players will be able to visit other secret bases to capture another players flag to get placed on leader boards. The more flags that your able to take the higher your ranking will be. Higher rankings give you improved secret pal abilities.
         </p>
         <p>
          To go along with these benefits the Pokemon Company has made it easier to connect with other players. You will be able to create a special QR code for your base so that other players can get to your base. These codes will be public so you can hunt down a particular trainer you want to battle.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.pokemonrubysapphire.com/">
           Pokemon Omega Ruby &amp; Alpha Sapphire
          </a>
          have been described as brand new takes on the classic games. Looking at the announcements that have been made its easy to believe that.
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=34.991675,135.766294444&amp;spn=0.01,0.01&amp;q=34.991675,135.766294444 (Nintendo)&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation" target="_blank" title="Nintendo">
           Nintendo
          </a>
          and the Pokemon Company seem to be going out of their way to break the mold on these games.
         </p>
         <p>
          We will be able to see for ourselves how well they did when the games release on November 21st. In the meantime stay tuned to I Wanna Be A Geek for all your Pokemon related news.
         </p>
