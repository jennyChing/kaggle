					&lt;h1 class="entry-title"&gt;WAIT !!! DON&#8217;T SLAP &#8216;EM YET !!!&lt;/h1&gt;
										&lt;div class="entry-meta"&gt;
						&lt;span class="onDate date updated"&gt;&lt;i class="icon-time icon-metas" title="Date"&gt;&lt;/i&gt;
					&lt;a href="http://sheperiod.com/wait-dont-slap-em-yet/" rel="bookmark"&gt;December 23, 2014&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span class="bl_categ"&gt;&lt;i class="icon-folder-open icon-metas" title="Categories"&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;a href="http://sheperiod.com/motivation/" rel="tag"&gt;Motivation&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://sheperiod.com/uhm-hmm/" rel="tag"&gt;She...period&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt; 					&lt;/div&gt;&lt;!-- .entry-meta --&gt;

					&lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;
						&lt;input class="jpibfi" type="hidden" &gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://sheperiod.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/image11.jpg"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-515" src="http://sheperiod.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/image11-300x288.jpg" alt="image" width="300" height="288" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Calm down, DO NOT SPITE THEE !! Ok lets be honest, weve all had run-ins or come in contact with people we dont like. I know Ive had more than several of these experiences, and have been on the other side as well. Now these people may be your family members, colleagues, supervisor, roommate, neighbor, and even your spouse. And at the mention of their name or sight of them we have become irritated and lost our God given sense. But what is it that we dont like about this person?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Lets take a look here: Is it the way this person speaks? Their lack of respect for themselves and others? Does he/she smell? Too much attitude? Not easy to get along with? Too vulgar? Whatever it is, you have built up a case of If this such and such say something crazy to me ONE MORE TIME!!! Hm, now what is this solving? What has happened is that youve allowed someone that you dont get along with, control your very thought process and action. Believe it or not THEY HAVE THE POWER, because you have empowered them. Im not writing to discuss right from wrong, the power and the powerless, my desire is to discuss the action of choosing to change within.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Now I know I should be writing thank you notes to each and every person Ive had difficulty with. What these individuals have brought in my life, besides being extremely annoyed and uncomfortable, was a challenge for me to look at my own behavior. To look at the woman in the mirror and decide how I wanted to live my life. I decided to be in control of my perspective and temperament.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;It is inevitable that you will meet those you just dont like and agree with. And thats ALRIGHT. Allow yourself to learn in moments of adversity. Learn to take control of your thoughts and reaction. Learn to be fluid and flexible. In the end, agree to disagree, learn the lesson, an as my folks say, Keep it movin !&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;P.S. Its okay if the lesson learned isnt understood immediately, or even days later. **But just in case, do not allow adversity to attack your essence of abundance. ; )&lt;/p&gt;
