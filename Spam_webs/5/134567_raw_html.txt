          <div class="post-content post-content-full">
           <p dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-4511387d-0b9a-cf01-9979-61ce62ccdb89">
            <strong>
             Manage inventory and orders from multiple Amazon marketplaces through Orderhive
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            The Amazon marketplace continues to be the leading marketplace until date and offers retailers one of the fastest way to boost sales and profits. With a million customers on tap worldwide, it is a serious platform that all profit-driven retailers must consider.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            Orderhive makes selling on multiple Amazon marketplaces quick and simple as possible. Integrating your multiple Amazon stores with Orderhive, makes managing your back-end operation easy. Enjoy a complete Amazon inventory management and order management software solution that connects your stock to your ecommerce website, eBay, ShopClues and many more.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Steps to integrate your Amazon marketplaces with Orderhive
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            Orderhive is fully-integrated with Amazon marketplaces that include Amazon.in, Amazon.ca, Amazon.com and Amazon.co.uk.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            The first thing you need to do is register your Amazon account with Amazon Marketplace Web Services (MWS), this will allow your seller account to communicate and integrate with Orderhive.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <img alt="amazonmws" class="lazyload aligncenter size-full wp-image-1397" data-original="http://www.orderhive.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/amazonmws.png" height="298" width="440"/>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Register with MWS
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            If you have a (.in) Amazon seller account go to
            <a href="http://developer.amazonservices.in/">
             http://developer.amazonservices.in.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            If you have a (.ca) Amazon seller account go to
            <a href="http://developer.amazonservices.ca/">
             http://developer.amazonservices.ca.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            If you have a (.com) Amazon seller account go to
            <a href="http://developer.amazonservices.com/">
             http://developer.amazonservices.com.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            If you have a (.co.uk.) Amazon seller account go to
            <a href="http://developer.amazonservices.co.uk/">
             http://developer.amazonservices.co.uk.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            To integrate your Amazon marketplaces with Orderhive we would require the
            <strong>
             Channel name Merchant ID, Marketplace ID
            </strong>
            and
            <strong>
             Merchant Token
            </strong>
            .
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Step 1
            </strong>
             To get
            <strong>
             Merchant ID
            </strong>
            and
            <strong>
             Marketplace ID
            </strong>
            follow the below mentioned steps:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Click the
              <strong>
               Sign up for MWS
              </strong>
              button.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              <strong>
               Log into
              </strong>
              your Amazon seller account.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              On the
              <strong>
               MWS registration page
              </strong>
              , click the
              <strong>
               button
              </strong>
              for  I want to give a developer access to my Amazon seller account with MWS.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              In the
              <strong>
               Developers Name text box
              </strong>
              , type the name of the
              <strong>
               third-party developer
              </strong>
              to whom youre giving access. This doesnt need to be the exact name; it is merely for your reference in the future.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              In the
              <strong>
               Developer Account Number text box
              </strong>
              , enter below numbers as MWS developer account identifier.
             </p>
             <p>
              For Amazon.com  5251-3833-1511
             </p>
             <p>
              For Amazon.co.uk.  0390-4433-2593
             </p>
             <p>
              For Amazon.ca: 5251-3833-1511
             </p>
             <p>
              For Amazon.in: 2758-3494-4882
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Click the
              <strong>
               Next
              </strong>
              button.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              <strong>
               Accept
              </strong>
              the Amazon MWS License Agreements and click the
              <strong>
               Next
              </strong>
              button.
             </p>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <ul>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Copy your account identifiers (
              <strong>
               Merchant ID
              </strong>
              and
              <strong>
               Marketplace ID
              </strong>
              ) as your third-party developer needs them in order to programmatically access your Amazon seller account.
             </p>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Step 2
            </strong>
             To get the
            <strong>
             Merchant Token
            </strong>
            follow the below mentioned steps:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              <strong>
               Login
              </strong>
              to your seller panel.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Go to
              <strong>
               Settings
              </strong>
              &gt;
              <strong>
               Account Info
              </strong>
              .
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              You will find your
              <strong>
               merchant token
              </strong>
              at the
              <strong>
               bottom
              </strong>
              of the page.
             </p>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <img alt="amazone" class="lazyload aligncenter size-full wp-image-1398" data-original="http://www.orderhive.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/amazone.png" height="298" width="440"/>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Step 3  Head back to the App and paste details
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Now that you have your the details, head back to Orderhive portal.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              For the
              <strong>
               Channel Name
              </strong>
              , as mentioned earlier use the name of your Amazon store.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              For the
              <strong>
               Amazon Merchant ID
              </strong>
              , use the one created step 1.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              For the
              <strong>
               Amazon Marketplace ID
              </strong>
              , use the one created step 1.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              For the
              <strong>
               Merchant token
              </strong>
              , use the one created in step 2.
             </p>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p dir="ltr">
            Now after you fill the above details and before you integrate your Amazon store with Orderhive, you will be asked as How would you like to manage your inventory?
            <strong>
             Auto sync
            </strong>
            or
            <strong>
             Manual sync.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             When Auto sync on
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            This setting will allow you up-to-date inventory levels. This means any changes made to your inventory in your Orderhives account will be auto-updated in all your Amazon stores as well as other channels.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             When Manual sync on
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            This setting will allow you manually sync inventory for your Orderhive account and Amazon storefronts individually. This means any changes made to your inventory in your Orderhives account will not be auto-updated in all your Amazon stores. In such cases any changes done in inventory will only be updated only when you opt to sync through the channels dashboard screen.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Benefits of this integration
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Get your inventory on Amazon fast
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            Orderhives multi-channel integration with your Amazon store will give you an ability to manage and update inventory levels across multiple Amazon stores and other marketplaces. So when a product is sold on one of your Amazon store or marketplace, Orderhive will quickly and automatically update stock levels on all marketplaces. This ensures your inventory has maximum exposure whilst minimizing the risk of overselling.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Boost your order processing
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            Orderhive will help you manage a faster and more accurate order processing to improve your customer service satisfaction, reduce labor costs and increase your sales.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             This feature will allow:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              You sync your orders coming from multiple marketplaces in one screen.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              View and bulk process all your orders from a single screen.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Batch process orders with a click.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Easily manage multiple orders for the same customers.
             </p>
            </li>
            <li dir="ltr">
             <p dir="ltr">
              Up-to-date real-time analytics of all your orders and saves order history for future reference.
             </p>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             Optimize your inventory across multiple Amazon store and marketplaces
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            With Orderhives Amazon integration you can see across your business operations in real-time and get a complete visibility of your best selling products, your top customers, which marketplaces are performing best, and what your profits and losses are looking like. This way you can optimize your inventory in the correct area and make maximum profit.
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            To get a 30 day free trial with Orderhive, please call us at 91-79- 65445102 or write us at support@orderhive.com. We will respond you quickly.
           </p>
