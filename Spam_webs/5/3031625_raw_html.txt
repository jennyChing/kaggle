           <h1>
            How to Prevent Home Break-Ins
           </h1>
           <h2>
            Deter Burglars from Targeting Your Home  Infographic
           </h2>
           <p>
            Halloween is scary for lots of reasons, most of which are fun and a great way to celebrate the holiday! One not-so-fun scary thing that can happen at Halloween, or at any time of year, is a home break-in. Burglars are always looking for ways to break into your home. There are many ways to deter potential intruders, such as keeping a tidy lawn, suspending newspaper, mail, and package delivery, installing a
            <a href="/home-security-systems">
             home security system
            </a>
            , etc. Keep the monsters at bay and help prevent home break-ins throughout the year by using the tips that are detailed in this helpful Infographic.
           </p>
