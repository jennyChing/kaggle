      <article class="post-165 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail category-april-fools category-food-fun-recipes category-fun-for-kids category-holidays category-recipes entry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Easy April Fools Day Breakfast
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-03-18T14:39:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          March 18, 2013
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.cleanandscentsible.com/author/jenn" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jenn Lifford
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.cleanandscentsible.com/2013/03/easy-april-fools-day-breakfast.html#comments">
           30 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <div style="text-align: center;">
          For the past couple of years I have done an
          <span style="font-size: large;">
           <span style="color: #e06666;">
            April Fools Day
            <span style="font-size: large;">
             B
            </span>
            reakfast
           </span>
          </span>
          for the boys. It takes hardly any time to put together but they always think that it is
          <i>
           SO
          </i>
          funny and talk about it multiple times throughout the year. Kids love April Fools day jokes!
          <br class="none"/>
          <br class="none"/>
         </div>
         <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast.jpg">
           <img alt="Fun April Fool's Day breakfast.  The kids will love this!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9143" height="667" src="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast.jpg" width="500"/>
           <br class="none"/>
          </a>
          Last year we did a simple breakfast of cereal, a poached egg and juice.
          <br class="none"/>
          <br class="none"/>
         </div>
         <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-3.jpg">
           <img alt="Fun April Fool's Day breakfast.  The kids will love this!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9146" height="255" src="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-3.jpg" width="510"/>
           <br class="none"/>
          </a>
          For the cereal, I filled up a bowl with cereal the night before, added some milk {until I could just see it below the cereal} and placed it in the freezer over night. The next morning, I brought the cereal out before the kids came downstairs and made sure they saw me add the milk to top it off a bit.
          <br class="none"/>
          <br class="none"/>
         </div>
         <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-4.jpg">
           <img alt="Fun April Fool's Day breakfast.  The kids will love this!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9142" height="255" src="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-4.jpg" width="510"/>
           <br class="none"/>
          </a>
          The egg is just vanilla yogurt and a peach half.
          <br class="none"/>
          <br class="none"/>
         </div>
         <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Fun April Fool's Day breakfast.  The kids will love this!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9144" height="375" src="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-1.jpg" width="500"/>
           <br class="none"/>
          </a>
          For the juice I just made some jello in a cup and added a straw part way through hardening.
          <br class="none"/>
          <br class="none"/>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-2.jpg">
            <img alt="Fun April Fool's Day breakfast.  The kids will love this!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9145" height="667" src="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/April-Fools-Day-Breakfast-2.jpg" width="500"/>
            <br class="none"/>
           </a>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            It really doesnt take much to make them happy!
            <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cf.cleanandscentsible.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
            <br class="none"/>
            <br class="none"/>
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            If you are looking for more fun April Fools Day ideas, you can find lots more
            <a href="http://www.cleanandscentsible.com/2012/03/april-fools-day-ideas.html" target="_blank">
             <span style="color: #e06666;">
              here
             </span>
            </a>
            <span style="color: #e06666;">
             .
            </span>
            For older kids, it would be fun to team up with them to play a prank on Dad too!
           </span>
          </div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            <br class="none"/>
            So what do you do on April Fools Day? I am working on this years plan!
           </span>
          </p>
