        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Makeup Wars | My Favourite Detox Mask and Overnight Treatment
        </h1>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
       <figure class="base-image">
        <img alt="makeup wars masks" class="attachment-big-size wp-post-image" height="473" itemprop="image" src="http://fw010416-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/makeup-wars-masks-1050x473.jpg" width="1050"/>
       </figure>
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8 column-1">
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           This week the
           <strong>
            Makeup Wars
           </strong>
           team is sharing our
           <strong>
            favourite masks and intensive treatments
           </strong>
           . I am sharing with you a couple ofrecently discovered products that instantly became favourites. I actually like to use them together for a real one, two punch to liven up and restore my skin.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="IMG_6389" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-22848" height="420" src="http://fw010416-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6389-700x420.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.perilouslypale.com/2014/09/makeup-wars-favourite-detox-mask-overnight-treatment.html&amp;media=http://www.perilouslypale.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6389-700x420.jpg&amp;description=Makeup Wars | My Favourite Detox Mask and Overnight Treatment')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The
           <strong>
            Bio-Beaut by Nuxe Vitamin-Rich Detox Mask with Orange Water
           </strong>
           ($27 CAD/50ml) was just released this month exclusively at Shoppers Drug Mart. This mask has the most incredible and invigorating citrus scent thanks to the Orange, Lemon, and Mandarin Essential Oils it contains. It is a gel formula that once massaged into the skin turns into a sensual oil. It traps impurities on the surface the way a cleansing oil would. The Organic Sesame Oil and Sugar Esters combined with the essential oils are responsible for the unique 3 stage texture of this mask. The final stage is that once you add water to remove the mask it transforms into a milky emulsion for easy rinsing. The mask goes beyond just clarification of the skin. It also contains Organic Myrtle Leaf Extract which has a high concentration of tannins and polyphenols to deeply detoxify the skin.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="IMG_6393" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-22849" height="525" src="http://fw010416-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6393-700x525.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.perilouslypale.com/2014/09/makeup-wars-favourite-detox-mask-overnight-treatment.html&amp;media=http://www.perilouslypale.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6393-700x525.jpg&amp;description=Makeup Wars | My Favourite Detox Mask and Overnight Treatment')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           I love everything about this mask. The delicious scent, the sensorial experience of applying it and experiencing the changing textures, and of course the resulting clarified and detoxified complexion.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The second product I want to introduce you too is a new product that is being released tomorrow. The
           <a href="http://bit.ly/YJFC9C">
            <strong>
             NARS NARSskin Restorative Night Treatment
            </strong>
           </a>
           ($95 CAD/30ml) is an overnight mask that helps replenish dull and dry skin so you can awaken to a renewed and revitalized complexion the next morning. This treatment is another transforming formula. It starts as a very dense solid gel. Onceapplied and massaged into the skin it melts into the most heavenly texture. TheAdvanced Gel Matrix Technology creates a veil that locks in moisture, recharging skin at while you sleep so youawaken to a nourished, softer, more luminous complexionwith a 146% boost in hydration. A little bit goes a long way with this product. I have used it several times and Im still getting the hang of resisting scooping to much from the jar. At first I was nervous that the jar only contained 30ml but given how little you need for each treatment that will last a long time.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="IMG_6397" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-22850" height="524" src="http://fw010416-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6397-700x524.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.perilouslypale.com/2014/09/makeup-wars-favourite-detox-mask-overnight-treatment.html&amp;media=http://www.perilouslypale.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6397-700x524.jpg&amp;description=Makeup Wars | My Favourite Detox Mask and Overnight Treatment')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The formula contains natural almond, plum, and rice bran oils which are packed with essential fatty acids and omegas. This helps reinforce the skins natural lipid barrier while infusing it with antioxidants. Salicornia extract works to boost the hydration level of the skin over time and Alaria extract helps reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. NARS exclusive Light Reflecting Complex features a blend of ingredients that work in synergy to boost skins clarity, infusing it with moisture and minerals. I just love how soft, smooth, and plump my skin is when I wake up. I cant believe the difference in my skin. It looks practically poreless and it somehow seems to magically erases my wrinkles. Usually when I wake up in the morning I have quite a few sleep wrinkles and my existing wrinkles are accentuated for several hours after having my face smooshed up on my pillow. Now, instead of having those wrinkles look deeper and aggravated, they are barely visible. I look better, feel better, and my makeup goes on more smoothly. Im becoming a little too hooked on this little funky square jar to the point Iveused it three nights in a row! This is my first experience with NARSskin but I am determined to try more from the range.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Available October 1, 2014 at select NARS retailers (including
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1CALql0">
            Hudsons Bay
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://bit.ly/YJFC9C">
            Sephora
           </a>
           , and Murale) and November 1 at remaining NARS retailers (including Holt Renfrew), and
           <a href="http://rstyle.me/ad/eu2nsbtnw">
            www.narscosmetics.com
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="IMG_6401" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-22851" height="524" src="http://fw010416-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6401-700x524.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.perilouslypale.com/2014/09/makeup-wars-favourite-detox-mask-overnight-treatment.html&amp;media=http://www.perilouslypale.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6401-700x524.jpg&amp;description=Makeup Wars | My Favourite Detox Mask and Overnight Treatment')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Have you tried anything from the NARSskin line?  Do you love the idea of a detox mask that transforms from a refreshing gel to a delicious smelling oil like the Bio-Beaut by Nuxe Detox Mask?
