So why not go to the source  if you have a reader or bookworm that you need to buy for then there is no- one better to advise you than me as I amthe worlds biggest bookworm.

The houses are built to stay cool which is great in summer but come winter we take a stroll outside to warm up! So I huddled down in my office next to my fan heater and let my imagination and book gluttony take me on a road of discovery to pick the perfect Christmas gifts for the readers &amp; bookworms in your life. Hope I eased your shopping burden just a bit with these gifts that I can assure you any bookworm will love.
