      <h1 class="post-title">
       Autumn Outfit Inspirations
      </h1>
      <p class="metadata">
       <span class="date">
        14 Nov 2012
       </span>
       <a class="comments" href="http://www.alinaloves.com/2012/11/autumn-outfit-inspirations.html#disqus_thread">
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="http://www.alinaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/21.jpg" width="426"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       When the temperature outside plummets, all we want to do initially is to wrap ourselves warm and cover every inch of our bodies from the cold (I certainly do that  I guess its the initial temperature shock and this first gust of cold wind). However, we can still cover up and wear lots of clothes, look stylish AND feel warm. Autumn outfits are probably the most stylish out of the whole years plethora of outfits. Just think layers of gorgeous fabrics (tweed, leather, chiffon, cotton), chunky knits thrown over dresses (those summer dresses that you dont want to part with just yet), variety of shoes and boots, heavy jewellery and more defined make up. With all of this in mind, Ive put together few outfits that inspire me to stay stylish (and at the same time cosy and warm) throughout this season.
      </p>
