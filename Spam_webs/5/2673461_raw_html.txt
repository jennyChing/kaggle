         <p>
          Something must be wrong with the Internet. Its been almost 24 hours and I havent seen anything new about Dez Bryant.
         </p>
         <p>
          No fresh lawsuits alleging he trashed a house or didnt pay his jewelry bills. According to court documents, one things apparent. Bryant never ordered a diamond-encrusted belt buckle.
         </p>
         <p>
          If he owned a belt, its obvious somebody needs to take it to his backside. But the Dallas Cowboys receiver is basking in lockout immunity. Teams cant talk to, much less discipline their employees. Dez and his entourage can saunter through Neiman Marcus with their pants around their knees. If anyone in police uniform objects, Bryant can cuss them out without having to worry hell immediately be called into Principal Roger Goodells office.
         </p>
         <p>
          Not that Bryant ever worried much about that stuff. But his teammates, the NFLPA or The SuperNanny need to corral this kid because hes destroying the players public relations strategy.
         </p>
         <p>
          They want to look like oppressed victims in their labor battle. And heres Bryant dopily rubbing the most sensitive nerves of millions of fans.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lets be honest, watching a cocky young guy throw money around irritates the hell out of people who are worried if theyll get a paycheck next week. It really rankles them when Mr. Bling is not even paying his bills.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its pointless to say that the vast majority of NFLPA members dress and act like adults. Boneheads like Bryant and Aqib Talib are shaping the pubics perception.
         </p>
         <p>
          By all accounts, Bryant isnt the sociopath Talib allegedly is. At least he hasnt been charged with trying to settle a squabble with bullets this week. Dez is just a product of a broken home and years of jock worship.
         </p>

         <p>
          The guy makes Bart Simpson look like the Duke of Cornwall. Hes certainly not the only one in the NFL. But he plays for the Cowboys, meaning every misstep is magnified.
         </p>
         <p>
          The NFLPA would like you to concentrate on the injustice of an 18-game season. All fans are talking about is Dezs not-so-excellent adventures.
         </p>
         <p>
          It started with that mall excursion last week. Bryant claims he wasnt violating NorthPark Malls dress code, his posse was. Whether his underwear was showing or not, Dez certainly got it in a wad when off-duty cops asked his group to hike it or leave.
         </p>
         <p>
          From there, the hits just kept a coming. Two lawsuits were filed in Texas claiming Bryant loaded up on almost $800,000 in jewelry that he hasnt paid for. There was another one in Oklahoma claiming Bryant got almost $200,000 in jewelry and other gifts while he was at Oklahoma State.
         </p>
         <p>
          If true, he apparently didnt use the money to pay rent. A Tulsa paper reported Monday that Bryant was sued for $3,500 last year over allegations he damaged a rental house and didnt pay for living there.
         </p>
         <p>
          So far, nobody has tied him to the Fukushima nuclear power plant malfunctions. But the lockout is just getting started.
         </p>

          Even ex-mentor/jewelry expert Deion Sanders has gone on Dallas radio shows distancing himself from Bryant.

          I told the Cowboys from Day One that he needs help, Sanders said.
         </p>
         <p>
          Hes not getting it from his advisors. They smoothed over things with the mall, which now says Bryant is welcome to return. The way he throws money around, merchants probably dont care if Dez wears no pants.
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats just the kind of sucking up that has created this little monster. If you didnt know better, youd think Bryant was a plant. Owners concocted him to make the players look like a bunch of spoiled brats.
         </p>
         <p>
          They need to realize that Bryant makes everybody associated with the NFL look bad. The two sides need to call an emergency meeting and pass some sort of Dez Rule. Waive the ban on employer contact.
         </p>
         <p>
          Get him constant supervision. Get him out of the public eye.
