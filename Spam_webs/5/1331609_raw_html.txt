         <p>
          A start-up company called
          <a href="http://www.envisionsolar.com/index.php?page=portfolio&amp;id=1" rel="nofollow">
           Envision Solar
          </a>
          has begun installing solar canopies in the parking lots of shopping centers. These lots, usually barren, heat-producing landscapes, can now provide shade for customers, while generating up to a half-megawatt of electricity. Envisions Solar Groves incorporate lights that turn on automatically at night, while blocking skyward
          <a href="http://www.darksky.org/mc/page.do" rel="nofollow">
           light pollution
          </a>
          .
