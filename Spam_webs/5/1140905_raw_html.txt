             <p>
              What distinguishes one fringe, one set of
              <a href="http://www.fashionising.com/trends/b--fringe-bangs-5456.html">
               bangs
              </a>
              , from another is not so much the cut but the attitude with which it is worn. In 2012 there are several ways to carry it that will easily have it classed as being an
              <a href="http://www.fashionising.com/trends/b--2012-hairstyles-2012-haircuts-2012-hair-trends-4338.html">
               on trend hairstyle
              </a>
              : this is one of them.
             </p>
             <p>
              Messy, scruffy even if you will, but in that devil-may-care way that looks like youve perpetually just rolled out of bed, its what the girls chasing the boys who wear the
              <a href="http://www.fashionising.com/trends/b--mens-indie-hairstyle-7914.html">
               mens indie rock hairstyle
              </a>
              can often be seen sporting. Its far from alternative or mainstream, but it does connote something that appeals to a particular demographic. Its youthful with a grungy edge.
             </p>
             <p>
              Find out more after the break. And if youd like to keep up to date with all of the latest fashion trends and inspiration on how to wear them, subscribe to
              <a href="http://www.fashionising.com/newsletter/">
               Fashionising.coms newsletter
              </a>
              or
              <a href="http://feeds.feedburner.com/RogueFashionTrends" rel="nofollow">
               RSS feed
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              <span id="more-19479">
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3>
              Indie bangs: the look
             </h3>
             <p>
              Shes just as likely to pair her sequin minidress with combat boots as she is heels; denim cutoff shorts are her summer staple and borrowing from the boys is a given. Youll often spot her with the band, or getting muddy at Glastonbury. While good girls wear their bangs neat and groomed, shes happy to have something of a bad girl rep. For her, the uncombed strands covering her eyes are a sign advertising her carefree attitude.
             </p>
             <p>
              Thats not to say you have to be a particular type of girl for this hairstyle to suit you. But fashion is often about projecting something, so its the perfect style to choose if you
              <em>
               want
              </em>
              to give off a particular vibe.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="grunge fringe hairstyle" class="alignnone" src="http://i0.wp.com/cs1.fashionising.com/media/hair-trends/grunge-fringe-hairstyle.jpg?w=620" title="Grunge fringe hairstyle"/>
             </p>
             <h3>
              Who to channel: well-known indie fringes
             </h3>
             <p>
              If you want more inspiration you need only look to the worlds of fashion, music and film:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               You can channel the likes of Zooey Deschaniel for a sweeter look, though in 2012 a little inkling of reckless abandon is a good thing.
              </li>
              <li>
               For that, look to
               <strong>
                Daisy Lowe
               </strong>
               as inspiration. She rolls the rock star look together with grungy modern pin-up girl.
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              <img alt="daisy lowe indie fringe" class="alignnone" src="http://i0.wp.com/cs1.fashionising.com/media/hair-trends/daisy-lowe-indie-fringe.jpg?w=620" title="Daisy Lowe indie fringe"/>
              <br/>
              <small>
               Daisy Lowe for Esquire.
              </small>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               <em>
                The Kills
               </em>
               
               <strong>
                Alison Mosshart
               </strong>
               epitomises the indie rock queen look with careless hair in eyes. Florence Welch rocks it in red.
              </li>
              <li>
               Sweeping, messy bangs seem a part of French DNA:
               <strong>
                Chlo Sevigny
               </strong>
               has been sporting some of late while
               <strong>
                Lea Seydoux
               </strong>
               (circa
               <em>
                Mission Impossible 3
               </em>
               ) couldnt have the look more perfectly down pat.
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              <img alt="Lea Seydoux, Allison Mosshart, Chloe Sevigny fringes" class="alignnone" src="http://i0.wp.com/cs1.fashionising.com/media/hair-trends/famous-indie-fringes.jpg?w=620" title="Lea Seydoux, Allison Mosshart, Chloe Sevigny fringes"/>
              <br/>
              <small>
               Lea Seydoux in Mission Impossible 3; Alison Mosshart polaroid via Vogue UK; Chloe Sevigny Tomboy S/S 12 campaign.
              </small>
             </p>
             <h3>
              The cut and how to wear it
             </h3>
             <p>
              Theres not a lot of rules or fussiness associate with a
              <strong>
               grungy fringe hairstyle
              </strong>
               and thats precisely the point. But if you do want to go and ask your hairdresser for the cut, here are some things to bare in mind:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               This style works best if your hair is straight, or a bit wavy. Slight waves can add to the tousle of the look, but anything more will require regular straightening, so only make the cut if youre prepared to spend time styling it.
              </li>
              <li>
               The thicker the bangs the better. If your hair is thin consider that the fringe will have to be quite deep (i.e. start from far back on the crown) in order to achieve thickness.
              </li>
              <li>
               Dont go for wispy layers  as with bangs generally in 2012, a blunt cut is best.
              </li>
              <li>
               Length-wise you want below the eyebrows or just sweeping the eyes.
              </li>
              <li>
               The rest of the hair can be bobbed or long  the key is just to keep it relatively straight but tousled.
              </li>
              <li>
               Use a texturising product to get that messy effect even on cleanly washed hair.
              </li>
              <li>
               For a styling variation messily curtain the hair from the centre sweeping out.
              </li>
             </ul>
