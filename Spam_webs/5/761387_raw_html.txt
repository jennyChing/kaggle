             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               <a href="http://mashable.com/follow/topics/Adele">
                Adele
               </a>
               isn't just a shoe-in for the Grammys. The British singer just took home her first Oscar at the 85th Academy Awards on Sunday night for Best Original Song.
              </p>
              <p>
               Following weeks of
               <a href="http://mashable.com/social-media/">
                social-media
               </a>
               buzz that predicted she would come out on top in the category, Adele was awarded the golden statue for her song, "Skyfall," from the James Bond film of the same name. This is the first time a song from a Bond movie has won the coveted award.
              </p>
              <p>
               "Skyfall" beat out other songs for the win, including "Before My Time" from
               <em>
                Chasing Ice
               </em>
               , "Pi's Lullaby" from
               <em>
                Life Of Pi
               </em>
               , "Suddenly" from
               <em>
                Les Misrables
               </em>
               and "Everybody Needs A Best Friend" from
               <em>
                Ted
               </em>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/DeumyOzKqgI?enablejsapi=1&amp;" width="560">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               The news doesn't come as too much of a surprise  Adele recently brought home a Golden Globe award for Best Original Song for "Skyfall," which many believed would foreshadow an Oscar win. The singer has also been trending on Twitter, along with the keyword "James Bond," since the nominations were announced in January.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <div class="see also">
                 SEE ALSO:
                 <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/02/23/oscar-winners-2013/">
                  Social Media Predicts Oscar Winners
                 </a>
                </div>
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               Although there were no musical performances at the Oscars in 2012, Adele  dressed in a Burberry gown  took the stage this year to sing "Skyfall" live for the first time. It was her first U.S. televised performance since she swept the Grammys in 2012.
              </p>
              <p>
               "It's an honor to be nominated and terrifyingly wonderful to be singing in front of people who have captured my imagination over and over again," Adele said in a statement, following her nomination. "It's something I've never experienced and probably only ever will once."
              </p>
