        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         how to make green grape caterpillars
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-03T06:43:44+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 3, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://foodlets.com/author/charityc/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            charityc
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://foodlets.com/2014/10/03/how-to-make-green-grape-caterpillars/#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         THESE CREEPY CRAWLY CATERPILLARS arent just a
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/tag/halloween/" title="healthy halloween treats for kids">
          healthy Halloween treat
         </a>
         , theyre also great for
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/category/birthday-party-ideas/" title="party ideas foe kids">
          parties
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/2014/08/15/how-to-pack-awesome-and-easy-school-lunches/" title="awesome and easy lunches">
          lunch boxes
         </a>
         any time of the year. I think I know three little people who are going to squeal when they see these on the table at snack time today
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/green-grape-caterpillars-foodlets.com_.jpg">
          <img alt="green grape caterpillars, foodlets.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12387" height="454" src="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/green-grape-caterpillars-foodlets.com_.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         WHAT YOU NEED:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Extra long toothpicks (I got mine in the kitchen accessories section at Walmart)
         </li>
         <li>
          Green grapes
         </li>
         <li>
          Mini chocolate chips
         </li>
         <li>
          Cream cheese (or frosting)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         HOW TO DO IT:
        </p>
        <p>
         Thread the grapes onto each toothpick until theres no toothpick sticking out.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_0419.jpg">
          <img alt="thread grapes onto toothpickthread grapes onto toothpick" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11749" height="454" src="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_0419.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Apply a dab of cream cheese as glue.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_0425.jpg">
          <img alt="cream cheese glue" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11750" height="454" src="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_0425.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Stick on eyes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/grape-caterpillars-foodlets.jpg">
          <img alt="grape caterpillars foodlets" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11747" height="454" src="http://foodlets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/grape-caterpillars-foodlets.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
         Happy
         <a href="http://foodlets.com/tag/halloween/" title="nutritious halloween food for kids">
          Halloween
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
