    <h1>
     2 Ways to Activate a New iPhone 5
    </h1>
    <div class="wzy_yh">
     <img height="45" src="../../images/icon/perry_wood.jpg" width="53"/>
     <p>
      Mar. 09, 2014 10:05 am / Posted by
      <a href="https://plus.google.com/u/0/100232437419698269472/posts?rel=author" target="_blank">
       Andrew Coffe
      </a>
      to
      <a href="../../itunes-alternatives/ios-manager/">
       iOS Data Manager
      </a>
      <br/>
      <span class="blue2">
       <a href="https://twitter.com/AndrewCoffe1017" target="_blank">
        Follow @Andrew Coffe
       </a>
      </span>
     </p>
    </div>
    <p>
     <img align="left" alt="active a new iphone 5" src="../../images/tips/iphone/activate-iphone-5.jpg" style="padding-right:10px;" width="300"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     Finally we got our dear iPhone 5 at hand! Then going through a wonderful experience with the new iPhone 5 is on the way. But before we could start to use the new device into its potential,
     <strong>
      iPhone 5 activation
     </strong>
     blocks ahead. This article is specially designed for Apple users who get the new device at hand and have a hot desire to know "how to activate my iPhone 5".
    </p>
    <div class="resource_border">
    </div>
    <h2>
     How to Activate New iPhone 5?
    </h2>
    <p>
     Activating new iPhone 5 is an easy but still meticulous job. You can activate new iPhone 5 in 2 methods. We would like to specify the typically efficient ways on how to activate your iPhone 5 into 2 parts.
    </p>
    <div class="resource_border">
    </div>
    <h3>
     Part 1: Activate iPhone 5 on Verizon Wireless
    </h3>
    <p>
     To activate iPhone 5 wirelessly, you should verify that you have all the information about your network at hand. Besides, you should also make sure that you have the latest version of iTunes. Then you can begin.
    </p>
    <ul class="resource_ul">
     <li>
      Hold down the sleep/power button in the top right corner to turn on your iPhone 5.
     </li>
     <li>
      Set your iPhone 5 language and location.
     </li>
     <li>
      Connect to a Wi-Fi network, and turn on Location Services, which are the GPS features and help you get lots of useful functionality.
     </li>
     <li>
      Select how to set up iPhone 5. You can choose to set up your iPhone 5 as a new iPhone, restore from iCloud backup, or
      <a href="../../product/itunes-data-recovery.htm">
       restore from iTunes backup
      </a>
      .
     </li>
     <li>
      Create a new Apple ID. If you have already had one, enter the existing Apple ID. If you don't have, follow the on screen prompts to create one.
     </li>
     <li>
      Set up iCloud and choose where to
      <a href="../../topics/mobile-devices/backup-iphone-5.html">
       backup your iPhone 5
      </a>
      .
     </li>
     <li>
      Activate Find My iPhone. It can help you locate your iPhone 5 if it is lost or stolen by using the iPhone 5 built-in GPS.
     </li>
     <li>
      Enable Siri. It is the voice-activated assistant that you can talk to in order to perform actions.
     </li>
     <li>
      Share information about your iPhone 5. Only the basic information about the iPhone 5 working situation is shared and no your personal information.
     </li>
     <li>
      Connect iPhone 5 to PC and add content to your iPhone 5 with
      <a href="../../product/itunes-cleanup.html">
       iTunes
      </a>
      . If you have already have data in iTunes from other Apple devices, sync with iTunes, and the data will be in your new iPhone 5. If you want to transfer data from your old devices (iPhone 4S/4/3GS, iPad, iPod touch, etc.) to the new iPhone 5, use
      <a href="../../product/iphone-data-recovery.html">
       iPhone Data Recovery
      </a>
      to freely transfer any wanted files from old devices to your PC and add them to iTunes to sync with iPhone 5.
     </li>
    </ul>
    <div class="resource_border">
    </div>
    <h3>
     Part 2: Activate an iPhone 5 on AT&amp;T
    </h3>
    <p>
     AT&amp;T is the American Telephone and Telegraph Company and you can fix the issue of "how do I activate my new iPhone 5" with its help.
    </p>
    <ul class="resource_ul">
     <li>
      Navigate to the AT&amp;T wireless activation site:
      <a href="https://www.wireless.att.com/activations/" rel="nofollow">
       https://www.wireless.att.com/activations/
      </a>
     </li>
     <li>
      Follow the instructions on the pop-up screen.
     </li>
     <li>
      Restart iPhone 5. After setting up AT&amp;T iPhone 5, turn off the device, wait for 5 minutes and turn on iPhone 5. Then the activation is finished.
     </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
     Many of you may wonder "what should I do after I activate my iPhone 5". Well, do what you like as you did with the other phones. You can also
     <a href="apple-ios-6.1.4-update-on-iphone-5.html">
      update your iPhone 5 to the newly iOS 6.1.4
     </a>
     for experience as many other users speak highly of it.
    </p>
