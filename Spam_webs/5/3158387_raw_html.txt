         <a href="/fitness-center-boston-ma/how-to-lose-weight-braintree-ma" title="How To Lose Weight? Braintree, MA">
          How To Lose Weight? Braintree, MA
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-details">
         Friday, April 24, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <p itemprop="description">
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">
           <img alt="" src="/images/blogs/PT2.JPG" style="border: 0px solid; float: right; margin-left: 10px;"/>
           Losing weight
          </a>
          isn't easy. Understanding what you need to do in order to lose just one pound is important. This way you will know what you need to do to lose 5, 10, or 15 pounds. Losing weight requires commitment, but you can have fun doing it. But, what you really need is understanding and the right plan.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           How losing weight works:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          For every pound that you want to lose, you'll need to eliminate 3,500 calories. This sounds awful and impossible, but you can do this through eating less, working out, or a combination both. Here is the good news. Even at rest you are using calories. While you are doing nothing you are burning calories. So, you need to increase the amount of calories you burn while exercising, you need to eat fewer calories, but what you really need to do is increase the number of calories you burn while your body is at rest.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cutting a few hundred calories through diet and the right type of exercise will often be enough to help people lose weight. As you begin exercising, you will burn more calories through the act of the exercise itself.
          <strong>
           A leaner body with more muscle will burn more calories while doing nothing at al
          </strong>
          l. This means that strength training and burning muscle is very important to burning calories. Be sure that
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">
           strength training
          </a>
          is part of your routine.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you are not losing weight you may be miscalculating the number of calories you are burning, miscalculating the number of calories you are consuming, or you may have hit an exercise plateau. Track everything that you eat and drink in a day, calculate the calories you have burned while working out, and change up your exercise routine often.
         </p>
         <p>
          To lose weight it is often recommended that you cut your calorie intake down to 1,500 calories a day. Eating less than this can throw off your metabolism, and eating too little is not healthy. Increase your activity level and youll increase your calorie deficit.
         </p>
         <p>
          For more information on weight loss, contact
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/contact.htm">
           Fit Factory in Braintree, MA
          </a>
          .
         </p>
