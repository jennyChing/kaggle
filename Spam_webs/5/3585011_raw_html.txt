         With more than 69 million mouths to feed every day in 119 countries, McDonald's has a massive range of taste buds to please. That means the fast food retailer has had to get pretty creative with its offerings, especially in foreign markets, which accounted for 68% of the company's $27.6 billion in revenue last year.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         From Samurai burgers to seaweed-flavored fries and deep-fried bites of brie cheese, McDonald's has tried just about everything to cater to local tastes overseas.

         1. McDonald's Ebi Burger
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" border="0" src="http://r.m.yahoo.com/api/res/1.2/i6RO8IUqL0ZOakyGXDMfsg--/Y2w9cHJldmlldztmaT1maXQ7aD03MjtxPUhpZ2g7dz05Ng--/http://l.yimg.com/os/publish-images/finance/2013-11-18/c738a97a-e03b-457d-8929-837d878cd85d_1mcdonalds-ebi-burger.jpg"/>
         Where: Japan, Singapore and other Asian markets
         <br/>
         <br/>
         What it is: This sandwich features whole shrimp embedded in a crispy patty and topped with lettuce and spicy sauce. It's served on a Big Mac roll. In Japan, it's called the Ebi Filet-O.

         <br/>
         <br/>

         5. McDonald's Black and White Hamburger Combo
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" border="0" src="http://r.m.yahoo.com/api/res/1.2/BEJNkmgHK9ZzCRLKtAf3FA--/Y2w9cHJldmlldztmaT1maXQ7aD03MjtxPUhpZ2g7dz05Ng--/http://l.yimg.com/os/publish-images/finance/2013-11-18/6fdb2903-be0e-490f-99c6-9cc0681358b9_5mcdonalds-black-and-white-hamburger-combo.jpg"/>
         Where: China
         <br/>
         <br/>
         What it is: The black burger, which is topped with white sesame seeds, has a black pepper sauce with chopped onions, according to
         <a class="inline " href="http://kotaku.com/5942177/in-china-mcdonalds-makes-both-a-white-burger-and-a-black-burger">
          <span>
           Brian Ashcraft at Kotaku
          </span>
         </a>
         . The white burger is topped with black sesame seeds and has a spicy sauce.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The burgers were available for a limited time last year and now
         <a class="inline " href="http://kotaku.com/mcdonalds-chinese-black-and-white-burgers-are-okay-1163159796">
          <span>
           they are back
          </span>
         </a>
         again.

         6. McDonald's Taro Pie
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" border="0" src="http://r.m.yahoo.com/api/res/1.2/BIy0LQhbTcs66u3Hxygp2w--/Y2w9cHJldmlldztmaT1maXQ7aD03MjtxPUhpZ2g7dz05Ng--/http://l.yimg.com/os/publish-images/finance/2013-11-18/e2fcf003-b0a3-4792-854c-08dfedbdded8_6mcdonalds-taro-pie.jpg"/>
         Where: Japan and other Asian markets
         <br/>
         <br/>
         What it is: Chunks of taro root covered in a sweet, bright purple sauce inside a flaky pie crust. Taro root resembles potatoes in consistency but has a stronger flavor. Taylor Tamblin compared the taste of the treat to a mild sweet potato in his blog, Man Reviews Food.
