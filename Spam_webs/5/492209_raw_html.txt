        <div id="mainInfo">
         <h2>
          List Of Places To Visit In Clark
         </h2>
         <h3>
          Clark Freeport Zone (CFZ) is the former Clark Air Base in Angeles City, which is the one of the bases of the United States before the Mt. Pinatubo eruption. When the Americans left Clark, the local government here decided to make it as a Freeport zone and open it to the public for different purposes. Today, Clark features some of the best hotels and resorts in the Philippines, besides offering different tourist destinations for countless of visitors here daily. If you want to visit this place, here is the list of the best places to visit inside Clark.
         </h3>
        </div>
        <!--end #mainInfo-->
       </div>

       <ul class="mainList clearfix">
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            1
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 1" class="listImg" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-LYMDILVfwCk/T52CRXBSKoI/AAAAAAAAC_c/Xkp8LDupAWg/s1600/Clark+Museum+(54).JPG"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             Clark Museum
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Many people who are visiting the former Clark Air Base in Angeles City are not aware of the Clark Museum. One reason is that they usually focus on visiting the Shopping centers, golf courses and other activities inside the Clark Freeport Zone. Well, this museum is very interesting because people who visit here can discover useful information and unique artifacts about the history of the place before. When you enter the museum, there are many exhibited photos, relics, actual historical items, paintings and more. This place is very important for many students who want to discover the history of Clark Air Base.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            2
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 2" class="listImg" src="http://clarkisit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/nayon-filipino-121.jpg"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             Nayong Filipino
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The original location of Nayong Filipino is besides the Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA) in Pasay City. Unfortunately, they need to expand the airport and so they decided to relocate this historical tourist destination in Clark, Angeles City.  Today, many people who are visiting Clark will have the chance to revive their memories of the rich Filipino cultures through visiting the Nayong Filipino. The location of this tourist destination is behind the Centennial Park and in most cases; different schools are using this place as one of their field trip destinations.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            3
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 3" class="listImg" src="http://aviationmuseum.eu/World/Asia/Philippines/Clark_AB/Clark/F-8H_48661.JPG"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             Air Force City Park
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Clark is actually is one of the locations of the Philippine Air Force personnel. The Air Force City Park is located near the Diosdado Macapagal International Airport (DMIA) and offer different activities to tourists who are visiting here. It has a picnic ground for those who want to say here while relaxing under the old Acacia trees with their families. The park likewise has mini playground and unused aircrafts and choppers displayed around the place.  At the left side of this park, there is an available public golf club for those who want to play golf and relax.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            4
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 4" class="listImg" src="http://fontana.com.ph/images/Images_Ani/HP4.jpg"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             Fontana Water Park
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The Fontana Water Park is perhaps one of the biggest water parks in the Philippines today. It has several nicely designed swimming pools and many people are enjoying their time while staying here. Aside from the different pools, Fontana likewise has decent villas for family units who desire to stay longer and a nice casino for those who love to spend time gambling.  Of course, for visitors who want to stay the whole day in the swimming pool with their love ones, the Fontana Water Park is the best place to visit in Clark.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            5
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 5" class="listImg" src="http://elkabayo.com/images/bigsee7.jpg"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             El Kabayo
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            For people who want to experience real horseback riding, El Kabayo is a perfect here but it turned out to be quite expensive to do so. Of course, these are the targeted customers of this place because it has expert riders who guide their costumers to learn the exact horseback and riding techniques. Definitely, this will be an enjoyable place to visit because people get the chance to see big and small horses around the place. Like any other tourist destinations with entrance fee, El Kabayo likewise has its own implemented rules to follow.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            6
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 6" class="listImg" src="http://wfiles.brothersoft.com/p/paradise-ranch-view_99201-1280x1024.jpg"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             Paradise Ranch
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Another great tourist destination inside Clark is the Paradise Ranch. This place offers a unique environmental setup and aims to educate people on how to preserve the wildlife. For those who got the chance to visit Tagaytay, this place is very similar because it is very relaxing and located on the hill of Bamban, Taclac. When you visit this place, you get the chance to see different animals, lovely gardens and picturesque views surrounding the entire area. Paradise Ranch is open for public without membership fees and it is an all-in-one venue for camping, picnic, venue for special occasions, team building, field trips and more. This place likewise offers the Dinosaur Island for people who want to discover these huge creatures before.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
        <li>
         <div class="table">
          <div class="col1">
           <span class="listRank">
            7
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="listInfo infoClass1">
           <img alt="List Of Places To Visit In Clark list 7" class="listImg" src="http://swisschaletph.com/images/sm-clark.jpg"/>
           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             SM City Clark
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Before leaving Clark going to any point of destination, many visitors here are stopping by the SM City Clark. This is the place to relax, go shop, eat, watch movies and allow the kids to play in the different amusement parks. Although this is not as huge as the other SM malls in the Philippines, this place offers remarkable choices and a very comfortable traffic situation unlike in Manila. The location of this mall is at the right side before reaching the main gate of the Clark Freeport zone, which is in Angeles City, Pampanga.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </li>
