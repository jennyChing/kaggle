             <h2 class="blog-title">
              <a class="blog-title-link" href="/2/post/2013/06/garage-storage-and-space-solution-in-one-package.html">
               Garage Storage and Space Solution in One Package
              </a>
             </h2>
             <p class="blog-date">
              <span class="date-text">
               06/10/2013
              </span>
             </p>
             <!-- 377320412186362452 -->
             <p class="blog-comments">
              <a class="blog-link" href="/2/post/2013/06/garage-storage-and-space-solution-in-one-package.html#comments">
               0 Comments
              </a>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="blog-separator">
            </div>
            <div class="blog-content">
             <span class="imgPusher" style="float:left;height:84px">
             </span>
             <span style="z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:20px;*margin-top:40px">
              <a>
               <img alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" src="/uploads/1/3/0/4/13045931/9474390.jpg?655" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;"/>
              </a>
              <span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;">
              </span>
             </span>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;">
              Be practical! Find items that will not only serve as a solution to one problem. Say, you can always have a thing which can both cater
              <strong style="">
               <a href="http://www.garagetek.co.uk/our-solutions/garage-storage" target="_blank">
                garage storage
               </a>
              </strong>
              and space problems at the same time. That way, you will have less the expense and more of the comfort and convenience at the same time.
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              Pulling out this trick is an easy feat. All you have to do is to have a good grasp of how much space you need in order for you to find a solution that would definitely provide such. From there, you can choose among the following storage items to see where you can have the extra space:
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <strong style="">
               1.
              </strong>
              <strong style="">
               Baskets and bins
              </strong>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              Baskets have different shapes and sizes, and would be able to help you in all your space and budget needs. Bins work the same way, too. The good thing about these two storage units is you can find them almost everywhere whether you are buying from a department store, or a local
              <a href="http://store.garagetek.co.uk/" target="_blank">
               garage sale
              </a>
              .
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              They are really good for almost any garage item that you want to store. And if for instance you do not want to use them anymore in your garage, you can use them in your room, in the garden, or anywhere you can possibly think of.
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <strong style="">
               2.
              </strong>
              <strong style="">
               Multi-level Racks
              </strong>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              For space solution, having multi-level racks are very helpful. They may be located on just one spot but they can accommodate your items by the levels that they got. They are especially good for items that you use frequently such as paint brushes, slippers, paints, and many more.
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              Just make sure that the items that you are going to put there are well-capped if there is any. Also consider the presence of kids which might be harmed by the items you stored there. Avoid putting heavy items in racks as they are not as stable compared to cabinets.
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <strong style="">
               3.
              </strong>
              <strong style="">
               Small yet movable cabinets
              </strong>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              Accidents often happen in tall cabinet, this is the reason why small cabinets are now recommended for garage use particularly because it is expected that heavy items are stored there.
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              To maximize space you need to rearrange your garage solutions from time to time. This would be difficult for tall cabinets. Having small ones which are easy to move would be great. You may want to go for those which have removable or lockable wheels for maximum mobility and satisfaction.
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="">
              </span>
             </div>
