                <p>
                 This is by far one of the most shocking videos I have personally ever seen
                </p>
                <p>
                 Julia Merfeld was captured here on tape as she hired a hit man to kill her husband. The only thing was the entire ordeal was caught on tape. In 9:00 minutes of cold hearted video footage you can hear the caviler conversation as she ordered her husbands murder.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Merfeld says that she was not getting along with her husband and says that its easier to kill him then to get a divorce. This way she doesnt have to face being judge by her family.
                </p>
                <p>
                 When the undercover detective posing as a hit man asks her if she wants it done in the house she says no because it would be messy and her friend is planning to move in with her and she doesnt want her friend to be scared. She didnt want her friend to think that her place was unsafe.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The detective makes sure shes serious by asking her to get a few things for him to allow him to effectively execute the plan. Merfeld wants it to look like a robbery, so the undercover asked her to make a list of the items she wants him to take, bring a recent picture of her husband and to bring a floor plan of the house.
                </p>
                <p>
                 In the next clip in the video Julia is back in different clothes and carrying a picture of what appears to be a map and a list. She also has the $100 down payment that would basically seal the deal. The undercover detective double, triple and quadruple checks if Merfield is certain about following through letting her know that once she leaves the car that this is going to happen and he would not be available to be contacted to call things off. The detective gave her so many opportunities to back out but she still wanted to go through it.
                </p>
                <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
                </div>
                <p>
                 Her husband had an insurance plan worth $400,000 of which she was going to pay the hit man $50,000. She planned to pay him in $9000 installments.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Merfeld
                 <a href="http://www.woodtv.com/dpp/news/local/muskegon_county/julia-merfeld-pleads-guilty-to-solicitation-of-murder" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.woodtv.com/dpp/news/local/muskegon_county/julia-merfeld-pleads-guilty-to-solicitation-of-murder');" target="_hplink">
                  pled guilty to solicitation of murder in June
                 </a>
                 and is set to be sentenced on July 30, with the minimum set at six years. Here is the shocking video.
                </p>
