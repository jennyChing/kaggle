    <h2>
     THE NOVEMBER BEARD CLUB
    </h2>
    <p>
     This month Uncle Tweed remembers the
     <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/19981202163131/http://planet.ten.net/~nbc/">
      The November Beard Club
     </a>
     , a group of men who
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      Firmly believed that because men have the natural ability to grow beards, they should. 
  At least for a month. And the chosen month is November, due to its chilly climate and the 
  fact men seem to have a natural inclination to grow facial hair around this time every year.
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     Although last heard of around 1998, the November Beard Club has inspired many to grow facial hair,  sometimes in the styles pictured below, sometimes not.
    </p>
    <p>
     Maybe one day their dream of making November the
     <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/19991011211440/planet.ten.net/~nbc/whatis.htm">
      International Beard Month
     </a>
     will come true.
    </p>
