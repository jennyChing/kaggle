          </h1>
          <p class="no-break">

          </div>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/WIKI-Sunrise.jpg">
            <img alt="WIKI-Sunrise" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-33923" height="151" src="http://www.wakingtimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/WIKI-Sunrise-300x151.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
           Dylan Charles,
           <em>
            Editor
           </em>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/01/15/free-yourself-from-the-conspiracy-against-enlightenment/">
            <strong>
             Waking Times
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           To those whove made the effort to cultivate their spiritual nature and whove learned to see beyond the five sense reality that has most of us isolated in the material world, it is clear that enlightenment and higher states of consciousness are part of our original human nature. Our birthright. The fact that so many of us typically remain in
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2013/07/31/9-key-components-of-the-shift-towards-higher-consciousness/" title="9 Key Components of the Shift Towards Higher Consciousness">
            contemporary consciousness
           </a>
           is a sign that weve somehow been led astray, or spiritually dumbed down.
          </p>
          <p>
           Enlightenment is a concept that is at once appealing to almost all people, while at the same time seemingly unattainable to most people. We are naturally drawn to the idea that we can live a life of peace and universal love, finding happiness and joy in even the most mundane or startling moments in life. Yet, as seekers, we so often look at enlightenment as an elusive prize that is rewarded to only the most dedicated and ascetic spiritual masters. Always just beyond our reach.
          </p>
          <p>
           The reality is that the human being is born fully equipped to enjoy all of the spiritual riches experienced by the greatest of historys sages, but, we live in a climate that reduces our ability to attain spiritual wisdom by polluting our bodies and our minds with toxic substances and ideas that take us further and further from truth, and deeper and deeper into lower states of awareness.
          </p>
          <p>
           For those whove explored the abundant information available on the dominant power structures present on earth at this time, there is evidence of a clear link between many of the factors that limit our spiritual awareness and the aspirations of the global elite. Those in power remain in power because the vast majority of people are unaware of their own personal power, and therefore limit their potential in accordance with the traps and routines set by the elite.
          </p>
          <div align="center" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 1em;">
           <script type="text/javascript">

          <p>
           An awakened person is a free person, someone who has been liberated from the fear of living life to the fullest. An awakened population is simply impossible to contain and control, and as individuals we must strive to awaken ourselves to this powerful realization. This is the elites greatest fear, having a world full of minions who no longer behave as such.
          </p>
          <p>
           Spiritual teacher
           <a href="http://manithyasudevi.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
            Ma Nithya Sudevi
           </a>
           has produced a keen roadmap for overcoming the roadblocks to enlightenment that have been thrown in our way by the cultural and political forces bent on maintaining a network of control over humanity. She has produced an eye-opening series of
           <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1YbXpidAcz0&amp;list=PLB33CE7D38CDD7387&amp;index=1" target="_blank">
            videos
           </a>
           aimed at helping you to see through this conspiracy and liberate yourself from its wicked clutches. She remarks:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            Almost everyone alive on Earth today is caught in a trap set by the global elite to prevent anyone from realizing a state of God Consciousness, or Enlightenment. Truth has been degraded to fact, and jealously guarded by those who dont understand it themselves, and who fear the implications of its discovery by others. We are being stripped of our birthright, but we can overcome the many obstacles set against us. This video series is made with the intention to equip all who view it to free themselves from the conspiracy against their innate, natural intelligence, intuition, and freedom to realize enlightenment. Truly, if you live in the world today, and dont want to be a victim of the conscienceless global elite, its a must watch.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Here are her 10 steps to free yourself from the conspiracy against enlightenment:
          </p>

           1. Do not consume any fluoride, ever.
          </h3>

           2. Do not consume any aspartame, ever.
          </h3>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="300" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/c6DhayTukVE?feature=player_detailpage" width="480">
           </iframe>
          </center>
          <h3>
           3. Avoid eating genetically modified, non-organic and highly processed, foods.
          </h3>
          <p>
          </p>

           4. Avoid watching television.

          With these simple guidelines, it is my hope that you will find inspiration and assistance in your journey to enlightenment.

           Namaste!
