           <div class="post-detail" itemprop="text">
            Seems odd with patch 2.1 in the works and the release of UEE coming up that they would announce that they have no intention of having 2.1 go through for current generation consoles.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            I mean, doesn't this patch introduce a plethora of new content? I could care less about patches after 2.1 but it seems silly for them to openly state that they have no intention of at least bringing 2.1 to the Ps3 and Xbox 360.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Those that bought the game for these consoles, should they not be allowed to experience the new things coming about in 2.1?
            <br/>
            <br/>
            I would be a happy camper with patch 2.1 and nothing else really; if anything .
            <br/>
            <br/>
            I feel that maybe since they have just announced that they will keep the next generation consoles up to date with the PC, that they can, as a gesture of good will for those that purchased the games on current gen; announce they'll bring 2.1 to the current gen. Instead of saying we give up on you. Just my 2 cents
