      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6485.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 1" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6485.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       2
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Place a saucepan on a medium heat. Peel and slice the garlic. Pick the basil and parsley leaves and put to one side. Finely chop all of the stalks.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6486.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 2" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6486.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       3
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Add a splash of olive oil to the hot pan then add the garlic and cook until lightly golden. Add the basil and parsley stalks to the pan, fry for a few seconds, then tip in the tinned tomatoes and bring to the boil. Turn down the heat and simmer gently for 10 minutes.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6487.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 3" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6487.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       4
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Meanwhile... Slice each chicken breast into 3-5 strips and lay on a chopping board. Cover with a sheet of clingfilm and bash with the bottom of a pan until they are the thickness of a pound coin (4-5 mm).
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6488.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 4" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6488.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       5
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Peel the flattened chicken off the chopping board, place on a tray and dust with the flour and a good pinch of salt and pepper. Toss until evenly coated. Wash your hands!
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6489.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 5" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6489.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       6
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Crack the egg into a small bowl and beat with a fork. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it, reserving a cupful of the cooking water, then return to the hot pan. Add a splash of of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper and cover with a lid.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6490.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 6" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6490.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       7
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Trim the crusts off the ciabatta and discard. Tear the inside into chunks and whiz to breadcrumbs in a food processor. Add a splash of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper and whiz again. Pour the oiled crumbs onto another tray, and place by the bowl with the egg in it.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6491.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 7" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6491.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       8
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       After 10 minutes, taste the tomato sauce and season with a good pinch of salt and pepper. Cover with a lid and turn off the heat.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6492.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 8" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6492.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       9
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Place a wide frying pan on a medium heat and add a splash of olive oil.  Take a piece of chicken, give it a shake to get rid of any excess flour then dip in the beaten egg. Let any excess drip off then lay it in the tray of breadcrumbs. Shake and turn the chicken over in the crumbs until evenly coated. Repeat with all the pieces of chicken.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6493.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 9" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6493.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       10
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Lay the breaded chicken pieces in the hot pan and fry until golden on the side  this will take around 4 to 5 minutes. Using a fish slice or tongs, carefully turn each piece over. Fry for another 4 to 5 minutes until cooked through and golden brown on the other side.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6494.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 10" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6494.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       11
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Lay a couple of sheets of kitchen paper on a plate and transfer the crispy cooked chicken onto it to drain.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6495.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 11" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6495.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       12
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Make your table look respectable  get the cutlery, salt and pepper and drinks laid out nicely. Roughly chop the basil and parsley leaves and stir through the cooked pasta, adding a splash of the reserved cooking water to loosen the pasta if necessary.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6568.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 12" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6568.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="step">
      <h2>
       13
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Divide the pasta between the two plates, top with a good dollop of tomato sauce and some fried chicken then serve.
      </p>
      <div>
       <a data-lightbox="step_s_box" href="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6497.large.jpg">
        <img alt="Favourite fried chicken: Step 13" class="stepimg" src="http://media.getitcooked.com/resources/object/recipe/4242/6497.medium.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
