                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     Top Five Blog Hop Pet Peeves
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content">
                     Not all blog hops are created equal. Here are my pet peeves:
                     <br/>
                     <ol>
                      <li>
                       Slow-loading blogs. My goodness, please make the photo files on your blog smaller so it doesn't take forever to load. Sometimes it takes so long to load, that I don't hang around to follow you.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Hide and seek hops. If you are hosting a hop, it should at least be on your home page, preferably towards the top. Making me look all over the place for the hop is not nice.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Security nightmares. Submitting a comment should not be like going through the airport security scanners. I don't think I need to do math or submit two codes on two different pages just to say, "I'm following your blog."
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Expired hops. I am beginning to think that some people don't check the links on their pages often. If you're advertising a hop that someone else is hosting, you might want to make sure the buttons work.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Disappearing bloggers. You know who you are (we know who you are too). You put your link on the page and then leave without even following the host. It's just not cool.
                      </li>
                     </ol>
