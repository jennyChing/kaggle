        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://img853.imageshack.us/img853/6296/2013040215main.jpg" title=""/>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Talented duo 15&amp; made their comeback with Somebody on the season finale of  K-Pop Star 2 and the public has definitely found them somebody to love on music charts!
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Immediately upon release, Somebody grabbed the #1 spot on Melon, Bugs, Soribada, Naver Music, Olleh Music, and more, achieving a real-time  all-kill on major music charts.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Somebody is written by Shim Eun Ji who also wrote 15&amp;s debut single I Dream as well as Jo Kwons The Day I Confessed, and IUs I Really Hate Him.  It is co-composed with Ryan S. Jhun and Antwann Frost. The song features Park Ji Min and Baek Ye Rins unique groove and soulful voices.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        JYP Entertainment stated, Since their debut, 15&amp;s Park Ji Min and Baek Ye Rin have been receiving a lot of love for their impressive singing ability. The group dominated major music charts immediately upon their comeback with Somebody. We ask that you continue to show them your love and support.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        15&amp; will begin their music show comeback stages on music shows starting on M!Countdown on the 11th. No word yet on when we can expect the highly anticipated music video parodying the K-pop Star judges.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Congrats 15&amp;!
