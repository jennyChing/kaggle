     <h1>
      Basil Bunting on Creative Writing
     </h1>
     <p class="blog_legend">
      16 April 2014 by
      <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.infideas.com/author/infinite-ideas/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Infinite Ideas">
       Infinite Ideas
      </a>
      in
      <a href="http://www.infideas.com/category/basil-bunting-and-a-strong-song-tows-us/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Basil Bunting and A strong song tows us">
       Basil Bunting and A strong song tows us
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://www.infideas.com/category/entertainment/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Entertainment">
       Entertainment
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      by Richard Burton, author of
      <a href="http://www.infideas.com/books/a-strong-song-tows-us/">
       <em>
        A strong song tows us
       </em>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Hanif Kureishi isnt the first author to cause outrage with off the cuff remarks about creative writing (see
      <a href="http://www.theguardian.com/books/2014/mar/04/creative-writing-courses-waste-of-time-hanif-kureishi" target="_blank">
       this
       <em>
        Guardian
       </em>
       article
      </a>
      ). Over 40 years ago Basil Bunting (probably deliberately) blew up the English department at the University of Victoria over the same issue. At that time the department was dominated by the flamboyant and charismatic poet Robin Skelton who was pioneering the new field of Creative Writing. Skelton had vigorously opposed Buntings appointment as a visiting lecturer, citing Buntings lack of experience of Creative Writing, which rather points up the difference between Creative Writing and creative writing, a difference that Bunting was to expose cruelly.
     </p>
     <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_10674" style="width: 160px">
      <img alt="the only thing worse than a Creative Writing student is a Creative Writing professor --Basil Bunting" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-10674 " height="150" src="http://www.infideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Reading-glasses-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
      <p class="wp-caption-text">
       the only thing worse than a Creative Writing student is a Creative Writing professor. Basil Bunting
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      As soon as he arrived Bunting gave interviews to the
      <i>
       Victoria Times
      </i>
      and
      <i>
       The Daily Colonist
      </i>
      and at some point in the course of each of them told the reporters that the only thing worse than a Creative Writing student is a Creative Writing professor. Perhaps unsurprisingly Skelton accepted this as the personal insult that was probably intended and Bunting was ostracized by the entire English faculty with one or two honourable exceptions. He returned prematurely to the UK, on the way pocketing a full years professorial salary for about one third of a years teaching. Hanif Kureishis cause might not be lost. Criticizing creative writing from within can be quite profitable.
     </p>
     <p>
      For more information on the life and work of Basil Bunting, visit our dedicated site at
      <a href="http://www.basilbunting.com/">
       www.basilbunting.com
      </a>
      .
     </p>
