        <p>
         Dark, beautiful, mysterious and dangerous, that is what caves are like. Created gradually over thousands of years by water erosion, atmospheric influences or in a violent moment of volcanic eruption or earthquakes, caves are one on natures many marvelous wonders.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are many types of caves, some can be visited by anyone, others pose a challenge even for the most experience cave explorers or spelunkers, but all are breathtaking in their own way. There are many caves yet to be discovered and some are still being explored due to the fact that they can stretch for hundreds of miles and have parts where people cant reach.
        </p>
