       <h2>
        How to React to a Bad Air Conditioning System
       </h2>
       <div class="margin_10_0">
        <p>
         Since the ac system has many parts, in case you have an air-conditioning system 1 of the elements that can fail is the AC condenser, and like all things mechanical, they can be prone to failure quite often.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Factors behind AC Condenser Malfunction
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          The unit could be put in a location where there isnt suitable air flow, and this may cause failure of the condenser as strain can be caused by lack of airflow on the components since it needs to work hard. Technicians can check to present you the right place for the air-conditioning system unit.
         </li>
         <li>
          The system has not yet been scrutinized or maintained properly through time, and the components have reached the end of their lifetime. By properly maintaining an ac system (like through
          <a href="http://www.eliairconditioning.com/">
           http://www.eliairconditioning.com/
          </a>
          ), these possible failures can be pinpointed before they happen, and new components can be set up.
         </li>
         <li>
          There might be liquid slugging down the compressor brought on by refrigerant thats charged wrong.
         </li>
         <li>
          The components might not be lubricated correctly or the refrigerant tube may be sloped or improperly routed.
         </li>
         <li>
          There may be a lousy start-run capacitor which makes the compressor strive to start its motor for quite a while. It is likely to really have a performance or the whole system might not function in any respect, after the machine goes bad.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         It is vital that appropriate air-conditioning maintenance be done on a normal basis, to ensure small issues are taken care of before they develop into something large. A routine review will make sure that your air-conditioning home unit is operating well. The technicians will scrutinize the residential AC condenser and other areas of the whole system.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          AC Repair
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         There are quite a few things to remember, before you contract for any residential HVAC
         <a href="http://www.eliairconditioning.com/index.php/repair/">
          repair services
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Be sure to check with skilled ac services firm that has an established track record  experienced, dependable, and affordable.
         </li>
         <li>
          Check references  do not simply hire the first service that you happen across.
         </li>
         <li>
          Use the Internet to check for services and request your friends and co-workers give you recommendations.
         </li>
         <li>
          In case you imagine your ac system may need repairs, dont strive to work with the system yourself as you may make the issue worse.
         </li>
         <li>
          The issues can be minor or perhaps you need repairs which are far more extensive. Your residential AC condenser is merely one of the components that can fail in your ac structure, so care is constantly recommended.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Clean up of the condenser unit:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This consists of the removal of particles and dust from the inside and exterior of the machine. If theyre bent the fins may also be cleaned and straightened. Lubrication of the fan motor is carried out to enable it to run easily without making any disagreeable sounds.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Shifting the air filter:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This measure is simple and may be carried out by the master of the house himself. It is best to alter the filters at least once per month, in case the AC is in heavy utilization. These are normally found in the condenser and occasionally even in the evaporator. Replace the filter by removing the casing or grilles. The part number, as well as the filter size, could be obtained from your old filter.
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition, leaks can become a possible entrance path for contaminants like pollen, dust, etc which on accretion, becomes breeding grounds for mould. Mold exposure carries potential side effects like allergies, asthma and a number of other respiratory difficulties. Clearing and cleansing the ductwork and sealing any leakages are thus vital to preserve the welfare, the spaces environment, and the performance of the air-conditioning unit.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Examining the Coolant
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is an air conditioner maintenance basic step that requires checking the line joining the evaporator unit located inside your residence and the condenser unit put outside. The lines must be replaced, in case any damages or damage are discovered. In addition, the coolant needs to be examined for a scarcity of the substance since they will lessen the cooling capacity of your air-conditioning unit. They should be noted to the tech for repair, if any defects are seen.
        </p>
