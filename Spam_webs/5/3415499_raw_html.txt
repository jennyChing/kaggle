      <h3>
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <a href="http://www.wibki.com/blog/?p=449" target="_blank">
        <img alt="10 Jaw Dropping Cool Websites" class="aligncenter" height="359" id="irc_mi" src="https://mlpforums.com/uploads/monthly_04_2013/post-9999-0-12140800-1367335760.gif" title="10 Jaw Dropping Cool Websites" width="639"/>
       </a>
       <strong>
        Its amazing how creative people can be.
       </strong>
       We live in a material world though, and people are expecting to get paid for their creativeness This is why most of the cool websites are promoting a brand, an artist, or even an agenda. Luckily, as users we dont care why this amazing content was created, were just happy that its there for us to enjoy. On this blog post
       <strong>
        we have gathered 10 of the coolest, jaw dropping, and most likely to make you go Wow! websites
       </strong>
       that the past year has provided us with.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-449">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       While you scroll through this list, make sure to
       <a href="http://www.wibki.com" target="_blank" title="Create a special tab for the cool websites you find while browsing on your Wibki account!">
        bookmark your favorites to your Wibki
       </a>
       so it will not get lost
      </p>
      <h2 style="font-size: 130%; font-weight: bold;">
       1.
       <a href="http://www.perriersecretplace.com/" target="_blank" title="on Perrier Secret Place you take part in a crazy party, trying to find the alluring 'Golden Woman'.">
        Perrier Secret Place
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.perriersecretplace.com/" target="_blank">
        <img alt="On Perrier Secret Place you take part in a crazy party, trying to find the alluring 'Golden Woman'." class="alignright size-full wp-image-477" height="288" src="http://www.wibki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/on-Perrier-Secret-Place-you-take-part-in-a-crazy-party-trying-to-find-the-alluring-Golden-Woman.1.jpg" title="On Perrier Secret Place you take part in a crazy party, trying to find the alluring 'Golden Woman'." width="342"/>
       </a>
       Perrier Secret Place is a perfect example for a brand that is using the webs innovation for some smart branding. When you look at their product; its sparkling water probably as far from exciting as a product can be. So they try to brand it as something that is more then a simple soda,
       <strong>
        a lifestyle.
       </strong>
       They started a year ago by creating 
       <a href="http://societeperrier.com/" target="_blank" title="Socit Perrier is a guide to fashion, music, art, travel, nightlife, cocktail culture, mixology, and global hot spots.">
        societe perrier
       </a>
        which is a culture and lifestyle online guide, and then launched this amazing interactive website. on Perrier Secret Place you
       <strong>
        take part in a crazy party, trying to find the alluring Golden Woman.
       </strong>
       By clicking on different people during thehip, dream-like party, you take on their first-person perspective. You only have 90 seconds to find her, and over 60 people to go through Can you do it?
      </p>
      <h2 style="font-size: 130%; font-weight: bold;">
       2.
       <a href="http://ngm.nationalgeographic.com/serengeti-lion" target="_blank" title="The Serengeti Lion is a project by National Geographic designed to get you up close and personal with some of the most majestic and mysterious creatures in the world.">
        The Serengeti Lion
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://ngm.nationalgeographic.com/serengeti-lion" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Enter the world of the Vumbi pride. The lions strut and grimace, bare their teeth. One drapes a paw indolently, another nuzzles." class="alignleft size-full wp-image-474" height="280" src="http://www.wibki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/the-serengeti-lion.jpg" title="Enter the world of the Vumbi pride. The lions strut and grimace, bare their teeth. One drapes a paw indolently, another nuzzles." width="350"/>
       </a>
       National Geographic is known for their amazing wildlife photos and videos for years. When YouTube, Instagram and other powerful photo and video sharing services have conquered the web, many thought that the old fashioned gient will slowly fade away, but the outcome was the exact opposite. This past year National Geographic launched several mini sites, each celebrating a different part of earths wildlife.
       <a href="http://ngm.nationalgeographic.com/serengeti-lion" target="_blank" title="The Serengeti Lion is a project by National Geographic designed to get you up close and personal with some of the most majestic and mysterious creatures in the world.">
        The Serengeti Lion
       </a>
       is
       <strong>
        probably their most impressive project.
       </strong>
       This amazing site is designed to get you up close and personal with some of the most majestic and mysterious creatures in the world. Using a specially designed robot that allowed them to get close, unobtrusive shots of the beasts, the photographers really make you feel like you actually spent some time among the lions, and not on your laptop. Whether youre a cat person or not,
       <strong>
        you will be amazed by the range of personality and character the photographers capture
       </strong>
       , and will fall in love with the king of the savanna.
      </p>
      <h2 style="font-size: 130%; font-weight: bold;">
       3.
       <a href="http://middle-earth.thehobbit.com/" target="_blank" title="The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug - A Journey Through Middle-earth">
        The Hobbit: A Journey Through Middle-earth
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://middle-earth.thehobbit.com/" target="_blank">
        <img alt="A Journey Through Middle-earth was a promotional site for The Hobbit - The Desolation of Smaug movie." class="alignright size-full wp-image-675" height="314" src="http://www.wibki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/A-Journey-Through-Middle-earth-was-a-promotional-site-for-The-Hobbit-The-Desolation-of-Smaug-movie..jpg" title="A Journey Through Middle-earth was a promotional site for The Hobbit - The Desolation of Smaug movie." width="350"/>
       </a>
       <a href="http://middle-earth.thehobbit.com/" target="_blank" title="A Journey Through Middle-earth was a promotional site for The Hobbit - The Desolation of Smaug movie.">
        A Journey Through Middle-earth
       </a>
       was a promotional site for The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug movie. This amazing site allows Tolkien fans to travel first-person into the places you spent your childhood (and adulthood) reading about, and
       <strong>
        see all the awesome details that Peter Jackson made come to life on his latest film.
       </strong>
       You can explore the fantastic landscapes of Middle Earth and enter some of the movies spectacular locations for some interactive glimpses at important scenes from it.
       <strong>
        When we talked about cool websites at the title, this is what we meant!
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <a href="https://life-saver.org.uk/" target="_blank" title="Learning emergency life-saving skills made easy - learn anywhere, anytime, and for free with Lifesaver">
        Life-Saver
       </a>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <a href="https://life-saver.org.uk/" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Life-Saver throws you into the middle of an emergency situation - Would you know what to do" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-677" height="313" src="http://www.wibki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Life-Saver-throws-you-into-the-middle-of-an-emergency-situation-Would-you-know-what-to-do.jpg" title="Life-Saver throws you into the middle of an emergency situation - Would you know what to do" width="350"/>
       </a>
       Every one should know First Aid basics. It is an essential asset that can literally become a lifesaver when you least expect it.
       <a href="https://life-saver.org.uk/" target="_blank" title="Life-Saver throws you into the middle of an emergency situation - Would you know what to do">
        Life-Saver
       </a>
       does not certify you as a paramedic, but
       <strong>
        it can definitely make a difference if an emergency crosses your path.
       </strong>
       This interactive site, created by The Resuscitation Council (UK), throws you into the middle of an emergency situation. It gives you the chance to test your knowledge, and see how you will function in these life-or-death circumstances. As a father to three beautiful kids, I know how to perform a heimlich maneuver, because
       <strong>
        I refuse to take the chance that I might need to do it one day and will not know how.
       </strong>
       Visit Life-Saver and learn what to do if a situation like this ever happens to someone you care about, or even to someone that is randomly passing by you.
      </p>
      <h3>
       <a href="http://mb.mercedes-benz.com/en/avd/" target="_blank" title="The Avant/Garde Diaries is an online interview magazine. The digital interview magazine asks people from the creative scene to document their personal view on the avant-garde.">
        The Avant Garde Diaries
       </a>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <a href="http://mb.mercedes-benz.com/en/avd/" target="_blank">
        <img alt="The Avant-Garde Diaries presents a wide-ranging exploration of the extraordinary  the people, places, and ideas that inspire, fascinate, and encourage us to push the boundaries of the possible." class="alignright size-full wp-image-680" height="312" src="http://www.wibki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/he-Avant-Garde-Diaries-presents-a-wide-ranging-exploration-of-the-extraordinary--the-people-places-and-ideas-that-inspire-fascinate-and-encourage-us-to-push-the-boundaries-of-the-possible..jpg" title="The Avant-Garde Diaries presents a wide-ranging exploration of the extraordinary  the people, places, and ideas that inspire, fascinate, and encourage us to push the boundaries of the possible." width="350"/>
       </a>
       <a href="http://mb.mercedes-benz.com/en/avd/" target="_blank" title="The Avant-Garde Diaries presents a wide-ranging exploration of the extraordinary  the people, places, and ideas that inspire, fascinate, and encourage us to push the boundaries of the possible.">
        The Avant Garde Diaries
       </a>
       is an amazing website, sponsored by Mercedes-Benz, that
