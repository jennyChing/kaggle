       <h1 class="entry-title">
        25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-03-05T23:21:43+00:00">
         March 5, 2012
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trupanion.go2cloud.org/aff_c?offer_id=2&amp;aff_id=1852&amp;file_id=67">
          <img alt="Trupanion affiliates 728x90a 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" height="90" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Trupanion_affiliates_728x90a.jpg" title="25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" width="728"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Dogs come in all shapes, sizes, colors, and temperaments, and this diversity has been achieved through selective breeding. Unfortunately, this practice occasionally yields undesirable results, including a higher incidence of certain hereditary defects, deformities, or infirmities within a given breed.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Siberian Husky
          </span>
         </strong>
         <em>
          -
         </em>
         <strong>
          <em>
          </em>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <em>
            Autoimmune Disorders
           </em>
          </span>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Siberian-Husky-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Siberian Husky 1 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-137" height="531" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Siberian-Husky-1.jpg" title="Siberian-Husky-1" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Siberian Huskies seem to be predisposed to a variety of autoimmune disorders, many of which affect the skin. These conditions cause sores and hair loss, often on the face. One immune condition affects both the skin and the eyes and can lead to eye problems like glaucoma and cataracts. Treatment for these disorders is corticosteroids to inhibit the immune system.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Bulldog-
           <em>
            Respiratory Problems
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 2 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-68" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-2.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-2" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Like all dogs with those adorable smashed-in faces, bulldogs can suffer from breathing problems. Your bulldogs small nostrils, elongated soft palate, and narrow trachea are the reasons why he probably snores, and they can lead to a life-threatening emergency if he gets overheated or overtired. Thats why its important to keep bulldogs cool in the summer and never overdo it with exercise.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Pug-
           <em>
            Eye Problems
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 1 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-141" height="531" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-1.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-1" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          With their squashed faces and bulgy eyes, pugs are at risk for eye problems. The most serious is an eye popping out of its socket. This can happen if a pug gets into an accident or a fight with another dog. If this happens, cover the eye with a damp cloth and rush your dog to the vet. The vet can put the eye back in place, although whether the dog will retain vision in the eye depends on the severity of the damage.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           German Shepherd:
           <em>
            Hip Dysplasia
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 4 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-70" height="541" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-4.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-4" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Many large breeds are prone to hip dysplasia. In hip dysplasia, the joints ball and socket dont fit together properly, which causes pain, arthritis, and problems walking. When looking for a German shepherd puppy, ask the breeder whether the parents have been screened for hip dysplasia. Parents with healthy hips are more likely to produce puppies with healthy hips.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Labrador Retriever:
           <em>
            Obesity
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 5 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-71" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-5.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-5" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Any dog can become overweight, but labs are especially prone to it. And just like with people, obesity is linked to health problems in dogs. Labs need vigorous daily exercise. If your lab is constantly begging for more food, try giving her raw carrots, green beans, or apples to snack on. Since prevention is easier than weight loss, its best to consult with your vet on a diet plan thats right for your pet.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Beagle-
           <em>
            Epilepsy
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-6.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 6 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-91" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-6.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-6" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Epilepsy, a brain disorder that causes seizures, seems to be more common in beagles than in other dog breeds. Epileptic dogs will usually have their first seizure between 6 months and 3 years old. Though epilepsy cant be cured, frequent seizures (more than one a month) can usually be managed with antiseizure medication.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Shih Tzu-
           <em>
            Wobbly Kneecaps
           </em>
           <br/>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-7.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 7 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-92" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-7.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-7" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Wobbly kneecaps, or patellar luxation, are extremely common in toy breeds like shih tzus. In patellar luxation, the kneecap occasionally pops out of place, causing the dog to hobble, skip a step, or limp. The kneecap will usually pop back into position on its own, but in severe cases, surgery may be necessary to correct the problem and prevent arthritis.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Boxer-
           <em>
            Cancer
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-8.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 8 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-93" height="541" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-8.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-8" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Boxers are at higher risk for certain types of cancer, including lymphoma and mast cell tumors. Lymphoma is cancer of the lymph nodes, and mast cell tumors are a type of skin cancer. In both cases, the cancer is often felt as an unusual lump or bump on your dogs body. Both of these cancers might be treatable, but its important to catch them early. So if you have a boxer, be sure to check him regularly for lumps.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Dachshund- Back Problems
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-9.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 9 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-94" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-9.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-9" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Because of their long bodies, dachshunds are at higher risk for back injuries and spinal disk problems. The best way to keep your dachshund feeling her best is to keep her at a healthy weight. Excess weight puts strain on the back. Also try to limit stair-climbing and jumping down from furniture, as it can also put stress on the back.
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Doberman Pinscher-
           <em>
            Heart Condition
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-10.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 10 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-95" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-10.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-10" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM) is a serious heart condition in which the hearts chambers are stretched out and dont pump blood effectively. Often, owners of dogs with DCM dont even realize something is wrong until their dog collapses. Because DCM is so common in Dobermans, many vets suggest annual screenings. Medications can regulate heart rhythm and improve the hearts ability to pump, but there is no cure for DCM.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Cocker Spaniel-
           <em>
            Ear Infections
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-11.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 11 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-96" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-11.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-11" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Dogs like cocker spaniels with floppy, furry ears are prone to frequent ear infections. The best way to prevent ear infections is to clean your dogs ears every couple of weeks and occasionally flip her ears back to let them breathe. Also trim any hair growing on the underside of the ears with clippers to help keep the ear canals dry. Minimizing the frequency of ear infections may also prevent major problems down the road.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Yorkshire Terrier-
           <em>
            Portosystemic Shunt
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-12.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 12 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-97" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-12.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-12" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Portosystemic shunt (PSS) is a blood vessel birth defect thats common in small breeds like the Yorkie. The portal vein carries toxins from the intestines to the liver, which cleans the blood. With PSS, the vein bypasses the liver, and toxins arent removed. PSS may cause poor growth, vomiting, confusion, and seizures. Most of the time, PSS can be corrected with surgery, and the dog will go on to live a normal, healthy life.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Golden Retriever-
           <em>
            Skin Allergies
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-13.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 13 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-98" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-13.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-13" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <!-- adman_adcode (middle, 1) -->
        <center>
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- Beautiful-Animals Top -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-5822537813558737" data-ad-slot="4437952302" style="display:inline-block;width:728px;height:90px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </center>
        <!-- /adman_adcode (middle) -->
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Does it seem like your golden is constantly licking? Unlike people, dogs dont sneeze when theyre allergic to something. Instead, they tend to get itchy skin. And frequent licking, scratching, and chewing can lead to hot spots (red, oozing sores). To soothe itchy skin, give your dog baths with oatmeal shampoo, add an omega-3 supplement to his diet, and make sure he has regular flea treatment.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Poodle-
           <em>
            Glaucoma
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-14.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 14 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-99" height="543" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-14.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-14" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Poodles are one of a handful of breeds that are at increased risk for this serious eye disease. Glaucoma is a buildup of fluid in the eye, which causes pressure, pain, and eventually blindness. Early on, glaucoma can be treated with medications. But surgery and even removal of the affected eye may be necessary.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Rottweiler-
           <em>
            Joint Problems
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-15.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 15 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-100" height="543" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-15.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-15" width="801"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Large breeds like the Rottweiler are at risk for a variety of joint problems, including hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, arthritis, and osteochondrosis dissecans (OCD). OCD is a condition that develops in large, fast-growing puppies in which the cartilage in a joint doesnt form properly. Feeding the right amount of a balanced diet may help keep your Rottweilers joints healthy. However, many dogs require surgery to remove the abnormal cartilage.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Miniature Schnauzer-
           <em>
            Diabetes
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-16.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 16 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-101" height="541" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-16.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-16" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Has your miniature schnauzer been drinking water like hes just run a marathon? Is he suddenly having accidents in the house? He could have diabetes. Any dog can develop diabetes, but miniature schnauzers seem to be at higher risk. Diabetes is a serious condition, but with the insulin and diet changes your dog can live a normal, healthy life.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Chihuahua-
           <em>
            Collapsing Trachea
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-17.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 17 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-102" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-17.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-17" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Does your Chihuahua make a honking noise when she gets excited? She may have a collapsed trachea  a common problem in toy breeds. With collapsed trachea, the cartilage that normally holds the trachea open is weak, so the trachea flattens. Some dogs go their whole lives with collapsing trachea and have no problems from it; others require medication. In severe cases, surgery may be necessary to prop the trachea open.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Pomeranian-
           <em>
            Hair Loss
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-18.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 18 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-103" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-18.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-18" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Pomeranians are predisposed to an adrenal gland disease called alopecia X, which causes hair loss. Alopecia X usually begins when a dog is young. If a dog with alopecia X is intact, spaying or neutering often causes the hair to grow back (the hair loss is caused by excess production of sex hormones). Melatonin supplements can also help.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           German Shorthaired Pointer-
           <em>
            Aortic Stenosis
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-19.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 19 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-104" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-19.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-19" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Aortic stenosis is a narrowing of the aorta, the large blood vessel that carries oxygen-rich blood from the heart to the body. Aortic stenosis puts strain on the heart and over time might cause an irregular heart rhythm. When aortic stenosis is mild, there may be no symptoms. In more severe cases, the dog may be weak and tire easily. Unfortunately, aortic stenosis usually shortens a dogs lifespan, but it can be managed with medications.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Great Dane-
           <em>
            Bloat
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-20.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 20 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-105" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-20.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-20" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Giant breeds like Great Danes are at higher risk for gastric dilation and volvulus, or bloat. This is a life-threatening condition that develops when the stomach fills up with gas and then twists, trapping food and gas in the stomach. If you notice your dog pacing, panting, and drooling excessively right after eating, call the vet right away. Bloat can be corrected with surgery, but it can be fatal if not treated quickly.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Shetland Sheepdog-
           <em>
            Collie Eye
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-21.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 21 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-106" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-21.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-21" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Shelties can be affected by a group of related eye problems known as collie eye anomaly. Collie eye affects the retina and the optic nerve. Mild cases may not affect the dogs vision at all, but moderate to severe cases can lead to blindness. There is no treatment for collie eye, and its fairly widespread among the breeds that are affected by it. So before you bring a Sheltie puppy home, ask the breeder if hes been tested.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Maltese:
           <em>
            Little White Shaker Syndrome
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-22.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 22 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-107" height="544" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-22.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-22" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          This funny-named condition is just what it sounds like: tremors in small dogs that are white (although dogs with other coat colors can get it, too). Its caused by inflammation in the cerebellum, which causes shaking that can be so bad the dog can barely walk. But the good news is that its treatable with corticosteroids, its not painful for the dog, and it usually subsides after a few weeks.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Boston Terrier-
          <em>
           Cherry Eye
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-23.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 23 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-108" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-23.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-23" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Because of their protruding eyes, Boston Terriers are susceptible to a number of eye problems, including cherry eye. In cherry eye, a tear-producing gland pops out from behind the dogs third eyelid. Its called cherry eye because the gland is round and bright red. Cherry eye can be repaired with surgery. Besides cherry eye, Boston Terriers are also at risk for dry eye, cataracts, and entropion (turned-in eyelids).
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           French Bulldog-
           <em>
            Breathing Problems
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-24.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 24 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-109" height="542" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-24.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-24" width="800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Like his English cousin, the French bulldog is susceptible to breathing problems. Also known as brachycephalic airway syndrome, this set of breathing problems is a result of the breeds pushed-in nose, elongated soft palate, and narrow trachea. Extreme heat or too much exercise can cause a French bulldog to struggle to breathe, so its best to keep him indoors on hot days.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Cavalier King Charles Spaniel-
           <em>
            Mitral Valve Disease
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-25.jpg">
          <img alt="Most Popular Dog Breeds 25 25 Most Popular Dog Breeds and Their Health Issues" class="aligncenter wp-image-110" height="541" src="http://www.beautiful-animals.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-25.jpg" title="Most-Popular-Dog-Breeds-25" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          This heart problem is common in elderly small dogs, but cavaliers often develop it early on. In mitral valve disease, the valve between the left atrium and ventricle doesnt close tightly, allowing blood to leak backward when the heart pumps. This puts strain on the heart. Symptoms include lethargy and coughing. With monitoring and the right medication, a dog with mitral valve disease can live for years with very few symptoms.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
