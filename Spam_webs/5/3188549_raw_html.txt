        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          I first blogged about the
          <a href="http://www.thefirearmblog.com/blog/2011/12/01/the-700-wtf-for-hunting-dinosaurs/">
           .700 WTF (What The F***) round this time last year
          </a>
          . The inventors girlfriend has become the first woman to shoot the monster cartridge 
         </p>
         <div class="tfb_youtube_container" style="width:420px; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; margin-bottom:1.5em;margin-top:1.5em;">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/JwlyvfiJdKQ" width="420">
          </iframe>
          <div style="width:100%; text-align:center; display:block; font-style:italic; ">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div style="display:none">
          <img src="http://img.youtube.com/vi/JwlyvfiJdKQ/0.jpg"/>
         </div>
         <p>
          I know who I want with me if I ever end up in a Jurassic Park-like scenario.
         </p>
         <p>
          You might have noticed Jarods girlfried was shooting without a muzzlebrake. The reason the recoil was not uncontrollable is because she was shooting a moderate load (1200 fps with a 1132 bullet). The hot load, which requires a muzzlebrake, is the same bullet but fired at over 2000 fps.
         </p>
         <p>
          The .700 WTF is made by fire forming a .50 BMG case and then sizing and trimming it to 3 in length. It is loaded with a 1132 grain lead bullet.
         </p>
