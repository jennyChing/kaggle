          &lt;h3 class="entry-title"&gt;What I Know About Germans &#8211; The Book!&lt;/h3&gt;
          &lt;h4 class="vcard author"&gt;by &lt;span class="fn"&gt;James Glazebrook&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h4&gt;
          &lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;
              &lt;p id="top" /&gt;It&#8217;s finally here! We&#8217;re very proud and ber-excited to announce that our first book, &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a title="What I Know About Germans" href="http://www.uberlin.co.uk/what-i-know-about-germans/"  target="_blank"&gt;What I Know About Germans: 101 Observations&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;, is out to buy today.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;What I Know About Germans&lt;/strong&gt; is based on our insanely popular blog post of the same name, which generated over 40,000 Facebook Likes, 600 blog comments, coverage by the likes of&lt;em&gt;Bild&lt;/em&gt;and&lt;em&gt;Financial Times Deutschland&lt;/em&gt; &#8211; and enough traffic to crash our servers on more than one occasion! A lovingly compiled collection of an Australian writer&#8217;s hilarious observations about her adopted beloved countrymen, &lt;strong&gt;What I Know About Germans&lt;/strong&gt; has been brought to life as a beautiful book, available in both print and eBook versions.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Want to see what we&#8217;re so excited about? Take a peek inside&#8230;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;iframe src="//e.issuu.com/embed.html#9225509/5685403" height="494" width="650" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;What I Know About Germans: 101 Observations&lt;/strong&gt; has been a year in the making, and is the result of a lot of hard work from a small but dedicated team. We&#8217;re indebted to author &lt;a title="Liv Hambrett" href="http://www.livhambrett.com" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.livhambrett.com']);" target="_blank"&gt;Liv Hambrett&lt;/a&gt;in particular for allowing us to publish her post in the first place, for writing new and improved observations for the book, and for being the unofficial WIKAG cheerleader while we pull it all together. I&#8217;d also like to thank &lt;a href="http://www.caffeinatedtoothpaste.com" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.caffeinatedtoothpaste.com']);" target="_blank"&gt;Josh Bauman&lt;/a&gt;, whose awesome illustrations really bring Liv&#8217;s words to life, &lt;a href="http://spectiv.es" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://spectiv.es']);" target="_blank"&gt;Eric&lt;/a&gt;for producing our impressive &lt;a title="What I Know About Germans - The Book" href="http://www.uberlin.co.uk/what-i-know-about-germans/"  target="_blank"&gt;What I Know About Germans web page&lt;/a&gt;, Sharmaine, Evi and the epubli team for their help and support &#8211; and of course Zo for beautifying everything with her boundless design talent! Group hug &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Anyway, enough of my gabbing.&lt;strong&gt;To find out more about the book, and to pick up a copy, &lt;a title="What I Know About Germans - The Book" href="http://www.uberlin.co.uk/what-i-know-about-germans/"  target="_blank"&gt;visit the What I Know About Germans: 101 Observations page&lt;/a&gt; now!&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
