        <b>
         Summary
        </b>
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       The document presents a secret 15 Dec 2008 High Court media suppression
order obtained by UK lawyers Carter Ruck on behalf of Zac Goldsmith and Jemima
Kahn. The order is aimed at the UK media and this copy is addressed
to "Guido Fawkes" of the well-known political blog
       <a class="external text" href="http://www.order-order.com" rel="nofollow" title="http://www.order-order.com">
        Order-Order
       </a>
       .
      </p>
