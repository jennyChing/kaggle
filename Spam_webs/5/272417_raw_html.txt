           <h3>
            BIMTECH commencement: Learn from mistakes; Shun arrogance, says Prabhu Chawla
           </h3>
           <p class="date">
            MBAUniverse.com | 17 June  2014 10:10 IST
           </p>
           <p class="medium">

                "Build strong teams by inducting brighter team members, learn from mistakes and shun arrogance" Prabhu Chawla, Editorial Director, The New Indian Express Group
               </p>
              </div>

        <div class="genericModule">
         <div class="articleFull">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            The 27th batch of PGDM program of
            <a href="http://www.mbauniverse.com/admissions/institute_profile1.php?institute_id=297" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              Birla Institute of Management Technology
             </strong>
             <strong>
              (BIMTECH),
             </strong>
            </a>
            Greater Noida for the academic year 2014-16 commenced on June 14, 2014 with a new wave of freshness. In all 429 students have been admitted to the two year program with  specialization in International Business, Insurance, Retail, and Sustainable  Development stream that includes nearly 160 women candidates. This number is  roughly higher than previous years enrolment by about 20 per cent.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            WhileDr.  H Chaturvedi, Director of BIMTECH declared the ceremony open by addressing the  gathering, the Chief Guest Mr. Prabhu  Chawla, Editorial Director of The New Indian Express Group delivered a thought  provoking speech on the occasion.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            The celebration  day at BIMTECH began with prayer service. Speaking on the occasion Mr. Prabhu  Chawla, began by sharing his life history and how a burning desire to achieve  led him to taste success amidst adversities. From being a Pakistani refugee and  school dropout, he fought for survival but never blamed his destiny. His  philosophy is to first survive, then thrive. He feels blessed to be taught by  stalwarts like Dr.Manmohan Singh, Dr. Amartya Sen.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Mr Chawla  suggested the students to develop qualities to increase their market share in  this competitive world and encouraged them to be driven by certain standard and  ethics, not by profit only to be ridiculed as a money making entity. He quoted  how TATA and Birlas earn profit and share with community. He shared the Mantra  of being grateful to the team and a leader with convictions and advised  management students to build strong teams by inducting brighter team members,  learn from mistakes and shun arrogance. With the Mantra of 3Cs of credibility, compatibility and  connectivity
            <strong>
             ,
            </strong>
            students  should become aware citizens of future and stop taking a recluse in their self-  built silos by getting trapped in the world of internet and cell phone.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Dr. H Chaturvedi, Director of
            <a href="http://www.mbauniverse.com/admissions/institute_profile1.php?institute_id=297" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              BIMTECH
             </strong>
            </a>
            began with hope for better days promised by the new Government and how the new government is focusing on economic  development, social investment, transparency and the 3Ss of skill, scale and  speed. He praised the Indian media for exposing weak governance, critical  political analysis, corruption and other ills.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Dr.  Chaturvedi congratulated the students for choosing BIMTECH over 3,500 B-schools  in India. He emphasized that BIMTECH is an industry backed b-school, from the  house of Birla's. It is not owners driven but faculty and student driven  college which started its journey in 1980. It is backed by 6,500 successful  alumni and has been partnered with more than 70 foreign Universities/  B-schools. He further pointed the homogeneity of campus. There are students and  faculties from around 25 states and celebrate this diversity. All the  festivities are celebrated with togetherness and equality.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Dr  Chaturvedi motivated the students by advising them to focus on marathon not  sprint, commit for a career 25 years down the line and place futuristic goals.  Quoting the examples of Indira Nooyi, Satya Nadella, Vishal Sikka, he  emphasized consistency and 100% commitment is the key to success. He gave the  mantra of compete and win with  attitude and ethics with the promise to work together to bloom into achievers.  He advised the students to imbibe in them the idea of VUCA - volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity to  achieve success in their corporate career.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Dr Anupam Verma, Deputy Director,
            <a href="http://www.mbauniverse.com/admissions/institute_profile1.php?institute_id=297" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              BIMTECH
             </strong>
            </a>
            , in  his thanks giving address reiterated the strong academic legacy and a deep  commitment to excellence with values, being built by a 6,000 strong alumni of BIMTECH  in the world of business and entrepreneurship globally.
           </span>
          </p>
