       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #d62222;">
          FANDUEL NBA DAILY INSIGHT 3/21
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          PGs
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Reggie Jackson$7400-
         </strong>
         PG does not have very many middle end guys that can put up good numbers. Jackson is one of those guys. He has played well after struggling for a few games. He scored 34.2 and 55 FDP in his last two games. You dont want to spend up for Curry who plays a tough Utah team. Jackson is your best option at PG on Saturday.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          George Hill$6400-
         </strong>
         Hill is another guy that is a good middle range option on Saturday. Hill has 40 FDP potential and he has a low downside. His salary is still very reasonable too. Hill should be a good value play on Saturday.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          SGs
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          James Harden$11000-
         </strong>
         coming off a career high 50 points against the Nuggets Harden is primed for another big performance on Saturday. There is not much to say about this pick on a short slate against a really poor defensive team in Houston Harden is almost a must play.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Kentavious Caldwell Pope$4900-
         </strong>
         The second SG spot is a tough one on Saturday. After Harden there is a lot of uncertainty. Caldwell pope is coming a couple of good games in a row. He is playing huge minutes, and with Greg Monroe likely out again Pope will be counted on more on the offensive end. His salary is really low, and this is a great spot to get a guy who should crush his salary.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          SFs
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Trevor Ariza$5900-
         </strong>
         Ariza has been really good as of late. He has gone over 30 FDP in five of his last six games. There is no reason to think he will slow down on Saturday. The Suns are terrible on defense and love to play an up tempo game. Ariza should get plenty of looks on Saturday.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Corey Brewer$5500-
         </strong>
         Brewer has also played really well as of late. He has gone over 30 FDP in three of his last six games. He is getting more minutes lately, and this is the type of game he loves very fast paced. You will see a lot of people roll Ariza and Brewer at the SF spot on Saturday, and we might be one of them.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          PFs
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Nikola Mirotic$7000-
         </strong>
         This guy has played so well over the last month I seriously think he needs more consideration for the rookie of the year. He has now gone over 30 FDP in six straight games and has gone over 44 FDP in two straight games. His minutes were not affected by Taj Gibson being back Friday. With no Greg Monroe and the Pistons already being terrible on defense against bigs look for a huge game from Nikola.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Donatas Montiejunas$5600-
         </strong>
         With Terrance Jones and Dwight Howard out Montie should see all the minutes he can handle. He is a really talented big man, and the Suns are really small up front. He should have his way against the Suns on Saturday.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          Centers
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Brandan Wright$5600-
         </strong>
         He has been a monster the last two games starting for Len. He has gone over 35 FDP in both of those games. The Rockets are really banged up up front, and with all the injuries to the Suns Wright should continue to see big minutes.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Rudy Gobert$7900-
         </strong>
         I absolutely love this guy. He is such an impact player on the defensive end, and a great rebounder. If he can ever develop and offensive game watch out. His price has dropped a little bit after a subpar night, but look for him to bounce back on Saturday.
        </p>
