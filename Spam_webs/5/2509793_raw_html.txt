        Bollywood movies
       </a>
       . My heart pumps at the thought of happy endings and the Indian movie industry knows how to deliver these better than anybody else.
      </p>
      <p>
       In Delhi, we came across this magical box that allows you to watch a movie inside, as you take a sneak peek through a little hole.
      </p>
      <p>
       After paying 10 Rupees all I saw was unrelated magazine cut out photos of Bollywood actors passing by, as they were being iluminated by a dim bulb in the interior of the box.
      </p>
      <p>
       There was no sequence, no plot but I stillmarveledat the thought of this being how movies started back in the day. And so worn out images of Shahrukh Khan and Aishwarya Rai would pass by almost with as much style as the man behind the Movie Box.
