All you have to do is familiarise yourself with a somewhat jovial inforgraphic provided, and then insert a witty caption to impart your feelings towards said image. Go on. Do it. It will be mildly amusing for you. Plus you'd get a chance at winning some great goodies from...

           <b>
            Step One)
           </b>
           Look at the image below.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Step Two)
           </b>
           Write one -- and only one -- stupid caption in the Disqus comment system provided.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Step Three)
           </b>
           Just in case you win, you must log into Disqus with an account I can contact you by.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Step Four)

           Once you've posted your comment, you have to share this post via Twitter, Facebook, G+, or... whatever.
