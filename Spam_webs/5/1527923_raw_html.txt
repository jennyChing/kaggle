         <span class="cb-title-fi">
          <h1 class="entry-title cb-entry-title cb-single-title" itemprop="headline">
           Oscar gets a makeover for 85th Academy Awards poster
          </h1>
          <div class="cb-byline">
           <div class="cb-author cb-byline-element">
            <i class="fa fa-user">
            </i>
            <a href="http://www.thegate.ca/author/w-andrew-powell/">
             W. Andrew Powell
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="cb-date cb-byline-element">
            <i class="fa fa-clock-o">

            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="cb-category cb-byline-element">
            <i class="fa fa-folder">
            </i>

       <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         The
         <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="http://www.thegate.ca/tag/academy-awards/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with Academy Awards">
          Academy Awards
         </a>
         have given Oscar a rare makeover this year84 makeovers, in factat least when it comes to a new commemorative poster that celebrates the events 85th year.
        </p>
        <p>
         In celebration of The
         <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="http://www.thegate.ca/tag/oscars/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with Oscars">
          Oscars
         </a>
         on February 24, the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences unveiled today the newest addition to its show poster collection, the Academy announced in a press release. The commemorative poster was designed by English artist, graphic designer and illustrator Olly Moss in collaboration with Gallery1988.
        </p>
        <p>
         Click the image for the large version of the poster, or head over to
         <a href="http://oscar.com/best-pictures-tribute" target="_blank">
          www.oscar.com
         </a>
         to get a closeup on each Oscar, including the names of each Oscar-winning
         <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="http://www.thegate.ca/tag/film-2/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with film">
          film
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thegate.ca/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/85oscarsposter.jpg">
          <img alt="85th Oscars poster" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-13640" height="600" src="http://www.thegate.ca/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/85oscarsposter-404x600.jpg" width="404"/>
         </a>
        </p>
