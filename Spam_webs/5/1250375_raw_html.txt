    <div class="post-content clearfix">
     <p style="text-align: left;">
      Night stand lamps are an essential in every dorm room. Why you may ask? Well they keep you from straining your eyes during those all night study sessions, make your room more cozier and gives it a fresh feeling. With so many options, you must remember that you are only in a dorm for a year and should always consider purchasing items that can be translated into a new space easily!
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/149322543867628201/">
       <img alt="318e3af59d6effb0a5dfe7b947bdb370" class="aligncenter wp-image-32686" height="370" src="http://blog.dormify.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/318e3af59d6effb0a5dfe7b947bdb370.jpg" width="246"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: left;">
      Lamps are considered essential in any dorm room and the ones that are usually provided are hideous fluorescent ones that cause headaches and sometimes glare. So investing in a nice lamp is very much ideal as a student.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/149322543867877613/">
       <img alt="804d2dfa6b30b7e04e0d0f9929f8b567" class="aligncenter wp-image-32692" height="329" src="http://blog.dormify.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/804d2dfa6b30b7e04e0d0f9929f8b567.jpg" width="248"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: left;">
      A good place to head if on a budget but also interested in a diy project would be a flea market or garage sales. Revamping lamp stands are as simple as giving it a fresh coat of paint and then purchasing inexpensive tops or whatever your heart desires. With a wee bit of artsy talent, you can create a unique lamp that will help you study and feel comfortable in your space. I say this because you dont want to always be stuck at your desk, laying in bed and studying is my prime location, that is why a great night stand lamp is more certainly an investment. If youre creative enough, paper hanging lamps are another option that can be purchased for a reasonable price and will give your room a chic cool vibe.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Enjoy!
     </p>
