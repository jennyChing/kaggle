      <h1 class="title">
       Lets
      </h1>
      <p class="half_break">
      </p>
      <p>
       <span class="bold_caps">
        Lets take off
       </span>
       our clothes and fool around.
       <br/>
       We can roll all over
       <br/>
       like dogs off leash at Lighthouse Beach. Lets rummage
       <br/>
       through each others bodies
       <br/>
       like a Fourth of July blowout sale, pawing through the orgy
       <br/>
       of tweed and twill, silk and sequins swirling up in flurries.
       <br/>
       The Buddha says dont argue until its necessary.
       <br/>
       Lets shuck oysters,
       <br/>
       wash them down with dirty martinis,
       <br/>
       the table littered with pearly shell. We can fill
       <br/>
       the bathtub and pretend were looking out
       <br/>
       at sunset over Tomales Bay. Your breasts
       <br/>
       are lanterns flickering on the water.
       <br/>
       Your hips are still Californias golden hills.
       <br/>
       This morning I opened an email from Texas
       <br/>
       that said Im going to hell and you dont really love me,
       <br/>
       but if I repent, though my sins be scarlet,
       <br/>
       they shall be as white as snow.
      </p>
