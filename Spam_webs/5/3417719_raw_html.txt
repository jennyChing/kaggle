             <p>
              <a href="http://s1.totalprosports.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/This-Vince-Neil-National-Anthem-Went-as-Well-As-Youd-Expect.png">
               <img alt="Vince Neil National Anthem" class="aligncenter wp-image-179548" height="386" src="http://s1.totalprosports.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/This-Vince-Neil-National-Anthem-Went-as-Well-As-Youd-Expect-595x376.png" width="610"/>
              </a>

             <p>
              You have to know what youre signing yourself up for when you request a
              <strong>
               Vince Neil
              </strong>
              National Anthem rendition. However, seeing as how Vince Neil is a partial owner of the brand new
              <a href="http://www.totalprosports.com/2015/02/19/las-vegas-tribute-to-jerry-tarkanian-video/">
               Las Vegas
              </a>
              Outlaws franchise, he probably inserted himself into this position. In any event, the Motley Crue vocalisthas enough trouble singing his own songs these days, so its no surprise that he mangled the National Anthem.
             </p>
             <p>
              Its easy to make excuses like, Oh, he was messed up on booze anddrugs, but its equally likely in my opinion that a lifetime of booze and drugs has made his brain pretty mushy all the time now. How else can you explain some of those hurried, slurred lines? Or the two cheerleaders escorting him out to sing?
             </p>
             <p>
              A Vince Neil National Anthem isnt like a regular national anthem, but its probably the Anthem that an
              <a href="http://www.totalprosports.com/2014/07/14/arena-football-fan-destroyed-hit-video/">
               arena football
              </a>
              team in Las Vegas deserves.
             </p>

              Take a look, and dont hurt yourself cringing:
