       <h1 class="ptitle">
        Illustrations by Trish Biddle
       </h1>
       <hr/>
       <div class="meta">
        <div>
         May 19, 2011
        </div>
        <div class="icoAuthor">
         Cuded

       <p>
        <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-8155" height="372" src="http://www.cuded.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Hamptons-Classic-Combined-Scene-Limited-Edition-Gicle-600x372.jpg" title="Hamptons Classic Combined Scene Limited Edition Gicle" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Born in Minneapolis, Minnesota, American artist Trish Biddle studied at the Dallas Institute of Art, before beginning her career as an illustrator and textile designer. Her process of drawing, painting and designing eventually melded onto canvases, creating romantic images and her unmistakable Art Deco figurative paintings.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

       <p>
        Her expertise in capturing nature and light creates richly colored, breath-taking canvases. With a well-defined style, broad, romantic strokes and vibrant color, Trish paints figurative, floral, fashion icons and childrens art. She travels the world and enjoys translating her experiences into oil on canvas.
       </p>
