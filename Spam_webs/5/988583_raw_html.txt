        <h1 class="entry-title">
         THE THIRD TERRORIST: Isnt it time to put to bed the myth that Islamic terrorists were not involved in the Oklahoma City bombing?
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.barenakedislam.com/2011/04/19/oklahoma-city-bombing-isnt-it-time-to-put-to-bed-the-myth-that-islamic-terrorists-were-not-involved/book_sm-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-110709">
          <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-110709" height="300" src="http://barenakedislam.files.wordpress.com/2011/04/book_sm1.jpg?w=201" title="book_sm" width="201"/>
         </a>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          Why did Bill Clinton want the Middle East terrorist connection to Oklahoma City squashed? Was he so intent on blaming the bombing on the vast right wing conspiracy that his administation made sure that only the two white suspects involved would ever be blamed for what was the second (after the 1993 World Trade Center bombing also on Clintons watch) Islamic terrorist attack on American soil?
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <a href="http://www.jaynadavis.com/highlights.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.jaynadavis.com/highlights.html', 'Jayna Davis']);">
             Jayna Davis
            </a>
           </span>
           
           <span style="color: #1c4587;">
            Moments after 9:02 AM on April 19, 1995, investigative TV journalist Jayna Davis found herself staring at the smoldering shell of the Alfred P. Murrah Building with a photographer by her side recording the unspeakable bloodshed and carnage.
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          On June 7, 1995, KFOR-TV broadcast its first story in which witnessesidentified the disgruntled Gulf War veteran,
          <strong>
           Timothy McVeigh, drinking beer with a swarthy, soft-spoken foreigner in an Oklahoma City tavern.
          </strong>
          Those brief moments of television history launched Jaynas decade long quest to unveil the Middle Easts hand in the heartland massacre.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="new-top" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-267519" height="190" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/new-top.jpg" width="455"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          In April 2004, Nelson Current Publishers released the
          <em>
           New York Times
          </em>
          best-seller,
          <strong>
           <em>
            The Third Terrorist: The Middle East Connection to the Oklahoma City Bombing.
           </em>
          </strong>
          Jaynas book outlines a riveting body of facts that convincingly links the Oklahoma City bombers to an Iraqi/Al-Qaeda hit squad, operating under Iranian state sponsorship. Why do questions still swirl around the official account of April 19, 1995? A
          <em>
           CNN/USA Today
          </em>
          poll reveals that 68 percent of Americans believe other bombing conspirators are still out there, somewhere. I found them, writes Jayna Davis, hiding in plain sight..
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.americanthinker.com/2011/04/confession_of_the_oklahoma_cit.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.americanthinker.com/2011/04/confession_of_the_oklahoma_cit.html', 'American Thinker']);">
            American Thinker
           </a>
          </span>
          
          <span style="color: #1c4587;">
           Today, April 19th, the 16th anniversary of the bombing, the prospect of shadowy terrorists walking free still pervades the countrys psyche. This time, however, the FBI itself is fueling the flames of public distrust.
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="highlightstitle" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-267521" height="434" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/highlightstitle1.gif" width="584"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          Last week the vault of top-secret bombing evidence cracked open. The Department of Justice, responding to Freedom of Information Act requests, released a declassified report detailing the May 26, 2005 interrogation of Terry Nichols. According to the record, Nichols broke his decade-long silence, acknowledging his hands-on role in constructing the massive truck bomb that demolished the Oklahoma City Murrah Building. While this revelation was all but academic, what Nichols said next threatens to rewrite history.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <strong>
           During the interview, the convicted bomber unleashed a startling admission: John Doe 2 exists.
          </strong>
          The FBI report states, Nichols advised that John Doe 2s name had not been mentioned during the (FBI) investigation, and therefore, he feared for his life and his familys well-being should it become public.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          <a href="http://www.barenakedislam.com/2011/04/19/oklahoma-city-bombing-isnt-it-time-to-put-to-bed-the-myth-that-islamic-terrorists-were-not-involved/john_doe_2_question/" rel="attachment wp-att-110710">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-110710" height="385" src="http://barenakedislam.files.wordpress.com/2011/04/john_doe_2_question.jpg" title="John_Doe_2_question" width="499"/>
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <strong>
           One seemingly innocuous statement in this recently declassified FBI memo effectively shatters the government myth that two angry white males singlehandedly pulled off the crime of the 20thcentury.
          </strong>
          To begin, Nichols clearly implied that he knew the identity of John Doe 2 when asserting that his co-conspirators name had not been mentioned during the prodigious FBI investigation. Keep in mind, the bomber had direct access to sealed court files and classified discovery materials that federal prosecutors were legally required to disclose to his defense lawyers during the state and federal trials. As a result, Nichols was informed about every suspect that surfaced on law enforcements radar screen.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <strong>
           The man who helped execute the deadliest attack on U.S. soil prior to 9-11 perceives the formidable John Doe 2 as omnipotent, with the ability to kill his family, and even him, in a maximum security lockup. Why?
          </strong>
          And what did the FBI do in response to the Oklahoma City bombers unnerving confession that John Doe 2 exists? Nothing.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="120915120454-oklahoma-city-bombing-horizontal-gallery" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-267518" height="360" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/120915120454-oklahoma-city-bombing-horizontal-gallery.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <strong>
           A month after Nichols told the FBI that a third man participated in the bombing, he repeated this same daunting revelation to a U.S. congressman.
          </strong>
          An FBI agent was present during the June 2005 Colorado prison cell interview with California Representative Dana Rohrabacher, scribbling handwritten notes of what was discussed.
          <strong>
           But Bureau policy strictly forbids the recording of suspect interrogations; therefore, the most trustworthy account of Nichols earthshaking testimony rests with the congressman.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #1c4587;">
            Rohrabacher described the prisoner as apprehensive and hesitant to name the infamous third terrorist, but he offered not-so-subtle hints of foreign complicity in a crime that the government has classified as domestically inspired.
           </span>
          </strong>
          <span style="color: #1c4587;">
           When Rohrabacher bluntly asked Nichols to assess the plausibility of the multiple eyewitness sightings placing Timothy McVeigh in the presence of Iraqi soldiers in Oklahoma City, Nichols shockingly conceded that the central theory presented in my 2004 book, The Third Terrorist: The Middle East Connection to the Oklahoma City Bombing, could be correct.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="oklahoma-city-1995" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-267520" height="603" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/oklahoma-city-1995.jpg" width="778"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <strong>
           Dispelling the image of Timothy McVeigh as the bombing mastermind, Nichols resolutely confirmed that the decorated Gulf War veteran had numerous liaisons with men of Arab extraction, boldly proclaiming, McVeigh talked about Middle Easterners on a number of occasions, and quite frequently, but Nichols claimed that he could not remember the context of those discussions. Once again, Nichols refused to reveal the third terrorist, terrified of retribution.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          <span style="color: #1c4587;">
           This incriminating declaration from the countrys most notorious mass murderer should have been the FBIs top investigative priority. After all, the implications were enormous. This was the first time the FBI learned directly from the Oklahoma City bomber that John Doe 2 exists. While Nichols declined to name the mystery accomplice, he dropped an unmistakable clue as to his identity when implying to the congressman that the premise of my book, The Third Terrorist, could be accurate.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          <strong>
           Now, we connect the dots further. If McVeigh did, indeed, collaborate with Saddam Husseins former soldiers, then John Doe 2 has escaped justice for slaughtering 171 innocent Americans. But, not surprisingly, the FBIs final summary of the prison interview, which was declassified and published last week, redacted Nichols damning statements that McVeigh associated with Middle Easterners in the very city where the terrorist bombing took place.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="zawahiriintheheartland" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-267523" height="345" src="http://www.barenakedislam.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/zawahiriintheheartland1.png" width="575"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #1c4587;">
          For 16 years, the FBI has brazenly refused to speak to two dozen Oklahomans who encountered Timothy McVeigh colluding with Iraqi ex-enemy combatants in an act of terror that murdered more civilians within our borders than all the U.S. soldiers who perished on the sands of the Persian Gulf War.
          <strong>
