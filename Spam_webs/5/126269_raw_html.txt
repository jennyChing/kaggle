      <h2 class="post_title">
       My Christmas tree smells spicy and magnificent
      </h2>
      <div class="post_body">
       <div class="pad">
        <p>
         A few months ago I stopped working from home, got an
         <a href="http://www.facebook.com/events/445028178879778/">
          office in Fremont
         </a>
         , 
		and hired someone to help me with the business side of The Oatmeal. Her name is Amanda and she's kind of an assistant / business development person / bookkeeper / spreadsheet wizard. 
		I went to
         <a href="http://www.livescience.com/25238-colorado-hotel-dinosaur-theme.html">
          Denver
         </a>
         this week and while I was away 
		she put up a Christmas tree in the office and placed it directly under a canvas-mounted print of the
         <a href="http://shop.theoatmeal.com/products/sriracha-grizzly-flamethrower-signed-print">
          Sriracha grizzly.
         </a>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="The Oatmeal Christmas tree smells spicy and incredible" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com/theoatmeal-img/blog/christmas_tree/christmas_tree.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         It's covered in a
         <a href="http://shop.theoatmeal.com/products/sriracha-popcorn">
          Sriracha popcorn
         </a>
         garland and
         <a href="http://shop.theoatmeal.com/products/sriracha-air-freshener">
          Sriracha air fresheners
         </a>
         , 
		so the tree is quite pungent and now the whole office stinks of awesomeness.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="The Oatmeal Christmas tree smells spicy and incredible" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com/theoatmeal-img/blog/christmas_tree/christmas_tree_top.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Best. assistant. ever.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Stinkiest. tree. ever.
        </p>
       </div>
