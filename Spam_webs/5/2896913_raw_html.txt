          <h1 class="news-title instapaper_title entry-title" itemprop="name">
           Batman: Arkham Knight Video Game Release Date Pushed Back
          </h1>
          <h3 class="news-byline">
           by
           <a href="/profile/g_man/" rel="author">
            Tony 'G-Man' Guerrero
           </a>
           on
           <time datetime="2014-09-08" itemprop="datePublished" pubdate="pubdate">
            September 8, 2014
           </time>
          </h3>
          <h2 class="news-deck" itemprop="description">
           New collector's edition packages unveiled.
          </h2>
         </section>
         <section class="article-body typography-format " itemprop="articleBody">
          <p style="">
           There's some good news and some bad news for those waiting for the next Batman video game release, Arkham Knight. Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment and DC Entertainment have announced the release date will now be June 2, 2015. That's the bad news.
          </p>
          <p style="">
           The good news is two limited collector's edition have been unveiled as well.
          </p>
          <figure data-align="center" data-embed-type="image" data-img-src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4086598-bak_limited+edition.jpg" data-ratio="0.54158633985128" data-ref-id="1300-4086598" data-resize-url="" data-resized="" data-size="large" data-width="640" style="width: 640px">
           <a class="fluid-height" data-ref-id="1300-4086598" href="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4086598-bak_limited+edition.jpg" style="padding-bottom:54.2%">
            <img src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/ignore_jpg_scale_super/0/40/4086598-bak_limited+edition.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </figure>
          <p style="">
          </p>
          <h3>
           <b>
            <i>
             Batman: Arkham Knight
            </i>
           </b>
           Limited Edition will include
          </h3>
          <ul>
           <li>
             Custom Art Book  80 Page, Full Colour Art Book Showcasing the concept art of Batman Arkham Knight
           </li>
           <li>
             Limited Edition SteelBook  Unique SteelBook case and game disc
           </li>
           <li>
             Comic Book  Limited Edition DC Comics
            <i>
             Batman: Arkham Knight #0
            </i>
            Comic Book
           </li>
           <li>
             Exclusive Character Skin Pack  Three Unique Skins from DC Comics  The New 52
           </li>
           <li>
             Batman Memorial Statue  Imposing statue commemorating Gothams Protector, the Dark Knight
           </li>
           <li>
             SRP:
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="">
           o Console  $99.99 / 119.99  / 89.99
          </p>
          <div class="js-mapped-ad mapped-ad mapped-incontent_top mapped-incontent_top-dart" id="incontent_top-wrap">
           <div class="ad-wrap ad-wrap-incontent_top" id="incontent_top">
           </div>
          </div>
          <p style="">
           o PC  99.99 / 74.99
          </p>
          <figure data-align="center" data-embed-type="image" data-img-src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4086599-bak_batmobile+edition.jpg" data-ratio="0.54158633985128" data-ref-id="1300-4086599" data-resize-url="" data-resized="" data-size="large" data-width="640" style="width: 640px">
           <a class="fluid-height" data-ref-id="1300-4086599" href="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/original/0/40/4086599-bak_batmobile+edition.jpg" style="padding-bottom:54.2%">
            <img src="http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/ignore_jpg_scale_super/0/40/4086599-bak_batmobile+edition.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </figure>
          <p style="">
          </p>
          <h3>
           <b>
            <i>
             Batman: Arkham Knight
            </i>
           </b>
           Batmobile Edition will include
          </h3>
          <ul>
           <li>
             Custom Art Book  80 Page, Full Colour Art Book Showcasing the concept art of Batman Arkham Knight
           </li>
           <li>
             Limited Edition SteelBook  Unique SteelBook case and game disc
           </li>
           <li>
             Comic Book  Limited Edition DC Comics
            <i>
             Batman: Arkham Knight #0
            </i>
            Comic Book
           </li>
           <li>
             Exclusive Character Skin Pack  Three Unique Skins from DC Comics  The New 52
           </li>
           <li>
             Transforming Batmobile Statue  Fully transformable
            <i>
            </i>
            Batmobile statue realised in exquisite detail by TriForce
           </li>
           <li>
             SRP: $199.99 / 199.99 / 169.99 (Console only)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="">
           So we're going to have to wait. The game was original set for an October 14 release date. I hate waiting as much as the next person but I'd much rather wait than get a game released before it's fully ready. As enjoyable as Batman's last game was, there were just too many glitches that drove me...
           <em>
            batty
           </em>
           . Let's hope the extra time makes the game even better than we imagined.
          </p>
          <p style="">
           Here's the last trailer
           <strong>
            released in JUNE
           </strong>
           . How much will change by the time we get our hands on it?
          </p>
