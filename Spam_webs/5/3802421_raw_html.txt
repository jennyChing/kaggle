            <a href="http://www.sochi2014.com/en" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.sochi2014.com']);" target="_blank" title="2014 Winter Olympics">
             2014 Winter Olympics
            </a>
            and those of us at the L&amp;L Automotive Group would like to express our sincerest congratulations! Our Olympians have made us proud by winning four medals in this years competition, more than in any Olympics since those held in Germany in 1936 where Great Britain brought three medals home. The four medal milestone was last met in the 1924 Olympics when only 16 countries were represented at the games, this year there were 88 countries taking part.
           </p>
           <p>
            Congratulations go to
            <a href="http://www.jennyjonessnowboarder.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.jennyjonessnowboarder.com']);" target="_blank" title="Jenny Jones">
             Jenny Jones
            </a>
            , who at 33 years old was older than her next oldest competitor by seven years! That didnt stop the snowboarder from taking the bronze in slopestyle nor did it stop her from a youthful celebration. What a thrilling performance!
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Jenny jones" class="wp-image-623 alignright" height="175" src="http://news.landlautomotive.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/snowboarding-z-300x194.jpg" style="margin: 5px;" width="270"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            L&amp;L Automotive also recognizes
            <a href="http://lizzyyarnold.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://lizzyyarnold.com']);" target="_blank" title="Lizzy Yarnold">
             Lizzy Yarnold
            </a>
            from Sevenoaks. Lizzy only started her skeleton training full-time two years ago, yet here she is a gold medal winner in the exciting and dangerous event. The 25 year olds life has become an open book, with everything from her love life to her taste in music being discussed in the media. We know she will enjoy her new status as a celebrity.
           </p>
           <p>
            One of the greatest parts of watching the Olympics is the pure, rawemotion that we witness. We sawthe tearful disappointment of
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eve_Muirhead" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://en.wikipedia.org']);" target="_blank" title="Eve Muirhead">
             Eve Muirhead
            </a>
            in Vancouver in 2010 when her curling team was eliminated early. This year we got to see tears of joy as she and her teammates celebrated a 6-5 win over Switzerland to win the bronze medal. Eve and her teammates Claire Hamilton, Anna Sloan, and Vicki Adams were joined on the ice by Coach Rhona Howey. Rhona won gold at the 2002 Salt Lake City Olympics. It is a scene that we will long remember.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://news.landlautomotive.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/1920203_10152059302452600_1377957879_n.jpg" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             <img alt="" class=" wp-image-619 alignleft" height="177" src="http://news.landlautomotive.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/1920203_10152059302452600_1377957879_n-300x197.jpg" style="margin: 5px;" width="270"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Team GBs mens curling team has guaranteed our fourth medal this year. The team will win at least a silver medal when they face Canada in the finals. The
            </span>
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Murdoch" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://en.wikipedia.org']);" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank" title="David Murdoch">
             David Murdoch
            </a>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             led team secured their place by coming from behind to defeat Sweden 6-5 in an exciting semi-final. Nerve wracking is what teammate Greg Drummond called it. We loved it.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            The L&amp;L Automotive Group, with Mercedes and smart retail locations in Stevenage, Hertford and Bishops Stortford, join with the rest of our countrymen in saluting the efforts of these athletes. It is a record setting performance that we appreciate and have been proud to witness.
           </p>
