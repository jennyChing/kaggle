                  <div class="post-content">
                   <p>
                    <img alt="Soccer" class="alignnone wp-image-9769" height="698" src="http://www.ipsmarx.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Soccer.jpg" width="1040"/>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    The 2014 FIFA World Cup Brazil has arrived. Billions of people all over the planet are watching the Beautiful Game and rooting for their favorite teamswhether these teams are from their country of origin, country of residence or a separate country all together. And that means a lot of people are making international phone calls to talk with their friends and family overseas about the latest match, best plays, their hopes for their favorite teams and more.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    As weve
                    <a href="http://www.ipsmarx.com/company/news-and-press-releases/" target="_blank" title="IPsmarx Clients Experience Up to 75 Percent Traffic Surge During Holiday Season">
                     previously noted
                    </a>
                    , IPsmarx clients experience a surge in traffic during the holidays that translates into increased revenue during that time of the year.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Spikes in traffic such as these offer opportunities for VoIP and prepaid service providers. For example, service providers can
                   </p>
                   <p>
                     Increase rates during peak traffic periods to increase revenue directlyit is recommended, however, that service providers only increase their rates modestly so as to not lose customers; the high volume of traffic can still allow significant extra revenue to be earned despite the only modest increase in rates.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                     Offer discount rates during peak times in order to demonstrate the value of your service and boost customer loyalty; moreover, its also possible that youll actually encourage more traffic by offering discount rates which could, in turn, translate into more revenue than would otherwise be earned.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                     Beyond rates, you can customize your IVR or e-store messages to mention the World Cupthese messages may even prompt your customers to call their contacts overseas, which could, in turn, translate into extra revenue for you.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Whatever you decide to do, we hope that you enjoy the World Cup! May the best team win.
                   </p>
