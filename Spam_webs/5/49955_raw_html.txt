       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Two Simple Words
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="7674927" data-link="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/two-simple-words/" data-summary="" data-title="Two Simple Words">

        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/thank-you.jpg">
          <img alt="thank-you" class="wp-image-283 aligncenter" height="234" src="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/thank-you.jpg" width="354"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <h2>
         Two Simple Words
        </h2>
        <p>
         In todays world far too often we, yes, you and I forget these
         <strong>
          simple words
         </strong>
         . When do these simple words become lost on society? These two words can mean so much to someone whether is be in business, a friend, or just a stranger you have never met. If you have not guessed by now the two simple words are
         <strong>
          Thank you.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h2>
         My Children
        </h2>
        <p>
         My wife and I have three kids, 9, 6, and 21 months needless to say we are crazy busy. It may be the park, bike rides, running, dance, or swimming. My kids are active, what this has to do with Thank you is that this is a lost saying. You are taught at a young age to say
         <strong>
          please and thank you
         </strong>
         , agree Yes, as we get older we tend to say
         <strong>
          please and thank you
         </strong>
         less, now this is just my thinking, it is as we get older we are seem to think the little things dont matter as much.
        </p>
        <p>
         As I watch my kids grow up we will reinforce the
         <strong>
          please and thank you,
         </strong>
         my 21 month old just gets it, just as my girls did at a younger age. They are still
         <strong>
          very polite
         </strong>
         but will every now and then forget
         <strong>
          please and thank you.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Business World
        </h2>
        <p>
         This brings me to the business world how you are so geared towards making more sales and money, there are many people out there pitching this and that and not once
         <strong>
          <em>
           looking at you
          </em>
         </strong>
         . They see you as a dollar sign, how does that make you feel, cheap. Sure every now and them you get a Thank you at the end to of the transaction and you should you just spent money. By now you are saying
         <strong>
          <em>
           Thats right, and so true.
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         You as a business owner you need to be going for the
         <strong>
          Thank you
         </strong>
         from you customers. How would your business change if your customers looked for you just to say
         <strong>
          Thank you.
         </strong>
         How would that make you feel? Imagine right now that 9 out of 10 customers left a comment you how your product changed their life? How your company turned them into a positive person? How your leadership inspired them to conquer that Mountain of fear you had?
        </p>
        <h2>
         Smile
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/punja.jpg">
          <img alt="punja" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-282" height="194" src="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/punja.jpg" width="260"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Right now
         </em>
         you have a
         <strong>
          smile
         </strong>
         , you are
         <strong>
          feeling pretty good
         </strong>
         , and you should you have just
         <strong>
          helped many people
         </strong>
         who now as saying
         <strong>
          Thank you
         </strong>
         say it with me
         <strong>
          Thank you
         </strong>
         These two simple words can make your business grow and help you reclaim back you life, it will be like having customers buy on autopilot.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can do this and all it comes down to is your
         <strong>
          thinking
         </strong>
         . Think of going for the
         <strong>
          Thank you
         </strong>
         instead of the sale. Once you start going for the Thank you, you are putting yourself in the customers shoes, youre looking through their eyes, and you are helping them make the decision now.
         <em>
          We can agree on this ,
          <strong>
           Yes?!
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/moving-forward.png">
           <img alt="moving forward" class="size-full wp-image-281 alignright" height="182" src="http://michaelseanmcdonald.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/moving-forward.png" width="277"/>
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h2>
         One Step Ahead
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Moving forward
         </strong>
         go for the Thank you and watch your customers flock to you with money in hand just waiting for you to help them. You might be thinking, too good to be true  here is an example.
        </p>
        <p>
         When you have a headache where do you go, the pharmacy, why? To ease the pain. Once the headache is gone, you generally say Thank you to the manufacturer of the product.
        </p>
