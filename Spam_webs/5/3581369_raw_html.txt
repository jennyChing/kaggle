                      <span>

                       FGM
                      </a>
                      . It was addressed to the UK's education minister Michael Gove, and posted by a young woman called Fahma Mohamed. We asked will you stand with Fahma and sign her petition and the good news is many people did just that.
                      <br/>
                      <span>
                       <br/>
                       <span>
                        Below is the update received Monday:
                       </span>

                      <br/>
                      <span style="">

                       my petition
                      </a>
                      for Michael Gove to take the issue of FGM seriously and write to all headteachers before the summer holidays.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      It is incredible and we are already having an impact;
                      <span style="">
                       Scotland's government has announced it will write to every headteacher
                      </span>
                      asking them to train their staff and educate parents about FGM.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      We still haven't had a response from Michael Gove. On Thursday the  Department of Education said it was reviewing school safeguarding  guidance to include a reference to FGM. But this is not enough.
                      <strong style="">
                       Michael Gove has the power to help us end FGM within a generation.
                      </strong>
