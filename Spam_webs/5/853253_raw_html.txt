         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Does Anyone Else Think Its Weird That Gwyneth Paltrow Bathes With Her Kids?
         </h1>
         <ul class="post-details clearfix">
          <li>
           3 years ago by
           <a href="http://www.crushable.com/author/achatel/" rel="author" title="Posts by Amanda Chatel">
            Amanda Chatel
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.crushable.com/2012/02/02/entertainment/does-anyone-else-think-its-weird-that-gwyneth-paltrow-bathes-with-her-kids-849/#comments" title="Comment on Does Anyone Else Think Its Weird That Gwyneth Paltrow Bathes With Her Kids?">
            <span class="dsq-postid" rel="413140 http://crushable.com/?p=413140">
             18 Comments 
            </span>
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="#top" onclick="show_modal_window('share-tip-window');">
            Share a Tip
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
          </li>
         </ul>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-413145" height="480" src="http://cdn.crushable.com/files/2012/02/Gwyneth-Paltrow-026-640x480.jpg" width="640"/>
          Do you remember the last time you saw your parents naked? Unless you grew up in a naked house, to quote Charlotte York from
          <em>
           Sex and the City
          </em>
          , or you were raised in a nudist colony, you probably cant remember when you last saw either one of your parents in the buff.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Gwyneth Paltrow
          </strong>
          s kids, however, see her naked on a daily basis. Not only that, but
          <a href="http://www.celebitchy.com/206046/gwyneth_paltrow_talks_beyonce_feminism_infidelity_with_harpers_bazaar/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.celebitchy.com']);" target="_blank">
           when it comes to bath time, she just gets right in there with Apple and Moses and scrubs them on down
          </a>
          . While this could be deemed environmentally friendly because theyre using the same bath water for all three of them, her kiddos are seven and five years old. If they didnt have private tutors and actually went to school like the rest of the kids their age, one has to wonder exactly how a teacher would respond to a seven-year-old telling them Mommy gets right in the tub with us and we have a wash fest!
         </p>
         <p>
          As Paltrow
          <a href="http://www.celebitchy.com/206046/gwyneth_paltrow_talks_beyonce_feminism_infidelity_with_harpers_bazaar/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.celebitchy.com']);" target="_blank">
           told
          </a>
          <em>
           <a href="http://www.celebitchy.com/206046/gwyneth_paltrow_talks_beyonce_feminism_infidelity_with_harpers_bazaar/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.celebitchy.com']);" target="_blank">
            Harpers Bazaar
           </a>
          </em>
          quite frankly:We all get into the tub together. On top of that somewhat creepy factor, what sort of tub do these people have? Is it the size of a small swimming pool? And is there the potential for these kids to be scarred from seeing their moms hooha at that age? But I wasnt raised in a naked house, so I dont know whats appropriate naked behavior.
         </p>
         <p>
          If someone could fill me on whether or not this is normal, Id greatly appreciate it.
         </p>
