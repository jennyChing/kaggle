        <a id="dd_start">

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/SHORTLIST-THE-RANIA-EXEPERIENCE-MALDIVES.png">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3022" height="594" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/SHORTLIST-THE-RANIA-EXEPERIENCE-MALDIVES.png" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="954"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You know that fantasy you have of leaving everything behind and flying away to a tiny private island so beautiful it would make computer screensavers jealous? The one where youre just a spec in the middle of a far flung ocean and miles away from the stresses of the world. Well, it turns out thats actually a real place, and its called
         <a href="http://www.raniaexperience.com/lite/en/" target="_blank">
          The Rania Experience
         </a>
         in the Maldives.
        </p>
        <p>
         It could be called an experience of a lifetime, but chances are, if you can afford to go once, youve got enough in the bank to go again. But lets not talk about price just yet, for now, lets keep on dreaming.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/THE-RANIA-EXPERIENCE.png">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter" height="359" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/THE-RANIA-EXPERIENCE.png" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="597"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/CVT_MAL3_19323.jpg">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2996" height="390" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/CVT_MAL3_19323.jpg" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="592"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Looking out to the horizon from this seven acre, private island paradise, youll see nothing but theaquamarine waters of the Indian Ocean  oh yeah, and an 86-foot yacht,which, like the island, is also completely and totally yours for the duration of your stay.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Rania-Experiecne-Plunge-Pool.png">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3016" height="403" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Rania-Experiecne-Plunge-Pool.png" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="594"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And like any good fantasy, The Rania Experience just keeps getting better. A team of private butlers will be on hand to tend to your every need and a team of chefs will prepare anything you want, whenever you want it. If all this relaxing is stressing you out, dont forget about the spa counselorswho are on hand to provide unlimited treatments.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you get bored on land, you can island hop on your yacht, try big game fishing for wahoo, grouper, barracuda, jackfish, and sailfish, orgoscuba diving with your very own PADI registered dive instructor.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Rania-Yacht-Jacuzzi-Couple.jpg">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2998" height="391" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Rania-Yacht-Jacuzzi-Couple.jpg" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="588"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC5904aHR.jpg">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3001" height="393" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC5904aHR.jpg" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="587"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you were kind enough to bring your friends along, theres a three bedroom residence with a plunge pool and three separate beach villas that, when combined, can house up to 12 people. After dinner, which is served in a surprise location around the island every night, everyone can gather in the entertainment pavilion where a home theater, pool table, and board games await.
        </p>
        <p>
         But the feature of the island sure to provide you and your guests with the most enjoyment is the professional karaoke system (in which youll definitely not cover
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gUhRKVIjJtw" target="_blank" title="Mo Money Mo Problems">
          Mo Money Mo Problems byNotorious B.I.G
         </a>
         .).Maybe try an ironic cover of
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8aRor905cCw" target="_blank" title="Billionaire by Bruno Mars">
          Billionaire by Bruno Mars
         </a>
         or after a few mojitosgive
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q0SyUgw98tE" target="_blank" title="Glamorous by Fergie">
          Glamorous by Fergie
         </a>
         a shot  your butler can do the Ludacris rap.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Rania-Experience.png">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3012" height="550" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Rania-Experience.png" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="598"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/IMG_0102_straighten.jpg">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3004" height="382" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/IMG_0102_straighten.jpg" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="603"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC6682a.jpg">
          <img alt="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3006" height="393" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/DSC6682a.jpg" title="The Rania Experience - Maldives - Trevor Morrow Travel" width="587"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So what does it cost to actually live inside a stock photo representation of the words
         <em>
          relaxation
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          paradise?
         </em>
         The Rania Experience nightly rates range from $9,850  $17,500 per night for two people with each additional person costing $800. A three night minimum is required.
        </p>
