              <h1 class="article-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
               Red Velvet  Luxe Winter Styling in Leather and Lace
              </h1>
             </div>
             <div class="article-meta article-meta-top">
              <span class="article-date article-meta-item">
               <time class="updated" datetime="2014-11-17" itemprop="datePublished">
                November 17, 2014
               </time>
              </span>
              by
              <span class="article-author-link article-meta-item vcard" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
               <a class="fn url" href="http://heyweddinglady.com/author/heyweddinglady/" itemprop="name url">
                Ariana - Hey Wedding Lady
               </a>
              </span>

             <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="Red Velvet  Luxe Winter Styling in Leather and Lace" data-frizzlyposturl="http://heyweddinglady.com/red-velvet-luxe-winter-styling-leather-lace/" type="hidden" value=""/>
             <p>
              Todays stunning luxe winter styled shoot in red, gold, and green will put you in the holiday spirit, but its gorgeous all on its own!
              <a href="http://www.samanthakirkphotography.com/" target="_blank">
               Samantha Kirk Photography
              </a>
              and
              <a href="http://botanicaevents.com/rentals/" target="_blank">
               Botanica Event and Floral Design
              </a>
              crafted this truly incredible shoot to celebrate the beauty of winter in California. Lush
              <a href="http://heyweddinglady.com/tag/botanical/" target="_blank">
               botanical
              </a>
              details in deep red and
              <a href="/tag/berry/" target="_blank">
               berry
              </a>
              add warmth to the rich winter sunlight, along with the gleam of antique gold. Touches of silvery green from succulents, jasmine vine, and dusty miller added a lovely rustic element beautifully suited to the location at
              <a href="http://lawleyranch.com/" target="_blank">
               Lawley Ranch
              </a>
              . Vintage club chairs in burgundy leather, a settee in plush crimson velvet, and fur throws in winter white add gorgeous textural elements to the shoot that stand out beautifully in the film photographs. A gorgeous wedding dress  long, lace sleeves perfect for a romantic
              <a href="/tag/winter/" target="_blank">
               winter
              </a>
              bride  with a vintage fur shrug add to the luxe detailing, especially when paired with an antique gold and diamond ring and subtly glamorous bridal styling.
             </p>
             <p>
              The loving attention to each and every detail is what endeared this shoot to me, above and beyond the initial beauty of the photographs.
              <a href="http://botanicaevents.com/rentals/" target="_blank">
               Botanicas
              </a>
              design skills and vintage inventory were given the chance to truly shine with the intricate styling of the Sweetheart Table and mantelpiece. Vintage
              <a href="/tag/gold/" target="_blank">
               gold
              </a>
              vessels and goblets are paired with cut crystal glassware and china bearing delicate metallic patterns. The calligraphy place cards by
              <a href="http://www.steelemyheart.com/" target="_blank">
               Steele My Heart
              </a>
              bear a delicate hand painted floral scroll, echoing the elaborate table runner of greenery, interspersed with massive protea and gilded succulents. The lavish cake display by
              <a href="http://www.paperheartpatisserie.com/" target="_blank">
               Paper Heart Patisserie
              </a>
              is not to be outdone, with petite cakes and tiny tarts in the same abundance as petite arrangements of pepper berry, carnations, and cattails.
             </p>
