         <div class="author">
          Written By Steve Watts

        <div class="gradient">
         <p class="blurb">
          Valve and Dark Horse Comics have teamed up to bring a hardcover collection of the various comics from the Valve universes, including Team Fortress 2, Portal, and Left 4 Dead.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <article class="post">
       <p>
        Valve has been making comics around some of its biggest properties for months for free online, but now the company has partnered with Dark Horse Comics to bring them out in print.
        <a href="http://www.comicbookresources.com/?page=article&amp;id=33181" target="_blank">
         Comic Book Resources
        </a>
        (via
        <a href="http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/35767/Comic_Book_Based_On_Valve_Strips_Coming_This_November.php" target="_blank">
         Gamasutra
        </a>
        ) reports that "Valve Presents: The Sacrifice and Other Steam-Powered Stories" will go on sale November 16 for $29.99.
       </p>
       <p>
        The collection is a bit pricier than the average comic collection, but it's also more than
        <i>
         three-hundred
        </i>
        pages long and sports a hardcover. That makes it hard to complain. It will include stories from the worlds of
        <a href="http://www.shacknews.com/game/team-fortress-2">
         Team Fortress 2
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.shacknews.com/game/portal">
         Portal
        </a>
        , and
        <a href="http://www.shacknews.com/game/left-4-dead">
         Left 4 Dead
        </a>
        . All indications are that this is a compilation of comics you can already find online, but it wouldn't surprise us if Valve slipped a bonus or two in there.
       </p>
      </article>
