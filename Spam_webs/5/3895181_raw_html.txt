       Admission notice for the summer semester 2015 at
       <strong>
        Central Womens University
       </strong>
       .
       <p>
        <strong>
         Central Womens University
        </strong>
        is one of the first private universities in Bangladesh, established in 1993. It was the first university in Bangladesh established exclusively for the purpose of female education.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Semester: Summer 2015
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Admission Deadline:
        </strong>
        May 16, 2015
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Classes Start:
        </strong>
        May 17, 2015
       </p>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Four Year Undergraduate Programs:
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <ul>
        <li>
         BBA, English, Computer Science &amp; Engineering (CSE), Sociology &amp; Gender Studies, Political Science &amp; Governance Studies, Geography &amp; Environmental Studies, History of the Muslim World.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         One/Two year Graduate programs:
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <ul>
        <li>
         English, Bangla, Economics, Political Science &amp; Governance Studies, Geography &amp; Environmental Studies, Sociology &amp; Gender Studies, History of the Muslim World
        </li>
       </ul>
       <h3>
        MBA (60 Credit) &amp; EMBA (45 Credit)
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Specialization Areas for MBA &amp; EMBA:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Finance &amp; Banking, Marketing, Human Resource Management, Bank Management, Accounting Information System, Women Entrepreneurship
       </p>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         University Highlights:
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Permanent Campus in city center
        </li>
        <li>
         Highly qualified faculty members
        </li>
        <li>
         10 to 100%
         <a href="http://varsityeduinfo.com/category/scholarships/">
          <strong>
           Scholarships
          </strong>
         </a>
         for meritorious students
        </li>
        <li>
         Financial assistance for deserving students
        </li>
        <li>
         Tuition discounts to siblings
        </li>
        <li>
         Credit waiver/transfer available
        </li>
        <li>
         Modern computer lab with internet facilities
        </li>
        <li>
         Rich library with wide selection of books and journals
        </li>
        <li>
         Wide choice of co-curricular activities/clubs
        </li>
        <li>
         Career counseling &amp; placement facilities
        </li>
        <li>
         Evening/Weekend classes for working students
        </li>
       </ul>
