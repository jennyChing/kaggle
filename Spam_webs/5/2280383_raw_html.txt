&lt;h2&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Travis Browne&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Vitals&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;6&#8217;7&#8243; 243 lbs (Heavyweight)&lt;br /&gt;
78&#8243; reach, Orthodox&lt;br /&gt;
July 17, 1982&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Record&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;17-3-1 (UFC: 8-3-1)&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Current Streak&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;1 straight loss&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Training&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Purple belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Championships Held&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;VFC Heavyweight Champion: 2010 (no title defenses)&lt;br /&gt;
Gladiator Challenge Heavyweight Champion: 2010 (no title defenses)&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Strengths&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#8211; great athlete &#8211; former college basketball player&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; great size (6&#8217;7&#8243;) and reach (78&#8243;)&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; very well coached (&lt;a href="http://www.mma-manifesto.com/2012-articles/august/top-ten-mma-fight-teams-2-team-jackson-winklejohn.html"  target="_blank"&gt;Team Jackson-Winklejohn&lt;/a&gt;)&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; knockout power with his hands and feet&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; decent submission game&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; finishes fights&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; good clinch &amp; ground striker&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; great at passing guard&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; superb at landing takedowns&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; great takedown defense&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; can survive heavy shots&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; diverse striker &#8211; mixes in unorthodox strikes&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; good footwork &amp; movement for a big man&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Weaknesses&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#8211; old given his level of experience&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; questionable cardio&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; inaccurate striker&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; poor striking defense&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; gets hit more than he hits his opponent&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; can be knocked out&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Synopsis&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Travis Browne&#8217;s meteoric rise up the heavyweight division was stopped by Fabricio Werdum. And then by Andrei Arlovski.&lt;/p&gt;
