     <div class="article-body" itemprop="articleBody">
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <strong>
        [Warning! MAJOR
        <em>
         Breaking Bad
        </em>
        SPOILERS!]
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       AMCs widely-acclaimed drama
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/breaking-bad/">
        <em>
         <strong>
          Breaking Bad
         </strong>
        </em>
       </a>
       certainly went out with a bang, not only drawing in a series-high audience of
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/breaking-bad-finale-series-high-ratings-behind-scenes/">
        10.3 million viewers
       </a>
       , but also  if the
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/breaking-bad-series-finale-ending-spoilers/">
        response from our readers
       </a>
       is anything to go by  giving fans a satisfying conclusion to a story that has been consistently gripping for five seasons. For a series whose characters are predominantly criminals, theres a lot of love for
       <em>
        Breaking Bad
       </em>
       and for all the people whose stories have been woven into the tale of high school teacher Walter Whites transformation into meth kingpin Heisenberg.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <span id="more-374525">
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="advert zergnet">
       <span>
        From The Web
       </span>
       <div id="zergnet-unit-in-article">
       </div>
       <script>
        /*&lt;![CDATA[*/document.write('\
                &lt;div id="zergnet-widget-30857"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;\
                &lt;script language="javascript" type="text/javascript"&gt;\
                    (function() {\
                        var zergnet = document.createElement(\'script\');\
                        zergnet.type = \'text/javascript\'; zergnet.async = true;\
                        zergnet.src = \'http://www.zergnet.com/zerg.js?id=30857\';\

                        znscr.parentNode.insertBefore(zergnet, znscr);\
                    })();\
                &lt;\/script&gt;\
            ');/*]]&gt;*/
       </script>
      </div>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Until something else comes along to fill the gaping wound in our viewing schedules, fans of the show have been using the time since the finale aired to create
       <em>
        Breaking Bad
       </em>
       tributes. The first of these, shown above, is a 7-minute tribute titled
       <em>
        The Heisenberg Legacy
       </em>
       and edited together by Nick Bosworth of
       <em>
        JoBlo
       </em>
       . It recaps the story of the show from start to finish, and the changes that all the different characters underwent as events unfolded. It also gives you a welcome reminder of some of the shows most memorable moments (though sadly not the 
       <em>
        Get off the toilet
       </em>
        scene  you can watch that
       <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7cHoKwqIXjE" target="_blank">
        here
       </a>
       ).
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Breaking Bad Best Moments Header Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Breaking-Bad-Best-Moments-Header.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h200-q90-c380:200/,481:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,768:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,1024:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,481:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,768:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,1024:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 52.6315789474%;">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Breaking Bad Best Moments Header Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/wp-content/uploads/Breaking-Bad-Best-Moments-Header.jpg" title="Breaking Bad Best Moments Header Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]"/>
        </noscript>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Somewhat bizarrely, considering the subject of the show,
       <em>
        Breaking Bad
       </em>
       has been a great boon to Albuquerque tourism industry and Heisenbergs blue meth has become the Mickey Mouse of Albuquerque.
       <a href="http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/10/01/entertainment-us-usa-television-breaking-idUSBRE98T19320131001" target="_blank">
        <em>
         Reuters
        </em>
       </a>
       reports that the citys Rebel Donuts chain sold $10,000 dollars worth of its 
       <em>
        blue meth donuts
       </em>
        (blue frosted with crystal rock candy) in the hours leading up to the finale, andspa products firm owner Keith West-Harrison said that sales of the companys 
       <em>
        Bathing Bad
       </em>
        blue bath salts paid for building renovation work that needed to be carried out. Even the
       <em>
        Albuquerque Journal
       </em>
       profited from the finale, as in the wake of the shows end, some fans clubbed together to pay for a very special obituary.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Huell and Patrick at peace in Breaking Bad Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Huell-and-Patrick-at-peace-in-Breaking-Bad.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h200-q90-c380:200/,481:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,768:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,1024:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/" data-size2x="0:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,481:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,768:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/,1024:slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 52.6315789474%;">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Huell and Patrick at peace in Breaking Bad Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w570-h300-q90-c570:300/wp-content/uploads/Huell-and-Patrick-at-peace-in-Breaking-Bad.jpg" title="Huell and Patrick at peace in Breaking Bad Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]"/>
        </noscript>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       No, it wasnt for Huell and Patrick, who thankfully were amongst the survivors of the show (were pretty sure that Huell is still sitting in that safe house), but instead for Heisenberg himself. Love him or hate him,
       <em>
        Breaking Bad
       </em>
       wouldnt exist without Walter White and his increasingly dubious moral compass, and he finally found a fitting end after his long battle with cancer and his relatively short battle with a rebounding bullet.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Los Lunas resident David Layman and members of the Facebook group
       <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/161417077202383/" target="_blank">
        
        <em>
         Unofficial
        </em>
        Breaking Bad
        <em>
         Fan Tour
        </em>
        
       </a>
       placed an obituary for Walter White in the Friday 4th October issue of the
       <em>
        <a href="http://www.abqjournal.com/275038/news/abq-news/it-provides-closure.html" target="_blank">
         Albuquerque Journal
        </a>
       </em>
       , as a means of providing closure to a series that Layman says turned Albuquerque into 
       <em>
        no longer a stopover [but] a destination
       </em>
       . In (sort of) loving memory, heres the obituary of Walter White.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="Breaking Bad Walter White obituary in the Albuquerque Journal Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]" data-image="wp-content/uploads/Breaking-Bad-Walter-White-obituary-in-the-Albuquerque-Journal.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/,481:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/,768:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/,1024:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/" data-size2x="0:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/,481:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/,768:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/,1024:slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 124.475524476%;">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Breaking Bad Walter White obituary in the Albuquerque Journal Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w286-h356-q90-c286:356/wp-content/uploads/Breaking-Bad-Walter-White-obituary-in-the-Albuquerque-Journal.jpg" title="Breaking Bad Walter White obituary in the Albuquerque Journal Breaking Bad: Relive Entire Series in 7 Minute Tribute Video [Spoilers]"/>
        </noscript>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Donating to a drug abuse prevention charity is probably a pretty good way to pay tribute to the show; after all, if
       <em>
        Breaking Bad
       </em>
       can help businesses selling donuts and bath salts then maybe the show could also help a real-life Jesse Pinkman from ever getting involved with the world of meth amphetamine in the first place.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       If youve found another fun tribute to
       <em>
        Breaking Bad
       </em>
       that youd like to share, or if you just want to reminisce about your favorite moments from the show, then leave us a note in the comments. To find out what we thought of the series finale, check out the
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/breaking-bad-series-finale-review/">
        Screen Rant review
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <strong>
        <em>
         Breaking Bad: The Complete Series
        </em>
       </strong>
       will be available on Blu-ray from November 26th, 2013.
      </p>

        Cinema Blend
       </a>
       )
      </p>
