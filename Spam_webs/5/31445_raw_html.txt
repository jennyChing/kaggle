                       This is the third headband pattern from my elasticized crochet headbands series! Wear it with or without the flower applique, the choice is yours!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-B_qXGGKsarI/U5tCVTZQOeI/AAAAAAAAIjQ/rWOifPdS4pE/s1600/crossed+stitch+crochet+headband+free+pattern.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-B_qXGGKsarI/U5tCVTZQOeI/AAAAAAAAIjQ/rWOifPdS4pE/s1600/crossed+stitch+crochet+headband+free+pattern.jpg" width="418"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0cm; text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0cm; text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                        <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/crossed-stitch-headband-with-flower-applique" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                         Click here to add this pattern to your Ravelry Queue and to your Favorites
                        </a>
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        Materials
                       </b>
                       :
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       small amount (approx 20 g) of cotton yarn in 2 colors; yarn weight:DK 3 (Light)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">
                        I used
                       </span>
                       <a href="http://www.redheart.de/yarn/miami" rel="nofollow" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;" target="_blank">
                        Red Heart Miami
                       </a>
                       .
                       <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        You can replace it with other cotton yarn having the same weight.
                       </span>
                       <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       3 mm crochet hook
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       2 elastic hair ties
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       sewing needle
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        Stitches, terms (US) and abbreviations used
                       </b>
                       :
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        ch
                       </b>
                       : chain
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        sc
                       </b>
                       : single crochet
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        dc
                       </b>
                       : double crochet
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        ssc
                       </b>
                       : standing single crochet: wrap yarn around hook once; while holding the yarn tail with the other hand, insert hook in the stitch indicated in the pattern, yarn over, pull up a loop, yarn over and pull through both loops on hook.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        csdc
                       </b>
                       : chainless starting double crochet (See
                       <a href="http://www.mooglyblog.com/chainless-starting-double-crochet/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                        Tutorial
                       </a>
                       by Moogly)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        sl st
                       </b>
                       : slip stitch
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       -
                       <b>
                        crossed double crochet
                       </b>
                       (explained in the pattern)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        Size available
                       </b>
                       : This pattern is written for an adult women, but  you can easily adapt for smaller sizes working fewer rows. You can also use only one elastic and work more rows until you reach the desired length.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <h2>
                       <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: x-large;">
                        Charted Pattern
                       </b>
                       <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                        :
                       </span>
                      </h2>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-OT_cnmMB62Q/U5tnwjh8ynI/AAAAAAAAIkg/5iYnRjGIWu4/s1600/crossed+stitch+headband+crochet+chart.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-OT_cnmMB62Q/U5tnwjh8ynI/AAAAAAAAIkg/5iYnRjGIWu4/s1600/crossed+stitch+headband+crochet+chart.jpg" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <h2>
                       <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         Written Instructions:
                        </span>
                       </b>
                      </h2>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        Take one hair tie; we will cast on directly on it. See the picture tutorial from my
                       </span>
                       <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <a href="http://www.myhobbyiscrochet.com/2013/08/thread-headband-free-pattern-and.html" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-weight: bold;" target="_blank">
                         Thread Headband
                        </a>
                        <b>
                        </b>
                        pattern.
                       </span>
                       <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        <br/>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        Row 1
                       </b>
                       : With red yarn begin with a standing sc around the elastic band. Work  6 sc more around the elastic band.  (7 sc total) . Turn.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       Row 2
                      </b>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       : 1 csdc, 1 dc in next st, sk 1 st, 3 dc in next st, sk 1 st, 1 dc in each of next 2 sts. Turn.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       Row 3
                      </b>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       : 1 csdc, 1dc in next st, sk 2 dc, 1 dc in next dc, ch 1, working back over last dc made, make 1dc into first of 2 dc just skipped; 1 dc in each of next 2 sts.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       Row 4
                      </b>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       : 1 csdc, 1 dc in next st, 3 dc into ch 1 space of the crossed st, 1 dc in each of last 2 sts.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: x-large;">
                       Row 5- 32
                      </b>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       :  Repeat as row 3- 4.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       Take the second hair tie, insert it through the first one, fold in the middle and pass one side through the other side, pull till the both elastics are forming a knot. See photo collage below!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5R6GVK7vM7Q/U5tDxgOmKYI/AAAAAAAAIjY/gmVD2rLQdW0/s1600/crochet+headband+with+elastic.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5R6GVK7vM7Q/U5tDxgOmKYI/AAAAAAAAIjY/gmVD2rLQdW0/s1600/crochet+headband+with+elastic.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       Casting the second tail on the hair tie: ch 1,  make 1 sc
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       around the elastic and  in each of 7 sts
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       of previous row.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       Cut yarn leaving a tail.
                      </span>
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       You will note that the top loops of the stitches from the last row are at the edge of the hair tie. You will need to pull them to the inside of the headband and sew them like in the collage below, inserting the needle through the both top loops of the stitches from the last row and though the stitches of the row before. Continue to stitch along the row. Cut yarn. Fasten off.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="color: black; font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-74fFZcowcno/U5tEATZ1ExI/AAAAAAAAIjg/SZ05xZWNV3Y/s1600/PicMonkey+Collage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-74fFZcowcno/U5tEATZ1ExI/AAAAAAAAIjg/SZ05xZWNV3Y/s1600/PicMonkey+Collage.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <b style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        Flower Pattern
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        <i>
                         Flower Center
                        </i>
                       </b>
                       : With red, ch 5, sl st to form a ring.  Ch 1, ( 1 sc into ring, ch 3) x 8 times, sl st to first sc. Cut yarn. Fasten off.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        <i>
                         Petals
                        </i>
                       </b>
                       : Join white yarn with standing sc into a ch 3 space of the center, ch2, (3 dc, ch2, 1 sc) into same ch space, * ( 1 sc, ch 2, 3 dc, ch 2, 1 sc) into next ch space *, repeat from * to * for each remaining ch space; sl st into first sc of the round.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       (8 petals made)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       Cut yarn. Fasten off.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        <i>
                         Note
                        </i>
                       </b>
                       : If your flower is starting to curl, block it.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: large;">
                      Sew the flower on the headband.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div align="LEFT" style="margin-bottom: 0cm;">
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jT128JXK_4k/U5tKAM-a9kI/AAAAAAAAIkA/o9zOIzuW0b8/s1600/free+crochet+pattern+headband+with+elastic.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jT128JXK_4k/U5tKAM-a9kI/AAAAAAAAIkA/o9zOIzuW0b8/s1600/free+crochet+pattern+headband+with+elastic.jpg" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
