          <br/>
          The concept lamp, Nissyoku from igendesign is one beautiful creation. Working on magnetic mechanism, this unique lamp will make for the most amazing bedside light source. Inspired by the solar eclipse, this lamp is truly breath taking. The magnet is situated on the two len-form panels, and there is metal surface which fixes the magnet. The len-form panels found on both sides can move in various directions and the strength of lighting is adjusted by the degree of turning. The two panels move on the concave surface made out of the intersection of the two globes. The lamp is fashioned through vacuum forming. There is a 5W high powered LED per panel. By touching the middle ring, you can either switch on or off this beautiful lamp.
         </p>
