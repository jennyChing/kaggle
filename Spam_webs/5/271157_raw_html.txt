         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           This summer hit thebeachlike its 1969!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/teen-beach-movie.png">
           <img alt="teen-beach-movie" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19300" height="260" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/teen-beach-movie.png" width="390"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Schools out, surfs up and Disney Channel is taking a dip into the teen beach party movie genre with Teen Beach Movie, a modern no more complications, just good vibrations, break into song and dance comedy in which surfer girl McKenzie and her boyfriend Brady literally go into a classic surf movie musical where its bikers versus surfers  and theyre in for trouble if they dont back down. While they hatch a plan to get back home to present day, the duo try to blend in as everyone around them spontaneously breaks into song and dance. But when they interfere with the plot of the movie, their fate may be changed forever.
           <span id="more-19299">
           </span>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           The movie begins on the last day of summer, and McKenzies surf and sand life is about to be upturned when she departs for boarding school to fulfill her familys dream to graduate from the elite institution. But when she and Brady find themselves literally transported into his favorite classic beach musical, set in the 1960s, about a rivalry between the surfers and bikers and a star-crossed teenage boy who likes a girl, he is ecstatic for endless summer while she is eager to get back home to keep her promise to her family about her schooling. While they hatch a plan to get back to present day, the duo try to blend in and understand 1960s teenagers, but its not so easy when everyone around them spontaneously breaks into song and dance. And when they accidentally interfere with the plot of the movie and the lead characters, surf guy Tanner and biker girl Lela, fall for them, instead of each other, Brady and McKenzie fear their fate may be changed forever.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/teen-beach-movie-1.png">
           <img alt="teen-beach-movie (1)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19301" height="301" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/teen-beach-movie-1.png" width="216"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I received a copy of Teen Beach Movie to review and sat down with my boys last weekend to watch it. My oldest son has seen some of the tv commercials for the movie, so when it showed up on DVD, he was pretty excited. As you read above, its a teen romance about a couple of surfers who get mysteriously get taken into Bradys favorite movie, the 1962 beach party movie, Wet Side Story. The whole movie centers about Brady and Mack trying to get back to present day, but to do that they need to get through the whole movie. By accident they end up altering the romantic story line of the movie and they have to rectify it before they can leave and go home.  And whats a classic beach party movie without lots of singing and dancing along the way. Teen Beach Movie had a pretty predictable outcome and my little guy (almost age six) was bored with it after a few minutes, but my nine year old absolutely loved it. I foresee us watching this many more times in the near future.
         </p>
         <p>
          In case youve managed to miss all the recent hype surrounding the movie, heres the trailer:
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>

          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/6FuIBuXCiTo" width="560">

          </center>
         </center>
         Since July 30th the DVD has been available at retailers nationwide and the DVD comes specially packaged with a collectible surfboard zipper pull-while supplies last. The DVD also includes an exclusive new music, a dance number and includes behind-the-scenes rehearsal footage.
