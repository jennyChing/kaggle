           <h1 class="entry-title">
            Canicross for beginners: How to get started
           </h1>
           <p class="meta-line">
            <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://www.canicrossusa.com/2014/06/17/" rel="bookmark" title="6:17 pm">
             <i class="icon-left fa fa-calendar-o">
             </i>
             <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-06-17T18:17:33+00:00">
              June 17, 2014
             </time>
            </a>
           </p>
          </header>
          <div class="smartlib-single-image-container">
           <img alt="6.17 canicross 2" class="attachment-wide-image wp-post-image" height="620" src="http://www.canicrossusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/6.17-canicross-2-1000x620.jpg" width="1000"/>
          </div>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Canicross is a fantastic new sport that allows you and your dog to enjoy the rigors of a cross-country run. If you have never heard about this enjoyable sport, though, you may not have any idea about how to get started! Canicross involves attaching your dogs to a harness that goes around your waist, which can be an usual and somewhat intimidating setup for those not familiar with the sport. Today, we give you some
            <a href="http://www.k9trailtime.co.uk/information/canicross-training-notes" target="_blank">
             tips
            </a>
            about getting started in this exciting new activity.
            <a href="http://www.canicrossusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/6.17-canicross-1.jpg">
             <img alt="6.17 canicross 1" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-117" height="199" src="http://www.canicrossusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/6.17-canicross-1-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            First, you need to find the right equipment to suit your needs. You will need a waist harness and at least one lead line if you intend to run with one dog. Some people choose to have two dogs run ahead of them; it is best to wait until you have had some practice to try this. The best way to determine which equipment is right for you: Borrow someone elses and see what you think! Canicross clubs are springing up throughout the nation, and many participants are more than happy to provide you with a loaner harness until you get going in the sport.
           </p>
           <p>
            Next, it is useful to teach your dogs some specialized commands that will be needed during canicross. You can do this while you are simply out walking your dog. Try training for haw  turn left  and gee  turn right. Other useful commands include hike for going faster and whoah for slowing down.
           </p>
           <p>
            You need your dog to be able to recognize these commands at some distance ahead of you, so you should train with a relatively long leash. Do not tie on to your dog until she has at least a rudimentary understanding of these basic commands.
           </p>
           <p>
            With a few simple tricks of the trade, you can have your pup strapped in to a
            <a href="http://www.kurgostore.com/dog-harnesses/tru-fit-enhanced-strength-harness-3055-series/" target="_blank">
             dog harness
            </a>
            and ready to run in no time at all!
           </p>
          </div>
