              <h1 class="blog-title">
               <a href="http://twinsopinion.com/doj-targeting-legal-businesses-2/">
                DOJ Targeting Legal Businesses?
               </a>
              </h1>
              <div class="blog-info-wrapper gdl-item-border">
               <div class="blog-date">
                <span class="head">
                 Posted On
                </span>
                <a href="http://twinsopinion.com/2014/12/11/">
                 11 Dec 2014
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="blog-author">
                <span class="head">
                 By :
                </span>
                <a href="http://twinsopinion.com/author/alecceravolo/" rel="author" title="Posts by Alec Ceravolo">
                 Alec Ceravolo
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="blog-comment">
                <a href="http://twinsopinion.com/doj-targeting-legal-businesses-2/#respond">
                 Comment: 0
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="blog-tag">
                <span class="head">
                 Tag:
                </span>
                <a href="http://twinsopinion.com/tag/doj-emails-irs-lerner-holder-scandal-congress-obama-taxes/" rel="tag">
                 DOJ Emails IRS Lerner Holder Scandal Congress Obama Taxes
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="clear">
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="blog-content">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Emails Reveal Federal Officials Targeting Morally Objectionable Businesses
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                A report by Kelsey Harkness (These 7 Revealing Emails Show Federal Officials Scheming to Target Legal Businesses) in the Daily Signal alleges senior officials at the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation actively sought to crackdown on legal businesses that the Obama administration  or the officials themselves  deemed morally objectionable, a new congressional report finds. An investigative report by the House Oversight and Government Reform Committee reveals that the FDIC implemented Operation Choke Point, in conjunction with the Justice Department, to essentially attempt to shut down payday lenders and other businesses the administration was not fond of.
                <br/>
                <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/NJjBZ2WlwxY" width="420">
                </iframe>
                <br/>
                The emails showed scheming by regulatory officials to influence banks to essentially cut off financial credit to industries the regulators dont like. Operation Choke Point represents another abuse of power by the Justice Department and the FDIC by exercising moral latitude into the regulatory process. We have seen the Eric Holder led Justice Department selectively enforce our nations immigration laws and now we are seeing an attempt to enforce laws that dont exist on legal businesses.
               </p>
               <p>
                According to Harkness, The Obama administration contends that Operation Choke Point combats unlawful, mass marketing consumer fraud. However, an earlier report by the House Oversight Committee found that the Justice Department initiatives targets included legal businesses such as short term lenders, firearms and ammunition merchants, coin dealers, tobacco sellers and home-based charities.
               </p>
               <p>
                It never ceases to amaze us how the one entity responsible for seeing that our nations  laws are upheld is involved in controversy after controversy regarding their actual duties. The Justice Department is corrupt and has been since Holder took office. The message it sends to law abiding citizens and businesses is that none of us are safe from their wrath. Simply not liking the type of legal business one does is not part of our Constitutional protections as citizens. Failing to enforce our existing nations laws is also not part of our Constitutional protections. And damn sure Eric Holder indiscriminately deciding to affect our personal or business environments based on moral objection is not part of our Constitutional protections.
               </p>
