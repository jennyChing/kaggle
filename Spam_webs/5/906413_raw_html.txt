          <img alt="Car Loans" height="640" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7183/7067726727_e89feec8fd_z.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/76657755@N04/">
           Tax Credits/
          </a>
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/76657755@N04/7067726727/in/photolist-bLxWVn-9s9xuj-5VPkkK-eARede-gLi9z-8mqThK-7SzkWv-q3sGW-5sLsej-5sG4bM-ch8MXQ-5sG46Z-joxqe-gLi9v-gLi9y-gLmm3-gLi9x-dKva8V-y2xg4-8YmZQi-BxSon-ahzg3S-4CfVeZ-9Wty1h-5sLs57-7FSkkj-5sLs85-5RMCKR-4Wcfmd-PPxrE-5H6qh-p23CK">

        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Is your car no longer safe? Do you just want to upgrade your vehicle for a more luxurious, spacious and comfortable one? Whether its for repairs or for pleasure, a car loan is big and pricey decision that gets people thinking of all the different financing options available. Just like with mobile phones, the demand for new cars is huge and likeyou might expect, there are a number of ways to purchase a vehicle even if you dont have the money right now.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Buying a new car is one of the
         <a href="https://www.bidsbypros.com/portal/the-most-common-reasons-to-take-out-loans/" rel="nofollow">
          top reasons
         </a>
         why people take out loans. From personal loans to bank loans and handy online applicationsto help you compare and figure out if you can afford them, here are the top 3 car loan options in Canada:
        </p>
        <h2>
         1. Collateral Lending
        </h2>
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/ekonon/6233007553" title="2011.10.07 by Eric Konon, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Car Loans" class="" height="237" src="https://farm7.staticflickr.com/6042/6233007553_82c9f5a088.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/ekonon/">
           Eric Konon/
          </a>
          <a href="//www.flickr.com/photos/ekonon/6233007553/in/photolist-auMMZt-gvC8sx-6yFbD-bvu8e6-4uqegn-9RJd1A-asWf6G-ahQpmz-aeJVfn-73oDmL-9hkiFd-HV13Y-7CDS5-f6kTfg-5TgYxe-6NCBdR-9dn2y2-4yhvpi-BqbUa-NvEYi-d39UuY-bw9jvL-gXbH3z-65sd3g-7Pi77z-4kmrCK-7JzgeA-J6hbL-9p17xB-fQEhis-D91LX-5zDNgD--f6kTBK-9XZSVH-9XZUbn-bQfz1K-akE96P-c2QZHo-SRK4n-3w6xzf-6nZkB-e8nsDx-9dxh3W-eas1hU-5UAkcg-baGq4F-baGrax-4FGRJ5-6nZkF">
           Flickr
          </a>
          /Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic (
          <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/2.0/">
           CC BY-NC-SA 2.0
          </a>
          )
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Collateral lending is perfect for anyone who has some money already saved up, but needs some help to cover thefull price. If you happen to have personal assets of high value, you can use them as collateral and get the funding you need.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         As one of the most popular and acclaimed providers of loans against personal possessions, Zillidy offers great conditions for
         <a href="http://www.zillidy.com/personal-loans/">
          personal loans
         </a>
         . What assets can you use to secure the loan? Zillidy offers loans against the following items:
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          Jewelry
         </li>
         <li>
          Precious metals
         </li>
         <li>
          Watches
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.zillidy.com/what-is-asset-based-lending/">
           And more
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Collateral lending is a great option for those in a hurry, as the process is quick and can be done online. Furthermore, your personal assets are kept safe and you get them back once youve returned your loan in the exact same shape in which you sent them out.
        </p>
        <h2>
         2.
         <a href="https://www.cibc.com/ca/loans/car-loan.html">
          CIBC Personal Car Loans
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.zillidy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/CIBC1.jpg">
          <img alt="CIBC" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2888" height="188" src="http://www.zillidy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/CIBC1.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         CIBC is one of the leading financial institutions based in Canada. CIBC offers a wide arrange of loans including the Personal Car Loan. Worried about slow approval time? You can apply online to assure faster approval. The CIBC personal car loan provides really good flexibility with repayment  you get up to 8 years to repay the loan. You have the option to lower your monthly payments, so they fit the term of up to 96 months.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         You can borrow a minimum of $5000, which you have the option to pay through weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly or monthly payments to coincide with your pay periods. Furthermore, you can even skip up to two payments yearly, which lets you breathe easy, and not fear small financial bumps along the way. With CIBC, you can also pay all or part of your Personal Car Loan at any time, without having to endure penalties.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         When it comes down to taking out a loan, are you sure you can really afford it? In case you are wondering how taking out a loan can fit your lifestyle, CIBC offers this great tool called the CIBS Car Loan Calculator:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         #
         <a href="https://www.cibc.com/ca/loans/calculators/car-loan-calculator.html">
          The Car Loan Calculator
         </a>
         byCIBC
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         A huge part you need to consider when taking out a loan of any kind is if your lifestyle will allow you to repay it without having to struggle for money at any particular time during the repayment process.If you want to know how much you will be repaying monthly, the loan calculator applicationon the CIBCs website is a wonderful and easy-to-use tool to help you figure out the bitsand boltsof the loans they offer.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The information you are required to input in order to get the calculations is extremely basic, it asks for:
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          Age of the vehicle you want to purchase (so it works for both new and used cars)
         </li>
         <li>
          Province/territory of purchase
         </li>
         <li>
          Cost of the vehicle  you are required to input the total cost of the vehicle quoted by the dealer or manufacturer, make sure you dont include the taxes.
         </li>
         <li>
          Down payment  make sure it allows over $ 5 000 margin between the cost and the down payment as theres a minimum for CIBCs Car Loan ($ 5 000)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Also, you are going to have to complete the section called Loan Information, where you will be asked about the
         <a href="https://www.cibc.com/ca/rates/loans.html">
          interest rate
         </a>
         on the loan, the term in which you would like to pay off your loan (starting from 12 months) and the Cash and Dealer Incentives  a Cash Incentive being the variety of deals offered directly to the customers by the dealers, and the Dealer Incentives being the dealers cost to obtain the vehicle after all the different price reductions they may be offered.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Voila! The results are in: you get to see your monthly payments and budget according to the number you got, this way you can see if you can afford taking out the loan. The additional information makes you aware of the total interest on your loan, depending on the repayment period youve chosen  you can take up to 8 years and borrow 100% of the funds required to obtain the vehicle.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Want to make the final information even more precise? Theres an optional trade in field you can complete, where you can input the Vehicle Value and the Amount Owing (in case you are also using personal funds to contribute to the purchase). Furthermore, even though the loan calculator is designed to work with CIBCs Car Loan only,you still get the chance to compare it to other financing options  again you need to input the loan information you have in mind in the Compare Other Financing section, the graph on the side will reflect that information and compare CIBCs Car Loan to the other financing optionyou have chosen to help you make a final decision as to whether and what loan you can take.
        </p>
        <h2>
         3.
         <a href="https://www.tdcanadatrust.com/products-services/banking/personal-credit/autoloan.jsp">
          Auto Loan from TD Canada Trust
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.zillidy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/td-logo.jpg">
          <img alt="td-logo" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2885" height="73" src="http://www.zillidy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/td-logo.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         TD Bank Group is ranked one of the
         <a href="http://www.gfmag.com/awards-rankings/best-banks-and-financial-rankings/worlds-50-safest-banks-2013#axzz2q0f2nV6B">
          safest banks in 2013
         </a>
         by to Global Finance. TD Canada Trust provides banking, insurance and loaning services. One of their most popular loans is the Auto Loan that help youget the vehicle thats right for you.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The great thing about TD Canada Trusts Auto Loan is that you get to choose the type of interest rate on the money you take  fixed or variable. The Fixed Interest Rate protects you from changes and provides structured and set payments, while the Variable Interest Rate allows you to benefit whenever the rate is lower, which in turn helps you pay off your loan quicker.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The Auto Loan also allows you to choose from a variety of repayment options that fit your budget for extra comfort. Furthermore, with the Auto Payment you can defer up to 2 payments a year in case you are experiencing financial difficulties.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Which loan would you use to buy a car? Let us know why in the comment section below.
        </p>
