       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         Freefall: Conceptual Photography by Lee Howell
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8">
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <p>
           Falling with style is how
           <strong>
            conceptual photographer
            <a href="http://www.leehowellphotography.com/" target="_blank">
             Lee Howell
            </a>
           </strong>
           describes his project
           <em>
            <a href="https://www.behance.net/gallery/Freefall/14551185" target="_blank">
             Freefall
            </a>
           </em>
           . The first six images released in this series show fashionably-dressed people in urban settings, tripping down stairs, slipping from rooftops, and in other situations that are simultaneously awkward and beautiful. The photos are processed in a vivid, HDR style which enhances the sense of hyper-reality the photographer has captured. Howell, from Edinburgh, UK, specializes in creative advertising and contemporary editorial portraiture.
          </p>
