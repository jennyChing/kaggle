       <p>
        Its no secret by now  we LOVE
        <a href="http://www.dariabishop.com/" target="_blank" title="Daria Bishop Photography">
         Daria and Andy Bishops photography
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        We featured Amanda &amp; Jeffs gorgeous Shelburne Farms wedding just a couple of days ago along with some great reasons on the decision to have a first look. If you havent already seen it, take a
        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2009/01/06/wedding-photography-the-first-look-from-daria-bishop/" target="_blank" title="Amanda and Jeff's &quot;First Look&quot;">
         peek
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        And back in February 08 Abby featured another of Darias weddings  one that a loyal Style Me Pretty reader, Robyn, sent in. Robyn &amp; Keiths wedding took place at
        <a href="http://www.theroundbarn.com/" target="_blank" title="The Round Barn Farm">
         The Round Barn Farm
        </a>
        in Waitsfield, Vermont. Robyn said that she had always considered herself fairly creative, but it wasnt until planning her own wedding that she realized just how much she could do, herself!
        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2008/02/25/a-real-wedding-2/" target="_blank" title="Robyn &amp; Keith's Round Barn Wedding In Waitsfield">
         Heres
        </a>
        the first of the posts from that day. For lots of DIY ideas, be sure and read
        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2008/02/25/a-real-wedding-ii/" target="_blank" title="Vermont Wedding At The Round Barn In Vermont">
         Part II
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2008/02/25/a-real-wedding-iii/" target="_blank" title="Round Barn Wedding From Daria Bishop Photography">
         Part III
        </a>
        . By the way, the color palette was rich! Apple green (chartreuse), deep purples, browns, and ivories. Rich.
       </p>
       <p>
        So moving on to todays featured wedding  when we saw Brandi and Martys Adirondack wedding on Darias blog, our list of photo requests was a mile long! (Andy actually said we were like kids in a candy store ) We have lots of delightful shots from this wedding, and there are tons more on
        <a href="http://www.dariabishop.com/2008/10/brandi-martys-adirondack-wedding.html" target="_blank" title="Brandi and Marty's Adirondack Wedding">
         Darias blog
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        Doesnt Brandi look like shes just about to burst with excitement?
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Wedding Anticipation Brandi Bridesmaids Trolley Ride" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/brandy-marty-5.jpg" title="Lake Placid Wedding Anticipation From Daria Bishop" width="601"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Such a doll! Ill bet this little girl feels pretty important right about now.
       </p>
       <p>
        Brandi and Marty were married at St. Agnes, an incredible Roman Catholic Church in Lake Placid, NY.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Daria Bishop Photography St. Agnes Catholic Church Lake Placid" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/brandi-marty-1.jpg" title="Brandi and Marty's Adirondack Wedding" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The photo of Brandi (above) won 2nd place in the Bride Getting Ready category of the Wedding Photojournalist Associations quarterly contest! Way to go Daria!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Daria Bishop Sunshine Yellow Color Palette" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/brandi-marty-2rev.jpg" title="Sunshine Yellow Color Palette For Spectacular Lake Placid Wedding" width="603"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        I think
        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/about/all-about-abby/" target="_blank" title="All About Abby">
         Abby
        </a>
        and I were having one of our same color karma days today! Her featured wedding (
        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2009/01/08/real-wedding-julia-and-brian/" target="_blank" title="Julia &amp; Brian's Wedding Feature on Style Me Pretty">
         Julia &amp; Brian
        </a>
        ) also showcases a yummy yellow color palette. Sunshine, joy, happiness  a perfect wedding color!
       </p>
       <p>
        Now, a word about babys breath. Tons of people say they dont care for it  but when its used like this  mmm  gorgeous.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Daria Bishop Floral Details Baby's Breath Yellow" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/brandy-marty-3.jpg" title="Baby's Breath En Masse For Weddings Is &quot;IN&quot;" width="602"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        What Daria and Andy can do with color . !!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Daria Bishop Colors Bouquet " src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/brandy-marty-4.jpg" title="Wedding Details and Fabulous Colors From Daria Bishop Photography" width="602"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        If youve been following our Little Black Book Blog for long, you know I love a great ring shot  (and precious mini-maids).
        <br class="spacer_"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Daria Bishop Ring Shot Antler Chandelier" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/brandy-marty-9.jpg" title="Daria Bishop Photography" width="603"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The reception was held at the Lake Placid Club Golf House
        <em>
         (said to have the most beautiful view of the High Peaks in the Adirondacks, with an open-beamed ceiling, a massive stone fireplace &amp; magnificent antler chandeliers)
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        So, what else can we say? Brandi and Marty, you guys had an awesome wedding. And
        <a href="http://www.dariabishop.com/" target="_blank" title="Daria Bishop Photography">
         Daria and Andy
        </a>
        , you guys rock!
       </p>
