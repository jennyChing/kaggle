               <em>
                Doctor Who
               </em>
               , while the Weeping Angels were arguably the best brainchild of Steven Moffat. Both creatures have been terrifying the audience every since they first appeared on the screen, and have been a part of some of the most memorable
               <em>
                Doctor Who
               </em>
               episodes in the history of the show.
              </p>
              <p>
               Understandably, these two creatures have become the favorite enemies among the fanbase for The Doctor to face off against. Both have their strengths and weaknesses. In the case of the Daleks, those weaknesses have decreased, as they eventually adaptedtoovercometheir initial inability to use stairs.A persistent weakness forthe Daleks is their extreme hatred and xenophobia, which hinders their efforts to take over the universe. For the Weeping Angels, as long as they are being watched, they are rendered immobile. And there is that whole thing the Daleks have about looking like pepper shakers. Well, style isnt everything, I suppose.
