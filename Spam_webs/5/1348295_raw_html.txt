               <h1>
                ANTONIO MUIZ
               </h1>
               <p>
                Antonio Muiz is an abstract surrealist artist from Los Angeles who uses the
                <i>
                 fumage
                </i>
                technique. His primary mediums are oil on canvas, gouache and color pencil on paper.
               </p>
               <p>
                Originally from Chihuahua Mexico, Muiz is a self-taught painter. His training as an architect at the New School of Architecture in San Diego as well as mentoring by the renowned Mexican painter
                <a href="http://www.rodrigopimentel.com" onclick="recordOutboundLink(this, 'Text Link');return false;" target="_new">
                 Rodrigo Pimentel
                </a>
                informed his talents. Muiz has exhibited in his native Mexico as well as California.
               </p>
               <p>
                Check out the
                <a href="news.htm" target="_self" title="News Section">
                 news section
                </a>
                for updates on events and exhibits.
               </p>
               <h1>
                FUMAGE
               </h1>
               <p>
                Fumage is a painting technique invented by
                <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wolfgang_Paalen" onclick="recordOutboundLink(this, 'Text Link');return false;" target="_new">
                 Wolfgang Paalen
                </a>
                that uses the smoke from candles  to leave surealist impressions on the canvas.
               </p>
