            <div class="section post-body">
             <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" style="">
              Being able to market your product/service is crucial to any business success. Problem is, in todays day and age, no one
              <em>
               wants
              </em>
              to be marketed to. People have become immune to the standard advertisements in all the normal places. From fast forwarding commercials on our new nifty DVRs, to chucking junk mail right in the trash can, people dont have the time to be bothered with all these pesky advertisements.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              So how the heck are we supposed to get our product/service out there to our potential customers? Enter marketings Avenger-like superhero team:
              <em>
               <strong>
                Inbound Marketing
               </strong>
              </em>
              . Let'stake a step back for a second, andask a question that
              <em>
               most
              </em>
              readers will answer the same way. If you have a problem youre looking to solve, where is the first place you turn? Most people will turn to the internet and Google (how awesome is that when your companys name becomes a verb?) their question.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              From things like How to fix a leaky faucet to Best places to eat in Tampa Google helps users find the answers to their questions, and helps them find it fast! (because none of us have any time to waste, right?) There are 3.5 billion Google searches a day that number is staggering!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              So how can this marketing superhero team help your company? Im glad youve asked!
              <br/>
              <!--more-->
              <h2 style="text-transform: initial;">
               Blogging is Inbounds
               <strong>
                IRON
               </strong>
               clad Solution
              </h2>
              <p>
               <img alt="ironman" data-constrained="true" src="http://www.bluleadz.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/blog/ironman.png?t=1435167466445&amp;width=263" style="margin: 0px 0px 10px 10px; float: right;" width="263"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               Iron Man is arguably the most important piece of the Avengers team. That's why its only fair to relate him to the most important part of
               <a href="/inbound-marketing/our-method" target="_blank">
                Inbound marketing
               </a>
               :
               <em>
                Blogging
               </em>
               . Iron Man is an inventor who'sconstantly coming up with new ideas that could help save the world! Start coming up with new ideas and solutions to your persona's pain points and
               <strong>
                START BLOGGING
               </strong>
               about them.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               By beginning to blog on a more regular basis and get new ideas out there for your target audience to
               <em>
                find
               </em>
               themselves, you will start building their trust. When youre writing these blog articles, keep in mind:
              </p>
              <ol>
               <li>
                Whos going to be reading this?
               </li>
               <li>
                Will the audience find this information valuable?
               </li>
               <li>
                Be helpful, write just enough, not more. (No filler)
               </li>
              </ol>
              <h2 style="text-transform: initial;">
               Creating Premium Content: Be Known As
               <strong>
                CAPTAIN
               </strong>
               Content!
              </h2>
              <p>
               <img alt="Captain-America-Shield-04" data-constrained="true" src="http://www.bluleadz.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/blog/Captain-America-Shield-04.png?t=1435167466445&amp;width=320" style="margin: 0px 10px 10px 0px; float: left;" width="320"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               Captain America always does things for the
               <em>
                greater good
               </em>
               . This is where creating premium content comes into the Inbound equation. Start creating premium content (eBooks, tip sheets, white papers) that will help walk your persona through a common problem. Do something for the
               <em>
                greater good
               </em>
               and dont be afraid to give away your secret sauce.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               Being able to help your audience solve a specific problem will make you a thought leader in their eyes. They will be extremely likely to come back to the aforementioned thought leader that helped them in the past if they run across a problem they are unable to solve themselves.
              </p>
              <h2 style="text-transform: initial;">
               <img alt="The_Hulk" data-constrained="true" src="http://www.bluleadz.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/blog/The_Hulk.gif?t=1435167466445&amp;width=190" style="margin: 0px 0px 10px 10px; float: right;" width="190"/>
               Start
               <strong>
                GROWING
               </strong>
               Your Social Following!
              </h2>
              <p>
               Everyone knows what happens when The Hulk gets angry He Grows! Start
               <strong>
                GROWING
               </strong>
               your social following! Be more active in social media and start communicating with your target audience. Be there to answer their questions and help them with anything they may need! Its all about being there for them when
               <em>
                <strong>
                 they need you
                </strong>
               </em>
               . You can saturate your social channels with valuable information such as blog posts or premium content that your audience can use.(HULK SMASH!)
              </p>
              <h2 style="text-transform: initial;">
               Dont Let Search Engines Be A
               <strong>
                THOR
               </strong>
               n In Your Side
              </h2>
              <p>
               <img alt="Thor_Hammer" data-constrained="true" src="http://www.bluleadz.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/blog/Thor_Hammer.png?t=1435167466445&amp;width=186" style="margin: 0px 10px 10px 0px; float: left;" width="186"/>
               Businesses of any size seem to be afraid of ranking on search engines. Thor is the only one of the Avengers that is not only a superhero, but also a God! Everyone is always worried about not being able to lift the proverbial hammer and rank on (the godly) Google but youre in luck!
