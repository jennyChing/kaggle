     <h1>
      Creative Spring Backyard Wedding Ideas
     </h1>
     <div class="entry">
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <p>
       Celebrating your wedding in the backyard is a romantic way to enjoy one of the most memorable days of your life. A Spring backyard wedding can end up saving you thousands if not tens of thousands on a venue. Besides, home is where the heart is!
      </p>
      <p>
       Be resourceful when choosing the setting for your outdoor wedding. If you need a little more space, see if you know somebody who has a large backyard that wouldnt mind taking the honor of hosting your backyard wedding. Spring is the perfect way to mark the new beginning of a new chapter in your life!
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 550px">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/spring-backyard-wedding-2014.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/spring-backyard-wedding-2014.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Spring Backyard Wedding 2014" class=" " height="359" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/spring-backyard-wedding-2014.jpg" title="Spring Backyard Wedding 2014" width="540"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        <em>
         Images via Pinterest
        </em>
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       <span id="more-6681">
       </span>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Wedding Ideas for Spring Outside
      </h2>
      <p>
       There are many different ways that you can plan your backyard wedding. The size of your backyard may limit you on the number of invites you are able to send out but hey, that can be a good thing! A small backyard wedding can only lead to a stronger bond between those enjoying this shared experience. Time for everyone to get cozy and become one big happy family!
      </p>
      <p>
       Spring is the season where flowers (and love) begins to bloom. Not only is the weather great, you can also enjoy a honeymoon season afterwards that is just as bright during the Summer months. This is just a glimpse of what could be. Tailor your backyard wedding to your preferences and let the scenery cater to you. Here are some of my favorite Spring Backyard Wedding Ideas for 2014!
      </p>
      <h2>
       8 Spring Backyard Wedding Ideas for 2014
      </h2>
      <h3>
       1. Hanging Light Canopy
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/lighting-canopy-backyard-wedding.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/lighting-canopy-backyard-wedding.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Lighting Canopy Backyard Wedding" class="aligncenter" height="576" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/lighting-canopy-backyard-wedding.jpg" title="Lighting Canopy Backyard Wedding" width="383"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       This barnyard hanging canopy illuminates the scene, creating the fairy tale backyard wedding. Trust me, your bridesmaids will be jealous (in a good way)!
      </p>
      <h3>
       2. Old World Carnival
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/old-world-carnival-backyard-wedding-theme.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/old-world-carnival-backyard-wedding-theme.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Old World Carnival Backyard Wedding Theme" class="aligncenter" height="389" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/old-world-carnival-backyard-wedding-theme.jpg" title="Old World Carnival Backyard Wedding Theme" width="585"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Get in the swing of things during your backyard wedding reception. An old world carnival theme will set a lively tone filled with nothing but fun, delight, and an air filled with laughter. All of your friends will remember this one of a kind idea and reflect on all the good times you had. Who ever thought a backyard wedding could be so much fun! Dont forget to designate an area for the first dance!
      </p>
      <h3>
       3. Floating Mason Jar Candles
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/mason-jar-outdoor-wedding.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/mason-jar-outdoor-wedding.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Mason Jar Outdoor Wedding" class="aligncenter" height="383" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/mason-jar-outdoor-wedding.jpg" title="Mason Jar Outdoor Wedding" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       This is a cute spring backyard wedding idea that is low budget and charming. Simply string mason jars to a tree or along the eaves along your patio and place small candles inside.
      </p>
      <h3>
       4. Take a leap of Faith
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/parachute-canopy-outdoor-wedding.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/parachute-canopy-outdoor-wedding.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Parachute Canopy for Outdoor Wedding" class="aligncenter" height="560" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/parachute-canopy-outdoor-wedding.jpg" title="Parachute Canopy for Outdoor Wedding" width="373"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       This couple rented a parachute for only $35. The effect that it has on the ceiling is amazing and youd never guess what it was. Brilliant!
      </p>
      <h3>
       5. Light up the Night
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/backyard-wedding-lighting-ideas.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/backyard-wedding-lighting-ideas.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Backyard Wedding Lighting Ideas" class="aligncenter" height="626" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/backyard-wedding-lighting-ideas.jpg" title="Backyard Wedding Lighting Ideas" width="418"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Light up the night with smiles, laughter, and outdoor lighting! Check out these
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/blog/home-decor/6-enchanting-outdoor-lighting-ideas/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/blog/home-decor/6-enchanting-outdoor-lighting-ideas/', '9 Enchanting Outdoor Lighting Ideas']);">
        9 Enchanting Outdoor Lighting Ideas
       </a>
       . You can use Christmas lights or stringed lighting to hang around trees and in shrubs. This will provide ample lighting to make your backyard wedding more enjoyable and decrease the safety concern for grandma and grandpa and those who have limited eye sight in dimly lit areas.
      </p>
      <h3>
       6. Tree Stump Stands
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/tree-stump-backyard-wedding-ideas.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/tree-stump-backyard-wedding-ideas.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Tree Stump Backyard Wedding Ideas" class="aligncenter" height="573" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/tree-stump-backyard-wedding-ideas.jpg" title="Tree Stump Backyard Wedding Ideas" width="383"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Line the aisle with tree stumps and a petite floral arrangement to emphasize the natural ambiance that an outdoor wedding provides. Schedule the ceremony to commence an hour prior to sunset so that you can say your final I do when the sun is setting over your backyard wedding! A picture perfect moment. Later in the night, jubilance will ignite as you and your closest friends celebrate a night to remember!
      </p>
      <h3>
       7. Pew and Aisle
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/pews-backyard-spring-wedding-ideas.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/pews-backyard-spring-wedding-ideas.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Pews and Aisles Backyard Spring Wedding Ideas" class="aligncenter" height="405" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/pews-backyard-spring-wedding-ideas.jpg" title="Pews and Aisles Backyard Spring Wedding Ideas" width="576"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Invite your church family to enjoy the coming together of two souls. You can rent pews for a traditional feel. The bride will walk down the aisle and enjoy her shining moment as the melodic Here comes the bride plays in the background.
      </p>
      <h3>
       8. Chair Backs
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/mr-and-mrs-wedding-chairs.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://www.patioproductions.com/images/mr-and-mrs-wedding-chairs.jpg', '']);">
        <img alt="Mr. and Mrs. Wedding Chairs" class="aligncenter" height="599" src="https://www.patioproductions.com/images/mr-and-mrs-wedding-chairs.jpg" title="Mr. and Mrs. Wedding Chairs" width="360"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Newlyweds can enjoy a playful seatback arrangement that designates a spot for the bride and groom at the head of the table. The whimsical Mr. &amp; Mrs. chair backs are a fun way to add a giggle or two to your spring backyard wedding. Have the time of your life and your friends wont even know you were planning on a budget! You can use craft paper or any durable canvas of your choice.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Luxury Outdoor Seating
      </h3>
      <p>
       Need a little extra seating? Make sure that you have a spot set out for each of your special guests. Visit

      </p>
