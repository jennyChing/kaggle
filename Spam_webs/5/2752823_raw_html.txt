                  <p>
                   Remember that clown riding a unicycle, at that old circus? Ever thought you can do that now?... Well today you can!
                   <br/>
                   <strong>
                    <br/>
                    EcoBoomer
                    <em>
                     iGO
                    </em>
                   </strong>
                   , A
                   <em>
                    self-balancing electric unicycle,
                   </em>
                   is the first of its kindthat let's you ride a unicycle, without being skilled or balanced enough to ride one.
                   <strong>
                    EcoBoomer
                   </strong>
                   <strong>
                    <em>
                     iGO
                    </em>
                   </strong>
                   does all the work for you.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    EcoBoomer
                   </strong>
                   <strong>
                    <em>
                     iGo
                    </em>
                   </strong>
                   got the look of a modern Italian design with classic color options, and comfort, all in one. It is as fast as 20KM per Hour. It's lithium ion battery included with the
                   <strong>
                    EcoBoomer
                    <em>
                     iGO
                    </em>
                   </strong>
                   charges up in no time, and let you have a ride within a range of 1.6KM.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <img alt="EcoBoomer-iGO" height="263" src="/images/easyblog_images/265/b2ap3_thumbnail_EcoBoomer-iGO-Front--Rear.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="b2ap3_thumbnail_EcoBoomer-iGO-Front--Rear.jpg" width="316"/>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   This personal electric human transporter has a quiet motor for ease of use in the city, suburban, or rural areas. With zero emissions and an MPG rating over 400, you can contribute to saving the environment, and save those time-wasting trips to the gas station!
                  </p>
