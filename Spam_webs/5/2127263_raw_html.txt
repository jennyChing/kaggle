        <p>
         You know the best way to send better email newsletters?
        </p>
        <p>
         Subscribe to and read
         <em>
          lots
         </em>
         of them.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are 50 great ones to keep tabs on. These may not reach the widest audience
         <strong>
          but they resonate with the right audience
         </strong>
         . Some are niche, some are general  but all of them go above and beyond to deliver a great experience. Theyve earned to right to be opened based on the sender, not the subject line. (If your emails are good enough over time, people will open them regardless of what the subject says.)
        </p>
        <p>
         While the content may not be relevant to you, look for ideas on how to better communicate with your own subscribers. Some newsletters, like Help Scouts, contain a single link while other have more than 50. Some are beautifully designed while others have a community bulletin feel to them. There is no right way to do it, as long as it engages the reader.
        </p>
        <p>
         I think youll really enjoy these newsletters. There are obviously more than 50 great ones out there so please let us know what we missed in the comments.
        </p>
        <p>

          <strong>
           Editors note:
          </strong>

         </em>

         <div class=" small-image" style="width: 300px; float: right; padding: 20px;300px">
          <img alt="Nieman Journalism Lab" height="300" src="http://blog.getvero.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/nieman-lab-300.png" width="300"/>
         </div>
         <p style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold;">
          44. Nieman Journalism Lab
         </p>
         <p>
          The Nieman Journalism Lab is an attempt to help journalism figure out its future in an Internet age. The newsletter embodies that mission. As journalism and content marketing overlap more and more, this newsletter is becoming more important for marketers to pay attention to.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.niemanlab.org/subscribe/" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="width: 100%; min-height: 325px; background-color: #e4eef2; padding: 20px;">
         <div class=" small-image" style="width: 300px; float: right; padding: 20px;300px">
          <img alt="FontShop" height="300" src="http://blog.getvero.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/fontshop-300.png" width="300"/>
         </div>
         <p style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold;">
          45. FontShop
         </p>
         <p>
          Anything and everything you ever wanted to know about typography can be found here. The newsletter is not only interesting, its also beautiful. As a bonus, a subscription also earns you free downloadable fonts.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fontshop.com/blog/newsletters/aug2014a/" target="_blank">
           Sample
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fontshop.com/blog/newsletters/" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>

         <div class=" small-image" style="width: 300px; float: right; padding: 20px;300px">
          <img alt="Hacker Newsletter" height="300" src="http://blog.getvero.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/hackernews-newsletter.png" width="300"/>
         </div>
         <p style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold;">
          46. Hacker Newsletter
         </p>
         <p>
          Kale Davis has been curating the best stories from Hacker News for four years. More than 27,000 subscribers get his weekly update which regularly includes 40 or more articles.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://us1.campaign-archive2.com/?u=faa8eb4ef3a111cef92c4f3d4&amp;id=340345c30b" target="_blank">
           Sample
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.hackernewsletter.com/" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>
         </p>

        </p>

         <div class=" small-image" style="width: 300px; float: right; padding: 20px;300px">
          <img alt="Seth Godin" height="300" src="http://blog.getvero.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/seth-godin.png" width="300"/>
         </div>
         <p style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold;">
          47. Seth Godin
         </p>
         <p>
          Hes so popular in the marketing world that many of his foundational principles have become cliche. Still, his daily posts always inspire, and his consistency  hes been blogging since 2002  is admirable in and of itself. Even though his newsletter is simply an RSS feed of his posts, its worth collecting his genius in your inbox.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://sethgodin.typepad.com/" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>

          <img alt="James Maher Photography" height="300" src="http://blog.getvero.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/james-maher.png" width="300"/>
         </div>
         <p style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold;">
          48. James Maher Photography
         </p>
         <p>
          If you love street photography, you will really enjoy James monthly newsletter. It includes photos, the stories behind them and posts about photography.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.jamesmaherphotography.com/join_mailing_list" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>
         </p>

        </p>

          <img alt="Draft" height="300" src="http://blog.getvero.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/draft-300.png" width="300"/>

          49. Draft
         </p>
         <p>
          Nathan Kontnys emails focus on product updates. His app Draft is a popular markdown editor and his emails detail product updates, often using GIFs. Hes a one-man show so his emails chronicle his personal journey as well as Drafts.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ninjasandrobots.com/draft-announcements-a-chrome-app-inline-calculator-and-more" target="_blank">
           Sample
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://draftin.com/draft/users/sign_up" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>

          <a href="http://link.greatist.com/view/53fa1fae8561e09f498b4fc21rqot.uti/dc4749fa" target="_blank">
           Sample
          </a>

          <a href="http://greatist.com/newsletter" target="_blank">
           Subscribe
          </a>
