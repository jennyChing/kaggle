        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The Social Dangers of Political Correctness
        </h1>
        <div class="article-header">
         <div class="article-meta no-author">
          <div class="meta-date updated">

        <p>
         <b>
          THE SOCIAL DANGERS OFAPOLITICALCORRECTNESS
         </b>
          Have you ever met a liberal who is willing to admit their mistakes or even to consider another point of view that somehow opposes or even dispels their ideology of political correctness?
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre not a thoroughly indoctrinated liberal then you will provide an honest answer, that being no, but of course youll never get a true liberal to admit anything, even when the obvious stares them right in the face.
        </p>
        <p>
         Watch the video for an insight as to what its like debating with a liberal progressive:
        </p>
        <div class="video-container">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="615" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/xK4cjFPvDWo?rel=0" width="820">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         If you watched the video, and you should have, youll see that theres no debating with a liberal, they simply cannot stay on topic and each time a fact is presented theyll deny its existence and pursue some morally ambivalent higher ground in an attempt to challenge the subject.
        </p>
        <p>
         Basically, if what you are saying doesnt fit in to their approved propaganda then youll be labelled a racist or a bigot probably both.
        </p>
        <p>
         So why are the liberal progressives so frightened to debate in an open and honest way? Is it really because they fear to declare that their ideology and propaganda is driving communities apart and effectively removing our right to speak freely?
        </p>
        <p>
         We talk about freedom as a fundamental right but liberals have another agenda of political correctness that is both insidious and dishonest; in fact I would go as far as declaring that the liberal ideology that successfully indoctrinates the masses is nothing short of pure evil and a clear and present threat to the freedom of speech as is communism and fascism.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are supposed to live in an era of intellectual reasoning but there is no reasoning with a liberal, especially those which now prefer to call themselves progressives.
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course the ideology of being progressive is also fundamentally flawed and dishonest when peddled by a liberal for to be progressive means to move forward in a positive way by taking a path that is both moral and useful to all that it affects.
        </p>
        <p>
         Progressive politics however doesnt take this into consideration for one-size doesnt fit all philosophy is perceived as the greater good.
        </p>
        <p>
         The very reason we have a democracy is that such a philosophy is fundamentally flawed for whilst it might not address everyones needs it does at least attempt to address the majority.
        </p>
        <p>
         Progressive liberals however dont care if the masses find a particular issue offensive, morally detached or even outright subversive for they will continue to push the mantra that people will get use to the one-size fits all whether they like it or not.
        </p>
        <p>
         There is nothing progressive about the liberal movement and it has become a plague, a cancer the western world has been subjected to and its about time we realised that you cant put all of humanity in a box and declare them all to be the same nor allow minority groups to take over, often through the discourse of violence, in order to attempt to create some ill conceived utopia.
        </p>
        <p>
         A liberal is only interested in two things, one being silence and the other being compliance and in modern society their ability to silence the masses has been disturbingly successful but there still remains a segment that refuses to accept the propaganda and will speak openly in order to remove the veil of deceit and declare that the liberal utopia does not exist, it is morally detached and it will continue to oppress the opinions of others as long as its permitted to do so.
        </p>
