       <h2 class="post">
        <a href="http://lightingnews.com.au/2482/stack-unveils-smart-light-bulb-that-adjusts-itself/">
         Stack unveils smart light bulb that adjusts itself
        </a>
       </h2>
       <p class="meta">
        November 25, 2014 
        <a href="http://lightingnews.com.au/category/news/" rel="category tag">
         News
        </a>
       </p>
       <div style="clear: both;">
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Smart lighting startup
         <a href="https://stacklighting.com/" target="_blank">
          Stack
         </a>
         has unveiled its wirelessly connected, energy-efficient Alba light bulb capable of adjusting itself, much like the backlight of your computer screen.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2483" style="width: 310px">
        <a href="http://lightingnews.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Untitled.png" rel="prettyPhoto">
         <img alt="Image credit: stacklighting.com" class="size-medium wp-image-2483" height="255" src="http://lightingnews.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Untitled-300x255.png" width="300"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Image credit: stacklighting.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The Alba bulb features sensors capable of measuring the amount of sunlight in the room, which allows it to brighten, dim or shut off completely, depending on the brightness from the sun.
       </p>
       <p>
        According to the
        <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/gadgets/smart-light-bulb-designed-ex-tesla-engineer-adjusts-itself.html" target="_blank">
         article
        </a>
        on Tree Hugger, it is also equipped with sensors which can pick up movement (or a lack of movement) in the room and turn the bulbs off if a room is empty or on if someone walks in.
       </p>
       <p>
        The bulb has a corresponding app, which can be used to select preferences once the user has installed the bulb and connected the hub to his/hers wireless router.
       </p>
       <p>
        This smart light bulb detects the presence of a pet and doesnt flip on the lights every time it runs through a room and allows the user to select spots in his/hers where they want certain types of lighting.
       </p>
       <p>
        Ex-Tesla CEO and founder of Stack Neil Joseph said the Alba presents a 60 to 80% savings compared to regular LED bulbs and for a 1,500 square foot home using four hours of lighting per day, it will save $670 to $710 per year compared to incandescent bulbs.
       </p>
       <p>
        The starter kit of two bulbs and the hub can be pre-ordered for $150, shipping in early 2015, and additional bulbs will go for $60.
       </p>
       <p>
        The company plans on introducing other bulb formats over the next year.
