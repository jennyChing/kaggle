         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Blocks of Fun! 44 Block Activities for Preschoolers
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p class="entry-filing">
          <span class="entry-categories">
           <a href="http://handsonaswegrow.com/kids-activities/" rel="category tag">

         </p>
         <p>
          I put together this list of lots of
          <a href="http://handsonaswegrow.com/blocks/">
           block activities
          </a>
          for preschoolers and even toddlers using common blocks that are found in most homes  wooden blocks, Legos, and ABC blocks.
         </p>
         <p>
          We tend to have
          <a href="http://handsonaswegrow.com/blocks/">
           blocks
          </a>
          out a lot around here.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whether its
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0038BDKNW/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=thereimhous-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0038BDKNW" target="_blank">
           legos
          </a>
          , or
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004SGUQDI/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=thereimhous-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004SGUQDI" target="_blank">
           wooden blocks
          </a>
          , or our
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0006GUY18/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=thereimhous-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0006GUY18" target="_blank">
           ABC blocks
          </a>
          (these are all affiliate links, thank you for your support!), we include them in a lot of the activities that we do.
         </p>
         <p>
          Blocks are kind of a staple in most households, am I right? Boys and girls both like blocks. Theyre a lot of fun!
         </p>
         <p>
          Turns out, kids can learn a lot from these simple toys. But, what exactly?
         </p>
         <p>
          Childhood 101 goes into some specifics of adding
          <a href="http://childhood101.com/2011/03/literacy-spot-12-literacy-in-the-block-corner/" target="_blank">
           literacy to the block corner
          </a>
          , and Picklebums gives us a little insight on
          <a href="http://picklebums.com/2011/06/07/block-play-is-important-work/" target="_blank">
           why block play is so important to kids
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          And Ive collected some wonderful ways to play with blocks, so we can maximize our use of these awesomely, simple toys (arent the simple toys the best because they can be used the most ways?).
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Lots of block activities for kids to do" height="650" src="http://handsonaswegrow.com/wp-content/uploads/Blocks1-433x650.jpg" width="433"/>
         </p>
         <h2>

         </h2>
