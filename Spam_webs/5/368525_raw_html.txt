            <h2>
             Dont miss Jewelry Designer Gaia Repossi
            </h2>
            <p>
             Stunning and emerging young jewelry designer,
             <em>
              Gaia Repossi
             </em>
             , seems to have jewelry flowing in her blood. And if
             <em>
              Cate Blanchette
             </em>
             chose her ring for her award winning night, you too should pay attention.
            </p>
            <p>
             First, have a deep look into this poster from Repossis Fall 2014 collection. Notice the playful lines, the smooth design, and oh, those eyes This is some of her recent beautiful jewelry.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Jewelry-Designer-Fall-2014.jpg">
              <img alt="Gaia Repossi Jewelry Designer Fall 2014" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3344" height="1024" src="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Jewelry-Designer-Fall-2014-791x1024.jpg" width="791"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <h2>
             Cate Blanchette featuring a Gaia Repossi Ring
            </h2>
            <p>
             Not that she needs affirmation from anyone but its still nice to receive it. The beautiful actress Cate Blanchette wore a beautifully matching ring to her winning night at the Annual Critics Choice Movie Award. You probably guessed that the ring was designed by Gaia Repossi.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Cate-Blanchett-with-a-Gaia-Repossi-Ring-at-Annual-Critics-Choice-Movie-Award.jpg">
              <img alt="Cate Blanchett with a Gaia Repossi Ring at Annual Critics Choice Movie Award" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3341" height="387" src="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Cate-Blanchett-with-a-Gaia-Repossi-Ring-at-Annual-Critics-Choice-Movie-Award.jpg" width="620"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <h2>
             Meet Italian Jewelry Designer Gaia Repossi
            </h2>
            <p>
             Born in Italy, in 1986, Gaia Repossi is a second generation to a family of jewelry designers. Her father initiated the Repossi brand and led it with the design of inspirational jewelry pieces. And yet she did not suffice with genes only and pursued her studies at the Beaux Arts of Paris. Then, at the very early age of 21, in 2007, Gaia took the position of Creative and Artistic Director of the Repossi Jewelry House. Ever since, she has been surprising the fashion world with bold jewelry collections which attracted attention and applause from artists, celebrities and jewelristas.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Jewelry-Designer.jpg">
              <img alt="Gaia Repossi Jewelry Designer" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3346" height="517" src="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Jewelry-Designer.jpg" width="400"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <h3>
             Repossi Designer Jewelry Collections
             <br/>
             <h3>
              Among Gaia Repossis jewelry collections, here are two examples showing her unique stamp  the
              <em>
               Art Nouveau
              </em>
              Collection and the recent
              <em>
               White Noise
              </em>
              Collection. Both jewelry collections are creative, happy and nonetheless stylish and full of statement.
             </h3>
            </h3>
            <p>
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Art-Nouveau-Designer-Jewelry-Collection.jpg">
              <img alt="Gaia Repossi Art Nouveau Designer Jewelry Collection" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3342" height="400" src="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Art-Nouveau-Designer-Jewelry-Collection.jpg" width="600"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-White-Noise-Designer-Jewelry-Collection.jpg">
              <img alt="Gaia Repossi White Noise Designer Jewelry Collection" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3347" height="490" src="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-White-Noise-Designer-Jewelry-Collection.jpg" width="916"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Some of Gaia Repossis creative work can be found in many boutiques around the world and online. She is also often featured in fashion publications, including this recent production for the Pop Magazine in black and white. Warning: some of the photos in in her own
             <a href="http://www.repossi.com/" target="_blank" title="Repossi Website">
              website
             </a>
             are not suitable for work, but are recommended for jewelristas.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Jewelry-Designer-Pop-Magazine.jpg">
              <img alt="Gaia Repossi Jewelry Designer Pop Magazine" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3345" height="893" src="http://jewelrista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Gaia-Repossi-Jewelry-Designer-Pop-Magazine.jpg" width="604"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             It seems like Italy keeps contributing to the Worlds culture talented young designers. And if you liked Gaia Repossi, you are invited to get acquainted with Delfina Delettrez, another emerging
             <a href="http://jewelrista.com/blog/2014/11/30/delfina-delettrez-italian-jewelry-designer/" title="Italian Jewelry Designer">
              Italian jewelry designer
             </a>
             .
