     <h1>
      Christmas Party In The Office
     </h1>
     <div class="lead-img">
      <img alt="Christmas Party In The Office" class="alignleft" src="http://cdn03.cdn.justjared.com/wp-content/uploads/headlines/2006/12/the-office-christmas-party.jpg"/>
     </div>
     <div class="entry">
      <span>
       <p>
        Did you all catch last nights episode of
        <em>
         The Office
        </em>
        ? If not, you better get to somebody who has it TiVod because its one thats not to be missed!
       </p>
       <p>
        When the party planning committee disagrees on how to pull off the office Christmas party, Pam (
        <strong>
         Jenna Fischer
        </strong>
        ) and Karen (
        <strong>
         Rashida Jones
        </strong>
        ) band together to throw their own separate alcohol-and-karaoke party against Angelas (
        <strong>
         Angela Kinsey
        </strong>
        ) wishes. In the midst of it all, Michael Scott (
        <strong>
         Steve Carell
        </strong>
        ) copes with his split with his girlfriend by being persuaded to join the guys, Andy (
        <strong>
         Ed Helms
        </strong>
        ), Jim (
        <strong>
         John Krasinksi
        </strong>
        ), and Dwight (
        <strong>
         Rainn Wilson
        </strong>
        ) at a feast at Benihana Japanese restaurant.
       </p>
       <p>
        I wont go into it much in case some of you havent seen it (dont want to spoil anything), but do make sure you stay and watch the whole thing from beginning to end. Theres a great gag at the end played on Dwight. Thats all Ill say about that.
       </p>
       <p>
        I always think their holiday-themed episodes are really good and
        <em>
         A Benihana Christmas
        </em>
        is no exception. Here are some promo shots from the production of that episode which aired yesterday.
       </p>
