          <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
           Discover The Luxury Of Dubai
          </h1>
          <div class="meta">
           <span class="date updated">
            April 30, 2014
           </span>
           <span class="categories">
            <a href="http://blog.ihg.com/travel" rel="category tag">
             Travel
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://blog.ihg.com/vacation-inspiration" rel="category tag">
             Vacation Inspiration
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="dslc-content dslc-clearfix" id="dslc-content">
           <a id="dd_start">
           </a>
           <h2>
            Visit The Tallest Building, See A Water Show &amp; Tour The Desert
           </h2>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Dubai is one of seven emirates making up the United Arab Emirates that provides a great contrast between the desert and urban life. With its luxury and glitz, Dubai is a top destination for international travelers, attracting millions of tourists every year. Choose from several
            </span>
            <a href="http://www.ihg.com/hotels/us/en/hd/united-arab-emirates/dubai-hotels" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.ihg.com/hotels/us/en/hd/united-arab-emirates/dubai-hotels', 'hotels in Dubai']);" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             hotels in Dubai
            </a>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             from lavish to more economical, so choose your style and go find out what makes Dubai so special.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="Burj-Khalifa" class=" wp-image-402 alignleft" height="520" src="http://blog.ihg.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Burj-Khalifa.jpg" style="border: 5px solid white;" width="345"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
           </h3>
           <h3>
            See Dubai From The Tallest Building In The World
           </h3>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             The focus of downtown Dubai is the
            </span>
            <a href="http://www.burjkhalifa.ae/en/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.burjkhalifa.ae/en/', 'Burj Khalifa']);" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Burj Khalifa
            </a>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             . This building is the tallest in the world, towering over all of the other tall buildings in the world as it stands at more than 828 meters. Being the tallest building, the Burj Khalifa also has an elevator with the longest travel distance in the entire world. Step inside and let this high-speed elevator take you up to level 124 to the observation deck. At the Top (the observation deck) has glass walls from the floor to the ceiling, giving you breathtaking 360-degree views of Dubai and the surrounding desert and ocean. If the views from inside arent enough, step outside onto the open-air terrace for a more adventurous experience. While there is no time limit, people spend an average of an hour up there.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Hours: At the Top is open daily from 8:30 a.m.-1:00 a.m., but the last entry is 45 minutes before closing time.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <em>
             Admission: Individual ticket prices are 125 AED for adults 12 years and older, 95 AED for children 4-12 years, and free for children under 4 years. These prices are for general admission.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3 dir="ltr">
            Witness The Water Show At The Dubai Fountain
           </h3>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Dubai is also home to the worlds largest choreographed dancing water fountain.
            </span>
            <a href="http://www.thedubaimall.com/en/Entertain/TheDubaiFountain.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thedubaimall.com/en/Entertain/TheDubaiFountain.aspx', 'The Dubai Fountain']);" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             The Dubai Fountain
            </a>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             , sitting in Burj Khalifa Lake just outside of The Dubai Mall, shoots water high in the sky in sync with various types of music. It performs daily for passersby, giving daily afternoon and evening shows. With five different sized circles, the fountain is 900 feet long and sprays water as high as 500 feet. The Dubai Fountains best performances are at night when you get the water and music accompanied with a light show.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
              Hours: Afternoon shows are at 1:00 p.m. and 1:30 p.m. on Saturday-Thursday, and then at 1:30 p.m. and 2:00 p.m. on Fridays. Evening shows are daily and happen every 30 minutes from 6:00 p.m.-11:00 p.m. Show times are subject to change by a few minutes depending on that days prayer times.
             </span>
            </em>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <em>
             Admission: Shows are free to all ages.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3 dir="ltr">
            Go On A Joy Ride At Al Sahra Desert Resort Equestrian Centre
           </h3>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Roam the desert lands of Dubai on horseback or camelback at
            </span>
            <a href="http://www.jaresortshotels.com/Properties/AlSahraDesertResort/EquestrianCenter/Overview.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.jaresortshotels.com/Properties/AlSahraDesertResort/EquestrianCenter/Overview.aspx', 'Al Sahra Desert Resort Equestrian Centre.']);" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Al Sahra Desert Resort Equestrian Centre.
            </a>
            <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Any experience level of rider is welcomed to go desert riding on a horse or venture through the desert on a camel. Those choosing by way of a soft nosed horse are out for an hour and a half riding the desert hills with chances of seeing gazelles or desert foxes, as well as the sunset or sunrise over Dubais skyline. If a spitting camel sounds more fun, then you spend your hour and a half learning about the camels history, riding your camel for 40 minutes and tasting camels milk. Either choice makes for an interesting story to tell your friends and family back home.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
              Hours: Times were not listed on their website. Call or email the resort for more information.
             </span>
            </em>
           </p>
           <p dir="ltr">
            <em>
             Admission: Desert horse rides are 300 AED. Camel excursions are 200 AED.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            Words can only do so much, meaning you need to experience Dubai for yourself to see why its rolling sand dunes, tallest building in the world and life of luxury draw in millions of tourists in annually.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
