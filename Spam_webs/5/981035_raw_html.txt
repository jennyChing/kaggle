                     <span lang="EN-US" style='font-family: "Times New Roman","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US;'>
                      As home keepers,
our eyes must always be on our homes and our knees must be busy in prayer.
Jesus counseled his disciples to watch and pray seeing that the time they were
in was very challenging and that they will need Gods grace to overcome. We are
in challenging times and we need to be super-watchful at home.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      What does watch mean? It means:
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        To look at or observe attentively
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        An act or instance of carefully observing someone
or something over a period of time
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                       We need to observe things carefully and pay much
attention to what happens in our homes. One thing is being available to see and
the other is carefully paying attention to what goes on when we are right there
at home. Know exactly what happens from when your family members wake up till
when they sleep. Check on the children even while they are sleeping.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                       Concerning the woman of proverbs 31, the bible says
she carefully watches everything in her household - Proverbs 31:27. The good
news is that God gives his daughters grace to watch over the home. The issue
is: Are we walking in that grace? Every daughter of God has the Spirit of God.
The Spirit of God can help you uncover the deepest secrets; He knows all things
because He searches the mind of God. Many disasters, disappointments, troubles
etc. will be averted if we depend on the Holy Spirit to run our homes. When we
watch, we will nurture great potentials in our spouses and children and we will
stop the mouth of lions from devouring us.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                       What can help us watch our homes?
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        In this season of prayer and fasting, ask the
Holy Spirit to fill your heart and reveal himself more to you
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        Study the word of God  it naturally makes your
heart more inclined to Gods counsel
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        Be available to see what is actually going on in
the home. Make spending time with the family a priority for 2014
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        Pay attention to the things you see, dont just
sweep them under the carpet.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                        Pray unceasingly about what you observe, take
advantage of this season of prayer
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span class="textexposedshow">
                       As we do this, the good Lord will overtake our
homes and establish us as more than conqueror in Jesus Mighty Name.
                      </span>
                     </span>
