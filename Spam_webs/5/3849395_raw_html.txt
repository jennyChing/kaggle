               Woman Breaks Up with Boyfriend After He Pushes Her Off a Canyon Cliff (Video)

             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://www.totalprosports.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/boyfriend-pushes-girlfriend-off-cliff.jpg">
               <img alt="boyfriend pushes girlfriend off cliff" class="size-full wp-image-112191 aligncenter" height="340" src="http://www.totalprosports.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/boyfriend-pushes-girlfriend-off-cliff.jpg" title="boyfriend pushes girlfriend off cliff" width="610"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              Some couples
              <a href="http://www.totalprosports.com/2012/11/21/15-high-profile-sports-divorces/" title="15 High Profile Sports Divorces">
               break up
              </a>
              because they just cant get along. Others break up because one of them was unfaithful. And then there are those who split because they simply do not have the same goals in life.
             </p>
             <p>
              However, one poor woman recently discovered a brand new reason for breaking up with her boyfriend: he pushed her off a cliff.
             </p>
             <p>
              Dont worry, shes totally fine. You see, the woman was wearing a harness attached to a rope swing and was preparing to take an intentional leap over a canyon anyway. However, after a number of countdowns and false starts, she just couldnt bring herself to take the leap. Thats when her loving boyfriend gave her a shove.
             </p>
             <p>
              The footage was shot by a guy named
              <strong>
               Devin Graham
              </strong>
              as part of another video called Worlds Most Insane Rope Swing Ever. We dont know who the formerly happy couple is, but we do know that the woman, whether she really meant it or not, shouted Im breaking up with you! while she was dangling from a rope over the canyon after being pushed.
             </p>
             <p>
              Have a look:
             </p>
             <p>
              <iframe frameborder="0" height="343" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/Iuv__-nyO1M" width="610">
              </iframe>
             </p>
             <p>
              Now, I have to say that when I first heard about this clip I thought it was going to be fake. However, after watching it, if the terror in that girls voice isnt real, she is an absolutely incredible actress. So no, I dont think this was stagedor, in any case, the woman probably wasnt in on it.
             </p>
             <p>
              (Note to self:
              <em>
               dont
              </em>
              push girlfriend off cliff.)
             </p>
