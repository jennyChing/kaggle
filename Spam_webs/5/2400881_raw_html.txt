        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         Trash is most definitely a relative word.
        </p>
        <p>
         I may or may not be the creepy lady stalking her neighbors as they take trash out to make sure they arent throwing something away I may want.
        </p>
        <p>
         A similar occurrence as mentioned above may or may not have happened just last night.
        </p>
        <p>
         And since I may or may not have a fondness for what others consider trash, I am stoked about this new series:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/trash-to-treasure.jpg">
          <img alt="Trash to Treasure Series {rainonatinroof.com}" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1930" height="640" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/trash-to-treasure.jpg" width="430"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-1929">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The first Friday of every month you can join myself,
         <a href="http://www.thistlewoodfarms.com" target="_blank">
          Thistlewood Farms
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.somuchbetterwithage.com" target="_blank">
          So Much Better with Age
         </a>
         and a Guest Host for a fabulous trash to treasure project that costs $10 or less. This month, we are graced by the presence of
         <a href="http://www.atthepicketfence.com" target="_blank">
          At the Pickett Fence
         </a>
         as our guest host.
        </p>
        <p>
         For my trash to treasure project this month, I took a bulletin board my husband was going to toss and covered it in fabric.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-message-board-2-e1378403118970.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter wp-image-1941 size-full" height="871" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-message-board-2-e1378403118970.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         But, Jenna, this is nothing new.
        </p>
        <p>
         Fabric covered message boards are everywhere.
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, mine is slightly different.
        </p>
        <p>
         Look closer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/fabric-bulletin-board-3-e1378403213629.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1933" height="900" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/fabric-bulletin-board-3-e1378403213629.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Closer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/gold-hardware-e1378403257416.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1937" height="825" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/gold-hardware-e1378403257416.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         No, not at that beautiful gold hardware I added to give it a campaign feel.
        </p>
        <p>
         But, yes, go on and admire it too.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, look closer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-fabric-covered-bulletin-board-e1378403140841.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1940" height="367" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-fabric-covered-bulletin-board-e1378403140841.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There ya go. See, its a sassy bulletin board.
        </p>
        <p>
         This was simple!
        </p>
        <p>
         First, I cut my fabric a little bigger than my board.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/IMG_0443-e1378403905487.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1944" height="667" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/IMG_0443-e1378403905487.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I wanted the definition of the frame around the bulletin board, so first, I ran hot glue around the front of the board where the cork and frame meet, then quickly pushed the fabric into that crevice.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/IMG_0448-e1378403865438.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1946" height="667" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/IMG_0448-e1378403865438.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And after that , I just hot glued the fabric along the back to secure it.
        </p>
        <p>
         That fabulous gold hardware is just a
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00C97FL10/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00C97FL10&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=raonatiro-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          four pack of L brackets
         </a>
         that I spray
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/2013/07/40-gold-projects/" target="_blank">
          painted gold
         </a>
         and then screwed in the corners.
        </p>
        <p>
         Oh, and those fantastic little golden animal pushpins.
        </p>
        <p>
         They are simply spray painted plastic animals I picked up at a yard sale and then hot glued them to tacks.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/IMG_0440-e1378404311578.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1947" height="413" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/IMG_0440-e1378404311578.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, for the fun.
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres what some of my sassy photos say and my commentary.
        </p>
        <p>
         Get excited.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-bulletin-board-e1378403159700.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1939" height="367" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-bulletin-board-e1378403159700.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Absolutely. I have a clear purpose.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-bulletin-board-5-e1378403189241.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1938" height="825" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-bulletin-board-5-e1378403189241.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         How rude.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you just pictured Stephanie Tanner saying that, you are my best friend.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-fabric-covered-bulletin-board-e1378403140841.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1940" height="367" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/sassy-fabric-covered-bulletin-board-e1378403140841.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Every time, call me. Every single time. Im sure I will have something to say as well. And then we will both feel better about ourselves.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/fabric-message-center-3.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1935" height="825" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/fabric-message-center-3-e1378403304489.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Everyday. Everyday, put on your awesome pants. My tend to be stretchy pants or shorts.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/diy-fabric-covered-message-board-e1378403234250.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1932" height="367" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/diy-fabric-covered-message-board-e1378403234250.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I beg of you.
         <em>
          Please
         </em>
         do this. Its really for the good of mankind.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/message-board-e1378404475709.jpg">
          <img alt="Sassy Fabric Covered Message Board {rainonatinroof.com} #messageboard #trashtotreasure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1949" height="367" src="http://rainonatinroof.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/message-board-e1378404475709.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          So
         </em>
         confusing. I mean, really, someone should pay me to just be me. Because Im that fabulous.
        </p>
        <p>
         I hope you enjoyed that as much as I did.
        </p>
        <p>
         And if you are wondering where I got all these fabulous little images, I just found them on Pinterest, saved the image and printed them off. You can find most of these, as well as many, many more on my

          Pinterest boards
