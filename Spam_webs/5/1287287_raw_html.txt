        <p>
         Taj Mahal is a remarkable landmark and a favorite with the tourists also. People the world over have recently voted it to rank third in the list of top 25 landmarks.
         <span id="more-2091">
         </span>
         The survey conducted by TripAdvisor in 2013 under its coveted Travelers Choice Attractions Awards has found only two landmarks more popular than the Taj  Machu Picchu in Peru and Angkor Wat in Cambodia.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2093" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://www.hoodaki.com/blog/index.php/see-taj-trip-india/taj-mahal/" rel="attachment wp-att-2093">
          <img alt="Taj Mahal" class=" wp-image-2093 " height="250" src="http://www.hoodaki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Taj-Mahal.jpg" title="Taj Mahal" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Taj Mahal (Source: Wikipedia)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This is not the first time international community has shown their love and preference for the iconic landmark of India. People voted generously for the Taj when an initiative was taken in 2000 to choose Wonders of the World from a selection of 200 existing monuments. More than 100 million votes were cast, a significant number of which were obviously in favor of this beautiful monument as it was one among the New7Wonders of the World when the result was announced in 2007 in Lisbon.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          India  the Land of Wonderful Landmarks
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Already a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Taj Mahal receives over 2 million visitors every year. This number also includes over 200,000 tourists from overseas who specifically choose India to see the Taj and other famous landmarks in India. As the finest specimen of Mughal architecture, which is necessarily a combination of Persian, Turkish and Indian architectural styles, it enchants visitors and makes their holidays a worthwhile experience.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2094" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://www.hoodaki.com/blog/index.php/see-taj-trip-india/lotus-temple/" rel="attachment wp-att-2094">
          <img alt="Lotus Temple" class=" wp-image-2094 " height="250" src="http://www.hoodaki.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Lotus-Temple.jpg" title="Lotus Temple" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Lotus Temple (Source: Wikipedia)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         However, tourists on India holidays have not just the Taj, but a diverse array of attractions to see and experience. Here are some other notable landmarks popular with international tourists.
        </p>

          Lotus Temple

        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          The Golden Triangle Tour
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Agra where Taj Mahal is located is at a distance of 231 km from Delhi, the capital of India. Jaipur, another popular tourist destination known as Pink City, is situated at a distance of 234 km from Agra and 273 km from Delhi. Together, the three cities make a popular tourist circuit known as the Golden Triangle. The circuit is about 1,000 km by road and it may not take more than 4 to 6 hours of drive to cover any of its legs. It is possible to complete the Golden Triangle tour in 7 or 8 days, starting your journey from Delhi and finishing it in the same city.
        </p>
