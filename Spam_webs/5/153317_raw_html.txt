        </a>
       </h1>

        <p>
         <em>
          Individuals act when there is an
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Incentive" target="_blank">
           incentive
          </a>
          . So whats the incentive
          <a href="http://dappered.com/2013/01/what-goes-into-having-style/?" target="_blank">
           to being well dressed
          </a>
          ? The benefits are numerous, and just a few are below. Add yours in the comments section. Note that this post isnt arguing that all men should dress well. Far from it. Not everyone wants to dress well.
          <a href="http://dappered.com/2011/09/thank-goodness-for-the-schmucks/" target="_blank">
           And thats completely fine
          </a>
          . Plus, there areplenty of situations
          <a href="http://dappered.com/2013/03/11-situations-where-its-okay-to-look-like-crap/" target="_blank">
           where its more than okay to look like crap
          </a>
          .
          <em>
           <br/>
          </em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Total strangers usually give you the benefit of the doubt.
         <br/>
         2. Youll seem a bit mysterious while travelling. Even if your trip is painfully boring.
         <br/>
         3. You usually have a coat or umbrella to offer someone who has romantic potential.
         <br/>
         4. You look less like a weirdo when out,
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/08/the-getting-a-drink-by-yourself-checklist/" target="_blank">
          having a drink by yourself
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         5. As long as youre respectful,
         <a href="http://youtu.be/hQM8UKgt3Qs?t=2m11s" target="_blank">
          some police officers might treat you better
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         6.
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/01/the-suggestion-avoid-wearing-just-a-shirt-and-tie/" target="_blank">
          Suit Jackets and Blazers
         </a>
         make you look like youre in great shape, even if youre not quite there.
         <br/>
         7. Its a great reason to get in shape. Well Dressed + In Shape = The world is your oyster.
         <br/>
         8. Its a great reason to
         <em>
          STAY
         </em>
         in shape. Cmon, you cant eat yourself out of that new suit.
         <br/>
         9. The look on the face of the girl who dumped you years ago when you run into her at the airport
         <br/>
         10. especially if you buy her a drink, listen to how her life has gone since the breakup, and leave after that one drink, letting her wonder what you must be up to.
        </p>
        <p>
         11. Your handshake seems stronger.
         <br/>
         12. With a smile and confident eye contact, you can look like you belong just about anywhere.
         <br/>
         13. If your boss is a bit of a slob, and youre dressed appropriately for your position yet better than him/her, its awfully hard for them to be a real jackass to you during your yearly review.
         <br/>
         14. The bridesmaids quickly forget about the groomsman that walked with them down the aisle.
         <br/>
         15. Shop online?You can develop a great relationship
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/07/10-pros-every-well-dressed-guy-needs-in-his-corner/" target="_blank">
          with your UPS man
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         16. Ticket agents and flight attendants seem to give you extra attention.
         <br/>
         17. Those extra pockets inside your jacket come in real handy.
         <br/>
         18. It makes it easier to get away with less professional stuff at work (having scruff, getting a beer at lunch, etc)
         <br/>
         19. As a younger guy, you give
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/01/the-10-style-benefits-of-getting-older-as-a-man/" target="_blank">
          all those older gentlemen hope
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         20.Youre happy to help when your not so well dressed pal asks you to help him pick out some new clothes for his wedding day.
        </p>
        <p>
         21. You finally start to take care of your clothes and
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2013/01/storing-your-clothes-hangers-101/" target="_blank">
          store them properly
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         22. Taking off your jacket buys you extra time for the authorities to step in if youre grosslyover-matchedin a fist fight.
         <br/>
         23. Walking a dog or carrying a baby while well dressed makes you 25% more handsome.
         <br/>
         24. If you
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/12/those-two-little-words/" target="_blank">
          stink at accepting compliments
         </a>
         (of any kind), youll probably get better at taking them.
         <br/>
         25. Youll also be more willing to pay someone a compliment, which can open many doors if its genuine.
        </p>
        <p>
         26. If you look younger than your actual age, people will take you as serious as you should be taken.
         <br/>
         27. If you look older than your actual age, people might think you own the place.
         <br/>
         28. Believe it or not,
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2011/12/the-importance-of-not-overthinking-your-look/" target="_blank">
          it helps keep things in perspective
         </a>
         . You live in your clothes. Things will happen to your clothes. At some point youre going to spill something on a favorite pair of shoes or shirt and yknow what? Its just stuff.
         <br/>
         29. The salesman wont give you the side eye if you want to take that car you cant afford under any circumstances out for a test drive.
         <br/>
         30. Your backup
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/10/halloween-costume-ideas-for-the-well-dressed-guy/" target="_blank">
          Halloween costume every year
         </a>
         :
         <em>
          Mayhem
         </em>
         from the Allstate commercials.
        </p>
        <p>
         31. Sometimes, your date wants to get dressed up and go somewhere fancy. Sometimes, your date may want to date someone who initiates that getting dressed up and going somewhere fancy.
         <br/>
         32. Youll become
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/09/how-to-iron-the-guys-guide-to-eradicating-wrinkles/" target="_blank">
          fluent in ironing
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         33. Retail sales people are less likely to
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2011/03/style-debate-can-i-help-you-find-anything-today/" target="_blank">
          pester you
         </a>
         since you seem to know what youre doing.
         <br/>
         34. Youll slay em at
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/12/style-scenario-the-company-christmas-party/" target="_blank">
          the company Christmas party
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         35.
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2011/06/style-chore-battle-shining-shoes-vs-ironing/" target="_blank">
          Shining a great pair of shoes
         </a>
         is sort of like hand washing a really cool car.
        </p>
        <p>
         36. Its about potential. Most want to max out their personal potential. Maximizing your potential, at surface level, often includes how you present yourself. That includes your clothes. And they certainly dont have to be expensive to help you reach the level of confidence you deserve.
         <br/>
         37. Down the road, old photos wont look so bad after all.
         <br/>
         38. That feeling of not knowing what to wear? It goes away after a while.
         <br/>
         39. Bartenders seem to keep a good eye on you for when you need another drink.
         <br/>
         40. Because theres going to come a time when youll HAVE to be dressed well. And chances are its going to be an important event. If youre not already used to dressing well, youre going to feel awkward. And itll show.
         <a href="http://dappered.com/2012/08/places-to-try-wearing-a-suit-to-where-you-normally-wouldnt/" target="_blank">
          Practice makes perfect
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Your turn guys. What else? If someone who doesnt normally dress well, told you that they might start giving it a little more thought, what reasons would you give them to give it a shot? Leave it below.
         </em>
        </p>
