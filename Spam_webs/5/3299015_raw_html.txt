            <div class="component component--box component--box-flushbody-vertical component--box--primary component--feature">
             <div class="component__header br-box-page">
              <h2>
               Love Me Do
              </h2>
             </div>
             <div class="component__body br-box-page">
              <div class="feature br-keyline">
               <div class="feature__img thirteen-twentyfourths">
                <img alt="Love Me Do" class="rsp-img" data-img-src-176="http://ichef.bbci.co.uk/images/ic/192xn/p00z6jrh.jpg" data-img-src-208="http://ichef.bbci.co.uk/images/ic/256xn/p00z6jrh.jpg" data-img-src-304="http://ichef.bbci.co.uk/images/ic/336xn/p00z6jrh.jpg" data-img-src-440="http://ichef.bbci.co.uk/images/ic/480xn/p00z6jrh.jpg" data-img-src-576="http://ichef.bbci.co.uk/images/ic/640xn/p00z6jrh.jpg" src="http://ichef.bbci.co.uk/images/ic/160xn/p00z6jrh.jpg"/>
               </div>
               <div class="feature__description centi">
                <p>
                 Moon River was the number one song as 1962dawned and Cliff Richard also topped the charts with The Young Ones in January.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 The Cavern in Liverpool was the place to play. The Beatles played 292 times in this underground venue which was also an important musical breeding ground for groups like Gerry and the Pacemakers, The Searchers, The Merseybeats, and The Swinging Blue Jeans.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Manager Brian Epstein signed The Beatles to a five year management deal after seeing them play at the Cavern club.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Brian Epsteinsmanagement work paid off when EMI Records offered The Beatles arecording deal on their small Parlophone label.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 On 4 September, 1962The Beatles went into Abbey Road studios with producerGeorge Martin to record their first single.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 TheBeatlesreleased Love Me Do on 5 October, 1962. It went to number 17 in the UKcharts.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Several versions of the songs were recorded. An alternative recording of the song was released around the world and went to number one in the USA in 1964.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Love Me Do was written by John Lennon and Paul McCartney.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 George Martin toyed with the idea of issuing How Do You Do It? as the Beatles' first single.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 From Me to You was the first Beatles number one single in 1963 in most UK charts.
                </p>
               </div>

                host = loc.host,

                url += 'test.';

                        theme = themes[ +(theme === themes[0]) ];

                    return segment;
