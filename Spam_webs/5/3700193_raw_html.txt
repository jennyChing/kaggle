        <div id="intro">
         <div class="dkimg-c">
          <span class="image_container">
           <img alt="beautiful sunset at Waikiki beach on the tropical island of Oahu, Hawaii. " height="333" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com/dk-production/images/58285/large/dreamstime_s_20041927.jpg?1384888200" width="550"/>
          </span>
          <div class="dkimg-cap">
           People live longer in Hawaii, and other blue states
          </div>
         </div>
         The 10 states with the
         <a href="http://kff.org/other/state-indicator/death-rate-per-100000/">
          lowest mortality rates
         </a>
         :
         <br/>
         <div class="indent">
          <br/>
          1. Hawaii
          <br/>
          2. California
          <br/>
          3. Connecticut
          <br/>
          4. Minnesota
          <br/>
          5. New York
          <br/>
          6. Massachusetts
          <br/>
          7. Colorado
          <br/>
          8. New Hampshire
          <br/>
          9. New Jersey
          <br/>
          10. Washington
         </div>
         <p>
          The 12 states with the
          <b>
           highest
          </b>
          mortality rates:
          <br/>
         </p>
         <div class="indent">
          1. Mississippi
          <br/>
          2. Alabama
          <br/>
          3. West Virginia
          <br/>
          4. Oklahoma
          <br/>
          5. Kentucky
          <br/>
          6. Louisiana
          <br/>
          7. Arkansas
          <br/>
          8. Tennessee
          <br/>
          9. South Carolina
          <br/>
          10. Georgia
          <br/>
          11. Indiana
          <br/>
          12. Missouri
         </div>
         <p>
          Of the 25 states above the national average, 19 are blue and just six are red. Of the 25 states below the national average, 17 are red and just eight are blue.
         </p>
         <p>
          Environmental policies, access to health insurance, workplace safety regulations ... these things matter. They are literally a matter of life and death. And it's clear that conservatism is hazardous to your health.
         </p>

            <p>
             There are a number of comments below looking at this correlation and that. The definitive study can be found in
             <a href="http://www.equalitytrust.org.uk/about-inequality/spirit-level">
              The Spirit Level
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             Thousands of man-hours of research from developed countries around the world were pulled together and analyzed by Wilkinson and Pickett. The one factor that correlated with
             <a href="http://www.equalitytrust.org.uk/about-inequality/effects">
              differences in outcomes on a variety of quality of life measures
             </a>
             (mortality, crime, education, drug abuse, education outcomes, etc. etc.) was - in developed countries, that should have the resources to deal with those problems - the greater the inequality within such, the worse the outcomes were for everyone, from bottom to the top. (They found the relationship can be seen comparing the 50 U.S. states as well.)
            </p>

             <a href="http://www.equalitytrust.org.uk">
              website created around this
             </a>
             has many resources and new research that continues to confirm and expand upon their findings.
            </p>

             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/The-Spirit-Level-Equality-Societies/dp/1608193411">
              the original book
