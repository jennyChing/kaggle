        <h1 class="single-title" itemprop="headline">
         Hedera helix
        </h1>
        <p class="meta" style="float:right; padding-right:10px; border-bottom:1px solid #f4f4f4;">
         Posted  under
         <a href="http://www.olaladirectory.com.au/category/gardening-landscaping/" rel="category tag">
          Gardening | Landscaping
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.olaladirectory.com.au/category/plants-flowers/" rel="category tag">
          Plants | Flowers
         </a>
         <br/>
         Posts By:
         <a href="http://www.olaladirectory.com.au/u/sc_9e5dcc5564011008d97bfbd0e13a96f4/" rel="author" title="Posts by Plants Lady">

       <section class="post-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         Hedera helix is an ideal houseplant for people who have pets. Scientifically termed as Hedera Helix, this plant is known to filter indoor pollutants like fecal particles, formaldehyde aerosols and much more and keep your house toxin free.
         <br/>
         Its known for removing the chemical benzene, a known carcinogen found in cigarette smoke, detergents, pesticides, and the off-gasing of other synthetic materials, is said to be fantastic for asthma and allergies and also removes formaldehyde. Hedera helixis particularly effective against benzene found in oil-based paints, detergents and plastic material.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
         </strong>
         Hedera helix is a popular ornamental, valued for its ability to thrive in shady places, provide excellent groundcover and cover unsightly walls, sheds and tree stumps.
         <strong>
         </strong>
         The evergreen, woody-stemmed plants are often seen trailing across yards and gardens, climbing walls, or encouraged as climbers along a supporting pole inside homes for a beautiful and decorative houseplant accent.Very easy to grow, ivy makes a very attractive hanging plant. Although the specimen above looks leggy, ivy can be very bushy if planted in mass.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Recommended varieties:
         </strong>
         There are several different species of theHedera helix that have different leaf shape, size and color.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Chicago
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Chicago has 2-3 cm (0.7-1.2 inch) long and 3cm (1.2 inch) wide, medium greenleaves. The lives of one of its forms, Hedera helix Chicago Variegata are creamy-edged. Those of another Hedera helix Golden Chicago are marked with golden yellow patches.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Chicago.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Chicago'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1203" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Chicago-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Chicago-Variegata.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Chicago Variegata'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1205" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Chicago-Variegata-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Cristata
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Cristata (Parsley Ivy) has 3-4cm (1.2-1.5 inch) long and 4cm (1.5 inch) wide medium green leaves that are so notably undulate as to seem curly-edged.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Cristata.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Cristata'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1207" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Cristata-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Hedera helix Emerald Gem &amp; Hedera helix Emerald Jewel
          </em>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Emerald Gem and Hedera helix Emerald Jewelboth have 2cm (0.7 inch) long and 3cm (1.2 inch) wide, sharply pointed, emerald green leaves.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Emerald-Gem.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Emerald Gem'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1208" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Emerald-Gem-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Emerald-Jewel.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Emerald Jewel'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1209" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Emerald-Jewel-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Glacier
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Glacier has 3cm (1.2 inch) long and 2cm (0.7 inch) wide leaves which are medium green with grey-green blotches, white marginal patches and pink edges. For dense growth this plant needs pinching out two or three times a year.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Galcier.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Galcier'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1206" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Galcier-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Jubilee
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Jubilee has 2cm long and 1.5-2cm (0.5-0.7 inch) wide dark green leaves variegated with grey and white. This variety is notably dense.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Jubilee.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Jubilee'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1210" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Jubilee-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Little Diamond
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Little Diamond has roughly diamond shaped, 2cm (0.7 inch) long and 1.5cm (0.5 inch) wide, medium green leaves thinly bordered with white. It needs pinching out to become bushy.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Little-Diamond.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Little Diamond'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1212" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Little-Diamond-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Lutzii
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Lutzii has 3cm (1.2 inch) long and 2 cm (0.7 inch) wide, dark green leaves covered with pale green and yellow spots. Not self-branching it needs pinching out two or three times a year.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Lutzii.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Lutzii'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1211" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Lutzii-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hedera helix Sagittifolia
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         Hedera helix Sagittifolia has arrow-head-shaped, 4cm (1.5 inch) long and 3cm (1.2 inch) wide, dark green leaves. A variegated form, Hedera helix Sagittifolia Variegata has light green and pale yellow markings. These plants make an excellent trailers, but growing points must be pinched out if bushy growth is desired.
        </p>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Sagittifolia.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Sagittifolia'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1213" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Sagittifolia-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Sagittifolia-Variegata.jpg" rel="fancybox">
         <img alt="Hedera helix 'Sagittifolia Variegata'" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1214" height="150" src="http://plantsrescue.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Hedera-helix-Sagittifolia-Variegata-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are interested in how to care the Hedera helix, please fell free to follow us on
         <a href="http://plantsrescue.com/hedera-helix/" title="Hedera helix">
          Plants Rescue website
         </a>
         .
        </p>
