          <h1 style="text-align: center;">
           Chicken Cacciatore
          </h1>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc11.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13054" height="347" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc11.jpg" title="cacc1" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc11.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I love Chicken Cacciatore, but rarely made it {until recently}. I looked at several recipes online, then created my own version. I hope you enjoy it as much as we do!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Ingredients:
           </strong>
           <br/>
           1 whole fryer chicken plus 6 thighs (skin on)
           <br/>
           1 large can Italian crushed tomatoes
           <br/>
           1/4 tsp sage
           <br/>
           1/2 cup dry white wine
           <br/>
           6 cloves garlic (peeled)
           <br/>
           1 onion
           <br/>
           1 bell pepper each: Green, Red, Yellow, Orange (or any combination of).
           <br/>
           1 1/2 cups chicken stock
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Wash and pat dry chicken pieces, then salt and pepper both sides.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc2.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13055" height="331" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc2.jpg" title="cacc2" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc2.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Dredge each piece in flour:
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc4.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13059" height="367" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc4.jpg" title="cacc4" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc4.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Fry in olive oil until golden brown and crisp on each side:
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc7.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13057" height="358" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc7.jpg" title="cacc7" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc7.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           While chicken is frying, cut up the bell peppers and onions, and peel the cloves of garlic. Crush two of the cloves. Note: I chose not to use green peppers, as my husband doesnt care for them.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc6.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13058" height="331" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc6.jpg" title="cacc6" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc6.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           When chicken pieces have been browned on both sides, transfer them onto a large plate. Drain excess oil from pan (leaving brown bits in the pan!), and add white wine. Add peppers and garlic and saute for about 3 minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Add sage, Italian crushed tomatoes and chicken stock. I decided to try the Knorrs Homestyle concentrated chicken stock that I received at BlogHer. I am impressed! It is super easy to use, and it doesnt take up much room in my cabinet  that is always a plus.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc8.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13060" height="331" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc8.jpg" title="cacc8" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc8.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Cover and simmer for about 2 hours (or until meat is almost falling off bone) on medium low heat. If sauce gets too thick, you can add a little water or wine.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc111.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="chicken-cacciatore-recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13061" height="331" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc111.jpg" title="cacc11" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/cacc111.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Serve
           <strong>
            Chicken Cacciatore
           </strong>
           over rice or noodles.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="chicken cacciatore,recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25248" height="331" src="http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/chickenc.jpg" width="500"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eightymphmom.com/2011/11/easy-chicken-cacciatore-recipe.html&amp;media=http://eightymphmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/chickenc.jpg&amp;description=Easy Chicken Cacciatore recipe')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
