      <p>
       We have seen a lot of Super Mario Bros comics on the web but this ones among the more special ones we have ever seen.
      </p>
      <p>
       The subtleness yet its ability to make us laugh is what attracted us the most.
      </p>
      <p>
       I mean, who knew that troopas were such valiant soldiers, putting themselves on the line for the sake of their King and honor.
      </p>
      <p>
       Super Mario sure has a lot of backstories and this just allows us to discover one of them.
      </p>
