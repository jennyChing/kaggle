      <p>
       If, like me, youre a huge fan of writer/director Quentin Tarantino, you will be excited by this little piece of news from
       <a href="http://www.badtaste.it/index.php?option=com_content&amp;task=view&amp;id=9753&amp;Itemid=29" target="_blank">
        BadTaste.it
       </a>
       : He intends to continue the messy adventures of The Bride with a third
       <em>
        Kill Bill
       </em>
       movie! During an interview on the Italian chat show
       <em>
        Parla con Me
       </em>
       (or Talk To Me), Tarantino was asked(from what I can understand  the questions are being asked in Italian)  if he would be making any follow up films to his previous work, and after saying,
       <em>
        it will never happen,
       </em>
       to a
       <em>
        Pulp Fiction
       </em>
       sequel, he reveals his intention to make
       <em>
        <strong>
         Kill Bill 3
        </strong>
       </em>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-28666">
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="advert zergnet">
       <span>
        From The Web
       </span>
       <div id="zergnet-unit-in-article">
       </div>
       <script>
        /*&lt;![CDATA[*/document.write('\
                &lt;div id="zergnet-widget-30857"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;\

                        zergnet.src = \'http://www.zergnet.com/zerg.js?id=30857\';\

                        znscr.parentNode.insertBefore(zergnet, znscr);\
                    })();\
                &lt;\/script&gt;\
            ');/*]]&gt;*/
       </script>
      </div>
      <p>
       Tarantino doesnt give us any solid details on what
       <em>
        Kill Bill 3
       </em>
       (Vol. 3?) might be about (Ill get to what I think it will be in a minute), but this is understandable since its (presumably) just in the idea stages at the moment. But he DOES let us know a couple of things: He wants it to take place 10 years after the events of
       <em>
        Kill Bill Vol.2
       </em>
       (for that to be in real time, too, hed have to wait until 2014) and he reveals part of his reasoning for wanting to make another in that he loves the character of The Bride so much.
      </p>
      <p>
       Sounds like reason enough to me! If you want to hear the announcement from the lips of Mr. Tarantino himself (
       <em>
        The Bride will fight again!
       </em>
       ), check out the video below from the Italian talk show
       <em>
        Parla con Me
       </em>
       (the
       <em>
        Kill Bill 3
       </em>
       stuff starts at 5:55):
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <p>
       Well, even if it isnt exactly OFFICIAL official confirmation of plans for
       <em>
        Kill Bill 3
       </em>
       (casting news, production start date and so forth), and as weve learned in the past, Tarantino is notorious for announcing projects and never getting around to them (or at least taking ages to, as he did with
       <a href="http://www.screenrant.com/tag/inglourious-basterds/">
        <em>
         Inglourious Basterds
        </em>
       </a>
       ). But I still am very excited that a third
       <em>
        Kill Bill
       </em>
       is now on the table.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Some Kill Bill spoilers follow
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Tarantino has hinted in the past at the possibility of another
       <em>
        Kill Bill
       </em>
       , not least by the scene in Vol.1 where The Bride tells Vernita Greens (played by Vivica A. Fox) daughter, after killing her mother, that if shes still,
       <em>
        raw about it,
       </em>
       then The Bride will be waiting. I honestly think thats at least ONE of the routes Tarantino will go with
       <em>
        Kill Bill 3
       </em>
        it just seems too fitting NOT to be. The Bride went to get revenge on those who killed her daughter (or so she thought), her husband and at least TRIED to kill her, and so it makes sense that Vernita Greens daughter, Nikki, would want revenge on her.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        End Spoilers
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Perhaps well even get to see The Brides daughter, B.B., go sword-to-sword with Vernita Greens daughter
      </p>
      <p>
       Of course,
       <a href="http://screenrant.com/david-carradine-dies-72-ross-11656/">
        the sad passing of Bill himself, David Carradine
       </a>
       , means that they cant really have any flashback scenes involving the character. Not unless they recast which, out of respect for Carradine, I dont think Tarantino would do. So I assume that, along with the Vernita Greens daughter getting revenge on The Bride storyline, Tarantino will exclude any Bill storyline (maybe hed include one done in anime, like the O-Ren Ishii sequence in Vol. 1). Which begs the question: Will Tarantino still call it
       <em>
        Kill Bill
       </em>
       ? After all  and in case you havent seen the first two you might wanna skip the next few words  The Bride did ultimately kill Bill, so it doesnt really make sense to call the third movie as such. Or am I way off?
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 460px">
       <span class="responsiveImg img_article_content" data-alt="kill bill david carradine Tarantino Announces His Intention To Make Kill Bill 3" data-image="wp-content/uploads/kill-bill-david-carradine.jpg" data-size="0:slir/w380-h253-q90-c380:253/,481:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/,768:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/,1024:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/" data-size2x="0:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/,481:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/,768:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/,1024:slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/" data-src-base="http://static.srcdn.com/" style="display:block; padding-bottom: 66.6666666667%;">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="kill bill david carradine Tarantino Announces His Intention To Make Kill Bill 3" src="http://static.srcdn.com/slir/w450-h300-q90-c450:300/wp-content/uploads/kill-bill-david-carradine.jpg" title="kill bill david carradine Tarantino Announces His Intention To Make Kill Bill 3"/>
        </noscript>
       </span>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        David Carradine as "Bill"
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       As I said, Tarantino has a penchant for saying hes going to be making something and then either not or taking a long time to do so, but I think we can count
       <em>
        Kill Bill 3
       </em>
       as more of a possibility/probability than most. I dont know about you but Im stoked to see Uma Thurmans Bride back-in-action after years of peace and quiet with her daughter.
      </p>
      <p>
       What do you think of the prospect of a third
       <em>
        Kill Bill
       </em>
       ? Do you like the idea of revisiting The Bride character after 10 years and continuing on the bloody fighting we saw so much of in Volumes 1 and 2? Or is the story told and Tarantino should just leave it alone? Let us know your thoughts and opinions below.
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Screen Rant
       </em>
       will be sure to keep you up to date on the
       <strong>
        <em>
         Kill Bill 3
        </em>
       </strong>
       news front!
      </p>
