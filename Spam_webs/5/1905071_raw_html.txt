    <div class="g_txt_5">
     Grande Prairie, Canada, September 12,2014/Free-Press-Release.com/ -- Buying a home is a big decision.  It is important to make sure that people are well informed during this process so that they are ready to take on the payments and responsibility that is associated with owning a home.  Gert Martens is available to help individuals who are looking at buying homes.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Canadian mortgage rates are at an all-time low right now, so making this decision now, can really help people out.  Gert Martens has a lot of information about different companies and will be working with clients that are interested in learning more.  She has a lot of experience and a lot of information that is going to be shared.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     The company spokesperson added, We have a lot of clients that purchase homes and are happy with the entire process.  We also have clients who choose not to purchase because they find out that they may not be able to afford it.  These people are going to take the steps that are necessary so that they are able to make a home purchase.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Affordability is something that is extremely important for any type of loan.  This is something that people need figure out.  They need to know the amount that they can afford before they even start looking for a home.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     There are certain responsibilities that homeowners will need to take into consideration too.  Some homes may require some repairs right away also.  This can reduce the cost that they have in purchasing it, but it may not be an expense that can be rolled into the loan.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Gert Martens is going to help people determine the type of loan that they need.  They will also be able to figure out what is affordable for them.  People can dream on what home that they want, but when they consider the other factors, it may not be a feasible option for them.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     There are many decisions to make when purchasing a home and getting a mortgage for that.  The down payment can affect the amount of the monthly payment.  The amount that is financed and the length of the mortgage can affect interest rates too.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Financing is something that can have its difficulties.  Gert Martens has been in this business for a long time and has seen it all.  This is why she is the best person to consult with before purchasing any type of home with a Canadian mortgage.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     About Us:  When consumers are looking to purchase a home, very few of them are going to pay cash for it.  They will be looking for the best financing options that they can find.  Every bank is offering a different interest rate and different repayment terms.  Dominion Lending Centres are going to be there to help consumers find the best place to finance this large purchase.  They have teamed up with many different kinds of financial companies and banks to bring consumers the best options.  To see what is available and how Dominion Lending Centres can help, check them out at http://www.gertmartens.ca.
