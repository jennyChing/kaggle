            The Secrets of Food Marketing and How Youre Being Fooled By Advertising
           </a>
          </h1>
          <p class="no-break">
           <span class="st_facebook" st_title="The Secrets of Food Marketing and How Youre Being Fooled By Advertising" st_url="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/06/19/secrets-food-marketing-youre-fooled-advertising/">

           </span>
          </p>
          <div class="fb-like" data-action="like" data-font="" data-href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/06/19/secrets-food-marketing-youre-fooled-advertising/" data-layout="standard" data-send="false" data-width="450" show_faces="false" style="margin: 0px 0px 5px 0px; height:25px; overflow:hidden;">
          </div>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/new-improved.jpg">
            <img alt="new-improved" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-40439" height="200" src="http://www.wakingtimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/new-improved-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
           April McCarthy,
           <a href="http://preventdisease.com/news/14/060614_Secrets-of-Food-Marketing-How-Youre-Being-Fooled.shtml" target="_blank">
            <em>
             Prevent Disease
            </em>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/06/19/secrets-food-marketing-youre-fooled-advertising/">
            <strong>
             Waking Times
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           There are large divisions within every population in the world where people have absolutely no idea where their food comes from. They believe cows, pigs and chickens run around in green pastures, without any comprehension of what theyre fed, how theyre treated and most of all, completely unaware of the confined quarters they live in for the majority of their lives. This is the reality of factory farming. The speaker in this video is portraying herself as a so-called expert revealing food marketings secret weapon and how they fool the public through deceptive strategies to encourage the false belief that meat comes from humanely treated animals. The audiences expression says it all.
          </p>
          <p>
           The speaker in this video is actually an actress named Kate Miles, but the facts about produce and its marketing are 100% real. The audience is also real, and thus the looks of disgust are totally real too.There are things we can all do as individuals to end factory farming, but there are some things that we can only do together: Exposing the suffering, changing the minds of food businesses, of politicians, and of the public.There is no doubt that consumer awareness and concern about the lives led by animals raised for food is on the rise. Ethical concerns are playing an increasingly important role in purchasing decisions. Labeling makes a difference but they are both educating and confusing consumers. For example, egg producers  perhaps more than any other  have
           <a href="http://preventdisease.com/news/14/042814-How-Much-Do-Labels-Tell-About-Eggs-Produced.shtml" target="_blank">
            responded to this trend by adding an abundance of confusing claims
           </a>
           on egg cartons.Most ingredients commonly used in factory farms include genetically modified grains, plastic pellets, animal byproducts, dangerous chemicals including drugs, antibiotics and vaccines in misguided attempts to fight disease and control parasites.
          </p>
          <div align="center" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 1em;">
           <script type="text/javascript">

           </script>
          </div>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/mKTORFmMycQ" width="630">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://preventdisease.com/news/13/121213_FDA-Concedes-Antibiotic-Use-In-Farm-Animals-Must-Be-Phased-Out.shtml" target="_blank">
            Antibiotics are added to animal feed or drinking water
           </a>
           of cattle, hogs, poultry and other food-producing animals to make them gain weight faster or use less food to gain weight.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its time to create awareness at all levels. Inform your families, relatives, colleagues, friends about the tactics these industries use to manipulate public opinion and conceal what is really going on behind close doors.
          </p>
          <p>
           Most of all, speak your mind with your wallet. Avoid toxic factory farmed meat. As a consumer, select organic, grass-feed varieties whenever and wherever possible. Action first begins with you and your choices.
          </p>
          <p>
           No amount of marketing makes factory farming acceptable. You can stop the spin.
          </p>
