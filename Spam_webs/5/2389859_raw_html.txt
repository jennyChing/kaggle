                     Most of you will have heard of Empower Network and I am sure some of you are thinking of joining and others will have previously made the decision not to join for whatever reason.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: red;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: medium;">
                        Here is why you should buy and promote the Empower Network training products in general:
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </span>
                     <b>
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      * Excellent Products -
                     </b>
                     The training products at Empower Network are excellent. There is a direction and focus there that teaches you how to generate traffic and build your online business. Much of the complications and distractions are removed and the focus is all on getting traffic. From the products you basically get to learn exactly what other succesful marketers are doing to promote their own business. You also get resell rights to sell these products as an affiliate for 100% commissions.
                     <b>
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      * Built In Sales Funnel 
                     </b>
                     To resell the products as an affiliate you dont need to do much else apart from send traffic to the lead capture pages that you are provided with and the built in sales funnel does the rest. All the selling and up-selling is done for you.
                     <b>
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      * High Earnings Potential
                     </b>
                     In my opinion it is easier to make good money with Empower Network due to the monthly recurring commissions than making one-off Clickbank sales with review sites etc.

The following figures have been mentioned as typical earnings for a certain level of output as an affiliate (this is not guaranteed or anything but these figures are frequently outlined in the Empower Network audio interviews and updates):
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: red;">
                      People making 1 basic level $25 sale per day for 60 to 90 days seem to be earning around $10,000 a month after that.
                     </span>
                     <span style="color: red;">
                      People making 2 to 3 basic level $25 sales for 60 to 90 days seem to be making around $20,000 to $30,000 per month.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The high numbers is due to the upsells etc.

I am currently around the 1 sale per day mark, probably a bit under, but am working hard to get to the $10,000/month milestone in the near future.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      * Build An Email List 
                     </b>
                     With Empower Network you are focused on doing just one thing: sending traffic to the lead capture page. So you are building a big email list as you go along that you can also promote other products to if you wish.

and the ability to earn 100% commissions for the basic level products as an affiliate.
                     <br/>

                     There is also a $19.95/month affiliate fee for the E-Wallet payment system to allow you to receive 100% commissions.

And because of the multi-tiered nature of the program, you not only make sales from your own efforts, but when your team members make sales then you make even more money as well!

The 2nd, 4th, 6th, 11th, and 1 in every 5 sales that your direct line team members make are passed-up a level to you for each product.

There are also upgrades available for additional training products that you can do if and when you feel comfortable to really make a big push with things.
