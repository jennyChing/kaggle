                <p>
                 (
                 <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/15-things-you-should-never-waste-your-money-on-2012-3?op=1" target="_blank">
                  Mandi Woodruff
                 </a>
                 ) The shopping mall isnt the only place where consumers blow cash on
                 <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/heres-how-to-tell-the-deals-from-the-duds-at-a-dollar-store-2011-11" target="_blank">
                  pointless purchases
                 </a>
                 . Were likely to spend money on something we dont need if we get what we think is a
                 <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/redeem-expired-groupons-2011-10" target="_blank">
                  good deal
                 </a>
                 and assume the item might be handy at some point, says Danielle Lescure, a professional organizer.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Stumped on whether its worth the cash? Ask yourself two questions:
                </p>
                <p>
                 -Does it serve a real purpose?
                </p>
                <p>
                 -Do I have somewhere to keep it?
                </p>
                <p>
                 With the items on our list, your answers had better be NO.
                </p>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Cable TV
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Cable TV" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static2.businessinsider.com/image/4f6106ad6bb3f7ef61000016-1200/cable-tv.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     Screengrab
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   With the advent of Hulu, Netflix, Amazon Instant Video,
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/the-10-best-alternatives-to-paying-for-cable-2011-9#video-game-consoles-3" target="_blank">
                    video game shortcuts
                   </a>
                   , Apple TV, and just about every major TV network posting shows online, theres hardly a reason to splurge on a fancy DVR system or even basic cableso long as youre willing to be patient.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Most shows are added at least 24-hours after airing and some networks wont give them up until eight days.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/the-10-best-alternatives-to-paying-for-cable-2011-9#vga-cables-1" target="_blank">
                    See 15 great alternatives to cable TV here.
                   </a>
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Bank Fees
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Bank Fees" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static4.businessinsider.com/image/4f3c226e6bb3f7f753000056-1200/bank-fees.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     AP Images
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Banks clearly love to slap you with fees at the drop of a hat, but that doesnt mean youve got to
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/national-bank-transfer-day-bank-of-america-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    put up with it.
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Consider going with a credit union, which are
                   <a href="http://articles.businessinsider.com/2012-01-17/news/30634303_1_balance-transfer-federal-credit-union-credit-card" target="_blank">
                    better than banks in many ways
                   </a>
                   , to avoid some of these fees, says Andrew Schrage, founder of
                   <a href="http://www.moneycrashers.com/" target="_blank">
                    MoneyCrashers.com
                   </a>
                   .
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   If you travel abroad often, make sure you use credit cards without
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/avoiding-credit-card-foreign-transaction-fees-when-abroad-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    foreign transaction fees
                   </a>
                   , otherwise youll be paying an extra 3% to 5% on all your purchases.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Extended Warranties
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Extended Warranties" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static4.businessinsider.com/image/4f3d37876bb3f78d3a000014-1200/extended-warranties.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/theyoungthousands/2482389516/sizes/m/in/photostream/">
                      Flickr / youngthousands
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Retailers push hard to sell you
                   <a href="http://articles.businessinsider.com/2012-01-03/news/30583251_1_initial-warranty-tvs-day-warranty" target="_blank">
                    extended warranties
                   </a>
                    and conveniently pump up their sales figures at the same time.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Dont do it, Schrage warns.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   The only instance Id recommend a warranty is in the case of a laptop. Otherwise, the warranties themselves can often cost as much as simply buying a used or new replacement for your item, or repairing it, he added.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  The roof over your head
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="The roof over your head" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static2.businessinsider.com/image/4f5e72426bb3f77f6200001d-1200/the-roof-over-your-head.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/dumbonyc/2911516569/sizes/m/in/photostream/">
                      Flickr / dumbonyc
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   If youre
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/renters-are-losing-more-than-a-third-of-their-paycheck-to-the-lousy-housing-market-2011-9" target="_blank">
                    blowing most of your income
                   </a>
                   on a loft in Midtown, youre making a BIG
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/even-the-experts-lose-their-homes-sometimes-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    mistake
                   </a>
                   , says Jeremy Gregg, executive director of the PLAN Fund.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   His organization provides loans to low-income
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/jami-krause-balloon-survive-on-the-road-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    entrepreneurs
                   </a>
                   , who Gregg says he often sees
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/renters-are-losing-more-than-a-third-of-their-paycheck-to-the-lousy-housing-market-2011-9" target="_blank">
                    spend more than half their income on rent
                   </a>
                   and utilities.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   The U.S. Department of Housing &amp; Urban Development recommends spending less than one-third of your income on housing.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Fancy phones
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Fancy phones" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static4.businessinsider.com/image/4f19b3fbeab8ea9a47000022-1200/fancy-phones.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/graft/1600789262/sizes/m/in/photostream/">
                      Florin Hatmanu
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Yes, it is still possible to function in society without a
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/heres-an-easy-way-to-get-your-identity-stolen-at-the-mall-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    smartphone
                   </a>
                   in the palm of your hand, Gregg says.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   We have seen people making less than $1,000/month who are spending over $100 of that monthly on a cell phone, he says. Get an affordable plan and avoid the bells and whistles phones that can be used to upsell you to a higher data plan.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    Bonus tip
                   </strong>
                   : Cut your landline at home if you never use it and put the extra cash into a fancy mobile phone, if you must.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Online Shipping
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Online Shipping" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static6.businessinsider.com/image/4f317f2cecad04f70a000044-1200/online-shipping.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/nffcnnr/6596346867/sizes/m/in/photostream/">
                      Flickr / nffcnnr
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Nearly all retailers offer some sort of option that gets your purchases to your doorstep without additional fees.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Rockstars like
                   <a href="http://www.zappos.com/" target="_blank">
                    Zappos
                   </a>
                   and
                   <a href="http://www.llbean.com/" target="_blank">
                    L.L. Bean
                   </a>
                   are among the rarest breed of businesses offering free shipping on every single purchase, says
                   <a href="http://www.nerdwallet.com/">
                    NerdWallet
                   </a>
                   CEO Tim Chen. But most companies will demand a minimum purchase.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   To help track down deals on shipping, use
                   <a href="http://www.freeshipping.org/" target="_blank">
                    Freeshipping.org
                   </a>
                   . The site stores information on expiration dates, tells you much to spend to qualify, and lets you search by store name or product.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Otherwise, check out
                   <a href="http://www.couponsherpa.com/" target="_blank">
                    CouponSherpa
                   </a>
                   or
                   <a href="http://www.retailmenot.com/" target="_blank">
                    Retailmenot
                   </a>
                   , which offer discount codes for free shipping.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Crappy Art
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Crappy Art" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="535" src="http://static2.businessinsider.com/image/4d9b4d68cadcbb2a2d630000-1200/crappy-art.jpg" width="580"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.regretsy.com/">
                      Regretsy
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Environmental designer Pablo Solomon says picking up knockoff prints and other art is a great way to blow cash for no good reason.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Nothing sends me through the roof like the art sold on cruise ships and at resorts, Solomon says. (Theyre) basically glorified posters being sold as originals.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   The best way to
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/housing-works-book-store-thrift-shops-offering-holiday-deals-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    score deals
                   </a>
                   on art is to track up and comers, he says. You can nab their art while theyre starving and desperate and laugh your way to the bank after theyve made it big.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Fast Food
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Fast Food" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static6.businessinsider.com/image/4f69e3a1eab8eaad13000038-1200/fast-food.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/icanchangethisright/3351829238/sizes/m/in/photostream/">
                      Flickr / bradleygee
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Youre only hurting yourself (and your wallet) if youre feeding yourself out of the bodega around the corner from your home or office.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   I am shocked at how many people live
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/having-the-wrong-mindset-is-keeping-you-poor-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    paycheck-to-paycheck
                   </a>
                   and yet routinely spend $10 per day on fast food and convenience store food, Gregg says.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   If youre looking for an alternative to brown-bagging it, these
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/bento-box-lunches-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    15 simple bento box lunch ideas
                   </a>
                   are sure to give you some inspiration.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Piecemeal Insurance
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Piecemeal Insurance" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static2.businessinsider.com/image/4f4c4aadeab8eaaa76000015-1200/piecemeal-insurance.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     Fox
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Buying
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/6-open-enrollment-lessons-health-care-costs-will-rise-again-2011-10" target="_blank">
                    overpriced insurance
                   </a>
                   for things like accidental death and diseases is an easy way to blow your funds.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   I knew someone who only had accidental life insurance, which didnt help much when he died of a disease, says Sally Herigstad, a certified public accountant and
                   <a href="http://www.creditcard.com/" target="_blank">
                    Creditcard.com
                   </a>
                   columnist.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Instead of buying
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/some-uk-pet-owners-are-so-desperate-theyre-killing-their-pets-for-insurance-money-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    piecemeal
                   </a>
                   insurance policies, get good term life insurance and disability insurance, she says.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Lousy gifts
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Lousy gifts " border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="375" src="http://static3.businessinsider.com/image/4f69017e6bb3f7e875000056-1200/lousy-gifts.jpg" width="500"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/jasonbrummels/5339998228/sizes/m/in/photostream/">
                      Flickr via jasonbrummels
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Personal finance expert Dani Johnson suggests you think twice before rushing out to buy Dad another tie this Christmas.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   You should make a pact with your friends and family to
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/revealed-the-secret-to-self-control-and-physical-endurance-is-giving-your-money-away-2011-9" target="_blank">
                    give back
                   </a>
                   instead, Johnson says. Pool a percentage of money you were going to spend on gifts and give a secret blessing to somebody who is truly in need.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Co-signing a loan
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Co-signing a loan" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static1.businessinsider.com/image/4f63a686ecad04564c00002f-1200/co-signing-a-loan.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:FEMA_-_38927_-_Cars_at_Cruise_ship_parking_lot_damaged_by_Hurricane_Ike.jpg">
                      Wikimedia Commons
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Before you co-sign a loan for a friend on something like a
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/heres-how-to-avoid-getting-hosed-by-a-shady-used-car-seller-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    used car
                   </a>
                   or a boat, think about the financial risks involved, says Bob Williams, vice president of Delta Trust Investments, Inc.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   If your friend gets into financial problems or loses his job, youre responsible for the loan, he says. Its not worth the risk.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   If youre unable to make payments in your pals place, you can bet it will drag your
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/5-easy-ways-to-trash-your-credit-score-2011-10" target="_blank">
                    credit score
                   </a>
                   down to the pits.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Weight Loss Traps
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="image-container slide-image">
                   <img alt="Weight Loss Traps" border="0" class="slide-image" src="http://static4.businessinsider.com/image/4e72822becad047226000004-1200/weight-loss-traps.jpg"/>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Weight loss pills and supplements marketed as miracles for overweight couch potatoes are nothing but traps.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Not only are there enough pills and potions that you could start a new one each week, but the negative effects on your health outweighs the money you will waste, says nutritionist
                   <a href="http://www.essentialnutritionforyou.com/" target="_blank">
                    Rania Batayneh
                   </a>
                   .
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Often, the testimonials in infomercials or online are false and the models sometimes never had a weight problem to begin with.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   This is a billion dollar industry and the truth is that a lean body does not come in a pill, Batayneh says.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="slide-module clearfix">
                 <h3 class="slide-title">
                  Lottery Tickets
                 </h3>
                 <div class="slide-content">
                  <div class="KonaFilter image-container slide-image">
                   <div>
                    <img alt="Lottery Tickets" border="0" class="slide-image alignright" height="300" src="http://static3.businessinsider.com/image/4f68ae1c69bedd5a59000037-1200/lottery-tickets.jpg" width="400"/>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    <span class="source">
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/pierrelaphoto/3992835307/">
                      Pierre LaScott on flickr
                     </a>
                    </span>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   Sure, you can (
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/an-insider-shares-the-secrets-of-the-state-lottery-2012-3" target="_blank">
                    buy a lotto ticket
                   </a>
                   ) every once in a while just for fun, but never make a lotto purchase with any
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/dont-fall-for-those-stupid-sweepstakes-scams-2011-11" target="_blank">
                    real expectation of winning
                   </a>
                   , Schrage warns.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   The odds are significantly stacked against you and why waste your hard-earned money on lottery tickets when you could be saving for
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/long-term-care-can-be-good-for-heart-bad-for-wallet-2011-10" target="_blank">
                    retirement
                   </a>
                   or treating yourself to a
                   <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/5-overpriced-restaurant-foods-you-should-skip-2011-10" target="_blank">
                    nice meal
                   </a>
                   ?
                  </p>
