         <strong>
          <span style="color: #c94d4d;">
           Collections and Obsessions? HmmmWhat should I call them?
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Collecting Vintage Cups and Saucers" class="aligncenter wp-image-11073 size-full" height="446" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Collecting-Vintage-Cups-and-Saucers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Collecting-Vintage-Cups-and-Saucers.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I prefer to call them collections, and if I am not careful it could be an obsession.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Vintage Sugar and Creamer Set" class="aligncenter wp-image-11055 size-full" height="705" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Vintage-Sugar-and-Creamer-Set.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Vintage-Sugar-and-Creamer-Set.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Let me tell you why. It all started when I first got married. My grandma thought every home needed a sugar bowl and a creamer. This was my first set.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Tea Cups Collection" class="aligncenter wp-image-11057 size-full" height="649" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Cups-Collection.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Cups-Collection.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Well, then I needed some cups in the same colors, right? Touches of pinks, mauve and gold trim are my fave.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Cups and Saucers Collection" class="aligncenter wp-image-11058 size-full" height="450" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Cups-and-Saucers-Collection.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Cups-and-Saucers-Collection.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         It could be an obsession because EVERY-TIME I walk into a thrift store or consignment shop, I NEED to see if there are tea cups and saucers. They pull me in. Really, they do
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Vintage Sugar Bowl" class="aligncenter wp-image-11054 size-full" height="668" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Vintage-Sugar-Bowl.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Vintage-Sugar-Bowl.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Sometimes there doesnt even have to be a match for the saucer. See what I mean?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Hand painted tea cup" class="aligncenter wp-image-11067 size-full" height="450" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Hand-painted-tea-cup.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Hand-painted-tea-cup.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         The tea cup in the front left was hand painted by the same grandma that gave me my first sugar bowl and creamer. I love the delicate flowers, dont you?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Handmade Ceramic TeaCup" class="aligncenter wp-image-11059 size-full" height="800" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Handmade-Ceramic-TeaCup.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Handmade-Ceramic-TeaCup.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         When she received Desert Rose Franciscan Ware as a gift, she made tea cups to match.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Handmade Tea Bag Saucer" class="aligncenter wp-image-11060 size-full" height="450" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Handmade-Tea-Bag-Saucer.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Handmade-Tea-Bag-Saucer.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         But, she did not stop there. She also made tea bag saucers with tiny ceramic roses. Okay-are you seeing where I MIGHT have inherited this collection or obsession?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Handmade ceramic salt and pepper shakers" class="aligncenter wp-image-11061 size-full" height="534" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Handmade-ceramic-salt-and-pepper-shakers.jpg" width="555"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Handmade-ceramic-salt-and-pepper-shakers.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Tea Cups and Saucers Collection" class="aligncenter wp-image-11062 size-full" height="433" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Cups-and-Saucers-Collection.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Cups-and-Saucers-Collection.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         AND, yes she made salt and pepper shakers to match. I know! See how I come by this honestly?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Plates Above Pantry Door" class="aligncenter wp-image-11063 size-full" height="1065" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Plates-Above-Pantry-Door.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Plates-Above-Pantry-Door.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Tea Saucer Collection" class="aligncenter wp-image-11056 size-full" height="785" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Saucer-Collection.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Saucer-Collection.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I like using tea plates and/or saucers as home decor, too.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Living Room Plates" class="aligncenter wp-image-11065 size-full" height="800" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Living-Room-Plates.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Living-Room-Plates.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Tea Saucer and Plate Gallery Wall" class="aligncenter wp-image-11064 size-full" height="881" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Saucer-and-Plate-Gallery-Wall.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Tea-Saucer-and-Plate-Gallery-Wall.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         To see how I hang my tea saucers without plate hangers, visit
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/easy-plate-gallery-wall/" target="_blank">
          My Plate Gallery Wall post
         </a>
         . It is super easy and will amaze you.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-11075 size-full" height="450" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Vintage-Tea-Saucers-as-a-Table-Top.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/collections-and-obsessions/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Vintage-Tea-Saucers-as-a-Table-Top.jpg&amp;description=Collections and Obsessions')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I am also working on a
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/thrifted-and-painted-chairs-progress-so-far-2/" target="_blank">
          dining room table top using thrifted plates and saucers
         </a>
         . I actually have been working on it for awhile (UM, years). I am hoping to have it done within the next 2 weeks. I hope you come back then to see my work.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Collections and Obsessions??? What do YOU think? In my mind, I use them because I am trying to create a cottage feel in my desert condo in Las Vegas. It works for me. And, I love each and every piece.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #c94d4d;">
          <strong>
           So, lets have more fun and visit my friends collections and design ideas, below. They each are incredibly talented and so you will enjoy every one.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Thanks for joining me today as I share myCollections and Obsessions. Make it a collecting kind of day!
        </p>
