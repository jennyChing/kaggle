        <div class="textjustify" id="highlightSection">
         <p>
          <strong>
           March 2, 2015:
          </strong>
          Toyota Kirloskar Motor Pvt. Ltd. is offering benefits upto Rs. 25,000 on the facelifted
          <strong>
           Etios Liva
          </strong>
          hatch. The benefits include, standard insurance @ Rs. 1 and genuine accessories worth Rs. 5,000. Earlier in October 2014, this minor facelifted version was launched at a at a starting price of Rs. 4.76 lac for petrol and Rs. 5.94 lac for diesel. Highlighted changes at the front - Liva comes with a new body colored grille, electrically adjustable ORVMs, chrome inserts on door handles. Speaking of the changes inside, refreshed series sports a new premium fabric upholstery; updated instrumentation cluster with a larger multi-info LCD display; seatbelt, door ajar and headlamp buzzer. Mechanically, facelifted version is unchanged; under the hood is a 1.4liter diesel and 1.2 liter diesel. The 1.4 liter D-4D oil burner chucks out 67.1bhp and 170Nm of torque mated to a five speed unit. The 1.2 liter petrol in Liva belts out 78.9bhp and 104Nm of torque mated to a five-speed unit.

        <h3>
         Overview
        </h3>
        <div class="textjustify" id="reviewSection">
         <p>
          This company is the latest among car makers to introduce the facelift version of its hatchback model,
          <strong>
           Toyota Etios Liva
          </strong>
          . It went through minor updates in terms of both exteriors and interiors, which gives it a brand new look. Its exteriors gets a newly designed radiator grille with chrome treatment. Apart from this, the remaining aspects like headlight cluster, bumper and fog lamps have been retained from the outgoing model. This hatchback has an attractive body structure featuring several styling features like 12-spoke alloy wheels, black B pillars and an expressive tailgate with chrome garnish. Although, it looks compact from outside, it has ample seating space for at least five passengers. This hatchback also gets a few updates in the form of refined refined fabric upholstery. In addition to this, its air vents surround, steering wheel and the gearshift lever gets chrome inserts, which adds to the elegance. On the other hand, it retains the same petrol and diesel engines, which are mated with a five speed manual transmission gearbox. The car maker is now offering driver's seat belt warning and dual front airbags as standard across all its variants, which enhances the safety quotient. While the other comfort and safety features have been retained from the outgoing model. The hatchback series is currently available in three variants like Base, G, V and VX for the buyers to choose from. The top end variants have been bestowed with features like a rear defogger, tachometer and an advanced 2-DIN audio system with Bluetooth connectivity. While the entry level variants have features like an air conditioning system, power windows and several storage spaces. This sedan also has safety features like engine immobilizer, an inside rear view mirror, and headlight-on notification. In addition to this, the company is offering anti lock braking system including electronic brake force distribution. However, this feature is not available in the base variant. The car maker has placed it in the premium hatchback segment, where it will face the likes of Nissan Micra, Maruti Swift, Honda Brio and Ford Figo. It is available with a standard warranty period of 100000 kilometers or 3-years (whichever is earlier).

The car maker is offering this feature as a standard fitment to all the mid and top end variants only.         Interiors
       </h3>
       <div class="textjustify" id="interiorSection">
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            Ed's take:
           </strong>
           Liva has no different interiors than the Etios sedan. Despite the shorter wheelbase, there is sufficient room for five in the car. The only disappointment is the plastic quality and the instrument cluster is at the centre.
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MarginT5">
        </p>
        <p>
         The internal cabin of this refurbished Etios Liva hatchback is bestowed with an improved fabric upholstery, which gives it a refined look. The door panels are also decorated with fabric inserts, which adds to the elegance of interiors. In addition to this, the dashboard, AC vent surround, steering wheel and gearshift lever gets chrome inserts. The top end VX and VX D variants also have inside door panels with a new wood finish, which further adds to its elegance. The seats have been retained from the outgoing model, which have integrated headrests and armrest.
         <strong>
          Apart from the entry level trim, the other variants have height adjustment facility to driver's seat.
         </strong>
         While the rear bench seat is foldable, which helps to increase the boot storage capacity. Its dual tone dashboard is equipped with features like a 2-DIN music system, an air conditioning unit and a few storage spaces. In addition to this, its three spoke steering wheel has silver inserts along with chrome plated company's badge. The top end variant gets it with a leather cover and houses control switches for audio and Bluetooth. The instrument cluster is positioned above the center fascia, which has blue illuminated background. It has a speedometer, tachometer, digital tripmeter and a few other warning lamps. Furthermore, it is now incorporated with notifications for driver's seatbelt, door ajar, and headlamp on. Its cabin has a lot of leg, shoulder and head space as it is designed with a long wheelbase and width.

          Interior Comfort:
         </strong>

Its top end VX trim is now bestowed with rear parking sensors along with power adjustable wing mirrors. The base trim has features like electric steering column with tilt adjustment, cooled glove box, front cabin lights, digital tripmeter, seven bottle holders, a day and night inside rear view mirror and door pockets. Furthermore, they also have dual front sun visors, an air conditioning system, assist grip with coat hook and remote operation for opening of tailgate and fuel flap. The mid range G and V variants also have aspects like power windows with driver's side auto-down function, central locking system, 12V power socket, adjustable headrests, digital tripmeter, height adjustable driver's seat and passenger's side vanity mirror. On the other hand, the top end VX and VXD variants also has aspects like a multi-functional steering wheel, tachometer, rear defogger and rear wiper with washer.

          Interior Measurements:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <br/>

It has a large 251-litre boot compartment, which can be extended by folding rear bench seat. It is also fitted with a huge fuel tank, which has 45-litre storage capacity.
