       <p>
        Bono, 54, made the admission to Graham Norton on his BBC1 talk show when asked whether he ever removes his trademark eyewear. The fact is, the Irish rocker hasn't just been dressing for the part, he's been dealing with a sensitivity to light.
       </p>
       <div class="inline-img-container">
        <figure class="entry-img retina-enabled">
         <img alt="Image: Bono" src="http://media1.s-nbcnews.com/j/streams/2014/October/141017/1D274907022330-141017-bono-tease.today-inline-large.jpg" title=""/>
         <small class="entry-img-credit">
          Richard Bord/Getty Images file
         </small>
         <div class="entry-img-caption">
          Bono attends the Cannes Film Festival in Cannes, France, on June 21.
         </div>
        </figure>
       </div>
       <p>
        "This is a good place to explain to people that Ive had glaucoma for the last 20 years," Bono said,
        <a href="http://www.theguardian.com/music/2014/oct/17/bono-glaucoma-20-years-u2-dark-glasses" target="_blank">
         according to The Guardian
        </a>
        . "I have good treatments and I am going to be fine."
       </p>
       <div class="widget youtube">
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/9foFAoWUucw">
        </iframe>
       </div>
       <p>
        The singer then added, "Youre not going to get this out of your head now and you will be saying, Ah, poor old blind Bono.'" (Actually, everyone is probably too distracted
        <a href="http://www.nbcnews.com/tech/social-media/oops-u2s-bono-apologizes-automatic-songs-innocence-downloads-n225986" target="_blank">
         trying to get his band's free album
        </a>
        out of their iTunes).
       </p>
       <div class="widget youtube">
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/9foFAoWUucw">
        </iframe>
       </div>
       <p>
        "I just think they're cool," Robert Downey Jr., another guest on Norton's show, chimed in. "And I think Bono looks kill in them."
       </p>
       <p>
        Glaucoma is a
        <a href="http://www.nei.nih.gov/health/glaucoma/glaucoma_facts.asp#a" target="_blank">
         chronic eye condition
        </a>
        in which pressure can cause damage to the optic nerve resulting in vision loss and blindness.
       </p>
       <p>
        On stage, in front of world leaders, on the cover of Time magazine and anywhere else he has been photographed over the decades, Bono is always shading his eyes behind varying styles of sunglasses. Take a closer look at his unforgettable style.
       </p>
