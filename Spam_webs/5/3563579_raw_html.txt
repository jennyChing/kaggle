        <h1 class="single-title" itemprop="headline">
         Addicted To Life
        </h1>
        <p class="meta">
         <time datetime="2013-09-16" pubdate="">
          September 16th, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <a href="http://lifestyledezine.com/author/christopher-rivas/" rel="author" title="Posts by Christopher Rivas">
          Christopher Rivas
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="meta post-categories">
         Posted In:
         <span class="category-inside-color">
          <a href="http://lifestyledezine.com/category/uncategorized/">
           General
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
       </header>
       <!-- end article header -->
       <section class="post-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <div class="wwsgd" style="display:none;">
         <p style="border:thin dotted black; padding:3mm;">
          If you're new here, you may want to subscribe to my
          <a href="http://lifestyledezine.com/feed/">
           RSS feed
          </a>
          . Thanks for visiting!
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://lifestyledezine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/precessed-beauty.jpg?de847d">
           <span class="responsive-image-wrap aligncenter" data-facebook="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?s=100&amp;p[url]=http%3A%2F%2Flifestyledezine.com%2Fsee-reflection-god-every-person%2F&amp;p[images][0]=http%3A%2F%2Flifestyledezine.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F09%2Fprecessed-beauty.jpg&amp;p[title]=Addicted+To+Life" data-pinterest="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Flifestyledezine.com%2Fsee-reflection-god-every-person%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Flifestyledezine.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F09%2Fprecessed-beauty.jpg&amp;description=Addicted+To+Life" data-twitter="http://twitter.com/home?status=Addicted+To+Life%20-%20http%3A%2F%2Flifestyledezine.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F09%2Fprecessed-beauty.jpg-%20via%20%40lifestyledezine">
            <img alt="processed beauty" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5513 responsive-img" src="http://lifestyledezine.com/assets/img/3_2.gif?de847d"/>
            <noscript class="responsivity" data-lazy-med-src="http://lifestyledezine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/precessed-beauty.jpg?de847d" data-lazy-mob-src="http://lifestyledezine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/precessed-beauty-450x638.jpg?de847d" data-lazy-src="http://lifestyledezine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/precessed-beauty.jpg?de847d">
             <img alt="processed beauty" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5513" height="300" src="http://lifestyledezine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/precessed-beauty-211x300.jpg?de847d" width="211"/>
            </noscript>
           </span>
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          See the reflection of God in each and every person.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          The greatest manifestation of the miracle of God is life. Paulo Coelho
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         We think that money, power and prestige are all that matters. We think that narcissism is natural and right. We believe that oppressing/blaming someone else will make the truth we know about our own 
         <del>
          reality
         </del>
         , our own shortcomings and errors go away! Because we believe that without converting others to our way we must be wrong.
         <b>
          Because we dont believe in anything really
         </b>
         , so we must make another believe in our way.
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont want to convert you to anything,
         <strong>
          I do want you to join me
         </strong>
         <strong>
          in being addicted to life
         </strong>
         , to being child-like, open, receptive, loving life, loving freedom, being alive. Why? So that all of us can appreciate the Miracle of Life, a little bit more. So that all of us can share the mystery and magic of life with each other and everyone else. So that all of us can join together to find the path to knowing through caring for each other, creating and discovering with each other. So that each of us can live lives of meaning, purpose and passion. Your way is great, her way is great, and my way is great if we all are on the way to playing, enlarging, sharing and enjoying LIFE a little more each day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #339966;">
          <strong>
           Peace and Blessings
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
       </section>
