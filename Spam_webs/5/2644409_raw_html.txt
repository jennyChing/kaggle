        Invisible Girlfriend: the website that fakes your relationship
       </h1>
       <h2 itemprop="alternativeHeadline description">
        Being single isn't always easy  but employing a company to create a false 
  girlfriend for you is beyond the pale, says Helen Coffey
       </h2>

        <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="firstPar">
          <p>
           Weve all been there. Youre at a family party, say, or a friends engagement bash, having a smashing time. Youre doing your thing, youre grooving your groove, and all is right with the world. Until, that is, you have the brutal moment of realisation: every single person is, at some stage during proceedings, going to ask if youre seeing anyone special.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           Its a low point.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">
          <p>
           Now, you can either be totally honest here, giving a run-down of your recent failed experiments in the murky waters of romance  encounters with people who have Friends episode-style monikers rather than actual names, like The One Who Left the Bar Every Five Minutes to Chain Smoke, The One Who Talked Incessantly About What Our Children Would Look Like, and The One Who Described Various Episodes of Breaking Bad in Detail Even Though Id Already Seen Them.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           Or, you can go for a playful, upbeat line such as, Oh, Im just between girl/boyfriends right now, giving the kind of overly hearty, barking laugh that makes you sound like youre playing a retired colonel in a wartime drama.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           Based on these options then, the idea of a service that helps you to fake a relationship is perhaps the tiniest bit tempting. Imagine just being able to say yes to that question, with a warm, confident smile, flashing a picture of your beloved before smoothly segueing into a description of your amazing job/incredible flat/plans to run three ultra marathons back to back in aid of canine epilepsy.
          </p>
         </div>

         <div class="body">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.invisiblegirlfriend.com" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             Invisiblegirlfriend.com
            </strong>
           </a>
           (and
           <a href="https://invisibleboyfriend.com/" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             invisibleboyfriend.com
            </strong>
           </a>
           ) is a new site and corresponding app that was launched to the public yesterday. Although still officially in 'beta', it claims to provide social and real world proof that youre in a relationship  so that, in their words, you can get back to living life on your own terms. There are various packages ranging from Just talking to Getting serious to Almost engaged, each involving different levels of service. So far, so intriguing.
          </p>
          <p>
           But then things start to get a little more, well  creepy.
          </p>
          <p>
           Create your own girlfriend, the home page instructs, sounding more like a command from the Mad Scientists Handbook than a fun suggestion. Youll give her a name, choose her age and photo and create her personality.
          </p>
          <p>
           The site also helps you create a backstory for your relationship and provides a real-time interactive texting service. Text your invisible girlfriend, and shell text you right back! it promises. Woah. S*** just got real.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="" declared-caption="" height="334" name="Untitled-1" photographer="" refid="3171478" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03171/Untitled-1_3171478a.jpg" width="460"/>
           <strong>
            <sup>
             A screenshot from the Invisible Girlfriend website
            </sup>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Is this texting business honestly to provide you with digital proof of your girlfriends existence? Take a moment to consider how frequently your friends hold out their phones, smugly crowing, Look, a text! A text from my girlfriend! I have an actual girlfriend, see? Read it! READ IT. (If the answer is anything other than never, you should probably get new friends.)
          </p>
          <p>
           The whole thing is weirdly reminiscent of the virtual relationships which are becoming increasingly popular in Japan. As the countrys birth rate continues to shrink, more and more Japanese men are conducting online romances with what are essentially intelligent computer games. Known as otaku, this generation of men, obsessed with their own fantasy worlds, often rejects the idea of a flesh and blood relationship  because who wants to deal with a real woman and all her real problems, when you could just make up the perfect girlfriend instead?
          </p>
          <p>
           Is that the stage were entering now  is invisiblegirlfriend.com the reimagining of this virtual dating escapism for the West? After all, however much you dress up the idea, we all know that many of the people who actually end up using the service will be those who are the most lonely and vulnerable. People who just want someone to message them at the end of the day, even if they know its not real.
          </p>
          <p>
           For the rest of us, while the idea might be faintly alluring, we know it would never work. When do we get to meet her? your mum asks. Er  you reply. Game over.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, I say, dont fake it 'til you make it  just accept that being single sometimes is a natural part of life. Own it. Embrace it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Or take a blow-up doll with you everywhere and pretend you think its your girlfriend like in that Ryan Gosling film, because guaranteed no one will ask you about your love life ever again.
          </p>

         Read Next Widget

        var readNextWidget = (function(){

            var settings = {
                articleImage: "",
                articleTitle: "",
                articleUrl: "#",
                articleId: "",
                currentArticleId: "",
                articleList: [],
                articleListToSkip: [],
                readNextCookieName: "tmg_read_next",
                openButton: "#readNextOpen a",
                closeButton: "#closeBtn a",
                articleContainer: "#readNextArticleContainer",
                widgetContainer: "#readNextWidget",
                displayThumbnail: false,
                panelOpen: false,
                autoOpen: true
            };

            var renderTemplate = function() {
                var template = '&lt;div id="readNextWidget" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextOpen"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div id="readNextOpenInner"&gt;&lt;a href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Next Story&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span class="icn"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextArticleContainer" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticle"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleInnerWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;p id="readNextLabel"&gt;MOST VIEWED IN NEWS&lt;/p&gt;';
                        
				if(settings.articleImage &amp;&amp; settings.articleImage!="null" &amp;&amp; settings.displayThumbnail){
					template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageThumbnail" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;img src="'+ settings.articleImage +'"/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;';
				}
                        
				template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageTitle" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;' + settings.articleTitle + '&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="closeBtn"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;a href="#"&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
