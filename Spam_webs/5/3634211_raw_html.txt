         <h1 class="title_smaller">
          Video: Because Everyone Loves a New Star Wars App
         </h1>
         <div class="submitted">
          <span class="posted_date">
           Posted 07/09/2010 at 5:49am
          </span>
          <span class="posted_by">

          </div>
          <span class="st_facebook_hcount" displaytext="Share" st_title="Video: Because Everyone Loves a New Star Wars App" st_url="http://www.maclife.com/article/news/video_because_everyone_loves_new_%E2%80%9Cstar_wars%E2%80%9D_app">
          </span>
          <span class="st_twitter_hcount" displaytext="Tweet" st_title="Video: Because Everyone Loves a New Star Wars App" st_url="http://www.maclife.com/article/news/video_because_everyone_loves_new_%E2%80%9Cstar_wars%E2%80%9D_app">
          </span>
          <span class="st_sharethis_button" displaytext="Share" st_title="Video: Because Everyone Loves a New Star Wars App" st_url="http://www.maclife.com/article/news/video_because_everyone_loves_new_%E2%80%9Cstar_wars%E2%80%9D_app">
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="blue_line">
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="Star Wars: The Battle of Hoth game" height="257" src="/files/u220903/Star_Wars_-_The_Battle_of_Hoth_game_380px.jpg" width="380"/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         If you havent gotten enough of
         <em>
          Star Wars
         </em>
         on your iDevice (or really, life in general), theres yet another game based on the sci-fi movies winging its way to the App Store next week.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://toucharcade.com/2010/07/08/upcoming-tower-defense-star-wars-the-battle-of-hoth-gameplay-video-revealed/" target="_blank">
          touchArcade is reporting
         </a>
         that a new
         <em>
          Star Wars
         </em>
         game is coming, this time based on one of the key set pieces from the original second chapter,
         <em>
          The Empire Strikes Back
         </em>
         . The game is called
         <em>
          Star Wars: The Battle of Hoth
         </em>
         , scheduled for release on July 15 for a mere $2.99.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As you probably already know if youre even a modest
         <em>
          Star Wars
         </em>
         fan, Hoth is the snow planet where the first act of
         <em>
          The Empire Strikes Back
         </em>
         takes place and based on the YouTube video embedded below, the new game appears to be faithful to the big battle scene from the film, complete with Empire-driven AT-AT Walkers, TIE Fighters and Rebel Snowspeeders.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Gameplay looks fairly standard for a tower defense game, touchArcade writes after previewing
         <em>
          The Battle of Hoth
         </em>
         , and aside from all kinds of
         <em>
          Star Wars
         </em>
         units, it also sports some cut scenes from the movie. Needless to say, as a huge closet
         <em>
          Star Wars
         </em>
         nerd, I cannot wait for this game to get released.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         July 15 is right around the corner, so the wait for
         <em>
          Star Wars: The Battle of Hoth
         </em>
         will be a short one. Get your $2.99 ready now!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Follow this articles author,
