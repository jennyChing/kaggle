       <br/>
       IT organizations are continuously challenged with protecting business-critical applications and data against various causes of downtime. VMware provides you with better flexibility to protect your business information in VMware vSphere environments, whether you choose to build your own solution, subscribe to disaster recovery services on VMware vCloud Hybrid Service or use a combination of both options to meet your specific requirements. Built on the foundation of vSphere, our solutions enable you to simplify and automate your data protection plans for your virtualized applications and infrastructure.
