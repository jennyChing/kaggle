        Well its happened, I am now the proud mother of a high school student. With it comes parties, school functions, after school clubs and activities and of course.the boyfriend. Okay, they have been together since 8th grade, but now they are going to different schools so they dont get to see each other as often as they like.
        <p>
         Because of all of the above and more, she needs a good phone and the
         <a href="http://www.walmart.com/cp/Family-Mobile/1076544" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Cheap(est) Wireless Plans
         </a>
         in order to keep in touch with friends, mom and dad and the boyfriend. She has been with Walmart Family Mobile for a few months now and loves the service, but it was time for a phone upgrade. It turns out that the students are allowed to use their phones for research as long as they ONLY use them for school work and only when the teacher tells them to. For this reason, she needed a great smartphone to go with the reliable service she gets from Walmart Family Mobile.
        </p>

        </p>

         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

          </span>
         </span>

        After doing some research and looking around, she decided on theAlcatel One Touch Fierce whichis on rollback from $129.88 to a cool $99.88 which put it right in her price range. The large 4.5 display is perfect for helping her with her research and of course playing games and chatting with friends. So far she is loving the phone. As she puts it, this phone is fierce mom! And with
        <a href="http://www.walmart.com/cp/Family-Mobile/1076544" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Unlimited Talk Text and Data/Web
        </a>
        at only $34.88, she wont have to work too hard to pay her bill. Yes, SHE pays her own bill.
        <p>
         Now that she has a great phone, I dont have to worry about her when she is at an after school club, which she has joined several and hey, its high school, there will be football games and dances and OMG, am I ready for this? Did I mention that her boyfriend is on the football team at his school? Yeah, I am guessing she will want to go to EVERY one of his games. Will I ever see my beautiful daughter again outside of meal time? Sure I will, but not sure if I will ever be able to get the phone out of her hands.
