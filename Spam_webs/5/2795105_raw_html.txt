               <p>
                Featuring your website within the top pages of any search engine is virtually a dream for every user who has ever opened a website on the World Wide Web. Popularly known as Search Engine Optimization, SEO is the most important tool to feature your site among the top players and gives you the traffic you have been wishing for.
               </p>
               <p>
                Let us surf through some of basic tips which can guide your site for high search engine rank:
               </p>
               <h2>
                1. Search engine accessible site
               </h2>
               <p>
                Your site should be accessible to the search engines. The search engines spiders should be able to interpret the content on your web pages.
               </p>
               <h2>
                2. Well-designed site
               </h2>
               <p>
                Search engines cannot perceive web pages the way we humans do, so limitations tend to exist while indexing. A well constructed website can sort this problem out, strengthening the rankings.
               </p>
               <h2>
                3. Domain selection
               </h2>
               <p>
                When assigning a domain name for your site, the domain name should be a dot-com, be easy to type and remember, possess top searched keywords and it should uniquely brand your product.
               </p>
               <h2>
                4. Relevant keywords
               </h2>
               <p>
                Search engines face a tough task of indexing the whole of the web page for the content. Uncovering popular words or lines used on your site for the product or services you offer can make it easier for the search engines, which in turn benefits you.
               </p>
               <h2>
                5. Linking of sites
               </h2>
               <p>
                The whole scenario of SEO today revolves around the link popularity. The greater number of links you possess the better chances you have for higher rankings.
               </p>
               <h2>
                6. Anchored texts
               </h2>
               <p>
                Linking your site externally or internally should always include anchored texts, such as the name of the relevant linking text instead of words like click here. Using anchor texts for linking are beneficial for SEO.
               </p>
               <h2>
                7. Site age
               </h2>
               <p>
                Search engines tend to value sites that have been around for a while; such sites are usually spam free and thus rank higher.
               </p>
               <h2>
                8. Meta tags
               </h2>
               <p>
                Meta tags are the list of keywords you wish to associate with a page. Great Meta work adds to your site; they can be tough to write but can drive a considerable amount of traffic.
               </p>
               <h2>
                9. XML sitemaps
               </h2>
               <p>
                Using sitemap protocol you can supply the search engines a list of all the URLs you would like them to crawl and index. It results in crawling of the pages that were otherwise not ignored.
               </p>
               <h2>
                10. Heading (H1, H2, and H3) tags
               </h2>
               <p>
                The heading tags are designed to indicate headline hierarchy in a document. Search engines have shown slight preference for keywords appearing in heading tags.
               </p>
               <h2>
                11. Image filenames
               </h2>
               <p>
                Use of images can enrich user experience, however, search engines cannot read the images directly. Provide an alt text or a file name to it.
               </p>
               <h2>
                12. Unique content
               </h2>
               <p>
                While posting new information on the site you should keep in mind about the plagiarism extent of the post. Your content should be different from others in more than just replacement key words and nouns.
               </p>
               <p>
                Improving a sites rank is never an easy job; however these points will boost up the ranking to significant levels. Are there any tips that I missed that you use? Lets discuss in the comments below.
               </p>
