        <p>
         Here are some of the most grueling incidents that have occurred at Six Flags parks nationwide. There are many other
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/crazy-semi-accident-right-in-front-of-this-guys-camera-this-would-be-terrifying-to-see-coming-at-you/" title="Crazy Semi Accident Right In front Of This Guys Camera. This would Be Terrifying To See Coming At You!">
          accidents
         </a>
         that have happened but these were the ones we found the worst. Six Flags actually purchases some used rides and roller coasters to save themselves money.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          June 21, 2007, a 13-year-old girl lost both her
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/22-interesting-fun-facts-about-the-human-body/" title="22 Interesting Fun Facts About The Human Body">
           feet
          </a>
          during a Superman: Tower Of Power ride when the cable snapped and whipped around severing both her feet.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/superman.jpg">
          <img alt="superman" class="aligncenter wp-image-1432" height="678" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/superman.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          On March 21, 1999 a
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/woman-misses-her-ferry-decides-to-have-a-slight-mental-fit/" title="Woman Misses Her Ferry, Decides To Have A Slight Mental Fit!">
           woman
          </a>
          on the Roaring Rapids water park ride drowned when her inflatable raft deflated, overturned and trapped her underneath.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/roar.jpg">
          <img alt="roar" class="aligncenter wp-image-1438" height="317" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/roar.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          June 12, 2007, a 14-year-old
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/girl-slides-down-escalator-and-smashing-her-face-at-the-bottom/" title="Girl Slides Down Escalator And Smashing Her Face At The Bottom">
           girl
          </a>
          , riding the Poltergeist, was paralyzed after she fell into a gap between the roller coasters
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/all-fun-and-games-until-a-giraffe-sticks-his-head-in-your-car-and-does-this/" title="All Fun And Games Until A Giraffe Sticks His Head In Your Car And Does This">
           cars
          </a>
          .
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/polt.jpg">
          <img alt="polt" class="aligncenter wp-image-1433" height="359" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/polt.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li>
          May 11, 1984, eight teenage visitors were trapped inside the Haunted Castle attractionwhen a fire broke out, they all lost their lives in the event.Six Flags Great Adventure and its parent company Six Flags were subsequently indicted for aggravated manslaughter, accused of recklessly causing the
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/some-of-the-most-bizarre-deaths-you-ever-heard/" title="Some Of The Most Bizarre Deaths You Ever Heard!">
           deaths
          </a>
          by taking inadequate precautions against a fire.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/haunted.jpg">
          <img alt="haunted" class="aligncenter wp-image-1434" height="336" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/haunted.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          In 1978, a 20-year-old woman died after falling out of the Colossus ride.The lap bar was locked in place but due to the womans
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/unfortunate-and-disturbing-facts-of-being-overweight-or-obese/" title="Unfortunate And Disturbing Facts Of Being Overweight &amp; Obese">
           obesity
          </a>
          it proved to be ineffective.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/coi1.jpg">
          <img alt="coi1" class="aligncenter wp-image-1437" height="314" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/coi1.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          In May 2009, four children became ill while riding the Wheelie. The ridefailed to stop at the end of its cycle. After returning to a horizontal position, a limit switch failed and the
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/take-a-ride-on-one-of-the-most-terrifying-roller-coasters-ever/" title="Take A Ride On One Of The Most Terrifying Roller Coasters Ever!">
           ride
          </a>
          continued to spin for five to ten minutes.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/wheel.jpg">
          <img alt="wheel" class="aligncenter wp-image-1436" height="373" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/wheel.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
