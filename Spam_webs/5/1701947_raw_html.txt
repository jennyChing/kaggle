           <p>
            When you think of a romantic hotel, what are the first images that come to mind? For most people, romantic hotels are private villas overlooking the ocean or luxury boutique hotels. While these places are great, most of them have one huge disadvantage: a huge price tag.
           </p>
           <p>
            The truth is,
            <strong>
             you dont need to pay a fortune to find a romantic place to stay
            </strong>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Whether youre staying in a hostel in Budapest or one of the
            <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=KYKU7zoyhuI&amp;subid=&amp;offerid=184383.1&amp;type=10&amp;tmpid=4710&amp;RD_PARM1=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.hostelbookers.com%2Fhotels%2Ffrance%2Fparis%2F" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="budget hotels and hostels in Paris">
             great budget hotels in Paris
            </a>
            , its entirely possible to have a romantic night without emptying your wallet.
           </p>

           <h2>
            Htel Taylor, le-de-France, France
           </h2>
           <p>
            Paris is known as the city of love for a reason. The
            <a href="http://www.booking.com/hotel/fr/taylor.html?aid=361881%20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Hotel Taylor">
             Htel Taylor
            </a>
            is a shortten minute walk from the Marais district, and close to attractions like thePlace des Vosges, the Bastille and Canal Saint Martin. The closest station is Jacques Bonsergent which is only a few minutes walk away. A buffet breakfast is also available in the small dining room.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Your room:
            </strong>
            A superior or classic double with private bathroom
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Cost:
            </strong>
            About $110 USD for a classic double, breakfast not included
           </p>

             <strong>
              Your room
             </strong>
             : Double with private bathroom
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Cost
             </strong>

            </p>

            <h2>
             Aonang Phu Petra Resort  Krabi, Thailand
            </h2>
            <p>
             Overlooking Aonang Beach in Krabi, Thailand, the
             <a href="http://www.booking.com/hotel/th/aonang-phu-petra-resort-krabi.en-gb.html?aid=361881" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Aonang Phu Petra ">
              Aonang Phu Petra
             </a>
             is a 4 star hotel with great amenities like a swimming pool, cooking classes and massage.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Your room
             </strong>
             : A hillside villa with private bathroom
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Cost
             </strong>
             : About $120 USD
            </p>

            <h2>
             Crusoes Retreat, Coral Coast, Fiji
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.booking.com/hotel/th/aonang-phu-petra-resort-krabi.en-gb.html?aid=361881" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Crusoe's Retreat ">
              Crusoes Retreat
             </a>
             is a tropical resort with a sandy beach and lagoon. Rooms are in individually decorated Fijian-inspired Bures, and include complimentary activities like snorkeling, sea kayaking, and a kava ceremony.
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Your room
            </strong>
            : Sea view bungalow with private bathroom
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Cost
            </strong>
            : About $90 USD
           </p>
           <h2>
            Our criteria for this list
           </h2>
           <p>
            To make this list, all rooms had to have that special something that makes them romantic. In some cases, old world charm and a cozy bedroom was enough. Others made the list because of their location next to a gorgeous beach or because they happen to be in a romantic city.
           </p>
           <p>
            No matter what, to be truly romantic, all rooms need to be private, be special in their own way and come with a private washroom.
