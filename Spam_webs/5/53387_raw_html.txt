          <p>
           Hyderabad, IND 25-MAY-2015  Jabaj Publications is pleased to announce the launch of a new paperback book written by the Jabaj expert team. The volume is titled Banking Awareness and it sells for Rs. 39 ($. 614). The English-language banking awareness India setting book is a must for anyone who needs to know the basis for the banking system in India. Eighteen chapters offer a wealth of information about the financial system in the country.
          </p>
          <p>
           The book begins with a discussion of the overall Indian financial system and describes commercial, retail and agricultural banking in additional chapters. Development Banks are another topic which is covered in the pages of the helpful volume. The relationship between bankers and customers is discussed so that readers have a better understanding of how the entire system operates in India.
          </p>
          <p>
           An understanding of the applicable terms related to banking and the regulations which apply. The overview of banking includes the role and function of the Exchange Board of India and of Capital Market Security is described in detail, as well as the role and capabilities of IRDA and of mutual funds. Readers who need to know about banking sector reforms in India or about credit management and the banks lending practices will find answers in the book. These and other topics of interest will lead to a fairly comprehensive understanding of banks and banking in India.
          </p>
          <p>
           Books may be ordered through the online website. Orders are fulfilled within one to three business days through India Post VPP Service. The postal worker will deliver the book and collect the cost of the book. Delivery occurs in approximately seven to ten business days.
          </p>
