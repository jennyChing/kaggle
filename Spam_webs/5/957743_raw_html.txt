         <p>
          Sometimes you just want to eat Smores without all the hassle of going on a camping trip. Am I right or am I right? Well, when that Smores craving comes over you and you are nowhere near a campfire, here is your solution:
          <strong>
           Smores Bars.
          </strong>
          These are kind oflike making Rice Krispies treats, except you use Golden Grahams cereal. And chocolate, of course!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.momalwaysfindsout.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Smores-Bars-Recipe-with-Golden-Grahams.jpg">
           <img alt="Smores-Bars-Recipe-with-Golden-Grahams" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19305" height="903" src="http://www.momalwaysfindsout.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Smores-Bars-Recipe-with-Golden-Grahams.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          These Smores bars are an easy no-bake dessert. I used milk chocolate chips, but if you prefer semisweet chocolate chips, go ahead and use them  they will taste great either way.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Smores Bars Recipe
          </strong>
          <span id="more-19301">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ingredients:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           6 cups Golden Grahams cereal
          </li>
          <li>
           1.5 cups Chocolate Chips
          </li>
          <li>
           5 cups mini marshmallows
          </li>
          <li>
           1/4 cup Light Karo syrup
          </li>
          <li>
           4 Tablespoons Butter
          </li>
          <li>
           1 tsp Vanilla
          </li>
          <li>
           additional 1-2 cups mini marshmallows
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          Directions:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           Grease a 139 inch pan. Measure out 6 cups Golden Grahams cereal into a large bowl and set aside.
          </li>
          <li>
           In a large saucepan, melt butter, vanilla, light Karo syrup, chocolate chips and 5 cups mini marshmallows on low heat, stirring constantly.
          </li>
          <li>
           Once marshmallow/chocolate mixture is completely melted, pour mixture over Golden Grahams cereal and stir until the cereal is evenly coated. Stir in1-2 cups of mini marshmallows. I like a lot of marshmallows in mine, so I added about 2 cups!
          </li>
          <li>
           Press mixture evenly into the 139 inch pan. Allow to cool for one hour until set. Cut into bars. Store at room temperature in an airtight container.
          </li>
         </ol>
