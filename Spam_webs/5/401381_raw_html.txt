        <h1 class="entry-title">
         This Guy Got A McDonalds Receipt Tattooed On His Arm
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-03-27T09:49:46+00:00">
          March 27, 2014
         </span>
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a class="fn n" href="http://www.tydknow.com/author/kickar/" rel="author" title="Richard Mertz">
            Richard Mertz
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://www.tydknow.com/did-you-know-that-a-customer-of-mcdonalds-had-a-receipt-tattooed-on-his-arm-just-because/#comments">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          There is probably nothing worse in life than losing a bet to your best friends. Recently, one 18-year-old teenager in Norway named Stian Ytterdal was punished by his best buddies in a more than cruel but unique manner. Because Ytterdal was way too active with the ladies according to his friends, he was given an option.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10723" style="width: 460px">
          <img alt="Image Source: YOUTUBE" class="lazy size-full wp-image-10723" data-original="http://d1wd8l4xj8vlky.cloudfront.net//wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Screenshot-from-2014-03-27-114705.png" height="262" src="http://d1wd8l4xj8vlky.cloudfront.net//wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="450"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
          </p>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Image Source: YOUTUBE" class="size-full wp-image-10723" height="262" src="http://d1wd8l4xj8vlky.cloudfront.net//wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Screenshot-from-2014-03-27-114705.png" width="450"/>
          </noscript>
          Image Source: YOUTUBE
         </div>
         <p>
          Either get a
          <strong>
           tattoo
          </strong>
          of Barbie on his rear cheeks or a special
          <strong>
           McDonalds receipt on his arm
          </strong>
          . Since Ytterdal is a regular customer of McDonalds he chose the second option. Now Ytterdal has a huge tattoo on his arm that depicts one of his recent purchases of a nonstop Flurry a soda and a cheeseburger of course. Because the choice of a tattoo was so rare the tattoo artist actually did it for free. The reasoning behind Ytterdals decision to actually do it is entirely unknown but lets hope hes lovin it.
         </p>
