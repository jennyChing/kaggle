             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2015/07/los-angeles-4th-of-july-fireworks-and.html">
               Los Angeles: 4th of July Fireworks and Celebrations
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-K_j0GReyQqw/VZNqwYhEfnI/AAAAAAAAByI/ytDvFIEGiX8/s1600/4th%2Bof%2BJuly%2BAmericafest%2Bat%2BRosebowl%2B2015%2B330x180-68kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="174" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-K_j0GReyQqw/VZNqwYhEfnI/AAAAAAAAByI/ytDvFIEGiX8/s320/4th%2Bof%2BJuly%2BAmericafest%2Bat%2BRosebowl%2B2015%2B330x180-68kb.jpg" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 4rh of July fireworks - Americafest at the Rose Bowl
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
               If youre in the Los Angeles area and havent yet decided where to celebrate the 4th of July, here are five Independence Day parties to consider for 2015.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.rosebowlstadium.com/events/americafest" target="_blank">
                 Americafest at the Rose Bowl
                </a>
               </strong>
               <br/>
               See the largest fireworks show in Southern California at the 89th Annual Americafest at the Rose Bowl. It features food, games, inflatable rides for kids, motorcycle stunts and a special performance from the Grammy nominated Beatles Tribute Band, Liverpool Legends. At the conclusion of the fireworks show, there will be a viewing of movie, Despicable Me 2.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Where:
               </strong>
               The Rose Bowl Stadium, 1001 Rose Bowl Dr., Pasadena, CA, 91103
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Time:
               </strong>
               Stadium parking opens at 9am; festival starts at 2pm; fireworks start at 9pm.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Cost:
               </strong>
               $13 General Admission (5yrs &amp; under free); $25 Reserved Seating.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Parking:
               </strong>
               $25 per car
               <br/>
               <strong>
                More Info:
               </strong>
               626-577-3100
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.lacoliseum.com/index.php/event/4th-of-july-extravaganza/" target="_blank">
                 4th of July Extravaganza at Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum
                </a>
               </strong>
               <br/>
               Bring your blanket or lawn chair and enjoy a fireworks extravaganza at the LA Memorial Coliseum. Viewing is from Christmas Tree Lane on the South Lawn of Exposition Park. There will be food, rides, games, sponsor exhibits and musical performances.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Where
               </strong>
               : Exposition Park, 700 Exposition Park Dr., Los Angeles, CA, 90037
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Time:
               </strong>
               12pm - 10pm. Fireworks at dusk (around 9pm).
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Cost:
               </strong>
               Free
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Parking:
               </strong>
               $10 per car in Exposition Park
               <br/>
               <strong>
                More Info:
               </strong>
               Peter Bellas 310-914-0015
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://palisadesparade.org/index.php" target="_blank">
                 Palisades Americanism Parade
                </a>
               </strong>
               <br/>
               The Palisades Americanism Parade is one of the top Independence Day parades in the U.S.A. and has been around for 67 years. This years parade Grand Marshal is KFI AM 640 radio host Bill Handel. Following the parade, head to Palisades Charter High School for a family-friendly concert, followed by spectacular fireworks. Additional activities include a kids fun zone and gourmet food trucks.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Where:
               </strong>
               Parade is on Sunset Blvd. between de la Paz and Drummond St. in Pacific Palisades. Entertainment and fireworks at Palisades Charter High School located at 15777 Bowdoin Street, Pacific Palisades, CA 90272.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Time:
               </strong>
               Skydivers 1:50pm, parade 2pm,entertainment 6:30pm,fireworks 9pm.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Cost:
               </strong>
               Parade is free. Concert and fireworks tickets are $10. (12yrs and under free).
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Parking:
               </strong>
               Limited free parking available at the Palisades Charter High School lot.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                More Info:
               </strong>
               Keith Turner 310-454-6190 or Rob Weber 310-595-3009.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.grandparkla.org/" target="_blank">
                 4th of July Block Party at Grand Park
                </a>
               </strong>
               <br/>
               Grand Park in LAs Civic Center will be the epicenter of a multi-block, alcohol-free Block Party. Musical acts perform on two main stages; additional entertainment is provided by mobile DJs. Bring your own picnic or purchase food from a variety of vendors, then watch an incredible rooftop fireworks display high above the civic center skyline.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Where:
               </strong>
               Grand Park, 200 North Grand Ave., Los Angeles, CA 90012
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Time:
               </strong>
               3pm  9:30pm. Fireworks at 9pm.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Cost:
               </strong>
               Free
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Parking:
               </strong>
               Parking garage rates vary. Public transportation is recommended.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                More Info:
               </strong>
               213-972-8080
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.valleycultural.org/july-4th-fireworks-extravaganza/" target="_blank">
                 July 4th Fireworks Extravaganza at Warner Park
                </a>
               </strong>
               <br/>
               Head to Warner Park in the San Fernando Valley for a free concert followed by an electrifying fireworks show set to live music. Over 15 local restaurants will be on hand along with arts and crafts vendors and local businesses. Children will have a special play area to explore.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Where:
               </strong>
               Warner Park, 5800 Topanga Canyon Blvd., Woodland Hills, CA 91367
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Time:
               </strong>
               6pm  9:30pm
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Cost:
               </strong>
               Free
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Parking:
               </strong>
               $20 in any of the Warner Center structures between Topanga and Canoga or Califa and Oxnard.
               <br/>
               <strong>
                More Info:
               </strong>
               818-704-1358
              </div>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2015/06/san-francisco-alice-973-summerthing.html">
               San Francisco: Alice 97.3 Summerthing 2015 Free Concert
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-BsU2RldOLps/VYujE5ZMyyI/AAAAAAAABxs/TivcqCpEXbg/s1600/San%2BFrancisco%2BAlice%2BSummerthing%2B2015%2B330x220-22kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="213" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-BsU2RldOLps/VYujE5ZMyyI/AAAAAAAABxs/TivcqCpEXbg/s320/San%2BFrancisco%2BAlice%2BSummerthing%2B2015%2B330x220-22kb.jpg" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 (Clockwise) Kelly Clarkson, Mat Kearney, Rachel Platten and Elle King perform at Alice's Summerthing
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               San Franciscos popular AC radio station,
               <a href="http://radioalice.cbslocal.com/" target="_blank">
                Alice 97.3
               </a>
               , presents its annual
               <a href="http://radioalice.cbslocal.com/2015/05/14/alices-summerthing-2015-general-info/" target="_blank">
                Summerthing
               </a>
               concert on Sharon Meadow in Golden Gate Park on Sunday, June 28, 2015. Offered free to the public, this years musical guests include Kelly Clarkson, Mat Kearney, Rachel Platten and Elle King. Doors open at 10:30am and the concert winds down around 4pm. Besides music, there will be interactive booths, fun activities, gourmet food trucks and more.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Public transportation is encouraged due to limited parking. Parking is available at St. Marys Hospital (off Fell St.) and UCSF (Irving &amp; Arguello), three blocks from Sharon Meadow.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Approximate Set Times
               </strong>
               <br/>
               Elle King 12:35pm
               <br/>
               Rachel Platten 1:20pm
               <br/>
               Mat Kearney 2:15pm
               <br/>
               Kelly Clarkson 3:30pm
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Alice 97.3 sponsors two other concerts each year; the Now and Zen Fest in September and Alice in Winterland in December.
              </div>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2015/06/san-francisco-fillmore-jazz-festival.html">
               San Francisco: Fillmore Jazz Festival 2015
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_ERe2oYi36k/VYKKsHVLR_I/AAAAAAAABxU/wWzdbYdFtWs/s1600/san%2Bfrancisco%2Bfillmore%2Bjazz%2Bfestival%2B2015%2Bposter%2Bcraig%2Bfrazier234x330-44kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Fillmore Jazz Festival in San Francisco, the largest free jazz festival on the West Coast" border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_ERe2oYi36k/VYKKsHVLR_I/AAAAAAAABxU/wWzdbYdFtWs/s320/san%2Bfrancisco%2Bfillmore%2Bjazz%2Bfestival%2B2015%2Bposter%2Bcraig%2Bfrazier234x330-44kb.jpg" title="" width="226"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 Fillmore Jazz Festival 2015 (poster: Craig Frazier)
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               More than 100,000 people are expected to attend the 31st Annual
               <a href="http://www.fillmorejazzfestival.com/index.php?op=home" target="_blank">
                Fillmore Jazz Festival
               </a>
               in San Francisco July 4 &amp; 5. Touted as the largest free jazz festival on the West Coast, it spans 12 blocks of historic Fillmore Street between Jackson and Eddy in the Pacific Heights neighborhood. Visitors will enjoy stellar performances on multiple stages  from contemporary jazz, to funk and soul, to reggae and traditional blues. Great music is just part of the fun. Street side merchants will offer a range of goods for sale and there will be plenty of tasty food and beverage choices. This years festival celebrates the discovery of new jazz musicians and the rediscovery of old friends and past festival experiences.Hours: 10am to 6pm both days. Heres the line-up:
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <strong>
               Saturday, July 4
              </strong>
              <br/>
              California Street Stage
              <br/>
              10:15 a.m.: California Jazz Conservatory Ensemble led by Luis Salcedo
              <br/>
              12:15 p.m.: Caili O'Doherty - "Padme" CD Release
              <br/>
              2:15 p.m.: Contemporary Jazz Orchestra
              <br/>
              4:15 p.m.: Kim Nalley
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Eddy Street Stage
              <br/>
              10:30 a.m.: Tiffany Austin
              <br/>
              12:30 p.m.: Valerie Troutt Quartet
              <br/>
              2:30 p.m.: Native Elements
              <br/>
              4:30 p.m.: Martin Luther
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Sutter Street Stage
              <br/>
              10:30 a.m.: Blue Grazz featuring Jenna Mammina
              <br/>
              12:30 p.m.: Jungle Biskit
              <br/>
              2:30 p.m.: Jonah Smith
              <br/>
              4:30 p.m.: Sugar Pie DeSanto
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <strong>
               Sunday, July 5
              </strong>
              <br/>
              California Street Stage
              <br/>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2015/06/lake-tahoe-stargazing-at-ritz-carlton.html">
               Lake Tahoe: Stargazing at Ritz-Carlton with Tahoe Star Tours
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-vCaq4us0fbI/VX8PFtqJbII/AAAAAAAABw8/0ywOB-C6efI/s1600/tahoe%2Bstar%2Btours%2Britz%2Bcarlton%2Blake%2Btahoe%2B330x201-22kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Starry starry nights with Tahoe Star Tours at Ritz-Carlton Lake Tahoe" border="0" height="194" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-vCaq4us0fbI/VX8PFtqJbII/AAAAAAAABw8/0ywOB-C6efI/s320/tahoe%2Bstar%2Btours%2Britz%2Bcarlton%2Blake%2Btahoe%2B330x201-22kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 Tahoe Star Tours at Ritz-Carlton Lake Tahoe (photo:
                 <a href="https://twitter.com/RitzCarlton" target="_blank">
                  @Ritz-Carlton
                 </a>
                 )
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               This summer enjoy a unique astronomy experience with
               <a href="http://tahoestartours.com/Default.aspx" target="_blank">
                Tahoe Star Tours
               </a>
               at its newest mountainside viewing venue,
               <a href="http://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/Properties/LakeTahoe/Default.htm" target="_blank">
                Ritz-Carlton Lake Tahoe
               </a>
               . On Friday evenings June 19 through September 4, award winning amateur astronomer
               <a href="https://twitter.com/thestarguide" target="_blank">
                Tony Berendsen
               </a>
               , owner of Tahoe Star Tours and president of the Northern Nevada Science Coalition, will lead guests on an informative exploration of the night sky. The event begins with a greeting and orientation, followed by stargazing using high-powered Celestron telescopes and binoculars. There will be plenty of comfortable seating, outdoor heaters and blankets and continuous cocktails served by the impeccable Ritz-Carlton staff. An optional barbeque buffet can be purchased for an additional charge. Early reservations are encouraged; tickets can be purchased at
               <a href="http://www.eventbrite.com/e/tahoe-star-tours-at-northstar-california-tickets-17013243068?ref=ebtnebtckt" target="_blank">
                Eventbrite
               </a>
               .
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               There is no self-parking at the Ritz-Carlton; only valet parking. Guests can park in the Northstar Village Parking Lot and take the Highlands Gondola from the Village to the resort where they will be greeted, checked in for the tour, and led to the function.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               2015 Schedule
               <br/>
               Friday 6/19, 6/26: Saturn and Jupiter Split the Sky
               <br/>
               Friday 7/3, 7/10, 7/17, 7/24, 7/31: Dolphin in the Cosmic Ocean
               <br/>
               Friday 8/7, 8/14, 8/21, 8/28: Dwarf Planets in the Microscope
               <br/>
               Friday 9/4: Tribute to Carl Sagan
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2015/06/aptos-mushroom-dome-cabin-off-beaten.html">
               Aptos: Mushroom Dome Cabin  Off the Beaten Path
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Ztd9IeHayU0/VXEeqYyYWwI/AAAAAAAABwY/5Uprje9rOAE/s1600/Aptos%2BMushroom%2BDome%2BCabin-330x251-37k.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Escape to the great outdoors. The Mushroom Dome Cabin is off the beaten path." border="0" height="243" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Ztd9IeHayU0/VXEeqYyYWwI/AAAAAAAABwY/5Uprje9rOAE/s320/Aptos%2BMushroom%2BDome%2BCabin-330x251-37k.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                <em>
                 Mushroom Dome Cabin in Aptos
                </em>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Looking for a secluded cabin in the woods; something a little out of the ordinary? The Mushroom Dome Cabin in Aptos might be the getaway youre after. This tiny bubble of a house sits quietly among a canopy of trees, blending in with the tranquil surroundings. Although youll feel like youre off the beaten path, youre just 10 minutes from the beach, 10 minutes from the quaint shops and restaurants of Aptos Village and less than 20 minutes from downtown Santa Cruz.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-a4ZSruuJuSk/VXEe-MpiGrI/AAAAAAAABwg/JE3nkaY656w/s1600/Aptos%2BMushroom%2BDome%2BCabin%2Bupstairs-330x220-27kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Upstairs loft at Mushroom Dome Cabin has a double bed" border="0" height="213" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-a4ZSruuJuSk/VXEe-MpiGrI/AAAAAAAABwg/JE3nkaY656w/s320/Aptos%2BMushroom%2BDome%2BCabin%2Bupstairs-330x220-27kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                <em>
                 Mushroom Dome Cabin - upstairs
                </em>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               The simple cabin has a loft bedroom upstairs. Windows in the geodesic dome ceiling make it the perfect spot for star gazing. Downstairs youll find a sitting area, a bathroom with a shower, and a kitchenette equipped with all the essentials. A large outdoor deck faces the redwoods and houses a barbeque grill.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-75tssTcxQr8/VXEfNCfA0lI/AAAAAAAABwo/EAD0WavbGq8/s1600/Aptos%2BMushroom%2BDome%2BCabin%2Binterior-330x220-32kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Downstairs at the Mushroom Dome Cabin is a kitchen, sitting area and bathroom." border="0" height="213" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-75tssTcxQr8/VXEfNCfA0lI/AAAAAAAABwo/EAD0WavbGq8/s320/Aptos%2BMushroom%2BDome%2BCabin%2Binterior-330x220-32kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                <em>
                 Mushroom Dome Cabin - downstairs
                </em>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Rates for the
               <a href="https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/8357#summary" target="_blank">
                Mushroom Dome Cabin
               </a>
               start at $110 per night and include breakfast and snacks. A two-day minimum stay is required on the weekends. Security deposit, cleaning fee and tax are additional. Reservations can be made through
               <a href="https://www.airbnb.com/" target="_blank">
                Airbnb
               </a>
                its currently the # 1 rental at the booking site.
              </div>

              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-4fKhhitKnoM/VXDzp2lzxnI/AAAAAAAABwA/p50AXGjXwIU/s1600/Quicksilver%2BMiniature%2BHorse%2BRanch%2Bfoal%2B330x309-27kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Miniature Horse at Quicksilver Ranch in Solvang, California" border="0" height="299" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-4fKhhitKnoM/VXDzp2lzxnI/AAAAAAAABwA/p50AXGjXwIU/s320/Quicksilver%2BMiniature%2BHorse%2BRanch%2Bfoal%2B330x309-27kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                <em>

                 <a href="http://www.yelp.com/user_local_photos?select=mUR_ZVVw5kLMDax4c9PN3A&amp;userid=43WiflwfTbmjizIfxvQ_jw" target="_blank">
                  Scott B
                 </a>
                 .)
                </em>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Part of the fun of a driving vacation is an unexpected roadside attraction. Quicksilver Ranch near the village of Solvang (30 miles north of Santa Barbara) is just that. This family-owned ranch has been around for over three decades and breeds and sells miniature horses  about 20 to 30 foals a year. Visitors can wander the grounds, free of charge, and admire these adorable little creatures. Open Monday through Saturday from 10am to 3pm, its the perfect place to stop for a stretch and soak in some cuteness.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-D1ipENTZZtk/VXDz7z3RvzI/AAAAAAAABwI/RQ108VdHmQo/s1600/Quicksilver%2BMiniature%2BHorse%2BRanch%2Btwo%2Bhorses%2B330-236x28kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="See adorable miniature horses at Quicksilver Ranch in Solvang, California" border="0" height="228" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-D1ipENTZZtk/VXDz7z3RvzI/AAAAAAAABwI/RQ108VdHmQo/s320/Quicksilver%2BMiniature%2BHorse%2BRanch%2Btwo%2Bhorses%2B330-236x28kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                <em>
                 Quicksilver Ranch (photo
                 <a href="http://www.yelp.com/user_local_photos?select=1PUGKuYeOx9hsjMFwaATsg&amp;userid=43WiflwfTbmjizIfxvQ_jw" target="_blank">
                  Scott B
                 </a>
                 .)
                </em>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Solvang is located in the scenic Santa Ynez Valley on the central California coast. The Valley is recognized as one of the most important horse locations in America. Besides high-quality horses (over 50 different privately owned breeds), its known for its prominent clinicians, veterinarians, showmen and judges. Many Kentucky Derby winners and contenders have trained and lived in the Valley.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <a href="http://qsminis.com/" target="_blank">
                Quicksilver Ranch
               </a>
               is located at 1555 Alamo Pintado Road, Solvang, CA, 93463. Phone: (805) 686-4002.

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2015/06/yosemite-yarts-bus-service-from-fresno.html">
               Yosemite: YARTS Bus Service from Fresno Up and Running
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               </div>
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-QgjbDqC7FDM/VW_DpAugJ9I/AAAAAAAABvE/ACJ8RDuFaXI/s1600/yosemite-yarts-transportation%2B330x190-30kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img alt="YARTS begins bus service between Fresno and Yosemite" border="0" height="184" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-QgjbDqC7FDM/VW_DpAugJ9I/AAAAAAAABvE/ACJ8RDuFaXI/s320/yosemite-yarts-transportation%2B330x190-30kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 <em>
                  YARTS bus service to Yosemite
                 </em>
                </span>
               </div>
               <br/>
               Just in time for summer,
               <a href="http://yarts.com/" target="_blank">
                YARTS
               </a>
               (Yosemite Area Regional Transportation System) has added a new route to its schedule: Fresno to Yosemite Valley via Highway 41.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Buses make five round-trips daily, picking-up passengers at the Greyhound and Amtrak stations, the Fresno Airport, CSU Fresno, and the Highway 145 Park and Ride. Stops are made at the Coarsegold Historic Village, the Oakhurst Best Western, and Fish Camps Tenaya Lodge on the way to Yosemite Valley.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Tickets can be purchased on the bus with cash, Visa, MasterCard, or Discover. Or pre-purchase your tickets at various local outlets. One way tickets from Fresno to Yosemite Valley are $15 or $30 round trip. Reduced fares are available for seniors (62+), children (12 and under without an adult paid fare), and persons with disabilities. Your YARTS ticket includes admission to the park, as well as one free child ride (12 and under) per adult ticket purchased.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               YARTS also has bus service to Yosemite Valley from Merced, Mammoth Lakes and Sonora. For a complete list of schedules and fares, visit the
               <a href="http://yarts.com/" target="_blank">
                YARTS website
               </a>
               .
              </div>

Rate includes, deluxe room, free breakfast for two at Aurea Cafe, two nightly beverages in Aurea Lounge, free Wi-Fi access and one Google Glass device to use.            </span>

          <div class="date-posts">
           <div class="post-outer">

             </a>
             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://postmarkcalifornia.blogspot.com/2014/07/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars.html">
               Santa Clarita: Walk of Western Stars  Legends of Old West
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-1XfbWOfYFdI/U9aYg1DJLbI/AAAAAAAABtk/NkrGrSJpiO8/s1600/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars-john-wayne=330x249-41kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Walk-of-Western-Stars-in-Santa-Clarita-honors-John-Wayne-and-others" border="0" height="241" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-1XfbWOfYFdI/U9aYg1DJLbI/AAAAAAAABtk/NkrGrSJpiO8/s1600/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars-john-wayne=330x249-41kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 Walk of Western Starshonors John Wayne andothers
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Walk down Main Street in Old Town Newhall in Santa Clarita and youll be reminded of legends of the Old West. Embedded in the pavement of the sidewalks are plaques honoring members of the entertainment industry  specifically those in Western film, TV and radio. Known as the
               <a href="http://walkofwesternstars.com/" target="_blank">
                Walk of Western Stars
               </a>
               , this stretch of sidewalk is a fitting testament to Santa Claritas deep-rooted Western heritage.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WWerIOBRs-0/U9aa1OnvRZI/AAAAAAAABtw/mB_dXG89S2w/s1600/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars-226x320-49kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Stroll-down-the-Walk-of-Western-Stars-in-Old-Town-Newhall" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WWerIOBRs-0/U9aa1OnvRZI/AAAAAAAABtw/mB_dXG89S2w/s1600/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars-226x320-49kb.jpg" title=""/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 Walk of Western Stars on Main Street, Old Town Newhall
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Santa Clarita has always had a love affair with the Hollywood Western movie. The wide open space, the diverse landscape, and close proximity to Los Angeles have attracted movie directors to the area for more than a hundred years. Singing cowboy Gene Autry and legendary actors Gary Cooper, John Wayne and Roy Rogers are among those that have filmed Old West movies here and have all been inducted into the Walk of Western Stars. Other notable inductees include Tex Ritter, Chuck Connors, and Dale Evans. The most recent honorees are Charles Bronson and Steve McQueen, whose plaques were revealed earlier this year at the annual
               <a href="http://cowboyfestival.org/" target="_blank">
                Santa Clarita Cowboy Festival
               </a>
               .
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Besides being a hub for movies, a variety of western-themed television shows have filmed in Santa Clarita, including Gunsmoke, Annie Oakley and The Wild, Wild West. Today, several movie ranches call Santa Clarita home and filming has extended past the Western genre to include movies such as action-packed Spider-Man and Fast &amp; Furious as well as television shows such as NCIS.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-5mcRTOz5yrs/U9abJufrhVI/AAAAAAAABt4/KwTTR1LzeuQ/s1600/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars-saddle-330x262-41kb.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Walk-of-Western-Stars-close-up-of-plaque-on-Old-Newhall-sidewalk-honors-Western-legends" border="0" height="254" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-5mcRTOz5yrs/U9abJufrhVI/AAAAAAAABt4/KwTTR1LzeuQ/s1600/santa-clarita-walk-of-western-stars-saddle-330x262-41kb.jpg" title="" width="320"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <em>
                <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                 A saddle is the centerpiece of each plaque
                </span>
               </em>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               As you browse the sidewalk tributes, consider a stop at the
               <a href="http://www.hartmuseum.org/" target="_blank">
                William S. Hart Museum and Park
               </a>
               on Newhall Avenue. Once the former home of William Surrey Hart, a silent screen actor who became one the first big stars of Western movies, it houses a collection of significant Western artwork, early Hollywood mementos, and furnishings and artifacts representing multiple Native American cultures.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Santa Claritas
               <a href="http://walkofwesternstars.com/" target="_blank">
                Walk of Western Stars
               </a>
               is less than 60 minutes from most areas of Los Angeles. It can be accessed via Interstate 5 at Lyons Avenue or off Highway 14 at Newhall Avenue.
              </div>
              <div style="clear: both;">
              </div>
