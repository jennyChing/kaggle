       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         kinda sorta grueling.
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-03-28T21:52:01+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          28 March, 2011
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://girlmeetslife.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Gracie
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://girlmeetslife.com/2011/03/kinda-sorta-grueling/#comments">
           26 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         I mustve jinxed myself by labeling yesterday Sunday Funday because today turned out to be quite the grueling Monday. It was mainly because I was just downright
         <em>
          exhausted.
         </em>
         You know  one of those tired moods that you just cant snap out of no matter how many ventis you guzzle??
        </p>
        <p>
         The fact that Ive convinced myself I have the worlds worst commute certainly didnt help. I never thought that my commute was half bad (and, its not) until I got my mind set on moving into the city.
         <em>
          Funny/annoying how that happens, isnt it?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thankfully, though, there were a number of things that helped take my mind off of the
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <del>
           pity party
          </del>
         </span>
         gruelish-ness.
        </p>
        <p>
         like during my train rides, where I zone out and listen to worship music.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="324" src="http://girlmeetslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/Picture0392.jpg" style="display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: 0px;" width="454"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         (Side note: if you need some comforting music in your life, definitely snag
         <a href="http://hillsongunited.com" target="_blank">
          Hillsong Uniteds
         </a>
         newest album
         <em>
          Aftermath
         </em>
         , k?)
        </p>
        <p>
         Saying peace out to Twitter for the day was also a good release from the gruel. In the same way that I got sick of knowing what everyone was doing via Facebook, it felt great to just bedisconnected, I guess. Dont worry, though, Im not getting rid of my favorite social network anytime soon
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://girlmeetslife.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="229" src="http://girlmeetslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/3.28.11.4.png" style="display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: 0px;" width="429"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Knowing that I had a nice semi-homemade meal to look forward to perked me up towards the end of the daywhich was definitely needed, considering my late-afternoon doughtnut habit is not doing my energy tank any favors (oops).
        </p>
        <p>
         Pasta + tuna + spinach + cheese = Tuna Casserole For One.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="342" src="http://girlmeetslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/Picture044.jpg" style="border: 0px none; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="454"/>
        </p>
