           <p>
            The frustrating thing about following the work of a lot of visual artists is that youll sometimes see a lovely illustration, filled with vibrant colors and lighting, attached to a caption along the lines of, Had some free time during lunch, or, Quick warmup sketch. Meanwhile, it takes me an hour to draw something that looks vaguely like a human being. I realize that this a skill some people develop with training and time, but it never fails to look like magic to me.
            <strong>
             <a href="http://ningyee7.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
              Matthew Lau
             </a>
            </strong>
            is one of those magicians, sharing his digital illustrations, frequently composed on his lunch hour.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span id="more-44005">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Lau recently worked on Gamelofts mobile game based on the animated film
            <em>
             Epic
            </em>
            . Personally, I think I need to see some piece of media featuring his fantastically witchy cat lady. Theres magic in that there yarn basket (and probably a few more kitties). You can follow Lau through
            <a href="http://ningyee7.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
             his blog
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://ningyee7.deviantart.com/" target="_blank">
             deviantART
            </a>
            .
           </p>
