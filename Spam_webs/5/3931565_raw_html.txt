      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       On Thursday we told you about a
       <a href="http://www.lifewithdogs.tv/2014/12/dog-and-cat-best-friends-are-found-together-as-strays/">
        stray dog and cat
       </a>
        best friends  who were found together in Georgia. Were pleased to tell you that theyve already found a home  together!
      </p>
      <p>
       Henni the dog and Penny the cat  both females were brought to the
       <a href="https://www.facebook.com/columbiacountyanimalservices">
        Columbia County Animal Services
       </a>
       in Grovetown after a woman discovered them in her yard.
      </p>
      <p>
       They were brought in as strays to us by a citizen in Martinez who found them in her yard and the owner actually found them online and contacted us to let us know that they were her pets, explained Shelter Operations Manager Daniel Mayne. She wasnt able to come back and reclaim them. Her husband is in the military and got stationed up in North Carolina so she wasnt able to come back and get them.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.lifewithdogs.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/12.7.14-Stray-Dog-and-Cat-Found-Together-Adopted-Together2.jpg">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="12.7.14 - Stray Dog and Cat Found Together Adopted Together2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-91836" height="400" src="http://www.lifewithdogs.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/12.7.14-Stray-Dog-and-Cat-Found-Together-Adopted-Together2.jpg" width="377"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lifewithdogs.tv/2014/12/stray-dog-and-cat-found-together-adopted-together/&amp;media=http://www.lifewithdogs.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/12.7.14-Stray-Dog-and-Cat-Found-Together-Adopted-Together2.jpg&amp;description=Stray Dog and Cat Found Together Adopted Together')">
         </span>
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       The former owner requested that they be put in the same cage, which has been done.
      </p>
      <p>
       Theyre inseparable. They are right next to each other all the time, Mayne said. In the mornings Henni will groom Penny, the cat. They will get to playing and we let them out of their cages to play around in the room while were cleaning.
      </p>
      <p>
       Because they are such good friends, the shelter refused to split them up, and insisted they be adopted together. The adoption fee was even lowered to make that possible. Now they are going to a new home together.
      </p>
      <p>
       Thanks to WJBF and everyone that spread the word about these two adorable fur-babies, our Christmas wish was answered, CCAS said. Henni and Penny were adopted this morning!
      </p>
