      <span class="persomavt">
       <p>
        The whole artistic community is now using
        <b>
         Artween
        </b>
        , the largest social networking site dedicated exclusively to art.
       </p>
       <p>
        Are you on the look out for new talent?
       </p>
       <p>
        Or trying to find an art gallery in New York, Roma or Beijing?
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Artween
        </b>
        s virtual doors are open to you, enabling you to access all the art which interests you in a few simple clicks.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Register for free
        </b>
        and you can link up with all the galleries that enthuse you and receive their latest news, as well as invitations to events. You can also discover artists from close to home or further afield and give international exposure to your own art before a public which is inspired by a common passion:
        <b>
         art.
        </b>
       </p>
