         Is anyone else having a serious case of
         <a href="http://www.sonypictures.com/tv/dawsonscreek/" target="_blank">
          <em>
           Dawsons Creek
          </em>
         </a>
         deja vu right now?!?
         <p>
          It seems that
          <a href="https://twitter.com/vanderjames" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            James Van Der Beek
           </strong>
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joshua_Jackson" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Joshua Jackson
           </strong>
          </a>
          had an unplanned run-in Saturday, which made for a photo op more than 10 years in the making.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" src="http://static.celebuzz.com/uploads/2014/11/16/Screen-Shot-2014-11-16-at-9.13.52-AM.png" width="300&lt;/p"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          James shared the photo with an unsuspecting public, which went up on his
          <a href="http://instagram.com/vanderjames" target="_blank">
           Instagram
          </a>
          along with the caption, The most pleasant run-ins can happen in the most unexpected places.
         </p>
         <p>
          Obviously, both men have gotten along pretty well since leaving the creek back in 2003. Jackson starred on the cult sci-fi series
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fringe_(TV_series)" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Fringe
           </em>
          </a>
          before landing a spot on the cast of Showtimes newest hit
          <a href="http://www.sho.com/sho/the-affair/home" target="_blank">
           <em>
            The Affair
           </em>
          </a>
          . Van Der Beek is following up playing himself on
          <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1819509/" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Dont Trust the B- in Apartment 23
           </em>
          </a>
          with a role in the upcoming
          <a href="http://www.cbs.com/shows/csi/" target="_blank">
           <em>
            CSI
           </em>
          </a>
          spin-off
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CSI:_Cyber" target="_blank">
           <em>
            CSI: Cyber
           </em>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Its nice to know that James has a soft spot for the role that made him famous though, even telling
          <a href="http://live.huffingtonpost.com/r/segment/james-van-der-beek-csi-cyber-flu-awareness/54106cb702a760098900035b?ncid=edlinkushpmg00000060" target="_blank">
