     <h1>
      <a href="http://dbzer0.com/blog/quote-of-the-day-gender-equality/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Quote of the Day: Gender Equality">
       Quote of the Day: Gender Equality
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div style="float:left; margin: 10px;">
      <script type="text/javascript">
       reddit_url='http://dbzer0.com/blog/quote-of-the-day-gender-equality/'; reddit_title='  Quote of the Day: Gender Equality'; reddit_bgcolor = "0c0c0c";
      </script>
      <script src="http://www.reddit.com/button.js?t=3" type="text/javascript">
      </script>
     </div>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.trickster.org/symposium/symp181.htm">
       Quoth Lucy Gillam
      </a>
     </p>
     <blockquote>
      <p>
       The first is that true gender equality is actually perceived as
       <em>
        inequality
       </em>
       . A group that is made up of 50% women is
       <em>
        perceived
       </em>
       as being mostly women. A situation that is perfectly equal between men and women is
       <em>
        perceived
       </em>
       as being biased in favor of women.
      </p>
      <p>
       And if you dont believe me, youve never been a married woman who kept her family name. I have had students hold that up as proof of my sexism. My own brother told me that he could never marry a woman who kept her name because everyone would know who ruled that relationship.
       <em>
        Perfect equality
       </em>
        my husband keeps his name and I keep mine  is held as a statement of superiority on my part.
      </p>
     </blockquote>
     <p>
      Ayeap. This is what the shrill cries of males about feminazis are all about.
     </p>
