          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            Final checks on CAT 2014 results are underway and the results    are expected to be announced in the middle of 3rd week of December 2014 even    before Dec 20. CAT Centre 2014 had promised in its press release while inviting    applications for CAT 2014 that CAT 2014 results would be declared in the third    week of December 2014. Despite many odds and delays in various activities    during the CAT 2014, it appears CAT Centre 2014 will adhere to the result declaration    schedule for the exam.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            Revealing the progress and expected date to declare the results    Dr Rohit Kapoor CAT 2014 convener says As far as dates are concerned,    we will shortly announce it on our website. Request the candidates to wait for    now. CAT 2014 test takers can now feel relaxed that with the good    amount of activity going on in the processing of CAT 2014 result, it may not    be delayed like issuance of CAT 2014 Admit Card.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            Speculation on CAT 2014 results whether they would be announced    as per the schedule is contemplated here and there. In fact according to Dr    Rohit Kapoor, CAT 2014 Convener We are in the activity of processing    the results. Final checks are also underway. While the gigantic    task of processing the result and normalizing the scores of 1.70 lakhs of CAT    2014 test takers is not an easy one, CAT Centre 2014is making all out efforts    to keep the promise of declaring the result on time.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            <strong>
             Dec 21: The last day of third week
            </strong>
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            It is expected that CAT Centre 2014 will not let the third    week pass. The result may be declared even before the last day of third week    of December i.e. before Dec 21. In all probability the result might be declared    on Dec 20, 2014 in the late evening or in the wee hours of Dec 21.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            <strong>
             The past trend of CAT result
            </strong>
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            CAT results were used to be declared in the second or third    week of January every year. In earlier CAT exams most of the times the result    was declared in the wee hours of the appointed date or a bit late in the morning.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            CAT 2014 is the first exam where instead of announcing the    result date, a week has been given as the range to declare the CAT 2014 result.    No doubt CAT Centre 2014 is processing the result very fast and will shortly    announce the result declaration date as well; still it has created a bit of    dilemma among the CAT 2014 takers.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            <strong>
             CAT 2014: The earliest result in past 6 years
            </strong>
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            As anticipated CAT 2014 results could be announced preferably    by Dec 20 or in the wee hours on Dec 21 before the 3rd week in Dec 2014 ends.    Despite the fact that in CAT 2014 the testing window was short and result processing    has also got a short span before declaration of result for almost same number    of test takers as appeared in CAT 2013, the test is proposed to be declared    one month before than in earlier CAT exams.
           </font>
          </p>
          <font size="2">
          </font>
          <p align="justify">
           <font size="2">
            IIMs had announced that the results would be declared in the    3rd week of December 2014. If this herculean task is completed in time it will    be a big achievement and CAT 2015 could be further shortened to only one day    test, may be in 2 sessions.
           </font>
          </p>
