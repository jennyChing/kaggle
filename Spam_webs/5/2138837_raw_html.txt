        The Worlds Tallest Teenage Girl (Gallery)
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">

         April 13, 2011
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/2011/04/13/the-worlds-tallest-teenage-girl/tallest_girl_01/" rel="attachment wp-att-2295">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2295" height="600" src="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/tallest_girl_01.jpg" title="tallest_girl_01" width="341"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         We recently covered the
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/2011/03/11/picture-of-the-day-new-contender-for-worlds-smallest-man/">
          worlds smallest man
         </a>
         , and now news comes of the worlds tallest teenage girl.
        </p>
        <p>
         Height is a desirable trait in men, but not as much (generally) in women, making this womans situation a mirror of the smallest mans.
        </p>
        <p>
         The worlds tallest teenage girl is named Malee Duangdee and lives in Thailand. As is so often the case with record height, she had a brain tumor that manifested when she was young, causing her to grow and grow until reaching her current height of 610 and weight of 287 pounds. Duangdee now takes expensive hormones to regulate the growth.
        </p>
        <p>
         The tumor has had other side effects, among them causing her to lose her eyesight.
        </p>
