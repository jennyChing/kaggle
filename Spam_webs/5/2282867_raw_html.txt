    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <p>
      In the
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Dungeons_%26_Dragons" title="Dungeons &amp; Dragons">
        Dungeons &amp; Dragons
       </a>
      </i>
      <a href="/wiki/Role-playing_game" title="Role-playing game">
       role-playing game
      </a>
      , players must choose a class of character to play. The
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Player%27s_Handbook" title="Player's Handbook">
        Player's Handbook
       </a>
      </i>
      , one of the three core rulebooks, lists eleven specific classes, and supplemental sourcebooks have added several.
     </p>
