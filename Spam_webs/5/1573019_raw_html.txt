         </a>

         </em>
         is stored up for last minute ideas! This list of easy hanukkah crafts for kids is one I am keeping on hold for the night before Hanukkah when I realize that it IS the night before the holiday and I want to do something with the kids, quick!

All of these projects use materials you probably have on hand at home. Ive included a few that weve done, as well as some from other crafty moms.

It doesnt have a lot about the specifics of why Hanukkah is celebrated, but is a great story about an interfaith celebration. One of the reasons Ive never done a Hanukkah book list is that I havent found many books that I really love. You can check out the PJ Librarys Hanukkah book list, though.

It might be fun if they made their own dreidels, maybe? That would give them a chance to write Hebrew letters! Good luck with your class visit!
