        <p>
         <a href="http://www.recipe-diaries.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/baked-buffalo-chicken-taquitos-045.jpg">
          <img alt="Baked Buffalo Chicken Taquitos for Weight Watcher's - 3 points - Recipe Diaries - Game day food " class="alignnone wp-image-10094 size-full" height="1036" src="http://www.recipe-diaries.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/baked-buffalo-chicken-taquitos-045.jpg" width="800"/>

        <p>
         Its been quite awhile since Ive made anything with buffalo chicken. A really long time! And its the weekend. Tomorrow, I am going to my FIRST Frozen themed birthday party and I must say, that I am pretty excited to go. I havent even seen the movie yet, but just by surfing through Pinterest I know who the characters are.
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.recipe-diaries.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you cook the chicken in a slow cooker all day long these buffalo chicken taquitos come together pretty fast. Other options are to have a chicken already cooked in t he fridge, a rotisserie chicken, or even canned chicken works with this. Add the warm chicken in a bowl with cream cheese, buffalo sauce, and stir. Stuff the chicken in a flour tortilla and bake them in the oven. I used the broiler to get them a little extra crispy on top. Then start dipping. Finger food is perfect for game day. Its a lot like buffalo chicken dip wrapped inside a tortilla.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.recipe-diaries.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/baked-buffalo-chicken-taquitos-055.jpg">

         </a>

         </a>
        </p>

         Enjoy!

            Ingredients

           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8 oz low fat cream cheese
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup buffalo sauce
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            12 small flour tortillas (low carb)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">

         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix cream cheese and buffalo sauce until well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir in chicken until well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place 2-3 Tablespoons of mixture spread into a thin line onto center of flour tortilla.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place on lightly greased cookie sheet and repeat for remaining taquitos.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Once taquitos are arranged on cookie sheet, lightly spray the tops with cooking spray.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake at 425 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until taquitos are golden brown.
           </li>
          </ol>
