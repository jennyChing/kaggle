      <h1>
       Volvo chasing low emissions with new 3-cylinder engine
      </h1>
      <div class="clearfix">
       <span class="pull-left">
        By
        <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/author/scott-collie/" rel="author">
         Scott Collie
        </a>
        - December 15, 2014
       </span>
       <a class="picture-count" href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures">
        8 Pictures
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="inline-item">
      <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures">
       <img alt="All variants of the new motor should meet the tough Euro 7 emissions standards" class="hero-img" data-at2x="http://images.gizmag.com/hero/volvo-drive-e-three-cylinder@2x.jpg" src="http://images.gizmag.com/hero/volvo-drive-e-three-cylinder.jpg"/>
      </a>
      <div class="caption caption--hero">
       <p>
        All variants of the new motor should meet the tough Euro 7 emissions standards
       </p>
       <a class="gallery-link" href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures">
        Image Gallery
       </a>
       (8 images)
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="article-body">
      <p>
       Volvo continues to chase better fuel economy with the development of a new three-cylinder engine, which will sit alongside the Swedish brand's Drive-E four-cylinder motors in its lineup.
      </p>
      <ul class="thumbnails">
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures#1">
         <img alt="Volvo's new three-cylinder motor produces up to 180 hp" src="http://images.gizmag.com/gallery_tn/volvo-drive-e-three-cylinder-0.jpg" title="Volvo's new three-cylinder motor produces up to 180 hp"/>
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures#2">
         <img alt="The engine is set to be produced on the same line that produces the brand's four-cylinder motors" src="http://images.gizmag.com/gallery_tn/volvo-drive-e-three-cylinder-1.jpg" title="The engine is set to be produced on the same line that produces the brand's four-cylinder motors"/>
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures#3">
         <img alt="Details about the new engine are scarce thus far" src="http://images.gizmag.com/gallery_tn/volvo-drive-e-three-cylinder-2.jpg" title="Details about the new engine are scarce thus far"/>
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/volvo-new-three-cylinder-engine/35210/pictures#4">
         <img alt="Ford, Mini and Audi are just some of the brands that have chased better economy by downsizing" src="http://images.gizmag.com/gallery_tn/volvo-drive-e-three-cylinder-3.jpg" title="Ford, Mini and Audi are just some of the brands that have chased better economy by downsizing"/>
        </a>
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       The new Volvo block joins the global trend for downsizing with big manufacturers like
       <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/ford-smallest-motor-3-cylinder-ecoboost/18814/" target="_blank">
        Ford
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/mini-cooper-oxford/29823/" target="_blank">
        Mini
       </a>
       and Audi chasing better fuel efficiency through small, three cylinder engines that challenge the idea that "there's no replacement for displacement."
      </p>
      <p>
       The three-cylinder engine is designed to work in a raft of different Volvo cars, and takes advantage of the company's experience with turbocharging to deliver plenty of power from the small block.
      </p>
      <p>
       Peak power for the engine is currently claimed to be 180 hp (134 kW), a figure which is significantly higher than the 95 hp (75 kW) that Audi's 1.0-liter, three-cylinder petrol engine produces in the
       <a href="http://www.gizmag.com/updated-2015-audi-a1/34803/" target="_blank">
        updated A1
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       "We have learned a lot from the development of our 4-cylinder Drive-E engines and translated this into a highly responsive, compact and powerful premium-quality 3-cylinder engine," says Michael Fleiss, Vice President Powertrain at Volvo Car Group.
      </p>
      <p>
       Fleiss says that the new engine will meet Euro 7 emission targets (though we are not sure exactly what that translates to at this point) and offers a flexible production option as the brand continues to grow. Production will take place on the same line that Volvo uses for its four-cylinder engines, which also helps keep costs down.
      </p>
      <p>
       Source:
       <a href="https://www.media.volvocars.com/global/en-gb/media/pressreleases/154663/volvo-car-group-already-testing-its-new-three-cylinder-engine" target="_blank">
        Volvo
       </a>
      </p>
