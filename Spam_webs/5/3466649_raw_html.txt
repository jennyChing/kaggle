       <h1 class="entry-title">
        what im loving lately
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span>
         April 7, 2014
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <img alt="" src="http://r.linqia.cc/e1ee216"/>
         <a href="http://www.justaddglam.com/im-loving-lately-4/" title="what i'm loving lately">
          <img alt="glitter heels" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5136" height="1080" src="http://www.justaddglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Edit-21.png" width="1626"/>
          <span id="more-5134">
          </span>
         </a>
         I picked up a pair of these sparkly
         <a href="http://bit.ly/QYiTnc" target="_blank" title="Vince Camuto">
          Vince Camuto
         </a>
         heels this past weekend for, get this$7. Brand new and perfect for a Friday night happy hour. Cant wait to take these out for a spin!
         <img alt="what's in my clutch" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5137" height="1080" src="http://www.justaddglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Edit-31.png" width="1507"/>
         I just recently got over a cold that just wouldnt quit and I converted my Gigi New York python clutch into my cold clutch to toss into my bag to keep me going. My cold clutch consisted of all of my essentials to keep my sniffles at bay and look somewhat decent.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Green tea bags  so I can make a hot cup of tea at a moments notice
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1lLQflK" target="_blank" title="Airborne">
            Airborne effervescent tablets
           </a>
            these tablets are the perfect dose of vitamins to give me an energy boost
          </li>
          <li>
           <a class="broken_link" href="http://bit.ly/1hlvTI4" target="_blank" title="Kleenex">
            Kleenex Slim Pack Tissues
           </a>
            If I must be sick, at least my tissues can be designerlove this print designed by
           <a class="broken_link" href="http://bit.ly/1hlvTI4" target="_blank" title="Isaac Mizrahi">
            Isaac Mizrahi
           </a>
           !
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1lLQflK" target="_blank" title="Covergirl + OLAY">
            Covergirl+OLAY
           </a>
            I keep this Covergirl+OLAY compact in my cold clutch for touch ups to remove redness from my nose. Its coverage is great camouflage for a cold!
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1g1rRp7" target="_blank" title="Ricola">
            Ricola herb drops
           </a>
            These Swiss herb drops are so soothing for a sore throat with their blend of 13 different herbs
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/PU0vum" target="_blank" title="Rose Vaseline">
            Rose and Almond Oil Vaseline
           </a>
            This stuff is great for chapped skin and smells amazing, and the vintage tin is just too cute
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1qfMMcF" target="_blank" title="Nivea A Kiss of Care &amp; Color">
            Nivea A Kiss of Care &amp; Color
           </a>
            I just discovered this lip balm / lip color duo and really like the idea of moisture with a pop of color.
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1i1okrg" target="_blank" title="Tory burch lipstick">
            Tory Burch
            <em>
             Pas Du Tout
            </em>
            Lipstick
           </a>
            You know that quote that says put on some lipstick and pull yourself together? Well, its true! Sometimes you just need a lipstick pick-me-up and this
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1i1okrg" target="_blank" title="Tory burch lipstick">
            Tory Burch
           </a>
           hue is my current favorite!
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1gnB92t" target="_blank" title="Gigi New York clutch">
            Gigi New York python clutch
           </a>
            You gotta have something cute to hold it all together!
          </li>
         </ul>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <img alt="Tory Burch lipstick" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5135" height="1437" src="http://www.justaddglam.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Edit-11.png" width="1080"/>
         Im always mixing up the styling of my coffee table tray and lately Ive been using red roses, a
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1qfMlPN" target="_blank" title="Voluspa candle">
          Voluspa Crane Flower candle,
         </a>
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1lLPwB6" target="_blank" title="Purple Agate Coasters">
          purple agate coaster
         </a>
         , and some spring beauty productsthe aforementioned gorgeous
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1i1okrg" target="_blank" title="Tory Burch lipstick">
          Tory Burch lipstick
         </a>
         that I cant get enough of and a sample of the new
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1jVH4g9" target="_blank" title="Marni rose fragrance">
          Marni Rose fragrance
         </a>
         which smells. so. good!
        </p>
