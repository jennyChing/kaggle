         <p>
          With the release of NVIDIAs GeForce GTX 780 Ti coming very soon a few photos of the card have been leaked on various sites.  As we
          <a href="http://www.thinkcomputers.org/nvidia-announces-gtx-780-ghz-edition-cards/" target="_blank">
           pointed out
          </a>
          there actually may be two versions of the 780 Ti, a normal version and a GHz Edition.  The normal version is what you see below.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <a href="http://cdn5.thinkcomputers.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/gtx-780ti-1.jpg">
           <img alt="NVIDIA GeForce GTX 780 Ti" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-17498" height="337" src="http://cdn5.thinkcomputers.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/gtx-780ti-1-600x337.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </center>
         <p>
          As you can see in the photos the normal version of the GeForce GTX 780 Ti will come with the normal black and silver cooler that we have seen on previous NVIDIA cards.  The GTX 780 Ti logo is towards the back of the card.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <a href="http://cdn5.thinkcomputers.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/gtx-780ti-2.jpg">
           <img alt="NVIDIA GeForce GTX 780 Ti" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-17497" height="337" src="http://cdn5.thinkcomputers.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/gtx-780ti-2-600x337.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </center>
         <p>
          We have heard that the GHz Edition of the card will have an all black reference cooler.  This will of course make it easier for users to differentiate between to the two cards.  The GHz edition of the card is also said to have 6GB of memory and a possible 12GB variant with unlimited TDP.  Of course these are just rumors until we know for sure.
         </p>
