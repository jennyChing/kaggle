                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wyDEc52G-1s/VYBX_NINhnI/AAAAAAAABDw/8ImxIIq1Y_A/s1600/BrienzeLipStickBlog.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="384" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-wyDEc52G-1s/VYBX_NINhnI/AAAAAAAABDw/8ImxIIq1Y_A/s640/BrienzeLipStickBlog.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      For all of you loyal followers of the BeautyScoop Blog, I am
the Meet the Staff member who couldnt admit to being a product junkie,
though I do have a makeup buying addiction. The detail I left out of that already
too complicated answer is that I dont actually WEAR the makeup I buy. Its
strange, I know.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      I love makeup. I love the pretty packaging, the gorgeous,
saturated colors, the eye-catching effect makeup has when worn well or boldly.
I loved watching my grandmother put on her face and the subsequent confidence
the already strong, passionate, independent woman gained. Makeup is a tool, a
weapon, and Im a fan of anything that assists us in getting through any given
day!
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      All of that being said, I traipsed into the beauty world
nine months ago barely applying mascara, let alone eyeliner, on the daily.
Okay, thats an exaggeration. I definitely dont wear mascara daily. Eye makeup
is to be worn on those special occasions when I actually want to make an
impression, to stand out, to look nice. You know, dates! Or interviews! Or in
moments when I need that extra bit of confidence. Unfortunately, for my new
career path, this doesnt cut it.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      My manager is a complete sweetheart and started gently, at
first. Brienz, why dont you buy this lipstick and let me know what you think
of it! Or Hey, Miss B, that new eye shadow that just came in would look
fantastic on you  put it on! My reluctance made her take a more firm approach.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Okay, ladies! New rule at BSD! Every Friday is Lipstick
Friday! Every Friday you MUST come into work wearing a fabulous shade of
lipstick! I will be watching!
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Well, goodness.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      I may not be competitive where my looks are concerned, but
my review matters to me,
                      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                      </span>
                      and that was
(hilariously) the implication. More importantly, if my inability to wear makeup
was changing company policy, maybe it was time to step up a bit. So it began, first
with
                      <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/pur-minerals-lip-gloss-stick/?page_context=category&amp;faceted_search=0">
                       Pur
Minerals Lip Gloss Sticks
                      </a>
                      (my favorite shade is Berry Pretty). Those are a
light and easy cross between a gloss and chapstick  some color, yes, but
subtle enough to not make me run screaming from my mirror, but also buildable
and easy to apply. I can look like Im wearing more color when my manager comes
to check on me, and I can throw it on without looking when I hear her heels in
the hallway. Next I ventured on to
                      <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/glominerals-glolip-stick/?page_context=category&amp;faceted_search=0">
                       Glominerals
Lip Stick
                      </a>
                      in Rose Petaland started getting compliments from my lovely and
stylish coworkers. Being forced to apply lipstick suddenly didnt seem like the
worst thing in the world!
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Finally, the moment of truth arrived. GloMinerals dropped a
new product, a beautiful new product, a popular new product, a product that
reflected runway trends the world over. Matte Lips. While our GloMinerals rep
was introducing the product to us, I stepped out of the office to answer a
call. When I returned, my fate was sealed. We had been asked to choose samples.
My manager had chosen mine.
                      <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/glominerals-suede-matte-stick/?page_context=search&amp;faceted_search=0">
                       GloMinerals
Suede Matte Stick
                      </a>
                      in Pinup was coming. For me.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      I waited nervously for the following Friday, ducking into
the office first thing in the morning, taking our staff mirror in hand and
applying the, ahem,
                      <i style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">
                       bright
                      </i>
                      product to
my bare lips. This wasnt just red. This was screaming red. Flame-wreathed red.
Terrifyingly bold and intimidating red. I wasoddly elated. True, despite its
application ease, my lips werent even. But my teeth looked whiter. My face
looked intentional. I looked like an adult.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      My manager strutted in not long after and stopped, shocked.
You look fantastic!!! She exclaimed. You look more mature, an affronted
sounding warehouse manager mentioned.
                      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                      </span>
                      Wow  I guess youre serious today! My boss let slip.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      I looked awesome. And felt awesome. Now maybe I can work on
using a lip liner to actually get my lips even and perfect! I think GloMinerals
has one that looks pretty easy to apply
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Theyve created a monster!
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      If you have tips or a similar story of your breakout into
the world of beauty, let us know! We love hearing your stories and thoughts in
the comments section below! ;)
                     </div>

                     <a href="http://blog.beautystoredepot.com/2015/06/ease-into-summer-withthese-hot-makeup.html">
                      Summer Makeup Trends
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">

                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1029956440294210335" itemprop="articleBody">

                      <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/">

                      <b class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130170">
                       <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130169" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                        Ease into Summer with
these Hot Makeup Trends:
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <span class="yiv4848747963" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130159" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                      <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130167">
                       Hello beautiful people! The sun is shining and
the heat is coming; summer is officially right around the bend! Try some of
these makeup trends to get your summer started.
                      </span>
                      <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130164">
                       This summer is all about bold features, fine
lines, and the ever so popular natural look. You can also try out a new spin on
the classic smoky eye by substituting black with brown!
                      </span>
                      <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      <b class="yiv4848747963">
                       Bold Features
                      </b>
                      <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130168">
                       Bright lips are SO in, as well as hints of bold
color on your lids! Remember to alternate each style so you don't end up
overdoing it. Try a colorful pop on your lips (like
                       <span style="background-color: white;">
                        <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/glominerals-suede-matte-stick/">
                         <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130172" style="background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position: 0% 0%; background-repeat: repeat; background-size: auto auto;">
                          gloMinerals Suede Matte Stick in Punch
                         </span>
                        </a>
                       </span>
                       ) and
then switch it up another day by pairing a nude lip with bright eyes (lik
                       <span style="background-color: white;">
                        e
                        <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/pur-minerals-double-ego-eyeliner/">
                         <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130173" style="background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position: 0% 0%; background-repeat: repeat; background-size: auto auto;">
                          Pur Minerals Double Ego Eyeliner
                         </span>
                        </a>
                        in Queensland or Rio De Janiero). A little color can go a long way!
                       </span>
                      </span>
                      <span style="background-color: white;">
                       <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                       <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                       <b class="yiv4848747963">
                        Fine Lines
                       </b>
                       <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                       <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130163">
                        Another thing that we are seeing a lot of this
season is the winged-out look. This is a dramatic look that is a step above the
classic cat eye! You can achieve this look by starting with a
                        <a href="http://www.beautystoredepot.com/cat-eyes/">
                         <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130175" style="background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-image: none; background-origin: padding-box; background-position: 0% 0%; background-repeat: repeat; background-size: auto auto;">
                          cat eye
                         </span>
                        </a>
                        and exaggerating
the tip up in the shape of a wing. We recommend the new Grande Liner in Rich
Black because it not only looks and feels amazing, but it also improves the
appearance of your lashes!
                       </span>
                       <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                       <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                       <b class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_134962">
                        Natural Look
                       </b>
                       <br class="yiv4848747963"/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="yiv4848747963" id="yiv4848747963yui_3_16_0_1_1433423961354_130162">
                       <span style="background-color: white;">
                        One of the most beautiful looks for this summer
is the seemingly bare face. Not all of us were blessed with immaculate skin and
naturally chiseled cheek bones. Because of this, there are many products that
help us achieve the "I'm naturally this perfect" look. The key to
healthy skin is to cleanse, tone, treat, and protect. Wearing sunscreen daily
