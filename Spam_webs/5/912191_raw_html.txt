        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Barbados-Needhams-Point-Beach.jpg">
          <img alt="Barbados Needham's Point Beach" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30338" height="943" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Barbados-Needhams-Point-Beach.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There are three different areas of Needhams Point, consisting of Pebbles Beach, Drill Hall Beach and of course  Needhams Point Beach, which is right in the middle! This beautiful beach has a natural reef that creates a swimming area protected from the strong waves, so its perfectly suited for sunbathers and swimmers.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/The-Bridgeport-Hilton-in-Barbados.jpg">
          <img alt="Needhams Point Beach in Barbados  at the Hilton Barbados Resort" class="aligncenter wp-image-30310 size-full" height="452" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/The-Bridgeport-Hilton-in-Barbados.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         While the Hilton Barbados Resort itself is a lovely property with an amazing staff, the main attraction is the location! Needhams Point Beach is oneof the prettiest beaches in the area is located right outside its doors, and youd be a fool not to book a room with an ocean view.
        </p>
        <p>
         Want to go body surfing? Just grab your board and walk outside
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Barbados-Body-Surfing.jpg">
          <img alt="Needhams Point Beach in Barbados  at the Hilton Barbados Resort" class="aligncenter wp-image-30308 size-full" height="677" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Barbados-Body-Surfing.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The sea is welcoming, the waters are warm and the sand feels like sugar between your toes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Want to do a little exploring? Hilton Barbados Resortwas built on the site of Fort Charles, which is onthe UNESCO World Heritage List. The walls of Fort Charles are preserved, and you can even see the canons up close!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bridgetown-Fort-in-Barbados.jpg">
          <img alt="Needhams Point Beach in Barbados  at the Hilton Barbados Resort" class="aligncenter wp-image-30311 size-full" height="677" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bridgetown-Fort-in-Barbados.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre lucky, youll catch a few crabs scuttling about on the rocks. Just be careful and dont get too close  they pinch!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bridgetown-Hilton-Beach-in-Barbados.jpg">
          <img alt="Needhams Point Beach in Barbados  at the Hilton Barbados Resort" class="aligncenter wp-image-30314 size-full" height="970" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bridgetown-Hilton-Beach-in-Barbados.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Take a walk along the beach and you just might find the skeleton of a sea urchin washed up on shore
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Sea-Urchin-Skeleton-in-Barbados.jpg">
          <img alt="Needhams Point Beach in Barbados  at the Hilton Barbados Resort" class="aligncenter wp-image-30316 size-full" height="740" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Sea-Urchin-Skeleton-in-Barbados.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There is a lifeguard stand on the beach, but still be wary of the current.
        </p>
        <p>
         The waves are strong and consistent, which is what makes the area so popular for body surfing!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Lifeguard-stand-in-Barbados.jpg">
          <img alt="Needhams Point Beach in Barbados  at the Hilton Barbados Resort" class="aligncenter wp-image-30312 size-full" height="771" src="//therebelchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Lifeguard-stand-in-Barbados.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can read more about the Hilton Barbados Resort
         <a href="http://happytravels.com/hilton-barbados-resort-bridgetown-barbados/" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Have you ever been to Barbados? Id love to hear your stories!
        </p>
