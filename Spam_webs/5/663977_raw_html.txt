        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Strawberry Pizza
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-06-03T01:01:37+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 3, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Leigh Anne
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

          </a>
         </span>

       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="417582" data-link="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/strawberry-pizza/" data-summary="" data-title="Strawberry Pizza">
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Strawberry Pizza
         </strong>
         is a delicious and fun way to serve those fresh ripe strawberries!
         <br/>
         <img alt="strawberry pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21578 pin-it" height="900" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/strawberry-pizza-e1370193069741.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         We picked our first strawberries from the garden this last week. There is nothing like a strawberry fresh out of the garden.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="strawberries" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21579 pin-it" height="400" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/photo4-e1370193154955.jpg" width="400"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I didnt have to think long about what to make with the strawberries. Traditionally one of our familys favorite first strawberries of the season recipes is Strawberry Pizza,a sure sign that strawberry season is here.  It is from my favorite cookbook  Portland Junior League Portland Palate.
        </p>
        <p>
         I never have trouble finding the recipe because it is the dirtiest page in the cookbook  a true sign of a great recipe!
        </p>
        <p>
         This is actually a recipe redo  one I shared back in 2008, not too long after I started the blog. It is totally worthy of a repeat and new photos. It is so good. When Logan saw me post a photo of it on instagram he quickly asked if I would make it again next weekend when he is home visiting!
        </p>
        <p>
         The pizza is made with a sugar cookie type crust, a layer of cream cheese and then strawberries and glaze on top  kind of sounds like the
         <a href="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/friday-favorite-strawberries-and-roses/">
          Strawberry Pie
         </a>
         I have shared  just in a different form! Guess I have a thing for strawberries and cream cheese.
        </p>
        <p>
         When you mix up the crust it will seem a bit crumbly but just use your hands and form it into a ball. It will come together nicely.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21571 pin-it" height="398" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0012-e1370193256817.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Just roll it out with a rolling pin on an ungreased cookie sheet or a baking stone will work too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21572 pin-it" height="398" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0013-e1370193608838.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
        </p>
        <p>
         While it is baking and cooling mix up your cream cheese mixture and your strawberry mixture. Use 1 cup of smashed strawberries. I do mine in the food processor.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21573 pin-it" height="800" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0014.jpg" width="531"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Cook the strawberries with sugar and cornstarch until it thickens and is glossy  will take about 5-10 minutes. Cool.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21574 pin-it" height="398" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0015-e1370193703775.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         After the crust is cool spread with cream cheese mixture you mixed up.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21576 pin-it" height="398" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0017-e1370193639349.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Add strawberry mixture and sliced strawberries.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21570 pin-it" height="398" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0011-e1370193779558.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The crust ends up being a bit fragile so it can be a bit messy to eat. I just use my pizza cutter to slice it up.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21569 pin-it" height="800" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0010.jpg" width="531"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You dont even have to remove it from the cookie sheet because it will disappear before you have a chance! So good!!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Strawberry Pizza" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21568 pin-it" height="398" src="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/Strawberry-Pizza_0009-e1370193832472.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         For other strawberry goodness check out these recipes:

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Crust
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 C flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Filling
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, room temperature
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Topping
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 C fresh strawberries, mashed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 Tbsp granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Tbsp cornstarch
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            10-12 strawberries, sliced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Crust
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a bowl combine flour, sugar and butter. Form into a ball with hands and press or roll into a 14" circle on an ungreased cookie sheet or pizza pan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake at 325 Degrees F for 15 minutes. Cool.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Filling
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a bowl combine cream cheese, vanilla, sugar and lemon juice. Spread on cooled crust.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Topping:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a saucepan over medium heat combine strawberries, sugar and cornstarch. Stir and simmer until thick and glossy. Cool.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread on top of cheese filling.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cut fresh strawberries crosswise to make circles and place on pizza and serve.
           </li>
          </ol>
