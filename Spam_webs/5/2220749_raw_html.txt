         <p>
          Everyday I have the pleasure of going to my job at a retirement home where I am overwhelmingly blessed by my residents who live there.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <center>
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

         <p>
          Tonight during their happy hour, where I serve them various hors doeuvres likescubes of cheddar cheese and crackers, I noticed a book sitting on a side table. It was a book about Ronald Reagan and it was a documentation of his life in photographs.
         </p>
         <p>
          With time to spare between dishing out strawberries on Styrofoam plates and refilling glasses, I took a minute to glance through it. I cracked open the thick book, started flipping through it, and came upon a page showing a picture of Reagan before the assassination attempt and one of him lying on the ground after. I thought to myself how much that mustve shook the nation, as I wasnt alive then to know.
         </p>
         <p>
          I turned the page and there was a giant photo of Reagan lifting weights. I thought, hmmm Ive seen a photo like this before, and immediately looked up President Obamas picture of him lifting weights. I couldnt help but start to laugh as I realized that Reagan had just been shot and was lifting weights as a way to heal, and Obama was struggling to lift half as much while he was perfectly healthy.
         </p>
         <p>
          Take what you want from this but I just had to share because I thought it was funny:
         </p>

          The full page:
         </p>

         <p>
          The contrast:
