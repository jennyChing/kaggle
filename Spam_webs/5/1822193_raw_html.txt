         <img alt="iCollection by Chivote" class="attachment-medium wp-post-image" height="182" src="http://leclubdesdouze.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/iCollection-by-Chivote-300x182.jpg" width="300"/>

       <div class="post-content">
        <h1 class="post-title">
         <a href="http://leclubdesdouze.com/three-perfect-laptop-sleeves/" title="Three Perfect Laptop Sleeves">
          Three Perfect Laptop Sleeves
         </a>
        </h1>
        <p class="post-meta">
         <span class="post-author">
          13 September, 2014
         </span>
         <span class="post-author">
          <a href="http://leclubdesdouze.com/en/author/olivia/" rel="author" title="Posts by Olivia Bonner">
           Olivia Bonner
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="post-category">
          <a href="http://leclubdesdouze.com/category/posts/features/" rel="tag">
           Features
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comment">
          <a href="http://leclubdesdouze.com/three-perfect-laptop-sleeves/#respond" title="Comment on Three Perfect Laptop Sleeves">
           0 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <!-- /.post-meta -->
        <div class="at-above-post addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Three Perfect Laptop Sleeves" data-url="http://leclubdesdouze.com/three-perfect-laptop-sleeves/">
        </div>
        <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
        </div>
        <p>
         I bought my MacBook almost three years ago and must admit that I still havent gotten a case for it. Although I dont travel with it often, when I do, Ive resorted to a scarf-wrapping system that is neither efficient nor
         <em>
          chic
         </em>
         . I think the reason that I put off the search for a laptop sleeve for so long is because I wanted something unlike the ones I see everywhere and I wanted something that was sturdy, attractive and worthy of carrying my laptop. Well the search is over and I found not only one
         <em>
          but three
         </em>
         that I love for different reasons.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-1019">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Chivotes iCollection
         <a href="http://twitter.com/home/?status=Chivote's%20iCollection%20and%20two%20other%20stylish%20laptop%20sleeves.%20http%3A%2F%2Fleclubdesdouze.com/three-perfect-laptop-sleeves/" target="_blank" title="Tweet it">
          [tweet]
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/740337841/icollection-by-chivote" target="_blank" title="Chivote's iCollection">
          <img alt="iCollection by Chivote" class="alignnone wp-image-1020 size-full" height="1215" src="http://leclubdesdouze.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/iCollection-by-Chivote.jpg" width="2000"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://chivote.com/" target="_blank" title="Chivote">
          British brand Chivote
         </a>
         makes a laptop sleeve that reminds me of a sleek version of a file folder. The buttery rich tan leather discreetly holds your laptop in a sleeve with a generous flap to cover it. The best part is that you can fold the case flap and turn it into a stand for your laptop, putting it on an angle and making it easier to type. In case you havent heard of Chivote,
         <a href="http://chivote.com/" target="_blank" title="Chivote">
          take a look at their website.
         </a>
         Youll see that the
         <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/740337841/icollection-by-chivote" target="_blank" title="iCollection Chivote">
          iCollection
         </a>
         isnt the first product they launched through Kickstarter. Their first release
         <a href="http://chivote.com/product/nubuck-2face-backpack-preorder/" target="_blank" title="Chivote 2 Face backpack">
          (the Two Face backpack)
         </a>
         was a beautiful and sturdy piece of British craftsmanship.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/740337841/icollection-by-chivote" target="_blank" title="Shop Chivote's iCollection">
          Click here to call dibs on
          <strong>
           Chivote
          </strong>
          s laptop sleeve and support the iCollection.
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Oppermanns Islington Laptop Sleeve
         <a href="http://twitter.com/home/?status=Oppermann's%20Islington%20case%20and%20two%20other%20stylish%20laptop%20sleeves.%20http%3A%2F%2Fleclubdesdouze.com/three-perfect-laptop-sleeves/" target="_blank" title="Tweet it">
          [tweet]
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.oppermann-london.com/products/islington-navy" target="_blank" title="Oppermann's Islington laptop sleeve">
          <img alt="oppermann" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1021" height="1006" src="http://leclubdesdouze.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/oppermann.jpg" width="1738"/>
         </a>
         In my search for the perfect laptop protection, I also stumbled upon another British leather goods brand that has been on my radar for a while,
         <a href="http://www.oppermann-london.com/" target="_blank" title="Oppermann London">
          Oppermann.
         </a>
         They make Italian leather laptop cases that are minimal but well crafted. Attention to detail is plentiful from the carefully sourced Italian zipper that runs along the top and side to the vegetable tanned leather. The navy color caught my eye immediately. It is a timeless color yet pretty uncommon in a laptop case. It stands apart without ever being showy. This case is simplicity done right.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.oppermann-london.com/products/islington-navy" target="_blank" title="Oppermann's Islington laptop sleeve">
          Shop
          <strong>
           Oppermann
          </strong>
          s sleeves collection here.
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Ateliers Augustes Verneuil Pouch
         <a href="http://twitter.com/home/?status=Ateliers%20Auguste's%20Verneuil%20pouch%20and%20two%20other%20stylish%20laptop%20sleeves.%20http%3A%2F%2Fleclubdesdouze.com/three-perfect-laptop-sleeves/" target="_blank" title="Tweet it">
          [tweet]
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://ateliers-auguste.fr/fr/accessoires/22-verneuil.html" target="_blank" title="Ateliers Auguste Verneuil laptop sleeve">
          <img alt="verneuil" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1022" height="727" src="http://leclubdesdouze.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/verneuil.jpg" width="1277"/>
         </a>
         I wanted to see what the French were doing in laptop cases. Ive said it before and Ill say it again, Im a huge fan and supporter of the new wave of made in France bags and leather accessories. And
         <a href="http://ateliers-auguste.fr/" target="_blank" title="Les Ateliers Auguste">
          Les Ateliers Auguste
         </a>
         is up there on the list.
         <a href="http://ateliers-auguste.fr/fr/accessoires/22-verneuil.html" target="_blank" title="Ateliers Auguste's Verneuil laptop sleeve">
          Their Verneuil computer sleeve
         </a>
         is a more casual version of the accessory, made with a mix of wool felt and leather. I had not considered a case that was not entirely leather until I saw this one. Other fabrics I saw did not seem like they would have longevity, but I feel confident that the sturdy wool felt will hold up over time.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://ateliers-auguste.fr/fr/accessoires/22-verneuil.html" target="_blank" title="Les Ateliers Auguste's Verneuil Pouch">
          Buy Les
          <strong>
           Ateliers Auguste
          </strong>
          s Verneuil laptop sleeve here.
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So what does you think? Which one is your favorite?
         <a href="http://www.twitter.com/leclubdesdouze" target="_blank" title="Le club des douze on Twitter">
          Join the conversation on Twitter and let us know!
         </a>
        </p>
