           <b>
            Russians
           </b>
           are an East Slavic ethnic group native to Russia, who speak the Russian language and primarily live in Russia. They are the most numerous ethnic group in Russia constituting more than 80% of the countrys population according to the census of 2010, and the most numerous ethnic group in Europe. At last, you have a splendid opportunity to see ordinary Russian faces, to know how true Russian people look like.
