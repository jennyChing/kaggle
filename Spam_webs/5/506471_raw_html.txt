        <h3 class="p-show p-head">
         How It Works
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p-hide">
         What It Is
        </h3>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Chicago CityPASS
         </strong>
         gives you admission to 5 must-see tourist attractions in Chicago. Save $101.95 per adult and $86.90 per youth, ages 311. Prices subject to change.
        </p>
        <div class="p-hide">
         <p>
          <strong>
           Option tickets
          </strong>
          allow you entry to one of two attractions. No need to decide now  choose when you get there.
         </p>
         <p>
          CityPASS booklets are
          <strong>
           valid for nine consecutive days
          </strong>
          beginning with the first day of use. Booklets purchased today will expire on February 28, 2017.
         </p>
         <p class="skip-lines-content">
          <strong>
           Skip most ticket lines
          </strong>
          once you have your ticket booklet. (You may encounter a line when you exchange a voucher for a booklet.)
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Booklets include
          </strong>
          one-time admission tickets, detailed attraction information, coupons and a map.
         </p>
         <h3>
          How To Buy
         </h3>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Buy Now
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Choose delivery option during checkout: Email voucher or ship ticket booklets. We accept Visa, Master Card, American Express, Discover, PayPal, UnionPay or Amazon Payments.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Buy at an Attraction
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Chicago CityPASS ticket booklets are sold at all Chicago CityPASS attractions for the
          <strong>
           same low price
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          How To Use
         </h3>
         <p>
          In Chicago, you have two options for accessing your voucher:
          <strong>
           Printable PDF Voucher
          </strong>
          or
          <strong>
           Mobile Voucher
          </strong>
          . Voucher must be presented in exchange for CityPASS ticket booklets within 6 months of purchase. ID may be required for voucher exchange. Exchange locations are included on voucher and may differ depending on which voucher you use. (Skip this step if you already have ticket booklet in hand.)
         </p>
         <p>
          With booklet in hand, you may start using your CityPASS at any attraction and visit the attractions in any order.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Present ticket booklets
          </strong>
          at each attraction. See each ticket front for entry instructions.
         </p>
         <p>
          Do not pull a ticket from the booklet or it will be invalid.
          <strong>
           Tickets removed by attraction
          </strong>
          staff only.
         </p>

You get to cut the line in basically every attraction which is awesome! The booklet will tell you how to get to all the attractions driving and using public transportation. It also includes a number of other coupons. You only have 9 days to use it but you can do it in 3 days if you plan it out properly. Make sure to calculate all the parking prices because its not included. Sometimes it would be much more beneficial to take public transportation or a taxi. Most of the attractions are in walking distance to the loop except for the Museum of Science and Industry. The Museum of Science and Industry is pretty far out of the way and parking costs $22. The CityPASS is definitely worth it if you are interested in seeing all the attractions! I will definitely be using it again for a different city.
