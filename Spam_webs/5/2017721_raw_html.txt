      <h1 class="omc-post-heading-standard">
       USA Today Maps Vape-Friendly Airports
      </h1>
      <p>
       USA Today has posted an informative map of which US airports allow vaping in their terminals, and which dont.
      </p>
      <p>
       While vaping is taboo on flights, several airports do allow it while youre on the ground, including international airports in Phoenix and Las Vegas, which are common transfer points for multi-stop flights. Several other airports allow vaping as well, though the issue is still under consideration at many of them.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/travel/flights/2013/12/04/airports-e-cigarettes-rules/3783347/">
        Some airports give green light to e-cigarettes | USA Today
       </a>
      </p>
