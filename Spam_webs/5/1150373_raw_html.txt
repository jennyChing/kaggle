        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name" style="display:none;">
         A little truths about latex
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
          1. Rubber trees only grow within about 10 degrees from the equator. They grow best in a hilly area.
          <br/>
          2. When cut, the incision must be made in a special angle, and a small amount of juice every day is going into special containers.
          <br/>
          3. Trees can be incised for about 180 days per year, except for the rainy season and the summer. The incision is about 1m long, and the juice is flowing out of it for about an hour. Then the tree is healing, and the juice stops flowing.
          <br/>
          4. Latex originally grown in Brazil, but now almost all the latex comes from the East.
          <br/>
          5. 2/3 of the juice is water.
          <br/>
          6. Latex juice in its pure form is not suitable for the manufacture of latex (it coagulates) and must be further processed.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://newlatexmattress.com/truths-latex/collecting-rubber-latex/">
           <img alt="A little truths about latex" class="alignnone wp-image-116" data-lazy-original="http://newlatexmattress.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/collecting-rubber-latex.jpg" height="500" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="750"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="A little truths about latex" class="alignnone wp-image-116" height="500" src="//newlatexmattress.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/collecting-rubber-latex.jpg" width="750"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          <br/>
          7. A small amount of ammonia is added to the juice of wood, so it does not clot before processing. Ammonia is a natural product, which is washed off in a later processing.
          <br/>
          8. Now we need to make latex foam. This can be done by adding a special component and foaming material in a centrifuge.
          <br/>
          9. Latex contains naturally occurring proteins that for some people are intolerant. Overall latex allergy is low, less than 1 % of the population.
          <br/>
          10. What if you want a mattress made from 100 % pure natural latex? In fact, you can find a product, that contains mostly 95 % natural latex. If someone tells you that they have a mattress from a 100 % natural latex in their finished product, they are misinformed. That kind of material does not exist.
          <br/>
          11. What kind of material are the remaining 5 % that is not latex? Of course everyone says that it is safe, and thats probably true.
          <br/>
          12. Are the remaining 5 % important for my health? The remaining 5 % is made from sulfur and other substances for stabilization. A deeper sleep and longer intervals of sleep, which give us the
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            latex mattress
           </strong>
          </span>
          and its hypo-allergenic qualities, have a much greater positive impact on health than any possible effect of a small drop unnatural material.
          <br/>
          13. How long lasts the
          <em>
           latex mattress
          </em>
          ? Regardless of the used technology and the different percentage the natural material, the latex is very durable. When used properly latex mattresses can serve you 30 years or more.
         </p>
