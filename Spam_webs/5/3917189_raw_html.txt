      Springtime Photos
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">

       March 26, 2014
      </time>
     </div>

       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         Wow, what a winter. Some states got dumped with snow, while others dealt with extreme drought. However, we can all be excited about one thing: spring is here!
        </p>
        <p>
         Its such a refreshing time of year, and were already beginning to see natural changes. Beautiful flowers peek out of the ground, temperatures even outeverything just
         <em>
          feels
         </em>
         optimistic.
        </p>
        <p>
         To celebrate the seasonal changes, weve asked some bloggers to share what spring looks like in their area. Weve included their photos below, and we want to see yours too!
        </p>
        <p>
         Just snap a photo that says spring to you and share it on Instagram with the hashtag #PFspring. Well share our favorites on our feed and tag you! Not a photo pro? Not to worry! Check out our
         <a href="http://www.proflowers.com/blog/instagram-photo-tips" target="new">
          mobile photography tips blog post
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         So, what does spring look like to you?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Heather Hills" height="500" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/image.proflowers.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/HeatherHills_blog140326" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="500"/>
        </p>
