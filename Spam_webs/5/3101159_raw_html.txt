        <p>
         I love a good margarita! Its one of those drinks that you can personalize to your own specific tastes.You want it sweet, add some fresh strawberry puree. You want it spicy, add a little sriracha. The sky is the limit with the flavor profiles that can go into a great margarita.
        </p>
        <p>
         This Strawberry Sriracha Margarita is one of my favorite recipes. Its the perfect blend of sweet and spicy. Throw in some fresh ingredients and you have a margarita worthy of any summertimeparty.
         <em>
          I hope you enjoy it!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.rickontherocks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberrysrirachamargarita-e1399232311635.jpg">
          <img alt="strawberry sriracha margarita recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-665" height="981" src="http://www.rickontherocks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/strawberrysrirachamargarita-e1399232311635.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Strawberry Sriracha Margarita Recipe
        </h2>
        <p>
         To make the fresh strawberry puree, freeze 3 de-stemmed strawberries. Once frozen, put them in blender with 3 Tbsp of water and blend until smooth. Youre going to build the margarita in the blender. (Make sure you
         <strong>
          Do Not
         </strong>
         add ice)
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 1/2 oz of Silver Tequila
         </li>
         <li>
          3 oz of Margarita Mix
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 tsp of Sriracha 
          <em>
           You can add more or less depending on how spicy you want it
          </em>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Blend your mixture for 15 seconds
         </li>
         <li>
          Make a simple syrup by microwaving 2 tbsp sugar &amp; 2 tbsp waterfor 20 seconds, stir vigorously and pour onto small plate.
         </li>
         <li>
          Put the rim of your glass into the simple syrup allowing excess to drip off and then dip into salt/sugar/chile powder mixture:1 tbsp kosher salt +1 tbsp sugar +1/2 tsp chile powder
         </li>
         <li>
          Fill the glass with ice and pour in the contents of the blender.
         </li>
         <li>
          Garnish with fresh lime and strawberry.
         </li>
        </ol>
