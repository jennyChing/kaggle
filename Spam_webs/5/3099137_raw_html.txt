               </strong>
               The most common reaction our clients get from customers is No Way! Theres nothing cooler than seeing the look on a potential customers face when it clicks and they realize that they can get a fully custom phone case right here &amp; right now. Until now, making a custom phone case has been possible only online, and even then you have to deal with long production waits, high shipping costs, and difficulty designing your case online. Now you can offer your customers this service for a lower price than online competitors and theyll get their case in 10 minutes or less with no hassle. Now thats awesome.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               Theres truly something to be said for the power of instant gratification on the minds of shoppers. The entire experience of retail shopping is designed to satisfy our human need for instant gratification. Thats the reason we have such a vast number of shopping malls, fairs, and gift shows; to provide our communities with that easy access to what they want. Im much more likely to buy something if I can walk away with it right away. Thats why the Case Escape Startup Kit has been experiencing such incredible results.
              </p>
