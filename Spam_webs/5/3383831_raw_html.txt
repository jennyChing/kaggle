         Anti-Valentines Day: Top 5 Worst Excuses
        </h1>

        <img alt="" src="http://static2.batcdn.com/uploadedfiles/Blog/files/2014/2/Main-w700-h600.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
        <p>
         If the thought of Valentines Day, with its hues of red and pink, its musky rose smell, and its tendency to whack people over the head with a heavy load of hearts and chocolates makes you want to hide in bed with the covers pulled up over your head until the (glitter) dust settles, you are not alone. For those of us who are more, shall we say, pragmatic in nature, Valentines Day looms around the corner as January comes to a close, like a perfumed, bejewelled golem, coming to beat the cynicism out of us with flower bouquets and cutesy greeting cards.
        </p>
        <p>
         Unless youve been lucky enough to find a partner willing to take shelter from the oncoming storm and marathon-watch zombie apocalypse films with you until the cupids fly away, you may need to take heed of these brilliant-even-if-I-do-say-so-myself excuses to get out of Valentine duty. Because, honestly, when has the dog ate the diamond ring I bought you ever worked?
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           The SMS Escape
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         You wake up on the morning of February 14 to a text message from your boyfriend, who waxes lyrical about your beauty and grace and the wonderful dinner he is going to prepare for you tonight. If this causes you to break out in hives, do not panic; the solution is simple: explain to your smitten sweetheart that your very good friend is going through a terrible time and needs you to be with her tonight. Or that your sister has asked you to babysit her kids at the very last minute, and you simply cant get out of it. Whatever the excuse, it should imply you are a Very Generous and Selfless Person. We can celebrate Valentines next week! you must enthuse, before hanging up on your beau. You may then proceed to spend the rest of the day eating fried chicken in your sweatpants and watching Jeremy Kyle re-runs while wondering while your relationships never seem to work out
         <br/>
         <img alt="Funny SMS lost phone" src="http://static2.batcdn.com/uploadedfiles/Blog/files/2014/2/funny-sms-lost-phone.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center!important;">
         <span style="font-size: x-small;">
          SMS: Saving us from face-to-face confrontation every day
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Feigning Ignorance
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         This one works wonders if you are foreign, and even better if you come from an obscure country like I do. Any Valentines Day advances should be met by a confused expression and a wide-eyed What is this Valentines Day? Practice your face of bewilderment in the mirror so you can use it when your partner patiently explains the tradition, then insist that youve never heard of it and suggest you go back to the rock youve been living under and watch subtitled movies together. Fool-proof.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center!important;">
         <img alt="" src="http://static2.batcdn.com/uploadedfiles/Blog/files/2014/2/Ignorance-w700-h600.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
         <span style="font-size: x-small; text-align: center!important;">
          Valen-what-now?
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Forgotten Something?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Imagine youve actually deigned to make dinner reservations at a pretty upscale restaurant  kudos to you, man! However, as your girlfriend walks through the door, the whole room lights up. No, its not her beauty and radiance; its that light bulb blinking figuratively on top of your head as you realise that youve gone and forgotten to buy a present. Breathe. Find a picture of a flashy bit of jewellery on your smartphone and show it to her (for this to work, you must have a convincing brow-furrow of disappointment at all times). Explain you have ordered this gift for her but it seems to have been stuck in the mail. The plus side: tell her shes still in time to cancel it and choose another gift, ensuring she gets the perfect present and you are elevated to the status of Best Boyfriend Ever. The down side: youre still going to have to buy that present; you didnt really think you could get out of this one, did you?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center!important;">
         <img alt="" src="http://static2.batcdn.com/uploadedfiles/Blog/files/2014/2/forgotten-w700-h600.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
         <span style="font-size: x-small;">
          Present-induced panic
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           I think Im Contagious
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im sick. Ah, an excuse thats even older than the sword that martyred St Valentine (so romantic!) and applicable to your cause. Even if your boyfriend thinks the sun shines out of your eyes, nobody wants to go on a romantic date with a snivelling, sick person  especially not with such a high chance that dessert will be shared. Text your beloved and say your throat hurts and you cant talk, and then send about three other messages saying how terribly sorry you are. Keep in mind that if you get away with it and for some reason youre silly enough to go out anyway after this little charade, you can be sure you will bump into your abandoned lover and it WILL be awkward. You know what they say about Karma
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center!important;">
         <img alt="" src="http://static2.batcdn.com/uploadedfiles/Blog/files/2014/2/ill-w700-h600.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
         <span style="font-size: x-small;">
          Put it in hot water: works a treat
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Singleton Bliss
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youre single. Youre single! You, my friend, can breathe a deep sigh of relief. This marshmallowy torture does not apply to you at all! Life has given you the best excuse to help you pretend Valentines isnt a thing. Blessed are the unattached, for they are free to let their hair down, pick up their fellow unattached friends, and go be awesome together. Who cares that your mum keeps hinting at her desire for grandchildren when Happy Hour is on at your local?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center!important;">
         <img alt="" src="http://static2.batcdn.com/uploadedfiles/Blog/files/2014/2/Single-w700-h600.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <span style="font-size: x-small;">
          Keep things simple
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Yes, I know this has been inspirational and enlightening, et cetera. Youre welcome. Thing is, though, if youre reading this, heres a reminder that you should actually go ahead and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.bookatable.com">
           book a table
          </a>
         </strong>
         at a nice restaurant and enjoy a meal with the person who, for some strange reason, seems enthusiastic about spending time with you. Go on. The zombie apocalypse can wait til another day.
        </p>
