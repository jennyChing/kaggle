While Patriot fans attempt to minimize the impact of cheating, well, no one got hurt, the cheater had the book thrown at him Monday evening, with the NFL suspending the man whom they love so much, that theyve actually commanded refs to penalize players who upset him. &lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
