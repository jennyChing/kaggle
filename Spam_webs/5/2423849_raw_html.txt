         <h1 class="post-title" property="dc:title">
          Montego Bay Jamaica is The Number One Vacation Spot.
         </h1>
        </span>
       </span>
       <span class="post-content" property="dc:content" style="display:block;width:710px;overflow:hidden;">
        Known as the Friendly City, nestled on the northwestern region of Jamaica with a population of approximately 150,000 people;  it is the number one vacation spot on the island.  Montego Bay Jamaica is a city that is filled with vibrance, color, and spirit. The name Montego Bay was derived from the word Manteca which is the Spanish word for lard. Back then the area was the main hunting ground for wild hogs which the Spaniards used to make hog butter. As a result, on some historic maps Montego Bay is shown as Lard Bay.
        <br/>
        <img border="0" class="imagen" src="http://k33.kn3.net/socialphy/0/1/6/2/9/3/stevertm/81D.jpg"/>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Dr. Mcatty, a local Doctor is credited for the early tourism developmental years of Montego Bay; he believed that the now illustrious Doctors Cave Beach had healing proprieties.  Dr. Mcatty  was one of the owner of this lovely beach when one of his friends who was suffering from arthritis used the beach and felt some level of relieved from his pains.  The news spread effortlessly and in the early 1920s wealthy aristocrats from various parts of the world visited this beautiful beach for its curative benefits.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Despite not being the biggest city in Jamaica, Montego Bay plays an integral role in the country's economy. It is home to Jamaica's busiest airport Sangster International and seaport alike. This flamboyant city is nicknamed the gateway of the Caribbean with flight connections to and from other neighboring islands.  Montego Bay plays host to the annual reggae festival dubbed Reggae Sumfest, and also Jamaica Jazz and Blues festival; an annual event also that attracts a fine blend of international and local artists.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img border="0" class="imagen" src="http://k33.kn3.net/socialphy/0/1/6/2/9/3/stevertm/306.jpg"/>
        <br/>
        Montego Bay has a wide array of hotels with different price range complimented by an extensive assortment of bars, shops, in-bond stores, craft-markets and restaurants all  in close proximity add to the its distinctiveness which all make Montego Bay an attractive vacation get-away town.
       </span>
