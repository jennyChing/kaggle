       <h1>
        Baby Slippers will probably scar your children
       </h1>
       <div class="info-post">
        by
        <span>
         ally
        </span>
        - on February 24th, 2009
       </div>
       <div class="post-content-area">
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1886" height="327" src="http://foolishgadgets.com/files/2009/02/504x_slippers.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         For those of you that found the
         <a href="http://foolishgadgets.com/200902/the-baby-mop-for-truly-responsible-parents/" target="_blank">
          Baby Mop
         </a>
         immature and started ranting about something to do with irresponsible parenting, you might want to look away. I somehow doubt that those that were appalled by the Baby Mop will see the humor in these Baby Slippers. I assure you, no actual babies were harmed in the making of these slippers, just fake plastic ones. Tragically, these arent being sold anywhere, so well just have to stare longingly at this picture of Chewbacca modeling the Baby Slippers.
        </p>
        <p>
         Source:
         <a href="http://www.slipperybrick.com/2009/02/if-baby-slippers-are-wrong-i-dont-wanna-be-right/" target="_blank">
          SlipperyBrick
         </a>
        </p>
