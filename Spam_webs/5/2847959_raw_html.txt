             <h1 class="main-title">
              Who is the most hated celeb in Hollywood?
             </h1>
             <h2 class="sub-title">
              Top 20 irritating stars revealed...
             </h2>
             <p class="published">
              Fri 19 Apr 2013
             </p>
            </header>
            <div class="content">
             <div class="content-body">
              <h3>
               Top 20 irritating stars revealed...
              </h3>
              <p>
               Star magazine has revealed the results of their annual poll of the most hated celebrities in Hollywood.
              </p>
              <p>
               Drum roll... The A-lister to scoop the dubious top spot is.... GwynethPaltrow. Yes, her age-defying looks and macrobiotic-munching seem to just rub people up the wrong way.
              </p>
              <p>
               Close on her designer heels in the unpopularity stakes is
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/211/Kristen-Stewart">
                Kristen Stewart
               </a>
               . Her dalliance with a certain director behind
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/210/Robert-Pattinson">
                R-Patz
               </a>
               's back appears to have lost her her Twilight fans.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/33/Jennifer-Lopez">
                Jennifer Lopez
               </a>
               scored third place, her bootylicious bottom apparently becoming a turn-off rather than a turn-on this year.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/news/film/8467/James-Franco-discusses-why-people-hate-Anne-Hathaway">
                <b>
                 RELATED:James Franco discusses why people "hate" Anne Hathaway
                </b>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Other stars who fell into the Top 20 include Madonna,
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/221/Kim-Kardashian">
                Kim Kardashian
               </a>
               and Lindsay Lohan. Men did not escape either, with
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/41/Justin-Bieber">
                Justin Bieber
               </a>
               , John Mayer and
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/86/Chris-Brown">
                Chris Brown
               </a>
               being amongst the most hated.
              </p>
              <p>
               Given her recent jibes by the press, Anne Hathaway may be pleased to find she's only the 9th most hated celeb in Hollywood.
              </p>
              <p>
               Star magazine's top 20 Most Hated Celebrities in Hollywood in full:
              </p>
              <p>
               1. Gwyneth Paltrow
              </p>
              <p>
               2. Kristen Stewart
              </p>
              <p>
               3. Jennifer Lopez
              </p>
              <p>
               4. John Mayer
              </p>
              <p>
               5. Katherine Heigl
              </p>
              <p>
               6. Matt Lauer
              </p>
              <p>
               7. Madonna
              </p>
              <p>
               8. Justin Bieber
              </p>
              <p>
               9. Anne Hathaway
              </p>
              <p>
               10. Kris Jenner
              </p>
              <p>
               11. Kim Kardashian
              </p>
              <p>
               12. LeAnn Rimes
              </p>
              <p>
               13. Ashton Kutcher
              </p>
              <p>
               14. Jay Leno
              </p>
              <p>
               15. Angelina Jolie
              </p>
              <p>
               16. Lindsay Lohan
              </p>
              <p>
               17. Shia LaBeouf
              </p>
              <p>
               18.
               <a href="http://www.4music.com/artists/91/Taylor-Swift">
                Taylor Swift
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               19. Jesse James
              </p>
              <p>
               20. Chris Brown
              </p>
              <p>
