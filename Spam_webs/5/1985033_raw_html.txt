      <h2 class="entry-title">
       Pinning Geeks Down
      </h2>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        If Twilight is Taco Bell and C is for Cookie, then Pinterest is smuggling a pint of ice cream home on a warm day and eating the entire thing yourself.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_6046" style="width: 310px">
        <a href="http://notyourmommascookie.com/2012/04/game-of-thrones-cake-pops/">
         <img alt="The king can do as he likes" class="size-medium wp-image-6046 " height="200" src="http://thecoolship.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Stark-pops-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Disturbing and delicious! Thanks, Pinterest! Image courtesy of Not Your Momma's Cookie
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Let me explain, and dont judge. Weve all done it. One scoop turns into half the carton since its so comically small and the spoon is so big. And then, well, there isnt enough left to really make it worth saving. And the next thing you know youre wearing mint chocolate chip all over your face and praying that a just God exists, that She likes ice cream too, and thats Shell simultaneously remove your well-earned belly ache and conquer your brain freeze.
       </p>
       <p>
        Gluttony is like #6 on the Seven Deadly Sins. Its the lesser Baldwins of vice.
       </p>
       <p>
        Ok, but back to
        <a href="http://pinterest.com/" target="_blank">
         Pinterest
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Last week, I talked about my wandering through the halls and falls of geekdom, looking for my personal passion.
       </p>
       <p>
        My friends counseled me and offered their personal preferences. And, Im going to look into them. The next few weeks, here and there, Ill offer updates on that front. But I had more lackadaisical research to do. Rather than Google Geek Communities or something equally insane, which, as you probably have guessed was my first instinct (122,000,000 results), I thought Id check out the Geek boards in Pinterest.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_6045" style="width: 223px">
        <a href="www.dorkly.com/picture/37190/the-origin-of-godzilla">
         <img alt="What have you done, Mario? He's huge and tipping...we'll he's tripping something." class="size-medium wp-image-6045 " height="300" src="http://thecoolship.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Mario-213x300.jpg" width="213"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         I've made a huge mistake. Image courtesy of Dorkly
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        What was organically bubbling up from people like me  semimotivated geeks who like to look at pretty pictures?
       </p>
       <p>
        The category is spectacularly diverse! Ned Stark cake pops next to cats next to stunning
        <em>
         Anastasia
        </em>
        cosplay next to astronomy next to Doctor Who. An almost disturbing amount of Doctor Who I mean there was a lot and at least 65% of it was awesome.
       </p>
       <p>
        Thats a high number coming from an enthusiastic cynic like myself. (Also, I know that one or two enthusiastic Doctor Who fans can skew how a category appears.)
       </p>
       <p>
        Yeah, if Pinterest is ice cream, this category is like the time that I got to tour the ice cream factory. (For real, it was the UDF factory, and it was the first time Id ever had mint chocolate chip ice cream. You remember major life moments like that. We also got a coupon for a free ice cream afterward and Mom actually took us to get ice cream immediately after having ice cream! Man, that was the best day ever.)
       </p>
       <p>
        Like every time I go onto this time vampire, I pin a lot of sweet projects that Ill never really do. Will I ever, really make an origami Yoda? Probably not, but it is now due to pure apathy not ignorance. So, thats something to be proud of.
       </p>
       <p>
        Side note: Ill be seeing the Star Wars movies in May and that R2D2-sized hole in my geeksperience will be filled.
       </p>
       <p>
        I loved this board. It really nicely summed up that geeks cant be summed up. It was particularly friendly for the newbie like myself.
       </p>
       <p>
        Ill keep examining these boards in my attempt to become a better geek, though theyre a little too big to go in without an objective or guidance (That is how you suddenly look around like a lushy Rip Van Winkle to realize that the wine and 5 hours are gone.).
       </p>
       <p>
        How do you use Pinterest? Do you use it with a focused subject or just to kill time?
       </p>
