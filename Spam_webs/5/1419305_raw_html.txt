        <p>
         I LOVE hosting a good Halloween Party! Making Halloween themed recipes are my favorite part of planning the party.
        </p>

         This week, Ive got another great Halloween Giveaway from
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1dmGFNt" target="_blank">
          Juicy Juice.
         </a>
         One lucky winner will receive a fun Halloween Party Kit so they can throw a spooktacular party for their kids!
        </p>
        <p>
         Juicy Juice asked me to give you tips for making Halloween fun and memorable. So of course I had to share my favorite party foods that I have made over the years!
        </p>
        <p>
         Besides fun food, I always have juice boxes for the kids. This year, Im excited about the new
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1dmGFNt" target="_blank">
          Juicy Juice Fruitfuls
         </a>
         which are made with 35% less sugar and a full serving of fruit. With less sugar, its a great way to give kids a break from all of the candy they will be eating!

         <a href="http://bit.ly/1dmGFNt">

A Juicy Juice Giveaway LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-22467" height="736" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Juicy-Juice-Halloween.jpg" title="13 Halloween Party Recipe Ideas. A Juicy Juice Giveaway LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>

        <h3>
         My Favorite 13 Monsterous Halloween Recipe Ideas
        </h3>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2008/10/8th-annual-locurto-halloween-bash/" target="_blank">
           Mummy Wrapped Hot Dogs
          </a>
           Nothing is easier than wrapping crescent rolls around a hot dog to create mummies.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2008/10/8th-annual-locurto-halloween-bash/" target="_blank">
           Easy Spider Cake
          </a>
           Take a store bought chocolate cake, add a face with candy or icing, then add some pipe cleaners for spider legs.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2009/10/halloween-boofett/" target="_blank">
           Caramel Apples
          </a>
           I love drizzling chocolate over mine!
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2010/09/owl-smores/" target="_blank">
           Owl SMores
          </a>
           Always a favorite treat at my house.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2010/10/easy-halloween-eyeball-pudding-treat/" target="_blank">
           Monster Pudding Treats
          </a>
           A few marshmallows and green sprinkles can turn this simple dish into a creepy treat!
         </li>
         <li>
          Simple Cupcakes with cute toppers. Get my free printable Happy Halloween Spider Tags
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2009/10/halloween-boofett/" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2009/10/halloween-boofett/" target="_blank">
           Lady Fingers
          </a>
           Always my personal favorite!
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2012/10/halloween-mummy/" target="_blank">
           Mummy Pizza Bites
          </a>
           Such an easy snack!
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2011/10/halloween-recipe-brains/" target="_blank">
           Brains in a Jar
          </a>
           Dont worry, its really cake!
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2012/10/halloween-fun-food/" target="_blank">
           Jack oLantern Grilled Cheese Sandwiches
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2008/10/8th-annual-locurto-halloween-bash/" target="_blank">
           Carrot Eyeball Appetizers
          </a>
           Healthy and creepy!
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2009/10/halloween-boofett/" target="_blank">
           Mummy Candy Pretzels
          </a>
           These simple treats will go fast at any party.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2008/10/8th-annual-locurto-halloween-bash/" target="_blank">
           Dip in a creepy pumpkin
          </a>
           Serve up your favorite dip inside a small pumpkin decorated with googly eyes and dracula teeth!
         </li>
        </ol>
