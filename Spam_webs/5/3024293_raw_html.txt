      <h1 class="csc-firstHeader">
       <h2>
        CREATING AN IMPACT ON THE WAY SPORT IS MANAGED WORLDWIDE
       </h2>
      </h1>
     </div>
     <!--  Header: [end] -->
     <!--  Text: [begin] -->
     <p class="bodytext">
      The International Centre for Sports Studies (CIES) is an independent study centre located in Neuchtel, Switzerland. It was created in 1995 as a joint venture between the Fdration Internationale de Football Association (
      <a href="http://www.fifa.com" target="_blank">
       FIFA
      </a>
      ), the
      <a href="http://www.unine.ch" target="_blank">
       University of Neuchtel
      </a>
      , the
      <a href="http://www.neuchatelville.ch" target="_blank">
       City
      </a>
      and
      <a href="http://www.ne.ch" target="_blank">
       State
      </a>
      of Neuchatel. Using a multi-disciplinary approach CIES provides
      <a href="research/about-cies-research/" target="_self">
       research
      </a>
      , top-level
      <a href="education/about-cies-education/" target="_self">
       education
      </a>
      and
      <a href="consulting/about-cies-consulting/" target="_self">
       consulting
      </a>
      services to the sports world with the aim of overcoming the complexities of sport in todays society and improving how it is governed and managed across all sports.
      <a href="cies/about-cies/" target="_self">
       Read more...
      </a>
     </p>
