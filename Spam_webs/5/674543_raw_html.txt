          First Trimester Pregnancy: Stage of Excitement And Caution
         </h1>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          First trimester pregnancy is very confusing and excited stage of the pregnancy. The pregnancy period is of 40 weeks in which it is split into the three trimesters. In these three trimesters you feel the change in your body and also in your baby.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The first trimester is very crucial for the expecting mothers because at this time the fetus is adjust in your body and your body is also preparing for your baby. Many times if you dont care of yourself then it becomes very harmful for you and your baby.
         </p>

         <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
          Symptoms during the first trimester pregnancy
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The
          <strong>
           first trimester pregnancy
          </strong>
          is the time in which you have many things to think about like choose the doctor, what happens to next. This is the very risky pregnancy period in which you have to be very careful about everything that you door even eats. You have to know about all the symptoms you have to face during the
          <em>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://http://www.acutezmedia.com/health/pregnancy-by-weeks-know-the-development-of-your-baby/" target="_blank">
             pregnancy by weeks
            </a>
           </strong>
          </em>
          and first trimester pregnancy.
          <a href="http://www.acutezmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/first-trimester.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="First Trimester Pregnancy: Stage of Excitement And Caution" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2544" height="250" src="http://www.acutezmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/first-trimester.jpg" title="first trimester pregnancy" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          During the first to fifth weeks of
          <strong>
           <em>
            <a href="http://http://www.acutezmedia.com/health/pregnancy-period-cope-with-the-changes/" target="_blank">
             pregnancy period
            </a>
           </em>
          </strong>
          there may be small amount of bleeding or spotting you experience. You dont have to worry about it because it happens due to implantation of the blastocyst into the uterus. You can find out due date by
          <strong>
           <a href="http://http://www.acutezmedia.com/health/women-health/pregnancy-calculator-solve-the-mystery-of-due-date/" target="_blank">
            <em>
             pregnancy calculator
            </em>
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is also called as the implantation bleeding which is totally normal during
          <em>
           first trimester pregnancy
          </em>
          . Now further on wards in fourth week the embryo is establish into the uterus and in the fifth week this should be developed three layers namely ectoderm, mesoderm and endoderm.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Now you enter into the sixth week which gives you the exciting news that the umbilical cord is starts to develop, formation of eyes and ears are begun. You may be lose or gain your weight during this and this is crucial you have to know that you neither remain hungry nor eat too much in the first trimester pregnancy.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          If you dont eat well nutrition diet in paramount then you feel morning sickness and nausea so it is very important to eat well. Your waistline is now become expands in the eighth week of
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           first trimester pregnancy
          </span>
          . In this stage your body releases too many hormones and your body is adjust these during this you have to deal with the pimples and blotches on your face.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          As ninth and tenth week is comes you feels that your breast becomes more sensitive and you feel your mood swings with small bit of morning sickness. Now the stage is comes when your body is get stable all hormones and you feel better. It may be happens some strong smell bothers you still that happens in starting weeks.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          During the twelfth week of the first trimester pregnancy your uterus is now shift towards upward and forward as it grows. Now if you are irritated with the frequent urination then your bathroom trips is comes to an end due to less pressure on your bladder. All the symptoms like morning sickness and feeling tired is now subsided.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Some exercises and diet for first trimester pregnancy
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The period of first trimester pregnancy needs much more care in everything related to the expecting mother. The first trimester pregnancy is the base of the expecting mother and the baby also so you have to be very careful in taking diet and doing some workout also. As the amniotic fluid is not formed around the fetus due to which small mistake may be becomes big.
          <a href="http://www.acutezmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pregnancy01.jpg" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="First Trimester Pregnancy: Stage of Excitement And Caution" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2545" height="300" src="http://www.acutezmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pregnancy01.jpg" title="first trimester pregnancy" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          In this time expecting mothers are experiencing the morning sickness and nausea as a
          <strong>
           <a href="http://http://www.acutezmedia.com/health/women-health/signs-of-pregnancy-new-life-knocks-at-doorstep/" target="_blank">
            <em>
             signs of pregnancy
            </em>
           </a>
          </strong>
          so the healthy and nutritious diet is must in the pregnancy. You have to eat in small quantity five times during the day. There are many tips for the well balanced diet in your first trimester pregnancy but you have to consult to your gynecologist.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Your diet must contain protein which is very useful for the pregnant women. You have to take more and vegetables and fruits in your daily meal. The fiber contain food is very important for the diet in first trimester pregnancy. Some others like calcium, minerals and mainly iron is also included in the diet for the expecting mother. More important thing is drinking water in large amount as you can drink. This can helps you a lot in cure of nausea. The
          <strong>
           <a href="http://http://www.acutezmedia.com/health/women-health/pregnancy-test-take-first-step-towards-pregnancy/" target="_blank">
            <em>
             pregnancy test
            </em>
           </a>
          </strong>
          is must in first trimester pregnancy.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Many people say that exercise in the first trimester pregnancy is must be avoided. But doctors in many cases advice to the expecting mothers to do very light exercises if this will not feel you hurting. Some light exercises are incline treadmill walking, wall squat and yoga. These exercises are done with the small intervals and slowly. The first trimester pregnancy is full of excitement and care also.
         </p>
