                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Lady Bug in Love Art for Valentine's Day
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-394900807231670934" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     I had a love/hate relationship while coloring my lady bug art, but we finally came to a compromise. I had too much of a heavy hand while coloring the black shell and heart spots so the detail in the line art was lost. However, I think the bolder shapes will reproduce better when I shrink it for the pendants my mom wants to make. Plus, I have the original ink illustration available as a
                     <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/175951601/lady-bug-coloring-page-unique-valentine" target="_blank">
                      unique digital stamp
                     </a>
                     on Etsy. I hope paper crafters will be enticed to buy the Art Nouveau design for Valentine's Day - I'd love to see how other creative souls would add color.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <img alt="Lady Bug Drawing by aquariann" border="0" src="http://aquariann.com/art/insect-ladybug.jpg" style="cursor: pointer; display: block; margin: 0px auto 10px; text-align: center; width: 555px;"/>
                     <br/>
                     Lady bug drawing is inked with black
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0008G8G8Y?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=aquariann-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0008G8G8Y" target="_blank">
                      Sakura Pigma Micron Pens
                     </a>
                     and colored with
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001TQ8JWA?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=aquariann-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B001TQ8JWA" target="_blank">
                      Prismacolor pencils
                     </a>
                     onto ACEO cut
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0027ACP0O/ref=as_li_tf_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=aquariann-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=217145&amp;creative=399373&amp;creativeASIN=B0027ACP0O" target="_blank">
                      Strathmore Smooth Bristol Paper
                     </a>
                     .
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                      Colored pencil art is linked at
                      <a href="http://www.bluechairdiaryillustrations.com/2014/01/sunday-sketches_18.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                       Sunday Sketches
                      </a>
                      .
