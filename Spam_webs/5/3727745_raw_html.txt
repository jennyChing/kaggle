            <h3>
             <span class="mw-headline" id="Featured_article">
              Featured article
             </span>
            </h3>
            <dl>
             <dd>
              <div class="floatleft">
               <a href="/Battle_for_Zendikar" title="Battle for Zendikar">
                <img alt="BFZ logo.png" height="86" src="http://hydra-media.cursecdn.com/mtgsalvation.gamepedia.com/thumb/3/3f/BFZ_logo.png/250px-BFZ_logo.png?version=3faa32891ad92f7d62ff16b4622a1b85" srcset="http://hydra-media.cursecdn.com/mtgsalvation.gamepedia.com/thumb/3/3f/BFZ_logo.png/375px-BFZ_logo.png 1.5x, http://hydra-media.cursecdn.com/mtgsalvation.gamepedia.com/3/3f/BFZ_logo.png 2x" width="250"/>
               </a>
              </div>
             </dd>
            </dl>
            <p>
             <i>
              <b>
               Battle for Zendikar
              </b>
             </i>
             will be the 68th
             <i>
              <a class="mw-redirect" href="/Magic" title="Magic">
               Magic
              </a>
             </i>
             expansion, and the first in the
             <a href="/Battle_for_Zendikar_block" title="Battle for Zendikar block">
              Battle for Zendikar block
             </a>
             . It will be released October 2, 2015. The block is the first
             <a class="mw-redirect" href="/Two-Block_Paradigm" title="Two-Block Paradigm">
              to only feature two sets
             </a>
             .
             <i>
              Battle for Zendikar
             </i>
             is a
             <a class="mw-redirect" href="/Large_expansion" title="Large expansion">
              large expansion
             </a>
             and contains 274 cards, which is 5 more than the current default for large sets.
            </p>
            <p>
             <i>
              Battle for Zendikar
             </i>
             returns the storyline to
             <a href="/Zendikar_(plane)" title="Zendikar (plane)">
              Zendikar
             </a>
             which previously has been featured  in the
             <a href="/Zendikar_block" title="Zendikar block">
              Zendikar block
             </a>
             . It is a plane of adventure, many treasures, and potent
             <a href="/Mana" title="Mana">
              mana
             </a>
             supplies. The plane is littered with large, floating polyhedron-shaped stones called
             <a href="/Hedrons" title="Hedrons">
              Hedrons
             </a>
             , that were created by
             <a href="/Nahiri" title="Nahiri">
              Nahiri
             </a>
             , the
             <a href="/Kor" title="Kor">
              Kor
             </a>
             Lithomancer. Together with
             <a href="/Ugin" title="Ugin">
              Ugin
             </a>
             and
             <a href="/Sorin_Markov" title="Sorin Markov">
              Sorin Markov
             </a>
             she used them to trap the horrendous
             <a href="/Eldrazi" title="Eldrazi">
              Eldrazi
             </a>
             on the plane. Thousands of years later, the meeting of
             <a href="/Sarkhan_Vol" title="Sarkhan Vol">
              Sarkhan Vol
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/Chandra_Nalaar" title="Chandra Nalaar">
              Chandra Nalaar
             </a>
             and
             <a href="/Jace_Beleren" title="Jace Beleren">
              Jace Beleren
             </a>
             in the
             <a href="/Eye_of_Ugin" title="Eye of Ugin">
              Eye of Ugin
             </a>
             chamber unvoluntary loosened the bonds that kept the Eldrazi in torpor. The Eldrazi were finally released by the
             <a class="mw-redirect" href="/Elf" title="Elf">
              elf
             </a>
             <a href="/Nissa_Revane" title="Nissa Revane">
              Nissa Revane
             </a>
             , thinking that they would leave Zendikar far behind them. She was wrong. Two years after the initial destruction, at least
             <a href="/Ulamog" title="Ulamog">
              Ulamog
             </a>
             is still around.
            </p>
            The adventurers of Zendikar, even severely outgunned as they are, won't fold to the Eldrazi abominations. They have the
            <a href="/Angels#Zendikar" title="Angels">
             Zendikar angels
            </a>
            , who are set to redeem themselves, at their side. The planet itself already created the
            <a href="/Roil" title="Roil">
             Roil
            </a>
            to battle the
            <a href="/Drones" title="Drones">
             drones
            </a>
            . But it will propably take the assistance of
            <a href="/Planeswalkers" title="Planeswalkers">
             planeswalkers
            </a>
            to overcome the Eldrazi again. Now,
            <a href="/Gideon_Jura" title="Gideon Jura">
             Gideon Jura
            </a>
            takes the lead of a group of planeswalkers in facing the Eldrazi alongside the residents of Zendikar. Meanwhile, the trapped planeswalker-turned-demon
            <a href="/Ob_Nixilis" title="Ob Nixilis">
             Ob Nixilis
            </a>
            is desperate for a way off of the plane.
           </div>
          </td>
         </tr>
