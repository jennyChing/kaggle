             <h2 align="center">
              Root Chakra Affirmations and Synchronicity
             </h2>
             <p>
              I am presently writing the second in a series of books focused on
              <em>
               Following
              </em>
              <a href="http://thewayofchakrahealing.com/chakra-books-2/chakra-books/" target="_blank">
               <em>
                Loves Message
               </em>
              </a>
              . The 1st in the series is entitled
              <a href="http://goo.gl/8Wn7jD" target="_blank">
               <em>
                Healing the Heart Chakra  21 Ways to Follow Loves Message.
               </em>
              </a>
              <em>
              </em>
              which is available on Amazon.
              <em>
              </em>
              This 2nd book is entitled
              <em>
               Healing the Root Chakra  21 Ways to Embody Loves Message.
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              In each of these books on Chakra Healing I share different chakra affirmations. When you open to the way in which the
              <em>
               Message of Love
              </em>
              affirms and expresses through you then you will receive all kinds of Love Letters from the Universe.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/postivieaffirmations.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" style="float: none; margin: 2px auto; display: block;"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="" src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/postivieaffirmations.jpg" style="float: none; margin: 2px auto; display: block;"/>
             </noscript>
             <p align="center">
              Source of Picture 
              <a href="http://2bepositive.blogspot.ie/2011/02/affirmation.html" title="http://2bepositive.blogspot.ie/2011/02/affirmation.html">
               http://2bepositive.blogspot.ie/2011/02/affirmation.html
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              This kind of experience is known as Synchronicity and it is something that you are advised to give your attention to . Synchronicity is akin to receiving a Love Letter from the Universe.
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               Synchronicity, a term coined by Swiss psychologist Carl Jung, is defined as inner and outer events coming together in a way that is meaningful to the observer and cant be explained by cause and effect. In other words: a meaningful coincidence.
              </p>
              <p>
               A synchronicity can serve as guidance, warning, affirmation, creative inspiration, confirmation that were on the right track. It can be evidence of individuation and psychological growth. Or it can simply serve to attune our awareness that beneath the surface of daily life theres an underlying reality and unity that may not be immediately obvious. All synchronicities, even the ones that caution us, have a mysterious quality.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.guidenseek.com/the-7-signs-of-synchronicity.html" target="_blank" title="http://www.guidenseek.com/the-7-signs-of-synchronicity.html">
                http://www.guidenseek.com/the-7-signs-of-synchronicity.html
               </a>
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              I received two Universal Love Letters (synchronies) over the period of the last 24 hours. One was by way of an unexpected email and the other was from an inspirational story.
             </p>
             <p>
              These two Universal Love Letters have helped me focus on where I intend to ground my energies particularly in relation to the the work that I have chosen to do. The work one does is related to the dynamic of the
              <a class="broken_link" href="http://localhost/wch/wordpress/?p=52" target="_blank" title="Root Chakra Healing  Real Embodiment">
               Root Chakra
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               Let the Beauty You Love be the Work You do  Rumi
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <h3>
              Universal Love Letter 1
             </h3>
             <p>
              Everyday I quickly scan my email inbox in order to see if there are any emails I need to reply to or have relevance to the work I love to do and share.
             </p>
             <p>
              This is one of the first tasks of my day following a period of
              <a href="http://thewayofchakrahealing.com/beginnersmeditation" onmouseout="self.status=''" onmouseover="self.status='meditation';return true;" rel="nofollow" style="" target="_blank">
               meditation
              </a>
              and writing.
             </p>
             <p>
              I received an email from someone named Alan Davidson. I didnt recognise the name so I was going to delete it but included in the
              <em>
               from
              </em>
              section of the email were the words
              <strong>
               Through Your Body.
              </strong>
              The title of the email was
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Your Outrageous Love Letter.
              </span>
              This intrigued me enough to spend a moment reading what Alan Davidson wrote:-
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               I thought about you while I was in Sedona last week. I was at an amazing event for spiritual entrepreneurs who are committed to raising humanitys consciousness.
              </p>
              <p>
               At breakfast one morning our table leader said, Lets cut the boring introductionsHow about we play a game?
              </p>
              <p>
               Imagine YOU are an outrageous love letter from the infinite. And that your LIFE is an outrageous love letter FROM you back to the infinite
              </p>
              <p>
               Im sharing my outrageous love letter below. And I got to wondering, What is your outrageous life, your outrageous love letter back to the infinite?
              </p>
              <p>
               That little game and its bigger question has haunted me since.
              </p>
              <p>
               Are you willing to play?
              </p>
              <p>
               If so
              </p>
              <ol>
               <li>
                Entertain the question, what is your outrageous love letter back to the infinite?
               </li>
              </ol>
              <ol>
               <li>
                Share itgift it to someone else and ask them this same simple, yet bold question.
               </li>
              </ol>
              <p>
               Share it with mesimple reply to this email. Or share it on my I Love Energy Medicine Facebook page:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="broken_link" href="http://throughyourbody.ontraport.com/c/s/Uyn/v83/6/v/Tb/6yuYx4/oKhkdVrVW">
                https://www.facebook.com/ILoveEnergyMedicine
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               As promised, here is my own outrageous love letter back to the kosmos:
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am embodied wisdom. I am the delightful dance of presence and love; of awareness and compassion.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I restore the bodys dignity to its rightful place in divinity.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am the court jester reminding us that there is no good reason to ever close our heart.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am the trickster kindling the light of every spirit, illuminating the soul sitting on its throne of consciousness.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               Thats it. Short, sweetand huge in its scope. Now to embody my own love letter
              </p>
              <p>
               So please ponder your love letter to the divine. Jot a few notes and let the love flow
              </p>
              <p>
               Alan Davidson
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <h3>
              The Power of Focus
             </h3>
             <p>
              It just so happened that when I was writing this morning I was trying to focus on how to best embody living my one wild and precious life.
             </p>
             <p>
              Here is my attempt at my outrageous love letter back to the Kosmos:-
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <img align="left" alt="" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/healing-the-heart-chakra-compressed.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="180" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" style="float: left; margin: 5px 10px; display: inline;" width="100"/>
                <noscript>
                 <img align="left" alt="" height="180" src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/healing-the-heart-chakra-compressed.jpg" style="float: left; margin: 5px 10px; display: inline;" width="100"/>
                </noscript>
                I am a REAL LIFE Storyteller inviting you to Open your Eyes and See.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am a Heart Coaxer inviting you to become a Star in the Making
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am a Singer of Heart Songs inviting you to connect to Your Universal Song.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am a Transformational Writer sharing the Message of Love.
               </strong>
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              As Alan Davidson says. Its short and sweet but it is huge in its scope. The challenge is now to embody this Letter of Love that has within it Loves Message.
             </p>
             <p>
              I have been wanting to find a name for the work that I do (1st Chakra dynamic) for a long time. I have played around with various ideas but none of them have really grabbed me to the point where I feel Yeah, this is it. Even this morning this wasnt happening until I received the email entitled
              <strong>
               Your Outrageous Love Letter
              </strong>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              The reason why is in a number of the lines from this email from Alan Davidson. These are the lines:-
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am the court jester reminding us that there is no good reason to ever close our heart.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am the trickster kindling the light of every spirit, illuminating the soul sitting on its throne of consciousness.
               </strong>
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              I was playing around with the idea of calling myself a Heart Coaxer but it didnt quite feel right. The reason why it didnt feel right was because that term seemed to be too close to the idea of being a Trickster. The trickster is a universal archetype or pattern that one is invited to embody.
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               In later folklore, the trickster/clown is incarnated as a clever, mischievous man or creature, who tries to survive the dangers and challenges of the world using trickery and deceit as a defence. He also is known for entertaining people as a clown does. For example many typical
               <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairy_tale">
                fairy tales
               </a>
               have the King who wants to find the best groom for his daughter by ordering several trials. No brave and valiant prince or knight manages to win them, until a poor and simple peasant comes. With the help of his wits and cleverness, instead of fighting, he evades or fools monsters and villains and dangers with unorthodox manners. Therefore the most unlikely candidate passes the trials and receives the reward. More modern and obvious examples of that type are Bugs Bunny, Pippi Longstocking.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trickster" title="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trickster">
                http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trickster
               </a>
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              My question to myself is this, How often do you get any kind of correspondence which refers to something as esoteric as The Trickster? The answer is never. Yet here I am cogitating the use of the word and along comes a specific instruction from a source that I have not used before. Would you not think that this is kind of synchronistic? I would and I do.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/trickster1.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" style="float: none; margin: 2px auto; display: block;"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="" src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/trickster1.jpg" style="float: none; margin: 2px auto; display: block;"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              I later reflected on this because when synchronicities arrive in your life you need to pay attention to them. I came to the following conclusion about this term.
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               It is as far as I am aware unique.
              </li>
              <li>

              </li>

              </li>

              </li>

              </li>

             <p>
              So I invite , coax people, to come to the edge and take a flying leap into the heart.
             </p>

              </p>

              </p>

               and we pushed,
              </p>

               And they flew.

               ?
               <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/6434130.Guilliame_Apollinaire_French_Poet">
                Guilliame Apollinaire, French Poet
               </a>
              </p>

             <h2>
              Universal Love Letter 2
             </h2>
             <p>
              Let me share with you the 2nd Love Letter from the Universe.
             </p>
             <p>
              Yesterday I was listening to a Youtube presentation by Dr. Joe Dispensa
              <em>
               - Rewire &amp; Evolve Your Brain  How to use Law Of Attraction.
              </em>
              This wonderful presentation shares with you information on how to create your day and connect to what Dr. Joe Dispensa refers to as The Field (The Field of Universal Intelligence which creates all things out of No-thing-ness).
             </p>
             <p>
              This presentation begins with the following story:-
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               I was leaving my office a couple of months ago and a staff member handed me this sheet of paper and she said, Listen, you have to read this quick.
              </p>
              <p>
               Im on my way to catch a plane so I grab this piece of paper and its this woman who decides to create her day and she says, Well, I never considered myself a special person so Im going to create my day and ask for a sign that without a doubt lets me know that Im special and I want it to come in a way that surprises me and leaves me in no doubt in my mind that I made contact with the Field.
              </p>
              <p>
               So shes driving down the road up this five mile incline at sunset and as shes driving up this road a bald eagle flies in her car, lands on her dashboard, spreads its wings out, makes eye contact with this woman. She pulls off the side of the road and the eagle flys out the other window.
              </p>
              <p>
               I said to my office assistant, You email that woman back and tell her that if she has any doubt that shes special we need to talk.
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              During the telling of the story there is a gasp of astonishment from the audience at the point where the bald eagle flies into the car and laughter when he shares the last line of the story.
             </p>
             <p>
              I love this story and it reminds me that I am special. This is not said in any egoistic sense. This kind of affirmation comes from that dimension beyond time and space so the individual is not controlling what arrives.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/bald-eagle-gallery-01-625x450.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" style="float: none; margin: 2px auto; display: block;"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="" src="http://www.thewayofchakrahealing.com/images/bald-eagle-gallery-01-625x450.jpg" style="float: none; margin: 2px auto; display: block;"/>
             </noscript>
             <h3>
              The Real Affirmation
             </h3>
             <p>
              The above could be considered synchronistic but the affirmation aspect came within a dream. In
              <em>
               <a href="http://goo.gl/QfGFW9" target="_blank">
                Healing the Heart Chakra  21 Ways to Follow Loves Message
               </a>
               ,
              </em>
              I share a dream that I have had recur over the last number of years. In this recurring dream I am in a car. When I want to drive forward the car moves backward.
             </p>
             <p>
              When I want to move backward the car either does not move or moves forward and cannot be stopped. Often the car careers out of control. Notice here the words careers out of control. This is a pun for the fact that I am not really in control of my career. This is a root chakra issue.
             </p>
             <p>
              This recurring dream came again last night.
             </p>
             <p>
              The difference was that this time when I awoke I tried to stay within the dream state. I was to some degree successful. I lay half-asleep feeling the feeling that was central to the dream. That feeling was the intense experience feeling of being Loved.
             </p>
             <p>
              In this recurring dream I was driving the car again. When I reversed I could not stop. When I went forward there were no brakes. However, in the car there was woman. In this recurring dream there has never been a woman appearing before. She put her hand on my hands, which were on the steering wheel. What I knew in that moment was that this woman loved me intensely. I stayed with that feeling and bathed in it until I had been half asleep for a long time.
             </p>
             <p>
              This is real affirmation. While I am still driving a car without brakes I am supported in the endeavour by the Love of the Divine Feminine. Dreams are often preludes to the coming attractions in ones life but the difference is that you are not practicing affirmation but recognizing it arising from beyond the limitation of the ego and the intellect.
             </p>
             <h3>
              How to Live the Invitation
             </h3>
             <p>
              You can practice affirmations and Chakra Affirmations but without being able to invoke and intense feeling that you can associate with that affirmation it is unlikely that you will manifest what it is you are affirming. Rather than choose an affirmation that you THINK you want to work with , pay more attention to the way in which synchronicity and dream images are given you.
             </p>
             <p>
              Carl Jung said that unacknowledged dreams are like letters from God that are left unopened. If you do not like the word God you can use your own word. In this way, you are aligning your inner world with the outer world and your personal sense of self with the Universal Self.
             </p>
