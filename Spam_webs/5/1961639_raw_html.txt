       <h2>
        Product Review: Kiss Instawave Automatic Curler
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         About this:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The revolutionary KISS InstaWave is a fully automatic curler that instantly creates beautiful, long-lasting curls with ease.
        <br/>
        No Need to Be a Pro, simply place a section of hair on the InstaWave and leave the rest of the curling to us.  With the touch of a button, the Curl Dial Instantly and Automatically catches and curls hair, transforming straight locks into tangle-free glamorous curls in seconds.
        <br/>
        Features
        <br/>
         Patent Pending Curl Dial: Creates Tangle-Free curls in both Left or Right directions
        <br/>
         Diamond-Ceramic Ionic Technology: Healthy, shiny styling results with less frizz and damage
        <br/>
         420F Max Temperature: Professional grade temperature locks in curls
        <br/>
         Auto Shutoff: Automatically shuts off after 90 minutes
        <br/>
         Power Control Switch: High/Low Temperature Settings
        <br/>
         Good for use on all hair lengths and types
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <h3>
         Our thoughts
        </h3>
        <p>
         <strong>
          This is a new generation of hair care.
          <br/>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         When you first take a look at this curling iron, it looks a big complicated. This looks like a run-of-the-mill curling iron with additional attachments that look like smaller irons that surround the main iron. For many of us, this brings images to mind of tangled hair problems.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Here it the scoop.
          <br/>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         You need to read the directions, or better yet, watch the video before you attempt to use this. Its not difficult to use once you take a minute to find out how to do it right.
        </p>
        <p>
         This curling iron works best on hair that is longer than shoulder length. Its like using large curlers- your hair may not be long enough to use this correctly.
        </p>
        <p>
         This curling iron comes with an on off switch and a high and low temperature setting. The  Diamond-Ceramic Ionic Technology is meant to give us less frizz and damage than the curling irons that we are use to using. This iron is also useful to both left and right handed users.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          We like that this curling iron is sturdy.
         </strong>
         It does a good job at those curls and waves that are especially popular right not. The curls and waves seemed to stay put a bit longer than the average curling iron, which is always nice.
        </p>
