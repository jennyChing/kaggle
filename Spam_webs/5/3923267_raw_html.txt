          <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
           If Best-selling Albums Had Been Books Instead
          </h1>
          <div class="post-header-meta">
           <span class="the-time updated">
            18 March 2013
           </span>
           <span class="the-comment-link">
            
            <a href="http://www.aerogrammestudio.com/2013/03/18/if-best-selling-albums-had-been-books-instead/#comments">
             2 Comments

        <div class="entry-content clearfix" id="content-1128">
         <p>
          What if these best-selling albums had been books instead? The Record Books are the creation of UK-based graphic designer and art director Christophe Gowans. Below are ten of our favourites.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <b>
            
           </b>
           Christophe Gowans. Reproduced with permission.
           <br/>
          </em>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Are You Experienced
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Charismatic Harvard whizkid Hendrixs self-help bible. A spin-off from his phenomenally successful TV reality show, The Experience.
          <br/>
          <a href="http://aerogrammestudio.com/2013/03/18/if-best-selling-albums-had-been-books-instead/" style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 19px;">
           <span style="font-size: small; line-height: 19px;">
            <span style="line-height: 19px;">
             <img alt="Record Books - Are You Experienced" class="wp-image-1142 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-are-you-experienced.jpg?resize=398%2C566"/>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/22/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-are-you-experienced/">
            <span id="more-1128">
            </span>
            Abbey Road
            <br/>
           </a>
          </strong>
          Classic paperback. The story of two catholic sisters growing up in a swiftly changing post-war Britain. Guess what? It doesnt end well.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/07/25/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-abbey-road/">
           <img alt="Record Books - Abbey Road" class="wp-image-1144 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-abbey-road1.jpg?resize=378%2C478"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Born to Run
          </strong>
          <br/>
          A rags to glory autobiography by Bruce Reginald Grayson Springsteen. The story of his rise from squalor to victory in the 1932 Los Angeles Olympics is well, its a pretty dull book.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/04/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-born-to-run-2/">
           <img alt="Record Books - Born to Run" class=" wp-image-1153 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-born-to-run1.jpg?resize=359%2C454"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           OK Computer
           <br/>
          </strong>
          Instruction manual for an obscure brand of personal computer. Slightly yellowed but completely unused, as the machine itself never worked. Indeed, upon opening it up to undertake a repair, it was found to have no working parts of any kind. In fact it contained a brick, taped to the inside of the housing.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/30/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-ok-computer/">
           <img alt="Record Books - OK Computer" class="wp-image-1150 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-ok-computer.jpg?resize=402%2C480"/>
           <br/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           At Folsom Prison
           <br/>
          </strong>
          A collection of poetry written while in confinement (7 years for grand theft). Each poem is inspired by a book in the prison library. This copy was stolen from the library, by the author. Ooops.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/30/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-ok-computer/">
            <br/>
           </a>
           <span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 19px;">
            <br/>
            <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/23/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-at-folsom-prison/">
             <img alt="Record Books - At Folsom Prison" class="size-full wp-image-1154 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-at-folsom-prison.jpg?resize=480%2C600"/>
            </a>
            <br/>
           </span>
           <strong>
            My Dark TwistedFantasy
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </strong>
          Very well preserved and neatly written diary of a 9-year-old schoolboy from Stoke Poges. His title is misleading to an adult reader; his most extreme fantasy extends to driving a Royal Mail overnight delivery train without wearing his glasses.
          <strong>
           <br/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/18/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-my-beautiful-dark-twisted-fantasy/">
           <img alt="Record Books - My Dark Twisted Fantasy" class="wp-image-1156 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-my-dark-twisted-fantasy.jpg?resize=410%2C480"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/30/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-ok-computer/">
           <br/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Let England Shake
          </strong>
          <br/>
          1959 compendium of dance instructions for all the latest ways to cut a rug.It came, apparently, with a set of footpad tiles to be used by the reader  in an adequately spacious room or hall  to perfect the steps. These are now missing.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/26/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-let-england-shake/">
           <img alt="Record Books - Let England Shake" class="wp-image-1159 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-let-england-shake1.jpg?resize=438%2C577"/>
           <br/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           Come On Over
           <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/26/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-let-england-shake/">
            <br/>
           </a>
          </strong>
          A compendium, full of games, stories, puzzles and activities for children of both sexes to enjoy. At a rough estimate, 70% of these games, stories, puzzles and activities involved a hoop and a stick.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 19px;">
           <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2013/02/18/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-come-on-over/">
            <img alt="Record Books - Come On Over" class=" wp-image-1161 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-come-on-over1.jpg?resize=465%2C572"/>
            <br/>
           </a>
          </span>
          <strong>
           Master of Puppets
           <br/>
          </strong>
          Issue 3 of Elektra Comics short-lived title. Even for a fantasy genre, the storylines were viewed as too far-fetched by the audience.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2013/02/20/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-master-of-puppets/">
           <img alt="Record Books - Master of Puppets" class=" wp-image-1204 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-master-of-puppets2.jpg?resize=478%2C634"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Horses
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Thorough and clear childrens reference book concerning all things equine. Sadly, many of the illustrations within have been disfigured with juvenile amendments and additions, in biro.
          <br/>
          <a href="http://ceegworld.com/2012/05/01/the-record-books-%E2%80%A2-horses/">
           <img alt="Record Books - Horses" class=" wp-image-1165 alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/aerogrammestudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/record-books-horses.jpg?resize=568%2C693"/>
           <br/>
          </a>
          You can view the full 48 album collection on
          <strong>
           <a href="http://ceegworld.com/the-record-books/" target="_blank">
            Christophes website
           </a>
          </strong>
          . Some of the images are available as prints and postcards at
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.rockpot.co.uk/" target="_blank">
            The Rockpot
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
