                    <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                     Outdoor furniture can transform your
                     <u>
                      <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2013/06/backyard-landscape-design-ideas.html" target="_blank">
                       backyard
                      </a>
                     </u>
                     , patio, deck, or front yard into a relaxing retreat and enjoyable gathering place for you and your family. Outdoor furniture adds comfort and function to your outdoor spaces.
                    </span>
                    <br/>
                    <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                     <br/>
                    </span>
                    <br/>
                    <a name="more">
                    </a>
                    <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                     Outdoor furniture is made from a variety of materials, each with its own look and feel. Materials can include wood, aluminum, wicker, wrought iron, plastic, and more. Each material type will be different when considering weather resistance, durability, and maintenance requirements. The weather where you live plays an important part in the kind of outdoor furniture you select for your outdoor space.
                    </span>
                    <br/>
                    <br/>
                    <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                     Here are some tips you need to consider when shopping for the right outdoor furniture that will provide you with many years of comfort and enjoyment:
                    </span>
                    <br/>
                    <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                     <br/>
                    </span>
                    <br/>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Consider the way in which you want to use your outdoor space.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Consider your climate, lifestyle, budget, and the
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2013/10/interior-design-styles.html" target="_blank">
                         interiordesign style
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       of your home when selecting your outdoor furniture.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Make sure you measure your available space, and have those measurements when you go shopping for the furniture; to ensure everything will fit comfortably.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-J_5CptsdsIQ/U-e8LDkyQzI/AAAAAAAABxQ/pt9zOMSu7s4/s1600/leovanpatio1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img alt="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture" border="0" height="228" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-J_5CptsdsIQ/U-e8LDkyQzI/AAAAAAAABxQ/pt9zOMSu7s4/s1600/leovanpatio1.jpg" title="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture - www.leovandesign.com" width="320"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Pick outdoor furniture pieces that have high-quality materials, are durable, and are easy-to-maintain.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Research and read several consumer reviews before you make a big purchase.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    </div>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Find out what the maintenance requirements are before purchasing an outdoor furniture set:
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Aluminum is one of the most long-lasting materials especially if you live in a cold environment.
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Wooden furniture is very traditional and popular but will require more maintenance and repair.
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Metal rusts easily if it is kept out in rain or winter but if you can maintain it properly, you will have an exclusive look.
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Plastic is durable and needs less maintenance but it gets easily discolored, faded, and scratched. Plastic furniture tends to look of low quality, and is not easy to match with any home dcor or
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/search/label/Interior%20Design%20Styles" target="_blank">
                         design style
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       .
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Wicker furniture should be kept in the shade because it can be damaged by direct sunlight.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                    </ul>
                    <div>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-IBfWP0e0mh4/U-e7nDd_RtI/AAAAAAAABxI/WvlVjVekAx8/s1600/leovandesign3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img alt="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture" border="0" height="316" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-IBfWP0e0mh4/U-e7nDd_RtI/AAAAAAAABxI/WvlVjVekAx8/s1600/leovandesign3.jpg" title="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture - www.leovandesign.com" width="320"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Make sure the design style of your home is reflected in your choice of outdoor furniture. For example g
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       lass and metal furniture goes well with
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2014/10/contemporary-interior-design-style7.html" target="_blank">
                         contemporary style
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       homes. Wood and wicker furniture goes well with
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2014/02/traditional-interior-design-style.html" target="_blank">
                         <span id="goog_729681860">
                         </span>
                         traditional style
                         <span id="goog_729681861">
                         </span>
                        </a>
                       </u>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       homes.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Make sure the color scheme of your outdoor furniture is harmonious with the colors in your
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2013/05/landscape-design-elements.html" target="_blank">
                         landscape design
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       .
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Make sure you leave sufficient space for people to freely move around your outdoor furniture.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Use large outdoor furniture on an expansive
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2014/06/deck-design-planning-tips.html" target="_blank">
                         deck
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       , use small outdoor furniture in a small outdoor space, and mix one large furniture piece with smaller furniture pieces in a medium-sized space; this will make your outdoor space both comfortable and stylish.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Try any new outdoor furniture in the store before you purchase it to make sure its comfortable, durable, secure, and reliable.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Try to buy a mix of pillows with different
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2013/07/mixing-patterns-and-prints-in-interior.html" target="_blank">
                         patterns and colors
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       , this will add texture and color to your outdoor space.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Select bold colors for cushions and accent pieces.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-c2Jzr9VhGGo/U-e7Myd49zI/AAAAAAAABw4/RzrSsqj2-_c/s1600/Leovandesign2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img alt="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture" border="0" height="199" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-c2Jzr9VhGGo/U-e7Myd49zI/AAAAAAAABw4/RzrSsqj2-_c/s1600/Leovandesign2.jpg" title="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture - www.leovandesign.com" width="320"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Use fabric cushions with your outdoor furnishings to boost the comfort and the appearance of your outdoor furniture.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Select outdoor furniture with cushions that can be unzipped and taken apart for repair or replacement.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Look for cushions that are waterproof, washable, and mildew and fade-resistant.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Look for cushions with polyester or acrylic filler to resist mold and mildew.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Make sure table legs and chairs have plastic or rubber feet that won't scratch your deck or
                       <u>
                        <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2013/08/patio-design-and-decor-ideas.html" target="_blank">
                         patio
                        </a>
                       </u>
                       .
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Look for outdoor furniture that is simply taken apart for compact storage; if your storage room is limited.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Consider storage such as a basement, shed, or garage for your outdoor furniture during rain, snow, and cold weather; to protect your outdoor furniture and to make it last longer.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <ul>
                     <li>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       Invest in outdoor furniture covers if you dont have storage space to protect your furnishings and to keep your outdoor furniture dry until it is ready for use in sunnier weather.
                      </span>
                     </li>
                    </ul>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-AVG0FG_8cd8/U-e7VSrmx8I/AAAAAAAABxA/13xrAdn-noU/s1600/leovandesign1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img alt="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture" border="0" height="213" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-AVG0FG_8cd8/U-e7VSrmx8I/AAAAAAAABxA/13xrAdn-noU/s1600/leovandesign1.jpg" title="Tips for Choosing Outdoor Furniture - www.leovandesign.com" width="320"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <br/>
                    <br/>
                    <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                     Whether your goal is to design an outdoor space for entertaining, dining, and gathering or to create an outdoor space for a quiet retreat, you should select the right outdoor furniture for your patio,
                     <u>
                      <a href="http://www.leovandesign.com/2014/07/front-yard-curb-appeal.html" target="_blank">
                       front yard
                      </a>
                     </u>
                     , backyard or deck to ensure that you are able to spend enjoyable occasions with your family and guests, in a welcoming and comfortable space.
                    </span>
                    <br/>
                    <br/>
