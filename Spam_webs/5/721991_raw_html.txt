       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Featured Shop: Dan-ah Kim
       </h1>
       <img alt="Etsy.com handmade and vintage goods" class="main" src="https://blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-large.jpg"/>
       <div class="share-byline">
        <p class="photo-by">
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="byline">
        <img alt="dkim" class="avatar photo avatar-default" height="75" src="//img1.etsystatic.com/iusa_75x75.5197897.jpg" width="75"/>
        <p class="store-by">
         Story by
         <a class="profile" href="https://blog.etsy.com/en/authors/dkim/">
          Dan-ah Kim
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="published">
         Published on
         <span class="posted">
          Sept 26, 2014
         </span>
         in
         <a class="category" href="https://blog.etsy.com/en/category/shop/">
          Shop
         </a>
        </p>
        <!-- get photo credits -->
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         My name is Dan-ah Kim, and Im an artist and designer based in Brooklyn, New York. I make paintings for gallery shows, work as a graphics artist in film and television, and make prints and books for
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim">
          my shop
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215223" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215223">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-001" class="wp-image-215223 size-full" height="400" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-001.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215228" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215228">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-006" class="wp-image-215228 size-full" height="400" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-006.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I love storytelling. Anything can be a source of inspiration  from the cosmos to prosaic moments with a cat. I like for my work to be narrative, to have a history and future determined by the viewer. Some of my pieces are rooted in memory, while others are just adventures Id like to have. Most are made with mixed media: pencil, pen, gouache or acrylic, layers of colored paper, and hand-sewn elements. Its fun to cut things up or poke holes into a piece that was just inked with painstaking detail.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215224" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215224">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-002" class="wp-image-215224 size-full" height="400" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-002.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215225" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215225">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-003" class="wp-image-215225 size-full" height="800" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-003.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I majored in art direction at school, but I fell in love when I took some illustration classes. After graduation I opened my shop as a way to motivate myself to keep creating and sharing my work. Having my prints featured on a few blogs helped tremendously; I began taking part in group shows and some new illustration opportunities came.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215227" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215227">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-005" class="wp-image-215227 size-full" height="400" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-005.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215226" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215226">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-004" class="wp-image-215226 size-full" height="400" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-004.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         My shop has gone through a lot of trial and error  from finding the best paper for prints to exploring safe and efficient shipping methods. Etsy helped me develop a career out of art making. I still work in film and television because I love it, but having my shop encourages me to keep making personal work, which keeps me sane. The thought of having my work in homes across the world is lovely, but getting messages from people who really connect with an image is unbeatable. Painting can be a bit isolating  until someone reaches out about how a piece made them feel.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215229" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215229">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-007" class="wp-image-215229 size-full" height="400" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-007.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_215230" style="width: 580px">
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/dkim" rel="attachment wp-att-215230">
          <img alt="etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-008" class="wp-image-215230 size-full" height="420" src="//blog.etsy.com/en/files/2014/09/etsy-featured-shop-dan-ah-kim-008.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         This year I self-published a childrens picture book, which is now available in my shop. My future goals are to expand my shop and have my work printed on fabrics and other useful objects. Id also love tomakeillustrations for all of the strange stories in my head and in the world.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Follow Dan-ah on
          <a href="http://instagram.com/danahsaurus" target="_blank">
           Instagram
          </a>
          and
