     <h1 class="title">
      <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/travel/ferragamo-amenity-kits-singapore-airlines/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Singapore Airlines First Class Ferragamo Amenity Kits">
       Singapore Airlines First Class Ferragamo Amenity Kits
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="meta-author">
      Posted by
      <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by Adrian">
       Adrian
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="clearfix post-76038 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-airline category-amenity-kits category-first-business-class-travel category-travel" id="post-76038">
      <div class="entry">
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_76041" style="width: 699px">
        <img alt="Singapore Airlines First Class Amenity Kit for women by Salvatore Ferragmo" class="size-full wp-image-76041" height="400" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Singapore-Airlines-First-Class-Amenity-Kits2.IMG_2481.jpg" width="689"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Singapore Airlines First Class Amenity Kit for women by Salvatore Ferragmo
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        When was the last time you flew on Singapore Airlines most talked about first class cabins or the First Class suites on its Airbus A380s? Well, we have not been so lucky yet. Weve never flown First Class but weve seen photos and watched videos of them on Youtube. As part of our series of feature stories about
        <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/category/travel/airline/amenity-kits/" target="_blank">
         airline amenity kits
        </a>
        , we venture into ultra luxurious and exclusive realm of Singapore Airlines.
       </p>
       <p>
        Previously, amenity kits in Singapore Airlines First Class contained products from brands such as Sothys or Kiehls. Unlike most other airlines, Singapore Airlines does not offer amenity kits to its business class passengers. In a recent revamp of a new set of First Class amenity kits, the airline has picked Salvatore Ferragamo Parfums. Singapore Airlines offers a generous
        <strong>
         30ml bottled eau de toilette
        </strong>
        in its amenity kit and has taken into consideration differentiation of products for male and female passengers.
        <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/2013/07/01/flying-emirates-first-business-class-amenity-kits/" target="_blank" title="Emirates First &amp; Business Class Amenity Kits">
         Emirates amenity kit
        </a>
        is the other one we reviewed that offers a generous portion of amenities such as the 40ml Body Lotion or After Shave Emulsion and 5ml eau de cologne.
       </p>
       <p>
        The contents in the amenity kits are colour coordinated with the typical blue for men and pink for ladies.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Ferragamo Amenity Kits for Men
       </h2>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_76043" style="width: 698px">
        <img alt="Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Men" class="size-full wp-image-76043 " height="400" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Singapore-Airlines-First-Class-Amenity-Kits4.IMG_2497.jpg" width="688"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Men
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Encased within the soft PVC wash bag are four items from the ACQUA Essenziale range by Salvatore Ferragamo. Everything else that you need to freshen up is available in the washrooms on board.
       </p>
       <p>
        ACQUA Essenziale offers a precious blend of breezy notes combined with the most precious Mediterranean aromatic and woody notes.
       </p>
       <p>
        The washbag is big enough for you to fit your toothrush and toothpaste, a deodarant, a shaver, facewash, perfume and more for your next trip. It comes with for side pocket compartments within and an external compartment. One side of the wash bag bears the logo of Singapore Airlines while pasted on the other side is a Salvatore Ferragamo Parfums metal plate.
       </p>
       <p>
        Contents
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         30ml Eau De Toilette
        </li>
        <li>
         10m Lip Balm
        </li>
        <li>
         30ml Hand Cream
        </li>
        <li>
         1 x Cleansing Towel
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_76044" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Men" class="wp-image-76044 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Singapore-Airlines-First-Class-Amenity-Kits5.IMG_2501.jpg" title="Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Men" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Inside the Singapore Airlines Salvatore Ferragamo First Class Amenity Kit
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Sewn Inside the washbag is a leather label with the words Salvatore Ferragamo Parfums for Singapore Airlines.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Ferragamo Amenity Kits for Women
       </h2>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_76040" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Women" class="size-full wp-image-76040 " height="400" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Singapore-Airlines-First-Class-Amenity-Kits1.IMG_2473.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Women
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The womens amenity kit has a thicker canvas wash bag, and also four side compartments inside with ample room for other toiletries. The kit contains four items from the Signorina Eau de Parum range. Signorina is a fragrance creation that reinterprets the iconic elegance of jasmine with the unexpected and delicate sweetness of pannacotta. The sweet scent is very soothing to the nose. The bottle is just too pretty.
       </p>
       <p>
        Contents
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         30ml Eau de Toilette
        </li>
        <li>
         10ml Lip Balm
        </li>
        <li>
         30ml Hand Cream
        </li>
        <li>
         1x Cleansing Towel
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_76042" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Women" class="wp-image-76042 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Singapore-Airlines-First-Class-Amenity-Kits3.IMG_2488.jpg" title="Singapore Airlines First Class Salvatore Ferragamo Amenity Kit for Women" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Inside Singapore Airlines Salvatore Ferragamo First Class ladies amenity kit.
        </p>
       </div>
