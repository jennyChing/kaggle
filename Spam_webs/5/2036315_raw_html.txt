                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5145700428859122696" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     So
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I can't believe I haven't mentioned this yet.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     This Fall I got to do something really excellent- collaborate with Von Zos, the talented publisher of artist multiples, on a very special project:
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-tVFNcT9dQ1c/UleIKReKOlI/AAAAAAAAA4c/wp8ibMp68CY/s1600/vonzos_aw_wb+(4).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <br/>
                      </a>
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-s_lRnBrznxs/UleJTibl6II/AAAAAAAAA4o/jkKiS-iEkgA/s1600/vonzos_aw_wb+(1).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-s_lRnBrznxs/UleJTibl6II/AAAAAAAAA4o/jkKiS-iEkgA/s640/vonzos_aw_wb+(1).jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <span style="line-height: 18px;">
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       The Way Back
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <span style="line-height: 18px;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      The piece consists of a complex, three-dimensional, free-standing statuary constructed from individual pieces of printed 24 pt cardstock. The piece is precision laser-cut and stands approximately five inches tall by seven inches wide when fully assembled.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      The pieces come housed in a custom-made foam enclosure within a box which itself is an extension of the free-standing piece as the boxs exterior artwork echoes the art and themes of the statuary inside.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      Additionally, each piece comes with a specially produced wishbone charm.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Z4NOTSIU-Pw/UleOk6QcB7I/AAAAAAAAA44/wRbXnFE6-RY/s1600/wishbone.tiff" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="315" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Z4NOTSIU-Pw/UleOk6QcB7I/AAAAAAAAA44/wRbXnFE6-RY/s320/wishbone.tiff" width="320"/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                      The Way Back is produced in a limited edition of 20 and is signed and numbered by the artist.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
