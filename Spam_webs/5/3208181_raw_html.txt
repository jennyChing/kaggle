         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          M&amp;Ms Basketball Cake
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-05-07T15:56:55+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           May 7, 2013
          </time>
          by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/author/Kara/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Kara
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/basketball-cake/#comments">
            30 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/MMs-basketball-cake.jpg">
           <img alt="Basketball cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3025" height="561" src="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/MMs-basketball-cake.jpg" title="M&amp;Ms basketball cake from TheJoysofBoys.com" width="600"/>
          </a>
          Yesterday we celebrated the 7th birthday of one of the coolest kids I know. When he grows up he wants to be a professional basketball player so naturally he would want a basketball cake for his birthday.
          <span id="more-3013">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I love using M&amp;Ms to decorate cakes and the last M&amp;M cake we made was the
          <a href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/2012/07/10/a-happy-birthday-and-an-mms-angry-birds-cake/">
           Angry Birds cake
          </a>
          we made for my husbands birthday last year. I have said, many, many times before that I am not a skilled cake decorator. I will never be featured on Cupcake wars or any food wars for that matter, but I feel the only critic I need to impress is the birthday boy himself and being only 7, this cake didnt disappoint. (Just pretend it turned out as a perfect circle okay? Thanks!)
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: left;">
          M&amp;M Basketball Cake
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Basketball-cake-with-MMs.jpg">
           <img alt="M&amp;Ms basketball cake" class="aligncenter wp-image-3015" height="412" src="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Basketball-cake-with-MMs.jpg" title="Basketball Cake with M&amp;Ms from TheJoysofBoys.com " width="420"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Supplies needed:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000VMFN4/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0000VMFN4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thjoofbo-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000VMFN4/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0000VMFN4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thjoofbo-20', 'Wilton Soccer Ball Pan']);" rel="">
            Wilton Soccer Ball Pan
           </a>
          </span>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=thjoofbo-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B0000VMFN4" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=M%26Ms%20orange&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;tag=thjoofbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Dgrocery" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=M%26Ms%20orange&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;tag=thjoofbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Dgrocery', 'Orange M&amp;Ms']);" rel="" target="_blank">
            Orange M&amp;Ms
           </a>
          </span>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="https://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=thjoofbo-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          bought in bulk
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=M%26Ms%20Black&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=n%3A16310101%2Ck%3AM%26Ms%20Black&amp;tag=thjoofbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Dgrocery" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=M%26Ms%20Black&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=n%3A16310101%2Ck%3AM%26Ms%20Black&amp;tag=thjoofbo-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Dgrocery', 'Black M&amp;Ms']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Black M&amp;Ms
           </a>
          </span>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="https://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=thjoofbo-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          bought in bulk
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Step 1- Bake two cakes using the soccer ball pan. This took awhile since I had to wait for one to cool enough to get out of the pan before putting the other in the oven. One tip I found is that it takes a lot longer than it says on the pan to cook completely. Just watch your cakes until you can insert a toothpick that comes out clean.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Step 2- Let cakes cool completely and then cut off bottom so they are flat.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Step 3- Frost one half of the basketball with orange frosting and add the M&amp;Ms in the pattern of a basketball before the frosting dries so they will stick.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Step 4- Let the decorated side sit for a bit until the frosting is set up and dried.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Step 5- Invert decorated cake onto a cake plate. This was the hardest part!! I had to be really careful not to knock off the M&amp;Ms.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Step 6- Add a thin layer of frosting and add second cake on top. Decorate as you did the first.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          And Voila! One cool cake and one happy birthday boy.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Basketball-cake.jpg">
           <img alt="M&amp;Ms basketball cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3014" height="513" src="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Basketball-cake.jpg" title="Basketball Cake with M&amp;Ms from TheJoysofBoys.com " width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Follow the link to see how I made this simple, inexpensive
          <a href="http://www.thejoysofboys.com/2013/05/09/diy-cake-stand/">
           basketball hoop cake plate
          </a>
          . Really, it is the coolest thing ever and Im sure my more crafty friends could have all sorts of fun with it.
         </p>
