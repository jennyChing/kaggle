        <a href="http://peanutbutterandwhine.com/3-important-health-benefits-to-cross-stitching/" rel="bookmark">
         3 Important Health Benefits to Cross-Stitching
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-07-07T04:32:11+00:00">
         July 7, 2015
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://peanutbutterandwhine.com/author/blogging/" rel="author">
           Connie Gruning
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://peanutbutterandwhine.com/3-important-health-benefits-to-cross-stitching/#comments">
          4 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div id="triberr_endorsement">
        </div>
        <p>
         There are many different reasons why a person takes up cross-stitching. Many times we learn that it was a skill passed down from generation to generation. Others take up cross-stitching later in life as a hobby. Regardless of how you started, one thing is certain: cross-stitching is good for our health. But how is it healthy for you to pick out
         <a href="http://threadedneedle.com.au/" target="_blank">
          detailed cross stitch patterns
         </a>
         and commit hours toward such an intricate project? Here are 3 important health benefits associated with cross-stitching.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Calming power
          </strong>
          . Cross-stitch enthusiasts who were recently interviewed overwhelmingly agreed that they feel a great calm as they work their way through a cross-stitch pattern. In fact, stitchings been compared to meditation. That task of pulling thread through fabric over and over again is like the repetition of a prayer or chant. In fact, when people feel overwhelmed by life, they can regain their composure by simply sitting down, breathing, and doing the simple and repetitive task of cross-stitching.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Increased focus
          </strong>
          . Although the motion is repetitive, you can easily miss a stitch, which is why you have to remain extremely focused. In fact, the repetitive motion of cross-stitching makes it even more challenging to stay focused, which is why when you do youre increasing your minds sharpness. Remaining focused with your mind staves off unwanted effects of aging, like dementia. Studies consistently show that a working mind is a healthy mind. Remaining focused is an extremely effective way of working your mind and keeping it youthful.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Flexibility of your hands
          </strong>
          . One of the best ways to keep your hands from stiffening up is to keep them moving. But what can you possibly do to keep them moving? Cross-stitching is the answer. The simple act of cross-stitching can help combat the effects of arthritis, while actually working to improve your hands dexterity. In fact, sufferers of arthritis claim that activities like cross-stitching help treat their aches and pains as good  if not better  than prescribed medications.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         The beauty of cross-stitching is that not only does the activity produce some beautiful artwork that you can give to others as gifts or hold onto for yourself, but its also good for your mind and body. Cross-stitch enthusiasts of all ages and skill level love discovering that next best cross-stich pattern or kit to conquer. Learn why more of these enthusiasts turn to Threaded Needle for their cross-stitching needs. Visit them online at http://threadedneedle.com.au/.
        </p>

         Transform Your Home One Step at a Time
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-07-06T19:28:44+00:00">
         July 6, 2015
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">

           Connie Gruning
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <div id="triberr_endorsement">
        </div>
        <p>
         Youre ready to give your home a makeover. Its your favorite place to be, but theres always room for improvements. Starting from the bottom up and laying
         <a href="http://www.renovationexperts.com/travertine-tileandstone.asp" target="_blank">
          natural stone tiles
         </a>
         can give your home a facelift. Before you go to work, consider bringing in the experts. Renovationexperts.com can help you to find the right contractor to get the job done. If youre going to make an investment in your home, you want the job done right the first time. Trust your floor work to the experts.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Look Before Your Leap
         </strong>
         <br/>
         It is a major decision when you decide to do a home improvement project. You want to take your time as you choose your contractor. Use renovationexperts.com as a valuable resource. You simply enter your location and the type of work that you want completed. You can also include information about your budget requirements. Contractors in your area will get in touch with you in order to discuss your project. Never go with the first contractor that contacts you. You will want to have a consultation with several, compare rates, find out about scheduling, and check reviews. You should be completely satisfied with the contractor that you hire in the end.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Make Sure You Have it in Writing
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Any time that you hire a contractor, you should have a written agreement concerning the job on your house. Whether you are building a new home or redoing your floors, you want all of the details laid out in black and white. This should include a time frame for your work and any warranty that is offered, as well as the cost of materials. You dont want any surprises when the work is completed except for a new floor that takes your breath away.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Enjoy the End Results
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Once renovationexperts.com has helped you to choose a contractor who is a good fit for you, sit back and watch your makeover happen. In the end, you will have attractive flooring that is easy to maintain. Being selective pays off.
        </p>
