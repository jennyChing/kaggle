      <p>
       Were a Liverpool based web design company. We have a creative team dedicated to working with you to bring your site alive in ways you may not have considered before.
      </p>
      <p>
       At iprogress our expertise lies in web design, content managed websites, eCommerce, email marketing and search engine optimisation. We make sure youre reaching the people you need to in order to maximise your businesss full potential.
      </p>
      <p>
       The key to designing a successful website is to understanding exactly what you need it to do. We spend time getting to know you and your business and ensure that were thinking the way youre thinking.
      </p>
      <p>
       Your website is the first point of contact for any potential clients. The impact should be immediate and lasting. Every visitor is a potential new customer and we hate to see a valuable marketing tool going to waste.
      </p>
      <p>
       We design sites to the
       <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://www.w3.org/standards/" rel="external nofollow" title="">

       </a>

      </p>
     </div>
     <div id="home_right">
      <p>
       We want as many people to see your website as possible. This is where search engine optimisation (SEO) comes in.
      </p>
      <p>
       Have you stopped to consider how many of your services are in the top ten in search engines? We can help you to reach markets you didnt even know about by researching the keywords people use to find your website. We consider keyword research to be vital part of any SEO campaign.
      </p>
      <p>
       iprogress is a small agency with a big vision. Weve deliberately kept the web design team small. This means that the agency owners, directors Peter Aland and Darren Walker, can be involved with every project that we undertake. This is because attention to detail is everything to us.
      </p>
      <p>
       Keeping the direct creative input of the directors is vital for us. It lets us deliver digital solutions to the high standard weve set for ourselves.
      </p>

     <h3 class="lines">
      <span>
       Our Web Design Methodology
      </span>
     </h3>
     <p>
      To us building a great site is a six phase process;
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <img alt="webdesign-sixsteps" class="alignright size-full wp-image-4684" height="480" src="https://www.iprogress.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/webdesign6steps.png" width="460"/>
       Step One
      </strong>
      is analysis where we discuss exactly what you need and how the site is going to work. This can include details like where the site will be hosted and what level of support you need for it.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Step Two
      </strong>
      is the specification you require where we outline every feature, explaining how it is going to work. This is also the phase where cost is finalised and the timescale were working to is agreed.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Step Three
      </strong>
      is the design phase where we make concepts for the site. Each one we design is available to preview and well work through all amendments with you.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Step Four
      </strong>
      is the build. We take the agreed designs and construct the site around them on our development servers, making sure youre involved at key milestones along the way.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Step Five
      </strong>
      is testing of the fully functional site. The tests are carried out by our designers and by the client so that we can jointly compile a bug list and iron out any difficulties.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Step Six
      </strong>
      is maintenance which we see as a continuation  and often starts the whole process again from Step One if any new or additional features are required.
     </p>
     <p>
      Wed like to work with you, give us a call and lets get started.
     </p>
