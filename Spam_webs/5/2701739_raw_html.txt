           <p class="submitted">
            Submitted by todd on Thu, 04/09/2015 - 22:09
           </p>
           Ah Chicago, home of the deep dish pizza. If you've never had a slice of deep dish pizza then you're truly missing out. Chicago may not be the first city you think of when discussing large American cities, but as a matter of fact it is the third largest in the country and its revenue is third in the country. O'Hare International Airport is almost always ranked number one for world's busiest airport each year. The city is also home to numerous cultural sites and has a seemingly endless number of places to visit and activities to partake in.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           One of the best parts of Chicago is its walkability as it was ranked fourth nationwide for walkable cities in a recent study. Given the level of activity in the city and its walkable nature, the city is an excellent place to hold a conference or large scale meeting.
           <a href="http://www.uniquevenues.com/venue_search/Illinois/Chicago">
            The Chicago venues are not only located in an ideal location, but the amenities included
           </a>
           at various venues make preparing for a conference or large scale event very easy. The walkability is great too because while visiting Chicago you will be tempted to sample many of the iconic dishes the city is famous for.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Several of the most exciting places to visit when in Chicago are the Willis Tower (formerly the Sears Tower), the Chicago Water Tower and the Magnificent Mile. The architecture of the Willis Tower and the Chicago Water Tower is simply stunning and the view from the Willis Tower is breath taking. The Magnificent Mile is a about a mile long stretch of road in the city that is home to upscale stores, restaurants and hotels; it attracts roughly 23 million visitors annually. There are many more places to visit beyond these and it'll definitely take more than one trip to Chicago to fully visit and appreciate them all.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Two of the best
           <a href="http://www.uniquevenues.com/MSIChicago">
            Chicago venues to book at are the Museum of Science and Industry
           </a>
           and Conference Chicago at University Center. The museum is right in the middle of the city, and provides all the amenities you could need along with theatres for spoken or video presentations. If that weren't enough, there are always amazing exhibits on display which are viewed by over 175 million guests each year. When attendees grow weary of being inside they can enjoy one of the 500 plus parks in Chicago or go to a sporting event like a baseball game. No matter what time of the year it is, there are sports going on in Chicago, which is perfect for attendees who are sports fans.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Few people outside of Chicago realize that because of Chicago's extensive park system it is one of the most scenic cities to visit. It has the nickname of "city within a park". Lastly, if you're a person who enjoys the arts then Chicago's extensive list of theaters and museums is sure to provide you with countless hours of enjoyment and relaxation.
