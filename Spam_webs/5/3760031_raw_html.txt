      <h1>
       About The Documentary
      </h1>
      <p>
       In
       <em>
        Guilty Except for Insanity
       </em>
       , Director and Professor of Psychology Jan Haaken goes behind the walls of the Oregon State Hospital, the location of
       <em>
        One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest
       </em>
       , and recounts the stories of real patients who enter this famous hospital for the "criminally insane." Their stories reveal the craziness of an American System where one must commit a crime to receive psychiatric help.  The documentary probes this maddening world and uncovers deeper psychological truths about the human need for care and connection, as well as for freedom. (85 minutes)
      </p>
