             <h1>
              <a class="link hubspot-editable" data-hubspot-form-id="name" data-hubspot-name="Blog Title" href="http://blog.kitchenmagic.com/blog/8-amazing-refacing-transformations" id="hubspot-name">
               <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_name" style="">
                8 Amazing Refacing Transformations [Before &amp; After Photos]
               </span>
              </a>
             </h1>
             <p class="hubspot-editable" data-hubspot-form-id="author_data" data-hubspot-name="Blog Author" id="hubspot-author_data">
              <a class="author-link" href="http://blog.kitchenmagic.com/blog/author/carrie-regular-contributor">
               Carrie (Regular Contributor)
              </a>
             </p>
             <h6 class="date">
              Mar 25, 2015 1:15:00 PM
             </h6>
             <p>
             </p>
             <!-- Go to www.addthis.com/dashboard to customize your tools -->
             <div class="addthis_jumbo_share hidden-xs share-counter">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section post-body">
             <span class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text" id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" style="">
              <p>
               The word "Transformation" is often used superfluously - but not in these cases. Just wait until you take a look at these 8 Amazing Refacing Transformations that took outdated kitchens and made them worthy of a kitchen design magazine spread.
              </p>
              <!--more-->
              <p>
               Have
               <a href="http://blog.kitchenmagic.com/blog/bid/101189/How-Much-Does-Refacing-Kitchen-Cabinets-Cost" target="_blank">
                questions about a kitchen cabinet refacing
               </a>
               example shown here - or cabinet refacing in general? Don't hesitate to contact us at
               <a href="http://www.kitchenmagic.com/" target="_blank">
                Kitchen Magic
               </a>
               ,or by calling
               <strong>
                1-855-5-REFACE
               </strong>
               .
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <p class="lg-cushion-t">
               <span class="num-list">
                1.
               </span>
               This kitchen was sweet - in a very 90's country kitchen sort of way. To bring it into the modern era, we refaced cabinets using laminate, Breckenridge doors - Cathedral on the uppers and Square on the lowers, all finished in antique white with a walnut glaze. An eye-catching Yellow Tiger granite countertop and neutral wall colors give this refreshed country kitchen an upscale look.
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation before" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformations_Before/Kitchen-1-Allain-Before-2.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=900" width="900"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformation/Kitchen-1-Allain-4.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=900" width="900"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformation/Kitchen-1-Allain.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <hr/>
              <p class="lg-cushion-t">
               <span class="num-list">
                2.
               </span>
               The original kitchenshouted "Blah-and-Blah!" It was dying for a makeover. We complied by installing Harvest Gold Cherry Wood cabinets. That simple change took the orange out of the kitchen tiles and brought out the warmer wood tones. Then we installed Baltic Brown granite countertops. Stenciling, backsplash and floor all remained the same - yet the final product is a whole new kitchen.
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation before" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformations_Before/Kitchen-2-Baer-Before-2.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformation/Kitchen-2-Baer.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <hr/>
              <p class="lg-cushion-t">
               <span class="num-list">
                3.
               </span>
               Mismatched becomes Choreographed with just a few simple changes. The previous kitchen had no unifying theme or style so everything looked jumbled. We used the fun flooring as inspiration and refaced the cabinets with a Frosty White laminate with an Elite Plus Raised Panel. A fun, fresh coat of paint and snazzyUbatubagranite countertops made it a
               <a href="http://blog.kitchenmagic.com/blog/bid/144907/Are-Laminate-Countertops-Like-Formica-out-of-Style" target="_blank">
                contemporary kitchen with a retro feel
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation before" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformations_Before/Kitchen-3-Bergen-Before-3.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformation/Kitchen-3-Bergen-2.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformation/Kitchen-3-Bergen-3.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <hr/>
              <p class="lg-cushion-t">
               <span class="num-list">
                4.
               </span>
               Kitchen or unfinished cave? That's what we wondered. Beautiful features, like the vaulted ceiling, were lost because the space was so cavernous. Anchoring it with a functional kitchen island in Autumn Hard Maple, and refacing the perimeter cabinets with Stonebridge laminate doors with Antique White glazecoziedeverything up.JuparanaCremaBordeaux granite adds rosy tones more successfully than the former pink laminate option. The new version says, "Welcome Home."
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation before" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformations_Before/Kitchen-4-Before-2.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=3200" width="3200"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://kitchenmagic.com/files/gallery/asset/kitchen14/kitchen1.jpg" width="2940"/>
              <hr/>
              <p class="lg-cushion-t">
               <span class="num-list">
                5.
               </span>
               The original kitchen couldn't make up its mind. Was it traditional? Contemporary? By refacing the cabinetry with laminate Cherry doors with a Black glaze, adding a peninsula and using ultra-durableSilestonecountertops inTealeaf, it can
               <a href="http://blog.kitchenmagic.com/blog/what-is-a-transitional-kitchen" target="_blank">
                officially relax into it's solid, transitional style
               </a>
               . Perfect for a growing family.
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation before" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformations_Before/Kitchen-5-Before-2.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformation/Kitchen-5-Dugatkin.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>
              <hr/>
              <p class="lg-cushion-t">
               <span class="num-list">
                6.
               </span>
               We thought this tiny kitchen was so cluttered - then realized it was just the horrid laminate pattern up against a busy wood-grain cabinets. We simplified things using Frosty White laminate cabinets with clean,Pershawstyle doors. The sleek RosemaryCoriancountertops with a complementary backsplash finished it off, and now we can all breathe a sigh of relaxed relief.
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <img alt="refaced kitchen transformation before" class="sm-cushion-t" data-constrained="true" src="http://landing.kitchenmagic.com/hs-fs/hubfs/images/Blog/8_Refacing_Transformations_Before/Kitchen-6-Before-2.jpg?t=1436561502567&amp;width=848" width="848"/>

Now this kitchen is able to be everything it every dreamed of.

               </span>

Refacing the cabinets with Washington Cherry wood doors with a black glaze helped to bring it back to earth and warm it up. In fact, that simple enriching of the overall kitchen tones even helped their existing granite countertop to
