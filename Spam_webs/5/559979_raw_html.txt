          <h1 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.interioridea.net/10-bedding-designs-that-works-superb-accent-in-the-bedroom/">
            10 Bedding Designs That Works Superb Accent In the Bedroom
           </a>
          </h1>
          <span class="author">
           <a href="http://www.interioridea.net/author/aleksandra/" rel="author" title="Posts by Aleksandra Andonovska">
            Aleksandra Andonovska
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="entry-audio">
          </div>
          <p>
           The easiest way to update your bedroom and boost up the aesthetics is by adding a new, ultra lively and vibrant bedding with creative design that will give a new perspective to the bedroom. Bedding with a striking pattern and vivid colors will instantly refresh the bedroom and break through as a superb visual effect in the interior. Naturally, the spectrum of bedding design is utterly wide and opulent, so you can choose a design that can easily blend in your bedroom. In order to provide you with fresh and interesting ideas we gathered a round up of super creative and bold bedding designs that could serve as an inspiration. Check the showcase below and get yourself inspired!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>

           </span>

          <p>
           <strong>
            BlueContemporaryBedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Blue-COntemporary-Bedding.jpg">
            <img alt="Blue COntemporary Bedding" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6214" height="627" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Blue-COntemporary-Bedding.jpg" width="1000"/>
           </a>
           Image Credit:AD
          </p>
          <p>
           You has surely noticed that your eyes got instantly attached to the superbly vibrant blue and green bedding with lively tribal pattern. The ultra vibrant bedding strikes as a focal point in this bedroom and brings ultimately upbeat and energetic ambiance.
          </p>

            Exotic Kids Bedroom Bedding
           </strong>

          <p>

          </p>

          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Black Striped Bedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>

           Image Credit:Decor Hub
          </p>
          <p>
           So eclectic and modern, right?  Black striped bedding decorated by various colorful pattern creates eclectic and sharp visual effect in the utterly charming and ease bedroom interior.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Exotic Bedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Exotic-Bedding.jpg">

           Image Credit:AD
          </p>
          <p>
           The bright and colorful geometric print bedding finds the perfect spot in the opulent spectrum of patterns, color and texture of this boho bedroom. The refreshing and bright color scheme of the bedding soothes the excessive dynamic and brings ease in the sumptuous interior.
          </p>

          </p>

           </b>

            Striped Bedding
           </strong>

            Eclectic Colorful Bedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Eclectic-Colorful-Bedding.jpg">

           </a>
           Image Credit:etsy
          </p>
          <p>
           How lovely and vibrant, right? -The bright and pastel color scheme incorporated with the geometric pattern works as a powerful and dynamic visual effect in this loose bedroom.
          </p>

          </p>

           <strong>
            Cottage Bedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Cottage-Bedding.jpg">

           </a>
           Image Credit:wayfair
          </p>
          <p>
           Warm colors and geometric pattern is a great idea to bring sharp and opulent ambiance in the bedroom interior. We love the warmth and sophisticated charm that this bedding brings to the lush and natural bedroom.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Boho Chic Bedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Boho-Chic-Bedding.jpg">
            <img alt="Boho Chic Bedding" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6216" height="1213" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Boho-Chic-Bedding.jpg" width="1000"/>
           </a>
           Image Credit:deco hub
          </p>
          <p>
           So eclectic and charming, right?  The colorful geometric pattern with exposed texture creates an incredibly lovely, warm and charming feel in this ecstatic and quirky bedroom.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Bohemian Bedding
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="lightbox" href="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Bohemian-Bedding.jpg">
            <img alt="Bohemian Bedding" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6215" height="987" src="http://www.interioridea.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Bohemian-Bedding.jpg" width="863"/>
           </a>
           Image Credit:decorpad
          </p>
          <p>
           The ethnic pattern and opulent colors of this handmade bedding are exquisite and fill this bedroom with dynamic and dramatic visual appearance. The colors are utterly bold and blend in the neutral color scheme of the exotic bedroom.
          </p>
