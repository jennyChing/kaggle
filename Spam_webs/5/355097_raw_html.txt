             More 
            </a>

         <div class="main">
          <div class="content entry-content">
           <div>
            <a href="http://travel.nationalgeographic.com/travel/travelers-of-the-year/booker-mitchell/" target="_blank" title="National Geographic Travel site - 2012 Travelers of the Year: Booker Mitchell">
             Traveler of the Year
            </a>
            <strong>
             Booker Mitchell
            </strong>
            was close to celebrating his fourth birthday when 9/11 happened. Millions call New York City home, and while no one personally knows even a percentage of the population, we unite at times when it matters most and act locally like any other small town in the country or world, he says. That is part of the magic of this city, and why everyone should visit it.
           </div>
           <div>
            It was hard moving a sleeping child in a stroller through the turnstiles on the subway, but New York was and continues to be the greatest playground for my kid, says Bookers mom and creative collaborator Tania Cypriano. Booker wants to see the world because living in New York he gets to see a little bit of it everyday. Heres the City That Never Sleeps through his eyes.
           </div>
           <div>
            <p>
             <em>
              Follow Bookers story on his
              <a href="http://bookertravels.com/" target="_blank">
               website
              </a>
              , or on Twitter
              <a href="https://twitter.com/BookerTravels" target="_blank" title="Twitter site - Booker Travels profile">
               @BookerTravels
              </a>
              .
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              New York City is My City
             </strong>
            </p>
            <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_47179" class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_47179" style="width: 423px">
             <img alt="Booker skateboarding in Union Square (Photograph byTania Cypriano)" class=" wp-image-47179 " height="253" src="http://intelligenttravel.nationalgeographic.com/files/2013/09/booker-mitchell-skateboarding-manhattan.jpg" width="413"/>
             <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_47179">
              Booker skateboarding in Union Square (Photograph byTania Cypriano)
             </figcaption>
            </figure>
            <p>
             <strong>
              When someone comes to visit me, the first place I take them is
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.explorechinatown.com/" target="_blank" title="Explore Chinatown site">
              Chinatown
             </a>
             . Though its notorious for strong fish smells and dirty sidewalks, I think Chinatown sums up the uniqueness of New York. Its crowded and noisy streets are picturesque as well, and I always have a blast finding bizarre foods and toys.
            </p>
            <p>
             Spring
             <strong>
              is the best time to visit my city
             </strong>
             becauseeveryone is on the street and the weather is perfect. Everyone gets excited that winter is over and that summer is coming. All of a sudden you see people on the streets again trying new restaurants, checking out stores, and just getting out the house to see all the green. Beautiful flowers and trees in bloom run down the streets.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              You can see my city best from
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.brooklynbridgepark.org/" target="_blank" title="Brooklyn Bridge Park site">
              Brooklyn Bridge Park
             </a>
             . Right by the water you can get an amazing view of the famous New York City skyline. Its free, not crowded, and you can stay there for as long as you want, so thats why I like it more than the Empire State Building or any other main tourist attraction.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Locals know to skip
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.timessquarenyc.org/index.aspx" target="_blank" title="Times Square site">
              Times Square
             </a>
             <strong>
              and check out
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.nycgovparks.org/parks/washingtonsquarepark" target="_blank" title="NYC Government site - Washington Square Park">
              Washington Square Park
             </a>
             <strong>
              instead
             </strong>
             . Times Square is wild and interesting to see, but if youre looking for a place to sit and enjoy a beautiful afternoon Washington Square Park is the place to be. Its really relaxing and there are always a handful of interesting characters and street performers to brighten up your day.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.chess-shop.com/" target="_blank" title="Chess Shop site">
              Village Chess Shop
             </a>
             <strong>
              is the place to buy authentic, local souvenirs
             </strong>
             . Right next to Washington Square Park you can find all sorts of cool chess boards that represent New York. I dont think you can buy chess boards like that anywhere else in the world, plus the shop is REALLY cool.
            </p>
            <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_47180" class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_47180" style="width: 423px">
             <img alt="Crowds relaxing in Central Park (Photograph by Elliott Scott, Flickr)" class=" wp-image-47180 " height="276" src="http://intelligenttravel.nationalgeographic.com/files/2013/09/central-park-manhattan.jpg" width="413"/>
             <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_47180">
              Crowds relaxing in Central Park (Photograph by Elliott Scott, Flickr)
             </figcaption>
            </figure>
            <p>
             <strong>
              In the past, notable people like
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.johnlennon.com/" target="_blank" title="John Lennon site">
              John Lennon
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.haring.com/!/about-haring#.Ui5AW2TSNzI" target="_blank" title="Haring site - About">
              Keith Haring
             </a>
             , and
             <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Gonzales" target="_blank" title="Wikipedia site - Mark Gonzales">
              Mark Gonzales
             </a>
             <strong>
              have called my city home
             </strong>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              My citys best museum is
             </strong>
             the
             <a href="http://www.moma.org/" target="_blank" title="MoMA site">
              Museum of Modern Art
             </a>
             becauseits massive, really well designed, and has more than enough stuff to see. Theres always at least one show there thats worth checking out.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              If theres one thing you should know about getting around my city, its
             </strong>
             that trains on the weekends are ridiculously unreliable. Its a weird tip to give, but on the weekends dont plan on getting anywhere thats more than 20 blocks away on time.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The best place to spend time outdoors in my city is
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.centralparknyc.org/" target="_blank" title="Central Park site">
              Central Park
             </a>
             . Thats a given. Its perfect for all occasions. When its nice out you can lay around in the grass and just hang out with your friends, and when its cold and snowy you can sled, build a snowman, or have a snowball fight.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              My city really knows how to celebrate
             </strong>
             Halloweenbecauseth
             <wbr>
             </wbr>
             e parade we have is such a city wide attraction. Whether you are sitting at home watching it live on TV, on the street watching it pass by, or in the parade, dressed up yourself, and a part of it, its always a blast. People take their costumes really seriously, and you can see some crazy things, its really mind-blowing.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              You can tell if someone is from my city if
             </strong>
             they are up for walking more than 10 blocks without complaining. Everyone in New York City is constantly moving.
            </p>
            <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_47181" class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_47181" style="width: 423px">
             <img alt="A couple admires an exhibit at the Museum of Modern Art (Photograph by Andrew Brannan, Flickr)" class=" wp-image-47181 " height="327" src="http://intelligenttravel.nationalgeographic.com/files/2013/09/couple-paintings-moma-manhattan.jpg" width="413"/>
             <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_47181">
              A couple admires an exhibit at the Museum of Modern Art (Photograph by Andrew Brannan, Flickr)
             </figcaption>
            </figure>
            <p>
             <strong>
              For a fancy night out, I
             </strong>
             like to walk around and try a new upscale restaurant. I dont usually go out for dinner, but New York definitely doesnt lack good restaurants, and there are always new ones to try.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Just outside my city, you can visit
             </strong>
             <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_Beach,_New_York" target="_blank" title="Wikipedia site - Long Beach, New York">
              Long Beach
             </a>
             or
             <a href="http://www.nycgovparks.org/parks/rockawaybeach" target="_blank" title="NYC government site - Rockaway Beach">
              Rockaway Beach
             </a>
             and surf. Surprisingly less than an hour away from the city you can find world class waves with the right swell. But even if surfing isnt your thing, a beach day is always refreshing, especially during a New York City summer.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              My city is known for being
             </strong>
             rude and aggressive,
             <strong>
              but its really
             </strong>
             welcoming. Every once in a while youll bump shoulders with someone, but there are wayyy too many nice and interesting people that cancel that out.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The best outdoor market in my city is
             </strong>
             the
             <a href="http://www.grownyc.org/greenmarket-site/manhattan/union-square-greenmarket" target="_blank" title="Union Square Market site">
              Union Square Market
             </a>
             . All types of fresh and organic foods come from local farms, and the entire park smells amazing for the whole day. You can tell its natural and fresh too because everything there is so seasonal.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.murraysbagels.com/" target="_blank" title="Murray's Bagel site">
              Murrays Bagel
             </a>
             <strong>
              is my favorite place to grab breakfast, and
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.zumschneider.com/" target="_blank" title="Zum Schneider site">
              Zum Schneider
             </a>
             <strong>
              is the spot for late-night eats
             </strong>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              To find out whats going on at night and on the weekends, read
             </strong>
             street posters. Theyre posted up everywhere, and they tell you all you need to know about upcoming events and concerts.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              My citys biggest sports event
             </strong>
             isthats too hard. Watch them at
             <a href="http://www.thegarden.com/" target="_blank" title="Madison Square Garden site">
              Madison Square Garden
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_47182" class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_47182" style="width: 423px">
             <img alt="A flower vendor at the Union Square Market (Photograph by Mat McDermott, Flickr)" class=" wp-image-47182 " height="275" src="http://intelligenttravel.nationalgeographic.com/files/2013/09/flowers-union-market-manhattan.jpg" width="413"/>
             <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_47182">
              A flower vendor at the Union Square Market (Photograph by Mat McDermott, Flickr)
             </figcaption>
            </figure>
            <p>
             <strong>
              When Im feeling cash-strapped, I
             </strong>
             just grab a hotdog from the classic New York City street vendors. But in Chinatown there is this little hole in the wall by Worth Street where you can get five dumplings for a steal ($1.25 at last check).
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              To escape the crowds, I
             </strong>
             drive out to Long Beach, go surfing, and grab a burger from
             <a href="http://www.fiveguys.com/" target="_blank" title="Five Guys site">
              Five Guys
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              If my city were a celebrity itd be
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000221/" target="_blank" title="IMDB site - Charlie Sheen">
              Charlie Sheen
             </a>
             becauseyou never know what is going to happen next.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The dish that represents my city best is
             </strong>
             pizza,
             <strong>
              and
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.snapple.com/" target="_blank" title="Snapple site">
              Snapple
             </a>
             <strong>
              is my citys signature drink
             </strong>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             The
             <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flatiron_Building" target="_blank" title="Wikipedia site - Flatiron Building">
              Flatiron
             </a>
             <strong>
              is my favorite building in town because
             </strong>
             its amazingly beautiful and it gives you a great view down two of New Yorks most exciting avenues.
            </p>
            <p>
             The most random thing about my city arethe advertisements that you see on the subway. Some of them make no sense or are about the most pointless things.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.terminal5nyc.com/" target="_blank">
              Terminal 5
             </a>
             is the best place to see live music, but if youre in the mood to dance, check outIm not sure; I dont do that yet.
            </p>
            <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_47183" class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_47183" style="width: 423px">
             <img alt="The Flatiron building at sunset (Photograph by Dan Nguyen, Flickr)" class=" wp-image-47183 " height="300" src="http://intelligenttravel.nationalgeographic.com/files/2013/09/flatiron-building-manhattan.jpg" width="413"/>
             <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_47183">
              The Flatiron building at sunset (Photograph by Dan Nguyen, Flickr)
             </figcaption>
            </figure>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.amnh.org/our-research/hayden-planetarium/resources/manhattanhenge" target="_blank" title="American Museum of Natural History site - Manhattanhenge">
              Manhattanhenge
             </a>
             <strong>
              could only happen in my city
             </strong>
             . Its when the sun lines up with the citys street grid and illuminates EVERYTHING. Its really impressive.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              In the spring you should
             </strong>
             watch a
             <a href="http://newyork.yankees.mlb.com/index.jsp?c_id=nyy" target="_blank" title="Major League Baseball site - New York Yankees">
              Yankees
             </a>
             game.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              In the summer you should
             </strong>
             go see a free event at Central Park or
             <a href="http://www.prospectpark.org/" target="_blank" title="Prospect Park site">
              Prospect Park
             </a>
             in Brooklyn. You can see free movies, the
             <a href="http://nyphil.org/" target="_blank" title="New York Philharmonic site">
              New York Philharmonic
             </a>
             and concerts from famous bands that come from all over the world.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              In the fall you should
             </strong>
             go upstate and see all the trees change color.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              In the winter you should
             </strong>
             go to the
             <a href="http://www.yelp.com/biz/union-square-holiday-market-new-york" target="_blank" title="Yelp site - Union Square Holiday Market">
              Union Square Holiday Market
             </a>
             to do your gift shopping, and have the best hot cider and hot chocolate ever. You should also walk around Fifth Avenue to check out all the Christmas decorations and lights.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              If you have kids (or are a kid at heart), you wont want to miss
             </strong>
             the
             <a href="http://social.macys.com/parade/" target="_blank" title="Macy's site - Parade">
              Macys Thanksgiving Day Parade
             </a>
             . Famous characters from movies, cartoons, comics, and plays fly down Broadway. I used to look forward to this event every year when I was younger.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The best book about my city is
             </strong>
             I dont know of any book that captures the feeling that New York gives.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              When I think about my city, the song that comes to mind is
             </strong>
             
             <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WiuorrXsngM" target="_blank" title='YouTube site - "New York is Killing Me," by Gil Scott-Heron'>
              New York is Killing Me
             </a>
              by
             <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gil_Scott-Heron" target="_blank" title="Wikipedia site - Gil Scott-Heron">
              Gil Scott-Heron
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <div>
             <strong>
              In 140 characters or less, the world should heart my city because
             </strong>
             it has everything you could ever want. A beach, parks, amazing food and museums, diverse neighborhoods, and world-class sports teams.
            </div>
           </div>
