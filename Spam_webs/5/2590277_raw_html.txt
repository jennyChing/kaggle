       <figure aria-describedby="figcaption_attachment_503527" class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_503527" style="width: 620px">
        <img alt="Fabolous poses on Tao red carpet. Fabolous Photos: Tony Tran" class="size-rtp-large-thumb wp-image-503527" height="434" src="http://hauteliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Fabolous-Poses-on-TAO-Red-Carpet-640x448.jpg" width="620"/>
        <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_503527">
         Fabolous poses on Tao red carpet. Fabolous Photos: Tony Tran
        </figcaption>
       </figure>
       <p>
        On Saturday night, R&amp;B and hip-hop sensation Fabolous hosted the night at Tao Nightclub at the Venetian. Fabolous posed on the red carpet and took over a VIP table on the packed dance floor of excited fans. Shortly after, the Grammy award-nominated artist headed to the stage to treat revelers to a performance of several of his hit songs. Cameras flew up into the air and partygoers sang along throughout the entire show. After closing out his performance, the rapper returned to his VIP table to join his friends.
       </p>
       <p>
        Spotted at a neighboring VIP table enjoying Fabolous performance was Real Housewives of Atlanta star NeNe Leakes. Leakes and a group of friends spent the night taking in the party atmosphere and sounds of Justin Credible.
       </p>
       <p>
        On Friday night, Leakes celebrated the her first performances at the Mistress of Sensuality in Zumanity at Lily Bar at the Bellagio. Before arriving, Leakes performed in two back to back shows, where she wore brilliant costumes created by the famed Chagoury Couture. After the show, Leakes and her entourage headed to Lily Bar &amp; Lounge at Bellagio for an intimate celebration. Dressed in a cropped white blouse, black pants and dainty stiletto sandals, the platinum blonde housewife posed for pics with friends and celebrated with champagne. Leakes will star in six more shows with the Cirque du Soleil production
       </p>
