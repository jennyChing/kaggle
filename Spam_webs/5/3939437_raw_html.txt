         </h2>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://podcasts.urbusinessnetwork.com/Write%20Out%20Loud/2013_11_25%20-%20Mary%20Farr%20-%20Never%20Say%20Neigh%20-%20Write%20Out%20Loud.mp3">
           Listen to Mary Farr on Write Out Loud.
          </a>
          <br/>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="Mary Farr" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-8462" height="150" src="http://urbusinessnetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/maryfarr-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
          <strong>
           We were so excited to welcome Saint Paul, Minnesota author Mary I. Farr to Write Out Loud.
          </strong>
          Mary Farr has devoted the past 30 years to exploring the worlds of hope, healing and humor. Today she has merged these life essentials into a wildly funny and gently inspirational book,
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Never-Neigh-Adventure-Funny-Power/dp/1938690591/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1385992076&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=Never+say+Neigh" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Never-Neigh-Adventure-Funny-Power/dp/1938690591/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1385992076&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=Never+say+Neigh', 'Never Say Neigh');">
           <i>
            Never Say Neigh
           </i>
          </a>
          <i>
           .
          </i>
          The book recently won honors from
          <a href="http://www.parisbookfestival.com/winners2013.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.parisbookfestival.com/winners2013.html', 'The Paris Book Festival');">
           The Paris Book Festival
          </a>
          , the
          <a href="http://www.greatmidwestbookfestival.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.greatmidwestbookfestival.com/', 'Great Midwest Book Festival');">
           Great Midwest Book Festival
          </a>
          and the
          <a href="http://www.animalsbookfestival.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.animalsbookfestival.com/', 'Animals, Animals, Animals Book Festival.');">
           Animals, Animals, Animals Book Festival.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          A retired hospital chaplain with plenty of wisdom under her belt and a lifelong passion for horses, Farr chose an unusual writing partner for her award-winning bookher American quarter horse, Noah Vail. Even his name says he has a funny bone of his own.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is a comical horse, Farr says. Hes just the kind of character I imagined could talk to people about life and its many lessons, but in a welcoming way. I figured why not use him as a humorous spiritual corrective in an often noisy world of gridlock.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Never-Neigh-Adventure-Funny-Power/dp/1938690591/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1383483256&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=Never+Say+Neigh" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Never-Neigh-Adventure-Funny-Power/dp/1938690591/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1383483256&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=Never+Say+Neigh', 'Never Say Neigh ');">
           <i>
            Never Say Neigh
           </i>
          </a>
          encompasses a year on the road with Noah and his partner Madam, sometimes referred to as The Management. Compassion is the order of the day for Noah. He eschews violence, prejudice and polarized politics  all with a generous dose of levity and fun.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its hard to argue with a horse, Farr says. Noah as the books narrator makes the most difficult topics approachable for readers. He also opines on a good deal of human behavior.
         </p>
         <p>
          Even Noahs blogs have won him acclaim as an
          <a href="http://humorwriters.org/2012/05/noah-vail-and-mary-farr/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://humorwriters.org/2012/05/noah-vail-and-mary-farr/', 'Erma Bombeck Writers Workshop');">
           Erma Bombeck Writers Workshop
          </a>
          Humor Writer of the Month. And hes nothing if not a well-rounded author. He keeps an active
          <a href="https://twitter.com/NoahVail1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://twitter.com/NoahVail1', 'Twitter');">
           Twitter
          </a>
          account, a
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/NoahVailPublishing" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/NoahVailPublishing', 'Facebook');">
           Facebook
          </a>
          page with some 90,000 friends and a
          <a href="http://www.noahvailpublishing.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.noahvailpublishing.com/', 'blog');">
           blog
          </a>
          . Fans can also find him on
          <a href="http://noahvailpublishing.com/work/noah-on-youtube/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://noahvailpublishing.com/work/noah-on-youtube/', 'YouTube');">
           YouTube
          </a>
         </p>
