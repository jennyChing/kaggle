       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15095" style="width: 514px">
         <img alt="regular customer main" class="wp-image-15095 size-full" height="336" src="https://blog.scottsmarketplace.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/regular-customer-main.jpg" title="A Fast and Easy Way to Reward Your Regular Customers by Scott's Marketplace" width="504"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Dont have a large budget? You might be surprised at how affordable and easy it is to show your regular customers how much you appreciate their loyalty.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         As we all know, regular customers equal regular profit. But if you go a bit deeper than that, you also know that regulars often become brand advocates who get you the coveted word-of-mouth promotion you desire, both in-person and online.
        </p>
        <p>
         Which means theyre also the ones who can help you build a great reputation for your business through various social media and review sites.
        </p>
        <p>
         When a person really, really
         <a href="https://blog.scottsmarketplace.com/customer-satisfaction/" title="10 Ways to Make Your Customers Love Your Business More">
          loves what you have to offer
         </a>
         , they want to tell others about it, and thats
         <strong>
          the most valuable marketing tool
         </strong>
         you could possibly ever hope for.
        </p>
        <p>
         Why, then, do businesses often do everything they can to encourage new customers with special first-purchase-only discounts and other incentives, while ignoring those whove been buying for years?
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out a fast and easy way you can begin
         <strong>
          rewarding your regular customers
         </strong>
         to help ensure they continue to choose your business for many more years to come.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          How ProFlowers Secured Customer Loyalty With a Simple Act
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15094" style="width: 460px">
         <img alt="ProFlowers2" class="wp-image-15094 size-full" height="420" src="https://blog.scottsmarketplace.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ProFlowers2.jpg" title="A Fast and Easy Way to Reward Your Regular Customers by Scott's Marketplace" width="450"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Thanks, Doug!
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Every time I want to order flowers for someone, I do a quick search on Google to see what my options are. (And there are many.)
        </p>
        <p>
         I wouldnt say Im exactly loyal to any flower company, but I have ordered from ProFlowers a few times, as well as a couple other companies.
        </p>
        <p>
         But my latest experience with ProFlowers has made me want to order from them again  and most likely will send me direct to their site next time, rather than back to a Google search.
        </p>
        <p>
         Not too long ago, I ordered 18 roses for my grandma from ProFlowers and the day she was due to receive them, I got an email from the company. I thought it was a confirmation email that theyd been delivered, but it actually was an email letting me know that theyd
         <strong>
          upgraded my order
         </strong>
         to 24 roses for no other reason than to thank me for my business, which I thought was really great.
        </p>
        <p>
         For ProFlowers, this strategy was simple, affordable, and incredibly
         <a href="https://blog.scottsmarketplace.com/excellent-customer-service/" title="How Customer Service Is a Lot Like Foreplay">
          easy to implement
         </a>
         , yet it really made all the difference to me, as no other company has ever done anything similar.
        </p>
        <p>
         Adding in that unexpected
         <strong>
          element of surprise
         </strong>
         to your customer service strategy can really make a lasting impression, and go a long way toward securing loyalty.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          How You Can Implement This Strategy
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Rewarding your regular customers doesnt have to be expensive or time-consuming. Below are some ideas on how you can begin implementing this customer service strategy today:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Upgrade shipping.
          </strong>
          Send your customer an email letting them know that since theyve been such a valued customer to you, youve upgraded their shipping to overnight or next-day at no extra charge.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Add a little something into their order.
          </strong>
          When I ordered from an online seller last holiday season, I found a cute, complimentary key chain in the box as a thank-you gift. You can do the same thing by adding in a smaller item that you sell into your regular customers orders.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Write a thank-you note.
          </strong>
          Sometimes its nice just to know your loyalty has been noticed. You can do this for your best customers by handwriting a thank-you note and dropping it in their online order. Reference them by name and let them know how much youve appreciated their business over the years.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Include a sizable coupon.
          </strong>
          Drop a coupon for $10 off your customers next order or 25-50% off.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Comp a product unexpectedly.
          </strong>
          This could get pricey fast, but if you have a handful of extremely loyal customers, it might be worth it. Let your customer know when theyre at the register that this ones on you for continually choosing your business over competitors. Or, if theyve placed an online order, refund their money and let them know why.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Upgrade their order.
          </strong>
          You can do a version of what ProFlowers did by upgrading a customers order unexpectedly. Sell jewelry? Upgrade the chain on a necklace or toss in one of your bestselling bracelets. Make candles? Throw in a complimentary candle in one of your latest scents.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Never assume that your regulars will keep coming back on their own.
         <strong>
          Show them theyre appreciated
         </strong>
         and that you truly value their decision to continually purchase from your business by choosing one of the ideas above or coming up with your own.
        </p>
        <p>
         Remember, even little touches, like an upgrade to six more roses, can make a big difference in how people feel about your business  and who they remember when it comes time to purchase again.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          How do you show appreciation for your regular customers?
         </strong>
        </h2>
