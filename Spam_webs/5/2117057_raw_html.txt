        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Rapid Realty Boss Offers Employees A 15% Pay Rise If They Get Tattooed With Company Logo
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="rapid-realty-tattoo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-26683" height="335" src="http://www.buzzpatrol.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/rapid-realty-tattoo.jpg" width="600"/>
          <br/>
          Anthony Lolli, the manager of New York real estate agency Rapid Realty has offered his staff a 15% pay rise if they are willing to get a tattoo of the company logo!
         </p>
         <p>
          Since Lolli made the unusual offer, around 40 staff have taken him up on it and had themselves inked with the companys green and black logo.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whilst some may suggest that the only reason that these employees have been tattooed is for the obvious financial benefits, many Rapid Realty workers say it means much more than that.
          <span id="more-26682">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          One employee, Robert Trezza said, I think its a good opportunity to show commitment to a company that makes going to work fun every day.  Whereas another, Joseph Tighe said, My wife was a little concerned but I said, you know what, it was the best commitment I could think of.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lolli got the idea to offer the financial tattoo incentive after one loyal staff member telephoned him to let him know he planned to get the logo inked.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Rapid Realty tattoos cost around $300 (obviously dependent on size) and there are no demands regarding their location on the body.   Lolli, who thought up the crazy scheme, has yet to get a Rapid Realty tattoo himself, but says he plans on getting one eventuallyYup, weve heard that one before!
         </p>
         <p>
          The cynic inside me hopes that none of the 40 employees who got inked get the sack, at least not before the 15% pay rise has amassed them enough funds to pay for laser removal!  But, in saying that , it does sound like it is a cool place to work, as I dont think people would be willing to have their bodies emblazed with a logo just for money  although a cash incentive certainly helps!
         </p>
