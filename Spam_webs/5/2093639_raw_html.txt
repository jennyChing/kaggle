           <h1 class="post-title">
            Cancer Cures More Deadly Than Disease
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://alterminds.xyz/author/guest/" rel="author" title="Posts by Guest">
             Guest
            </a>
             February 17, 2015
           </p>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <br/>
             <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
             </script>
             <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9729029036220847" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="5634946208" style="display:block">
             </ins>
             <script>
              (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
             </script>
             <br/>
             <div class="author">
              <a data-rel="lightbox-0" href="http://i1.wp.com/web02.pcheaven.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/1de85b93-1fdd-42ae-9f31-9354910fe603_post.jpg">
               <img alt="1de85b93-1fdd-42ae-9f31-9354910fe603_post" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-125" data-recalc-dims="1" data-wp-pid="125" src="//i1.wp.com/web02.pcheaven.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/1de85b93-1fdd-42ae-9f31-9354910fe603_post.jpg?resize=620%2C388"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              By Dr. Hardin Jones
             </div>
             <p>
              Treatment for cancer will kill you a lot sooner than if, nothing were done at all, claims a prominent cancer researcher.
             </p>
             <p>
              My studies have proved conclusively that untreated cancer victims actually live up to four times longer than treated individuals, Dr. Hardin B. Jones told MIDNIGHT.
             </p>
             <p>
              For a typical type of cancer, people who refused treatment lived for an average of 12 1/2 years. Those who, accepted surgery and other kinds of treatment lived an average of only three years!
             </p>
             <p>
              Beyond a shadow of a doubt, radical surgery on cancer patients does more harm than good.
             </p>
             <p>
              As for radiation treatment Most of the time it makes not the slightest difference whether the machine is turned on or not.
             </p>
             <p>
              Dr. Jones, a physiologist with the University of California Dept. of Medical Physics, has been studying cancer for more than 23 years.
             </p>
             <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
              <center>
               <script src="//ib.adnxs.com/ttj?id=4323755&amp;referrer=[REFERRER_URL]" type="text/javascript">
               </script>
              </center>
             </div>
             <p>
              He has traveled the world collecting data on the dreaded disease, and presented his findings to the American Cancer Society and medical schools.
             </p>
             <p>
              Asked why the medical world has ignored his findings, he replied: Frankly, I dont know the reasons. But they have probably become caught up in the tidal wave of individuals demanding treatment.
             </p>
             <p>
              This has been shown especially in the type of bone cancer  osteogenic sarcoma  that affects the large knee joints, Dr. Jones said.
             </p>
             <p>
              Sen. Edward M. Kennedys son, Ted Jr., suffered from that disease and his affected leg was amputated.
             </p>
             <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
              <center>
               <script src="//ib.adnxs.com/ttj?id=4323755&amp;referrer=[REFERRER_URL]" type="text/javascript">
               </script>
              </center>
             </div>
             <p>
              A surgeon is tempted to amputate, just for the relief of pain, Dr. Jones noted. But, unfortunately, it seems to be only a question of time, usually, before the disease pops up again all over the body.
             </p>
             <p>
              I attribute this to the traumatic effect of surgery on the bodys natural defense mechanisms, he said.
             </p>
             <p>
              The body has a natural defense against every type of cancer, Dr. Jones maintained.
             </p>
             <p>
              Medical treatment seems to interfere with and mess up this natural resistance, he said.
             </p>
             <p>
              You see, it is not the cancer that kills the victim. Its the breakdown of the defense mechanism that eventually brings death.
             </p>
             <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
              <center>
               <iframe src="//go.padstm.com/?id=242175&amp;t=iframe" style="width:728px;height:90px;border:0;overflow:hidden;">
               </iframe>
              </center>
             </div>
             <p>
              Dr. Jones said he advocates less surgery and chemotherapy.
             </p>
             <p>
              And he pooh-poohs what he said were claims by the medical profession of certain cancer cures.
             </p>
             <p>
              The fact remains, said Dr. Jones, that just as many people die today from cancer as they did in the year 1900. The mortality rate hasnt changed much.
             </p>
             <p>
              With every cancer patient who keeps in excellent physical shape and boosts his health to build up his natural resistance, theres a high chance the body will find its own defense against the cancer, he said.
             </p>
             <p>
              He may have many good years left in good health. He shouldnt squander them by being made into a hopeless invalid through radical medical intervention, which has zero chance of extending his life.
             </p>
             <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
              <center>
               <iframe src="//go.padstm.com/?id=242179&amp;t=iframe" style="width:728px;height:90px;border:0;overflow:hidden;">
               </iframe>
              </center>
             </div>
             <p>
              Dr. Jones agreed that lumps in the breasts should be checked out.
             </p>
             <p>
              But its utter nonsense to claim that catching cancer symptoms early enough will increase the patients chances of survival, he said.
             </p>
             <p>
              Not one medical scientist or study has proven that so in any way.
             </p>
             <p>
              Furthermore, untreated breast cancer, cases show a life expectancy four times longer than treated ones, he averred.
             </p>
             <p>
              My wife and I have discussed what she would do if breast cancer was diagnosed in her, Dr. Jones continued.
             </p>
             <p>
              And we both agree she would do nothing as regards treatment, except to keep as healthy as possible.
             </p>
             <p>
              I guarantee she would live longer!
             </p>
             <p>
              For not only does radical surgery or chemotherapy do nothing to prolong a cancer victims life, but that same victim will, in most cases, live a lot longer if he or she refuses, treatment.
             </p>
             <p>
              (via
              <a href="http://www.rethinkingcancer.org/resources/magazine-articles/2_1-2/cancer-cures-more-deadly-than-disease.php" target="_blank">
               RethinkingCancer
              </a>
              )
             </p>
