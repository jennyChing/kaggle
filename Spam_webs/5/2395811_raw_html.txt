     <h1>
      <a href="http://blog.doofix.com/how-to-remove-ads-by-vid-saver-adware-removal-guide/">
       How to Remove Ads by Vid-Saver (Adware Removal Guide)
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="auth" style="margin-top:10px;">
      <i>
       Posted by
       <a href="https://plus.google.com/u/0/101360546273200987710/about?rel=author" title="google plus">
        John Larkin
       </a>
       - December 8, 2014
      </i>
     </div>
     <p style="margin: 0 0 15px 0;padding:0;">
     </p>
     <p>
      What is Vid-Saver? How can I uninstall it from my computer? I cannot put up with the ads by Vid-Saver. Please show me how to get rid of Vid-Saver.
     </p>
     <h2>
      The Information of Vid-Saver
     </h2>
     <p>
      Vid-Saver (also known as Win32/VidSaver) is classified as an adware program that will hijack web browsers and cause chaos. This Vid-Saver can be installed onto targeted computers by freeware, shareware and spam email attachments. If you want to avoid this notorious adware program, you will also need to take careful of unsolicited free downloads and other resources. Once inside, Vid-Saver will insert a lot of advertisements into every web page that you visit. You will feel upset when your browser is attacked with tons of endless pop-ups. And it is pitiful that you have no idea how to eliminate these ads completely.
     </p>
     <p>
      When your browser is attacked by the ads for long, its usability and performance will be slowed down. Worse still, Vid-Saver can make use of web browser security flaws to track your browser histories. If this adware program collects your saved passwords, cookies and caches, it will utilize the data to perform malicious activities. One possibility is that Vid-Saver will leak out your data to third parties or cyber criminals. In a word, Vid-Saver is a potentially threat that should be removed from your computer.
     </p>
     <p>
      <span id="more-5133">
      </span>
     </p>
     <h2>
      The Screenshot of Vid-Saver
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5134" height="365" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2014/12/Vid-Saver-com.jpg" title="Vid-Saver-com" width="600"/>
     </p>
     <h2>
      Many More Troubles Caused by Vid-Saver Virus
     </h2>
     <p>
      1. Vid-Saver virus will slow up the whole PC performance greatly.
      <br/>
      2. Vid-Saver virus injects many malicious files to the system registry stealthily.
      <br/>
      3. Vid-Saver virus also drops many useless pop-up ads into your browsers.
      <br/>
      4. Many misleading browser add-ons are installed by Vid-Saver virus without approval.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Manually Get Rid of Vid-Saver Virus
     </h2>
     <div id="headerd">
      <img src="http://www.doofix.com/blog/img/headerd.jpg"/>
     </div>
     <div id="imgcss">
      <a href="#" onclick=" return df_fix()" target="_blank">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-801" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2014/01/livedown.gif"/>
      </a>
     </div>
     <h3>
      1. Uninstall malicious toolbars or software that cause Vid-Saver on your system.
     </h3>
     <p>
      To uninstall the toolbar or program which causes Ge-Force, please click
      <strong>
       Start
      </strong>
      button, click
      <strong>
       Control Panel
      </strong>
      , and then click
      <strong>
       Programs and Features
      </strong>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-92" height="340" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/11/programs_and-features.jpg" title="programs_and-features" width="520"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       For Windows XP users
      </strong>
      , please click
      <strong>
       Start
      </strong>
      button, click
      <strong>
       Control Panel
      </strong>
      , and then click
      <strong>
       Add or Remove Programs
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       For Windows 8 users
      </strong>
      , please right click the bottom-left corner in the screen and select
      <strong>
       Control Panel
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-97" height="382" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/win-8-control-panel.jpg" title="win 8 control panel" width="520"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Then select
      <strong>
       Uninstall a program and then uninstall malicious toolbars.
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-134" height="406" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/Windows-8-Uninstall-a-program-option-in-Control-Panel.jpg" title="Windows-8-Uninstall-a-program-option-in-Control-Panel" width="450"/>
     </p>
     <h3>
      2. Uninstall malicious add-ons that cause Vid-Saveron your browsers.
     </h3>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Vid-Saver Removal from Internet Explorer
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      1. To get rid of Vid-Saver from Internet Explorer, please run your Internet Explorer, click
      <strong>
       Tools
      </strong>
      and select
      <strong>
       Manage Add-ons
      </strong>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-143" height="362" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/ie-manage-add-ons.jpg" title="ie manage add-ons" width="304"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      2. Select Vid-Saver and other malicious toolbars or extensions and then click
      <strong>
       Disable
      </strong>
      button.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-152" height="351" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/disable-ie-add-ons.jpg" title="disable ie add-ons" width="538"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Vid-Saver Removal from Google Chrome
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      1. To remove Vid-Saver from Google Chrome, please run your Google Chrome, click on
      <strong>
       Customize and control Google Chrome
      </strong>
      , move your mouse to
      <strong>
       Tools
      </strong>
      and then select
      <strong>
       Extension
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-158" height="417" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/chrome-tools-extensions.jpg" title="chrome tools extensions" width="538"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      2. Find Vid-Saver and other malicious toolbars or extensions that cause Vid-Saverand then click
      <strong>
       Remove from Chrome
      </strong>
      button.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-161" height="412" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/remove-chrome-extensions.jpg" title="remove chrome extensions" width="620"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Vid-Saver Removal from Mozilla Firefox
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      1. To delete Vid-Saver from Mozilla Firefox, please run your Mozilla Firefox, click on
      <strong>
       Tools
      </strong>
      and select
      <strong>
       Add-ons
      </strong>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-165" height="243" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/firefox-addons.jpg" title="firefox addons" width="460"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      2. Find Vid-Saver and other malicious toolbars or extensions that cause Vid-Saver and then click
      <strong>
       Remove
      </strong>
      button.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-166" height="275" src="http://blog.doofix.com/removalimages/2013/12/remove-firefox-extensions.jpg" title="remove firefox extensions" width="627"/>
     </p>
     <h3>
      3. Delete all malicious registry entries of Vid-Saver virus from the system.
     </h3>
     <p>
      To open Registry Editor, please click
      <strong>
       Start
      </strong>
      , click
      <strong>
       Run
      </strong>
      , type in
      <strong>
       regedit
      </strong>
      and click OK.
     </p>
     <pre>
      HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\Main Use FormSuggest = yes
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Installer\Products\random
     </pre>
     <h3>
      4. Delete all malicious files of Vid-Savervirus from the folders.
     </h3>
     <pre>
      %UserProfile%\Start Menu\Programs\ Vid-Saver\
%AppData%[trojan name]toolbaruninstallIE.dat
%AppData%[Vid-Saver]toolbardtx.ini
     </pre>
