      <h1 class="entry-title">
       Save Money on Gutter Repairs in Newcastle with Regular Cleaning
      </h1>
      <div class="entry-meta">
       <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
        Posted on
       </span>
       <a href="http://www.whiteknightmaintenance.co.uk/blog/save-money-gutter-repairs-newcastle-regular-cleaning/" rel="bookmark" title="8:14 pm">
        <span class="entry-date">
         October 14, 2014
        </span>
       </a>
       <span class="meta-sep">
        by
       </span>
       <span class="author vcard">
        <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.whiteknightmaintenance.co.uk/blog/author/whiteknightm/" title="View all posts by White Knight Maintenance">
         White Knight Maintenance
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <!-- .entry-meta -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        Many gutter repairs in Newcastle are avoidable if homeowners are careful. Regular cleaning can save them hundreds of pounds on plumbers professional visits and the inconvenience caused by a blocked drop outlet, broken dropspout, or another part gone awry. Yet in more than a few homes in Newcastle guttering is not always in the best shape. It is in part because many individuals are unaware how to clean their gutters and when it is the right time to call a professional. It is mainly for these individuals that this article has been written. In the following paragraphs readers will find a few handy techniques to keep their gutters clean.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Frequency
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Before the reader rushes out of her chair to pull out a ladder and climb it with a broom in her hand, it is advisable for her to ask herself: when was the last time the gutters were cleaned? and then go ahead.
       </p>
       <p>
        Gutters need to be cleaned once every three months if there are trees nearby and no leaf screens are installed. The frequency can go up in autumn when trees shed their leaves. The falling leaves can build up, block drop outlets, and cause water to overflow and splash onto patios and gardens. It is not pretty to be stuck with dripping cold water in Newcastle winters. Fortunately cleaning not hard.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Techniques
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Home owners can either clean their gutters themselves or hire a
        <a href="http://www.whiteknightmaintenance.co.uk/roof_repair.html">
         guttering professional in Newcastle
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Readers willing to try their hands on gutter cleaning can start with a ladder and lean it against a solid surface. It is advisable not to lean against gutters because the weight of the ladder and the person on it can buckle and bend them.
        </li>
        <li>
         The debris can be either tossed on the ground or collected in a bucket. It is a smart idea to hook the bucket to ladder because it is less cumbersome than carrying it all the time.
        </li>
        <li>
         Gutter scoops and vacuums are safer alternatives to cleaning by bare hands. Touching wet leaves can cause allergy and the sharp bends of a gutter can lead to cuts.
        </li>
        <li>
         Water can be used to unclog downspouts. Mirror-clean gutters do not amount to much if the water in them cannot flow out through the downspout.
        </li>
        <li>
         Installation of gutter guards or leaf screens can keep gutters clean for so long that homeowners will not have to climb ladders for more than once every two or three years.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        For someone short on time, phoning a professional plumber offers an alternative. Top guttering repair services in Newcastle are open for business 24 hours a day. Although there are many guttering repair services in Newcastle, it is advisable to opt for one that no obligation inspections, free first visits, experienced (at least four years) plumbers, promises to turn up in the clients place in a day and guarantees to fix even the worst guttering problems.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Conclusion
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        A stitch in time saves nine. Regular cleaning of gutters  either oneself or at the hands of a professional plumber  can save one much trouble and expensive repairs.
       </p>
      </div>
