           <header class="entry-header">
            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Bird Nests in Flower Pots~ Little Spring Project
            </h1>
            <p class="entry-meta">
             <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-03-27T09:22:18+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
              March 27, 2011
             </time>
            </p>
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
            <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
            <div id="greet_block">
            </div>
            <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
             <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.allthingsheartandhome.com%2F2011%2F03%2Fbird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.allthingsheartandhome.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2011%2F03%2FDSC_0070-copy-391x590.jpg&amp;description=Bird%20Nests%20in%20Flower%20Pots~%20Little%20Spring%20Project" rel="nobox">
              <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Im ready for spring &amp; these little bird nests in flower pots are the perfect way to bring spring time into my space!
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/27/bird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project/dsc_0070-copy/" rel="attachment wp-att-12900">
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-12900 aligncenter" height="590" src="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0070-copy-391x590.jpg" title="Spring table decoration" width="391"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             I had 3 birds nests from last year, I bought 3 clay pots with mouths a tad bigger than my nests. Using watered-down craft paint in off-white I painted the pots to give them an aged effect. (It soaks in so I painted it twice and rubbed a bit off until it had the look I wanted,)
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/27/bird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project/dsc_0012-copy/" rel="attachment wp-att-12895">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12895" height="325" src="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0012-copy-490x325.jpg" title="Clay flower pot decoration tutorial" width="490"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             I had 3 kinds of moss leftover from my
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/15/moss-wreath-just-in-time-for-spring/" target="_blank">
              Spring Moss wreath: HERE.
             </a>
             Spanish, sheet and reindeer mossI love moss. Using the glue-gun I added some sheet moss to the edges of the pot, some Spanish moss to the nests
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/27/bird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project/dsc_0015-copy/" rel="attachment wp-att-12896">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12896" height="325" src="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0015-copy-490x325.jpg" title="y flower pot decoration tutorial" width="490"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Glue-gunned the nest into top of pot, tipping forward just a bit, and tucked in reindeer moss here there and everywhere, filling in any gaps.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Then cutting a bit of burlap and fraying it, I used Mod Podge to wrap it around the pot. Then tied a bit of twine around the whole thing
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/27/bird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project/dsc_0076-copy/" rel="attachment wp-att-12901">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12901" height="325" src="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0076-copy-490x325.jpg" title="y flower pot decoration tutorial" width="490"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/27/bird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project/dsc_0047-copy/" rel="attachment wp-att-12898">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12898" height="325" src="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0047-copy-490x325.jpg" title="Spring Table Decor Flower Pots and Birds Nests" width="490"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Just some spring-time for my table
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/2011/03/27/bird-nests-in-flower-pots-little-spring-project/dsc_0026-copy/" rel="attachment wp-att-12897">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12897" height="325" src="http://www.allthingsheartandhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0026-copy-490x325.jpg" title="Spring Table Decor-Flower Pot and Birds Nest Tutorial" width="490"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             If I had used smaller nests and pots I would have made 5 for a centerpiece. Also, little ones would be such nice party favors for your Easter party!
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             *****
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Have a delightful day my friends!
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             xo
            </p>
