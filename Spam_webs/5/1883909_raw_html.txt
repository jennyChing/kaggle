      <h2>
       Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)
      </h2>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <img alt="IaaS" class="rt" height="300" src="http://www.bksteam.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/iaas-bks.png" style="margin-left: 40px; margin-bottom: 40px; padding: 2px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" width="300"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Business Knowledge Systems creates solutions for you. We define what your immediate and long term IT needs are, we support you not just through the set-up and migration of data, but throughout the launch process. Depending on your needs, we can support your IT department long-term or be your IT department.
       </p>
       <p>
        You Might Be Ready for an IaaS If:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         If you havent moved to virtual
         <a href="http://www.bksteam.com/managed-it-services/chicago-area-cloud-computing-solutions/chicago-cloud-storage/" target="_blank" title="Cloud Storage Solutions">
          cloud storage
         </a>
         or cloud services because your business requires physical resources to run applications for day to day operations.
        </li>
        <li>
         IaaS Solution: You might find an Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) system can fill the void with pooled server and networking resources.
        </li>
        <li>
         You are afraid that the redundancy of the virtual servers that you host your web systems on are not strong enough to handle the surges in demand.
        </li>
        <li>
         IaaS Solution: An IaaS can utilize pooled resources of physical servers and on demand sociability to deal with unexpected website demands.
        </li>
        <li>
         The security of virtual servers just does not seem strong enough for some of your enterprise IT needs.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        IaaS Solution: An IaaS can support services through public and private clouds hosted virtually while benefiting from the physical security allowed for the servers hosted in the data center.
       </p>
       <p>
        If it is time for your business to consider upgrading expensive hardware, servers and other equipment to run your office infrastructure, now might be a great time to consider upgrading to a IaaS.
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <h3>
        Some Benefits an Infrastructure as a Service Can Provide Are:
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Minimize Hardware Investmen
        </strong>
        t  saving time and money up front as well as later on in supporting aging systems
        <br/>
        Scalability  immediate response to expanding capacity and no need to buy with growth in mind
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Pay For What You Need
        </strong>
         As you scale up, you pay for what you need
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Security
        </strong>
         With customizable features to ensure virtual data and physical server data is stored appropriately, you get the security your business requires
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Accessibility
        </strong>
         With internet connection and security protocol established, your business is now globally accessible
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Redundancy
        </strong>
         With precautions in place to handle failures at a server or network location, hardware resources and redundancy configurations keep your operation running.
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        Your data is your business and there are a lot of solutions out there. BKS Systems is proud to be a Green Cloud Technologies authorized partner. With their vast technologies as our backbone, we can provide the best IaaS possible for your company.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are ready to find out more or see how we might help you go virtual, give us a call today!
       </p>

         cloud based solutions
        </a>
       </p>
