   <h1>
    Top 10 Childcare Training Classes
   </h1>
   <p>
    Running a childcare classroom can be tough, add in the extra time for childcare training classes, paperwork, and general business concerns, and youve got a lot to worry about. As a provider, youre required (in most states) to take continuing education and childcare training classes. Sometimes, this involves a long commute, waiting periods, and inconvenient learning environments.
   </p>
   <h3>
    Childcare Training Classes
   </h3>
   <p>
    We created our online childcare training to be the best and most efficient solution for the continuing education and training of professional childcare providers. Below, are our Top 10 Childcare Training Classes that we suggest for providers. Take these classes and youll be at the top of the class of childcare providers:
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     1. The Developing Brain (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     2. Temper Tantrums (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     3. Helping Children Resolve Conflict (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     4. Keeping it Clean  Techniques for Sanitizing and Disinfecting (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     5. Dealing With Separation Anxiety (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     6. Beyond ABCs  Language and Literacy Basics (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     7. Stop that Bullying (3 credit hours)
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
    <strong>
     8. Biting Breakthroughs (3 credit hours)
    </strong>

    <strong>
     9. Establishing Parent Relationships (3 credit hours)
