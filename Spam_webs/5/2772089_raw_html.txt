    <div class="title">
     <h1>
      Why Istanbuls Beylikduzu district is a hit with Turkish and foreign investors
     </h1>
    </div>
    <div class="content left">
     <div class="post full">
      <div class="entry">
       <p class="date">
        Posted on 18 March 2014
       </p>
       <p>
        Demand for property in one of
        <a href="https://www.spotblue.com/turkey-real-estate/istanbul-property-for-sale">
         Istanbul
        </a>
        s most desirable districts, Beylikduzu, is expected to grow during 2014, as more investors discover what this exciting area on the citys European side has to offer.
       </p>
       <p>
        Together with the nearby Atakoy Marina district, luxury new developments in Beylikduzu are attracting an increasing number of upper-middle class Turks, as well as foreign buyers. Here are six key reasons why an investment in Beylikduzu makes sense:
        <br/>
        The district has excellent transport connections with the rest of
        <a href="https://www.spotblue.com/turkey-real-estate/istanbul-property-for-sale">
         Istanbul
        </a>
        . The metro, for example, takes you straight to
        <a href="https://www.spotblue.com/turkey-real-estate/istanbul-property-for-sale">
         Istanbul
        </a>
        centre. There is also a water taxi service offering a more scenic journey into the city, and the main
        <a href="https://www.spotblue.com/turkey-real-estate/istanbul-property-for-sale">
         Istanbul
        </a>
        airport (Ataturk International Airport) is just 20 minutes drive away.
        <br/>
        Beylikduzu boasts excellent shopping facilities, its malls including Marka City, Surmen Center, Beylicium Shopping Centre and the Beylikduzu Migros Shopping Centre.
        <br/>
        Beylikduzu has four university campuses, and many students choose to remain living in the district after they have finished their studies, so increasing the demand for rental properties.
        <br/>
        Beylikduzu is home to Turkeys largest exhibition centre, the Tuyap Exhibition Centre, which attracts visitors from all over Turkey and abroad.
        <br/>
        The district has two modern hospitals, including the Kolan Hospital and the Medicana Hospital, with both offering cutting edge care and a significant number of medical staff who find it convenient to live close to their place of work.
        <br/>
        Nearby beaches and the local marina are ideal for recreational activities, offering its inhabitants places to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.
       </p>
       <p>
        To see an example of property for sale in Beylikduzu, click here.
       </p>
