          <img alt="God Is Still By Your Side" class="attachment-post-thumb wp-post-image" src="http://i2.wp.com/iwrite4him.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/walking-away.jpg?resize=300%2C336"/>

         <p>
          Godseesthe things you arefacing, he knows what youre going through, he knows that you are hurt, youre tired andyoure almost ready to give up. God sees and he knows all of this. He understands that this storm that you are going through iswearing you out. He knows that you are feeling indecisive to whether you should go left or right, up or down, move or stand still. God knows. But what God wants you to know and be assured of is this, HE IS BY YOUR SIDE.
         </p>
         <p>
          You arenot alone, you have not been forgotten. Not only doesGod sees and knows, but he is going through it with you. You may not realize it, but he is instructing you and directing you, hes helping youand you will come out of this. Why?-because God is on your side.The wisdom of God issupreme and infinite, the Bible says There is no wisdom, no insight, no plan that can succeed against the LORD (Proverb 21:30).He knows exactly what he is doing with your life and he will not nor can not fail you. God has made you the head and not the tail, above and not beneath, the lender and not the borrower (Deuteronomy 28:12-14). He knows what he is doing with your life. You will not be defeated and you will not be destroyed, for God is your help.
         </p>
         <p>
          The children of Israel found themselves trapped, mountains on the side of them, the sea in front of them and pharaoh and his army behind them.Can you imagine what they were thinking? what they were feeling? How are we going to get out of this one? Theres no where to run, no where to turn, there was absolutely nothing that they could do in their own power. But what did God tell Moses, he said But I will gain glory for myself through Pharaoh and all his army, and the Egyptians will know that I am the LORD (Exodus 14:4). So what was God telling Moses? He was telling him I am by your side. Their situation looked liked a hopeless end, but God took something that was absolutely hopeless and was glorified through it. No one else could have delivered like God delivered. No one else could set free like God did. No one could make a way out of no waylike God did and because of this he was glorified. Moses instructed the children of Israel to Fear ye not, stand still, and see the salvation of the LORD, which he will shew to you to day: for the Egyptians whom ye have seen to day, ye shall see them again no more for ever (Exodus 14:13). God is telling you today to fear not, dont be afraid, God is with you, God is beside you and with the situation that you are going through God will be glorified and youWILL see the salvation of the Lord.
          <span class="p">
           <br/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Father God, I pray that all who readthis message will be encourage, strengthened and bless. Let them know in their spirit that they are not alone. Let them know with out a doubt that you are with them. Give them the strength and courage to keep moving, show them the path to take, show them the steps to make, by your power Guide them Lord, for you alone know the way. We wait on you. We love you and we praise you. In Jesus name I pray, amen.
         </p>
         <p>
          From my heart to yours, I ask that you please share this message. I know there is someone out there who needs to read it and please share a word of encouragement in the comment section below for other who read this post. . Thank you in advance.
