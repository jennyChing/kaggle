    <h1>
     Keepers of cotton disks and cotton swabs
    </h1>
    <p class="tags_header">
     TAGS:
    </p>
    <p class="tags_list">
     Dolls, Tilda
    </p>
    <p>
     In my opinion, it is a good idea to make a doll not just beautiful, but also useful. There are many ideas about the usage of toys in the home interior. I think, the most successful is a doll-keeper of cotton disks and cotton swabs.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <img alt="Keepers of cotton disks and cotton swabs, Tilda, Doll, Handmade, Bath angel" src="/files/2015/02/20150226/IMG_1311 .jpg" style="height:933px; width:700px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:justify">
     <img alt="Keepers of cotton disks and cotton swabs, Tilda, Doll, Handmade, Bath angel" src="/files/2015/02/20150226/IMG_1313 .jpg" style="height:1224px; width:700px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:justify">
     In fact, for me, it has always been difficult to solve the problem of storing. Especially cotton disks! They are objects of daily use. I dont want to hide them somewhere because I want to have them always available.
     <br/>
     When I saw a similar doll-keeper for the first time, I became enthusiastic about the idea! In fact, it is very a comfortable and functional option. Skirt, holding cotton disks is removable. It can be washed and ironed. The tube with cotton dicks is attached to the doll's body by buttons, and it also gives an opportunity for replacement.
     <br/>
     Decorating of jars with cotton swabs is limited only by your imagination.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <img alt="Keepers of cotton disks and cotton swabs, Tilda, Doll, Handmade, Bath angel" src="/files/2015/02/20150226/IMG_1519.JPG" style="height:864px; width:700px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:justify">
     All the keepers I made live in someone's bathrooms and delight their owners.
    </p>
