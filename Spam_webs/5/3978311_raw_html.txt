     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="In_organizations_and_companies">
       In organizations and companies
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sherpa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In organizations and companies">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <ul>
      <li>
       <a href="/wiki/Sherpa_(association)" title="Sherpa (association)">
        Sherpa (association)
       </a>
       , a French network of jurists dedicated to promoting corporate social responsibility
      </li>
      <li>
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sherpa_(khane-pani)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sherpa (khane-pani) (page does not exist)">
        Sherpa (khane-pani)
       </a>
       , a pure drinking water supplier in
       <a href="/wiki/Nepal" title="Nepal">
        Nepal
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/wiki/SHERPA_(organisation)" title="SHERPA (organisation)">
        SHERPA (organisation)
       </a>
       , a UK-based project team with expertise in open access and repositories
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/wiki/Cloud_Sherpas" title="Cloud Sherpas">
        Cloud Sherpas
       </a>
       , a company doing cloud implementation and migration
      </li>
      <li>
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sherpaa&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sherpaa (page does not exist)">
        Sherpaa
       </a>
       , a employer-provided solution that connects employees directly with doctors and insurance guides to diagnose, treat, and resolve healthcare needs.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="In_transportation">
       In transportation
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sherpa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: In transportation">
        edit

       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <ul>
      <li>
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sherpa_Aircraft_Manufacturing,_Inc.&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sherpa Aircraft Manufacturing, Inc. (page does not exist)">
        Sherpa Aircraft Manufacturing, Inc.
       </a>
       , manufacturer of
       <a href="/wiki/STOL" title="STOL">
        STOL
       </a>
       aircraft (K300 and K650T) based in Scappoose, Oregon
      </li>
      <li>
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leyland_Sherpa" title="Leyland Sherpa">
        Leyland Sherpa
       </a>
       , a light commercial vehicle produced from the 1970s until 2006
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/wiki/Short_C-23_Sherpa" title="Short C-23 Sherpa">
        Short C-23 Sherpa
       </a>
       , a military variant of the Short330 cargo aircraft, first flight was in 1982
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/wiki/Short_SB.4_Sherpa" title="Short SB.4 Sherpa">
        Short SB.4 Sherpa
       </a>
       , an experimental British aircraft whose first flight was in 1955
      </li>
      <li>
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Super_Sherpa" title="Super Sherpa">
        Super Sherpa
       </a>
       , a 250cc dual-purpose motorcycle produced by Kawasaki
      </li>
      <li>
       A series of military trucks produced by Renault Trucks Defense, including the
       <a href="/wiki/Renault_Sherpa_2" title="Renault Sherpa 2">
        Renault Sherpa2
       </a>
      </li>
