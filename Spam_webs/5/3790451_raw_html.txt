         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span class="x-dropcap">
           R
          </span>
          unning late for our flight, we decided to take a risk by squeezing in a visit to
          <a href="http://www.art-untitled.com/" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #666699;">
            UNTITLED
           </span>
          </a>
          . We heard nothing but good things about UNTITLED and knew that we couldnt miss it for the worldeven it meant missing our flight. Well be frank, UNTITLED was a minimalists wet dream. While there were many pieces that were beautifully complicated, we delighted in the predominately clean aesthetic. One of the highlights included seeing Jen Starks infamous Inside, Out up close, Phillip K. Smith IIIs Bent Parallel, the Masters Thesis Project (For Sale, Not Sale) and a sleek exhibition by the
          <a href="http://www.bronxmuseum.org/" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #666699;">
            Bronx Museum of the Arts
           </span>
          </a>
