             <p>
              The Global Education Fair Future Scope from May 29 2015
              <br/>
              The full name of HITEX is Hyderabad International Trade Exposition. The HITEX (Hyderabad International Trade Exposition) is organizing and hosting the 2nd edition of the Global Education Fair Future Scope 2015, a platform to a successful career. Future Scope Education Fair will guarantee that huge crowd of candidates in the county. This will be a 3 day event starting from 29th May 2015 to 31st May 2015 at the HITEX presentation Center, Hyderbad.
              <br/>
              As we know the HITEX (Hyderabad International Trade Exposition) is a product of public and private partnership with State Government and Larsen &amp; Toubro Limited.HITEX (Hyderabad International Trade Exposition) has been hosting and organizing Industrial trade fairs on different Subject which has Socio-Economic relevance to our Country.
