         The craze and passion of online games has been hitting the minds of people since last 2 decades. Ever since these games came into existence, they are being liked and played by people of almost all the generations. It is the most common thing to do in the part time and also the best source of entertainment.

        <h2>
         Top Upcoming Video Games in 2015
        </h2>
        <p>
         Every year, new games are launched with a totally new concept that never fails to impress the users. 2015 is already here and the launch of the new games is here too. Some of the games that would be launched in the year are mentioned below:
        </p>
        <h3>
         Dying Light-The Last of Us Easter Eggs
        </h3>
        <p>
         It is the next zombie-infested game from Techland, the developer that brought the Dead Islandfranchise to the world. Dying Light
         <a href="http://heavy.com/tag/dying-light/">
          <em>
          </em>
         </a>
         is different from
         <em>
          Dead Island
         </em>
         in several respects. Sure, theyre both first-person, open-world zombie games with a heavy focus on melee combat, butDying Lightfeatures free running.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/7NHCOCHRZrE" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Evolve
        </h3>
        <p>
         From the people who made the amazing co-op game
         <em>
          Left 4 Dead
         </em>
         comes
         <em>
          Evolve,
         </em>
         a five-player, four-versus-one shooting game. Basically, a team of four hunters comprised of different classes like trappers and medics take on a fifth player who controls a giant monster. It is going to be super fun for all the game lovers.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/-exYi8iwHjc" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <h3>
         The Order: 1886
        </h3>
        <p>
         Whats most alluring about
         <em>
          The Order: 1886
         </em>
         is its unusual premise: Its 1886 (obviously) in London, and you play as a knight in an order of protectors sworn to keep the world safe from monsters half man and half animal.With an intriguing plot, setting, cast of characters and game play,
         <em>
          The
         </em>
         Orderis shaping up to be one interesting game.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/USBc1R6MH3E" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Battlefield Hardline
        </h3>
        <p>
         The
         <em>
          Battlefield
         </em>
         series is doing something new to get the upper hand in its eternal battle for dominance over
         <em>
          Call of Duty
         </em>
         . It takes the fight from ravaged and desolated war zones to the streets of Los Angeles and Miami in cops-versus-robbers shootouts. Its definitely a new direction for the series, but one that might pay off big.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/GEg5TkcaKjc" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Bloodborne
        </h3>
        <p>
         From Software, the developers of the infamous
         <em>
          Dark Souls
         </em>
         series, is bringing PlayStation 4 owners
         <em>
          Bloodborne
         </em>
         in 2015. Like the franchise it mimics,
         <em>
          Bloodborne
         </em>
         is a third-person fantasy RPG full of dark and gritty undertones.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ONycX_qF8DI" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
