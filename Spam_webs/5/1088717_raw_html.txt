                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     What You See Depends On Where You Are In The World
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4592493406501302486" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <a href="http://slashninja.com/what-you-see-depends-on-where-you-are-in-the-world/" onblur="try {parent.deselectBloggerImageGracefully();} catch(e) {}">
                      <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5356224199441217778" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_jWNhyMUpjSE/SlUhh-kLePI/AAAAAAAAECw/-HhHj8FHrbU/s400/pic.jpg" style="margin: 0px auto 10px; display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 400px; height: 268px;"/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     So what did you see ?
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     What is above the womans head? When scientists showed a similar sketch to people from East Africa, nearly all the participants in the experiment said she was balancing a box or metal can on her head.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     In a culture containing few angular visual cues, the family is seen sitting under a tree. Westerners, on the other hand, are accustomed to the corners and boxlike shapes of architecture. They are more likely to place the family indoors and to interpret the rectangle above the womans head as a window through which shrubbery can be seen.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Reality is merely an illusion, albeit a very persistent one.
                     <br/>
                     -Albert Einstein
