           <div class="row sqs-row">
            <div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6">
             <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_3_1416439065862_90634">
              <div class="sqs-block-content">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 What:
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Brownies
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 1 2/3 cups cake flour
                </li>
                <li>
                 1.5 cups dark brown sugar
                </li>
                <li>
                 1 cup cocoa powder
                </li>
                <li>
                 4 large eggs
                </li>
                <li>
                 1 cup buttermilk
                </li>
                <li>
                 1 t baking powder
                </li>
                <li>
                 1/2 t salt
                </li>
                <li>
                 1.5 lb fresh black cherries, pitted and halved
                </li>
                <li>
                 1/2 cup melted butter, cooled
                </li>
                <li>
                 1/4 cup bacon fat rendered from bacon (below)
                </li>
                <li>
                 12 oz semi-sweet chocolatemorsels
                </li>
                <li>
                 1 t vanilla
                </li>
                <li>
                 10 oz raw pork bacon
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="sqs-block horizontalrule-block sqs-block-horizontalrule" data-block-type="47" id="block-yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_13548">
              <div class="sqs-block-content">
               <hr/>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_13675">
              <div class="sqs-block-content">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 For the topping:
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 8 oz dark chocolate (60-70%)
                </li>
                <li>
                 1/4cup heavy cream
                </li>
                <li>
                 2 T butter
                </li>
                <li>
                 1 cup cherries from above
                </li>
                <li>
                 1/2 cup crumbled bacon
                </li>
                <li>
                 1 t salt (optional)
                </li>
               </ul>
              </div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6">
             <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_3_1416439065862_125636">
              <div class="sqs-block-content">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 How:
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ol>
                <li>
                 Heat oven to 350 degrees
                </li>
                <li>
                 Cook bacon for topping according to instructions, reserve bacon fat and set aside.
                </li>
                <li>
                 In a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat eggs and brown sugar until creamy, about two minutes.
                </li>
                <li>
                 In a medium bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt and cocoa powder and add half of this mixture to the egg mixture.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Add bacon fat, butter, buttermilk and vanilla and mix to combine.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Add the remaining dry mix and beat for one minute.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Fold in cherries and chocolate morsels
                </li>
                <li>
                 Transfer to a prepared 9x13 pan and bake for about 40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Allow to cool for an hour before adding the topping.
                </li>
               </ol>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="sqs-block horizontalrule-block sqs-block-horizontalrule" data-block-type="47" id="block-yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_14771">
              <div class="sqs-block-content">
               <hr/>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_14897">
              <div class="sqs-block-content">
               <p id="yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_11669">
                <br/>
               </p>
               <ol id="yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_11664">
                <li id="yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_11736">
                 Bring heavy cream and butter to a boil in a saucepan over medium heat.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Remove from heat and whisk in dark chocolate until completely melted.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Pour over cooled cake, topping with cherries and bacon pieces.
                </li>
                <li>
                 I sprinkled some salt on top to make the sweet/salty effect a bit more pronounced.
                 <span data-cke-bookmark="1" id="cke_bm_270E" style="display: none;">
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p id="yui_3_17_2_17_1418938906177_11674">
