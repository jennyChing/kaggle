     <h1>
      In which i shot myself in the footone conversation at a time
     </h1>
     <div class="wwsgd" style="display:none;">
      <p style="border:thin dotted black; padding:3mm;">
       If you're new here or you have previously subscribed by Google Friend Connect, you may want to subscribe to my
       <a href="http://allfookedup.com/feed/">
        RSS feed
       </a>
       or by email (look to the right). Thanks for visiting!
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      <a href="http://allfookedup.com/in-which-i-shot-myself-in-the-foot-one-conversation-at-a-time/gunpoint/" rel="attachment wp-att-3553">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3553" height="300" src="http://allfookedup.com/wpcontent/uploads/gunpoint.jpg" title="gunpoint" width="400"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      I see that EVERYONE has written their BlogHer recap and gone on and on about all the AWESOMENESS. I have decided to take a different approach and talk about the MANY times that I shot myself in the foot with my unique conversational approaches. Poor
      <a href=" http://www.bywordofmouthmusings.com/p/about-me_06.html" target="_blank" title="By word of mouth musings">
       Nicole, my BlogHer buddy and good friend
      </a>
      , spent much time trying to keep my mouth shut to no avail. Hope you enjoy them.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      So, I was outside on a bench talking to a woman about a blog redesign:
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Her:  Have you ever heard of so and so? (Gives me the name of a company)
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me:         Oh yes. They dont know their ass from a hole in the ground. Why?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Her:  Theyre my partners.
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me:  Hahahaha
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Nicole: (shaking head sadly again at my mouth)
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      This one happened when I sat down at a table full of people I didnt know!
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: So your website is Christy Comes? Wow, what do you blog about? Sex?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Christy: No, my name is Christy Comes pronounced co-mays.
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: Really?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Christy: Yes, thats NOT MY WEBSITE, its my NAME!
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me:  oh, well this is awkward!
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      This one happened when I met a producer from ABC in New York:
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: So you work for ABC? Excellent. Would you like to hire my daughter?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Teri: We have internships.
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: No, she will actually be graduating and will need a real job.
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Teri: We have a hiring freeze.
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: Thats ok! She wont graduate until May. Im sure the timing will be perfect for both of you. Shall I consider it a done deal?
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      And during PathFinders day when I was asked what I thought of the session:
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Person: Sowhat did you think of that session?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: I thought it was incredibly boring
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Person: Really?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: Yeah
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Person: Well, I work for BlogHer, would you elaborate on that?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: Oh shitI did it again!
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
     </p>
     <p>
      And a conversation regarding a session:
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Nicole: Should we go to the panel on Blogging your way to self-esteem?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: No, Im already swimming in self-esteem.
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Nicole: What?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: Yeah, Andie already says I have too much. I cant afford anymore!
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Nicole: So, what should we do?
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      Me: Lets go to the pool and drink!
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      Also, an excellent text that i sent to my partner in crime, Nicole Morgan, who I was supposed to meet at 8:00 for breakfast and a welcome to BlogHer speech.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      <strong>
       Aug 5, 2011 7:03
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
      <strong>
       So its another welcome after breakfast and I already feel welcome so Ill show up around 10 or so
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
     </p>
     <p>
      So, there you are! That was basically my BlogHer conference; me saying stuff and then having to clean up the mess. Hope you enjoyed it!
     </p>
