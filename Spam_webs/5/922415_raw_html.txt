         <a href="/fitness-center-boston-ma/lose-weight-by-speeding-up-your-metabolism-braintree-ma" title="Lose Weight by Speeding up Your Metabolism - Braintree, MA">
          Lose Weight by Speeding up Your Metabolism - Braintree, MA
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-details">
         Joseph Coupal - Friday, June 26, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <p itemprop="description">
          <img alt="" src="/images/blogs/weight-train-dp.JPG" style="border: 0px solid; width: 356px; height: 259px; float: right; margin-left: 10px;"/>
          Have you noticed that some people are just skinny? And that is seems some people can eat whatever they want and stay thin? It is all about the metabolism and how fast it works.
         </p>
         <p>
          Genetics do come into play sometimes; some people do burn fat faster than others. But,
          <strong>
           age, weight, diet, and exercise can also have an effect on how fast your metabolism works
          </strong>
          . Unfortunately, as you age your metabolism slows down. This is because you lose 5-6 pounds of muscle every 10 years once you hit your 20s. The less muscle you have the slower your metabolism will work. This means that as you age, you burn fewer calories. But, you can
          <strong>
           speed up your metabolism with
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">
           weight training
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          You can also lose weight by cutting out extra calories. Of course you can cut calories, especially extra ones. But if you really want to lose weight, you should
          <strong>
           start working out with weights
          </strong>
          . Weight training is the best way to speed up your resting metabolic rate. This means that as you build muscle you will lose weight even at rest.
         </p>
         <p>
          Even though your metabolism slows as you age, you can speed it up again by weight training. A pound of muscle burns up to nine times the calories a pound of fat burns.
         </p>
         <p>
          For more information on
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">
           weight loss through strength training
          </a>
          , contact
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/contact.htm">
           Fit Factory in Braintree
          </a>
          .
         </p>

          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">

          </a>
          ? Which you should do first and does it matter? It actually does matter to your overall fitness.
         </p>
         <p>
          A study determined that there was no difference in overall fitness regardless of if you do your strength training or your cardio first when working out.
         </p>

          <strong>
           alternating strength training days with cardio training days
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          When performing a relatively moderate training volume and frequency the training order is up to personal preference.
         </p>
         <p>
          However, within each training session you may find some impact from the exercise order that you choose. A good cardio workout could affect leg muscle strength. And a hard lower body resistance session could have an effect your cardio workout that day. But the end result over time should be the same.
         </p>
         <p>
          However, sufficient rest is needed between sessions that involve both cardio and resistance training to ensure that gains are made.

        <h2 class="post-title">
         <a href="/fitness-center-boston-ma/zumba-is-the-fun-way-to-exercise-and-lose-weight-braintree-ma" title="Zumba is the Fun Way to Exercise and Lose Weight - Braintree, MA">
          Zumba is the Fun Way to Exercise and Lose Weight - Braintree, MA
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-details">
         Joseph Coupal - Friday, May 29, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <p itemprop="description">
          <img alt="" src="/images/blogs/beach.jpg" style="border: 0px solid; float: right; margin-left: 10px;"/>
          Are you looking for a
          <strong>
           fun and easy way to lose weight
          </strong>
          and get that beach body? Dont try fad diets or exercises that dont get you excited to head to the gym. Exercising is more fun when you exercise by doing
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/group-fit.htm">
           Zumba
          </a>
          .
          <strong>
           Zumba
          </strong>
          is an cario exercise class that combines Latin music and easy for dance steps that anyone can do, no matter how old or how uncoordinated. In Fact, Zumba feels more like a dance party than a way to lose weight and get in-shape.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Zumba exercise routines
          </strong>
          <strong>
           are actually a very fun way to do interval training
          </strong>
          , which is the best way to get fit. Interval training is highly effective because it switches between high and low intensity. Zumba switches between fast and slow rhythm. But Zumba is
          <strong>
           resistance training
          </strong>

        <h2 class="post-title">
         <a href="/fitness-center-boston-ma/weight-loss-and-exercise-motivation-tips-braintree-ma" title="Weight Loss and Exercise Motivation Tips  Braintree, MA">
          Weight Loss and Exercise Motivation Tips  Braintree, MA
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-details">
         Joseph Coupal - Thursday, May 21, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <p itemprop="description">
          <img alt="" height="215" src="/images/blogs/weight-loss-dp.JPG" style="border: 0px solid; float: right; margin-left: 10px;" width="323"/>
          It is not easy to fit exercise into your routine, especially if you are new to exercise. In fact, choosing to exercise can be a tough decision to make after a busy day or a long week. Here are some
          <strong>
           motivational tricks to help get you motivated.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Make the idea of exercise sweeter.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          You don t need willpower, you need 'want power'. To want to exercise, sugarcoat it. For some the incentive is to raise money for a cause. By training for a 5K charity race to support a foundation, exercising is easier.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Get competitive when you sweat.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Make
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training-team.htm">
           weight loss
          </a>
          a team sport. A study from Brown University suggested that weight loss can be contagious and that being part of a team of people who want to lose weight can help you drop extra pounds. Accountability, competition, and encouragement combined help to keep you motivated. There's good evidence that when you raise the stakes, it helps get your spirit up to actually attain your goal.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           One step at a time.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          If your long-range goal makes isnt giving you the results you want fast enough, change your goal. People think they have to get from A to Z, but you really only need to get from A to B, and then from B to C.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Make exercise social.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Getting more payoff from your workout boosts your desire to exercise. When people can make a single activity fulfill many goals, they're more likely to stick with it. One of the best ways to do that is to build your friends into your workouts. Make exercise dates.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           View exercise as a way to get confidence.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Conquering a
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/crossfit-factory.htm">
           CrossFit class
          </a>
          or a 10K race quiets your inner wimp and helps you feel like you can do anything.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Picture your future self.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Are you going to be fit, or are you going to be heavy, unhealthy, and wheezing to get up a flight of stairs? Imagine the benefits of exercise in a couple months and in 20 years; that vision can be very motivating.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Pick a workout for your mind and body.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The exercise routine that burns the most calories or gives you the tightest abs might not be your best choice if it isnt fun or if you dont enjoy it. Finding the right activity for you emotionally helps you get what you need out of exercise. A hip-hop class or a full-tilt run may be just the right speed for that mental sweet spot; other times, you may want something more contemplative like yoga.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Adapt.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Your favorite
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/group-fit-team.htm">
           group exercise
          </a>
          instructor moves. Try Pilates instead. If you get injured running, suit up for a swim. Roll with change. Toss out all-or-nothing thinking. Setbacks really can be opportunities.
         </p>
         <p>
          For more information and exercise motivation tips,
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/contact.htm">
           contact Fit Factory in Braintree
          </a>
          .
         </p>

          Weight Loss through Weight Lifting  Braintree, MA
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-details">
         Joseph Coupal - Thursday, April 30, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <p itemprop="description">
          <img alt="" src="/images/blogs/weight-train-dp.JPG" style="border: 0px solid; width: 339px; height: 246px; float: right; margin-left: 10px;"/>
          Most people who
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">
           want to lose weight
          </a>
          think of doing cardio workouts. When they think of body building they think of weight lifting or strength training.
         </p>
         <p>
          Unfortunately, this is not entirely accurate and the misconceptions make it difficult for people to reach their potential results. In order to lose weight, you need to understand
          <strong>
           how exercise relates to weight loss.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Weight lifting is very effective for losing weight
          </strong>
          . Weight lifting increases your metabolism both immediately and long term. After you do a strength training workout, your metabolic rate increases. However, weight lifting also increases lean muscle in your body. This causes you metabolism rate to increase permanently as well. Muscle burns more calories than fat, even at rest.
         </p>
         <p>
          With cardio exercise, your metabolic rate will absolutely increase while you are exercising. But, once you are done, it will once again go back to normal. This means you dont lose more calories after your workout is over like you will with strength training. Cardio health is very important and in fact it is imperative for overall health.
          <strong>
           Weight training is necessary for weight loss.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Cardio exercise will definitely help you lose weight. Weight lifting will help you lose body fat. This will change your body composition, giving you a more healthy and lean appearance. When you lose weight through only cardio exercise, you wont achieve that look you want. You wont look different. You may look smaller, but you wont look fit. This is because you will still have the same proportion of muscle to body fat.
         </p>
         <p>
          With
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/personal-training.htm">
           weight training for weight loss
          </a>
          you will replace the fat with muscle. This makes you look healthy and fit. Weight lifting for weight loss is a great way to lose weight and will give you better, more visible results.
         </p>
         <p>
          For more information,
          <a href="http://www.fitfactoryma.com/contact.htm">
           contact Fit Factory in Braintree, MA
          </a>
          .
