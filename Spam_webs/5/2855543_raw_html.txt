   <h1 class="heading heading--beta heading--uppercase type--light text--center">
    The 10 Questions You Should Ask On A First Date
   </h1>
   <ol class="keywords">
    <li>
     <a href="http://www.thegentlemansjournal.com/lifestyle/gentlemans-etiquette/">
      Gentleman's Etiquette
     </a>
    </li>
   </ol>
   <h5 class="heading heading--zeta heading--uppercase type--light text--center type--italic push--bottom">
    <span class="type--lowercase">
     by
    </span>
    <a href="http://www.thegentlemansjournal.com/author/holly/">
     Holly Macnaghten
    </a>
    / 25th April 2014
   </h5>
   <div class="grid flush--bottom">
    <div class="grid__item one-whole">
     <div class="ad--block" style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
      <script type="text/javascript">
       &lt;!--// &lt;![CDATA[
    OA_show(14);
// ]]&gt; --&gt;
      </script>
      <noscript>
       <a href="http://ads.roccamedia.com/www/delivery/ck.php?n=4c8943b" target="_blank">
        <img alt="" border="0" src="http://ads.roccamedia.com/www/delivery/avw.php?zoneid=14&amp;n=4c8943b"/>
       </a>
      </noscript>
     </div>
     <article class="article">
      <div class="old__content">
       <!-- @@3.12.7181 -->
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <a href="http://www.thegentlemansjournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/First-Date-Questions-TGJ.01.png">
         <img alt="First Date Questions - TGJ.01" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20417" height="475" src="http://www.thegentlemansjournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/First-Date-Questions-TGJ.01.png" width="660"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        There are no two ways about it, first dates can be beyond awkward. Nerves can get the better of even the most confidant of gentlemen, causing you to loose control of your limbs, trip up stairs, knock over glasses, or even break out in a cold sweat over which fork to use. Similarly, such stressful situations can cause eventhe most eloquent of man to suddenly come down with a case of severe verbal-diarrhoea or worse still, make them loose all ability to form a simple sentence. What questions should a man ask a woman on a first date? The most important thing to remember that it is a date, not an interview. Women do not want want to be bombarded with every question under the sun, but nor do they want to sit in silence making idol chit chat about the weather or the trials of tube travel. There is, (as with most things in life), a fine line betweenwhat is and is not ok to ask on a date. Yes, you want to find out certain things about each other, but ideally you want to find these things out naturally through a flowing conversation, not a routine 21 questions that has more in common with a police interrogation than it does a scene from a Katherine Heigl movie. So gentlemen, take some advice from
        <em>
         <span style="color: #222222;">
          The Correspondents
         </span>
        </em>
        <span style="color: #222222;">
         Mr Bruce
        </span>
        <span style="color: #222222;">
         and
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <em>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Skip the how ya dos, what ya dos, political views, youve heard the news?
         </span>
        </em>
        <br style="color: #000000;"/>
        <em>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          The oh my god its different here you have to tip with every beer
         </span>
        </em>
        <span style="color: #000000;">
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <span style="color: #000000;">
         and take these 10 questions with you in your mind, (or on the back of napkin if you suffer from short term memory loss in stressful situations), and throw a few of these in there instead when the time is right. It will make for a far more interesting and informative first date than the usual monotony we should all try and avoid from now on
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        1.
        <em>
         Where is the strangest place youve ever been?
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        2.
        <em>
         What do you do to unwind on weekends?
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        3.
        <i>
         Whats the first thing youd do if you won the lottery?
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        4.
        <em>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Whats the most spontaneous thing youve ever done?
         </span>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        5.
        <em>
         What is your favourite way to spend a Saturday?
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        6.
        <em>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Whats the luckiest thing thats ever happened to you?
         </span>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        7.
        <em>
         Whats your favourite thing to do in London?
        </em>
        (insert currentcity you are actually in)
       </p>
       <p>
        8.
        <em>
         Whats your favourite place in the world?
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        9.
        <em>
         Whats the naughtiest thing youve ever done?
        </em>
        (in a non sexual way gents, lets keep this clean please)
       </p>
       <p>
        10.
        <em>
         What never fails to make you laugh?
        </em>
       </p>
