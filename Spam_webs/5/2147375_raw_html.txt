             <h2>
              Welcome Bags for an Indian wedding
             </h2>
             <p>
              Welcome bags have become a must have for Indian weddings. The bags are perfect opportunity to let your personalities shine and share the things you love, along with providing some basic needs for your guests. You might have had to give in with the caterer or pare down on the floral arrangements, but you can have your way with the welcome bags! Here are some ideas and inspiration to help you create the perfect welcome bag for your guests.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="gap" style="line-height: 10px; height: 10px;">
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>

           <div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
            <div class="wpb_wrapper">
             <p>
              <strong>
               Must have items:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               2 Water bottles
              </li>
              <li>
               Agenda for the weekend complete with timeline, addresses and any important contact information
              </li>
              <li>
               2 Snacks
              </li>
              <li>
               Welcome/Thank you note: Let your guests know that you appreciate them taking the trip to come for your wedding
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="vc_col-sm-6 wpb_column column_container ">
          <div class="wpb_wrapper">
           <div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
            <div class="wpb_wrapper">
             <p>
              <strong>
               Optional items and ideas:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Mints/gum
              </li>
              <li>
               Wedding emergency kit: bobby pins, safety pins, advil, tissues
              </li>
              <li>
               Granola bars
              </li>
              <li>
               Chocolate or candy
              </li>
              <li>
               Alcohol nips
              </li>
              <li>
               Chap Stick/Lip Balm
              </li>
              <li>
               Hand Sanitizer/Wet Ones/Wet Naps
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>

               Local attractions brochures (grab these from your visitors bureau)
