             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
              </center>
              <br/>
              <em>
               Angry Birds
              </em>
              creator Rovio Entertainment turned the late Queen frontman Freddie Mercury into an honorary character on Monday, just in time for the rocker's Sept. 5 birthday.
              <p>
               The
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/angry-birds/">
                <em>
                 Angry Birds
                </em>
               </a>
               flock is taking a break from flying and toppling enemies to ride Mercury's bicycle  the animated video below is set to Queen's "Bicycle Race." Absent from the clip is
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2012/08/09/pink-bird-angry-birds-seasons/">
                Pink Bird
               </a>
               , the bubbly character Rovio introduced in August.
              </p>
              <p>
               Rovio's gesture supports the annual
               <a href="http://www.freddieforaday.com/" target="_blank">
                Freddie for a Day
               </a>
               campaign, which celebrates the singer and raises funds for HIV/AIDS charity Mercury Phoenix Trust.
              </p>
              <p>
               Mercury, who died in 1991 from complications stemming from AIDS, would have been 66 on Wednesday.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Et3PYo_CW-w?enablejsapi=1&amp;rel=0" width="640">
               </iframe>
              </center>
              <p>
               "'Freddie for a Day is such a great way to honor Freddie's fun and flamboyant spirit while delivering an important message," Rovio's CMO Peter Vesterbacka said in a statement. "Queen's music has endured for a reason, because its creativity and contagious fun appeals to fans of all ages, and we're happy to help keep Freddie's memory and music alive for new generations of fans."
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <strong>
                SEE ALSO:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2012/03/30/physics-angry-birds-space/">
                 Angry Birds Space  How the Physics Works
                </a>
               </strong>
              </center>
              <p>
               People can join the cause Wednesday by purchasing a Freddie Mercury Angry Bird T-shirt online at the
               <a href="http://shop.angrybirds.com/eu/" target="_blank">
                Angry Birds
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://www.shop.bravadousa.com/" target="_blank">
                Bravado
               </a>
               stores. Meanwhile in London, bandmates Brian May and Roger Taylor gathered with friends Monday for a Freddie for a Day fundraiser.
              </p>
              <p>
               Last year, Google commemorated Mercury's birthday with
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2011/09/04/freddie-mercury-google-doodle/">
                this memorable animated doodle
               </a>
               :
              </p>

              </p>

              </center>
