        <p>
         While I seriously enjoya good hamburger or
         <a href="http://www.somedayilllearn.com/bell-pepper-sandwiches/">
          salami sandwich
         </a>
         , I wasnt always a big meat lover. I lived a
         <a href="http://www.somedayilllearn.com/why-i-was-a-vegetarian/">
          strict vegetarian lifestyle
         </a>
         for 13 years. During thattime I built up an intensive repertoire of favorite meatless meals. This pregnancy Ive developed a pretty severeaversion to certain meats, running Nate out of the house in search of his usual chicken and fish dishes.
         <em>
          Beware the pregnant spidey sense of smell!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Meatless meals that won't leave you hungry" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18563" height="850" src="http://www.somedayilllearn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Meatless-meals-that-wont-leave-you-hungry.png" width="567"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its fortunate that Ihave so manymeatless meals to fall back on. Those years of experience havehelped me prepare hearty food and keepthe whole family satisfied for the last few months.
         <span id="more-18562">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Meatless Meals for Everyone
        </h2>
        <p>
         Pregnancy has me perpetually hungry, so its a big priority that mymeatless meals feature dishes that stick with me. I also have a hard-working husband and two rambunctious boys with crazy appetites. Hearty is definitely the word of the day! I love to use ancient grains like quinoa, spelt and chia for easy-to-digest options that provide tons of energy. Protein-packed black beans, chickpeas and leafy vegetables havethe nutrients we need in a balanced diet. Weve been working withMorningstar Farms and Ive found that its really convenientto have their pre-packaged options on-hand. Chop it up into a quick lettuce wrap or add a little more substance to a frittata.
         <em>
          Just because I start with a veggie burger doesnt mean Im making a veggie burger!
         </em>
         I love that they have items that are friendly for just about every dietary need, such as their non-GMO and vegan
         <a href="https://www.morningstarfarms.com/products/burgers/roasted-garlic-and-quinoa-burger" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Roasted Garlic and Quinoa Burger
         </a>
         .
        </p>
