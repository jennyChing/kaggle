                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-nnWsZ1gES3I/VA_1nc2B7eI/AAAAAAAADps/rBRqQXZDiDw/s1600/Picture%2B6.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-nnWsZ1gES3I/VA_1nc2B7eI/AAAAAAAADps/rBRqQXZDiDw/s1600/Picture%2B6.png" width="640"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         If I dont have time to put a lot of makeup on, Ill just swipe on some mascara and apply some under-eye concealer. This one by Benefit is great, since it has a hydrating ring around it, so it wont sink into fine lines and make you look 10 years older. Its not a full-coverage concealer, but it has a nice brightening effect that makes you look way more awake than you actually are in that 8am class.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="text-decoration: underline; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Save
                        </span>
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         : Maybelline Dream Lumi Touch Highlighting Concealer in Ivory ($8)
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ZeuOAYJqPk4/VA_1nnjuXdI/AAAAAAAADpg/j9vasCMjxLI/s1600/Picture%2B7.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ZeuOAYJqPk4/VA_1nnjuXdI/AAAAAAAADpg/j9vasCMjxLI/s1600/Picture%2B7.JPG" width="320"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         This is a great drugstore brightener, since its a liquid, meaning it wont get cakey under your eyes. As a fair-skinned gal, I use the Ivory shade, since the yellow undertones help negate any blue undereye circles Im sporting that morning. Definitely a must-have if you dont get enough sleep and youre on a budget.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="font-weight: bold; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Brow stuff: because youre not complete without your brows, and if you dont have to fill them in, at least put them in their place
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="text-decoration: underline; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Splurge
                        </span>
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         : Chella Eyebrow Defining Gel ($18 for .12 fl oz)
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XvnJTdEdMB4/VA_1oPLGfsI/AAAAAAAADpk/VX5mlAsMXxA/s1600/Picture%2B8.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XvnJTdEdMB4/VA_1oPLGfsI/AAAAAAAADpk/VX5mlAsMXxA/s1600/Picture%2B8.JPG" width="640"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         I like to think that your look is not complete unless your eyebrows are done. Call me crazy, but eyebrows need dressing just like our bodies, and people tend to neglect them. If I dont have time to fill them in with a colored powder, at the very least I like to tame them with some brow gel. This one from Chella works well, doesnt make your eyebrows feels crunchy, and lasts all day.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="text-decoration: underline; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Save
                        </span>
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         : ELF Essential Wet Gloss Lash &amp; Brow Clear Mascara
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-fi-97hIswUs/VA_1o0CJCrI/AAAAAAAADp0/Fo6giYf0n9Q/s1600/Picture%2B9.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-fi-97hIswUs/VA_1o0CJCrI/AAAAAAAADp0/Fo6giYf0n9Q/s1600/Picture%2B9.JPG" width="640"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         If I had a dollar and had to choose between this brow gel and a candy bar, this would win every time. Not only is it healthier for you than candy, but it also puts those brows in their place! You dont get as much in the tube as you do with other brow gels, but the clear mascara on the other side of the tube also doubles as brow gel, so its still a bargain. It feels a little bit more noticeable once it sets than the Chella, but for only a dollar, anyone can keeps their brows looking neat and clean.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="font-weight: bold; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Mascara: because even on days when I dont have time to put makeup on my face, I have time for mascara
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="text-decoration: underline; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Splurge
                        </span>
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         : Benefit Theyre Real ($23)
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7_UVvvIAOow/VA_1j6V7V9I/AAAAAAAADpY/ucbw-xtkGok/s1600/Picture%2B10.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7_UVvvIAOow/VA_1j6V7V9I/AAAAAAAADpY/ucbw-xtkGok/s1600/Picture%2B10.JPG" width="320"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Mascara takes a minute to apply at best, if you included curling your lashes, so its a must-have for me. Like many mascaras, the Benefit Theyre Real makes a lot of claims, only this one actually lives up to them! It really does look like youve applied false eyelashes or had extensions put in, because holy cow this is dramatic! If youre going out on the weekends, this is an especially good mascara to have on hand. Just be sure to have a powerful eye makeup remover waiting when you get back!
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="text-decoration: underline; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Save
                        </span>
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         : CoverGirl LashBlast Volume Mascara ($9)
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-0Lkgtae2z24/VA_1j8rcDSI/AAAAAAAADoo/zoPhSHjp23E/s1600/Picture%2B11.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="480" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-0Lkgtae2z24/VA_1j8rcDSI/AAAAAAAADoo/zoPhSHjp23E/s1600/Picture%2B11.JPG" width="640"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         As a beauty blogger, I have tried many mascaras in my time, but this is the one I always come back to. This has a large rubber-bristle brush, which lets it add a ton of volume and length to your lashes. It works for both day and night, depending on how many coats you put on, and it holds a curl like nothing else. Ive been asked if I was wearing false eyelashes with this on! I think if the drugstore beauty section has one thing done right, its the mascara, because this is my holy grail.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.1500000000000001; margin-bottom: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
                        <span style="vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
                         Whats on your back-to-school beauty checklist?
