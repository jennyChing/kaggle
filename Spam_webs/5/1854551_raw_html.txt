         <h1 class="page-title entry-title">
          Wet Dog
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           Wet Dog is a series of portraits of dogs photographed during their least favorite activity: bath time. Exposing the dogs at a vulnerable moment, photographer Sophie Gamand is able to capture their wide range of expressions. She believes dogs are more than animals and have acquired a status of persona. They mirror humans and the bond we have developed with them says a lot about our own solitude and social challenges.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sophie currently works on a book of wet dogs which will come out during the Fall 2015 (Grand Central Publishing/US, Aurum Press/UK, Livre de Poche/France, Riva/Germany). The Wet Dog series has won the Portraiture category of the
           <a href="http://worldphoto.org/about-the-sony-world-photography-awards/pro/sophie-gamand">
            Sony World Photography Awards 2014
           </a>
           , and has been published in numerous online and paper publications.
          </p>
          <p>
           Prints are available for purchase. For open edition of small formats (up to 1616 inches), visit my
           <a href="http://strikingpaws.shootproof.com/event/386761">
            American store
           </a>
           (for orders with the US) or my
           <a href="http://strikingpaws.photoshelter.com/gallery/Wet-Dog/G0000g2Ea6mnRhf4/">
            international store
           </a>
           if you are ordering from any other country. For limited large formats,
           <a href="mailto:contact@sophiegamand.com">
            contact me directly
           </a>
           .
          </p>
