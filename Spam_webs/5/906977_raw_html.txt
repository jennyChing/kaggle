       <p class="author">
        Posted by the Mass Office of Travel and Tourism
       </p>
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <div>
        <p>
         Ever notice how youre a little bit different on vacation? A little more relaxed. A little more carefree. And a little more fun.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Summer is the time for vacation, and with three months of warm weather on our doorstep, wed like to introduce you to a new way to enjoy all that Massachusetts has to offer:
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/vacationyou/" target="_blank">
         Vacation You
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        How does Vacation You work? Its simple. Just go to
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/vacationyou/" target="_blank">
         www.massvacation.com/vacationyou
        </a>
        and take a look around to see what tickles your fancy.
       </p>
       <p>
        When you arrive at the website, youll find a variety of activity and event suggestions, each one offering something a little bit different for summer travelers and adventurers.
       </p>
       <p>
        If your ideal summer day involves a whole lot of beach time, Vacation You has you covered. Click Explore in the Lounging on a Beach bubble and youll get a real-time look at some of the best Instagram beach scenes from across Massachusetts, as well as the latest Twitter buzz about Massachusetts beaches.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Lounging-on-a-Beach-Instagram.png">
         <img alt="Lounging on a Beach Instagram" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5633" height="914" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Lounging-on-a-Beach-Instagram.png" width="1898"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Once youve checked out what people are seeing and saying, click on the Fun Ideas tab and youll be able to view plenty of beach day trip ideas perfect for a summer day. At the Fun Ideas section, a
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/explore/beaches/">
         comprehensive list of Massachusetts beaches
        </a>
        will also be just one click away.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Lounging-on-a-Beach-Fun-Ideas.png">
         <img alt="Lounging on a Beach Fun Ideas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5634" height="915" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Lounging-on-a-Beach-Fun-Ideas.png" width="1900"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        If the beach isnt Vacation You, fear not! There are plenty of other ways to make the most of sunny weather, from
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/explore/arts/" target="_blank">
         getting artsy
        </a>
        to
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/explore/outdoors/" target="_blank">
         being outdoorsy
        </a>
        to
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/eat/type/dessert/" target="_blank">
         eating ice cream
        </a>
        . With seven total categories of entertaining ways to spend the day, you wont ever have to worry about any lazy days this summer (unless, of course, thats exactly what you want).
       </p>
       <p>
        Vacation You isnt just about serving up great ideas for summer fun in Massachusetts, though. Its also about giving you the chance to experience it all for free!
       </p>
       <p>
        Each month, well be giving away an awesome Massachusetts vacation to one lucky New England resident. To enter to win the trip, all you need to do is take a photo on Instagram and tag it with #vacationyou and one of the hashtags listed below.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Meet-Vacation-You.png">
         <img alt="Meet Vacation You" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5635" height="577" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Meet-Vacation-You.png" width="607"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Once youve done that, not only will you be entered for your chance to win a Massachusetts getaway, but your photo will also be pulled into the nifty Vacation You website and displayed in one of the bubbles.
       </p>
       <p>
        Along with real-time scenes and commentary from around Massachusetts, Vacation You also supplies you with a quick path to all the necessary resources for a great summer trip around the Commonwealth, including
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/stay/" target="_blank">
         where to stay
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/navigate/" target="_blank">
         how to get around
        </a>
        and much more.
       </p>
       <p>
        If youre keen on seeing even more summer scenes from around Massachusetts, keep an eye on our
        <a href="http://instagram.com/visitma" target="_blank">
         Instagram account
        </a>
        , as well be sharing spectacular #vacationyou scenes throughout the entire summer.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/vy-sunset1.jpg">
         <img alt="vy sunset" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5636" height="640" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/vy-sunset1.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <em>
         #vacationyou #sunset by Matthew Daniel in Rochester, MA
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        So, have at it! Enjoy the longer days, the summer weather and let Vacation You run free. You just might discover another side to yourself and youll definitely have a great time.
       </p>
