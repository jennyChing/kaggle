	&lt;h1 class="entry-title"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.terrislittlehaven.com/secrets-to-a-clutter-free-garden-shed/" rel="bookmark" title="Secrets to a Clutter-free Garden Shed"&gt;Secrets to a Clutter-free Garden Shed&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/h1&gt;
	&lt;div class="before-content"&gt;
		
		
				&lt;span class="clock-icon"&gt;
					&lt;time datetime="2015-06-05"&gt;June 5, 2015&lt;/time&gt;
				&lt;/span&gt;&lt;!-- .clock-icon --&gt;
			
				
		&lt;span class="author-icon"&gt;
			&lt;a href="http://www.terrislittlehaven.com/author/terris-little-haven/" title="Posts by Terri Beavers" rel="author"&gt;Terri Beavers&lt;/a&gt;		&lt;/span&gt;&lt;!-- .author-icon --&gt;

						&lt;a href="http://www.terrislittlehaven.com/secrets-to-a-clutter-free-garden-shed/#respond"&gt;No Comments&lt;/a&gt;					&lt;/span&gt;&lt;!-- .comments-icon --&gt;

				&lt;div class="content"&gt;
					&lt;a href="http://www.terrislittlehaven.com/secrets-to-a-clutter-free-garden-shed/" title="Secrets to a Clutter-free Garden Shed"&gt;&lt;img width="3264" height="2448" src="http://www.terrislittlehaven.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/1378244_22045377.jpg" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" alt="Secrets to a Clutter-free Garden Shed" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p&gt;Just like any other part of your home, organizing youroutdoor storage deck&lt;strong&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;is also an important task that you need to do every one in a while. A clean shed means that you dont have to waste your time and energy in searching for that trowel or moving that heavy wheelbarrow.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Keeping your shed clean may seem like a difficult task for most people. Yes, the job requires some effort, but its not impossible and expensive. It can also be a really fun experience, especially if you are tired of looking at messy gloves and gardening tools. Follow the simple guidelines below if you want to keep your&lt;a href="http://www.tesco.com/direct/garden/garden-storage/cat3375571.cat"&gt;compact garden shed from Tescoclean and organized&lt;strong&gt;.&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Wrap the hose in a hook&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;p&gt;If you frequently water your plants using a hose, youve probably experienced tripping on a tangled hose. To spare you from the pain, wrap your hose on a hook. That way, itll be easier to find and you can get it from underfoot. For best results, use a big hook with a steep angle.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol start="2"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Make your tools accessible&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Tools with long handles are ideally stored in areas where they are accessible but out of the way at the same time. To keep them organized, you can install a tool hanger in the door of your shed. This will free up the walls and allow you to add shelves or a potting bench.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol start="3"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Invest in cabinets&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;p&gt;These cabinets are easy to install and they will surely increase the storage capacity of your shed. With these compartments, you can arrange both small and large gardening tools. Depending on your preference, you can buy an assembled cabinet or just build one yourself. Of course, the latter is the cheaper option.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol start="4"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Use magnets&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Magnets arent just pretty accessories for your refrigerator. You can also use them to hold metallic tools. With these magnetic bars, your pruning shears or trowels wont be in the way. The trick here is to pick a magnetic bar that can hold the weight of your tools.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol start="5"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Dont forget the calendar&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Keeping a calendar inside your shed is very useful if you want to keep track of your landscaping activities. No more guessing the exact date when you planted a seed or fertilized your lawn.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol start="6"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt; Wicker Basket&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Hang a wicker basket on the door so that you can easily grab your most used garden tools like shears and gloves.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;Can you share other tips for organizing your shed? Join the discussion in the comments!&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
