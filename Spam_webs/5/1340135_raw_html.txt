            <h1 class="entry-title">
             <a href="http://vergehq.com/2014/02/05/top-entrepreneur-quotes/" rel="bookmark">
              20 Quotes From Historys 20 Top Entrepreneurs
             </a>
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta">
             <span class="itemDate">
              Tim Hickle | February 5, 2014
             </span>
            </div>
            <!-- .entry-meta -->
           </header>
           <!-- .entry-header -->
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             <img alt="Entrepreneur quotes" class=" wp-image-39151 alignleft" height="188" src="http://vergestartups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-02-at-10.36.43-AM.png" width="276"/>
             As you may have seen, we have compiled an infographic of the top 20 startups of all time. Revenue numbers are awesome, but what got them there? What sort of ideologies and ideas did it take to escalate these 20 people into this pantheon of awesome entrepreneurial talent? What can we learn from that to apply to our businesses? To answer these questions, we sat down and compiled our favorite quote from each entrepreneur on our list. This list of quotes on business, entrepreneurship, and startups contains wisdom from managing your time to managing your people.
             <strong>
              Here are our top 20 entrepreneurial quotes of all time.
             </strong>
             <span id="more-39147">
             </span>
            </p>
            <h1>
             Steve Jobs, Apple
            </h1>
            <p>
             Your time is limited, so dont waste it living someone elses life. Dont be trapped by dogma  which is living with the results of other peoples thinking. Dont let the noise of others opinions drown out your own inner voice. And most important, have the courage to follow your heart and intuition.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Larry Page, Google
            </h1>
            <p>
             Many leaders of big organizations, I think, dont believe that change is possible. But if you look at history, things do change, and if your business is static, youre likely to have issues.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Bill Gates, Microsoft
            </h1>
            <p>
             The most meaningful way to differentiate your company from your competitors, the best way to put distance between you and the crowd is to do an outstanding job with information. How you gather, manage and use information will determine whether you win or lose.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Jeff Bezos, Amazon
            </h1>
            <p>
             I strongly believe that missionaries make better products. They care more. For a missionary, its not just about the business. There has to be a business, and the business has to make sense, but thats not why you do it. You do it because you have something meaningful that motivates you.
            </p>
            <h1>
             <img alt="Top Entrepreneur Quotes - Zuckerberg" class="aligncenter wp-image-39153" height="300" src="http://vergestartups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-02-at-12.00.52-PM-237x300.png" width="237"/>
            </h1>
            <h1>
             Mark Zuckerberg, Facebook
            </h1>
            <p>
             I just think people have a lot of fiction. But, you know, I mean, the real story of Facebook is just that weve worked so hard for all this time. I mean, the real story is actually probably pretty boring, right? I mean, we just sat at our computers for six years and coded.
            </p>
            <h1>
             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
              Ma Huateng, Tencent
             </span>
            </h1>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
              The leader of the market today may not necessarily be the leader tomorrow.
              <br/>
             </span>
            </p>
            <h1>
             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
              Jerry Yang, Yahoo!
             </span>
            </h1>
            <p>
             If you find something that feels right but doesnt seem to fit into your master plan, take a chance and commit to it by working hard. You shouldnt be afraid to let passion get behind the wheel. You might really love where you end up.
            </p>
            <h1>
             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
              Robin Li, Baidu
             </span>
            </h1>
            <p>
             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
              People can get information  on entertainment, politics, finance  much easier than before. That will change the way people do business, the way people live.
              <br/>
             </span>
            </p>
            <h1>
             Bill Hewlett, Hewlett-Packard
            </h1>
            <p>
             The greatest success goes to the person who is not afraid to fail in front of even the largest audience.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Reid Hoffman, LinkedIn
            </h1>
            <p>
             People who take risk intelligently can usually actually make a lot more progress than people who dont.
            </p>
            <h1>
             <img alt="Top Entrepreneur Quotes - Twitter" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-39157" height="300" src="http://vergestartups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-02-at-12.01.11-PM-209x300.png" width="209"/>
            </h1>
            <h1>
             Jack Dorsey, Twitter
            </h1>
            <p>
             Make every detail perfect and limit the number of details to perfect.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Drew Houston, Dropbox
            </h1>
            <p>
             Weve had customers from the beginning. The reason people use Dropbox is because they really love it. We think more about who is going to be competing with what we are going to be doing, not with where we started.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Andrew Mason, Groupon
            </h1>
            <p>
             Ive been very lucky, from the beginning. Ive found that as long as youre fundamentally good  as long as youre not being bad to people  people give you a lot of room to be yourself, because being yourself is being honest. And thats what people want to see.
            </p>
            <h1>
             <a href="http://vergestartups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-02-at-12.01.28-PM.png">
              <img alt="Top Entrepreneur Quotes - Craigslist" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-39155" height="209" src="http://vergestartups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-02-at-12.01.28-PM.png" width="277"/>
             </a>
            </h1>
            <h1>
             Craig Newmark, Craigslist
            </h1>
            <p>
             In business, there are times when you disagree, and sometimes it turns out that youre just plain wrong. Humor takes away tension and helps you realize youre wrong.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Alex Karp, Palantir
            </h1>
            <p>
             Any business would IPO at our size, but that would make running a business like ours very difficult.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Reed Hastings, Neflix
            </h1>
            <p>
             But as an entrepreneur you have to feel like you can jump out of an aeroplane because youre confident that youll catch a bird flying by. Its an act of stupidity, and most entrepreneurs go splat because the bird doesnt come by, but a few times it does.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Jim McKelvey, Square
            </h1>
            <p>
             People who solve problems are happier.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Mark Pincus, Zynga
            </h1>
            <p>
             From the beginning I wanted to build a company that could sustain not for two years or four years or even ten years but be something that really matters over time the way Amazon and Google and others have.
            </p>
            <h1>
             Peter Vesterbacka, Rovio
            </h1>
            <p>
             You have to have more leadership, less management. Its about getting stuff done, you can sit around and analyze things forever but while you do that the competition has moved on.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              These are our favorite quotes from our
              <a href="http://vergestartups.com/top-20-startups-of-all-time/">
               top 20 entrepreneurs
              </a>
              . Did we miss any?
             </strong>
            </p>
