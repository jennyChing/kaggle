         <p>
          On Sunday Evening some big news shattered the sports world, a top NFL Prospect Michael Sam a Defensive End from the University of Missouri, announced that he was gay. This was big because in the NFL there has been no player who has been, openly gay. There have been inklings of it, the
          <a href="http://blog.chron.com/ultimatetexans/2013/12/two-members-of-1993-oilers-were-gay-teammates-knew-didnt-care/">
           1993 Houston Oiler
          </a>
          s,
          <a href="http://espn.go.com/new-york/nfl/story/_/id/9237535/vince-lombardi-proud-jason-collins">
           Vince Lombardis Green Bay Packers
          </a>
          , just to name a few. What does this mean for the NFL? What does this mean for Sam? Hard to say, time will tell. Sam is expected to be a mid 1st round pick(10-25), though many insiders have predicted his stock might have tumbled due to the announcement. Regardless of orientation, Elite Millennial believes he will be a contributor to someones defense this fall.
         </p>
