        <p>
         <mark class="mark mark-yellow">
          Ronit Baranga
         </mark>
         is a ceramic sculptor from Israel.
        </p>
        <p>
         Just by peeking at her
         <a href="http://www.ronitbaranga.com/" target="_blank" title="Ronit Baranga, Clay Sculpture -  ,  ">
          website
         </a>
         you can see that none of hercreations are normal: Theyre creepy, crawly, and some will make your skin cringeand herlatest work is even better.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Blurred Border Between Living &amp; Still
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_28521" style="width: 880px">
         <img alt="Hybrid Tea Set " class="size-full wp-image-28521" data-lazy-src="http://www.ripleys.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Hybrid-Tea-Set-Ronit-Baranga-3.jpg" height="585" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="880"/>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Hybrid Tea Set " class="size-full wp-image-28521" height="585" src="http://www.ripleys.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Hybrid-Tea-Set-Ronit-Baranga-3.jpg" width="880"/>
         </noscript>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Hybrid tea set will walk away
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Ronit created a standard set of tableware ceramics with
         <strong>
          human mouths, teeth, tongues, and fingers protruding
         </strong>
         from the plate.
        </p>
        <p>
         It looks like some of the plates will try to crawl away or nip at you or your food if you tried eating off of them.
        </p>
        <p>
         As cool as these are  I dont think I could ever eat off of them!
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          The useful, passive, tableware can now be perceived as an active object, aware of itself and its surroundings  responding to it. It does not allow to be taken for granted, to be used. It decides on its own how to behave in the situation. -Ronit Baranga
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/RonitBarangaSculpture" target="_blank" title="Ronit Baranga Sculpture">
          Ronit Baranga
         </a>
         asks you and us:
         <strong>
          This is how I prefer to think about my plates and cups. Metaphorically, of course. What about you ?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         I prefer to not think about my tableware at all.
         <em>
          Tell us what you think!
         </em>
        </p>
