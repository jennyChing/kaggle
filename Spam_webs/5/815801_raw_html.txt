                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Amazing Window Display Made Out of Paper and Leather
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-666120969952627006" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-64cQPCp0AFQ/U8Q3fxWXt6I/AAAAAAAAOSE/tSh5ijjhuMQ/s1600/FoxBurrow_1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-64cQPCp0AFQ/U8Q3fxWXt6I/AAAAAAAAOSE/tSh5ijjhuMQ/s1600/FoxBurrow_1.jpg" width="266"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <!-- facebreak -->
                      <br/>
                      Luxury brand
                      <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hermes" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Hermes">
                       Hermes
                      </a>
                      ' amazing window display for a
                      <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barcelona" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Barcelona">
                       Barcelona
                      </a>
                      store is made entirely from paper and
                      <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leather" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Leather">
                       leather
                      </a>
                      . French studio Zim&amp;Zou was appointed to create something unique and the concept used to create the exquisite
                      <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paper_craft" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Paper craft">
                       paper craft
                      </a>
                      was knows as
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        The Fox's Den
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      . It is a story about a fox who brings all of his belongings to his new apartment.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      It took more than a month to make the entire window display. The hand made sculpture of the fox was sculpted using Hermes' leather remnants. Other objects in the display were created using orange colored paper and glue. The entire installation took more than 60 tubes of glue and 144 sheets of paper.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      The detailed art instllation cleverly portrays the story and takes window displays to a whole new level.
