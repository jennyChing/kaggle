     <p>
      There are a lot of great cell phone games out there. BlackBerry has been a favorite for business professionals and championed under
      <a href="http://us.blackberry.com/business/blackberry-10/byod.html">
       BYOD
      </a>
      policies, but its also a great platform for
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/ipad-games-to-keep-you-occupied-on-a-long-flight/">
       mobile gaming
      </a>
       believe it or not. With a myriad of games available in the BB World marketplace, it can be difficult to figure out which are to try first! Check out these six
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/top-5-video-game-soundtracks/">
       games
      </a>
      that are sure to keep you occupied for hours on end.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8474" rel="attachment wp-att-8474">
       <img alt="Blackberry games" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8474" height="375" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/featureimage.jpg" width="500"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      Image via
      <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/chrisamichaels/3194840784/in/photolist-5SjoRN-6evWgp-6fngAE-6q8irT-6syPxm-6ytVSc-6yy42C-6GTN6U-6TzrK4-6VvuRr-6VJcqC-72s1SC-78yAfp-7dsqXt-dDGRBQ-7Vr7Bv-7Vr7Bx-dDGTRS-dDGTUw-dDGRCJ-dDGRvW-dDGSvj-dDGTSy-dDBthB-dDGTXo-dDGSsN-dDGSyJ-dDBuQZ-dDBtkZ-dDGREu-dDBuRK-dDBss2-dDGTWL-dDGRDQ-dDBuTi-dDBtrX-dDBsya-dDBuLp-dDBsrx-dDGTVo-dDBsp4-dDGSxQ-dDGSAo-dDBuNz-dDGSs7-dDBto6-dDBstx-dDGSwS-dDBtqR-dDGTTj-dDGSuN/">
       Flickr
      </a>
      by chrisamichaels
      <span id="more-8473">
      </span>
     </p>
     <h2>
      1. The Bards Tale
     </h2>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8475" rel="attachment wp-att-8475">
       <img alt="Blackberry games" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-8475" height="357" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/pic1-510x357.jpg" width="510"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Image via
      <a href="http://www.berryreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/TheBardsTale3_thumb.jpg">
       Berry Review
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      For those who love RPGs, The Bards Tale is the perfect BlackBerry game. This is a game that others said could never happen. Not only is it a huge RPG, its 3D. Perfect for the new BB10 OS, there are over 20 hours of gameplay, allowing you tons of things to do. With tons of items you can discover, this is a game that will entrance old-school gamers and those new to RPGs alike.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/content/119547/">
       Available here
      </a>
       Price: $5.99
     </p>
     <h2>
      2. Bubble Gum Air
     </h2>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8477" rel="attachment wp-att-8477">
       <img alt="Blackberry games" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8477" height="225" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/pic2.png" width="300"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Image via
      <a href="http://best-apps.t3.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bubble-Gum-Air-grab.png">
       best-apps.t3
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: left;">
      Whats better than
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/angry-birds-rio-trailer/">
       Angry Birds
      </a>
      ? A flying monkey being attacked by angry birds, of course! Bubble Gum features a monkey that loves bubble gum, whom uses his bubbles to fly up into the sky. Naturally, the birds dont like this very much. Dodge the angry, jealous birds for as long as you can and rack in some major points the longer you can survive the bird onslaught.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/content/55664/?countrycode=US">
       App available here
      </a>
       Price: Free; Premium version $1.99
     </p>
     <h2>
      3. The Oregon Trail American Settler
     </h2>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8478" rel="attachment wp-att-8478">
       <img alt="Blackberry games" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8478" height="320" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/pic3.jpg" width="480"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Image via
      <a href="http://c453.r53.cf1.rackcdn.com/7134/OT_01.jpg">
       Gamezebo
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Do you remember the old Oregon Trail computer game? The Oregon Trail American Settler is a continuation of the story. You have followed the trail and now are settling in your home. Youre going to come up against some crazy dangers that you simply wont expect the first time around. You can even play with friends by visiting each others towns and trading with one another.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/content/21197916/?countrycode=US">
       App available here
      </a>
       Price: Free (Within-game purchase options.)
     </p>
     <h2>
      4. World of Goo
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8479" rel="attachment wp-att-8479">
       <img alt="Blackberry games" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8479" height="279" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/pic4.jpg" width="480"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Image via
      <a href="http://2dboy.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/WorldOfGooBlackberry.jpg">
       2D Boy
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      With this physics-based game, youre going to get hours of play. World of Goo has gained popularity for a variety of reasons, including the difficulty of the levels and the visuals of the game itself. You can discover new species of the Goo Ball as you go, there are new ability and youre even uncovering a great story.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/content/20199743/?countrycode=US">
       App available here
      </a>
       Price: $4.99
     </p>
     <h2>
      5. Monopoly Here and Now
     </h2>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8481" rel="attachment wp-att-8481">
       <img alt="Monopoly" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8481" height="274" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Monopoly.jpg" width="409"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Image via
      <a href="http://www.playbookdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/monopoly-playbook-in-appworld.blackberry.com_.jpg">
       Playbook Daily
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      We all know that
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/why-are-there-so-many-themed-monopoly/">
       Monopoly comes in many forms and styles
      </a>
      , and that trend continues on the mobile platforms. The Here and Now version of the classic board game has received many updates and tweaks from the original. It uses locations that youll recognize, as well as new tokens. When you play on your phone, you can even play with your friends by passing the phone around. When you want to play a board game, but just dont feel like getting out the board, Monopoly Here and Now is the perfect option.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/content/3020/?countrycode=US">
       App available here
      </a>
       Price: $3.99
     </p>
     <h2>
      6. Jetpack Joyride
     </h2>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/?attachment_id=8480" rel="attachment wp-att-8480">
       <img alt="Blackberry games" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-8480" height="248" src="http://www.dailybits.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/pic5-510x248.jpg" width="510"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Image via
      <a href="http://cdn.n4bb.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Jetpack-Joyride.jpg">
       N4BB
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      If youre looking for a game that offers endless hours of fun, simply because it never ends, Jetpack Joyride is the game for you. This game is an endless runner with evil scientists, awesome jetpacks and more. You can upgrade your jetpack as you go, making it even more awesome that it was to start off with. Whether youre just running for cash or youre completing a quest, make sure you get what you need to get a better jet. It also has an awesome
      <a href="http://www.dailybits.com/top-5-video-game-soundtracks/">
       game soundtrack
      </a>
      that rivals some of the best in business. Theres always something more you can work toward.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/content/18919926/?countrycode=US">
       App available here
      </a>
       Price: Free
     </p>
     <p>
      There are tons of great games out there for BlackBerry phones. The game you choose depends on your favorite type of game.
     </p>
