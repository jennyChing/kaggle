        <div class="article_content clr">
         <p>
          Never one to shy away from snacking on an opponent when the opportunity arises, prodigiously talented but troubled Soccer Ballstar Luis Suarez was at his carnivorous best in his countrys encounter with Italy this past Tuesday.
         </p>
         <p>
          Not only did Suarezs Uruguay overhaul the Italians to make it through to the World Cups knockout stages, he also appeared to take a bite out of ItalysGiorgio Chielliniwhen his temper got the better of him.
         </p>
