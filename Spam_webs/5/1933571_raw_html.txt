       <h1 class="title">
        The Benevolent Space
       </h1>
       <div class="featured-image">
        <!-- Somehow add a featured image here -->
       </div>
       <!-- /featured-image -->
       <div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;">
       </div>
       <p>
        There is something wrong with me
        <br/>
        is a thought that can be deeply felt
        <br/>
        as a vibration of sound.
       </p>
       <p>
        The raw energy of that sound
        <br/>
        and any arising emotion
        <br/>
        can be totally allowed;
        <br/>
        Invited to reveal itself, even
        <br/>
        With the attitude of a scientist.
       </p>
       <p>
        After all, a scientist doesnt reject data
        <br/>
        Before he experiences it.
        <br/>
        He is a scientist
        <br/>
        He is looking for the true.
        <br/>
        He is not looking for a pre-existing
        <br/>
        theory.
        <br/>
        (If he is a true scientist
        <br/>
        and not just pretending to be one.)
       </p>
       <p>
        The true scientist discovers
        <br/>
        From non-biased inquiry
        <br/>
        That the vibration of sound
        <br/>
        the vibration of emotion
        <br/>
        occurs and unfolds
        <br/>
        In space-like awareness
        <br/>
        Which is not limited
        <br/>
        In its capacity to receive.
       </p>
       <p>
        A true Truth scientist
        <br/>
        Listens
        <br/>
        Senses
        <br/>
        Feels
        <br/>
        The space
        <br/>
        The open, space-like awareness.
       </p>
       <p>
        All that happens
        <br/>
        Is a vibration of sound
        <br/>
        or energy
        <br/>
        Unfolding in space
        <br/>
        In every direction.
        <br/>
        The openness reveals
        <br/>
        that it has no edge
        <br/>
        no border
        <br/>
        no barrier
        <br/>
        and no limit.
       </p>
       <p>
        What then, is the truth
        <br/>
        Revealed by the invitation
        <br/>
        Revealed by the allowing
        <br/>
        Revealed by the willingness of the true scientist?
       </p>
       <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
       </div>
       <p>
        The Truth is:
        <br/>
        The open, space-like awareness
        <br/>
        is a capacity
        <br/>
        Which allows any weather
        <br/>
        Even the most intense storm of all:
        <br/>
        The thought, me
        <br/>
        The feeling, me.
       </p>
       <p>
        The truth dawns.
        <br/>
        The energyeven intense at times
        <br/>
        eternally serves.
        <br/>
        Its purpose is to take
        <br/>
        the imagined separate self
        <br/>
        (made of the thought me
        <br/>
        and the feeling me)
        <br/>
        by the hand
        <br/>
        to reveal its true nature
        <br/>
        Awareness.
       </p>
       <p>
        And no matter how intense
        <br/>
        The thought, me
        <br/>
        The feeling, me
        <br/>
        No matter in which direction it explodes
        <br/>
        No matter in which direction it implodes
        <br/>
        It meets
        <br/>
        Only space
        <br/>
        The benevolent space.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
