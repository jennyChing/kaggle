        <p>
         <strong>
          Christmas cookies
         </strong>
         made with
         <a href="https://tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/V4atn/46B4I/c?p=DulSc&amp;i=hcFe2&amp;r=hqHKefqxxOPsaLMxFnjYwWkmAJK43ehsI3ndcSFiDNY%3D" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/V4atn/46B4I/c?p=DulSc&amp;i=hcFe2&amp;r=hqHKefqxxOPsaLMxFnjYwWkmAJK43ehsI3ndcSFiDNY%3D', 'DOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolate');" rel="nofollow">
          DOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolate
         </a>
         will soon become a family favorite. DOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolates are melted with butter to create the base of these amazing
         <strong>
          Christmas cookies
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Dove-Chocolate-Cookies-long.jpg">
          <img alt="Dark Chocolate Cookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13657" height="1200" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Dove-Chocolate-Cookies-long.jpg" width="730"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         When it comes to baking Christmas cookies, I try to find an easy cookie recipe that has amazing flavor. The holiday season can get so busy that it gets hard to find time to bake. When I saw this Christmas Chocolate Drops cookie recipe on
         <a href="https://tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/V4atn/46B4I/c?p=DulSc&amp;i=hcFe2&amp;r=hqHKefqxxOPsaLMxFnjYwWkmAJK43ehsI3ndcSFiDNY%3D" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/V4atn/46B4I/c?p=DulSc&amp;i=hcFe2&amp;r=hqHKefqxxOPsaLMxFnjYwWkmAJK43ehsI3ndcSFiDNY%3D', 'DOVEs website');" rel="nofollow">
          DOVEs website
         </a>
         , I knew it would be a winner. You simply cant go wrong with a cookie that is based on meltedDOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolates.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Dove-Chocolate-Chopped.jpg" style="width: 750px; height: 552px;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive mentioned before that Im a bit of chocolate snob, andDOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolates make my top 10 list of delicious chocolate. Not only do you use melted chocolate in these cookies, but you also chop up more and mix it in to the cookies. Chocolate with a side of chocolate.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Chocolate-cookies-2.jpg" style="width: 750px; height: 646px;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Christmas Chocolate Drops are really easy to make, too. I was a little concerned about the batter once it was made. The batter is more like a brownie batter than a stiff cookie dough. But dont worry! Just scoop the dough with a tablespoon or cookie scoop and I promise they will bake up into delicious, soft, chocolatey cookies.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="http://growingupgabel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Chocolate-cookies.jpg" style="width: 746px; height: 848px;"/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 bag DOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolate
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8 tablespoons butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup cocoa powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 large eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups chopped nuts: such as walnuts, pecans or toasted almonds
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. Line 2 cookie sheet pans with foil and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Melt half of the DOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolate with the butter in the top of a double
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            boiler until smooth. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Meanwhile, roughly chop the remaining chocolate and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            To make the batter, combine the flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt in a small bowl, set
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until thick and light yellow, about 2 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir in flour mixture and cooled chocolate and beat for 1 minute.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Fold in the remaining chopped DOVE PROMISES Dark Chocolate and nuts.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Drop tablespoons of batter onto the prepared cookie sheet pans about 2 inches apart.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake for approximately 10 minutes, or until just firm.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from the oven, transfer to a wire rack and cool for 5 minutes before removing from the cookie sheet pans.
           </li>
          </ol>
