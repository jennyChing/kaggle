            <a href="http://allenbwest.com/2014/04/top-10-reasons-vote-democrat-2014%E2%80%A8%E2%80%A8/">
             Allen West is the man.
            </a>
            The conservative movement needs a lot more stuff like this and we needed it yesterday.
           </p>
           <p>
            Ill add one of my own to this list.
           </p>
           <p>
            Ill vote Democrat because I hate capitalism. I hate it so much that I will tweet about how much I hate it from my iPhone while drinking a Starbucks.
           </p>

            <span>

            <p>
             <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
              10. Ill vote Democrat because I cant wait for college football season to be delayed or cancelled because the student athletes are union employees.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             9. Ill vote Democrat because I believe oil companys profits of 4% on a gallon of gas are obscene, but the government taxing the same gallon of gas at 15% isnt.
            </p>
            <p>
             8. Ill vote Democrat because I believe the government will do a better job of spending the money I earn than I would.
            </p>
            <p>
             7. Ill vote Democrat because Freedom of Speech is fine as long as nobody is offended by it.
            </p>
            <p>
             6. Ill vote Democrat because Im way too irresponsible to own a gun, and I know that my local police are all I need to protect me from murderers and thieves. I am also thankful that we have a 911 service that get police to your home in order to identify your body after a home invasion.
            </p>
            <p>
             5. Ill vote Democrat because Im not concerned about millions of babies being aborted so long as we keep all death row inmates alive and comfy.
            </p>
            <p>
             4. Ill vote Democrat because I think illegal aliens have a right to free health care, education, and Social Security benefits, and we should take away the Social Security from those who paid into it.
            </p>
            <p>
             3. Ill vote Democrat because I believe that businesses should NOT be allowed to make profits for themselves. They need to break even and give the rest away to the government for redistribution as the Democrats see fit.
            </p>
            <p>
             2. Ill vote Democrat because I believe liberal judges need to rewrite the Constitution every few days to suit some fringe kooks who would never get their agendas past the voters.
            </p>
            <p>
             And the Number One reason Ill vote Democrat is:
            </p>
            <p>
             1. Ill vote Democrat because I think that its better to pay billions for oil to people who hate us, but not drill our own because it might upset some endangered beetle, gopher, fish or frog.
