     <p>
      Pour me another with the Moore &amp; Giles Sidecar. Nothing beats coming home, mixing a drink, putting on that favorite record and relaxing. Sometimes easier said than done! Often times we are routing around the cupboards collecting all the ingredients we need, and one hour later our drink is ready (maybe thats a bit of an over statement, but you get the point).
      <a href="http://lumberjac.com/tag/moore-giles/" target="_blank" title="Moore &amp; Giles">
       Moore &amp; Giles
      </a>
      in collaboration with bartender Jim Meehan (check out his book,
      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/The-PDT-Cocktail-Book-Bartenders/dp/1402779232" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview','/yoast-ga/outbound-article/http://www.amazon.com/The-PDT-Cocktail-Book-Bartenders/dp/1402779232']);" target="_blank" title="The PDT Cocktail Book">
       The PDT Cocktail Book
      </a>
      ), have come to your aid with The Sidecar. This is one beautifully handcrafted sidecar. Made using an aluminum frame and stained Virginia black walnut, this sidecar is one of the nicest looking functional bar on wheels weve seen. The sidecar is highlighted by brass fittings covered in beautiful Virginia Natural/Tobacco leather, and features a butcher-like bar top flanked by two shelves. As design/drink connoisseurs, youll appreciate the details that make this drink cart worthy of your finest collection, adding the missing ingredient to your daily routine.
     </p>
     <p>
      To enjoy, contact
