        <p>
         These are two very controversial images that sparked outrage with fans everywhere! Detroit fans are screaming karma while Dallas fans are saying that they were robbed.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are a
         <a href="http://www.detroitlions.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.detroitlions.com/', ' Detroit Lions');" target="_blank" title="Detroit Lions">
          Detroit Lions
         </a>
         fan, this image may make you cringe as that call knocked the Lions out of position in the Playoffs. Dreams of their first playoff win since the 1991 season were snatched,the Lions at one time were leading Dallas 20-17 in the fourth quarter- then- a pass interference call gave the Lions a first down well into Dallas territory and was picked up without explanation by referee
         <a href="http://www.azcentral.com/story/sports/nfl/2015/01/10/bad-pass-interference-calls-credibility-problem-nfl/21566085/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.azcentral.com/story/sports/nfl/2015/01/10/bad-pass-interference-calls-credibility-problem-nfl/21566085/', 'Pete Morelli');" target="_blank" title="Pete Morelli">
          Pete Morelli
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         As per Fox, The Lions QB
         <a data-cqtag="Football/NFL/NFC/NFC_North/Lions/Matthew_Stafford" href="http://www.foxsports.com/nfl/player/matthew-stafford/638251" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.foxsports.com/nfl/player/matthew-stafford/638251', 'Matthew Stafford');">
          Matthew Stafford
         </a>
         threw incomplete to tight end
         <a data-cqtag="Football/NFL/NFC/NFC_North/Lions/Brandon_Pettigrew" href="http://www.foxsports.com/nfl/player/brandon-pettigrew/638289" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.foxsports.com/nfl/player/brandon-pettigrew/638289', 'Brandon Pettigrew');">
          Brandon Pettigrew
         </a>
         , but Cowboys LB
         <a data-cqtag="Football/NFL/NFC/NFC_East/Cowboys/Anthony_Hitchens" href="http://www.foxsports.com/collegefootball/player/anthony-hitchens/978175" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.foxsports.com/collegefootball/player/anthony-hitchens/978175', 'Anthony Hitchens');">
          Anthony Hitchens
         </a>
         was flagged for pass interference . . . until the officials picked up the flag and took away the penalty which would have given Detroit a first down well into Dallas territory.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 544px">
         <img alt="" class="" height="300" src="https://cdn3.vox-cdn.com/thumbor/kv57A549b0ypWw4uoL7RXL50CR0=/800x0/filters:no_upscale()/cdn0.vox-cdn.com/uploads/chorus_asset/file/2903016/dalpi.0.gif" width="534"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Robbed

        <p>

         <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/mike-pereira-dez-bryant-reversal-2015-1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.businessinsider.com/mike-pereira-dez-bryant-reversal-2015-1', ' Mike Pereira');" target="_blank" title="Mike Pereira">
          Mike Pereira
         </a>
         criticized that call during the FOX broadcast of the gameas he says it will haunt the Detroit Lions for a lifetime.
        </p>
        <p>
         With this being said,
         <a href="http://www.dallascowboys.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.dallascowboys.com/', 'Dallas');" target="_blank" title="Dallas Cowboys">
          Dallas
         </a>
         advanced to face the
         <a href="http://www.packers.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.packers.com/', 'Packers');" target="_blank" title="Packers">
          Packers
         </a>
         
        </p>

        <a href="http://www.gopjn.com/t/SUJGSkdGS0ZCRkhFSkdNQklFTk5K" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.gopjn.com/t/SUJGSkdGS0ZCRkhFSkdNQklFTk5K', '');">

        </a>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_227" style="width: 269px">

        <p>
         With this game being a nailbiter for most, the Cowboys were down 26-21 in the last minutes of the game. Receiver
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dez_Bryant" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dez_Bryant', 'Dez Bryant');" target="_blank" title="Dez Bryant">
          Dez Bryant
         </a>
         who is known for his athleticism and dynamic catchesmade a leaping 31-yard catch near the goal line that would have put the team in line to take the lead.
        </p>
        <p>
         Jaws dropped! Could the Cowboys be on their way to theirfirst NFC championship in nearly 20 years???
        </p>
        <p>
         Well they were, Bryants catch was
         <em>
          <strong>
           initially
          </strong>
         </em>
         ruled a catch at the one yard lineuntil Packers head coach
         <a href="http://www.packers.com/team/coaches/mike-mccarthy/5470bb6f-3c63-4020-b03e-a80910646c47" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.packers.com/team/coaches/mike-mccarthy/5470bb6f-3c63-4020-b03e-a80910646c47', ' Mike McCarthy');" target="_blank" title="Mike McCarthy">
          Mike McCarthy
         </a>
         made the game changing decision and threw the challenge flag.Now many will ask if the Cowboys were robbed of a chance for their first NFC championship in nearly 20 years. Lets go to the rulebook shall we?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="B7GRDzLCIAAVRDG" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-229" height="169" src="http://laseanrinique.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/B7GRDzLCIAAVRDG-300x169.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The NFLs official rulebook states: a forward pass is complete (by the offense) or intercepted (by the defense) if a player, who is inbounds: (a) secures control of the ball in his hands or arms prior to the ball touching the ground; and (b) touches the ground inbounds with both feet or with any part of his body other than his hands and (c) maintains control of the ball long enough, after (a) and (b) have been fulfilled, to enable him to perform any act common to the game (i.e., maintaining control long enough to pitch it, pass it,advance with it, or avoid or ward off an opponent, etc.)
        </p>
        <p>
         Watch the video and decide if it was a catch or fumble was Dallas robbed, or was it karma?
        </p>
