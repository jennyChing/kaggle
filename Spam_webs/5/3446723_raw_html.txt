&lt;p&gt;&lt;div style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;img style="margin: 0pt 10px 10px 0pt; float: left;" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Di1HzTLtBlM/Ru7JRLh92oI/AAAAAAAAB20/e9JLPEoG3E0/s400/putin.JPG" alt="" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5111243924102306434" border="0" /&gt;&lt;b&gt;The Russians have tested a new type of weapon, as powerful as a nuclear bomb, but without all that pesky radiation!&lt;/b&gt; Instead, this vacuum bomb gets its power from a fuel-air mixture, which unleashes a destructive shockwave. It's reportedly four times more powerful than the U.S.'s version, known as the MOAB (mother of all bombs), which was the most powerful non-nuclear weapon ever designed - until now.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;span id="fullpost"&gt;&lt;div style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;The weapon was successfully field-tested in the late evening of September 11, 2007, when it was dropped from a Tupolev Tu-160 heavy bomber with a parachute and exploded. The weapon yields the equivalent of 44 tons of TNT using 7.8 tons of a new type of high explosive, created applying nanotechnology. In comparison, the MOAB produces the equivalent of 11 tons of TNT from 8 tons of high explosive. The blast radius of 300 m is twice as large as the MOAB.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;If the blast doesn't incinerate people, its second phase employs a technology based on the "thermobaric" principle which uses heat and pressure to kill people targeted across a wide field - by sucking the air out of their lungs and rupturing internal organs.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;br /&gt;Below are a couple of video footages of the bomb from two different news sources.&lt;/div&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;center&gt;&lt;object height="350" width="425"&gt;&lt;param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/0LnPcSaRA2w"&gt;&lt;param name="wmode" value="transparent"&gt;&lt;embed src="http://www.youtube.com/v/0LnPcSaRA2w" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" wmode="transparent" height="350" width="425"&gt;&lt;/embed&gt;&lt;/object&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;object height="350" width="425"&gt;&lt;param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/S2FGA3Z-oYM"&gt;&lt;param name="wmode" value="transparent"&gt;&lt;embed src="http://www.youtube.com/v/S2FGA3Z-oYM" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" wmode="transparent" height="350" width="425"&gt;&lt;/embed&gt;&lt;/object&gt;&lt;/center&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
