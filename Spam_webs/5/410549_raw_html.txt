        <p>
         <a href="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivaegg.jpg">
          <img alt="godiva egg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8407" height="382" src="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivaegg.jpg" width="324"/>
         </a>
         Godiva is one of those brands that you dont realise is actually a bean to bar chocolate maker  but they are. They use beans from various places in the world and it all comes together as the delicious chocolate you get. Mostly Godiva is a praline expert but they do have ganaches and more which is what I love (oh and the cookies  )
        </p>
        <p>
         As kids love to keep it simple, the boxed milk chocolate egg called Mr Bunny is a fun choice! The perfectly child-size egg is expertly crafted in smooth chocolate milk chocolate and hand decorated with a pretty butterfly. It also comes with 4 colourful foiled wrapped chocolate egg POPS (no, I dont understand the naming of the filled eggs either). Priced at 16.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivapopeggs.jpg">
          <img alt="godiva pop eggs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8406" height="254" src="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivapopeggs.jpg" width="524"/>
         </a>
         Chocolate egg pop treasure (24 pops). More for adults, this lovely yellow ribbon wrapped box contains 3 each of the full assortment of filled Godiva mini egg POPS which includes 4 dark, 3 milk and 1 white chocolate, its a tasty Easter treat thats just right for sharing. Priced at 22
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivapencilcase.jpg">
          <img alt="godiva pencil case" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8405" height="393" src="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivapencilcase.jpg" width="273"/>
         </a>
         Mr Bunny pencil tin with milk carres  this super-cute chocolate filled pencil tin makes a smart 2-in-1 Easter gift for any scholars. It is filled with 15 milky carres inside ! Priced at 10
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivabeadedegg.jpg">
          <img alt="godiva beaded egg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8408" height="400" src="http://mostlyaboutchocolate.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/godivabeadedegg.jpg" width="348"/>
         </a>
         Beaded eggs with 15 chocolate pops.  Finally, if you are looking for a decadent gift to give this Easter, look no further than the Godiva Beaded Eggs. This bright and beautiful keepsake is covered in sparkling coloured beads, with a pretty Springtime flower design, and filled with Godiva foil-wrapped chocolate egg POPS Priced at *coughs* 55
        </p>
        <p>
         All available at
         <a href="http://www.godivachocolates.co.uk">
          www.godivachocolates.co.uk
         </a>
         and of course by popping in to the shop!
        </p>
