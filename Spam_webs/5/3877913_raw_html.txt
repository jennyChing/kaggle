          <p>
           From the depths of Hasbros ad department comes the Lost John Carpenters The Thing 1982 Toy Promo.  I wish  This retro style spoof toy commercial is awesome!  Complete with custom G.I. Joe figures sculpeyed into the 1982 versions of MacReady, Childs, Windows, and Blair.  A must see for any 80s action figure aficionado.
