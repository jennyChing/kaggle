              There are loads of books available on investing today. What sets this book apart from the herd is its comprehensiveness. It covers almost every aspect a lay person should be aware of. It introduces so many concepts  the various forms of investing, margin of safety, fundamental analysis etc. Simply applying these concepts to all investments- not just equities  would be beneficial.

              <br/>
              <br/>
