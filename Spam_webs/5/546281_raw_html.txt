     <p>
      <b>
       European route E40
      </b>
      is the longest
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/European_route" title="European route">
       European route
      </a>
      ,
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stalingrad_1-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-stalingrad-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      more than 8,000 kilometres (4,971 miles) long, connecting
      <a href="/wiki/Calais" title="Calais">
       Calais
      </a>
      in
      <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">
       France
      </a>
      via
      <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">
       Belgium
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">
       Germany
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">
       Poland
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">
       Ukraine
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">
       Russia
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">
       Kazakhstan
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Uzbekistan" title="Uzbekistan">
       Uzbekistan
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Turkmenistan" title="Turkmenistan">
       Turkmenistan
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="/wiki/Kyrgyzstan" title="Kyrgyzstan">
       Kyrgyzstan
      </a>
      , with
      <a href="/wiki/Ridder,_Kazakhstan" title="Ridder, Kazakhstan">
       Ridder
      </a>
      in
      <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">
       Kazakhstan
      </a>
      near the border to
      <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">
       China
      </a>
      .
     </p>
