       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Veins and Your Health
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="sep">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.alluremedicalspa.com/veins-and-your-health/" rel="bookmark" title="3:37 pm">
         <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-12-09T15:37:32+00:00">
          December 9, 2014
         </time>
        </a>
        <span class="by-author">
         <span class="sep">
          by
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.alluremedicalspa.com/author/cmg-admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by cmg-admin">
           cmg-admin
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
      </header>
      <!-- .entry-header -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25">
       </span>
       <div class="pf-content">
        <p>
         Theyre ever-present in your body but youre seldom aware of their purpose and function. That is, until you encounter a slight problem with your veins. Veins are the blood vessels that carry blood from the different parts of your body to your heart. Valves are crucial to the process, as they act like doors opening to allow blood to pass through and closing so the blood doesnt flow backwards.
        </p>
        <p>
         If your veins and valves are healthy, youll have nothing to worry about. However, if you have unhealthy veins and valves, there is the possibility of blood flowing backwards into the veins and causing a variety of vein diseases. Such diseases include spider veins, varicose veins, and Chronic Venous Insufficiency (CVI)  each can have significant effects on your body. Fortunately, there are different types of treatments to help reduce the effect such as
         <a href="http://www.alluremedicalspa.com/procedure/varicose-veins/treatment-types/">
          Sclerotherapy and Laser Ablation
         </a>
         to reduce the health impact of these venous diseases. Plus, there are some natural remedies to decrease the pain.
        </p>
        <p>
         To learn more about the effects of venous diseases and the different types of treatment, check out the infographic below or consult a professional at Allure Medical Spa.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.alluremedicalspa.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Allure-Veins-and-your-health-IG.jpg">
          <img alt="Veins and Your Health Infographic" class="size-large wp-image-6959 aligncenter" height="1228" src="http://www.alluremedicalspa.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Allure-Veins-and-your-health-IG-224x1024.jpg" width="258"/>
         </a>
        </p>
