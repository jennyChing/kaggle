         Caramel Crumb Cake Made with Sweet Rolls
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-27T07:33:19+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 27, 2015

         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

        <p>
         <a class="broken_link" href="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/crumbcake1.jpg">
          <img alt="Caramel Crumb Cake made with sweet rolls #warmupyourday #ad" class="aligncenter wp-image-21286 size-large" height="493" src="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/crumbcake1-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>

        <p>
         Breakfast is my favorite meal of the day, when done right. New Jersey is often called The diner capital of the world and the best thing about a true diner is that you can order whatever meal you want at any time day or night.My family often requests
         <a href="http://www.WarmUpYourDay.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          sweet rolls
         </a>
         and orange juice with breakfast.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/crumbs.jpg">
          <img alt="Caramel Crumb Cake made with sweet rolls #warmupyourday #ad" class="aligncenter wp-image-21282 size-large" height="493" src="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/crumbs-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Caramel Crumb Cake made with sweet rolls #warmupyourday #ad" class="aligncenter wp-image-21280 size-large" height="493" src="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/cinnamon-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         When I decide to treat them, they are happy campers. But lately I have been in the mood for crumb cake, and I thought,
         <em>
          oooh..
         </em>
         .
         <em>
          sweet rolls + crumb cake would be divine.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         So thats where the idea for this recipe came from  pure gluttony. Doesnt it sound good though? It is. And its the perfect treat to make the night before and enjoy for daysor as long as it lasts.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_1503.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_1503" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-21347" height="493" src="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_1503-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://girlgonemom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/cake-oj.jpg">

         </a>

          Caramel Sweet Roll Crumb Cake

         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            GirlGoneMom.com
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Brunch
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCuisine">
           Cuisine:
           <span itemprop="recipeCuisine">
            American
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            9 servings
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 pkg Pepperidge Farms Pecan Caramel Sweet Rolls, cut into pieces
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cups all-purpose flour, plus 1 cup for crumb mixture
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tablespoons oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 large egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup light cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup packed light-brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 stick butter, melted and cooled
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Confectioners sugar
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Spray a 9x9 inch baking dish with oil and dust with flour.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Sift together 1 cups flour, granulated sugar, baking powder and salt in a medium bowl.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, light cream, vanilla and oil, before folding in the flour mixture.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Distribute batter into the floured baking dish. Add the sweet roll pieces in an even layer, and then top with the caramel drizzle.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix together 1 cups flour with brown sugar and cinnamon. Incorporate the melted butter with a rubber spatula until crumbs form.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add the crumbs to the baking dish, followed by the pecans, and place on the oven's middle rack and bake for 40-45 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Let cool completely before topping with sifted confectioners sugar. Slice into squares and serve.
           </li>
          </ol>
