But rubber bands can be cleverly useful for a lot of things in our daily lives. This video presents ten ways to use rubber bands for something else than just keep our mail together. From a cutting board stabilizer to a paint drip catcher, there are plenty of cool hacks to try out! I personally tried the cutting board stabilizer and it works like a charm!
