             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              The greatest examples from history of human ingenuity and invention have come from some kind of need. This need provided the incentive that inspired true creativity. Every Halloween, a new need arises. People cry out, What are we to do with this abundance of sweet we have recently received!? One man, a Redditornamed
              <a href="http://www.reddit.com/user/dericpeace" target="_blank">
               DericPeace
              </a>
              , has answered the peoples cry.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              What he did makes him a hero and a genius.
             </p>
             <div class="image-box">
              <p>
               <strong>
                Halloween = costumes + a whole bunch of delicious.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.coolneeds.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/halloween-and-custome.png">

               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>

               </strong>
