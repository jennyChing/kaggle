      <h2 class="posttitle" style="font:36px/42px Arial, Helvetica, san-serif; font-weight:normal;">
       Jon Stewart lampoons Texas GOPs endorsement of gay cure therapy
      </h2>
      <!--Author byline-->
      <div class="meta-post">
       <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/author/admin/" rel="author" target="_blank">

        <span style="float:right; display:inline; text-align:right; margin: 0; ">
         <!-- AddThis Button BEGIN -->
         <div addthis:title="Jon Stewart lampoons Texas GOPs endorsement of gay cure therapy" addthis:url="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/2014/06/jon-stewart-lampoons-texas-gops-endorsement-of-gay-cure-therapy/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style addthis_16x16_style">

      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        Jon Stewart lampooned
        <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/tag/texas/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Texas
         </b>
        </a>
        <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/tag/republicans/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Republicans
         </b>
        </a>
        on Tuesday, mocking the party over its
        <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/tag/adoption/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          adoption
         </b>
        </a>
        of a platform that endorses the controversial practice of gay-to-straight
        <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/tag/reparative-therapy/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          reparative therapy
         </b>
        </a>
        , and Gov.
        <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/tag/rick-perry/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Rick Perry
         </b>
        </a>
        s recent remarks comparing homosexuality to alcoholism.
       </p>
       <p>
        Stewart suggests that the Texas GOP seek therapy to cure themselves:
       </p>
       <p>
        Being gay is not a choice. But being a Texas Republican is. So my advice is the next time you feel the urge to be a Texas Republican  to replace science textbooks with pictures of humans riding dinosaurs or ban
        <a href="http://www.lgbtqnation.com/tag/gay-marriage/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          same-sex marriage
         </b>
        </a>
        , or put on [a stuffed elephant] hat, shit in a jar and sniff it! It wont cure you  but do it anyway. Hopefully it will remind you of what assholes youre being.
       </p>
