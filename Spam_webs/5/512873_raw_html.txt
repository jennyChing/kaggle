                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     Salt Dough Ornaments and Gift Tags
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1303059103315701105">
                     <div align="center">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      I am so excited to share a great
                      <span style="color: #8f0712; font-family: French Script MT; font-size: x-large;">
                       Christmas
                      </span>
                      craft project today. Salt dough ornaments and gift tags. For pennies you can make a great gift for your family and friends. I am using the ones my girls and I made as gift tags that the recipients can later use as an ornament.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-3T3k7WQ0IVE/TsCN231n9tI/AAAAAAAALYM/X5ypa6xWk8w/s1600-h/189%25255B3%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="Salt dough ornaments" border="0" height="523" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-ieyMGpj9UE8/TsB4cYbHDDI/AAAAAAAALYU/cgAJ2ecCasU/189_thumb%25255B3%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="Salt dough ornaments" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-PxLcx8FVqFM/TsEbYlO55WI/AAAAAAAALaw/afem4seAxM8/s1600-h/015%25255B8%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="Salt dough ornaments" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-VlVdk1SOKN4/TsEbZYGV7pI/AAAAAAAALa4/IsX0gvf3Nn4/015_thumb%25255B5%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="Salt dough ornaments" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      All you need to get started is
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      1 cup all purpose flour
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      1/2 cup salt
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      1/2 water
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      1,2,3 easy peasy!!!
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-wahJuYSmJco/TsB4dNtpeAI/AAAAAAAALYY/A8ZFbMvlF9w/s1600-h/025%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="025" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-NSqpO4dMNao/TsB4dsGaYLI/AAAAAAAALYc/3DSeXSThkbo/025_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="025" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Toss it all in the mixer for a few minutes.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-T9xImYBiOSw/TsB4eVaN0_I/AAAAAAAALYg/iRbhaq_9Bj8/s1600-h/027%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="027" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-dwOHeKmYVBM/TsB4e8lWnMI/AAAAAAAALYk/Bpez7-kLPsU/027_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="027" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Once it is all combined
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-oezEPy7bt-A/TsB4f0cibsI/AAAAAAAALYo/jxjBwQsOWO0/s1600-h/029%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="029" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-Oo8llhqWLFg/TsB4gZo0UdI/AAAAAAAALYs/RFvHt7X1mLI/029_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="029" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Kneed until smooth
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-lJAe-9sG2kk/TsB4hecx0lI/AAAAAAAALYw/AeYMBi3m5OA/s1600-h/030%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="030" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-dNFdCLEuS8c/TsB4h2adezI/AAAAAAAALY0/v3dxu04wvVA/030_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="030" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      I separated mine, so each girl could have one.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-3HPw6qyxeh0/TsB4iosWMqI/AAAAAAAALY4/ywDnXm1MA64/s1600-h/037%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="037" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-heB1jkVEAEE/TsB4jKzRfCI/AAAAAAAALY8/uOAaMj-WtGo/037_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="037" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Roll the dough ball out on wax paper
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-zs_RYgKYSJ8/TsB4kG2ptiI/AAAAAAAALZA/xd1B25vtTWk/s1600-h/044%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="044" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-u4KARqzARCQ/TsB4kop6HqI/AAAAAAAALZE/ToQT8TSgekw/044_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="044" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Now for the fun part.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Grab a doily and press it into the dough with the rolling pin.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-GQ4VO2EENnw/TsB4lOgKkdI/AAAAAAAALZI/O4BFEQFuOOY/s1600-h/046%25255B3%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="046" border="0" height="466" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-gs2N84qZYeY/TsB4lnoqnGI/AAAAAAAALZM/pPP7r0y5mCQ/046_thumb%25255B3%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="046" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      When you lift the doily off you will be left with a beautiful pattern.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-FTDjjlehPZI/TsB4nJAjo5I/AAAAAAAALZQ/xH490sfA6eE/s1600-h/047%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="047" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-12d1WgRsMd4/TsB4nh4hyvI/AAAAAAAALZU/MFCR1SgptD4/047_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="047" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Next grab a cookie cutter and cut out your ornaments.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-YzmUIdgJWpg/TsB4oaJUioI/AAAAAAAALZY/3-kdxt0abnY/s1600-h/050%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="050" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-JJ9_IkJTMnY/TsB4oz8MO5I/AAAAAAAALZc/X7e_ckIsq-E/050_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="050" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                      <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-VoYuEMyx91s/TsB4puBpZDI/AAAAAAAALZg/w96e1qdcQjc/s1600-h/053%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="053" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-bR0q45p7CnE/TsB4qME15jI/AAAAAAAALZk/ybC0FDkwjAM/053_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="053" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Remove the dough from around your ornament shapes, lift and
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-bcga9xtN-kg/TsB4rN3SqGI/AAAAAAAALZo/dmmQ-x_QWBg/s1600-h/056%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="056" border="0" height="445" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-gP3GceHo2PM/TsB4rsNBXhI/AAAAAAAALZs/DzrTMhcvV6M/056_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="056" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      place on a cookie sheet and poke a hole with a straw.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Cook at 200 degrees for 5-6 hours.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Take them out and let them cool.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-rST1CdQzaiw/TsB4sL_tumI/AAAAAAAALZw/kib9PiNwxio/s1600-h/076%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="076" border="0" height="466" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-9tmu-ytBAQo/TsB4s7aSwMI/AAAAAAAALZ0/pIOcLBch0aU/076_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="076" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Paint them white, start with the back. After the back is dry, paint the front.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-kO1lk0paubw/TsB4thbZApI/AAAAAAAALZ4/jrrKITpatEU/s1600-h/081%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="081" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-19yuc5ROUAo/TsB4uXSld1I/AAAAAAAALZ8/dyeuAQAVT2g/081_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="081" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      After the paint is dry apply glue to the front.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-AQ2JwQkwKD4/TsB4vBxPZJI/AAAAAAAALaA/LFi1-gkILKQ/s1600-h/087%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="087" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-6dvywhQLn6Y/TsB4vxQC0WI/AAAAAAAALaE/UoM3sGSpvtQ/087_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="087" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      While the glue is still wet sprinkle with glitter.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-YZjRIgpcUS4/TsB4wqLQPmI/AAAAAAAALaI/JFWYF_zqBgw/s1600-h/089%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="089" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-7W8rRbxCRRQ/TsB4xXZhvnI/AAAAAAAALaM/GZTB3V0Iiac/089_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="089" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      Lightly wipe off the top, so that the glitter only sticks in the grooves.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-3W5OWAGTcQ4/TsB4yOCFuBI/AAAAAAAALaQ/nPACwwGkA5Y/s1600-h/093%25255B2%25255D.jpg">
                       <img alt="093" border="0" height="465" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-BtTpYMIa1GM/TsB4y54bxqI/AAAAAAAALaU/o5HM-s50Z5c/093_thumb%25255B2%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-width: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="093" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      After the glitter and glue has dried seal with a spray sealer.
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
