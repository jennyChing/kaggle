          <p>
           <img alt="mercury_scorpio_seshat" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-25323" height="250" src="http://www.lightworkersworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/mercury_scorpio_seshat-250x250.jpg" width="250"/>
           <strong>
            Cosmic Weather Report: Oct. 27- Nov. 3, 2013
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Hello Lightworkers World friends! We are looking at what many astrologers and energy readers have called the most potent window of time from now until the end of the year  especially 10.29 through 11.11.
          </p>
          <p>
           The week does have a certain spiritual trooper quality to it, but please do bear with me, it does get better. These arent necessarily easy energies, especially prior to 11.3, but they do serve quite a grand and cosmic purpose. No doubt everyone has been feeling the intensity of the stellium (three or more conjunct planets) with the Sun, Mercury (Rx) and Saturn all in deep, mysterious and intense Scorpio. These powerful energies provide a useful backdrop for introspection, energy movement and healing through the power of communication (i.e. writing, art and discussion.)
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Tuesday, Oct. 29: Mercury Retrograde conjunct Saturn in Scorpio.
           </strong>
           This is definitely a time to review a life decision that you may have made around 10.8. During the Mercury Rx, there is also the possibility of re-visioning your original thought/decision. Today may just be the day that you do it.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Thursday, Oct. 31:
           </strong>
           <strong>
            Mars in Virgo trine Pluto in Capricorn.
           </strong>
           This is an excellent aspect not only for increased physical strength and vitality, but also the energy to move through any healing issues (emotional, physical or both,) that may have emerged since the last Moon/Eclipse especially. Again, the Universe serving us up some wonderful cosmic support to assist us in letting go and moving forward. Happy Halloween.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Friday, November 1:
           </strong>
           <strong>
            Uranus square Pluto and Mercury Retrograde sextile Mars and Pluto.
           </strong>
           Oh yes its another round with the Uranus/Pluto square, this time made more interesting and useful with these other aspects. Though *everyone* is feeling the change in these energies, those with major personal planets in cardinal signs (Capricorn, Aries, Cancer &amp; Libra) may especially be feeling the Shift. Though we have the usual Uranus/Pluto action which can feel uncomfortable and bring what I lovingly call the Shakabuku or swift kick in the @$$ from the Universe; we also have Mercury in a supportive sextile with Mars and Pluto to perhaps not only soften the blow, but bring more immediate insights (aha! moments) than in previous passes of this energy. This is a good thing.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             The Keepers on the Stargate of 10.29-11.3.13:
            </strong>
            <strong>
             Dear ones though we see that there has been a bit of physical and soul weariness from the powerful solar encodements/upgrades of the past few weeks, they do indeed serve a powerful purpose both individually and collectively. You are on the brink of yet another Quantum Leap in consciousness. Do not be mislead by the appearance of confusion or perceived negativity that has arisen in your lives or in the lives of others. This is the darkness (Eclipse window) before the dawn (11.11 Stargate.) Though for some your lives have become more active and busy at the moment, it is recommended to take time in the upcoming window to align with the incoming energies for your own healing and also to ground them in physical reality on Gaia. All is well. Shanti.  The Keepers, 10.26.13
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           The New Moon/Solar Eclipse (in Scorpio) on Sunday, 11.3 promises to be pure magic.
           <i>
           </i>
           I highly recommend you use the week ahead to get your emotional and spiritual ducks in a row, in preparation for some high, healing manifestation. Dont be afraid to write that letter, have that talk, take out the trash and cry it out if need be. Be sure to check back at the Cosmic Weather page for the New Moon/Eclipse report to be posted by 11.1.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Cosmic Quote of the Week (as if spoken by your own Higher Self.) ~ Im here. I love you. I dont care if you need to stay up crying all night long, I will stay with you. Theres nothing you can ever do to lose my love. I will protect you until you die, and after your death I will still protect you. I am stronger than depression and I am braver than loneliness and nothing will ever exhaust me.  Elizabeth Gilbert  Eat. Pray. Love.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Have a great week ahead friends and remember to keep it Cosmic!
          </p>
