         <div class="thecontent" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           You cant win em all, and Ive just lost one. New matte lip stains like
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/first-look-loreal-glam-matte-lip-glosses/" target="_blank">
            LOreal Glam Matte
           </a>
           sand
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/bourjois-rouge-velvet-edition-lipsticks-review-swatches/" target="_blank">
            Bourjois Rouge Velvets
           </a>
           are rocking my world, so I picked up a Sleek Matte Me Lip Cream in the hopes that itd be similarly brillzo.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Sleek-Matte-Me-Lip-Cream-Petal.jpg">
            <img alt="Sleek Matte Me Lip Cream Petal" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1516" height="375" src="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Sleek-Matte-Me-Lip-Cream-Petal-1024x768.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
           <span id="more-1515">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Sleek-Matte-Me-Petal-Lip-Cream.jpg">
            <img alt="Sleek Matte Me Petal Lip Cream" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1517" height="375" src="http://johnitsonlymakeup.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Sleek-Matte-Me-Petal-Lip-Cream-1024x768.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
           <!--more-->
          </p>
          <p>
           Petal,
           <span style="color: #777777;">
            6.49 via Boots, is a baby pink with a chalky matte finish. It highlights dryness and devours what little moisture is left. So, yeah, no. Sleek Matte Me Lip Cream aint happening.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://twitter.com/ItsCherrySue" target="_blank">
            Cherry Sue
           </a>
           fixes this by mixing in a balm, but Im never bothered with making a product work when I have others that perform with no hassle.
          </p>
          <p>
           Theres not much more waffling to be done  it feels uncomfortable, it looks bad and it let everyone down. Weh.
          </p>
