        sophisticated elegance from percy handmade
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">

         </a>
         behind the stunning brand
         <a href="http://www.percyhandmade.com/" target="_blank">
          Percy Handmade
         </a>

          From Tania 
         </em>
         This collection is all about sophisticated elegance  its for women who appreciate vintage and traditional detailing within a refined and classic aesthetic. I am endlessly inspired by modern women who love beautiful fabrics, feminine glamour, and most importantly, exquisite attention to detail.

        </p>
        <p>
         Percy Handmades newest collection includes feminine silk flowers, simple yet sophisticated veils that can be worn with or without a headpiece, a fabulous new take on the bridal halo, and of course  (my favorites)  stunning
         <a href="http://burnettsboards.com/2013/06/percy-handmade-bridal-accessories/" target="_blank">
          crystal statement
         </a>
         headbands.
        </p>
        <p>
         True to her name, all of Tanias pieces are handmade and they can be customized to match your gown, hairstyle, and jewelry.
