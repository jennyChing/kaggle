          <h3 class="entry-header">
           "You're going to Sh*t funny for a week"
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             "umm......what?"
            </p>
            <p>
             "You're probably going to shit funny for a week. You are changing your diet for the first time, your body is going to adjust accordingly."
            </p>
            <p>
             Makes perfect sense, but he had never even considered it. I read diet and workout advice all the time that's catered for beginners, and not a one mentions "By the way, you are going to start shitting funny until your body gets use to it."
            </p>
            <p>
             Common knowledge is only shared through the community that is invested in it. With new employees, new jobs, or new relationships, missing some basic information can have terrible effects.
            </p>
            <p>
             Also, be careful of colloquialism. I met a Korean gentleman today who thought people were literallydefecating bananas in America because his boss told him "My shit is bananas".
            </p>
