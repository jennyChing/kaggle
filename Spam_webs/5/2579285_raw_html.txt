      <h1 class="title">
       Stylish Investment: Introducing the Kwanpen Signature Raffles
      </h1>
      <p class="post-meta">
       <span class="byline">
        By Staff Writer
       </span>
       |
       <span class="pubDate">
        Tuesday, 26 June 2012
       </span>
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="with-sidebar lazyload news-view" id="content">
      <div class="post">
       <div class="post_body single">
        <p>
         <img alt="Timeless Investment Introducing The Signature Raffles By Kwanpen 1" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/06/timeless-investment-introducing-the-signature-raffles-by-kwanpen_1.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p class="caption">
         The Kwanpen Signature Raffles, offered in sizes to suit every occasion
        </p>
        <p>
         When all you need is a polished bag with lovingly treated skin that speaks for itself, heritage leather-maker Kwanpen's Raffles collection is for you. A signature for the label since 1991, the bag's classic. structured silhouette and spacious interior make it an elegant piece that you can pass down to your daughter and granddaughters.
        </p>
        <p>
         Offered in various sizes - the Voyager would make a great weekender - the Kwanpen Raffles is crafted from top-notch exotic skin sourced from eco-sustainable farms, and deliberately kept free of superfluous hardware, so you can better appreciate the beauty of the scales and the skill it took for Kwanpen's artisans to dye the skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you're looking for timeless bags and other accoutrements made with the finest exotic skins - and guys, there's also an extensive collection for men that's to-die-for - head over to
         <a href="http://www.kwanpen.com%20" target="_blank">
          Kwanpen
         </a>
         or browse through the label's photo albums on their
         <a href="http://www.facebook.com/Kwanpen1938" target="_blank">
          Facebook page
         </a>
         . Have fun drawing up a shopping wishlist!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Timeless Investment Introducing The Signature Raffles By Kwanpen 6" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/06/timeless-investment-introducing-the-signature-raffles-by-kwanpen_6.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p class="caption">
         Raffles B Boxie, a more structured, formal option
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.kwanpen.com" target="_blank">
          Kwanpen
         </a>
        </p>
