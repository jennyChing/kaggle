         <p>
          <a href="http://fitnessfashionista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10-products-10-lbs-down.jpg">
           <img alt="10 products 10 pounds down" class="aligncenter wp-image-3458 size-full" height="914" src="http://fitnessfashionista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10-products-10-lbs-down.jpg" width="810"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I wrote in my
          <a href="http://fitnessfashionista.com/2014/07/21/living-a-more-active-life/" title="Living a More Active Life">
           update post about
          </a>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           being 10 pounds down
          </span>
          and I wanted to share with you all 10 products that helped me drop 10 pounds. I would also love to hear about any products that you all use that have been helpful to your journey to stay fit or lose the weight.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://fitnessfashionista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10pounds-10-products.jpg">
           <img alt="10 pounds down using these 10 products" class="aligncenter wp-image-3459 size-full" height="1000" src="http://fitnessfashionista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10pounds-10-products.jpg" width="1000"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://fitnessfashionista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10-pounds-10-products-2.jpg">
           <img alt="lose weight with water" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3461" height="600" src="http://fitnessfashionista.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/10-pounds-10-products-2.jpg" width="800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1z0eZdZ">
           <strong>
            Fitbit Flex
           </strong>
          </a>
           if you all have been following my Give it 100 series then you will know that I use my fitbit flex to track my daily activity. I love the fitbit flex because I am able to pull up my daily steps and calories burned right from my smartphone.  The fitbit flex is a great way to see how you stack up day to day and how you stack up against your friends and their daily activity.  If you are looking for a gadget to keep you motivated and help you drop a few pounds this is the ideal product for you.
         </p>
         <p>
          2.
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1udZ38P">
           <strong>
            CAP Weighted Barbells
           </strong>
          </a>
           I first discovered these at a gym I was going to and realized that I need some of these at my own house. I couldnt be happier. I currently have a 10 and 20 pound bar that I use with my flat bench to get in my weight training 3 days a week. I contribute these to much of my weight loss because lean muscle burns more calories than fat.  They come in different colors depending on the weight of the bar. They are sturdy and take up a lot less space than having conventional barbells and plates all over the house.
         </p>
         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1o9rNuI">
           <strong>
            CAP dumbbells
           </strong>
          </a>
           Certain exercises require dumbbells and I love CAP products so I have a few sets of CAP dumbbells to execute certain exercises. CAP makes exceptional workout equipment and dumbbells should be included in any of your weight training exercises.
         </p>
         <p>
          4.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://workoutlabs.com/">
            Workout Labs
           </a>
          </strong>
           This is a
          <strong>
           free
          </strong>
          site where you can create workout plans and they have detailed explanations of each exercise along with excellent illustrations to help you execute them properly. I create all of my workout plans using this site and then I print them out so that I know what exercises to do each day.
         </p>
         <p>
          5.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://amzn.to/1nzUqlT">
            Scosche Heart Rate Monito
           </a>
           r
          </strong>
           Once I got really serious about losing the weight I knew that I needed a heart rate monitor to accurately gauge how many calories I had burned during my workout sessions.  I researched ones that would work with my Fitbit and MyFitness Pal and the Scosche Heart Rate Monitor works with everything. It was a clear choice. It works with your smartphone to track your heart rate. Then it syncs with all of the other apps that you maybe using as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          6.
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1r62RDw">
           <strong>
            Skinny Rules
           </strong>
          </a>
           I have said this time and time again this is the best book if you are looking to lose weight. Bob Harper from the Biggest Loser gives clear and precise rules to help you drop the weight and keep it off. I love how easy the book is to read and how clear and easy the rules are to follow and incorporate into your daily life. He also breaks down ways to slowly ease into the new lifestyle set forth.
         </p>
         <p>
          7.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://amzn.to/WB3J9X">
            VitaFusion B Complex
           </a>
          </strong>
           I do tons of research before using any type of supplements. One thing I knew that I needed more of was B complex because its provides me with the energy to power through my workouts and to up my metabolism. I love the VitaFusion gummies because its the only sweet I get during the day so its like a treat to take my gummies daily.
         </p>
         <p>
          8.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://amzn.to/1kbmI5Q">
            Phillips Fiber Gummies
           </a>
          </strong>
           Fiber is essential for helping keep your appetite under control. Fiber supplements have been proven in helping people lose weight because of the effect it has on making you feel full. Fiber also has many other benefits some of which you can find
          <a href="http://articles.mercola.com/sites/articles/archive/2013/11/25/9-fiber-health-benefits.aspx" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          9.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://amzn.to/1ue16JY">
            Six Star Professional Strength Whey Isolate Elite Series
           </a>
          </strong>
           I have tried many different types of protein but I have found this kind has been the best because it has the highest amount of protein I have found. Whey Isolate has 60g of protein.
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Adults in the U.S. are encouraged to get 10% to 35% of their days calories from protein foods. Thats about 46 grams of protein for women, and 56 grams of protein for men. High protein diets can help tame your hunger helping you lose those extra pounds. Want to learn more about the power of protein you can check out this great
           <a href="http://www.shape.com/blogs/weight-loss-coach/double-your-protein-lose-weight-while-maintaining-muscle" target="_blank">
            article
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          10.
          <strong>
           Water
          </strong>
           No link just head to your kitchen and turn on your faucet or to your fridge and grab a bottle. Water is the one of the top contributors to my weight loss. Bob Harper talks about it in his book the Skinny Rules and everyone talks about the power of water in suppressing your appetite and aiding in keeping you full longer so you eat less.  Many experts say that most people confuse thirst for hunger so staying hydrated can help curb what you think is your body telling you that your hungry.  You should try to drink a glass of water before, during and after every meal to help you cut down on your portion sizes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What are some of the products that you swear by for your weight loss and fitness needs?
          </strong>
         </p>
