        <p>
         <a href="http://www.dumblittleman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Best-Year-Ever.jpg">
          <img alt="Best Year Ever" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11400" height="473" src="http://www.dumblittleman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Best-Year-Ever-715x473.jpg" width="715"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Its the time of year when goals are made and abandoned a few weeks later. Will you be one of them, or will you set goals that have the power to change your life?
        </p>
        <p>
         Not achieving what you set out to do means youll yet again fail to move toward the life you desire, which leads to nothing but regret. Inertia can be difficult to overcome, but something you may overlook is the influence from friends, parents, and society in general.
        </p>
        <p>
         Often what you believe you want is not what you want at all.
        </p>
        <div style="margin-left:-7px;float:left;padding-right:10px;margin-top:-5px;overflow:hidden;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- DLM 336 Article Top -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-2684800653163841" data-ad-slot="5946187531" style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        The trick lies in setting meaningful objectives.
        <div class="fullpost">
         It doesnt matter whether you set goals or not, as long as youre doing something that makes
         <span style="font-style: italic">
          you
         </span>
         come alive.
         <br/>
         <b>
         </b>
         <b>
         </b>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <span>
            Stay Here and Now
           </span>
           <br/>
           Before you decide what you want to do, stay present here and now. Forget about the past and the future. Let any and all thoughts pass. Dont give them meaning, and dont add to them.
          </li>
         </ol>
        </div>
        <p>
         It is from this blank, calm state that you can begin to look at what has gone well last year, and what hasnt.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span>
           Eliminate
          </span>
          <br/>
          The big mistake people make is they think more is better, but that isnt always the case. The more things you do, the less time you have. You want to make sure that every single thing in your life is something youve consciously chosen.
          <span style="font-size: 14px;line-height: 1.5em">
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Its easy to fall into the trap of doing things that distract you from what you really want to do, because youre afraid.
        </p>
        <p>
         Instead of letting distractions take over your life, look at what you can stop doing. Pick just one thing right now. Keep it simple, and then eliminate it from your life. It could be something small, such as a magazine subscription, or something bigger,
         <a href="http://www.dumblittleman.com/2008/05/how-to-kill-your-addictions-to-junk.html">
          like fast food
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <b>
           <span>
            Discriminate
           </span>
          </b>
          <br/>
          You have to be vigilant about what you let into your life.
          <a href="http://www.dumblittleman.com/2011/06/learning-to-say-no-6-simple-tips-to-do.html">
           Learn to say no
          </a>
          and learn to
          <a href="http://www.dumblittleman.com/2011/04/how-to-find-time-for-yourself.html">
           respect your own time
          </a>
          . You dont always have to say yes to friends and family. First, think about what you want to do. Otherwise youll end up
          <a href="http://www.dumblittleman.com/2011/12/whose-goals-are-you-really-chasing.html">
           living someone elses life
          </a>
          .
          <span style="font-size: 14px;line-height: 1.5em">
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         If you complain about
         <a href="http://www.dumblittleman.com/2009/06/3-critical-time-management-techniques.html">
          not having enough time
         </a>
         , you probably need to get clearer about what you want from life. And by clear, I mean really, really clear. Focus on ONE main thing at a time.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span>
           <b>
            Goals
           </b>
          </span>
          <br/>
          Next, set one big goal for the year. And by goal, I dont necessarily mean regular goal setting. Set a direction for where you want to go.I simply use the concept of goals to get a point across. Goal-setting is just a process that gets you results. What matters are the results; many get stuck on the process, but the process doesnt matter.Use whatever feels good, and take action.
          <span style="font-weight: bold">
           Start
          </span>
          <span style="font-weight: bold">
           now
          </span>
          .
          <span style="font-size: 14px;line-height: 1.5em">
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span>
           <b>
            Focus (on Your Next Step)
           </b>
          </span>
          <br/>
          When you have one big goal, its time to look at what your next step is. What can you do right now to move closer to that goal?
          <span style="font-size: 14px;line-height: 1.5em">
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Keep the next step as tiny as possible. It could be brainstorming ideas and action steps. Whatever it is, start now. Yes, you can put it off, but if you do that, youre putting off your life. If you make it a habit, youll end up living a mediocre life.
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you really want that?
        </p>
        <p>
         Living an extraordinary life takes effort, at least at first, which is why most people turn it down.
         <span style="font-size: 130%">
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 130%">
           <b>
            Momentum
           </b>
          </span>
          <br/>
          When you focus on taking one step at a time, you build momentum. You take one step, then the next, and then the next. It eliminates overwhelm, because you dont have to try and predict the future.
          <span style="font-size: 14px;line-height: 1.5em">
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Know where youre going, and keep taking the next step. Let the rest take care of itself.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span>
           <b>
            The Most Important Secret
           </b>
          </span>
          <br/>
          Last, but not least, remember to focus on what matters to you. Forget about what others think you should or shouldnt do. This is your life, and you are the one who has to live it.
          <span style="font-size: 14px;line-height: 1.5em">
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         If someone thinks you have to do something that you dont want, forget about them. If they try to push you to do it, eliminate them from your life. I know, easier said than done!
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres no one you have to put up with, and nothing you have to do, except follow what feels right for you. Always remember that, because that is what will help you create results that matter and make this year the best one of your life.
        </p>
