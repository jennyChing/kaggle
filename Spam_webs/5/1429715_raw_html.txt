           <p>
            Stacy Keibler showed off her growing baby belly in a long maxi dress while out shopping in Los Angeles with a friend on Tuesday(June 24).
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/06/24/mom-to-be-stacy-keibler-shops-in-la/mom-to-be-stacy-keibler-shops-in-la-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-89962">
             <img alt="Mom-To-Be Stacy Keibler Shops in LA" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-89962" height="750" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/Mom-To-Be-Stacy-Keibler-Shops-in-LA--500x750.jpg" width="500"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Currently in her third trimester
            <a href="http://celebritybabies.people.com/2014/06/18/stacy-keibler-pregnant-healthy-popchips-bbq-happy-hour/" target="_blank">
             Stacy recently told People.com
            </a>
            she is having an easy pregnancy.
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             I havent had one day where Ive been sick or tired, the mom-to-be revealed noting that her healthy lifestyle has likely played a big part.
            </p>
            <p>
             Ive had so much energy, but Im very conscious of making sure I work out every day and eating healthy.
            </p>
            <p>
             I try to be as organic as possible, especially with food. I not only try to eat organic, but no preservatives, nothing artificial, she explains.
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/06/24/mom-to-be-stacy-keibler-shops-in-la/mom-to-be-stacy-keibler-steps-out-in-la/" rel="attachment wp-att-89963">
             <img alt="Mom-To-Be Stacy Keibler steps out in LA" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-89963" height="750" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/Mom-To-Be-Stacy-Keibler-steps-out-in-LA-500x750.jpg" width="500"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            In August Stacy and husband Jared Pobre will welcome a baby girl. With the nursery done, the mom-to-be is now focused on stocking her little ladys closet!
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             Its funny because I was actually with Rebecca Minkoff, whos having a girl too  and we were talking about cute  little girl things, she says. I cant wait to get a little Rebecca Minkoff leather jacket!
            </p>
            <p>
             Adding, Im so excited, especially [because] a lot of my friends that are designers [are] talking about things theyve done for little girls clothing and I just cant wait.
            </p>
           </blockquote>
           <p>
            After spending the afternoon shopping on Robertson Boulevard its possible she is well on her way!
           </p>
