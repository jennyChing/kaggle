             <p>
              As I mentioned previously, my mother-in-law
              <a href="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/blogs/sharon-devellis-inside-scoop/not-a-horticulturist" target="_blank">
               can grow anything
              </a>
              . This woman has more knowledge about plants in her green thumb than I have in my entire body. Every year she starts her seeds indoorsboth flowers and vegetables. Here's the kicker. She doesn't even buy most of her seeds. She saves them from the previous year's plants, dries them and voila. Seeds. I'm pretty sure we've been eating the same family of tomatoes since I married into the family.
             </p>
             <p>
              She also doesn't use fancy pots or gimmicky things to start her seeds. She is a recycler though and through.
             </p>
             <p>
