         <div class="wwsgd" style="display:none;">
          <p style="border:thick double pink; padding:3mm;">
           Welcome to MassholeMommy.com. You should subscribe to my
           <a href="http://massholemommy.com/feed/">
            RSS feed
           </a>
           and follow my
           <a href="http://twitter.com/massholemommy" rel="nofollow">
            Twitter
           </a>
           . You can also have new entries
           <a href="http://feedburner.google.com/fb/a/mailverify?uri=massholemommy/uTYa" rel="nofollow">
            delivered directly to your inbox
           </a>
           . Thanks for visiting!
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Last weekend my nine year old was invited to play laser tag at
          <a href="http://lazergate.com/" rel="nofollow">
           Lazer Gate
          </a>
          in Fall River, MA for a classmates birthday.
         </p>
         <p>
          Fall River is one of those cities with way too many one way streets and with a detour on top of it, we had a super fun time trying to find the place (insert eye roll here). Finally Lance, my GPS, told me that I had arrived at the address I plugged in, but as I was sitting in front of this creepy, old factory  I had my doubts. Emphasis on the creepy and old in the last sentence.
         </p>
         <p>
          Anywho  I drove around the back and was somewhat relieved to see the Lazer Gate sign.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-creepy-building.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-creepy-building" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21763" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-creepy-building.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-21758">
          </span>
          I mean, seriously, half of the windows on the building were blown out. I bet more than one persons nightmares have come true in that creepy place.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-building-2.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-building-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21761" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-building-2.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So anyways, we park and go inside. To my delight, I found out that the laser tag place was up on the 4th floor and I got to take this rickety, old elevator up. Emphasis on the rickety and old in the last sentence.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-old-elevator.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-old-elevator" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21766" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-old-elevator.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I would have
          <em>
           gladly
          </em>
          taken the stairs to avoid the possibility of getting stuck in that elevator, but there was no staircase to be found. Fire hazard anybody?
         </p>
         <p>
          Anyways, I decided to bring my six year old with me and pay for him to play. He wasnt invited to the party, but I knew hed have fun playing laser tag for the first time  so he came along. And that meant I had to
          <del>
           drag
          </del>
          bring Chris to stay with him because I was going in with my oldest to the party. It was no problem, though, because they have a nice arcade and a decent snack bar.
         </p>
         <p>
          The prices for tokens arent outrageous, either (Im comparing to places like Chuck E Cheese here). They were 4 tokens for $1.00. so for $5 Chris kept the little man busy for an hour while I was in the party with my oldest.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-chris-games.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-chris-games" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21762" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-chris-games.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          As part of the party, my nine year old got to play two games of laser tag. I only bought a ticket for the little guy for the second game. Hes a peanut and I knew the equipment was going to weigh him down a little. It did and I was glad I only had him do one game.
         </p>
         <p>
          They pipe fog into the laser tag room, so my pictures arent great, but here they are anyways:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-boys-with-guns.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-boys-with-guns" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21760" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-boys-with-guns.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-boys-going-in.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-boys-going-in" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21759" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-boys-going-in.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-smoke.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-smoke" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21767" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-smoke.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-more-boys.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-more-boys" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21765" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-more-boys.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The game only lasted about 20 minutes and not that I was surprised in the least, my six year old came in dead last. He didnt mind, though. He had a blast and LOVED being included with his big brother and some of his friends.
         </p>
         <p>
          Before we left, they played a few more games with a couple of friends to use up some leftover tokens we had.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-fruit-ninja.jpg">
           <img alt="lazer-gate-fall-river-fruit-ninja" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21764" height="453" src="http://massholemommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/lazer-gate-fall-river-fruit-ninja.jpg" width="453"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Want to check it out? Lazer Gate is located at 288 Plymouth Ave in Fall River, Ma.
         </p>
