        <div class="title-meta">
         <h1>
          Top 10 Documentaries For Creatives That Will Inspire &amp; Inform
         </h1>
        </div>
        <div class="blog-entry">
         <div class="blog-content">
          <p>
           As SCGs senior art director, I try to incorporate design into as many aspects of my life as I can. This includes my personal life as well as our agencys B2B marketing and B2C marketing programs. One way I get my creative juices flowing and find inspiration is through watching documentaries about people just like mepeople who love art and design. So Ive created a list of my top 10 documentaries for creatives that will inspire and inform. The best part of this list? Many of these films can be found on Netflix! Read on.
          </p>
          <h2>
           #1 Art &amp; Copy
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/hLfvmiB4edI?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          Art &amp; Copy tells the story about advertising and inspiration by revealing the work and wisdom of some of the most influential advertising creatives of our time. Some creatives featured in this documentary were responsible for campaigns we all know, such as Just Do It, Got Milk and more.
          <h2>
           #2 Helvetica
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/wkoX0pEwSCw?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          This documentary is about typography, graphic design and global visual culture and looks at the many ways type affects our lives.
          <h2>
           #3 Objectified
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/S9E2D2PaIcI?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          Objectified uncovers the complex relationship people have with manufactured objects and, by extension, the people who design these products.
          <h2>
           #4 Milton Glaser: To Inform and Delight
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/zH-o1r7gYgc?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          For many, Milton Glaser is the personification of American graphic design and is best known for co-founding New York Magazine and the popular I
          <span style="vertical-align: baseline;font-size:0;line-height:26px;background-image:url(http://static.ak.fbcdn.net/rsrc.php/v2/ym/x/JeLhH4gSUuu.png);background-position:-224px 0px;height:16px;width:16px;display:inline-block" title="">
           
          </span>
          NY campaign.
          <h2>
           #5 Eames: The Architect and The Painter
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/_YMzmuBBBzo?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          This film features the husband-and-wife team of Charles and Ray Eames, who are widely regarded as Americas most important designers and best remembered for their mid-century plywood and fiberglass furniture.
          <h2>
           #6 Visual Acoustics: The Modernism of Julius Shulman
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/DUQErQtVI04?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          Narrated by Dustin Hoffman, Visual Acoustics celebrates the life and career of Julius Shulman, the worlds greatest architectural photographer, whose images brought modern architecture to the American mainstream.
          <h2>
           #7 Jean-Michel Basquiat: The Radiant Child
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/9Om2d2CxaQw?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          In his short career, Jean-Michel Basquiat was a phenomenon. He became notorious for his graffiti art in the late 1970s and through his friendship with Andy Warhol.
          <h2>
           #8 The Cool School
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/kDRcXgdiZtQ?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          This film is about how a few renegade artists built an art scene from scratch in Los Angeles that has forever changed the citys culture.
          <h2>
           #9 Beautiful Losers
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/JyRAHKTy6hI?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          Beautiful Losers celebrates the creative spirit behind one of the most influential cultural movements of the 1990s that, ever since, has been transforming pop culture.
          <h2>
           #10 Exit Through the Gift Shop
          </h2>
          <p>
           <!-- Start Shortcoder content -->
          </p>
          <div class="video">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/oHJBdDSTbLw?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </div>
          <!-- End Shortcoder content -->
          This film tells the incredible true story of how an eccentric shop-keeper-turned-documentary-maker attempts to locate Banksy (a mysterious graffiti artist with a global reputation), only to have the artist turn the camera back on its owner with spectacular results.
          <p>
           And I must give an honorable mention to
           <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fpdM_Fj5Cms" target="_blank">
            Herb &amp; Dorothy
           </a>
           , a documentary about two middle-class collectors of contemporary art who end up with an enormous and valuable collection.
          </p>
          <p>
           Mark your calendars! Im also very excited about this documentary that is coming soon to your area:
           <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E63ytv8lciQ" target="_blank">
            Design &amp; Thinking.
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Ive enjoyed every one of these documentaries and highly recommend them! Please let me know what you think about these films or share one of your favorites!
          </p>
