        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         10 Apples Up on Top Math Activity for Kids
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-03-10T09:51:20+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          March 10, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://mylittleme.com/author/jennifer-dickison/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jennifer Dickison
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://mylittleme.com/10-apples-up-on-top-math-activity-for-kids/#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input data-essbishovercontainer="" data-essbispostcontainer="" data-essbisposttitle="10 Apples Up on Top Math Activity for Kids" data-essbisposturl="http://mylittleme.com/10-apples-up-on-top-math-activity-for-kids/" type="hidden" value=""/>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         We have had a lot of fun with this 10 Apples Up on Top math activity. Its amazing how easy it is to have a little fun and learn at the same time with only a printed piece of paper and some little pom poms.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="10 Apples Up on Top Math Activity for Kids - My Little Me" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16889" height="1024" src="http://mylittleme.com/images/2015/03/10-Apples-Up-on-Top-Math-Activity-640x1024.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         For this activity, we first spent some time reading the book 10 Apples Up on Top by Dr. Seuss. Its a fun little book that teaches counting, kindness and silliness all in one. You can find it at your local library or order it on Amazon ( Click the picture to visit my affiliate link).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0394800192/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0394800192&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=mylime-20&amp;linkId=UWBOSWENADRNWPRX" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0394800192/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0394800192&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=mylime-20&amp;linkId=UWBOSWENADRNWPRX', '');">
          <img alt="" border="0" class=" aligncenter" src="http://ws-na.amazon-adsystem.com/widgets/q?_encoding=UTF8&amp;ASIN=0394800192&amp;Format=_SL250_&amp;ID=AsinImage&amp;MarketPlace=US&amp;ServiceVersion=20070822&amp;WS=1&amp;tag=mylime-20"/>
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=mylime-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=0394800192" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         I created this free printable math activity to go along with the book. (Feel free to use it without the book!)
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         10 Apples Up on Top Math Activity
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         You can use the worksheet however you like. We use it in a couple of different ways.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="10 apples up oacn top math activity my little me" class="aligncenter wp-image-16890" height="463" src="http://mylittleme.com/images/2015/03/10-apples-up-on-top-math-activity.jpg" width="599"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Find the number
         </strong>
          Call out a number and have the child place a pom pom apple in the corresponding column. This teaches number recognition and counting as they stack each apple one on top of the other, counting to the number they are given.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Ten Apples Math Activity" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16894" height="922" src="http://mylittleme.com/images/2015/03/Ten-Apples-Math-Activity.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Cover All
         </strong>
          My daughter wanted to cover it all. So we covered and counted each column until the paper was full. This activity teaches counting by starting from one each time.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Math Activity for Kids" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16895" height="400" src="http://mylittleme.com/images/2015/03/Math-Activity-for-Kids.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Im sure there are a few other ways you can use this sheet. Just get creative! And let me know in the comments if you think of a new way!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         You can print the 10 Apples Up on Top Math Activity for free here:

        </p>
