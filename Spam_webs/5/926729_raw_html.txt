          <h2>
           <em>
            Where good food is synonymous with good life
           </em>
          </h2>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.charmingitaly.com/destinations/emilia-romagna" title="Emilia Romagna Hotels">
            Emilia
           </a>
           is the only region in the world which takes its name from the roman Emilia way. It was built in 187 B.C. from
           <em>
            Emilio Marco Lepido
           </em>
           with the aim of realizing a speed and comfortable way for the transit of the Roman army and the control of the territories of the colonies. But these 250 km in a straight line from Piacenza to Rimini still represent
           <strong>
            <em>
             much more than a route
            </em>
           </strong>
           : it is an intersection of different people, a transit point for trade from North to South or, in just one word, the 
           <em>
            Mater Regionis
           </em>
            which has signed the history, the identity and the spirit of this exuberant and amiable land.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Nowadays what does a journey in Emilia mean?
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.charmingitaly.com/sites/charmingitaly.com/files/u341/foto1-emilia1.png">
            <span class="art-img">
             <img alt="Emilia Romagna: Good food, good life!" height="400" src="http://www.charmingitaly.com/sites/charmingitaly.com/files/u341/foto1-emilia1.png" width="550"/>
             <span class="dida">
              Emilia Romagna: Good food, good life!
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <em>
            Photo Credits
           </em>
           :
           <a href="http://www.carradicasatico.com/" target="_blank">
            Carra di Casatico
           </a>
           <br/>
           First of all a long glance at the
           <em>
            Po valley
           </em>
           which seems to go on forever until some undefined shapes of hills come into view,
           <em>
            <strong>
             quite unexpected
            </strong>
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Secondly,
           <strong>
            Emilia
           </strong>
           matches perfectly with the expectation of
           <a href="http://www.charmingitaly.com/destinations" title="Holiday in Italy">
            a classic holiday in Italy
           </a>
           : century-old cities with fine architecture, gently rolling landscapes potted with picturesque villages,
           <a href="http://www.charmingitaly.com/" title="Italian Lifestyle and Italian Holidays">
            charming accommodations and lively cultural events
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Emilia
           </strong>
           immediately offers to the visitor all its creativity and dynamism: a flow of fields which come and go, with small rivers in between, and a good balance among man and nature, agriculture and entrepreneurs. In this orchestra of different aspects made of tradition and experience, the old vocation of farmers has been preserved as primary resource: this makes
           <em>
            <strong>
             Emilia the temple of Italian gastronomy
            </strong>
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.charmingitaly.com/sites/charmingitaly.com/files/u341/emilia1.jpg">
            <span class="art-img">
             <img alt="Emilia Romagna Food and Wine " height="400" src="http://www.charmingitaly.com/sites/charmingitaly.com/files/u341/emilia1.jpg" width="550"/>
             <span class="dida">
              Emilia Romagna Food and Wine
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <em>
            Photo Credits
           </em>
           :
           <a href="http://it.fotolia.com/" target="_blank">
            Fotolia
           </a>
           <br/>
           And this is for sure
           <em>
            another wonderful reason
           </em>
           : the
           <strong>
            gastronomical excellences
           </strong>
           officially identified are five and their production secrets have been tabled by
           <em>
            Bologna Chamber of Commerce
           </em>
           and drawn up by the
           <em>
            Italian Academy of Cuisine
           </em>
           :
           <em>
            <strong>
             traditional balsamic vinegar, tagliatelle, rag, Parmigiano Reggiano and culatello
            </strong>
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           We could even say they are actually
           <em>
            part of mans heritage
           </em>
           !
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Winding around the whole region, many different 
           <strong>
            Food and Wine Trails and Routes
           </strong>
            (indicated by specific panels) cross the hearth of this florid and generous region and invite you to stop in small farms, wine cellars, traditional workshops or trattorie spread in the country side where genuine products come from: all year round opportunities to taste fine eating.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.charmingitaly.com/sites/charmingitaly.com/files/u341/foto-emilia3.png">
            <span class="art-img">
             <img alt="Emilia Romagna: Good Food, Good Life!" height="400" src="http://www.charmingitaly.com/sites/charmingitaly.com/files/u341/foto-emilia3.png" width="550"/>
             <span class="dida">
              Emilia Romagna: Good Food, Good Life!
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            Photo Credits
           </em>
           :
           <a href="http://www.italiainfoto.com/" target="_blank" title="Italia in foto">
            Italia in foto
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Let yourself be tempted by all possible gastronomic delicacies!
            </strong>
           </em>
           <br/>
           But do not forget to look beyond all this
           <br/>
           In this quiet and relaxing atmosphere, if you are a curious and busy traveller, try out the institutional sites and discover a colourful place where art, music and food are linked and where the philosophy of
           <em>
            <strong>
             good living
            </strong>
           </em>
           is firmly related to
           <em>
            <strong>
             good food
            </strong>
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <br/>
