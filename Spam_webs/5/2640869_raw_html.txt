       <h2>
        Christian Gamers Code
       </h2>
       <p>
        The Christian Gamer is a believer in Jesus Christ as the way to eternal salvation (
        <a href="http://christiananswers.net/bible/john3.html#16" target="_blank" title="John 3:16">
         John 3:16
        </a>
        ). As a Christian, responsible to God for my actions and thoughts, I promise to choose my games carefully. My goal is to be a good witness in my purchasing decisions, in my game play with others, and in the use of my time.
       </p>
