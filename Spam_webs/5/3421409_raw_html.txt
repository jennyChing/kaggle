          </p>

           <a href="http://skinny-teatox.com/">
            teatox
           </a>
           by now. A teatox is a
           <strong>
            healthy detox tea system
           </strong>

          </p>

           <strong>
            10 reasons
           </strong>
           to try a Skinny Teatox:

             <strong>

             </strong>

            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              You'll feel amazing.
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             Throughout your teatox and after, you will feel great. The daytime tea is a premium green tea with ginseng that gives you energy throughout the day, while helping suppress your appetite.
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             <strong>
              You'll look amazing.
             </strong>
             Teatox's help you lose unwanted weight. The evening tea contains senna leaf, which is a natural mild laxative which cleanses your body from unwanted toxins and excess. Enjoying the evening tea every other day during your teatox leaves you feeling light, full of energy, and ready to take on the world.
             <br/>
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              It turbo charges your metabolism.
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             The combination of our daytime and evening tea boosts your metabolism big time.
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              Skinny Teatox is delicious.
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             Our daytime tea in particular is insanely good quality. We buy only the best premium tea, so every day you'll look forward to your cup of tea.
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              <a href="http://skinny-teatox.com/pages/your-teatox-results" target="_blank">
               You will see results
              </a>
              .
             </span>
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              It's something you do for yourself.
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             Skinny Teatox is something you do just for yourself. Cleansing and detoxifying your body is important and your body will thank you.
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              We deliver fast.
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             While most teatox companies are based in the United Kingdom and Australia, Skinny Teatox is located in Canada, so we ship fast (and free) anywhere in Canada. We also ship fast to the United States, so instead of waiting 2-4 weeks from most companies, buy from Skinny Teatox and start your teatox sooner.
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
              Skinny Teatox costs less.
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">

             <a href="http://skinny-teatox.com/collections/teatox-packages/products/28-day-skinny-teatox">
              28 day teatox
             </a>
             packs with any other reputable company and you'll see that our prices are the best. Don't spend extra money needlessly, buy from Skinny Teatox.
            </span>

            <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
             <strong>
              Skinny Teatox is the
              <a href="http://skinny-teatox.com/collections/teatox-packages">
