     <h1 class="lead">
      Top 4 Moments from the Super Bowl Halftime Show
     </h1>
     <span class="redd">
      <b>
      </b>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div align="left">
       <b>
        <span style="background-color: #E0E0E0">
         Contributed by
         <span class="authorname">
          <a href="http://rapidnewsnetwork.com/author/jmaslow/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jmaslow">
           Jmaslow
          </a>
         </span>
         on February 2, 2015 at 12:19 pm
        </span>
       </b>
      </div>
     </span>
     <p>
      <img alt='Katy Perry at the "The Smurfs 2" Los Angeles Premiere, Village T' class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-7892" height="300" src="http://rapidnewsnetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/bigstock-Katy-Perry-at-the-The-Smurfs-58633490-294x300.jpg" width="294"/>
      Top 4 Moments from the Super Bowl Halftime Show
     </p>
     <p>
      Some people watch the Super Bowl for the game; others for the halftime show. And unless you were living under a rock Sunday, chances are you heard about Katy Perrys insane halftime performance. With lions, sharks, guest performances and fireworks, this years halftime show was one of the best yet. In case you missed it, here are the top 4 moments from last nights show:
     </p>
     <ol>
      <li>
       <strong>
        Katy Perrys Giant Animatronic Lion
       </strong>
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
      Who needs a stage when you have a giant animatronic lion? Riding in on a blinged-out lion, Katy Perry belted out Dark Horse and Roar. Perry said it would be a wild performance, and she definitely delivered on that promise.
     </p>
     <ol start="2">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Lenny Kravitz Kissed a Girl
       </strong>
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
      Okay, so technically Lenny Kravitz didnt kiss a girl on stage, but he did join Katy Perrys performance of her hit single. Kravtiz added a little rock n roll to the pop stars track. And of course, Lenny rocked his signature style  ripped jeans and a black leather jacket.
     </p>
     <ol start="3">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Missy Elliots Surprise Appearance
       </strong>
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
      By the time to halftime show came around, everyone knew about Missy Elliots surprise appearance. Someone let the cat out of the bag days before the game started. But that doesnt mean that her performance wasnt jaw-dropping. Missy performed Work It, Get Ur Freak On, and Lose Control.
     </p>
     <ol start="4">
      <li>
       <strong>
        California Gurls and Dancing Sharks
       </strong>
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
      Katy Perry promised everyone sharks  and sharks is exactly what she brought on stage. Well, sort of. For her performance of California Gurls, Perry sang alongside dancing sharks, palm trees and flying beach balls. But it was her backup dancers that got everyone talking. Dressed in the same polka dot bikinis Taylor Swift was spotted in, fans of the rivaling pop stars saw the gesture as a diss, subtly mocking Swift.
     </p>
     <p>
      Just as Katy Perry made an unforgettable entrance, she made an unforgettable exit too. The pop star flew high above the stage attached to a sparkling shooting star for her performance of Firework.
     </p>
