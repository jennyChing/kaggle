        <p>
         Since my Location 180 began over two years ago, Ive seen a lot of people come and go.
        </p>
        <p>
         Countless people have begun
         <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
          businesses
         </span>
         blogs, only to drop it all 6 months later because they arent sure why they arent making money.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im going to tell you right now that for 95% of the people out there that are trying to start a lifestyle business,
         <em>
          youre doing it all wrong
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         There are two reasons that I believe most people have failed with leaving their job, and beginning to live the life they are searching for.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1. The Four Hour Workweek.
         </strong>
         While I think the book is great, and provided a ton of inspiration, it set people up with unrealistic expectations. Too many people took what Tim said literally, and actually believed they could achieve that lifestyle with little effort. However
         <a href="http://www.seanogle.com/headline/anti-four-hour-workweek" target="_blank" title="The Anti Four Hour Workweek Movement">
          that conversation
         </a>
         has been had a million times already so Ill leave it at that.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2. Affiliate Marketing
         </strong>
         . Dont get me wrong, affiliate marketing can be a great money making strategy. There are people that make a ton of money doing it, and if you do it right, you can too. BUT, this is one of the most difficult ways I know of to create a business.
        </p>
        <p>
         When you combine thousands of people talking about how great the 4 hour work week lifestyle is, and then give them a surefire way to make passive income, its a recipe for disaster.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now youve got a ton of people trying to make millions via niche sites, theyre trying to monetize blogs with very little traffic, and most importantly, they arent even going about it the right way.
        </p>
        <p>
         NOTE: Banners do
         <strong>
          not
         </strong>
         lead to sales, and
         <strong>
          DO
         </strong>
         devalue your message  especially early on.
        </p>
        <p>
         From there they probably put out a half-assed ebook that they had to fake their way through because they havent actually learned anything about garnering attention, building a business, or whatever they are writing about.
        </p>
        <p>
         That may come off as callous, but the fact remains, its true. If your business plan revolves around an ebook, affiliate marketing, or doing very little work, then you need to make some serious changes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now that weve established what
         <em>
          doesnt
         </em>
         work, how
         <em>
          do
         </em>
         you go about building a real business that you can run from anywhere on Earth?
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Step #1: Remove the phrase passive income from your vocabulary
         </li>
         <li>
          Step #2: Learn how to do something that other people cant  and will pay money for.
         </li>
         <li>
          Step #3: Meet as many people as possible  and figure out how you can help (not sell) them.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Thats it.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre serious about building a real business, you need to stop trying to mimic people like
         <a href="http://www.chrisguillebeau.com" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.chrisguillebeau.com']);" target="_blank" title="Chris Guillebeau">
          Chris Guillebeau
         </a>
