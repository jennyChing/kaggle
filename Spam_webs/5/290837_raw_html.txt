          <strong>
           We have seen some strange friendships in our life like the elephant and labrador, but this one is truly a new one.Professional animal photographer
           <a href="http://www.tier-collagen.de/" target="_blank">
            Tanja Brandt
           </a>
           from Germany has found the strangest and most adorable couple to shoot that we have ever seen. Ingo the German Shepherd and Poldi the little owl are best friends and they sure know how to pose for the camera.Its amazing to see that nature has a magnificent way of connecting animals together.Ingo the shepherd has many other animal friends such as fierce birds and of courseother dogs. Brandt loves to create animal photos and enjoys every minute working with all kind of animals. See more photos at
