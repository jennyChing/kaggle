       </h1>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Lexar Professional 2000x SDHC/SDXC UHS-II
        </strong>
        cards come with UHS-II technology (UHS Speed Class 3 (U3)) for high-speed performance. It can dramatically accelerates workflow from start to finish process. If you want super high speed camera memory card for your 4K video, full-HD 1080p video or high resolution photos. Lexar Professional 2000x SDHC/SDXC UHS-II cards let you quickly capture and transfer with a read speed up to 2000x (300MB/s) maximum write speed is 260MB / sec. when used with the included SD UHS-II USB 3.0 compatible reader.

         </strong>
