        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
         Fitness &amp; Lifestyle: Womens Multivitamins
        </h1>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
       </header>
       <div class="clearfix entry-content" itemprop="description articleBody">
        <p>
         Recently a friend of mine, and a qualified PT, went through, very quickly, some nutritional advice for me. This is definitely part of an ongoing thing in my life. Since my injury I have been looking for little things that will make a big impact (cutting out lattes for example and drinking green tea?)
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the first bits of advice said friend gave me was that I should be taking a multivitamin for an active female. I didnt know there was such a thing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://averageadventures.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/multivitamin.jpg">
          <img alt="Average Adventures in Multivitamins" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-749" height="1333" src="http://averageadventures.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/multivitamin.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mission 1 was obviously to locate such a vitamin  I have to admit here that Lincoln, Holland and Barrett and Boots you let me down. Out of all of the options available in Lincoln, after visiting Holland &amp; Barrett and Boots I found only 1 multivitamin for an active female  there were several for men, several for women over 50 and a stock range that was definitely more than required for pregnant women. (Do you get hints of my distaste?)
        </p>
        <p>
         Vitabiotics was my only option and so far I have been taking them for about 5 days. They taste like, how I would imagine, dog food. They are also a larger tablet to take, but I have definitely felt an improvement. Generally I am a 4pm snoozer  this is when I am getting finished with my work day and want to, in a dream world, take a nap. Alas my day continues despite my tiredness, but since taking these multivitamins I really havent felt the desperation (exaggeration) for a nap at 4.
        </p>
        <p>
         I would say that there are aspects of my diet that still need considerable overhaul and so hopefully one day Ill be able to address my 4pm snooze hopes with my nutrition rather than a multivitamin but so far the experience has been a positive one.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://averageadventures.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/toni-may-copy.jpg">
          <img alt="toni may copy" class="alignright wp-image-690 size-full" height="50" src="http://averageadventures.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/toni-may-copy.jpg" width="200"/>
         </a>
        </p>
