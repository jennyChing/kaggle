        <div class="headline">
         <h1>
          Wood Substrates
         </h1>
        </div>
        <!-- START: mod_image170_text_links -->
        <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="type_a">
         <tr>
          <td class="left" valign="top">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="170" src="p/content/ss_prod_ditra_q.tif_rdax_170x170_80.jpg" width="170"/>
          </td>
          <td class="right" valign="top">
           <h2>
            Schluter-DITRA and Schluter-DITRA-XL
           </h2>
           <h3>
            Specifically designed as an underlayment for ceramic and stone tile
           </h3>
           <p>
            Ceramic and stone tiles are durable, easy to maintain, and hygienic, representing the ideal surface coverings. However, todays lightweight construction methods can make the installation of hard surface coverings particularly challenging. In order to protect the integrity of the tile assembly, an underlayment that performs multiple functions is required.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Schluter-DITRA and Schluter-DITRA-XL are specifically designed to allow the installation of ceramic and stone tile over any even and load-bearing substrate. The integration of four functionsuncoupling, waterproofing, load-distribution, and vapor managementmakes consistent results viable through the use of Schluter-DITRA or DITRA-XL.
            <br/>
           </p>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <!-- STOP: mod_image170_text_links -->
        <!-- START: mod_image170_text_links -->
        <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="type_a">
         <tr>
          <td class="left" valign="top">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="170" src="p/content/ss_mat_002_q_rdax_80.jpg" width="170"/>
          </td>
          <td class="right" valign="top">
           <h3>
            Every substrate presents unique challenges
           </h3>
           <p>
            All wood materials, including OSB, plywood, and framing members, are subject to expansion, contraction, bending, and deflection as a result of changes in moisture content and loading. Further, these deformations fluctuate over the life of the building structure.
           </p>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <!-- STOP: mod_image170_text_links -->
        <!-- START: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="type_b">
         <tr>
          <td class="left" valign="top">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="110" src="p/content/ss_trap_uncoupling_001_s_rdax_110x110_75.jpg" width="110"/>
          </td>
          <td class="right" valign="top">
           <h2>
            Uncoupling
           </h2>
           <p>
            Schluter-DITRA/ Schluter-DITRA-XLs uncoupling function protects the ceramic or stone tile covering from the aforementioned deformations by neutralizing the differential movement stresses between the wood structure and the tile, thus eliminating the major cause of cracking and delaminating of the tiled surface.
           </p>
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <!-- STOP: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <!-- START: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="type_b">
         <tr>
          <td class="left" valign="top">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="110" src="p/content/ss_trap_waterproofing_001_s_rdax_110x110_75.jpg" width="110"/>
          </td>
          <td class="right" valign="top">
           <h2>
            Waterproofing
           </h2>
           <p>
            Since wood structures are sensitive to moisture, Schluter-DITRA and Schluter-DITRA-XLs waterproofing function adds an essential element to the flooring assembly by providing simple, effective, and permanent moisture protection.
           </p>
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <!-- STOP: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <!-- START: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="type_b">
         <tr>
          <td class="left" valign="top">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="110" src="p/content/ss_trap_vapor_001_s_rdax_110x110_75.jpg" width="110"/>
          </td>
          <td class="right" valign="top">
           <h2>
            Vapor Management
           </h2>
           <p>
            Wood continually absorbs and releases moisture. The free space beneath the Schluter-DITRA/ Schluter-DITRA-XL matting allows the wood to breathe and provides a route for any residual moisture in the wood substrate to escape.Since wood structures are sensitive to moisture, Schluter-DITRA/ Schluter-DITRA-XLs waterproofing function adds an essential element to the flooring assembly by providing simple, effective, and permanent moisture protection.
           </p>
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <!-- STOP: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <!-- START: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="type_b">
         <tr>
          <td class="left" valign="top">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="110" src="p/content/ss_trap_support_001_s_rdax_110x110_75.jpg" width="110"/>
          </td>
          <td class="right" valign="top">
           <h2>
            Support/ Load Distribution
           </h2>
           <p>
            Since Schluter-DITRA and Schluter-DITRA-XL are virtually incompressible within the tile assembly, the advantages of uncoupling are achieved without sacrificing point load distribution capabilities.
           </p>
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <!-- STOP: mod_image110_text_links -->
        <!-- START: mod_text_links -->
        <div class="type_c">
         <p>
          By addressing all of the challenges associated with todays fast, lightweight construction methods, Schluter-DITRA and Schluter-DITRA-XL provide a durable installation system for ceramic and stone tile over wood substrates.
         </p>
