          <em>
           Rajshahi University Admission Result 2015 , Admission
          </em>
          Notice, Rajshahi University Admission 2015-16 Test Seat Plan 3 will be found here. Rajshahi University Admission Test for undergraduate 1st year honours students 2015-16 will start 19 October 2015 and continued up to 22 October 2015.The admission test of Rajshahi University 2015-16 will be held under eight units- unit A, B, C, D, E, F, G &amp; H.
