      <h1>
       Make a Stylish Statement with Wallpaper
      </h1>
      <p class="meta">
       Posted May 09, 2014 in
       <a href="/daily-features/category/newest-stories">
        Newest Stories
       </a>
       by
       <a href="/daily-features/contributors/dering-hall">
        Dering Hall
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="desc">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/browse/rugs-textiles?category=wall-coverings--2&amp;sort_list=rand">
         Wallpaper
        </a>
        adds luxury, texture and style to any room, whether it is a dining room, bathroom, bedroom or even a living room. The design industry has recently seen a resurgence of popularity for wallpaper, and with that a rich appreciation of the history and art of papering a space. Use a grasscloth to add understated texture, or to make a bold statement opt for a oversized pattern.
       </p>
