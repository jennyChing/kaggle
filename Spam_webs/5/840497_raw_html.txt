        <p class="ember-view" id="ember823" style="text-align: center;">
         A suspected Islamist was holding hostages inside a chocolate shop in Sydney on Monday and police were monitoring posts on Facebook and Twitter as they tried to negotiate a release.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember824" style="text-align: center;">
         Five hostages have run free, but police would not confirm how many were still being held inside the Lindt cafe  where
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         a black flag used by Islamist groups in the Middle East has been shown in the window.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember830" style="text-align: center;">
         Catherine Burn, deputy police commissioner in New South Wales state, wouldnt discuss the gunmans possible motive but said: We are in contact.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember831" style="text-align: center;">
         There is speculation about what he might want but we have to deal him on the level of a police negotiation, Burn told reporters. We are monitoring all forms of communication  Facebook, Twitter  for any information that might assist. Were monitoring what is happening on Facebook  that is forming part of our tactical response.
        </p>
        <div style="text-align:center;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;margin-top:8px;margin-bottom:8px;">
         <style>
          .brainmiddle{width:250px;height:250px}@media(min-width: 180px){.brainmiddle{width:250px;height:250px}}@media(min-width: 300px){.brainmiddle{width:250px;height:250px}}@media(min-width: 500px){.brainmiddle{width:300px;height:250px}}@media(min-width: 800px){.brainmiddle{width:336px;height:280px}}
         </style>
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <ins class="adsbygoogle brainmiddle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-6966664712061957" data-ad-slot="4201650621" style="display:inline-block">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember832" style="text-align: center;">
         The drama began shortly after 9:45 a.m. local time (5:45 p.m. ET Sunday). Lindt Australia Chief Executive Steve Loane told Sky Business there were probably 30 customers in the cafe and 10 workers. Police refused to confirm or speculate on the numbers involved.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember833" style="text-align: center;">
         For those that may be in that building rest assured we are doing all we can to set you free, New South Wales Police Commissioner Andrew Scipione told a news conference.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember834" style="text-align: center;">
         The first hostages to emerge were three men, who ran out of side exits toward armed police. An hour or so later, two women ran out. Authorities wouldnt say whether the hostages had escaped or were freed by the gunman, but they dashed out of the cafe in panic.
        </p>
        <div class="ember-view widget-container inline-video" id="ember838" style="text-align: center;">
         <div class="ember-view" id="ember841">
          <div class="stack-video-inline-meta">
           <div class="video-youvideo">
            <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/ON7PqmGDzzI" width="420">
            </iframe>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember847" style="text-align: center;">
         Our approach is to resolve this peacefully, Burn said. We are working through this methodically to make sure nobody is injured.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember848" style="text-align: center;">
         She added: It is an important part of the negotiations that that we wont talk about tactics or information that is being passed on. Its a well-tested system of negotiation that we use, its world class. It might take a bit of time but safety of the people inside is paramount.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember849" style="text-align: center;">
         Burn urged people in Sydney should remain calm. You should carry on your business as usual.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember869" style="text-align: center;">
         Earlier, Scipione said officers were responding on a footing consistent with a terrorism event.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember870" style="text-align: center;">
         Police closed off nearby streets and evacuated nearby office buildings, making sure we can secure and resolve this matter to a peaceful outcome, Scipione added. The U.S. Consulate in Sydney was evacuated and all U.S. personnel had been accounted for, the State Department told NBC News.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" style="text-align: center;">
         A suspected gunman inside a cafe in Sydney, Australia, on Monday.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember880" style="text-align: center;">
         The Lindt Chocolate Cafe is in Martin Place, a busy tourist, transportation and shopping district home to several major banks  including the Reserve Bank of Australia  as well as the state Parliament. The nearby Sydney Opera House cancelled Monday nights performances.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember881" style="text-align: center;">
         The flag shown in the cafes window as the hostage drama unfolded appeared to be the Tawhid Banner. NBC News terrorism analyst Evan Kohlmann, added that it was frequently used by Jabhat Al-Nusra and other armed Islamist groups in the region  but not ISIS.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember885" style="text-align: center;">
         Chris Kenny, associate editor of the national newspaper The Australian,
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         wrote on the papers website
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         that hed been in the cafe just five minutes before the gunman took over.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember886" style="text-align: center;">
         As police quickly swarmed and cleared the area, I turned to see a man against the window, facing out with his hands raised, Kenny wrote. At first I was relieved, thinking this was the gunman responding to police  but soon came the awful realization that customers were being forced against the windows.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember892" style="text-align: center;">
         Prime Minister Tony Abbott urged Australians to go about their business as usual, saying the whole point of politically motivated violence is to scare people out of being themselves.
        </p>
        <div class="ember-view widget-container inline-video" id="ember894" style="text-align: center;">
         <div class="ember-view" id="ember895">
          <div class="stack-video-inline-meta">
           <h1 class="stack-heading">
            Australian Police Monitoring Social Media to Help Tactical Response
           </h1>
           <div class="video-youvideo">
            <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/TUYccwYq09U" width="420">
            </iframe>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember896" style="text-align: center;">
         Grand Mufti Ibrahim Abu Mohamed, the highest Islamic office holder in Australia, said in a statement that the Muslim community was devastated by the incident, adding that such actions are denounced in part and in whole in Islam.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember897" style="text-align: center;">
         The hostage incident comes after police carried out a series of
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         major anti-terrorism raids
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         throughout Sydney in September. ISIS spokesman Abu Muhammad al-Adnani has also urged Muslims in Australia to carry out lone-wolf attacks against civilians, according to terrorism analysts at the
         <span class="Apple-converted-space">
         </span>
         SITE Intelligence Group.
        </p>
        <p class="ember-view" id="ember898" style="text-align: center;">
         It was one of the biggest security operations in Sydney a January 1984 when Turkish-born man went on a bank robbery spree, taking 11 people hostage and fleeing the scene before being shot dead in a dramatic road chase. In February 1978, a bomb outside the Sydney Hilton killed to garbage truck workers.
        </p>
