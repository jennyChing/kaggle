          <a href="http://a3145z2.americdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/for-all-smokers-how-to-make-a-miracle-elixir-to-clean-your-lungs.jpg">
           <img alt="for-all-smokers-how-to-make-a-miracle-elixir-to-clean-your-lungs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1517 img-responsive" height="517" src="http://a3145z2.americdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/for-all-smokers-how-to-make-a-miracle-elixir-to-clean-your-lungs.jpg" width="656"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The best thing you can do is quit smoking and live longer, but if this is just impossible for you, add these foods to your daily menu.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can also try a super-efficient recipe, and you can find it further in the article.
         </p>

         <p>
          <strong>
           Ginger
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This ancient spice has been long used for its healing effect. Ginger will also help you eliminate the excess mucus in your lungs.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Onion and garlic
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          We already know how healthy garlic and onion are. They both have strong anticancer properties, and they are an excellent prevention against many malignant conditions.
         </p>
         <p>
          Onions are especially efficient in the prevention of different respiratory infections.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Turmeric
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This incredible spice is loaded with vitamins and minerals, and it also contains omega-3 fatty acids.
         </p>
         <p>
          Turmeric has antiviral, antibacterial, and anticancer effects, so do your body a favor and add it to your everyday diet.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Lung cleansing elixir:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Ingredients:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           400 g (14 oz) onion
          </li>
          <li>
           1 l (34 oz) water
          </li>
          <li>
           400 g (14 oz) sugar
          </li>
          <li>
           2 tbsp turmeric
          </li>
          <li>
           ginger, a thumb-sized piece
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Preparation:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Cook the water and sugar together. Add the onion cut in quarters and shredded ginger root. Add the turmeric once the mixture boils, and reduce the heat to low.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cook until the mixture reduces by half, strain and keep it in a jar. Once it cools completely store it in your fridge.
         </p>
         <p>
          Take two tablespoons in the morning before you eat or drink anything and in the evening, two hours after your dinner.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Note:
          </strong>
          Always consult your doctor or pharmaceutist before using any natural remedy.
         </p>
