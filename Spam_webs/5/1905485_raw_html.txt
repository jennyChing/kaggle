         <h1 class="post-title">
          <a href="http://tipsytravel.com/best-bars-clubs-lounges-sandton/" title="Best bars, clubs, and lounges in Sandton">
           Best bars, clubs, and lounges in Sandton
          </a>
         </h1>
         <!-- /post-title -->
         <!-- post-image -->
         <figure class="post-image ">
          <a href="http://tipsytravel.com/best-bars-clubs-lounges-sandton/">
           <img alt="Best bars, clubs, and lounges in Sandton" src="http://tipsytravel.com/wp-content/themes/grido/themify/img.php?src=http://tipsytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Sandton-Lounges.jpg&amp;w=670&amp;h=" width="670"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
         <!-- /post-image -->
         <!-- post-content -->
         <div class="post-content">
          <p>
           If youre traveling to Johannesburg in South Africa, chances are youre going to spend at least part of your time in the beautiful and slightly ritzy Sandton area. This sophisticated neighborhood offers many lovely attractions that are all within walking distance to the Gautrain station, making it an ideal stop during your South African vacation. Couple in a thriving nightlife scene and Sandton is the perfect place to hang out in. But, where should you whet your whistle? Read on for the best clubs, bars, and lounges in Sandton!
           <span id="more-668">
           </span>
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_671" style="width: 560px">
           <img alt="Hotel Bar" class=" wp-image-671 " height="350" src="http://tipsytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Hotel-Bar.jpg" width="550"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            The bar at the hotel in which you stay is usually the place to begin with your drink tour.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           If youre itching for a cocktail, chances are you can head down the elevator at your
           <a href="http://www3.hilton.com/en/hotels/south-africa/hilton-sandton-JNBSATW/index.html" target="_blank" title="Sandton Hotel">
            Sandton hotel
           </a>
           and see what awaits you at your hotel bar. Most of the area hotels are pretty swanky, so their bars are too! We call that a win-win, folks. But you dont want to always just stumble up to your hotel room from the bar now, do you? Luckily Sandton has a bunch of awesome nightlife spots that are perfect for a night out on the town.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_672" style="width: 560px">
           <img alt="VIP Room" class=" wp-image-672 " height="350" src="http://tipsytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/VIP-Room.jpg" width="550"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            The VIP Room is an amazing club right in the middle of the Sandtons business district.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Head out to Icon Club. This sophisticated nightclub is perfect for those who arent still in college anymore. Tastefully decorated with a smart and sexy atmosphere, this club plays a nice selection of music genres throughout the weekend. For something more intimate, check out
           <strong>
            VIP Room
           </strong>
           in the heart of Sandtons business district. This club oozes luxury with an opulent dcor and a wide selection of unique cocktails and premium brands served tableside.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_673" style="width: 560px">
           <img alt="Cocoon Lounge" class=" wp-image-673 " height="350" src="http://tipsytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Cocoon-Lounge.jpg" width="550"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Sandtons Cocoon Lounge.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           For a bar where you can emerge a butterfly, head to
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CocoonJHB" target="_blank" title="Cocoon Lounge">
            Cocoon Lounge
           </a>
           . With a marriage of urban chic and what can only be described as ultra-lounge aesthetics, this champagne and cocktail bar is perfect for mingling with the best of Johannesburg. This bar is definitely a place to see and be seen. Just dont miss out on the scenic view of the Sandton skyline.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_674" style="width: 560px">
           <img alt="Sandton Sun" class=" wp-image-674 " height="350" src="http://tipsytravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Sandton-Sun.jpg" width="550"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            The Sandton Sun is the place to greet the Sun after a long night.
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           For another bar with a breathtaking view, youll want to meet up at
           <a href="http://www.tsogosunhotels.com/deluxe/sandton-sun/dining/pages/overview.aspx#.UlXttBAS6RM" target="_blank" title="San Bar at the Sandton Sun">
            San Bar at the Sandton Sun
           </a>
           . The outdoor deck here is perfect for lounging and taking in the view. If you get too chilly, just grab one of the couches by the huge fire pit. Friday and Saturday nights offer live music to enjoy while youre sipping on your beer, wine, or signature cocktail. Youll be hard-pressed to decide which Sandton bar is your absolute favorite, which is why youll have to check out all of the very best ones!
          </p>

function createCookie(name,value,days) {
  if (days) {
    var date = new Date();
    date.setTime(date.getTime()+(days*24*60*60*1000));
    var expires = "; expires="+date.toGMTString();
  } else {
    var expires = "";
  }
  document.cookie = name+"="+value+expires+"; path=/";
}

function readCookie(name) {
  var nameEQ = name + "=";
  var ca = document.cookie.split(';');
  for(var i=0;i &lt; ca.length;i++) {
    var c = ca[i];
    while (c.charAt(0)==' ') {
      c = c.substring(1,c.length);
    }
    if (c.indexOf(nameEQ) == 0) {
      return c.substring(nameEQ.length,c.length);
    }
  }
  return null;
}

function eraseCookie(name) {
  createCookie(name,"",-1);
