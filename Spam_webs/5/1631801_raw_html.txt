    <h1>
     If You're Over That Gallery Wall, This One's For You
    </h1>
    <img alt="Cover image" class="detail_hero" src="https://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_blog_web_detail_page_full_height,f_auto/https://s3.amazonaws.com/production-ol-images/0d2d250bb900ad0bcdcb1f42c63e932a.jpg"/>
    <div class="byline">
     <a class="author_name" href="/u/shanisilver/">
      <img alt="Shani Silver" class="author_avatar" src="https://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_navatar_hex_trans_large,f_auto/https://s3.amazonaws.com/production-ol-images/566b0fefb8cd18aa25c40781e45c7ca2.jpg"/>
      Shani Silver
     </a>
     <span class="blog_post_date">
      July 23, 2014
 
in
      <a href="/read/decor/">
       Decor
      </a>
     </span>
    </div>
    <div class="body">
     <p>
      Maaaaaybe it's time to try something different. I'm not saying gallery walls aren't cute, but they're cute
      <em>
       all
      </em>
      over Instagram these days. Let's explore our options, and there are a
      <em>
       lot
      </em>
      of options. Your home is uniquely you. Let your walls reflect that. Put the hammer down and keep reading.
     </p>
     <p>
      1) Wallpaper. I don't know if you've looked into this lately, but modern-day wallpaper kicks the crap out of Grandma's guest bathroom. It's even removable, so there's no stress.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Vinyl Wall Sticker Decal Art - Polka Dots" class="k_full_img" height="auto" itemprop="image" src="http://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_post_large/http://c389604.r4.cf1.rackcdn.com/86de7fd9f591101ddb67b15272cb2eb3.jpg" width="100%"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      2) Wall decals. And I don't mean a giant tree climbing up your wall or hokey welcome signs. What about some delicate, strategically placed gold dots? We have them at the Keep office, and they're so cute.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Apple-a-Day Lamp" class="k_full_img" height="auto" itemprop="image" src="http://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_post_large/http://c389604.r4.cf1.rackcdn.com/ea4d2eeae259e8884c1d49d7e7d60e4c.jpg" width="100%"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      3) Play with lighting. Hanging lights from corners or as an alternative to art can be just as visually alluring, given that there are so many minimalist, industrial, and handmade ways to go. Bonus? Light!
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Flowers Poster 36x48" class="k_full_img" height="auto" itemprop="image" src="http://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_post_large/http://c389604.r4.cf1.rackcdn.com/26a73f5c3211ce8a6f8148f2f6bc638d.jpg" width="100%"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      4) Big ol' art. Wipe out those 15 frames in favor of one main focal point. An oversize photo print, an original piece, etc. The room will seem pulled together, but not overly busy.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Metal Wall Mirror" class="k_full_img" height="auto" itemprop="image" src="http://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_post_large/http://c389604.r4.cf1.rackcdn.com/be9282a0903fb7351d819548f9804ac0.jpg" width="100%"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      5) Mirror, mirror. Mirrors count! In addition to making a room look bigger, mirrors are just cool. And you'll always know if your hair looks weird.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Set of 3 - Geometric air plant wall hangers - Brass Planter - Finnish himmeli mobile" class="k_full_img" height="auto" itemprop="image" src="http://res.cloudinary.com/keep/image/fetch/t_post_large/http://c389604.r4.cf1.rackcdn.com/eb9ab912c9326b7caf81c16c3abc0b6a.jpg" width="100%"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      6) All of the plants. Swap those frames for planters and bring a little green in. Wall-mounted planters are not in short supply, and if you stick to succulents or airplants, maintenance is minimal.
     </p>
     <p>
      Below, our favorite ways to de-gallery your walls.  You can do it.
     </p>
