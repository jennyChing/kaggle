            <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
             <p>
              In the summer it's always nice to have a variety of salads up your sleeve to take to a family gathering or serve up when you're grilling on the deck. This salad is packed with vegetables and lightly kissed with a home made balsamic dressing so it's light and fresh without the oily, heavy feel that sometimes comes with pasta salads. We love it alongside
              <a href="http://www.yummymummyclub.ca/easy-family-recipes/20130127/chipotle-maple-chicken-thighs-recipe">
               grilled meats
              </a>
              and a light, tossed green salad to make a great dinner. The leftover salad is wonderful tucked into a school lunchbox or packed along for a picnic and is so easy all you do is boil up some pasta, chop some veggies, toss everything together, and voila! Dinner is on it's way.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Ingredients:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              2 cups bow tie pasta
              <br/>
              1 cup halved grape tomatoes
              <br/>
              1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
              <br/>
              1 cup chopped cucumber
              <br/>
              1/2 sweet yellow pepper, seeded and chopped
              <br/>
              1/2 sweet red pepper, seeded and chopped
              <br/>
              4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
              <br/>
              1/4 cup black olives, sliced
              <br/>
              2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
             </p>
             <p>
              Dressing:
             </p>
             <p>
              3 tsp balsamic vinegar
              <br/>
              2 tbsp olive oil
              <br/>
              1/4 tsp honey
              <br/>
              salt and pepper
              <br/>
              squirt of mustard
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u466/article%20flower.jpeg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px; "/>
              In a pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cool water. Set aside.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u466/article%20flower.jpeg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px; "/>
              Toss the pasta with tomatoes, cucumber, pepper, feta, olives, and dill.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="" src="/sites/default/files/u466/article%20flower.jpeg" style="width: 15px; height: 15px; "/>
              Measure the dressing ingredients in a mason jar. Screw on the lid and give it a shake until everything is well mixed together. Drizzle over the pasta and toss. You dont have to use all the dressing, we typically use some and put the rest in the fridge for another day. Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve the pasta immediately or chill for about an hour before serving.
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <strong>
                Serves 4
               </strong>
              </em>
             </p>
