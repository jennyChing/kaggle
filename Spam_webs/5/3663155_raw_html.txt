            <p class="first-text" data-textannotation-id="584063d8e948dd121dacdb99c59cc767">
             Lollapalooza is a 3-day festival that gathers several musicians and icons to perform. It started in 1991 when Perry Farrell left the rock band Janes Addiction. Lollapalooza ran only until 1997 and was brought back in 2003. This year, Lollapalooza will be held in Grant Park, Chicago from July 31 to August 2.
            </p>
            <h2 data-textannotation-id="3cb4d08d73d140bbe3971b578aa8e233">
             What to expect in this glorious gathering?
            </h2>
            <h3 data-textannotation-id="746cc7a4a19d1249f03af7184cb7941f">

             5. The Teens
