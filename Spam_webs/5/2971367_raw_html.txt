       <div class="body">
        October 21, 2013
        <p>
         This was shot at sunrise on a rocky outcrop at Currumbin,Queensland.
        </p>
        <p>
         Itwas actually a bit of fun trying to time theshutter and get the best formation ofwater.
        </p>
        <p>
         I used aCanon 500D with a Sigma 10-20mm f/3.5 lens. It was shot atISO100, 12mm, f/10 at 1.3 seconds.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: right;">
         <em>
          Andrew Caitens, Brisbane, Qld

function trackLocation() {
