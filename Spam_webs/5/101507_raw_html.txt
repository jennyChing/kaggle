             <div class="article_title--before grid__item float--left lap-and-up-two-thirds" style="display: inline-block; width: 715.984px; padding-left: 1.71429rem; vertical-align: top; color: #4c4a4a; font-family: 'PT Sans',sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 24.0001px; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px;">
              <h1 class="article__title article__title--single" style="font-size: 3.28571rem; margin: 0px 0px 0.85714rem; padding: 0px; line-height: 1.05348; font-family: Arvo,serif; font-weight: 500; display: block; position: relative;">
               16 Signs That Youre a Slave to the Matrix
              </h1>
              <div class="article__title__meta" style="margin-bottom: 1.71429rem;">
               <div class="article__author-name" style="font-size: 1.07143rem; line-height: 1.61; font-family: Arvo,serif; font-weight: normal; text-transform: uppercase; color: #fb4834; display: inline-block; font-style: normal;">
                <a href="http://themindunleashed.org/author/tmuorg" rel="author" style="color: #ffffff; text-decoration: none; background-color: #34b6d1; text-transform: uppercase; letter-spacing: 0.0625em; font-size: 0.85714rem; line-height: 2.176; padding: 10px 15px; height: 42px;" title="Posts by The Mind Unleashed">
                 THE MIND UNLEASHED

               on 2 May, 2014 at 22:00
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="grid__item float--left two-thirds palm-one-whole article__featured-image" style="display: inline-block; width: 715.984px; padding-left: 1.71429rem; vertical-align: top; margin-bottom: 1.71429rem; position: relative; color: #4c4a4a; font-family: 'PT Sans',sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 24.0001px; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px;">
              <a class="image-wrap" href="http://themindunleashed.org/2014/05/16-signs-youre-slave-matrix.html" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: none; display: block; padding-top: 390.313px; background-color: #cccccc; position: relative; overflow: hidden;">
               <img alt="http://themindunleashed.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/slaveeee.jpg" src="http://themindunleashed.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/slaveeee.jpg" style="border: 0px none; font-style: italic; max-width: 100%; position: absolute; top: 0px; left: 0px; width: 691.984px; height: auto; z-index: 20; display: block;"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="GARD gard_group_4 async_mode ver2.3.22 large_skyscraper large_skyscraper_0" id="google-ads-X7Ej">

             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              Todays world is a strange place. We are inundated with signals from early on in life, encouraging each of us to walk a particular path, establishing blinders on us along the way to discourage us from looking for alternatives to what the herd is doing or thinking. Life is so complex that overtime, if we are paying attention, we realize that there are an infinite
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/04/25/within-range-possibilities/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="Within the Range of Possibilities">
               number of possibilities
              </a>
              to what the human experience could be, and we come see that the world is on fire because individuals all too infrequently question why things are the way they are, failing to notice that their mindset or behavior needs adjustment in favor of more intelligent, common sensical, or sustainable patterns of existence.
             </p>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              Not meant to be overtly critical of anyones lifestyle choices or personal situation, the following
              <em>
               16 signs that youre a slave to the matrix
              </em>
              are meant purely as an observational approach to helping you identify the areas of your life where you may be missing an opportunity to liberate yourself from someone elses self-destructive design for your life.
             </p>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              Read on, and please take a moment to comment below with anything youd like to add to the list or object to.
             </p>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              <strong style="font-weight: bold;">
               1. You pay taxes to people youd like to see locked up in jail.
              </strong>
              This is perhaps the biggest indicator that we are slaves to the
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/04/23/steeped-in-the-matrix-in-the-shadow-of-indifference/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="Steeped in the Matrix in the Shadow of Indifference">
               matrix
              </a>
              . The traditional notion of slavery conjures up images of people in shackles forced to work on plantations to support rich plantation owners. The modern day version of this is forced taxation, where our incomes are automatically docked before we ever see the money, regardless of whether or not we approve of how the money is spent.
             </p>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              <strong style="font-weight: bold;">
               2. You go to the doctor, but youre still sick.
              </strong>
              Modern medical care, for all of its scientific progress, has sadly become
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2013/02/19/follow-the-money-in-sick-care-whats-behind-the-drugs-and-needles/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" title="Follow The Money In Sick-care: Whats Behind The Drugs And Needles?">
               sick care
              </a>
              , where we are rarely advised to eat well and tend to our mental and physical health, but instead are routinely advised to consume expensive medications and procedures that are pushed by the
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2013/12/12/7-tips-surviving-medical-industrial-complex/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" title="7 Tips on Surviving The Medical Industrial Complex">
               for-profit healthcare matrix
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              <strong style="font-weight: bold;">
               3. Youve picked Team Democrat or Team Republican and argue with your friends, family and co-workers about politics.
              </strong>
              This is what the control strategy of
              <a href="http://themindunleashed.org/2013/12/divide-conquer-division-humanity-humanity.html" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;">
               divide and conquer
              </a>
              looks like in our society.
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/04/03/beyond-left-right-escaping-matrix/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="Beyond Left &amp; Right: Escaping the Matrix">
               Both of the major parties
              </a>
              are corrupt through and through, and independent candidates are not even allowed to participate in public debates. By believing in one of these parties and
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2012/02/22/politics-is-exhausting-%e2%80%93-and-that%e2%80%99s-exactly-its-point/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="Politics is Exhausting  And Thats Exactly Its Point">
               burning your personal energy
              </a>
              on arguing with other ordinary people you are turning over your soul
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/04/03/changing-face-revolution-3-inspiring-people/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="The Changing Face of Revolution  3 Inspiring People Who are Undermining the Matrix">
               the matrix
              </a>
              , and doing your share in making sure that we the people will never be united against corruption.
             </p>
             <div class="GARD gard_group_2 async_mode ver2.3.22 ad_unit ad_unit_0" id="google-ads-Dibn">
             </div>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
              <strong style="font-weight: bold;">
               4. You work hard doing something you hate to earn fiat dollars.
              </strong>
              Work is important and money does pay the bills, however, so many people lose the best years of their lives doing things they hate, just for money. The truth about money today is that we do not have money, but instead,
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2014/01/08/dollar-dies/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="When the Dollar Dies">
               inflationary fiat currency
              </a>
              that is
              <a href="http://themindunleashed.org/2014/01/10-things-everyone-know-federal-reserve.html" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;">
               privately owned and manipulated
              </a>
              . Since it is still necessary to get by in this world, it is best that you get more value for your time by doing something you enjoy or by working with people you do not despise. It is easier than you may think to live on
              <a href="http://www.wakingtimes.com/2012/02/17/money-the-rise-of-the-alternative-currency/" style="color: #4c4a4a; text-decoration: underline; font-style: italic;" target="_blank" title="Money &amp; the Rise of the Alternative Currency">
               less money
              </a>
              than we believe we need, we just have to be willing to go against the grain realize this.
             </p>
             <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.71429rem; padding: 0px;">
