					&lt;p&gt;Technology is changing is a drastic way. Every day there is a new invention to take a new step into a surprising world. It is said that anything can happen in the 21st century as nothing is impossible for the technology. Man has touched the Moon many years ago but there is always a new thought comes in mind when it is about the magic on the Earth. Science has given us unlimited power and gadgets have made are life easy and comfortable.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In this term, &lt;a title="Laptops in India" href="../../category/Electronics/Laptops"&gt;Laptops&lt;/a&gt; play an important part in our daily life. Whether you are working in a company or travelling in a train, work on laptop goes on and on. Assume that you need to &lt;strong&gt;charge your laptop&lt;/strong&gt; and there is no electricity than you will definitely get frustrated, but there is a simple way to charge it from the sunlight. So the &lt;strong&gt;solar energy&lt;/strong&gt; can be very helpful for us.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Yes, the technology and the nature have given us the opportunity to charge our laptops with the solar energy. Canadian company &lt;strong&gt;WeWi Telecommunication&lt;/strong&gt; has designed a laptop in which solar panel is used. According to this new technology, we can connect the panel with laptop and remove it by folding. Whenever you want to charge it you can open it in front of the Sunlight. It will be charged within two hours by the solar energy.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This solar powered laptop is designed for the places where electricity problem is at high level. Its hardware and software is designed according to military use. You can fold the panel if the battery power is very less. WeWi Telecommunications has invented this laptop with highly advanced and useful technique of solar energy.&lt;/p&gt;    &lt;/div&gt;
