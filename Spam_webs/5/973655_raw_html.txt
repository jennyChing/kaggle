     <h3>
      Art directories
     </h3>
     <p>
      Submitting your art website to
      <b>
       quality
      </b>
      art directories will help you rank better in search engines because 
Google, Yahoo and Msn puts more trust in quality sites.
     </p>
     <p>
      Unfortunately not all art directories are created equal. There are many
directories that haven't been updated for ages, have almost no traffic, poor page rank, or a several duplicates of the 
same directory (different site design but same content on different domains). Therefore submitting your website to only quality directories
will save you a lot of effort, frustration, and improve your search engine rankings.
     </p>
     <p>
      Before adding a directory to the list below we evaluate it based on 
the following criteria: age of the directory, backlink count, backlink quality (are they topically related from quality sites), 
alexa rank, page rank, site structure (are pages easily indexable by search engines), link type you get (direct, redirect, follow, nofollow).
     </p>
     <p>
      The following list represents quality art directories that in our opinion are worth submitting to. The submission price is
included for convenience.
     </p>
