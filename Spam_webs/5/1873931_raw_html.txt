           <h1 class="post-title">
            10 Things to avoid getting Longer Life of Shaver Foil
           </h1>
           <div class="post-format">
            <div class="image-container">
             <img alt="Shavers Foil" class="attachment-thumb-large wp-post-image" height="340" src="http://www.mybestshaver.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Shaver-Foil-720x340.jpg" width="720"/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry ">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              Healthy and proper shaving is not only a way to take care our precious skin, body and hair alone, it is also a great habit to keep the shaving kit fit for a long time to get hassle free performance for a long period of time. So, apart from doing the right thing for your face and facial skin you have to know how to take good care of your
              <a href="http://www.mybestshaver.com/">
               best electric shaver
              </a>
              and its accessories. It is very necessary to keep your foil and blades in a good condition. Today we are going to let you know the habits which kill the foil shaver before the guaranteed time estimated by the shaving kit manufacturer.
             </p>
             <ol>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Ignore Overheating:
               </strong>
               If you keep working the shaver for a long period of time, then the shaver will gets hot which is not good for the foil at all. Overheating mainly causes because of the friction between metals. In this case you should stop the machine and use a drop of oil to lubricate the metal and it will decrease the friction and heating as well.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Put too Much Pressure:
               </strong>
               For getting a close shave few men apply too much pressure on the head. This is not the right way to shave. The foil is made of thin metal. So too much pressure can easily damage the foil. Gentle pressure is enough to get the best performance from
               <a href="http://www.mybestshaver.com/best-electric-shaver-for-men-2015/">
                top rated electric shaver
               </a>
               and keep the foil in a good condition.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Tap the Shaver Head:
               </strong>
               During the cleaning process dont tap the shaver head with your palm or sink. Because the shaver head is connected with the body with a honeycomb-bond. If this bond breaks then there is no way you can use this shaver.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Force your Shaver through Hard Beard:
               </strong>
               Dont ever force your shaver through a heavy and hard beard. It is a wrong way of shaving. You just need to move the shaver gently through your beard. You can trim the upper section of the beard to make the work even easier for your electric shaver.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Store the Shaver in a Wet Place:
               </strong>
               After each shave clean the shaver and the accessories nicely and keep them in a dry place to lure away the water. Before keeping the shaver in the safety case you can apply a drop of oil on the blades and foil to make it ready for the next use.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Use a Cleaning Brush on Cutter Foil:
               </strong>
               Most of the electric shaver users use cleaning brush to keep the foil clean but it is a wrong way. You can keep the shaver under the tap or use the cleaning spray. Dont ever use the cleaning brush to keep the shaver clean.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Remove the Shaver Head Roughly:
               </strong>
               If you want to keep your electric shaver in a good shape you must avoid rough handling during removing the head and other attachments. Be patient and remove the header with proper care.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Neglect the Blade Protector Cap:
               </strong>
               The blade protective cap is very important during the journey. It is also important to protect the foil because a faulty foil can only give you a faulty shave.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Keep your Shaver Messy:
               </strong>
               You must clean your shaver after every 2 or 3 shave. It will keep the shaver effective for a long period of times.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Dont Give it to Children:
               </strong>
               An electric shaver is not a childs play thing. A child can drop the shaver on the floor, which cans do harm to the foil.
              </li>
             </ol>
