         <p>
          The poor residents of Northampton, England are living in a nightmare scenario straight out of a Steven King novel right now. Someone dressed as a clown keeps popping up around town and spooking grown adults and small children alike.
         </p>
         <p>
          The clown isnt doing anything violent, mind you, so its exactly like a Steven King novel. But its close! Because clowns are terrifying, and they scare people.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="http://www.northampton-news-hp.co.uk/News/Northampton-News/Spooky-clown-mystery-grips-Northampton-14092013.htm" rel="nofollow">
           Northampton Herald &amp; Postreports
          </a>
          clowns have been popping up in random places all across town lately, so much so that
          <a href="https://twitter.com/search?q=%23NorthamptonClown&amp;src=hash" rel="nofollow">
           a hashtag
          </a>
          and
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/spotnorthamptonsclown" rel="nofollow">
           a Facebook group
          </a>
          were created to track the sightings.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="http://www.northamptonchron.co.uk/news/local/police-warning-after-clowns-spotted-acting-suspiciously-in-northampton-1-5489909" rel="nofollow">
           Northampton Chronicle
          </a>
          reports police believe multiple people are behind the clown sightings. A warning was issued so residents wouldnt be so startled the next time aclown is standing in the middle of the side walk.
         </p>
