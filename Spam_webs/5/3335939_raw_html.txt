           <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
            <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2014/11/debtor-ledger-creation-in-tally.html">
             How to create Debtor Ledger Account in Tally.ERP 9?
            </a>
           </h1>
           <div class="post-header-line-1">
           </div>
           <br/>
           <div class="post-body entry-content">
            <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1798579668916519" data-ad-slot="9890625848" style="display:inline-block;width:500px;height:300px">
            </ins>
            <script>
             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
            <div id="aim12023390092991204834">
            </div>
            <div style="clear:both; margin:10px 0">
             <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1798579668916519" data-ad-slot="6511351911" style="display:inline-block;width:468px;height:15px">
             </ins>
             <script>
              (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
             </script>
            </div>
            <div id="aim22023390092991204834">
             <p>
              Usually, when we sell goods on credit or lend money to someone else we record the individual or company name in the Books of Accounts and we treat him as a
              <em>
               Debtor
              </em>
              . In
              <em>
               Tally.ERP 9 Accounting Software
              </em>
              we can create a Ledger Account for Debtors.
             </p>
             <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
              <tbody>
               <tr>
                <td style="text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Cy9wGu96Sxo/VHgr9zrDX1I/AAAAAAAAGHI/rQpCJR5deEc/s1600/how-to-create-debtor-ledger-account-in-tally-image.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" target="_blank">
                  <img alt="How to create Sundry Debtor Ledger Account in Tally.ERP 9" border="0" height="358" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Cy9wGu96Sxo/VHgr9zrDX1I/AAAAAAAAGHI/rQpCJR5deEc/s1600/how-to-create-debtor-ledger-account-in-tally-image.jpg" title="Debtor Ledger Creation in Tally.ERP 9" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </td>
               </tr>
               <tr>
                <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                 Debtor Ledger creation in Tally.ERP 9
                </td>
               </tr>
              </tbody>
             </table>
             <p>
              In
              <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2014/10/introduction-and-features-of-tally.html" target="_blank">
               Tally.ERP 9 Accounting Software
              </a>
              we can create as many Book Debts Accounts that we want to create for a Company we can do it using the
              <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2014/10/ledger-creation-in-tally.html" target="_blank">
               Ledger Creation screen
              </a>
              and with the help of
              <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2012/03/groups-for-ledgers-in-tally-erp-9-and.html" target="_blank">
               Sundry Debtor predefined Ledger Group
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              In order to create a
              <em>
               Sundry Debtor
              </em>
              ledger just
              <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2014/10/open-operate-quit-tally.html" target="_blank">
               open the Tally.ERP 9
              </a>
              and
              <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2014/10/select-company-in-tally.html" target="_blank">
               load the Company
              </a>
              to work with and get to the
              <a href="http://www.accountsguy.net/2014/10/ledger-creation-in-tally.html" target="_blank">
               Ledger Creation screen
              </a>
              .  In the Ledger Creation screen
             </p>
             <!-- adsense -->
             <ol>
              <li>
               Give a name for the
               <em>
                Debtor
               </em>
               .
              </li>
              <li>
               Choose
               <em>
                Sundry Debtors
               </em>
               from the
               <em>
                List of Groups
               </em>
               for under field.
              </li>
              <li>
               Provide the
               <em>
                Mailing Details
               </em>
               and other information if required.
              </li>
              <li>
               Enter the Closing Balance if there.
              </li>
             </ol>
             <p>
              And accept the screen to create the Debtor Ledger. This is how to create a Debtor Ledger Account in Tally.ERP 9 Accounting Software.
             </p>
