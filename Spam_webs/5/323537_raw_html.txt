       <p>
        DOUGLASWOOD, GA -Americans can look forward to a new family fun center in 2016. The Kyle Estate, in association with Fox News Corp, has announced plans to build an American Sniper Theme Park outside of Douglaswood. The new 300 acre facility will feature a multi-functional shooting range with targets and shooting obstacles based on the top grossing movie and book authored by Chris Kyle.
       </p>
       <p>
        Douglaswood is 20 miles West of Atlanta, an area plagued by a lack of jobs in poverty-stricken Georgia. The theme park will make use of a patch of wetlands newly zoned for commercial use by the state. The jobs and economic development that the theme park brings will be a tremendous benefit to Douglaswood and the surrounding area, said Mayor Russell Hodges. Were also in talks with Hilton Corporation to clear-cut and build a hotel and waterpark.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3AChosin_Range.jpg" title="By Photo by Gerry J. Gilmore;Palm dogg at en.wikipedia [Public domain], from Wikimedia Commons">
         <img alt="Chosin Range" class="aligncenter" height="296" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9b/Chosin_Range.jpg/512px-Chosin_Range.jpg" width="512"/>
        </a>
        A 1,000 yard shooting range (Based on a USMC firing range, right) which can accommodate 100 shooters will be the centerpiece of the magnificent park. Rides, patriotic themed exhibits, laser tag games, and instruction in the history and safe use of firearms are added attractions which will make the American Sniper Theme Park a one-of-a-kind experience the whole family can enjoy. Mom and Dad can spend quality time at the shooting range while the tykes play in a wholesome learning environment, added Hodges.
       </p>
       <p>
        Springboarding from the enormous popularity of the American Sniper book and film, the theme park will raise awareness levels of the difficult job our troops are tasked to perform as well as provide a fun place for children to learn firearms safety while handling some of the most powerful side arms ever produced.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://d1nt4a7y8dwdsx.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/am-sniper.png">
         <img alt="am-sniper" class="alignleft wp-image-40395 size-thumbnail" height="150" src="http://d1nt4a7y8dwdsx.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/am-sniper-150x150.png" width="150"/>
        </a>
        Chris Kyle was a Navy SEAL who tallied 160 confirmed kills in Iraq, making him the most lethal sniper in American Military history. His memoir was published in 2012. Tragically, Kyle was taken from us in 2013 at a shooting range near Chalk Mountain, Texas when a PTSD suffering veteran shot him dead.
       </p>
       <p>
        His estate is under legal pressure from lawsuits filed against it and Kyles publishing Company, HarpoCollins. The entirety of the Kyle Estate fortune from the book and film is on the line while litigation goes forward. Kyle was charged with false statements and printed libel in his autobiography.
       </p>
       <p>
        His legacy is assured with the announcement of the theme park. God bless Fox News for their interest in preserving Chris Kyles memory with this magnificent attraction, said Maria Kyle, Chris widow.
       </p>
       <p>
        One of the special features of the theme park is the Michael Moore/Jesse Ventura Range. Life-like popup targets based on the movie and antagonistic figures from Kyles life will test the moxie and reaction of shooters. Prizes will be awarded for marksmanship skill. This looks to be the most popular of the park attractions.
       </p>

       <p>
        Another popular attraction is sure to be the Fallujah Follies Party. Shooters will be able to lock in and test their sniping abilities in a detailed reconstruction of a Fallujah street battle scene. Robotic facsimiles of terrorists will appear at varying intervals and distances, just like the targets that Chris confronted during his tours of duty in Iraq.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Lil Buckaroo Shooting Squad Laser Tag will provide children with an adventurous opportunity to engage in safe small arms battles and close combat simulation using laser tag technology and nerf bayonets. Afterward, they can play in the family muster and fun center and enjoy Coke and Tootsie Roll Brand soft drinks and candies while waiting for their parents or legal guardians. The Patriots Lounge will be a family fun spot where parents and children can meetup and bond while discovering military exhibits, shows, and actual NRA training in firearms use and safety.
       </p>
       <p>
        All of this has the stamp of approval from the Kyle Estate.
       </p>
       <p>
        Naturally, leftist opposition to economic development of wastelands and general disdain for second amendment freedom is a hurdle to leap in moving the theme park plans into realization. Liberals cite the pending litigation, environmental concerns, and moral grounds as reasons to oppose the permit. How they arrive down that slippery slope is known only to their operatives as they howl from behind their Soros-funded think tanks.
