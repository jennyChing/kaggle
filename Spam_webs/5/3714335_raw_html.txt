          <p class="img_preview left">
           <a class="g" href="http://art-u2.infcdn.net/articles_uploads/2014/04/women-shopping.jpg" rel="g" title="picVpic: Compare Clothes before Purchasing">
            <img alt="" src="http://art-u2.infcdn.net/articles_uploads/2014/04/women-shopping-665x411.jpg" title="picVpic: Compare Clothes before Purchasing" width="420"/>
           </a>
           <span>
            picVpic: Compare Clothes before Purchasing
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            The inevitable spread of technology has completely revolutionized the way we shop. From purchasing some new clothes for work to getting another itty-bitty trinket - we go shopping on-line for any needed item. That said, when you're overwhelmed with that immense choice on-line shopping offers, picking up the real thing can become arduous. New comparison shopping service
            <a href="http://articles.informer.com/go/?go=http%3A%2F%2Fpicvpic.com%2F" target="_blank">
             picVpic
            </a>
            claims to land you a hand by narrowing down your choices and, therefore, reducing any uncertainty. Here's now.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Well, with the word, picVpic represents a so-called virtual mall with a huge database of clothing and accessories for both gender from the entire Internet. The idea is to help you find what you want by comparing items and prices across the fashion websites and major brands the service aggregates.
          </p>
          <p class="img_preview">
           <a class="g" href="http://articles.informer.com/articles_uploads/2014/04/Screenshot-from-2014-04-02-145748.png" rel="g" title="picVpic: Main Page">
            <img alt="Choose an item you look for" height="306" src="http://articles.informer.com/articles_uploads/2014/04/Screenshot-from-2014-04-02-145748-410x306.png" title="picVpic: Main Page" width="410"/>
           </a>
           <span>
            picVpic: Main Page
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Getting started, you are to select a gender and type of clothing you are interested in purchasing, that is presented upon landing the homepage with cute pictured mannequins. Then, after you've decided in favor of suits, for instance, you will be provided with a list compiled upon the price of the outfit, its color, freshness, and brand. Therewith, picVpic lets you filter garments according to discounts offered by a wide number of on-line stores, which facilitates your apparel shopping even more.
          </p>
          <p class="img_preview">
           <a class="g" href="http://articles.informer.com/articles_uploads/2014/04/Screenshot-from-2014-04-02-145658.png" rel="g" title="Box: Compare Your Choices ">
            <img alt="picVpic: compare items" height="189" src="http://articles.informer.com/articles_uploads/2014/04/Screenshot-from-2014-04-02-145658-410x189.png" title="Box: Compare Your Choices " width="410"/>
           </a>
           <span>
            Box: Compare Your Choices
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           As you browse, you can drag any eye-catching item into a box at the bottom of the page. When you put together your collection of likings, you can start the comparison process to look at each suit individually. This kind-like breakdown seems really worthwhile, as you can not only match the outward appearance of the outfits, but also find out how each of them is composed with the description pulled directly from each on-line shop, which should show you the size charts, fabric composition, and shipping details.
          </p>
          <p class="img_preview">
           <a class="g" href="http://articles.informer.com/articles_uploads/2014/04/Screenshot-from-2014-04-02-145719.png" rel="g" title="Voting Process ">
            <img alt="Vote to choose the right thing" height="255" src="http://articles.informer.com/articles_uploads/2014/04/Screenshot-from-2014-04-02-145719-410x255.png" title="Voting Process " width="410"/>
           </a>
           <span>
            Voting Process
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Concurrently, the service offers you to work out the right piece of clothing by voting between two choices until you have ranked all what you took a shine to. If you still feel a bit unsure, even after having completed such eliminating process, you can ask your friends via social networks, like Facebook, Twitter, or Pinterest.
          </p>
          <p>
           Since picVpic itself isn't an on-line store, once you make up your mind what you want to buy, it will redirect you to the selling site to obtain your item.
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="yt" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/MyzZ2-qnNTc">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           Sure enough, picVpic represents an appealing concept by assembling all vogue wardrobe options in one place and providing a visual experience of how outfits look in comparison to others. So, even if you are loath to shop on-line, picVpic may turn out to be a great solution to get inspiration and find out what style you like.
          </p>
