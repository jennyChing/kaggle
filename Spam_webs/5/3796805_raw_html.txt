                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Strawberry Limeade
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2689821658485580545" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-J8fkifg_Da8/Tct2j5krWuI/AAAAAAAAAX8/lBqxGJupCV0/s1600/DSC_0149web.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320px" j8="true" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-J8fkifg_Da8/Tct2j5krWuI/AAAAAAAAAX8/lBqxGJupCV0/s320/DSC_0149web.jpg" width="217px"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="border-bottom: medium none; border-left: medium none; border-right: medium none; border-top: medium none;">
                      Spring is here and so are all the delicious strawberries!!! This drink makes me feel likesitting onmy porch as the sun is setting, watching my kids ride their bikes down the street and talking to neighbors who are walking by....
                     </div>
                     I'm so excited that summer is just around the corner!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     There are only afew ingredients in this beautiful recipe,most of which are probably already in your kitchen.
                     <br/>
                     Ingredients:
                     <br/>
                     1 can limeade
                     <br/>
                     3/4 lb Strawberries
                     <br/>
                     1/2 c sugar
                     <br/>
                     3 C water
                     <br/>
                     approx 2 c. ice
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="border-bottom: medium none; border-left: medium none; border-right: medium none; border-top: medium none; clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-0a1VE-qJCqw/Tct2i-53JlI/AAAAAAAAAX4/-zjzQi06PCI/s1600/DSC_0130web.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="275px" j8="true" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-0a1VE-qJCqw/Tct2i-53JlI/AAAAAAAAAX4/-zjzQi06PCI/s320/DSC_0130web.jpg" width="320px"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="border-bottom: medium none; border-left: medium none; border-right: medium none; border-top: medium none;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     In a blender put in all ingredients, placing ice last. Blend away. If your blender iskindof small, you may need to pour a little bit out into a pitcherin order to finish addingwhatever wouldn't fit. Once it's allcombined intoyour serving pitcherit won't matter.You could also add more sugar if you want it sweeter, but I like the tart blend. Obviously this drink separates quickly, so stir often before pouring. Cheers!!
