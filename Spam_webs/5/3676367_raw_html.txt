           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal18.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal18" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29449" height="735" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal18-490x735.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            To contrast with the wood and the bamboo, the mercury glass vase adds glamour and sparkle. The pink marble box is a pretty feminine touch and is great for jewelry.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal22.jpg">
              <img alt="reveal22" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29448" height="735" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal22-490x735.jpg" width="490"/>
             </a>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            This basket was the first piece I bought for the room and the inspiration for the colors used in the space.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal16.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal16" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29447" height="735" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal16-490x735.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            While Dave has a more masculine piece on his side of the bed, Jennys beautiful pedestal table is a nice contrast to the dresser next to it. We needed the storage, so I wanted to keep her end table simple and round. This is a Lane piece from HomeGoods and I really wanted to take it home with me!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal211.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal21[1]" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29446" height="735" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal211-490x735.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Some master bedrooms can feel so formal and stiff. Keeping the pillows in an informal arrangement keeps the space inviting.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal20.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal20" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29445" height="593" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal20-490x593.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Cynthia Rowley throw is unbelievably soft and has a beautiful ombr effect. It actually gets light towards the middle of the bed. Really pretty.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal231.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal23[1]" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29444" height="735" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal231-490x735.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The white mirrored cabinet adds some glamour to the space and is echoed in the white mirror above the bed. The graphic pattern on the cabinet is also in keeping with the graphic detail on the window treatments. I wanted to create a gallery wall to allow the TV to blend more harmoniously. In doing this, it becomes another piece of the gallery wall. If you remember from
            <a href="http://www.athoughtfulplaceblog.com/2014/05/grand-giveaway-bedroom-makeover-winner.html">
             the before photos,
            </a>
            it was pretty much the only thing on this wall.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal15.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal15" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29443" height="735" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal15-490x735.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Three out of the five pieces I chose for the gallery wall already had art, so I needed to fill two more. I used a navy and white small print wrapping paper for one frame and a larger print for the second. It has the navy and teal from the window treatments and is a tote bag I found in the check out aisle. You never know where you will find art!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal24.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal24" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29442" height="675" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal24-490x675.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The couple also plans on mounting roller shades under the pelmet boxes to pull down at night. This will allow them to have gorgeous light during the day and privacy in the evening.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal_courtney.jpg">
             <img alt="reveal_courtney" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29441" height="649" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/reveal_courtney-490x649.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            If you made it though all of the photos, I am seriously impressed. I honestly wish you could walk into the room. I wish the photos did it justice. It is light and bright and you just dont want to leave. A huge thank you to Dave and Jenny for jumping in with both feet. It was fast-paced and crazy at times. They were so easy to work with and giving them this new space was an amazing experience for me. Thanks for following along on this adventure!
           </p>
           <p>
            I dont think you can fully appreciate this makeover in its entirety unless you click on the
            <a href="http://www.athoughtfulplaceblog.com/2014/05/grand-giveaway-bedroom-makeover-winner.html">
             before photos
            </a>
            .Its always so fun to see what the room looked like when Dave and Jenny submitted their space.
           </p>
           <p>
            One last look with side by side before and after photos:
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beforeandafter13.jpg">
             <img alt="beforeandafter1[3]" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29439" height="245" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beforeandafter13-490x245.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beforeandafter23.jpg">
             <img alt="beforeandafter2[3]" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-29440" height="245" src="http://www.homegoods.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beforeandafter23-490x245.jpg" width="490"/>
            </a>
           </p>
