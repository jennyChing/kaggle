        <h1 class="title entry-title">
         <a href="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/2014/04/28/messier-monday-an-elliptical-rotating-wrongly-m59-synopsis/" rel="bookmark">
          Messier Monday: An Elliptical Rotating Wrongly, M59 (Synopsis)
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="byline vcard author">
         <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-58 photo" height="58" src="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/files/2013/10/SWAB_logo-58x58.jpg" srcset="http://0.gravatar.com/avatar/6e260b0d51ec7a86c48b3360c8682a32?s=116&amp;d=mm&amp;r=pg 2x" width="58"/>

        <img alt="Image credit: Jim Mazurs Astrophotography, via http://www.skyledge.net/Messier59-L.htm; Messier 59 is at the top right." class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="445" src="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/files/2014/04/Messier59-L-590x445.jpg" width="590"/>

       <div class="main">
        <div class="content entry-content">
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           <em>
            Fantasy is a necessary ingredient in living, its a way of looking at life through the wrong end of a telescope. Which is what I do, and that enables you to laugh at lifes realities. 
           </em>
           Dr. Seuss
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          With 110 objects in the Messier catalogue, and 40 galaxies (more than any other class of object), you might take a if youve seen one, youve seen them all attitude. But if you did, youd be missing out on some truly amazing facts and phenomena happening
          <em>
           right here
          </em>
          in our own backyard!
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_30812" style="width: 610px">
          <a href="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/files/2014/04/galaxies.jpg">
           <img alt="Image credit: courtesy of Sloan Digital Sky Survey/WIKISKY, via http://www2011.mpe.mpg.de/highlights.html." class="size-medium wp-image-30812" height="280" src="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/files/2014/04/galaxies-600x280.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Image credit: courtesy of Sloan Digital Sky Survey/WIKISKY, via http://www2011.mpe.mpg.de/highlights.html.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          After all there are six giant elliptical galaxies in the Messier catalogue, but not only is todays object the
          <em>
           most
          </em>
          elliptical (departing from sphericity), but the core of this galaxy itself is
          <strong>
           rotating the wrong way
          </strong>
          !
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_30813" style="width: 610px">
          <a href="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/files/2014/04/ngc4621core_3.jpg">
           <img alt="Image credit: McDonald Observatory, NASA/AURA/STScI, via http://mcdonaldobservatory.org/news/gallery/core-galaxy-ngc-4621." class="size-medium wp-image-30813" height="420" src="http://scienceblogs.com/startswithabang/files/2014/04/ngc4621core_3-600x420.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Image credit: McDonald Observatory, NASA/AURA/STScI, via http://mcdonaldobservatory.org/news/gallery/core-galaxy-ngc-4621.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Whats its deal? Come find out
          <a href="https://medium.com/starts-with-a-bang/3228197387ac" target="_blank">
           here on todays Messier Monday
          </a>
          !
         </p>
