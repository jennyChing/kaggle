     <p>
      <a href="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/stumbleupon-logo.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-3629" height="150" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/stumbleupon-logo-150x150.jpg" title="stumbleupon logo" width="150"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      If you are an avid web-surfer you probably have heard of StumbleUpon (SU). In a nutshell SU is a massive database of reviewed websites, articles, photos, videos, music, and you name it that has been created by web surfers.  SU can either be used to surf the web based on your own personalized interests, or to promote your own website. Regardless it is a great tool to utilize. Hopefully this page will help give you an insight into how to successfully use it.
     </p>
     <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
     </h3>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Registering and Set-up
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
      </strong>
      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-weight: normal;">
       Firstly, you will need to go to
       <a href="http://www.stumbleupon.com/" target="_blank" title="stumbleupon">
        www.stumbleupon.com
       </a>
       and join for FREE. Joining is easy and you will be asked in the registration to check off some of your interests. Need not worry, you dont have to select everything at this moment. You can go back and adjust these at any time via your settings.
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      There are several ways to stumble. You can download a handy SU toolbar for any browser from here
      <a href="http://www.stumbleupon.com/addon/">
       http://www.stumbleupon.com/addon/
      </a>
      or you can you just go to
      <a href="http://www.stumbleupon.com/">
       www.stumbleupon.com
      </a>
      , enter your login and then start stumbling your interests. Note that it will probably prompt you to install the toolbar mentioned above (handy so you dont always have to go to the SU homepage).
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Stumbling
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      This is where things get fun. Click the stumble button located in the toolbar it looks like this:
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon stumble button" class="size-full wp-image-3626 alignnone" height="20" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-Stumble-Button.jpg" title="SU Stumble Button" width="643"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      This action will take you to a randomly selected webpage that is based on your interests. Click the Stumble button again and it will bring you to another random webpage.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      You can also select the drop-down box that says All Interests and narrow down what you want to stumble. For example you can select Travel and then click the stumble button and you will be directed to travel related pages in the SU database.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       To Like or Dislike
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      When you stumble onto a page you have the option to give it a thumbs-up or thumbs-down depending on what you think of the page.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumble thumb up or down" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3627" height="20" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-Thumb-up-down.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - Thumb up down" width="643"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Thumbs-Up (you like it!!)
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Its really easy, Click the I like it button and it will be logged as one of your favorites.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Thumbs-Down (you dont like it)
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Click this if this page does not sit well with you. Dont be afraid to click here. You arent going to lose friends. In fact SU encourages you to use it. What it does is allow SU to more accurately understand what your interests are.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      There are 4 reasons listed in the dropdown why you would give a thumbs-down rating. These are:
     </p>
     <ol style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Not For Me
        </strong>
       </em>
        simply this website is not related to your tastes
      </li>
      <li>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Report Spam
        </strong>
       </em>
        sometimes sites get through the filters SU has in place. Let them know!
      </li>
      <li>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Duplicate Content
        </strong>
       </em>
        youve seen this before somewhere else. Some other site has scraped this content and is using it.
      </li>
      <li>
       <em>
        <strong>
         Block a Website
        </strong>
       </em>
        you dont want to ever come across this site again
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Discoveries
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Somebody has just emailed you a great article or video via email. You happen to have the SU toolbar open and you click the I like it button.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      If nobody else has liked or disliked this, you will be Discovering a site for SU and taken to the discovery page. If you are curious whether or not a page has been discovered or not, click the cloud button on the toolbar.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon - cloud" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3623" height="20" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-cloud.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - cloud" width="643"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      If it has been discovered it will bring you to the SU information page. If it hasnt it will give you a message letting you know that it has not been discovered.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Here you will be asked:
     </p>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       Is this site safe for work? IE is there any nudity contained within?
      </li>
      <li>
       Categorize the discovery  what is the best category that this would fit under?
      </li>
      <li>
       Leave a review  leave a short one-liner what this article is about
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <a href="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-Discovery.jpg">
       <img alt="stumbleupon discovery" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3634" height="306" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-Discovery.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - Discovery" width="575"/>
      </a>
      <em>
       **An example of a SU discovery**
      </em>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Once this is submitted the now discovered article is available to the over 15 million SU users.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Creating an SU Community
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Like any social media platform, you need to have connections. Since there are 15 million SU users, finding some should not be that difficult.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Go to
      <a href="http://www.stumbleupon.com/">
       www.stumbleupon.com
      </a>
      and login or simply click your favorites on the right hand side of the toolbar.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon faves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3628" height="20" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-user.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - faves" width="643"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      You should be now on your profile within SU and on the right hand side of the page it will lists your followers and following.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon followers" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3624" height="260" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-profile-followers.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - profile - followers" width="635"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      If you have nobody set-up then these fields will be empty. If you know somebody who is using SU you can search for their handle. When you click on a profile. This will bring up a listing of the favorite sites and you can choose whether or not the users favorites are inline with yours. If so you can click the follow button and keep track of what we are liking.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      It is recommended to build up your following status. The more people you follow the more likely it is they will follow you in return (at some point). And the more people who follow you in return, the more SU connections you will have to share your favorites with. Are you seeing the link yet?
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Note that building a following can take a while because people generally will not follow you back until you have liked a few hundred websites and can see that your tastes are similar to theirs. So be patient and good things will come of it.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Sharing
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      On the SU toolbar there are a number of options to share articles that you have marked as your favorite in SU.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon - sharing" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3625" height="20" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-sharing.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - sharing" width="643"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      You can share this across various social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Linked In and of course with other SU members. Click any of the buttons while on the article you wish to share and it will automatically bring up a screen in which you can link it to your other social media accounts and instantly share said article.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Sharing with other SU users is another story. SU users do not want their share inbox to be flooded with articles from one user, so etiquette is to send 1-2 shares every other day.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      You will see on your stumbleupon toolbar how many stumble inbox items you have here.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon shares" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3635" height="19" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-shares.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - shares" width="643"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Click this and you will be brought to a page that somebody has shared with you. Dont forget to give it a thumbs up or down, and a review!
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       StumbleUpon Keys to Success
      </strong>
     </h4>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-3621" height="112" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/keys.jpg" title="keys" width="150"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      If you have your own website StumbleUpon can be a great tool to promote your content. There are however guidelines which we have followed and have made it very successful for us. If you dont follow these, the SU police (thats what I call them) can punish you and punishment is generally your content is not shared with the other users. But following these simple steps can ensure success:
     </p>
     <h5 style="text-align: justify;">
     </h5>
     <h5 style="text-align: justify;">
     </h5>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h5 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       DOs
      </strong>
     </h5>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Share your websites content
       </strong>
        liking ones own stuff is considered spammy, but sharing your own content once it has been discovered is a great way to get some hits and reviews. BUT remember to only share your personalized content every couple of days
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Review, Review, Review
       </strong>
        We have heard that the kiss of death for a page or an article not to get the recognition it deserves (views) is to not have a review. ALWAYS leave reviews on discoveries. Pages that have already been discovered check to see if there is a review left behind. The more reviews, the better. If there isnt one take the 10 seconds it takes and leave one. Not only does it give other readers an idea what the page is about, but there is a reason that SU tracks your reviews. It enhances your reputation as a quality stumbler.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Thumbs Down
       </strong>
        Make sure to give thumbs-down to articles or pages you dont like. Again, this can help SU determine the quality of the sites and other stumblers, and help your own status
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Build Your Community
       </strong>
        find other stumblers that are similar to you. Look at your favorites and click on the views. It will then bring up a listing of all the stumblers who have liked the page as well. Click on users and on the right hand side you can see how similar the user is in relation to you. Add them to your following if you choose so.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Stumble
       </strong>
        make sure to click that stumble button and surf the web. It shows SU that you are actively stumbling your interests. It doesnt hurt to change up your interests every once in a while either.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Allow Shares
       </strong>
        Make sure to keep the Allow shares option checked on when you add a stumbler. This will allow this user to share articles and pages with you and if they add you back, vice-versa.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumbleupon accepting shares" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3622" height="185" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/StumbleUpon-accepting-shares.jpg" title="StumbleUpon - accepting shares" width="639"/>
     </p>
     <h5 style="text-align: justify;">
     </h5>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h5 style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       DO NOTS
      </strong>
     </h5>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="stumble down" class="alignright size-full wp-image-3630" height="200" src="http://www.hecktictravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/us_stumble_200x200_200509.jpg" title="stumble down" width="200"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       <br/>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Discover Your Own Content
       </strong>
        No matter how good you think your own article or webpage is, dont do it. Let the other SU users discover this gem.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Be a Sole Self-Promoter
       </strong>
        Build your reputation with your stumble followers as a good stumbler by sharing quality content other than your own.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Flood Share Inboxes
       </strong>
        dont be pumping out 10 shares per day. As a general guideline 3-4 shares per week is a good base.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       <strong>
        Share Content With Users Who Have Already Reviewed
       </strong>
        Check to see your users and see if anyone has already given it a like or not. If they have reviewed, make sure to leave them off the share (again, dont flood the inbox).
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
     </p>
     <div style="text-align: justify;">
      So there you have it. A how-to for StumbleUpon. Nobody really knows how posts go viral within SU, but follow these guidelines and you will have some success with it.
     </div>
     <p>
     </p>
     <div style="text-align: justify;">
      If you have any recommendations that are not listed here that you believe make your StumbleUpon experience more useful, please leave a comment for our readers.
     </div>
