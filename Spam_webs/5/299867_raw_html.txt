              This
              <span class="removed_link" title="http://inspiringpretty.com/tag/weddings/">
               wedding

              <a href="http://inspiringpretty.com/tag/bride/">
               bride
              </a>
              pulls of going bare with style with no hair wear necessary at all. I also love the mixture of colors, as the couple did not only use fall colors. There are beautiful hues of pink, blue and purple incorporate with the classic autumn leave colors. And the decor is old-fashioned and perfect for this affair. The couple did a marvelous job and if their big day was 1/10 as great as it looks, it must have been magical :)
