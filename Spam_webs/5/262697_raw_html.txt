        <h1 class="entry-title">
         A conviction to keep you going
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div id="greet_block">
        </div>
        <p>
         What convictions keep you going?
        </p>
        <p>
         Its not a question that you may often think about, but a critical one to be aware of, particularly in times of trouble.
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="A conviction to keep you going" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5577" height="311" src="http://www.learygates.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/whatconvictionskeepyougoing.jpg" width="540"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         Four years ago, when our friend Beth sent her husband,
         <a href="http://www.learygates.com/2009/10/13/tribute-to-tim-wheatley/" target="_blank">
          Tim
         </a>
         , off to work with a kiss, she didnt expect it would be for the last time. Never could she imagine that he and their nine-year old daughter, Sarah, would be broadsided by a delivery truck, killing him instantly and leaving their daughter fighting for her life in intensive care.
        </p>
        <p>
         Rushing to the hospital she didnt know what to expect. What was Sarahs condition? Was she even still alive? Later she would tell her friends that with each step she took down the hospital corridor toward Sarahs room she recited a truth she and Tim believed: My grace is sufficient for you (2 Corinthians 12:9). It was a miracle that Sarah survived an internal decapitation, her skull jarred loose from her spine. Through the surgery to reattach her skull to her spine and the painful rehabilitation that followed, Beth steered clear of despair with the help of the convictions she and Tim faithfully cultivated.
        </p>
        <p>
         My wife, Anna, and I recently visited with Beth and she will tell you it was a hard journey and still is. But it would have been an impossible one without the solid conviction that God is present every day for her.
        </p>
        <p>
         And Hes present every day for you as well. No matter what you may be going through in your own journey, His grace is sufficient for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats a conviction that can keep us all going.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           What convictions keep you going?
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
