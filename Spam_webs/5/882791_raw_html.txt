       <p>
        <a href="http://www.frenchweddingstyle.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/wedding-at-hotel-du-cap-eden-roc.jpg">
         <img alt="wedding at hotel du cap eden roc" class="aligncenter wp-image-33181 size-full" height="401" src="http://www.frenchweddingstyle.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/wedding-at-hotel-du-cap-eden-roc.jpg" title="Wedding at Hotel du Cap Eden Roc | Image by Love Story, see more http://goo.gl/byXiU6" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        For todays real wedding of Alexandra and Thomas, we are off to the beautiful South of France for a luxury styled wedding at Hotel du Cap Eden Roc. The French Riviera is blessed with many stunning wedding venue locations, but
        <a href="http://www.hotel-du-cap-eden-roc.com/fr/accueil/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.hotel-du-cap-eden-roc.com/fr/accueil/']);" target="_blank">
         Hotel du Cap Eden Roc
        </a>
        is one that you might have heard off, being the favourite of celebrities such as Brad and Angelina, when visiting the area.
       </p>
       <p>
        Todays international couple based in London, met whilst working for the same company but started their wedding story off in true style, with Thomas proposing to Ali during a surprise weekend in Italy. Following the engagement Ali knew that they would get married on the French Riviera, in Cap dAntibes where her parents own a beautiful villa.
       </p>
       <p>
        Alis mom contactedplanners
        <a href="http://www.eternalevent.com" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.eternalevent.com']);" target="_blank">
         Eternal Event
        </a>
        , to discuss how to make the dream wedding come true. Maryne from Eternal Event takes us through the planning journey and big day. Maryne: 
        <em>
         We arranged an appointment in Antibes and I met the whole family, where we discussed what they had in mind and how I could assist them. This is how the planning started. During 8 months, we exchanged hundreds of emails, met several times to plan this beautiful day and leave no details behind.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
