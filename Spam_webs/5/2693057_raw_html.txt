         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            KADUPUL FLOWER
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The uniqueness and beauty of this flower make it special around whole world. But it is mostly found in Sri Lanka. Just few people can see the beauty and attraction of this
          <strong>
           flower
          </strong>
          because it blooms mostly in night.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="KODUPUL FLOWER" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2620" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/KODUPUL-FLOWER.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            CAMPION
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This flower is found just in area of Gibraltar in British. It is also blooms in night and has a very splendid smell. The flower is in red color and looks so beautiful.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="CAMPION" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2614" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/CAMPION.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            CHERRY BLOSSOMS
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Cherry blossom is one of most beautiful and attractive flowers in the world. This flowers blooms in season of spring. The arrival is celebrated in US and Japan with full enjoyment.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="CHERRY BLOSSOMS" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2615" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/CHERRY-BLOSSOMS.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            PLUMERIA
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Basically this flower belongs to dog bane family. It comes in several varieties and found in Brazil and some areas of America. The flowers are found in red, blue and yellow color.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="PLUMERIA" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2622" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/PLUMERIA.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            CHOCOLATE COSMOS
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This kind of flower is most famous and rare flowers in the world. This flower basically blooms in Mexico and it is known as chocolate cosmos because it produces chocolate fragrance. These flowers are found in deep red and maroon color which looks so attractive.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="CHOCOLATE COSMOS" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2616" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/CHOCOLATE-COSMOS.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            JADE VINE
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          It is most unique and beautiful flower in the family of bean and pea. It can be found just in Philippines. The flower color varies from blue to green. This flower has claw shaped flowers which look so impressive and marvelous.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="jade vine" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2619" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/jade-vine.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            CHRYSANTHEMUM
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This flower is known as father of advanced taxonomy. The meaning if its name is golden flower in words of Greek. These flowers arrive in various kinds but most generally color is yellow in which they are found.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="CHRYSANTHEMUM" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2617" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/CHRYSANTHEMUM.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            DAHLIA
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          It is one of most beautiful and rare flowers in the world. It comes in verity of form sand kinds. The small form is of 2 inch and big is of 1 foot. These flowers come in a number of color like red, yellow, white and etc.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DAHLIA" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2618" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/DAHLIA.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            ORCHIDS
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This flower has almost 25000 classes and it is found in whole world. It is most well-known among all lovers of flower. As it have unique features in color and kind as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="ORCHIDS" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2621" height="480" src="http://news-fly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/ORCHIDS.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            WATER LILIES
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Lilies are unusually stunning flowers which come with a fluctuating floor-covering of greenery (lily pads). The bloom continues for about 3 days and then it is substituted. Water lilies come in diverse colors and are often perfumed.
         </p>
         <p>
