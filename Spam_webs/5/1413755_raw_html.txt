         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Researchers study more than 15,000 penises to determine average lengths around the world
         </h1>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Finally, a research which may lay to rest a number of the lingering fallacies about penis measurement and alleviate the woes shared by many males worldwide. Based on new analysis, the average erect penis size is simply over thirteen centimeters, or around 5 inches. Moreover, theres no sturdy affiliation between foot size and penis size, so girls can stop judging males by the dimensions of their sneakers. You possibly can learn all the examine in BJU International.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Though many evaluate themselves to the average penis measurement, what this determine truly is has been surprisingly difficult to find out. As pointed out by Science, thats primarily as a result of most research have relied on self-reporting, which isnt very correct. To get around this downside and acquire a extra dependable information set, a team of U.K. researchers decided to compile measurements taken by health professionals who adopted a standardized measuring process. They included each girth (the circumference on the base or the middle) and size (pubic bone to the tip of the glans) measurements from 20 totally different research, which totaled 15,521 males from varied nations across the world. And the outcomes will probably be music to the ears of many.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The common flaccid penis was discovered to be 9.16 cm (3.61 inches) lengthy, whereas the common erect penis is 13.12 cm (5.sixteen inches) in size. When it comes to girth, the typical circumference of a flaccid penis turned out to be 9.31 cm (3.66 inches), and 11.66 cm (4.59 inches) for an erect one. Moreover, these at excessive ends of the spectrum have been discovered to be a lot much less widespread. For example, only 5 males out of every 100 have an erect penis longer than 16 cm (6.3 inches).
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Once they appeared for correlations with other physique options, such as testicular size, weight or foot size, no strong or constant associations have been discovered. Though they couldnt discover any vital proof for a relationship between penis dimension and race, they notice that conclusions cant be drawn because the vast majority of measurements had been from Caucasians.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          So whats the take home message? Males, dont be so hard on yourself as what you see on the TV or in magazines is not an correct illustration of the common penis. But if youre concerned, be sure tore measuring correctly.
