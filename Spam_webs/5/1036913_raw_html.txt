        <h2 class="entry-title">
         Undercity.org  Steve Duncan
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          As an urban historian &amp; photographer, I try to peel back the layers of a city to see whats underneath. From the tops of bridges to the depths of sewer tunnels, these explorations of the urban environment help me puzzle together the interconnected, multi-dimensional history and complexity of the great metropolises of the world.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          In the three links below, you can see me talk about sewers from TEDx Phoenixville 2012, follow my adventures through New York City with Andrew Wonder, or see a slideshow of photographs.
         </p>
