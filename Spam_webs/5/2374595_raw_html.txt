     <p>
      <b>
       Miscellaneous Symbols
      </b>
      is a
      <a href="/wiki/Unicode" title="Unicode">
       Unicode
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Unicode_block" title="Unicode block">
       block
      </a>
      (U+2600U+26FF) containing glyphs representing concepts from a variety of categories,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Astrological" title="Astrological">
       astrological
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Astronomical" title="Astronomical">
       astronomical
      </a>

      <a href="/wiki/Chess" title="Chess">
       chess
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Dice" title="Dice">
       dice
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Musical_notation" title="Musical notation">
       musical notation
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Political_symbol" title="Political symbol">
       political symbols
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Recycling" title="Recycling">
       recycling
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Religious_symbol" title="Religious symbol">
       religious symbols
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Trigram" title="Trigram">
       trigrams
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Warning_sign" title="Warning sign">
       warning signs
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="/wiki/Weather" title="Weather">
       weather
      </a>
      , among others.
