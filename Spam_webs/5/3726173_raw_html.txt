        <h2>
         Personalized Early Learning Fun
        </h2>
        <p>
         As moms we all know that kids co-operate and concentrate better if the task at hand includes something that interests them.
         <strong>
          Educators have found that young (and older) children learn much better when instruction and lessons are tailored to their interests, too.
         </strong>
         Well, I was beyond excited when
         <a href="http://timefortotslearningkits.com/?product=you-pick-custom-themed-kit#!prettyPhoto" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Time for Tots Learning Kits">
          Time for Tots
         </a>
         signed on as a sponsor of B-Inspired Mama, because thats their business; making early learning fun for kids and easy for moms and educators.
        </p>

         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Personalized Early Learning from Time for Tots
        </h3>
        <p>
         Nicole from Time for Tots was so kind to offer a learning kit for my preschooler, Priscilla. While Time for Tots offers tons of themed learning kits for various levels, we chose to order this
         <a href="http://timefortotslearningkits.com/?product=you-pick-custom-themed-kit#!prettyPhoto" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Custom Themed Kit from Time for Tots">
          Custom Themed Kit
         </a>
         personalized just for her with girly princesses.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cdn.b-inspiredmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Princess-Themed-Early-Learning-with-a-Time-for-Tots-Learning-Kit-at-B-InspiredMama.jpg">
          <img alt="Princess Themed Early Learning with a Time for Tots Learning Kit at B-InspiredMama.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3078" height="419" src="http://cdn.b-inspiredmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Princess-Themed-Early-Learning-with-a-Time-for-Tots-Learning-Kit-at-B-InspiredMama.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I was so impressed that it arrived all ready to go! No printing, cutting, laminating for me. Just nicely packaged game pieces and learning cards with clear directions for the various early learning activities. Awesome! Priscilla jumped right in with this fun princess and gem counting game.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cdn.b-inspiredmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Early-Learning-Fun-with-a-Time-for-Tots-Princess-Learning-Kit-at-B-InspiredMama.jpg">
          <img alt="Early Learning Fun with a Time for Tots Princess Learning Kit at B-InspiredMama.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3077" height="502" src="http://cdn.b-inspiredmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Early-Learning-Fun-with-a-Time-for-Tots-Princess-Learning-Kit-at-B-InspiredMama.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then she got some fine motor skill practice and number recognition learning with these number clip cards. She loved all of the kid-friendly princess designs. I know she worked longer with this personalized princess kit than she would have with a more generic one.
