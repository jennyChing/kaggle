        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Snowman Button Cards
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-16T05:00:21+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 16, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/author/blogging" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Stacey
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html/snowman_button_cards_cover" rel="attachment wp-att-3667">
          <img alt="Snowman Button Cards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3667" height="600" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/snowman_button_cards_cover-682x1024.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether buried in drifts or living in a more temperate climate  everyone LOVES a frosty friend! Enjoy building your own snowy creations with our
         <em>
          <strong>
           Snowman Button Card
          </strong>
         </em>
         tutorial. These cards are a perfect sentiment for the upcoming holidays or even just dropping a winter themed note.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether you fancy it as a card design or want to change it up &amp; make it into a gift tag  this craft is EASY to whip up! Give your frosty fellow a home with a handwritten message inside, while spreading some holiday cheer!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html/snowman_button_cards_7" rel="attachment wp-att-3665">
          <img alt="Snowman Button Cards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3665" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/snowman_button_cards_7-1024x682.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h1>
         <em>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            Supplies:
           </span>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </h1>
        <p>
         * Cardstock or Pre-made Cards
         <br/>
         * White Buttons
         <br/>
         * Decorative Red Bows
         <br/>
         * Red Santa Hats {Hobby Lobby}
         <br/>
         * Glue Dots
         <br/>
         * Sharpie
         <br/>
         * Ink/Holiday Stamp
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html/snowman_button_cards_6" rel="attachment wp-att-3664">
          <img alt="Snowman Button Cards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3664" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/snowman_button_cards_6-1024x682.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html/snowman_button_cards_5" rel="attachment wp-att-3663">
          <img alt="Snowman Button Cards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3663" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/snowman_button_cards_5-1024x682.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <h1>
         <strong>
          <em>
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            Directions:
           </span>
          </em>
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         * For each snowman card, you will need two white buttons, one red bow and one hat embellishment. Place a glue dot on the back of your buttons and place then on the front of your card, to look like a snowman. I even placed them with the holes of the buttons, looking like eyes.
         <br/>
         * Using your black Sharpie, draw on arms for the snowman.
         <br/>
         * To finish off the snowman, place a glue dot on the back of your red bow &amp; Santa hat and place them on the card.
         <br/>
         * Now stamp on your holiday embellishment and write a heart warming message inside.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html/snowman_button_cards_2" rel="attachment wp-att-3661">
          <img alt="Snowman Button Cards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3661" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/snowman_button_cards_2-1024x682.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/snowman-button-cards.html/snowman_button_cards_1" rel="attachment wp-att-3660">
          <img alt="Snowman Button Cards" class="aligncenter wp-image-3660" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/snowman_button_cards_1-1024x682.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you love handmade, youll LOVE sharing handmade crafts &amp; gifts even more this holiday season. Gather your materials &amp; start making these projects today! If you do decide to make them  please share a picture on our
         <a href="http://facebook.com/gluedtomycrafts" target="_blank">
          Facebook
         </a>
         page, so we can see our inspiration come to life! Happy Holidays my friends!
