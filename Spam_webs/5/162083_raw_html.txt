           <a href="http://www.womma.org/posts/2014/12/is-there-any-room-for-new-social-networks">
            Is there any room for new social networks?
           </a>
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span class="author">
           Author:
           <a href="http://www.womma.org/user/192">
            Mike Moran
           </a>
          </span>
          <div class="post-intro">
           <img alt="untitled.png" src="http://www.womma.org/files/large/x4a71cd351f30d431154ad44fdf70b2f3.png.pagespeed.ic.viPnp0zpdu.png"/>
           Not too long ago, you probably remember a period when you couldn't open your email without getting another request to join a new social network.
