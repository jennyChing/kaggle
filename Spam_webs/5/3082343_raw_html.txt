         <p>
          <img alt="Stainless steel DeLorean limo created by Rich Weissensel" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-773" height="380" src="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-limo-1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          This DeLorean stretch limo with three full sets of gull wing doors was created by Rich Weissensel. It began as a sketch and an order for a custom stainless steel frame after meeting John DeLorean at the DeLorean Car Show in 2000, and took the following 12 years to complete. Rich had hoped to give John a ride in his creation, but it was nowhere near completion when DeLorean died in March of 2005.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Stainless steel DeLorean limo created by Rich Weissensel" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-774" height="448" src="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-limo-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Stainless steel DeLorean limo created by Rich Weissensel" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-775" height="483" src="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-limo-3.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Stainless steel DeLorean limo created by Rich Weissensel" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-776" height="283" src="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-limo-4.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Stainless steel DeLorean limo created by Rich Weissensel" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-777" height="450" src="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-limo-5.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Rich is no stranger to crazy custom DeLoreans, as he is also the man responsible for D Rex, the
          <a href="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-monster-truck.jpg" rel="lightbox[772]" target="_blank">
           DeLorean monster truck
          </a>
          , a
          <a href="http://www.cultofweird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/delorean-hovercraft.jpg" rel="lightbox[772]" target="_blank">
           DeLorean hovercraft
          </a>
          , a DeLorean roadster and other projects.
         </p>
