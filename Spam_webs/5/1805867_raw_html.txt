       <div class="textcontent clearfix">
        <div>
         Not only is Tupac still alive, but P Diddy killed him. According to
         <a href="http://gaggedup.com/sean-puffy-combs-arrested-for-tupac-murder.php#sthash.KNkSBEEK.2lyaQcEw.dpbs" target="_blank">
          an article
         </a>
         posted earlier this month on gaggedup.coma source about as reliable as that crusty dude you met at the bar last weekendpeople close to the producer/mogul were able to confirm that he was arrested the morning of September 11th for his alleged involvement in the thus unsolved September 10, 1996 homicide of Tupac Shakur.
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Now, when I saw that people on my friends list were actually sharing this questionable report and getting gassed by it, my immediate reaction was:
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://oogeewoogee.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/LbyFu.gif">
          <img alt="LbyFu" class="size-full wp-image-11844 aligncenter img-responsive" height="200" src="http://oogeewoogee.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/LbyFu.gif" width="214"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div>
         No later did I feel compelled to perform a quick Google search in order to confirm my strong suspicion that the report was a hoax; you can always count on
         <a href="http://snopes.com/" target="_blank">
          snopes.com
         </a>
         to be the ghostbusters of online information!
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Lets not, however, let these recent events (or lack thereof) mislead us from the reality that Puff Daddy is a cold-blooded assassin. P Diddy is killing our kids and must be eradicated from the public sphere. Use the hashtag #stoppdaddy to bolster awareness of this sick fuck and prevent him from further feeding on the immortal souls of dead rappers.
        </div>
