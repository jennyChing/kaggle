         Massage &amp; Weight Loss: Relationship Explained!
        </h2>

        <p>
         <img alt="Massage &amp; Weight Loss" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-26" height="385" src="http://blog.phenocal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/weight-loss-relationship-explained.jpg" width="735"/>
         <br/>
         Ever thought massage could make you fat? Scientists are now aware of what massage, the powerful and effective tool for weight loss, can do for you.  Massage improves your bodys resilience, assisting in muscle nutrition and flexibility, and plays a psychological role for your wellbeing. If you aim to lose weight, massage will play an important role. The link between massage and weight loss is from a combination of factors, such as injury prevention and healing. Massage not only improves your muscle flexibility, but also makes you less hurtful in the gym.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What Does Massage Do?
        </h2>
        <p>
         During a massage, your blood circulation improves. By the persistent flow of blood in to the tissues, there is an increased supply of nutrients and oxygen.  The soothing cycles send you in to the much needed rest and relaxation mood. Your nervous system becomes relaxed. This means that a massage can cut short the recovery time during workouts making your fitness routine to run at full speed. In reference to Jeff Mann, the regional manager for Cortiva Institute Schools of Massage Therapy, the role of massage in weight loss seems like the padding on a steering wheel, whereby you cant do without it, but makes it a lot easier and more comfortable to hang on to.
        </p>
        <h2>
         How Does This Benefit Gym-Goer?
        </h2>
        <p>
         The role of massage in weight loss goes beyond what many people think. It helps you reduce cellulite by breaking down the subcutaneous tissue. Massage also helps in making your skin firm and supple, particularly for people with loose skin due to weight loss efforts. If you experience the after effects of losing weight, massage can do wonders in your struggle against the extra pounds. Massage not only relieves stress and pain, but also helps in treating depression and anxiety. However, it cannot replace the medical care that your doctor offers. In reference to a study conducted by SlimmingResource.com participants lost at least a centimeter from their waist area, credit to massage. Surprisingly, there was no reduction in overall body weight.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Massage and Body Fat
        </h2>
        <p>
         Are you wondering what a massage can do with your body fat? Its evident that a massage can accelerate some calorific energy consumption. Nonetheless, it cannot be as effective as exercising. Nevertheless, a massage carried out in your areas of fat can go a long way in helping the breakdown of the fatty cells, leading to weight loss. However, this is just a theory that lacks any scientific evidence. Another theory suggests that massaging your stomach helps the colon to empty properly. As a result, this aids in detoxification and clearing of the colon. This in turn makes your digestive system to absorb the right nutrients and eliminates the wastes effectively.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Stress and Weight Loss
        </h2>
        <p>
         It is common for some people to indulge in meals whenever they are stressed. The urge to eat doesnt come from hunger but as way to express some emotion such as sadness, anger or frustration. Cookies, chocolates, or other sweeter foods are the mostly consumed foods by people with stress. Additionally, stress hormone, cortisol, has been associated with buildup of abdominal fat. Nevertheless, stress can lead to loss of appetite resulting to weight loss. In some rare cases, stress can over-stimulate your thyroid gland leading to an increase in appetite and also burning off of the calories. If stress is interfering with your weight loss efforts, the good thing to do is tune in into why you are taking more portions. Before snacking or taking a meal, ask yourself if you are actually hungry.
        </p>
        <h2>
         How to Incorporate Massage in Your Fitness Routine?
        </h2>
        <p>
         Massage has a number of benefits. It increases the flow of blood to the muscles for better nourishment. Massage aids in the elimination of lactic acid and other toxins in the muscles. Other than improving flexibility and range of motion in muscles and joints, massage can help in weight loss by making your body absorb and burn fat stored under your skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you cant find out a professional massage therapist, you can do it on your own. Start by loosening your muscles in a hot bath for about 20 minutes. Concentrate more on the sore muscles. Massage using your fingers in small circles breathing slowly and deeply during the process. Consume a lot of water afterwards to flush out the toxins.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Precautions
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Do not engage in any exercise program without consulting a therapist or a physician.
         </li>
         <li>
          Moderate exercises are the best to start with to avoid overuse of the affected limb.
         </li>
         <li>
          Incorporate deep abdominal breathing before and after every workout session.
         </li>
         <li>
          Ensure you are wearing compression garment during exercise, except while under water.
         </li>
         <li>
          Avoid tight and restrictive clothing during workouts.
         </li>
         <li>
          Consume plenty of water after every exercise to stay hydrated.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         Conclusion
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you engage in regular workouts, you can easily add massage to your fitness plan. Consider professional massage once or twice a week if possible. A sport massage can do better if you exercise more often. It should be administered within two hours after a workout. You can find it in most spas and gyms. Additionally, you can go for pre-workouts massage which stimulates your muscles to enhance your workout. These are usually administered 45 minutes before a workout.
        </p>
