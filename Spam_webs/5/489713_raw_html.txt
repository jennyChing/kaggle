        <p>
         <em>
          This
          <strong>
           Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta Sauce
          </strong>
          is rich and decadent tasting, but oh so light. A unique technique is used to create a creamy texture with virtually no fat.
         </em>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-cover" rel="attachment wp-att-14324">
          <img alt="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta  Jeanette's Healthy Living" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14324 pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta Sauce Recipe ~ http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com" data-pin-url="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html" height="505" src="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Creamy-Tomato-Crab-Pasta-Cover.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This pasta dish is something I came up with just before my oldest son returned to college a few weeks ago. As a poor college student, he always looks forward to eating at home, so I try to make it special, especially the last few days hes with us.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta" rel="attachment wp-att-14327">
          <img alt="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta  Jeanette's Healthy Living" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14327 pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta Sauce Recipe ~ http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com" data-pin-url="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html" height="960" src="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Creamy-Tomato-Crab-Pasta.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         My son loves crab in just about everything, so I decided to make crab pasta in a creamy tomato sauce, just a healthier version. Often crab pasta is made with heavy cream, and although its always a treat to eat something so decadent, I wanted to make a creamy pasta dish that was actually pretty light, but tasted rich and creamy.
        </p>
        <p>
         The toasted garlicky bread crumbs on top make this crab pasta dish extra fun to eat. At first my boys were skeptical of these little crunchy bits, but I found them sneaking more on top.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta2" rel="attachment wp-att-14326">
          <img alt="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta  Jeanette's Healthy Living" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14326 pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta Sauce Recipe ~ http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com" data-pin-url="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html" height="640" src="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Creamy-Tomato-Crab-Pasta2.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Recently, I read in
         <em>
          The Yogurt Cookbook
         </em>
         by Arto Der Haroutunian that if you mix yogurt with an egg before adding it to a sauce, that the yogurt wont separate. Ive been using that trick in all sorts of recipes and it works great.
        </p>
        <p>
         Greek yogurt is a great substitute for full-fat products in so many recipes, both cooking and baking. Ive used it in
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2010/09/healthier-tailgating-crab-dip-recipe-and-buffalo-chicken-dip-recipe.html">
          dips
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2012/09/roasted-beet-and-apple-walnut-salad-with-honey-yogurt-dressing.html">
          dressings
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/08/smoked-red-curry-coconut-chicken.html">
          marinades
         </a>
         , in
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/08/creamy-smoked-gouda-macaroni-and-cheese.html">
          macaroni and cheese
         </a>
         , and in baked goods as a substitute for mayonnaise, sour cream, cream cheese, butter and heavy cream.
        </p>
        <p>
         In this
         <strong>
          Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta
         </strong>
         recipe, Greek yogurt replaces the heavy cream yet yields a rich creamy sauce.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html/creamy-tomato-pasta3" rel="attachment wp-att-14325">
          <img alt="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta  Jeanette's Healthy Living" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14325 pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="Creamy Tomato Crab Pasta Sauce Recipe ~ http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com" data-pin-url="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2013/09/creamy-tomato-crab-pasta-sauce.html" height="953" src="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Creamy-Tomato-Pasta3.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         When I told my son that there was yogurt in his pasta, he couldnt believe it. I hope youll give this pasta recipe a try. Its our new favorite.
        </p>

           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 cloves garlic, minced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 pound lump or jumbo fresh crab meat
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon red pepper flakes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">

             cup Citron vodka
           </li>

             cup non-fat Greek yogurt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">

         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Directions
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Breadcrumb Topping
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place breadcrumbs, oil, and garlic in saute pan and toss well. Season with salt and pepper. Cook over medium-low heat until browned and crunchy. Set aside.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Crab Pasta Sauce
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large saute pan; add garlic and saute until lightly browned. Remove half of the garlic from the pan and set aside. Add crab meat to pan and toss gently. Remove to a bowl and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Return reserved garlic to pan and add 1 tablespoon olive oil and red pepper flakes; heat. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down, about 10 minutes. Add vodka and cook 3-4 minutes for flavor to get absorbed. Whisk together yogurt and egg in a small bowl; slowly whisk into tomatoes; heat through. Transfer to a blender and blend until smooth. Return pureed sauce to pan; season with salt and pepper. Add sauteed crab meat and heat through.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cook pasta, reserving one cup of pasta water.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            To serve, toss cooked pasta with Crab Pasta Sauce, adding some pasta water as needed to loosen up pasta. Sprinkle with scallions and Breadcrumb Topping just before serving.
           </li>
          </ol>
