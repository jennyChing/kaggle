    <h1>
     Orange Julius
    </h1>
    <h2>
     Ingredients:
    </h2>
    <p>
     1 cup orange juice
     <br/>
     1 cup water
     <br/>
     2 egg whites
     <br/>
     3/4 teaspoon 
		vanilla extract
     <br/>
     1/4 cup sugar
     <br/>
     1 heaping cup ice
     <br/>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Directions:
    </h2>
    <p>
     Combine all of the ingredients in a blender set on high speed for 15-30 
		seconds.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Pour into glasses and serve.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Recipe Makes 2 
		Orange Julius drinks.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     This recipe is courtesy of Helen and is a 
		close clone recipe
     <br/>
    </p>
