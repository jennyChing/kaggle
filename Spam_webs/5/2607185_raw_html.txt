       <div class="post-description">
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p>
         If youre just starting out with your own business and want to know the tax basics, then this video below is a great guide on what to do and what not to do when getting your business off the ground.
        </p>
        <p>
         Anita Monteith, ICAEW Technical Manager, SME &amp; Personal Tax, provides some really useful tips to make sure youre prepared for the road ahead based around the three Rs: Register, Records and Returns.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether you want a bit more clarity on tax relief, tax returns, business car mileage or the minimum details you need to register your business, this quick guide gives you a good overview, alongside why using an ICAEW accountant can save you time and hassle.
        </p>
        <p>
         Have a look  5 tips in just 4 minutes!
