            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>

            </a>
           </p>
           <p>

            <strong>
             oval, teardrop and square
            </strong>

            <a href="http://fashionsnug.com/2014/beautiful-earring-designs-for-eid-ul-fitr-2014/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              earring designs
             </strong>
            </a>
            are ideal for
            <strong>
             narrow face
            </strong>
            . These earrings normally feature a single stone or gemstone.

            <strong>
             2. Stud Earrings:
            </strong>
           </p>

            </a>
           </p>
           <p>

            <strong>
             oval, square and circle
            </strong>
            . Studs are usually available in round but also available in oval and square shapes. These stud earrings are enough to wear in both casual and formal occasion.
           </p>

            <strong>
             3. Chandelier Earrings:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://fashionsnug.com/2014/5-latest-earrings-designs-2014/top-5-earring-designs-2014-for-women-10/" rel="attachment wp-att-37178">
             <img alt="Top 5 Latest Earrings Designs 2014 For Women Chandelier Earrings" class="aligncenter wp-image-37178 size-full" height="450" rel="tc-fancybox-group37170" src="http://fashionsnug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Top-5-Earring-Designs-2014-For-Women-10.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            This type of earrings are very popular among girls. It looks like a chandelier that hangs over the dining table. The chandelier earrings are an intricate ear piercing with eye-catching look. Mostly, they feature a wide middle section with few large stones and narrows at the bottom with small gemstones. These types of earrings are pairing with
            <strong>
             oval and square
            </strong>
            face shapes.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             4. Cluster Earrings:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://fashionsnug.com/2014/5-latest-earrings-designs-2014/top-5-earring-designs-2014-for-women-15/" rel="attachment wp-att-37183">
             <img alt="Top 5 Latest Earrings Designs 2014 For Women Cluster Earrings" class="aligncenter wp-image-37183 size-full" height="800" rel="tc-fancybox-group37170" src="http://fashionsnug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Top-5-Earring-Designs-2014-For-Women-15.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Cluster earrings are the most favorite among women. These type of earrings feature many small gemstones on a circular piece of metal. There earrings always look stylish and perfect for every occasion that requires formal attire. These earring are coming a head in a variety of two simple designs. Either it loos like a
            <strong>
             bunch of grapes
            </strong>
            with many small gems along straight bar in circular design with a cluster of stones close together with a short bar. These earrings are best for
            <strong>
             narrow and wide
            </strong>
            both type of faces.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             5. Teardrop Earrings:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://fashionsnug.com/2014/5-latest-earrings-designs-2014/top-5-earring-designs-2014-for-women-13/" rel="attachment wp-att-37181">
             <img alt="Top 5 Latest Earrings Designs 2014 For Women Teardrop Earrings" class="aligncenter wp-image-37181 size-full" height="450" rel="tc-fancybox-group37170" src="http://fashionsnug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Top-5-Earring-Designs-2014-For-Women-13.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Teardrop earrings are usually wearing in formal occasions. It looks like a teardrop shape. The teardrop gemstones attached to a bar or a thin chain that dangles from a stud it in a place. They are varying in different sizes and shapes. These
            <a href="http://fashionsnug.com/2014/latest-trend-of-hoop-earrings-for-girls/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              earrings
             </strong>
            </a>
            are perfect for
            <strong>
             wide-short face
            </strong>
            shapes as we all as long narrow shape.
