         <p>
          There are dozens of real estate websites out there, ranging from those managed by individual agents and real estate brokerage sites to portal sites that try to aggregate listings of homes for sale. A main difference between these types of sites is the quantity and quality of the listing inventory displayed. According to recent studies, the best sites to visit to ensure access to all of the agent-listed homes for sale are real estate brokerage sites such as Weichert.com, which features more than 1.5 million listings.
         </p>
         <p>
          A big misconception by consumers is that a broker website only lists homes for sale by that real estate company. In reality, because of how real estate companies share listings on Multiple Listing Services, many of these sites actually provide access to all homes on the market from all real estate companies, not just their own. And, because the information comes straight from the Multiple Listing Service, it is also more accurate than what can be found on other real estate search sites.
         </p>
         <p>
          Another thing people may not know is that you dont have to contact the real estate agent whose name is on the sign in order to get more information about a home. Any real estate agent from any company can access the Multiple Listing Service and answer questions about the home and set up a time to view it.
         </p>
         <p>
          With good homes often selling quickly, it makes it that much more important to get in touch with an agent as soon as possible. At Weichert.com, we are proud of our focus on customer satisfaction and typically respond to customer inquires in less than three minutes. This compares quite favorably to the average industry response time of 917 minutes (or 15.29 hours), according to a white paper prepared by the leading consulting firm WAV Group.
