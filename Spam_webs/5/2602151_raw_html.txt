                       Ever have aneating week where pretty much you feel like its all you had done? Basically every fraction of the day is centered around the meal you
                       <em>
                        had

                       , the meal you are

                       ? Now, in my defense (hand to the jury) we were on vacation, so cooking and weekly meal planning was not so much part of the agenda to say the least. (We all need a break sometimes, right?? :-) But, reality is here and its time to get back on cooking track! So..here we have a delightful veggie dish that sails us right back into some healthy culinary fare. The key to this dish is the baking and the brushing. You want a really hearty cooking time on the tofu and along with a double coating of the Hoisin-Teriyaki Mixture. I tossed in a couple of carrots and bell strips to the pan but add what ere veggie you like. A sprinkle of your favorite nut and you wont even realize that this lovely dish is your very own doing!
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       <strong>
                        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                         Ingredients
                        </span>
                       </strong>
                      </p>
                      <ul>
                       <li>
                        2 tablespoon Hoisin sauce (Otherwise known as an Asian Barbecue Sauce)
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        2 garlic cloves grated
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        1 knob ginger grated (about 1 tablespoon)
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        1-16 oz block firm light tofu completely drained, patted with all excess liquid removed. (Wrap snuggly for 20-30 minutes in a paper towel prior to slicing.)
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <p>
                       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                        <strong>
                         Directions
                        </strong>
                       </span>
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       1.) Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       2.) Slice tofu into eight pieces. (Using a sharp knife, slice width first into four and then each piece lengthwise into four again.)
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       3.) In a small bowl whisk together first 5 ingredients into a marinade.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       4.) Spray a small foil-lined cookie sheet and line up tofu slices making sure that they do not touch one another.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       5.) Using a small pastry brush, brush the tofu with the marinade coating all sides.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       6.) Place in oven and cook for 20 minutes; baste again with marinade and cook for another 20-30 minutes until the outside is a deep golden color.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       Nics Tips  To serve with roasted carrots and peppers simply toss them with a little sesame oil and roast right alongside tofu. Sprinkle with cashews and serve!
                      </p>
