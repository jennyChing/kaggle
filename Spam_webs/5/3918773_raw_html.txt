                  <h2 class="short-teaser">
                   The new trailer for Sharknado 2: The Second One explains the whole sharkstorm in a New York Minute
                  </h2>
                  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                   <div class="field-items">
                    <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                     <p>
                      Hey, you wanna know everything you wanna know about SyFys upcoming sequel to
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        Sharknado
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      ,
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        Sharknado 2: The Second One
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      ? Well, fuggedaboutit.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The new trailer uses a guy with an obvious California twang explaining the backstory of
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        Sharknado
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      in a faux New Yorkese so offensive the sharks are eating themselves to get away from it. So heres what you need to know The first
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        Sharknado
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      was a huge hit when it took a bite out of the West Coast. What happened was that these sharks got caught up in a tornado funnel and landed on the California beaches teeth first. John Heard swallowed his pride and played a drunk who wouldnt leave his stool. Ian Ziering played a guy named Fin who ran a bar on the beach. Tara Reid played the rest of the shark. Now the sharks want their fin back and are searching New York City.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Ian Ziering and Tara Reid will be joined by Kelly Osbourne, Mark McGrath, Vivica A. Fox, Andy Dick,
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        30 Rock
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      alum Judah Friedlander, Perez Hilton, Biz Markie, professional wrestler Kurt Angle, Robert Klein and Sandra Denton, aka Salt-N-Pepa's Pepa. Judd Hirsch will play on his old role in the TV sitcom
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        Taxi
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      .
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <em>
                       <strong>
                        Sharknado 2: The Second One
                       </strong>
                      </em>
                      will premiere on The SyFy channel on Wednesday, July 30 at 9/8 central time.
