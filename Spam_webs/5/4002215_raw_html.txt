          <p class="intro-education">
           Animated African dancing, flying cars, battered bicycles and river-dancing - just some of the things you can expect to find in the BFI's
           <em>
            Box of Delights
           </em>
           , a free resource for the Primary classroom.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Box of Delights
           </em>
           has been developed to support all Primary teachers in using short films and extracts in the classroom. The nine titles in this collection have been chosen for their breadth and diversity and can be used to support literacy as well as Art and Design, Citizenship, History, Music, Drama, Science and cross-curricular projects.
          </p>
          <p>
           Follow the links below to access the films and extracts in full as well as a range of teaching ideas, key questions, lesson plans and downloadable resources linked to the Key Stage 2 curriculum.
           <em>
            Teaching Techniques
           </em>
           is a broader introduction to ways of thinking about and using short films in the classroom. You can also visit the dedicated Primary web pages on the
           <a href="http://www.bfi.org.uk/education/teaching/primary.html" target="_blank">
            BFI website
           </a>
           .
          </p>
