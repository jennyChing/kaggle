        <img alt="Crown Spa Hotel, Scarborough" class="size-full wp-image-318" height="460" src="http://yorkshire.weddingvenue.info/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Crowns-Spa-Hotel.jpg" width="840"/>

       <p>
        <strong>
         A Hotel as your dream wedding venue
        </strong>
        <br/>
        Selecting your dream wedding venue amongst the many venues available is a difficult task. And most time you just dont have the time available to hike around the countryside to find that perfect spot. Before taking off time to consider what type of venue you might want to go for think about a hotel as the best solution.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Your wedding reception in a Hotel
        </strong>
        <br/>
        One thing is sure about holding a wedding reception in a hotel and that is that there are multitudes of choices available. In fact it could be an overwhelming experience in itself. Where to start you might even wonder.
       </p>
       <p>
        There will be modern hotels with banquet halls to tiny boutique hotels with intimate style facilities. The range is from huge to tiny. Perhaps at this stage it might even be an idea to consider how large your wedding party will be before even looking at options. The size of your guest list could be your determining factor.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Here are some general pros
        </strong>
        <br/>
        One of the big pros could be that the hotel banquet staff know what they are doing. You will have the support of staff who know everything there needs to be known about holding a function.
       </p>
       <p>
        They will be able to advise you on all of the components necessary to make sure your event runs smoothly. They will have venue decorators, florists on speed dial, all the tables and chairs, table linen and more available for you. And of course the catering and bar will be available for you to use.
       </p>
       <p>
        The hotel will also be able to provide you with rooms for the bridal party to change in, rest rooms for elderly guests, a person who will be on call during the duration of the event, security staff and often full parking facilities.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Some cons to consider
        </strong>
        <br/>
        Working with hotels will mean that your prices are not going to be as flexible. The hotel has to cover its overheads and is not able to negotiate huge discounts with you. Your pricing will therefore usually be fairly set with some room to negotiate for free extras. You might be able to get some extra services for free. As deal sweeteners so to speak. The only time you will be able to negotiate a little more is if you are able to hold your wedding during the hotels quiet times.
       </p>
       <p>
        In many cases hotels, especially the larger ones, tend to have very bland facilities. They are safe but can of course also be soulless. Of course it means you can decorate and create your own theme and atmosphere. So add this to your costs. But it does mean that intimate and romantic might have to give way to stylish and elegant.
       </p>
       <p>
        You will also not have as much leeway when it comes to menus and your bar bill might be higher as you will be paying corkage charges. You might also have restrictions in terms of access to the venue and find that your entertainment in the form of your music is restricted. The hotel does need to consider its guests.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Hotels as wedding venues can be winners
        </strong>
        <br/>
        For your important event the right choice of a wedding venue can be the big decision you need to make. Its especially important in terms of your budget as the reception will be a costly part of the overall expense of your wedding. It is therefore the one area where you have to be extra cautious. Hotels as wedding venues can be a very good choice.
       </p>
