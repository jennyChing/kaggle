         Duelling pianos. Literally.
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta-container">
        <ul class="post-meta">
         <li class="author">
          <a href="/author/liz">
           Liz Upton
          </a>
         </li>
         <li class="date">
          <a href="https://www.raspberrypi.org/blog/duelling-pianos-literally/">
           4th Mar 2015
          </a>
         </li>
         <li class="num-comments">
          <a class="open-comments-top" href="https://www.raspberrypi.org/blog/duelling-pianos-literally/#comments">

      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        When someone mailed me a link to this performance, I assumed it was going to be one of those setups where two pianists play jolly tunes together in a bar. How wrong I was.
       </p>
       <p>
        These two pianists (Alvise Sinivia and Lo Jassef from the Conservatoire National de Paris) are trying to kill each other in Streetfighter. Tunefully.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="embedly-embed" frameborder="0" height="281" scrolling="no" src="//cdn.embedly.com/widgets/media.html?src=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fembed%2F7v2B71RUaqQ%3Ffeature%3Doembed&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fwatch%3Fv%3D7v2B71RUaqQ&amp;image=http%3A%2F%2Fi.ytimg.com%2Fvi%2F7v2B71RUaqQ%2Fhqdefault.jpg&amp;key=c11abcc1b1e44d61afc42b3ded19bfce&amp;type=text%2Fhtml&amp;schema=youtube" width="500">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        These pianos have been transformed by Eric and Cyril of
        <a href="http://www.foobarflies.io/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.foobarflies.io/', 'Foobarflies');" target="_blank">
         Foobarflies
        </a>
        (
        <del>
         let me know your full names, Eric and Cyril, so I can add them here!
        </del>
        theyre Eric Redon and Cyril Chapellier, says Giorgio in the comments; thanks, Giorgio!) into Playstation controllers, using piezo triggers, a Pi, some Arduinos, and some custom Python firmware. The installation was set up for the reopening of Paris Maison de la Radio, now a cultural space open to the public.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="pianos" class=" size-large wp-image-12281 aligncenter" height="284" src="/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/pianos-500x284.png" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Eric and Cyril have made a
        <a href="http://www.foobarflies.io/pianette/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.foobarflies.io/pianette/', 'comprehensive writeup');" target="_blank">
         comprehensive writeup
        </a>
        available (and if, like me, youre terribly excited by the insides of pianos, youll love this one). This is one of my favourite projects in ages  thanks Foobarflies!
       </p>
