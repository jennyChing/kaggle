             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
               <span style="color: #000000;">

               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">

              </span>

              <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: black; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% white;">
               <span style="color: #008080;">
                <strong>
                 <img alt="" height="135" src="/images/fertilitytreatment1.jpeg" style="margin: 4px; float: right;" title="Fertility Treatment" width="141"/>
                 Assisted Reproductive Technology:
                </strong>
               </span>
               It is also known as ART treatment. It is a method by which a couple can conceive a baby by a partially artificial method. It is a reproductive technology to cure the problem of
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                <a href="/index.php/health-care/infertility/147-causes-of-infertility" target="_self" title="Causes of Infertility">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  infertility
                 </span>
                </a>
               </span>
               . ART treatment also focuses on delivering a healthy baby, if there is a problem of AIDS to any of the parents. It focuses that the baby is secure and safe without any mental disability and without any sign of the disease.
              </span>
             </p>
             <h2 style="margin: 4.8pt 0in 6pt; line-height: 14.4pt; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% white; text-align: justify;">

               </strong>
              </span>
             </h2>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
               In vitro fertilization technique the fertilization of the male gamete and the female gamete takes place. But the fertilization of the sperm and the egg takes place outside the body of the female.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
               <img alt="" src="/images/treatmentoffertility123.jpeg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Vitro Fertilization"/>
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
              <span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #008080; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
               <strong>
                <span style="line-height: 115%;">
                 Technique Followed During Vitro Fertilization
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </h3>
             <ol style="list-style-type: upper-roman; text-align: justify;">
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   OCR:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  It is known by the name of Transvaginal Ovum Retrieval. In this procedure, a small needle is inserted into the vagina. The location of the needle is seen through the ultrasound machine. The needle moves from vagina to the ovarian follicle, from there it gather the fluids that include the eggs.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <img alt="" src="/images/techniquesof fertilization.jpeg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="techniques of Fertilization"/>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   Transfer of Embryo:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  In this technique the embryo is placed in the vagina of the female. It is done to obtain pregnancy.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
             </ol>
             <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
              <span style="color: #008080; font-size: 14pt; font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
               <strong>
                <span style="line-height: 115%;">
                 Expansion of IVF
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </h2>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
               The expansion of IVF requires the techniques that are followed in vitro fertilization but the opposite is not true.
              </span>
             </p>
             <ol style="list-style-type: upper-roman; text-align: justify;">
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   AZH:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 it is known as Assisted Zona Hatching. This technique is performed prior to the time when the embryo is placed inside the uterus of the female. In this process a small opening is made in outer region that is surrounding the egg. This is done so that the embryo emerges out and helps in process of growing the embryo.zz
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <img alt="" src="/images/expansionofivf.jpef.png" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="expansion of image"/>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   ICSI:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 it is known as Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection. It is a technique use when the problem is conceiving is due to male infertility. In this case there is less number of sperms in the body of male. In this process a sperm is injected into the centre part of the egg with the help of a needle. This technique is also followed when the donor sperms are used in order to achieve positive
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <a href="/index.php/pregnancy/82-symptoms-of-early-pregnancy" target="_self" title="Synptoms of Early Pregnancy">
                   <span style="color: #008080;">
                    pregnancy
                   </span>
                  </a>
                 </span>
                 . It is done to the people who have failed the prior treatment of IVF.
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="text-decoration: none;">
                    Autologous Endometrial Coculture
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  :
                 </span>
                 This treatment is done for the people who have a low quality of embryo in the body. The fertilized eggs are placed on the top layer of cells in patient uterine lining. This is done in order to provide a normal surrounding for the embryo to grow.
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <strong>
                  <span style="color: #008080;">
                   ZIFT:
                  </span>
                 </strong>
                 It is known by the name of Zygote Intrafallopian Transfer. In this the eggs are removed from the ovaries of the women. The eggs will fertilize in the laboratory. The resulting fertilized egg is known as zygote. Then the zygote is placed in the fallopian tube.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <img alt="" src="/images/expandivf.jpeg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="expansion of ivf"/>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #333300;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="color: #008080;">
                    Cytoplasm Transfer:
                   </span>
                   T
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 his is a procedure which requires a donor. The donor will donate a fertile egg. Then the content of the fertile egg are transferred to the infertile egg of the women along with the sperm, in order to make the infertile egg fertile and ready to conceive.
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   Egg Donor:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 This process is done for the women who do not have eggs. The availability of no eggs is due to surgery or any other medical treatment such as chemotherapy. In this method the eggs are taken from the ovary of donor. The egg along with the sperm is fertilized in the lab. The healthy embryo is then returned back to the uterus.
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   Gestational Carrier:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 It is process that is done when the patient has ovaries but it does not have any uterus. The absence of the uterus is due to any medical surgery.
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: book antiqua,palatino; color: #000000;">
                 <span style="color: #008080;">
                  <strong>
                   Sperm Donation:
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                 This technique can be followed if the women who want to conceive have no husband or a male partner, sperm count is less in the male partner or if the partner has some disease. In order to have a safe baby this technique is selected.
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
             </ol>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
