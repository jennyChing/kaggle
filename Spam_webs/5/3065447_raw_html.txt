           Have you ever Mod Podged a table? Re-doing furniture is pretty much my most favorite thing ever, and I dont get to do these projects nearly as much as I like. My apartment is small, but soon Im going to be moving into a new space which will allow me to makeover as much furniture as I want! Im going to start with a large desk table first, and I cant wait. To get inspired, I put together this roundup of Mod Podge table projects. Im not sure which one Ill use yet, but Ill let you know when I figure out what Im doing. In the meantime, I think you should Mod Podge a table too. Youll need ideas, so click through the slideshow below to see my picks. I think youll like them!
