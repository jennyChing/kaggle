     <div class="single-post-title">
      Collectors preview: Worlds most expensive pocket watch
     </div>
     <div class="post-2051 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-basel-world category-collectors-preview tag-basel-shows tag-collectors-preview-worlds-most-expensive-pocket-watch tag-henry-graves tag-luxury-swiss-watchmaker-patek-philippe tag-sothebys-auction-in-geneva tag-swiss-made-pocket-watch">
      <div class="content clearfix">
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="8142323" data-link="http://baselshows.com/basel-world/collectors-preview-worlds-most-expensive-pocket-watch" data-summary="" data-title="Collectors preview: Worlds most expensive pocket watch">
       </div>
       <p>
        A Swiss-made pocket watch was last week sold for $24 million at a
        <strong>
         <span style="color: #00ffff;">
          <a href="http://www.sothebys.com/en.html" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #00ffff;">
            Sothebys
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </strong>
        auction in Geneva, the most ever paid for a timepiece.
       </p>
       <p>
        This timepiece by
        <strong>
         <span style="color: #00ffff;">
          <a href="http://baselshows.com/basel-world/baselworld-2014-preview-patek-philippe" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #00ffff;">
            Patek Philippe
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </strong>
        boasts 24 complications, or features, besides the basic telling of time. Some of these functions include showing moon phases, the time of sunset and a perpetual calendar that wont require resetting until the year 2100. The device has 920 individual parts, including 430 screws, 110 wheels and 70 jewels.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Henry Graves Jr. Supercomplication is named after a New York banker who competed with the car manufacturer James Ward Packard to commission the most elaborate watch possible in the early 20th century.
       </p>
       <p>
        With this sale Sothebys re-established its supreme status as the most valuable timepiece in auction history, beating the $11 million record set in 1999.
       </p>
