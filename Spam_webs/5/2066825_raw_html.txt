&lt;h2 class='post-title entry-title'&gt;
&lt;a href='http://www.belleindisguise.com/2015/03/bye-bye-insecurities.html'&gt;Bye-bye, Insecurities&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class='byline'&gt;By &lt;span&gt;&lt;a href='https://plus.google.com/103648495859698755534' rel='author'&gt;Arlette Sharie&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt; on 3:09 PM&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class='post-header-line-1'&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class='post-body entry-content'&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-KQNyAI1N7WI/VPNihH-_N1I/AAAAAAAABEs/3Gtvp57iNyE/s1600/body%2Binsecurities%2Bb%26w%2Bsml.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img border="0" height="476" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-KQNyAI1N7WI/VPNihH-_N1I/AAAAAAAABEs/3Gtvp57iNyE/s1600/body%2Binsecurities%2Bb%26w%2Bsml.jpg" width="640" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
I don&#8217;t think any human can say they have ALWAYS felt comfortable with their body. Like everyone else, we have certain insecurities that we think are totally noticeable. But can I tell you a secret? People notice their own insecurities far, far, far more than anyone else does. Heck, it&#8217;s sad, but just as we have our own insecurities, other people have them too; and they are probably too busy acknowledging their own to pay any attention to anyone else&#8217;s.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
We make far too big of a deal about our insecurities. Whether it is eye distance, nose shape, eyebrow thickness, hair type, beauty marks, weight&#8230; There are so many of them! It&#8217;s like a burden that brings us down and doesn&#8217;t let us enjoy our life because of how hyperaware we are of them. Well, it&#8217;s time to make them go away.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
First, don&#8217;t compare yourself with others. Especially not models and celebrities whose job requires them to have a personal trainer, strict diet, beauty treatments... Plus, the photos taken of them are obviously edited before being printed or published. We can&#8217;t compete with the final product, not because we are not good enough, but because the final product is not real.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Second, accept yourself the way you are. Those &#8220;insecurities&#8221; are what make you, you. See them in a positive way. They are your personal traits that only you have. And just like you, they are unique. So make the best of them. Rock &#8216;em!&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Third, and final tip, there is nothing more attractive than self confidence. It is truly the key to looking the best. With confidence a person can become bolder and happier. No beauty product, or anything money can buy, can compare to what confidence can do to a person; it literally transforms them.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Show the world how you can uncloak your insecurities to reveal the hidden beauty beneath.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align: center;"&gt;
Wish you the best,&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align: center;"&gt;
