             "One of the holiday decorations at the White House is a 400-pound gingerbread house. Isn't that nice? And in front of that is a 400-pound ginger bread foreclosed sign."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/conan-o'brien.html" target="noframes">
              Conan-O'brien
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "I was in the supermarket today, and I saw some Occupy Wall Street protesters in the dairy department. They were protesting the 1 percent milk."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jay-leno.html" target="noframes">
              Jay Leno
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "There's a man in Utah recovering from wounds after his dog shot him in the butt. The police think its because the man was wearing a Michael Vick jersey."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/craigferguson.html" target="noframes">
              Craig Ferguson
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "The number of households that own a television set is down for the first time since they started the survey. This is America! The only excuse for not having a TV in your home is, you're too fat to fit into Best Buy to get one."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jimmykimmel.html" target="noframes">
              Jimmy Kimmel
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "It is the holiday season over at the White House. The theme for this years Christmas is Shine, Give, Share. While rumor is, the theme of next years White House Christmas will be 'Clean, Pack, Move.'"
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jimmyfallon.html" target="noframes">
              Jimmy Fallon
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "High winds have been pummeling California for the last two days. It was so windy, all the women in Beverly Hills looked like Nancy Pelosi." Jay Leno
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Here in Los Angeles, we're getting a Harry Potter theme park. I dont know how excited I can pretend to be. Im a middle-aged dude." Craig Ferguson
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "The Senate is doing its first-ever Secret Santa gift exchange this year. Yeah, theres a $10 spending limit, but they plan to go $14 trillion over budget." Jimmy Fallon
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Unemployment is down. People are out looking for work. That's good news. In fact, Herman Cain applied at Domino's and Pizza Hut."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jay-leno.html" target="noframes">
              Jay Leno
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "In a new interview, Larry King says he wants to be cryogenically frozen when he dies. When asked why, Larry said, 'I really miss the Ice Age.'" Conan O'brien
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "The post office is closed. And I thought that, on the one hand that's awful. On the other hand, it will take the sting out of my hate mail."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/david-letterman.html" target="noframes">
              David Letterman
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Over the weekend, Herman Cain dropped out the Republican presidential race. Cain said he wants to spend more time with the wife. Not his wife."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/craigferguson.html" target="noframes">
              Craig Ferguson
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "According to reports, one of Moammar Gadhafis sons had an elaborate plan to sneak into Mexico. Authorities broke up the plan before it took place. Believe me, if there's one thing Mexico will not stand for, its people sneaking over their border." Jay Leno
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "There are 17 more shopping days until Christmas. So, guys, that means 16 more days till we start shopping, right?" Conan O'brien
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Mitt Romney now says the gloves are coming off. And then Ron Paul said, 'And my teeth are coming out.'" David Letterman
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Alec Baldwin was kicked off an American Airlines flight yesterday. The good news is, it freed up three seats for standby passengers." Craig Ferguson
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "This week, a designer in New York unveiled a bottle of perfume that costs $1 million. Yeah, a million bucks for a few ounces of liquid. Which explains its name: 'Starbucks.'" Jimmy Fallon
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "An executive from the E! Network has stated that there could be as many as four new Kardashian spinoff shows. He then added, 'Unless our demands are met.'"
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/conan-o'brien.html" target="noframes">
              Conan-O'brien
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "SantaCon is this weekend here in New York. This is where a bunch of people dress up like Santa and go on a pub crawl. Which is different than my typical weekend of going on a pub crawl and waking up the next morning dressed like Santa."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jimmyfallon.html" target="noframes">
              Jimmy Fallon
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "President Obama celebrated Hanukkah at the White House last night. He lit the menorah, and then Vice President Joe Biden came in sang happy birthday, and blew out all of the candles."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jay-leno.html" target="noframes">
              Jay Leno
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Everybody has been in a very festive mood. I even saw two strangers splitting a cab. One guy took the tires, one guy took the radio."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/david-letterman.html" target="noframes">
              David Letterman
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "A lot of packages this time of year get lost. That's awful. Youre promised something great. You wait and wait and wait. But nothing good ever comes. It's like voting for Obama."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/craigferguson.html" target="noframes">
              Craig Ferguson
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Iran is now in possession of an American drone. When I heard that I thought, 'Oh, my god, they captured Joe Biden?'" Jay Leno
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "According to a new survey, some people are waiting until after Christmas to do their holiday shopping. Yeah, these people are known as men." Conan O'brien
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "A campaign staffer on the Newt Gingrich campaign was fired because he was making negative comments about Mormons. I thought, 'Wait a minute, isn't Newt in favor of multiple wives?'" David Letterman
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Gary Busey said it's not that he doesn't like Newt Gingrich, but it was too soon for him to endorse anyone. He said as soon as a president is elected, he will let us know who he intends to vote for." Jimmy Kimmel
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "In ancient mythology, spiders were depicted as symbols of patience because they spend all that time weaving their web. Then they wait until unsuspecting prey stumbles into it and it's all over within matter of minutes. Nowadays we call that a Kardashian marriage."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/craigferguson.html" target="noframes">
              Craig Ferguson
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "I've got to admit, Christmas is strange in Los Angeles. People in Hollywood marvel when they see the nativity scene because rarely do people in this town ever see a baby being taken care of by both parents at the same time."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jay-leno.html" target="noframes">
              Jay Leno
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "It was leaked that tiger woods' divorce settlement ending up netting his wife almost $100 million. The news was leaked to the New York Times, the Los Angeles Times, and Kobe Bryant's wife."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/conan-o'brien.html" target="noframes">
              Conan-O'brien
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Donald Trump said he was going to run for president and then he didn't run. But now he may be serious because I understand he has demanded to see his own birth certificate."
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/david-letterman.html" target="noframes">
              David Letterman
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg apparently went hunting, killed a bison, nicknamed it Billy, then mounted its head on a wall. Yeah, then Zuckerberg was like, 'Anyone else want to complain about the new Facebook Timeline?'"
             <a href="http://www.myjokeindex.com/jimmyfallon.html" target="noframes">
              Jimmy Fallon
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             "President Barack Obama went out and did some shopping. He took the entire White House Press Corps with him, but still he's out there boosting the economy  the Chinese economy but still, he's doing what he can, ladies and gentlemen." David Letterman
