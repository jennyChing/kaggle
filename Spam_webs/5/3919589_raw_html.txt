       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Psychiatrist Joel Rice Shoots Seven Cows  Arrested
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://union-bulletin.com/news/2013/sep/02/la-grande-psychiatrist-accused-shooting-trespassin/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://union-bulletin.com/news/2013/sep/02/la-grande-psychiatrist-accused-shooting-trespassin/', 'Union-Bulletin']);">
          Union-Bulletin
         </a>
         <br/>
         La Grande psychiatrist accused of shooting trespassing cattle
         <br/>
         The Associated Press
         <br/>
         September 2, 2013
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.psychsearch.net/wp-content/uploads/Psychiatrist-Joel-Rice-PsychSearch.net_.jpg">
          <img alt="Psychiatrist Joel Rice PsychSearch.net" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9198" height="465" src="http://www.psychsearch.net/wp-content/uploads/Psychiatrist-Joel-Rice-PsychSearch.net_-1024x768.jpg" width="620"/>
         </a>
         LA GRANDE  The authorities arrested a La Grande psychiatrist accused of shooting seven cows  six fatally  that were trespassing on his property.
        </p>
        <p>
         #The Observer newspaper reports that 57-year-old Joel Rice was arrested Thursday after an investigation by the Union County Sheriffs Office. He was charged with aggravated animal abuse and criminal mischief.
        </p>
        <p>
         #Capt. Craig Ward told the newspaper that Rice was pretty upset when he came home Aug. 15 to find cattle blocking his driveway.
        </p>
        <p>
         #Ward said the seven cows belonged to several different owners and loose animals have been a problem. The area was previously open range, but Rice led an effort to create a livestock district and a closed range.
        </p>
        <p>
         #Ward says the remedy for trespassing cattle is to call the sheriffs office. He added that livestock owners are generally good about getting their animals back home.
        </p>
        <p>
         #Rice is free until trial.
        </p>
       </div>
