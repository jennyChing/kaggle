               <p>
                Below you will find a brief interpretation of each narcissism trait and what your
score relative to that trait may indicate about you.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Authority
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                Authority refers to a person's leadership skills and power.  People who score higher on
authority like  to be in charge and gain power, often for power's sake alone.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Self-Sufficiency
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                This trait refers to how self-sufficient a person is, that is, how much you rely
on others versus your own abilities to meet your needs in life.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Superiority
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                This trait refers to whether a person feels they are more superior than those around them.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Exhibitionism
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                This trait  refers to a person's need to be the center of attention, and willingness to
ensure they are the center of attention (even at the expense of others' needs).
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Exploitativeness
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                This trait refers to how willing you are to exploit others in order to meet your own
needs or goals.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Vanity
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                This trait refers to a person's vanity, or their belief in one's own superior abilities
and attractiveness compared to others.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Entitlement
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                This trait refers to the expectation and amount of entitlement a person has in their lives,
that is, unreasonable expectations of especially favorable treatment or automatic compliance with one's expectations.
People who score higher on this trait generally have a greater expectation of entitlement, while those
who score lower expect little from others or life.
               </p>
