        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Pudding 
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2012-10-26T05:00:30+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 26, 2012
         </time>
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          by:
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Chef in Training
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.chef-in-training.com/2012/10/pumpkin-chocolate-chip-pudding-cookies/#comments">
           55
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="white" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonPin" data-pin-zero="true" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.chef-in-training.com%2F2012%2F10%2Fpumpkin-chocolate-chip-pudding-cookies%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.chef-in-training.com%2Fblog%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2012%2F10%2FIMG_3845-1.jpg&amp;description=Pumpkin%20Chocolate%20Chip%20Pudding%20Cookies" rel="nobox">
          <img data-jpibfi-description="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png?c62cc9" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Now that it has snowed up here in Utah (yeah, you read that right SNOWED), I will soon be transitioning away from fall foods seeing as it is feeling much like winter. Before this transition happens, I want to share with you this recipe for Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Pudding Cookies.
        </p>
        <p>
         This is another cookie, similar to my
         <a href="http://www.chef-in-training.com/2010/09/pumpkin-chocolate-chip-cookies/">
          <strong>
           regular Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookie
          </strong>
         </a>
         s, that screams fall to me. My husband devoured the bag of these I sent with him to work. He loves all things pumpkin, and these sure fit the bill. They are soft and delicious.
        </p>
        <p>
         The pumpkin spice pudding is seasonal so you better go stock up on it while it lasts. Walmart is the only place I have been able to find it, and it is on display with their seasonal holiday food displays in the aisles. When you grab some, make sure you give these cookies a try!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20529" data-jpibfi-description="" height="1125" src="http://www.chef-in-training.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/IMG_3845-1.jpg?c62cc9" title="Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Pudding Cookies!.. The pudding makes these so soft. These are the perfect fall cookie! #cookie #dessert #pumpkin" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSRatings" itemprop="aggregateRating" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/AggregateRating">
          <div class="ERSRatingOuter">

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Pudding Cookies
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img data-jpibfi-description="" itemprop="image" src="http://www.chef-in-training.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/IMG_3845-1.jpg?c62cc9" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.chef-in-training.com/easyrecipe-print/20528-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          The pudding makes these so soft. These are the perfect fall cookie!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Dessert
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup butter, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 small pkg. pumpkin spice instant pudding mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp. vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp. baking soda
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cup flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1-2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cream butter, brown sugar and sugar together in a large bowl.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add in dry pumpkin spice instant pudding mix and beat until well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add eggs and vanilla and mix well.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add flour and baking soda and beat until well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir in chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Roll dough into 1" balls. Place them on a greased baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees F for 8-10 minutes.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>

          3.2.2708
         </div>

        Enjoy!
