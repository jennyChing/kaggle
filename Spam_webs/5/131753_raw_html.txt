      <p align="left" class="c3">
       Welcome to the Scouting Pages Website. The reason this site was developed was to hopefully provide a source of usual information about different aspects of Scouting's History both general and unusual facts about Scouting that are generally not known by people, for example Herge the creator of
       <strong>
        Tintin
       </strong>
       was a Scout and Tintin was based on a character called
       <strong>
        Totor
       </strong>
       that Herge created for a cartoon strip in a Scouting publication called
       <strong>
        Le Boy Scout
       </strong>
       ,.and was based on his experience as a Patrol Leader,
       <a href="tintin.html">
       </a>
       or that at least 3 steam locomotives have a connection with Scouting find out more about these facts and many more by using the
       <strong>
        Interesting Facts
       </strong>
       menu above
      </p>
      <p align="left" class="c3">
       Information is added to this site when time allows or information comes to light through visiting other web sites or reading various Scouting Publications or any other publication that I come across that mentions Scouting.
      </p>
      <p align="left" class="c3">
       Future plans for the site include articles on Scouting during two World Wars and more on Scouting Collectables
      </p>
