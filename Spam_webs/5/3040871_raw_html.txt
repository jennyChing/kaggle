     <h1 class="entry-title">
      McLaren-Honda Confirms Fernando Alonso and Jenson Button 2015 F1
     </h1>
     <!-- AddThis Button BEGIN -->
     <div class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">
      <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count">
      </a>
      <a class="addthis_button_tweet">
      </a>
      <a class="addthis_button_google_plusone" g:plusone:size="medium">
      </a>
      <a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style">
      </a>
     </div>
     <script src="http://s7.addthis.com/js/250/addthis_widget.js#pubid=addthis@motorverso.com" type="text/javascript">
     </script>
     <!-- AddThis Button END -->
     <div class="entry-content">
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       RecentlyMcLaren confirmed that theirtwo drivers for the 2015 F1 season will be
       <a href="http://www.motorverso.com/jenson-button-drive-rolls-royce-cars-2014-f1-season/">
        Jenson Button
       </a>
       and Fernando Alonso.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       These two drivers will be heavily involved in the big changes that are coming to
       <a href="http://www.motorverso.com/mclaren-mso-650s-spyder-goodwood/">
        McLaren
       </a>
       as they are going to be ditching the Mercedes engines and moving to Honda sourced units. Dont forget that this isnt a completely new thing for McLaren as back in the 80s they did use Honda engines and created the MP4/4 that
       <a href="http://www.motorverso.com/tribute-ayrton-senna-mp44/">
        Ayrton Senna
       </a>
       won 15 of 16 races in back in 1988. So in the past it has been a successful partnership.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.motorverso.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/mclarenhonamp44_1988_brunosenna-4.jpg">
        <img alt="mclarenhonamp44_1988_brunosenna-4" class="aligncenter wp-image-35660 size-medium" height="449" src="http://www.motorverso.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/mclarenhonamp44_1988_brunosenna-4-675x449.jpg" title="http://www.f1fanatic.co.uk/f1-pictures-wallpapers/cars/mclaren/mclaren-mp44-1988-pictures/" width="675"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
      </p>
      <blockquote class="testimonial">
       <p>
        Over the past year I have received several offers, some of them really tempting, given the current performance of some of the teams that showed interest. But, more than a year ago, McLaren-Honda contacted me and asked me to take part, in a very active way, in the return of their partnership  a partnership that dominated the Formula 1 scene for so long
       </p>
       <footer class="testimonialAuthor">
        Fernando Alonso
       </footer>
      </blockquote>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://www.motorverso.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/F1-Grand-Prix-of-Russia-Previews.jpg">
        <img alt="F1-Grand-Prix-of-Russia-Previews" class="aligncenter wp-image-35658 size-medium" height="449" src="http://www.motorverso.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/F1-Grand-Prix-of-Russia-Previews-675x449.jpg" width="675"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
      </p>
      <blockquote class="testimonial">
       <p>
        I am absolutely delighted to be on the threshold of leading McLaren-Honda to a new era of partnership, and I speak on behalf of all at McLaren when I say that. I am equally confident that our colleagues at Honda share that determination and passion to win. As regards drivers, McLarens policy has always been to assemble the strongest line-up possible, and in Fernando and Jenson I firmly believe that is exactly what we have.
       </p>
       <footer class="testimonialAuthor">
        chief executive of McLaren, Ron Dennis
       </footer>
      </blockquote>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Alongside Fernando and Jenson, Kevin Magnussen will act as the teams reserve and test driver.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Source:
       <a href="http://www.gtspirit.com/" target="_blank">
        GT Spirit
       </a>
      </p>
