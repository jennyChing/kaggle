        <p>
         In honor of National Burger Day today, I wanted to name this post DIY mini macaron cheeseburgers and fries and milkshakes and sprinkles, but Google said that was too long, so just consider the mini milkshake and sprinkles an added bonus from me to you.Technically I would never have known it was National BurgerDay if it wasnt for
         <a href="http://studiodiy.com" target="_blank">
          Kelly
         </a>
         (thanks for having my back Kel!), but the point is is that Im not one to pass up a good #glossaryofmacs opportunity anyways, now am I?!
        </p>
        <p>
         Also, can we have an open honest discussion about how freaking trickymaking mini sugar cookie french fries can be?! I mean, holy baked goods. It took me three tries to get something that didnt look like giant, deflated fingers next to these mini cheeseburgers,
         <em>
          but,
         </em>
         I think I figured out the secret!
        </p>

         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16422" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8739.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8739.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-16414">
         </span>
        </p>
        <div id="materials">
         <h1>
          Ingredients:
         </h1>
         <ul>
          <li>
           We used birthday cake macarons with sprinkles for the bun, but your flavor preference is fine!
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1QbD342" target="_blank">
            Dark chocolate HannahMax cookie chips
           </a>
           for the patty
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1GFgc0g" target="_blank">
            Red cookieicing
           </a>
           for the ketchup
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1J3lk00" target="_blank">
            Mint green fondant
           </a>
           for the lettuce
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1J3lu7q" target="_blank">
            Yellow Shape-n-amaze decorating dough
           </a>
           for the cheese
          </li>
          <li>
           Frozen
           <a href="http://www.pillsbury.com/products/cookies/ready-to-bake-cookies/sugar-cookies" target="_blank">
            sugar cookie squares
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           Knife
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1J3micz" target="_blank">
            Square cookie cutter
           </a>
           (or you can just use a knife for this too!)
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1ckfusz" target="_blank">
            Mini rolling pin
           </a>
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16426" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8643.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8643.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         To make the cheese, roll out the Shape-n-amaze (or you can use yellow fondant for this too) and cut it into shape with a mini square cookie cutter, an set aside to firm up a bit.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16427" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8646.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8646.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         For the lettuce, make a small ball of mint green fondant, and start to spread it out with your fingers, spreading it thin around the edges. This will make it start to curl and crease randomly like to appear like pieces of real lettuce.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16429" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8652.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8652.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you have all of your pieces, assemble them with ketchup, aka red cookie icing, and place the layers together. I got pretty generous along the cheese layer so it would peek through to look like real sauce on a cheeseburger. A macaron cheeseburger, that is.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16415" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8674.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8674.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16417" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8692.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8692.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         To make the french fries, cut a single tiny sliver, using a non-serrated knife, from the semi frozen sugar cookie squares. The slivers should only be about about a 1/4 of an inch thick on all sizes, and about an inch long, because they expand and rise a bit as theyre baked. Its important that the dough be semi frozen otherwise the fries lose their shape too easily once theyre baked.
        </p>
        <p>
         Place them about an inch apart on a backing sheet, and only bake them for about 4-5 minutes instead of the usual package bake time.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16419" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8716.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8716.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16420" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8721.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8721.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course no mini cheeseburger and fries is complete without a mini milkshake, and we needed a little pop of pink to this shindig, so I went with classic strawberry!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16424" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8802.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8802.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16416" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8683.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8683.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY mini cheeseburger and french fries with a mini milkshake to top it off! | sugar &amp; cloth" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16418" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8698.jpg?resize=540%2C810"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://sugarandcloth.com/2015/05/diy-mini-macaron-cheeseburgers-and-fries/&amp;media=http://sugarandcloth.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/MS4A8698.jpg&amp;description=DIY Mini Macaron Cheeseburgers and Fries')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Wouldnt thesebe a really cute dessert for July 4th or as mini favor baskets for summer pool parties and BBQs? I have about a million ideas for serving these little guys!
        </p>
        <p>
         Just in case you make your own, be sure to tag #mysugarandcloth to share your rendition of the projects from the blog so we can follow along, plus you can see more
         <a href="http://sugarandcloth.com/diy-macarons/" target="_blank">
          DIY macaron projects right here
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
