      <p>
       <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-family: Helvetica;">
        Americans love Halloween. In fact, according to a
       </span>
       <a href="https://nrf.com/news/infographic-hair-raising-halloween-highlights-2013" target="_blank">
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         recent survey
        </span>
       </a>
       <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-family: Helvetica;">
        by the National Retail Federation, nearly 160 million people celebrate the spooky holiday every year.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-family: Helvetica;">
        If you want to make sure your Halloween activities are more treat than trick, weve got you covered.
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Here, in our opinions, are five of the best Halloween travel destinations in the United States, and the celebrations that make them worth the trip.
       </span>
       <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-family: Helvetica;">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         New Orleans
        </span>
       </strong>
       <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-family: Helvetica;">
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Halloween in
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/New-Orleans.d178292.Destination-Travel-Guides?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         New Orleans
        </a>
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        is as much a holiday as it is a cultural experience. With the moniker of The Most Haunted City in America, graveyards filled with above-ground tombs, and a history of voodoo,
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/New-Orleans-Hotels.d178292.Travel-Guide-Hotels?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         New Orleans
        </a>
        is perfect destination for a Halloween-themed trip.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Check out
       </span>
       <a href="https://www.frenchquarterphantoms.com" target="_blank">
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         French Quarter Phantoms Ghost Tours
        </span>
       </a>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        for spine-tingling tours led by real historians. Also join the
       </span>
       <a href="http://www.kreweofboo.com" target="_blank">
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         Krewe Of Boo
        </span>
       </a>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Halloween parade, or just watch the procession of extravagant and scary floats crafted by local artists.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="vertical-image-container landscape-3c2e37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150">
       <img alt="Halloween in New Orleans" src="http://viewfinder.expedia.com/images/default-source/user-no-vacation-required/halloweencontent-[size_235x380].jpg?Status=Master&amp;sfvrsn=2"/>
       <p>
        Halloween in New Orleans
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         Los Angeles
         <br/>
        </span>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        West Hollywood is home to Halloween Carnaval, the largest Halloween street party in the world and the
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/Los-Angeles.d178280.Destination-Travel-Guides?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         Los Angeles
        </a>
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        areas second-largest annual bash. Each year the free event draws hundreds of thousands of revelers to Santa Monica Boulevard for live entertainment. The
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/Los-Angeles-Hotels.d178280.Travel-Guide-Hotels?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         L.A.
        </a>
        events popularity might also have something to do with the over-the-top and often risqu costumes (and the beautiful people donning them).
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Make sure to stick around for the nights main event, the crowning of an honorary Queen of the Carnaval.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         Orlando, Florida
        </span>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        For the ultimate family-friendly Halloween celebration, visit
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/Orlando.d178294.Destination-Travel-Guides?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         Orlando
        </a>
        and head to the place were every day is a costume party:
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/p/info-partner/disney/disneyworld?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         the Magic Kingdom
        </a>
        . Here, at
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Mickey's Not-So-Scary Halloween Party, the happiest place on earth treats visitors to Mickey's "Boo-to-You" Halloween Parade, fireworks, and trick-or-treat stations. Make sure to come in costume so that you can walk in the costume parade. Heck, you may even consider bringing the kids.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="margin-left: 40px;">
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        Viewfinder Tip: If you're bringing kids, make sure the Halloween party you're attending is kid-friendly BEFORE you go.
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         New York City
         <br/>
        </span>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/New-York.d178293.Destination-Travel-Guides?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         New York City
        </a>
        , a.k.a., "The City that Never Sleeps," is a perfect place to celebrate Halloween. One
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        of the
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/New-York-Hotels.d178293.Travel-Guide-Hotels?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         Big Apple
        </a>
        's best Halloween parties is the annual
       </span>
       <a href="http://www.halloween-nyc.com/index.php" target="_blank">
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         Village Halloween Parade
        </span>
       </a>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        . More than 50,000 participants from around the world don costumes to march in the parade and celebrate the unveiling of giant puppets created by artists and groups commissioned for the project.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        If youre a local or you're in town ahead of time, you might want to attend a Village Halloween Parade Puppet-Building Workshop. These occur every weekend in October prior to the parade. Sign up early, they are popular activities!
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         Las Vegas
         <br/>
        </span>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/Las-Vegas.d178276.Destination-Travel-Guides?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         Las Vegas
        </a>
        has become a Halloween hotspot in recent years. Bars overflow with costumed revelers, and braving the sidewalks of the Las Vegas Strip becomes a contact sport. One of the most outrageous, sexy, and risqu Halloween parties
       </span>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        in
        <a href="http://www.expedia.com/Las-Vegas-Hotels.d178276.Travel-Guide-Hotels?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
         Sin City
        </a>
        is the Fetish and Fantasy Ball at the
       </span>
       <a href="http://www.expedia.com/Las-Vegas-Hotels-Hard-Rock-Hotel-Casino.h198225.Hotel-Information?brandcid=social.vf.No Vacation Required.New Orleans.985a37a7-50ee-63e1-bbde-ff000073f150" target="_blank">
        <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
         Hard Rock Casino
        </span>
       </a>
       <span style="font-family: Helvetica;">
        . You have to be over 18 just to view the website, so you can only imagine what the party is like.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-family: Helvetica;">
        <strong>
         Where are your favorite destinations to celebrate Halloween and why?
        </strong>
