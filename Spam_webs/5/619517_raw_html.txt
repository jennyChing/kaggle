         </div>
         <div class="article_body">

That's exactly what this thing is, a tank, and it is designed to tear up the dirt in any contest thrown its way. But we say attache a plow to this thing and lets see some farming get done a fraction of the time you'd expect farmer Joe to get through the fields. Why not?
