        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/romanceisnotdeadpostimage2.2.121.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-1267 alignleft" height="226" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/romanceisnotdeadpostimage2.2.121-300x226.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
          Hopeful romantics take note! Romance is NOT dead according to a recent study. In fact, digital technology can help stoke a relationships fire. So with that in mind, we decided to search for the best amorous gestures online to honor Saint Valentines Day.
          <span id="more-1224">
          </span>
          Our findings resulted in the following: The Sweetest, The Confusing, and The Funniest romantic viral moments of 2011.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Sweetest
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://vimeo.com/33507366" target="_blank" title="Tian Video">
           <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1275" height="103" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Screen-shot-2012-02-02-at-12.20.28-PM-300x161.png" width="185"/>
          </a>
          We have two unusual wedding proposals that suggest putting a ring on it is a full-scale production requiring theatrical staging and a video crew. First up, we have
          <a href="http://vimeo.com/33507366" target="_blank">
           Timothy Tian
          </a>
          , who surprised his future fiance
          <em>
           <em>
           </em>
          </em>
          by using Internet memes when he asked for her hand.
          <em>
          </em>
          WARNING, this may not sound romantic but before you hit play make sure you grab a tissue. There is a darn good chance you will get verklempted!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://youtu.be/2zYM00-A-bY" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-1280 alignleft" height="138" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Screen-shot-2012-02-02-at-12.18.02-PM1-300x163.png" width="242"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          What do you get when you combine a
          <a href="http://youtu.be/2zYM00-A-bY" target="_blank">
           faux ice-skating party
          </a>
          , a handful of people in Winnie the Pooh costumes, a human sized lobster (hint: it is a nod to a Friends episode), along with an unsuspecting girlfriend? Exactly what the video is titled, The Most Creative Marriage Proposal Ever. or at least the most creative for 2011. If you are totally confused, just watch this joyful clip.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Confusing
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          A proposal is defined as the act of offering or suggesting something for acceptance. The following vide
          <a href="http://youtu.be/2e5qfcbtfEI" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1291" height="202" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Screen-shot-2012-02-02-at-12.29.44-PM-300x230.png" width="259"/>
          </a>
          o features Jim Carrying proposing not marriage to Emma Stone, but a relationship. Some might consider Carreys love confession while romantic, a tad cringy and confusing. Even so, we must confess, we have a soft spot for a man who wears his
          <a href="/2012/01/12/top-ten-cities-to-find-a-sensitive-man/" target="_blank">
           heart on his sleeve
          </a>
          . Plus, Emma Stone is undeniable remarkable, even
          <a href="http://www.askmen.com/celebs/women/celeb_profiles_actress_100/111_emma_stone.html">
           Askmen.com
          </a>
          agrees. Check out the
          <a href="http://youtu.be/jDOWNb9xtYE" target="_blank">
           Songified Jim Carrey/ Emma Stone Remix
          </a>
          or the
          <a href="http://youtu.be/2e5qfcbtfEI" target="_blank">
           original confessional video.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://youtu.be/g_pYAIpL_nQ" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1295" height="120" src="/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Screen-shot-2012-02-02-at-12.34.25-PM1-300x201.png" width="176"/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           The Funniest
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Are you wondering how to proclaim your love for someone in the digital universe? No worries,
          <a href="http://youtu.be/g_pYAIpL_nQ" target="_blank">
           Viral Video Film School
          </a>
          has your back. The following tutorial will demonstrate how to woo your object of affection via you guessed it viral video.
         </p>
         <p>
          Have a viral video romantic moment ? Share the link in the comments below!
         </p>
