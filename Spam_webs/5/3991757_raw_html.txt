&lt;p&gt;If you love wine as more than just a beverage, it makes sense that youd be interested in reading more about  the topic from several angles. We&#8217;ve previously discussed the &lt;a href="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wine-books/"&gt;best books about wine&lt;/a&gt;, but here are the most popular magazines about wine. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Below are 10 of the best examples of wine magazines worth subscribing to. &lt;/p&gt;

&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/bon-appetit.jpg" alt="Bon Appetit Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;1. Bon Apptit&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;As the name suggests, this magazine is all about your taste buds. From good food to fabulous wine, youll find it all here and more. This publication even highlights fun entertainment ideas to ensure your meals never have a chance to become boring. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In each edition, youll learn about new trends in the world of food and wine. Youll also be able to read reviews about both and get recipes to cook up your own dishes at home. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;For the best of fine dining outside of your kitchen, Bon Apptit magazine shows you where the best restaurants are all around the world and travel options to consider when youre on your way there.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Bon Apptit &lt;a href="https://w1.buysub.com/pubs/N3/BNA/subBR2015_S5OBNA19.jsp?cds_page_id=183297&#038;cds_mag_code=BNA&#038;id=1430411559683&#038;lsid=51201131030043308&#038;vid=2" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://w1.buysub.com/pubs/N3/BNA/subBR2015_S5OBNA19.jsp?cds_page_id=183297&amp;cds_mag_code=BNA&amp;id=1430411559683&amp;lsid=51201131030043308&amp;vid=2', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/decanter-magazine.jpg" alt="Decanter Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;2. Decanter &lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This magazine focuses specifically on wine, spirits and little else. While casual drinkers will find plenty to enjoy, those who take their wines seriously will feel right at home in the pages of Decanter. Youll gain access to countless wine reviews throughout the year that are about more than just commercial versions you can find at the local grocer. In fact, with a subscription, youll get well over 4,000 wine recommendations every year.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;With readers in over 90 countries, you dont have to worry that youre getting a skewed perspective designed just for a domestic audience. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Decanter Magazine also hold the annual Decanter World Wine Awards, which is the world&#8217;s largest wine competition.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Decanter &lt;a href="http://www.magazinesdirect.com/subscription/decanter/33087581/decanter.thtml?utm_medium=Text+link&#038;utm_source=BRAND+WEBSITE&#038;utm_campaign=XDC+Brandsite+offer+HARDLINKS&#038;utm_content=Top+Nav+Text+Link" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.magazinesdirect.com/subscription/decanter/33087581/decanter.thtml?utm_medium=Text+link&amp;utm_source=BRAND+WEBSITE&amp;utm_campaign=XDC+Brandsite+offer+HARDLINKS&amp;utm_content=Top+Nav+Text+Link', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/food-and-wine-cover.jpg" alt="Food and Wine Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;3. Food &#038; Wine&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Food &#038; Wine magazine is another publication that makes its subject abundantly clear in the title. Food &#038; Wine focuses on the best of both worlds in the kitchen and, of course, how to pair them together in order to create an amazing result. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;With each issue, youll find countless recommendations, whether its in regards to wine and food, locations to visit, upcoming events to check out and more. Their writers also cover emerging culinary trends that youll want to keep an eye on.   &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;For coming up on 40 years now, Food &#038; Wine has also kept subscribers mesmerized by covering their Food &#038; Wine Classic held annually in Aspen, Colorado. Guests who decide to attend can take in guest speakers, cooking demonstrations and, of course, wine tasting.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Food &#038; Wine Magazine &lt;a href="https://subscription.foodandwine.com/storefront/subscribe-to-food-and-wine/site/wi-3termdig-lexsell-sunset12.html?tcmid=Magazine_Nav_TL_Subscribe&#038;link=1024126&#038;fpa_oc=WI+McKinsey+Creative+Test+2015" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://subscription.foodandwine.com/storefront/subscribe-to-food-and-wine/site/wi-3termdig-lexsell-sunset12.html?tcmid=Magazine_Nav_TL_Subscribe&amp;link=1024126&amp;fpa_oc=WI+McKinsey+Creative+Test+2015', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/imbibe.jpg" alt="Imbibe Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;4. Imbibe&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This title covers a bit wider range of subjects, though they all have to do with alcohol. Each issue features news and information related to wine, as well as cocktails, beer and other spirits. Youll even find pieces on non-alcoholic beverages such as tea and coffee squeezed in for good measure. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Inside this magazine, youll receive a number of recipes for preparing alcoholic beverages and other kinds. Writers also cover fun destinations related to liquid culture. This kind of writing has won Imbibe nearly a dozen awards over the past eight years, most recently the Tales of the Cocktail Spirited Award: Best Cocktail Writing in 2014.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Imbibe &lt;a href="https://imbibemagazine.com/subscribe/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://imbibemagazine.com/subscribe/', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/finewine_magazine.jpg" alt="World of Fine Wine Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;5. The World of Fine Wine&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The World of Fine Wine magazine is a publication that specializes in all aspects of wine. They print articles about the worldwide goings-on about the wine lifestyle as well as report the best wines every month. On their site and in their magazine you can also find information about ideal travel destinations for winery and restaurant tours, or you can look up the perfect wine pairing for your meal tonight. This magazine truly offers information that can cater to any type of wine-lover.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to The World of Fine Wine &lt;a href="http://www.worldoffinewine.com/subject/subscribe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.worldoffinewine.com/subject/subscribe', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/saveur-magazine.jpg" alt="Saveur Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;6. Saveur&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Saveur magazine is about taking you around the world to explore where your favorite wines and dishes come from and then giving you a perspective on what they feel and taste like in their native land today. Its a great publication for those who savor the taste, smell and consistency of their food and beverage, as well as an understanding of its past. This magazine strives to cover every context of food, not just the recipes you need to follow in order to make it.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Aside from reading chef profiles, youll also be treated to treatises on where certain foods, recipes and wines came from and how exactly they got here. Due to this unique storytelling quality, Saveur Magazine has received 17 James Beard journalism awards and four American Society of Magazine Editor awards. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Saveur &lt;a href="https://subscribe.saveur.com/pubs/BN/SAV/allaccess.jsp?cds_page_id=182198&#038;cds_mag_code=SAV&#038;id=1430416422163&#038;lsid=51201253421027756&#038;vid=1&#038;cds_response_key=IWAPLAYC0" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://subscribe.saveur.com/pubs/BN/SAV/allaccess.jsp?cds_page_id=182198&amp;cds_mag_code=SAV&amp;id=1430416422163&amp;lsid=51201253421027756&amp;vid=1&amp;cds_response_key=IWAPLAYC0', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Wine_Spirits_cover.jpg" alt="Wine &#038; Spirits Magazine" style="width:250;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;7. Wine &#038; Spirits&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Wine &#038; Spirits magazine is another title with several  James Beard Awards to their name. They received five of these awards over the years for their excellence in wine writing. Each year, they apply those talents toward over 12,000 wines. If youre in the mood to expand your collection or just want to make sure youre spending your money wisely, Wine &#038; Spirits is the publication for you.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Aside from helping you decide which wines to buy, this magazine will also provide you with news about the industry for those who love more than just the taste of their favorite spirits. They even cover restaurant openings that provide especially compelling wine lists. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Wine &#038; Spirits &lt;a href="http://www.wineandspiritsmagazine.com/S=0/subscriptions" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.wineandspiritsmagazine.com/S=0/subscriptions', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Wine-Enthusiast.jpg" alt="Wine Enthusiast Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;8. Wine Enthusiast &lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Wine enthusiasts at every level will be happy with a subscription to this magazine. Every single issue comes packed with reviews of both new wines and other kinds of spirits. The magazine also features a comprehensive buying guide, as well as advice on where to travel in order to satisfy your longing for fine wines, amazing dishes, and eye-catching surroundings. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The magazines history actually goes back over 30 years, when couple and co-founders Adam and Sybil Strum started a wine catalog in their attic. In less than 10 years, they had found so much interest within the industry, that they started Wine Enthusiast Magazine. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can view the Wine Enthusiast blog &lt;a href="http://www.winemag.com/?afid=WENET" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.winemag.com/?afid=WENET', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/wine-spectator.jpg" alt="Wine Spectator Magazine" style="width:250px;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;9. Wine Spectator &lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;For all things wine, consider Wine Spectator. You get a whole 15 issues every year and each brings you reviews of fine restaurants, vacation ideas specifically for wine lovers and more. They even interview compelling personalities from the wine world to help you better appreciate the industry and your favorite choices.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to Wine Spectator &lt;a href="https://sub.winespectator.com/pubs/M5/WNE/WS-Sub-FREETrialJune302014Pittissue.jsp?cds_page_id=167186&#038;cds_mag_code=WNE&#038;id=1430418661281&#038;lsid=51201331012024151&#038;vid=1" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://sub.winespectator.com/pubs/M5/WNE/WS-Sub-FREETrialJune302014Pittissue.jsp?cds_page_id=167186&amp;cds_mag_code=WNE&amp;id=1430418661281&amp;lsid=51201331012024151&amp;vid=1', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;hr class="faq" /&gt;
&lt;div class="photofloat30"&gt;&lt;img src="http://learn.winecoolerdirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/wine-maker.jpg" alt="Winemaker Magazine" style="width:250;" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;10. Winemaker &lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;If your love of wine has brought you to the world of winemaking, youll love Winemaker Magazine. This magazine focuses specifically on people just like you who are making their own wine from home. Youll find everything from wine recipes to tips to interviews with winemakers who have found success creating their own versions.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;With so many options, youre bound to find at least one or two titles worth reading. Over the course of a single year, any one of them will help you explore your love for wine even more.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can subscribe to WineMaker &lt;a href="http://winemakermag.com/product/332-apr-may-2015-issue?lang=en" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://winemakermag.com/product/332-apr-may-2015-issue?lang=en', 'here']);" &gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;

	function getQueryVariable(variable)
	{
		var uriStartIndex = window.location.href.indexOf("?");
		if( uriStartIndex &gt; -1 )
		{
			var query = window.location.href.substring( uriStartIndex+1 );
			var vars = query.split("&amp;");
			for ( var i = 0; i &lt; vars.length ;i++ ) {
