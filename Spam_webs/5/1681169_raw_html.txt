                     Jaime + Nathan : Rustic Engagement Session

                      Take a gorgeous setting, add two beautiful people, throw in a talented photographer, and you have the perfect recipe for an amazing engagement shoot. Shot in Mt. Olive, Alabama, the rustic setting was perfect for Jaime, who grew up on a horse farm, and Nathan, a lifelongoutdoors-man. "
                      <span style="background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.917969);">
                       We wanted something very natural and organic, outside, and simple" said Jaime.

                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-OHS3ZpwkWR0/T72SFq8_1zI/AAAAAAAAA-Q/6TOfMz2mNEc/s1600/40704_412357825591_19630915591_4807090_3996341_n.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

He was insistent that he help me decorate my Christmas tree the day after Thanksgiving, which I did NOT want to do. Each year, Nathan has given me a beautiful Christmas ornament for the tree. He was picking me up to go to dinner with his family and brought me an early Christmas present, which was the Christmas ornament - so I wasnt thinking anything. Then, he recommended a PERFECT place for the Christmas ornament on the tree. On that particular branch was my engagement ring. He got down on one knee and proposed."
