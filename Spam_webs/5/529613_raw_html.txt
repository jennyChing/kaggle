        <p>
         Favorites of the fall.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <img alt="Allie's Delicious Baked Dumplings" height="600" id="__wp-temp-img-id" src="http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/600x600/25011.jpg" title="" width="600"/>

         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Apple pie stands on top, of course. But dont overlook other outstanding desserts: try apple dumplings, crisps, and strudel.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/video/816/apple-pie-by-grandma-ople/" target="_blank">

        <p>
         <strong>
          Apple Pie
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Whether double-crusted or streusel-topped, deep dish or custard-style, apple pie is Americas dessert.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <img alt="Chef John's Caramel Apple Pie" height="600" id="__wp-temp-img-id" src="http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/600x600/1955176.jpg" title="" width="600"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo by TnVolsFan
         </p>
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/video/410/chef-johns-caramel-apple-pie/" target="_blank">
           VIDEO: Chef Johns Caramel Apple Pie
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/recipe/chef-johns-easy-apple-pie/" target="_blank">
           Chef Johns Easy Apple Pie
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/recipe/apple-pie-i/" target="_blank">
           Apple Pie I
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Apple Dumplings and Turnovers
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Apple dumplings are whole apples, peeled and cored, stuffed with a sweet mix of spices, raisins and nuts, folded into pastry, and baked until the apples are soft and the pastry is golden.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <img alt="Country Apple Dumplings" height="600" id="__wp-temp-img-id" src="http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/600x600/892347.jpg" title="" width="600"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo by Molly
         </p>
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/video/1222/country-apple-dumplings/" target="_blank">
           VIDEO: Country Apple Dumplings
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/Fried-Apple-Pies/" target="_blank">
           Fried Apple Pies
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Apple Crisps, Crumbles, Cobblers, and Brown Betties
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Homier than pies, crisps and crumbles are easy to make, perfect when youre in a hurry. The crunchy topping is simply a mixture of oats, flour, butter, and sugar.
        </p>
        <p>
         Serve these warm, comforting desserts with vanilla ice cream. To dress up your crisps, assemble them in individual ramekins or custard cups instead of in one big pan.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <img alt="Apple Crisp II" height="600" id="__wp-temp-img-id" src="http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/600x600/1526580.jpg" title="" width="600"/>

          Photo by fcardwell

        <p>
         <strong>
          Apple Strudel and Other Pastries
         </strong>
         <br/>
         This classic Austrian dessert consists of spiced apples and nuts rolled into a log of crisp, fine pastry.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <img alt="Apple Strudel" height="600" id="__wp-temp-img-id" src="http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/600x600/729946.jpg" title="" width="600"/>

         <strong>
          Cakes and Cookies
         </strong>
         <br/>
         And when youre done with the pies and pastries, there are still cakes and cookies to consider!
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <img alt="Apple Squares" height="600" id="__wp-temp-img-id" src="http://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/600x600/1077876.jpg" title="" width="600"/>
