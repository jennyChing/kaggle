        <p>
         Jason Narducy is best known as the bass player in Bob Moulds band and the male Laura Ballance, as he
         <a href="/article/superchunks-laura-ballance-has-hearing-issues-wont-97895">
          fills in for her
         </a>
         at Superchunk performances these days. But Narducy has a long music career that stretches back to his teenage punk band in the 80s (Verbten) and his alt band Verbow in the 90s. His latest project is called Split Singlefeaturing Jon Wurster on drums and Britt Daniel on bassand is releasing its debut full-length,
         <i>
          Fragmented World
         </i>
         , on April 1. As the video below makes clear, though, Narducys career as a musician has been overshadowed by hissexiest elbows in rock, which have been a blessing and a burden, as Wurster, Daniel, Fred Armisen, Bob Mould, Dave Grohl, and a bunch of others attest.
