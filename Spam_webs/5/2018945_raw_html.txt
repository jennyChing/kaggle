        <h1 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
         NCAA Week 3: Oklahoma/FSU Most Viewed ABC Game Since 09
        </h1>
        <a class="comments" href="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/2011/09/ncaa-week-3-oklahomafsu-most-viewed-abc-game-since-09/#comments">
         <i class="fa fa-comments-o">

        </a>

       <div class="post-meta">
        <span class="posted-by">
         By
         <span class="reviewer" itemprop="author">

           Paulsen
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="posted-on">
         on
         <span class="dtreviewed">
          <time class="value-datetime" datetime="2011-09-20T21:37:06+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           09/20/2011
          </time>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="cats">
         <a href="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/category/networks/abc/" rel="category tag">
          ABC
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/category/college-sports/college-football/" rel="category tag">
          College football
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/category/featured/" rel="category tag">

         <a href="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/category/ratings/" rel="category tag">

        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="post-container cf">
        <div class="post-content-right">
         <div class="post-content description " itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           A battle of top five teams earned ABCs largest regular season college football audience since 2009.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/abccfb2.gif">
            <img alt="" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-8724" height="150" src="http://www.sportsmediawatch.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/abccfb-150x150.gif" title="oufsu" width="150"/>
           </a>
           Saturdays #1 Oklahoma/#5 Florida State college football game earned a 5.8 final rating and 9.307 million viewers on ABC, up 23% in ratings and 24% in viewership from last year (TEX/TTU*: 4.7, 7.528M), and up 42% and 40%, respectively, from 2009 (TTU/TEX: 4.1, 6.673M).
           <br/>
           <span id="more-8723">
           </span>
           <br/>
           Oklahomas win ranks as the highest rated, most-viewed regular season college football game on ABC since the 2009 Big 12 Championship Game (TEX/NEB: 7.5, 12.693M).
          </p>
          <p>
           The game ranked as eleventh-most viewed program of the week on broadcast, the only non-NFL sporting event to make the top 25. Among adults 18-49, the games 3.3 rating was good for a tenth-place finish.
          </p>
          <p>
           (This weeks numbers from
           <a href="http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/09/20/tv-ratings-broadcast-top-25-sunday-night-football-emmy-awards-americas-got-talent-top-week-ending-september-18-2011/104209/">
            TV By the Numbers
           </a>
           )
          </p>
