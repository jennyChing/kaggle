       <article class="regular post-2130 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-tech-news tag-16 tag-apple" id="post-2130">
        <div class="post-content">
         <div class="content-inner">
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.pointsquad.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Apple-Store-pointsquad.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[gallery-WWtx]">
            <img alt="Apple Store pointsquad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2134" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.pointsquad.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Apple-Store-pointsquad.jpg?resize=750%2C450"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.pointsquad.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Bent-iPhone-6-Plus-pointsquad.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[gallery-WWtx]">
            <img alt="Bent iPhone 6 Plus pointsquad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2042" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.pointsquad.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Bent-iPhone-6-Plus-pointsquad.jpg?resize=750%2C450"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Applefinally broke its silence about the
           <a href="http://www.pointsquad.com/2014/09/10/the-all-new-iphone-6-bigger-than-bigger/" target="_blank" title="The all new iPhone 6 Bigger than bigger">
            iPhone 6 Plus
           </a>
           bendgate
           <a href="http://www.pointsquad.com/2014/09/24/iphone-6-plus-prone-to-accidental-bending/" target="_blank">
            controversy
           </a>
           that took over the internet over the past couple of days. The Cupertino giant told
           <a href="http://recode.net/2014/09/25/apple-reports-only-nine-complaints-of-bent-iphones/" target="_blank">
            <i>
             Re/code
            </i>
           </a>
           that the problem is
           <strong>
            extremely rare
           </strong>
           and is pointing to the design of iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus as proof that the company took several steps to prevent bending or any permanent deformations.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <strong>
             Apple told
             <em>
              Re/code,
             </em>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <em>
             <strong>
              Wechose these high-quality materials and construction very carefully for their strength and durability,
             </strong>
            </em>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <em>
             <strong>
              We also perform rigorous tests throughout the entire development cycle including 3-point bending, pressure point cycling, sit, torsion, and user studies. iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus meet or exceed all of our high quality standards to endure everyday, real life use.
             </strong>
            </em>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Despite all the controversy, Apple has so far received a total of nine complaints from customers with bent iPhone 6 devices. We reckon that the number doesnt include the
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=znK652H6yQM" target="_blank">
            YouTubers
           </a>
           who bent their own devices for the sake of web notoriety. As it all began, when people in total of nine complaints about the bending of theiriPhone 6 Plus with images and videos appeared showing Apples 5.5-inch phablet with a noticeable bend, and the damage resulted simply from storing the phone in a pocket or other everydayusage.
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="450" src="//www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/znK652H6yQM" width="750">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           If youriPhone 6 or iPhone 6 Plus is bent from normal use, it will need to pass Visual Mechanical Inspection in order to qualify for warranty replacement. I presume that you either go to a Apple store or go to
           <a href="http://www.apple.com/support/iphone/" target="_blank">
            apple.com
           </a>
           support page and sign in with your apple deatails.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Yet, the controversy hasnt exactly stopped Apple fromselling its latest iPhones. Even now, amidst ofall the bending hysteria, iPhone 6 Plus remains sold out at most retailers internationally.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://recode.net/2014/09/25/apple-reports-only-nine-complaints-of-bent-iphones/" target="_blank">
            <em>
             Source
            </em>
           </a>
          </p>

            Written By: Ahsan Ul Kabir
