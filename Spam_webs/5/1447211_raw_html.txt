      <h1 class="title">
       Jaw-Dropping: One-Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed for US$21.8M
      </h1>
      <p class="post-meta">
       <span class="byline">
        By Staff Writer
       </span>
       |
       <span class="pubDate">
        Friday, 06 April 2012
       </span>
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="with-sidebar lazyload news-view" id="content">
      <div class="post">
       <div class="post_body single">
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 10" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_10.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         With an astonishing asking price of US$21.8 million, this Tokyo pad may just be the most expensive one-room apartment in the world. Simply dubbed The House, the apartment is situated in the hyper-exclusive Minami-Azabu district of Tokyo. And at 4,434 square feet, this ain't no shoebox apartment either.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 6" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_6.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 5" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_5.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Boasting bespoke furniture by Cecotti Collezioni and filled with lots of soothing Italian wood and stone, the place is suffused with a contemporary sense of elegance. There're lots of other interesting details in the apartment, such as original works from noted Japanese artist Hiroshi Senju that were painted
         <em>
          on site,
         </em>
         and a Japanese Ryotei style dining room to allow you or your specially hired chef to serve guests directly from the kitchen.
        </p>
        <p>
         A zen-like enclosed courtyard garden, a Parisian-style terrace overlooking Arisugawa park and an august entrace hallway with an expansive 200 shoe storage unit completes the experience. Scroll through for more pictures of the ultimate bachelor's pad.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 1" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_1.jpg?width=650"/>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 2" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_2.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 8" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_8.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 3" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_3.jpg?width=650"/>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 4" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_4.jpg?width=650"/>
         <img alt="Jaw Dropping One Bedroom Tokyo Pad Listed For Us218m 7" src="http://i.luxury-insider.com/uploads/news/2012/04/jaw-dropping-one-bedroom-tokyo-pad-listed-for-us218m_7.jpg?width=650"/>
        </p>
