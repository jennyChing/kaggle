     <h1 class="h-bttm-line h-no-bullet">
      Street Delivery 2014  Bucharest
     </h1>
     <div class="row">
      <div class="col-sm-6 col-sm-offset-3">
       <p>
        Between June 13th-15th, we visited
        <a href="http://2014.streetdelivery.ro/" target="_blank" title="Street Delivery 2014">
         <strong>
          Street Delivery 2014
         </strong>
        </a>
        in Bucharest.
       </p>
       <p>
        Each year, in Bucharest, but alsoin Timioara and Iai, a street is closed for cars and open for the people. The event offers the opportunity to take part in various artistic, social or civic activities, turning the public space into a fun, interactive and interesting place.
       </p>
       <p>
        This year, we spent a lot of time reading with some fine square cats on the rooftop of Crtureti library, lost our golden heads in the crowd, took a ride in the carousel of exploitation, decodedthe urban prophecy, coloured some very nice books, had a t-shirt upgrade and enjoyed an underground cocktail. See for yourselves.
       </p>
