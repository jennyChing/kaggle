       Chakaia Booker | Artist and Sculptor
      </a>
     </h1>
     <p>
      <a class="comments-link" href="http://bumbumbum.me/2010/10/18/chakaia-booker-artist-and-sculptor/#comments" title="Comment on Chakaia Booker | Artist and Sculptor">
       One comment... 
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      African American artist
      <a href="http://www.chakaiabooker.com/" target="_blank">
       Chakaia Booker
      </a>
      is best known for the material and the process she uses in the majority of her work: cut-up automobile tires that are reassembled on wooden or steel armatures to create abstract sculptures.
     </p>
     <p>
      Over the past decade,
      <a class="broken_link" href="http://www.decordova.org/art/exhibitions/current/booker.html" target="_blank">
       Chakaia Booker
      </a>
      has become one of America's most important contemporary sculptors. And it's with out a doubt that her work is indeed impressing.
     </p>
