              <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
               Most Useful iPad Apps 2015
              </h3>
              <div class="post-header">
               <div class="post-header-line-1">
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4333128674081945634">
               <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 There are thousands of iPad apps available in the apple appstore but do we require all of these apps? My answer is no. I am giving you the name of the most useful iPad apps in the year 2014 and I am sure these apps will remain their popularity till 2015. Now the question comes about your interest. If you are a businessman then your targeted apps will be business related apps, if you are a musician then your targeted apps will be music related apps and so on.
                 <br/>
                 <a name="more">
                 </a>
                 <br/>
                 Most useful iPad apps will fulfill the needs of all iPad users. At first, think about the storage capacity of your iPad. It might be 16 GB, 32 GB or 64 GB. Will it not be amazing if you can use additional storage for your iPad or iPhone? You know iPad does not have USB port so you cant connect your classic printer with your iPad. Will it not be amazing if you can
                 <a href="http://www.bestiosapps.org/2014/08/how-to-print-from-ipad.html">
                  print documents from you iPad
                 </a>
                 using cloud services? I am sure you are looking for the answers of these questions.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: left;">
                 <tbody>
                  <tr>
                   <td style="text-align: center;">
                    <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Cp3VE50hdYo/U_AlpdODhOI/AAAAAAAAANE/eTYm8ow4P5Q/s1600/Most%2Buseful%2Bipad%2Bapps%2B2015.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                     <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Cp3VE50hdYo/U_AlpdODhOI/AAAAAAAAANE/eTYm8ow4P5Q/s1600/Most%2Buseful%2Bipad%2Bapps%2B2015.jpg" width="240"/>
                    </a>
                   </td>
                  </tr>
                  <tr>
                   <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                    Most Useful iPad Apps
                   </td>
                  </tr>
                 </tbody>
                </table>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Most useful iPad apps are given below:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Hard Drive Management:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 If you are a new iPad user then the first question will click on your mind about its storage drive location. You might ask - how can I save documents to my iPad storage like I do in my home PC? To do that
                 <a href="http://appcrawlr.com/ios/filemaster-file-manager-downloa">
                  filemaster
                 </a>
                 app will be the right choice for you. In this app you can make folders and can store separate category files to separate folders.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Storage Management:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 Now lets think about additional storage for iPad or iPhone. You can use
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/google-drive-free-online-storage/id507874739?mt=8">
                  Google drive
                 </a>
                 and
                 <a href="https://www.dropbox.com/ipad">
                  dropbox
                 </a>
                 to store your files or documents. These two apps will provide you storage facility to their server. You can access to their server anytime and from anywhere. You will get 15 GB storage if you use Google drive. Dropbox will provide you 2 GB storage but you can purchase more storage from them.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Notes Management:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 To manage your daily notes you can use iOS inbuilt notes app and
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/myscript-memo/id446368116?mt=8">
                  MyScript Memo
                 </a>
                 . You can use stylus on MyScript Memo and can easily convert your hand written text into word format. If you want to add graphics then
                 <a href="http://evernote.com/evernote/guide/ios/">
                  Evernote
                 </a>
                 will be the right choice for you.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Email Management:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 iOS has an inbuilt email app. You can easily setup your email to that mail app or you can go for any third party email apps. If you are a Gmail user then the best choice will be
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/gmail-email-from-google/id422689480?mt=8">
                  Gmail app
                 </a>
                 for you. Gmail has modified their iOS mail client app and now this app is in good shape.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Office Work Management:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 There are lots of apps available in the apple app store to do office related works. These apps are
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/microsoft-word-for-ipad/id586447913?mt=8">
                  Microsoft word for iPad
                 </a>
                 ,
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/microsoft-excel-for-ipad/id586683407?mt=8">
                  Microsoft excel for iPad
                 </a>
                 ,
                 <a href="https://www.apple.com/ios/numbers/">
                  Numbers
                 </a>
                 ,
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/pages/id361309726?mt=8">
                  Pages
                 </a>
                 ,
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/keynote/id361285480?mt=8">
                  Keynotes
                 </a>
                 ,
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/officesuite-professional-mobile/id675054186?mt=8">
                  Office Suite pro
                 </a>
                 , etc. You can try office suite pro. This app can easily synchronize your google drive/dropbox/one drive documents and you ca update your documents directly from this app.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Scanner:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 You can use
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/camscanner-free-pdf-document/id388627783?mt=8">
                  CamScanner app
                 </a>
                 to scan your documents. You can upload your scanned documents to google drive/dropbox/one drive/evernote/ box through this app. You can print or email your scan documents by using this CamScanner app.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Navigation:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/google-maps/id585027354?mt=8">
                  Google Maps
                 </a>
                 has updated their navigation system. I should say this is the best navigation system for iPad or iPhone users.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Photo-shoot:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 iOS has an inbuilt Camera app but this app does not have customization facility. So you can use
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/procam-xl-2-camera-photo-editor/id734275619?mt=8">
                  ProCam XL 2
                 </a>
                 to take photographs or to capture videos. This camera app has lots of interesting features and setting options. You will enjoy using it.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Calculator:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 You can use
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/pcalc-lite-best-free-calculator/id300311831?mt=8">
                  PCalc Lite
                 </a>
                 to do mathematical calculation. This is a scientific calculator.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <u>
                  <b>
                   Entertainment:
                  </b>
                 </u>
                 To listen songs or to play videos you can use VLC media player. If you want to record audio then you can use
                 <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/quickvoice-recorder/id284675296?mt=8">
                  Quick Voice recorder
                 </a>
                 .
                </div>
               </div>
