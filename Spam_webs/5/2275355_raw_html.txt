     <h2 class="post-title entry-title">
      Ten Things Every Gun Owner Must Know About Barack Obama
     </h2>
     <div id="belowTitleAd">
     </div>
     <div class="postmeta">
      <span class="meta-date">
       Posted on
       <a href="http://www.conservativedailynews.com/2012/03/ten-things-every-gun-owner-must-know-about-barack-obama/" rel="bookmark" title="6:44 pm">

      </span>
      <span class="meta-author sep">

       <a href="http://www.conservativedailynews.com/author/r-mitchell/" rel="author" title="View all posts by R. Mitchell">

     <div class="entry clearfix">
      <p>
       NRA-ILA is starting a new campaign to inform gun owners and those that want to become gun owners of the dangers the current administration poses to their second amendment rights.
      </p>
      <p>
       The second amendment to the constitution does not afford the government the right to decide how you exercise your right to bear arms, it instead restricts the government from infringing upon that right.
      </p>
      <p>
       For those that have a gun for the occasional trip to the range or that dont hunt regularly, the idea that the government is already trying to strip them of their right to do so may not be apparent. The NRA has distilled the actions and plans of President Obama into a shareable and informative fact sheet (
       <em>
        click the image below to download your own larger version to share with others
       </em>
       ).
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://conservativedailynews.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/10-things-every-gun-owner-should-know.pdf">
        <img alt="obama on the second amendment" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-44175" height="590" src="http://conservativedailynews.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/10-things-every-gun-owner-should-know-457x590.jpg" title="10-things-every-gun-owner-should-know" width="457"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Share this post and the fact sheet with every gun owner or prospective gun owner you know. If you dont protect your right to bear arms, who will?
      </p>
