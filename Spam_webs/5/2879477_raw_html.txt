    <p>
     <b>
      Even if you don't watch
     </b>
     <i>
      Mad Men
     </i>
     , you're probably aware that the characters drink. A lot. Eat Like a Man's own Elizabeth Gunnison
     <a href="http://www.esquire.com/blogs/food-for-men/mad-men-drinking-at-work">
      tried drinking and working
     </a>
     like Roger Sterling, which explained why Sterling seems to achieve so little. Now supercut veterans Slacktory have made a five-minute supercut of all the drinks consumed on
     <i>
      Mad Men
     </i>
     , set to jazz music. Despite the apparent monotony, you do learn a few things about the characters: Don Draper is a brown-spirits guy, Roger slightly more a vodka guy. Betty drinks either daintily or angrily, depending on whether there are other people around. Peggy drinks hungrily. And Don drinks the most of anyone, until he stops drinking so much and goes swimming instead.
