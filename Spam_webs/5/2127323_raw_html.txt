           <strong>

           </strong>

           The Dalmatian today is muscular and strong, having an athletic look, standing 48cm to 58cm quantified between and weighs between 20kg and 27kg. The spots vary in the size of a dime to the size of a half dollar. The expression is intelligent and attentive. They have a long tail, round, dark eyes plus floppy ears.

The coach may have to find something that catches the interest of the dog to maintain focus and their motivation. Socialization is also crucial, Dalmatians are wary of strangers.
