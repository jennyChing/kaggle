          </h3>
         </div>
         <span class="datetxt author-text">
          Written By Brian Ford
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="postbody">
         <div class="post_image">
          <a href="https://www.certifiedgoldexchange.com/the-truth-about-u-s-unemployment-statistics/">
           <img alt="The Truth about U.S. Unemployment Statistics" src="https://www.certifiedgoldexchange.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/The-Truth-about-US-Unemployment-Statistics.jpg" title="The Truth about U.S. Unemployment Statistics"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="post_content">
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           TheU.S. Department of Labor recently made news with its claim of a 5.6% unemployment rate, but conservative blog Bankrupting America and others have questioned the accuracy of that figure.
          </p>
          <p>
           According to BankruptingAmerica.org the official unemployment rate does not factor people who have given up looking for work into the equation. It also counts people with less than part-time jobs, including people who mow lawns and sell knick-knacks online, as employed.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you believe the government should be transparent with its unemployment statistics, Like and Share this post.
