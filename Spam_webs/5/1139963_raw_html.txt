    <h1>
     Pamper Parties
    </h1>
    <p>
     <strong>
      Pamper parties are a great way for girls to celebrate any occasion.  Pamper parties are perfect for birthdays, sleep-overs, end of term or leaving  parties
     </strong>
     .
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p>
     All of our pamper parties are 2 hours long. Each party  includes an age appropriate make over in addition to fun and games tailored to  each specific age group. Our pamper parties are suitable for girls aged 4 - 16.
    </p>
    <p>
     We have been providing pamper parties to Hertfordshire and  beyond for in excess of 8 years so you can be assured that if you trust us to  host your party that you are in safe hands!
    </p>
    <p>
     Please choose a party that suits your requirements best from  the list opposite. Some frequently asked questions are answered beneath.
    </p>
    <p>
     If  your question is not answered here please contact us and we will be glad to  help.
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <h2>
     Pamper Party FAQ's
    </h2>
    <p>
     <strong>
      What is a pamper party?
     </strong>
     <br/>
     Our pamper parties involve fun, games and obviously a bit of pampering! We make sure that each party is taiolred specidically to each partucular age group, meaning that the guest at your event will only receive an age appropriate make-opver.  Our pamper parties generally involve 
 each girl receiveing hair styling (usually put up, curling, straightening etc...), age apporpriate make-up and nail art. Each girl will receive a make-over that is specific to them.
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      How long do pamper parties last?
     </strong>
     <br/>
     All of our pamper parties are tow hours along with the exception of the limo party which is two and a half hours.
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      How many guests can I invite to a pamper party?
      <br/>
     </strong>
     This varies depending upon which pamper party you sllect. Please cick on a party opposite to find out full details about what it includes.
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      Where should I host my pamper party?
     </strong>
     <br/>
     Most people host the party in their own home. this generally leads to a relaxed atmosphere and an all round more comforatble experience for the girls. However if you do not wish to host the party at home you can hire a local hall. We do not currently have a venue for you to come to.
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
