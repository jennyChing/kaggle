       <p>
        This November
        <a href="https://krrb.com" target="_blank" title="Krrb Classifieds">
         Krrb Classifieds
        </a>
        turns fourand how our babys growing up! From a fledgling start-up to a network powering the classifieds for
        <a href="http://classifieds.apartmenttherapy.com" target="_blank" title="Apartment Therapy Classifieds">
         Apartment Therapy
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://classifieds.chicagomag.com" target="_blank" title="Chicago Magazine Classifieds">
         Chicago Magazine
        </a>
        and the newly launched
        <a href="http://buycheapart.com" target="_blank" title="Cheap Art Classifieds">
         Cheap Art Classifieds
        </a>
        , weve grown beyond our wildest dreams
        <em>
         (ok, so our dreams are a bit wilder)
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Today nearly a million dollars worth of merchandise is sold each month and over 46% of all listings published to the Krrb network sell within 30 daysmany of which are sold within hours of publishing. These numbers are growing month over month and our team is working hard every day to make Krrb the most effective place to sell locally.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Were so confident that youll sell through our classifieds network, weve decided to create
         <em>
          The Krrb Seller Challenge
         </em>
         . List (5) items to Krrb (or any of our partner sites) and if none of them sell within 30 days, well pay you $25 cash
         <span id="more-38976">
         </span>
         !
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Its free, its easy  so why not give it a try? If we pass the challenge, youve found a fun, friendly and safe place to sell your stuff locally. If we failwell, $25 is nothing to shake a stick at, eh?
       </p>
       <h4>
        How It Works:
       </h4>
       <ol>
        <li>
         <a href="https://krrb.com" target="_blank" title="Join Krrb For Free">
          Join Krrb for free
         </a>
         . You can also join via
         <a href="http://classifieds.apartmenttherapy.com" target="_blank" title="Apartment Therapy Classifieds">
          Apartment Therapy
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://classifieds.chicagomag.com" target="_blank" title="Chicago Magazine Classifieds">
          Chicago Magazine
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://buycheapart.com" target="_blank" title="Cheap Art Classifieds">
          Cheap Art Classifieds
         </a>
         . It doesnt matter where you join, your Krrb account will work on all of them.
        </li>
        <li>
         Create (5) new listings or more and price them fairly. Take great photos and write thorough descriptions. Use our
         <a href="https://krrb.com/iphone" target="_blank" title="Krrb's iPhone App">
          iPhone app
         </a>
         to
         <em>
          snap &amp; list
         </em>
         or our
         <a href="https://krrb.com/krrb-it" target="_blank" title="Krrb It Button">
          Krrb It button
         </a>
         to bring over your listings from Etsy or eBay.
        </li>
        <li>
         You must comment below to enter the challenge and include a link to your corner or listings. This will allow us to help promote your listings for you!
        </li>
        <li>
         If none of your (5) listings sell within the 30 days of publishing, email us at
         <a href="mailto:support@krrb.com">
          support@krrb.com
         </a>
         and we will send you the cash.
        </li>
       </ol>
       <h4>
        Tips for Selling:
       </h4>
       <ol>
        <li>
         Complete your profile. Sellers with a photo most definitely sell more.
        </li>
        <li>
         Get the word out to your friends, family and followers. Link your account to Facebook and Twitter to share your listings with friends and followers.
        </li>
        <li>
