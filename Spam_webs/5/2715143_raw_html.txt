           <h2>
            <img alt="orchid-262640_1280" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1820" height="225" src="http://livingwellforsuccess.com/wp-content/uploads/orchid-262640_1280-300x225.jpg" title="entrepreneur" width="300"/>
            Entrepreneur &amp; FlowerLover!
           </h2>
           <p>
            I surprised myself the other day when I begancomparing the growing of flowers, particularly orchidsto
            <a href=" http://thesfm.com/lapoo831?pg=video-series&amp;t=orchid+blog+2%2F5%2F15" target="_blank">
             growing my
             <b>
              business
             </b>
            </a>
            ! I absolutely love orchids, there are so many varieties and colors. They are just beautiful, but I have not had a lot of success keeping them. Ok, I must admit, I just outright kill them!
           </p>
           <p>
            I was very blessed to have received a beautiful potted orchid combination for Christmas (from someone who didnt realize I am a serial orchid killer) and it is doingextremelywell! I experimented on the best place for it, which fortunately ended up in my office where I can enjoy it as I work.
           </p>
           <p>
            According to the
            <a href="https://www.aos.org/Default.aspx?id=222" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             American Orchid Society
            </a>
            , Orchids are far tougher and hardier than most people think, and are, by and large, extremely adaptable. I find true entrepreneurs to be the same!
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
             The trick to keeping orchids happy and thriving seems to be providing just the right culture. This led me to think about entrepreneurs, and how they need the right culture to thrive as well.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            There are3 main elements to keeping
            <b>
             successful
            </b>
            orchids:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Light
            </li>
            <li>
             Planting Medium
            </li>
            <li>
             Water
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h3>
            What Does It Take To Be a Successful Entrepreneur?
           </h3>
           <p>
            When I thought about it, growing as a
            <a href=" http://thesfm.com/lapoo831?pg=video-series&amp;t=orchid+blog+2%2F5%2F15" target="_blank">
             <i>
              successful
             </i>
             <b>
              entrepreneur
             </b>
            </a>
            requires similar elements:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Light
             </strong>
             : As entrepreneurs we need to constantly be learning while we build. We mustnever stop growing. By reading, listening to podcasts, watching videos, attending conferences and connecting with mentors we open our minds to new thoughts and possibilities. Our mind processes the information we draw in much like a plant photosynthesizes light.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              <img alt="orchid-607351_1280" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1821" height="225" src="http://livingwellforsuccess.com/wp-content/uploads/orchid-607351_1280-300x225.jpg" title="entrepreneur" width="300"/>
              Planting Medium
             </strong>
             : A best practice for new or seasoned entrepreneurs is community. Your friends and family members that are not on their ownentrepreneurial journey will probably not understand why you want to build a
             <u>
              business
             </u>
             , the drive you feel to do so, or the struggles you deal with every day. Community provides the environment to be nurtured and encouraged.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            If you are a budding
            <i>
             entrepreneur
            </i>
            and can connect with a training platform that includes community you have everything you need to succeed. It is difficult knowing how to pick a
            <a class="broken_link" href="%20http://thesfm.com/lapoo831?pg=video-series&amp;t=orchid+blog+2%2F5%2F15" target="_blank">
             reputable training platform
            </a>
            , but when you find it, you will be ready to set your roots and soar!
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Water:
             </strong>
             Every living thing needs water to thrive. Mentorship is the water to our business.
             <a href="http://livingwellforsuccess.com/top-5-attributes-good-mentor/" target="_blank">
              Finding a mentor
             </a>
             can be challenging, and is often not free, but is most necessary to the success of all entrepreneurs, especially the newbies. A mentor should be a seasoned and
             <u>
              successful
             </u>
             business man or woman in the same type of business you are in. A mentors job is not to be your best friend, but to guide you through the obstacles, hoping to save you time and money.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            Just as I have learned what it takes to grow orchids and my business successfully, Itrust you will take away information that will help you to grow as an
            <u>
             entrepreneur
            </u>
            !
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #800080;">

                <em>
                 How To Start A Profitable Online Business Starting From Scratch
                </em>
               </a>

              </span>
