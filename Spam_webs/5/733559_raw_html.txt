         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="group meta">
         <p class="byline">
          8 May, 2013
         </p>
         <div class="vertical_pages">
          <a class="vertical-badge" href="/great-for/photography" style="background-color: #0098CB">

        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="box-inner">
        <div class="group description">
         <p>
          I wanted a GoPro mount robust enough to resist the shocks to be mounted in the fork of my mountain bike.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="group kit">
         <h3>
          Kit list
         </h3>
         <ul>
          <li>
           GoPro Tripod mount
          </li>
          <li>
           1 single-use foil pack of Sugru (5g)
          </li>
          <li>
           L shaped metal bracket
          </li>
          <li>
           Clamp
          </li>
          <li>
           GoPro to mount on
          </li>
          <li>
           Cling film
          </li>
         </ul>

        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="steps">
        <div class="box-inner">

       </div>
       <div class="steps">
        <div class="box-inner">

          </p>

          </p>
