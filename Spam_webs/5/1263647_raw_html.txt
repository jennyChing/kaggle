          <div class="content-detail clearfix">
           <p>
            The fishing knots below are commonly used across the globe to help you catch more fish. TheBlood Knot and Improved Clinched Knotare heavily used by our team at Trouts Fly Fishing and are good for any angler to know.
            <span style="line-height: 1.6;">
             Whether you are just learning to fly fish or you're a seasoned veteran, it's always good to freshen up and review your knots before hitting the river. Speaking of rivers, if you are getting out this weekend, make sure you check out our
             <a href="http://troutsflyfishing.com/info/fishing-information/stream-flows">
              stream flow reports
             </a>
             .
            </span>
           </p>
