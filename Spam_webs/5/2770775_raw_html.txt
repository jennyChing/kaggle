         Chocolate Mousse Oreo Pie Recipe
        </h1>

        <p>
         This is a rich and creamy pie that combines Oreo cookies with chocolate mousse with amazing results! I made this for my daughter on her birthday because she is not a big fan of cake, and it was delicious!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.upstateramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/chocolate-mousse-oreo-pie.png">
          <img alt="Chocolate Mousse Oreo Pie - Delicious Dessert" border="0" class="alignnone" height="477" src="http://www.upstateramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/chocolate-mousse-oreo-pie_thumb.png" style="padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px none;" title="Chocolate Mousse Oreo Pie #pie #dessert" width="539"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Most of the Oreo pies I have seen are just cookies and cream, but I wanted to include some chocolate to make it even better. The pie consists of a crust made of Oreo cookie crumbs, followed by a layer of Chocolate Mousse. It is topped off with a creamy layer of whipped topping and cream cheese, with chopped up cookie pieces. To decorate it I added a ring of mini-Oreos around the edge.

         </a>

          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Crust
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            24 Oreo Cookies
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 T butter
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Mousse
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8 oz. package semi-sweet chocolate chips
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 T butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 egg yolks
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. heavy cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             t. vanilla
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Cream Topping
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8 Oreo Cookies
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 c. whipped topping, thawed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            25 Mini Oreo Cookies
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Crust
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Take 24 Oreo cookies and make them into crumbs in a food processor.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Melt the butter and combine it with the cookie crumbs.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Press into a pie plate and refrigerate while you make the Mousse.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Mousse
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine the chocolate chips and butter in a microwave safe bowl.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat in the microwave for about 30 seconds, and stir until melted completely.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Put the chocolate in a mixer bowl and add the egg yolk, sugar, cream and vanilla.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Beat until it is creamy.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread the Mousse on top of the crust and refrigerate for at least an hour.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            This is a good time to get out the cream cheese so it comes to room temperature.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Cream Topping
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Chop the remaining 8 Oreo cookies into small pieces.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a mixer bowl combine the whipped topping and cream cheese and beat until smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Fold in the chopped cookies, and then spread on top of the pie.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add mini Oreos around the edge of the pie.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
