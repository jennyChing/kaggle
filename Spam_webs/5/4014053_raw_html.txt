        Grafton VT: Ski Magic and Irreverent History
       </h1>

       <p>
        $5 dollars and a jug of rum was the price of naming rights when Grafton was renamed in 1791, the same year Vermont entered the Union. Samuel Spring was already selling spirits on the site where theOld Tavern now stands, but over time the enterprise added a hotel. A popular stopover on the Boston-to-Montreal and Boston-to-Albany routes, Grafton Inn hosted the likes of Rudyard Kipling, Ralph Waldo Emerson, Oliver Wendell Holmes, and Henry David Thoreau. Nowadays Winter visitors to Grafton, VT find history, community ties, andsome ofthe USAs most magical snow terrain.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6909" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Ponds-Covered-Bridge.jpg">
         <img alt="McWilliam Bridge, a covered bridge at Grafton Ponds Outdoor Center in Grafton, VT." class="size-medium wp-image-6909" height="435" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Ponds-Covered-Bridge-640x435.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Covered bridge at Grafton Ponds Outdoor Center in Grafton, VT.
        </p>
       </div>
       <h2>
        What To Do
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         HIKE/NORDIC SKIING
        </strong>
        : Winter fun at
        <strong>
         <a href="http://graftonponds.com/" title="Grafton Ponds Outdoor Center">
          Grafton Ponds Outdoor Center
         </a>
        </strong>
        runs the gamut: FAT biking; dog sled rides; tubing on 600-foot run; snowshoeing over 10K trails, even Wine &amp; Cheese Snowshoe tours; plus cross-country skiing atone of Southern Vermonts best Nordic areas.
        <em>
         Winter season December 15 through March 14th. Tubing $10-15/2 hour; trail rates $5-16; lessons &amp; rentals available.
        </em>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6911" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Magic-Mountain-Ski-Mystery.jpg">
         <img alt="Mystery trail at Magic Mountain Ski Area in Southern Vermont." class="size-medium wp-image-6911" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Magic-Mountain-Ski-Mystery-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Mystery trail at Magic Mountain Ski Area in Southern Vermont.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         SKI:
        </strong>
        Nothing but corduroy packed powder lay ahead on a trail named, Mystery. My son and husband had ditched me in favor of double-black-diamonds, and only the wind whispered in my ear as I leaned over the jumpoff onto the intermediate trail at
        <a href="http://www.magicmtn.com/" title="Magic Mountain Ski Area">
         <strong>
          Magic Mountain Ski Area
         </strong>
        </a>
        in Londonberry, Vermont. It was 2 oclock, well into a bluebird day on a Winter weekend, but pristine snow belied the presence of skiers.Its not unusual to have ski trails all to yourself, even in peak season.
        <em>
         Open Thursday thru Sunday, all Holiday Periods and any Monday, Tuesday or Wednesday following asnow storm of 6 or more in Season (December-April.) Full day tickets range from $32-$63, with occasional $20 Throwback Thursdays. Tubing, lessons, and rentals available.
        </em>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6927" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Ski-Glades.jpg">
         <img alt="Ski Glades - Magic Mountain - Vermont" class="size-medium wp-image-6927" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Ski-Glades-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Skiing the glades at Magic Mountain.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         SLEIGH RIDES
        </strong>
        : Reserve a sleigh ride from
        <a href="http://friesiansofmajesty.com/" title="Friesians of Majesty">
         Friesians of Majesty
        </a>
        , and glidethrough 650-acres of forests and snow meadowspulled by jet-black Friesian horses.
        <em>
         Sleigh ride &amp; tour $78 per adult ($62 sleigh ride only.)
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         SHOP
        </strong>
        : Buy cheese and specialty food items at
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.graftonvillagecheese.com/" title="Grafton Village Cheese">
          Grafton Village Cheese
         </a>
        </strong>
        , one of Graftons earliest businesses. Founded in 1892, in the days before refrigeration, local dairy farmers formed a cooperative to turn their surplus milk into cheese.
        <em>
         Open daily 10-5; on select days watch cheese being made at the production facility nearby.
        </em>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6921" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Village-Store.jpg">
         <img alt="Grafton Village Store - Grafton VT" class="wp-image-6921 size-medium" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Village-Store-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Can you spot the dog guarding the snow plow, motor running, while his human picks up a cup of Joe?
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         EAT/SHOP
        </strong>
        : Grab a cup of coffee and check local flyers at
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.graftonvermont.org/shopping.html" title="Grafton Village Groceries">
          Grafton Village Store
         </a>
        </strong>
        where you can also stock up on staples and Vermont products.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         VISIT
        </strong>
        : Environmental education and the Fairy House Festival draws visitors to
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.nature-museum.org/index.html" title="Grafton Nature Museum">
          Grafton Nature Museum
         </a>
        </strong>
        . Hands on exhibitsfocus on the regions plants, animals, and geology; outdoors, explore hiking trails andthe cave, a historic rock structure created in the early 1900s. Indoors,
        <em>
         Free admission, open on Thursdays and by appointment.
        </em>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6925" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Forge.jpg">
         <img alt="Grafton Forge" class="size-medium wp-image-6925" height="416" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Forge-640x416.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Grafton Forge
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         SHOP/VISIT
        </strong>
        : Observe the ancient art of blacksmithing at
        <a href="http://www.windham-foundation.org/programs/blacksmith.html" title="Grafton Forge ">
         <strong>
          Grafton Forge
         </strong>
        </a>
        as practiced by master craftsman,Aaron Anderson. The shop houses a rotating exhibit of historic tools and artifacts; home and garden ironware available for sale also on display.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         SHOP/SUGAR HOUSE:
        </strong>
        During sugar season (mid-February through mid-April) stop by
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.plummerssugarhouse.com/index.html" title="Plummer's Sugar House">
          Plummers Sugar House
         </a>
        </strong>
        to see sap turned to Vermonts finest maple syrup. Gift shop is open 7 days a week year round, free tour of house with any purchase.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6923" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Old-Firestation.jpg">
         <img alt="Old Fire Station - My Mind's Design - Grafton VT" class="size-medium wp-image-6923" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Old-Firestation-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         My Minds Design located in landmark Old Fire Station.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         SHOP
        </strong>
        : Housed in Graftons historic fire station, Jason Ballards woodworking studio,
        <a href="http://www.mymindsdesign.com/home.html" title="My Mind's Design">
         <strong>
          My Minds Design
         </strong>
        </a>
        carves out whimsical heirloom furniture.
        <em>
         Open Monday-Saturday, by chance or by appointment.
        </em>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6917" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Phelps-Barn-Pub.jpg">
         <img alt="Phelps Barn Pub - Grafton, VT" class="size-medium wp-image-6917" height="387" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Phelps-Barn-Pub-640x387.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Phelps Barn Pub
        </p>
       </div>
       <h2>
        Where To Eat
       </h2>
       <p>
        <strong>
         EAT/DRINK:
        </strong>
        Cosy up to one of two fireplaces in
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.graftoninnvermont.com/dining/phelps-barn/" title="Phelps Barn Pub">
          Phelps Barn Pub
         </a>
        </strong>
        wherecasual menu turns extraordinary: ambrosial Arugula Salad, grass-fed Vermont burgers topped with Grafton cheddar, plus Flatbread Fridays and Live Music Saturdays. Word to the wise, dont miss thehouse brewed 1801 Ale.
        <em>
         (Tip: Get there early, the restaurant stops serving at 9pm.)
        </em>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6919" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Old-Tavern-Restaurant.jpg">
         <img alt="Old Tavern Restaurant - Grafton VT" class="size-medium wp-image-6919" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Old-Tavern-Restaurant-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Old Tavern Restaurant
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         EAT:
        </strong>
        Surprisingly affordable farm to table fine dining at historic
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.graftoninnvermont.com/dining/old-tavern-restaurant/" title="Old Tavern Restaurant">
          Old Tavern Restaurant
         </a>
        </strong>
        , served in period rooms at Grafton Inn. Full Vermont Country Breakfast, complementary to hotel guests, includes a very generous entree, as well as homemade muffins served and cream served in fine pewter accompanied by decorative butter and fresh jam. To get a sense of the deliciousness to be had, check out a few
        <a href="http://www.graftoninnvermont.com/category/recipes/" title="house recipes">
         house recipes
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         EAT/DRINK/NIGHTLIFE:
        </strong>
        On the top floor of Magic Mountains lodge,
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.magicmtn.com/nightlife.php" title="Black Line Tavern">
          Black Line Tavern
         </a>
        </strong>
        facese ski and tubing trails (perfect for parents taking a break while kids play.) Hearty entreese.g. Chicken Pot Pie and Tuna Meltand Friday Taco Bar, plus a laid back aprs-skiscene.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Inn.jpg">
         <img alt="The Old Tavern at Grafton Inn - Grafton, Vermont" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-6914" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Inn-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Where To Stay
       </h2>
       <p>
        Entering
        <a href="http://www.graftoninnvermont.com/" title="Grafton Inn">
         <strong>
          Grafton Inn
         </strong>
        </a>
        is like stepping back in time at a very well-preserved historic museum where its OK to sit on the furniture. Small-scale rooms, as was typical of early 19th-century architecture, feature rich blue wall paneling, leather-bound books, and gilt-trimmed portraits.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Inn-Lobby.jpg">
         <img alt="Grafton Inn Lobby" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-6915" height="388" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Inn-Lobby-640x388.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Run by the Windham Foundation, committed to preserving and promoting Vermonts rural communities, the Grafton Inn is a great example of a historic inn that doesnt skimp on modern comforts.
       </p>
       <p>
        Grafton Inn offers guest rooms in the Main Tavern, as well as in multiple annex buildings spread about town. Our room, a Village Room with 2 double beds, was housed on the second floor in the Woodard House (circa 1874.) The upstairs housed two rooms with 1-2 more rooms on the ground level; common areas included a full kitchen, dining room, and TV/living room.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6913" style="width: 650px">
        <a href="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Inn-Guest-Room.jpg">
         <img alt="Grafton Inn Village Room at Woodard House in Grafton VT" class="size-medium wp-image-6913" height="424" src="http://www.getawaymavens.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Grafton-Inn-Guest-Room-640x424.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Grafton Inn Village Room at Woodard House.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The guest room itself was very spacious, while an entrance alcove provided space to store luggage out of sight. Beds were comfy, with fluffy comforters and better-quality linens. Extra pillows and blankets were stored in a closet.The bathroom, adorned with a cheery colonial print wallpaper and country lace curtains, was more than adequate, while the stash of free toiletries was both eco-friendly and pleasant-smelling.
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Winter rates $119-$275 per night includes complementary full country breakfast and Grafton Ponds trail use.
        </em>
       </p>
