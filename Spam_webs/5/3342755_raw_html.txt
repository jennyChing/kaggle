          <h2>
           'I Am a Stuffed Animal' Creates a Plush Toy of Your Likeness
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="references">
          <span class="mobileAuthor">
           By: Ashley C.R.-
          </span>
          Published:
          <span content="2010-03-03" itemprop="datePublished">
           Mar 3, 2010
          </span>
           References:
          <a href="http://iamastuffedanimal.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           iamastuffedanimal
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="articleText">
         Remember when you thought you had to be famous to get a doll made to your likeness? Well, gone are those days! Now you can become your own stuffed animal with 'I Am a Stuffed Animal.'
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The artists at 'I Am a Stuffed Animal' will reference a photo of your face, draw up a caricature and transfer it to a plush doll. In 4-6 weeks, youll receive the stuffed toy version of yourself! This is a fun gift for that good-humored friend who is hard to shop for.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Each custom doll costs $65.
