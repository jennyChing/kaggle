        <h1>
         Aesthetically Pleasing Doggie Doors
        </h1>
        <div class="entry">
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <a href="http://archwin.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Doggie-Doors.jpg">
           <img alt="Doggie Doors" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-641" height="200" src="http://archwin.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Doggie-Doors-300x200.jpg" title="Doggie-Doors" width="300"/>
          </a>
          Are you one of the nearly two-thirds of Americans who own at least one or more pets? If so, you know how quickly those cute and cuddly critters can become a part of your family. Unfortunately, unlike your husband or children, your four-legged family member needs to do their business outdoors. And you arent always around to help them with your magical opposable thumbs. So you have two options:
         </p>
         <ol style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           Clean up after your furry friend.
          </li>
          <li>
           Invest in an aesthetically pleasing doggie door.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Thanks to JELD-WEN, doggie doors are no longer the eyesores they once were. The JELD-WEN Vinyl Sliding Patio Door with an integrated pet door is a stylish new door system that provides a unique indoor outdoor access point for pets. Best of all, these patio doors have the same great energy saving benefits as JELD-WENs vinyl patio door line. Thats what we like to call a win-win! Additional benefits include:
         </p>
         <ol style="text-align: left;">
          <li>
           An integrated solution for maximum style and reliability.
          </li>
          <li>
           Available in an ENERGY STAR qualified configuration.
          </li>
          <li>
           Secure-closing pet panel with an easy to operate WEN-Lock system.
          </li>
          <li>
           Flap stays closed thanks to a magnetic pull, allowing easy passage, while blocking wind.
          </li>
          <li>
           The patio door operates the same as any standard patio door.
          </li>
          <li>
           Because of its vinyl construction, the door never needs to be painted.
          </li>
          <li>
           The pet door is available medium, large and extra-large.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Homeowners want reliable options to accommodate their pets that are also convenient, and look and perform great, said JELD-WENs director of product marketing for doors, Derek Brosterhous. These doors are a wonderful solution.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          JELD-WEN also offers a complete special order steel or fiberglass door with an installed pet door solution, available in a prehung unit.
         </p>
