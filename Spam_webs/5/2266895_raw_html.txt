        <blockquote class="ubb-synopsis">
         Jesus Rollerblading Christ--another helping of TheOatmeal! Mrow, MOAR kitty comics. Mr. Oats delivers a sidesplitting serving of cat comics in his new book, How to Tell If Your Cat Is Plotting to Kill You.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         If your cat is kneading you, that's not a sign of affection. Your cat is actually checking your internal organs for weakness. If your cat brings you a dead animal, this isn't a gift. It's a warning. How to Tell If Your Cat Is Plotting to Kill You is a hilarious, brilliant offering of cat comics, facts, and instructional guides from the creative wonderland at TheOatmeal.com.
        </blockquote>

         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="280" src="http://media.giphy.com/media/lnCggcJbfrY8E/giphy.gif" width="500"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://bellasshelf.com/book-review/6-ways-to-tell-if-your-cat-thinks-its-a-mountain-lion/&amp;media=http://media.giphy.com/media/lnCggcJbfrY8E/giphy.gif&amp;description=6 Ways To Tell If Your Cat Thinks Its A Mountain Lion')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Pawing at your face while you sleep: Cats arent very good at smothering people, but this wont stop them from trying
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Cat lovers who appreciate good satire will flip over this book. A humorous and sometimes morbid look at the mindset of the average house cat. Cute cartoons and funny narrations set the tone for this graphic novel. The author makes the reader exclaim Finally! A book that explains it in simple Laymans terms. All of these cute and quirky things cats do, are not just cute and quirky things cats do.
         <br/>
         This book has educated me and opened my eyes. I finally know how to protect myself from my felines. This book uses pictures to prove how normal cat behavior is in fact not so normal after all. Well, its normal for the felines who believe theyrestealth killing machines.                                                          Once a cat gets to a certain age they grudgingly accept the fact they failed at world domination. They realize their cozy lives arent so bad after all. Commence purring.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         If you appreciate satire and dark feline humor this book will make you purr. Or hack up a fur ball. Whichever.
        </p>
