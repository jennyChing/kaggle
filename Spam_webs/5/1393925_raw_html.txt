        Taste Local!
       </h2>
       <div class="content-entry" style="">
        Fresh and delicious is always on the menu in BruceGreySimcoe.  Spring and summer warmth means farm fresh produce and scrumptious baked treats at our farmers markets, farm stores and bakeries - perfect fare to cap off a day at the beach, to pack for your day on the trails or your driving tour adventure.  Savour local fare at one of our many eateries or pick up the goods for supper at the cottage.  Whatever your choice, our bakeries, food shops, butchers and specialty shops are stocked with all youll need for meal prep.  Bon apptit!
