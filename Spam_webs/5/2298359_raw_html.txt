       <p>
        Toronto, Canada (PRWEB), April 7, 2014  Clean Slate Janitorial Services (
        <a href="http://www.clean-slate.ca/" target="_blank">
         www.Clean-Slate.ca
        </a>
        ), a Greater Toronto Area-based professional cleaning company that specializes in commercial office cleaning, retail maintenance, floor restoration, carpet cleaning, and construction site cleanup, is stressing the importance of having a professional window cleaning performed for a business, particularly following the harsh winter the Toronto area has just endured.
       </p>
       <p>
        After the extreme cold, snow and ice the GTA experienced this past winter, its even more important to eliminate the lingering effects these conditions have had on ones windows, says Sam Panousis, owner and operator of Clean-Slate Janitorial Services, The record amount of salt used this past winter formed a fine dust that has accumulated on window surfaces, not to mention that mould, mildew, and mineral stains from water can become etched into the glass.
       </p>
       <p>
        Over time, this build-up can actually negatively alter the colour of the windows, he continues, If this level of contamination is allowed to remain, no amount of cleaning will ever get windows looking completely clean. (Source: Howlett, K., Dash for salt: Cities scramble to find this winters hottest commodity, The Globe and Mail, March 4, 2014 http://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/national/dash-for-salt-cities-scramble-to-find-this-winters-hottest-commodity/article17311222/.)
       </p>
       <p>
        The residual impact of unclean windows can leave a negative impression on colleagues, clients and visitors to an office. Panousis notes that windows are actually one of the first things someone sees when entering an office and are a factor in determining their opinion of a company.
       </p>
       <p>
        Dirty windows also mean less light, which impacts the mood of the office and its employees. Natural light is key to the atmosphere of an office, especially for those offices that feature tall windows throughout, he adds. Now that winter is over and more sunshine can be expected, this is the best time to have a professional cleaning service find an effective cleaning solution that can address these issues.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its important that cleaners use environmentally friendly products, as they are often working outside, where their chemicals will be released directly into the ozone. Thats why Clean-Slate Janitorial Services uses green products for all of its
        <a href="http://www.clean-slate.ca/window-blind-cleaning" target="_blank">
         window cleaning
        </a>
        jobs, Panousis concludes. We not only strive to provide clients with the very best in office cleaning services, but also recognize and adhere to our environmental responsibilities.
       </p>
       <p>
        Clean-Slate Janitorial Services has been serving commercial customers in the Greater Toronto Area, including downtown Toronto, Vaughan, Mississauga, Brampton, and Markham, for eight years. Services provided by Clean-Slate Janitorial Services include commercial office cleaning, retail management and janitorial services, floor restoration and buffing, carpet cleaning, window and blind cleaning, and construction site cleanup. To learn more about Clean-Slate Janitorial Services and to get a free quote, visit the companys web site at
        <a href="http://www.clean-slate.ca/" target="_blank">
         www.Clean-Slate.ca
        </a>
        or call 416-509-7454.
       </p>
