               <p>
                Calling all lovers of the California eclectic aesthetic!
                <a href="http://www.potterybarnkids.com/" target="_blank" title="Pottery Barn Kids">
                 Pottery Barn Kids
                </a>
                has tapped cult fashion designer andlifestyle guru Jenni Kayne for its first ever designer partnership. Announced today, the collaboration will offer a beautiful mix of modern, antique-mindedpieces thatreflect Kayne's relaxed andtimeless, California-inflected sensibility.The collection will include more than 50 pieces for nurseries, bedrooms, and playrooms, and will feature furniture, bedding, lighting, rugs, wall art, toys, decorative accessories, and other nursery essentials.
               </p>
               <p>
                Though this marks Kaynes first foray into the childrens home furnishings market,it's nosecret that the mother of two hasimpeccable style, so we're certain it will be loved by parents and kids alike. The collection willhit select Pottery Barn Kids stores in the United States, Canada, and Australia and
                <a href="http://www.potterybarnkids.com/" target="_blank" title="online">
                 online
                </a>
                in June 2015.
               </p>
