         Trick: Lock the Fn key

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.surfaceproblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/SURFACE-PRO-black-orange.png">
          <img alt="surface pro trick" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14" height="187" src="http://www.surfaceproblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/SURFACE-PRO-black-orange-300x187.png" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         For those like me who use the F1, F2, F3, etc. keys a lot, it can be annoying to press the Fn key every time (and this is all the more difficult for shortcuts like Ctrl-Shift-F5, which would have any regular person break her hand in the process).So the latest Microsoft update for the Surface Pro brings an easy way to Lock the Fn key
         <span id="more-277">
         </span>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         The latest Microsoft update for the Surface Pro brought an easy way to Lock the Fn key: Just Press Fn + Caps keys at the same time and here you go. Follow the same process to make the unlock.
        </p>
