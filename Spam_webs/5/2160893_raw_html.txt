        <div id="article-title">
         <h1>
          The 4 Best Careers For Your Zodiac Sign
         </h1>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="article-container" id="TNArticleBodySection">
        <div id="article-body">
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="media-image media-image-left" src="http://artstg.icbdr.com/sites/all/files/iStock_000002664160XSmall.jpg" style="margin: 4px 10px; float: left" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
           Contributed to jobs.net by
          </span>
          <a href="https://plus.google.com/107830037213142884188?rel=author" style="line-height: 1.5">
           Kim Evans
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
           People born under different
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrological_sign" target="_blank">
            signs of the zodiac
           </a>
           exhibit different personality traits. Naturally, different career paths are better suited to certain dispositions.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
           Discovering your career path through the zodiac isn't as far out as it seems. Find out what jobs are best suited to your sign.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
          </span>
         </p>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Capricorn (Born December 22 through January 19)
         </h2>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Capricorn is dependable and reliable. If you're a Capricorn, you're responsible, organized, and inclined to work hard to meet your goals. ACapricornis also logical, and loves jobs that work with math or money. A clever Capricorn should look for careers like:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Information Technology
           </strong>
           - Showcase your organizational skills and clever problem-solving .
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Medicine
           </strong>
           - Achieve your goals, as well as the goals of your patients.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Accounting
           </strong>
           - Work with both things you love: math and money.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Law
           </strong>
           - Embrace your responsibility and logical thinking.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Aquarius (Born January 20 through February 18)
         </h2>
         <p>
          Those born under the sign of Aquarius are smart, original thinkers, and visionaries. If you're an Aquarius, you're also likely a kind humanitarian and hard worker. A progressive Aquarius should consider careers in:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Astronomy
           </strong>
           - Take your unique ideas to the ends of the universe.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Natural history
           </strong>
           - Work toward the lofty goal of a better future based on our past.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Aviation
           </strong>
           - Use your intellect and work ethic to navigate the skies.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            The Arts
           </strong>
           - Showcase your vision humanitarianism through poetry, music, or acting.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Pisces (Born February 19 through March 20)
         </h2>
         <p>
          Pisces is generous, loves people, and is sensitive. Pisces is also popular, artistic, compassionate, and versatile. If you're a Pisces, you have a deep spiritual self too. You can exercise your best gifts as a/an:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Artist
           </strong>
           - Express your compassion and versatility through music, painting, or literature.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.jobs.net/jobs/wynn-resorts/en-us/search/?keyword=hair%20stylist&amp;location=" target="_blank">
             Hair stylist
            </a>
           </strong>
           - Show your creativity, as well as meet people and make them happy.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Philanthropist
           </strong>
           - Exhibit your generosity and compassion for others.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Justice
           </strong>
           - Harness compassion, generosity, and sensitivity to uphold the law.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Aries (BornMarch 21 through April 19)
         </h2>
         <p>
          Aries are an enthusiastic bunch, outspoken and full of ambition. You're strong-willed as well as creative. If you're an Aries, you can exhibit your best traits in:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            The Media -
           </strong>
           Voice your strong opinions inTV or "talk" radio.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Advertising
           </strong>
           - Use your creativity to achieveyour ambitions.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            The Military
           </strong>
           - Apply your strong will and enthusiasm to any military job.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.jobs.net/jobs/us-security-associates/en-us/search/uniformed-security-officer-jobs/ST7F1BV62K37BVS8KB1F/" target="_blank">
             Security guard
            </a>
           </strong>
           - Embrace your enthusiasm for upholding the law and protecting others.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Taurus (BornApril 20 through May 20)
         </h2>
         <p>
          A Taurus is practical-minded and methodical. If you're a Taurus, you're also determined, yet patient, dependable, and honest in your pursuits. Taurus is a great team player. Taurus should consider career options in:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Banking
           </strong>
           - Use your methodical ways to help others with their finances.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Accounting
           </strong>
           - Channel your practicality into the love of numbers.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Science
           </strong>
           - Showcase your determination and patience in solving problems.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Research
           </strong>
           - Open your practical mind to a world of information.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Gemini (Born May 21 through June 21)
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Gemini is optimistic and inquisitive. If you're a Gemini, you're also intelligent and energetic. These qualities make the Gemini most suitable for careersin:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Travel
           </strong>
           - Use your inquisitiveness and energy to help customers get to new places and discover new things.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Nature
           </strong>
           - Inquire about the earth and use that intelligence to discover new things about it.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Traveling sales
           </strong>
           - Experience new places and people with your boundless energy.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Business
           </strong>
           - Harness your energy and optimism to change a corporation from the ground up.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Cancer (Born June 22 through July 22)
         </h2>
         <p>
          Those born under the Cancer sign are imaginative and dramatic. If you're a Capricorn, you're a philosophical type who's also nurturing and protective of others. You should consider careers in:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Law
           </strong>
           - Exercise your passion for advising others.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Psychology
           </strong>
           - Nurture and offer sound counsel.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Teaching
           </strong>
           - Combineyour nurturing side with philosophy and imagination.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Nursing
           </strong>
           - Deal with drama and console those in pain.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Leo (BornJuly 23 through August 22)
         </h2>
         <p>
          Leo is spontaneous and often gregarious. If you're a Leo, you're an independent thinker and a born leader. A Leo should look for professions such as a/an:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            CEO
           </strong>
           - Channel your independent thinkinginto a leadership position.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Manager -
           </strong>
           Useyour spontaneous nature to make important decisions on the fly.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Editor
           </strong>
           - Lead writers in their craft.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Government worker -
           </strong>
           Use your power and disposition to make progressive decisions.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Virgo (Born August 23 through September 22)
         </h2>
         <p>
          A Virgois precise. If you're a Virgo, you balance your perfectionist tendencies with cheerfulness and wit. You're also hard-working, detail oriented, and have a gift for languages. A Virgo loves a job as a:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Statistician
           </strong>
           - Exercise your perfectionism and work ethic.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Medical Researcher
           </strong>
           - Perk up detail-oriented work with your cheerfulness.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Investigator
           </strong>
           - Channel your wit and precision.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Translator
           </strong>
           - Help others communicate through your bubbly personality.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Libra (Born September 23 through October 22)
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Libra is diplomatic and charming. If you're a Libra, you're a sociable person who's easy-going and cooperative. TheLibradoes well in the following jobs as:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Government workers
           </strong>
           - Channel your diplomacy into foreign relations.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Negotiators
           </strong>
           - Make use of your cooperative nature
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Administrators
           </strong>
           - Help others get along better to get work done.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Lawyers
           </strong>
           - Useyour charm and diplomacy to expertly argue a case.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Scorpio (born October 23 through November 21)
         </h2>
         <p>
          Scorpio is hard-working and highly motivated. The Scorpio isalso resourceful, intuitive, smart, and analytical. If you're aScorpio, you'llenjoy jobs in:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Espionage -
           </strong>
           Find information and stay out of troubleusing your intelligence and intuition.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Writing -
           </strong>
           Weave insightful stories of both fiction and non-fiction with your resourcefulness and intuition.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Physics
           </strong>
           - Make use of your analytical thinking and motivation.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Research
           </strong>
           - Showcase your analytical thinking and intellect.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Sagittarius (Born November 22 through December 21)
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Sagittarius has an upbeat attitude and is full of energy. If you're a Sagittarius, you also love to travel and have a strong spiritual side. Sagittariuscan benefit from careers in:
         </p>
         <br/>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Theology
           </strong>
           - Thrive in your spirituality.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Public relations
           </strong>
           - Make use of your positive attitude and energetic nature.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Sales
           </strong>
           - Show your energy and upbeat attitude, all while occasionally traveling for work.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Administration
           </strong>
           - Use your energy and enthusiasm to motivate others.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Do any of the four jobs for your sign look like something you'd like to pursue? Share your newwisdom about your future career in the comments below.
         </p>
