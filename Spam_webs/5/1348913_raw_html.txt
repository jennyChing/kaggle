        <p>
         Among the nastiest nightmares that could befall any young woman,
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/himalaya-acne-n-pimple-cream.html" target="_blank">
          pimples and acne
         </a>
         are the most common and the most dreaded of them all  taunting us since our fresh-faced teens, and continuing till we grow into self-assured adults, and sometimes, even after that. Appearing on the face, neck, chest, back and shoulders (pimples especially), these are problems afflicted upon not only those with oily skin, but also, at times, people with normal and dry skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Acne-and-pimples.jpg">
          <img alt="Acne and pimples" class=" wp-image-803 aligncenter" height="328" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Acne-and-pimples.jpg" width="334"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Acne and pimples start forming due to the clogging of facial pores by excessive sebum build-up, dirt and dead cells shed by the skin. The pores on the skin surface which allow it to breathe, are joined to sebaceous glands present in the epidermis, just below the surface. These secrete sebum, an oily lubricant matter, which keeps the skin naturally soft and moisturized. The pores release this on the skin along with the dead cells. Due to hormonal imbalance, there is an over-secretion by the glands, and the excessive sebum blocks the smooth passage of the pores. This basically shows up as a red spot on the skin, and when the dust and dead cells accumulate here, the bacteria start to multiply and swell up the skin, causing a pimple to form.
        </p>
        <p>
         To be honest, just watching your face in the mirror with a fiery red, swollen pimple on your forehead is a downer, and an instant killjoy. One can either grin and bear it and leave it alone, or attempt a few home remedies for acne and pimples for the pain to subside and regain peace as soon as possible. These are some of the ways you could use for quick treatment of pimples and reduction of acne.
        </p>
        <h2>
         7 Home Remedies for Acne and Pimples
        </h2>
        <h4>
         Brown Sugar Scrub
        </h4>
        <p>
         Exfoliating the skin is one of the prime steps to a thorough skincare and beauty regimen, to have healthy, radiant skin that is free of blemishes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Brown-Sugar-Scrub.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Sugar Scrub" class="size-full wp-image-792 aligncenter" height="355" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Brown-Sugar-Scrub.jpg" width="266"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Brown sugar is something natural and gentle you could use for exfoliating your face to remove dirt, dead cells, oil and impurities which cause pimples and in serious cases, acne.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Medicated Creams
        </h4>
        <p>
         If the pimples have been persistent over a long time, applying a medicated cream solely to the target areas could be the best way as far as quick
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/Health-Care/Face-Care/alite-anti-acne-gel.html" target="_blank">
          pimple home remedies
         </a>
         go, especially a cream that includes benzoyl peroxide and salicylic acid.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Alite-Anti-Acne-Gel.jpg">
          <img alt="Alite Anti Acne Gel" class="size-full wp-image-793 aligncenter" height="473" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Alite-Anti-Acne-Gel.jpg" width="347"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Crushing a few aspirin tablets (which contains salicylic acid) and adding a few drops of water will be an easy-to-make paste that you could apply overnight and rinse in the morning.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Detoxification
        </h4>
        <p>
         One could also try taking the detoxifying approach. Taking a glass of warm water mixed with a teaspoon each of lime juice and honey, on an empty stomach in the morning releases all the toxins produced in the body overnight and detoxifies the system.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/lime-juice-and-honey.jpg">
          <img alt="lime juice and honey" class="size-full wp-image-794 aligncenter" height="291" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/lime-juice-and-honey.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It helps balance the pH value of the skin, and thus helps banishing pimples and acne.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Apple Cider Vinegar
        </h4>
        <p>
         This is a sure-fire method in the field of acne home remedies. This is because the weakened acetic acid in the vinegar destroys the bacteria that are the cause of the acne infection itself. Also, it becomes alkaline to suit the pH value of the skin and restores balance after eradication of bacteria, which further makes it difficult for survival of bacteria.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Apple-Cider-Vinegar.jpg">
          <img alt="Apple Cider Vinegar" class="size-full wp-image-795 aligncenter" height="282" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Apple-Cider-Vinegar.jpg" width="425"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Also, like lemon juice, it is an astringent and helps stave off surplus oil. But it should also be considered to never carry the vinegar treatment too far and make the skin too dry, which could trigger a massive pimple outbreak should the sebaceous glands produce sebum in excess to cope up.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Aloe Vera
        </h4>
        <p>
         Aloe vera gel is used generally as an ingredient in curing acne, but using it on its own works well too. For this it is highly recommended to have a small aloe vera plant in your residence itself, or, with some research, a good pure gel that is available in stores, with minimal amount of additive preservatives.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Aloe-Vera.jpg">
          <img alt="Aloe Vera" class=" wp-image-796 aligncenter" height="254" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Aloe-Vera.jpg" width="377"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The aloe is soothing, as well as anti-inflammatory and anti-bacterial. Using the gel regularly will help reduce the pigmentation and swelling.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Banana Peels
        </h4>
        <p>
         The banana peels contain an orange xanthophyll lutein, which is a super-effective antioxidant that reduces the puffiness and redness, and promotes vigorous cell growth.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Banana-peel-for-acne.jpg">
          <img alt="Banana peel for acne" class="size-full wp-image-797 aligncenter" height="355" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Banana-peel-for-acne.jpg" width="416"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is one of the lesser-known home remedies for acne marks that can help diminish the irritation, prominence and overall pain of acne.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Baking Soda
        </h4>
        <p>
         This is arguable the best among home remedies to vanquish pimples as it is a quick method and works for sensitive skin as well, different from other home remedies for dark spots that could vary according to skin type and may cause roughness or dryness.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baking-Soda.jpg">
          <img alt="Baking Soda" class=" wp-image-798 aligncenter" height="211" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Baking-Soda.jpg" width="375"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         First, one needs to make a paste by adding a few water drops to the baking soda powder. Apply it on the pimples only, and leave it on till dry. Rinse with lukewarm water, and apply a suitable moisturizer gently. One must take care to not leave it on for longer than necessary as it may cause extreme dryness or irritation due to alkaline action.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Other Home Remedies
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Applying a dab of toothpaste on the pimple can work wonders overnight. If it stings, use one that is mint-free.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Toothpaste.jpg">
          <img alt="Toothpaste" class=" wp-image-800 aligncenter" height="232" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Toothpaste.jpg" width="351"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Take a shower after returning home from the gym.
         </li>
         <li>
          Have a balanced diet that includes abundant amount of water which helps to flush out toxins
         </li>
         <li>
          Never squeeze or try to burst the pimples, or blackheads.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Never-squeeze-or-try-to-burst-the-pimples.jpg">
          <img alt="Never squeeze or try to burst the pimples" class=" wp-image-801 aligncenter" height="324" src="https://www.diabeticpick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Never-squeeze-or-try-to-burst-the-pimples.jpg" width="269"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          This only triggers the sebaceous glands to overproduce, and also allows the bacteria to spread on the skin, causing more pimples. This also causes permanent marks on the skin, which persist even after the pimple has been cured.
         </li>
        </ul>
