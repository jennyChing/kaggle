        <p>
         We did it, the Pinhole Podcast has made it to double digits! To celebrate the pinhole crew does the thing they do best, talk lensless photography.
        </p>
        <p>
         For episode 10, we had the honor of interviewing
         <a href="http://www.kellyangood.co.uk/">
          Kelly Angood&lt;/&gt; from the
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.thepopuppinholecompany.com/">
          Pop-Up Pinhole Company
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.kellyangood.co.uk/">
          . Kelly made waves within photography circles and the social media circuit with her beautifully designed cardboard Pinhole Hasselblad. Since then she has taken leveraged her design expertise and love for cardboard creations and launched her own pinhole company.
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Join us as we discuss Kellys passion for film photography, experiences with crowdfunding, favorite pinhole photographers and the Pop-Up Pinhole Companys origin story. Along the way there is also mention of the Kellys next project. What is it you ask? youll just have to listen to find out.
        </p>
