        Jwoww Plastic Surgery (A Nose &amp; Boob Job) Before &amp; After Photos!
       </h1>

       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://plasticsurgerytalks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Jwoww-Plastic-Surgery-Before-After.jpg">
         <img alt="Jwoww Plastic Surgery Before &amp; After" class="aligncenter wp-image-2142 size-full" height="360" src="http://plasticsurgerytalks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Jwoww-Plastic-Surgery-Before-After.jpg" title="Jwoww Plastic Surgery Before &amp; After" width="570"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        If youre a fan of MTVs reality show, Jersey Shore, you may be curious about one of its stars, JWOWW, and whether or not shes had any work done. While we cant say for sure if many Jwoww
        <a href="http://plasticsurgerytalks.com/tara-reid-plastic-surgery-disaster/" target="_blank">
         plastic surgery
        </a>
        rumors (except one) are true or false, we can offer our own opinions about her looks and whether or not they have been tweaked by a plastic surgeon.
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
        Is JWoww Having Plastic Surgery?
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Jwowws real name is Jenni Farley, and shes been looking quite a bit different lately, mostly in terms of her face, which seems a lot tighter and shinier than it used to be. Sometimes, this type of firmness is triggered by Botox injections, which temporarily reduce or eliminate fine lines and which are also known for creating a shiny forehead look, especially in the portion of the forehead that is right above the nose.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        So, Botox may well be the reason she has a different facial look as of late. Her nose hasnt really changed very much, so we dont believe that rhinoplasty is the reason for her new face.
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Botulinum_toxin" target="_blank">
         Botox
        </a>
        is often performed by plastic surgeons, but its not bona fide plastic surgery, as it is an in-office beauty procedure.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        In other words, if Botox was all that Jwoww opted for, she would not be considered to have had plastic surgery at all. However, the reality TV star has confessed to having one major plastic surgery procedure in the past
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        JWoww Has Breast Implants
       </h3>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        JWoww has spoken about her own
        <a href="http://plasticsurgerytalks.com/category/boob-job/" target="_blank">
         breast implants
        </a>
        , so we do know for sure that shes had plastic surgery on this part of her body. Shes also said that she may have her breast implants redone after she has a child, in order to keep her perky new look at its best.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        So, her stunning figure is in part due to her genetics, her diet and her workout routines  however, a plastic surgeon is responsible for her sexy and full breasts look, which she shows off in a range of swimsuits and revealing outfits while filming Jersey Shore.
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        Are Breast Implants Right for You?
       </h3>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Breast implant operations are safer than ever before. However, they always carry risk factors, like all forms of plastic surgery do. Therefore, anyone who wants to get the JWoww look should think long and hard before signing on for this type of procedure, which typically costs thousands of dollars. Risk factors include hardening of implants, bleeding and bruising from surgery and implant punctures (which necessitate implant removals or replacements).
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Now that you know more about JWoww plastic surgery, youll be able to draw your own conclusions about whether or not this reality TV celeb has changed her face via the plastic surgeons knife, whether shes had Botox injections or whether shes done nothing at all in order to alter her pretty face.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        However, you will know for sure that her sexy breast look is not one hundred percent natural. Sometimes, this is useful knowledge, as comparing oneself to perfect celebrities are often a little daunting. Its important to know that these stars have a lot of help getting the Hollywood look and that they didnt start out looking the way that they do now.
       </p>
