        <p>
         We know youre stressed. We just know. Wed ask why, but if youre anything like us, we already know this too. Your email account is flooded with requests you dont have time to complete and your days are spent accommodating to your bosss every need. When you finally get to go home you find yourself heating up leftovers from earlier this week and making small talk with the family about how mediocre everyones day was. Before sitting down to relax in front of the TV, you do dishes and pick up after the kids  nothing to help that ever-present kink in your back.
        </p>
        <p>
         Finally, youre lying in bed thinking about life. You suppose youre happy enough, but somehow youre always tired and dont ever seem to have enough time to do it all. And to make matters worse, theres that one person in your life whom, no matter how nice they are, you cant help but secretly hate. Whether its your sister, neighbor, or that new receptionist at the office, she seems to have it all together. Her hair lays perfectly flat and smooth, her skin is flawless, and she probably never even chips a nail. But its not even just that. She seems really happy. Not happy enough, but just effortlessly, constantly happy. And its just not fair.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont tell her we told you, but we know her secret. She sets aside time for herself to put down the phone, stop thinking about the kids and go to the spa. And it works really darn well.
        </p>
        <p>
         Trust us, you too need a spa outing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-10972">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         And well even make it easy on you. Well take out the stressful part that entails reading reviews and checking out pictures, finding the best deal, and worrying about whether youre spending your money wisely.
        </p>
        <p>
         Just short of booking your appointment, weve already done all of the dirty work for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its your turn to sit back, relax, and let someone else take care of you. Well go first. After scouring the web for just what you need, we came up with these two UK spas:
        </p>
        <h2>
         Sequoia, The Grove, Hertfordshire
        </h2>
        <p>
         Nestling on Londons doorstep, The Grove is a popular choice for city dwellers to easily escape the smoke and rest in the countryside.
        </p>
        <p>
         Youre going to get a lot to pick from says their 20-page spa treatment menu. Theres the English Rose, inspired by The Groves walled garden and roses, or the Yummy Mummy, designed to target areas prone to stress and tension during pregnancy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Or if you want, you could even make it a couples outing since theres
         <a href="http://www.wahanda.com/" target="_blank" title="spa treatments">
          spa treatments
         </a>
         tailored for guys too. All About Him is designed with men in mind to relieve stress, dissolve knots and tension, and rejuvenate the body.
        </p>
        <p>
         With advanced facials, hot stones and Ayurvedic treatments all featuring natural products, you cant go wrong catering to your face, body, and mind at Sequoia.
        </p>
        <p>
         Given these extensive choices, its not going to be difficult to find something that will fit your mood. Unless your mood is to be close to water. Lots of water. In which case, you should really consider
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Spa, Chewton Glen, Hampshire
        </h2>
        <p>
         Nestling on the fringes of the New Forest, this spa offers considerable appeal for water-lovers. Among its many facilities are a hydrotherapy spa pool, outdoor whirlpool and aromatherapy saunas.
        </p>
        <p>
         Youre sure to be impressed not only with what this spa has to offer (more facial,
         <a href="http://www.wahanda.com/massage-deals/" target="_blank" title="Massage">
          massage
         </a>
         , and spapackages than you can imagine) but how they present themselves too. Top of the notch equipment and the best service around. Not to mention their incredible pool. Youve got to check it out.
        </p>
        <p>
         But regardless of all the amazing treatments and facilities they have to offer, the best thing about this place is without doubt the staff  they are all lovely and smiley, and make you feel as though nothing you could ask for could possibly be too much trouble. As such, whoever is responsible for their hiring policy deserves a great pat on the back
        </p>
        <p>
         The only thing missing? A second barman for after-dinner drinks.
        </p>
        <p>
         Room Tip: Pick a Croquet Lawn room if you like having lots of space!
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, there you have it: two amazingly luxurious spas to choose from. Now take a deep breath, book your stay, and let yourself start to relax. We promise you wont regret giving yourself a break. You deserve it.
        </p>
