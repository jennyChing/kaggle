      <h2>
       Furnace Maintenance Services in Annapolis
      </h2>
      <div class="entry">
       <div addthis:title="Furnace Maintenance Services in Annapolis " addthis:url="http://www.4fac.com/blog/hvac-maintenance-2/furnace-maintenance-annapolis/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">

        </a>
        <a class="addthis_button_tweet">

       </div>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <a href="http://www.4fac.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Furnace-Maintenance-Services-in-Annapolis.jpg">
         <img alt="Furnace Maintenance Services in Annapolis" class="alignright wp-image-775" height="331" src="http://www.4fac.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Furnace-Maintenance-Services-in-Annapolis-272x300.jpg" width="300"/>
        </a>
        Winter is upon us. This is the time of year when HVAC professionals tell homeowners they need to schedule their yearly
        <strong>
         Furnace Maintenance
        </strong>
        and homeowners write off these recommendations as a scam. We assure you, annual furnace maintenance is no scam.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        ENERGY STAR, a U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) program, advises homeowners to keep their cooling and heating systems at peak performance by having a contractor do annual pre-season check-ups. Energy.gov, meanwhile, preaches that in order to maintain your heating systems efficiency and ensure healthy indoor air quality, its critical to maintain the unit and its venting mechanism.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Proper maintenance extends the life of your furnace or boiler and saves you money.
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: left;">
        Winter is coming to Annapolis. Are You Ready?
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <em>
         The Farmers Almanac
        </em>
        and climate experts at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration are calling for another harsh winter here in Annapolis, Maryland. Are you ready? More importantly, is your furnace ready? If not, it could be a very, very long winter: long and cold.
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: left;">
        Furnace Maintenance Services in Annapolis
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Fresh Air Concepts offers furnace maintenance and installation services throughout Annapolis and most areas in central Maryland. We also offer several
        <a href="http://www.4fac.com/blog/hvac-maintenance-2/preventive-maintenance-from-your-baltimore-hvac-contractor/">
         maintenance contracts
        </a>
        geared towards extending the life of your HVAC system through meticulous inspections and annual maintenance.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.4fac.com/contact_us_maryland_md_heating_and_cooling_contractor_hybrid_heat_baltimore_howard_county_md.php">
          Click Here
         </a>
        </strong>
        to get started!
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: left;">
        Benefits of Annual Furnace Maintenance
       </h2>
       <ol style="text-align: left;">
        <li>
         <strong>
          Minimize Breakdowns:
         </strong>
         When your furnace breaks down in the middle of winter, you will literally be left out in the cold. Preventive maintenance can help minimize breakdowns and keep you warm and fuzzy all winter long.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Lower Energy Bills:
         </strong>
         Preventive furnace maintenance will help lower utility bills by 25-30%.
         <em>
          Ca-ching!
         </em>
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Extend Equipment Life:
         </strong>
         Regular maintenance will help extend the life of your equipment. After all, no one wants to pay for a brand new furnace.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          And More!
         </strong>
        </li>
       </ol>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Just think of it this way. You wouldnt drive your car year after year without having it services (or at least changing the oil), right? So why operate your furnace year after year without having it serviced by a professional HVAC technician?
