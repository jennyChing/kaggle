       <div class="txt step-body">
        <p>
         Cut a piece of perfboard so it covers all the pin headers on the arduino, make sure the holes in the perfboard line up with the holes in the headers.
         <br/>
         It should be about 45 by 52 mm, leaving you with a grid of 17 by 20 holes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Start assembling the circuit, I have added a picture showing how I built the circuit on my piece of perfboard.
         <br/>
         All the components are on top of the board
         <em>
          (the side without the solder rings)
         </em>
         , the blue lines are the connections on the underside of the board.
         <br/>
         If you are using a different transistor, make sure it has the same pinout as mine
         <em>
          (Base - Collector - Emitter)
         </em>
         , otherwise you will need to build the circuit differently.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are using the same LED strip that I am using, you can cut the strip into 6 smaller segments
         <em>
          (on the indicated lines)
         </em>
         , you can then add some wire between each segment, which makes it easier to position them.
        </p>

         <br/>
         http://www.sparkfun.com/products/10312
