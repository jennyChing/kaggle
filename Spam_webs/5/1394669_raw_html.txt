       <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         La prima volta che ho visto unillustrazione di Gemma Capdevila  stato su Pinterest, ho postato un suo lavoro nella mia board di illustrazione e lei lo ha commentato. Cos  iniziato larticolo che stai per leggere: un p di ricerca e qualchescambio di mail ed
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           ecco finalmente lintervista in esclusivaad una giovane illustratrice che merita di essere letta e osservata con molta attenzione.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span id="more-14300">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14305" height="547" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-1.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Queste fantastiche illustrazioni allacquarello e matite colorate hanno catturato subito la mia attenzione.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Un p perch sono sempre stata unamante del blu e un p perch lacquarello ha sempre un posto riservato nelle mie preferenze. Riesci ad immaginarecosa vuol dire scoprire unartista che unisce le mie passioni e che  anche disponibile a chiaccherare?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Gemma Capdevila  una giovane illustratrice (1988) freelance che vive e lavora in Spagna, perla precisione in Catalogna e per essere ancora pi precisi nella meravigliosa e soleggiata Barcellona, e crea illustrazioni per tutte le et assolutamente magiche e sognanti, romantiche e fortemente ispirate al mondo marino con una vera e propria passione per le sirene.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Intervista a Gemma Capdevila
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: Ciao Gemma, thanks for accepting the interview and being kind enough to answer my questions. Tell us something about yourself.
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         Im an children, young and adult illustrator. I have some published books and made several exhibitions. I love to go to concerts and draw what music makes me feel.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: Which media you use to create your illustrations?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         I normally work with watercolor, pencil, ink and collage.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14306" height="1004" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-2.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: You are a true lover of blue! Why you often use this color? It comes to an artistic choice or this color has a special meaning for you?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         Im not sure. Actually, I didnt realize I used it since somebody told me that he did recognise an illustration as mine because of the blue. I cannot remember the day I decided to use blue more than other colors. I bought a watercolor case, many years ago, and the first color to get finished was blue. Then, other colors were gone too, and some of them just stayed there. So I decided to replace these colors with blue. Now, I have 9 different blues in my case!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14308" height="481" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-4.jpg" width="750"/>
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14307" height="812" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-3.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: Your site is really full of fantastic illustrations, I personally can not choose my favorite. But what is the illustration that youre more connected?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         Im specially connect with the illustrations where a mermaid appears. She is like a mother that protects the ocean. But she can also be a furious best when somebody destroys what she loves. She guides sailors to the right port but can sink a transatlantic if she wants to.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: If you could choose between an infinite number of books to illustrate which would you choose and why?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         Some story about the sea, sailors, islands, monsters, lighthouses, mountains and clouds. Is there a book about it? I dont know, lot of good books have precious illustrations. Im not sure if I would make better a book that already have its images.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14309" height="1267" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-5.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: What are your points of reference? What artists or illustrators are a source of inspiration?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         There are so many of them! Im really in love with the art of a lot of illustrators. But probably, some of my favorites would be
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/2013/10/11/le-piccole-storie-shaun-tan/" target="_blank">
           Shaun Tan
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Zuzana Celej
          </strong>
         </span>
         ,
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Geninne
          </strong>
         </span>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/2013/11/20/guim-tio-zarraluki/" target="_blank">
           Guim Ti
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Patricia Metola
          </strong>
         </span>
         ,
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Elena Odriozola
          </strong>
         </span>
         ,
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Jorge Gonzlez
          </strong>
         </span>
          Its an infinite list of artists I love.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: I saw on your website that you have written a book for children, where did you find the inspiration to tell a childrens story?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         In children. I work with children and for children. I give art classes in a school and kids are my best inspiration. Things they think and draw, what they say or the believe in are amazing and beautiful.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14310" height="942" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-6.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: Do you prefer illustrate or write?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         Id like to do both things in my next projects. I have some ideas for new books. I wish I can see them published!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: What is your favorite bedtime story?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         One of my favorites books is Where the Wild Things Are, a classic one, by Maurice Sendak.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14312" height="595" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-8.jpg" width="750"/>
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14311" height="595" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-7.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: What are you working on now?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         I just finished a school book and now Im working on my art degree final project. Its a theatre book. And when Im done with it, Id like to write and illustrate my own stories.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           Q: I would like to close this interview with you some helpful advice: if you had to talk to a girl who wants to be an illustrator, what advice would you give to?
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           A:
          </strong>
         </span>
         Just believe you can get it and work hard for it. Sometimes we get amazing things because we didnt know they were impossible.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="Intervista a Gemma Capdevila" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14315" height="1192" src="http://www.inspirewetrust.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Gemma-Capdevila-11.jpg" width="750"/>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Ecco gli indirizzi dove puoi trovare tutte le opere di
