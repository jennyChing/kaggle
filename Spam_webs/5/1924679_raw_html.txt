				&lt;p&gt;This is a list of all the free to play MMO games featured in the MMO Worlds blog in 2012. All of the titles are listed in alphabetical order with no ranking.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Client-Based Free MMO Games 2012 List&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/allods"&gt;Allods Online&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; topnotch and very polished game design, full 3D graphics and tons of content make this MMORPG one of the best of 2012&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/elsword"&gt;Elsword&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; this game is a side-scrolling manga-style action MMO that plays almost like an arcade game with lots of button-mashing, combo attacks, cool moves, fast PvP auto-matches, etc.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/fiesta-online"&gt;Fiesta Online&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; one of the most successful free MMORPGs in the world, Fiesta Online is played by over 2 million people worldwide and is available in English, German, French and Spanish.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/flyff"&gt;Flyff&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a cool free to play 3D MMORPG with colorful graphics that focuses on flying.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/jadedynasty"&gt;Jade Dynasty&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; the world&#8217;s top martial arts MMORPG, Jade Dynasty is a 3D game set in a magical Asian fantasy world&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/LordoftheRingsOnline"&gt;Lord of the Rings Online&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; originally a pay to play game, LOTRO has top-of-the-line production values due to its big budget and years-long game development; if you want to play a game that has pay to play quality for free, LOTRO is the ideal MMORPG for you&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/LeagueofLegends"&gt;League of Legends&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; the DotA-like multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game that started it all&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/wizard101"&gt;Wizard 101&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; the best free to play MMO game for kids, tweens and teens; its fun for the whole family because even adults like this colorful Harry Potter-inspired magic school game&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/worldoftanks"&gt;World of Tanks&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a 3D MMO action game where players take control of a tank to fight strategic Pvp battles; its popularity is so great that it holds the Guinness World Record in the category of Most Players Online Simultaneously on one MMO Server&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;object width="300" height="250" classid="clsid:d27cdb6e-ae6d-11cf-96b8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0"&gt;&lt;param name="quality" value="high" /&gt;&lt;param name="flashvars" value="amfServer=http%3A%2F%2Famf.mmotraffic.com%2F%3Flang%3Den%26affiliate_%3D3324%26creative_%3D4600" /&gt;&lt;param name="menu" value="false" /&gt;&lt;param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always" /&gt;&lt;param name="allownetworking" value="external" /&gt;&lt;param name="src" value="http://static.mmotraffic.com/creative/widgets/Top5v2Static.swf?1326184352" /&gt;&lt;param name="pluginspage" value="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" /&gt;&lt;embed width="300" height="250" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" src="http://static.mmotraffic.com/creative/widgets/Top5v2Static.swf?1326184352" quality="high" flashvars="amfServer=http%3A%2F%2Famf.mmotraffic.com%2F%3Flang%3Den%26affiliate_%3D3324%26creative_%3D4600" menu="false" allowscriptaccess="always" allownetworking="external" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" /&gt;&lt;/object&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Browser-Based MMO Games 2012 List&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/aMysticalLand"&gt;A Mystical Land&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; one of the newest free to play browser MMORPG games&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/bgo"&gt;Battlestar Galactica Online&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a space combat MMO based on the hit science fiction TV series Battlestar Galactica&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/crystalsaga"&gt;Crystal Saga&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a full-featured 3D browser-based MMORPG game like Runescape&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/godfather"&gt;Godfather Five Familes&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; review coming soon&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/habbohotel"&gt;Habbo Hotel&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; the world&#8217;s top social networking game for tweens&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/Nadirim"&gt;Nadirim&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; an Arabian-themed fantasy browser MMORPG available in English, German, French, Spanish, Italian and Hungarian&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/pirate-galaxy"&gt;Pirate Galaxy&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; review coming soon&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/pockie-ninja"&gt;Pockie Ninja&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a Naruto vs Bleach anime RPG game&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/woozworld"&gt;Woozworld&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a fun and colorful virtual world where kids and teens can create their own communities&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://mmo-worlds.info/wgt"&gt;World Golf Tour&lt;/a&gt; &#8211; a realistic golf simulation game, this is one of 2012&#8217;s top sports MMO game&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Those are all of the top free to play MMO Games of 2012 that are featured in this blog. More games will be added as our writers try out more titles in the future.&lt;/p&gt;
