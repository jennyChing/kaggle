               <p>
                He is a famous Pakistani Nuclear Scientist and has helped build the Atomic bomb for Pakistan, was the person who made the country to come from behind and become a rival of the Indians that has been at war with the for the past four six decades. He had completed his PhD in metallurgical engineering from a foreign country and had laid out the plan to make the country get the nuclear capability that few have in these times.
               </p>
               <p>
                He had given the plan for the nuclear plant in 1973 and the weapon had been developed and tested in 1998 which was when the Indians had also done their tests. He is regarded as the founder of the centrifuge machine and the one who laid the foundation in the process of getting the various tool and the mechanics of the Nuclear energy and the supply that might be part of it as might be there.
               </p>
               <p>
                He had given the design of the nuclear development program that was incumbent for use by the different organization and agencies that were willing to see the capabilities to be part of them and their country. The Nuclear program of Pakistan is a source of extreme National Pride and has changed the shape of the country as a whole. The person Doctor Abdul Qadeer Khan has been declared a national hero because of his hard work that he had put into for the development of the nuclear project that he had put forth in the time to come.
               </p>
               <p>
                He was born in 1936 in Bhopal, India and was the son of a Muslim family which was in the country for just a decade more or so and at the partition the majority of the Muslims had migrated to Pakistan because of the partition that they were afflicted by. He had come to Pakistan with his family in 1947 and these migrants had to start off from new end and getting the education was very difficult at that time for the Muslims in Pakistan especially as it had been dominated by the working class Hindus and Sikhs at the time of partition.
               </p>
               <p>
                After studying at St Anthonys High School he had completed his Intermediate from DJ Science School which are now the leading institutions that educate the people of Pakistan in recent times. He had gotten his earlier degrees from the University of Karachi in Metallurgy and then later on travelled to the Netherlands for a degree in PhD from an institution that was recognized and he got the skills to become a Nuclear Scientist.
