     <h3>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Interjection">

      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">

      </span>
     </h3>
     <p>
      <strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">
       hmm
      </strong>
     </p>
     <ol>
      <li>
       Indicating
       <a href="/wiki/thinking" title="thinking">
        thinking
       </a>
       or
       <a href="/wiki/pondering" title="pondering">
        pondering
       </a>
       .
       <dl>
        <dd>
         <i>
          <b>
           Hmm
          </b>
          ... that's an interesting idea.
         </i>
        </dd>
        <dd>
         <i>
          <b>
           Hmm
          </b>
          ... I just can't decide between the two.
         </i>
        </dd>
       </dl>
      </li>
      <li>
       A demand for an answer to a question.
       <dl>
        <dd>
         <i>
          Just where were you until 2 a.m.? ...
          <b>
           Hmm
          </b>
          ?
         </i>
        </dd>
       </dl>
      </li>
     </ol>
