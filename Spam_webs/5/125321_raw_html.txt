                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://heymomimbored.blogspot.com/2012/12/santa-hat-brownies.html">
                      Santa Hat Brownies
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3214305689151792397" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Make these Santa Hat Brownies with the kids!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ZqkVikBN-Ys/UNML1ILzc0I/AAAAAAAABxM/u2sZ9Le_2Oo/s1600/Santa-Hat-Brownies2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="188" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ZqkVikBN-Ys/UNML1ILzc0I/AAAAAAAABxM/u2sZ9Le_2Oo/s200/Santa-Hat-Brownies2.jpg" width="200"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <ul id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_418" style="background-color: #fafafa; color: #323232; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', Helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; list-style-image: initial; list-style-position: initial; margin: 0px 0px 0px 38px; padding: 0px;">
                      <li id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_417" style="margin: 0px; padding: 4px 0px 0px;">
                       1 box brownie mix
                      </li>
                      <li id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_423" style="margin: 0px; padding: 4px 0px 0px;">
                       Fresh strawberries
                      </li>
                      <li id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_425" style="margin: 0px; padding: 4px 0px 0px;">
                       1 tub Cool Whip
                      </li>
                     </ul>
                     <div id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_429" style="background-color: #fafafa; color: #323232; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', Helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; padding: 0px;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_431" style="background-color: #fafafa; color: #323232; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', Helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; padding: 0px;">
                      Step 1: Mix and bake the brownie mix according to directions on the box. Let cool completely.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Step 2: While the brownies are cooling, wash the strawberries and cut the top of the strawberry off so it is flat.
                     </div>
                     <div id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_431" style="background-color: #fafafa; color: #323232; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', Helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; padding: 0px;">
                      <br/>
                      Step 3: Take a round cookie or biscuit cutter and cut the brownies into circles.
                     </div>
                     <div id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_480" style="background-color: #fafafa; color: #323232; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', Helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; padding: 0px;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div id="yui_3_7_3_1_1356008156125_480" style="background-color: #fafafa; color: #323232; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', Helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; padding: 0px;">
                      Step 4: Now take a small amount of Cool Whip or frosting and spread it on the top of the cut out brownie circles or mini muffins.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Step 5: Now place the strawberry upside down on top of the cut out brownie circles or mini brownies. Then put a tiny drop of Cool Whip or frosting on the tip of the strawberry to resemble Santas hat.
                     </div>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://heymomimbored.blogspot.com/2012/12/here-are-some-more-ideas-to-make.html">
                      Santa Fun!
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8716218486745010232" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Here are some great ideas to make your Christmas morning magical!
                     <br/>
                     <strong style="background-color: white; font-family: Verdana; font-size: 13px; line-height: 16px; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </strong>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-b_5whCw_MlU/UNMEhX6nOZI/AAAAAAAABw8/LT5mu27AjnE/s1600/vintage-santa-claus-with-red-background-and-stars-free-clip-art.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-b_5whCw_MlU/UNMEhX6nOZI/AAAAAAAABw8/LT5mu27AjnE/s320/vintage-santa-claus-with-red-background-and-stars-free-clip-art.jpg" width="260"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <strong style="background-color: white; font-family: Verdana; font-size: 13px; line-height: 16px; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </strong>
                     <strong style="background-color: white; font-family: Verdana; font-size: 13px; line-height: 16px; text-align: center;">
                      Image(s) courtesy
                      <a href="http://vintageholidaycrafts.com/" style="border: none; color: #416e90; text-decoration: initial;" target="_blank" title="vintage art">
                       VintageHolidayCrafts.com
                      </a>
                     </strong>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     1. Put gold bells around the yard and tell the kids that they fell off Santa's sleigh! How much fun would it be to have the kids running around the yard on Christmas morning picking up "jingle bells"?
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     2. Put out carrots and apples for the reindeer - leave a few pieces with reindeer chomp marks.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     3. We make reindeer food (glitter and uncooked oats) and sprinkle it on the front lawn. The oats are food for the tired and hungry reindeer and the glitter guides them to our house.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     4. Sneak outside and shake jingle bells. Just enough to get the kids thinking they hear Santa's sleigh.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     5.
                     <a href="http://reindeercam.com/">
                      http://reindeercam.com/
                     </a>
                     Is also an AWESOME site to go to!! It's a 24/hr web cam of Santa's back up reindeer, Mr &amp; Mrs Santa come to feed them every day at 11, 6, &amp; 9 EST.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     6. Take glitter (white glitter of course) and sprinkle it around the Christmas tree, over the presents and pets, around the cookies and milk and even sometimes into the bathroom showing Santa's magic!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     7. Are the kids driving you nuts? Have 'Santa' call them up and talk to them about better behavior. Have someone call your house,  ask to talk with the kids and tell them exactly what 'naughty' things they were doing, with details, and that if they worked hard they could still make the 'good' list. How convincing would that be?
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     8. Take some boots and dip them in the ashes from the fire place and leave boot tracks on the fire place.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     9. Leave a torn piece of Santa's hat stuck in the fireplace.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     10. Santa brings family gifts (Wii, etc). Kids get ... Something you want, something you need, something to wear, and something to read!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     11. Start a "santa trade" , have your child put 3-4 toys of his under the tree before Santa comes so that Santa is can take them to  kids in need, so that they can grow up knowing it truly is better to give than receive...
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     12. On Christmas morning leave a thank you note where those delicious cookies once were. Be sure to write it on stationary that hasn't been used before and disguise your hand writing!
                     <br/>

                      HOW DO THEY SAY "MERRY CHRISTMAS" IN....
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-9037524795573386897" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-yBO-slgJg1s/Tut3S1-tuxI/AAAAAAAABuA/D5StTdHE8QY/s1600/MERRY+CHRISTMAS.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="212" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-yBO-slgJg1s/Tut3S1-tuxI/AAAAAAAABuA/D5StTdHE8QY/s320/MERRY+CHRISTMAS.jpg" width="320"/>

                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Here's a great way to explore language with your kids. With Christmas just around the corner, think about how those that live in other countries wish people a Merry Christmas. Take it a step further and go to the library, find books on one of these countries and learn about their Christmas or other holiday traditions!

                     <a href="http://heymomimbored.blogspot.com/2011/12/paint-snowman.html">
                      PAINT A SNOWMAN!
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7180224341962768796" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     We all love to make snowmen but here is a way to make it extra special and it's sure to get your neighbors attention!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     SUPPLIES
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Snow (it's a must for this project)
                     <br/>
                     Squirt bottles
                     <br/>
                     Food coloring
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     WHAT TO DO
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Build a snowman- get the entire family involved and make it a big one!
                     <br/>
                     Get your squirt bottles and fill them with water, letting each child add food coloring to make their desired color
                     <br/>
                     Go out and paint your snowman! You can paint his face, eyes, clothing, hat, mittens. Let your imagination run wild.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     You could also do this with an igloo or any other type of snow creation you wish- go crazy! Have a great time!

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://heymomimbored.blogspot.com/2011/12/cookie-competition.html">
                      COOKIE COMPETITION
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8686456165153228625" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Here's a way to have a little fun with your friends and family!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Cookies are a part of the holiday season. Many of us bake cookies but here's a twist that isguaranteedto be fun!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <ul>
                      <li>
                       First- find at least 3 family members or friends that love to bake. Each "contestant" will bake their favorite holiday cookie recipe.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Next- Each team spends the day baking their favorite cookie recipes with their kids!
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Each team makes recipe cards to share with everyone that attends the competition
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Then- have a cookie bake off party.Invitefamily and friends! Feature each type of cookie that was baked by the participants.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Everyone that attends votes for their favorite cookie and the winning team gets a special prize!
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       Hand out extra cookies to guest along with recipe cards
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       MOST OF ALL HAVE FUN!
                      </li>
                     </ul>

                <h2 class="title">
                 Hey Mom I'm bored
                </h2>
                <div class="widget-content">
                 <span style="color: rgb(51, 204, 0); font-weight: bold;">
                  We have all heard this before...Your kids are looking for something new to do and you are at a loss. The purpose of this blog is to provide you with fun, easy, educational idea's that will be fun for both you and your kids.
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="color: rgb(51, 204, 0); font-weight: bold;">
                  After spending nearly 20 years in Early childhood education I have a lot of great idea's to share- I hope that you have fun!
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="color: rgb(51, 204, 0); font-weight: bold;">
                  If you have an idea to share please let me know!
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <span style="color: rgb(51, 204, 0); font-weight: bold;">
                  ENJOY!
                 </span>
