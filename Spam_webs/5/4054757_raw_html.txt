         <br/>
         <br/>
         Welcome to the Dermatology and Skin Cancer Institute, where we put your needs first.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The Dermatology and Skin Cancer Institute, with offices in Fort Washington and Lansdale, is focused on outstanding patient care. To achieve our goals for excellence, we have assembled the best team of skin care experts, including board-certified physicians and experienced staff, which include two physicians assistants, a licensed practical nurse, and highly trained aestheticians and medical assistants.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Our experience is combined with a desire to meet patients' needs using the latest technologies and therapies. Our specialization is the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of skin cancer and conditions that affect the skin, hair, and nails. Additionally, our patients may also visit our caring practitioners to address their aesthetic concerns such as fine lines and wrinkles, hyperpigmentation, unwanted hair, and more. Our modern, comfortable facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art devices that allow us to provide the utmost in dermatologic care.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         We work with the goal of providing each individual patient with personalized care that begins with a thorough consultation and assessment of immediate concerns. Our consultation and examination rooms are well appointed and well lit, allowing us to accurately identify skin problems and treat them accordingly.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Our method of treatment stands out in that we get to know our patients. Dermatologic care is not a "once and done" experience. By teaming with our expert dermatology practitioners, you can not only gain control over immediate concerns but also plan for ongoing skin health based on specific needs.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         At the Dermatology and Skin Cancer Institute in Montgomery County, we provide treatments that help our patients attain their desired appearance and maintain skin in an optimally healthy condition. In a single visit, patients can receive a skin cancer screening and consult with a highly trained physician regarding their dermatologic concerns to learn about the most effective treatment options available.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         We are proud to offer our patients the highest standard of care, which includes the latest treatment for skin cancer  Mohs Micrographic surgery. This procedure for the removal of skin cancer has a very high success rate and is suitable for many who have received a skin cancer diagnosis. Skin cancer is understandably a frightening condition to face. Our team provides expert care in a gentle and compassionate manner that puts our patients at ease.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Thank you for visiting our site. We are happy to answer your questions or schedule a convenient appointment in one of our welcoming offices. Call today to speak with one of our friendly staff.
