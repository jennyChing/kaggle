                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <b>
                        <u>
                         HOW TO MAKE MONEY In Your Business Store? THEN LIVE YOUR PASSION:
                        </u>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Now for me, my passion is teaching and sharing knowledge with others. Just what I am doing right now, and my Tune has always been : Wisdom Knowledge and Understanding, and soon as I got all 3 in life or on any subject I burn up with anxiety until I can teach and share it with someone else. For I live to see their face light up once they learn the truth and understand it like I did, and for me that feeling is my Heaven every time. And I do it over and over again, and I lose myself in time when I am teaching anyone about anything and never worry about anything else. And now I turned my passion into income to live on, not because I look for money. NO, but because I do what I am passionate about and the money flows in from the Universe through People, donations, lectures, books, blogs, articles etc...
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       So now that I have shared with you, now tell me what you love to do the most with your time. It could be listen to music, play with children, read, sow, do hair, shop, laugh, drive, type, decorate and on and on... And can you see dear friend in any of the things I just mentioned that if any was your passion, how money could flow from these things as you do what you love?. For there is a profession or business for each one of these subjects.
                      </span>

                        <u>
                         How To MAKE MONEY FAST... AND ENJOY SWEET SUCCESS With Your Business Store?.
                        </u>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Well The easy way to make money by being creative and smart, is to always give a dose, a sample, a first part for free. For everybody loves Free, and that is always the trap for all of us, even though we all know there is nothing for Free. Free, is the idea that we are in control, that we are getting over and that we are smart. Remember, a closed fist can not collect water - so give and it shall be given to you. Also, you do not have to reinvent the wheel, just go with what works. For look at how they give you treats at your local supermarket, and first month free from your Cable company, also first month 's rent free at your complex. See where I am going with this?...
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Additionally, to make money by being smart and creative is to create repeat customers. So give a card, a key chain, a mug, an air freshener - all with your name and information on them. Or if it is Online, collect an email address and send a coupon to use on next purchase. Remember,
                       <b>
                        first time customers make you money and repeat customers make you rich for years. And it is cheaper to keep a repeat customer than to advertise for a new one.Your Business Store Advocate, James Dazouloute
