          </h1>
          <h1 style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <span style="color: #3366ff;">
             <a href="http://www.poweredbyintuition.com/2013/07/06/intuition-is-intelligence-for-your-life/">
              <span style="color: #3366ff;">
               Intuition
              </span>
             </a>
            </span>
            Stories of Guidance for Your Life
           </span>
          </h1>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="color: #000080;">
            <strong>
             Everyone is intuitive.
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           Our
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            <a href="http://www.poweredbyintuition.com/2011/10/16/the-chasm-between-wishful-thinking/">
             <span style="color: #3366ff;">
              intuition is a primal sense
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           that has been with us since mankind stood upright. It is there to warn us about danger in our environment.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           Have you ever had the feeling someone was following you? This feeling is the intuitive knowing you get to warn you that you may be in danger. Feeling that someone is there you turn round and see them and then RUN which saves your life.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           Intuition does come through differently for everyone though and sometimes
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            <a href="http://www.poweredbyintuition.com/2011/07/17/intuition-and-logic-handling-conflicting-intuitive-responses/">
             <span style="color: #3366ff;">
              it can be confusing.
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           Read these three stories to get a sense of how intuitive insight shows up for these writers.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           The more you know about how intuition works the more likely you are to recognize it in your own life. And, the more you follow your intuition the more confident you will become in trusting it and
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            <a href="http://www.poweredbyintuition.com/2013/01/20/intuitive-guidance-8-tips-to-help-you-get-through-a-crisis-of-the-soul/">
             <span style="color: #3366ff;">
              the more successful you will be in your life.
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #000080;">
             Intuitive guidance for relationships:
            </span>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           My husband and I were having issues early in our marriage.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           We were butting heads and our arguments seemed to be getting worse.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           I had reached the point where I had had enough and was ready to walk.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           Just before I told my husband that I was done, something stopped me.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           I suddenly realized that I was following a pattern.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           I started to see how I had done this in every other relationship I had ever had.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           I remember the voice of my intuition saying to me quite firmly, Not this time.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Stay.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Learn something.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           You can always leave later.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           For now, stay.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           It was tough to stay but I did.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           For the first time in my life I realized that I had to work on myself.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           The problems in my life werent always someone elses fault.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           If I kept experiencing the same kinds of relationship issues, regardless of who I was with or where I lived, then I must be a big part of it.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Like Jon Kabat-Zinn says, Wherever you go, there you are.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           I put my relationship on hold while I saw a therapist and did some deep work on myself.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           The more I changed, the more my husband seemed to change into the person I had been looking for.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           Our marriage improved.
           <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
           </span>
           Since then, Ive never stopped looking deeper into myself, seeking opportunities for changing myself into the person Ive been looking for.
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           My intuition was right.
          </p>
