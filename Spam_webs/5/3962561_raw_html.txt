   <header class="entry-header">
    <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
     Adventuring in Oman  Wadi Shab, the Valley Less Travelled
    </h1>
    <p class="entry-meta">
     <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
      Written by
      <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/author/chad/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
       <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
        Chad
       </span>
      </a>
     </span>
     <span class="entry-categories">
      in
      <a href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/category/fine-living/travel/" rel="category tag">
       Travel
      </a>
     </span>
    </p>
   </header>
   <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
    <img alt="wadi-shab-in-oman04" class="post-image entry-image attachment-content-wide wp-post-image" height="683" itemprop="image" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman04.jpg" width="1024"/>
    <p>
     When it comes to visiting the Middle East, the first thing that comes to my mind is Egypt. Well, that or rioting, of course the two are kind ofsynonymousthese days. At least, thats how it was for me. I actually spent almost a month in Egypt and Jordan a few years ago, and through the protests and rioting were somewhat frightening, I found both paces rather incredible. The
     <a href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/getting-the-most-trip-to-great-pyramids/" title="Egypt Adventures  Getting the Most From Your Trip to The Great Pyramids">
      Great Pyramids of Giza
     </a>
     were a sight to behold, and Little Petra in Jordan? Amazing.
    </p>
    <p>
     I love nature, and as I wrote about recently, Ive become an avid climber over the past year. So what better place for my next adventure than Wadi Shab in Oman?
     <span id="more-56075">
     </span>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Wadi Shab Aint so Shabby
    </h2>
    <p>
     If youve never heard of Oman before, well suffice to say its an Arab state in southwest Asia on the southeast coast of the Arabian Peninsula, officially called theSultanateof Oman. If youre interested, the country has an incredible rich history, and is full of beautiful towns and historical sites. The British are especially fond of the place.Theyve had trading links with the country going back centuries  more than 350 years to be exact. Longer even longer than the United States has existed.
    </p>
    <p>
     But for today, Im going to focus on Wadi Shab, surely one of the most beautiful places in the world.
     <strong>
      Pro Tip:
     </strong>
     A Wadi is simply a valley thats dry most of the year, save for the rainy season.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: center;">
     <a name="imageclose-56076">
      <div class="lb-album">
       <a href="#image-56076">
        <img alt="the wadi shab valley" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-56080" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman05-e1368631265262.jpg"/>
        <span>
        </span>
       </a>
      </div>
     </a>
     <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-56076">
      <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-56076">
       <img alt="image-56076" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman05.jpg"/>
      </div>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     Not too shabby, eh? If youve got a taste for adventure, then Wadi Shab should be right up your valley. You probably wont be alone either;Oman has a population of just under 3 million people and about a third of them are expats from Britain, the United States,
     <strong>
      Canada
     </strong>
     , Australia, New Zealand and South Africa. I dont know about the other countries, but us Canadians love nature and adventure.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: center;">
     <a name="imageclose-56077">
      <div class="lb-album">
       <a href="#image-56077">
        <img alt="water in wadi shab" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-56076" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman-e1368631324226.jpg"/>
        <span>
        </span>
       </a>
      </div>
     </a>
     <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-56077">
      <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-56077">
       <img alt="image-56077" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman.jpg"/>
      </div>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     Im guessing the expats are enjoying more than just the adventuring in Oman. Wages are extremely high for those with a bit of talent. You know, the type of wages to help fund the type of life that allows them to bum around in awesome places like the photo above. Doesnt hurt that their banks are the way they are either a bit of a necessity given the high salaries on offer and zero personal taxation.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: center;">
     <em id="__mceDel">
      <a name="imageclose-56078">
       <div class="lb-album">
        <a href="#image-56078">
         <img alt="wadi shab oman" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-56077" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman02-e1368631353408.jpg"/>
         <span>
         </span>
        </a>
       </div>
      </a>
      <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-56078">
       <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-56078">
        <img alt="image-56078" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman02.jpg"/>
       </div>
      </a>
     </em>
    </p>
    <p>
     For a place thats supposed to be dry most of the year, it looks anything but. In fact, the water looks fantastic.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: center;">
     <a name="imageclose-56079">
      <div class="lb-album">
       <a href="#image-56079">
        <img alt="ocean of oman" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-56078" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman03-e1368631379204.jpg"/>
        <span>
        </span>
       </a>
      </div>
     </a>
     <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-56079">
      <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-56079">
       <img alt="image-56079" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/wadi-shab-in-oman03.jpg"/>
      </div>
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     So much climbing. You climb to the top, and then you can dive right off into that beautiful water just like the werewolves in Twilight. I mean, not that Ive watched that or anything. They also werent in Oman, but you know. Anyway
    </p>
    <h3>
     Oh Man, Its Time to Visit Oman
    </h3>
    <p>
     Dont worry, I never run out of terrible puns its a curse. But Ill let you off the hook for now, and instead that you run far, far away  perhaps right to Omans waiting arms, or cliffs.
    </p>
    <p>
     Im not going to lie, tickets to the Middle East are not inexpensive, but prices have been slightly depressed given the *ahem* state of affairs there. You can use that to your advantage and browse around, or go straight to the top with a travel network like
     <a href="http://www.virtuoso.com/home/" target="_blank">
      Virtuoso
     </a>
     . If youre into extreme luxury and have the cash for it, Ive heard theyre tops. In fact theyll be offering flights on Virgin Galactic one of these days, so perhaps youll be able to take a short jaunt into space before landing in Oman.
    </p>
