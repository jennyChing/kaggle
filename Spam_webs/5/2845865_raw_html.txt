or retweet their posts and collect points. If you want to be in your niche,
you can select your category from the categories list. Its really easy to
collect 5000 points in 10 minutes.

HASHTAGS for more exposure). Set the number of credits you want to give for a
single share (Google+ 1, FB like, Tweet). I usually prefer between 40-80
