        <p>
         When I first glimpsed todays wedding I spotted the brides Jenny Peckham dress and I knew right away that it was going to be a good wedding. I didnt realize just how amazing! A little retro, a pinch of whimsy, and a whole lotta awesome would be the best description. This couple looks like they can really throw a great party. I guess the brides profession as an interior designer means she has an eye for combining beautiful colors, hand-made touches, and mid-century accents to create a seamless and perfect Palm Springs mid-century wedding.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         Palm Springs Mid-Century Wedding at Casa Verona
        </h2>
        <div>
         <p>
          Comments from the bride: We wanted our wedding to have a very fun and relaxed feel, as that is how we are as a couple. We knew we didnt want anything traditional or too formal. It was important to us that the focus of the day be a celebration of love. We wanted toincorporate bright colors and playful touches that reflected us and our lifestyle. We both love music very much and it was a pivotal part of our early relationship, so it was a must have theme we had to incorporate. From the string trio, to the CD favors and even our table numbers tied into a system using old vinyl records and printed lyrics of our favorite songs that we listened to when we first started dating.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           <br/>
          </span>
          <img alt="Wooden welcome sign for wedding in with yellow and white writting" class="alignnone wp-image-51319 size-medium" height="488" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith923243461396O_low-325x488.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Sandwhich board with a Blown up version of a bride and grooms wedding invitation" class="alignnone wp-image-51320 size-medium" height="488" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith943243461694O_low-325x488.jpg" width="325"/>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           Where did you buy your wedding invitations?
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          My husband and I are both designers so we had a specific vision. We designed them and had them printed through Smartpress.com (which I highly recommend!)
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Wooden sign with a quote about stars aligning in white writting" class="alignnone wp-image-51310 size-full" height="434" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith473243446560O_low.jpg" width="650"/>
          <img alt="Dr seuss quote in white paint on a wooden sign " class="alignnone wp-image-51315 size-full" height="434" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith723243454040O_low.jpg" width="650"/>
          <img alt="Bridal bouquets of pink and coral peonies, ranunculus, king protea, pin cushion protea, white anemones, succulents." class="alignnone wp-image-51321 size-large" height="434" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith1013243464796O_low-650x434.jpg" width="650"/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           What types of flowers did you have?
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          My bouquet and the flowers for the tables had hot pink and coral peonies, peach ranunculus, king protea, orange pin cushion protea, white anemones, succulents, air plants and lemon leaf. The bridesmaids bouquets were a gathering of pink and white wildflowers and theboutonniereswere made from craspedia (Billy Balls).
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Groom wearing polka dot bow tie with a billy ball boutonniere " class="alignnone wp-image-51336 size-medium" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3723245415777O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Bride carrying a bouquet of pink and coral peonies, peach ranunculus, king protea, orange pin cushion protea, white anemones " class="alignnone wp-image-51342 size-medium" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4693245444343O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <br/>
          <img alt="groom wearing purple argyle socks " class="alignnone wp-image-51333 size-medium" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3143245394115O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Groomsmen standing on two separate balconies above each other" class="alignnone wp-image-51334 size-medium" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3553245409923O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           What was the best advice you received when you were planning your wedding?
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          The best advice I received was to try to take time to enjoy the process, rather than count down the days to your wedding, not lose sight of your vision (but also not get carried away), and to soak up as much of the day as possiblebecauseit goes by so quickly! My advice toother brides would be to not stress over the small things, because at the end of the day you will have married the love of your life and it doesnt get any better than that!!
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3583245412139O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51335" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3583245412139O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3983245423234O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51337" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith3983245423234O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <br/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4143245428739O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51338" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4143245428739O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4203245430936O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51340" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4203245430936O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <br/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4253245432706O_low" class="alignnone wp-image-51341 size-large" height="434" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4253245432706O_low-650x434.jpg" width="650"/>
          <br/>
          How many guests were invited and what was the final guest count?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We knew we wanted a relatively small wedding so that we wouldnt lose the intimacy of it. We invited 80 and had a final count of 75.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_ashleykeith9723424551589O_low" class="alignnone wp-image-51350 size-large" height="434" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_ashleykeith9723424551589O_low-650x434.jpg" width="650"/>
          <br/>
          Which pieces of music did you use during the ceremony?
         </h4>
         <p>
          We had a string trio to perform all of our ceremony music. During the beginning of the ceremony they played The Beatles 
          <em>
           All you Need is Love
          </em>
          and 
          <em>
           Here Comes the Sun
          </em>
          , The Postal Service 
          <em>
           Such Great Heights
          </em>
          , Elton John 
          <em>
           Your Song
          </em>
          , Iron and Wine
          <em>
           Flightless Bird,
          </em>
          and Bright Eyes
          <em>
           First Day of My Life
          </em>
          . The processional, brides entrance and interlude was all the same song, which was the string trio playing Arcade Fires 
          <em>
           Wake Up
          </em>
          and the recessional was the string trio to Black Eyes Peas
          <em>
           I Gotta Feeling
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith873243459451O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51317" height="488" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith873243459451O_low-325x488.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4763245446716O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51343" height="488" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith4763245446716O_low-325x488.jpg" width="325"/>
          <br/>
          What were the highlights from the ceremony and reception?
         </h4>
         <p>
          The biggest highlights to us were exchanging our vows that we wrote, saying 
          <em>
           I do
          </em>
          , dancing (definitely dancing) and the number one highlight would without a doubt be when we jumped into the pool both still fully dressed in our wedding attire  shoes and all. Afterwards all of our friends and family jumped in and we continued the dance party as a pool dance party. It was the most fun I have ever had!
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith5283245463183O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51345" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith5283245463183O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith5473245469432O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51346" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith5473245469432O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <br/>
          Describe the Brides Attire
         </h4>
         <p>
          My style was elegant with a touch of whimsy. I wore a Jenny Packham dress. The style was called
          <em>
           Aspen
          </em>
          . My shoes were strappy metallic with an art deco feel to them, by Vince Camuto. I kept my makeup simple and wore a side fishtail braid to give a slight bohemian touch.
          <br/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith7533245534383O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51348" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith7533245534383O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
          <img alt="Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith10603245651399O_low" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-51352" height="217" src="http://www.loveandlavender.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Carter_Asher_Shirock_Photography_AshleyKeith10603245651399O_low-325x217.jpg" width="325"/>
         </p>
         <h4>
          What did you give your bridesmaids as a gift?
         </h4>
         <p>
          I gave them each a bracelet and candle.
         </p>
         <h4>
          Describe the Bridesmaids Attire
         </h4>
         <p>
          The bridesmaids wore flowing, romantic, floor length gowns in shades of blush and gray by Anthropologies wedding line
          <a href="http://bit.ly/1tDySU1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://bit.ly/1tDySU1', 'BHLDN');">
           BHLDN
          </a>
          . For their shoes I just asked that they be gold and metallic.
         </p>
