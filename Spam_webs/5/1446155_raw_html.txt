        <h1 class="post-heading">
         Interview with Venchito Tampon on SEO
        </h1>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1. What is SEO?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can Google it or visit
         <strong>
          <a href="http://searchengineland.com/guide/what-is-seo" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://searchengineland.com/guide/what-is-seo', 'this resource page');" target="_blank" title="What is SEO?">
           this resource page
          </a>
         </strong>
         to learn more about SEO.
        </p>
        <p>
         But basically, its a compilation of processes that aims to increase ranking potential for every website (targeting to rank for higher spots in search results) in order to generate leads and sales for the company.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2. How long have you been doing SEO?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         2  years now.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3. Why is it important to always learn the latest techniques?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         The search landscape is constantly changing. Some of the techniques that you used three years before Penguin and Panda updates were released are not applicable anymore in todays search marketing campaign.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4. What is Link Building?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Again, do a google search for it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Link building is building links to a specific page/domain in order to rank higher in search results, bring in more traffic to the website and create a solid community of brand evangelists (followers in laymans term).
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5. What are some Link Building examples?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can check out my compilation of posts here (
         <strong>
          <a href="http://digitalphilippines.net/link-building-strategies/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://digitalphilippines.net/link-building-strategies/', 'link building strategies');" target="_blank" title="Link Building Strategies">
           link building strategies
          </a>
         </strong>
         ).
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          6. Who are your role models in the SEO industry?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Jason Acidre  simply by reading his SEO blog, you can learn new techniques in SEO that you can immediately implement to your campaigns.
         </li>
         <li>
          Jon Cooper  my former boss
         </li>
         <li>
          Ross Hudgens
         </li>
         <li>
          Matthew Barby
         </li>
         <li>
          Glen Dimaandal
         </li>
         <li>
          Sean Si  entrepreneurship
         </li>
         <li>
          Gary Viray
         </li>
         <li>
          Benj Arriola
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          7. Why do you love doing SEO?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is a challenging industry.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          8. What piece of advice would you give to young (starting) SEOs?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Learn and apply.
        </p>
