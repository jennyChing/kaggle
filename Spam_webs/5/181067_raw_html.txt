     <h2>
      <a href="/2012-zombie-proof-house-anyone">
       2012: Zombie Proof House Anyone?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p class="time">
      <span class="month">
       May
      </span>
      <span class="day">
       5
      </span>
      <span class="year">
       2011
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      In fear of a zombie apocalypse? Have no fear any longer, an architecture firm with name of
      <strong>
       KWK Promes
      </strong>
      has designed a home to protect its occupants from zombies
      <em>
       (and anything else that can be thrown at it, by the look of it)
      </em>
      . Take a look inside at the gallery of this architecture marvel.
     </p>
     <p>
      The summary these architects give this home is
      <em>
       "The most essential item for our clients was acquiring the feeling of maximum security"
      </em>
      ... and that is exactly what this home does -  with its
      <strong>
       thick slabs of concrete
      </strong>
      on every side.
      <strong>
       The house is located in Poland.
      </strong>
     </p>
