       <div class="entry-content content">
        <p>
         Now, were not talking about those ones where a dude glowers over the freeway dressed in a $150 suit with a dark dress shirt like hes Regis Philbin circa 1998. Those things are a dime a dozen. These billboards go the extra mile (Pun totally intended. Sue us.)to make lawyers look absurd.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1. The Super Honest One
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/7d50bf2cdd2092c4f3f042050c91b971.jpeg">
          <img alt="7d50bf2cdd2092c4f3f042050c91b971" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-41649" height="350" src="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/7d50bf2cdd2092c4f3f042050c91b971.jpeg" width="573"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Well? Who can argue with this as your marketing scheme? Who among us hasnt thought this at one time or another? If youre hating on this guy, youre just jealous.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2. The Dreadhead Dude One
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/BOA_2012_DavidKomieBillboard.jpg">
          <img alt="David Komie Billboard" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-41652" height="399" src="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/BOA_2012_DavidKomieBillboard.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you have a friend who got hurt at a Phish concert because he took the bad acid? Send them to this guy so they can compare sweet white dude dreads.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          3. The Low Expectations One
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/dogsbillboard.jpg">
          <img alt="dogsbillboard" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-41653" height="372" src="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/dogsbillboard.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You should hire this guy because his faithful companions (who are really never in need of legal services) trust him.That said, we guess wed rather hire a guy who is beloved by mans best friend rather than loathed.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          4. The Ball So Hard One
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/6a00d8341f704253ef01b8d0872b91970c.jpg">
          <img alt="6a00d8341f704253ef01b8d0872b91970c" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-41655" height="375" src="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/6a00d8341f704253ef01b8d0872b91970c.jpg" width="497"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         God, we wish there was a better picture of this one. Rumor has it that it was pulled down, which is a goddamn shame. They also apparently had a billboard with a little old lady waving some hardcore cash and saying Im Rich, Bitch, so these guys are pretty much our heroes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          5. The Super Sexxxxy One
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Chicago-Law-Firm-Presents-New-Divorce-Ad-Campaign-r8QElXPbTmrl.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicago+Law+Firm+Presents+New+Divorce+Ad+Campaign+r8QElXPbTmrl" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-41656" height="396" src="http://29odkrngwwiml6xqsb8nbfh.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Chicago-Law-Firm-Presents-New-Divorce-Ad-Campaign-r8QElXPbTmrl.jpg" width="594"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Were particularly fond of this billboard because its on wheels. Were also pretty into it because it sort of implies either that if you get a divorce youll be hella sexy or that the lawyers getting you said divorce are hella sexy. Maybe both.
        </p>
