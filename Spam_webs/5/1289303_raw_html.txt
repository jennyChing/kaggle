           <p>
            <img alt="sword" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43322" height="260" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/sword.jpg" title="sword" width="600"/>
            When it comes to real-life fighting, a sword is not the best weapon of choice. Sure, youll stumble upon a top-quality sword every now and then, but most swords these days are crappy for display only models that will shatter if you swing them to hard. On top of that, a real sword is much heavier than they make it look in the movies. Fortunately,
            <a href="http://egotvonline.com/2011/05/02/the-10-best-video-game-consoles/" target="_blank">
             video games
            </a>
            are nothing like real life. In
            <a href="http://egotvonline.com/2011/05/12/the-10-best-video-game-sidekicks-of-all-time/" target="_blank">
             video games
            </a>
            , swords reign supreme. In fact, some of the most formidable weapons in the history of gaming have been swords. Here are the 10 best video game swords of all time:
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Sephiroths Masamune Sword
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Final Fantasy series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="429" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/sephiroth-500x358.jpg" width="600"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Final    Fantasys biggest bad ass also has a big bad ass sword.  A  seven foot    katana would be a cool sword in anyones hands, but it just  so happens   to  be wielded by one of the best villains in RPG history.   Dont let   his  silly looking hair-do fool you, because this guy is no joke.   And his sword   does  all of his talking for him. Where does he put that sword when hes not using it, anyway? Does he have a separate garage for it?
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             The Sword of Altair
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Assassins Creed series
            </em>
            <br/>
            <img alt="sword of altair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43316" height="338" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/altair.jpg" title="sword of altair" width="600"/>
            What better sword to have on the list than the sword of one of gamings   best assassins.  The sword of Altair is one of the most powerful swords   in the Assassins Creed series and was used by both Altair (its name   sake) and Ezio in the renaissance.  The sword of Altair may be shorter   and not as flashy as other swords on this list but it gets the job done.    Just ask the last few guys it cut to pieces.
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Dantes Force Edge/Sparda
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Devil May Cry series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="dante devil may cry" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-43317" height="450" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/dante_1-600x450.jpg" title="dante devil may cry" width="600"/>
            In
            <em>
             Devil May Cry
            </em>
            , Dante gets to wield two awesome swords in one neat   package.  The Force Edge looks like a normal cool sword but once Dante   has all of the pieces of the amulet it turns into an awesome   scythe-esque sword.  Cool!  Plus, on lunge attacks it can turn into a   spear.  Kick Ass!  Dante looks like the keyboardist in an emo band.  Not   cool.
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             The Beam Katana
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             No More Heroes series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             <img alt="travis touchdown beam katana" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43318" height="337" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/5320823840_024e624627.jpg" title="travis touchdown beam katana" width="600"/>
            </em>
            Sure,  Travis Touchdowns weapon of choice is basically a light saber  rip off,  but its still a great weapon.  Travis Touchdown wields the  Blood Berry  beam katana, but they come in all shapes, sizes and colors.   Every  assassin needs a weapon, and Travis laser beam katana is as cool a weapon as your  going to  find.  Now comb your hair and get a real job, you vagabond  lay-a-bout!
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Keyblades
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Kingdom Heart series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="379" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/8137.png" width="600"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            In the Kingdom Heart series, Keyblades are keys. Theyre also swords, hence the name keyblades. And there are dozens of them, too. So many  keyblades, so little time!  There are too many awesome keyblade  designs  and powers to narrow it down to just one best keyblade, and the look and powers of  a  keyblade are really defined by the keychain attached to the it.  If  I  had to pick one keyblade to represent all of the keyblades in the
            <em>
             Kingdom  Hearts
            </em>
            series, it would be the Kingdom Key.  But thats Soras   keyblade.  And Sora is a punk so no.  Keyblades, in general, are cool.
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Light Sabers
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             KOTOR, Star Wars series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="448" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/kotor-5b-500x375.jpg" width="599"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The  OG bad ass Sci-Fi sword, the Light Saber, has had a big impact in video games.  From  the  Atari 2600 to the XBOX 360, Star wars games have been all over the   gamer-verse and the light saber is the main weapon of every Jedi in a   galaxy far, far away.  It doesnt matter whether its a single blue or   two-saber dual sided staff, the comforting Schoom sound effect of a light saber   powering up is as recognizable as it is awesome. If this was a list of the Best Swords in Pop Culture, the light saber would be an obvious one, but theres a little more competition in the world of video games. Also, what happens if you drop a light saber blade-side down? Does it just fall through the Earth?
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Energy Sword
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Halo series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/energysword3-500x375.jpg" width="601"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            When
            <em>
             Halo 2
            </em>
            came out, most multi-player games were  won and lost  based on the beginning mad dash for this sword.  With the  one hit kill  brutality and the fact that it never ran out of ammo,  this sword was a  force to be reckoned with.  If you dont have this mighty blade on your side, you  better pray  someone on your team grabbed that sniper rifle. Otherwise, youre probably toast.
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Buster Sword
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Final Fantasy series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             <img alt="final fantasy buster sword" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43319" height="450" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/final_fantasy_game-1961.jpg" title="final fantasy buster sword" width="600"/>
            </em>
            Just  look at that freakin sword!  It is definitely the most impressive sword based on   physical appearance, and its also the most iconic of all the swords in the
            <em>
             Final   Fantasy
            </em>
            series.  Sure, the Ultima is usually the best sword stat-wise,   and the Masamune Sword appears in all of the series installments BUT LOOK AT   THAT FING BUSTER SWORD!  ITS HUGE!
           </p>
           <h1>
            <strong>
             Master Sword
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <em>
             Legend of  Zelda series
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="480" src="http://egotvonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/master-sword-500x400.jpg" width="600"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            When  you think of video games, you think of
            <em>
             Legend of Zelda
            </em>
            .  So  naturally  when you think of video game swords, the master sword  instantly comes to mind.  But the greatest thing about the master sword is  the hunt.  You know  you have to save Zelda, and you know that you need  the master sword to do  it.  When you finally get that master sword in  Links hands and you  hear the swell of music that accompanies the find,  the sense of  accomplishment is one of the most satisfying feelings in  all of gaming, and that reward (as a result of the painstaking quest) is what propels the Master Sword to the top of this list.
