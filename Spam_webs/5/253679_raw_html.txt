            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Purin the Super Beagle  the Cutest Soccer Slick
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-above">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2014-06-14T15:14:51+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
              June 14, 2014

             <a href="http://wayoutofworld.com/category/entertainment/" rel="category tag">
              Entertainment
             </a>
            </div>

            <div class="essb_sidebar_start_scroll">

            <p>
             With Brazil exhaling, Croatia fuming and Netherlands hammering down Spain in FIFA World Cup 2014, the football fever is gripping the world, not even leaving our four legged friendbehind. Purin the Super Beagle is an amazing soccer marveland an all star athlete. She has good on-field awareness, cuts to the net in a jiffy and is a mean goalkeeper. She is already a huge star in Japan with her own
             <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/purin1124/videos">
              YouTube channel
             </a>
             and
             <a href="https://www.facebook.com/purinthesuperbeagle/info">
              Facebook Page
             </a>
             .
             <span id="more-174">
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             The eight year old beagleis in the limelight, mostly for her extraordinary ability to catch with her paws. Having mastered the football, she is onto various other activitieslike riding a skateboard or stopping a Samurai sword with her paw or balancing on a Yoga ball or jumping the rope or doing handstands (well in her case, paw-stands) as her celebration move.
            </p>
            <p>
             Keeping aside her canine status, she could definitely play in the World Cup or they could keep a day aside for Fido World Cup.
            </p>
            <p>
             Purin (English meaning  pudding), Tokyo resident has a long way to go with her incredibleskills. She iseven under contract to Stardog (Stardust), Animal talent agent in Tokyo, Japan as a talent dog. She is an international staras the
             <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2606352/Japanese-beagle-talent-skateboarding-piano-playing-samurai-defence-internet-hit-lands-TV-jobs.html">
              Daily Mail
             </a>
             quoted:
            </p>
            <p>
             Owner Makoto Kumagai has been inundated with showbiz requests from television companies hoping to feature Purins skills  so much so that the pooch now has her own agent.
            </p>
            <p>
             Mr Kumagai said: She has become famous all over the world and regularly have people asking her to be in an advert, on their TV show or compete in a talent contest.
            </p>
            <p>
             She is very talented and sweet and thoroughly entertains people.
