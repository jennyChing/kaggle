          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Have you ever pondered on what effect do the seasons have on you? Is it your likings and disliking, joys and happiness, moods and activities? Other than that have you noticed the fashion and trend change with the season? The fashion and trends come and go just as the seasons come and pass by. Fashion is something inevitable in this race of life. One has to go chasing the trends no matter he likes or dislikes. But yes there is a lot that you can have in your hands and that is called style. Why people are sad and depressed in winters ever thought about that? May be because of the colors and lesser activities simply because of staying indoors too much. Why you see people in elevated moods in spring and summers? It is always the color element that adds charm in your style. Its recommended and you will be indeed convinced once you read along that once the spring is over you should go for brighter colors rather than dull and boring ones!
          </p>

          <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
           Spring is all over; Go for brighter colors:
          </h3>
          <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_18169" style="width: 600px;">
           <a href="http://menfash.us/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Go-for-brighter-colors-1.jpg">
            <img alt="Go for brighter colors, men fashion outfits, men dressing tips, men style guide, " class="size-full wp-image-18169" height="400" src="http://menfash.us/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Go-for-brighter-colors-1.jpg" width="600"/>

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           As the summers are around the corner and like every summer its to go for some brighter colors. Summer has some special association of brighter shades. Its always the way that you get the colors play on you. This season you need to go through a transition phase of your wardrobe. Turn your dull shade collection into a brighter one. But yes wait a second! You do not need to discard your older collection. All that you need to have is a little sharpness and a sense of shades and combinations.
          </p>
          <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_18172" style="width: 600px;">
           <a href="http://menfash.us/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Go-for-brighter-colors-4_mini.jpg">

           </figcaption>

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Remember that having an appropriate trend combination is a blessing that is not given to everyone. So its time to gear you up for a colorful life. Are you up and ready for a change? Now you must be wondering what change a men wardrobe can go through. You can add
           <strong>
            some colored denim
           </strong>
           to your existing wardrobe. Or you can add some brightly
           <strong>
            colored shirts and Tshirts
           </strong>
           . Remember that bright shirt and jeans do not ever go along. You will look over loaded with colors if you commit this mistake.
          </p>
          <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           When talking of shades we can go shopping for some pinks, greens, blues may be orange and yellows (only if you have fair complexion). Purple is a good color to have in your wardrobe. Are you thinking of red? Yes! Red goes along with almost every season and person. Plain red is never too good especially when you are of the age that requires some decency and sophistication. For that if you are a red fan, do not crave for some red shades. Get yourself some red striped shirts or a red tie.
          </p>
          <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_18171" style="width: 600px;">
           <a href="http://menfash.us/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Go-for-brighter-colors-3_mini.jpg">

           </a>
           <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           So to all the dull people out there try to relieve yourself from the false myth of restricting yourself between the same old shades where even the temperature and mood change has nothing to do with the colors. You need to make a signature of yours. Trying out
           <strong>
            bold colors make
           </strong>
           you look confident and easy going. Other than the clothing shades you can experiment with colors in terms of eye shades, caps, scarfs or even cufflinks. Remember that going too bold with anything is never recommended. Take the change step by step and little by little. The world of colors is all open to you for experimentations. Just try them out!!
          </p>
