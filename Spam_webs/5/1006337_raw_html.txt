              </a>

              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/botrailonline/sets/72157655093068612">
               HERE
              </a>
              to view pictures from the first annual B&amp;O Locomotion event.

              <a href="/images/ofd_bike_tour_2015.pdf">
               HERE

              to view the registration form or access the link to register on-line.

       <h3>
        New Map, New Opportunities
       </h3>
       <div>
        <a href="/images/images/bota-maphendcountytrailenlarged.gif">
         <img alt="bota-maphendcountytrailenlarged" height="128" src="/images/images/bota-maphendcountytrailenlarged.gif" style="vertical-align: top;" width="312"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       Our map has been updated!  Click above to see enlarged image of the map.  New construction will start in the summer of 2015 at Green Street and westward.  More sections were purchased and cleared in 2014 as we try to close the gap between our two completed sections.  Sometimes progress can seem to be slow but it has certainly been steady.  New committees are being formed for 2015 and there are more opportunities than ever to get involved and help the B&amp;O Trail.  Contact us today!
