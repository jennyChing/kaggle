        <p>
         Today I went into polytech to meet up with my Linux assignment partner. We discussed what we had done so far, and looked at what needs to be done  before our presentation just over a week from now. My job is to experiment with some more features of
         <a href="http://www.cirt.net/nikto2" target="_blank" title="Nikto">
          Nikto
         </a>
         , and come up with reasons why it should be used. Ill also need to discuss some limitations so I dont sound biased in what I present. My assignment partner will be starting off the presentation, with me explaining the technical side of things, including a live demo (as opposed to screenshots of previous tests) if there is time. Well be taking turns with the Q and A session at the end. My assignment partner also did a really good job on the PowerPoint for the presentation.
        </p>
        <p>
         I also read more about the Blueskin Resilient Communities Trust via their websites  understanding the motivations behind their projects, and what they are aiming to achieve. One of the things I hope to discuss for the meeting tomorrow is how they plan to engage people into active participation  e.g. Taking measures to reduce energy usage (produced by fossil fuels). More information about the trust and associated projects can be found by visiting the following websites:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.brct.org.nz/" target="_blank" title="Blueskin Resilient Communities Trust (BRCT)">
           Blueskin Resilient Communities Trust (BRCT)
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.blueskinpower.co.nz/" target="_blank" title="Blueskin Energy Project  Blueskin Power">
           Blueskin Energy Project  Blueskin Power
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.transitiontowns.org.nz/waitati" target="_blank" title="Transition Towns  Waitati">
           Transition Towns  Waitati
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.blueskin.co.nz/" target="_blank" title="The Blog of Blueskin Media">
           The Blog of Blueskin Media
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://newzealand.wikia.com/wiki/Blueskin_News" target="_blank" title="Blueskin News">
           Blueskin News
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         As of now, for my Databases 3 assignment, I have only 3 more classes to test. These will be finished by tomorrow night, with work to be done on the interface-backend integrations. So far, testing has gone well  apart from the errors that kept reoccurring. Before I start the interface-backend integration, Ill need to re-factor the code I have done, and include some comments (as per industry standards)  so the integration process will be easier.
        </p>
        <p>
         To do tomorrow (9/10/2013)
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Drive out to Waitati School and have meeting with Scott Willis (Blueskin Resilient Communities Trust)
         </li>
         <li>
          Finish Databases 3 Entity classes testing, start refactoring and commenting code (yes, this should have been done from the beginning)
         </li>
         <li>
          Experiment more with Nikto , come up with good reasons to use it, and limitations
         </li>
         <li>
          Update work blog
         </li>
        </ul>
