         <h1 class="entry-title">
          The Magic Happens Outside Your Comfort Zone
         </h1>
         <div class="post-info">
          <span class="date published time" title="2013-07-02T06:58:05+00:00">
           July 2, 2013
          </span>
          By
          <span class="author vcard">
           <span class="fn">
            <a href="http://www.excitedbylife.com/blog/author/excitedbylife/" rel="author">
             Danny Sheehan
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="post-comments">
           <a href="http://www.excitedbylife.com/blog/magic-happens-outside-your-comfort-zone/#comments">
            <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="3535 https://www.excitedbylife.com/?p=3535">
             2 Comments
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <img alt="comfort-zone" height="480" src="http://www.excitedbylife.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/sites/3/2013/07/comfort-zone1.jpg" style="float: none;margin-left: auto;margin-right: auto" title="comfort-zone" width="580"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Its easy to be apathetic and lethargic and stay within our comfort zones. We get locked into a very narrow point of view of whats possible. But we cant expect to gain respect or help in life as wall flowers or observers as Life passes us by. Doing so stifles our creativity and limits our opportunities for growth. Life will never be exciting unless we treat it as an adventure and move out of our comfort zone and change our point of view. We must be bold, as the magic only happens outside our comfort zone.
          </p>
          <p>
           In the following TED talk Michael Johnson talks about the defining moment in his life when he moved out of his comfort zone to setup a business in China. Michael shows how to get comfortable outside your comfort zone. It just takes one breakthrough or insight and a commitment to action. Action, boldness, determination and courage are all that are required. Take the first step now, as so many opportunities are abandoned because we did not take action out of fear of embarrassment or failure.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="colorbox_video" href="//www.youtube.com/embed/231ji8caj0Y?wmode=transparent&amp;fs=1&amp;hl=en&amp;modestbranding=1&amp;iv_load_policy=3&amp;showsearch=0&amp;rel=1&amp;theme=dark">
            <img height="344" src="//img.youtube.com/vi/231ji8caj0Y/0.jpg" width="425"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The area outside your comfort zone is actually your growth zone, and your comfort zone is actually an area that gives a very false sense of security anyway. We should welcome frustration, setbacks and embrace failure. If you really want to be comfortable then get comfortable being outside your comfort zone.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <em>
             If you want to be really good at something, its going to involve relentlessly pushing past your comfort zone, along with frustration, struggle, setbacks and failures. ~Tony Schwartz
            </em>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <em>
             You gain strength, courage and confidence by every experience in which you really stop to look fear in the face. You are able to say to yourself, I have lived through this horror. I can take the next thing that comes along. You must do the thing you think you cannot do.
             <br/>
             Eleanor Roosevelt, You Learn by Living: Eleven Keys for a More Fulfilling Life
            </em>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <em>
             The opposite of love is not hate, its indifference. The opposite of art is not ugliness, its indifference. The opposite of faith is not heresy, its indifference. And the opposite of life is not death, its indifference ~Elie Wisel
            </em>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           We all live in the box called our comfort zone, but in reality it is a place in which we have become trapped. Outside the comfort zone is a whole new area of inspiration, adventure, interaction with others and possibility. So, be bold, step out and let the magic happen today towards an exciting life.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
