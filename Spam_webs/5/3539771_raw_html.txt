     <p>
      Welcome to Artlegarth Country Lodges.  Situated in the picturesque Eden Valley, in the quaint village of Ravenstonedale, Cumbria, you will discover our real log cabins nestled between the Lake District and Yorkshire Dales National Parks.
     </p>
     <p>
      There are three luxurious wood cabins for you to experience, thoughtfully positioned with exceptional space in 7 acres of wooded and landscaped private grounds. Artlegarth Lodges offer a true secluded countryside log cabin experience and yet is within close proximity of three great food pubs within the village, designated as an area of outstanding natural beauty and described by Wainwright as one of
     </p>
     <blockquote>
      <q>
       Westmorlands loveliest villages with the finest parish in the whole of Lakeland.
      </q>
     </blockquote>
     <p>
      Artlegarth Country Lodges lies at the foot of the Howgill and Wild Boar fells, providing the perfect base for exploring, outdoor pursuits or simply just enjoying the far reaching views from your cabins porch or hot tub. Each solid wood cabin is fully equipped with everything you need for your holiday home from home.
     </p>
     <p>
      From the comfort of your Lodge there is an abundance of wildlife for you to admire
     </p>
     <div class="frame">
      <img alt="Red Squirrel" src="img/artlegarth_redsquirrel.jpg"/>
     </div>
     <p>
      including our native red squirrels, rabbits and occasionally deer along with a vast array of visiting birds for you to enjoy all year round.
     </p>
    </div>
    <div class="span6">
     <p>
      You might choose to spend all your time relaxing, enjoying the peace and tranquility of the natural surroundings or taking a short stroll to the local pubs to enjoy a delicious meal and sample the local beers and wines.
     </p>
     <div class="frame">
      <img alt="Lots of walks around Artlegath" src="img/localarea/footpath.jpg"/>
     </div>
     <p>
      For guests of a more energetic persuasion, there is a wealth of countryside pursuits on the doorstep; scenic walks, fishing, golf, cycle routes, horse riding, bird watching, swimming and tennis courts. Day trippers will be in their element enjoying the scenery whilst exploring the National Parks, historic villages and market towns.
     </p>
     <div style="text-align:center">
      <img alt="Cabin" src="img/cabin.png"/>
     </div>
     <p>
      For those looking to venture a little further afield the border to the Scottish Highlands, cities of Liverpool and Manchester and both East and West coasts can all be reached within a 1.5 hour drive, making Artlegarth the perfect destination to explore this beautiful part of the United Kingdom.
