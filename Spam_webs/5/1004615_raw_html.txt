     <article class="post-2336 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-basic-crochet-stitches category-beginner-crochet-crochet-2 tag-basic-crochet tag-beginner-crochet tag-crochet tag-crochet-instructions tag-crochet-stitch tag-easy-crochet tag-learn-crochet tag-learn-to-crochet tag-treble-crochet">
      <header>
       <h1>
        HOW TO: crochet the treble crochet
       </h1>
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </header>
      <section class="entry fix">
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
          one of the basic crochet stitches
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Copy of Beginners (2) (10)" class="aligncenter wp-image-2291 size-full" height="1102" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Copy-of-Beginners-2-10.png" width="735"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Today Im going to go through all the necessary steps on how to crochet the Treble Crochet. This is one of the most basic crochet stitches &amp; it is also frequentlyused.
       </p>
       <p>
        As the namesuggests it is a little more complex than the
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/blog/beginners-guide-crochet-part-2/" title="The Beginners Guide to Crochet  Part 2">
         Single Crochet
        </a>
        &amp;
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/blog/crochet-double-crochet/">
         Double Crochet
        </a>
        . You can find an overview of all basic crochet stitches
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/basic-crochet-stitches/" title="Basic crochet stitches">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        The stitch is going to be explained in a step-by-step picture guide as well as a short soundless video.
       </p>
       <p>
        Just grab your hook &amp; yarn  lets start.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         Abbreviation:
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        The Treble Crochet is often shortened to tr or tc in crochet patterns.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         The steps:
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        1. Yarn over
       </p>
       <p>
        2. Yarn over
       </p>
       <p>
        3. Draw up a loop
       </p>
       <p>
        4. Yarn over
       </p>
       <p>
        5. Pull through 2 loops on hook
       </p>
       <p>
        6. Yarn over
       </p>
       <p>
        7. Pull through 2 loops on hook
       </p>
       <p>
        8. Yarn over
       </p>
       <p>
        9. Pull through all loops on hook
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         Starting:
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Im starting this guide strait off without any extra explanation on how to chain or start a row.
       </p>
       <p>
        However you should have a few chains before you start (read more about chains
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/blog/beginners-guide-crochet-part-1/" title="The Beginners Guide to Crochet  Part 1">
         here
        </a>
        ).
       </p>
       <p>
        In the pictures I have chained 10 stitches, turned &amp; crocheted single crochets across the chain row.
       </p>
       <p>
        I have also chained 3 stitches to get the height of the treblecrochet stitch.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         1. Yarn over
         <img alt="double crochet 1-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2323" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/double-crochet-1-400.png" width="400"/>
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Yarn over from back to front.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         2. Yarn over
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-2-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 2-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2367" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-2-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Yarn over from back to front.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         3. Draw up a loop
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-3-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 3-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2368" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-3-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Draw up a loop from next stitch.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        You now have 4 loops on the hook.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         4. Yarn over
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-4-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 4-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2370" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-4-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Yarn over from back to front.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         5. Pull through 2 loops on hook
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-5-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 5-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2371" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-5-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Pull yarn through 2 loops on the hook.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        You now have 3 loops on the hook.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         6. Yarn over
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-6-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 6-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2372" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-6-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Yarn over from back to front.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         7. Pull through 2 loops on hook
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-7-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 7-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2373" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-7-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Pull yarn through 2 loops on hook.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        You now have 2 loops on the hook.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         8. Yarn over
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-8-400-.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 8-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2374" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-8-400-.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Yarn over from back to front.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         9. Pull through all loops on hook
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-9-400.png">
         <img alt="treble crochet 9-400" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2375" height="266" src="http://joyofmotion.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/treble-crochet-9-400.png" width="400"/>
        </a>
        Pull yarn through both (all) loops on hook.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        You have now finished a Treble Crochet.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="color: #ec119a;">
         Video guide:
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/otfmdyVwDD4?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
         </iframe>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        The video has no sound, but is a great compliment to the written text &amp; video tutorial.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
       </p>
       <div class="woo-sc-hr">
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Hope you enjoy this tutorial!
       </p>
