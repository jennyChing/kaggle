           <p>
            Designer create impressive concepts that look really beautiful. The so-called Microsoft Sport
            <a href="http://itechfuture.com/concept-smartwatch-truly-intelligent-watch/">
             Smartwatch concept
            </a>
            is based on the existing rumors and comes with a really appealing design that could also allow it to be used as a
            <a href="http://itechfuture.com/concept-fitness-tracker/">
             fitness
            </a>
            band.
            <br/>
            As you can see for yourselves, the concept adopts a really slim design for the smartwatch in order to be in line with some leaks pointing to a fitness band-like form factor for the device.
            <br/>
            The Watch looks like it was taken straight out of a Halo game and it features a narrow portion with the display that seems landscape oriented, although that probably can change, like on Samsungs Gear Fit. Theres pins for wireless charging and an interesting enlarged circular area at the back of the device. The designer claims that the rounded and cool looking part holds the sensors and
            <a href="http://itechfuture.com/concept-superfine-touch-wireless-keyboard/">
             wireless technology
            </a>
            .
            <a href="http://itechfuture.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/microsoftsportsmartwatchconcept1490x306.jpg">

            <span id="more-15782">

           </p>
           <p>
            As usual, chances are that Microsofts smartwatch, if it ever gets the chance to see daylight, would come with a completely different design, but theres no doubt that Redmond would do itself a favor to adopt such an idea.
            <br/>
            Of course, theres nothing confirmed so far, but Microsofts smartwatch is rumored to debut in 2015, which means that the company wont say anything about it until the end of the year.
