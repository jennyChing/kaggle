                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Legendary Pre-Flood Civilizations
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-9115937406710428358" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: right;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-OLtodB2ZvcM/U1FS1E8aj7I/AAAAAAAAAxY/aIeNgeYaK54/s1600/hyperborea.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="202" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-OLtodB2ZvcM/U1FS1E8aj7I/AAAAAAAAAxY/aIeNgeYaK54/s1600/hyperborea.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      The legendary pre-flood civilizations like
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5528598" target="_blank">
                       Atlantis, Thule, and Hyperborea
                      </a>
                      were said to have been built on highly advanced science and technology, with a possibly direct connection with what is happening now around the world.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Sir Francis Bacon, a key leader in the Rosicrucians which later became known as the Illuminati, planned for America to become the New Atlantis and the center of the
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5495472" target="_blank">
                       New World Order
                      </a>
                      about a hundred years before America became a nation. To better understand the occult connections between antediluvian civilizations, such as Atlantis or Thule, and modern times, it becomes necessary to review the accounts of those who supposedly established thesecivilizations.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      In the pre-Flood world of Noah, it is
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5528598" target="_blank">
                       written
                      </a>
                      that there was interspecies breeding going on between the sons of God, or the Bnai Elohim and mortal human women. The ancient rabbinic scholars believed that the term sons of God specifically referred to fallen angels and this was in fact the teaching of the early church for several hundred years.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-6QwfP44aRU4/U1FYTdebDmI/AAAAAAAAAyE/CKO1RNk8PYQ/s1600/11atl.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-6QwfP44aRU4/U1FYTdebDmI/AAAAAAAAAyE/CKO1RNk8PYQ/s1600/11atl.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                      The offspring of these fallen angels procreating with human women wherecalled the Nephilim. Much of these storieshave been effectively suppressed for centuries and banned from inclusion in later versions of the Bible, but have been recently re-discovered; for example inpassages from
                      <em>
                       The Book of Enoch
                      </em>
                      .
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     There are
                     <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5508323" target="_blank">
                      numerous other
                     </a>
                     universal mythologies of pre-Flood civilizations that speak of these god men coming from the stars and seeding the human race.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     In addition, traditional theories of evolution are starting to be replaced by a "new" understanding that the humanity on Earth was likely seeded by beings from other worlds.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Anomalies which mayindicatehighly advanced pre-Flood civilizations include the monolithic monuments that survived the Flood which ended the Pleistocene era, as well as massive architectural wonders such as the pyramids,Sphinx and the Incan and Mayan monuments. Many of these structures could only have been built with math and coordination belonging to a high civilization, and technology that is superior tothat which wassaid to beavailable.
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-H9fCCfto4eM/U1FUN5PGRaI/AAAAAAAAAxk/UBpom2A2Zk4/s1600/1innerEarth.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="200" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-H9fCCfto4eM/U1FUN5PGRaI/AAAAAAAAAxk/UBpom2A2Zk4/s1600/1innerEarth.jpg" width="185"/>
                      </a>
                      One of the forces supposedly used by certain groups in these pre-Flood times was a mysterious life energy, that the Nazis re-discovered, called the Vril force. The Vril force, the energy fields studied by Nicola Tesla, and what is now called scalar technology are based on similar theories deriving fromquantum mechanics.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                      Scalar Technology is based on the theory that there is a fourth dimension where powerful energies for destruction or healing on a planetary level can be transferred into our three dimensional reality and change that reality.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      The Nazi concept of a Vril force is based on the idea that a race of god men came from the stars in ships powered by this force and settled on the legendary island of Thule where they founded the civilization called Hyperborea.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Plato believed in the
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5528598" target="_blank">
                       Egyptian legend of Atlantis
                      </a>
                      and Herodotus spoke of the another Egyptian legend of the continent of Hyperborea in the far north. When an ice age supposedly destroyed Hyperborea, this race of god men migrated south and it may be they who created the empire of Atlantis in the Atlantic.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ZpS8GYmvB9w/U1FWZRDlSUI/AAAAAAAAAx0/8ABwEVHwqsc/s1600/vsevolod-ivanov-the-grandchildren-of-perun-exodus-of-the-hyperboreans-2006-e1272273045806.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ZpS8GYmvB9w/U1FWZRDlSUI/AAAAAAAAAx0/8ABwEVHwqsc/s1600/vsevolod-ivanov-the-grandchildren-of-perun-exodus-of-the-hyperboreans-2006-e1272273045806.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      The
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5508323" target="_blank">
                       secret occult societies
                      </a>
                      that put Hitler into power were extremely interested in Antarctica beginning in the 1930s. The Vril and Thule societies diligently researched the ancient Tibetan, Indian, and Greek historic texts concerning pre-history. They were conducting the type of archeology that the
                      <em>
                       Indiana Jones
                      </em>
                      movies were based on.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Documenting the cultural, linguistic, and genetic similarities of these legends with the Nordic legends of blonde haired blue eyed "gods", the occult societies that influenced NAZI ideology came to theirconclusion that the Earth undergoes periodic global cataclysms, and that vast portions of the inner Earth is hollow and has been inhabited within for many millennia by another unknown branch of humanity.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Ultima Thule is supposed to have been the name of the capital city of the continent of Hyperborea, which was older than Atlantis. According to occult teachings, the Hyperboreans were the most highly technologically and socially advanced society that existed in the pre-Flood age.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-UzG8AEZPDzU/U1FZuUWIb8I/AAAAAAAAAyM/fyp5JspPnZg/s1600/1464000_612102245503326_1064593735_n.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="184" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-UzG8AEZPDzU/U1FZuUWIb8I/AAAAAAAAAyM/fyp5JspPnZg/s1600/1464000_612102245503326_1064593735_n.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                      When the Flood came the Hyperboreansused the large tunnels which penetrate through the Earths crustto permanentlysettle under the Himalayas.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      According tosome legends they named their new kingdom Agharta or Agharti and its capital city was Shamballah, which was themythical paradisebelow the surface of theEarth.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      There are many
                      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Robert-Sepehr/e/B00XTAB1YC/" target="_blank">
                       scientists and researchers
                      </a>
                      who believe that the founders of Hyberborea, Atlantis, Thule and other ancient civilizations werebeings from other planets.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      There are alsoa growing number of Biblical scholars who believe that these 'aliens' did not come from other planets, but other dimensions, and that they were actually the fallen angels who mated with human women who produced the Nephilim. The Nazis believed that theNordic-looking"gods"who came from the stars were a genetically distinctrace from another world, had RH negative blood type, and did not"evolve" from apes in Africa.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XWrZj-rP9_g/U1DN8TJnNJI/AAAAAAAAAwo/ZgZ-bMQUpns/s1600/1shadow.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-XWrZj-rP9_g/U1DN8TJnNJI/AAAAAAAAAwo/ZgZ-bMQUpns/s1600/1shadow.jpg" width="180"/>
                      </a>
                      The Nazis supposedly were aided by a group of attractive females with clairvoyant powers called the
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5508323" target="_blank">
                       Vril Maidens
                      </a>
                      who could channel scientific and technological information including rocket design and even how to build UFO-type flying machines.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      TheVrilMaidens were not only famous for theirgifted psychic ability, but forwearing their hair long and never cutting it.Theyclaimed that a planet around the Aldebaran starsystem was inhabited by a race of people similar to ourselves with a possible shared ancestry.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      The entire Nazi secret energy and propulsion program went hand in hand with their understanding ofalternative physicsand their occultscience of consciousness. Nazi scientists working on their most top secret programs relied on the occult Vril power, a form of usable free energy, genetic manipulation, and ancient occultmeditation techniques. Unfortunately, much of this technology was later used for mind control, both by the Nazi war machine and the allied powersthat defeated them.
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Werner von Braun, the founder of Americas NASA space program, was an eliteNazi scientist who had advanced technology on propulsion and rocketry.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      During
                      <a href="http://atlanteangardens.blogspot.com/2014/04/operation-paperclip.html" target="_blank">
                       Operation Paperclip
                      </a>
                      , when America, in competition with Russia over Nazi scientists after World War II, smuggled in over 8,000 scientists. These scientists brought with them their occult practices and beliefs, along with the science which the US further developed formind control (see
                      <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_MKUltra" target="_blank">
                       MKULTRA
                      </a>
                      ).
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-eF32uDARrbg/U0VshfEvMlI/AAAAAAAAAdA/PtthC5_yVC4/s1600/1tatitan.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-eF32uDARrbg/U0VshfEvMlI/AAAAAAAAAdA/PtthC5_yVC4/s1600/1tatitan.jpg" width="235"/>
                      </a>
                      In 1871, British novelist Edward Bulwer-Lytton, in The Coming Race, described a superior race called the Vril-ya, who lived underground andsustained themselves with the Vril force or psychokinetic energy. They may have been the descendants of the Hyperboreans, inhabitants of the island of Thule. The Hyperboreans supposedly harnessed the power of
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5508323" target="_blank">
                       Vril
                      </a>
                      to manipulatematter andenergy,enabling themextended lifespansand existing inwhat some might regard as agod-like existance.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      The German philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche (1844-1900) wrote of the bermensch (Superman) in his work, Der Antichrist. Nietzsche wrote Let us see ourselves for what we are. We are Hyperboreans. We know well enough how we are living off that track.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      When Nietzsche published The Will to Power he was speaking of the
                      <a href="https://www.createspace.com/5508323" target="_blank">
