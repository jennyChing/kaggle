      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <img alt="blogging e1308562004766 The Best Travel Blogs and Posts in June..." class="alignright size-full wp-image-24031" height="191" src="http://blog.hostelbookers.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/blogging-e1308562004766.jpg" title="The best travel bloggers " width="540"/>

         Written by
        <a href="https://plus.google.com/113386010413435472113?rel=author" target="_blank">
         Victoria Philpott
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <em>
       </em>
       The blogosphere is filled with travel blogs containing the rants and musings of people from all around the world. Some are set up to entertain friends and family while others evolve into candid authorities on where to go and what to do on your hols.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       The best blogs combine inspirational travel writing with unique images and fun stories. Theyre also beautifully laid out and easy to navigate.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       We love people who love travel, so
       <strong>
        every month were going to celebrate our favourite blogs to read and travel writing
       </strong>
       in the monthly blogger round up.
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span style="font-weight: normal;">
        Blog of the month
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/wildjunket" rel="nofollow">
        Nellie Huang
       </a>
       from
       <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/" rel="nofollow">
        wildjunket.com
       </a>
       would be the perfect travelling partner. Shes up for trying out adventure sports, likes a drink and has some incredible stories to tell to keep you busy on those long bus journeys.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Nellies originally from Singapore but has found her home in Spain. Shes been blogging for almost 4 years now and as well as her comprehensive and fun blog shes also been published in several magazines. When it comes to travelling she knows the best places to go and what to do while youre there.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <strong>
        Why we like it
       </strong>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <img alt="Huang 150x150 The Best Travel Blogs and Posts in June..." class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-24047" height="150" src="http://blog.hostelbookers.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Huang-150x150.jpg" title="Nellie Huang from WildJunket" width="150"/>
        She updates regularly
       </li>
       <li>
        Great layout for navigating and shes just published a post saying
        <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2011/06/20/brand-new-life-brand-new-look/" rel="nofollow">
         its due to get even better
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        Lots of
        <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2010/07/01/top-7-destinations-for-photography-enthusiasts/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         excellent photos
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        She doesnt just write about her experiences but also has some
        <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2009/03/19/top-10-spanish-swear-words/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         funny posts
        </a>
        in there too
       </li>
       <li>
        Insightful,
        <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2011/06/17/a-month-to-reflect-on-travel-love-and-life/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         personal blog posts
        </a>
        that help you get to know her, and really like her.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       If you need some inspiration to get your walking shoes on check out her post on the
       <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2011/05/27/7-of-the-most-famous-walking-trails-in-the-world/" rel="nofollow">
        most famous walking trails in the world
       </a>
       . Or if eating, or salivating over photos, is more your thing check out her piece on
       <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2010/11/15/photoblog-street-food-in-seoul/" rel="nofollow">
        street food in Seoul
       </a>
       . And, if youre the soppy sort, like me, check out
       <a href="http://www.wildjunket.com/2009/02/13/valentine%E2%80%99s-special/" rel="nofollow">
        how her husband proposed
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span style="font-weight: normal;">
        More good reads
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       <span style="font-weight: normal;">
        <img alt="Beth Randy in Iceland 150x150 The Best Travel Blogs and Posts in June..." class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-24179" height="150" src="http://blog.hostelbookers.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Beth-Randy-in-Iceland-150x150.jpg" title="Beth Randy in Iceland" width="150"/>
        <a href="http://beersandbeans.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Beersandbeans.com
        </a>
        is another favourite here at HostelBookers. Travel bloggers
        <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/BeersandBeans" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Randy and Beth
        </a>
        come together as the perfect team with their professional photography and blogging skills. They offer
        <a href="http://beersandbeans.com/2011/05/10/essential-dollar-store-pack-list/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         money-saving tips
        </a>
        as well as interesting and
        <a href="http://beersandbeans.com/2011/05/23/a-guide-to-the-galapagos-islands/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         intelligent guest posts
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://beersandbeans.com/photo-gallery/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         a brilliant photo gallery
        </a>
        .
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-weight: normal;">
        If youre looking for a round up of useful travel information in one place visit
        <a href="http://www.traveldudes.org/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Traveldudes.org
        </a>
        . Its written for travellers, by travellers, allowing you to join the online community and share your knowledge. You can start your blog on the site and scour other peoples to
        <a href="http://www.traveldudes.org/traveltips/south-america/uruguay" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         get insider information
        </a>
        on your chosen destination.
       </span>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span style="font-weight: normal;">
        Junes best articles
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <img alt="Tequila 150x150 The Best Travel Blogs and Posts in June..." class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-24040" height="150" src="http://blog.hostelbookers.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Tequila-150x150.jpg" title="Tequila shots on the blog " width="150"/>
       Its amazing what you can find on the internet  this week we have been mostly remembering nights of a few too many with this article on
       <a href="http://monkeybrewster.com/2011/06/08/historical-guide-real-mexican-tequila/" rel="nofollow">
        the history of tequila
       </a>
       . Read it and wow your fact-loving mates next time the salt and lemon come round.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Photography fans should check out these
       <a href="http://www.lovethesepics.com/2011/05/creepy-crusty-crumbling-illegal-tour-of-abandoned-six-flags-new-orleans-75-pics/" rel="nofollow">
        creepy/amazing pics of the ruined Six Flags Magic Mountain
       </a>
       in New Orleans. Looks like the perfect location for a creepy zombie movie to us
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       And
       <a href="http://www.everywhereist.com/sleeping-on-planes-a-pastime-for-deities/#more-4210" rel="nofollow">
        this article by the Everywhereist on the Zeus-like man
       </a>
       who managed to sleep his way through screaming babies, rumbling beverage carts and noisy speaker announcements made us laugh, a lot.
      </p>
