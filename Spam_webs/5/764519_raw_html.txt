                      <h1 class="entry-title">
                       Vacation versus Staycation for Spring Break and Family Day Weekend
                      </h1>
                      <div class="metabar">
                       <em>
                        <span class="sword">
                         By
                        </span>
                        <span class="author vcard">
                         <span class="fn">
                          <a href="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jody">

                   <div class="entry_content">
                    <p class="p1">
                     <a href="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Arizona-Grand_FamilyPool.jpg">
                      <img alt="Vacation versus Staycation for Spring Break and Family Day Weekend" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4659" height="433" src="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Arizona-Grand_FamilyPool.jpg" width="650"/>
                     </a>
                    </p>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      How far in advance do you make plans for spring break? We usually have a rough idea early in the New Year and book by mid-February (is that late?). This year, were Maui bound. While Im looking forward to tropical breezes and drinking from a coconut, Id be lying if I didnt admit Im a bit freaked out about the tumble the Canadian dollar has taken. Canadians travelling to the US will be paying 20% more than they did a few months ago. With that in mind, heres a smack down of staycation versus vacation destinations to consider in these last few weeks (months?) of winter.
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      Family Trip
                     </span>
                    </h3>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      <b>
                       Staycation:
                      </b>
                      Drumheller. The Royal Tyrrell Museum is world class and only a 90-minute drive away from Calgary. Spend the day at the museum, explore the hoodoos (Im amazed these arent yet fenced off and people can scramble over them) or climb to the top of the worlds largest T-Rex. Stay at either the Ramada or Super 8. Both have a pool and water slide. Or recreate Night at the Museum and
                      <a href="http://www.tyrrellmuseum.com/programs/camp_ins.htm" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        Camp In at Royal Tyrrell
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      .
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4660" style="width: 660px">
                     <a href="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Boogey-boarding-Maui.jpg">
                      <img alt="That's my girl on Polo Beach in Wailea, Maui." class="size-full wp-image-4660" height="432" src="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Boogey-boarding-Maui.jpg" width="650"/>
                     </a>
                     <p class="wp-caption-text">
                      Thats my girl on Polo Beach in Wailea, Maui.
                     </p>
                    </div>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      <b>
                       Vacation:
                      </b>
                      <a href="http://www.gohawaii.com/en/maui/" target="_blank">
                       Maui
                      </a>
                      , because Ill be there. In all seriousness, its easy, safe and youre pretty much guaranteed good weather. You know youre not going to get sick from drinking the water and everyone speaks English. Go hiking,
                      <a href="http://www.rappelmaui.com/" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        rappel down a waterfall
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      or hole up on the beach if fly and flop is more your style.
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      Couples Dirty Weekend
                     </span>
                    </h3>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      <b>
                       Staycation:
                      </b>
                      Sneak away to
                      <a href="http://www.germaincalgary.com/en/home" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        Hotel Le Germain
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      for some sophisticated sexy time. With Charcut and Sante spa under the same roof, you dont even have to leave. If you want to venture out, stroll around the river paths, work out at the Eau Claire Y or shop
                      <a href="http://www.coreshopping.ca/">
                       <span class="s2">
                        The Core
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      .
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <a href="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Le-Germain-Calgary.jpg">
                      <img alt="Le Germain lobby" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4658" height="320" src="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Le-Germain-Calgary.jpg" width="660"/>
                     </a>
                    </p>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      <b>
                       Vacation:
                      </b>
                      You cant go wrong heading someplace warm (where you wear minimal clothing). Cabo has daily direct flights from Calgary, so a Thursday to Sunday night stay is totally do-able. You can go high end (
                      <a href="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/hotel-el-ganzo/" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        El Ganzo
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      is the latest celeb spot), stay at an all-inclusive or rent a one-bedroom condo on VBRO sub $100/night. Since the hurricane, there are deals to be had. Party at Squid Row, take a boat tour to see The Arches or just play in the sand while the whales breach before your eyes.
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      Girlfriend Getaway
                     </span>
                    </h3>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      <b>
                       Staycation
                      </b>
                      : Ladies, make a beeline for the
                      <a href="http://www.solararesort.ca/">
                       <span class="s2">
                        Solara Resort and Spa
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      in Canmore. You can spend the day at
                      <a href="http://www.onewellnessandspa.com/">
                       <span class="s2">
                        One Wellness Spa
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      , where the services go beyond your basic facial or massage. With their Pathways treatments, you chose a focus like joy or sleep, (whatever you want more of in your life), and they incorporate breath and visualization techniques into your treatment. Canmore boasts a vibrant restaurant scene and a charming main street laced with independent shops (Indigo Bay is my fave). For an easy, fun workout, hit up the Nordic Centre, where you can rent cross country skis or cruise down their toboggan hill.
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_4661" style="width: 660px">
                     <a href="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Usery-Regional-Park.jpg">
                      <img alt="hiking Phoenix" class="size-full wp-image-4661" height="500" src="http://www.jodyrobbins.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Usery-Regional-Park.jpg" width="650"/>
                     </a>
                     <p class="wp-caption-text">
                      Hike up to the Wind Cave Tunnel at Usery Mountain Regional Park, outside of Phoenix.
                     </p>
                    </div>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      <b>
                       Vacation
                      </b>
                      : Greater Phoenix area. The shopping in and around Phoenix is superb, as are the spas.
                      <a href="http://fuchsiaspa.com/" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        Fuchsia
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      is a lovely day spa in Mesa thats totally affordable yet pampering. Scenic desert hiking trails are abundant in
                      <a href="http://www.maricopa.gov/parks/usery/" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        Usery MountainRegional Park
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      and theres occasional yoga classes at their information center. Even if youre not a health nut, its worth a visit to
                      <a href="http://www.swherb.com/" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        Southwest Herb
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      , where you can try your hand at tincture making, or just get great recommendations to cure whatever ails you. Flower Pot and True Food are my first choices for restaurants, but check out my piece for the Calgary Herald on the
                      <a href="http://calgaryherald.com/life/food/7-spots-to-eat-at-now-in-phoenix" target="_blank">
                       <span class="s2">
                        7 dining experiences you dont want to miss
                       </span>
                      </a>
                      .
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <p class="p1">
                     <span class="s1">
                      Do you have plans for spring break? Where are you heading this year?
