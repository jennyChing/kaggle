     <div class="story-body" itemprop="articleBody">
      <p class="">
       Take yourself back to your high school chemistry class. Now think about the storage room, full of all those boxes and bottles of chemicals. What were they all? What havoc could they wreak? How can you get your hands on some now?
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Assuming your chemistry teacher wasn't Walter White, those questions have probably remained unanswered after all these years. You've grown up to find that it's not so easy to buy carbon disulfide, ammonium dichromate, mercuric sulphocyanate, or any of those other fun-sounding chemicals.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Luckily for all of us, there's a Vine account called
       <a href="https://vine.co/learn" target="_blank">
        Chemical Reactions
       </a>
       that gathers up all the coolest experiments that scientists do, sets them to charming classical music and dishes them out so you can enjoy science safely from the comfort of your mobile device of choice.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Now you can pretend to be a scientist too.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded playing" frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/M07mmKtmeAO/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       We'll start out with a crowd favorite: some ferrofluid, live in action. Those spikes sure look pointy.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MPY9lTw3pel/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       This seems like it would really hurt, but it all turns out O.K. in the end.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MTFHx93YP9X/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       This is actually the most environmentally friendly way to dispose of packing peanuts.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MjHYenXWiWX/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Solving some serious science problems!
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MwH9nXbMhWD/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Can't you buy these things at Spencer's?
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MwzzJtLU3KQ/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Top that, dragons.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MHJAZim5ZMY/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       It seems like no one should be combining these things. Like, ever.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MXY2zg9dLjr/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       This makes no sense.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/M6hw0ndtPpK/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       This is how they make ketchup and mustard flavored ice cream!
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MrEi3x5EWlI/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       I'm thirsty, but not that thirsty. Give me half a bubble please.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MnE6OBYQI5w/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       All science is just magic, there's no other explanation for this monstrosity.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       <iframe class="vine-embed loaded " frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/MpWHlXiuWhD/embed/simple" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Shoot fire like it's water  that's always a good idea.
      </p>
      <p class="">
       Phew, glad we got the science bug out of our system! And please, do not try these at home.
