              </h3>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-4zGOCTSdYQo/VQV89S30yOI/AAAAAAAACkc/kVBAgyRohFg/s1600/1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                 </a>
                </div>
               </h3>
              </div>
              <div>
               <h3 data-diply="2" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: border-box; color: #333333; font-family: Roboto, sans-serif; font-size: 24px; font-weight: 500; margin: 12px 0px;">

               </h3>
              </div>

               </h3>
              </div>
