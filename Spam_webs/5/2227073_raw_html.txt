          <p>
           Heres a new digital medical kit which is going to revolutionise the way parents assess regular childhood illnesses. Its called the Clinicloud and soon every household is going to have one.
           <span id="more-4130">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           So smart, the Clinicloud medical kit combines a digital stethoscope and no touch thermometer that are both activated via an app on your phone.
          </p>
          <p>
           The stethoscopeis a small disk shape that attaches to your phone. It sends a sound recording directly to your phone and creates a medical history. in the Clinicloud app. Parents can actually use the sound recording to identify the severity of asthma and chest infections, and/or take the information to their local doctor or use it for a remote consultation.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="450" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/J8DfPZt9ESI" width="800">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           The no-touch thermometer sends its reading to the app via bluetooth. Theres a guiding light to help you locate the optimum position to take a reading. Because its no touch you can take readings while your baby is asleep.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Clinicloud app also records heart beat and respiratory rates to give you an overall picture of your baby or childs heath (or your own).
          </p>
          <p>
           The Clinicloud is available for pre-order
