      <h1>
       2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept is the Unique Collaboration
      </h1>

      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanjayacars.com/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-is-the-unique-collaboration.html/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-front-angle-photo" rel="attachment wp-att-10300">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10300" height="430" src="http://www.sanjayacars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-BMW-Zagato-Coupe-Concept-Front-Angle-Photo.jpg" title="2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept-Front Angle Photo" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Milanese coachbuilder Zagato team up with BMW unveiled the 2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept unique collaboration at the 2012 Concorso dEleganza Villa dEste. The body of the BMW Zagato Coupe has been built entirely by hand. As in the past, the new skin was tailored to fit the cars mechanical architecture, tracing its lines yet giving it space to breathe. The Zagato experts spent many hours crafting the aluminium sheet metal by hand and meticulously moulding it to give the car its unique form.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanjayacars.com/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-is-the-unique-collaboration.html/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-front-photo" rel="attachment wp-att-10307">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10307" height="433" src="http://www.sanjayacars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-BMW-Zagato-Coupe-Concept-Front-Photo.jpg" title="2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept-Front Photo" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanjayacars.com/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-is-the-unique-collaboration.html/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-top-photo" rel="attachment wp-att-10302">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10302" height="433" src="http://www.sanjayacars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-BMW-Zagato-Coupe-Concept-Top-Photo.jpg" title="2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept-Top Photo" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The new BMW Zagato Coupe comes through in the design of the car, hallmark design cues from both companies merging to form an emotion-laden coup in the best Zagato tradition. The brief for the car was clear from the outset: the BMW Zagato Coupe was to be a Vmax concept, a road-registered, aerodynamically optimised machine capable of achieving high speeds yet at the same time meeting all legal requirements and crash-related stipulations  a car that is ready and eager to be driven. The extremely long bonnet, greenhouse set well back and double bubble roof and Kamm Tail sum up the dynamic focus of the car as only a coup could. The flowing roofline melts away into the compact, muscular rear, where the cars power is transferred to the road. Surfaces moulded with eye-catching depth underline the sporting statement of the BMW Zagato Coupe.
      </p>
      <p>
       The side air vents reference the form of the bonnet intakes and extend their dynamic agenda to the cars flanks. Below the side vents, the silver z provides another nod to the cooperation between BMW and Zagato. Slightly offset behind the silver z, a matching indentation provides greater depth and three-dimensionality. The two zs in the air intakes and BMW badges are the only gleaming chrome elements on the BMW Zagato Coupe.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanjayacars.com/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-is-the-unique-collaboration.html/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-side-photo" rel="attachment wp-att-10303">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10303" height="425" src="http://www.sanjayacars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-BMW-Zagato-Coupe-Concept-Side-Photo.jpg" title="2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept-Side Photo" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanjayacars.com/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-is-the-unique-collaboration.html/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-wheels-photo" rel="attachment wp-att-10308">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10308" height="429" src="http://www.sanjayacars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-BMW-Zagato-Coupe-Concept-Wheels-Photo.jpg" title="2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept-Wheels Photo" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The BMW Zagato Coupe gets 19-inch light-alloy wheels in classically sporty five-spoke design have a hint of propeller about them, offering a subtle nod to the origins of both companies. A very special highlight of the BMW Zagato Coupe is its paintwork. The exclusive exterior paint finish Rosso Vivace an expressive shade of red  brings the surfaces and forms of the BMW Zagato Coupe to life. Depending on how the light hits the body, the colour spectrum ranges from something close to black all the way to a brilliant red. The primer coat made up of a black application, followed by a layer of shimmering metallic silver is applied first. Then come six ultra-thin coats of the exclusively developed red shade to deliver this unique effect. Finally, two layers of clear coat seal in the colour.
      </p>
      <p>
       Interior the BMW Zagato Coupe comes with horizontal lines in the instrument panel and doors, heighten the interiors sense of sporting elegance. The lines culminate in a dynamic upward curve for extra sporting effect. The base colours for the exclusive leather trim are a light and dark shade of grey. Red decorative stitching in the instrument panel, doors and seats brings the colour of the body into the interior and adds extra sporting touches. A z embroidered into the seats is the clearest reference in the interior to the origins of the BMW Zagato Coupe. Two exclusive accessories a hat bag and a travel bag set the seal on Zagatos colour and materials upgrade. The travel bag also recreates the form of the double-bubble roof and is designed to maintain its bulbous lines even when empty.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sanjayacars.com/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-is-the-unique-collaboration.html/2012-bmw-zagato-coupe-concept-interior-photo" rel="attachment wp-att-10305">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10305" height="426" src="http://www.sanjayacars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2012-BMW-Zagato-Coupe-Concept-Interior-Photo.jpg" title="2012 BMW Zagato Coupe Concept-Interior Photo" width="600"/>

      </p>
