        <h1 class="pagetitle">
         Why Hiring an HVAC Specialist Is the Right Decision
        </h1>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.maxmechanical.com/files/air-conditioner-contractor.jpg">
          <img alt="Air Conditioner Contractor Dallas" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-2333" height="450" src="http://www.maxmechanical.com/files/air-conditioner-contractor-300x450.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         As a homeowner in Dallas, youre rightfully proud of your DIY talents. Its satisfying to fix things around the house and save a little money on repair bills, but there are limits to even the most accomplished home handymans skills. If the air conditioned stops working in the middle of July, it might be tempting to fix it yourself. However, the benefits of calling in an air conditioner contractor in Dallas far outweigh any savings you might manage with a DIY job.
        </p>
        <h2>
         When to Make That Call
        </h2>
        <p>
         Youre comfortable with routine car care, but if the transmission needs work, you turn to a trusted mechanic. The same principle applies to your homes HVAC system. Replacing the filters on a regular basis makes a big difference in its performance, but youre not going to clean out the ductwork. You keep the grass trimmed around the outside unit and make sure it stays free of branches and debris. However, when it starts to squeal off key or rattle the housing, it needs professional attention. Your best DIY intentions can turn a simple repair job into a major project, so hiring an air conditioner contractor in DFW is always a smart strategy.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Why Its the Right Decision
        </h2>
        <p>
         Your homes heating and cooling system represents a major investment.
         <a href="http://www.maxmechanical.com/residential-ac-service/preventative-maintenance/" title="Professional AC Tune-Ups">
          Professional AC tune-ups
         </a>
         keep everything running efficiently, hold down utility bills and add years of life to your HVAC unit. When something goes wrong, a certified technician knows what to look for, fixes the problem and restores your system to peak operating condition. The DIY approach might work on a broken kitchen blender, but replacing that counter appliance wont wreck the budget if your efforts fall short. Attempting HVAC repairs on your own leaves you wide open for financial disaster. Keep in mind that home insurance and warranty contracts dont cover unlicensed repair work, so its important to hire someone with a verifiable certification.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Who Is a Certified HVAC Technician?
        </h2>
        <p>
         The state of Texas sets rigorous standards for HVAC certification beginning with a required two years of field experience. This apprenticeship includes on-the-job training, offsite classes and covers the entire range of residential and commercial heating, cooling and refrigeration systems. Specialized training includes maintenance, repairs and installations on everything from traditional HVAC units to heat pumps and solar heating and cooling. Once a technician completes his training and passes the state-required test, his certification stands just like any degree from an institution of higher education. Before you hire someone to work on your homes system, ask about their certification. Professional companies never hesitate to share their credentials.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What to Expect From an Air Conditioning Contractor
        </h2>
        <p>
         With a certified technician on the job, you have the assurance that he brings his special education and industry experience to the project. From seasonal inspections to major repairs and replacements, you know youve hired someone whos licensed and bonded. A professional technicians training doesnt end with certification. Ongoing classes keep him on top of the latest techniques and technologies, so his expertise advances along with todays HVAC innovations. When you hire a professional, you can expect smart diagnostics and a clear explanation of your options. His maintenance, repair and installation work is backed by the companys guarantees, and you can count on a job done well, on time and on budget.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here at Max Mechanical, our certified HVAC specialists have taken professional care of home and business owners throughout Arlington, Dallas and Fort Worth for more than 20 years. Were proud of our longstanding Air Conditioning Contractors of America membership and our sterling BBB accreditation.

           service appointment online
          </a>
          , or contact our friendly staff at 817-459-4100 or 972-233-1637
         </strong>
         . When you call us, you know youre making the right decision.
        </p>
