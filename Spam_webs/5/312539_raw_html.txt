            </em>

           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="10293" data-inline-quote-symbol="ATVI" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/ATVI" target="_self">
             Activision Blizzard
            </a>
            - The video-game publisher posted second-quarter earnings per share of 6 cents on $658 million in revenue, beating estimates of EPS of 2 cents on $608 million in sales. Shares rose in after-hours trading.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="144500" data-inline-quote-symbol="FEYE" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/FEYE" target="_self">
             FireEye
            </a>
            - The provider of cloud-computing security tallied a second-quarter earnings per share loss of 55 cents, excluding items, on $94.5 million in revenue, versus estimates of an EPS loss of 60 cents on $90 million in sales. Shares rose in after-hours trading.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="52153" data-inline-quote-symbol="FSLR" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/FSLR" target="_self">
             First Solar
            </a>
