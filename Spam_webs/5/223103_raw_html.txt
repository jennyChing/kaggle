        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         5 Simple Ways to Make a House a Home
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-06-30T07:00:33+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 30, 2014
         </time>
         By:
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Mel Lockcuff
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://adventuresofmel.com/2014/06/5-simple-ways-to-make-a-house-a-home.html#comments">
           6 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <img alt="Wayfair Disclosure" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23441" height="23" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Wayfair-Disclosure.png" width="700"/>
         Its been a year since we moved in to our new to us home, and I found myself just recently emptying my last boxes. Ive been decorating in spurts, but every time I put something else on the wall or in a cabinet or on a shelf. it begins to look a little more like home. Of course, it doesnt hurt that Ive signed up for Wayfair Daily Sales emails, so now those Daily Sales kinda beckon to me, tempting me with new treasures to add more home to our house.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          What are Wayfair Daily Sales?
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Wayfair Daily Sales are something new Wayfair has implemented. If youve never shopped
         <a href="http://www.wayfair.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Wayfair
         </a>
         , you are missing out, let me tell you. Their Daily Sales include things like furniture, storage items, bedding, outdoor furniture and equipment, rugs, appliances, cookware, dishes, bathroom items, wall art, lighting, kids items, and more.
        </p>
        <p>
         <br class="Apple-interchange-newline"/>
         <img alt="Wayfair Daily Sales" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23440" height="509" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Wayfair-Daily-Sales.png" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Daily Sales launch every day at noon EST and are limited time only. Each sale group will state when the sale ends. Youll find items up to 70% off, which is especially helpful if youve already been eyeing something, just waiting for it to go on sale.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          Ways to Make a House a Home
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Exploring Wayfairs Daily Sales the past few weeks inspired me to think about the way I decorate our home; so, I came up with 5 simple ways that anyone can make a house a home.
         <img alt="5 Simple Ways to Make a House a Home - MamaBuzz {mamabzz.com}" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23439" height="750" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/5-Simple-Ways-to-Make-a-House-a-Home-MamaBuzz-mamabzz.com_.png" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Use simple decor to liven up any space, whether its a set of canisters in the kitchen, wire baskets, throw pillows, family photos, or anything else you feel would make a great addition to your home decor. One of the items I found was this
         <a href="http://www.wayfair.com/Certified-International-Lille-Rooster-Canister-Set-of-3-10180-CEI1723.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Rooster Canister Set
         </a>
         , which adds a little variety to our kitchen.We kinda have a retro, sentimental, country chic look going.
         <img alt="Decorative Canister Set from Wayfair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23435" height="546" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Decorative-Canister-Set-from-Wayfair.jpg" width="700"/>
         Wire baskets are all the rage right now, so this
         <a href="http://www.wayfair.com/Zingz-and-Thingz-Rooster-Wire-Kitchen-Basket-ZNGZ1354.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Rooster Wire Kitchen Basket
         </a>
         was another great addition to our kitchen space.
         <img alt="Rooster basket from Wayfair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23437" height="531" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Rooster-basket-from-Wayfair.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Tie in sentimental or vintage items with your home decor. Maybe you have an old cookbook, or maybe you have a dish that means a lot to you. maybe an apron or a throw blanket that was made for you. Tie those things in with your decor. what better way to decorate a home than to be surrounded by things that are meaningful?
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Add in color. Whether its a bedspread, a throw pillow, or even a piece of furniture. be bold! Im about to paint our kitchen cabinets a lighter shade of green, and I feel like its a bold choice but one that will just lift the atmosphere of our kitchen.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Dont forget about your outdoor space. The key to a great outdoor space is to make it inviting and relaxing. Decorate that outdoor space, and add items and activities that will lend to amazing family memories together. The kids have already been busy playing with this
         <a href="http://www.wayfair.com/GoSports-3-Hole-Washer-Toss-CornHole-Game-CHWT-01-PFL1029.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          3 Hole Washer Toss/CornHole Game
         </a>
         .
         <img alt="Washer Toss and Cornhole Game from Wayfair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23438" height="535" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Washer-Toss-and-Cornhole-Game-from-Wayfair.jpg" width="700"/>
         Its so much fun to finish up dinner and head outside, whether were lazing in the hammock, putzing around in the garden, playing in the pool, or playing a game of CornHole.
         <img alt="Playing Cornhole in the yard" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23436" height="526" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Playing-Cornhole-in-the-yard.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         5. Live and laugh with the ones you love. Seriously, theres no better way to make a house a home than to fill it with love.
         <br/>
        </p>

		save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append("Processing Your Request...");
		var dataString = 'eStore_cart_action=eStore_retrieve_cart&amp;cart_id=' + cart_id;
		$.ajax({
			type: "POST",
			url: process_script_url,
			data: dataString,
			dataType: "json",
			success: function(data) {
				if(data.status == "success"){

					window.location.href = target_url;

				}	
			}
		});
