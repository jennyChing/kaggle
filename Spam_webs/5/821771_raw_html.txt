        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Mushroom And Cheese Chicken
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/" rel="bookmark" title="8:08 pm">
          <span class="entry-date">
           September 25, 2011
          </span>
         </a>
         <span class="meta-sep">
          by
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://zoomyummy.com/author/zoomyummy/" title="View all posts by zoomyummy">
           zoomyummy
          </a>
         </span>
         |
         <a href="http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/#comments" rel="nofollow">
          Leave a comment
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14455" height="367" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-14.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-14.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          I love chicken recipes.
         </p>
         <p>
          I always have.
         </p>
         <p>
          When I was a kid I preferred the taste of chicken to any other kind of meat.
         </p>
         <p>
          And now, when Im a kid no more, not only has my love for the taste of chicken remained but Ive also developed deep love for simplicity of these meals.
         </p>
         <p>
          With addition of few basic ingredients you can create a nice dish in almost no time.
          <br/>
          And this recipe is exactly of that kind.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its tasty, its quick to make, and its flavorful and comforting just like a good home-made dish should be.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you are interested, heres the step-by-step recipe
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14462" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese-ingredients.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese-ingredients" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese-ingredients.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          What needs to be said first, needs to be said first  the ingredients.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, slicing chicken breasts in half, chicken butterfly cut" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14465" height="367" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-16.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-16.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Slice each chicken breast in half horizontally.
         </p>
         <div class="readmore">
          <span id="more-14437">
          </span>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, slicing mushrooms, cutting mushroom, white button mushrooms" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14442" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-2.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-2.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Rinse the mushrooms under running water and dry them with paper towels.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Slice the mushrooms.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, grating cheese" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14443" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-3.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-3.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Shred the cheese.
         </p>
         <p>
          I used Edam because we buy that one most often and I just found it in the fridge, but you can use any kind you like.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, whisking dry ingredients to coat chicken breasts before cooking" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14446" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-41.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-41.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          5. In a shallow bowl, combine the flour (1/4 cup  35 grams), salt (1/4 tsp) and black pepper (1/4 tsp).
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, coating chicken breasts in flour and black pepper mixture" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14447" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-61.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-61.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          6. Coat each chicken piece with the flour mixture.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, cooking coated chicken breasts in large coated frying pan" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14448" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-7.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-7.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          7. In a large frying pan (or skillet), heat the oil over medium-high heat.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          8. Add the chicken pieces and cook them, about 5 minutes per side.
         </p>
         <p>
          Then remove the chicken to a separate plate.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, cooking mushrooms in large oiled frying pan" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14449" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-8.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-8.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          9. Place the mushrooms into the same pan.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          10. Add the salt (1/2 tsp), chicken broth, balsamic vinegar, and thyme.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cook over medium heat about 7 minutes, stirring frequently.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          11. Then add the cornstarch and cook about 1-2 minutes, or until the sauce gets thick.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, mushroom ragout in a bowl, mushroom sauce in a bowl" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14450" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-9.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-9.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          12. Remove the mushroom sauce into a separate bowl.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, spooning mushroom sauce over cooked chicken breast fillets in a large frying pan" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14451" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-10.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-10.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          13. With the heat under your frying pan on medium, return the chicken pieces into the pan.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          14. Spoon the mushroom sauce over the chicken.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, distribute shredded cheese evenly over cooked chicken fillets covered with mushroom sauce" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14452" height="367" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-11.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-11.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          15. Distribute the cheese evenly over each chicken piece.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, chicken fillets covered with mushroom sauce and shredded cheese in a large frying pan, skillet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14453" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-12.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-12.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Just like this.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, cooked chickne breast fillets covered with mushroom sauce and melted cheese in a large frying pan skillet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14454" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-13.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-13.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          16. Cover and cook about 5 more minutes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="chicken with mushrooms and cheese step by step recipe with ingredients and pictures, chicken fillets with mushroom sauce, melted cheese, peas, corn and cooked tomatoes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14438" height="363" src="http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-15.jpg" title="chicken-with-mushrooms-and-cheese-recipe" width="545"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zoomyummy.com/2011/09/25/mushroom-and-cheese-chicken/&amp;media=http://zoomyummy.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/balsamic-mushroom-chicken-15.jpg&amp;description=Mushroom And Cheese Chicken')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Serve with potatoes and vegetables that you like. We had corn and peas and we liked that a lot.
         </p>
         <p>
          Enjoy, dear friends!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Love,
          <br/>
          Petra
         </p>
