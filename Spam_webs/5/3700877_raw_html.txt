     <h6 class="underlined">
      Por Vocao Store
     </h6>
     <div class="post-4352 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-lifestyle tag-accessories tag-bracelets tag-comme-des-garcons tag-creating-different-ambiences tag-international-labels tag-latest-trends tag-leather tag-magazines tag-male-lifestyle tag-martin-margiela tag-neil-barrett tag-paul-smith tag-pendents tag-por-vocacao tag-porto tag-raf-simons tag-rings tag-urban-fashion">
      <div class="content clearfix">
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Por Vocao is, certainly, the coolest shop in Porto when we talk about contemporary mens fashion. There we can find an excellent selection of some of the best international labels such as Paul Smith, Comme des Garons, Martin Margiela, Neil Barrett, Raf Simons
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://portugalbrands.com/blog/por-vocacao-store/379940_335644193129852_162274770466796_1300587_256725576_n/" rel="attachment wp-att-4353">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4353" height="493" src="http://portugalbrands.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/379940_335644193129852_162274770466796_1300587_256725576_n.jpg" title="379940_335644193129852_162274770466796_1300587_256725576_n" width="740"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        But it would be reductive to classify it as a simple clothing store, since there we also can find a careful selection of perfumes, candles, limited edition tennis/sneakers, magazines, accessories, leather, pendents, bracelets, rings  etc. Clothing and accessories for those who, even if not admitting it, follow the latest trends in urban fashion.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://portugalbrands.com/blog/por-vocacao-store/1-800x770-contain-07_1/" rel="attachment wp-att-4354">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4354" height="493" src="http://portugalbrands.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/1-800x770-contain-07_1.jpg" title="1 800x770-contain-07_1" width="740"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Everybody outside feels the marvellous scent that comes from inside. The space is modern and cosmopolitan and is characterized by its versatility, because according to the needs of each collection, furniture are moved around and the lighting is adjusted, creating different ambiences. The shop window is another attractive, following each change with original unique design that is worth a visit whenever you come to Porto. Ring the bell and get in this male lifestyle cool spot!
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <a href="http://portugalbrands.com/blog/por-vocacao-store/800x770-contain-01_1/" rel="attachment wp-att-4355">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4355" height="494" src="http://portugalbrands.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/800x770-contain-01_1.jpg" title="800x770-contain-01_1" width="740"/>
        </a>
       </p>
