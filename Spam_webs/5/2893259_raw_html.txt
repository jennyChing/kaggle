          <p>
           Talented artist
           <a href="http://www.carnegriffiths.com/" target="_blank" title="Carne Griffiths">
            Carne Griffiths
           </a>
           uses unique materials to create paintings.Originally from Liverpool in the UK, he primarily works with calligraphy inks, graphite and liquids, such as tea, brandy and vodka, drawing focuses on the creation and manipulation of the drawn line.
          </p>
