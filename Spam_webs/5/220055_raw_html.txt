       <div class="post-23097 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-crochet-patterns-crochet-resources tag-crochet-dress tag-free-crochet-patterns tag-how-to-crochet-a-dress" id="post-23097">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         15 Beautiful Free Crochet Dress Patterns for Women
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="onDate date updated">
          <i class="icon-time icon-metas" title="Date">
          </i>
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/2012/09/15-beautiful-free-crochet-dress-patterns-for-women/" rel="bookmark">
           September 5, 2012
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <i class="icon-folder-open icon-metas" title="Categories">
          </i>
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/category/crochet-resources/crochet-patterns-crochet-resources/" rel="tag">
           Crochet Patterns
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          People often ask me about the crochet dresses that I make for myself. I actually dont work from patterns most of the time and my dresses are all patternless designs. That doesnt work for everyone, though, so I thought Id do a roundup of some of the best crochet dress patterns available. This weeks edition is all free crochet patterns and next week Ill follow up with patterns that are worth paying to get.
         </p>
         <h2>
          1. Openwork Crochet Dress Pattern
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/openwork-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23100" height="511" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/openwork-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg" title="openwork crochet dress pattern" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          I love Kristen TenDykes crochet dress, designed to be worn over something since its openwork in design. Its bulky and textured and cool-looking! Free pattern via
          <a href="http://www.classiceliteyarns.com/WebLetter/82/Issue82.php" target="_blank">
           Classic Elite Yarns
          </a>
          . Photo by Carrie Bostick Hoge.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: left;">
          2. Classic White Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/classic-white-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23101" height="568" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/classic-white-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg" title="classic white crochet dress pattern" width="403"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This
          <a href="http://garnstudio.com/lang/en/pattern.php?id=1053&amp;lang=en" target="_blank">
           DROPS dress
          </a>
          is lovely with a classic, sophisticated sense of style. Note: the pattern is in British English.
         </p>
         <h2>
          <span id="more-23097">
          </span>
          3. Yellow Beach Cover Up Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/crochet-dress-pattern.jpeg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23102" height="500" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/crochet-dress-pattern.jpeg" title="crochet dress pattern" width="333"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This
          <a href="http://www.redheart.com/files/patterns/pdf/SN0154.pdf" target="_blank">
           free crochet pattern
          </a>
          from Stitch Nation is by Ann Regis. Its designed to be worn over a slip as a summer style.
         </p>
         <h2>
          4. Neon Mesh Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/neon-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23103" height="420" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/neon-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg" title="neon crochet dress pattern" width="270"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Heres another example of an openwork dress that can be worn over another dress. I like this style because it is easy to create even with beginner crochet skills.
          <a href="http://www.redheart.com/free-patterns/webby-sundress-or-skirt" target="_blank">
           This free pattern is from Red Heart
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          5. Summer Tunic Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/crochet-tunic-pattern.png">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23105" height="546" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/crochet-tunic-pattern.png" title="crochet tunic pattern" width="363"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This
          <a href="http://knits4kids.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/Thai-Style-Summer-Tunic.pdf" target="_blank">
           crochet dress pattern
          </a>
          from Knits for Kids can be worn as a tunic with leggings or shorts or made longer to wear as a dress.
         </p>
         <h2>
          6. Simply Soft Doris Chan Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/doris-chan-crochet-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23106" height="500" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/doris-chan-crochet-dress.jpg" title="doris chan crochet dress" width="374"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This Doris Chan crochet dress, pattern available free through
          <a href="http://www.michaels.com/on/demandware.store/Sites-Michaels-Site/default/Product-Show?pid=e06094&amp;viewId=true" target="_blank">
           Michaels
          </a>
          , is made using Caron Simply Soft, an affordable soft yarn
         </p>
         <h2>
          7. Sexy Night Out Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/sexy-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23107" height="330" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/sexy-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg" title="sexy crochet dress pattern" width="125"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Go daring with this crochet dress pattern from
          <a href="http://www.hookyarnandneedles.com/oldstuff/node/104" target="_blank">
           Hook, Yarn and Needles
          </a>
          . You could also wear it over a top or slip for a more sophisticated look.
         </p>
         <h2>
          8. Tammys Slip Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/crochet-slip-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23109" height="500" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/crochet-slip-dress.jpg" title="crochet slip dress" width="254"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This
          <a href="http://www.creativepartnersllc.com/Pattern-LoveLaceLingerie.html" target="_blank">
           crochet dress by Tammy Hildebrand
          </a>
          is designed to be a lingerie pattern but with the popularity of innerwear as outerwear it could be styled with a jacket and tights to be a great dress to wear out on the town.
         </p>
         <h2>
          9. Textured Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/textured-crochet-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23110" height="468" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/textured-crochet-dress.jpg" title="textured crochet dress" width="287"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          I think itd be tough to pull off wearing this
          <a href="http://bit.ly/NImyCI" target="_blank">
           tweed crochet dress
          </a>
          designed by Treva F. McCain since its bulky but I love the texture. Its worked with Carons Simply Soft (same as Doris Chans) and the crochet pattern is available free through Caron.
         </p>
         <h2>
          10. Chanel Style Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/designer-inspired-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23111" height="606" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/designer-inspired-crochet-dress-pattern.jpg" title="designer inspired crochet dress pattern" width="450"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          I adore this designer-inspired crochet dress. The crochet pattern is available in several languages and is
          <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/chanel-style-dress" target="_blank">
           free through Ravelry
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          11. Red Cotton Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/red-crochet-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23113" height="640" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/red-crochet-dress.jpg" title="red crochet dress" width="313"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          I love the design on this crochet dress thats worked with red cotton. It can be worked in the round or made in two pieces, front and back.
          <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/red-cotton-dress" target="_blank">
           Free Ravelry Download
          </a>
          . The pattern is an English translation of a Russian crochet pattern.
         </p>
         <h2>
          12. A-Line Crochet Dress Pattern
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/a-line-crochet-dress-.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23114" height="711" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/a-line-crochet-dress--400x711.jpg" title="a line crochet dress" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This
          <a href="http://estherlunie.blogspot.com/2012/05/crocheted-simple-line-dress.html" target="_blank">
           free crochet dress pattern
          </a>
          comes from A Craft A Day Keeps Boredom Away. I think this is one of the most practical designs in this roundup.
         </p>
         <h2>
          13. Italian Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/italian-crochet-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-23115" height="533" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/italian-crochet-dress-400x533.jpg" title="italian crochet dress" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          The pattern for this
          <a href="http://tastyknit.blogspot.it/2012/07/un-vestitino-alluncinetto-per-le-tue.html" target="_blank">
           super cute crochet dress
          </a>
          is written in Italian but also has an English translation. This crochet dress is worked in pieces and stitched together at the end.
         </p>
         <h2>
          14. Sophisticated Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/sophisticated-crochet-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23116" height="500" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/sophisticated-crochet-dress.jpg" title="sophisticated crochet dress" width="299"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          One of my favorite free crochet dress patterns is this one, called
          <a href="http://www.crochetmagazine.com/printer.php?mode=article&amp;article_id=2575" target="_blank">
           Nightfall
          </a>
          , which was designed by Paula Bennett and is available through the Talking Crochet newsletter archives from Crochet World
         </p>
         <h2>
          15. Cool Weather Crochet Dress
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/winter-crochet-dress.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23117" height="510" src="http://www.crochetconcupiscence.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/winter-crochet-dress.jpg" title="winter crochet dress" width="365"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          The long sleeves and cozy look make this crochet dress a great option for fall and winter. Its a
          <a href="http://www.garnstudio.com/lang/us/pattern.php?id=1675&amp;lang=us" target="_blank">
           DROPS design
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          What questions do you have about crocheting dresses?
         </p>
