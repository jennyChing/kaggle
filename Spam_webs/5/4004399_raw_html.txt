       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Whipped Chocolate Mint Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-12-19T15:29:50+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 19, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://momstart.com/author/momstart/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Momstart
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://momstart.com/2013/12/whipped-chocolate-mint-peanut-butter-bars-recipe/#comments">
           6 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         I loved how easy these peanut butter bars were to make. I used Hungry Jack pancake mix as the flour and then added JIF whipped peanut butter, not only was it peanut butter it was their limited edition chocolate mint whipped peanut butter and these bars just melt in your mouth. They are moist and so good. My husband said he LOVED them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Peanut-Butter-Mint-Bars.jpg">
          <img alt="Peanut Butter Mint Bars" border="0" height="754" src="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Peanut-Butter-Mint-Bars_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto 5px; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="Peanut Butter Mint Bars" width="504"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Peanut Butter Mint
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Crisco
         </b>
          Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
         <br/>
         1/2 cup
         <b>
          Jif
         </b>
          Whipped Chocolate Mint Peanut Butter
         <br/>
         1 cup water
         <br/>
         1 large egg
         <br/>
         1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
         <br/>
         1 (7 oz.) package
         <b>
          Hungry Jack
         </b>
          Easy Pack Funfetti Buttermilk Pancake Mix
        </p>
        <p>
         OR
         <br/>
         1 1/2 cups
         <b>
          Hungry Jack
         </b>
          Complete Funfetti Buttermilk Pancake &amp; Waffle Mix
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4 cup Andes Mints
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/IMG_0009.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_0009" border="0" height="337" src="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/IMG_0009_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto 5px; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="IMG_0009" width="504"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. HEAT oven to 375F. Coat 13 x 9-inch baking pan with no-stick cooking spray.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. WHISK peanut butter and water until blended. Beat in egg and brown sugar. Stir in pancake mix until smooth. Pour evenly in prepared baking pan.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. BAKE 18 to 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/IMG_0004.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_0004" border="0" height="754" src="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/IMG_0004_thumb.jpg" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto 5px; border-left: 0px; display: block; padding-right: 0px" title="IMG_0004" width="504"/>
         </a>
        </p>
