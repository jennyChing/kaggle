          <h3>
           Ingredients for Cinnamon Swirl Cookies
          </h3>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Flour: 500 gr.
           </li>
           <li>
            Warm water: 280 ml.
           </li>
           <li>
            Fresh
            <strong>
             yeast
            </strong>
            20 gr.
           </li>
           <li>
            Sugar: 2 tbsp
           </li>
           <li>
            Salt: 0.5 tsp
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Butter
            </strong>
            : 180 gr.
           </li>
           <li>
            Half cup of sugar and little more + 1-2 tbsp ground
            <strong>
             cinnamon
            </strong>
            for sprinkling.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h3>
           Recipe for Cinnamon Swirl Cookies
          </h3>
          <ol>
           <ol>
            <li>
             Dissolve yeast in 5 spoons of
             <strong>
              water
             </strong>
             with sugar, add a couple tablespoons of
             <strong>
              flour
             </strong>
             , leave for 20 minutes.
            </li>
            <li>
             Sift the flour, make an indentation, and add activated yeast, salt.
            </li>
            <li>
             Knead soft dough that does not stick, gradually adding the remaining water.
            </li>
            <li>
             Roll a ball, place it in a bowl, cover, and let rise within an hour.
            </li>
            <li>
             Then add butter, knead for 10 minutes. The dough should be soft and airy. Leave to rise for another hour.
            </li>
            <li>
             Then divide the dough into 2 parts, roll each out on a floured surface, sprinkle thickly with
             <strong>
              sugar
             </strong>
             and cinnamon.
            </li>
            <li>
             Make rolls and cut them into pieces of 1-2 cm wide.
            </li>
            <li>
             Put the slices on a baking sheet, cuts placed vertically.
            </li>
            <li>
             Bake in preheated oven 392F (200C) 15-20 min. until golden brown.
            </li>
           </ol>
          </ol>
