          <p>
           Good and comfortable pair of lingerie gives you the desired look. Hence, you need to understand that which bra would complement your outfit. The wrong bra makes you look uneasy and heavy on the top, while the right option can make you look stunning irrespective of what you wear. Lets take a look at the bras that you must-have in your cupboard.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Everyday Bra
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You should have comfortable cotton bra for everyday use that is sturdy and reliable. An everyday bra should have straps centred exactly above the nipple area and a wide back strap that doesnt show lumps. This bra gives your breasts the best lift and your shoulders the most comfort.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Strapless Bra
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           This pair is essential when wearing strappy summer dresses and tops. Visible bra straps look tacky and it spoils the vibe and style of a great dress. On the other hand strapless bra provides you the needed comfort without ruining the look of a dress. Opt for a nude or beige colour strapless bra as they will coordinate well with all kinds of apparel.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Convertible Bra
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           This is the most chic bra that is popular as well as trendy. Its versatile feature makes haute and it can be worn in nine different ways such as- halter style, strapless, open back, cross front, racer back, one strap, low front, low back, asymmetric.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <strong>
             The Sports Bra
            </strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           This particular type is a must for every active woman. Especially for those who exercise every day because it supports your assets from bounce and avoid breast tissue to stretch. And, it also keeps you cool within.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Black Lace Bra
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           A pair of sexy, lacy and functional bra can be seen in every womans wardrobe. It makes you feel good no matter what you wear. It not only makes you feel sexy but also makes you irresistible to your partner.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The T-shirt Bra
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           This basic type of bra is common among women all around the world. It gives you a perfect fit with t-shirts and other kinds of casual apparels too. Cotton t-shirt bras are the best that offer good coverage with no lumps or straps seen.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Push-up Bra
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The push-up bra adds volume and shape to your bosom. They are available in a large variety of shapes and sizes such as padding, straps, clasps and a lot more. They are most ideal to wear with plunging necklines in your dresses and shirts, when you want to show a little cleavage.
          </p>
