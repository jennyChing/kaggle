       <img alt="" class="" src="/photos/620x0/21787.jpg?1436807285" title="5  Valuable Things You Can Give that Do Not Cost a Thing [LIST]" width="620"/>
       <div class="caption">
        <p>
         Shutterstock Image
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <span id="docs-internal-guid-af45b7d9-856b-1f20-614a-687a6f875ad6">
         <span>
          While money supposedly makes the world go round, individuals and groups can make an impact without using any form of currency. Learn about five cost-free ways to contribute to yourself, others and the planet.
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <div style="margin-top: -20px;">
        <div class="cf">
         <h3>
          <strong>
           1. YOUR VOICE
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <div class="has_h2">
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            THE BASICS:
           </strong>
           Social media is a great outlet to make a difference, by making your opinion heard and gathering attention around a certain issue.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            WHERE TO TURN:
           </strong>
           To amplify your voice, check out
           <a href="https://www.thunderclap.it/" target="_blank">
            Thunderclap
           </a>
           which allows a singular cause or message to be shared in mass, flash mob-style via Facebook, Twitter or Tumblr.
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="" class="" src="/photos/620x0/21788.jpg?1436807285" title="5  Valuable Things You Can Give that Do Not Cost a Thing [LIST]" width="620"/>
         <div class="caption">
          <p>
           <span>
            Shutterstock Image
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="cf">
         <h3>
          <strong>
           2. YOUR TIME
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <div class="has_h2">
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            THE BASICS:
           </strong>
           Time is one of the most valuable commodities out there and giving it to others can be a priceless gift.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            WHERE TO TURN:
           </strong>
           Get your volunteering on with
           <a href="http://www.volunteermatch.org/" target="_blank">
            VolunteerMatch
           </a>
           , a top notch search engine which finds volunteer opportunities in your area according to your interests.
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="" class="" src="/photos/620x0/21781.jpg?1436807285" title="5  Valuable Things You Can Give that Do Not Cost a Thing [LIST]" width="620"/>
         <div class="caption">
          <p>
           <span>
            Shutterstock Image
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="cf">
         <h3>
          <strong>
           3. YOUR GOOD INTENTIONS
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <div class="has_h2">
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            THE BASICS:
           </strong>
           Acting in a kind manner does not cost a thing, with
           <a href="http://www.wired.com/2010/03/kindness-spreads/" target="_blank">
            studies
           </a>
           revealing that good deeds and kind acts have a ripple effect that increase the amount of positivity surrounding us.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            WHERE TO TURN:
           </strong>
           For a bit of inspiration,
           <a href="/articles/1033" target="_blank">
            watch this video
           </a>
           by the nonprofit
           <a href="http://www.lifevestinside.com/" target="_blank">
            Life Vest Inside
           </a>
           which demonstrates the way kindness spreads. On an everyday basis, simple acts like smiling at a stranger or holding the door open for the next person are don't require much effort and can make all the difference to someone else.
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="" class="" src="/photos/620x0/21782.jpg?1436807285" title="5  Valuable Things You Can Give that Do Not Cost a Thing [LIST]" width="620"/>
         <div class="caption">
          <p>
           <span>
            Shutterstock Image
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="cf">
         <h3>
          <strong>
           4. YOUR CARE FOR THE PLANET
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <div class="has_h2">
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            THE BASICS:
           </strong>
           Living in a cleaner and greener world is something that you can bestow upon future generations.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            WHERE TO TURN:
           </strong>
           Picking up stray pieces of litter that you come across is an important contribution that can be made into a daily routine. Take it a step further by posting your trash on Instagrams
           <a href="http://litterati.org/index.php?utm_source=goodnet-site&amp;utm_medium=footer-related&amp;utm_campaign=goodnet-related" target="_blank">
            Litterati
           </a>
           which serves as a useful tool to identify what type of garbage is accumulating and where.
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="" class="" src="/photos/620x0/21784.jpg?1436807285" title="5  Valuable Things You Can Give that Do Not Cost a Thing [LIST]" width="620"/>
         <div class="caption">
          <p>
           <span>
            Shutterstock Image
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="cf">
         <h3>
          <strong>
           5. YOUR HEALTH
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <div class="has_h2">
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            THE BASICS:
           </strong>
           Being in good health means that you can be around to enjoy life and spend time with those who are most important.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            WHERE TO TURN:
           </strong>
           Here is a list of
           <a href="/articles/5-essential-apps-for-healthy-shopping" target="_blank">
            five apps
           </a>
           to get you started paying attention to your nutrition by helping you choose healthy foods. For those looking to improve their physical activity, enjoy these
           <a href="/articles/5-kids-games-to-get-you-in-shape" target="_blank">
            five fun games you used to play as a kid
           </a>
           to get your heart pumping, and monitor your fitness progress with the free app
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/id300235330?mt=8" target="_blank">
            RunKeeper
           </a>
           .
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="" class="" src="/photos/620x0/21789.jpg?1436807285" title="5  Valuable Things You Can Give that Do Not Cost a Thing [LIST]" width="620"/>
         <div class="caption">
          <p>
           <span>
            Shutterstock Image
           </span>
          </p>
