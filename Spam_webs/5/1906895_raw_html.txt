         <div class="center" style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #ffffff;">
           "All controversy begins with DOUBT"
          </span>
          <p>
           <img alt="" height="150" src="http://media.moddb.com/images/games/1/9/8067/auto/1342550673Turbocycle.gif" width="233"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            The year is 2047 : A breed of artificial life-forms, or bioroids known as "
            <span style="color: #ff0000;">
             Snatchers
            </span>
            " began appearing in the artificial island of Neo Kobe City, killing their victims and taking their place in society.
           </span>
           <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            As Gillian Seed, an amnesiac detective working for an Anti-Snatcher task force called J.U.N.K.E.R., the player's goal is to track down the source of the snatchers and discover Gillian's mysterious past.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" height="101" src="http://media.moddb.com/images/games/1/9/8067/auto/1342550676metal.gif" width="78"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Snatcher
           </span>
           <span style="color: #888888;">
            is based on the
           </span>
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hideo_Kojima" title="Hideo Kojima">
            <span style="color: #ffffff;">
             Hideo Kojima
            </span>
           </a>
           <span style="color: #888888;">
            game
           </span>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Snatcher
           </span>
           <span style="color: #888888;">
            oringally released in 1988.
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="color: #888888;">
            For more information about the original game please visit this link :
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snatcher">
             En.wikipedia.org
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <span style="color: #ffffff;">
             <span style="color: #ff9900;">
              *
             </span>
             This game is
             <strong>
              completely unofficial
             </strong>
             and in no way endorsed by Konami.
             <span style="color: #ff9900;">
              *
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="color: #888888;">
            The goal is to faithfully re-create the original 2D
           </span>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Snatcher
           </span>
           <span style="color: #888888;">
            in an explorable 3D world.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            <span style="color: #ff0000;">
             
            </span>
            <em>
             <span style="color: #ffffff;">
              This idea of an open world in 3D is very good, and I would love to do something like Snatcher, but I do not have the time or the means to do so.
              <br/>
              But if anyone else would like to develop it, I would love it.
             </span>
             <span style="color: #ff0000;">
              
             </span>
            </em>
           </span>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            
           </span>
           <span>
            <span style="color: #ff0000;">
             <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hideo_Kojima" title="Hideo Kojima">
              <span style="color: #ffffff;">
               Hideo Kojima
              </span>
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
