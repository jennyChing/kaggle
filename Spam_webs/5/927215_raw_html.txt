          </p>
          <p>
           The Flight Deal is on fire today! Jeanne posted yesterday about
           <a href="http://heelsfirsttravel.boardingarea.com/2014/10/14/fares-europe-west-coast-465/" target="_blank" title="Fares to Europe from West Coast at ~$465">
            $465 deals to Europe from the West Coast
           </a>
           , that deal is not only still live, but its expanded to include
           <a href="http://www.theflightdeal.com/2014/10/13/american-us-airways-united-400-700-united-states-europe-roundtrip-including-all-taxes/" target="_blank">
            East Coast cities as well and additionalairline carriers have jumped in
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="cheap fares US to Europe The Flight Deal" class="size-full wp-image-12434 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/heelsfirsttravel.boardingarea.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/cheap-fares-US-to-Europe-The-Flight-Deal.jpg?resize=580%2C357"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           But the big win, if you wanted to go to Bali, are the
           <a href="http://www.theflightdeal.com/2014/10/14/delta-730-philadelphia-bali-indonesia-roundtrip-including-all-taxes/" target="_blank">
            $532 fares on Delta from Philadelphia to Bali
           </a>
           now through April. The only catch is you need to stay more than 14 days. 
          </p>
          <p>
           If you cant take off that much time (and honestly you might get bored), then its still a reasonable $760+. I did a quick search and looks like inventory is limited but theres still availability, particularly February and after, if you act quickly.
           <em>
            Prices below as of 6:15PM EST.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="cheap flights to Bali PHL-DPS" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12433" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/heelsfirsttravel.boardingarea.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/cheap-flights-to-Bali-PHL-DPS.jpg?resize=542%2C412"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.theflightdeal.com/2014/10/14/delta-730-philadelphia-bali-indonesia-roundtrip-including-all-taxes/" target="_blank">
            For details on how to book, and to support The Flight Deal, visit their website
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           The real trick with deals like this is to book first, check your calendar later as they can often be gone in hours if not minutes! And make sure youre
           <a href="http://heelsfirsttravel.boardingarea.com/2014/10/14/fares-europe-west-coast-465/" target="_blank" title="Fares to Europe from West Coast at ~$465">
            getting instant updates on deals from The Flight Deal
           </a>
           !
          </p>
