         <h1>
          No plans to ban alcohol in Oman
         </h1>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          According to latest news, presently there is no plan to ban alcohol in Oman.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Learningland latest news about Oman, according to the Al Arabiya news, there is no plan to ban alcohol in Oman yet. However, earlier, there were news to ban alcohol in Oman. But now, the news has reported through an official that presently, the Sultanate of Oman has no plan to bank the consumption and trade of alcohol in the country.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          In December, 2014, it is told through the official of the advisory board that the Shoura council of Sultanate of Oman had recommended through voting to bank alcohol in the country. It had also been reported that the members of the Shoura council voted for the punishment for practicing alcohol related activities such as production of alcohol, consumption of alcohol and trade of alcohol in the Sultanate. However, the same remained a recommendation and did not become law, till now.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          It is added that the official said, if there is no response from the cabinet, regarding recommendations of ban on alcohol, the council will then look into other methods to refrain the trade of alcohol and its consumption in Oman.
         </p>
