        <h1 class="post-title size_1" itemprop="headline">
         This Insane Video Shows a Lorry Driving Over a Formula One Car at Full Speed
        </h1>
        <div class="meta meta--post">
         <span class="meta__full">
          By
          <a class="is-author" href="/author/jesus-diaz/">
           Jesus Diaz
          </a>
          on
          <time datetime="2014-11-23T22:00:52Z" format="dd MMM yyyy" itemprop="datePublished">
          </time>
          at
          <time datetime="2014-11-23T22:00:52Z" format="h:mma" itemprop="datePublished">
          </time>
         </span>

        <div class="single-article__content group" itemprop="text">
         <p data-textannotation-id="482cff6e44a37106bbdd88482563b6b0">
          Holy mother of God,
          <a href="http://truckyeah.jalopnik.com/truck-jumps-an-f1-car-and-sets-a-world-record-doing-it-1661766862" target="_blank">
           this has to be one of the craziest vehicle stunts I've ever seen:
          </a>
          The Formula 1 team Lotus got one of their giant trailer lorries to jump over one of their race cars running at full speed. It is absolutely insane.
         </p>
         <p data-textannotation-id="141f62302b78469c0de562d4bda61fab">
          <iframe data-chomp-id="TVBcEg6klJI" frameborder="0" height="360" id="youtube-TVBcEg6klJI" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/TVBcEg6klJI?wmode=transparent&amp;rel=0&amp;autohide=1&amp;showinfo=0&amp;enablejsapi=1" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p data-textannotation-id="c9eeadad5e7b612a4e84583fa1dd618f">
          Lotus claims this is a new distance world record for a truck jump: 83 feet and 7 inches.
         </p>
         <p data-textannotation-id="e5869965f64af6a358632184c142f827">
          <a href="http://truckyeah.jalopnik.com/truck-jumps-an-f1-car-and-sets-a-world-record-doing-it-1661766862" target="_blank">
           According to Raphael Orlove at TruckYeah!
          </a>
          the stunt was coordinated by Mike Ryanan stuntman who worked on Terminator 2. He also drove the lorry.I love crazy people.
         </p>
