       <h1 class="title">
        <a href="http://www.collater.al/kim-jung-gi/" title="Kim Jung Gi">
         Kim Jung Gi
        </a>
       </h1>
       <span class="avatar">
        <a href="http://www.collater.al/author/buddy/">
         <img alt="avatar" class="avatar avatar-70 avatar-default" height="70" src="http://www.collater.al/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/buddy_avatar.jpg" style="width: 70px; height: 70px;" width="70"/>
         <span class="mask">

       </span>
       <span class="details">
        <span class="author">
         scritto da

          Buddy
         </a>
        </span>

         il 13/04/2013
        </span>

      <section>
       <div class="thumbnail wp-caption">
        <img alt="Kim Jung Gi - Illustratore e comic book artist coreano" class="attachment-featured wp-post-image" src="http://www.collater.al/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/340054_224665340996461_1162297713_o-580x300.jpg"/>
       </div>
       <div class="content">
        <p>
         
         <em>
          What moves those of genius, what inspires their work is not new ideas, but their obsession with the idea that what has already been said is still not enough.
         </em>
         
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Eugene Delacroix
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Kim Jung Gi - Illustratore e comic book artist coreano" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-43675" height="795" src="http://www.collater.al/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Schermata-03-2456377-alle-11.14.11-e1365847180123.png" title="Kim Jung Gi - Illustratore e comic book artist coreano" width="560"/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Kim Jung Gi - Illustratore e comic book artist coreano" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-43678" height="687" src="http://www.collater.al/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Schermata-03-2456382-alle-19.42.02-e1365846761581.png" title="Kim Jung Gi - Illustratore e comic book artist coreano" width="560"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-43332">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         C un momento in cui la cura dei dettagli si trasforma in eccesso
         <strong>
          maniacale
         </strong>
         e
         <strong>
          poetico
         </strong>
         , espressione di una curiosit e di una attenzione morbosa. Una fame sensuale e violenta, avida di sentire il peso, la superficie, lodore di volti e oggetti soltanto tratteggiati e bidimensionali, di istanti costruiti in ogni sfumatura e particolare per proiettarci in quel luogo e in quel tempo e
         <strong>
          in quel preciso stato danimo
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Amo
         <strong>
          Kubrick
         </strong>
         per questo motivo, amo
         <strong>
          Hitchcock
         </strong>
         e
         <strong>
          Cronenberg
         </strong>
         , amo
         <strong>
          Vittorio Giardino
         </strong>
         e
         <strong>
          Geoff Darrow
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Amo
         <strong>
          <a href="http://kimjunggi.net" target="_blank">
           Kim Jung Gi
          </a>
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
