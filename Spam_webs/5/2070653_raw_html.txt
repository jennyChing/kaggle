        21 Blogs Suggesting Homeopathic Remedies for Spring Allergies
       </a>
      </h1>
      <br/>
      <div class="socialize-in-content" style="float:left;">
       <div class="socialize-in-button socialize-in-button-left">

      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aupairjobs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/allergies.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-294" height="228" src="http://www.aupairjobs.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/allergies.jpg" title="allergies" width="245"/>
       </a>
       According to WebMD, one out of every five people suffers from some form of allergies. Allergies can be very different from person to person. Food allergies or intolerances are very common, with one out of every three people claiming to have a problem with a certain food. There are also allergies to pet dander, dust, insects and pollen. Determining what kind of allergies you have is the first step in learning what you can do to treat your symptoms. These 21 blog entries will explain homeopathic remedies for adults, kids and pets.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        For You
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Sometimes, treatment for allergies seems straight forward. If you are allergic to pet dander, then stay away from pets, and if you are allergic to nuts, dont eat them. However, sometimes its not possible to avoid a pet if someone in your family has an animal. The good news is that there are certain things you can do to live your life normally and not in a sterile bubble. On windy days when pollen counts are high you should wear your hair tied back so that the pollen doesnt catch in your hair, and you should wash your hair every night before you go to bed so that you dont transfer pollen to your pillow case. Learn some homeopathic treatments for allergies in the following seven blog posts. Be sure to discuss any homeopathic treatment plans with your doctor before trying them out.
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://we-must.com/featured-posts/eight-natural-remedies-for-spring-allergies/">
         Eight Natural Remedies for Spring Allergies
        </a>
        Watch what you eat and you may be able to reduce or eliminate your allergies.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.care2.com/greenliving/homeopathic-treatment-of-allergies-including-hay-fever.html">
         Homeopathic Treatment of Allergies (Including Hay Fever)
        </a>
        Get rid of the pollen as soon as you can after youve been exposed to it by washing out your eyes and nose.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.missclinic.com/2013/03/9-home-remedies-for-allergic-rhinitis.html">
         9 Home Remedies for Allergic Rhinitis
        </a>
        Simple remedies such as removing dust, maintaining humidity and purifying the air are discussed in this blog article.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://cause2stress.info/?p=10http://www.care2.com/greenliving/homeopathic-treatment-of-allergies-including-hay-fever.html">
         Allergies Getting You Down? Try These Tips!
        </a>
        Increase your consumption of vitamin C and keep pets outside to limit the exposure to pet dander are among the tips given in this blog post.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.mnn.com/health/allergies/stories/natural-remedies-for-allergies">
         Natural Remedies for Allergies
        </a>
        Ease your allergy symptoms by eating properly and by taking the right supplements.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.drvita.com/blog/natural-ways-to-help-treat-allergy-symptoms/">
         Natural Ways to Help Treat Allergy Symptoms
        </a>
        Avoid eating certain foods like gluten and sugar and instead eat foods like yogurt that contains probiotics.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.findhomeremedy.com/effective-home-remedies-for-allergies/">
         Effective Home Remedies for Allergies
        </a>
        Eat local raw honey to decrease your allergy symptoms.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <strong>
        For Kids
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Kids suffer with allergies just like adults do, and as parents you are often told not to give your child over the counter treatments. So what can you do? There are several natural or homeopathic remedies you can give your child to help them build up their ability to fight off the allergens, as well as treat the symptoms of allergies that they have now. Check out these seven blog posts for more information, and always check with your childs doctor before trying any natural or homeopathic remedy.
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.naturalcompounder.com/first-day-of-spring-snow-on-the-ground-allergies-around-the-corner/">
         First Day of Spring, Snow on the Ground, Allergies Around the Corner
        </a>
        Histaminum 30C comes in pellet form and can be taken under the tongue by kids every 15 minutes to get rid of allergy symptoms.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://cure-guide.com/holistic-allergy-treatment/">
         Holistic Allergy Treatment
        </a>
        Both kids and adults can treat allergies by taking or eating probiotics.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://life.gaiam.com/article/natural-relief-allergy-symptoms">
         Natural Relief for Allergy Symptoms
        </a>
        Show your child how to use saline to rinse his nose out to help clear the pollen out every day. This should reduce his suffering.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.kidsloveacupuncture.com/2013/03/25/opting-out-part-2-the-doctor-of-the-future-is-you-your-healthcare-team/">
         Opting Out Part 2: The Doctor of the Future is You and Your Healthcare Team
        </a>
        Acupuncture is recommended for treatment of allergies in kids.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.pccnaturalmarkets.com/taste/1304/allergies.html">
         Top Picks for Allergy Relief
        </a>
        Licorice root, onion and more are prepared in a tablet, liquid or nasal spray that is safe for children as well as adults.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.goodgirlgonegreen.com/natural-remedies-2/uses-of-essential-oils-for-allergy-relief">
         Benefits and Uses of Essential Oils for Allergy Relief
        </a>
        Lavender and Tea Tree oil are recommended topically for allergies and should be safe for kids, but if you are concerned ask your pediatrician.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.1-800homeopathy.com/blog/symptoms-of-hayfever/">
         Recognizing Unique Symptoms of Hayfever
        </a>
        Two different homeopathic drops that are made for kids are referenced.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <strong>
        For Pets
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       It may sound odd, but animals have allergic reactions to food and environmental issues just like humans do. While your dog or cat cant verbalize what they are feeling, you can see the symptoms, such as when the dog scratches all of the time or is constantly licking his paw and making a sore. To learn more about homeopathic treatments for pets, read these seven blog articles and check with your vet should you be interested in trying them.
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://homeopathic-remedies.thedogproblems.com/homeopathic-remedies-for-allergies-in-dogs/">
         Homeopathic Remedies for Allergies in Dogs
        </a>
        Find a list of homeopathic treatments for your dog in this blog article.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.evancarmichael.com/Franchises/6969/Dog-Allergies-and-the-Nature-of-Natural-Treatments.html">
         Dog Allergies and the Nature of Natural Treatments
        </a>
        You can treat allergies in dogs by changing their food, giving them a bath and washing their bedding.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://ottawavalleydogwhisperer.blogspot.com/2013/04/diy-natural-herbal-treatments-for-dogs.html">
         DIY Natural, Herbal Treatments for Dogs with Environmental Allergies
        </a>
        Raw local honey is recommended for dogs, plus much more.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.vetinfo.com/cat-allergy-natural-remedies.html">
         Natural Cat Allergy Remedies Examined
        </a>
        Introducing foods one by one to a cats diet can determine if your cat is allergic to a type of food.
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.squidoo.com/natural-remedies-for-dogs-and-cats-with-allergies">
