          <p>
           Cabbage nourishes your hair, combats hair loss and leaves you with silky shining hair. After all,it is loaded with nutrients like vitamins, calcium, sulphur, iron and magnesium.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Juice It
           <br/>
           Extract the juice of a cabbage and apply it on to the roots of your hair and also on the scalp. Leave it on for an hour and shampoo your hair. This will give you silky smooth hair.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Blend It
           <br/>
           Boil 2 cabbage leaves and mash them in a blender. Mix 2 tablespoons of starch and 1 teaspoons of oil into the slurry. Mix the ingredients and apply it on the hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes and wash it off with lukewarm water. This mixture will nourish your hair and will promote hair growth.
          </p>
