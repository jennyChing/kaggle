        Wedding Ring Designs
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="info">
       <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/Review">

      <p>
       <strong>
        RingCollect 
        <a href="http://ringcollect.com/wedding-ring-designs/">
         Wedding Ring Designs
        </a>
       </strong>
       When you have found the right person to spend the rest of your life with, it will be the time for you to decide the right engagement ring for that beloved one as well. Deciding on an engagement ring may be an overwhelmed task to do, especially if that will be the first time for you. However, if you have managed it well, you still need to decide on wedding ring. And to choose the right wedding ring will neither be a difficult task nor easy task. Even though the wedding ring is almost always purchased last, it should not play the second fiddle to your engagement ring. As possible as it can, it should be as precious as the engagement ring.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Wedding Ring Designs
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://ringcollect.com/wedding-ring-designs/wedding-ring-designs-gold/" rel="attachment wp-att-207">
        <img alt="wedding ring designs gold" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-207" height="260" src="http://ringcollect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/wedding-ring-designs-gold-300x260.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Like when you purchase the engagement ring, you need to set a budget as well when you want to purchase the wedding ring. Generally, a wedding ring tends to run from $500 to $4000, but the average price is around $1500. This cost, no matter what, is less of shock after the purchasing of the engagement ring.
      </p>
      <p>
       There is also a tendency that a bride plans to wear the engagement ring and the wedding ring on the same finger. If your beloved one also has the same tendency, you need to consider that the wedding ring should fit the engagement ring well when they are worn together. But there is also a tendency that the bride plans to wear the wedding ring and the engagement ring on separate hands. If your beloved one has this tendency, there is no need for you to get the matching wedding ring.
      </p>
      <p>
       Sometimes, a few years later, there will be a need to upgrade the wedding ring. It is not wrong to make a change on the wedding ring, especially when the wedding ring no longer matches your style. However, unlike engagement ring, the wedding ring is not the one that typically is upgraded. Ring enhancers can help you to solve that problem.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Wedding Ring Designs
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://ringcollect.com/wedding-ring-designs/wedding-ring-designs/" rel="attachment wp-att-208">
        <img alt="Wedding Ring Designs" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-208" height="285" src="http://ringcollect.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Wedding-Ring-Designs-300x285.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       There will be a problem if the engagement ring was passed down to a bride, a heritage engagement ring instead of the one purchased on the jewellery store, since in this case, it will be difficult to personalize the wedding ring. However, for the ring which had been passed down, it is usually made of yellow gold. You can make it white by rhodium plating the ring, however you need to redone it at least once a year, to keep the white colour, but no need to worry, though, since it transforms the ring with a safe process with price about $30 to $100. Thus, there a chance to customize the brides preference about wedding ring that can be worn alone.
      </p>
      <p>
       There will a tendency as well among couples to have the wedding ring for both. Nowadays, there are many wedding rings for men available in jewellery stores with different designs and styles. If you wish, you can purchase a wedding ring for her and a wedding ring for him, with the same design and style to match perfectly together.
