        </p>
        <p>
         If not, which was your favorite?
        </p>
        <div class="quote-wrapper">
         <div class="quote">
          The week between Christmas and New Years. This is when we all rack our brains trying to find a New Years resolution that we can make and stick too. I have a better idea for your week. Lets spend it praying that God will show us a glimpse of what we are missing by not following every step He has for us. If we could only see what God has for us when we are living under His Grace, then we would run to meet Him where ever He is calling us to be. Dont be afraid to stand up to the test of God. He has already prepared the best years of your life and is just waiting for you to figure out that you cant get there without Him.
          <div class="quoteauthor">
           -Rick Evans
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>

        <p>
         Im really looking forward to continuing the journey with God and to see what He has in store for me this year. Im even more excited to take you on this journey with and to see what He has in store for you as well! Remember, its about the journey and the journey is the destination!
        </p>

         2014..bring it on!!!
