          <h1 class="post-title">
           <a href="http://politicalblindspot.com/car-runs-1-million-miles-on-8-grams-of-thorium/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Car Runs 1 Million Miles on 8 Grams of Thorium">
            Car Runs 1 Million Miles on 8 Grams of Thorium
           </a>
          </h1>
          <p>
           <a href="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-1-625x320.jpg">
            <img alt="cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-1-625x320" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4214" height="321" src="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-1-625x320.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           In breaking news on the energy and technology front, Laser Power Systems, a U.S. company based out of Connecticut is developing a method of automotive propulsion using the element thorium to produce electricity. The results far surpass anything currently powering automobiles. To put it in perspective, 8 grams of Thorium produce enough power for a car to drive 1 million miles.
          </p>
          <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <!-- START YieldSelect.com Private Marketplace Tag For PoliticalBlindSpot.com -->
           <!-- Size: 300x250 -->
           <script src="http://ads-by.yieldselect.com/tags/6296/168/async/300x250.js" type="text/javascript">
           </script>
           <!-- END YieldSelect.com Private Marketplace Tag For PoliticalBlindSpot.com -->
          </div>
          <p>
           Thorium is similar in structure to the element uranium. Because it is an incredibly dense material it has the potential to produce tremendous heat, and thus energy.
          </p>
          <p>
           Charles Stevens, the CEO of Laser Power Systems CEO, explains that just one gram of thorium yields more energy than 28,000 liters of gasoline. Just eight grams of thorium, Stevens explains, would produce more energy than the vehicle could use in its entire life, without the need for refueling ever.
          </p>
          <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
           </script>
           <!-- PBS Content-01 -->
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8705125424764574" data-ad-slot="2421817444" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p>
           <a href="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/images-4.jpeg">
            <img alt="images (4)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4213" height="225" src="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/images-4.jpeg" width="225"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
           </script>
           <!-- PBS Content-01 -->
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8705125424764574" data-ad-slot="2421817444" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p>
           Stevens explained in an interview with
           <em>
            Wards Auto
           </em>
           , that small pieces of thorium have been used to generate heat, being positioned to create a thorium laser in the vehicle. The laser heats water which produces steam, which in turn powers a series of mini-turbines.
          </p>
          <p>
           The entire engine weighs only about 500 lbs and is light and compact enough to fit under the hood of any conventional vehicle.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-3.jpeg">
            <img alt="cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4212" height="420" src="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-3.jpeg" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Stevens and his 40 employees are now trying to answer the question of: How do you take the laser and put these things together efficiently?
          </p>
          <p>
           The question is not, however, if they can get it to work, but when they can get it efficiently produced. When they do, they will have a vehicle that will wear out before the engine. There is no oil, no emissions  nothing.
          </p>
          <p>
           Far from conceptual, this has worked in thethorium-powered 2009, Cadillac World Thorium Fuel Concept, presented byLoren Kulesus. Aside from adjusting this innovative models 24 tyres every five years, Kulesus explained that nothing else would need to be added to the vehicle, including fuel for more than a century of use.
           <a href="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-2-625x390.jpg">
            <img alt="cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-2-625x390" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4211" height="390" src="http://politicalblindspot.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cadillac-world-thorium-fuel-concept-2-625x390.jpg" width="625"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           What are your thoughts about this and other potentially thorium-powered vehicles?
          </p>
