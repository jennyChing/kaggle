          <h1>
           M-Games
          </h1>
          <p>
           <img alt="M-Games" border="0" class="alignright" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/nezzmedia.mymomentis.co.uk/images/sidebar_prod_mgames_logo.jpg?resize=159%2C120"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           M-Games is a subscription-based, electronic purchase/download and try-before-you-buy video game service. The service provides subscribers with the ability to play hundreds of video games and view game-related video content for a fixed monthly price.
           <br/>
           The M-Games service works on the following models:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Full Purchase Downloads (Day 1 releases)
           </li>
           <li>
            Games on Demand subscriptions (Games older than 6 months)
           </li>
           <li>
            Video Game Rentals (Day 1 release rentals for maximum of 3 days)
           </li>
          </ul>
