Our clients are primarily looking for great prices, service and selection on in-stock lighting. We have the largest selection of in-stock lighting in the Phoenix metro area. Our sales staff offers unparalleled service in selecting and specifying the perfect lighting for your project.
                  </p>

                   Our selection of lighting includes thousands of fixtures from hundreds of lighting lines. At Valley Light Gallery, we carry a full assortment of lighting including
                   <a href="http://www.valleylights.com/products/chandeliers/" title="Chandeliers">
                    chandeliers
                   </a>
                   , table lamps, floor lamps, bathroom lights, bath bars, wall sconces, flush mount ceiling fixtures, entry way lights, pool table lights, mini pendants, ceiling fans, outdoor landscape lighting and outdoor sconces.

                   <a href="http://www.valleylights.com/manufacturers/kichler-lighting/" title="Kichler Lighting">
                    Kichler Lighting
                   </a>

                   </a>
                   , Monte Carlo Fans,
                   <a href="http://www.valleylights.com/manufacturers/fanimation/" title="Fanimation">
                    Fanimation
                   </a>
                   and Regency Ceiling Fans.
