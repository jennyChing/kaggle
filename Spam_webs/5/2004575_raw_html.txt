            <h1 class="arw-postheader">
             <span class="arw-postheadericon">
              Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown
             </span>
            </h1>
            <div class="arw-postheadericons arw-metadata-icons">
             <span class="arw-postdateicon">
              <span class="date">
               Published
              </span>
              <span class="entry-date" title="1:54 am">
               February 13, 2013
              </span>
             </span>
             |
             <span class="arw-postauthoricon">
              <span class="author">
               By
              </span>
              <span class="author vcard">
               <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.stepandrepeatla.com/author/jewel/" title="View all posts by Jewel">
                Jewel
               </a>
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="arw-postcontent">
             <!-- article-content -->
             <p>
              Charlie Brown and The Peanuts Gang, some of the most beloved animated characters in books and films, started out as a comic strip first seen on October 2,1950 in the newspapers like
              <em>
               The Washington Post
              </em>
              and
              <em>
               The Chicago Tribune
              </em>
              . It was later brought to life in their animated movies such as
              <em>
               A Charlie Brown Christmas
              </em>
              and
              <em>
               Happy Thanksgiving Charlie Brown
              </em>
              .
             </p>
             <div>
              <div>
               The comic strip was created by Charles M. Schulz and was considered to be the example that all other childrens comic strips went by.
              </div>
              <div>
               <em>
                Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown
               </em>
               first came out in 1975 and has been remastered and re-digitized and can be purchased at
               <a href="http://Amazon.com">
                Amazon.com
               </a>
               .
              </div>
              <div>
              </div>
              <div>
               <em>
                Youre A Good Man Charlie Brown
               </em>
               is a fantastic Broadway musical and is a favorite play to be produced in professional and academic venues.
              </div>
              <div>
               This is also a great theme for a fun Valentines Day party for kids of all ages!
              </div>
              <div>
              </div>
              <div>
               Step and Repeat LA can help customize a great media wall to set off your theme beautifully.
              </div>
              <div>
              </div>
              <div>
               You can Google and find the best places for party themed decorations along with special Peanuts valentines cards and toys. Special Peanuts cakes and cupcakes will put smiles on your guests faces and you can even find the great jazzy music that is used in all the films!
              </div>
              <div>
              </div>
              <div>
               Costumes can also be found and would be a great addition to all those photos you are going to take in front of your media wall!
              </div>
              <div>
               Have a very happy Charlie Brown Valentines Day!
              </div>
             </div>
