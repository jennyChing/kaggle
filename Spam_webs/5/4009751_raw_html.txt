       <p>
        A few weeks ago I wrote
        <a href="http://landlopers.com/2015/01/26/40-before-40" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          My Personal 40 Before 40 Challenge
         </strong>
        </a>
        , which I published on my 39
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        birthday. The idea is to set a variety of different goals, both mundane and ambitious, to achieve before I turn 40. Its a little bit about facing fears, exploring more of the world and learning life skills I probably should have mastered well before now. Im thrilled to say that many of them are planned to be completed, including one Im particularly excited about:
        <strong>
         #22 Go to Machu Picchu
        </strong>
        . This has been on my travel bucket list for a very long time and thanks to Intrepid Travel not only am I going, but you can too! Intrepid Travel is sponsoring this adventure, but as always that has no impact on my thoughts and opinions, probably to their chagrin.
       </p>
       <p>
        Earlier this year
        <a href="http://www.intrepidtravel.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.intrepidtravel.com', 'Intrepid Travel']);" target="_blank">
         Intrepid Travel
        </a>
        , a small group adventure travel company, started a special promotion designed to help people everywhere conquer their own personal challenge list. With an endless number of challenges on offer  from conquering Everest Base Camp to eating a spicy road-side soup in Asia or yes, traveling to Machu Picchu  Intrepid has an experience for everyone this year. And to help lock in that commitment to a new challenge, any Intrepid adventure can be secured for a
        <a href="http://www.intrepidtravel.com/us/dollar-deposit" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.intrepidtravel.com/us/dollar-deposit', '$1 deposit']);" target="_blank">
         $1 deposit
        </a>
        , if reserved by 28 February 2015. This is an amazing opportunity and the challenge can be anything, from the extreme to the more low key but what is important is that you use the opportunity to take that leap and check off another item on your personal challenge list.
       </p>
       <p>
        Ill be doing just that in June when I join Intrepid Travel on a trip to Peru and finally visit the legendary Incan site perched amongst the clouds. I added this to my personal challenge list for a couple of reasons. As a history geek, Ive long wanted to see this famous site for myself for as long as I can remember. Just like seeing the pyramids in person was the culmination of years of pining, so is this. But I also want to see more of Peru, learn a little about the country and maybe push my comfort zone just a tiny bit more. Thats what travel is all about and so when Intrepid Travel invited me to partner with them on this, the answer was an immediate YES.
       </p>
       <p>
        Im traveling with Intrepid Travel because I like their mission and ethos as a company and always have. Theyre a business of course, but their efforts in giving back to local communities and ensuring that their trips are responsible and sustainable have been revolutionary in the travel world. So its with a clean conscience and a happy heart that I will join them for the trek to Machu Picchu in June.
       </p>
       <p>
        You can join me on this extraordinary trip or go on any number of others, all for an easy $1 deposit if you book before February 28
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        . Theres no excuse, stop telling yourself that youll do it someday and take the leap now and start on the road to ticking off those challenges while you can.
       </p>
