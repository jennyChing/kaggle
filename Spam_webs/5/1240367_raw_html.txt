            <header>
             <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
              Police Found This Famous Clown While Arresting A Devious Criminal
             </h1>
             <div class="meta-info">
              <ul class="td-category">
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://soshway.com/category/amusementpark/">
                 amusementpark
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://soshway.com/category/crime/">
                 crime
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://soshway.com/category/criminals/">
                 criminals
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="entry-category">
                <a href="http://soshway.com/category/police/">
                 police
                </a>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <time class="entry-date updated td-visibility-hidden" datetime="2015-02-23T20:49:05+00:00" itemprop="dateCreated">
               Feb 23, 2015
              </time>
              <meta content="UserComments:0" itemprop="interactionCount"/>
              <div class="entry-comments-views">
              </div>
             </div>
            </header>
            <div class="td-post-featured-image">
             <a data-caption="" href="http://i1.wp.com/soshway.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Police-Found-This-Famous-Clown-While-Arresting-A-Devious-Criminal-2.jpg?resize=580%2C327">
              <img alt="" class="entry-thumb td-modal-image" height="" itemprop="image" src="http://i1.wp.com/soshway.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Police-Found-This-Famous-Clown-While-Arresting-A-Devious-Criminal-2.jpg?w=700" title="Police-Found-This-Famous-Clown-While-Arresting-A-Devious-Criminal-2.jpg" width=""/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="td-post-text-content">
             <p>
              Tim is an aspiring announcer for the Harlem Globetrotters.
             </p>
             <p>
              Joyland, a small but cheerful amusement park in Witchita, Kansas, is a source of nostalgia for those who grew up in the town. Although it closed down in 2004, those who went as a child remember the Whackey Shack, a wooden roller coaster with a clown named Louie, who regaled visitors with his organ playing.
             </p>
             <p>
              But in 2005, the clown went missing. Its disappearance wasnt reported until 2010, so the police believed there was no way to track down the beloved mascot. They didnt think so, that is, until they found Louie in the home of the worst kind of criminal.
             </p>
             <h2>
              Louie had been missing for a decade, but then he was found while police were searching the home of Damian Mayes.
             </h2>
             <p>
              <img alt="Police Found This Famous Clown While Arresting A Devious Criminal 0" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/soshway.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Police-Found-This-Famous-Clown-While-Arresting-A-Devious-Criminal-0.jpg?w=700"/>
             </p>
             <div class="via_source">
              <a href="http://ksn.com/2015/02/19/louie-the-joyland-clown-found-in-sex-offenders-home/" target="_blank">
               ksn
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              Mayes is a 39-year-old who recently was convicted for aggravated indecent liberties with a child and aggravated criminal sodomy.
             </p>
             <h2>
              He was an organ repair man for the park and met Louie while fixing the clowns favorite instrument. When the park closed in 2004, Mayes must have come back for him.
             </h2>
             <p>
              <img alt="Police Found This Famous Clown While Arresting A Devious Criminal 1" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/soshway.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Police-Found-This-Famous-Clown-While-Arresting-A-Devious-Criminal-1.jpg?w=700"/>
             </p>
             <div class="via_source">
              <a href="http://ksn.com/2015/02/19/louie-the-joyland-clown-found-in-sex-offenders-home/" target="_blank">
               ksn
              </a>
             </div>
             <h2>
              Police took special interest in the case of Louie since they all remember him from their childhoods. They were happy to return him to Joyland, which has until recently been the clowns home for 50 years.
             </h2>
             <p>
              <img alt="Police Found This Famous Clown While Arresting A Devious Criminal 2" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/soshway.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Police-Found-This-Famous-Clown-While-Arresting-A-Devious-Criminal-2.jpg?w=700"/>
             </p>
             <div class="via_source">
              <a href="http://ksn.com/2015/02/19/louie-the-joyland-clown-found-in-sex-offenders-home/" target="_blank">
               ksn
              </a>
             </div>
             <p>
              From a bright amusement park to a dreary basement of a convicted sexual deviant
             </p>
             <p>
              Its always nice when a musician makes a comeback. Lets hope Louie will use this press momentum to get Joyland back open. Fingers crossed!
             </p>
