        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Vices: Four Loko For A Cheap Buzz
        </h1>
        <div style="float:left;margin-right:10px">
         <!--?php echo get_avatar( get_the_author_email(), '35', 'http://gravatar.com/avatar/4ee704aed9f08506e3c881fd8fba2a43/?s=120&amp;d=mm' ); ?-->
        </div>
        <a href="http://nyulocal.com/author/luis/" rel="author" title="Posts by Luis Paez-Pumar">
         Luis Paez-Pumar
        </a>
        <div class="author-meta">
         on 23 September, 2010
        </div>
        <div style="clear:both">
        </div>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://nyulocal.com/entertainment/2010/09/23/vices-four-loko-for-a-cheap-buzz/attachment/3799880578_aea1425491/" rel="attachment wp-att-32880">
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-32880" height="150" src="http://nyulocal.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/3799880578_aea1425491-150x150.jpg" title="Luis Paez-Pumar" width="150"/>
         </a>
         I was at a sketchy Brooklyn basement party and the keg died. A short walk later, me and a friend were at a deli with around 5 dollars between us (we had gone to the keggar partly because of this fact). A quick glance at the fridge revealed that there was a 2-for-1 deal on Four Loko, 2 dollars per can. Yes, we got 4. About an hour later, all 4 cans downed, we were done. Skipped the tipsy stage, and went straight into shit-faced.
        </p>
        <p>
         Ill skip to the next morning. Have you ever thrown up blue? This is not an isolated event, either. It should say on the label Will Give You Worst Hangover, because the next morning will feel like the entire world is yelling at you. Of course, Four Loko will also make you do some hilarious (-ly stupid) things;
         <a href="http://nyulocal.com/on-campus/2010/09/08/drunk-sober-high-at-big-bois-mystery-concert/">
          read the Drunk part for an explanation
         </a>
         .
         <span id="more-32854">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         But weve all been there. We go to one of the most expensive colleges in the world, and as a result, we are always broke. And with Four Loko, you get energy,
         <em>
          and
         </em>
         you get drunk! What could go wrong? Well, in order to mask the demon juice within its container, Four Loko spikes its drinks with a lot of sugar. Numerically speaking, that means
         <a href="http://dailyburn.com/nutrition/four_loko_approximation_calories">
          60g in a 23.5 oz can
         </a>
         . The result is a vile concoction that is proof that God hates poor college students desperate for a buzz.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its not enough to scare me off from Four Loko though. I just dont have money for a fancy Budweiser. I need a cheap, easy buzz. Of course, there are other options. For instance:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <del datetime="2010-09-23T04:50:18+00:00">
           Two
          </del>
          <del datetime="2010-09-23T04:50:18+00:00">
           Three
          </del>
          Four-Buck-Chuck: Trader Joes cheap and not all that awful tasting, a bottle of this will get the job done. Unfortunately, you can only get them at TJs (the line!), not your convenient, every day corner deli.
         </li>
         <li>
          40s  This is probably the next most viable option to 4L. But its heavier and harder to conceal. And props to anyone who can brave Olde English.
         </li>
         <li>
          PBR  They go for 6 packs for under 7 dollars if you look in the right place, like Brooklyn, and tastes OK. But the alcohol percentage doesnt come near that of Four Loko.
         </li>
         <li>
          Water  I mean, I guess could get drunk another night. Who am I kidding?
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Its quite a money-strapping predicament. Also, this video exists:
         <br/>
         <object classid="clsid:d27cdb6e-ae6d-11cf-96b8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0" height="385" width="480">
          <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
          </param>
          <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
          </param>
          <param name="src" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/KVWWre8tn_U?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US">
          </param>
          <param name="allowfullscreen" value="true">
          </param>
          <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="385" src="http://www.youtube.com/v/KVWWre8tn_U?fs=1&amp;hl=en_US" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="480">
          </embed>
         </object>
         <br/>
         So, basically, Im doomed. And in need of a job.
        </p>
