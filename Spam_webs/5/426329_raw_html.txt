         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          When it comes to dental care, there are many aspects that you need to consider. Without proper planning and oral care routine, you may end up with a mouth filled with cavities. Here are the most effective dental care tips by
          <a href="http://www.monarchsmiles.com/">
           Monarch Smiles, a dental clinic in Vienna
          </a>
          , to help you keep your teeth and gums healthy.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Make sure you are adequately caring for your teeth and gums. Brush and floss your teeth twice a day, and use a good quality mouthwash at least once a day. This is one of the most common ways to protect your teeth and gums. Brushing your teeth is the best method to keep your teeth clean; however, do not brush hard, as brushing too hard can trigger irritability, discomfort, gum problem, and eventually loose teeth. To avoid these problems, use a soft bristled toothbrush and make use of gentle, circular brushing movements while brushing. If you are not sure what oral care products are good for you, then better ask your family dentist to suggest some. Moreover, it can also help you prevent any troubles from ever occurring.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Eating nutritious and healthy food is another way to achieve or maintain good oral health. Stay away from foods that lead to cavities and other oral problems. Avoid food items such as pretzels, chips, candy, and other unhealthy foods, because these food items stick and remain on the surface of your teeth, causing unexpected and expensive oral problems. If you consume foods that are sticky, then it is advisable to brush and floss your teeth as soon as possible once you are done eating. Eat foods such as apples, raw carrots, and celery, that work as cleaning agents. These foods normally clean your mouth as you eat them.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Visiting your dentist at least twice a year is highly recommended. However, visit more than twice if your situation calls for it. There are countless advantages of periodic dental visits, and one of the most important is, it can help you avoid annoying oral issues from occurring. Moreover, frequent visits to a dental clinic will make you more comfortable with your dentist.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          If you experience pain or irritation when eating or drinking hot and cold food and beverages, then schedule a visit to your dentist to ascertain whether you have sensitive teeth or not. Sensitive teeth might be a measure of significant dental problems, such as nerve swelling, cavity, or nerve irritation, which you do not wish to ignore.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Consult your family dentist for more oral care tips and products. If you are living in Virginia, then visit Monarch Smiles for a routine dental checkup. To schedule an appointment online, please fill this contact form
          <a href="http://www.monarchsmiles.com/contact.php">
           http://www.monarchsmiles.com/contact.php
          </a>
         </p>
