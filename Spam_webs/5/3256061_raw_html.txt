        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Nobody can take the place of Superstar  , claims Surya 
        </h1>
        <p class="meta post-meta">
         Posted on
         <span class="updated">
          August 13, 2014
         </span>
         by
         <span class="vcard author">
          <a class="fn" href="http://eiway.com/author/eiway1/">

         <a href="http://eiway.com/category/entertainment/" rel="category tag">

       <div class="entry clearfix">
        <div style="text-align:justify">
         Suriya has opened his comment on Next Superstar issue . He claimed that it is tough and an improbable task to even thinks of anyone as superstar other than superstar Rajinikanth. Rajini Sir is the only superstar; nobody fits the bill as perfectly as he does, he said.
         <p>
          Suriyas Anjaan, directed by Lingusamy, is releasing all over the State and elsewhere this Friday on the eve of the countrys 67th Independence Day celebrations. The film is dubbed into Telugu as Sikandar and is released in more than 100 halls in Andhra Pradesh. In Kerala, also the Tamil version is released as Suriya has a sizeable number of fans in Gods Own Country.
         </p>
         <div class="content-ad">
          <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
          </script>
          <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-7306066045310316" data-ad-slot="2519687036" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
          </ins>
          <script>
           (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
          </script>
         </div>
         <p>
          Suriya was in Cochin to promote the film and addressed newsmen on the sidelines of an event. He was queried about the remarks made at the function held to mark the audio-launch of Anjaan a few days back in Chennai where he was hailed as Indias next superstar.
         </p>
         <p>
          Surya replied that , I have grown up watching and enjoying superstar Rajinis films. There is only one superstar in India and this is none other than Rajini Sir. It is very difficult to even visualize someone else in his place, he said and added that his films should never be compared with Rajini and Kamal Haasans films.
         </p>
