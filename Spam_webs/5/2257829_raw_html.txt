             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             This year we celebrated the Fourth on the Third, to accommodate the work schedule of two family members which we couldn't do without. As mentioned earlier, the house was duly festooned...
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef014e899905b0970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef014e899905b0970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
             </a>
             A fantastic strawberry pie by our niece Alison was consumed (among a lot of other items).
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef01538fa58d1c970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             And we spent the afternoon on the porch, watching the kids play in a goofy sprinkler thing, shoot each other (and us) with water pistols and set off the most benign "fireworks" I've ever seen.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef014e8999066f970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

            </p>

             </a>
             But seriously, today is all about the celebration of the birth of America, and lately I've been very conscious of the sacrifices of the men and women who step up to serve this country. Many pay the ultimate price for the freedom that we all enjoy. I salute and thank you and your families...
            </p>
            <p>

            Happy May Day ~ a new theme and an ebook for Mother's Day
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef014e8830200e970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0154320f90aa970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="float: left;">
              <img alt="A" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef0154320f90aa970c" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0154320f90aa970c-800wi" style="margin: 0px 5px 5px 0px;" title="A"/>

             A new theme for a new month... May Flowers! Everywhere I look I see Spring in bloom, so this one was super easy to decide on. Also, we're coming up on Mother's Day, which is definitely a "flower event" as we called them at my daughter's school. It is an all-girl's school with lots of lovely traditions, one of the nicest being frequent exchanges of flowers. In fact for graduation they wear long white dresses with hats crowned with gorgeous flowers. Graduations are in May... and weddings... May is like one long flower event!
            </p>
            <p>
             Well, my posting has lagged horribly in the last few weeks. For that I apologize, I've been absolutely
             <em>
              obsessed
             </em>
             with making interactive ebooks.
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/allsorts/2011/04/freebie-friday-my-top-secret-project-is-revealed.html" target="_blank">
              The first one was so much fun
             </a>
             , I immediately went to work on a second one! This one is based on a poem I wrote for Mother's Day, called
             <em>
              Who Loves a Little?
             </em>
             and features a floral theme, too!
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef01538e3cad3f970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef01538e3cad3f970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
             </a>
             It works on iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch, and hopefully the Android version will be ready very soon.If you've already downloaded the iTouch Books app, all you have to do is open it and look in the Books to Buy section and you'll see it. Oh, and both my ebooks are
             <em>
              free,
             </em>
             as is the iTouch Books app. Click ---&gt;
             <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/interactive-touch-books/id423957406?mt=8&amp;ls=1" target="_blank">
              here
             </a>
             &lt;--- if you haven't got the app yet.

             <em>
              fertile
             </em>
             ground for my imagination, so the ideas are
             <em>
              blossoming
             </em>
             ! I'll be back soon with lots of new things to share!
            </p>

             Cheers,

            <p>

        <h2 class="date-header">
         October 12, 2010
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-garden entry-category-out_and_about entry-author-jenny_b_harris entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504ccf9970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/allsorts/2010/10/new-england-part-i.html">
            New England Part I
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488243760970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pumpkins" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488243760970c" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488243760970c-800wi" title="Pumpkins"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Here we are, back from our little jaunt around New England. My grand plan to blog from the road didn't quite pan out as well as expected. There were just too many glitches with blogging from my iphone, from only being able to do one image per post, and the images displaying too large, to not getting a proper cell signal. Oh well, we're home now so I'll do my blogging from the comfort of my studio.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488244f37970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="White church2" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488244f37970c" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488244f37970c-800wi" title="White church2"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             We started from Hartford, CT. It was raining for the first couple of days, which made for a beautiful and somewhat meloncholy drive through the tiny villages along the back roads of western Connecticut and Massachusetts. We stopped somewhere in the Berkshires to snap photos of the white clapboard church above, which looked a moody grey in the drizzle. There was also the mandatory drive across a covered bridge in Kent...
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488248994970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Bridge" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488248994970c" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488248994970c-800wi" title="Bridge"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Still, it was cozy in our rented little suv, listening to eclectic choral music.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f5049f1f970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Garden gables2" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f5049f1f970b" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f5049f1f970b-800wi" title="Garden gables2"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Our first night was spent in Lenox, Mass at the
             <a href="http://www.gardengablesinn.com/" target="_blank">
              Garden Gables Inn
             </a>
             . It was a truly lovely inn, a classic New Engand bed and breakfast with beautiful period decor and gracious hosts. The grounds were lusciously landscaped, the color vivid in the rain. I took lots of photos while gracefully (I'm sure) balancing my little Cath Kidston umbrella overhead.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488247adb970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Berries" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488247adb970c" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef013488247adb970c-800wi" title="Berries"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504c9c2970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Cherries" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504c9c2970b" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504c9c2970b-800wi" title="Cherries"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Lots of pumpkins and berries and cherries everywhere you looked. A feast for the eyes! And breakfast was a maple-syrupy-fantastic feast as well, which we enjoyed while watching the chef wander out into the yard and harvest herbs from the little garden patch. So civilized and utterly charming.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504e0b0970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pumpkin" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504e0b0970b" src="http://allsorts.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c5c8953ef0133f504e0b0970b-800wi" title="Pumpkin"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Tomorrow, Part II... where it continues to rain but we don't mind!
            </p>
            <p>
             Cheers,
            </p>
            <p>
             Jenny
            </p>
