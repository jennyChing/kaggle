              Google has got a couple of apps for users to help with their messaging stuff. Hangouts and Messenger are those two apps and the later today got an update that pushes it up with a couple of extra facilities. Lets take a look at it.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.mobinerd.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/googlemessengerscreens.jpg">
               <img alt="Google Messenger" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-118" height="420" src="http://www.mobinerd.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/googlemessengerscreens-1024x598.jpg" width="720"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              Googles Messenger apps would help users with their SMS but not with online messaging. This SMS app alternative has the material design from Google. So in todays update Google brought in support for GIF images in Messenger which is almost all over the internet and this would really make your messaging fun and cool. There is also an extra thing that is added to the update and that is the Google Messenger widget that would allow you to add a widget to your home screen so that you wont need to go the app every time you need to send a message.
             </p>

              Personally I usually dont use SMS but still when I use it Google Messenger would be my choice. Maybe this update would tempt me to use this app a lot more than usual. What are your thoughts leave them below in the comments section
