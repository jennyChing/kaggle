        <h1 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
         That Time Louis C.K. Got Too High Off Vape Hits
        </h1>
        <a class="comments" href="http://www.marijuana.com/blog/news/2015/01/that-time-louis-c-k-got-too-high-off-vape-hits/#comments">
         <i class="fa fa-comments-o">
         </i>
         0
        </a>
       </header>
       <!-- .post-header -->
       <div class="post-meta">
        <span class="posted-by">
         By
         <span class="reviewer" itemprop="author">
          <a href="http://www.marijuana.com/blog/news/author/tangell/" rel="author" title="Posts by Tom Angell">
           Tom Angell
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="posted-on">
         on
         <span class="dtreviewed">
          <time class="value-datetime" datetime="2015-01-30T08:56:50+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           January 30, 2015
          </time>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="cats">
         <a href="http://www.marijuana.com/blog/news/category/entertainment/" rel="category tag">
          Entertainment
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.marijuana.com/blog/news/category/funny/" rel="category tag">

         </a>

         <a href="http://www.marijuana.com/blog/news/category/people/" rel="category tag">

         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="post-container cf">
        <div class="post-content-right">
         <div class="post-content description" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           Louis C.K. has previously
           <a href="http://thedailybanter.com/2014/01/the-best-of-louis-ck-and-jerry-seinfeld/">
            admitted
           </a>
           that going to see 3D IMAX movies whilehigh on marijuanais his favorite thing in life. But in a
           <a href="https://louisck.net/purchase/live-at-the-comedy-store">
            new stand-up special
           </a>
           , the comedian says hes not a huge fan of the vaporizationtrend thats sweeping the cannabis community. Thats because he dabbled in vaping one night and got a little too high.
          </p>
          <p>
           Im a little worried, honestly, by the young people with the vaping, says C.K.in his classic crabby fashion. They just stand on the corner and puff with the fuckin thing.
          </p>
          <p>
           But C.K.figured hed at least give puffing on vapors atry before he criticizes it too much.
          </p>
          <p>
           I have a younger friend who vapes, and she told me its just to kinda like calm down. Shes like, I barely feel it. Sometimes it helps me sleep. So one night were hanging out and Im like, Im gonnago home and go to sleep so maybe Ill just try it. So I took one little tiny hit.
          </p>
          <p>
           And thats where things went wrong.
          </p>
          <p>
           I was insane. I was completely insane. I was in my house, I couldnt even walk past awindow. I had to go under the windows because I was afraid of the inside of my own brain. So I started texting people, just texting, Im so high. Im so fuckin high.'
          </p>
          <p>
           Thats embarrassing enough, but it gets worse. Much worse.
          </p>
          <p>
           I text another friend, Im so high I wanna suck the pot jizz outta my own dick and get higher. Thats what I wrote. Im not bragging, Im just telling you what I wrote. And I sent it.
          </p>
          <p>
           But would you believe it gets even worse?
          </p>
          <p>
           And then later I looked at my texts and I was like, I think I might have sent that to my 12-year-old daughter. I think its possible.'
          </p>
          <p>
           Uh-oh.
          </p>
          <p>
           Luckily, though, C.K.didnt actually send that outrageous message to his own daughter.
          </p>
          <p>
           But I too easily could have. Now that my kids are in my phone, there needs to be some very reliable firewall that says, These numbers are much harder to text. That you cant just  whoops traumatize her irreversibly.
          </p>
          <p>
           Phew. Hopefully, thats a lesson learned, Louis. Dont vape and text.
          </p>
          <p>
           C.K. relays the stoned saga as part of his new special
           <a href="https://louisck.net/purchase/live-at-the-comedy-store">
            Live at the Comedy Store
           </a>
           , which he released this week on his website. Itsavailable for download andstreaming for the low price of just $5.
          </p>
          <p>
           And that one story isnt all C.K. has to say about getting high during the new routine.
          </p>
          <p>
           I dont do drugs. I never do drugs. Never. So that when I do, theyre way more fun, he quips.
          </p>
          <p>
           To me, thats my best advice about drugs.If you want to enjoy drugs, never do them. Never. Because then when you do, theyre actually fun.
          </p>
          <p>
           Maybe he hasa point there. Then again, if C.K. vaped just a little more often he might build up a working tolerance and be less likely to indiscriminatelysend texts about pot jizz after taking a single puff.
          </p>
