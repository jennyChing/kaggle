         <h4>
          <strong>
           #Sedan type Cars
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          It is the car type that we found around us every time any where, well sedan is a passenger car that has two rows of seats and adequate passenger space in the rear compartment for adult passengers.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <a href="http://shoutmycar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2013-malibu-model-overview-byo-cnt-well-1-829x13-01R1.png">

         </p>

          <a href="http://shoutmycar.com/2015-porsche-panamera-turbo-s-car-review-price-and-specifications/" title="2015 Porsche Panamera Turbo S Car Review, Price and Specifications">

          <h4>
           <strong>
            #MUVs type cars
           </strong>
          </h4>
          <p>
           The full form of MUV is Mutli-utility-vehicleis a type of automobile similar in shape to a van that is designed larger than sedan, hatchback or a station wagon, for maximum interior room and personal use.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <a href="http://shoutmycar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/muvs.jpg">

          </p>

           #Coupes type Cars
          </h4>

           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://shoutmycar.com/2015-nissan-gt-r-nismo-review-price-and-specification/" title="2015 Nissan GT-R Nismo Review, Price and Specification">
            Nissan GT-R
           </a>
          </p>
          <h4>
           <strong>
            #HatchBacks type Cars
           </strong>
          </h4>
          <p>
           These are a small cars that have passenger cabin with an integrated cargo space. HatchBacks car have commonly has two rows of seat with the rear seat able to fold according to the need to make more space and rear window opens upward.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://shoutmycar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Types-of-Cars-HatchBack.png">
            <img alt="Types of Cars" class="aligncenter wp-image-114" height="186" src="http://shoutmycar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Types-of-Cars-HatchBack.png" title="Hatch back" width="368"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           These car occupy less parking area and are perfect for city use. Large popularity and need of HatchBacks car has encouraged the Manufacturer to make Luxury Hatchbacks cars. You can see mere
           <a href="https://www.google.co.in/search?hl=en&amp;site=imghp&amp;tbm=isch&amp;source=hp&amp;biw=1453&amp;bih=888&amp;q=coupes+type+car&amp;oq=coupes+type+car&amp;gs_l=img.3...946.9043.0.9323.15.9.0.6.0.0.176.1132.1j8.9.0....0...1ac.1.22.img..7.8.955.lsHRHSNu4JI#hl=en&amp;site=imghp&amp;tbm=isch&amp;sa=1&amp;q=HatchBacks&amp;oq=HatchBacks&amp;gs_l=img.3..0l8j0i5l2.7028.7251.2.7902.2.2.0.0.0.0.181.321.0j2.2.0....0...1c.1.22.img..0.2.321.snjWIDeyDgg&amp;bav=on.2,or.r_cp.r_qf.&amp;bvm=bv.49641647%2Cd.bmk%2Cpv.xjs.s.en_US.NyLNrjc7wJY.O&amp;fp=b6ea4c915a3699d5&amp;biw=1453&amp;bih=888" target="_blank" title="Hatchback Cars">
            HatchBack type cars here.
           </a>
          </p>
          <h4>
           <strong>
            #State Wagon type cars
           </strong>
          </h4>
          <p>
           There cars are just a extended versions of HatchBcks cars, according to
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wagon_(car)">
            Wikipedia
           </a>
           an automobile with one or more rows of folding or removable seats behind the driver and no luggage compartment but an area behind the seats into which suitcases, parcels, etc., can be loaded through a tailgate.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1046" style="width: 461px">
           <a href="http://shoutmycar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/BMW-328i-state-wagon.jpg">
            <img alt="types of cars BMW 328i state wagon" class=" wp-image-1046" height="182" src="http://shoutmycar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/BMW-328i-state-wagon.jpg" width="451"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            BMW 328i State Wagon
           </p>
