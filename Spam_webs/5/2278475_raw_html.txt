         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
           <img alt="" height="450" src="../../../img/upload/top10trance2014.jpg" style="float: right; margin-left: 10px;" width="600"/>
           It was a big year in trance. The same could be said about any of the past ten years, for that matter. No matter how quickly trends come and go in the mainstream and underground electronic worlds, the staying power of trance is undeniable. It's still one of the most powerful genres on a global scale, and continues to dominate the top line of international music festivals in Europe, Asia, and South America.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
           Another wild thing about the genre, is that the best artists producing trance tend to remain right there at the top of the heap. There are certainly hot, young artists who come in and make a splash, but the reigning champions perpetuate their dynasties as those same young acts come and go.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
           In our top tier of trance from 2014, there are some new acts, but also some of those old guard mainstays who continue to craft these emotionally rich soundscapes for all of us to enjoy. We hope you take a listen to all of them.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt; color: #0000ff;">
           10. Andrew Rayel - One in a Million ft Jonathan Mendelsohn
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
           Fall in love over and over to this bright, shining tune from
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="../../../artists/andrew-rayel">
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">
              <strong>
               Andrew Rayel
              </strong>
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           . The vocals of
           <strong>
            Johnathan Mendelson
           </strong>
           reach out and grab you on one of the top
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="../../../labels/armada-music">
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">
              <strong>
               Armada Music
              </strong>
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           releases of the year.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>

           9. Gaia - Empire of Hearts
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
           The
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="../../../artists/armin-van-buuren">
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">
              <strong>
               Armin van Buuren

             </span>

           </span>
           side project
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="../../../artists/gaia">
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">
              <strong>
               Gaia
              </strong>
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           took elements of hardstyle and big room to craft "Empire of Hearts" just in time for another spectacular set of Ultra Music Festival performances back in spring 2014.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">

           8. Cosmic Gate - Sparks After Sunset ft Sarah Lynn
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">
           Veteran German duo
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="../../../artists/cosmic-gate">
             <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">
              <strong>
               Cosmic Gate
              </strong>
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           continue to release big track after big track, touring the world with their heartstring-pulling, emotional trance, which grips you tight and doesn't let go.
           <strong>
