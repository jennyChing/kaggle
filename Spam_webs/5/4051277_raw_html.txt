      Perfect for fans of... Really?!
     </h2>
     <div class="journal_entry">

      by Girly Reads

       </p>

        Today Im wrong.
       </p>
       <p>
        With an opulent cast of characters, Anna-Lou Weatherley writes dastardly villains we love to hate and beautiful women wed love to emulate, especially when they kick-ass in their towering Louboutin heels!
       </p>
       <p>
        Is she perfect for fans of Tasmina Perry? Yes, this fan thinks so. Her books are fabulous. An excellent way to escape from life, and from Christmas if youre already over that too!
