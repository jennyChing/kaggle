             <h2 class="post-title semibold">
              10 Ways to Not Waste Your Summer as a Creative
             </h2>
            </div>
            <p>
             Right about now you may be sitting around your home in the clothes you woke up in, while finishing your fifth episode of an all day marathon of
             <em>
              House
             </em>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             Fear not, nows the perfect time to create a plan to make your summer productive, rather than endless marathons on Netflix, playing a non-stop chauffeur for the kids or wasting three months of your year youll never get back. With a solid plan and new ideas to stay creative, when summers over and things get back to normal, youll be further ahead than those who fell victim to those time-killing, wasteful distractions.
            </p>
            <p>
             Weve compiled a list of 10 ways to not waste your summer and make it a success  for yourself, your career and even your community. Pick one, pick them all, just dont waste your summer.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <h3>
             1. Connect with others online to.
            </h3>
            <p>
             The world is a big place and the internet has made the global community a powerful resource like never before. Take advantage of the online community to connect with people with different perspectives, share ideas that feed off each other or
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/get-good-feedback-work/%20">
              get feedback
             </a>
             on your projects from others with the same interests. Websites such as the
             <a href="http://www.digitaltutors.com/forum/forum.php">
              Digital-Tutors forum
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="https://www.behance.net/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.behance.net/', 'Behance']);" target="_blank">
              Behance
             </a>
             and
             <a href="http://www.deviantart.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.deviantart.com/', 'DeviantArt']);" target="_blank">
              DeviantArt
             </a>
             are great for seeing work from other people while gaining constructive feedback for your own projects.
            </p>
            <p>
             Its amazing whats out there in the online creative world and what opportunities can open up when you network and interact with others. Start small by posting work or go all in and start a project with others.
            </p>
            <h3>
             2. Learn a new software
            </h3>
            <p>
             <img alt="02-NewSoftware" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24069" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.digitaltutors.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/02-NewSoftware.png"/>
             As a digital artist or designer, the more software you can learn the more possibilities there are to bring your ideas to life  and better represent your true vision for the project. Learning software you are unfamiliar with will allow for you to become more diverse in your skills and even become faster in your workflow.
            </p>
            <p>
             Learning a new software doesnt have to be as daunting as it might seem and in most cases you can even try the app for free. If you need help jumping in, make sure to explore our post on
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/8-tips-mastering-new-3d-application-shortest-amount-time/%20">
              8 Tips for Mastering a New Software
             </a>
             and our resource for
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/resources-for-accessing-free-creative-software/">
              finding free trials
             </a>
             for creative software.
            </p>
            <h3>
             3. Cancel your cable
            </h3>
            <p>
             <img alt="03-CancelCable" class="alignnone wp-image-24073 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/blog.digitaltutors.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/03-CancelCable.png?resize=800%2C450"/>
             Youve thought about it before, now do it. Cancel your cable subscription for the summer (OK, or wait until after the World Cup) and youll not only save money, but also cut back on unnecessary distractions. Even putting re-runs of your favorite show on in the background while you work is easy cause for distraction.
            </p>
            <p>
             In survey conducted by
             <a href="http://www.nielsen.com/us/en/reports/2013/a-look-across-media-the-cross-platform-report-q3-2013.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.nielsen.com/us/en/reports/2013/a-look-across-media-the-cross-platform-report-q3-2013.html', 'Nielson']);" target="_blank">
              Nielson
             </a>
             , yes we found legit stats to back this problem up, Americans watch an average of 35.1 hours of television per week. Thats 1,825.2 hours of television a year! While that may not represent our global usage, distractions can take many forms. Its not that you dont deserve your down time to relax, but just imagine what you could get done with all of this extra free time in your life. How you spend this new-found, extra time may surprise you.
            </p>
            <p>
             Use this summer as a time to test yourself. If you experience television withdrawals or dont see any productive changes, you can always go back, but we think youll like the results.
            </p>
            <h3>
             4. Read and stay up to date on events in your field
            </h3>
            <p>
             <img alt="04-Reading" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24074" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/blog.digitaltutors.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/04-Reading.png"/>
             For most of your life, parents, teachers and even PSAs have encouraged you to read, read, read. Yes, this may be clichd, but its undoubtedly true as it keeps you in the know on what to expect in your industry, as well as whats going on in the world outside of your bubble.
            </p>
            <p>
             Knowing current industry events will keep you up to date on latest, cutting-edge technology, software updates and on any other relevant industry news you need to know. There are many resources at your disposal for you to find this information, one of those being, you guessed it, right here on the
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/">
              Digital-Tutors blog
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             Another great resource and RSS reader,
             <a href="http://feedly.com/#discover" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://feedly.com/#discover', 'Feedly']);" target="_blank">
              Feedly
             </a>
             , allows for you to choose a topic and then gives you suggestions on which sites or blogs to follow with the best content. Build a list of blogs and inspirational sites, grow it and use it to keep up to date and ideas flowing.
            </p>
            <h3>
             5. Develop or update your portfolio or demo reel
            </h3>
            <p>
             First off, if you dont have a portfolio use one of the links below to create one. Your portfolio or reel is how you showcase creative work for everyone to see, including potential employers. Thats why it is imperative that your portfolio or reel is always in the best possible condition.
            </p>
            <p>
             A portfolio or reel should consist of only projects that are 100 percent polished, contain no mistakes and are relevant. Remember to fill your portfolio with quality, not quantity and that youre only as strong as the weakest piece you put in there. Use part of your summer to give your portfolio or reel the love it deserves, whether you want to get your first job after graduation or youre ready to move jobs.
            </p>
            <p>
             Check out more Digital-Tutors tips for creating an outstanding
             <a href="http://www.digitaltutors.com/11/portfolio.php">
              portfolio
             </a>
             or
             <a href="http://www.digitaltutors.com/11/demoreel.php">
              demo reel
             </a>
             , plus more:
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/tips-to-make-your-motion-graphics-work-portfolio-ready/">
              Tips to Make Your Motion Graphics Work Portfolio-Ready
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/demo-reel-and-portfolio-tips-from-game-industry-professionals/">
              Demo Reel and Portfolio Tips From Game Industry Professionals
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Online portfolio resources:
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.wordpress.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.wordpress.com/', 'www.wordpress.com']);" target="_blank">
              www.wordpress.com
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.visualcv.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.visualcv.com/', 'www.visualcv.com']);" target="_blank">
              www.visualcv.com
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.behance.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.behance.com/', 'www.behance.com']);" target="_blank">
              www.behance.com
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.dunked.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.dunked.com/', 'www.dunked.com']);" target="_blank">
              www.dunked.com
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.deviantart.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.deviantart.com/', 'www.deviantart.com']);" target="_blank">
              www.deviantart.com
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.portfoliobox.net/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.portfoliobox.net/', 'www.portfoliobox.net']);" target="_blank">
              www.portfoliobox.net
             </a>
            </p>
            <h3>
             6. Intern  youve got to start somewhere
            </h3>
            <p>
             Internships are one of the best ways to gain hands-on experience in a professional environment. You may have already started your summer break, but companies are likely still looking for interns. Openings for fall internships usually open up about this time too, so keep an eye out for them to make the most of your fall.
            </p>
            <p>
             If you get an internship, its not promised that you wont be asked to fetch coffee, file documents or complete a list of other mundane tasks. In addition, you may not receive a paycheck for your time. However, looking past unglamorous intern duties and lack of good, or any, pay, the overall experience and networking will be worthwhile and put you ahead of most of your peers.
            </p>
            <p>
             Explore sites like
             <a href="http://www.internships.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.internships.com/', 'Internships.com']);" target="_blank">
              Internships.com
             </a>
             and
             <a href="http://www.internmatch.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.internmatch.com/', 'Internmatch.com']);" target="_blank">
              Internmatch.com
             </a>
             to help you on you internship quest. Also, if there are specific companies you know you want to work for, visit the career sections of their websites.
            </p>
            <h3>
             7. Join/start a user group
            </h3>
            <p>
             <img alt="05-UserGroup" class="alignnone wp-image-24075 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.digitaltutors.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/05-UserGroup.png?resize=800%2C496"/>
             Another way to gain experience and meet people in your field is through user groups. User groups typically meet once a month to use and discuss the software or topic of their choice. User groups can be found in your area through a simple online search or if you cant find a group thats appealing to you, dont be afraid to start your own. Sites like
             <a href="http://www.meetup.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.meetup.com/', 'meetup']);" target="_blank">
              meetup
             </a>
             are a great place to find and organize your own group.
            </p>
            <p>
             Weve met some of our favorite people, made new friends and have even hired team members out of being a part user groups and events. Take thelead of a group and watch things start to happen!
            </p>
            <h3>
             8. Finish a project you have been putting off
            </h3>
            <p>
             Most creatives, if not all, have at least one project theyve started and somehow managed to never see it to completion. You began it with a ground-breaking idea, but for either lack of motivation or knowledge decided to put it to the back burner. Take some time out this summer to tackle one of those postponed projects.
            </p>
            <p>
             Check out this poston what could be
             <a href="http://blog.digitaltutors.com/5-things-keeping-you-from-finishing-your-creative-project/">
              preventing you from finishing your creative projects
             </a>
             and how to overcome them. Find that one project that keeps visiting you while trying to fall asleep or the project youve wanted to do for someone else  trust us, theyll love it.
            </p>
            <h3>
             9. Donate your time and creative skills to a non-profit
            </h3>
            <p>
             <img alt="06-NonProfit" class="alignnone wp-image-24076 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.digitaltutors.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/06-NonProfit.png?resize=800%2C421"/>
             As a creative, you have skills that are valuable not only to companies, but also to charity organizations. Whether you use your graphic design skills to create fliers for a charity 5K or use your motion graphics skills to create a promotional video for the launch of a new, local non-profit. Your effort will go a long way and do a lot of good for the community.
            </p>
            <p>
             Not only do you have the opportunity to give back and be a part of something bigger than yourself, but you also get to practice your skills and possibly learn new ones. Plus, everything you create during this time can also be showcased in your portfolio as well as the opportunity to network with professionals.
            </p>
            <h3>
             10. Develop productive, personal habits
            </h3>
            <p>
             We are surrounded by distractions, but even small changes in personal habits can lead to considerable improvement in productivity. One of the first steps you can take is to clean your workspace. A distraction-free and organized space is a clutter-free mind, a key for any creative. Having unnecessary stuff in your surroundings can lead to distractions in your work and cause a higher level of stress if those distractions lead to bigger problems.
            </p>
            <p>
             Cleaning your workspace can also mean cleaning your computer. Its time to trash all of those old files, saved documents and outdated downloads youll never open again. All of these take up space on your computer and add to the list of things preventing you from doing your work and getting better.
            </p>
            <p>
             You probably already missed your spring cleaning opportunity, so redeem yourself and defeat the evil dust bunnies behind your machine.
            </p>
            <p>
             Another key to your productivity is adequate sleep, so consider not staying up all night playing online games of FIFA or going out every night. Its difficult to wake up at a decent hour and get the most out of your day if you cant focus due to lack of sleep  no matter how much coffee you can pump into your system. Work to develop this habit now, because it will pay off tenfold when you work a nine-to-five job in the future or give you that energy youve been lacking at work now.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Whats next?
            </h3>
            <p>
             Pick one, pick two or pick them all  and remember to also make time for fun during your summer. No other time in your life will you have this much time to try to improve yourself around your own schedule.
            </p>
            <p>
             A few years from now, you dont want to look back and regret all of those hours spent watching re-runs of Game of Thrones when you could have been learning a software or joining a user group and meeting people who truly share the same passions you do.
            </p>
            <p>
             Now go out, you creative genius, and make the most of your summer!
            </p>

      <script type="text/javascript">
       jQuery(document).ready(function( $ ) {var Header = {
		
	sticky_nav_top : 0,
	scroll_top : 0,
	mobile_width : 767,
	menu_class_when_opened : "open",
	search_string : "",
	search_suggested_keywords : [],
		
	Init : function() 
	{		
		this.SetVariables();
		this.BindElements();
		this.StickyNavigation();
	},

	SetVariables : function() 
	{
		this.sticky_nav_top = $('.nav').offset().top;
		
		/*if (this.IsOnVideoPlayerPage())
			this.menu_class_when_opened = "open push";
		else
			this.menu_class_when_opened = "open";*/
	},
		
	BindElements : function() 
	{
		$(window).scroll(function() {
			Header.StickyNavigation();
		});		
		
		$('ul.navbar-nav &gt; li').hoverIntent(Header.MenuHover);
		
		$('ul.navbar-nav &gt; li').click(function (e) {			
			if (Header.IsOnVideoPlayerPage() &amp;&amp; DTBrowserDetect.isIE.any())
				Header.HandleNavigationOnVideoPage($(this), e);
			else if ($(this).hasClass("search"))
				Header.HandleNavigationForSearch($(this), e);
			else
			{
				if ($(window).width() &lt;= Header.mobile_width)
					Header.HandleNavigationForMobile($(this), e);
			}						
		});
		
		$(document).on('keyup', '#txtHeaderSearchString', function(e){
			if (e.keyCode == 13)
				Header.DoSearch($(this));
			else
				Header.ShowSearchSuggestions($(this));
		});
		
		$("body").delegate(".dropdown-menu .search_keyword", 'click', function(e){
			Header.SearchFromSuggestedKeyword($(this).text());
		});
		
	},
	
	MenuHover: function (e)
	{
		if (Header.IsOnVideoPlayerPage() &amp;&amp; DTBrowserDetect.isIE.any())
			Header.HandleNavigationOnVideoPage($(this), e);
		else if ($(this).hasClass("search"))
			Header.HandleNavigationForSearch($(this), e);
		else
		{
			if ($(window).width() &gt; Header.mobile_width)
				Header.HandleNavigationForDesktop($(this));	
		}
	},
	
	StickyNavigation : function()
	{
		this.scroll_top = $(window).scrollTop();  
        
		if (this.scroll_top &gt; this.sticky_nav_top) 
		{
			if ($('.header').hasClass('dt_blog'))
				$('.header').addClass('sticky'); 
			else
				$('.navbar').addClass('sticky');  
		} 
		else
		{
			$('.navbar').removeClass('sticky');
			$('.header').removeClass('sticky');
		}
	},
	
	HandleNavigationForSearch : function(nav_element, e)
	{
		e.preventDefault();		
		if (e.type == "click")
		{
			if (e.target.nodeName.toLowerCase() != "input")
			{
				nav_element.toggleClass( this.menu_class_when_opened ).siblings().removeClass( this.menu_class_when_opened );	
				$("#txtHeaderSearchString").focus();
			}			
		}
	},
	
	// Menu Opens on Hover for Desktop
	HandleNavigationForDesktop : function(nav_element)
	{
		nav_element.toggleClass('open').siblings().removeClass('open');	
	},
	
	// Menu opens on click for Mobile
	HandleNavigationForMobile : function(nav_element, e)
	{
		e.preventDefault();
		var target_nodeName = e.target.nodeName.toLowerCase();
		
		if ($(e.target).hasClass("main_nav_element") || (target_nodeName == "span" &amp;&amp; $(e.target).closest("a").hasClass("main_nav_element")))
		{
			nav_element.toggleClass( this.menu_class_when_opened ).siblings().removeClass( this.menu_class_when_opened );
		}
		else if ($(e.target).attr("data-toggle") === undefined)
		{
			var url = "";
			if (target_nodeName == "a")
				url = $(e.target).attr('href');
			else if (target_nodeName == "span")
				url = $(e.target).closest("a").attr('href');
	
			if (url != '')
				window.location = url;			
		}
	},
	
	IsOnVideoPlayerPage : function()
	{
		if ($("#divCourseVideo").length &gt; 0 &amp;&amp; $("#divCourseVideo").is(":visible"))
			return true;
		else
			return false;		
	},
	
	HandleNavigationOnVideoPage : function(nav_element, e)
	{
		e.preventDefault();		
		if (e.type == "click")
		{
			if (e.target.nodeName.toLowerCase() != "input")
			{
				window.location = nav_element.find("a:first").attr("href");
			}			
		}
	},
	
	ShowSearchSuggestions : function(search_input_element)
	{
		this.search_string = search_input_element.val();
		
		if (this.search_string.length &gt; 0)
		{	
			if (this.search_string.length &gt; 2) 
			{
				if (window.location.hostname == "localhost")
				{
					$.getJSON("/11/gateway/training.php", {action: "GetSuggestedSearchKeywords", search_string: this.search_string}, function(json){
						$(".search_suggestion").empty();
