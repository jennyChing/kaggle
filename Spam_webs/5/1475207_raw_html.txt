       How To Enlarge Your Eyelashes
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="postmeta">

      <p>
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/wp-content/uploads/How-To-Enlarge-Your-Eyelashes.jpg">
        <img alt="Portrait of female eyes" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-519" height="187" src="http://www.lapizfashion.com/wp-content/uploads/How-To-Enlarge-Your-Eyelashes-300x187.jpg" title="How To Enlarge Your Eyelashes" width="300"/>
       </a>
       Every girl in this world wants to have long eyelashes. Long eyelashes are gift of nature. Long eyelashes dont only make your
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/5-ways-to-thicken-your-eyelashes/" target="_blank">
        eyes look greater
       </a>
       and prominent but also defend your eyes from danger. However, if you are not blessed with deep and long eyelashes, you dont have to trouble at all. There are some simple tips that you can follow for enlarging your eyelashes.
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Vaseline method
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Try Vaseline method. For this youve to take a mascara brush from an older bottle and wash it well with warm water. Now dip mascara brush in baby powder and slowly use brush on to your eyelashes. Rinse the brush later and dip it in vasleine. Repeat this technique for at leats five weeks and see the difference.
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Castor oil
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Castor oil is the best for expansion of eyelashes. It forms eyelashes to grow thicker and longer. You just have to dip your mascara brush in to castor oil and use it on your eyelashes on regular basis before going to bed. Wash it off soundly next morning. Do this experiment and relish long lashes. You can also use vegetable oil or olive oil in place of castor oil. Brush them with care to promote the
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/how-to-make-your-eyes-large-and-expressive/" target="_blank">
        growth of your eyelashes
       </a>
       .
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Lip balm
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Lip balm is another valuable remedy to get long lashes. Take a lip balm or chap stick and use it on your lashes to take longer and thicker lashes. Apply lip balm from inner corner to outer side. It is better to use it prior to going on bed and allow it for the whole night so that lip balm absorbs well. Dont forget to wear a silk sleeping mask when you go to bed. In the morning, wash lip balm with warm water. Make certain to use flavored lip balm as it is more appropriate than unflavored balm.
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Diet
       </strong>
       <br/>
       You must also look at your eating habits. You need to take appropriate diet to have longer eyelashes. Your most of appearance and outer look depends more on what you eat. You should consume healthy food specially green vegetables, fruits, minerals and vitamins. So to have long lashes better your diet and increase the use of fresh
       <a href="http://www.lapizfashion.com/how-to-make-your-eyes-large-and-expressive/" target="_blank">
        fruits and vegetables
       </a>
       .
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Eyelid massage
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Eyelid massage is an other alternative way for the growth of eyelashes. Wash your eyelashes daily with fresh water and massage them by the eyelashes and lash line. Use your fingertips for massaging your eyes.
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Stop applying eyelashes curler
       </strong>
       <br/>
       In order to give your eyelashes time to regrowth make certain to not apply eyelashes curler atleast for a month. Often the usage of hard instrumets like this exercise a brutal affect on your delicate texture of eyelashes. In severe case it can harm your lashes and even break them.
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Green tea
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Green tea also assists in growing eyelashes. You have to cook an unsweeten green tea and set it apart for som time until it cool well. For using it on your lashes, dip cotton ball in tea and use it to your lashes
       <br/>
       Whatever you apply to your lashes make certain this may not harm your lashes.
