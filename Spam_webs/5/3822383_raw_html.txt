        <h2 class="p1" style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <em>
           <img alt="Just Let Yourself be Wild Yogendra174:Flickr" class="size-full wp-image-140066" height="350" src="//goodmenproject.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Just-Let-Yourself-be-Wild-Yogendra174Flickr.jpg" width="588"/>
          </em>
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <h2 class="p1" style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <em>
           I am a firm believer that letting yourself bewild is the biggest step we can take toward diverse social tolerance.
          </em>
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          
         </strong>
         <strong>
          
         </strong>
         <strong>
          
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1">
         We lose the desire to restrict others if we dont contain and restrict ourselves.
        </h3>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Go a little wild, or go a lot
         </li>
         <li>
          Run downthe street
         </li>
         <li>
          Skip down the road
         </li>
         <li>
          Flail your arms around chaotically
         </li>
         <li>
          Dance in the park, in the fountain, in the crosswalk
         </li>
         <li>
          Make people smile with your smile as you gowild
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Let Go!
          </strong>
         </li>
         <li>
          Run, jump, and yellfor no reason
         </li>
         <li>
          Dont react, act
         </li>
         <li>
          Let whatever emotion comes be released through the movement
         </li>
         <li>
          Keep moving in a steady flow
         </li>
         <li>
          Pull yourself out of the normal routine. Spontaneityenlivens creativity and enlivens life!
         </li>
         <li>
          Run naked in the forest
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          When we let the physical, emotional, mental, vocal everything of ourentire beings  be caught in the moment of the moment, we can move as wefeel and express with our whole being.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          The release of stress whencomprehensively acting at a humans full range action is overwhelming in its ability to completely relax us.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          <strong>
           Let go!
          </strong>
          There is nothing to worry about. Let go with a smile, or a grin, and maybe the people around you just might join in.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          Let yourself out of your box. These boxes are not needed anymore. The containment and protection provided is minimal at best, and toxic to our minds and bodies at worst.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          We move and speak and think and feel and flow together in the chaotic symphony of dancing vibrational strings.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          Lets dance wild, no matter how off beat. wildcan be good 
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: right;">
         <span class="s1">
           no, wild
          <em>
           is
          </em>
          good! Wildfrees our spirits.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1">
         Lets go wild, it is OK.
        </h3>
