        Heavyweight Luis Ortiz Tests Positive for Steroids
       </h1>

        <img alt="box_ortiz_kayode1_1296x729" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-21437" height="168" src="http://www.badculture.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/box_ortiz_kayode1_1296x729-300x168.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        BOXING News
       </strong>
        According to reports to
       <a href="http://espn.go.com/boxing/story/_/id/11745451/heavyweight-luis-ortiz-tested-positive-steroid-wake-winning-interim-title" target="_blank">
        ESPN.com
       </a>
       , Luis King Kong Ortiz tested positive for the anabolic steroid nandrolone, a banned substance. The positive result comes from a sample provided to the Nevada State Athletic Commission before his September 11th fight against Lateef Kayoda. A win that earned Ortiz the interim WBA world heavyweight title.
      </p>
      <p>
       At the current time, Ortiz is on ice per the instructions of the NSAC until a formal hearing is conducted.
      </p>
      <p>
       In light of the positive test result, Ortiz potentially faces fines, additional suspensions, and a portion of his purse from the win turned over.
