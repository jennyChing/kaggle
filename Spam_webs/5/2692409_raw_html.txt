         Who can apply for cash advance payday loans?

       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Does the process take long?
        </strong>
        <br/>
        A customer typically receives approval within hours of applying. Since the money is wired directly into their checking or savings account, they can usually have access to your funds the very next day. However, if the day an applicant applies for it is a holiday, the amount is deposited in the borrowers bank account the very next working day.
