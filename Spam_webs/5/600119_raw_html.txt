         <p>
          If youre a regular follower of all the tech news around the world, or for this post if you at least follow everything that is happening around Facebook, there is a chance that youve read the news about some analyst talking about the clothing style of Mark Zuckerberg. He wears his hoodie everywhere and not everyone is pleased with that.
          <a href="http://www.betabrand.com/" target="_blank">
           Betabrand
          </a>
          , an online-only clothing company based in San Francisco, shows us how you could handle such problems in a creative way. Theyve designed an Exectutive Pinstripe Hoodie, which you can perfectly wear when you have to go to an important meeting. Over the last few days this hoodie started popping up everywhere and it came to my attention thanks to
          <a href="http://thenextweb.com/shareables/2012/05/14/this-executive-pinstripe-hoodie-is-perfect-for-any-facebook-ipo-fan/" target="_blank">
           The Next Web
          </a>
          . I think this is a perfect solution for those who dont like to wear suits (including myself) but need to wear something formal Business Casual. The
          <a href="http://www.betabrand.com/hoodies/executive-pinstripe-hoodie.html#ModelCitizen" target="_blank">
           executive pinstripe hoodie
          </a>
          will start shipping in June and will cost you $148.
         </p>
