           <p>
            <strong>
             Beautiful Fusion, a new brand of all-natural hair moisturizers and hair care products that have been created for natural hair care. Our products are formulated from natural oils and herbs in a handmade solution.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Atlanta, Georgia, USA,February 24, 2015 - (PressReleasePoint) -
            </strong>
            Beautiful Fusion provides a natural alternative to moisturizing scalp and hair. The products are handmade and the all-natural ingredients are rigorously chosen to promote optimal hair health and growth. The founder, Monique Nurses Biology and chemistry background have equipped her with the knowledge and tools needed to create natural products that cure, cares and caters to the hair.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Products that are currently available include Cake Batter Hair Butter priced at $15 for dry, damp, damaged, thin, thick, kinky, curly or relaxed hair. This moisturizer infused with leave-in conditioner can be used daily or several times a week to maintain desired moisture levels in the hair. Gold Rush (available at the same price) rejuvenates dry or damaged hair as well as invigorates, stimulates and moisturizes hair. Additionally, due to its anti-inflammatory properties, it keeps the scalp healthy while stimulating hair follicle growth. Gold Rush can be used as a hot oil treatment or to seal in moisture and provide extra moisture to the hair, especially for protective hairstyles such as braids or weave installments.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The extensive list of ingredients in these Beautiful Fusion products can be found here
            <a>
             http://beautifulfusionhair.com/about/
            </a>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Speaking about the inspiration behind Beautiful Fusion, Monique said, Beautiful Fusion was created from a need. Several years ago, I searched various stores looking for products that would work on my hair. I found some that semi-worked, but did not find that one product, that do it all product. After watching many DIY videos, I came up with a hair mix. Over time, people requested me to make my moisturizing butter and oil mixture for their personal use. I decided to bottle up my gold in a bottle and introduce it to the world and Beautiful Fusion was born.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The creation process of these products can take up to 12 months and offers the unique benefits of manufacturing ourselves, with complete control over the concept, ingredients, formulation and final product.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Currently, Beautiful Fusion products are available for purchase online at
            <a>
             http://beautifulfusionhair.com/
            </a>
            and at select salons in Georgia.
