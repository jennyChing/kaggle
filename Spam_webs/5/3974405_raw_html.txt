       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         So Im having trouble getting any work done because even the AC cant break todays heat.
        </p>
        <p>
         As such, I give you these two thoughts:
        </p>
        <p>
         1) I made this poster
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.redbubble.com/people/leeinlimbo/works/9072325-hot-enough">
          <img alt="Too Hot" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3858" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.clearvisionstudios.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Too-Hot.jpg?resize=518%2C800"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         at least two years ago, and none of you have bought it;
        </p>
        <p>
         and 2) I wrote this short story
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.smashwords.com/books/view/390753">
          <img alt="Winterlude" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3859" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.clearvisionstudios.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Winterlude.jpg?resize=640%2C324"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         in part for my wife, but also because I hate summer, much prefer winter, and wanted to write a story from Jeannie Kinnemans point of view.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im also listening to Brian Wilsons remake of SMiLE and reading about David Johansen and The New York Dolls.
        </p>
        <p>
         I plan on publishing a book some time today or tomorrow at latest. I expect you all to buy a copy. No excuses this time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thank you for reading. Talk to you soon.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lee.
        </p>
