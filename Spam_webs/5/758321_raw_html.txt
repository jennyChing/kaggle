       <h2>
        <a href="/beauty-tips/2346-brighten-up-your-tired-eyes-with-a-simple-make-up-trick.html">
         Brighten up your tired eyes with a simple make-up trick
        </a>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img _mce_src="/images/Greinar/1/9/istock_000004459261xsmall1.jpg" alt="" height="435" src="http://goodideasforyou.com/images/Greinar/1/9/istock_000004459261xsmall1.jpg" width="528"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        When you need a quick fix for tired eyes, forget the creams and  Botox.An easy alternative to greasy eye creams or expensive out-patient  services is a beauty item you may already have in your
        <span class="inline_link">
         make-up
        </span>
        bag.
       </p>
       <p>
        Draw a thick coat of white eyeliner on your lower inner rim of the  eyelid can be an amazingly dramatic look. Apply it before any other  makeup. Blink lots to get used to the feeling. Then apply your normal  eye makeup. Keeps your look fresh and your eyes will look hypnotic.
       </p>
