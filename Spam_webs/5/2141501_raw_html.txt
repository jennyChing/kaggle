       <h1 class="entry-title">
        My Spring Mantel &amp; What I Stole from My Mom-In-Law!
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-03-27T04:30:20+00:00">
         March 27, 2014
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://eclecticallyvintage.com/author/kelly/" rel="author">
           Kelly @ Eclectically Vintage
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Spring Mantel Decorating Ideas - this is stunning!  See how to make the garland eclecticallyvintage.com" class="aligncenter" height="848" src="http://eclecticallyvintage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/spring-mantel.jpg" title="Spring Mantel Decorating Ideas - this is stunning!  See how to make the garland eclecticallyvintage.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Remember when you were a kid and your parents would buy a case of soda and return the empty bottles to get 5 cents back?
        </p>
        <p>
         If I knew then what I know now, I would have hoarded all of those bottles away instead of returning them.
        </p>
        <p>
         So when I discovered these dusty, neglected glass beauties in my mom-in-laws basement, I had to rescue them (Im nice like that).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Spring mantel - WOW!  eclecticallyvintage.com" class="aligncenter" height="900" src="http://eclecticallyvintage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easter-Spring-Garland.jpg" title="Spring mantel - WOW!  eclecticallyvintage.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont they just scream spring?
        </p>
        <p>
         Or is it just me who hears voices from inanimate objects?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="This spring mantel is stunning!  Love the fresh colors eclecticallyvintage.com" class="aligncenter" height="900" src="http://eclecticallyvintage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easter-Decorating-Mantel.jpg" title="This spring mantel is stunning!  Love the fresh colors eclecticallyvintage.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Oh Easter egg garland that I made last year, how Ive missed you (see how I made it

          here
         </a>
         ).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Cute decoupage bunnies! eclecticallyvintage.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17631" height="900" src="http://eclecticallyvintage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easter-Decor.jpg" title="Cute decoupage bunnies! eclecticallyvintage.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Spring tulips eclecticallyvintage.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17636" height="900" src="http://eclecticallyvintage.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Spring-Tulips.jpg" title="Spring tulips eclecticallyvintage.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">

         Now if the snow would just melt so I can retire my mittens.
