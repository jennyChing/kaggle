            <div class="node-title">
             Green Leaf-Like Bug -
             <i>
              Microcentrum retinerve
             </i>
             -
             <img alt="Male" height="13" src="themes/bugguide/images/male.gif" width="13"/>
            </div>
            <div class="bgimage-where-when">
             West Chester, Chester County, Pennsylvania,  USA
             <br/>
             August 15, 2007
             <br/>
             Size: 2"
             <br/>
            </div>
            <div class="node-body">
             This flew into my house about 8:00pm last night. I captured it and placed it on a Bamboo plant to photograph then we let it go. It was not to excitable and stayed on the plant to be photographed. It can fly very well. It was moderately humid, and the temperature was about 85 degrees outside.
            </div>
