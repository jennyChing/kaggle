       <div class="ten columns content omega clearfix">
        <h2>
         <a href="/blogs/news/15196225-design-your-dress-for-carnival">
          Design your Dress for Carnival
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="info clearfix">
         <div class="eight columns alpha">
          Posted by
          <b>
           Feather Treasures
          </b>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="wysiwyg">
         <p>
          The famous North American,
          <strong>
           Miami Festival
          </strong>
          is around the corner. The Miami Broward Carnival takes place in the month of October every year, on the Sunday before Columbus Day. This year it is on 12
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          October 2014. It is one of the largest Caribbean Carnivals in North America and considered one of the best Carnivals in the United States. Best thing about this carnival is that you get to enjoy the warm Caribbean weather. Masquerade and spectators dress up colorfully for the fetes and parties.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you wish to attend it, you require some planning in advance. Prior bookings for hotel and flight are quite necessary. Apart from this you need to pay some attention to your costumes as well. You have the choice buy ready-made costumes but, you would find them quite expensive. Instead of that you can yourself design your costumes. You can buy
          <a href="http://www.feathertreasures.com/pages/buy-craft-feathers" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            craft feathers
           </strong>
          </a>
          in bulk from a wholesaler and create costumes in the color of your choice. Feathers and sequins are the two main patterns of the costumes designs. Sequins add shine and feathers add vigor to a carnival costumes. No costume can go without feathers.
         </p>
         <p>
          Following are the costume parts that you would like to dress up in:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <strong style="line-height: 1.2;">
            Angel Feather wings
           </strong>
           <span style="line-height: 1.2;">
            -Feather wings would add oomph to your dress. If you are bold enough to carry it, than you should not miss it. You can mix different feathers for making it. Depending upon the color of your costumes, buy dyed feathers.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Feather Headdresses
           </strong>
           - Headdresses are the fun part of carnival costume. They would make you feel like a royalty.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Feather Headpieces 
           </strong>
           If you dont want to have a headdress as it is too big. You can go for a small feather headpiece.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Feather Harnesses
           </strong>
           - If you dont want to wear complete costume, you should at least wear a Boa harness. You can find
           <a href="http://www.feathertreasures.com/collections/ostrich-feathers" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             ostrich feathers for sale
            </strong>
           </a>
           online and make it yourself.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Feather Collars-
           </strong>
           Feather collar look awesome with a headdress. You can buy feathers in neon colors to make color and headdress to shine in the crowd.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Feather Dance Fans
           </strong>
           - Feather fans look gorgeous and elegant. You can buy ostrich feathers or marabou feathers for making a burlesque dancer like fan. Later on, you can use these fans for decorating your house. You can get feathers custom-dyed for the color of your choice.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          Usually feather pieces are made of Ostrich, Coque, Pheasant and Nandu Feathers. But, you can buy feathers of your own choice to make the costume set for the carnival.
         </p>
