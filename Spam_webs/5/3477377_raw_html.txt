      <h2 class="head headline2 mg-1">
       Instructions
      </h2>
      <ol>
       <li>
        <p>
         Purchase a smartphone with a QWERTY design. This will allow you to type easily while on the go. There are many smartphone devices that offer spacious keyboards to type emails or reports. While traveling to multiple destinations, you will be able to quickly type important messages or documents without missing a laptop.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Add mobile applications that will allow you to work on the go. Mobile applications such as Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel and Outlook will let you remain productive even when you're not near a computer. You won't have to depend on a laptop or desktop computer to get work done. Usually these mobile applications are inexpensive and user-friendly.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Save important documents and information on an online storage system. Services provided by Box (box.net), Dropbox (dropbox.com) and IBackup (ibackup.com) are convenient ways to have access to work documents while on the go. Instead of waiting until you get back to the office to share work documents or display presentations, you can always have access.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Add a data plan to your existing cellular service. Since much of your important information and documents will be stored online, you'll need a reliable data package to prevent overage charges, or charges for minutes beyond your cellphone plan. You will be able to depend on a reliable Internet connection through your cellular company. Having to find a workable hot spot can become a major headache, but your cellular company will provide you with an Internet connection as long as you have a cellular signal.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Buy a SD or mini SD card for your smartphone. This will give you the opportunity to expand your storage space. Instead of carrying a laptop or other devices for music listening, you can save your favorite tunes to the SD or mini SD card. A 1GB or 2GB card will provide you with enough space to store your tunes for multiple hours of listening.
        </p>
       </li>
      </ol>
