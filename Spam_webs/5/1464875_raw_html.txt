            <h2>
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              <a name="more">
              </a>
              Carefully Select Your Bike
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </h2>
            <div class="MsoNormal">
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              You might think it is cool to drive a machine that can
accelerate quickly and drive fast. But, if you are going to be riding the bike
for long distances, that model might not be the best choice. Each type of bike
is built for different needs. For example, some give you a better posture and
more comfortable sitting positions that are better suited for distance. When
deciding which machine to buy, think about how you will be using it the most.
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </div>
            <h2>
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              Plan Your Budget
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </h2>
            <div class="MsoNormal">
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              Before you go shopping, figure out how much money you want
to spend each month on a payment. It is wise to avoid using all your discretionary
income for a monthly bike payment. Keep some reserves for savings, fun, and
emergencies.
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </div>
            <h2>
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              Include the Cost of Insurance
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </h2>
            <div class="MsoNormal">
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              When figuring out your monthly payment, include the cost of
your insurance. Motorcycle insurance can be much more expensive than regular
auto insurance. Contact your insurance agent and get a quote so that you know
how much to plan on spending.
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </div>
            <h2>
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              Shop around for the Interest Rates
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </h2>
            <div class="MsoNormal">
             <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
              Many dealerships offer financing, but they might not offer
the best rate you can get. Before you go shopping, check out the various rates
offered by banks or credit unions. Check online, too. Getting pre-qualified for
a loan does not mean that you have to use the loan. But, a pre-qualified loan
gives you negotiation power with the dealership. They might be able to match or
beat your loan offer.
              <o:p>
              </o:p>
             </span>
            </div>
