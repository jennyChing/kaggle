     <h1>
      SAVE THE DATE: Sheldon Book Launch Party!
     </h1>
     <h3 class="date">
      Wednesday, July 8, 2009 - 06:24 PM
     </h3>
     <div class="content">
      Mark this down in your calendars: The book launch party for the next Sheldon collection "Living Dangerously (With Saturated Fats)" is coming August 9th, from 7-10 PM, in Beverly Hills, at the very cool Crescent Lounge (see below pic). It's free and open to all Sheldon readers, family and friends...so come on out!
      <p>
       Will there be free drinks?
       <b>
        YES.
       </b>
       Will there be free sketches?
       <b>
        HECK YEAH.
       </b>
       Will there be free high-fives?
       <b>
        YOU BETCHA.
       </b>
       Will there be much awesomeness?
       <b>
        ONLY IF YOU ATTEND. THIS CAN NOT BE STRESSED ENOUGH.
       </b>
       :)
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        EDITED TO ADD:
       </b>
       Folks asked for address, etc., so here you go: The Crescent Lounge, 403 N. Crescent Drive, Beverly Hills, CA 90210 - (310) 247-0505.  Here is their
       <a href="http://www.crescentbh.com">
        site,
       </a>
       and here's a Google Map you can
       <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;source=s_q&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=403+N.+Crescent+Drive,+Beverly+Hills,+CA+90210&amp;sll=34.071219,-118.400316&amp;sspn=0.006808,0.007027&amp;g=403+N.+Crescent+Drive,+Beverly+Hills,+CA%E2%80%8E&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;ll=34.071482,-118.400058&amp;spn=0.001702,0.001757&amp;z=19">
        use for directions
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <img src="http://cdn.sheldoncomics.com/media/posts/blogimages/blog_090709.jpg"/>
