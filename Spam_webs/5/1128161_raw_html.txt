            <p>
             Bring on the the Fireworks because these
             <strong>
              Red, White andBlue Strawberries
             </strong>
             are very festive! Here is a 4th of July goodie that will make your kids and guests very happy! These Strawberries are easy to create!!!! They look pretty, no matter how messy you make them!
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Strawberries
             </li>
             <li>
              Red and Blue Food Picks &amp; Toothpicks
             </li>
             <li>
              White Chocolate Melts or Wilton White Candy Melts
             </li>
             <li>
              Blue Sprinkles
             </li>
            </ul>
            <ol>
             <li>
              In a glass bowl melt the white chocolate or candy melts, stirring every 45 seconds.
             </li>
             <li>
              Dip the pre-rinsed strawberries in the chocolate or candy melts.
             </li>
             <li>
              Then dip them in blue sprinkles!
             </li>
            </ol>
