       <div class="contentBoxImageText" id="imageText_shrine">
        <p>
         In the early hours of 29 May 1892, Bahullh passed away at the Mansion of Bahj. Nine days later His will was unsealed. It designated Abdul-Bah as His  successor and head of the Bah' Faith  the first time in history that the founder of a world religion had made explicitly clear whom people should follow after His death. This declaration of a successor is the pivotal provision of what is known to Bah's as the "Covenant of Bahullh." It has enabled the Bah' Faith to remain united around one central authority for over a century.
        </p>
