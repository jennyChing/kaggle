        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <font style="font-size:1.2em">
          Daredevil tourists have a new place to get their kicks after one of the worlds most thrilling  or terrifying  attractions officially opened in China this week.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <font style="font-size:1.2em">
          The horseshoe-shaped glass walkway in Chongqing extends 87.5ft from the edge of a cliff, allowing visitors to feel as though they are walking on air with the valley floor 2,350ft below their feet.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <font style="font-size:1.2em">
          With jaw-dropping panoramic views from its observation deck, it is the longest cantilever bridge in the world, beating Arizonas Grand Canyon Skywalk by 16.4ft.
         </font>
