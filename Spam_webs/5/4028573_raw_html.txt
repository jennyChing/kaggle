             5 Must-Have Money-Saving Apps
            </h1>
            <span class="single-postmeta">
             <time>

            <p>
             Smart shoppers are always on the lookout for money-saving apps. With so many of them out there, families are saving a ton of hard-earned cash just by having these apps in their pocket, purse or on a computer. Check out our quick list of 5 must-have money-saving apps and make sure youre getting all of the savings you deserve.
            </p>
            <h4>
             <a href="https://www.keyringapp.com/home" target="_blank">
              1. Key Ring
             </a>
            </h4>
            <p>
             Have a bunch of reward cards stacked in your wallet or dangling from your key ring? Well, this app will help you store all of them in one accessible placeyour phone. If youre like me, you have two key rings one for house keys and another for the car. I also switch purses a lot and, depending on space, will omit a lot of what I usually carry in order to make a better fashion statement. More than once, Ive stood in line at a store kicking myself for not bringing a rewards card or for not recalling which telephone number I used when I first completed the rewards application eons ago.
            </p>
            <p>
             This app is a lifesaver since my phone is always with me and that rewards card collection is only a tap away.
            </p>
            <h4>
             <a href="http://www.flipp.com/flipp/contest" target="_blank">
              2. Flipp
             </a>
            </h4>
            <p>
             Their website advertises that people who shop with sales flyers save at least 40% every week. Good to know! Whats also good is this convenient app, which allows users to view weekly flyers and clip deals while you browse. Theres a special place in my heart for apps that allow users to personalize their experience and Flipp doesnt disappoint here since you can shop by brand and save your favorite flyers for regular viewing.
            </p>
            <h4>
             <a href="https://ibotta.com/" target="_blank">
              3. Ibotta
             </a>
            </h4>
            <p>
             Next up on our list of money-saving apps is one that actually pays you backin cash! See, they offer rebates from some of the top brands nationwide and each time you use the app to upload a receipt verifying your purchase of an item eligible for a rebate, Ibotta deposits the cash due to you in your account within two days. The minimum amount for payout is $10, which can be paid by PayPal, Venmo or you can select a gift card.
            </p>
            <h4>
             <a href="http://www.joinhoney.com/" target="_blank">
              4. Honey
             </a>
            </h4>
            <p>
             All money-saving apps are not the same and Honey is a prime example of this. It is not a mobile app, but is one thats used through your computers browser to help you find discount codes whenever you shop online. Having used it for more than a year now, I can vouch for the fact that it really does come in handy. Add it to your browser and, before you checkout anywhere you shop online, click the little fancy H button and apply any coupon code that is available for your purchase.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              TIP
             </strong>
             : You dont have to wait until you checkout to use Honey. Whenever you find yourself shopping online, click the Honey button first to generate a list of coupon codes for various deals available on the site. Just be careful not to overspend on bargains you may find, which werent on your shopping list.
             <img alt=";)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://couponconnections.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif"/>
            </p>
            <h4>
             <a href="http://cartwheel.target.com/" target="_blank">
              5. Target Cartwheel
             </a>
            </h4>
            <p>
             Our regular readers have been tapping away at this one for a long time. But if youre new to this site or to the world of money-saving apps, you definitely want to grab this one. Simply download the app, find the deals you want, save them to the app, head to your nearest
             <a href="http://couponconnections.com/category/target/" target="_blank">
              Target
             </a>
             to shop and scan your personalized barcode at checkout to receive the savings you stored earlier. Offers can be used multiple times and can even be stacked on top of other coupons.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://couponconnections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Must-Have-Money-Saving-Apps.jpg">
              <img alt="Must-Have Money-Saving Apps" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-126710" height="612" src="http://couponconnections.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Must-Have-Money-Saving-Apps.jpg" width="612"/>
             </a>
            </p>

             Whats On Your Phone?
            </h2>

            </p>
