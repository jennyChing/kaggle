         <h1 class="siteact">
          Product Safety Engineer
         </h1>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="grids ie7links clearfix">
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
        <div class="gridfloat">
         <div class="articlecss wobjectTuevArticle templateId_1" id="anchor_1411479125554918370268">
          <div class="grid_12">
           <p>
            TV SD Canada Inc., a global leader in product safety and EMC testing, regulatory affairs and certification, has animmediate opening at our facility in Newmarket, Ontario (Toronto Area) for a:
           </p>
           <h3>
            <b>
             Product Safety Engineer
            </b>
           </h3>
           <h3>
            <b>
             Summary
            </b>
           </h3>
           <p>
            At TV SD Canada, we are passionate about technology, people and the environment. For almost 150 years, our employees
            <br/>
            contributions and enthusiasm have been essential to the success of our business. Our commitment is to invest in our employees as well as to hire, develop, and engage the best talent to help us continue to build our team. We are dedicated to creating a work environment where all levels of employees are inspired to learn, grow, and be empowered to achieve both their professional as
            <br/>
            well as personal goals.
           </p>
           <p>
            This position will be the key contact to proactively provide superior customer service to our customers. Your role and responsibilities
            <br/>
            include inspecting, testing products for conformity assessment within international regulations including related client and project management. We are looking for a detail oriented individual with conformity testing experience, ideally with European Directive field and who wants to add value to our organization.
           </p>
           <hr/>
           <h3>
            <b>
             Roles &amp; Responsibilities
            </b>
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Act as Product Safety Certifier of products for TV SD America; inspect, test, and photograph products for conformity assessment with international regulations, with the end result being the certificate of conformance for the client
            </li>
            <li>
             Prepare reports and documentation for certification of customer products to the appropriate standards. Generate revenue by billing engineering time to our clients, usually on a project basis, for Constructional Review, Testing, Preliminary Evaluations and Certifications activities
            </li>
            <li>
             Correspond, orally, and in writing, with Product Safety Agencies such as UL, CSA and VDE with respect to Product Design Approaches for Safety
            </li>
            <li>
             Perform Factory Inspections and field evaluation services to NFPA, UL, ANSI and CSA requirements for related products
            </li>
            <li>
             Participate in Technical standards Committees with Standards Development organizations such as IEC, UL and CSA
            </li>
            <li>
             Keep abreast of present and future Product Safety requirements, test procedures, instrumentation, technology and TV America
             <br/>
             Engineering service capabilities by internal training sessions, external seminars and reading of technical articles.
            </li>
            <li>
             Travel to customers locations and perform testing and evaluation of products
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
           <hr/>
           <h3>
            <b>
             Requirements
            </b>
           </h3>
           <div>
            <ul>
             <li>
              A BS in Electrical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering is required, or equivalent
             </li>
             <li>
              Technical knowledge of Certification Testing for Gas Heat, Oil Heat and Electrical Refrigeration preferred
             </li>
             <li>
              A minimum of 3 years Product Safety experience is preferred
             </li>
             <li>
              Knowledge of European directives and international standards is preferred
             </li>
             <li>
              Knowledge of Windows, MS Office and electronic mail necessary
             </li>
            </ul>
           </div>
           <p>
           </p>
           <hr/>
           <div>
            <h3>
             <b>
              Why should you join TV SD Canada?
             </b>
            </h3>
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li>
             A competitive compensation package that includes health &amp; dental insurance
            </li>
            <li>
             Retirement savings plan with matching contributions
            </li>
            <li>
             Paid time off for volunteering in the community
            </li>
            <li>
             Wellness Programs
            </li>
            <li>
             Opportunities to expand your career internationally and work alongside people from diverse cultures and backgrounds
            </li>
            <li>
             Develop and grow in a performance-based environment that recognizes and rewards top performers
            </li>
            <li>
             Continuous training and learning opportunities to support the professional and personal development of our employees
            </li>
            <li>
             Work alongside experts and market leaders in a company with a strong recognizable brand worldwide
            </li>
            <li>
             Make a positive contribution to change and progress; doing your part to ensure safety, quality and environmental sustainability
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
