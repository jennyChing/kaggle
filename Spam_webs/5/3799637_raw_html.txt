            <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
             4 Fatty Foods with Health Benefits
            </h3>
            <div class="post-header">
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4978450975550403962" itemprop="description articleBody">
             <div class="postmeta-primary">
              <span class="author">
               By:
               <a href="https://plus.google.com/109659299842779274714">
                Juliet Nick
               </a>
              </span>
              <span class="post-comment-link">
               <a class="comment-link" href="http://www.celebsfunda.com/2013/10/4-fatty-foods-with-health-benefits.html#comment-form" onclick="">
                0
comments
               </a>
              </span>
             </div>
             <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
             </script>
             <!-- 336x280, created 7/22/11 -->
             <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9449459874938227" data-ad-slot="2659953868" style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px">
             </ins>
             <script>

             </script>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Vy3YN8-sIZk/UnYfiy-JguI/AAAAAAAAABk/Qdasl3VNNes/s1600/Fatty+Foods+with+Health+Benefits.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="Fatty Foods with Health Benefits" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Vy3YN8-sIZk/UnYfiy-JguI/AAAAAAAAABk/Qdasl3VNNes/s1600/Fatty+Foods+with+Health+Benefits.jpg" title="Fatty Foods with Health Benefits"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The food you eat every day can affect your health. Therefore many health experts recommend eating a healthy diet consisting mostly of fruits and vegetables. Rarely do they encourage you to eat fatty foods because it is believed to add weight which of course is bad for health. However here are 4 fatty foods are actually good for you to consume for the sake of your health.
             <br/>
             <a name="more">
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <b>
              1. Butter
             </b>
             <br/>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3JhrAmGenl8/UnYfp5XCvfI/AAAAAAAAABs/s2Dv2vUNY4g/s1600/1.+Butter.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="1. Butter" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3JhrAmGenl8/UnYfp5XCvfI/AAAAAAAAABs/s2Dv2vUNY4g/s1600/1.+Butter.jpg" title="1. Butter"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Butter milk contains a lot of fat which is certainly not good for weight loss. But butter has many health benefits. Butter can improve mood because the content of selenium in it that can make the body become relaxed nerves. Additionally butter can also prevent the body from thyroid damage as vitamin A in it.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <b>
              2. Dark Chocolate
             </b>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rkMScPctlIk/UnYfwRhzMaI/AAAAAAAAAB0/vgRHYOqvdlM/s1600/2.+Dark+Chocolate.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="2. Dark Chocolate" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rkMScPctlIk/UnYfwRhzMaI/AAAAAAAAAB0/vgRHYOqvdlM/s1600/2.+Dark+Chocolate.jpg" title="2. Dark Chocolate"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Dark chocolate is known to be good for health because it is rich in antioxidants that are useful to counteract free radicals.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <b>
              3. Potato
             </b>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-gOu0UECMElU/UnYf-wTcyVI/AAAAAAAAACA/KTrByzXAntg/s1600/3.+Potato.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="3. Potato" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-gOu0UECMElU/UnYf-wTcyVI/AAAAAAAAACA/KTrByzXAntg/s1600/3.+Potato.jpg" title="3. Potato"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Potatoes have long been known as a replacement for carbohydrates in the diet due to lower sugar content than rice or bread. Besides the potato is rich in potassium, phosphorus, magnesium, and zinc are very good for skin health.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <b>
              4. Eggs
             </b>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-baFh55Hsa7U/UnYnMDN-H-I/AAAAAAAAACQ/nPDlUgmmvXc/s1600/4.+Eggs.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="4. Eggs" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-baFh55Hsa7U/UnYnMDN-H-I/AAAAAAAAACQ/nPDlUgmmvXc/s1600/4.+Eggs.jpg" title="4. Eggs"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Both the egg white and egg yolk contains 13 types of minerals that are essential to the body, among which are calcium, magnesium, iron, potasiu, sodium, and selenium. Eggs also contain essential amino acids that help the body's ability to absorb calories. 4 That's the kind of fatty foods is very important for the health of the body. You are allowed to eat them every day but still with a limited number so it will not interfere with health.
