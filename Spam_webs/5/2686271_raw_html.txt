        <h1 class="entry-title">
         St. Patricks Day Shamrocks Printable
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-03-13T07:00:24+00:00">
          March 13, 2014
         </span>
         By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/author/guest/" rel="author">
            Guest
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/st-patricks-day-shamrocks-printable/#comments">
           1 Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
         <div>
          <p style="text-align: justify">
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
            Im so excited to be sharing here today at LCI! My name is Doreen and Im the newest contributor here. The ladies of LCI have been so welcoming to me and Im so happy to be joining them! My blog is called
            <a href="http://www.hymnsandverses.com/">
             Hymns and Verses
            </a>
            . If you havent been there to visit before, Id be thrilled if youd stop by!
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
            <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Doreen-Blog-Photo1.jpg">
             <img alt="Doreen Hymns and Verses" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-4103" height="300" src="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Doreen-Blog-Photo1-293x300.jpg" width="293"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify">
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
            Its just a few days before St. Patricks Day and I thought Id bring you a little touch of Irish for your home. I dont normally decorate for St. Patricks Day, but when I saw these gorgeous shamrocks on a recent visit to the greenhouse, I decided they would be perfect for the holiday and later into spring as well! I planted the shamrocks in a large silver trophy cup that I lined with a glass Pyrex dish for protection.
           </span>
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone';font-size: 14px">
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
            <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrocks-in-Silver-Trophy-Cup.jpg">
             <img alt="Shamrocks-Silver-Trophy-Cup" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4104" height="1024" src="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrocks-in-Silver-Trophy-Cup-682x1024.jpg" width="682"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div>
          <p style="text-align: justify">
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
            To complete my little ode to the Irish vignette, I created a shamrocks printable to share with you  May your blessings outnumber the shamrocks that grow. I printed my copy on ivory card stock and put it in an 8 x 10 silver frame that I had on hand.
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
            <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrock-Printable.jpg">
             <img alt="St.-Patrick's-Day-Shamrock-Printable" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4105" height="682" src="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrock-Printable-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center">
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
            You can save the image below to your home computer for printing.
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: justify">
          <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrock-Printable016.jpg">
           <img alt="Shamrock-Printable" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-4106" height="300" src="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrock-Printable016-232x300.jpg" width="232"/>
          </a>
          <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
           The shamrocks are so beautiful and I hope that I can keep them alive for a while. Its so nice to have a bit of green inside  we still have some snow remaining on the ground here in the northeast!
          </span>
         </p>
         <div>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
            <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Close-Up-Shamrocks-in-Silver-Cup.jpg">
             <img alt="Shamrocks-Silver-Cup" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4108" height="1024" src="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Close-Up-Shamrocks-in-Silver-Cup-682x1024.jpg" width="682"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center">
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
            Wishing you a wonderful St. Patricks Day!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrock-Close-up-2.jpg">
            <img alt="Shamrock-Old-Book" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4110" height="644" src="http://www.livecreativelyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Shamrock-Close-up-2-1024x644.jpg" width="1024"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left">
           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
            If you enjoyed this post, I hope youll stop by and see me at
            <a href="http://www.hymnsandverses.com" target="_blank">
             Hymns and Verses
            </a>
            .
           </span>
          </p>

           <span style="font-family: 'antic didone'">
