            <p>
             Vegetarian Wild Rice and Mushroom Pilaf. The flavors of fall combine with wild rice for a dish that's perfect for a
             <a href="/od/vegetarianlifestyle/tp/thanksgivingrecipes.htm">
              vegetarian Thanksgiving
             </a>
             entree or everyday
             <a href="/od/sidevegetabledishes/Side_Dishes_and_Vegetable_Dishes.htm">

             . This
             <a data-inlink="65x5oFizj1_BuF1okVPNhQ==" href="http://lowcaloriecooking.about.com/od/grains/ss/How-To-Make-Wild-Rice-Pilaf.htm">

           <h5 class="heading p-col">
            Ingredients
           </h5>
           <ul class="content ingredients-list">
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup wild rice, uncooked
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             2 3/4 cups vegetable broth
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 cups brown rice, uncooked
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1 onion, diced
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             4 cloves garlic, minced
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tbsp
             <a data-inlink="nLjiwAufd66VCt1ZENdcHQ==" href="http://localfoods.about.com/od/recommendedproducts/a/All-About-Olive-Oil.htm">
              olive oil
             </a>
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             3 cups mushrooms, sliced
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             2 celery stalks, sliced
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 tsp sage
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 tsp marjoram
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             salt and pepper to taste
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 cup chopped
             <a data-inlink="_U2i1XToh4rBJxzM3axBog==" href="/od/saucesdipsspreads/r/herbedpate.htm">
              fresh parsley
             </a>
            </li>
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 cup chopped almonds
            </li>
           </ul>
          </div>
          <div class="preparation expert-content-text" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           <h5 class="heading-style-a p-col">
            Preparation
           </h5>
           <p>
            Bring the
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="1" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/od/soupsstewsandchili/r/veggiebroth.htm">
             vegetable broth
            </a>
            to a boil in a large saucepan and add the
            <a data-component="link" data-inlink="L6N_1OGBSWzL5N-ZIXYRog==" data-ordinal="2" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="http://localfoods.about.com/od/preparationtips/qt/howtowildrice.htm">
             wild rice
            </a>
            . Cook for 15 to 20 minutes, then add
            <a data-component="link" data-inlink="4vPB5xpZT-719HH_bK_cXg==" data-ordinal="3" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/od/glossary/qt/Brown-Rice-Syrup.htm">
             brown rice
            </a>
            . Cover and cook for another 45 minutes, or until rice is done cooking.
           </p>
           <p>
            In a large skillet, sautee the onions and garlic until onions are brown and carmelized, about 8 to 10 minutes. Add mushrooms, celery and spices and cook for another 5 minutes, adding more oil or a little bit of broth if needed.
           </p>
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="1" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense1">
           </div>
           <p>
            Add the cooked rice, fresh parsley and almonds, and stir well to combine. Cook for another minute or two, until everything is just heated through.
           </p>
