     <p>
      Firstly, sorry for the two day delay. Secondly, welcome to Old Stuff Week. This is going to be a bit of a hodge-podge of a theme week leading up to the exciting numerical milestone coming up. Old Stuff Week is going to be about three things:
     </p>
     <p>
      1. Going back to old characters, jokes and concepts and tying up loose/split ends.
      <br/>
      2. Maybe some meta-humor?
      <br/>
      3. Finally executing some ideas I really like that Ive been sitting on for a while.
     </p>
     <p>
      I started drawing this comic a few of months ago the one time in my life I was in Long Island. This was before I drew
      <a href="http://cowbirdsinlove.com/904">
       The Forever Line
      </a>
      and before a lot of things. I started on the comic and I have no idea why I didnt finish it. I really like Friendship Woman as a character and I didnt want to mess up her debut, I think.
     </p>
     <p>
      I guess I could have saved
      <a href="http://cowbirdsinlove.com/987">
       Weather Girl
      </a>
      coming back for this. But shes already back and better than ever and youll just have to deal with that.
     </p>
