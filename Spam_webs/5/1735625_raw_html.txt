           <div class="field-item even" property="schema:articleBody content:encoded">
            <p>
             Trying to
             <a class="inline_link" href="/topic/quit-smoking">
              quit smoking
             </a>
             is a thing which you have desired to do for a long duration. In fact, you have tried not to smoke for years, but each time you tried you just failed to maintain. This is natural, as you intake nicotine you get addicted physically to it, and also you will be psychologically dependent on it. So withdrawal of smoking creates a great problem for you.
            </p>
            <section class="article-content__embedded">
             <figure class="article-image article-image--portrait">
              <img alt="Stop Smoking" class="zoomable" data-large="http://cdn2-b.examiner.com/sites/default/files/styles/image_content_width_large/hash/1d/55/1d55494520fae22b043df2466a7c8297.jpg?itok=xY2Zy--a" height="350" rel="image_src" src="http://cdn2-b.examiner.com/sites/default/files/styles/image_content_width/hash/1d/55/1d55494520fae22b043df2466a7c8297.jpg?itok=25zPllf6" title="Stop Smoking" typeof="foaf:Image" width="464"/>
              <figcaption class="caption media__body">
               <div class="caption__credit">
                Photo by Stuart Wilson
               </div>
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
            </section>
            <p>
             So far making the decision to quit smoking is really crucial but very difficult to follow through. According to some smokers, quitting cigarettes is just next to impossible. So provided are some tips to help you quit smoking. When you decide to quit smoking, don't do so aimlessly. Decide the day that you are trying to quit, then confirm this to the people close to you, and people you are spending time around. Make them aware of your decision. You definitely require their support and action in some important ways.
            </p>
            <p>
             You will be affected by
             <a href="http://www.webmd.com/smoking-cessation/understanding-nicotine-withdrawal-symptoms" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              withdrawal symptoms
             </a>
             that may not only result in making you feel irritated, but also sour your mood. At the beginning you may ask for just one cigarette, but you need people support to talk you out of it, and motivate you to stay bold with your decision. As part of your planning to quit smoking, get away from all the things that are related to smoking from your sight, your car, and from your workplace. You just need to throw away your cigarettes as well as the lighter and dump the ash tray. While feeling awkward withdrawal issues, keep in your mind that the circumstances will change after a few days or so. If you remember this, then it will help to boost your willpower and dedication.
            </p>
            <p>
             For those people whose willpower is quite low, there is a therapy to help them quit smoking which is known as Hypnotism. This therapy has shown quite positive result to quit this habit fast. You have to realize that the habit of smoking is built on continuous repetition and various aspects like time passing. Sometimes stressful situations make you smoke. You have to identify those situations and be aware of them. You can find other various tips to quit smoking over the internet.
            </p>
            <p>
             You have to take time to recognize that some tips will work for certain situations, as every individual has different needs. Cigarettes can penetrate , every aspect of our lifestyle, which is not good. Sadly, it makes it very hard to quit. Some
             <a href="http://www.cancer.org/healthy/stayawayfromtobacco/guidetoquittingsmoking/guide-to-quitting-smoking-types-of-nrt" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              nicotine products
             </a>
             are also available which help you to prevent the habit of smoking.
            </p>
