           <img alt="healthy family recipes" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-11656" height="190" src="http://cdn.missomoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/salmon-asparagus-e1412010939930.jpg" width="306"/>
           I don't understand why, or to whom has been the one ringleading everyone to believing that certain foods are good for you, and leaving out the actual good foods. Whoever they are, they are dumb because if people knew these secrets then the whole world(not kidding) would be happy and healthy.
          </p>
          <p>
           Check out what I've discovered that are important foods to eat that in the long run will keep you satisfied andenergetic
          </p>
          <h2>
           Wild Salmon
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Slimming superpowers:
           </strong>
           The fish's omega-3 fatty acids could help youfight flabmore effectively. They alter the expression of certain genes, shifting your body to burn fat rather than store it.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The amazing proof:
           </strong>
           In a study analyzing the diets of 35,000 women, published in
           <em>
            Public Health Nutrition
           </em>
           , those subjects who ate oily fish such assalmontwo to four times per week had the lowest basal metabolic indexes, a common measure of body fat.Kana Okada
          </p>
          <h2>
           Apples
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.missomoms.com/actually-healthy-foods-to-eat/">
            <img alt="Homemade Peanut Butter" class="alignleft wp-image-13442" height="180" src="http://cdn.missomoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/apples-and-peanut-butter-e1415601155522.png" width="339"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Slimming superpowers:
            </strong>
            This fruit's 4 to 5 grams of fiber not only are filling but also help ferry out some of the fat and calories you take in from other foods.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The amazing proof:
            </strong>
            People who ate anapple15 minutes before lunching on cheese tortellini consumed 187 fewer calories in total than those who snacked on nothing beforehand, a study from Penn State University in University Park determines. How about
            <em>
             them
            </em>
            apples?Kana Okada
           </p>
           <h2>
            Quinoa
           </h2>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Slimming superpowers:
            </strong>
            A complete protein, quinoa has all the essential amino acids needed tobuild metabolism-revving muscle.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The amazing proof:
            </strong>
            Reduced-calorie dieters eating about 115 g of protein daily lost 22 percent more fat after four months than those who ate 70 g per day,
            <em>
             The Journal of Nutrition
            </em>
            reports.Cond Nast Digital Studio
           </p>
          </div>
          <div>
           <h2>
            Lentils
           </h2>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Slimming superpowers:
            </strong>
            Theselegumesare rich in resistant starch (RS), a carbohydrate that may encourage fat burning and shrink fat cells.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The amazing proof:
            </strong>
            When study participants enjoyed a meal with 5 g of RSabout what you get from 3/4 cup cooked lentilsthey burned 23 percent more fat over 24 hours than when they had a meal without the starch, researchers at the University of Colorado in Denver say.Kana Okada
           </p>
           <h2>
            Olive Oil
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.missomoms.com/actually-healthy-foods-to-eat/">
             <img alt="olive oils" class="alignleft wp-image-10707 size-medium" height="300" src="http://cdn.missomoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/olive-oil-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Slimming superpowers:
            </strong>
            Healthful monounsaturated fats found inolive oilcould potentially switch on genes related to fat burning and storage.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The amazing proof:
            </strong>
            Dieters on a low-cal plan emphasizing monounsaturated fats, protein and complex carbs lost almost double the weight that dieters who ate the same calories but less total fat and protein and more carbs lost, a study in the
            <em>
             Archives of Internal Medicine
            </em>
            reveals.Kana Okada
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Eggs
           </h2>
           <div>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Slimming superpowers:
             </strong>
             Thebreakfaststaple is loaded with choline, a compound known to help block fat absorption.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The amazing proof:
             </strong>
             After eight weeks, dieters who ate twoeggs, toast and jelly for breakfast five days a week lost 65 percent more weight than those who had a same-calorie bagel breakfast without eggs, according to a study in the
             <em>
              International Journal of Obesity
             </em>
             .Kana Okada
            </p>
            <h2>
             Yogurt
            </h2>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Slimming superpowers:
             </strong>
             Lowfat and nonfatGreekand regular yogurts contain 20 percent or more of your daily calcium needs. The mineral slows production of cortisol, a hormone that encourages belly-flab buildup.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The amazing proof:
             </strong>
             People on alow-cal diet that included yogurt lost 81 percent more belly fat than those on a similar but no-yogurt plan, a study from the University of Tennessee at Knoxville concludes. This dairy is a must-do. Kana Okada
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <h2>
           Sweet Potatoes
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.missomoms.com/actually-healthy-foods-to-eat/">
            <img alt="sweet potato" class="alignleft wp-image-11660" height="232" src="http://cdn.missomoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/sweet-potato-fries-e1412800257807.jpg" width="355"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Slimming superpowers:
           </strong>
           These spuds have RS, the same carbs found inlentilsthat may turn up the body's fat-scorching furnace. RS may also increase production of peptide hormone compounds that signal the brain to stop eating.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The amazing proof:
           </strong>
           After a breakfast and a lunch containing RS, subjects ate about 10 percent fewer calories over the next 24 hours compared with when they had similar meals with a placebo, research from the University of Surrey indicates.Kana Okada
          </p>
          <h2>
           Kiwifruit
          </h2>
          <div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Slimming superpowers:
            </strong>
            A largekiwihas 84 milligrams of vitamin Cmore than a day's quota. C helps form carnitine, a compound that transports fat into cell mitochondria, where it's burned for energy during exercise.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The amazing proof:
            </strong>
            People with low blood levels of C burned 10 percent less fat per pound of body weight while walking than did those with normal levels of C, a study at Arizona State University in Mesa shows. But when subjects got a dose of C, their fat burning increased fourfold.Kana Okada
           </p>
           <h2>
            Edamame
           </h2>
           <div>
            <strong>
             Slimming superpowers:
            </strong>
            The greensoybeanssupply 17 g of protein per cup, and your body torches more calories digesting protein than it does processing carbs and fat.
           </div>
           <div>
            <p>
             <strong>
              The amazing proof:
             </strong>
             Researchers from the Federal University of Vicosa found that people burned about 70 more calories per day when their A.M. meal contained soy protein versus other types of protein. Cool beans!Kana Okada
            </p>
