            Would You Dare Get A Tattoo Without Any Sketches?  These Mind Blowing Tattoos are Done FREEHAND, On The Spot !

             <p>
              Getting a tattoo is a tough decision  and finding a design you like is much harder. You dont want to regret something that is permanent and end up having a FAIL tattoo. But would you risk your precious skin to an artist who would create it directly on your skin? Without any sketches  on the spot! I doubt you wouldnt 
             </p>
             <p>
              But people are now flocking to a tattoo artist who inks from his imagination on the spot no sketchpad in sight. Meet Jay Freestyle, a talented Amsterdam based tattoo artist, whocreates his art directly onto his clients skin without any previous sketches. andthe result? WOW! A stunning, soulful artworks, which last forever on the skin.
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Give me a piece of your skin and Ill give you a piece of my soul. Jays motto says it all.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-3" class="aligncenter wp-image-9212" height="468" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-3.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Jay is anextraordinarily talented artist who grew up in a conservative Chinese family in Johannesburg and emigrated to Amsterdam.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-15" class="aligncenter wp-image-9225" height="432" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-15.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Inspired by traditional painting techniques such as watercolour and liquid ink, he brings in modern and graphic elements.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-11" class="aligncenter wp-image-9210" height="434" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-11.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               The 29-year-old South African tattoo artist makes his designs up as he goes along while he is working on his clients.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-10" class="aligncenter wp-image-9221" height="537" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-102.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Each tattoo he comes up with is a unique illustration from his imagination.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-18" class="aligncenter wp-image-9226" height="432" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-18.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               I prefer to combine various styles, such as geometrical shapes and pointillism, while at the same time mixing realism with abstract, Jay says.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-4" class="aligncenter wp-image-9213" height="446" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-4.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Hes building on his trademark of using simple imagery and bringing it to life through an avant-garde style.
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-7" class="aligncenter wp-image-9216" height="474" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-7.jpg" width="650"/>
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               But not everyone wants Jays style,Usually from the first couple of minutes of talking to someone, I can see if they want my style or if they want me to do something realistic or traditional. he said.
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-6" class="aligncenter wp-image-9215" height="419" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-6.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-5" class="aligncenter wp-image-9214" height="429" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-5.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Normally when hes abroad people ask much more for Jays own style.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-2" class="aligncenter wp-image-9211" height="481" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-2.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand2" class="aligncenter wp-image-9224" height="481" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand2-1024x757.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Jay is now narrowing down his focus and developing a more graphical style and feel to his work.
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand1" class="aligncenter wp-image-9223" height="488" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand1-1024x768.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-9" class="aligncenter wp-image-9218" height="430" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-9.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Heexposes himself to the influence from top artists from around the world.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-1" class="aligncenter wp-image-9220" height="432" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-1.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-20" class="aligncenter wp-image-9227" height="488" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-20.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Jay is always eager to learn something new, he emerged as a broadly skilled tattooist.
              </strong>
             </h4>
             <p>
              <img alt="freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-12" class="aligncenter wp-image-9222" height="861" src="http://wereblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/freehand-tattoo-art-jay-freestyle-12-773x1024.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              <strong>
               Visit his
               <a href="https://www.facebook.com/JFreestyle" target="_blank">
                Facebook Page
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://www.tattooj.com/" target="_blank">
                Website
               </a>
               for more of his amazing works.
              </strong>
             </h4>
