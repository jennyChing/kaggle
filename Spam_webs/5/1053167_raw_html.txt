      <h1>
       Thomas Barbey: Living in a Surreal World
      </h1>
     </figcaption>
     <img alt="" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/themes/fullscreen-2015/images/blank.gif"/>

       <img alt="Thomas Barbey: Living in a Surreal World" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-78131" height="2266" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/thomas-barbey-10.jpg" width="2000"/>
       <div class="adv-single-top-leaderboard-box">
        <div class="adv-single-top-leaderboard">

       <p>
        In every location
        <a href="http://thomasbarbey.com/" target="_blank">
         Thomas Barbey
        </a>
        has traveled to, he has taken photographs and used them to create artistic montages of his envisioned fictional worlds. Working with negatives and double exposures, each scene is displayed in black and white or sepia; inspired by his travels, everyday life and art by Rene Magritte, M.C. Escher and Roger Dean.
        <!--more-->
       </p>
       <img alt="piano and zebra surreal photo by thomas barbey" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-78134" height="1179" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/thomas-barbey-13.jpg" width="1500"/>
       <img alt="waterfall, photo thomas barbey" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-78135" height="1922" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/thomas-barbey-11.jpg" width="1500"/>

       <img alt="skiing, thomas barbey" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-78133" height="1500" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/thomas-barbey-14.jpg" width="1500"/>
