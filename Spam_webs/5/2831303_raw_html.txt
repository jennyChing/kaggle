        <p>
         Dont try this at home. Try this on the street says the Italian artist
         <a href="http://www.fra-biancoshock.org/" style="text-decoration: none; border-bottom: 1px dotted;" target="_blank">
          Fra.Biancoshock
         </a>
         . Although he resisted to call himself an artist for a long time, he soon realised thats the purpose of his work. What he does to expose his artistic talent is street art for
         <span id="more-31023">
         </span>
         most of us but he prefers naming it Ephemeralism and defines as a movement that has the purpose of producing works of art that have to exist briefly in space but limitlessly in time. Whatever the name of this art, or experience, it is definitely one of a kind work which is installed in the streets of Italy, Spain, Portugal, Croatia, Hungary, Czech Republic, Malaysia and Singapore so far and seems he will never stop doing it.
        </p>
