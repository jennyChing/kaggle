Guitar picks are elusive little jerks.



Usually, when I need a pick, I can take one look at my floor and find one 
in plain site. Most of the time I even have a choice.



But recently I haven't been able to find ANY. I keep trying to use other 
things such as circular shaped earrings. Where have all my picks gone? I 
don't know, but they're probably in a black hole in my room along with all 
of the bobby pins and nailclippers I have ever purchased.

Last night, for example, I looked everywhere for a pick. When I finally 
found one, I dropped it while strumming, and it disappeared. It fell out of 
my hand and into the invisible guitar pick hiding place.




I looked everywhere and I could not for the life of me figure out where it 
had gone.












I've come to the conclusion that they slip into a fold in time and are 
never seen again.



That, or there's got to be some sort of guitar pick-eating monster under my 
bed that comes out at night and eats them all off the floor.

             <h1 class="entry-title" data-content-field="title">
              <a href="/verbvomit/2011/04/guitar-picks-are-elusive-little-jerks.html">
               Guitar Picks are Elusive Little Jerks
              </a>
             </h1>
             <span class="article-dateline">
              <span class="date">
               <a href="/verbvomit/2011/04/guitar-picks-are-elusive-little-jerks.html">
                <time class="published" datetime="2011-04-15">

            <div class="body entry-content">
             <div class="sqs-layout sqs-grid-12 columns-12" data-layout-label="Post Body" data-type="item" id="item-545c0fabe4b065226e7de4ad">
              <div class="row sqs-row">
               <div class="col sqs-col-12 span-12">
                <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-545c0fb1e4b065226e7de84b">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                   I've started doing the drawings for the second installment of the "How to be a Hipster" series. It should be done in a couple of weeks. If it's not, that means I didn't make it through final exams. Here's a little post while you wait.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   Guitar picks are elusive little jerks.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    <a href="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4ae/1415319467844/1000w/" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                     <img border="0" height="640" src="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4af/1415319467844/1000w/" width="593"/>
                    </a>
                   </div>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   Usually, when I need a pick, I can take one look at my floor and find one in plain site. Most of the time I even have a choice.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    <a href="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b0/1415319467844/1000w/" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                     <img border="0" height="640" src="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b1/1415319467844/1000w/" width="564"/>
                    </a>
                   </div>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   But recently I haven't been able to find ANY. I keep trying to use other things such as circular shaped earrings. Where have all my picks gone? I don't know, but they're probably in a black hole in my room along with all of the bobby pins and nailclippers I have ever purchased.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   Last night, for example, I looked
                   <i>
                    everywhere
                   </i>
                   for a pick. When I finally found one, I dropped it while strumming, and it
                   <i>
                    disappeared
                   </i>
                   . It fell out of my hand and into the invisible guitar pick hiding place.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    <br/>
                   </div>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    <br/>
                   </div>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    <a href="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b2/1415319467844/1000w/" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                     <img border="0" height="640" src="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b3/1415319467844/1000w/" width="489"/>
                    </a>
                    <a href="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b4/1415319467844/1000w/" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                     <img border="0" height="640" src="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b5/1415319467844/1000w/" width="489"/>
                    </a>
                   </div>
                   <br/>
                   I looked everywhere and I could not for the life of me figure out where it had gone.
                   <br/>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                   </div>
                   <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                    <a href="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b6/1415319467844/1000w/" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                     <img border="0" height="640" src="https://static.squarespace.com/static/545bf0f0e4b0fcdeec19743c/545c0fa1e4b065226e7ddcff/545c0fabe4b065226e7de4b7/1415319467844/1000w/" width="595"/>
                    </a>
                   </div>
