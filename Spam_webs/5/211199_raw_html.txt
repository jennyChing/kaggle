         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          DIY Nautical Towel Holder
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-02-21T12:36:34+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           February 21, 2014
          </time>
           by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://h2obungalow.com/author/wendi-wachtel" itemprop="url">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Wendi
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html#comments">
            20 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <script>
          &lt;!--
dynamicgoogletags.update();
//--&gt;
         </script>
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="7446280" data-link="http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html" data-summary="" data-title="DIY Nautical Towel Holder">
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Banner-2.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="DIY Nautical Towel Holder " class="aligncenter wp-image-2578" height="837" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Banner-2.jpg" title="DIY Nautical Towel Holder " width="565"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html&amp;media=http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Banner-2.jpg&amp;description=DIY Nautical Towel Holder')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I put this DIY nautical towel holder together with a few cleats from Home Depot and some jute rope I had left over from another project. It puts the finishing touch on my coastal bath makeover!
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-2570">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/side-view1.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Side of DIY nautical rope towel bar" class="aligncenter wp-image-2576" height="448" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/side-view1.jpg" title="Side of DIY nautical rope towel bar" width="662"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html&amp;media=http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/side-view1.jpg&amp;description=DIY Nautical Towel Holder')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Adding a nautical rope towel holder like this one is easy and it works great for hand towels or bath towels.
         </p>
         <p>
          This little half bath has no extra space. Its so tiny your knees almost hit the sink from the commode. No kidding! Using nautical rope as a towel holder worked well for us because it didnt stick out far from the wall. I dont think I could have fit a store bought hand towel bar as well in the tight space I had to work with, even if Id wanted to.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you happened to be Girl Scout or Sea Scout at one time youd probably show me a thing or two about knots. If not, then heres what I did to attach the rope to the cleats. The next two pictures show how I made the knot around the cleat.
         </p>
         <p>
          First, here is a closeup of just the knot on my DIY nautical towel holder.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/knot.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="knot on diy naiutical towel holder" class="aligncenter wp-image-2574" height="535" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/knot.jpg" title="knot on diy naiutical towel holder" width="800"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html&amp;media=http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/knot.jpg&amp;description=DIY Nautical Towel Holder')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This is the same picture. Ive added arrows and directions on how I made the knot. This knot will hold your towels without slipping off of the cleat. Do the same for the other end of the towel holder.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/knot-directions.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="diy nautical towel knot instructions" class="aligncenter wp-image-2573" height="535" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/knot-directions.jpg" title="diy nautical towel knot instructions" width="800"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html&amp;media=http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/knot-directions.jpg&amp;description=DIY Nautical Towel Holder')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I like the nautical rope and cleat for a unique way to hang hand towels. Boating, and sailing in particular are a big part of our life here in FL. My DIY nautical towel holder was a fun way to bring our love of boating indoors.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/towel.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Full view of diy nautical towel holder and cleats" class="aligncenter wp-image-2572" height="744" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/towel.jpg" title="Full view of diy nautical towel holder and cleats" width="502"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html&amp;media=http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/towel.jpg&amp;description=DIY Nautical Towel Holder')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
          The nautical rope towel holder works beautifully with my copper fixtures too. If you havent seen my reinvented copper toilet paper stand for this half bath you can read all about it
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-copper-toilet-paper-holder.html" target="_blank">
           HERE
          </a>
          .  Its gorgeous, and youd never believe it was plain chrome in its earlier life!
         </p>
         <p>
          I would have liked to show you more pictures of my DIY nautical towel holder in my little half bathbut that would spoil my big reveal for Monday
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
          So until then my friends..
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          May you have smooth seas,
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          and following winds..
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/signature.png">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="signature" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1738" height="81" src="http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/signature.png" width="134"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://h2obungalow.com/2014/02/diy-nautical-towel-holder.html&amp;media=http://h2obungalow.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/signature.png&amp;description=DIY Nautical Towel Holder')">
            </span>
