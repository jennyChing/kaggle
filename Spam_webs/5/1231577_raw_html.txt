        <h1>
         Just what is the best picture on the internet then?
        </h1>
        <span class="datecomment">
         February 27th, 2011
         <span class="bullet">
          
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.bitterwallet.com/just-what-is-the-best-picture-on-the-internet-then/41142#comments" title="Comment on Just what is the best picture on the internet then?">

        </span>
        <span class="author">
         By Andy Dawson
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry body">
        <p>
         Theres been some mass debate (stop laughing at the back) over the past few days about just what is the best picture on the internet. The row was sparked by the appearance of this picture
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="T3Zox 500x365 Just what is the best picture on the internet then?" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-41143" height="365" src="http://www.bitterwallet.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/T3Zox-500x365.jpg" title="T3Zox" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thousands of people have united, all of them saying: Yes! THAT is the best picture on the internet! But there has been some dissent over the past 48 hours. A rival faction has appeared, saying: No! THIS is the best picture on the internet!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="248015299 500x311 Just what is the best picture on the internet then?" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-41144" height="311" src="http://www.bitterwallet.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/248015299-500x311.jpg" title="248015299" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, we like the cat picture, but we also like the cow picture. But which is best? Theres only one way to find out  VOTE!
        </p>
        <p>
         Youve got until noon tomorrow (Monday) and then well be able to settle this important issue once and for all
        </p>
