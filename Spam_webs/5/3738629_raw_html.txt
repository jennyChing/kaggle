      <h1>
       The Smarthomes Of Yesterday
      </h1>

        <p>
         In 2014 we have seen several homes transformed into technological heavens. Smarthomes are a newer way of keeping your home safe remotely, rather than with traditional CCTV or alarm systems. Some systems even allow for you to control the security within your home using an app on a smartphone or tablet PC. Its not just security either, our entire home has become smart with smart appliances such as heating, boilers, meters and even kettles! Weve made an infographic to show you how smart living isnt a totally new idea and how weve evolved old ideas into new ones.
