       <h1 class="entry-title">
        #TheHelpMovie &amp; Book!
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2011-07-12T10:36:08+00:00">
         July 12, 2011
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://crunchybeachmama.com/author/courtney/" rel="author" title="Courtney">
           Courtney
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://crunchybeachmama.com/2011/07/thehelpmovie-book/#comments">
          4 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://crunchybeachmama.com/2011/07/thehelpmovie-book/the-help/" rel="attachment wp-att-3899">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3899" height="219" src="http://crunchybeachmama.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/the-help.jpg" title="the help" width="147"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes I wish I was part of a book club. Just for those books that when you shut the page each night before going to sleep, you start dreaming about it. When the book is finished, it stays with you for days or even weeks.
        </p>
        <p>
         When I finished The Help a couple of days ago I couldnt wait to talk to someone about it! I told Hubby he had to read it just so I can have a discussion partner. I emailed my best blogger friend
         <a href="http://ayearwithmomanddad.com" target="_blank">
          Julie
         </a>
         with a couple of my thoughts and we had a nice virtual chat.
        </p>
        <p>
         Overall, I thought it was a terrific book! I was anxious to pick it up and read a big chunk each day. There were a couple of parts that I wish were different and this is mostly because I like happy, upbeat, everything is nice in life books. But real topics arent always like that huh?
        </p>
        <p>
         On August 10th there will be a Disney movie coming out. I am anxious to see it and put some names and faces together. I hope they arent to different than what I have already imagined. Here is a trailer I found for you to watch. Makes me even more excited to see the movie!
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="349" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/J_ajv_6pUnI" width="350">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="http://www.facebook.com/thehelpmovie " target="_blank">
          Facebook
         </a>
         page for The Help is pretty cool. There is a Social Video Player that
         <em>
          embraces the collaborative spirit of the story by allowing fans to share their voice. Users are encouraged to add comments and start a dialogue directly in the video player.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Also,
         <em>
          DreamWorks Pictures has created an awesome 
          <strong>
           Helpful Book Club Kit
          </strong>
          which contains everything you need to create a perfect
          <strong>
           THE HELP
          </strong>
          themed meeting for your club! The Book Club Kit includes southern style recipes
         </em>
         <strong>
          (including Millys Pecan Pie!)
         </strong>
         <em>
          , drinks inspired by the characters, discussion group questions and can be downloaded.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Have you read the book? Did you like it? Are you going to see the movie?
         </strong>
        </p>
