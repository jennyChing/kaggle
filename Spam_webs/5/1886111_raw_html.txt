        <h2>
         15 Things White Girls like to do on Facebook:
        </h2>
        <p>
         1. Take pictures of their feet.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i2.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/girls-feet.jpeg">
          <img alt=" wtf cool stuff tumblr funny pics the best redneck engineering diy uncategorized my fave meme funny pics funny video funny video foreigners fails funny pics drunks funny pics cool stuff amazing cool stuff  Tweet That: 25 Hilarious Twitter &amp; Facebook Fails " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/girls-feet.jpeg?resize=400%2C259" title="girls feet"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Express their extreme annoyance at this work day today and hint that it deserves a much needed alcoholic beverage at the end of it.
         <em>
          WINK WINK.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Thank their hubby for being the best hubby in the world while their hubby is sitting right next to them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i1.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/funny-facebook-fails-best-hubby-world-e1323207260812.png">
          <img alt="funny facebook fails best hubby world e1323207260812 wtf cool stuff tumblr funny pics the best redneck engineering diy uncategorized my fave meme funny pics funny video funny video foreigners fails funny pics drunks funny pics cool stuff amazing cool stuff  Tweet That: 25 Hilarious Twitter &amp; Facebook Fails " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/funny-facebook-fails-best-hubby-world-e1323207260812.png?resize=488%2C125" title="funny-facebook-fails-best-hubby-world"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Complain about bad service at restaurants.
         <em>
          Never eating at Applebees AGAIN!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         5. Express their extreme excitement to see their best friends tonight, Britney, Whitney, and Sarah!!!
         <em>
          LUV U GIRLSSS!!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         6. Take pictures of undeserving food.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i0.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/creamy-oatmeal-diabetes-recipes-healthy-recipes.jpeg">
          <img alt=" wtf cool stuff tumblr funny pics the best redneck engineering diy uncategorized my fave meme funny pics funny video funny video foreigners fails funny pics drunks funny pics cool stuff amazing cool stuff  Tweet That: 25 Hilarious Twitter &amp; Facebook Fails " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/creamy-oatmeal-diabetes-recipes-healthy-recipes.jpeg?w=400" title="oatmeal"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         7. Make their status the song lyrics of any Kings of Leon song.
        </p>
        <p>
         8. Take a picture of someone they deem inferior to themselves in some way with the question:
         <em>
          Really?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         9. Write angry letters to companies (
         <em>
          Dear EZ PARK, I hate you!
         </em>
         ), unorganized groups of people (
         <em>
          Dear slutty freshmen who think that leggings can be worn as pants
         </em>
         ), and non-entities (
         <em>
          Dear unseasonably cold weather, WTF?!
         </em>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         10. Subtly yell at no one in particular while being very specific.
         <em>
          Wow, its hard to believe that you think you know someone and then they turn around and STAB YOU IN THE BACK. Will never make that mistake again. EVER.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i2.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/funny-facebook-fails-backstab.png">
          <img alt="funny facebook fails backstab wtf cool stuff tumblr funny pics the best redneck engineering diy uncategorized my fave meme funny pics funny video funny video foreigners fails funny pics drunks funny pics cool stuff amazing cool stuff  Tweet That: 25 Hilarious Twitter &amp; Facebook Fails " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/funny-facebook-fails-backstab.png?resize=481%2C87" title="funny-facebook-fails-backstab"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         11. Document exceedingly mundane activites for the day:
         <em>
          Getting my oil changed today. Then getting much needed groceries. Then its off to the post office to mail some bills. Then stopping by the gyno. Will probably need some gas by the end, so I may stop at the gas station. But I might be tired so Ill probably just get it in the morning on my way to pick up a prescription. But if Im not very tired Ill probably just get the gas on the way home. Again, unless I am tired.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         12. Express their distaste for Facebook on Facebook and threaten to leave Facebook to their Facebook friends.
        </p>
        <p>
         13. Take pictures wearing a lot of makeup and looking really preppy while simultaneously making a hard facial expression and holding up what they consider to be a gangster sign. Potential caption:
         <em>
          Straight thuggin.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i2.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/funny-facebook-fails-white-girls-gang-signs.jpg">
          <img alt="funny facebook fails white girls gang signs wtf cool stuff tumblr funny pics the best redneck engineering diy uncategorized my fave meme funny pics funny video funny video foreigners fails funny pics drunks funny pics cool stuff amazing cool stuff  Tweet That: 25 Hilarious Twitter &amp; Facebook Fails " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/cheezfailbooking.files.wordpress.com/2011/12/funny-facebook-fails-white-girls-gang-signs.jpg?w=400" title="funny-facebook-fails-white-girls-gang-signs"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         14. Ask seemingly redundant questions.
         <em>
          Its cool to do a bunch of meth and babysit 20 six year-olds, right?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         15. Write a status in another language.
         <em>
          Parce que, Je suis tres intelligente!!
         </em>
        </p>
