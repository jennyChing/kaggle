      <h1>
       Ryan Cabrera Reveals Leg Tattoo of Ryan Goslings Face
      </h1>
     </div>
     <!-- / heading -->
     <div class="post-info">
      <div class="left">
       Published at 2:13 pm EST, April 3, 2013
      </div>
      <div class="right">
       <a class="print-page" href="javascript:void(0)" onclick="window.print();return false;" title="Print Page">

      <div class="image-wrapper onetwentypx">
       <a class="enlarge-photo-link" href="http://www.hispanicallyspeakingnews.com/uploads/images/article-images/Ryan_Cabrera_Tattoo_of_Ryan_Goslings_Face_thumb.jpg" title="Ryan Cabrera Reveals Leg Tattoo of Ryan Goslings Face">
        <img alt="Ryan Cabrera Reveals Leg Tattoo of Ryan Goslings Face" height="114" src="/images/sized/uploads/images/article-images/Ryan_Cabrera_Tattoo_of_Ryan_Goslings_Face_thumb-180x115.jpg" width="180"/>
       </a>
       <p class="photo-credits">
        Photo: Ryan Cabrera Reveals Leg Tattoo of Ryan Gosling's Face
       </p>
       <p class="enlarge-photo">
        <a class="enlarge-photo-link" href="http://www.hispanicallyspeakingnews.com/uploads/images/article-images/Ryan_Cabrera_Tattoo_of_Ryan_Goslings_Face_thumb.jpg" title="Ryan Cabrera Reveals Leg Tattoo of Ryan Goslings Face">
         Click Here to Enlarge Photo
        </a>
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Some get tattoos in remembrance of lost love ones. Others get tattoos because they love to wear art on their bodies.
      </p>
      <p>
       However, if youre singer Ryan Cabrera, you get tattoos as part of a game or dare.
      </p>
      <p>
       Recently, while walking down a red carpet in Las Vegas, Cabrera showed off the leg tattoo he recently had done; a tattoo of Ryan Goslings face.
      </p>
      <p>
       Yep, the 30-year-old singer now has the A-lister permanently on his body.
      </p>
      <p>
       So why would he do it?
      </p>
      <p>
       According to Cabrera he and his friends like to play a game called Tattoo Roulette. They each agree to get a tattoo without seeing it beforehand. The tattoo receiver has no idea what the tattoo is until the ink is complete.
      </p>
      <p>
       Cabrera has numerous tattoos, and because of Tattoo Roulette a unicorn and a Care Bear are among them.
      </p>
      <p>
       The On the Way Down singer recently announced he would be going out on tour with Teddy Geiger and Tyler Hilton in May.
      </p>
      <p>
       Cabrera was born in Dallas, Texas to a Colombian-American father. He has famously dated Ashlee Simpson, Audrina Patridge, Lisa Origliasso of The Veronicas, and Elvis granddaughter Riley Keough.
      </p>
