        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Kendall Jenner's kimono: shop her boho chic look for $44
        </h1>
        <div class="article_byline partner">
         <span class="vcard author">
          <span class="fn" itemprop="author">
           <a class="url" href="http://hollywoodlife.com/" target="_blank">
            Hollywood Life
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="date updated" content="2014-08-03" itemprop="datePublished">
          | 8.3.2014
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content post-content" itemprop="articleBody">
         <br/>
         The reality star turned professional model gave her designer threads a rest and opted for a much more affordable look from PacSun! SHOP for the fabulous piece from her very own collection right here.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/celeb/kendall-jenner/" target="_blank">
          Kendall Jenner
         </a>
         , 18, stopped by the Dallas, Texas, PacSun location on July 24, 2014 to meet her fans and show off her and little sister,
         <a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/celeb/kylie-jenner/" target="_blank">
          Kylie Jenner's
         </a>
         , new fall clothing line. Kendall looked super chic, rocking one of their own designs, which you can purchase from their collection today!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Kendall Jenner's Kimono - Get Her PacSun Look:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Kendall and Kylie spent the day at the NorthPark Center Store in Dallas, Texas to debut their fall PacSun collection, sign some autographs, and take a few photos. We love the entire collection and think it's perfect for transitioning into fall.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         We especially love the black kimono Kendall wore, which can be bought
         <a href="http://www.pacsun.com/kendall-%26-kylie/maxi-kimono-0731462870073.html?start=4&amp;cgid=kendall-and-kylie-jenner-womens&amp;dwvar_0731462870073_color=001" target="_blank">
          HERE
         </a>
         . The maxi kimono retails for $44.95 and is perfect for transitioning into next season.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The lightweight material will keep you cool, comfortable and very fashionable this season. The colorful detailing adds a bright pop of color against black fabric, and we love how inexpensive it is! Whether you rock it over a bikini or layer it over jeans as we head into fall, there's so many ways to wear it.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         We love how versatile the Kendall and Kylie maxi kimono is. You can style it just like Kendall did by wearing a very basic white tank top or crop top. Pair the kimono with denim shorts and ankle booties to complete your look.
