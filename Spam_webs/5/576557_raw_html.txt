           6 Strategic Games Thatll Have You Glued to the TV and Game Console
          </h1>

          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Few things are more engrossing than video games. Movies and television series offer a finite viewing opportunity and dont typically incorporate interactive elements. Thats why
            <a href="http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/news/washington/story/2012-01-26/edcuational-video-games-white-house/52908052/1" target="_blank">
             popular video games
            </a>
            earn legions of fans who are addicted to the experience. Browse this list of six strategic games to see how many have sunk their claws into your brain and kept you glued to the screen.
           </p>
           <h2>
            The Sims Games
           </h2>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/sims2.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31932" height="263" src="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/sims2.jpg" width="350"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            While there are a variety to choose from in this collection, gamers find The Sims 2, released in 2004, to have highly addictive qualities. (Thats not to say The Sims 3 isnt worth your time because it is.) For starters, The Sims 2 has some fun, quirky elements that keep you entertained for hours. Rife with humor, difficulties, touching moments, and intense undertakings, this game mimics many situations and emotions from reality.
           </p>
           <p>
            Thats why you have to keep in mind that playing any Sims game is about being the puppet master, creating scenarios, relationships, and entire communities, and then watching them unfold. Given that most people would love to be able to experiment with
            <a href="http://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/the-real-story-risk/201212/control-freak-or-healthy-sense-control" target="_blank">
             some level of enhanced control
            </a>
            in the real world, this series of games earns major points for bringing gamers back again and again.
           </p>
           <h2>
            The Civilization Games
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/nuke.jpg">
             <img alt="nuke" class="aligncenter wp-image-31934" height="218" src="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/nuke-300x187.jpg" width="350"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Civ IV and V let you shepherd a group of people from primitive hunt-and-gather activities to times beyond modernity in games for multiple platforms including PC and PlayStation. The endgame is to become world dominant while engaging in a range of strategies for navigating political, social, financial, and combat situations. With so much to do and so many variables to try to manipulate, youll have a hard time stopping. In fact, you may feel you cant stop and take part in the real world because youre so absorbed in the game. Seek
            <a href="http://www.12keysrehab.com/programs/model.html" target="_blank">
             addiction treatment
            </a>
            if you miss sleep, meals, work, or school because of a video game.
           </p>
           <h2>
            The Warcraft Franchise
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/maxresdefault.jpg">
             <img alt="maxresdefault" class="aligncenter wp-image-31935" height="196" src="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/maxresdefault-300x168.jpg" width="350"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Delve into the world of orcs, elves, trolls, dwarves, and gnomes with the real-time strategy of these games. Thousands of gamersparticipate and interact within the same world simultaneously. Interwoven with sophisticated
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/video-games/8859915/Top-15-Fantasy-worlds-in-video-games.html" target="_blank">
             fantasy
            </a>
            storylines, these games envelop any gamer who dares to start.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Total War Games
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Tuetonic_Order_02.jpg">
             <img alt="Tuetonic_Order_02" class="aligncenter wp-image-31936" height="221" src="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Tuetonic_Order_02-300x190.jpg" width="350"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Take strategy to a whole new level with the games in this series. Among the standouts on this PC-based game are Shogun and Shogun II. They feature amazing visuals, especially in the war scenes where thousands of individually rendered graphical elements create a feast for the eyes. Rome and Rome 2 also rank high on the list for artfully combining intense, in-your-face battle experiences with turn-based activities off the battlefield. Brilliant for its combination of different strategic opportunities, gamers cant seem to get enough Rome.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Warhammer 40,000: Dawn of War
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/img-800x-watermarked-screen05.jpg">
             <img alt="img-800x-watermarked-screen05" class="aligncenter wp-image-31937" height="196" src="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/img-800x-watermarked-screen05-300x168.jpg" width="350"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Set in the far, far future (try to imagine the 41st millennium), gamers play against a savage and brutal backdrop that lets them put their military strategies to the test. This game for PCs drops you right in the middle of the action, making it difficult to pause and walk away. Engage in minimal building and management. Instead, focus most of your attention on intense warfare. This real-time strategy phenom infuses game play with an unusual amount of violence for this genre.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Company of Heroes
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Company-of-Heroes.jpg">
             <img alt="Company-of-Heroes" class="aligncenter wp-image-31938" height="262" src="http://cdn.geekpr0n.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Company-of-Heroes-300x225.jpg" width="350"/>
            </a>
           </p>
