         <h2 class="date-header">
          Tuesday, October 8, 2013
         </h2>
         <div class="post hentry uncustomized-post-template">
          <a name="7847294265978378787">
          </a>
          <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.eyeadorepretty.com/2013/10/too-faced-pretty-rebel-palette-review.html">
            Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette: Review &amp; Swatches
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="post-header-line-1">
          </div>
          <div class="post-body entry-content">
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-O-t1cMJgRNQ/UlNSmdzI_LI/AAAAAAAABIg/vk8ro0PjY7s/s1600/e5a6c6f8-627c-4dde-a57b-9a64017a819e_zpscdb01372.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img alt="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-O-t1cMJgRNQ/UlNSmdzI_LI/AAAAAAAABIg/vk8ro0PjY7s/s400/e5a6c6f8-627c-4dde-a57b-9a64017a819e_zpscdb01372.jpg" title="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" width="400"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <br/>
           it's been a while since i've been tempted to buy eyeshadows. between my MAC palettes, my NARS eyeshadow duos and my Urban Decay palettes, i've got more eyeshadows than i'll ever use in a lifetime. coupled witht the fact that most days i find myself keeping it simple for work, it just doesn't make sense to invest in more eyeshadows. but every now and then a palette comes along that i simply can't resist.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           i debated purchasing this palette for weeks. i swatched it over and over again every time i went into Ulta or Sephora and for weeks i resisted temptation and talked myself out of buying it. and then i caved. and i have no regrets. look at how stunning it is...
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-nkwS3Wkc30g/UlNVm7YMynI/AAAAAAAABIs/MqohVjuckIA/s1600/ac413bf5-5358-4f6b-9a01-9a46c7668f92_zpsfea495f1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img alt="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" border="0" height="172" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-nkwS3Wkc30g/UlNVm7YMynI/AAAAAAAABIs/MqohVjuckIA/s400/ac413bf5-5358-4f6b-9a01-9a46c7668f92_zpsfea495f1.jpg" title="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" width="400"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <br/>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-oGaXSoUowQA/UlNXkZUaPjI/AAAAAAAABI4/tDRpv_8-zQQ/s1600/86a09cdd-e560-4af0-85f1-c16f05bb0be3_zps2f111ffc.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img alt="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" border="0" height="183" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-oGaXSoUowQA/UlNXkZUaPjI/AAAAAAAABI4/tDRpv_8-zQQ/s400/86a09cdd-e560-4af0-85f1-c16f05bb0be3_zps2f111ffc.jpg" title="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" width="400"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <br/>
           </div>
           this palette is filled with richly pigmented jewel-toned eyeshadows. each shade is so buttery soft and the color-payoff is phenomenal. the Pretty Rebel palette contains mostly frosts (there are 2 matte shadows in this palette), so if you're not a fan of that texture, this may not be the palette for you.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           i was super impressed with the pigmentation of these shadows. the swatches below were all on my bare skin without a primer or base. the only shade that i had some trouble out of was Badass (the blue shade), which took 2 swipes of my finger to get the pigmentation that you see in the swatch below.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-l3wt853nRzE/UlNZPhjeiDI/AAAAAAAABJE/cE-G5XOSgZE/s1600/88B446AF-24EB-45EB-9ED4-3A4851CDBD4A-1112-000000F1B4AE2DAE_zpsb58698d3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img alt="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" border="0" height="287" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-l3wt853nRzE/UlNZPhjeiDI/AAAAAAAABJE/cE-G5XOSgZE/s400/88B446AF-24EB-45EB-9ED4-3A4851CDBD4A-1112-000000F1B4AE2DAE_zpsb58698d3.jpg" title="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" width="400"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <i>
             left to right: Dainty, Charming, Girly, Totally Fetch and Miss Sparkles
            </i>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <br/>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-35qzW_KCxV8/UlNZViWz8aI/AAAAAAAABJM/iVkJ7SmQTDM/s1600/5BC58317-17FD-453B-88E5-4717238FE17A-1112-000000F1AB2DC849_zpsa989974f.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img alt="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" border="0" height="335" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-35qzW_KCxV8/UlNZViWz8aI/AAAAAAAABJM/iVkJ7SmQTDM/s400/5BC58317-17FD-453B-88E5-4717238FE17A-1112-000000F1AB2DC849_zpsa989974f.jpg" title="Too Faced Pretty Rebel Palette" width="400"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <i>
             left to right: Ringleader, Gangsta, Instigator, Badass and Jailbird
            </i>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <br/>
           </div>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
            the Too Faced Pretty Rebel palette is limited edition and retails for $46 and is available online through
            <a href="https://www.toofaced.com/p-1379-pretty-rebel-beauty-eye-shadow-palette.aspx?" target="_blank">
             Too Faced
            </a>
            , or at
            <a href="http://www.ulta.com/ulta/browse/productDetail.jsp?productId=xlsImpprod5530011" target="_blank">
             Ulta
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://www.sephora.com/pretty-rebel-eyeshadow-palette-P380831?skuId=1533447" target="_blank">
             Sephora
            </a>
           </div>
