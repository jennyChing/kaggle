    <h1>
     MBRSS Redirect launch new subcontractor compliance and self-assessment tax return services
    </h1>
    <!-- Article Start -->
    <p class="articleIntro">
     Redirect the respected and forward thinking company specialising in self-assessment services and subcontractor compliance, have launched their website.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Posted via
     <a href="http://www.industrytoday.co.uk">
      Industry Today
     </a>
     . Are you into it?  Follow us on Twitter
     <a href="http://twitter.com/IndustryToday">
      @IndustryToday
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     Redirect (part of the MBRSS Group) the respected and forward thinking company specialising in self-assessment services and subcontractor compliance, have launched their website at
     <a href="http://www.mbrss-redirect.co.uk" target="_blank" title="MBRSS Redirect">
      www.mbrss-redirect.co.uk
     </a>
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: center;">
     <img alt="" src="http://industrytoday.co.uk/app/webroot/files/image/Redirect_1401706688.jpg" style="height:362px; width:512px"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.mbrss-redirect.co.uk/subcontractor-compliance-management.php" target="_blank" title="Subcontractor compliance management">
      Subcontractor compliance management
     </a>
     provided by Redirect, provides support to businesses so they can work more effectively. The compliance management service will minimise the administrative burden associated with resourcing due to the Redirect team ensuring good practice is employed without exception and all HMRC and employment requirements are met whatever the industry.
    </p>
    <p>
     Additionally, Redirect have a dedicated legal and tax team to ensure they remain compliant and up to date with latest legislation. Their support team is on hand to offer advice and planning around tax affairs, tax planning and completing and submitting HMRC compliant tax returns.
    </p>
    <p>
     Carley Robinson, Business Director at Redirect said:
    </p>
    <p>
     Redirect has spent over 6 years working with resources from the construction sector to ensure that companies understand their responsibilities when engaging bonifide contractors and that they and their contractors work within the prescribed HMRC guidelines.
    </p>
    <p>
     Compliance is the cornerstone of our business. We have invested considerable resources into ensuring we provide the right service for our clients and the wider industry. The new legislation has confirmed our approach to compliance is the right one. Our clients appreciate the transparency and the support we provide to help them manage their resource requirements effectively and compliantly.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://issuu.com/mbrss/docs/redirect" target="_blank" title="MBRSS Redirect Brochure">
      <img alt="" src="http://industrytoday.co.uk/app/webroot/files/image/Redirect 1_1401706713.jpg" style="float:left; height:359px; margin:5px; width:253px"/>
     </a>
     The new legislation will have an impact on all industries, but will only negatively impact companies that are not operating within the confines of the law and have not made the necessary investment in their business model to ensure that they are compliant. Our Redirect Standard, sets the benchmark for compliant and quality resourcing for the industry. We do not rely on gimmicks or false promises to attract clients, which on occasion means we may lose a prospect, however those that work with us are reassured of our commitment to serve the industry properly.
    </p>
    <p>
     Redirect offers a self-assessment tax return service for sole traders and subcontractors that is cost effective, efficient, hassle free and starts at just 120 plus VAT.
    </p>
    <p>
     For further information on Redirects Self-Assessment Tax Return Service, Resourcing Services and HMRC Compliance, download the services brochure
     <a href="http://www.mbrss-redirect.co.uk/" target="_blank" title="MBRSS Redirect">
      here
     </a>
     .
    </p>
    <p>
     ENDS.
    </p>

    </p>

Our pragmatic approach means we can help you understand your exposure, provide you with a realistic picture of whats involved and help you get the best value for your money.
