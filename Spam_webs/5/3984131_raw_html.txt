      <p>
       If you have a trip to Yosemite National Park in your sights, then you would do well to pick up the recently released book
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.triumphbooks.com/yosemite-adventures-products-9781600789144.php">
         Yosemite Adventures: 50 Spectacular Hikes, Climbs and Winter Treks
        </a>
       </strong>
       by Matt Johanson.
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_32191" style="width: 670px">
       <a href="http://www.hikebiketravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Yosemite-NP-199hsrs.jpg" rel="lightbox[32190]">
        <img alt='"Yosemite National Park"' class="size-full wp-image-32191" height="437" src="http://www.hikebiketravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Yosemite-NP-199hsrs.jpg" width="660"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Yosemite National Park
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       I got a small taste of Yosemite last December and its a place that left me wanting more.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
        <strong>
         This book presents loads of adventure ideas across the four seasons
        </strong>
        .
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       Suggestions for snowshoeing and cross-country skiing are provided. Some of the suggestions are super easy and family-friendly whereas one of the adventures  for the hardy and skilled  can take anywhere from three to six days as you journey 39 miles across Tuolumne Meadows.
      </p>
      <p>
       Loads of hikes and backpacking trips are included in the book and as Hans Florine, a speed climber and 30 year veteran of the Yosemite area suggests in the forward, Matt has
       <em>
        listed some of the best but also some of the less-frequented areas
       </em>
       . Thats the sort of informationI know I like to get  a favourite hotspot only a local would know about.
      </p>
      <p>
       Ahandful of rock climbing suggestions with difficulties ranging from 5.0 to 5.10 are provided; most are in the 5.7 range. Its been a while since Ive been rock climbing, but I do like the way the routes are drawnout on the actual rock face  and then a description provided, pitch by pitch.
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_32194" style="width: 275px">
       <a href="http://www.hikebiketravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Yosemite-adventures.jpg" rel="lightbox[32190]">
        <img alt='"Yosemite Adventures by Matt Johanson"' class="size-full wp-image-32194" height="425" src="http://www.hikebiketravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Yosemite-adventures.jpg" width="265"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Yosemite Adventures by Matt Johanson
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Another feature I likearethe two maps presented at the beginning of the book showing the location of all 50 adventures.Each map is afforded two pages and all adventures are numbered. Its verylogically presented.
      </p>
      <p>
       More than
       <strong>
        100 colour photographs and another 48 maps
       </strong>
       (on a much smaller scale) round out the book  and provide lots of visual inspiration to visit. Youll also find elevation charts and insider tips, where applicable. Mattalso tells you how to go about getting a permit to climb Half Dome  something thats on my bucket list, even with the crowds.
      </p>
      <p>
       With this book in your hand, the biggest problem youll have is deciding where to spend your precious time in Yosemite National Park. It will definitely be coming with me on my next visit.
      </p>
      <p>
       The book can be purchased on the
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.triumphbooks.com/yosemite-adventures-products-9781600789144.php">
         publisherswebsite
        </a>
       </strong>
       for $US19.95 or$CDN 21.95.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
         YOSEMITE ADVENTURES BOOK GIVEAWAY
        </span>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        I have two books to give away
       </strong>
       , providing your mailing address is in Canada or the US. Just leave a comment below and tell me what season and what type of adventure youd do if you visited
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.nps.gov/yose/index.htm">
         Yosemite National Park
        </a>
       </strong>
       . I will randomly draw two names at 9 AM MST on Tuesday, September 9th.
      </p>
