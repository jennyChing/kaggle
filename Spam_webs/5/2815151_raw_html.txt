          Dan, my boyfriend, overheats. Hes a human radiator. So for cycling or running in cooler weather he wants a gilet to keep his core warm with maximum ventilation for his arms. So Ive gone on the hunt for gilets with Dan in mind and Ive collated the best I can find. However, I must say its been a tall order trying to find stylish activewear gilets, there are plenty of highly functional options out there that sadly ignore style detailing altogether.
          <p>
           In the final post in this series on jackets, Im guilty of
           <em>
            Rapha
           </em>
           favouritism again, featuring two of their pieces but they really do fit the brief. The
           <em>
            Outlier g
           </em>
           ilet (at the time of writing this) is currently out of stock but I like it so much, I decided to include it anyway in the hope that they restock.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Gilets  My Top 6 Selection:
          </h2>
          <p>
