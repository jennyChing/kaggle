          <h2>
           <em>
            Theyre going to have to take him from my cold, dead hands before they get a hold of him
           </em>
           
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a href="http://tftppull.freethoughtllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/rottweiller.png">
            <img alt="rottweiller" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-12737" height="411" src="http://tftppull.freethoughtllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/rottweiller.png" width="790"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Moreauville,Louisiana  A town inLouisiana has recently voted to banall Pit Bulls and Rottweilers, calling them vicious breeds of dogs. Current owners of these specific breeds have just one more month to either move out of town, or hand their family pet over to the government to be killed.
          </p>
          <p>
           While there has traditionally been heavy debate over larger breeds of dogs, in recent years the debate has began to die down, because an overwhelming majority of people now agree that like humans, a dogs temperament and personality arelargely based on their upbringing and life experiences.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sadly, town officials in Moreauville,Louisiana are stuck in a very immature state of mind where they are basically prejudice against breeds of dogs.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dog owners were notified of the recent ordinance by mail, in a letter which stated that the dogs will be taken away bythe local government and killed on December 1st.
          </p>
          <p>
           The letter reads:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            TO ALL PITT BULL AND ROTTWEILER OWNERS:
           </p>
           <p>
            PLEASE BE INFORMED THAT THE VILLAGE OF MOREAUVILLE IS ENFORCING THE ORDINANCE THAT WAS PASSED ON OCTOBER 13TH, 2014 THAT BANS PITT BULLS AND ROTTWEILERS WITHIN THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF MOREAUVILLE, LA.
           </p>
           <p>
            YOU ARE HEREBY GIVEN NOTICE THAT YOU HAVE UNTIL DECEMBER 1, 2014 TO REMOVE YOUR PITT BULLS OR ROTTWEILERS FROM YOUR PREMISES. IF YOUR PITT BULLS OR ROTTWEILERS ARE NOT REMOVED BY THIS DATE, YOUR ANIMALS WILL BE IMPOUNDED AND TRANSPORTED TO A VETERINARY CLINIC FOR FURTHER DISPOSITION. YOU WILL ALSO BE FINED FOR VIOLATING THE ORDINANCE.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           KALB News channel 5 asked town officialPenn Lemoine why the ordinance was put into place, and if there were any documented dog attacks in the area to justify the recent decision. Lemoine responded by saying that there were dog attacks, but they were not documented, and he claimed that these specific breeds of dogs were running the street, and frightening neighbors.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            We had several residents that were complaining about not being able to walk along the neighborhoods because these dogs were basically running along town,
           </em>
           Lemoine said.
          </p>
          <p>
           When asked about the disposing of the dogs, Lemoine attempted to dodge the question saying 
           <em>
            Id rather not elaborate on that.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="screen-shot-2014-11-23-at-5-31-19-am" class="alignleft" height="300" src="http://www.theind.com/images/screen-shot-2014-11-23-at-5-31-19-am.png" width="194"/>
           When pressed further about whether or not the government was going to be killing the animals, Lemoinefinally admitted
           <em>
            If thats what the ordinance says, then thats what it says.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Some local residents are refusing to go along with the new ordinance, and have promised not to back down easily.
          </p>

          <blockquote>
           <p>
            OHara Owens is trying to spend as much time as she can with her beloved pit bull, Zeus,

            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            If anything ever happened to him, I would just shut down, she said.

             OHara suffers from severe neck problems and has been forced to wear a halo brace and use a wheelchair.
