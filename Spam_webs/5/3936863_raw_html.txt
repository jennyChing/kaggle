      <div class="news_detail doc">
       <h3>
        Strengthen International Technical Exchanges, Face Together Food Security Challenges of Agriculture
       </h3>
       <!-- &lt;div class="date"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;-->
       <div align="center">
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:16px;color:#000000;">
         <strong>
          Strengthen International Technical Exchanges, Face Together Food Security Challenges of Agriculture
         </strong>
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <br/>
       <div align="right">
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
         - Muyang participated 2104 APCE and attained CCTV concerns
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <br/>
       <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        <strong>
         APEC Food Technology, Facility and Resource Exhibition
        </strong>
        was held form 14th to 16th in Beijing. Muyang, as the special enterprise, attended the meeting and China National Food Authority, COFCO, the American Soybean Export Council, the regional exhibition NEW ZEALAND, JAPAN, Peru, Malaysia and other 21 APEC economies and relevant enterprises participate the exhibition and conference.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <div align="center">
        <img alt="" src="/public/images/cms/53f09d8f84754.png"/>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        This exhibition focuses on the food storage technology and food saving technology, and Muyang Drying Equipment Company and Steel Silo Company as representatives to attend the exhibition. Director General of State Grain Administration Zhengxiao Ren visited Muyang stand and asked in detail about the development of Muyang storage and drying in recent years, and made affirms on Muyangs contribution on the protection of national food security.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <div align="center">
        <img alt="" src="/public/images/cms/53f09769d9efe.png"/>
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <div align="center">
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
         Director General of State Grain Administration, Deputy Director, President of Muyang Fantian Ming, General Manager of Drying Equipment Company
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <div align="center">
        <img alt="" src="/public/images/cms/53f0978f176a7.png"/>
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <div align="center">
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
         General Manager of Muyang Drying Equipment Company was making details on Muyang silo products
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <br/>
       <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        Director Zhai made much more interests in input-output ratio for wheat and drying models on corns and rice and the cost of heat drying. Currently, Muyang drying products had been sold to various well-known grain and oil processing enterprises throughout the country and exported to Thailand, the Philippines, Sri Lanka and many other countries and regions, widely recognized.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <div align="center">
        <img alt="" src="/public/images/cms/53f097a8b73b1.png"/>
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <div align="center">
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
         CCTV Made Report on APCE
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <div align="center">
        <img alt="" src="/public/images/cms/53f097d70e0d1.png"/>
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <div align="center">
        <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
         CCTV Interview for General Manager of Muyang Drying Company
        </span>
        <br/>
       </div>
       <br/>
       <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        Asia-Pacific agricultural population accounts for the proportion of the world's agricultural as high as 73%, and agricultural economy occupies a crucial position. 2013 APEC informal leadership meeting of the twenty-first, and the Council of Ministers have made it clear that food security challenges would be as one of the priority areas of APEC and the emphasis on strengthening cooperation in the transfer of technology, and actively respond to climate change and pressure on resources and the
       </span>
       <span style="font-family:Verdana;font-size:14px;color:#000000;">
        environment challenges. Muyang Drying Equipment and Steel Silo Company have engaged in grain drying and storage technology and related product design and development.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </div>
