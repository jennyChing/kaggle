        <div id="article-content">
         <p>
          Nothing motivates us better at the gym than good music.
         </p>
         <p>
          Why?
         </p>
         <p>
          Because music engages our brains and determines our levels of concentration and alertness. Listening to music also increases levels of optimism and pleasure, which is why it's a perfect way to motivate and push ourselves when we're working out; it makes the experience more enjoyable.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://tcnjjournal.pages.tcnj.edu/2013/04/13/the-science-behind-music/" target="_blank" title="The Science Behind Music">
           Research has shown
          </a>
          that listening to classical music can slow down your heart and breathing rates. However, genres such as rock, pop and dance help clear our minds when we're in the middle of stressful and complex activities - making them perfect genres for your gym playlist.
         </p>
         <p>
          Armed with that knowledge, we've put together a workout playlist full of upbeat dance tunes that will compliment your cardio training perfectly. Whether you're running, rowing, biking or cross-training - this playlist is designed to keep you motivated and feeling energised.
         </p>

          (traindecibel) for more workout playlists.
         </p>
