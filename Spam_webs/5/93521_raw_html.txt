       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Granny Heart Crochet Pattern {Daisy in the Center}
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-05-03T17:22:00+00:00">
         May 03
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          Lauren
         </span>
        </span>
        
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://daisycottagedesigns.net/crochet/granny-heart-crochet-pattern-daisy-in-the-center/#comments">
          24 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.daisycottagedesigns.net/free-crochet-patterns" title="Granny Square Heart Pattern by Lauren Brown, on Flickr">
          <img alt="This granny heart crochet pattern has a twist - a beautiful daisy in the center. Perfect for use as a crochet dishcloth or a beautiful bunting, it's sure to brighten up any room. " class="alignnone" height="480" src="//farm8.staticflickr.com/7451/14074242726_ba9a1300b2_z.jpg" title="This granny heart crochet pattern has a twist - a beautiful daisy in the center. Perfect for use as a crochet dishcloth or a beautiful bunting, it's sure to brighten up any room. " width="640"/>
         </a>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Since spring is now in full swing, Im pretty sure that spring cleaning is supposed to be finished. It hasnt even started around my house  hopefully this coming week. Last night, I whipped up this adorable granny heart dishcloth in hopes of getting myself motivated. Cute cleaning supplies are always helpful, arent they?
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          These dishcloths are quick and adorable. Theyre great for your own kitchen, and theyd also make fantastic gifts. With Mothers Day just around the corner, you still have time to make a set of these (check out this list of
          <a href="http://www.daisycottagedesigns.net/2014/05/25-free-crochet-patterns-for-mothers-day.html">
           25 Mothers Day Ideas
          </a>
          , too).
          <b>
          </b>
          <b>
           Materials:
          </b>
          <br/>
           small amounts of worsted weight yarn
          <br/>
          
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008J140RQ/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008J140RQ&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=daiscottdesi-20&amp;linkId=OGSBYHFAXGDSNYM6">
           H Crochet Hook
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="//ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=daiscottdesi-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B008J140RQ" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
          <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
           <!--TBN 300x250 - pos - 4 STARTS-->
           <div id="tbn-c5a32fc0-6a84-4ddc-9ac3-3e3ec424ca7b">
            <script type="text/javascript">
             window.$tbn = window.$tbn || {"d":function(){}}; $tbn.d("c5a32fc0-6a84-4ddc-9ac3-3e3ec424ca7b");
            </script>
           </div>
           <!--TBN 300x250 - pos - 4 ENDS-->
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.shareasale.com/m-pr.cfm?merchantID=29190&amp;userID=644826&amp;productID=532909252" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" border="0" src="//daisycottagedesigns.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/product_3639_-13946548949052.jpg"/>
            <br/>
            Lion Brand Kitchen Cotton Yarn  $2.96
           </a>
           <br/>
           from:
           <b>
            Craftsy.com
           </b>
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <b>
           Pattern Notes:
          </b>
          <br/>
          <b>
           <br/>
          </b>
          <b>
           Size:
          </b>
          Heart measures approximately 7 tall.
          <br/>
          <b>
           <br/>
          </b>
          <b>
           Starting Triple Crochet Cluster (STCC):
          </b>
          ch 3, wrap yarn around hook twice. Insert hook into a dc from the previous round. Pull up a loop; yarn over, pull through 2 loops; yarn over, pull through two loops. There should be 2 loops remaining on your hook. Yarn over twice, insert hook into the SAME stitch; pull up a loop; yarn over, pull through 2 loops; yarn over, pull through 2 loops. There should now be 3 loops on your hook. Yarn over, pull through all loops.
          <b>
           Triple Crochet Cluster (TCC):
          </b>
          Wrap yarn around hook twice. Insert hook into a dc from the previous round. Pull up a loop; yarn over, pull through 2 loops; yarn over, pull through two loops. There should be 2 loops remaining on your hook. Yarn over twice, insert hook into the SAME stitch; pull up a loop; yarn over, pull through 2 loops; yarn over, pull through 2 loops. There should now be 3 loops on your hook. Yarn over twice, insert hook into the SAME stitch. Pull up a loop; yarn over, pull through 2 loops; yarn over, pull through 2 loops; There should now be 4 loops on your hook. Yarn over, pull through all loops.
          <b>
           Half Treble Crochet (HTC):
          </b>
          Bunny Mummys pattern calls for half treble crochets. Heres how you do those:Wrap yarn around hook twice. Insert hook into a stitch on the previous round. Pull up a loop. yarn over, pull through 2 loops. You should now have 3 loops on your hook. Yarn over, pull through all three loops.
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.daisycottagedesigns.net/free-crochet-patterns" title="heart shaped dishcloth crochet pattern by Lauren Brown, on Flickr">
            <img alt="This granny heart crochet pattern has a twist - a beautiful daisy in the center. Perfect for use as a crochet dishcloth or a beautiful bunting, it's sure to brighten up any room. " class="alignnone" height="640" src="//farm3.staticflickr.com/2935/13910703647_8322d6f533_z.jpg" title="This granny heart crochet pattern has a twist - a beautiful daisy in the center. Perfect for use as a crochet dishcloth or a beautiful bunting, it's sure to brighten up any room. " width="480"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
           <div style="text-align: justify;">
            Now that weve got all that out of the way, were ready to get started.
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: justify;">
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: justify;">
            <b>
             Daisy Center:
            </b>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: justify;">
            ch 3.
            <br/>
            <b>
             Round 1:
            </b>
            12 dc into the first ch. Sl st to the top of the first dc (not the chains). Cut yarn and weave ends. (12)
            <br/>
            <b>
             Round 2:
            </b>
            ch 3, STCC in the same stitch, ch 2. *TCC in the next stitch, ch 2* around. Sl st to the top of the STCC. Cut yarn and weave ends. (12 clusters and 12 ch 2 spaces made)
            <br/>
            <b>
             <br/>
            </b>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           Have your daisy ready? Great! Now head on over to
           <a href="http://bunnymummy-jacquie.blogspot.co.uk/2012/06/sunburst-granny-hearts.html">
            this post from Bunny Mummy
           </a>
           to make the granny heart portion of the dishcloth  the directions begin about halfway through the post. At the end, dont sl st. Just come back over here and do the final round.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Final Round.
           </b>
           <br/>
           sc in the first 5 stitches, 2 sc in the next stitch, *sc in the next 2 stitches, 2 sc in the next stitch* two more times. Sc in the next 24 stitches (this includes the ch 1 spaces). At the bottom of the heart, sc, ch 2, sc. Sc in the next 24 stitches. 2 sc in the next stitch, *sc in the next 2 stitches, 2 sc in the next stitch* two more times. Sc in the next 5 stitches. Sl st to the first sc. Cut yarn and weave ends.
          </p>
          <p>
           Now, make a few more as dishcloths or string them together as a beautiful bunting.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://daisycottagedesigns.net/crochet/10-crochet-square-with-daisy-center/">
            <img alt="Daisy+Heart+Granny+Square.png" class="aligncenter wp-image-1050 size-full" height="640" src="//daisycottagedesigns.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Daisy+Heart+Granny+Square.png" width="427"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           If youd like to make this heart into a square, the pattern for that is available
           <a href="http://daisycottagedesigns.net/crochet/10-crochet-square-with-daisy-center/">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://daisycottagedesigns.net/crochet/granny-heart-purse-crochet-pattern/">
            <img alt="Granny Heart Purse" class="aligncenter wp-image-1851 size-full" height="640" src="//daisycottagedesigns.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Small-Purse-Crochet-Pattern.jpg" width="427"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           You could also make two hearts and join them to make a purse. Find that pattern
           <a href="http://daisycottagedesigns.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Small-Purse-Crochet-Pattern.jpg">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
