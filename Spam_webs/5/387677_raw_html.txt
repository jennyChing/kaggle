        <p>
         <strong>
          About the project:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         A distributor of reusable Healthcare products used in hospitals required a controlled environment to store mattresses and other items. After a washing and sterilising cycle these products are redistributed in the Healthcare Industry.
        </p>

         <span id="more-1061">
         </span>

          Solutions:
         </strong>

        </p>

        </p>
