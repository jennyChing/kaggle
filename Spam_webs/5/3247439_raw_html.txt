          No Dye Glitter Easter Eggs
         </h1>
         <div class="post-meta">

         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 16pt; color: #333333;">
            No Dye Glitter Easter Eggs
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://momapprovedreviewsandnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Eggsfinal.png">
            <img alt="Glitter Easter Eggs" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1941" height="568" src="http://momapprovedreviewsandnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Eggsfinal.png" width="650"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
            I love glitter! These glitter Easter eggs were a ton of fun to make. Do you want festive, beautiful Easter eggs without the mess and hassle of dying them? Well here is a super simple way to make no dye glitter Easter eggs! These are fast and pretty much mess free. You will have a little glitter to clean up but if you keep it on a paper plate you can just throw it away!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
            Materials:
           </span>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             A dozen hard boiled eggs cooled
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Clear school glue
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Paintbrush
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Ultra fine glitter in assorted colors
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Plate or bowl for rolling eggs in glitter
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
            Directions:
           </span>
          </p>
          <ol>
           <li>
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Boil your eggs and let them cool completely. I let mine sit in the fridge overnight
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Paint the egg with the clear school glue. Be sure to cover completely
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Lay egg on plate and sprinkle the glitter while rolling the egg around
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
             Repeat for remaining eggs
            </span>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
            Thats it! You now have beautiful fun glitter Easter eggs! If you want to do these as an Easter display you can get the plastic or styrofoam eggs and get the same results.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #333333;">
            Be sure to check out our other Easter projects! Our
