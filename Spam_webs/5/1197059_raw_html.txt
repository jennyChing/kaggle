    <h1 class="article_title" title="Replace Missing Tooth Vernon Hills IL">
     Replace a missing tooth with an implant in Vernon Hills, IL and enjoy lifelong function
    </h1>
    A missing tooth can be a much bigger problem than it may seem. No matter where the tooth is located in the mouth, the resulting empty space after tooth loss can affect oral function and cosmetics. For this reason, Dr. Kumar works with patients to determine how best he can replace a missing tooth in our office near Vernon Hills, IL. One of the most popular treatments for tooth replacement today is the dental implant.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    <h2>
     What are dental implants?
    </h2>
    Dental implants are tiny posts that are made from durable, biocompatible titanium. This metal is resistant to corrosion and lasts decades in the human body. Titanium is, in fact, one of the primary materials used for other types of replacement, such as hip or knee. When used in the tooth replacement process, the titanium implant takes the place of the tooth's root, which is necessary for stability and also for ongoing bone regeneration.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    Stability from implants comes from a process called osseointegration. This natural occurrence involves the growth of bone tissue around the implant in the months following insertion by your skilled implant dentist. It is due to osseointegration, as well, that implants can last a lifetime.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    Replacing a missing tooth by having a titanium post inserted into the jawbone can seem a little frightening. Patients of Dr. Kumar at Smile More Today can rest assured they are in good hands. Dr. Kumar has completed hundreds of hours of additional training in the area of dental implants and is skilled in the use of innovative technique and planning to achieve the best results in a painless procedure.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    In consultation regarding implant treatment, patients can get all of their questions answered and can arrange for their procedure to include sedation along with local anesthetic to ease any nerves they may have on treatment day. Following the precision implantation of titanium posts, patients tend to feel little if any soreness. Should mild discomfort occur, over the counter pain relievers are typically sufficient for greater comfort.
    <br/>
    <br/>
    Tooth replacement has never been so efficient. With implants, there is no need to anchor a restoration onto healthy teeth and therefore no need to modify healthy tooth structure. To experience the best method of tooth replacement and restore confidence in your smile after tooth loss, call Smile More Today in Vernon Hills.
