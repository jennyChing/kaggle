                     Hey everyone!  I just thought I'd drop a quick note about
                     <a href="http://www.polaroin.com/">

In art school I used to print a lot of my photos to look like polaroids (I made a template).  It's also pretty easy to do yourself in photoshop, but why not take all the work out of it by using a website that only takes a few clicks?

                     <br/>
                     Go nuts!  If you want to share some images feel free to
                     <a href="http://www.flickr.com/groups/1810485@N22/">
