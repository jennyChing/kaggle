        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Bakers Twine Spring Wreath
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2015-03-09T07:00:29+00:00">
          March 9, 2015
         </span>
         By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/author/blogging/" rel="author">
            Sarah
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/bakers-twine-spring-wreath/#comments">
           3 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div id="greet_block">
          <noscript>
           <div class="greet_block wpgb_cornered">
            <div class="greet_text">
             <div class="greet_image">
              <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/feed/rss" rel="nofollow">
               <img alt="WP Greet Box icon" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/plugins/wp-greet-box/images/rss_icon.png"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             Hello there! If you are new here, you might want to
             <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/feed/rss" rel="nofollow">
              <strong>
               subscribe to the RSS feed
              </strong>
             </a>
             for updates on this topic.
             <div style="clear:both">
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </noscript>
         </div>
         <p>
          Do you know what I found kinda ironic about this post for my Spring Wreath?
         </p>
         <p>
          That I had to trudge through the snow to take pictures of it. Yes, the sun is staying out longer, and the numbers on the thermometer are starting to increase (although we have yet to make it out of the negatives), which are all signs pointing to Spring, so I guess I will just have to take their word for it, right?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-1a.jpg">
           <img alt="This Baker's Twine Spring Wreath is a breeze to put together thanks to premade Easter decorations!" class="size-full wp-image-4360 aligncenter" height="987" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-1a.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Spring is on its way. I will keep telling myself that as I warm up my car in the frigid -24 cold. Spring is almost here. Have faith. Soon the mountains of snow will be gone and you can finally have that outdoor Easter Egg Hunt  just keep believing.
         </p>
         <p>
          Hence, my wreath. I am believeing that Spring will soon be here, so I better get ready! Plus, the bright and cheerful colors will make me feel better when all I can see for miles around is white.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-3.jpg">
           <img alt="This Baker's Twine Spring Wreath is a breeze to put together thanks to premade Easter decorations!" class="size-full wp-image-4362 aligncenter" height="650" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-3.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This wreath, while not the quickest wreath ever, is very simple. It takes a bit of time  about 45 minutes  to wrap the wreath form in the twine, but if you put on a good show (I reccommend a rerun of How I Met Your Mother, like where Ted proposes to Stella), youll be done before you know it.
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres what youll need to make your own version of this spring wreath:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0018N41J4/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0018N41J4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=becommarth07-20&amp;linkId=JQ23WY53WR52MTHU">
            a round styrofoam wreath form
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00DKMSR1Y/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00DKMSR1Y&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=becommarth07-20&amp;linkId=NYD5YIFINM5IK6IR">
            bakers twine
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           Easter decor, picks, flowers, etc. (I used two springs of speckled eggs and a moss covered bunny, both from Michaels)
          </li>
          <li>
           pins to attach the decor (I used
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003O8M8DW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B003O8M8DW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=becommarth07-20&amp;linkId=6CGN2DUC4BLL5LXY">
            u shaped floral pins
           </a>
           )
          </li>
          <li>
           bunting (optional  I used apremade bunting garland from Dear Lizzys 5th &amp; Frolic line. I couldnt find the one I used for sale, but a
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B009MRL4MU/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B009MRL4MU&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=becommarth07-20&amp;linkId=XN6AHXNZMTY5EBUN">
            similar one
           </a>
           like this one would also work)
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001B1YU08/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B001B1YU08&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=becommarth07-20&amp;linkId=6RJNGMNRZDUGN44G">
            alphabet stickers
           </a>
           (optional  I put happy spring on the bunting, but it is so tiny you can barely see it in photos)
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-4.jpg">
           <img alt="This Baker's Twine Spring Wreath is a breeze to put together thanks to premade Easter decorations!" class="size-full wp-image-4359 aligncenter" height="650" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-4.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I didnt take step by step photos (does anyone else want to sing ooooh baby whenever they read step by step?) because they werent really many steps. You wrap the wreath form in twine, and you pin on your decor items. Its really pretty simple.
         </p>
         <p>
          To pin the bunny on, I poked one side of the pin into the bottom of the bunny, and the other side into the wreath form.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-2.jpg">
           <img alt="This Baker's Twine Spring Wreath is a breeze to put together thanks to premade Easter decorations!" class="size-full wp-image-4361 aligncenter" height="975" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-2.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you look closely on the photo above, you can kind of see the letters on the banner. Its easy to see in person, but when you take photos of things so tiny, its hard to capture them all  especially with the crazy wing. (Spring is coming spring is coming.)
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-5.jpg">
           <img alt="This Baker's Twine Spring Wreath is a breeze to put together thanks to premade Easter decorations!" class="size-full wp-image-4363 aligncenter" height="987" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bakers-twine-spring-wreath-5.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you need some more inspiration for a Spring Wreath, you can check out some of my other wreath posts like these:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/aqua-striped-spring-wreath-with-felt-flowers/">
           <img alt="springwreathedit" class="aligncenter wp-image-4369 size-medium" height="300" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/springwreathedit-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/a-bright-summer-wreath/">
           <img alt="summer wreath" class="aligncenter wp-image-4370 size-medium" height="300" src="http://becomingmartha.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/summer-wreath-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Or of course, if all else fails, visit the motherload of wreath posts  my
          <a href="http://becomingmartha.com/the-best-wreath-tutorials-for-every-season-all-year-long/" title="The Best Wreath Tutorials for Every Holiday and Season">
           160 DIY Wreaths
          </a>
          , all organized by season and holiday!
         </p>
         <p>
          Enjoy!
         </p>
