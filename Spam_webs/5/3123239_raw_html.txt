           Miss Monsters Cthulhu Mask
          </a>
         </h1>
         <div class="byline">
          Published by

           1 Response

         </div>

        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Miss Monster (
          <a href="http://www.missmonster.com/" target="BLANK">
           website
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/missmonstermel" target="BLANK">
           Twitter
          </a>
          ) has dug deep into the darkest parts of the sea and freed the
          <i>
           Great Cthulhu
          </i>
          . . . as a wearable and creepy mask available now
          <a href="http://missmonster.bigcartel.com/" target="BLANK">
           at her online store.
          </a>
          And quite reasonably priced, coming in at $32 for the blank or $46 for the finished/colored piece.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_27830" style="width: 400px">
          <a href="http://missmonster.bigcartel.com/" target="BLANK">
           <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-27830" height="430" src="http://www.battlegrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/mask.jpg" title="mask" width="400"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Click to visit Miss Monster's shop!
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          This is either a great display piece or the first step to a wicked Halloween costume, but either way this is your chance to get out there and show the world just how much of a
          <i>
           Cthulhu
          </i>
          nerd you really are.
         </p>
        </div>
