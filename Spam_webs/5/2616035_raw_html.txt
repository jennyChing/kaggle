       <p>
        LED lighting is quickly becoming the most preferred type of lighting for both indoor and out use. It can add ambience to a room and the LED bulb is energy efficient and long lasting (they have a life span of around 50,000 hours which equates to 17.1 years).
       </p>
       <p>
        Here at LED Light Zone, we provide a wide range of LED lighting solutions which include:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Tape lights, strip lights and rope lights
        </li>
        <li>
         Tube and panel lights
        </li>
        <li>
         Flood lights, spot lights and wall washer LEDs
        </li>
        <li>
         Green solar LEDs which are ideal for outdoor use
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        If you are looking for new and exciting way to light your home, office or outdoor space this year; LEDs are one of the best ways to create the atmosphere you have always wanted. They have the ability to completely change the look of an object or piece of furniture within the home or a flower bed or driveway outside of the home.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here are some of our top 5 best value products:
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         <strong>
          1.2M Tube and Anion Lights in Green
         </strong>
          By switching to a low energy strip light bulb you can save 68% from low energy consumption. The LED strip light bulbs lifespan is 25,000 hours which is five times the lifespan of the normal fluorescent tubs enabling you to save 80% in monetary value.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Single Colour LED Floodlight
         </strong>
          Use this handy light for outdoor use on the side or front of your home or workplace or alternatively have it fitted to an out building so that you can find your way around late at night. This is also available in the colour changing option.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          4W GU10 LED Bulb 45 Degree Beam Angle 50W equiv
         </strong>
          This non dimmable spot light in an aluminium case is currently the best on the market. You are able to save more than 90% energy with this light.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          LED Solar Net Garden Light
         </strong>
          If you are looking to spruce up any big trees in your front or back garden, this is the type of lighting to use. It is easy to install and is attractive to look at as it has a fade-in, fade-out or twinkle effect option.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          LED Colour Changing Tape Starter Kit
         </strong>
          This handy and attractive starter kit contains 5 metres of flexible RGB LED Tape, 1 power supply, 1 colour controller and the LEDs are fully dimmable.
        </li>
       </ol>
