A 10-day canoe along the Dordogne River basin in France is scheduled for October 2004 with the

After my first three canoes, I bult a 2000-square-foot shop behind my cottage on Lake Santa Fe in Melrose, Fla., and designed and equipped it to build custom wood boats in relative comfort.
