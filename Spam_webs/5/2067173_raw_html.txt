				&lt;div class="entry-meta"&gt;
			&lt;span class="sep"&gt;Posted on &lt;/span&gt;&lt;a href="http://dmti.in/blog/hyperlapse-makes-timelapse-video-creation-fun/" title="7:17 am" rel="bookmark"&gt;&lt;time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-08-27T07:17:04+00:00"&gt;August 27, 2014&lt;/time&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span class="by-author"&gt; &lt;span class="sep"&gt; by &lt;/span&gt; &lt;span class="author vcard"&gt;&lt;a class="url fn n" href="http://dmti.in/blog/author/zaira-maqubool/" title="View all posts by Zaira Maqubool" rel="author"&gt;Zaira Maqubool&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;		&lt;/div&gt;&lt;!-- .entry-meta --&gt;

	&lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;
		&lt;p&gt;Yet another application but for iOS users for now&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This is the next-gen Camera app for sure. All those who have loved playing with Instagram and Vine till date, here is another app that gives you an absolutely new experience to create videos. HyperLapse can be fun for regular users but at the same time, for the creatively inclined, fresh new innovations and creative videos are bound to flood the web soon. Hyperlapse seems to have a broad future ahead.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The best thing is that you dont have to create a new account to start and then you can share your videos to your Instagram account directly.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Here is the extract of the blog that Instagram posted yesterday Read, Download and Enjoy!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;&#8220;Today, were excited to announce Hyperlapse from Instagram, a new app to capture high-quality time lapse videos even while in motion.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Traditionally, time lapse videos depend on holding your phone or camera still while you film. Hyperlapse from Instagram features built-in stabilization technology that lets you create moving, handheld time lapses that result in a cinematic look, quality and feela feat that has previously only been possible with expensive equipment&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;We designed Hyperlapse to be as simple as possible From documenting your whole commute in seconds or the preparation of your dinner from start to finish to capturing an entire sunset as it unfolds, were thrilled about the creative possibilities Hyperlapse unlocks.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Let us know your first hand reviews.&#8221;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
