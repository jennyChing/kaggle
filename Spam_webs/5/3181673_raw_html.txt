         <h1>
          Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.
         </h1>
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <img alt="windows" class="alignleft featured_image wp-post-image" height="262" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/windows-292x262.png" width="292"/>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="post-date">
           February 20, 2014
          </span>
          &amp;nbsp
         </p>
         <p>
          In 1994, Steve Jobs never even imagined the smartphone. Still this was an important year for the tech
         </p>
         <p>
          industry because it was then that the World Web Web also known today as the Internet was born. During this
         </p>
         <p>
          time there werent any iPhones, iPads, no Google, no netflix and no flat screen TVs, something that is hard to
         </p>
         <p>
          imagine for us today.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here is a trip down memory lane to see how far we have progressed since then.
         </p>
         <p class="slide-title">
          <strong>
           Most people used Windows PCs in 1994 and this how Windows looked like
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="windows" class="alignnone wp-image-1872 size-full" height="450" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/windows-e1409612768341.png" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/windows-e1409612768341.png&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Cells phones were available but expensive, didnt have touchscreens, ugly and big compared to the ones today. Check out the one from Ericsson below. The battery was as big as the phone.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="old cell phones" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1883" height="492" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/old-cell-phones-e1409612947610.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/old-cell-phones-e1409612947610.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="slide-title">
          <strong>
           Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1994. Here is the first website he published.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="first website" class="alignnone wp-image-1879 size-full" height="313" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/first-website-e1409613047795.png" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/first-website-e1409613047795.png&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           In 1994, Steve Jobs was still in charge of NeXT Software, formerly NeXT Computer. Here is the actual NeXT PC that Berners-Lee used to invent the Web, and host the first web pages.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
          </strong>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="next software and computer" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1885" height="450" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/next-software-and-computer-e1409613227334.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/next-software-and-computer-e1409613227334.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           This is Microsofts first web page in 1994. Since it had a picture it took a long time to load.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="first microsoft" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1878" height="788" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/first-microsoft-e1409613322291.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/first-microsoft-e1409613322291.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Yahoo was founded in 1994. This is their first web page.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="first yahoo website" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1882" height="426" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/first-yahoo-website.jpg" width="591"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/first-yahoo-website.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Most people used paid, dial-up online services like AOL, instead of the web. You paid for every minute you used it.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="dial up internet" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1873" height="450" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/dial-up-internet-e1409613523698.gif" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/dial-up-internet-e1409613523698.gif&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           AOL was installed using a floppy disk (or CD) that arrived most the time unasked for in the mail.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="aol floppy disk" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1884" height="450" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/aol-floppy-disk-e1409613583977.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/aol-floppy-disk-e1409613583977.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           There were no thumb drives, external USB hard drives or cloud storage. Floppy disks were used to back up files.
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="floppy disk" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1887" height="400" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/floppy-disk-e1409613614741.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/floppy-disk-e1409613614741.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="breaking-module-rendered" id="breaking-module-rendered">
          <strong>
           In 1994, Doom the legendary first-person shooter video game which has been a hit ever since arrived.
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="doom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1886" height="375" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/doom-e1409613658714.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/doom-e1409613658714.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Those who didnt play Doom played Donkey Kong.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="dk" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1877" height="535" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/dk--e1409613700596.jpg" width="600"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/dk--e1409613700596.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The video game industry was forever changed with the arrival of the Sony Playstation in 1994.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="ps1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1875" height="263" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ps1-e1409613782422.png" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ps1-e1409613782422.png&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The DVD wasnt invented until 1995. So in 1994, VHS tapes were used to watch and record movies.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="vhs" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1874" height="450" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/vhs-e1409613830320.jpg" width="600"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/vhs-e1409613830320.jpg&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Since there wasnt any streaming and no Netflix you got your got your movies from the video store.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="video store" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1880" height="450" src="http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/video-store-e1409613865673.png" width="600"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zapmylife.ca/here-is-what-tech-was-like-in-1994-no-google-no-netflix-no-iphone/&amp;media=http://zapmylife.ca/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/video-store-e1409613865673.png&amp;description=Here Is What Tech Was Like In 1994. No Google. No Netflix. No iPhone.')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The fax machine was very popular since e-mail was expensive and not everyone had it.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
