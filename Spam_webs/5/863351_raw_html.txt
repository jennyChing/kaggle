       <h1>
        Punctuation Game
       </h1>
       <span class="author">
        By Sharon
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="alignleft">
       <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

      <p>
       So you think you know your punctuation? Now you can put it to the test.
       <a href="http://www.dailywritingtips.com/review-of-eats-shoots-and-leaves/">
        Eats, Shoots and Leaves
       </a>
       , reviewed by Maeve in July, has a punctuation game online.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <center>
       <img alt="punctuation game" border="1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1043" height="269" src="http://www.dailywritingtips.com/wp-content/uploads/punctuation-game.jpg" title="punctuation game" width="400"/>
      </center>
      <br/>
      <p>
       There are to questions on the placement of the apostrophe and comma, and at the end of the game you get a score showing how much of a stickler you are for correct punctuation use.
      </p>
      <p>
       Review our tips on
       <a href="http://www.dailywritingtips.com/category/punctuation/">
        punctuation
       </a>
       , then take the quiz. Good luck! Come back and share your score in the comments.
      </p>
