         <p class="p1">
          For decades, AMG has been content to spice of the lives of Mercedes-Benz drivers, honing the all-important performance facet, which even luxury-car drivers crave, such as can easily be seen in the Mercedes-Benz
          <a href="http://www.motoringview.com/mercedes-benz-gle-450-amg-coupe-coupe-styling-performance-crossover-versatility/" title="Mercedes-Benz GLE 450 AMG Coupe  Coupe Styling and Performance with Crossover Versatility">
           <span class="s1">
            GLE 450 AMG Coupe
           </span>
          </a>
          or
          <a href="http://www.motoringview.com/the-pace-quickens-with-the-2015-mercedes-benz-s63-amg-4matic-coupe/" title="The Pace Quickens with the 2015 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG 4MATIC Coupe">
           <span class="s1">
            S63 AMG 4MATIC Coupe
           </span>
          </a>
          . With the inception of Mercedes-AMG, however, a new era has begun, an all-new brand of Mercedes that lives and breathes performance. It started with the 2015
          <a href="http://www.motoringview.com/2015-mercedes-amg-c-63-revealed/" title="2015 Mercedes-AMG C 63 Revealed">
           <span class="s1">
            Mercedes-AMG C 63
           </span>
          </a>
          , the first creation of this new brand.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          The all-new Mercedes-AMG GT S is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0 liter V8 generating 503 hp and 479 lb-ft, which can launch the new sports car to 60 mph in just 3.7 s (the base Mercedes-AMG GT engine generates 456 hp and 443 lb-ft and is 0.2 s slower 0-60 mph). Starting at $129,000, the car comes with a long list of standard features. Outside, there are standard 19- and 20-inch sport wheels, through which one can easily see red-painted brake callipers, and LED headlights. Inside, AMG Performance seats and Nappa leather upholstery. The sophisticated infotainment system features parking assistance, rearview camera, and Burmester Surround Sound. Aside from the rearview camera, safety systems include collision prevention and a suite of eight airbags.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          The release of the Mercedes-AMG GT marks the second sports car model to be completely developed in-house by the new company, featuring a unique character and thoroughbred motorsport technology. The high-performance Mercedes-AMG GT S will be launched first, starting in April 2015, and the base Mercedes-AMG GT will be released Spring 2016. As their launch dates draw close, Mercedes-AMG will release more details regarding pricing, equipment levels, and specifications.
         </p>
