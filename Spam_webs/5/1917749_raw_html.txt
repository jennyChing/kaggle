         <h1 style="text-align: justify;">
          YSL Le Teint Encre de Peau Fall 2014
         </h1>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           YSL Le Teint Encre de Peau Fall 2014
          </strong>
          will be launched July and August 2014 and based on the info I got, this one looks amazing! The new foundation offers a light yet natural finish which feels very light-weight on the skin while offering a long-lasting stay power. Based on this, it reminds me of the
          <a href="http://smashinbeauty.com/ysl-le-teint-touche-eclat-illuminating-foundation-makeup-review/" title="YSL Le Teint Touche Eclate Illuminating Foundation Makeup Review">
           YSL Le Teint Touche Eclate Illuminating Foundation
          </a>
          but which offers an all day stay.What is a plus and this means that I have to try it since I loved the YSL le teint touche illuminating foundation but with a better stay power.This new foundation besides offering a weightless feeling on the skin (amazing) it also offers a matte effect without looking heavy or cakey on the skin. This means that this will work with oily skin.
         </p>
