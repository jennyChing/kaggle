            <h2 class="title">
             <a class="journal-entry-navigation-current" href="/blog/2014/8/8/5-ways-to-get-adventurous-in-thailand.html">
              5 ways to get adventurous in Thailand
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="journal-entry-tag journal-entry-tag-post-title">
             <span class="post-snippet-2">
              <!-- Go to www.addthis.com/dashboard to customize your tools -->
              <div class="addthis_native_toolbox">
              </div>
             </span>
             <span class="share-item">
              <a href="javascript:noop();" onclick="Squarespace.Interaction.shareLink(this,'%2Fblog%2F2014%2F8%2F8%2F5-ways-to-get-adventurous-in-thailand.html','5%20ways%20to%20get%20adventurous%20in%20Thailand%20');">
               <img alt="Share Article" class="inline-icon share-icon" rel="0" src="/universal/images/transparent.png" title="Share Article"/>
               Share Article
              </a>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="body">
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               Thailand is famous all over the world for its relaxing atmosphere, wondrous massage practises and the balanced sweet-and-sour blend of its food. However, Thailand is a country brimming with opportunities for adventures, explorations and sport activities. Meditation is healthy and rewarding, but the so-called Golden Land is an attractive destination for daredevils and action-lovers, too. Here is a list of the top 5 busting things that will make you jump out of your hammock and start a new adventure.
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
              <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
               <img alt="" src="/storage/3665462673_b0bf12fa39_z.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1407492969765"/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: center; font-size: 80%;">
              Image courtesy of
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/39891373@N07/3665462673/in/photolist-6zUsmi-bpVQvh-nAyZ1V-bCUHi4-814a77-9y1tMn-bCScPi-bpWCFw-5AbxYT-5A9fiH-5Afeem-5A9zrK-5SRKq5-aCXXXa-9MeLbz-bCTRc8-2DSX8f-9SDN7q-9MdEfK-5AaKVV-5A9e1F-5Ab1VD-5Af1dj-5AaECc-5AaFzz-5Af74h-5Abtxk-5A9j8r-5AdTiW-5A9iy6-5AdCJ7-5A9mKe-5AdBAU-5Ae3e7-9QBohU-5A9hZ2-5A9JUe-5Adyub-fKZsX2-7zxKeo-acW3eh-9MdDET-bCQNqv-5kEK5V-6zYC9w-5Af2vC-5AaDmp-5AbEo6-5AdMHJ-bpVLyh">
               Ilse Reijs and Jan-Noud Hutten
              </a>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/39891373@N07/3665462673/in/photolist-6zUsmi-bpVQvh-nAyZ1V-bCUHi4-814a77-9y1tMn-bCScPi-bpWCFw-5AbxYT-5A9fiH-5Afeem-5A9zrK-5SRKq5-aCXXXa-9MeLbz-bCTRc8-2DSX8f-9SDN7q-9MdEfK-5AaKVV-5A9e1F-5Ab1VD-5Af1dj-5AaECc-5AaFzz-5Af74h-5Abtxk-5A9j8r-5AdTiW-5A9iy6-5AdCJ7-5A9mKe-5AdBAU-5Ae3e7-9QBohU-5A9hZ2-5A9JUe-5Adyub-fKZsX2-7zxKeo-acW3eh-9MdDET-bCQNqv-5kEK5V-6zYC9w-5Af2vC-5AaDmp-5AbEo6-5AdMHJ-bpVLyh">
              </a>
              <strong style="text-align: justify; font-size: 12px;">
               Diving
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <strong style="text-align: justify; font-size: 12px;">
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
              The island of Koh Tao, literally Turtles Island is one of the global snorkelling capitals. Do not be misled by its modest size (21 km squared). It is a microcosm of biodiversity, framed by enchanting clear waters, golden beaches and lush vegetation. Koh Nang Yuan, Freedom beach, Laem Thian, Mango Bay, Hin Wong Bay and Tanote Bay are perfect for day excursions and easily accessible from the road. You cannot miss Shark Bay, the most popular diving spot of the island. Despite its scary name, the bay hosts harmless and peaceful black tip reef sharks. Grab the chance to dive safely with sharks!
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               Rafting
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
               <img alt="" src="/storage/4870607359_e2ca6097d3_z.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1407493318572"/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: center; font-size: 80%;">
              Image courtesy of
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/rukakuusamo/4870607359/in/photolist-8qsfw9-8qp8SX-8qseKS-8qp8HP-6ZCYnD-5SrAkn-nSeUd3-iCiQiR-nSiPD5-cEBRR9-dAF4A2-7fB8fQ-cHH8yW-fexbkA-f8ksE8-bHdhMK-dg7js-nHD5Dt-dVLQ9P-8Ecd5r-dz2Dp1-nSjagE-o9APnC-iZar97-7Mp1sw-jW7zH-fughXM-6qCFCj-o9ANRN-qoZv2-6ZCYAz-dWDWiv-c8eZn5-iZaPmJ-iZ7rP8-fwyGS7-fTWsUh-gWwmeM-6ZCYKV-dWKzwS-4UpcSC-jZgok-jZgom-jZgoj-5QnWRC-6hzhYB-75qNKu-fe2aYB-7ScVkf-evefsR">
               Heather Sunderland
              </a>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              If you love water sport, but prefer lively rivers stream to the flat and smooth sea surface: you can rush the rapids of Southern Thailand's Phang Na province. Whether you are a white water rafter or a reckless waves-lover, you will find a tailor-made trip. The Phang Nga River has a spot for safe and comfortable cruises as well as more boisterous stretches, off-limit for beginners. Put your life jacket on and...All aboard!
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               Rock Climbing or walking
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
               <img alt="" src="/storage/4787742164_b41f659901_z.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1407493425978"/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: center; font-size: 80%;">
              Image courtesy of
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/mariachily/4787742164/in/photolist-8i5qWG-5sSRVN-5sSSU9-hiN115-8n4aVo-8n4aNy-8mZXUk-8n4biL-8n17gH-8n4aY5-8mZYU8-39qauY-6UJDDb-c41djN-aVXSZH-LDUxZ-4bMHk3-bDLMiE-76LUtr-4FoXva-2yDmmn-frcsG-6CgBnf-iHpNk1-iHoBxk-iHpKky-iHnq66-iHrMU9-aQm1g4-6oHDux-6oJEyv-6oHYeT-6oNZbW-6oNYzd-6oNx11-7YV4H7-hiMCc4-8n4dry-8n14jZ-8n47uw-6CubsR-6UDRCR-6UEvPp-8YxbFe-5PL6SG-75c7FT-8nY5U4-9Pd433-bNSESt-gg8AH">
               Maria Ly
              </a>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <span style="text-align: justify; font-size: 12px;">
               Considered the Southeast Asia Mecca for rock climbers, Railay offers challenging and overhanging routes. There are plenty of opportunities to explore caves, shorelines and sea stacks reachable by boat only. The greatest concentration of climbing sides is on Tonsai, a bay on the west side of Railay. The best place for waterfall abseiling is Doi Inthanon in Chiang Mai. With its 2,565 metres above the sea level, it is the highest mountain of the Kingdom. The peak is surrounded by a national park with hiking trails, ancient monuments and eight waterfalls diving off the mountain.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <span style="text-align: justify; font-size: 12px;">
               <br/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               Jungle Trekking
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
               <img alt="" src="/storage/615293797_4cceeaae21_z.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1407493530733"/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: center; font-size: 80%;">
              Image courtesy of
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/doxi/615293797/in/photolist-WnxpK-iecTq4-2FEH5v-hbC87-Ag8TG-aAUmTi-Ag9zN-d5uuUA-bwozRm-5StNH7-6UiD4c-fpx2op-hbBgM-6t98h-6t96d-6JHgac-hbBm8-jz9bN8-5Gb1LE-4cgKsY-mwYqk-miPcE-hbBMo-jz8sQc-56tSv2-56xZ3N-56y1Q9-56tQqc-56tPZv-56xX1b-56tRik-56xXRu-56xXsY-hbBCx-hbBz5-jzadp3-BerwE-5UE14V-hbBFL-5vsHns-5vsHKU-89EdX-4QbH7y-4QbGxh-4Q7urM-6Ui4Sc-agKHcV-7NG66v-jzb8b5-jzaZpd">
               Raymond Bosma
              </a>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/doxi/615293797/in/photolist-WnxpK-iecTq4-2FEH5v-hbC87-Ag8TG-aAUmTi-Ag9zN-d5uuUA-bwozRm-5StNH7-6UiD4c-fpx2op-hbBgM-6t98h-6t96d-6JHgac-hbBm8-jz9bN8-5Gb1LE-4cgKsY-mwYqk-miPcE-hbBMo-jz8sQc-56tSv2-56xZ3N-56y1Q9-56tQqc-56tPZv-56xX1b-56tRik-56xXRu-56xXsY-hbBCx-hbBz5-jzadp3-BerwE-5UE14V-hbBFL-5vsHns-5vsHKU-89EdX-4QbH7y-4QbGxh-4Q7urM-6Ui4Sc-agKHcV-7NG66v-jzb8b5-jzaZpd">
              </a>
              <span style="font-size: 12px; text-align: justify;">
               Jungle trekking is an ageless activity. Whether you are a well-trained sportive or out of shape, you can immerse yourself in the lively and bustling jungle. Northern Thailand is home to some of the most enchanting trekking paths full of caves and waterfalls. March is the best period of the year to visit, however raining season brings out lush and unique tropical foliage.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <span style="font-size: 12px; text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               Kayaking
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <strong>
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste" style="text-align: justify;">
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
              <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
               <img alt="" src="/storage/4528833319_6fa8e421e7_z.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1407493638512"/>
              </span>
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: center; font-size: 80%;">
              Image courtesy of
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/bjvs/4528833319/in/photolist-7Ucsn6-weiU5-bKUi9-L7hFx-hrYX9R-6ZDttm-6ZDwFY-6ZDuZQ-6ZztDZ-6ZzuTD-61aFBB-6gdyBa-6ZDwgQ-6Zzt1D-a6viTw-a6viYj-a6viJ9-a6viP3-eaY28Y-a6srN4-wZn6-wZn8-a6vhky-HL8Mc-a6sqjT-a6sptV-a6vg7y-a6sphH-eaY2qA-a6sq8P-a6vhW3-a6sq1Z-a6vf9m-4tmcJk-a6vfxG-a6vhxL-a6vhtb-a6srsK-a6vfSd-a6vhdm-a6vgu1-a6sqDX-a6vhgm-a6sr62-a6sqTB-a6vh3u-a6sosr-7Ucxhe-a6vhJb-a6vfFq">
               Benjamin Vander Steen
              </a>
             </div>
             <div style="font-size: 80%;">
              <span style="text-align: justify; font-size: 12px;">
               Forget crowded escalators and the chaos of rush hours, the underground has never been more relaxing. If you are not fond of extreme sport you can discover the heart of Thailand by staying well grounded. The Mae Hong Son Province is spotted by scenic cave, ancient caverns and geological labyrinths closed between scenic mountain facades. By lying back in a kayak you can admire cliffs emerging straight from the sea waters and caves dug by the sea. Rowing on a kayak you could find one of those hidden lagoons which became famous after the Leonardo Di Caprios movie The Beach.
              </span>
             </div>
             <div id="_mcePaste">
              On top of these sport activities, there are plenty of opportunities to discover the wild side behind Thailands contemplative and ascetic atmosphere. All you need to do is putting yourself on the line and cross it.
             </div>
             <div style="text-align: justify;">
