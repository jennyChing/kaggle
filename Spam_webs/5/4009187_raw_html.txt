         <div class="fb-like" data-href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/travel-tips/52-ideas-family-adventure-san-diego/" data-layout="button_count" data-send="false" data-show-faces="false" data-width="200">
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/5-Cedar-Creek-Falls.jpg">
        <img alt="Cedar Creek Falls" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-22199" height="200" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/5-Cedar-Creek-Falls-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       1.Follow the sound of rushing waters to the 80 ft tall
       <a href="http://www.recreation.gov/permits/Cedar_Creek_Falls/r/wildernessAreaDetails.do?contractCode=NRSO&amp;parkId=109033 " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Cedar Creek Falls
        </b>
       </a>
       ! The hike on the way up to the waterfall is often spent
       <b>
        bird watching
       </b>
       !
      </p>
      <p>
       2.Enjoy a variety of ethnic cuisine and live music at the
       <a href="http://www.littleitalysd.com/mercato/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Little Italy Mercato Market
        </b>
       </a>
       , San Diegos LARGEST farmers market, which has hundreds of vendors selling organic produce, fresh flowers, and art pieces!
      </p>
      <p>
       3.
       <a href="http://www.balboapark.org/decembernights/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         December Nights
        </b>
       </a>
       is an annual Christmas event at Balboa Park that is awaited by all. A huge holiday festival of music and food, and get this, many of the museums are even open for free!
      </p>
      <p>
       4. The
       <a href="http://www.nbcsandiego.com/news/local/Open-Air-Fish-Market-San-Diego-Tuna-Harbor-Fresh-Catch-267440101.html " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Open Air Fish Market
        </b>
       </a>
       down at the harbor lets you really experience the bustling life of a seaside town. Grab some freshly caught fish for dinner!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2-Waterfront-Park.jpg">
        <img alt="Waterfront Park" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-22200" height="200" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/2-Waterfront-Park-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       5.Let your kids splash and play at
       <a href="http://www.sdcounty.ca.gov/parks/Waterfrontpark.html" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Waterfront Park
        </b>
       </a>
       s fountain pool areas, playgrounds, and picnic field!
      </p>
      <p>
       6.Go flying at
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.sunsetcliffs.info/ " target="_blank">
         Sunset Cliffs
        </a>
        !
       </b>
       This location is known for its adventurous hang gliders, parasailers, and cliff divers!
      </p>
      <p>
       7. Cool off at
       <a href="http://aquaticabyseaworld.com/en/sandiego/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Aquatica
        </b>
       </a>
       with a peaceful ride along the lazy river or have an exhilarating adventure on one of their high-speed thrills.
      </p>
      <p>
       8. Explore the marine ecosystems in
       <a href="http://aquaticabyseaworld.com/en/sandiego/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Cabrillo National Monument
        </b>
       </a>
       s protected tidepools! Be sure to also visit the historic
       <a href="http://www.nps.gov/cabr/historyculture/old-point-loma-lighthouse.htm" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Old Point Loma Lighthouse
        </b>
       </a>
       , one of the eight original lighthouses of the west coast.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/8-Arroyo-Tapiado-Mud-Caves.jpg">
        <img alt="Arroyo Tapiado Mud Caves" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-22201" height="200" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/8-Arroyo-Tapiado-Mud-Caves-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       9. Get down and dirty in the estimated 5 million year old
       <a href="http://www.desertusa.com/anza_borrego/Mud_Caves.html" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Arroyo Tapiado Mud Caves
        </b>
       </a>
       . Search for fossils, but try not to get lost in what is said to be one of the most extensive caves in the world!
      </p>
      <p>
       10. Catch a film under the stars in the comfort of your own car at the
       <a href="http://www.santeedriveintheatre.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Santee Drive-In Theatre
        </b>
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       11. San Diego has some neighborhoods that are filled to the top with holiday spirit! Visit one of the neighborhoods hosting
       <a href="http://www.hiddensandiego.net/christmas-light-displays.php" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Christmas Card Lane
        </b>
       </a>
       to be surrounded by amazing christmas lights and holiday decorations.
      </p>
      <p>
       12. Revitalize your taste buds in downtowns
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.gaslamp.org/" target="_blank">
         Gaslamp
        </a>
       </b>
       area! Indulge yourself in the city life with its many urban cafes, rowdy bars, and shopping boutiques.
      </p>
      <p>
       13. Filled with diverse marine life,
       <a href="http://www.sandiego.com/beaches/la-jolla-cove" target="_blank">
        <b>
         La Jolla Cove
        </b>
       </a>
       s abundant reefs and caves make it a prime location for
       <b>
        snorkeling
       </b>
       and
       <b>
        kayaking!
       </b>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/18-Flower-Fields.jpg">
        <img alt="Flower Fields" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-22202" height="163" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/18-Flower-Fields-300x163.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       14. Get lost in a field of flowers at Carlsbads
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.theflowerfields.com/" target="_blank">
         Flower Fields
        </a>
        .
       </b>
       This 50 acre vibrant hillside full of blooms happens yearly during the months of April and May!
      </p>
      <p>
       15. Walk further along the cove to find the adorable seals and sea lions lounging around at the
       <a href="http://www.lajollabythesea.com/la-jolla-childrens-pool " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Childrens Pool
        </b>
       </a>
       !
      </p>
      <p>
       16. Pay tribute to fallen soldiers at
       <a href="http://www.soledadmemorial.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Mt. Soledad Veterans Memorial
        </b>
       </a>
       , a beautiful monument atop a hill that gives sight-seers a mouth-dropping view.
      </p>
      <p>
       17. Ride a ferry to
       <a href="http://www.ecoronado.com/page/coronado-island-activities " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Coronado
        </b>
        <b>
         Island
        </b>
       </a>
       where you can go ice-skating outside right next to a beautiful beach!
      </p>
      <p>
       18. Revisit the past at the historic
       <a href="http://www.oldtownsandiegoguide.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Old Town San Diego
        </b>
       </a>
       where you can shop and dine in a unique nostalgic atmosphere!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/22-SD-Fair.jpg">
        <img alt="SD Fair" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-22203" height="200" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/22-SD-Fair-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       19. Get the best greasy fair foods at
       <a href="http://www.sdfair.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         San Diego County Fair
        </b>
       </a>
       . Some of our favorites are the Blooming Onion, Triple-Decker Donut Hamburgers, and Deep-Fried Butter. Yum!
      </p>
      <p>
       20. Release your inner geek at the largest comic convention in the country,
       <a href="http://www.comic-con.org/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Comic-Con International
        </b>
       </a>
       . Hundreds of thousands attend this famous event because it is chock full of celebrity panels, artists, and video games!
      </p>
      <p>
       21. Catch some waves and soak in some sun at one of San Diegos
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.sandiego.org/what-to-do/beaches.aspx" target="_blank">
         finest beaches
        </a>
        !
       </b>
       And if you want to go to the beach at night, host a
       <b>
        bonfire
       </b>
       , eat some smores, and even go
       <b>
        grunion running
       </b>
       !
      </p>
      <p>
       22. Tour the
       <a href="http://www.teamusa.org/about-the-usoc/training-centers-and-sites/chula-vista" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Olympic Training Center
        </b>
       </a>
       and spectate over the intense Cal-State Games, such as archery, BMX, and field hockey competitions.
      </p>
      <p>
       23. Tour the most-visited naval ship museum in the world! The
       <a href="http://www.midway.org/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         USS Midway
        </b>
       </a>
       fitted with restored aircraft allows visitors to experience what it was like on a real war battleship! Stop by the HUGE
       <a href="http://www.roadsideamerica.com/story/20274" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Unconditional Surrender
        </b>
       </a>
       kissing statue right outside the museum!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/24-Snow-Mt.-Laguna.jpg">
        <img alt="Mt. Laguna" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-22204" height="204" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/24-Snow-Mt.-Laguna-300x204.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       24. San Diego is known for its sunny weather, but head on up to
       <a href="http://www.lagunamountain.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Mount Laguna
        </b>
       </a>
       or
       <a href="http://www.julianca.com/attractions/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Mount Julian
        </b>
       </a>
       for some snow! Ski, snowboard, and stuff your face with their famous Julian Apple Pie.
      </p>
      <p>
       25. Try your hand at a potters wheel and make/paint your own pottery at
       <a href="http://claytime-ceramics.com/potters-wheel" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Claytime Ceramics
        </b>
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       26. See Mother Nature at work on one of San Diegos many
       <a href="http://sandiegohikers.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         hikes
        </b>
       </a>
       . Admire the beautiful scenery on an easy paved path, or trek on a more difficult one for our more experienced outdoorsmen!
      </p>
      <p>
       27. Get away from the coast and skip some rocks, feed some ducks, or have a nice stroll around one of San Diegos glistening and serene
       <a href="http://www.sandiego.gov/water/recreation/ " target="_blank">
        <b>
         lakes
        </b>
       </a>
       , such as Miramar Lake, Otay Lake, and El Capitan Lake!
      </p>
      <p>
       28. Want to have a nice scare? Get chased by zombies at the
       <a href="http://www.thescreamzone.com/ " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Screamzone
        </b>
       </a>
       held at Delmar Fairgrounds. There is Zombie Paintball, The Chamber, the House of Horror, and a Haunted Hayride.
      </p>
      <p>
       29. Get out of the house and go on a camping trip at
       <a href="http://www.mccscp.com/san-onofre-beach/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Camp Pendletons Camping Grounds
        </b>
       </a>
       next to San Onofre beach!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/49-Seaport-Village.jpg">
        <img alt="Seaport Village" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-22205" height="134" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/49-Seaport-Village-300x134.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
       30. Do some fine dining and shopping at
       <a href="http://www.seaportvillage.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Seaport Village
        </b>
       </a>
       , famous among celebrities, the waterfront cobblestoned center is a great place to get souvenirs!
      </p>
      <p>
       31.
       <a href="http://batesnutfarm.biz/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Bates Nut Farm
        </b>
       </a>
       is a unique rural festival that allows kids to go through hay mazes, pick through a pumpkin patch, and pet some animals every Fall!
      </p>
      <p>
       32. The
       <a href="http://www.sdnightmarket.com/ " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Night Market
        </b>
       </a>
       on Convoy is a great asian cultural experience full of street food, live entertainment, and traditional festive games.
      </p>
      <p>
       33. Eat some peanuts and hot dogs while watching a ball game at
       <a href="http://www.petcoparkevents.com/ " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Petco Park
        </b>
       </a>
       stadium.
      </p>
      <p>
       34. If baseball isnt your thing, then head over to
       <a href="http://www.sandiego.gov/qualcomm/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Qualcomm Stadium
        </b>
       </a>
       where they hold huge football events with firework shows and awesome half-time performances.
      </p>
      <p>
       35. Jump around in a gym where the floor is a huge trampoline!
       <a href="https://www.jumpskyhigh.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Skyhigh Sports
        </b>
       </a>
       has several foam pits and rounds of dodgeballs for our fellow athletes to do some flips over!
      </p>
      <p>
       36. Tour the beautiful campus of
       <a href="http://www.salk.edu/ " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Salk Institute
        </b>
       </a>
       where they hold events and presentations that share the wonders of the complex human body.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/52-Seaworld.jpg">
        <img alt="Seaworld" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-22206" height="179" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/52-Seaworld.jpg" width="281"/>
       </a>
       37. Plunge into the vibrant marine life with
       <a href="http://seaworldparks.com/en/seaworld-sandiego/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Seaworld
        </b>
       </a>
       s many interactive animal experiences, thrilling water rides, and inspirational shows/exhibits. Get splashed by Shamu!
      </p>
      <p>
       38. Watch the sunset on the west coasts longest concrete structure, the
       <a href="https://oceanbeachsandiego.com/attractions/ocean-beach-pier " target="_blank">
        <b>
         OceanBeach Pier
        </b>
       </a>
       . Grub at the OB Cafe or rent a pole and catch your own fish!
      </p>
      <p>
       39. The
       <a href="http://zoo.sandiegozoo.org/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         San Diego
        </b>
        <b>
         Zoo
        </b>
       </a>
       is one of the biggest and one of the best. Come see their #1 attraction, their lovable Chinese Giant Pandas!
      </p>
      <p>
       40. Have an up-close experience at the
       <a href="http://www.sdzsafaripark.org/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         San Diego Zoo Safari Park
        </b>
       </a>
       where you can get a guided tram tour through fields to see animals in their natural habitat!
      </p>
      <p>
       41. Meditate and find your core at the tranquil gardens of the
       <a href="http://www.yogananda-srf.org/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Self-Realization Fellowship Temple
        </b>
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       42. Eat some ice cream along the boardwalk and ride some of the attractions at
       <a href="http://www.belmontpark.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Belmont Park
        </b>
       </a>
       , like the huge wooden rollercoaster, the Big Dipper!
      </p>
      <p>
       43.Tour the beautiful
       <a href="http://san-diego-attractions.10-best.info/harbor.html " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Downtown Harbor
        </b>
       </a>
       and catch its coastal breezes on a pedicab! There are ship museums, short cruises, and the BEST seafood restaurants.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Legoland" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-22207" height="168" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/42-Legoland-300x168.jpg" width="300"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       44. Get hands-on with seastars and stingrays at
       <a href="http://aquarium.ucsd.edu/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Birch Aquarium
        </b>
       </a>
       ! Let your kids goggle at the huge tanks full of deep sea monsters.
      </p>
      <p>
       45. Channel your inner child by visiting the huge lego-themed amusement park and resort,
       <a href="http://california.legoland.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         LEGOLAND
        </b>
       </a>
       !
      </p>
      <p>
       46. Jump on a
       <b>
        jet ski
       </b>
       or play some volleyball at
       <a href="http://www.sandiego.gov/park-and-recreation/parks/regional/missionbay/ " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Mission Bay
        </b>
       </a>
       . Then, if you want to escape the crowds, cross the bay to
       <a href="http://www.sandiego.gov/park-and-recreation/parks/regional/missionbay/fiestaisland.shtml" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Fiesta Island
        </b>
       </a>
       and visit the Youth Camp and Aquatic Center!
      </p>
      <p>
       47.Catch a Broadway show or ballet play at one of San Diegos regal theaters such as the
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.sleeptrain.com/about-sleep-train-amphitheatre-chula-vista.html " target="_blank">
         Chula Vista Amphitheatre
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.lajollaplayhouse.org/" target="_blank">
         La Jolla Playhouse
        </a>
        ,
       </b>
       and the
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.theoldglobe.org/" target="_blank">
         Old Globe Theater
        </a>
        .
       </b>
      </p>
      <p>
       48. Awe at
       <a href="http://www.balboapark.org/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Balboa Park
        </b>
       </a>
       s beautiful architecture and immerse yourself in their culturally rich museums!
      </p>
      <p>
       49.Feast at
       <a href="http://www.balihairestaurant.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Bali Hai
        </b>
       </a>
       restaurant on
       <a href="http://www.thebigbay.com/seven-waterfront-areas-on-the-big-bay-san-diego/shelter-island.html " target="_blank">
        <b>
         Shelter Island
        </b>
       </a>
       for one of the best views of the citys skyline!
      </p>
      <p>
       50. With its exquisite architectural character,
       <a href="http://theheadquarters.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         The
        </b>
        <b>
         Headquarters
        </b>
       </a>
       is a restored historic treasure that is full of outdoor entertainment such as fashion shows, concerts, and a weekly farmers market.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Dog beach" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-22208" height="199" src="http://www.52perfectdays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/47-Dog-beach-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       51. Are you a nifty thrifter?
       <a href="http://kobeyswap.com/" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Kobeys Swap Meet
        </b>
       </a>
       is a great place for you to trade, sell, and scrummage for hidden treasures
      </p>
      <p>
       52. Help your dog beat the dog days of summer and let him crash through waves and run through the sand leash-free at the designated
       <b>
        <a href="http://www.sandiego.org/articles/pets/california-dog-beaches-parks-recreation-areas-in-san-diego.aspx " target="_blank">
         Dog Beaches
        </a>
       </b>
       in Ocean beach, Del Mar, Coronado, and Mission Bay
       <br/>
       .
      </p>
