       <p>
        Dont call it a comeback. German musician and YouTube personality
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/djflula" target="_blank">
         Flula Borg
        </a>
        busts out his best autotuned version of LL Cool Js classic rap song Mama Said Knock You Out.
       </p>
       <p>
        To add to the weirdness of it all, he ropes in his mom and dad too. Mom takes up the driving while delivering the odd deadpan line, while dad sits in the back playing along on his accordion and dropping the odd huh when needed.
       </p>

        Its probably the oddest thing youre going to see all day. Or maybe not, depending on how long you spend on the internet.
