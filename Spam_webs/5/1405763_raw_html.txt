Since most girls love men with talent, playing a guitar is one such talent that can make them fall for you. Playing the guitar is a passion for many of us, especially teenagers who enjoy sitting around and learning new songs all time. In a situation where you want to impress your lady love, playing some love songs on your guitar for her can make things work. But where most of the guys go wrong is in selecting songs.
