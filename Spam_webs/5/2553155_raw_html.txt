             <p>
              Android smartwaches lets you get notifications and perform quick actions on your smartphone. But what if you are in bathroom and your phone and smartwatch both are not with you. You can still see notifications and access internet by using this smart mirror.Nick Nothom has made a smart mirror which runs on Android wear and works exactly similar to Android wear powered smart watches.
             </p>
             <p>
              He also uploaded a video demonstrating how this smart mirror works. And it works great. It listens to your voice commands and perform actions accordingly. Watch the demo video below.
             </p>
             <p>
              <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="361" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9aoHJAbqkYs" width="640">
              </iframe>
             </p>
             <p>
              No only in bathroom, you can use this mirror in your bedroom and use it without touching your smartphone. It will be like reading messages on big screen. You can also watch YouTube videos, perform Google search and more.
             </p>
             <p>
              He also estimated the budget to be around $500.
             </p>
