         <div id="description-text">
          Spiraling energy of fours rowan wood rune set. This rune set is made with the intent of having the energy of the rune row spiral &amp; flow down the branch in consecutive order. The branch was cut into 6 2 1/4" pieces/sections. Beginning with the first section (tip of branch) it is split into quarters which becomes the first 4 runes of FEHU thru ANSUZ. The next quarter split section is RAIDHO thru WUNJO and so on down the rowan branch . The fifth photo illustrate the cut &amp; split pattern.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          This Rowan wood rune set was made during the waxing moon and in sacred space. Each stave is sanded to a fine finish. I galdr (chant/sing) the rune row when I burn the rune symbols into each rowan stave.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          I soak the Rowan staves in a linseed oil mixture 5 separate times at 12-24 hours. The oil mixture is linseed, almond, basil, sage, vetiver, mugwort, &amp; hyssop oils blended with ground copal, frankincense, pipe stone, dragons blood, red ochre, and cimmaron hematite. While I work the oil into each rune stave with a cloth, I galdr (chant/sing) the name of the rune being oiled and I do this for the entire rune row. The rune staves are finished with a beeswax orange oil conditioner/polish.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          During the waning moon the runes are oiled 2 more times and I hand stitch the gusseted leather pouch. The pouch is held closed with a cut &amp; polished button of rowan and a hand braided artificial sinew cord to wrap around the rowan button. The cord is adorned with amber, pipestone &amp; yak bone beads.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          According to ancient tree wisdom~ Rowan~ ruling planet is the sun. Some of the magical properties of rowan are; protection against enchantment, protection of stone circles &amp; places of powerful energy/magic, magic of the highest &amp; purist power, control of all senses, healing. Connected to Dragon energy. To do with the element of fire.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The rune staves measure approx. 2" long X 5/8" wide { stave sizes vary }. The pouch dimensions; 6" wide X 5 1/2"tall X 7/8" thick.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          **As you work with the Rowan rune staves some of the bark may fall off with use &amp; they may need to be oiled once a month or so.**
