               <h2 class="pane-title">
                How To Know If You Are On The Right Path
               </h2>
               <div class="pane-content">
                <div class="node" id="node-2880">
                 <div class="picture">
                  <a href="/users/dr-danielle-dowling" title="View user profile.">
                   <img alt="Dr. Danielle Dowling's picture" src="http://www.owningpink.com/sites/default/files/images/user-pictures/picture-80.jpg" title="Dr. Danielle Dowling's picture"/>
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <span class="submitted">
                  <a href="/users/dr-danielle-dowling">
                   Dr. Danielle Dowling
                  </a>
                  , May 17, 2014
                 </span>
                 <div class="social-buttons-blog-head">
                  <div class="tweet-button">
                   <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="horizontal" data-url="http://www.owningpink.com/blogs/owning-pink/how-to-know-if-you-are-the-right-path" href="http://twitter.com/share">
                    Tweet
                   </a>
                   <script src="http://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" type="text/javascript">
                   </script>
                  </div>
                  <div class="facebook-like-button">
                   <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="http://www.facebook.com/plugins/like.php?href=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.owningpink.com%2Fblogs%2Fowning-pink%2Fhow-to-know-if-you-are-the-right-path&amp;layout=button_count&amp;show_faces=false&amp;width=90&amp;action=like&amp;colorscheme=light&amp;height=21" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; width:90px; height:21px;">
                   </iframe>
                  </div>
                 </div>
                 <div class="content clear-block">
                  <p class="rtecenter">
                   <img alt="You're on the Right Path" class="imagecache-Max" src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/Max/uploads/user80/images/blossom.jpg" title="Blossom"/>
                  </p>
                  <h5>
                   <strong>
                    How do I know Im on the right path?
                   </strong>
                  </h5>
                  <p>
                   Asking this question can send us into a knee-bending spiral of:
                   <br/>
                   nail biting, insomnia-inducing, confusion stricken, bleary eyed
                   <strong>
                    unsure-ness.
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Been there? I know I have!
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Your head + heart asks: :
                   <br/>
                   How do I know for sure this is the right dream, career choice or partner for me?
                   <br/>
                   Yet, knowing we are on the right path is not nearly as hard as we think.
                  </p>
                  <h5>
                   <strong>
                    You know you are on the right path when you are not put in a position to
                   </strong>
                   <strong>
                    betray yourself.
                   </strong>
                   -
                   <a href="http://www.myss.com/">
                    Caroline Myss
                   </a>
                   <br/>
                  </h5>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    Youre on the right path when:
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   :: Youre not doing things that make you uncomfortable
                   <br/>
                   :: Youre not saying things you later apologize for
                   <br/>
                   :: You feel your sense of value or worth + see it mirrored back by your environment
                   <br/>
                   :: Youre confident in your choices and life (and arent afraid to say so)
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   These are all telltale signs that you are fulfilling your divine potential in life.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    You are on the wrong path when:
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   :: The situation at hand is costing you your power.
                   <br/>
                   :: You feel drained.
                   <em>
                    Tired
                   </em>
                   is ok. Drained means depleted + thats a red flag
                   <br/>
                   :: You feel wildly confused about where your life is going
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    You are especially on the wrong path when
                   </strong>
                   you feel like you are losing yourself. to a particular situation. If you find yourself saying I cant help feeling like I lost myself then youve probably betrayed yourself in some way.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    Compromise
                   </strong>
                   , incidentally,
                   <strong>
                    is not betrayal
                   </strong>
                   . Compromise is when you willingly say Alright, I can give here.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   But if youve compromised to the point where you feel drained or depleted?
                   <br/>
                   Then you have betrayed yourself.
                  </p>
                  <h5>
                   <strong>
                    The choices you make in your life will either enhance your spirit
                   </strong>
                   <strong>
                    or drain your spirit. What are you choosing?
                   </strong>
                  </h5>
                  <p>
                   How often have you said:
                   <br/>
                   I want to get out of this circumstance but I am too afraid.
                   <br/>
                   Im just going to slap on a happy face instead?
                  </p>
                  <h5>
                   <strong>
                    Yup, you know it:
                   </strong>
                   <strong>
                    betrayal.
                   </strong>
                  </h5>
                  <p>
                   People who are in touch with their inner guides can better navigate
                   <br/>
                   around life's stumbling blocks.
                   <br/>
                   They quit jobs they know arent right for them.
                   <br/>
                   They end relationships that arent fulfilling.
                   <br/>
                   They make tough choices - because they know theyre the right choices.
                  </p>
                  <h5>
                   <strong>
                    Think of every small right action as a massive billboard created
                   </strong>
                   <strong>
                    for the Universe which says: I am ready now. I believe.
                   </strong>
                  </h5>
                  <p>
                   The powerful act of staying tuned in + aligned with what is true for you
                   <br/>
                   will create quantum shifts in your reality; your relationships, your confidence + energy field.
                  </p>
                  <h5>
                   <strong>
                    Some guidance to get you on the right track
                   </strong>
                  </h5>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    :: Don't be so hard on yourself.
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   Stop with the self-attack! Its not productive.
                   <br/>
                   Dont get down on yourself because you ended up on the wrong track - it happens to everyone.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    :: Determine + then take the first step
                   </strong>
                   .
                   <br/>
                   Change starts with one step. For example: If you were trying to lose weight,
                   <br/>
                   a small first step would be to select whole grain bread
                   <br/>
                   instead of white bread. Cutting down on fats or sweets is also a good first step.
                   <br/>
                   If youre looking for a special someone, sign up for an online dating site.
                   <br/>
                   Dont worry about writing your profile or going on dates - your first step is just joining.
                   <br/>
                   Career-wise, spend 20 minutes refreshing your resume.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    :: Set a series of small goals
                   </strong>
                   .
                   <br/>
                   Dont bite off more than you can chew. Tiny, doable goals will get you there!
                   <br/>
                   Break down your BIG goals into a series of little, totally doable steps.
                   <br/>
                   Step one: go for a 20 minute walk today. Step two: go for a 20 minute walk and switch
                   <br/>
                   to skim milk in your coffee.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    :: Relax more.
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   It may seem counterproductive to relax, but if this is what you need,
                   <br/>
                   then go ahead and relax for a day or so.
                   <br/>
                   Contemplate how youve been living your life, your delayed goals + what
                   <br/>
                   things you want to change about yourself and your life.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Find a serene spot + write down your thoughts and desires in notebook.
                   <br/>
                   Begin by writing down what you need to get, want to give, and what your
                   <br/>
                   deal breakers are in regards to that area of your life. Make this day a mini retreat!
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    :: Have a mantra or meditation that helps to keep you on focus.
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   If you tend to be discouraged easily by some things, create a simple mantra
                   <br/>
                   that will help to keep you on target, one that will help you shift
                   <br/>
                   into a more loving and empowered perspective.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    :: Accomplish a goal every week, every month and every year.
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   Enjoy the snowball effect of gaining confidence as you accomplish more and more goals!
                   <br/>
                   When you set small, doable goals for yourself, youll be that much more likely to take on big ones.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   You wont have to hunt, seek or find the right path.
                   <br/>
                   Rather, it will rise up below your feet + meet you.
                   <br/>
                   Right where you are.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    Now.
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   <strong>
                    Thats.
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                   <strong>
                    Magic.
                   </strong>
                  </p>
