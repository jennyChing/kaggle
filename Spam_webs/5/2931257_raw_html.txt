     <h1 class="articleTitle">
      Adnan Oktar offers 8 trillion dollars for a fossil
     </h1>
     <div class="cmsContent">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_1947" style="width: 210px">
       <a href="http://www.harunyahya.com/theauthor.php?sayfa_no=3">
        <img alt="Adnan Oktar: multi-trillionaire, fantasist, narcissist, creationist clown (Click the pic for more - &lt;em&gt;lots&lt;/em&gt; more" class="size-full wp-image-1947" height="198" src="http://www.freethinker.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/adoktar.jpg" title="adnan oktar" width="200"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Adnan Oktar: multi-trillionaire, fantasist, narcissist, creationist clown (Click the pic for more -
        <em>
         lots
        </em>
        more)
       </p>
      </div>
      <br/>
      The Freethinkers favourite creationist clown, Adnan Oktar (aka Harun Yahya, see
      <a href="http://www.freethinker.co.uk/2007/07/17/more-nonsense-from-planet-islam/">
       here
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="http://www.freethinker.co.uk/2008/06/21/stand-by-for-jesus's-return---as-a-muslim/">
       here
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="http://www.freethinker.co.uk/2008/07/17/crafty-muslim-creationists-are-given-the-brush-off-in-israel/">
       here
      </a>
      ) has really surpassed himself this time. According to
      <a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/news/science/creationist-offers-prize-for-fossil-proof-of-evolution-945289.html">
       The Independent
      </a>
      , the man who describes himself as a prominent Turkish intellectual has offered 10 trillion Turkish lira (thats about 4.4 trillion or $8 trillion) to
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        anyone who produces a single intermediate-form fossil demonstrating evolution.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       As creationists of his ilk simply do not accept evidence which contradicts their world-view, it seems unlikely that he would pay out the reward  even if he had that kind of money. Which he doesnt.
      </p>
      <p>
       The hilarious Mr Aktar
       <a href="http://www.harunyahya.com/theauthor.php?sayfa_no=1">
