          <p>
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="A list of variety of online dating tips" class="size-medium wp-image-69 alignright" height="181" src="http://idatingassistant.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Online-Dating-Tips-300x181.jpg" title="Online Dating Tips" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Online dating tips are invaluable for people new to the online dating scene. Dating sites are one of the best ways to meet new people with similar interests and relationship goals, especially for people who are either too shy or too busy, or just plain tired of the traditional dating routine. Once youve made the decision to give
           <a href="http://www.idatingassistant.com/blog/online-dating-courting-for-the-21st-century/" target="_blank">
            online dating
           </a>
           a try, learning to navigate this new virtual world can be a little confusing at first. But it doesnt need to be that complicated. Follow these simple online dating tips to get the most out of your experience.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <span id="more-68">
            </span>
            Make a great profile
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Your
           <a href="http://www.idatingassistant.com/blog/online-dating-profile-make-one-that-rocks/" target="_blank">
            dating profile is your first e-impression
           </a>
           . You want to make it catchy, creative, and fun, but it also has to be accurate and complete. Make sure you answer all the questions honestly, which also means being honest with yourself about what you are looking for in a partner. Be sure you proofread and use proper grammar and spelling. You dont want the date youd be interested in passing you by because of errors or an incomplete profile. Remember, you are marketing yourself; this means presenting yourself in the best possible light. If youre the modest type, recruit the help of a friend or family member who can help you assert your bragging points.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Pictures speak a thousand words
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Selecting
           <a href="http://blog.okcupid.com/index.php/dont-be-ugly-by-accident/" target="_blank">
            the right picture for your profile
           </a>
           is just as important as the carefully-crafted words you write. Make sure your photo is current and clear. Ideally, you should use both a full-body shot and a headshot that depicts you smiling and looking approachable. If possible, also include a photo with a background that says something about who you are. If you like hiking, theres nothing wrong with using a snap from the trail. Hiring a professional photographer is a wise investment for getting a great profile pic
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Getting started
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Now that
           <a href="http://www.idatingassistant.com/blog/online-dating-profile-make-one-that-rocks/" target="_blank">
            your dating profile
           </a>
           is active, its time to start making connections. Carefully read profiles that grab your attention. Look for signs of compatibility and shared interests that meet your expectations. Create a checklist of traits from most to least important. Its good to be flexible on some points, but dont overlook the deal-breakers.
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Making contact
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Ready to connect with someone? The
           <a href="http://www.idatingassistant.com/blog/online-dating-first-message-etiquette-dos-donts/" target="_blank">
            first message is important
           </a>
           , so make it a good one. The purpose is more than just introducing yourself; you also want to generate reciprocal interest. To do this, put the same attention to detail in your message that you put into your profile. Does it need to be a comprehensive? Of course not. However, you should tell your prospective match enough for them to get an idea of who you are.
           <a href="http://blog.okcupid.com/index.php/online-dating-advice-exactly-what-to-say-in-a-first-message/" target="_blank">
            Studies show
           </a>
           that using net speak and coming on too strong with physical compliments are turn offs in a first message. Be concise and friendly for best results.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Ready for the date?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Youve exchanged a couple emails, even chatted on the phone a couple times, so now its time to move the budding relationship forward. Setting up a
           <a href="http://www.idatingassistant.com/blog/first-date-tips-for-a-fun-stress-free-date/" target="_blank">
            good first date
           </a>
           can make or break your potential romance. Keep it simple. Pick something that will be enjoyable for both parties. Meeting in a public place is smart for
           <a href="http://www.idatingassistant.com/blog/dating-safety-tips-everyone-needs-to-know/" target="_blank">
            safety reasons
           </a>
           , plus it generally makes for a less stressful date. Stick to light topics for conversation and just allow yourself to relax and enjoy the date. Approach the date with an easy-going, nothing-to-lose attitude and youll have a better time. Practice good date etiquette and youre sure to get a second date.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Take your time
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Theres nothing wrong with taking things slow and developing a foundation of friendship first, so dont rush into a physical relationship. If its right, youll get there in good time. If its not, youve saved yourself from doing something you might regret in the morning. Dont attach too quickly to one person. Slow down and give yourself an opportunity to find the right person before taking a relationship to the next level.
          </p>
          <p>
           Have you just started dating online? If you found these tips helpful, please tell us now.
          </p>
