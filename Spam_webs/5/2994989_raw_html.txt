         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Onions to grow hair fast
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="sep">
           Posted on
          </span>
          <a href="http://womenbeautyconcepts.com/2014/11/onions-to-grow-hair-fast.html" rel="bookmark" title="7:02 am">
           <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-11-06T07:02:29+00:00" pubdate="">
            November 6, 2014
           </time>
          </a>
          <span class="by-author">
           <span class="sep">
            by
           </span>
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://womenbeautyconcepts.com/author/admin" rel="author" title="View all posts by admin">

           </span>
          </span>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <a href="http://womenbeautyconcepts.com/wp-content/uploads/Onions-to-grow-hair-fast.jpg">
           <img alt="Onions to grow hair fast" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-6530" height="161" src="http://womenbeautyconcepts.com/wp-content/uploads/Onions-to-grow-hair-fast.jpg" width="256"/>
          </a>
          Welcome to the Magnificent. Our superhero today is the onion. Anyway, I had so much
          <strong>
           onion
          </strong>
          recipes I decided to make them a sort of mini-series.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          One will be home remedies for
          <strong>
           hair
          </strong>
          with onion, another will be home remedies with onion skin. And the last will be several remedies that have to do with onions.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Okay, so todays chapter is home remedies for hair. Onions are very good for hair health because it has a high sulfur content as well as other vitamins and minerals.
          <span id="more-6529">
           <script type="text/javascript">

&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;But sulfur is very beneficial to health mineral &lt;strong&gt;hair&lt;/strong&gt;, skin and nails. Well, now let&#8217;s turn to make home remedies.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;First I want to show you how to take out the onion juice because it is an ingredient that we will be using in various recipes. We put a plate, cover it with a cloth, cheesecloth. Then we put up a grater with the finite part we will work. And then, one onion cut it in half and begins to grate.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;Well, when we have the well grated onion, we will collect the canvas. Let&#8217;s grab a cup of glass, let&#8217;s put a funnel so that we do not go to water the juice and we will squeeze above the funnel. Then we can also take the little juices left in the pan to avoid waste.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Remedy for having abundant hair&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;To have a hair full of shine and more abundant, a tablespoon of lemon juice mixed with a tablespoon of onion juice. Mix well and massage the scalp with the mixture. It is left for about two to three hours and then washed normalemnte. The juice will not only help shine but will also help reduce the smell of onion juice.&lt;/p&gt;
