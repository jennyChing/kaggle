           <img alt="Rockstar Games Confirms Double RP Weekend for Grand Theft Auto Online" height="369" src="//www.gamerheadlines.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/rsz_screen_shot_2015-01-02_at_144659-720x369.jpg" width="720"/>
          </div>
          <div class="entry_content_post">
           <p>
            Grand Theft Auto V users will receive double RP and double cash for completing jobs in Grand Theft Auto Online this weekend,
            <a href="http://www.rockstargames.com/newswire/article/52371/double-gta-double-rp-featured-playlists-in-gta-online?utm_source=twitter&amp;utm_medium=social&amp;utm_content=newswire&amp;utm_campaign=gtaonline2xcashrp01022015" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Double GTA$ &amp; Double RP Featured Playlists in GTA Online this Weekend">
             Rockstar Games has confirmed
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Running from January 2nd to January 5th, the double RP event will have Featured Playlist based on a range of modes accessible in Grand Theft Auto Online, including Deathmatch, Capture, Last Team Standing Job and Race.
           </p>
           <p>
            Rockstar has also revealed that theyve carefully curated the new playlists to include four vehicles  Slam Van, Hot Rod Christmas Ratloader, Massacro and Jester Race  from
            <a href="http://www.gamerheadlines.com/2014/12/grand-theft-auto-online-holiday-gift-revealed/" target="_blank" title="Grand Theft Auto Online Holiday Gift Revealed">
             GTA Online Festive Surprise
            </a>
            , a holiday-themed update added to the multiplayer last month.
           </p>
           <p>
            PlayStation 4 and Xbox Ones four playlists feature First Base, On the GOH, Fridgit Factory LTS and GTA: Grass is Greener, while Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3 users receive Vespucci Shoreline, Hit The Apex, Paleto Bay and Contend: Townhall Trinity.
           </p>
           <p>
            Grand Theft Auto Online is also expected to receive the eagerly awaited
            <a href="http://www.gamerheadlines.com/2014/12/grand-theft-auto-online-heists-confirmed-early-2015/" target="_blank" title="Grand Theft Auto Online Heists Confirmed for Early 2015">
             Heists feature
            </a>
            in early 2015, according to Imran Sarwar, GTA Online producer and lead mission designer.
           </p>
           <p>
            Executing a Heist will require four players who have reached rank 12 or above in Grand Theft Auto Online, and one member, specifically the Heist leader,
            <em>
             must
            </em>
            owna high-end apartment in order to plan out the mission beforehand.
           </p>
