         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Chako heroically protected his guardian from a domestic assault. He is now recovering." height="653" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com/heirnet/photos/170510/chako.jpg" width="490"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Chako the Pit Bull is being hailed as a hero after he came to his guardian's aid when she was attacked in a domestic assault. Chako reacted when he saw his mom being attacked and put himself between her and her attacker. But as a result, the brave Pit Bull was stabbed a dozen times around the neck and chest, according to Ring Dog Rescue and Gracie's Guardians, who stepped forward to help the injured dog.
         </p>
         <p>
          Ring Dog Rescue wrote about the incident on their
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Ring-Dog-Rescue/150605255003200">
           Facebook page
          </a>
          :
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          "On 10/31, in the Richmond [Virginia] area, Chako's owner was the victim of a domestic assault. As any good dog would do, when he saw her in trouble, Chako intervened and came to her aid, trying his best to keep the attacker from hurting his beloved owner. As a result of Chako putting himself in harm's way, the attacker turned on him and stabbed him no less than a dozen times around the neck and chest. Chako was rushed to a local veterinary emergency center but the owner, who survived the attack thanks in part to her beloved companion, did not have the money to pay for live saving treatment.
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          Ring Dog Rescue and Gracie's Guardians quickly found out about the situation and pooled resources to fund the initial treatment to save Chako's life."
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Chako heroically protected his guardian from a domestic assault. He is now recovering." height="475" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com/heirnet/photos/170505/chako-2.jpg" width="472"/>
          <br/>
          <em>
           Chako recovering. Photo:
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/graciesguardians1">
            Gracie's Guardians
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          After putting out their call for help, dog lovers generously stepped forward and fully covered Chako's medical expenses within a few days.
         </p>
         <p>
          Chako survived his emergency surgery and is now recuperating at home.
         </p>
         <p>
          Ring Dog Rescue said on their Facebook page, "We are proud to report he is resting comfortably at home with his Mom. He has a long road to recovery, but is doing well &amp; getting tons of love!"
         </p>
         <p>
          They added, "We thank each &amp; every one of you from the bottom of our bully loving hearts for pulling together &amp; helping these victims out of a really bad situation."
         </p>
