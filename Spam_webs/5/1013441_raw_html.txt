      <h1 class="title">
       10 Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids
      </h1>
     </div>
     <div class="blog">
      <div class="content_bar clearfix">
       <p class="meta">
        2014-03-18
        <a href="http://promocode4share.com/category/easter-day/">
         Easter Day

       <p class="author">

      <!-- END:Content Bar -->
      <div class="post_detail clearfix">
       <p>
        Looking for some fun and easy crafts for kids to do this Easter? Whether you just want to do a fun craft with your child or need to find an Easter craft that doubles as a decoration, you'll find it here on our list.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         1. Easter Finger Puppets
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Easter Finger Puppet" height="236" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_1.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Easter Finger Puppet" width="500"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        These Easter finger puppets are really quick and easy- plus they're suitable for young Picasso's too! All you need for this Easter craft is a cotton balls, googlie eyes, glue, and pipe cleaners (all very easy items to find at Target, and cheap with
        <a href="http://promocode4share.com/stores/target-promo-code/" target="_blank" title="Target Promo Code">
         <em>
          <strong>
           Target promo codes
          </strong>
         </em>
        </a>
        ). Simply wrap the pipe cleaner around your finger. Remove, then add googlie eyes (and whatever else you'd like) to the cotton ball. Glue to the top of the pipe cleaners and viola! Cute little Easter finger puppets perfect for storytelling.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: right;">
        Image credit:
        <em>
         http://www.flickr.com/photos/wendypiersall/
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         2. Bunny Hat
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Bunny Hat" height="333" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_2.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Bunny Hat" width="500"/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Who doesn't want to become a bunny? Can't think of anyone? Me neither! This fun and easy craft only requires a few supplies including a piece of white paper, crayons, and scissors. Feel free to dress up your bunny with other goodies too! Draw your bunny on the white paper and cut him out. Add ears to the top of the paper. Fit around your child's head. It's really that easy!
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: right;">
        Image credit:
        <em>
         http://www.flickr.com/photos/brendan-c/
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         3. Easter Sun Catcher
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Easter Sun Catcher" height="667" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_3.png" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Easter Sun Catcher" width="500"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Sun catchers are seriously so cool, right? So why not make an awesome Easter sun catcher that is not only enjoyable to make, but makes a very stunning decoration for Easter Sunday. All this fun craft requires is some sticky contact paper, some scrap pieces of paper, foil, sequins, and basically anything else you'd like to go on your masterpiece. Hang it with a pretty ribbon outside. Perfect outdoor decoration from
        <a href="http://www.heartfelt.co.nz/my_weblog/2013/03/an-easy-easter-craft.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Heartfelt
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         4. Ombre Easter Eggs
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Ombre Easter Eggs" height="375" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_4.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Ombre Easter Eggs" width="500"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        We love dying Easter eggs. We love ombre. Now we can combine the two to create a twist on the classic dying of Easter eggs. It's a match made in heaven! Simply set out 12 glasses, 8 ounces each. Add one cup of boiled water with two teaspoons of white vinegar to each glass. Stir. Add a different number of (any color) color drops to each glass to get the desired ombre effect. Place egg in the glass for 5 minutes then let dry in egg carton. Such a cool idea, and you can find all the products on Amazon. Don't forget to use
        <a href="http://promocode4share.com/stores/amazon-promotional-code/" target="_blank" title="Amazon Promotional Code">
         <strong>
          <em>
           Amazon discount codes
          </em>
         </strong>
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: right;">
        Image credit:
        <em>
         http://www.flickr.com/photos/mrsflinger/
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         5. Handprint Chick Card
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Handprint Chick Card" height="420" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_5.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Handprint Chick Card" width="420"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        This cute handprint chick card from
        <a href="http://spoonful.com/crafts/easter-chick-card" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Spoonful
        </a>
        is so adorable- plus it lets you document your child's hand size! This easy craft for kids only needs 2 sheets of yellow construction paper, 1 sheet of orange construction paper, 2 paper fasteners, googlie eyes, glue, scissors, pencil and a dark crayon. Use the body and wings (child's hands) using the yellow paper and make feet out of the orange. Glue the face and use paper fasteners to add the hands to the body.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         6. Peeps Gift
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Peeps Gift" height="500" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_6.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Peeps Gift" width="500"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Want to share the joy of Easter? Then check out this Peeps Gift craft, a really fun and easy craft for kids! All you need is a white ink pad to make chick thumb prints, paper, stapler, and some bags- oh, and peeps of course! (Which you can find a ton of at Oriental Trading. Use

       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         7. Egg Carton Easter Basket
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Egg Carton Easter Basket" height="752" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_7.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Egg Carton Easter Basket" width="500"/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you still don't have an Easter basket, no problem! Let your child get creative and make their own. This simple and easy craft for kids lets them explore their talents while making something they can utilize. You really just need an egg carton and decorations of your choice. Let their imaginations run wild! Find some really cool decorating stuff at Kohls and use
        <a href="http://promocode4share.com/stores/kohls-coupons/" target="_blank" title="Kohls Coupons">

       </p>
       <p style="text-align: right;">
        Image credit:
        <em>
         http://www.flickr.com/photos/linecon0/
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         8. Shaving Cream Egg Painting
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Shaving Cream Egg Painting" height="231" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_8.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Shaving Cream Egg Painting" width="320"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        If your child loves to get messy, he'll really sink his fingers into this cute Easter craft from
        <a href="http://www.wral.com/lifestyles/goaskmom/blogpost/10897326/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Wral!
        </a>
        It only takes some shaving cream and craft paper, plus paint or food coloring. Swish the shaving cream around and dip paper (shaped like eggs) into the mixture. Set out to dry and then decorate the house! Talk about an easy craft for kids of all ages!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         9. Hidden Easter Bunny Flower Pot
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Hidden Easter Bunny Flower Pot" height="583" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_9.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Hidden Easter Bunny Flower Pot" width="500"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Another great Easter craft that works as a decoration! This fun craft will need a flower pot, paint, any type of spring plant, and bunny ears. Decorate your flower pot however you wish with whatever color and decorations. Add the plant then hide the bunny ears on the inside. Seriously an adorable craft that can be placed indoors or outdoors!
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: right;">
        Image credit:
        <em>
         http://www.flickr.com/photos/1finecookie/
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         10. Egg Paitning
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         <img alt="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Egg Painting" height="333" src="http://promocode4share.com/data/upload/blog/funandeasyeastercraftforkids/Fun_and_Easy_Easter_Crafts_for_Your_Kids_-_10.jpg" title="Fun and Easy Easter Crafts for Your Kids - Egg Painting" width="500"/>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        This fun and easy craft only requires 2 supplies: paint and eggs! Ditch the typical dying of Easter eggs and let your child's creativity run wild with this Easter craft. Set them up with plenty of color paints to choose from, some brushes, and a set of eggs. They'll know what to do- and what child doesn't love to paint? It's a definite win in our book!
       </p>

        <em>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/fotologic/" rel="nofollow">
          http://www.flickr.com/photos/fotologic/
