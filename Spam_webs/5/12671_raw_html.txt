     <h9 style="padding: 30px 10px; font-size: 2em; width: 50%; color:#fff;">
      Questions about Earl
     </h9>
     <div class="faqsleft">
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        What type of GPS does Earl use?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl is GPS, GLONASS (Russian GPS) and WAAS (land-based, Wide Area Augmentation System) compatible.  Using up to 50 channels, Earl is able to track up to 20 satellites at once with accuracy of up to 3 meters with 30 second cold start fix at 5 Hz using the NMEA 0183 protocol.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        How does Earl know the weather?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl uses a thermometer for measuring temperature, hygrometer for measuring the water density of air (humidity) with dew point, and a barometer for measuring air pressure and altitude.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        What radio frequencies can I use Earl with?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        GMRS or General Mobile Radio Service: 15 channels, up to 20 miles with 2 watts of power.  PMR446  (Europe).  UHFCB (AU/NZ)
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Does Earl require specific gloves?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Using an capacitive touchscreen rated for use outdoors,  Earl is compatible with all styles of gloves.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        How long is the battery life under actual use?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl was designed with the weekend backpacker in mind. With a 3100mAh battery, Earl's battery lasts around 20 hours. Data logging intervals and sensors settings can be changed to increase battery life. Use the attached solar panel to keep Earl charged on the go.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Can I use Earl to connect to the internet?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Sure. Earl is a full- featured Android tablet with bluetooth and wifi included.  Choose from thousands of apps already available for Android and use Earl to share GPX and KML routes and waypoints with users around the world.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        What are the dimensions and weight?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl's rugged shell is 178mm x 125mm x 15mm and weighs about 400 grams.  Dimensions and weight of the final product may change but will be very close to these.
       </h22>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="faqsright">
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Does Earl's GPS really work anywhere?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl combines GPS with its internal magnetometer, accelerometer and gyroscope to accurately track your position in dense vegetation and dense urban areas when a direct line of site cannot be acquired.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        How can Earl help in an emergency?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Quickly access Earl's  two-way radio features to signal for rescue.  Combine a heart rate monitor and Earl can send out an alert when sensing a loss or extreme change in BPM.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        What radio bands can Earl listen to?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Worldwide FM band (64108 MHz).  Worldwide AM band (5201710 kHz).  Shortwave band (2.321.85 MHz).   Longwave band (153279 KHz).  NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) Weather Band using SAME technology (162 MHz).
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Does Earl work at night? Does it have a flashlight?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl's has a front-light which illuminates it's screen in dark conditions.  It's bright enough to use as a lantern if you need some extra light around camp.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Can I charge Earl without using the solar panel?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Every Earl comes with a durable USB lanyard that can be used to transfer data and charge Earl when the sun's out of sight.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Does Earl have a camera built in?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        We designed Earl to last for years to come. Current smart device cameras are expensive and constantly evolving.  So opted to exclude this from Earl's current design.
       </h22>
      </div>
      <div class="toggle">
       <h21>
        Does Earl only work in North America?
       </h21>
      </div>
      <div class="hidden">
       <h22>
        Earl works worldwide, and will be preloaded with a worldwide basemap.  We've designed the VHF/UHF transceiver to work worldwide so you'll be able to connect no matter if your in North America, Europe or Australia.
       </h22>
      </div>
