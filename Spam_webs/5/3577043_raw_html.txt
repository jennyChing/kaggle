      <h1 class="entry_title">
       Unpiloted X-37B returns to Earth after seven months in orbit
      </h1>
      <div class="singlepostmeta" style="font-size:14px">
       <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-68 photo" height="68" src="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/1fe4fac83d156a9401e0967e145c56d3?s=68&amp;d=monsterid&amp;r=g" srcset="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/1fe4fac83d156a9401e0967e145c56d3?s=136&amp;d=monsterid&amp;r=g 2x" width="68"/>
       <strong>
        About Holte Ender
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Holte Ender will always try to see your point of view, but sometimes it is hard to stick his head that far up his @$$.
       <br/>
       <a href="http://madmikesamerica.com/author/holte-ender/">
        View all posts by Holte Ender 
       </a>
       <div class="clear">
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
       A top secret prototype spaceplane built for the US military has returned to Earth after seven months in orbit. The unpiloted X-37B touched down at Vandenberg Air Force base in California at 0116 PST.
      </p>
      <p>
       The project has been shrouded in secrecy, prompting
       <a href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-11911335">
        widespread speculatio
       </a>
       n about the crafts purpose. The Air Force has not said whether it carried anything in its cargo bay, but insists the primary purpose of the mission was to test the craft itself.
      </p>
      <p>
       The robotic X-37B was launched atop an Atlas 5 rocket from Floridas Cape Canaveral Air Force Station on 22 April, with a maximum mission duration of 270 days.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://madmikesamerica.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/spaceplane.jpg">
        <img alt="spaceplane returns to earth" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-34324" height="350" src="http://madmikesamerica.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/spaceplane.jpg" title="spaceplane" width="540"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The vehicle returned to Earth on auto-pilot; the successful return marks the first autonomous re-entry and landing in the recorded history of the US space program.
      </p>
      <p>
       Because the X-37B started life as a Nasa program, the Air Force is in a position to talk openly about the crafts design, but its purpose remains classified.
      </p>
      <p>
       The secrecy surrounding the project has prompted much speculation about uses to which the craft might be put.
      </p>

We hitch rides on their technology to get to the space station! not the other way around folks! China concentrates on newer,better,safer Thorium fueled, nuclear reactors, Google Tsinghua University China, pebble bed, gas reactors  they have one up and running, without the magical help of the U.S.A.  they did it themselves! No reverse engineering, no spy network to look at how we did ours! They developed a reactor we never even dreamed of! now, they invest Billions of very valuable, very stable, unmanipulated, Yuan and fleets of intelligentsia, the top minds, of all the huge Asian gene pool, towards developing safe, plutonium-free, waste product benign, cheap to build, decommissionable, Thorium fueled nuclear reactors. Yes! They can so do it! Its just that America will not be first! Americas stuck on 1950s bull Shiite, corporately governed, GE Mk5 uranium fed, horse-Shiite reactors that  satisfy corporate interests before human interests!
             <br/>
             It is long past time for America to do something socially significant! Something good for mankind, not just commerce. Time for a new life-style system, a sustainable one, even for the very poorest among us. A breakthrough for all humanity, like the Thorium fueled nuclear reactors generating cleaner electricity for electric bullet trains and their electrically powered infrastructures, about to be daisy-chained across the whole Asian continent. Masses served, not just a few capitalist elite!
