      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="authmetasingle">
      Posted
      <abbr class="timeago" title="2011-08-19 18:30:55Z">
       Friday, August 19th, 2011 02:30 pm GMT -4
      </abbr>
      by

       Janice Kay
      </a>
      <span class="sharetwhm">
       <span class="st_twitter_hcount" st_title="Newly Released Trailer For Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance Burns Up The Web" st_url="http://sciencefiction.com/2011/08/19/newly-released-trailer-for-ghost-rider-spirit-of-vengeance-burns-up-the-web/" st_username="ScienceFiction" st_via="ScienceFiction">
       </span>
       <span class="st_facebook_hcount" st_title="Newly Released Trailer For Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance Burns Up The Web" st_url="http://sciencefiction.com/2011/08/19/newly-released-trailer-for-ghost-rider-spirit-of-vengeance-burns-up-the-web/">
       </span>
       <span class="st_sharethis_hcount" st_title="Newly Released Trailer For Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance Burns Up The Web" st_url="http://sciencefiction.com/2011/08/19/newly-released-trailer-for-ghost-rider-spirit-of-vengeance-burns-up-the-web/">
       </span>

     </div>
     <div style="clear:both;">
     </div>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18003" height="252" src="http://cdn1.sciencefiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/Ghost-Rider-2.png" title="Ghost Rider" width="593"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      At Comic Con last month, directors Mark Neveldine and Brian Taylor announced how they were eager to take Ghost Rider and move him fromthe superhero genre into the horror one. Taylor explains, Hes one of the most badass characters of all time. Hes not really a superhero. Hes more like a horror character. His superpower is that he sucks out your soul. Thats horrifying.
     </p>
     <p>
      Did they succeed in their goal? You can decide as the trailer for Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance just hit the internet today! The directors were on hand to introduce the trailer for the movie that is set to be released next year.
     </p>
     <p>
      Heres the official synopsis:
      <a href="http://cdn1.sciencefiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/ghost-rider-spirit-of-vengence-movie-poster.png" rel="lightbox[18001]">
       <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-18002 alignright" height="300" src="http://cdn1.sciencefiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/ghost-rider-spirit-of-vengence-movie-poster-201x300.png" title="Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance Movie Poster" width="201"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Nicolas Cage returns as Johnny Blaze in Columbia Pictures and Hyde Park Entertainments Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance. In the successor to the worldwide hit Ghost Rider, Johnny  still struggling with his curse as the devils bounty hunter  is hiding out in a remote part of Eastern Europe when he is recruited by a secret sect of the church to save a young boy (Fergus Riordan) from the devil (Ciaran Hinds). At first, Johnny is reluctant to embrace the power of the Ghost Rider, but it is the only way to protect the boy  and possibly rid himself of his curse forever.
     </p>
     <p>
      As an extra treat,we also havethe first movie poster for the film!It doesnt have Nicholas Cage on the front or his Ghost Rider counterpart, but it does have the fiery chains. You can click on the image for a larger view.
     </p>
     <p>
      Neveldine and Taylor have been heard boasting that any injuries sustained on set will make it to the final cut and that Any broken bones you see in our movie will be real. Looks like were in for a lot of action!
     </p>
     <p>
      Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengence stars Nicholas Cage, Idris Elba (Prometheus), Christopher Lambert (Highlander) and Ciaran Hinds as the Devil and is scheduled to open in theaters on February 17, 2012.
     </p>
