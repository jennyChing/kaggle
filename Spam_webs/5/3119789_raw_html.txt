        <header class="entry-header">
         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          DIY Wooden Monogram
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          By
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/author/ashleyphipps/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Ashley Phipps
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wooden-monogram/#comments">
            21 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="7382499" data-link="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wooden-monogram/" data-summary="" data-title="DIY Wooden Monogram">
         </div>
         <p>
          I love wooden monograms! They are trendy but even though they are popular, monograms are still completely unique! My mom had recently seen a wooden monogram and really liked it. So while they were in town, we decided to have a little fun with my new BladeRunner X2 and create a fun and unique DIY Wooden Monogram.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DIY-Wooden-Monogram.png">
           <img alt="DIY Wooden Monogram | #monogram #diy #diyblogger #homedecor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8393" height="850" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DIY-Wooden-Monogram.png" width="613"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I was so excited whenRockwell sent me a
          <a href="http://www.rockwelltools.com/en-us/BladeRunner_Wall_Mount.aspx" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           BladeRunner X2
          </a>
          to try out! (Thanks Rockwell!) The BladeRunner X2 uses jigsaw blades and can cut wood, metal, pvc and tile! And it is idealfor making quick rip-cuts, scroll cuts,miter cuts and crosscuts. You can use this to make many simple DIY projects but I, of course, decided to try a more complex project to really seewhat this could do!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/BladeRunner.png">
           <img alt="BladeRunner" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8389" height="466" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/BladeRunner.png" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Wooden Monogram
         </h2>
         <p>
          I enlisted the help of my sweet husband to sketch out the design for me. We made this for my mom and dad and had a basic design in mind. So thankfully he helped me put our vision onto the plywood. (We used 3/4 plywood for this project, but you can cut wood up to 1 1/2 thick.)
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sketch-Design.png">
           <img alt="Sketch Design" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8396" height="466" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sketch-Design.png" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Didnt he do a great job!? I love the style of the monogram and was excited to begin cutting it out!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Monogram-Design.png">
           <img alt="Monogram Design" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8395" height="466" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Monogram-Design.png" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          After the design was sketched on, I began by drilling a hole into each of the interior spaces to be cut out. We cut out each interior space first by placing the blade into the hole we drilled and then turned I turned the BladeRunner X2 on and started to cut!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Drill-Pilot-Holes.png">
           <img alt="Drill holes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8394" height="850" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Drill-Pilot-Holes.png" width="566"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Of course it is important to always wear safety protection over your eyes and ears and keep your hands away from the blade.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was really impressed with how nicely this cut. And I was really amazed that for my first time using this tool, I was able to come out with a really nice end result!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cut-out-Monogram.png">
           <img alt="Cut Out Monogram | #monogram #diy #diyblogger #homedecor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8391" height="466" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cut-out-Monogram.png" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          After the monogram was cut out, we sanded and painted it! I just used a spray paint primer and then a Rustoelum Metallic spray paint to give it a beautiful, copper metallic look!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cut-Monogram.png">
           <img alt="Cut Monogram  | #monogram #diy #diyblogger #homedecor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8390" height="466" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cut-Monogram.png" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I love the idea of hanging this wooden monogram as a wreath on your front door! But it would also be really pretty as wall art as well!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Wooden-Monogram.png">
           <img alt="Wooden Monogram | #monogram #diy #diyblogger #homedecor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8397" height="466" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Wooden-Monogram.png" width="700"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I am so happy that I finally have the right tool to cut out fun shapes and designs like this wooden monogram with! So so fun!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DIY-Monogram.png">
           <img alt="DIY Monogram  | #monogram #diy #diyblogger #homedecor" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8392" height="850" src="http://www.simplydesigning.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DIY-Monogram.png" width="566"/>
          </a>
         </p>
