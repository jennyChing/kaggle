       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Lounging Around: Our Outdoor Living Space
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-05-20T05:00:36+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 20, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/author/shaunaoberg/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Shauna Oberg
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2014/05/lounging-around-our-outdoor-living-space/#comments">
           40 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Last July, we got most of the foundational pieces in place for our
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2013/07/our-backyard-updates/" title="Our Backyard Updates">
          outdoor living space
         </a>
         . However, when it came time to finish the decorating, most of the outdoor decor was picked over and I had to put the process on hold. This year, I decided to be ahead of the game and started picking up pieces when they first started arriving in stores. With seasonal items, Ive learned its best to nab them before theyre gone.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_2.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Wicker Chaise Lounge and Red Garden Stool | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16688 size-full" height="875" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_2.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Perhaps it was the long drawn out winter that had me craving color, but I couldnt resist this red garden stool I spotted at
         <a href="http://www.homesense.ca" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.homesense.ca', 'HomeSense']);" target="_blank">
          HomeSense
         </a>
         to use as a side table between our loungers. I love that its no maintenance and so versatile.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_6.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Red Garden Stool | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16689 size-full" height="875" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_6.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_9.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Red Garden Stool | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16697 size-full" height="543" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_9.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         At first, I tried to talk myself out of the stool. Red wasnt even a consideration for our backyard, but then I came across these
         <a href="http://www.potterybarn.com/products/penelope-outdoor-pillows/?pkey=coutdoor-pillows&amp;cm_src=outdoor-pillows||NoFacet-_-NoFacet-_--_-" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.potterybarn.com/products/penelope-outdoor-pillows/?pkey=coutdoor-pillows&amp;cm_src=outdoor-pillows||NoFacet-_-NoFacet-_--_-', 'multi-colored paisley pillows']);" target="_blank">
          multi-colored paisley pillows
         </a>
         that tied everything together.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_3.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Wicker Chaise Lounge and Paisley Outdoor Pillow" class="aligncenter wp-image-16691 size-full" height="800" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_3.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Our aqua umbrella, blue-grey area rug, and yellow and turquoise pillows from last year were all linked together in one single pillow. And, how great is it that all of the colors are set on a fresh white background? Typically, I try to find my jumping off point first, but this time I worked backwards. Thankfully it all turned out!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_7.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Lounge Area | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16692 size-full" height="400" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_7.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I still have a few things on my outdoor living space to-do list. One is to purchase or make cushions for our loungers. Ive been shopping around, but its difficult to find pre-made ones that fit properly and look nice. Custom ones are just too expensive to justify for our short outdoor season. If you come across any basic neutral ones in your travels, please let me know!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_5.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Wicker Lounge Chairs, Red Garden Stool and Yellow Chevron Pillow | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16693 size-full" height="412" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_5.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For now, I have ornamental grass in the tall grey planters. Im thinking about adding some trailing white plants once the rain stops. Last year, I went with
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2013/05/green-and-white-plants/" title="Keeping it Simple: Green and White Plants">
          simple white and green plants
         </a>
         and I liked the way they looked all summer long.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_11.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Ornamental Grass and Red Garden Stool | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16695 size-full" height="900" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_11.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Chances are, if you stop by the blog often, youll recognize the blue and yellow pillows from our
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2014/05/spring-front-porch-adding-cottage-charm-giveaway/" title="Spring Front Porch- Adding Cottage Charm + Giveaway!">
          front porch
         </a>
         . Im always moving my decor around to create different looks. You?
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_1.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Wicker Chaise Lounge, Red Garden Stool and Blue Pillow | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16698 size-full" height="427" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_1.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im really looking forward to the sun coming out and temperatures rising so we can enjoy our lounging area. Reading a good book and sipping a
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2014/05/refreshing-basil-cucumber-mojito/" title="Refreshing Basil Cucumber Mojito + More Summertime Drinks">
          mojito
         </a>
         on a weekend afternoon sounds heavenly to me right now!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_12.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Outdoor Living Space with Oversized Aqua Offset Umbrella | Satori Design for Living" class="aligncenter wp-image-16694 size-full" height="477" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Outdoor-Living-Space_12.jpg?585d16" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next up, well be getting our outdoor dining area together on the upper deck. Perhaps the weather will turn around long enough this week to get it done! If you follow me on
         <a href="http://instagram.com/shaunaoberg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://instagram.com/shaunaoberg', 'instagram']);" target="_blank">
          instagram
         </a>
         , then you know we had a hailstorm come through moments after I started taking photos for this post. I wasnt able to show everything I wanted to (and some of the photos are a bit dark), so I plan on sharing update photos within the next couple of weeks.
        </p>
        <p>
         Perhaps organizing the Outdoor Extravaganza is a weather curse!
        </p>
        <p>
         Be sure to come back tomorrow for the
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2014/05/join-the-outdoor-extravaganza-decorating-link-party/" target="_blank" title="Join the Outdoor Extravaganza Decorating Link Party!">
          Outdoor Decorating link party
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Join-the-Outdoor-Extravaganza-2014-.jpg?585d16">
          <img alt="Join the Outdoor Extravaganza 2014" class="aligncenter wp-image-16338" height="475" src="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Join-the-Outdoor-Extravaganza-2014-.jpg?585d16" width="536"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For garden inspiration, I highly encourage you to check out last weeks
         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/2014/05/outdoor-extravaganza-plants-flowers-link-party-2/" title="Outdoor Extravaganza Plants &amp; Flowers Link Party">
          Plants &amp; Flowers link party
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         See you tomorrow!
        </p>

         <a href="http://satoridesignforliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Shauna-for-website5.jpg?585d16">
