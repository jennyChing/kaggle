          <h2>
           <span class="mw-headline" id="Warnings">
            Warnings
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="section_text" id="warnings">
           <ul>
            <li>
             Most essential oils (and artificial fragrances) are not recommended for some pets. Omit the essential oil or be careful using this around them.
            </li>
            <li>
             Use caution when using the mix in bottle method. Adding hot water to a container, sealing it and shaking will cause pressure to build up inside and possibly explode or shoot out of the top. Gentle, side-to-side shaking is all it takes.
            </li>
            <li>
             Know the properties of any essential oil used. Those safest for skin touch are
             <a href="/Make-Lavender-Oil" title="Make Lavender Oil">
              lavender
             </a>
             and
             <a class="extiw" href="http://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tea_tree_oil" title="wikipedia:Tea_tree_oil">
              tea tree oil
             </a>
             .
            </li>
            <li>
             To avoid leaving moisture droplets use a good quality spray bottle or better still a perfume atomizer.
            </li>
