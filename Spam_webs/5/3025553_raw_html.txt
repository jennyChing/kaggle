     <div class="col-md-6 col-lg-7 col-sm-9 col-xs-12 post_content">
      <p>
       The
       <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/tag/coachella/" target="_blank">
        Coachella
       </a>
       2015 lineup
       <a href="http://www.coachella.com/splash/" target="_blank">
        is out
       </a>
       . It features headliners
       <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/tag/acdc/" target="_blank">
        AC/DC
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/tag/jack_white/" target="_blank">
        Jack White
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/tag/drake/" target="_blank">
        Drake
       </a>
       . Do you have opinions on it? Of course you do, that's what the internet is for. Not us.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/news/tracks-news/2014/01/09/playlist-25-songs-to-coax-you-to-coachella-2014/" target="_blank">
        Like last year
       </a>
       , we're going to save our "worst. lineup. ever"s and our "OMG I'm LITERALLYdying at how good this is, I can't even"s because, well, what's the point? As much as the North American music festival market has expanded and multiplied, Coachella still has the upper hand. This is the one your Danny Devitos will be at, the one your Pitchfork friend will go to, the one for your friend to blufftheir way through sets by
       <a href="http://www.chartattack.com/tag/two_door_cinema_club/" target="_blank">
        Two Door Cinema Club
       </a>
       and
       <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W_IzYUJANfk" target="_blank">
        The Obesity Epidemic
       </a>
       . There's enough support to sell out two identical weekends. This thing is designed to appeal to everyone, from EDM heads to your dad (
       <em>
        especially
       </em>
       your dad). It doesn't need our support.
      </p>
      <p>
       So instead of a pass or fail, we're going to play deskchair fantasy booker and build a dream lineup out of the existing three day lineup (or 6, depending on how you're counting) and distill it down to one great day. Here it is:
      </p>
