      <p>
       The MLB regular season is a long grind, but through the first few weeks of action you would have to be a big fan of the Kansas City Royals in the American League and the New York Mets in the National League when it comes to betting on the games.
      </p>
      <p>

Through Tuesday nights action, Kansas City has won its last three games to remain tied with Detroit atop the AL Central. The Tigers were clear favorites to win this division heading into the new season and so far they have met all expectations with an 11-3 record and a +$648 return on the money line odds.
      </p>

       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Looking at the rest of the teams in the AL, Boston at 9-5 and surprising Houston at 8-6 are the only other teams that have provided a positive return on a money line bet. The Red Sox have won their last two games to raise their year-to-date return to +$275 and the Astros have built a four-game

New York is just 2-2 as an underdog this season, but it has paid off in nine of 10 games as a favorite.
      </p>
