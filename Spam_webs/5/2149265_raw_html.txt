        <h2>
         <a href="/story/2014/06/13/1306344/-Cartoon-Jihadi-Rifle-Association" id="titleHref">
          Cartoon: Jihadi Rifle Association
         </a>
         <span style="white-space: nowrap">
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p class="author">
         <span>
          by
         </span>
         <a href="/user/Mark Fiore">
          Mark Fiore
         </a>
         <a alt="Follow this author" class="ajax type-user do-hotlist do-updateContent size-tiny off altTooltip" href="#?friend_id=310710&amp;is_stream=1">
          Follow
         </a>
         <span class="for">

        </p>
        <ul class="front-page-share clearfix">

         <li>
          <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="horizontal" data-related="markos" data-text="Cartoon: Jihadi Rifle Association" data-url="http://www.dailykos.com/story/2014/06/13/1306344/-Cartoon-Jihadi-Rifle-Association" data-via="dailykos" href="https://twitter.com/share">
           Tweet
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="comments comment_count_with_new" data-comment_count="43" data-story_id="1306344" href="/story/2014/06/13/1306344/-Cartoon-Jihadi-Rifle-Association#comments">

       <div class="article-body">
        <div id="intro">
         <center>
          <iframe defang_allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/98004218?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=c96134" width="500">
          </iframe>
         </center>
         <p>
          (If you don't want to spoil the cartoon, read this after you watch the video.)With the
          <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2014/06/10/us/couple-in-las-vegas-killings-embraced-anti-government-ideology.html?hp&amp;_r=0" target="_blank">
           recent shooting
          </a>
          in Las Vegas, it's becoming more apparent that the crazed anti-government conspiracy nuts are a danger, to say the least.What is the largest, most well-funded group fanning these flames of insanity?You guessed it, the NRA!I've dealt more specifically with Wayne LaPierre's recent
          <a href="http://home.nra.org/home/video/2014-nra-members-meeting-wayne-lapierre/list/good-guys" target="_blank">
           apocalyptic diatribe
          </a>
          in another
          <a href="https://www.markfiore.com/mark-fiore-blog/cartoons/nra-hypnosis.html" target="_blank">
           cartoon
          </a>
          .Since that cartoon was published, there has been an attack on a courthouse in Georgia and a "
          <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/couple-accused-killing-las-vegas-cops-civilian-white-supremacist-meth-heads-authorites-article-1.1822387" target="_blank">
           revolution
          </a>
          " started by killing two police officers who were on their lunch break. After said revolutionaries made sure that their cats would be cared for.)
         </p>
         <p>
          I thought it might be a fun exercise to imagine all of those heavily armed
          <a href="carry-advocates.html" target="_blank">
           open-carry advocates
          </a>
          and Wayne LaPierre himself were Muslims.What do you think our politicians and NRA-supporters would be saying then?Can you imagine a dozen or so fully-armed Muslim men carrying AR-15s into a Chili's?Or what the news coverage would be if it was someone of Pakistani descent who attacked the
          <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2014/06/07/us/gunman-attacks-georgia-courthouse-and-dies-in-firefight-authorities-say.html" target="_blank">
           Georgia courthouse
          </a>
          or killed the cops in Vegas?
         </p>
         <p>
          I don't take comparing someone to a violent Islamic extremist lightly, but after the
          <a href="http://www.motherjones.com/politics/2012/07/mass-shootings-map" target="_blank">
           umpteenth
          </a>
          mass shooting and a couple of attacks on our justice system, I just had to do it.All you have to do is listen to some of LaPierre's speeches, then listen to the ravings of a few of these shooters and you'll see it's not a very far leap.And just like the cartoon says, the NRA isn't a jihadi organization, they just kinda act like one.You can find more links to articles behind this cartoon on
          <a href="http://www.markfiore.com" target="_blank">
           my website
          </a>
          , as usual.
          <br/>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="divider-doodle">
        </p>
        <div class="article-body" id="body">
         <p>
          [Ominous VO]
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens when a group of zealots preach extremism and the overthrow of our modern Western society . . .
         </p>
         <p>
          . . . espousing ancient beliefs of violence, retribution and might.
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens when this heavily-armed sect wants to destroy our values and blow up our laws with multiple attacks.
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens when their leaders spout anti-U.S. sermons and enable their adherents to arm themselves and carry out attacks on the Homeland?
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens when the leaders of the cell recruit a growing number of followers with minds warped by fear and a disturbingly paranoid doctrine . . .
         </p>
         <p>
          . . . a doctrine that twists the teaching and laws of revered prophets into an unrecognizable, violent new form.
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens when they begin forcing their beliefs into every part of our daily lives?
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens when followers, with minds poisoned by these teachings, attack American soft targets on a regular basis, then move to attack our very system of Justice?
         </p>
         <p>
          What happens?
         </p>
         <p>
          You send them your membership dollars, accept their campaign contributions and implement sharia-- er, gun rights legislation.
         </p>
         <p>
          (The National Rifle Association is not, repeat, not, a jihadi organization. Follow them with all your heart and do what they ask of you.)
          <br/>
         </p>
