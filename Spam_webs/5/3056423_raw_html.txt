       <h3>
        Brigittes Naturally Alive
       </h3>
       <p>
         101 S. Coombs St., Suite Y-4, Napa, CA 94559
       </p>
       <p>
         GetGreenSmoothies.com
       </p>
       <p>

     <div class="entry-content">
      <p>
       Brigitte Center, founder of Brigittes Naturally Alive, a Napa-based company that makes green power smoothies, is on a quest to help people feel better one smoothie at a time. She refers to her product, which she said delivers 100 percent of the daily recommended serving of fruits and vegetables in the 32 oz. bottle, as liquid sunshine.
      </p>
      <p>
       The drinks, which Center also calls full-vitality health drinks are packed with fresh kale, fruit, chia seeds and a proprietary blend of dehydrated greens to deliver antioxidants, living enzymes, plant-based protein and ample fiber.
      </p>
      <p>
       Using fresh, raw ingredients and not pasteurizing the juices is one of the key components to Brigittes Naturally Alive, Center explained. Another is not using any added sugars which is why she uses fruit, lemon juice and ground vanilla to sweeten the smoothies.
      </p>
      <p>
       Marketing materials for the drink tout benefits like long lasting energy, clearer skin, a sharper mind, help with weight management and reduced cravings. Center, who is certified in Plant-Based Nutrition by Cornell University and David Wolfes Raw Nutrition Program by BodyMind Institute, attributes the weight management and reduced cravings to the body receiving the nutrients it needs.
      </p>
      <p>
       She explained that when we are hungry, our bodies are actually craving nutrients, so when we get those nutrients, the hunger subsides. Plants, she noted, have phyto- and micro-nutrients that our bodies require and cannot get from processed foods, carbohydrates or animal proteins alone.
      </p>
      <p>
       Center said she started drinking green smoothies in 2009 after completing a three-day raw food and juice cleanse and instantly feeling better. She began to research everything she could on plant-based nutrition and then wondered, Why didnt I know all of this stuff? Raw food for real life is how she describes where she finally ended up.
      </p>
      <p>
       Although she believes we should be eating to live, not living to eat, she recognized the need to make healthy living convenient enough to be part of a daily routine.
      </p>
      <p>
       I want to have a life full of everything delicious and wonderful but stay as balanced and healthy as I can, she explained. This mindset was the impetus behind starting Brigittes Naturally Alive.
      </p>
      <p>
       Center said she wanted to create a healthy option that people could grab and go. With a suggested retail price of approximately $11.70 per 16 oz. bottle, she considers the drinks a meal replacement that is in line with the price of the sandwich one would usually buy for lunch. The smoothies can be purchased directly from the company in packs of four, five or eight 32 oz. jars for $92, $111, and $179, respectively.
      </p>
      <p>
       Locals, however, can use the coupon code Napapickup on the companys website for a discount on products that they pick up from the companys kitchen on Coombs St. The drinks may also be purchased at Browns Valley Market, Sunshine Foods and Vallergas, she said.
      </p>
      <p>
       Since they are raw and unpasteurized, they have a shelf life of six days. Center said some of her customers buy them in bulk and freeze them for later use.
      </p>
      <p>
       When asked who her target customer is she responded, Everybody who wants to feel great!
      </p>
      <p>
       When Center broke it down, she guessed about 60 percent are women and 40 percent are men. Some are busy moms, others are professionals, and thanks to one of her retail outlets, a private gym in San Francisco, quite a few are focused on working out. The Golden State Warriors basketball team, she noted, is also a customer, drinking the smoothies before workouts and games.
      </p>
      <p>
       Center, who formerly worked in advertising and media sales, originally started the company in San Francisco in 2010. In the summer of 2012 she moved to Napa and now focuses on the endeavor full-time.
      </p>
      <p>
       I truly feel like I can help people, she said. And that just makes my day.
      </p>
      <p>
       Along with the individuals and athletes who currently drink Brigittes Naturally Alive, Center is planning to begin targeting corporations, which she thinks should swap out the sodas and cookies they often serve in their meetings for her green power smoothies. She even makes them in 8 oz. servings with labels that say things like Drink this. Youll think better. and Brain Food.
      </p>
      <p>
       To help her meet her goals of expansion, she said she is considering raising capital and taking on a partner. However, Center is cautious about how she goes forward.
      </p>
      <p>
       The challenge, she explained, is how to scale this business and keep the product pure and unpasteurized.
      </p>
      <p>
       Of course, while she works on figuring that out, she has a bigger goal to tackle: finding a local kale farmer so she doesnt have to drive to Petaluma every week.
      </p>
