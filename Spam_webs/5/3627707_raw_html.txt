        <strong>
         The buffet awaits:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        o The difference between successful and very successful people is that very successful people say no to almost everything.  Warren Buffet
       </p>
       <p>
        o It has become almost a clich to remark that nobody boasts of ignorance of literature, but it is socially acceptable to boast of ignorance of science and proudly claim incompetence in mathematics.  Richard Dawkins
       </p>
       <p>
        o When asked about the practical applications of studying physics, Richard Feynman [FINE-mun] would sometimes say, Well, its kind of like sex. It has practical applications, but thats not usually why we do it.
       </p>
       <p>
        o Character is like a tree and reputation like a shadow. The shadow is what we think of it; the tree is the real thing.  Abraham Lincoln
       </p>
       <p>
        o It always seems impossible until its done Nelson Mandela
       </p>
       <p>
        o Put simply, the greatest testimony to the power of real leadership is what happens in its absence  very little.  Mike Myatt
       </p>
       <p>
        o The illiterate of the 21st century will not be those who cannot read and write, but those who cannot LEARN, UNLEARN, and RELEARN.  Alvin Toffler
       </p>
       <p>
        o The problem with being in the rat race is that even if you win, youre still a rat.  Lily Tomlin
       </p>
       <p>
        o Talk for others to know youask questions for them to know you better.  Voltaire
