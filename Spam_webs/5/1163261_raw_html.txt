           <article class="marTop25 clearfix">
            <span class="articleIntro">
             Both drivers fined, placed on four-race probation
            </span>
            <link href="/nscr_design_v7.3/clientlibs/components/richText.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css"/>
            <div class="flelemtwidth">
             <p>
              <b>
               RELATED:
               <a href="http://www.nascar.com/en_us/news-media/articles/2014/10/13/matt-kenseth-brad-keselowski-fight-chase-for-the-nascar-sprint-cup-timeline-charlotte-motor-speedway-bank-of-america-500.html">
                Full coverage/timeline of Charlotte incident
               </a>
              </b>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              DAYTONA BEACH, Fla. (Oct. 14, 2014) -- NASCAR has assessed behavioral penalties to drivers
              <a href="/en_us/sprint-cup-series/drivers/brad-keselowski.html">
               Brad Keselowski
              </a>
              and
              <a href="/en_us/sprint-cup-series/drivers/tony-stewart.html">
               Tony Stewart
              </a>
              for their involvement in post-race incidents on Oct. 11 at
              <a href="/en_us/sprint-cup-series/schedule/tracks/charlotte-motor-speedway.html">
               Charlotte Motor Speedway
              </a>
              .
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="floatElement rtMargin" id="floatArticleQuotes">
             <div class="flelemtwidth">
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.nascar.com/en_us/sprint-cup-series.html">
                <img src="http://static.nascar.com/content/dam/nascar/promospots/Series logos/nscs_chase_logo_article_template_235x120.png" style="max-width: 100%;"/>
                <b>
                 <i>
                 </i>
                </b>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                <i>
                 FULL CHASE COVERAGE
                </i>
               </b>
               <i>
                <br/>
               </i>
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                
                <a href="http://www.nascar.com/chase.html">
                 Chase hub page
                </a>
                <br/>
                
                <a href="http://www.nascar.com/grid">
                 Chase Grid games
                </a>
                <br/>
                
                <a href="http://www.nascar.com/en_us/chase-nation-social-landing.html">
                 #MyChaseNation
                </a>
               </b>
               <br/>
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="flelemtwidth">
             <p>
              Keselowski has been fined $50,000 and placed on NASCAR probation for the next four NASCAR Sprint Cup Series championship events through Nov. 12 for violating:
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
                Section 12-1
              </b>
              : Actions detrimental to stock car racing
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
                Section 12-4.9
              </b>
              : Behavioral penalty -- involved in post-race incidents
             </p>
             <p>
              Stewart has been fined $25,000 and placed on NASCAR probation for the next four NASCAR Sprint Cup Series championship events through Nov. 12 for violating:
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
                Section 12-1
              </b>
              : Actions detrimental to stock car racing
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
                Section 12-4.9
              </b>
              : Behavioral penalty -- involved in a post-race incident
             </p>
             <p>
              "These penalties are about maintaining a safe environment following the race," said Robin Pemberton, NASCAR senior vice president, competition and racing development. "We knew that the new Chase format was likely going to raise the intensity level and we want our drivers to continue to be themselves. However, the safety of our drivers, crew members, officials, and workers is paramount and we will react when that safety could be compromised."
