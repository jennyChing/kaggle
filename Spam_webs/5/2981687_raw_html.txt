        <p>
         The ACLU has called attention to a recent video showing a Barstow, California police officer throwing an eight month pregnant woman to the ground. They are calling the assault horrifying and demanding that action be taken against the officer.
        </p>
        <p>
         Police were called to the scene after two women got into an argument over a parking spot at an elementary school. Charlena Cooks, a black woman, got into an argument with a white female employee of the school. The responding officer immediately singles Charlena out and begins to escalate the situation, leading some to question the racial motives of the officer. His escalation eventually leads to the officer roughly handling the woman, cuffing her and slamming her to the ground.
        </p>
        <p>
         A summary of the incident
         <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2015/05/28/us/barstow-california-police-video-pregnant-woman-arrest/index.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cnn.com/2015/05/28/us/barstow-california-police-video-pregnant-woman-arrest/index.html', 'as reported by CNN');" target="_blank">
          as reported by CNN
         </a>
         :
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Cooks makes a phone call, and while she does, she tells the officer her name is Michelle. Cooks often goes by her middle name, she said.
         </p>
         <div class="code-block code-block-4" style="margin:8px 0px;">
          <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
          </script>
          <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-4667713798232162" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="8660465530" style="display:block">
          </ins>
          <script>
           (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
          </script>
         </div>
         <p>
          Officer: Maam, Im going to give you two minutes.
         </p>
         <p>
          But the argument between the officer and Cooks escalates. The officer grabs Cooks, and a second officer joins him.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cooks begins to shout and scream.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cooks: Dont touch me, dont touch me!  Im pregnant, do not touch me!
         </p>
         <p>
          The two officers pin her against a chain-link fence, and the pregnant Cooks lands on the ground, on her stomach.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.copblock.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/292AA94200000578-3101879-image-a-22_1432872973252.jpg">
           <img alt="cop assaults pregnant woman" class="alignright size-full wp-image-127993" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.copblock.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/292AA94200000578-3101879-image-a-22_1432872973252.jpg?resize=322%2C235"/>
          </a>
          Officer: Why are you resisting?
         </p>
         <p>
          Cooks: Please stop this!
         </p>
         <p>
          The officers handcuff her as she faces the ground.
         </p>
         <p>
          Officer: OK, get up, maam.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cooks: I cant because Im pregnant!  Please dont touch me!
         </p>
         <p>
          The officers struggle to lift Cooks from the ground.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cooks: Stop it! Let my arm go! This is ridiculous. What are you doing?
         </p>
         <p>
          Officer: OK, youre under arrest, and the officer walks her while she is handcuffed to the squad car.
         </p>
         <p>
          Cooks weeps, telling police she doesnt want to get into the squad car.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         Cooks was then arrested for resisting arrest, that most recursive script of legal malarkey. The charge was later dismissed by the judge, not just because of the abuse she received, but because it was an unlawful arrest to begin with.
        </p>
