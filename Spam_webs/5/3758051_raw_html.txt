    <h1 class="post-title">
     Gunners hosts laggard QPR
    </h1>
    <section class="post-content">
     <p>
      ARS at home have only lost once in their last 5 matches while QPR havent managed to win any of their last 5 away matches. ARS should take advantage of this situation and grab three crucial points irrespective of the opposition.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="https://fantasyfootball.dream11.com/IN/my-team/English-Fantasy-League/248/2314">
       <strong>
        ARS
       </strong>
      </a>
      - Szczesny, Debuchy, Monreal, Mertesacker, Gibbs, Flamini, Ox- Chamberlain, Sanchez, Cazorla, Welbeck, Giroud
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="https://fantasyfootball.dream11.com/IN/my-team/English-Fantasy-League/248/2314">
       <strong>
        QPR
       </strong>
      </a>
      - Green, Isla, Dunne, Caulker, Onuaha, Vargas, Henry, Barton, Fer, Mutch, Austin
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Watch out for
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Cazorla
      </strong>
      - He is an attacking mid-fielder who also plays well in the flanks. His ability to adjust with his team-mates has proved to be an advantage for his side. He should be a sure pick in all fantasy teams.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Sanchez
      </strong>
      - This Chilean forward needs no introduction of how lethal he has been in this league. ARS rides on his shoulders for regaining their spot at the top.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Value for Money
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Giroud
      </strong>
      - He is in a fine form as his side prepares to host QPR and has grabbed four goals in his last six appearances. He also scored in the last game against LIV and he is a player not to missout with in fantasy football.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Austin
      </strong>
      - A hat-trick after coming back for suspension is a dream for any striker. Austin just did that last week and there was no stopping him. His goal scoring capability makes him a perfect pick for your fantasy football team.
     </p>
    </section>
