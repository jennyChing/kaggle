         <h2>
          Paintings by American artist Johnie Thornton.
         </h2>
         <p>
          Born and raised in Southern California, Johnie Thornton is a Los Angeles based artist who is mainly inspired by sociology, geometry, architecture, and their impact on our environment. While experimenting with many different mediums, the self-taught artist developed a unique style.
         </p>
         <p>
          Johnie Thornton loves to play with diversecolors and geometric forms. He says:
          <em>
           its an expression of movement, communication and growth to explore the uneasy relationship between humans and nature.
          </em>
          For further information, please visit:
          <a href="http://www.f16life.com/" target="_blank">
           www.f16life.com
          </a>
         </p>
