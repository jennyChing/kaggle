        <p>
         Were getting close to the holidays when things move at the speed of lightning. And also the time that treats are made and delivered. Or just made and eaten. Ahem.  Im always looking for simple things to make for family and friends. These Turtle Pretzels only have 3 ingredients and are the perfect salty/sweet/crunchy combo.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Super simple dessert - 3 Ingredient Turtle Pretzels www.thirtyhandmadedays.com" class="size-full wp-image-25318 aligncenter" height="948" src="//www.thirtyhandmadedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/3ingredientturtlepretzels.png" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Yes they have been around for a million years and you might have already tried them. I found the recipe in an old cookbook and thought theyd be amazing. I dont love chocolate (vanilla and caramel all the way) but I do love Take 5 bars and thats what this recipe reminds me of. Its got chocolate, crunch and nuts.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Turtle pretzel befores " class="size-full wp-image-25320 aligncenter" height="828" src="//www.thirtyhandmadedays.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/turtlepretzels1.png" width="630"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Just like this they would taste good. But add one more ingredient and they that much better. Yum!
        </p>

             <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="//stats.ziprecipes.net/piwik.php?idsite=1&amp;rec=1" style="width: 1px; height: 1px; border:0" width="1"/>

            </li>

             1 small package pecan halves
            </li>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ul class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Preheat the oven to 350.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Put parchment paper on a baking sheet.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Lay pretzels down on the parchment paper.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Place a Rolo on each pretzel.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake for 3-5 minutes watching for them to soften but not melt.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Take out of the oven and add 1 pecan half per pretzel.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Let cool completely.
            </li>
