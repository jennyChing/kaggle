           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Whether you are an experienced salesman looking for leads or a young entrepreneurlooking to get in touch with a CEO, finding email addresses is difficult and time-consuming. But Ive discovered a neat little trick that can help you
            <b>
             find any email address contact you need in 2 minutes tops.
            </b>
            It has worked for me 90% of thetime, whether I am reaching out to major press outlets, getting in touch with prominent investors, and most often, selling
            <a href="http://tintup.com" target="_blank">
             TINT
            </a>
            to Fortune 500 executives. This trick has helped me grow TINT from a failing startup to a
            <a href="http://www.tintup.com/blog/how-our-tech-startup-tint-hit-290000month-in-14-months/">
             400k/month profitable
            </a>
            business.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Heres what you need to do:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            1)
            <a href="http://gmail.com" target="_blank">
             Get a Gmail account
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://rapportive.com" target="_blank">
             download Rapportive on it
            </a>
            (http://rapportive.com).
           </p>
           <p>
            2) Connect your social networks on the top right hand corner after installing Rapportive.
           </p>
           <p>
            3) Click Compose on the left hand side.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://tint-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Screen-shot-2013-02-12-at-10.28.36-PM.png">
             <img alt="Rapportive will help you find email address contacts" class="aligncenter wp-image-113" height="132" src="http://tint-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Screen-shot-2013-02-12-at-10.28.36-PM.png" width="753"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             After installing Rapportive, connect your social networks and click Compose.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            Now here comes the fun part. Basically how Rapportive works is if you enter in a personsvalid email address, the side of the email will show the persons social networks, general location, etc. Rapportive is meant to be a widget to give you supplemental information on a person youre emailing, but I found out that you can take this one step further:
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             find any persons email address through trial-and-error until the persons information appears.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://tint-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Screen-shot-2013-02-12-at-10.41.55-PM.png">
             <img alt="find email addresses easily" class="aligncenter wp-image-116" height="544" src="http://tint-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Screen-shot-2013-02-12-at-10.41.55-PM.png" width="238"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <em>
             Example of what appears if you enter a valid email address.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            Now start guessing the email address and click out of the To: field after you try. Through many trial and errors I have done myself, I found out that there are
            <strong>
             6 combinations
            </strong>
            you can try to find it and they take &lt; 2 minutes. Lets say I want to find Ben Golds email and he works at tintup.com .
           </p>
           <p>
            1
            <sup>
             st
            </sup>
            : ben@tintup.com
           </p>
           <p>
            2
            <sup>
             nd
            </sup>
            : bengold@tintup.com (or ben.gold@tintup.com)
           </p>
           <p>
            3
            <sup>
             rd
            </sup>
            : bgold@tintup.com
           </p>
           <p>
            4
            <sup>
             th
            </sup>
            : beng@tintup.com
           </p>
           <p>
            5
            <sup>
             th
            </sup>
            : ben_gold@tintup.com
           </p>
           <p>
            The 6
            <sup>
             th
            </sup>
            one is one I found out works surprisingly well. Go see if they have a Facebook/Twitter/LinkedIn username. Copy that username and add @gmail.com, @yahoo.com, or @hotmail.com to the end of it.
           </p>
           <p>
            6
            <sup>
             th
            </sup>
            : twitterusername@gmail.com
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Youll know you found the right email address when see the persons information appear on the right hand side like so:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://tint-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Screen-shot-2013-02-12-at-10.44.16-PM.png">
             <img alt="email addresses" class="aligncenter wp-image-114" height="322" src="http://tint-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Screen-shot-2013-02-12-at-10.44.16-PM.png" width="529"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            And there you have it! Go ahead and try it for yourself by finding my email address (my name is Tim Sae Koo and I work at tintup.com). And if you do, say hi!
           </p>
           <p>
            If you liked this and it worked for you, feel free to share it out to others who could benefit.
            <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.tintup.com/blog/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            -Tim, CEO
           </p>
