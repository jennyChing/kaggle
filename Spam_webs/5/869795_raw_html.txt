             <span>
              When I see
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/39325907@N04/" target="_blank">
               Ben Chen's illustrations
              </a>
              I imagine an endless orgy of movies, video games, comics, and random pop culture icons going on inside his head. The resulting hangover transforms into these hooraying but witty and elegant pieces of graphic humor.
