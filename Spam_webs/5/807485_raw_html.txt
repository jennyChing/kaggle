         I found this quite amusing.
        </p>
        <p>
         This Kung Fu class poster looked relatively standard:
        </p>

         <img alt="jackie_chan" src="http://img.skitch.com/20080718-m8x53e725xyrarngfqmu5qbqfr.jpg"/>
