        <h1 class="h1Blog">
         New Petzval Art Lens Reinvented for Canon EF and Nikon F DSLR Mount Cameras
        </h1>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <span class="author">
          <a href="http://www.exposureguide.com/author/patricia-ramos/" rel="author" title="Posts by Patricia Ramos">
           Patricia Ramos
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="separator">
          
         </span>
         <span class="date">
          Jul 31, 2013
         </span>
         <span class="separator">
          
         </span>
         <span class="comment_count">
          <a href="http://www.exposureguide.com/equipment/new-petzval-art-lens-reinvented-for-canon-ef-and-nikon-f-dslr-mount-cameras/#disqus_thread">
          </a>
          <a href="http://www.exposureguide.com/equipment/new-petzval-art-lens-reinvented-for-canon-ef-and-nikon-f-dslr-mount-cameras/#comments">
           Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="post_content segmentblog">
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.exposureguide.com/?p=6895">
           <img alt="petzval mounted on dslrs" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6899" height="310" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-4-600x310.png" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Photography, for sure, has come a long way from its early beginnings. While this generation enjoys all the power, conveniences and economies made possible by digital technology, there are still some original concepts and blueprints worth revisiting. The
          <a href="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/lomography/the-lomography-petzval-portrait-lens">
           Petzval lens
          </a>
          is certainly one of them.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Folks over on
          <a href="http://microsites.lomography.com/petzval-lens/">
           Lomography
          </a>
          <a href="http://microsites.lomography.com/petzval-lens/">
           .com
          </a>
          and Zenit are behind the reincarnation of one of the very first and greatest lenses of all time originally invented by Joseph Petzval in Vienna, Austria more than 150 years ago.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-6895">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="new petzval lens" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6900" height="335" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-5-600x335.png" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          By supporting the Lomogaphy team on Kickstarter, you can help them resurrect this incredible lens which will fit Canon EF and Nikon F (D)SLR mount cameras of the present. Give your photos that distinctsharpness and crispness, strong color saturation, wonderful swirly bokeh effect, artful vignettes and narrow depth of field straight out of camera instead of spending hours in post replicating the very same effect.
         </p>
         <p>
          Those familiar with a traditional Waterhouse aperture system will put those skills to use with this model. The aperture options are:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           f/2.2
          </li>
          <li>
           f/4
          </li>
          <li>
           f/5.6
          </li>
          <li>
           f/8
          </li>
          <li>
           f/11
          </li>
          <li>
           f/16
          </li>
         </ul>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6905" style="width: 610px">
          <img alt="details petzval" class="size-large wp-image-6905" height="160" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-10-600x160.jpg" width="600"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           The Waterhouse Aperture System
          </p>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The new Lomography Petzval lens has great color saturation and high contrast. The resolution of the focused area of each photo is extremely high. Thanks to the new and improved Petzval optical design, the Lomography Lens has a large f/2.2 maximum aperture; its very hard to find older Petzval lenses which have an aperture larger than f/3.5. Because of the large aperture of the new lens, youll get photos with a very narrow depth of field, and when shooting with the aperture wide open, youll get a beautiful vignetting effect in your shots. Its the ideal portrait lens! says the literature on the
          <a href="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/lomography/the-lomography-petzval-portrait-lens">
           Kickstarter webpage
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Photos taken with the first working prototype of the new Petzval lens:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="sample photos petzval" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6902" height="424" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-7-600x424.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="petzval-lens-1" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6896" height="405" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-1-600x405.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="portrait of man taken with petzval lens" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6898" height="402" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-3-600x402.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Photos taken with an old Petzval lens from the 19th century:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="portrait of girl petzval lens" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6908" height="399" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-13-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="petzval sample photos" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6903" height="399" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-8-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="portrait of man petzval lens" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-6909" height="399" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-14-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Delivery date of the lenses is set on February 2014. However, a plan to have the first 1000 lenses shipped out by December 2013 is being set as a target.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Retail launch price of the Petzval lens is currently pegged at $499/499, with Special Edition models at $599/599. Significant savings can be made by supporting the Lomography team through Kickstarter. So far the effort has been incredibly successful.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6910" style="width: 610px">
          <img alt="old vs new petzval lens" class="size-large wp-image-6910" height="400" src="http://www.exposureguide.com/media/2013/07/petzval-lens-15-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           An old Petzval lens (shown on the left) and the new Lomography Petzval Lens (shown on the right)
          </p>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Head over to
          <a href="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/lomography/the-lomography-petzval-portrait-lens">
           Kickstarter
          </a>
          if you dont want to miss out on significant savings and a lens with a pedigreed history.
         </p>
