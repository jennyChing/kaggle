         <div id="content-area">
          <p>
           The 71st annual Golden Globes kicked off award season last night and it was a great start. We saw an awkward moment between Diddy and Bono and got plenty of laughs. So what were the highlights of last nights show? Read on for the top five:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #800080; font-size: 1.17em; line-height: 1.5em;">
             Tina Fey and Amy Poehler
            </span>
            <span style="color: #800080; font-size: 1.17em; line-height: 1.5em;">
             
            </span>
            <span style="color: #800080; font-size: 1.17em; line-height: 1.5em;">
             best hosting duo ever
            </span>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Tina-Fey-Amy-Poehler2.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Golden-Globes-2014-Tina-Fey-Amy-Poehler2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1174" height="460" src="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Tina-Fey-Amy-Poehler2.jpg" width="635"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://livingfiesta.com/golden-globes-2014-5-best-moments-of-the-evening/&amp;media=http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Tina-Fey-Amy-Poehler2.jpg&amp;description=Golden Globes 2014: 5 Best Moments of the Evening')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           As if their flawless outfits werent enough,
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Tina Fey and Amy Poehler raised the bar
           </span>
           in terms of whats required of an award show host. Their opening monologue was filled with jokes that were actually funny including a stab at George Clooneys affinity for dating women half his age. As the night went on they kept us laughing by poking fun at Leonardo DiCaprios love of supermodels and having Amy dress up as Tina Feys ill-mannered teenage son.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Jacqueline Bissets weird acceptance speech
           </span>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Jacqueline-Bissett-Best-Supporting-Actress.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Golden-Globes-2014-Jacqueline-Bissett-Best-Supporting-Actress" class="size-full wp-image-1167 aligncenter" height="415" src="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Jacqueline-Bissett-Best-Supporting-Actress.jpg" width="560"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://livingfiesta.com/golden-globes-2014-5-best-moments-of-the-evening/&amp;media=http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Jacqueline-Bissett-Best-Supporting-Actress.jpg&amp;description=Golden Globes 2014: 5 Best Moments of the Evening')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           After beating out Sofia Vergara in the Best Supporting Actress category for her role in
           <em>
            Dancing on the Edge,
           </em>
           British actress
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Jacqueline Bisset
           </span>
           took a long walk to the stage to deliver a lengthy and awkward speech. Its hard to decipher exactly what she said  partly because it was long and drawn out, and partly because we were too distracted looking at her disheveled hair.
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Jacqueline sported the laziest award show hair
           </span>
           since
           <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/2013-american-music-awards-red-carpet-highlights/" target="_blank" title="2013 American Music Awards: Red Carpet Highlights">
            Rihanna rocked a doobie at the AMAs
           </a>
           last year. Since she hadnt won a Globe in her career, didnt bother to comb her hair
           <em>
            and
           </em>
           got hammered before the second award was even presented lets just assume she thought she wouldnt win anything therefore didnt have to put in any effort. Not a good decision!
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Emma Thompson drunk on stage
           </span>
          </h3>
          <p>
           http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&amp;v=dKdQbFT2HFo
          </p>
          <p>
           Jacqueline Bissett wasnt the only star noticeably under the influence of a substance 
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Emma Thompson
           </span>
           looked like she had a drink or six herself. Thompson presented the award for Best Screenplay with her
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Louboutins in one hand and a martini in the other
           </span>
           . At least her dress was cute?
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #800080;">
             Apparently we should all be watching Brooklyn Nine-Nine
            </span>
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #800080;">
             <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Brooklyn-Nine-Nine.jpg">
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Golden-Globes-2014-Brooklyn-Nine-Nine" class="size-full wp-image-1168 aligncenter" height="276" src="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Brooklyn-Nine-Nine.jpg" width="490"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://livingfiesta.com/golden-globes-2014-5-best-moments-of-the-evening/&amp;media=http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-Brooklyn-Nine-Nine.jpg&amp;description=Golden Globes 2014: 5 Best Moments of the Evening')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </a>
            </span>
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Andy Sambergs
           <em>
            Brooklyn Nine-Nine
           </em>
           was one of a handful of shows that debuted this fall and though no one seemed to be watching, according to the Golden Globes we all
           <em>
            should
           </em>
           be. The FOX sitcom was two for two nabbing Best TV series musical or comedy and Best Actor in a TV series (Samberg). Interested in checking it out?
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Season 1 returns tomorrow, Tuesday January 14 at 8:30pm on FOX.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <span style="color: #800080;">
            Did 12 Years a Slave get snubbed?
           </span>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-12-Years-A-Slave.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Steve McQueen celebrates after winning Best Motion Picture, Drama at the 71st annual Golden Globe Awards in Beverly Hills" class="size-full wp-image-1164 aligncenter" height="378" src="http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-12-Years-A-Slave.jpg" width="567"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://livingfiesta.com/golden-globes-2014-5-best-moments-of-the-evening/&amp;media=http://livingfiesta.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Golden-Globes-2014-12-Years-A-Slave.jpg&amp;description=Golden Globes 2014: 5 Best Moments of the Evening')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           This biopic based on the personal diaries of Solomon Northup, a free man sold into slavery, managed to nab seven nominations. As fans watched the film get passed up for Best Original Score, Best Screenplay, Best Director, Best Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture, Best Supporting Actor in a Motion Picture and Best Actor in a Motion Picture (Drama) all hope was lost. Then 12 Years a Slave took home one of the top honors of the night 
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #800080;">
             Best Motion Picture, Drama.
            </span>
           </strong>
           At least we got another chance to see Lupita Nyongo in that red dress right?
          </p>
