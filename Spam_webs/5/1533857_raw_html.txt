          <p>
           Mike and Christy Irish sent me these pictures of their recent project the Wee Irish Cottage. Here is what Mike has to say about the cottage.
          </p>
          <p>
           The cottage is 18x8, built on a tandem axel trailer. It has a sleeping loft, undercounter fridge, small bathroom with shower and composting toilet. Shower is rudimentary; toilet is brand new.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://netdna.tinyhouseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/PB300049.jpg">
            <img alt="PB300049" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-9002" height="450" src="http://netdna.tinyhouseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/PB300049-600x450.jpg" title="PB300049" width="600"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The interior is finished in pine with bamboo flooring, therompane windows. The floor, walls and ceiling are well insulated. 4x4 opening Velux skylight makes for light filled interior. High ceiling (with ceiling fan) gives the cozy cottage a spacious feeling.
           <span id="more-9001">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Included is a stainless marine wood stove that burns solid fuel (from pellets to pine cones). This little place epitomizes living large in a small space.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Irishs are selling their cottage for $14,000 and you can contact them via e-mail: irish@gorge.net, or phone: 541-296-5225. The Wee Irish Cottage is located in The Dalles, Oregon.Be sure and tell them you heard about it through the Tiny House Blog.
          </p>
