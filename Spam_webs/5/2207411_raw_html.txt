          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://iaremunyee.com/token/" rel="bookmark">
            Token.
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="posted-on">
            <a href="http://iaremunyee.com/token/" rel="bookmark" title="5:49 pm">
             <i class="icon-calendar-empty">
             </i>
             <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2015-03-19T17:49:54+00:00">
              19th March 2015
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           |
           <a class="comments-link" href="#">
            15 Comments
           </a>
          </div>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <a data-rel="lightbox-0" href="http://iaremunyee.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/-F927DF1CD7D87258FB0746544AC174BF813C53EE00AF386933-pimgpsh_fullsize_distr.jpg?0f8286">
            <img alt="^F927DF1CD7D87258FB0746544AC174BF813C53EE00AF386933^pimgpsh_fullsize_distr" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2703" height="484" src="http://iaremunyee.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/-F927DF1CD7D87258FB0746544AC174BF813C53EE00AF386933-pimgpsh_fullsize_distr-1024x799.jpg?0f8286" width="620"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           I am standing in a line of greens and blues.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its moving but I know its never going to be my turn.
          </p>
          <p>
           Because although
          </p>
          <p>
           I am the 3am phone calls, the tear-streaked confessions and angry monologues,
          </p>
          <p>
           the guardian of secrets, the healer of wounds, the wisest of sages,
          </p>
          <p>
           I am merely a placeholder until someone better comes along.
          </p>
          <p>
           I am your crutch, your splint; when someone reaches in, cracks your ribs and breaks your heart,
          </p>
          <p>
           but only ever a temporary importance, until your wounds scab over and your bones mend.
          </p>
          <p>
           I am the first person you run to but never the one you choose.
          </p>
          <p>
           I am the one who fixes you butnever good enough to make you stay.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           I dont understand,
          </p>
          <p>
           why you insist on finding someone who loves you the way I already do,
          </p>
          <p>
           why you keep looking for whats already in front of you,
          </p>
          <p>
           why you would give everyone a chance but me.
          </p>
          <p>
           I know love is blind but Cupid, did you get her in the eyes?
          </p>
          <p>
           Is that why she doesnt see me?
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           I thought Id let you know that I am tired,
          </p>
          <p>
           of being good but not good enough,
          </p>
          <p>
           of being close but never close enough,
          </p>
          <p>
           of always being in the blind spot of your love.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tired of making you my forever when I am your never.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Every time I want to give up playing this game that I cannot win, you keep offering me token after token, asking me to
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           insert credit try again, insert credit try again, insert credit try again
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           I thought Id let you know that I dont want to play anymore.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: right;">
           <sub>
            <em>
             Let me in or let me go.
            </em>
           </sub>
          </p>
