        <p>
         <a href="http://www.dinnertimeapp.com/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="dinnertime-app-logo" class="alignright wp-image-15872 size-full" height="96" src="http://www.ricksdailytips.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dinnertime-app-logo.gif" width="300"/>
         </a>
         Do you wish you could control when your children can use theirmobile phones and which apps they can use at whattimes, even when you arent with them?
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, thanks to a great free app called
         <a href="http://www.dinnertimeapp.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           DinnertTime
          </strong>
         </a>
         , you can. Simpy install the app on both your childs phone and your own phone, then link the two phones via a text message.
         <span id="more-15870">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once the apps are installed and the phones are linked, youll be able to designateperiods of time when your childs phone will be placed into an inactive state. Texting, playing games and running apps wont be allowed, but by default the phone can still make and receive calls in case an emergency arises.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can change the behavior of your childs phone remotely, in real time, and you can enable certain apps even when the rest of the apps are disabled. For example, you can allow the use of the camera app during a break or the calculator app during study time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dinnertime is free, although you can choose a version that allows in-app purchases. (The free version is the one I recommend.)
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.dinnertimeapp.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Click here
          </strong>
         </a>
         to read more about the DinnerTime app and access the download links.
        </p>
