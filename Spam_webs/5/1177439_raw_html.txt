confused yet contemplative face as if to say, "But what do you
mean?" He's a good boy who spends his days asleep in unusual
positions under my desk at work or chasing our beautiful cat in our

       <p class="desc">
        Kevin's mom says: Kevin is our Bernese mountain dog and he is
growing before our eyes! He is happy to just be wherever we are and
would definitely be one of the most docile pups I have ever met. He
is smart (but a bit slow) and he often looks at us with this
confused yet contemplative face as if to say, "But what do you
mean?" He's a good boy who spends his days asleep in unusual
positions under my desk at work or chasing our beautiful cat in our
home. :)
       </p>
