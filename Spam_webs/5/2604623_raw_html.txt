             <p>
              Contrary to popular belief, celebrities are not just like us.
             </p>
             <p>
              They are so beyond us, that even their tales of drunken arrests put us to shame. Case in point: Shia LaBeouf explaining to
              <a href="http://mashable.com/category/jimmy-kimmel/">
               Jimmy Kimmel
              </a>
              the insane story behind his run-in last June with the law.
             </p>
             <p>
              Apparently the incident involved lots of whiskey, grabbing Alan Cummings' butt, chasing a homeless man, a Broadway play, feeding a woman strawberries, spitting on a cop's shoe and a McDonald's egg sandwich.
             </p>
             <p>
              Thanks for putting the drunken nights of us plebeians' to shame, Shia.
