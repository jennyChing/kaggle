        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Get Stoked on Social: Santa Cruzs Best Twitter Accounts
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://santacruzlife.com/author/josh/" rel="author">
            Joshua Vincent
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="Twitter Santa Cruz" class="alignright size-full wp-image-3705" height="295" src="http://santacruzlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/twitter-santa-cruz.png" width="300"/>
          Depending on whether youre on Twitter or not, youve either heard the phrase or said it yourself in regard to the simple, yet somehow befuddling, social media platform:
         </p>
         <p>
          I dont know what I would say.
         </p>
         <p>
          Believe it or not, you can just listen  and you probably should. Twitter is a real-time circus of information where there are perpetual conversations happening around every topic you can imagine, including Santa Cruz.
         </p>
         <p>
          Every best of list is imperfect and this one is no exception. I encourage readers to add their favorite local accounts (or your own) in the comments section below.
         </p>
         <p>
          For the quick and dirty version of Santa Cruzs best Tweeters, Ive added these accounts to a
          <a href="https://twitter.com/JoshuaMVincent/santa-cruz-tweeters/members" target="_blank">
           Twitter list
          </a>
          for easy following.
          <br/>
          <span id="more-3240">
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Who To Follow On Twitter In Santa Cruz
         </h3>
         <h4>
          Bloggers
         </h4>
         <p>
          <img alt="The Aums Mama" class="alignleft" height="48" src="https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_images/378800000605302682/97e6c8c62ab79c82ef144ecc85cf6115.jpeg" style="border: 1px solid gray;" width="48"/>
          <a href="https://twitter.com/theaumsmama" target="_blank">
           @theaumsmama
          </a>
           Video correspondent and writer for iVillage/NBC,
          <a href="http://www.theaums.com/" target="_blank">
           mom blogger
          </a>
          and writer for the Santa Cruz Visitors Council.
         </p>
         <div style="clear: both">
         </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="Motherhood: A Descent into Madness" class="alignleft" height="48" src="https://si0.twimg.com/profile_images/3223396212/a7b01dbcca2984f578efb42699af2c3e.jpeg" style="border: 1px solid gray;" width="48"/>
          <a href="https://twitter.com/MotherhoodADIM" target="_blank">
           @MotherhoodADIM
          </a>
           Hilariously R-rated insight and musings from a Santa Cruz mom raising three boys. You can find her blog
          <a href="http://motherhoodadescentintomadness.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <div style="clear: both">
         </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="Santa Cruz Life" class="alignleft" height="48" src="https://si0.twimg.com/profile_images/3742638813/0430742bbe63d38af8b864bd3b7fef83_bigger.jpeg" style="border: 1px solid gray;" width="48"/>
          <a href="https://twitter.com/SantaCruzLife" target="_blank">
           @SantaCruzLife
