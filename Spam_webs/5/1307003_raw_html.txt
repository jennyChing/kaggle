        <p>
         The tuning specialists from
         <strong>
          Edo Competition
         </strong>
         have modified only super cars until now and their latest tuning project is based on a
         <strong>
          Ferrari Enzo
         </strong>
         . Edo Competition modified this cars V12 6.0L engine to develop more than 700 HP at 8200 rpm. The car will accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.5 second and the its maximum speed is a breathtaking 370km/h. To improve the performances of this incredible Enzo the guys from Edo have installed a modified exhaust system, new RAM admission and new clutch. At all these they added some carbon fiber elements to this cars body kit to make it weight less and they also installed light rims that will go really well at incredible speeds. Even though its color is not pure red like a normal Ferrari, this car will surely impress everyone with its performances.

         </span>
