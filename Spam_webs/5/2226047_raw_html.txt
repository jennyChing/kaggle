         <a data-rel="prettyPhoto" href="http://onthedeck.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/homeowners-gallery-pool-decking.jpg">

        <p>
         As awareness around the devastating effects of global warming, deforestation and pollution increases, more and more people across the world are making an effort to be kinder to the environment. There are numerous little things that one can do to play ones part in helping to save and conserve our planet: recycling, using bio-degradable products, solar heating and attempting to use more sustainable green materials in our homes, are just a few of the things on the list. When it comes to things like
         <a href="http://www.envirodeck.co.za/swimming-pool-decking">
          outdoor decking
         </a>
         , gone are the days when wood was the only option. Plastic wood materials have been developed as an alternative to high maintenance materials previously used. Heres all you need to know about composite decking and all its benefits.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <b>
          What is composite decking, exactly?
         </b>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Any material that is composite is a product not made of wood, but rather comprises of 95 percent recycled materials. Wood is essentially a non-renewable resource that eventually warps, develops mould and even fades in colour. Composite materials are gentler on the environment in that they are easy to maintain, are longer lasting and they do not contain any toxic chemicals. Using composite decking, such as that offered by
         <a href="http://www.envirodeck.co.za/about-envirodeck-composite-decking/composite-decks-profile">
          Envirodeck
         </a>
         , means that homeowners can enjoy all the benefits of a wooden deck without all maintenance fuss and cost.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <b>
          What are the benefits of choosing composite decking?
         </b>
        </h2>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Low Maintenance
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         The beauty about decks made from composite materials is that they dont need to be re-painted, re-stained or re-oiled, which means that you save both time and money. The most work youll ever do is a quick wash or sweep every so often. It really is that simple!
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Long-Lasting Colour
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         The unique combination of plastic and wood, coupled with the fact that composite decking does not need to be stained or painted means that the vibrant colour remains locked in and long-lasting. Your decking will always look as good as new.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Moisture Resistant
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         The specialised encapsulation process used by Envirodeck during the production of their composite decking results in a significant reduction in moisture build-up. For those living in coastal areas, this is ideal.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Safe and Comfortable
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Composite decking does not splinter like wood decking and is not slippery. These features make it the best option for areas around the pool as well as for patio decks. You can confidently walk around barefoot.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Durability
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Traditional wood decking tends to rot, buckle and warp over time. Decks made from composite material are generally more sturdy and durable, once again saving you time and money.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Natural Beauty
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Composite decking has a natural wood grain texture and with a range of different contemporary colours available, your deck can look just the way youd like. It also doesnt hurt that its scratch, stain, fade and mould resistant, particularly in the South African climate.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Easy Installation
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         An integrated concealed clip system with grooved decking allows for simple and fast installation and a seamless finish.
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres really no reason why composite decking shouldnt be your first choice when it comes to choosing a suitable and sustainable deck material for your home. Envirodeck offers a range of wood plastic decking options together with a collection of quality
         <a href="http://www.envirodeck.co.za/wood-plastic-decking-outdoor-showers">
          accessories
         </a>
         to compliment your patio or pool deck. With composite decking, you can give your outdoor area a facelift while still being green and kind to the environment.
        </p>
