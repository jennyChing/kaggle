        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         Weapons for a ceasefire
        </h2>
        <div class="imgright" style="width: 184px;">
         <div class="imgmain">
          <a href="/img/3-xydeldimafqzu2/Ukrainian-soldiers">
           <img alt="Ukrainian soldiers" height="117" src="http://img.wikinut.com/img/3-xydeldimafqzu2/jpeg/180x300/Ukrainian-soldiers.jpeg" title="Ukrainian soldiers" width="180"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="imgtitle">
          <a href="/img/3-xydeldimafqzu2/Ukrainian-soldiers">
           Ukrainian soldiers
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         The contemplated in Washington's delivery of weapons against the government in Kiev could stop the rebels. A little over a week, the pro-Russian separatists announced in eastern Ukraine, they wanted to conquer the entire Donetsk region. In other words, you want to carry the war into areas in which it was not yet or not fought since last summer so far.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Since then, the violence escalates. At the same time, the leader of the separatist sabotage the talks on a truce in which they send negotiators without powers and always put new conditions on further meetings.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The fact that so far always tried to balance, and neutrality of the OSCE mediators have assigned on weekends separatists blamed on the failure of the talks, speaks for itself.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As always, the messages from both sides from the war zone are often contradictory, but from the claims of the separatists over territorial gains and from the regularly following denials of Ukrainians, it is, nevertheless, an overall picture: The Ukrainian armed forces attacking pro-Russian forces barely withstand.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The danger is really that ever larger areas of Ukraine are drawn from the war, that to Donetsk and Luhansk Other cities are destroyed, there will be more refugees.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Now, reports the "New York Times," the United States were considering departing from its previous line and yet to supply weapons to the Ukrainian armed forces. This could be the army might enable to stop the advance of the Separatists - and thus create the conditions to ensure that they are ready again to talk seriously about a ceasefire.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         More weapons but can also lead to a rapid escalation of fighting - from the Russian perspective, it would be logical to try to gain as much territory against a possible supply of American arms and inflict much damage as possible to the Ukrainian armed forces.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         And new Russian tanks and guns are in doubt faster in the war zone as American. In addition, it is unclear whether in the past twenty years systematically neglected Ukrainian armed forces, in general, have staff that can deal with American weapons.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         It is certain, however: To a reform of the Ukrainian government is to strengthen all of its institutions - including the armed forces. For this purpose, both the United States and the Europeans should be independent from the question of how useful are weapon's deliveries actually contribute - with material, but especially with training.
         <br/>
