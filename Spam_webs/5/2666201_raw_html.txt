       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Why did Hyoun Ju Sohn, Brown University physics student jump to his death?
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-01T22:01:18+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 1, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://scallywagandvagabond.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Christopher Koulouris
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://scallywagandvagabond.com/2015/04/hyoun-ju-sohn-brown-university-physics-student-suicide-jump-to-his-death/#comments">
           <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="131560 http://scallywagandvagabond.com/?p=131560">
            1 Comment
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_131561" style="width: 644px">
         <img alt="Hyoun Ju Sohn" class="wp-image-131561 size-full" height="476" src="//scallywagandvagabond.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/h2.jpg" width="634"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Did a culture of super success at the ivy league school lead to the first year doctoral student, Hyoun Ju Sohn (pictured) to take his own life?
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          Authorities are trying to understand what inspired
          <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/pub/hyoun-ju-sohn/81/993/384">
           <strong>
            Hyoun Ju Sohn
           </strong>
          </a>
          , a 25 year old
          <strong>
           Brown University
          </strong>
          graduate student to commit suicide after
          <a href="http://www.browndailyherald.com/2015/04/01/grad-student-dies-after-fall-from-scili/">
           <strong>
            jumping from the 12 floor
           </strong>
          </a>
          of a campus building.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p1">
         <span class="s1">
          A first year doctoral student from
          <strong>
           South Korea
          </strong>
          studying physics, Sohn died Tuesday around noon after plunging from the
          <strong>
           Sciences Library building
          </strong>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p3">
         <span class="s2">
          Pursuant to the shock death of the student,
         </span>
         <span class="s1">
          <strong>
           Brown President Christina Paxson
          </strong>
          said in a
          <a href="http://www.golocalprov.com/news/brown-university-graduate-student-dies-in-fall-from-science-library-buildin">
           <strong>
            statement
           </strong>
          </a>
          that the university was 
          <em>
           deeply saddened
          </em>
          by the students death.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p3">
         <span class="s1">
          Making the mans suicide strange was the fact that Hyoun Ju Sohn had received 
          <em>
           glowing evaluations
          </em>
          as a teaching assistant at Brown.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <span class="s1">
          He was named a
          <strong>
           Rabi Scholar
          </strong>
          for
          <em>
           exceptional promise in the sciences
          </em>
          when he entered
          <strong>
           Columbia University
          </strong>
          and completed his undergraduate degree in 2013.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <span class="s1">
          The student was particularly interested in the physics of condensed matter.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         Unconfirmed reports
         <a href="http://www.browndailyherald.com/2015/04/01/grad-student-dies-after-fall-from-scili/">
          <strong>
           told
          </strong>
         </a>
         that Providence Police officers were already at the library searching for a distressed student before the incident occurred after a possible suicide attempt earlier in the day.
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <img alt="Hyoun Ju Sohn" class="aligncenter wp-image-131562" height="679" src="//scallywagandvagabond.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/h1.jpg" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <span class="s1">
          A vigil of 350 people was held Tuesday night outside the campus center whilst smaller meetings were held for students and faculty reports
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <span class="s1">
          Paxson said counseling will be available for graduate and undergraduate students.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <span class="s1">
          The 12th floor of the library was closed off as of 2.30pm, according to the
          <a href="http://www.providencejournal.com/article/20150331/NEWS/150339860">
           <span class="s3">
            <b>
             Providence Journal
            </b>
           </span>
          </a>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <span class="s1">
          A worker was seen sweeping away a broken window below the library.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         And then there was these comments that made me wonder:
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <em>
          Maybe now the university, the Graduate School, and CAPS will recognize how crucial it is to reach out to the grad students on campus who are suffering. There are (minimal) resources on campus, but many grad students are unaware of them, dont have the time, or are afraid of using them because of stigma. Wellness sessions arent enough. People need to know when theyre NOT well, there is somewhere to go, no judgement allowed.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <em>
          Something pushed him to do it. There are a lot of pressures at Brown to succeed.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="p5">
         <em>
          <img alt="Hyoun Ju Sohn" class="aligncenter wp-image-131563" height="679" src="//scallywagandvagabond.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/h.jpg" width="550"/>
         </em>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_131564" style="width: 560px">
         <img alt="Hyoun Ju Sohn" class="wp-image-131564" height="733" src="//scallywagandvagabond.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/h3.jpg" width="550"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Sciences Library building
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="p5">
