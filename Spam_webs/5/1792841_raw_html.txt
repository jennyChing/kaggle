                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     <a href="http://leafytreetopspot.blogspot.com/2013/06/free-pattern-friday-crocheted-apple-hat.html">
                      Free Pattern Friday: Crocheted Apple Hat by Micah Makes
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content">
                     <a href="http://www.bloglovin.com/blog/2091875/?claim=3d7ynuavwz3">

                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-H1CghilFiVo/UcRgWh3QBvI/AAAAAAAAIJQ/BxYP9uDpH4g/s400/crocheted+apple+hat+pattern+free.jpg" width="300"/>

                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I love a free pattern, and an easy one at that. I've been in a bit of a crocheting mood, so I couldn't resist trying out the adorable
                      <a href="http://www.micahmakes.com/blog/crochet-apple-hat-pattern" target="_blank">
                       Crocheted Apple Hat Pattern
                      </a>
                      by
                      <a href="http://www.micahmakes.com/" target="_blank">
                       Micah Makes
                      </a>

                     </div>

                      <br/>
                     </div>

                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-b8zXTX38llA/UcRgT95kbMI/AAAAAAAAIJI/FSiKoc8Pusc/s1600/free+crochet+apple+hat+pattern.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-b8zXTX38llA/UcRgT95kbMI/AAAAAAAAIJI/FSiKoc8Pusc/s400/free+crochet+apple+hat+pattern.jpg" width="300"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     It worked up so quickly, and the addition of a simple stem, and leaf really make this hat. I think it will make an adorable photo prop for a little babe.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     You can find the
                     <a href="http://www.micahmakes.com/blog/crochet-apple-hat-pattern" target="_blank">
                      free crochet apple hat pattern
                     </a>
                     on Micah's website, along with several other free patterns.
