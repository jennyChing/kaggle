         <h1>
          Free Crochet Pattern: Midnight Wrap
         </h1>
        </div>
        <div class="post-byline">
         By The Steady Hand on February 27th, 2014 
609 views
        </div>
        <div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
         <p>
          I have another FREE crochet pattern to share with you! This pattern is to work up a wrap that is soft and very comfortable to wear. The nights here in TN are still getting pretty cold and I wanted something to wrap myself up in when we went out for dinner. I hope you like it!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="midnight wrap crochet pattern - thesteadyhandblog" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19971" height="667" src="http://thesteadyhandblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/midnight-wrap-crochet-pattern-thesteadyhandblog.jpg" width="500"/>
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <h2 align="center">
           Midnight Wrap
          </h2>
          <p>
           <b>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             BEFORE STARTING
            </span>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Size:
           </b>
           One size.
           <br/>
           <b>
            Finished Measurements:
           </b>
           13 wide and 68 long.
           <br/>
           <b>
            Difficulty:
           </b>
           Easy
           <br/>
           <b>
            Yarn:
           </b>
           Worsted/Aran/Afghan/Medium weight. I used Caron One Pound in Royalty.
           <br/>
           <b>
            Hook size:
           </b>
           G (4.00mm)  or hook needed to obtain gauge
           <br/>
           <b>
            Notions:
           </b>
           stitch markers, yarn needle &amp; scissors.
           <br/>
           <b>
            Gauge:
           </b>
           12 tc = 4
          </p>
          <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             ABBREVIATIONS
            </span>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           Ch chain
           <br/>
           Sl st Slip stitch
           <br/>
           St Stitch
           <br/>
           Sc Single crochet
           <br/>
           Dc Double crochet
           <br/>
           Tc Triple crochet
          </p>
          <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             INSTRUCTIONS
            </span>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             Notes
            </span>
           </b>
           <br/>
           This wrap is worked in short rounds. Ch 1 does not count as a sc. Ch 3 counts as a dc. Ch 4 counts as a tc.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ch 43.
           <br/>
           Row 1: Dc in 4
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           ch from hook, dc in each st across. Turn. (40)
           <br/>
           Row 2: Ch 4, tc in each st across. Turn. (40)
           <br/>
           Row 3: Ch 1, sc in 1
           <sup>
            st
           </sup>
           st and each st across. Turn (40)
           <br/>
           Row 4: Ch 3, dc in each st across. Turn (40)
           <br/>
           Repeat Rows 2-4 until your piece is at least 60 long. Keep going if you want it to be longer!
           <br/>
           Once the wrap is the length you desire fasten off and sew in all loose ends.
          </p>
