       <h2>
        The Worlds Tallest Inflatable Waterslide Hits the Market
       </h2>
       <div class="pl_info_post">
        <div class="plcount_comment">
         0 Comment
        </div>
        <div class="plauthor">
         2014-06-26 11:39:06
        </div>
        <div class="plclear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="plpost_content">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          <strong>
           The 4-story tall unit showcases some of the industry's best safety features.
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Santa Fe Springs, CA---eInflatables, the largest manufacturer of inflatable units made in the USA, announces an exciting new unit for 2014. The two-piece
         <strong>
          <a href="http://einflatables.com/inflatable-water-slides/inflatable-42-blue-crush-2-lane-run-n-splash-combo.html" rel="nofollow">
           42 Blue Crush Run N' Splash Combo
          </a>
         </strong>
         is the worlds tallest inflatable waterslide! The unit measures 42 high and 115 long, can be used wet or dry, and features many cutting edge safety features. This is the latest and most exciting slide to be added to the best selling line of Blue Crush waterslides, which already features 27, 32 and 36 slides.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         This slide can be set up at the beginning of the season, and bring in profits for months. Its high quality, low maintenance, and is proving to be one of the most popular products we have ever created, says Steve Nero, CEO of eInflatables. This unit brings the ultimate in excitement for riders, while still maintaining the highest safety standards. Were so proud of this product.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="350" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/5ohSNVyIz2E" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="425">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The waterslide hosts dual lanes for maximum throughput, and can be used in a pay-per-play business model for high profitability. Its perfect for resorts, campgrounds, fairs, festivals, corporate events, and promotional events.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         An emphasis on safety is clear in every aspect of the slides design. Safety rules are clearly and strategically placed on the unit to inform riders of the slide rules. The ladder design features built in steps that force riders to lean forward while climbing up the slide. It eliminates the looped handles found on many other inflatable units, which allow riders to lean backwards, and assume an unsafe position, increasing the likelihood of a dangerous fall. Mesh screens cover the steepest parts of both the ladder and the slide in order to enclose and protect riders until safer inclines are reached.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         100% constant air helps keep pressure in the unit at a safe and consistent rate, in comparison to sealed air units where leaks and punctures are more likely to occur. Additionally, with constant air, the two-piece
         <strong>
          <a href="http://einflatables.com/inflatable-water-slides/inflatable-42-blue-crush-2-lane-run-n-splash-combo.html" rel="nofollow">
           42 Blue Crush Run N' Splash Combo
          </a>
         </strong>
         can be set up once and left to run all season long.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <strong>
          <a href="http://einflatables.com/inflatable-water-slides/inflatable-42-blue-crush-2-lane-run-n-splash-combo.html" rel="nofollow">
           42 Blue Crush Run N' Splash Combo
          </a>
         </strong>
         features custom fit slide covers that are fastened to the unit with heavy-duty velcro to provide a faster and smoother ride. The covers also help prevent situations in which riders become stuck in spots that lose slickness over time, increasing the thrill factor of this innovative attraction.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The ride ends on a nearly 100 long landing strip that is lined with several arches with continually running sprinklers. These sprinklers keep the strip constantly wet so riders can end their experience in comfort.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
