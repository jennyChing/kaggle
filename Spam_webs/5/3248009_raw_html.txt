        <h1 itemprop="headline">
         How To Brew The Perfect Coffee: Lifehacker's Complete Guide
        </h1>
        <div class="meta-wrap">
         <div class="meta">
          <a class="tracking_article_author" data-tracking-name="Tessa Miller" href="/author/tessa-miller/" rel="author">
           <span itemprop="author">
            Tessa Miller
           </span>
          </a>
          <time datetime="2013-03-18T10:00:00+11:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           18 March 2013 10:00 AM
          </time>
         </div>
         <div class="social tracking_article_social">
          <a class="btn btn-mini share anchor-link" data-target="#post-490978 .collapse" data-toggle="collapse" data-tracking-name="Share" href="#share" rel="nofollow" title="Share">
           <i class="icon-share-alt">
           </i>
           Share
          </a>
          <a class="btn btn-mini discuss anchor-link" data-tracking-name="Discuss" href="#comments" rel="nofollow" title="Discuss">
           <i class="icon-comment">
           </i>
           Discuss
          </a>
          <a class="btn btn-mini bookmark disabled" data-post-id="490978" data-target="/wp-admin/admin-ajax.php?id=login_to_bookmark" data-toggle="modal" data-tracking-name="Bookmark" title="Bookmark this post">
           <i class="icon-bookmark">

         </div>
        </div>
        <div id="content_post" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge centre">
          <a href="http://img.gawkerassets.com/img/18gvtpcr93yumjpg/original.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://img.gawkerassets.com']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://img.gawkerassets.com/img/18gvtpcr93yumjpg/ku-xlarge.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          We spent last week learning all about coffee with some help our friends from
          <a href="http://www.tonx.org" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.tonx.org']);">
           Tonx
          </a>
          , the US fresh roast subscription service. We covered everything from why you should care about quality coffee and how to pick the best beans to the nitty gritty of gear selection and the best resources to tackle a coffee hobby head on. Heres the complete guide in all its glory.
          <span id="more-490978">
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="media_wrap image medium right">
          <a href="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-01__2_.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://img.gawkerassets.com']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_medium" src="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-01__2_.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2013/03/how-to-brew-the-perfect-coffee-lesson-1-the-case-for-better-coffee">
           Lesson 1: The Case for Better Coffee
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          In this first lesson, we tackle the case for a better cup of coffee. Is there really much difference between convenience store sludge and an expensive cafe blend? And if there is, why should I care?
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="media_wrap image medium right">
          <a href="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-02__2_.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://img.gawkerassets.com']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_medium" src="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-02__2_.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2013/03/how-to-brew-the-perfect-coffee-lesson-2-how-to-select-your-beans/">
           Lesson 2: How To Select Your Beans
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Even the simplest brewing device can produce otherworldly brews when paired with great coffee. Finding an approach into the often confusing landscape of consumer coffee bean choices is the essential first step to improving your morning ritual.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="media_wrap image medium right">
          <a href="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-03__2_.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://img.gawkerassets.com']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_medium" src="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-03__2_.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2013/03/how-to-brew-the-perfect-coffee-lesson-3-the-basics-of-brewing/">
           Lesson 3: The Basics Of Brewing
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Contrary to how it might seem if you listen to hardcore coffee geeks, brewing good cups of coffee at home doesnt have to be approached as if youre taking on a whole new hobby. A few basic principles will give you a toehold into almost every brew method and with just a bit of trial and error (and of course good beans) youll discover that a great cup doesnt have to be hard to find.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="media_wrap image medium right">
          <a href="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-04__3_.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://img.gawkerassets.com']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_medium" src="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-04__3_.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2013/03/how-to-brew-the-perfect-coffee-lesson-4-how-to-choose-your-weapons/">
           Lesson 4: How To Choose Your Weapons
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          There are many manual methods to choose from, and some that appear simple are actually somewhat fussy. Others that look complicated at first glance turn out to be easy and reliable with practice. There is no absolute right way to prepare a cup, and most of the popular methods will give you good results if you begin with great fresh roasted beans and take a bit of care with your measurements.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="media_wrap image medium right">
          <a href="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-05__4_.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://img.gawkerassets.com']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_medium" src="http://img.gawkerassets.com/post/17/2013/03/coffee-morning-school-part-05__4_.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2013/03/how-to-brew-the-perfect-cup-lesson-5-additional-resources/">
           Lesson 5: Additional Resources
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Now that youve subjected yourself to our good coffee propaganda, learned the basics of brewing, and explored some of the popular brew methods, well cut through much of the online clutter and leave you with some selected resources that can continue your path to full-on coffee nerd.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
