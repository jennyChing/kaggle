          <h1>
           Aerosmith Announce Summer 2012 Tour
          </h1>
          <div class="post_meta">
           <span class="author_avatar">
            <a href="http://ultimateclassicrock.com/author/billydukesgmail-com/" title="Billy Dukes">
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_author">
            <span>

            </span>

           </span>
           <span class="the_date">
            February 7, 2012 1:11 PM
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="social-group-top large">
           <div class="social_icons size_large">
            <a class="facebook share_link" href="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fultimateclassicrock.com%2Faerosmith-announce-summer-2012-tour%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dfbshare_top&amp;t=Aerosmith+Announce+Summer+2012+Tour" target="_blank" title="Share on Facebook">
             <div class="social_icon_large">
             </div>
             <span class="social_icon">
              Share on Facebook
             </span>
            </a>
            <a class="twitter share_link" href="http://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Check+out+Aerosmith+Announce+Summer+2012+Tour+http%3A%2F%2Fultimateclassicrock.com%2Faerosmith-announce-summer-2012-tour%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dtwitter_top+%40UltClassicRock" target="_blank" title="Share on Twitter">

            <img alt="Steven Tyler" class="size-full wp-image-48039" height="450" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/ultimateclassicrock.com/files/2012/02/Steven-Tyler.jpg" title="Steven Tyler" width="300"/>
            <figcaption style="width:300px;">
             Ethan Miller, Getty Images
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ultimateclassicrock.com/tags/aerosmith/">
             Aerosmith
            </a>
            guitarist
            <a href="http://ultimateclassicrock.com/tags/joe-perry/">
             Joe Perry
            </a>
            has revealed that the band will indeed tour in 2012, but not until after frontman
            <a href="http://ultimateclassicrock.com/tags/steven-tyler/">
             Steven Tyler
            </a>
            finishes up his duties as 
            <a href="http://ultimateclassicrock.com/category/american-idol/">
             American Idol
            </a>
             judge. AEROSMITH U.S. TOUR STARTS IN JUNE! tickets on sale in March, Perry
            <a href="https://twitter.com/#!/JoePerry/status/166729199359496193" target="_blank">
             tweeted
            </a>
            on Monday night.
            <span id="more-48028">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Nothing else about the tour  including dates, venues, opening acts and length  has been revealed. It seems like the run could sync up nicely with the new Aerosmith album, which Perry recently said has a feel like some of the early stuff because theyre once again working with producer Jack Douglas ( who worked with the band on Toys in the Attic, Rocks and more).
           </p>
           <p>
            Weve tried to bring that back, Perry
            <a href="http://ultimateclassicrock.com/aerosmith-guitarist-joe-perry-new-record-has-a-feel-like-some-of-the-early-stuff/">
             says
            </a>
            . People are always asking for something that sounds like the old stuff, but you cant rewriteMama Kin orSame Old Song And Dance. All you can do is start from the same place you started when you made those records.
           </p>
           <p>
            It may be too early to make predictions, but it seems like taking advantage of Americas biggest stage to release a new single would make sense for the rockers. Wouldnt a performance during the American Idol finale make a lot of sense?
           </p>
           <p>
            It certainly would be a great chance to familiarize viewers with the bands new product and touring schedule, if any tickets are still available in late May.
           </p>
