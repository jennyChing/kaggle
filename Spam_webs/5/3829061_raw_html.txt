        <h2 class="single-title">
         15 Deliciously Geeky Nintendo Cakes
        </h2>
        <div class="after-title">
         <script type="text/javascript">
          &lt;!--
google_ad_client = "ca-pub-2687663125856668";
/* NEWwalyouSEO336-afterpost */
google_ad_slot = "9807439098";
google_ad_width = 336;
google_ad_height = 280;
google_ad_region= "test";
//--&gt;
         </script>
         <script src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
         Every now and then, which means every day pretty much, somewhere around the world someone makes a Nintendo Inspired Cake, be it the console itself, or one of the characters (mostly Mario) from the legendary stock of well, umm  Legends.
         <span id="more-115556">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, as a new mega-awesome cake appeared on the Webwaves, with Mario, Luigi, Peach, Link, Megaman and Donkey Kong all in one delicious looking bakery, it was a good excuse to round up the best Nintendo-esque Cakes ever made.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Best Cake Ever
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/nes-cake-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-115560">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115560" height="276" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/NES-Cake1-e1320951957179.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The best one ever  The system, remote control, Mario, Luigi, Megaman, Princess Peach, Link and even Donkey Kong making an appearance.
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Classic Look
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/nintendo-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115561">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115561" height="441" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Nintendo-Cake.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Via:
          <a href="http://nsider2.com/2007/11/18/nintendo-cakefest/" target="_blank">
           Source
          </a>
         </em>
         <br/>
         No characters, no games, simply the best gaming console ever made.
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Controller Cake
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/controller-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115562">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115562" height="332" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Controller-Cake-e1320952186470.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         As expected, the greatest (and simplest) controller deserves a cake all by himself. This one actually looks rather edible.
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Mario Kart Cake
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/mario-kart/" rel="attachment wp-att-115563">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115563" height="314" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Mario-Kart-e1320952342781.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Wii Cake
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/wii-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115565">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115565" height="333" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Wii-Cake-e1320952638212.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Carrying on with a little more modern twist, the Wii, with a rather unappealing bluecolor, gets a cake as well.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Bulbasaur Cake
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/bulbasaur-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115566">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115566" height="375" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Bulbasaur-Cake-e1320952814582.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A strong contender for Nintendos mostsuccessfulfranchise,
         <em>
          Pokemon,
         </em>
         represented by my favorite Pokemon, whos been described once as
         <em>
          the Carmed Sandiego of Pokemon figures,
         </em>
         Bulbasaur.
        </p>
        <h2>
         The DS Cake
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/nintendo-ds-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115568">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115568" height="333" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Nintendo-DS-Cake-e1320953340248.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Moving on with the consoles, its due time we get the DS up here, although it does look like the least tasty cake of all those weve put here on display.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Brothers and Best Buds
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/mario-luigi-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115569">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115569" height="666" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Mario-Luigi-Cake-e1320953700584.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Stars, Clouds, question Mark Blocks and on top of it all, the best two brothers in the world, Mario &amp; Luigi.
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Hylian Shield
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/hylian-sheild-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115570">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115570" height="666" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Hylian-Sheild-Cake-e1320954118902.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, on to another franchise, and legend, with a double meaning. But you dont have to put an actual character to make the reference awesome. This Hylian Shield from The Legend of Zelda is more than enough to make this one super cool.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Zelda Cake
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/zelda-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115571">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115571" height="670" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Zelda-Cake.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Megaman Cakes
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/megaman-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115572">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115572" height="375" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Megaman-cake-e1320954432245.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The coolest thing about Megaman is that he always seems angry to me, which is always preferable to a mild mannered hero. That edge, which is portrayed in his cakes, make him the best Nintendo character IMO.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/mm-cake/" rel="attachment wp-att-115573">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115573" height="373" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/MM-Cake-e1320954646157.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/mega-man-villians/" rel="attachment wp-att-115574">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115574" height="375" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Mega-Man-Villians.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Last AND Least, Donkey Kong
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/donkey-kong/" rel="attachment wp-att-115575">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115575" height="351" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Donkey-Kong-e1320954966546.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Donkey Kong is also the first appearance of the Mario character, four years before the appearance of
         <em>
          Super Mario Bros.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://walyou.com/geeky-nintendo-cakes/donkey-kong-mario/" rel="attachment wp-att-115576">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-115576" height="375" src="http://netdna.walyou.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads//2011/11/Donkey-Kong-Mario.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
