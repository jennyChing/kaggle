      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Official_overview">
        Official overview
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       For years Toy-Volt had been producing games and toys for children all over the world. Within months of introducing their first products they shot to the top of their trade. However, it appears that the products have gained minds of their own. Escaping from the shelves that held them prisoners they have entered the real world, and are now creating chaos as they enjoy their new found freedom... the Toy-volt R/C cars have Re-Volted!
      </p>
      <p>
       Re-Volt lets you guide one of 28 remote control cars through insane race tracks set in unusual environments. Since the cars are the size of real remote control vehicles, objects found around the track are bigger than life. Curbs become huge walls, toys are obstacles, and everything is fair game to drive on, through, or over. There are 14 different tracks spread throughout four Cups, including races set in museums, supermarkets, and settings which may look like your own neighborhood.
      </p>

      <h4>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Re-Volt_1.2_Patch">
        <a class="external text" href="http://rv12.revoltzone.net/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Re-Volt 1.2 Patch
        </a>
       </span>
      </h4>
      <p>
       Re-Volt on GOG currently utilizes a modified version of Re-Volt 1.2 Beta released the 8th February 2011. In the past 2 years a lot of development has happened, though. The latest version of the 1.2 patch was released The 8th February 2014. By installing it, you gain new features such as:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        "Late Joining" (Players can join a game already started)
       </li>
       <li>
        Split Screen Multi-player Mode
       </li>
       <li>
        Up to 12 multi-player opponents and 4 spectators
       </li>
       <li>
        Player profiles
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       For a complete list of changes, consult the
       <a class="external text" href="http://rv12.revoltzone.net/downloads/rv1.2a14.0208_changelog.txt" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Re-Volt rv1.2a14.0208 Changelog
       </a>
       .
      </p>

      <h4>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Track_Editor_Update">

      </p>

       <li>
        Added Reverse Compatibility
       </li>
       <li>
        High Screen Resolution
       </li>
       <li>
        Options to change track theme
       </li>
       <li>
        Many bugfixes
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       For a complete list of changes, see the changelog provided in the Track Editor Update *.zip file.

      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Online_Playing">
        Online Playing
       </span>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="RV_House">
        <a class="external text" href="http://rvhouse.revoltzone.net/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         RV House
        </a>
       </span>
      </h4>
      <p>
       RV House is a piece of software created by Re-Volt player 'arto' for Re-Volt players to have a chat and play some online Re-Volt. Once installed all you have to do is to create an account at
       <a class="external text" href="http://revoltzone.net/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Re-Volt Zone
       </a>
       . After creating an account, you can log in, chat and play.
      </p>
      <p>
       Each player has the ability to join rooms and create rooms. The rooms act as "waiting rooms" and the player who created the room, decides when to start the Re-Volt game. Once the host player starts the game, RV House will start Re-Volt and connect you to the game.
      </p>
      <p>
       Since 2007 RV House has been the main place to go for fulfilling your everyday online racing needs. You can download RV House from the official site below:
       <a class="external free" href="http://rvhouse.revoltzone.net" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        http://rvhouse.revoltzone.net
       </a>
      </p>
      <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="background: transparent;">
       <tr>
        <td nowrap="nowrap" valign="top">
         <span style="position: relative; top: -2px;">
          <img alt="Warning" height="18" src="/images/thumb/0/0a/80px-Attention_niels_epting.svg.png/18px-80px-Attention_niels_epting.svg.png" width="18"/>
         </span>
         <b>
          Note
         </b>
         :
        </td>
        <td style="padding-left:0.5em;" valign="top">
         To play Re-Volt online, you must make sure that the following ports are open: 47624 TCP and 2300-2400 TCP &amp; UDP. For help consult your router's manual, Google, other players or even your ISP.
        </td>
       </tr>
      </table>

      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="User-made_Tracks_and_Cars">
        User-made Tracks and Cars
       </span>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Re-Volt_Zone">
        <a class="external text" href="http://revoltzone.net/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Re-Volt Zone
        </a>
       </span>
      </h4>
      <p>
       Re-Volt is over 10 years old and there has literally been uploaded 1000s of user-made tracks and cars for the game. Re-Volt Zone is the largest database of Re-Volt tracks available online. Downloading a track is free and is installed through the following procedure:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Go to a track's individual page and press the image to start the download.
       </li>
       <li>
        Once the download has finished, you should now have a *.zip file in your destination folder.
       </li>
       <li>
        Unzip the *.zip file into your Re-Volt folder (by default, C:\GOG Games\Re-Volt\). Merge the "levels" folder and the "gfx" folder with Re-Volt, when prompted.
       </li>
       <li>
        Start Re-Volt. The track/car should now be available for you to play with!
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Uploading tracks and cars requires an account. Cars and tracks are approved by a moderator before being published online.
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Known_issues">
        Known issues
       </span>
      </h2>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Windows_8">
        Windows 8
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       With the current patch GOG utilizes, you might experience bad performance. Install the latest
       <a class="external text" href="http://rv12.revoltzone.net/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Re-Volt 1.2
        <b>
         Alpha
        </b>
        Patch
       </a>
       and do the following to fix the poor performance:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Right-click on the Re-Volt shortcut
       </li>
       <li>
        Go to Properties
       </li>
       <li>
        Go to the 'Shortcut' Tab.
       </li>
       <li>
        In the 'Target' field, write -emulatefullscreen after "C:\GOG Games\Re-Volt" (or whatever destination path you are using).
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Linux_compatibility">
        Linux compatibility
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Re-Volt should work out-of-box except for Multi-Player. If you want to play Multi-player over LAN/Internet, please consult the notes found in the WINE
       <a class="external text" href="http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&amp;iId=24023" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        AppDB entry for Re-Volt 1.20
       </a>
       .
      </p>
