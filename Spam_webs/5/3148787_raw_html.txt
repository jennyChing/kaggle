        <p>
         Whats not to love about a pinata? You get to take out aggression on a dangling object and then you are rewarded with a candy shower! Its no wonder kids and adults love piantas! Pinatas are a fun and easy craft project that can be made a bazillion different ways (doilies, cupcake liners, Post It notes, cardboard, cereal boxes, foil).
