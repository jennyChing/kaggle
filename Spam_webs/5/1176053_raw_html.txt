             </h1>

          <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">

           <p>
            Are you tired of the Gameplay style of Borderlands series? A little care has come from Gearbox and 2K Games side,
            <strong>
             the creators of the award-winning franchise Borderlands today announced Battleborn. A new Tripple AAA Shooter game which is scheduled for 2016, the game will be landed for PS4, Xbox One and PC.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://tech4gamers.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Battleborn_2014_07-08-14_002.jpg">
             <img alt="2K Games and GearBox Announce Battleborn" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3454" height="348" src="http://tech4gamers.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Battleborn_2014_07-08-14_002-1024x576.jpg" width="620"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://tech4gamers.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Battleborn_2014_07-08-14_001.jpg">
             <img alt="Battleborn_2014_07-08-14_001" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3456" height="348" src="http://tech4gamers.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Battleborn_2014_07-08-14_001-1024x576.jpg" width="620"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
           <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
            </script>
            <!-- Box -->
            <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-6108735258392185" data-ad-slot="8911832158" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
            </ins>
            <script>
             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
           </div>
           <p>
            The
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="2b2da83e-04d5-4f43-ab6a-aaaddfb867f1">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="a003e8bb-9564-48c5-8c0f-0ff1d64fbc94">
              Battleborn
             </span>
            </span>
            is developed by the acclaimed team of Borderlands 2, which is a game that blends multiple genres ambitiously. The game combines a highly stylized graphics and frantic first-person shooting, with the classic battle of Gearbox co-op and a vast collection of new heroes.
            <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="727b6dc5-1fd0-4dc5-accb-44f0b8f6fd86">
             <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="f10ee2de-66e5-4c31-a91b-418f135d89d8">
              Battleborn
             </span>
            </span>
            is set in a distant future universe hued sci-fi, where Players will experience a narrative experience in a campaign Co-op or competitive multiplayer match.
            <strong>
             Randy Pitchford, president of Gearbox Software said that A fusion of genres, competitive co-op with a crazy style and an imaginative universe, Battleborn is the most ambitious game that Gearbox has ever created.
            </strong>
           </p>
