          <p class="post-content post-content-justify" itemprop="articleBody">
           Hey!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Here`s the outfit that I wore for a casual Sunday afternoon. We were happy enough to feel the last touches of summery warmth in September. Due to this fact I decided no longer waste any time lying around in my apartment and go out to explore the new area I`m living in. I chose a simple black dress from Zara, put a black belt around my waist to make some sort of shape of a straight dress. Afterwards I put on my favorite denim jacket to keep the whole outfit casual and finally paired it with my navy blue H&amp;M handbag. There`s no jewellery  included in this look. At the time I had my hair curled, so it seamed to be already enough of everything. That`s all for this time. Hope you enjoyed.
