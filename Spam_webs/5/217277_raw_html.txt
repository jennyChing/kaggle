            <em>
             Top Chef
            </em>
            Season 12

          <b>
           Katie Weinner
          </b>
          found herself with the least favorite dish. To fight to stay in the competition, she had to take on one of the eliminated chefs from this season in a cookoff! The eliminated chefs voted and they decided
          <strong>
           George Pagonis
          </strong>
          deserved another chance, seeming he went home on the first Sudden Death challenge this season and never really got a chance to cook. They cooked a rabbit dish and George won, so he got a spot back in the kitchen and Katie was eliminated!
