          <p style="border:thick double pink; padding:3mm;">
           Welcome to MassholeMommy.com. You should subscribe to my
           <a href="http://massholemommy.com/feed/">
            RSS feed
           </a>
           and follow my
           <a href="http://twitter.com/massholemommy" rel="nofollow">
            Twitter
           </a>
           . You can also have new entries
           <a href="http://feedburner.google.com/fb/a/mailverify?uri=massholemommy/uTYa" rel="nofollow">
            delivered directly to your inbox

          </p>

         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Hi Masshole Mommy readers!
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Chris here again with another guest post.
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Its perfect timing for the holiday season and I wanted to talk about the importance of giving the wife/girlfriend the perfect Christmas gift.
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Your significant other has probably been hinting probably for at least the last six months about what they want.But ladies, guys are thick-skulled, so they dont pay all that much attention and dont listen really well.So, if you didnt cut out a picture, put it in an envelope with the exact change, safety pin it to his shirt, drive him to the store and gave him the salespersons name to speak to  then YOU ARE NOT getting the gift you want.
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Now, fellas there are some dos and donts for gift giving and I am speaking from experience..
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             1. Dont give Love coupons for back massages or foot rubs.
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             2. Dont give a Tiffanys Box with something other than Tiffanys inside of it.
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             3. Dont give something that is for the household (Blender, mop, or new car headlight).
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Do buy tickets for shows (hint: pick a show that you will most likely hate the most and get those because she will love them).
             <span id="more-21261">
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             My personal favorite and it involves the least amount of work on my part is to say Honey for my Christmas present to you, I will not look at the credit card bill for one month,
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Buying clothes is also a
             <strong>
              big
             </strong>
             no no.You dont want to be getting the wrong size!!
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             One big gift for each other is a great win/win because she will buy it and she will like it (ie: a new TV)
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
             Last, but not least, you can never go wrong with jewelry. And
             <strong>
              do not
             </strong>
             wrap the jewelry box in 5 rolls of wrapping paper with two rolls of tape, because it will not be funny to her (especially in my case because would lose on my birthday  which falls only three weeks after Christmas -because she will remember what a pain in the butt that present was and then you are stuck on your birthday with nothing but a headache).
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Just remember, you could end up in the doghouse like this guy:
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>

          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/eyduncFpzl4" width="420">
          </iframe>
          <center>
          </center>
         </center>
         <center>
         </center>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
            Until next time,
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
