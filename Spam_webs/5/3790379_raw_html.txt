      <p>
       As "
       <b>
        True Detective
       </b>
       " season two comes together, the rumors have been flying back and forth.
       <a class="" href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/colin-farrell-puts-on-bade-for-true-detective-season-2-taylor-kitsch-in-contention-for-role-20140711" target="_blank" title="Link: http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/colin-farrell-puts-on-bade-for-true-detective-season-2-taylor-kitsch-in-contention-for-role-20140711">
        Last month
       </a>
       we learned that
       <b>
        Colin Farrell
       </b>
       was in talks for one of the lead roles, with
       <b>
        Taylor Kitsch
       </b>
       and
       <b>
        Garrett Hedlund
       </b>
       up for key parts. But today, coming completely out of left field, comes news that would've left Rust Cohle blindsided.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="" href="http://tvline.com/2014/08/04/vince-vaughn-true-detective-season-2-cast-hbo/" target="_blank">
        TVLine
       </a>
       reports that
       <b>
        Vince Vaughn
       </b>
       yes, the funnyman from "
       <b>
        Wedding Crashers
       </b>
       ," "
       <b>
        Swingers
       </b>
       " and "
       <b>
        Old School
       </b>
       " is in preliminary talks for a role in
       <b>
        HBO
       </b>
       's neo-noir drama. Now, before you say WTF and LOLWUT, you should note that Vaughn's recent movements behind the scenes makes this news not entirely surprising. While "
       <b>
        Business Or Pleasure
       </b>
       " the kind of comedy you expect from Vaughn is on the way, Vaughn has also wrapped on the crime drama "
       <b>
        Term Life
       </b>
       " co-starring
       <b>
        Hailee Steinfeld
       </b>
       . And at one point a year ago, he had been lining up several non-comedic projects including
       <a href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/vince-vaughn-wants-to-be-the-risk-agent-20120725" title="Link: http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/vince-vaughn-wants-to-be-the-risk-agent-20120725">
        the thriller
       </a>
       "
       <b>
        The Risk Agent
       </b>
       ," the
       <a href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/vince-vaughn-lines-up-action-adventure-flick-gunslingers-penned-by-braveheart-scribe-randall-wallace-20120716" title="Link: http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/vince-vaughn-lines-up-action-adventure-flick-gunslingers-penned-by-braveheart-scribe-randall-wallace-20120716">
        <b>
         Randall Wallace
        </b>
        -penned
       </a>
       "
       <b>
        Gunslingers
       </b>
       " and
       <a href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/vince-vaughn-is-working-triple-time-20121031" target="_blank" title="Link: http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/vince-vaughn-is-working-triple-time-20121031">
        road adventure
       </a>
       "
       <b>
        Triple Time
       </b>
       ."And let's not forget the actor has "
       <b>
        Psycho
       </b>
       " and "
       <b>
        The Cel
       </b>
       l" to his name.
      </p>
      <p>
       In short, Vaughn has got dramatic chops, but he's rarely given the chance to show them off. He's clearly been eager to move away from the kind of comedies he can knock out in his sleep, and "True Detective" could give him a helluva chance to reinvent his onscreen persona.
      </p>
      <p>
       That said, this is still pretty unexpected. No word yet on his role, but it will be one of the three lead parts (there are four central characters in the next season). And you can read all about the
       <a class="" href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/rumor-chinatown-esque-plot-details-for-true-detective-season-2-emerge-20140717" target="_blank">
        rumored "
       </a>
       <b>
        <a class="" href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/rumor-chinatown-esque-plot-details-for-true-detective-season-2-emerge-20140717" target="_blank">
         Chinatown
        </a>
       </b>
       <a class="" href="http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/rumor-chinatown-esque-plot-details-for-true-detective-season-2-emerge-20140717" target="_blank">
        "-esque plot details right here
       </a>
       .
