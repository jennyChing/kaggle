     <h1>
      Infographic: Top 10 Famous White Collar Crimes
     </h1>
     <br/>
     <p>
      Sure white-collar criminals wear sharp suits and drive fancy cars but dont be fooled  their crimes hurt society like any other shadowy and illegal conduct.  Here are the top ten white-collar crimes that affected thousands, ruined them financially and stole their piece of mind.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Each one of these individuals made a decision to steal, commit fraud or cheat in order to gain at the expense of others. With an executive MS degree in Financial Crime and Compliance Management, YOU could help prevent these situations from happening!
      <br/>
      <br/>
     </p>
