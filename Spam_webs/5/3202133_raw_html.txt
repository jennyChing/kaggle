      <p>
       Denna is a cute puppy that was adopted by a caring and loving family several years ago. At that time, she had perfect eyesight.
      </p>
      <p>
       She built strong relationships with her human family and never left her young masters side. They would spend countless hours playing, exploring, snuggling
      </p>
      <p>
       But her family noticed that her behavior was changing over time. She seemed less adventurous. And the color of her eyes started to fade.
      </p>
      <p>
       After a visit to the vet, they learned that Denna was losing her eyesight. It got gradually worse until she became blind.
      </p>
      <p>
       Denna is now a blind dog, but she is still part of a great family and they are showing her with love. Maybe even more than before. She is awesome Even if she cant see.
      </p>
