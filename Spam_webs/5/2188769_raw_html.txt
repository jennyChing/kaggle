        <p>
         Where would I be without my mommys love? Shes a superwoman, an endless source of inspiration and lessons.
        </p>
        <p>
         A few weeks ago, we asked our community: What is something that your mom taught you? On
         <a href="http://instagram.com/etsy" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         ,

          Facebook
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://twitter.com/etsy" target="_blank">
          Twitter
         </a>
         , you all shared words of wisdom that only special moms could give.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are a few of your responses:
        </p>

         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/stephscozycreations">
          Stephscozycreations

Sometimes she drives me crazy, all mothers do, but she is my best friend and I am so glad and lucky that she is my mom.
        </p>

- By age 40 she had undergone merchant marine training as a nurse, and she proceeded to travel the world for the next 10 years, facing difficult conditions and incredible storms at sea!
- She rode her bicycle everywhere and kept in excellent physical shape.
- What I am proudest about My Mom is that, now over age 70, she has been a pioneer for over 10 years, teaching people living in far away areas of Brazil about God's purpose for mankind, the hope of resurrection, and Bible principles that help them become better people and live happier lives.
Even though we live far apart, every day is My Mother's Day for me.
