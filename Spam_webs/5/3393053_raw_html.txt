     <h1 class="headline" itemprop="name">
      <a href="http://www.siegfriedandjensen.com/3-keys-infographic/" rel="bookmark">
       Ever Wondered If You Have a Valid Injury Claim?
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="post_content" itemprop="articleBody">
      <p>
       No one wants to get injured and none of our clients leave our office looking forward to working with us again in the future.
      </p>
      <p>
       Getting injured can change your life in an instant.
      </p>
      <p>
       It can be expensive, leave you handicapped and diminish your earning capacity potentially costing you hundreds of thousands of dollars in lower wages over the course of your lifetime.
      </p>
      <p>
       We have created this infographic to help people know if their injury likely qualifies as a valid personal injury claim.
      </p>
      <p>
       Give us a call, either way, and well review the facts with you and let you know right over the phone if you have a valid claim or not.
      </p>

       </a>
      </p>
