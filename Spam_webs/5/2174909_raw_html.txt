                       <p>
                        Just as we become reflections of our environment, our setting too can become a reflection of our perception. By looking into the studio of an artist we can gain a slight glimpse into the inner workings of their mind. Their studios range from expansive lofts, to rooms that appear barely large enough to fit a canvas in.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        In each studio you get a sense of creative chaos, artistic order, or anything in between. These are the places the mind roams free. These are the places where art is born.
