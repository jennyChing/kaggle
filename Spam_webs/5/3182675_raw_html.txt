       <p>
        As we all know, the month of December can be a bit tough on the ol' waistline. Within just a few holiday parties myself and I am already considering sweat pants for Christmas dinner. No, seriously, I'm going to be dressed like Jane Lynch from Glee if I keep this up! To prevent that from happening, this week's meal plan is 100% vegan.
       </p>
       <p>

         Tuesday:
        </strong>
        <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/tempeh-broccoli-and-red-bell-pepper-stir-fry.html">
         Tempeh and Broccoli Stir Fry
        </a>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Wednesday:
        </strong>
        <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/weekday-vegetarian-getting-your-legumes-with-this-cannellini-and-kale-ragout.html">
         White Bean and Kale Ragout
        </a>
        <br/>
        <strong>

        <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/easy-vegetarian-recipes/cauliflower-and-sweet-onion-soup.html">
         Cauliflower and Sweet Onion Soup
        </a>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Friday:
        </strong>
        <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/chickpea-flour-crepes-with-leeks.html">
         Chickpea Flour Crepes With Leeks
        </a>

        <strong>
         Saturday:

        <strong>
         Sunday Brunch:
        </strong>
        <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/easy-vegetarian-recipes/banana-and-coconut-french-toast-vegan.html">
         Banana and Coconut French Toast
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Your Shopping List
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img src="http://media.treehugger.com/assets/images/2011/10/tempeh-stirfry-10.jpg" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; display: block;"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/easy-vegetarian-recipes/curry-and-maple-sweet-potato-soup-vegan.html">
          Curry and Maple Sweet Potato Soup
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         4 sweet potatoes
        </li>
        <li>
         1 small sweet onion
        </li>
        <li>
         3 cloves of garlic, minced
        </li>
        <li>
         6 cups of light, salt-free vegetable broth
        </li>
        <li>
         2 1/2 tablespoons mild curry
        </li>
        <li>
         2 tablespoons maple syrup
        </li>
        <li>
         Salt to taste
        </li>
        <li>
         Cilantro, nutmeg and clove for garnish
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/tempeh-broccoli-and-red-bell-pepper-stir-fry.html">
          Tempeh and Broccoli Stir Fry
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         4 6 oz. pieces of tempeh
        </li>
        <li>
         4 cups vegetable stock
        </li>
        <li>
         1 cup red wine
        </li>
        <li>
         1/2 cup soy sauce
        </li>
        <li>
         4 tablespoon brown sugar
        </li>
        <li>
         1" piece of ginger cut into slices
        </li>
        <li>
         6 whole star anise
        </li>
        <li>
         2 teaspoon smoked paprika
        </li>
        <li>
         1 teaspoon chile flakes
        </li>
        <li>
         1/3 cup of your favorite stir fry sauce, or
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/tempeh-broccoli-and-red-bell-pepper-stir-fry.html">
          see our recipe
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         Small handful of broccoli, cut into 1 1/2" long spears.
        </li>
        <li>
         Small handful of bell peppers, cut into thick julienne
        </li>
        <li>
         1/4 cup green onions, cut into 1" pieces
        </li>
        <li>
         2 tablespoons of cooking oil
        </li>
        <li>
         Rice
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/weekday-vegetarian-getting-your-legumes-with-this-cannellini-and-kale-ragout.html">
          White Bean and Kale Ragout
         </a>
        </strong>
        (Modified)
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
        </li>
        <li>
         4 1 1/2-inch-thick slices Italian bread, crusts removed, each slice quartered
        </li>
        <li>
         1 teaspoon plus 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
        </li>
        <li>
         1 small onion, diced
        </li>
        <li>
         3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
        </li>
        <li>
         1/4 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper
        </li>
        <li>
         5 cups (packed) thinly sliced kale (about 1 large bunch)
        </li>
        <li>
         1 14 1/2-ounce vegetable broth
        </li>
        <li>
         1 14 1/2-ounce can diced tomatoes
        </li>
        <li>
         1 15-ounce can cannellini (white kidney beans), drained
        </li>
        <li>
         Salt and pepper to taste
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/easy-vegetarian-recipes/cauliflower-and-sweet-onion-soup.html">
          Cauliflower and Sweet Onion Soup
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         1 medium sweet onion
        </li>
        <li>
         1 head very fresh cauliflower
        </li>
        <li>
         5 cups water
        </li>
        <li>
         Salt to taste
        </li>
        <li>
         Extra virgin olive oil
        </li>
        <li>
         Ground black pepper
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/green-food/chickpea-flour-crepes-with-leeks.html">
          Chickpea Flour Crepes With Leeks
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         1 cup chickpea flour
        </li>
        <li>
         1 cup water
        </li>
        <li>
         1 tbsp sesame oil
        </li>
        <li>
          tsp chili powder
        </li>
        <li>
         1 tsp cumin seeds
        </li>
        <li>
         1 tsp fennel seeds
        </li>
        <li>
         1 tsp sea salt
        </li>
        <li>
          tsp turmeric
        </li>
        1/2 leek, thinly sliced
        <li>
         2 tbsp cilantro, coarsely chopped
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/easy-vegetarian-recipes/butternut-squash-gnocchi.html">
          Butternut Squash Gnocchi
         </a>
        </strong>
        (Modified)
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         1 large butternut squash
        </li>
        <li>
         2 cups of flour, more as needed
        </li>
        <li>
         1 teaspoon of nutmeg
        </li>
        <li>
         1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.treehugger.com/easy-vegetarian-recipes/banana-and-coconut-french-toast-vegan.html">
          Banana and Coconut French Toast
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         2 ripe bananas
        </li>
        <li>
         1 cup coconut milk
        </li>
        <li>
         1 tablespoon vanilla
        </li>
        <li>
         1 teaspoon cardamom
        </li>
        <li>
         1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
        </li>
        <li>
         1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
        </li>
        <li>
         1 tablespoon flour
        </li>
        <li>
         1/2 teaspoon sea salt
        </li>
        <li>
         1 loaf bread
        </li>
        <li>
         Coconut oil (optional)
        </li>
       </ul>
