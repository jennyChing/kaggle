       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Bacon Wrapped Water Chestnuts Appetizer Recipe
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-07-25T13:00:46+00:00">
         July 25, 2012
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://sweeptight.com/author/april" rel="author">
           April
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Bacon wrapped water chestnuts make a quick and easy appetizer idea. I love appetizers, I would eat them in place of a meal any day of the week! My mouth waters at the thought of the flavor combinations that appetizers offer, sweet, spicy, salty, and savory YUMMO!
        </p>
        <h3>
         Simple and delicious appetizer recipe, a bacon lovers dream, 3 ingredientsis all you need!
        </h3>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         Bacon Wrapped Water Chestnuts
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://sweeptight.com/2012/07/bacon-wrapped-water-chestnuts-appetizer-recipe.html/bacon-wrap-apps" rel="attachment wp-att-31459">
          <img alt="Bacon wrapped appetizers" class="aligncenter wp-image-31459" height="186" src="http://sweeptight.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bacon-Wrap-Apps-600x422.jpg" width="265"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         In Hawaii we callappetizerspupus (I know, doesnt sound appetizing to mainlanders, lol) give me a pupuplatter and Im in heaven! Im also a huge fan of bacon (everything tastes better wrapped in bacon!), thats one of the reasons I love this recipe so much, besides the fact that it only take 3 ingredients, is so very simple to create, and leaves your guestcravingmore.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         No joke, every time I serve my bacon wrapped water chestnuts, they are the first thing to disappear and every time I ask what I can bring to a get together or a party, the request is always the same.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         So Idecided to pass this well loved, super simple recipe on to all of you!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://sweeptight.com/2012/07/bacon-wrapped-water-chestnuts-appetizer-recipe.html/appetizer-how-to" rel="attachment wp-att-31458">
          <img alt="Appetizer ingredients" class="aligncenter wp-image-31458" height="283" src="http://sweeptight.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/appetizer-how-to-600x404.jpg" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           What you will need:
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          1 lb. of bacon (reg or thick, pork or turkey)
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          1-8 oz can  whole water chestnuts
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          brown sugar for sprinkling on top!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          toothpicks
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          pre-heat over to 350
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          makes approx 30-32 pieces
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://sweeptight.com/2012/07/bacon-wrapped-water-chestnuts-appetizer-recipe.html/appetizer-prep" rel="attachment wp-att-31463">
          <img alt="appetizer prep" class="aligncenter wp-image-31463" height="232" src="http://sweeptight.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/appetizer-prep-600x331.jpg" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         First you need to cut the chestnuts into smaller pieces, in 1/2 for smaller and 1/3 or 1/4 for larger. Cut bacon strips into 1/3 size pieces just big enough to wrap completely around the water chestnut and overlap a little. (When using thinner bacon you can wrap it around twice)
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://sweeptight.com/2012/07/bacon-wrapped-water-chestnuts-appetizer-recipe.html/wrap-the-chesnuts" rel="attachment wp-att-31464">
          <img alt="bacon app. prep" class="aligncenter wp-image-31464" height="273" src="http://sweeptight.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/wrap-the-chesnuts-600x390.jpg" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Line a pan/cookie sheetwith parchment paper, roll each piece of water chestnut in bacon and secure with a round, sharp toothpick. Sprinkleeach with brown sugar. (can be prepared ahead of time if needed)
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://sweeptight.com/2012/07/bacon-wrapped-water-chestnuts-appetizer-recipe.html/bacon-wrapped-water-chesnuts" rel="attachment wp-att-31479">
          <img alt="Bacon wrapped water chesnuts" class="aligncenter wp-image-31479" height="279" src="http://sweeptight.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Bacon-wrapped-water-chesnuts-600x399.jpg" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Place pan ofprepared appetizers into oven and bake at 350 for approx 25-30 mins, or until bacon is done to your liking.When done, remove and let cool a bit, serve warm.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
