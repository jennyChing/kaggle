        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Quick Tips: Receptionists
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.halloo.com/Blog/quick-tips-receptionists/" rel="bookmark" title="5:49 pm">

           February 24, 2011
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.halloo.com/Blog/author/kimm/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Kimm Antell">
            Kimm Antell
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div style="margin:0">
         <b>
          Share it now!
         </b>
        </div>
        <div class="loginRadiusHorizontalSharing" data-share-url="http://www.halloo.com/Blog/quick-tips-receptionists/">
        </div>
        <br/>
        <p>
         Here are five tips you should give to your receptionist.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Slow down.
          </strong>
          If someone asks you more than once what you just said, then you are speaking to fast.
         </li>
         <p>
          <img alt="Receptionist Tips" src="http://www.halloo.com/Blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/doll.jpg" style="float: right; width: 150px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 15px;" title="Office Receptionist"/>
         </p>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Enunciate.
          </strong>
          Because the person on the phone cannot see your lips, certain nuances in enunciation are lost.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Ask for a Number.
          </strong>
          If you dont know the answer, ask for the callers name and number and tell them that you find the answer and call them back.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Dont Be in a Rush.
          </strong>
          Always make the caller feel like you have a lot of time for them even if it means forwarding a call to a
          <a href="http://www.halloo.com/Virtual-Receptionist" title="Cheap Virtual Receptionist">
           virtual receptionist
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Smile While You Talk.
          </strong>
          As crazy as this sounds, the caller can hear it when you have a smile on your face even if you arent in a good mood.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         These five tips will transform your receptionist from a good to great.
        </p>
