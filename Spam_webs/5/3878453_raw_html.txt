                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     All Hands on Deck: Preparing for Baby #3!
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7036076470816670132">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       And the award for most neglected blog goes to...Confessions of the Chromosomally Enhanced!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Again.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Ugh!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       We are so sorry. It's not that we don't love the blog - we love it with ever fiber of our being. It's just that third trimester fatigue, coupled with two energetic toddlers, on top of two weeks of solo parenting thanks to a husband's hectic business travel schedule, etc. etc. etc...means that any remaining "free time" (oh how this concept is lost on me), is usually spent in peaceful slumber. That is, if "peaceful slumber" is definedas tossing and turning and getting up to pee every 10 minutes.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                       Please accept this extra long blog post with tons of photos as a token of our sincere apology.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                       Guess who graced us with a sensational visit last week: That's right - Mama Hop and Aunt Leanne!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-bFUh7mwNZvc/U0vIlSO3FeI/AAAAAAAAHAw/zFUj-pme4IM/s1600/Mama+Hop+%2526+Aunt+Leanne+5-13-14.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="512" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-bFUh7mwNZvc/U0vIlSO3FeI/AAAAAAAAHAw/zFUj-pme4IM/s1600/Mama+Hop+%2526+Aunt+Leanne+5-13-14.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Aren't they the cutest?! And I think we can see where Merryn gets her dimples!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Oh what fun we had! The Divas were reunited and as always, it was apparent that neither time nor distance could impact their treasured bond.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-JieHeoSh4p8/U0vItyZqOEI/AAAAAAAAHBw/jBVq1x2ywcg/s1600/IMG_2483.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="496" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-JieHeoSh4p8/U0vItyZqOEI/AAAAAAAAHBw/jBVq1x2ywcg/s1600/IMG_2483.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       And we all know that grandmas are at their happiest when they've got armloads full of grand kids to love and spoil.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-SNMT3FwgL7A/U0vIs-JJVQI/AAAAAAAAHBo/kRBvsvIVpMQ/s1600/IMG_2388.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">

                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-FHIw6YUljJY/U0vIr5lE_LI/AAAAAAAAHBg/1X2hH_6i4BY/s1600/IMG_2357.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       We also got right down to business nesting in preparation for the arrival of Baby #3 next month! I asked Travis to bring up the bin full of infant stuff. He emerged with FIVE bins full of infant stuff. Hello, my name is Elizabeth and I am addicted to dressing up my little girls like doll babies. This is my daughter, Merryn. I anticipate she will need a similar 12-step program someday. While Mama Hop was benevolently sorting and hanging infant clothes, Merryn stumbled upon some preemie diapers and quickly swiped them for her dolls. Dolls, whom I might add, are subjected to hourly wardrobe changes. Yes, it runs in the family.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                       But in all seriousness, I am SO grateful to Mama Hop for tackling this project for me. Most days I am completely overwhelmed by the standard demands of caring for two toddlers and a household while fighting through pregnancy fatigue. Finding the extra time and energy to do this with rambunctious tots underfoot was beginning to look like a pipe dream. Thank goodnessfor grandmaswho step in and save the day! Now Baby Number 3 will be adorned in outfits properly hung in a closet; not haphazardly fished from a Rubbermaid bin. Thank you, Mom!!!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>

                       <a href="http://www.confessionsofthechromosomallyenhanced.com/2014/03/aunt-leanne-and-fine-art-of-being-diva.html" target="_blank">
                        personal transportationbutler
                       </a>
                       comes along, she's going to have to keep practicing getting into and out of the car independently.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-BrwamKrMpmg/U0vIpuDE6XI/AAAAAAAAHBQ/7XvTHm97zPA/s1600/IMG_2258.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       And as always,
                       <a href="http://www.confessionsofthechromosomallyenhanced.com/2014/02/little-mother-hen-continued.html" target="_blank">
                        Mother Hen
                       </a>
                       was right there making her baby doll practice climbing into the car seat.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-LCZ_V5kmqaI/U0vInmqnDFI/AAAAAAAAHBA/qXOKLP5aEaA/s1600/IMG_2248.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-P68PjZtcbFo/U0vIou4vEbI/AAAAAAAAHBI/g6uaV5PdPvs/s1600/IMG_2255.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       At one point I asked Aunt Leanne to keep an eye on Merryn. Little did I know what an exhausting assignment that would be. Aunt Leanne is about as athletically inclined as Miss Josie is, but she did her absolute best to trail our littlehurricane and protect her from harm.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rJTbpuJa8mk/U0r5ZxD928I/AAAAAAAAHAQ/uizCx9qhL_c/s1600/Mar+1-13.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                        <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rJTbpuJa8mk/U0r5ZxD928I/AAAAAAAAHAQ/uizCx9qhL_c/s1600/Mar+1-13.jpg" width="426"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       It's a good thing Mama Hop was back at the condo preparing a hearty dinner for us after all that fresh air and exercise! Look at her! Doesn't she just need her own cooking show?! Call us Food Network! We promise delicious food and a hefty helping of humor!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ctjqqxOdpE0/U0vIqoA3BXI/AAAAAAAAHBY/JFzwc__WFpw/s1600/IMG_2316.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="512" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ctjqqxOdpE0/U0vIqoA3BXI/AAAAAAAAHBY/JFzwc__WFpw/s1600/IMG_2316.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Unfortunately, all good things must come to an end and so we were forced to say goodbye to Mama Hop and Aunt Leanne. And as sad as it was to see them go, we are comforted by the fact that we'll see them again soon...
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-9N9sB4eZXgQ/U0vIvBGp3BI/AAAAAAAAHB4/v6j-w8RTNDs/s1600/IMG_2517.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="510" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-9N9sB4eZXgQ/U0vIvBGp3BI/AAAAAAAAHB4/v6j-w8RTNDs/s1600/IMG_2517.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       ...very soon! Next month, in fact! And just in time for the World's Best Aunt to welcome one more niece into her loving arms. What a lucky little girl to have thesecharming chicaseagerly awaiting her arrival!
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-iI39ptj59uU/U0vIy4dOQxI/AAAAAAAAHCQ/GCK8VPNrW8s/s1600/Leanne+%2526+Merryn+4-13-14.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="512" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-iI39ptj59uU/U0vIy4dOQxI/AAAAAAAAHCQ/GCK8VPNrW8s/s1600/Leanne+%2526+Merryn+4-13-14.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>

                     <div align="center">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Let's hope little lady #3 enjoys a good hug fest!
                      </span>
                      
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ydIWSsnWCkE/U0vIxU2sjqI/AAAAAAAAHCI/ZJ-sLlLXOdM/s1600/IMG_2504.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="512" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ydIWSsnWCkE/U0vIxU2sjqI/AAAAAAAAHCI/ZJ-sLlLXOdM/s1600/IMG_2504.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <span style='font-family: "Trebuchet MS", sans-serif;'>
                       Aunt Leanne said the sweetest thing to me. She was telling me all about her birthday plans (yes, it is completely typical for Leanne to start planning her birthdayfestivities months and months in advance) and she said the best gift she will receive this year is my baby.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                       As I reached to wipe the hormotional tears from my eyes, she amended it with "...and Tic Tacs and Starbucks cards. I want six Starbucks cards."
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div align="center">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS;">
                       And that, my friends, is the innocent candor that we've all come to expect from the one and only Aunt Leanne! Oh Leanne! Here's hoping while I'm wandering around in a post-partumsleep-deprived haze, you're toasting the good life with your favorite Frappuccino!
