             <h1>
              Venice: The best grocery stores, supermarkets, delis and bakeries
             </h1>
             <div class="row row-post-title-widget">
              <div class="col-s-12">
               <div class="b_module b_module_o widget_text" id="text-3">
                <div class="textwidget">
                 <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing">
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="row">
            <div class="col-s-12">
             <div class="meta">
              <div class="row">
               <div class="col-s-5">
                <p class="post_authoring">
                 <span class="post_author">
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/author/monica-cesarato" rel="author">
                   Monica Cesarato
                  </a>
                 </span>
                 <span class="post_stamp">
                  November 25, 2010
                 </span>
                </p>
               </div>
               <div class="col-s-5">
                <div class="b_post_button b_post_button_category">
                 <p>
                  <span>
                   Posted in:
                   <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/category/venice/venice-eating-and-drinking" rel="category" title="View all posts in Venice Eating and Drinking">
                    Venice Eating and Drinking
                   </a>
                  </span>

           <div class="row">
            <div class="col-s-12">
             <div class="entry singleentry">
              <div class="row">
               <div class="col-m-12">
                <div class="b_module_post_thumb_holder">
                 <div class="b_module_post_thumb">
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/venice-the-best-grocery-stores-supermarkets-delis-and-bakeries.html">
                   <img alt="Venice Billa supermarket" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="250" src="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/450x250xbilla.jpg.pagespeed.ic.OpxPUr6EsG.jpg" width="450"/>
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <div class="b_module_caption">
                  A Billa supermarket in Venice's Cannaregio neighborhood. Photo: Tom Meyers
                 </div>
                </div>
                <p>
                 Have you ever wandered around
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice">
                   Venice
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 after realizing that, say, youve run out of toothpaste, shampoo or body lotion? It can actually be a bit tricky to locate a supermarket amidst the winding alleys of the old city.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Well, even if you havent run out of toothpaste, Im pretty sure that its helpful to make a list of supermarkets located in all the main tourist areas of Venice to use as a reference. These major grocery stores provide not only the basics, but also a wide range of typical Italian products (like pasta, olive oil and cakes) that can make an excellent cheapo souvenir!
                </p>
                <blockquote>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   Related:
                  </strong>
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/venice-budget-hotels-with-unique-features.html" title="Venice hotels with perks">
                   <strong>
                    Affordable Venice hotels with special perks
                   </strong>
                  </a>
                 </p>
                </blockquote>
                <h2>
                 Supermarkets
                </h2>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Coop
                  <br/>
                  <a href="http://www.e-coop.it/" target="_blank" title="Coop">
                   website
                  </a>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_15503" style="width: 260px">
                 <img alt="Water for sale in Venice" class="size-full wp-image-15503 " height="250" src="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/250x250xwater.jpg.pagespeed.ic.gB4o0Cju2w.jpg" title="Water Venice" width="250"/>
                 <p class="wp-caption-text">
                  Save when you buy water at the supermarket.
                 </p>
                </div>
                <p>
                 The COOP is a very large Italian grocery chain that seems to emphasize quality over profits. I find their items to be reliably good. Several locations can be found in central Venice, among them these four:
                </p>
                <p>
                 Fondamenta Santa Chiara (Near Piazzale Roma)
                </p>
                <p>
                 Sestiere
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice/hoods/santa-croce-venice.html">
                   Santa Croce
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 , 1493 (telephone: 041 275 0218)
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice/hoods/cannaregio.html">
                   Cannaregio
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 , 1976 (telephone: 041 720 374)
                </p>
                <p>
                 Sestiere Giudecca, 484 (telephone: 041 241 3381)
                </p>
                <p>
                 There are, of course, other supermarkets in Venice. These include:
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Billa A.G.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 1491,
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice/hoods/dorsoduro.html">
                   Dorsoduro
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 041 241 8820
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Supermercato Punto Sma City
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 Sestiere Castello, 5065
                </p>
                <blockquote>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   Related:
                   <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/venice-5-sure-ways-to-save-in-venice.html" title="5 ways to save in Venice">
                    5 simple way to save in Venice
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                </blockquote>
                <h2>
                 Food shops
                </h2>
                <p>
                 Along with a wide range of supermarkets, there are also several food shops in Venice that are worth checking out, even if you just like to window shop. These include classic old Venetian delicatessens and bakeries. Here you will be able to enjoy some of the best food Venice can offer.
                </p>
                <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_38349" style="width: 510px">
                 <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/Gastronomia-Venice.jpg">
                  <img alt="Gastronomia Venice" class="size-full wp-image-38349" height="375" src="http://www.eurocheapo.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/500x375xGastronomia-Venice.jpg.pagespeed.ic.eKw3hPbFKP.jpg" width="500"/>
                 </a>
                 <p class="wp-caption-text">
                  Gastronomia Aliani is a food lovers delight, packed with cheese, meat and other delicacies. Photo:
                  <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/spunkysmama/403617009/">
                   spunkymama
                  </a>
                 </p>
                </div>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                  Gastronomia Aliani
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 San Polo 654
                 <br/>
                 Ruga Rialto/Ruga Vecchia San Giovanni
                 <br/>
                 Vaporetto: Rialto Mercato or San Silvestro
                </p>
                <p>
                 This is my favorite food shop of them all in Venice. Its a traditional delicatessen which stocks a very large selection of cold meats and cheeses from every part of Italy and a very large range of prepared dishes and roasted meats (try the
                 <em>
                  baccala mantecato
                 </em>
                 ). It is run by Davide Aliani and his lovely mother.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Casa del Parmigiano
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <a href="http://www.aliani-casadelparmigiano.it/" target="_blank" title="Casa del Parmigiano">
                  <strong>
                   website
                  </strong>
                 </a>
                 <br/>
                 San Polo 214/215
                 <br/>
                 Erberia Rialto
                </p>
                <p>
                 This cheese and meat shop has been selling a wide variety of delicious products since 1936! With a location just next to
                 <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice/hotels-near/rialto-bridge.html" title="Rialto Bridge">
                  <strong>
                   Rialto Bridge
                  </strong>
                 </a>
                 , its a must-visit in a central location.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  I Tre Mercanti srl
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <a href="http://www.itremercanti.it/" target="_blank">
                  <strong>
                   website
                  </strong>
                 </a>
                 <br/>
                 Campo della Guerra
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice/hoods/castello.html">
                   Castello
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 , 5364
                 <br/>
                 041 522 2901
                </p>
                <p>
                 Gourmet shop with fine Italian ingredients to take home or create a sandwich with. They also have a nice wine selection.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Panifici Majer
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <em>
                  Three locations:
                 </em>
                 <br/>
                 San Polo 2307, near Rialto
                 <br/>
                 Santa Croce 1630
                 <br/>
                 Dorsoduro 3108/D (near
                 <a href="http://www.eurocheapo.com/venice/hotels-near/campo-santa-margherita.html" title="Campo Santa Margherita">
                  <strong>
                   Campo Santa Margherita
                  </strong>
                 </a>
                 )
                </p>
                <p>
                 Panifici Majer is a small chain of Venetian bakeries. Aside from typical food items, it also sells cakes, wines and more.
                </p>
                <p>
                 I hope this reference list will be of use to you out there, in your moment of supermarket need. Happy Shopping!
                </p>
