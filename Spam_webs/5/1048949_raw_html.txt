         <h1 class="post-title" data-post-id="560482" itemprop="name">
          15 Floating Shelves That Make the Most of Your Space
         </h1>
         <h4 class="post-meta clearfix">
          <div class="avatar nopin">
           <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-300 photo legacy-userphoto nopin" height="300" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/userphoto/465.jpg" width="300"/>
          </div>
          <div class="posted-on-wrapper">
           <p>
            by
            <a href="//www.brit.co/author/kristin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Kristin Appenbrink">
             <span itemprop="author">
              Kristin Appenbrink
             </span>
            </a>
            on
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-07-16T08:50:45+00:00" pubdate="">
             July 16, 2013
            </time>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing" style="display: none">
          </div>
         </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="post-content inner">
         <p>
          What do you do when you have a little extra wall space and lots of stuff to store? Install some floating shelves, of course! These 15 options are great for
          <a href="//www.brit.co/tag/small-space/" target="_blank" title="Small Spaces">
           small spaces
          </a>
          because you can stack them as high vertically as you can reach. And most of them have such a small profile that they wont make your home feel cluttered. Looking for something more substantial, but still just as airy? Go for
          <a href="//www.brit.co/25-bright-ideas-for-incorporating-open-shelves-into-your-space/" target="_blank" title="Open Shelving Ideas">
           open shelving
          </a>
          instead.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Umbra Conceal Invisible Bookshelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560536" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="357" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/1-umbra.jpg" title="Umbra Conceal Invisible Bookshelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/16EOASx" target="_blank">
           Umbra Conceal Invisible Floating Bookshelf
          </a>
          ($15): The ultimate floating shelf, this clever design makes it appear that you have a stack of books just floating on your wall. You can also get the
          <a href="http://bit.ly/16EVuak" target="_blank">
           double stack version here
          </a>
          . Or get more inspiration for
          <a href="//www.brit.co/bookshelves/" target="_blank" title="Bookshelves">
           storing your books
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Pottery Barn Holman Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560537" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="481" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2-holman01.jpg" title="Pottery Barn Holman Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          2.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/18YZHf6" target="_blank">
           Holman Shelf
          </a>
          ($60): We love how versatile this deep floating shelf is. You can choose any number of finishes, and you can even add on additional shelves and turn your shelves into a
          <a href="//www.brit.co/diy-wine-racks/" target="_blank" title="DIY Wine Racks">
           mini-bar
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Stone Resin Wall Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560538" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="653" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/3-stoneresin.png" title="Stone Resin Wall Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.cb2.com/wall-mounted-storage/storage/stone-resin-wall-shelf/f7814" target="_blank">
           Stone Resin Wall Shelf
          </a>
          ($20): Get the look of stone slab shelving without the weight. These polyresin bricks are much easier to hang.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Honeycomb Wall Shelves" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560539" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="293" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/4-honeycomb.jpg" title="Honeycomb Wall Shelves" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          4.
          <a data-affiliate-link="" href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=233801&amp;clickref=&amp;p=http%3A%2F%2Fetsy.com%2Flisting%2F151103977%2Fset-of-3-white-floating-honeycomb" rel="noskim" target="_blank">
           White Floating Honeycomb Shelves
          </a>
          ($95 for three): Arrange these reclaimed plywood shelves in any configuration you can dream up. We love em stacked or in a more organic configuration.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Triton Peg Board Shelves" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560540" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/5-tritonpegboard.jpg" title="Triton Peg Board Shelves" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          5.
          <a href="http://www.hayneedle.com/product/tritonsteellockingpegboard2shelfset.cfm?source=pjn&amp;subid=73861" target="_blank">
           Triton Steel Locking Pegboard Shelves
          </a>
          ($46 for two): If you have pegboard already set up in your kitchen or workspace, you can easily add these brightly painted steel shelves. And if you dont have pegboard, whats stopping you?
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Bin There Done That Bookshelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560542" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/6-bookbin.jpg" title="Bin There Done That Bookshelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          6.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/18Z1HnF" target="_blank">
           Bin There Done That Wall Book Bin
          </a>
          ($69): Yes, this is
          <em>
           technically
          </em>
          designed for a toddlers room, but we think its a great way to keep your most recent magazines if youre against coffee table clutter.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Cedar Floating Shelves" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560543" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="368" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/7-cedarshelves.jpg" title="Cedar Floating Shelves" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          7.
          <a data-affiliate-link="" href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=233801&amp;clickref=&amp;p=http%3A%2F%2Fetsy.com%2Flisting%2F121659955%2Ffloating-shelves-shelf-upcycled-cedar" rel="noskim" target="_blank">
           Floating Shelves Upcycled Cedar
          </a>
          ($55): If you want something a bit more rustic, and cedar scented, this is the shelf for you.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Triple Decker Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560544" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="480" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/8-threelevel.jpg" title="Triple Decker Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          8.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/16ETq28" target="_blank">
           Triple Decker Shelf
          </a>
          ($34): If you want three shelves for the price of one, consider this tiered version. We think it would be great for a little extra storage in the bathroom.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Reclaimed Wood Black Bracket Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560545" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="645" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/9-reclaimedwood.jpg" title="Reclaimed Wood Black Bracket Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          9.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/16EUbIm" target="_blank">
           Reclaimed Wood Shelf + Black Basic Brackets
          </a>
          ($78-98): Available in either 3-foot or 4-foot segments, these reclaimed wood shelves are perfect for storing your favorite cookbooks, or a few kitchen utensils you use everyday.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Connect Blue Cube Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560590" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="430" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/10-bluedotshelf.png" title="Connect Blue Cube Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          10.
          <a href="http://www.cb2.com/wall-mounted-storage/storage/connect-blue-cube-shelf/f8772" target="_blank">
           Connect Blue Cube Shelf
          </a>
          ($30): You get two shelves in one with this cube fixture. Its perfect for keeping a few supplies hidden, like matches for the candle you set on top.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Benchwright Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560548" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="479" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/11-benchwright.jpg" title="Benchwright Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          11.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/18Z5mSn" target="_blank">
           Benchwright Shelf
          </a>
          ($299): This 5+ foot textured, mango wood shelf can stand in for a mantel if you dont have one in your home.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Palette Free Floating Shelves" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560549" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="432" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/12-palletshelves.jpg" title="Palette Free Floating Shelves" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          12.
          <a data-affiliate-link="" href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=233801&amp;clickref=&amp;p=http%3A%2F%2Fetsy.com%2Flisting%2F152022410%2Fpallet-free-floating-shelves" rel="noskim" target="_blank">
           Pallet Free Floating Shelves
          </a>
          ($55): Another good use of shipping palettes, floating shelves in a walk-in closet, or a nook that youre pretending is a walk-in closet.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Set of 3 Floating Shelves" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560550" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="480" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/13-floatingshelves.png" title="Set of 3 Floating Shelves" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          13.
          <a href="http://www.cb2.com/set-of-3-floating-shelves/s485438" target="_blank">
           Set of 3 Floating Shelves
          </a>
          ($100): Arrange these raw iron shelves in any configuration you choose. Were partial to some sort of overlap to make them feel more connected.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Crown Molding Photo Rails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560551" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="480" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/14-crownmolding.jpg" title="Crown Molding Photo Rails" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          14.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/16EXfEv" target="_blank">
           Crown Molding Picture Ledge
          </a>
          ($24-$39): Mimic your traditional decor to store your photos with these simple picture ledges. They are available in black or espresso.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Modern Wall Shelf" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-560552" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="480" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/15-modernshelf.jpg" title="Modern Wall Shelf" width="645"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          15.
          <a href="http://bit.ly/16EXEXp" target="_blank">
           Modern Wall Shelf
          </a>
          ($22): Add a few pops of color to your decor with these simple shelves. We love the look of three colors stacked together.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Do you have floating shelves in your home? Tell us what you stash on them in the comments!
          </strong>
         </p>
