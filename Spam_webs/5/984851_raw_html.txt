           St. George, Utah  its early Tuesday morning March 31, 2015 and its just about time to get ready for the WCBR (Washington County Board of Realtors) brokers tour. Today from 9:30 till 12:30 there will be
           <strong>
            23 Southern Utah Real Estate listings
           </strong>
           on tour.
          </p>
          <p>
           Every Tuesday morning beginning at 8:30
           <strong>
            realtors
           </strong>

           <strong>
            Washington County
           </strong>
           gather at the Board in St. George, Utah to discuss their real estate

            expose
            <strong>
             their newest real estate listings
            </strong>
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <a href="https://www.utahmlsrealestate.com/st-george-utah-real-estate-listings-new-pending-sold-6/" title="View All MLS Real Estate For Sale In Southern Utah">
            southern Utah real estate listings
           </a>
           presented below represent a few of
           <strong>
            todays newest properties on the WCBR MLS
           </strong>
           (multiple listing service). For those that happen to be in the greater St. George area shopping for that southwestern getaway, please feel free to
           <strong>
            stop by any of these listings
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           While the
           <strong>
            WCBR brokers tour
           </strong>
           is normally reserved as a realtors only event, those
           <a href="http://alexyeager.evusa.com/expertise/realestateglossary#p" title="Pre-approval A loosely used term which is generally taken to mean that a borrower has completed a loan application and provided debt, income, and savings documentation which an underwriter has reviewed and approved">
            <strong>
             pre-approved homebuyers
            </strong>
           </a>
           that are prepared and ready to act are always welcome.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Assuming that your time is valuable
           </em>
            and that you would like to make the most if it while youre here in Southern Utah; should any of these listings be of interest, please do not hesitate to give me a call to arrange a private showing 
           <strong>
            Alex Yeager @ 435.619.2206
           </strong>
