                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Calef Brown: Gertrude Stein...en franais!
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6869869731420542611" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Artist and Poet
                     <a href="http://www.jenvaughnart.com/about/calef-brown.php">
                      Calef Brown
                     </a>
                     illustrated a children's book about Gertrude Stein titled, "
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Gertrude-Jonah-Winter/dp/141694088X">
                      Gertrude is Gertrude is Gertrude is Gertrude
                     </a>
                     ", written by Jonah Winter. The book focuses on the relationship between Gertrude and her partner Alice B. Toklas, their colorful life in Paris, and the people who surrounded them. The book was originally published in 2009, and it has just been published in a
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.co.uk/Gertrude-est/dp/2711859282">
                      French edition
                     </a>
                     .
