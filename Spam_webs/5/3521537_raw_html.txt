        <h1 class="post-title">
         <a href="/blog/2013/08/5-easy-summer-hairdos-simple-pretty-hairstyles-allison-norton-august-2013/">
          Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="post-meta">
         Posted by
         <a href="/blog/category/contributors/allison-norton/">
          Allison Norton
         </a>
         <time class="post-time" datetime="2013-08-12T04:00:00+00:00">
          August 12th, 2013
         </time>
        </div>
        <div class="post-copy">
         <p>
          <img alt="summer_hair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13587" height="558" src="https://laurenconrad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/summer_hair1.jpg" width="648"/>
          <br/>
          <a href="/blog/letter-from-lauren-hot-hot-haute-august-trends-summer-fall-lauren-conrad-august-2013" target="_blank">
           August
          </a>
          is the hottest month of the entire summer, and there is nothing worse than facing sweltering temps without a
          <a href="/blog/hot-hot-heat-5-steps-to-melt-proof-makeup-sweat-proof-beauty-coachella-april-2013" target="_blank">
           hot weather beauty regimen
          </a>
          to rely on. I dont know about you, but I cant stand the feeling of my hair being on my neck when the thermometer is climbing into three-digit territory. Instead, I like to keep my tresses off my neck, away from my face, and pulled back into an effortless do. When the standard ponytail has worn out its welcome, there are a few other options to explore
         </p>
         <h2>
          wrapped ponytail
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6246" height="601" src="http://laurenconrad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/2249240d40d37c01c90524895e023792.jpg" title="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" width="400"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Put a fresh spin on your basic pony by wrapping a piece of hair around the base and holding it in place with a bobby pin. Give it that beachy summer touch by tousling it up a little bit.
         </p>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
          <h2>
           messy bun
          </h2>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6247" height="400" src="http://laurenconrad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/f88f4df6d3ce0293ca7adca61d1c9c3e.jpg" title="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" width="400"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           This hairstyle is the easiest way to get your hair off your face and neck, and it looks cute too. Gather your hair and twist it until it makes a donut shape. Fasten your hair tie around the outer edge, secure with bobby pins and pull out a few wispy pieces for an undone look.
          </p>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           <h2>
            topsy turvy
           </h2>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <img alt="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6250" height="344" src="http://laurenconrad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/c0c994948790028fce4a9fe5aaf8ac1e.jpg" title="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            This simple trick looks like it takes a lot of effort, but you can do it in no time at all. First throw your hair into a loose low ponytail. Then spread the hair above your hair tie into two sections and loop the ponytail through it. Pull it all the way through and loosen up the twisted sides.
           </p>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div>
            <h2>
             milkmaid braids
            </h2>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6249" height="400" src="http://laurenconrad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/5d9e38c3d79caaffc37a2b3d1824f396.jpg" title="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" width="400"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             You might remember this hairstyle from our festival beauty hair ideas post. Just make two braids in your hair, wrap them around your head, and secure them to the top of your head with bobby pins. Click the photo for our step-by-step tutorial.
            </p>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <h2>
              loose fishtail
             </h2>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <img alt="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6248" height="451" src="http://laurenconrad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/tumblr_lxpg6vjLHz1qaurhio1_500.jpg" title="Hot Hot Heat: 5 Easy Summer Hairdos" width="400"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              This hairstyle is great for one of those straight-off-the-beach days when your hair is salty and windblown. The salt should even make it textured enough to hold without an elastic! Never tried a fishtail? Click the photo above for an easy tutorial.
             </p>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Which hairstyle is your favorite?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          xx
          <br/>
          Allison
