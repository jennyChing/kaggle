          Ways to Save Electricity  Ways to Save Energy

        <div class="post_content entry-content">
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           A few decades back you would have been labelled a hippy when looking into
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <strong>
             <a href="http://3ed3a2icxpy-gxd6qcu65zeq0u.hop.clickbank.net/" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #ff0000;">
               ways to save energy
              </span>
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
            but how that has changed! People are becoming more and more concerned with their surrounding environment and their steadily increasing energy bills.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           I initially took an interest in this subject about eight years back when I was looking into various ways to save electricity. I lived in a three story house situated on an estuary in South Wales UK and the weather elements took their toll on my bills.
           <span id="more-107">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           I started looking into both ways to save energy and ways to save electricity as a whole. I did not consider myself very
           <b>
            green
           </b>
           in any way really but the more research I did the more shocked I became with the whole state of affairs.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
          <b>
           Most ways to save energy are actually FREE!
          </b>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           This is something most people should take note of  the following ways to save energy are all free to implement. The only factor you have to worry about is remembering to use them on a daily basis!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <strong>
             A
            </strong>
           </span>
           The simplest of the ways to save electricity -
           <b>
            Turn thelight off after you!
           </b>
           Always switch off the light when you leave a room. So many people ignore this simple task through no other reason other than laziness. Why not take it a
           <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-110 alignright" height="370" src="http://product.siterubix.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Save_Energy_Save_Money.jpg" width="324"/>
           step further and replace the regular bulbs with energy saving models?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <strong>
             B
            </strong>
           </span>
           This again is one of the ways to save energy most people tend to overlook though a little laziness. Filling your
           <b>
            kettle
           </b>
           to the halfway mark when you are only making a couple of cups of coffee is pretty pointless. I know it takes effort to put water into the kettle for every cup of tea or coffee you fancy but you will save money doing so.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <strong>
             C
            </strong>
           </span>
           When looking at
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <strong>
             <a href="http://3ed3a2icxpy-gxd6qcu65zeq0u.hop.clickbank.net/" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #ff0000;">
               ways to save electricity
              </span>
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
           most people tend to pass over the
           <b>
            dishwasher
           </b>
           . Not all of us have one but those who do probably dont realize how much energy it takes to heat the water in these machines. Always make sure your dishwasher is full of dirty items before you use it  this will save you money in the long run.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <strong>
             D
            </strong>
           </span>
           How many of us decide to unplug an appliance after we have finished using it? This again is one of the simplest free ways to save electricity. Leaving stuff on standby is a big drain on your household energy so take a little effort to bend over and unplug the TV and satellite box before going out for the day!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <strong>
             E
            </strong>
           </span>
           In my old house I only had the option of having a bath every day  no shower fitted in the bathroom. If you do have a shower then always make it first choice over a bath. This is one of the more effective ways to save energy in the bathroom ( as long as you are not using a power shower model! ).
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           Where Can I Get More Advice?
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           The Centre for Alternative Technology (CAT) provides factsheets on saving energy that are very popular. If you are looking for one of the more D.I.Y. ways to save electricity then you may be interested in the offer below..
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h1>
          Earth For Energy
         </h1>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           So you have arrived here after shifting through the ways to save energy ( or ways to save electricity ) above. We thought this may be of interest to a few of the visitors to this page considering the subject matter and the success of the product.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <strong>
             <a href="http://3ed3a2icxpy-gxd6qcu65zeq0u.hop.clickbank.net/" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #ff0000;">
               Earth 4 Energy
              </span>
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
           is a creation of
           <strong>
            Michael Harvey
           </strong>
           and it claims to be one of the leading ways to save energy on the market today. We have seen quite a few of these products come and go but this one seems to be standing the test of time.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
          <strong>
           So What is Earth For Energy?
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Earth 4 Energy claims to help lower your energy bills by showing you how to produce your own power source at home. It is one of the new and unique ways to save electricity and in turn  energy.
           <a href="http://3ed3a2icxpy-gxd6qcu65zeq0u.hop.clickbank.net/">
            <img alt="bookcd" class="size-full wp-image-116 alignright" height="180" src="http://product.siterubix.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/bookcd.jpg" width="211"/>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           The product is available for a one time fee but it also comes with one of those sixty day money back guarantees ( that all good products should have these days! ). The product itself comes in video and book format ( which is all set out in a step-by-step format ) but the payment also includes a year long email coaching membership ( which we found very helpful ).
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           There are also a few ways to save energy bonuses that come along with the package but these are constantly being added to  presently these bonuses include a
           <strong>
            Solar Sizing Calculator
           </strong>
           and
           <strong>
            Make a Windmill
           </strong>
           guidebook.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
          <strong>
           Whats Really Inside The product?
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Most people are looking at this product for one of the ways to save electricity and energy  so what does it really offer?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           With this guide, you can easily build your first wind turbine or solar panel easily. There are many ways to save energy with this method but not so many are as easy to construct. This along with the reliable customer service were the selling points for me.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           This product provides you with the basic knowledge that is required to understand the how solar and wind power works, how to
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <strong>
             <a href="http://3ed3a2icxpy-gxd6qcu65zeq0u.hop.clickbank.net/" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #ff0000;">
               make a home built generator
              </span>
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
           for wind and solar energy and how to use ethanol as a source of energy.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           A lot of traditional ways to save electricity will only save you a small portion of money  this product aims to save you a lot more.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Before you make your mind up lets have a look at the good and the bad points of Earth 4 Energy:
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
          <strong>
           The Pros:
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Everything is included within the product package  it is a complete product. A lot of the other new ways to save energy involve you purchasing many different products and combining them.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            The instructions are quite impressive and easily laid out. I found the colour diagrams really well positioned and labelled.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            The product is constantly upgraded with new ways to save electricity and functions/methods.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            The information is also supplied on CD ROM as a backup option which I found a big help. This basically means if your internet is down you can still continue working on the project.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Many of these modern ways to save energy cost a large amount of money but I thought that Earth 4 Energy was very reasonable costing just under $50. I have come across many other products similar to this commanding price nearer $200.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Most constructive ways to save electricity use up a lot of expensive materials but this product is based around materials you can find lying about the back yard.
           </span>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h2>
          The Cons:
         </h2>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            There are a lot of products around that offer ways to save energy and are already assembled. Whilst these products are more expensive they are a lot easier for a newcomer to use. Earth 4 Energy is aimed at people who like to build products themselves.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            The guide and the product come hand in hand. The product will only offer ways to save energy once you have completed the project  the guide only works with a completed project.
           </span>
          </li>
         </ul>
