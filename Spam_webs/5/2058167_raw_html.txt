     <h1>
      Ways to Screenshot on Xperia Z
     </h1>
     <div id="bookmark">
     </div>
     <p>
      <img alt="xperia z" class="img_float_left" height="128" src="http://screenshot.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/xperia-z.jpg" width="104"/>
      The Xperia Z is an Android based smart phone that was designed and manufactured by Sony Corporation. This device boasts a Quad-core 1.5 GHz Krait CPU, a Qualcomm MDM9215M / APQ8064 chipset, and a 13MP primary camera. This smart phone is also one of the first that has a water resistant protection, and has a 5 inches screen with 16M colors, and 1080 x 1920 pixels. To
      <strong>
       screenshot Xperia Z
      </strong>
      is important these days, since a screenshot can help users to keep a certain data from a social media message, blog post, or other online communications. It will also help solid gamer in capturing a game play achievement that they want to cherish, or share to their friends. For whatever reason that you have, you can always refer to these solutions that will show you the ways on how to screenshot on Xperia Z, the best way possible.
     </p>
     <p>
      I.
      <a href="#I">
       Using Key Combination
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      II.
      <a href="#II">
       Using Power/Lock button
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      III.
      <a href="#III">
       Using the Menu button
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      IV.
      <a href="#IV">
       Using Screenshot Ultimate
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      V.
      <a href="#V">
       Better Way using Phone Manager
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      VI.
      <a href="#VI">
       Share Screenshot with Free Image Uploader
      </a>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a id="I">
      </a>
      Solution One: Screenshot Xperia Z by Pressing Key Combination
     </h2>
     <p>
      In every smart phone, theres always a default print screen function, just like in computers. However, to take screenshot on Xperia Z will require you a different key combination. To capture screen on this device all you need to do is to press Power + Volume down button at the same time for about two seconds. A camera shutter will be heard as soon as a screenshot is successfully made. The captured image can be viewed at photo gallery.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="xperia screenshot-phone manager" class="imagefloat aligncenter" height="282" src="http://screenshot.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/xperia-screenshot-phone-manager.jpg" width="547"/>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a id="II">
      </a>
      Solution Two: Pressing Power/Lock Button
     </h2>
     <p>
      If case you are not a fan of key combination, then you can use this method. Instead to pressing multiple buttons, this solution will only require you to press the Power button for about one or two seconds, and wait for a pop up box. After that simply choose Take screenshot option.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="xperia z- phone manager" class="imagefloat aligncenter" height="366" src="http://screenshot.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/xperia-z-phone-manager.jpg" width="358"/>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a id="III">
      </a>
      Solution Three: Using the Menu Button to Screenshot Xperia Z
     </h2>
     <p>
      Another way on how to take a screenshot on Xperia Z is by using the Menu button located beside the Home button. After tapping the Menu button, select Small App Active Clip and tap its icon. Now on the minibox, simply hit the Capture Screen button. After that, you can open the captured screenshot on the Sketch application.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="capture image" class="imagefloat aligncenter" height="241" src="http://screenshot.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/01.jpg" width="323"/>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a id="IV">
      </a>
      Solution Four: Screenshot Xperia Z with Mobile App
     </h2>
     <h3>
      Screenshot Ultimate
     </h3>
     <p>
      <img alt="screenshot-ultimate" class="img_float_left" height="88" src="http://screenshot.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/screenshot-ultimate.jpg" width="91"/>
      If you are a mobile app fanatic, then you can use Screenshot Ultimate. This application can be downloaded free from Google Play. This application works well whether your device is rooted or not. To take screenshot on Xperia Z with this app, follow the steps below:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Install the program.
      </li>
      <li>
       Select one of the trigger methods like shake, overlay icon, audio, and shake.
      </li>
      <li>
       Choose a portion to capture and activate the screenshot using the trigger methods. Once done, you will be given two options, whether to edit, view, or share your captured screenshot.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <h2>
      <a id="V">
      </a>
      Solution Five: Better Way to Screenshot Xperia Z
     </h2>
     <h3>
      Phone Manager
     </h3>
     <p>
      For a better way to take Sony Xperia Z screenshot is by utilizing a powerful application called
      <a href="http://www.apowersoft.com/phone-manager" target="_blank">
       Phone Manager
      </a>
      . This application can help you take screenshot of your Xperia device anytime you want. What makes this app better is its mirroring function that will project your Xperia Z screen into your computer, for a more detailed screenshot. Plus it allows users to manage their phone data, such as music, picture, video, application, and many more. All of these features can be used absolutely free of charge.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="phone manager" class="imagefloat aligncenter" height="355" src="http://screenshot.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/phone-manager1.jpg" width="558"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Here is a detailed guide on using this app:
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="button_download" href="http://download.apowersoft.com/down.php?softid=androidtransfer" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       Download Now
      </a>
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Download Phone Manager by hitting the download button.
      </li>
      <li>
       Once the program is installed, open the app and connect your Xperia Z into your PC via data cable.
      </li>
      <li>
       Next, open your smart phones USB debug mode to sync with Phone Manager.
      </li>
      <li>
       After that, your Xperia Z will be connected to Phone Manager, including the details about your phone will be shown.
      </li>
      <li>
       Then, go to the page or mobile program that you want to take screenshot.
      </li>
      <li>
       To capture Xperia Z screen just click the camera icon, and then select whether you wish to copy the taken screenshot on Clipboard or Save it to local drive.
      </li>
      <li>
       And when youre done, click Disconnect.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <h2>
      <a id="VI">
      </a>
      Sharing your Captured Screenshot
     </h2>
     <p>
      There can be times where in you want to share your captured screenshot to your friends online. In this event, it is best to use
      <a href="http://screenshot.net/free-image-uploader" target="_blank">
       Free Image Uploader
      </a>
      . By using this tool you will have the privilege to upload image faster without losing its quality. Plus Free Image Uploader has added interesting features such as sharing function that will allow you to share pictures on various social media sites, and a photo editor which is useful in adding annotations.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Conclusion
     </h2>
     <p>
      All of these methods are useful in capturing Sony Xperia Z screen. However, it is best to use a well rounded application such as Phone Manager, for it could provide you with a high quality screenshots, at the same time let you manage your phone files as well.
     </p>
