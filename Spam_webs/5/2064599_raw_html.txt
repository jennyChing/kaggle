       <p>
        We all have got tired of the trivial things in our life that make it dull and even more boring. Amazing and not standard ideas help us to make it multicolored and bright. We dont want the routine and the drab days anymore, we crave for sun and happiness in the eyes. The main goal of the modern architecture is to create the bright environment for citizens and make it eye-catchy. Today you can find various original architectural solutions in the streets of your city and most of them are really stunning. Without them the city can lose its identity and become bleak.
       </p>
       <p>
        This post is dedicated to the great examples of creative bus stops (some of them are real and some of them are futuristic) that are to be found all around the world. Hope, you will enjoy them too.
