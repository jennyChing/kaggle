            Dogless shepherd controls flock of sheep with wolf poster
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="date">
           <span class="author">
            Posted by
            <span class="fn">
             hitsu
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="clock">

           <tr>
            <td>
             <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_ajJvd3h_l6k/SU57CBSa3UI/AAAAAAAAAIo/aV_Rn-K4nxU/s1600-h/1duhebing.jpg" onblur="try {parent.deselectBloggerImageGracefully();} catch(e) {}">
              <img alt="Dogless shepherd controls flock of sheep with wolf poster" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5282294687587818818" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_ajJvd3h_l6k/SU57CBSa3UI/AAAAAAAAAIo/aV_Rn-K4nxU/s400/1duhebing.jpg" style="float:left; margin:0 10px 10px 0;cursor:pointer; cursor:hand;width: 400px; height: 222px;" title="Dogless shepherd controls flock of sheep with wolf poster"/>

          <br/>
          <div align="justify">
           The credit crunch has forced us all to cut back on life's luxuries.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           But this shepherd may have tightened his purse strings a little too much in the face of the recession.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Farmer Du Hebing couldn't afford another sheepdog after his old one died, so he learned how to control his flock using just a poster of a wolf after seeing one in a local wildlife park.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Du told
           <span style="font-style:italic;">
            Huashang Daily
           </span>
           that he shot the picture by chance.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "After visiting
           <span style="font-style:italic;">
            Qinling Wild Animal Park,
           </span>
           on the way home I saw a group of sheep walking along the road with a man holding a picture following behind them.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "The man was using the wolf picture to scare the sheep and drive them ahead - it was a really funny scene.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "But I noticed that the sheep never went near it.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "People laugh and point but it works - and the poster eats a lot less than any sheepdog."
