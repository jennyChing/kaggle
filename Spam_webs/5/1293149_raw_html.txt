      Paper Giraffe Folding Kit
     </div>
     <div class="project_content">
      <img align="left" border="0" data-hi-res="http://payload313.cargocollective.com/1/8/275135/8561542/Paper_Giraffe2_o.jpg" data-mid="46167645" data-title="670  670  946" height="946" height_o="1600" src="http://payload313.cargocollective.com/1/8/275135/8561542/Paper_Giraffe2.jpg" src_o="http://payload313.cargocollective.com/1/8/275135/8561542/Paper_Giraffe2_o.jpg" width="670" width_o="1132"/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      *** DIY Kit for Paper Ninja's and Expert Crafters! ***
      <br/>
      <br/>
      An animal friendly rhino folding kit to create a big paper wall trophy. The assembled size of the giraffe is 65x42x50 cm. It looks really great and modern on your wall. The kit contains 3 large posters, glue, a folding lath and includes all the information needed to assemble this giraffe yourself! :)
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Difficulty:
      <br/>
      <br/>
      More difficult then the other kits. Because of the fact that it's a 3d puzzle it takes some time, patience and Houdini moves to complete the job, but that's a part of the fun! assembling time is 5 hours.
