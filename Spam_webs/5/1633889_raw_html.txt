		&lt;image src="http://blogs.perceptionsystem.com/wp-content/themes/perceptionblog/images/inverted-commas-left.png" style=" width:20px; height:20px;"/&gt;News is coming that Sony is in talks with Microsoft to launch a Windows Phone smart-phone in 2014. To launch its wonderful device based on Windows Phone OS, Sony would use its previously PC-centric Vaio brand. To get more information on this news, continue reading this blog&lt;image src="http://blogs.perceptionsystem.com/wp-content/themes/perceptionblog/images/inverted-commas-right.png" style=" width:20px; height:20px;"/&gt;

	&lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;
           
		&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://blogs.perceptionsystem.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Windows-phone-Sony-Vaio.jpg" alt="Windows phone Sony Vaio" width="700" height="300" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10833" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;We all know that Sony is one of the biggest consumer electronic products provider companies that has launched a complete array of products like DVD, Walkman, Blu-Ray players, VHS, televisions and this generations Playstation console. As compare to everything that Sony produces, its influence in the mobile world has been seemed somewhat mum. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Now, the information is also coming that Microsoft is in talks with Sony to soon release a Windows Phone smart-phone in 2014. As per the latest news, Sony is going to use its previously PC-centric Vaio brand for the smart-phone. &lt;p&gt;Both Sony as well as Microsofts Windows Phone 8 are perfect match for each other as together they can built an excellent smart-phone that will surely loved by users. However, the deal is still not final, but a new Windows Phone OEM will be a biggest win for Microsoft in its attempts to win mobile market-share. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The most popular Windows Phone leveraging the Vaio brand-name that can also help Sony Company, an Android supporter, stands out from the hardware manufacturers.
