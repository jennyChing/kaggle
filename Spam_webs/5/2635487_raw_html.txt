      <div class="post-entry">
       <p>
        The
        <a href="http://kck.st/1a8XUrg" title="Makers on Kickstarter">
         Bright Girls Kickstarter campaign
        </a>
        is abook and activities that teach girls about business in a fun and engaging way. It shows two girls who are makers andgirlpreneurs. The characters, Lucy and Kylie come up with a unique bracelet band concept, they test their idea, learn how to source materials, price and market their product, and of course create it.
       </p>
       <p>
        I have attended maker faires with my children and my son has participated in some of the mini faires with his robotics team. But until I saw this graphic I didnt realize just how gigantic and dynamic the maker movement has become. Approximately 135million adults in the United States are makers!
       </p>
       <p>
        Glad to be part of this huge creative movement as I showyoung girls how to make and then monetize. Get your girls involved and learn more about our campaign
        <a href="http://kck.st/1a8XUrg">
         here
        </a>
        !
       </p>
