       <p>
        <a href="http://blog.clarity.fm/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Crowdfunding-Short.png">
         <img alt="Crowdfunding Short" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-6140" height="339" src="http://blog.clarity.fm/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Crowdfunding-Short-603x339.png" width="603"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Crowdfunding is
        <strong>
         democratizing entrepreneurship
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Last December, a portal desktop computer that anyone can assemble called Kano
        <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/alexklein/kano-a-computer-anyone-can-make" target="_blank" title="Kano - Kickstarter">
         raised $1.5 million on Kickstarter
        </a>
        . In July 2014, Bug-A-Salt, a toy like shotgun that shoots salt blasts to kill insects,
        <a href="http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/bugasalt-the-final-push--3" target="_blank" title="Indiegogo">
         raised more than half-a-million on Indiegogo
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        What these projects have in common is that consumers wanted them and were willing to pay to have them developed from an entrepreneurs imagination.
       </p>
       <p>
        In the last 3 years, the crowdfunding economy has more than tripled to more than $5.1
        <em>
         billion
        </em>
        .
        <em>
         <a class="twitter-share-button tweet-btn" data-count="none" data-lang="en" data-text="Did you know that the crowdfunding economy is worth $5.1 billion? #entrepreneurship" data-url="http://blog.clarity.fm/clarity-exclusive-crowdfundings-epic-growth-infographic/" data-via="getmoreclarity" href="https://twitter.com/share">
          Tweet This
         </a>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        But whats hard to imagine is that this market is still in its infancy. The crowdfunding economy is about to undergo a dramatic shift. Keep reading to learn why.
        <span id="more-6104">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://blog.clarity.fm/clarity-exclusive-crowdfundings-epic-growth-infographic">

        </a>
       </p>
