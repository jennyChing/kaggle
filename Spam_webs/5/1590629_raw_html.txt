          <p>
           The fans at Sprint Center will be treated to a game between the West Virginia Mountaineers and the Baylor Bears when they take their seats on Thursday.
          </p>
          <p>
           Oddsmakers currently have the Bears listed as 4-point favorites versus the Mountaineers, while the games total is sitting at 137.
          </p>
          <p>
           In their last action, Baylor was a 77-74 winner at home against Texas Tech. They failed to cover the -15.5-point spread as favorites.
          </p>
          <p>
           West Virginia was a 81-72 winner in their most recent outing at home against Oklahoma State. They covered the -5.5-point spread as favorites.
          </p>
          <p>
           West Virginia:
           <br/>
           Team record: 23-8 SU,18-12 ATS
           <br/>
           West Virginia is 5-1 ATS in its last 6 games
           <br/>
           West Virginia is 16-7 SU in its last 23 games
           <br/>
           The total has gone OVER in 4 of West Virginias last 5 games
           <br/>
           West Virginia is 1-5 ATS in its last 6 games when playing Baylor
          </p>
          <p>
           Baylor:
           <br/>
           Team record: 23-8 SU,15-12 ATS
           <br/>
           Baylor is 4-1 ATS in its last 5 games
           <br/>
           Baylor is 5-1 SU in its last 6 games
           <br/>
           Baylor is 5-1 ATS in its last 6 games when playing West Virginia
           <br/>
           Baylor is 6-1 SU in its last 7 games when playing West Virginia
          </p>
