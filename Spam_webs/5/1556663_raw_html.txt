       <h1 class="post-title">
        Things to keep in mind while remodeling a small bathroom
       </h1>
       <p class="meta">
        <span class="meta-date">
         Published on
         <time data-hover="February 11 2015" datetime="2015-02-11T11:35:02+01:00">
          February 11 2015
         </time>
        </span>
        <span class="meta-author">
         by Carl Hill
        </span>
       </p>
      </header>
      <div class="body">
       <div class="ob-sections">
        <div class="ob-section ob-section-text">
         <figure class=" ob-pull-right ob-media-right ob-img-size-300 ">
          <a class="ob-link-img" href="http://img.over-blog-kiwi.com/1/33/54/39/20150211/ob_957ada_8.jpg" target="_blank" title="bathroom remodeling contractors VA">
           <img alt="Things to keep in mind while remodeling a small bathroom" class="ob-media ob-img" src="http://img.over-blog-kiwi.com/300x300/1/33/54/39/20150211/ob_957ada_8.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
         <div class="ob-text">
          <p>
           Often, the space that we live in starts to appear small because of our increasing needs, the clutter in the house or because of a new family member. In such a case, our attention goes to the kitchen area that remains hidden and unused for so long. Because the kitchen often goes ignored, it gets stashed up with unnecessary stuff and may even get damp and damaged. In such a case,
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.merrifieldgraniteandmarble.com/remodeling-kitchen-countertops.php">
             kitchen remodeling contractors VA
            </a>
           </strong>
           or any other area becomes necessary. But if you have a small kitchen that needs remodeling, then you may need to follow certain tips and suggestions to see the best results. The following are the top things to keep in mind while remodeling a small kitchen:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Flooring
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           It is very important to pay enough attention to the flooring of the kitchen. Often, laminated flooring or vinyl flooring is apt for the kitchen but materials like hardwood planks must be avoided as they tend to get damaged due to weather changes and humidity, which is common in kitchens.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Professional service
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           A small kitchen is a confusing space to remodel or renovate since there is a lot you want to do with it but the space restriction might not be able to help you out. Thus it is always better to contact and hire a professional service provider for
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.merrifieldgraniteandmarble.com/remodeling-bathroom-countertops.php">
             bathroom remodeling contractors Virginia
            </a>
           </strong>
           or any other area. These contractors suggest the best changes, keep in mind your expectations and are experienced to help you make optimal use of the small space.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Moisture control
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           One of the biggest reasons why people hesitate in using their kitchens is because these spaces have the tendency to absorb the ground water and become moist. The moisture can easily spread and may interfere with the construction. Hence it is important to follow moisture control tips such as installing interior drain tiles, installing roof guttering and grading the yard to slope away from the home.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Ceiling
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           For the kitchen space, the best kind of ceiling is the ceiling with the drop feature in which there is a grid and a lightweight acoustical panels and duct work. This particular type of ceiling can reduce the headroom in the kitchen. But since you have a
           <strong>
            <a class="popup" href="http://cambriaquartzandbathroomcountertop.blogspot.com/2015/02/jazz-up-your-kitchen-with-a-chic-backsplash-design.html">
             small kitchen
            </a>
           </strong>
           , you can order a custom made smaller grid and tiles because the regular ones can overwhelm the space.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Electrical work
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           It is important to pay attention to the electrical wiring and duct work as well during kitchen remodeling as you may need some extra drilling work which has to be done at the right time. Thus, hiring an electrician can be a good idea in such a case.
          </p>
          <p>
           Small kitchens can be tricky spaces and must be dealt with the maximum care and consideration. This is why; hiring a contractor is always a good idea. If you wish to hire a remodeling contractor for kitchen work or any other work, then you can contact
           <a href="http://www.merrifieldgraniteandmarble.com/">
            Merrifield Granite and Marble
           </a>
           .
          </p>
