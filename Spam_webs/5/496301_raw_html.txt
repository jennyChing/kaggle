       <h2>
        <span class="hidden">
         How it works
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Youll be pleasantly surprised at just how simple donating your clothes with us will be. Take a look for yourself
       </p>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="520" mozallowfullscreen="" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/86889522?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="925">

      <div class="pagecontent-block full panel-middle">
       <h3>
        <span class="hidden">
         How to donate with us, step by step
        </span>
       </h3>
       <ol>
        <li class="pic1">
         <span>
          <strong>
           <a href="/account/" style="color: #2DABE2;">
            Register
           </a>
          </strong>
          your details on this website.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li class="pic2">
         <span>
          <strong>
           <a href="/find-a-charity/" style="color: #2DABE2;">
            Visit the find a charity tab
           </a>
          </strong>
          to choose your favourite charity
         </span>
        </li>
        <li class="pic3">
         <span>
          <strong>
           <a href="/find-a-charity/" style="color: #2DABE2;">
            Click your favourite charity
           </a>
          </strong>
          and request a bag. Well post it out, alongside a barcode and instructions on what to do next.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li class="pic4">
         <span>
          <strong>
           Fill it up with clothes, shoes &amp; accessories.
          </strong>
          Were sorry, but we can only accept things in good, reusable condition  no bric-a-brac, please.
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <span>
          <strong>
           Courier collections:
          </strong>
          minimum weight of 15kg and a maximum weight of 25kg.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li class="pic5">
         <span>
          When youre ready, come back to the website, log in and
          <strong>
           <a href="/account/return-your-bag/" style="color: #2DABE2;">
            arrange a collection
           </a>
          </strong>
          . We can pick up from home, work or wherever suits you.
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <span>
          Our courier company, Parcelforce, will collect your bag between 1pm and 6pm. If you book before 2pm you can also get next day collections.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li class="pic6">
         <span>
          Once its been safely received and scanned, well send you an email to say thanks.
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Thats it. Simple, secure and safe in the knowledge that the charity you chose will benefit from your donation.
        </li>
       </ol>
