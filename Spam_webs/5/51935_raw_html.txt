                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Why choose plastic bins over cardboard boxes during relocation?
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5313142417212194762" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      Packing, taping and moving countless of cardboard boxes from one location to another during relocation, can be one of the most stressful tasks. There is always so much to worry about during relocation like; packing properly in the correct boxes, and making sure not to break anything, or getting things crushed in the truck, because the boxes or containers were not placed properly.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Transporting objects while at the same time protecting them against all kinds of damage like; brokerage, water from rain fall or snow, dirt, and even mold, is very challenging.
                      <br/>
                      Hence, successfully relocation without any damage to peoples property, is the reason why people gradually have switched to plastic moving bins for packing things, during relocation.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Here are a few of the many reasons why
                      <a href="http://www.totalrelocationservices.com/ez-crate.php" target="_blank">
                       plastic moving bins
                      </a>
                      are a smart decision.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       It is safe
                      </b>
                      - Unlike cardboard boxes, plastic bins are safe. They have a comfortable grip and are easy to lift, which makes the moving process, much safer than with cardboard boxes. Moreover, lifting plastic bins does not cause any back pain or injury, as bins are held closer to the body.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Convenient
                      </b>
                      - These bins are available with packing paper, labels, bubble wrap, and a moving dolly, which makes the relocation process much easier.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Dust/dirt-free-
                      </b>
                      With plastic bins, the chances of dirt and debris are minimal because the plastic bins lids will protect the valuables inside the bins. Moreover, things kept in bins are not likely to get spoiled even if it rains, which is a major concern in the case of cardboard boxes.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Disinfected
                      </b>
                      -There is no risk of illness for individuals carrying the bins, because the bins are previously treated with disinfectants.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Easily pre assembled
                      </b>
                      -No taping is required for packing things in plastic bins, because all that is needed to be done is open the lid, fill the bin and then close it.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Efficient
                      </b>
                      - with plastic moving bins, compact packing can be done, therefore using less space in the truck, which calls for hiring a smaller truck. Subsequently, this saves money and cuts down the relocation time.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Green
                      </b>
                      - While this point does not affect people directly, it does have an important impact on the cost effective aspect of using plastic bins. These bins can be reused to make new plastic boxes, when they are no longer functional.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Affordable-In contrast to cupboard boxes; plastic bins are more affordable. Plastic bins costs 50% less than cupboard boxes.
                     </div>
