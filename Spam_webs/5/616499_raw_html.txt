        <div class="article-content clearfix">
         <p>
          Meet Angelica, a Dunkin Donuts drive-thru worker easily in the running for the national chains Employee of the Year. Does Dunkin even hand out an Employee of the Year award? If so, this Connecticut resident earned consideration after last weekends act of valor and sass. Not only did she thwart a robbery at the 967 First Ave location in West Haven by chucking a pot of hot coffee in the suspects face, but she gave the perp lip in perfect 80s action hero cadence. Just
          <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wDztrw_0N8M" target="_blank">
           like this guy
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          The employee didnt even give her last name when retelling the incident of a white Ford Explorer pulling up to her drive-thru window on Saturday. Thats the kind of selfless heroine she is. As
          <a href="http://www.nbcconnecticut.com/news/local/Dunkin-Donuts-Employee-Stops-Robbery-With-Coffee--194800331.html?2" target="_blank">
           told
          </a>
          to NBC Connecticut:
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           He rushed himself, tried to get out of his car. Thats when I realized he was serious. I tried to close the [drive-thru] window. He put his arm in it. He knew not to touch, because he knew there would be fingerprints  I did see his coffee that I made him, so I looked at [it] quick, threw him the first one. Looked at the pots of coffee and threw the pot of coffee at him. Thats when he started running into his truck and then he left, and I said, Go run on Dunkin!
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          Watch the
          <a href="http://youtu.be/EmdJ4IgGzII" target="_blank">
           security footage
          </a>
          below to see Angelica in action:
         </p>
