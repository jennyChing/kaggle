     <div class="moat-trackable pb-f-theme-normal pb-2 pb-feature pb-layout-item pb-f-article-article-body" id="fhJQMo1Yy6crip" moat-id="article/article-body|local">
      <div class="article-body" id="article-body">
       <div class="pb-sig-line hasnt-headshot has-0-headshots hasnt-bio is-not-column">
        <span class="pb-byline" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
         By
         <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/people/angela-fritz">
          <span itemprop="name">
           Angela Fritz
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="pb-timestamp" content="2014-11-07T12:05-500" itemprop="datePublished">
         November 7, 2014
        </span>
        <span class="pb-tool email">
         <a href="mailto:angela.fritz@washpost.com?subject=Reader feedback for 'Hurricane-force wind warning issued for Bering Sea, Aleutians ahead of bomb cyclone'">
          <span class="fa fa-envelope">
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="tweet-authors">
         <span class="pb-twitter-follow unprocessed">
          <a class="twitter-follow-button" data-lang="en" data-show-count="false" href="https://twitter.com/@angelafritz">
           Follow @angelafritz
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <article itemprop="articleBody">
        <div class="inline-content inline-photo inline-photo-normal">
         <a name="769a8531f4">
         </a>
         <img class="unprocessed is-gif" data-hi-res-src="https://images.washingtonpost.com/?url=https://img.washingtonpost.com/blogs/capital-weather-gang/files/2014/11/mar24.gif&amp;op=noop" data-low-res-src="https://img.washingtonpost.com/wp-apps/imrs.php?src=https://img.washingtonpost.com/blogs/capital-weather-gang/files/2014/11/mar24.gif&amp;w=480" src="https://img.washingtonpost.com/wp-apps/imrs.php?src=https://img.washingtonpost.com/blogs/capital-weather-gang/files/2014/11/mar24.gif&amp;w=480"/>
         <br/>
         <span class="pb-caption">
          Surface forecast of the Bering Sea cyclone for Friday evening. (NWS)
         </span>
        </div>
        <p>
         The National Weather Service is warning of hurricane-force winds on Friday  up to 80 mph  ahead of the remnants of Typhoon Nuri, which are expected to intensify significantly as it approaches the Bering Sea.
        </p>
        <p>
         The remnant low pressure system associated with former Typhoon Nuri will track north through the Pacific Ocean on Friday, intensifying from a low pressure of 964 mbon Friday morningto around 925 mb by Friday night. The storm constitutes a meteorological bomb, which is a pressure drop of at least 24 mbin 24 hours.
        </p>
        <div class="inline-content inline-photo inline-photo-normal">
         <a name="59d3101fd1">
         </a>
         <img class="unprocessed" data-hi-res-src="https://img.washingtonpost.com/wp-apps/imrs.php?src=https://img.washingtonpost.com/blogs/capital-weather-gang/files/2014/11/bering-warnings.png&amp;w=1484" data-low-res-src="https://img.washingtonpost.com/wp-apps/imrs.php?src=https://img.washingtonpost.com/blogs/capital-weather-gang/files/2014/11/bering-warnings.png&amp;w=480" src="https://img.washingtonpost.com/wp-apps/imrs.php?src=https://img.washingtonpost.com/blogs/capital-weather-gang/files/2014/11/bering-warnings.png&amp;w=480"/>
         <br/>
         <span class="pb-caption">
          A hurricane-force wind warning (red) is in effect for the Bering Sea, including the western Aleutian Islands, through Friday night. (NWS)
         </span>
        </div>
        <p>
         A hurricane-force wind warning has been issued for parts of the Bering Sea, including the western Aleutian Islands. The warning is in effect through Friday night, when winds up to 80 mph are expected to sweep across the region. These strong winds will kick up huge waves at least 50 feet in height, and possibly larger.
        </p>
        <p>
         The population of these far western Aleutian islands, which are expected to take the worst blow from the storm was around 400 people as of the 2000 census.
        </p>
        <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
         <p>

         </p>
          BuzzFeed Storm (@BuzzFeedStorm)
         <a href="https://twitter.com/BuzzFeedStorm/status/530467828563197953">
          November 6, 2014
         </a>
        </blockquote>
        <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
        </script>
        <p>
         The storm has the potential to set a low pressure record for the Bering Sea.
         <span style="color: #2c2c2c;">
          The previous low pressure recordfor Alaska is 927 mb, which was recorded at Dutch Harbor in 1977. The lowest extratropical pressure on record for any locationis913 mb, which was set in the North Sea east of Scotlandin 1993.
         </span>
        </p>
