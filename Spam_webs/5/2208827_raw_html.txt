         <h1>
          Interested in Introverts: 10 Careers for the Quiet Ones in Society
         </h1>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="article-container" id="TNArticleBodySection">
        <div id="article-body">
         <p>
          If you've ever watched Susan Cain's inspiring TED Talk, "
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c0KYU2j0TM4" target="_blank">
           The Power of Introverts
          </a>
          ," you'd know just how talented and skilled those who are somewhat quieter than others really are. Although introverts sometimes get a bad rap for enjoying long periods of solitude, having small groups of close friends, or being hard to get to know, they have a lot to offer -- both professionally and personally. Here are 10 careers to choose from if you're a proud introvert.
         </p>
         <h2>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          Geoscientist
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="scientist exploring ground rubble" class="media-image" src="http://artstg.icbdr.com/sites/all/files/9713514646_8d5f24e79c.jpg" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Image via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dvanzuijlekom/9713514646/sizes/m/" target="_blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          by dvanzuijlekom
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Geoscientists analyze the physical aspects of the Earth, including its structure, composition, and processes, to learn about its past, present, and future. Their median pay in 2012 was
          <a href="http://www.bls.gov/ooh/life-physical-and-social-science/geoscientists.htm" target="_blank">
           $90,890 per year
          </a>
          , with a faster than average 16 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. If you want to pursue a career as a geoscientist, you can expect to split your time between working in an office, in a laboratory, and outdoors in the field. The good news for introverts: field work usually means traveling to remote locations and working irregular hours.
         </p>
         <h2>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          Veterinarian
         </h2>
         <p>
          Veterinarians take care of animals and nurture them back to health, improving public health in the process. In addition to performing medical research on conditions and diseases of pets, livestock, and other animals, they diagnose and treat them.Their median pay in 2012 was
          <a href="http://www.bls.gov/ooh/healthcare/veterinarians.htm" style="line-height: 1.5" target="_blank">
           $84,460 per year
          </a>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
           , with an as fast as average 12 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. If you're interested in becoming a veterinarian, you can expect to work in anything from a hospital or private clinic to a farm or classroom.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          Technical Writer
         </h2>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
           Also known as technical communicators, technical writers draft journal articles, instruction manuals, and other supporting documents to effectively communicate complex information. They also develop and disseminate technical information among manufacturers, customers, and designers. Their median pay in 2012 was
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.bls.gov/ooh/media-and-communication/technical-writers.htm" style="line-height: 1.5" target="_blank">
           $65,500 per year
          </a>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5">
           ,with a faster than average 15 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. Most technical writers concentrate on computer and engineering industries, working in office settings or even on their own -- perfect for introverts!
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          Social Media Manager
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="man holding social media circle sign" class="media-image" src="http://artstg.icbdr.com/sites/all/files/12799560873_3ecbdf06d9.jpg" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Image via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/esthervargasc/12799560873/sizes/m/" target="_blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          by clasesdeperiodismo
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Social media managers create and maintain social media accounts for the companies they represent, ensuring they shape favorable public perception while increasing awareness. Their median pay in 2012 was $54,170 per year,with an as fast as average 12 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022.Although you might attend some community events as a social media manager, you usually work in an office. The advantage for introverts is that instead of interacting face to face with people, they get to do it online without compromising themselves.
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Film/Video Editor
         </h2>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Video editors manipulate images that inform or entertain an audience, working in office settings, studios, or on location (when shooting raw footage). Their median pay in 2012 was $46,280 per year,with a slower than average 3 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. If you're an introvert and want to get those creative juices flowing, consider editing video for a living. Not only will you get to work behind a camera and computer on your own, but you'll receive praise once your video is up on the big screen (without having to stand in front of everyone).
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Court Reporter
         </h2>
         <br/>
         <p>
          <img alt="guy with microphones pointed at him" class="media-image" src="http://artstg.icbdr.com/sites/all/files/5162615114_fa741f0b18.jpg" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Image via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/monewshorizon/5162615114/sizes/m/" target="_blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          by MoNewsHorizon
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Court reporters transcribe anything said at depositions, trials, and any other legal proceedings. Additionally, some court reporters might provide television captioning and offer translation for deaf people in classrooms, at public events, and at business meetings.Their median pay in 2012 was $48,160 per year,with an as fast as average 10 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. While you might think court reporters are in the middle of the action, they're actually more like a fly on the wall.
          <a href="http://www.forbes.com/sites/susanadams/2014/03/04/the-10-best-jobs-for-introverts/" target="_blank">
           According to Forbes
          </a>
          , "they come, they sit, they record, they leave."
         </p>
         <h2>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          Financial Clerk
         </h2>
         <p>
          Financial clerks are responsible for performing any administrative work for the organization they work for, keeping records, helping customers, and carrying out financial transactions.Their median pay in 2012 was $34,960 per year,with an as fast as average 11 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. Although financial clerks can work in a variety of settings, including medical offices, bank branches, and government agencies, they work on their own in most cases tucked away in a corner. If you're an introvert who likes numbers, this is the job for you.
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Astronomer
         </h2>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Astronomers analyze how various forms of energy and matter interact, studying the nature of time or trying to figure out the origin of the universe. Astronomers in applied fields might develop new sources of energy, produce new military technologies, or monitor space debris that could negatively impact satellites.Their median pay in 2012 was $106,360 per year,with an as fast as average 10 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. Any introvert who likes gazing at the stars wondering and dreaming about the universe is a perfect fit for this job.
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Graphic Designer
         </h2>
         <br/>
         <p>
          <img alt="girl designing car on computer" class="media-image" src="http://artstg.icbdr.com/sites/all/files/6304834975_6d1c726560.jpg" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Image via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dboyfotopages/6304834975/sizes/m/" target="_blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          by dboyfotopages
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Graphic designers use either their hands or computer software to create visual concepts, communicating ideas that inform, inspire, or captivate consumers. In most cases, they're the ones who develop the entire layout and production design for brochures, magazines, advertisements, and corporate reports.Their median pay in 2012 was $44,150 per year,with a slower than average 7 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022. Since about 24 percent of graphic designers are self-employed, it's a great gig for an introvert who likes working from home.
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          Computer Programmer
         </h2>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Computer programmers create software programs by writing code for them. Essentially, they take what the software developers and engineers designed and turn it into instructions that a computer follows. Their median pay in 2012 was $74,280 per year,with an as fast as average 8 percent job outlook between 2012 and 2022.If being buried in your computer and coding sounds more appealing than standing face to face with people, then this job could be the right fit for you.
         </p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Are you an introvert? Consider any one of these 10 jobs that'll match your personality type.
         </p>
