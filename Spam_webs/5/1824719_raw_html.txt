       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Our
         <a href="http://www.tor.com/blogs/2012/01/vote-in-the-torcom-2011-readers-choice-awards" target="_blank">
          2011 Tor.com Readers Choice Awards
         </a>
         poll covers novels, short fiction, and comics, but were also interested in seeing what readers are attracted to in regards to covers.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thus far in the poll, voting for covers has been mostly unconsidered, with fans lumping in a cover based on their feelings for the book or author. But then again, a striking cover is much harder to recall than a favorite story, so weve started assembling cover galleries so that voters can make a more considered choice.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="attachment- alignleft " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.tor.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/orbit-books.jpg?resize=250%2C176&amp;type=vertical"/>
         Were hoping to add more as the poll goes on, but for now you can peruse book covers from the following publishers:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.orbitbooks.net/orbit-us-springsummer-2011/" target="_blank">
           Orbit Books spring and summer 2011
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.orbitbooks.net/orbit-us-september-2011-march-2012/" target="_blank">
           Orbit Books fall and winter 2011
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/media/set/?set=a.10150531758643205.401838.76967918204&amp;type=1" target="_blank">
           Tor Books 2011
          </a>
          (Facebook link)
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.wizards.com/dnd/Catalog.aspx?category=novels" target="_blank">
           Wizards of the Coast 2011
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         These are not complete lists, but hopefully they jog ones memory! Well be updating the
         <a href="http://www.tor.com/blogs/2012/01/vote-in-the-torcom-2011-readers-choice-awards" target="_blank">
          main voting post
         </a>
         as more come in. Happy viewing!
        </p>
