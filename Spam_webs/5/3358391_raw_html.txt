        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tripoto.com/" style="font-family: Bitter, Georgia, serif; font-size: 20px; line-height: 1.3;" target="_blank">
          <img alt="gap-year" height="427" src="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/gap-year.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4 dir="ltr">
         1. Because you are thinking about it.
         <span id="more-621">
         </span>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         2. Because you should take advantage of not knowing what to do next.
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Screen-shot-2014-04-21-at-7.47.35-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen shot 2014-04-21 at 7.47.35 PM" height="590" src="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Screen-shot-2014-04-21-at-7.47.35-PM.png" width="880"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         3. Because you should let the world take you by the collar and shove you into the unfamiliar. Your parents have been doing it all your life. Youll get used to it.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         4. Because you can be an engineer, doctor, academician, consultant, 365 days later too.
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/GapYearExperience.jpg">
          <img alt="GapYearExperience" height="667" src="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/GapYearExperience.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         5. Because youll see life in itstruest,
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="1b8fb0ec-3122-4141-adec-2ba25bd2d4e1">
          barest
         </span>
         form. And, itll educate you. Not the way books do. In a more subliminal manner.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         6. Because you might find yourself while waiting at a bus stop for a day and getting nothing. Or, you might not. But youll learn something else. Something essential. Patience.
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/tumblr_n0hq1kxq7j1st5lhmo1_1280.jpg">
          <img alt="tumblr_n0hq1kxq7j1st5lhmo1_1280" height="832" src="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/tumblr_n0hq1kxq7j1st5lhmo1_1280.jpg" width="1280"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         7.Because you might end up actually knowing what you might want to do with your life.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         8.Because you have stayed in the same place, slept inthe same bed, woken up to the same people for far too long. Change that. Move.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         <a href="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/3920059537_ed704396e6_z.jpg">
          <img alt="3920059537_ed704396e6_z" height="426" src="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/3920059537_ed704396e6_z.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         9. Because youll get pissed drunk and disillusioned and wake up in an empty swimming pool after the Full Moon Party. But, thats okay.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         10. Because youll see love for more than what you thought it was. Youll see love in its most giving, caring form.
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/2282157646_5c3b34bdec_b.jpg">
          <img alt="2282157646_5c3b34bdec_b" height="753" src="http://static2.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/2282157646_5c3b34bdec_b.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         11. Because youll grow as a person and an entity. Youll start comprehending the world, and your importance in it.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         12. Because youll come back with stories.
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/RunningoftheReindeer.jpg">
          <img alt="Anchorage Fur Rendezvous" height="360" src="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/RunningoftheReindeer.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         13. Because your heart actually wants you to. Take the plunge. Youll survive it. And, when you resurface, things wouldve changed. For the better.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         14. Because white collars and corner offices can wait.
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/d17ebc644bf3a331b1e744ba40e1d4f4.jpg">
          <img alt="d17ebc644bf3a331b1e744ba40e1d4f4" height="532" src="http://static1.tripoto.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/d17ebc644bf3a331b1e744ba40e1d4f4.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4>
         15. Because you can survive another year without much money. You survived high school.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         16. Because there will never be a better time. You are young. You are eager. And lifes
         <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="2dadcadd-2b01-49cd-9b8b-8b7f2b49e757">
          diktat
         </span>
         hasnt been able to bog you down. Yet.
        </h4>
        <h4>
         And,
        </h4>
        <h4>
         17.
         <a href="http://airbnb.com/tripoto" target="_blank" title="Airbnb">
          Because Tripoto is giving you 25$ to kickstart your dream of travel.Go. Travel. Experience. Learn.
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         (If you are trying to get your 25$ through mobile then you will need to first download the mobile app and then enter the code tcom when asked)
