     <div>
      <strong>
       architectural camouflage by snarkitecture for PAOM
      </strong>
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <div>
      <strong>
       architectural camouflage  designed by
       <a href="http://www.snarkitecture.com" target="_blank">
        snarkitecture
       </a>
       and produced by
       <a href="https://go.madmimi.com/redirects/1431104964-660b28c220f6a38372a5dc49e198c089-8791e77?pa=30176846390" target="_blank">
        print all over me
       </a>
        is anew series of apparel that uses allover prints of white building materials,allowing the wearer to blend into their architectural surroundings.
      </strong>
      the first release of this series includes patterns that replicate subway tile, marble, and hex tile all printed at a 1:1 scale on a range of wearable PAOM pieces from shirts to caps, jackets, backpacks and trousers.
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <div>
      <em>
       architectural camouflage allows the wearer to create moments of architectural confusion through interaction with the built environment, making architecture perform the unexpected.
      </em>
      snarkitecture
     </div>
