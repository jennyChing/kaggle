        <p>
         Bryan Dunn is a graphic designer, illustrator and devoted father who draws on his sons brown lunch paper bags every day. His kiddo has just finished 1st grade and Bryan decided to present his favorite illustrations on his
         <a href="http://moremonger.tumblr.com/" style="text-decoration: none; border-bottom: 1px dotted;" target="_blank">
          Tumblr page
         </a>
         . Sometimes the kid asks for things, but most of the time he
         <span id="more-34053">
         </span>
         draws whatever he thinks up. After lunch, he starts the work and colors the drawing while watching TV. He generally illustrates cartoon characters which range from Spider Man to Pikachu and from the Calvin and Hobbes to dinosaurs with lightsabers. Wanna see them now? Just scroll down.
