        <p>
         Officers who approached a Rahway woman because she was tossing litter from her car found her carrying 28 packets of heroin, Linden police said today.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div>
         <div>
          Kristen DeCarlo, 22, smelled of marijuana when the officers asked her for identification at 10:45 p.m., Capt James Sarnicki said.
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
          The officers approached DeCarlo because shed tossed something from her 2010 Toyota, parked on the Corner of West Curtis Street and Miltonia Street, he said.
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
          When she opened her purse, the captain said, the officers spotted 28 glassine folds of heroin wrapped in rubber bands.
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
          DeCarlo has aMarch 28court date to answer charges of littering from a vehicle and possession of drugs in her car.
