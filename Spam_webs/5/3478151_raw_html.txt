         <div class="pbody">
          <p>
           <img alt="snoop-dogg-with-cat.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="387" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/snoop-dogg-with-cat.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             Snoop Lion (aka Snoop Dogg) with a cat (Photo:
             <a href="http://www.reviler.org/2012/03/21/snoop-dogg-with-a-cat/">
              Reviler
             </a>
             )
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           Sometimes a picture comes along that must be shared. This shot of Snoop hanging with a feline companion is one of those pictures.
          </p>
          <p>
           Of course the internet (basically a giant cat-photo-generating machine) is full of sites featuring shots like this: humans, both famous and not, spending quality time with cats.
          </p>
          <p>
           Here are a few we really like, starting with, of course, this forum page of rappers with cats, which kicks off with the Snoop pic above:
          </p>
          <p>
           <big>
            <strong>
             5. Rappers With Cats
            </strong>
           </big>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://rapgenius.com/groups/the-forum/discussions/8804-Rappers-with-cats">
            <img alt="cat-with-jay.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="394" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/cat-with-jay.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             Jay Z and cat (Photo: Rap Genius/Martin Schoeller)
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           Tyler the Creator, the Beastie Boys, producer Just Blaze, Jay Z: they've all spent a little quality time with cats.
          </p>
          <p>
           And the evidence was gathered on
           <a href="http://rapgenius.com/groups/the-forum/discussions/8804-Rappers-with-cats">
            this Rap Genius forum
           </a>
           , when user Grendel posted that shot of Snoop, along with one word: "GO!"
          </p>
          <p>
           <big>
            <strong>
             4. Cats With Famous People
            </strong>
           </big>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://catswithfamouspeople.tumblr.com/">
            <img alt="cats-with-famous-people.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="319" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/cats-with-famous-people.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             James Dean and cat (Photo: Cats With Famous People)
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://catswithfamouspeople.tumblr.com/page/2">
            Cats With Famous People
           </a>
           keeps things simple: it's just a long blog roll featuring celebrities with felines.
          </p>
          <p>
           Andy Warhol, Joey Ramone, Sean Connery on the set of Dr. No: a lot of the celebrities featured on the blog are people you'd look at pictures of anyway. But adding a cat sweetens the deal.
          </p>
          <p>
           There are actually a lot of sites on this theme. If you need more, check out
           <a href="http://yourcatwasdelicious.tumblr.com/">
            Almost Famous Cats
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://pinterest.com/gullringsgal/famous-people-and-their-cats/">
            this Pinterest board
           </a>
           , and this list from
           <a href="http://thecatalyst.typepad.com/the_catalyst/famous-people-cats/">
            The Catalyst
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <big>
            <strong>
             Cute Boys With Cats
            </strong>
           </big>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://cuteboyswithcats.net/">
            <img alt="cute-boys-with-cts.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="405" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/cute-boys-with-cts.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             A male human (who is "cute", apparently) and cat (Photo: Cute Boys With Cats)
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           Why does this website exist? According to the masthead, "because there is (was) a serious shortage of pictures of cute boys with cats." So there you have it.
          </p>
          <p>
           If this is your thing, then you will definitely enjoy it. And in case you're wondering, yes, there is a blog called
           <a href="http://cutegirlswithcats.tumblr.com/">
            Cute Girls with Cats
           </a>
           (it's inspired by the boys site, apparently).
          </p>
          <p>
           <big>
            <strong>
             2. Writers And Kitties
            </strong>
           </big>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://writersandkitties.tumblr.com/">
            <img alt="cat-with-william-burroughs.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="435" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/cat-with-william-burroughs.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             William Burroughs and cat (Photo: Writers and Kitties)
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           It seems like a lot of great writers enjoyed the company of cats, as the Tumblr
           <a href="http://writersandkitties.tumblr.com/">
            Writers and Kitties
           </a>
           shows.
          </p>
          <p>
           From Foucault and Sartre to Patti Smith and Louise Erdrich, it's a feast for the eyes of literature lovers and cat lovers alike.
          </p>
          <p>
           <big>
            <strong>
             1. Cats Enjoying Human Food
            </strong>
           </big>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://thechive.com/2010/10/18/cats-like-the-humans-foods-28-photos/">
            <img alt="cats-with-human-food.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="419" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/cats-with-human-food.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             A human enjoying corn with cat (Photo: The Chive)
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           As all these sites prove, cats spend a lot of time with their human servants. Er, friends.
          </p>
          <p>
           So it was only a matter of time before they'd develop a taste for our food.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://thechive.com/2010/10/18/cats-like-the-humans-foods-28-photos/">
            The Chive
           </a>
           has gathered some excellent photographic evidence of cats who prefer their food fit for human consumption.
          </p>
          <p>
           <big>
            <strong>
             Bonus: Cats Sitting Like Humans
            </strong>
           </big>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://catssittinglikehumans.tumblr.com/">
            <img alt="cats-sitting-like-humans.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="406" src="http://www.cbc.ca/strombo/content/images/cats-sitting-like-humans.jpg" style="" width="580"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <small>
            <em>
             A cat, just sitting there like a human (Photo: Cats Sitting Like Humans)
            </em>
           </small>
          </p>
          <p>
           We found lots of pictures of cats with humans online, but if you've spent any time with a feline, you know they also like their alone time.
          </p>
          <p>
           And if a human isn't available to pose with them, some cats are more than capable of stepping up, and sitting down.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://catssittinglikehumans.tumblr.com/">
            Cats Sitting Like Humans
           </a>
           is an entire Tumblr dedicated to cats who are doing it for themselves. No humans required.
          </p>
