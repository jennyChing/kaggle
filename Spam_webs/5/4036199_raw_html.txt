         May 23, 2011
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-just_plain_fun entry-category-look_what_i_found entry-author-susan_borgen entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d8345159f569e201538ea533df970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           Upcycling Inspiration on Etsy
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             As you know, I am a fan of repurposing. A favorite pastime of mine is searching out vintage items and then coming up with fresh, clever uses for my discoveries. Always on the lookout for inspiration, I often turn to
             <a href="http://www.etsy.com/?ref=si_home" target="_self">
              Etsy
             </a>
             to view some of the most creative recycled items around. Here are a few fellow Etsians who not only embrace this design aesthetic, they take it to a new level.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b7c6e11aed970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Il_fullxfull.125221301" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201b7c6e11aed970b image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b7c6e11aed970b-800wi" title="Il_fullxfull.125221301"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             From tables to pet beds, Sherri's shop,
             <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/lovenostalgicwhimsy/sold" target="_self">
              Love Nostalgic Whimsy,
             </a>
             is a testament to what you can do with old luggage. So fun! Where other people may see worthless junk, Sherri sees everything as something.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15a4970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Lovable Luggage Pet Bunk Bed - Upcycled Suitcases, Reclaimed Softballs - 2 dollars goes to TLC CAT RESCUE" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15a4970c image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15a4970c-800wi" title="Lovable Luggage Pet Bunk Bed - Upcycled Suitcases, Reclaimed Softballs - 2 dollars goes to TLC CAT RESCUE"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Jessica, of
             <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/DIYStudio?ref=seller_info" target="_self">
              DIY Studio
             </a>
             , constructs whimsical assemblages she calls
             <em>
              Artbots
             </em>
             . Made with found junk and 100% upcycled, these conversation pieces are loaded with personality.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17102d970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Artbot, Assemblage Robot, Belinda Butterfly, upcycled artbot created by Jessica Ramey" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17102d970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17102d970d-800wi" title="Artbot, Assemblage Robot, Belinda Butterfly, upcycled artbot created by Jessica Ramey"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Jessica puts this "disclaimer" on her Artbot listings:
             <em>
              Operate only under the influence of imagination!
             </em>
             (Love that!)
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17103b970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Artbot, Assemblage Robot, Baby Bubble Bee, upcycled artbot created by Jessica Ramey" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17103b970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17103b970d-800wi" title="Artbot, Assemblage Robot, Baby Bubble Bee, upcycled artbot created by Jessica Ramey"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Douglas, of
             <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/darkwaterengineworks?ref=seller_info" target="_self">
              Dark Water Engine Works
             </a>
             , is an eco-conscious artist. When possible, he has made it his personal policy to remove trash from nature areas. Douglas then uses these finds in his unique creations, like his
             <em>
              Kicked Can Clock
             </em>
             below, made from a repurposed steel can he found along a bike path.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15c8970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Il_fullxfull.208731739" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15c8970c image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15c8970c-800wi" title="Il_fullxfull.208731739"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Douglas's brilliant arrangement, called
             <em>
              Power Flower
             </em>
             , consists of repurposed electrical plugs, cords, electrical tape leaves and wire resting in a salvaged glass vase.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e171048970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Power Flowers" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201a73e171048970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e171048970d-800wi" title="Power Flowers"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Heather's shop, called
             <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/ReaDo?ref=seller_info" target="_self">
              ReaDo
             </a>
             , has the subhead
             <em>
              Repurposing Inspired by Literature
             </em>
             . She transforms vintage books and buttons into unique, one-of-a-kind purses.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e171054970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="How to Cope with Cope - Repurposed Book Purse" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201a73e171054970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e171054970d-800wi" title="How to Cope with Cope - Repurposed Book Purse"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             So clever. And just look how pretty the inside is, too!
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b7c6e11b37970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="How to Cope with Cope - Repurposed Book Purse-1" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201b7c6e11b37970b image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b7c6e11b37970b-800wi" title="How to Cope with Cope - Repurposed Book Purse-1"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Jeff and Mark, of
             <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/BootsNGus?ref=pr_shop" target="_self">
              BootsNGus
             </a>
             , have a shop where "handmade and vintage come together." Look at these recycled Bundt cake pans! With the addition of Snap-in Socket cords and standard night light bulbs, they have created this sweet pair of
             <em>
              Wall Flowers
             </em>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17105c970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="UpCycled ReCycled Bright Orange and Red Colored Bundt Cake Wall Flower Hanging Lit Art" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17105c970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201a73e17105c970d-800wi" title="UpCycled ReCycled Bright Orange and Red Colored Bundt Cake Wall Flower Hanging Lit Art"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Perfect for the kitchen, Jeff and Mark have transformed a matching set of three vintage Pyrex mixing bowls into reclaimed pendant lights. I love their warm glow.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15f4970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Summer Sunlight Butterfly Gold Pattern Pyrex Pendant Lights - UpCycled ReCycled Hanging Lighting Fixture - Set of 3 Reclaimed Mixing Bowl Light" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15f4970c image-full img-responsive" src="http://tparty.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8345159f569e201b8d06b15f4970c-800wi" title="Summer Sunlight Butterfly Gold Pattern Pyrex Pendant Lights - UpCycled ReCycled Hanging Lighting Fixture - Set of 3 Reclaimed Mixing Bowl Light"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             These Etsy sellers are masters of working old objects into new contexts. I hope you have enjoyed this peek at their inventive creations.
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              Susan
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
