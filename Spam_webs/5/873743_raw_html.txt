        <h1 class="main-title post-view-title">
         The One Thing You Must Do Today To Save The Planet
        </h1>
        <div class="blog-post-details blog-post-details-header">
         Written by Sebrina Zerkus Smith
.
         <span class="blog-post-date">
          Jul 06, 2012
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="clearer">
        </div>
        <div class="blog-post-abstract">
         Three minutes is all you need to make a difference.
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="blog-post-content">
        <link href="http://www.recyclebank.com/media/blog_thumbnails/thumbnails/proper-green-100.jpg" rel="image_src"/>
        <p>
         <img src="http://greenopolis.com/files/images/one-thing-to-save-planet-615.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          According to stats compiled by
          <a href="http://www.oberlin.edu/recycle/">
           Oberlin College
          </a>
          , if everyone did this one simple thing, it could keep 250 million pounds of recyclables out of the waste stream.
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Stop, right now -- and go clean out your car.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         You read right. Just three minutes is all you need to properly dispose of all the wrappers, soda cans and plastic water bottles that are tumbling around in your floorboard.
        </p>
        <p>
         I know it sounds like a small thing, but wouldnt you normally handle this nuisance chore while youre filling up your car or stopping at a convenience store for a cold drink? In the absence of a proper recycling bin, where does all that trash end up?
        </p>
        <p>
         Get it, now?
        </p>
        <p>
         By thoughtfully disposing of these recyclable items, youll keep them out of our already clogged landfills.
        </p>
        <p>
         Three minutes is all you need to make a difference.
        </p>
        <p>
         Isnt the planet worth three minutes of your time?
        </p>
