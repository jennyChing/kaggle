           <span style="font-size: small; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
            SmokeTip is an e-cigarette company that appears to genuinely sympathize with smokers and are eager to find a way to help them kick the habit. They claim that their electronic cigarette range is more advanced than any of their rivals. Like an increasing number of companies, SmokeTip have incorporated a 2-piece e-cig design in a bid to make their product more attractive and convenient to smokers who dont like having to assemble before they smoke. The company is also so confident in their ability to deliver that their electronic cigarettes carry a lifetime warranty.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              How Does SmokeTip Work?
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-size: small; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
            Instead of relying on combustion to release the nicotine and thus be the victim of numerous bans, SmokeTip e-cigarettes emit vaporized nicotine which is released by a battery operated atomizer. The second you inhale, the e-cigarette sensors help release the ideal amount of nicotine every time. Their Eazy-Drag System allows for better air circulation which makes the process of smoking effortless.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Products &amp; Prices
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-size: small; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
            SmokeTip keep things uncomplicated and only have one single S
            <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
             tarter Kit
            </strong>
            priced at $59.95. Each kit contains 2 batteries, 5 cartridges, a wall adapter, a USB charger and gift box packaging. SmokeTip claims that both batteries last for life and have backed up this statement with a lifetime guarantee. In addition, you receive a free e-cigarette battery every time you order 3 x 10-packs of cartridges. 10-pack cartridges cost $19.95 each with discounts for bulk orders.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-size: small; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
            Their D
            <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
             isposable Electronic Cigarette
            </strong>
            is $9.95 and lasts for approximately 500 puffs. It comes in three flavors (regular, cowboy and menthol) and two strengths (6mg and 16mg). Their electronic cigars are $19.95 each and come in Cuban or Havana flavors. One of these disposable cigars last for 1,000 puffs which is the equivalent of 12 cigars. The Cuban cigar flavor has a full flavor while the Havana option is milder and sweeter though both contain 18mg of nicotine.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Accessories
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-size: small; background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
            SmokeTip do have a lengthy accessory list including a 350mAh battery ($24.95) which is almost double the strength of the normal battery. The travel charger case ($18.95) allows you to wirelessly charge your batteries and it can hold one battery and two cartridges. The SmokeTip cleaning kit is $6.95 and the lanyard is for $8.95. Other accessories include a USB charger, USB pass-through, batteries and carry cases. SmokeTip claims to offer the lowest prices of any electronic cigarette company online. If you find cheaper products anywhere else, they will refund the difference.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              General Information
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: .25in; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             ?
            </span>
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Flavors:
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             There are a total of 19 flavors including apple, cherry, grape, peach and pineapple.
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: .25in; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             ?
            </span>
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Strength:
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             0mg nicotine, Ultra Light 6mg, Light 12mg and Full Flavor 16mg.
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: .25in; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             ?
            </span>
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Shipping:
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             Orders over $59 have free shipping and will arrive in 1-3 business days. Orders under $59 arrive in 2-6 business days. All orders are processed within 24 hours of payment.
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: .25in; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             ?
            </span>
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Potency:
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             Each cartridge offers 250 puffs and is equal to 1.25 packs of cigarettes.
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <strong>
             <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
              Verdict
             </span>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="MsoNormal">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva; font-size: small;">
            <span style="background: none repeat scroll 0% 0% yellow;">
             SmokeTip really is on a mission to simplify the electronic cigarette industry. They dont offer a range of kits with different accessories. They believe that theyve perfected the starter kit and offer just one choice. Each cartridge comes with an in-built atomizer for convenience and the company claims to offer 24/7 phone support. Where SmokeTip really stand out is with their warranties and guarantees. There is no equivalent starter kit available for less and they offer a lifetime warranty on their starter kit which is almost unheard of in the industry. The products they sell are high quality and as a result, SmokeTip gains a well-earned seal of approval.
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
