     <h1 class="entry-title">
      The work of Katie Kirk
     </h1>
     <!--BEGIN .entry-meta .entry-header-->
     <div class="entry-meta entry-header">
      <span class="published">
       June 22, 2010
      </span>
      <span class="meta-sep">
       /
      </span>
      <span class="comment-count">
       <a href="http://blog.signalnoise.com/2010/06/22/the-work-of-katie-kirk/#comments" title="Comment on The work of Katie Kirk">
        2 Comments
       </a>
      </span>
      <!--END .entry-meta entry-header -->
     </div>
     <!--BEGIN .entry-content -->
     <div class="entry-content">
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3211" height="471" src="http://blog.signalnoise.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/i_katiekirk1.jpg" title="Katie Kirk" width="585"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3212" height="196" src="http://blog.signalnoise.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/i_katiekirk2.jpg" title="Katie Kirk" width="585"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3213" height="446" src="http://blog.signalnoise.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/i_katiekirk3.jpg" title="Katie Kirk" width="585"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3214" height="763" src="http://blog.signalnoise.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/i_katiekirk4.jpg" title="Katie Kirk" width="585"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3215" height="468" src="http://blog.signalnoise.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/i_katiekirk5.jpg" title="Katie Kirk" width="585"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3216" height="448" src="http://blog.signalnoise.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/i_katiekirk6.jpg" title="Katie Kirk" width="585"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Really digging the clean and stylized work by Minneapolis-based illustrator
       <a href="http://www.katiekirk.net/" title="Katie Kirk">
        Katie Kirk
       </a>
       . I stumbled upon her
       <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/katiekirk/" title="Katie Kirk on Flickr">
        Flickr stream
       </a>
       quite by accident last week and spent a great deal of time flipping through her portfolio. Whats really interesting is seeing the progression of her work, from her earliest to recent. She has an obvious eye for style, and instead of adding more things to her illustrations, its as if she spent time taking things away which results in a more pure form.
      </p>
      <p>
       In a time when it seems
       <em>
        more is more
       </em>
       , its refreshing to find calming work like Katies out there in the wilderness. Check out her
       <a href="http://www.katiekirk.net/" title="Katie Kirk">
        official site
       </a>
       , her
       <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/katiekirk/" title="Katie Kirk on Flickr">
        Flickr stream
       </a>
       and drop by her
       <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/eighthourday" title="Katie Kirk on Etsy">
        Etsy shop
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <!--END .entry-content -->
