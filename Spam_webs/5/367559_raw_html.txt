       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Awesome Tops Sewing Patterns and Inspiration and the Return of Sew Our Stash
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-05-04T01:40:00+00:00">
         May 4, 2015
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.raegunramblings.com%2F2015%2F05%2Fawesome-tops-sewing-patterns-inspiration.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.raegunramblings.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F05%2FSOS-Tops-collage-square.jpg&amp;description=Awesome%20Tops%20Sewing%20Patterns%20and%20Inspiration%20and%20the%20Return%20of%20Sew%20Our%20Stash" rel="nobox">

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.raegunramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/SOS-Tops-collage-square.jpg">
          <img alt="12 great top pattern, tutorials and inspiration to sew for women" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14592" height="747" src="http://www.raegunramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/SOS-Tops-collage-square.jpg" width="746"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Arent all these womens tops sewing patterns and inspiration amazing?! I need new clothes like crazy but I am SO not feeling the styles and fits in the stores this season. Maybe its my post baby body combined with some ready to wear duds but Ive know for a while that if Ive going to get new clothes that I like Im gonna have to make some.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.raegunramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Sew-Our-Stash-2015-01-01.jpg">
          <img alt="This looks like fun. Great way to get more sewn for myself. Sew Our Stash" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14590" height="400" src="http://www.raegunramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Sew-Our-Stash-2015-01-01.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So in totally related news I have a super exciting announcement! Now that Teddy is sleeping a bit (knock on wood right) and
         <a href="http://www.flamingotoes.com/" target="_blank">
          Flamingo Toes
         </a>
         Bev is almost done with her book (I cant wait!!!) were bring back
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.raegunramblings.com/category/sew-our-stash" target="_blank">
           Sew Our Stash
          </a>
         </strong>
         . Yaaaaayyyy!!!!!!! If SOS is brand new to you what we do is commit to sew upup at least one new item for ourselves. If we do more than that awesome but our goal is at least 1.
         <a href="http://www.raegunramblings.com/2013/06/sew-our-stash-challenge.html" target="_blank">
          Bev and I started this
         </a>
         as a way to help ussew more for ourselves and use the fabric in our stash but sometimes a girls just gotta go to the fabric store right? ;)
        </p>
        <p>
         As were planning, making things all month long (and then wearing those things later) we like to share pictures on
         <a href="https://instagram.com/raegunramblings/" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         using #sewourstash and we would love to invite you to join. All you have to do is sew for yourself! The last Thursday of every month is Sew Our Stash day where Bev and I show what we made that month. If you blog and want to write a post feel free if you just want to share pictures on IG thats great too. Well be going through the hashtag every month and sharing some of our favorites.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.raegunramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/SOS-Tops-Tall-collage.jpg">
          <img alt="Must make all of these. Great list of sewing patterns and inspiration for women tops and shirts. " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14593" height="1637" src="http://www.raegunramblings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/SOS-Tops-Tall-collage.jpg" width="746"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          May our theme is Tops. As a bit of inspiration we have each rounded up some of our favorite DIY shirts for women. Here are my personal pics and make sure to hop over to see all the
          <a href="http://www.flamingotoes.com/2015/05/15-fabulous-tops-to-sew/" target="_blank">
           Fabulous Tops to Sew
          </a>
          that Bev found!
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://seekatesew.com/new-pdf-pattern-the-soho-blouse/" target="_blank">
           Soho Blouse
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>

           Easy Flutter Tee Tutorial

         <li>

           Button Back Boatneck Pattern Hack

         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.anthropologie.com/anthro/product/clothes-blouse/4110030216466.jsp#/" target="_blank">
           Manon Kimono Top
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://grainlinestudio.com/2013/08/05/hemlock-tee-pattern-its-free" target="_blank">
           Hemlock Tee Free Pattern
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.victorypatterns.com/products/roxanne" target="_blank">
           Roxanne Top
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://lainglesita.blogspot.com.es/2012/03/mi-segunda-blusa-para-el-spring-top.html" target="_blank">
           Sorbetto Top
          </a>
          sewn by la Inglesita(free pattern by
          <a href="https://www.colettepatterns.com/sewing/sorbetto" target="_blank">

          )
         </li>
         <li>

         </li>
         <li>

           Fun Summer Tee Tutorial

          <a href="http://www.sewmuchado.com/2013/05/the-seafarer-top-pattern.html" target="_blank">
           Seafarer Top

        </ul>
        <p>
         I hope youll join us as we Sew Our Stash. Here are the themes for the rest of the year! Happy sewing and dont forget to #sewourstash on Instagram.
        </p>
