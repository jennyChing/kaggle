      <h1>
       A Crisis In 2014 China Trust Industry
      </h1>
      <div class="blank5">
      </div>
      <div class="infor">
       <span>
        From: Qianzhan Business Information
       </span>
       <span>
        Time: 2014-02-10 10:32:26
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="blank5">
      </div>
      <div class="summary">
       <strong>
        Summary:
       </strong>
       It is revealed that, Xinhua Trust's product - the real estate investment project will face early maturity default. Product debtor has been missing, involving a total amount of 850 million yuan. Recently, a CCT scaled 3,000,000,000 class mineral collection trusts faced deadline, and the financiers went behind bars.
      </div>
      <div class="art_cont">
       <p style="text-align:justify">
        It is revealed that,  Xinhua Trust's product - the real estate investment project will face  early maturity default. Product debtor has been missing, involving a  total amount of 850 million yuan. Recently, a CCT scaled 3,000,000,000  class mineral collection trusts faced deadline, and the financiers went  behind bars.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="China Trust Industry" height="546" src="http://img3.qianzhan123.com/news/201402/10/20140210-60baeb75ea62d6f1.jpg" width="486"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align:justify">
        <a href="http://en.qianzhan.com" target="_blank">
         Forward Intelligence
        </a>
        statistics show that  currently the total amount of trust subsisting products reached over 11  trillion yuan, the size of two-year increments only issued in 2012 and  2013 have been nearly 10 trillion yuan. According to a conservative  projection with one third maturity, the total amount due in 2014 has  more than 3.6 trillion yuan of trust products, including the amount of  real estate projects due in about 970 billion yuan, which is a quarter  of all maturity trust products. With the national housing estate  information networking and the introduction of real estate taxes, the  housing market supply and demand situation could be reversed at any  time, and the impact on the future prospects of the booming commercial  real estate to generate e-commerce is not yet determined. In this  situation, cash flow real estate trust projects in jeopardy in 2014.
       </p>
      </div>
