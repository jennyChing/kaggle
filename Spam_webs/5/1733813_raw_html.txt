           <div class="s-element-content s-text emojify">
            The economy of Kolkata is in full swing. Moreover, the rapid industrialization post independence has ignited the growth of the city. Currently, Kolkata is the main business, commercial and finance hub in the eastern India. This article is written to give an account of the banking sector and available finance and accounting jobs for freshers in Kolkata.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            If you have just completed your finance education and looking for a good job, then Kolkata is the right city to kick start your career. There is no dearth of good accounting jobs in the city, which is an imperative center for banking and finance. At present, it is headquarters of three large public sector banks- Allahabad Bank, UCO Bank and United Bank of India. Furthermore, various large financial and insurance companies are also headquartered in Kolkata, where hundreds of people are employed. The massive growth in the finance sector has produced a good number of opportunities for people and therefore, there is always a demand of people who have good experience and knowledge in the financial sector.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Many Indian banks, multi-national banks and the World Bank have also opened their branch offices in the city. Similarly, big financial banks like Standard Chartered Bank, Bank of America and HSBC Bank have also opened their offices in Kolkata.

            <br/>
            <br/>
            Recently, the Bandhan Financial, the largest Microfinance Group in India from Kolkata and the 2nd largest of its kind in the World has got permission from the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) to open banks throughout the country. The Bandhan Bank will be having its headquarter in the city.
            <br/>
            Indeed, Kolkata is the financial capital of East India.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The state government has set up the financial hub at Rajarhat on the outskirts of Kolkata, the second finance hub in India after Bandra Kurla in Mumbai. Vesting over 300 acres, the finance hub will be housing various banking and financial institutions, stock exchanges and insurance companies, apart from educational &amp; hospitality industries. Various nationalized banking &amp; financial institutions have decided to open their offices in the hub, including State Bank of India, UCO Bank, United Bank, UTI Mutual Fund and Industrial Financial Corporation of India.The government is hopeful that the project would generate around 20,000 banking and

             accounting jobs for freshers in Kolkata
            </a>
            . Even, as per the report of the leading job portal, Kolkata had witnessed 8% increase in the hiring trend in November 2014. For those who are interested in building their career in the banking sector, should surely explore the career choices which are available in this eastern part of the country.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            It has been seen that accounting jobs are available across wide markets. However, candidates who want to work in this domain should be trained enough before they can apply for finance jobs. Moreover, computer skills would play an indispensable role in this domain. Gone are those days, when manual transactions were carried out in the log books. Now in the current scenario, candidates are required to achieve great expertise in one of these softwares.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            In Kolkata, accounting jobs are available in industries, CA firms, educational institutions, event management companies and other organizations. Although, event management company is a new concept, but there is a great demand of accountants who can help in expenses. Moreover, accounting jobs at educational institutions are created for handling the fees/expenses in terms of salaries, etc.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <b>
             How much can you earn in Kolkata?
             <br/>
            </b>
            <br/>
            On an average, an accountant in Kolkata can earn Rs 178,657/year. The highest paying skills associated with the accounting job for freshers in Kolkata are accounts receivable, sales tax reports, accounts payable, microsoft office and account management.
