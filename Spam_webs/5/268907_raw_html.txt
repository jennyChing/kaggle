       <h2>
        Home Reports for properties in and around Edinburgh
       </h2>
       <h2>
        <span style="font-size: 10px;">
         If you have property to sell in Edinburgh, Harvey, Donaldson &amp; Gibson can help get your property ready for sale by carrying out a Home Report on your behalf. Our Edinburgh office has a team of Chartered Surveyors who have the expertise to carry out a speedy and compliant Home Report in your area.
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        With vast expertise in both energy assessments and single surveys, our flexible approach allows you to request a report at a time that suits you at a competitive price. If your property is based outside of the Edinburgh area, our surveyors can still help - have a look below for the areas where we regularly provide Home Reports .
       </p>
       <p>
        If you have a home to sell in Edinburgh, you are well positioned to take advantage of a very positive and resilient property market. The average asking price in Edinburgh in 2011 was approximately 225,000, considerably higher than the overall Scottish average of 117,675. There are a wide variety of property types in the area ranging from beautiful historic town houses to luxury contemporary apartments. Affordability in Edinburgh in 2010 stood at around 8 times average earnings. This reflects the huge popularity of the area and its importance as Scotland's Capital City.
       </p>
       <p>
        As Scotland's largest city, Edinburgh has been the country's capital since 1437. It has a population of around 450,000 and among other things, the city is famous for its magnificent castle, medieval buildings, gothic churches, amazing galleries, a thriving nightlife and numerous festivals.
       </p>
       <p>
        Edinburgh is more expensive than other cities in Scotland and but property is at premium with just cause. The city is a fabulous place to live and sellers should have no problem in finding buyers keen to soak up the diverse culture of such an important city.
       </p>
       <p>
        Commission a Home Report from Harvey, Donaldson &amp; Gibson to begin your journey to a successful sale in Edinburgh today. Our Edinburgh team provide Home Reports in West Lothian, Mid Lothian, East Lothian and the Borders.
       </p>
