                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Recipe: Homemade enchilada sauce
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2549752951770001443" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      Hello!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      I've had several people ask for my recipe for my enchilada sauce. I use it in several dishes...chilaquiles casserole, scramble eggs, soup, quesadillas and more. I consider it a little on the time consuming side but that may just be my laziness shining through. LOL Enjoy!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        Tools needed:
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       large pot
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       food processor or blender
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       large bowl
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       fine mesh colander
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       wooden spoon
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ZwAA8_40E5k/UtmVP5dU_0I/AAAAAAAAAP0/KqKBEm0ZsYE/s1600/DSC06806+(2).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ZwAA8_40E5k/UtmVP5dU_0I/AAAAAAAAAP0/KqKBEm0ZsYE/s1600/DSC06806+(2).jpg" width="320"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        Ingredients:
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Dried red chillie peppers
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       tomatoes 1 large or 2 Roma
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       onions 1 small onion
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       garlic 5 or 6 cloves
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       salt to taste
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       water
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://images-blogger-opensocial.googleusercontent.com/gadgets/proxy?url=http%3A%2F%2F2.bp.blogspot.com%2F-lIVU9NNAif8%2FUtmVS7ZNBbI%2FAAAAAAAAAQc%2F252ii5465Dw%2Fs1600%2FDSC06809%2B(2).jpg&amp;container=blogger&amp;gadget=a&amp;rewriteMime=image%2F*" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="240" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-lIVU9NNAif8/UtmVS7ZNBbI/AAAAAAAAAQc/252ii5465Dw/s1600/DSC06809+(2).jpg" width="320"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        Instructions:
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Step 1: Grab a couple of large handfuls of dried red peppers and place into pot. Add tomatoes to the pot. Fill pot with water. Cover. Bring to a boil. Lower heat to medium.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-I_8_DzZptW4/UtmVQrje0mI/AAAAAAAAAQI/zwRMRvfvBIc/s1600/DSC06811+(2).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-I_8_DzZptW4/UtmVQrje0mI/AAAAAAAAAQI/zwRMRvfvBIc/s1600/DSC06811+(2).jpg" width="320"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Step 2: Cook till peppers are re hydrated and tomatoes are soft. While peppers and tomatoes are boiling, chop onion and garlic.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-C_Sg-cp7wiM/UtmVQ7lPg4I/AAAAAAAAAQE/WKajZzp-nb8/s1600/DSC06812+(2).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-C_Sg-cp7wiM/UtmVQ7lPg4I/AAAAAAAAAQE/WKajZzp-nb8/s1600/DSC06812+(2).jpg" width="240"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Step 3: Fill blender or food processor 1/2 to 3/4 full with peppers, tomatoes, onions and garlic. Add a couple of cups of the liquid from pot to the blender. Puree. *Remember this is hot. Please use caution*
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-U0DbDT6eHJQ/UtmVR1x9KGI/AAAAAAAAAQQ/I2RpVzASOVM/s1600/DSC06813+(2).jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="240" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-U0DbDT6eHJQ/UtmVR1x9KGI/AAAAAAAAAQQ/I2RpVzASOVM/s1600/DSC06813+(2).jpg" width="320"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Step 4: Get out a large bowl. Put a fine mesh colander on bowl. Pour pureed sauce into mesh colander. Take wooden spoon and stir sauce that is in the colander. Work out the liquid.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-QabV9HVby0A/UtmVSFWbXsI/AAAAAAAAAQk/LY2ET8htIRs/s1600/DSC06816.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="240" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-QabV9HVby0A/UtmVSFWbXsI/AAAAAAAAAQk/LY2ET8htIRs/s1600/DSC06816.JPG" width="320"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Step 5: What is left in the colander is bits of seeds, skin, etc that you do not want in your sauce. Toss that in the trash. Continue same process with the rest of your veggies.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-LCcap8X5gXk/UtmVS35be2I/AAAAAAAAAQg/wpGZkS7Kguc/s1600/DSC06822.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <img border="0" height="240" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-LCcap8X5gXk/UtmVS35be2I/AAAAAAAAAQg/wpGZkS7Kguc/s1600/DSC06822.JPG" width="320"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Salt to taste. Place in jars and refrigerate or use right away in your next meal.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
