        <h1 class="entry-title">
         crochet uno dress
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn" href="http://whipup.net/author/kath_red/">
           kath_red
          </a>
         </span>
         on
         <abbr class="published" title="2008-02-20">
          20/02/2008
         </abbr>
        </p>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         in
         <span>
          <a href="http://whipup.net/category/knitting-crochet-yarn/" rel="category tag">
           knitting+crochet+yarn
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p>
         Part of our alt craft month we will be bringing you ideas, inspiration and patterns that might be considered alternative crafting that is crafts that fit within:
         <em>
          Making unconventional choices (materials + methods); taking different approaches to the problem, not conforming to accepted rules or standards; out of the ordinary and unexpected; slightly odd or even a bit weird.
         </em>
         if you have some ideas or want to contribute email me whipup[at]gmail.com
        </p>
        <p>
         this crochet uno dress won the Craftster Total Board Game Re-vamp Challenge, by
         <a href=" http://kittyscreations.wordpress.com/">
          kitty creates 
         </a>
         there is no pattern but she explains how she made it at
         <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/">
          ravelry.
         </a>
         <em>
          First, I hole punched the Uno cards (invest in a good hole puncher for this, or your hands will hurt). Then I made half double crochets around the edges, making 3 in each corner, then joining and making another row of hdcs. 
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" height="500" src="http://whipup.net/wp-content/images/unodress.jpg" title="" width="332"/>
        </p>
