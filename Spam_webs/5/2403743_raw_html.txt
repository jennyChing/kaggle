                    </h3>

                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4384404054052699470" itemprop="description articleBody">

Sure I froze, and quite literally screamed at the wind one day while on my way to meet a friend at the Met...But in keeping in contact with my NYC friends via Snapchat and Instagram, I miss the city with a strangeness I don't quite recognize. I imagine New York in the summertime is even more vibrant than I witnessed in the winter; With warm and sticky evenings, and bright muggy days, I bet New York is bursting with even more color under the hazy season of summer.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     As mentioned earlier on my
                     <a href="http://www.instagram.com/amusedblog" target="_blank">
                      Instagram
                     </a>
                     , now that I have an office job I am trying to encourperate more office-friendly attire here on A Mused. Living in California my work atmosphere has always been relaxed. Denim goes down pretty easy here. For me this would be a perfect summer office outfit that would effortlessly take me from the office, to lunch meetings, to meeting my photographer in the evening for a blog shoot.
