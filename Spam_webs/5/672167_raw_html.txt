         Burberry Prorsum Pre-Fall 2014
        </h1>

        <p>
         <img alt="Burberry Prorsum Pre-Fall 2014. Image courtesy of Burberry Prorsum." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-109458" height="749" src="http://www.glamourgoddessfashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/BurberryProrsum141.jpg?bffdfb" width="500"/>
         Christopher Bailey chose a soft color palette with dominating colors of dark green, brown, navy, and wine. The designer played with the contrast of volumes like bigger pieces on top combined with pencil skirts, light chiffon dresses, and so on. The t-shirts were printed with black-and-white drawings of St. Pauls Cathedral in London, the Chrysler building in New York, the Forbidden City in Beijing, and etc. Burberry Prorsum collection carried cozy outerwear: coats with geometric patterns, short fur jackets with voluminous sleeves, and large knitting sweaters. Modern urban elements were present in the clothing. For accessories, Christopher Bailey showed ankle boots with open toes and lacing, sandals and bags with short handles. The lineup for next pre-fall season was young, feminine, and contemporary.
