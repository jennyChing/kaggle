     <p>
      This list contains the names of
      <b>
       albums that contain a
       <a href="/wiki/Hidden_track" title="Hidden track">
        hidden track
       </a>
      </b>
      and also information on how to find them. Not all printings of an album contain the same track arrangements, so some copies of a particular album may not have the hidden track(s) listed below. Some of these tracks may be hidden in the
      <a href="/wiki/Pregap" title="Pregap">
       pregap
      </a>
      , and some hidden simply as a track following the listed tracks. The list is ordered by artist name using the surname where appropriate.
     </p>
