        <h1 class="single-title post-title" itemprop="headline">
         7 U.S. Vacation Destinations You Will Never Want to Leave
        </h1>
        <p class="meta">
         <time datetime="2013-08-21">
          08/21/2013
         </time>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.eventsbooking.net/author/jdaly_22/" rel="author" title="Posts by JDaly_22">
          JDaly_22
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.eventsbooking.net/7-u-s-vacation-destinations-leave/#comments" title="Comment on 7 U.S. Vacation Destinations You Will Never Want to Leave">
          1 Comment
         </a>
        </p>
       </header>
       <!-- end article header -->
       <section class="post_content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         The U.S. is full of amazing travel destinations. Whether youre into art, dining, or the great outdoors, theres a virtually endless number of places for you to plan an exciting getaway. Here are seven awesome vacation destinations that youll never want to leave.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         Washington D.C.
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <img alt="" src="http://farm1.staticflickr.com/26/44210501_62d9a4b5a8_n.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <em>
          Image via Flickr by DUCKofD3ATH
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Washington D.C. is a fascinating vacation spot for anyone whos interested in American history and government. Besides being the center of national politics, it also contains many of the famous buildings that have shaped the country over the years.
        </p>
        <p>
         D.C. has no shortage of historical relics: the Washington Monument, Jefferson Memorial, and Lincoln Memorial are a few of the most popular tourist destinations. But aside from these sites, you can also take a stroll through the Natural History Museum, or catch a live show at the Kennedy Center. These attractions make D.C. an entertaining destination for history fanatics and tourists alike.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         San Francisco, California
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/jeffgunn/6663212147/" title="San Francisco by jeffgunn, on Flickr">
          <img alt="San Francisco" height="240" src="http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7161/6663212147_53a1a87a10_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Image via Flickr by jeffgunn
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         For anyone with a wacky, expressive, and fun-loving attitude,
         <a href="http://www.eventsbooking.net/gladness-san-francisco-tour/">
          San Francisco
         </a>
         is the place to be. Its a hot spot for anyone interested in art, or even for those just looking to explore some unique cultures and neighborhoods.
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the most popular attractions in San Francisco is the Fishermans Wharf, from which you can view the Golden Gate Bridge and the famous Alcatraz prison. Anyone looking for a cultural experience will also find something to love in its many districts, such as the Mission district, the Haight, and the Castro.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         New York City, New York
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/gee01/118969477/" title="times square by _gee_, on Flickr">
          <img alt="times square" height="157" src="http://farm1.staticflickr.com/46/118969477_5a83f5f086_m.jpg" width="240"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Image via Flickr by _gee_
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         No matter what youre into, theres something for you to do in New York City. From amazing nightlife to Central Park, the Big Apple is the one-size-fits-all destination for travelers.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are many attractions in New York City, which include Times Square, Central Park, and Broadway. Visitors can also enjoy some of the countrys best shopping on Fifth Avenue, or check out museums such as the Metropolitan Museum of Art and the Museum of Modern Art.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         Austin, Texas
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/stuseeger/4895843907/" title="Austin Texas Lake Front by StuSeeger, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Austin Texas Lake Front" height="240" src="http://farm5.staticflickr.com/4102/4895843907_19076b1191_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Image via Flickr by annainaustin
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         When it comes to live music, nobody messes with Texas. Austin is known as Live Music Capital of the World, and contains some of the most famous music venues in the country. Of course, the city also has a friendly, colorful culture, which may explain why so many people make their
         <a href="http://www.taylormorrison.com/new-homes/texas/austin" target="_blank">
          homes in Austin
         </a>
         and other surrounding areas.
        </p>
        <p>
         However, you dont have to live in Austin to enjoy what the city has to offer. From Zilker Park, to 6th Street and the Blanton Museum of Art, theres an endless array of activities for almost anyone visiting from out of town.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         Las Vegas, Nevada
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/30478819@N08/6337511396/" title="Las Vegas Strip at Night by wuestenigel, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Las Vegas Strip at Night" height="213" src="http://farm7.staticflickr.com/6238/6337511396_7ecf17dd85_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Image via Flickr by wuestenigel
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Las Vegas is the ultimate escape from reality, and the perfect destination for gambling, clubbing, and other nightlife. Its known as Sin City for a reason, and many visitors find Vegas as overwhelming as it is enticing.
        </p>
        <p>
         The obvious thing to do in Vegas is hit the casinos. Whether youre trying your luck at the Bellagio, the Venetian, or the Mirage, there are a million different places to gamble. However, Vegas isnt only for gambling, but also offers amazing live entertainers, such as Cirque du Soleil and the Blue Man Group.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         New Orleans, Louisiana
        </h2>
        <p>
         New Orleans is a place unlike anywhere else in the country. Its unique blend of European and Caribbean cultures makes it a fascinating destination for partying, jazz music, and succulent Creole cuisine.
        </p>
        <p>
         Most people who visit New Orleans spend much of their time in the French Quarter, but the city has many other attractions to offer. Museums such as the National World War II Museum and the New Orleans Museum of Art are also options, as are the Garden District and City Park.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center">
         Seattle, Washington
        </h2>
        <p>
         Just because Seattle is known for its rainy weather doesnt mean its not an outstanding vacation destination. For anyone into trendy cafs, independent bookstores, and artistic culture in general, there are few better places to visit in the country.
        </p>
        <p>
         No visit to Seattle is complete without checking out the Olympic Sculpture Park, or doing some shopping at the Pike Place Market. Many people also enjoy the Seattle Art Museum, the Museum of Flight, and, on sunny days, the 400-acre Discovery Park. Consider scheduling your Seattle vacation during the summer months, when the warm weather makes Washington one of the most beautiful destinations in the country.
        </p>
        <p>
         With all of the great vacation spots in the U.S., deciding where to travel can be overwhelming. But rest assured, choose one of these seven vacation destinations and youll never want to leave.
        </p>
