      <h2 style="text-align: center;">
       PADI 5 Star IDC Training Center
      </h2>
      <h2 style="text-align: center;">
       Serving the Greater Phoenix Area
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Screen Shot 2013-03-10 at 1.20.50 PM" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-195" height="210" src="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Screen-Shot-2013-03-10-at-1.20.50-PM.png" width="480"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/gopro_padi-com_flashgraphic_june.jpg">
        <img alt="IDC Instructor Development Course" class="aligncenter wp-image-835" height="210" src="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/gopro_padi-com_flashgraphic_june-300x181.jpg" title="Go Pro" width="480"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Already certified? Thinking of making scuba diving a career? Go Pro! We have an IDC class starting July 23rd.
       <a href="http://thescubashop.org/scubaclasses/instructor-development/">
        Click here!
       </a>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Every week at The Scuba Shop, we offer a full range of scuba and snorkeling classes to the general public.
      </p>
      <p>
       Whether you are looking to polish your diving skills or just beginning your underwater adventure  the possibilities are endless.
      </p>
      <p>
       As an authorized
       <a href="https://www.padi.com/" target="_blank">
        PADI
       </a>
       5 Star IDC Scuba Training Center, we offer a wide range of courses for the beginner, who wants to learn how to scuba dive, to our specialty and advanced recreational courses that are conducted throughout the year. We also offer the PADI eLearning online courses for those who want to complete the classroom portion of their Open Water Diver certification online.
      </p>
      <p>
       At The Scuba Shop, you will experience a professional environment that is family-friendly and focused on your diving needs.
      </p>

      <h2>
       From the Blog
      </h2>
      <ul class="posts">
       <li class="post">
        <h3>
         <a href="http://thescubashop.org/industry-leaders-not-followers-why-we-are-different/">
          Industry Leaders, not Followers  Why We are Different
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Classroom-Back-Wall.jpg">
          <img alt="SONY DSC" class=" wp-image-628 alignright" height="179" src="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Classroom-Back-Wall-300x199.jpg" title="Classroom" width="270"/>
         </a>
         We have made every effort to provide our customers a professional scuba training center that supports some of the industrys best scuba diving equipment manufacturers, a state-of-the-art indoor heated pool that is on-site at each of our locations, along with two large training rooms and a diverse experienced dive staff to help make your diving adventure complete.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Retail-Floor.jpg">
          <img alt="SONY DSC" class=" wp-image-629 alignright" height="179" src="http://thescubashop.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Retail-Floor-300x199.jpg" title="Retail Floor" width="270"/>
         </a>
         We are the only scuba shop in the East Valley that can offer each of our scuba divers an indoor pool experience that allows our participants to enjoy warm water while attending our classes.
