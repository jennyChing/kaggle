Mine has been much more chilled-out than last week which was fun but very busy, so last night I enjoyed some nice quality time curled up in bed with a stack of Oxygen and Womens Health magazines. Im not a big TV watcher at all (I dont even have cable any more) so mags are my way of vegging out!           <strong>
           2. Simply Whey Bars.
          </strong>
         </span>
         Before I talk about this new product, let me preface by saying this: If I had to pick an all-time favourite protein bar, it would be
         <a href="http://www.wellnessfoods.ca/SimplyBarHighProteinLowCaloriebar.html" target="_blank" title="Wellness Foods - The Simply Bar">
          <strong>
           The Simply Bar
          </strong>
         </a>
         by
         <a href="http://www.wellnessfoods.ca/#" target="_blank" title="Wellness Foods">
          <strong>
           Wellness Foods
          </strong>
         </a>
         . Ive waxed poetic about my love for Simply Bars in the past, especially the cinnamon and cinnamon-pecan flavoured ones  they are AMAZING! I always have a stash of Simply Bars in my car and purse, and often recommend them to friends and clients looking for a portable, high-protein snack. Heres how the stats of Simply Bars stack up against others:
        </p>

         <a href="http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Bar-Comparison.png">

           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/2013/02/27/recap-try-something-new-tuesday-146/&amp;media=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Bar-Comparison.png&amp;description=Recap: Try Something New Tuesday 146')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The protein in Simply Bars comes from soy puff cereal, but we all know that theres controversy around soy. If this is a concern of yours, theres a new kid on the block that you should know about. A few weekends ago, I discovered
         <a href="http://wellnessfoods.ca/SimplyWheyProteinBarsWellnessFoods_000.html" target="_blank" title="Simply Whey Bars - Wellness Foods">
          <strong>
           Simply Whey Bars
          </strong>
         </a>
         at Bulk Barn.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Apple-Cinnamon.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/2013/02/27/recap-try-something-new-tuesday-146/&amp;media=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Apple-Cinnamon.jpg&amp;description=Recap: Try Something New Tuesday 146')">

          </span>
         </a>

Unlike Simply Bars, these arent vegan, but theyre still super low in sugar, fat and calories than others on the market. Currently theyre only available in Apple Cinnamon and Coconut, and heres how the Coconut one compares:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Bar-Comparison.png">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Simply Whey Bar Comparison" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17320" height="163" src="http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Bar-Comparison.png" width="529"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/2013/02/27/recap-try-something-new-tuesday-146/&amp;media=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Bar-Comparison.png&amp;description=Recap: Try Something New Tuesday 146')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pretty impressive hey? But now youre probably wondering how they taste
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Apple-Cinnamon-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Simply Whey - Apple Cinnamon" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17334" height="304" src="http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Apple-Cinnamon-2.jpg" width="456"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/2013/02/27/recap-try-something-new-tuesday-146/&amp;media=http://eat-spin-run-repeat.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Simply-Whey-Apple-Cinnamon-2.jpg&amp;description=Recap: Try Something New Tuesday 146')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Judging by the fact that I made sure every last one of those itty bitty puffed circles made their way into my mouth yesterday afternoon, Id say pretty awesome! The whey definitely tasted different from the soy crisp cereal, but Ive decided I like both equally. These also seem to be more firm and hold together a bit better than The Simply Bars, which can be a little crumbly. I havent tasted the Coconut bars yet (theyre next!) but this one was a big winner!
        </p>

         In case youre looking for The Simply Bars, you can find them for sale

           Healthy Foods and More
