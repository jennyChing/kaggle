            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             Tired of your same old espresso but still want to drink great tasting cup of coffee? Here are 5 different kinds of coffee to try out.
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
              <br/>
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
              Siphon Coffee
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <b style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              What is it:
             </b>
             <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              Magic. Well, close enough. You've got a double beaker set up that looks (to Breaking Bad fans, anyways) like a meth-making kit, where water from the bottom is sucked up via vacuum pressure through coffee grounds, and then filters back trough to the bottom vessel once cooler.
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <b style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              What's it like:
             </b>
             <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              Fragrant and not too bitter. It's got a bit more zing than a pour over, and watching it being made is pretty awesome.
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b style="color: #444444;">
              Try it out:
             </b>
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              You can get all the gear from
             </span>
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002CVTKTW/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002CVTKTW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=latartgui-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #cc0000;">
               Amazon from $59
              </span>
             </a>
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              .
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              <br/>
             </span>
            </span>
            <table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
             <tbody>
              <tr>
               <td style="text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-wa46P4jiK9U/UbLakaoaBtI/AAAAAAAAAsA/XfUi39dcTVA/s1600/Screen+Shot+2013-06-08+at+5.16.53+PM.png" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                 <span style="color: #444444;">
                  <img border="0" height="191" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-wa46P4jiK9U/UbLakaoaBtI/AAAAAAAAAsA/XfUi39dcTVA/s400/Screen+Shot+2013-06-08+at+5.16.53+PM.png" width="400"/>
                 </span>
                </a>
               </td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
               <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="color: #444444;">
                 Siphon Coffee
                </span>
               </td>
              </tr>
             </tbody>
            </table>
            <a name="more">
            </a>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             Cold Drip
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <b>
               What is it:
              </b>
              The patient drinker's coffee. Cold water is dripped onto a coffee pack, slowly filtering though over many hours, finally dropping into a vessel underneath. It results in what is essentially the 'raw' coffee taste.
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b>
              What's it like:
             </b>
             Earthy rather than sweet, or as one barista puts it: "like stale Easter egg chocolate and dirt."
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b style="color: #444444;">
              Try it out:
             </b>
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              The alchemist looking gear is
             </span>
            </span>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             a lot moreexpensive at
            </span>
            <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            </span>
            <span style="color: #cc0000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002RL9DW6/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002RL9DW6&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=latartgui-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #cc0000;">
               $244 from
              </span>
              <span style="color: #cc0000;">
               Amazon
              </span>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             , but many morespecialitycafes are introducing cold drip coffee to their menu, there might be one near you.
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <br/>
            </span>
            <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">

                 <span style="color: #444444;">
                  <img border="0" height="255" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X3RpzCRPAx8/UbLWDvqKCuI/AAAAAAAAArU/sMHS3UvgVts/s400/cold+drip.jpg" width="400"/>
                 </span>

                </span>
               </td>
              </tr>
             </tbody>
            </table>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             </span>
             <br/>
            </span>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
              Pour Over
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b>
              What is it:
             </b>
             When you've got to coax the best of some delicate coffee wallflowers, pour-overs are arguably the best and easiest way to do it. Over two-and-a-half minutes, hot water is carefully and steadily poured over fresh coffee grounds held in a filter cup, infusing and dripping down into the catcher cup below.
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444;">
             <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              <b>
               What's it like:
              </b>
              It's served black, but it's light-on for
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              depth as the beans don't really stew in the water.
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b style="color: #444444;">
              Try it out:
             </b>
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              Prices vary greatly for pour over gear. You can either get inexpensive one serve cones orreusable ones with beakers.
             </span>
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;keywords=Pour%20Over&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;qid=1371701938&amp;rh=n%3A1055398%2Ck%3APour%20Over%2Cp_72%3A1248915011&amp;tag=latartgui-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              <span style="color: #cc0000;">
               Check out them out here
              </span>
             </a>
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              .
             </span>
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <span style="color: #444444;">
              <br/>
             </span>
            </span>

            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
             AeroPress
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b>
              What is it:
             </b>
             It looks kind of like a breast pump, and is a little like a French press - only with holes and a paper filter at the bottom. Coffee and water are added (the leakage), and after a minute of mingling you pushing the brewed coffee out the bottom.
            </span>
            <br/>
            <span style="color: #444444; font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b>
              What's it like:
             </b>
             It's a great brewed coffee -- a leaner, cleaner plunger taste with no gritty little bits in it.
