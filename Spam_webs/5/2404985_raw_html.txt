        <h2>
         A Military Wedding Day
        </h2>
        <p class="postedby">
         Posted by Emma in:
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.wedbits.com/category/real-weddings/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Real Weddings">
          Real Weddings
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="col-980 alignleft">
       <p>
        You readers seem to like real weddings where the groom is in dress uniform!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346016_IMG_0001_low" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16875" height="600" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346016_IMG_0001_low.jpg" width="900"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Last week we
        <a href="http://www.wedbits.com/2014/07/theres-something-man-uniform/" target="_blank" title="Dashing Officer Bridegroom Marries In A Dashing Uniform">
         had a Naval wedding
        </a>
         and today we have a bridegroom whos in the military who looked very dashing and handsome as he led his new bride back down the aisle
        <a href="http://www.tonyfitzgeraldphotography.com/" target="_blank">
         . Photography is by Tony Fitzgerald.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The venue is at the brides fathers hotel in California and its the prettiest day (with a spectacular wedding cake too).
       </p>
       <p>
        We all loved the Royal Wedding with Prince William in his Irish Guards uniform and Prince Harry in his Blues and Royals dress uniform. Theres certainly something about a man in uniform.
       </p>
       <p>
        And there are some other fabulous traditions at military weddings, like cutting the wedding cake with a sword and having an arch of swords to walk underneath!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346048_IMG_0885_low" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16885" height="900" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346048_IMG_0885_low.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16889" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="bride and groom at outdoor wedding" class=" wp-image-16889 " height="420" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346088_IMG_0515_low.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         You can get married outside in the UK as long as you are underneath a fixed and licensed structure  which could be a gazebo.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <img alt="white bridal bouquet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16873" height="900" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346106_IMG_1751_low.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16892" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="bridesmaids at wedding" class="size-full wp-image-16892" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346102_IMG_02492_low-e1407418618222.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Deep colours for the bridesmaids bouquets contrasted beautifully with the simplicity of their grey frocks
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16872" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="bridesmaid bouquet in rich colours" class="size-full wp-image-16872" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346098_IMG_1592_low-e1407418714413.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Roses in pinks, reds, purples 
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16882" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="table centre in silver" class="size-full wp-image-16882" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346039_IMG_0780_low-e1407418901113.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         An easy table centre you could make yourself  with silver tea lights and mini vases. Keep the flowers white for contrast.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16883" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="Don't forget to have photographs taken of your order of service, table plan and menus .." class=" wp-image-16883 " height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346040_IMG_0096_low.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Dont forget to have photographs taken of your order of service, table plan and menus ..
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16895" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346113_IMG_03882_low" class="size-full wp-image-16895" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346113_IMG_03882_low-e1407419009996.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Think about having a midnight blue or deep purple tablecloth to ring the changes 
        </p>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346124_IMG_0047_low" class="aligncenter wp-image-16897" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346124_IMG_0047_low.jpg" width="600"/>
        <img alt="Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346114_IMG_03912_low" class="aligncenter wp-image-16896" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346114_IMG_03912_low.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16874" style="width: 610px">
        <img alt="cutting wedding cake with sword" class="size-full wp-image-16874" height="400" src="http://www.wedbits.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Rodriguez_Chelini_Tony_Fitzgerald_Photography_346138_IMG_2277_low-e1407420332376.jpg" width="600"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Cutting the wedding cake with a sword  a military tradition!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
