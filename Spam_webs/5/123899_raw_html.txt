          Transglobal Free Press is a newspaper written, managed and edited by the readers and users. It is built in a 2.0 website for the publication of articles, reports,
news, opinions and so on and the creation of magazines. This
is a big newspaper open to everybody where anyone can become editor and
participate in the management and operation only by registering and creating a user account. In this space are welcome
the professional journalists, such as non-professional, so they can
spread their voice on an equal footing.
         </p>
         <p>
          We belive that is more open-minded people in the world than radical and selfish, so we are sure that this open and free newspaper will become naturally fair, positive and respectful, we have no doubt about it, and this is our dream and real wish.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           In
this site, you can find all the tools, links and connections to manage, store,
share and promote the different articles and works which are been published. The
users have all kinds of facilities to find articles, subjects, authors,
magazines, etc being able to take out a subscription for the authors piece of
works or magazines of their interest.
           <br/>
           From
this site, the users are able to post news and e-mail links and insert any of
the Transglobal Free Press articles to other sites or areas of Internet.
           <br/>
           This
site is designed to be a universal news and magazines center in several
languages which keeps on updating and spreading in a dynamic and logic way. The
news of the front page and the different sections and magazines, will be placed
automatically according to the date of publication, interest and quality, where only
the votes and scores of the same users will take part.
           <br/>
           This
site operation system is extremely intuitive, and it aims to achieve the readers
and users the maximum participation, so that the evaluation and spread of the authors
and magazines is balanced, fair and logic.
           <br/>
           The
philosophy of this website is that anyone anywhere in the world can freely
write any article, news, and opinions and so on, so it can spread worldwide, by
enjoying of an immediate tool in order to explain quotidian events at any time
as to express any opinion, thought or work of general interest. Accepting
always the terms and conditions of the website, based on the peaceful and
respectful expression with others.
           <br/>
           The
main inspiration and design of the site is based on the press publications from
the end of the nineteenth to the beginning of the twentieth century, where there
was a taste for the refined writing and where it was possible to find any kind
of article, from literary to present news, either in prose or poetry or music
scores and others. The illustrations and engravings included in the
publications were also of a great importance.
           <br/>
           It is
also possible to publish cartoons and graphic works which will be visible in
the users profile, and may be published on the front page according to the users
valuation.
          </span>
