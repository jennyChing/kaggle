                       <div class="step-block">
                        <p class="step-block-number">
                         01
                        </p>
                        <p class="step-block-text">
                         Pretend to be somebody else. How would you take notes for this person? 
        Would you use different colored pens for different topics? 
        Would you capture ideas neatly on
                         <a href="http://www.post-it.com/wps/portal/3M/en_US/PostItNA/Home/Products/?N=4327+3294630620&amp;rt=rud">
                          Post-it Super Sticky Notes
                         </a>
                         ? 
        When you get creative with your learning, youre more likely to remember it.
                        </p>
                        <br/>
                        <img alt="Great ways to retain information" src="/3MContentRetrievalAPI/BlobServlet?assetId=1361804305792&amp;assetType=MMM_Image&amp;blobAttribute=ImageFile"/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="step-block">
                        <p class="step-block-number">
                         02
                        </p>
                        <p class="step-block-text">
                         Invite some friends to learn with you. Break up topics and teach them back to each other. 
        Challenge yourself and your friends with practice tests. When people learn together, your memory is more active.
                        </p>
                        <br/>
                        <img alt="Great ways to retain information" src="/3MContentRetrievalAPI/BlobServlet?assetId=1361804305792&amp;assetType=MMM_Image&amp;blobAttribute=ThumbnailImage"/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="step-block">
                        <p class="step-block-number">
                         03
                        </p>
                        <p class="step-block-text">
                         Say it out loud. Mark down important ideas with
                         <a href="http://www.post-it.com/wps/portal/3M/en_US/PostItNA/Home/Products/?N=4327+3294630666&amp;rt=rud">
                          Post-it Flags
                         </a>
                         and then read them aloud to yourself at least twice. When you see and hear information, 
        youre activating two of the human senses instead of relying on one.
                        </p>
