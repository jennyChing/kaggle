          <br/>
         </strong>

        <p>
         Canton, MA  5 month old Rosie, a German Shepherd got into a bit of trouble with the police this week. Rosies owner John Costello was moving his car at a local park when the pup jumped in with him. She ended up hitting the gear shift and falling onto the gas pedal, she drove them right into a pond. The car, a Dodge Neon, was a total loss but they both made it out of the pond unharmed.
        </p>
