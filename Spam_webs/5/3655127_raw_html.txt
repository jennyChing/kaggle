         </li>
         <li>
          Hand warmers
         </li>
         <li>
          Smores kit (everything needed to make a few smores in a bag)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar.jpg">
          <img alt="Keep warm kit gift in a jar -- perfect for cold winter holidays!  See the list of things to add to this fun mason jar gift idea!" class="wp-image-12296 aligncenter" height="421" src="//www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar.jpg" width="631"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Add all of those items to your mason jar. A quart size with a wide mouth will work best as the items are really crammed in this one.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar.jpg">
          <img alt="Keep warm kit gift in a jar -- perfect for cold winter holidays!  See the list of things to add to this fun mason jar gift idea!" class="wp-image-12295 aligncenter" height="945" src="//www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar-001.jpg" width="630"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I wanted to add a little flair to that plain lid so I added some glitter tape around the edge. Then I hand stamped a gift tag and tied it on as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar.jpg">
          <img alt="Keep warm kit gift in a jar -- perfect for cold winter holidays!  See the list of things to add to this fun mason jar gift idea!" class="wp-image-12294 aligncenter" height="945" src="//www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar-002.jpg" width="630"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This gift in a jar is ready to be given to anyone on your holiday list. This is perfect for ladies and gentlemen. Give to neighbors, teachers, mailmen, and more! Everyone will love this keep warm kit gift in a jar.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/keep-warm-kit-gift-in-a-jar.jpg">
          <img alt="Keep warm kit gift in a jar -- perfect for cold winter holidays!          </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you love mason jar gifts, be sure to come over to The Country Chic Cottage for tons more ideas. You can visit my
