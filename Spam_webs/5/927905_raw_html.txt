        <h1>
         Top 5 Gadgets Launched in 2014
        </h1>
       </div>
       <!-- end of nk-posttitle -->
       <div class="grid-100 tablet-grid-100 mobile-grid-100 grid-parent nk-postcontent">
        <div id="upinpost-header" style="clear:both">
        </div>
        <p>
         In the sense of technological innovations, 2014 was a marvel of a year, bringing a very large variety of gadgets that could make a tech freak forget everything and the fact that this advancement was not limited to a certain type of gadgets, but reached far and wide, makes it all the more great. From cameras to Smartphones to e-readers, the list covered something for everyone. So here we have picked 5 of them which beat all the other gadgets released in 2014 and that you will love to have now. Here are the
         <b>
          top 5 Gadgets
         </b>
         Launched in 2014.
        </p>
        <h1>
         Top 5 Gadgets Launched in 2014
        </h1>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          1. Pebble Steel Smartwatch
         </strong>
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i1.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Pebble-Smartwatch.jpg">
          <img alt="Pebble Smartwatch" class=" wp-image-389 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Pebble-Smartwatch.jpg?resize=745%2C279"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This Smartwatch stole the show at CES 2014 and there is a pretty good reason for it too. This watch was the first one which was made to look nothing out of the ordinary and so users can just go around wearing it without getting stares at their wrists. This watch is Pebbles stepping up for the mainstream Smartwatch market. It also has great looks with the leather and aluminium adding up to its elegance. Pebble Smartwatches are at no 1 position on out
         <i>
          Top 5 Gadgets
         </i>
         of 2014 list.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          2.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Polaroid SocialMatic Camera
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <a href="http://i1.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Polaroid-Socialmatic.jpg">
         <img alt="Polaroid Socialmatic" class=" wp-image-390 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Polaroid-Socialmatic.jpg?resize=631%2C324"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <p>
         This Android based Socialmatic camera is one of the most important gadgets in this list. It revives the beloved Polaroid and enables the users to customize the pictures taken with a 4.5 inch touchscreen and to even print stickers of the sepia-tinted images. It also looks incredibly cool but at the same time has the similar hipster feel to it. They are at no 2 position in
         <u>
          top 5 gadgets
         </u>
         list of 2014.
        </p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          3. Sony 4K ultra Short Throw Projector
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i1.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Sony-4K-ultrashot-projector.jpg">
          <img alt="Sony 4K ultrashot projector" class=" wp-image-391 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Sony-4K-ultrashot-projector.jpg?resize=588%2C441"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This projector was one of Sonys most exciting and peculiar gadget this year. This Ultra Short Throw projector shows a surprisingly sharp and bright image even when being placed at a short distance from a wall or a screen. This innovation definitely points the way not only towards the future of projectors but also of televisions and computer monitors. This projector is at 4th position in top 5 gadgets list.
        </p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          4. LGs Curved TV
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <a href="http://i2.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/LG-Curved-TV.jpg">
         <img alt="LG Curved TV" class=" size-full wp-image-392 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/LG-Curved-TV.jpg?resize=620%2C348"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <p>
         This TV by LG became a star as soon as it was launched attracting buyers with a gimmick of a concept: a curved 105 OLED 4K screen encased in a beautiful body. Once you have experienced this, you can be sure that currently there isnt a single TV to compete it. This curved display literally enhances the TV viewing experience by many times and although it is quite expensive, it succeeded in raising the TV standards.
         <strong>
         </strong>
         This curved sharp tv has got 4th place in our top 5 gadgets of 2014 list.
        </p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          5. Samsung Galaxy Note Pro
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i2.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Galaxy-Note-Pro.jpeg">
          <img alt="Galaxy Note Pro" class=" wp-image-393 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/redlacnet.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Galaxy-Note-Pro.jpeg?resize=609%2C406"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The answer given by Samsung to iPad Air was in the form of a humongous 12.2 inch tablet. It was described as stamping Android with the Magazine UX. It combines a few ideas taken from Windows 8, i.e., home screen tiles and Android skin interface turning away from Samsungs typical TouchWiz skins. It also comes with a stylus to turn it in a notepad for the user to scribble or doodle. You can also read the whole Samsung Galaxy Note Pro review from
         <a href="http://redlacnet.net/most-exciting-tablet-device-samsung-galaxy-note-pro-review/">
          here
         </a>
         . Samsung Galaxy Note Pro is at the 5th place on our best 5 gadgets list.
        </p>
        <p>
         Although there were many competitors of varying genres in line but the exceptional features and admirable craftsmanship won these superb gadgets a place in the top 5 gadgets launched in 2014 and though there will be many newer ones in the upcoming years, these gadgets would not lose their place. It you think we may have missed out something, please share it with us in the comments below.
        </p>
