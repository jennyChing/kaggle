        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="text-align: justify;">
          Our domesticated animal friends spend a lot of time with us, and theyre often smarter than we might think, so it certainly makes sense that they would pick up a trick or two from us. As proof, here are 22 animals that are slowly mastering the art of acting human.
         </span>
         <a href="#" id="read-more-inside-post">
          [Read more...]
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class=" hiddenPostText" style="text-align: justify;">
         Some of these are just coincidental photos which, while funny, are not quite as awesome as photos in which the animals have actually been trained to do something funny or cool. With the exception of the practices of cruel owners and trainers, complicated training is a great way to keep intelligent dog breeds or primates occupied. These animals can become depressed or destructive without challenging tasks for them to learn and complete.
        </p>
        <p class=" hiddenPostText" style="text-align: justify;">
         Or could it be that, because these little guys live with us and because we call them our friends, we consider them to be, in a way, one of us? For more examples of animals being people, check out our funny post about
         <a href="http://www.boredpanda.com/funny-bears-doing-human-things/" target="_blank">
          bears doing human things
         </a>
         .
