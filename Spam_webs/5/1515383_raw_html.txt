          </p>

            Using data from our 100,000 London based online dating members

           , we have determined the 25 Sexiest Neighborhoods in the city! According to our member data, these 25 areas have the highest rate of singles looking for a hook-up versus a serious, committed relationship. Some areas are hot with bankers, lawyers and politicians (no surprise there!) while others are seemingly quiet and surburban. Weve plotted each neighborhood on a tube map, so now youll know exactly where to go when youre in the mood.
          </p>
