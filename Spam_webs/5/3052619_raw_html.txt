       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Kids Craft: Tin Can Robots
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-07-23T06:00:02+00:00">
         July 23, 2014
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/author/laurasilva" rel="author">
           Laura Silva
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/2014/07/kids-craft-tin-can-robots.html#comments">
          7 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         It is time again for another round of Craft Lightning. If you are new to Craft Lightning, a group of your favorite bloggers come together to each create a craft that takes less than 15 minutes to make. This months theme is camp crafts. Head over to
         <a href="http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net', 'The Country Chic Cottage');" target="_blank">
          The Country Chic Cottage
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.30minutecrafts.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.30minutecrafts.com', '30 Minute Crafts');" target="_blank">
          30 Minute Crafts
         </a>
         to see all the crafts that were shared this week!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thecountrychiccottage.net', '');" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="aligncenter" src="http://30minutecrafts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/craft-lightning-camp-crafts-button-300x300.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you subscribe to
         <a href="http://www.parents.com/familyfun-magazine/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.parents.com/familyfun-magazine/', 'Family Fun magazine');" target="_blank">
          Family Fun magazine
         </a>
         ? If you have kids, it is a great source of inspiration for fun things to do with the kids  crafts, recipes, holiday activities, etc. I saw the idea to make tin can robots in a special edition issue of Family Fun.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Kids-Craft-Tin-Can-Robots-by-Lauras-Crafty-Life.jpg">
          <img alt="Kid's Craft - Tin Can Robots by Laura's Crafty Life" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3761" height="900" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Kids-Craft-Tin-Can-Robots-by-Lauras-Crafty-Life.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Supplies needed
         </span>
         <em>
          (contains affiliate links)
         </em>
         :
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Cans
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0036F9FKM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0036F9FKM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lauscralif02-20&amp;linkId=IMPOAFF5N2CSSY5J" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0036F9FKM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0036F9FKM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lauscralif02-20&amp;linkId=IMPOAFF5N2CSSY5J', 'Magnets');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Magnets
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=lauscralif02-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B0036F9FKM" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008MWMQL4/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008MWMQL4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lauscralif02-20&amp;linkId=CGN3276LD24MVGM2" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008MWMQL4/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008MWMQL4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lauscralif02-20&amp;linkId=CGN3276LD24MVGM2', 'Glue gun/glue sticks');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Glue gun/glue sticks
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=lauscralif02-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B008MWMQL4" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         </li>
         <li>
          Various metal parts
         </li>
         <li>
          Various craft supplies
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step One:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Remove the labels from your cans. Wash and dry your cans. Be sure there are no rough edges where you removed the top. File down any rough edges, if necessary.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/cans-for-robots.jpg">
          <img alt="cans for robots" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3760" height="400" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/cans-for-robots.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step Two:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Gather robot parts. We used various nuts, bolts, screws, washers, etc. that we had in our workshop. We also used pipe cleaners, jewels, googly eyes, buttons, and beads.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/parts-to-create-robots.jpg">
          <img alt="parts to create robots" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3764" height="400" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/parts-to-create-robots.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You will also need small magnets and a glue gun.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0036F9FKM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0036F9FKM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lauscralif02-20&amp;linkId=IELSJD5YM7B54XHJ" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0036F9FKM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0036F9FKM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lauscralif02-20&amp;linkId=IELSJD5YM7B54XHJ', '');" rel="nofollow">
          <img alt="magnets for robots" class="aligncenter wp-image-3762 size-full" height="900" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/magnets-for-robots.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step Three:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Start assembling your robot parts. Use your imagination. The metal parts will stick to the magnets and no glue is necessary. For non-metal parts an adult can use a hot glue gun to adhere the parts to the magnets.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/use-glue-gun-for-non-metal-parts.jpg">
          <img alt="use glue gun for non-metal parts" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3767" height="400" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/use-glue-gun-for-non-metal-parts.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step Four:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Attach your robot parts to the can with the magnets to create faces or whatever your heart desires. The great thing about these robots is you can make new creations over and over again. Even though the actual preparation of the robot parts takes a short amount of time, my children happily played with them for over an hour!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/T-making-robots.jpg">
          <img alt="T making robots" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3765" height="900" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/T-making-robots.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/O-making-robots.jpg">
          <img alt="O making robots" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3763" height="900" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/O-making-robots.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Tin-Can-Robot-Ideas-via-Lauras-Crafty-Life.jpg">
          <img alt="Tin Can Robot Ideas via Laura's Crafty Life" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3766" height="400" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Tin-Can-Robot-Ideas-via-Lauras-Crafty-Life.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Can-Robot-by-Lauras-Crafty-Life.jpg">
          <img alt="Can Robot by Laura's Crafty Life" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3759" height="900" src="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Can-Robot-by-Lauras-Crafty-Life.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          What activities do you do to keep the kids busy in the summer?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.laurascraftylife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/signature-for-posts-e1400529382104.png">
