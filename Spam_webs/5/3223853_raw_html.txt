      'CAPTAIN AMERICA' GETS A DIRECTOR
     </a>
    </div>
    <div class="subtitle-text mrl5">
     Movie Will Be Set in WWII
    </div>
    <div class="date-text mrl5">
     Posted by ICv2 on November 10, 2008 @ 12:00 am CT
    </div>
    <a class="float-left" href="http://icv2.com/print/article/13698" target="_blank">

    <div class="article-text clearfix mrl5">
     <a href="http://icv2.com/image/13698/1">
      <img class="float-left" src="http://icv2.com/images/13698captainamerica37LG.jpg"/>
     </a>
     <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
      Marvel Studios has chosen Joe Johnston to direct
      <i>
       First Avenger: Captain America
      </i>
      .
      <span>
      </span>
      Johnston, who storyboarded the convoy action sequence in
      <i>
       Raiders of the Lost Ark
      </i>
      and directed
      <i>
       Jurassic Park III, Hidalgo
      </i>
      , and the new remake of
      <i>
       The Wolfman
      </i>
      , began talking with Marvel about
      <i>
       Captain America
      </i>
      nearly two years ago and, according to
      <i>
       The Reporter
      </i>
      much of the projects current directions resulted from those early conversations.
     </p>
     <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
     </p>
     <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
      <i>
       First Avenger: Captain
       <st1:country-region w:st="on">
        <st1:place w:st="on">
         America
        </st1:place>
       </st1:country-region>
      </i>
      will be set during World War II and will likely include an origin saga.
      <span>
      </span>
      Cap will also be appearing in Marvel Studios
      <i>
       Avengers
      </i>
      , which will be set in the present.
     </p>
     <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
     </p>
     <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt">
      Marvel Studios expects to hire writersfor the
      <i>
       Captain America
      </i>
      film in the very near future. The filmis currently slated to debut on May 6th, 2011.
     </p>
