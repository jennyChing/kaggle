       <p>
        Coming of spring doesnt necessarily mean that all of your attention should be given to the outdoor renovations. Our home also deserves some love and what is a better way to show it than by moving in some new gadgets or the cutting edge tech. We are fortunate for living in a world we could only watch in such shows as Beyond 2000 just a few years ago, so possibilities for upgrades are pretty much endless. We will try to sum up few choices that are useful, efficient, affordable or just plain cool. Why not, we all have to indulge ourselves sometimes.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Kohler Numi toilet 400x400 Tech upgrades for you ideal tech equipped home" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3330" height="400" src="http://www.cyber-lizard.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Kohler-Numi-toilet-400x400.jpg" title="Kohler Numi toilet 400x400 " width="400"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Green power source
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Aside from being tech-savvy why wasting opportunity to be environmentally aware? This kind of power sources is hardly a new thing but its never too late to jump on the bandwagon and do something useful, both for you and for the community as well. Electricity costs are going sky high with the times passing and if you switch to gas stoves and gas heating a solar panel installed on your rooftop can pretty much provide all the electrical power you need. Keep in mind, though, that you need reasonable amount of sunlight during the year. Alternatively you can harness a wind power with wind turbines. If you use them both, they will complement each other beautifully.
        <span id="more-3326">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Home Security 447x297 Tech upgrades for you ideal tech equipped home" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3327" height="297" src="http://www.cyber-lizard.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Home-Security-447x297.jpg" title="Home Security 447x297 " width="447"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Home security upgrades
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        You can never be too sure about the safety of your family and that is why investing in home security is always a good idea. One of the ways to improve it is by installing a keyless entry system. Fingerprint scanner will compare several of unique fingerprint patterns and open doors only to trusted people. For extra safety, strengthen the whole system with fingerprint/password combo. Home security camera is another thing that has been here for a while but you can always go next-gen. With the enhanced picture quality you will be able to monitor everything thats happening around your house, recorded or in real time. You can check the status of your home through apps like Lockitron that will give you the information whether your house is locked or not and which family members left or entered.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt=" Tech upgrades for you ideal tech equipped home" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3329" height="400" src="http://www.cyber-lizard.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Cybertecture-mirror-302x400.jpeg" title=" " width="302"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Little things that make you happy
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        This is the part where things start to get interesting. Have you ever considered buying a smart plate? You should. This thing tracks and analyzes everything you eat and helps you to stay fit and stop overeating. Kohler Numi toilet, on the other side, has motion detected seat, bidet function with integrated air drier and a built-in foot warmer to make your stay in the toilet more pleasant.
        <a href="http://www.dudeiwantthat.com/gear/gadgets/cybertecture-mirror.asp" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Cybertecture mirror
        </a>
        will, aside from the sight of yourself in the morning, give you an update about weather, news, or any other information you need at a glance. Memory Coffee Maker is ideal for people who are drinking this beverage occasionally because it scans your hand and makes the coffee just the way you like it and Green Smart Glass will harness the energy from the hot beverage and store it so it can be used later.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="TV wall installation 447x268 Tech upgrades for you ideal tech equipped home" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3328" height="268" src="http://www.cyber-lizard.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/TV-wall-installation-447x268.jpg" title="TV wall installation 447x268 " width="447"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         A feast for an eye
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Any proper tech update should include some kind of
        <a href="http://www.universalhometheatre.com.au/home-theatre-services/home-theatre-wall-plate-tv-mounting" rel="dofollow" target="_blank">
         TV wall installation
        </a>
        , and so should yours. Instead of having your TV at the center of the room, why wouldnt you buy one that is designed to fit right to the wall and spare you some space, while creating visually appealing effect in the process? If you dont like them hanging, you can still impress your friends with the sheer size of screen or futuristic transparent TV that is an art statement as much as a piece of tech. One thing that never gets old and that will accompany your new TV wonderfully is a next-gen console. There is nothing bad about unleashing your inner manchild every now and then. If you are more of a casual gamer, some of Nintendos systems will help you to kill time efficiently and lure the whole family into your devious world.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here we are at the end now and the bottom line is that any way you spend your money will be a good one. Whether you are investing in home security, green power sources or you are simply trying to turn your house into the USS Enterprise, go for it. Tech is a good thing and you should enjoy it.
       </p>
