       <h2 class="normal mb1">
        <a class="green-dark" href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/krissman/chips-universal-bluetooth-wireless-all-in-one-head">
         CHIPS - Universal Bluetooth Wireless All-In-One Headphones
        </a>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span>
         Headphones that adapt to work with your active lifestyle, no matter what you are up to.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="remote_modal_dialog green-dark" data-modal-class="modal_project_by" data-modal-title="About the creator" href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/krissman/chips-universal-bluetooth-wireless-all-in-one-head/creator_bio">
         Outdoor Tech Design Lab
        </a>
        -is one of those reoccurring, serial kickstarter companies that continue to think up and deliver cool products like the
        <strong>
         <a class="green-dark" href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/krissman/outdoor-techs-turtle-shell-rugged-wireless-boombox?ref=users">
          The Turtle Shell
         </a>
        </strong>
        and the
        <strong>
         <a class="green-dark" href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/krissman/the-big-turtle-shell-rugged-wireless-boombox-and-p?ref=users">
          The BIG Turtle Shell Wireless BoomBox
         </a>
         .
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        They do quality work and have proved to their funders that they have a great insight in to their market.
       </p>
       <p>
        Now they bring you the CHIPS headphones developed for those with extreme lifestyles or just those on the go who want to stay hands free.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         <span>
          We knew we wanted incredible sound so we included 40MM drivers. We wanted our fingers to stay toasty warm, so we made over-sized buttons to control everything with gloved hands. We also knew it needed to stand up to the elements: IPX4 and the ability to handle frigid temps (-20C/-4F). With a built-in mic we could take calls and leave our phone in our pocket. - Outdoor Tech
         </span>
        </p>
