           <h2>
            WWE Scrapping NXT at the ECW Arena?
           </h2>
           <p>
            A few weeks ago, we noted WWE was considering running NXT live events at the old ECW Arena in Philadelphia.
           </p>
           <p>
            In an update to that story, The Wrestling Observer is reporting WWE is no longer considering the venue, as the company wants to run bigger arena like Philadelphias Liacouris Center at Temple University.
           </p>
           <p>
            In addition, on the Saturday night before a PPV Sunday, WWE is looking to start running NXT live events in the PPV city. This of course would put NXT head-to-head against ROH, something Mark Madden foreshadowed in a recent WZ column, which you can check out
            <a href="http://www.wrestlezone.com/editorials/565265-roh-tick-tock" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              at this link

            </a>
