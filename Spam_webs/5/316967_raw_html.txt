         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          Medifast Meal Makeovers Week Three
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="font-size: xx-small;">
           I receive free product in order to evaluate and comment on my experiences on the Medifast Program. Medifast products and the Medifast Program are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any illness. Any medical improvements noted while on the program are related to weight loss in general, and not to Medifast products or programs. No other compensation received, opinions are 100% my own.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.displacedyinzer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Adjusting-To-The-Medifast-Diet-3.png">
           <img alt="Medifast Meal Makeovers food recipe meal variety weight loss diet" class="aligncenter wp-image-5445 size-full" height="600" src="http://www.displacedyinzer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Adjusting-To-The-Medifast-Diet-3.png" width="600"/>

          <br/>
          I love how simple the
          <a href="http://www.medifast1.com/index.jsp?cm_mmc=Blog-_-DisplacedYinzer-_-Link-_-na" target="_blank">
           Medifast
          </a>
          diet is to use. They provide you with 5 of the meals you eat each day. So, you really dont need to stress over meal planning. The meals are delicious just as is, and most are convienient to take with you anywhere.
         </p>
         <p>
          But, there will be days where you need to mix it up. You may get a little tired of eating the same things every day, and you may get cravings. I know, I really wanted pizza last week! In cases like this, there are Medifast Meal Makeovers! These meals use either the Medifast Meals, or your Lean and Green meals as the base, but create a whole different meal!
          <br/>
          <span id="more-5443">
          </span>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://www.displacedyinzer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/CO-1320_CRD_FitFestRecipe41096.pdf">

          </a>
          <a href="http://www.medifast1.com/index.jsp?cm_mmc=Blog-_-DisplacedYinzer-_-Link-_-na" target="_blank">
           Medifast
          </a>
          provides a variety of Medifast Meal Makeovers right on their website. I love the Cinnamon &amp; Brown Cereal Crunch Cereal right out of the bag. Even better? You can use this cereal to make
          <a href="http://www.medifastblog.com/2014/07/28/healthy-french-toast/" target="_blank">
           healthy fresh toast
          </a>
          ! The site not only gives you the recipe, but video on how to make it as well! Check out this recipe and other
          <a href="http://www.medifastblog.com/tag/meal-makeover/" target="_blank">
           Medifast Meal Makeovers here
          </a>
          !
         </p>
         <p>
          You can also print out the Medifast
          <a href="http://www.displacedyinzer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/CO-1320_CRD_FitFestRecipe41096.pdf" target="_blank">
           Fit &amp; Festive Spring Recipe Book
          </a>
          and the
          <a href="CO-1650_CRD_FitFestRecipeFA" target="_blank">
           Fit &amp; Festive Fall Recipe Book
          </a>
          for more meal makeovers!
         </p>
         <p>
          Want even more variety? Check out my
          <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/displacedyinzer/medifast/" target="_blank">
           Medifast Pinterest Board
          </a>
          formore recipes, including those created by others on the Medifast plan!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="font-size: x-large;">
           <span style="color: blue;">
            Now, to share this weeks progress!
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Week three, as expected, brought lower numbers. I am not discouraged at all, as there was still a loss in weight and inches!
          <a href="http://www.displacedyinzer.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/1-3.png">

          </a>
         </p>
