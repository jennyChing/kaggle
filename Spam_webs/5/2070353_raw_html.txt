         <span itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           Friends with benefits.
          </p>
          <p>
           This phrase has been the topic of many discussions among my friends and me, not to mention that various movies and articles have been dedicated to it. It even has its own abbreviation: FWBs.
          </p>
          <p>
           However pervasive, the notion of friends with benefits is quite intricate one with many layers and, at times, complications.
          </p>
          <p>
           Thus, I shall list a small guideline to choosing a proper counterpart for this type of relationship and how to deal with the relationship itself.
          </p>
          <h3>
           1)	Choose someone you dont want to date.
          </h3>
          <p>
           I know this might sound odd, but you cannot get into a no strings attached relationship with someone who you could possibly see yourself dating in the future. Things will get messy.
          </p>
          <h3>
           2)	Dont be FWBs with the hope of turning it to a relationship.
          </h3>
          <p>
           This happens a lot. You think, Ow hell like me more once he gets to know me better, I can change his ways. No you cant, dont even try.
          </p>
          <h3>
           3)	Set rules for the relationship.
          </h3>
          <p>
           Be clear with a set of guidelines before you launch into the physical fun. It eases complications or messes and (hopefully) guarantees the greatest comfort Also, make an escape plan. If either one of you wants out of the relationship, set clear rules for a clean break beforehand.
           <br/>
           <a href="http://2famous.TV/2013/05/03/the-dos-and-donts-of-friends-with-benefits/hotnerd/" rel="attachment wp-att-11656">
            <img alt="Sexy Nerd Guy" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-11656" height="300" src="http://2famous.TV/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/hotnerd-231x300.jpg" width="231"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           4)	Dont get jealous.
          </h3>
          <p>
           Easier said than done, I know. But if your counterpart goes out with other women or men, you have no reason to get jealous because he/she is not cheating on you since you are not in a monogamous relationship.
          </p>
          <h3>
           5)	Choose someone you are physically attracted to.
          </h3>
          <p>
           Duhhhh, he could be the bad boy your parents would never approve of or the geek with a big dick. She could be the hippie flower child you cant connect with or a whipsmart businesswoman who is way out of your league (goodluck on that one, btw). Indulge yourself in someone you wouldnt normally choose to date.
          </p>
          <h3>
           6)	Dont feel guilty.
          </h3>
          <p>
           You are not doing anything wrong! Repeat this to yourself: You deserve some pure unadulterated, safe, sexual fun. It is not a mistake and you are not going to be doomed for it (if you follow all the rules.)
           <a href="http://2famous.TV/2013/05/03/the-dos-and-donts-of-friends-with-benefits/suicide-girls08/" rel="attachment wp-att-11657">
            <img alt="Sexy Suicide Girl" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-11657" height="200" src="http://2famous.TV/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/suicide-girls08-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           7)	Use protection, always.
          </h3>
          <p>
           This is THE golden rule.
          </p>
          <h3>
           8)	Stop when you desire.
          </h3>
          <p>
           FWBs are fun, a lot of fun actually, but they arent a permanent state. Whenever you feel like you want to get back into relationship mode, or you are just bored of it, move on.
          </p>
          <h3>
           9)	Just relax and enjoy it.
          </h3>
          <p>
           Dont overcomplicate it by expecting dinner and deep emotional talks. It really is just physical and that might be the only thing you two have in common. Just enjoy the ride (pun intended).
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Taylor is the perpetually single Lebanese girl. Before you start labeling her as forever alone, her single state has mostly been out of choice and lack of interesting men. She will sometimes blog about dating in Lebanon.
           </em>
          </p>
