           <h2>
            15 things you should know before dating a bartender
           </h2>
           <span class="date">
            4:50PM, Feb 26, 2015
           </span>
           <h6 class="author">
            Catriona Harvey-Jenner
           </h6>
          </div>
          <div class="body">
           <div class="sell">
            We're not morning people.
           </div>
           <form action="/relationships/relationship-advice/2015/2/15-things-you-should-know-before-dating-a-bartender/" id="frm_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_cphCosmoMasterLHS_cphCosmoMasterLHS_ArticleBody_13_ctl00" method="POST" name="frm_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_cphCosmoMasterLHS_cphCosmoMasterLHS_ArticleBody_13_ctl00">
            <input id="frm_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_cphCosmoMasterLHS_cphCosmoMasterLHS_ArticleBody_13_ctl00_IsPostBack" name="frm_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_cphCosmoMasterLHS_cphCosmoMasterLHS_ArticleBody_13_ctl00_IsPostBack" type="hidden" value="True"/>
            <input name="hdnFormName" type="hidden" value="frm_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_cphCosmoMasterLHS_cphCosmoMasterLHS_ArticleBody_13_ctl00"/>
           </form>
           <!-- Old articles -->
           <div class="article-image">
            <img alt="" src="//d3lp4xedbqa8a5.cloudfront.net/s3/digital-cougar-assets/Cosmo/2015/02/26/49420/coyoteugly.jpg?Image=%2fs3%2fdigital-cougar-assets%2fCosmo%2f2015%2f02%2f26%2f49420%2fcoyoteugly.jpg&amp;Width=650&amp;AllowUpSizing=False&amp;Mode=Crop"/>
           </div>
           <div class="top">
            <p>
             <strong>
              1.Romantic weekends away will never be a thing.
             </strong>
             Because we don't get weekends, unless you count the occasional consecutive Monday-Tuesday off.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              2.We will pay for most things in cash.
             </strong>
             We're not tax-evaders, promise. We just live off our tips. It does get a bit awkward when we're down to our last cents and have to start emptying out bags of shrapnel everywhere we go, though.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              3.There's no point trying to plan dates in advance.
             </strong>
             Because everyone
             <em>
              knows
             </em>
             the rotas only come out about 12 hours before they start.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              4.We will get hit on at work. A lot.
             </strong>
             And it's uncomfortable, but we can't help it that unwelcome flirty 'banter' just doesn't come with the territory of your office job.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              5.We're notmorning people.
             </strong>
             Unless awoken with a bacon roll and a steaming hot coffee, thank you.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              6.We'll probably need keys to your flat sooner rather than later.
             </strong>
             Unless you
             <em>
              like
             </em>
             being woken up to come down and let us in after work at 2am
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              7.We're still intelligent, you know.
             </strong>
             Just because we're not
             <em>
              currently
             </em>
             using the degree we may or may not have shed blood, sweat and tears for, doesn't mean we're not as smart as everyone else.
            </p>
            <p>
             RELATED:
             <a href="http://www.cosmopolitan.com.au/relationships/relationship-advice/2015/2/13-things-women-think-guys-want-in-bed-but-they-dont/" target="_blank">
              13 things women think guys want in bed (but they don't)
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              8.We're a JOY to go to the pub with.
             </strong>
             Once we've got all the gripes out the way, that is. Just let us have our say about this pub's incorrect glass-polishing technique, and how they're doing their beer-to-head ratio
             <em>
              all
             </em>
             wrong. Then we're an absolute hoot!
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              9.We wear a lot of black.
             </strong>
             No, we're not professional funeral-goers, it's called a uniform.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              10.Winter is our favourite season.
             </strong>
             Because winter contains the shortest days and therefore the least sunlight that we are missing out on by either being at work, exhausted from work, or hungover from work.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              11.We like to eat chips with everything.
             </strong>
             We work split-shifts in a bar. It's literally inbuilt in our psyche to order a side of chips with every staff meal just so we won't feel famished with three hours of shift still to go. Which is why,yes,we
             <em>
              will
             </em>
             have some chips on the side with that risotto, thanks.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              12.You will grow to love our natural musky scent
             </strong>
             of stale beer and tequila. All the more reason to splash out on perfume for us though, right?
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              13.We're low-maintenance.
             </strong>
             When it comes to ordering cocktails, we are, anyway. Like we'd DREAM of ordering a mojito when we know what a ball ache it is to concoct.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              14.We've got GREAT stories
             </strong>
             . Bars really are a window to the world's soul. The world's stupid, drunken, emotional soul. And we've seen it all.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              15.We're good listeners.
             </strong>
             In fact, why do ourselves down? We're practically therapists after having listened to Jim the regular bang on about his involuntaryredundancy for the last six months.
            </p>
            <p>
             RELATED:
             <a href="http://www.cosmopolitan.com.au/relationships/relationship-advice/2015/2/man-turns-his-girlfriend-into-disney-princesses-for-valentines/">
              Man turns his girlfriend into Disney princesses for Valentine's
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             RELATED:
             <a href="http://www.cosmopolitan.com.au/relationships/relationship-advice/2015/2/19-reasons-why-living-with-your-significant-other-is-the-best/">
              19 reasons living with your significant other is the best
             </a>
            </p>
