     <h1>
      ISLAND KITCHEN 1
     </h1>
     <div class="gallery_breadcrumbs">

       <div class="gallery_feature">
        <a class="fancybox" href="http://www.impalakitchens.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/amodern-island-kitchen-1a.jpg" rel="gallery">
         <img src="http://www.impalakitchens.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/amodern-island-kitchen-1a.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="gallery_text">
        Impalas client was building a new house on Sydneys North Shore. The house was contemporary and was to have the newest fittings. Although a neutral colour scheme was requested for the kitchen, some warmth and interest was also in the brief. To this end, Impala designed a streamlined kitchen with no handles and concealed appliances. The base colour is white, however the stone bench top is a taupe/grey colour. The ends of the island unit are thick to balance the length of the island and are in a light satin polished timber veneer. The result is a gorgeous kitchen that will stand the test of time.
