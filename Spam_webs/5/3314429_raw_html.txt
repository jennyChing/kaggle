You know some of them were probably done by professionals, and most of us dont have the time to accomplish that. My goal is to help you find ideas that can be realistic, like when I give you all of the

          </span>
         </a>

        </p>

         <a href="http://www.thecreativityexchange.com/2014/01/diy-garage-pegboard-storage-wall-pegboard-possibilities.html#comment-17304" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thecreativityexchange.com/2014/01/diy-garage-pegboard-storage-wall-pegboard-possibilities.html#comment-17304', 'The Creativity Exchange']);" target="_blank">

        <h2>
         Beginning Steps To Organize Your Garage:
        </h2>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Back the car(s) out of the garage and away from the doors, so you dont have to work around them.
         </li>
         <li>
          Pick up trash, if any, that is layingaround, so you dont have to work around it, and you can get a fresh look, at what is there.
         </li>
         <li>
          Get things off of the floor. Dont worry about permanent storage just yet. Just get it up on shelves or in the driveway for now.
         </li>
         <li>
          Grab a broom {a large rectangular push broom kicks butt at this.} Sweep that nasty garage out, and youll instantly see an improvement.
         </li>
         <li>
          Oh yeah baby, time to organize. See below for my ideas on how to organize your garage in categories.
         </li>

         , I found this super cute way to organize toys for the kiddos. I believe she used a vinyl cutter to make the labels, but there are tons of ways you could personalize your buckets. Print off tags or labels and have them tied with string to each bucket (this is what Ill do), or you could stencil on the bucket with paint! Options are actually endless!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Buckets-for-toys-Garage-Organization.jpg">
          <img alt="Buckets for toys - Garage Organization http://fantabulosity.com" class="size-large wp-image-1093 aligncenter" height="1024" src="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Buckets-for-toys-Garage-Organization-673x1024.jpg" width="673"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Quit Tripping Over Balls:
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://usefulbeautifulhome.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://usefulbeautifulhome.com', 'Useful Beautiful Home']);" target="_blank">
          Useful Beautiful Home
         </a>
         had a great idea of using a tall laundry basket to store all of the balls that are usually strung all over the garage! What a cheap and creative idea!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ball-Basket-Garage-Organization.jpg">
          <img alt="Organize Your Garage, Great storage idea for balls http://fantabulosity.com" class="size-full wp-image-1095 aligncenter" height="600" src="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ball-Basket-Garage-Organization.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Each Toy Needs a Home.
        </h3>
        <p>
         This is BY FAR, my most favorite way to organize the large toy items for any garage. Once again
         <a href="http://usefulbeautifulhome.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://usefulbeautifulhome.com', 'Useful Beautiful Home']);" target="_blank">
          Useful Beautiful Home
         </a>
         , came through with an awesome idea. By creating sections, in the garage for these large toys, your kids can be apart of the organization process. It will teach them to always put the item in the correct location. Love it!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Large-Toy-Organization-for-Garage.jpg">
          <img alt="Large Toy Organization for Garage http://fantabulosity.com" class="size-full wp-image-1096 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Large-Toy-Organization-for-Garage.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         No Mudroom?
        </h3>
        <p>
         Now, if you dont have a mudroom, you may want to create a space in your garage for muddy and dirty shoes, right by the entry door to your home, right? Makes complete sense. Check out how
         <a href="http://www.homeyhomedesign.com/2013/04/more-garage-organization.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.homeyhomedesign.com/2013/04/more-garage-organization.html', 'Homey Home Design ']);" target="_blank">
          Homey Home Design
         </a>
         created a shoe rack in the garage out of what looks like a bookshelf! Keep your eyes open at a yard sale, flea market, or an auction, and paint it a super cute color for your garage.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Shoe-Organization-Idea-for-Garage.jpg">
          <img alt="Shoe Organization Idea for Garage http://fantabulosity.com" class="size-full wp-image-1097 aligncenter" height="584" src="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Shoe-Organization-Idea-for-Garage.jpg" width="472"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Gosh I love this monogrammed peg board idea. Possibly because it has a B on it, and thats what I would have? Nah, I love it because its cute and every garage needs some cuteness. Sorry dudes. If a gal is organizing it, we need to be able to put a little flair on it, right?
         <a href="http://organizingmadefun.blogspot.com/2012/10/31-days-of-organizing-fun-day-19.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://organizingmadefun.blogspot.com/2012/10/31-days-of-organizing-fun-day-19.html', 'Organizing Made Fun']);" target="_blank">
          Organizing Made Fun
         </a>
         created this awesome peg board, and for super cheap! They said a 48 sheet is only about $12 at a home improvement store. Boo-Yah!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Monogram-Peg-Board-Garage-Organization-Idea.jpg">
          <img alt="Monogram Peg Board Garage Organization Ideas http://fantabulosity.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1098" height="392" src="http://fantabulosity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Monogram-Peg-Board-Garage-Organization-Idea.jpg" width="397"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ok I could go on and on with garage organization ideas, but I better stop there, so I can get to planning my own garage organization.
        </p>
