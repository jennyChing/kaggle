          <div class="preparation expert-content-text" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           <h5 class="heading-style-a p-col">
            Preparation
           </h5>
           <b>
            Grilling Instructions:
           </b>
           Follow directions below, except heat the grill instead of the oven, don't use a baking sheet, and turn once during the cooking.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           1) Preheat oven to 425 F.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           2) Prepare the asparagus, either just by snapping off the ends, or trimming the very end and peeling tough part with vegetable peeler.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           3) Put on cookie sheet or baking pan.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           4) Drizzle the oil over the asparagus, followed by the salt, pepper, herbs, and lemon.
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="1" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense1">
           </div>
           Get in there with your hands and smear it all over.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           5. Bake for 5-10 minutes, until tender.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Nutritional Information:
           </b>
           One medium asparagus spear (5-7 inches long) has .3 grams effective carbohydrate and .3 grams fiber.
