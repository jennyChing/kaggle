      <h2 class="headline">
       Ill be supporting the Springboks at the Rugby World Cup:, Roger Federer.
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.springbokfans.com/rugby-world-cup/roger-federer-springbok-rugby-fan/">
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Roger Federer is a big supporter of the Springbok rugby team and will be cheering for the Boks to win the World Cup. The tennis greats mother, Lynette, is South African. She met his Swiss father Robert while he was working in Gauteng  and this is where his allegiances lie.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.timeslive.co.za/thetimes/2015/01/22/i-ll-be-supporting-the-springboks-at-the-rugby-world-cup-roger-federer">
        [ Read full story here.. ]
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Roger is also a big family man and in the video below you can see his cute twins..
      </p>
      <p>
       Thank you Roger for supporting the Springboks.
      </p>
