         Booking.com
        </a>
        to find an accommodation that reflects my definition of epic. I thought hard about what I look for in a hotel and the factors that make me want to gush about the hotel to everyone. It took a bit of soul-searching and slightly less time finding an accommodation that matched the requirements. I knew it, once I found it, that this accommodation was truly epic. So, what did I choose? Please allow me to introduce you to *DRUMROLL*
       </p>
       <h2>
        Hotel Alfonso XIII in Sevilla
       </h2>
       <p>
        So, why did I choose this hotel? Not hard to see why once youve seen photos of it. The
        <a href="http://www.hotel-alfonsoxiii-seville.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.hotel-alfonsoxiii-seville.com/', 'Hotel Alfonso XIII ']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Hotel Alfonso XIII
        </a>
        is visually striking to say the least. This hotel is without a doubt, fit for a king!
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15787" style="width: 490px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-entrance-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15787" height="640" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5983.jpg" width="480"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2014/11/hotel-alfonso-xiii/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5983.jpg&amp;description=A hotel fit for a king')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The glorious entrance to Hotel Alfonso XIII.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Built by command of King Alfonso XIII of Spain to house VIP guests during the Iber-American Expo in Sevilla in 1928, the hotel is a lavish display of pomp with a distinct Andalucian flavour. Mudjar, an architectural style that blends Islamic and European classical influences (particularly Gothic), is evident the second you step inside the hotel. I was greeted by a rich symphony of sweeping arches, columns and exquisite ceramic tile-work. It was an incredible sight that left me feeling quite bewildered.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15794" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-front-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15794" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5984.jpg" width="640"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2014/11/hotel-alfonso-xiii/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5984.jpg&amp;description=A hotel fit for a king')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The Hotel Alfonso XIII in Sevilla by night.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15795" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-entrance-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15795" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_6040.jpg" width="640"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2014/11/hotel-alfonso-xiii/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_6040.jpg&amp;description=A hotel fit for a king')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         and by day.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15788" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-lobby-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15788" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5982.jpg" width="640"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2014/11/hotel-alfonso-xiii/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5982.jpg&amp;description=A hotel fit for a king')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The beautiful lobby with its sweeping arches and stunning tiles.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The check-in was swift and when I arrived in my room, my bag was already there. The hotel has three styles of rooms: Andaluz, Moorish and Castilian. My Grand Deluxe room was in the Andaluz style. The room was spacious with a large, extremely comfortable Luxury Collections bed and Andalucian-style furnishings. The bathroom, with its marble floor and counter and gorgeous tiles, came equipped with a big bath-tub. The design was simple and functional and yet there was an unmistakable air of luxury.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15789" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-room-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15789" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_6045.jpg" width="640"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2014/11/hotel-alfonso-xiii/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_6045.jpg&amp;description=A hotel fit for a king')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         My room was spacious, functional and luxurious  that bed was fit for a king!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        As I explored more of the hotel in the following days, it became increasingly apparent to me why this hotel is simply epic. I mean, it ticked all the boxes. To start, the architecture is just breathtaking; the lobby was stunning whilst the courtyard just blew me away! Add to that a rich history (the hotel even has its own museum filled with photos of royal families, statesmen and celebrities from the past century), attentive service and to top it off, a fantastic location in the heart of Sevillas historic centre (the Cathedral and Alcazar palace are just a few minutes walk away).
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15790" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-lobby-flowers-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15790" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_6019.jpg" width="640"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://velvetescape.com/2014/11/hotel-alfonso-xiii/&amp;media=http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_6019.jpg&amp;description=A hotel fit for a king')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The floral arrangements in the lobby were gorgeous!
        </p>
       </div>

        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

       <p>
        Breakfast was served each morning  a sumptuous spread I may add  in the San Fernando restaurant, a space reminiscent of a grand ballroom complete with Venetian chandeliers, coffered ceilings and gleaming marble floors. I felt like royalty every morning!
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_15793" style="width: 650px">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt="hotel-alfonso-xiii-sevilla-breakfast-room-photo" class="size-full wp-image-15793" height="480" src="http://velvetescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_5994.jpg" width="640"/>

         </span>
        </span>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Breakfast is served in this grand room!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The hotel also has another restaurant, Ena by Carles Abellan, located on a beautiful terrace with an inviting bar. This restaurant serves a mix of Andalucian and Catalan cuisines in a contemporary style.
       </p>
       <p>
        On my last evening, I treated myself to a gin &amp; tonic at the hotels stylish American Bar. As I sipped on my drink, I turned my gaze to the wall and spotted Grace Kelly staring straight back at me. I thought of all the famous people who once had a drink at this same spot  not a difficult task as their photos adorn the walls of the bar  and I smiled. Like, how
        <em>
         EPIC
        </em>
        is that?
       </p>
