        <div class="node-body" id="node-body">
         <div class="news-tldr">
          <h3>
           TLDR;
          </h3>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="imagecache imagecache-100x100" height="100" src="http://www.screwattack.com/sites/default/files/imagecache/100x100/profile_photos/54991/profile-photo-54991.jpg" title="" width="100"/>
           <img alt="" class="imagecache imagecache-100x100" height="100" src="http://www.screwattack.com/sites/default/files/imagecache/100x100/profile_photos/71095/profile-photo-71095.jpg" title="" width="100"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Interviewer
            </strong>
           </em>
           :
           <a href="http://www.screwattack.com/user/master-wayne" rel="nofollow">
            Master Wayne
           </a>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Interviewee
            </strong>
           </em>
           :
           <a href="http://www.screwattack.com/user/sonicfan00715" rel="nofollow">
            Sonicfan00715
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <em>
             Hey g1's... we are hiring for a new g1 interviewer!!! Want to talk with fellow g1's about video games?! get in touch... lines are open NOW!!!
            </em>
           </strong>
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Hello there my fellow g1's and welcome to another g1 interview. I am your humble interviewer Master Wayne and Im joined by a play-through POWERHOUSE!!! Ladies and Gents, a very good friend of mine... g1 Sonicfan00715!!!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : How's it goin fellow g1's?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : How are you Sonicfan? It's been a while.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Im good man, it has been a while though, how are you?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I've just eaten a big ass steak... from a big ass cow... from its big ass.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : That's a big piece of cow ass right there.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : So Sonicfan... How did you discover Screwattack.com?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Let's see if I can remember. I think it was around 2007 or 2008, I was bored on youtube one day and wanted to look for something gaming related. I had stumble-upon found Screwattack and their website and I was really excited to see what kind of content they were putting up on the site.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : So, Stumble-upon got you to the site?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Hahaha yes!!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I wish I had that kinda luck. All I mostly get is stupid cat pictures and old man p***s.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : That's never a good time that can always lead to weird parts of the internet...Very, very weird parts of the internet.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : What is your favorite original show on screwattack.com?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Ohh thats a tough question because that means we have to go back to 2007-2008 hmm, like for example clip of the week or captain S.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img class="rg_i" data-sz="f" name="" src="https://encrypted-tbn2.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQsTsl12FD_dKJqxV_F-8CDueiFUWoCI-sw2lT6JZ6eD-LB0T5dGA"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I've never seen Captain S... as a Sega fan-boy... I'm very disappointed in myself. What did you like about that particular show?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : For shame!! you really should Ive just recently started watching it &amp; holy crap its really good a lot of video game references &amp; it has a captain N vibe but with a sega twist instead of Nintendo.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Sweet!!! Did you catch the latest Death Battle?
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" src="http://www.screwattack.com/sites/default/files/images/dUSE.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Yes I did, it was EPIC!!!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I gotta say, I loved the animation and the way Godzilla and Gamera looked. Pure legendary.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I 100 percent agree Im gonna watch it again on youtube since it's posted there later on today.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Who is your favorite Screwattack member of staff?... Past or present.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : hmm my favorite definitely has to be Chad because he is a past &amp; present member of Screwattack team Yoshi forever!!! He seems like a really cool guy to hang out with.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img class="rg_i" data-="" data-sz="f" name="" src="https://encrypted-tbn2.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcRFNZ0gc03RiODRO-5TwWB_CJtnS4p08ZHnM45plYsfkoKM2Dh8fA"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I'd love to grab a beer with Chad. And just talk utter bollocks all night. What was the last thing on the site that made you laugh?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : They had put up what they call, classic Sunday. I believe that is what it's called and they would show old clips of the week and they showed this one video where they were basically making fun of the console war between Sega &amp; Nintendo, that video definitely made me laugh
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : What are your fondest memories of screwattack.com?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I have a lot of fond memories of Screwattack. I got to meet a lot of g1's, you being one of them of course, I definitely have memories of SGC, Ive seen the live streams of SGC this past year and last year and both years have been a blast. Hopefully, I can actually make it out to SGC one of these years but if not, Ill always enjoy the live-streams.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I'll drink to that Thus ends the Screwattack section of the interview. And now... it's all about you.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : WHOO!!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : As I said at the beginning, you are a bit of a play-through fanatic. Offering up your own commentary and your own kick-ass style... What made you do play-throughs?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I appreciate the compliment means a lot. Ive wanted to do play-throughs for a few years and I was not able to with my old laptop setup. Since I got a new operating system put onto my laptop I finally been able to. Which is really exciting for me and I can talk about my memories with the games I play-through and I try to keep the commentary going non stop without any awkward pauses and I love retro gaming. I was inspired by other famous let's players and I thought to myself, I can do this and so I just started doing it and its been a blast.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : You're now playing through
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TaUvNodcbs0&amp;list=UU04LRS1IE6yPD3Im5m8UofA" rel="nofollow">
           Sonic 3
          </a>
          ... Something tells me you're a bit of a fan. Is there anything you know about that game that other retro gamers might not know about?
         </p>
         <p>
          <img class="rg_i" data-="" data-sz="f" name="" src="https://encrypted-tbn3.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcT44Hl69ZyT0UIS0IfWEKkhXrcZ7MKEotYJc6ATqTgkNtWmsFyjhw"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : Yes actually and it's my favorite game of all time I know a few things that people might not know like for example: Michael Jackson actually did compose the music, but went un-credited because of the charges he was facing at the time. He said he really didn't like the sound of the genesis sound chip and one of the levels actually sounds very similar to one of his songs called Strangers in Moscow.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : WOW!!! Even I didn't know that! The strangers in Moscow fact I mean, I knew everything else.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Sonicfan
           </em>
          </strong>
          : The more you know *cue the more you know nbc jingle*
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Master Wayne
           </em>
          </strong>
          : I also caught your play-through of
