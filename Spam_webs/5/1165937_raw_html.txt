         <p>
          The Huawei HG8010/ Huawei HG8110/ Huawei HG8240/ Huawei HG8242/ Huawei HG8245 is an indoor Huawei optical network terminal (
          <a href="http://www.huanetwork.com/huawei-ont-optical-network-terminal-price_c42">
           Huawei ONT
          </a>
          ) designed for home users and small office and home office (SOHO) users. Its upper shell adopts the natural heat dissipation material, and its optical port adopts the dust-proof design with a rubbe plug. The HG8010/HG8110/HG8240/HG8242/HG8245 is eye-pleasing and energy-efficient. It can be deployed on a workbench or mounted on a wall, meeting users deployment requirements in different scenarios.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          By using the gigabit-capable passive optical network (GPON) technology, the HG8010/HG8110/HG8240/HG8242/HG8245 provides a high-speed data channel through a single optical fiber with an upstream rate of 1.244 Gbit/s and a downstream rate of 2.488 Gbit/s. In this way, you can enjoy quality high-speed data service, voice service, and video service. In addition, the HG8245 and HG8247 provide reliable wireless access service, and convenient storage and file sharing services within a home network.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          As an ONT, the HG8010/HG8110/HG8240/HG8242/HG8245 provides convenient and efficient remote management functions. The HG8010/HG8110/HG8240/HG8242/HG8245 supports ONT Management and Control Interface (OMCI) protocol and the U2560 (Huawei TR-069 server) and manages all home terminals in a unified manner, thus implementing remote fault diagnosis, service provisioning, and performance statistics measurement.
         </p>
         <p>
