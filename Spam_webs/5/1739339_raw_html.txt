            A Cereal Offender: Kentucky Man Charged with Public Masturbation
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="meta">
           <span class="meta-cats">

            <a href="http://gather.com/a-cereal-offender-kentucky-man-charged-with-public-masturbation/#comments" rel="bookmark" title="Comments for A Cereal Offender: Kentucky Man Charged with Public Masturbation">

          <p>
           A Kentucky man is charged with a lewd act after committing public masturbation in a grocery store cereal aisle. After a brief manhunt, West Point police found the culprit who they say ejaculated on a woman in the middle of a cereal aisle.
          </p>
          <p>
           The woman, who chose to remain nameless for obvious reasons, told police that she was shopping in the cereal aisle a little after 10 a.m. of March 2, when she felt 
           <a href="http://www.dreamindemon.com/2011/04/07/sex-offender-charged-after-ejaculating-on-woman-in-cereal-aisle/" rel="nofollow">
            something
           </a>
            hit her backside.
          </p>
          <p>
           When the victim reached back to feel what it was, her fingers became wet with the slimy substance. Her pants were sent for analysis, which confirmed what the woman had already guessed. It was semen.
          </p>
          <p>
           The man, 51-year-old Bobby Carter, was following women around the store with his hand in his pants.
          </p>
          <p>
           There was some pretty good sleuth work on the part of the police. Carter is a truck driver. He is also a registered sex offender. Police reviewed the stores security camera, then checked surveillance videos of businesses along the I-95 corridor where hed travel; they checked his drivers log and determined that Carter was their man.
          </p>
          <p>
           This isnt the first time Carters gotten up close and personal with the ladies in grocery store aisles. In 2004, a 21-year-old woman said that
           <a href="http://www.wave3.com/story/1854974/convicted-sex-offender-charged-in-wal-mart-attack" rel="nofollow">
            Carter
           </a>
           had gotten so close to her during his masturbatory event, that she could feel his privates on her back side. In this case, the victim turned the tables on him and chased him around the store, screaming to get the attention of managers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ladies, let this be a lesson to you. Never let a strange man follow you around the store unless you want to get yourself into a sticky situation. Especially in the cereal aisle.
          </p>
