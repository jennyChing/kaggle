       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Fruitcake Cut Out Cookies
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-12-21T09:57:30+00:00">
         December 21, 2012
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="10767095" data-link="http://www.createdby-diane.com/2012/12/fruitcake-cut-out-cookies.html" data-summary="" data-title="Fruitcake Cut Out Cookies">
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-6722" height="353" src="//www.createdby-diane.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/fruitcake-Cut-Out-Cookies@createdbydiane-530x353.jpg" title="fruitcake Cut Out Cookies@createdbydiane" width="530"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This isnt in anyway the OLD type of hard as a rock type of fruitcake item,
        </p>
        <p>
         these are a new, prettier, lighter version just in time for Christmas.
        </p>
        <p>
         These are pretty quick to make and ice too.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im sure all your neighbors would LOVE some of these this year.
        </p>
        <p>
         I know I would.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im all wrapped up in everything Christmas right now, except I havent wrapped one single gift yet.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im really all wrapped up in baking fun, unique and yummy treats for Christmas.
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you like fruitcake? Or do you fear even trying it, after all the bad attention it gets? Well as I mentioned on my
         <span style="color: #339966;">
          <a href="http://www.createdby-diane.com/2012/12/fruitcake-scones.html" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            Fruitcake Scone
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         post, fruitcake is my dads favorite, so I not only made him a batch of scones but I also made him these fruitcake cookies.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-6723" height="353" src="//www.createdby-diane.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/fruitcake-cut-out-cookie-dough-530x353.jpg" title="fruitcake cut out cookie dough" width="530"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I used a donut cutter to make the wreath shapes. I got the idea after making the
         <span style="color: #339966;">
          <a href="http://www.createdby-diane.com/2012/12/chocolate-wreath-cakes.html" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            mini wreath cakes
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         with my donut pan, oh they are so cute. Did you see them? I just love alternate uses for these cute donut items.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-6725" height="353" src="//www.createdby-diane.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/icing-fruitcake-cookies-530x353.jpg" title="icing fruitcake cookies" width="530"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I used
         <span style="color: #339966;">
          <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1v9r3Yr_UX7hgtNk85ADDaNLY_uO8QN27fRH7-c_oqfc/edit" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            Royal Icing
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         to decorate these cookies. I mixed avocado green with leaf green to get this pretty green color. I piped the royal icing on with a #2 tip and filled it in with it as well. I tinted a portion of icing red for the berries and applied them with a #2 tip also.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-6721">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
        </p>

          Fruitcake Cut Out Cookies
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cups powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon rum emulsion
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cup flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoon baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup fruit cake mix (the diced type)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            beat butter and sugar until smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add the egg, vanilla and rum.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add to it the flour along with baking powder and salt.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix in fruit cake mix.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix until dough pulls away from the bowl.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Roll out and cut with donut cutter.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake at 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
           </li>
          </ol>
