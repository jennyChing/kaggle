                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       Marketing automation techniques are the ways to carry out a
particular task in such a way that it lead to the growth of successful
business. Marketing automation has opened various new ways of marketing to the modern
time marketers.
                       <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                       </span>
                       The top four marketing
automation techniques or you can say strategies that play a vital role for successful
marketing of any business are:
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lU7NFSJUbww/VGxV6gmkv5I/AAAAAAAAAQc/u9t-_3GrZeQ/s1600/12.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="253" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lU7NFSJUbww/VGxV6gmkv5I/AAAAAAAAAQc/u9t-_3GrZeQ/s1600/12.jpg" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
                        Engagement of Users:
                       </b>
                       The first and foremost thing we consider is that how to engage
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-0">
                         the users attention
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       and to increase traffic. Marketing automation helps in engaging users through
social media marketing, PPC advertising, content marketing and search engine
optimization.
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-1">
                         All
these activities
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       have different roles and help in the promotion
of
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-2">
                         a product
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       or service which further helps in generating maximum sales. Marketing
automation also helps us to
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-3">
                         monitor
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       the traffic, sales and other
things that come through all these platforms.
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
                        Campaign Management:
                       </b>
                       <span class="correction">
                        Marketing campaign is
                       </span>
                       a great source for
grasping traffic and sales. Anyone can start a
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-1">
                         campaign, but
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       only an effective
campaign can generate more sales and can make a business successful.
                       <a href="http://www.saleoid.com/marketing-automation/">
                        Marketing automation
                       </a>
                       starts a successful campaign and helps to increase conversion rates.
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
                        Lead Management:
                       </b>
                       Lead
management is a set of systems that helps in generating leads.
                       <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                       </span>
                       These leads are further converted into sales
with the help of marketing automation which in turn increase sales for the
business. So, if lead management is effective and efficient then obviously it
will lead to success. It also helps in managing and monitoring behavioral
scoring,
                       <a href="http://www.saleoid.com/reasons-implement-demographic-scoring-effective-lead-management/">
                        demographic scoring
                       </a>
                       , user scoring, product based scoring, multiple
scoring and custom scoring.
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
                        Customer Relation
Management:
                       </b>
                       <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                       </span>
                       Customer Relation
management is a system that helps in developing, building and managing
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-0">
                         customer
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       relationships. It is mainly used to manage and automate sales, marketing,
customer service and technical support. Marketing automation helps in automatic
field mapping by automatically converting all the data into CRM software and
ensuring the consistency of data between two systems. It
                       <span class="correction">
                        <span id="GingerWidget-correction-1">
                         also helps
                        </span>
                       </span>
                       to
ensure that are no duplicates in the system, which is important for
segmentation, lead nurturing, and lead scoring.
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       These four marketing automation techniques can really help a
business to grow and can also make it successful. Marketing automation is amazing
software that helps in business marketing and also measure results to check the
value of marketing programs.
                      </div>
