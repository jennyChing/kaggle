         <h2>
          Current Affairs On 12th April 2015
         </h2>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Sania Mirza Becomes World No.1 in Doubles Tennis
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          Sania Mirza has become world No.1 in tennis doubles after winning the Family Circle Cup in Charleston with partner Martina Hingis.Sania Mirza on Sunday created history by becoming the first female tennis player from India to achieve the world number one rank in doubles.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Modi thanks France as he wraps up visit
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          Prime Minister Narendra Modi today thanked France and wrapped uphis first visit here that saw the two nations elevate their strategic ties to a new level as they agreed on a deal for 36 Rafale fighter jets and decided to move ahead with the log-jammed Jaitapur nuclear project.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            SEBI moves US court with $12 mn claim on Sahara jet sale
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          In a new twist to the high profile SEBI-Sahara case, the Indian capital markets regulator has approached a US court with a USD 12-million claim on proceeds from sale of a corporate jet owned by a Sahara Group firm.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Lewis Hamilton wins Chinese Grand Prix of Formula One
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          Mercedes Formula 1 driver Lewis Hamilton from Britain on 12 April 2015 won Chinese Grand Prix of Formula One on the Shanghai International Circuit in China. This was his second win of the season and his fourth Chinese Grand Prix win. It was also Lewis Hamiltons overall 35th Grand Prix win. In Chinese Grand Prix, Hamilton was followed by teammate Nico Rosberg at second place.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            PNB opens 121 ATMs in one day
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          State-owned Punjab National Bank on Sunday opened 121 ATMs across the country to commemorate its 121th foundation day.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Air Pegasus Begins Commercial Operations
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          Air Pegasus, promoted by Decor Aviation Private Limited, on Sunday commenced its commercial operation with a daily to-and-fro flight to Hubballi and Thiruvananthapuram from here.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            NITI Aayog begins mid-term appraisal of 12th Five Year Plan
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          The NITI Aayog has initiated the mid-term appraisal process of the 12th Five Year Plan, an exercise which has long been followed prior to establishment of the new body that replaced the Planning Commission.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Asteroid named after Malala
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          The youngest Nobel Peace Prize winner and girls education campaigner Malala Yousafzai now has an asteroid named after her, media reported.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Foreign fund inflows cross Rs. 81,000 cr so far in 2015
           </strong>
          </span>
          <br/>
          With overseas investors pumping in over Rs. 2,000 crore in the Indian capital markets so far this month, total foreign fund inflows have crossed the Rs. 81,000 crore mark since the beginning of the year.
         </p>
