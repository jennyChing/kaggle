           Joe Satriani and Paul Gilbert Pairing Up for G4 Experience in 2014
          </span>
         </h1>

          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3492" style="width: 670px">
           <a href="http://www.rock-expert.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Joe-Satriani-and-Paul-Gilbert-Pairing-Up-for-G4-Experience-in-2014.jpg">
            <img alt="Joe Satriani and Paul Gilbert Pairing Up for G4 Experience in 2014" class="size-full wp-image-3492" height="350" src="http://www.rock-expert.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Joe-Satriani-and-Paul-Gilbert-Pairing-Up-for-G4-Experience-in-2014.jpg" width="660"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Joe Satriani and Paul Gilbert Pairing Up for G4 Experience in 2014
           </p>
          </div>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Another exciting music project set to arrive for guitar heads as
           <strong>
            Joe Satriani
           </strong>
           is working out in an expansion of his G3 project by introducing The G4 Experience which includes three other top guitar maestros,
           <strong>
            Paul Gilbert
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            Andy Timmons
           </strong>
           along with
           <strong>
            Mike Keneally
           </strong>
           . This upcoming project is not the normal guitar concert tour, but its more of a music camp which will be held at Cambria Pines Lodge in Cambria, California starting from August 11th until the 14th. This setup is pretty similar with recent guitar camp, including Pauls Great Guitar Excape that have been achieving success.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The camp will allow a maximum of 175 attendees from every level of playing, who are interested and have no issue in shelling out cash in the range of $1899 to $3299. Theres also a 16+ age limitation for attendants as everyone registering under that limit must be accompanied by an adult for eligibility. Moreover, those who are keen on attending this interesting guitar camp shouldnt be concern with foods or lodging as the prizes they paid for also includes all of those stuffs. Making things more exciting, those who registered before January 31, 2014 will be receiving free Ibanez RG guitars, which they can grabbed directly at the event.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Explaining about
           <strong>
            The G4 Experience
           </strong>
           project, Joe Satriani revealed that he has been pondering on creating this kind of event for a while now, especially after the success of G3. Satriani stated that his upcoming camp is an opportunity in expanding that previous idea and also providing aspiring guitar players the chance in improving their skills and learn many interesting stuffs.The G4 Experience is my chance to expand on the G3 idea. G4 will give everyone a chance to improve their skills, meet like minded players, be inspired, and make some great guitar music, the maestro stated.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Meanwhile, Paul Gilbert, is also excited with his involvement with this exciting guitar camp as he believes that this kind of project can provide plenty experience for attendants aside from musical knowledge. The
           <strong>
            Mr. Big
           </strong>
           axeman also ensured that G4 experience is not something for a certain level of player, but anyone will have similar advantages, such as experience, ideas and also new knowledge.I see it as fuel for the musical soul. Whether youre a pro or a weekend warrior, this fuel is something that every musician needs. New ideas. New knowledge. And just the experience of being around like-minded people. Thats often the best vacation of all!Paul stated.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           More info about the even can be viewed at http://g4experience.com site

            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             <br/>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
