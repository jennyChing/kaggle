In todays world, information spreads really fast. Preventing the free flow of information can really backfire.
           </p>

            I think its already served the purpose the [environmental ministry] had intended, Angel Hsu, an expert in environmental governance and policy at Yale University, told
            <em>
             Bloomberg.
            </em>
