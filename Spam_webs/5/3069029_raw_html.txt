        <h1 class="entry-title">
         MH17: Are Pristine and Expired Passports a Smoking Gun for a False Flag?
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-07-21T17:30:58+00:00">
          July 21, 2014
         </span>
         By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a class="fn n" href="http://21stcenturywire.com/author/21wire/" rel="author" title="21wire">
            21wire
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://21stcenturywire.com/2014/07/21/mh17-are-pristine-and-expired-passports-a-smoking-gun-for-a-false-flag/#comments">
           <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="30904 http://21stcenturywire.com/?p=30904">
            97 Comments
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://wp.me/p3bwni-82s" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           21st Century Wire
          </a>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          SPECIAL REPORT
          <br/>
          <br/>
          As the dust settles on this latest air tragedy, a number of very visible anomalies exist, all of which have been willingly glossed over by western media analyists and government officials.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
         Putting the current media storm into context, its essential to establish the political motives of those parties rushing to assign blame. Western government and major western media outlets, namely in the US and Great Britain, have beenkeen to politicize the incident and pin the blame for this tragedy either directly, or indirectly on Russia.
         <a href="http://21stcenturywire.com/2014/07/19/flight-mh17-conjures-mh370-exposing-western-deception-leading-to-more-questions/" target="_blank">
          21st Century Wires first report on MH17 provided details
         </a>
         revealing western media omissions, as well as our analysis of why the wests story doesnt add up.
         <strong>
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
         On July 17th, Flight MH17, a Boeing 777
         <span style="color: #222222;">
          <span style="color: #222222;">
           Malaysian Airlines passenger jet traveling from Amsterdam, Netherlands to to Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia,crashed near the settlement of
           <strong>
            Grabovo
           </strong>
           , in Eastern Ukraines Donetsk region, with the 298 persons on board.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Authorities claim that the flightdisappeared from radar prior to crashing, and western media, along with the western-backed governmentin Kiev, claimed from the onset that the airliner was shot down by by pro-Russian separatists with a Russian-made BUK surface-to-air missile (SAM) system, although no evidence, other than
           <em>
            <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #000000;">
             anecdotal
            </span>
           </em>
           , exists the back Washingtons official conspiracytheory.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <img class="alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/resources2.news.com.au/images/2014/07/18/1226992/993514-ca4c7778-0df1-11e4-b832-315b08e711dd.jpg?resize=563%2C423"/>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Questions About the Passports
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Its worth pointing out some interesting anomalies surrounding the incident, the first images broadcast over US and European broadcast networks, including reports our research team viewed from ABC and CNN, included shot of dozens of
           <strong>
            Passports
           </strong>
           being handled, presumably near the crash site, by an unknown man in front of a camera. Were these passports gathered from hundreds of charred, individual bodies, or was it something else?
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Observers may consider this as one smoking gun, albeit  without the smoke,
           <em>
            or even a scratch.
           </em>
           Oddly, all the Passports appear in mint condition, including many contained in perfectly preserved plastic protectors  no damage, no scratches, none are deformed in any way.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The first problem with this is that,
           <em>
            clearly
           </em>
           , a number of these Passports have been
           <strong>
            hole-punched
           </strong>
           in the upper left-hand corner, and
           <strong>
            clipped
           </strong>
           (triangular cut-out from right-hand center front cover) indicating that they are either expired, and are
           <em>
            invalid
           </em>
           for travel  making it impossible that any of these decommissionedpassports could beused to travel internationally at the flights port of Departure in Amsterdam.Watch this video:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
            <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="374" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/RkIEpuy4VV8?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="610">
            </iframe>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The clip which ran across all US and western media outlets was only a partial clip, however, showing the passports to viewers in order to establish the human connection to the crash site.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The full clip, which did not make it to US and world audiences show this pile of pristine passports being taken out of the rear trunk (or boot) of this mans motor vehicle, suggestingthat these items may have been planted at the scene, or used to fulfill a media image required to craft the early stages of this story.
           <em>
            Watch the full clip here:
           </em>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
            <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="374" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/P0wwVkCP2ZI?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="610">
            </iframe>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           It is very unlikely, and most probably impossible that all these passports were collected within hours  from each individual charred, dismembered or otherwise injured victim said to have died fromthisairline crash, and moreover, its
           <em>
            impossible
           </em>
           that all of those passports would be in mint condition, without a scratch, or bent, and everyone without burn marks etc. One could conclude here, that these Passports shown here across global media, especially the invalid/expired passports, could not have been used to travel by all the perished victims on Flight MH17.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Yet, the man going through this pile presents tothe camera  the names on specificpassports, and amazingly, these names do in fact match the names on Flight MH17s manifest released to the public. The names shown by the passport man out of the truck are as follows:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            van Keulen, Allard Tomas
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            van derMeer,Sophie Charlotte
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Sanjid Singh Sandhu Juar Singh
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           In a separate clip, these names are shown:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Vorsselman, Wourter Johannes Wilhelm
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            van der Leij, Jennifer
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
            <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="374" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/QfsV7ZcF2oI?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="610">
            </iframe>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Here is the master list of passengers released to the public 36hours ago:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <img class="alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/resources0.news.com.au/images/2014/07/19/1226994/454472-6fdc9408-0ee6-11e4-ae81-95568c4ed36e.jpg?resize=610%2C938"/>
           <br/>
           <img class="alignnone" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/resources1.news.com.au/images/2014/07/19/1226994/463913-25a01544-0eec-11e4-ae81-95568c4ed36e.jpg?resize=610%2C938"/>

         <p>
          It would be inappropriate at this point in time to draw any conclusions, or speculate whether or not anything untoward has taken place here, and we are only stating the facts, and obvious observations. Readers and researchers can draw their own conclusions as to where these exhibits may lead.
          <strong>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Number and Nationalities of Victims:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <span style="color: #222222;">
          <span style="color: #222222;">
          </span>
         </span>
         <p>
          Malaysia Airlines has released a new list of the nationalities of 295 passengers who were on boardMH17, though it lists 27 Australian names,but according to Foreign Minister Julie Bishop is 28, including a dual-citizen:
         </p>
         <span style="color: #222222;">
          <span style="color: #222222;">
          </span>

          Netherlands: 189,Malaysia: 44,Australia: 27,Indonesia: 12,UK: 9,Belgium: 4,Germany: 4,Philippines: 3,Canada: 1,New Zealand: 1 (and USA: 1*).

          <span style="color: #222222;">

          *As of Saturday, four passengers nationalities  including the one American later announced Saturday afternoon named as
          <strong>
           Quinn Lucas Schansman
          </strong>
          , had not yet been verified as of the weekend.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Official statements claim the total number of passengers and crew should be 298  which means 283 passengers and 15 Malaysian crew.

          Malaysia Airlines are said to have released this list of only 283 names to FOX News yesterday, show here:
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.foxnews.com/world/interactive/2014/07/19/malaysia-airlines-mh17-passenger-manifest/" rel="nofollow" style="color: #222222;" target="_blank">

          Decomposed bodies
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
         <span color="#1a1a1a" style="color: #1a1a1a;">
          One of the main talking points being repeated by Washington and the US media claimsthat, pro-Russian rebels are denying access to the MH17 crash site in Grabovo. Conversely, officials in Kiev claim to have dispatched some 200 emergency workers to the crash site on July 17th, with the simultaneous claim that some 800-900 pro-Russian militants are guarding the crash site.When asked on air Saturday morning,
          <strong>
           CNN
          </strong>
          International correspondent
         </span>
         <strong style="color: #1a1a1a;">
          Mark Stevens
         </strong>
         <span color="#1a1a1a" style="color: #1a1a1a;">
          remarked that his team had seen neither the 200 emergency workers, nor 800 militants, as was announcedby Kiev. Just as CNN reported this segment, the news ticker below the moving image stated,
         </span>
         <span color="#1a1a1a" style="color: #1a1a1a;">
          
         </span>
         <span style="color: #545454;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           OSCE
          </span>
         </span>
         <span color="#000000" style="color: #000000;">
          officials are now
         </span>
         <span color="#1a1a1a" style="color: #1a1a1a;">
         </span>
         <em style="color: #1a1a1a;">
          worried
         </em>
         <span color="#1a1a1a" style="color: #1a1a1a;">
          that bodies are beginning to decompose

         </span>
