          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-Star-Luminary-Craft1.png" title="Holiday Star Luminary Craft" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <b>
          Welcome to an early holiday-themed edition of Crafty Hangouts!
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         This month our sponsor is
         <a href="http://bit.ly/onlinelabels" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #990000;">
           <b>
            Online Labels
           </b>
          </span>
         </a>
          also known as OnlineLabels.com  an awesome resource for all your label needs. Labels arent just for labeling things. I love how versatile labels can be for so many different DIY and craft projects.
        </p>
        <p>
         You may remember my video tutorial for
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/2014/07/polka-dot-tissue-paper-flowers-big.html" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #990000;">
           <b>
            DIY giant-sized, polka-dot tissue paper flowers
           </b>
          </span>
         </a>
         that I shared hereback in July. The polka dots on those flowers were made using 1-inch circle labels from Online Labels.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Holiday Crafty Hangouts!
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         This month, the team members of Crafty Hangouts are sharing our DIY ideas for holiday gifts using labels.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.craftyhangouts.com/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Handmade Holiday Gift Ideas via Crafty Hangouts and Online Labels" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="400" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BCrafty-2BHangouts1.jpg" title="Handmade Holiday Gift Ideas via Crafty Hangouts and Online Labels" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <b>
          Im showing you how to make holiday star luminaries.
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         The stars that I use are
         <a href="http://www.onlinelabels.com/OL195.htm?src=dlc-66" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #990000;">
           <b>
            awesome star-shaped labels
           </b>
          </span>
         </a>
         which come in many colors, including metallics. I chose to use kraft-colored stars, to give a country look and feel to these holiday candle luminaries.
        </p>
        <p>
         Click the star template below to see all of the available styles and colors.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.onlinelabels.com/OL195.htm?src=dlc-66" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="star-shaped labels from OnlineLabels.com" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="400" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/star-2Blabel-2Bstickers-2B-23OL195WX1.png" title="star-shaped labels from OnlineLabels.com" width="307"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I found these glass jar lanterns at the dollar store, just 99 cents each: a very economical way to create a special gift!
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/glass-2Bjar-2Blanterns1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/glass-2Bjar-2Blanterns1.jpg" title="glass jar lanterns from the dollar store" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
         Theyre a great size and have some heft to them. Nothing flimsy, which is nice!
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/glass-2Bjar-2Blantern1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/glass-2Bjar-2Blantern1.jpg" title="glass candle holder lanterns" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Heres a short video on how I made these luminaries, and below the video are more step-by-step pictures for you:
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/wKWC12Po4so?list=UUDDUVRUBGPdO8OgzM7NUdBw" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Distressing the star-shaped labels
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         I distressed the stars to give them a vintage look. Heres a few that I stuck onto a piece of cardstock so you could see the distressed effect up close:
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/distressed-2Bstar-2Blabels-2B-2Bonlinelabels.com_1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/distressed-2Bstar-2Blabels-2B-2Bonlinelabels.com_1.jpg" title="kraft-colored star labels, distressed with ink" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Distressing these labels  or any of the kraft-colored labels from OnlineLabels.com  can be done easily using an ink pad, such as the one I used below.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Distress-2BInk-2C-2BFrayed-2BBurlap1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Distress-2BInk-2C-2BFrayed-2BBurlap1.jpg" title="Distress Ink, frayed burlap - an easy way to distress labels or stickers" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Simply take your ink pad, turn it over and rub the pad lightly on the label sheet. I use both a large circular motion as well as a random back-and-forth motion. Practice on scrap paper first.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/distress-2Blabels-2Bwith-2Ban-2Bink-2Bpad1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="320" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/distress-2Blabels-2Bwith-2Ban-2Bink-2Bpad1.jpg" title="how to distress labels or stickers for crafts" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Whats nice about the distress ink is that it dries quickly. After a few minutes, I tested with a paper towel, and no ink came up:
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/distress-2Bink-2Bdries-2Bquickly1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/distress-2Bink-2Bdries-2Bquickly1.jpg" title="make sure ink is dry before attaching the labels to the candle jars" width="526"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <b>
          Attaching the stars
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         Apply the stars to the glass jar lanterns. Each star label peels off the sheet easily. I centered the first star on the front of each jar. For the rest of the stars, I spaced them evenly  some higher and some lower than the first star I applied.
        </p>
        <p>
         Smooth out the labels with your fingers. Dont worry if you get a wrinkle or two, as this just adds to the character!
         <i>
          (In the video I explain why I have the rubber gloves on, and why its a good idea!)
         </i>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/star-2Blabel-2Bstickers-2Bon-2Bglass-2Bjars1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="320" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/star-2Blabel-2Bstickers-2Bon-2Bglass-2Bjars1.jpg" title="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <b>
          Add some sparkle
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you have your star labels on the jars, its time to add a layer of Sparkle Mod Podge. I like to use recycled plastic lids whenever Im mod-podging a small object like these lanterns. The lid  used as a base  makes it easy to turn your project around without getting your fingers in the glue.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sparkle-2BMod-2BPodge-2B-2Bglass-2Bjars1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sparkle-2BMod-2BPodge-2B-2Bglass-2Bjars1.jpg" title="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I applied two coats of the Sparkle Mod Podge, letting the first coat dry for about 20 minutes before applying the second coat. The glue goes on milky white, but will dry clear and sparkly!
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BStar-2BCandle-2BJars1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="480" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BStar-2BCandle-2BJars1.jpg" title="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <b>
          Add some snow
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         The snow at the bottom of each luminary is leftover Halloween webbing thats used indoors or outside for decoration. You could also take wool roving or pull apart some cotton balls.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/cotton-2Bfor-2Bsnow-like-2Beffect1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/cotton-2Bfor-2Bsnow-like-2Beffect1.jpg" title="use cotton webbing to create the look of snow" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I took a battery-operated tealight and poked it through the center of the cotton webbing, gently wrapping the edges around the tealight before placing inside the lantern.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/cotton-2Baround-2Bbattery-operated-2Btealights1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="480" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/cotton-2Baround-2Bbattery-operated-2Btealights1.jpg" title="cotton webbing pulled apart and around battery-operated tealights" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         These make great hostess gifts or teacher gifts, in addition to a serene, country holiday decoration.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BStar-2BGlass-2BLanterns1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BStar-2BGlass-2BLanterns1.jpg" title="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Im grouping these three among miniature Christmas trees for a pretty display.
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BStar-2BLuminary-2BCraft1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" border="0" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.petscribbles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Holiday-2BStar-2BLuminary-2BCraft1.jpg" title="Holiday Star Luminary Craft #holidaygifthoa" width="480"/>
