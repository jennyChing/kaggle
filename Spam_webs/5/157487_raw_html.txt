        We offer both PLC and relay logic wastewater treatment process control centers to suit your needs and budget. All of our instrumentation centers are supplied in a NEMA 4, water-resistant enclosure, unless otherwise required.

        Ask us about our selection of operator interfaces. The instrumentation panels come complete with all the required motor starters, fuses, relays, transformers, and disconnects. Both audio and visual alarms with push-to-silence and emergency stop buttons are included with each panel. All the necessary pH and ORP analyzers, as well as lights and switches for pumps and industrial fluid mixers are included.
