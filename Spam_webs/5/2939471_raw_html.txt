         <div class="pf-content">
          <h1>
           Lets Learn a New Crochet Stitch!
          </h1>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/third-dimension.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19208">
            <img alt="third dimension" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19208" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/third-dimension.jpg?resize=700%2C1054"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Stitch: Third Dimension
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Yarn:
           </strong>
           Red Heart with Love
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Hook:
           </strong>
           5.5 mm (I)
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Finished Dimensions:
           </strong>
           8 inch by 8 inch
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stitch Multiple:
           </strong>
           Chain a multiple of 4 stitches, plus an additional 2 stitches for the starting chain
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Abbreviations:
           </strong>
           R  row, RS  right side, ch  chain, chs  chains, dc  double crochet, sc  single crochet, ch3 sp  chain 3 space, FPsc  front post single crochet
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Special Stitches:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            FPsc: Front Post Single Crochet 
           </strong>
           (you will be working around the post of a stitch)  insert your hook from the front of your work to the back of your work and back to the frontaround the post of the specified stitch; yarn over your hook and pull up 1 loop, yarn over and draw through both loops on your hook
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Third Dimension 8 Inch Afghan Block
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Chain 34
          </p>
          <p>
           R1 (RS): 1 sc in the 2nd ch from the hook, * ch 3, skip 3 chs, 1 sc in the next ch *. Repeat from * to * across.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-1-Third-Dimension.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19156">
            <img alt="Row 1 Third Dimension" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19156" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-1-Third-Dimension.jpg?resize=700%2C390"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           R2: ch 3 (Note: this counts as 1 dc), turn, 1 dc in the first sc, skip the next ch3 sp, * 3 dc in the next sc, skip the next ch3 sp *. Repeat from * t0 * across to the last sc. Work 2 dc in the last sc.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-2-Third-Dimension.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19157">
            <img alt="Row 2 Third Dimension" class="aligncenter wp-image-19157 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-2-Third-Dimension-e1415476050435.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-2-Third-Dimension-Complete.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19158">
            <img alt="Row 2 complete Third Dimension" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19158" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-2-Third-Dimension-Complete.jpg?resize=700%2C371"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           R3: ch 1, turn, 1 sc in the first dc, ch 3 * skip the next 2 dc, work 1 FPsc around the next dc, ch 3 *. Repeat from * to * across to the last 3 dc (remember the initial ch 3 from R2 counts as 1 dc). Skip the next 2 dc and 1 sc in the last dc.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-3-Third-Dimension.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19159">
            <img alt="Row 3 Third Dimension" class="aligncenter wp-image-19159 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-3-Third-Dimension-e1415476865384.jpg?resize=300%2C300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-3-complete.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19160">
            <img alt="Row 3 Third Dimension " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19160" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Row-3-complete.jpg?resize=700%2C365"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           R4: ch 3, turn, 1 dc in the first sc, skip the next ch3 sp. * 3 dc in the next FPsc, skip the next ch3 sp*. Repeat from * to * across to the last sc. 2 dc in the last sc.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/3-dc-in-the-FPsc.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19161">
            <img alt="Row 4 Third Dimension" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19161" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/3-dc-in-the-FPsc.jpg?resize=700%2C381"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Repeat R3 &amp; R4: 8 times
          </p>
          <p>
           Do not finish off  proceed to the Border.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Border:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           ch 1, turn to work along the top edge of your square. 1 sc in each of the next 3 sts, 1 FPsc in the next st, * 1 sc in each of the next 2 sts, 1 FPsc in the next st *. Repeat from * to * across to the last 3 sts. 1 sc in each of the last 3 sts, ch 1 turn to work along the side of your square
          </p>
          <p>
           sc 25 times evenly along the row ends, ch 1, turn
          </p>
          <p>
           1 sc in the corner st, skip 1 ch st, 1 sc in each of the next 2 ch sts, *1 sc in the next sc, skip 1 ch st, 1 sc in each of the next 2 ch sts* repeat from * to *across,ch 1, turn
          </p>
          <p>
           sc 25 times evenly along the row ends,ch 1, turn and invisible join to finish off.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/third-dimension-2.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-19209">
            <img alt="lets learn a new crochet stitch 3rd dimension" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19209" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/oombawkadesigncrochet.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/third-dimension-2.jpg?resize=700%2C808"/>
           </a>
          </p>
