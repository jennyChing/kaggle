        <h1 class="entry-title">
         COMMERCIAL CATERING CONTRACTS
        </h1>
        <div class="comments-link">
         <a href="http://www.garnersfse.co.uk/blog/commercial-catering-contracts/#respond" title="Comment on COMMERCIAL CATERING CONTRACTS">

          </span>

       </header>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Whether it is to be a new restaurant or a replacement commercial kitchen or even just a new item of kitchen equipment, Garners Food Service Equipment will always appoint one of their senior management team right from the initial enquiry to project manage the whole process.
        </p>
        <p>
         Experience has shown over the past thirty years of operations that such a policy ensures a continuity that clients appreciate. It is always reassuring to know the person and not just the company.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.garnersfse.co.uk/service-and-maintenance-212.html" target="_blank" title="products and services">
          Amongst the many
          <b>
           product and services
          </b>
          available
         </a>
         are things like working drawings and technical support. Planning a kitchen is a highly individual experience as no two kitchens are ever going to be exactly the same not least because of the shape and size of the space that is available.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Commercial catering contracts" height="502" src="http://www.garnersfse.co.uk/images/www.garnersfse.co.uk/Flash/big/fl41364307923Image%202.jpg" width="925"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The type of restaurant, canteen, coffee shop and other type of food service will also obviously have considerable bearing on the design.
        </p>
        <p>
         Commercial equipment gets a far bigger hammering than the domestic version and for this reason the equipment has to be tough and of a very high and reliable standard.
        </p>
        <p>
         This means the equipment is not cheap but one of the services Garners provides is help with finance. Working with some major manufacturers of large pieces of kitchen equipment the company has been able to negotiate repayment terms that can be passed on to the customer.
        </p>
        <p>
         With design help, finance solutions, on-going service and maintenance along with training if needed the company believes in a philosophy of partnership not just during an installation but into the future as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         As a thirty year old business the importance of displaying the capabilities of the companys work has led them to appreciate the importance of a strong presence at catering trade shows and exhibitions.
        </p>
        <p>
         The company portfolio includes complete turnkey packages and venues as varied as school and football canteens as well as office and prison outlets. Whilst these examples are generally not run as profit making enterprises the many privately owned coffee shops and restaurants are and this is where the companys long experience is really much appreciated.
        </p>
        <p>

          products and services

          http://www.garnersfse.co.uk/

        </p>
