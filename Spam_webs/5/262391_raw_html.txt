         <h2 id="@3">
          Interpretation
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3 id="@4">
          Goals
         </h3>
         What happens after the initial mission has been accomplished - there is a need to balance the reality with the unexpected results. When we set out on a mission, we focus on a specific goal, but we cannot plan for everything that accompanies the goal - the typical 'fantasy  island' lesson - be careful what you wish for. The antiquarian goal is to understand the consequences of what is and seek a optimal path to what comes next. Looking at the big picture. Analyzing everything as if for the first time. Focus is on what endures beyond now.
         <p>
          The projection of noble ideal into concrete form of beauty and significance, thus of the radiation of a civilizing power into reach a higher, more organized and productive, level of activity.
         </p>
         <p>
          Immortalization through making the ideal into something concrete and real. Testing, socializing the ideas and seeking buy in from the group.
         </p>
         <p>
          Trying to know something new and unusual by becoming inventive. Its the best time to put together long-range plans for the future, innovatory elaboration, and reading.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3 id="@5">
          General Meaning
         </h3>
         <p>
          Idealistic, altruistic, detached, independent, original, surprising, gifted, contradictory, innovative, humanistic, likeable, friendly, self-confident, impassive, quiet, intuitive, creative, charitable, elusive, disconcerting, generous, tolerant, paradoxical, and you cannot stand any kind of constraint, marginal, resigned, distant, utopian, maladjusted, eccentric and cold, progressive, unconventional, sociable, broadminded, individualistic, intuitively, restless, eccentric, distancely
         </p>
         <p>
          The Aquarian is cool, detached, and speaks his own truth or not at all. Values personal independence and will fight for it with his favorite weapon of words used in a witty, razor sharp way. Creative in a practical way, seeks news ways of doing things. Disdain for the old as it was. Air working to establish freedom for self and others.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Detached, the view from a safe distance, but speaks as if in the middle of the fray.
          </li>
          <li>
           Not afraid of words, nor to use them.
          </li>
          <li>
           Values rational and logical, seeks knowledge and to understand.
          </li>
          <li>
           Enjoys inciting change and innovation.
          </li>
          <li>
           Insecure, but hides it.
          </li>
          <li>
           Focus on differences
          </li>
          <li>
           Liberation from crystalline form
          </li>
          <li>
           Big gestures cover-up for personal insecurity
          </li>
          <li>
           Seeks to locate individual within the system
          </li>
          <li>
           Afraid of being himself
          </li>
          <li>
           Friendly, humanitarian, erratic, refined, artistic, tenacious, perverse, intuitive, independent and original.
          </li>
          <li>
           Considerate, but can also have a severe inferiority complex. A great observer. Original, witty, imaginative, bizarre, rational and aloof. Thinks a lot, but difficult to translate this into someone real.
          </li>
          <li>
           Not so sure what freedom means, but it is desirable.
          </li>
          <li>
           At worst can be cranky, opportunist, rebel, unsympathetic, or irresponsible.
          </li>
          <li>
           Fear of lost control emotions so adopts a certain facade of coolness that can cause isolation.
          </li>
          <li>
           Rules that have developed yourself - either to share with others or to find your way around the Capricorn-like rules of others. There is an idealism around these rules.
          </li>
          <li>
           You want to pursue your goals in ways that what matters is the experience of pursuing a goal in terms of your  current values and beliefs.
          </li>
          <li>
           You will frame things related to planets or houses ruled by Aquarius in terms of your values and beliefs and feel others would be better off if they adopted your ways of doing things. You will readily and openly discuss these values.
          </li>
         </ul>
