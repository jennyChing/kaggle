alt="Portobello Mushroom Banh Mi with Sriracha Mayonnaise | deliciouseveryday.com" width="600" height="900"&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;My parents have spent the last few months in France, and heading to theboulangerie to purchase freshly baked baguettes has become a daily ritual for them. After months of hearingtales from my parents of the amazing baguettes they&#8217;d been enjoying I started baking my own in earnest.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;span id="more-16533"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Things were going well. I&#8217;d started to get the shaping the way I wanted it and it started to become a weekend ritual to bake baguettes after a morning trip to the farmers markets.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Then, a couple of weeks ago, I&#8217;d resolved to make a batch of baba ganoush before baking my weekly baguettes, when, the interior glass in my new oven exploded, while I was standing next to it, with a loud bang and a shower of glass inside the oven and on the floor outside.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;While the oven door and damaged wracks, were thankfully replaced by the manufacturer, I&#8217;ve been a little hesitant to crank the oven up to full force to bake bread since. Perhaps over the Christmas break I&#8217;ll give it a get back into the habit. Because as we all know there is nothing quite like freshly baked bread! Especially a good crusty baguette!&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;A good crusty baguette forms the basis ofBnh m. Baguettes were introduced into Vietnam during the French colonisation during the 18th century and, by 1954, when French rule ended, the baguette hadformed the basis of this amazing fusion of crusty French bread and the fresh bright flavoursfor which Vietnamese food is famous.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;While traditional bnh m recipes use meat I&#8217;ve used big juicy &lt;a title="Mushroom Wellington recipe {vegetarian}" href="http://www.deliciouseveryday.com/mushroom-wellington/"&gt;portobello mushrooms&lt;/a&gt; that have been marinated in soy, honey, ginger and &lt;a title="Lemongrass, mint, ginger and kaffir lime syrup" href="http://www.deliciouseveryday.com/lemongrass-mint-ginger-kaffir-lime-syrup/"&gt;lemongrass&lt;/a&gt;. Fried over a high heat, so that the edges caramelise slightly, and topped with cucumber, pickled carrot and coriander (cilantro) leaves and a sriracha mayonnaise these mortobello mushroombnh m can be enjoyed for lunch or a light dinner. Mini baguettes would also make fabulous baby mushroom bnh m which can be served with cocktails.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16536" title="Portobello Mushroom Banh Mi with Sriracha Mayonnaise | deliciouseveryday.com" src="http://www.deliciouseveryday.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Mushroom-Banh-Mi-1-2.jpg" alt="Portobello Mushroom Banh Mi with Sriracha Mayonnaise | deliciouseveryday.com" width="600" height="900"&gt;&lt;div class="easyrecipe" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe"&gt;&lt;div itemprop="name" class="ERSName"&gt;Portobello Mushroom Banh Mi with Sriracha Mayonnaise {vegetarian}&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="ERSClear"&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="ERSTopRight"&gt; tbs honey&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;1 tsp ginger, grated&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;1&frac12; tsp lemongrass, white part only, grated&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;2 tbs olive oil&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;PICKLED CARROTS:&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;1 cup rice wine vinegar&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;&frac12;
