                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     5 Top Tips on getting your perfect wedding marquee
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5467492023891731875" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      You have made the wonderful decision to get married, and
spend the rest of your life with someone you love very much. Your wedding is one of the most important
days that you will have in your life  its so significantly important, that its
normal to want everything on your special day to be perfect. Whether its a big, lavish wedding you want
or something small and intimate, wedding marquees can cater for all your needs,
to make your special day as memorable as possible.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Wedding marquees offer lots of benefits; they come in a
range of sizes and can be pitched in a variety of scenic open spaces, to give
you those beautiful photographs which you will cherish forever, and be able to
pass down through the generations to come.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      If you have been considering a marquee for your wedding, its
good to look at pictures for ideas and scour the internet for previous marquees,
which have been hired for weddings to get a rough idea of what yours could be
like.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Here are 5 top tips which can inspire you to get your perfect
wedding marquee for your big day:
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <!--[if !supportLists]-->
                      <b>
                       1.
                       <span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-weight: normal;">
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <!--[endif]-->
                      <b>
                       Location:
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      Pick somewhere that you like, and
which you know will give you a lovely background for any photography or
videography to take place. After all, you want something to offset the amazing
bridal dress you will have on.
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      Whilst picking somewhere
picturesque is great, make sure that its somewhere that has enough open space,
so that your guests wont feel too restricted. Its also important to look at
where the marquee will be situated, and to look for any cables or wires that
are noticeable, which could cause an obstruction on the day. Health and safety
is important, so make sure that the surrounding areas are well lit, and there
are no overhead cables or trees.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <!--[if !supportLists]-->
                      <b>
                       2.
                       <span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-weight: normal;">
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <!--[endif]-->
                      <b>
                       Traditional v Modern:
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      This choice is entirely down to
you, but either way there are many styles of wedding marquees which can be
tailored to suit both traditional and modern ideas. Whilst the design element is important, make
sure that the marquee you choose can hold up any decorations like chandeliers
well. Ensure that the structure is strong and sturdy, and meets all the
necessary safety standards.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <!--[if !supportLists]-->
                      <b>
                       3.
                       <span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-weight: normal;">
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <!--[endif]-->
                      <b>
                       Size and numbers:
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      Planning how many people come to
your wedding can often attract varying opinions, as weddings can escalate into big
numbers before you know it. Allocate
spaces as accurately as possible, so that your marquee will suit the number of
guests you have. The last thing you would want is to have too many guests, and
not enough tables and chairs to seat them at.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      Have a look at marquee size
guides, and work out what would be best to have. Marquees can be made to hold a
significant number of guests so this shouldnt be too much of a problem.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <!--[if !supportLists]-->
                      <b>
                       4.
                       <span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-weight: normal;">
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <!--[endif]-->
                      <b>
                       Dcor:
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      You can personalise your marquee
with your own style, you could choose to colour co-ordinate everything if you
wanted to, from the chandeliers to the table cloths, and overall marquee
design. You could also choose to have
flower arrangements, to add that touch of elegance to your tables and
marquee.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <!--[if !supportLists]-->
                      <b>
                       5.
                       <span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-weight: normal;">
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <!--[endif]-->
                      <b>
                       Good planning and less worrying:
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoListParagraph" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
                      <b>
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      Whilst your big day is definitely
one of the grandest moments in your life, try not to worry. As stressful as it
may be, its important to relax and remember that as long as everything is
organised for the day, it will all go well.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;">
                      Marquees are unique in
themselves, and they often come with organised staff who can help offer you
some great ideas to help make your wedding day special, along with the support
of your friends and family.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      Your big day may seem like miles away, but it will be fast
approaching so dont forget to book the dream marquee of your choice for your wedding. They are extremely versatile, and can offer
elegance and glamour, giving you a day to remember.
                      <o:p>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                      <br/>
