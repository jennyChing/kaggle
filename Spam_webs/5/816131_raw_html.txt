         <p>
          Some or maybe most of the people are not particular with cleaning their room especially if someone does the cleaning for them. There are even individuals who are fine with a room full of clutters as long as the bed is there where they can rest after long hours of stay in the office. A clean room is very important because it sets your mood and prevents you from getting sick easily. Your room will never be organized if you cant even clean it yourself. It is true that no one will ever go to your room to check if it is neat and organized but it is still important to maintain it that way. A clean and organized room presents positive aura and serenity to the owner. You should treat your room as one of those especial places where you can go if you are feeling stressed or would just want to relax. Also, your room will play a big part on your day by day attitude. A clean room will give you motivation to run the day while a cluttered room can distract you and ruin your day.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here are some helpful tips on how you can organize your room:
         </p>
         <h2>
          Remove the Dirt and Clutter
         </h2>
         <p>
          This is the first and the simplest of all. Whatever you see in your way that you think are not needed and are just obstacles making your room smaller, get rid of them. Place them in a box or throw those stuffs that are totally messy and not important. Make this de-cluttering a little faster because there are still other cleaning and organizing tasks that need to be done. For the stuff you have brought in your room like coffee mug, bring them to their proper storage. So for the coffee mug, return it to the kitchen.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Arrange your Closet
         </h2>
         <p>
          Remove shirts or dresses that you havent worn for months already. Include those that do not fit you, those that are smaller or bigger to your body size. Put them in a box or you can give them to someone you know. Dont forget to open your drawers too. Remove unnecessary items and merge same stuff together so it wouldnt be hard for you to find them when the time comes you need them.
         </p>
         <h2>
          The Clean Sweep
         </h2>
         <p>
          This is the give a little effort task. You will have to remove your bed sheets and window curtains and bring them to the laundry basket. It is important to change your bed linens and curtains once a week to avoid common colds and skin allergies from the dirt that accumulates. Wash them when youre done cleaning your room or when you have extra time. But for now, you need to wipe the windows and blinds to remove dusts before putting a new curtain. You will need a vacuum cleaner to clean the walls and ceiling. Make sure that all corners of the room are clean because dusts and cobwebs that remain will just spread on the other side of the room. Dust all the surfaces from headboards to computer tables. Dont forget to wash the blades of your fan and mop the floor. Vacuum the carpet if there is any.
         </p>
         <h2>
          How to Keep it Clean and Organized
         </h2>
         <p>
          After cleaning your room, it doesnt mean you are already allowed to clutter your things again. It will be nice to keep it that way so the next time you need to do the general cleaning; it will not be as hard as the first. Keep all the things in place and always make your bed. If children are staying in your room, make sure to remind them of playing outside the room and must maintain the room as neat and organized as possible. With proper guidance, they will learn and get used to it easily. Sweep the floor everyday and return the books or magazines in the drawer or filing cabinet if you are reading inside the room during your past time or before sleeping. Bring soiled garments in the laundry room to keep the smell of the room fresh and clean. Soiled garments leave stinky odor to the room.
         </p>
         <p>
          Consider your room as your peaceful haven so keep it clean and organized. This is the best place to catch you when youre stressed and needs to unwind.
         </p>
