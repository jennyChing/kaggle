         <div class="marbot-15 html-content">
          <p>
           <strong class="date-line">
            June 13, 2014	(Newswire) -
           </strong>
           Absolutely Audrey is a shoe accessory company trading online in the United States. The company was formed in 2004 with a passion for shoes and creating personalized designs for any occasion.
          </p>
          <p>
           Today the company has grown into a global company offering international deliveries to customers throughout the world. They recently redesigned their website, creating an easy to use site brimming with an extensive range of shoe clips, boot chains, heel jewelry and more.
          </p>
          <p>
           Absolutely Audrey prides them on providing their customers with the highest quality products and superior customer service. The Rachel shoe chains are in silver and will complement any black boot, turning it into a modern and unique design.
          </p>
          <p>
           A spokesperson from Absolutely Audrey said "Often customers turn to us because they cannot find the perfect shoe. They have a design idea in mind, whether it's for a special occasion, every day wear or their wedding day. Our extensive range of shoe accessories helps them be creative and turn their shoes and boots into designer masterpieces."
          </p>
          <p>
           The company advised that each accessory is made to ensure it doesn't damage the shoe or boot in any way. This way customers can add boot jewelry to their everyday boots and remove it when they get home, this enables them to wear one pair and tailor it to suit their specific design requirements.
          </p>
          <p>
           Absolutely Audrey's range of shoe clips range from beautiful crystal designs to bows and more. The clips can be added to the front or back of the shoe and enhanced with heel rings. The clips can be added and removed with ease, without causing any damage, enabling customers to make the most of their favorite pair of shoes.
          </p>
          <p>
           This company has grown exponentially over the years and has become a popular choice for women looking for ways to add their own personal touch to their shoe collection.
          </p>
          <p>
           Absolutely Audrey advised that they started the website to offer modern and stylish shoe accessories based on a desire to create spectacular foot wear. Today they have grown into an industry leader with a selection of shoe accessories to suit every customers taste and requirements.
          </p>
          <p>
           The company also mentioned that their range of shoe clips can also be used to personalize handbags, belts and scarves, making them versatile items to have around the home.
          </p>
          <p>
           About Us: Absolutely Audrey is a shoe accessory company based in the United States. This company was founded in 2004 and offers elegant and stylish accessories to add flair and personality to any style of shoe. The shoe accessories available are versatile, original and fun. The company offers an extensive range of shoe clips, boot chains and heel jewels through its newly designed website. Absolutely Audrey also offers international shipping with free delivery to the US on orders over $40. The advantage is that the shoe clips can also be used to dress up handbags, belts and scarves and come in a wide selection of styles, colors and designs. To find out more visit Absolutely Audrey at www.absolutelyaudrey.com
          </p>
