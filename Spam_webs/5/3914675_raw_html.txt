     <p>
      Delhi International School is an institution that aims at amalgamation of international
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Educational_norms&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Educational norms (page does not exist)">
       educational norms
      </a>
      with the
      <a href="/wiki/Education_in_India" title="Education in India">
       Indian system of education
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">
       [
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Please_clarify" title="Wikipedia:Please clarify">
         <span title="The text near this tag may need clarification or removal of jargon. (February 2011)">
          clarification needed
         </span>
        </a>
       </i>
       ]
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       List of institutions
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       DIS Sector-23
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      DIS Sector-23 is a
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Co-education" title="Co-education">
       co-educational
      </a>
      school.It has children from a few countries. The school is affiliated to the
      <a href="/wiki/Central_Board_of_Secondary_Education" title="Central Board of Secondary Education">
       Central Board of Secondary Education
      </a>
      (CBSE).The medium of instruction is English. The current principal of the school is Mrs. Rashmi Malik, under the guidance of Dr. R.P. Singhal, the Chairman of the school. It provides education from Nursery to class XII. The subjects taught are English, Hindi, French, Mathematics, General Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History and Civics, and Geography. The school offers medical, non-medical, and commerce streams in classes XI and XII. Its facilities include
      <a href="/wiki/Computer_lab" title="Computer lab">
       computer lab
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Physics_lab&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Physics lab (page does not exist)">
       physics lab
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chemistry_lab" title="Chemistry lab">
       chemistry lab
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Library" title="Library">
       library
      </a>
      with over 15,000 books, Audio-Visual room, etc. There are separate rooms for Art and Craft and Music. Sports facilities include cricket, football, etc.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       DIS Sector-18
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      DIS Sector-18 is a co-educational school.The medium of instruction is English. It provides education from play wing to class KG. Its facilities include computer lab, etc.
     </p>
