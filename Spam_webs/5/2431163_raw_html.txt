         3 Magento Extensions to Boost Revenue this Christmas

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Christmas is round the corner and all the online retailers are vying to churn maximum revenue during this season as the people are in buying spree. Festivals are the occasions when people are generous to spend and buy more cloths, gifts and other products.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can increase your revenue manifolds by configuring under mentioned 3 really amazing and useful extensions to your online store.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Magento One Step Checkout Extension" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-6932" height="281" src="http://blog.biztechconsultancy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/onestep-checkout-_store.biztech-300x281.jpg" width="300"/>
         <strong>
          One Step Checkout
         </strong>
         People use to avoid complex process of checkout particularly when they have limited time to shop. Better you just ensure that your checkout process is simple and fast. It has been discovered that 25% of abandonment takes place because of the complex process of checking out.
         <br/>
         One Step Checkout is an extension that can simplify the process of checkout easy by shrinking it into one single page checkout process eliminating multiple pages. The payment method, billing address, shipping method, review of order and discount code all will appear in one single page.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         To get this extension today, visit
         <a href="http://store.biztechconsultancy.com/magento-one-step-checkout-extension.htm">
          http://store.biztechconsultancy.com/magento-one-step-checkout-extension.htm
         </a>
         and make your store fully featured.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         <img alt="Magento Order Delivery Date Pro Extension" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-6942" height="281" src="http://blog.biztechconsultancy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/order-delivery-date-pro_store.biztech-300x281.jpg" width="300"/>
         <strong>
          Order Delivery Date Pro
         </strong>
         As the festive season is packed with loads of orders delivery, having a seamless and easy to handle order delivery process can help you organize your bulk of orders easily without any hassle.You can specify off days and can review different shipping dates and times for various deliveries. In addition, you can add comments for each delivery and can have details of future deliveries instantly, which will make your work simpler.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         To get this extension visit
         <a href="http://store.biztechconsultancy.com/magento-order-delivery-date-pro-extension.htm ">
          http://store.biztechconsultancy.com/magento-order-delivery-date-pro-extension.htm
         </a>
         and manage your delivery load better.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         <img alt="Magento Advance Shop By Brand / Manufacturer Extension" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-6943" height="281" src="http://blog.biztechconsultancy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/advance-shop-by-brand_store.biztech-300x281.jpg" width="300"/>
         <strong>
          Advance Shop by Brand
         </strong>
         On this festive occasion, arm your store with Advance Shop by Brand/Manufacturer feature and offer your customers and  improved and easier shopping experience.Configuring this extension would allow your online customers to search and navigate the pages given their preference. With this extension customers would be able to use the layered navigation system by filtering the product search. This will help them find their desired brand product easily and quickly without cross passing several pages.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Using this, you can also display a page having the logos of all brand available in your store. In addition, you can also add as many brands as you wish.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         This will not only add new feature to your store but will also help flock more visitors to your store due to easy navigation and filtered search options.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         To know more about this product visit
         <a href="http://store.biztechconsultancy.com/magento-advance-shop-by-brand-extension.htm">
          http://store.biztechconsultancy.com/magento-advance-shop-by-brand-extension.htm
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There are several other extensions that can incorporate added value to your online store and help you generate huge chuck of revenue this Christmas season! Above stated are the most advisable extensions you must have before this Christmas!
        </p>
