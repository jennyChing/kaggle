     <h1 title="Dental Implants Dentist Miami">
      Why Miami residents may speak to a dentist about dental implants
     </h1>
     <div style="margin-bottom:15px">
      The loss of natural teeth can have a dramatic impact on one's appearance and the functionality of their smile. Using restorative services through a trusted dental team such as Drs. Marlon Pasquier Sr. and Marlon Pasquier Jr. of Miami Modern Dental, patients in the Miami, FL area can address missing teeth. Dental implants are a popular method of restoration.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Dental implants are small posts that are surgically implanted into the bone of the jaw. This allows them to act as a natural tooth root. The dentist can then restore the top of the implant by using a dental crown. These crowns are made of porcelain and are fabricated to match the shape and color of the existing teeth for seamless integration with the smile.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Dental implants are popular because they have a number of benefits. Unlike dental bridges and dentures, dental implants can last a lifetime with appropriate maintenance. They are extremely successful and predictable. Dental implants provide a strong, stable solution, maintaining the functionality of the smile while improving its beauty. Dental implants can also be used to support dental bridges or to hold cosmetic dentures firmly in place, avoiding the shifting and rubbing that can occur with full dentures. Dental implants are used in a variety of situations, and our comprehensive dental practice offers many solutions for tooth loss. Drs. Marlon Pasquier Sr. and Marlon Pasquier Jr. of Miami Modern Dental are excited to offer dental implants as one of the many solutions available through their state-of-the-art practice.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Patients who are struggling with the loss of natural teeth may enjoy the advantages of dental implants over alternative solutions. Those interested in learning more about dental implants are encouraged to call Miami Modern Dental and schedule a consultation visit with one of our dedicated team members. Drs. Marlon Pasquier Sr. and Marlon Pasquier Jr. are here to help new and existing patients with their dental concerns in a way that fits their needs, desires, and budget.
