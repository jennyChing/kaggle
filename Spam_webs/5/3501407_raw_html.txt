       <div class="text">
        <br/>
        <p>
         The internet has made music discovery via random stumbling a daily occurrence and few know the true value of those discoveries like the rising artists who flood
         <a href="http://fuse.tv/festivals/sxsw" title="South by Southwest Hub">
          SXSW
         </a>
         . So we got curious about their own personal unearthing and had to ask for stories. Their answers range from
         <a href="http://fuse.tv/tags/justin-bieber" title="Justin Bieber">
          Justin Bieber
         </a>
         covers, to becoming "Instant ninjas" after finding out about
         <a href="http://fuse.tv/tags/die-antwoord" title="Die Antwoord">
          Die Antwoord
         </a>
         , to being forever changed by Neutral Milk Hotel. Check the video and let us know: What music have you stumbled upon?
        </p>
