           <p>
            Friends is more than your average sitcom; not only is it a million times funnier than almost anything, it's a guide to life too. If it happened to Ross, Rachel, Monica, Chandler, Joey or Phoebe, the chances are it'll happen to you.
           </p>
           <p>
            That's why it's such a handy resource for going to uni! You probably didn't realise it when you were watching, but you were actually preparing yourself for almost every eventuality that university life could throw at you. Bet you're glad you got the boxset now, huh?
           </p>
