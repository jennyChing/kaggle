         The chair is called the
         <strong>
          Cut Chair
         </strong>
         , and its is the latestwork of designer Peter Bristols wonderfully twisted imagination. A reinforced beam anchored to a plate beneath the rug stabilizes the cantilevered seat while three cleverly placed dismembered legs give the illusion that the chair has just been magically sliced apart.
