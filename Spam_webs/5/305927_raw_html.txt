         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          I Love these Sneakers DIY
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          Posted by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/post/author/admin" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Allison Murray
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          on
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-01-08T18:53:59+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           January 8, 2013
          </time>
          
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/post/i-love-these-sneakers-diy.html#comments">
            7 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/009-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          I feel like it cant possibly be time to start preparing for Valentines Day. I mean, Christmas is barely even over, isnt it? Despite the fact that Im a bit pissy about how quickly time is moving on me, I still love, love, love Valentines Day.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its not about the actual holiday Not feeling sorry for myself, but to be honest I cant remember how many years it has been since I was given anything. What Valentines Day is about for me is the decor. I adore little hand drawn hearts and written XOXO and these things are in abundance around Valentines Day. Conversation hearts dont exactly hurt the holiday either.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/007-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          These love sneakers are cute and easy. If you can write the word love and draw little hearts youre pretty well set, my friend.
         </p>
         <p>
          For this DIY you will need:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004O6M8YW/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004O6M8YW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=drealitbig-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Fabric Markers (red and black)
           </a>
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=drealitbig-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B004O6M8YW" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B007GB6WJY/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B007GB6WJY&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=drealitbig-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            White canvas sneakers
           </a>
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=drealitbig-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B007GB6WJY" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          </li>
          <li>
           White craft paint &amp; fine brush
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/000-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          First youll need your shoes  I chose to work with lace up canvas sneakers. The cat assistant is totally optional, and to be honest I cant recommend one. They tend to get in the way more than anything
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/001-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Take your fabric marker and write LOVE continue to write it in a combination of uppercase, lowercase and cursive. Draw a heart sporadically. To keep things interesting, constantly turn the shoe all willynilly before writing so each word is at a different angle. If youre a bit apprehensive, it might be a good idea to practice on a bit of paper.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/002-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Continue filling in the space.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/003-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          And fill in the hearts as the black ink dries (or else it will bleed something nasty).
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/004-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Feel free to sneak in a little saying
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="005-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9231" height="406" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/005-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger3.jpg" width="550"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Looking for a little more oomph, I filled in the occasional O with black marker and then placed a dot randomly inside of white craft paint.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Write out XOXO on your tongues in various sizes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <img alt="See how to make these cute tennies from dream a little bigger. I love these sneakers!" src="http://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/008-Love-Sneakers-Dream-A-Little-Bigger2.jpg"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          And prepare to fall in love with your new sneakers! Happy Shoesday!
         </p>
         <p>
          *** And be sure to come back next Tuesday when youll have the chance to win a pair of gorgeous hand painted shoes valued at $90 in my very first ever giveaway!!!
