                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-T_ZNuivvJZE/U5Tt7qMp4BI/AAAAAAAAEuE/ya02Z4uxoBM/s1600/DSCN0652.JPG" imageanchor="1">

                     We've been doing some thrifty DIY in our Master Bathroom! It's not quite done but I wanted to share what we've accomplished so far. I saved thousands of dollars by reusing the cabinet and some of the fixtures. So here's the before......
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-mqtZ8aLo_Lg/U5ie7MRHdlI/AAAAAAAAE0k/SVQ0PRGmSdQ/s1600/101_5820.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <br/>
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-mqtZ8aLo_Lg/U5ie7MRHdlI/AAAAAAAAE0k/SVQ0PRGmSdQ/s1600/101_5820.JPG" title="After pic of our under $300.00 DIY Mstr Bath Update!" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Here's the After! We wanted to replace the vanity with marble or granite but after many hours of searching the cost was out of our budget. So I decided to reuse some of the fixtures that were in good shape and update the rest. My husband is handy and cut down the premade counter top to fit our space. We paid $84.00 for a six foot section of Valencia Typhoon Ice counter top at The Home Depot. It fit right over the old counter top and raised it up so that it's not as low as it was. It's actually very pretty and easy to care for.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Fw01HMaFxC0/U5TuDPYFj9I/AAAAAAAAEuc/XCqcdxdsM3c/s1600/DSCN0653.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Fw01HMaFxC0/U5TuDPYFj9I/AAAAAAAAEuc/XCqcdxdsM3c/s1600/DSCN0653.JPG" title="Counter tops going in" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-cgOREG6JeiU/U5eR_hcvc8I/AAAAAAAAEw8/cyXtEEoajJ8/s1600/DSCN0677.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-cgOREG6JeiU/U5eR_hcvc8I/AAAAAAAAEw8/cyXtEEoajJ8/s1600/DSCN0677.JPG" title="Used a framed mirror that we had.  " width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Took down the old mirror and hung one we had. Cost =Free!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gqC1hBkIrhs/U5eSBKbMXpI/AAAAAAAAExE/o5mUo8RQXuc/s1600/DSCN0678.JPG" imageanchor="1">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gqC1hBkIrhs/U5eSBKbMXpI/AAAAAAAAExE/o5mUo8RQXuc/s1600/DSCN0678.JPG" title="Home Depot Valencia Typhoon Ice Counter Top " width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     Keeping the old sink saved us $50.00-100.00. The toilet is also gray and even though I don't like them, they're in good shape and will do for now. I do like that they match and the new faucet really helps update the sink. I love the faucet and there's no finger prints with the nickel finish. We replaced all the cabinet and door knobs for $15.00.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-2Whv7Kbhe1A/U5eSLP4wbFI/AAAAAAAAExk/N-h5ixLZPb8/s1600/101_5824.JPG" imageanchor="1">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-2Whv7Kbhe1A/U5eSLP4wbFI/AAAAAAAAExk/N-h5ixLZPb8/s1600/101_5824.JPG" title="Removed the old ball light and added chandelier and mirror" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="text-align: center;">
                      Toilet area has another mirror we owned and a light I found at a thrift store for $15.00
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-_epqQWufW0c/U5eSO_o6BtI/AAAAAAAAEx0/1ugjF8geq78/s1600/101_5830.JPG" imageanchor="1">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-_epqQWufW0c/U5eSO_o6BtI/AAAAAAAAEx0/1ugjF8geq78/s1600/101_5830.JPG" title="New bathroom vanity light " width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="text-align: center;">
                      Bathroom Vanity Light is another great thrift store find for $20.00!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-DKvd86eMt0s/U5eSTDtovMI/AAAAAAAAEx8/CZ0S9e807_A/s1600/101_5833.JPG" imageanchor="1">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-DKvd86eMt0s/U5eSTDtovMI/AAAAAAAAEx8/CZ0S9e807_A/s1600/101_5833.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="text-align: center;">
                      Drapes, pictures and plants found in other rooms = FREE  You can see that we're still working with paint sample cards and tubes to try to match the white paint for touch up. White is very hard to match!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_nhB8bRGj0Q/U5eShzSxU2I/AAAAAAAAEzE/oRztiXsY59g/s1600/DSCN0679.JPG" imageanchor="1">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_nhB8bRGj0Q/U5eShzSxU2I/AAAAAAAAEzE/oRztiXsY59g/s1600/DSCN0679.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     I did a lot of shopping for faucets and found this
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003H48IF0/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B003H48IF0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=glufrehombak-20" target="_blank">
                      Pegasus Verdanza
                     </a>
                     /aff. at Amazon for $59.00 and love the no tax and fast free shipping.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rN6cRGeSnqc/U5eSe3n8-uI/AAAAAAAAEy4/BZ8auUQrOLI/s1600/DSCN0683.JPG" imageanchor="1">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rN6cRGeSnqc/U5eSe3n8-uI/AAAAAAAAEy4/BZ8auUQrOLI/s1600/DSCN0683.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="text-align: center;">
                       Home Depot Replacement Lavatory Faucet handles $14.97 replaced the awful phony crystal ones.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-zf7AFOu2xrk/U5eSiviv6ZI/AAAAAAAAEzI/F6nvD0TUg3A/s1600/DSCN0684.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-zf7AFOu2xrk/U5eSiviv6ZI/AAAAAAAAEzI/F6nvD0TUg3A/s1600/DSCN0684.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Love my magnifying mirror. Here's one from
                      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000G666HE/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000G666HE&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=glufrehombak-20" target="_blank">
                       Jerdon in a nickel finish
                      </a>
                      /aff.that would match the faucet and light. We have a mix of nickel and chrome and it works fine.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9_a31MATqfM/U5eSk9METvI/AAAAAAAAEzc/mgvDtunUlY4/s1600/DSCN0686.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9_a31MATqfM/U5eSk9METvI/AAAAAAAAEzc/mgvDtunUlY4/s1600/DSCN0686.JPG" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                      Hang some pretty towels and you can't see the old towel rack too much.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-iueuHMiz4Rc/U5eaL36tZeI/AAAAAAAAEz4/VyvHnKuhHA4/s1600/DSCN0668.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-iueuHMiz4Rc/U5eaL36tZeI/AAAAAAAAEz4/VyvHnKuhHA4/s1600/DSCN0668.JPG" title="Master Bath Update is looking good!" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     We also bought clear caulk, liquid nails and new faucet connector hoses that were $9.95. That takes us to $215.00 so far. Not bad for a few days work and it feels so much nicer. Someday when we do the new vanity we won't feel bad about what we spent on this update. I might also order unfinished cabinets doors and drawer fronts for apx. $10.-15.00 each. Then just a little paint and caulking and I'm done! It's was fun! Next up is the kitchen :)
