Lets take a look at some real tulip accents and get springtime inspired!
       </p>
       <h3>
        1.Colorful Wreaths.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/tulip-wreath/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194728" height="628" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/tulip-wreath.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Buy one or made one yourself using fresh florals. Although a flower wreath wont last forever, itll definitely make a lasting mark on your door and every guest that walks through.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-194720">
        </span>
       </p>
       <h3>
        2. Modern Pop.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/white-dining-room-table-chairs-tulip-flowers/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194730" height="900" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/white-dining-room-table-chairs-tulip-flowers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Tulips have very clean lines and a posh appeal, theyre perfect for a small pop of sass in a modern kitchen, breakfast nook or even in the guest bathroom! Stick with bright colors for a nice contrast.
       </p>
       <h3>
        3. Girlish Charm.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/plates-flowers/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194731" height="896" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/plates-flowers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        All you need are a couple of blushing tulips to set off afternoon tea time. Sitting on the breakfast nook table or on the kitchen counter, theyll certainly perk up your day.
       </p>
       <h3>
        4. Eclectic Drama.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/living-room-coffee-table-flowers/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194732" height="893" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/living-room-coffee-table-flowers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        For something with a more outside-the-box look, try accenting your space with some variegated tulips. The mix of colors are sure to add an eclectic, dramatic style to your funky space.
       </p>
       <h3>
        5. Large Focus.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/contemporary-fireplace-living-room-tulip-flowers/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194733" height="900" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/contemporary-fireplace-living-room-tulip-flowers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Since tulips have such a large look compared to more dainty florals, they can really take over and transform the space, whether its the coffee table or mantle. Use them wisely!
       </p>
       <h3>
        6. Traditional Touch.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/dining-table-scandinavian-decor-tulips/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194734" height="818" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/dining-table-scandinavian-decor-tulips.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        You can style these beauties to have a warm and traditional flavor as well. No matter the color you choice, tulips will bring a sense of lightness, freshness, spring time flair and a welcoming vibe too.
       </p>
       <h3>
        7. Separate Countries.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/glass-bottles-tulup-flowers-mantel/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194735" height="415" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/glass-bottles-tulup-flowers-mantel.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Some rustic styles can come out of these seasonal petals too. Divide them up, put them all in one vase, it all depends on the surroundings. The best thing is that tulips are so versatile, they fit any theme.
       </p>
       <h3>
        8. Luxury Feelings.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/night-stand-lucite-furniture-tulip/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194736" height="900" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/night-stand-lucite-furniture-tulip.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Spruce up the bedroom with a bit of these posh pretties. All you have to do is grab some from the store and style them on your nightstand. Instantly, new live and vitality enters the space.
       </p>
       <h3>
        9. Clean Vibes.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/bathroom-flowers-accents/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194737" height="439" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bathroom-flowers-accents.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        The bathroom always calls for a bit of freshness and flowers always do the trick. Especially once spring rolls around, these flowers will always work.
       </p>
       <h3>
        10. Vintage Ethos.
       </h3>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/freshly-potted-real-tulip-accents/tulips-on-mantel/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-194738" height="467" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/tulips-on-mantel.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Help create a vintage ethos by use your flowers to transform your space into a fresh, old-age spirited room. Line them on the mantle and use antique vases to style.{found on
        <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/2013/03/tulips-table-runner-pillows/">
         centsational
