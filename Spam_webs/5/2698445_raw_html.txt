          <h3 class="entry-header">
           Video Backgrounds can take your video to a whole new level
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <div>
             <strong>
              Get ready because Video Backgrounds can take your video to a whole new level.
             </strong>
            </div>
            <div>
             <strong>
              <br/>
             </strong>
            </div>
            <div>
             <strong>
              Video Backgrounds
             </strong>
             can be found in most video productions such as music videos, corporate videos, worship and religious video clips and TV commercials. They even make their way into TV and films.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </div>
            <div>
             Video has become the single most powerful and efficient tools of visual communication. It is able to deliver a direct and clearmessaging in less time than photos or text. We all now know that video is an excellent tool to present a product or a brand, as well as engage an audience with a rich visual experience
            </div>
            <div>
             Today, using a
             <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royalty-free" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Royalty-free">
              royalty free
             </a>
             video background or stock
             <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motion_graphics" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Motion graphics">
              motion graphic
             </a>
             is an inexpensive way to link video scenes while also speeding up the video production. Most video backgrounds are special effects wipes or motion graphics like explosions, scenes from space, speeding clouds, glass exploding or liquid pouring on screen
            </div>
            Also, websites shouldn't be a static experience;
            <strong>
            </strong>
            <a href="http://www.anistock.com/Video-Backgrounds.html" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              video backgrounds
             </strong>
            </a>
            can be relied on to increase the sticking power to a sitewithout any compromise site performance. Of course, videos do more than move  they also make sound. If you choose to put a video in the background of your website, you can disable the audio or give your visitors the ability to mute and unmute it by clicking a button in the bottom corner.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Also when shooting a video, maybe an interview or infomercial, one key decision to make is what
            <em>
             background
            </em>
            to have behind the subject. Background video can really support the video theme.Using video backgrounds has become cheap, with
            <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High-definition_video" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="High-definition video">
             HD video
            </a>
            backgrounds now available for less than $1. Hiring an animation or graphics studio to produce a video background is old hat, as any video background library will most likely have the background already in stock ready for download. Why do it again when the web has millions of royalty free
            <strong>
             <a href="http://www.anistock.com/video-motion-graphics.html" target="_blank">
              motion graphics
             </a>
            </strong>
            clips that works perfect for corporate, associations, churches, web and marketing videos.
           </div>
          </div>
