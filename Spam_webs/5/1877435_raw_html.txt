       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Coconut Brown Sugar Glazed 
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-02T05:00:41+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 2, 2014
         </time>
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          by:
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Chef in Training
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.chef-in-training.com/2014/07/coconut-brown-sugar-glazed-grilled-pineapple/#comments">
           3
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="white" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonPin" data-pin-zero="true" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.chef-in-training.com%2F2014%2F07%2Fcoconut-brown-sugar-glazed-grilled-pineapple%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.chef-in-training.com%2Fblog%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F07%2FCoconut-Brown-Sugar-Glazed-Grilled-Pineapple.jpg&amp;description=Coconut%20Brown%20Sugar%20Glazed%20Grilled%20Pineapple" rel="nobox">
          <img data-jpibfi-description="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png?c62cc9" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I adore Grilled Pineapple. It is seriously amazing. I have tried my fair share of grilled pineapple recipes but I felt it needed a hint of something special. Coconut Extract :) MMMM it is incredible. I mean, we all like that Pina Colada flavor right? Coconut and Pineapple are meant to be BFFS. Warm grilled pineapple topped with a sweet glaze, is there anything better?!
        </p>
        <p>
         If you need a quick grill side dish recipe for your next BBQ, let this be it! You will fall in love!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Coconut Brown Sugar Glazed Grilled Pineapple from chef-in-training.com This recipe is AMAZING and perfect for summer!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24505" data-jpibfi-description="" height="1050" src="http://www.chef-in-training.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Coconut-Brown-Sugar-Glazed-Grilled-Pineapple.jpg?c62cc9" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Coconut Brown Sugar Glazed Grilled Pineapple
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img data-jpibfi-description="" itemprop="image" src="http://www.chef-in-training.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Coconut-Brown-Sugar-Glazed-Grilled-Pineapple-200x300.jpg?c62cc9" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.chef-in-training.com/easyrecipe-print/24504-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          This recipe is SO delicious and perfect for summer grilling!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Nikki
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Side Dish
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            4-5
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Pineapple, trimmed and cut into wedges
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup butter, melted
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup brown sugar, packed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 Tablespoons Honey
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon imitation coconut extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine melted butter, brown sugar, honey and coconut extract in a medium bowl. Stir until well combined and smooth in consistency. Set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat gril on medium heat to 350 degrees F.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Once grill is preheated, place wedges on grill sideways to create grill lines. Grill until golden brown lines can be seen on each side.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Once pineapple wedges are removed from grill and placed on tray, immediately lather on glaze so that it melts over it. If needed, microwave the glaze in a microwave safe bowl for 30 seconds and proceed to lather on pineapple.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Serve pineapple warm.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="endeasyrecipe" style="display: none" title="style002">
          3.2.2708
         </div>
        </div>
        <br/>
        Enjoy!
