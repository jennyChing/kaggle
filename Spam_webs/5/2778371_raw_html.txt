      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        The Dow Jones Industrial Average (ticker: $DJI) hasnt recovered since I wrote about the trend line break
        <a href="http://mytradersjournal.com/stock-options/2008/06/22/dow-jones-dji-chart-june-20-2008/" title="Dow Jones Chart - June 20, 2008">
         two week ago
        </a>
        . In fact a second break occurred on June 26th and now the old support level (floor)is acting as a ceiling as the downward trend continues.
       </p>
       <p>
        The DJIAs 10, 20, 50, 100 and even its 200 day moving averages are far above its current trading price. Each could act as a road block onthe Dowsway back higher, whenever that day comes. Im not calling for a sustained rally any time soon although well likely see some bull runs mixed in before we find some real footing.
       </p>
       <p>
        Todays my 37th birthday, so Im cutting this post short to go do birthday stuff with my family.
       </p>
