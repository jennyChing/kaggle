       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Blushing Kiss Martini
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-01-17T14:50:41+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 17, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.bakersroyale.com/author/bakersroyale/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Naomi Robinson
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.bakersroyale.com/cocktail-desserts/blushing-kiss-martini/#comments">
           18 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         Its Friday and we are a doing a Valentines Day cocktail. I know we are a few weeks away, but thats okay, right? This just gives you time to play around and tweak this recipe to your liking. Go ahead, get your boozy testing on.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Blushing Kiss Martini | Bakers Royale" class="aligncenter wp-image-15786" height="825" src="http://bakersroyale.sympledesignllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/1a-Blushing-Kiss-via-Bakers-Royale.jpg" width="550"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.bakersroyale.com/cocktail-desserts/blushing-kiss-martini/&amp;media=http://www.bakersroyale.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/1a-Blushing-Kiss-via-Bakers-Royale.jpg&amp;description=Blushing Kiss Martini')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         As for the Valentines cocktail billing of this Blushing Kiss Martiniits actually a drink I developed a while back and just re-named it for this holiday (have I mentioned I use to be in marketing?).
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-15761">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Many of you know Im pregnant and with that, shooting can be a real pain sometimes, especially in tight spaces, and in particular with this set of photos. For this shot I had to shoot in our very narrow laundry room because of the time of day (yes, I move around throughout the house chasing the light). Anyhow, after my belly knocked over two drinks and much cursing, I texted for reinforcementwhy holler downstairs when you can be lazy like that?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Blushing Kiss Martini {Gin + St-Germain and Grapefruit} via Bakers Royale" class="aligncenter wp-image-15789" height="825" src="http://bakersroyale.sympledesignllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/2a-Blushing-Kiss-via-Bakers-Royale1.jpg" width="550"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.bakersroyale.com/cocktail-desserts/blushing-kiss-martini/&amp;media=http://www.bakersroyale.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/2a-Blushing-Kiss-via-Bakers-Royale1.jpg&amp;description=Blushing Kiss Martini')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         My reinforcement: Matt, who after a five-minute lesson killed it with these shots. Im ridiculously happy and excited about this because as I get bigger, Im gonna enlist him much more to help with the shooting
         <i>
          much
         </i>
         more.
        </p>
        <p>
         . . .back to the drink. Sorry, Im everywhere today.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, if you know me, you know that I love gin, so Im always looking for ways to mix it into drinks. If you dont know me, you probably dont care to know that. But I guarantee you will like this drink.
        </p>
        <div class="recipe hrecipe">
         <div class="innerrecipe">
          <img align="right" class="photo" height="125" nopin="nopin" src="http://bakersroyale.sympledesignllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/1a-Blushing-Kiss-via-Bakers-Royale1-210x260.jpg" width="125"/>
          <p class="printbutton">
           <a href="http://www.bakersroyale.com/cocktail-desserts/blushing-kiss-martini//print/" title="Print Recipe">
            <img alt="Print" src="http://bakersroyale.sympledesignllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/themes/bakersroyale2013/images/print.png"/>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Print
            </strong>
            <br/>
            this recipe
           </a>
          </p>
          <span class="item">
           <h2 class="fn">
            Blushing Kiss Martini
           </h2>
          </span>
          <div class="time">
           <p>
            <strong>
             Yield:
            </strong>
            <span class="yield">
             Makes 1 cocktail
            </span>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="left">
           <h3 style="clear:left">
            Ingredients:
           </h3>
           <div class="ingredient">
            <ul>
             <li>
              2 parts Gin
             </li>
             <li>
              1 part St. Germain
             </li>
             <li>
              1 part grapefruit juice
             </li>
             <li>
              grapefruit wedge
             </li>
             <li>
              1 sprig of thyme
             </li>
            </ul>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="right">
           <h3 style="clear:left">
            Directions:
           </h3>
           <div class="instructions">
            <p>
             Shake all ingredients with ice and pour through a fine strainer into a chilled Martini glass. Garnish with grapefruit wedge and thyme sprig into glass. **Optional: Rim glass with grapefruit wedge and stir drink with thyme for an extra kick.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
