                 Everyone should strive to spend quality time with his or her partner. That means date nights are a crucial part of any relationship. However, many couples dont have date nights very often or even eliminate them entirely in an effort to save money. If youre strapped for cash and are still looking for a way to keep the spark in your relationship alive, here are some cheap date ideas.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://tweakinglife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bigstock-20318699-couple-on-bench-20318699.jpg">
                  <img alt="Couple by the lake" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-6436" height="204" src="http://tweakinglife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bigstock-20318699-couple-on-bench-20318699-300x204.jpg" width="300"/>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <ol>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Visit a local park.
                   </strong>
                   The park is a fantastic place for a relaxing date. You can simply go for a walk or find your inner child by spending some time on a swing set. Even an impromptu game of tag might be in order. Just have fun with it.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Volunteer to do charity work.
                   </strong>
                   This is a great way to hang out together and help those in need in the process. Find a cause that is close to you or your partners heart. You could help out at a soup kitchen or an animal shelter, for example.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    See a play.
                   </strong>
                   Many local production companies offer free performances on a regular basis. Even non-professional groups, such as high schools can showcase some real talent.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Walk around the mall.
                   </strong>
                   Going to the mall can be a great way to spend an evening, particularly during inclement weather. Just because youre visiting stores doesnt mean you have to purchase anything. Remember that its free to look!
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Find a free movie playing in your area.
                   </strong>
                   A night at the movie theater can be quite costly. However, several cities offer movie nights at parks or amphitheaters at no cost. Pack some lawn chairs and enjoy the show!
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Have a movie night at home.
                   </strong>
                   If you cant find a movie playing in your area, you can always take advantage of an online video streaming service such as Amazon Prime or a cheap movie kiosk such as Redbox. If you dont like to stream and are able to plan your date ahead, Amazon Prime also offers free 2-day shipping. For a little over a dollar (or less if you seek out a promo code), you can check out a new release with your partner in the privacy of your own home.
                   <p>
                    Amazon Prime currently offers free trial.
                    <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/video/primesignup/?ref_=assoc_tag_ph_1402131641212&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;linkCode=pf4&amp;tag=twealife0f-20&amp;linkId=VLFZMATA45DND6SI" target="_blank">
                     Join Amazon Prime  Watch Over 40,000 Movies &amp; TV Shows Anytime  Start Free Trial Now
                    </a>
                    <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=twealife0f-20&amp;l=pf4&amp;o=1" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
                    <br/>
                    <em>
                     * We are a participant in their affiliate advertising program.
                    </em>
                   </p>
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Look at old photos together.
                   </strong>
                   If youre still in the early stages of your relationship, this can be a great way to get to know each other. Show your loved one some funny pictures from your childhood or family vacations and tell the stories behind them. If you and your partner have been together for a while, this can also be an opportunity to look back at the good times youve spent together.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Create a scrapbook.
                   </strong>
                   Once youve looked through those old photos, why not document your relationship? It can make for a fun date night and the scrapbook you create will be something you cherish for years to come.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    Play a round of miniature golf.
                   </strong>
                   If you do your research, mini-golf can actually be pretty cheap. Be on the lookout for half price coupons online or in local coupon books. Some places even offer buy one get one free nights.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <div>
                   <strong>
                    See a local sporting event.
                   </strong>
                   Sports are exciting no matter the level. Even a high school football, basketball, soccer, or baseball game can make for a fun evening. Admission is usually only a couple of bucks or even free in some cases.
                  </div>
                 </li>
                </ol>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://tweakinglife.com/blog/2014/03/04/50-cheap-date-ideas-budget/2/" title="50 Cheap Date Ideas for Any Budget - Page 2">
                   Click here
                  </a>
                  for more ideas for a cheap date.
                 </strong>
                </p>
