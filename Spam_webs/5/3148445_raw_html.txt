        <h1 class="hLargeTitle">
         Get More, Not Less, Busy
        </h1>

         <div class="articleCrd">
          <span>

        <strong>
         The happiest people are
         <a href="http://www.bakadesuyo.com/2013/02/busy-happiness/" target="_blank">
          those that are very busy but dont feel rushed
         </a>
         .
        </strong>
        Who among us are the most happy? Newly published research suggests it is those fortunate folks who have little or no excess time, and yet seldom feel rushed. I know, youre tired. You want a break. But doing nothing is not the answer. Too much time is a burden.
        <strong>
         Surveys continue to show the least happy group to be those who quite often have excess time. Boredom, it seems, is burdensome.
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        So what do you need to be doing?
        <br/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.bakadesuyo.com/2012/11/secret-happy-home-fulfilled-work/" target="_blank">
           Things youre good at
          </a>
          .
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         'Signature strengths'are the things you are uniquely talented at -- andusing them brings you joy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          People who deliberately exercised their signature strengths on a daily basis became significantly happier for
          <em>
           months.
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </em>
         </strong>
         <em>
          Via
         </em>
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0307591549/?tag=spacforrent-20" target="_blank">
           The Happiness Advantage: The Seven Principles of Positive Psychology That Fuel Success and Performance at Work
          </a>
          :
         </em>
         When 577 volunteers were encouraged to pick one of their signature strengths and use it in a new way each day for a week, they became significantly happier and less depressed than control groups. And these benefits lasted: Even after the experiment was over, their levels of happiness remained heightened a full month later. Studies have shown that the more you use your signature strengths in daily life, the happier you become.
         <em>
          <br/>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The old saying, those who do what they love never work a day in their life seems true.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          (More on the more theory of happiness
          <a href="http://www.bakadesuyo.com/2013/02/busy-happiness/" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          .)
         </em>
        </p>
