                       </b>

A question arise here that when someone sign up in your email list then what would they receive? A piece of software or a e-book with a master resell and giveaway rights which can further pass on and sell to others with your ad in it. By doing this more people will see it and they will contact you by your provided numbers in your ad. This will eventually increase the number of people by your marketing.

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://sourceslinux.blogspot.com/2015/04/what-is-network-marketing.html">
                      What is Network Marketing?
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7243853612919635256" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       It is a type of marketing by means of which the products and services are directly delivered to the customers by eliminating the influence of retailers and wholesalers. It is also called as multi-level marketing that is gaining tremendous importance from the time of its introduction. Such a building of network is essential for building a business. The members of such firms earn from the sale they make as well as they earn money by the recruitment of new people.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       <b>
                        Network Marketing Organisations:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Network Marketing organisations are nothing but direct sales companies that operate through a sales organisation which comprises of non-employed individuals called distributors.The Network Marketing Company formulates the concept of Network Marketing and provides goods and services to the sales organisation. The distributors belonging to this sales organisation, sell the goods and services directly to end customers. Such a sales organisation expands and grows with the addition of new people who are good enough to sell products and thus enhance sale of the company. For example if a distributor hires three people, each of the three can further higher three each and thus the network grows.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       <b>
                        Not a get-rich scheme:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       There are misconceptions associated with the network marketing. Some people think that network marketing is a license of becoming rich. But, it is a created misconception. On the contrary, network marketing can be considered an
                       <a href="http://www.sourceslinux.com/">
                        <b>
                         opportunity to earn money
                        </b>
                       </a>
                       . Common people can earn money by the sale of goods like Tupperware, Amway products, cosmetics etc. and thus run their small businesses which may be either part-time or full-time. Thus network marketing aids you by proving you with an alternative form of income which depends on the sale of products by you. The sale can fluctuate i.e. sometimes low or sometimes high depending on how many customers are fascinated to buy products from you. Thus network marketing is not a get-rich scheme.
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <b>
                        Advantages of Network Marketing:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       1.A good source of alternative business and income.
                       <br/>
                       2.Unlimited Income potential.
                       <br/>
                       3.A small initial investment.
                       <br/>
                       4.Returns are guaranteed as they are completely depended on direct sales by you to the end customers.
                       <br/>
                       5.Good job opportunity for house-wives as well as men to create and develop a home-based business.
                       <br/>
                       6.There is freedom of time and location.
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <b>
                        Tips for the enhancement and success of your Network Marketing business:
                       </b>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       Here a few tips that will help individuals to succeed in their Network Marketing initiative:
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       1.
                       <b>
                        Be wise in Choosing:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Choose a stable and reputed company. Make sure to check if it pays decent money by sales of products. Do check if the products and services provided are god and have a name and demand in the market. For example, Amway is the number one Networking Company known globally for its products.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       2.
                       <b>
                        Do a research before entering the business:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       This business is unlike any other business. Hence, you must do a deep research before entering into it, consult your lawyers about taxation and returns you would earn and then make a decision.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       3.
                       <b>
                        Make use of the internet:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Internet is the best medium to get a follow up of the customers who are interested in buying your products. You can thus promote the products online and get follow up of people interested. This will thus give you a clear idea of the number of end users who want to seriously buy your products.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       4.
                       <b>
                        Dont quit your job for entering Network Marketing business:
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       It is business based on how much sales you will do and may earn sufficient returns depending upon them. Hence, it is an alternative income opportunity and hence dont leave your permanent job for entering this business.
                      </div>
