        June Real Estate Trends for the Parker and Lone Tree Communities [Infographic]
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="post-text">
       <div class="wp_plus_one_button" style="margin: 0 8px 8px 0; float:left; ">
        <g:plusone callback="wp_plus_one_handler" href="http://heritagehillscoloradoblog.com/june-real-estate-infographic/">
        </g:plusone>
       </div>
       <p>
        As we look to close the book on June and the mid-point of 2014, we thought it would be a fun exercise to create an infographic featuring information about housing and lifestyle trends for the communities of Parker and Lone Tree, Colorado, where the
        <a href="http://www.heritagehillscolorado.com/">
         Heritage Hills
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.praderacolorado.com/">
         Pradera
        </a>
        communities are located. Using publicly available data from Realtor.com, the infogaphic features recent real estate market statistics, showing that the average home valuation in Lone Tree is north of one million dollars, over double the state average, and more than 5x greater than the national average. Unsurprisingly, the infographic also shows that the Lone Tree community is home to an affluent demographic, as the percentage of households making not just six figures, but in excess of $250k or even $500k annually is fairly significant.
       </p>
       <p>
        Over 85% of Lone Tree residents are married and/or married with kids, and most of them are college graduates working in positions such as sales jobs, administrative roles, executive leadership, and also the food and beverage industry. With average summertime highs of 89 degrees, affluent residents, and a family and pet-friendly environment, this infographic shows that Lone Tree is a great place to settle down with a family or retire.
       </p>
