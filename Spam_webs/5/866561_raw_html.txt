        <p>
         Once upon a time... Ruche made putting together vintage-inspired summer outfits a breeze! Be transported by our summer outfits feature, An Enchanted Tale, where paper visions dance in a magical landscape, and create your own whimsical summer wardrobe. Whether you're looking for enchanting attire made for social soirees, whimsical wandering, or perhaps even to kiss a dashing frog prince, look no further for summer style inspiration.
        </p>

         Read More
        </div>
        <div class="hide">
         <p>
          When creating summer outfits, every Ruchette needs soft pastel mint, effortless chiffon, and cute detailing that feels as fresh as flora. Daydream the afternoon away in eclectic print summer dresses and cute maxis or sail towards the horizon in vintage-inspired polka dots. Celebrate two summer trends (like floral and peplum!) becoming friends in the same adorable piece and get garden party ready in a little white dress, a Ruchette summer must-have. Be sure to add a splash of coral or turquoise to summer outfits with a dazzling statement necklace, and adorn yourself with pretty bracelets and rings fit for a storybook romance too.
         </p>
         <p>
          We love structured summer outfits, built around summer dresses with a defined shape, skirts with lady-like volume, and cropped pants that create a seamless transition into the next season. Breathe life into your closet with earthy tan and bright accessories to tie everything together and with vintage-inspired pieces, of course, for storybook-enchanted sunshine!
