         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
          Are Robotic Lawn Mowers Safe?
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-format border" title="standard">
          <i class="icon-file-alt">
          </i>
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-format -->
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-thumb">
         <img alt="Are Robotic Lawn Mowers Safe?" src="http://newsrule.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Automower_Solar_Hybrid-770x577.jpg" title="Are Robotic Lawn Mowers Safe?"/>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-thumb -->
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody" style="color: #363636">
         <p>
          Youre standing in your front yard minding your own business when a robot with three razor sharp blades spinning at 5,800 rpm starts racing toward you.
         </p>
         <p>
          What do you do?
         </p>
         <p>
          Is this some kind of nightmare? No, youre awake and this is really happening, so once again, what do you do?
         </p>
         <p>
          The answer is nothing. It will just bounce off you and go another direction.
         </p>
         <p>
          Rather anti-climatic ending wasnt it?
         </p>
         <p>
          That is just the way the robotic lawn mower companies want it to be.
         </p>
         <p>
          The greatest concern most people have about robotic
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lawn_mower" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Lawn mower">
           lawn mowers
          </a>
          is safety. Bots with Blades just cant be safe. According to Ames Tiedeman, National Sales Manager for Systems Trading Corp.,
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=38.8833333333,-77.0166666667&amp;spn=10.0,10.0&amp;q=38.8833333333,-77.0166666667 (United%20States)&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation" target="_blank" title="United States">
           the US
          </a>
          distributor for the
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.robomow.com/home.html" rel="homepage" target="_blank" title="Friendly Robotics">
           Friendly Robotics
          </a>
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robotic_lawn_mower" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Robotic lawn mower">
           Robomower
          </a>
          , over 72,000
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emergency_department" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Emergency department">
           emergency room
          </a>
          visits involved lawn mowers in 2005.
         </p>
         <p>
          ZERO involved robotic lawn mowers.
         </p>
         <p>
          These
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mower" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Mower">
           mowers
          </a>
          have so many safety features and shut-off triggers that it is amazing that they are actually functional.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Friendly Robotics Robomower turns the blades off at 16 degrees, not even high enough to get a hand under.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 310px">
          <a href="http://commons.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Friendly_Robotics_Robomow_RM400.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[2475]" target="_blank">
           <img alt="English: Friendly Robotics Robomow RM400 robot..." class="zemanta-img-inserted zemanta-img-configured" height="225" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/39/Friendly_Robotics_Robomow_RM400.jpg/300px-Friendly_Robotics_Robomow_RM400.jpg" title="English: Friendly Robotics Robomow RM400 robot..." width="300"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           English: Friendly Robotics Robomow RM400 robotic lawn mower (Photo credit: Wikipedia)
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Even if a small hand could get under, the blades are far back and recessed into a chamber in the body. It would take creativity, not stupidity, to get to the blades while they are still turning.
         </p>
         <p>
          Unlike push and riding lawn mower safety devices, robotic lawn mower features are not easily disabled.
         </p>
         <p>
          The tilt detection devices are soldered on to the main boards.
         </p>
         <p>
          Even the resistance against the blades and the ability of the software to stop them is measured in thousandths of seconds.
         </p>
         <p>
          Since these robotic lawn mowers are designed to mulch there is no easy outlet for flying debris. Pebbles and
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conifer_cone" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Conifer cone">
           pine cones
          </a>
          do not get tossed very far and at low velocity.
         </p>
         <p>
          Some robotic lawn mowers have sonar detection systems that if any moving objects get close theyll slow down and at another point stop the blade before even being touched.
         </p>
         <p>
          All have some sort of touch sensors that when they lightly bump into a stationary object that they change direction. So actually getting run over is unlikely.
         </p>
         <p>
          Another big concern is pets getting chopped to bits, again, no reported accidents involving robotic lawn mowers.
         </p>
         <p>
          Dogs have a tendency to chase and bark, but not attack. A dog actually chewing up a robotic lawn mower is more feasible than a robotic lawn mower chewing up a dog.
         </p>
         <p>
          Robotic lawn mowers move relatively slow, people and animals can easily move out of the way.
         </p>
         <p>
          Although it takes a lot longer to mow, if everyone used them there would be 72,000 fewer hospital visits this year.
         </p>
         <p>
          This article was written to educate people that robotic mowers under intended circumstances are safe.
         </p>
         <p>
          There has been a great deal of thought and engineering involved to make robotic lawn mowers safe for public use.
         </p>

          <a class="zemanta-pixie-a" href="http://www.zemanta.com/?px" title="Enhanced by Zemanta">

          </a>
         </div>
