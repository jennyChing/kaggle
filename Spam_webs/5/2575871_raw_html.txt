        <h3 class="post-title">
         Porsche RS Spyder: Street version in the works?
        </h3>
       </a>
       <!-- Kontera -->
       <div class="KonaBody">
        <div class="post-body">
         <p>
         </p>
         <div style="clear:both;">
         </div>
         <a alt="Porsche RS Spyder" href="http://www.egmcartech.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/porsche_rs_spyder_wide_main.jpg" rel="lightbox [Porsche RS Spyder]" title="Porsche RS Spyder">
          <img alt="Porsche RS Spyder" src="http://www.egmcartech.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/porsche_rs_spyder_wide_main.jpg" title="Porsche RS Spyder" width="400"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Rumor has it that Porsche is working on a street-legal version of the Porsche RS Spyder.
         <br/>
         <blockquote>
          Sources say that Porsche is working on converting the ultra-lightweight, open cockpit LMP2 race-car to a limited-run road version that will become a two-seater. The car is almost being seen as a successor to the Carrera GT, which ended production in 2005.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          What do you mean almost? Well, instead of using the 630-hp V10 from the Carrera GT, Porsche will be using a 3.4L V8 making around 400-hp.
         </blockquote>
