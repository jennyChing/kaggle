         <h1 itemprop="name headline">
          Laverne Cox to Premiere New Documentary,
          <em>
           The T Word
          </em>
         </h1>
         <div class="node-teaser" itemprop="about description">
          Laverne Cox's new documentary will explore the stories of 'fearless' transgender youth.
         </div>
         <h4 class="clearfix">
          <span class="author">
           <span>
            BY
           </span>
           <span itemprop="author">
            Mitch Kellaway
           </span>
          </span>
         </h4>
         <h3>
          <span itemprop="datePublished">
           September 18 2014 5:00 AM ET
          </span>
          <span>
           UPDATED:
          </span>
          <span itemprop="dateModified">
           September 18 2014 6:14 PM ET
          </span>
         </h3>
         <div class="addthis_top">
          <div class="clearfix">
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_sharing_toolbox">
          </div>
          <a class="print-page" href="/print/politics/transgender/2014/09/18/laverne-cox-premiere-new-documentary-t-word" onclick="window.open(this.href); return false" rel="nofollow" title="Display a printer-friendly version of this page.">
           <img src="/sites/advocate.com/themes/advocate/images/icon-print.jpg"/>
          </a>
          <a class="addthis_button_email">
           <img src="/sites/advocate.com/themes/advocate/images/icon-email.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="body cunlock_main_content">
          <div class="image" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <img alt="" itemprop="thumbnailUrl image contentUrl" src="http://www.advocate.com/sites/advocate.com/files/imagecache/stories/LaverneCox_x400d.jpg" title=""/>
          </div>
          <span itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            Laverne Cox continues her ascent to full media mavenry with her latest project, a
            <a href="http://www.logotv.com/shows/laverne_cox_presents_the_t_word/series.jhtml" target="_blank">
             new documentary
            </a>
            on the lives of transgender youth titled
            <em>
             Laverne Cox Presents: The T Word.
            </em>
            The film will debut on
            <a href="http://www.logotv.com/shows/laverne_cox_presents_the_t_word/series.jhtml" target="_blank">
             Logo
            </a>
            and MTV simultaneously on October 17.
           </p>
           <p>
            Hosted and executive produced by the Emmy-nominated
            <em>
             Orange is the New Black
            </em>
            actress, the one-hour program will explore the experience of seven trans young adults who range from ages 12 to 24 and live across the U.S. The project was
            <a href="http://www.advocate.com/politics/transgender/2014/04/29/laverne-cox-produce-mtv-logo-tv-documentary-about-trans-teens" target="_blank">
             first announced in April
            </a>
            , with a working title of
            <em>
             Trans Teen: The Documentary
            </em>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             The T Word
            </em>
            will address topics ranging from dating, starting college, antitrans violence, coming out, and playing sports. Immediately following its premiere, Cox will cohost a live "Trans Forum," where she'll discuss trans issues with the documentary's participants and take audience questions via social media.
           </p>
           <p>
            "For many of us, the 'T' in 'LGBT' means more than 'transgender'  it also means 'truth,'" Cox explained in a
            <a href="http://www.logotv.com/shows/laverne_cox_presents_the_t_word/series.jhtml" target="_blank">
             statement today
            </a>
            . "The cast members in this documentary are fearlessly living their truths and in sharing their stories will send the message to other trans youth that it's okay to be who you are."
           </p>
          </span>
