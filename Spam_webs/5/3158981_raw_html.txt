       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        North Dakota based artist John Lopez creates amazing sculptures by combining old metal and machinery parts. The artist is heavily influenced by his passion about horses, his familial history and his ranch life in America.
        <br/>
        He collects all kinds of metalic objects and parts from farm machines, when combines and welds them together to make gorgeous detailed sculptures about the wild west.
