        <h1 class="entry-title">
         6 Wonderful Benefits from Eating Collard Greens
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_106" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://www.juthan.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/collard-greens-juthan.jpg">
          <img alt="Collard Greens" class="size-medium wp-image-106" height="210" src="http://www.juthan.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/collard-greens-juthan-300x210.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Collard Greens
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Collard green vegetables are one of the most sought vegetables generally categorized as cruciferous vegetables. Collard greens contains highly nutritious contents that are essential for a healthy living and an easy lifestyle. It is very important that you eat good quality food. If you are eating the right thing then there will be no problems. Collard greens are on the best things that you can have and should be a part of your daily eating. Once you keep a check on your diet, things will be very good. Always look for something healthy which keeps you in good shape.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Their importance biologically is drawn from their known interaction with three major systems that take part in crucial processes of human body. Collard greens contain highly valued anti disease properties against medical conditions whose medical management is still widely being sought after. This makes it very good for people of all ages and it helps them stay healthy and that goes a long way and hence more and more people are making use of these.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The three systems that are affected by collard green from which they draw their importance are:
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          The anti-oxidation system.
         </li>
         <li>
          Anti-detoxification system.
         </li>
         <li>
          Anti- inflammatory system.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         These three widely take part in the disease control and management in the human biological functioning. It is important to know that anti-oxidation system and anti-inflammatory functioning play a big role in the emergence as well as control of malignancies development and severity. This is a very serious issue and you need to keep these things in mind before going in for something. There are many benefits of collards green based on these interactive processes as well as their contents. The many benefits collards green include; these are only some of the advantages there are many more, now let us look at some of them.
        </p>
        <ol style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          Anti-cancer effect; Collards green have been found to contain chemical components that are important in reducing the probability of getting cancer as well as reducing the severity of cancer in cancer patients. The studies conducted so far by some of the high ranking scholars and dieticians show that regular consumption of these vegetables has a high probability of lowering the incidence of cancer of the colon, bladder, lungs and the prostate. The studies have identified so far up to four chemical compounds that confer anti-cancer properties. This is a very big advantage and you should take care that this is in your daily diet. Once you have them in daily diet things will be easy. Hence if your intake is good then, you will not have a problem. There are lots of people, who are not aware of these things and for them, this is something which is very good. Hence get this done and things will be good for your health. Cancer is a very dangerous thing and not many people are want to get it and would prefer eating collards each day then getting cancer and suffering.
         </li>
         <li>
          Digestion system smooth functioning; Collard greens contain high amount of fiber and water as well. These have an advantage of enhancing digestion and reducing the risk of constipation. Collard greens consumption help in relaxation of the gastro-intestinal wall muscles which in turn facilitate smooth movement and release of waste without effects of diarrhea or even constipation. It is important, that you have good digestion. Once you have good stomach, then your health will be good. Once you are sure that the health is good then there is no problem and this can be done with the help of this.
         </li>
         <li>
          Anti-diabetic effect; A number of studies that have been done so far by nutritionists and diet specialists around the world are all the basis of this factual data. Literatures available also show that these foods have advantage of reducing the pathogenicity of type 1 diabetes as well as the risk of developing this condition. This is because high consumption of fiber has effect of lowering the glucose levels which gives a negative feedback in sugar homeostasis in the body system.
         </li>
         <li>
          Beauty: The anti-oxidant effect of collard greens is drawn from their contents of Vitamin A, C &amp; E. These anti-oxidants play a vital role in ensuring the attainment of smooth skin, strong and colorful hair. The frequent consumption of collard green can guarantee the persistence of good looking skin which in turn enhances ones beauty. Existing literature and research conducted so far are all indicators of this great breakthrough. In addition to the above, it is widely documented that iron is a great contributing factor to the health of hair. Collard greens contain iron in plenty which can help counter the effect of iron deficiency in the unhealthy individuals.
         </li>
         <li>
          Enhanced good sleep and mood behaviors: The studies that have so far been conducted on collard greens reveal that these vegetables just like some of the cruciferous family vegetables contain Choline. This chemical has a positive effect on relaxing muscles, enhancing nerve impulse transmission and regulation of moods and mood patterns. The Choline effect enables one experience calm nights and steady cerebral functioning due to the enhanced acetylcholine conductivity. Acetylcholine is chemical found at the synaptic junction of nerves that enable nerve impulses transmission. This can keep you in good health and once, you have proper and sound sleep then things become easy.
         </li>
         <li>
          Finally, consumption of collard green is of great importance for healthy bones and stability. These vegetables contain very high amount of vitamin K as well as Vitamin C. These play a very vital role in the healthy bone development and growth. It is a very serious thing and you should keep that in mind.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         It is recommended that one takes at least up to a cup of collard greens in a day for maximum benefits. The minerals, Vitamins and other nutrients present are of great relevance when consumed in the right amount. Once you have these then things will be very good for your health.
        </p>
