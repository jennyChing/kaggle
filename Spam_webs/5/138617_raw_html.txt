                     Chocolate Chip Shortbread Hearts
                    </h3>

                     <div class="post-header-line-1">

                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                       With Valentine's Day just a week away I've used it as an excuse to pretty up some chocolate shortbread. Of course you could just eat these without dipping them in chocolate but pretty food always tastes much better in my opinion.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://s1123.photobucket.com/user/heljessup/media/food/chocolatechipshortbread_zps4e815a09.jpg.html" target="_blank">
                       <img alt="Chocolate Chip Shortbread Hearts" border="0" src="http://i1123.photobucket.com/albums/l559/heljessup/food/chocolatechipshortbread_zps4e815a09.jpg" title="" width="700"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Courier New, Courier, monospace; font-size: large;">
                      Chocolate Chip Shortbread
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                      <i>
                       120g butter, softened
                      </i>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                      <i>
                       120g caster sugar
                      </i>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                      <i>
                       165g plain flour
                      </i>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                      <i>
                       15g cocoa powder
                      </i>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                      <i>
                       50g chocolate chips
                      </i>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <ul>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Beat the butter and sugar together until creamy
                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Stir in the flour and cocoa powder and mix well
                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Stir in the chocolate chips
                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Roll out on a floured surface to approx 5-6mm thick
                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Cut out shapes with a cookie cutter &amp; place on a lined baking sheet, spaced apart
                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Bake in a preheated 180C oven for approximately 12 minutes
                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                        Allow to cool on a wire rack before decorating
                       </span>
                      </li>
                     </ul>
                     <br/>
                     <i style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </i>
                     <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                      I chose to decorate them with white chocolate and sprinkles by dipping one side of the shortbread into melted white chocolate.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
