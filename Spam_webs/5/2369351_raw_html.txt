          <h1>
           50 Date Ideas to Escape the Ordinary
          </h1>
          <div class="article__header-info">
           <b>
            <time datetime="2014-09-03" pubdate="">
             September 3rd, 2014
            </time>
           </b>

          <div class="clear">

        <div class="article__body">
         <div class="article__content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.welovedates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/photodune-229732-happy-couple-cook-in-kitchen-with-cookbook-s.jpg">
            <img alt="Happy couple cook in kitchen with cookbook" class="alignnone wp-image-18236 size-full" height="632" src="http://www.welovedates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/photodune-229732-happy-couple-cook-in-kitchen-with-cookbook-s.jpg" width="948"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Movies, dinner, drinks, coffeedating can get really boring and repetitive. Here are 50 date ideas that will get you out of your rut!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            1. Do a jigsaw puzzle.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Put your problem solving to work. You guys will make a great team!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            2. Eat a participatory dinner.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Lots of ethnic restaurants, such as Korean restaurants and Japanese steakhouses, offer dinners that a real cultural experience. Why not add a twist to your average date night?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            3. Play miniature golf.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Find a competitive streak you didnt know you had when you play a few holes of mini-golf.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            4. Play laser tag.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Remember laser tag, your favorite activity for your friends middle school birthday party? Its still lots of fun these days, too!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            5. Take a bartending class.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Do you think you know how to make a few drinks? (No, rum &amp; coke and vodka soda dont count!) Take a class together, then reap the rewards when youre done.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            6. Take a self defense class.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Kick some ass this date night.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            7. Do a scavenger hunt.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           For varsity level scavenger hunting, use a theme like important places for your relationship, or historic sites.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            8. Explore your neighborhood.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You might be surprised what you find walking around your own neighborhood.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            9. Have a progressive dinner.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Dinner doesnt have to be over in an hour. Take a whole evening to have a dinner adventure, starting with appetizers at one place (pro tip: start before 6pm to get some great happy hour deals!), the main course at another, then end the night with dessert or drinks in a third location.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            10. Offer to babysit.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Give some friends a date night sans kids while you and your SO stay in and get in touch with your inner child.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            11. Make cooking a game.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           See who can make the best spaghetti, burger, or pot of chili, or compete to make the bestdish using the same set of ingredients.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            12. Invent a drinking game for your favorite TV show.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Nuff said.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            13. Play pinball.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Cheap. Fun. Whats not to love?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            14. Get a caricature.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           A great evening activity, plusa new piece of art for the apartment.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            15. Get acupuncture.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Acupuncture lets yourelax together as a couple and do something completely unusual.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            16. Take a staycation.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Head to
           <a href="http://www.groupon.com/" target="_blank">
            Groupon
           </a>
           and find a great deal on a hotel in town, then spend a weekend relaxing without even having to go far from home.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            17. Paint a picture.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           BYOB painting and wine parties are all the rage. Find a spot locally and create some boozy masterpieces.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            18. Take a walk in a park.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Its not just an expression. Find a park in your town and enjoy your usual evening chat in a new setting.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            19. Read a book together.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Whether you read book club style, listen to an audiobook, or take turns reading aloud, getting lost in a fictional world together is a great way to spend a fewevenings.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            20. Take a hike.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           No, I dont mean to be rude. I mean literally go on a hike and enjoy the great outdoors.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            21. Eat at a food truck.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Many cities even have a special night where all the food trucks are in one spot so you have choices galore.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            22. Try an exotic cuisine that youve never tried before.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Ethiopian, anyone?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            23. Visit the farmers market.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Fall in love with arugula and support local businesses.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            24. Sneak into a hot tub.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           If your apartment doesnt have a hot tub, sneak into a hotel that does.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            25. Go to a biker bar.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You know that dodgyspot surrounded by Harleys every weekend? Pay it a visit and enjoya cold beer.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            26. Play Sporcle together.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Online quiz site
           <a href="http://www.sporcle.com/" target="_blank">
            Sporcle
           </a>
           is even more addictive than Reddit.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            27. Have dessert for dinner.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Being a grown up is rough sometimes, but at least you get to eat whatever you want for dinner. Celebrate with some froyo or brownies.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            28. Plan a trip.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You dont need to have any real plans to go. Just let your imagination run wild!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            29. Play an online game against each other right next to each other.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Words with Friends is even more annoying when you lose sitting right next to the winner.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            30. Plant something.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You can even plant a few small herbs
           <a href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/how-to-make-a-o-35198" target="_blank">
            inside
           </a>
           or on your
           <a href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/gardening-without-a-garden-10-ideas-for-your-patio-or-balcony-renters-solutions-167221" target="_blank">
            balcony
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            31. Go to a random convention.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Check your citys convention city calendar and get tickets to whatevers happening. I promise you wont be disappointed!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            32. Find the best deal in town.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Find that $3 half-pound burger and fries only served between 4 and 6pm on Sundays.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            33. Go to a Meetup.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Find a local
           <a href="http://www.meetup.com/" target="_blank">
            meetup
           </a>
           for one of you and your partners mutual interests. Its a great place tolearn and meet new people!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            34.Look around at the Asian supermarket.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Bonus points if you buy items to make an authentic Asian recipe.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            35. Run around town together.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Youve got to run those errands anyway, right? Grab some Starbucks on the way, then have lunch together when youre done.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            36. Take turns trying a new exercise routine.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Whether its yoga, free weights, or kickboxing, show each other how you prefer to get sweaty.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            37. Go to the zoo.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Lions and tigers and bears? Oh yeah!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            38. Volunteer at an event.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Lending a helping hand at your local non-profit is a great way to spend free time together.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            39. Go to a local science exploration museum.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Most cities have science museums with interactive educational exhibits. Learn about geology and anatomy while spending some quality time together.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            40. Explore your local library or bookstore.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Pick out a book for each other to read.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            41. Get crafty.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You dont need to be a DIY expert. Just find an activity online that seems interesting, cheap, and fun for both of you and give it a whirl.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            42. Attend a game.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Head to a baseball, football, or soccer game. Look for weeknight baseball games where there might be deals on beers and food.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            43. Take part in a hot dog eating contest.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           You dont even have to try to win. Participation fees are often reasonable, and sometimes you even get a T-shirt that says you participated!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            44. Do some indoor rock climbing.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Show off that upper body strength at an indoor rock climbing facility.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            45. Have a coffee shop afternoon.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Spend the day at a coffee shop with your books or laptops and relax.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            46. Serve at a soup kitchen.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Helping others can bring couples closer together.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            47. Play video games.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Borrow your friends Nintendo Wii and let the games begin.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            48. Sing karaoke.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Or if its not your thing, watch other people sing.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            49. Go ice skating.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Classic and fun.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            50. Have a Photo Booth shoot.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Have fun at home with Photobooth or, better yet, find a photo booth in town and say cheese.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             What are your favorite out-of-the-ordinary date ideas?
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
