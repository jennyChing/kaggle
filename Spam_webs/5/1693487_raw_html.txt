      <h1>
       Lighten Up with Automated Shade Systems: [INFOGRAPHIC]
      </h1>
      <div class="byline">
       Posted on 2/27/2014
      </div>
      <div class="content">
       <span>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Have you ever noticed that your moods are vastly different on sunny days as opposed to cloudy days? This isnt just a coincidence. Exposure to natural daylight increases levels of serotonin, a feel-good hormone that can ward off the symptoms of depression. In places with long winters such as Milwaukee, getting plenty of natural daylight can help reduce the occurrence of seasonal affective disorder. However, there are times when sunlight can cause glare and disrupt your daily activities. Installing automated shade systems on your windows and doors can improve your mood as well as the look of your home, as youll easily be able to let sunlight in or block it out. Automated shade systems are ideal for
         <a href="http://www.techteriorswisconsin.com/811035/2014/02/20/planning-tips-for-your-new-home-theater.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Tips for Planning a Home Theater - techteriorswisconsin.com">
          home theaters
         </a>
         , bedrooms, kitchens, and anywhere else you want to easily control the amount of light that comes into your home. Take a look at this infographic from the Milwaukee automated shade installation experts at Techteriors, LLC to learn more about automated window treatments. Please share with your friends and family!
        </p>
        <p>
