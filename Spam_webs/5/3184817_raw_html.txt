              <strong>

             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span class="font-size-3">
              <span>
               The death was ruled a homicide by the medical examiner's office. It was determined that Garner died from
              </span>
              "compression of neck (chokehold), compression of chest and prone positioning during physical restraint by police."
             </span>

             <span class="font-size-3">
              During a news conference on Wednesday, December 3,
              <strong>
               President Obama
              </strong>
              addressed the grand jury's decision.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>

"[Eric Garner's death reflects]concern on the part of too many minority communities that law enforcement is not working with them, and dealing with them in a fair way."
