					&lt;div class="entry-content"&gt;
						&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;The &lt;a href="http://samratnepal.com/Nepal/trekking/everest_panorama_trekking.htm" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Sagarmatha National Park&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt; entry fee has been increased by 13% effective from Monday, 16th March 2015. According to the office of Sagarmatha National Park at Jorosalle, the increment was done based on the Financial law of Nepal 2071. Although the new rate was restructured from 1st March, the new increment has been implemented only from 15th March in the &lt;a href="http://samratnepal.com/Nepal/expedition/everest_expedition.htm" target="_blank"&gt;Khumbu area&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.samratnepal.com/Nepal/trekking/everest_base_camp_trek20.htm" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-589" src="http://samratnepal.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Everest-KalaPatthar.jpg" alt="Everest-KalaPatthar" width="1280" height="960" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;With the increment, the entry fee for visitors from SAARC countries will be Rs.1615 whereas the visitors from the third country need to pay Rs. 3390 and for Helicopter landing , it will charge Rs. 2260 . However, the entry fee for porter will remain the same. According to the office, the entry fee for porters was increased last year only.&lt;/p&gt;
