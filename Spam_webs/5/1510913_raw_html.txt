       <img alt="post-5520 thumbnail" height="400" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Tower-Hill-COVER-large.jpg" width="675"/>
       <p class="author">
        Posted by the Mass Office of Travel and Tourism
       </p>
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <div>
        <p>
         Taking a stroll outside is always a good way for you to give yourself some down time.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        With green shoots popping up around us and flowers just beginning to blossom, the next few months will present the perfect opportunity to explore Massachusetts various verdant gardens.
       </p>
       <p>
        For Mothers Day or any other day, from Beverly to the Berkshires, here are seven spots where you can smell the roses (or a few other flowers, at least) and let your mind unwind. Enjoy!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.thetrustees.org/places-to-visit/cape-cod-islands/mytoi.html" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Mytoi Garden in Edgartown
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        If youre taking a trip to Marthas Vineyard in the near future, be sure to immerse yourself in Mytoi, a truly mesmerizing Japanese-style garden with delightful pathways, inviting views and a quaint footbridge that leads to a private island.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Mytoi.jpg">
         <img alt="Mytoi" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5521" height="683" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Mytoi.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Footbridge in Mytoi Garden
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        Close by, you can also find the
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/business/804/" target="_blank">
         Cape Poge Wildlife Refuge
        </a>
        for a little more exploring, the
        <a href="http://edgartowninn.com/" target="_blank">
         Edgartown Inn
        </a>
        for an overnight and the
        <a href="http://theseafoodshanty.com/" target="_blank">
         Seafood Shanty
        </a>
        for a mid-day bite.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.towerhillbg.org/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Tower Hill Botanic Garden in Boylston
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Just a 15-minute drive from Worcester, Tower Hill Botanic Garden offers elegant views of native perennials spread across 132 pristine acres. This year-round spot, known to many as a living museum of plants, is especially lovely outdoors during the warmer months. Nearby, you can enjoy casual fare at
        <a href="http://www.oconnorsrestaurant.com/" target="_blank">
         OConnors Restaurant &amp; Bar
        </a>
        or extend your day of fresh air at the
        <a href="http://www.tougasfarm.com/" target="_blank">
         Tougas Family Farm
        </a>
        . If youre spending a few days in the area, the
        <a href="http://www.beechwoodhotel.com/" target="_blank">
         Beechwood Hotel
        </a>
        will keep you comfy.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Tower-Hill.jpg">
         <img alt="Tower Hill" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5522" height="676" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Tower-Hill.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Tower Hill Botanic Garden Mountain Laurel
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.berkshirebotanical.org/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Berkshire Botanical Garden in Stockbridge
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        This Berkshires haven not only educates its visitors on the value of conservationism and environmentalism, but also serves as a peaceful escape for the avid nature lover. Stockbridges best-known botanical garden is a standout in a region that counts many scenic vistas, and its also a location that consistently hosts
        <a href="http://www.berkshirebotanical.org/see-and-do/featured-events/" target="_blank">
         special events
        </a>
        , which are always good to check before any visit.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Berkshires-Botanic.jpg">
         <img alt="Berkshires Botanic" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5523" height="409" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Berkshires-Botanic.jpg" width="900"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Berkshire Botanical Garden in spring
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        To take a look around before you stop by, click
        <a href="http://www.berkshirebotanical.org/news/whats-in-bloom/" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        . And for a peek at lodging options and food stops in Stockbridge, click
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/stay/by-region/?stype=1&amp;cid=11&amp;tid=0&amp;gid=0&amp;sid=0&amp;rid=0&amp;attr1=0&amp;attr2=0&amp;so=0&amp;map=0&amp;pnum=1&amp;dnum=20&amp;mid=400" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/eat/by-region/?stype=1&amp;cid=13&amp;tid=0&amp;gid=0&amp;sid=0&amp;rid=0&amp;attr1=0&amp;attr2=0&amp;so=0&amp;map=0&amp;pnum=1&amp;dnum=20&amp;mid=400" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://arboretum.harvard.edu/" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Arnold Arboretum of Harvard University in Jamaica Plain
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Home to more than 176 varieties of lilacs, Arnold Arboretum is one of Massachusetts most historic natural sites. Since its founding in 1872, this nationally recognized landmark and research facility has preserved hundreds of acres of diverse floral plants, trees, vines, and much more.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Arnold.jpg">
         <img alt="Arnold" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5524" height="628" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Arnold.jpg" width="628"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Arnold Arboretum landscape
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        For a day filled with the arts, consider adding a customized private art tour with
        <a href="http://www.mws-boston.org/" target="_blank">
         My Art Boston
        </a>
        . If youre in the area during summer, the
        <a href="http://www.emeraldnecklace.org/calendar/events/summer-sundays-2013/" target="_blank">
         Emerald Necklace Conservancy
        </a>
        often hosts special events as well. And of course, a trip to Jamaica Plain is never complete without a stop at the original JP Licks (more food options
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/eat/by-region/?stype=1&amp;cid=13&amp;tid=0&amp;gid=0&amp;sid=0&amp;rid=0&amp;attr1=0&amp;attr2=0&amp;so=0&amp;map=0&amp;pnum=1&amp;dnum=20&amp;mid=198" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        )! To rest your head in a similarly pastoral setting, consider a stay at
        <a href="http://www.taylorhouse.com/" target="_blank">
         The Taylor House Bed &amp; Breakfast
        </a>
        , which has some delightful gardens of its own.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.thetrustees.org/places-to-visit/northeast-ma/long-hill.html" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Long Hill Arboretum in Beverly
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Formerly an early 20
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        -century summertime family retreat, this North Shore spot showcases an impressive collection of rare tree and shrub species. Each garden, or room, surrounds the Federal-style estate, a spectacle in itself for those with an appreciation for New Englands rich architectural history.
       </p>
       <p>
        An apple orchard, peaceful meadow, organic farm, childrens garden and scenic loop trail are among the other features that make Long Hill a springtime favorite for many. For more attractions in the area, click
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/explore/by-region/?stype=1&amp;cid=1&amp;tid=0&amp;gid=0&amp;sid=0&amp;rid=0&amp;attr1=0&amp;attr2=0&amp;so=0&amp;map=0&amp;pnum=1&amp;dnum=20&amp;mid=39" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        , and for a list of suitable lodgings, just click
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/stay/by-region/?stype=1&amp;cid=11&amp;tid=0&amp;gid=0&amp;sid=0&amp;rid=0&amp;attr1=0&amp;attr2=0&amp;so=0&amp;map=0&amp;pnum=1&amp;dnum=20&amp;mid=39" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Beverly.jpg">
         <img alt="Beverly" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5525" height="688" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Beverly.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Flowers in Long Hill Arboretum
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.thetrustees.org/places-to-visit/berkshires/ashintully-gardens.html" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Ashintully Gardens in Tyringham
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Much like other great sites, the Ashintully Gardens werent built in a day. This serene landscape, which is accompanied by a rushing stream and tranquil meadows, is the culmination of 30 years of work by contemporary composer John McLennan and is a true a springtime treat.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ashintully.jpg">
         <img alt="Ashintully" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5526" height="679" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ashintully.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Ashintully Fountain
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        Typically open to the public beginning in June, visitors can relax by strolling the winding footpaths, taking in the delightful terraces and scaling the stone stairs. To complete your nature-inspired escape, opt for an overnight at the
        <a href="http://www.cobbleviewbandb.com/" target="_blank">
         Cobble View B&amp;B
        </a>
        or the
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/business/4244/" target="_blank">
         Tollgate Inn
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.plimoth.org/dining-functions/photo-gallery/hornblower-garden" target="_blank">
         <b>
          Plimoth Plantation Hornblower Garden in Plymouth
         </b>
        </a>
        <b>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        A popular and romantic wedding destination, this authentic 20
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        -century formal perennial garden is tucked away in the areas living history museum, right along the South Shore. Spending a few minutes in the garden makes for some nice down time in between activities at
        <a href="http://www.plimoth.org/" target="_blank">
         Plimoth Plantation
        </a>
        , where theres always plenty to
        <a href="http://www.plimoth.org/what-see-do/visitor-center" target="_blank">
         see and do
        </a>
        . To spend more time around Plymouth, try a night or two at the
        <a href="http://www.weneedavacation.com/Cape-Cod/Plymouth-Vacation-Rental-4329/" target="_blank">
         Bramhall Cottage,
        </a>
        <a href="http://www.thorntonadams.com/" target="_blank">
         Above the Bay at Thornton Adams House B&amp;B
        </a>
        , or any of
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/stay/by-region/?stype=1&amp;cid=11&amp;tid=0&amp;gid=0&amp;sid=0&amp;rid=0&amp;attr1=0&amp;attr2=0&amp;so=0&amp;map=0&amp;pnum=1&amp;dnum=20&amp;mid=325" target="_blank">
         these
        </a>
        additional lodging areas.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Plimoth-Garden.jpg">
         <img alt="Plimoth Garden" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5527" height="680" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Plimoth-Garden.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <i>
         Photo: Hornblower Garden at Plimoth Plantation
        </i>
       </p>
       <p>
        For those who like to spend time outside but seek something a little more ambitious, browse the
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/explore/outdoors/" target="_blank">
         Outdoors
        </a>
        section of our website. You can find even more Massachusetts spring ideas
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/imagine/idea-board/9/" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Whats one nature spot you like to visit each year in Massachusetts? Let us know in the comments!
