        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Flow Hive  Bee Hives That Are Bee Friendly and Awesome
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         OK so this is not a regular ViceSpy post. Every once and awhile we come across something that us just unique and theFlow Hive is just that. Cool design meets organic/tip-of-the-hipsters-spear cool.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.vicespy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Flow-Hive-Stuart-and-Cedar-Anderson.jpg?98b033">
          <img alt="Flow Hive" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1000" height="461" src="http://www.vicespy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Flow-Hive-Stuart-and-Cedar-Anderson-820x461.jpg?98b033" width="820"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Bees have not had a good decade.Bee Hivesare dying off at ridiculousrate. Since 2007, we have lost 30% of the bees in the US and 20% in Europe and Canada and
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          Heads up
         </span>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          90% of our global food supply depends on bee-pollinated plants. We need a way for the general population (amateurs) to be compelled to keep bees
         </span>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          and we think
         </span>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
         </span>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          Flow Hive may bee (get it) a way to help get this done.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.vicespy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Flow-Hive-Inside.jpg?98b033">
          <img alt="Flow Hive" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-999" height="461" src="http://www.vicespy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Flow-Hive-Inside-820x461.jpg?98b033" width="820"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Stuart and Cedar Anderson have been working on this invention for a decade and for the past three years have been fieldtesting their Flow Hive design. The main issue with bee keeping is that itisnearly impossible to get the honey out without killing or injuringbees. The Flow Hive gives anyone the abilitytoharvest honey with little or no disturbance to the bees. Simply turn the honey tapandthe honey drains right out of the bee hives. Easyfor the beekeeper and less stressful for the bees.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.vicespy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Flow-Hive-Honey.jpg?98b033">
          <img alt="Flow Hive" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-998" height="461" src="http://www.vicespy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Flow-Hive-Honey-820x461.jpg?98b033" width="820"/>
          <br/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <del>
          The Andersons have a KickStarter that begins on February 23rd 2015 and ViceSpy will update this page the moment they go live.
         </del>
         It would appear the Anersons decided to go with Indiegogo to host their crowd funding campaign. Checkit out here
         <a href="https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/flow-hive-honey-on-tap-directly-from-your-beehive" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/flow-hive-honey-on-tap-directly-from-your-beehive', 'Flow Hive on Indiegogo');" target="_blank">
          Flow Hive on Indiegogo
         </a>
         ! We are glad to see their campaign is already doing so well. This product is not easily to categorize as one of the seven deadly sins but for the moment ViceSpy is going with Gluttony.
        </p>
