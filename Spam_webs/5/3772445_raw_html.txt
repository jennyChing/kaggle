           <h3>
            Its shocking to see the conditions in which the food served in New Delhi railway station is prepared.
           </h3>
           <p>
            Everyone knows about the standard of food served in the trains but after watching this it should be the last thing to have.
           </p>
           <p>
            The video by
            <span style="color: #ff6600;">
             <a class=" yt-uix-sessionlink spf-link g-hovercard" data-name="" data-sessionlink="ei=8entVKChGcOi4ALkz4KABw" data-ytid="UCef1-8eOpJgud7szVPlZQAQ" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCef1-8eOpJgud7szVPlZQAQ" style="color: #ff6600;" target="_blank">
              IBNLive
             </a>
            </span>
            shows the main cook of New Delhi railway station urinating at the place where all the utensils and vegetables are washed. But despite regretting he does find anything wrong in that as he might be using this place for urinating for quite a while now.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="cook-uninates-at-railway-station" class=" wp-image-780 size-full alignnone" height="313" src="http://eyespy.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/cook-uninates-at-railway-station.jpg" width="562"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            A day before the Railway Budget is presented by the government in the parliament where a portion is kept for thehygiene and sanitation this shameful video comes out. We can only hope that Railway Mister takes a strict action against this.
           </p>
