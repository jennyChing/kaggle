          <p>
           <img alt="the_internet" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5497" height="579" src="//inktank.fi/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/the_internet-1030x579.jpg" width="1030"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Beatles sang it and countless philosophers have claimed it, the best things in life are free. But is it really the case? Well, if youre hanging out on the Internet the answer is a resounding yes. To see why check out the sixteen fantastic freebies down below.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            1. Learn to code
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Learn coding for free at
           <a href="http://www.Codecademy.com/">
            Code Academy
           </a>
           . They offer interactive lessons in several computer languages, such as HTML and Python.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            2. Retouch photos
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.gimp.org/">
            GIMP
           </a>
           offers free photo manipulation software.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            3. Read a book
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Find tons of classic e-books at
           <a href="http://www.gutenberg.org/">
            Project Gutenberg
           </a>
           and
           <a href="https://openlibrary.org/">
            Open Library
           </a>
           . If you want to organize your ebooks, check out
           <a href="http://calibre-ebook.com/">
            Calibre
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            4. Get free boxes
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Moving? Get free boxes from
           <a href="https://store.usps.com/store/browse/category.jsp?categoryId=shipping-supplies&amp;amp;amp;categoryNavIds=shipping-supplies">
            USPS
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-5485">
           </span>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            5. Learn a language
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.duolingo.com/">
            Duolingo
           </a>
           offers free language learning in Spanish, French, Italian, German, and Portuguese.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <div id="adsense">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

          <h3>
           <strong>
            6. Take free online courses
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.coursera.org/">
            Coursera
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://ocw.mit.edu/index.htm">
            MIT
           </a>
           , and
           <a href="https://www.khanacademy.org/">
            Khan Academy
           </a>
           all offer free online courses that cover just about everything.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            7. Improve your vocabulary and fight world hunger
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://freerice.com/#/english-vocabulary/1545">
            Free Rice
           </a>
           donates 10 grains of rice for every vocabulary question that you get right. If you use AdBlock, be sure to turn it offits how theyre able to donate!
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            8. Get medicine
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           You can find coupons for your prescriptions at
           <a href="http://www.goodrx.com/">
            GoodRx
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            9. Get free sheet music
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://imslp.org/">
            IMSLP
           </a>
           has a library of free downloadable sheet music in the public domain.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            10. Manage your finances
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.mint.com">
            Mint
           </a>
           helps you track your spending easily, for free.
           <br/>
          </p>
          <center>
           <div id="adsense">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

          <h3>
           <strong>
            11. Watch a complete documentary
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://documentaryheaven.com/">
            Documentary Heaven
           </a>
           has tons of amazing documentaries available online for free.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            12. Listen to your favourite music
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Listen to your favorite songs, find new artists, and make playlists at
           <a href="https://www.spotify.com">
            Spotify
           </a>
           , or listen to online radio at
           <a href="http://grooveshark.com/">
            Grooveshark
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            13. Talk with a therapist
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           You can talk online privately with a trained active listener or therapist at
           <a href="https://www.7cupsoftea.com/">
            7 Cups of Tea
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            14. Find your laptop
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="https://preyproject.com/">
            Prey Anti-Theft
           </a>
           helps you to protect your devices from theft by tracking their location.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            15. Make friends with an inmate
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.meet-an-inmate.com/">
            Meet-An-Inmate
           </a>
           is an online penpal service for the currently incarcerated.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            16. Relax
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Check out
           <a href="http://www.rainymood.com/">
            Rainy Mood
           </a>
           for a little ambiance.
          </p>
          <p>
           Bonus thing: Smile, thank to the
           <a href="http://inktank.fi/smile-41-of-the-worlds-happiest-facts/">
            worlds happiest facts
           </a>
           .
          </p>
