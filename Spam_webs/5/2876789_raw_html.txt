        <h2 class="date-header">
         May 11, 2011
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-author-crate_paper entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ce4c970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           Crate DIY :: Remake of Home
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a72c2970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="6a00e55210ddf58834014e86c08820970d-800wi" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf588340154323a72c2970c" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a72c2970c-800wi" title="6a00e55210ddf58834014e86c08820970d-800wi"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b0c2b970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop up book 2" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b0c2b970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b0c2b970d-800wi" title="Pop up book 2"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Hello, it's CP Girl
             <a href="http://lariscrap.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
              Larissa
             </a>
             here to show you another 3-D project today and this time I'm here to inspire you to create a book for your children to play with! First, let me introduce you to my creative process:
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             From the moment I saw the
             <em>
              Books
             </em>
             patterned paper I knew I just had to give life to one of those adorable book titles that Lisa brought to us. So, it's been a while that I've been thinking about creating a children's book. I also remembered when Lisa presented to us Crate Paper's new collections and shared the story of entering her son and daughter's bedrooms and from the two rooms, she worked up all the inspiration required to create CP's amazing new collections - Emma's Shoppe and Toy Box! Since having read Lisa's story, I've been unable to stop thinking about them two adorable rooms. So, for CP DIY project this month, I decided to create a 2-floor little house pop-up book that hosts a children's world of textured and layered rooms. Here's another look...
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b210e970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop up book 4" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b210e970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b210e970d-800wi" title="Pop up book 4"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <span style="color: #ff0000;">
              <em>
               Now for the instructions to make the pop-up book...
              </em>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b21f7970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 1" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b21f7970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b21f7970d-800wi" title="Photo 1"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             First, trim four pieces of patterned paper measuring 11.5" x 12".
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a88d1970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 2" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf588340154323a88d1970c" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a88d1970c-800wi" title="Photo 2"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Fold each of the papers at the 11" mark and 5.5" mark {see image above}.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a8a85970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 3" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf588340154323a8a85970c" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a8a85970c-800wi" title="Photo 3"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Adhere the pieces of paper together to create the shown accordion above - the small tab should be adhered to the backside of it's neighboring paper. Remember, that for the accordion, you'll need to alternately fold one section back, then one section forward.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b2a3b970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 4" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b2a3b970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b2a3b970d-800wi" title="Photo 4"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Put the accordion aside to give it time to dry. Next, cut and fold 8 triangles like those shown above. To ensure an accurately sized triangle, pre-measure the area in which they'll be placed within the accordion.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ba74970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 5" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ba74970b" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ba74970b-800wi" title="Photo 5"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             After the triangles are cut and folded, insert them into the accordion.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67bbb1970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 6" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67bbb1970b" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67bbb1970b-800wi" title="Photo 6"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Continue by adding cut-outs, embellishments and attaching tabs.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b2dec970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 7" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b2dec970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b2dec970d-800wi" title="Photo 7"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             For the roof, trim four more triangles and fold them in the same fashion as the previous ones. Insert them into position along the top of the accordion as to create a roof for it.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a9550970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 8" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf588340154323a9550970c" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a9550970c-800wi" title="Photo 8"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             For the covers, trim two pieces of chipboard measuring at 5.75" x 12".
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3041970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 9" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3041970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3041970d-800wi" title="Photo 9"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Cover the chipboard pieces with paper...
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a967b970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 10" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf588340154323a967b970c" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a967b970c-800wi" title="Photo 10"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Before you adhere the accordion to the inside of the two covers, you'll need to add ribbon. Measure and place a mark at the middle point of each chipboard piece. Position the ribbon using double-stick tape on center both above and below the mark.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b37ca970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Phoro 11" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b37ca970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b37ca970d-800wi" title="Phoro 11"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Now it's time to position the accordion inside the book!
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a9a4d970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Photo 12" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf588340154323a9a4d970c" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf588340154323a9a4d970c-800wi" title="Photo 12"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Cover the chipboard covers with patterned paper and an individual book spine trimmed from the aforementioned
             <em>
              "books"
             </em>
             patterned paper. Continue to further decorate the book covers!
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67c8b5970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop Up book" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67c8b5970b image-full" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67c8b5970b-800wi" title="Pop Up book"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <span style="color: #ff0000;">
              <em>
               And your book is ready!
              </em>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67c966970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop up book 1" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67c966970b" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67c966970b-800wi" title="Pop up book 1"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3b67970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop up book 2" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3b67970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3b67970d-800wi" title="Pop up book 2"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3bc1970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop up book 3" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3bc1970d" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf58834014e885b3bc1970d-800wi" title="Pop up book 3"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ca36970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Pop up book 4" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ca36970b" src="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67ca36970b-800wi" title="Pop up book 4"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             You can now encourage your children to play with the book! Than's to Lisa for the inspiration - hope you like my version of your "home". I'm moving on! See you soon - Larissa Albernaz.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://crate.typepad.com/.a/6a00e55210ddf5883401538e67cb40970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
