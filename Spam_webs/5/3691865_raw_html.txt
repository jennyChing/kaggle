              Serrapeptase has a remarkable history. This enzyme comes from the silk worm. The Japanese and Europeans have used this enzyme for years for the reduction of inflammations as well as the additional benefit of pain relief. Only in recent years has Serrapeptase been introduced in the United States. People familiar with Serrapeptase are pleased with its benefits of natural healing in lieu of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).

              In the US, Serrapeptase is classified as a dietary supplement. No doubt Serrapeptase is a natural miracle enzyme for many people suffering from a number of common ailments.
