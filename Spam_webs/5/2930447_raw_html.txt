       <h1>
        How to Make a Loom Band Minion Bracelet  Video Tutorial
       </h1>
       <span class="meta">
        05 November 2014
       </span>
       <p>
        <img alt="How to make a loom band minion bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro-8.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Loom band minion bracelet Tutorial"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        If your kids are anything like our mini VoucherCodesPro team members, theyre probably obsessed with loom bands and making everything and anything loomy.
       </p>
       <p>
        Another thing theyre loving (still) is Despicable Me, more specifically, the Despicable Me minions. To be fair, those minions are marvellous so we cant blame the kids for loving all things minion. If your mini-mes are heavily into loom bands and minions, the obvious next step is loom band minions.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here we have a very helpful video tutorial on how to make a loom band minion bracelet. If were being honest, we have no clue how to put one of these together but luckily Caitlin knows exactly what shes talking about and guides you through the process clearly with easy to follow instructions.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe frameborder="0" height="350" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/bHacpltOdO4" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="425">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         To make a loom band minion bracelet you will need:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Blue loom bands
        </li>
        <li>
         Yellow loom bands
        </li>
        <li>
         Black loom bands
        </li>
        <li>
         Rainbow loom board
        </li>
        <li>
         Hook
        </li>
        <li>
         Googly eye button
        </li>
        <li>
         2 x S clips
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <img alt="Materials needed for Minion loom band bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro.JPG" title="Items required for Minion loom band bracelet"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Starting to make minion loom band bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro-3.JPG" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Caitlyn making the Minion loom band bracelet "/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Different stages of creating the Minion loom band bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro-4.JPG" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Stages of making the Minion loom band bracelet"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Learning how to loom is a tricky process so this video should really help explain things to your kids without them having to figure it out from written instructions. When we were filming this, we were all captivated by the process and despite never making any loom band bracelets ourselves, felt that Caitlins instructions made it so easy even we could make one.
       </p>
       <p>
        Since the trailer for the Minions movie was released, weve been a little bit obsessed with these loom creations. Caitlin is so quick at looming, weve now each got a minion bracelet to wear and theyre so cute!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Attaching the loom bands to make the Minion bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro-5.JPG" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Creating the Minion loom band bracelet"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="The finished loom band Minion bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro-6.JPG" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Minion loom band bracelets"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Caitlyn wearing the finished Minion loom band bracelet" src="https://www.vouchercodespro.co.uk/images/how-to-tutorial-minion-loom-band-despicable-me-craft-bracelet--youtube-video-easy-quick-vouchercodespro-voucher-codes-pro-2.JPG" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Finished Minion loom band bracelet"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you know someone else who would love to know how to make a loom band minion bracelet, share it on Facebook/Twitter and spread the minion love! Send us a picture of the finished creation and why not experiment with purple bands to make the naughty minion from Despicable Me 2?
       </p>
