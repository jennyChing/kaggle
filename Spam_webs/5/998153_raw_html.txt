       <div class="headline_area">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Oh So Easy Quiche!
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard fn">
          zesty
         </span>
         on
         <abbr class="published" title="2008-10-21">
          October 21, 2008
         </abbr>
         
         <span>
          <a href="http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/#comments" rel="nofollow">
           42 comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <div class="pf-content">
         <p>
          Hey Everyone Happy Tuesday! I had a huge
          <strong>
           family gathering
          </strong>
          on the weekend and with that came a lot of food. One of the
          <strong>
           feature brunch
          </strong>
          dishes that I chose was a wonderful, yet simple, kicked up quiche. Talk about an easy recipe that packed a punch. Over 20 people tried the
          <strong>
           quiche
          </strong>
          and everyone of them were raving about both the homemade pastry and filling.
         </p>
         <p>
          Follow along below and try out this quiche. I made a couple of different types. Our feature quiche was ham and spinach with
          <strong>
           monterey jack
          </strong>
          cheese. I also made a traditional bacon, mushroom quiche with
          <strong>
           cheddar
          </strong>
          cheese.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/spinach_ham_plate.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-891" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/spinach_ham_plate.jpg" title="spinach_ham_plate" width="500"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/spinach_ham_plate.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Ingredients
         </h3>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1/2 cup butter
          </li>
          <li>
           1 (5 ounce) container process sharp cheese spread
          </li>
          <li>
           1 cup all-purpose flour
          </li>
          <li>
           2 tablespoons water
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span id="more-882">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           FILLING
          </strong>
          :
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1 egg
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 cup milk
          </li>
          <li>
           1/4 teaspoon salt
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 cup finely chopped ham
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 cup Spinach chopped
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <strong>
           OR
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1/2 cup finely chopped bacon
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 cup finely chopped mushrooms
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h3>
          Method
         </h3>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             In a small bowl, cut butter and cheese spread into flour until well blended.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/pastry_ingredients.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-883 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/pastry_ingredients.jpg" title="pastry_ingredients" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/pastry_ingredients.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             Add water and toss with a fork until a ball forms.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/add_water.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-884 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/add_water.jpg" title="add_water" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/add_water.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             Refrigerate for 1 hour.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/dough_ball.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-885 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/dough_ball.jpg" title="dough_ball" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/dough_ball.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             Press tablespoonfuls onto the bottom and up the sides of greased miniature muffin cups.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/pastry_pan.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-886 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/pastry_pan.jpg" title="pastry_pan" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/pastry_pan.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             In a bowl, beat the egg, milk and salt. Stir in ham and cheese.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/ham.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-892 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/ham.jpg" title="ham" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/ham.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/spinach_cheese.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-887 aligncenter" height="405" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/spinach_cheese.jpg" title="spinach_cheese" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/spinach_cheese.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/mixed_filling.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-888 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/mixed_filling.jpg" title="mixed_filling" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/mixed_filling.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             Spoon a rounded teaspoonful into each shell.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/ready_bake.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-889 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/ready_bake.jpg" title="ready_bake" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/ready_bake.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             Bake at 350F for 30 minutes or until golden brown. (This is actually a shot of the bacon and mushroom quiche in case you thought I was losing my mind)
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/bacon_mush_complete.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-890 aligncenter" height="375" src="http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/bacon_mush_complete.jpg" title="bacon_mush_complete" width="500"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://zestycook.com/oh-so-easy-quiche/&amp;media=http://zestycook.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/bacon_mush_complete.jpg&amp;description=Oh So Easy Quiche!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span>
            <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
             ENJOY!
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          <span>
           <span style="font-family: Verdana; color: #333333;">
            So as always, do tell Zesty
            <strong>
             whats your favorite quiche filling
            </strong>
            . I cant wait to hear everyones responses. Hope everybody has a great Tuesday!
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
