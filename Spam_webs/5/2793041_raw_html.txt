          <h1 class="entry-title">
           The Best 12 Versions of Pharrells Happy
          </h1>
          <div class="post-meta">
           <ul>
            <li>
             <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2014-06-11T21:44:17+00:00">
              11. Jun
             </time>
            </li>
            <li class="divider">
             /
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://funmozar.com/category/story/" title="View all posts in Story">
              Story
             </a>
            </li>
            <!--&lt;li class="divider"&gt;/&lt;/li&gt;-->
            <li>
            </li>
            <!--&lt;li class="divider"&gt;/&lt;/li&gt;
	&lt;li&gt;&lt;a href="http://funmozar.com/the-best-12-versions-of-pharrells-happy/#comments"&gt;No Comments&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;-->
           </ul>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          Pharrell Williams really made an impact with Happy, not only because it is a good song, but especially because he allowed anybody to make different versions, with no financial claims. In other words, you can film a Happy version with your friends whenever you like, you can post it on YouTube, and you can enjoy all the benefits of it. Today, we have 1000 versions of the song in 100 countries, each one of them bringing something new on the table. It is impossible not to dance when you hear this song. It is the official song of the International Day of Happiness, and the best method to begin a new day. Here is our selection of the best Happy versions. We tried to choose them based on their popularity, uniqueness, but also considering that special thing that makes them special.
         </p>
         <p>
          12. Happy Piano Version Melbourne (Reworked)
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/S4vKtJeffbQ" width="560">
          </iframe>
          <br/>
          <span id="more-526">
          </span>
          <br/>
          One woman and a piano in Melbourne, and some great voices can make a beautiful song
         </p>
         <p>
          11. Happy in Madagascar
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/48l07lXs300" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Because the decors in an exotic country make a difference. Happy Madagascar takes us to another world, unknown for the majority of us. This song is a better promotion method for tourism in Madagascar than any other touristic presentation is.
         </p>
         <p>
          10. Happy in Berlin (Remix)
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/FD62ZFNoJSc" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Because we like originals, and because Germans are not as boring as we like to think sometimes. It is a 13 minutes version which already gathered one million views, and we are sure that you will enjoy it also!
         </p>
         <p>
          9. Happy in Angers
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/guMfsv_0meA" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          People from the small city of Angers in France thought about promoting their city with a Happy version, and they did a great job! It is incredible how big a small city could be when it is presented in a happy way. The video has 1 million views in six months, which is incredible for a city of 200 000 people.
         </p>
         <p>
          8. Happy Kids (Reworked)
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/8bhfu1KnKjM" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          The children in this video are at least thrilling! Watch this version of Happy with children dancing, singing, and showing adults what a great time really means.
         </p>
         <p>
          7. Happy in Russia
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/AQhDUI0cK2U" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Songs like this show us that Russia is not only about Putin, invading armies, and freezing temperatures. The young Russians made an effort in showing us that, and the result is pretty good.
         </p>
         <p>
          6. Happy in Senegal (Remix)
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/r1IEseJJca0" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          How about a Happy version reworked with some African sounds? Zuman Begge made this in Senegal, and we never believed how great Happy sounds when it is reworked with some African accords!
         </p>
         <p>
          5. Happy with puppies (Reworked)
         </p>
         <p>
          Maybe Pharrell anticipated that his song will be reworked in many versions, but we bet that he didnt expect for the Puppy version of Happy to be so funny!
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/Dpahtm6oJ28" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Happy in Dubai
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/t9Q-VC9GaHE" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          It is impossible not to like this version of Happy, especially because of the incredible dcor. You can watch this version without sound, and you will still be amazed!
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Happy in Iran
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/r4ouQRuo-20" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Islamist rulers of this country considered this video as an offence brought to the Iranian traditions, therefore some of the people that made this video are now in jail. However, their message remains on the internet as a part of the fight for emancipation of the young generation in this country.
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Happiness Mowe Version (Remix)
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/MDNhveE0wzA" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Austrian duo Mel&amp;Clem are making a great impact in Central and Eastern Europe lately, and this version of Happy comes as a promise on behalf of the new group. It is incredible what they can do by putting some extra sounds on an already famous song! This version sounds like the original, but it is so original! If you have the chance to see Mowe in your city, dont miss it!
         </p>
         <p>
          1. The Original Pharrell Williams
          <br/>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/y6Sxv-sUYtM" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Of course, the Pharrells version must be on the first place of our top, simply because it is the first one! It is not the first time when Pharrell Williams comes with a unique concept, but this is the one that made the biggest impact, bringing happiness in all the corners of the world!
         </p>
