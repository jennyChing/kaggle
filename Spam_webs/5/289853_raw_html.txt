        <p>
         Disney movies are full of crazy conspiracy theories.

          <em>
           101 Dalmatians
          </em>
         </a>

           Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs
          </em>
         </a>

          <em>
           Alice in Wonderland
          </em>
         </a>
         are both about drugs Yeah, that sort of crazy. So you shouldnt be all that surprised that
         <strong>
          <em>
           Tarzan
          </em>
         </strong>
         is caked with its own gems. Here are some of the most confusing:
        </p>
        <p>
         1.
         <strong>
          Elsa and Anna are Tarzans sisters!
         </strong>
         What is going on with all these
         <a href="/2014/10/03/movies/frozen-theories-hidden-messages/">
          theories about
          <em>
           Frozen
          </em>
         </a>
         ?? According to some of the latest theorists, the King &amp; Queen of Arendelle, where Elsa and Anna live, didnt die at sea, but actually got ship-wrecked on a jungle-filled island, only to be killed by a leopard. Before they died, though, they had a son, who survived the chaos and moved on to be raised by gorillas; that boy turned out to be Tarzan.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1591787" style="width: 510px">
         <a href="http://www.teen.com/2015/03/12/movies/tarzan-theories-hidden-messages/attachment/tarzan-growing-up/" rel="attachment wp-att-1591787">
          <img alt="Blogspot (eluniversodisney)" class="size-full wp-image-1591787" height="290" src="http://cdn3.teen.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/tarzan-growing-up.gif" title="Tarzan Growing Up GIF" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Blogspot (
          <a href="http://eluniversodisney.blogspot.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://eluniversodisney.blogspot.com/', 'eluniversodisney']);" target="_blank">
           eluniversodisney
          </a>
          )
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         2.
         <strong>
          Meanwhile, Jane is connected to Belle.
         </strong>
         If you recall, among the list of
         <a href="/2015/02/13/movies/beauty-and-the-beast-theories-hidden-messages/">
          <em>
           Beauty and the Beast
          </em>
          theories
         </a>
         , one insinuated that Jane is the granddaughter of Belle and Beast! Let us reiterate: For one, Tarzan was kind of like a beast, yet Jane could understand him. Then theres the whole fact that a Mrs. Potts-looking teapot and Chip-looking cups made their way into the 1999 film.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1581561" style="width: 510px">
