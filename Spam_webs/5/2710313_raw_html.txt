         </h1>

         <span class="small">
          By
         </span>

         <div class="fb-social-plugin fb-like" data-font="lucida grande" data-href="http://www.feelnumb.com/2010/03/10/tom-morello-reveals-he-was-in-the-movie-iron-man/" data-ref="above-post" data-send="true" data-width="450">

         <p>
          After
          <strong>
          </strong>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Tom Morello
           </strong>
          </em>
          suggested that he was to work on the score for 
          <em>
           Iron Man 2
          </em>
          soundtrack, he also confirmed a rock star easter egg
          <em>
          </em>
          via his Twitter page.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="https://twitter.com/tmorello" target="_blank">
           twitter.com/tmorello
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a class="width:90%;max-width:900px" href="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/screen-shot-2010-03-10-at-40534-pm.png">
           <img alt="tom_morello_iron_man_rage_twitter" class="width:90%;max-width:900px aligncenter" height="187" src="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/screen-shot-2010-03-10-at-40534-pm.png" title="tom_morello_iron_man_rage_twitter" width="615"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="width:90%;max-width:900px" href="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/screen-shot-2010-03-10-at-40357-pm.png">
           <img alt="tom_morello_iron_man_twitter_post" class="width:90%;max-width:900px aligncenter" height="88" src="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/screen-shot-2010-03-10-at-40357-pm.png" title="tom_morello_iron_man_twitter_post" width="580"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Yes that was
          <em>
           <strong>
            Morello
           </strong>
          </em>
          in
          <em>
           Iron Man
          </em>
           where he appeared as a terrorist character. Heres a still of
          <strong>
           <em>
            Morello
           </em>
          </strong>
          from the movie
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="width:90%;max-width:900px" href="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/vlcsnap307733.png">
           <img alt="tom_morello_iron_man_rage_audioslave" class="width:90%;max-width:900px aligncenter" height="400" src="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/vlcsnap307733.png" title="tom_morello_iron_man_rage_audioslave" width="720"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Here he is with
          <em>
           Iron Man
          </em>
          director
          <em>
           <strong>
            Jon Favreau
           </strong>
          </em>
          on the set.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="width:90%;max-width:900px" href="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/normal_jon-tom-sean.jpg">
           <img alt="jon_favreau_tom_morello_iron_man_terrorist_rage" class="width:90%;max-width:900px aligncenter" height="266" src="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/normal_jon-tom-sean.jpg" title="jon_favreau_tom_morello_iron_man_terrorist_rage" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Tom
           </strong>
          </em>
          must be good friends or have a deal with
          <em>
           <strong>
            Favreau
           </strong>
          </em>
          because he was also the best man in another
          <em>
           <strong>
            Favreau
           </strong>
          </em>
          movie 
          <em>
           Made
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="width:90%;max-width:900px" href="http://www.feelnumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/tom-morello-made.png">
