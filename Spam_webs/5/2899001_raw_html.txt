         <p>
          <a href="http://www.theemike.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/FREE-4-Football-Comics.pdf" target="_blank">
           <img alt="MRO 47 Young Team" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2403" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.theemike.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/MRO-47-Young-Team.jpg?resize=600%2C600"/>

          <a href="http://www.theemike.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/FREE-4-Football-Comics.pdf" target="_blank">
           FREE football comic book
          </a>
          
         </p>
         <p>
          The really young players on this team are apparently unfazed by the more mature football team looming on the horizon.
         </p>
         <p>
          Football fans would never witness a scene of diaper clad toddlers wearing football helmets on an actual
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296', 'NFL']);" target="_blank">

          or
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296', 'NCAA']);" target="_blank">
           NCAA
          </a>
          football field.
         </p>
         <p>
          Yet, the
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296', 'really young team']);" target="_blank">
           really young team
          </a>
          in this comic figuratively depicts confident players on
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296', 'NFL']);" target="_blank">
           NFL
          </a>
          teams comprised of several rookies or
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296', 'NCAA']);" target="_blank">
           NCAA
          </a>
          teams made up of lots of underclassmen.
         </p>
         <p>
          Regardless of their size and age,
          <a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-6258952-10702296', 'really young teams']);" target="_blank">
           really young teams
          </a>
          armed with skill and confidence never become intimidated by bigger, stronger and more experienced opponents.
         </p>
