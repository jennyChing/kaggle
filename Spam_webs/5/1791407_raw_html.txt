       <p class="on">
        <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/category/cookingwithbeer/">
         Cooking with Beer
        </a>
        -
        <span itemprop="datePublished">
         December 12, 2014
        </span>
       </p>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14891" height="394" src="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/mashfiles/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014_TopCookingBeer-Gridsmall.jpg" title="2014bestof-cookingwbeer1" width="590"/>
        </p>
        <p class="wp-blank">
         We don't just like making and drinking beer - we love
         <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/category/cookingwithbeer/">
          cooking
         </a>
         with it too! When added to your favorite appetizers, entrees, and even desserts, beer adds a whole new level of flavor and complexity to standby recipes. It can also inspire you to try something new - like swapping wine for beer in
         <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/recipe-curry-beer-mussels/">
          mussels
         </a>
         , taking a
         <a href='http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/beer-cocktail-recipe-the-hop-collins/"'>
          hoppy approach
         </a>
         to cocktails or adding some stout our porter to
         <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/recipe-beer-pretzel-ice-cream-sundae/">
          homemade ice cream
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         We have way more fun cooking with beer when we also have a beer in hand, so pick up a few bottles and try your hand at our five favorite
         <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/category/cookingwithbeer/">
          beer-infused recipes
         </a>
         from the past year:
        </p>
        <p class="wp-blank">
         <!--more-->
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <div class="tasting-beers">
           <h3>
            <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/recipe-beer-soaked-grilled-cheese/">
             Beer Soaked Grilled Cheese
            </a>
           </h3>
           <p class="beer-notes">
            Is there any other sandwich that says comfort like a grilled cheese? This beer soaked version marries brown ale (like our
            <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/beer-making-mixes/chestnut-brown-ale-mix">
             Chestnut Brown Ale
            </a>
            ) and cheddar to thick slices of sourdough for something deliciously sweet and sharp.
           </p>
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div class="tasting-beers">
           <h3>
            <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/recipe-coconut-porter-brussel-sprouts/">
             Coconut Porter Brussels Sprouts
            </a>
           </h3>
           <p class="beer-notes">
            Even if you need a little convincing on this tiny cabbage, we trust you'll head straight to the kitchen once you find out that our succulent version is made with
            <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/beer-making-kits/chocolate-maple-porter-1-gallon-kit-with-mix">
             Chocolate Maple Porter
            </a>
            , coconut and shallots. It's a revelation, and you'll never see sprouts the same way again.
           </p>
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div class="tasting-beers">
           <h3>
            <a href="http://brooklynbrewshop.com/themash/recipe-beer-queso/">
             Beer Queso
            </a>
