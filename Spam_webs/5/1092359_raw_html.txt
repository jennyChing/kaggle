        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Yogurt Streusel Coffee Cake {Plus Keurig 2.0}
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-20T01:38:41+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 20, 2014
         </time>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/2014/12/20/yogurt-streusel-coffee-cake-plus-keurig-2-0/#comments">
           15 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <i>
          This shop has been compensated by Collective Bias, Inc. and its advertiser. All opinions are mine alone. #Keurig400 #CollectiveBias
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6132" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not going to lie. Im slightly addicted to coffee. Coffee in the morning is a must. Im notorious for filling up on coffee and forgetting to eat breakfast. Coffee in the afternoon? Perfect. Especially on these cold days. And then theres not much more comforting than a cup of coffee after dinner. I mostly drink decaf and that is why I tell myself that this coffee habit is totally acceptable.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6127" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-4.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I was so excited to get to try out the new
         <a href="http://www.target.com/p/keurig-2-0-k400-coffee-maker-brewing-system-with-carafe/-/A-15351560#prodSlot=medium_1_4&amp;term=keurig+2.0" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Keurig
          <sup>
           
          </sup>
          2.0
         </a>
         that can brew not only a single cup, but also a carafe of coffee. Throughout the year, but even more so during the Christmas season, we have groups of people over to our house to visit and hang out. Some of our friends are coffee drinkers, others drink tea or hot cocoa. This new Keurig is perfect for entertaining because it makes both hot and cold drinks. Keurig offers over 400 varieties of coffees, teas, cocoa and other beverages from over 60 brands.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_5328.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6138" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_5328.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll be able to serve your guests their favorite drink, made their favorite way. The new Keurig 2.0 even features a brewing technology that reads each lid to give you, your friends and family a perfectly brewed beverage, each and every time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Not only did we get to have a Keurig ourselves, we also got to give a Keurig away to our good friends. They love Keurigs and were so surprised to get this special gift! It came just at the perfect time for them. I love giving gifts and making people happy, dont you? These little machines make great gifts.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6128" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-5.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-6.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6129" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-6.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Coffee with dessert is a must when we are entertaining. Today Im sharing with you one of our favorite coffee cakes that pairs perfectly with a freshly brewed cup of coffee. Yogurt Streusel Coffee Cake.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-7.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6130" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-7.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Why yogurt? My moms original recipe called for sour cream. Weve enjoyed this recipe made with sour cream for years. I switched it up for you and substituted plain Greek yogurt for the sour cream. I like to think that substitution makes the recipe just a little more healthy. After all, Greek yogurt is full of protein and lower in fat than sour cream. And youll never miss the sour cream!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-8.jpg">
          <img alt="Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake #keurig400 #collectivebias #ad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6131" height="800" src="http://www.tastesoflizzyt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Yogurt-Streusel-Coffee-Cake-8.jpg" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This classic cinnamon coffee cake is thick, but so moist. I love that it is best served the day after you make it. Recipes that get better when they sit are so helpful during busy times and hosting guests. No need to make this coffee cake seem like it is fresh from the oven since it is better eaten the next day!
        </p>
        <p>
         Head to
         <a href="http://link.brightcove.com/services/player/bcpid2764084546001?bckey=AQ~~,AAACW2gIl2k~,nDiY24zt2mATEj2GDSIRR1yuUXTrGPcg&amp;bctid=3944971783001" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Target
         </a>
         , pick up a
         <a href="http://www.target.com/p/keurig-2-0-k400-coffee-maker-brewing-system-with-carafe/-/A-15351560#prodSlot=medium_1_4&amp;term=keurig+2.0" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Keurig
          <sup>
           
          </sup>
          2.0
         </a>
         so you are prepared for those guests. Or even bettergive one for a gift! What would you brew first with aKeurig
         <sup>
          
         </sup>
         2.0? Are you a coffee, tea or cocoa lover?
         <br/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup butter, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups white sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup plain Greek yogurt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            <strong>
             Streusel:
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup chopped walnuts
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons brown sugar
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a tube/bundt pan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar and eggs. Fold in the yogurt and vanilla carefully. Add the dry ingredients and mix well.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spoon half of the batter into the prepared pan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small bowl, combine the streusel ingredients. Sprinkle the streusel over the batter.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spoon the remaining batter over the streusel.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake at 350 degrees for 50-55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the cake comes out clean.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Allow the cake to cool for at least 35 minutes. Using a knife, loosen the edges of the cake from the pan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Turn the cake out onto a plate. Cover until serving.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            This cake is best when made the day before serving.
           </li>
          </ol>

              Thanks, Jen! Yes, that is a cream cheese powdered sugar glaze. Heres my recipe:
              <br/>
              6 oz cream cheese, softened
              <br/>
              5 tablespoons butter, softened
              <br/>
              2 cups powdered sugar
              <br/>
              Mix it all together. You can warm this drizzle in the microwave for 20 seconds or so it is drizzling consistency, or you can add a bit of milk to make it thinner. And you can half the recipe if you dont think youll need that much drizzle.
              <br/>
              Enjoy!
