	Whether youre training to be the next UFC Heavyweight Champion or you just like to spar a few days per week to stay in shape, you can definitely appreciate these newest MMA training aids and accessories.  Here are five of our favorite to keep an eye on in 2015--
Motion Master Grappling Dummy by Erik Paulson --

	
	Sure hitting the heavy bag or perfecting your footwork are key to a great MMA workout, but in order to truly grasp the technique necessary to train hard and win, you must try and structure your workouts to mimic real fights as much as possible.  With the Motion Master Grappling Dummy, you can do just that.  This 55lbs bag is vaguely shaped like a persons body, allowing you to practice your striking and your transitions in a way that more closely resembles a persons body.  Not only will it help your transitions and ground game, but its great for conditioning as well.
	&amp;nbsp;
Vapor Fresh Sports Cleaning Spray --

	
	If you train hard, you know how sweaty and smell all of your gear can get.  From your gloves and other training aids, to your gym bags and gym equipment, everything smells.  Typically people resort to using cleaning sprays that are not intended for sports equipment, and in turn, not designed to ever come in direct contact with skin.  These sorts of sprays cause skin allergies and irritation that you definitely want to avoid.  Even some sports cleaning sprays use harsh chemicals like Chlorine, Ammonia and artificial fragrances that can cause skin, eye and lung irritation.  Vapor Fresh Sports Cleaning Spray allows you to train hard and finish fresh, as its formulated with 100% botanical active ingredients and is non-irritating and non-toxic.
	&amp;nbsp;
Phoenix Sparring Mitts --

	
	Going along the same idea as the grappling dummy, the Phoenix Sparring Mitts allow you to train at a higher level and a higher intensity by helping design your training sessions to more closely mimic a real fight.  This mitt is designed to allow the wearer to both catch strikes with its forearm pad as well as deliver counter-punches with its built-in glove.  Sold in a pair and designed with very comfortable and durable padded straps, these mitts are essential for all boxing, kickboxing and MMA training sessions.
	&amp;nbsp;
Thai Destroyer Shin Guards --

	
	If you incorporate kicks into your training style, you need these shin guards-- designed with high-performance padding, anti-shock technology, cushioned straps and a pre-curved design that fits perfect right out of the box, these shin guards are the cream of the crop.  The padding continues down to the top of the foot to help prevent metatarsal bruising and injuries.
	&amp;nbsp;
Youth Deluxe MMA Gloves &amp; Shin Guards --

	
	
	Its never too early to teach children how to stay in shape as well as how to protect themselves, so providing them with the proper equipment to learn MMA in a fun and safe way is a great way to introduce them to this healthy extracurricular activity. Both designed to fit children, they are built well to withstand heavy usage as well as priced affordably to equip a beginner.
	&amp;nbsp;
	So those are some of our favorite new MMA training aids and accessories for 2015.

       <h1 class="PostTitle" itemprop="name">
        5 New MMA Training Aids and Accessories You Need In 2015
       </h1>
       <div class="BlockContent">
        <!-- &lt;p class="NewsDate" style="margin: 0; color:#6e6e6e;"&gt;Posted by 
            &lt;span itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"&gt;
                &lt;span itemprop="name"&gt;Stephen Steinberg&lt;/span&gt;
            &lt;/span&gt;
            &lt;span itemprop="datePublished" content=""&gt;
                on February 03, 2015
            &lt;/span&gt; 
        &lt;/p&gt; -->
        <div class="BlockContent BlogShare">
        </div>
        <div class="PostBody">
         <p itemprop="articleBody">
         </p>
         <p>
          Whether youre training to be the next UFC Heavyweight Champion or you just like to spar a few days per week to stay in shape, you can definitely appreciate these newest MMA training aids and accessories.  Here are five of our favorite to keep an eye on in 2015--
         </p>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.revgear.com/product/motion-master-grappling-dummy/grappling-grappling-dummies" target="_blank">
           Motion Master Grappling Dummy by Erik Paulson
          </a>
          --
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="motion-master-grappling-dummy-by-erik-paulson" src="https://cdn2.bigcommerce.com/server5100/rug8ggw/product_images/uploaded_images/motion-master-grappling-dummy-by-erik-paulson-ra.jpg?t=1422926267" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px;"/>
          Sure hitting the heavy bag or perfecting your footwork are key to a great MMA workout, but in order to truly grasp the technique necessary to train hard and win, you must try and structure your workouts to mimic real fights as much as possible.  With the Motion Master Grappling Dummy, you can do just that.  This 55lbs bag is vaguely shaped like a persons body, allowing you to practice your striking and your transitions in a way that more closely resembles a persons body.  Not only will it help your transitions and ground game, but its great for conditioning as well.
         </p>
         <p style="clear: both;">
         </p>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.revgear.com/product/vapor-fresh-sports-spray/cleaners-body-care" target="_blank">
           Vapor Fresh Sports Cleaning Spray
          </a>
          --
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="vapor-fresh-spray" src="https://cdn2.bigcommerce.com/server5100/rug8ggw/product_images/uploaded_images/vapor-fresh-spray-ra.jpg?t=1422926271" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px;"/>
          If you train hard, you know how sweaty and smell all of your gear can get.  From your gloves and other training aids, to your gym bags and gym equipment, everything smells.  Typically people resort to using cleaning sprays that are not intended for sports equipment, and in turn, not designed to ever come in direct contact with skin.  These sorts of sprays cause skin allergies and irritation that you definitely want to avoid.  Even some sports cleaning sprays use harsh chemicals like Chlorine, Ammonia and artificial fragrances that can cause skin, eye and lung irritation.  Vapor Fresh Sports Cleaning Spray allows you to train hard and finish fresh, as its formulated with 100% botanical active ingredients and is non-irritating and non-toxic.
         </p>
         <p style="clear: both;">
         </p>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.revgear.com/product/phoenix-sparring-mitts/brands-revgear-mitts-thai-pads" target="_blank">
           Phoenix Sparring Mitts
          </a>
          --
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="phoenix-sparring-mitts" src="https://cdn2.bigcommerce.com/server5100/rug8ggw/product_images/uploaded_images/phoenix-sparring-mitts-ra.jpg?t=1422926270" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px;"/>
          Going along the same idea as the grappling dummy, the Phoenix Sparring Mitts allow you to train at a higher level and a higher intensity by helping design your training sessions to more closely mimic a real fight.  This mitt is designed to allow the wearer to both catch strikes with its forearm pad as well as deliver counter-punches with its built-in glove.  Sold in a pair and designed with very comfortable and durable padded straps, these mitts are essential for all boxing, kickboxing and MMA training sessions.
         </p>
         <p style="clear: both;">
         </p>
         <h2>
          <a href="http://www.revgear.com/product/thai-destroyer-shin-guards/" target="_blank">
           Thai Destroyer Shin Guards
          </a>
          --
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="thai-destroyer-shin-guards" src="https://cdn2.bigcommerce.com/server5100/rug8ggw/product_images/uploaded_images/thai-destroyer-shin-guards-ra.jpg?t=1422926270" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px;"/>
          If you incorporate kicks into your training style, you need these shin guards-- designed with high-performance padding, anti-shock technology, cushioned straps and a pre-curved design that fits perfect right out of the box, these shin guards are the cream of the crop.  The padding continues down to the top of the foot to help prevent metatarsal bruising and injuries.
         </p>
         <p style="clear: both;">
         </p>
         <h2>
          Youth
          <a href="http://www.revgear.com/product/youth-deluxe-mma-gloves/" target="_blank">
           Deluxe MMA Gloves
          </a>
          &amp;
          <a href="http://www.revgear.com/product/youth-shin-guard/" target="_blank">
           Shin Guards
          </a>
          --
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="Youth MMA gloves" src="https://cdn2.bigcommerce.com/server5100/rug8ggw/product_images/uploaded_images/youth-mma-gloves-ra.jpg?t=1422926272" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px;"/>
          <img alt="Youth MMA shin guards" src="https://cdn2.bigcommerce.com/server5100/rug8ggw/product_images/uploaded_images/youth-mma-shin-guards-ra.jpg?t=1422926272" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px;"/>
          Its never too early to teach children how to stay in shape as well as how to protect themselves, so providing them with the proper equipment to learn MMA in a fun and safe way is a great way to introduce them to this healthy extracurricular activity. Both designed to fit children, they are built well to withstand heavy usage as well as priced affordably to equip a beginner.
         </p>
         <p style="clear: both;">
         </p>
         <p>
          So those are some of our favorite new MMA training aids and accessories for 2015.
         </p>
         <p>
          Train Hard. Finish Fresh.
         </p>
