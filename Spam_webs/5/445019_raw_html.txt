              </h1>

             <div class="entry-content clearfix">
              <h3>
               NCLEX-RN Review Course
              </h3>
              <p>
               <strong>
                Our next class will be held on Tuesday February 17th to February 19th starting at 8am.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               3 Day Course  $300 with book
              </h3>
              <p>
               This 3 day course is designed to prepare you for the very difficult NCLEX for RN test. The NCLEX Review course includes
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Student handbook
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 Pass or no fee for retaking the course
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 3 days of live lectures
                </strong>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               The topics covered include but are not limited to
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 MEDICAL SURGICAL NURSING
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 WOMENS HEALTH &amp; MATERNAL / NEWBORN NURSING
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 PEDIATRIC NURSING
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 PSYCHIATRIC NURSING
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 The NCLEX-RN Practice Exam
                </strong>
               </li>
               <li>
                <strong>
                 PHARMACOLOGY
                </strong>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               If you are interested in this course or would like more information please give us a call at 1-800-477-6193 or schedule a class online in our Rancho Cucamonga office. Course is held 1 time per month.
              </p>
             </div>
