        We have the habit of not being at the right place at the right time. But, 60 other people in Mansfield Woodhouse, Notts were certainly the lucky ones with impeccable timing to be at the right place as they had queued up at the ATM which was dispensing free cash. It was due to some fault in the ATM machine of The Lloyds Bank. As soon as the bank realized the fault in the machine, they put it out of order.
