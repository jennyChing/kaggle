        <script>
         &lt;!--

        <p>
         Its no secret that smartphone battery technology has lacked anymajor advancements, other than being able to charge them super-fast. Sure, you could always buy one of those battery-backups, but if youre like me, you forget to charge them ahead of time. They also take up some prime pocket real estate, too.
        </p>
        <p>
         Your other option is carrying your charger around with you all the time, but then youre just one of those people always on the prowlfor an outlet. No thank you.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre looking for an easier fix,
         <a href="http://www.flintu.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.flintu.com/', 'Flintu']);">
          Flintu
         </a>
         has a slightly easier solution. Flintu is a keyring-sized device that charges your phone with a 9-volt battery. Theyre coining the product as Plan V.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://knowtechie.com/is-this-9-volt-keychain-smartphone-charger-really-worth-it-123/screen-shot-2014-12-03-at-2-11-26-pm/" rel="attachment wp-att-5957">
          <img alt="Plan-z" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5957" height="254" src="http://kt-media.knowtechie.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Screen-Shot-2014-12-03-at-2.11.26-PM.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          This thing is really tiny, too. You could fit it right on your key chain.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://knowtechie.com/is-this-9-volt-keychain-smartphone-charger-really-worth-it-123/o-flintu-keyring-key-570/" rel="attachment wp-att-5958">
          <img alt="o-FLINTU-KEYRING-KEY-570" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5958" height="230" src="http://kt-media.knowtechie.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/o-FLINTU-KEYRING-KEY-570.jpg" width="570"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          See it in action here
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/112464732" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         OK, so this eliminates the need for carrying a phone charger or an extra battery pack, but now youll just have to carry around a 9-volt battery, not to mention buying one. I mean seriously, whens the last time you bought a 9-volt battery?
        </p>
        <p>
         On the other hand, I guess its another solution to our smartphone battery blues.
        </p>
