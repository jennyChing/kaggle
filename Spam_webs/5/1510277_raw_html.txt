      How Long Does Fruit Last?
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-09-22T12:02:50+00:00">
       September 22, 2014
      </time>
      <p class="byline author vcard">

   <div class="wrap">

      <main class="main " role="main">
       <p class="lead">
        Wondering if your apples and apricots are still good? It's not always easy to tell. Depending on the fruit, you may have a month or only a few days to enjoy it, especially if they're left in a bowl in the pantry. Chilling your fruits in the fridge can certainly extend their shelf life, but some last longer than others. Our chart has all the details.
       </p>
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>

        </p>

         <img alt="How Long Does Fruit Last" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2826" height="2254" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/how-long-does-fruit-last.png" width="722"/>
        </p>
        <h4>
         How Long Does a Dipped Berry Last?
        </h4>
        <p>
         Were glad you asked. We make it our business to keep our dipped strawberries fresh and delicious from the farm to their door. How do we do it? We pack each berry by hand in protective packaging, complete with ice packs, to keep the fruit sweet and cool. Then we ship your gift express, so it spends less time on a truck, and more time on their table. Once the berries arrive, they stay fresh for 48 hours in the refrigerator.
        </p>
        <h4>
         More Fruit Tips
        </h4>
        <p>
         Have any savvy tips for making fruit last? Share your fruit wisdom in the blog comments.
