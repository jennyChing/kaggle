But in order to make the most of these technologies, they will require skilled coders who know how to use these systems.

They should hire the best coders for testing new systems and existing systems for ICD-10.

This will give them an edge when finding a high-paying job in a hospital or private practice in the US. For instance, to use computer assisted coding, coders need to focus on testing ICD-9 workflows as well as dual coding and future workflows for
         <a href="http://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/blog/dont-let-icd-10-coding-changes-disrupt-cash-flow-of-your-practice.html">

        </p>

         They will have to spend some time on online education, accessing online courses that will fit their tight schedules. Coders will have to learn and re-learn college level medical terminologies, anatomy and physiology.
