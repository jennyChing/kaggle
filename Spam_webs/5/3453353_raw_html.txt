			&lt;h1 class="post-title"&gt;Comment on Radiation 2010 &#8211; 2014&lt;/h1&gt;
			&lt;p class="single_postmeta"&gt;
				&lt;span&gt;Posted by &lt;a href="http://www.veteranstoday.com/author/bobnichols/"&gt;Bob Nichols&lt;/a&gt; on October 25, 2014&lt;/span&gt;
				&lt;span&gt;
					&lt;a rel="nofollow" href="#" id="increase-font" class="fontsize-button fontSizeMinus" title="Decrease font size"&gt;&lt;i class="icon-minus-sign"&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/a&gt;

					&lt;a class="email-this-page" href="mailto:?subject=Veterans%20Today%20%3A%20Comment%20on%20Radiation%202010%20-%202014&amp;body=I%20recommend%20this%20page%3A%20Comment%20on%20Radiation%202010%20-%202014.%0AYou%20can%20read%20it%20on%3A%20http%3A%2F%2Fwww.veteranstoday.com%2F2014%2F10%2F25%2Fcomment-on-radiation-2010-2014%2F" title="Send by email : Comment on Radiation 2010 - 2014"&gt;&lt;i class="icon-envelope-alt"&gt;&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/a&gt;				&lt;/span&gt;

	&lt;section class="postbody-wrapper postlayout-default"&gt;
&lt;br/&gt;
		
	&lt;div class="post-column pull-left"&gt;
		&lt;article class="entry post-327381 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-of-interest tag-america tag-billings tag-bob-nichols tag-radiation tag-san-diego tag-veteranstoday last_archivepost"&gt;
				&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h2 style="text-align: center"&gt; &#8230; by &lt;a href="http://www.veteranstoday.com/staff-writers"&gt;Bob Nichols&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_327384" style="width: 330px" class="wp-caption alignleft"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.veteranstoday.com/2014/10/25/comment-on-radiation-2010-2014/dead-planet/" rel="attachment wp-att-327384"&gt;&lt;img class="wp-image-327384 size-medium" src="http://www.veteranstoday.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Dead-Planet-320x320.jpg" alt="Dead Planet" width="320" height="320" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;Earth&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;(San Francisco)  High Radiation levels exceeded the Evacuation Level requiring the wholesale Evacuation of civilians at many locations in the States over the past four years, from 2010 to 2014. &lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Those who remain are supposed to be wearing Hazmat Suits.&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt;Did Evacuation happen in your town or city? No, of course not. It did not happen anywhere that I know of.&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This heavy radiation influx precedes the Fukushima Daiichi, Japan three reactor meltdowns. Therefore, by definition it cannot be just strictly from the Fukushima radiation releases.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The killing radiation came from some place else, plus Fukushima&#8217;s Rad. This pattern is repeated again and again in cities throughout America. What happened to beautiful Zapped America? Certain scuttlebutt in the nuclear community is that America&#8217;s is the most irradiated population in the world.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Getting Zapped like that leads to shortened life spans in the quaint expression of the American Nuclear Regulatory Commission. &lt;strong&gt;It means the Rad kills you&lt;/strong&gt; before your time. This is from greatly increased rates of radiation induced heart disease, radiation induced cancers and hundreds of other radiation induced illnesses.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Today&#8217;s HazMat Suit Required and Evacuation Level cities include a lot of locations. Notable due to very high Radiation levels are the following:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt;Billings, Montana, 769 CPM&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt; Idaho Falls, Idaho, 488 CPM&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt; Rapid City, South Dakota, 463 CPM&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt; Grand Junction, Colorado, 465 CPM&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt; Colorado Springs, Colorado, 536 CPM&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt; San Diego, California, 417 CPM and&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;strong&gt; Raleigh, North Carolina, 474 CPM&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span style="color: #000080"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;THE EVACUATION REQUIRED LEVEL IS 300 CPM [Counts Per Minute.] A single Count is One Radioactive Decay.&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;EVACUATE TO WHERE?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Evacuation is required by national and International law, with no mechanisms to implement it, other that private funds, ie, the victims own money in most cases. Where the radioactive people are to go is unknown. How to pay for their required health care is unknown. How to dispose of that many radioactive bodies is unknown.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;IS FUKUSHIMA TO BLAME?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The three destroyed and melted down reactors are not solely to blame for the nuclear deaths, illnesses and these, as yet undone, Required Evacuations. The current Evacuation Required cities of Billings, Idaho Falls, Rapid City, Grand Junction, Colorado Springs, San Diego, and Raleigh endured greatly elevated Radiation levels before Fukushima happened. The contaminated cities were close to or above Evacuation Levels 4 years ago and they still are. They are just higher because of Fukushima.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;WHAT TO DO?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Although excess radiation is now planet-wide, for people with two or more houses in different parts of the country or world it is easy to simply move further away from the Radioactive Contamination hot spots. The vast majority of people can&#8217;t do that.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Their only solution to the contagion is euphemistically called Shelter In Place. People do the best they can with what they have. Everybody gets irradiated by recent or previous radiation releases. Everyone is included. No one is left out. No one escapes.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Those with two or more homes may be able to extend their lives a bit. How much? Don&#8217;t know  it depends on the individual and the locations. Those that don&#8217;t own two or more houses in safe areas? Do the best they can with their shortened life span?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;That&#8217;s absurd; but, what other answer is there? EXAMPLE: See the graph of Billings&#8217; radiation below. The deadly level of radiation in Billings preceded Fukushima, as it did in many other American cities.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_327386" style="width: 650px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.veteranstoday.com/2014/10/25/comment-on-radiation-2010-2014/billings-mt-4-year-2014-10-20-14_27_17-raw-data-charts-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-327386"&gt;&lt;img class="wp-image-327386 size-large" src="http://www.veteranstoday.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Billings-MT-4-Year-2014-10-20-14_27_17-Raw-Data-Charts-640x504.jpg" alt="Billings MT 4 Year 2014-10-20 14_27_17-Raw Data Charts" width="640" height="504" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;Billings, Montana Four Year Radiation Graph Above Evacuation Level 10/20/2014 Raw Data Chart&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;The 4 year CPM graph clearly shows&lt;/strong&gt; the radiation count at or above the Evacuation Level for four years. The Sept 8, 2010 radiation level was 630 CPM. That was before three Fukushima reactors blew up on March 11, 2011.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The peak Billings&#8217; radiation level in the last four years was 1,087 CPM on Nov 1, 2012. That is 3.6 times the Evacuation Level and more than 10 Times the Alert Level of 100 CPM. Oct 20&#8217;s Gamma CPM: current 769 High 1,087 Low 435 Average 597&lt;/p&gt;
