   <p>
    The world of the Ancients' Crossroads is based on the Dungeons
and Dragons role-playing game.  Because of this, it is only fitting that I present you
with several tools and documents to help you with playing or creating campaigns.
These archives contain materials for 2nd Edition AD&amp;D, 3.5E, and D20 systems.
More tools and online documentation are added every month, so keep
checking here for the latest updates!
   </p>
