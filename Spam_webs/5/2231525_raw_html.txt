        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="lazy lazy-hidden alignnone size-full wp-image-12349" data-lazy-src="http://cdn.twentytwowords.com/wp-content/uploads/Cord-switch-e1292789532694.jpg?27a767" data-lazy-type="image" height="220" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" title="lamp switch on the cord" width="585"/>
