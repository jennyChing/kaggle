        <p>
         I first met this family run firm in Grand Designs Live London in May. Their stand caught my eye, their beautiful wrought iron beds were dressed in scrumptious bedding ripe for snuggling under. This family run company is set amidst the beautiful countryside, nature reserves and miles of unspoilt coastline of Norfolk. Its only nine years ago since the
         <a href="http://www.wroughtironandbrassbed.co.uk/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.wroughtironandbrassbed.co.uk/', 'Wrought Iron and Brass Bed Company']);">
          Wrought Iron and Brass Bed Company
         </a>
         was launched but he Oldfield family have built a strong reputation for quality bespoke beds with exceptional service.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tryingtobalancethemadness.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Family-photo-SMALL.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.tryingtobalancethemadness.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Family-photo-SMALL.jpg', '']);">
          <img alt="Family photo SMALL" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9490 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://www.tryingtobalancethemadness.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Family-photo-SMALL.jpg" data-pin-url="http://www.tryingtobalancethemadness.com/the-colour-range-from-the-wrought-iron-brass-bed-company/" height="1548" src="http://www.tryingtobalancethemadness.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Family-photo-SMALL.jpg" width="2000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         All of our wrought iron, brass and nickel beds are handmade in Norfolk by a team of craftsmen using traditional construction techniques. Their unique daisy trademark is welded to one leg of the bed which is such a personal touch,
        </p>
        <p>
         October is going to be an exciting an crazy month for the company, not only will they be exhibiting at Grand Designs Live in Birmingham, but they are also introducing their new Colour Range. The Colour Range consists of four new colours; Raspberry, Yellow, Nautical Blue and Purple; which can be applied to any bed style.
        </p>
        <p>
         By introducing vibrant new colours, we aim to give our customers full flexibility in creating their ideal wright iron bed; but still within a few confines. Out new colours aim to help our customer, explore their own individual style and imagination. However, sadly at this stage we havent mastered the art of pin stripe yet.
        </p>
