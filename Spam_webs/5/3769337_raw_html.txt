     <h2 style="">
      <iframe align="right" frameborder="0" height="80" scrolling="no" src="http://digg.com/tools/diggthis.php?u=http%3A//www.infobarrel.com/Poutine_V2_Recipe" style="" width="52">
      </iframe>
      The Ultimate Cholesterol Bomb Poutine
     </h2>
     After posting all the bacon recipes over the last week I decided to go out and make one of my very own to see if I could come up with a concoction nearly as disgusting as the bacon roll or the Ultra Bacon Whopper while simultaneously maintaining tastiness.
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     I would like to let you know that I succeeded on all fronts.  After hitting up our local Safeway, the total cost of the ingredients to build this legend was $65.  Well worth every penny.
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     Ladies and gentlemen, I introduce to you what I like to call the Poutine 2.0. (Known as the Carny Casserole on this is why you're fat)
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     This made three massive servings
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     <h3 style="">
      Ingredients:
     </h3>
     <ul style="">
      <li style="">
       700g ground beef
      </li>
      <li style="">
       2KG McCain Super Fries
      </li>
      <li style="">
       1 Large Bag Perogies
      </li>
      <li style="">
       500g Marble Cheese
      </li>
      <li style="">
       250g Provolone Cheese
      </li>
      <li style="">
       500g Bacon (1 package)
      </li>
      <li style="">
       1 bundle chopped green onion
      </li>
      <li style="">
       3 avocados
      </li>
      <li style="">
       3 fried eggs
      </li>
      <li style="">
       1 packet 4 peppercorn gravy
       <br style=""/>
      </li>
     </ul>
     <br style=""/>
     <div style="text-align: center;">
      <div class="articleImage" style=" width: 500px;">
       <img alt="Poutine V2 Ingredients" border="0" hspace="" src="http://media.infobarrel.com/media/image/972_max.jpg" style="" vspace=""/>
      </div>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <div style="text-align: left;">
       <h3 style="">
        Preparation:
       </h3>
      </div>
     </div>
     I'm not going to tell you how to cook all the ingredients as they are all simple things to make. But cook them all individually and have 3 disposable large casserole dishes ready to go, and have your oven pre-heated to 450F for the final baking.
     <br style=""/>
     <br style=""/>
     <div style="text-align: center;">
      Once all the items have been cooked, do the following:
      <br style=""/>
     </div>
     <br style=""/>
     <div style="text-align: center;">
      <span style="font-weight: bold;">
       Place fries on the bottom of the casserole dish, then top off with bacon slices.
      </span>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <div class="articleImage" style=" width: 500px;">
       <img alt="Fries and Bacon" border="0" hspace="" src="http://media.infobarrel.com/media/image/973_max.jpg" style="" vspace=""/>
      </div>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <span style="font-weight: bold;">
       Cover bacon and fries with all the marble cheese, then cover the cheese in ground beef!
      </span>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <div class="articleImage" style=" width: 500px;">
       <img alt="Fries, Bacon, Cheese &amp; Ground Beef" border="0" hspace="" src="http://media.infobarrel.com/media/image/974_max.jpg" style="" vspace=""/>
      </div>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      C
      <span style="font-weight: bold;">
       over the ground beef with perogies, then cover the perogies with provolone cheese.
      </span>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <div class="articleImage" style=" width: 500px;">
       <img alt="Fries, Bacon, Cheese, Ground Beef, Perogies, Provolone Cheese" border="0" hspace="" src="http://media.infobarrel.com/media/image/975_max.jpg" style="" vspace=""/>
      </div>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <br style=""/>
      <span style="font-weight: bold;">
       Sprinkle freshly chopped green onion over the provolone cheese then place in the oven and cook for 5 minutes at 450F.
