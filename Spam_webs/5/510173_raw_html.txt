        <img alt="bwyxsgrzzb2u44bnjlfl" class="size-large wp-image-475778 aligncenter" height="670" src="http://www.xxlmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/bwyxsgrzzb2u44bnjlfl-670x670.jpg" width="670"/>
        <p>
         It looks like the rumors were true. Drake releases
         <em>
          If Youre Reading This, Its Too Late
         </em>
         , a project that has been rumored for a while now. He shared the link
         <a href="https://twitter.com/Drake/status/566085353400852480" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://twitter.com/Drake/status/566085353400852480']);" target="_blank">
          through his Twitter
         </a>
         . A source from Drakes camp has confirmed with XXL that the project is a mixtape not and album. Earlier in the day Drizzy released anew short filmcalled
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2015/02/drake-drops-new-short-film-jungle/" target="_blank">
           Jungle
          </a>
          ,
         </em>
         which featuredthe OVO crew maneuvering through Toronto.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          If Youre Reading This, Its Too Late
         </em>
         comes right before his upcoming LP,
         <em>
          Views From the 6
         </em>
         .Toronto Raptors shooting guard DeMar Derozan
         <a href="http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2014/11/demar-derozan-drake-mixtape-january/" target="_blank">
          hinted
         </a>
         at an impending mixtape from the
         <em>
          Nothing Was The Same
         </em>
         rapper last November. P. Reign, an affiliate of Drizzys imprint OVO Sound,
         <a href="http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2015/01/drake-may-dropping-mixtape-year/" target="_blank">
          also confirmed last month
         </a>
         that a new project is coming out.With a series of headlining slots coming in the summer (Coachella, Wireless, Governors Ball), it would only make sense to have new music.
        </p>
        <p>
         Drakes new project,
         <em>
          If Youre Reading This Its Too Late
         </em>
         , is available on iTunes right
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/know-yourself/id966988592" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://itunes.apple.com/us/album/know-yourself/id966988592']);" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         . Its a good mixture of new songs and a couple of loosies we heard (6 God). Heres the tracklist below for the 17-track project plus read his handwritten thank you note.
        </p>
        <img alt="yjduse2flcr6cctxlaf2" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-475786" height="522" src="http://www.xxlmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/yjduse2flcr6cctxlaf2-670x522.png" width="670"/>
        <img alt="vyq3vh2cdmlmcewt92ft" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-475787" height="670" src="http://www.xxlmag.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/vyq3vh2cdmlmcewt92ft-670x670.png" width="670"/>
        <p>
         Drake put the songs on
         <a href="https://soundcloud.com/octobersveryown" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://soundcloud.com/octobersveryown']);" target="_blank">
          OVOs soundcloud
         </a>
         (
         <strong>
          Update:
         </strong>
         it was removed from Soundcloud and is now on Spotify), stream it here:
         <br/>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:album:2bfWH730j0HtAJrNcg1LuP" width="670">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         In other news,Drake-Diddy feud got a lot more interesting. The beef came to a head last December during Art Basel in Miami, when Diddy allegedly sent Drake to the hospital following a confrontation outside a nightclub. Now, Aubreys one-time mentor,
         <a href="http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2015/02/j-prince-drake-diddy-diss-track/" target="_blank">
          Rap-A-Lot CEO J. Prince
         </a>
         , has gotten in on the action, dropping a spoken-word diss track.
        </p>
