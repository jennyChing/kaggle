    &lt;h1&gt;Two found dead near Moama, on NSW-Victoria border; police investigate double murder&lt;/h1&gt;



  
    &lt;p class="published"&gt;
      Updated 
    &lt;span class="timestamp"&gt;
      November 12, 2014 18:21:56
    &lt;/span&gt;
    &lt;/p&gt;
  
  

&lt;div class="attached-content"&gt;
      &lt;div class="inline-content photo left"&gt;
    &lt;a href="/news/2014-11-12/police-cars-at-old-deniliquen-road-near-moama/5885204"&gt;











  
  
  
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
    
  &lt;img src="http://www.abc.net.au/news/image/5885186-3x2-340x227.jpg" alt="Police cars at Old Deniliquin Road, north of Moama where two people were found dead" title="Police cars at Old Deniliquin Road, north of Moama where two people were found dead" width="340" height="227"/&gt;
&lt;/a&gt;&lt;a href="/news/2014-11-12/police-cars-at-old-deniliquen-road-near-moama/5885204" class="inline-caption"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;
        Photo:&lt;/strong&gt;
       Police cars at a property on Old Deniliquin Road north of Moama, where a man and woman were found murdered yesterday. &lt;span class="source"&gt;(Supplied: Riverine Herald)&lt;/span&gt;
      &lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="inline-content map left"&gt;
      &lt;div class="story-map"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
      &lt;a class="inline-caption" href="http://maps.google.com/?q=-36.1421,144.7505(Echuca%203564)&amp;z=5"&gt;
        &lt;strong&gt;Map: &lt;/strong&gt;
        Echuca 3564&lt;/a&gt;
    &lt;/div&gt;
  &lt;/div&gt;
  &lt;p&gt;A man and a woman have been found shot dead at a property on the NSW-Victoria border in what police believe is a double murder.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Police and emergency services were called to the property on Old Deniliquin Road around 9:30am (AEDT), after a passer-by discovered the bodies near the side of a bush track, about four kilometres from the town of Moama.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;No gun was found at the scene.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Local police and State Crime Command's Homicide Squad have formed a strike force to investigate and appealed for information from the public.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Superintendent Paul Condon said the man was found seated inside a car.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;"The female's been located partially inside the vehicle and partially outside the vehicle," he said.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;"Police are looking at all avenues of possibility here. We have not processed the crime scene.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;"So it's unknown at this stage what motivations could have led to this horrific crime."&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Superintendent Condon said the area was surrounded by farms, but the crime scene was in a secluded area.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;"[The road] is used, but it just depends on the time of day and the time of the week."&lt;/p&gt;
