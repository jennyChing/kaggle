       <h2 class="post-title">
        AMC Releases Details and First-Look Photo for
        <em>
         Better Call Saul
        </em>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <p>
         Posted by
        </p>
        <p>
         1 year ago
        </p>
       </div>
      </header>
      <section class="entry-content">
       <div class="featured-image">
        <img alt="better-call-saul-first-look-copyright-560" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" height="330" src="http://images.amcnetworks.com/blogs.amctv.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/better-call-saul-first-look-copyright-560.jpg" width="560"/>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-text">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/better-call-saul" title="AMC - Better Call Saul">
          <!-- REMOVED_FI &lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-906221318" alt="better-call-saul-first-look-copyright-560" src="http://images.amcnetworks.com/blogs.amctv.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/better-call-saul-first-look-copyright-560.jpg" width="560" height="330" /&gt; FI_REMOVED -->
         </a>
         AMC today provided the first of several planned updates on the
         <em>
          Breaking Bad
         </em>
         prequel
         <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/better-call-saul" title="AMC - Better Call Saul">
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
         </a>
         , including the release of a first-look photo from the Albuquerque, New Mexico set where the widely-anticipated drama began production on its first 10-episode season earlier this month.
        </p>
        <p>
         This update includes:
         <br/>
         <span id="more-1192137">
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/breaking-bad" title="AMC - Breaking Bad">
           <em>
            Breaking Bad
           </em>
          </a>
          creator and showrunner
          <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/breaking-bad/crew/vince-gilligan" title="Breaking Bad - Crew Bio - Vince Gilligan">
           Vince Gilligan
          </a>
          is directing the
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          series premiere, and co-showrunning along with fellow
          <em>
           Breaking Bad
          </em>
          alum Peter Gould, creator of the
          <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/breaking-bad/cast/saul-goodman" title="Breaking Bad - Character Bio - Saul Goodman">
           Saul Goodman
          </a>
          character.
         </li>
         <li>
          AMC confirmed it has already ordered a second season of 13
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          episodes, for an initial total of 23.
         </li>
         <li>
          The network also announced a change of the series schedule, with
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          premieres slated for early 2015 and early 2016, respectively. Specific dates will follow, but the show will join
          <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/the-walking-dead" title="AMC - The Walking Dead">
           <em>
            The Walking Dead
           </em>
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/talking-dead" title="AMC - Talking Dead">
           <em>
            Talking Dead
           </em>
          </a>
          , two of televisions top-rated hits, on AMC in the first quarter of each of the next two years.
         </li>
         <li>
          The first-look photo (above) shows Gilligan and Gould from the set, with star
          <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/breaking-bad/cast/saul-goodman/bob-odenkirk" title="Breaking Bad - Cast Bio - Bon Odenkirk">
           Bob Odenkirk
          </a>
          (Saul Goodman).
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Production on
         <em>
          Better Call Saul
         </em>
         is underway, and we could not be more proud of, nor more excited about, the work to date. We join the fans in eager anticipation for this series, and today we happily confirm that our initial
         <em>
          Saul
         </em>
         order is for two seasons and a total of 23 episodes, said AMC president Charlie Collier. When introducing any series, especially one with the DNA of
         <em>
          Breaking Bad
         </em>
         , there are countless factors to consider in making sure the show gets the launch it deserves. We have a strong history with Vince, Peter, Bob, the studio, and so many involved with this production; we are enjoying the process on
         <em>
          Saul
         </em>
         and all share a focus on making it a true television event. No half measures.
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition to Gilligan and Gould, several
         <em>
          Breaking Bad
         </em>
         writers have returned to work on
         <em>
          Saul
         </em>
         , including Thomas Schnauz and Gennifer Hutchison. Bradley Paul has also joined the writing team, along with Gordon Smith, who was a writers assistant on
         <em>
          Breaking Bad
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Better Call Saul
         </em>
         is executive produced by Gilligan, Gould, Mark Johnson (
         <em>
          Breaking Bad
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Diner
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Rain Man
         </em>
         ) and Melissa Bernstein (
         <em>
          Breaking Bad
         </em>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.sundance.tv/series/rectify" target="_blank" title="SundanceTV - Rectify">
          <em>
           Rectify
          </em>
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/halt-and-catch-fire" title="AMC - Halt and Catch Fire">
          <em>
           Halt and Catch Fire
          </em>
         </a>
         ) with Breaking Bad alums Stewart A. Lyons and Schnauz as co-executive producers for Sony Pictures Television.
        </p>
        <p>
         To keep up with the latest news about the series,
         <a href="http://www.amc.com/shows/breaking-bad/better-call-saul-news" title="Better Call Saul - Newsletter Signup">
          sign up for the
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          newsletter &gt;&gt;
         </a>
        </p>
