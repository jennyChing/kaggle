       Drew Brees made

        National Football League history

       when he signed a five-year, $100 million contract with the New Orleans Saints in 2012, making him the highest paid NFL player ever. But Breess bucks havent all been earned yet.
       <a href="http://www.spotrac.com/" target="_blank">
        Spotrac
       </a>
       tracks the salaries and earnings of professional athletes, including NFL players. The following are the10 top career earners in the NFL.

      <h2>
       10. Richard Seymour
      </h2>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Career earnings
       </span>
       :$99,621,000
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Position
       </span>
       : Defensive tackle
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Teams
       </span>
       : New England Patriots (2001-2008), Oakland Raiders (2009-2012)
      </p>
      <p>
       Currently a free agent, Seymour has
       <a href="http://espn.go.com/nfl/player/stats/_/id/2554/richard-seymour" target="_blank">
        324 total tackles and 172 assisted tackles
       </a>
       . Seymour is a three-time Super Bowl champion, earning rings at Super Bowls XXXVI, XXXVIII, and XXXIX, all with the New England Patriots. He was named to the NFLs All-Decade team for the 2000s and to seven Pro Bowls.
