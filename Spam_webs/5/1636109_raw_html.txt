      <p>
       As you might already know by now, Evasi0n 7 iOS 7  7.0.4 jailbreak is out already. Here you can find a complete tutorial on how to jailbreak iOS 7 through iOS 7.0.4 on all the supported iPhone and iPod touch devices.
      </p>
      <p>
       Please read everything carefully before proceeding with the jailbreak.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="iOS 7 untethered" border="0" height="320" src="http://cdn.redmondpie.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/iOS-7-untethered.png" style="border-left-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom-width: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border-top-width: 0px" title="iOS 7 untethered" width="590"/>

      <p>
       Supported Devices:
      </p>
      <p>
       As mentioned in the previous post, all iOS devices are supported, including the latest iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c. Apple TV though is not supported yet.
      </p>
      <p>
       Supported iOS version:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        7.0
       </li>
       <li>
        7.0.1
       </li>
       <li>
        7.0.2
       </li>
       <li>
        7.0.3
       </li>
       <li>
        7.0.4
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Requirements:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        The latest version of iTunes must be installed on your Windows PC or Mac.
        <a href="http://www.redmondpie.com/download-itunes-11.1.3-for-os-x-and-windows-features-equalizer-fixes-and-other-improvements/">
         Download iTunes 11.1.3
        </a>
        from here.
       </li>
       <li>
        It is highly recommended you
        <em>
         Restore
        </em>
        to the latest iOS 7.0.4 on your device through iTunes (NOT OTA) before running Evasi0n 7.
        <a href="http://www.redmondpie.com/ios-7.0.4-download-for-iphone-5s-5c-5-4s-4-ipad-ipod-touch-is-out-direct-links/">
         Download iOS 7.0.4
        </a>
        from here.
       </li>
       <li>
        Make sure there is no lock screen passcode or Touch ID set on your iOS device. You can disable Passcode lock from:
        <em>
         Settings &gt; General &gt; Touch ID &amp; Passcode &gt; Turn Passcode Off
        </em>
       </li>
       <li>
        Backup your data to either iCloud or your PC via iTunes before jailbreaking.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <strong>
        How-to jailbreak iOS 7 / 7.0.4
       </strong>
       (instructions + video tutorial):
      </p>
      <p>
       Step 1:
       <a href="http://www.redmondpie.com/download-evasi0n-ios-7-7.0.4-jailbreak-for-iphone-5s-5c-5-ipad-ipod-touch-windows-mac/">
        Download iOS 7  7.0.4 Jailbreak Evasi0n 7
       </a>
       for Windows or Mac.
      </p>
      <p>
       Step 2: Connect your iOS device to your computer and start Evasi0n 7.
      </p>
      <p>
       Step 3: Now click on Jailbreak button to start the process.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="evasi0n 7" border="0" height="462" src="http://cdn.redmondpie.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/evasi0n-71.png" style="border-left-width: 0px; border-right-width: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom-width: 0px; float: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px auto; display: block; padding-right: 0px; border-top-width: 0px" title="evasi0n 7" width="590"/>
      </p>

      <p>
       Evasi0n will now perform the jailbreak in a series of automated steps. Just sit back and dont do anything silly. Allow it to complete the process. It may take a few minutes to complete.
