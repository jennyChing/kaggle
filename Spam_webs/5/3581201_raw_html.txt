      <h1 class="entry-title">
       Furthest Point From McDonalds
      </h1>
     </header>
     <div class="entry-content clearfix">
      <p>
       I came across this map today. It is a visualization by distance to the nearest McDonalds. First and foremost, this is not my data or visualization. I came across this on weathersealed.com on a post entitled
       <a href="http://www.weathersealed.com/2009/09/22/where-the-buffalo-roamed/">
        Where The Buffalo Roam
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       The map is all cool and dandy, we have seen these kind of things with Wal-Mart, it really comes as no surprise. However, the best stat to take away from this is that
       <strong>
        the furthest point you can get away from a McDonalds in the contiguous US
       </strong>
       <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;source=s_q&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=45.45955,-101.91356&amp;sll=37.0625,-95.677068&amp;sspn=47.972233,93.076172&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;ll=45.460131,-101.90918&amp;spn=5.347935,11.634521&amp;z=7&amp;iwloc=A">
        <strong>
         is in South Dakota
        </strong>
       </a>
       <strong>
        , where you can be 107 miles away
       </strong>
       from the
       <a href="/post/2009/12/10/ode-to-mcgriddle.aspx">
        wonderment that is the McGriddle
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p style="background-color: #8daad1; border-width: 1px; border-color: black; border-style: solid; padding: 5px; margin: 5px;">
       <a href="http://digg.com/travel_places/Furthest_Point_From_McDonald_s?t=28419159#c28419159">
        Digg
       </a>
       has brought to my attention that I am stupid and it should read farthest, not furthest. I shall leave it unchanged so I can feel the full extent of the ridicule I have earned.
       <img alt=":(" class="wp-smiley" src="http://blog.dagoosh.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/frownie.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="McDonald's Density Map" src="http://blog.dagoosh.com/pics/mcdonalds-density-map.jpg"/>
      </p>
