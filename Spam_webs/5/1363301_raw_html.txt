          <p>
           Do you remember the very first time you had a glass of wine? It probably made you feel like an adult, perhaps even a little bit classy? While you may have loved that adult feeling, you may not have loved the taste. It is true it takes some time to truly appreciate wine. Once you acquire that taste for a really good wine, it only deepens with time.
          </p>
          <p>
           You may feel like time has reversed and wish to go back to that time when you had that first sip of wine. If you are looking to have the best of both worlds there are these incredible wine glasses with built in straws. Truth be told the straw was designed to enhance the flavor of your favorite wine. You and I know the real truth here it is for grownups to have the chance to drink from sippy cups once again.
          </p>
          <p>
           While there are no lids or cartoon characters adorning the glass, the principle is still the same. You get to drink out of a straw without any judgment. And isnt that worth the price of admission in itself?
          </p>
          <p>
           You can purchase either a single wine glass with a built-in straw or a set of four. Personally, if I am going to be drinking wine from a straw that is not a solo event. I would shoot for the set of four and make it one small wild party. Because nothing says a crazy night like four grownups drinking wine from a sippy cup!
          </p>
