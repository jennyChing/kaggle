           <h2>
            <a href="http://moonbattery.com/?p=33252" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Saint Skittles and Purple Drank">
             Saint Skittles and Purple Drank
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="entry">
            <p>
             We know that Trayvon Martin had
             <a href="http://www.foxnews.com/us/2013/07/08/zimmerman-defense-to-continue-presentation-monday/" target="_blank">
              marijuana in his system
             </a>
             on the night he tried to kill George Zimmerman, and had been
             <a href="http://www.cbsnews.com/8301-504083_162-57404732-504083/trayvon-martin-update-school-marijuana-suspension-no-criminal-record/" target="_blank">
              suspended from school
             </a>
             for using marijuana. Some believe he was a
             <a href="http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v606/rubycalaber/In%20use/trayvondrugdealing.png" target="_blank">
              drug dealer
             </a>
             . You might assume his famous mission to acquire Skittles candies and Arizona iced tea (actually watermelon juice, but the media is only good at narratives, not factual details) was a classic example of a pothead with the munchies. Heres
             <a href="http://www.examiner.com/article/trayvon-s-skittles-arizona-tea-and-something-called-purple-drank" target="_blank">
              another theory
             </a>
             :
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              RashManly.com accuses Martin of admitting on his Facebook account as early as June 27, 2011 of being an abuser of a codeine, soft drink and candy beverage popularly known as Purple Drank or Lean. 
             </p>
             <p>
              Purple Drank is commonly used by Southern Rappers and wannabe suburban teenagers according to Urban Dictionary.
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             By mixing some cough syrup with the juice and Skittles, Martin could have produced some Purple Drank.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://theconservativetreehouse.com/2012/05/24/update-26-part-2-trayvon-martin-shooting-a-year-of-drug-use-culminates-in-predictable-violence/" target="_blank">
              The Last Refuge
             </a>
             backs up the theory with extensive documentation.
            </p>
            <p>
             Purple Drank has been referred to as the
             <a href="http://www.erowid.org/chemicals/dxm/dxm_article1.shtml" target="_blank">
              poor mans PCP
             </a>
             due to its potential effect of turning users into psychotic maniacs. A habit of indulging in such drugs might explain why the medias little darling attacked a neighborhood watch captain and attempted to dash out his brains against the pavement.
            </p>

             <img alt="purple-drank" border="0" src="purple-drank.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <div align="center">
             Purple Drank ingredients.
            </div>

             On tips from
             <a href="http://www.brycchancarey.com/abolition/wilberforce.htm" target="_blank">
              Wilberforce
             </a>
             and |.
            </p>
