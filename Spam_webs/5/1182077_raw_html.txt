          </h1>

            <img alt="Man attending phone call in theater." class="size-full wp-image-121" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/technozel.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/phone-in-theater.png?resize=650%2C366"/>

           <p>
            Sometimes piercing ringtones and luminous ringtones can often ruin your enjoyment of a movie or a play when someone forgets to switch their smartphones off in theater. Microsoft know how annoying it is, so company wants to do something about it, at least on its own devices. Microsoft wants to make our smartphones more smart and discreet. Microsoft is planning to add an inconspicuous mode that would turn off sounds and dim the screen in such situations.
           </p>
           <p>
            The best thing about this idea is that this mode can be activated automatically rather than depend on user to activated himself. Triggers such as a GPS (that can locate your location), event is your calender, or even an action in your browser (like buying cinema tickets) are discussedfor the idea filling, which was granted to Microsoft this week.
           </p>
