         <h3 class="summary" itemprop="description">
          Frank Ocean brought out Tyler, The Creator during his set.
         </h3>
         <div itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           Frank Ocean made his debut at
           <a href="http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.18250/title.dr-dre-snoop-dogg-to-headline-coachella-2012">
            Coachella
           </a>
           last night with a 40-minute performance that featured a guest appearance from his Odd Future cohort
           <a href="http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.19287/title.tyler-the-creator-gets-punkd-by-bam-margera">
            Tyler, The Creator
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           During the 37-minute set, the smooth crooner ran through a handful of cuts including
           <a href="../../../index/audio/id.19374">
            "Thinking About You,"
           </a>
           <a href="javascript:void(null);" onclick="loadTrack('a',19374,this)">
            <img border="0" onmouseout="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif'" onmouseover="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_orn.gif'" src="http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif" style="vertical-align: middle;"/>
           </a>
           <a href="../../../index/audio/id.19517">
            "Whip Appeal,"
           </a>
           <a href="javascript:void(null);" onclick="loadTrack('a',19517,this)">
            <img border="0" onmouseout="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif'" onmouseover="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_orn.gif'" src="http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif" style="vertical-align: middle;"/>
           </a>
           "Novacane" and
           <a href="../../../index/audio/id.18808">
            "Voodoo."
           </a>
           <a href="javascript:void(null);" onclick="loadTrack('a',18808,this)">
            <img border="0" onmouseout="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif'" onmouseover="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_orn.gif'" src="http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif" style="vertical-align: middle;"/>
           </a>
           Before performing
           <a href="../../../index/audio/id.19501">
            "Analog 2,"
           </a>
           <a href="javascript:void(null);" onclick="loadTrack('a',19501,this)">
            <img border="0" onmouseout="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif'" onmouseover="this.src='http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_orn.gif'" src="http://ocdn.hiphopdx.com/play_button_article_blk.gif" style="vertical-align: middle;"/>
           </a>
           included on
           <em>
            <a href="http://hiphopdx.com/reviews/id.1859/title.odd-future--of-tape-vol-2">
             The OF Tape Vol. 2
            </a>
           </em>
           , Frank brought out Tyler, The Creator to join him on the cut.
          </p>
          <p>
           Frank was recently announced as a performer at the upcoming
           <a href="http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.19340/title.lollapalooza-2012-line-up-announced-features-red-hot-chili-peppers-j-cole-wale-doomtree-more">
            Lollapalooza 2012
           </a>
           festival, taking place in Chicago, Illinois. He will serve as opening act alongside Marina &amp; The Diamonds on Coldplay's upcoming European tour.
          </p>
          <p>
           Watch the full performance below (via
           <a href="http://www.mrworldpremiere.net/2012/04/frank-ocean-coachella-2012-set.html" target="_blank">
            Yardie
           </a>
           ).
          </p>
