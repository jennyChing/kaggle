         <td class="fileinfo-paramfield" id="fileinfotpl_desc" lang="en">
          Description
          <span class="summary fn" style="display:none">
           Standard Model of Elementary Particles.svg
          </span>
         </td>
         <td class="description">
          <div class="description mw-content-ltr en" dir="ltr" lang="en" style="">
           <span class="language en" title="">
            <b>
             English:
            </b>
           </span>
           Standard model of elementary particles: the 12 fundamental fermions and 4 fundamental bosons. Brown loops indicate which bosons (red) couple to which fermions (purple and green). Please note that the masses of certain particles are subject to periodic reevaluation by the scientific community. The values currently reflected in this graphic are as of 2008 and may have been adjusted since. For the latest consensus, please visit the Particle Data Group website linked below.
          </div>
          <div class="description mw-content-ltr ro" dir="ltr" lang="ro" style="">
           <span class="language ro" title="">
            <b>
             Romn:
            </b>
           </span>
           Modelul standard al particulelor fundamentale: 12 fermioni (6 cuarcuri i 6 leptoni) la care se adaug tot attea antiparticule corespondente i 12 particule de etalonare (bosoni), i anume 3 bosoni vector slabi (W
           <sup>
            +
           </sup>
           , W
           <sup>
            -
           </sup>
           i Z
           <sup>
            0
           </sup>
           ), 8 gluoni (difereniai de sarcina color) i 1 foton. Acestora li se adaug nc 2 bosoni teoretici, bosonul Higgs i gravitonul (nelistat n tabelul de mai sus). De avut n vedere faptul c masele anumitor particule constituie subiectul reevalurii periodice ale comunitii tiinifice. Valorile reflectate de acest tabel n momentul de fa dateaz din 2008 i e posibil s fi suferit ajustri. Pentru ultimele consensuri, putei accesa situl Particle Data Group listat mai jos.
          </div>
