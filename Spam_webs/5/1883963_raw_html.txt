           <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
            <p>
             You can add new states and countries under Setup &gt; Data Management &gt; State and Country Picklists, click Configure states and countries. Click New button to add a new country, or click a country name, then New button, to add a state to that country.
             <a href="https://help.salesforce.com/HTViewHelpDoc?id=admin_state_country_picklists_configure.htm&amp;language=en_US" rel="nofollow">
              https://help.salesforce.com/HTViewHelpDoc?id=admin_state_country_picklists_configure.htm&amp;language=en_US
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Once states and countries have been added, you can also change certain attributes using the Metadata API:
             <a href="http://www.salesforce.com/us/developer/docs/api_meta/Content/meta_addresssettings.htm" rel="nofollow">
              http://www.salesforce.com/us/developer/docs/api_meta/Content/meta_addresssettings.htm
             </a>
            </p>
