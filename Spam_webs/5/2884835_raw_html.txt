       <p>
        <span id="_ap_wp_post_left_84012" style="float: left;display:none;">
        </span>
        <span id="_ap_wp_post_right_19873" style="float: right;display:none;">
        </span>
        <img alt="Sharp Introduces Smartphone with 9.6 megapixel camera" class="alignnone" data-lazy-src="http://img338.imageshack.us/img338/8382/500xsharpsharp.jpg" height="255" src="http://chillopedia.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Sharp Introduces Android Powered Smartphone with 9.6 Megapixel Camera" width="351"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="Sharp Introduces Smartphone with 9.6 megapixel camera" class="alignnone" height="255" src="http://img338.imageshack.us/img338/8382/500xsharpsharp.jpg" title="Sharp Introduces Android Powered Smartphone with 9.6 Megapixel Camera" width="351"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        Sharp has unveiled its first Android Phone which they are calling it IS03. The most interesting thing about the Sharps Android high end phone is it possesses a Retina Display like screen resolution.Sharp has announced this Android phone for the Japanese KDDI, which comes with a 9.6-megapixel camera and a display
        <span id="more-3980">
        </span>
        that has a  resolution that matches the
        <a href="http://chillopedia.com/technology/apples-new-iphone-4-ad-shows-off-retina-display/">
         iPhone 4s 960640 Retina display
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <div id="_ap_wp_post_bottom_4236" style="margin: 0 auto; text-align:center; display:none;">
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Its Features are explained as under:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Processor
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <strong>
         Sharp IS03
        </strong>
        is powered by a 1GHz Snapdragon processor.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <strong>
          Display:
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Its screen is a 3.5-inch  capacitive touchscreen with 960640 resolution which also supports the  low-power mode allowing you to read status, missed calls and other info  without the backlight on.
       </p>
       <div id="_ap_wp_post_top_80630" style="margin: 0 auto; text-align:center; display:none;">
       </div>
       <p>
        <span id="_ap_wp_post_left_84012" style="float: left;display:none;">
        </span>
        <span id="_ap_wp_post_right_19873" style="float: right;display:none;">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <strong>
          Connectivity:
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <strong>
         Sharp IS03
        </strong>
        packs a number of connectivity including EVDO  Rev.A, Bluetooth 2.1+EDR , WiFi and Infrared. It is equipped with GPS, a  1Seg digital TV tuner and FM transmitter and also supports the KDDIs  mobile wallet e-payment system.
       </p>
       <div id="_ap_wp_post_bottom_4236" style="margin: 0 auto; text-align:center; display:none;">
       </div>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <strong>
          Memory
         </strong>
        </span>
        :
       </p>
       <p>
        The handset has an internal memory of 512MB and is expandable via  microSD card for up to 32GB. It also comes preloaded with a 2GB microSD  card.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Sharp Introduces Smartphone with 9.6 megapixel camera" class="alignnone" data-lazy-src="http://img525.imageshack.us/img525/2166/kddisharpis031540x298.jpg" height="230" src="http://chillopedia.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Sharp Introduces Android Powered Smartphone with 9.6 Megapixel Camera" width="400"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="Sharp Introduces Smartphone with 9.6 megapixel camera" class="alignnone" height="230" src="http://img525.imageshack.us/img525/2166/kddisharpis031540x298.jpg" title="Sharp Introduces Android Powered Smartphone with 9.6 Megapixel Camera" width="400"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        <img alt="Sharp Introduces Smartphone with 9.6 megapixel camera" class="alignnone" data-lazy-src="http://img841.imageshack.us/img841/8042/sharpandroid577x319.jpg" height="230" src="http://chillopedia.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Sharp Introduces Android Powered Smartphone with 9.6 Megapixel Camera" width="400"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="Sharp Introduces Smartphone with 9.6 megapixel camera" class="alignnone" height="230" src="http://img841.imageshack.us/img841/8042/sharpandroid577x319.jpg" title="Sharp Introduces Android Powered Smartphone with 9.6 Megapixel Camera" width="400"/>

       </div>
       <p>
        <span id="_ap_wp_post_left_84012" style="float: left;display:none;">
        </span>
        <span id="_ap_wp_post_right_19873" style="float: right;display:none;">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        The entire handset weighs 138grams and is available in white,  black and orange. Currently, the IS03 handset is only intended for the  Japanese KDDI and it will be available in November and there is no indication whether itll be headed to  Europe or US.
       </p>
