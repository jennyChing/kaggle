    <h2>
     Worlds Beautiful Lakes: Melissani Cave Lake, Greece
    </h2>
    <p>
     <img alt="Melissani Cave Lake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-719" height="563" src="http://all-that-is-interesting.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/melissani.jpg" width="750"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     The Melissani Cave Lake, located near Kefalonia, Greece, was a hidden treasure up until the roof of the cave collapsed after an earthquake in 1953. The mystical sky-blue lake, surrounded by a thick forest, is its most charming in July and August when sunlight pervades the area, changing the color of the water and reflecting it on the surrounding walls. Melissani is named after a nymph, Melissanthe, who, according to Greek mythology, resided in the cave and drowned herself when the god, Pan, rejected her romantic advances.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="Melissani Cave" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-720" height="500" src="http://all-that-is-interesting.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/melissani2.jpg" width="750"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="Most Beautiful Lakes Melissani Greece Picture" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-721" height="505" src="http://all-that-is-interesting.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/melissani3.jpg" width="750"/>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Lake Matheson, New Zealand
    </h2>
    <p>
     <img alt="Lake Matheson Beautiful Lake Photograph" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-722" height="500" src="http://all-that-is-interesting.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/matheson.jpg" width="750"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="Lake Matheson Beautiful Lakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-723" height="563" src="http://all-that-is-interesting.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/matheson2.jpg" title="matheson2" width="750"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     In every season, from dawn to dusk, Lake Matheson is a splendor of color, immaculately reflecting the surrounding, towering mountains in its pristine waters.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="Beautiful Lakes Lake Matheson" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-724" height="512" src="http://all-that-is-interesting.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/matheson3.jpg" width="750"/>
    </p>

    </p>
