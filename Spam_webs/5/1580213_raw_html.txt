         <h3 class="entry-title">
          Helping Disabled Children Lead Independent Lives
         </h3>
         <h3 class="entry-submitter">
          Submitted by: 
						Queen of Hearts Foundation for Disabled Children, Inc.
         </h3>
         <ul class="entry-meta clearfix">
         </ul>
         <p>
          Hannah Fouche is an incredible little girl from Ocala Florida who is in need of a safe handicapped accessible Van. Her parents could use the $5000 as a down payment for a Van so Hannah can use her Legs other then just at home. She finally feels independent with her new motorized chair but needs transportation to get her where she needs to go!!
         </p>
         <p>
          Thank you from the bottom of our hearts,
          <br/>
          Michele Knott &amp; Staff
          <br/>
          Queen of Hearts Foundation for Disabled Children
         </p>
