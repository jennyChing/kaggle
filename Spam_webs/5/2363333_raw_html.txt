        <h2>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          Inspiration For The Iron Body 700 Workout Plan
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Awhile ago I was blown away by this 60 year old man that did 700 pushups a day. I was like wow! I dont think I can do that!
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, in celebration of the newly launched
         <a href="http://ashotofadrenaline.net/iron-body-club">
          <strong>
           Iron Body Club
          </strong>
         </a>
         , I decided that our first one month workout plan would be designed off of this mans regimen. Heres a video to see my inspiration.
         <br/>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/BzlJ_xDzmdg?rel=0" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>

        </p>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          The Iron Body 700 Workout Explained
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Description:
         </strong>
         For the next 4 weeks, as a community, we will be working through the below bodyweight exercise program. Each day we will be doing only 3 exercises. It will incorporate upper body, lower body and the abdominal region. Two days will be upper body. Two days will be lower body. And Ive interspersed abdominal exercises throughout the weeks.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you dont recognize some of the exercises below, sit tight.
         <a href="http://ashotofadrenaline.net/iron-body-club">
          <strong>
           Join the IBC
          </strong>
         </a>
         and Ill show you how to do them when we do that days workout.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Time Period:
         </strong>
         1 Month; 4 Workouts per week; About30 min per workout
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Split:
         </strong>
         Upper Body 2X/week; Lower Body 2X/week  I will be releasing the videos like this: Monday  Upper, Tuesday  Lower, Thursday  Upper, Friday  Lower. But feel free to tailor the days to your own personal schedule.
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Download:
          <a href="http://1tljmj43yo4m291xrgtfdtsq.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/downloads/2012/10/A-Shot-of-Adrenaline-The-Iron-Body-700.pdf">
           The Iron Body 700 Workout Plan Worksheet
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Print off this worksheet and as you follow along to the videos mark an X after you perform each set. Hang the workout plan on your refrigerator or some other place you will see it often. That way, youll stay focused and committed.
        </p>
