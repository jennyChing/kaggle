      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #000000;">
        From dune bashing through the Dubai desert in a Mercedes G-wagon to jumping out of a plane in Las Vegas, 2013 was certainly a year to be remembered. This year I also
        <a href="http://www.worldofwanderlust.com/the-world-of-wanderlust-story/">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          launched my first book
         </span>
        </a>
        to answer the seemingly endless questions surrounding what my job is, how I travel so much, and what its like to be a travel blogger.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #000000;">
        I started my year in Los Angeles, which was also followed by Las Vegas, San Francisco, and Yosemite National Park on the west coast of North America. From there, I made my way to the east coast to visit Washington DC, New York City (of course), and Boston. After six weeks in the USA, I reluctantly made my way back home to Australia to carry on with my university degree.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #000000;">
        Once I returned home, I finally gave in to my desire to travel as a career and gave my law degree the boot. However I was adamant that I would not quit or give up my education, so I chose to complete my Bachelor of International Relations alongside my global escapades, having been able to study this course via distance education. Throughout the year I also travelled to Vietnam, Queensland, Russia, Latvia, Turkey, Germany, Italy, Switzerland, The United Kingdom, Morocco, France, and Dubai.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #000000;">
        Once home from this incredible Europe itinerary, it was not long before I was off again on an adventure to Bali, Indonesia. After this trip I returned home to complete my final university exams (EVER!). With university out of the way, I made my way back to Los Angeles and Hawaii for the final trip of the year.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #000000;">
        After 60 flights, 4 cab rides exceeding triple digits, a handful of trains, 5 continents, and countless smiles for the camera, I have rounded up my favourite snaps from around the world to show you the best in travel for 2013
