           <p>
            There isnt a person who likes their personal space to be invaded or their personal items being used by others. Then there are incidents where one is a victim of theft or a burglary. What follows is a list of gadgets and items which can work as anti-theft gadgets and can also guarantee that your personal items shall not be used by your room-mates or colleagues. Check out the list of gadgets and tell us about the ones you already use in the comments section below.
           </p>
           <h1>
            16. Fake Security Cameras
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/16.-Fake-Security-Cameras.jpg">
             <img alt="16. Fake Security Cameras" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3362" height="431" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/16.-Fake-Security-Cameras-610x431.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            Whether its fake or real, having a security camera surely decreases the theft rate. The idea is a simple incorporation of scarecrow technique.
           </p>
           <p>
            You can get it
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1hmf9Gd" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
           </p>
           <h1>
            15. Anti Theft Bicycle Wheel
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/15.-Anti-Theft-Bicycle-Wheel.jpg">
             <img alt="15. Anti Theft Bicycle Wheel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3361" height="636" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/15.-Anti-Theft-Bicycle-Wheel.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            This is a cool way of rendering your bicycle wheel broken or useless by making use of collapsible bike wheel. This idea is still in prototyping stage. However, you can get bicycle anti-theft gadgets
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1abGyC4" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            14. 80?s Walkman iPod Case
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/14.-80?s-Walkman-iPod-Case3.jpg">
             <img alt="14. 80's Walkman iPod Case" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3365" height="1" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/14.-80?s-Walkman-iPod-Case3.jpg" width="1"/>
            </a>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/14.-Walkman-iPod-Case.jpg">
             <img alt="14. Walkman iPod Case" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3366" height="457" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/14.-Walkman-iPod-Case-610x457.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            Unless your thief is looking for a classic thing to steal which belongs to the 80s your iPod will be quite safe using this case.
           </p>
           <h1>
            13. Ugly Digital Camera
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/13.-Ugly-Digital-Camera.jpg">
             <img alt="13. Ugly Digital Camera" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3359" height="374" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/13.-Ugly-Digital-Camera.jpg" width="498"/>
            </a>
            Cameras are quite useful even when youre trying to re-sale and that is precisely why snatching or stealing of cameras is a common thing. However, this improvement, tested in Brazil suggests that if you render the camera ugly it is less likely to be stolen.
           </p>
           <h1>
            12. Biometric USB Flash Drive
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/12.-Biometric-USB-Flash-Drive.jpg">
             <img alt="12. Biometric USB Flash Drive" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3358" height="518" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/12.-Biometric-USB-Flash-Drive-610x518.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            The next generation USB is the biometric USB drive which allows access only on the basis of fingerprint detection.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1ik8mhC" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            11. Ironkey USB Flash Drive
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/11.-Ironkey-USB-Flash-Drive.jpg">
             <img alt="11. Ironkey USB Flash Drive" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3357" height="344" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/11.-Ironkey-USB-Flash-Drive-610x344.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            Say hello to this military level secure USB drive which is waterproof and self-destructs data when damaged or if the password is entered wrongly for 10 times.
            <br/>
            You can get yours
            <a href="http://amzn.to/IpeWTt" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            10. Postal Envelope Notebook Sleeve
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/10.-Postal-Envelope-Notebook-Sleeve.jpg">
             <img alt="10. Postal Envelope Notebook Sleeve" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3356" height="610" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/10.-Postal-Envelope-Notebook-Sleeve-610x610.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            This might work if the thief has not been watching the old Macbook Air commercials. Still, it is fun to use and can be rolled when not in use.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1b2U2nf" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            9. Newspaper Laptop Sleeve
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/9.-Newspaper-Laptop-Sleeve.jpg">
             <img alt="9. Newspaper Laptop Sleeve" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3355" height="505" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/9.-Newspaper-Laptop-Sleeve-610x505.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            This one works by concealing your laptop as a 50 cent newspaper. You can choose from a total of 5 newspaper designs to carry your notebook around in.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1abJbUj" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            8. Tesla Car Burglar Alarm
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/8.-Tesla-Car-Burglar-Alarm.jpg">
             <img alt="8. Tesla Car Burglar Alarm" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3354" height="320" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/8.-Tesla-Car-Burglar-Alarm-610x320.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            An innovative technique which once employed, upon triggering, will trap the thief within the Tesla Circle. There is a good chance though that this becomes more famous among dare-devils. However, this system hasnt been commercialized yet and youll have to make one as DIY.
           </p>
           <h1>
            7. Home Security Paintball Gun Turret
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/7.-Home-Security-Paintball-Gun-Turret.jpg">
             <img alt="7. Home Security Paintball Gun Turret" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3353" height="343" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/7.-Home-Security-Paintball-Gun-Turret-610x343.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            Heres a DIY gadget for your home security which employs the Paintball Gun. This turret can track intruders and fire paintball ammo. This sure will be a thing thieves would want to avoid.
           </p>
           <h1>
            6. The Brief Safe
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/6.-The-Brief-Safe.jpg">
             <img alt="6. The Brief Safe" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3352" height="272" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/6.-The-Brief-Safe-610x272.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            Dirty underwear is something no one wants to come in contact and this is the idea which has been employed when designing this brief safe. The underwear has been stained on purpose and one may place the valuables in it.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1cBQiIP" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            5. The Anti Theft Plug Mug
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/5.-The-Anti-Theft-Plug-Mug.jpg">
             <img alt="5. The Anti Theft Plug Mug" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3351" height="406" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/5.-The-Anti-Theft-Plug-Mug-610x406.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            We all hate it if someone uses our mugs during our absence and it sure drives us crazy. Here the solution for that. This mug comes with a plug which can be taken out rendering the cup useless with a hole in it. You can plug the hole once you come back and use the mug again.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://amzn.to/17ZZ1op" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            4. Anti Theft Stickers
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/4.-Anti-Theft-Stickers.jpg">
             <img alt="4. Anti Theft Stickers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3350" height="758" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/4.-Anti-Theft-Stickers.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
            In order to render your bicycle, motorcycle or car damaged, these stickers are the best deal. We all know thieves go for shiny and prettier stuff.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://shop.variationsonnormal.com/product/anti-theft-car-bike-device-rust-stickers" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            3. Fake Cassette Tape Car Stereo
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/3.-Fake-Cassette-Tape-Car-Stereo.jpg">
             <img alt="3. Fake Cassette Tape Car Stereo" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3349" height="457" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/3.-Fake-Cassette-Tape-Car-Stereo-610x457.jpg" width="610"/>
            </a>
            Car stereos have advanced quite a lot during the past few years and are now an attraction to thieves. However, by making use of this 90s old cassette tape deck one may easily fool the thieves.
           </p>
           <h1>
            2. Secret Electric Socket Stash
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/2.-Secret-Electric-Socket-Stash.jpg">
             <img alt="2. Secret Electric Socket Stash" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3348" height="300" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/2.-Secret-Electric-Socket-Stash.jpg" width="450"/>
            </a>
            This might look like a regular electric socket but it actually is a safe compartment where one can store personal belongings and no one would ever think of checking it out.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://amzn.to/1809Itt" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <h1>
            1. Anti Theft Lunch Bags
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/1.-Anti-Theft-Lunch-Bags.jpg">
             <img alt="1. Anti Theft Lunch Bags" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3347" height="330" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/1.-Anti-Theft-Lunch-Bags.jpg" width="497"/>
            </a>
            You will always find the kind which somehow finds it okay to treat themselves to their colleagues lunch boxes. Here is the solution for that; it makes your lunch look bad enough to keep these people away from it.
            <br/>
            You can get one
            <a href="http://www.thinkofthe.com/product.php?name=anti-theft-lunch-bags" target="_blank">
