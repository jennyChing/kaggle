                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

                  <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Ws-GyXoGSeI/U-julfV-uqI/AAAAAAAAlCU/DFF7bjvXAZw/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-01.jpg" width="500"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 The Buddha Bar hotels have been on our bucket list of places to stay for quite a while. There are only three such hotels in the world, one in Paris, one in Prague and one in Budapest. But I guess their romantic elegance preceded them and piqued our curiosity.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-79b3DD3fICo/U-jupVAPP5I/AAAAAAAAlDI/OC1zomoi6ak/s1600/hotel-buddha-bar-budapest-superior-room-01.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-79b3DD3fICo/U-jupVAPP5I/AAAAAAAAlDI/OC1zomoi6ak/s1600/hotel-buddha-bar-budapest-superior-room-01.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 We were given one of the corner rooms, which has outstanding views and a hanging balcony over the wide street leading to the nearby Elizabeth Bridge. In spite of the three large windows, the dark dcor can still be appreciated. It is a sexy chic ambiance, with colonial style wenge furniture, Asian artifacts and sleek details. No wonder it received the Best International Hotel Interior Award!
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-PMgpUqHteIQ/U-jupwUB8YI/AAAAAAAAlDQ/s0gz_AG9kVk/s1600/hotel-buddha-bar-budapest-superior-room-bathroom-02.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-PMgpUqHteIQ/U-jupwUB8YI/AAAAAAAAlDQ/s0gz_AG9kVk/s1600/hotel-buddha-bar-budapest-superior-room-bathroom-02.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 It is a very romantic room that invites us to relax at the sound of the Buddha Bar compilations available. The bed is incredibly comfortable and the toilet seat heated, a feature weve been missing ever since we visited Japan. Its easy to feel spoiled, as the positive vibes are everywhere.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-uG6I9bhiWJY/U-julTvGunI/AAAAAAAAlCY/MV5un1mgUdY/s1600/01_Buddha_resto.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-uG6I9bhiWJY/U-julTvGunI/AAAAAAAAlCY/MV5un1mgUdY/s1600/01_Buddha_resto.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-al3txs_SJ4U/U-julai2o8I/AAAAAAAAlCQ/JLyQ1jkvRa0/s1600/14a_Buddha_staircase.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-al3txs_SJ4U/U-julai2o8I/AAAAAAAAlCQ/JLyQ1jkvRa0/s1600/14a_Buddha_staircase.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 The historical Klotild Palace is elegant to say the least. On one side it faces Vaci Street, the largest shopping street in Budapest. The entrance to the Buddha Bar Restaurant is from here, separate from the hotel and quite inviting.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 The restaurant preceded the hotel and features high-class Asian fusion cuisine in a distinctive and luxurious atmosphere. A giant golden Buddha statue dominates the place, and the beautiful chandeliers, mirrors and candles create the perfect setting for a very romantic dinner. The atmosphere is complemented by Buddha Bar music and innovative dishes to die for.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 Eager to try the Buddha Bar signature cocktails, we start our dinner with the
                 <i>
                  Bubble W@p
                 </i>
                 (cocktail based on sparkling wine, elderflower liquor, strawberry and raspberry puree) and
                 <i>
                  The Ultimate
                 </i>
                 (based on almond milk, Cointreau, passion fruit juice and maraschino). We are smitten with the incredible flavours and cant wait to try the food. In all honestly, if you find yourself in Budapest, do yourself a favour and stop by.
                 <b>
                  The cocktails are memorable and really worth it.
                 </b>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 The menu is an interesting selection of Pacific Rim dishes, fine ingredients and Western touches. For starter I have the 
                 <i>
                  spicy tuna tartar with avocado and pink grapefruit
                 </i>
                 , that in spite of my suspicions that spicy in Hungary might mean spicier than in other places, I find quite mild.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 My husband on the other hand, cant have enough of the f
                 <i>
                  oie gras rolls with hoisin and truffle sauce
                 </i>
                 . Hungary is the second largest producer of foie gras in the world and for good reason. Their foie gras is absolutely divine.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-m4Pih8shAR0/U-jungGCLoI/AAAAAAAAlC4/Xqls9JjWZEY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-05.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-m4Pih8shAR0/U-jungGCLoI/AAAAAAAAlC4/Xqls9JjWZEY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-05.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 I cant wait for my main dish to arrive, since I ordered the Buddha Bar Budapest exclusive  the 
                 <i>
                  tataki salmon &amp; foie gras teriyaki kata sushi
                 </i>
                 . This is the most unusual sushi Ive ever had, and possibly the very best. And this is coming from someone who ate sushi daily for 3 weeks in Japan not so long ago. Its a bucket list dish!
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 Cipri opted for the 
                 <i>
                  black Angus entrecote and wasabi truffle with seasonal vegetables
                 </i>
                 , and while the dish was delicious, the wasabi flavor was not strong at all, so I guess if you are not into spicy food, you are safe here. We washed down everything with sake and Hungarian wine as recommended by our friendly waitress.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-z3Pv-FhYG90/U-jun2AqxiI/AAAAAAAAlC0/1J_a806hgIY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-06.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-z3Pv-FhYG90/U-jun2AqxiI/AAAAAAAAlC0/1J_a806hgIY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-06.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 The dessert is also on the exotic side, as we go for the 
                 <i>
                  green tea tiramisu
                 </i>
                  and 
                 <i>
                  chili strawberry tart with passion fruit ice cream
                 </i>
                 . Everything is exquisite and carefully prepared and the service excellent.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 Breakfast is an adventure through various continents. There is falafel, and smoothies, Danish pastries, and couscous. Apart from the buffet, we also order the blueberry pancakes from the menu on the table and a cappuccino so creamy that I ask for a second one.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 The dynamic atmosphere of the Siddharta Caf is complemented by Arabic music from the Buddha Bar collection.
                </div>
                <div>
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-vKlL_5pNBvU/U-juou-RY1I/AAAAAAAAlDo/BJB9CwIZHiY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-07a.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-vKlL_5pNBvU/U-juou-RY1I/AAAAAAAAlDo/BJB9CwIZHiY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-07a.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-IKGtfQ8jGTM/U-jumd9TDoI/AAAAAAAAlCg/0kW5T7SWT5I/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-03.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-IKGtfQ8jGTM/U-jumd9TDoI/AAAAAAAAlCg/0kW5T7SWT5I/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-03.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ofdphHHwNnk/U-jumCuDARI/AAAAAAAAlC8/NjhlwYnt5rY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-02.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ofdphHHwNnk/U-jumCuDARI/AAAAAAAAlC8/NjhlwYnt5rY/s1600/Buddha-Bar-Budapest-02.jpg" width="500"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <br/>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 The Buddha Bar is more than a boutique hotel; its a lifestyle, just as their motto says. To the relaxing atmosphere and delicious food, the Buddhattitude Spa adds an extra dash of luxury. And all we can say is that Buddha Bar not only met our expectations, but it exceeded them.
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ShJv-R8MdK4/U-juqNWB2TI/AAAAAAAAlDU/xjulhLSM_Z8/s1600/hotel-buddha-bar-budapest-wellness-04.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                  <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ShJv-R8MdK4/U-juqNWB2TI/AAAAAAAAlDU/xjulhLSM_Z8/s1600/hotel-buddha-bar-budapest-wellness-04.jpg" width="700"/>
                 </a>
