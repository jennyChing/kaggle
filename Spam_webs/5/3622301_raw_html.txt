2 carrots
1 inch piece fresh ginger
1 cup coconut water
1/2 orange, juiced
1 lime, juiced
1 pinch ground cardamom
DIRECTIONS

        <header class="entry-header">

         <h1 class="entry-title">
          <div class="tkk-post-indicator">
           <div class="indicator">
            <!-- &lt;img src="images/post-indicator.png" width="32" height="9" /&gt;	 -->
           </div>
           <span class="post-title">
            RISING SUN JUICE
           </span>
           <div class="clearfloat">
           </div>
          </div>
         </h1>
         <div class="clearfloat">
         </div>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <!-- content-RECIPES.php template file -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="RISING SUN JUICE // The Kitchy Kitchen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6204" height="813" src="http://www.thekitchykitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_5735.jpg" style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
           I love this combination of carrot, coconut, orange, and a bit of spice. Slightly sweet, nicely balanced, its super thirst quenching and refreshing. This would also make a brilliant smoothie if you blended coconut meat in as well. Enjoy!
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="RISING SUN JUICE // The Kitchy Kitchen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6205" height="813" src="http://www.thekitchykitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_5738.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="RISING SUN JUICE // The Kitchy Kitchen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6206" height="813" src="http://www.thekitchykitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_5744.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="RISING SUN JUICE // The Kitchy Kitchen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6208" height="813" src="http://www.thekitchykitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_5759.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          INGREDIENTS
         </h3>
         <p>
          2 carrots
         </p>
         <p>
          1 inch piece fresh ginger
         </p>
         <p>
          1 cup coconut water
         </p>
         <p>
          1/2 orange, juiced
         </p>
         <p>
          1 lime, juiced
         </p>
         <p>
          1 pinch ground cardamom
         </p>
         <h3>
          DIRECTIONS
         </h3>
         <p>
          Juice the carrots and ginger, combine with the juices and cardamom. If you dont have a juicer, pop everything in the blender, and puree super thoroughly. Pour through a strainer. The juice is best enjoyed immediately!
         </p>
