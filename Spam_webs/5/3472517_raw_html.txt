       <h1 class="single-title no-margin-b" itemprop="name headline">
        King for the Day, Draftkings Plays 3/8/13
       </h1>
       <span class="meta-time">
        posted by Mike
       </span>
       <meta content="Mike C." itemprop="author"/>
       <div class="margin-15b">
       </div>
       <div class="clear">
       </div>
      </div>

      <span itemprop="articleBody">
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="4732010" data-link="http://thefakebasketball.com/2013/03/king-for-the-day-draftkings-plays-3813/" data-summary="" data-title="King for the Day, Draftkings Plays 3/8/13">
       </div>
       <p>
        <b>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Studs:
         </span>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         LeBron James ($11,400):
        </b>
        Most people will debate LeBron or Durant today. Yes Durant has the better matchup but he is also on a back to back and could be in for a blowout vs. Charlotte. LeBron on the other hand will get you his 50 plus fantasy points against the 76ers.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Al Horford ($9,800):
        </b>
        Horford continues to stay on a tear averaging 51.7 fantasy points the last six games. Boston has had their issues against big men this season and with how hot Horford is he should be able to continue that tonight.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         James Harden ($9,000):
        </b>
        Harden gets himself a matchup with the defenseless Warriors. Over the last four games Harden has averaged 46.6 fantasy points per game and is averaging 43.1 fantasy points per game vs. Golden State this season. We should see another 40-50 point performance tonight from Harden.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <b>
          Mid-Priced:
         </b>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Jeremy Lin ($5,400):
        </b>
        I am a big fan of Linsmatch uptonight. It is no secret Stephen Curry does not play defense which has been able to help Lin averaging 39.6 fantasy points per game vs. Golden State this season. For this price he should return great value.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Dwight Howard ($7,300):
        </b>
        This season Howard has been inconsistent at times and worries owners when putting him in a lineup. Tonight though against Toronto he should be able to return some value for his price tag. Averaging 39.1 fantasy points the last five with this price looks good for meeting value.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         O.J. Mayo ($5,200):
        </b>
        Mayo has been on a decline which has brought his price down. Tonight Mayo faces a Pistons team that ranks in the bottom 10 for fantasy points allowed to SGs. Mayo had 41.8 in the first meeting with Detroit. Nice price tag and has great potential hard to pass this one up.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Chandler Parsons ($6,800):
        </b>
        I know I mentioned a few Rockets already but Parsons has been on fire as of late averaging 35 fantasy points per game the last four. In two games vs. GS this season Parsons is averaging 41.8 fantasy points per game. Harrison Barnes cannot contain him.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a data-ved="0CAUQjRw" href="http://www.google.com/url?sa=i&amp;rct=j&amp;q=&amp;esrc=s&amp;frm=1&amp;source=images&amp;cd=&amp;cad=rja&amp;docid=JQrq9zRccMmKaM&amp;tbnid=MIFLmKDF4rQ4aM:&amp;ved=0CAUQjRw&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.sbnation.com%2Fnba%2F2012%2F11%2F29%2F3708834%2Fchandler-parsons-shoulder-injury-houston-rockets&amp;ei=4AY6UdnoA6Sy2wWBz4C4Bw&amp;bvm=bv.43287494,d.b2I&amp;psig=AFQjCNGn0BMiChbEvJvmsIQ7rPkHqKm2Xw&amp;ust=1362843730461115" id="irc_mil">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="234" id="irc_mi" src="http://cdn1.sbnation.com/uploads/chorus_image/image/4145967/20121119_ajl_ai4_144.0_standard_352.0.jpg" width="352"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <b>
          Value Plays:
         </b>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Marco Belinelli ($3,400):
        </b>
        Marco continues to put up big minutes due to the Richard Hamilton injury. We should not see a change in that tonight which gives us a great opportunity to pick up a guy at a dirt cheap price who should meet value with no problem.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Nate Robinson ($3,900):
        </b>
        Robinson could be getting another start tonight which means good things for a guy who is priced this cheap. In the first meeting with Utah Robinson had 37 fantasy points; we might not see that kind of return but he should be able to get above value.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Markieff Morris ($4,000):
        </b>
        The injury to Marcin Gortat is going to move Sciola to center and Morris into the starting lineup. Morris has great upside tonight against the Kings who ranks 29
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        for fantasy points allowed to PFs. For this price tag on Morris he could be worth the gamble tonight.
       </p>
