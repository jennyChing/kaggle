I follow quite a few coupon bloggers and I am always impressed by how much they save! As much as I am inspired by them... I am just not organized enough to clip coupons and organize with sales. I have been looking at ways to coupon along without the time suck that is coupon clipping and Walgreens'
                     <a href="http://m5.walgreens.com/mweb5/urischema/redirect.html?desktop=https://www.walgreens.com/offers/offers.jsp&amp;locale=couponshub&amp;ec=disp_Paperless_CollectiveBias_BloggerOutReach_1x1_7.1.14" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
