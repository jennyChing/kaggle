            <h1 class="title">
             Airscape - Manami's Theme
            </h1>
            <p class="meta">
             on Luned, 05 Maggio 2014. 
Posted in
             <a href="/news-release/category/news">
              News
             </a>

             <a href="/news-release/release">

             </a>

           <p class="pos-subtitle">
            Out Now Through Black Hole Recordings
           </p>
           <div class="content clearfix">
            <div class="pos-content">
             <div class="element element-textarea first">
              <div>
               With Endless Forever, Welcome Home and year kiss-off Promise, 2013 saw Airscape deliver a flawless run of uplifting sun-seeking trance.
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="element element-textarea">
              <p>
               Acting as the precursor to a new Airscape LP, the Dutch outfit drew fan delight and press applause (
               <em>
                <strong>
                 a track that fills every square inch of the biggest room!
                </strong>
               </em>
               proclaimed MIXMAG on Endless Forever 
               <em>
                <strong>
                 Tune of the Month
                </strong>
               </em>
               ) in equal measures.
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="element element-link">
              <ul>
               <li>
                <a href="http://www.beatport.com/release/manamis-theme/1288841" target="_blank" title="Buy on Beatport">
                 Buy on Beatport
                </a>
               </li>
              </ul>
             </div>
             <div class="element element-textarea">
              <p>
               Now, with the epic entrancement of Manamis Theme, production masterminds Johan Gielen and Virtual Vault are back. With the bit firmly between their teeth, theyre ready to do even more floor damage in 2014 than they did in 2013!
              </p>
              <p>
               With more than a hint of the Orient in both its title and sonics, Manamis Theme sets up with battle-ready drums, rolling, Richter-scale-registering bass and echoing sub-riffs. With its air strung melodies generating atmosphere and altitude, Johan &amp; Ben use all the pads, chords, phase and delay at their disposal to build Manamis Theme up to its final super-string-driven conclusion.
              </p>
              <strong>
               A summers coming track, if ever there was one  Manamis Theme arrives in download stores on May 5th.
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div class="element element-link">
