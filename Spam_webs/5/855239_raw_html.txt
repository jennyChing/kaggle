      <h1>
       Watchathon Week  The Biggest Week in Television Is Here!
      </h1>
      <p>
       by

        Todd Gold
       </a>

      </p>

       <a href="http://xfinity.comcast.net/blogs/tv/files/2015/02/watchathonheader1.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-526483" height="291" src="http://xfinity.comcast.net/blogs/tv/files/2015/02/watchathonheader1.jpg" width="615"/>

      </p>
      <p>
       TVs biggest watching event of the year is back, now through April 12..
      </p>
      <p>
       And so are TVs biggest shows.
      </p>
      <p>
       During Watchathon Week, XFINITY(r) TV customers will have an all access pass to full seasons of TVs hottest shows, for FREE, only with XFINITY On Demand.
      </p>
      <p>
       Youll be able to watch entire seasons of favorites such as:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Game of Thrones
       </li>
       <li>
        Girls
       </li>
       <li>
        Outlander
       </li>
       <li>
        Black Sails
       </li>
       <li>
        House of Lies
       </li>
       <li>
        Penny Dreadful
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Plus, you can catch up on current top shows or classics you may have missed, including:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Empire
       </li>
       <li>
        Finding Carter
       </li>
       <li>
        Awkward
       </li>
       <li>
        Chicago P.D.
       </li>
       <li>
        Fresh Off the Boat
       </li>
       <li>
        The Last Ship
       </li>
       <li>
        The Wire
       </li>
       <li>
        The Sopranos
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       And dozens of others from top networks including HBO, SHOWTIME, STARZ, ABC, CBS, FOX, NBC, and more.
      </p>
      <p>
       In 2013, the inaugural Watchathon Week event shattered viewing records as XFINITY customers logged millions of hours catching up on TVs hottest shows. Last year was even more popular with customers watching nearly 50 million hours of shows over seven days. In fact, the last Saturday of Watchathon week, with nearly 11 million views that day alone, became the biggest day ever for XFINITY On Demand.
      </p>
      <p>
       This is our third Watchathon Week  and customers love it - as you can see from this Tweet we received last year: Watchathon! OMG! OMG! Im watching everything.
      </p>
      <p>
       We know the feeling. What will you watch?
      </p>
      <p>
       Let the binge-watching begin!
