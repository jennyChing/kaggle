      <div class="text">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/michael-symon/lamb-chops-with-eggplant-caponata-recipe.html">
         <img alt="michael-symon-lamb" class="aligncenter" height="462" src="http://foodnetwork.sndimg.com/content/dam/images/food/fullset/2010/3/3/1/FNM_040110-Michael-Symon-Easter-004_s4x3.jpg.rend.sni18col.jpeg" width="616"/>
        </a>
        Eating healthy for the holidays doesnt mean you have to forgo your favorite dishes. This Easter, indulge in all of the classics like lamb, asparagus and lemon meringue pie with a few lighter takes on the classics.
        <span id="more-53818">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/michael-symon/lamb-chops-with-eggplant-caponata-recipe.html">
         <strong>
          Lamb Chops with Eggplant Caponata
         </strong>
        </a>
        (pictured above)
        <br/>
        Just when you thought it would be impossible to find a main dish thats festive, healthy and easy to prepare, enter Michael Symons low calorie, gluten-free Easter main. Simply seasoned and grilled lamb chops are served with caponata, a sweet-and-sour Italian relish. Cooking the vegetables in balsamic vinegar adds robust, tangy flavor, so theres no need for extra oil.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="green-bean-and-egg-salad" class="alignnone" height="462" src="http://foodnetwork.sndimg.com/content/dam/images/food/fullset/2009/4/14/2/FNM060109WN017_s4x3.jpg.rend.sni18col.jpeg" width="616"/>
        <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchens/green-bean-and-egg-salad-with-goat-cheese-dressing-recipe.html">
         <strong>
          Green Bean and Egg Salad with Goat Cheese Dressing
         </strong>
        </a>
        <br/>
        Save yourself from the notorious post-Easter food coma by serving this energizing salad of fresh veggies, potatoes and hard-boiled eggs. The goat cheese-buttermilk dressing adds just the right touch of creaminess without pushing the dish into caloric territory.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="smashed" src="http://foodnetwork.sndimg.com/content/dam/images/food/fullset/2014/9/1/0/FNK_Smashed-Potatoes-with-Greek-Yogurt-and-Scallions_s4x3.jpg.rend.sni18col.jpeg"/>
        <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchens/smashed-potatoes-with-greek-yogurt-and-scallions.html#!">
         <strong>
          Smashed Potatoes with Greek Yogurt and Scallions
         </strong>
        </a>
        <br/>
        These low-fat smashed potatoes are just as flavorful as the traditional versionand you wont miss thebutter and cream. Tangy Greek yogurt not only adds protein and calcium, but its also a probiotic, which is important for gut health.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="mushroom-barley-roasted-asparagus" src="http://foodnetwork.sndimg.com/content/dam/images/food/fullset/2013/12/9/0/FNK_Mushroom-Barley-and-Roasted-Asparagus-Salad_s4x3.jpg.rend.sni18col.jpeg"/>
        <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchens/mushroom-barley-and-roasted-asparagus-salad-recipe.html">
         <strong>
          Mushroom, Barley and Roasted Asparagus Salad
         </strong>
        </a>
        <br/>
        Pile fragrant seasoned barley and button mushrooms on top of roasted asparagus for a guilt-free holiday indulgence.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="little-lemon-pies" src="http://foodnetwork.sndimg.com/content/dam/images/food/fullset/2008/8/12/0/piestarts_LittleLemonMeringuePies.jpg.rend.sni18col.jpeg"/>
        <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchens/little-lemon-meringue-pies-recipe.html">
         <strong>
          Little Lemon Meringue Pies
         </strong>
        </a>
        <br/>
        These elegant, individually-sized lemon meringue pies areperfect for capping off a filling holiday meal. Start by preparing a simple buttery crust in your muffin tin for easy portioning, then fill each one with tart lemon custard and a dollop of meringue.
       </p>
