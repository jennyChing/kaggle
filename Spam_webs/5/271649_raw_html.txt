                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     A Must Read About Delhi Rape Case - Awareness Message
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6012408906227970959" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">
                       <b>
                        <u>
                         <i>
                          #Voices Against Rape::
                         </i>
                        </u>
                       </b>
                       <br/>
                       A year ago in-front of his eyes his girlfriend was brutally raped by six bastards in a running bus of Delhi.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       He fought till the time he was beaten blue with an iron rod. They broke his leg,robbed him, snatched his mobile &amp; left him almost naked..
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       He fought till the time he could. Then on that chilling cold night he was thrown from the running bus along with his girl friend.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       They were naked,drained,exhausted &amp; both were bleeding for different reasons.
                       <br/>
                       He tried to stop passing cars &amp; kept on begging for a piece of shawl to wrap his naked girl friend &amp; finally he got it after 40 minutes.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Then he took her to the hospital,admitted her,called the police,called her parents &amp; did every possible co-operation that a true friend should do in the hour of need.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       He could have fled from the bus - he did not. He could have left her on the road - he did not. He could have claimed fame &amp; money ( as the father of Nirbhaya who took Rs 25 lakhs from UP CM,a job for his son &amp; many more from many people in his daughter's name ) - but he did not. He could have claimed his treatment money from Govt - he did not. He could have become the most frequently seen face in the electronic media in exchange of money - he did not. If this is not friendship then what is friendship??
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       When the entire country roared for the girl no one thought what this boy had gone through that night.
                       <br/>
                       He is not a film star or a cricketer. He is Awindra Pratap Pandey a boy from Gorakshapur in UP the boy who loved his girl from the core of his heart.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       When this boy is there somewhere in anonymity, why we Indians should celebrate friendship day on 3rd August to copy other countries??
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       We should celebrate friendship day in India  on 16th December to salute the spirit of Awindra who proved what friendship means.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Let 16th December be an official friendship day in India.
                       <br/>
                       Am I talking sense??
                       <br/>
                       If yes then please share this &amp; give your opinion here. Make Whatsapp a right platform of social awareness!!
                       <br/>
                       This is a broadcast message Forward to all your Friends
