        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         How To Make Chocolate Cobbler
        </h1>
        <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/">
         <img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pinit_fg_en_rect_red_20.png"/>
        </a>
        <!-- Please call pinit.js only once per page -->
        <script async="" defer="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/js/pinit.js" type="text/javascript">
        </script>
        <a class="YUMMLY-YUM-BUTTON" href="//yummly.com" rel="nofollow">
         Yum
        </a>
        <script src="https://www.yummly.com/js/widget.js?wordpress">
        </script>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.southernplate.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/chocolate-cobbler.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-662 pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://www.southernplate.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/chocolate-cobbler.jpg" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" height="300" src="http://www.southernplate.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/chocolate-cobbler-400x300.jpg" title="chocolate-cobbler" width="400"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Growing up, we had this probably once a week. I always thought it was because everyone liked it so much but according to Mama, the frequency of this dessert had a lot more to do with it being so quick, easy, and most importantly:
         <span style="font-style: italic;">
          budget friendly
         </span>
         . Generally, most folks have these ingredients on hand. It doesnt hurt that you dont even need an egg for it!
        </p>
        <p>
         As kids, we always called this Mud Pie. I guess it was a fun name for the three of us. Were having mud pie for supper!. I was introduced to the actual name only a few years ago while at a quilt retreat with my mother. I was speaking with a few of her friends and someone mentioned
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Oh! I had your mothers chocolate cobbler, it was WONDERFUL!
         </span>
         . I was awful confused.
         <span style="font-style: italic;">
          Mama makes chocolate cobbler? Ive never had chocolate cobbler. When did she make this?
         </span>
         Having honestly tasted everything my mother has made in the past thirty ummsomething years, I was a bit put off that she had not shared what was obviously a grand success of a recipe. After asking her, she shook her head and waved it off Oh, its just mud pie!.
        </p>
        <p>
         This cobbler bakes up magically. When you pull it out of the oven youll have a rich chocolate cake layer on top and a hot fudge saucy layer beneath. I highly recommend it be served with ice cream.
        </p>
        <p>
         We frequently package the dry ingredients and give them along with the recipe as Christmas Gifts. People are always intrigued by the name and very happy with the results!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh47aa1tI/AAAAAAAAAwY/iAfiWVtHtmA/s1600-h/HPIM3017.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh47aa1tI/AAAAAAAAAwY/iAfiWVtHtmA/s1600-h/HPIM3017.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh47aa1tI/AAAAAAAAAwY/iAfiWVtHtmA/s320/HPIM3017.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Youll need some self rising flour (see my buttermilk biscuit tutorial on how to substitute plain for self rising), vegetable oil, milk, sugar, cocoa, brown sugar, and vanilla.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh5Azl0fI/AAAAAAAAAwg/3PthMMbMdxw/s1600-h/HPIM3018.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh5Azl0fI/AAAAAAAAAwg/3PthMMbMdxw/s1600-h/HPIM3018.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh5Azl0fI/AAAAAAAAAwg/3PthMMbMdxw/s320/HPIM3018.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         In medium bowl, add flour
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp3.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh5Zu88gI/AAAAAAAAAwo/FwLDIlI4APE/s1600-h/HPIM3020.JPG">

         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         and white sugar.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh52U3BqI/AAAAAAAAAww/JNKXl4fAdXM/s1600-h/HPIM3021.JPG">

         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Cocoa powder..
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh6NrGkWI/AAAAAAAAAw4/eQIHQMjJbgI/s1600-h/HPIM3022.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh6NrGkWI/AAAAAAAAAw4/eQIHQMjJbgI/s1600-h/HPIM3022.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISh6NrGkWI/AAAAAAAAAw4/eQIHQMjJbgI/s320/HPIM3022.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         And stir to combine.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISiaVj6-FI/AAAAAAAAAxA/dxkdtXd031k/s1600-h/HPIM3024.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISiaVj6-FI/AAAAAAAAAxA/dxkdtXd031k/s1600-h/HPIM3024.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISiaVj6-FI/AAAAAAAAAxA/dxkdtXd031k/s320/HPIM3024.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
         To this, add milk
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISias9FCDI/AAAAAAAAAxI/J48OPsJSge8/s1600-h/HPIM3025.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISias9FCDI/AAAAAAAAAxI/J48OPsJSge8/s1600-h/HPIM3025.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISias9FCDI/AAAAAAAAAxI/J48OPsJSge8/s320/HPIM3025.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Vegetable oil
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibHMLmXI/AAAAAAAAAxQ/SJmsjYU10Kc/s1600-h/HPIM3026.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibHMLmXI/AAAAAAAAAxQ/SJmsjYU10Kc/s1600-h/HPIM3026.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibHMLmXI/AAAAAAAAAxQ/SJmsjYU10Kc/s320/HPIM3026.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         and vanilla.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibTqvmaI/AAAAAAAAAxY/1HsXJHkwh9U/s1600-h/HPIM3027.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibTqvmaI/AAAAAAAAAxY/1HsXJHkwh9U/s1600-h/HPIM3027.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibTqvmaI/AAAAAAAAAxY/1HsXJHkwh9U/s320/HPIM3027.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Stir.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibuM5V-I/AAAAAAAAAxg/WRSzE6rlW0k/s1600-h/HPIM3028.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibuM5V-I/AAAAAAAAAxg/WRSzE6rlW0k/s1600-h/HPIM3028.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISibuM5V-I/AAAAAAAAAxg/WRSzE6rlW0k/s320/HPIM3028.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Until it looks like this.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjDT1ftLI/AAAAAAAAAxo/AIMhmZrzkDs/s1600-h/HPIM3029.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjDT1ftLI/AAAAAAAAAxo/AIMhmZrzkDs/s1600-h/HPIM3029.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjDT1ftLI/AAAAAAAAAxo/AIMhmZrzkDs/s320/HPIM3029.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Spray an 88 pan with vegetable spray (or grease however you like).
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjEAQ4K1I/AAAAAAAAAxw/wf-ZYuI6VHM/s1600-h/HPIM3030.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjEAQ4K1I/AAAAAAAAAxw/wf-ZYuI6VHM/s1600-h/HPIM3030.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjEAQ4K1I/AAAAAAAAAxw/wf-ZYuI6VHM/s320/HPIM3030.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Spread into prepared pan.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjEX_nBcI/AAAAAAAAAx4/sWKH-hinfu4/s1600-h/HPIM3031.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjEX_nBcI/AAAAAAAAAx4/sWKH-hinfu4/s1600-h/HPIM3031.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjEX_nBcI/AAAAAAAAAx4/sWKH-hinfu4/s320/HPIM3031.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
         In a separate bowl, combine brown sugar and more cocoa
         <br/>
         (As always, actual recipe at bottom of page).
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjE2-NpmI/AAAAAAAAAyA/yuZFCSn0kXU/s1600-h/HPIM3032.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjE2-NpmI/AAAAAAAAAyA/yuZFCSn0kXU/s1600-h/HPIM3032.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp2.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjE2-NpmI/AAAAAAAAAyA/yuZFCSn0kXU/s320/HPIM3032.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Stir
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjFKnaSYI/AAAAAAAAAyI/Yuz-E3C9j9w/s1600-h/HPIM3033.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjFKnaSYI/AAAAAAAAAyI/Yuz-E3C9j9w/s1600-h/HPIM3033.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp0.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjFKnaSYI/AAAAAAAAAyI/Yuz-E3C9j9w/s320/HPIM3033.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         until it looks like this.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjyXj-8pI/AAAAAAAAAyQ/hHB-64sZoEI/s1600-h/HPIM3034.JPG">
          <img alt="" border="0" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-description="How To Make Chocolate Cobbler ~ http://www.southernplate.com" data-pin-media="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjyXj-8pI/AAAAAAAAAyQ/hHB-64sZoEI/s1600-h/HPIM3034.JPG" data-pin-url="http://www.southernplate.com/2008/07/how-to-make-chocolate-cobbler.html" src="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjyXj-8pI/AAAAAAAAAyQ/hHB-64sZoEI/s320/HPIM3034.JPG" style="display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; margin: 0 auto 10px;"/>

        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the top of your batter.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjytP9ZiI/AAAAAAAAAyY/kNqr2LVjSL0/s1600-h/HPIM3037.JPG">

        <div style="text-align: center;">
         SLOWLY pour hot water over batter. SLOWLY.
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bp1.blogger.com/_IH_ndl2Su2M/SISjzFjzf7I/AAAAAAAAAyg/5XAFQgnO08w/s1600-h/HPIM3038.JPG">

         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         SLOWLY
        </div>

             Chocolate Cobbler

            <div class="ERSTopRight">

             <div class="ERSSavePrint">
              <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">

            <div class="divERSHeadItems">

            <div class="ERSIngredients">
             <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
              Ingredients
             </div>
             <ul>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               1 Cup self Rising Flour
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                Cup sugar
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               2 Tablespoons Cocoa
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                Cup Milk
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               1 teaspoons Vanilla
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                Cup Brown Sugar
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                Cup Cocoa
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               1 Cup hot tap water
              </li>
              <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
               1 Cup chopped pecans (optional)
              </li>
             </ul>
             <div class="ERSClear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="ERSInstructions">
             <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
              Instructions
             </div>
             <ol>
              <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
               Mix together flour, sugar, and cocoa. Add milk, vanilla, oil, and nuts (if using). Mix well. Pour into greased 8x8 inch pan. Mix  C brown sugar and  C cocoa together. Sprinkle over batter. Pour, very slowly, hot tap water over dry mixture and batter. Bake in 350 oven for forty to forty five minutes.
              </li>
             </ol>
