       How Does Har Vokse Work?
      </h2>
      <p>
       Har Vokse is a unique hair loss product, in that it uses all natural ingredients. The developers of this product took their time in choosing ingredients that would work together in harmony, to help reduce hair loss naturally. Har Vokse is also unique because it uses both a topical spray and an ingestible supplement.
      </p>
      <p>
       The spray protects and strengthens existing hair, and gets right to the root of the problem and helps stimulate hair follicles for growth, while the supplement works behind the scenes to promote hair growth and supply all the nutrients that are necessary for a healthy head of hair.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="har vokse ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-514" height="267" src="http://www.mensanswer.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/har-vokse-ingredients.jpg" width="590"/>

      <p>
       This combination product works better than most other hair loss products, since it attacks the problem from two angles at once.  One of the main active ingredients in this product is Marine Polysaccharides, which helps promote re-growth of hair follicles. This ingredient is used both in the spray and in the supplement. Amino acids are also used heavily in this product. Amino acids have been proven to assist in the circulation of blood and nutrients inside hair roots.
      </p>
      <p>
       Grape seed extract, a powerful antioxidant, is also used, which helps strengthen and protect the hair follicle. The Har Vokse spray helps by cleaning out dead cells from the scalp, and stimulating the growth of new hair follicles. The supplement promotes new growth from the inside out. It is truly amazing that all these benefits are offered by a product that is 100% natural!
      </p>
