        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Free (or nearly so) Software for Authors
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <div class="entry-author">
          <div class="entry-author-byline">
           by
           <a class="vcard fn" href="http://novelexperience.info/author/hazellvenus/">
            Hazel Longuet
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div id="triberr_endorsement">
        </div>
        <p>
         The last year has taught me a thing or two, not least that writing these days is an entrepreneurial activity and, as any entrepreneur will tell you, surrounding yourself with the best tools to do the job is a must. However this can be a budget buster  but it neednt be.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          TOP FIVE ALTERNATIVE GO-TO SOFTWARE FOR WRITERS
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Ive gathered together my 5 go-to software choices for authors. The 5 together cost (125* $189**)where as the branded alternatives (Microsoft Word, Adobe Photoshop etc.) cost a budget breaking 1970* ($2978**)  thats a whopping saving of 1845* ($2789**) and in my opinion they are just as good if not better than the costlier versions.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://novelexperience.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Fridays_Fab_Five_Software3.jpg">
          <img alt="Free or nearly so Software for Authors" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter wp-image-260 size-large" data-lazy-src="http://novelexperience.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Fridays_Fab_Five_Software3-409x1024.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="1024" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="409"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Free or nearly so Software for Authors" class="aligncenter wp-image-260 size-large" height="1024" src="http://novelexperience.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Fridays_Fab_Five_Software3-409x1024.jpg" width="409"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          SOFTWARE:
         </strong>
        </p>

          see more

        <p>
         2. Scribus

        </p>
        <p>
         3. Pixlr Apps 
         <a href="http://bit.ly/14Zy9Fx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://bit.ly/14Zy9Fx', 'see more']);" target="_blank" title="Pixlr - Photo editing software">

        </p>
        <p>
         4. Wix Websites

        <p>
         <strong>
