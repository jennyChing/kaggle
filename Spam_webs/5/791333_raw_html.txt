      <div class="col-sm-5 instructions_responsive">
       <h2>
        Ingredients
       </h2>
       <ul class="ingredients">
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 cup milk
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/4 cup oil or butter
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/2 teaspoon salt
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 pound Yuca (cassava/manioc) flour
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         3 eggs
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
        </li>
       </ul>
       <a class="grey_button" href="/recipes/762293-P-o-de-Queijo/shopping_list" onclick="siteCatalystEvent('eVar35', 'Shopping List | Open Modal'); RecipeShowPage.listIngredients(this); return false;" rel="nofollow">
        Save a shopping List

      </div>
      <div class="col-sm-7 directions_responsive">
       <h2>
        directions
       </h2>
       <p class="steps_title">
        From scratch:
       </p>
       <ul class="instructions">
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          1
         </strong>
         <p>
          Preheat oven to 350F. Bring the milk, oil or butter, and salt to a boil in a large saucepan over medium-high flame.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          2
         </strong>
         <p>
          Remove from heat and slowly whisk in the yuca flour until it forms a smooth batter. Set aside to cool slightly, 15-20 minutes.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          3
         </strong>
         <p>
          Beat in the eggs one at a time until they are fully incorporated, then stir in the Parmesan cheese.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          4
         </strong>
         <p>
          Form 2-3 tablespoons of the batter at a time into balls with moistened hands and set on a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake until puffed and golden brown, 20-25 minutes. Serve hot. Yields: 20 rolls
         </p>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p class="steps_title">
        Yoki brand Po de Queijo Mix:
       </p>
       <ul class="instructions">
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          1
         </strong>
         <p>
          Preheat oven to 350F. Place the contents of the bag in a medium size bowl; add 1/2 cup water and 2 eggs.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          2
         </strong>
         <p>
          Mix well until all the liquid is absorbed by the dough. Keep working the dough until it gets smooth and homogeneous. Add a little salt to dough mixture.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          3
         </strong>
         <p>
          Form small balls and place them in a tray leaving some space in between so that they do not stick together when they grow.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          4
         </strong>
         <p>
          Place the tray in the oven for about 25 minutes or until they turn a golden color.
         </p>
        </li>
       </ul>
