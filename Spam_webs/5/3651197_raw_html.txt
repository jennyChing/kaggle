         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Franchise Opportunities: How Important Is Territory In The Scheme Of Things?
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          Posted by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/author/the-franchise-king" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             The Franchise King
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <div class="getsocial gs-inline-group">
         </div>
         <br/>
         <p>
          <a href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/do-this-before-you-sign-a-franchise-agreement">
           <img alt="map of franchise territory" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-13774" height="409" src="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/wp-content/uploads/territory-map-620x409.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Youve found a great (
          <em>
           hopefully
          </em>
          ) franchise opportunity.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youve talked to at least a dozen current franchisees.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youre starting to get excited about 
          <em>
           the possibilities
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          You havent been
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/franchise-business-tip-dont-get-derailed" title="don't get derailed when looking at franchises">
            derailed
           </a>
          </strong>
          by friends and family.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youve discussed some potential
          <strong>
           territories
          </strong>
          with your franchise sales rep.
         </p>
         <p>
          And, by doing that, youre starting to realize
          <strong>
           how important territory is in the scheme of things
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Now, about that 
          <em>
           territory
          </em>
          
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          How Does The Franchisor Look At Territory?
         </h2>
         <p>
          Territory is super-important.
         </p>
         <p>
          Not only is your potential franchise territory important when it comes to things like, size, geography, and demographics, its important in one other way. Here it is:
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Is Your Territory
           <em>
            Yours
           </em>
           ?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <a class="broken_link" href="http://reports.idealspot.com/helpmechoosefranchise/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="locations">
           <strong>
            Go Here For A Tool That Will Help You Choose A Great Franchise Location
           </strong>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Franchise Opportunities: How Important Is Territory In The Scheme Of Things?
         </h2>
         <p>
          So, is your franchise territory yours?
         </p>
         <p>
          In other words, is it
          <em>
           only
          </em>
          yours?
         </p>
         <p>
          Or
         </p>
         <p>
          Is it
          <em>
           not
          </em>
          only yours?
         </p>
         <p>
          In other words, is it
          <em>
           exclusive
          </em>
          ,  or can neighboring franchisees market and sell there?
         </p>
         <p>
          This is something you may want to find out before you get too far down the franchise highway youre cruising on.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <a href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/digital-franchise-ownership-products">
           <img alt="franchise highway" class="alignnone wp-image-13773" height="695" src="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/wp-content/uploads/cruising-down-the-franchise-road.jpg" width="391"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/how-to-change-the-franchise-world-commit-to-an-exclusive-sales-territory-model" title="exclusive franchise territories">
            You really need to read this article about exclusive franchise territories
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Did that article open your eyes up a bit? Are you thinking about calling your franchise sales rep to 
          <em>
           double-check
          </em>
           on a few things? Good. That means Ive succeeded in my goal of making sure you havent left any stones unturned.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Other Franchise Territory Questions
         </h2>
         <p>
          One of the writers at
          <b>
           Franchise Direct
          </b>
          just wrote an article on the topic of territory exclusivity in franchising.
         </p>
         <p>
          The article is a good one because reading it encourages you to ask your franchise sales rep more questions about their territory model. The article also comes with an added bonus.
         </p>
         <p>
          Holly Brochards article on franchise territories includes some questions (
          <em>
           4 of them
          </em>
          ) you need to ask
          <em>
           yourself
          </em>
          with regards to territory.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.franchisedirect.com/blog/exclusiveterritoryoraccountprotections4questionstoconsiderasyouresearchyourfranchise/?r=4577" title="franchise direct article about franchise territories">
            Read Hollys article on Franchise Direct
           </a>
          </strong>
          to find out if youve thought things through enough regarding your potential territory.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Know this:
          </strong>
          Once you sign your franchise agreement-which will include your defined territory and any restrictions on it, you own it.
         </p>
