       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Spicy Stir-Fried Tofu with Broccolini &amp; Snap Peas
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <!--September 7, 2014-->
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://bijouxs.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6251web.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_6251=web" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4514" height="900" src="http://bijouxs.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6251web.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In a hurry? Fire up the work for Spicy Stir-fried Tofu with Broccolini and Snap Peas. Dinner in 15 minutes, always a little jewel.
         <span id="more-4513">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Wok cooking is my favorite for quick, healthy meals  one-pan wonders that cook up in less time than it takes to prep the ingredients. This is a very simple stir-fry, a combination using just three main ingredients. Tofu, brocolini, and snap peas are served in a spicy sauce made with on-hand pantry ingredients. Easy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/95631192062831204/" target="_blank">
          My wok
         </a>
         is a treasured cooking tool. It has prepared hundreds of meals, its weighty frame maintains the heat, a very hot wok or skillet is key when stir-frying. Because cooking happens so quickly, having all the ingredients ready to go, i.e.
         <em>
          mise en place,
         </em>
         is paramount before you fire up the wok. A large, heavy gauge skillet can stand-in for a wok.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you have not cooked with tofu before, this stir-fry is a great introduction. The airy tofu cubes just love the spicy sauce. I use broccolini, with smaller florets and longer thin stalks, that cook very quickly. In the mix are crispy snap peas for a sweet little crunch. The sauce gets a kick from
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/95631192062831208/" target="_blank">
          garlic chili paste
         </a>
         , a Bijouxs Basics refrigerator pantry staple, used to spice up everything from eggs, to soups and grilled meats.
        </p>
        <p>
         Serve this spicy stir-fry classic with steamed rice, or toss some pre-soaked rice noodles into the wok at the end of cooking, for a substantial spiced noodle meal.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dinner in 15, always a little jewel.
        </p>
        <div id="as_always">
         <span>
          As always, enjoy.
         </span>
