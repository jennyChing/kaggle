       <div class="body-text">
        Police say one man is dead and another is in critical condition after an attempted murder-suicide in the Paoli section of Tredyffrin Township, Chester County.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Offices were called to the unit block of Summit Avenue at 11:47 p.m. Saturday for a report of a shooting.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        There they found the victim, John R. Williams, who allegedly told them he was shot by a family member.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Investigators say that family member, 70-year-old John Richard Berry, shot himself in the head with a revolver after he was found by police.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Both men were taken to the hospital. Berry was pronounced dead and Williams is listed in critical condition.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Police believe the murder-suicide attempt stemmed from an argument over "financial matters."
        <br/>
        <br/>
        One neighbor was stunned by the news.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        'They're very nice people.  We all grew up together, our kids play with each other, we go in and out of each other's house.  This is a close-knit neighborhood, everyone has been here for a very, very long time," Penny Jackson said.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        As the investigation continues, police ask anyone with information to contact investigators at 610-644-3221.
