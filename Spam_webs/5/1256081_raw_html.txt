      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span itemprop="name">
        5 Ways to Use Lean Inventory to Boost Your ROI
       </span>
      </h1>
      <p class="post-meta">
       <span>
        Posted by:
        <a href="http://www.systemid.com/learn/author/jschofield/" title="">
         Jay Schofield
        </a>
       </span>
       <span>
        April 14, 2015
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
        <p>
         <img alt="ways-to-boost-your-roi-0415-banner" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-6332" height="330" src="http://barcodereader.systemid.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/ways-to-boost-your-roi-0415-banner.jpg" width="940"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre a small business, lean inventory management may be a profit-boosting opportunity. Inventory management has a direct effect on your business level of effectiveness. You need to know the
         <a href="http://www.systemid.com/inventory-management-software/" target="_blank">
          state of your physical inventory
         </a>
         , as well as the level of demand for that inventory.
        </p>
        <p>
         When a business carries too much inventory, it tends to spend more on overhead. In some cases inventory may be losing value. On the other hand, when inventory is too lean, a company runs the risk of not being able to deliver its products in a reasonable amount of time in order to meet demand.
        </p>
        <p>
         According to Forbes,
         <a href="http://www.forbes.com/sites/billconerly/2014/08/07/inventories-a-problem-for-economic-growth-and-individual-companies/" target="_blank">
          GDP is notably affected by inventory
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <div class="su-divider su-divider-style-default" style="margin:15px 0;border-width:2px;border-color:#999999">
        </div>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #005c5d;">
          <em>
           Inventory swings destabilize the economy, and now many businesses are carry too much inventory, to the detriment of themselves as well as the entire country. Bill Conerly
          </em>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <div class="su-divider su-divider-style-default" style="margin:15px 0;border-width:2px;border-color:#999999">
        </div>
        <h2>
         What is the origin of lean inventory?
        </h2>
        <p>
         In the 1940s Toyota began to manufacture automobiles. While striving to develop an efficient manufacturing system that was good at eliminating waste, they derived the methodology of Lean
         <a href="http://www.systemid.com/learn/how-to-select-right-inventory-control-system/" target="_blank">
          Inventory Management
         </a>
         . The goal was to create the most effective process. Today, lean inventory has become popular in many industries and in businesses of all sizes. When you right size your inventory, the five major categories of finished goods, sub assembly, office supplies, raw components and MRO will have a size that is just right.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are 5 ways lean inventory can benefit your business:
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
         </b>
         1. Pre-asses you companys suitability for lean inventory
        </h3>
        <p>
         Changing your business processes is costly. Thats why you need to find out if it makes sense for your company. Fortunately, you can analyze your business processes to see if the benefits will be cost effective. According to Adam Sommers of
         <a href="http://www.inboundlogistics.com/cms/article/managing-inventory-from-fat-to-lean/" target="_blank">
          Tefen
         </a>
         NY When companies transition to lean, they are more susceptible to changes among their suppliers, and more sensitive to fluctuations in their supply lines.
        </p>
        <p>
         Important questions that you should answer are:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          What must you do to convert to lean inventory management?
         </li>
         <li>
          How much will the conversion cost?
         </li>
         <li>
          How much will you save by implementing lean inventory?
         </li>
         <li>
          How does your forecast look? Does the cost analysis show that your proposed process change will be cost effective?
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Answering these questions requires a great deal of honesty and critical assessment. You need to uncover those things that are well done as well as those things that your company struggles with. Implementing a new process will only succeed when its on the solid foundation of a well-designed operational structure.
        </p>
        <p>
         Creating value for your customers should always be the ultimate goal. This is an essential principle of lean inventory management.
        </p>
        <h3>
         2. Implement demand based flow to be cost effective
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="iStock_000000651302_Large" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-6331" height="256" src="http://barcodereader.systemid.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/iStock_000000651302_Large-300x256.jpg" width="300"/>
         The flow of inventory through your warehouse affects the value that you offer your customers, as well as the overall value of your business. In a lean manufacturing setting, inventory is only processed by a production center when its needed to fill a customers order. Its easy to overproduce if your production is based on anticipated demand. A major waste of time is due to product searches. Make it
         <a href="http://www.systemid.com/small-business-solutions/ic7.asp" target="_blank">
          easy to locate inventory
         </a>
         and spend the saved time on more valuable activities. Excessive inventory leads to higher storage costs.
        </p>
        <h3>
         3. Pull dont push your inventory
        </h3>
        <p>
         Conventional push based operations use historic data as a basis for inventory management. The result is often wasteful and costly overstocking of obsolete items.
         <a href="http://smallbusiness.chron.com/push-system-vs-pull-system-inventory-control-12650.html" target="_blank">
          In a pull based model
         </a>
         , only minimal inventory is stocked, replenished as needed. Effective pull based demand management requires continuously monitoring inventory flow.
        </p>
        <h3>
         4. Responsiveness leads to cost effective inventory
        </h3>
        <p>
         Demand for inventory is constantly changing. A continuous and rigorous evaluation of your inventory flow, along with effective demand management, empowers you to respond and quickly adapt to market changeswhen applied with business intelligence you will keep your inventory at the ready.
        </p>
        <p>
         Care must also be taken that lean inventory isnt overly lean. According to Gartner,
         <a href="http://www.supplychainbrain.com/content/research-analysis/gartner/single-article-page/article/devastating-inventory-shortages-require-companies-to-extend-their-lean-thinking/" target="_blank">
          Hondas sales declined by 28% in a single month
         </a>
         due to inventory shortfalls. In another example, shortages in lifesaving
         <a href="http://www.theglobeandmail.com/life/health-and-fitness/health/shortage-of-vital-bladder-cancer-drug-prompts-rationing/article20464929/" target="_blank">
          cancer medication
         </a>
         also left some patients at risk. Such examples illustrate how important it is to correctly determineinventory reserve.
        </p>
        <div class="su-divider su-divider-style-default" style="margin:15px 0;border-width:2px;border-color:#999999">
        </div>
        <h4>
         Related Article:
         <span style="color: #3366ff;">
          <a href="http://www.systemid.com/learn/inventory-management-software/" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #3366ff;">
            What is Inventory Management Software?
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </h4>
        <div class="su-divider su-divider-style-default" style="margin:15px 0;border-width:2px;border-color:#999999">
        </div>
        <h3>
         5. Commit to a continuous process of perfection
        </h3>
        <p>
         Commit to continuous refinement of your entire inventory management process. It will improve efficiency, cost, quality and cycle time, while supporting responsiveness to changing conditions. Theessence of the leaninventory concept is based on continuous product and process improvement in tandem with waste elimination.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Summary
        </h2>
        <p>
         Utilizing these
         <a href="http://www.systemid.com/learn/abcs-inventory-management/" target="_blank">
          inventory management principles
         </a>
         will save you money and strengthen your overall business strategy. Innovative inventory management techniques can improve service, reduce overhead and help foster more opportunities for growth.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          To learn more about inventory management software to boost your ROI, contact
          <a href="http://www.systemid.com/" target="_blank">
           System ID
          </a>
          at888.648.4452.
         </em>
        </p>
