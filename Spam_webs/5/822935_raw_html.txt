      <p>
       Ive seen tons of books mimicking our alphabet using our environment, but its great to see this refreshing book and set of photographs using a medium we all have access to; the sky and surrounding buildings. I have no clue where to buy this book, but have a glance at
       <a href="http://www.slanted.de/1368">
        this German website
       </a>
       or
       <a href="http://babelfish.altavista.com/">
        babelfish
       </a>
       the webpage to get a somewhat english translation of the page. If any of yall can find out where to buy this book, please send a link by commenting. Ill be looking up towards the sky more often now 
      </p>
