       <h2 class="title">
        <a href="http://www.catcancook.com/awesome-banana-muffin-recipe/" title="Awesome Banana Muffin Recipe">
         Awesome Banana Muffin Recipe
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="categories">
        <a href="http://www.catcancook.com/category/muffin-recipes/" rel="category tag">
         Muffin Recipes
        </a>
       </div>
       <!-- /.categories -->
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         These are by far the
         <strong>
          EASIEST
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          BEST
         </strong>
         Banana Muffins I have ever tasted! Even if you think you cant cook, they are 100% fool-proof which makes them all that much better! With only 8 ingredients (which you probably have on-hand) I am sure youll agree these are super easy. See for yourself in the feedback! There are over 1000 positive comments  if thats not good feedback, I dont know what is!
        </p>
        <p>
         They are extremely easy, and great tasting! So versatilechange them up and let me know how they turned out! You just cant go wrong with this GREAT RECIPE! If you like them, leave me a comment and tell me about your muffin victory!
         <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.catcancook.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.catcancook.com/wp-content/uploads/2006/01/banana-muffin.jpg">
          <img alt="Banana Muffin" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2229" height="180" src="http://www.catcancook.com/wp-content/uploads/2006/01/banana-muffin.jpg" title="banana-muffin" width="240"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Banana Muffins (makes 12 muffins)
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         3 or 4 Large bananas, mashed (the more bananas the moister, so I use 4)
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 cup white sugar (original recipe calls for 1 cup, but I dont like them too sweet)
        </p>
        <p>
         1 slightly beaten egg
        </p>
        <p>
         1/3 cup melted margarine or butter
        </p>
        <p>
         1 teaspoon baking soda
        </p>
        <p>
         1 teaspoon baking powder
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 teaspoon salt
        </p>
        <p>
         1 1/2 cups flour
        </p>
        <p>
         Mix the mashed banana, sugar, egg and margarine together. Set aside. In a separate bowl, mix together baking soda, baking powder, salt and flour. Mix wet and dry ingredients all together! Pour into greased muffin tins, and bake in 350 degrees F oven for approximately 20 minutes. Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         *These are much more flavourful the next day, and they freeze very nicely too!
        </p>
        <p>
         *Also, if you like walnuts, you can throw a handful of those in there as well for some added flavour and texture, or try adding chocolate chips, or better yet, BOTH! Yum 
        </p>

         UPDATE: If you like these banana muffins, try my
