      <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/technology/samsung-galaxy-camera-2/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Samsung GALAXY Camera 2 To Debut at CES 2014">
       Samsung GALAXY Camera 2 To Debut at CES 2014
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="meta-author">

      <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by Adrian">

        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Samsung Galaxy Camera 2
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Samsung GALAXY Camera 2
        </strong>
        is the successor to the
        <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/technology/samsung-galaxy-note-ii-samsung-galaxy-camera-hands-on/" target="_blank" title="Samsung GALAXY Note II &amp; Samsung GALAXY Camera Hands On">
         <strong>
          GALAXY Camera
         </strong>
        </a>
        which combines the quality imaging of a high end camera with sharing capabilities and an intuitive design to match most up-to-date smartphones. It will be featured at CES 2014 from 7 to 10 January 2014 at booth #12004 in the Central Hall of the Las Vegasntion Centre.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Samasung GALAXY Camera 2 now comes with upgraded and new features including a
        <em>
         <strong>
          1.6 GHz Quad Core processor
         </strong>
        </em>
        on the Android platform, an enhanced battery life of 2000mAh, 16M BSI CMOS Sensor, 21x Optical Zoom and 2.0GB of RAM. It will be faster and easier for users to shoot, edit and share high quality images. The GALAXY Camera 2 has a lightweight, retro design and a 121.2mm HD Super Clear Touch LCD.
       </p>
       <p>
        Like its predecessor, sharing is made simple with advanced Wi-Fi and NFC capabilities. There is a new 
        <strong>
         Tag &amp; Go
        </strong>
         function that makes it easy to connect the camera to NFC-enabled smartphones and devices to pair and transfer images swiftly.
        <strong>
         Photo Beam
        </strong>
        lets you view the picture on the camera and automatically send it to the paired smartphone.
        <strong>
         Mobile Link
        </strong>
        , on the other hand, lets you select images you wish to transfer to your smartphone at your own time. The
        <strong>
         Remote Viewfinder
        </strong>
        lets you control the camera using your smartphone  great for group photos and selfies too.
       </p>
       <p>
        28 different pre-set modes for different shooting scenarios are available through the
        <strong>
         Smart Mode
        </strong>
        . The
        <strong>
         Smart Mode Suggest
        </strong>
        feature analyses the scene while recognising lighting conditions, scenery, objects and the instantly recommends the optimal Smart Mode to get you that perfect shot. A new Smart Mode
        <strong>
         Selfie Alarm
        </strong>
        shoots five consecutive high resolution images for you to select the best angle to share immediately on your favourite social media sites.
       </p>
