           The Taj Mahal at Sunrise and a Palm Reading in Agra
          </h1>
          <div class="post-info">

           <p>
            My true wish for India was to see the mountainous North (which will come next) but I knew I couldnt be in Northern India and not see the Taj Mahal. My expectations were low if Im honest. I thought it would be crowded, stressful and disappointing, and thankfully I was wrong.
           </p>
           <p>
            We were there for opening, almost at the front of the line, and I was awe-struck when we turned a corner to see the Taj in all its magnificence, lit by the hazy glow of the morning sun to the East.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="taj mahal at sunrise" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2483" height="890" src="http://www.allthingsgo.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/taj_mahal_sunrise-600x890.jpg?85672e" width="600"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            We took our all-important photos at the entrance then donned some sexy shoe covers and entered the Taj itself, a mausoleum where sounds of low chatter echoed around the dome creating an eerie humming sound. Outside, the sun, still low in the sky and shrouded by mist, looked otherworldly, a huge red orb suspended in the sky.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="taj mahal close up" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2484" height="404" src="http://www.allthingsgo.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/taj_mahal-600x404.jpg?85672e" width="600"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Lunch was made interesting by a friendly and wise-looking palm reader in the gardens of the restaurant, where I took a chair and awaited my fate. He started by asking me my date of birth and marriage status then began scrutiny of both hands. You will marry in two years he said with a smile. Your first child will be a boy when you are 32 and a girl at 35 and your marriage will be long and happy he will not find another woman and you will also not find another man. I struggled to think who Id be marrying so soon but went with the flow.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="palm reading in india" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2485" height="398" src="http://www.allthingsgo.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/palm_reading_india-600x398.jpg?85672e" width="600"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            You will live until 87 he said as he penned dots down a line on my left hand, your family average is 82. He then looked at my other hand and spotted a freckle on my wrist. This is very lucky he said with a twinkle in his eye. More scrutiny revealed my prospects. You will be wealthy yes rich very successful. and youll live abroad he said, squeezing different areas of my hand. You are strong, the boss, and your first career was good but your second will be better. He also told me that the tips of my fingers are a perfect ten for health.
           </p>
           <p>
            I went away feeling quite pleased. He didnt break any bad news to anyone and I found something quite compelling about him so the encounter left me happy. I promptly invited everyone in the group to my wedding in 2 years time and pondered when I might become rich.
           </p>
