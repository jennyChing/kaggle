            <li>
             Be sure to rest your arm and hand often. Overuse can lead to injury. You must be careful.
            </li>
            <li>
             Left handers have to push the pen on the surface they are writing on if they are writing English, German, French or other languages that are written from left to right. This may cause the paper to tear, but this can be avoided easily with a correct posture and pen. This is not a problem when writing Hebrew and Arabic or other right-to-left languages with left hand.
            </li>
           </ul>
