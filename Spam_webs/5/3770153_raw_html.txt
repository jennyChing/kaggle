         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Extravagant Diamonds
         </h1>
         <p class="headline_meta">
          by
          <span class="author vcard fn">
           Kimberly
          </span>
          on
          <abbr class="published" title="2012-03-28">
           March 28, 2012
          </abbr>
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="format_text entry-content">
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           Diamonds are a girls best friend
          </span>
          <span style="font-size: small">
           they say.  For most women, a fabulous diamond ring given by the love of our life is far more than perfect. But for the ladies who require something a little ostentatious might be interested to know that diamonds can be adorned on just about anything
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           The Diamond Cake
          </span>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/B3D87082107BBC1074BAB5711FDDE0.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1296" height="407" src="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/B3D87082107BBC1074BAB5711FDDE0.jpg" width="299"/>
          </a>
          <span style="font-size: small">
           Not sure if I would recommend serving this cake at a wedding but it would surely be a show stopper.  This fruit cake is covered with 223 diamonds, weighing a total of 170 carats and costs $1.72 million.  Definitely a tough cake to swallow.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: xx-small">
           <i>
            via:
            <a href="http://money.ca.msn.com/savings-debt/gallery/gallery.aspx?cp-documentid=25358027&amp;page=1">
             money.ca.msn.com
            </a>
           </i>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           The Diamond Business Card
          </span>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/mm-630-black-astrum-diamond-business-card01-630w.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1302" height="220" src="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/mm-630-black-astrum-diamond-business-card01-630w.jpg" width="450"/>
          </a>
          <span style="font-size: small">
           They say a business card says a lot about your company.  If thats the case, Black Astrum must say I am hella rich, son! Can you imagine anything more impressive than handing a potential client your business card, which is worth $1,500?  The cards are made with a cast acrylic base covered with a scratch and chemical resistant coating.  Talk about a good first impression.
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: xx-small">
            <i>
             via:
             <a href="http://news.yahoo.com/blogs/technology-blog/serious-impression-exclusive-diamond-encrusted-1-500-business-023051573.html">
              new.yahoo.com
             </a>
            </i>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           The Diamond Manicure
          </span>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/top_15_luxury_items_at_harrods_l17xw.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1308" height="290" src="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/top_15_luxury_items_at_harrods_l17xw.jpg" width="450"/>
          </a>
          <span style="font-size: small">
           Nail art is all the craze!  For the girl who has a few extra bucks to spend on her mani-pedi  try the $51,000, 10 carat diamond manicure.  Hate to be the one to break a nail after this treatment.
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: xx-small">
            <i>
             via:
             <a href="http://www.bornrich.com/entry/top-15-luxury-items-harrods/">
              bornrich.com
             </a>
            </i>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           The Diamond Sprinkles
          </span>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/12739287D7F0AEAB803B713C78EE22.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1317" height="330" src="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/12739287D7F0AEAB803B713C78EE22.jpg" width="450"/>
          </a>
          <span style="font-size: small">
           Scotlands
           <i>
            Cupcake Glasgow
           </i>
           adorned their cake with diamonds supplied by
           <i>
            ROX
           </i>
           , making it worth a mind-blowing $164,000!  I dont know about you  but Grandma sure didnt make them like this
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: xx-small">
            <i>
             via:
             <a href="http://money.ca.msn.com/savings-debt/gallery/gallery.aspx?cp-documentid=25358027&amp;page=4">
              money.ca.msn.com
             </a>
            </i>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           The Diamond, Diamond Ring
          </span>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Shawish-Diamond-Ring_BonjourLife.com1_.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1287" height="310" src="http://blog.envisionweddings.ca/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Shawish-Diamond-Ring_BonjourLife.com1_.jpg" width="450"/>
          </a>
          <span style="font-size: small">
           For the conventional girl who wants a little something extra; I give you the worlds first diamond, diamond ring! It weighs a whopping 150 carats  costing a ridiculous $70 million. The Geneva-based jeweler,
           <em>
            Shawish
           </em>
           carved the entire ring from one piece of diamond.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: xx-small">
           <i>
            via:
            <a href="http://www.bonjourlife.com/2012/03/ring-carved-entirely-from-diamond.html">
             bonjourlife.com
            </a>
           </i>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium">
           *Sparkle Sparkle
           <br/>
           <i>
            - Jr. Coordinator
           </i>
          </span>
         </p>
