       <p>
        Get the Tools You Need to Stop Writing Today!
       </p>
       <h2>
        BY
        <a class="coloredLink" href="/authors/max-andrew-dubinsky" rel="author">
         MAX ANDREW DUBINSKY
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="break">
        - - - -
       </div>
       <div class="articleBody">
        <p>
         Are you a writer?
        </p>
        <p>
         Are words just pouring out of you?
        </p>
        <p>
         Are you excited to wake up each day and get in front of that laptop with a hot cup of coffee and your latest idea?
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you struggle experiencing life as its meant to be lived because you just cant stop completing written works?
        </p>
        <p>
         Then youre missing out on a crucial part of the creative process if you dont spend endless hours staring at a blank page questioning your existence. If youre not facing crippling battles with depression, questioning your place in the universe, or not developing tendencies for getting black-out drunk, then it sounds like you need help not writing your book.
        </p>
        <p>
         And Id like to coach you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Because if The Greats can go years without writing, so can you! Its time to get back to the blank page and stay there.
        </p>
        <p>
         I am offering exclusive classescontent Ive never shared beforeto help you get better at
         <em>
          <u>
           not
          </u>
         </em>
         writing.
        </p>
        <p>
         What qualifies me, you ask? For starters, Ive never written a single goddamned book.
        </p>
        <p>
         Every day I have countless ideas for new novels, screenplays, childrens books, young adult fiction franchises,
         <span class="caps">
          BDSM
         </span>
         erotica, and even college dissertation and thesis papers. And I dont have
         <em>
          one
         </em>
         completed manuscript to show for it.
        </p>
        <p>
         By signing up for my class, Ill teach you the secret art of procrastination by exploring the delicate arts of bingeing in excess on things like:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Netflix
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Pornography
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Texting your Ex
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Stalking your Ex
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Cooking classes
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Vacuuming
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Alcohol
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Inventing board games
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <em>
           Candy Crush
          </em>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Isolating yourself from loved ones
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Sleeping in until noon
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          And more!
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Plus! Ill share with you my personal and exclusive packagenot available anywhere elseof time-wasting websites carefully compiled by me for those days when Ive got an exceptional idea I just have to get on paper. This exclusive package is guaranteed to tie up hours and hours of your precious writing time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Feeling overly confident in your work? Use my Automated Rejection Letter Generator guaranteed to bring you back down to earth with the debilitating knowledge that everyone else is a better writer than you so why even try.
        </p>
        <p>
         My services are proven to get you to stop writing today. Just look at these testimonials:
        </p>
        <p style="padding-left:2em;">
         Max Andrew Dubinskys classes made me realize there are already too many books in the world. Whos got time to read another one? 
         <em>
          Alex P., 45
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="padding-left:2em;">
         Thanks to Mr. Dubinsky, Ive got so much more time in my day for Buzzfeed quizzes. Without him, I never would have known that Im the Ross of my friend group!  
         <em>
          Steven R., 34
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="padding-left:2em;">
         Dubinsky knows his craft better than anyone. One class with him and youll want to stop writing immediately and start watching
         <em>
          Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt
         </em>
         .  
         <em>
          Elizabeth M., 24
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="padding-left:2em;">
         Im drunk rite now and defanitely not writing. 
         <em>
          Todd, 29
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im blushing. Thanks, guys and gals!
        </p>
        <p>
         And if those testimonials werent enough, everyone who signs up today gets a free photocopy of some important notes I once scribbled on a cocktail napkin.
        </p>
        <p>
         This coaching course is one-of-a-kind. And for one easy payment that includes the rights to your latest manuscript, youll always have a reason to not put on pants and start drinking at noon.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its time to
         <em>
          <u>
           stop
          </u>
         </em>
         writing, and
         <em>
          <u>
           start
          </u>
         </em>
         talking about that book youve always wanted to write but just dont have the time.
        </p>
       </div>
