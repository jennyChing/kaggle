       <div class="headline_area">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         That time when I almost killed my husbands evil plant
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         <abbr class="published" title="2013-02-07">
          February 7, 2013
         </abbr>
        </p>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         in
         <span>
          <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/category/blog/humor-essays/" rel="category tag">
           Humor Essays
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/category/blog/illustrations/" rel="category tag">
           Illustrations
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p>
         If youve read my blog for a while you know I do not have a green thumb. At all. I can smile at a plant and it dies. Well, I have a story to tell you about this plant that David brought into our marriage. He and his plant were a package deal.
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Here-is-a-man-with-a-plant.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Here-is-a-man-with-a-plant.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4458" height="575" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x575xHere-is-a-man-with-a-plant.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.ih9XNqK-NI.jpg" title="Here is a man with a plant" width="575"/>
         </a>
         Heres the deal with thisplant. It is now 12 years old. And that makes it special.
         <em>
          To David.
         </em>
         He talks about this plant like it is his childhood sweetheart. He dotes on it, checks on it, and pretty much loves it unconditionally. I know the plants history. I know how long David has kept it alive. I know it all.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          This plant is-
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         12 years old, I interrupt.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Its very-
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Special to you, I know! I inform him.
        </p>
        <p>
         So the other day as I was busy doing some cleaning I noticed plant looked a little dry.
         <em>
          I should water it
         </em>
         , I said to myself. And so I did.
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Wow-a-plant.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Wow-a-plant.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4459" height="539" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x539xWow-a-plant.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.KXkI2yqC7K.jpg" title="Wow - a plant!" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There you go little fellaor little misses, or whatever you are. I sat plant back where it came from and went on with my day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/watering-plants.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/watering-plants.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4460" height="629" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x629xwatering-plants.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.X4pi1cMk9G.jpg" title="watering plants" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A couple hours passed by and I noticed something just wasnt quite right.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/plant-is-sick.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/plant-is-sick.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4461" height="538" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x538xplant-is-sick.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.cbU3_lydnw.jpg" title="plant is sick" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Great! I guess I gave it too much water. I decided to sit it outside on the deck and let the sunshine dry it out a bit. I mean that would do it, right? Surely good ole sunshine will put a spring back in its step. Ha! Brilliant! I needed the kids to leave it alone though. I was worried about them messing with it and making matters worse than they already were.
         <strong>
          Do NOT touch this plant, kids. Are you listening? Leave. It. Alone. It needs to get some sunshine, OK?
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Okay
         </strong>
         ,
         <em>
          OKAY
         </em>
         ,
         <strong>
          okay
         </strong>
         , OKAY, I heard four small voices sweetly confirm.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/We-are-watching-that-plant.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/We-are-watching-that-plant.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4462" height="495" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x495xWe-are-watching-that-plant.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.wD0mPj5vCo.jpg" title="We are watching that plant" width="575"/>
         </a>
         <em>
         </em>
         <em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Sigh, OKeverything will be fine now
         </em>
         , and off I went to finish some cleaning.
         <em>
          Man I sure hope it dries out soon!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Unfortunately, I was unaware that the kids just finished learning about taking care of plants at school.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
         </em>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/plants-need-water.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/plants-need-water.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4463" height="429" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x429xplants-need-water.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.FpWaoHYFJk.jpg" title="plants need water" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The rest of the day flew by. I started dinner and then went to grab plant to put it back in its spot before David came home from work. And I see this:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Look-at-that-spilled-plant.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Look-at-that-spilled-plant.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4464" height="449" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x449xLook-at-that-spilled-plant.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.Zb89-XdlGq.jpg" title="Look at that spilled plant!!" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A soggy, wilted, greenish-brown, sad mess.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Youre-kidding-right.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Youre-kidding-right.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4465" height="475" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x475xYoure-kidding-right.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.8OdhAnYcih.jpg" title="You're kidding right" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Tell me something. Why is the ONE thing you ask kids not to touch, the ONE thing they cant leave alone? It looked worse than it did before I SAT IT OUTSIDE! I placed it back on the counter and was hoping things would be really crazy and loud as they
         <strong>
          usually were
         </strong>
         and David
         <em>
          would not
         </em>
         notice his plant. The kids were loud. Good! The kids were jumping and running all over the kitchen. Good!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/MY-PLANT.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/MY-PLANT.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4466" height="475" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x475xMY-PLANT.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.zs7K3WtUVq.jpg" title="MY PLANT!!!!" width="575"/>
         </a>
         <em>
         </em>
         <em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Well, Crapola! Not happening.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         So I go on my rant. OK Look David, I try my best to take care of everything. YOUR plant was dry-as-a-bone. It was wilting so I gave it some water. SUE me, OK?? Something is
         <strong>
          WRONG
         </strong>
         with that plant, I hissed. It is purposely acting all
         <em>
          Ohhhh, Im siiick.Ohhhh, look what
          <strong>
           she
          </strong>
          did to me..ooohhhhh, blah blah blah.
         </em>
         YOUR plant is evil and it has a BRAIN!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Your-plant-is-stupid.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Your-plant-is-stupid.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4467" height="425" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x425xYour-plant-is-stupid.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.HUPtZuWgQ8.jpg" title="Your plant is stupid!" width="575"/>
         </a>
         <em>
         </em>
         <em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Just let me do ALL the watering and caring for my plant from now on, OK!!?
         </em>
         David
         <del>
          barked
         </del>
         suggested.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/whatever.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/whatever.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4468" height="525" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x525xwhatever.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.czu5GxA-fd.jpg" title="whatever!" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And since that time, I
         <strong>
          have
         </strong>
         let him do all the caring for it. I refuse to touch it, move it, or even wipe off cake batter that my blender throws its way. Whatever. And yall, late at night? When its dark and Im walking through the kitchen, I swear it
         <em>
          hisses at me.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
         </em>
         <em>
         </em>
         <a href="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/you-are-a-scary-plant.png?c8ea6c" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/you-are-a-scary-plant.png', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4469" height="413" src="http://www.wrinkledmommy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/575x413xyou-are-a-scary-plant.png,qc8ea6c.pagespeed.ic.GBV3ZgA_n2.jpg" title="you are a scary plant" width="575"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
