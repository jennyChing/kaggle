            <h5 class="post-title">
             Hovering Home: Near-Invisible Mirrored Forest Residence
            </h5>
            <div class="post-author">
             Article by
             <a href="/author/Steph/">
              Steph
             </a>
             , filed under
             <a href="http://weburbanist.com/category/architecture/houses-residential/">
              Houses &amp; Residential
             </a>
             in the
             <a href="http://weburbanist.com/category/architecture/">
              Architecture
             </a>
             category.
            </div>
            <div class="urb-ads-top">
            </div>
            <div class="postcontent">
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
               <img alt="hovering home 1" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-77151" height="331" src="http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-1-468x331.jpg" width="468"/>
              </span>
             </p>
            </div>
            <!-- .postcontent -->
           </div>
           <!-- .bigmove -->
           <!-- old ozh units 3xx 2xx in article after 1st image -->
           <div class="post-ads-toc-box urb-ads-toc" id="urb-ads-toc-box" style="display: none;">
           </div>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://weburbanist.com/2015/03/06/hovering-home-near-invisible-mirrored-forest-residence/&amp;media=http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-1-468x331.jpg&amp;description=Hovering Home: Near-Invisible Mirrored Forest Residence')">
           </span>
           <p>
            A white box seems to hover in mid-air in the middle of the forest, with no visible supports suspending it from adjacent trees. This structure isnt a treehouse at all, and its actually at least twice the size it appears. It virtually disappears into its natural environment thanks to the mirrors covering most of its bottom story, reflecting nearby trees and the forest floor.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
             <img alt="hovering home 6" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-77152" height="331" src="http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-6-468x331.jpg" width="468"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://weburbanist.com/2015/03/06/hovering-home-near-invisible-mirrored-forest-residence/&amp;media=http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-6-468x331.jpg&amp;description=Hovering Home: Near-Invisible Mirrored Forest Residence')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
             <img alt="hovering home 3" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-77149" height="331" src="http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-3-468x331.jpg" width="468"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://weburbanist.com/2015/03/06/hovering-home-near-invisible-mirrored-forest-residence/&amp;media=http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-3-468x331.jpg&amp;description=Hovering Home: Near-Invisible Mirrored Forest Residence')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Izabelin House by
            <a href="http://www.reformarchitekt.pl/">
             Reform Architekt
            </a>
            is a woodland retreat outside Warsaw, Poland, designed as a tranquil getaway that blends into the forest, becoming a part of it. Approaching the two-story dwelling from the street side, it doesnt appear out of the ordinary. Its when you step to the side or the back that the illusion comes into play.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
             <img alt="hovering home 5" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-77153" height="331" src="http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-5-468x331.jpg" width="468"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://weburbanist.com/2015/03/06/hovering-home-near-invisible-mirrored-forest-residence/&amp;media=http://weburbanist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hovering-home-5-468x331.jpg&amp;description=Hovering Home: Near-Invisible Mirrored Forest Residence')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
