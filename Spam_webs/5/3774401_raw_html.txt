       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <strong>
          written by: Gerald01
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.letsleepingblogslie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Depositphotos_5721276_xs.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="Flying Sheep" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.letsleepingblogslie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Depositphotos_5721276_xs_thumb.jpg?resize=322%2C431" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: left; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; border-left: 0px; display: inline; padding-right: 0px" title="Flying Sheep"/>
         </a>
         Are there times you have woken up suddenly and wondered where your dream has come from? Have you ever wondered how weird a dream you had was? And asked yourself why you would dream about pigeons, rabid dogs or snakes? Actually, most dream symbols are weird, shocking, confusing and can be frightening at times. Some people believe that these symbols appear for a reason. In my own view, I would say they are the purpose for our personal development, self awareness and self growth. Other people use the power of interpreting dreams as way of making money.
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you realise that most of the symbols that appear in your dreams are not random? We all have a mind selected to give us information about some things that are important to us. They give us a chance for self discovery and self healing. The minute you understand what a specific symbol means, you will see the logic behind why it appeared in the first place. This will give you an insight into the weird and odd understandable world of the dream language; giving you a way of interpreting weird dreams.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dreaming is an astounding fact in human existence. There is no other place you can experience rich emotions and different experiences.
        </p>
        <p>
         You do not have to be psychic for you to have the power of interpreting weird dreams. Anyone can learn how to understand dreams. People even go to have their weird dreams translated to them by a dream translator.
         <br/>
         There are several techniques used in the process of interpreting of weird dreams. First, you need to identify the symbol that was in the dream and write it down. Whether it was a dog, a tree, a mouse, or anything that was in the dream. Then use this symbol to analyse the qualities that the symbol has. Analyse the symbol with the adjectives you would use to describe it. If its a bull, for example, I would signify it with strength and dominance.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thirdly, think about the symbol and the emotions that you get when you think about it. Does it scare you? Does it remind you of a past you would like to forget? Does it remind you of someone? Note all these emotions down. Now link all this part together at the end. Sum up all these findings and find out what the dream actually meant to you. You will find that there is a connection with all your findings at the end. These findings will often be related to your life at that moment in time.
        </p>
        <p>
         This is a better way of interpreting weird dreams as compared to finding symbols in a dream dictionary and trying to match them up with your dream. This is because most symbols in the dictionary are generalised rather than being personalised. The symbol and meaning descriptions are usually out of context of someones dream. Have an open mind when interpreting weird dreams; this will help you interpret the dreams and nightmares you could have accurately.
         <br/>
         Now that you know how to interpret your dream, you should be able to sleep with no worries in the world like him.
        </p>
