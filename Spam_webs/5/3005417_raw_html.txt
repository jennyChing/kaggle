          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="body-text">
          <p>

          </p>
          <p>
           Calgary Photo Booth is no new word for people who love to be clicked. Gone are the days of traditional photography where you needed a person to click photographs running around to capture the picture perfect moments. This is the latest technology has to offer to world of photography. These photo booths are a perfect reason to make an event more eventful and photogenic.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            About
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Photo Booth is a setup or lets us say a Booth which we all see at different setups and exhibitions which is temporary and ready to assemble. It is fitted with a high-end professional camera to click pictures as well as a printer to print high quality pictures. This photo booth offers you many options to get clicked using different customized backgrounds and variety of props to make your photographs come alive.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, now be it a Birthday celebration, corporate party or get together, Graduation, anniversary, baby shower, wedding or a promotion a photo booth can make that event exciting.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            How it works - Its easy!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           First you need to book a
           <a href="http://www.photoeventbooth.ca/">
            <strong>
             <em>
              Photo Event Booth
             </em>
            </strong>
           </a>
           for your planned event. Once you zero down on a photo booth rental supplier tell them about your event, number of guests attending, your specific requirement in terms of backgrounds and props as per the theme of your event. Generally these photo booths supplier provide you with multiple background with themes like, Black and white, Flowers, Chalk board, Silver/Gold Sequin, Wood effect and many more. But if your event has a specific requirement for any particular themed background it can be discussed and made to make your event more meaningful.
          </p>
          <p>
           The same goes with props they normally supply all the general props like, moustaches, lips, shades, colourful paper signs, masks, hats but you still need something to add to it say it prior while discussing the event.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Setup
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Secondly on the scheduled date and event time the photo booth attendant will arrive much earlier to your event time to set up the photo booth for you. All it requires is a little place at your venue being it indoors or outdoors depending upon the availability of the space. One also needs to make sure to provide a electric point to connect the camera and printer and few chairs or a table to keep the props on proper display. In no time your booth would be ready and in shape for the action to begin. On an average these booths can accommodate 5-8 persons easily the rest depends how you manage.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Start Clicking
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Now the action begins and you and guests needs to come either in groups or solo the way you would wish to get your photographs clicked. Either the attendant can operate the camera or you may even choose to put the camera to self click options by following simple steps on the machine. Once it is set, get in front of the camera choosing your favourite background and using your choice of props smile and get clicked. The printer automatically gets into action to start printing the pictures immediately for you. You can come later in few minutes to collect your photographs.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.photoeventbooth.ca/">
            <strong>
             <em>
              Calgary photo booths
             </em>
            </strong>
           </a>
           are becoming popular and favourite choice for photography these days for events as they are simple and good fun.
          </p>
