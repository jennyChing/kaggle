     <p>
      Ive always found it interesting that appearance-wise, Deans type when it comes to women has always been the exact opposite of his mother. If casual sex is one of his coping mechanisms, then it would stand to reason hes not looking to re-visit the memory of his mother during these periods of escape.
     </p>
     <p>
      But cut to Dean being a demon and completely not caring at all and we get this. This beautiful, sweet girl who looks like his mother and doesnt even know him but tries to take care of him when he needs it. And what does he do? Well he attempts to be nice to her at first, but at the very first sign of her calling him on his shit he calls her a skank. This could just be another way demon!Dean is rejecting his old life or maybe just indicative of the fact that as a demon, his mothers memory doesnt weigh all that heavy on him at all.
     </p>
     <p>
      Another thing is that Dean and Ann Marie didnt touch each other even once during the entire episode. They didnt kiss or hold hands, a hand never gently brushed across a cheek their shoulders didnt even touch when they were lying side-by-side in bed. The subtle brush of her fingers against his as she took the shot away is the most contact their bodies ever made on screen. If we take into account the fact that Mary was always so affectionate with Dean, I cant imagine that wasnt a very deliberate choice in how this was all played out.
