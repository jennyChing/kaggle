     <h1 class="title">
      <a href="http://www.spontaneouschick.com/2013/05/21/2013-home-color-trends-2/" rel="bookmark" title="2013 Home Color Trends">
       2013 Home Color Trends
      </a>
     </h1>
     <p class="date">
      <span class="day">
       21
      </span>
      <span class="month">
       May
      </span>
     </p>
     <div class="entry">
      <p>
       Its Joanna Crain here from Arcadian Home blog, a fabulous place to find lots of ideas for decorating with wall art and an array of lighting fixtures from beautiful
       <a href="http://arcadianhome.com/c/mini-pendant-lighting" target="_blank" title="Mini Pendant Lights">
        mini pendant lights
       </a>
       to grand chandeliers. Im so happy to be with you here at Spontaneous Chick with a guest post about 2013 color trends.
      </p>
      <p>
       To inspire you to bring color into your own interior spaces, Ive pulled together eight images of rooms and vignettes that illustrate some of the color trends featured by the experts at Pantone and paint companies, Behr and Benjamin Moore. From soft coastal colors to a deep pink called Daah-ling, theres a yummy hue for everyone. See what you think and I hope you enjoy my take on a few of this years color trends!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/1-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       The walls in this pretty office are painted in Benjamin Moores 2013 color of the year Lemon Sorbet which is a part of the paint companys Coastal color palette and one of its featured color trends of the year.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/2-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       This modern living room blends three of Pantones color picks for 2013Monaco Blue, Tender Shoots and Grayed Jade. These are all new takes on very old colors. The great zig-zag patterned pillows pull the colors in the room together very nicely. I love the unexpectedly tall
       <strong>
        <a href="http://arcadianhome.com/c/contemporary-table-lamps" target="_blank" title="Contemporary Lamps">
         contemporary lamps
        </a>
       </strong>
       and the armless settee in pale green.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/3-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       One of the colors in Behrs Classic Caprice theme is the yummy traditional hue called Daah-ling. For me it evokes lovely memories of summer days on the porch with a bowl of raspberry sherbet.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/4-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Behr calls this palette Sweet Jazz. Its reminiscent of the 1920s. The soft blush pink is the Bees Knees, while the mint green is named Jazzy Jadegreat names, appealing colors.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/5-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       This retro living room uses parts of a combination of colors Pantone calls The Out of the Ordinary Palette. Its said to have been created especially for those who arent afraid to experiment with color. These eye-catching hues have charming names like Rosebud, Golden Rod and Linden Green.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/6-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       This close-up of a charming foyer vignette include three more of Pantones 2013 color choices, Lemon Zest, African Violet and Dusk Blue. Its a lovely blending of hues that are just past pale and not quite bright. In other words, theyre just right.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/7-2013-Color-Trends.jpg" title="2013 Color Trends" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       This sophisticated kitchen is painted in colors from Benjamin Moores Urbanite, one of their 2013 color trends. Moonlight White, Polo Blue, Marblehead Gold and Kendall Charcoal are represented here. The gray cupboard looks great painted inside with the same pretty gold as the ceiling. The
       <a href="http://arcadianhome.com/r/pendant-lighting_glass" target="_blank" title="Glass Pendant Light">
        glass pendant light
       </a>
       hung above the table is just right.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="2013 Color Trends" class="aligncenter wp-image-36523" src="http://arcadianhome.com/blog/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/8-2013-Color-Trends.jpeg" title="2013 Color Trends"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Pulled from Pantones Connoisseur Palette, the colors used in this Mid-Century style living room show how well Champagne Beige, Beechnut (green) and a lovely array of orchids and lilacs work together. This look is one of my favorites. Images
       <a href="http://www.benjaminmoore.com/en-us/for-your-home/coastal" target="_blank">
        1
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.redoitdesign.com/2012/09/13/2013-color-trends-fashion-color-report/" target="_blank">
        2
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://colorfullybehr.com/index.php/2013-color-trend-collection/" target="_blank">
        3
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://colorfullybehr.com/index.php/2013-color-trend-collection/" target="_blank">
        4
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.countryliving.com/homes/decor-ideas/2013-color-trends#slide-2" target="_blank">
        5
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.redoitdesign.com/2012/09/13/2013-color-trends-fashion-color-report/" target="_blank">
        6
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.benjaminmoore.com/en-us/for-your-home/urbanite" target="_blank">
        7
       </a>
       |
       <a href="http://www.casasugar.com/Pantone-Color-Design-Trends-2013-22202458" target="_blank">
        8
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       What do you think of these color trends? Leave us your comments and stop by our blog for more home decor, lighting and interior inspirations!
      </p>
