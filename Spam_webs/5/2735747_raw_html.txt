           <p>
            Did you hear the big news?
            <a href="http://www.searchenginejournal.com/instagram-adds-ability-to-edit-captions-improves-people-search/120031/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.searchenginejournal.com/instagram-adds-ability-to-edit-captions-improves-people-search/120031/', 'Instagram');" target="_blank" title="Instagram Adds Ability To Edit Captions, Improves People Search">
             Instagram
            </a>
            recently unveiled several new features that are making its users all giddy.
           </p>
           <p>
            For those uninitiated, Instagram finally updated its system so that users can edit captions on their posts  previously this was a head scratching limitation. As the
            <a href="http://blog.instagram.com/post/102294612077/discovery-and-caption-editing" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://blog.instagram.com/post/102294612077/discovery-and-caption-editing', 'press release');" target="_blank">
             press release
            </a>
            states, a typo shouldnt get in the way of capturing your favorite moments. The other update was improvements tothe Explore page which now features a Photos tab and a People tab. This will make it easier for you to find the accounts that you think are more interesting or relevant.
           </p>
           <div class="header-unit sej-unit" style="text-align:center;padding:10px;clear:both">
            <!-- SEJ_320x50_In_Post_Mobile -->
            <div id="div-gpt-ad-1397407000033-15">
             <script type="text/javascript">
              googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('div-gpt-ad-1397407000033-15'); });
             </script>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120211" style="width: 622px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/7892198898_f89512a7ce_o.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/7892198898_f89512a7ce_o.jpg', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120211 size-full" height="612" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/7892198898_f89512a7ce_o.jpg" width="612"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Image Source:
              <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/jam_project/7892198898/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.flickr.com/photos/jam_project/7892198898/', 'JAM Project/Flickr');" target="_blank">
               JAM Project/Flickr
              </a>
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Even without these new features, Instagram was already an established powerhouse. For example, it has over
            <a href="http://instagram.com/press/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://instagram.com/press/', '200 million monthly users');" target="_blank">
             200 million monthly users
            </a>
            who share 60 million photos everyday. For marketers, however, the power of images isthe most appealing reason for joining.
           </p>
           <p>
            It isno secret that social media users are crazy about pictures. In 2012,
            <a href="http://www.socialbakers.com/blog/1749-photos-make-up-93-of-the-most-engaging-posts-on-facebook" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.socialbakers.com/blog/1749-photos-make-up-93-of-the-most-engaging-posts-on-facebook', 'Socialbakers');" target="_blank">
             Socialbakers
            </a>
            reported that 85% of the most engaging content on Facebook are photos. One year later, that percentage increased to 93%! In short, if youre not creating visual content, youre missing out on a big opportunity to connect with your audience.
           </p>
           <p>
            If you want to take advantage of the new Instagram features  and the power that visual content has on social media  for your business needs, here are 10 recommended platforms and apps to give your Instagram campaign a major boost.
           </p>
           <h2>
            1. Collecto (formerly Followgram)
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120198" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_44_33-Collecto-Instagram-Web-Viewer.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_44_33-Collecto-Instagram-Web-Viewer.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120198 size-large" height="486" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_44_33-Collecto-Instagram-Web-Viewer-760x486.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot of
              <a href="http://collec.to/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://collec.to/', ' Collecto');" target="_blank">
               Collecto
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            If youre an Instagram power user, then youve most likely come across Followgram in the past because its just awesome. This amazing platform gives you the ability to boost your presence on Instagram by providing stats on your account and a dashboard with the most recent images from people/brands youre following. This gives you the chance to engage with influencers and your audience from Collectos dashboard
           </p>
           <p>
            What really makes
            <a href="http://collec.to/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://collec.to/', 'Collecto');" target="_blank">
             Collecto
            </a>
            amazing, however, is how easy customization.For example, you can create and collect custom photos albums, receive a vanity URL to display your albums, and even take personality quizzes.
           </p>
           <p>
            Collecto offers three separate plans. Theres a free plan  which offers the RSS feed, stats, and up to five custom albums  as well as Pro (around $12 a year) and Brand ($210 a year) plans if you want to take advantage of the additional features.
           </p>
           <h2>
            2. IconoSquare (formerly Statigram)
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120199" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_46_12-Iconosquare-All-Instagram-online.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_46_12-Iconosquare-All-Instagram-online.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120199 size-large" height="443" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_46_12-Iconosquare-All-Instagram-online-760x443.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <a href="http://iconosquare.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://iconosquare.com/', 'Screenshot of IconoSquare 11/13/14');" target="_blank">
              Screenshot of
              <em>
               IconoSquare
              </em>
              11/13/14
             </a>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Another popular Instagram platform that has changed names recently is Statigram. Despite the name change to
            <a href="http://iconosquare.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://iconosquare.com/', 'IconoSquare');" target="_blank">
             IconoSquare
            </a>
            , this is still a top-notch platform for managing and improving your Instagramaccount. This is achieved by offering tons of stats, including the numberof followers youve gained or lost within a week, growth history of comments/likes, best time to share images, and what your engagement has been like over the last month.
           </p>
           <p>
            Other features include a viewer for easy navigation through images/videos, the ability to run a contest, and management of conversations  for example, you can direct messages through Twitter. Perhaps the best feature is how convenient it is to promote your content onto other social media accounts. You can install a photo widget toallow your Facebook followers the chance to see your content even if they arent on Instagram.
           </p>
           <p>
            Just sign-in using your Instagram account information and youre ready to use the beneficial features of IconoSquare.
           </p>
           <h2>
            3. Ink 361
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120200" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_48_00-Instagram-Web-Viewer-INK361-View_Print-photos-or-videos-online.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_48_00-Instagram-Web-Viewer-INK361-View_Print-photos-or-videos-online.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120200 size-large" height="375" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_48_00-Instagram-Web-Viewer-INK361-View_Print-photos-or-videos-online-760x375.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot of
              <a href="http://ink361.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://ink361.com/', ' Ink361');" target="_blank">
               Ink361
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a href="http://ink361.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://ink361.com/', 'Ink 361');" target="_blank">
             Ink 361
            </a>
            is another incredible platform that you should be using for your Instagram account. Like Connecto and IconoSquare, Ink 361 can be used to organize your images into albums  which can also be used to share with non-Instagram users  and you can even set-up alerts for your followers. Theres also stats that you can analyze, a Facebook page app, a Facebook timeline cover photo generator, and Twitter background generator that use your Instagram images.
           </p>
           <p>
            However, the most promising feature is the ability to make meaningful connections. You can do this by exploring the profiles of the people who you are following. After logging into your profile, you can view the profile description, their latest five images, and their follower stats. This is a great way to discover influencers and users that can help you network on Instagram.
           </p>
           <h2>
            4. Search Instagram
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120201" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_49_41-Searchin-Stagram.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_49_41-Searchin-Stagram.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120201 size-large" height="364" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_49_41-Searchin-Stagram-760x364.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://searchinstagram.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://searchinstagram.com/', 'Search Instagram');" target="_blank">
               Search Instagram
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Search tools are kind of important when a business wants to connect with its audience or attract new followers. Thats where there are a wide range of tools, like Google Alerts or Social Mention  that clue us in on trending on social media. But, what about discovering whats trending on Instagram?
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://searchinstagram.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://searchinstagram.com/', 'Search Instagram');" target="_blank">
             Search Instagram
            </a>
            gives you the ability to search for images related to your industry by either hashtag or location. Just type in the keyword and see what kind of content appears based on your search query. You can also hover over the selected images and see how popular they are by viewingthe amount of likes it has received.
           </p>
           <h2>
            5. TakeOff
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120202" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_51_19-Right-friends-everywhere-_-Find-and-unfollow-twitter-users-who-unfollowed-me.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_51_19-Right-friends-everywhere-_-Find-and-unfollow-twitter-users-who-unfollowed-me.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120202 size-large" height="231" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_51_19-Right-friends-everywhere-_-Find-and-unfollow-twitter-users-who-unfollowed-me-760x231.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://www.justunfollow.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.justunfollow.com/', 'JustUnFollow');" target="_blank">
               JustUnFollow
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Unlike other social media platforms, its been challenging to schedule Instagram posts in advance  apparently this has something to do with not being allowed to upload via the
            <a href="http://instagram.com/developer/endpoints/media/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://instagram.com/developer/endpoints/media/', 'API');" target="_blank">
             API
            </a>
            . In other words, you cant use scheduling tools like HootSuite, Buffer, or IFTTT to automatically post content onto Instagram. While there have been some tools that have assisted with this,
            <a href="https://postso.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://postso.com/', 'Post So');" target="_blank">
             Post So
            </a>
            was a popular choice but you have to pay for it,
            <a href="http://www.justunfollow.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.justunfollow.com/', 'TakeOff');" target="_blank">
             TakeOff
            </a>
            is a free application that can help solve this problem.
           </p>
           <p>
            TakeOff comes from the developers of JustUnfollow  a very cool management tool that notifies you of your followers and non-followers. You can use TakeOff to schedule Instagram posts in advance by selecting the times when your followers are most active. So, if your followers are sharing your images at 9am, 12pm and 6pm, then you can schedule posts for those times. TakeOff will also suggest relevant keywords for your caption based on the image description.
           </p>
           <p>
            TakeOff may not have completely solved this problem  it wont automatically upload the images, instead you receive notifications, and its still in beta  this is a promising app that should be able to help boost your Instagram account.
           </p>
           <h2>
            6. Like2Buy/Inselly
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120203" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_53_03-Like2Buy-Make-Your-Instagram-Account-Shoppable.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_53_03-Like2Buy-Make-Your-Instagram-Account-Shoppable.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120203 size-large" height="385" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_53_03-Like2Buy-Make-Your-Instagram-Account-Shoppable-760x385.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://www.curalate.com/solutions/like2buy/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.curalate.com/solutions/like2buy/', 'Like2Buy');" target="_blank">
               Like2Buy
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Have you ever wanted to monetize your Instagram images? Now you can by using
            <a href="http://www.curalate.com/solutions/like2buy/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.curalate.com/solutions/like2buy/', 'Like2Buy');" target="_blank">
             Like2Buy
            </a>
            from marketing and analytics firm Curalate. Essentially, you display your products through Instagram-inspired images, customers click on the products they like and are taken to item page on the retailers website. Nordstrom and Target have already jumped on board, which probably means that Like2Buy may be out of the range for a lot of small businesses.
           </p>
           <p>
            If thats the case, you may want to like into other platforms that can help convert your Instagram images into profits. For example, theres
            <a href="http://inselly.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://inselly.com/', 'Inselly');" target="_blank">
             Inselly
            </a>
            . All you have to do is sign into your Instagram account, take pictures of your products, add relevant keywords  along with the #inselly tag, and sell your products . Best of all? Inselly doesnt take any commission and you get paid via PayPal  who will unfortunately take a cut  which makes this tool convenient and easy-to-setup.
           </p>
           <h2>
            7. Websta (formerly Webstagram)
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120206" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_56_04-Websta-the-best-Instagram-web-viewer-online-Webstagram.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_56_04-Websta-the-best-Instagram-web-viewer-online-Webstagram.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120206 size-large" height="443" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_56_04-Websta-the-best-Instagram-web-viewer-online-Webstagram-760x443.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://websta.me/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://websta.me/', 'Websta');" target="_blank">
               Websta
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a href="http://websta.me/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://websta.me/', 'Websta');" target="_blank">
             Websta
            </a>
            is another powerful platform that can improve your Instagram account. While it provides the usual suspects of features like stats, web viewer and ability to create albums, it also features a number of other unique options, such as search tools, a Follow Me button, and Repost This.
           </p>
           <p>
            On top of these useful features, it the community that Websta has to offer. Theres a blog where Instagram users can interact and seek advice, a board to organize posts into groups and the ability to send direct messages to Instagram users.
           </p>
           <h2>
            8. Twtrland
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120212" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-16_15_45-Twtrland-Social-Media-Analytics.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-16_15_45-Twtrland-Social-Media-Analytics.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120212 size-large" height="446" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-16_15_45-Twtrland-Social-Media-Analytics-760x446.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://twtrland.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://twtrland.com/', ' Twtrland');" target="_blank">
               Twtrland
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Dont be confused if you thought that
            <a href="http://twtrland.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://twtrland.com/', 'Twtrland');" target="_blank">
             Twtrland
            </a>
            was used only for Twitter. Originally, it was. Today, however, this platform can also be used for Facebook and Instagram to spice up your online presence. This is done by entering a simple search of a name, location, or skill. The results will give you a profile of the company/individual, how influential they are, and what their top content is.
           </p>
           <p>
            You can use this information to discover important information  such as audience demographics  in order to effectively target your fanbase. You can also use this tool analyze your competitors. For example, you can see what kind of content they are posting and who they interact with. Its a fairly simple way to discover and influencers in your niche.
           </p>
           <h2>
            9. Latergram.me
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120208" style="width: 770px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_59_07-Latergram-Schedule-your-Instagram-Posts.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_59_07-Latergram-Schedule-your-Instagram-Posts.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120208 size-large" height="406" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-15_59_07-Latergram-Schedule-your-Instagram-Posts-760x406.png" width="760"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://www.latergram.me/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.latergram.me/', 'Latergram');" target="_blank">
               Latergram
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            If you werent impressed with the scheduling capabilities of TakeOff, then consider looking into
            <a href="http://latergram.me/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://latergram.me/', 'Latergram.me');" target="_blank">
             Latergram.me
            </a>
             no this isnt related to
            <a href="http://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=Latergram" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=Latergram', '#latergram');" target="_blank">
             #latergram
            </a>
             when post an image to Instagram at a later date. This app has an impressive interface containing a calendar where you can easilydrag over the posts that you wish to schedule from your desktop or mobile device. You can even schedule Facebook, Twitter, and text messages whenever you want to push a piece of content.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Latergram app is available for free  all you have to do is sign-in with your Instagram account  but theres also a premium version that allows up to two members, the scheduling of 250 posts per month and the ability to search Instagram if you need to regram a post.
           </p>
           <h2>
            10. Repost
           </h2>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_120209" style="width: 756px">
            <a href="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-16_00_41-PicFlow-InstaQuote-Repost-iPhone-Apps-for-Instagram-Red-Cactus.png" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-16_00_41-PicFlow-InstaQuote-Repost-iPhone-Apps-for-Instagram-Red-Cactus.png', '');">
             <img alt="10 Tools To Give Your Instagram Campaign a Boost" class="wp-image-120209 size-full" height="438" src="http://cdn.searchenginejournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-13-16_00_41-PicFlow-InstaQuote-Repost-iPhone-Apps-for-Instagram-Red-Cactus.png" width="746"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             <em>
              Screenshot
              <a href="http://www.redcact.us/#repost" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.redcact.us/#repost', 'Repost for Instagram');" target="_blank">
               Repost for Instagram
              </a>
              11/13/14
             </em>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Speaking of regramming, you should make use of this pretty neat app  which is available on both iTunes and Google Play. You can repost your favorite images and videos on Instagram while still giving attribution to the original Instagrammer. In fact, thats pretty much it. Find an image and repost it. Its kind of like retweeting on Twitter.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.redcact.us/#repost" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.redcact.us/#repost', 'Repost');" target="_blank">
             Repost
            </a>
            also gives you the ability to select the filter of your liking, as well as where to position the attribution button.
           </p>
           <p>
            This is a great way to like images, discover new Instagramers and the ability to build a community by engaging others users.
           </p>
