       <p style="text-align: justify">
        Mulching is a really great way to protect your garden, suppress weed and conserve the moisture of soil. But when you buy from just anywhere and the mulch is not of quality which means it is not prepared properly. If you are not careful when choosing mulch then instead of making your soil fertile and protecting plants it can actually kill your plants and there will be nothing much for you do and save the situation once you have applied the much and it has starting decomposing. For quality and
        <span style="color: #ff0000">

          <strong>
           cheap mulch Perth
          </strong>
         </a>
        </span>
        contact us at Oak fire. Here are few ways to identify toxic mulch
       </p>
       <p>
        Identifying toxic mulch:
       </p>
       <p>
        1. Smell: it is probably the best way to identify your mulchs quality. Smell before applying and if the mulch smells pungent like silage, rotten eggs or vinegar then do not spread it around because the high chances with such kind of smelly mulch is that it is toxic. Your mulch should smell like fertile garden manure or freshly cut wood. For fresh
        <span style="color: #ff0000">
         mulch delivery Perth
        </span>
        services call Oak Fire services.
       </p>
       <p>
        2. Ask: does not sound such a bright idea but it is one. You can ask the supplier about the quality and if it does not come out as promised you know you have someone to hold responsible. But before getting there you should always remember to only buy from genuine providers who provide quality mulch. For quality but cheap mulch Perth consider buying from Oak Fire.
       </p>
       <p>
        A little note to everybody, if you are going to sue wood chips or pine barks as mulch then make sure you get them from the provider who brings them to you from re-growth areas because you want to environment friendly as well.
