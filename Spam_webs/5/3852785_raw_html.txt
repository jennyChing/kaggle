     <h1>
      The Ultimate Guide to Captive Insurance
     </h1>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      The modern private sector has changed drastically in the last few decades, prompting businesses to seek innovative, alternative approaches to management in order to boost performance and compete in increasingly saturated markets. While risk management and corporate insurance coverage may seem inflexible in the face of change, even small business owners are now following in the footsteps of their larger counterparts and turning to captive insurance in order to overcome the high costs of health care and the increased complexity that has come with widespread reforms.
     </p>
     <p>
      While captive insurance was once the domain of Fortune 500 companies alone, its now spreading across industries and size sectors as a viable option for more targeted and progressive approaches to risk financing. With increased control and stronger spend efficiency, as well as the potential to create a new source of revenues, captive insurance is rapidly becoming a more popular option for corporate coverage. Our guide provides answers to your most frequently asked questions about captive insurance and the alternative risk financing arena.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/what-is-captive-insurance/" id="Q1" target="_blank">
       1. What is captive insurance?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="i-captive-insurance" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4325" height="120" src="http://www.owendunn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/i-captive-insurance.png" width="120"/>
      The captive insurance industry has been around for centuries, but has certainly become a bit more complex and diversified more recently. Having a basic understanding of captives is critical foryour overall success, and it doesnt take long to attain.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/what-is-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/common-types-captive-insurance/" id="Q2" target="_blank">
       2. What are the most common types of captives?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Captives come in a variety of shapes and sizes, while they are often dictated by specific internal corporate needs and pressure points. Choosing the right type is important to align the investment with unique objectives, and the variation can be large.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/common-types-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/uses-captive-insurance/" id="Q3" target="_blank">
       3. Who uses captive insurance?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Captive insurance is most common among Fortune 500 companies, as well as larger enterprises in general. However, smaller and mid-market businesses from a range of industries are now using the approach to tackle their insurance needs.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/uses-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/tax-considerations-involved-captives/" id="Q4" target="_blank">
       4.What tax considerations are involved in captive insurance?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Many companies will eventually take the leap into captive insurance because of the fairer tax climates involved when compared to traditional coverage. However, business leaders must understand the tax implications and how they should be managed.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/tax-considerations-involved-captives/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-programs-formed/" id="Q5" target="_blank">
       5. How are captive insurance programs formed?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="i-captive-formed" class="alignright size-full wp-image-4328" height="120" src="http://www.owendunn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/i-captive-formed.png" style="margin-bottom: 20px;" width="120"/>
      It might seem as though captives are created in a highly mystical and unconscious fashion, but this is simply not the case. Understanding how captive insurance programs are formed and what goes into the front end can help guide long-term management.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-programs-formed/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/companies-use-captive-insurance/" id="Q6" target="_blank">
       6. Why do companies use captive insurance?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Risk management has long been a simple and consistent process, but remember Thoreaus warning that a foolish consistency is the hobgoblin of little minds. Companies have a lot of reasons for using captive insurance, and there are several common threads.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/companies-use-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/domicile/" id="Q7" target="_blank">
       7. What is a domicile?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Domicile selection is a critical aspect of captive insurance adoption, and the climate for these host states and areas is becoming more preferable. Many states continue to target domicile-related rules and guidelines to attract companies to their borders.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/domicile/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>

     <p>
     </p>

      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/tax-benefits-captive-insurance-compared-traditional-coverage/" id="Q8" target="_blank">
       8.What are the tax benefits or disadvantages of captive insurance compared to traditional coverage?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="i-tax" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4340" height="120" src="http://www.owendunn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/i-tax.png" width="120"/>
      Aside from the managerial differences between captive and traditional insurance, there are a few considerations when tax season comes around. Understanding these differences is a critical aspect of oversight and accounting.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/tax-benefits-captive-insurance-compared-traditional-coverage/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>

     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/controls-claims-administration-premium-rates-captive/" id="Q9" target="_blank">
       9. Who controls claims administration and premium rates in a captive?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      In short, many companies will adopt captive insurance to gain control of administrative and premium components of corporate coverage, rather than leaving it in the hands of a traditional provider. There is more to understand in terms of control, though.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/controls-claims-administration-premium-rates-captive/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>

     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/can-get-help-search-captive-insurance-program/" id="Q10" target="_blank">
       10. Where can I get help in my search for a captive insurance program?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="i-search" class="alignright size-full wp-image-4333" height="120" src="http://www.owendunn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/i-search.png" width="120"/>
      The increased demand for captive insurance has led to a variety of new resources sprouting up for interested business owners. Some come from government agencies, others from experts and insurance brokers.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/can-get-help-search-captive-insurance-program/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>

     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/industries-use-captive-insurance/" id="Q11" target="_blank">
       11. Which industries use captive insurance the most?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Captive insurance is simply better-suited to certain industries than others, especially given the varying need for customized and comprehensive coverage. Knowing if your industry is one of these key areas for captive success can help guide purchasing decisions.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/industries-use-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/reinsurance-market-involve-captive-insurance/" id="Q12" target="_blank">
       12. What is the reinsurance market and how does it involve captive insurance?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Reinsurance and self-insurance markets are essentially the opposite of traditional insurance industries. Captives are generally classified within these markets, and understanding how it all works can guide management and procurement procedures.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/reinsurance-market-involve-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>

     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/never-heard-captive-insurance/" id="Q13" target="_blank">
       13. Why have I never heard of captive insurance?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="i-research" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4331" height="120" src="http://www.owendunn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/i-research.png" width="120"/>
      Captive insurance is not necessarily new, but many individuals are just now beginning to hear about it in the modern market. Whats more, the rate of captives and participants has spiked more recently, and we will guide you through the evolution.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/never-heard-captive-insurance/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-used-short-long-term-needs/" id="Q14" target="_blank">
       14. Is captive insurance used for short or long-term needs?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      In a word, both. Captive insurance can be used for relatively short-term and one-off purposes, but best practice is often believed to demand long-term utilization, management arrangements and approaches to adoption.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-used-short-long-term-needs/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>
     <hr/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-program-terminated/" id="Q15" target="_blank">
       15. How is a captive insurance program terminated?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Sometimes business owners will be afraid to take a new approach to insurance coverage when they believe that it is immutable after the initial adoption. Captive programs can, and sometimes are, terminated.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-program-terminated/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>

     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-meet-risk-distribution-requirements-law/" id="Q16" target="_blank">
       16. How do captives meet the risk distribution requirements of insurance regulations?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="i-certificate" class="alignright size-full wp-image-4335" height="120" src="http://www.owendunn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/i-certificate.png" width="120"/>
      One of the key aspects of setting up and maintaining a legal and legitimate captive insurance program ties back to the requirements of risk distribution. These are in fact the most important insurance regulations to understand when approaching captives.
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/captive-insurance-meet-risk-distribution-requirements-law/" target="_blank">
       Read More
      </a>
     </p>

     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.owendunn.com/blog/risks-involved-captive-insurance/" id="Q17" target="_blank">
