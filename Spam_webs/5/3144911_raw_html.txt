         <h1 class="span6">
          Whoa, Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes Are Divorcing
         </h1>
         <div class="byline-container top">
          <div class="byline">
           By
           <cite class="vcard author">
            <a href="/contributors/anna-moeslein">
             Anna Moeslein
            </a>
           </cite>
          </div>
          <div class="date-time-stamp">
           June 29, 2012
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="social-container">
          <div class="sharrre-tools">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="article-text">
         <p>
          A shocking, breaking-news announcement: After five years of marriage,
          <a href="http://www.glamour.com/about/tom-cruise">
           Tom Cruise
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.glamour.com/about/katie-holmes">
           Katie Holmes
          </a>
          are getting divorced, according to
          <em>
           <a href="http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20608003,00.html">
            People
           </a>
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="0629_tom-cruise-katie-holmes_ob.jpg" class="mt-image-none" height="298" src="http://blog.glamour.com/entertainment/blogs/obsessed/2012/06/29/0629_tom-cruise-katie-holmes_ob.jpg" style="" width="448"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          No word yet exactly
          <em>
           why
          </em>
          , but her attorney Jonathan Wolfe says, "This is a personal and private matter for Katie and her family. Katie's primary concern remains, as it always has been, her daughter's best interest."
         </p>
         <p>
          Tom's rep says that Katie was the one who filed for divorce. "Tom is deeply saddened and is concentrating on his three children," the rep said in a statement.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Um... WHAT!?! Are you shocked by this announcement? I honestly didn't see this coming.
          </strong>
         </p>
