           <h2>
            <a class="taggedlink entry-title" href="http://www.metal-rules.com/metalnews/2012/08/30/steve-harris-confirms-maiden-england-dvd-in-2013/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to STEVE HARRIS CONFIRMS MAIDEN ENGLAND DVD IN 2013">
             STEVE HARRIS CONFIRMS MAIDEN ENGLAND DVD IN 2013
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="date">
            <abbr class="published updated" title="2012-08-30T20:32:41Z">
             August 30th, 2012
            </abbr>
           </div>
           <span class="vcard author">
            by
            <span class="fn">
             EvilG
            </span>
           </span>
           <div class="description entry-content">
            <div dir="ltr" id=":5g">
             <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_22867" style="width: 177px">
              <a href="http://www.metal-rules.com/metalnews/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/maidenengland-vhs.jpg" rel="lightbox[22810]">
               <img alt="Maiden England (VHS Cover)" class="size-full wp-image-22867" height="302" src="http://www.metal-rules.com/metalnews/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/maidenengland-vhs.jpg" title="Maiden England (VHS Cover)" width="167"/>
              </a>
              <p class="wp-caption-text">
               Maiden England (VHS Cover)
              </p>
             </div>
             <p>
              In an interview with German magazine Rock Hard in August 2012, bassist Steve Harris confirmed that Maiden England will be re-released in 2013, with the concert footage expanded to comprise the shows encores which were not included in the original VHS. In 2012, Iron Maiden undertook their Maiden England World Tour, which is largely based around the original video in setlist and stage design.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Maiden England setlist:
              </strong>
              <br/>
              Moonchild
              <br/>
              Can I Play With Madness
              <br/>
              The Prisoner
              <br/>
              2 Minutes To Midnight
              <br/>
              Afraid To Shoot Strangers
              <br/>
              The Trooper
              <br/>
              The Number Of The Beast
              <br/>
              Phantom Of The Opera
              <br/>
              Run To The Hills
              <br/>
              Wasted Years
              <br/>
              Seventh Son Of A Seventh Son
              <br/>
              The Clairvoyant
              <br/>
              Fear Of The Dark
              <br/>
              Iron Maiden
              <br/>
              Aces High
              <br/>
              The Evil That Men Do
              <br/>
              Running Free
             </p>
