        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: medium;">
          Before we announce the winners of the
          <a href="http://theletteredcottage.net/mary-kay-andrews-summer-rental-giveaway" target="_blank">
           <em>

           </em>
          </a>

         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="//i268.photobucket.com/albums/jj29/LaylaPalmer/Mary%20Kay%20Andrews/Breeze_Inn.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: medium;">
          <span>
           Our visit took place in the Spring of 2010, and it was an absolutely magical place to call home for a few days.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: medium;">
          <span>
           Through the front door, we were greeted by lots of sweet, striped seating and a big ol wall full of books
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="//i268.photobucket.com/albums/jj29/LaylaPalmer/Mary%20Kay%20Andrews/Mary_Kay_Andrews_Library.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: small;">
          <span>
           (Theres a few of Mary Kays colorful creations down there on the bottom shelf!)
          </span>
         </span>
