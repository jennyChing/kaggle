          <a href="http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20065841,00.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20065841,00.html', 'Susan');">
           Susan
          </a>
          <a href="http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20065841,00.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20065841,00.html', ' Brownmiller');">
           Brownmiller
          </a>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         So Susan Brownmiller is not a feminist. Whod a thunk it?
        </p>
        <p>
         So what of Ms Rawlings contention that feminists are silencing people, especially women? Lets (re) educate Ms. Rawlings critics.
        </p>
        <p>
         Maybe they overlooked that, at Ryerson University, Canadian Federation for Equality and
         <a href="http://theeyeopener.com/2013/03/new-rsu-policy-challenges-new-mens-issues-group/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://theeyeopener.com/2013/03/new-rsu-policy-challenges-new-mens-issues-group/', 'similar groups were banned by the Student Union');">
          similar groups were banned by the Student Union
         </a>
         from organizing events on campus, under the thin pretext of their misogyny. Under this rule,
         <a href="http://www.avoiceformen.com/feminism/feminist-censorship/on-cafe-free-speech-and-the-ryerson-university-extortion-racket/">
          they attempted to extort security fees for the lecture of Karen Straughan
         </a>
         in a scheme that would make
         <a href="https://michaelfranzese.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://michaelfranzese.com/', 'Michael Franzese ');">
          Michael Franzese
         </a>
         blush. Karen was only allowed to speak when
         <a href="http://www.ryersonian.ca/why-ryerson-paid-for-security-at-the-mens-rights-event/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ryersonian.ca/why-ryerson-paid-for-security-at-the-mens-rights-event/', 'the President, Sheldon Levy, intervened.');">
          the President, Sheldon Levy, intervened.
         </a>
         The catalyst for the censorship rule?
         <a href="http://www.macleans.ca/education/uniandcollege/ryerson-students-union-censors-mens-issues-group/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.macleans.ca/education/uniandcollege/ryerson-students-union-censors-mens-issues-group/', 'Two WOMEN sought to organize a mens issues group on campus');">
          Two WOMEN sought to organize a mens issues group on campus
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Maybe theyve forgotten that in Toronto, attendees of
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iARHCxAMAO0" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iARHCxAMAO0', 'Dr. Warren Farrells lecture were violently obstructed');">
          Dr. Warren Farrells lecture were violently obstructed
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/iARHCxAMAO0?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Or maybe they have no idea that the woman who started the womens shelter movement was
         <a href="//www.youtube.com/watch?v=_lxoStFBrjo">
          literally run out of her country by feminist death threats
         </a>
         for saying women were just as violent in relationships as men.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/_lxoStFBrjo?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Gender feminism is hegemonic among MMA media like most sports media, so any use of the word with a whiff of detectable impudence is met with the same accommodation as a roach crawling across your dinner plate, as the Rawlings matter has demonstrated. Indeed, using the term feminist jurisprudence gets you a warning on MMAFighting.com.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.avoiceformen.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2015/01/mma_fighting.jpg">
          <img alt="mma_fighting" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102988" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.avoiceformen.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2015/01/mma_fighting.jpg?resize=300%2C169"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Any criticism of Ronda Rousey brings white knights out like cicadas in mating season.Engage in reasoned debate on sensitive subjects,
         <a href="http://www.bloodyelbow.com/2014/9/12/6140949/ufc-ronda-rousey-on-ray-rice-assault-incident-it-would-have-been-much-different-mma-news" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bloodyelbow.com/2014/9/12/6140949/ufc-ronda-rousey-on-ray-rice-assault-incident-it-would-have-been-much-different-mma-news', 'and get banned');">
          and get banned
         </a>
         (see comments of kosezky). These same people were (rightfully) indignant when a fighter beat a woman half to death. However, in light of their indifference after War Machine got light or no sentences and continued to fight after beating the piss out of
         <a href="http://www.cagepotato.com/war-machines-porn-career-jeopardy-after-allegedly-assaulting-girlfriend-agent-and-several-terrified/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cagepotato.com/war-machines-porn-career-jeopardy-after-allegedly-assaulting-girlfriend-agent-and-several-terrified/', 'man');">
          man
         </a>
         after
         <a href="http://legacy.utsandiego.com/news/metro/20071213-1328-bn13fight.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://legacy.utsandiego.com/news/metro/20071213-1328-bn13fight.html', 'man');">
          man
         </a>
         after man, (CagePotato.com being the exception), their hands are not exactly clean.There are countervailing voices who mock white knights and
         <a href="http://www.examiner.com/article/feminist-ideologies-to-blame-for-invicta-fc-7-s-lack-of-buzz" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.examiner.com/article/feminist-ideologies-to-blame-for-invicta-fc-7-s-lack-of-buzz', ' critique the feminist undercurrent');">
          critique the feminist undercurrent
         </a>
         , but they are few and far between.
        </p>
        <p>
         Rawlings has, in microcosm, exposed threadbare how the ideology not only chases its own tail, but often devours it. Womens MMAhas skyrocketed in popularity, yet when NOW seeks to ban MMA, they are denying women as well as men employment opportunities. That they had no reservations in heaping invective and ridicule upon Rawlings, whilst making noattempt to educate NOW, the feminist hegemon, about their misguided obstructionism and smearing of the sport they love, is telling, an object lesson in Voltaires old saw about how to knowwho rules you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bec Rawlings had the courage to speak truth to this hegemony, accept the slings and arrows, and continues to own her words, not backing down, a luxury afforded few men. For truly being a strong woman, she is to be saluted. And to her critics, we kindly invite them to. . .well, Bec said it best.
        </p>
