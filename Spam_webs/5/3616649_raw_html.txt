          <img alt="Buffalo Chicken Pasta Bake" class="aligncenter wp-image-5122 size-full" height="867" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/buffalo-chicken-pasta-bake-delicous-chicken-recipes.jpg" title="Buffalo Chicken Pasta Bake #shop " width="650"/>

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Delicious Chicken Recipes: Buffalo Chicken Pasta Bake
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #555555;">
          Ourhouse is full of hungry teens and college age kiddos! Not only ourown kids but their significant others, video game playing friends and even a few strays (LOL) that just wonder in. I bet you can imagine, they make a lot of noise and eat a lot of food! It makes me happy to have ourteen and 20 something kids at home, so I am always looking for an array of
          <a href="http://drvsusa.com/tyson/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           delicious chicken recipes
          </a>
          that satisfy their appetites and keep them coming back for more.
         </span>
         Last week our daughter graduated from college and we wanted to have a small celebration that evening. I am proud to say she was the first one of ourchildren to graduate with a college degree. Thats definitely something worth celebrating!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/collage-easy-grilled-chicken.jpg">
          <img alt="delicious-chicken-recipes" class="aligncenter wp-image-5129 size-full" height="650" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/collage-easy-grilled-chicken.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Her and I headed out to Walmart to pick up the ingredients to one of her favorite dishes; Buffalo Chicken Pasta Bake. Mom also treated her toa few new movies, some clothes and a few other goodies. It isnt often you can get your 20 something daughter to spend extra time with Mom, so shopping and food usually do the trick.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/easy-grilled-chicken.jpg">
          <img alt="delicious-chicken-reicpes" class="aligncenter wp-image-5127 size-full" height="957" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/easy-grilled-chicken.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="a%20href=%22http://drvsusa.com/tyson/%22%20target=%22_blank%22%20rel=%22nofollow%22">
          <img alt="delicious-chicken-recipes" class="alignnone wp-image-5132 size-full" height="569" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/tyson-deal.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Speaking of movies, I am super excited that the movie, BLENDED is coming out in a few short days. Blended stars twoof my favorite Hollywood stars, Adam Sandler and Drew Barrymore. Werent they awesome in the movie 50 First Dates?? Right now when you buy 2 specially marked package of Grilled &amp;Ready Tyson Chicken you can get 2 FREE tickets (while supplies last) to the Blended Movie debuting in theaters on May 23rd. #TysonMovieTicket.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/collage-1.jpg">
          <img alt="collage 1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5145" height="1001" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/collage-1.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Head over to Walmart and look for the specially marked packages of Tyson Grilled &amp; Ready and you will be ready for meals this week. Its super easy to use the Tyson Grilled &amp; Ready Chicken to makeyour family some
         <a href="http://drvsusa.com/tyson/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          deliciouschicken recipes
         </a>
         . The Southwest Chicken Strips are great for
         <a href="http://drvsusa.com/tyson/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          grilled chicken fajitas
         </a>
         and salads.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chicken-recipes-for-two.jpg">
          <img alt="delicious-chicken-recipes" class="aligncenter wp-image-5133 size-full" height="647" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chicken-recipes-for-two.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         We have our own little blended family. When your kids are teens and 20 somethings your family structure can change quickly. Our oldest son, now has a fiance, which has been a joy to our family. Ourcollege graduate daughter has had the same boyfriend for 4 years and I dont see that changing anytime soon. Adding your childrens signifigant others to your family pictures, is a perfect blend of love and family. After a few family pictures, it was time to get this celebration started with something to eat.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/buffalo-chicken.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="delicious-chicken-recipes" class="alignnone wp-image-5134 size-full" height="488" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/buffalo-chicken.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         My daughter started making this dish at college. I added a few of my own special twists and its the new favorite of this blended family. I am enjoying this season of being able to gather all of our kids around the family table. Itwill truly be a bittersweet day when I have to start preparing
         <a href="http://drvsusa.com/tyson/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          chicken recipes for two
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Does your family have any favorite
          <a href="http://drvsusa.com/tyson/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           delicious chicken recipes
          </a>
          ?
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSRatings" itemprop="aggregateRating" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/AggregateRating">
          <div class="ERSRatingOuter">
           <div class="ERSRatingInner" style="width: 100%">

             <span class="average" itemprop="ratingValue">

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Buffalo Chicken Pasta Bake
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://just2sisters.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/buffalo-chicken-pasta-bake-delicous-chicken-recipes-224x300.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://just2sisters.com/easyrecipe-print/5120-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          We have made this recipe with several different kinds of pasta. Egg noodles seem to work best, keeping the casserole creamy and delicious.
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            12 oz Egg Noodles
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3- 8 oz blocks Low Fat Cream Cheese
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            5 oz Frank's Red Hot Sauce
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             Cup Ranch or Blue Cheese Dressing (we prefer ranch)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 Cups Tyson Grilled &amp; Ready Chicken Chunks
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat oven to 350 degrees
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Prepare Egg Noodles as directed on package.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Meanwhile, heat cream cheese and red hot sauce over low until melted and creamy. (careful it doesn't stick).
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Once melted, stir in dressing and chicken,
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir until well combined and heated through.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix in cooked noodles.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spoon all ingredients into a 9 x 12 casserole dish sprayed with non-stick spray.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cover noodle mixture with grated cheese.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake for 15 - 20 minutes, until bubbly and hot.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Enjoy!
           </li>
          </ol>
