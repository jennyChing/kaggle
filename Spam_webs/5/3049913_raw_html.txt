           <h1>
            Burglar Alarms: A simple guide to help you make the right choice
           </h1>
           <p class="p-head where-start" id="where-start">
            Where do I start?
           </p>
           <div class="row">
            <div class="span4 notice">
            </div>
            <div class="span5 notice">
             <span class="blue">
              If you're thinking
             </span>
             of protecting your home first time or upgrading
                    your existing home alarm system, you might be feeling daunted by number of alarm devices and
                    monitoring services available.
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="row">
            <div class="delimiter">
            </div>
            <div class="questions-container">
             <p class="questions">
              <img height="19" src="/img/security-alarms/arr_r.png" width="19"/>

             </p>
             <p class="questions">
              <img height="19" src="/img/security-alarms/arr_r.png" width="19"/>

             <p class="questions">
              <img height="19" src="/img/security-alarms/arr_r.png" width="19"/>

             <h2>
              Help is at hand!
             </h2>
             <p class="first-p">
              We're here to explain the options, technologies and benefits.
             </p>
             <p>
              We'll guide you through the three categories of alarm system
             </p>
             <ul class="options row">
              <li class="span3">
               <img height="41" src="/img/security-alarms/img_opt_1.png" width="41"/>
               <a href="#audible-systems" title="Audible-only alarm systems">
                Audible-only
               </a>
              </li>
              <li class="span3">
               <img height="41" src="/img/security-alarms/img_opt_2.png" width="41"/>
               <a href="/auto-diallers/" title="Auto diallers, speech dialler, GSM diallers">
                Auto diallers
               </a>
              </li>
              <li class="span3 last">
               <img height="41" src="/img/security-alarms/img_opt_3.png" width="41"/>
               <a href="/monitored-alarms/" title="Monitored alarm systems">
                Monitored
               </a>
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p class="second-p">
              and set out the pros and cons of each, so you're in a good position
                        to make the right decision on a system that meets all of your needs
                        and suits your situation.
             </p>
             <p class="second-p">
              Unless you opt for a very basic audible-only system
                        - the kind of thing you can buy off the shelf in a DIY store -
                        you'll need a professional alarm installer to supply, install, set
                        up and maintain your alarm system.
                        We'll give you the information you need to make a good choice and to
                        be able to talk to alarm providers confidently about what they can
                        offer.
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="row separator">
           </div>
           <div class="row">
            <div class="audible-only-block" id="audible-systems">
             <div class="row">
              <div class="span9 best-for-container">
               <div class="row paragraph first">
                <h1>
                 Audible-only alarm systems
                </h1>
               </div>
               <div class="span5 best-for">
                <p class="p-head best-for">
                 Best for:
                </p>
                <ul class="best-list">
                 <li>
                  A visible deterrent to opportunist thieves
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  Making a noise to scare off an intruder
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  Waking you up if you're at home and someone tries to break in
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  Making neighbours aware of a break-in
                 </li>
                </ul>
               </div>
               <div class="span4 best-for">
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <p class="row first-p">
              These are also known as non-monitored or bells-only alarm systems.
             </p>
             <p class="simple-p">
              You set your alarm by entering a code or some systems provide one or
                        more fobs to go on your key ring. You swipe them against the control
                        panel instead of typing on the keypad. To deactivate the alarm you
                        enter the code again or swipe the fob.
             </p>
             <p class="second-p">
              When the system is activated by an intruder, it will
                        trigger sounders that are mounted inside and on the external walls
                        of your building. A strobe light on the external sounder will flash
                        as well.
             </p>
             <p class="second-p">
              To stop the noise and the flashing strobe, someone
                        needs to enter the correct code or swipe the fob. The external
                        sounder will only continue for a maximum of 20 minutes. This limit
                        is to prevent noise pollution  we've all cursed that annoying alarm
                        that doesn't stop in the middle of the night! The internal sounder
                        and outdoor strobe light will continue to flash until the system is
                        turned off.
             </p>
             <div class="row pros-cons-container">
              <div class="span4 pros-cons audible-only pros">
               <p class="p-head pros-cons pros">
                Pro's
               </p>
              </div>
              <ul class="span5 note pros-list audible-only pros">
               <li>
                The loud noise and attention it attracts is likely
                                to scare off a chance intruder
               </li>
               <li>
                There's no service charge or running cost once
                                you've bought and installed the system
               </li>
               <li>
                Battery back-up in external sounder means it keeps
                                ringing even if the mains connection is cut
               </li>
              </ul>
             </div>
             <div class="row pros-cons-container">
              <div class="span4 pull-right pros-cons audible-only cons">
               <p class="p-head pros-cons cons">
                Con's
               </p>
              </div>
              <ul class="span5 note cons-list audible-only cons">
               <li>
                This is the bare minimum of protection: if nobody
                                responds to the noise any intruder can carry on
                                intruding!
               </li>
               <li>
                Canny intruders can remove external sounders from
                                the wall and put them in a bucket of water to muffle
                                the sound
               </li>
              </ul>
             </div>
