         <p>
          <em>
           It happens to the best of us: burnt hair, a picked-at blemish, terrible sunburn the list goes on. But dont fret, because there are solutions to these three common problems you can try at home that can help you out of a panic.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/09/dry-hair-emergency.jpg" value="quick-fixes-for-3-common-beauty-emergencies">
           <img alt="dry hair emergency Quick Fixes for 3 Common Beauty Emergencies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6916" height="320" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/dry-hair-emergency.jpg" title="Quick Fixes for 3 Common Beauty Emergencies" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           1 Hair straighteners leave your hair feeling burnt and broken?
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Try a product that is rich in avocado oil to infuse hair with moisture. We love
          <a href="http://www.wella.co.uk/professional/en-EN/product/care/oil-reflections#products" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Wella Professionals Oil Reflections" value="quick-fixes-for-3-common-beauty-emergencies">
           Wella Professionals Oil Reflections
          </a>
          which boasts Avocado oil as one of its key ingredients and can be used to condition, style and finish your hair.
         </p>
         <p>
          Or try our favourite DIY Avocado Hair Mask Recipe from
          <a href="http://laurenconrad.com/blog/2013/09/diy-avocado-hair-and-face-masks-apple-cider-vinegar-beauty-lauren-conrad-september-2013/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="LaurenConrad.com" value="quick-fixes-for-3-common-beauty-emergencies">
           LaurenConrad.com
          </a>
          at home:
         </p>
         <h4>
          DIY Avocado Hair Mask
         </h4>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration:underline">
           Ingredients
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1 banana
          </li>
          <li>
           1 ripe avocado
          </li>
          <li>
           1 tsp olive oil
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration:underline">
           Instructions
          </span>
         </p>
         <ol>
          <ol>
           <li>
            Blend all the ingredients together and massage into your hair from roots to ends, covering thoroughly.
           </li>
           <li>
            Let sit for 30 minutes.
           </li>
           <li>
            Shampoo well and then rinse with apple cider vinegar.
           </li>
          </ol>
         </ol>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/09/natural-ways-to-get-rid-of-spots.jpg" value="quick-fixes-for-3-common-beauty-emergencies">
           <img alt="natural ways to get rid of spots Quick Fixes for 3 Common Beauty Emergencies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6915" height="320" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/natural-ways-to-get-rid-of-spots.jpg" title="Quick Fixes for 3 Common Beauty Emergencies" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <h4>
          2 Picked at a blemish?
         </h4>
         <p>
          Combine a crushed Aspirin with a few drops of water to form a paste. Apply it to the irritated area, allowing it to dry, about 15 minutes. Aspirins anti-inflammatory properties will calm things down and the salicylic acid will kill any bacteria.
         </p>
         <p>
          Want to take a more natural route? Garlic has amazing anti-viral and anti-septic properties, plus the sulphur it contains promotes healing. Cut a fresh clove in half and rub onto your blemish, wait five minutes and then wash off. Repeat a few times a day until your blemish disappears.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/09/sun-burn-remedy.jpg" value="quick-fixes-for-3-common-beauty-emergencies">
           <img alt="sun burn remedy Quick Fixes for 3 Common Beauty Emergencies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6918" height="320" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/sun-burn-remedy.jpg" title="Quick Fixes for 3 Common Beauty Emergencies" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3 Scorched by the sun after a spa treatment?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Wearing sunscreen any time you are outdoors is very important, but particularly so if youve recently had a treatment, like a facial peel or exfoliation. Procedures like this leave skin very sensitive to the elements. The key to healing a burn like this is to soothe the skin and moisturise intensively. Stay away from any moisturiser containing alpha or beta hydroxy acids and use a gentle, but thick, cream. Try something with ingredients like green tea or other antioxidant-rich elements.
         </p>
