&lt;p&gt;After just a few hours afterthe official announcement ofBatman: Arkham Knight postersNeoGAFbegan to publish details of the project from an exclusive pre-review, and the subject of the April issue of the American magazineGame Informer.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-18525 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-1" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" data-original="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-11.jpg" width="720" height="450" /&gt;&lt;noscript&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-18525 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-1" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-11.jpg" width="720" height="450" /&gt;&lt;/noscript&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
The final part of the series, afterunconvincingBatman: Arkham Originsreturned into the hands of the British from Rocksteady, tries to compensate for the weight of last year&#8217;s result of courageous decisions.First, the game will count on a huge open world &#8211; as assured a leading project developer Sefton Hill, the size of it is five times greater than the &#8220;close to claustrophobia&#8221; card gameBatman: Arkham City.&lt;em&gt;&#8220;However, our goal &#8211; not creation of the game with the largest open world&lt;/em&gt;&#8211; immediately made a reservation developer.&#8211;&lt;em&gt;We want to make the world a truly rich, full-bodied, lively and deep. &#8220;&lt;/em&gt;Updated Gotham will be divided into three major areas, with no loading screens, gamers will not see.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="lazy alignnone size-medium wp-image-18526 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-2" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" data-original="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-21.jpg" width="720" height="377" /&gt;&lt;noscript&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-18526 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-2" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-21.jpg" width="720" height="377" /&gt;&lt;/noscript&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In Batman: Arkham Knight developers fulfill longstanding request and will give fans the opportunity to explore Gotham loosely controlled Batmobile equipped with machine gun and rocket launcher.In this regard, they are transforming the city itself: expand the road network, for the sake of diversity will add objects blocking the way, and even other vehicles, and the number of roaming the locations of enemies will increase three to four times.Superhero cause vehicle permit at any time, and sweep the streets with its use promises to be a fascinating example, Batman can knock opponents off their feet, jumping from his steel horse on the go.In addition, secondary missions Riddler in the new game will be closely linked with the Batmobile.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Serious test for the players in the Batman: Arkham Knight would be a clash with a completely new villain, not belonging to the classical universe DC Comics.Perhaps we are talking about a &#8220;dark twin&#8221; Batman, journalists noticed that in the final demo.However, the protagonist himself and become stronger, he will be able to enjoy all their gadgets during flight, acquire mass spectacular fighting techniques (for example, be able to &#8220;throw&#8221; the enemy and perform a back flip with a cast betarangi) and replenish its arsenal of new weapons.The important role of the characters will be assigned the Oracle (Batgirl) &#8211; the hero finally meet her face to face.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="lazy alignnone size-medium wp-image-18527 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-3" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" data-original="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-3.jpg" width="720" height="450" /&gt;&lt;noscript&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-18527 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-3" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-3.jpg" width="720" height="450" /&gt;&lt;/noscript&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In his previous projects in the Batman universe Rocksteadypurposely refusedto multiplayer, putting all efforts in single-player campaign.This tradition does not change the studio and in the case of the newest game &#8211; especially as a series of multiplayer mode hasacquiredin Batman: Arkham Origins.&lt;em&gt;Even at the stage of preparation for manufacture, we agreed that threw all their forces to create an awesome single player&lt;/em&gt;&#8211; said Hill.&#8211;&lt;em&gt;We simply do not have enough time for a decent multiplayer, and in general &#8211; this game, it seems, does not need it. &#8220;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="lazy alignnone size-medium wp-image-18528 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-4" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" data-original="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-4.jpg" width="720" height="426" /&gt;&lt;noscript&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-18528 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-4" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-4.jpg" width="720" height="426" /&gt;&lt;/noscript&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;As to the visual part, here Rocksteady promises to make a real breakthrough.Batman: Arkham Knight created on a modified Unreal Engine (generation is not specified) and uses some powerful technology simulation destruction (especially journalists Game Informer impressed as broken glass objects).Only one model of the character of the first plan, as noted in the review, has more polygons than the entire environment Batman: Arkham Asylum, and the Batmobile consists of &#8220;hundreds of thousands&#8221; of polygons, textures and shaders.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="lazy alignnone size-medium wp-image-18529 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-5" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" data-original="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-5.jpg" width="720" height="426" /&gt;&lt;noscript&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-18529 aligncenter" alt="Batman Arkham Knight-5" src="http://yournewsticker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Batman-Arkham-Knight-5.jpg" width="720" height="426" /&gt;&lt;/noscript&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;To attract game voiced actors involved in the creation of the first two parts of the series.Voice of the protagonist again give Kevin Conroy, the famous Penguin announce Nolan North, in the role of Two-Face will be not less famous Troy Baker, Riddler will talk voice Wally Wingert, and Tara Strong returns to the image of Harley Quinn.&lt;/p&gt;
