          <p>
           <br/>
           We would like to introduce you to 
           <strong>
            The Round Emerald Linear Journey Pendant
           </strong>
           . Handcrafted in Los Angeles, California, this symbolic pendant is a perfect way to celebrate May.
           <a href="http://blog.angara.com/news/may-birthstone-emerald/" title="May Birthstone  Emerald">
            Emerald, official birthstone of May
           </a>
           , symbolizes everlasting nature, prosperity, fortune and other metaphysical properties.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Lush-Green-Journey-Pendant" border="0" height="402" src="http://blog.angara.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Lush-Green-Journey-Pendant.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="Lush-Green-Journey-Pendant" width="690"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           More specifically, a classy design featuring gradually increased round luxurious emeralds bezel set in 14K Yellow Gold creates a remarkable combination most jewelry enthusiasts admire, flaunt and treasure. Five stunning green emeralds set in exotic linear spiral design signify the beautiful journey you have traveled over time.
          </p>
          <p>
           Journey jewelry, especially pendants, makes an emotional gift choice. They symbolize many special moments, including journey of a relationship. In fact, a relationship journey is considered most important and sentimental. Relationships can be in the form of lovers, friends and even a mother and a child. Representing different phases of life, our jewelry makes a timeless gift. With addition of emeralds to your journey jewelry, your significance becomes more meaningful and valued.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.angara.com/p/round-emerald-linear-journey-pendant-sp0135e.html">
            <img alt="Round-Emerald-Linear-Journey-Pendant" border="0" height="350" src="http://blog.angara.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Round-Emerald-Linear-Journey-Pendant.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border: 0px;" title="Round-Emerald-Linear-Journey-Pendant" width="350"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           In addition, this green beryl stands for fertility, forever youth and new beginnings. When exotic journey jewelry design concept blends with the meaning of an emerald, we see extraordinary results, incomparable and ideal.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are a first-time father and finding a fabulous gift for a new mother, your search ends with our journey pendant. You have multiple ways to personalize your jewelry, including four different stone quality grades (Good, Better, Best and Heirloom) and four different metal types (Yellow Gold, Rose Gold, White Gold and Platinum). Your jewelry will become more meaningful than ever. Surprisingly,, this lush green pendant is available at an extra

           off discount. Even better, there will be guaranteed jewelry gifts to make your shopping experience more delightful. Therefore, take advantage of this bundled limited time offer by applying code

            GIFTS
           </strong>
           at checkout today.
          </p>

            Emerald Pendant
           </a>
           ? What is your most memorable life journey? We look forward to hearing from you.
