         <div class="post-content description " itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           <strong>
            insurance companies to pay fine of upto Rs 1 crore on violations by agents IRDA strict rule
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://todaytimesnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image1.jpg">
            <img alt="image" class="alignnone wp-image-2785" height="423" src="http://todaytimesnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image1.jpg" width="836"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Insurance regulatory and Development Authority of India (Irda) on tuesday said an insurer are going to be answerable for all acts and omissions of its agents and can be liable to a penalty of up to Rs one crore.
           <br/>
           Issuing tips for appointment of insurance agents, Irda said, The insurer financial institution} shall be answerable for all acts and omissions of its agents together with violation of code of conduct such under these tips, and shall be vulnerable to a penalty which can be one large integer rupees.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Further, anyone UN agency acts as AN general agent in resistance of the provisions of norms would be vulnerable to a penalty which can be Rs 10,000.
           <br/>
           Also, any insurer or anyone performing on behalf of AN insurer, UN agency appoints anyone as AN general agent not permissible to act in and of itself or interact any insurance business in Asian country through any such person shall be vulnerable to penalty which can be Rs one large integer, the rules by the insurance regulator aforesaid.
           <br/>
           According to the new norms, no one might act as AN general agent for quite one life nondepository financial institution, one general insurer, one health nondepository financial institution and one amongst every of alternative mono-line insurers.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://todaytimesnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/1281528144647bb6b146556f15cf408407733fb576.jpg">
            <img alt="1281528144647bb6b146556f15cf408407733fb576" class="alignnone wp-image-2780" height="639" src="http://todaytimesnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/1281528144647bb6b146556f15cf408407733fb576.jpg" width="852"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Irda additionally said there ought to be be a centralised list of agents that contains all details of agents appointed by all insurers.
           <br/>
           Listing out code of conduct, the insurance watchdog said wherever the agent represents quite one insurer providing same line of merchandise,he ought to dispassionately advice the customer on the merchandise of all Insurers whom hes representing and therefore the product best suited to the particular desires of the prospect.
          </p>
          <p>
