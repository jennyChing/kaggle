&lt;h2&gt;Made to Measure 8 Bit Gamer Blinds &lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;If you&rsquo;re a fan of classic arcade games, an old school gamer or just love that geeky pixel look, this new collection of digitally printed roller blinds created by the team at English Blinds offers a great way to add some retro gaming fun to the windows. Prices will start from 95 and they&rsquo;ll be available to buy online shortly. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;These retro chic blinds pay homage to classic games of the late 70&rsquo;s and early 80&rsquo;s such as Space Invaders and Pac Man and are perfect to give your pad that 8 bit look. With a choice of alien characters and classic game action dressing the windows has never been some much fun!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;To view a gallery of larger images from the collection click &lt;a href="http://www.englishblinds.co.uk/retro-gaming-blinds-gallery" title="Gallery"&gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
