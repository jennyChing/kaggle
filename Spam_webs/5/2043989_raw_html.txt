        <h1 class="headline">
         Master Your Health and Become 20 Pounds Younger with Michele Promaulayko
        </h1>
        <a class="num_comments_link" href="http://lewishowes.com/podcast/michele-promaulayko/#comments" rel="nofollow">

       <div class="post_content">
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignright" height="269" src="http://lewishowes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/SchoolOfGreatnessPodcastArt.jpg" width="269"/>
         <em>
          Interested in learning how to build a life and business around doing what you love? So am I.
          <a href="https://theschoolofgreatness.leadpages.net/email-opt-in-blog-post/" target="_blank">
           Lets connect
          </a>
          .
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         When youre the former Executive Editor of Cosmopolitan Magazine, and the former Editor in Chief of Womens Health Magazine, not to mention the current Editor in Chief of Yahoo! Health, there are a lot of opportunitiesthat come your way.
        </p>
        <p>
         But one in particular has taken the attention of todays guest on
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-school-of-greatness/id596047499" target="_blank" title="The School of Greatness">
          The School of Greatness
         </a>
         ,
         <strong>
          Michele Promaulayko
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Around her 40th birthday, this super successful woman realized that health is something really important (and fleeting if we dont take care of it).
        </p>
        <p>
         That is why she took all her years of tapping into the best health minds in the world and put her experiences into a new book
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1623364035/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1623364035&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lewhow07-20&amp;linkId=Q2JY5KS4PTA7KXRC" target="_blank">
          <em>
           20 Pounds Younger
          </em>
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         I first met Michele years ago at a retreat in Tahoe and was immediately impressed with her knowledge and how down to earth she was.
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve stayed in touch through the years and I loved sitting down with her to talk face to face about her viewpoint on health, diet, fitness, and meditation.
        </p>
        <p>
         I know youll love this episode because Michele gives practical, actionable tips to start improving your health TODAY.
        </p>
        <p>
         She also shares what some of her struggles have been and how she is using meditation to overcome them.
        </p>
        <p>
         I am excited to share this power-packed Episode 125 with my good friend
         <strong>
          Michele Promaulayko
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
