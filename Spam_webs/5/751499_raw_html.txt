        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Gov. Palin said attorneys and experts have identified 25 high crimes and misdemeanors. Her tipping point was Obamas handing of the illegal immigration crisis. She posted:
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/us4palin/posts/800284013335455" target="_blank">
         More Pie Eaters Than Pie Makers = Everyone Begs For Crumbs
        </a>
        . Hot on the impeachment trail, Gov. Palin outlined many
        <a href="http://us4palin.com/impeachable-offenses/" target="_blank">
         offenses
        </a>
        in her
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/us4palin/posts/801160643247792" target="_blank">
         Case to Impeach
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Obama is President. He is not king. He swore to uphold our laws. Palin, Pirro, West, and others want him impeached because broke his oath. POTUS cant just do what he wants. He cant make up his own laws by fiat. He cant strike down laws the same way. We have three equal branches. He cant target those who dont agree with him. He cant lie about laws that affect everyone. He cant start wars without Congress approval.
       </p>
       <p>
        Will it be easy? No. The House must start by filing the Articles. Unless we push them to, they wont do it. If we want it, we must make it happen. Is the Senate on our side? Of course not. But, some Democrats, stand for the rule of law. They can be pushed to do the right thing. If we throw our hands up, it will be more of the same.
       </p>
       <p>
        Lex Rex not Rex Lex. Rule of law, not rule of kings. That is why Gov. Palin wants to impeach him. If we dont, he might just go from President to king.
