             <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="name">
              Police Decipher Mafia Code Resembling Ancient Greek
             </h1>
             <div class="meta-info">
              <ul class="td-category">
               <li class="entry-category">

                Nikoleta Kalmouki

             <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_30018" style="width: 500px;">
              <a href="http://eu.greekreporter.com/files/code-mafia.jpg">
               <img alt="The secret mafia document" class="size-full wp-image-30018" height="387" src="http://eu.greekreporter.com/files/code-mafia.jpg" width="500"/>
              </a>
              <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
               The secret mafia document
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
             <p>
             </p>
             <div class="td-a-rec td-a-rec-id-content_inlineleft td-rec-hide-on-p">
              <div class="social">
               <span class="st_facebook_vcount" displaytext="Facebook">
               </span>
               <span class="st_twitter_vcount" displaytext="Tweet">
               </span>
               <span class="st_email_vcount" displaytext="Email">
               </span>
               <span class="st_sharethis_vcount" displaytext="ShareThis">
               </span>
              </div>
             </div>
             The Italian police authorities have managed to crack a three page document describing a
             <a href="http://eu.greekreporter.com/tag/mafia/">
              Mafia
             </a>
             initiation ritual, written in a code that resembles
             <a href="http://greece.greekreporter.com/?s=ancient+Greek&amp;submit=">
              ancient Greek
             </a>
             and Egyptian hieroglyphics.
             <p>
              The document was foundin Rome during an investigation into the murder of a member of the NdranghetaMafiawhich is based in Calabria in southern
              <a href="http://eu.greekreporter.com/?s=Italy&amp;submit=">
               Italy
              </a>
              . The victim,Vincenzo Femia, was shot dead on the streets of Rome.
             </p>
             <p>
              According to the document, those wishing to join the Mafia must take an oath of eternal loyalty, referred to as a pledge of blood and honor.
             </p>
             <p>
              For the initiation, five people are needed  no more and no less, says the document. The candidateuses a knife or another sharp instrument to draw blood, with which they make a blood oath and swear eternal allegiance to the Mafia. The ceremony should be held in a place considered as sacred, sacrosanct and secure.
             </p>
             <p>
              The document was written in a code that resembles ancient Greek and Egyptian. It was decoded by two police officers who are interested in breaking codes and word puzzles. Initially they managed to decipher the letter C which helped them to understand the rest of the alphabet used.
             </p>
             <p>
              The NdranghetaMafia has becomepowerful and rich from cocaine trafficking, extortion and other criminal activities. According to the Italian authorities, the fact that the document was found in the capital shows that theNdranghetaMafia is also active in other parts of Italy.
             </p>
