          <h3>
           There may be a few stand-up men in Hollywood and Seagal is one of them
          </h3>
          <h6>
           By Staff Writer
          </h6>
          <p>
           PHOENIX (
           <a href="http://intellihub.com/actor-steven-seagal-take-country-back/" target="_blank">
            INTELLIHUB
           </a>
           )  Hollywood superstar Steven Seagal had a few words to say at the Western Center for Journalisms recent gathering which took place on Feb. 22, 2014.
          </p>
          <p>
           Seagal said he had no idea he was speaking that night, but some think otherwise as he spoke on the need to take this country back.
          </p>
          <p>
           Seagal said:
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            When we have a leadership that thinks the Constitution is a jokewhen we have a president who has almost a thousand executive orders nowwhen we have a Department of Justice that thinks that any kind of a judicial system that they make up as theyre going along can get by with whatever they decide that they want to do [] Whats happened with the truth? [] If the truth about Benghazi had come out now, I dont think this man would make it through this term, I think he would be impeached.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           The brief speech ended with some applause from the crowd.
          </p>
