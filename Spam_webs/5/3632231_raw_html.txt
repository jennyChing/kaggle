       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         I never ever share pictures of kitchen on here. I feel like its so beyond DIY it needs DEMO. Im talking 80s laminate cabinets and matching laminate counters. Ive spruced it up by plastering up my kids art work all over the place, still its bad! But, its a rental so we deal with it because there are many other great qualities about this house like cheap rent! Beyond it being totally hideous, its tiny. Forget two people cooking at one time, luckily thats a non issue since Matt can make two things: cereal and grilled cheese. Also, figuring out some sort of organization in such a small space is struggle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Tips to Organize A Small Kitchen" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5776" data-jpibfi-description="" height="843" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Tips-to-Organize-A-Small-Kitchen.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im always on the hunt for ways to organize a small kitchen. Things like keeping the countertop space free, having an organized pantry, all are super important when your space is the size of a postage stamp. Amen?
        </p>
        <p>
         Im helping us both out today and Im sharing some of the ideas Ive found for organizing a small kitchen. I cant wait to incorporate some of this stuff.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="How-to-organize-kitchen-cabinets-9-Clean-and-Scentsible-r" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5771" data-jpibfi-description="" height="375" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/How-to-organize-kitchen-cabinets-9-Clean-and-Scentsible-r.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love these tips from
         <a href="http://www.cleanandscentsible.com/2014/04/organize-kitchen-cabinets.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cleanandscentsible.com/2014/04/organize-kitchen-cabinets.html', 'Clean &amp; Scentsible']);" target="_blank">
          Clean &amp; Scentsible
         </a>
         to maximize cabinet space.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="51b06a2374c5b675ac000872-1._w.540_s.fit_" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5767" data-jpibfi-description="" height="540" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/51b06a2374c5b675ac000872-1._w.540_s.fit_.jpeg" width="404"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         And speaking of maximizing space. Isnt this
         <a href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/how-to-install-a-pegboard-105114" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/how-to-install-a-pegboard-105114', 'pegboard']);" target="_blank">
          pegboard
         </a>
         a great way to organize kitchen utensils while freeing up cabinet space.
         <img alt="51eb4f55fb04d6463f00389e._w.540_" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5768" data-jpibfi-description="" height="960" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/51eb4f55fb04d6463f00389e._w.540_.jpeg" width="540"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I mentioned freeing up counter space. These
         <a href="http://www.thekitchn.com/rebeccas-removable-solutions-rental-kitchen-small-cool-kitchens-2013-192803" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thekitchn.com/rebeccas-removable-solutions-rental-kitchen-small-cool-kitchens-2013-192803', 'hanging baskets']);" target="_blank">
          hanging baskets
         </a>
         are great for keeping the counters clutter free.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="9962bfdf5717cc3e28cc7f602989bdb6" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5769" data-jpibfi-description="" height="1024" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/9962bfdf5717cc3e28cc7f602989bdb6-493x1024.jpg" width="493"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love the look of kitchen command centers but there is just no free wall space in my kitchen to incorporate one. I love this
         <a href="http://www.twotwentyone.net/2012/08/hidden-kitchen-organizer/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.twotwentyone.net/2012/08/hidden-kitchen-organizer/', 'hidden kitchen organizer']);" target="_blank">
          hidden kitchen organizer
         </a>
         because its tucked away behind acabinet door, hiding the clutter. When you have a small kitchen even the smallest bit of clutter than make the whole space feel unruly.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_8474-001" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5772" data-jpibfi-description="" height="600" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_8474-001.jpg" width="428"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://designdininganddiapers.com/2013/01/new-pantry-organization/#_a5y_p=1150787" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://designdininganddiapers.com/2013/01/new-pantry-organization/#_a5y_p=1150787', ' Baskets in the pantry']);" target="_blank">
          Baskets in the pantry
         </a>
         seem pretty genius to me. Its a great way to keep clutter contained and everything in its place.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_9646" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5773" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_9646.jpg" width="416"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Speaking of genius pantry storage this one is on point.
         <a href="http://makebakecelebrate.blogspot.com/search?updated-max=2011-10-07T20:30:00-07:00&amp;max-results=7" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://makebakecelebrate.blogspot.com/search?updated-max=2011-10-07T20:30:00-07:00&amp;max-results=7', 'Velcro acrylic containers']);" target="_blank">
          Velcro acrylic containers
         </a>
         stuck onto the inside of the pantry to hold the loose packets that always seem to get lost and disappear randomly (until you move and then you discover 12 taco seasonings you didnt know you had).
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Making-Home-Base-Etched-Glass-Canister-Labels" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5774" data-jpibfi-description="" height="475" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Making-Home-Base-Etched-Glass-Canister-Labels.jpg" width="712"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pretty storage is just as important as storage in general in my book. Putting ingredients in glass containers makes them pretty instantly. These
         <a href="http://www.makinghomebase.com/diy-etched-glass-canister-labels/" target="_blank">
          etched labeled glass canisters
         </a>
         take it to another level.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="metal basket shelving 1" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5775" data-jpibfi-description="" height="566" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/metal-basket-shelving-1.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I have a bajillion cook books that never get used because I store them in a completely separate room. When I do get one out I end up leaving it on the counter for days until I get sick of looking it at and finally give in and put it away. I love these
         <a href="http://rainonatinroof.com/2012/07/basket-shelving/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://rainonatinroof.com/2012/07/basket-shelving/', 'metal basket shelves']);" target="_blank">
          metal basket shelves
         </a>
         because they make beautiful storage. Having all of your cookbooks nearby is also a huge bonus.
         <img alt="vita ranunkler 41" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5777" data-jpibfi-description="" height="640" src="http://www.makinghomebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/vita-ranunkler-41.jpg" width="428"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes you cant help but have a few things out on the counter. The only way to keep the clutter at bay is to have them all corralled in a tray or
         <a href="http://romantiskahem.blogspot.com/2010/10/mmmmmmmmaries-fina-hem.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://romantiskahem.blogspot.com/2010/10/mmmmmmmmaries-fina-hem.html', 'beautiful vintage box']);" target="_blank">
          beautiful vintage box
         </a>
         like this one. Perfect for tucking away those cutting boards, regularly used spices and oils. It would also be great as a coffee station.
        </p>
        <p>
         I know the struggle of having a small kitchen. Its real. I feel it. If you are in the same boat, I hope some of these ideas sparked some inspiration so you can start making your kitchen work for you! Or if you have some tips to organize a small kitchen, Id love to hear them!
        </p>

        </div>

         </script>
        </p>
