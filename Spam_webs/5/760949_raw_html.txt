        <h2 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
         <a href="http://www.calmer-you.com/is-it-a-snake-or-a-rope/" title="Is it a Snake or a Rope?">
          Is it a Snake or a Rope?
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
          Have you heard the story about the snake and the rope? Self help author Byron Katie tells a story about seeing a rope in the dessert and being terrified,
          <i>
           thinking
          </i>
          it was a snake.
         </p>
         <p>
          When she realised it was just a rope, she knew there was nothing to be afraid of.
         </p>
         <p>
          It was a rope all along.
         </p>
         <p>
          It made me think about the anxious mind; we see snakes everywhere, when really there are only ever ropes.
         </p>
         <p>
          The anxiety makes us think that there is danger and threat everywhere, but 99.9% of the time, its nothing dangerous at all.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its a rope, not a snake.
         </p>
         <p>
          So next time your anxious mind tries to tell you something is dangerous or that you should be fearful, remember,
          <strong>
           <em>
            its only a rope.
           </em>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Id love to hear about one specific example of something in your life that you realise is just a rope and not a snake  please let us know in the comments below.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            If you liked this article or know anyone that could benefit from it, please liked it and share it.
           </em>
          </strong>
