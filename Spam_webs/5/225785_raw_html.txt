       <h1>
        Rustic &amp; Boho - Joanna &amp; Piotr's Woodland Wedding
       </h1>
       <p>
        An incredibly fun and gorgeous wedding in the woods, we're a bit jealous of Joanna's two gunas...
       </p>
       <img alt="floral crown couple " class="mob featured-image" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-148.jpg" width="620"/>
       <div class="post-footer">
        <a href="real-weddings/">
         <span class="tag">
          Real Weddings
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="by">
         by
         <a href="author/lucy/">
          Lucy
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="text-block">
        <p>
         This morning's wedding is one I've swooned over many times, since starting this post a few weeks ago. Not only is it beautiful, it's different and so personal to the couple - exactly what a wedding should be.
         <strong>
          Joanna and Piotr
         </strong>
         did it their way, planning a mix of eclectic boho style with a little bit of tradition and a whole lot of love and remarkable it seemed all very effortless and fun too! Their rustic celebration comes all the way from Poland, so for any of you planning a polish wedding this will be hugely inspirational - it has some seriousstyle to boot, with not one but two incredible wedding dresses and one talented photographer to top it off! So join us as we check out how
         <a href="http://www.pawelbebenca.com/" target="_blank" title="pawel bebenca">
          Pawel Bebenca's
         </a>
         captured this awesome day...
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish Real Wedding" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95484" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-2.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish Real Wedding" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95392" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-3.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I'm excited to hear all about how Joanna and Piotr got together, so who better to tell us then the bride herself...
         <em>
          Once upon a time there was an ordinary love story, were we met on a dating website! Piotr was a habitu, I got in there to remove my account, as I have been receiving too many inapriopriate mails that spamed my mailbox. As I began to remove each email one by one, I came across a special email from Piotr, I didnt delete it, however I wasnt sure if I wanted start a new relationship in this way, so we began to email each other and then Piotr asked me to go on a date. I had nothing to lose but prepared a plan for an elegant escape just in case! When the D-date came, I did everything to be late, and when I finally got there and saw him sitting on the bench I thought What a hot guy!. He saw me too and knew it was me, so I smiled, waving my hand, and then I pretended I just got a call. After 5 minutes, when he was still waiting after a 25 minute delay I decided to go and spend my evening with him This first date lasted almost a whole night! After few another meetings we knew we couldnt live without each other And so it started
         </em>
         Talk about a chance meeting, always check your emails ladies!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="BHLDN Wedding Dress" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95394" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-9.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pastel rustic bouquet" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95483" height="930" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-19.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I like this couple already, why? Because they took a chance on each other and really that's what love is all about isn't it? After less than a year together Piotr knew Joanna was the one (well actually he knew after a few dates) but he decided to seal the deal on Joanna's birthday...
         <em>
          It was in the middle of the week, after a hard day at work, I had no plans for my birthday, well at least I thought that! When I came home I saw a beautiful flowers and a huge, pink, meringue cake. Piotr gave me a kiss and showed me a little box he had in his hand. Its now! I thought But it was not. It was a lovely Swarovski necklace ended with a teat! I asked him Did I miss something?' But he just smiled, cut the cake and said In 15 minutes were going out. He had made a reservation in our favorite restaurant. We celebrated my birthday, ate a delicious meal but he kept popping out for a few minutes at a time and I began to get suspicious. We finished our dinner and headed home, when we got their he went to the bedroom and came back with something in his hand. His face was white, he kneeled and asked if I would marry him! It took all night but it was perfect!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt=" Joanna and Piotr  " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95395" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-14.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic boutonniere" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95397" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-20.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="bridal makeup" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95398" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-21.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic bridal floral hair garland" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95400" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-25.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic bridal floral hair garland" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95401" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-26.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Joanna is one stylish bride, she has not one but two wedding dresses. She accessorised with a unique floral crown, which mixed soft shades of astilbe and roses with bright crimson and blue cornflowers, a combination you don't often see but works incredibly beautifully.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic bridal floral hair garland" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95403" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-30.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Piotor" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95404" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-32.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pawel-Bebenca-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95491" height="207" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-1.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Navy suit and pink shirt groom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95405" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-34.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Piotr also brought out his a-game with a petrol blue suit, blue bow tie and a pink shirt, and blue dicky bow. Looking suave.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="bridal preparations" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95407" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-37.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic bridal floral hair garland" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95408" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-38.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now the first of Joanna's uber amazing wedding dresses - a Savannah dress from
         <a href="http://www.slubnesuknie.com.pl/" target="_blank" title="Nabla">
          Nabla
         </a>
         and it's just stunning. With lace detail, a sweetheart neckline, layered skirt detail and the cutest cap sleeves, it's angelic and delicate in all the right ways. Teamed with the floral crown gives it the boho vibe that Joanna wanted.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="boho bridal dress" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95410" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-48.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Navy suit and pink shirt groom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95411" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-50.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The pair planned their wedding ceremony for 5:30pm, meaning they had plenty of time to sleep in (apparently very important for the couple) have breakfast, make sure their plans were on track and just relax and spend time together.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Joanna and Piotr" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95412" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-52.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Joanna and Piotr" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95413" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-54.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         In Poland, before the church ceremony everyone gathers at the home of the bride to accompany the couple to the church, but also to witness the blessing and symbolic farewells of the bride with her parents, relatives, and friends. The blessing by the parents before the church is just as important as the church ceremony.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95414" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-58.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95415" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-62.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         A crucifix, a lighted candle, a bowl of holy water and a sprinkler (if the regular Polish brush-type sprinkler is not available, this can be a leafy tree branch) should be prepared. Traditionally the mother of the bride gives the blessing. Either kneelers or some cushions are provided for the bride and groom to kneel on. They hold hands as they kneel in front of their parents. The mother then sprinkles the bride and groom-to-be with Holy Water, whereupon they make the Sign of the Cross. She then gives them the crucifix to kiss.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95416" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-64.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The father of the bride may utter a blessing of his own or simply sprinkle the couple with Holy Water. The bridegrooms parents may also impart their blessing. Others, for instance grandparents, or godparents may also bless the couple.After the blessing, the bride and groom thank, hug and kiss their parents and the wedding party prepares to leave for church.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt=" Joanna and Piotr  " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95418" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-74.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="vintage wedding car" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95417" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-68.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95499" height="284" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-2.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic church decor" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95419" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-80.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic church decor" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95420" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-82.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The pair headed to the centre of Warsaw, Christian Church on the New Market Place in a lovely old VW beetle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pawel Bebenca - Joanna Piotr  (88)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95422" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-88.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pawel Bebenca - Joanna Piotr  (86)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95421" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-86.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Groom Piotr" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95423" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-90.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95424" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-92.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95425" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-94.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          After all the lazy preparations in the morning, our parents were afraid we wouldn't get to the church on time! But we did and the ceremony was beautiful. My lovely best friend sang Ave Maria for us and we couldnt wait to say our vows as, it was the most important part for us.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95426" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-96.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95427" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-98.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95494" height="930" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-99.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95431" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-112.jpg" style="font-size: 13px;" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish  wedding ceremony" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95433" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-116.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="just married confetti!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95436" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-123.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         What an amazing confetti show!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="just married" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95437" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-127.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt=" Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95438" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-134.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt=" Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95440" height="930" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-141.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt=" Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95442" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-154.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="vintage wedding car" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95444" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-158.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         And after the church, where else but to party? When it came to the venue, Joanna and Piotr were searching for a long time to find somewhere that was just right...
         <em>
          We were looking for a rustic venue for a long time, and we almost decided to leave the idea of a rustic wedding as we couldnt find anything we liked, until we saw Gorki Zielone. We had spent many weekends just 200 metres away from this venue last summer, and we didnt know about it! When we first visited, we saw a huge white fireplace in the middle of a round building created from wood and glass situated in a lovely, dark, wild forest! The place is magical and we knew it was the one.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pawel Bebenca - Joanna Piotr  (164)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95445" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-164.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pawel Bebenca - Joanna Piotr  (206)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95458" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-206.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95459" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-208.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Another popular Polish tradition is theBread and Salt Blessing.
         <em>
         </em>
         At the wedding reception, the parents of the bride and groom greet the newly married couple with bread, which is lightly sprinkled with salt and a goblet of wine.Parents usually say : 
         <em>
          According to our Old Polish tradition, we greet you with bread and salt, so that your home might always enjoy abundance
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95460" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-210.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95461" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-215.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ah, the old carrying over the threshold, I think I like this tradition too!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding reception" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95446" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-166.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pawel Bebenca - Joanna Piotr  (220)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95462" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-220.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          When we came to our stunning venue we saw our lovely guests, family and friends, sharing their best wishes with us, admiring the place we prepared for them, we knew its gonna be a beautiful night.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding stationery" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95463" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-232.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         For the decor of the wedding, the venue was an incredible backdrop to Joanna and Piotr's desired look - they wanted their wedding tobe simple, homely and rustic with a delicious meal and the best live music, making sure their guests were well looked after and entertained. Not to mention impressed, which I'm sure they were!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding reception" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95451" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-182.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding reception" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95452" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-184.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Like Joanna's beautiful floral crown, the tables were decorated with flowers of muted tones and foliage with touches of colour.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding reception" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95454" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-190.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The space lent itself perfectly to their rustic and homely vision, with exposed beams hanging lampshades and the softly lit glow of the lights.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding stationery" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95482" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-326.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding stationery" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95455" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-194.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Craft paper place cards and mason jar illustrated menus tied with twine were simple touches to the white tables.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="rustic wedding reception" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95456" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-196.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="wedding signage" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95449" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-172.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="drinks dispenser" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95485" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-221.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="wedding photobooth " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95464" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-241.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="wedding photobooth " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95465" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-243.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         As the ceremony was late in the afternoon the couple took advantage of the night time sky and the stunning forest backdrop to take some intimate photographs with Pawel Bebenca.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95467" height="930" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-249.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you liked dress number one you are going to
         <em>
          love
         </em>
         dress number two from
         <a href="http://www.bhldn.com/" target="_blank" title="BHLDN">
          BHLDN
         </a>
         at Anthropologie. The lace inlay dress channels the 70s boho look but the sheer neckline and sleeves make it a bit more party than the ceremony gown and the geometric pattern adds an eclectic feel to Joanna's look - Perfect with the flower crown too, of course!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95468" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-252.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95470" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-258.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Wed had been planning surprises for each other but we didnt have enough time to do that during our reception, as I got sick early before our first dance. We thought that were doing this reception for us, but when we left to rest a bit our guest carried the party on in such a wonderful way and waited for us to return. Today we think that even if we had added a million things to the day it wouldn't have made a difference, it was out family and friends who made the day so special for us - Just two big families who accepted, loved and saved us and our celebration.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Joanna and Piotr by Pawel Bebenca" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-282.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Polish real wedding traditions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95478" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-286.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         So what advice did Joanna and Piotr have for others planning a wedding day?
         <em>
          The bride has to be a Bride only. No coordinating, no decorating, no calls, just relax, SPA yourself and hire a good wedding planner on the day. Find a place with a well-organized venue manager and a band with a host who will look after your guests, even if youre not around. Start your wedding ceremony earlier, as when youre coming to your reception the time is running so fast, you hardly have time to talk with everyone!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="first dance" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95479" height="413" src="http://images.onefabday.com/2014/02/Pawel-Bebenca-Joanna-Piotr-288.jpg" width="620"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         A huge congratulations to Joanna and Piotr and we wish you a lifetime of happiness together! A big Thank You goes to our pal, Pawel Bebenca for sharing his work with us at One Fab Day! To see more of Pawel's work pop over to
         <a href="http://www.pawelbebenca.com/" target="_blank" title="pawel bebenca">
          www.pawelbebenca.com
         </a>
         .
        </p>
