      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        Tatooine shouldnt be your first choice for a vacation destination in the
        <em>
         <a href="nerdist.com/tag/star-wars/" target="_blank">
          Star Wars
         </a>
        </em>
        galaxy. Its unbearably hot, the Dune Sea is full of sand instead of water, and then there are all the dangers: Sarlaccs, Tusken Raiders, Jawasoh my! Yeah, their chamber of commerce has a lot of challenges to overcome.
        <a href="http://www.nerdist.com/tag/liam-lynch/" target="_blank">
         Liam Lynch
        </a>
        s latest song, Bad Motivator, shows what happens to one well-meaning guy (whos a little bit of a doofus) when he goes to Tatooine for spring break.
        <a href="http://nerdist.com/tag/chris-hardwick/" target="_blank">
         Chris Hardwick
        </a>
        stars as the underachieving droid mechanic with a bad motivator like R5-D4. Awww, poor ol Red.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://nerdist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/R5D4-bad-motivator-05032015.gif">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-249690" src="http://nerdist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/R5D4-bad-motivator-05032015.gif"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Gif:
        <a href="http://ewokthisway.tumblr.com/post/10004249158" target="_blank">
         Ewok This Way
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        If only R5-D4 would have held that motivator together for just a few more minutes
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether youre spending this
        <a href="http://nerdist.com/tag/may-the-4th/" target="_blank">
         May the 4th
        </a>
        repairing droids or drinking blue milk in a Cantina, we hope the Force is with you. And remember: We should all aspire to be more like R2-D2. That astromech knows whats what.
       </p>
       <p>
        Song and video by
        <a href="http://www.liamlynch.net/" target="_blank">
         Liam Lynch
