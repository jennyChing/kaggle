      <div class="post_header">
       <h2 class="post_title">
        <a href="http://www.scenicnh.com/blog/2015/01/boott-spur-trail-white-mountains/" rel="bookmark" title="Boott Spur Trail, White Mountains">
         <span>
          Boott Spur Trail, White Mountains
         </span>
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="post_date">
        20 Jan
        <br/>
        2015
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="post_content">
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/img-show/I0000ClYsDsYhtQM">
         <img alt="Clouds engulf Tuckerman Ravine from Boott Spur Trail during the summer months in the scenic landscape of the White Mountains, New Hampshire USA ..Notes: Boott Spur Trail is located on the eastern slopes of Mount Washington." src="http://www.photoshelter.com/img-get/I0000ClYsDsYhtQM/s/575/384/SCD075615.jpg" title="Boott Spur Trail - Mount Washington, New Hampshire USA"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        Tuckerman Ravine  Boott Spur Trail, New Hampshire
        <br/>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <strong>
         Five images from Boott Spur Trail 
        </strong>
        This year I will be trying a few new approaches on the blog to see what works and doesnt work. One new subject will be
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/search?I_DSC=%22hiking%22%2C+%22Trail%22&amp;I_FILE_NAME=&amp;I_SDATE[MM]=&amp;I_SDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_SDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_EDATE[MM]=&amp;I_EDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_EDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_CITY=&amp;I_STATE=&amp;I_COUNTRY_ISO=&amp;I_ORIENTATION=&amp;I_IS_RELEASED=&amp;I_IS_PRELEASED=&amp;I_SORT=DATE&amp;I_DSC_AND=t&amp;V_ID=&amp;G_ID=&amp;C_ID=&amp;_ACT=search" target="_blank" title="Photos of hiking trails click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          hiking trails
         </span>
        </a>
        . I will being doing one or two blog posts a month that will include five images from any given trail in the White Mountains of New Hampshire. Beautiful scenes can be found along many of the trails in the White Mountains and I want to share them with you.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        If I had to pick my top ten favorite trails in the White Mountains, Boott Spur Trail would make the list. Much of the Boott Spur Trail is above tree-line, and the views are breathtaking! First timers hiking this trail could suffer from view overload, and will be taking many more breaks than expected to enjoy the mountain landscape.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-30766">
        </span>
       </p>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/img-show/I0000pNOl6y3SE6E">
         <img alt="Scenic view from Boott Spur Trail in the White Mountains, New Hampshire." src="http://www.photoshelter.com/img-get/I0000pNOl6y3SE6E/s/575/413/SCM1011-05.jpg" title="Mount Washington - White Mountains, New Hampshire USA"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        Tuckerman Ravine (autumn)  Boott Spur Trail, New Hampshire
        <br/>
       </div>
       <p>
        I have explored Boott Spur Trail in all four seasons and think autumn and winter are the best times to create scenic imagery along the trail. The alpine flowers, when in bloom, are also excellent on Boott Spur Mountain. And for summer night hikers, the trail is very well marked and for the most part easy to follow during the night.
       </p>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/img-show/I0000u7Jmsg7fBpg">
         <img alt="Mount Washington - Tuckerman Ravine in extreme weather conditions from Boott Spur Trail in the White Mountains, New Hampshire USA during the winter months. Strong winds cause snow to blow across the mountain tops." src="http://www.photoshelter.com/img-get/I0000u7Jmsg7fBpg/s/575/383/SCWD0711059.jpg" title="Boott Spur Trail - Mt Washington, Tuckerman Ravine"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        Tuckerman Ravine (winter)  Boott Spur Trail, New Hampshire
        <br/>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        The view of Tuckerman Ravine from Boott Spur Trail is excellent anytime of year, but during the
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/search?I_DSC=tuckerman+ravine+winter+snow&amp;I_FILE_NAME=&amp;I_SDATE[MM]=&amp;I_SDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_SDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_EDATE[MM]=&amp;I_EDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_EDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_CITY=&amp;I_STATE=&amp;I_COUNTRY_ISO=&amp;I_ORIENTATION=&amp;I_IS_RELEASED=&amp;I_IS_PRELEASED=&amp;I_SORT=DATE&amp;I_DSC_AND=t&amp;V_ID=&amp;G_ID=&amp;C_ID=&amp;_ACT=search" target="_blank" title="Winter photos of Tuckerman Ravine click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          winter months
         </span>
        </a>
        when the landscape is covered in snow is my favorite time. Watching the snow blow in and out of the ravine is a sight to see!
       </p>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/img-show/I0000B2lAzvA_p9g">
         <img alt="Split Rock located on Boott Spur Trail in the White Mountains, New Hampshire USA. Split Rock is an glacier erratic." src="http://www.photoshelter.com/img-get/I0000B2lAzvA_p9g/s/575/419/SCM1953-04.jpg" title="Boott Spur Trail - Mount Washington, New Hampshire USA"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        Split Rock  Boott Spur Trail, New Hampshire
        <br/>
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        An interesting feature along Boott Spur Trail is a glacial erratic known as
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/search?I_DSC=Split+Rock&amp;I_FILE_NAME=&amp;I_SDATE[MM]=&amp;I_SDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_SDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_EDATE[MM]=&amp;I_EDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_EDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_CITY=&amp;I_STATE=&amp;I_COUNTRY_ISO=&amp;I_ORIENTATION=&amp;I_IS_RELEASED=&amp;I_IS_PRELEASED=&amp;I_SORT=RANK&amp;I_DSC_AND=t&amp;V_ID=&amp;G_ID=&amp;C_ID=&amp;_ACT=search" target="_blank" title="Photos of Split Rock click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          Split Rock
         </span>
        </a>
        (above). Glacial erratics can be found all over the White Mountains, but this one is really cool. I also enjoy the impressive
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/search?I_DSC=scree+wall+boott+spur+trail&amp;I_FILE_NAME=&amp;I_SDATE%5BMM%5D=&amp;I_SDATE%5BDD%5D=DD&amp;I_SDATE%5BYYYY%5D=YYYY&amp;I_EDATE%5BMM%5D=&amp;I_EDATE%5BDD%5D=DD&amp;I_EDATE%5BYYYY%5D=YYYY&amp;I_CITY=&amp;I_STATE=&amp;I_COUNTRY_ISO=&amp;I_ORIENTATION=&amp;I_IS_RELEASED=&amp;I_IS_PRELEASED=&amp;I_SORT=RANK&amp;I_DSC_AND=t&amp;V_ID=&amp;G_ID=&amp;C_ID=&amp;_ACT=search" target="_blank" title="Scree wall photos click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          scree walls
         </span>
        </a>
        that line this trail.
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/search?I_DSC=Scree+walls&amp;I_FILE_NAME=&amp;I_SDATE%5BMM%5D=&amp;I_SDATE%5BDD%5D=DD&amp;I_SDATE%5BYYYY%5D=YYYY&amp;I_EDATE%5BMM%5D=&amp;I_EDATE%5BDD%5D=DD&amp;I_EDATE%5BYYYY%5D=YYYY&amp;I_CITY=&amp;I_STATE=&amp;I_COUNTRY_ISO=&amp;I_ORIENTATION=&amp;I_IS_RELEASED=&amp;I_IS_PRELEASED=&amp;I_SORT=DATE&amp;I_DSC_AND=t&amp;V_ID=&amp;G_ID=&amp;C_ID=&amp;_ACT=search" target="_blank" title="Scree wall photos click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          Scree walls
         </span>
        </a>
        are built on the edge of trails in alpine areas to discourage hikers from going off trail and stepping on fragile alpine habitat.
       </p>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/img-show/I0000Le.TBGP9pWw">
         <img alt="A hiker enjoys the view of Mount Washington from the summit of Boott Spur Mountain during the summer months. Located in the White Mountains, New Hampshire" src="http://www.photoshelter.com/img-get/I0000Le.TBGP9pWw/s/575/383/SC095911.jpg" title="Boott Spur - Mount Washington, New Hampshire  USA"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="caption center" style="text-align: center;">
        Boott Spur Summit  Boott Spur Trail, New Hampshire
        <br/>
       </div>
       <p>
        Eventually, Boott Spur Trail connects with Davis Path on the summit of Boott Spur Mountain where there are 360 degree views. From here hikers can continue on to
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/gallery/Mount-Washington-New-Hampshire/G0000Glo__AHSI24/" target="_blank" title="Mt Washington photos click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          Mt Washington
         </span>
        </a>
        or head back down the Boott Spur Trail.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are looking of an enjoyable trail consider hiking Boott Spur Trail. You will spend a large amount time in the alpine zone, and photographers will have a field day on this trail. All of the above images can be licensed for publications by clicking on the image you are interested in, and you can view more images from along Boott Spur Trail
        <a href="http://scenicnh.photoshelter.com/search?I_DSC=+%22Boott+Spur+Trail%22+&amp;I_FILE_NAME=&amp;I_SDATE[MM]=&amp;I_SDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_SDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_EDATE[MM]=&amp;I_EDATE[DD]=DD&amp;I_EDATE[YYYY]=YYYY&amp;I_CITY=&amp;I_STATE=&amp;I_COUNTRY_ISO=&amp;I_ORIENTATION=&amp;I_IS_RELEASED=&amp;I_IS_PRELEASED=&amp;I_SORT=DATE&amp;I_DSC_AND=t&amp;V_ID=&amp;G_ID=&amp;C_ID=&amp;_ACT=search" target="_blank" title="Photos of Boott Spur Trail click here">
         <span style="color:#0000CD;">
          here
         </span>
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Happy image making..
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
