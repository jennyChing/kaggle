       <h1>
        MAC Cosmetics  Dazzleglass Sneak Peek, Part 2
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="meta-info">
       <div class="date-post">
        Wednesday, February 27th, 2008
       </div>
       <div class="disc-btns">
       </div>
      </div>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="" border="0" height="300" src="http://www.temptalia.com/images/2008/swatches_dazzleglass4.jpg" width="390"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
        Remember when we saw Dazzleglasses available on eBay? Well, I purchased several of them out of curiosity, and while they seem legitimate, there is no way for me to know for sure quite yet. The packaging is new, which was expected of Dazzleglass (launching in May), so its even more difficult to determine if these are the real deal. As a result, Ive been sitting on these swatches for nearly a month now, and I figured Id share them as long as I disclaim that these may NOT be real. Since theres that chance, I also am not going to be doing a full review until much later, when they actually launch.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="" border="0" height="140" src="http://www.temptalia.com/images/maccosmetics/media_dazzleglass001.jpg" width="424"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.kissandmakeup.tv/2008/02/mac-to-release.html" target="_blank">
        Kiss and Makeup
       </a>
       revealed official color information
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Comet Blue
       </strong>
       Violet blue
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Funtabulous
       </strong>
       Purple with violet pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Ms. Fizz
       </strong>
       Bright pink with blue pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Like Venus
       </strong>
       Clean light bubble gum pink with red pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Baby Sparks
       </strong>
       Pale pink with violet pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Money Honey
       </strong>
       Mid-tone rosy mauve with red pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Rags to Riches
       </strong>
       Plumy pink with teal and red pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Date Night
       </strong>
       Deep plum with blue and gold pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Spanking Rich
       </strong>
       Raspberry pink with red and gold pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Get Rich Quick
       </strong>
       Warm yellow brown
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Bare Necessity
       </strong>
       Caramel apricot with red and gold pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Steppin Out
       </strong>
       Clean yellow pink with red pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Glamour O.D.
       </strong>
       Bright coral red with red pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Suugarrimmed
       </strong>
       Milky pale pink with red, teal and white pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Pleasure Principle
       </strong>
       Clear white violet, teal and white pearl
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Love Alert
       </strong>
       Raspberry red with red pearl
      </p>
      <p>
       Looks like the eBay Dazzleglasses are dead on for the names AND color descriptions! This is good news and definitely adds to the PRO column for legitimacy!
      </p>
      <p>
       I will give you a quick rundown of the texture/finish/overview of these, though! They are less sticky than lipglasses, but a little more sticky than lustreglasses. For those hoping for a non-tacky gloss, this is definitely not an answer to you. They are all sheer colors, so dont expect a lot of opacity or color, especially for those with pigmented lips. There is a good deal of shimmer in each gloss, and the finish is a high, glossy sheen.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="" border="0" height="452" src="http://www.temptalia.com/images/2008/swatches_dazzleglass.jpg" width="390"/>
      </p>
