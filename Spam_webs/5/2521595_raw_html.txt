              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <b>
                The best $12 I have ever spent?
               </b>
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <br/>
              </span>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               It was just this past weekend when my husband Paul and I drove from our rental at the beach up to Beverly Hills 90210 at 9:30 AM on Saturday morning for an
               <b>
                aerobics workout class
               </b>
               with
               <a href="http://www.richardsimmons.com/j15/" target="_blank">
                Richard Simmons
               </a>
               !!! Feeling slightly hungover after one too many drinks with Wendy at Chateau Marmont the night before (we hadn't seen each other in three months!), Paul and I dragged ourselves out of bed and made it to '
               <a href="http://www.richardsimmons.com/j15/index.php?option=com_jcalpro&amp;Itemid=125&amp;extmode=cat&amp;cat_id=1" target="_blank">
                Slimmons
               </a>
               ' studio (brilliantly named by the way) by 10 AM to sign-up early for the 11:15 AM class.
              </span>

              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               We were probably a bit too eager as we were one of the first ones there. I would probably just aim to show at 10:45 AM next time, as our class was
               <b>
                packed with around 60-80 people
               </b>
               - but they did not turn anyone away at the door, which was our worry. So we grabbed some coffee and came back to the studio at 11 AM and the place had filled right up with more people streaming in through the door!
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <br/>
              </span>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               At 11:15 AM
               <b>
                the doors FLEW right open
               </b>
               and disco music started pumping, people started jockeying for a spot and the class started right then and there. Step, clap. Step clap. I tried not to smack into anyone! We even had the backup dancers for Justin Bieber and Rihanna taking our class. Richard grabbed his remote and powered on the disco ball hanging from the centre of the room. It was amazing.

              </a>

              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               Richard greeted us all
               <b>
                in an orange hummingbird top
               </b>
               , matching floral rimmed glasses and bright orange hair. It was like a comedy show and aerobics show in one! He is so sassy, so sharp, he hurls out his one-liners while
               <b>
                spraying himself down with Pam
               </b>
               cooking spray and threatens to spray you if you don't work hard enough!!!
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <br/>
              </span>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <b>
                Between all the jokes
               </b>
               , the laughs, the disco and top 40 hits - your ass gets worked! You will not be able to peel that smile off your face and the 90-minute class flies right on by. We did floor work facing the mirror, then having the class face each other from each half, then my favourite - facing each other in a circle, when Richard picks out all sorts of volunteers to do the moves with him in the middle. Of course, he gets
               <b>
                all the guys to take off their shirts to strut their stuff
               </b>
               for the class - it's a really good laugh and everyone really gets into it and of course, we all loved it.

               <b>
                lifting some light weights, crunches
               </b>
               and then a few inspirational words from Richard. We also celebrated SIX birthdays that day and each celebrant received a little Richard Simmons mini doll necklace made out of fimo, which was very sweet.
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               <br/>
              </span>
              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               What do I love about Richard besides his energy, humour and passion for active living? He's a very genuine human being and you can feel that from him - he truly does care and you can tell that he LOVES what he does. He
               <b>
                stayed for almost 30 minutes
               </b>
               after class taking pictures, chatting with regulars and out-of-towners and does not leave until he gets through the line-up of people waiting to meet with him after class. $12? I would've easily paid $20!
              </span>

               <b>
                What to bring
               </b>
               - A towel to lie on top of your mat for crunches, stretch and floor exercises, bottled water or purchase for $1 from the studio and a good sense of humour. Be prepared for anything and all sass from Richard Simmons. Bring extra cash if you want to purchase a souvenir T-shirt, towel, key chain or DVD.
              </span>

              <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">

               <b>
                What to wear
               </b>
               - Proper workout clothing that you can move in, you're going to be sweating - don't doubt this. Good strong running shoes and a smile! Richard sometimes shoots his classes for his YouTube channel.
