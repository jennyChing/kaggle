        <p>
         The summer is almost over and it is said that every summer has a story. Most of the summer stories are romantic. They are all filled with sun, sand and smiles. To keep the romantic mood, however, we need to make the atmosphere at home also cosy and fabulous. We have prepared for you some tips to help you change your home mood without overdoing it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img data-id="e59fc472fa7d43cf9df193e95181d056" data-uploaded="true" src="https://d2ia58550xamqo.cloudfront.net/e59fc472fa7d43cf9df193e95181d056.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Romantic Details at Home
        </h2>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Vase with flowers in "love" colours
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The flowers have the ability to attract the attention wherever you place them. Take advantage of their magic to create a romantic atmosphere. You may use the classics red roses, but if you want to escape the clich, there are other options like purple freesia, white lilies, pink hyacinths, tulips mix, etc.. No matter what type of flowers you choose, the room will certainly acquire a new look and atmosphere.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img data-id="a4436d92de1447b58ca9f41827830daa" data-uploaded="true" src="https://d2ia58550xamqo.cloudfront.net/a4436d92de1447b58ca9f41827830daa.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Fragrances for the home
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The fragrances should not be ignored when we try to create a romantic atmosphere. It is not only the St. Valentines Day when everything smells like roses, vanilla and something sweet. When preparing your home for a romantic evening, choose aroma that will suit you. Some people prefer citrus fragrances, others like the scent of sandalwood and Ylang Ylang (which works as aphrodisiacs), and others may like mountain smells of pine, eucalyptus and herbs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img data-id="4bb77ff4f8f844adb88a6cf459c8818f" data-uploaded="true" src="https://d2ia58550xamqo.cloudfront.net/4bb77ff4f8f844adb88a6cf459c8818f.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Vintage ornaments on the table
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The vintage style also brings a dose of romance. A floral tablecloth and napkins and a beautifully arranged table are sufficient to show your partner that you are trying to make the evening special. A nice meal and a bottle of red wine will make your evening even better.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img data-id="a95b68e2d3394848b918074f21ca4a02" data-uploaded="true" src="https://d2ia58550xamqo.cloudfront.net/a95b68e2d3394848b918074f21ca4a02.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           Journey home
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Not everyone has the opportunity to travel to different countries and to visit the world romantic destinations. So make your home one of those destinations. Recreate the characteristic atmosphere of Paris or Venice with the appropriate music, proper menu and some interesting film that will take you there.
        </p>
        <p>
         You may also recreate the typical Oriental atmosphere by adding some cushions on the floor. Put in the centre a board with a variety of dishes from the Eastern cuisine, light a candle and enjoy the atmosphere.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img data-id="e5bc854810e94bd49f6cc4d2ea16c6d8" data-uploaded="true" src="https://d2ia58550xamqo.cloudfront.net/e5bc854810e94bd49f6cc4d2ea16c6d8.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         We all need a little romance in our lives. You do not need a lot of money to travel or visit fancy places  everything can be done at home. You just need more imagination and enthusiasm to make your beloved ones happy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Before you make yor home the most romantic place on Eeath you have to clean it, right? Take a look at the
         <a href="https://medium.com/@Just_Eva_/the-ultimate-home-cleaning-guide-302ffe451f22" rel="nofollow">
          <strong>
           Ultimate Home Cleaning Guide.
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
