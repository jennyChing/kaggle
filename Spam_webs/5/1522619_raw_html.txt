       <h1>
        The US shutdowns
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-info">
       <div class="entry-author">
        <img alt="" height="70" src="http://www.blogbigtime.com/assets/user-uploads/userCNC165/profile/Ranjeet-Kumar-150x150.jpg" width="70"/>
        Written by
        <span>
         <a href="http://www.blogbigtime.com/view/RanjeetKumar" title="Written by Ranjeet Kumar">
          Ranjeet Kumar
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="entry-content-wrapper row">
       <!--&lt;time class='entry-date'&gt;&lt;/time&gt;-->
       <div class="entry-content pagepost">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt: auto; mso-margin-bottom-alt: auto; text-align: justify; line-height: 150%;">
         <img alt="" height="174" src="/assets/user-uploads/userCNC165/posts/Shutdown.jpg" style="float: left; margin: 5px;" width="290"/>
         Well! After a gap of a decade and a half once again, there is a disagreement on the budget of 2013-2014 as the financial year ended on 30th. As we go back to the nineties, it was President Clinton and his house of representatives that failed to agree on the budget and there was a shutdown. For those who dont have much idea about shutdown and those who are unaware of Americas democratic process, a shutdown is a part of Americas democratic process which comes to force when there is a disagreement between the two houses of representatives, the congress and the senate,in sectors such as the budget. The process of shutdown begins as the government starts shutting down all its non essential services, although federal agencies continue to function.
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt: auto; mso-margin-bottom-alt: auto; text-align: justify; line-height: 150%;">
         According to the Constitution of United States of America, the United States Congress decides the funds to be sanctioned which then needs the aprovement of the Senate and the House of Representatives and after that it goes to the president of the United States of America. And there if the president signs than these sanctions become law and if the president vetoes the bill then it again go back to the Congress where they observe the one third majority of the vote and decide and can be overridden. There were several instances when these U.S. shut downs happened in the past. During the Gerald Ford administration there were six shut down which lasted for a couple of weeks and affected majorly the labour and health sectors. Again during the period when Ronald Regan was the president of United States of America total of eight shut down happened. After that it was during the George H.W. Bush period that four shut down were in a process that lasted for four days. And the latest before this current one was during the period of President Clinton were two shutdowns happened and this was when the houses of representatives and the president Clinton fail to agree on the budget to fund federal services. These shut down functions by only shutting down the non essential services.
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt: auto; mso-margin-bottom-alt: auto; text-align: justify; line-height: 150%;">
         The social security, Medicare payments and the U.S. military will continue operating. In such cases the other services which consist of thousands of employees are asked to take leave which again will not be paid. Because of this people with the financial crisis will be affected and more over people will restrict themselves from buying more stuffs for stores that will directly impact the economy of the country. People will also struggle to meet the mortgages and credit card payments. This might be a hard hit to the countrys economy if the shutdown process goes too long. So it is expected to get resolved as soon as possible so that this does not drag too long like the one in the period of Jimmy Carter administration that lasted till 18 days. The effect of shut down is very severe sometimes as observed in the past that has forces school to get close and other utilities to non functional.
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%;">
