      <p>
       These shot glasses say the word 'drink' on them, and once you consume the sweet precious nectar that's buried below the brim of the glass and tip it over onto the counter to show how manly and fast you drank the shot, it will then say the word 'drunk'. The word drunk is designed in a special way to be an ambigram, meaning depending on what way you look at the word it will say two different things. Assuming you and your colleagues are currently holding a BAC that is able to read, this drink/drunk ambigram will certainly make you the king of the party, well that and the 8 ball of coke you have in your pocket might do it as well.
      </p>
