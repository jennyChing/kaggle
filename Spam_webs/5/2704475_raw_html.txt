          <b>
           Xolo Launches Q510S Model Smartphone with Android 4.4.2 OS Rs.6,499
          </b>
         </h2>
         <p>
          A new smartphone hits the Indian market at an affordable price of Rs 6,499 from the smartphone and tablet maker Xolo.This smartphone runs on 1.3GHz quad core processor which will help the users in the multi-tasking,gaming and browsing with an full filled experience.
         </p>
         <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          The special features of this smartphones are dual SIM slots,Micro USB,MicroSD slot and a 3.5mm audio jack.The operating system of this smartphone is Android 4.4.2 version.Its was also captured with its 5MP rear camera and front camera for operator calling purposes.
         </p>
         <p>
          The camera also provided with flash support, scene detection and tuning,face recognition, panorama,geotagging, best shot and HDR.The sensors of the smartphone are accelerometer,ambient light sensor and proximity sensor.
         </p>
