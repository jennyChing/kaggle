             <h3>
              Ingredients
             </h3>
             <br/>
             <span itemprop="ingredients">
              3 cups whole milk
              <br/>
              1/2 cup dry chocolate cake mix
              <br/>

              <br/>

              <br/>
              <br/>

             <h3>
              Directions
             </h3>
             <br/>
             <span itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              <span class="redBold">
               1)
              </span>
              Bring milk to a simmer in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat.  Whisk in cake mix until dissolved.  Add chocolate and whisk until melted.  Remove from heat and whisk in vanilla extract.

              <br/>
              <span class="redBold">
               2)
              </span>
              To rim your mugs, rub around the rims of 2 large mugs with vanilla frosting.  Dip the rim of the mugs into colorful sprinkles.

              <br/>
              Serves 2
