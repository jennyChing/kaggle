       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Stamped Typography Blocks for PSA Essentials
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2012-11-13T13:04:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 13, 2012
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://madincrafts.com/author/madincrafts/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            madincrafts
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://madincrafts.com/stamped-typography-blocks-for-psa/#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5447254" data-link="http://madincrafts.com/stamped-typography-blocks-for-psa/" data-summary="" data-title="Stamped Typography Blocks for PSA Essentials">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-4AXZyrIcrKQ/UKJE_2NwThI/AAAAAAAAQGQ/FzguiVfoXlo/s1600-h/Stamped-Typography-Block3.jpg">
          <img alt="Stamped Typography Block" border="0" height="528" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-_Qu-MM_O5r8/UKJFB42_CFI/AAAAAAAAQGY/iIEKkdgOEGU/Stamped-Typography-Block_thumb1.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Stamped Typography Block" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <a count-layout="horizontal" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.madincrafts.com%2F2012%2F11%2Fstamped-typography-blocks-for-psa.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Flh5.ggpht.com%2F-_Qu-MM_O5r8%2FUKJFB42_CFI%2FAAAAAAAAQGY%2FiIEKkdgOEGU%2FStamped-Typography-Block_thumb1.jpg%3Fimgmax%3D800&amp;description=DIY%20Stamped%20Typography%20Blocks%20-%20Mad%20in%20Crafts">
          <img border="0" src="http://assets.pinterest.com/images/PinExt.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         By now you know my fondness for including typography in our home. I have made
         <a href="http://www.madincrafts.com/2011/11/upcycled-house-number-wall-art/" target="_blank">
          Number Wall Art
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.madincrafts.com/2012/02/decorative-typography-spheres/" target="_blank">
          Decorative Spheres
         </a>
         , an
         <a href="http://www.madincrafts.com/2011/09/typographical-globe-with-upcycled/" target="_blank">
          Art Globe
         </a>
         , and more DIY projects to bring in the fun of typography to our living spaces. I used my new PSA Essentials stamps to create some personalized typographic dcor that was so easy to make.
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          To make Stamped Typography Blocks, you will need:
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          wood blocks
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          dictionary pages
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000YQKLO0/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000YQKLO0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=madincra-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Mod Podge for paper
          </a>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          paint
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          paintbrushes
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          paper cutter or scissors
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.psaessentials.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           PSA Essentials
          </a>
          stamps and inks
         </em>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000DEZL9/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0000DEZL9&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=madincra-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Dremel
          </a>
          with sanding attachment or sandpaper
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-gyhowVWd3G0/UKJFEnTsbLI/AAAAAAAAQGg/QYoQJQO_Z5E/s1600-h/country-goose.jpg">
          <img alt="country goose" border="0" height="480" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-yRGokupya4E/UKJFG5sYbXI/AAAAAAAAQGo/gG7ykwtFx0I/country-goose_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="country goose" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         I found these blocks at Goodwill a year or so ago. WHY, HELLO, KERCHIEF-WEARING GOOSE! You could also cut scrap wood into 3x3 cubes if you cant find blocks to use.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-bQ-RoGtc1Eo/UKJFJYgZXYI/AAAAAAAAQGw/Qarc7SH4UpE/s1600-h/paint-colors.jpg">
          <img alt="paint colors" border="0" height="480" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-K190xhBIk2s/UKJFLRjNpnI/AAAAAAAAQG4/jq4ifxdeQ_w/paint-colors_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="paint colors" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         I gave the blocks a quick facelift using some paint samples from Glidden in colors that coordinate with our living room (Almost Aqua, Spanish Olive, Bittersweet Chocolate) .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-Y2IJntZ-jB0/UKJFN0p_m9I/AAAAAAAAQHA/173ovaZOIxc/s1600-h/painted-blocks.jpg">
          <img alt="painted blocks" border="0" height="480" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-IsLCAbg5nRo/UKJFP9uyWpI/AAAAAAAAQHI/X66TYQ1RfWk/painted-blocks_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="painted blocks" width="480"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         Much better, I think. Next, I cut squares of dictionary pages to fit each side of the three cubes. I used the Vanilla Peel &amp; Stick sets for these blocks and stamped the letters with plain black ink. Each cube has the first initial of each family member, our last initial, and an ampersand on the 6 sides. Then I used
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000YQKLO0/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000YQKLO0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=madincra-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Paper Mod Podge
         </a>
         to glue the dictionary pages in place. Finally I roughed up the edges using the sanding attachment on my
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000DEZL9/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0000DEZL9&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=madincra-20">
          Dremel
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-ZyOD1TQWrGw/UKJFR-MHYpI/AAAAAAAAQHQ/AhEiWcub5uI/s1600-h/stamped-typography-blocks-square4.jpg">
          <img alt="stamped typography blocks square" border="0" height="500" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-qJmcHF8-IYA/UKJFTsi02NI/AAAAAAAAQHY/35lDz0BoMs8/stamped-typography-blocks-square_thu.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="stamped typography blocks square" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont you think this would be a cute gift for a new home?
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-XiMvG8-9Nos/UKJFWByHhWI/AAAAAAAAQHg/o7W3-zUs4ac/s1600-h/stamped-typography-on-blocks.jpg">
          <img alt="stamped typography on blocks" border="0" height="500" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-1IizDldk5WY/UKJFYXeff0I/AAAAAAAAQHo/oA1-IfCxPBs/stamped-typography-on-blocks_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="stamped typography on blocks" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The blocks look adorable next to my vintage typewriter. Do you use typography in your home decorating?
        </p>
