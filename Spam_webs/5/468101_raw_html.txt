       <p>
        <a href="http://cdn2.us4palin.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Household.Groceries.jpg">

       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Earlier, I posted
         <a href="http://www.patheos.com/blogs/bristolpalin/2014/06/instead-of-helping-witnesses-film-crash-victims-as-they-burned-to-death/">
          this terrible story about how onlookers filmed people burning in a car crash instead of trying to save their lives
         </a>
         .
         <span id="more-5444">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Today, I saw this article about how a mom was killed in an accident, leaving her two small children in the backseat relatively unharmed.
         <span style="font-size: 13px;">
          According to eyewitnesses, people who saw the tragic accident
          <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.chron.com/news/houston-texas/houston/article/Onlookers-allegedly-steal-groceries-from-woman-5531076.php" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">

          :
         </span>
        </p>

         <p>
          She was pronounced dead at the scene. The driver of the Mercedes was not injured.
         </p>
         <p>
          Senties said if investigators determine items were stolen from the vehicle they could pursue criminal charges against the perpetrators, but he said police did not have any information in about stolen groceries.
