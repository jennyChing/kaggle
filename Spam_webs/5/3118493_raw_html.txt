      <header class="entry-header wrapper">
       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         5-Ingredient Orange Julius
        </span>
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="entry-author vcard author">
         <span class="fn">
          <img alt="Kayley McCabe" class="avatar avatar-36 wp-user-avatar wp-user-avatar-36 alignnone photo" height="36" src="http://media3.handmadecharlotte.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/mccabe-197x197.jpg" width="36"/>
          By
          <a href="http://www.handmadecharlotte.com/author/kmccabe/" rel="author">
           Kayley McCabe
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="entry-date updated">
         February 16, 2015
        </span>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8">
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <img alt="Easy Orange Julius Recipe for Kids " class="alignnone wp-image-50106 size-full" height="1038" src="http://media3.handmadecharlotte.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kayley_Orange.jpg" width="690"/>
          <p>
           Wintertime means an abundance of citrus! Especially oranges. They are easiest to find, priced at their lowest, and most flavorful this time of year. Not only that, but they pack a powerful punch of vitamin C, something we could all use right about now. Why not make a creamy orange julius with all that fresh juice at your fingertips?
          </p>
          <p>
           This version is tangier and healthier than those made with concentrates and it only calls for five simple ingredients. Orange Greek yogurt gives the drink a nice and creamy texture, though vanilla would work just as well if you cant find orange. My kids thought this made the most dreamy after-school snack. I agree!
          </p>
          <img alt="Easy Orange Julius Recipe for Kids " class="alignnone wp-image-50102 size-full" height="452" src="http://media3.handmadecharlotte.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kayley_Orange_Materials.jpg" width="690"/>
          <div class="sc-title">
           <h2 class="title">
            <span>
             What You Need
            </span>
           </h2>
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li>
            20 ice cubes, or as needed
           </li>
           <li>
            1/4 cup sugar
           </li>
           <li>
            1 teaspoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li>
            1/2 cup Greek yogurt, orange
           </li>
           <li>
            2cups orange juice, freshly squeezed
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="sc-title">
           <h2 class="title">
            <span>
             How-To
            </span>
           </h2>
          </div>
          <img alt="Easy Orange Julius Recipe for Kids " class="alignnone wp-image-50104 size-full" height="459" src="http://media2.handmadecharlotte.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kayley_Orange_Scooping.jpg" width="690"/>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Step 1
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Place the ice cubes in a blender. You may need more or less depending on how big your cubes are. Once the drink is blended, you can add in more ice cubes to achieve a slushier drink.
          </p>
          <img alt="Easy Orange Julius Recipe for Kids " class="alignnone wp-image-50105 size-full" height="459" src="http://media3.handmadecharlotte.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kayley_Orange_Squeezing.jpg" width="690"/>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Step 2
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Add the sugar, vanilla, yogurt, and orange juice into the blender. Blend on high for about 1 minute, or until the drink is well-blended and frothy.Serve right away.
          </p>
          <img alt="Easy Orange Julius Recipe for Kids " class="alignnone wp-image-50103 size-full" height="955" src="http://media3.handmadecharlotte.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Kayley_Orange_Drinking.jpg" width="690"/>
          <p>
           Enjoy!
          </p>
