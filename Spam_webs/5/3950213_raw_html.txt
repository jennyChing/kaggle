        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Caramel Apple Cheesecake Bars: My October Secret Recipe Club Entry
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-10-17T06:00:38+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 17, 2011
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://busy-at-home.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Glenda Embree
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://busy-at-home.com/caramel-apple-cheesecake-bars-my-october-secret-recipe-club-entry/#comments">
           51 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 430px">
         <img alt="caramel apple cheesecake bars" height="280" src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/served.jpg" title="cheesecake bars" width="420"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          These taste like MORE! :)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This month I was assigned to check out all the recipes at
         <a href="http://www.deliciousexistence.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.deliciousexistence.com', 'Delicious Existence');" target="_blank">
          Delicious Existence
         </a>
         and choose one to make my own, for this months
         <a href="http://www.secretrecipeclub.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.secretrecipeclub.com', 'Secret Recipe Club');" target="_blank">
          Secret Recipe Club
         </a>
         . The majority of Danielles recipes are raw, vegan or vegetarian, so there were tons of healthy options to choose from, but wouldnt you know one of her desserts was just calling my name.
         <a href="http://www.deliciousexistence.com/7/post/2011/10/caramel-apple-cheesecake-bonus-maple-caramel-sauce-recipe-included.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.deliciousexistence.com/7/post/2011/10/caramel-apple-cheesecake-bonus-maple-caramel-sauce-recipe-included.html', 'Caramel Apple Cheesecake Bars');" target="_blank">
          Caramel Apple Cheesecake Bars
         </a>
         looked and sounded like the perfect dessert for this brisk fall weather weve been having, so I began my baking adventure. I had to laugh, reading through her post, as it turns out this recipe was one she made for her Secret Recipe Club selection.
        </p>
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1178758925615690" data-ad-slot="9800722080" style="display:inline-block;width:468px;height:60px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         I dont have many of the ingredients in my kitchen that Danielle used in her original recipe, but I was able to substitute things from my own kitchen with great results. This recipe turned out absolutely delicious and well definitely be making it again, though I think we will substitute our favorite cheesecake crust recipe, next time. Ill share that soon in an upcoming post, but for now, on to the recipe as I put it together for Octobers Secret Recipe Club Challenge.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSRatings" itemprop="aggregateRating" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/AggregateRating">

         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Delicious dessert to usher in fall!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            24 bars
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Crust Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups white whole wheat flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup butter, softened, but not melted
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Filling Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 (8 oz) blocks cream cheese, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 large eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Tablespoon Madagascar Bourbon Vanilla extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Apple Topping Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, &amp; finely chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            (Streusel Topping Ingredients)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup oats
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup butter, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup King Arthur Flour
            <a href="http://www.kingarthurflour.com/shop/items/cinnamon-mini-baking-chips-16-oz" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.kingarthurflour.com/shop/items/cinnamon-mini-baking-chips-16-oz', 'cinnamon chips');" target="_blank">
             cinnamon chips
            </a>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup chopped pecans
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Drizzle Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             -  cup of your favorite caramel sauce
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cover a 9x13" cake pan with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            For the crust:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine the butter, flour and brown sugar, until mixture is crumbly and evenly distributed.  You could use a pastry blender or forks to do this by hand.  I used my stand mixer.  A few pulses in a food processor would work, as well.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/crustingredients.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Press into foil lined cake pan and bake for 15 minutes, or until lightly browned.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/pressinpan.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            For the cheesecake filling:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In the bowl of your stand mixer, beat all the filling ingredients until smooth and creamy.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/fillingingredients.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pour the filling over your warm crust.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/fillingoncrust.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            For the apple filling:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir together chopped apples, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/appleingredients.jpg"/>
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/mincedapples.jpg"/>
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/appletopping.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spoon evenly over cheesecake mixture.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/applesonfilling.jpg"/>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           For the streusel topping:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            I used my stand mixer, again, to mix all the streusel ingredients together, but it could just as easily be mixed with a fork.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/streuselingredients.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Sprinkle streusel topping over apple mixture.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/streuselonapples.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from oven and drizzle with caramel topping.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/caramel.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Allow to cool and then set in refrigerator.  (This is at it's flavor-best when served cold.)
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.busy-at-home.com/images/apple/served.jpg"/>
           </li>
          </ol>
