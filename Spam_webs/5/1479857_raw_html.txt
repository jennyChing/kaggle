        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Meet Julia
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="author vcard">
          Article by
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://fitblogger.ca/author/guest-blogger/" rel="author">
            Guest Contributor
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Hi there! How are you?
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Well, if you are not familiar with myself or
          <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/">
           my blog
          </a>
          , let me introduce myself.
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="text-align: center; clear: both;">
          <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_atqA235lqBM/TUiOvQkhroI/AAAAAAAAB-0/cfO3yMRDSvA/s1600/photo_3-2.JPG" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_atqA235lqBM/TUiOvQkhroI/AAAAAAAAB-0/cfO3yMRDSvA/s320/photo_3-2.JPG" width="320"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          My name is Julia; better known online as
          <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/">
           JewliaGoulia
          </a>
          . I am a 26 year old, flip-flop wearing, iPhone addicted, wife, homeowner, and adoptive mother to a 14 year old American Pit Bull Terrier. I am obsessed with leopard print, my flower of choice is the Orchid, and I love the scent of vanilla. Oh. . . my favorite color? Black, of course.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Currently I am a junior-level college student at the
          <a href="http://www.miad.edu/">
           Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design
          </a>
          . My major is Photography. At this time, the range of my photographic work is broad; I shoot both
          <a href="http://juliakozerskifineart.com/">
           fine-art photography
          </a>
          as well as
          <a href="http://juliakozerski.com/">
           editorial photography
          </a>
          .
         </div>
         <p>
          Outside of the realms of school and work, I am like any other normal college student. Well. . . not really. I have struggled with my weight for almost my entire life and last year I decided to do something about it. So far, I have lost nearly 150 lbs. and have documented my entire journey on
          <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/">
           my blog
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          I started my self-titled blog on December 11, 2009 just three days before I publicly declared that I would be embarking on a whole new chapter of my life involving healthy eating, exercise, and weight loss. In all honesty, at that time, I was just looking for a challenge. I always knew I was overweight and had been privately been suffering from related depression and bipolar disorder for years. Again, in the spirit of full disclosure, I gave myself a 0% chance of ever successfully losing weight, getting healthy, or even maintaining my blog. (I didnt have the greatest history with following through on things.)
         </p>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="line-height: 22px; font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
          </span>
         </div>
         <p>
          Luckily for me, my blog and I survived that three day grace period and on December 14th, 2009 I officially started my journey.
         </p>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <p>
          My first task along this journey was to write
          <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2009/12/big-fat-ugly-truth.html">
           an initial post
          </a>
          laying everything on the line for both my reader(s) and for myself. When I saw my statistics (338 lbs., age 25, pant size 28/30, and a BMI of 49.9,) I finally realized just how out of control I had let my weight and health get. From then on, I understood that I needed to take this journey seriously. It was literally a life or death situation.
         </p>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
            <strong>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="text-align: center; clear: both;">
          <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_atqA235lqBM/TUiPWJLfKiI/AAAAAAAAB-4/zU96bshQN7w/s1600/JULIA+-+December%252C+14th%252C+2009.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="231" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_atqA235lqBM/TUiPWJLfKiI/AAAAAAAAB-4/zU96bshQN7w/s400/JULIA+-+December%252C+14th%252C+2009.jpg" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
            <strong>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
            Despite all odds (and my own serious doubts) I have maintained not only my blog, but my weight-loss and healthy-living journey as well. To date, I have lost 148.2 pounds and have shed 138
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 22px; font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           inches
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 22px; font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           (or 11 1/2 FEET) from my body.
          </span>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="line-height: 22px; font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="separator" style="text-align: center; clear: both;">
          <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_atqA235lqBM/TUiPdFR9t8I/AAAAAAAAB-8/sTahTAh2Ih0/s1600/JULIA+-+February+1st%252C+2011+%252872dpi%2529.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="232" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_atqA235lqBM/TUiPdFR9t8I/AAAAAAAAB-8/sTahTAh2Ih0/s400/JULIA+-+February+1st%252C+2011+%252872dpi%2529.jpg" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
            With statistics like that, it mustnt be surprising to find that the last year of my life has been an absolute roller-coaster.
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
           <span style="line-height: 22px;">
            I am so honored to be able to share all of my experiences openly and honestly (
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/05/katie-from-katie-j-is-on-her-way-and.html">
             maybe too honestly
            </a>
            ) with people from all over the world. My friends (blog readers) have shared essentially every part of my journey with me. They were with me at my first
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2009/12/weight-in-1-crying.html">
             weigh-in
            </a>
            , they were there when my newlywed husband and I chose to
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/01/outlaws_15.html">
             estrange ourselves
            </a>
            from my in-laws, they
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/05/friendapalooza-giveaway.html">

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/06/want-to-get-into-my-pants.html">
             in

             some
            </a>
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/06/giveaway-in-exchange-for-your-help.html">
             awesome
            </a>
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/05/friendapalooza-giveaway.html">
             giveaways
            </a>
            , they read some
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/05/review-fitnessbuilder.html">
             product reviews
            </a>

            <a href="http://www.eatingjourney.com/exposed/">
             exposed
            </a>
            myself to them, not
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/02/exposed-take-it-or-leave-it.html">
             once
            </a>
            , but
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/10/happy-anniversary-exposed-movement.html">
             twice
            </a>
            ! They were there during the happy times, eating
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/02/eat-me.html">
             tasty
            </a>
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/07/week-in-review.html">
             recipes

            , participating in the

            </a>
            , and participating in my
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/05/rock-n-run-recap.html">
             first
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/06/weigh-in-25-upaf-recap-other.html">
             second
            </a>
            fitness-based charity events. My friends witnessed my continued attempts to 
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/07/weigh-in-32-photos-galore.html">
             girlify
            </a>
             myself, they celebrated my husband and

             my First Wedding Anniversary
            </a>

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/07/challenge-we-gon-party-like-its-my.html">
             birthday

            to ride
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/07/challenge-results.html">

            (in less then a week) on my favorite new toy, my Electra Townie bicycle.

           <span style="line-height: 22px;">

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/03/holy.html">

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/07/100-lbs-lost-how-i-did-it.html">

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/12/ramp-up-your-fitness.html">
             a fitness regimen
            </a>
            , and my epic entrance into 
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/12/weigh-in-50-awesome-stuff.html">
             Onederland
            </a>

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/05/inside-my-head-not-pretty-picture.html">
             negative self-talk
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/12/i-pulled-trigger_29.html">
             feelings of worthlessness
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/12/binge-recap.html">
             eating binges
            </a>
            , my
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/06/reason-for-denial-heightweight.html">
             rejection for healthcare coverage
            </a>
            , my
            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/06/6-month-journey-anniversary-present.html">
             emergency

            <a href="http://jewliagoulia.blogspot.com/2010/06/weigh-in-27-recovery-progress.html">

          <span style="line-height: 22px; font-family: arial, helvetica; color: #333333;">
