        <a class="title" href="http://www.boredpanda.com/lane-duck-path-london-share-the-space/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Ducks Get Their Own Duck Lanes  Near the Canal Walkways in London">
         Ducks Get Their Own Duck Lanes  Near the Canal Walkways in London
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info-block">
        <div class="left post-info">

        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Duck lanes along waterway paths in London, Birmingham, and Manchester are a temporary new initiative to encourage peopleto be mindful of the animals they share the road with when walking or biking. The Canal &amp; River Trust has painted a white line marking off areas to be used by birds with a stenciled silhouette of a duck. You can follow them at #sharethespace.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Weve painted in duck lanes on the tow path, just to highlight[that] theres only so much space you can share..[a]nd actually probably ducks need the priority, Dick Vincent, a London Towpath Ranger told ruptly.tv
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The Canal River Trust is responsible for maintaining over 2000 miles (3220km) of inland waterways in England and Wales. Their motto is share the space; drop your pace; its a special place. Whether or not the ducks will agree to abide by these rules is another matter.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         More info:
         <a href="https://canalrivertrust.org.uk/news-and-views/features/share-the-space-drop-your-pace-our-towpath-code" target="_blank">
          canalrivertrust.org.uk
         </a>
         (h/t:
