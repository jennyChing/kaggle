           <div class="panel entry-content" id="tab-description">
            <h2>
             Product Description
            </h2>
            <p>
             Are you looking for a blade to cut life fibers, but dont want the headache and hassle of a life consuming quest to find your fathers killer and save the world from clothing? Look no further!
            </p>
            <p>
             The replica kit is cast in SmoothCast 320 urethane plastic and measures 48 long from tip to handle end. Just like a real scissor, the blade is completely flat on one side! Unlike a real scissor, the blade isnt (dangerously) sharp! Each casting has a 1/4 steel rod embedded down the length of the blade and into the upper handle to ensure the entire weapon is durable and strong. Final castings weigh approximately 3.5lbs and will require sanding and trimming before primer and paint.
            </p>
            <p>
             The kit will arrive untrimmed with the rear hinge clip piece attached to casting sprues inside the handle area, as shown in the photos of the pink blade in the attached gallery. This part will cover the pour and vent lines on the finished assembled piece.
            </p>
