     <br/>
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <span>
      Andy Murray is currently shouldering the burden of expectation at Wimbledon and although the worlds most famous tennis competition is synonymous with strawberries and cream, Belvedere Vodka would like to share a sweet and refreshing long drink cocktail recipe, to celebrate the championships.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <em>
       <strong>
        Did you know:
       </strong>
       The All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club was founded in 1868 and Wimbledon is subsequently the oldest Grand slam in tennis, dating back to 1877.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The first singles event was won by Spencer Gore in 1877.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Maud Watson was the first ladies singles champion in 1884.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       A staggering 28,000kg of strawberries are consumed during Wimbledon fortnight, along with over 7,000 litres of fresh cream.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The outbreak of World War II forced Wimbledons cancellation, as civil defence and military personnel replaced elite tennis players inside the All-England Club. On October 11, 1940, German bombs struck a corner of Centre Court stands and destroyed 1,200 seats. After the resumption of the tournament in 1946, the seats remained out of commission and the grounds were not fully repaired until 1949. Since World War II Wimbledon has retained a military presence as each year over a hundred uniformed members of the armed forces volunteer as stewards.
      </em>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <div>
       The Belvedere Wimbledon Fizz is a great cocktail recipe, made with fresh grapefruit, POM juice and strawberries  this cocktail will give you an ultimate boost of fruity flavours with a fizzy finish to quench those summer thirsts.
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
       <strong>
        <u>
         Belvedere Wimbledon Fizz Recipe
        </u>
       </strong>
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <u>
       Ingredients
      </u>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      140ml Belvedere Pink Grapefruit Vodka
      <br/>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
       30ml Pomegranate Juice
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
       Two dashes of Lavender bitters
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
       Two whole strawberries
      </div>
      <div>
      </div>
      <div>
       Fever Tree Tonic top
      </div>
      <br/>
      <u>
       Method
      </u>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Muddle the strawberries in a cocktail shaker then add the Belvedere Pink Grapefruit vodka, POM juice and lavender bitters
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Shake and strain into a highball glass filled with crushed ice
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Top with Fever Tree tonic
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <div>
       Garnish with a mint sprig.
      </div>
