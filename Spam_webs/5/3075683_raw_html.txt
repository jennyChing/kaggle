            <h1>
             WEEKEND LIFE: Personal Vanity
            </h1>
           </div>
           <div class="boxContent">
            <div class="productDetail">
             <span class="image">
              <a href="/images/200001536-3e2f33f29b/Vanity-Tables.jpg" onclick="return !window.open(this.href);" title="Page will display in a new window.">
               <img alt="WEEKEND LIFE: Personal Vanity" height="270" src="http://files.loveweb2.webnode.com/system_preview_detail_200001536-3e2f33f29b/Vanity-Tables.jpg" width="270"/>
              </a>

             <div class="text">
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                Weekends are here and so what is your plan? Sometimes we feel like staying at home only to stay out from the heat and traffic. So while at home thinkfor extra attention to some parts of your house or apartment.Let us take a lookat your bedroom. Your bedroom is the best place for a rest, where you can sleep with all the comfort and privacy there is. There is something more that you need to check - your vanity drawers.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                1.
                <u>
                 <em>
                  <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                   <strong>
                    Make up kits
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                 </em>
                </u>
                . Cosmetics like lipsticks, blush on, face powder, facial tissues and masks should be properly kept in a separate drawer. Unused and old cosmetics that you are not using anymore must be discarded. Keep your cosmetics dust-free and in good condition. Sometimes they cause skin irritation or boil in the lips if they are expired.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                2.
                <u>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                    Accessories
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </u>
                . Necklaces, earrings and bracelets, be they are gold or made of pearls, they should have their own area. Whether you have them displayed on top of your vanity or kept in their own jewelry boxes, place them in safer place like drawers with locks or arranged in a jewely stand. Earrings are smaller details so maybe it's better they are placed in a compartment box.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                3.
                <u>
                 <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                  <em>
                   <strong>
                    Electrical accessories
                   </strong>
                  </em>
                 </span>
                </u>
                . Hair blower, flat hair iron, shavers should be placed in a separate drawer too. In case you use extension wires, place them altogether in one compartment.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                4.
                <u>
                 <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                  <strong>
                   <em>
                    Hair accessories
                   </em>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </u>
                .Hair brush, combs, hairpins, hair clips, ribbons and head bands should be arranged in a way that you can easily reach for them. Remove stuck hairs in combs and hair brush beforewashing. Smaller things can be in compartment box inside a drawer.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                5.
                <strong>
                 <em>
                  <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                   <u>
                    Perfumes
                   </u>
                   .
                  </span>
                 </em>
                </strong>
                <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                 <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                  You can place your bottles of perfumes or colognes on top of your vanity. Needless to keep them inside a drawer, depending on your choice. Be sure they are not too many and combined with empty bottles. Throw away those unusable empty bottles and make room for the ones you are always using. If you want to keep the vintage bottle from your late grandmother then you can keep it aside inside a drawer.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   Little things mean a lot.
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
                 <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                  Keep your personal vanity clean and organized, ready for the next weekdays of your life.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
