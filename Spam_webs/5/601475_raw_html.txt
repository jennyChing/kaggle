         <h1 class="blog-h1">
          What I LOVED at Maison &amp; Objet Paris
         </h1>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="grid_8">
        <img src="http://www.sarahwardassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-29-at-10.12.25-455x296.png"/>
       </div>
       <div class="grid_12">
        <p>
         Just back from my trip to the
         <em>
          wonderful inspiration for interior design
         </em>
         that is Maison &amp; Objet in Paris.
        </p>
        <p>
         I spent a happy couple of days browsing, finding some intriguing ideas that Im going to use in some new interior design projects Im working on at the moment. Heres a few Id like to share with you.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 789px">
         <a href="http://www.aquamass.com/images/design/slideshow/access/access-big-35-d-lighting-1-slide@2x.jpg">
          <img alt="green neon bathtub " class=" " height="403" src="http://www.aquamass.com/images/design/slideshow/access/access-big-35-d-lighting-1-slide@2x.jpg" width="779"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Childrens bathtime will never be the same. Bathtub by Aquamass
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.aquamass.com/en/access/access/big-35-d-lighting" target="_blank">
          Bathtub by Aquamass
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This stopped me in my tracks and immediately got me thinking. Ideal for an ultra-modern property and a client who wants a definite wow factor. It would work well as a freestanding bath in the master bedroom  but also would be a dream in a childrens bathroom.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 636px">
         <a href="http://www.atmosphere-bois.com/pc/images/maison-bois/maison-de-jardin/maison-019.jpg">
          <img alt="reclaimed timber garden house " height="470" src="http://www.atmosphere-bois.com/pc/images/maison-bois/maison-de-jardin/maison-019.jpg" width="626"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Garden house that would work beautifully as a home office by Atmosphere &amp; Bois
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.atmosphere-bois.com/uk/house-in-old-wood/garden-house" target="_blank">
          Wooden pavilions by Atmosphere Bois
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Working from home is on the rise and Ive a client who has asked me to create a dedicated workspace in the garden. So I was delighted when I spotted some beautiful wooden pavilions (they are most definitely not sheds!) by Atmosphere Bois. Created from reclaimed wood, they blend into the surroundings, avoiding the garishness of new timber. Im looking forward to being able to furnish an interior like this.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1476" style="width: 626px">
         <img alt="large cast bronze apple" class="size-full wp-image-1476 " height="581" src="http://www.sarahwardassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-28-at-09.47.04.png" width="616"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Bumper harvest. Cast bronze apple by Bull &amp; Stein.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bullstein.com/files/castbronze.pdf" target="_blank">
          Cast Bronze by Bull &amp; Stein
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Whilst on the subject of exteriors, I adored these oversized (and they were large) cast bronze apples by Bull &amp; Stein. Adds a contemporary touch to a formal garden  I also think they would work well around a swimming pool. They draw the eye in a good way and also soften the lines of a structured, minimal area.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 626px">
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-gZ-Rkw4HFI4/Tifvjcfp89I/AAAAAAAAAPA/vYJZicV3HcQ/s1600/etienne1.jpg">
          <img alt="decorative wooden wall panels " class=" " height="305" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-gZ-Rkw4HFI4/Tifvjcfp89I/AAAAAAAAAPA/vYJZicV3HcQ/s1600/etienne1.jpg" width="616"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Wooden wall panels by Etienne Moyat
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.etienne-moyat.fr/en/collections/#mg" target="_blank">
          Sculpted Wall Panels by Etienne Moyat
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Created by a true artist, these wooden wall panels come in the most compelling designs and give a natural, organic energy to a surface.
