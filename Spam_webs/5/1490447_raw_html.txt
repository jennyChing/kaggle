             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              Skinny Chocolate Fruit Dip Recipe
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-394350962392822707" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-nAzbHPUi2YA/VQ__99uN40I/AAAAAAAAK7c/eN3cscAPLDQ/s1600/Fruit%2BDip.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="502" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-nAzbHPUi2YA/VQ__99uN40I/AAAAAAAAK7c/eN3cscAPLDQ/s1600/Fruit%2BDip.jpg" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                I recently had a party for my daughter and some of her school friends. Now when I party, I like to do it up and that includes a well thought out menu. My menus usually include finger type sandwiches, vegetables, fruit and of course something sweet to go along with it. Now I am not going to lie, a lot of times I buy those prepackaged fruits trays that always come with a fruit dip. For this party, I was already stocked up with fruit in my refrigerator, so I only needed to find a recipe for an EASY fruit dip to accompany my fruit tray. I stress easy because I am a busy working mom and ain't nobody got time to prepare too much from scratch. Well, low and behold I came across this awesome chocolate fruit dip recipe that was not only easy but also SKINNY! And to top it off, it was delicious. So, of course I had to share this recipe with my favorite readers:
               </span>
              </div>
              <a name="more">
              </a>
              <br/>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                <b>
                 <u>
                  Skinny Chocolate Fruit Dip Recipe
                 </u>
                </b>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Ingredients
                </b>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                1 box Sugar-Free Instant Chocolate Pudding
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                1 (12 oz) Container of Lite Cool Whip
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                1 (16 oz) container of Vanilla Greek Yogurt
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 Directions
                </b>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <b>
                 <br/>
                </b>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                1. Blend all ingredients with a hand mixer.
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                2. Refrigerate about 1 hour before serving.
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                3. Enjoy!
               </span>
              </div>
