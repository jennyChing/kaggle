Assuming it does -- it's still not 100% final -- this will almost certainly become the brand's best-selling model. That's thanks to an added dash of practicality from its two full-size rear doors and back seats.

         <a href="http://money.cnn.com/2014/12/04/autos/lamborghini-plug-in-hybrid/">
          potentially, be offered a plug-in hybrid model
