      5000th Time Looking at Mona Lisa
     </h1>

     <h3 class="subtitle">
     </h3>
     <div class="inner_content">
      <p>
       She has hair,
       <br/>
       eyes,foldedhands, wry smile
       <br/>
       Shes lazy
       <br/>
       Only sits there
       <br/>
       What a waste
       <br/>
       Who cares
       <br/>
       Shes ugly, tiny
       <br/>
       just a person
       <br/>
       no dining savior
       <br/>
       no night sky
       <br/>
       no scary moment
       <br/>
       no melting clocks
       <br/>
       Whocares
       <br/>
       I cant make my masterpeace
       <br/>
       With a piece of shit like this
      </p>
