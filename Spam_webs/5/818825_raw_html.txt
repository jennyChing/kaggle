                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Drive M7 Aspar Honda MotoGP Retains Britains Leon Camier as Reserve Rider
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-435351105091344150" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      According to the latest updated the Drive M7 Aspar Honda Moto GP team has decided to retain the 2009 British Super bike Champion
                      <a href="http://www.bikesportnews.com/news-detail.cfm?newstitle=Camier-retained-as-Aspar-Honda-MotoGP-reserve-rider&amp;newsid=12230" target="_blank">
                       Leon Camier as a reserve rider
                      </a>
                      . As a result of this decision, now Camier will be on hand to replace Nicky Hayden or his teammate Hiro Aoyama whenever the need arise. The British rider was also available to ride for Drive M7 Aspar in place of Hayden the seventh round of the Moto GP World Champion. However, in the end Aspar Honda decided to go with Hayden as his wrist was all right, but the team also kept Camier with them just in case if they may need him.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://s3.yimg.com/bt/api/res/1.2/CMTps5XmEky5mAhpdFUbQg--/YXBwaWQ9eW5ld3M7Zmk9ZmlsbDtoPTU0MDtweW9mZj0wO3E9NzU7dz05NjA-/http://media.zenfs.com/en_GB/Sports/Eurosport/1263747-27266561-2560-1440.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="https://s3.yimg.com/bt/api/res/1.2/CMTps5XmEky5mAhpdFUbQg--/YXBwaWQ9eW5ld3M7Zmk9ZmlsbDtoPTU0MDtweW9mZj0wO3E9NzU7dz05NjA-/http://media.zenfs.com/en_GB/Sports/Eurosport/1263747-27266561-2560-1440.jpg" width="590"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://s3.yimg.com/bt/api/res/1.2/CMTps5XmEky5mAhpdFUbQg--/YXBwaWQ9eW5ld3M7Zmk9ZmlsbDtoPTU0MDtweW9mZj0wO3E9NzU7dz05NjA-/http://media.zenfs.com/en_GB/Sports/Eurosport/1263747-27266561-2560-1440.jpg" target="_blank">
                       Image
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Now Camier is a full-time ride for the remaining 2014 season, but he is not going to latch onto any racing opportunity just to sate himself. The 2005 British Super sport Champion said while talking to
                      <a href="http://www.bikesportnews.com/" target="_blank">
                       bikesportnews
                      </a>
                      that if he will race during this year, he would look to have right package that should be competitive and have a god squad around it. Leon said that he already had racing offers from IDM both in Britain and Germany along with World Super bikes, but he wants them to be right. Otherwise, he would not mind being just available for the Aspar Honda squad and securing a nice ride for 2015.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://www.bikesrepublic.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/146-1204-03-o+WSB-imola-test-leon-camier+.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="430" src="http://www.bikesrepublic.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/146-1204-03-o+WSB-imola-test-leon-camier+.jpg" width="590"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://www.bikesrepublic.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/146-1204-03-o+WSB-imola-test-leon-camier+.jpg" target="_blank">
                        Image
                       </a>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Adding to it, Camier said that it would be terrific if he gets an opportunity to race in Moto GP, but it appears that Haydens wrist is fine. In this case Nicky is obviously the best choice for the team, said Leon. Hayden had to go under the knife after he faced severe pain in his right wrist. The American rider was bearing this pain in his wrist for the past two or three rounds, but it got more severe just before French Grand Prix. It is when Nick decided to take his doctors advice and went through
                      <a href="http://nickyhayden.com/2014/06/04/nicky-hayden-undergoes-successful-surgery-in-italy" target="_blank">
                       anarthroscopic cleaning procedure
                      </a>
                      at the start of this month.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://media.crash.net/original/PA1463184.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="390" src="http://media.crash.net/original/PA1463184.jpg" width="590"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <a href="http://media.crash.net/original/PA1463184.jpg" target="_blank">
                        Image
                       </a>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Though, Hayden had undergone a major surgery, but in spite of it he was able to recover just before the Spanish Grand Prix. That completely diminished Camiers chances to ride Aspars machine during this season. Now it remains to be seen how long Camier have to wait to ride Aspar Hondas bike or find a competitive deal where he gets to ride with a good squad.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      In contrast to Leon, riders who have been looking to buy a top quality motorcycle accessory or clothing in the UK or Europe will not have to wait too long to get their hands on their desired product. As,
                      <a href="http://jsaccessories.co.uk/" target="_blank">
                       J&amp;S Accessories
                      </a>
                      outlets and online store has made shopping more affordable for bikers by making top motorcycle brands clothing and accessories available at retail price.
