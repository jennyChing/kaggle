        <p>
         Asking the universe to answer to your needs is a bigthing. What is often overlooked is that we are more than capable of answering our needs ourselves.
        </p>
        <p>
         Is not about sending vibrations out to the universe and it answering your needs. The act of asking the universe and sending the right vibrational message is a half truth. Asking the universe might work, but its not the universe answering its the version of you that is ready, doing the right things and making the right actions.
        </p>
        <p>
         The real truth is the process you put yourself through when you ask yourself the deep questions and picture your life as it would be. That process clarifies and crystallises out the steps you need and want to take to your deep down subconscious. Its about knowing what you want so your heart, soul and brain are ready to act upon things without delay.
        </p>
        <p>
         You have to look inwards and understand what you want from life and actually understand what you want. With knowledge of your true self you can really act in your own best interest.
        </p>
        <p>
         When opportunities arise, and they do every day, you will be in a place to just step forward and go with it. Wise up, understand yourself, ask the questions and then you will be ready to get what you want. If not, you will keep missing those opportunities.
        </p>
