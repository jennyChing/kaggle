       <p>
        Whats your
        <a href="http://harrypotter.wikia.com/wiki/Patronus_Charm" target="_blank">
         patronus
        </a>
        ? Artist
        <a href="http://alicia-mb.tumblr.com/post/90349337593/just-one-of-those-things-that-i-always-wondered" target="_blank">
         Alicia Braumberger
        </a>
        created this whimsical (and sometimes awesomely adorably) Spotters Guide to Rare and Unusual Patronuses, which makes us wish a person could have more than one:
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Just one of those things that I always wondered about. Stags and otters are all very well, but what if you end up with a tiny chameleon or giant blue whale? I mean, it could be a giant tub of nutella
        </p>
        <p>
         Anyway, so glad I got around to doing this pic -drawing the less attractive animals was awesome.
        </p>
        <p>
         Popped it up on Redbubble because they have tote bags and cushions now which is just wow - can grab it also on cards or posters -
         <a href="http://www.redbubble.com/people/aliciamb/works/12206490-a-guide-to-rare-and-unusual-patronuses">
          check it out here!
         </a>
        </p>
       </blockquote>
