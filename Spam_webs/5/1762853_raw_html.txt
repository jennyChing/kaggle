         <h1 class="entry-title">
          AURA Preview: Getting Romantic in the Suwannee
         </h1>
         <p class="p-meta">
          <span>
           In
           <a href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/category/dubera-2" title="View all posts in: DubEra">
            DubEra
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/category/fests" title="View all posts in: Festivals">
            Festivals
           </a>
           by Chris Tart
          </span>
          <span>
           <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-01-22T10:02:26+00:00">
            January 22, 2014
           </time>
          </span>
          <span>
           <a class="meta-comments" href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/aura-valentines#comments" title="Leave a comment on: AURA Preview: Getting Romantic in the Suwannee">
            Leave a Comment
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content content">
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           Words by Taylor Cleary
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/aura-valentines/screen-shot-2014-01-22-at-9-53-40-am" rel="attachment wp-att-17668">
           <img alt="aura valentines" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17668" height="566" src="http://www.dubera.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Screen-Shot-2014-01-22-at-9.53.40-AM.png" width="605"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           [ Image via AURA Music &amp; Arts Festivals Instagram
           <a href="http://instagram.com/auramusicfest" target="_blank">
            @auramusicfest
           </a>
           ]
          </em>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          AURA Music Festival is upon us, and it almost overshadows the other major event of that weekend: Valentines Day. But thats not to say you cant celebrate both! Here are some V-Day ideas you can take heart in (sorry, had to) with your Suwannee sweetie:
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <strong>
           Take a nature walk
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          If theres one thing the Suwannee isnt short on, its nature. Duh. So go enjoy it! Take a break from the whiskey (or dont) and get walking. The camps got dirt roads, hiking trails, and more woods than youll ever need, and they all provide a stunning glimpse of Floridas natural state. From the SOSMP wesbite: breathe the fresh, sweet air, witness the myriad wildlife and listen to the gentle sounds of nature. Couldnt have said it better myself. With this years weather, keep an eye out for polar bears.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <strong>
           Visit the Bat House
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          Not for the queasy couple, this luxurious romantic getaway comes complete with hordes of screeching bats. In the middle of the Farm Field camping area sits the largest bat house in the country, and inside are UP TO FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND BATS, all of which come flying out full speed when the sun sets (cover your hair, ladies). Not traditionally romantic, but definitely exciting to watch. The gorgeous sunset and groups of fire-spinners dont hurt, either.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_17671" style="width: 530px">
          <a href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/aura-valentines/hammock-2" rel="attachment wp-att-17671">
           <img alt="Who wouldn't want to get down on some munchies with this sexy beast?" class=" wp-image-17671 " height="346" src="http://www.dubera.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/hammock-2-650x433.jpg" width="520"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Who wouldnt want to get down on some munchies with this sexy beast?
          </p>
         </div>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <strong>
           Have a picnic  in the air
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          It may sound crazy, but if youve ever been to a festival at SOSMP, youve probably seen crazier. Im talking about a hammock picnic. Its the perfect way to stay cozy and take care of those munchies at the same time. Set up your picnic between two trees, and hang your hammock right over it! We suggest the trees around Spirit Lake or, of course, the Amphitheater Stage.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <strong>
           Candles? Why not light a WHOLE FIRE?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          Theres no doubt itll be chilly out there, so a candlelight dinner might not cut it. Opt for a fireside meal instead  just make sure you know what youre doing! The General Store toward the front of the venue (near the Music Hall) will have everything you need to set up a romantic campfire dinner for two, and theres always firewood available on the main road. Just remember to check out the AURA website for campfire regulations. Oh, and PUT IT OUT when youre done.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <a href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/aura-valentines/beach-couple" rel="attachment wp-att-17672">
           <img alt="beach couple" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-17672" height="433" src="http://www.dubera.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/beach-couple-650x433.jpg" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <strong>
           Get to the river
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          Swimming will probably be out of the question (unless youre crazy), but the river itself is a gorgeous beach spot. If youre looking for some quiet time away from the excitement of the festival, take a trip (about a mile outside camp, so youll want to drive) to the beach and enjoy the view. This would also be a great spot for that air-picnic we talked about.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <strong>
           Build a tent-nest
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          This ones pretty self-explanatory, but a tent nest could be the best spot in camp if you build it right. An air mattress is of course the perfect base, and from there, just pile on the blankets. Use pillows (as many as you can get your hands on) to make walls and burrow down with your sweetheart! Of course, the more people you let in, the warmer itll be (and probably more fun).
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <a href="http://www.dubera.com/blog/aura-valentines/love-birds" rel="attachment wp-att-17673">
           <img alt="love birds" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-17673" height="650" src="http://www.dubera.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/love-birds-433x650.jpg" width="433"/>
          </a>
         </p>

    jQuery(document).ready(function($) {

    //
    // Scroll top anchor.
    //
    // 1. Get the number of pixels to the bottom of the page.
    // 2. Get the difference from the body height and the bottom offset.
    // 3. Output the adjusted height for the page for acurate percentage parameter.
    //

    $.fn.scrollBottom = function() {
      return $(document).height() - this.scrollTop() - this.height();
    };

    var windowObj            = $(window);
    var body                 = $('body');
    var bodyOffsetBottom     = windowObj.scrollBottom(); // 1
    var bodyHeightAdjustment = body.height() - bodyOffsetBottom; // 2
    var bodyHeightAdjusted   = body.height() - bodyHeightAdjustment; // 3
    var scrollTopAnchor      = $('.x-scroll-top');

    function sizingUpdate(){
      var bodyOffsetTop = windowObj.scrollTop();
      if ( bodyOffsetTop &gt; ( bodyHeightAdjusted * 0.75 ) ) {
        scrollTopAnchor.addClass('in');
      } else {
        scrollTopAnchor.removeClass('in');
      }
    }

    windowObj.bind('scroll', sizingUpdate).resize(sizingUpdate);
    sizingUpdate();

    scrollTopAnchor.click(function(){
      $('html,body').animate({ scrollTop: 0 }, 850, 'easeInOutExpo');
      return false;
    });
