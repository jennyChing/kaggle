               9 Ways to Live Longer With Rheumatoid Arthritis
              </div>
              <h1>
               How to Stay Healthier Longer With Rheumatoid Arthritis
              </h1>
              <h2>
               A rheumatoid arthritis diagnosis can put you at greater risk for developing additional health problems, but these simple ideas may help you live longer and happier.
              </h2>
             </div>
             <div class="bottom">
              <div class="floatl">

                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  1
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  If you have
                  <a href="/solutions/landing/better-manage-rheumatoid-arthritis/">
                   rheumatoid arthritis
                  </a>
                  , there are promising new treatments that may add years to your life. But there are also simple lifestyle changes that will improve your health and increase your chances of living longer. People with a rheumatoid arthritis diagnosis are at greater risk for other debilitating diseases such as heart attack, stroke, and osteoporosis, says Stacy Ardoin, MD, MSH, a rheumatologist at Ohio State University. So try these tips for staying healthy and improving your rheumatoid arthritis prognosis at the same time.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>

              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="sleep well to live longer with ra" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-02-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="sleep well to live longer with ra" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-02-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Sleep Well
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  2
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  If you don't get enough sleep, the hormones responsible for hunger (leptin) and fullness (ghrelin) can get out of balance, causing you to eat more and gain weight. Weight gain can make the
                  <a href="/health-report/rheumatoid-arthritis/10-ways-to-get-rheumatoid-arthritis-relief.aspx">
                   pain
                  </a>
                  and inflammation from rheumatoid arthritis worse. Sleep also regulates the release of the hormone cortisol. Cortisol stimulates cells that boost the immune system. A strong immune system helps you defend against colds and other illnesses  complications you won't want to deal with when you're already handling a rheumatoid arthritis diagnosis.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-03-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-03-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Lift Weights
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  11
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  Suggesting you
                  <a href="/health-report/rheumatoid-arthritis/6-hand-exercises-for-rheumatoid-arthritis.aspx">
                   lift weights
                  </a>
                  if you have sore joints may sound like a bad
idea. However, research from Great Britain shows that strength training
has many long-term benefits for people with rheumatoid arthritis.
It's best if you work with a qualified trainer, especially when first
starting a strength-training program as part of your rheumatoid
arthritis treatment. You might begin with weight machines and progress
to dumbbells. For maintenance, you might consider resistance bands. Fit
strength training into your schedule for a half hour two to three times a
week for a better rheumatoid arthritis prognosis.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="eat fish to live longer with ra" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-04-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="eat fish to live longer with ra" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-04-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Eat More Fish
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  4
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  Decide to improve your diet as part of your rheumatoid arthritis treatment. Get into the habit of eating fatty fish  such as albacore tuna, mackerel, salmon, and halibut  at least twice a week. Fatty fish are a good source of protein, relatively low in saturated fat, and rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which have been shown to reduce the risk of heart disease and cancer, Dr. Ardoin says. A number of small studies also have found that fish oil helps reduce rheumatoid arthritis symptoms, including
                  <a href="/health-report/rheumatoid-arthritis/10-ways-to-get-rheumatoid-arthritis-relief.aspx">
                   joint pain and morning stiffness
                  </a>
                  .
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="quit smoking to live longer with ra" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-05-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="quit smoking to live longer with ra" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-05-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Quit Smoking
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  5
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  "Not smoking is really important for several reasons," Ardoin says. Smoking can increase your risk of heart disease and lung diseases including cancer. Also, a recent Swedish study has found that patients with early rheumatoid arthritis who smoke are less likely to respond to their rheumatoid arthritis treatment. "Knowing that if you smoke, you may be less likely to respond to commonly prescribed rheumatoid arthritis medications (such as methotrexate and TNF inhibitors) should encourage you to quit," Ardoin says.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="get regular check-ups to live longer with ra" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-06-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="get regular check-ups to live longer with ra" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-06-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Get Regular Check-Ups
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  6
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  "Sometimes, when people have a rheumatoid arthritis diagnosis, they forget about the usual things that help them stay healthy," Ardoin says. To add years to your life
                  <em>
                   and
                  </em>
                  improve your rheumatoid arthritis prognosis, take the time to get a flu shot, pneumonia vaccine, and other immunizations as necessary. Follow recommendations for your age group for cancer screenings, such as mammograms and colonoscopies. Check your cholesterol and blood sugar and
                  <a href="/health-report/guide-to-managing-ra/sleep-better-with-ra.aspx">
                   take all medications
                  </a>
                  your doctor has given you as prescribed, both those for rheumatoid arthritis and any other health conditions.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="dental health to live longer with ra" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-07-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="dental health to live longer with ra" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-07-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Be Kind to Your Mouth
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  7
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  Taking care of your teeth and gums can improve your longevity, too. Brush and floss regularly to keep bacteria from dental plaque from building up and entering your bloodstream where it could block blood vessels and cause heart disease, a condition you don't want to add to your
                  <a href="/solutions/landing/better-manage-rheumatoid-arthritis/">
                   rheumatoid arthritis
                  </a>
                  diagnosis. Researchers have found links between oral bacteria and a number of potentially deadly health issues, including stroke and diabetes. To protect yourself from other conditions when managing rheumatoid arthritis, be sure to get regular dental check-ups and cleanings.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="printslide galleria-container">
               <div class="galleria-stage">
                <img alt="healthy weight to live longer with ra" class="slideshow-image" data-src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-08-full.jpg" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/blank.gif"/>
                <img alt="healthy weight to live longer with ra" class="gallery-thumbnail" src="http://images.agoramedia.com/cs/eh/pg-ra-healthy-living-habits-08-full.jpg" style="display:none;"/>
               </div>
               <div class="galleria-info">
                <div class="galleria-info-text">
                 <h3 class="galleria-info-title">
                  Maintain a Healthy Weight
                 </h3>
                 <div class="galleria-info-id">
                  8
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-type">
                  image
                 </div>
                 <div class="galleria-info-description">
                  Being overweight can put additional stress on your joints  not what you need when you're undergoing rheumatoid arthritis treatment. Being overweight also can mean your heart has to work harder to pump blood, putting you at risk for a heart attack or stroke. One of the best ways to maintain a healthy weight is to eat a low-fat, high-fiber diet and exercise regularly. A rheumatoid arthritis workout plan can safely include three types of exercises: stretching, strengthening, and cardiovascular conditioning, such as walking or swimming.
                  <div class="galleria-credits">
                  </div>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
