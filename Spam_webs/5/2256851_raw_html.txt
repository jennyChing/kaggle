        <h1>
         Moisture Care Scalp Soother
        </h1>
        <p>
         I have been trying different things to keep my scalp fresh between washings, but I wasnt coming up with a lot of options. When I spotted this in store I grabbed it. It claims to soothe the scalp and improve moisture balance using a peppermint oil complex and vitamin E. It has a little spray nozzle, so I liked that I could just lift up my curls and spritz my scalp without disturbing my second day curls.
        </p>
        <p>
         This is easy enough to use. I love that it has a little nozzle that extends out, instead of the typical sprayers you get on hairspray containers. It makes it easy to get down to the roots. All you do is spray on and massage in. It really does instantly soothe the scalp, and it feels fresh again. My scalp smells good too, but the smell isnt strong or overpowering. I am not sure if it has done anything for my scalps moisture balance, since I dont have issues with dry scalp. It didnt make it look oily or greasy though.
        </p>
        <p>
         I really like this, and it has become my go to for my non-shampoo days. The
