         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           Too Faced Rock N Roll Eye Shadow Palette
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="tie-date">
           <i class="fa fa-clock-o">
           </i>
           August 20, 2014
          </span>
          <span class="post-cats">
           <i class="fa fa-folder">
           </i>
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/category/makeup/" rel="category tag">
            Makeup
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/category/miscellaneous/" rel="category tag">
            Misc
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="post-views">
           <i class="fa fa-eye">
           </i>
           78 Views
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="entry">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-N-Roll-Palette-.jpg">
            <img alt="Too Faced Rock N Roll Palette" class="alignnone wp-image-29451" height="353" src="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-N-Roll-Palette--450x299.jpg" width="531"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           So here I am trying to be a nice and sweet and
           <a href="https://www.toofaced.com/" target="_blank">
            Too Faced Cosmetics
           </a>
           comes out with this
           <a href="https://www.toofaced.com/p/eye-shadow-palettes/rock-n-roll/" target="_blank">
            Rock N Roll Palette
           </a>
           to make me feel like a complete badass. If the packaging isnt enough to make you want to pick it up and rock out with the best of them then maybe this will, the palette has seven, yes, seven brand new shades so if you were already wondering if you needed this palette that should be another coin into the buy it side. The shadow formulas include two mattes, three weightless sparkles and four metallic shimmer finishes. Of course per usual this palette comes with a step-by-step application instructions that show you how to create awesome looks using every color.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-In-Roll-Palette-Open.jpg">
            <img alt="Too Faced Rock In Roll Palette Open" class="alignnone wp-image-29452" height="497" src="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-In-Roll-Palette-Open-450x443.jpg" width="504"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I swatched all of the shades and the lighter colors seen on the left of the palette barely showed up on my skin which is something that Ive noticed to be a pattern with Too Faced Palettes. I understand that these shades are meant to be highlights but sometimes they barely show up on even the palest of complexions. The
           <br/>
           swatches below are all done without a primer. What I love about this palette is that all the remaining shades are deep and dark enough to make the perfect smokey eye. Im already making plans for what I want to do with Glam Rock, which is a slate grey with flecks of gold seen throughout.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-and-Roll-Palette-Swatches-1.jpg">
            <img alt="Rock N Roll Palette Swatches 1" class="alignnone wp-image-29453" height="366" src="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-and-Roll-Palette-Swatches-1-450x299.jpg" width="551"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-N-Roll-palette-Swatches-2.jpg">
            <img alt="Too Faced Rock N Roll palette Swatches 2" class="alignnone wp-image-29454" height="342" src="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-N-Roll-palette-Swatches-2-450x276.jpg" width="558"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Though the palette came with three predesigned looks I decided to just free will it and choose colors to create my own look. My eyes were automatically drawn to Punk and Rap so I decided to try this new halo technique that I learned during a makeup class to create my eye look. I did this look without a Primer and I was kicking myself afterwards because I know the color payoff would have been amazing if Id used one. To start off I lined my eyes with a Black liner, both top and bottom lash lines, then I smudged the line out. I then did the same thing with the color Punk that I applied to my lid. Next, I followed up by applying the color Rap to my Crease. I also applied this to my lower lash line blending it in with Punk . To my Brow Bone I applied New Romantic, though the color is very soft and barely shows on my skin.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-in-Roll-Palette-Up-close.jpg">
            <img alt="Too Faced Rock in Roll Palette Up closeToo Faced Rock in Roll Palette Up close" class="alignnone wp-image-29455" height="332" src="http://www.mybeautybunny.com/wp-content/uploads/Too-Faced-Rock-in-Roll-Palette-Up-close-450x264.jpg" width="566"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           The Too Faced Rock N Roll Palette retails for $36 and can be purchased at
           <a href="https://www.toofaced.com/" target="_blank">
            TooFaced.com
           </a>
           or Ulta.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
