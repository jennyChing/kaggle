            <div class="descr">
             Whether backpacking trails or traveling internationally, you will need reliable access to safe and potable water. Ditch those unappetizing iodine tablets, unwieldily water pumps, and inconvenient boiling pots. The SteriPEN Classic uses Ultraviolet light rays to safely sterilize clear water from 99.9% of bacteria, protozoa, and other viruses that cause water-borne illnesses. Just set the power button to the size of the bottle you are cleaning (1L or .5L), submerge the metal sensors into the water, and gently rock bottle until sensor indicates water is clean! Powered by 4 AA batteries, the Classic model lasts 200 half liter purifications per charge, and 8,000 treatments per lamp. The Classic model comes with a pre-filter that fits over wide-mouth one liter water bottles and removes particulates that can get in the way of UV light in water.
