        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Music Festival Hair Tutorial
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <em>

        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/music-festival-hair1.jpg">
          <img alt="Music Festival Hair Tutorial" class="aligncenter wp-image-1626" height="422" src="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/music-festival-hair1.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Music Festival season is upon us and I know that you are probably feverishly searching Pinterest and Youtube for a fun hairstyle to rock. I am going to make your life a little easier with my Music Festival Hair Tutorial! I am only using a curling wand, hair color powder and
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1feGntz" rel="nofollow">
          <strong>
           Herbal Essences Naked Dry Shampoo
          </strong>
         </a>
         ! Super simple, right? Not only is the product great for immediate styling but I would keep it with me in my bag because some of these festivals are a couple days long! Definitely keep your hair looking freshly styled with touch-ups!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/herbal-essences.jpg">
          <img alt="Herbal Essences" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1625" height="530" src="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/herbal-essences.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I am going to preface by saying that I have long, limp, and generally oily hair. For me, Dry Shampoo has become a way of life for combatting these problems. Herbal Essences Naked Dry Shampoo is part of their Naked Collection. The collection basically dares you to bare your softest, sexiest hair! The line is infused with revitalizing mint essences and is formulated to deliver more of what you love and less of what you dont, like heavy residues, parabens and dyes. The Naked Dry Shampootransforms my limp, oily hair into fresh, touchable hair with a natural tapioca formula that instantly absorbs excess oil and gives my hair lift, volume, and oomph! All of those reasons are why I use dry shampoo on my clean hair while I am styling.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/herb-ess-2.jpg">
          <img alt="herbal essences dry shampoo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1627" height="576" src="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/herb-ess-2.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont know about you but I hate washing my hair everyday because of the time it takes to let it dry and style it. In between washes I love using this to keep my hair looking and smelling awesome. What I love is that this dry shampoo doesnt leave behind the ugly white residue that others do. It also doesnt coat or weigh down my hair with parabens or dyes, so my hair is left movable, bouncy and voluminous with a tingly mint fragrance and a freshly styled look!
        </p>
        <p>
         To make sure the product performs the way it should you should always shake well before spraying and working it into your roots. Brush hair for even distribution!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/herb-ess-3.jpg">
          <img alt="Herbal Essences Dry Shampoo " class="aligncenter wp-image-1628 size-medium" height="485" src="http://www.mynewestaddiction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/herb-ess-3-750x485.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://bit.ly/1feGntz" rel="nofollow">
           Herbal Essences Naked Dry Shampoo
          </a>
         </strong>
         is my new favorite! It does everything I want it to while leaving out everything I dont like! It smells great and does what it says!

         to keep up with the brand for other fun photos and exciting news!

        <p>
         Without further rambling here is my Music Festival Hair Tutorial. As I mentioned before, style your hair with the dry shampoo for volume and oomph and keep it with you at the festival for touch ups! And definitely, click through to my channel, thumbs up and subscribe for more exciting videos!
