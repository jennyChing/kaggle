       <h1>
        12 Modern and Functional Cutting Boards
       </h1>
       <div class="meta">
        Feb 22, 2010 by
        <a href="http://www.gadgetsharp.com/author/kaman/" rel="author" title="Posts by kaman">
         kaman
        </a>
        <img alt="" src="http://www.gadgetsharp.com/wp-content/themes/gadgetgrey/images/ico_post_comments.png"/>
        <a href="http://www.gadgetsharp.com/20100222/12-modern-and-functional-cutting-boards/#comments" title="Comment on 12 Modern and Functional Cutting Boards">
         3 Comments
        </a>
        <img alt="" src="http://www.gadgetsharp.com/wp-content/themes/gadgetgrey/images/ico_post_date.png"/>
        Posted under:
        <a href="http://www.gadgetsharp.com/category/cool-home/" rel="category tag">
         Cool Home
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.gadgetsharp.com/category/featured/" rel="category tag">
         Featured
        </a>
       </div>
       <div style="float:left;height:250px;width:300px;margin:0 20px 20px 0;background:#dedede;">
        <script type="text/javascript">
         &lt;!--

       <p>
        If you are bored with the old, regular and conventional cutting board, check this post. We have collected 12 modern and functional cutting board designs from the world which we are sure will speed up or increase the pleasure to your food preparation. So, are you ready to spice up your kitchen with these modern and functional cutting boards?
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         1. Over-the-Sink Cutting Boards
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.kaboodle.com/reviews/over-the-sink-cutting-board-with-colander-8" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        Increase food-prep space with this ingenious kitchen tool. The solid maple board, fitted with an 18/10 stainless-steel mesh colander, straddles your sink to create a convenient area for rinsing, draining and chopping. The removable colander makes it easy to transport ingredients between sink and stove. Handcrafted in Vermont, the board reverses, so you can use one side for produce, the other for meats and poultry.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Over-the-Sink Cutting Boards" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/1.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <br/>
       <span id="more-568">
       </span>
       <p>
        <strong>
         2. Encanal Carving Board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.amordemadre.com/?s=objetos&amp;lang=en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        Carving board for cooking. The channel in the middle makes sure that nothing falls out of the frying pan.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Encanal Carving Board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/2.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         3. Bamboo Appetito Cutting Board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.designswan.com/archives/life-gadget-design-7.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        A combination of form and function, the Bamboo Appetito is a cutting board designed to reinvent conventional food preparation. With traditional boards you have to sweep waste off to make room for more food. The Bamboo Appetito solves this problem with a split cutting surface and track running underneath that conceals a stainless steel utensil tray.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Bamboo Appetito Cutting Board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/3.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         4. Pinzon Santoku Knives and Bamboo Cutting Board Set
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B000GFYYX8?tag=desswa-20&amp;camp=14573&amp;creative=327641&amp;linkCode=as1&amp;creativeASIN=B000GFYYX8&amp;adid=0ZWYXQRRJJ1SKTJB88PF&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        # Includes 5- and 7-inch santoku knives, bamboo storage drawer, and bamboo cutting board
        <br/>
        # Blades stamped from 2mm-gauge stainless steel; double welding produces sharp edges
        <br/>
        # Alternating hollows on blades allow slices to fall away; tangs extend to handles
        <br/>
        # Hollow, stainless-steel handles are ergonomically designed
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Pinzon Santoku Knives and Bamboo Cutting Board Set" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/4.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         5. Cut &amp; Carve Double Sided Cutting/Serving Board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.delight.com/Cut-Carve-Double-Sided-CuttingServing-Board" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        This double sided chopping board has a meat grip, an easy pour lip for gravy and juices and a nice big flat surface with a lip that is perfect for serving or cutting breads, veggies or whatever else youre whipping up in the kitchen!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Cut &amp; Carve Double Sided Cutting/Serving Board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/5.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <div class="ad_outer2">
        <!-- ca-pub-2323508928664496/300x250_1 -->
        <script type="text/javascript">
         GA_googleFillSlot("300x250_1");
        </script>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         6. Index Chopping Boards
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B001K7HXM0?tag=desswa-20&amp;camp=14573&amp;creative=327641&amp;linkCode=as1&amp;creativeASIN=B001K7HXM0&amp;adid=14WVGZNNBZSNGZKAENXC&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        These four color-coded polypropylene chopping boards are designed to avoid cross-contamination of various food types: red/raw meat, white/cooked foods, blue/fish, green/vegetables. The boards are stored in an ABS plastic horizontal filing unit with a non-slip rubber bottom. Boards are dishwasher safe.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Index Chopping Boards" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/6.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         7. Folding Cutting Board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B001K7HXES?tag=desswa-20&amp;camp=14573&amp;creative=327641&amp;linkCode=as1&amp;creativeASIN=B001K7HXES&amp;adid=0VJJCQF0S17CEFF24CM4&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        Chop foods on this multi-purpose cutting board and then fold it into a chute to transfer ingredients without spills. Made of polypropylene.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Folding Cutting Board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/7.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         8. Ventiquattro Cutting Board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.tomas-alonso.com/TomasAlonso_Projects_Ventiquattro-food-bag%202.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        Jorre Van Ast, Mathias Hahn &amp; Toms Alonso collaborated to design the Ventiquattro Food Bag  a cutting board made of American oak and paired with a flush, sterling silver knife. Come with a washable synthetic bag.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Ventiquattro Cutting Board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/8.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         9. Pinzon Round Bamboo Chopping Block with Inset Mezzaluna
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B001D8RP6K?tag=desswa-20&amp;camp=14573&amp;creative=327641&amp;linkCode=as1&amp;creativeASIN=B001D8RP6K&amp;adid=105ESFZ2S6TJ547NR4CZ&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        # Round, reversible bamboo chopping block with mezzaluna inset
        <br/>
        # Flat side for everyday chopping; bowl-shaped side for mezzaluna s curved blade
        <br/>
        # Chopping block made from bamboo, a renewable resource; more durable than maple
        <br/>
        # Food-safe, stain-free, hard surface; 10 inches in diameter and 2-3/4 inches high
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Pinzon Round Bamboo Chopping Block with Inset Mezzaluna" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/9.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         10. ES cutting board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://mocoloco.com/archives/000933.php" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        Unusual in design, the board breaks with conventional boards by establishing a slope, allowing liquids to run freely to a collection drain from where they can be poured easily away. The cutting angle and single-hand lifting are other ergonomic working benefits of ES. The product is reversible so that meat and vegetables can be prepared separately. Non-slip, reversible, hygienic and durable HD-PE, light weight, and dish washable are other features.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="ES cutting board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/10.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        And the last two are still concept designs and which one you want to come true more?
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         11. Cutting Board Scale
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://www.designswan.com/archives/11-creative-and-practical-kitchen-gadgets.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        This cutting board has an integrated scale within a defined area on its surface. This allows a person to both cut and measure ingredients on the same surface with very little extra effort. Simply put, it precisely reduces the steps of preparation, dishes to clean, and time wasted. There has been a tranformative trend in cooking based around the science of food. Central to this is the idea that precise measurement leads to more possibilities for new flavors. Recipies will become more demanding, requiring simple ways to be precise in the kitchen.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="Cutting Board Scale" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/11.jpg"/>
       </center>
       <p>
        <strong>
         12. LCD Cutting Board
        </strong>
        [
        <a href="http://tonytone.wordpress.com/2008/04/10/lcd-cutting-board/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         link
        </a>
        ]
       </p>
       <p>
        Created by Nardo Design, the Digital Cutting Board features a built in LCD allowing users to download and display recipes of their choice.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <img alt="LCD Cutting Board" src="http://img.gadgetsharp.com/2010/02/cutting/12.jpg"/>
       </center>
