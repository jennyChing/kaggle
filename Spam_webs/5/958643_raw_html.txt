        <p>
         Girls and tea just go together. I love having tea, alone or with other people. This month, the ladies of our church are hosting a tea. The pastors wife got the idea from her mom, who has done quite a few in her time. She asked if I would help her with the planning process. I was a little nervous, but I said yes right away. My mother-in-law is the queen of teas. Being a pastors wife, she has thrown a tea at every church she has every been to. My first thought was I have to invite her, she would love this, but then I eventually thought I better do a good job. Im well aware that my mother-in-law is going to love anything I do, shes a great supporter. However I cant help but feel a little nervous, the queen is sitting at my table. Of course, the first place I went to for a little tea inspiration was Pinterest. With the tea 10 days away, I figured I would share a few of my favorites.
