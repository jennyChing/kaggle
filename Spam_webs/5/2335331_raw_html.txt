George Foreman indoor grill until done.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
MAKE THE
BRUCHETTA&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
Chop
tomatoes, garlic and basil. Add to a bowl with olive oil. Set aside.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
COOK PASTA&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
Dump in
enough pasta for two people in boiling water and cook according to the package
directions. Drain and divide into two bowls.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
FINISH THE
BOWLS&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
Cover the
pasta with Alfredo sauce from the jar. Spoon on the fresh bruchetta, add the
grilled sliced chicken and sprinkle on the Italian cheeses. Put in the
microwave for 1-2 minutes to heat the sauce and melt the cheeses.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0in;"&gt;
