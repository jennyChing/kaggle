     <ul>
      <li>
       My dear Aknot, what about those two little planes you borrowed?
       <i>
        [sees Aknot's human face]
       </i>
       Aknot, is that you? What an ugly face. It doesn't suit you. Take it off.
       <i>
        [Aknot's face transforms into a Mangalore's]
       </i>
       Much better. Never be ashamed of who you are. You're warriors, be proud. So what if the Federal Government scattered your people into the wind? What doesn't kill you makes you stronger. Your time for revenge is at hand. Voila! The ZF-1.
       <i>
        [activates a ZF-1 and holds it]
       </i>
       It's light; handle's adjustable for easy carrying; good for righties and lefties; breaks down into four parts; undetectable by X-ray; ideal for quick discreet interventions. A word on firepower. Titanium recharger; 3000-round clip with bursts of 3 to 300. With the replay button, another Zorg invention, it's even easier.
       <i>
        [lights reveal a mannequin in police gear]
       </i>
       One shot...
       <i>
        [shoots mannequin]
       </i>
       ...and replay sends every following shot to the same location.
       <i>
        [turns around, shooting in the direction of the Mangalores; bullets curve their trajectory and hit the mannequin instead]
       </i>
       And to finish the job, all the Zorg oldies-but-goldies.
       <i>
        [fires every weapon at the mannequin as he mentions them]
       </i>
       Rocket launcher... arrow launcher, with exploding or poisonous gas heads, very practical... our famous net launcher... the always-efficient flamethrower, my favorite...
       <i>
        [winks to the Mangalores]
       </i>
       and for the grand finale, the all-new 'Ice-cube System'!
       <i>
        [fires a cloud of liquid nitrogen which freezes the remains of the mannequin. Mangalores applaud politely by carnage]
       </i>
      </li>
      <li>
       I don't like warriors. Too narrow-minded, no subtlety. And worse, they fight for hopeless causes. Honor? Huh! Honor's killed millions of people, it hasn't saved a single one. Tell you what I do like though: a killer. A dyed-in-the-wool killer. Cold-blooded, clean, methodical and thorough. Now a real killer, when he picked up the ZF-1, he would have
       <i>
        immediately
       </i>
       asked about the little red button on the bottom of the gun.
       <i>
        [a Mangalore presses the button, detonating a large explosive]
       </i>
       Bring me the priest.
      </li>
      <li>
       Torture who you have to. The President, I don't care. Just bring me those stones. You have one hour.
      </li>
      <li>
       BRAVO! Bravo! My compliments, little lady. Thank you for doing all the dirty work. I couldn't have done the job myself. Hand over the stones.
      </li>
      <li>
       <i>
        [listening to people screaming and panicking]

       I... am... VERY... DISAPPOINTED!
