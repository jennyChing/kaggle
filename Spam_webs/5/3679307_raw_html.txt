        <p>
         My daughter is an average, healthy little girl. But she has inherited a quality of mine which I kinda feel bad for passing onto her.She has spring (and fall)allergies. Just like me, her poor little nose needs constant tissue attention. Pair our allergies with the bugs she brings home from school which go from her to my husband and finally to me, our noses kinda have it rough.
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="250" src="http://i1128.photobucket.com/albums/m481/SassyScorp/Reviews/Kleenex_SWS_300x250_3.jpg" title="Kleenex" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats why I chose
         <a href="http://bit.ly/Kleenex_SC" rel="nofollow nofollow" target="_blank">
          Kleenex Facial Tissue
         </a>
         , Americas Softest Tissue for my family to share. But using the best can be a little costly, especially when I like to send some to school with my daughter, and carry a box in the car. You just dont know when one of those yucky sneezing fits may attack, so I always like to be prepared. When I saw Kleenex Facial Tissue 10-packat my local Sams Club I was way excited.
        </p>
        <p>
         Not only do I get to save money for something my family and I need, but I can spread the love. My mom is also an allergy sufferer and always reaching for the toilet paper, so I plan on giving her a few boxes to give her nose a break. Also, give some for my husband to take to work. His allergies arent as bad as mine, but he always scuffs up his nose when allergy season is at its peak. Hes too cute for that ;).
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
