        <a href="http://coconutheadsurvivalguide.com/wedding/" rel="bookmark" title="Practical Wedding Planning Advice from Award Winning Experts">
         Practical Wedding Planning Advice from Award Winning Experts
        </a>
       </h1>
       <ul class="post-meta">
        <li class="post-date">
         <time class="updated" datetime="2015-02-26">
          February 26, 2015
         </time>
        </li>
        <li class="post-category">

        <p>
         <!--google_ad_section_start-->
        </p>
        <p>
         <!--holly bertone blog, holly bertone book, coconut head's cancer survival guide, breast cancer and self esteem, wedding, planning, guide, advice, DIY blog, design blog, family blog, breast cancer blog, breast cancer awareness, pink ribbon products, pink fortitude LLC-->
         <br/>
         You are engaged and over the moon. Wedding season has officially begun and I have the honor to share with you some practical wedding planning advice from three award winning experts.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://coconutheadsurvivalguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Practical-Wedding-Advice-from-the-Experts-by-coconutheadsurvivalguide.com_.jpg">
          <img alt="Practical Wedding Advice from the Experts by coconutheadsurvivalguide.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-12776 size-full" data-jpibfi-description="Practical Wedding Advice from the Experts by coconutheadsurvivalguide.com" height="1000" src="http://coconutheadsurvivalguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Practical-Wedding-Advice-from-the-Experts-by-coconutheadsurvivalguide.com_.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Planning
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         Always the first on any list  create a plan! Wedding planning can be extremely overwhelming. Two of the top recommended books are both by industry leader, The Knot, 
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0770433367/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0770433367&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thecocheasdur-20&amp;linkId=4NERV4AUSYTRWGOQ" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            The Knot Ultimate Wedding Planner
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
          and 
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0307341933/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0307341933&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thecocheasdur-20&amp;linkId=PI5WYRPPAFREA6C3" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            The Knot Book of Wedding Lists
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0770433367/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0770433367&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thecocheasdur-20&amp;linkId=4NERV4AUSYTRWGOQ" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="book1" class=" size-full wp-image-12747 alignleft" data-jpibfi-description="" height="291" src="http://coconutheadsurvivalguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/book1.jpg" width="260"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0307341933/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0307341933&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thecocheasdur-20&amp;linkId=PI5WYRPPAFREA6C3" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="book2" class=" size-full wp-image-12748 alignnone" data-jpibfi-description="" height="346" src="http://coconutheadsurvivalguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/book2.jpg" width="228"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont forget about
         <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pinkfortitude/" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            Pinterest
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         ! Make it your wedding BFF. Pinterest has no judgement, and Pinterest will not give you any unsolicited advice. Make boards for your different categories  dress, flowers, reception, etc. Dont forget about secret boards if you are planning something that you dont want others to see, or group boards (which can also be secret) with your groom to be, bridesmaids, family, etc.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Invitations and Photography
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         Photographer and Graphic Designer Makya Renee of
         <a href="http://www.maretacreations.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            Mareta Creations
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         is The Knots 2013, 2014, and 2015 top pick. She advises, Start with a budget for everything sothat you know how much you can afford and how many guests you can invite. THEN create your guest list. Ensure everyone who is financially contributing to your wedding has a voice as to who is and isnt invited. Once you have a tentative headcount, find a venue that will accommodate them and lock it in. Then research vendors, ensure they can service your venue, and collect multiple estimates to ensure you are receiving the industry rate for what youre requesting.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Flowers
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         Wedding Coordinator and Wedding Wire award recipient Laura Shaffer of
         <a href="http://www.thegreenarbor.com/weddings.php" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            Green Arbor Flowers
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         , suggests, Find a professional florist and when you meet, make sure you feel comfortable with him or her. Do they understand your style and vision? Are they interested in your ideas or do they push their own ideas on you? Do they give suggestions without making you feel like you are clueless or your style is boring? Will they work with your budget?
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Jewelry
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         Los Angeles celebrity designer Michelle Mirkovich from
         <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=224573&amp;clickref=&amp;p=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.etsy.com%2Fshop%2Fdivinityjewelry%3Fref%3Dshop_sugg" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            Divinity Jewelry
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         offers a modern twist when choosing jewelry, Brides can now work one-on-one with jewelry designers to create one of a kind pieces for themselves and their bridal party. Incorporating the brides birthstone or a gemstone that matches her color scheme are two ways to create unique jewelry.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Gifts
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Groom or Groomsmen
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         How do you pick out unique and meaningful gifts for your groom, groomsmen, FOB, FOG, and any other men in the wedding? These
         <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=224573&amp;clickref=&amp;p=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.etsy.com%2Flisting%2F220709305%2Fcustom-made-gift-boxed-wedding-cufflinks%3Fref%3Dsc_1%26plkey%3D1975a3e26c854a3cd301c56763dfc8e851e89343%253A220709305%26ga_search_query%3Dgroom%2Bgift%2Bcuff%2Blinks%26ga_search_type%3Dall%26ga_view_type%3Dgallery" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            cuff links
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=224573&amp;clickref=&amp;p=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.etsy.com%2Flisting%2F125433232%2Fcustom-collar-stays-steel-25-collar%3Fref%3Dsc_2%26plkey%3D7de1fc32802f669f16bb3d9fb67da4f43df31da6%253A125433232%26ga_search_query%3Dgroom%2Bgift%2Bcollar%2Bstay%26ga_search_type%3Dall%26ga_view_type%3Dgallery" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            collar stays
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         make wonderful (and affordable) gifts.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&amp;awinaffid=224573&amp;clickref=&amp;p=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.etsy.com%2Flisting%2F220709305%2Fcustom-made-gift-boxed-wedding-cufflinks%3Fref%3Dsc_1%26plkey%3D1975a3e26c854a3cd301c56763dfc8e851e89343%253A220709305%26ga_search_query%3Dgroom%2Bgift%2Bcuff%2Blinks%26ga_search_type%3Dall%26ga_view_type%3Dgallery" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
