      <h1 style="line-height:25px; font-size:18px;">
       Hardwood Lumber in Laguna Niguel and Mission Viejo, California (CA)
      </h1>
      <p class="textwrapimage">
       <img alt="Prestidge Beams" src="imageshardwood/prestidgebeams.png"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Capistrano Hardwoods stocks a large selection of several different lumber species including (but not limited to):
      </p>
      <p>
       Alder, Birch, Cherry, Mahogany, Maple, MDF, Oak, Poplar, and Teak
      </p>
      <p>
       Many hardwood lumber species are stocked in 1/2, 4/4, 5/4, 6/4, and 8/4 thicknesses, random widths and lengths. This is called S3S material, 
                which means it is finished on 3 sides. We also stock oak and poplar in specified widths, called S4S, or finished 4 sides. Namely, we stock 1x2, 
                1x3, 1x4, 1x6, 1x8, 1x10, and 1x12 material at random lengths.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Figured Bubinga" src="imageshardwood/figuredbubingah.png"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       We also stock many different sheet products such as plywood and MDF.  We have several sheets of the various species, so feel free to come and 
                find the sheet that fits your needs.
      </p>
      <p>
       Capistrano Hardwoods has a new display of exotic hardwoods like Black and White ebony and tulip wood.  Be sure to stop in and see the beautiful 
                pieces now in stock.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Prestidge Ceiling Pic" src="imageshardwood/prestidgewoodceiling.png"/>
