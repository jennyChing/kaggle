         <div class="standfirst">
          <p>
           Stream the producer's
           <em>
            The Pleiadian Adventure
           </em>
           , part of Bedouin Records's Originator series of cassette releases
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Along with Roni Size, DJ Krust was a key player in Bristol's Reprazent drum 'n' bass collective and the Full Cycle and V Records labels. Krust's 1994 track "Jazz Note" on Size's Full Cycle label, signalled the beginning of the more melodic, overtly jazz-infected school of drum 'n' bass, and then his 1997 12" "Warhead" signposted the brutal route that led to robotic two-step style of later years.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           The Pleiadian Adventure
          </em>
          was released last year as part of
          <a href="http://bedouinrecords.com/">
           Bedouin Records
          </a>
          's Originator series, in a run of 100 cassettes
         </p>
