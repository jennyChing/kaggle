     Sworn to Secrecy!
    </h1>
    <meta content="194" itemprop="wordCount"/>
    <meta content="Personal" itemprop="articleSection"/>
    <div id="article-body" itemprop="articleBody">
     <img itemprop="image" src="http://d2kym60b0ykakr.cloudfront.net/articleimages/25455-sworn-to-secrecyLnmPluaH/bc4e2d700efbc1cb0268a3f9b1c0ca99.jpg" style="padding-bottom:30px; max-width: 30%; float: left; max-height: 70%;margin-right: 30px; z-index: 10;"/>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
       My husband and I have rented a small ranch house in Florida for next winter.  We need to escape the Chicago area winters for a month.
      </span>
      <br/>
      <br/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
       This is a good plan.  However, we cannot tell anyone in my husband's family our vacation location.
      </span>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
       My husband's family lives all around Florida.  I love his family, but drama is always in the works with his side of the family.  I think there are just too darn many of them!
      </span>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
       I have been told I am not to mention the location of our Florida vacation to any member of his family.  I cannot even tell my mother-in-law who is my best buddy.
      </span>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
       If our Florida location were leaked to any family member, then we would be invaded by brothers, sisters, aunts, uncles, cousins, nieces, nephews and their spouses.   No way does my husband what the drama to come to our peaceful vacation abode.
      </span>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
       So, I can only tell my family where we will be for one month in Florida.  They are not prone to invasion, and do they do not like drama!
       <br/>
      </span>
     </p>
