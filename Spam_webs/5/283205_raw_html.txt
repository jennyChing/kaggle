       <h2>
        Ingredients
       </h2>
       <ul class="ingredients">
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 cup butter (salted), softened
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 cup sugar
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 cup brown sugar, lightly packed
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         2 eggs
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 tsp instant coffee granules (either a really strong one or I also like hazelnut)
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 tsp vanilla extract
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         3 cups all purpose flour
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         2 tsp ground cinnamon
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 tsp baking soda
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/2 tsp salt
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/2 tsp (heaping) cayenne pepper
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         parchment paper
        </li>
       </ul>

        Save a shopping List

      <div class="col-sm-7 directions_responsive">
       <h2>
        directions
       </h2>
       <ul class="instructions">
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          1
         </strong>
         <p>
          Preheat oven to 350C.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          2
         </strong>
         <p>
          Melt 1 cup choclate chips in heatproof bowl set over simmering water. Cool to room temp.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          3
         </strong>
         <p>
          Beat butter with granulated sugar and brown sugar till fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Stir coffee with vanilla; beat into butter mixture. Blend in melted chocolate.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          4
         </strong>
         <p>
          In seperate bowl, stir flour with cinnamon, baking soda, salt and cayenne pepper. Add to butter mixture; mix on low just until combined. Stir in remaining chocolate chips.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          5
         </strong>
         <p>
          Drop dough by the spoonful, 1-inch apart, onto baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          6
         </strong>
         <p>
          Bake for 10 min or until set but still soft --&gt; they will look uncooked but take them out to cool. After 2 min or so tranfer to wire rack to cool completely.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
         <strong>
          7
         </strong>
         <p>
          Enjoy!
         </p>
        </li>
       </ul>
