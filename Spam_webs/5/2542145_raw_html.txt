           Healthy looking eyes are the most delicate and essential part of your face without which you can hardly survive in this world. So this is your responsibility to make sure that your eyes remain healthy and in good condition. Healthy looking eyes are the vision of very fit and good looking person. Healthy looking eyes can we achieved easily if proper care is taken and some simple rules to followed.
          </p>
          <h1 style="text-align: center;">
           Healthy Looking Eyes
          </h1>
          <p>

            </noscript>

          </p>
          <h2>
           Appropriate Diet:
          </h2>
          <p>
           The first thing to do is to care your eyes by starts with the food on your plate. Twitch to take proper diet and nutrients such as omega-3 fatty acids, lutein, zinc, and vitamins C and will certainly help your connected vision difficulties such as macular failure and. Try to consume frequently these foods that can help lead to good eye health:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Green, leafy vegetables such as spinach, kale, and collards
           </li>
           <li>
            Salmon, tuna, and other oily fish
           </li>
           <li>
            Eggs, nuts, beans, and other non-meat protein sources
           </li>
           <li>
            Oranges and other citrus fruits or juices
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           Trust me you think that what this bunch of leafy things can do, but you have no idea how much they are valuable for eyes. Eating a well-proportioned diet also helps you reserve a healthy weight, which makes you less likely to get obesity-related illnesses.
