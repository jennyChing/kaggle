        <strong>
         Selection Procedure:
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The test has two components: Written Assessment and Interview. The written assessment component evaluates applicants aptitude in Language, Mathematics, and Analytical ability. An applicant must obtain a minimum qualifying score in each of the above areas to pass of the written test and appear for the interview.
       </p>
       <p>
        All Bangladeshi applicants must take the admission test. An applicant with foreign nationality with minimum 75 percentile in GMAT may be exempted from the written assessment. However, they will be required to appear for an interview.
       </p>

        charge).
