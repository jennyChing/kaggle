            <img alt="Achieving Thicker, More Beautiful Eyebrows" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3003" height="300" src="http://www.lashreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Achieving-Thicker-More-Beautiful-Eyebrows-217x300.jpg" style="float: right; margin: 15px 10px 5px;" width="217"/>

           <p>
            Lets focus on thick, lush, and gorgeous eyebrows in this post. May your brows stand out with proper plucking and waxing, as well as other tried-and-tested ways to groom this part of your face. However, beware of overplucking or waxing too much, because you may end up with a much sparser set of eyebrows than you probably wanted. Instead of panicking, find ways to make naturally thin or overplucked/overwaved brows thicker and more beautiful.
           </p>
           <p>
            Try these few techniques that can help you achieve a more ideal set of eyebrows:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Avoid intense scrubbing of brows
             </strong>
             , especially when removing your makeup. Never rub the hairs or your eye area with a towel, because it can remove more hair. When removing makeup at night, gently dip a cotton ball or swab in makeup removed and swipe it along your brow line.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Meantime, avoid tweezing, threading, or waxing your brows.
             </strong>
             Stay away from the hot wax or tweezers as you wait for your eyebrows to grow thicker. Even if you are bothered by a few stray hairs and you want to shape your brows, resist the temptation  you might overpluck again and make your brows thinner! Wait it out for about a month before you remove hairs, and fill in the sparse areas using a light brown pencil as your brows are getting thicker.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Ask your dermatologist about using Minoxidil, an eyebrow regrowth drug.
             </strong>
             This is a prescription hair loss medication that comes in cream form and can effectively stimulate new hair growth and prevent hair loss. Beware the potential risks and side effects, too, especially when using Minoxidil around your brows. Discuss these risks and compare them with the benefits with your doctor.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Try using an eyelash growth serum.
             </strong>
             If you are wondering, yes, there are high-quality eyelash growth conditioners and products that can also cause thicker, lusher, and stronger brows when used regularly. You can apply it both in the morning and evening, and buy one over the counter. These usually combine vitamins, amino acids, essential oils, and other non-drug ingredients to help stimulate hair growth. Since the product is mainly created for lashes, they are gentle enough on your eyebrows. Buy them in local health stores or online.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            It is important that you make sure your eyebrows are in a thick, healthy, and lush state, because they are crucial in highlighting and framing your face, particularly your eyes. Always keep in mind to avoid overgrooming both your lashes and brows. Keep reading this blog for more eyelash and eyebrow enhancement tips and techniques!
           </p>
