        <div class="entry-content">
         <h1>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'arial black', sans-serif;">
           NUTRIPLANT ORGANIC PLUS FERTILIZER (water Soluble)
          </span>
         </h1>
         <h1>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
           Property and General Advantages:
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p>
          The product which contains multiple small molecules of amino acids, peptides, chelates and their chelates with trace elements is a kind of natural NUTRITIONS for plants. It is
          <u>
           non-poisonous
          </u>
          ,
          <u>
           non-toxic
          </u>
          and safe for the environment, which provides plants (vegetables, fruit trees, flowers and crops, e.t.c) with various nutrients.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
           Benefits:
          </span>
         </h3>
         <ol>
          <li>
           Promotes the growth of
           <strong>
            plant roots, stems and leaves
           </strong>
           .
          </li>
          <li>
           Improves the
           <strong>
            quality and flavor
           </strong>
           of the harvestable parts of crops significantly.
          </li>
          <li>
           Enhances the
           <strong>
            drought resistance
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            infertility resistance
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            diseases and insect pests resistance
           </strong>
           of plants.
          </li>
          <li>
           Helps the seeds
           <strong>
            germinate faster and stronger
           </strong>
           .
          </li>
          <li>
           Improves
           <strong>
            soil structure
           </strong>
           and enhances the bioactivity of the soil.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          Shelf Life:
          <strong>
           24 Months
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Suggested Use
          </strong>
          : Dilute into 1:1000 solution
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Caution:
          </strong>
          Keep at room temperature. Avoid direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.
         </p>
