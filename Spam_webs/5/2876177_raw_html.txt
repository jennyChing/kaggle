                 <h2>
                  Travelers Company Overview
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   The Travelers Companies, Inc.
                  </strong>
                  is the second largest commercial property and casualty insurer in the United States. It also provides personal and financial insurance products and services to businesses, government units, associations and individuals. It was founded in 1853 as Saint Paul Fire and Marine Insurance Co.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   Business Model:
                  </strong>
                  Travelers diverse business lines offer customers a wide range of coverage mainly through independent agents and brokers. The company earns its revenue primarily through sales of insurance premiums -
                  <strong>
                   approximately 87% of TRV's revenue is attributed to premiums earned
                  </strong>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <p>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Similar to standard practice in the insurance industry,
                  <strong>
                   Travelers relies on investing its premium revenue to create a pool of assets known as claims loss provision
                  </strong>
                  , which it can use to pay out future claims.
                 </p>
                 <h3>
                  Segmentation
                 </h3>
                 <p>
                  Travelers divides its business into three main segments namely, Business Insurance, Financial, Professional and International Insurance and Personal Insurance. Business Insurance records 53% revenues, Financial, Professional and International Insurance generates 13% revenues and Personal insurance contributes 34% revenues.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-trv-company-discription-587524833eaf98eb779a387e33768c6a.jpg"/>
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  1. Business Insurance
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  This segment is further divided into six business lines, namely, Select Accounts which sells packaged property and casualty coverage to small businesses and generates 50% rvenues, Commercial Accounts which markets tailored insurance services to mid-size businesses records 13% revenues, National Accounts involves in insurance and risk management services for large companies generates 4%, Target Risk Underwriting provides insurance products and services to address large property, inland marine, ocean marine, equipment breakdown and excess casualty risks generates 7% revenues, Industry-Focused Underwriting and Specialized Distribution which tailors coverage to complex industries, including oil &amp; gas, technology, agriculture, trucking and construction, as well as to the public sector contributes 11% revenues.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-trv-business-revenue-384cb854644425079baeec43aa82534f.jpg"/>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-trv-business-insurance-63d5fb54a858dd033fe90e6e4a74b0f0.jpg"/>
                 </h2>
                 <h2>
                  2. Financial, Professional and International Insurance
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  This segment is divided into two business lines such as Bond &amp; Financial Products which provides bond, insurance products and risk management services. Its Coverage includes Performance, payment and commercial surety and fidelity bonds for construction and general commercial enterprises, Management liability, professional liability and crime coverage and Traditional property and casualty coverage to financial institutions which contributes 65% revenues to the segment. Second Business line is International Business - provides property and casualty insurance products and surety bonds in the United Kingdom, Canada and the Republic of Ireland, and internationally through its operations at Lloyds contributing 35% revenues to the segment.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-trv-business-share-6c91724b896cdcf48285039ab05b3aaf.jpg"/>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-trv-business-financia-and-international-56d326d8139f904b679084778f1b3285.jpg"/>
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  3. Personal Insurance
                 </h2>
                 <p>
                  It has two business lines namely, Personal Automobile Insurance generating 48% revenues and Homeowners Insurance generating 52% revenues. Additional Coverage Offerings: condominium, tenant, home saver (dwelling fire), flood, identity theft, valuable items, boat and yacht, and wedding/special events coverage.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-trv-business-financial-table-e94fe9ac8dc10dd8b9a239e6abee2848.jpg"/>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <img class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source3/dow30-table2-7cac11e2f46ed46c339ec3d569853759.jpg"/>
                 </p>
