          <br/>
          <br/>
          Skill level: Easy
          <br/>
          <br/>
          To crochet the coaster you will need:
          <br/>
          	Cotton yarn with metalized effect, light 3; crochet with 2, 25 mm hook or US size B or
          <br/>
          	1 ply Cotton yarn, fine 2 plus 1 ply thin metalized yarn crochet with 2, 25 mm hook or US size B
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Gauge: with the mentioned above yarn and hooks size, 8 sc = approximately 1 inch
          <br/>
          <br/>

          	Width: 12 cm or approximately 4,70 inches
          <br/>
          	Length: 12 cm or approximately 4,70 inches
