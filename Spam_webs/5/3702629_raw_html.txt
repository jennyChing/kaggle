        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Clues That Someone Really Likes You
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://tipsofdivorce.com/clues-that-someone-really-likes-you/" rel="bookmark" title="5:01 am">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-11-11T05:01:55+00:00">
           November 11, 2013
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://tipsofdivorce.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by TipsofDivorce">
            TipsofDivorce
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://tipsofdivorce.com/a-healthy-relationship-from-the-get-go/">
          <img alt="Someone Likes You" class="size-full wp-image-4798 aligncenter" height="294" src="http://tipsofdivorce.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/someone-likes-you.jpg" title="Clues that Someone Likes You" width="464"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Are you in the early stages of a relationship or
         <a href="http://tipsofdivorce.com/how-to-get-out-there-and-start-dating-again/">
          dating someone new
         </a>
         ? Some people are easy to read, others not so much. Whether youre paranoid or not, we all think twice about what a little gift means, or a nice comment. Are they sincere in their compliments and presents or is there some kind of ulterior motive? Some people think themselves in circles over these things. Other people seem to have a knack for locating sincerity. If you are of the former party, fear not. Though you may be overanalyzing, step back and take a look at what they say to you, what they do and their behavior, and look for a pattern. There are clues for when someone really likes you or if they are just trying to leverage you in some direction.
        </p>
        <p>
         Do they ask about your day (
         <a href="http://thoughtcatalog.com/2013/how-you-know-when-someone-cares/" target="_blank">
          http://thoughtcatalog.com/2013/how-you-know-when-someone-cares/
         </a>
         )? When they do, do they really listen or do so just because they know you like it? If you want a test, ask them follow up questions to check and see if theyve been listening. Does he or she do little things for you? Its this
         <a href="http://tipsofdivorce.com/good-relationships-dont-just-happen/">
          attention to tiny details
         </a>
         that reflect a persons level of caring. People who care for someone often share more than those that dont. Of course, some people are more private, shy or reserved than others. You should keep that in mind. Does this person go out of their way to spend time with you? This is a huge clue. Lastly, those who really care respond in times of crisis. Dont manufacture one to see if they respond; that isnt nice. Its playing with someone elses feelings and you may hurt them just when you find out that they authentically care. Instead, tell them that you are going through a rough time and see if they will be there for you. If they listen to you and take care of you, then
         <a href="http://tipsofdivorce.com/making-your-spouse-your-best-friend/">
          they obviously care
         </a>
         about you.
        </p>
