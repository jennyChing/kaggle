      <h1>
       Eating According to Your Zodiac Sign
      </h1>
      <div class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style addthis_32x32_style clearfix">
       <a class="addthis_button_pinterest_share">
       </a>
       <a class="addthis_button_facebook">
       </a>
       <a class="addthis_button_twitter">
       </a>
       <a class="addthis_button_email">
       </a>
       <a class="addthis_button_compact">
       </a>
       <div class="clear">
       </div>
      </div>
      <!-- Content Area Start -->
      <div class="photo-widget-image">
       <img align="left" alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-98744" src="http://www.peta.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/250_2D00_zodiac.jpg"/>
      </div>
      <p>
       <i>
        The following article was written by Keegan Baur.
       </i>
      </p>
      <p>
       Do you believe in
       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrology" target="_blank">
        astrology
       </a>
       ?Ive never taken my
       <a href="http://www.psychicguild.com/horoscopes_explained.php" target="_blank">
        zodiac sign
       </a>
       or
       <a href="http://www.0800-horoscope.com/weekly.php" target="_blank">
        weekly horoscopes
       </a>
       very seriously, although the latter can be fun to read from time to time. Id heard of determining whether or not a
       <a href="http://zodiaccompatibility.me/" target="_blank">
        romantic relationship will be successful
       </a>
       based on zodiac signs, but until recently, Id never heard that your sign could
       <a href="http://www.shedyourweight.com/personalize_your_diet_by_your_zodiac_sign.html" target="_blank">
        dictate the foods that you should and shouldnt eat
       </a>
       !It makes sense, thoughif your zodiac sign determines your personality, your compatibility with others, and who you are in general, why shouldnt it also determine which foods are best for you? I browsed a few websites, and below is a compilation of what I found. Next time you get the munchies, consider which foods might be a good fit for you, based on your sign. Whether or not youre a believer, these delicious and healthful recipes are sure to please your palate.
      </p>
      <h3>
       <b>
        Fire Signs: Dont Burn Yourself Out
       </b>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Aries
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Brown rice, bananas, fruit juices, olives, tomatoes, onions, lettuce, cauliflower, cucumbers, spinach, radishes, broccoli, beans, lentils, pumpkin, walnuts, figs, dried apricots, garlic, and mustard
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Spicy food, salt, and alcohol
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Leo
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Whole grains, rice, citrus fruits, apples, peaches, root vegetables (e.g., potatoes, carrots, and radishes), spinach, broccoli, bitter greens (e.g., kale and mustard greens), nuts, almonds, sunflower seeds, and figs
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Spicy food, and dairy products
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Sagittarius
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Whole grains, whole-grain cereals, pears, apples, oranges, strawberries, root vegetables (e.g., potatoes, carrots, and radishes), onions, olives, figs, and garlic
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Spicy food, sweets, refined sugar, and alcohol
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Recipes for Fire Signs
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://kblog.lunchboxbunch.com/2011/05/easy-herbed-potato-soup.html" target="_blank">
         Herbed Potato Soup
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/fill-fiber-lentil-quinoa-salad/">
         Lentil-Quinoa Salad
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://blogs.babble.com/family-kitchen/2011/09/14/grumpy-gator-green-smoothie/" target="_blank">
         Grumpy Gator Green Smoothie
        </a>
        or
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/superfood-cuisine-cheesy-kale-crisps/">
         Cheesy Kale Crisps
        </a>
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h3>
       <b>
        Air Signs: Go Nuts
       </b>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Gemini
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Plums, oranges, grapefruit, grape juice, raisins, apples, lettuce, cauliflower, spinach, carrots, celery, green beans, tomatoes, vegan yogurt, almonds, cayenne, garlic, and ginger
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Coffee, root vegetables (e.g. potatoes and radishes), yeasty foods, and refined sugar
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Libra
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Whole grains, oats, apples, grapes, strawberries, raisins, steamed vegetables, spinach, tomatoes, peas, carrots, corn, nondairy cheeses, vegan yogurt, nuts, and almonds
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Alcohol, carbonated beverages, yeasty foods, and refined sugar
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Aquarius
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Apples, oranges, pears, steamed vegetables, cabbage, celery, corn, carrots, tomatoes, broccoli, soy yogurt, nuts, dates, figs, cayenne, garlic, ginger, and protein bars
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Coffee, yeasty foods, and refined sugar
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Recipes for Air Signs
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.theppk.com/2011/02/mac-shews/" target="_blank">
         Mac &amp; Shews
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/recipes/ginger-baked-tofu/">
         Ginger Baked Tofu
        </a>
        with steamed vegetables (Make sure that its
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/perfect-tofu/">
         Pan-Fried Tofu
        </a>
        !)
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/homemade-almond-milk/">
         Homemade Almond Milk
        </a>
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h3>
       <b>
        Water Signs: Avoid the Oil
       </b>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Cancer
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Wheat, whole-grain rye, rice, oats, fruits, bananas, steamed vegetables, broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, tomatoes, beets, pumpkins, cucumbers, seaweed, vegan yogurt, beans, and natural sugars
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Oily foods, salt, sweets, and refined sugar
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Scorpio
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Fruits, bananas, black cherries, coconut, steamed vegetables, green salads, leeks, cauliflower, onions, radishes, tomatoes, asparagus, cucumbers, beets, beans, lentils, and almonds
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Oily foods, yeasty foods, salt, sweets, and refined sugar
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Pisces
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Wheat, whole-grain cereals, rice, oats, fruits, apples, grapes, oranges, lemons, peaches, plums, steamed vegetables, spinach, onions, seaweed, beans, dates, and natural sugars
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Coffee, oily foods, yeasty foods, asparagus, salt, sweets, and refined sugar
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Recipes for Water Signs
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/likes-sushi/">
         Vegan Sushi
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.veganpiggy.com/2010/06/el-cheapo-burritos-vegan-black-bean.html" target="_blank">
         Black-Bean Veggie Burritos
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/wheres-raw-oatmeal/">
         Raw Oatmeal
        </a>
        topped with your favorite fruit
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h3>
       <b>
        Earth Signs: Keep Things Light
       </b>
      </h3>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Taurus
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Cranberries, asparagus, beets, cauliflower, cucumbers, spinach, onions, radishes, pumpkin, nuts, beans, and horseradish
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Rich and heavy foods, and excessive amounts of carbohydrates
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Virgo
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Whole grains and cereals, oats, fruit salads, fruit juices, lemon juice, bananas, oranges, leafy greens, vegetables with spices, sprouts, soups, teas, and almonds
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Rich and heavy foods, and chocolate
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Capricorn
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Eat:
        </b>
        Rice, fruit salads, fruit juices, oranges, lemons, vegetables with spices, cabbage, corn, potatoes, sprouts, soups, teas, peanuts, figs, and flaxseeds
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Avoid:
        </b>
        Rich and heavy foods, spicy foods, and chocolate
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4>
       <b>
        Recipes for Earth Signs
       </b>
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/elusive-vegan-engagement-chicken/">
         Vegan Engagement Chicken
        </a>
        with
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/recipes/steamed-asparagus/">
         steamed asparagus
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/living/food/crispy-cucumber-sandwiches/">
         Crispy Cucumber Sandwich
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.peta.org/recipes/fruit-salad-papaya-mint-puree/">
         Fruit SaladWith Papaya-Mint Pure
        </a>
        or a
        <a href="http://kblog.lunchboxbunch.com/2010/05/strawberry-lemonade-smoothie-frosty.html" target="_blank">
         Strawberry Lemonade Smoothie
        </a>
       </li>
      </ul>

       Let us know which of the recipes recommended for your zodiac sign sound the best to you!
