            <h1>
             Relocating to Palm Bay  Florida
            </h1>
            <p>
             <em>
              Posted
									by
              <a href="http://www.nationalrealtyfla.com/blog/author/gale-bray/">
               Gale Bray
              </a>
              on Wednesday, March 2nd, 2011 at 4:00am.
             </em>
            </p>
           </header>
           <div class="body">
            <p>
             <img alt="Palm Bay Florida" height="152" src="http://www.nationalrealtyfla.com/images/palm-bay-florida_250.gif" style="float: right; margin: 10px;" title="palm-bay-florida_250" width="250"/>
             For many people, the idea of relocating to Palm Bay, Florida, seems like a faraway dream. But the dream of moving to Palm Bay might be closer than you think. Its possible for you to move to Palm Bay and enjoy all the benefits and amenities of living in this friendly community with its booming economy and commitment to education. This jewel of the Space Coast of Florida is a great place to live for young people, families, and retirees alike. Theres something for everyone in Palm Bay, and you can enjoy these benefits year-round if you choose to relocate to this beautiful city.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Palm Bay Employment
            </h2>
            <p>
             There are plenty of jobs available in the aerospace industry that is the heart of Palm Bays economy. The Space Coast gets its name from its proximity to Cape Canaveral and because its a hub for the aerospace industry in the area. But if you dont work in aerospace, there is plenty of other work to be had. Orlando is only forty-five minutes away, and this major metropolitan area has plenty of jobs in all different kinds of industries so that you can make a living for yourself. Relocating to Palm Bay is just as much for the worker as it is for the retiree.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Kids Love Palm Bay
            </h3>
            <p>
             For kids, the thought of relocating to Palm Bay can be particularly exciting, since the city is so close to Orlando, which is the amusement park capital of the world. With Disney World, Sea World, Universal Studios, and countless other theme parks so close by, your kids will never have to worry about being bored or having nothing to do. And you dont need to be nearly as creative in thinking up fun places for your family to visit on days off. Living in Palm Bay is special because there are so many special attractions nearby.
            </p>
            <p>
             But relocating to Palm Bay doesnt mean that youre sacrificing education for the sake of fun. Brevard County actually boasts some of the highest-rated public schools in the United States. SAT scores consistently rank among the countrys highest, and there are also several prestigious colleges and universities in the area. Brevard County has a high percentage of college graduates, which goes to show that the county is committed to education, but also that its residents are committed to the community. Once you move to Palm Bay, you will never want to wander too far away.
            </p>
