             QBs guru and "biomechanics devotee" Dennis Gile says Colin Kaepernick's delivery has been altered with the intent of improving his accuracy, particularly on downfield passes.

             Gile worked with Kaepernick from January through April at EXOS training facility in Phoenix.  Gile says Kaepernick's head positioning and footwork were altered to make his delivery more "compact," and his stance has been widened, shortening up Kaepernick's release.  Kaepernick will resume his offseason regimen with Gile in early July.  Delivery alterations have a tendency to revert to original form when the bullets go live, but it's good to see Kaepernick acknowledging his deficiencies and working to improve.
