         <a href="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/marriage1.jpg">
          <img alt="marriage1" class=" size-full wp-image-1632 aligncenter" height="248" src="http://funfacts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/marriage1.jpg" width="384"/>

        <p>
         Some interesting, odd and disturbing marriage facts that you might not want to read if you have your big day coming up any time soon.
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Due to jobs,
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/kids-sleeping-in-the-most-uncomfortable-places-possible/" title="Kids Sleeping In The Most Uncomfortable Places Possible!">
           kids
          </a>
          , hobbies, and home and family responsibilities, the average married couple spends just four minutes a day alone together.
         </li>
         <li>
          Over 75% of people who marry partners from an affair eventually divorce.
         </li>
         <li>
          Washington, D.C., has the lowest marriage rate in the nation.
         </li>
         <li>
          A woman named Erika La Tour Eiffel married the Eiffel Tower in 2007.
         </li>
         <li>
          Traditionally, bridesmaids would be dressed in similar bride-like gowns to confuse rival suitors, evil spirits, and robbers.
         </li>
         <li>
          Married couples tend to have
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/fat-people-falling-down-too-funny/" title="Fat People Falling Down! Too Funny!">
           fatter
          </a>
          waistlines, which can lead to a decrease in sexual attraction and general health. Additionally, a spouses chances of becoming obese increase by 37% if his or her partner is obese.
         </li>
         <li>
          Getting married younger than age 25 dramatically raises the divorce risk. Also, the divorce risk is higher when the
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/woman-head-butts-walmart-employee-unbelievable/" title="Woman Head Butts Walmart Employee! Unbelievable!">
           woman
          </a>
          is much older than the man.
         </li>
         <li>
          A study found that mail-order marriages are less likely to end in divorce than marriages overall in the U.S.
         </li>
         <li>
          The average married couple has
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/odd-and-interest-fun-facts-about-sex/" title="Odd And Interest Fun Facts About Sex">
           sex
          </a>
          58 times per year, just a littlemore than once per week.
         </li>
         <li>
          1 in 5 couples have a sexless marriage, having less than 10 sessions per year.
         </li>
         <li>
          In the United States, over 50% of first marriages end in divorce, 67% of second marriages end in divorce, and nearly 74% of third marriages end in divorce.
         </li>
         <li>
          Interracial marriages were banned in the United States between 1776  1967
         </li>
         <li>
          In Iran, a law from 2013 allows men to marry their 13-year-old adopted daughters.
         </li>
         <li>
          Couples that live together prior to
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/the-truth-behind-your-diamond-engagement-ring/" title="The Truth Behind Your Diamond Engagement Ring">
           engagement
          </a>
          have a higher divorce rates than those who do not.
         </li>
         <li>
          80% of marriages throughout history have been between 2nd cousins or closer.
         </li>
         <li>
          Nearly 60% of married adults have had at least one affair.
         </li>
         <li>
          About 50%of emotional affairs become
          <a href="http://funfacts.ca/15-interesting-and-odd-fun-facts-about-masturbation/" title="15 Interesting And Odd Fun Facts About Masturbation">
           sexual
          </a>
          affairs.
         </li>
         <li>
          A study found that couples who spend more money on their wedding have a higher divorce rate.
         </li>
         <li>
          No sex in a marriage has a much more powerful negative impact on a marriage than good sex has a positive impact.
         </li>
        </ol>
