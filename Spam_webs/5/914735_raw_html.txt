          Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio: #Wine
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-byline">
          by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://findingourwaynow.com/author/findingourway" itemprop="url" rel="author" title="Posts by Susan P. Cooper">

           Mar 8, 2015
          </time>
          
          <a class="comments-link" href="http://findingourwaynow.com/2015/03/luna-vineyards-pinot-grigio-wine.html#comments" itemprop="discussionURL">

        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="at-above-post addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio: #Wine" data-url="http://findingourwaynow.com/2015/03/luna-vineyards-pinot-grigio-wine.html">
         </div>
         <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
         </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio, findingourwaynow.com" class=" alignright wp-image-30032" height="243" src="http://findingourwaynow.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Luna-Vineyards-Pinot-Grigio-Label-Image.png" title="Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio, findingourwaynow.com" width="160"/>
          It was one of those times when I just needed a good glass of wine to end the day. Enter Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio. I do find a good Pinot Grigio to be very refreshing, and this one is just that.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           If youve ever been to the Napa Valley Wine Region, then you know about the
           <a href="http://silveradotrail.com/">
            Silverado Trail
           </a>
           . Its often called the road less traveled. But it is so worth the time to do so. The Silverado Trail is a beautiful drive and a fitting location for any winery. Thats where youll find Luna Vineyards.
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           One of the many stops to make
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           on
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           this g
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           orgeous
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
           drive is the Luna Vineyards lovely Tuscan Tasting room.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
          <strong>
           <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
            Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio
           </span>
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a href="http://findingourwaynow.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Luna-Vineyards.png">
           <img alt="Luna Vineyards, findingourwaynow.com" class=" alignleft wp-image-30034" height="265" src="http://findingourwaynow.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Luna-Vineyards.png" title="Luna Vineyards, findingourwaynow.com" width="326"/>
          </a>
          <a href="https://www.lunavineyards.com/">
           Luna Vineyards
          </a>
          began in 1995. It all started when the founders fell in love with the location where they now exist. After buying the land, they startedwith the Pinot Grigio grape varietal. They have since progressed into a winery and vineyard of note, critically acclaimed for many of their current offerings.
         </p>
         <p>
          With a deep appreciation for the land that yields their wine, Luna Vineyards maintains sustainable farming practices that allow the diverse ecosystem to continue to thrive. In addition to their Pinot Grigio, they now offer a variety of labels and vintages  both red and white alike.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://findingourwaynow.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Luna-Vineyards-Pinot-Grigio-Bottle-Image-Version-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio, findingourwaynow.com" class="alignright wp-image-30031" height="492" src="http://findingourwaynow.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Luna-Vineyards-Pinot-Grigio-Bottle-Image-Version-2-260x1024.jpg" title="Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio, findingourwaynow.com" width="125"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This is what the winemaker has to say about their Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio.
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          <em>
           Fresh aromas of honey, lemon curd, and citrus blossom greet the nose. Bright grapefruit on the palate, our Pinot Grigio has a refreshingly brisk citrus, lively texture, and good balance. With the dominance of citrus zest and a steely edge, this wine finishes with a juicy acidity and bright, long finish.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          You can find this wine at bevmo.com, wine.com, totalwine.com and just about any good wine purveyor.
         </p>
         <p>
          One of the great joys I have is the fact that we can hop in our car at any given time and visit some fantastic places such as the Silverado Trail in NapaValley. We never take that for granted. Because of that I have the opportunity to showcase some fun wines I find along the way, like this Luna Pinot Grigio. Enjoy.
         </p>
         <p>
          Time for a glass
         </p>
         <p>
          If you enjoyed reading about this Luna Vineyards Pinot Grigio, check out
          <a href="http://findingourwaynow.com/2014/10/estancia-winery-pinot-grigio-wine.html" title="Estancia Winery Pinot Grigio: #Wine">
           Estancia Winery Pinot Grigio
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://findingourwaynow.com/2013/08/coppola-bianco-pinot-grigio-wine.html" title="Coppola Bianco Pinot Grigio: Wine">
           Clay Station Winery Pinot Grigio
          </a>
          , and
          <a href="http://findingourwaynow.com/2013/01/fritz-winery-pinot-noir-2002-wine.html" title="Fritz Winery Pinot Noir: #Wine">
           Coppola Bianco Pinot Grigio
          </a>
