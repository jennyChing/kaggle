           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even">
             <p>
              Start Your Morning With an Alarm Clock That Makes You Coffee  For those who cant function in the morning, a cup of coffee is key. For those who cant even function enough to make that cup of coffee, theres
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/new-gallery-5/" target="_blank">
               The Barisieur
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <div class="file file-image file-image-png" id="file-189673--21">
              <div class="content">
               <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/mentalfloss.com/sites/default/files/styles/insert_main_wide_image/public/cofee-1.png?resize=620%2C465" title=""/>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption" style="text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/" target="_blank">
               JoshuaRenouf
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption">
             </div>
             <p>
              This innovative gadget awakens the sleeper with the smell of coffee and the gentle rattle ofstainless steel ballbearings as the water boils.Picture waking up slowly to the smell of roasted coffee beans and only having to sit up toenjoy.
             </p>
             <div class="file file-image file-image-png" id="file-189675--21">
              <div class="content">
               <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/mentalfloss.com/sites/default/files/styles/insert_main_wide_image/public/coffee-3.png?resize=620%2C465" title=""/>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption" style="text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/" target="_blank">
               JoshuaRenouf
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption">
             </div>
             <p>
              Take sugar or milk? Theres a special compartment for milk so the liquid stays fresh and cool until youre ready to use it in the morning. On the front, theres a drawer for sugar. The whole tray can even be removed for easy cleaning.
             </p>
             <div class="file file-image file-image-png" id="file-189674--21">
              <div class="content">
               <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/mentalfloss.com/sites/default/files/styles/insert_main_wide_image/public/coffee-2.png?resize=620%2C465" title=""/>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption" style="text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/" target="_blank">
               JoshuaRenouf
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="file file-image file-image-png" id="file-189676--21">
              <div class="content">
               <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/mentalfloss.com/sites/default/files/styles/insert_main_wide_image/public/coffee-4.png?resize=620%2C465" title=""/>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption" style="text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/" target="_blank">
               JoshuaRenouf
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="imagecaption">
             </div>
             <p>
              The gadget also has an actual alarm that can be set to sound before or during the coffee making process.
             </p>
             <p>
              This invention was thought up by
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/" target="_blank">
               Joshua Renouf
              </a>
              ,a recent graduate from Nottingham Trent University, for his final project. You cant buy the product just yet, but Renouf is in the process of making it accessible to the public this year. You can send in an inquiry
              <a href="http://www.joshrenoufdesign.com/purchase-products/" target="_blank">
               here.
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               [h/t:

                Design-Milk.com
               </a>
               ]
              </em>
