      <h1 class="title">
       Command Center-Getting Organized for Back To School
      </h1>
      <div class="date-comments">
       <p class="fl">
        September 2, 2014
       </p>
       <p class="fr">
        <a href="http://www.crafttestdummies.com/category/commentary/" rel="category tag">
         Jenny Sez

        <a href="http://www.crafttestdummies.com/category/commentary/sponsored-posts/" rel="category tag">

       <p>
        <span class="kix-wordhtmlgenerator-word-node" style="color: #000000;">
         <i>
          This shop is part of a social shopper marketing insight campaign with Pollinate Media Group and Office Depot, but all my opinions are my own. #pmedia #inspirestudents
         </i>
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.google.com/url?q=http%3A%2F%2Fmy-disclosur.es%2FOBsstV&amp;sa=D&amp;sntz=1&amp;usg=AFQjCNFJ_0UY86ahwQm6YDhmMpbtVVRGKw" style="color: #6611cc;" target="_blank">
         <span class="kix-wordhtmlgenerator-word-node" style="color: #386eff;">
          <i>
          </i>
          http://my-disclosur.es/OBsstV

       </p>
       <p>
        My kiddos headed back to school last week and it became clear
        <em>
         <strong>
          on the first day
         </strong>
        </em>
        that this was going to be a super-busy year. My son is in his last year of middle school and is participating in band, two soccer teams, piano lessons and a wind symphony. Little girl is also taking pianoandparticipating in soccer, and Im volunteering for the music boosters, Market Day, and am an AFS liaison. And hubby travels. A LOT.
       </p>
       <p>
        In order to keep all our schedules straight, I decided that we needed a Command Center- a place we could keep our schedules, notes, lists, and important information. And I even had a little nook in mind:
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_19440" style="width: 383px">
        <img alt="Command Center DIY BEFORE" class="wp-image-19440 size-large" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_2922-373x500.jpg" width="373"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Heres how the area looked before- a calendar on the wall and things just shoved on a shelf.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Its the area right next to the fridge, so I know that everyone will be looking in that general direction in the morning. But obviously, it needed a lot of work.
       </p>
       <p>
        So FIRST, I went shopping at Office Max.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         <em>
          Side note- this is a guilty pleasure of mine anyway. I love office supply stores and walking up the aisle with all of the markers and pens gives me the happy shivers.
         </em>
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_19441" style="width: 510px">
        <img alt="Command Center DIY" class="wp-image-19441 size-large" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_2937-500x500.jpg" width="500"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Colored Sharpie Markers give the me the happy shivers.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        I knew that one of the components had to be color-coding- so that I could easily identify the person, activity, and time!
       </p>
       <p>
        I wanted to also have a write/on wipe/off calendar- so I made sure I picked up some Expo markers, too.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Command Center DIY" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19439" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_2918-373x500.jpg" width="373"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        In the end, I made sure that the markers, pens, and Sharpies all had the same colors.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_19448" style="width: 510px">
        <img alt="Command Center DIY- HAUL" class="wp-image-19448 size-large" height="333" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6226-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         I even got the little Post-it flags so that I could mark areas for signatures and special days!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Now I had to actually create the Command Center space. First, I removed the old switchplates and hardware. There used to be place for a corded phone- but we dont even have one of those. Modern age, eh?
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6223" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19445" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6223-333x500.jpg" width="333"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Next I coated the wall space with 3 coats of chalkboard paint.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6224" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19446" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6224-333x500.jpg" width="333"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Then I got busy with the cork tiles. I cut them with an Xacto knife so that I could nestle them in the lip of the cabinetry.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6225" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19447" height="333" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6225-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6227" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19449" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6227-333x500.jpg" width="333"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        And guess what? Double sided sticky tape was enough to hold them in place.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6229" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19451" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6229-333x500.jpg" width="333"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        I decided to forego a calendar on the wall, and instead put in on the fridge. And I used all of my color-coded Expo markers to write in our schedules!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Command Center! " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19452" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6240-333x500.jpg" width="333"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        I put a little caddy on the shelf to hold the markers and pens and some white note cards. Now I can write each member of the family a note in THEIR COLOR. The kids even seem to be less grumbly about their chores when they are written on the note card.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6241" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19453" height="333" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6241-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        And here you can see where they are on the cork wall. Its right at their level, too- so I know they wont miss it!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="IMG_6244" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19456" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6244-356x500.jpg" width="356"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY Command Center with Chalkboard wall" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19455" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6243-333x500.jpg" width="333"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        I like the chalk wall, too- I can add notes for the day, post the snack options or even reminders to do the chores. And I can plug my cell phone in there to charge too!
       </p>
       <p>
        Ive been having working on my chalk art/writing skills. Better keep practicing!
       </p>
       <p>
        And here is the full Command Center with a view of the calendar, chalkboard and cork wall.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_19454" style="width: 366px">
        <img alt="Office Max DIY Command Center! " class="wp-image-19454 size-large" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_6242-356x500.jpg" width="356"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         AFTER- a place for a color-coded calendar, notes, reminders, and special events. We are finally organized!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Even though there is a glare on the chalk wall, I still think it gives you a great view of the whole area and how FUNCTIONAL it all is now. And it wasnt expensive for the supplies- about $50!
       </p>
       <p>
        By the way, right now there are some great prices at

       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Family Command Center DIY on CraftTestDummies.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19457" height="500" src="http://crafttherapy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/bts3-500x500.jpg" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Its working so well already that I cant believe I waited this long to transform that dingy little corner.
        <br/>
        How do YOU organize your busy schedules? Id love to hear your family organization tips!
