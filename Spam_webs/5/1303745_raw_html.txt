   Autism Dog Services Inc.(ADS)relies on donations from the public as it raises, trains &amp; places service dogs for children with autism &amp; related disorders. Dakota's anxiety is often overwhelming for him, causing him to withdraw or alienate himself &amp; elevates other behaviors related to his disabilities. Thanks to ADS, Dakota's new pal Victor not only helps reduce his anxiety but also provides him with safety, companionship &amp; independence. Your donation is life changing.
