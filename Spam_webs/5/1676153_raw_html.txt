         <p>
          Packing a lunch is a mission, yes its cheaper and probably healthier but I think we can all agree that its a bit of a task planning and packing a lunch, especially when there are a million other things on your mind. So often Im astounded by the long line of cars at the Macdonalds drive in, or the ques of people at Woolies (guilty as charged).
          <a href="http://jobest.co.za/love-food/" target="_blank">
           Love Food
          </a>
          in
          <a href="http://www.braamfontein.org.za/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.braamfontein.org.za/', 'Braamfontein');" target="_blank">
           Braamfontein
          </a>
          however is the answer to all your lunch packing and belly wobbling woes.
