         Alpha G:Corp Management Services Private Limited is a subsidiary company of one of the leading real estate companies in the country dedicated towards creating spaces that reflect state-of-the-art architecture, elegance and opulence.          <a href="http://www.alphagcorp.com" target="_blank">
          Alpha G:Corp
         </a>

        </p>

         However, the construction of the building and its amenities is  just the beginning. Providing infrastructure that can last long and can work 24 hours a day is most important to any commercial, retail or residential project. And that formed the genesis of Alpha Management Services. A state of the art management company that not only provides the basic facilities like security and housekeeping for smooth functioning but also brings on board value additions that can help to control costs and give the development a long term value leading to better asset valuations years down the line.
        </p>
