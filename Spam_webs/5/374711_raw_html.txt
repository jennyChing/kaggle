                     FREDDY AND THE PHANTOMS IN #HSPMF 2015
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7026386334070829654" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                       <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <h2 style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="color: white;">
                       <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                        <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                         #HSPMF - Danish bands on stage 2015.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </h2>
                     <h2 style="text-align: center;">
                     </h2>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-x5ByD8zQ_AI/VGxpO48pkzI/AAAAAAAAFfY/-yucFLv5tS4/s1600/FREDDY-AND-THE-PHANTOMS-foto-2012.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="414" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-x5ByD8zQ_AI/VGxpO48pkzI/AAAAAAAAFfY/-yucFLv5tS4/s1600/FREDDY-AND-THE-PHANTOMS-foto-2012.jpg" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: white;">
                        <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                         Freddy and the Phantoms is
a Danish blues rock group formed in 2010, led by "Freddy" (Frederick
Schnoor) which is the band's lead singer, songwriter and lead guitarist. The
rest of the band members are
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                         -
Rune Hansen: Drums, Mads Wilken: bass Morten Rahm: Guitar and Pedal Steel and
Anders Haahr: Organ.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <span style="color: white;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                        The band recorded between 2010-2014 three studio albums and played over 150
concerts in Denmark and toured in Germany in 2012 and 2013. Freddy and the
Phantoms released in Augsut 2014 their third studio album Times of Division.
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <b>
                      <span style="color: white;">
                       <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       </span>
                       <span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </b>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <b>
                      <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                      </span>
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: white;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <b>
                        More
from the Danish music scene is:
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                       <b>
                        #HSPMF on stage: Pretty Maids, The Danish Mafia, Kings of Rock and Glam Gang.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <span style="color: white; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">
                     </span>
                     <span style="color: white;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
