    <h1>
     Cut The Confusion: On-Grid, Off-Grid, Grid-Tied, Grid-Connect
    </h1>
    <br/>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     Confused by the terms on-grid, off-grid, grid-tied, grid-connect and almost any phrase that contains the word grid? Youre not alone. Thats why we compiled this handy guide with definitions, so you can impress your friends with your solar prowess  or at least speak the same language as your installer!
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      Same same: on-grid, grid-connect, grid-tied
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p>
     Youll be relieved to know that the terms on-grid, grid-connect and grid-tied are different names for the same system. They all relate to a solar photovoltaic (PV) system that is connected to mains electricity (the main power or utility facility in your area).
    </p>
    <p>
     Whenever your system draws power from the grid, your provider charges you and adds the usage to your next power bill.
    </p>
    <p>
     In a grid-tie or grid-connect system, a special inverter is used to ensure electricity can flow to and from the grid. This happens when your solar power system has produced excess energy, which is sent to the grid for others to use. Youll likely receive solar credits for power you send off to the grid.
    </p>
    <p>
     And
     <a href="http://www.3quotes.com.au/solar-power/do-solar-panels-work-on-cloudy-days.html">
      at night or on cloudy days
     </a>
     , electricity is supplied from the grid to make up for the shortfall  since solar panels dont produce much or any power at these times.
    </p>
    <p>
     You can hook up a back-up battery bank to a grid-connect system, if you dont want to rely on the grid for supply. However, these can be costly, bulky and unreliable.
    </p>
    <p>
     This is why
     <a href="http://www.3quotes.com.au/solar-power/should-i-go-grid-connected-or-of-off-grid.html">
      grid-connect systems
     </a>
     are so popular  they provide the benefits of solar, with the backup of a reliable electricity source. However, for this reason, they put more pressure on resources and are less gentle on the environment. And be aware that in the event of a power outage, you wont receive any electricity since the system shuts down.
    </p>
    <p>
     Other terms for on-grid solar are utility-interactive, grid-direct and grid-intertied.
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      What is an off-grid solar power system?
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p>
     If you long to be free from rising electricity bills and cut your ecological footprint, an off-grid solar power system may be for you. These are most common in rural and regional Australia, where a connection to the grid isnt possible.
    </p>
    <p>
     An off-grid system isnt connected to the grid  so it doesnt provide or receive any extra energy. Instead, it sources and stores solar energy and allows your household to be totally self-sufficient.
    </p>
    <p>
     Most properties with off-grid solar also have a back-up battery or generator to store energy for when the sun in MIA  this scenario is sometimes referred to as hybrid. Despite this, the system needs to be big enough to provide 100 percent of the propertys energy needs. Youll also need to keep an eye on the off-grid systems output, to ensure it falls below the maximum levels.
    </p>
