           Cooling Tower Solutions - A Necessity in Modern Age
          </div>
          <div class="bw_blogentry_date">
           05.06.2014 18:25
          </div>
         </div>
         <div style="margin:0px 0px 10px 0px">
          <p>
           You must have noticed cooling towers present on the rooftops of offices, huge residential buildings, shopping complexes, data centers and so on. These towers came in varied sizes and are a very important component of the HVAC system of any building. HVAC mean heating, ventilation and air-conditioning. More than in residential buildings, schools, hospitals, shopping complexes, heat is generated in thermal and chemical power plants where Cooling Tower Solutions are a must.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           There are many Indian companies who offer quality Cooling Tower Solutions to its clients. Aeron Composite Pvt. Ltd. is one such reputed name that has carved its niche in offering Cooling Tower Solutions in the market. Based in Gujarat, the company uses high level of engineering and its R&amp;D to continually deliver excellent
           <a href="http://www.aeroncomposite.com/cooling_towers.html" target="_blank">
            cooling tower solutions
           </a>
           to its worldwide clients.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            List of Cooling Tower Solutions:
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Some of the Cooling Tower Solutions are mentioned below to cope up with the common problems encountered by cooling towers in course of time.
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            FRP Sections for structural Framing
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Decking
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Stairs, Ladders and Handrails
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Claddings
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Cable Tray
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Partition Wall
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Fan Stacks
           </li>
           <li>
            FRP Louvers
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>

         <div style="margin:0px 0px 10px 0px">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Unique Features of Cooling Tower Solutions:
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Some of the unique features of the Cooling Tower Solutions offered by reputed manufactures are including but not limited to the following ones.
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Resistance to corrosion
           </li>
           <li>
            Easy in fabrication
           </li>
           <li>
            Low maintenance requirement
           </li>
           <li>
            Long shelf life
           </li>
           <li>
            Cost effective
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           The Cooling Tower Solutions are very effective to tackle problems like algae, corrosion and scaling among cooling towers. In the top deck of these towers, a sight of algae is very common and this eventually led to clogging of pipes, tubes, etc and in turn decreases the heat transfer efficiency. Continual evaporation also results in scaling as pure water is evaporated and minerals are left behind in the cooling towers. The chemicals used in treatment of water used for the cooling purpose are also major contributors of all the problems that are eradicated or lessen by applying Cooling Tower Solutions. Thus, the new age building needs such solutions because cooling towers are now a common fixture in buildings.
          </p>
