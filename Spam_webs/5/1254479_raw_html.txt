    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <p>
      The 2008-2009 Roster
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ohio_Secretary_of_State_1-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-Ohio_Secretary_of_State-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      provided by the Ohio Secretary of State enumerates 251 cities (and 681
      <a href="/wiki/List_of_villages_in_Ohio" title="List of villages in Ohio">
       villages
      </a>
      ) in
      <a href="/wiki/Ohio" title="Ohio">
       Ohio
      </a>
      . Municipality names are not unique: there is a village of
      <a href="/wiki/Centerville,_Gallia_County,_Ohio" title="Centerville, Gallia County, Ohio">
       Centerville
      </a>
      and a city of
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Centerville,_Montgomery_County,_Ohio" title="Centerville, Montgomery County, Ohio">
       Centerville
      </a>
      ; also a city of
      <a href="/wiki/Oakwood,_Montgomery_County,_Ohio" title="Oakwood, Montgomery County, Ohio">
       Oakwood
      </a>
      and two similarly named villages:
      <a href="/wiki/Oakwood,_Cuyahoga_County,_Ohio" title="Oakwood, Cuyahoga County, Ohio">
       Oakwood, Cuyahoga County, Ohio
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Oakwood,_Paulding_County,_Ohio" title="Oakwood, Paulding County, Ohio">
       Oakwood, Paulding County, Ohio
      </a>
      . Population is as of the
      <a href="/wiki/2010_United_States_Census" title="2010 United States Census">
       2010 census
      </a>
      .

      </sup>
