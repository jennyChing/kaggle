        <p>
         Some of the best food in Paris isnt even French. Hell, it isnt even English, despite the fact that the chefsbehindLe Bal Caf originally hail from the UK.
        </p>
        <p>
         Le Bals Anna Trattles and Alice Quillet recently took us on
         <a href="http://munchies.vice.com/videos/chefs-night-out-le-bal-cafe" target="_blank">
          a boozynight out through Paris
         </a>
         that ended back at the cafwith a hearty, Asian-influenced meal of pork belly over rice, topped off with kimchi, vegetables, and a fried egg.
        </p>
        <p>
         Itll take about 10 hours to cook, so start this in the morning with an expertly madecup of coffeenone of that
         <a href="http://munchies.vice.com/articles/bad-coffee-is-dying-a-slow-death-in-paris" target="_blank">
          old-school Parisian sludge
         </a>
         and refrigerate it overnight. Then youve got pork belly ready to crisp up for dinner all week long.Life could be a lot worse, you know.
        </p>
