     <h2>
      Uncle Sam's Grave Still Closed After Vandalism and Flag Burning
     </h2>
    </div>
    <div class="tourismNewsListIcon">
    </div>
    <p>
     In mid-March, two vandals went on a rampage of desecration in Troy, New York's Oakwood Cemetery, where
     <a href="/set/HISTsam.html">
      Uncle Sam
     </a>
     is buried. Sam "Uncle Sam" Wilson's decorative urn and headstone were damaged, and a flag pole was knocked over, the American flag set on fire. The ghouls knocked over five other headstones. Officials offered a reward, and two suspects where apprehended (one was already in jail on an unrelated charge). The cemetery is once again open to the public, although the Uncle Sam plot has been cordoned off for the time being...
    </p>
    <p>
     <em>
      Thanks to Ken J. for the tip.
     </em>
    </p>
