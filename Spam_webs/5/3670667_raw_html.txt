       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Top 5 Free Music Apps
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/top-5-free-music-apps/" rel="bookmark" title="11:49 am">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-07-04T11:49:55+00:00" pubdate="">
           July 4, 2013
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/author/marvin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Marvin">
            Marvin
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         |
         <a href="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/index.php">
          Back Home
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         In this day and age when technology has taken its full advantage of the market, people have gone digital when it comes to listening to music. Thus, major music companies as well as independent labels have made their tunes available for downloads.
         <span id="more-2277">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The rise of smart phones and other devices such as iPad, iPod has created a new market that can end the life of physical cds. Developers have created different downloadable applications for music playing in these gadgets, so here I list down my top 5 free music apps available in no chronological order.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.windowsphone.com/en-us/store/app/shazam/2f8d5271-2b81-e011-986b-78e7d1fa76f8" target="_blank">
           Shazam
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2278" height="209" src="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/ScreenHunter_2059-Jul.-04-19.12.jpg" title="Shazam" width="263"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This allows you to identify songs quickly. Its a great app because its easy to use and its accurate! Also displays the lyrics in time with the music, artist info and tour info. Another cool feature is the Facebook integration as you can see your friends who are using this and post comments.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/soundhound/id355554941?mt=8" target="_blank">
           SoundHound
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2279" height="206" src="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/ScreenHunter_2060-Jul.-04-19.12.jpg" title="SoundHound" width="257"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         SoundHound is another great app that identifies the song and allows you to see artists info and social media sharing. This is quite similar to Shazam, the only differences are it can recognize melodies that are sung and hummed and can access your music library on your iOS device and play it, lyrics included. It is available at iTunes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mp3downloaderandroid&amp;hl=en" target="_blank">
           Download Free Music MP3 App
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2283" height="261" src="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/ScreenHunter_2061-Jul.-04-19.121.jpg" title="Download Free Music MP3 APP" width="399"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This app is known as every music fanatics best friend. With millions of mp3 music available, you can download any music you want. It can also help you download the mp3 to your Android phone and manage them after downloading. Downloading is fast too, only takes you 15 minutes for 50 songs! Big bonus as there is available artwork on a lot of songs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.windowsphone.com/en-us/store/app/pandora/de2df279-485d-49bb-b53e-3f6a2a9401c1" target="_blank">
           Pandora
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2281" height="207" src="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/ScreenHunter_2061-Jul.-04-19.13.jpg" title="Pandora" width="260"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the most popular music apps out there, Pandora is highly usable and has great featured design. It does well on Windows phones. The only problem is that it stops during songs. It activates and reactivates after disconnecting Bluetooth or when you receive a call and youre using Bluetooth in listening to a tune.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/songza/id453111583?ls=1&amp;mt=8" target="_blank">
           Songza
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2282" height="203" src="http://www.online-radio-stations.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/ScreenHunter_2062-Jul.-04-19.13.jpg" title="Songza" width="254"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This is a cool app as it helps you select music you like based on the time of day and what youre doing at the moment. So if you tell Songza about your current mood status, like if youre at work, it will then play music that soothes your mind and help you focus, if you tell it youre on a workout, it will then play more upbeat to pump you up. It has a variety of HQ music to choose from. You can also save and share your playlists through social media network that includes Facebook and Twitter.
        </p>
       </div>
