            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Button Flag Art
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-above">
             Posted on
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2012-06-26T07:00:11+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
              June 26, 2012
             </time>
             by Cheryl Spangenberg |
             <a class="entry-comments" href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/2012/button-flag-art/#comments" title="Button Flag Art  Comments">
              11 Comments
             </a>
            </div>
            <!-- .entry-meta -->
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
            <p>
             This is the first year that I am really decorating for the 4th of July. That sentence is very strange to me because the 4th of July is one of my favorite holidays so I am not sure why it took me so long to decorate for it? AnywayMichael and I made this really cute, really simple Button Flag Art for our mantle. This would be a great idea if you are looking for some craft ideas for your kids! Michael loved it!
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-in-Frame.jpg">
              <img alt="Button Flag Art in Frame" border="0" height="437" src="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/654x437xButton-Flag-Art-in-Frame_thumb.jpg.pagespeed.ic.kAsHWkRhjj.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="Button Flag Art in Frame" width="654"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Items needed:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Assortment of Red, White &amp; Blue buttons
             </li>
             <li>
              Glue
             </li>
             <li>
              Kraft paper or a brown paper lunch or grocery bag
             </li>
             <li>
              Frame
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-Supplies.jpg">
              <img alt="Button Flag Art Supplies" border="0" height="437" src="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-Supplies_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="Button Flag Art Supplies" width="653"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             I started out by cutting out a 5 x 7 square of Kraft paper (a paper bag will work great too) and inserted it into the frame I chose to use. Then mark your corners so that you dont place buttons where the frame overlaps the photo area
            </p>
            <p>
             Next, we got to it! Michael helped me sort through this awesome bunch of buttons I purchased from
             <a href="http://www.theplaidbarn.com" title="Lets DrinkShall We?">
              The Plaid Barn
             </a>
             . We chose a size and then picked through the bunch to get a collection!
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-Buttons.jpg">
              <img alt="Button Flag Art Buttons" border="0" height="437" src="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-Buttons_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="Button Flag Art Buttons" width="654"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Then I made some thin lines with school glue and we placed all our buttons! Once dried, I put it in the frame and on the mantle it went!
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag.jpg">
              <img alt="Button Flag" border="0" height="437" src="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="Button Flag" width="654"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-in-Frame1.jpg">
              <img alt="Button Flag Art in Frame" border="0" height="437" src="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Button-Flag-Art-in-Frame_thumb1.jpg" style="background-image: none; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px; border-width: 0px;" title="Button Flag Art in Frame" width="654"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             If you missed my post yesterday, click over to get your
             <a href="http://www.thatswhatchesaid.net/?p=4393">
              FREE Flag Printable
             </a>
             ! Apparently I am loving Flags at the moment :)
            </p>
