The company soon thrived and Bilson moved into the Sun Valley, CA, prophouse that was home to ISS for 26 years. In 2003, all ISS operations were combined and moved to our current, expanded facility on Wentworth Street in Sunland. This facility houses our primary prop inventory, as well as our manufacturing and graphics facilities, and is open Monday through Friday 8am to 6pm.
