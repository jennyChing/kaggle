        <p>
         <a href="http://www.hopeforpaws.org/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Hope For Paws
         </a>
         , a non-profit animal rescue organization, received an emergency call for help about a puppy who had been abused and then thrown 9 metres down into a canal of the L.A. river. On the way to the animal hospital the puppy was named Jordan, and his inspiring road to recovery began from there.
         <!--more-->
        </p>
        <p>
         When Jordan was taken into the ER, his rescuer mentions that a man cut his leg and threw him down into the river. Doctors said it was a miracle he was still alive. But after surgery (including a full amputee of his leg) and a few days of recovery, Jordan the puppy was back on his feet and taken in by foster parent Lisa Chiarelli.
        </p>
        <p>
