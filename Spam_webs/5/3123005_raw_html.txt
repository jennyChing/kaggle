        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         DIY Seashell Ampersand
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-05-20T05:00:58+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 20, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://anightowlblog.com/author/kimberlys/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Kimberly Sneed
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://anightowlblog.com/2014/05/diy-seashell-ampersand.html/#comments">
           34 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Make your own DIY Seashell Ampersand in just a few easy steps!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15354" height="981" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DIY-Seashell-Ampersand-1.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/05/diy-seashell-ampersand.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DIY-Seashell-Ampersand-1.jpg&amp;description=DIY Seashell Ampersand')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hi friends! Summer is around the corner, so were breaking out the seashells and beach-themed goodies and welcoming the heat, right?! Well at least the first part is right  Im not sure Im quite ready for the 100+ degree heat we get here in sunny Arizona! But what I DO know is that were ready to simplify and brighten up our home decor. And today we have JUST the fun, beachy project for you  a DIY Seashell Ampersand!
        </p>
        <p>
         Ok  I KNOW Im not the only one who is obsessed with ampersands these days! Ive found that I have a small little collection growing at home, and I have had PLENTY of ideas how to spruce some plain ones up  crafty DIY style! And this project is no exception. I love the beauty and complexity of seashells, and when I found a bag of seashell vase filler that were little pieces of iridescent goodness I KNEW that this would be the perfect little Summer project! So lets go over the details so you can make your own (you KNOW you wanna!)
        </p>
        <h3>
         What youll need:
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000LNR27U/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000LNR27U&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=aniow04-20&amp;linkId=52XKGMENRNQLOUB4" target="_blank">
           Elmers Craft Bond Tacky Glue
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00IRLJUPU/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00IRLJUPU&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=aniow04-20&amp;linkId=UUHO35NY4EN2REZ6" target="_blank">
           Wooden ampersand
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          Seashell vase filler
         </li>
         <li>
          Foam brush
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Make your own DIY Seashell Ampersand in just a few easy steps!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15355" height="1487" src="http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DIY-Seashell-Ampersand-2.jpg" width="650"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/05/diy-seashell-ampersand.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DIY-Seashell-Ampersand-2.jpg&amp;description=DIY Seashell Ampersand')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         And the steps:
        </h3>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Simply apply Elmers Craft Bond Tacky Glue (it goes on white, dries clear!) to a section of the wooden ampersand.
         </li>
         <li>
          Spread the glue evenly over the section with a foam brush.
         </li>
         <li>
          Try sprinkling and placing the seashell pieces in place until the section is full to your liking.
         </li>
         <li>
          Repeat these steps until the whole ampersand is full of seashells!
         </li>
         <li>
          At this point you might want to look over the ampersand to see if there are any large holes you want to fill with little bits of seashells.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         And youre done! And the result is one adorable DIY Seashell Ampersand, dont you think?!
        </p>

         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/05/diy-seashell-ampersand.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DIY-Seashell-Ampersand-3.jpg&amp;description=DIY Seashell Ampersand')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://anightowlblog.com/2014/05/diy-seashell-ampersand.html/&amp;media=http://anightowlblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DIY-Seashell-Ampersand-4.jpg&amp;description=DIY Seashell Ampersand')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>

         This pretty little ampersand would be perfect for your Summer beach-themed mantel or your growing gallery wall  come on  you know you have one! How fabulous would a WHOLE gallery wall of ampersands be? I think you should make one and show me. Just saying.
