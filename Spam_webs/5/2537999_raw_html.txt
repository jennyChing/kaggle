         Celebrate with Ice Cream Cone Cupcakes
        </h1>

         </span>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          My son had a bake sale at school this week and well, hes a little competitive  kinda like his mama! He wanted to have the best treat to sell, one that was sure to sell out instantly. And so I went to work thinking about the ultimate kid-friendly sweets! I knew I wanted to make cupcakes because they are single serving, easy to transport and, oh yeah, yummy! But knowing that every single person on the planet would be making cupcakes I wanted to do something to set ours apart. I had seen people make ice cream cone cupcakes before but they generally didnt end up looking realistic. So with an idea in my mind, I set up to make ice cream cone cupcakes that looked just like the soft serve you would get in an ice cream shop  only they would be delicious iced cupcakes on the inside! Heres how you can make them too!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cone-Cupcakes.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cone Cupcakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23455" height="900" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cone-Cupcakes.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="javascript:void((function(){var%20e=document.createElement('script');e.setAttribute('type','text/javascript');e.setAttribute('charset','UTF-8');e.setAttribute('src','http://assets.pinterest.com/js/pinmarklet.js?r='+Math.random()*99999999);document.body.appendChild(e)})());">
           <img alt="Pin It" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.clickinmoms.com/cmprodaily/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/pinit.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            What You Will Need:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Box of Yellow Cake Mix
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Box of Jell-O Vanilla Instant Pudding
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Cup of Sour Cream
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          4 Eggs
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1/2 Cup of Vegatable Oil
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1/2 Cup of Water
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Flat Bottom Ice Cream Cones
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          For the Icing:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Stick of Butter
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Package of Cream Cheese
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1/2 Cup of Crisco Vegetable Shortening
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          1 Tablespoon of Vanilla Extract
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          2 Cups of Confectioners Sugar
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Sprinkles of Your Choice for the Topping
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            How To Make Them:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Preheat your oven to 350 degrees. Then mix together your cake mix, Jell-O, eggs, sour cream oil and water. Blend them well until they form a dough consistency. It will be a little thicker and that is how you want it. Set your cupcake batter aside and get your ice cream cones ready. To do this, cover your cupcake tins with aluminum foil, poking a hole in the middle of each tin. Use your finger to increase the size of the hole and then push your ice cream cone through the holes, using the aluminum foil to secure them.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cupcakes 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23448" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes-1.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Once your cones are ready, place your cupcake batter into a large icing bag and cut off the tip (there is no need to use an icing tip since youre simply filling the cones this way).
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes2.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cupcakes2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23449" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes2.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Fill each ice cream cone about 2/3 of the way full with cupcake batter, tapping them gently to help the batter settle.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes3.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cupcakes3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23450" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes3.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Bake your cupcakes for 17 to 19 minutes or until the top turns golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean. Them remove them from the oven and allow them to cool completely before icing!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes4.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cupcakes4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23451" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes4.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Once your ice cream cone cupcakes are completely cooled, mix together your butter, cream cheese, shortening, and vanilla extract in an electric blender. Allow it to mix for about 5 minutes to ensure your frosting is fluffy enough to create an ice cream look. Then slowly mix in your 2 cups of Confectioners Sugar and continue mixing until your icing is smooth. Once your icing is ready, transfer it to an icing bag with large star tip and pipe your icing onto your cupcakes in a circular motion, in the same way soft serve ice cream would be added.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes1.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cupcakes1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23452" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes1.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          If you are not ready to serve them, place your iced cupcakes immediately in the refrigerator to help the icing set. Once you are ready to serve them, remove them from the fridge and add sprinkles to the top!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes.jpg">
           <img alt="Ice Cream Cupcakes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23453" height="400" src="http://jmanandmillerbug.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Ice-Cream-Cupcakes.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Serve them immediately for a treat that is sure to thrill any age! The mixture of crispy ice cream cone, moist cupcakes and decedent icing combines for a treat that will be a winner each and every time!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          So, back to my sons bake sale. Our ice cream cone cupcakes were a hit! In fact they sold out almost immediately and I was his hero for a day! For just little effort and creativity in the kitchen that was the best reward ever!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Happy baking!
         </p>
