          Diya Aur Baati Hum: Rajkumar is Again Come Back for Revenge
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         We saw Suraj and Sandhyas marriage anniversary celebration in the last episode of Diya Aur Baati Hum.
         <em>
          Suraj Rathi
         </em>
         (Anas Rashid) and
         <em>
          Sandhya Rathi
         </em>
         (Deepika Singh), exchanging gifts. Suraj gifted a hand watch to Sandhya and thanks her for lovely cake she made for him. This however comes across as a shock to Sandhya, who said that she never prepared this cake for him. The duo was troubled about the same and wondered about who sent the cake.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Rajkumar (Gaurav Sharma) on the other side was seen playing a slick trick with the jail guard, and eventually escaping from jail.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         In the upcoming episodes of Diya Aur Baati Hum, we will see a jail guard dropping by to check on Rajkumar. He will see a figure sleeping in the jail cell, thinking that Rajkumar is in the jail locker. Sandhya will decide to visit Rajkumar in jail, but will be shocked that he has escaped and someone else is sleeping in his locker instead.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         What will happen next? What will Sandhya do after detected about Rajkumars escape?
