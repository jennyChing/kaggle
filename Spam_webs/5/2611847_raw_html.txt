           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <a href="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283DE28400000578-3044227-image-a-65_1430587864097.jpg">
             <img alt="283DE28400000578-3044227-image-a-65_1430587864097" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10582" height="448" src="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283DE28400000578-3044227-image-a-65_1430587864097.jpg" width="688"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            The wait is finally over. The Duchess of Cambridge, Catherine33 years, has given birth to a little princess, an 8lb 3oz sister for George. The little girl came into the world at 8:34 a.m. (3:34 a.m. ET) Saturday at Londons St. Marys Hospital, Kensington Palace announced.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            Her Royal Highness and her child are both doing well, the palace tweeted.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            The baby comes nearly two years after the birth of Prince George, formally named His Royal Highness Prince George of Cambridge. The baby, regardless of gender, will be fourth in line to the throne. When George came into the world on July 22, 2013, he catapulted ahead of Prince Harry to become the third in line to the throne  right behind his grandfather, Prince Charles, and his father, William. Kate and Williams newborn is now fourth in line, pushing Harry to fifth place. Prince Andrew, Queen Elizabeth IIs second son, now shifts to sixth in line to the throne.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            Kate carried her daughter out of the Lindo Wings front door before posing for a few minutes with William. The duchess was wearing a yellow and white summer dress and high heels. Prince William was dressed in a white shirt, a blue v-neck jumper and dark blue slacks.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <a href="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283DF26800000578-3044227-The_Royal_baby_who_will_be_fourth_in_line_to_the_throne_emerged_-a-139_1430591742387.jpg">
             <img alt="Kate carried her daughter out of the Lindo Wing's front door " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7874" height="427" src="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283DF26800000578-3044227-The_Royal_baby_who_will_be_fourth_in_line_to_the_throne_emerged_-a-139_1430591742387.jpg" width="800"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            After waving to supporters the royal couple returned inside before re-emerging minutes later with William carrying the newborn princess in a baby carrier. He put his daughter in the back of a black Land Rover before the Cambridges drove off to Kensington Palace.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <a href="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283E18BA00000578-3044227-image-a-138_14305917421902.jpg">
             <img alt="kate and william waving to supporters" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7877" height="437" src="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283E18BA00000578-3044227-image-a-138_14305917421902.jpg" width="800"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            Following the announcement, a framed notice of the birth was put on display on a ceremonial easel in front of Buckingham Palace. London immediately broke into celebration over the news of the little princess, with royal fans holding signs outside of the Lindo Wing at St. Marys. Even the Tower Bridge was lit up to mark the occasion.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <a href="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283E18BA00000578-3044227-image-a-138_14305917421901.jpg">
             <img alt="Celebration - kate new born baby girl" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7876" height="415" src="http://leboncoin.nu/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/283E18BA00000578-3044227-image-a-138_14305917421901.jpg" width="800"/>
            </a>
           </p>
