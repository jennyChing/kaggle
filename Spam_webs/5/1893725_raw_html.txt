                    <meta content="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-hEXHV-2IZ84/TrH9lq75-kI/AAAAAAAAEr4/r2x-ut5CDXE/s1600/IMG_7772.JPG" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Roll Your Own Wraps (Homemade Tortillas)
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6944323692361753821" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-hEXHV-2IZ84/TrH9lq75-kI/AAAAAAAAEr4/r2x-ut5CDXE/s1600/IMG_7772.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="how to make your own tortillas" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-hEXHV-2IZ84/TrH9lq75-kI/AAAAAAAAEr4/r2x-ut5CDXE/s1600/IMG_7772.JPG" title="homemade tortilla recipe"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      I grew up very spoiled - my mother always made us fresh tortillas from scratch.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      We stuffed them with veggies and soybeans and cheese and guacamole, and anything else you might fancy in your wrap (it's a hotly debated and highly personalized matter in my family!).
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      To this day, it's still everyone's favourite meal. Even my husband requests it every time we visit my parents.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      I was so spoiled growing up that I only ever tried those rubbery, tasteless store bought tortillas after I moved out on my own.... and let me tell you, they are NOT the same thing!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      Rolling out your own tortillas is a lot less difficult than you might imagine, and it's definitely worth the effort every now and then when you have a little extra time to spare.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      Read on for the how to.....
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        <b>
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                          <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                           Homemade Flour Tortillas
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; min-height: 14px; text-align: left;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                          2 cups flour
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                          1/2 tsp salt
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                          1 cup warm water
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                          1/2 tsp dry active yeast
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: center;">
                      <div style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                          1/2 tsp sugar
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: 12.0px Helvetica; margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; min-height: 14.0px;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         In a small bowl mix water, sugar and yeast, and let stand for 5 minutes.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         In another bowl mix flour and salt.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         Add wet ingredients all at once into the flour, and mix with a wooden spoon.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         Then, using clean fingers, mix until incorporated into a ball and knead on the countertop for about 1 minute. Lightly oil the bottom of the bowl, and place dough back into the bowl. Cover with a damp cloth and let sit on the counter at room temperature for 30-60 minutes.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; min-height: 14px; text-align: left;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                        <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         Divide the dough into approximately 2-inch balls.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         On a well-floured surface, roll out each tortilla to 3-4 mm thickness. Add flour as necessary to avoid sticking.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-c8UX_dWF6Jw/TrH-s35Z_NI/AAAAAAAAEsA/A63kIIez4WQ/s1600/fryingtortillas.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <img alt="how to make your own tortillas" border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-c8UX_dWF6Jw/TrH-s35Z_NI/AAAAAAAAEsA/A63kIIez4WQ/s640/fryingtortillas.jpg" title="how to make your own tortillas" width="428"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: normal normal normal 12px/normal Helvetica; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="letter-spacing: 0px;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: large;">
                         Place on dry heated frying pan (no oil), and cook tortilla for approximately 1 minute, until it starts to bubble up &amp; rise, then flip over and cook the other side for another minute. Place the cooked tortilla on a plate and cover with a damp tea towel, while rolling &amp; frying up the rest.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="font: 12.0px Helvetica; margin: 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px 0.0px; min-height: 14.0px;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Times, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                       <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: small;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
