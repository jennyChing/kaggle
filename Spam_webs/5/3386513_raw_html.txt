      <h2>
       <a href="http://www.ericasweettooth.com/2009/12/chocolate-hazelnut-mousse.html" rel="bookmark">
        Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="postmeta">
       <em>
        posted by
       </em>
       <a href="http://www.ericasweettooth.com" rel="author external" title="Visit Ericas website">
        Erica
       </a>
       <em>
        on
       </em>
       December 29, 2009
       <div class="ccount">
        <a href="http://www.ericasweettooth.com/2009/12/chocolate-hazelnut-mousse.html#comments" title="Comment on Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse">
         48
         <em>
          comments 
         </em>
        </a>
       </div>
      </div>
     </div>
     <p>
      <span style="font-size: 100%;">
       <span style="font-family: georgia;">
        I had great intentions of serving this mousse on Christmas  I was going to make it into more of a cake using fancy pirouette cookies standing upright as the outer crust. But I was tired of baking (gasp!) by the time Friday rolled around and thus somehow we survived on only apple pie, the cupcake wreath, and sugar cookies. I still had all the ingredients though, so I rethought the recipe to fit the next big holiday: New Years Eve!
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/SzpdC7q6ekI/AAAAAAAABN4/2vqwQvXSzzs/s1600-h/IMG_1786.JPG" style="font-family: georgia;">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5420747406452619842" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/SzpdC7q6ekI/AAAAAAAABN4/2vqwQvXSzzs/s400/IMG_1786.JPG" style="margin: 0px auto 10px; display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 348px; height: 521px;"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ericasweettooth.com/2009/12/chocolate-hazelnut-mousse.html&amp;media=http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/SzpdC7q6ekI/AAAAAAAABN4/2vqwQvXSzzs/s400/IMG_1786.JPG&amp;description=Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       This was my first time making mousse, it was really fun to use my mixer to make the whipped cream and then gently fold in the chocolate-hazelnut mixture using my knowledge from Ina and Martha.
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/SzpcqF2HaxI/AAAAAAAABNo/3-h4q2PLG3k/s1600-h/IMG_1779.JPG" style="font-family: georgia;">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5420746979687230226" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/SzpcqF2HaxI/AAAAAAAABNo/3-h4q2PLG3k/s400/IMG_1779.JPG" style="margin: 0px auto 10px; display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 494px; height: 332px;"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ericasweettooth.com/2009/12/chocolate-hazelnut-mousse.html&amp;media=http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/SzpcqF2HaxI/AAAAAAAABNo/3-h4q2PLG3k/s400/IMG_1779.JPG&amp;description=Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
      <span style="font-size: 100%;">
       <br/>
       <span style="font-family: georgia;">
        I served the mousse in martini glasses (a new gift from Ryan to my parents!) to be festive. They are really the perfect size for a dessert like this and dress up this extremely simple recipe.
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/Szpc4eUEmOI/AAAAAAAABNw/MFwlu4iZTos/s1600-h/IMG_1781.JPG" style="font-family: georgia;">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5420747226773493986" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/Szpc4eUEmOI/AAAAAAAABNw/MFwlu4iZTos/s400/IMG_1781.JPG" style="margin: 0px auto 10px; display: block; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 489px; height: 326px;"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ericasweettooth.com/2009/12/chocolate-hazelnut-mousse.html&amp;media=http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_Ig27CzOevQY/Szpc4eUEmOI/AAAAAAAABNw/MFwlu4iZTos/s400/IMG_1781.JPG&amp;description=Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       The mousse is topped with chocolate shavings and toasted hazelnuts, and those pirouette cookies got some use as well. Very cute and delicious!
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span style="font-style: italic; font-family: georgia;">
       <span style="font-weight: bold;">
        Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       1-1/2 cups whipping cream
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       1/2 cup Nutella spread
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       2 tbsp hot water (not boiling)
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       2 tsp instant espresso
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       Dash of Kahlua
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       6 Chocolate-hazelnut pirouette cookies
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       Chocolate and chopped hazelnuts for topping
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       - Dissolve instant espresso in hot water in a medium sized bowl
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       - Add the Nutella and Kahlua, beating until smooth
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       - In a mixer, whip the whipping cream until it forms stiff peaks
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       - Gently fold the nutella mixture into the whipped cream until the color is uniform
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       - Divide mousse into serving glasses (I think martini glasses work great!)
      </span>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-family: georgia;">
       - Add toasted hazelnuts (to toast, just stick them in a 350 degree oven for about 10 minutes), chocolate shavings, and pirouette cookies for decoration
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
