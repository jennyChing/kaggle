         Our Hot Dog Tour Stops at Nu Way in Macon, Ga.
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-12T07:58:57+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 12, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/author/blogging" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Shirley Wood
           </span>

         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Our Hot Dog Tour Stops at Nu Way in Macon, Ga.
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           We love hot dogs in my family!Because my husband, Stacy and I aresuch big fans of hot dogs, we have an ongoing hot dog tour that we do. We like to look for the local favorite hot dog place on our journeys.Well of course we both thoughtI should share that journey on my blog.
          </span>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           The
          </span>
          <span style="color: #ff6600;">
           <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/hot-dog-tour" style="color: #ff6600;" target="_blank">
            Hot Dog Tour
           </a>
          </span>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           has its own page on the blog,
          </span>
          <span style="color: #ff6600;">
           <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/hot-dog-tour" style="color: #ff6600;" target="_blank">
            here.
           </a>
          </span>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Be sure to click over and see the places we have shared so far.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Having lived in the middle Georgia area most of my life, it would just be wrong not to share the local favorite,
          </span>
          <span style="color: #ff6600;">
           <a href="http://www.nu-wayweiners.com/home.html" style="color: #ff6600;" target="_blank">
            Nu Way
           </a>
           .
          </span>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Its where the locals eat in Macon, Ga.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Nu-Way-Hot-Dogs-Macon-Ga.-stop-on-the-hot-dog-tour-with-us-at-intelligentdomestications.com_.jpg">
           <img alt="Nu Way Hot Dogs Macon, Ga. stop on the hot dog tour with us at intelligentdomestications.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5788" height="1024" src="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Nu-Way-Hot-Dogs-Macon-Ga.-stop-on-the-hot-dog-tour-with-us-at-intelligentdomestications.com_-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Order you dog all the way for mustard, onions and chili sauce. Nu Way has really good slaw so a regular order for me is one of each along with a soda swimming in their famous flaky ice. New York times voted the slaw dogs as No. 1 in the nation!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           I always get the slaw dog and Stacy always gets both. Not only would be go back but we make it a habit! We give Nu Way four thumbs up, my thumbs and Stacys too!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Nu-Way-Mega-Burger.-Macon-Ga.intelligentdomestications.com_.jpg">
           <img alt="Nu Way Mega Burger. Macon, Ga.intelligentdomestications.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5786" height="657" src="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Nu-Way-Mega-Burger.-Macon-Ga.intelligentdomestications.com_-1024x657.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Even though we are on the Hot Dog Tour, we simply must mention the Mega Burger at Nu Way. I do not have a habit of ordering hamburgers in restaurants but sometimes I actually skip the hot dogs and get the Mega Burger at Nu Way!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Author and news columnist,
           <a href='&lt;a%20target="_blank" href="http://www.amazon.com/Ed-Grisamore/e/B001K8TO26/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;tag=www.intelligentdomestications.com-20&amp;linkId=VMKYGB3TLSM5XX73"&gt;Ed Grisamore&lt;/a&gt;&lt;img src="https://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=www.intelligentdomestications.com-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" width="1" height="1" border="0" alt="" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" /&gt;' target="_blank" title="book by Ed Grisamore available on Amazon">
            Ed Grisamore
           </a>
           recorded the history and legacy of Nu Way in his book,
           <a href='&lt;a%20href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0881462691/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=0881462691&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=www.intelligentdomestications.com-20&amp;linkId=BAOQOJLC4E3TQ3JA"&gt;There Is More Than One Way to Spell Wiener: The Story of Nu-Way&lt;/a&gt;&lt;img src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=www.intelligentdomestications.com-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=0881462691" width="1" height="1" border="0" alt="" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" /&gt;' target="_blank" title="There Is More Than One Way To Spell Wiener available on Amazon">
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             <em>
              <strong>
               The Is More Than One Way To Spell Wiener.
              </strong>
             </em>
            </span>
           </a>
           Included in the book iswhy the word weiner came to be spelled wrong for the Nu Way restaurants.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           The secret to grilling a Nu Way hot dog is revealed in this You Tube video shared by The Macon Telegraph.
           <br/>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/tO_0MJrgdOM?list=PLbx7DCwqFkH_cAyTP75hJmYIATjVMJyZC" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <br/>
           With about 9 locations in 3 middle Georgia cities, you wont have to go too far for a Nu Way.
          </span>
         </p>
