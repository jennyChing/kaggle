         <h1 class="post-title" data-post-id="876816" itemprop="name">
          Make These Easter Brunch Place Cards in Under 5 Minutes
         </h1>
         <h4 class="post-meta clearfix">
          <div class="avatar nopin">
           <img alt="Anjelika Temple" class="avatar avatar-44 wp-user-avatar wp-user-avatar-44 alignnone photo nopin" height="44" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/temple-anj-11.jpg?fit=crop&amp;w=44&amp;h=44&amp;ver=1423172653.1" width="44"/>
          </div>
          <div class="posted-on-wrapper">
           <p>
            by
            <a href="//www.brit.co/author/anjelika/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Anjelika Temple">
             <span itemprop="author">
              Anjelika Temple
             </span>
            </a>
            on
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-03-18T07:50:05+00:00" pubdate="">
             March 18, 2015
            </time>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="addthis_responsive_sharing" style="display: none">
          </div>
         </h4>
        </div>
        <div class="post-content inner">
         <p class="p1">
          Easter brunch is right around the corner, and its time to start pinning all the decorations you want to make and taste-testing all the yummy recipes youre going to cook. Custom place cards add a touch of sophistication to any affair, so weve teamed up with
          <a href="http://www.ghirardelli.com/easter-2015" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Ghirardelli
          </a>
          to turn their sweet little squares into the sweetest place cards ever.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="1-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876818" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/1-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Add a few colorful plates and linens, a vase of daffodils and gold flatware to the scene and your brunch is already looking pretty on point. And if you need even more sweetness on your table, whip up a batch of
          <a href="//www.brit.co/peanut-butter-easter-eggs/" target="_blank">
           Peanut Butter Easter Eggs
          </a>
          and youll be in business!
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="13-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876830" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/13-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Who doesnt love kicking off Easter brunch with dessert? ;)
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <b>
           Materials:
           <br/>
          </b>
          - hot pink washi tape
          <br/>
           mint card stock
          <br/>
           teal or turquoise card stock
          <br/>
           Brit + Co Place Card Printable (
          <a href="https://pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Brit-co_Punny-Easter-printable.pdf" target="_blank">
           download here
          </a>
          )
          <br/>
          Ghirardelli Spring Impressions Squares
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <b>
           Tools:
           <br/>
          </b>
          - xacto knife
          <br/>
           cutting mat
          <br/>
           ruler
          <br/>
           tape
          <br/>
           scissors
          <br/>
           calligraphy pen
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <b>
           Instructions:
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          1. Create the base card by cutting one piece of card stock in half, lengthwise. Fold each card.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          2. Cut smaller pieces of card stock out of mint that fit on the right side of the card.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          3. Use washi tape to add diagonal stripes to the mint piece.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          4. Print out our punny place card printables, cut and attach to the other side of the folded card.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          5. Assemble the card! Tape the striped card to the right side of the folded card and attach a Ghirardelli Square right in the middle.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          6. Fold the cards shut and label with your guests names and youre done!
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="New-SUPPLIES-SHOT-2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-882439" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/New-SUPPLIES-SHOT-2.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Were betting you already have most of these materials in your house  just add Ghirardelli Squares as the finishing touch!
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="4-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Card" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876821" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/4-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Card.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Create the base card by cutting one piece of 8 1/2 x 11 inch card stock in half, lengthwise. Fold each card.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="5-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Tape" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876822" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="1100" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/5-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Tape.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Cut smaller pieces of card stock out of mint that fit on the right side of the card. Then use washi tape to add diagonal stripes to the mint piece.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="6-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Printable" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876823" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/6-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Printable.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Print out our punny place cards printable.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="7-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Printable" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876824" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/7-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards-Printable.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Cut and attach to the other side of the folded card.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="8-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876825" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/8-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Assemble the card! Tape the striped card to the right side of the folded card and attach a Ghirardelli Square right in the middle.Fold the cards shut and label with your guests names and youre done!
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="9-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876826" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/9-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="10-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876827" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/10-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          How sweet is that?
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="11-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876828" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/11-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Adorn your table with these cards to sweeten things up on Easter Sunday.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="12-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876829" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/12-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="14-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876831" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/14-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <img alt="13-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-876830" data-pin-no-hover="true" height="733" src="//pixel.brit.co/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/13-Ghirardelli-Place-Cards.jpg" width="1100"/>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Hoppy Easter!
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <i>
           This post is a collaboration with Ghirardelli.
          </i>
         </p>
