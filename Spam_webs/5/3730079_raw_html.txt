                              <h1 class="entry-title">
                               Peanut Butter Pupcakes
                              </h1>
                             </div>
                             <div class="metabar">
                              <div class="metabar-pad">
                               <em>
                                <time class="date time published updated sc" datetime="2015-03-10T06:00:00+00:00">

                            <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
                            <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="15439516" data-link="http://fakeginger.com/2015/03/10/peanut-butter-pupcakes/" data-summary="" data-title="Peanut Butter Pupcakes">
                            </div>
                            <p>
                             <img alt="Peanut Butter Pupcakes - treat your pup to a fun dog-friendly cupcake!" class="aligncenter" height="800" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7650/16551657317_982ac02239_c.jpg" width="536"/>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             In case you didnt know, Im kind of a crazy dog lady.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             I mean, maybe.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Just a little.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Sometimes I look at my Instagram and think, Omg, Ive got to post some food or some mountains or SOMETHING before all my people unfollow me because of all the dog photos!
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Its bad.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             But anyway, Clementine turned 4 on Sunday which is CRAZY to me. How has 4 years passed already?!
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             We got her right after we moved to Colorado  I made my husband drive up to Black Forest to put a deposit on her before we even had a house! And Id had her name picked out for literally years.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             So for her birthday Imade her some Peanut Butter Pupcakes because what pup doesnt like homemade, dog-friendly cake for their birthday?
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             I cant really vouch for their taste but both dogs gobbled them up and they were a lot of fun to bake. Less stress when youre baking for dogs, thats for sure!
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <img alt="Peanut Butter Pupcakes - treat your dog to cake! " class="aligncenter" height="428" src="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8621/16138973183_94b5e3dd9e_z.jpg" width="640"/>
                            </p>

                                 Peanut Butter Pupcakes

                               <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
                                <div class="fl-l width-50">
                                </div>
                                <div class="fl-l width-50">
                                 <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
                                  <span itemprop="recipeYield">
                                   6 pupcakes
                                  </span>
                                 </p>
                                </div>
                                <div class="zlclear">
                                </div>
                               </div>
                               <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
                                Ingredients
                               </p>
                               <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 cup flour
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 teaspoon baking soda
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/4 cup peanut butter
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/4 cup vegetable oil
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 cup shredded carrots
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/3 cup honey
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 egg
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7">
                                 For the frosting:
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/2 cup peanut butter
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 6 small dog bones
                                </div>
                               </span>
                               <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
                                Instructions
                               </p>
                               <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 Preheat oven to 350F. Line 6 muffin cups with paper liners.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda until no clumps remain. Set aside.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 In a separate bowl, beat peanut butter and vegetable oil with an electric mixer until smooth. Add in carrots, honey, and the egg and beat. Add flour mixture and beat until just combined. Divide the batter into the 6 prepared muffin cups.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 Bake for 22 - 25 minutes or until cooked through. Cool completely before frosting.
                                </li>
                                <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4">
                                 To make the frosting:
                                </div>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 Beat the cream cheese and peanut butter until smooth. Top the cupcakes with a generous mound of frosting. Decorate with a dog bones.
                                </li>
                               </ol>
