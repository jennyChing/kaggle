         <p>
          Year after year, I have so much to be thankful. I have an amazing son, a loving family, good friends, and two adorable little dogs. Blogging has given me the opportunity to have some amazing experiences, as well as a chance to help other people in my community. I am truly blessed.

         <p>
          I am really looking forward to the Thanksgiving holiday. Just this morning I mentioned on Twitter that the list of friends and family joining us for Thanksgiving this year, seems to be growing by the minute. Its going to be a joyous occasion. I really look forward to making some of my sons favorites in the kitchen, and catching up with old family friends.
         </p>
         <p>
          The extra people at the house and tempting smells from the kitchen are sure to have my two adorable (spoiled rotten) dogs on edge. Winston and Lilly are such hound dogs to begin with, so the antics and begging is sure to be off the charts on Thanksgiving. Lucky for them, I have been challenged by Big Heart Pet Brands to write this sponsored post sharing some ideas for DIY Thanksgiving dog gifts.
         </p>
         <p>
          These diy dog gifts are the perfect way to celebrate the fall season with my furry family members. I know these
          <strong>
           Thanksgiving Doggie Bags
          </strong>
          will be much enjoyed by my two begging pups.
         </p>

         </p>
         <h1>
          Thanksgiving Doggie Bags

          In addition to the dog treat bags for Winston and Lilly, we made up some extra Thanksgiving doggie bags for our guest to take home to their pets.
         </p>
         <p>

          <a href="http://see.walmart.com/treat-the-pups?cid=sbo.724.11408.2842" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Walmart
          </a>
          . Then I raided my craft closet and kitchen for supplies.
         </p>
         <p>
          Suggested materials for these Thanksgiving doggie bags include: brown paper lunch bags, bakers twine, paw print stamps and ink, cardboard favor boxes, clear treat bags, ribbon, and gift tags. You can download and print my printable dog treat gift tag for your own personal use.
         </p>
