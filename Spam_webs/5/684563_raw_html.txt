      <h2>
       Asus today announced the PQ321 31.5 inch monitor which is going to be available in the US at the end of June 2013.
      </h2>
      <p>
       The PQ321 is a 31.5 inch LED backlit Ultra HD monitor with an 8ms response time, Ultra HD 38402160 resolution which is four times the resolution of a full HD display with 176 degree viewing angles.
      </p>
      <p>
       The PQ321s ultra HD display features 140 pixels-per-inch with 16:9 aspect ratio and supports 10-bit RGB color.
      </p>
      <p>
       2w speakers are also hidden inside this beauty  while the speakers are nothing to write home about its still great that Asus managed to squeeze them into this extremely thin monitor enclosure.
      </p>
      <p>
       Also worth mentioning: The PQ321 31.5 Inch monitor is the thinnest 4K Ultra HD monitor on the market today.
      </p>
      <p>
       The ASUS PQ321 True 4K UHD Monitor uses cutting-edge Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide rather than traditional amorphous silicon for the active layer of its LCD panel. IGZO panels support much smaller transistors than amorphous silicon, which in turn gives much smaller pixels and the 3840 x 2160 resolution of the PQ321 is four times that of a 1920 x 1080 Full HD display.
      </p>
