          <h1 class="post_title theme_title entry-title" itemprop="name">
           Mt Martha Beach House by Wolveridge Architects
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="post_text_area" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           <strong>
            the mt martha beach house is a four bedroom dwelling by australian firm,
            <a href="http://wolveridge.com.au/" target="_blank">
             wolveridge architects
            </a>
           </strong>
           . despite a steep slope crossing the site, wolveridge was able to seamlessly connect the kitchen, dining and living spaces to the north facing pool, deck and grass areas at a single living level. the design includes
           <strong>
            a combination of open and intimate areas that maximize solar access and views to north
           </strong>
           , while simultaneously ensuring protection from harsh coastal winds for year round access.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="mt. martha beach house designboom" class="alignnone" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/dbsub/392161/2015-03-04/img_1_1425428411_3f3974d0eb7257e3709dbacc447a0029.jpg" title="mt. martha beach house designboom" width="900"/>
           <br/>
           living room
          </p>
          <p>
           built for a young family, the
           <strong>
            childrens bedrooms are sized to accommodate suitable study areas, sleep-overs, and amble storage throughout the years
           </strong>
           . theyre disconnected from the main floor, enabling the day to day activities of the household to go unbothered by whatever weekend activities are happening above.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/dbsub/392161/2015-03-04/img_2_1425428411_bef6bbaa6cf4ab9d4793b27c7af9aca9.jpg" width="900"/>
           <br/>
           living and dining areas
          </p>
          <p>
           australian ecologically sustainable development
           <strong>
            (ESD) principles such as cross ventilation play an important role in ensuring spaces can be cooled efficiently whilst maintaining a connection to the coastal region
           </strong>
           . the home is made of traditional materials such as timber and cement sheets, combined with more robust materials like concrete, stone, and zinc where weather will have more of an effect.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="mt. martha beach house designboom" class="alignnone" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/dbsub/392161/2015-03-04/img_3_1425428411_06c1d7c112e4cd8179fd54e9eaf44c08.jpg" title="mt. martha beach house designboom" width="900"/>
           <br/>
           the home merges seamlessly from interior to exterior
          </p>
          <p title="">
           <img alt="mtmarthadesignboom02" class="alignnone wp-image-250284 size-medium" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/mtmarthadesignboom02-818x546.jpg" title="" width="900"/>
           <br/>
           (left) single level deck and yard
           <br/>
           (right) view from the pool facing south
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="mtmarthadesignboom01" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-250283" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/mtmarthadesignboom01-818x546.jpg" width="900"/>
           <br/>
           steep slope
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="alignnone" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/dbsub/392161/2015-03-04/img_7_1425428411_0d893d685f7f5c444d066b60aff227b7.jpg" title="mt. martha beach house designboom" width="900"/>
           <br/>
           sun and privacy screening
          </p>
          <p class="from-source">
           From::
           <a href="http://www.designboom.com/architecture/mt-martha-beach-house-wolveridge-architects-03-12-2015/" target="_blank" title="mt. martha beach house by wolveridge perches above aussie coast">
            Designboom
           </a>
          </p>
